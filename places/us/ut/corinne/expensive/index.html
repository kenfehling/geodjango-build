Compared to the surrounding areas, Corinne is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Corinne is $172,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $77,440 (30%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $45,100 (20%) lower, and is approximately $142,100 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Corinne the median rent is $1,188 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $308 (34%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $126 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $203 (20%) higher.
