Home prices

The median home cost in Vernal is $166,400. This is roughly $21,540 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $51,100 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Rock Springs it is about $45,400 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Vernal is $732 a month. This is about $103 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $330 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Rock Springs is about $146 (16%) lower.
