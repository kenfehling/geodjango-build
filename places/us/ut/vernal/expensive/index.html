Home prices

The median home cost in Vernal is $166,400. This is roughly $21,540 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $51,100 (23%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rock Springs is roughly $45,400 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Vernal the median monthly rent is $732. It is about $103 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $330 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $146 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Rock Springs.
