Home prices

The cost of the median home in Duchesne is $165,000. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $12,300 (8%) higher. It is approximately $52,500 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $106,300 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.

Rents

In Duchesne the median rent is $863 a month. This is about $36 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $199 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.
