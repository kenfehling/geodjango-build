Compared to other places in this area, Kanarraville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. George, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kanarraville is valued at $189,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $59,620 (23%) lower. It is about $28,300 (13%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $96,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of St. George.

Rents

The median rent in Kanarraville is $638 a month. This is roughly $381 (37%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $354 (35%) lower compared to the city of St. George.
