Home prices

The median home in Kanarraville costs $189,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $59,620 (23%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $28,300 (13%) lower, and is about $96,800 (33%) lower compared to the city of St. George.

Rents

In Kanarraville the median rent is $638 per month. This is roughly $381 (37%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $424 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of St. George it is roughly $354 (35%) lower.
