Home prices

Ferron's median home price is $151,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $13,500 (9%) higher. It is about $66,200 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $120,000 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.

Rents

The median rent in Ferron is $541 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $93 (14%) lower. It is roughly $521 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Provo it is about $336 (38%) lower.
