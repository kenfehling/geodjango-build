Home prices

The median home in Mantua costs $285,500. This is about $37,460 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $68,000 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $29,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

Mantua's median rent is $925 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $109 (13%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $137 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $60 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
