Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mantua is $925. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $109 (13%) higher.It is approximately $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $60 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
