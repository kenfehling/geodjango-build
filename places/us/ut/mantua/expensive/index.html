Home prices

The median home in Mantua costs $285,500. This is about $37,460 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $68,000 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $29,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

Mantua's median monthly rent is $925. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $109 (13%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $137 (12%) lower, and is roughly $60 (6%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
