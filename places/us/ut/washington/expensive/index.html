Home prices

The median home in Washington is valued at $291,900. It is about $22,220 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $74,400 (34%) higher, and is roughly $5,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of St. George.

Rents

Washington's median rent is $1,169 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $78 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $107 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of St. George is about $177 (17%) higher.
