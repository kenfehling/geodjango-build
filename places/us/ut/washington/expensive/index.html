Home prices

The median home in Washington is valued at $291,900. It is approximately $22,220 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $74,400 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $5,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of St. George.

Rents

In Washington the median monthly rent is $1,169. This is roughly $78 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $107 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of St. George it is approximately $177 (17%) higher.
