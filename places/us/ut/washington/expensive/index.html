Home prices

The cost of the median home in Washington is $291,900. It is approximately $22,220 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $74,400 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. George is roughly $5,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Washington the median monthly rent is $1,169. This is approximately $78 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $107 (10%) higher, and it is approximately $177 (17%) higher compared to the city of St. George.
