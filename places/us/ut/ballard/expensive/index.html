In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ballard is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Vernal.

Home prices

The median home in Ballard costs $163,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $9,520 (5%) lower. It is roughly $53,800 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Vernal it is approximately $2,700 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Ballard the median monthly rent is $1,109. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $269 (32%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $47 (4%) higher, and is roughly $377 (51%) higher compared to the city of Vernal.
