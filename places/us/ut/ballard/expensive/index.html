Home prices

The median home cost in Ballard is $163,700. It is about $9,520 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $53,800 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $2,700 (1%) lower compared to the city of Vernal.

Rents

In Ballard the median monthly rent is $1,109. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $269 (32%) higher.It is about $47 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Vernal is approximately $377 (51%) higher.
