Home prices

Ballard's median home cost is $163,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $9,520 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $53,800 (24%) lower, and is approximately $2,700 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Vernal.

Rents

The median rent in Ballard is $1,109 a month. It is about $269 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $47 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Vernal is approximately $377 (51%) higher.
