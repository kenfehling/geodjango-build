Compared to nearby areas, Syracuse is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Syracuse is $316,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $92,340 (41%) higher.It is about $98,700 (45%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is is very similar.

Rents

In Syracuse the median rent is $1,514 a month. This is roughly $346 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $452 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $529 (53%) higher.
