Compared to nearby areas, Syracuse is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Syracuse is valued at $316,200. This is roughly $92,340 (41%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $98,700 (45%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is is very similar.

Rents

In Syracuse the median monthly rent is $1,514. This is approximately $346 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $452 (42%) higher, and it is roughly $529 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
