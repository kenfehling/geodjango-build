In comparison to other places in this area, Syracuse is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Syracuse is valued at $316,200. It is about $92,340 (41%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $98,700 (45%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Syracuse is $1,514 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $346 (29%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $452 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $529 (53%) higher.
