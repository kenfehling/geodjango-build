Home prices

The median home in Riverdale is valued at $206,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $2,500 (1%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $10,600 (4%) lower, and it is roughly $107,600 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverdale is $854. This is approximately $119 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $208 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $131 (13%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
