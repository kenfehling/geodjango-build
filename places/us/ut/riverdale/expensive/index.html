Home prices

The median home in Riverdale is valued at $206,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $2,500 (1%) lower. It is about $10,600 (4%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $107,600 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverdale is $854. This is approximately $119 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $208 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $131 (13%) lower.
