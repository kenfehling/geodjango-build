Home prices

Rents

Ogden's median monthly rent is $818. It is approximately $130 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $244 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $167 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
