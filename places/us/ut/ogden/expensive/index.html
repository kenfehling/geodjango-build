Compared to its nearby areas, Ogden is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Ogden is $170,100. This is about $55,660 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $47,400 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $144,400 (45%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Ogden the median rent is $818 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $130 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $244 (22%) lower, and is roughly $167 (16%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
