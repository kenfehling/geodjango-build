In comparison to other places in this area, Ogden is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Ogden is $170,100. This is approximately $55,660 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $47,400 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $144,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Ogden the median monthly rent is $818. This is approximately $130 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $244 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $167 (16%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
