Compared to its surrounding areas, Ogden is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ogden costs $170,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $55,660 (24%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $47,400 (21%) lower, and is roughly $144,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Ogden's median monthly rent is $818. This is roughly $130 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $244 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $167 (16%) lower.
