Rents

Marriott-Slaterville's median rent is $972 per month. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $90 (8%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $13 (1%) lower.
