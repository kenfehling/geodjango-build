In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Provo, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cleveland is $164,300. Compared to nearby areas it is about $34,860 (26%) higher.It is roughly $53,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Provo it is roughly $107,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $700 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $66 (10%) higher.It is approximately $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $177 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.
