Home prices

The median home value in Cleveland is $164,300. This is roughly $34,860 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $53,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $107,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $700 per month. It is approximately $66 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $177 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.
