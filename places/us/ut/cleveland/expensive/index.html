Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $700 per month. This is about $66 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $362 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $177 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.
