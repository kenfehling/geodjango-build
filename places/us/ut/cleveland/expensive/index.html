Home prices

The median home price in Cleveland is $164,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $34,860 (26%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $53,200 (24%) lower, and is approximately $107,000 (39%) lower compared to the city of Provo.

Rents

Cleveland's median monthly rent is $700. This is about $66 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and it is about $177 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.
