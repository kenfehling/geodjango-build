In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Provo, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Cleveland's median home cost is $164,300. This is approximately $34,860 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $53,200 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Provo it is about $107,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $700 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $66 (10%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Provo it is approximately $177 (20%) lower.
