Home prices

The median home in Clarkston is valued at $159,900. This is approximately $32,780 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $57,600 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $154,600 (49%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Clarkston's median rent is $917 a month. This is about $115 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $145 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $68 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
