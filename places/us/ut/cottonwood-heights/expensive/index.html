Home prices

The median home in Cottonwood Heights costs $382,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $63,580 (19%) higher. It is roughly $165,400 (76%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $68,400 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Cottonwood Heights the median rent is $1,230 a month. This is roughly $31 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $168 (15%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $245 (24%) higher.
