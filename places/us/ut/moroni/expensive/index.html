Home prices

The median home in Moroni costs $162,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $21,920 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $54,800 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $151,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

Moroni's median rent is $658 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $179 (21%) lower. It is roughly $404 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $327 (33%) lower.
