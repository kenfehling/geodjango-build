Compared to the surrounding areas, Moroni is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Moroni costs $162,700. This is about $21,920 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $54,800 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $151,800 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Moroni the median monthly rent is $658. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $179 (21%) lower. It is roughly $404 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $327 (33%) lower.
