Home prices

The median home in Provo is valued at $271,300. It is about $83,960 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $53,800 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $43,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

Provo's median monthly rent is $877. This is about $349 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $185 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $108 (10%) lower.
