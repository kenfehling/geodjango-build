Home prices

The cost of the median home in Provo is $271,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $83,960 (23%) lower. It is about $53,800 (24%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $43,200 (13%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Provo is $877 a month. It is approximately $349 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $185 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
