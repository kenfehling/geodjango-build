Much like other places in this area, it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Provo.

Home prices

The median home price in Gunnison is $172,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $8,060 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $44,900 (20%) lower, and is about $98,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.

Rents

Gunnison's median monthly rent is $647. This is approximately $156 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $415 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Provo it is roughly $230 (26%) lower.
