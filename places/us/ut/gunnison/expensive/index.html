Home prices

The median home price in Gunnison is $172,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $8,060 (4%) higher.It is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $98,700 (36%) lower compared to the city of Provo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gunnison is $647. This is about $156 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $415 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Provo it is approximately $230 (26%) lower.
