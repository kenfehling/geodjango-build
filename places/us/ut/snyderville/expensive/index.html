Home prices

The median home in Snyderville costs $777,800. This is about $121,420 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $560,300 (257%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $463,300 (147%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Snyderville is $1,481. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar. Compared to the entire country it is about $419 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $496 (50%) higher.
