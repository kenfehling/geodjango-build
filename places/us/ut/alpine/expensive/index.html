Home prices

The median home in Alpine is valued at $569,300. This is about $161,340 (39%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $351,800 (161%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $254,800 (81%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alpine is $1,740. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $238 (15%) higher.It is about $678 (63%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $755 (76%) higher.
