Home prices

The median home in Alpine costs $569,300. It is roughly $161,340 (39%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $351,800 (161%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $254,800 (81%) higher.

Rents

Alpine's median monthly rent is $1,740. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $238 (15%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $678 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $755 (76%) higher.
