Home prices

The value of the median home in Copperton is $189,800. It is about $133,460 (41%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $27,700 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $124,700 (39%) lower.

Rents
