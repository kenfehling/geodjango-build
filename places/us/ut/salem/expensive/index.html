Home prices

The median home price in Salem is $331,900. This is roughly $14,580 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $114,400 (52%) higher, and is approximately $17,400 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Salem the median monthly rent is $1,250. It is approximately $21 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $188 (17%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $265 (26%) higher.
