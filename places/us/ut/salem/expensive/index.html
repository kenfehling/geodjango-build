Home prices

The median home in Salem costs $331,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $14,580 (4%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $114,400 (52%) higher, and it is approximately $17,400 (5%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Salem is $1,250 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $21 (1%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $188 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $265 (26%) higher.
