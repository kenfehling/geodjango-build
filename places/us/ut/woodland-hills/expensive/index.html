Compared to other places in this area, Woodland Hills is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Woodland Hills costs $485,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $169,500 (53%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $267,800 (123%) higher, and it is approximately $170,800 (54%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Woodland Hills's median monthly rent is $1,875. This is roughly $729 (63%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $813 (76%) higher, and is roughly $890 (90%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
