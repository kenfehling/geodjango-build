Home prices

The value of the median home in West Haven is $301,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $91,120 (43%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $84,400 (38%) higher, and is approximately $12,600 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

West Haven's median rent is $1,161 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $256 (28%) higher.It is about $99 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $176 (17%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
