In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Haven is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home cost in West Haven is $301,900. This is about $91,120 (43%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $84,400 (38%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $12,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

In West Haven the median rent is $1,161 per month. It is about $256 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $99 (9%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $176 (17%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
