Home prices

The median home in South Ogden costs $213,800. It is roughly $18,520 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $3,700 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $100,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

South Ogden's median rent is $963 per month. This is approximately $29 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $99 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $22 (2%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
