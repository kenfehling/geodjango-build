Compared to other places in this area, Aurora is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Springville.

Home prices

Aurora's median home cost is $171,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $18,960 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $45,600 (20%) lower, and is roughly $88,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Springville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Aurora is $950. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $146 (18%) higher. It is about $112 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $141 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Springville.
