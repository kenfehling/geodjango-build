Rents

The median rent in Bluffdale is $1,422 per month. This is approximately $29 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $360 (33%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $437 (44%) higher.
