Home prices

The price of the median home in Bluffdale is $485,400. It is roughly $94,960 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $267,900 (123%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $170,900 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bluffdale is $1,422 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $29 (1%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $360 (33%) higher, and it is approximately $437 (44%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
