Home prices

The median home in Tremonton costs $191,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $34,460 (15%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $25,700 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $122,700 (39%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Tremonton the median rent is $730 a month. This is about $80 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $332 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $255 (25%) lower.
