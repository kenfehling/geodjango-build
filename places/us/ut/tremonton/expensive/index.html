Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tremonton is $191,800. It is approximately $34,460 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $25,700 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $122,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Tremonton the median rent is $730 per month. This is approximately $80 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $332 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $255 (25%) lower.
