Compared to its surrounding areas, Fruit Heights is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fruit Heights costs $396,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $66,820 (20%) higher. It is approximately $178,500 (82%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $81,500 (25%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fruit Heights is $1,669. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $540 (47%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $607 (57%) higher, and it is roughly $684 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
