The median home in Fruit Heights costs $396,000. This is roughly $66,820 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $178,500 (82%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $81,500 (25%) higher.

Rents
