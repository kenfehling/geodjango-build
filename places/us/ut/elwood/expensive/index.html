In comparison to its surrounding areas, Elwood is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Elwood costs $268,500. It is approximately $59,920 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $51,000 (23%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $46,000 (14%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Elwood the median rent is $1,200 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $384 (47%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $138 (12%) higher, and it is roughly $215 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
