Compared to its surrounding areas, Elwood is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Elwood is $268,500. This is approximately $59,920 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $51,000 (23%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $46,000 (14%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Elwood is $1,200 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $384 (47%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $138 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $215 (21%) higher.
