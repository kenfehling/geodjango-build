In comparison to other places in this area, Elwood is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Elwood is $268,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $59,920 (28%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $51,000 (23%) higher, and it is about $46,000 (14%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Elwood the median monthly rent is $1,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $384 (47%) higher. It is about $138 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $215 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
