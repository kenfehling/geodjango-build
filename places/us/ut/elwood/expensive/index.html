Home prices

The median home in Elwood is valued at $268,500. It is about $59,920 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $51,000 (23%) higher, and is approximately $46,000 (14%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Elwood's median rent is $1,200 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $384 (47%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $138 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $215 (21%) higher.
