Rents

The median monthly rent in Lewiston is $850. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $29 (3%) lower.It is approximately $212 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $135 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
