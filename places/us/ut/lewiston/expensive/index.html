Home prices

Lewiston's median home cost is $185,900. This is about $3,180 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $31,600 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $128,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lewiston is $850. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $29 (3%) lower. It is about $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $135 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
