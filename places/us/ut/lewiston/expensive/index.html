Home prices

The median home in Lewiston is valued at $185,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $3,180 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $31,600 (14%) lower, and is roughly $128,600 (40%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Lewiston is $850 a month. This is approximately $29 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $135 (13%) lower.
