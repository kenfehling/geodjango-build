Home prices

The median home in Price is valued at $144,700. This is approximately $12,400 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $72,800 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Provo it is roughly $126,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

Price's median rent is $668 per month. It is about $21 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $394 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Provo it is about $209 (23%) lower.
