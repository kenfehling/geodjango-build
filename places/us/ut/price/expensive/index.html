Home prices

The median home value in Price is $144,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $12,400 (9%) higher. It is roughly $72,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $126,600 (46%) lower compared to the city of Provo.

Rents

Price's median monthly rent is $668. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $21 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $394 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Provo is approximately $209 (23%) lower.
