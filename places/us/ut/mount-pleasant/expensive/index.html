Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant is valued at $167,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $16,760 (9%) lower.It is roughly $50,500 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $147,500 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Pleasant is $914. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $128 (16%) higher.It is approximately $148 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $71 (7%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
