Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant is valued at $167,000. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $16,760 (9%) lower. It is about $50,500 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $147,500 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median monthly rent is $914. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $128 (16%) higher. It is approximately $148 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $71 (7%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
