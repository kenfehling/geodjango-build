Home prices

The median home price in Mount Pleasant is $167,000. It is roughly $16,760 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $50,500 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $147,500 (46%) lower.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median rent is $914 a month. It is about $128 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $148 (13%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $71 (7%) lower.
