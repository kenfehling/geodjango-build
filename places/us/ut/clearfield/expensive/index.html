Home prices

Clearfield's median home price is $194,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $53,940 (21%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $23,200 (10%) lower, and it is about $120,200 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Clearfield the median monthly rent is $1,098. It is about $153 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $36 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $113 (11%) higher.
