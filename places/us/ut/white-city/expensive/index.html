In comparison to the surrounding areas, White City is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in White City costs $248,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $126,340 (33%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $31,200 (14%) higher, and it is about $65,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in White City is $1,316. It is approximately $19 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $254 (23%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $331 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
