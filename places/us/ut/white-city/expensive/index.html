Compared to the surrounding areas, White City is a lot less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in White City costs $248,700. This is roughly $126,340 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $31,200 (14%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $65,800 (20%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in White City is $1,316. This is roughly $19 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $254 (23%) higher, and is about $331 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
