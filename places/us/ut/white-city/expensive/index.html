In comparison to its surrounding areas, White City is substantially less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home price in White City is $248,700. It is roughly $126,340 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $31,200 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $65,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in White City is $1,316. This is roughly $19 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $254 (23%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $331 (33%) higher.
