Home prices

The median home in Clinton is valued at $243,400. It is roughly $18,700 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $25,900 (11%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $71,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clinton is $1,348. It is approximately $139 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $286 (26%) higher, and it is about $363 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
