Compared to its surrounding areas, Clinton is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Clinton is $243,400. This is approximately $18,700 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $25,900 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $71,100 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Clinton the median rent is $1,348 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $139 (11%) higher. It is approximately $286 (26%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $363 (36%) higher.
