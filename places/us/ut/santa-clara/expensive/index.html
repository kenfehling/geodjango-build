Similar to other places in this area, it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. George, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Santa Clara's median home price is $315,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $5,500 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $97,500 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. George is roughly $29,000 (10%) higher.

Rents

Santa Clara's median rent is $1,445 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $409 (39%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $383 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of St. George is about $453 (45%) higher.
