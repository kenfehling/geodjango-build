Home prices

The median home in Santa Clara costs $315,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $5,500 (1%) higher.It is roughly $97,500 (44%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $29,000 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of St. George.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Clara is $1,445. It is approximately $409 (39%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $383 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. George it is approximately $453 (45%) higher.
