Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Highland is $1,655. It is approximately $192 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $593 (55%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $670 (68%) higher.
