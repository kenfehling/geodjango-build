Home prices

The value of the median home in Huntsville is $366,700. This is approximately $54,220 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $149,200 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $52,200 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Huntsville the median monthly rent is $1,250. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $202 (13%) lower. It is approximately $188 (17%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $265 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
