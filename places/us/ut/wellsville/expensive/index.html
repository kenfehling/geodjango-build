Home prices

The median home price in Wellsville is $258,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $4,940 (1%) higher. It is roughly $40,900 (18%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $56,100 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wellsville is $930. It is about $99 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $132 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $55 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
