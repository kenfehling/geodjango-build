Home prices

Rents

Wellsville's median monthly rent is $930. This is roughly $99 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $132 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $55 (5%) lower.
