Home prices

The median home cost in Wellsville is $258,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $4,940 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $40,900 (18%) higher, and it is about $56,100 (17%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Wellsville is $930 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $99 (11%) higher. It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $55 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
