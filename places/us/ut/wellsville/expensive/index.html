Rents

In Wellsville the median rent is $930 per month. It is approximately $99 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $132 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $55 (5%) lower.
