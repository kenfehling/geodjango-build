Home prices

The value of the median home in Murray is $287,100. This is roughly $20,060 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $69,600 (32%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $27,400 (8%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Murray's median monthly rent is $1,109. This is roughly $39 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $47 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $124 (12%) higher.
