Home prices

The median home in Murray costs $287,100. This is roughly $20,060 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $69,600 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $27,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Murray the median monthly rent is $1,109. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $39 (3%) higher. It is approximately $47 (4%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $124 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
