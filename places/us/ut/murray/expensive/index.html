Home prices

Rents

Murray's median monthly rent is $1,109. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $39 (3%) higher.It is roughly $47 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $124 (12%) higher.
