Home prices

Rents

Murray's median monthly rent is $1,109. This is roughly $39 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $47 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $124 (12%) higher.
