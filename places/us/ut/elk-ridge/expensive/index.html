Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elk Ridge is $1,536. This is approximately $252 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $474 (44%) higher, and it is roughly $551 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
