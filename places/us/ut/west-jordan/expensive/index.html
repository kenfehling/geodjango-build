Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Jordan is $1,234. It is roughly $37 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $172 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $249 (25%) higher.
