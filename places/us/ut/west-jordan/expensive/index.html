In comparison to other places in this area, West Jordan is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in West Jordan is valued at $284,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $24,600 (9%) higher. It is roughly $66,700 (30%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $30,300 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in West Jordan is $1,234 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $37 (3%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $172 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $249 (25%) higher.
