Home prices

The value of the median home in West Jordan is $284,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24,600 (9%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $66,700 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $30,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in West Jordan is $1,234 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $37 (3%) higher.It is roughly $172 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $249 (25%) higher.
