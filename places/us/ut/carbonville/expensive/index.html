Home prices

The median home cost in Carbonville is $142,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $9,160 (6%) higher. It is approximately $75,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Provo it is about $129,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

Carbonville's median rent is $671 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $25 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $391 (36%) lower, and is roughly $206 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.
