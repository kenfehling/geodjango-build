Compared to the surrounding areas, Payson is significantly less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Payson is $231,000. It is approximately $176,540 (43%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $13,500 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $83,500 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Payson is $1,017. It is about $331 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $45 (4%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $32 (3%) higher.
