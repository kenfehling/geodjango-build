In comparison to the surrounding areas, Payson is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Payson is $231,000. It is roughly $176,540 (43%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $13,500 (6%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $83,500 (26%) lower.

Rents

Payson's median rent is $1,017 per month. It is about $331 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $45 (4%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $32 (3%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
