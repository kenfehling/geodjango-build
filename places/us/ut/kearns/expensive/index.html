Home prices

Kearns's median home value is $202,000. It is approximately $56,320 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $15,500 (7%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $112,500 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kearns is $1,309 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $181 (16%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $247 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $324 (32%) higher.
