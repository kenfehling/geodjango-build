Compared to its surrounding areas, Midway is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Midway is $448,800. This is about $229,640 (33%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $231,300 (106%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $134,300 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Midway the median monthly rent is $2,298. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $1,136 (97%) higher. It is approximately $1,236 (116%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $1,313 (133%) higher.
