Home prices

The median home in Midway is valued at $448,800. It is about $229,640 (33%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $231,300 (106%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $134,300 (42%) higher.

Rents

In Midway the median rent is $2,298 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $1,136 (97%) higher. It is approximately $1,236 (116%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $1,313 (133%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
