Compared to other places in this area, Plain City is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Plain City costs $301,500. This is roughly $18,420 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $84,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $13,000 (4%) lower.

Rents

Plain City's median rent is $736 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $293 (28%) lower. It is roughly $326 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $249 (25%) lower.
