Compared to its nearby areas, Plain City is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Plain City costs $301,500. This is roughly $18,420 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $84,000 (38%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $13,000 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Plain City is $736 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $293 (28%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $249 (25%) lower.
