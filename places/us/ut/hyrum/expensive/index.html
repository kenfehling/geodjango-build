In comparison to other places in this area, Hyrum is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home price in Hyrum is $190,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $76,500 (28%) lower. It is approximately $27,300 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $124,300 (39%) lower.

Rents

Hyrum's median rent is $853 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $35 (3%) lower. It is about $209 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $132 (13%) lower.
