Home prices

The median home in Mayfield costs $169,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $4,220 (2%) higher. It is approximately $48,100 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Provo is about $101,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mayfield is $1,022. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $294 (40%) higher. It is about $40 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Provo is approximately $145 (16%) higher.
