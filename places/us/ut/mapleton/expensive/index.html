In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mapleton is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mapleton is $429,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $82,460 (23%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $212,400 (97%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $115,400 (36%) higher.

Rents

In Mapleton the median monthly rent is $1,164. It is roughly $64 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $102 (9%) higher, and is about $179 (18%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
