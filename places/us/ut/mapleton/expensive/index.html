In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mapleton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Mapleton is $429,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $82,460 (23%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $212,400 (97%) higher, and is approximately $115,400 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Mapleton the median monthly rent is $1,164. This is roughly $64 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $102 (9%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $179 (18%) higher.
