Compared to nearby areas, Mapleton is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Mapleton's median home value is $429,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $82,460 (23%) higher. It is approximately $212,400 (97%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $115,400 (36%) higher.

Rents

Mapleton's median rent is $1,164 a month. It is approximately $64 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $102 (9%) higher, and is about $179 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
