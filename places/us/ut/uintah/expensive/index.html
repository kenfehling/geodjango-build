Home prices

The median home value in Uintah is $274,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $54,400 (24%) higher.It is about $56,500 (25%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $40,500 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Uintah the median monthly rent is $1,125. This is approximately $158 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $63 (5%) higher, and is roughly $140 (14%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
