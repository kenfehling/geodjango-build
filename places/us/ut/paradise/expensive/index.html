Compared to its surrounding areas, Paradise is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Paradise costs $298,800. This is roughly $53,100 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $81,300 (37%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $15,700 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Paradise is $825 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $57 (6%) lower. It is about $237 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $160 (16%) lower.
