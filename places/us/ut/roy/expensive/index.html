Home prices

The median home cost in Roy is $191,800. It is roughly $26,540 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $25,700 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $122,700 (39%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Roy is $1,061 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $53 (5%) higher. It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $76 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
