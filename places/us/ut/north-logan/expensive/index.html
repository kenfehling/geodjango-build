Home prices

The median home in North Logan costs $274,900. It is about $23,400 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $57,400 (26%) higher, and is roughly $39,600 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In North Logan the median monthly rent is $1,092. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $229 (26%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $30 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $107 (10%) higher.
