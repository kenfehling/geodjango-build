Compared to the surrounding areas, North Logan is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The value of the median home in North Logan is $274,900. It is roughly $23,400 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $57,400 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $39,600 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Logan is $1,092. This is about $229 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $30 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $107 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
