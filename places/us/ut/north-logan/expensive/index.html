Home prices

The median home in North Logan costs $274,900. It is roughly $23,400 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $57,400 (26%) higher, and is roughly $39,600 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

North Logan's median monthly rent is $1,092. It is approximately $229 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $30 (2%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $107 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
