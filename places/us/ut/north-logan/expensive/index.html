Home prices

The median home in North Logan costs $274,900. It is about $23,400 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $57,400 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $39,600 (12%) lower.

Rents

North Logan's median rent is $1,092 a month. It is roughly $229 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $30 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $107 (10%) higher.
