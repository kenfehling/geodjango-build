Compared to nearby areas, North Logan is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in North Logan is $274,900. This is approximately $23,400 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $57,400 (26%) higher, and is approximately $39,600 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Logan is $1,092. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $229 (26%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $30 (2%) higher, and it is roughly $107 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
