Compared to its surrounding areas, North Logan is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Logan is valued at $274,900. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $23,400 (9%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $57,400 (26%) higher, and is approximately $39,600 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In North Logan the median rent is $1,092 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $229 (26%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $30 (2%) higher, and it is approximately $107 (10%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
