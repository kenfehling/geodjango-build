Home prices

The price of the median home in Richmond is $212,100. This is roughly $10,820 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $5,400 (2%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $102,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Richmond's median rent is $797 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $21 (2%) higher.It is approximately $265 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $188 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
