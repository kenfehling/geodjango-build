Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $212,100. It is roughly $10,820 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $5,400 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $102,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

Richmond's median monthly rent is $797. This is approximately $21 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $265 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $188 (19%) lower.
