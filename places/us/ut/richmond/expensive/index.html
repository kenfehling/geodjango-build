Home prices

The median home price in Richmond is $212,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $10,820 (5%) higher. It is approximately $5,400 (2%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $102,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond is $797 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $21 (2%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $265 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $188 (19%) lower.
