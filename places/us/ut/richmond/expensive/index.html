Home prices

The price of the median home in Richmond is $212,100. This is roughly $10,820 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $5,400 (2%) lower, and it is approximately $102,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Richmond the median rent is $797 per month. This is about $21 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $265 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $188 (19%) lower.
