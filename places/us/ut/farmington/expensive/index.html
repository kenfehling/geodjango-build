Home prices

Rents

Farmington's median rent is $1,178 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $56 (4%) lower.It is roughly $116 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $193 (19%) higher.
