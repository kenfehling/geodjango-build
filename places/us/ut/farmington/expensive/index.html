Home prices

The median home in Farmington is valued at $386,500. This is about $60,080 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $169,000 (77%) higher, and it is approximately $72,000 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Farmington's median rent is $1,178 per month. This is roughly $56 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $116 (10%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $193 (19%) higher.
