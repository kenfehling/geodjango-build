In comparison to other places in this area, Farmington is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Farmington is $386,500. This is about $60,080 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $169,000 (77%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $72,000 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Farmington the median monthly rent is $1,178. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $56 (4%) lower. It is approximately $116 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $193 (19%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
