Home prices

The median home price in Farmington is $386,500. It is about $60,080 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $169,000 (77%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $72,000 (22%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Farmington the median rent is $1,178 per month. This is roughly $56 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $116 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $193 (19%) higher.
