Home prices

The price of the median home in Holladay is $442,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $138,200 (45%) higher. It is about $225,400 (103%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $128,400 (40%) higher.

Rents

Holladay's median monthly rent is $1,148. Compared to nearby areas it is about $51 (4%) higher. It is approximately $86 (8%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $163 (16%) higher.
