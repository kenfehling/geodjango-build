Home prices

Rents

Holladay's median rent is $1,148 a month. This is about $51 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $86 (8%) higher, and is about $163 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
