Compared to the surrounding areas, Riverton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Riverton is $349,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $47,120 (11%) lower. It is approximately $132,100 (60%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $35,100 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Riverton's median monthly rent is $1,584. It is about $198 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $522 (49%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $599 (60%) higher.
