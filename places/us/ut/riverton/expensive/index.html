Home prices

Riverton's median home cost is $349,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $47,120 (11%) lower.It is approximately $132,100 (60%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $35,100 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Riverton's median rent is $1,584 a month. This is approximately $198 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $522 (49%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $599 (60%) higher.
