In comparison to the surrounding areas, Riverton is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Riverton is $349,600. This is about $47,120 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $132,100 (60%) higher, and is about $35,100 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverton is $1,584. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $198 (14%) higher. It is approximately $522 (49%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $599 (60%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
