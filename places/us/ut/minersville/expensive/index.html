Home prices

Rents

Minersville's median rent is $850 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $80 (10%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $212 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $142 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of St. George.
