Home prices

The median home value in Logan is $198,500. This is approximately $61,460 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $19,000 (8%) lower, and it is roughly $116,000 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Logan is $764 a month. This is roughly $64 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $298 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $221 (22%) lower.
