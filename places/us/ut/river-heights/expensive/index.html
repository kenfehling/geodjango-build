Home prices

The median home in River Heights is valued at $257,600. This is roughly $7,660 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $40,100 (18%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $56,900 (18%) lower.

Rents

River Heights's median rent is $836 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $72 (7%) lower. It is approximately $226 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $149 (15%) lower.
