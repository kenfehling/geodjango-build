In comparison to the surrounding areas, Providence is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Providence's median home cost is $281,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $36,820 (15%) higher.It is about $64,400 (29%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $32,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Providence the median rent is $982 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $103 (11%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $80 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is is very similar.
