In comparison to its surrounding areas, Providence is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Providence costs $281,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $36,820 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $64,400 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $32,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Providence is $982. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $103 (11%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $80 (7%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
