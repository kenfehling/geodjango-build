In comparison to its nearby areas, Providence is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Providence is $281,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $36,820 (15%) higher. It is approximately $64,400 (29%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $32,600 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Providence the median monthly rent is $982. This is about $103 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $80 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
