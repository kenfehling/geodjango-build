Compared to its surrounding areas, Providence is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Providence is valued at $281,900. It is about $36,820 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $64,400 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $32,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Providence the median monthly rent is $982. This is roughly $103 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $80 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
