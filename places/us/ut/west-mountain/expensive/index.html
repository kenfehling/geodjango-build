Home prices

Rents

In West Mountain the median monthly rent is $1,281. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $161 (14%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $219 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $296 (30%) higher.
