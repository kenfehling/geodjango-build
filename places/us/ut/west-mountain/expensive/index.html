Home prices

Rents

In West Mountain the median rent is $1,281 a month. It is roughly $161 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $219 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $296 (30%) higher.
