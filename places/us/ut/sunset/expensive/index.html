Compared to other places in this area, Sunset is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Sunset is valued at $166,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $35,140 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $50,800 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $147,800 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Sunset the median monthly rent is $858. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $178 (17%) lower. It is roughly $204 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $127 (12%) lower.
