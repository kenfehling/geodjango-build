Home prices

The value of the median home in Sunset is $166,700. It is approximately $35,140 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $50,800 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $147,800 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Sunset the median monthly rent is $858. It is approximately $178 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $204 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $127 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
