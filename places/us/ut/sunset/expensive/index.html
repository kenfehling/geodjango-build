Compared to its surrounding areas, Sunset is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sunset is valued at $166,700. This is roughly $35,140 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $50,800 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $147,800 (46%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Sunset's median monthly rent is $858. This is about $178 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $204 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $127 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
