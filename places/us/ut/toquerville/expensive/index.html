Compared to other places in this area, Toquerville is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. George, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Toquerville costs $314,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $37,800 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $97,000 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. George is about $28,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Toquerville is $1,225 a month. This is about $273 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $163 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. George is roughly $233 (23%) higher.
