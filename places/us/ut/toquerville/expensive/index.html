Home prices

The median home value in Toquerville is $314,500. This is roughly $37,800 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $97,000 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of St. George it is roughly $28,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Toquerville is $1,225. This is approximately $273 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $163 (15%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $233 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of St. George.
