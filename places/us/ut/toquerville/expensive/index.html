Home prices

The value of the median home in Toquerville is $314,500. It is approximately $37,800 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $97,000 (44%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $28,500 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of St. George.

Rents

In Toquerville the median monthly rent is $1,225. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $273 (28%) higher. It is about $163 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. George it is about $233 (23%) higher.
