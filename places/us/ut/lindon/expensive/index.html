Home prices

Rents

Lindon's median rent is $1,367 per month. This is approximately $46 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $305 (28%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $382 (38%) higher.
