Compared to other places in this area, Heber is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Heber is valued at $348,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $119,340 (25%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $130,600 (60%) higher, and it is roughly $33,600 (10%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Heber is $1,241 a month. This is roughly $175 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $179 (16%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $256 (25%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
