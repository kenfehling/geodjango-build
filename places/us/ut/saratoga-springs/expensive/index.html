Compared to its nearby areas, Saratoga Springs is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Saratoga Springs is $340,700. This is approximately $37,940 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $123,200 (56%) higher, and it is about $26,200 (8%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Saratoga Springs's median rent is $1,485 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $423 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $500 (50%) higher.
