Home prices

The median home in Saratoga Springs is valued at $340,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $37,940 (10%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $123,200 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $26,200 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Saratoga Springs the median rent is $1,485 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $423 (39%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $500 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
