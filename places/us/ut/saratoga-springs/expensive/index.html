Home prices

The median home in Saratoga Springs is valued at $340,700. Compared to nearby areas this is about $37,940 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $123,200 (56%) higher, and it is approximately $26,200 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Saratoga Springs's median monthly rent is $1,485. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $423 (39%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $500 (50%) higher.
