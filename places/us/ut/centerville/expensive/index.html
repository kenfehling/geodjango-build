Much like other places in this area, it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. It is close to average compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Centerville is valued at $321,700. This is about $3,860 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $104,200 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $7,200 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Centerville the median monthly rent is $1,157. Compared to nearby areas this is about $48 (3%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $95 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $172 (17%) higher.
