Home prices

The median home value in Centerville is $321,700. This is about $3,860 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $104,200 (47%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $7,200 (2%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Centerville the median monthly rent is $1,157. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $48 (3%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $95 (8%) higher, and it is roughly $172 (17%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
