Compared to other places in this area, Manti is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Provo, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manti is valued at $168,400. This is approximately $13,580 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $49,100 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Provo it is roughly $102,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

Manti's median rent is $754 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $11 (1%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Provo is approximately $123 (14%) lower.
