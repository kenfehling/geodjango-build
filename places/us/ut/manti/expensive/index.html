Compared to the surrounding areas, Manti is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Provo, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Manti's median home value is $168,400. It is about $13,580 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $49,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $102,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of Provo.

Rents

In Manti the median rent is $754 per month. It is about $11 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $308 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Provo is roughly $123 (14%) lower.
