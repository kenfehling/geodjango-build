In comparison to its surrounding areas, Manti is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Provo.

Home prices

The median home in Manti is valued at $168,400. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $13,580 (7%) lower. It is about $49,100 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $102,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of Provo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Manti is $754. It is roughly $11 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $308 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Provo is about $123 (14%) lower.
