In comparison to other places in this area, Manti is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Provo however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manti costs $168,400. This is approximately $13,580 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $49,100 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $102,900 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Provo.

Rents

In Manti the median monthly rent is $754. It is roughly $11 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $123 (14%) lower compared to the city of Provo.
