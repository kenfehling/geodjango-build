Home prices

The median home cost in Summit Park is $705,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $107,940 (13%) lower.It is about $488,300 (224%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $391,300 (124%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Summit Park is $1,627. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $565 (53%) higher, and it is about $642 (65%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
