Home prices

The median home in Summit Park costs $705,800. This is about $107,940 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $488,300 (224%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $391,300 (124%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Summit Park the median monthly rent is $1,627. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $565 (53%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $642 (65%) higher.
