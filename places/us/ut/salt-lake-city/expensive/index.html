In comparison to nearby areas, Salt Lake City is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Salt Lake City costs $314,500. It is approximately $31,200 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $97,000 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Salt Lake City is $985 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $119 (10%) lower.It is about $77 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country.
