Compared to the surrounding areas, Salt Lake City is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home price in Salt Lake City is $314,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $31,200 (11%) higher. It is approximately $97,000 (44%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole.

Rents

Salt Lake City's median monthly rent is $985. This is about $119 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $77 (7%) lower.
