In comparison to its surrounding areas, Salt Lake City is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

The median home value in Salt Lake City is $314,500. This is roughly $31,200 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $97,000 (44%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole.

Rents

In Salt Lake City the median rent is $985 a month. This is approximately $119 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $77 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole.
