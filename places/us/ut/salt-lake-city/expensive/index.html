In comparison to other places in this area, Salt Lake City is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Salt Lake City is $314,500. It is about $31,200 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $97,000 (44%) higher.

Rents

Salt Lake City's median monthly rent is $985. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $119 (10%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $77 (7%) lower.
