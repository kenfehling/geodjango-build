In comparison to its nearby areas, North Salt Lake is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in North Salt Lake is valued at $303,200. It is roughly $80,440 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $85,700 (39%) higher, and it is approximately $11,300 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Salt Lake is $1,158. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $103 (8%) lower.It is about $96 (9%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $173 (17%) higher.
