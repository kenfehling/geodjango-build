In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Salt Lake is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Salt Lake Cityhowever, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in North Salt Lake is $303,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $80,440 (20%) lower. It is roughly $85,700 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $11,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

North Salt Lake's median rent is $1,158 a month. This is roughly $103 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $96 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $173 (17%) higher.
