Compared to its nearby areas, North Salt Lake is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home price in North Salt Lake is $303,200. This is approximately $80,440 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $85,700 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $11,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

In North Salt Lake the median rent is $1,158 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $103 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $96 (9%) higher, and is approximately $173 (17%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
