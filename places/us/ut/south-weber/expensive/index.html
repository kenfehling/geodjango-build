Home prices

The median home in South Weber costs $337,800. This is about $112,220 (49%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $120,300 (55%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $23,300 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Weber is $1,341. This is approximately $384 (40%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $279 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $356 (36%) higher.
