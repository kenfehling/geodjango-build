Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Weber is $1,341. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $384 (40%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $279 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $356 (36%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
