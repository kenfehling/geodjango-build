Compared to other places in this area, South Weber is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Weber is $337,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $112,220 (49%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $120,300 (55%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $23,300 (7%) higher.

Rents

In South Weber the median monthly rent is $1,341. This is approximately $384 (40%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $279 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $356 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
