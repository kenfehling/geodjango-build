Home prices

Trenton's median home value is $196,600. This is approximately $3,660 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $20,900 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $117,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Trenton's median monthly rent is $621. This is roughly $260 (29%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $441 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $364 (36%) lower.
