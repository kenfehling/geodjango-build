Home prices

Trenton's median home price is $196,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $3,660 (1%) lower. It is roughly $20,900 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $117,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $621 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $260 (29%) lower. It is roughly $441 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $364 (36%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
