Home prices

The median home in Trenton costs $196,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $3,660 (1%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $20,900 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $117,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Trenton is $621. This is approximately $260 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $441 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $364 (36%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
