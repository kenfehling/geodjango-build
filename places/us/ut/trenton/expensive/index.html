Home prices

The value of the median home in Trenton is $196,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $3,660 (1%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $20,900 (9%) lower, and it is about $117,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Trenton's median monthly rent is $621. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $260 (29%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $441 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $364 (36%) lower.
