Compared to nearby areas, Lehi is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lehi costs $353,100. It is approximately $55,660 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $135,600 (62%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $38,600 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lehi is $1,478 a month. This is roughly $26 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $416 (39%) higher, and it is roughly $493 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
