In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lehi is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Lehi's median home cost is $353,100. This is about $55,660 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $135,600 (62%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $38,600 (12%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Lehi is $1,478 a month. This is about $26 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $416 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $493 (50%) higher.
