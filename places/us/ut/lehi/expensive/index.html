Compared to the surrounding areas, Lehi is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Salt Lake Cityhowever, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lehi is valued at $353,100. It is approximately $55,660 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $135,600 (62%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $38,600 (12%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Lehi the median rent is $1,478 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $26 (1%) lower. It is about $416 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $493 (50%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
