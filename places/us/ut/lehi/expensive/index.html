Compared to its surrounding areas, Lehi is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lehi is valued at $353,100. This is roughly $55,660 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $135,600 (62%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $38,600 (12%) higher.

Rents

Lehi's median rent is $1,478 per month. This is about $26 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $416 (39%) higher, and is about $493 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
