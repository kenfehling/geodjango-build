Home prices

The median home in Kaysville is valued at $339,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $28,820 (9%) higher. It is approximately $122,000 (56%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $25,000 (7%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kaysville is $948. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $348 (26%) lower. It is roughly $114 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $37 (3%) lower.
