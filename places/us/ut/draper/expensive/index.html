Rents

In Draper the median rent is $1,415 per month. It is about $58 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $353 (33%) higher, and it is approximately $430 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
