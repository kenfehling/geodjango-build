In comparison to the surrounding areas, Draper is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Draper costs $469,000. This is about $67,100 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $251,500 (115%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $154,500 (49%) higher.

Rents

Draper's median monthly rent is $1,415. It is approximately $58 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $353 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $430 (43%) higher.
