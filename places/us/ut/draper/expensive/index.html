Home prices

The median home in Draper costs $469,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $67,100 (16%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $251,500 (115%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $154,500 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Draper is $1,415. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $58 (3%) lower. It is approximately $353 (33%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $430 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
