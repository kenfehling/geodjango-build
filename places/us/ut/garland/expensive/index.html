Compared to the surrounding areas, Garland is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Garland is $163,100. It is roughly $68,900 (29%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $54,400 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $151,400 (48%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Garland is $743. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $65 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $319 (30%) lower, and is roughly $242 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
