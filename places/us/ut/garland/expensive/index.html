Compared to other places in this area, Garland is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Garland is $163,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $68,900 (29%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $54,400 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $151,400 (48%) lower.

Rents

Garland's median rent is $743 a month. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $319 (30%) lower, and it is about $242 (24%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
