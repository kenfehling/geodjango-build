In comparison to other places in this area, Garland is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Garland is valued at $163,100. This is approximately $68,900 (29%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $54,400 (25%) lower, and it is about $151,400 (48%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Garland the median rent is $743 a month. This is about $65 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $319 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $242 (24%) lower.
