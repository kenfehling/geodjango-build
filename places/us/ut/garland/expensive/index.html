Compared to other places in this area, Garland is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Garland is valued at $163,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $68,900 (29%) lower.It is approximately $54,400 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $151,400 (48%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Garland the median rent is $743 a month. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $319 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $242 (24%) lower.
