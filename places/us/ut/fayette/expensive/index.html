In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fayette is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Provo, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Fayette is $143,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $36,140 (20%) lower. It is approximately $73,700 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $127,500 (46%) lower compared to the city of Provo.

Rents

Fayette's median rent is $675 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $102 (13%) lower. It is roughly $387 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Provo it is about $202 (23%) lower.
