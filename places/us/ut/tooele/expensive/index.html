Home prices

The median home value in Tooele is $195,300. This is about $64,960 (24%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $22,200 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $119,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tooele is $1,031 a month. It is roughly $143 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $31 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $46 (4%) higher.
