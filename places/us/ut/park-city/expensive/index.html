In comparison to other places in this area, Park City is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Salt Lake City, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Park City's median home cost is $1,035,300. It is about $187,580 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $817,800 (376%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $720,800 (229%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Park City is $1,495. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $31 (2%) higher.It is about $433 (40%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $510 (51%) higher.
