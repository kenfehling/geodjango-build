Compared to its surrounding areas, Park City is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Salt Lake City, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Park City is $1,035,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $187,580 (22%) higher.It is about $817,800 (376%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $720,800 (229%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Park City's median rent is $1,495 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $31 (2%) higher.It is approximately $433 (40%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $510 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
