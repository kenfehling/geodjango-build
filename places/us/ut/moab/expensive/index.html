Home prices

Moab's median home cost is $237,700. It is roughly $71,300 (42%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $20,200 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Junction is is very similar.

Rents

Moab's median monthly rent is $829. This is approximately $23 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $233 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Junction it is approximately $106 (11%) lower.
