Compared to the surrounding areas, South Jordan is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Jordan costs $405,400. This is about $115,920 (40%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $187,900 (86%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $90,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in South Jordan is $1,418 per month. This is about $102 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $356 (33%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $433 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
