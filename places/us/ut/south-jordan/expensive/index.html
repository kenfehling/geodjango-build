Home prices

South Jordan's median home value is $405,400. It is approximately $115,920 (40%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $187,900 (86%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $90,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in South Jordan is $1,418 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $102 (7%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $356 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $433 (43%) higher.
