In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Jordan is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Jordan costs $405,400. This is approximately $115,920 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $187,900 (86%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $90,900 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in South Jordan is $1,418 a month. This is about $102 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $356 (33%) higher, and it is approximately $433 (43%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
