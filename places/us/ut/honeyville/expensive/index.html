Home prices

The median home in Honeyville costs $245,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $13,500 (5%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $28,200 (12%) higher, and it is roughly $68,800 (21%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Honeyville the median monthly rent is $695. It is roughly $260 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $367 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $290 (29%) lower.
