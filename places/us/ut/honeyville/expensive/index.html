Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Honeyville is $695 per month. It is roughly $260 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $367 (34%) lower, and is about $290 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
