Home prices

The median home in Perry is valued at $268,000. It is roughly $28,740 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $50,500 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $46,500 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Perry the median monthly rent is $843. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $97 (10%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $219 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $142 (14%) lower.
