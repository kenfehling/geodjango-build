Home prices

The median home in Perry costs $268,000. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $28,740 (12%) higher. It is roughly $50,500 (23%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $46,500 (14%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Perry's median monthly rent is $843. Compared to nearby areas it is about $97 (10%) lower. It is roughly $219 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $142 (14%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
