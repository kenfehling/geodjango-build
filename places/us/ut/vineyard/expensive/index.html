Home prices

The value of the median home in Vineyard is $413,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $107,360 (35%) higher. It is approximately $195,500 (89%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $98,500 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Vineyard's median monthly rent is $1,474. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $357 (31%) higher. It is roughly $412 (38%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $489 (49%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
