Home prices

The median home in Vineyard costs $413,000. It is approximately $107,360 (35%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $195,500 (89%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $98,500 (31%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Vineyard's median monthly rent is $1,474. Compared to nearby areas it is about $357 (31%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $412 (38%) higher, and it is about $489 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
