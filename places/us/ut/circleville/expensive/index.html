Home prices

Circleville's median home price is $206,600. This is about $29,300 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $10,900 (5%) lower, and it is roughly $9,300 (4%) lower compared to the city of Cedar City.

Rents

In Circleville the median rent is $690 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $57 (7%) lower. It is approximately $372 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $104 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar City.
