Home prices

Spring City's median home price is $198,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $20,440 (11%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $19,500 (8%) lower, and is approximately $116,500 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Spring City the median monthly rent is $881. It is roughly $88 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $181 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $104 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
