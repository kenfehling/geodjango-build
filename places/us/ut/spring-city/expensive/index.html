In comparison to its nearby areas, Spring City is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Spring City costs $198,000. This is roughly $20,440 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $19,500 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $116,500 (37%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Spring City is $881 per month. This is roughly $88 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $181 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $104 (10%) lower.
