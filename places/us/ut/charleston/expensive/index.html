Compared to its nearby areas, Charleston is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Charleston's median home price is $376,700. It is approximately $248,380 (39%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $159,200 (73%) higher, and it is approximately $62,200 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Charleston is $1,700 a month. This is about $484 (39%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $638 (60%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $715 (72%) higher.
