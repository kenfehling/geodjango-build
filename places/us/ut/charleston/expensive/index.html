Compared to nearby areas, Charleston is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Charleston is $376,700. This is roughly $248,380 (39%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $159,200 (73%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $62,200 (19%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Charleston the median monthly rent is $1,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $484 (39%) higher.It is approximately $638 (60%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $715 (72%) higher.
