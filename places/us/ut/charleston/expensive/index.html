In comparison to other places in this area, Charleston is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Charleston's median home cost is $376,700. This is about $248,380 (39%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $159,200 (73%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $62,200 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Charleston is $1,700 a month. It is approximately $484 (39%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $638 (60%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $715 (72%) higher.
