Home prices

The median home in South Salt Lake is valued at $226,000. It is approximately $85,000 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $8,500 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $88,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In South Salt Lake the median rent is $908 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $203 (18%) lower. It is roughly $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $77 (7%) lower.
