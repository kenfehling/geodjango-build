Home prices

Garden City's median home cost is $256,900. This is about $53,400 (26%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $39,400 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $57,600 (18%) lower.

Rents

Garden City's median monthly rent is $821. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $16 (1%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $241 (22%) lower, and is roughly $164 (16%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
