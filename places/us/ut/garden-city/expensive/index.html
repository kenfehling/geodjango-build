Compared to the surrounding areas, Garden City is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Garden City is $256,900. This is roughly $53,400 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $39,400 (18%) higher, and it is roughly $57,600 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Garden City is $821 per month. This is about $16 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $241 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $164 (16%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
