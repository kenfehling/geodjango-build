Compared to nearby areas, Cornish is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home in Cornish is valued at $188,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $10,020 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $29,300 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $126,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Cornish is $1,325 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $517 (63%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $263 (24%) higher, and it is approximately $340 (34%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
