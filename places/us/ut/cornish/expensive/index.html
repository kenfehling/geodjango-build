In comparison to other places in this area, Cornish is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cornish is valued at $188,200. This is about $10,020 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $29,300 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $126,300 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Cornish the median rent is $1,325 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $517 (63%) higher.It is about $263 (24%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $340 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
