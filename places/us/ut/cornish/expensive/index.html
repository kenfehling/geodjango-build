Compared to its surrounding areas, Cornish is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cornish is valued at $188,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10,020 (5%) higher. It is about $29,300 (13%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $126,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Cornish's median monthly rent is $1,325. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $517 (63%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $263 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $340 (34%) higher.
