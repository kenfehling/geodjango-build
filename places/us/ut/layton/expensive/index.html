Home prices

Layton's median home cost is $260,400. It is about $47,920 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $42,900 (19%) higher, and it is about $54,100 (17%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Layton's median rent is $1,022 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $214 (17%) lower. It is roughly $40 (3%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $37 (3%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
