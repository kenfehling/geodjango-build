Compared to its surrounding areas, Grantsville is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Grantsville costs $254,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $33,840 (15%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $37,200 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $59,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grantsville is $862. It is roughly $287 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $200 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $123 (12%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
