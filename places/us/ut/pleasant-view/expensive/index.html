Home prices

Pleasant View's median home value is $323,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $44,700 (16%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $105,900 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $8,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Pleasant View the median monthly rent is $1,104. This is about $149 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $42 (3%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $119 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
