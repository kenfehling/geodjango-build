Home prices

The median home cost in Pleasant View is $323,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $44,700 (16%) higher.It is roughly $105,900 (48%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $8,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pleasant View is $1,104. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $149 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $42 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $119 (12%) higher.
