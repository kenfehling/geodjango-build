Home prices

The median home in Mendon is valued at $269,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $26,100 (10%) higher. It is roughly $52,400 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $44,600 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Mendon's median monthly rent is $725. This is approximately $81 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $260 (26%) lower.
