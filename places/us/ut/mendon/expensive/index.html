Home prices

The value of the median home in Mendon is $269,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $26,100 (10%) higher. It is approximately $52,400 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $44,600 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Mendon's median monthly rent is $725. This is roughly $81 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $260 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
