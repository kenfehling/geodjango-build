Compared to the surrounding areas, Cedar Hills is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cedar Hills is valued at $371,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $51,460 (12%) lower. It is about $153,800 (70%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $56,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cedar Hills is $1,791 per month. It is about $377 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $729 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $806 (81%) higher.
