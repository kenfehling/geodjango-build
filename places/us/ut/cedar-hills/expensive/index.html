Home prices

It is approximately $51,460 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $153,800 (70%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $56,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

Cedar Hills's median rent is $1,791 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $377 (26%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $729 (68%) higher, and it is approximately $806 (81%) higher in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
