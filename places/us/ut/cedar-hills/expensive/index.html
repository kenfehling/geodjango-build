Home prices

Cedar Hills's median home cost is $371,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $51,460 (12%) lower. It is roughly $153,800 (70%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $56,800 (18%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Cedar Hills the median rent is $1,791 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $377 (26%) higher. It is roughly $729 (68%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $806 (81%) higher.
