Compared to other places in this area, Rockville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of St. George, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rockville costs $383,800. This is roughly $77,420 (25%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $166,300 (76%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $97,800 (34%) higher compared to the city of St. George.

Rents

Rockville's median monthly rent is $840. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $69 (7%) lower. It is about $222 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of St. George is roughly $152 (15%) lower.
