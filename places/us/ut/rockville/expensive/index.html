Home prices

The median home price in Rockville is $383,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $77,420 (25%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $166,300 (76%) higher, and compared to the city of St. George is about $97,800 (34%) higher.

Rents

In Rockville the median monthly rent is $840. It is approximately $69 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $222 (20%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $152 (15%) lower compared to the city of St. George.
