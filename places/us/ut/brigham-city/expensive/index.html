Home prices

Brigham City's median home price is $181,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $59,520 (24%) lower.It is roughly $36,000 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $133,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Brigham City is $739 per month. It is roughly $140 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $246 (24%) lower.
