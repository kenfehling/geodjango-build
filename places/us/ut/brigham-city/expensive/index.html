Home prices

The price of the median home in Brigham City is $181,500. It is roughly $59,520 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $36,000 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $133,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Brigham City the median monthly rent is $739. This is approximately $140 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $323 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $246 (24%) lower.
