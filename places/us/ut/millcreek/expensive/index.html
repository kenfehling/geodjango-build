Compared to its surrounding areas, Millcreek is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Millcreek is $366,100. This is about $46,040 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $148,600 (68%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $51,600 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Millcreek is $1,120 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $58 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $135 (13%) higher.
