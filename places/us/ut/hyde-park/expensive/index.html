Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hyde Park is $282,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $38,320 (15%) higher.It is about $64,900 (29%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $32,100 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Hyde Park the median monthly rent is $827. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $27 (3%) lower.It is about $235 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $158 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
