In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hyde Park is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hyde Park is $282,400. It is roughly $38,320 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $64,900 (29%) higher, and is about $32,100 (10%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in Hyde Park is $827 per month. This is about $27 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $235 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $158 (16%) lower.
