Compared to its surrounding areas, Hyde Park is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hyde Park is $282,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $38,320 (15%) higher. It is about $64,900 (29%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $32,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Hyde Park the median monthly rent is $827. It is about $27 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $235 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $158 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
