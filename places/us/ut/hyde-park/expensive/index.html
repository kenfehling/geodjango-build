In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hyde Park is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Hyde Park is $282,400. This is approximately $38,320 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $64,900 (29%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $32,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hyde Park is $827. It is about $27 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $235 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $158 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.
