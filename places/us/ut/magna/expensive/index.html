Home prices

The median home in Magna costs $205,000. This is about $41,720 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $12,500 (5%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $109,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Magna is $1,120. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $140 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $58 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $135 (13%) higher.
