In comparison to the surrounding areas, Magna is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Magna is valued at $205,000. It is about $41,720 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $12,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is about $109,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Magna is $1,120 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $140 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $58 (5%) higher, and is approximately $135 (13%) higher compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
