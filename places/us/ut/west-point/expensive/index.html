Home prices

The median home in West Point costs $254,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32,020 (14%) higher. It is roughly $37,000 (17%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $60,000 (19%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In West Point the median rent is $1,515 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $339 (28%) higher. It is roughly $453 (42%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is roughly $530 (53%) higher.
