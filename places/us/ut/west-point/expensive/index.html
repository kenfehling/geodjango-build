In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Point is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in West Point is $254,500. This is roughly $32,020 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $37,000 (17%) higher, and is about $60,000 (19%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median rent in West Point is $1,515 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $339 (28%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $453 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $530 (53%) higher.
