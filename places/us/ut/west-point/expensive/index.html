Compared to its nearby areas, West Point is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Salt Lake City, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

West Point's median home value is $254,500. It is about $32,020 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $37,000 (17%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $60,000 (19%) lower.

Rents

West Point's median monthly rent is $1,515. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $339 (28%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $453 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $530 (53%) higher.
