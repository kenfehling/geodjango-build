Home prices

Rents

Lincoln's median monthly rent is $1,538. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $882 (134%) higher.It is roughly $476 (44%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bismarck is about $671 (77%) higher.
