Home prices

The median home in Lincoln is valued at $238,200. This is about $93,080 (64%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $20,700 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Bismarck it is approximately $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lincoln is $1,538 per month. It is about $882 (134%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $476 (44%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $671 (77%) higher in comparison to the city of Bismarck.
