Home prices

The median home in Lincoln costs $238,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $93,080 (64%) higher.It is roughly $20,700 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Bismarck it is about $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lincoln is $1,538. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $882 (134%) higher.It is roughly $476 (44%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bismarck is approximately $671 (77%) higher.
