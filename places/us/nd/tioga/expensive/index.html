Home prices

The median home in Tioga costs $157,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $33,100 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $59,600 (27%) lower, and is about $80,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Williston.

Rents

In Tioga the median rent is $1,088 a month. It is approximately $266 (32%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $26 (2%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Williston it is about $124 (12%) higher.
