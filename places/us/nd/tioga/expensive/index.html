Home prices

The value of the median home in Tioga is $157,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $33,100 (26%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $59,600 (27%) lower, and is approximately $80,800 (33%) lower compared to the city of Williston.

Rents

In Tioga the median rent is $1,088 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $266 (32%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $26 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Williston it is approximately $124 (12%) higher.
