Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tolna is $73,300. It is approximately $12,320 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $144,200 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Forks is roughly $137,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Tolna the median monthly rent is $392. It is about $166 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $670 (63%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $419 (51%) lower compared to the city of Grand Forks.
