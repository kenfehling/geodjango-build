Home prices

The median home in Mandan costs $215,300. This is approximately $64,000 (42%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $2,200 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $30,000 (12%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.

Rents

In Mandan the median monthly rent is $868. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $99 (12%) higher. It is about $194 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Bismarck is is very similar.
