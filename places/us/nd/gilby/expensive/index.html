Home prices

The cost of the median home in Gilby is $66,700. It is roughly $47,280 (41%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $150,800 (69%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo is approximately $145,400 (68%) lower.

Rents

Gilby's median rent is $850 per month. This is approximately $159 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $212 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $27 (3%) higher compared to the city of Fargo.
