Home prices

The median home value in Glenfield is $72,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $7,860 (12%) higher. It is roughly $145,000 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jamestown is roughly $72,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

Glenfield's median rent is $363 per month. This is roughly $47 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $699 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Jamestown it is approximately $317 (46%) lower.
