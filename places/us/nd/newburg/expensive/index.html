Home prices

The median home in Newburg is valued at $107,500. This is about $6,220 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $110,000 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minot it is about $101,200 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Newburg the median monthly rent is $431. This is approximately $311 (41%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $631 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $487 (53%) lower compared to the city of Minot.
