Home prices

The median home in Wahpeton costs $123,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $33,560 (37%) higher. It is approximately $94,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fargo is roughly $88,800 (41%) lower.

Rents

Wahpeton's median rent is $669 a month. This is about $8 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $393 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fargo is approximately $154 (18%) lower.
