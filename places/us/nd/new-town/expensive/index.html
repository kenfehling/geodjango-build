Home prices

The median home in New Town costs $137,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $28,740 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $80,000 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minot it is approximately $71,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in New Town is $692 a month. This is approximately $8 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $226 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Minot.
