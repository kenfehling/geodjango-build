Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marion is $63,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $25,860 (28%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $153,700 (70%) lower, and it is about $98,000 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Aberdeen.

Rents

In Marion the median monthly rent is $625. Compared to other places in this area it is about $165 (35%) higher. It is about $437 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $60 (8%) lower compared to the city of Aberdeen.
