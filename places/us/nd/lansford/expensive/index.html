Home prices

Rents

Lansford's median rent is $619 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $240 (27%) lower. It is approximately $443 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minot is approximately $299 (32%) lower.
