Home prices

The median home in Lansford is valued at $145,300. This is approximately $34,220 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $72,200 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minot is about $63,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

Lansford's median rent is $619 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $240 (27%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $443 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $299 (32%) lower compared to the city of Minot.
