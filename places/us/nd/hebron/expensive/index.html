Home prices

The median home in Hebron costs $96,100. This is roughly $31,700 (24%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $121,400 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $149,200 (60%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.

Rents

In Hebron the median rent is $644 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $178 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $418 (39%) lower, and is approximately $223 (25%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.
