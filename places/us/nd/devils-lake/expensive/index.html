Home prices

The median home in Devils Lake is valued at $138,600. It is approximately $82,420 (146%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $78,900 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $6,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Jamestown.

Rents

In Devils Lake the median rent is $566 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $31 (5%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $496 (46%) lower, and is about $114 (16%) lower compared to the city of Jamestown.
