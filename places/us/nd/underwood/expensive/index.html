Home prices

Underwood's median home cost is $163,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $29,900 (22%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $54,000 (24%) lower, and is approximately $81,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Bismarck.

Rents

In Underwood the median monthly rent is $721. This is roughly $102 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $146 (16%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.
