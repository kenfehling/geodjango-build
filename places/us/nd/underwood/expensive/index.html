Home prices

Underwood's median home cost is $163,500. It is roughly $29,900 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $54,000 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Bismarck is roughly $81,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Underwood the median monthly rent is $721. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $102 (12%) lower. It is roughly $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $146 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Bismarck.
