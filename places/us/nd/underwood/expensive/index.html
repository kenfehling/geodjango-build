Home prices

The median home price in Underwood is $163,500. This is approximately $29,900 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $54,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bismarck is approximately $81,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

Underwood's median monthly rent is $721. It is about $102 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $341 (32%) lower, and is approximately $146 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Bismarck.
