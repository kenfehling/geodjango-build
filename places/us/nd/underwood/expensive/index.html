Home prices

The cost of the median home in Underwood is $163,500. It is approximately $29,900 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $54,000 (24%) lower, and it is approximately $81,800 (33%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.

Rents

The median rent in Underwood is $721 per month. This is about $102 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $341 (32%) lower, and is roughly $146 (16%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.
