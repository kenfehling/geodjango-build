Home prices

The median home in Wimbledon costs $105,100. This is about $41,900 (66%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $112,400 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jamestown it is roughly $39,700 (27%) lower.

Rents
