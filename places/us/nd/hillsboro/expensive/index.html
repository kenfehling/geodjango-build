Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro costs $156,600. It is roughly $43,240 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $60,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $55,500 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.

Rents

In Hillsboro the median monthly rent is $599. It is approximately $67 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $463 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo it is about $224 (27%) lower.
