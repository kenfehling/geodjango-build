Home prices

The median home cost in Hannaford is $52,700. This is roughly $15,900 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $164,800 (75%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $157,600 (74%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Forks.

Rents

Hannaford's median monthly rent is $440. This is roughly $45 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $622 (58%) lower, and is about $371 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Forks.
