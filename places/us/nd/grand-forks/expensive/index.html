Home prices

The median home price in Grand Forks is $210,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $55,760 (36%) higher. It is roughly $7,200 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fargo is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Forks is $811 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $81 (11%) higher. It is roughly $251 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fargo it is roughly $12 (1%) lower.
