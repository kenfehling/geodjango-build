In comparison to the surrounding areas, Grand Forks is significantly more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Fargo, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Grand Forks costs $210,300. This is about $55,760 (36%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $7,200 (3%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Fargo is is very similar.

Rents

Grand Forks's median rent is $811 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $81 (11%) higher. It is approximately $251 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.
