Home prices

Grand Forks's median home cost is $210,300. It is about $55,760 (36%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $7,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fargo it is is very similar.

Rents

In Grand Forks the median rent is $811 a month. This is approximately $81 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $251 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Fargo it is approximately $12 (1%) lower.
