Home prices

The median home in New Rockford costs $83,500. This is approximately $9,220 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $134,000 (61%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jamestown it is roughly $61,300 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Rockford is $631. This is about $93 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $431 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $49 (7%) lower compared to the city of Jamestown.
