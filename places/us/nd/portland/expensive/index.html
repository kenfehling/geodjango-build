Much like the surrounding areas, it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Fargo.

Home prices

Portland's median home cost is $136,300. This is about $5,260 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $81,200 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fargo is about $75,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $445. This is about $248 (35%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $617 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo it is roughly $378 (45%) lower.
