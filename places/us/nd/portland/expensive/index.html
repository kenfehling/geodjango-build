Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $136,300. This is approximately $5,260 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $81,200 (37%) lower, and is roughly $75,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.

Rents

Portland's median rent is $445 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $248 (35%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $617 (58%) lower, and is about $378 (45%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.
