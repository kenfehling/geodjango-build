The median home in Portland costs $136,300. This is roughly $5,260 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $81,200 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $75,800 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $445. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $248 (35%) lower. It is about $617 (58%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Fargo it is roughly $378 (45%) lower.
