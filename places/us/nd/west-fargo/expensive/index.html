Home prices

The median home in West Fargo is valued at $241,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $22,360 (10%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $23,500 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fargo it is approximately $28,900 (13%) higher.

Rents

In West Fargo the median rent is $920 per month. This is about $124 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $142 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo is approximately $97 (11%) higher.
