Home prices

The median home in Surrey costs $232,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $66,200 (39%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $14,500 (6%) higher, and it is approximately $13,300 (5%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.

Rents

The median rent in Surrey is $1,542 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $679 (78%) higher. It is about $480 (45%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bismarck is roughly $675 (77%) higher.
