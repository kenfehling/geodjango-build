Home prices

Rents

Surrey's median monthly rent is $1,542. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $679 (78%) higher.It is roughly $480 (45%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $675 (77%) higher in comparison to the city of Bismarck.
