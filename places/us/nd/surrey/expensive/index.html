Compared to its surrounding areas, Surrey is significantly more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Bismarck, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Surrey is valued at $232,000. This is roughly $66,200 (39%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $14,500 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $13,300 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Bismarck.

Rents

Surrey's median rent is $1,542 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $679 (78%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $480 (45%) higher, and is about $675 (77%) higher compared to the city of Bismarck.
