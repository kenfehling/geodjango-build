Home prices

Rents

Taylor's median monthly rent is $533. This is roughly $306 (36%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $529 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dickinson it is about $384 (41%) lower.
