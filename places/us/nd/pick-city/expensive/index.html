Home prices

The median home in Pick City costs $154,600. This is about $16,380 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $62,900 (28%) lower, and is about $90,700 (36%) lower compared to the city of Bismarck.

Rents

In Pick City the median monthly rent is $1,297. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $617 (90%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $235 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bismarck is about $430 (49%) higher.
