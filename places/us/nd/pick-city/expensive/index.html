Compared to its nearby areas, Pick City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Bismarck, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pick City is valued at $154,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $16,380 (11%) higher. It is about $62,900 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bismarck it is roughly $90,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pick City is $1,297. Compared to nearby areas this is about $617 (90%) higher. It is about $235 (22%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $430 (49%) higher compared to the city of Bismarck.
