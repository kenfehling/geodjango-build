Home prices

The median home in Pick City is valued at $154,600. It is roughly $16,380 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $62,900 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bismarck is about $90,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

Pick City's median monthly rent is $1,297. It is approximately $617 (90%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $235 (22%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $430 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Bismarck.
