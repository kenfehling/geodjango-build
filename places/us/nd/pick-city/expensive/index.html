Home prices

The median home in Pick City costs $154,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $16,380 (11%) higher. It is about $62,900 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Bismarck it is about $90,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

Pick City's median rent is $1,297 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $617 (90%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $235 (22%) higher, and it is roughly $430 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Bismarck.
