Home prices

The median home in Burlington is valued at $188,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $20,100 (11%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $29,400 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $20,600 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Minot.

Rents

In Burlington the median monthly rent is $994. It is approximately $36 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $68 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minot it is about $76 (8%) higher.
