Rents

Ellendale's median monthly rent is $553. This is about $13 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $509 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Aberdeen is approximately $132 (19%) lower.
