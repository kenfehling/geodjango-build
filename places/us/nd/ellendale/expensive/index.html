Rents

Ellendale's median monthly rent is $553. It is roughly $13 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $509 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Aberdeen it is approximately $132 (19%) lower.
