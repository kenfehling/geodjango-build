Home prices

The median home in Ellendale is valued at $77,300. It is roughly $10,220 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $140,200 (64%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $84,500 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Aberdeen.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ellendale is $553. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $13 (2%) higher. It is approximately $509 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Aberdeen it is approximately $132 (19%) lower.
