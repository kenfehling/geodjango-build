Home prices

The price of the median home in Ellendale is $77,300. This is roughly $10,220 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $140,200 (64%) lower, and is approximately $84,500 (52%) lower compared to the city of Aberdeen.

Rents

Ellendale's median monthly rent is $553. This is roughly $13 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $509 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $132 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Aberdeen.
