Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rutland is $344. This is approximately $264 (43%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $718 (67%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $479 (58%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.
