Home prices

The median home in Michigan City is valued at $72,500. This is roughly $15,980 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $145,000 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $137,800 (65%) lower compared to the city of Grand Forks.

Rents

The median rent in Michigan City is $488 a month. It is about $58 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $574 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Forks is about $323 (39%) lower.
