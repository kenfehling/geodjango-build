Home prices

The median home in Hazen costs $157,700. This is roughly $17,380 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $59,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bismarck is approximately $87,600 (35%) lower.

Rents

Hazen's median rent is $862 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $39 (4%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $200 (18%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Bismarck.
