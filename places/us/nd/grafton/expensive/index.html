The median home cost in Grafton is $84,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $1,280 (1%) higher. It is approximately $132,800 (61%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $125,600 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Forks.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grafton is $676. This is roughly $42 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $386 (36%) lower, and is approximately $135 (16%) lower compared to the city of Grand Forks.
