Compared to the surrounding areas, Four Corners is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Bozeman, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners costs $396,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $106,940 (36%) higher. It is approximately $178,500 (82%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $30,400 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

In Four Corners the median rent is $1,102 per month. It is approximately $13 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $40 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is roughly $24 (2%) higher.
