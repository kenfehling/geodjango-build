Compared to other places in this area, Four Corners is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Bozeman, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners is valued at $396,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $106,940 (36%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $178,500 (82%) higher, and compared to the city of Bozeman is roughly $30,400 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Four Corners is $1,102 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $13 (1%) higher. It is approximately $40 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Bozeman is about $24 (2%) higher.
