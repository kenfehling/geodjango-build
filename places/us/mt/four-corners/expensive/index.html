Home prices

The median home in Four Corners is valued at $396,000. It is about $106,940 (36%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $178,500 (82%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is about $30,400 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Four Corners the median monthly rent is $1,102. This is approximately $13 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $40 (3%) higher, and is roughly $24 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Bozeman.
