Home prices

The median home in East Helena costs $168,100. This is approximately $104,860 (38%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $49,400 (22%) lower, and is approximately $197,500 (54%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

The median rent in East Helena is $814 per month. This is approximately $103 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $248 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $264 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.
