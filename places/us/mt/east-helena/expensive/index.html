Home prices

The median home in East Helena is valued at $168,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $104,860 (38%) lower.It is roughly $49,400 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is roughly $197,500 (54%) lower.

Rents

East Helena's median monthly rent is $814. This is approximately $103 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $248 (23%) lower, and is approximately $264 (24%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.
