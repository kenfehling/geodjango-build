Home prices

The median home price in Dixon is $105,400. It is approximately $15,300 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $112,100 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $174,300 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Dixon's median rent is $575 a month. This is about $17 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $487 (45%) lower, and is about $284 (33%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
