Home prices

The price of the median home in Dixon is $105,400. It is roughly $15,300 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $112,100 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $174,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Dixon the median rent is $575 per month. This is about $17 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $284 (33%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
