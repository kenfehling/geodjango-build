Home prices

Troy's median home price is $153,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $36,680 (19%) lower. It is approximately $64,000 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $81,400 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Coeur d'Alene.

Rents

Troy's median monthly rent is $538. Compared to nearby areas this is about $266 (33%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $524 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $444 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Coeur d'Alene.
