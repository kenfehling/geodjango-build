Home prices

Boulder's median home price is $139,100. This is roughly $93,980 (40%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $78,400 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $226,500 (61%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

The median rent in Boulder is $612 a month. This is about $183 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $450 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is roughly $466 (43%) lower.
