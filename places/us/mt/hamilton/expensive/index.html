Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $177,600. It is approximately $4,040 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $39,900 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $102,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Hamilton the median rent is $754 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $76 (11%) higher. It is about $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $105 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
