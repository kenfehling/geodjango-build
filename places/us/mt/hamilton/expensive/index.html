Home prices

The median home in Hamilton is valued at $177,600. It is approximately $4,040 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $39,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $102,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Hamilton the median rent is $754 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $76 (11%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is roughly $105 (12%) lower.
