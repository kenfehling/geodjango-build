Home prices

The median home in Charlo costs $151,900. It is approximately $50,020 (49%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $65,600 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $127,800 (45%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

In Charlo the median rent is $703 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $125 (21%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $359 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $156 (18%) lower.
