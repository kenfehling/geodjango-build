Home prices

The median home value in Superior is $116,300. It is roughly $14,540 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $101,200 (46%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $163,400 (58%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Superior is $330. This is about $258 (43%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $732 (68%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is about $529 (61%) lower.
