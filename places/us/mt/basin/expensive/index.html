Compared to other places in this area, Basin is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Bozeman however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Basin costs $156,300. This is roughly $37,320 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $61,200 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $209,300 (57%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

Basin's median monthly rent is $513. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $190 (27%) lower. It is roughly $549 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman is about $565 (52%) lower.
