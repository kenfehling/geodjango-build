Compared to nearby areas, Corvallis is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Missoula, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corvallis costs $165,100. It is about $10,960 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $52,400 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is approximately $114,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Corvallis the median monthly rent is $737. It is roughly $55 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is about $122 (14%) lower.
