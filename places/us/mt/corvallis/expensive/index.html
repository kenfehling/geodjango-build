Compared to its surrounding areas, Corvallis is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Missoula, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corvallis is valued at $165,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $10,960 (6%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $52,400 (24%) lower, and it is approximately $114,600 (40%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corvallis is $737. It is about $55 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $325 (30%) lower, and is about $122 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
