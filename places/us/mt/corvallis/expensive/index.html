In comparison to other places in this area, Corvallis is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Missoula, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corvallis is valued at $165,100. It is approximately $10,960 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $52,400 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $114,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

Corvallis's median monthly rent is $737. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $55 (8%) higher.It is approximately $325 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Missoula it is approximately $122 (14%) lower.
