In comparison to other places in this area, Corvallis is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Missoula.

Home prices

Corvallis's median home cost is $165,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $10,960 (6%) lower.It is about $52,400 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is about $114,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Corvallis the median rent is $737 per month. This is roughly $55 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Missoula is about $122 (14%) lower.
