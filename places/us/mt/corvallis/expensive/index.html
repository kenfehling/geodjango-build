In comparison to the surrounding areas, Corvallis is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Missoula however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Corvallis's median home value is $165,100. It is roughly $10,960 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $52,400 (24%) lower, and is approximately $114,600 (40%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

In Corvallis the median rent is $737 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $55 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $325 (30%) lower, and it is about $122 (14%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
