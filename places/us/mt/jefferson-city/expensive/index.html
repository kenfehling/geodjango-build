Home prices

The median home price in Jefferson City is $249,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $9,680 (4%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $31,500 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is about $116,600 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jefferson City is $547. This is approximately $277 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $515 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $531 (49%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.
