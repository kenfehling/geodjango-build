Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City costs $249,000. It is roughly $9,680 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $31,500 (14%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $116,600 (31%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

In Jefferson City the median rent is $547 per month. This is approximately $277 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $515 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman is roughly $531 (49%) lower.
