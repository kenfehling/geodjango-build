Home prices

Jefferson City's median home value is $249,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $9,680 (4%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $31,500 (14%) higher, and is approximately $116,600 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

Jefferson City's median rent is $547 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $277 (33%) lower.It is roughly $515 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Bozeman is roughly $531 (49%) lower.
