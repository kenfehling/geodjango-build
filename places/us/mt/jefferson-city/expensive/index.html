Much like the surrounding areas, it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Bozeman, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Jefferson City is $249,000. This is roughly $9,680 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $31,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is approximately $116,600 (31%) lower.

Rents

Jefferson City's median monthly rent is $547. This is roughly $277 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $515 (48%) lower, and it is roughly $531 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.
