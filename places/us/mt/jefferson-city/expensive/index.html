Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City is valued at $249,000. It is roughly $9,680 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $31,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman is approximately $116,600 (31%) lower.

Rents

Jefferson City's median monthly rent is $547. This is about $277 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $515 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Bozeman is roughly $531 (49%) lower.
