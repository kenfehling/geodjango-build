Jefferson City's median home price is $249,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $9,680 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $31,500 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Bozeman is about $116,600 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jefferson City is $547. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $277 (33%) lower. It is roughly $515 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is roughly $531 (49%) lower.
