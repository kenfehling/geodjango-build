Home prices

The median home in St. Ignatius is valued at $123,400. This is roughly $6,300 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $94,100 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Missoula it is about $156,300 (55%) lower.

Rents

St. Ignatius's median rent is $675 a month. It is roughly $103 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $184 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
