Home prices

The median home value in Evergreen is $192,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $38,660 (16%) lower. It is approximately $25,100 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is about $87,300 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Evergreen is $984 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $150 (17%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $78 (7%) lower, and it is roughly $125 (14%) higher compared to the city of Missoula.
