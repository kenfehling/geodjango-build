Home prices

The median home cost in Thompson Falls is $163,900. It is about $23,820 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $53,600 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Kalispell is approximately $69,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

Thompson Falls's median monthly rent is $725. It is roughly $19 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and is approximately $80 (9%) lower compared to the city of Kalispell.
