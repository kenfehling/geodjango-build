Home prices

The median home value in St. Regis is $155,300. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $32,420 (26%) higher. It is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is about $124,400 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in St. Regis is $588 a month. This is roughly $58 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $474 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $271 (31%) lower.
