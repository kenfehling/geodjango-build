Home prices

The median home cost in St. Regis is $155,300. This is approximately $32,420 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $62,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $124,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median rent in St. Regis is $588 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $58 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $474 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is roughly $271 (31%) lower.
