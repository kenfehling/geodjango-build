Home prices

The median home price in St. Regis is $155,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $32,420 (26%) higher.It is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $124,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median rent in St. Regis is $588 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $58 (11%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $474 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula is about $271 (31%) lower.
