Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Regis is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Missoula, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Regis costs $155,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $32,420 (26%) higher. It is about $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Missoula it is approximately $124,400 (44%) lower.

Rents

In St. Regis the median rent is $588 per month. This is approximately $58 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $474 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Missoula is approximately $271 (31%) lower.
