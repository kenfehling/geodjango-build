Home prices

The price of the median home in Evaro is $253,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $49,260 (24%) higher. It is roughly $36,300 (16%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Missoula is about $25,900 (9%) lower.

Rents

In Evaro the median rent is $700 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $162 (18%) lower. It is roughly $362 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $159 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
