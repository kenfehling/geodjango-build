Home prices

Big Sandy's median home cost is $93,800. This is roughly $19,960 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $123,700 (56%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Great Falls it is approximately $82,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Big Sandy is $575. It is approximately $167 (41%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $487 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Great Falls is about $154 (21%) lower.
