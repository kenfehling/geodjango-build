Home prices

The median home in Townsend is valued at $160,400. It is roughly $96,480 (37%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $57,100 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $205,200 (56%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

Townsend's median rent is $645 per month. This is about $233 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $417 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $433 (40%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.
