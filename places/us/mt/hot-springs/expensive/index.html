Home prices

The median home in Hot Springs is valued at $96,000. It is about $41,420 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $121,500 (55%) lower, and is approximately $183,700 (65%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Hot Springs's median rent is $517 per month. This is approximately $57 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $545 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $342 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
