Home prices

The median home in Hot Springs costs $96,000. This is approximately $41,420 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $121,500 (55%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $183,700 (65%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Hot Springs's median monthly rent is $517. This is about $57 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $545 (51%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $342 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
