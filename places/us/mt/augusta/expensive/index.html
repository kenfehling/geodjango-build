Home prices

The median home value in Augusta is $191,100. This is about $48,760 (34%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $26,400 (12%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Great Falls is roughly $14,600 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Augusta the median rent is $557 per month. This is approximately $182 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $505 (47%) lower, and is about $172 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Great Falls.
