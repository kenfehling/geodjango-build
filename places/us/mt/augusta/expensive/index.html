Compared to its surrounding areas, Augusta is substantially more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Great Falls however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Augusta is $191,100. It is roughly $48,760 (34%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $26,400 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Great Falls it is roughly $14,600 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Augusta is $557 a month. It is roughly $182 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $505 (47%) lower, and is roughly $172 (23%) lower compared to the city of Great Falls.
