Home prices

The median home price in Stanford is $85,800. It is approximately $8,140 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $131,700 (60%) lower, and it is about $90,700 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Great Falls.

Rents

In Stanford the median rent is $433 a month. It is approximately $241 (35%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $629 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $296 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Great Falls.
