Home prices

The median home in Pray is valued at $421,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $128,660 (43%) higher. It is about $204,400 (93%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $56,300 (15%) higher compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

In Pray the median monthly rent is $1,184. This is about $334 (39%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $122 (11%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is approximately $106 (9%) higher.
