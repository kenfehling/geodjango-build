Rents

The median monthly rent in Pray is $1,184. It is approximately $334 (39%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $122 (11%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is about $106 (9%) higher.
