Home prices

The median home in Fortine is valued at $172,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $34,400 (16%) lower. It is approximately $45,300 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Kalispell is about $61,300 (26%) lower.

Rents

Fortine's median monthly rent is $747. It is about $100 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $315 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $58 (7%) lower compared to the city of Kalispell.
