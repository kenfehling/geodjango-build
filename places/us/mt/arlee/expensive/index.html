Home prices

The median home price in Arlee is $165,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $28,620 (14%) lower. It is about $51,800 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $114,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

In Arlee the median monthly rent is $542. This is roughly $215 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $520 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $317 (36%) lower.
