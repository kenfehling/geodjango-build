Home prices

The median home cost in St. Pierre is $65,000. This is approximately $4,420 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $152,500 (70%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Havre is about $84,300 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Pierre is $338. This is approximately $83 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $724 (68%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Havre it is approximately $327 (49%) lower.
