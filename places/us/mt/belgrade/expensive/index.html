Home prices

The median home value in Belgrade is $243,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $76,420 (23%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $25,700 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman is approximately $122,400 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Belgrade is $914 a month. This is roughly $213 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $148 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Bozeman is approximately $164 (15%) lower.
