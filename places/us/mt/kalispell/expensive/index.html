Home prices

The median home in Kalispell is valued at $233,500. It is about $3,720 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $16,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $46,200 (16%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

In Kalispell the median monthly rent is $805. This is roughly $50 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $257 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula is about $54 (6%) lower.
