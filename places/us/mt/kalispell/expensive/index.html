Home prices

The median home in Kalispell is valued at $233,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $3,720 (1%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $16,000 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $46,200 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kalispell is $805 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $50 (5%) lower. It is roughly $257 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $54 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
