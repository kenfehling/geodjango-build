Home prices

The value of the median home in Kalispell is $233,500. It is about $3,720 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $16,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $46,200 (16%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median rent in Kalispell is $805 a month. It is about $50 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $257 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $54 (6%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
