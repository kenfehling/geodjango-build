Home prices

The median home in Kalispell is valued at $233,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $3,720 (1%) lower. It is roughly $16,000 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $46,200 (16%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Kalispell's median monthly rent is $805. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $50 (5%) lower. It is about $257 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $54 (6%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
