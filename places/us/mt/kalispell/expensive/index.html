Home prices

The median home in Kalispell costs $233,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $3,720 (1%) lower.It is roughly $16,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is about $46,200 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Kalispell the median monthly rent is $805. It is approximately $50 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $257 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is roughly $54 (6%) lower.
