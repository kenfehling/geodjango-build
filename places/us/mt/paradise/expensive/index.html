In comparison to its surrounding areas, Paradise is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Missoula.

Home prices

The median home in Paradise costs $101,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $20,560 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $116,100 (53%) lower, and it is approximately $178,300 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Paradise's median rent is $413 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $122 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $649 (61%) lower, and it is roughly $446 (51%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
