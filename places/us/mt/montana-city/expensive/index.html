In comparison to its nearby areas, Montana City is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Bozeman however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montana City is valued at $345,300. It is roughly $116,680 (51%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $127,800 (58%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $20,300 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

In Montana City the median monthly rent is $1,143. This is roughly $221 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $81 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $65 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Bozeman.
