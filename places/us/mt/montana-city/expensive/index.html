Home prices

Montana City's median home price is $345,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $116,680 (51%) higher.It is approximately $127,800 (58%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman is about $20,300 (5%) lower.

Rents

Montana City's median monthly rent is $1,143. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $221 (23%) higher.It is about $81 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $65 (6%) higher compared to the city of Bozeman.
