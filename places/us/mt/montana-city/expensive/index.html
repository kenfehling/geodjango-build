In comparison to nearby areas, Montana City is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Bozeman, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montana City is valued at $345,300. This is about $116,680 (51%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $127,800 (58%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $20,300 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

The median rent in Montana City is $1,143 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $221 (23%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $81 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is roughly $65 (6%) higher.
