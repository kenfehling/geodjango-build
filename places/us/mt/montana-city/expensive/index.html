Compared to its nearby areas, Montana City is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Bozeman, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Montana City is $345,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $116,680 (51%) higher. It is about $127,800 (58%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is about $20,300 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Montana City is $1,143 a month. It is about $221 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $81 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $65 (6%) higher compared to the city of Bozeman.
