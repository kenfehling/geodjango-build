Compared to nearby areas, Montana City is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Bozeman.

Home prices

The median home price in Montana City is $345,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $116,680 (51%) higher.It is about $127,800 (58%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is approximately $20,300 (5%) lower.

Rents

Montana City's median rent is $1,143 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $221 (23%) higher.It is about $81 (7%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is about $65 (6%) higher.
