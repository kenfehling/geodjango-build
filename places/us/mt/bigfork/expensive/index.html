Home prices

The median home in Bigfork costs $365,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $105,120 (40%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $147,800 (67%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $85,600 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bigfork is $884. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $178 (16%) lower, and is roughly $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Missoula.
