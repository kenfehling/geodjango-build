Compared to its surrounding areas, Bigfork is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Missoula.

Home prices

The median home cost in Bigfork is $365,300. It is approximately $105,120 (40%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $147,800 (67%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $85,600 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median rent in Bigfork is $884 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar. It is about $178 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Missoula.
