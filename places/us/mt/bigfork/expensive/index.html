Home prices

The median home cost in Bigfork is $365,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $105,120 (40%) higher.It is about $147,800 (67%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $85,600 (30%) higher compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

In Bigfork the median monthly rent is $884. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $178 (16%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $25 (2%) higher.
