In comparison to other places in this area, Bigfork is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Missoula however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bigfork costs $365,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $105,120 (40%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $147,800 (67%) higher, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $85,600 (30%) higher.

Rents

Bigfork's median rent is $884 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $178 (16%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $25 (2%) higher compared to the city of Missoula.
