Home prices

The cost of the median home in Garrison is $156,300. It is approximately $18,580 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $61,200 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $123,400 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Garrison the median monthly rent is $413. It is about $247 (37%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $649 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula it is approximately $446 (51%) lower.
