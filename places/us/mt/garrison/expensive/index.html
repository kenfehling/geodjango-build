Compared to other places in this area, Garrison is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Missoula, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Garrison costs $156,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $18,580 (13%) higher.It is roughly $61,200 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $123,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Garrison's median rent is $413 a month. This is about $247 (37%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $649 (61%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $446 (51%) lower.
