Home prices

Florence's median home value is $282,800. This is roughly $61,060 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $65,300 (30%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $3,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Florence the median rent is $754 a month. This is roughly $61 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $308 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $105 (12%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
