Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $282,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $61,060 (27%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $65,300 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of Missoula is approximately $3,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $754. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $61 (7%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $308 (29%) lower, and is approximately $105 (12%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
