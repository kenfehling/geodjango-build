Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $754. It is about $61 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $308 (29%) lower, and it is about $105 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
