Compared to nearby areas, Florence is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Missoula.

Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $282,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $61,060 (27%) higher. It is about $65,300 (30%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is roughly $3,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

Florence's median rent is $754 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $61 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $308 (29%) lower, and is about $105 (12%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
