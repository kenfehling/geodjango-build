Home prices

The median home in Marion costs $253,900. It is about $15,540 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $36,400 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is about $25,800 (9%) lower.

Rents

Marion's median monthly rent is $900. It is about $144 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $162 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $41 (4%) higher.
