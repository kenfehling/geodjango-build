Home prices

The median home price in Kila is $267,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $42,020 (18%) higher. It is about $50,100 (23%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $12,100 (4%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Kila's median rent is $633 a month. This is roughly $253 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $429 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $226 (26%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
