Home prices

The median home in Manhattan costs $253,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $21,160 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $36,400 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Bozeman is about $111,700 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Manhattan is $1,394 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $414 (42%) higher. It is approximately $332 (31%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman is approximately $316 (29%) higher.
