In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lincoln is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Missoula, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lincoln is $164,400. It is about $23,080 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $53,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is approximately $115,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Lincoln the median monthly rent is $664. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $20 (2%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $398 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula is roughly $195 (22%) lower.
