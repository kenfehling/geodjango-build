In comparison to other places in this area, Lincoln is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Missoula, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lincoln is $164,400. This is approximately $23,080 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $115,300 (41%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

In Lincoln the median rent is $664 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $20 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $398 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula is about $195 (22%) lower.
