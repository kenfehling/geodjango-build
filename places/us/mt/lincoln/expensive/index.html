Home prices

The value of the median home in Lincoln is $164,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $23,080 (12%) lower. It is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Missoula is approximately $115,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Lincoln the median rent is $664 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $20 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $398 (37%) lower, and it is roughly $195 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
