Home prices

Lincoln's median home value is $164,400. This is about $23,080 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $53,100 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is about $115,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Lincoln the median monthly rent is $664. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $20 (2%) lower.It is about $398 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $195 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
