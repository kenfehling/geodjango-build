Home prices

Cooke City's median home cost is $281,300. This is approximately $62,720 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $63,800 (29%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is approximately $84,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

In Cooke City the median monthly rent is $830. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $50 (5%) lower. It is approximately $232 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $248 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.
