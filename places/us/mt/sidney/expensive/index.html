Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sidney is $233,800. It is roughly $86,320 (58%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $16,300 (7%) higher, and is about $4,900 (2%) lower compared to the city of Williston.

Rents

The median rent in Sidney is $840 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $255 (43%) higher.It is roughly $222 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Williston it is approximately $124 (12%) lower.
