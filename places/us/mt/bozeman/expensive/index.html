Compared to its nearby areas, Bozeman is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Bozeman costs $365,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $56,150 (18%) higher. It is about $148,100 (68%) higher compared to the US as a whole.

Rents

In Bozeman the median rent is $1,078 per month. This is about $32 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $16 (1%) higher.
