Home prices

Columbus's median home price is $185,600. This is about $10,000 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $31,900 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Billings it is roughly $35,500 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Columbus is $735 a month. This is approximately $85 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $327 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $164 (18%) lower compared to the city of Billings.
