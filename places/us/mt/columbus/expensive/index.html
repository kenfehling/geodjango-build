Home prices

The median home cost in Columbus is $185,600. This is approximately $10,000 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $31,900 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $35,500 (16%) lower compared to the city of Billings.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbus is $735. This is roughly $85 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $327 (30%) lower, and is approximately $164 (18%) lower compared to the city of Billings.
