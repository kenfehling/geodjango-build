Home prices

The cost of the median home in Baker is $154,300. It is roughly $49,560 (47%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $63,200 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $79,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Sidney.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Baker is $746. It is approximately $129 (20%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $316 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Sidney is roughly $94 (11%) lower.
