Home prices

Rents

In Baker the median monthly rent is $746. This is approximately $129 (20%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $316 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sidney is approximately $94 (11%) lower.
