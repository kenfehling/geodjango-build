Home prices

Stevensville's median home price is $182,400. It is approximately $17,380 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $35,100 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Missoula is approximately $97,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Stevensville the median monthly rent is $681. It is about $46 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $381 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula it is roughly $178 (20%) lower.
