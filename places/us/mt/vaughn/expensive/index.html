Home prices

The cost of the median home in Vaughn is $140,200. This is about $4,960 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $77,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Great Falls it is about $36,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Vaughn the median rent is $760 a month. This is about $57 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $302 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Great Falls it is roughly $31 (4%) higher.
