Home prices

Ennis's median home cost is $227,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $77,580 (25%) lower.It is about $10,000 (4%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $138,100 (37%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

In Ennis the median monthly rent is $682. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $262 (27%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $380 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is about $396 (36%) lower.
