Home prices

The median home price in Livingston is $224,200. It is roughly $50,480 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $6,700 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Bozeman is roughly $141,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Livingston is $813 a month. This is roughly $102 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman it is approximately $265 (24%) lower.
