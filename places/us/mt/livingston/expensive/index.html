Home prices

The median home in Livingston costs $224,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $50,480 (18%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $6,700 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bozeman is roughly $141,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

Livingston's median monthly rent is $813. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $102 (11%) lower.It is about $249 (23%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $265 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.
