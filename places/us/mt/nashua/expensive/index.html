Home prices

The median home price in Nashua is $129,200. This is roughly $21,320 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $88,300 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Glasgow is approximately $21,000 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Nashua is $640 a month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $422 (39%) lower, and is roughly $82 (14%) higher compared to the city of Glasgow.
