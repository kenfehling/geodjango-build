Home prices

The median home in Columbia Falls costs $195,700. It is about $51,860 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $21,800 (10%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is approximately $84,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

Columbia Falls's median rent is $705 a month. This is approximately $225 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $357 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is about $154 (17%) lower.
