In comparison to nearby areas, Helena is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Bozeman, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Helena is $244,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $13,420 (5%) lower.It is about $26,800 (12%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $121,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

Helena's median monthly rent is $820. It is approximately $96 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $242 (22%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is approximately $258 (23%) lower.
