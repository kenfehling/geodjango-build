Compared to nearby areas, Helena is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Bozeman however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Helena is $244,300. It is roughly $13,420 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $26,800 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Bozeman is approximately $121,300 (33%) lower.

Rents

Helena's median monthly rent is $820. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $96 (10%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $242 (22%) lower, and is about $258 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Bozeman.
