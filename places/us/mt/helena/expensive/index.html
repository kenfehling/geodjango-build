Compared to its surrounding areas, Helena is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Bozeman, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Helena's median home price is $244,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $13,420 (5%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $26,800 (12%) higher, and is about $121,300 (33%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

Helena's median monthly rent is $820. It is approximately $96 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $242 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman it is roughly $258 (23%) lower.
