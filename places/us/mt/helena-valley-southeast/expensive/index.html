In comparison to other places in this area, Helena Valley Southeast is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Bozeman however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Helena Valley Southeast is $210,400. This is about $54,100 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $7,100 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $155,200 (42%) lower compared to the city of Bozeman.

Rents

Helena Valley Southeast's median monthly rent is $854. This is roughly $55 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $208 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bozeman is approximately $224 (20%) lower.
