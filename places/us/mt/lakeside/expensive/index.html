Home prices

The median home in Lakeside is valued at $398,000. This is about $130,080 (48%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $180,500 (82%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $118,300 (42%) higher compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

Lakeside's median rent is $599 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $276 (31%) lower.It is roughly $463 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is roughly $260 (30%) lower.
