Home prices

The median home in Lakeside costs $398,000. This is approximately $130,080 (48%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $180,500 (82%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $118,300 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median rent in Lakeside is $599 a month. It is roughly $276 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $463 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Missoula is roughly $260 (30%) lower.
