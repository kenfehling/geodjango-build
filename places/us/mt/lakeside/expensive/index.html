In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lakeside is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Missoula, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lakeside is $398,000. It is about $130,080 (48%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $180,500 (82%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is about $118,300 (42%) higher.

Rents

Lakeside's median monthly rent is $599. It is approximately $276 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $463 (43%) lower, and is approximately $260 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Missoula.
