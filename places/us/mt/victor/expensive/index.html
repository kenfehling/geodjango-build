Home prices

The median home value in Victor is $165,500. It is roughly $37,660 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is about $52,000 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Missoula it is about $114,200 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Victor is $820. This is roughly $121 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $242 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $39 (4%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.
