Home prices

Victor's median home value is $165,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $37,660 (18%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $52,000 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $114,200 (40%) lower compared to the city of Missoula.

Rents

The median rent in Victor is $820 a month. It is approximately $121 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $242 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Missoula it is roughly $39 (4%) lower.
