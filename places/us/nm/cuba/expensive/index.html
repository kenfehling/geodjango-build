Home prices

The price of the median home in Cuba is $101,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $68,280 (40%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $115,900 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Albuquerque is approximately $96,600 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cuba is $571 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $190 (24%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $491 (46%) lower, and it is roughly $302 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.
