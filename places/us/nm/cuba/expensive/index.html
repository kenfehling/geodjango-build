Home prices

Rents

Cuba's median rent is $571 per month. This is about $190 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $491 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is about $302 (34%) lower.
