Home prices

Rents

Cuba's median rent is $571 per month. It is about $190 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $491 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $302 (34%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.
