Rents

The median rent in Mesita is $217 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $319 (59%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $845 (79%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is about $656 (75%) lower.
