Home prices

The median home in Ranchos de Taos is valued at $305,500. It is approximately $110,440 (56%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $88,000 (40%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Santa Fe is about $24,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Ranchos de Taos the median rent is $526 per month. It is about $129 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $536 (50%) lower, and it is about $554 (51%) lower compared to the city of Santa Fe.
