Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ranchos de Taos is $305,500. It is roughly $110,440 (56%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $88,000 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of Santa Fe is approximately $24,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Ranchos de Taos the median rent is $526 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $129 (19%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $536 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Santa Fe is about $554 (51%) lower.
