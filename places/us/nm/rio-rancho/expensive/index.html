Compared to other places in this area, Rio Rancho is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Albuquerquehowever, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Rio Rancho is valued at $190,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $40,540 (17%) lower. It is approximately $26,700 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $7,400 (3%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

Rio Rancho's median monthly rent is $1,167. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $174 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $105 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is about $294 (33%) higher.
