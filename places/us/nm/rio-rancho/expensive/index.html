Home prices

The median home cost in Rio Rancho is $190,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $40,540 (17%) lower. It is approximately $26,700 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $7,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

In Rio Rancho the median monthly rent is $1,167. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $174 (17%) higher. It is roughly $105 (9%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Albuquerque it is approximately $294 (33%) higher.
