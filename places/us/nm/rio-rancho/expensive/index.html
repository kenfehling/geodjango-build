Compared to the surrounding areas, Rio Rancho is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Home prices

The median home in Rio Rancho is valued at $190,800. It is about $40,540 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $26,700 (12%) lower, and is roughly $7,400 (3%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

In Rio Rancho the median rent is $1,167 a month. It is about $174 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $105 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $294 (33%) higher.
