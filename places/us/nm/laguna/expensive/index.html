Home prices

The median home in Laguna costs $77,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $4,320 (5%) higher.It is approximately $139,600 (64%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $120,300 (60%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

Laguna's median rent is $336 a month. This is roughly $177 (34%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $726 (68%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $537 (61%) lower.
