Home prices

The median home in Laguna is valued at $77,900. It is roughly $4,320 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $139,600 (64%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $120,300 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

In Laguna the median monthly rent is $336. This is about $177 (34%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $726 (68%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Albuquerque it is approximately $537 (61%) lower.
