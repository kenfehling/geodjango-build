Home prices

The cost of the median home in Happy Valley is $208,300. This is approximately $33,880 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $9,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $103,200 (98%) higher compared to the city of Roswell.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Happy Valley is $1,261. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $337 (36%) higher.It is about $199 (18%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $483 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Roswell.
