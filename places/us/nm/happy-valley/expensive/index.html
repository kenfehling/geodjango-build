Home prices

The price of the median home in Happy Valley is $208,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $33,880 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $9,200 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roswell it is about $103,200 (98%) higher.

Rents

Happy Valley's median rent is $1,261 per month. This is about $337 (36%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $199 (18%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Roswell is approximately $483 (62%) higher.
