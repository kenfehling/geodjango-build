Home prices

The median home in Agua Fria is valued at $206,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $114,520 (35%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $11,500 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $7,800 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Agua Fria is $799. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $286 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $263 (24%) lower, and is roughly $74 (8%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.
