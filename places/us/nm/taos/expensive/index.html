Compared to other places in this area, Taos is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Santa Fe, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Taos is $260,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $56,800 (27%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $43,300 (19%) higher, and is about $20,000 (7%) lower compared to the city of Santa Fe.

Rents

In Taos the median monthly rent is $708. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $89 (14%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $354 (33%) lower, and is about $372 (34%) lower compared to the city of Santa Fe.
