Compared to its nearby areas, Taos is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Santa Fe.

Home prices

Taos's median home cost is $260,800. This is approximately $56,800 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $43,300 (19%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Santa Fe it is approximately $20,000 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Taos is $708 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $89 (14%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $354 (33%) lower, and is roughly $372 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Fe.
