Compared to other places in this area, Taos is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Santa Fe.

Home prices

The median home in Taos costs $260,800. This is approximately $56,800 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $43,300 (19%) higher, and is approximately $20,000 (7%) lower compared to the city of Santa Fe.

Rents

In Taos the median rent is $708 a month. It is about $89 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $354 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $372 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Fe.
