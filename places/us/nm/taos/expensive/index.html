Compared to nearby areas, Taos is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Santa Fe, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Taos is $260,800. This is roughly $56,800 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $43,300 (19%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Santa Fe is about $20,000 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Taos is $708. It is roughly $89 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $354 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $372 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Fe.
