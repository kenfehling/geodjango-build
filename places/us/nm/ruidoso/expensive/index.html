Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ruidoso is $180,700. It is about $78,280 (76%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $36,800 (16%) lower, and it is about $75,600 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Roswell.

Rents

In Ruidoso the median monthly rent is $770. This is roughly $214 (38%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $292 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roswell is about $8 (1%) lower.
