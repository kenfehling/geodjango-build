Home prices

Ruidoso's median home cost is $180,700. This is about $78,280 (76%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $36,800 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Roswell it is about $75,600 (71%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ruidoso is $770. It is approximately $214 (38%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $292 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roswell is about $8 (1%) lower.
