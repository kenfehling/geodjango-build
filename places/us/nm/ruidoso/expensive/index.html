Home prices

The median home in Ruidoso costs $180,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $78,280 (76%) higher. It is approximately $36,800 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roswell it is roughly $75,600 (71%) higher.

Rents

Ruidoso's median rent is $770 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $214 (38%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $292 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roswell is about $8 (1%) lower.
