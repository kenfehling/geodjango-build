In comparison to its surrounding areas, Socorro is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Home prices

The median home in Socorro is valued at $135,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $8,340 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $81,900 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $62,600 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Socorro the median rent is $636 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $154 (19%) lower. It is approximately $426 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $237 (27%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.
