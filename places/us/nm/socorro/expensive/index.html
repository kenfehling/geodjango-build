Home prices

Rents

In Socorro the median monthly rent is $636. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $204 (24%) lower.It is approximately $426 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Albuquerque it is approximately $237 (27%) lower.
