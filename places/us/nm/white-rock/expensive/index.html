Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in White Rock is $1,253. It is about $311 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $191 (17%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is approximately $380 (43%) higher.
