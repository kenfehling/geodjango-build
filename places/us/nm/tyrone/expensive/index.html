Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tyrone is $131,500. This is about $20,940 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $86,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $36,300 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Deming.

Rents

Tyrone's median rent is $930 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $105 (12%) higher. It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Deming it is about $412 (79%) higher.
