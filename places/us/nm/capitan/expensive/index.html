Home prices

The median home cost in Capitan is $152,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $44,920 (41%) higher. It is about $64,600 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $47,800 (45%) higher compared to the city of Roswell.

Rents

The median rent in Capitan is $795 per month. This is about $244 (44%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $267 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roswell it is about $17 (2%) higher.
