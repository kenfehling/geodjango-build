Compared to the surrounding areas, Aztec is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Farmington, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Aztec is $155,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $9,480 (6%) higher. It is about $62,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $31,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Farmington.

Rents

The median rent in Aztec is $781 a month. It is approximately $239 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $281 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Farmington it is approximately $87 (10%) lower.
