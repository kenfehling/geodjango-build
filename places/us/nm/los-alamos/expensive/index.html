Home prices

Los Alamos's median home price is $308,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $57,960 (23%) higher.It is about $90,600 (41%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $109,900 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Los Alamos is $1,042. This is approximately $68 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $20 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is approximately $169 (19%) higher.
