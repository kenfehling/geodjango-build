Home prices

The median home in Los Alamos costs $308,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $57,960 (23%) higher.It is approximately $90,600 (41%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is about $109,900 (55%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Los Alamos is $1,042. This is about $68 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $20 (1%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $169 (19%) higher.
