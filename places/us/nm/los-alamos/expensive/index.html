In comparison to the surrounding areas, Los Alamos is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Home prices

The median home in Los Alamos is valued at $308,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $57,960 (23%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $90,600 (41%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $109,900 (55%) higher.

Rents

Los Alamos's median rent is $1,042 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $68 (7%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $20 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is roughly $169 (19%) higher.
