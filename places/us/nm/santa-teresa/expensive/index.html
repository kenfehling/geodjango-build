Home prices

The median home in Santa Teresa is valued at $186,300. This is approximately $94,500 (102%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $31,200 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of El Paso is about $58,900 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Santa Teresa the median monthly rent is $849. It is approximately $98 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $213 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $12 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of El Paso.
