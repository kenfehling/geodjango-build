Home prices

The value of the median home in Santa Clara is $79,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $42,060 (34%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $138,500 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of Deming is roughly $16,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Santa Clara the median monthly rent is $714. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $154 (17%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $348 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Deming it is about $196 (37%) higher.
