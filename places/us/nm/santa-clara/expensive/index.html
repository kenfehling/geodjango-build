Compared to its nearby areas, Santa Clara is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Deming, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Santa Clara is $79,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $42,060 (34%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $138,500 (63%) lower, and it is approximately $16,200 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Deming.

Rents

Santa Clara's median rent is $714 per month. This is approximately $154 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $348 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Deming is approximately $196 (37%) higher.
