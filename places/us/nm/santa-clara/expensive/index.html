In comparison to other places in this area, Santa Clara is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Deming.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Clara costs $79,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $42,060 (34%) lower.It is about $138,500 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Deming is about $16,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Santa Clara the median monthly rent is $714. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $154 (17%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and is approximately $196 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Deming.
