Compared to other places in this area, Santa Clara is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Deming, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Santa Clara is $79,000. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $42,060 (34%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $138,500 (63%) lower, and it is about $16,200 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Deming.

Rents

Santa Clara's median monthly rent is $714. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $154 (17%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $348 (32%) lower, and it is about $196 (37%) higher compared to the city of Deming.
