Compared to other places in this area, Santa Clara is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Deming, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Santa Clara's median home price is $79,000. It is about $42,060 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $138,500 (63%) lower, and is approximately $16,200 (17%) lower compared to the city of Deming.

Rents

Santa Clara's median monthly rent is $714. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $154 (17%) lower. It is approximately $348 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $196 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Deming.
