Compared to other places in this area, El Cerro is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Albuquerque, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

El Cerro's median home cost is $185,300. This is roughly $39,600 (27%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $32,200 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $12,900 (6%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median monthly rent in El Cerro is $938. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $46 (5%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $65 (7%) higher.
