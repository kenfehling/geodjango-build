Compared to its surrounding areas, Red River is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Santa Fe, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Red River's median home cost is $259,700. This is approximately $39,380 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $42,200 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $21,100 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Fe.

Rents

In Red River the median monthly rent is $792. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $116 (17%) higher.It is about $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Santa Fe it is about $288 (26%) lower.
