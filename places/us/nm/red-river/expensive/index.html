Home prices

The price of the median home in Red River is $259,700. It is approximately $39,380 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $42,200 (19%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $21,100 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Fe.

Rents

Red River's median rent is $792 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $116 (17%) higher. It is approximately $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Santa Fe it is roughly $288 (26%) lower.
