In comparison to its surrounding areas, Silver City is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Deming, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Silver City is valued at $134,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $24,420 (22%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $83,100 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Deming is approximately $39,200 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Silver City is $666 a month. This is roughly $211 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $396 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $148 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Deming.
