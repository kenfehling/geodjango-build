In comparison to the surrounding areas, Silver City is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Deming however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Silver City is valued at $134,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24,420 (22%) higher. It is approximately $83,100 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Deming it is roughly $39,200 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Silver City is $666. It is about $211 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $396 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Deming is about $148 (28%) higher.
