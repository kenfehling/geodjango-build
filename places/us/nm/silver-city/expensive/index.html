Home prices

The median home in Silver City costs $134,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $24,420 (22%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $83,100 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Deming is about $39,200 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Silver City is $666. This is about $211 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $396 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $148 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Deming.
