Home prices

The value of the median home in Clayton is $77,500. This is roughly $13,080 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $140,000 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dalhart is approximately $33,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Clayton the median monthly rent is $556. This is about $39 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $506 (47%) lower, and it is roughly $239 (30%) lower compared to the city of Dalhart.
