Home prices

Rents

Clayton's median monthly rent is $556. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $39 (7%) higher. It is approximately $506 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $239 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Dalhart.
