Rents

In Clayton the median monthly rent is $556. This is roughly $39 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $506 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $239 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Dalhart.
