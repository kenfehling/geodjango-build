Home prices

The median home price in Clayton is $77,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $13,080 (20%) higher. It is roughly $140,000 (64%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Dalhart it is about $33,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

Clayton's median rent is $556 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $39 (7%) higher. It is approximately $506 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Dalhart is about $239 (30%) lower.
