Compared to the surrounding areas, Tularosa is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of El Paso, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tularosa costs $103,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $23,820 (18%) lower.It is about $114,200 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of El Paso is about $24,100 (18%) lower.

Rents

Tularosa's median rent is $575 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (11%) lower.It is approximately $487 (45%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $262 (31%) lower compared to the city of El Paso.
