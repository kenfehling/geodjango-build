Home prices

The median home in Peralta costs $190,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $31,260 (19%) higher. It is about $27,500 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $8,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median rent in Peralta is $533 a month. This is roughly $399 (42%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $529 (49%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $340 (38%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.
