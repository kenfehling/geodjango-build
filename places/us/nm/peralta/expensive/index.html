Home prices

Peralta's median home value is $190,000. This is approximately $31,260 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $27,500 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is approximately $8,200 (4%) lower.

Rents

Peralta's median rent is $533 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $399 (42%) lower.It is roughly $529 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is roughly $340 (38%) lower.
