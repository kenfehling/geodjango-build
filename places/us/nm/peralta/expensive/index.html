Home prices

Rents

In Peralta the median monthly rent is $533. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $399 (42%) lower.It is about $529 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $340 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.
