Home prices

The price of the median home in Mesilla is $213,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $102,860 (92%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $4,000 (1%) lower, and it is approximately $86,100 (67%) higher in comparison to the city of El Paso.

Rents

The median rent in Mesilla is $727 per month. It is roughly $92 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $335 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of El Paso is roughly $110 (13%) lower.
