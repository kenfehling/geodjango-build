Home prices

The median home in Mesilla costs $213,500. This is about $102,860 (92%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $4,000 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of El Paso it is roughly $86,100 (67%) higher.

Rents

Mesilla's median monthly rent is $727. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $92 (14%) higher.It is approximately $335 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of El Paso it is approximately $110 (13%) lower.
