Home prices

The median home in Spencerville is valued at $137,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $12,240 (8%) lower.It is about $80,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Farmington is approximately $49,600 (26%) lower.

Rents

Spencerville's median monthly rent is $1,279. It is roughly $359 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $217 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $411 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Farmington.
