Compared to its surrounding areas, San Ysidro is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Albuquerque, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in San Ysidro is $119,600. It is approximately $30,680 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $97,900 (45%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $78,600 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median monthly rent in San Ysidro is $878. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $200 (29%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $184 (17%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.
