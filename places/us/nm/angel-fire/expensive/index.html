Home prices

The median home in Angel Fire costs $324,600. This is about $117,260 (56%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $107,100 (49%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Santa Fe is about $43,800 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Angel Fire the median rent is $629 a month. It is approximately $80 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $433 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Santa Fe is approximately $451 (41%) lower.
