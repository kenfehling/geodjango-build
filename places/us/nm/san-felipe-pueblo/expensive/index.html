Home prices

The median home in San Felipe Pueblo is valued at $147,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $60,300 (28%) lower. It is about $69,600 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $50,300 (25%) lower.

Rents

In San Felipe Pueblo the median rent is $625 a month. It is roughly $184 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $437 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $248 (28%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.
