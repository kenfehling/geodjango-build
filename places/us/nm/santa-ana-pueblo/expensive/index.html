Home prices

Santa Ana Pueblo's median home price is $91,000. It is approximately $123,900 (57%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $126,500 (58%) lower, and is roughly $107,200 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Ana Pueblo is $813. It is about $38 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque is roughly $60 (6%) lower.
