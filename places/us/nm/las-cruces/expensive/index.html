Compared to its surrounding areas, Las Cruces is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of El Paso, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Las Cruces's median home price is $155,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $32,900 (26%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $62,300 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of El Paso is approximately $27,800 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Las Cruces the median monthly rent is $788. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $165 (26%) higher. It is about $274 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $49 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.
