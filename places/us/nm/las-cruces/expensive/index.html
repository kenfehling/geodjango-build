Home prices

The value of the median home in Las Cruces is $155,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $32,900 (26%) higher. It is approximately $62,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $27,800 (21%) higher compared to the city of El Paso.

Rents

In Las Cruces the median monthly rent is $788. This is about $165 (26%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of El Paso it is roughly $49 (5%) lower.
