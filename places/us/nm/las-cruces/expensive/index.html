In comparison to its surrounding areas, Las Cruces is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of El Paso.

Home prices

The median home price in Las Cruces is $155,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $32,900 (26%) higher.It is roughly $62,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of El Paso is approximately $27,800 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Las Cruces is $788. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $165 (26%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of El Paso is about $49 (5%) lower.
