Home prices

The price of the median home in San Ildefonso Pueblo is $262,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $23,280 (9%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $44,600 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Albuquerque is about $63,900 (32%) higher.

Rents

San Ildefonso Pueblo's median rent is $1,071 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $268 (33%) higher. It is is very similar compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Albuquerque is about $198 (22%) higher.
