Home prices

The median home in San Ildefonso Pueblo is valued at $262,100. This is about $23,280 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $44,600 (20%) higher, and is about $63,900 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

In San Ildefonso Pueblo the median rent is $1,071 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $268 (33%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and is about $198 (22%) higher compared to the city of Albuquerque.
