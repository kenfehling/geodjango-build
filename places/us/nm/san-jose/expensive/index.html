Home prices

San Jose's median home price is $151,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $22,220 (12%) lower. It is approximately $65,700 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $46,400 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

San Jose's median rent is $669 per month. It is about $22 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $393 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $204 (23%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.
