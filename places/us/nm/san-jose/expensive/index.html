Compared to the surrounding areas, San Jose is significantly less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Albuquerque, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Jose is valued at $151,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $380,740 (71%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $65,700 (30%) lower, and is about $46,400 (23%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

In San Jose the median monthly rent is $669. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar.It is approximately $393 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $204 (23%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.
