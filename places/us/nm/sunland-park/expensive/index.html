Much like its surrounding areas, it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of El Paso, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sunland Park is $111,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $4,260 (3%) higher. It is approximately $106,400 (48%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of El Paso it is roughly $16,300 (12%) lower.

Rents

Sunland Park's median rent is $623 per month. This is roughly $173 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $439 (41%) lower, and is roughly $214 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.
