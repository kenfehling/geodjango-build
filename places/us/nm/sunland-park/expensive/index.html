Similar to other places in this area, it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of El Paso, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sunland Park is $111,100. This is approximately $4,260 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $106,400 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $16,300 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.

Rents

Sunland Park's median rent is $623 per month. This is about $173 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $439 (41%) lower, and is roughly $214 (25%) lower compared to the city of El Paso.
