Compared to other places in this area, Corrales is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Albuquerque, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corrales costs $447,600. This is approximately $255,580 (133%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $230,100 (105%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Albuquerque it is about $249,400 (125%) higher.

Rents

In Corrales the median rent is $1,094 per month. It is roughly $76 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $32 (3%) higher, and is roughly $221 (25%) higher compared to the city of Albuquerque.
