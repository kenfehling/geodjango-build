Home prices

Rents

In Bloomfield the median monthly rent is $760. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $264 (25%) lower.It is roughly $302 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $108 (12%) lower compared to the city of Farmington.
