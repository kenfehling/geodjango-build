Compared to its surrounding areas, Albuquerque is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Albuquerque is $198,200. It is about $51,683 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $19,300 (8%) lower compared to the entire country.

Rents

Albuquerque's median rent is $873 per month. This is roughly $104 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $189 (17%) lower.
