In comparison to its surrounding areas, Albuquerque is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Albuquerque is valued at $198,200. This is roughly $51,683 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $19,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Albuquerque is $873. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $104 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $189 (17%) lower.
