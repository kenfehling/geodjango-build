Compared to other places in this area, Las Vegas is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Santa Fe, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Las Vegas is $117,600. This is roughly $169,160 (58%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $99,900 (45%) lower, and it is about $163,200 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Fe.

Rents

Las Vegas's median monthly rent is $609. It is about $506 (45%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $453 (42%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Santa Fe it is roughly $471 (43%) lower.
