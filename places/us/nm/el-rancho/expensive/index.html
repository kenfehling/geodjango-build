Home prices

The value of the median home in El Rancho is $223,900. This is about $22,560 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $6,400 (2%) higher, and it is about $25,700 (12%) higher compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median monthly rent in El Rancho is $857. This is approximately $11 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $205 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is roughly $16 (1%) lower.
