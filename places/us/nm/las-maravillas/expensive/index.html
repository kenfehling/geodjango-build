Home prices

The median home in Las Maravillas is valued at $139,500. This is approximately $12,160 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $78,000 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $58,700 (29%) lower compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median rent in Las Maravillas is $1,135 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $238 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $73 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is approximately $262 (30%) higher.
