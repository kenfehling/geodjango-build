Compared to other places in this area, Las Maravillas is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Las Maravillas is $139,500. It is approximately $12,160 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $78,000 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Albuquerque it is roughly $58,700 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Las Maravillas is $1,135. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $238 (26%) higher. It is roughly $73 (6%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Albuquerque is approximately $262 (30%) higher.
