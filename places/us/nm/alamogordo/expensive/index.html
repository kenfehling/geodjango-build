In comparison to other places in this area, Alamogordo is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of El Paso.

Home prices

The median home in Alamogordo is valued at $113,500. It is about $32,560 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $104,000 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $13,900 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.

Rents

In Alamogordo the median rent is $791 a month. This is approximately $128 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $271 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of El Paso is roughly $46 (5%) lower.
