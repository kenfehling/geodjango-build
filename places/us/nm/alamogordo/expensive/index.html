Home prices

The median home in Alamogordo is valued at $113,500. This is roughly $32,560 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $104,000 (47%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of El Paso it is about $13,900 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Alamogordo the median monthly rent is $791. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $128 (19%) higher. It is roughly $271 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of El Paso it is roughly $46 (5%) lower.
