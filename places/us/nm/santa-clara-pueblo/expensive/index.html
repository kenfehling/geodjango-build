Home prices

The price of the median home in Santa Clara Pueblo is $207,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $336,580 (61%) lower.It is approximately $10,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is roughly $9,200 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Clara Pueblo is $725 per month. This is roughly $12 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $337 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $148 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.
