Compared to its surrounding areas, Carlsbad is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Roswell.

Home prices

The median home in Carlsbad costs $141,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $46,520 (24%) lower.It is roughly $76,200 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $36,200 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Roswell.

Rents

Carlsbad's median rent is $1,012 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $38 (3%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $50 (4%) lower, and is roughly $234 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Roswell.
