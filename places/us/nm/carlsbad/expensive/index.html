Compared to its surrounding areas, Carlsbad is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Roswell, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Carlsbad's median home cost is $141,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $46,520 (24%) lower.It is approximately $76,200 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Roswell it is roughly $36,200 (34%) higher.

Rents

In Carlsbad the median rent is $1,012 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $38 (3%) higher.It is approximately $50 (4%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $234 (30%) higher compared to the city of Roswell.
