In comparison to other places in this area, Carlsbad is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Roswell, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Carlsbad costs $141,300. This is roughly $46,520 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $76,200 (35%) lower, and is approximately $36,200 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Roswell.

Rents

The median rent in Carlsbad is $1,012 per month. It is about $38 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $50 (4%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Roswell it is roughly $234 (30%) higher.
