Compared to nearby areas, Carlsbad is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Roswell.

Home prices

The median home in Carlsbad costs $141,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $46,520 (24%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $76,200 (35%) lower, and is about $36,200 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Roswell.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carlsbad is $1,012. This is roughly $38 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $50 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Roswell is approximately $234 (30%) higher.
