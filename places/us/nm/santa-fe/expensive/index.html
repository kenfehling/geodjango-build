Compared to its surrounding areas, Santa Fe is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Albuquerque, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Fe costs $280,800. This is approximately $24,760 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $63,300 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Albuquerque it is approximately $82,600 (41%) higher.

Rents

In Santa Fe the median rent is $1,080 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $51 (4%) higher. It is approximately $18 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is about $207 (23%) higher.
