Compared to its nearby areas, Santa Fe is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Albuquerque however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Santa Fe is $280,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $24,760 (8%) lower. It is about $63,300 (29%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $82,600 (41%) higher compared to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

Santa Fe's median rent is $1,080 a month. It is approximately $51 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $18 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $207 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.
