Compared to its surrounding areas, Santa Fe is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Albuquerque, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Santa Fe's median home cost is $280,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $24,760 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $63,300 (29%) higher, and it is about $82,600 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

In Santa Fe the median monthly rent is $1,080. This is roughly $51 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $18 (1%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albuquerque it is about $207 (23%) higher.
