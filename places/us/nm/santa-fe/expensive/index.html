In comparison to other places in this area, Santa Fe is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Albuquerque, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Fe costs $280,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $24,760 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $63,300 (29%) higher, and it is approximately $82,600 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Albuquerque.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Fe is $1,080 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $51 (4%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $18 (1%) higher, and it is approximately $207 (23%) higher compared to the city of Albuquerque.
