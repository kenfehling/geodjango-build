Home prices

Bonner Springs's median home cost is $167,800. This is about $18,880 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $49,700 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $13,200 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bonner Springs is $936 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $55 (6%) higher. It is about $126 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is is very similar.
