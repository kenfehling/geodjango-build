Compared to other places in this area, Bonner Springs is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bonner Springs is $167,800. It is roughly $18,880 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $49,700 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $13,200 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bonner Springs is $936. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $55 (6%) higher. It is roughly $126 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
