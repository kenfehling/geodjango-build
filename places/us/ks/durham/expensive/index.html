Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $50,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $27,520 (35%) lower. It is about $167,500 (77%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $83,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Durham's median monthly rent is $670. This is about $83 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $392 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Wichita is approximately $139 (17%) lower.
