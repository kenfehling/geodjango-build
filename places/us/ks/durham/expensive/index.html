Home prices

The median home in Durham costs $50,000. This is about $27,520 (35%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $167,500 (77%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is approximately $83,400 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Durham the median rent is $670 a month. This is roughly $83 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $392 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Wichita it is approximately $139 (17%) lower.
