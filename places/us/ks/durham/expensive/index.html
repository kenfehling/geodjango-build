Home prices

The median home value in Durham is $50,000. This is roughly $27,520 (35%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $167,500 (77%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita is roughly $83,400 (62%) lower.

Rents

Durham's median monthly rent is $670. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $83 (14%) higher.It is roughly $392 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $139 (17%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
