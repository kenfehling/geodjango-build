Home prices

The median home price in Lakin is $115,500. It is roughly $23,000 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $102,000 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $6,400 (5%) higher compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

In Lakin the median rent is $624 a month. It is roughly $125 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $438 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Dodge City it is approximately $161 (20%) lower.
