Home prices

The median home in Burden is valued at $62,600. This is approximately $3,420 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $154,900 (71%) lower, and is about $70,800 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burden is $694. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $18 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $368 (34%) lower, and is about $115 (14%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
