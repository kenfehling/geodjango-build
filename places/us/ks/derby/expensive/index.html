Home prices

The median home in Derby costs $165,200. This is roughly $40,420 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $52,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $31,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median rent in Derby is $951 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $28 (2%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $111 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is about $142 (17%) higher.
