Home prices

Edgerton's median home value is $131,900. This is about $54,540 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $85,600 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $22,700 (14%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Edgerton the median rent is $938 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $27 (3%) higher. It is approximately $124 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Kansas City.
