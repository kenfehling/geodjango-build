Similar to other places in this area, it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Olathe is $238,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $6,780 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $20,600 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $83,500 (54%) higher.

Rents

Olathe's median monthly rent is $1,019. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $43 (4%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $78 (8%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
