Home prices

The median home price in Olathe is $238,100. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $6,780 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $20,600 (9%) higher, and it is roughly $83,500 (54%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Olathe's median monthly rent is $1,019. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $43 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $78 (8%) higher.
