Much like the surrounding areas, it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Olathe is valued at $238,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $6,780 (2%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $20,600 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $83,500 (54%) higher.

Rents

Olathe's median rent is $1,019 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is about $43 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $78 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
