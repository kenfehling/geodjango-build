Home prices

The median home in Olathe is valued at $238,100. This is roughly $6,780 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $20,600 (9%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $83,500 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Olathe is $1,019 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is is very similar.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $43 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $78 (8%) higher.
