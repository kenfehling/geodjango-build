Home prices

The median home in Frontenac is valued at $131,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $51,060 (63%) higher. It is about $86,100 (39%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $46,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Rogers.

Rents

The median rent in Frontenac is $804 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $181 (29%) higher. It is roughly $258 (24%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $94 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Rogers.
