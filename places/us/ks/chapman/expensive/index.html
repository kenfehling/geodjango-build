In comparison to its surrounding areas, Chapman is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Wichita however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Chapman is $156,900. This is approximately $71,440 (83%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $60,600 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $23,500 (17%) higher.

Rents

Chapman's median rent is $857 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is about $205 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Wichita is roughly $48 (5%) higher.
