Home prices

The cost of the median home in Holton is $116,700. It is about $49,020 (72%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $100,800 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $37,900 (24%) lower.

Rents

Holton's median rent is $550 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $130 (19%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $512 (48%) lower, and it is about $391 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
