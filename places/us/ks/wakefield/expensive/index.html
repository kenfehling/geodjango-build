In comparison to other places in this area, Wakefield is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Topeka.

Home prices

Wakefield's median home price is $136,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $27,540 (25%) higher. It is approximately $80,700 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $33,600 (32%) higher compared to the city of Topeka.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wakefield is $878. This is about $208 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $184 (17%) lower, and is about $62 (7%) higher compared to the city of Topeka.
