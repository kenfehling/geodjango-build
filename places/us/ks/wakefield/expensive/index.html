Home prices

Rents

In Wakefield the median monthly rent is $878. This is approximately $208 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $184 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Topeka is approximately $62 (7%) higher.
