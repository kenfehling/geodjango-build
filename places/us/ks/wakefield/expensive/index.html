In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wakefield is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Topeka.

Home prices

The median home price in Wakefield is $136,800. This is approximately $27,540 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $80,700 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Topeka is roughly $33,600 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Wakefield the median rent is $878 a month. This is roughly $208 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $184 (17%) lower, and is about $62 (7%) higher compared to the city of Topeka.
