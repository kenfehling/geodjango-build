In comparison to other places in this area, Wakefield is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Topeka however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wakefield is valued at $136,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $27,540 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $80,700 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Topeka is about $33,600 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Wakefield the median rent is $878 per month. This is roughly $208 (30%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $184 (17%) lower, and is approximately $62 (7%) higher compared to the city of Topeka.
