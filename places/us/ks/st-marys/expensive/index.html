Home prices

The median home in St. Marys costs $151,100. This is about $40,200 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $66,400 (30%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $55,500 (58%) higher.

Rents

In St. Marys the median monthly rent is $723. This is about $155 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $339 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $162 (18%) lower.
