Home prices

The median home in St. Marys costs $151,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $40,200 (36%) higher.It is roughly $66,400 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $55,500 (58%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in St. Marys is $723 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $155 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $339 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $162 (18%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
