In comparison to other places in this area, Marion is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Wichita, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Marion is $71,400. This is approximately $10,380 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $146,100 (67%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is roughly $62,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Marion the median rent is $606 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $40 (7%) higher. It is roughly $456 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $203 (25%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
