Home prices

Kansas City's median home value is $95,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $119,040 (55%) lower. It is approximately $121,900 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $59,000 (38%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Kansas City the median monthly rent is $885. It is approximately $88 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $177 (16%) lower, and is roughly $56 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
