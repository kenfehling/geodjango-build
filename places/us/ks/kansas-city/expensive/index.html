Home prices

Kansas City's median home cost is $95,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $119,040 (55%) lower.It is approximately $121,900 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $59,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Kansas City the median monthly rent is $885. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $88 (11%) higher.It is about $177 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $56 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
