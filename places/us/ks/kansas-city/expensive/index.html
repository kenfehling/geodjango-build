Home prices

Kansas City's median home cost is $95,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $119,040 (55%) lower.It is approximately $121,900 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $59,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Kansas City's median monthly rent is $885. It is roughly $88 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $177 (16%) lower, and it is about $56 (5%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
