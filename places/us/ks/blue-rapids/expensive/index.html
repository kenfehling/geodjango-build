Compared to its surrounding areas, Blue Rapids is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Lincoln, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Blue Rapids is valued at $80,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $13,680 (20%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $136,600 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lincoln it is about $88,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blue Rapids is $599. This is about $53 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $463 (43%) lower, and is about $253 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Lincoln.
