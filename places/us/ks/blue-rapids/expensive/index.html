Home prices

The median home in Blue Rapids costs $80,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $13,680 (20%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $136,600 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lincoln is roughly $88,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blue Rapids is $599. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $53 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $463 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lincoln it is about $253 (29%) lower.
