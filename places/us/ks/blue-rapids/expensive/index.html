Home prices

The median home in Blue Rapids is valued at $80,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $13,680 (20%) higher. It is roughly $136,600 (62%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Lincoln is roughly $88,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blue Rapids is $599. It is approximately $53 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $463 (43%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $253 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Lincoln.
