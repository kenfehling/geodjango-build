Home prices

The median home in Riverton costs $87,200. This is roughly $26,080 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $130,300 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $120,400 (57%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median rent in Riverton is $607 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $168 (21%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $455 (42%) lower, and it is about $192 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
