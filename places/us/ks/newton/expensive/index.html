Home prices

The median home value in Newton is $107,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $13,440 (11%) lower. It is approximately $110,000 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $25,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newton is $747. It is about $47 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $315 (29%) lower, and is approximately $62 (7%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
