Home prices

The median home in Bucklin is valued at $84,600. It is about $3,600 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $132,900 (61%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $24,500 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

The median rent in Bucklin is $597 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $143 (19%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $465 (43%) lower, and is roughly $188 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Dodge City.
