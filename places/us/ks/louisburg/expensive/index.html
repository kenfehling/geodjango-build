Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Louisburg is $976. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $221 (29%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $86 (8%) lower, and is approximately $35 (3%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
