Home prices

The median home in Louisburg costs $181,100. This is roughly $52,180 (40%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $36,400 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $26,500 (17%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Louisburg's median monthly rent is $976. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $221 (29%) higher. It is approximately $86 (8%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $35 (3%) higher.
