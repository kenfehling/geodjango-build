Home prices

The median home in Longton costs $28,200. This is about $17,500 (38%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $189,300 (87%) lower, and it is approximately $111,700 (79%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Longton the median monthly rent is $473. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $106 (18%) lower. It is about $589 (55%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is roughly $356 (42%) lower.
