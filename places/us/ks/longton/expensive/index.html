Home prices

The price of the median home in Longton is $28,200. This is about $17,500 (38%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $189,300 (87%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is approximately $111,700 (79%) lower.

Rents

In Longton the median rent is $473 per month. It is roughly $106 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $589 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $356 (42%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
