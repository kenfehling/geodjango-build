Home prices

The median home cost in Great Bend is $95,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $38,180 (66%) higher.It is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Salina it is about $33,500 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Great Bend the median rent is $655 per month. This is roughly $21 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $407 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Salina it is about $103 (13%) lower.
