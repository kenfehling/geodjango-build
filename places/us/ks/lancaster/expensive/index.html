Home prices

The median home cost in Lancaster is $85,000. It is about $10,700 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $132,500 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $69,600 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $713. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $122 (20%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $349 (32%) lower, and it is about $228 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
