Home prices

The median home in Lancaster is valued at $85,000. It is approximately $10,700 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $132,500 (60%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $69,600 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lancaster is $713. Compared to other places in this area this is about $122 (20%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $228 (24%) lower.
