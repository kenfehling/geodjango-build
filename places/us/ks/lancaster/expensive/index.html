In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lancaster is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $85,000. This is approximately $10,700 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $132,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $69,600 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lancaster is $713. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $122 (20%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and it is about $228 (24%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
