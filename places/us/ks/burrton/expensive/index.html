Home prices

The median home price in Burrton is $70,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $18,700 (20%) lower. It is approximately $146,700 (67%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $62,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

Burrton's median rent is $643 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $87 (11%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $419 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is approximately $166 (20%) lower.
