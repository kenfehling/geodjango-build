Home prices

The price of the median home in Wamego is $160,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $29,420 (22%) higher. It is approximately $56,800 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $57,500 (55%) higher compared to the city of Topeka.

Rents

In Wamego the median monthly rent is $890. It is about $158 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $172 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Topeka is approximately $74 (9%) higher.
