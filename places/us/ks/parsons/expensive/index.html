Compared to its surrounding areas, Parsons is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Tulsa, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Parsons costs $65,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $4,640 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $152,500 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $74,900 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Parsons the median monthly rent is $653. This is roughly $59 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $409 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $176 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
