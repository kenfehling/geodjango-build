Home prices

Parsons's median home cost is $65,000. It is roughly $4,640 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $152,500 (70%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $74,900 (53%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Parsons's median rent is $653 per month. This is approximately $59 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $409 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $176 (21%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
