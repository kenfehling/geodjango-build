Rents

The median monthly rent in Lincoln Center is $508. This is approximately $29 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $554 (52%) lower, and it is approximately $250 (32%) lower compared to the city of Salina.
