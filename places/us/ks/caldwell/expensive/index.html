Home prices

The median home value in Caldwell is $48,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $14,880 (23%) lower.It is approximately $169,300 (77%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Wichita is approximately $85,200 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Caldwell is $509. It is roughly $127 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $553 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is roughly $300 (37%) lower.
