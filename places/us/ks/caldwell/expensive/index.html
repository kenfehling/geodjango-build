Home prices

The median home in Caldwell is valued at $48,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $14,880 (23%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $169,300 (77%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita is approximately $85,200 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Caldwell the median rent is $509 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $127 (19%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $553 (52%) lower, and it is about $300 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
