Home prices

The median home cost in Oakley is $101,000. This is about $11,220 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $116,500 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Garden City is approximately $48,300 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Oakley the median rent is $687 per month. This is about $124 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $375 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $127 (15%) lower compared to the city of Garden City.
