Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marquette is $85,600. This is roughly $31,640 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $131,900 (60%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is about $47,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

Marquette's median monthly rent is $669. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $78 (10%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $393 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $140 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
