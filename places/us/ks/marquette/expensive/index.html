In comparison to the surrounding areas, Marquette is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Wichita, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marquette costs $85,600. It is approximately $31,640 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $131,900 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $47,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

Marquette's median monthly rent is $669. It is about $78 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $393 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $140 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
