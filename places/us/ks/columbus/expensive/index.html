Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $66,200. This is approximately $2,020 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $151,300 (69%) lower, and it is about $141,400 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

Columbus's median monthly rent is $532. Compared to nearby areas this is about $103 (16%) lower. It is approximately $530 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $267 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
