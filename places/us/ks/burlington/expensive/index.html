In comparison to other places in this area, Burlington is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Overland Park, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Burlington's median home price is $100,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $20,980 (26%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $116,900 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Overland Park is approximately $175,500 (63%) lower.

Rents

Burlington's median rent is $644 a month. This is approximately $42 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $418 (39%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $520 (44%) lower compared to the city of Overland Park.
