Home prices

The median home in Burlington is valued at $100,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $20,980 (26%) higher.It is about $116,900 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Overland Park it is approximately $175,500 (63%) lower.

Rents

Burlington's median rent is $644 a month. It is approximately $42 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $418 (39%) lower, and is roughly $520 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Overland Park.
