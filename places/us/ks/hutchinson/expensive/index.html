In comparison to nearby areas, Hutchinson is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hutchinson is $96,300. It is approximately $21,620 (28%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $121,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $37,100 (27%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

In Hutchinson the median rent is $713 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $48 (7%) higher. It is approximately $349 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $96 (11%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
