Home prices

The median home value in Hutchinson is $96,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $21,620 (28%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $121,200 (55%) lower, and it is about $37,100 (27%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

In Hutchinson the median rent is $713 a month. It is roughly $48 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $349 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $96 (11%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
