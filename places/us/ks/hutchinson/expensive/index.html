Compared to other places in this area, Hutchinson is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Wichita, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hutchinson is $96,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $21,620 (28%) higher. It is about $121,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Wichita it is about $37,100 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Hutchinson the median monthly rent is $713. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $48 (7%) higher. It is about $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $96 (11%) lower.
