In comparison to its surrounding areas, Coffeyville is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tulsa, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Coffeyville is $55,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $7,560 (11%) lower. It is approximately $161,700 (74%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is about $84,100 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Coffeyville is $614 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $78 (11%) lower. It is approximately $448 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $215 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
