Home prices

The median home in Pittsburg is valued at $88,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $11,560 (15%) higher. It is about $129,000 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $89,500 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Rogers.

Rents

In Pittsburg the median monthly rent is $712. This is approximately $103 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $350 (32%) lower, and is approximately $186 (20%) lower compared to the city of Rogers.
