Home prices

Rents

In Pittsburg the median rent is $712 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $103 (16%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $350 (32%) lower, and is about $186 (20%) lower compared to the city of Rogers.
