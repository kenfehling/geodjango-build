Compared to the surrounding areas, Pittsburg is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Rogers, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pittsburg is valued at $88,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $11,560 (15%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $129,000 (59%) lower, and it is about $89,500 (50%) lower compared to the city of Rogers.

Rents

In Pittsburg the median monthly rent is $712. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $103 (16%) higher.It is approximately $350 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Rogers it is about $186 (20%) lower.
