In comparison to the surrounding areas, Scranton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Scranton's median home price is $80,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $8,040 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $136,800 (62%) lower, and it is roughly $73,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Scranton the median monthly rent is $717. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $99 (15%) higher. It is approximately $345 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $224 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
