Compared to the surrounding areas, Williamsburg is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

Williamsburg's median home value is $81,700. This is approximately $6,040 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $135,800 (62%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $72,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Williamsburg the median rent is $831 per month. This is about $131 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $231 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $110 (11%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
