Home prices

The median home in Leawood is valued at $463,200. It is about $225,640 (94%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $245,700 (112%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $308,600 (199%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Leawood is $1,619. This is approximately $467 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $557 (52%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $678 (72%) higher.
