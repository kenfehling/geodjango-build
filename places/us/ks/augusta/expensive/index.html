Compared to its surrounding areas, Augusta is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Wichita, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Augusta is valued at $125,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $9,440 (8%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $91,600 (42%) lower, and is roughly $7,500 (5%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Augusta's median rent is $830 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $59 (6%) lower. It is approximately $232 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Wichita is about $21 (2%) higher.
