Home prices

The median home in Augusta costs $125,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $9,440 (8%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $91,600 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita is roughly $7,500 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Augusta is $830. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $59 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $232 (21%) lower, and is approximately $21 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
