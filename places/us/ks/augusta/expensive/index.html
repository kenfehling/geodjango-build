Compared to other places in this area, Augusta is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Wichita however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Augusta is valued at $125,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $9,440 (8%) higher.It is roughly $91,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $7,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

In Augusta the median rent is $830 per month. This is about $59 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $232 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $21 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
