In comparison to its surrounding areas, Augusta is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The median home price in Augusta is $125,900. This is approximately $9,440 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $91,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $7,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Augusta is $830. It is about $59 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $232 (21%) lower, and is roughly $21 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
