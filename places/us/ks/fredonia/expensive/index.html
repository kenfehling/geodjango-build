Home prices

The median home in Fredonia costs $74,000. This is roughly $25,120 (51%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $143,500 (65%) lower, and it is approximately $65,900 (47%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Fredonia's median monthly rent is $695. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $128 (22%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $367 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa is about $134 (16%) lower.
