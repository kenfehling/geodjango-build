Home prices

The median home in Kechi costs $190,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $40,540 (27%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $27,500 (12%) lower, and is about $56,600 (42%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Kechi's median rent is $1,551 a month. This is about $617 (66%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $489 (46%) higher, and is roughly $742 (91%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.
