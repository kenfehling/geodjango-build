Home prices

The median home cost in Kechi is $190,000. This is approximately $40,540 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $27,500 (12%) lower, and it is roughly $56,600 (42%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median rent in Kechi is $1,551 a month. It is about $617 (66%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $489 (46%) higher, and is about $742 (91%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
