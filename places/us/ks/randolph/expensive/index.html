Compared to the surrounding areas, Randolph is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Lincoln however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Randolph is $116,700. This is about $11,280 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $100,800 (46%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lincoln it is approximately $53,100 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Randolph is $788 per month. This is about $82 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lincoln is approximately $64 (7%) lower.
