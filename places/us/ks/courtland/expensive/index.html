Home prices

The cost of the median home in Courtland is $56,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $11,300 (25%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $161,200 (74%) lower, and it is approximately $84,500 (60%) lower compared to the city of Grand Island.

Rents

The median rent in Courtland is $576 per month. It is about $104 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $486 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Island it is approximately $191 (24%) lower.
