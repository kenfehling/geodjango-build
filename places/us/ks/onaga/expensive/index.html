Home prices

The value of the median home in Onaga is $65,400. It is about $20,500 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $152,100 (69%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Topeka is about $37,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Onaga is $595 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $29 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $467 (43%) lower, and it is about $221 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Topeka.
