Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atchison is $615. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $37 (5%) lower. It is about $447 (42%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $326 (34%) lower.
