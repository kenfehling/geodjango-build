Home prices

The median home value in Johnson City is $75,200. This is roughly $7,980 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $142,300 (65%) lower, and is about $74,100 (49%) lower compared to the city of Garden City.

Rents

In Johnson City the median monthly rent is $720. Compared to other places in this area this is about $22 (3%) higher.It is about $342 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $94 (11%) lower compared to the city of Garden City.
