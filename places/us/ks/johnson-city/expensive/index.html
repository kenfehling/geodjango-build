Home prices

The median home value in Johnson City is $75,200. It is approximately $7,980 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $142,300 (65%) lower, and it is roughly $74,100 (49%) lower compared to the city of Garden City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Johnson City is $720. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $22 (3%) higher. It is approximately $342 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $94 (11%) lower compared to the city of Garden City.
