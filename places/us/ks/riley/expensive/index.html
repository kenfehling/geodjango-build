Home prices

The median home cost in Riley is $139,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $26,940 (23%) higher. It is about $77,700 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Topeka it is approximately $36,600 (35%) higher.

Rents

In Riley the median monthly rent is $810. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $33 (4%) higher. It is roughly $252 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Topeka it is is very similar.
