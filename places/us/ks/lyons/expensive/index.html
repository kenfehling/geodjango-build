Home prices

Lyons's median home cost is $71,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $17,800 (32%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $145,700 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $61,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Lyons the median rent is $588 per month. It is roughly $60 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $474 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is roughly $221 (27%) lower.
