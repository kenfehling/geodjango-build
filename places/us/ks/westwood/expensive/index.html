Compared to its surrounding areas, Westwood is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Westwood costs $250,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $38,220 (13%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $33,000 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $95,900 (62%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westwood is $1,652. This is roughly $312 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $590 (55%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $711 (75%) higher.
