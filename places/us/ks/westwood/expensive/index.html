Home prices

The median home in Westwood is valued at $250,500. It is about $38,220 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $33,000 (15%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $95,900 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Westwood is $1,652 per month. It is about $312 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $590 (55%) higher, and it is roughly $711 (75%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
