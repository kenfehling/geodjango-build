In comparison to its nearby areas, Westwood is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Westwood is $250,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38,220 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $33,000 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $95,900 (62%) higher.

Rents

In Westwood the median rent is $1,652 per month. This is roughly $312 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $590 (55%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $711 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
