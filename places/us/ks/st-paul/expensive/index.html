Home prices

St. Paul's median home cost is $91,000. It is roughly $28,000 (44%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $126,500 (58%) lower, and is roughly $34,900 (27%) lower compared to the city of Joplin.

Rents

In St. Paul the median monthly rent is $566. This is approximately $88 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $496 (46%) lower, and is about $223 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Joplin.
