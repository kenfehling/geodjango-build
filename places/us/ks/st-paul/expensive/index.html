Home prices

The median home price in St. Paul is $91,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $28,000 (44%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $126,500 (58%) lower, and is approximately $34,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Joplin.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Paul is $566. This is roughly $88 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $496 (46%) lower, and is about $223 (28%) lower compared to the city of Joplin.
