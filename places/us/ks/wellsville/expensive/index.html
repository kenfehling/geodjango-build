Home prices

The value of the median home in Wellsville is $130,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $4,320 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $87,100 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $24,200 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Wellsville the median rent is $663 a month. This is approximately $195 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $278 (29%) lower.
