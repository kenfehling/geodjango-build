Home prices

The median home in Wellsville is valued at $130,400. This is approximately $4,320 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $87,100 (40%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $24,200 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wellsville is $663. It is roughly $195 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $278 (29%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
