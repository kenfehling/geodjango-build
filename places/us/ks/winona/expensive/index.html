Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Winona is $386 per month. It is about $233 (37%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $676 (63%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $428 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Garden City.
