In comparison to other places in this area, Benton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Benton is $140,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,680 (7%) lower. It is approximately $76,700 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $7,400 (5%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Benton's median rent is $830 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $288 (25%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $232 (21%) lower, and is approximately $21 (2%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.
