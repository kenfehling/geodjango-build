Home prices

The price of the median home in Benton is $140,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10,680 (7%) lower.It is about $76,700 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $7,400 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Benton is $830. It is roughly $288 (25%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $232 (21%) lower, and is approximately $21 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
