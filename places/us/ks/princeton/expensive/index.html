Home prices

The median home in Princeton is valued at $85,000. This is roughly $5,200 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $132,500 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $69,600 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Princeton is $596 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $115 (16%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $466 (43%) lower, and it is about $345 (36%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
