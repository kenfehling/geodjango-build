Home prices

Rents

In Uniontown the median monthly rent is $438. This is about $183 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $624 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $503 (53%) lower.
