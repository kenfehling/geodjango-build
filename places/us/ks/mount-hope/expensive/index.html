The median home in Mount Hope is valued at $108,200. This is roughly $7,740 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $109,300 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $25,200 (18%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Mount Hope's median rent is $753 per month. This is approximately $88 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $309 (29%) lower, and is approximately $56 (6%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
