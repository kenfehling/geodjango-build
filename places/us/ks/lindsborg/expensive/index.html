Home prices

The median home price in Lindsborg is $154,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $51,880 (50%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $63,200 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $20,900 (15%) higher.

Rents

Lindsborg's median monthly rent is $668. This is about $98 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $394 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $141 (17%) lower.
