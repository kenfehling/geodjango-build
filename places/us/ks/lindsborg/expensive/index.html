Home prices

Rents

In Lindsborg the median monthly rent is $668. This is about $98 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $394 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $141 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
