Home prices

The median home cost in Lindsborg is $154,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $51,880 (50%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $63,200 (29%) lower, and is about $20,900 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Lindsborg's median rent is $668 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $98 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $394 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $141 (17%) lower.
