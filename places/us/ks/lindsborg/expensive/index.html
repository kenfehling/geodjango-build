Home prices

The median home cost in Lindsborg is $154,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $51,880 (50%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $63,200 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita is approximately $20,900 (15%) higher.

Rents

Lindsborg's median rent is $668 a month. This is about $98 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $394 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is approximately $141 (17%) lower.
