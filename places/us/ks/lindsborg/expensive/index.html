Home prices

The median home value in Lindsborg is $154,300. This is approximately $51,880 (50%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $63,200 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $20,900 (15%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median rent in Lindsborg is $668 per month. It is about $98 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $394 (37%) lower, and it is about $141 (17%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
