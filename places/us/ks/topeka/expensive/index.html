Home prices

The median home in Topeka costs $103,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $8,300 (7%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $114,300 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $51,400 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Topeka the median rent is $816 a month. It is approximately $69 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $246 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $125 (13%) lower.
