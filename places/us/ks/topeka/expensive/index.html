Home prices

Topeka's median home value is $103,200. It is approximately $8,300 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $114,300 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $51,400 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Topeka the median monthly rent is $816. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $69 (9%) higher.It is about $246 (23%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $125 (13%) lower.
