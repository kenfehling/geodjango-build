Home prices

The median home in Topeka is valued at $103,200. It is approximately $8,300 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $114,300 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $51,400 (33%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Topeka the median monthly rent is $816. This is roughly $69 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $246 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $125 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
