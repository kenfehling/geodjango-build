Compared to its surrounding areas, Topeka is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Topeka's median home cost is $103,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $8,300 (7%) lower. It is roughly $114,300 (52%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $51,400 (33%) lower.

Rents

Topeka's median rent is $816 a month. This is about $69 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $246 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $125 (13%) lower.
