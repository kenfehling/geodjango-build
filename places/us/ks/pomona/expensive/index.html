Compared to its surrounding areas, Pomona is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Pomona's median home cost is $105,700. It is about $17,540 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $111,800 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $48,900 (31%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Pomona the median monthly rent is $834. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $13 (1%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $107 (11%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
