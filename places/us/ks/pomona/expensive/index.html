Compared to its surrounding areas, Pomona is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Pomona is $105,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $17,540 (19%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $111,800 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $48,900 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pomona is $834 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $13 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and is roughly $107 (11%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
