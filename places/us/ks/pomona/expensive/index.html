Compared to its surrounding areas, Pomona is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Pomona is $105,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $17,540 (19%) higher. It is roughly $111,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $48,900 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Pomona the median monthly rent is $834. Compared to other places in this area it is about $13 (1%) higher. It is about $228 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $107 (11%) lower.
