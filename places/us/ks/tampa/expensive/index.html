Home prices

Rents

In Tampa the median monthly rent is $515. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $74 (12%) lower. It is approximately $547 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $294 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
