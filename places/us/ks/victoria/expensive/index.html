Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Victoria is $809 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $98 (13%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $253 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $102 (14%) higher compared to the city of Hays.
