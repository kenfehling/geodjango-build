Home prices

Victoria's median home price is $145,400. It is about $77,300 (113%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $72,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $31,500 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Hays.

Rents

In Victoria the median rent is $809 per month. This is approximately $98 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $253 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Hays it is about $102 (14%) higher.
