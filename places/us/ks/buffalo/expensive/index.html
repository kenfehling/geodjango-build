Home prices

The median home value in Buffalo is $38,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $15,220 (28%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $178,600 (82%) lower, and is approximately $64,300 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Topeka.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Buffalo is $538. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $89 (14%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $524 (49%) lower, and is roughly $278 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Topeka.
