Home prices

The value of the median home in Buffalo is $38,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $15,220 (28%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $178,600 (82%) lower, and compared to the city of Topeka it is approximately $64,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median monthly rent is $538. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $89 (14%) lower. It is about $524 (49%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Topeka it is approximately $278 (34%) lower.
