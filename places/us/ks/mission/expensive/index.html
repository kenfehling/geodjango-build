In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mission is significantly less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mission is valued at $179,700. It is roughly $98,940 (35%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $37,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $25,100 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Mission the median rent is $984 per month. This is about $411 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $78 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $43 (4%) higher.
