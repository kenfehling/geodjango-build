Compared to other places in this area, Mission is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mission is $179,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $98,940 (35%) lower.It is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $25,100 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mission is $984 a month. This is roughly $411 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $78 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $43 (4%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
