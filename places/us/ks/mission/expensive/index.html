In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mission is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mission is valued at $179,700. This is roughly $98,940 (35%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower, and is about $25,100 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Mission's median monthly rent is $984. It is about $411 (29%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $43 (4%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
