Home prices

Mission's median home value is $179,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $98,940 (35%) lower. It is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $25,100 (16%) higher.

Rents

Mission's median monthly rent is $984. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $411 (29%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $78 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $43 (4%) higher.
