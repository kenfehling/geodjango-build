Home prices

The median home in Atlanta costs $56,900. It is roughly $12,680 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $160,600 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is approximately $76,500 (57%) lower.

Rents

Atlanta's median monthly rent is $725. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $24 (3%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita is approximately $84 (10%) lower.
