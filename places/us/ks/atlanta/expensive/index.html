Compared to its nearby areas, Atlanta is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The median home in Atlanta costs $56,900. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $12,680 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $160,600 (73%) lower, and is about $76,500 (57%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Atlanta's median rent is $725 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24 (3%) higher. It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $84 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
