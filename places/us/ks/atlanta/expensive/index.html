Compared to other places in this area, Atlanta is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Wichita, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Atlanta's median home cost is $56,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $12,680 (18%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $160,600 (73%) lower, and is about $76,500 (57%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Atlanta's median monthly rent is $725. It is approximately $24 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita it is approximately $84 (10%) lower.
