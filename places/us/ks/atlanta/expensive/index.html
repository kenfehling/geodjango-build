Home prices

Atlanta's median home price is $56,900. This is approximately $12,680 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $160,600 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $76,500 (57%) lower.

Rents

Atlanta's median monthly rent is $725. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24 (3%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $337 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $84 (10%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
