Home prices

The median home in Merriam is valued at $169,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $84,680 (33%) lower. It is about $48,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $14,800 (9%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Merriam's median rent is $945 per month. It is approximately $359 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $117 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is is very similar.
