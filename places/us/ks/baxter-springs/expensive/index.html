Home prices

The median home in Baxter Springs is valued at $68,800. It is about $36,980 (34%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $148,700 (68%) lower, and is approximately $138,800 (66%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Baxter Springs the median monthly rent is $595. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $92 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $467 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $204 (25%) lower.
