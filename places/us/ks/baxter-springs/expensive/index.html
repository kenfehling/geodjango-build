Home prices

The value of the median home in Baxter Springs is $68,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $36,980 (34%) lower.It is roughly $148,700 (68%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $138,800 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Baxter Springs the median rent is $595 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $92 (13%) lower.It is approximately $467 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $204 (25%) lower.
