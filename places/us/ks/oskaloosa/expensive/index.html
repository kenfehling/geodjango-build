Home prices

The value of the median home in Oskaloosa is $120,700. It is roughly $10,600 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $96,800 (44%) lower, and it is about $33,900 (21%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Oskaloosa the median monthly rent is $738. This is about $45 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $324 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $203 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
