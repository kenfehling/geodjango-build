Compared to other places in this area, Denton is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Denton is $68,800. This is approximately $8,000 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $148,700 (68%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $85,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

Denton's median rent is $575 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $89 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $487 (45%) lower, and it is about $366 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
