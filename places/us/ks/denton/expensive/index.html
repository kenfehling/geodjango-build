In comparison to its surrounding areas, Denton is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Denton is valued at $68,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $8,000 (13%) higher. It is about $148,700 (68%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $85,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Denton the median rent is $575 a month. This is about $89 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $366 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
