Home prices

The median home in Denton costs $68,800. It is roughly $8,000 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $148,700 (68%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $85,800 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Denton's median monthly rent is $575. It is approximately $89 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $366 (38%) lower.
