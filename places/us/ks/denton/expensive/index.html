Home prices

The median home in Denton costs $68,800. This is about $8,000 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $148,700 (68%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $85,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Denton the median rent is $575 a month. This is about $89 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $487 (45%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $366 (38%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
