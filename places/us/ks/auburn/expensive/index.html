Home prices

Auburn's median home cost is $106,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $26,180 (32%) higher. It is approximately $110,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $48,000 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Auburn the median rent is $638 a month. This is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $424 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $303 (32%) lower.
