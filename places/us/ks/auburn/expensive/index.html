Compared to its surrounding areas, Auburn is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Auburn is $106,600. It is roughly $26,180 (32%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $110,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $48,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Auburn the median rent is $638 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $12 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $424 (39%) lower, and is approximately $303 (32%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
