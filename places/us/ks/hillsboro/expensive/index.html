Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro is valued at $88,700. This is approximately $11,640 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $128,800 (59%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $44,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $606 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is is very similar. It is roughly $456 (42%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $203 (25%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
