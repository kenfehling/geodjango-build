In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hillsboro is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro costs $88,700. This is approximately $11,640 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $128,800 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $44,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Hillsboro the median rent is $606 per month. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas.It is about $456 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is about $203 (25%) lower.
