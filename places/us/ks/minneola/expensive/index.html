In comparison to other places in this area, Minneola is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Dodge City however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Minneola costs $88,900. This is roughly $24,280 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $128,600 (59%) lower, and is roughly $20,200 (18%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

Minneola's median rent is $619 per month. It is about $58 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Dodge City it is roughly $166 (21%) lower.
