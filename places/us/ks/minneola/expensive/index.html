Home prices

The value of the median home in Minneola is $88,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,280 (37%) higher.It is roughly $128,600 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dodge City is roughly $20,200 (18%) lower.

Rents

Minneola's median rent is $619 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $58 (8%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and is approximately $166 (21%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.
