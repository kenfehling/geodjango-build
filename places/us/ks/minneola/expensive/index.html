Home prices

The median home in Minneola is valued at $88,900. This is approximately $24,280 (37%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $128,600 (59%) lower, and is roughly $20,200 (18%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

In Minneola the median rent is $619 per month. This is roughly $58 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Dodge City is about $166 (21%) lower.
