Compared to nearby areas, Lenexa is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Lenexa costs $260,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $31,120 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $43,400 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $106,300 (68%) higher.

Rents

Lenexa's median rent is $1,148 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $168 (17%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $86 (8%) higher, and it is roughly $207 (21%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
