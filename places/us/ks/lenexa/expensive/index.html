In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lenexa is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lenexa is $260,900. This is about $31,120 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $43,400 (19%) higher, and is roughly $106,300 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Lenexa is $1,148 per month. It is approximately $168 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $86 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $207 (21%) higher.
