Home prices

Lenexa's median home value is $260,900. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $31,120 (13%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $43,400 (19%) higher, and it is approximately $106,300 (68%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Lenexa is $1,148 a month. It is approximately $168 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $86 (8%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $207 (21%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
