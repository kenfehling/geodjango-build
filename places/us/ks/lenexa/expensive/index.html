Compared to its nearby areas, Lenexa is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lenexa is $260,900. It is approximately $31,120 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $43,400 (19%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $106,300 (68%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Lenexa the median monthly rent is $1,148. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $168 (17%) higher.It is about $86 (8%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $207 (21%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
