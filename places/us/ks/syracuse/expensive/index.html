Home prices

The price of the median home in Syracuse is $83,000. It is approximately $7,960 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $134,500 (61%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Garden City it is approximately $66,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

Syracuse's median rent is $665 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $23 (3%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $397 (37%) lower, and is roughly $149 (18%) lower compared to the city of Garden City.
