Home prices

Syracuse's median home cost is $83,000. It is approximately $7,960 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $134,500 (61%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $66,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Garden City.

Rents

In Syracuse the median monthly rent is $665. This is roughly $23 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $397 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Garden City is approximately $149 (18%) lower.
