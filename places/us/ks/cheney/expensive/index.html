Home prices

Cheney's median home cost is $122,800. This is roughly $2,780 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $94,700 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Wichita is approximately $10,600 (7%) lower.

Rents

Cheney's median monthly rent is $874. This is roughly $15 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $188 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $65 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
