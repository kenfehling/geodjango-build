Much like other places in this area, it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Overland Park is $276,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $3,820 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $58,600 (26%) higher, and is approximately $121,500 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Overland Park the median monthly rent is $1,164. It is approximately $27 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $102 (9%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $223 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
