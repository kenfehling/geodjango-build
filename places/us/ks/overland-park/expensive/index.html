Home prices

Overland Park's median home value is $276,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $3,820 (1%) higher. It is about $58,600 (26%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $121,500 (78%) higher.

Rents

Overland Park's median rent is $1,164 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27 (2%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $102 (9%) higher, and it is approximately $223 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
