Home prices

The median home in Overland Park is valued at $276,100. This is about $3,820 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $58,600 (26%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $121,500 (78%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Overland Park the median monthly rent is $1,164. This is about $27 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $102 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $223 (23%) higher.
