In comparison to other places in this area, Park City is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Wichita however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Park City is $134,500. It is approximately $24,620 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $83,000 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Park City is $940. This is roughly $107 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $122 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $131 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
