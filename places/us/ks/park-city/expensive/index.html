Compared to nearby areas, Park City is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The median home cost in Park City is $134,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24,620 (15%) lower.It is approximately $83,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Wichita is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Park City is $940 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $107 (10%) lower.It is roughly $122 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $131 (16%) higher.
