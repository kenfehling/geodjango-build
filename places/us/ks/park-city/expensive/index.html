In comparison to other places in this area, Park City is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The median home cost in Park City is $134,500. It is approximately $24,620 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $83,000 (38%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Park City's median monthly rent is $940. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $107 (10%) lower.It is about $122 (11%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $131 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
