In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sedan is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sedan is $37,100. This is about $6,060 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $180,400 (82%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is roughly $102,800 (73%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Sedan is $629 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $49 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $433 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $200 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
