Home prices

The price of the median home in Sedan is $37,100. This is roughly $6,060 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $180,400 (82%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $102,800 (73%) lower.

Rents

Sedan's median rent is $629 a month. This is roughly $49 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $433 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is about $200 (24%) lower.
