In comparison to its nearby areas, Hesston is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Wichita however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Hesston costs $138,200. This is roughly $27,280 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $79,300 (36%) lower, and it is about $4,800 (3%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Hesston's median monthly rent is $648. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $29 (4%) lower. It is about $414 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $161 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
