Home prices

The price of the median home in Hesston is $138,200. This is about $27,280 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $79,300 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $4,800 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hesston is $648 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $29 (4%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $414 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $161 (19%) lower.
