Home prices

The value of the median home in North Newton is $158,000. It is roughly $47,160 (42%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $59,500 (27%) lower, and is approximately $24,600 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

In North Newton the median monthly rent is $604. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $125 (17%) lower.It is about $458 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $205 (25%) lower.
