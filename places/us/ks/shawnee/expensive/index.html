In comparison to nearby areas, Shawnee is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Shawnee costs $237,500. This is roughly $55,560 (30%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $20,000 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $82,900 (53%) higher.

Rents

Shawnee's median monthly rent is $1,008. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $65 (6%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $54 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $67 (7%) higher.
