Compared to its surrounding areas, Shawnee is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Shawnee is valued at $237,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $55,560 (30%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $20,000 (9%) higher, and is approximately $82,900 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Shawnee the median rent is $1,008 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $65 (6%) higher.It is roughly $54 (5%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $67 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
