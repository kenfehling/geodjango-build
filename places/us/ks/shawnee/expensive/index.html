In comparison to its nearby areas, Shawnee is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Shawnee costs $237,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $55,560 (30%) higher. It is about $20,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $82,900 (53%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Shawnee is $1,008. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $65 (6%) higher. It is about $54 (5%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $67 (7%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
