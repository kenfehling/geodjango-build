Compared to its surrounding areas, De Soto is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in De Soto is $227,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $42,500 (22%) higher. It is about $10,300 (4%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $73,200 (47%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in De Soto is $763 a month. It is roughly $228 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and it is about $178 (18%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
