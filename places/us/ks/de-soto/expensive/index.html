Home prices

De Soto's median home value is $227,800. This is about $42,500 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $10,300 (4%) higher, and is approximately $73,200 (47%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in De Soto is $763. This is roughly $228 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $178 (18%) lower.
