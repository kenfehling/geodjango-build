Compared to other places in this area, De Soto is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in De Soto is $227,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $42,500 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $10,300 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $73,200 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in De Soto is $763 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $228 (22%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $299 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $178 (18%) lower.
