Home prices

The value of the median home in New Strawn is $132,200. This is about $47,480 (56%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $85,300 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $22,400 (14%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In New Strawn the median monthly rent is $688. It is approximately $18 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $374 (35%) lower, and it is about $253 (26%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
