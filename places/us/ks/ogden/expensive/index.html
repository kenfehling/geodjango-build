Compared to its surrounding areas, Ogden is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Topeka however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Ogden is $106,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $30,260 (22%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $110,900 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Topeka it is about $3,400 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ogden is $812. It is approximately $74 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $250 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Topeka is is very similar.
