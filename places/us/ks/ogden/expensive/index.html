Home prices

The median home value in Ogden is $106,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $30,260 (22%) lower. It is roughly $110,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Topeka is roughly $3,400 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Ogden the median monthly rent is $812. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $74 (8%) lower. It is roughly $250 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Topeka.
