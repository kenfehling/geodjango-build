Home prices

The median home in Ogden is valued at $106,600. Compared to nearby areas this is about $30,260 (22%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $110,900 (50%) lower, and is approximately $3,400 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Topeka.

Rents

The median rent in Ogden is $812 per month. This is about $74 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $250 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is is very similar compared to the city of Topeka.
