Home prices

Rents

In Woodbine the median monthly rent is $930. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $197 (26%) higher. It is about $132 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is approximately $121 (14%) higher.
