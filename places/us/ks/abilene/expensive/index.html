Compared to its surrounding areas, Abilene is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

Abilene's median home value is $117,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $23,280 (24%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $100,000 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is roughly $15,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Abilene is $672 a month. This is approximately $83 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $390 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $137 (16%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
