Home prices

The price of the median home in Carbondale is $79,900. This is about $21,360 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $137,600 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $74,700 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Carbondale the median monthly rent is $543. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $183 (25%) lower. It is approximately $519 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $398 (42%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
