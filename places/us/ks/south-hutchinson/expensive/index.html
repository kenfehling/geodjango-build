Rents

South Hutchinson's median monthly rent is $532. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $169 (24%) lower. It is roughly $530 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $277 (34%) lower.
