In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Hutchinson is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Wichita however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Hutchinson costs $98,200. This is approximately $23,900 (32%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $119,300 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $35,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median rent in South Hutchinson is $532 per month. It is roughly $169 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $530 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is approximately $277 (34%) lower.
