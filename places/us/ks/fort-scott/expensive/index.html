Home prices

Fort Scott's median home cost is $58,400. It is roughly $13,040 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $159,100 (73%) lower, and is roughly $96,200 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Fort Scott the median rent is $622 a month. It is about $41 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $440 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $319 (33%) lower.
