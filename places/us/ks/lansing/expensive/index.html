In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lansing is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Lansing is valued at $173,000. This is about $88,120 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $44,500 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $18,400 (11%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Lansing is $1,169 a month. It is about $103 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $107 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $228 (24%) higher.
