Compared to nearby areas, Lansing is a lot less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lansing is valued at $173,000. This is roughly $88,120 (33%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $44,500 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $18,400 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lansing is $1,169. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $103 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $107 (10%) higher, and it is roughly $228 (24%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
