In comparison to other places in this area, Lansing is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lansing costs $173,000. It is roughly $88,120 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $44,500 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $18,400 (11%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Lansing's median monthly rent is $1,169. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $103 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $107 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $228 (24%) higher.
