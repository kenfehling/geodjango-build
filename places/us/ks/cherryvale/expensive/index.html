Home prices

The median home in Cherryvale is valued at $48,700. It is about $14,880 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $168,800 (77%) lower, and it is approximately $91,200 (65%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Cherryvale's median monthly rent is $678. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $39 (6%) higher. It is roughly $384 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is about $151 (18%) lower.
