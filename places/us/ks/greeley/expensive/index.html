Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greeley is $688. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $43 (6%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $374 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $253 (26%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
