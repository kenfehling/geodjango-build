Compared to other places in this area, Lawrence is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lawrence is $195,500. It is approximately $63,060 (47%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $22,000 (10%) lower, and it is approximately $40,900 (26%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Lawrence the median rent is $924 a month. It is approximately $58 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $138 (12%) lower, and it is about $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
