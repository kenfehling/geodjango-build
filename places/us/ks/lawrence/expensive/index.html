In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lawrence is substantially more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Lawrence's median home cost is $195,500. It is approximately $63,060 (47%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $22,000 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $40,900 (26%) higher.

Rents

In Lawrence the median monthly rent is $924. This is approximately $58 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $138 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $17 (1%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
