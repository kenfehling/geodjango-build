In comparison to other places in this area, Lawrence is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lawrence is $195,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $63,060 (47%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $22,000 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $40,900 (26%) higher.

Rents

Lawrence's median monthly rent is $924. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $58 (6%) higher.It is about $138 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
