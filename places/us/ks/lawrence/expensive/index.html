Compared to nearby areas, Lawrence is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lawrence is $195,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $63,060 (47%) higher.It is about $22,000 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $40,900 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Lawrence the median rent is $924 a month. This is about $58 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $138 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
