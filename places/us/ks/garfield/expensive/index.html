Home prices

The value of the median home in Garfield is $50,700. This is roughly $8,000 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $166,800 (76%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $45,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Hutchinson.

Rents

In Garfield the median rent is $575 per month. This is roughly $64 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Hutchinson is approximately $138 (19%) lower.
