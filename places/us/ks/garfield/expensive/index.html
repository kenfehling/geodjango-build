Home prices

The median home price in Garfield is $50,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $8,000 (13%) lower. It is approximately $166,800 (76%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Hutchinson it is about $45,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Garfield the median rent is $575 a month. This is approximately $64 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $487 (45%) lower, and it is approximately $138 (19%) lower compared to the city of Hutchinson.
