Home prices

The price of the median home in Goddard is $148,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $2,540 (1%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $69,500 (31%) lower, and is about $14,600 (10%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median rent in Goddard is $1,147 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $318 (38%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $85 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $338 (41%) higher.
