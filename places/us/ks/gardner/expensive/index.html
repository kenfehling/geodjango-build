Home prices

The value of the median home in Gardner is $189,100. It is approximately $2,920 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $28,400 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $34,500 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Gardner's median monthly rent is $1,066. This is about $202 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is is very similar compared to the entire nation, and is about $125 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
