Similar to other places in this area, it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Gardner is $189,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $2,920 (1%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $28,400 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $34,500 (22%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Gardner is $1,066 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $202 (23%) higher. It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $125 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
