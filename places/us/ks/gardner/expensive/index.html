Home prices

The median home in Gardner costs $189,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $2,920 (1%) higher.It is about $28,400 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $34,500 (22%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Gardner's median rent is $1,066 a month. This is approximately $202 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is is very similar, and is roughly $125 (13%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
