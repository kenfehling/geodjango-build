Home prices

The median home in Canton is valued at $103,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $3,660 (3%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $113,600 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita is approximately $29,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Canton is $675. It is approximately $48 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $387 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is approximately $134 (16%) lower.
