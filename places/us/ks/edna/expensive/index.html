Home prices

The median home in Edna costs $67,200. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $6,760 (11%) higher. It is approximately $150,300 (69%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $72,700 (51%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edna is $619. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $25 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $210 (25%) lower.
