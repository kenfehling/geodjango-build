Home prices

The median home in Peabody costs $64,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $15,840 (19%) lower. It is approximately $152,900 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Wichita it is about $68,800 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Peabody is $522 per month. This is roughly $133 (20%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $540 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $287 (35%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
