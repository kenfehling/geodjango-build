Home prices

Rents

Peabody's median monthly rent is $522. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $133 (20%) lower. It is approximately $540 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $287 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
