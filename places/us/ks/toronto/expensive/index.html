Home prices

The price of the median home in Toronto is $28,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $15,780 (35%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $189,100 (86%) lower, and compared to the city of Wichita it is roughly $105,000 (78%) lower.

Rents

In Toronto the median monthly rent is $525. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $35 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $537 (50%) lower, and is approximately $284 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
