Home prices

The median home in Alta Vista is valued at $65,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $13,160 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $152,300 (70%) lower, and it is approximately $38,000 (36%) lower compared to the city of Topeka.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alta Vista is $750. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $120 (19%) higher. It is about $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Topeka it is roughly $66 (8%) lower.
