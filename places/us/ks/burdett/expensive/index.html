Home prices

The median home in Burdett is valued at $50,800. This is roughly $940 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $166,700 (76%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $58,300 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

Burdett's median monthly rent is $646. This is about $83 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $416 (39%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $139 (17%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.
