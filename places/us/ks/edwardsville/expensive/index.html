Home prices

The cost of the median home in Edwardsville is $131,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $50,280 (27%) lower.It is about $85,600 (39%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $22,700 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Edwardsville's median rent is $704 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $259 (26%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $358 (33%) lower, and is roughly $237 (25%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
