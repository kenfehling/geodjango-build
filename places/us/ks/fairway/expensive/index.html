Home prices

The median home in Fairway is valued at $376,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $113,220 (42%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $159,200 (73%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $222,100 (143%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairway is $1,363. This is approximately $35 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $301 (28%) higher, and it is about $422 (44%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
