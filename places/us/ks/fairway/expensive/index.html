Home prices

The median home in Fairway is valued at $376,700. This is about $113,220 (42%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $159,200 (73%) higher, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $222,100 (143%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairway is $1,363. Compared to other places in this area it is about $35 (2%) lower.It is roughly $301 (28%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $422 (44%) higher.
