Home prices

The median home in Bennington is valued at $103,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $13,080 (14%) higher. It is approximately $114,500 (52%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $106,700 (50%) lower compared to the city of Manhattan.

Rents

In Bennington the median monthly rent is $813. This is approximately $224 (37%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $249 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $97 (10%) lower compared to the city of Manhattan.
