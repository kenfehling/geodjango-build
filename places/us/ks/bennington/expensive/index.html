Home prices

The median home in Bennington is valued at $103,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $13,080 (14%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $114,500 (52%) lower, and is about $106,700 (50%) lower compared to the city of Manhattan.

Rents

In Bennington the median rent is $813 per month. It is approximately $224 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $249 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Manhattan it is roughly $97 (10%) lower.
