Home prices

The median home price in Hartford is $62,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $43,760 (41%) lower. It is about $155,200 (71%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Topeka it is approximately $40,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Hartford the median rent is $700 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $12 (1%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and it is about $116 (14%) lower compared to the city of Topeka.
