Home prices

Rents

In Elkhart the median rent is $603 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $66 (9%) lower.It is roughly $459 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $188 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Liberal.
