Home prices

Rents

Elkhart's median rent is $603 per month. This is roughly $66 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $459 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Liberal it is approximately $188 (23%) lower.
