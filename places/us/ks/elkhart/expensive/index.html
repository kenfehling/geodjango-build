Home prices

The median home in Elkhart is valued at $93,500. This is about $14,960 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Liberal is approximately $12,900 (12%) lower.

Rents

Elkhart's median monthly rent is $603. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $66 (9%) lower. It is about $459 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Liberal it is about $188 (23%) lower.
