Home prices

Rents

Ashland's median monthly rent is $492. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $85 (14%) lower. It is approximately $570 (53%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $293 (37%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.
