Home prices

Paola's median home price is $134,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $8,120 (6%) higher. It is roughly $82,700 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $19,800 (12%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Paola is $728. It is roughly $122 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $334 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $213 (22%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
