Compared to nearby areas, Ottawa is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ottawa is $109,000. This is roughly $24,940 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $108,500 (49%) lower, and is approximately $45,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Ottawa the median monthly rent is $792. It is about $83 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $149 (15%) lower.
