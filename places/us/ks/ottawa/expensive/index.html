Compared to the surrounding areas, Ottawa is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Ottawa's median home cost is $109,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $24,940 (29%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $108,500 (49%) lower, and is roughly $45,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ottawa is $792. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $83 (11%) higher.It is roughly $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $149 (15%) lower.
