Compared to its nearby areas, Ottawa is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ottawa is $109,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24,940 (29%) higher. It is roughly $108,500 (49%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $45,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Ottawa the median rent is $792 per month. This is about $83 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $149 (15%) lower.
