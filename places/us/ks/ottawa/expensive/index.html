Compared to other places in this area, Ottawa is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ottawa costs $109,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24,940 (29%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $108,500 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $45,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

Ottawa's median rent is $792 a month. This is about $83 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $149 (15%) lower.
