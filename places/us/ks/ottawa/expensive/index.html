Compared to nearby areas, Ottawa is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Ottawa is valued at $109,000. It is about $24,940 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $108,500 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $45,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

Ottawa's median rent is $792 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $83 (11%) higher. It is about $270 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $149 (15%) lower.
