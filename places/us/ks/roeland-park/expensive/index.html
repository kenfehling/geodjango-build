Compared to other places in this area, Roeland Park is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Roeland Park is valued at $172,900. It is roughly $107,100 (38%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $44,600 (20%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $18,300 (11%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Roeland Park's median rent is $1,182 a month. It is roughly $173 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $120 (11%) higher, and is roughly $241 (25%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
