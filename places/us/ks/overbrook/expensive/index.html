Home prices

The median home in Overbrook costs $118,900. It is roughly $21,120 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $98,600 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $35,700 (23%) lower.

Rents

Overbrook's median rent is $736 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $14 (1%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $326 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $205 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
