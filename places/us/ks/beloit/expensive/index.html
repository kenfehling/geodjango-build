Home prices

The median home in Beloit is valued at $93,200. It is about $48,900 (110%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $124,300 (57%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $36,100 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Salina.

Rents

In Beloit the median monthly rent is $576. It is roughly $77 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $486 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Salina it is approximately $182 (24%) lower.
