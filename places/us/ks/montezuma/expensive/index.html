Home prices

The median home in Montezuma costs $105,700. It is about $19,140 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $111,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dodge City is roughly $3,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Montezuma the median rent is $672 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $54 (8%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $390 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Dodge City it is roughly $113 (14%) lower.
