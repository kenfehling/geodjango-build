Home prices

The median home in Montezuma is valued at $105,700. It is roughly $19,140 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $111,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Dodge City it is approximately $3,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

Montezuma's median monthly rent is $672. It is roughly $54 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $390 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $113 (14%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.
