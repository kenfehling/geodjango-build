Home prices

The median home value in Montezuma is $105,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $19,140 (22%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $111,800 (51%) lower, and is about $3,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

Montezuma's median rent is $672 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $54 (8%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $390 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $113 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Dodge City.
