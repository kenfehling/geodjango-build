Compared to its nearby areas, Baldwin City is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Baldwin City costs $171,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $26,020 (17%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $45,600 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $17,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Baldwin City the median rent is $870 per month. This is about $45 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $192 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $71 (7%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
