Home prices

The median home in Baldwin City is valued at $171,900. It is approximately $26,020 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $45,600 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $17,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Baldwin City the median rent is $870 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $45 (4%) lower. It is roughly $192 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $71 (7%) lower.
