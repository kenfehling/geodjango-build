Compared to other places in this area, Baldwin City is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Baldwin City is valued at $171,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $26,020 (17%) higher. It is approximately $45,600 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $17,300 (11%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Baldwin City's median rent is $870 per month. This is approximately $45 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $192 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $71 (7%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
