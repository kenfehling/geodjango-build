Compared to the surrounding areas, Altamont is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tulsa, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Altamont is $81,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $20,820 (34%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $135,800 (62%) lower, and it is approximately $58,200 (41%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Altamont the median monthly rent is $586. It is roughly $23 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $476 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $243 (29%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
