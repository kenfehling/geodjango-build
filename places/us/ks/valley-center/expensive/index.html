In comparison to its surrounding areas, Valley Center is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Wichita.

Home prices

The median home price in Valley Center is $145,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $11,180 (7%) lower. It is roughly $71,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $12,300 (9%) higher compared to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median rent in Valley Center is $941 a month. It is about $106 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is approximately $132 (16%) higher.
