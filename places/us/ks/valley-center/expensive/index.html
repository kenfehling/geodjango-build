Home prices

The value of the median home in Valley Center is $145,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $11,180 (7%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $71,800 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $12,300 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Valley Center is $941. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $106 (10%) lower.It is about $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $132 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Wichita.
