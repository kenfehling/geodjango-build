Home prices

The median home in Valley Center costs $145,700. It is about $11,180 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $71,800 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is approximately $12,300 (9%) higher.

Rents

Valley Center's median rent is $941 a month. This is about $106 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is about $132 (16%) higher.
