Home prices

The price of the median home in St. George is $196,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $60,420 (44%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $21,400 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Topeka it is approximately $92,900 (90%) higher.

Rents

St. George's median rent is $1,063 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $258 (32%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Topeka is roughly $247 (30%) higher.
