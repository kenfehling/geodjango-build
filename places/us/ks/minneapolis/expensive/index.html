Home prices

The median home value in Minneapolis is $91,000. It is about $25,640 (39%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $126,500 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Manhattan it is about $118,700 (56%) lower.

Rents

Minneapolis's median rent is $612 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $24 (3%) lower. It is approximately $450 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Manhattan it is approximately $298 (32%) lower.
