Compared to other places in this area, Minneapolis is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Manhattan, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Minneapolis is $91,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $25,640 (39%) higher.It is about $126,500 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $118,700 (56%) lower compared to the city of Manhattan.

Rents

In Minneapolis the median rent is $612 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $24 (3%) lower.It is approximately $450 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $298 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Manhattan.
