Compared to other places in this area, Minneapolis is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Manhattan.

Home prices

The median home in Minneapolis is valued at $91,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $25,640 (39%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $126,500 (58%) lower, and is roughly $118,700 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Manhattan.

Rents

Minneapolis's median rent is $612 a month. This is about $24 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $450 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Manhattan it is approximately $298 (32%) lower.
