Home prices

The median home in Scott City is valued at $131,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $47,420 (56%) higher. It is approximately $86,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $22,400 (20%) higher compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

In Scott City the median rent is $827 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $221 (36%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $235 (22%) lower, and is about $42 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Dodge City.
