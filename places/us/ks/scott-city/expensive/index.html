Home prices

The median home cost in Scott City is $131,500. This is approximately $47,420 (56%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $86,000 (39%) lower, and it is roughly $22,400 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

Scott City's median rent is $827 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $221 (36%) higher. It is about $235 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $42 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Dodge City.
