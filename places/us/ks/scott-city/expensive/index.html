Home prices

The median home value in Scott City is $131,500. It is about $47,420 (56%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $86,000 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Dodge City it is about $22,400 (20%) higher.

Rents

In Scott City the median rent is $827 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $221 (36%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $235 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $42 (5%) higher compared to the city of Dodge City.
