Home prices

Scott City's median home cost is $131,500. This is about $47,420 (56%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $86,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $22,400 (20%) higher compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scott City is $827. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $221 (36%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $235 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Dodge City is approximately $42 (5%) higher.
