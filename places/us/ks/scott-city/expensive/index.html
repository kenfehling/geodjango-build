In comparison to nearby areas, Scott City is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Dodge City, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Scott City costs $131,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $47,420 (56%) higher. It is approximately $86,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $22,400 (20%) higher compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

The median rent in Scott City is $827 a month. This is roughly $221 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $235 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Dodge City it is roughly $42 (5%) higher.
