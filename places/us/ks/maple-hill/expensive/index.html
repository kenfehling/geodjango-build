Home prices

The median home value in Maple Hill is $151,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $33,940 (28%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $65,900 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $3,000 (1%) lower.

Rents

Maple Hill's median rent is $1,050 per month. This is roughly $375 (55%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $109 (11%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
