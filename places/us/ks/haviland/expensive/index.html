Home prices

The cost of the median home in Haviland is $71,800. This is roughly $3,740 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $145,700 (66%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Hutchinson is roughly $24,500 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Haviland the median rent is $619 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $443 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Hutchinson it is about $94 (13%) lower.
