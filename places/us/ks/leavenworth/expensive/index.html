In comparison to its surrounding areas, Leavenworth is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home price in Leavenworth is $123,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $37,620 (23%) lower. It is approximately $93,700 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $30,800 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Leavenworth's median monthly rent is $958. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $216 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $104 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $17 (1%) higher.
