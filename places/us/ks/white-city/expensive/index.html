Home prices

The median home in White City costs $87,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $16,960 (24%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $130,300 (59%) lower, and it is roughly $46,200 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

The median rent in White City is $810 a month. It is approximately $113 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $252 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita is is very similar.
