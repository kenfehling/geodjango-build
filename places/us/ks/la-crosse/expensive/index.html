Home prices

The median home in La Crosse costs $83,800. It is about $21,500 (34%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $133,700 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dodge City is approximately $25,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

La Crosse's median rent is $689 per month. It is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $373 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dodge City is about $96 (12%) lower.
