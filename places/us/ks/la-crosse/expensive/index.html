Compared to its surrounding areas, La Crosse is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Dodge City, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in La Crosse is $83,800. This is roughly $21,500 (34%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $133,700 (61%) lower, and is roughly $25,300 (23%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

La Crosse's median rent is $689 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $373 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Dodge City it is roughly $96 (12%) lower.
