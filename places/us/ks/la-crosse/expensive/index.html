In comparison to the surrounding areas, La Crosse is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Dodge City, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in La Crosse costs $83,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $21,500 (34%) higher.It is roughly $133,700 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $25,300 (23%) lower compared to the city of Dodge City.

Rents

In La Crosse the median rent is $689 a month. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dodge City is about $96 (12%) lower.
