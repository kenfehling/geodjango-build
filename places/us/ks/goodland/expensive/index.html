Compared to other places in this area, Goodland is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Colby.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Goodland is $103,700. This is roughly $21,040 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $113,800 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Colby is approximately $21,800 (17%) lower.

Rents

Goodland's median monthly rent is $834. It is roughly $195 (30%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $228 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Colby it is about $200 (31%) higher.
