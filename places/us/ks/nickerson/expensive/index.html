Home prices

The price of the median home in Nickerson is $68,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,140 (17%) lower. It is about $149,400 (68%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $65,300 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.

Rents

Nickerson's median rent is $662 a month. This is about $27 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $400 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $147 (18%) lower.
