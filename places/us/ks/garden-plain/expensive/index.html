Rents

The median monthly rent in Garden Plain is $725. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $188 (20%) lower. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $84 (10%) lower.
