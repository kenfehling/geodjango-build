Garden Plain's median home cost is $154,200. This is approximately $4,900 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $63,300 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Wichita is roughly $20,800 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Garden Plain the median monthly rent is $725. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $188 (20%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $84 (10%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
