Home prices

Geneseo's median home price is $31,200. This is about $35,240 (53%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $186,300 (85%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is about $102,200 (76%) lower.

Rents

In Geneseo the median rent is $426 a month. This is about $221 (34%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $636 (59%) lower, and is roughly $383 (47%) lower compared to the city of Wichita.
