The median home in Burlingame costs $72,100. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $12,220 (14%) lower. It is roughly $145,400 (66%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $82,500 (53%) lower.

Rents

Burlingame's median rent is $611 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $23 (3%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $451 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $330 (35%) lower.
