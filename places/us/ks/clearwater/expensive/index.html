Home prices

The median home in Clearwater is valued at $143,900. This is approximately $41,740 (40%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $73,600 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wichita it is roughly $10,500 (7%) higher.

Rents

Clearwater's median monthly rent is $711. It is roughly $113 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $98 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Wichita.
