Home prices

Rents

In Dighton the median monthly rent is $444. This is approximately $179 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $618 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dodge City it is approximately $341 (43%) lower.
