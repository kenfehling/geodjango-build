Home prices

The value of the median home in Eudora is $169,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $6,120 (3%) lower.It is roughly $48,500 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $14,400 (9%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Eudora's median monthly rent is $1,117. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $243 (27%) higher.It is approximately $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $176 (18%) higher.
