Home prices

The median home in Eudora is valued at $169,000. It is about $6,120 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $48,500 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $14,400 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Eudora is $1,117 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $243 (27%) higher. It is about $55 (5%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $176 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
