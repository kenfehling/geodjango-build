In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Garland is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pueblo, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Garland costs $111,300. This is roughly $29,220 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $106,200 (48%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pueblo it is about $29,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Garland is $499. This is roughly $169 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $563 (53%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pueblo it is approximately $300 (37%) lower.
