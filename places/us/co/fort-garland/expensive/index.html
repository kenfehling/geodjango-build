Home prices

The median home in Fort Garland costs $111,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $29,220 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $106,200 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $29,700 (21%) lower compared to the city of Pueblo.

Rents

Fort Garland's median monthly rent is $499. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $169 (25%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $563 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pueblo it is about $300 (37%) lower.
