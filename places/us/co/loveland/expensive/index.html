Home prices

The median home in Loveland costs $313,900. It is about $49,180 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $96,400 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $76,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Loveland is $1,267 a month. It is about $86 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $205 (19%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $44 (3%) lower.
