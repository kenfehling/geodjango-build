Home prices

The cost of the median home in Loveland is $313,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $49,180 (13%) lower.It is approximately $96,400 (44%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $76,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

Loveland's median monthly rent is $1,267. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $86 (6%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $205 (19%) higher, and is approximately $44 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
