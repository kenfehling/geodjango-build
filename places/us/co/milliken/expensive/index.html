Home prices

The median home value in Milliken is $275,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $31,880 (13%) higher.It is approximately $57,500 (26%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $115,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Milliken is $995 a month. This is roughly $192 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $67 (6%) lower, and is about $316 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
