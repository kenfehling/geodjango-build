In comparison to the surrounding areas, Milliken is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milliken costs $275,000. This is approximately $31,880 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $57,500 (26%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $115,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Milliken is $995 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $192 (16%) lower. It is roughly $67 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $316 (24%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
