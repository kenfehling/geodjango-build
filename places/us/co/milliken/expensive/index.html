Compared to its surrounding areas, Milliken is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milliken is valued at $275,000. It is roughly $31,880 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $57,500 (26%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $115,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Milliken's median rent is $995 per month. This is approximately $192 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $67 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $316 (24%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
