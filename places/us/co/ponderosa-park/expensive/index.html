Home prices

The median home in Ponderosa Park is valued at $453,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $90,320 (24%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $236,100 (108%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $63,000 (16%) higher.

Rents

Ponderosa Park's median monthly rent is $2,402. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $957 (66%) higher. It is approximately $1,340 (126%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $1,091 (83%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
