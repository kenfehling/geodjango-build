Home prices

The median home in Minturn is valued at $646,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $81,380 (14%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $428,800 (197%) higher, and it is roughly $255,700 (65%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Minturn's median rent is $1,508 per month. It is roughly $126 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $446 (41%) higher, and is about $197 (15%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
