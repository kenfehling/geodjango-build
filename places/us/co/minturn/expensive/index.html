Home prices

The median home in Minturn is valued at $646,300. This is about $81,380 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $428,800 (197%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $255,700 (65%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Minturn's median monthly rent is $1,508. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $126 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $446 (41%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $197 (15%) higher.
