Compared to nearby areas, Commerce City is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Commerce City is $320,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $87,900 (37%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $102,600 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $70,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Commerce City is $1,286 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $42 (3%) lower.It is roughly $224 (21%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $25 (1%) lower.
