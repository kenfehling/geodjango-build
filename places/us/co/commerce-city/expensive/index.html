Compared to other places in this area, Commerce City is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Denverhowever, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Commerce City is valued at $320,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $87,900 (37%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $102,600 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $70,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

Commerce City's median rent is $1,286 a month. It is roughly $42 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $224 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $25 (1%) lower.
