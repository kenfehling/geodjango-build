In comparison to other places in this area, Commerce City is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Denver however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Commerce City is $320,100. It is roughly $87,900 (37%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $102,600 (47%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $70,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Commerce City is $1,286 per month. This is about $42 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $224 (21%) higher, and is roughly $25 (1%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
