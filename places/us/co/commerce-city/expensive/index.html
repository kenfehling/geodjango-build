In comparison to the surrounding areas, Commerce City is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Denver, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Commerce City is $320,100. It is about $87,900 (37%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $102,600 (47%) higher, and it is roughly $70,500 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Commerce City the median rent is $1,286 a month. It is roughly $42 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $224 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $25 (1%) lower.
