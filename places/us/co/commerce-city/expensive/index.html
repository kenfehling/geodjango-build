In comparison to nearby areas, Commerce City is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Commerce City costs $320,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $87,900 (37%) higher.It is about $102,600 (47%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $70,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Commerce City is $1,286. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $42 (3%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) higher, and is about $25 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
