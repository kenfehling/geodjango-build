Home prices

The value of the median home in Commerce City is $320,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $87,900 (37%) higher. It is about $102,600 (47%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver is about $70,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Commerce City is $1,286 per month. This is roughly $42 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $224 (21%) higher, and it is approximately $25 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
