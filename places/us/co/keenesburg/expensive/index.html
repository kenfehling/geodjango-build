Home prices

The median home in Keenesburg costs $234,700. This is about $27,600 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $17,200 (7%) higher, and is roughly $155,900 (39%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Keenesburg is $1,077 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $109 (9%) lower.It is approximately $15 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $234 (17%) lower.
