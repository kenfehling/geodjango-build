Home prices

The price of the median home in Keenesburg is $234,700. This is about $27,600 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $17,200 (7%) higher, and it is about $155,900 (39%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Keenesburg the median monthly rent is $1,077. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $109 (9%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $15 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $234 (17%) lower.
