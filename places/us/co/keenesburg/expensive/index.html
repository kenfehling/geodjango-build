In comparison to other places in this area, Keenesburg is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Keenesburg is $234,700. It is roughly $27,600 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $17,200 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $155,900 (39%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Keenesburg's median monthly rent is $1,077. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $109 (9%) lower. It is about $15 (1%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $234 (17%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
