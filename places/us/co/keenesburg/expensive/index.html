Home prices

The median home in Keenesburg is valued at $234,700. It is approximately $27,600 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $17,200 (7%) higher, and it is about $155,900 (39%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Keenesburg's median rent is $1,077 a month. It is roughly $109 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $15 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $234 (17%) lower.
