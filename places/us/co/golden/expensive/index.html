Compared to other places in this area, Golden is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Golden is valued at $522,200. It is approximately $42,380 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $304,700 (140%) higher, and is about $131,600 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Golden is $1,386 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $297 (17%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $324 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $75 (5%) higher.
