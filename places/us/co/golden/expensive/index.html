In comparison to its surrounding areas, Golden is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Golden costs $522,200. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $42,380 (8%) higher. It is approximately $304,700 (140%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $131,600 (33%) higher.

Rents

Golden's median rent is $1,386 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $297 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $324 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is approximately $75 (5%) higher.
