In comparison to other places in this area, Golden is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Golden is $522,200. This is approximately $42,380 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $304,700 (140%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $131,600 (33%) higher.

Rents

Golden's median rent is $1,386 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $297 (17%) lower.It is about $324 (30%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $75 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
