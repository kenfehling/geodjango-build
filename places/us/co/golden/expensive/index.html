Home prices

The median home in Golden costs $522,200. It is roughly $42,380 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $304,700 (140%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $131,600 (33%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Golden the median rent is $1,386 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $297 (17%) lower.It is about $324 (30%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $75 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
