In comparison to nearby areas, Golden is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Denver, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Golden is $522,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $42,380 (8%) higher. It is approximately $304,700 (140%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $131,600 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Golden's median monthly rent is $1,386. This is about $297 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $324 (30%) higher, and it is approximately $75 (5%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
