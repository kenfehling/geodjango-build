Home prices

The median home in Morrison is valued at $541,700. It is roughly $115,340 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $324,200 (149%) higher, and is approximately $151,100 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Morrison's median rent is $1,275 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $427 (25%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $213 (20%) higher, and it is roughly $36 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
