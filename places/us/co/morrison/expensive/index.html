Compared to its surrounding areas, Morrison is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Morrison is valued at $541,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $115,340 (27%) higher. It is about $324,200 (149%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $151,100 (38%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Morrison the median rent is $1,275 per month. It is roughly $427 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $213 (20%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $36 (2%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
