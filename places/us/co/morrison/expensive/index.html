Home prices

The price of the median home in Morrison is $541,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $115,340 (27%) higher.It is approximately $324,200 (149%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $151,100 (38%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Morrison's median monthly rent is $1,275. It is roughly $427 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $213 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $36 (2%) lower.
