Compared to other places in this area, Morrison is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Denver, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Morrison is valued at $541,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $115,340 (27%) higher. It is roughly $324,200 (149%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $151,100 (38%) higher.

Rents

In Morrison the median monthly rent is $1,275. This is about $427 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $213 (20%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $36 (2%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
