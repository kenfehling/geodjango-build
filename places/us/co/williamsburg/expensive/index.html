Home prices

Williamsburg's median home value is $129,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $18,000 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $88,100 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $261,200 (66%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Williamsburg's median monthly rent is $863. This is about $14 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $199 (18%) lower, and it is about $448 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
