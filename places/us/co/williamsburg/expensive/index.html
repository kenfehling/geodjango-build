In comparison to the surrounding areas, Williamsburg is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Denver, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Williamsburg costs $129,400. This is about $18,000 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $88,100 (40%) lower, and it is about $261,200 (66%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Williamsburg's median monthly rent is $863. This is roughly $14 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $199 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $448 (34%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
