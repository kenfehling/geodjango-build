Home prices

The median home in Williamsburg costs $129,400. This is roughly $18,000 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $88,100 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $261,200 (66%) lower.

Rents

Williamsburg's median rent is $863 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $14 (1%) lower. It is roughly $199 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $448 (34%) lower.
