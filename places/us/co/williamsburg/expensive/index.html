Home prices

Williamsburg's median home value is $129,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $18,000 (12%) lower. It is roughly $88,100 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $261,200 (66%) lower.

Rents

Williamsburg's median monthly rent is $863. This is about $14 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $199 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $448 (34%) lower.
