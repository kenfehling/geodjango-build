Home prices

The price of the median home in New Castle is $367,300. It is approximately $46,980 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $149,800 (68%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Junction is about $130,200 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in New Castle is $1,480 per month. It is about $109 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $418 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Grand Junction it is about $545 (58%) higher.
