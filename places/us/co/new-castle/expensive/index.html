Home prices

Rents

New Castle's median rent is $1,480 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $109 (6%) lower. It is roughly $418 (39%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Junction is roughly $545 (58%) higher.
