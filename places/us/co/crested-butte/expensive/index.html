Home prices

The cost of the median home in Crested Butte is $568,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $57,840 (11%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $351,000 (161%) higher, and compared to the city of Montrose it is approximately $356,800 (168%) higher.

Rents
