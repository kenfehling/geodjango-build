Home prices

The median home in Rifle costs $246,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $35,600 (12%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $28,800 (13%) higher, and is roughly $9,200 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Junction.

Rents

Rifle's median monthly rent is $984. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $136 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $78 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Junction is approximately $49 (5%) higher.
