Compared to its surrounding areas, Rifle is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Grand Junction.

Home prices

The median home in Rifle costs $246,300. This is approximately $35,600 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $28,800 (13%) higher, and is approximately $9,200 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Junction.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rifle is $984. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $136 (12%) lower. It is about $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Junction is about $49 (5%) higher.
