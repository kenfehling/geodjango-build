Compared to its surrounding areas, Aspen is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Montrose.

Home prices

Aspen's median home cost is $636,400. It is approximately $45,720 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $418,900 (192%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $424,700 (200%) higher in comparison to the city of Montrose.

Rents

In Aspen the median rent is $1,500 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $113 (7%) lower. It is approximately $438 (41%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $595 (65%) higher in comparison to the city of Montrose.
