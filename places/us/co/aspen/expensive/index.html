Home prices

The median home in Aspen is valued at $636,400. It is about $45,720 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $418,900 (192%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Montrose is approximately $424,700 (200%) higher.

Rents

In Aspen the median monthly rent is $1,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $113 (7%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $438 (41%) higher, and compared to the city of Montrose is roughly $595 (65%) higher.
