Compared to other places in this area, Aspen is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Montrose.

Home prices

The median home in Aspen costs $636,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $45,720 (7%) higher.It is roughly $418,900 (192%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Montrose is approximately $424,700 (200%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Aspen is $1,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $113 (7%) lower.It is roughly $438 (41%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Montrose is approximately $595 (65%) higher.
