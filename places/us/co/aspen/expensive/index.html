Home prices

Aspen's median home value is $636,400. It is approximately $45,720 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $418,900 (192%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Montrose is about $424,700 (200%) higher.

Rents

In Aspen the median monthly rent is $1,500. This is roughly $113 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $438 (41%) higher, and is about $595 (65%) higher compared to the city of Montrose.
