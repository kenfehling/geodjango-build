Home prices

The median home price in Flagler is $116,900. It is roughly $39,380 (50%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $100,600 (46%) lower, and is roughly $53,000 (31%) lower compared to the city of Fort Morgan.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Flagler is $672. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $19 (2%) higher. It is roughly $390 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $139 (17%) lower compared to the city of Fort Morgan.
