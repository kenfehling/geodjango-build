Rents

The median rent in Littleton is $1,286 per month. It is roughly $343 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $224 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $25 (1%) lower.
