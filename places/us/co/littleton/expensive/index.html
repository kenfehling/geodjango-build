Compared to its nearby areas, Littleton is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Denver, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Littleton costs $410,900. It is roughly $115,880 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $193,400 (88%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $20,300 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Littleton is $1,286 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $343 (21%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $224 (21%) higher, and it is roughly $25 (1%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
