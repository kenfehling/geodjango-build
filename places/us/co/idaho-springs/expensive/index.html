Compared to its surrounding areas, Idaho Springs is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Idaho Springs is $250,500. This is roughly $56,860 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $33,000 (15%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $140,100 (35%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Idaho Springs's median monthly rent is $948. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $333 (26%) lower. It is approximately $114 (10%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $363 (27%) lower.
