Home prices

Idaho Springs's median home cost is $250,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $56,860 (18%) lower.It is about $33,000 (15%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $140,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Idaho Springs the median rent is $948 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $333 (26%) lower.It is roughly $114 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $363 (27%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
