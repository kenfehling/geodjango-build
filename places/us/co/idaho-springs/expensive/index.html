In comparison to other places in this area, Idaho Springs is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home cost in Idaho Springs is $250,500. It is roughly $56,860 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $33,000 (15%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $140,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Idaho Springs the median rent is $948 a month. This is approximately $333 (26%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $114 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $363 (27%) lower.
