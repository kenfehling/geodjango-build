Compared to its nearby areas, Idaho Springs is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Denver, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Idaho Springs is $250,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $56,860 (18%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $33,000 (15%) higher, and is about $140,100 (35%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Idaho Springs is $948 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $333 (26%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $114 (10%) lower, and it is about $363 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
