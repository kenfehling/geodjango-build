Home prices

Idaho Springs's median home value is $250,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $56,860 (18%) lower. It is roughly $33,000 (15%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $140,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Idaho Springs the median rent is $948 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $333 (26%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $114 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $363 (27%) lower.
