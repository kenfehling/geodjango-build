Home prices

Mountain View's median home value is $350,700. This is roughly $45,280 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $133,200 (61%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $39,900 (10%) lower.

Rents

Mountain View's median rent is $1,147 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $50 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $85 (8%) higher, and is about $164 (12%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
