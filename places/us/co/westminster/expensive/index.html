Home prices

The median home in Westminster costs $340,900. It is roughly $72,940 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $123,400 (56%) higher, and it is approximately $49,700 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Westminster the median monthly rent is $1,430. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $368 (34%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $119 (9%) higher.
