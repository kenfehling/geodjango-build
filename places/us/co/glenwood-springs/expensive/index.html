Compared to its surrounding areas, Glenwood Springs is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Grand Junction, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Glenwood Springs is $409,500. It is roughly $73,640 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $192,000 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Grand Junction is roughly $172,400 (72%) higher.

Rents

In Glenwood Springs the median monthly rent is $1,206. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $584 (32%) lower. It is roughly $144 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $271 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Junction.
