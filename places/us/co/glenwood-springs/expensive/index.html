Home prices

Glenwood Springs's median home value is $409,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $73,640 (15%) lower. It is about $192,000 (88%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Grand Junction it is roughly $172,400 (72%) higher.

Rents

Glenwood Springs's median rent is $1,206 a month. This is approximately $584 (32%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $144 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Grand Junction is roughly $271 (28%) higher.
