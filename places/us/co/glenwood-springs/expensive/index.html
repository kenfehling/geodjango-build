Home prices

The median home value in Glenwood Springs is $409,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $73,640 (15%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $192,000 (88%) higher, and is roughly $172,400 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Junction.

Rents

In Glenwood Springs the median rent is $1,206 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $584 (32%) lower.It is about $144 (13%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $271 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Junction.
