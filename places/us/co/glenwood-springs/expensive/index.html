Home prices

The median home price in Glenwood Springs is $409,500. It is approximately $73,640 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $192,000 (88%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Junction is roughly $172,400 (72%) higher.

Rents

In Glenwood Springs the median monthly rent is $1,206. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $584 (32%) lower.It is approximately $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $271 (28%) higher compared to the city of Grand Junction.
