In comparison to other places in this area, Louisville is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Louisville is $587,000. It is approximately $52,960 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $369,500 (169%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $196,400 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Louisville the median rent is $1,607 a month. It is roughly $129 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $545 (51%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $296 (22%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
