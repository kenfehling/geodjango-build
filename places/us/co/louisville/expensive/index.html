In comparison to nearby areas, Louisville is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

Louisville's median home price is $587,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $52,960 (8%) lower. It is about $369,500 (169%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $196,400 (50%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Louisville is $1,607. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $129 (8%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $545 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $296 (22%) higher.
