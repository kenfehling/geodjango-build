In comparison to its surrounding areas, Louisville is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Louisville costs $587,000. This is approximately $52,960 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $369,500 (169%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is about $196,400 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Louisville the median monthly rent is $1,607. This is about $129 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $545 (51%) higher, and it is roughly $296 (22%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
