In comparison to the surrounding areas, Louisville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Denver, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Louisville is $587,000. It is roughly $52,960 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $369,500 (169%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $196,400 (50%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Louisville is $1,607. It is approximately $129 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $545 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $296 (22%) higher.
