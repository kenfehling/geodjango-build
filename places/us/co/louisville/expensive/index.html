In comparison to other places in this area, Louisville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Louisville is $587,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $52,960 (8%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $369,500 (169%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $196,400 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Louisville the median rent is $1,607 per month. This is about $129 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $545 (51%) higher, and it is approximately $296 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
