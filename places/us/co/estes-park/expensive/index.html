Home prices

Estes Park's median home cost is $413,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $58,500 (16%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $195,600 (89%) higher, and is approximately $22,500 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Estes Park the median monthly rent is $835. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $440 (34%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $227 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $476 (36%) lower.
