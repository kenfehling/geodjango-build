Compared to other places in this area, Estes Park is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Denver, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Estes Park is $413,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $58,500 (16%) higher. It is approximately $195,600 (89%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $22,500 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Estes Park is $835. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $440 (34%) lower. It is about $227 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $476 (36%) lower.
