Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Park costs $190,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $49,840 (35%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $27,500 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $200,600 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Lincoln Park the median rent is $526 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $399 (43%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $536 (50%) lower, and is approximately $785 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
