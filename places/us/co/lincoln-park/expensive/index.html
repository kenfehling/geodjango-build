In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lincoln Park is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Denver, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Park costs $190,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $49,840 (35%) higher.It is roughly $27,500 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $200,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lincoln Park is $526. It is roughly $399 (43%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $536 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $785 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
