Compared to its nearby areas, Lincoln Park is substantially more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Park is valued at $190,000. This is about $49,840 (35%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $27,500 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $200,600 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lincoln Park is $526 per month. It is about $399 (43%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $536 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $785 (59%) lower.
