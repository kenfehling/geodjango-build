Home prices

The cost of the median home in Arvada is $384,500. This is roughly $20,840 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $167,000 (76%) higher, and it is roughly $6,100 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Arvada's median rent is $1,358 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $73 (5%) lower.It is roughly $296 (27%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $47 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
