Home prices

The median home cost in Arvada is $384,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $20,840 (5%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $167,000 (76%) higher, and it is roughly $6,100 (1%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Arvada the median monthly rent is $1,358. It is about $73 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $296 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is about $47 (3%) higher.
