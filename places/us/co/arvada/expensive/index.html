Home prices

The value of the median home in Arvada is $384,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $20,840 (5%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $167,000 (76%) higher, and is about $6,100 (1%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Arvada is $1,358 a month. It is roughly $73 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $296 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $47 (3%) higher.
