In comparison to the surrounding areas, Shaw Heights is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Shaw Heights costs $290,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $54,480 (23%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $73,100 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $100,000 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Shaw Heights is $1,540. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $248 (19%) higher.It is about $478 (45%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $229 (17%) higher.
