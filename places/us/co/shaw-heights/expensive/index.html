Home prices

The median home price in Shaw Heights is $290,600. This is approximately $50,880 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $73,100 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $100,000 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Shaw Heights the median monthly rent is $1,540. It is about $279 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $478 (45%) higher, and is about $229 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
