Home prices

The median home value in Fountain is $229,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14,700 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $11,700 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $161,400 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fountain is $1,424 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $343 (31%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $362 (34%) higher, and is about $113 (8%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
