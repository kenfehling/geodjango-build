Home prices

The median home cost in Fountain is $229,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $14,700 (6%) lower. It is roughly $11,700 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $161,400 (41%) lower.

Rents

Fountain's median rent is $1,424 per month. It is roughly $343 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $362 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $113 (8%) higher.
