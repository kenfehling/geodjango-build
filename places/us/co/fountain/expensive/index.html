In comparison to its nearby areas, Fountain is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fountain is $229,200. It is about $14,700 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $11,700 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $161,400 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fountain is $1,424 a month. This is about $343 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $362 (34%) higher, and it is approximately $113 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
