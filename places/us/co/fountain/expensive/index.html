Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Fountain is $1,424 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $343 (31%) higher.It is about $362 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $113 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
