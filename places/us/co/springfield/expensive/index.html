Home prices

The price of the median home in Springfield is $79,200. It is roughly $21,260 (36%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $138,300 (63%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of La Junta it is roughly $12,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Springfield the median rent is $502 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $28 (5%) lower. It is roughly $560 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $186 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of La Junta.
