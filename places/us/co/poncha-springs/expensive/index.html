Rents

Poncha Springs's median rent is $828 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $29 (3%) lower. It is roughly $234 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $303 (26%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
