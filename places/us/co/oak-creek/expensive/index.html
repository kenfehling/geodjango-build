Rents

The median rent in Oak Creek is $971 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $146 (13%) lower.It is about $91 (8%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs is approximately $235 (19%) lower.
