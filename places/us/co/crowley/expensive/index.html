Home prices

The median home in Crowley is valued at $50,300. This is about $17,160 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $167,200 (76%) lower, and is about $219,500 (81%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.

Rents

The median rent in Crowley is $925 per month. This is approximately $254 (37%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $206 (18%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
