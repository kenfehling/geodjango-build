Georgetown's median home price is $294,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $41,760 (16%) higher. It is roughly $77,400 (35%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $95,700 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Georgetown the median monthly rent is $1,090. This is approximately $53 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $28 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $221 (16%) lower.
