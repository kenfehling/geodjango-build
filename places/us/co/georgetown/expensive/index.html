Compared to its surrounding areas, Georgetown is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Denver, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Georgetown is valued at $294,900. It is approximately $41,760 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $77,400 (35%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $95,700 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Georgetown is $1,090 per month. This is about $53 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $28 (2%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $221 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
