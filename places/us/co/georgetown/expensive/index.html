Compared to the surrounding areas, Georgetown is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Denver, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Georgetown is $294,900. This is roughly $41,760 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $77,400 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is about $95,700 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Georgetown is $1,090. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $53 (5%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $28 (2%) higher, and is roughly $221 (16%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
