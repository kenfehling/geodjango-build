Compared to other places in this area, Edgewater is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Denver however, it's close to average.

Home prices

Edgewater's median home price is $408,500. This is about $32,020 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $191,000 (87%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $17,900 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edgewater is $1,192. It is about $103 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $130 (12%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $119 (9%) lower.
