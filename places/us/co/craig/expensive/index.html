Home prices

The median home price in Craig is $173,600. It is about $77,120 (30%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $43,900 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Glenwood Springs is about $235,900 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Craig is $779. This is about $379 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $283 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $427 (35%) lower compared to the city of Glenwood Springs.
