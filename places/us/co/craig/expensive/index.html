Compared to its surrounding areas, Craig is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Glenwood Springs.

Home prices

The median home in Craig costs $173,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $77,120 (30%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $43,900 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $235,900 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs.

Rents

Craig's median monthly rent is $779. It is roughly $379 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $283 (26%) lower, and is approximately $427 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs.
