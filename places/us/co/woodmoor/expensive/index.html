In comparison to its surrounding areas, Woodmoor is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Woodmoor is $481,100. It is approximately $99,660 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $263,600 (121%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $90,500 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Woodmoor the median monthly rent is $1,782. This is about $323 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $720 (67%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $471 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
