Compared to other places in this area, Frederick is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Denverhowever, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Frederick's median home cost is $363,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $24,860 (7%) higher. It is approximately $145,700 (66%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $27,400 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Frederick is $1,480 per month. This is about $57 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $418 (39%) higher, and is about $169 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
