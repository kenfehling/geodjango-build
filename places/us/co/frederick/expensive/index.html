Compared to other places in this area, Frederick is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

Frederick's median home price is $363,200. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $24,860 (7%) higher.It is roughly $145,700 (66%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $27,400 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Frederick the median monthly rent is $1,480. This is about $57 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $418 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $169 (12%) higher.
