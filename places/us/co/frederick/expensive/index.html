Home prices

The median home in Frederick costs $363,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $24,860 (7%) higher. It is roughly $145,700 (66%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $27,400 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Frederick is $1,480. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $57 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $418 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $169 (12%) higher.
