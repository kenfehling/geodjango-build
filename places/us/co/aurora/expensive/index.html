Compared to its nearby areas, Aurora is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Denver, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Aurora is $290,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $117,920 (28%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $72,500 (33%) higher, and it is about $100,600 (25%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Aurora the median monthly rent is $1,328. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $200 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $266 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $17 (1%) higher.
