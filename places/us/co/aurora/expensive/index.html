In comparison to its surrounding areas, Aurora is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Aurora is valued at $290,000. It is about $125,940 (30%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $72,500 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $100,600 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Aurora the median rent is $1,328 per month. It is about $212 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $266 (25%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $17 (1%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
