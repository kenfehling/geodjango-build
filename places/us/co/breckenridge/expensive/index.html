Compared to its nearby areas, Breckenridge is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Denver, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Breckenridge is $474,400. It is about $114,360 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $256,900 (118%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $83,800 (21%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Breckenridge the median rent is $1,357 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $75 (5%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $295 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $46 (3%) higher.
