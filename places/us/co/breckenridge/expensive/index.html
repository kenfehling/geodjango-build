Home prices

The median home cost in Breckenridge is $474,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $114,360 (19%) lower. It is roughly $256,900 (118%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $83,800 (21%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Breckenridge is $1,357 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $75 (5%) higher. It is approximately $295 (27%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is about $46 (3%) higher.
