In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Lupton is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Fort Lupton is $240,500. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $50,680 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $23,000 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $150,100 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Lupton the median rent is $886 per month. This is roughly $411 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $176 (16%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $425 (32%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
