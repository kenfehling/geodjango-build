Home prices

The median home cost in Pueblo West is $221,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $76,180 (52%) higher. It is roughly $4,200 (1%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs is about $48,100 (17%) lower.

Rents

Pueblo West's median monthly rent is $1,037. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $100 (10%) higher. It is roughly $25 (2%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $94 (8%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
