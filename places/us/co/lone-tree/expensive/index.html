Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lone Tree is $1,724. Compared to nearby areas it is about $27 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $662 (62%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $413 (31%) higher.
