Compared to its nearby areas, Erie is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Erie is $468,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $69,260 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $251,100 (115%) higher, and is roughly $78,000 (19%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Erie the median monthly rent is $2,177. Compared to other places in this area it is about $697 (47%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $1,115 (104%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $866 (66%) higher.
