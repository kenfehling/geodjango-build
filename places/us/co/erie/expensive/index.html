In comparison to its surrounding areas, Erie is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Denver, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Erie's median home price is $468,600. This is approximately $69,260 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $251,100 (115%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver is approximately $78,000 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Erie the median monthly rent is $2,177. It is about $697 (47%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $1,115 (104%) higher, and is approximately $866 (66%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
