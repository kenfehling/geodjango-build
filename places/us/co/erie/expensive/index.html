In comparison to its surrounding areas, Erie is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Erie is $468,600. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $69,260 (17%) higher.It is about $251,100 (115%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $78,000 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Erie the median monthly rent is $2,177. This is roughly $697 (47%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $1,115 (104%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $866 (66%) higher.
