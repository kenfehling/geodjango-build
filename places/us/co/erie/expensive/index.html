Home prices

The median home in Erie costs $468,600. It is approximately $69,260 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $251,100 (115%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $78,000 (19%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Erie's median monthly rent is $2,177. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $697 (47%) higher.It is roughly $1,115 (104%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Denver is approximately $866 (66%) higher.
