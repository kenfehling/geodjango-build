Home prices

The median home in Englewood costs $344,400. This is roughly $552,640 (61%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $126,900 (58%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $46,200 (11%) lower.

Rents

Englewood's median monthly rent is $1,193. It is approximately $485 (28%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $131 (12%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $118 (9%) lower.
