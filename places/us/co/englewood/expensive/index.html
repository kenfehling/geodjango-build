Compared to its surrounding areas, Englewood is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

Englewood's median home value is $344,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $552,640 (61%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $126,900 (58%) higher, and is approximately $46,200 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Englewood the median monthly rent is $1,193. This is roughly $485 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $131 (12%) higher, and is roughly $118 (9%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
