Home prices

The median home in Cañon City is valued at $160,800. This is about $7,880 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $56,700 (26%) lower, and is approximately $229,800 (58%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Cañon City the median rent is $800 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $25 (3%) lower. It is about $262 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $511 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
