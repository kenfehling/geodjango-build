Home prices

The median home in Cañon City is valued at $160,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $7,880 (5%) higher. It is approximately $56,700 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $229,800 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cañon City is $800 per month. It is about $25 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $262 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $511 (38%) lower.
