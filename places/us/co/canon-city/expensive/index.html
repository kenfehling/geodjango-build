Compared to other places in this area, Cañon City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Cañon City is $160,800. It is about $7,880 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $56,700 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $229,800 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Cañon City the median rent is $800 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $25 (3%) lower.It is roughly $262 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $511 (38%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
