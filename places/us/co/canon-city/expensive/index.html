In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cañon City is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Denver, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cañon City costs $160,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $7,880 (5%) higher.It is approximately $56,700 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $229,800 (58%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Cañon City is $800 a month. It is roughly $25 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $262 (24%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $511 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
