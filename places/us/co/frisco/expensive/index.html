Home prices

The median home in Frisco is valued at $545,700. It is roughly $28,800 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $328,200 (150%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is about $155,100 (39%) higher.

Rents

Frisco's median monthly rent is $1,757. It is approximately $555 (46%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $695 (65%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $446 (34%) higher.
