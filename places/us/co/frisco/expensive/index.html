Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Frisco is $1,757 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $555 (46%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $695 (65%) higher, and is approximately $446 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
