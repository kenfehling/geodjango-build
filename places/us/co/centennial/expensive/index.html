Compared to the surrounding areas, Centennial is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Centennial costs $433,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $130,060 (23%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $216,300 (99%) higher, and it is roughly $43,200 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Centennial the median rent is $1,740 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $678 (63%) higher, and is roughly $429 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
