Home prices

The median home price in Centennial is $433,800. It is about $130,060 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $216,300 (99%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $43,200 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Centennial is $1,740 per month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $678 (63%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $429 (32%) higher.
