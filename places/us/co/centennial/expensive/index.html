Compared to its surrounding areas, Centennial is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Centennial is valued at $433,800. It is about $130,060 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $216,300 (99%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $43,200 (11%) higher.

Rents

Centennial's median rent is $1,740 per month. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $678 (63%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is approximately $429 (32%) higher.
