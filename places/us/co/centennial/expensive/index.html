In comparison to its surrounding areas, Centennial is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Centennial is valued at $433,800. It is about $130,060 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $216,300 (99%) higher, and is roughly $43,200 (11%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centennial is $1,740. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $678 (63%) higher, and is roughly $429 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
