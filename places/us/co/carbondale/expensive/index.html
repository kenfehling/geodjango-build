Compared to nearby areas, Carbondale is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Grand Junction, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Carbondale's median home cost is $496,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $146,980 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $278,800 (128%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Grand Junction is approximately $259,200 (109%) higher.

Rents

Carbondale's median rent is $1,747 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $47 (2%) lower. It is roughly $685 (64%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $812 (86%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Junction.
