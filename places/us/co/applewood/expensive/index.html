Home prices

The value of the median home in Applewood is $494,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $79,340 (19%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $277,100 (127%) higher, and it is approximately $104,000 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Applewood's median rent is $1,611 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $308 (23%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $549 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $300 (22%) higher.
