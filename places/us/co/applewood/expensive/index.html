Home prices

The median home in Applewood costs $494,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $79,340 (19%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $277,100 (127%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $104,000 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Applewood is $1,611 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $308 (23%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $549 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $300 (22%) higher.
