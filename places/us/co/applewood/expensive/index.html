Home prices

The median home value in Applewood is $494,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $79,340 (19%) higher. It is about $277,100 (127%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $104,000 (26%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Applewood's median monthly rent is $1,611. This is approximately $308 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $549 (51%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $300 (22%) higher.
