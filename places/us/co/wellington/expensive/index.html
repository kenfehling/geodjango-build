Home prices

The median home value in Wellington is $323,500. It is about $28,200 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $106,000 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $67,100 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wellington is $1,477. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $336 (29%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $415 (39%) higher, and is roughly $166 (12%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
