Home prices

The value of the median home in Wellington is $323,500. This is roughly $28,200 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $106,000 (48%) higher, and it is about $67,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Wellington's median rent is $1,477 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $336 (29%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $415 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $166 (12%) higher.
