Home prices

Wellington's median home value is $323,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $28,200 (9%) higher. It is about $106,000 (48%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $67,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Wellington the median monthly rent is $1,477. This is roughly $336 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $415 (39%) higher, and it is about $166 (12%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
