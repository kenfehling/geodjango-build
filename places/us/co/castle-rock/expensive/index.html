In comparison to its surrounding areas, Castle Rock is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Castle Rock is valued at $422,100. This is approximately $180,320 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $204,600 (94%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is roughly $31,500 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Castle Rock is $1,536. It is about $448 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $474 (44%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $225 (17%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
