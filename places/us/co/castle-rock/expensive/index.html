In comparison to other places in this area, Castle Rock is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Denver, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Castle Rock costs $422,100. This is about $180,320 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $204,600 (94%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $31,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Castle Rock the median monthly rent is $1,536. This is about $448 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $474 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $225 (17%) higher.
