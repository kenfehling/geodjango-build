In comparison to its surrounding areas, Castle Rock is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Denver, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Castle Rock is valued at $422,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $180,320 (29%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $204,600 (94%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $31,500 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Castle Rock is $1,536 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $448 (22%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $474 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $225 (17%) higher.
