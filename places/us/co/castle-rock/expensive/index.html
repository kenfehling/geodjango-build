Home prices

The median home value in Castle Rock is $422,100. This is roughly $180,320 (29%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $204,600 (94%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $31,500 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Castle Rock the median monthly rent is $1,536. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $448 (22%) lower. It is about $474 (44%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $225 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
