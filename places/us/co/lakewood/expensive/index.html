Home prices

Rents

In Lakewood the median monthly rent is $1,361. It is approximately $86 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $299 (28%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver is about $50 (3%) higher.
