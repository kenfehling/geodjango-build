In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denver is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Denver is valued at $390,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $150,700 (62%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $173,100 (79%) higher.

Rents

In Denver the median monthly rent is $1,311. This is roughly $38 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $249 (23%) higher compared to the nation as a whole.
