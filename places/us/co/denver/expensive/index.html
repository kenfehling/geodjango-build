In comparison to its nearby areas, Denver is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

The median home value in Denver is $390,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $151,867 (63%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $173,100 (79%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Denver is $1,311. This is roughly $60 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $249 (23%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole.
