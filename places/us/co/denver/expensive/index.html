In comparison to nearby areas, Denver is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Denver costs $390,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $151,867 (63%) higher. It is about $173,100 (79%) higher compared to the rest of the nation.

Rents

In Denver the median rent is $1,311 per month. It is approximately $60 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $249 (23%) higher compared to the rest of the country.
