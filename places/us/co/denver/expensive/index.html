In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denver is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Denver is $390,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $151,867 (63%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $173,100 (79%) higher.

Rents

Denver's median rent is $1,311 a month. It is approximately $60 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $249 (23%) higher.
