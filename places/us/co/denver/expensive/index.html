In comparison to its surrounding areas, Denver is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

Denver's median home price is $390,600. This is about $150,700 (62%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $173,100 (79%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Denver is $1,311. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $38 (2%) higher.It is approximately $249 (23%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation.
