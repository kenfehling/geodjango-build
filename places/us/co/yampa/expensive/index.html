Home prices

Yampa's median home cost is $224,000. It is about $47,960 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $6,500 (2%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $185,500 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs.

Rents

Yampa's median monthly rent is $1,175. This is roughly $99 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $113 (10%) higher, and is about $31 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs.
