Home prices

Hotchkiss's median home value is $157,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $29,620 (15%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $60,200 (27%) lower, and is roughly $79,800 (33%) lower compared to the city of Grand Junction.

Rents

In Hotchkiss the median rent is $889 a month. It is about $49 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $173 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $46 (4%) lower compared to the city of Grand Junction.
