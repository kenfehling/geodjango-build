Home prices

The median home in Boulder costs $700,000. It is approximately $14,300 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $482,500 (221%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $309,400 (79%) higher.

Rents

Boulder's median monthly rent is $1,554. It is roughly $167 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $492 (46%) higher, and is about $243 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
