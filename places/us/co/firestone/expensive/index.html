In comparison to its nearby areas, Firestone is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Firestone is valued at $354,000. This is approximately $58,900 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $136,500 (62%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $36,600 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Firestone is $1,545. This is approximately $370 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $483 (45%) higher, and it is approximately $234 (17%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
