Compared to its surrounding areas, Firestone is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Denver, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Firestone is $354,000. This is about $58,900 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $136,500 (62%) higher, and it is about $36,600 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Firestone is $1,545. It is approximately $370 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $483 (45%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $234 (17%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
