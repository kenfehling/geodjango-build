Home prices

The median home in Vail costs $773,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $226,060 (41%) higher. It is approximately $556,200 (255%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $383,100 (98%) higher.

Rents

In Vail the median rent is $1,597 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $90 (6%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $535 (50%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $286 (21%) higher.
