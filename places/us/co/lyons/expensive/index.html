Home prices

The median home in Lyons is valued at $577,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $122,580 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $359,700 (165%) higher, and is approximately $186,600 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Lyons is $1,313 per month. It is roughly $31 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $251 (23%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Denver.
