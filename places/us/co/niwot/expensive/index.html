Home prices

The median home in Niwot costs $677,600. This is approximately $47,500 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $460,100 (211%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $287,000 (73%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Niwot the median rent is $1,122 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $356 (24%) lower.It is about $60 (5%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $189 (14%) lower.
