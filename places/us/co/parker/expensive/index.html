Compared to the surrounding areas, Parker is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Denver, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Parker is $420,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $53,960 (11%) lower. It is roughly $202,500 (93%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $29,400 (7%) higher.

Rents

Parker's median monthly rent is $1,610. This is about $78 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $548 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $299 (22%) higher.
