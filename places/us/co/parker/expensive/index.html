Home prices

The median home price in Parker is $420,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $53,960 (11%) lower. It is roughly $202,500 (93%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $29,400 (7%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Parker is $1,610 a month. This is approximately $78 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $548 (51%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $299 (22%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
