Home prices

The value of the median home in Manitou Springs is $365,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $117,680 (47%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $147,800 (67%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $25,300 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Manitou Springs the median rent is $943 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $80 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $119 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is about $368 (28%) lower.
