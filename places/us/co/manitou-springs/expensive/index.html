In comparison to other places in this area, Manitou Springs is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Manitou Springs is valued at $365,300. It is approximately $117,680 (47%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $147,800 (67%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $25,300 (6%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Manitou Springs is $943. This is about $80 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $119 (11%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $368 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
