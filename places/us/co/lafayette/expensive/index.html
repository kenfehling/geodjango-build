Compared to its surrounding areas, Lafayette is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home price in Lafayette is $422,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $204,680 (32%) lower.It is approximately $204,500 (94%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $31,400 (8%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Lafayette the median rent is $1,495 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $130 (8%) lower.It is roughly $433 (40%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $184 (14%) higher.
