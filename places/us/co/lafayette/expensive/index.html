Compared to nearby areas, Lafayette is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home value in Lafayette is $422,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $204,680 (32%) lower.It is about $204,500 (94%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $31,400 (8%) higher.

Rents

Lafayette's median rent is $1,495 per month. It is about $130 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $433 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $184 (14%) higher.
