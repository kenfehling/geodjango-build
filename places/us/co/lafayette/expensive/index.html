Home prices

The value of the median home in Lafayette is $422,000. It is roughly $204,680 (32%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $204,500 (94%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $31,400 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Lafayette the median monthly rent is $1,495. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $130 (8%) lower.It is about $433 (40%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $184 (14%) higher.
