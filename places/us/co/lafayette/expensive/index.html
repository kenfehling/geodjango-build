Compared to nearby areas, Lafayette is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Denver, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lafayette is valued at $422,000. This is about $204,680 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $204,500 (94%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $31,400 (8%) higher.

Rents

Lafayette's median monthly rent is $1,495. This is approximately $130 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $433 (40%) higher, and it is approximately $184 (14%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
