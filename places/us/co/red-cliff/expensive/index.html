Home prices

The median home in Red Cliff costs $345,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $279,820 (44%) lower. It is roughly $127,800 (58%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $45,300 (11%) lower.

Rents

Red Cliff's median rent is $1,875 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $314 (20%) higher. It is approximately $813 (76%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $564 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
