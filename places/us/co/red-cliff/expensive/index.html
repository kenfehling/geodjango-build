In comparison to the surrounding areas, Red Cliff is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Denver, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Red Cliff is $345,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $279,820 (44%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $127,800 (58%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $45,300 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Red Cliff is $1,875 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $314 (20%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $813 (76%) higher, and is roughly $564 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
