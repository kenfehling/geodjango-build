Home prices

The median home in Byers costs $168,800. It is about $42,980 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $48,700 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $221,800 (56%) lower.

Rents

Byers's median rent is $1,125 per month. It is roughly $63 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $186 (14%) lower.
