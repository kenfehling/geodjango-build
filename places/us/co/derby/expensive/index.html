In comparison to other places in this area, Derby is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Denver, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Derby's median home cost is $187,500. It is approximately $63,180 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $30,000 (13%) lower, and is about $203,100 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Derby's median monthly rent is $1,224. It is roughly $35 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $162 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $87 (6%) lower.
