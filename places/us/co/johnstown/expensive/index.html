In comparison to the surrounding areas, Johnstown is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Johnstown is valued at $336,100. This is roughly $30,680 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $118,600 (54%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is approximately $54,500 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Johnstown the median rent is $1,589 per month. It is about $378 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $527 (49%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $278 (21%) higher.
