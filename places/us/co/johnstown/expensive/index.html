Compared to its surrounding areas, Johnstown is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Johnstown is valued at $336,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $30,680 (10%) higher. It is about $118,600 (54%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $54,500 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Johnstown's median monthly rent is $1,589. It is about $378 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $527 (49%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver is about $278 (21%) higher.
