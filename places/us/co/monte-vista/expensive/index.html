Home prices

The median home in Monte Vista costs $115,100. This is approximately $5,020 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $102,400 (47%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cañon City it is roughly $45,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Monte Vista is $618 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $8 (1%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $444 (41%) lower, and is roughly $182 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cañon City.
