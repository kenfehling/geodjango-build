Home prices

The median home cost in Monte Vista is $115,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $5,020 (4%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $102,400 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cañon City is about $45,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Monte Vista is $618 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $8 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $444 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cañon City is approximately $182 (22%) lower.
