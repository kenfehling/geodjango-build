Home prices

The median home in Monte Vista costs $115,100. This is roughly $5,020 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $102,400 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cañon City is about $45,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Monte Vista the median rent is $618 per month. This is about $8 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $444 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cañon City is roughly $182 (22%) lower.
