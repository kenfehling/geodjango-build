In comparison to its surrounding areas, Colorado Springs is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Colorado Springs costs $269,800. This is roughly $16,280 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $52,300 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $120,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Colorado Springs the median rent is $1,131 a month. This is roughly $65 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $69 (6%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $180 (13%) lower.
