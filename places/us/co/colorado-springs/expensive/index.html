In comparison to other places in this area, Colorado Springs is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Colorado Springs is $269,800. It is roughly $16,280 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $52,300 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $120,800 (30%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Colorado Springs the median monthly rent is $1,131. This is roughly $65 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $69 (6%) higher, and it is approximately $180 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
