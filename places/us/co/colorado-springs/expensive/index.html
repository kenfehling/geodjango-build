In comparison to nearby areas, Colorado Springs is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Colorado Springs is $269,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $16,280 (6%) higher. It is approximately $52,300 (24%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $120,800 (30%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Colorado Springs is $1,131. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $65 (6%) higher. It is approximately $69 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $180 (13%) lower.
