Home prices

The median home price in Broomfield is $413,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $31,420 (7%) lower.It is about $196,000 (90%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $22,900 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Broomfield the median monthly rent is $1,679. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $114 (7%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $617 (58%) higher, and it is approximately $368 (28%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
