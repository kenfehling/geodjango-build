In comparison to the surrounding areas, Twin Lakes is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Twin Lakes costs $306,400. This is approximately $65,220 (27%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $88,900 (40%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $84,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Twin Lakes the median monthly rent is $1,096. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $245 (18%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $34 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $215 (16%) lower.
