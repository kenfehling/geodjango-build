Compared to the surrounding areas, Blende is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Colorado Springs.

Home prices

The median home cost in Blende is $153,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $8,580 (5%) lower. It is roughly $64,100 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $116,400 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs.

Rents

Blende's median monthly rent is $790. This is approximately $114 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $272 (25%) lower, and is about $341 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs.
