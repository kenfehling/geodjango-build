Compared to other places in this area, Blende is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Colorado Springs.

Home prices

The median home in Blende costs $153,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $8,580 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $64,100 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $116,400 (43%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.

Rents

In Blende the median monthly rent is $790. This is about $114 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $272 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs is approximately $341 (30%) lower.
