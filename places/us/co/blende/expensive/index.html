Home prices

Rents

Blende's median monthly rent is $790. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $114 (12%) lower.It is about $272 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $341 (30%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
