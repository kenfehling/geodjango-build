In comparison to other places in this area, Blende is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Colorado Springs however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Blende is $153,400. It is about $8,580 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $64,100 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $116,400 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blende is $790. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $114 (12%) lower.It is roughly $272 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $341 (30%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
