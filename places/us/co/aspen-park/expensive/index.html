Home prices

The median home in Aspen Park is valued at $434,800. This is roughly $66,100 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $217,300 (99%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $44,200 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Aspen Park the median rent is $1,611 a month. This is approximately $216 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $549 (51%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $300 (22%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
