Home prices

The price of the median home in Hayden is $209,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $58,060 (21%) lower.It is approximately $8,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Glenwood Springs it is roughly $200,400 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Hayden the median rent is $930 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $154 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $132 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs is approximately $276 (22%) lower.
