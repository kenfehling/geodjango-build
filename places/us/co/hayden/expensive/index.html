Home prices

The median home in Hayden is valued at $209,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $58,060 (21%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $8,400 (3%) lower, and it is approximately $200,400 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs.

Rents

Hayden's median rent is $930 per month. It is about $154 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $276 (22%) lower compared to the city of Glenwood Springs.
