Rents

The median monthly rent in Hayden is $930. It is approximately $154 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $132 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Glenwood Springs is roughly $276 (22%) lower.
