Home prices

The median home cost in Wheat Ridge is $383,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $27,880 (6%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $166,400 (76%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $6,700 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Wheat Ridge the median monthly rent is $1,104. This is approximately $231 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $42 (3%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $207 (15%) lower.
