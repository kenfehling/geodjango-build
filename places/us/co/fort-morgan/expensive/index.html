Rents

Fort Morgan's median rent is $811 a month. This is roughly $109 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $251 (23%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $500 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
