Compared to other places in this area, Fort Collins is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Collins costs $367,900. It is approximately $23,520 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $150,400 (69%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is roughly $22,700 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Collins the median rent is $1,346 per month. This is approximately $138 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $284 (26%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $35 (2%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
