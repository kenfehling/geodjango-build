In comparison to other places in this area, Fort Collins is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Denver however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Fort Collins's median home value is $367,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $23,520 (6%) higher.It is roughly $150,400 (69%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $22,700 (5%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Fort Collins's median rent is $1,346 a month. It is roughly $138 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $284 (26%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $35 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
