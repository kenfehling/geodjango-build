In comparison to other places in this area, Fort Collins is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Denverhowever, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Collins costs $367,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $23,520 (6%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $150,400 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $22,700 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Collins is $1,346 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $138 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $284 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $35 (2%) higher.
