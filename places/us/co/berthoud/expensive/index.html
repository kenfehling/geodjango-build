Home prices

The median home in Berthoud is valued at $342,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,500 (4%) higher.It is about $125,300 (57%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $47,800 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Berthoud is $1,391 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $44 (3%) higher.It is about $329 (30%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $80 (6%) higher.
