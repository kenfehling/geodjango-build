Home prices

The value of the median home in Berthoud is $342,800. It is approximately $14,500 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $125,300 (57%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $47,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

Berthoud's median monthly rent is $1,391. It is about $44 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $329 (30%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver is about $80 (6%) higher.
