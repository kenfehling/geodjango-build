Home prices

The median home in Berthoud costs $342,800. This is about $14,500 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $125,300 (57%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $47,800 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Berthoud the median monthly rent is $1,391. It is about $44 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $329 (30%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $80 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
