Much like its nearby areas, it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home cost in Berthoud is $342,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,500 (4%) higher. It is roughly $125,300 (57%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $47,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

Berthoud's median rent is $1,391 a month. It is about $44 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $329 (30%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $80 (6%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
