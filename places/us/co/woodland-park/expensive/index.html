Home prices

Rents

Woodland Park's median monthly rent is $1,276. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $91 (7%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $214 (20%) higher, and is roughly $35 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
