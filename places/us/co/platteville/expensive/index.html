In comparison to its surrounding areas, Platteville is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Denver, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Platteville is $243,200. This is about $21,680 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $25,700 (11%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $147,400 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Platteville's median monthly rent is $1,002. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $138 (12%) lower. It is roughly $60 (5%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $309 (23%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
