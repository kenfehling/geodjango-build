Home prices

The median home price in Brighton is $308,900. This is approximately $28,700 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $91,400 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $81,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

Brighton's median rent is $1,301 per month. This is approximately $57 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $239 (22%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Denver.
