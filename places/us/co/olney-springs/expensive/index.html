Rents

The median monthly rent in Olney Springs is $438. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $322 (42%) lower. It is approximately $624 (58%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs it is roughly $693 (61%) lower.
