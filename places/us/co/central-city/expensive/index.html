Home prices

The median home cost in Central City is $291,700. It is about $3,180 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $74,200 (34%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver it is roughly $98,900 (25%) lower.

Rents

Central City's median rent is $1,153 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $60 (5%) higher. It is about $91 (8%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $158 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
