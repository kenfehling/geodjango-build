Home prices

The median home in Central City costs $291,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $3,180 (1%) higher. It is about $74,200 (34%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is about $98,900 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Central City is $1,153. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $60 (5%) higher. It is about $91 (8%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $158 (12%) lower.
