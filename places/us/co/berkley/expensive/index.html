Home prices

The median home cost in Berkley is $288,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $16,180 (5%) higher. It is about $70,900 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $102,200 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Berkley the median rent is $1,251 per month. This is about $46 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $189 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver is roughly $60 (4%) lower.
