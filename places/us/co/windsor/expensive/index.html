Compared to its nearby areas, Windsor is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Denver, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Windsor is $406,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $80,480 (24%) higher. It is roughly $188,600 (86%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Denver it is about $15,500 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Windsor is $1,276 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is is very similar. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $214 (20%) higher, and is about $35 (2%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
