Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairmount is $516,700. This is roughly $101,920 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $299,200 (137%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $126,100 (32%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairmount is $1,472. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $141 (10%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $410 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $161 (12%) higher.
