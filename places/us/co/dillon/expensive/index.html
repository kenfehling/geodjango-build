Home prices

The median home in Dillon is valued at $558,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,800 (2%) lower.It is roughly $340,700 (156%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $167,600 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Dillon's median rent is $1,042 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $303 (22%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $20 (1%) lower, and it is approximately $269 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
