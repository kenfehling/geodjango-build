Home prices

The median home in Dillon is valued at $558,200. This is approximately $13,800 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $340,700 (156%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $167,600 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dillon is $1,042. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $303 (22%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $20 (1%) lower, and is roughly $269 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
