Rents

In Sugar City the median rent is $763 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $72 (10%) higher.It is approximately $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $368 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs.
