Home prices

Rents

In Sugar City the median rent is $763 per month. This is roughly $72 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $299 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $368 (32%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
