Home prices

Pueblo's median home cost is $141,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $13,140 (8%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $76,500 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Colorado Springs it is roughly $128,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pueblo is $799. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $75 (8%) lower.It is approximately $263 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $332 (29%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
