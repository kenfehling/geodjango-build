In comparison to nearby areas, Pueblo is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Colorado Springs.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Pueblo is $141,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $13,140 (8%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $76,500 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Colorado Springs it is approximately $128,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Pueblo the median rent is $799 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $75 (8%) lower. It is approximately $263 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $332 (29%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.
