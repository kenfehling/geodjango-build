Compared to other places in this area, Pueblo is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Colorado Springs, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pueblo costs $141,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13,140 (8%) lower. It is approximately $76,500 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $128,800 (47%) lower compared to the city of Colorado Springs.

Rents

In Pueblo the median rent is $799 a month. It is about $75 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $263 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Colorado Springs is roughly $332 (29%) lower.
