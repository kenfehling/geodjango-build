Compared to its nearby areas, Longmont is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Longmont costs $362,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $138,280 (27%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $145,000 (66%) higher, and is about $28,100 (7%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Longmont the median rent is $1,340 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $68 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $278 (26%) higher, and is roughly $29 (2%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
