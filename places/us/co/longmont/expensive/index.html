Home prices

The median home in Longmont costs $362,500. It is roughly $138,280 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $145,000 (66%) higher, and it is approximately $28,100 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Longmont the median monthly rent is $1,340. This is about $68 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $278 (26%) higher, and is approximately $29 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
