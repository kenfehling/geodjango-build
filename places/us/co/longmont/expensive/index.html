Compared to nearby areas, Longmont is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home in Longmont costs $362,500. Compared to nearby areas it is about $138,280 (27%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $145,000 (66%) higher, and compared to the city of Denver is about $28,100 (7%) lower.

Rents

Longmont's median rent is $1,340 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $68 (4%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $278 (26%) higher, and is about $29 (2%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
