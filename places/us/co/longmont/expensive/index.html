Compared to its surrounding areas, Longmont is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Denver, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Longmont is valued at $362,500. It is about $138,280 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $145,000 (66%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $28,100 (7%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Longmont is $1,340 a month. This is roughly $68 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $278 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $29 (2%) higher.
