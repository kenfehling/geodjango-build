Home prices

Longmont's median home price is $362,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $138,280 (27%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $145,000 (66%) higher, and it is about $28,100 (7%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Longmont is $1,340. This is about $68 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $278 (26%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Denver is about $29 (2%) higher.
