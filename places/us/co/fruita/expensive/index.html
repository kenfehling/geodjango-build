Home prices

The median home in Fruita costs $223,500. This is roughly $19,140 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $6,000 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Grand Junction is about $13,600 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fruita is $1,169 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $102 (9%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $107 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Grand Junction is roughly $234 (25%) higher.
