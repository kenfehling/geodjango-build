Home prices

The value of the median home in Fruita is $223,500. This is approximately $19,140 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $6,000 (2%) higher, and it is roughly $13,600 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Junction.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fruita is $1,169. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $102 (9%) higher. It is roughly $107 (10%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $234 (25%) higher compared to the city of Grand Junction.
