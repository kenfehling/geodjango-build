Compared to its surrounding areas, North La Junta is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Pueblo however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North La Junta is valued at $102,000. This is approximately $19,960 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $115,500 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $39,000 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Pueblo.

Rents

North La Junta's median rent is $727 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $114 (18%) higher.It is roughly $335 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $72 (9%) lower compared to the city of Pueblo.
