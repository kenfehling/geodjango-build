In comparison to other places in this area, Thornton is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Denver however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Thornton is $322,200. It is roughly $86,860 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $104,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $68,400 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Thornton the median rent is $1,495 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $157 (11%) higher. It is about $433 (40%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $184 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Denver.
