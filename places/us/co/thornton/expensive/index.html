Home prices

The median home cost in Thornton is $322,200. This is roughly $86,860 (36%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $104,700 (48%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $68,400 (17%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Thornton the median rent is $1,495 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $157 (11%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $433 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $184 (14%) higher.
