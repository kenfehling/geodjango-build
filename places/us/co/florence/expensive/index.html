In comparison to its surrounding areas, Florence is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Denver, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Florence is $136,400. It is approximately $9,600 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $81,100 (37%) lower, and is about $254,200 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $899. It is about $29 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $163 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $412 (31%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
