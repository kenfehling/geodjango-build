Compared to its surrounding areas, Florence is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Denver, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $136,400. It is about $9,600 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $81,100 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Denver it is approximately $254,200 (65%) lower.

Rents

Florence's median rent is $899 a month. It is about $29 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $163 (15%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $412 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
