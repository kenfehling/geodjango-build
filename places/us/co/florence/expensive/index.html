Compared to nearby areas, Florence is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Denver.

Home prices

The median home value in Florence is $136,400. It is approximately $9,600 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $81,100 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is approximately $254,200 (65%) lower.

Rents

Florence's median monthly rent is $899. This is roughly $29 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $163 (15%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Denver is approximately $412 (31%) lower.
