In comparison to nearby areas, Florence is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Denver, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Florence is $136,400. It is approximately $9,600 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $81,100 (37%) lower, and is about $254,200 (65%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

In Florence the median rent is $899 per month. This is approximately $29 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $163 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $412 (31%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
