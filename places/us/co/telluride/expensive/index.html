Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Telluride is $1,412. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $457 (47%) higher. It is roughly $350 (32%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $544 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Farmington.
