Home prices

Telluride's median home value is $410,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $76,560 (15%) lower. It is about $193,300 (88%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Farmington it is approximately $224,000 (119%) higher.

Rents

In Telluride the median monthly rent is $1,412. This is about $457 (47%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $350 (32%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $544 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Farmington.
