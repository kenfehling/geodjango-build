Home prices

The price of the median home in Telluride is $410,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $76,560 (15%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $193,300 (88%) higher, and is roughly $224,000 (119%) higher compared to the city of Farmington.

Rents

Telluride's median rent is $1,412 a month. This is about $457 (47%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $350 (32%) higher, and it is about $544 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Farmington.
