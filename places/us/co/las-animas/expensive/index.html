Home prices

The median home in Las Animas is valued at $52,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $40,680 (43%) lower. It is roughly $164,900 (75%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $88,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Pueblo.

Rents

Las Animas's median monthly rent is $666. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $98 (17%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $396 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $133 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Pueblo.
