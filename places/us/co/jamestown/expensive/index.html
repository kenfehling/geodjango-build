Home prices

The median home in Jamestown is valued at $358,300. This is about $202,180 (36%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $140,800 (64%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Denver is about $32,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

Jamestown's median monthly rent is $1,287. This is approximately $529 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $225 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $24 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
