Home prices

The median home in Rockvale costs $135,900. It is roughly $10,200 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $81,600 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $254,700 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

Rockvale's median monthly rent is $906. Compared to nearby areas it is about $37 (4%) higher. It is roughly $156 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $405 (30%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
