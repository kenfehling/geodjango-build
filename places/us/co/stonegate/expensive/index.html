Home prices

The cost of the median home in Stonegate is $447,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $52,840 (10%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $230,200 (105%) higher, and it is about $57,100 (14%) higher compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Stonegate is $1,656 a month. This is about $39 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $594 (55%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $345 (26%) higher compared to the city of Denver.
