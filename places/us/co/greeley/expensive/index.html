Home prices

Greeley's median home value is $247,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $3,600 (1%) lower. It is about $30,200 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $142,900 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Greeley is $1,007 per month. It is roughly $40 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $55 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $304 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
