Home prices

The value of the median home in Greeley is $247,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $3,600 (1%) lower.It is roughly $30,200 (13%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $142,900 (36%) lower.

Rents

Greeley's median monthly rent is $1,007. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $40 (3%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $55 (5%) lower, and is approximately $304 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.
