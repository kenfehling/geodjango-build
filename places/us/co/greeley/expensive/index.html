Home prices

The median home in Greeley is valued at $247,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $3,600 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $30,200 (13%) higher, and it is roughly $142,900 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Denver.

Rents

The median rent in Greeley is $1,007 per month. It is about $40 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $55 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Denver is about $304 (23%) lower.
