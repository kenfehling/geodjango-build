Home prices

The median home in Oxford is valued at $145,300. It is roughly $58,680 (67%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $72,200 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $54,200 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oxford is $839. It is roughly $186 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $223 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Birmingham.
