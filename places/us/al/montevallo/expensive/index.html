Compared to its surrounding areas, Montevallo is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Montevallo's median home price is $160,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,900 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $57,200 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $69,200 (75%) higher.

Rents

In Montevallo the median rent is $743 per month. It is about $207 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $319 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $94 (11%) lower.
