Compared to its surrounding areas, Montevallo is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Montevallo is $160,300. This is about $13,900 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $57,200 (26%) lower, and is roughly $69,200 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Montevallo the median monthly rent is $743. It is about $207 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $319 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $94 (11%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
