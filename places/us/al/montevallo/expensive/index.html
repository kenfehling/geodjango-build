Compared to its nearby areas, Montevallo is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montevallo costs $160,300. This is approximately $13,900 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $57,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $69,200 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Montevallo's median monthly rent is $743. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $207 (21%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $319 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $94 (11%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
