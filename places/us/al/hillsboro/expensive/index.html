Compared to its nearby areas, Hillsboro is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro is valued at $127,700. Compared to nearby areas this is about $19,760 (18%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $89,800 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $36,600 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $850 per month. It is about $358 (72%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $13 (1%) higher.
