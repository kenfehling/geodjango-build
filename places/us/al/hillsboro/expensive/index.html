Compared to other places in this area, Hillsboro is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hillsboro is $127,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $19,760 (18%) higher. It is approximately $89,800 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $36,600 (40%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $850 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $358 (72%) higher. It is approximately $212 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $13 (1%) higher.
