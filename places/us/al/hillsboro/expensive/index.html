In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hillsboro is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro costs $127,700. This is roughly $19,760 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $89,800 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $36,600 (40%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Hillsboro's median monthly rent is $850. It is approximately $358 (72%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $13 (1%) higher.
