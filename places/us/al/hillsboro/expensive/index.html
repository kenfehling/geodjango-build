Compared to its surrounding areas, Hillsboro is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Hillsboro's median home price is $127,700. This is roughly $19,760 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $89,800 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $36,600 (40%) higher.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $850 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $358 (72%) higher. It is approximately $212 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $13 (1%) higher.
