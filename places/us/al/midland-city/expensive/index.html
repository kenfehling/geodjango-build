Home prices

The median home in Midland City is valued at $100,800. It is roughly $5,540 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $116,700 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $20,400 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Montgomery.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Midland City is $647. This is about $106 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $415 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Montgomery it is approximately $234 (26%) lower.
