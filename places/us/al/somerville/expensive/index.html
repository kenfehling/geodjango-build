Home prices

The median home in Somerville is valued at $90,800. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $43,380 (32%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $126,700 (58%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Somerville's median rent is $594 a month. This is about $139 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $468 (44%) lower, and it is about $243 (29%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
