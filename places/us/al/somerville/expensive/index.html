Home prices

The median home cost in Somerville is $90,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $43,380 (32%) lower. It is about $126,700 (58%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Somerville is $594 per month. It is approximately $139 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $468 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $243 (29%) lower.
