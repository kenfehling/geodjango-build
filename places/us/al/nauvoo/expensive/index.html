Home prices

The median home in Nauvoo is valued at $57,500. This is roughly $12,280 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $160,000 (73%) lower, and is approximately $33,600 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Nauvoo's median rent is $617 per month. It is roughly $10 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $445 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $220 (26%) lower.
