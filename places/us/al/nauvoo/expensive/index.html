In comparison to other places in this area, Nauvoo is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Nauvoo costs $57,500. It is approximately $12,280 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $160,000 (73%) lower, and it is about $33,600 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Nauvoo's median monthly rent is $617. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $10 (1%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $445 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $220 (26%) lower.
