Home prices

The median home in Greensboro is valued at $66,500. It is about $18,660 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $151,000 (69%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $24,600 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Greensboro's median monthly rent is $458. This is roughly $167 (26%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $604 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $379 (45%) lower.
