Home prices

The cost of the median home in Linden is $90,600. This is approximately $18,080 (24%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $126,900 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Tuscaloosa it is about $88,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Linden the median monthly rent is $624. This is about $83 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $438 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Tuscaloosa is about $220 (26%) lower.
