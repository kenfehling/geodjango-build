Compared to its surrounding areas, Linden is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tuscaloosa.

Home prices

The median home in Linden is valued at $90,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $18,080 (24%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $126,900 (58%) lower, and it is about $88,300 (49%) lower compared to the city of Tuscaloosa.

Rents

The median rent in Linden is $624 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $83 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $438 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Tuscaloosa it is approximately $220 (26%) lower.
