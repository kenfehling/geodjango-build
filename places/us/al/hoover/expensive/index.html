Compared to its surrounding areas, Hoover is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home price in Hoover is $291,000. It is approximately $35,640 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $73,500 (33%) higher, and is roughly $199,900 (219%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Hoover is $1,123 a month. It is about $31 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $61 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $286 (34%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
