Compared to other places in this area, Westover is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Westover is valued at $193,800. It is approximately $54,480 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $23,700 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $102,700 (112%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Westover the median monthly rent is $867. Compared to other places in this area this is about $477 (35%) lower. It is about $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $30 (3%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
