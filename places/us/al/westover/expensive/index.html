Home prices

The median home in Westover costs $193,800. It is approximately $54,480 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $23,700 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $102,700 (112%) higher.

Rents

In Westover the median monthly rent is $867. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $477 (35%) lower. It is approximately $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $30 (3%) higher.
