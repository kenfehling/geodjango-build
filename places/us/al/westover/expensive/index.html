Compared to the surrounding areas, Westover is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Westover is $193,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $54,480 (21%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $23,700 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $102,700 (112%) higher.

Rents

Westover's median monthly rent is $867. This is roughly $477 (35%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $30 (3%) higher.
