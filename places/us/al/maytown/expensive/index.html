Compared to nearby areas, Maytown is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Maytown costs $93,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $11,220 (10%) lower.It is approximately $124,200 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $2,200 (2%) higher.

Rents

Maytown's median monthly rent is $939. It is roughly $75 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $123 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $102 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
