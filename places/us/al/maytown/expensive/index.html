In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maytown is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Maytown is valued at $93,300. It is roughly $11,220 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $124,200 (57%) lower, and is approximately $2,200 (2%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Maytown's median monthly rent is $939. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $75 (8%) higher. It is about $123 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $102 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
