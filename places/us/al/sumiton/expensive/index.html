Compared to its surrounding areas, Sumiton is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sumiton is $128,900. It is about $23,460 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $88,600 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $37,800 (41%) higher.

Rents

Sumiton's median rent is $534 a month. This is about $189 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $528 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $303 (36%) lower.
