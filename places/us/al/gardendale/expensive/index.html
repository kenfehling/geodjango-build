Home prices

The median home in Gardendale costs $171,700. This is roughly $52,100 (43%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $45,800 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $80,600 (88%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Gardendale the median rent is $909 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $95 (11%) higher.It is roughly $153 (14%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $72 (8%) higher.
