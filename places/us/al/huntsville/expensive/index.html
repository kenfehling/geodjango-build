Home prices

Rents

In Huntsville the median rent is $827 a month. It is roughly $152 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $235 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
