Much like nearby areas, it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Huntsville costs $182,900. This is about $6,120 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $34,600 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $91,800 (100%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Huntsville's median rent is $827 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $152 (15%) lower.It is approximately $235 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
