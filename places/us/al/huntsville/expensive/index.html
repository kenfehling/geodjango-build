The median home in Huntsville costs $182,900. It is roughly $6,120 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $34,600 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $91,800 (100%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Huntsville's median monthly rent is $827. This is about $152 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $235 (22%) lower, and is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
