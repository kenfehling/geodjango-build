Much like other places in this area, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Huntsville is valued at $182,900. It is approximately $6,120 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $34,600 (15%) lower, and it is about $91,800 (100%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Huntsville is $827 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $152 (15%) lower. It is about $235 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $10 (1%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
