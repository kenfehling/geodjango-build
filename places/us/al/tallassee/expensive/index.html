Home prices

The median home in Tallassee is valued at $134,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,820 (12%) higher. It is roughly $82,900 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $43,500 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Tallassee the median rent is $735 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $92 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $327 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $102 (12%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
