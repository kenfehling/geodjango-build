In comparison to other places in this area, Montgomery is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $121,200. This is about $82,100 (40%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $96,300 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $30,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montgomery is $881. This is about $237 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $181 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $44 (5%) higher.
