Compared to its surrounding areas, Montgomery is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $121,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $82,100 (40%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $96,300 (44%) lower, and is roughly $30,100 (33%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Montgomery's median rent is $881 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $237 (21%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $181 (17%) lower, and is about $44 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
