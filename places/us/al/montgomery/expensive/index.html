Compared to the surrounding areas, Montgomery is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Birminghamhowever, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home value is $121,200. It is about $82,100 (40%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $96,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $30,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $881. It is roughly $237 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $181 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $44 (5%) higher.
