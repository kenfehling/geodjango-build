Compared to other places in this area, Montgomery is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $121,200. This is approximately $82,100 (40%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $96,300 (44%) lower, and is about $30,100 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomery is $881 a month. It is roughly $237 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $181 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $44 (5%) higher.
