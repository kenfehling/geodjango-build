In comparison to the surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Atlanta however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $147,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $21,260 (16%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $69,600 (31%) lower, and is about $142,500 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median monthly rent is $644. It is about $247 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $418 (39%) lower, and it is roughly $509 (44%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
