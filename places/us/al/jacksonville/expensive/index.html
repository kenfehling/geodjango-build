Compared to its surrounding areas, Jacksonville is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Jacksonville's median home value is $147,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $21,260 (16%) higher.It is roughly $69,600 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $142,500 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Jacksonville is $644 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $247 (27%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $418 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $509 (44%) lower.
