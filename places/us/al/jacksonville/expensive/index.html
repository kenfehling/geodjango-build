Compared to nearby areas, Jacksonville is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

Jacksonville's median home price is $147,900. It is approximately $21,260 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $69,600 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $142,500 (49%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Jacksonville's median rent is $644 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $247 (27%) lower.It is roughly $418 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $509 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
