Compared to the surrounding areas, Jacksonville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Jacksonville is $147,900. It is approximately $21,260 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $69,600 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $142,500 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksonville is $644. It is roughly $247 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $418 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $509 (44%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
