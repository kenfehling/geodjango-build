In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Jacksonville is $147,900. It is approximately $21,260 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $69,600 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $142,500 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median rent is $644 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $247 (27%) lower. It is approximately $418 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $509 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
