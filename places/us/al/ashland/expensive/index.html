Compared to its surrounding areas, Ashland is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ashland is $87,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,060 (10%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $129,600 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $3,200 (3%) lower.

Rents

Ashland's median rent is $365 per month. This is roughly $187 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $697 (65%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $472 (56%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
