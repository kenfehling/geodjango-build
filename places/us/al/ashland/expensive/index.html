In comparison to nearby areas, Ashland is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $87,900. This is roughly $10,060 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $129,600 (59%) lower, and it is about $3,200 (3%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Ashland is $365 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $187 (33%) lower.It is roughly $697 (65%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $472 (56%) lower.
