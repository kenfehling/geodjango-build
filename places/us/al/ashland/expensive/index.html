In comparison to nearby areas, Ashland is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ashland is $87,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $10,060 (10%) lower. It is roughly $129,600 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $3,200 (3%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Ashland the median rent is $365 a month. This is roughly $187 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $697 (65%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $472 (56%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
