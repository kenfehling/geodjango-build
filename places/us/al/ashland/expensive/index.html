Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $87,900. This is about $10,060 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $129,600 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $3,200 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $365. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $187 (33%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $697 (65%) lower, and is approximately $472 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
