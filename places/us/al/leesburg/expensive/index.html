Home prices

Rents

Leesburg's median monthly rent is $969. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $357 (58%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $93 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $132 (15%) higher.
