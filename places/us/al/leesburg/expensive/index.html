Home prices

The cost of the median home in Leesburg is $156,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $43,140 (38%) higher.It is roughly $61,200 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $65,200 (71%) higher.

Rents

Leesburg's median rent is $969 per month. It is roughly $357 (58%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $93 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $132 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
