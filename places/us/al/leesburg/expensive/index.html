Home prices

The value of the median home in Leesburg is $156,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $43,140 (38%) higher. It is approximately $61,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $65,200 (71%) higher.

Rents

Leesburg's median rent is $969 per month. This is about $357 (58%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $93 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $132 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
