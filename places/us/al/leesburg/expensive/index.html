In comparison to the surrounding areas, Leesburg is a lot more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Leesburg costs $156,300. This is approximately $43,140 (38%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $61,200 (28%) lower, and is approximately $65,200 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Leesburg's median monthly rent is $969. It is approximately $357 (58%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $93 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $132 (15%) higher.
