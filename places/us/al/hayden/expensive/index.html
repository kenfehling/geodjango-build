In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hayden is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Hayden is valued at $155,100. It is approximately $33,380 (27%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $62,400 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $64,000 (70%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hayden is $911 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $176 (23%) higher. It is approximately $151 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $74 (8%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
