Home prices

Rents

Hayden's median rent is $911 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $176 (23%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $151 (14%) lower, and is approximately $74 (8%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
