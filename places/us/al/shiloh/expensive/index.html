Home prices

The value of the median home in Shiloh is $91,100. It is approximately $18,080 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $126,400 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Shiloh the median rent is $604 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $46 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $458 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $233 (27%) lower.
