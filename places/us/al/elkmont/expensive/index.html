Home prices

The median home in Elkmont is valued at $132,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $9,180 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $85,400 (39%) lower, and is approximately $50,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

In Elkmont the median monthly rent is $375. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $348 (48%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $687 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is approximately $452 (54%) lower.
