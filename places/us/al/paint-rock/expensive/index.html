Compared to other places in this area, Paint Rock is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Paint Rock costs $65,000. This is approximately $55,440 (46%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $152,500 (70%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $26,100 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Paint Rock the median rent is $496 a month. This is roughly $193 (28%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $566 (53%) lower, and it is about $341 (40%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
