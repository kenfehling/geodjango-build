In comparison to its surrounding areas, Daphne is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Mobile however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Daphne is $201,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $25,340 (11%) lower. It is about $16,100 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is approximately $77,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

Daphne's median rent is $1,200 a month. It is approximately $217 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $138 (12%) higher, and it is about $343 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
