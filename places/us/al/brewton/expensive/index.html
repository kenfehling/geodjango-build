Home prices

The median home in Brewton costs $111,600. It is about $33,540 (42%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $105,900 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $12,000 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.

Rents

The median rent in Brewton is $638 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $66 (9%) lower.It is approximately $424 (39%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $219 (25%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.
