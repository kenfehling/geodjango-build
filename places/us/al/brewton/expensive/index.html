Home prices

The median home cost in Brewton is $111,600. It is about $33,540 (42%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $105,900 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $12,000 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.

Rents

In Brewton the median monthly rent is $638. It is about $66 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $424 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is roughly $219 (25%) lower.
