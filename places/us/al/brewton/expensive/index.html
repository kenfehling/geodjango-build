Compared to its surrounding areas, Brewton is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Mobile, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brewton costs $111,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $33,540 (42%) higher. It is roughly $105,900 (48%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $12,000 (9%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brewton is $638. This is roughly $66 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $219 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.
