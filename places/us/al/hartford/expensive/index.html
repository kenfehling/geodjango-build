The price of the median home in Hartford is $92,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $17,380 (23%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $125,400 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dothan is roughly $58,600 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hartford is $654 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $45 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $408 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dothan it is approximately $97 (12%) lower.
