Home prices

The median home cost in Hartford is $92,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $17,380 (23%) higher. It is approximately $125,400 (57%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $58,600 (38%) lower compared to the city of Dothan.

Rents

In Hartford the median rent is $654 a month. It is approximately $45 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $408 (38%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $97 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Dothan.
