Home prices

The median home value in Hartford is $92,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $17,380 (23%) higher.It is approximately $125,400 (57%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Dothan is roughly $58,600 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Hartford the median monthly rent is $654. This is roughly $45 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $408 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $97 (12%) lower compared to the city of Dothan.
