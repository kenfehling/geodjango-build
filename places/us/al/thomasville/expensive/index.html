Home prices

The median home price in Thomasville is $156,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $58,020 (59%) higher. It is approximately $61,400 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Mobile is approximately $32,500 (26%) higher.

Rents

In Thomasville the median rent is $541 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $48 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $521 (49%) lower, and it is about $316 (36%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.
