Compared to other places in this area, South Vinemont is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

South Vinemont's median home price is $98,800. It is approximately $26,060 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $118,700 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $7,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

In South Vinemont the median monthly rent is $695. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $41 (5%) lower. It is about $367 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $142 (16%) lower.
