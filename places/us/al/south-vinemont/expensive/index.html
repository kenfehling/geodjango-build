Home prices

The median home in South Vinemont is valued at $98,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $26,060 (20%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $118,700 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $7,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

In South Vinemont the median monthly rent is $695. This is roughly $41 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $367 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $142 (16%) lower.
