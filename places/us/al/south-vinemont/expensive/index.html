Home prices

South Vinemont's median home value is $98,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $26,060 (20%) lower. It is about $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $7,700 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Vinemont is $695. It is about $41 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $367 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $142 (16%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
