Home prices

Rents

In Avon the median monthly rent is $633. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $95 (13%) lower. It is about $429 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $390 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
