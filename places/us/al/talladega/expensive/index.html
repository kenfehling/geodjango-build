In comparison to other places in this area, Talladega is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home price in Talladega is $86,600. This is about $39,780 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $130,900 (60%) lower, and is about $4,500 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Talladega's median rent is $548 a month. This is approximately $256 (31%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $514 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $289 (34%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
