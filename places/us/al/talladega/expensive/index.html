Home prices

The cost of the median home in Talladega is $86,600. It is about $39,780 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $130,900 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $4,500 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Talladega the median monthly rent is $548. This is about $256 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $514 (48%) lower, and is approximately $289 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
