Home prices

The cost of the median home in Talladega is $86,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $39,780 (31%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $130,900 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $4,500 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Talladega is $548 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $256 (31%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $514 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $289 (34%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
