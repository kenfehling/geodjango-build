Home prices

Rents

In Altoona the median rent is $299 per month. It is roughly $369 (55%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $763 (71%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $538 (64%) lower.
