In comparison to other places in this area, Muscle Shoals is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Huntsville however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Muscle Shoals's median home price is $147,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $32,440 (28%) higher. It is about $70,400 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is about $35,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

Muscle Shoals's median rent is $831 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $152 (22%) higher. It is about $231 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
