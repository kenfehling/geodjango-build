Compared to the surrounding areas, Muscle Shoals is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Muscle Shoals costs $147,100. It is about $32,440 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $70,400 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $35,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Muscle Shoals is $831 per month. It is approximately $152 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $231 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is is very similar.
