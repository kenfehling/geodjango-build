In comparison to other places in this area, Muscle Shoals is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Muscle Shoals is $147,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $32,440 (28%) higher.It is approximately $70,400 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is about $35,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Muscle Shoals the median monthly rent is $831. This is approximately $152 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $231 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Huntsville.
