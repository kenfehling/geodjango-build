Home prices

The value of the median home in Steele is $76,200. It is about $40,780 (34%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $141,300 (64%) lower, and is roughly $14,900 (16%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Steele's median monthly rent is $824. This is about $67 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $238 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $13 (1%) lower.
