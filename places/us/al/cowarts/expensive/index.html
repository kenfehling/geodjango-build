Home prices

The price of the median home in Cowarts is $104,500. This is roughly $16,640 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $113,000 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tallahassee it is approximately $99,300 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Cowarts the median rent is $650 per month. This is about $74 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $412 (38%) lower, and is approximately $373 (36%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.
