Compared to the surrounding areas, Lincoln is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Lincoln is $131,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $9,900 (8%) higher.It is roughly $86,500 (39%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $39,900 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Lincoln the median rent is $856 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $188 (28%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $206 (19%) lower, and is about $19 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
