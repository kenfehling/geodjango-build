Home prices

Rents

Lincoln's median monthly rent is $856. It is approximately $188 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $206 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $19 (2%) higher.
