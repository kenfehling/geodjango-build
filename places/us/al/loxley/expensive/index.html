Home prices

The median home price in Loxley is $204,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $11,820 (6%) higher. It is approximately $12,600 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is about $81,300 (65%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Loxley is $728. This is about $270 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $334 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is approximately $129 (15%) lower.
