Compared to its surrounding areas, Loxley is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Mobile, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Loxley's median home price is $204,900. This is approximately $11,820 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $12,600 (5%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $81,300 (65%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Loxley is $728. This is approximately $270 (27%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $334 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is approximately $129 (15%) lower.
