Compared to other places in this area, Loxley is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Mobile.

Home prices

The median home price in Loxley is $204,900. This is approximately $11,820 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $12,600 (5%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Mobile it is about $81,300 (65%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Loxley is $728. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $270 (27%) lower. It is approximately $334 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $129 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.
