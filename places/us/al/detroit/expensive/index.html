Compared to its surrounding areas, Detroit is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Detroit is valued at $66,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $25,800 (27%) lower. It is about $151,000 (69%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $24,600 (27%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Detroit is $315 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $176 (35%) lower. It is about $747 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $522 (62%) lower.
