Home prices

The median home cost in Detroit is $66,500. This is roughly $25,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $151,000 (69%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $24,600 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Detroit the median rent is $315 a month. It is roughly $176 (35%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $747 (70%) lower, and it is about $522 (62%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
