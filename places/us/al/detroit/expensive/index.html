Compared to the surrounding areas, Detroit is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Detroit is valued at $66,500. This is roughly $25,800 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is about $151,000 (69%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $24,600 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Detroit is $315. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $176 (35%) lower.It is approximately $747 (70%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $522 (62%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
