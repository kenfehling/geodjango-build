Home prices

The value of the median home in Detroit is $66,500. It is roughly $25,800 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $151,000 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $24,600 (27%) lower.

Rents

Detroit's median rent is $315 a month. It is roughly $176 (35%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $747 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $522 (62%) lower.
