Home prices

The median home price in Andalusia is $112,500. This is roughly $12,160 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $105,000 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Montgomery it is approximately $8,700 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Andalusia the median rent is $647 per month. This is approximately $36 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $415 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $234 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Montgomery.
