Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Andalusia is $647 a month. This is about $36 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $415 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Montgomery is about $234 (26%) lower.
