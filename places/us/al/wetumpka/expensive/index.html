Compared to its surrounding areas, Wetumpka is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Wetumpka is $137,100. It is approximately $29,600 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $80,400 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $46,000 (50%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wetumpka is $792 a month. This is roughly $196 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $270 (25%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $45 (5%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
