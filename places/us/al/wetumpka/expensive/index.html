Compared to other places in this area, Wetumpka is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wetumpka is $137,100. This is about $29,600 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $80,400 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $46,000 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Wetumpka the median monthly rent is $792. It is roughly $196 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $270 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $45 (5%) lower.
