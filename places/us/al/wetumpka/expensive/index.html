In comparison to other places in this area, Wetumpka is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wetumpka is $137,100. It is roughly $29,600 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $80,400 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $46,000 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Wetumpka the median rent is $792 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $196 (19%) lower. It is about $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $45 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
