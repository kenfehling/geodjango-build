Compared to nearby areas, Columbiana is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Columbiana costs $125,000. This is approximately $40,680 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $33,900 (37%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Columbiana is $593 a month. This is approximately $514 (46%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $469 (44%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $244 (29%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
