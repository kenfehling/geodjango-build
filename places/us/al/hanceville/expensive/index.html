Home prices

Hanceville's median home value is $99,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $19,660 (16%) lower. It is about $117,600 (54%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $8,800 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hanceville is $668 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $62 (8%) lower. It is roughly $394 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $169 (20%) lower.
