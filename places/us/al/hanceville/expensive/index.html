Home prices

The median home value in Hanceville is $99,900. This is roughly $19,660 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $117,600 (54%) lower, and it is roughly $8,800 (9%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hanceville is $668. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $62 (8%) lower.It is about $394 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $169 (20%) lower.
