Compared to its surrounding areas, Rock Creek is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Rock Creek's median home price is $115,000. This is approximately $15,400 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $102,500 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $23,900 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rock Creek is $860 per month. It is approximately $92 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $202 (19%) lower, and is roughly $23 (2%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
