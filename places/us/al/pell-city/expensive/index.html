Compared to nearby areas, Pell City is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pell City is valued at $183,800. It is roughly $40,940 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $33,700 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $92,700 (101%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Pell City's median rent is $858 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $91 (11%) higher. It is approximately $204 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $21 (2%) higher.
