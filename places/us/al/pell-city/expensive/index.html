Home prices

The value of the median home in Pell City is $183,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $40,940 (28%) higher. It is roughly $33,700 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $92,700 (101%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Pell City is $858 per month. It is about $91 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $204 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $21 (2%) higher.
