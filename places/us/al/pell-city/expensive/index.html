Home prices

The median home in Pell City is valued at $183,800. It is approximately $40,940 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $33,700 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $92,700 (101%) higher.

Rents

Pell City's median rent is $858 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $91 (11%) higher.It is approximately $204 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $21 (2%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
