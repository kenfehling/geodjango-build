Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon is valued at $87,400. This is roughly $50,620 (36%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $130,100 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is roughly $36,200 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Vernon is $746 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $115 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $316 (29%) lower, and is about $111 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.
