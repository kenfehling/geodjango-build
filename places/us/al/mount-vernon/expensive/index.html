Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon is valued at $87,400. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $50,620 (36%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $130,100 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $36,200 (29%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

Mount Vernon's median rent is $746 a month. This is approximately $115 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $316 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is roughly $111 (12%) lower.
