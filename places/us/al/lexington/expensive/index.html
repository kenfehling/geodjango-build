Home prices

The price of the median home in Lexington is $115,400. This is approximately $5,680 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $102,100 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $67,500 (36%) lower.

Rents

Lexington's median monthly rent is $554. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $75 (11%) lower.It is roughly $508 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $273 (33%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
