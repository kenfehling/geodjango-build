Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hobson City is $91,400. It is roughly $6,000 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $126,100 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Hobson City's median monthly rent is $716. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38 (5%) higher. It is roughly $346 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $121 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
