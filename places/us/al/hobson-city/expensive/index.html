In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hobson City is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Hobson City is valued at $91,400. This is approximately $6,000 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $126,100 (57%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is is very similar.

Rents

In Hobson City the median monthly rent is $716. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $38 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $346 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $121 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
