Compared to other places in this area, Fort Payne is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Payne is valued at $124,900. This is about $8,120 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $92,600 (42%) lower, and is roughly $58,000 (31%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Fort Payne's median monthly rent is $595. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $59 (10%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $467 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $232 (28%) lower.
