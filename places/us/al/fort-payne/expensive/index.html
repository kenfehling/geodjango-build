In comparison to nearby areas, Fort Payne is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Payne is $124,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $8,120 (6%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $92,600 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $58,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

Fort Payne's median rent is $595 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $59 (10%) higher.It is roughly $467 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $232 (28%) lower.
