Home prices

The median home value in Fort Payne is $124,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $8,120 (6%) higher.It is approximately $92,600 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $58,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Payne the median monthly rent is $595. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $59 (10%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $467 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is about $232 (28%) lower.
