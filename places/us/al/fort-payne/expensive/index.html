In comparison to other places in this area, Fort Payne is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Payne is valued at $124,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $8,120 (6%) higher. It is approximately $92,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $58,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Payne the median monthly rent is $595. This is approximately $59 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $467 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is approximately $232 (28%) lower.
