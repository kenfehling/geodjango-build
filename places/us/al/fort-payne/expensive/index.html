Compared to nearby areas, Fort Payne is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Huntsville however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Payne costs $124,900. This is roughly $8,120 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $92,600 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $58,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Payne is $595. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $59 (10%) higher. It is approximately $467 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $232 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
