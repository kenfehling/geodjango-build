Home prices

The median home in Mosses is valued at $53,400. This is roughly $6,340 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $164,100 (75%) lower, and compared to the city of Montgomery it is approximately $67,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mosses is $533. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $26 (4%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $529 (49%) lower, and is roughly $348 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Montgomery.
