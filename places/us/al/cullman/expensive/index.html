Home prices

The median home in Cullman costs $142,800. This is roughly $20,720 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $74,700 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $51,700 (56%) higher.

Rents

In Cullman the median monthly rent is $671. This is approximately $38 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $391 (36%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $166 (19%) lower.
