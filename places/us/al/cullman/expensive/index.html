Compared to other places in this area, Cullman is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cullman costs $142,800. This is roughly $20,720 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $74,700 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $51,700 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Cullman's median monthly rent is $671. This is roughly $38 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $391 (36%) lower, and is roughly $166 (19%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
