In comparison to other places in this area, Anniston is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Anniston costs $97,600. This is approximately $22,800 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $119,900 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $192,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

Anniston's median rent is $672 a month. It is approximately $136 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $390 (36%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $481 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
