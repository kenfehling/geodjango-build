Home prices

The median home in Anniston costs $97,600. This is approximately $22,800 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $119,900 (55%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $192,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Anniston the median rent is $672 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $136 (16%) lower. It is approximately $390 (36%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $481 (41%) lower.
