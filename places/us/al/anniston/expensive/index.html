Home prices

The cost of the median home in Anniston is $97,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $22,800 (18%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $119,900 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $192,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Anniston the median rent is $672 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $136 (16%) lower.It is about $390 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $481 (41%) lower.
