Compared to nearby areas, Florence is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

Florence's median home price is $142,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $7,020 (5%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $75,100 (34%) lower, and is about $40,500 (22%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median rent in Florence is $643 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $156 (19%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $419 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $184 (22%) lower.
