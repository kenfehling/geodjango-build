In comparison to its nearby areas, Florence is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Huntsville however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Florence is $142,400. This is roughly $7,020 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $75,100 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $40,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $643. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $156 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $419 (39%) lower, and it is about $184 (22%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
