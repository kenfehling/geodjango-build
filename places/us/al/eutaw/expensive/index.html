Rents

The median rent in Eutaw is $608 a month. It is approximately $28 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $454 (42%) lower, and is approximately $229 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
