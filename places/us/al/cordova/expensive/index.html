Home prices

Rents

In Cordova the median monthly rent is $558. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $6 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $504 (47%) lower, and it is about $279 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
