Home prices

The median home in Camden costs $103,800. It is about $12,240 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $113,700 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Montgomery is roughly $17,400 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Camden the median rent is $417 per month. This is roughly $69 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $645 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Montgomery is approximately $464 (52%) lower.
