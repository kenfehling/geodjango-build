Home prices

The median home in Roanoke is valued at $85,900. This is approximately $23,720 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $131,600 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $204,500 (70%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Roanoke's median rent is $602 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $96 (19%) higher. It is about $460 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $551 (47%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
