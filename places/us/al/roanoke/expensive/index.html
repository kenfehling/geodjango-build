Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roanoke is $602. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $96 (19%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $460 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $551 (47%) lower.
