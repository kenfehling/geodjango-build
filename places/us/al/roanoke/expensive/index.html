Compared to its nearby areas, Roanoke is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Roanoke costs $85,900. It is roughly $23,720 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $131,600 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $204,500 (70%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Roanoke's median monthly rent is $602. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $96 (19%) higher.It is approximately $460 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is approximately $551 (47%) lower.
