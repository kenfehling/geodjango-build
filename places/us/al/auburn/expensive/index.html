Home prices

The value of the median home in Auburn is $266,200. This is roughly $141,160 (112%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $48,700 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $124,900 (88%) higher.

Rents

In Auburn the median rent is $884 per month. It is about $211 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $178 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $22 (2%) lower.
