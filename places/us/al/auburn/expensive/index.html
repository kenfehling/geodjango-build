Home prices

The cost of the median home in Auburn is $266,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $141,160 (112%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $48,700 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $124,900 (88%) higher.

Rents

Auburn's median rent is $884 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $211 (31%) higher. It is approximately $178 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $22 (2%) lower.
