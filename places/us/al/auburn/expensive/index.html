Home prices

The median home in Auburn is valued at $266,200. It is about $141,160 (112%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $48,700 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $124,900 (88%) higher.

Rents

In Auburn the median rent is $884 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $211 (31%) higher. It is roughly $178 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $22 (2%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
