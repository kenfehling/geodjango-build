In comparison to the surrounding areas, Auburn is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Auburn is $266,200. It is about $141,160 (112%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $48,700 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $124,900 (88%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $884 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $211 (31%) higher. It is roughly $178 (16%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $22 (2%) lower.
