Home prices

Rents

In Ashville the median monthly rent is $1,205. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $477 (65%) higher.It is approximately $143 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $368 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
