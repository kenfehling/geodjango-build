Rents

Ashville's median monthly rent is $1,205. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $477 (65%) higher. It is about $143 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $368 (43%) higher.
