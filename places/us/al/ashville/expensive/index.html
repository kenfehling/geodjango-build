Home prices

The median home in Ashville is valued at $110,200. It is about $31,040 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $107,300 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $19,100 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashville is $1,205. This is approximately $477 (65%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $143 (13%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $368 (43%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
