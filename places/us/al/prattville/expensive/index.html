Compared to nearby areas, Prattville is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Prattville costs $162,700. This is about $37,600 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $54,800 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $71,600 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Prattville the median rent is $1,046 a month. This is roughly $172 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $16 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $209 (24%) higher.
