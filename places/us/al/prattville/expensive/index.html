Compared to other places in this area, Prattville is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home value in Prattville is $162,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $37,600 (30%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $54,800 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $71,600 (78%) higher.

Rents

Prattville's median rent is $1,046 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $172 (19%) higher.It is about $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $209 (24%) higher.
