Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fultondale is $147,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $35,600 (31%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $69,800 (32%) lower, and it is about $56,600 (62%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Fultondale's median monthly rent is $993. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $202 (25%) higher.It is roughly $69 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $156 (18%) higher.
