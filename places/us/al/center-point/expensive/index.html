Compared to its surrounding areas, Center Point is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Center Point is $97,800. It is approximately $50,160 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $119,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $6,700 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Center Point is $861. It is about $104 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $201 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $24 (2%) higher.
