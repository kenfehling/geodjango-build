Home prices

The median home in Center Point costs $97,800. It is about $50,160 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $119,700 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $6,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

Center Point's median monthly rent is $861. This is about $104 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $201 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $24 (2%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
