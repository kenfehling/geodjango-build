Home prices

Center Point's median home cost is $97,800. It is roughly $50,160 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $119,700 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $6,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

Center Point's median rent is $861 a month. This is approximately $104 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $201 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $24 (2%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
