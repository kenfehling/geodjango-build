In comparison to its surrounding areas, Gulf Shores is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Mobile.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Gulf Shores is $243,100. This is roughly $39,060 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $25,600 (11%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $119,500 (96%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

Gulf Shores's median rent is $1,086 a month. It is approximately $206 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $24 (2%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is about $229 (26%) higher.
