In comparison to its nearby areas, Gulf Shores is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Mobile, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Gulf Shores's median home price is $243,100. This is approximately $39,060 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $25,600 (11%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Mobile it is roughly $119,500 (96%) higher.

Rents

In Gulf Shores the median rent is $1,086 per month. This is roughly $206 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $24 (2%) higher, and is approximately $229 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
