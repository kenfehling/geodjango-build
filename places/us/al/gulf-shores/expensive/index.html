In comparison to nearby areas, Gulf Shores is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Mobile, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Gulf Shores's median home value is $243,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $39,060 (19%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $25,600 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Mobile it is roughly $119,500 (96%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Gulf Shores is $1,086 per month. This is approximately $206 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $24 (2%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is roughly $229 (26%) higher.
