Compared to its surrounding areas, Gulf Shores is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Mobilehowever, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gulf Shores is valued at $243,100. It is roughly $39,060 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $25,600 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $119,500 (96%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

Gulf Shores's median rent is $1,086 a month. It is roughly $206 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $24 (2%) higher, and is roughly $229 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
