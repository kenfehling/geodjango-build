Compared to the surrounding areas, Dunnavant is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dunnavant costs $125,000. It is about $81,920 (39%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $92,500 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $33,900 (37%) higher.

Rents

Dunnavant's median rent is $746 per month. It is about $378 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $316 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $91 (10%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
