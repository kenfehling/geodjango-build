In comparison to other places in this area, Kansas is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Kansas costs $56,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $14,640 (20%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $160,900 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $34,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Kansas the median monthly rent is $650. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $42 (6%) lower. It is roughly $412 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $187 (22%) lower.
