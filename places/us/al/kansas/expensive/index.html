Home prices

The median home in Kansas costs $56,600. This is about $14,640 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $160,900 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $34,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kansas is $650. This is approximately $42 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $412 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $187 (22%) lower.
