Compared to its surrounding areas, Boaz is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Boaz's median home cost is $105,100. It is approximately $8,260 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $112,400 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $14,000 (15%) higher.

Rents

Boaz's median rent is $618 a month. It is approximately $88 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $444 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $219 (26%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
