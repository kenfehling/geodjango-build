Compared to its surrounding areas, Wilton is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Wilton is $106,400. It is roughly $28,500 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $111,100 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $15,300 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wilton is $518. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $378 (42%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $544 (51%) lower, and is roughly $319 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
