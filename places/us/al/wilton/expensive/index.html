Home prices

The median home cost in Wilton is $106,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $28,500 (21%) lower. It is approximately $111,100 (51%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $15,300 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Wilton's median monthly rent is $518. It is roughly $378 (42%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $544 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $319 (38%) lower.
