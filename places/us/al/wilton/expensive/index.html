Wilton's median home value is $106,400. This is roughly $28,500 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $111,100 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $15,300 (16%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Wilton is $518 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $378 (42%) lower.It is roughly $544 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $319 (38%) lower.
