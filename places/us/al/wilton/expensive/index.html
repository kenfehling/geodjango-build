In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wilton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wilton costs $106,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $28,500 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $111,100 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $15,300 (16%) higher.

Rents

Wilton's median monthly rent is $518. It is roughly $378 (42%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $544 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $319 (38%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
