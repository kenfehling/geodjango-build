In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wilton is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Wilton is $106,400. It is about $28,500 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $111,100 (51%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $15,300 (16%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Wilton's median rent is $518 a month. It is roughly $378 (42%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $544 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $319 (38%) lower.
