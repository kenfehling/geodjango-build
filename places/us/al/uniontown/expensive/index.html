Home prices

The median home value in Uniontown is $60,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $25,220 (29%) lower. It is about $157,100 (72%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $30,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Uniontown the median monthly rent is $492. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $76 (13%) lower. It is approximately $570 (53%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $345 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
