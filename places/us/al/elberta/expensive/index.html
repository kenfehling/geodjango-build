Home prices

The median home in Elberta is valued at $121,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $93,240 (43%) lower. It is about $96,300 (44%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $2,400 (1%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

The median rent in Elberta is $676 per month. This is roughly $233 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $386 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is roughly $181 (21%) lower.
