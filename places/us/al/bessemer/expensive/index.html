Home prices

The median home value in Bessemer is $86,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $18,060 (26%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $131,000 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $4,600 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bessemer is $743. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $37 (4%) lower.It is roughly $319 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $94 (11%) lower.
