Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Bessemer is $743 a month. It is about $37 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $319 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $94 (11%) lower.
