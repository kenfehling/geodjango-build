In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bessemer is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Bessemer's median home cost is $86,500. This is approximately $18,060 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $131,000 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $4,600 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Bessemer the median monthly rent is $743. It is roughly $37 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $319 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $94 (11%) lower.
