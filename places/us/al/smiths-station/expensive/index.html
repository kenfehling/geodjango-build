Home prices

The median home in Smiths Station costs $151,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $18,120 (10%) lower. It is roughly $66,300 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $9,900 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Smiths Station is $899 per month. It is about $25 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $163 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is is very similar.
