Home prices

The median home in Decatur is valued at $134,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $2,300 (1%) higher. It is about $83,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $43,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Decatur is $655. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $63 (8%) lower. It is approximately $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $182 (21%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
