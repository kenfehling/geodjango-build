Home prices

The median home cost in Decatur is $134,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $2,300 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $83,000 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $43,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Decatur the median rent is $655 per month. It is approximately $63 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $182 (21%) lower.
