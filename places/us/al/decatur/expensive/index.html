Much like other places in this area, it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Decatur's median home value is $134,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $2,300 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $83,000 (38%) lower, and is approximately $43,400 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Decatur is $655 a month. This is roughly $63 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $407 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $182 (21%) lower.
