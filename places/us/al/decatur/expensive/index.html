Home prices

Decatur's median home cost is $134,500. This is roughly $2,300 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $83,000 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $43,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Decatur is $655. This is approximately $63 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $182 (21%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
