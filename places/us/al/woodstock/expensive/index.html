Compared to other places in this area, Woodstock is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Woodstock is $130,300. It is approximately $14,000 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $87,200 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $39,200 (43%) higher.

Rents

Woodstock's median rent is $569 per month. It is roughly $500 (46%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $493 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $268 (32%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
