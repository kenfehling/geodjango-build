Home prices

The median home in Hamilton is valued at $106,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $21,940 (25%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $110,800 (50%) lower, and it is about $15,600 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Hamilton is $515 per month. It is approximately $113 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $547 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $322 (38%) lower.
