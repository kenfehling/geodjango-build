In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hamilton is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hamilton is valued at $106,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $21,940 (25%) higher. It is roughly $110,800 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $15,600 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Hamilton is $515 a month. This is approximately $113 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $547 (51%) lower, and is about $322 (38%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
