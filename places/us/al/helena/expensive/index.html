Home prices

The price of the median home in Helena is $195,100. It is approximately $34,280 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $22,400 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $104,000 (114%) higher.

Rents

Helena's median monthly rent is $1,341. It is about $405 (43%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $279 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $504 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
