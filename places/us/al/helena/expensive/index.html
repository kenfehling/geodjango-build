Home prices

The median home in Helena costs $195,100. It is about $34,280 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $22,400 (10%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $104,000 (114%) higher.

Rents

In Helena the median rent is $1,341 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $405 (43%) higher.It is about $279 (26%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $504 (60%) higher.
