Home prices

The median home in Helena costs $195,100. It is approximately $34,280 (21%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $22,400 (10%) lower, and is about $104,000 (114%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Helena is $1,341. It is approximately $405 (43%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $279 (26%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $504 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
