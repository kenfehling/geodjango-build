Home prices

Helena's median home value is $195,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $34,280 (21%) higher. It is approximately $22,400 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $104,000 (114%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Helena is $1,341. It is approximately $405 (43%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $279 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $504 (60%) higher.
