Home prices

The median home in Pelham costs $189,000. This is about $49,200 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $28,500 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $97,900 (107%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Pelham's median rent is $1,119 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $162 (12%) lower.It is roughly $57 (5%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $282 (33%) higher.
