Compared to its nearby areas, Pelham is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home price in Pelham is $189,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $49,200 (20%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $28,500 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $97,900 (107%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Pelham the median rent is $1,119 a month. It is approximately $162 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $57 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $282 (33%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
