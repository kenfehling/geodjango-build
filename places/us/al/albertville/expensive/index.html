Compared to the surrounding areas, Albertville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Albertville is valued at $120,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $9,800 (7%) lower. It is approximately $97,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $29,000 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Albertville the median monthly rent is $671. In comparison to its nearby areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $391 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $166 (19%) lower.
