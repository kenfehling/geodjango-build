Home prices

The median home in Albertville costs $120,100. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $9,800 (7%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $97,400 (44%) lower, and is approximately $29,000 (31%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Albertville the median monthly rent is $671. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. Compared to the entire country it is about $391 (36%) lower, and is approximately $166 (19%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
