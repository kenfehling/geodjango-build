In comparison to the surrounding areas, Triana is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Triana's median home value is $139,500. It is about $19,280 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $78,000 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $48,400 (53%) higher.

Rents

In Triana the median rent is $1,051 a month. It is approximately $283 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $214 (25%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
