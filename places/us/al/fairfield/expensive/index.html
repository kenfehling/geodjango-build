Home prices

Rents

In Fairfield the median monthly rent is $754. It is approximately $85 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $83 (9%) lower.
