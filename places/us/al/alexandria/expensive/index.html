In comparison to its surrounding areas, Alexandria is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Alexandria is $129,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $12,600 (10%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $87,700 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $38,700 (42%) higher.

Rents

In Alexandria the median monthly rent is $919. This is about $134 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $143 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $82 (9%) higher.
