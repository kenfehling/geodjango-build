Home prices

The median home in Alexandria is valued at $129,800. This is about $12,600 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $87,700 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $38,700 (42%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Alexandria is $919 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $134 (17%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $143 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $82 (9%) higher.
