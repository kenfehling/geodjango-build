Compared to other places in this area, Alexandria is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Alexandria is $129,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $12,600 (10%) higher. It is about $87,700 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $38,700 (42%) higher.

Rents

In Alexandria the median monthly rent is $919. This is roughly $134 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $143 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $82 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
