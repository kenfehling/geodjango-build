Rents

In Hurtsboro the median rent is $519 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $120 (18%) lower. It is roughly $543 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $387 (42%) lower.
