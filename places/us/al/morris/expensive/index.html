Home prices

The median home in Morris is valued at $184,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $41,020 (28%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $33,200 (15%) lower, and it is about $93,200 (102%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Morris is $821 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $52 (6%) higher. It is about $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
