Home prices

The value of the median home in Morris is $184,300. It is approximately $41,020 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $33,200 (15%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $93,200 (102%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morris is $821. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $52 (6%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $241 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $16 (1%) lower.
