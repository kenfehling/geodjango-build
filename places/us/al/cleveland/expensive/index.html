Compared to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cleveland is $95,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $26,900 (21%) lower. It is about $121,800 (56%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $4,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $638. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $88 (12%) lower. It is about $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $199 (23%) lower.
