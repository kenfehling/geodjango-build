Compared to its nearby areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cleveland is $95,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $26,900 (21%) lower. It is about $121,800 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $4,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $638 per month. This is approximately $88 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $199 (23%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
