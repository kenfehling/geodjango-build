In comparison to its nearby areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $95,700. This is approximately $26,900 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $121,800 (56%) lower, and it is roughly $4,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $638 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $88 (12%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $424 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $199 (23%) lower.
