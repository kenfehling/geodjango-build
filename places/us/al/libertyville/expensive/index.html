Home prices

The cost of the median home in Libertyville is $156,300. This is approximately $64,720 (70%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $61,200 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Montgomery is about $35,100 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Libertyville the median rent is $675 per month. This is roughly $70 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $387 (36%) lower, and is approximately $206 (23%) lower compared to the city of Montgomery.
