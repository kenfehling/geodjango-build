Home prices

The median home in Homewood costs $347,800. It is approximately $54,060 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $130,300 (59%) higher, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $256,700 (281%) higher.

Rents

In Homewood the median rent is $1,112 per month. It is is very similar compared to nearby areas. It is about $50 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $275 (32%) higher.
