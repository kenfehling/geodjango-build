Compared to other places in this area, Graysville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Graysville is $92,200. This is approximately $24,140 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $125,300 (57%) lower, and it is roughly $1,100 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Graysville is $862 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $47 (5%) higher. It is roughly $200 (18%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $25 (2%) higher.
