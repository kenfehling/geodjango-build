Home prices

The median home value in West Jefferson is $108,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $5,600 (5%) higher. It is about $108,600 (49%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $17,800 (19%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

West Jefferson's median monthly rent is $917. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $203 (28%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $145 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $80 (9%) higher.
