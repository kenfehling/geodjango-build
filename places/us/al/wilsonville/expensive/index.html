Home prices

The median home in Wilsonville costs $154,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $42,260 (37%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $63,100 (29%) lower, and is about $63,300 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Wilsonville's median monthly rent is $979. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $244 (33%) higher. It is roughly $83 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $142 (16%) higher.
