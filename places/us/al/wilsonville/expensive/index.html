Home prices

Wilsonville's median home price is $154,400. This is about $42,260 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $63,100 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $63,300 (69%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Wilsonville the median monthly rent is $979. Compared to other places in this area this is about $244 (33%) higher. It is roughly $83 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $142 (16%) higher.
