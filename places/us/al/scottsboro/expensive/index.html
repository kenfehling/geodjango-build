In comparison to the surrounding areas, Scottsboro is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Scottsboro costs $117,900. This is roughly $21,620 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $99,600 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $26,800 (29%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Scottsboro is $599 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $26 (4%) lower.It is approximately $463 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $238 (28%) lower.
