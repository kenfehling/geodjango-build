Compared to other places in this area, Scottsboro is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Scottsboro is valued at $117,900. This is roughly $21,620 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $99,600 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $26,800 (29%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scottsboro is $599. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $26 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $463 (43%) lower, and is approximately $238 (28%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
