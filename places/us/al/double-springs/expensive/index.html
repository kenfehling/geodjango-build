In comparison to other places in this area, Double Springs is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Double Springs is $93,600. It is about $10,080 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $123,900 (56%) lower, and is roughly $2,500 (2%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Double Springs's median rent is $482 per month. This is about $52 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $580 (54%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $355 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
