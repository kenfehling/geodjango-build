Rents

The median rent in Dauphin Island is $1,111 per month. This is roughly $235 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $49 (4%) higher, and it is about $254 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
