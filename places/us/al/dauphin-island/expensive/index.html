Compared to the surrounding areas, Dauphin Island is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Mobile.

Home prices

Dauphin Island's median home value is $257,600. This is approximately $41,840 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $40,100 (18%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Mobile is approximately $134,000 (108%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Dauphin Island is $1,111 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $235 (26%) higher. It is roughly $49 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is about $254 (29%) higher.
