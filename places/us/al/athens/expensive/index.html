In comparison to other places in this area, Athens is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Athens costs $161,400. This is approximately $10,100 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $56,100 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $70,300 (77%) higher.

Rents

Athens's median rent is $664 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $61 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $398 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $173 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
