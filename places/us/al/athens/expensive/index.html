Home prices

The median home in Athens costs $161,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $10,100 (5%) lower.It is approximately $56,100 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $70,300 (77%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Athens is $664 per month. This is roughly $61 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $398 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $173 (20%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
