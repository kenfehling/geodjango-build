Home prices

Midfield's median home value is $73,600. This is approximately $12,840 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $143,900 (66%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $17,500 (19%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Midfield's median rent is $911 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $103 (12%) higher. It is about $151 (14%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $74 (8%) higher.
