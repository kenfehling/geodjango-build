Home prices

The cost of the median home in Selma is $87,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $13,540 (18%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $130,200 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $3,800 (4%) lower.

Rents

Selma's median monthly rent is $612. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $47 (8%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $450 (42%) lower, and is about $225 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
