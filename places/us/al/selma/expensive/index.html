Home prices

The median home in Selma is valued at $87,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $13,540 (18%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $130,200 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $3,800 (4%) lower.

Rents

Selma's median monthly rent is $612. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $47 (8%) higher.It is about $450 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $225 (26%) lower.
