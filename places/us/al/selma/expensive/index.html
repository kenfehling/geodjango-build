Home prices

The median home in Selma costs $87,300. This is approximately $13,540 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $130,200 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $3,800 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Selma the median monthly rent is $612. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $47 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $450 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $225 (26%) lower.
