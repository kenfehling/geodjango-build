Compared to other places in this area, Irondale is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Irondale costs $142,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $75,560 (34%) lower. It is about $75,000 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $51,400 (56%) higher.

Rents

Irondale's median monthly rent is $1,041. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $83 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $21 (1%) lower, and is approximately $204 (24%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
