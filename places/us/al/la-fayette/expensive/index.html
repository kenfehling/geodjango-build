In comparison to its surrounding areas, La Fayette is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in La Fayette costs $105,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $7,740 (7%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $111,900 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $184,800 (63%) lower.

Rents

In La Fayette the median rent is $558 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $82 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $504 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $595 (51%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
