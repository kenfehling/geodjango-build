Home prices

The median home in Huguley costs $84,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $33,740 (28%) lower.It is roughly $133,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $205,900 (70%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Huguley's median rent is $717 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $28 (4%) higher.It is about $345 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $436 (37%) lower.
