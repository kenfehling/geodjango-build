In comparison to other places in this area, Falkville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Falkville is $86,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $40,040 (31%) lower. It is approximately $130,700 (60%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $4,300 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Falkville is $692 a month. This is approximately $50 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $370 (34%) lower, and it is about $145 (17%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
