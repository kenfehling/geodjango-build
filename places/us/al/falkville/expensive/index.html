The median home in Falkville costs $86,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $40,040 (31%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $130,700 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $4,300 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Falkville is $692. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $50 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and is roughly $145 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
