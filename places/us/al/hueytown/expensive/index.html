In comparison to nearby areas, Hueytown is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hueytown costs $116,700. This is roughly $28,840 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $100,800 (46%) lower, and is roughly $25,600 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Hueytown's median monthly rent is $874. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $66 (8%) higher. It is approximately $188 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $37 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
