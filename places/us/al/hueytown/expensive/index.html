Rents

In Hueytown the median monthly rent is $874. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $66 (8%) higher.It is roughly $188 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $37 (4%) higher.
