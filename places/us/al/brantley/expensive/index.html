Compared to its surrounding areas, Brantley is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Montgomery, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brantley is valued at $89,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $5,680 (6%) higher. It is about $128,200 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $31,900 (26%) lower compared to the city of Montgomery.

Rents

In Brantley the median rent is $434 a month. It is roughly $154 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $628 (59%) lower, and is about $447 (50%) lower compared to the city of Montgomery.
