Home prices

The median home in Brantley costs $89,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $5,680 (6%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $128,200 (58%) lower, and it is about $31,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Montgomery.

Rents

In Brantley the median rent is $434 per month. This is approximately $154 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $628 (59%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Montgomery it is approximately $447 (50%) lower.
