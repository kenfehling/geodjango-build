Home prices

Rents

In Brantley the median monthly rent is $434. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $154 (26%) lower.It is about $628 (59%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $447 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Montgomery.
