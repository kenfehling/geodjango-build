Rents

In Adamsville the median rent is $982 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $171 (21%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $80 (7%) lower, and it is about $145 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
