Compared to other places in this area, New Hope is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Hope costs $124,700. It is roughly $7,220 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $92,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $33,600 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

New Hope's median monthly rent is $826. This is approximately $208 (33%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $236 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $11 (1%) lower.
