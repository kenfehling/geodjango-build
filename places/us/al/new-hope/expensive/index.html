Home prices

The price of the median home in New Hope is $124,700. It is approximately $7,220 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $92,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $33,600 (36%) higher.

Rents

In New Hope the median rent is $826 per month. It is about $208 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $236 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $11 (1%) lower.
