Compared to the surrounding areas, Chelsea is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home value in Chelsea is $232,700. This is roughly $57,360 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $15,200 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $141,600 (155%) higher.

Rents

In Chelsea the median monthly rent is $1,448. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $130 (9%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $386 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $611 (72%) higher.
