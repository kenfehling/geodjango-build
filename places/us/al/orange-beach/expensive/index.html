The median home price in Orange Beach is $297,800. It is about $104,700 (54%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $80,300 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Mobile it is roughly $174,200 (140%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Orange Beach is $1,117 a month. It is approximately $243 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $55 (5%) higher, and it is about $260 (30%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
