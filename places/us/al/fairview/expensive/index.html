Home prices

The median home in Fairview is valued at $161,500. This is roughly $43,220 (36%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $56,000 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $70,400 (77%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Fairview's median rent is $585 per month. This is about $112 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $477 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $252 (30%) lower.
