Home prices

The median home in Clay is valued at $155,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $3,540 (2%) higher.It is roughly $62,500 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $63,900 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Clay the median monthly rent is $848. It is approximately $233 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and is approximately $11 (1%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
