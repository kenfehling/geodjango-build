Similar to other places in this area, it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Clay costs $155,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $3,540 (2%) higher. It is roughly $62,500 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $63,900 (70%) higher.

Rents

In Clay the median monthly rent is $848. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $233 (21%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
