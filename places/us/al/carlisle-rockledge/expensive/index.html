Home prices

The median home price in Carlisle-Rockledge is $127,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $16,820 (15%) higher.It is roughly $89,800 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $36,600 (40%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Carlisle-Rockledge's median rent is $683 a month. This is approximately $15 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $379 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $154 (18%) lower.
