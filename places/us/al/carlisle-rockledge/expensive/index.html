Compared to its surrounding areas, Carlisle-Rockledge is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Carlisle-Rockledge is $127,700. It is about $16,820 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $89,800 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $36,600 (40%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carlisle-Rockledge is $683. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $15 (2%) lower. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $154 (18%) lower.
