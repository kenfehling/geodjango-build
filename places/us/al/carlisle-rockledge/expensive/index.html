Home prices

The median home in Carlisle-Rockledge is valued at $127,700. This is approximately $16,820 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $89,800 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $36,600 (40%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Carlisle-Rockledge's median monthly rent is $683. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $15 (2%) lower.It is roughly $379 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $154 (18%) lower.
