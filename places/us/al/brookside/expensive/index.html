Home prices

Brookside's median home value is $90,000. This is approximately $39,340 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $127,500 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $1,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

Brookside's median monthly rent is $651. This is approximately $205 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $411 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $186 (22%) lower.
