Compared to other places in this area, Brookside is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brookside is $90,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $39,340 (30%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $127,500 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $1,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookside is $651. This is about $205 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $411 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $186 (22%) lower.
