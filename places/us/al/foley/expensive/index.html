Home prices

The median home in Foley is valued at $177,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $25,560 (12%) lower.It is roughly $39,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is about $54,000 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Foley is $855. This is about $18 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $207 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Mobile.
