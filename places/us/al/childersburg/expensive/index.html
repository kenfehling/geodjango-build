Childersburg's median home cost is $107,800. It is approximately $8,760 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $109,700 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $16,700 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Childersburg the median monthly rent is $506. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $288 (36%) lower. It is approximately $556 (52%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $331 (39%) lower.
