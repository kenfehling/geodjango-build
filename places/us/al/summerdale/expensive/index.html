Much like other places in this area, it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Mobile however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Summerdale costs $173,200. It is roughly $2,120 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $44,300 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Mobile it is approximately $49,600 (40%) higher.

Rents

Summerdale's median rent is $821 per month. This is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $241 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $36 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.
