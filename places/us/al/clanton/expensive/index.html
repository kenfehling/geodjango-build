Home prices

The median home in Clanton is valued at $131,700. This is roughly $30,380 (29%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $85,800 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $40,600 (44%) higher.

Rents

Clanton's median rent is $685 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $24 (3%) lower.It is about $377 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $152 (18%) lower.
