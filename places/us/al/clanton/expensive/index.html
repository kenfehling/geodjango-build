Home prices

The median home in Clanton costs $131,700. This is approximately $30,380 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $85,800 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $40,600 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Clanton is $685 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $24 (3%) lower. It is roughly $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $152 (18%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
