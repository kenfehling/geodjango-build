Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clanton is $131,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $30,380 (29%) higher.It is approximately $85,800 (39%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $40,600 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clanton is $685. It is roughly $24 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $377 (35%) lower, and is approximately $152 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
