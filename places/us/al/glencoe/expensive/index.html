Home prices

The median home in Glencoe costs $154,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $34,440 (28%) higher.It is roughly $63,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $63,400 (69%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Glencoe the median rent is $556 a month. This is roughly $232 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $506 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $281 (33%) lower.
