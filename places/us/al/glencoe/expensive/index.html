Compared to the surrounding areas, Glencoe is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Glencoe costs $154,500. It is roughly $34,440 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $63,000 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $63,400 (69%) higher.

Rents

In Glencoe the median monthly rent is $556. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $232 (29%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $506 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $281 (33%) lower.
