Home prices

The median home in Glencoe is valued at $154,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $34,440 (28%) higher.It is about $63,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $63,400 (69%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Glencoe is $556. Compared to other places in this area it is about $232 (29%) lower.It is roughly $506 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $281 (33%) lower.
