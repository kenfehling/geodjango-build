In comparison to the surrounding areas, Glencoe is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Glencoe is valued at $154,500. This is approximately $34,440 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $63,000 (28%) lower, and is roughly $63,400 (69%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Glencoe's median rent is $556 a month. It is roughly $232 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $506 (47%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $281 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
