Home prices

The median home in Lipscomb is valued at $64,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $20,540 (24%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $152,900 (70%) lower, and it is roughly $26,500 (29%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Lipscomb's median rent is $606 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $206 (25%) lower. It is approximately $456 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $231 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
