Compared to the surrounding areas, Alabaster is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Alabaster is valued at $173,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $13,240 (8%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $44,500 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $81,900 (89%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alabaster is $1,088. This is approximately $130 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $26 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $251 (29%) higher.
