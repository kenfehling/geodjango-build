Home prices

The cost of the median home in Alabaster is $173,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $13,240 (8%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $44,500 (20%) lower, and is roughly $81,900 (89%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alabaster is $1,088. It is roughly $130 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $26 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $251 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
