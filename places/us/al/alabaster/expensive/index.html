Compared to the surrounding areas, Alabaster is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Alabaster's median home cost is $173,000. It is roughly $13,240 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $44,500 (20%) lower, and is roughly $81,900 (89%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Alabaster the median rent is $1,088 per month. It is approximately $130 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $26 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $251 (29%) higher.
