Home prices

The median home in Dodge City costs $129,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $27,160 (26%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $88,000 (40%) lower, and it is about $38,400 (42%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Dodge City is $769 per month. It is roughly $53 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $293 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $68 (8%) lower.
