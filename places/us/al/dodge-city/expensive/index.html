Compared to the surrounding areas, Dodge City is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dodge City is $129,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $27,160 (26%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $88,000 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $38,400 (42%) higher.

Rents

In Dodge City the median monthly rent is $769. This is approximately $53 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $293 (27%) lower, and is roughly $68 (8%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
