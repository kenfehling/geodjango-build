Home prices

The median home value in Dodge City is $129,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $27,160 (26%) higher.It is roughly $88,000 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $38,400 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Dodge City the median rent is $769 a month. This is approximately $53 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and it is about $68 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
