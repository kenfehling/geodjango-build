In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dodge City is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Dodge City is valued at $129,500. This is approximately $27,160 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $88,000 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $38,400 (42%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Dodge City's median rent is $769 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $53 (7%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $68 (8%) lower.
