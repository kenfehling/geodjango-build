Rents

Rogersville's median monthly rent is $669. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $128 (23%) higher. It is about $393 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $158 (19%) lower.
