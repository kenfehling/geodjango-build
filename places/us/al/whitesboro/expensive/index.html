Home prices

The median home price in Whitesboro is $83,900. It is roughly $35,740 (29%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $133,600 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $7,200 (7%) lower.

Rents

Whitesboro's median monthly rent is $681. It is roughly $17 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $156 (18%) lower.
