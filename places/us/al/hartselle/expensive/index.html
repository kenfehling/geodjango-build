In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hartselle is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Hartselle is $148,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $27,400 (22%) higher. It is about $68,900 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $57,500 (63%) higher.

Rents

Hartselle's median monthly rent is $681. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $381 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $156 (18%) lower.
