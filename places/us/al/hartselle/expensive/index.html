Home prices

The price of the median home in Hartselle is $148,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $27,400 (22%) higher.It is about $68,900 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $57,500 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Hartselle the median monthly rent is $681. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $156 (18%) lower.
