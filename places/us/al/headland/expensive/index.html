Headland's median home cost is $145,200. This is approximately $71,060 (95%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $72,300 (33%) lower, and is approximately $3,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Headland's median rent is $627 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $107 (14%) lower. It is roughly $435 (40%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $279 (30%) lower.
