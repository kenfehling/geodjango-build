Home prices

Southside's median home price is $153,100. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $32,740 (27%) higher. It is about $64,400 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $62,000 (68%) higher.

Rents

Southside's median monthly rent is $942. It is about $251 (36%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $120 (11%) lower, and it is roughly $105 (12%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
