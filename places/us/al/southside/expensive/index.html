Home prices

Southside's median home cost is $153,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $32,740 (27%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $64,400 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $62,000 (68%) higher.

Rents

Southside's median monthly rent is $942. It is roughly $251 (36%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $120 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $105 (12%) higher.
