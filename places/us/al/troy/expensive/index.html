Home prices

The median home value in Troy is $154,000. This is about $60,620 (64%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $63,500 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $12,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Troy the median monthly rent is $687. This is approximately $93 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $375 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $219 (24%) lower.
