Home prices

The median home in Monroeville costs $126,500. This is about $39,420 (45%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $91,000 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Montgomery it is about $5,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Monroeville the median rent is $523 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $101 (16%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $539 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $358 (40%) lower compared to the city of Montgomery.
