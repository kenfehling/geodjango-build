Compared to the surrounding areas, Millbrook is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Millbrook is $154,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $11,040 (7%) higher.It is roughly $63,200 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $63,200 (69%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Millbrook's median rent is $951 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $15 (1%) lower.It is approximately $111 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $114 (13%) higher.
