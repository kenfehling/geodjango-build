Home prices

The median home price in Hackleburg is $85,300. It is approximately $9,420 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $132,200 (60%) lower, and is about $5,800 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Hackleburg's median rent is $542 a month. This is about $16 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $520 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $295 (35%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
