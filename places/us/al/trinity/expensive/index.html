The median home in Trinity costs $154,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $50,480 (48%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $63,100 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $63,300 (69%) higher.

Rents

In Trinity the median rent is $415 a month. It is roughly $157 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $647 (60%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $422 (50%) lower.
