Home prices

The price of the median home in Pinson is $137,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $23,860 (14%) lower. It is about $80,100 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $46,300 (50%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median rent in Pinson is $884 a month. This is about $92 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $178 (16%) lower, and is about $47 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
