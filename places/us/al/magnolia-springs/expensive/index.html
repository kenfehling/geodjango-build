Home prices

The median home in Magnolia Springs costs $251,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $6,840 (2%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $34,000 (15%) higher, and is about $127,900 (103%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

The median rent in Magnolia Springs is $832 a month. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $230 (21%) lower, and is roughly $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.
