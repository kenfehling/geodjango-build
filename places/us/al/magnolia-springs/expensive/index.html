Rents

Magnolia Springs's median monthly rent is $832. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $230 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is about $25 (2%) lower.
