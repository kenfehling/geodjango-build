Home prices

The median home value in Magnolia Springs is $251,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $6,840 (2%) higher.It is roughly $34,000 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $127,900 (103%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.

Rents

In Magnolia Springs the median monthly rent is $832. It is approximately $65 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $230 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $25 (2%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.
