Compared to nearby areas, Riverside is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside is valued at $175,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $34,280 (24%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $41,900 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $84,500 (92%) higher.

Rents

Riverside's median rent is $916 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $88 (10%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $146 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $79 (9%) higher.
