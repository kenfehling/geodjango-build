In comparison to nearby areas, Riverside is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside is valued at $175,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $34,280 (24%) higher. It is approximately $41,900 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $84,500 (92%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Riverside is $916 a month. This is approximately $88 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $146 (13%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $79 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
