In comparison to its nearby areas, Riverside is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Riverside's median home price is $175,600. It is approximately $34,280 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $41,900 (19%) lower, and is about $84,500 (92%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Riverside the median rent is $916 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $88 (10%) higher. It is roughly $146 (13%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $79 (9%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
