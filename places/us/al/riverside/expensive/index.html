In comparison to the surrounding areas, Riverside is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Birminghamhowever, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside is valued at $175,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $34,280 (24%) higher. It is about $41,900 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $84,500 (92%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Riverside is $916 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $88 (10%) higher. It is about $146 (13%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $79 (9%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
