Home prices

The value of the median home in Riverside is $175,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $34,280 (24%) higher.It is approximately $41,900 (19%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is about $84,500 (92%) higher.

Rents

In Riverside the median monthly rent is $916. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $88 (10%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $146 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $79 (9%) higher.
