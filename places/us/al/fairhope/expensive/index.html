In comparison to other places in this area, Fairhope is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Mobile, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fairhope is $299,200. This is about $57,380 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $81,700 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is approximately $175,600 (142%) higher.

Rents

In Fairhope the median monthly rent is $1,196. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $217 (22%) higher. It is approximately $134 (12%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is approximately $339 (39%) higher.
