In comparison to other places in this area, Mount Olive is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mount Olive is $158,300. It is about $21,120 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $59,200 (27%) lower, and is approximately $67,200 (73%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Olive is $734. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $113 (13%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $328 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $103 (12%) lower.
