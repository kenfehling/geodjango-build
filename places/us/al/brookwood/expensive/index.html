Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookwood is $1,080. It is roughly $279 (34%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $18 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $243 (29%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.
