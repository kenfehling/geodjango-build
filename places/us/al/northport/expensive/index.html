Compared to its surrounding areas, Northport is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home price in Northport is $177,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $42,820 (31%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $40,300 (18%) lower, and is about $86,100 (94%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Northport the median rent is $824 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $238 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $13 (1%) lower.
