In comparison to other places in this area, Northport is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Northport costs $177,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $42,820 (31%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $40,300 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $86,100 (94%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Northport the median monthly rent is $824. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is about $238 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $13 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
