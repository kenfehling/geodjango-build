Home prices

Ohatchee's median home price is $135,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $21,800 (19%) higher. It is approximately $82,100 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $44,300 (48%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ohatchee is $915 a month. This is approximately $113 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $147 (13%) lower, and is about $78 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
