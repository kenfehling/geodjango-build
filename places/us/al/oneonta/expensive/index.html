Compared to other places in this area, Oneonta is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Oneonta costs $156,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24,720 (18%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $61,200 (28%) lower, and is approximately $65,200 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Oneonta the median monthly rent is $666. This is roughly $120 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $396 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $171 (20%) lower.
