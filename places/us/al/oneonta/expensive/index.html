Compared to its surrounding areas, Oneonta is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Oneonta is $156,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $24,720 (18%) higher.It is approximately $61,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $65,200 (71%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oneonta is $666. It is roughly $120 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $396 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $171 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
