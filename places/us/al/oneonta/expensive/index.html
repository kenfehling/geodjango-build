In comparison to other places in this area, Oneonta is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Oneonta is $156,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $24,720 (18%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $61,200 (28%) lower, and is approximately $65,200 (71%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Oneonta the median monthly rent is $666. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $120 (15%) lower. It is about $396 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $171 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
