In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oneonta is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Birminghamhowever, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Oneonta's median home cost is $156,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $24,720 (18%) higher. It is roughly $61,200 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is approximately $65,200 (71%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oneonta is $666. This is roughly $120 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $396 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $171 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
