In comparison to other places in this area, Oneonta is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Oneonta is $156,300. This is about $24,720 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $61,200 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is roughly $65,200 (71%) higher.

Rents

In Oneonta the median monthly rent is $666. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $120 (15%) lower.It is approximately $396 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $171 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
