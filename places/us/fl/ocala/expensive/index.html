In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ocala is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Ocala is $142,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $32,680 (29%) higher.It is roughly $75,300 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $31,000 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Ocala the median rent is $922 a month. It is roughly $98 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $140 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $143 (13%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
