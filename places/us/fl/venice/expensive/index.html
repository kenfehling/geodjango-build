In comparison to its nearby areas, Venice is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Venice costs $252,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $38,360 (17%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $34,700 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $13,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Venice the median rent is $1,355 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $117 (9%) higher. It is roughly $293 (27%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $224 (19%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
