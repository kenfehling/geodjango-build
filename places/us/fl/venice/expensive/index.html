Compared to other places in this area, Venice is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Venice is $252,200. This is approximately $38,360 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $34,700 (15%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $13,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Venice the median monthly rent is $1,355. It is approximately $117 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $293 (27%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $224 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
