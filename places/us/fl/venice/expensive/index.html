Home prices

The median home in Venice costs $252,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $38,360 (17%) higher.It is approximately $34,700 (15%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $13,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Venice is $1,355 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $117 (9%) higher.It is approximately $293 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $224 (19%) higher.
