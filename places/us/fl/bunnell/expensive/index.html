Home prices

The median home price in Bunnell is $128,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $73,620 (36%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $88,700 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $44,400 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bunnell is $820 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $187 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $242 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $245 (23%) lower.
