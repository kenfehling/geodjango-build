Home prices

The cost of the median home in Daytona Beach Shores is $294,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $102,280 (53%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $76,900 (35%) higher, and it is roughly $121,200 (69%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Daytona Beach Shores the median rent is $993 a month. This is approximately $246 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $69 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is about $72 (6%) lower.
