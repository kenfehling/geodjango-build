In comparison to its surrounding areas, Southgate is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Southgate is $256,200. This is approximately $23,060 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $38,700 (17%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $17,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Southgate the median monthly rent is $1,396. This is about $157 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $334 (31%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $265 (23%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
