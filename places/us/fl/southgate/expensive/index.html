In comparison to its surrounding areas, Southgate is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Southgate is $256,200. This is about $23,060 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $38,700 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $17,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Southgate's median monthly rent is $1,396. This is roughly $157 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $334 (31%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $265 (23%) higher.
