In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lockhart is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Lockhart costs $158,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $69,300 (30%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $59,300 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $80,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

Lockhart's median monthly rent is $1,150. This is approximately $24 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $88 (8%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $19 (1%) higher.
