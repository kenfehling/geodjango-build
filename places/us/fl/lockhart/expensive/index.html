Home prices

The median home in Lockhart costs $158,200. It is roughly $69,300 (30%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $59,300 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $80,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Lockhart the median rent is $1,150 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $24 (2%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $88 (8%) higher, and is about $19 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
