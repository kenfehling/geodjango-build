Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lockhart is $158,200. It is approximately $69,300 (30%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $59,300 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $80,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lockhart is $1,150 a month. This is about $24 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $88 (8%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $19 (1%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
