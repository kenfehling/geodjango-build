Compared to other places in this area, Oakland Park is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home value in Oakland Park is $214,600. It is approximately $58,820 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $2,900 (1%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $103,100 (32%) lower.

Rents

Oakland Park's median monthly rent is $1,224. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $45 (3%) lower.It is about $162 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
