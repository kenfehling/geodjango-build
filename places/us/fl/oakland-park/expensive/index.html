In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oakland Park is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland Park costs $214,600. It is roughly $58,820 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $2,900 (1%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $103,100 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Oakland Park the median rent is $1,224 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $45 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $162 (15%) higher, and is about $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
