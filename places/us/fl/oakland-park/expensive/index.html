Compared to its surrounding areas, Oakland Park is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home cost in Oakland Park is $214,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $58,820 (21%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $2,900 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $103,100 (32%) lower.

Rents

Oakland Park's median rent is $1,224 a month. This is about $45 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $162 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $41 (3%) higher.
