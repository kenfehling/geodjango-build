Home prices

The median home price in Boynton Beach is $207,500. This is about $309,280 (59%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $10,000 (4%) lower, and is about $110,200 (34%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Boynton Beach the median monthly rent is $1,501. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $174 (13%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $439 (41%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $318 (26%) higher.
