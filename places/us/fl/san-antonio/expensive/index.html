In comparison to other places in this area, San Antonio is substantially more expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Antonio is valued at $226,600. This is roughly $121,540 (115%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $9,100 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $12,300 (5%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In San Antonio the median monthly rent is $743. It is approximately $156 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $319 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $388 (34%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
