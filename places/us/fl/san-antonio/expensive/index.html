In comparison to nearby areas, San Antonio is a lot more expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in San Antonio is $226,600. It is about $121,540 (115%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $9,100 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $12,300 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in San Antonio is $743. This is about $156 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $319 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $388 (34%) lower.
