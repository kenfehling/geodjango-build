In comparison to its nearby areas, San Antonio is substantially more expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in San Antonio costs $226,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $121,540 (115%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $9,100 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $12,300 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in San Antonio is $743 a month. This is about $156 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $319 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $388 (34%) lower.
