Compared to other places in this area, San Antonio is significantly more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home cost in San Antonio is $226,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $121,540 (115%) higher. It is roughly $9,100 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $12,300 (5%) lower.

Rents

San Antonio's median monthly rent is $743. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $156 (17%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $319 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $388 (34%) lower.
