In comparison to the surrounding areas, Tavares is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home value in Tavares is $144,900. This is about $53,400 (26%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $72,600 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $94,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tavares is $1,018 a month. It is about $141 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $44 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $113 (9%) lower.
