In comparison to nearby areas, Keystone is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Keystone is $379,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $80,640 (27%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $161,500 (74%) higher, and it is about $140,100 (58%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Keystone's median monthly rent is $2,450. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $1,194 (95%) higher. It is approximately $1,388 (130%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $1,319 (116%) higher.
