In comparison to its surrounding areas, Keystone is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Keystone is $379,000. It is about $80,640 (27%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $161,500 (74%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $140,100 (58%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Keystone's median rent is $2,450 per month. This is about $1,194 (95%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $1,388 (130%) higher, and is roughly $1,319 (116%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
