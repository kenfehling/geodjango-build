Compared to other places in this area, South Miami is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in South Miami costs $569,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $108,560 (23%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $351,800 (161%) higher, and is roughly $251,600 (79%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Miami is $1,338. This is approximately $210 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $276 (25%) higher, and it is about $155 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
