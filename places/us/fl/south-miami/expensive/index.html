Compared to its nearby areas, South Miami is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Miami is $569,300. This is about $108,560 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $351,800 (161%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $251,600 (79%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Miami is $1,338. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $210 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $276 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $155 (13%) higher.
