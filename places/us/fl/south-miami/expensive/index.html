Compared to the surrounding areas, South Miami is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Miami is $569,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $108,560 (23%) higher.It is about $351,800 (161%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $251,600 (79%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in South Miami is $1,338 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $210 (13%) lower.It is roughly $276 (25%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $155 (13%) higher.
