Home prices

The median home value in Pompano Beach is $213,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $337,880 (61%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $3,800 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $104,000 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Pompano Beach the median rent is $1,265 a month. It is approximately $666 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $203 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $82 (6%) higher.
