In comparison to its surrounding areas, Miami Shores is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Miami, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Miami Shores is $558,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $244,580 (77%) higher. It is about $341,200 (156%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $241,000 (75%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miami Shores is $1,523. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $220 (16%) higher. It is about $461 (43%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $340 (28%) higher.
