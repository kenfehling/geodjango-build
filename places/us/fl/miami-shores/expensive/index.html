In comparison to its surrounding areas, Miami Shores is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Miami, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Miami Shores is $558,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $244,580 (77%) higher.It is roughly $341,200 (156%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $241,000 (75%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Miami Shores the median rent is $1,523 per month. It is approximately $220 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $461 (43%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $340 (28%) higher.
