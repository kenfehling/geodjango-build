Compared to the surrounding areas, Miami Shores is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Miami Shores is $558,700. This is roughly $244,580 (77%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $341,200 (156%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $241,000 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Miami Shores's median rent is $1,523 a month. It is roughly $220 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $461 (43%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $340 (28%) higher.
