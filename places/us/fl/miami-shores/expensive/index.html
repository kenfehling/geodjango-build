In comparison to its surrounding areas, Miami Shores is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Miami Shores costs $558,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $244,580 (77%) higher. It is approximately $341,200 (156%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $241,000 (75%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Miami Shores the median monthly rent is $1,523. This is about $220 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $461 (43%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $340 (28%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
