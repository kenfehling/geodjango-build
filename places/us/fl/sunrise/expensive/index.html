In comparison to its nearby areas, Sunrise is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sunrise costs $205,100. It is roughly $21,140 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $12,400 (5%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $112,600 (35%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Sunrise the median monthly rent is $1,550. This is about $209 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $488 (45%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $367 (31%) higher.
