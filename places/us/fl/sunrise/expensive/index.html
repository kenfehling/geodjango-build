Home prices

Sunrise's median home price is $205,100. It is about $21,140 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $12,400 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $112,600 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Sunrise the median rent is $1,550 a month. It is about $209 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $488 (45%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $367 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
