Home prices

Sunrise's median home cost is $205,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $21,140 (11%) higher.It is approximately $12,400 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $112,600 (35%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Sunrise is $1,550 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $209 (15%) higher.It is about $488 (45%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $367 (31%) higher.
