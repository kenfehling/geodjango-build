Home prices

The median home in Sunrise is valued at $205,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $21,140 (11%) higher. It is approximately $12,400 (5%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Miami is about $112,600 (35%) lower.

Rents

Sunrise's median monthly rent is $1,550. It is approximately $209 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $488 (45%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $367 (31%) higher.
