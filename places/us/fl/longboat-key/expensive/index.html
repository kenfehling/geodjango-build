Home prices

The median home in Longboat Key is valued at $740,500. This is roughly $540,740 (270%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $523,000 (240%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $501,600 (209%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Longboat Key is $1,625. This is approximately $561 (52%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $563 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $494 (43%) higher.
