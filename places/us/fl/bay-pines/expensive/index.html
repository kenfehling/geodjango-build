The median home in Bay Pines is valued at $201,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $161,500 (44%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $15,700 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $37,100 (15%) lower.

Rents

Bay Pines's median rent is $1,178 per month. This is roughly $139 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $116 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $47 (4%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
