Compared to nearby areas, North Miami Beach is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Miami Beach is valued at $194,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $78,900 (28%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $23,200 (10%) lower, and it is approximately $123,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

North Miami Beach's median monthly rent is $1,195. This is roughly $205 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $133 (12%) higher, and it is roughly $12 (1%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
