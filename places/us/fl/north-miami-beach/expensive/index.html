Compared to the surrounding areas, North Miami Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

North Miami Beach's median home price is $194,300. This is about $78,900 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $23,200 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $123,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in North Miami Beach is $1,195 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $205 (14%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $133 (12%) higher, and it is about $12 (1%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
