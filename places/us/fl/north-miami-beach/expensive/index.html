In comparison to other places in this area, North Miami Beach is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in North Miami Beach costs $194,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $78,900 (28%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $23,200 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $123,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Miami Beach is $1,195. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $205 (14%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $133 (12%) higher, and is roughly $12 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
