In comparison to nearby areas, North Miami Beach is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

North Miami Beach's median home price is $194,300. This is approximately $78,900 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $23,200 (10%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $123,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

North Miami Beach's median rent is $1,195 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $205 (14%) lower. It is about $133 (12%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
