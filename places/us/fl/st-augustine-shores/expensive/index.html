Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Augustine Shores is substantially less expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Augustine Shores costs $211,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $125,260 (37%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $6,300 (2%) lower, and it is approximately $38,000 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

St. Augustine Shores's median monthly rent is $1,179. It is about $87 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $117 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is about $114 (10%) higher.
