Compared to the surrounding areas, Palm Coast is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Palm Coast is $209,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $14,900 (6%) lower. It is approximately $8,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $36,100 (20%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palm Coast is $1,298. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $303 (30%) higher. It is roughly $236 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $233 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
