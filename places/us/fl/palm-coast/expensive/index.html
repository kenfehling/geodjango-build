Compared to the surrounding areas, Palm Coast is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Coast is valued at $209,300. It is approximately $14,900 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $8,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $36,100 (20%) higher.

Rents

In Palm Coast the median monthly rent is $1,298. It is about $303 (30%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $236 (22%) higher, and it is approximately $233 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
