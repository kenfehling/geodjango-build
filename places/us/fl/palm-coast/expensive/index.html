Compared to other places in this area, Palm Coast is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Coast is valued at $209,300. It is roughly $14,900 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $8,200 (3%) lower, and is approximately $36,100 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Palm Coast the median rent is $1,298 per month. This is about $303 (30%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $236 (22%) higher, and is roughly $233 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
