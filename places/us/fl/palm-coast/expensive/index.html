In comparison to nearby areas, Palm Coast is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Palm Coast's median home value is $209,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $14,900 (6%) lower.It is roughly $8,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $36,100 (20%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Palm Coast the median rent is $1,298 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $303 (30%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $236 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $233 (21%) higher.
