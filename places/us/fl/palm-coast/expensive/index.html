Compared to the surrounding areas, Palm Coast is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Palm Coast is $209,300. This is about $14,900 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $8,200 (3%) lower, and it is approximately $36,100 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median rent in Palm Coast is $1,298 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $303 (30%) higher.It is approximately $236 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $233 (21%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.
