Home prices

Miami Beach's median home cost is $459,000. Compared to nearby areas it is is very similar.It is roughly $241,500 (111%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $141,300 (44%) higher.

Rents

In Miami Beach the median monthly rent is $1,396. This is approximately $232 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $334 (31%) higher, and it is about $213 (18%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
