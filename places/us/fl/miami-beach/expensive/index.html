Home prices

Miami Beach's median home price is $459,000. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $241,500 (111%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $141,300 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Miami Beach is $1,396 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $232 (14%) lower.It is about $334 (31%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $213 (18%) higher.
