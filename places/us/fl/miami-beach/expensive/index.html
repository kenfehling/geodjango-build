Home prices

Miami Beach's median home price is $459,000. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $241,500 (111%) higher, and is approximately $141,300 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Miami Beach's median rent is $1,396 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $232 (14%) lower.It is roughly $334 (31%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $213 (18%) higher.
