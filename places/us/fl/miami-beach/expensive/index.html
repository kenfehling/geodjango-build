Home prices

The price of the median home in Miami Beach is $459,000. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $241,500 (111%) higher, and is about $141,300 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Miami Beach the median rent is $1,396 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $232 (14%) lower. It is about $334 (31%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $213 (18%) higher.
