Similar to nearby areas, it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Miami, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Miami Beach is valued at $459,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is approximately $241,500 (111%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $141,300 (44%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Miami Beach the median monthly rent is $1,396. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $232 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $334 (31%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $213 (18%) higher.
