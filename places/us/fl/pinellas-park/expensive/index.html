Home prices

The median home in Pinellas Park costs $154,700. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $27,920 (22%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $62,800 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $84,200 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Pinellas Park the median rent is $1,085 per month. This is about $54 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $23 (2%) higher, and is approximately $46 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
