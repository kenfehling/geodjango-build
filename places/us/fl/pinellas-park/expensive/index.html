Compared to its surrounding areas, Pinellas Park is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pinellas Park is $154,700. It is about $27,920 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $62,800 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $84,200 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pinellas Park is $1,085 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $54 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $23 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $46 (4%) lower.
