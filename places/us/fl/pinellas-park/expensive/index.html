Home prices

The median home value in Pinellas Park is $154,700. This is about $27,920 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $62,800 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $84,200 (35%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Pinellas Park's median monthly rent is $1,085. This is about $54 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $23 (2%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $46 (4%) lower.
