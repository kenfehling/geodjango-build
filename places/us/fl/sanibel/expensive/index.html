Home prices

The median home in Sanibel costs $721,200. This is approximately $427,000 (145%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $503,700 (231%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cape Coral is roughly $491,800 (214%) higher.

Rents

In Sanibel the median rent is $1,545 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is about $483 (45%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cape Coral is roughly $301 (24%) higher.
