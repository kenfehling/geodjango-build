In comparison to its nearby areas, Marco Island is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Pembroke Pines.

Home prices

The median home in Marco Island is valued at $678,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $211,300 (45%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $461,400 (212%) higher, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines it is approximately $390,200 (135%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Marco Island is $1,638 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $178 (12%) higher. It is approximately $576 (54%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines it is roughly $123 (8%) higher.
