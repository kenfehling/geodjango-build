Compared to its surrounding areas, Marco Island is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Pembroke Pines.

Home prices

The median home cost in Marco Island is $678,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $211,300 (45%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $461,400 (212%) higher, and is roughly $390,200 (135%) higher in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines.

Rents

In Marco Island the median rent is $1,638 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $178 (12%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $576 (54%) higher, and is about $123 (8%) higher compared to the city of Pembroke Pines.
