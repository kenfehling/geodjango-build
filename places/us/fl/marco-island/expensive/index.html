Home prices

The median home in Marco Island costs $678,900. It is roughly $211,300 (45%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $461,400 (212%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines it is approximately $390,200 (135%) higher.

Rents

Marco Island's median monthly rent is $1,638. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $178 (12%) higher.It is roughly $576 (54%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $123 (8%) higher compared to the city of Pembroke Pines.
