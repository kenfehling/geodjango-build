Compared to other places in this area, Eustis is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Eustis is $162,000. It is about $35,280 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $55,500 (25%) lower, and it is about $76,900 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Eustis the median rent is $845 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $82 (8%) lower. It is about $217 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $286 (25%) lower.
