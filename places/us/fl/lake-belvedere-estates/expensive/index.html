Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake Belvedere Estates is $211,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $32,460 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $5,600 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $105,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

Lake Belvedere Estates's median rent is $1,545 a month. This is roughly $236 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $483 (45%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $362 (30%) higher.
