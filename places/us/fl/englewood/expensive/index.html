Home prices

The median home price in Englewood is $169,300. This is about $57,240 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $48,200 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $69,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Englewood is $932. It is about $382 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $130 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $199 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
