Home prices

The median home cost in Key Biscayne is $1,211,000. It is roughly $593,980 (96%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $993,500 (456%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $893,300 (281%) higher.

Rents

Key Biscayne's median rent is $2,548 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $1,052 (70%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $1,486 (139%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $1,365 (115%) higher.
