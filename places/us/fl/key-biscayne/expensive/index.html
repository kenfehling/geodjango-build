In comparison to its surrounding areas, Key Biscayne is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Key Biscayne's median home value is $1,211,000. This is approximately $593,980 (96%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $993,500 (456%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $893,300 (281%) higher.

Rents

In Key Biscayne the median rent is $2,548 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $1,052 (70%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,486 (139%) higher, and is about $1,365 (115%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
