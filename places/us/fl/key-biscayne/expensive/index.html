Home prices

Key Biscayne's median home cost is $1,211,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $593,980 (96%) higher. It is approximately $993,500 (456%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $893,300 (281%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Key Biscayne is $2,548. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $1,052 (70%) higher. It is about $1,486 (139%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $1,365 (115%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
