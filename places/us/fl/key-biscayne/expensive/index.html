Home prices

The median home value in Key Biscayne is $1,211,000. It is about $593,980 (96%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $993,500 (456%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $893,300 (281%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Key Biscayne is $2,548. It is roughly $1,052 (70%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $1,486 (139%) higher, and it is roughly $1,365 (115%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
