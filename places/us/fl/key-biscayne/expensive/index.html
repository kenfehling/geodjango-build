Home prices

The median home in Key Biscayne is valued at $1,211,000. It is roughly $593,980 (96%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $993,500 (456%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $893,300 (281%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Key Biscayne is $2,548. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $1,052 (70%) higher. It is roughly $1,486 (139%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $1,365 (115%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
