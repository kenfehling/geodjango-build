Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lauderdale Lakes is $119,800. It is approximately $52,200 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $97,700 (44%) lower, and it is roughly $197,900 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Lauderdale Lakes the median monthly rent is $1,120. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $107 (8%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $58 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $63 (5%) lower.
