Compared to the surrounding areas, Lauderdale Lakes is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home price in Lauderdale Lakes is $119,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $52,200 (30%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $97,700 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $197,900 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Lauderdale Lakes the median monthly rent is $1,120. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $107 (8%) lower.It is approximately $58 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $63 (5%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
