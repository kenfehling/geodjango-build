Home prices

The median home value in Lauderdale Lakes is $119,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $52,200 (30%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $97,700 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $197,900 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Lauderdale Lakes the median monthly rent is $1,120. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $107 (8%) lower. It is roughly $58 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $63 (5%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
