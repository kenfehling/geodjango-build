Home prices

The median home in Palatka is valued at $75,200. It is about $18,580 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $142,300 (65%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $98,000 (56%) lower.

Rents

Palatka's median monthly rent is $714. It is about $70 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $348 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $351 (32%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
