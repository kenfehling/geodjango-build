Home prices

Palatka's median home cost is $75,200. It is approximately $18,580 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $142,300 (65%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $98,000 (56%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Palatka's median monthly rent is $714. It is roughly $70 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $348 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is about $351 (32%) lower.
