Compared to its surrounding areas, Palatka is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palatka is valued at $75,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $18,580 (19%) lower. It is roughly $142,300 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is about $98,000 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Palatka the median monthly rent is $714. This is approximately $70 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $348 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $351 (32%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
