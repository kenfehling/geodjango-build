In comparison to other places in this area, St. Pete Beach is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Pete Beach is $462,500. This is about $151,280 (48%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $245,000 (112%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $223,600 (93%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in St. Pete Beach is $1,103 per month. It is approximately $253 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $41 (3%) higher, and is approximately $28 (2%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
