Compared to its nearby areas, St. Pete Beach is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Tampa, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in St. Pete Beach is $462,500. It is about $151,280 (48%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $245,000 (112%) higher, and is roughly $223,600 (93%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Pete Beach is $1,103. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $253 (18%) lower.It is roughly $41 (3%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $28 (2%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
