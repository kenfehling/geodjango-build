In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Pete Beach is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Pete Beach is $462,500. It is approximately $151,280 (48%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $245,000 (112%) higher, and is roughly $223,600 (93%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

St. Pete Beach's median rent is $1,103 per month. This is roughly $253 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $28 (2%) lower.
