In comparison to nearby areas, St. Pete Beach is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in St. Pete Beach is $462,500. This is about $151,280 (48%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $245,000 (112%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $223,600 (93%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in St. Pete Beach is $1,103 a month. It is about $253 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $41 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $28 (2%) lower.
