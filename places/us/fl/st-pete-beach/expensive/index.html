In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Pete Beach is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in St. Pete Beach costs $462,500. It is approximately $151,280 (48%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $245,000 (112%) higher, and it is about $223,600 (93%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In St. Pete Beach the median monthly rent is $1,103. It is approximately $253 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $41 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $28 (2%) lower.
