Home prices

The median home price in Lake Mary is $320,100. It is about $48,460 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $102,600 (47%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $81,200 (33%) higher.

Rents

Lake Mary's median monthly rent is $1,633. This is about $328 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $571 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $502 (44%) higher.
