Compared to its surrounding areas, Lake Mary is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Tampa, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Mary is $320,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $48,460 (17%) higher.It is roughly $102,600 (47%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $81,200 (33%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Lake Mary's median monthly rent is $1,633. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $328 (25%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $571 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $502 (44%) higher.
