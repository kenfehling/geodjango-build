In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lake Mary is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Tampa, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Mary is $320,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $48,460 (17%) higher.It is about $102,600 (47%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $81,200 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Mary is $1,633 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $328 (25%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $571 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $502 (44%) higher.
