Home prices

The median home price in Lake Mary is $320,100. It is about $48,460 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $102,600 (47%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $81,200 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Lake Mary the median rent is $1,633 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $328 (25%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $571 (53%) higher, and it is roughly $502 (44%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
