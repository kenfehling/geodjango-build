Home prices

The price of the median home in Manasota Key is $394,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $212,400 (116%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $176,500 (81%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $155,100 (64%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Manasota Key is $1,824 per month. It is approximately $689 (60%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $762 (71%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $693 (61%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
