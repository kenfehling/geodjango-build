In comparison to its surrounding areas, Davenport is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Davenport costs $175,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $13,620 (8%) higher. It is roughly $42,500 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $63,900 (26%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Davenport the median monthly rent is $1,451. This is approximately $324 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $389 (36%) higher, and is about $320 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
