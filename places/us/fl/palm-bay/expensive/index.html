In comparison to the surrounding areas, Palm Bay is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Orlando.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Palm Bay is $155,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $97,780 (38%) lower. It is about $62,300 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Orlando it is approximately $84,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Palm Bay is $1,059 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $128 (10%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is is very similar, and is about $137 (11%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
