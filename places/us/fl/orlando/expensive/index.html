Home prices

The value of the median home in Orlando is $240,000. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $22,500 (10%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Orlando the median monthly rent is $1,196. This is about $158 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $134 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $65 (5%) higher.
