Much like its surrounding areas, it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Orlando is valued at $240,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $22,500 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Orlando the median monthly rent is $1,196. It is approximately $158 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $134 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $65 (5%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
