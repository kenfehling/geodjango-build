Home prices

Rents

In Orlando the median rent is $1,196 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $158 (15%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $134 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $65 (5%) higher.
