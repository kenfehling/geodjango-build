Home prices

The median home in Holmes Beach is valued at $618,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $371,360 (150%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $400,900 (184%) higher, and is roughly $379,500 (158%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Holmes Beach's median monthly rent is $1,325. This is about $145 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $263 (24%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $194 (17%) higher.
