Home prices

The price of the median home in Holmes Beach is $618,400. It is roughly $371,360 (150%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $400,900 (184%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $379,500 (158%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Holmes Beach the median rent is $1,325 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $145 (12%) higher. It is roughly $263 (24%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $194 (17%) higher.
