Home prices

The median home in Winter Springs is valued at $246,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $26,560 (12%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $29,000 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $7,600 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Winter Springs the median monthly rent is $1,260. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $198 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $129 (11%) higher.
