Home prices

The median home in Connerton costs $320,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $170,240 (113%) higher. It is about $102,700 (47%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $81,300 (34%) higher.

Rents

Connerton's median rent is $834 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $233 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $228 (21%) lower, and is roughly $297 (26%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
