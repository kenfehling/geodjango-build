In comparison to the surrounding areas, Floral City is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Floral City is $114,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $9,440 (8%) higher. It is roughly $102,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $124,000 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Floral City the median monthly rent is $713. This is about $130 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $349 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $418 (36%) lower.
