Compared to other places in this area, Floral City is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Floral City costs $114,900. This is roughly $9,440 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $102,600 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $124,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Floral City the median monthly rent is $713. It is roughly $130 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and is roughly $418 (36%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
