In comparison to other places in this area, Hunters Creek is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hunters Creek is $289,900. It is approximately $83,580 (40%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $72,400 (33%) higher, and it is about $51,000 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Hunters Creek's median monthly rent is $1,460. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $73 (5%) higher. It is about $398 (37%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $329 (29%) higher.
