Home prices

The median home in Hunters Creek is valued at $289,900. It is roughly $83,580 (40%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $72,400 (33%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $51,000 (21%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Hunters Creek's median rent is $1,460 per month. It is approximately $73 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $398 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $329 (29%) higher.
