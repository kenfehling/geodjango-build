In comparison to the surrounding areas, Punta Gorda is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

Punta Gorda's median home value is $325,800. This is roughly $182,180 (126%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $108,300 (49%) higher, and it is roughly $86,900 (36%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Punta Gorda the median monthly rent is $1,029. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $98 (10%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $33 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $102 (9%) lower.
