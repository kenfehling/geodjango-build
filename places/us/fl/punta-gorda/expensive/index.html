Home prices

The median home value in Punta Gorda is $325,800. It is roughly $182,180 (126%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $108,300 (49%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $86,900 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Punta Gorda is $1,029 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $98 (10%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $33 (3%) lower, and it is roughly $102 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
