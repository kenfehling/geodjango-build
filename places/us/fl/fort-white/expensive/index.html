Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fort White is $167,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $36,520 (27%) higher.It is approximately $49,600 (22%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $5,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort White is $758 per month. This is about $88 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $304 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $307 (28%) lower.
