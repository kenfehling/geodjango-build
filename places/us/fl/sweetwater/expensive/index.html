Compared to its surrounding areas, Sweetwater is substantially less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Miami, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Sweetwater is $178,700. This is about $126,840 (41%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $38,800 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $139,000 (43%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sweetwater is $1,330. This is about $255 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $268 (25%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $147 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
