Compared to the surrounding areas, Sweetwater is significantly less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Sweetwater costs $178,700. It is roughly $126,840 (41%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $38,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $139,000 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Sweetwater's median rent is $1,330 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $255 (16%) lower. It is approximately $268 (25%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $147 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
