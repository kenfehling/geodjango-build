Home prices

The value of the median home in Miramar is $299,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $12,960 (4%) higher. It is roughly $82,400 (37%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $17,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miramar is $1,458. It is about $167 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $396 (37%) higher, and it is about $275 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
