In comparison to other places in this area, Miami Springs is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Miami Springs is $410,400. This is about $167,480 (68%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $192,900 (88%) higher, and it is about $92,700 (29%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Miami Springs the median rent is $1,134 a month. This is approximately $104 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $72 (6%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
