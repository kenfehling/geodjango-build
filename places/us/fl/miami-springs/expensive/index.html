Compared to its nearby areas, Miami Springs is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Miami Springs is valued at $410,400. This is roughly $167,480 (68%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $192,900 (88%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $92,700 (29%) higher.

Rents

Miami Springs's median rent is $1,134 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $104 (8%) lower. It is about $72 (6%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
