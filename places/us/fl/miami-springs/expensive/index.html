In comparison to other places in this area, Miami Springs is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Miami Springs's median home value is $410,400. It is approximately $167,480 (68%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $192,900 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $92,700 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Miami Springs the median rent is $1,134 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $104 (8%) lower. It is about $72 (6%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
