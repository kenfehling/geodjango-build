In comparison to the surrounding areas, Miami Springs is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Miami Springs is $410,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $167,480 (68%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $192,900 (88%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $92,700 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Miami Springs the median monthly rent is $1,134. This is about $104 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $72 (6%) higher, and it is roughly $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
