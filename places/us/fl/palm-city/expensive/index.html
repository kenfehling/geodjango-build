Compared to its nearby areas, Palm City is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Palm City is $350,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $130,160 (58%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $133,400 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale is approximately $16,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

Palm City's median rent is $1,614 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $513 (46%) higher.It is roughly $552 (51%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale is approximately $321 (24%) higher.
