Home prices

The median home in Palm City is valued at $350,900. It is roughly $130,160 (58%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $133,400 (61%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $16,900 (5%) higher compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.

Rents

The median rent in Palm City is $1,614 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $513 (46%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $552 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale it is approximately $321 (24%) higher.
