In comparison to its surrounding areas, Palm City is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palm City costs $350,900. This is approximately $130,160 (58%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $133,400 (61%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale is about $16,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Palm City the median rent is $1,614 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $513 (46%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $552 (51%) higher, and is approximately $321 (24%) higher compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.
