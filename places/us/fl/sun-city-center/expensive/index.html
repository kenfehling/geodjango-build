Home prices

The median home in Sun City Center costs $182,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $22,460 (10%) lower. It is roughly $34,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $56,000 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Sun City Center the median monthly rent is $1,768. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $594 (50%) higher. It is approximately $706 (66%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $637 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
