Compared to its nearby areas, Sun City Center is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Sun City Center is valued at $182,900. This is roughly $22,460 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $34,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $56,000 (23%) lower.

Rents

Sun City Center's median rent is $1,768 per month. It is about $594 (50%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $706 (66%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $637 (56%) higher.
