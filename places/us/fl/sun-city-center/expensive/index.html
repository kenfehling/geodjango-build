Home prices

The median home price in Sun City Center is $182,900. This is approximately $22,460 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $34,600 (15%) lower, and is approximately $56,000 (23%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Sun City Center's median monthly rent is $1,768. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $594 (50%) higher.It is roughly $706 (66%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $637 (56%) higher.
