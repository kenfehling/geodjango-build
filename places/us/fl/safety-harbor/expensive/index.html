Home prices

The value of the median home in Safety Harbor is $268,800. This is about $27,460 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $51,300 (23%) higher, and is about $29,900 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Safety Harbor is $1,319 per month. This is about $54 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $257 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $188 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
