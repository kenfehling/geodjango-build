Home prices

The median home cost in Groveland is $199,100. This is approximately $22,000 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $18,400 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $39,800 (16%) lower.

Rents

Groveland's median rent is $1,387 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $124 (9%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $325 (30%) higher, and it is approximately $256 (22%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
