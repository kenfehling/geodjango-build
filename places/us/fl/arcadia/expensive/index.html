Home prices

The value of the median home in Arcadia is $105,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $16,880 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $111,600 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $133,000 (55%) lower.

Rents

Arcadia's median monthly rent is $745. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $159 (17%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $317 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $386 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
