Home prices

The median home in Melbourne Beach costs $368,000. This is approximately $111,120 (43%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $150,500 (69%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $128,000 (53%) higher compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

The median rent in Melbourne Beach is $1,968 a month. It is about $786 (66%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $906 (85%) higher, and it is approximately $772 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Orlando.
