Rents

Melbourne Beach's median rent is $1,968 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $786 (66%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $906 (85%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Orlando it is approximately $772 (64%) higher.
