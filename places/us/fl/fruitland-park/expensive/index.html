Home prices

The median home in Fruitland Park costs $228,200. It is roughly $37,260 (19%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $10,700 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $10,700 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fruitland Park is $978 per month. This is roughly $93 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $84 (7%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $153 (13%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
