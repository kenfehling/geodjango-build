Home prices

Fruitland Park's median home value is $228,200. It is roughly $37,260 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $10,700 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $10,700 (4%) lower.

Rents

Fruitland Park's median monthly rent is $978. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $93 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $84 (7%) lower, and is approximately $153 (13%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
