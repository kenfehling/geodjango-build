Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake Worth is $194,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $48,220 (19%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $23,200 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $123,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Worth the median rent is $1,126 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $230 (16%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $64 (6%) higher, and it is about $57 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
