Compared to nearby areas, Lake Worth is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Lake Worth's median home cost is $194,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $48,220 (19%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $23,200 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $123,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Worth is $1,126 per month. This is roughly $230 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $64 (6%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $57 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
