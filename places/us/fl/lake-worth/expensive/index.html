In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lake Worth is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Worth is valued at $194,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $48,220 (19%) lower.It is about $23,200 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $123,400 (38%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Worth is $1,126. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $230 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $64 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $57 (4%) lower.
