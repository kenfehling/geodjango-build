In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lake Worth is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Miami, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Worth is $194,300. This is roughly $48,220 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $23,200 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $123,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Worth the median rent is $1,126 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $230 (16%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $64 (6%) higher, and is approximately $57 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
