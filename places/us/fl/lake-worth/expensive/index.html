Compared to its surrounding areas, Lake Worth is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lake Worth is $194,300. This is roughly $48,220 (19%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $23,200 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $123,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

Lake Worth's median monthly rent is $1,126. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $230 (16%) lower. It is roughly $64 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $57 (4%) lower.
