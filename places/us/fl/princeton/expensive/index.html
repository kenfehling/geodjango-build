Home prices

Rents

In Princeton the median monthly rent is $1,468. It is about $281 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $406 (38%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $285 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
