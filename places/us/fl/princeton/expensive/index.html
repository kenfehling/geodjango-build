Home prices

The value of the median home in Princeton is $260,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $32,200 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $42,900 (19%) higher, and it is roughly $57,300 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Princeton the median monthly rent is $1,468. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $281 (23%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $406 (38%) higher, and is roughly $285 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
