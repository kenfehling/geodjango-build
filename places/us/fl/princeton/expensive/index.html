Compared to its surrounding areas, Princeton is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Miami, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Princeton is valued at $260,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $32,200 (14%) higher. It is about $42,900 (19%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $57,300 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Princeton is $1,468 per month. This is roughly $281 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $406 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $285 (24%) higher.
