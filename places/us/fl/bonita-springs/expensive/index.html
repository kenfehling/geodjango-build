Compared to its nearby areas, Bonita Springs is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Cape Coral, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Bonita Springs is $311,900. This is about $56,800 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $94,400 (43%) higher, and is roughly $82,500 (35%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

The median rent in Bonita Springs is $1,295 a month. This is approximately $298 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $233 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $51 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
