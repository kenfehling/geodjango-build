Home prices

The median home in Bonita Springs costs $311,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $56,800 (15%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $94,400 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is roughly $82,500 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bonita Springs is $1,295 per month. It is about $298 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $233 (21%) higher, and it is roughly $51 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
