Home prices

Bonita Springs's median home value is $311,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $56,800 (15%) lower. It is approximately $94,400 (43%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cape Coral it is about $82,500 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bonita Springs is $1,295. This is approximately $298 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $233 (21%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $51 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
