In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tiger Point is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Mobile, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Tiger Point is $243,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $21,600 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $25,600 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Mobile is approximately $119,500 (96%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Tiger Point is $1,650 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $281 (20%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $588 (55%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is about $793 (92%) higher.
