In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tiger Point is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Mobile.

Home prices

The median home value in Tiger Point is $243,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $21,600 (8%) lower.It is about $25,600 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is approximately $119,500 (96%) higher.

Rents

Tiger Point's median monthly rent is $1,650. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $281 (20%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $588 (55%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is approximately $793 (92%) higher.
