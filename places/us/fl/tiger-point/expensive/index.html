Home prices

Rents

Tiger Point's median monthly rent is $1,650. It is about $281 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $588 (55%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $793 (92%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
