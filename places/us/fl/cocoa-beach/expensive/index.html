In comparison to other places in this area, Cocoa Beach is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Orlando, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cocoa Beach is $318,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $101,780 (47%) higher. It is about $100,500 (46%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Orlando it is roughly $78,000 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Cocoa Beach the median monthly rent is $1,196. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $128 (11%) higher. It is about $134 (12%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is is very similar.
