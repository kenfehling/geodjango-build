Home prices

Royal Palm Beach's median home value is $273,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $10,300 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $56,400 (25%) higher, and is roughly $43,800 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Royal Palm Beach is $1,743. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $244 (16%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $681 (64%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $560 (47%) higher.
