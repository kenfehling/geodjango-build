Much like its surrounding areas, it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Royal Palm Beach is valued at $273,900. This is approximately $10,300 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $56,400 (25%) higher, and is approximately $43,800 (13%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Royal Palm Beach's median monthly rent is $1,743. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $244 (16%) higher. It is approximately $681 (64%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $560 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
