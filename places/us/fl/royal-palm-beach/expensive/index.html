Home prices

Royal Palm Beach's median home price is $273,900. It is about $10,300 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $56,400 (25%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $43,800 (13%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Royal Palm Beach's median rent is $1,743 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $244 (16%) higher.It is approximately $681 (64%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $560 (47%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
