Home prices

Royal Palm Beach's median home price is $273,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $10,300 (3%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $56,400 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $43,800 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Royal Palm Beach is $1,743 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $244 (16%) higher. It is roughly $681 (64%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $560 (47%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
