Home prices

The median home in Ocean Ridge costs $993,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $633,560 (176%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $775,700 (356%) higher, and it is about $675,500 (212%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ocean Ridge is $1,467. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $133 (9%) higher. It is about $405 (38%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $284 (24%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
