In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fuller Heights is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fuller Heights is valued at $170,100. This is roughly $13,300 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $47,400 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $68,800 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Fuller Heights the median monthly rent is $1,296. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $186 (16%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $234 (22%) higher, and is about $165 (14%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
