Compared to the surrounding areas, Fort Myers Beach is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Cape Coral, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fort Myers Beach is $448,100. It is roughly $182,980 (69%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $230,600 (106%) higher, and is approximately $218,700 (95%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

Fort Myers Beach's median monthly rent is $1,155. This is about $491 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $93 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral it is approximately $89 (7%) lower.
