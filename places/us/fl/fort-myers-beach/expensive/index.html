Compared to other places in this area, Fort Myers Beach is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Myers Beach costs $448,100. It is about $182,980 (69%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $230,600 (106%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral it is roughly $218,700 (95%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Myers Beach is $1,155. Compared to other places in this area this is about $491 (29%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $93 (8%) higher, and it is roughly $89 (7%) lower compared to the city of Cape Coral.
