Compared to the surrounding areas, Clermont is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Tampa, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Clermont is $233,000. This is about $39,200 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $15,500 (7%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $5,900 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Clermont is $1,415 a month. This is approximately $18 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $353 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $284 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
