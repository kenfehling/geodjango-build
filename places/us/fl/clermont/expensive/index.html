Compared to nearby areas, Clermont is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Clermont is $233,000. It is roughly $39,200 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $15,500 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $5,900 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clermont is $1,415 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $18 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $353 (33%) higher, and is approximately $284 (25%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
