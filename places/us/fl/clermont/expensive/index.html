Home prices

The median home in Clermont costs $233,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $39,200 (14%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $15,500 (7%) higher, and is roughly $5,900 (2%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Clermont is $1,415 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $18 (1%) lower. It is roughly $353 (33%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $284 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
