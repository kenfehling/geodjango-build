Compared to the surrounding areas, Belle Isle is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Tampa, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Belle Isle's median home price is $321,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $100,380 (45%) higher. It is approximately $104,200 (47%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $82,800 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Belle Isle the median rent is $884 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $216 (19%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $178 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $247 (21%) lower.
