Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Medulla is $1,045 a month. It is about $115 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $17 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $86 (7%) lower.
