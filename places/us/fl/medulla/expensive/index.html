In comparison to its surrounding areas, Medulla is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Medulla is $180,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $26,260 (16%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $36,600 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $58,000 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Medulla is $1,045 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $115 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $17 (1%) lower, and is approximately $86 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
