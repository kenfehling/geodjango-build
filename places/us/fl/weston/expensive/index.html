Compared to its nearby areas, Weston is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Miami, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Weston is valued at $481,800. It is approximately $185,520 (62%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $264,300 (121%) higher, and it is about $164,100 (51%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Weston the median monthly rent is $2,188. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $614 (38%) higher. It is roughly $1,126 (106%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $1,005 (84%) higher.
