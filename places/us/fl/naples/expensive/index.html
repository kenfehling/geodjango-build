Compared to other places in this area, Naples is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Naples is $988,500. This is roughly $555,720 (128%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $771,000 (354%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is approximately $759,100 (330%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Naples is $1,357. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $26 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $295 (27%) higher, and is roughly $113 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
