In comparison to the surrounding areas, Naples is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Cape Coral, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Naples costs $988,500. This is approximately $555,720 (128%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $771,000 (354%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $759,100 (330%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

In Naples the median monthly rent is $1,357. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $26 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $295 (27%) higher, and it is roughly $113 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
