Compared to nearby areas, Naples is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Cape Coral, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Naples's median home value is $988,500. It is approximately $555,720 (128%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $771,000 (354%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $759,100 (330%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

Naples's median monthly rent is $1,357. It is about $26 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $295 (27%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $113 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
