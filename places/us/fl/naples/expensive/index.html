In comparison to its surrounding areas, Naples is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Naples is $988,500. This is approximately $555,720 (128%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $771,000 (354%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral it is about $759,100 (330%) higher.

Rents

Naples's median rent is $1,357 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $26 (1%) lower. It is about $295 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is roughly $113 (9%) higher.
