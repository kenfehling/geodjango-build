Home prices

The median home in Naples is valued at $988,500. It is roughly $555,720 (128%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $771,000 (354%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $759,100 (330%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

In Naples the median rent is $1,357 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $26 (1%) lower.It is roughly $295 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cape Coral is about $113 (9%) higher.
