Home prices

The cost of the median home in Winter Haven is $154,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $35,280 (29%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $63,400 (29%) lower, and is approximately $84,800 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Winter Haven is $961 per month. This is about $33 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $101 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $170 (15%) lower.
