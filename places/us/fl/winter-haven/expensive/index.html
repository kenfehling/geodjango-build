Home prices

The median home in Winter Haven is valued at $154,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $35,280 (29%) higher. It is roughly $63,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $84,800 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Winter Haven's median monthly rent is $961. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $33 (3%) higher. It is approximately $101 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $170 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
