Home prices

The price of the median home in Haines City is $141,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $16,400 (10%) lower. It is approximately $76,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $97,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Haines City the median monthly rent is $1,024. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $118 (10%) lower. It is approximately $38 (3%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $107 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
