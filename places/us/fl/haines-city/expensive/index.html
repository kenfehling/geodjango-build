In comparison to the surrounding areas, Haines City is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Haines City is $141,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,400 (10%) lower.It is approximately $76,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $97,500 (40%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Haines City the median monthly rent is $1,024. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $118 (10%) lower.It is approximately $38 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $107 (9%) lower.
