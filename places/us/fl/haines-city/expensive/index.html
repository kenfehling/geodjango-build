Compared to other places in this area, Haines City is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

Haines City's median home value is $141,400. This is approximately $16,400 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $76,100 (34%) lower, and is approximately $97,500 (40%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Haines City the median rent is $1,024 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $118 (10%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $38 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $107 (9%) lower.
