Home prices

Doral's median home value is $394,900. It is approximately $168,320 (74%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $177,400 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $77,200 (24%) higher.

Rents

Doral's median rent is $1,991 a month. This is roughly $781 (64%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $929 (87%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $808 (68%) higher.
