Home prices

The median home in Doral costs $394,900. This is about $168,320 (74%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $177,400 (81%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $77,200 (24%) higher.

Rents

In Doral the median monthly rent is $1,991. This is approximately $781 (64%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $929 (87%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $808 (68%) higher.
