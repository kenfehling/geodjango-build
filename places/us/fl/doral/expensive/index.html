Home prices

The value of the median home in Doral is $394,900. This is roughly $168,320 (74%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $177,400 (81%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $77,200 (24%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Doral is $1,991 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $781 (64%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $929 (87%) higher, and it is approximately $808 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
