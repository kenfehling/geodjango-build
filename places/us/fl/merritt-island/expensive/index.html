Home prices

The median home in Merritt Island costs $264,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $27,460 (11%) higher. It is approximately $47,100 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $24,600 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Merritt Island's median rent is $1,019 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $246 (19%) lower. It is approximately $43 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $177 (14%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
