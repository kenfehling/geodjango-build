Home prices

The median home value in Everglades is $321,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $84,280 (20%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $103,900 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $3,700 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Everglades is $863. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $653 (43%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $199 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $320 (27%) lower.
