Home prices

The median home in Golden Gate costs $233,800. It is about $378,100 (61%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $16,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines is roughly $54,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Golden Gate the median monthly rent is $1,213. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $473 (28%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $151 (14%) higher, and is approximately $302 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines.
