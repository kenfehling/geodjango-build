Compared to other places in this area, Golden Gate is substantially less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Pembroke Pines.

Home prices

The median home cost in Golden Gate is $233,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $378,100 (61%) lower. It is about $16,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines it is roughly $54,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Golden Gate is $1,213 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $473 (28%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $151 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines is roughly $302 (19%) lower.
