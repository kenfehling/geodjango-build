Home prices

The median home in Golden Gate is valued at $233,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $378,100 (61%) lower.It is approximately $16,300 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $54,900 (19%) lower compared to the city of Pembroke Pines.

Rents

Golden Gate's median rent is $1,213 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $473 (28%) lower.It is approximately $151 (14%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $302 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines.
