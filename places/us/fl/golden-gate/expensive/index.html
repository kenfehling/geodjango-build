Home prices

Rents

Golden Gate's median monthly rent is $1,213. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $473 (28%) lower.It is roughly $151 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines it is roughly $302 (19%) lower.
