Compared to other places in this area, Golden Gate is significantly less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Pembroke Pines however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Golden Gate is $233,800. It is approximately $378,100 (61%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $16,300 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines it is roughly $54,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

Golden Gate's median monthly rent is $1,213. It is roughly $473 (28%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $151 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $302 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines.
