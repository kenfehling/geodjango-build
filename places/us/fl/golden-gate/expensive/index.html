Home prices

Golden Gate's median home cost is $233,800. This is about $378,100 (61%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $16,300 (7%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines it is about $54,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

Golden Gate's median monthly rent is $1,213. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $473 (28%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $151 (14%) higher, and is roughly $302 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines.
