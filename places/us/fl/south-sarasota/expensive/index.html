Compared to its nearby areas, South Sarasota is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Tampa, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Sarasota is valued at $474,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $141,760 (42%) higher. It is approximately $257,100 (118%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $235,700 (98%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Sarasota is $1,037. This is roughly $303 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $25 (2%) lower, and is roughly $94 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
