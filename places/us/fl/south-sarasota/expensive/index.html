Home prices

South Sarasota's median home value is $474,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $141,760 (42%) higher.It is approximately $257,100 (118%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $235,700 (98%) higher.

Rents

South Sarasota's median monthly rent is $1,037. It is roughly $303 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $25 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $94 (8%) lower.
