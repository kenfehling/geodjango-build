Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Sarasota is $474,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $141,760 (42%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $257,100 (118%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $235,700 (98%) higher.

Rents

South Sarasota's median monthly rent is $1,037. It is about $303 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $94 (8%) lower.
