Compared to its surrounding areas, Springfield is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Home prices

The median home in Springfield is valued at $111,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $33,880 (23%) lower. It is approximately $105,800 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $92,100 (45%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

In Springfield the median rent is $886 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $124 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $176 (16%) lower, and it is about $137 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
