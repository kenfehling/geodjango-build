Compared to nearby areas, Springfield is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Tallahassee, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Springfield is $111,700. It is approximately $33,880 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $105,800 (48%) lower, and is approximately $92,100 (45%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

In Springfield the median rent is $886 per month. It is about $124 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $176 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tallahassee it is approximately $137 (13%) lower.
