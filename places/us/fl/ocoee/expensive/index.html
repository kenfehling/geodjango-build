In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ocoee is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Ocoee is $242,200. It is approximately $23,940 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $24,700 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $3,300 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Ocoee the median rent is $1,441 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $162 (12%) higher. It is approximately $379 (35%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $310 (27%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
