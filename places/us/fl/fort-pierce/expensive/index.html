Home prices

The median home in Fort Pierce costs $113,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $72,640 (39%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach is roughly $138,100 (54%) lower.

Rents

Fort Pierce's median monthly rent is $943. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $102 (9%) lower.It is about $119 (11%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $360 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach.
