Home prices

The median home cost in Fort Pierce is $113,600. This is approximately $72,640 (39%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $103,900 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach it is roughly $138,100 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Pierce the median monthly rent is $943. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $102 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $119 (11%) lower, and is about $360 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach.
