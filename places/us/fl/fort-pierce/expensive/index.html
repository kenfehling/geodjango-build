Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Pierce is $113,600. It is approximately $72,640 (39%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $103,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of West Palm Beach is roughly $138,100 (54%) lower.

Rents

Fort Pierce's median monthly rent is $943. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $102 (9%) lower.It is roughly $119 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of West Palm Beach it is about $360 (27%) lower.
