Much like its nearby areas, it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Coconut Creek's median home price is $192,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $9,020 (4%) lower. It is roughly $25,000 (11%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $125,200 (39%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Coconut Creek is $1,664. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $285 (20%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $602 (56%) higher, and is roughly $481 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
