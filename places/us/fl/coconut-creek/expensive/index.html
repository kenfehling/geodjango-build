Much like other places in this area, it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Coconut Creek's median home value is $192,500. This is roughly $9,020 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $25,000 (11%) lower, and it is about $125,200 (39%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Coconut Creek is $1,664 per month. This is roughly $285 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $602 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $481 (40%) higher.
