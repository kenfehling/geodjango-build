Home prices

The median home in Coconut Creek is valued at $192,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $9,020 (4%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $25,000 (11%) lower, and it is roughly $125,200 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Coconut Creek the median rent is $1,664 per month. It is roughly $285 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $602 (56%) higher, and it is roughly $481 (40%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
