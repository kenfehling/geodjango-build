Home prices

The median home in Lake Magdalene is valued at $231,000. It is approximately $50,440 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $13,500 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $7,900 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Magdalene is $1,027 per month. This is about $207 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $35 (3%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $104 (9%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
