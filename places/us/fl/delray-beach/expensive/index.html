Home prices

The median home in Delray Beach is valued at $280,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $527,480 (65%) lower.It is roughly $62,700 (28%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $37,500 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Delray Beach is $1,580 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $97 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $518 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $397 (33%) higher.
