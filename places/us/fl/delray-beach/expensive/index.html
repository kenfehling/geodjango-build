Home prices

The median home in Delray Beach is valued at $280,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $326,120 (53%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $62,700 (28%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $37,500 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Delray Beach is $1,580 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $37 (2%) higher. It is roughly $518 (48%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $397 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
