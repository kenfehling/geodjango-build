Compared to its surrounding areas, Delray Beach is significantly less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Delray Beach costs $280,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $326,120 (53%) lower. It is roughly $62,700 (28%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $37,500 (11%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Delray Beach the median monthly rent is $1,580. It is about $37 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $518 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $397 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
