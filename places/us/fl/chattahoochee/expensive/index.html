Compared to its surrounding areas, Chattahoochee is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Home prices

Chattahoochee's median home value is $67,100. This is roughly $15,020 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $150,400 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee is roughly $136,700 (67%) lower.

Rents

Chattahoochee's median rent is $716 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $36 (5%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $346 (32%) lower, and is roughly $307 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
