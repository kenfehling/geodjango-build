Home prices

The median home cost in Chattahoochee is $67,100. This is approximately $15,020 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $150,400 (69%) lower, and it is roughly $136,700 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

In Chattahoochee the median rent is $716 per month. This is about $36 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $346 (32%) lower, and is approximately $307 (30%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.
