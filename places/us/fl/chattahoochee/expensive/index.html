Home prices

The median home in Chattahoochee is valued at $67,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $15,020 (18%) lower. It is about $150,400 (69%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $136,700 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

Chattahoochee's median rent is $716 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $36 (5%) higher. It is approximately $346 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee it is approximately $307 (30%) lower.
