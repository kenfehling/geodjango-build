Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chattahoochee is $67,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $15,020 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $150,400 (69%) lower, and is roughly $136,700 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

The median rent in Chattahoochee is $716 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $36 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $346 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Tallahassee it is approximately $307 (30%) lower.
