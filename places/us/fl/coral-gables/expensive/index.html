In comparison to the surrounding areas, Coral Gables is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Coral Gables's median home value is $846,100. It is approximately $328,600 (63%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $628,600 (289%) higher, and is roughly $528,400 (166%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Coral Gables the median monthly rent is $1,693. This is approximately $195 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $631 (59%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $510 (43%) higher.
