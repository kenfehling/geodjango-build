Compared to nearby areas, Coral Gables is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Coral Gables costs $846,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $328,600 (63%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $628,600 (289%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $528,400 (166%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Coral Gables is $1,693 a month. This is about $195 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $631 (59%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $510 (43%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
