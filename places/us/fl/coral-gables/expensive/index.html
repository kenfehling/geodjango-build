In comparison to its nearby areas, Coral Gables is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home price in Coral Gables is $846,100. It is approximately $328,600 (63%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $628,600 (289%) higher, and is roughly $528,400 (166%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Coral Gables the median monthly rent is $1,693. It is about $195 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $631 (59%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $510 (43%) higher.
