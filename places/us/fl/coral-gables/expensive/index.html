In comparison to its surrounding areas, Coral Gables is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Miami, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Coral Gables costs $846,100. It is about $328,600 (63%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $628,600 (289%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $528,400 (166%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Coral Gables is $1,693. This is roughly $195 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $631 (59%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Miami is about $510 (43%) higher.
