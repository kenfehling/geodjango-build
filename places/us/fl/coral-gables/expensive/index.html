In comparison to the surrounding areas, Coral Gables is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Coral Gables is valued at $846,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $328,600 (63%) higher. It is about $628,600 (289%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $528,400 (166%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Coral Gables is $1,693 a month. This is roughly $195 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $631 (59%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $510 (43%) higher.
