Home prices

The median home in DeLand is valued at $209,200. It is roughly $83,880 (66%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $8,300 (3%) lower, and it is about $36,000 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents
