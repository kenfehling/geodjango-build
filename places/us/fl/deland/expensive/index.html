Home prices

DeLand's median home value is $209,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $83,880 (66%) higher. It is approximately $8,300 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $36,000 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

DeLand's median monthly rent is $966. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $76 (7%) lower. It is roughly $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $99 (9%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
