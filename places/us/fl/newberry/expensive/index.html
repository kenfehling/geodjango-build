Newberry's median home price is $175,600. It is roughly $38,100 (27%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $41,900 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $2,400 (1%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Newberry the median monthly rent is $906. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $144 (18%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $156 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $159 (14%) lower.
