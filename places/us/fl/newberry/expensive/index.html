Home prices

The value of the median home in Newberry is $175,600. This is about $38,100 (27%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $41,900 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $2,400 (1%) higher.

Rents

Newberry's median monthly rent is $906. It is roughly $144 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $156 (14%) lower, and is about $159 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
