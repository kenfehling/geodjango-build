The median home in Odessa is valued at $294,700. It is about $7,660 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $77,200 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $55,800 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Odessa the median rent is $1,250 per month. This is approximately $421 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $188 (17%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $119 (10%) higher.
