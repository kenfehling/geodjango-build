Home prices

The value of the median home in Citrus Hills is $248,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $115,280 (86%) higher. It is roughly $30,900 (14%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $9,500 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Citrus Hills the median rent is $1,150 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $340 (41%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $88 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $19 (1%) higher.
