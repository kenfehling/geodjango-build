Home prices

The price of the median home in Citrus Hills is $248,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $115,280 (86%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $30,900 (14%) higher, and is about $9,500 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Citrus Hills is $1,150. It is approximately $340 (41%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $88 (8%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $19 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
