Home prices

Hollywood's median home price is $254,000. This is about $80,960 (46%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $36,500 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $63,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Hollywood the median rent is $1,217 per month. This is roughly $129 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $155 (14%) higher, and it is roughly $34 (2%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
