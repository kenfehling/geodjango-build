Compared to the surrounding areas, Hollywood is substantially more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hollywood costs $254,000. This is approximately $80,960 (46%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $36,500 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $63,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Hollywood the median monthly rent is $1,217. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $129 (9%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $155 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $34 (2%) higher.
