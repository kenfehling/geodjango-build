In comparison to other places in this area, Hollywood is a lot more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Hollywood's median home value is $254,000. It is about $80,960 (46%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $36,500 (16%) higher, and it is approximately $63,700 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Hollywood the median rent is $1,217 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $129 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $155 (14%) higher, and it is about $34 (2%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
