In comparison to other places in this area, Hollywood is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hollywood is $254,000. It is roughly $80,960 (46%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $36,500 (16%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $63,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

Hollywood's median monthly rent is $1,217. It is about $129 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $155 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $34 (2%) higher.
