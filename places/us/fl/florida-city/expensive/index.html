Compared to its surrounding areas, Florida City is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florida City costs $153,100. It is approximately $68,980 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $64,400 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $164,600 (51%) lower.

Rents

Florida City's median rent is $1,168 per month. This is about $19 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $106 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $15 (1%) lower.
