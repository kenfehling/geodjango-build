In comparison to other places in this area, Florida City is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home value in Florida City is $153,100. This is roughly $68,980 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $64,400 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $164,600 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Florida City the median monthly rent is $1,168. This is about $19 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $106 (9%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
