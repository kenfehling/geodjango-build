Compared to its nearby areas, Florida City is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Florida City is $153,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $68,980 (31%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $64,400 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $164,600 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florida City is $1,168. This is about $19 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $106 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
