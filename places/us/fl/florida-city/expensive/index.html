Compared to the surrounding areas, Florida City is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Florida City is valued at $153,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $68,980 (31%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $64,400 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $164,600 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Florida City the median monthly rent is $1,168. This is roughly $19 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $106 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
