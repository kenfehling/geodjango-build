Home prices

The median home price in Florida City is $153,100. It is about $68,980 (31%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $64,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $164,600 (51%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florida City is $1,168. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $19 (1%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $106 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $15 (1%) lower.
