In comparison to its nearby areas, Orange City is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Orlando, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Orange City costs $116,600. This is approximately $42,820 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $100,900 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $123,400 (51%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Orange City's median rent is $1,131 per month. It is about $105 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $69 (6%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is about $65 (5%) lower.
