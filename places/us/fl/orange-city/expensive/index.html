In comparison to nearby areas, Orange City is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Orlando, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Orange City is valued at $116,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $42,820 (26%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $100,900 (46%) lower, and is about $123,400 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

In Orange City the median rent is $1,131 per month. This is roughly $105 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $69 (6%) higher, and it is approximately $65 (5%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
