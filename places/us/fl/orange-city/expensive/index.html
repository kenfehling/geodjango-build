Compared to other places in this area, Orange City is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Orlando.

Home prices

The median home cost in Orange City is $116,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $42,820 (26%) lower. It is roughly $100,900 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Orlando it is about $123,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Orange City is $1,131. This is about $105 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $69 (6%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $65 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.
