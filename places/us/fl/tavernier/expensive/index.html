Home prices

The median home in Tavernier is valued at $444,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $36,020 (8%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $227,200 (104%) higher, and it is roughly $127,000 (39%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Tavernier is $1,345 a month. This is about $75 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $283 (26%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $162 (13%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
