In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tavernier is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Tavernier is valued at $444,700. This is about $36,020 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $227,200 (104%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $127,000 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Tavernier the median monthly rent is $1,345. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $75 (5%) lower. It is roughly $283 (26%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $162 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
