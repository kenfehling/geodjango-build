In comparison to its surrounding areas, Alford is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Alford is $74,300. This is about $12,400 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $143,200 (65%) lower, and is approximately $129,500 (63%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

Alford's median monthly rent is $742. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $110 (17%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $320 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee is about $281 (27%) lower.
