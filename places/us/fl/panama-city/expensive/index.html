Home prices

The median home in Panama City is valued at $155,800. This is roughly $2,160 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $61,700 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tallahassee is approximately $48,000 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Panama City is $945 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $25 (2%) lower.It is roughly $117 (11%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
