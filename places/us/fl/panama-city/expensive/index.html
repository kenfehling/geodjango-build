Home prices

The median home price in Panama City is $155,800. It is roughly $2,160 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $61,700 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $48,000 (23%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

In Panama City the median monthly rent is $945. It is about $25 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $117 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee it is about $78 (7%) lower.
