Home prices

The median home in Sunny Isles Beach is valued at $359,900. This is approximately $86,060 (19%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $142,400 (65%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $42,200 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Sunny Isles Beach's median monthly rent is $1,610. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $87 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $548 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $427 (36%) higher.
