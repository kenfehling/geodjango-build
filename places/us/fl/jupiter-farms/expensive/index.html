Compared to the surrounding areas, Jupiter Farms is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Jupiter Farms is valued at $392,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $145,660 (27%) lower. It is roughly $175,200 (80%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $75,000 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Jupiter Farms's median rent is $2,191 per month. This is roughly $333 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,129 (106%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $1,008 (85%) higher.
