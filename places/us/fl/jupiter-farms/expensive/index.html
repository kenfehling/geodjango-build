Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jupiter Farms is $392,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $145,660 (27%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $175,200 (80%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $75,000 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jupiter Farms is $2,191. This is roughly $333 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $1,129 (106%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $1,008 (85%) higher.
