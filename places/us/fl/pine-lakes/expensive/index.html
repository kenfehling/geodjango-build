Home prices

The median home value in Pine Lakes is $105,700. This is approximately $27,940 (20%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $111,800 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $133,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Pine Lakes is $907 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $208 (18%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $155 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $224 (19%) lower.
