Compared to the surrounding areas, Glenvar Heights is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Glenvar Heights costs $385,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $112,360 (22%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $167,700 (77%) higher, and it is about $67,500 (21%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Glenvar Heights is $1,415. This is about $118 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $353 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $232 (19%) higher.
