Home prices

The value of the median home in Glenvar Heights is $385,200. This is roughly $112,360 (22%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $167,700 (77%) higher, and is roughly $67,500 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Glenvar Heights's median rent is $1,415 per month. It is roughly $118 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $353 (33%) higher, and it is about $232 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
