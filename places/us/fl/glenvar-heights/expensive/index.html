Home prices

The value of the median home in Glenvar Heights is $385,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $112,360 (22%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $167,700 (77%) higher, and it is roughly $67,500 (21%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Glenvar Heights's median rent is $1,415 per month. This is roughly $118 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $353 (33%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $232 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
