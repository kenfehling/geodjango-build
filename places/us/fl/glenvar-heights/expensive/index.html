Home prices

The median home in Glenvar Heights costs $385,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $112,360 (22%) lower. It is approximately $167,700 (77%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $67,500 (21%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Glenvar Heights's median monthly rent is $1,415. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $118 (7%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $353 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $232 (19%) higher.
