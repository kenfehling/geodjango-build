Home prices

The median home in Mary Esther costs $186,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $28,620 (13%) lower. It is roughly $30,700 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $4,000 (2%) higher compared to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mary Esther is $1,105. It is approximately $19 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $43 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola is roughly $151 (15%) higher.
