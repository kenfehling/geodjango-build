Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Keystone Heights is $1,077. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $332 (44%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $15 (1%) higher, and is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.
