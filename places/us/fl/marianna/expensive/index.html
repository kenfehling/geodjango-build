Home prices

The median home price in Marianna is $88,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $3,700 (4%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $129,300 (59%) lower, and it is roughly $115,600 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

The median rent in Marianna is $605 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $79 (11%) lower. It is about $457 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $418 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
