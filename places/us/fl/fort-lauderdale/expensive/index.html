In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fort Lauderdale is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Lauderdale costs $334,000. This is roughly $129,340 (63%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $116,500 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $16,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Fort Lauderdale the median monthly rent is $1,293. This is approximately $40 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $231 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $110 (9%) higher.
