In comparison to nearby areas, Fort Lauderdale is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Fort Lauderdale is $334,000. This is approximately $129,340 (63%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $116,500 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $16,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

Fort Lauderdale's median monthly rent is $1,293. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $40 (3%) higher.It is about $231 (21%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $110 (9%) higher.
