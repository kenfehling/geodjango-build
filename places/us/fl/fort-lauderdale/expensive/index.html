In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fort Lauderdale is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Lauderdale costs $334,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $129,340 (63%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $116,500 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $16,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Fort Lauderdale the median rent is $1,293 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $40 (3%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $231 (21%) higher, and is about $110 (9%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
