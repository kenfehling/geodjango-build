In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fort Lauderdale is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fort Lauderdale is $334,000. This is approximately $129,340 (63%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $116,500 (53%) higher, and is about $16,300 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Lauderdale is $1,293 per month. This is about $40 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $231 (21%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $110 (9%) higher.
