Home prices

The median home in Wildwood costs $169,100. This is about $8,600 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $48,400 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $69,800 (29%) lower.

Rents

Wildwood's median rent is $968 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $116 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $94 (8%) lower, and is approximately $163 (14%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
