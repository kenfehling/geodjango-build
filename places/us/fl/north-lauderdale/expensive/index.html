Much like nearby areas, it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in North Lauderdale is $167,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $50,400 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $150,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

North Lauderdale's median monthly rent is $1,473. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $214 (16%) higher. It is roughly $411 (38%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $290 (24%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
