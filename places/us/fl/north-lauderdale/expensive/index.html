Much like the surrounding areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Lauderdale is $167,100. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $50,400 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $150,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

North Lauderdale's median rent is $1,473 a month. It is approximately $214 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $411 (38%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $290 (24%) higher.
