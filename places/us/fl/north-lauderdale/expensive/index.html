Similar to other places in this area, it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home value in North Lauderdale is $167,100. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $50,400 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $150,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In North Lauderdale the median monthly rent is $1,473. It is about $214 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $411 (38%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $290 (24%) higher.
