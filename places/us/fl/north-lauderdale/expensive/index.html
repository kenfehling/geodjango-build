Much like other places in this area, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in North Lauderdale costs $167,100. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $50,400 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $150,600 (47%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in North Lauderdale is $1,473 a month. This is about $214 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $411 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $290 (24%) higher.
