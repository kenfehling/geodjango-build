Home prices

The median home in North Lauderdale is valued at $167,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $50,400 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $150,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

North Lauderdale's median rent is $1,473 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $214 (16%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $411 (38%) higher, and it is approximately $290 (24%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
