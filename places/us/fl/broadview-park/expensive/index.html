Home prices

The median home in Broadview Park is valued at $180,500. It is about $16,780 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $37,000 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $137,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Broadview Park's median rent is $1,181 per month. This is about $112 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $119 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is is very similar.
