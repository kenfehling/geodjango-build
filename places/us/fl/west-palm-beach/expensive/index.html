Compared to other places in this area, West Palm Beach is significantly more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Miami, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Palm Beach costs $251,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $78,420 (45%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $34,200 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $66,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in West Palm Beach is $1,303 per month. It is approximately $130 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $241 (22%) higher, and is approximately $120 (10%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
