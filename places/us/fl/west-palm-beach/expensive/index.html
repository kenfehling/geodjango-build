In comparison to other places in this area, West Palm Beach is substantially more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

West Palm Beach's median home price is $251,700. This is roughly $78,420 (45%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $34,200 (15%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $66,000 (20%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

West Palm Beach's median rent is $1,303 a month. This is roughly $130 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $241 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $120 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
