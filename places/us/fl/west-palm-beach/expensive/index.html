Compared to its surrounding areas, West Palm Beach is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

West Palm Beach's median home price is $251,700. This is roughly $78,420 (45%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $34,200 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $66,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

In West Palm Beach the median rent is $1,303 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $130 (11%) higher.It is roughly $241 (22%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $120 (10%) higher.
