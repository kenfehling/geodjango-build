Compared to the surrounding areas, West Palm Beach is significantly more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in West Palm Beach costs $251,700. This is approximately $78,420 (45%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $34,200 (15%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $66,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

In West Palm Beach the median monthly rent is $1,303. This is roughly $130 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $241 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $120 (10%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
