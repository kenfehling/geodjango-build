Compared to other places in this area, West Palm Beach is a lot more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home price in West Palm Beach is $251,700. It is approximately $78,420 (45%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $34,200 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $66,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Palm Beach is $1,303. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $130 (11%) higher. It is about $241 (22%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $120 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
