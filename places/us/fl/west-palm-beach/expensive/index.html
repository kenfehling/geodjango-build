Compared to the surrounding areas, West Palm Beach is a lot more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

West Palm Beach's median home cost is $251,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $78,420 (45%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $34,200 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $66,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Palm Beach is $1,303. It is roughly $130 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $241 (22%) higher, and it is about $120 (10%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
