Home prices

The median home in Highland Park is valued at $227,300. It is about $95,840 (72%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $9,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $11,600 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Highland Park is $800 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $136 (14%) lower. It is about $262 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $331 (29%) lower.
