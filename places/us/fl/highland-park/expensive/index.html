Home prices

The price of the median home in Highland Park is $227,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $95,840 (72%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $9,800 (4%) higher, and is roughly $11,600 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Highland Park's median monthly rent is $800. It is approximately $136 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $262 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $331 (29%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
