Home prices

The median home cost in Cheval is $355,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $61,900 (21%) higher. It is roughly $138,300 (63%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $116,900 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Cheval's median rent is $1,090 a month. This is about $374 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $28 (2%) higher, and it is about $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
