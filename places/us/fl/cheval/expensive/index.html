Home prices

The price of the median home in Cheval is $355,800. It is about $61,900 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $138,300 (63%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $116,900 (48%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Cheval's median rent is $1,090 a month. This is about $374 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $28 (2%) higher, and it is approximately $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
