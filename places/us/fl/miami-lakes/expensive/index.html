In comparison to its surrounding areas, Miami Lakes is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Miami, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Miami Lakes costs $386,900. It is approximately $154,760 (66%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $169,400 (77%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami is about $69,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miami Lakes is $1,563. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $89 (6%) higher.It is roughly $501 (47%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $380 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
