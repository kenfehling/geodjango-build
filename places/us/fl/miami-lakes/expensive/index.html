Compared to the surrounding areas, Miami Lakes is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Miami Lakes is $386,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $154,760 (66%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $169,400 (77%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $69,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

Miami Lakes's median monthly rent is $1,563. This is roughly $89 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $501 (47%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $380 (32%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
