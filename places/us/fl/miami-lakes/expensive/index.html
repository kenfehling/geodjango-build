Home prices

Rents

Miami Lakes's median rent is $1,563 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $89 (6%) higher. It is about $501 (47%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $380 (32%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
