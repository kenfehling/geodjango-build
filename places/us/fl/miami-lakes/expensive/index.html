In comparison to its nearby areas, Miami Lakes is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Miami Lakes costs $386,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $154,760 (66%) higher.It is about $169,400 (77%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $69,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Miami Lakes is $1,563 a month. It is roughly $89 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $501 (47%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $380 (32%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
