Compared to the surrounding areas, South Palm Beach is significantly more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home value in South Palm Beach is $262,300. This is about $79,620 (43%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $44,800 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $55,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

In South Palm Beach the median monthly rent is $1,500. This is roughly $237 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $438 (41%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $317 (26%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
