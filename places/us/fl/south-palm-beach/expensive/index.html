In comparison to nearby areas, South Palm Beach is significantly more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Palm Beach costs $262,300. It is about $79,620 (43%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $44,800 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $55,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

South Palm Beach's median monthly rent is $1,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $237 (18%) higher.It is approximately $438 (41%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $317 (26%) higher.
