Home prices

The median home cost in Oviedo is $289,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $85,700 (42%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $71,500 (32%) higher, and it is about $50,100 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Oviedo's median rent is $1,629 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $425 (35%) higher. It is approximately $567 (53%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $498 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
