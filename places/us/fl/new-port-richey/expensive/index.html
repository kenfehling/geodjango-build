Compared to the surrounding areas, New Port Richey is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

New Port Richey's median home price is $87,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,960 (15%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $130,000 (59%) lower, and is roughly $151,400 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

New Port Richey's median monthly rent is $823. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $177 (17%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $239 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $308 (27%) lower.
