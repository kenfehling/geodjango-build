Compared to the surrounding areas, New Port Richey is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Port Richey costs $87,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $15,960 (15%) lower. It is roughly $130,000 (59%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $151,400 (63%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

New Port Richey's median monthly rent is $823. It is approximately $177 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $239 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $308 (27%) lower.
