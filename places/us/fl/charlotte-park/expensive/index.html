Home prices

The median home in Charlotte Park is valued at $196,800. It is about $27,380 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $20,700 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $42,100 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Charlotte Park the median rent is $974 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $32 (3%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $88 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $157 (13%) lower.
