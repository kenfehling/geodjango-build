Compared to other places in this area, Forest City is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Forest City is $250,900. This is roughly $61,540 (32%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $33,400 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $12,000 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Forest City is $1,193 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $109 (10%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $131 (12%) higher, and is about $62 (5%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
