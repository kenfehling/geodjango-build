Compared to its surrounding areas, Forest City is a lot more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Forest City costs $250,900. This is roughly $61,540 (32%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $33,400 (15%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $12,000 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Forest City is $1,193 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $109 (10%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $131 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $62 (5%) higher.
