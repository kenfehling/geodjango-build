Compared to other places in this area, Forest City is substantially more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a little more expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Forest City is $250,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $61,540 (32%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $33,400 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $12,000 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Forest City is $1,193. This is about $109 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $131 (12%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $62 (5%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
