Rents

The median monthly rent in Port Orange is $1,161. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $198 (14%) lower. It is approximately $99 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $96 (9%) higher.
