Home prices

The median home in Lake City is valued at $106,800. This is about $18,120 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $110,700 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $66,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Lake City the median rent is $856 per month. This is roughly $186 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $206 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $209 (19%) lower.
