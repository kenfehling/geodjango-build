Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake City is $856. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $186 (27%) higher. It is about $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $209 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
