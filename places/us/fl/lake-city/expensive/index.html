Home prices

The median home in Lake City costs $106,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $18,120 (14%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $110,700 (50%) lower, and is roughly $66,400 (38%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Lake City's median monthly rent is $856. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $186 (27%) higher.It is approximately $206 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $209 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
