Home prices

The median home in Lake City costs $106,800. This is roughly $18,120 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $110,700 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $66,400 (38%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median rent in Lake City is $856 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $186 (27%) higher. It is roughly $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $209 (19%) lower.
