Home prices

The median home cost in North River Shores is $229,800. It is about $15,160 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $12,300 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale it is approximately $104,200 (31%) lower.

Rents

North River Shores's median rent is $1,223 a month. This is roughly $43 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $161 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale is about $70 (5%) lower.
