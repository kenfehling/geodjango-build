Compared to its surrounding areas, Redington Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Tampa, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Redington Beach costs $456,700. This is roughly $77,220 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $239,200 (109%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $217,800 (91%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Redington Beach the median monthly rent is $2,163. It is roughly $930 (75%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $1,101 (103%) higher, and is approximately $1,032 (91%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
