Much like nearby areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Palmetto's median home price is $156,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $3,600 (2%) lower. It is roughly $60,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $82,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Palmetto the median monthly rent is $938. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $164 (14%) lower. It is approximately $124 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $193 (17%) lower.
