Similar to its surrounding areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Palmetto's median home price is $156,700. This is about $3,600 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $60,800 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $82,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Palmetto is $938 per month. This is approximately $164 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $193 (17%) lower.
