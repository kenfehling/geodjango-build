Much like other places in this area, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Palmetto is $156,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $3,600 (2%) lower. It is roughly $60,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $82,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

Palmetto's median monthly rent is $938. This is approximately $164 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $124 (11%) lower, and it is about $193 (17%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
