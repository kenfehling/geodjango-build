Much like its nearby areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palmetto is valued at $156,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $3,600 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $60,800 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $82,200 (34%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Palmetto the median rent is $938 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $164 (14%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $124 (11%) lower, and it is roughly $193 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
