Home prices

The median home in Lady Lake is valued at $146,300. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $61,020 (29%) lower.It is approximately $71,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $92,600 (38%) lower.

Rents
