Home prices

The median home in Lady Lake is valued at $146,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $61,020 (29%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $71,200 (32%) lower, and is roughly $92,600 (38%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Lady Lake is $1,034 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $25 (2%) lower. It is roughly $28 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $97 (8%) lower.
