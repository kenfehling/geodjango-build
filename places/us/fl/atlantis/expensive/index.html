In comparison to other places in this area, Atlantis is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Atlantis costs $387,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $207,880 (115%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $170,200 (78%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $70,000 (22%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atlantis is $1,841. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $665 (56%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $779 (73%) higher, and it is about $658 (55%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
