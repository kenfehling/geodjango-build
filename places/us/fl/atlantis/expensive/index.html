Home prices

The median home in Atlantis costs $387,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $207,880 (115%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $170,200 (78%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $70,000 (22%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atlantis is $1,841. This is roughly $665 (56%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $779 (73%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $658 (55%) higher.
