Home prices

The median home value in Lakewood Park is $131,100. This is roughly $73,400 (35%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $86,400 (39%) lower, and it is approximately $108,900 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Lakewood Park's median rent is $975 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $58 (6%) higher. It is about $87 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is roughly $221 (18%) lower.
