Home prices

The median home price in Jensen Beach is $232,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21,560 (8%) lower. It is roughly $15,100 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $101,400 (30%) lower compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.

Rents

In Jensen Beach the median rent is $972 a month. It is about $294 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $90 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale it is roughly $321 (24%) lower.
