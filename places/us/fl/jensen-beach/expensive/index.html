Home prices

The median home in Jensen Beach costs $232,600. This is approximately $21,560 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $15,100 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale it is approximately $101,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

Jensen Beach's median monthly rent is $972. It is about $294 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $90 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale is about $321 (24%) lower.
