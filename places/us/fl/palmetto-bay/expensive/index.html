Home prices

The median home in Palmetto Bay costs $562,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $184,600 (48%) higher.It is roughly $344,800 (158%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $244,600 (76%) higher.

Rents

In Palmetto Bay the median monthly rent is $1,426. In comparison to other places in this area it is is very similar.It is approximately $364 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $243 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
