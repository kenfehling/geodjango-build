Home prices

The median home in Brookridge costs $76,400. This is roughly $17,240 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $141,100 (64%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $162,500 (68%) lower.

Rents

Brookridge's median rent is $996 a month. This is approximately $215 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $66 (6%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $135 (11%) lower.
