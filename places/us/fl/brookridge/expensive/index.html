Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brookridge is $76,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $17,240 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $141,100 (64%) lower, and is about $162,500 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Brookridge the median monthly rent is $996. It is approximately $215 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $66 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $135 (11%) lower.
