Home prices

The value of the median home in River Park is $115,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $78,760 (40%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $102,200 (46%) lower, and is roughly $136,400 (54%) lower compared to the city of West Palm Beach.

Rents

River Park's median rent is $1,150 per month. It is about $95 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $88 (8%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of West Palm Beach it is approximately $153 (11%) lower.
