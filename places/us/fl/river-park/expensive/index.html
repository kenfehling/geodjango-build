Home prices

The median home in River Park is valued at $115,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $78,760 (40%) lower.It is approximately $102,200 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $136,400 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach.

Rents

The median monthly rent in River Park is $1,150. It is approximately $95 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $88 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach it is about $153 (11%) lower.
