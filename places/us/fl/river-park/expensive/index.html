Home prices

The median home in River Park is valued at $115,300. It is approximately $78,760 (40%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $102,200 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach is about $136,400 (54%) lower.

Rents

River Park's median rent is $1,150 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $95 (7%) lower.It is approximately $88 (8%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $153 (11%) lower compared to the city of West Palm Beach.
