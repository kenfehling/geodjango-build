Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Poinciana is $1,247 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $44 (3%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $185 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $116 (10%) higher.
