Home prices

The median home price in Altamonte Springs is $184,100. It is about $75,200 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $33,400 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $54,800 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Altamonte Springs the median rent is $1,185 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $74 (5%) lower. It is roughly $123 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $54 (4%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
