Compared to other places in this area, Altamonte Springs is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Altamonte Springs is $184,100. It is about $75,200 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $33,400 (15%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $54,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Altamonte Springs is $1,185. This is approximately $74 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $123 (11%) higher, and it is about $54 (4%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
