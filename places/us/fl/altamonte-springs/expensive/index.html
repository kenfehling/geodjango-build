Home prices

The cost of the median home in Altamonte Springs is $184,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $75,200 (29%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $33,400 (15%) lower, and is about $54,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Altamonte Springs's median rent is $1,185 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $74 (5%) lower.It is roughly $123 (11%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $54 (4%) higher.
