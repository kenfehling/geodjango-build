Home prices

The price of the median home in Altamonte Springs is $184,100. It is approximately $75,200 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $33,400 (15%) lower, and is about $54,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Altamonte Springs the median rent is $1,185 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $74 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $123 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $54 (4%) higher.
