Home prices

The median home in Altamonte Springs is valued at $184,100. It is approximately $75,200 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $33,400 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $54,800 (22%) lower.

Rents
