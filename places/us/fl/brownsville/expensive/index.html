Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brownsville is $160,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $118,680 (42%) lower. It is approximately $56,800 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $157,000 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Brownsville the median rent is $809 per month. It is about $306 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $253 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $374 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
