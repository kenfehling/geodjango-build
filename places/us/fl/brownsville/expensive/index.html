Home prices

The median home in Brownsville costs $160,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $118,680 (42%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $56,800 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $157,000 (49%) lower.

Rents

Brownsville's median rent is $809 a month. It is roughly $306 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $253 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $374 (31%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
