Home prices

The median home in Cinco Bayou costs $235,700. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $12,820 (5%) higher. It is roughly $18,200 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $52,900 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

In Cinco Bayou the median rent is $1,105 a month. It is roughly $32 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $43 (4%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pensacola it is approximately $151 (15%) higher.
