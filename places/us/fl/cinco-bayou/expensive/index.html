Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cinco Bayou is $235,700. This is roughly $12,820 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $18,200 (8%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $52,900 (28%) higher compared to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

The median rent in Cinco Bayou is $1,105 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $32 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $43 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Pensacola is roughly $151 (15%) higher.
