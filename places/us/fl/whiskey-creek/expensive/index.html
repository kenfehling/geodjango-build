Compared to the surrounding areas, Whiskey Creek is a lot more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Home prices

Whiskey Creek's median home cost is $232,900. This is approximately $67,500 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $15,400 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is roughly $3,500 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Whiskey Creek the median rent is $1,196 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $103 (9%) higher. It is roughly $134 (12%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $48 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
