Compared to the surrounding areas, Dade City is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Dade City's median home cost is $106,300. It is roughly $16,420 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $111,200 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $132,600 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Dade City is $1,061 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $254 (31%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $70 (6%) lower.
