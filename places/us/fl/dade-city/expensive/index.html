Home prices

The value of the median home in Dade City is $106,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $16,420 (13%) lower.It is approximately $111,200 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $132,600 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Dade City is $1,061 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $254 (31%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $70 (6%) lower.
