Home prices

The median home in Three Lakes costs $294,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $15,600 (5%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $76,600 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $23,600 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Three Lakes the median monthly rent is $1,836. It is approximately $103 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $774 (72%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $653 (55%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
