Home prices

The median home in Three Lakes costs $294,100. This is approximately $15,600 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $76,600 (35%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $23,600 (7%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Three Lakes is $1,836. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $103 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $774 (72%) higher, and it is approximately $653 (55%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
