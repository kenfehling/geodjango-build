Home prices

The median home in Three Lakes costs $294,100. This is about $15,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $76,600 (35%) higher, and it is approximately $23,600 (7%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Three Lakes the median rent is $1,836 a month. It is approximately $103 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $774 (72%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $653 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
