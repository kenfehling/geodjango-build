Compared to other places in this area, Three Lakes is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Three Lakes is $294,100. This is roughly $15,600 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $76,600 (35%) higher, and is approximately $23,600 (7%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Three Lakes's median monthly rent is $1,836. This is roughly $103 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $774 (72%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $653 (55%) higher.
