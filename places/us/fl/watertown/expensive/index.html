Compared to other places in this area, Watertown is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Watertown is $105,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $19,300 (15%) lower. It is about $111,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $67,500 (38%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Watertown's median rent is $773 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $133 (20%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $289 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $292 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
