Home prices

La Crosse's median home price is $106,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $32,940 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $110,900 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $66,600 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in La Crosse is $1,083. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $251 (30%) higher. It is about $21 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $18 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
