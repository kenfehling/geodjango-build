Compared to other places in this area, North Fort Myers is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cape Coral, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Fort Myers costs $97,900. This is roughly $42,940 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $119,600 (54%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $131,500 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

North Fort Myers's median monthly rent is $878. It is roughly $55 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $184 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cape Coral is roughly $366 (29%) lower.
