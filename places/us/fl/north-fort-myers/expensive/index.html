In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Fort Myers is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cape Coral, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Fort Myers costs $97,900. This is about $42,940 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $119,600 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Cape Coral is about $131,500 (57%) lower.

Rents

In North Fort Myers the median rent is $878 a month. This is about $55 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $184 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $366 (29%) lower compared to the city of Cape Coral.
