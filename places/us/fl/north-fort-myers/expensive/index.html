Home prices

North Fort Myers's median home price is $97,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $42,940 (30%) lower. It is about $119,600 (54%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $131,500 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

In North Fort Myers the median rent is $878 a month. This is roughly $55 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $184 (17%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral it is approximately $366 (29%) lower.
