Compared to the surrounding areas, North Fort Myers is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cape Coral, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

North Fort Myers's median home price is $97,900. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $42,940 (30%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $119,600 (54%) lower, and is roughly $131,500 (57%) lower compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

North Fort Myers's median monthly rent is $878. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $55 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $184 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $366 (29%) lower compared to the city of Cape Coral.
