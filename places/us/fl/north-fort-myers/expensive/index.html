Home prices

The median home price in North Fort Myers is $97,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $42,940 (30%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $119,600 (54%) lower, and it is roughly $131,500 (57%) lower compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

North Fort Myers's median monthly rent is $878. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $55 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $184 (17%) lower, and is about $366 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
