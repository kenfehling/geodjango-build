Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Citrus Springs is $1,075. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $221 (25%) higher. It is about $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $56 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
