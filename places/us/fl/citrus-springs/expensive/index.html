Home prices

The median home cost in Citrus Springs is $142,700. This is roughly $16,360 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $74,800 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $96,200 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Citrus Springs is $1,075 per month. This is about $221 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $13 (1%) higher, and it is about $56 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
