Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jacksonville Beach is $368,200. This is about $63,700 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $150,700 (69%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $195,000 (112%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksonville Beach is $1,454. It is roughly $65 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $392 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is about $389 (36%) higher.
