Compared to nearby areas, Jacksonville Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Jacksonville Beach is $368,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $63,700 (14%) lower. It is roughly $150,700 (69%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $195,000 (112%) higher.

Rents

Jacksonville Beach's median monthly rent is $1,454. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $65 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $392 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $389 (36%) higher.
