Compared to other places in this area, Jacksonville Beach is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jacksonville Beach is $368,200. It is about $63,700 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $150,700 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $195,000 (112%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksonville Beach is $1,454. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $65 (4%) lower.It is approximately $392 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $389 (36%) higher.
