Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bee Ridge is $245,600. This is about $12,320 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $28,100 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $6,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

Bee Ridge's median monthly rent is $1,394. It is about $44 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $332 (31%) higher, and is approximately $263 (23%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
