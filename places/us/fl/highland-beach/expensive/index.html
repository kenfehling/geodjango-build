In comparison to its nearby areas, Highland Beach is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Highland Beach is $591,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $162,140 (37%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $374,000 (171%) higher, and is about $273,800 (86%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Highland Beach's median rent is $2,325 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $907 (63%) higher. It is approximately $1,263 (118%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $1,142 (96%) higher.
