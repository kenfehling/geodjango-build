Home prices

The median home in Highland Beach is valued at $591,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $204,140 (25%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $374,000 (171%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $273,800 (86%) higher.
