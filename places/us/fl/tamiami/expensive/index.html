In comparison to nearby areas, Tamiami is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Miami, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Tamiami is $297,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $31,040 (11%) higher.It is about $80,200 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $20,000 (6%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Tamiami's median rent is $1,614 a month. It is roughly $185 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $552 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $431 (36%) higher.
