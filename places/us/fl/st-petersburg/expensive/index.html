Compared to nearby areas, St. Petersburg is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

St. Petersburg's median home value is $205,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $28,040 (15%) higher.It is about $12,500 (5%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $33,900 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In St. Petersburg the median rent is $1,116 per month. This is about $76 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $54 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $15 (1%) lower.
