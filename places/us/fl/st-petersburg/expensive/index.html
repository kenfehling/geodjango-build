In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Petersburg is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in St. Petersburg is $205,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $28,040 (15%) higher.It is approximately $12,500 (5%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $33,900 (14%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Petersburg is $1,116. Compared to nearby areas it is about $76 (7%) higher.It is roughly $54 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
