Compared to other places in this area, St. Petersburg is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

St. Petersburg's median home price is $205,000. It is approximately $28,040 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $12,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $33,900 (14%) lower.

Rents

In St. Petersburg the median monthly rent is $1,116. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $76 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $54 (5%) higher, and is about $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
