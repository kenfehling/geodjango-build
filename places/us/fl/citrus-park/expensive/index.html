Home prices

The median home in Citrus Park costs $240,600. This is about $11,740 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $23,100 (10%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Citrus Park is $1,302 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $46 (3%) higher.It is roughly $240 (22%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $171 (15%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
