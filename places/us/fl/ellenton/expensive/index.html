Compared to the surrounding areas, Ellenton is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Ellenton costs $121,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $14,100 (10%) lower. It is approximately $96,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $117,800 (49%) lower.

Rents

Ellenton's median monthly rent is $887. This is roughly $130 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $175 (16%) lower, and it is about $244 (21%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
