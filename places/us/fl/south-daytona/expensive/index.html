Compared to its nearby areas, South Daytona is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The median home in South Daytona costs $146,800. It is approximately $74,840 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $70,700 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $26,400 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Daytona is $995. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $244 (19%) lower.It is about $67 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $70 (6%) lower.
