Home prices

The median home price in South Daytona is $146,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $74,840 (33%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $70,700 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $26,400 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Daytona is $995. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $244 (19%) lower. It is roughly $67 (6%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $70 (6%) lower.
