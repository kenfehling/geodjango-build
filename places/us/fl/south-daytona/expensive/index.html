In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Daytona is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

South Daytona's median home value is $146,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $74,840 (33%) lower.It is roughly $70,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $26,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

South Daytona's median monthly rent is $995. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $244 (19%) lower.It is about $67 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $70 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
