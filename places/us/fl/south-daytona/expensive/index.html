In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Daytona is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Daytona is valued at $146,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $74,840 (33%) lower.It is approximately $70,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $26,400 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in South Daytona is $995 per month. This is roughly $244 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $67 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $70 (6%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
