Compared to its nearby areas, South Daytona is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonvillehowever, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Daytona costs $146,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $74,840 (33%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $70,700 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $26,400 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in South Daytona is $995 per month. It is about $244 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $67 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $70 (6%) lower.
