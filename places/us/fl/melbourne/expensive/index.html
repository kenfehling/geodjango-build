Home prices

The value of the median home in Melbourne is $169,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $101,020 (37%) lower.It is roughly $48,500 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $71,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Melbourne's median monthly rent is $1,030. Compared to nearby areas this is about $154 (13%) lower.It is approximately $32 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is roughly $166 (13%) lower.
