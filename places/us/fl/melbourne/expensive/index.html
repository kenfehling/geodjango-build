In comparison to its surrounding areas, Melbourne is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Orlando, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Melbourne costs $169,000. This is approximately $101,020 (37%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $48,500 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Orlando is approximately $71,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Melbourne is $1,030 a month. It is about $154 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $32 (3%) lower, and is roughly $166 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.
