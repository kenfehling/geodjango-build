Home prices

The median home in Plant City costs $169,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $3,120 (1%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $48,100 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $69,500 (29%) lower.

Rents

Plant City's median monthly rent is $997. This is approximately $52 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $65 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $134 (11%) lower.
