The median home in Plant City is valued at $169,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $3,120 (1%) higher. It is approximately $48,100 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $69,500 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Plant City is $997. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $52 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $65 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $134 (11%) lower.
