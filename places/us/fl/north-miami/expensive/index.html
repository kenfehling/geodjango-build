Compared to the surrounding areas, North Miami is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in North Miami is valued at $202,600. This is approximately $299,960 (59%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $14,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $115,100 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

North Miami's median rent is $1,172 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $294 (20%) lower. It is roughly $110 (10%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Miami.
