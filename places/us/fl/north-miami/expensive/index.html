Compared to other places in this area, North Miami is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home value in North Miami is $202,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $299,960 (59%) lower. It is roughly $14,900 (6%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $115,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

In North Miami the median rent is $1,172 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $294 (20%) lower. It is approximately $110 (10%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is is very similar.
