In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jupiter is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Miami, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Jupiter's median home cost is $372,800. It is roughly $138,060 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $155,300 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $55,100 (17%) higher.

Rents

Jupiter's median monthly rent is $1,644. It is roughly $363 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $582 (54%) higher, and it is about $461 (38%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
