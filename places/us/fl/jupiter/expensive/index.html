Compared to its nearby areas, Jupiter is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Jupiter is $372,800. This is approximately $138,060 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $155,300 (71%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $55,100 (17%) higher.

Rents

Jupiter's median rent is $1,644 a month. It is about $363 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $582 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $461 (38%) higher.
