In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jupiter is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Jupiter is $372,800. Compared to nearby areas it is about $138,060 (27%) lower.It is about $155,300 (71%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is about $55,100 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Jupiter the median rent is $1,644 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $363 (18%) lower.It is about $582 (54%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $461 (38%) higher.
