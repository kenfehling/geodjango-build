Home prices

Temple Terrace's median home price is $228,900. It is roughly $62,820 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $11,400 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $10,000 (4%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Temple Terrace's median monthly rent is $1,143. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $92 (8%) higher. It is approximately $81 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $12 (1%) higher.
