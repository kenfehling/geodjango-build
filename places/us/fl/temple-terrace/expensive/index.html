Home prices

The median home in Temple Terrace costs $228,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $62,820 (37%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $11,400 (5%) higher, and is roughly $10,000 (4%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Temple Terrace is $1,143. This is about $92 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $81 (7%) higher, and is roughly $12 (1%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
