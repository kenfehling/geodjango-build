Home prices

The median home cost in Temple Terrace is $228,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $62,820 (37%) higher.It is roughly $11,400 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $10,000 (4%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Temple Terrace's median monthly rent is $1,143. This is roughly $92 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $81 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $12 (1%) higher.
