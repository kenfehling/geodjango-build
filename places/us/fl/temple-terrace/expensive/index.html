Home prices

The price of the median home in Temple Terrace is $228,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $62,820 (37%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $11,400 (5%) higher, and it is roughly $10,000 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Temple Terrace's median rent is $1,143 a month. This is about $92 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $81 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $12 (1%) higher.
