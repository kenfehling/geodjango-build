Home prices

The value of the median home in Yankeetown is $166,300. This is approximately $19,260 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $51,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $72,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Yankeetown is $1,018 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $280 (37%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $44 (4%) lower, and is roughly $113 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
