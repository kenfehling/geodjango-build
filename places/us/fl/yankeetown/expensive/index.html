Rents

The median monthly rent in Yankeetown is $1,018. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $280 (37%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $44 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $113 (9%) lower.
