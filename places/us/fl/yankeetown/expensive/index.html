Home prices

Rents

In Yankeetown the median rent is $1,018 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $280 (37%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $44 (4%) lower, and is about $113 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
