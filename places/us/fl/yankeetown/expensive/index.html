Home prices

The median home in Yankeetown costs $166,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $19,260 (13%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $51,200 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $72,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Yankeetown is $1,018 a month. It is about $280 (37%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $44 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $113 (9%) lower.
