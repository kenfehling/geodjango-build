Home prices

The median home cost in Bonifay is $107,600. It is roughly $32,500 (43%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $109,900 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $43,100 (28%) lower compared to the city of Dothan.

Rents

Bonifay's median rent is $756 a month. This is approximately $104 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $306 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Dothan is is very similar.
