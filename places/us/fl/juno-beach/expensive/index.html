The median home in Juno Beach is valued at $430,700. This is approximately $73,380 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $213,200 (98%) higher, and it is approximately $113,000 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents
