Home prices

The median home price in Cortez is $289,100. It is about $5,440 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $71,600 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $50,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cortez is $1,212 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $33 (2%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $150 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $81 (7%) higher.
