Home prices

The median home in Cortez is valued at $289,100. This is approximately $5,440 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $71,600 (32%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $50,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Cortez's median monthly rent is $1,212. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $33 (2%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $150 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $81 (7%) higher.
