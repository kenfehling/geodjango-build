Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cortez is $1,212. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $33 (2%) higher.It is approximately $150 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $81 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
