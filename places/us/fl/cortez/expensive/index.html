Home prices

Cortez's median home cost is $289,100. This is roughly $5,440 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $71,600 (32%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $50,200 (21%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cortez is $1,212. This is roughly $33 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $150 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $81 (7%) higher.
