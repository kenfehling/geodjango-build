Compared to its surrounding areas, Cocoa is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Orlando, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cocoa is valued at $115,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $45,120 (28%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $102,000 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $124,500 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cocoa is $829. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $191 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $233 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is approximately $367 (30%) lower.
