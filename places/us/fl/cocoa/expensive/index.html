Compared to other places in this area, Cocoa is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Orlando.

Home prices

The median home in Cocoa is valued at $115,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $45,120 (28%) lower. It is about $102,000 (46%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Orlando it is about $124,500 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Cocoa the median monthly rent is $829. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $191 (18%) lower. It is approximately $233 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $367 (30%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
