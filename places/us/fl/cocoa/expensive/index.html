Compared to the surrounding areas, Cocoa is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Orlando, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cocoa costs $115,500. It is approximately $45,120 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $102,000 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Orlando it is approximately $124,500 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Cocoa the median rent is $829 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $191 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $233 (21%) lower, and it is about $367 (30%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
