Home prices

Cocoa's median home value is $115,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $45,120 (28%) lower.It is roughly $102,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $124,500 (51%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Cocoa's median rent is $829 per month. This is approximately $191 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $233 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Orlando it is approximately $367 (30%) lower.
