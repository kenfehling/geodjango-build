Home prices

Auburndale's median home value is $180,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $84,500 (87%) higher. It is approximately $36,700 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $58,100 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Auburndale is $888. This is approximately $12 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $174 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $243 (21%) lower.
