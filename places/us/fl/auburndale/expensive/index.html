Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Auburndale is $888. This is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $174 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $243 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
