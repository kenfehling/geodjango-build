Home prices

The median home cost in McGregor is $325,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $178,740 (121%) higher.It is roughly $108,100 (49%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $96,200 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

In McGregor the median monthly rent is $1,316. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $247 (23%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $254 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is approximately $72 (5%) higher.
