Home prices

The median home in McGregor is valued at $325,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $178,740 (121%) higher. It is approximately $108,100 (49%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $96,200 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

In McGregor the median monthly rent is $1,316. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $247 (23%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $254 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is approximately $72 (5%) higher.
