Home prices

The median home cost in Palm Springs is $148,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $75,660 (33%) lower.It is about $68,700 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $168,900 (53%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Palm Springs the median monthly rent is $1,186. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $128 (9%) lower.It is about $124 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Miami.
