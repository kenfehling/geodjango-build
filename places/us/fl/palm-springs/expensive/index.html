Compared to its surrounding areas, Palm Springs is substantially less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Palm Springs is $148,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $75,660 (33%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $68,700 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $168,900 (53%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Palm Springs's median rent is $1,186 a month. It is approximately $128 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $124 (11%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Miami.
