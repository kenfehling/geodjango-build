In comparison to the surrounding areas, Palm Springs is significantly less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home cost in Palm Springs is $148,800. This is about $75,660 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $68,700 (31%) lower, and is about $168,900 (53%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Palm Springs's median rent is $1,186 per month. This is approximately $128 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $124 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Miami.
