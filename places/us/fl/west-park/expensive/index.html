Home prices

The median home in West Park is valued at $174,800. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $38,780 (18%) lower. It is roughly $42,700 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $142,900 (44%) lower.

Rents

West Park's median monthly rent is $1,426. Compared to nearby areas this is about $22 (1%) higher. It is roughly $364 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $243 (20%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
