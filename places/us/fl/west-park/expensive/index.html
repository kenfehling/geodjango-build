Home prices

The median home price in West Park is $174,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $38,780 (18%) lower. It is approximately $42,700 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $142,900 (44%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In West Park the median rent is $1,426 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $22 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $364 (34%) higher, and is approximately $243 (20%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
