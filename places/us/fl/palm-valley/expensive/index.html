In comparison to its surrounding areas, Palm Valley is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Valley is valued at $476,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $66,620 (16%) higher. It is about $259,300 (119%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $303,600 (175%) higher.

Rents

Palm Valley's median rent is $1,397 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $134 (8%) lower. It is about $335 (31%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $332 (31%) higher.
