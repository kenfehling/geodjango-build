Compared to nearby areas, Palm Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Valley is valued at $476,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $66,620 (16%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $259,300 (119%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is about $303,600 (175%) higher.

Rents

In Palm Valley the median monthly rent is $1,397. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $134 (8%) lower. It is approximately $335 (31%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $332 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
