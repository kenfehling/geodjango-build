Home prices

Palm Valley's median home price is $476,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $66,620 (16%) higher.It is roughly $259,300 (119%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $303,600 (175%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Palm Valley's median rent is $1,397 a month. This is roughly $134 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $335 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is about $332 (31%) higher.
