Home prices

The price of the median home in June Park is $204,600. This is roughly $72,580 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $12,900 (5%) lower, and it is approximately $35,400 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

In June Park the median monthly rent is $1,295. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $233 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is roughly $99 (8%) higher.
