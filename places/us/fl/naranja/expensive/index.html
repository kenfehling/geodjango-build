Home prices

The median home cost in Naranja is $172,700. It is approximately $60,100 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $44,800 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $145,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Naranja's median rent is $1,168 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21 (1%) lower.It is approximately $106 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
