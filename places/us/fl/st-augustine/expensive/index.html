In comparison to nearby areas, St. Augustine is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Jacksonville however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Augustine is valued at $271,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $37,340 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $53,500 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $97,800 (56%) higher.

Rents

In St. Augustine the median rent is $1,052 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $235 (18%) lower. It is is very similar compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $13 (1%) lower.
