Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Augustine is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Augustine is valued at $271,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $37,340 (12%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $53,500 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $97,800 (56%) higher.

Rents

St. Augustine's median rent is $1,052 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $235 (18%) lower.It is is very similar compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
