Compared to its nearby areas, St. Augustine is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonvillehowever, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Augustine costs $271,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $37,340 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $53,500 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $97,800 (56%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Augustine is $1,052. This is about $235 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
