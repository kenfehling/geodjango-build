Compared to nearby areas, St. Augustine is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Augustine is $271,000. It is about $37,340 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $53,500 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $97,800 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Augustine is $1,052. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $235 (18%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $13 (1%) lower.
