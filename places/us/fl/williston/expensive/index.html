Compared to its surrounding areas, Williston is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Williston is valued at $120,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $13,920 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $97,000 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $52,700 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Williston the median rent is $722 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $31 (4%) higher. It is approximately $340 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $343 (32%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
