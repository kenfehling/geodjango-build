Compared to the surrounding areas, Pine Ridge is significantly more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pine Ridge is $232,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $97,640 (72%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $15,200 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $6,200 (2%) lower.

Rents

Pine Ridge's median rent is $926 a month. This is about $36 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $136 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $205 (18%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
