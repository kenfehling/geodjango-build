Home prices

The median home in West Miami costs $335,800. This is approximately $19,360 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $118,300 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $18,100 (5%) higher.

Rents

West Miami's median monthly rent is $1,515. It is approximately $16 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $453 (42%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $332 (28%) higher.
