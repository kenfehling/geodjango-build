Home prices

The median home in West Miami costs $335,800. It is roughly $19,360 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $118,300 (54%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $18,100 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

West Miami's median rent is $1,515 a month. This is about $16 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $453 (42%) higher, and is about $332 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
