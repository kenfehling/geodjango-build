Home prices

Rents

In West Miami the median monthly rent is $1,515. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $16 (1%) higher.It is about $453 (42%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $332 (28%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
