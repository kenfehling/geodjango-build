In comparison to its nearby areas, Ocean City is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Pensacola, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Ocean City is valued at $174,800. This is approximately $60,260 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $42,700 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $8,000 (4%) lower compared to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

In Ocean City the median rent is $947 a month. It is roughly $222 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $115 (10%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Pensacola.
