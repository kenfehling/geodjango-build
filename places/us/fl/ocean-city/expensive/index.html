Home prices

The median home in Ocean City costs $174,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $60,260 (25%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $42,700 (19%) lower, and is about $8,000 (4%) lower compared to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ocean City is $947. This is about $222 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $115 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Pensacola is is very similar.
