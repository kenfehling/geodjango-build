Compared to its nearby areas, Ocean City is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Pensacola however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Ocean City is valued at $174,800. It is about $60,260 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $42,700 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $8,000 (4%) lower compared to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

Ocean City's median monthly rent is $947. Compared to nearby areas it is about $222 (18%) lower. It is roughly $115 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Pensacola.
