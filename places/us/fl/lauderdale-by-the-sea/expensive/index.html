Home prices

The median home in Lauderdale-by-the-Sea is valued at $441,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,340 (2%) lower.It is about $223,500 (102%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $123,300 (38%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Lauderdale-by-the-Sea the median monthly rent is $1,422. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $248 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $360 (33%) higher, and it is approximately $239 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
