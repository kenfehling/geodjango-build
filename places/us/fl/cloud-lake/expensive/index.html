Compared to other places in this area, Cloud Lake is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Cloud Lake costs $311,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $106,040 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $93,600 (43%) higher, and it is roughly $6,600 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Cloud Lake is $1,201 a month. This is roughly $196 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $139 (13%) higher, and it is about $18 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
