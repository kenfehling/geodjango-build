Home prices

The median home value in Cloud Lake is $311,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $106,040 (25%) lower. It is approximately $93,600 (43%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $6,600 (2%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Cloud Lake the median monthly rent is $1,201. It is about $196 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $139 (13%) higher, and is roughly $18 (1%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
