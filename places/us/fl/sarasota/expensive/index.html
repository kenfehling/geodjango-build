Compared to the surrounding areas, Sarasota is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Sarasota costs $268,400. This is roughly $16,100 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $50,900 (23%) higher, and it is about $29,500 (12%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sarasota is $1,134. This is about $155 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $72 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is is very similar.
