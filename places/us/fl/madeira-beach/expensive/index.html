Compared to nearby areas, Madeira Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

Madeira Beach's median home cost is $431,800. It is about $78,820 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $214,300 (98%) higher, and is roughly $192,900 (80%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Madeira Beach is $1,010 a month. This is approximately $361 (26%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $52 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $121 (10%) lower.
