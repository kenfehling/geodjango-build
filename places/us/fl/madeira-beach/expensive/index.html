Compared to the surrounding areas, Madeira Beach is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home price in Madeira Beach is $431,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $78,820 (22%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $214,300 (98%) higher, and it is roughly $192,900 (80%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Madeira Beach the median monthly rent is $1,010. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $361 (26%) lower. It is about $52 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $121 (10%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
