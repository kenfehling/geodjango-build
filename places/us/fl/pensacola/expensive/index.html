Compared to its nearby areas, Pensacola is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Mobile however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Pensacola is $182,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $32,400 (21%) higher.It is roughly $34,700 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is approximately $59,200 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Pensacola the median monthly rent is $954. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $69 (6%) lower.It is about $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $97 (11%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
