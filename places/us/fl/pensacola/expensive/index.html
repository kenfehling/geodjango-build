In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pensacola is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Mobile however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Pensacola is $182,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $32,400 (21%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $34,700 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Mobile is approximately $59,200 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Pensacola the median monthly rent is $954. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $69 (6%) lower. It is about $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $97 (11%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
