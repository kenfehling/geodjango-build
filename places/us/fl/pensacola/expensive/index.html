Compared to its surrounding areas, Pensacola is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Mobile.

Home prices

Pensacola's median home price is $182,800. This is roughly $32,400 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $34,700 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Mobile is approximately $59,200 (47%) higher.

Rents

Pensacola's median monthly rent is $954. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $69 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $108 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $97 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
