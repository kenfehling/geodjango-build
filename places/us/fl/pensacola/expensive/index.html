In comparison to the surrounding areas, Pensacola is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Mobile however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Pensacola's median home cost is $182,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $32,400 (21%) higher. It is about $34,700 (15%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $59,200 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.

Rents

The median rent in Pensacola is $954 per month. It is approximately $69 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is roughly $97 (11%) higher.
