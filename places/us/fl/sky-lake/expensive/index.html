Home prices

The median home in Sky Lake costs $154,700. It is about $47,040 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $62,800 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $84,200 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Sky Lake the median rent is $1,044 per month. It is roughly $67 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $18 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $87 (7%) lower.
