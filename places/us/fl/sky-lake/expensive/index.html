Home prices

Rents

In Sky Lake the median monthly rent is $1,044. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $67 (6%) higher.It is roughly $18 (1%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $87 (7%) lower.
