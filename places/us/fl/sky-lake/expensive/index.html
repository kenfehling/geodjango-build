Home prices

Rents

In Sky Lake the median monthly rent is $1,044. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $67 (6%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $18 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $87 (7%) lower.
