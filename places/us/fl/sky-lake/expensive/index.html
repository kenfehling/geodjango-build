Home prices

The median home in Sky Lake costs $154,700. It is approximately $47,040 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $62,800 (28%) lower, and is roughly $84,200 (35%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sky Lake is $1,044. It is approximately $67 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $18 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $87 (7%) lower.
