The median home in Celebration costs $494,100. It is about $311,100 (170%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $276,600 (127%) higher, and it is about $255,200 (106%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Celebration is $1,754. This is about $425 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $692 (65%) higher, and it is approximately $623 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
