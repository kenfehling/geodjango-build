In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Walton Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Pensacola.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fort Walton Beach is $185,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $29,700 (13%) lower.It is approximately $31,600 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola is roughly $3,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

Fort Walton Beach's median monthly rent is $988. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $160 (13%) lower.It is roughly $74 (6%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $34 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Pensacola.
