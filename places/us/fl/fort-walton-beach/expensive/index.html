Home prices

The median home in Fort Walton Beach costs $185,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $29,700 (13%) lower. It is approximately $31,600 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pensacola is about $3,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Walton Beach is $988 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $160 (13%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $74 (6%) lower, and is roughly $34 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Pensacola.
