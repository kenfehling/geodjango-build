In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Walton Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Pensacola.

Home prices

Fort Walton Beach's median home cost is $185,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $29,700 (13%) lower.It is about $31,600 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola is roughly $3,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Walton Beach is $988 per month. This is roughly $160 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $74 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $34 (3%) higher compared to the city of Pensacola.
