Compared to its surrounding areas, Bartow is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Bartow is valued at $137,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $7,580 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $80,400 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $101,800 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Bartow the median monthly rent is $841. It is approximately $193 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $221 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $290 (25%) lower.
