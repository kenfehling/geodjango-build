Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bartow is $137,100. It is about $7,580 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $80,400 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $101,800 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Bartow the median monthly rent is $841. This is approximately $193 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $221 (20%) lower, and is approximately $290 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
