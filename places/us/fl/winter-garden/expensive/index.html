Compared to other places in this area, Winter Garden is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Winter Garden costs $313,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $26,260 (7%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $96,100 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $74,700 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Winter Garden the median rent is $1,106 per month. It is roughly $622 (35%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
