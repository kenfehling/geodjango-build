In comparison to the surrounding areas, Winter Garden is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Winter Garden is valued at $313,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $26,260 (7%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $96,100 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $74,700 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Winter Garden the median monthly rent is $1,106. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $622 (35%) lower. It is about $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $25 (2%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
