In comparison to its surrounding areas, Winter Garden is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Winter Garden is $313,600. It is roughly $26,260 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $96,100 (44%) higher, and is roughly $74,700 (31%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Winter Garden's median rent is $1,106 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $622 (35%) lower. It is approximately $44 (4%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
