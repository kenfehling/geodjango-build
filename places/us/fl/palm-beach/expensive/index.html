Home prices

Palm Beach's median home price is $1,177,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $932,160 (380%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $959,600 (441%) higher, and is approximately $859,400 (270%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Palm Beach the median monthly rent is $1,744. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $497 (39%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $682 (64%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $561 (47%) higher.
