Home prices

The median home in Palm Beach costs $1,177,100. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $932,160 (380%) higher. It is approximately $959,600 (441%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $859,400 (270%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Palm Beach's median monthly rent is $1,744. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $497 (39%) higher. It is approximately $682 (64%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $561 (47%) higher.
