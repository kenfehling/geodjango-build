Home prices

The median home cost in Navarre is $235,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $32,160 (12%) lower.It is about $18,300 (8%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Mobile is approximately $112,200 (90%) higher.

Rents

Navarre's median monthly rent is $1,360. It is about $179 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $298 (28%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $503 (58%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
