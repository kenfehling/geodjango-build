Compared to other places in this area, Navarre is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Mobile however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Navarre is $235,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $32,160 (12%) lower. It is approximately $18,300 (8%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is about $112,200 (90%) higher.

Rents

Navarre's median rent is $1,360 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $179 (11%) lower. It is approximately $298 (28%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is roughly $503 (58%) higher.
