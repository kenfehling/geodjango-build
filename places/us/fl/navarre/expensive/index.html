Home prices

The median home in Navarre is valued at $235,800. This is about $32,160 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $18,300 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is about $112,200 (90%) higher.

Rents

In Navarre the median monthly rent is $1,360. It is roughly $179 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $298 (28%) higher, and it is about $503 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
