Home prices

The median home in Navarre is valued at $235,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $32,160 (12%) lower.It is approximately $18,300 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $112,200 (90%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

In Navarre the median rent is $1,360 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $179 (11%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $298 (28%) higher, and compared to the city of Mobile it is roughly $503 (58%) higher.
