Home prices

Wellington's median home cost is $382,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $150,340 (64%) higher. It is about $164,500 (75%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $64,300 (20%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Wellington is $1,867 per month. This is roughly $413 (28%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $805 (75%) higher, and it is roughly $684 (57%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
