Compared to the surrounding areas, Wellington is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Wellington is valued at $382,000. It is approximately $150,340 (64%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $164,500 (75%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is about $64,300 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wellington is $1,867. Compared to other places in this area this is about $413 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $805 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $684 (57%) higher.
