Compared to its surrounding areas, Wellington is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Wellington is $382,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $150,340 (64%) higher.It is about $164,500 (75%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $64,300 (20%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Wellington the median monthly rent is $1,867. This is approximately $413 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $805 (75%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $684 (57%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
