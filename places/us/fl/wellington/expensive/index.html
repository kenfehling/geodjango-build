Home prices

Wellington's median home price is $382,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $150,340 (64%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $164,500 (75%) higher, and it is approximately $64,300 (20%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Wellington the median monthly rent is $1,867. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $413 (28%) higher.It is roughly $805 (75%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $684 (57%) higher.
