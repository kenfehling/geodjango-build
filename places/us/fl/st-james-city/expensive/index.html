Home prices

The price of the median home in St. James City is $230,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $142,200 (38%) lower. It is approximately $13,300 (6%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents
