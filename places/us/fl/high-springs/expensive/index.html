Compared to the surrounding areas, High Springs is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The median home in High Springs is valued at $167,800. It is roughly $9,040 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $49,700 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $5,400 (3%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

High Springs's median rent is $745 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $181 (19%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $317 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $320 (30%) lower.
