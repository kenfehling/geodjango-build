Compared to the surrounding areas, High Springs is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Jacksonville however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in High Springs is $167,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $9,040 (5%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $49,700 (22%) lower, and is about $5,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median rent in High Springs is $745 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $181 (19%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $317 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $320 (30%) lower.
