Compared to nearby areas, High Springs is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Jacksonville however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in High Springs costs $167,800. This is about $9,040 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $49,700 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $5,400 (3%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

High Springs's median monthly rent is $745. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $181 (19%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $317 (29%) lower, and is about $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
