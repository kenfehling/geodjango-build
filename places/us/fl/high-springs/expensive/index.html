Home prices

The median home in High Springs is valued at $167,800. This is approximately $9,040 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $49,700 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $5,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

In High Springs the median monthly rent is $745. This is roughly $181 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $317 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
