Home prices

The cost of the median home in Laguna Beach is $237,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $9,960 (4%) higher. It is roughly $19,500 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dothan is approximately $86,300 (57%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Beach is $996. This is about $20 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $66 (6%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dothan it is approximately $245 (32%) higher.
