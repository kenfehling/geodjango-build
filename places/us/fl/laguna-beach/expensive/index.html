Home prices

The median home price in Laguna Beach is $237,000. This is about $9,960 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $19,500 (8%) higher, and it is roughly $86,300 (57%) higher compared to the city of Dothan.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Beach is $996. It is roughly $20 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $66 (6%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dothan is roughly $245 (32%) higher.
