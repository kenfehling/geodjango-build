Home prices

The median home in Laguna Beach is valued at $237,000. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $9,960 (4%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $19,500 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dothan it is approximately $86,300 (57%) higher.

Rents

In Laguna Beach the median monthly rent is $996. This is about $20 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $66 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Dothan is approximately $245 (32%) higher.
