Similar to its surrounding areas, it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Dothan, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Laguna Beach costs $237,000. This is about $9,960 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $19,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dothan is roughly $86,300 (57%) higher.

Rents

Laguna Beach's median rent is $996 a month. This is about $20 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $66 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $245 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Dothan.
