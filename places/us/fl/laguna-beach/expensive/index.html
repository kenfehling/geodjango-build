Home prices

The median home cost in Laguna Beach is $237,000. This is approximately $9,960 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $19,500 (8%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $86,300 (57%) higher compared to the city of Dothan.

Rents

Laguna Beach's median rent is $996 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $20 (1%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $66 (6%) lower, and is approximately $245 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Dothan.
