Compared to its surrounding areas, Surfside is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Surfside costs $625,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $80,960 (14%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $408,000 (187%) higher, and is approximately $307,800 (96%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Surfside's median monthly rent is $1,977. This is approximately $361 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $915 (86%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $794 (67%) higher.
