Compared to nearby areas, Surfside is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Surfside is valued at $625,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $80,960 (14%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $408,000 (187%) higher, and is about $307,800 (96%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Surfside is $1,977. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $361 (22%) higher. It is about $915 (86%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $794 (67%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
