In comparison to other places in this area, Dunnellon is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Dunnellon is valued at $144,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $14,520 (11%) higher. It is about $73,500 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $94,900 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Dunnellon's median rent is $663 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $111 (14%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $399 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $468 (41%) lower.
