Home prices

The median home cost in Dunnellon is $144,000. This is about $14,520 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $73,500 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $94,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

Dunnellon's median monthly rent is $663. It is roughly $111 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $468 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
