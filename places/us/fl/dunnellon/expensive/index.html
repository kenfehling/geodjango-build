Home prices

The median home value in Dunnellon is $144,000. This is roughly $14,520 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $73,500 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $94,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dunnellon is $663. It is roughly $111 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $468 (41%) lower.
