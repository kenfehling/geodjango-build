Home prices

The median home cost in Dunnellon is $144,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $14,520 (11%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $73,500 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $94,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dunnellon is $663. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $111 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $399 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $468 (41%) lower.
