The median home in Yulee is valued at $186,400. This is roughly $11,540 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $31,100 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $13,200 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Yulee the median rent is $1,178 a month. This is roughly $249 (26%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $116 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $113 (10%) higher.
