Home prices

The value of the median home in Yulee is $186,400. It is roughly $11,540 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $31,100 (14%) lower, and is about $13,200 (7%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median rent in Yulee is $1,178 a month. This is about $249 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $116 (10%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $113 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
