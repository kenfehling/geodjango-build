Home prices

The median home in Sunset is valued at $388,800. It is approximately $49,840 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $171,300 (78%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $71,100 (22%) higher.

Rents

Sunset's median monthly rent is $1,443. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $100 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $381 (35%) higher, and is approximately $260 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
