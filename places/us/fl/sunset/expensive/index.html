Home prices

The median home in Sunset costs $388,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $49,840 (14%) higher. It is approximately $171,300 (78%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $71,100 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Sunset's median rent is $1,443 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $100 (6%) lower. It is approximately $381 (35%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $260 (21%) higher.
