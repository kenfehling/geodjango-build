Home prices

The median home in Daytona Beach costs $153,000. It is roughly $34,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $64,500 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $20,200 (11%) lower.

Rents

Daytona Beach's median monthly rent is $934. It is about $111 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $128 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is about $131 (12%) lower.
