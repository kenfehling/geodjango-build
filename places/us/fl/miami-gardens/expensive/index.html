Home prices

The median home cost in Miami Gardens is $190,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $6,000 (3%) lower. It is approximately $27,300 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $127,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

Miami Gardens's median monthly rent is $1,278. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $216 (20%) higher, and is approximately $95 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
