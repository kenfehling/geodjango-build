Home prices

The value of the median home in Miami Gardens is $190,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $6,000 (3%) lower. It is approximately $27,300 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $127,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miami Gardens is $1,278. Compared to its surrounding areas it is is very similar. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $216 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $95 (8%) higher.
