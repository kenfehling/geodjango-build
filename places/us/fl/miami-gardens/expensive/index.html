Home prices

The median home in Miami Gardens costs $190,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $6,000 (3%) lower.It is about $27,300 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $127,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Miami Gardens is $1,278 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is roughly $216 (20%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $95 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
