Home prices

The median home cost in Miami Gardens is $190,200. It is about $6,000 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $27,300 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $127,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Miami Gardens the median monthly rent is $1,278. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $216 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $95 (8%) higher.
