Compared to the surrounding areas, Westview is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Westview costs $208,400. This is roughly $16,500 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $9,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $109,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westview is $1,043. This is approximately $25 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $19 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $140 (11%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
