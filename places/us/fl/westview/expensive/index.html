Home prices

The value of the median home in Westview is $208,400. This is about $16,500 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $9,100 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $109,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

Westview's median monthly rent is $1,043. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $25 (2%) lower. It is approximately $19 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $140 (11%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
