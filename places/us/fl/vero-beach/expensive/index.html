Home prices

The median home in Vero Beach costs $259,800. This is approximately $5,080 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $42,300 (19%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $19,800 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Vero Beach's median monthly rent is $890. It is about $107 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $172 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is about $306 (25%) lower.
