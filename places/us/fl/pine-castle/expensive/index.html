Compared to other places in this area, Pine Castle is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Pine Castle is $161,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $67,760 (29%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $56,300 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $77,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Pine Castle the median rent is $1,006 per month. It is roughly $36 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $56 (5%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $125 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
