Compared to nearby areas, South Bradenton is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Bradenton costs $83,900. This is about $57,820 (40%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $133,600 (61%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $155,000 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in South Bradenton is $957 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $146 (13%) lower. It is approximately $105 (9%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $174 (15%) lower.
