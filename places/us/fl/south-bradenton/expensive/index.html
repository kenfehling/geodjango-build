Home prices

The median home price in South Bradenton is $83,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $57,820 (40%) lower. It is about $133,600 (61%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $155,000 (64%) lower.

Rents

South Bradenton's median monthly rent is $957. It is approximately $146 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $105 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $174 (15%) lower.
