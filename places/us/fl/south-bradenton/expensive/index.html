Compared to the surrounding areas, South Bradenton is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Bradenton is valued at $83,900. This is about $57,820 (40%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $133,600 (61%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $155,000 (64%) lower.

Rents

In South Bradenton the median rent is $957 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $146 (13%) lower.It is about $105 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $174 (15%) lower.
