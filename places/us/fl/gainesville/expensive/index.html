Home prices

The price of the median home in Gainesville is $161,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $24,820 (18%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $55,600 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $11,300 (6%) lower.

Rents

Gainesville's median rent is $953 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $49 (5%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $109 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is about $112 (10%) lower.
