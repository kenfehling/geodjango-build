Compared to the surrounding areas, Gainesville is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

Gainesville's median home price is $161,900. It is about $24,820 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $55,600 (25%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $11,300 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gainesville is $953. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $49 (5%) higher. It is about $109 (10%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $112 (10%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
