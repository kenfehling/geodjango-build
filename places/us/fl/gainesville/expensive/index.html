Home prices

The median home in Gainesville costs $161,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $24,820 (18%) higher. It is approximately $55,600 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $11,300 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gainesville is $953. This is approximately $49 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $109 (10%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $112 (10%) lower.
