Compared to its nearby areas, Gainesville is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Gainesville is $161,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,820 (18%) higher.It is approximately $55,600 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $11,300 (6%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Gainesville's median rent is $953 per month. It is approximately $49 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $109 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $112 (10%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
