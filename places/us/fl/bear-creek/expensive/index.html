Home prices

The median home cost in Bear Creek is $339,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $57,400 (20%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $122,100 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $100,700 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bear Creek is $1,290 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $164 (14%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $228 (21%) higher, and is roughly $159 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
