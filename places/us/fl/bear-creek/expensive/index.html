Compared to other places in this area, Bear Creek is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bear Creek is $339,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $57,400 (20%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $122,100 (56%) higher, and is roughly $100,700 (42%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Bear Creek's median rent is $1,290 per month. It is about $164 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $228 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $159 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
