In comparison to other places in this area, Bear Creek is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bear Creek is valued at $339,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $57,400 (20%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $122,100 (56%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $100,700 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bear Creek is $1,290 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $164 (14%) higher. It is approximately $228 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $159 (14%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
