Home prices

The median home in St. George Island is valued at $483,300. It is about $319,280 (194%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $265,800 (122%) higher, and compared to the city of Tallahassee is approximately $279,500 (137%) higher.

Rents

In St. George Island the median monthly rent is $1,340. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $413 (44%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $278 (26%) higher, and compared to the city of Tallahassee it is roughly $317 (30%) higher.
