Compared to other places in this area, Fleming Island is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fleming Island is $269,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $43,560 (19%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $52,100 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $96,400 (55%) higher.

Rents

In Fleming Island the median rent is $1,488 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $112 (8%) higher. It is about $426 (40%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $423 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
