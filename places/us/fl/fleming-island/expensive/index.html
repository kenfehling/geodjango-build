Compared to other places in this area, Fleming Island is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fleming Island costs $269,600. This is about $43,560 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $52,100 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $96,400 (55%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fleming Island is $1,488 per month. It is approximately $112 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $426 (40%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $423 (39%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.
