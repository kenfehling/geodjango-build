Home prices

The value of the median home in Kendall is $382,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $45,740 (10%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $164,900 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $64,700 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kendall is $1,543 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $63 (3%) lower. It is approximately $481 (45%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $360 (30%) higher.
