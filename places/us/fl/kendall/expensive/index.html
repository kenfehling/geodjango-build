Home prices

The median home in Kendall is valued at $382,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $45,740 (10%) lower.It is roughly $164,900 (75%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $64,700 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kendall is $1,543 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $63 (3%) lower.It is approximately $481 (45%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $360 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
