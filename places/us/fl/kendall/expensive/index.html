Compared to its surrounding areas, Kendall is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Miami, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kendall costs $382,400. It is about $45,740 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $164,900 (75%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $64,700 (20%) higher.

Rents

Kendall's median monthly rent is $1,543. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $63 (3%) lower. It is approximately $481 (45%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $360 (30%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
