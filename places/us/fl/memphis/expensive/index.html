Home prices

The median home in Memphis costs $157,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $3,240 (2%) lower.It is roughly $60,500 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $81,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Memphis the median rent is $1,032 per month. This is about $51 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $99 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
