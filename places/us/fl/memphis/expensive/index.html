Much like the surrounding areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Memphis costs $157,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $3,240 (2%) lower.It is approximately $60,500 (27%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $81,900 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Memphis is $1,032. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $51 (4%) lower.It is roughly $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $99 (8%) lower.
