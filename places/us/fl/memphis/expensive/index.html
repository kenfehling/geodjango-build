Similar to its nearby areas, it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Memphis's median home price is $157,000. This is approximately $3,240 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $60,500 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $81,900 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Memphis is $1,032. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $51 (4%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $30 (2%) lower, and is roughly $99 (8%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
