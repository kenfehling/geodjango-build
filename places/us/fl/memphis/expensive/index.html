Home prices

The cost of the median home in Memphis is $157,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $3,240 (2%) lower. It is approximately $60,500 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $81,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Memphis the median monthly rent is $1,032. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $51 (4%) lower. It is about $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $99 (8%) lower.
