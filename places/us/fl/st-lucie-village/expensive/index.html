In comparison to nearby areas, St. Lucie Village is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of West Palm Beach, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Lucie Village is $350,000. This is approximately $227,860 (186%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $132,500 (60%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of West Palm Beach is about $98,300 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in St. Lucie Village is $865 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $93 (9%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $197 (18%) lower, and is roughly $438 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach.
