Home prices

The median home in Indialantic costs $374,500. It is roughly $112,900 (43%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $157,000 (72%) higher, and is roughly $134,500 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Indialantic's median rent is $1,220 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $61 (4%) lower. It is approximately $158 (14%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Orlando it is roughly $24 (2%) higher.
