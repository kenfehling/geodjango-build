In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lakeland is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeland is valued at $149,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $31,420 (26%) higher.It is about $68,000 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $89,400 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Lakeland's median monthly rent is $999. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $17 (1%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $63 (5%) lower, and it is roughly $132 (11%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
