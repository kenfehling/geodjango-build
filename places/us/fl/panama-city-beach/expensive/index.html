Compared to other places in this area, Panama City Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Dothan however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Panama City Beach's median home cost is $243,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $12,680 (5%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $25,800 (11%) higher, and it is about $92,600 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Dothan.

Rents

In Panama City Beach the median rent is $1,268 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $220 (20%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $206 (19%) higher, and it is roughly $517 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Dothan.
