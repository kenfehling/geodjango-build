In comparison to its nearby areas, Panama City Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Dothan.

Home prices

Panama City Beach's median home value is $243,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $12,680 (5%) higher. It is roughly $25,800 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dothan is roughly $92,600 (61%) higher.

Rents

In Panama City Beach the median rent is $1,268 a month. This is approximately $220 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $206 (19%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $517 (68%) higher compared to the city of Dothan.
