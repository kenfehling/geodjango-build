In comparison to its surrounding areas, Panama City Beach is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Dothan.

Home prices

The median home cost in Panama City Beach is $243,300. This is roughly $12,680 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $25,800 (11%) higher, and is approximately $92,600 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Dothan.

Rents

The median rent in Panama City Beach is $1,268 per month. This is about $220 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $206 (19%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dothan it is roughly $517 (68%) higher.
