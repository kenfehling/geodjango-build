Compared to nearby areas, Panama City Beach is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Dothan, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Panama City Beach is valued at $243,300. This is approximately $12,680 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $25,800 (11%) higher, and it is about $92,600 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Dothan.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Panama City Beach is $1,268. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $220 (20%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $206 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Dothan is approximately $517 (68%) higher.
