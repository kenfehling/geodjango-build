Home prices

The median home in Cutler Bay is valued at $287,000. It is approximately $11,060 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $69,500 (31%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $30,700 (9%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Cutler Bay's median rent is $1,556 a month. This is roughly $338 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $494 (46%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $373 (31%) higher.
