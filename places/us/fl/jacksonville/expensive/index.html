In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jacksonville is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville is valued at $173,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $111,400 (39%) lower. It is roughly $44,300 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median monthly rent is $1,065. This is roughly $200 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is is very similar in comparison to the US as a whole.
