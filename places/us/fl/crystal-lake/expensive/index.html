Home prices

The median home cost in Crystal Lake is $82,800. This is approximately $32,360 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $134,700 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $156,100 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Crystal Lake the median monthly rent is $914. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $50 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $148 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $217 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
