Home prices

The price of the median home in Westgate is $142,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $50,820 (26%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $75,000 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami is about $175,200 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Westgate the median rent is $1,127 a month. This is roughly $266 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $65 (6%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $56 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
