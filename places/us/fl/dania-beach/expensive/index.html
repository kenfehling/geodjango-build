In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dania Beach is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Dania Beach's median home value is $194,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $23,640 (13%) higher. It is about $22,800 (10%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $123,000 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Dania Beach the median rent is $1,371 a month. It is approximately $148 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $309 (29%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $188 (15%) higher.
