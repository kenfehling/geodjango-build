Home prices

The price of the median home in Dania Beach is $194,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $23,640 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $22,800 (10%) lower, and is approximately $123,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dania Beach is $1,371. It is roughly $148 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $309 (29%) higher, and is approximately $188 (15%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
