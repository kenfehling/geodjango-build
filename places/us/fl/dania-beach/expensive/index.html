In comparison to nearby areas, Dania Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Dania Beach is $194,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $23,640 (13%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $22,800 (10%) lower, and it is about $123,000 (38%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Dania Beach's median rent is $1,371 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $148 (12%) higher. It is about $309 (29%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $188 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
