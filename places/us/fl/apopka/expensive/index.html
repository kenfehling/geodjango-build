Home prices

The median home value in Apopka is $228,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $37,740 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $10,900 (5%) higher, and is about $10,500 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Apopka the median rent is $1,296 per month. This is roughly $247 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $234 (22%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $165 (14%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
