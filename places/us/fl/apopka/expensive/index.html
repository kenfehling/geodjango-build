Home prices

The median home in Apopka is valued at $228,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $37,740 (19%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $10,900 (5%) higher, and is about $10,500 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Apopka is $1,296. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $247 (23%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $234 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $165 (14%) higher.
