In comparison to its surrounding areas, Apopka is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Apopka is $228,400. This is roughly $37,740 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $10,900 (5%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $10,500 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Apopka the median rent is $1,296 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $247 (23%) higher. It is approximately $234 (22%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $165 (14%) higher.
