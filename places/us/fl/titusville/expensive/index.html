Home prices

The median home in Titusville costs $143,700. This is approximately $8,580 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $73,800 (33%) lower, and it is about $96,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

The median rent in Titusville is $948 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $64 (7%) higher.It is about $114 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Orlando is approximately $248 (20%) lower.
