Compared to the surrounding areas, Titusville is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Orlando, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Titusville costs $143,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $8,580 (5%) lower. It is about $73,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $96,300 (40%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

In Titusville the median monthly rent is $948. This is about $64 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $114 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Orlando is approximately $248 (20%) lower.
