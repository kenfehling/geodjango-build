Home prices

Kissimmee's median home value is $173,200. It is roughly $25,580 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $44,300 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $65,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Kissimmee the median monthly rent is $1,144. It is about $266 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $82 (7%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $13 (1%) higher.
