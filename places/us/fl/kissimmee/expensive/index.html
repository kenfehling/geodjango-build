In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kissimmee is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kissimmee is valued at $173,200. It is approximately $25,580 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $44,300 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $65,700 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kissimmee is $1,144. It is roughly $266 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $82 (7%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $13 (1%) higher.
