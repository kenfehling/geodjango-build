Compared to the surrounding areas, Kissimmee is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kissimmee is $173,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $25,580 (12%) lower. It is approximately $44,300 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $65,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Kissimmee the median rent is $1,144 a month. This is about $266 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $82 (7%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
