Home prices

The median home in Grove City costs $170,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $55,920 (24%) lower. It is about $47,100 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $68,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Grove City the median monthly rent is $1,110. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $168 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $48 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $21 (1%) lower.
