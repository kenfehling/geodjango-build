Home prices

The price of the median home in Grove City is $170,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $55,920 (24%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $47,100 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $68,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Grove City is $1,110 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $168 (13%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $48 (4%) higher, and is about $21 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
