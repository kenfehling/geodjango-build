Home prices

The median home in Williamsburg costs $207,300. This is about $10,360 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $10,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $31,600 (13%) lower.

Rents

Williamsburg's median monthly rent is $1,483. It is about $83 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $421 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $352 (31%) higher.
