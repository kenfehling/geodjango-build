Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Meade is $77,900. This is about $16,000 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $139,600 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $161,000 (67%) lower.

Rents

Fort Meade's median monthly rent is $771. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $34 (4%) lower.It is about $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $360 (31%) lower.
