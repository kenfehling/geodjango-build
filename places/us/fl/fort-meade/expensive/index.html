Home prices

The value of the median home in Fort Meade is $77,900. This is about $16,000 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $139,600 (64%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $161,000 (67%) lower.

Rents

Fort Meade's median rent is $771 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $34 (4%) lower. It is approximately $291 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $360 (31%) lower.
