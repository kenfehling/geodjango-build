In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Meade is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Meade is $77,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,000 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $139,600 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $161,000 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Meade is $771 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $34 (4%) lower. It is approximately $291 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $360 (31%) lower.
