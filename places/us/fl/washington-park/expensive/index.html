Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Washington Park is $1,112. It is approximately $108 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $50 (4%) higher, and is approximately $71 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
