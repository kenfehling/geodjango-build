Compared to its surrounding areas, Washington Park is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Miami, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Washington Park is valued at $178,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $9,440 (5%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $39,300 (18%) lower, and is about $139,500 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Washington Park is $1,112 per month. This is approximately $108 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $50 (4%) higher, and it is approximately $71 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
