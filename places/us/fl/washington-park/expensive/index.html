Home prices

The median home in Washington Park is valued at $178,200. It is about $9,440 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $39,300 (18%) lower, and is roughly $139,500 (43%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Washington Park is $1,112. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $108 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $50 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $71 (6%) lower.
