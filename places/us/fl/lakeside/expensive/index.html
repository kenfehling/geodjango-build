Compared to other places in this area, Lakeside is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeside costs $167,800. It is about $78,600 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $49,700 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $5,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Lakeside the median monthly rent is $1,051. This is approximately $412 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $11 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $14 (1%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
