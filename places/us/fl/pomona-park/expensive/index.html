Home prices

The median home in Pomona Park costs $74,800. It is approximately $23,560 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $142,700 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $98,400 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Pomona Park the median monthly rent is $816. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $99 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $246 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $249 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
