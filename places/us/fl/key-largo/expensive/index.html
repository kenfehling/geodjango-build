In comparison to the surrounding areas, Key Largo is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Miami, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Key Largo is $421,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $181,000 (30%) lower.It is approximately $204,400 (93%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $104,200 (32%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Key Largo the median monthly rent is $1,449. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $81 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $387 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $266 (22%) higher.
