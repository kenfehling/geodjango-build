Compared to other places in this area, Key Largo is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Miami, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Key Largo is valued at $421,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $184,460 (77%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $204,400 (93%) higher, and it is roughly $104,200 (32%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Key Largo's median monthly rent is $1,449. This is about $232 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $387 (36%) higher, and is approximately $266 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
