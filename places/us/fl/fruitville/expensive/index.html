Compared to its surrounding areas, Fruitville is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fruitville is $258,700. This is about $27,860 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $41,200 (18%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $19,800 (8%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Fruitville's median monthly rent is $1,501. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $96 (6%) higher. It is about $439 (41%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $370 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
