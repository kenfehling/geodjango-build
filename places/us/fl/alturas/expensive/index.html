Compared to the surrounding areas, Alturas is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

Alturas's median home price is $111,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $46,360 (29%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $105,900 (48%) lower, and is roughly $127,300 (53%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Alturas is $885 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $52 (6%) higher.It is roughly $177 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $246 (21%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
