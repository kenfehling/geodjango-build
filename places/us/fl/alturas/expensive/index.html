Compared to other places in this area, Alturas is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Alturas costs $111,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $46,360 (29%) lower. It is approximately $105,900 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $127,300 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Alturas is $885 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $52 (6%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $177 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $246 (21%) lower.
