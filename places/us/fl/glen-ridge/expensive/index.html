Home prices

The median home in Glen Ridge is valued at $354,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $54,320 (13%) lower. It is approximately $136,700 (62%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $36,500 (11%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Glen Ridge is $1,167. This is about $237 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $105 (9%) higher, and is about $16 (1%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
