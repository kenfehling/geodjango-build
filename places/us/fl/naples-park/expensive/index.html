In comparison to the surrounding areas, Naples Park is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Cape Coral, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Naples Park is $362,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $115,040 (24%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $145,100 (66%) higher, and it is roughly $133,200 (58%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

Naples Park's median rent is $1,428 a month. It is approximately $140 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $366 (34%) higher, and compared to the city of Cape Coral it is roughly $184 (14%) higher.
