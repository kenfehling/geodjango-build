Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Naples Park is $1,428. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $140 (8%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $366 (34%) higher, and is approximately $184 (14%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.
