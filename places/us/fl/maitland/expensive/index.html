In comparison to other places in this area, Maitland is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Maitland is $372,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $136,160 (57%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $155,000 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $133,600 (55%) higher.

Rents

In Maitland the median monthly rent is $1,409. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $272 (23%) higher. It is about $347 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $278 (24%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
