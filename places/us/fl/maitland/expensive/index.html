In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maitland is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maitland costs $372,500. It is approximately $136,160 (57%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $155,000 (71%) higher, and it is approximately $133,600 (55%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Maitland the median rent is $1,409 a month. This is roughly $272 (23%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $347 (32%) higher, and is about $278 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
