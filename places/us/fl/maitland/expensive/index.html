Compared to the surrounding areas, Maitland is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maitland is valued at $372,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $136,160 (57%) higher.It is roughly $155,000 (71%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $133,600 (55%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Maitland is $1,409 a month. It is roughly $272 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $347 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $278 (24%) higher.
