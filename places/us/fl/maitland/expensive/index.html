Compared to its nearby areas, Maitland is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Maitland is $372,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $136,160 (57%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $155,000 (71%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $133,600 (55%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Maitland is $1,409 per month. It is roughly $272 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $347 (32%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $278 (24%) higher.
