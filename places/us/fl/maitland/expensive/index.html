Home prices

The median home in Maitland costs $372,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $136,160 (57%) higher.It is approximately $155,000 (71%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $133,600 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Maitland's median monthly rent is $1,409. It is about $272 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $347 (32%) higher, and it is approximately $278 (24%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
