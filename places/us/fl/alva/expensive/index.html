Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Alva is $912 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $53 (5%) lower.It is roughly $150 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cape Coral it is about $332 (26%) lower.
