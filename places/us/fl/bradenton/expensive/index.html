Home prices

Bradenton's median home cost is $169,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $33,200 (24%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $47,700 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $69,100 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Bradenton the median monthly rent is $1,117. This is about $54 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $55 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $14 (1%) lower.
