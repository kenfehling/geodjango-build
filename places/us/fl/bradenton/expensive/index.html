Home prices

Bradenton's median home value is $169,800. This is about $33,200 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $47,700 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $69,100 (28%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Bradenton is $1,117 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $54 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $55 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $14 (1%) lower.
