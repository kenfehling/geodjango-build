Home prices

Bradenton's median home price is $169,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $33,200 (24%) higher.It is roughly $47,700 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $69,100 (28%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Bradenton's median monthly rent is $1,117. It is approximately $54 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $14 (1%) lower.
