Compared to the surrounding areas, Bradenton is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bradenton costs $169,800. It is approximately $33,200 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $47,700 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $69,100 (28%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Bradenton's median rent is $1,117 per month. This is roughly $54 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $14 (1%) lower.
