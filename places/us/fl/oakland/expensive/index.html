Home prices

The median home price in Oakland is $327,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $10,060 (2%) lower. It is roughly $109,600 (50%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $88,200 (36%) higher.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $1,798 a month. This is approximately $209 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $736 (69%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $667 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
