Home prices

The median home in Niceville costs $238,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,560 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $21,300 (9%) higher, and is about $88,100 (58%) higher compared to the city of Dothan.

Rents

The median rent in Niceville is $1,117 per month. It is roughly $130 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $366 (48%) higher compared to the city of Dothan.
