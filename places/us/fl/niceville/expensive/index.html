Compared to the surrounding areas, Niceville is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Dothan however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Niceville is valued at $238,800. This is roughly $24,560 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $21,300 (9%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $88,100 (58%) higher compared to the city of Dothan.

Rents

In Niceville the median monthly rent is $1,117. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $130 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $55 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Dothan is roughly $366 (48%) higher.
