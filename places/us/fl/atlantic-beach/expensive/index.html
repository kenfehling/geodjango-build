In comparison to other places in this area, Atlantic Beach is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Atlantic Beach is $362,300. This is approximately $70,780 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $144,800 (66%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $189,100 (109%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Atlantic Beach's median monthly rent is $1,335. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $208 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $273 (25%) higher, and it is roughly $270 (25%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.
