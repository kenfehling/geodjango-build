In comparison to its nearby areas, Atlantic Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The median home in Atlantic Beach costs $362,300. This is about $70,780 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $144,800 (66%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $189,100 (109%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Atlantic Beach is $1,335 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $208 (13%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $273 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $270 (25%) higher.
