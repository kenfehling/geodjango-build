In comparison to its nearby areas, Westchester is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Westchester is $332,600. This is about $28,220 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $115,100 (52%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $14,900 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Westchester is $1,383 per month. This is roughly $145 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $321 (30%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $200 (16%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
