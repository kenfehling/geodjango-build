In comparison to its surrounding areas, Westchester is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Westchester is $332,600. It is about $28,220 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $115,100 (52%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $14,900 (4%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Westchester the median rent is $1,383 a month. This is approximately $145 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $321 (30%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $200 (16%) higher.
