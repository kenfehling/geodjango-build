Home prices

The price of the median home in Siesta Key is $715,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $430,240 (151%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $497,500 (228%) higher, and is about $476,100 (199%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Siesta Key is $1,730 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $529 (44%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $668 (62%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $599 (52%) higher.
