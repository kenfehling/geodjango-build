Compared to nearby areas, North Palm Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home cost in North Palm Beach is $328,100. It is about $58,740 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $110,600 (50%) higher, and is about $10,400 (3%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In North Palm Beach the median rent is $1,526 a month. It is about $176 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $464 (43%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $343 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
