Compared to its nearby areas, North Palm Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in North Palm Beach is valued at $328,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $58,740 (21%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $110,600 (50%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $10,400 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Palm Beach is $1,526 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $176 (13%) higher.It is approximately $464 (43%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $343 (28%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
