In comparison to its nearby areas, North Palm Beach is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in North Palm Beach costs $328,100. It is roughly $58,740 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $110,600 (50%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is about $10,400 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Palm Beach is $1,526. It is roughly $176 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $464 (43%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $343 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
