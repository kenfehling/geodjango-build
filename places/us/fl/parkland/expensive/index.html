In comparison to other places in this area, Parkland is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Miami, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Parkland is valued at $611,800. It is about $414,520 (210%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $394,300 (181%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $294,100 (92%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Parkland's median monthly rent is $2,439. It is roughly $980 (67%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,377 (129%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $1,256 (106%) higher.
