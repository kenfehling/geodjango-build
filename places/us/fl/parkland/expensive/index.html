Compared to other places in this area, Parkland is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Parkland is $611,800. It is approximately $414,520 (210%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $394,300 (181%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $294,100 (92%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Parkland is $2,439 a month. This is roughly $980 (67%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $1,377 (129%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $1,256 (106%) higher.
