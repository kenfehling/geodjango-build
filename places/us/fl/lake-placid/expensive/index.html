In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lake Placid is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

Lake Placid's median home cost is $84,100. It is approximately $22,960 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $133,400 (61%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $154,800 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Placid is $706 per month. This is approximately $38 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $356 (33%) lower, and it is about $425 (37%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
