Home prices

Rents

West Pensacola's median monthly rent is $873. It is roughly $64 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $189 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $16 (1%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
