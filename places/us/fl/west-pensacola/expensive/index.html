Compared to the surrounding areas, West Pensacola is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Mobile, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in West Pensacola is $65,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $31,060 (32%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $151,600 (69%) lower, and it is about $57,700 (46%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Pensacola is $873. This is roughly $64 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $189 (17%) lower, and is roughly $16 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
