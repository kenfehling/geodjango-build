Compared to its surrounding areas, Umatilla is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home cost in Umatilla is $126,500. This is roughly $45,180 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $91,000 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $112,400 (47%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Umatilla is $978. It is roughly $21 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $84 (7%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $153 (13%) lower.
