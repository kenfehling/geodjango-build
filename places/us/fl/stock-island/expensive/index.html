Compared to the surrounding areas, Stock Island is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Key West.

Home prices

Stock Island's median home cost is $309,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $136,220 (30%) lower.It is about $92,200 (42%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $301,700 (49%) lower compared to the city of Key West.

Rents

In Stock Island the median monthly rent is $1,323. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $289 (17%) lower.It is roughly $261 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $493 (27%) lower compared to the city of Key West.
