Compared to its surrounding areas, Stock Island is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Key West however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Stock Island is $309,700. This is approximately $136,220 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $92,200 (42%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $301,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Key West.

Rents

Stock Island's median monthly rent is $1,323. Compared to other places in this area this is about $289 (17%) lower. It is approximately $261 (24%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Key West it is roughly $493 (27%) lower.
