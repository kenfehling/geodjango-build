Home prices

Rents

In Stock Island the median rent is $1,323 per month. It is roughly $289 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $261 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Key West it is approximately $493 (27%) lower.
