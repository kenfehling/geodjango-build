Compared to nearby areas, Key West is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Key West is $611,400. This is approximately $114,700 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $393,900 (181%) higher compared to the nation as a whole.

Rents

The median rent in Key West is $1,816 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $186 (11%) higher. It is roughly $754 (70%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation.
