Compared to other places in this area, Key West is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home in Key West costs $611,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $114,700 (23%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $393,900 (181%) higher.

Rents

In Key West the median monthly rent is $1,816. It is roughly $186 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $754 (70%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole.
