Home prices

The median home in Pembroke Pines costs $288,700. This is about $22,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $71,200 (32%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $29,000 (9%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Pembroke Pines is $1,515 a month. This is roughly $201 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $453 (42%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $332 (28%) higher.
