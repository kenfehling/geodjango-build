Home prices

The median home in Pembroke Pines costs $288,700. This is about $22,600 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $71,200 (32%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $29,000 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Pembroke Pines the median rent is $1,515 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $201 (11%) lower. It is about $453 (42%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $332 (28%) higher.
