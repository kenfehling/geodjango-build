Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mount Dora is $244,100. This is about $77,080 (46%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $26,600 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $5,200 (2%) higher.

Rents

Mount Dora's median monthly rent is $1,060. Compared to other places in this area it is about $74 (7%) higher.It is is very similar compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $71 (6%) lower.
