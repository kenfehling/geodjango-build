Compared to other places in this area, Mount Dora is significantly more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Dora costs $244,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $77,080 (46%) higher.It is roughly $26,600 (12%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $5,200 (2%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Mount Dora's median rent is $1,060 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $74 (7%) higher.It is is very similar in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $71 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
