Home prices

Rents

In East Palatka the median rent is $823 a month. This is roughly $41 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $239 (22%) lower, and is about $242 (22%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
