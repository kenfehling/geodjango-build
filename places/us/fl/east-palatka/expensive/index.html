Home prices

The value of the median home in East Palatka is $119,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $27,960 (30%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $98,400 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is about $54,100 (31%) lower.

Rents

In East Palatka the median monthly rent is $823. Compared to nearby areas it is about $41 (5%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $239 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $242 (22%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
