Compared to its surrounding areas, East Palatka is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in East Palatka is $119,100. It is about $27,960 (30%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $98,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $54,100 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in East Palatka is $823 per month. It is about $41 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $239 (22%) lower, and is about $242 (22%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
