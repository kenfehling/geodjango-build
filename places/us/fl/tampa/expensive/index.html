Compared to its surrounding areas, Tampa is significantly more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Tampa costs $238,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $60,700 (34%) higher.It is approximately $21,400 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tampa is $1,131. This is roughly $22 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $69 (6%) higher.
