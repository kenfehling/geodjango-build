In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tampa is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Tampa costs $238,900. This is roughly $60,700 (34%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $21,400 (9%) higher.

Rents

Tampa's median monthly rent is $1,131. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $22 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $69 (6%) higher.
