In comparison to its nearby areas, Cleveland is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

Cleveland's median home cost is $73,200. This is about $120,940 (62%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $144,300 (66%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $165,700 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $872 a month. This is about $90 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $190 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $259 (22%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
