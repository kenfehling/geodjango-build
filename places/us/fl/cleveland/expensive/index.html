In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Tampa, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $73,200. This is approximately $120,940 (62%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $144,300 (66%) lower, and is approximately $165,700 (69%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Cleveland's median monthly rent is $872. This is approximately $90 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $190 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $259 (22%) lower.
