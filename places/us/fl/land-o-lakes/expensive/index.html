Compared to other places in this area, Land O' Lakes is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Land O' Lakes is valued at $241,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $52,780 (17%) lower. It is approximately $23,600 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is is very similar.

Rents

Land O' Lakes's median rent is $1,374 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $42 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $312 (29%) higher, and is approximately $243 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
