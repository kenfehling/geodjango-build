Home prices

The median home in Land O' Lakes is valued at $241,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $52,780 (17%) lower.It is about $23,600 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is is very similar.

Rents

Land O' Lakes's median monthly rent is $1,374. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $42 (3%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $312 (29%) higher, and is roughly $243 (21%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
