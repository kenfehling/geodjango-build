Home prices

The median home price in Okeechobee is $113,700. This is about $2,660 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $103,800 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of West Palm Beach it is roughly $138,000 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Okeechobee the median rent is $775 per month. This is about $209 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $528 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach.
