Home prices

The median home value in Okeechobee is $113,700. This is roughly $2,660 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $103,800 (47%) lower, and it is about $138,000 (54%) lower compared to the city of West Palm Beach.

Rents

In Okeechobee the median rent is $775 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $209 (21%) lower.It is about $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach it is roughly $528 (40%) lower.
