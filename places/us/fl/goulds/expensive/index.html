Home prices

The median home value in Goulds is $260,600. It is approximately $30,300 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $43,100 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $57,100 (17%) lower.

Rents

Goulds's median monthly rent is $895. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $393 (30%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $167 (15%) lower, and is approximately $288 (24%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
