Compared to its nearby areas, Biscayne Park is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Miami, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Biscayne Park is $457,300. It is approximately $142,940 (45%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $239,800 (110%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $139,600 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Biscayne Park is $1,389 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $186 (15%) higher. It is approximately $327 (30%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $206 (17%) higher.
