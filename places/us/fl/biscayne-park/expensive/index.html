Compared to other places in this area, Biscayne Park is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Miami, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Biscayne Park is $457,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $142,940 (45%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $239,800 (110%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $139,600 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Biscayne Park is $1,389. It is roughly $186 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $327 (30%) higher, and it is approximately $206 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
