Home prices

The value of the median home in Biscayne Park is $457,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $142,940 (45%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $239,800 (110%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $139,600 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Biscayne Park the median rent is $1,389 per month. It is roughly $186 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $327 (30%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $206 (17%) higher.
