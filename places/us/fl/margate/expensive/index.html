In comparison to the surrounding areas, Margate is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Margate is $183,800. This is about $35,460 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $33,700 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $133,900 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Margate the median monthly rent is $1,311. It is roughly $156 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) higher, and is about $128 (10%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
