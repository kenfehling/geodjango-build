Compared to its nearby areas, Margate is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Margate's median home price is $183,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $35,460 (16%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $33,700 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $133,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Margate is $1,311. This is about $156 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $128 (10%) higher.
