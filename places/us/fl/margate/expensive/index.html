Compared to other places in this area, Margate is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Margate's median home price is $183,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $35,460 (16%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $33,700 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $133,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Margate is $1,311. It is approximately $156 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $249 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $128 (10%) higher.
