Tamarac's median home value is $168,900. It is about $2,860 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $48,600 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $148,800 (46%) lower.

Rents
