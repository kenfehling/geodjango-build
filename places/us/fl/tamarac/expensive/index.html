Home prices

The median home in Tamarac costs $168,900. It is approximately $2,860 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $48,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $148,800 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Tamarac the median monthly rent is $1,402. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $64 (4%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $340 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $219 (18%) higher.
