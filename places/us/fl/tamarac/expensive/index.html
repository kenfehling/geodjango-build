Home prices

The price of the median home in Tamarac is $168,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $2,860 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $48,600 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $148,800 (46%) lower.

Rents

Tamarac's median monthly rent is $1,402. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $64 (4%) higher. It is roughly $340 (32%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $219 (18%) higher.
