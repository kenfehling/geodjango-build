Home prices

The median home cost in Buckingham is $294,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $93,500 (46%) higher. It is about $77,100 (35%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cape Coral it is about $65,200 (28%) higher.

Rents
