Home prices

Buckingham's median home cost is $294,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $93,500 (46%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $77,100 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is about $65,200 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Buckingham the median rent is $1,109 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $15 (1%) lower. It is about $47 (4%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $135 (10%) lower compared to the city of Cape Coral.
