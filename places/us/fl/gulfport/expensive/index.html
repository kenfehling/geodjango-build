Compared to the surrounding areas, Gulfport is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Gulfport is $185,600. This is about $127,400 (40%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $31,900 (14%) lower, and is approximately $53,300 (22%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Gulfport the median rent is $1,071 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $99 (8%) lower. It is is very similar compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $60 (5%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
