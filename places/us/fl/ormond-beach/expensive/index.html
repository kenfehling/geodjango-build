Home prices

The median home value in Ormond Beach is $208,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $26,580 (14%) higher. It is approximately $8,700 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $35,600 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median rent in Ormond Beach is $1,141 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $143 (14%) higher. It is approximately $79 (7%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $76 (7%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.
