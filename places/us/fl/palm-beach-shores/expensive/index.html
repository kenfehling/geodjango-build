Compared to other places in this area, Palm Beach Shores is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Miami, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Palm Beach Shores is $523,900. This is about $305,180 (139%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $306,400 (140%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $206,200 (64%) higher.

Rents

Palm Beach Shores's median monthly rent is $1,344. It is about $100 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $282 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $161 (13%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
