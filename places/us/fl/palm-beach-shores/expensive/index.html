Home prices

The median home in Palm Beach Shores is valued at $523,900. This is roughly $305,180 (139%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $306,400 (140%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $206,200 (64%) higher.

Rents

Palm Beach Shores's median rent is $1,344 per month. It is approximately $100 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $282 (26%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $161 (13%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
