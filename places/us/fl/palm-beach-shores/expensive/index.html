Compared to other places in this area, Palm Beach Shores is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Beach Shores is valued at $523,900. This is about $305,180 (139%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $306,400 (140%) higher, and is approximately $206,200 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Palm Beach Shores is $1,344 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $100 (8%) higher.It is approximately $282 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $161 (13%) higher.
