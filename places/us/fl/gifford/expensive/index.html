Home prices

The price of the median home in Gifford is $231,100. This is roughly $74,160 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $13,600 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $8,900 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Gifford's median monthly rent is $789. This is about $247 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $273 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $407 (34%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
