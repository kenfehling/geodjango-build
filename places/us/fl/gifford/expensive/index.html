Compared to other places in this area, Gifford is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Orlando, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Gifford is $231,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $74,160 (24%) lower. It is roughly $13,600 (6%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $8,900 (3%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

In Gifford the median rent is $789 per month. This is roughly $247 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $273 (25%) lower, and is approximately $407 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.
