Home prices

The value of the median home in Edgewater is $155,900. This is about $131,260 (45%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $61,600 (28%) lower, and is roughly $84,100 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Edgewater's median monthly rent is $1,086. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $370 (25%) lower. It is approximately $24 (2%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $110 (9%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
