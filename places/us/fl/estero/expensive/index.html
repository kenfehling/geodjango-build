Home prices

The median home value in Estero is $339,600. This is roughly $70,960 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $122,100 (56%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral it is roughly $110,200 (48%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Estero is $1,572 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $292 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $510 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is approximately $328 (26%) higher.
