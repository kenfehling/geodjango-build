Home prices

The median home in Port Richey is valued at $157,600. It is approximately $66,040 (72%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $81,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Port Richey the median monthly rent is $868. It is about $110 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $194 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $263 (23%) lower.
