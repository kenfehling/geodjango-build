Home prices

The median home value in Inwood is $79,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $69,640 (46%) lower. It is roughly $137,700 (63%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $159,100 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Inwood the median rent is $775 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $238 (23%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $287 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $356 (31%) lower.
