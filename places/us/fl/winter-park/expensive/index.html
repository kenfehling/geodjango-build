Home prices

The price of the median home in Winter Park is $447,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $219,740 (96%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $230,300 (105%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $208,900 (87%) higher.

Rents

In Winter Park the median monthly rent is $1,289. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $123 (10%) higher.It is approximately $227 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $158 (13%) higher.
