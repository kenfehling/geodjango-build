Home prices

The median home cost in Whitfield is $261,000. It is about $33,560 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $43,500 (19%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $22,100 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Whitfield's median rent is $1,042 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $97 (8%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $20 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $89 (7%) lower.
