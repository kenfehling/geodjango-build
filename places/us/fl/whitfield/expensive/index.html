Home prices

The median home in Whitfield is valued at $261,000. This is approximately $33,560 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $43,500 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $22,100 (9%) higher.

Rents

Whitfield's median monthly rent is $1,042. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $97 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $20 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $89 (7%) lower.
