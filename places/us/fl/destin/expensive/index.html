Home prices

The median home in Destin costs $332,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $44,080 (15%) higher.It is about $114,500 (52%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $149,200 (81%) higher compared to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Destin is $1,440. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $207 (16%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $378 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola is about $486 (50%) higher.
