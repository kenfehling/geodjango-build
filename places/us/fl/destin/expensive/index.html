In comparison to the surrounding areas, Destin is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Pensacola.

Home prices

The median home in Destin is valued at $332,000. It is approximately $44,080 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $114,500 (52%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pensacola it is roughly $149,200 (81%) higher.

Rents

In Destin the median monthly rent is $1,440. This is approximately $207 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $378 (35%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pensacola is about $486 (50%) higher.
