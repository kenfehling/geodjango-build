In comparison to nearby areas, Destin is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Pensacola, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Destin is $332,000. It is approximately $44,080 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $114,500 (52%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola it is roughly $149,200 (81%) higher.

Rents

Destin's median rent is $1,440 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $207 (16%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $378 (35%) higher, and is about $486 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Pensacola.
