In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bloomingdale is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Bloomingdale is valued at $243,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $13,260 (5%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $26,100 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $4,700 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Bloomingdale the median monthly rent is $1,536. It is about $94 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $474 (44%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $405 (35%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
