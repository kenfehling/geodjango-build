Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomingdale is $1,536. This is approximately $94 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $474 (44%) higher, and it is roughly $405 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
