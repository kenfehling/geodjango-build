Home prices

The median home price in Bloomingdale is $243,600. This is about $13,260 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $26,100 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $4,700 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomingdale is $1,536. It is approximately $94 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $474 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $405 (35%) higher.
