Home prices

The median home price in Bloomingdale is $243,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $13,260 (5%) higher.It is roughly $26,100 (12%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $4,700 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Bloomingdale's median rent is $1,536 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $94 (6%) higher.It is about $474 (44%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $405 (35%) higher.
