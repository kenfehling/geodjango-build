Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bloomingdale is $243,600. This is approximately $13,260 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $26,100 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $4,700 (1%) higher.

Rents

Bloomingdale's median rent is $1,536 a month. It is about $94 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $474 (44%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $405 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
