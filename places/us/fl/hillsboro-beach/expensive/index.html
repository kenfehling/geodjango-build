Home prices

The median home cost in Hillsboro Beach is $417,900. It is approximately $38,360 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $200,400 (92%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is roughly $100,200 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Hillsboro Beach the median monthly rent is $1,986. This is approximately $218 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $924 (87%) higher, and it is about $803 (67%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
