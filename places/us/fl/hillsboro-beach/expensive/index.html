Home prices

The price of the median home in Hillsboro Beach is $417,900. It is approximately $38,360 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $200,400 (92%) higher, and it is roughly $100,200 (31%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Hillsboro Beach the median rent is $1,986 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $218 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $924 (87%) higher, and is approximately $803 (67%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
