Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro Beach is valued at $417,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $38,360 (8%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $200,400 (92%) higher, and it is approximately $100,200 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Hillsboro Beach's median monthly rent is $1,986. It is roughly $218 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $924 (87%) higher, and it is about $803 (67%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
