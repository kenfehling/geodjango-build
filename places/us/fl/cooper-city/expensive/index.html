Home prices

The median home in Cooper City costs $378,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $34,000 (9%) higher.It is approximately $160,600 (73%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $60,400 (19%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Cooper City's median rent is $1,875 a month. It is approximately $49 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $813 (76%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $692 (58%) higher.
