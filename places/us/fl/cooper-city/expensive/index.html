Home prices

The value of the median home in Cooper City is $378,100. It is approximately $34,000 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $160,600 (73%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $60,400 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cooper City is $1,875. This is about $49 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $813 (76%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $692 (58%) higher.
