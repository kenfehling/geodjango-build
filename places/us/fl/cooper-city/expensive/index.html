In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cooper City is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Cooper City's median home price is $378,100. It is about $111,560 (41%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $160,600 (73%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $60,400 (19%) higher.

Rents

Cooper City's median monthly rent is $1,875. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $506 (36%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $813 (76%) higher, and is about $692 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
