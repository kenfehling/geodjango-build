In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cooper City is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Miami, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cooper City is $378,100. This is approximately $111,560 (41%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $160,600 (73%) higher, and is approximately $60,400 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Cooper City is $1,875 a month. This is about $506 (36%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $813 (76%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $692 (58%) higher.
