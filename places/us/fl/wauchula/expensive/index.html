Home prices

The median home in Wauchula is valued at $67,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $26,020 (27%) lower. It is about $150,400 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $171,800 (71%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Wauchula's median rent is $683 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $59 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $379 (35%) lower, and it is about $448 (39%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
