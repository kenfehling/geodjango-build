Home prices

Rents

West Bradenton's median monthly rent is $1,420. It is about $386 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $358 (33%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $289 (25%) higher.
