Compared to other places in this area, West Bradenton is substantially more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in West Bradenton is $249,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $92,240 (58%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $32,400 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $11,000 (4%) higher.

Rents

In West Bradenton the median monthly rent is $1,420. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $386 (37%) higher. It is roughly $358 (33%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $289 (25%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
