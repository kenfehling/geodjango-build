Home prices

West Bradenton's median home value is $249,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $92,240 (58%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $32,400 (14%) higher, and is approximately $11,000 (4%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In West Bradenton the median rent is $1,420 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $386 (37%) higher. It is roughly $358 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $289 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
