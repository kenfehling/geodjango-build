Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Neptune Beach is $1,372. This is roughly $164 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $310 (29%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $307 (28%) higher.
