Home prices

The median home in Tarpon Springs is valued at $209,400. This is about $19,540 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $8,100 (3%) lower, and is approximately $29,500 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Tarpon Springs is $851 per month. This is about $491 (36%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $211 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $280 (24%) lower.
