In comparison to nearby areas, Tarpon Springs is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home value in Tarpon Springs is $209,400. This is about $19,540 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $8,100 (3%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $29,500 (12%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Tarpon Springs's median rent is $851 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $491 (36%) lower. It is roughly $211 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $280 (24%) lower.
