Home prices

Tarpon Springs's median home cost is $209,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $19,540 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $8,100 (3%) lower, and is roughly $29,500 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Tarpon Springs the median monthly rent is $851. This is roughly $491 (36%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $211 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $280 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
