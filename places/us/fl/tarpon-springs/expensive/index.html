Home prices

The median home in Tarpon Springs costs $209,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $19,540 (10%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $8,100 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $29,500 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Tarpon Springs the median rent is $851 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $491 (36%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $211 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $280 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
