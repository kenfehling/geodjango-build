Home prices

Dundee's median home value is $121,400. It is approximately $36,580 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $96,100 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $117,500 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Dundee the median rent is $907 a month. This is approximately $113 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $155 (14%) lower, and is approximately $224 (19%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
