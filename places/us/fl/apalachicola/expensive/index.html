Home prices

The median home in Apalachicola is valued at $156,000. This is approximately $73,480 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $61,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $47,800 (23%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Apalachicola is $776. Compared to other places in this area it is about $264 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $286 (26%) lower, and is about $247 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.
