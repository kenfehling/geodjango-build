Compared to other places in this area, Apalachicola is significantly less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Tallahassee, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Apalachicola is $156,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $73,480 (32%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $61,500 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee it is about $47,800 (23%) lower.

Rents

Apalachicola's median monthly rent is $776. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $264 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $286 (26%) lower, and is approximately $247 (24%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.
