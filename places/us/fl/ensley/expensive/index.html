Rents

Ensley's median monthly rent is $1,041. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $54 (5%) higher.It is approximately $21 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is approximately $184 (21%) higher.
