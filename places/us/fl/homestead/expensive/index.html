Compared to its surrounding areas, Homestead is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home cost in Homestead is $222,300. This is roughly $14,060 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $4,800 (2%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $95,400 (30%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Homestead's median monthly rent is $1,267. This is about $100 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $205 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $84 (7%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
