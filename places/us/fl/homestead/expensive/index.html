Compared to its surrounding areas, Homestead is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Homestead costs $222,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,060 (6%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $4,800 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $95,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Homestead is $1,267 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $100 (8%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $205 (19%) higher, and is roughly $84 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
