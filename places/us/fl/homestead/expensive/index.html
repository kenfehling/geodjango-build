Home prices

Homestead's median home value is $222,300. This is roughly $14,060 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $4,800 (2%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $95,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Homestead is $1,267. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $100 (8%) higher.It is about $205 (19%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $84 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
