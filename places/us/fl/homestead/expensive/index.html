Home prices

The price of the median home in Homestead is $222,300. This is approximately $14,060 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $4,800 (2%) higher, and it is approximately $95,400 (30%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Homestead is $1,267. Compared to nearby areas it is about $100 (8%) higher. It is roughly $205 (19%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $84 (7%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
