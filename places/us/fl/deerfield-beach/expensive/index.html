Home prices

The median home in Deerfield Beach is valued at $168,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $121,440 (41%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $48,900 (22%) lower, and is roughly $149,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Deerfield Beach is $1,326 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $236 (15%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $264 (24%) higher, and it is approximately $143 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
