In comparison to other places in this area, Leesburg is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Leesburg costs $153,000. It is roughly $35,360 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $64,500 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $85,900 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Leesburg the median rent is $889 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $85 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $173 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $242 (21%) lower.
