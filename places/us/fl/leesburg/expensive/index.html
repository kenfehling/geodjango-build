In comparison to other places in this area, Leesburg is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Tampa however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Leesburg is valued at $153,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $35,360 (18%) lower.It is approximately $64,500 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $85,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Leesburg's median monthly rent is $889. This is about $85 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $173 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $242 (21%) lower.
