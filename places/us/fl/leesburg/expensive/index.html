Home prices

The value of the median home in Leesburg is $153,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $35,360 (18%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $64,500 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $85,900 (35%) lower.

Rents

Leesburg's median monthly rent is $889. It is about $85 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $173 (16%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $242 (21%) lower.
