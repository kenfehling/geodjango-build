In comparison to nearby areas, Leesburg is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Leesburg's median home price is $153,000. It is approximately $35,360 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $64,500 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $85,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Leesburg is $889 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $85 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $173 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $242 (21%) lower.
