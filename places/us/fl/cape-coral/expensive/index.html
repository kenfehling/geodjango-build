The median home in Cape Coral costs $229,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $15,667 (7%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $11,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

Cape Coral's median monthly rent is $1,244. Compared to other places in this area it is about $216 (20%) higher. It is approximately $182 (17%) higher compared to the entire country.
