Compared to other places in this area, Fort Myers is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Myers is $238,700. This is about $75,920 (46%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $21,200 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Cape Coral is approximately $9,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

Fort Myers's median rent is $1,033 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $56 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $29 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral is approximately $211 (16%) lower.
