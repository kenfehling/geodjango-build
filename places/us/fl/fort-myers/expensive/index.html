Compared to its nearby areas, Fort Myers is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cape Coral however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Myers is $238,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $75,920 (46%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $21,200 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral it is roughly $9,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Myers is $1,033 a month. It is roughly $56 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $29 (2%) lower, and is roughly $211 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
