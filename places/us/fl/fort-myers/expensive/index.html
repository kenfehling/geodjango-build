In comparison to other places in this area, Fort Myers is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cape Coral, it's close to average.

Home prices

Fort Myers's median home value is $238,700. It is about $75,920 (46%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $21,200 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $9,300 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

Fort Myers's median rent is $1,033 per month. It is approximately $56 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $29 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cape Coral it is roughly $211 (16%) lower.
