In comparison to nearby areas, Fort Myers is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fort Myers is $238,700. This is about $75,920 (46%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $21,200 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $9,300 (4%) higher compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Myers is $1,033. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $56 (5%) lower.It is approximately $29 (2%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $211 (16%) lower compared to the city of Cape Coral.
