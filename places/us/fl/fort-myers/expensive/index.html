In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Myers is a lot more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Cape Coral.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Myers is valued at $238,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $75,920 (46%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $21,200 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Cape Coral is approximately $9,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Myers is $1,033 a month. This is about $56 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $29 (2%) lower, and is approximately $211 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cape Coral.
