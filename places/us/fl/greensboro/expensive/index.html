Compared to nearby areas, Greensboro is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Home prices

The median home price in Greensboro is $72,100. This is about $6,700 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $145,400 (66%) lower, and it is about $131,700 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

In Greensboro the median rent is $831 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $185 (28%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $231 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Tallahassee is about $192 (18%) lower.
