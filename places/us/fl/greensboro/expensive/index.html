In comparison to the surrounding areas, Greensboro is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Home prices

The median home in Greensboro is valued at $72,100. This is approximately $6,700 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $145,400 (66%) lower, and it is approximately $131,700 (64%) lower compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

In Greensboro the median rent is $831 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $185 (28%) higher. It is approximately $231 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee it is approximately $192 (18%) lower.
