Compared to other places in this area, Coral Springs is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home value in Coral Springs is $354,100. It is roughly $89,280 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $136,600 (62%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $36,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Coral Springs the median monthly rent is $1,532. This is approximately $126 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $470 (44%) higher, and is roughly $349 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
