Home prices

The median home price in Coral Springs is $354,100. This is about $89,280 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $136,600 (62%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami is roughly $36,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Coral Springs is $1,532 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $126 (7%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $470 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $349 (29%) higher.
