Compared to its nearby areas, Coral Springs is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Coral Springs is $354,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $89,280 (33%) higher. It is approximately $136,600 (62%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $36,400 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Coral Springs's median rent is $1,532 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $126 (7%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $470 (44%) higher, and it is approximately $349 (29%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
