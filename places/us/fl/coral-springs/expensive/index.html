Compared to its surrounding areas, Coral Springs is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Coral Springs's median home cost is $354,100. It is approximately $89,280 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $136,600 (62%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $36,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Coral Springs is $1,532 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $126 (7%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $470 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $349 (29%) higher.
