Home prices

The cost of the median home in Midway is $236,000. It is approximately $2,960 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $18,500 (8%) higher, and is approximately $112,400 (90%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.

Rents

Midway's median monthly rent is $1,236. Compared to other places in this area it is about $230 (15%) lower.It is approximately $174 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $379 (44%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
