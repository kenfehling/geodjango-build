Home prices

The price of the median home in Midway is $236,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $2,960 (1%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $18,500 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Mobile it is approximately $112,400 (90%) higher.

Rents

Midway's median monthly rent is $1,236. It is about $230 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $174 (16%) higher, and is about $379 (44%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
