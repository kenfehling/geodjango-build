Home prices

The value of the median home in Rio is $235,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $8,680 (3%) lower. It is about $17,700 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $98,800 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale.

Rents

In Rio the median rent is $1,102 a month. This is approximately $102 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $40 (3%) higher, and is about $191 (14%) lower compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.
