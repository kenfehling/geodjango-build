Home prices

Rents

Rio's median rent is $1,102 a month. This is approximately $102 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $40 (3%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $191 (14%) lower compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.
