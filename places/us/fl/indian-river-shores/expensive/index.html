The median home in Indian River Shores is valued at $712,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $420,560 (143%) higher.It is about $495,200 (227%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is roughly $472,700 (196%) higher.

Rents

In Indian River Shores the median rent is $1,096 per month. It is approximately $213 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $34 (3%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Orlando it is roughly $100 (8%) lower.
