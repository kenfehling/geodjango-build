Home prices

The median home in Holly Hill costs $105,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $97,260 (47%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $111,900 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $67,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Holly Hill is $934. This is about $106 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $128 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $131 (12%) lower.
