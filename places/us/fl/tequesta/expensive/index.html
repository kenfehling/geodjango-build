Home prices

The median home in Tequesta is valued at $381,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $170,020 (30%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $163,600 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Hialeah is approximately $152,500 (66%) higher.

Rents

In Tequesta the median rent is $1,481 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $617 (29%) lower. It is roughly $419 (39%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $311 (26%) higher compared to the city of Hialeah.
