Compared to its surrounding areas, Lake Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Miami however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Park costs $201,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $81,820 (28%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $16,100 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $116,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Lake Park the median rent is $1,140 per month. This is approximately $145 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $78 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $43 (3%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
