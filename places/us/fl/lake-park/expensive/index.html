Home prices

The value of the median home in Lake Park is $201,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $81,820 (28%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $16,100 (7%) lower, and is approximately $116,300 (36%) lower compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Park is $1,140 per month. It is approximately $145 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $78 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $43 (3%) lower.
