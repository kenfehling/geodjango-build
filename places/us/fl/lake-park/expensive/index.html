Compared to the surrounding areas, Lake Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Park is $201,400. It is about $81,820 (28%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $16,100 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $116,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Lake Park's median monthly rent is $1,140. It is roughly $145 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $78 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $43 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.
