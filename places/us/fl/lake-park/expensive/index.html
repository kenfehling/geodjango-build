In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lake Park is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lake Park is $201,400. This is about $81,820 (28%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $16,100 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is about $116,300 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Park is $1,140. This is approximately $145 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $78 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is about $43 (3%) lower.
