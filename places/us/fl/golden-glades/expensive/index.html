Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Golden Glades is $1,127 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $14 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $65 (6%) higher, and is roughly $56 (4%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
