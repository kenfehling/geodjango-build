Home prices

The cost of the median home in Palm Beach Gardens is $378,500. This is about $65,780 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $161,000 (74%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is roughly $60,800 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Palm Beach Gardens the median rent is $1,706 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $644 (60%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $523 (44%) higher.
