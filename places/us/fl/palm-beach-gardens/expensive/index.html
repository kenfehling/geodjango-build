In comparison to other places in this area, Palm Beach Gardens is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home price in Palm Beach Gardens is $378,500. This is about $65,780 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $161,000 (74%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $60,800 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Palm Beach Gardens the median rent is $1,706 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $644 (60%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $523 (44%) higher.
