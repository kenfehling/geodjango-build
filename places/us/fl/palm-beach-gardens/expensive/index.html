Compared to other places in this area, Palm Beach Gardens is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Palm Beach Gardens is $378,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $65,780 (21%) higher. It is about $161,000 (74%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami is about $60,800 (19%) higher.

Rents

Palm Beach Gardens's median monthly rent is $1,706. In comparison to nearby areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $644 (60%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $523 (44%) higher.
