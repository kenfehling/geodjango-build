Compared to nearby areas, Palm Beach Gardens is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

Palm Beach Gardens's median home value is $378,500. This is roughly $65,780 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $161,000 (74%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $60,800 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Palm Beach Gardens the median monthly rent is $1,706. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas.It is about $644 (60%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Miami it is approximately $523 (44%) higher.
