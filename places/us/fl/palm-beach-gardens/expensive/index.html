Compared to its surrounding areas, Palm Beach Gardens is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Miami, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Palm Beach Gardens's median home cost is $378,500. This is about $65,780 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $161,000 (74%) higher, and it is roughly $60,800 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Palm Beach Gardens's median rent is $1,706 per month. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $644 (60%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $523 (44%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
