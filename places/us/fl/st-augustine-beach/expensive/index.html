In comparison to nearby areas, St. Augustine Beach is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Augustine Beach is $415,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $135,940 (48%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $197,900 (90%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is about $242,200 (139%) higher.

Rents

In St. Augustine Beach the median rent is $1,347 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $119 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $285 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $282 (26%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.
