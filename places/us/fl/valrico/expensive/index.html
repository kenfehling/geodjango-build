Home prices

The median home in Valrico costs $231,600. This is roughly $14,540 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $14,100 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $7,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Valrico is $1,357 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $27 (1%) higher. It is about $295 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $226 (19%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
