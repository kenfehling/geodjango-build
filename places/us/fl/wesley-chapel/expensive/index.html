Home prices

The median home in Wesley Chapel costs $231,600. This is approximately $61,580 (36%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $14,100 (6%) higher, and it is approximately $7,300 (3%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Wesley Chapel's median rent is $1,481 a month. This is about $387 (35%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $419 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Tampa it is about $350 (30%) higher.
