Compared to its surrounding areas, Wesley Chapel is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Tampa, it is close to average.

Home prices

Wesley Chapel's median home cost is $231,600. This is roughly $61,580 (36%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $14,100 (6%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $7,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Wesley Chapel is $1,481 a month. This is about $387 (35%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $419 (39%) higher, and is roughly $350 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
