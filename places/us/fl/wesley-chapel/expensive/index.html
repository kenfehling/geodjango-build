Home prices

The median home in Wesley Chapel costs $231,600. This is about $61,580 (36%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $14,100 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $7,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Wesley Chapel the median monthly rent is $1,481. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $387 (35%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $419 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $350 (30%) higher.
