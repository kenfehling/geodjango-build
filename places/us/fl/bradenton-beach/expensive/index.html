In comparison to other places in this area, Bradenton Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Tampa, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bradenton Beach is valued at $369,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $26,660 (6%) lower.It is approximately $152,200 (69%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $130,800 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bradenton Beach is $1,214. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $94 (7%) lower.It is roughly $152 (14%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $83 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
