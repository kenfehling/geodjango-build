In comparison to its nearby areas, Bradenton Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bradenton Beach is $369,700. This is approximately $26,660 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $152,200 (69%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $130,800 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bradenton Beach is $1,214. It is roughly $94 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $152 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $83 (7%) higher.
