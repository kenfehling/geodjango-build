In comparison to other places in this area, Bradenton Beach is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Tampa, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bradenton Beach is valued at $369,700. This is approximately $26,660 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $152,200 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is approximately $130,800 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bradenton Beach is $1,214 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $94 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $152 (14%) higher, and it is roughly $83 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
