Home prices

The median home in Glencoe is valued at $276,500. This is about $17,240 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $59,000 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is about $36,500 (15%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Glencoe is $1,537 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $126 (8%) higher. It is about $475 (44%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Orlando it is approximately $341 (28%) higher.
