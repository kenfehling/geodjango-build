Home prices

The median home in Fort Pierce North is valued at $87,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $102,280 (53%) lower. It is about $130,200 (59%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $164,400 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of West Palm Beach.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Pierce North is $811 a month. It is roughly $166 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $251 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of West Palm Beach it is roughly $492 (37%) lower.
