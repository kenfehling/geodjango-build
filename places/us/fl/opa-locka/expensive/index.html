In comparison to its surrounding areas, Opa-locka is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Miami, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Opa-locka is $154,300. This is roughly $40,180 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $63,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $163,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Opa-locka is $904. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $227 (20%) lower. It is approximately $158 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $279 (23%) lower compared to the city of Miami.
