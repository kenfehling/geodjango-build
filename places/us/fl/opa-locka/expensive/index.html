In comparison to other places in this area, Opa-locka is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Opa-locka costs $154,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $40,180 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $63,200 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami is about $163,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Opa-locka is $904 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $227 (20%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $158 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $279 (23%) lower.
