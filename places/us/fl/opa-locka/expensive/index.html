Home prices

The median home in Opa-locka is valued at $154,300. This is roughly $40,180 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $63,200 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami it is about $163,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

Opa-locka's median rent is $904 a month. It is approximately $227 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $158 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $279 (23%) lower.
