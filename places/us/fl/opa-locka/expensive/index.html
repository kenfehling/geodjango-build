Compared to its nearby areas, Opa-locka is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Miami, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Opa-locka's median home cost is $154,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $40,180 (20%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $63,200 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $163,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Opa-locka is $904 per month. This is approximately $227 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $158 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $279 (23%) lower.
