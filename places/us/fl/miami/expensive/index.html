Compared to nearby areas, Miami is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The median home in Miami is valued at $317,700. This is roughly $18,317 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $100,200 (46%) higher compared to the nation as a whole.

Rents

In Miami the median rent is $1,183 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $121 (11%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole.
