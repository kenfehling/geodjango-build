In comparison to its surrounding areas, Miami is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home price in Miami is $317,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $18,317 (6%) higher. It is roughly $100,200 (46%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole.

Rents

Miami's median monthly rent is $1,183. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $121 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country.
