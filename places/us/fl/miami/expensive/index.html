In comparison to its surrounding areas, Miami is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

The median home in Miami costs $317,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $18,317 (6%) higher.It is about $100,200 (46%) higher compared to the United States as a whole.

Rents

Miami's median rent is $1,183 per month. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $121 (11%) higher.
