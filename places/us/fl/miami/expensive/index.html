Compared to the surrounding areas, Miami is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

The median home in Miami is valued at $317,700. This is about $18,317 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $100,200 (46%) higher compared to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miami is $1,183. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar.In comparison to the entire country it is about $121 (11%) higher.
