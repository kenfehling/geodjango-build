Compared to other places in this area, Miami is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Miami is valued at $317,700. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $18,317 (6%) higher. It is approximately $100,200 (46%) higher compared to the United States as a whole.

Rents

The median rent in Miami is $1,183 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $121 (11%) higher.
