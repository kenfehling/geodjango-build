The median home cost in North DeLand is $151,300. This is roughly $5,980 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $21,900 (12%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North DeLand is $868. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $189 (17%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $194 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $197 (18%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
