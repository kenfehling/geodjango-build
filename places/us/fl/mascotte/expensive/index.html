Similar to other places in this area, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Mascotte costs $157,700. This is approximately $7,160 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $59,800 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $81,200 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Mascotte the median monthly rent is $897. This is approximately $213 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $165 (15%) lower, and is about $234 (20%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
