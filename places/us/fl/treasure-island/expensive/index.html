Home prices

The value of the median home in Treasure Island is $382,200. It is approximately $115,300 (43%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $164,700 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $143,300 (59%) higher.

Rents

Treasure Island's median rent is $1,240 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $110 (9%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $178 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $109 (9%) higher.
