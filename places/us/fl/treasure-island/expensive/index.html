In comparison to its surrounding areas, Treasure Island is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home in Treasure Island costs $382,200. This is about $115,300 (43%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $164,700 (75%) higher, and is about $143,300 (59%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Treasure Island's median rent is $1,240 per month. It is roughly $110 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $178 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $109 (9%) higher.
