Home prices

The median home in Treasure Island is valued at $382,200. It is about $115,300 (43%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $164,700 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $143,300 (59%) higher.

Rents

Treasure Island's median monthly rent is $1,240. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $110 (9%) higher.It is about $178 (16%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $109 (9%) higher.
