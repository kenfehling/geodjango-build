In comparison to the surrounding areas, Boca Raton is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Miami, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Boca Raton costs $458,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $149,480 (48%) higher. It is about $241,100 (110%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $140,900 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Boca Raton is $1,760. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $24 (1%) higher. It is about $698 (65%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $577 (48%) higher.
