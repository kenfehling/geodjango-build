In comparison to its surrounding areas, Boca Raton is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Boca Raton costs $458,600. This is approximately $149,480 (48%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $241,100 (110%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $140,900 (44%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

Boca Raton's median monthly rent is $1,760. This is about $24 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $698 (65%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $577 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
