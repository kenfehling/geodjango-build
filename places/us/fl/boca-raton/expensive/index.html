In comparison to the surrounding areas, Boca Raton is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Boca Raton's median home price is $458,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $149,480 (48%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $241,100 (110%) higher, and it is roughly $140,900 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

Boca Raton's median rent is $1,760 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $24 (1%) higher.It is approximately $698 (65%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami it is approximately $577 (48%) higher.
