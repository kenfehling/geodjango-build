In comparison to its nearby areas, Boca Raton is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Miami.

Home prices

The median home in Boca Raton costs $458,600. It is about $149,480 (48%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $241,100 (110%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Miami is approximately $140,900 (44%) higher.

Rents

In Boca Raton the median monthly rent is $1,760. This is about $24 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $698 (65%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $577 (48%) higher compared to the city of Miami.
