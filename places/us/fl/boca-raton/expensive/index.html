In comparison to its nearby areas, Boca Raton is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Miami, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Boca Raton costs $458,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $149,480 (48%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $241,100 (110%) higher, and it is about $140,900 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Boca Raton the median rent is $1,760 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $24 (1%) higher. It is roughly $698 (65%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $577 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
