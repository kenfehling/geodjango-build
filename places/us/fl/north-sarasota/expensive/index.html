Home prices

The median home in North Sarasota costs $127,600. This is about $115,340 (47%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $89,900 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $111,300 (46%) lower.

Rents

North Sarasota's median monthly rent is $1,188. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $64 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $126 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $57 (5%) higher.
