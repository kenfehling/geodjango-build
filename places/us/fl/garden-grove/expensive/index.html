Home prices

The median home in Garden Grove costs $106,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16,300 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $110,600 (50%) lower, and is about $132,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Garden Grove the median monthly rent is $723. It is roughly $79 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $339 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $408 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
