Compared to other places in this area, Jasper is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

Jasper's median home cost is $78,600. It is roughly $18,240 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $138,900 (63%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is approximately $94,600 (54%) lower.

Rents

Jasper's median rent is $473 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $167 (26%) lower. It is about $589 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $592 (55%) lower compared to the city of Jacksonville.
