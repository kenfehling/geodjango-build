Compared to its surrounding areas, Jasper is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Jasper is $78,600. It is about $18,240 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $138,900 (63%) lower, and it is roughly $94,600 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

In Jasper the median rent is $473 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $167 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $589 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is roughly $592 (55%) lower.
