Compared to its surrounding areas, Cape Canaveral is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Orlando.

Home prices

The median home in Cape Canaveral costs $244,900. It is roughly $47,520 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $27,400 (12%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $4,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Orlando.

Rents

Cape Canaveral's median rent is $893 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $128 (12%) lower. It is roughly $169 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $303 (25%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.
