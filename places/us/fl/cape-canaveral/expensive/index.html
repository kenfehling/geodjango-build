Home prices

The value of the median home in Cape Canaveral is $244,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $47,520 (24%) higher.It is roughly $27,400 (12%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $4,900 (2%) higher compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cape Canaveral is $893. This is roughly $128 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $169 (15%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $303 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.
