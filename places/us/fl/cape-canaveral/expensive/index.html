In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cape Canaveral is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Orlando.

Home prices

The median home cost in Cape Canaveral is $244,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $47,520 (24%) higher.It is about $27,400 (12%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $4,900 (2%) higher compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

In Cape Canaveral the median rent is $893 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $128 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $169 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Orlando is roughly $303 (25%) lower.
