Compared to its nearby areas, Orange Park is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Jacksonville however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Orange Park is $180,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $63,000 (25%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $36,700 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $7,600 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Orange Park is $981 a month. This is roughly $496 (33%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $81 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $84 (7%) lower.
