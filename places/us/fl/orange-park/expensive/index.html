In comparison to the surrounding areas, Orange Park is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Orange Park is $180,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $63,000 (25%) lower. It is approximately $36,700 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $7,600 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Orange Park's median rent is $981 a month. This is about $496 (33%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $81 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is approximately $84 (7%) lower.
