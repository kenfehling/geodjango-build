In comparison to nearby areas, Orange Park is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Orange Park is $180,800. This is roughly $63,000 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $36,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $7,600 (4%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Orange Park's median rent is $981 per month. It is about $496 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $81 (7%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is about $84 (7%) lower.
