In comparison to its nearby areas, Four Corners is significantly less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Four Corners is $182,800. It is approximately $206,380 (53%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $34,700 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $56,100 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Four Corners is $1,349. This is roughly $416 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $287 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $218 (19%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
