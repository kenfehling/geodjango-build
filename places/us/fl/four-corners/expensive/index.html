Home prices

The median home cost in Four Corners is $182,800. This is roughly $206,380 (53%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $34,700 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $56,100 (23%) lower.

Rents

Four Corners's median rent is $1,349 a month. It is roughly $416 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $287 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $218 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Tampa.
