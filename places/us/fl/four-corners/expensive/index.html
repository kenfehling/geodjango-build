In comparison to its surrounding areas, Four Corners is a lot less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Four Corners is $182,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $206,380 (53%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $34,700 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $56,100 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

The median rent in Four Corners is $1,349 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $416 (23%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $287 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is about $218 (19%) higher.
