Compared to its surrounding areas, Lighthouse Point is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Miami, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lighthouse Point is $538,700. It is approximately $106,600 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $321,200 (147%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $221,000 (69%) higher.

Rents

Lighthouse Point's median monthly rent is $1,435. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $443 (23%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $373 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is roughly $252 (21%) higher.
