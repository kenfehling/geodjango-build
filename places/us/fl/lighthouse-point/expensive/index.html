Home prices

Lighthouse Point's median home value is $538,700. This is approximately $106,600 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $321,200 (147%) higher, and it is approximately $221,000 (69%) higher compared to the city of Miami.

Rents

In Lighthouse Point the median rent is $1,435 a month. It is approximately $443 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $373 (35%) higher, and is roughly $252 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Miami.
