Home prices

The median home in Lighthouse Point costs $538,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $106,600 (24%) higher.It is approximately $321,200 (147%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Miami is about $221,000 (69%) higher.

Rents

In Lighthouse Point the median monthly rent is $1,435. This is roughly $443 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $373 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is approximately $252 (21%) higher.
