Home prices

The median home in South Gate Ridge is valued at $247,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $33,860 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $29,700 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $8,300 (3%) higher.

Rents
