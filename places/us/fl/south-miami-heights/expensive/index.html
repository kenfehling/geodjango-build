Home prices

The median home in South Miami Heights costs $226,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $33,640 (12%) lower. It is about $8,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $91,400 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Miami.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Miami Heights is $1,179. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $205 (14%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $117 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Miami is is very similar.
