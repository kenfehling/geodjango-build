Home prices

Rents

Milton's median rent is $1,155 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $226 (24%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $93 (8%) higher, and it is about $298 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Mobile.
