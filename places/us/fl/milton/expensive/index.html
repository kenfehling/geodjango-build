Home prices

The median home in Milton is valued at $129,000. It is about $15,860 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $88,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Mobile is about $5,400 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milton is $1,155. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $226 (24%) higher. It is approximately $93 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $298 (34%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.
