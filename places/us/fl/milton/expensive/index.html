Home prices

Milton's median home price is $129,000. It is approximately $15,860 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $88,500 (40%) lower, and is roughly $5,400 (4%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

In Milton the median monthly rent is $1,155. It is about $226 (24%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $93 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Mobile is about $298 (34%) higher.
