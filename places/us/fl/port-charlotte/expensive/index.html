Compared to other places in this area, Port Charlotte is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The median home value in Port Charlotte is $164,000. This is approximately $22,000 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $53,500 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is roughly $74,900 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Port Charlotte the median monthly rent is $1,016. This is about $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $46 (4%) lower, and it is about $115 (10%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.
