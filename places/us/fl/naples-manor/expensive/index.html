Compared to its surrounding areas, Naples Manor is substantially less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pembroke Pines however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Naples Manor is valued at $225,300. This is roughly $244,000 (51%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $7,800 (3%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines it is approximately $63,400 (21%) lower.

Rents

Naples Manor's median rent is $1,106 per month. This is approximately $375 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pembroke Pines is about $409 (26%) lower.
