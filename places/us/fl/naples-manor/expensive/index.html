Home prices

The median home in Naples Manor costs $225,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $244,000 (51%) lower. It is roughly $7,800 (3%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $63,400 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines.

Rents

In Naples Manor the median monthly rent is $1,106. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $375 (25%) lower. It is about $44 (4%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pembroke Pines is approximately $409 (26%) lower.
