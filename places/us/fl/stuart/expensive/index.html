In comparison to other places in this area, Stuart is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Stuart is $185,700. It is roughly $68,080 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $31,800 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale it is roughly $148,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

Stuart's median rent is $1,127 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $72 (5%) lower.It is roughly $65 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale it is roughly $166 (12%) lower.
