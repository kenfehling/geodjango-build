Compared to nearby areas, Stuart is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale.

Home prices

The median home cost in Stuart is $185,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $68,080 (26%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $31,800 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Lauderdale it is roughly $148,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Stuart is $1,127 per month. It is roughly $72 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $65 (6%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Lauderdale is about $166 (12%) lower.
