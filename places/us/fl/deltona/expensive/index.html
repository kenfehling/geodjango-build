Compared to other places in this area, Deltona is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Orlando.

Home prices

The median home in Deltona is valued at $155,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $13,780 (9%) higher. It is roughly $62,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $85,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Deltona is $1,174. It is approximately $127 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $112 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Orlando it is roughly $22 (1%) lower.
