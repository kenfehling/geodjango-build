In comparison to other places in this area, Deltona is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Orlando however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Deltona costs $155,000. This is roughly $13,780 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower, and is about $85,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Orlando.

Rents

The median rent in Deltona is $1,174 per month. It is approximately $127 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $112 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Orlando is roughly $22 (1%) lower.
