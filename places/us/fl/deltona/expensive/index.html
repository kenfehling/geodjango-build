In comparison to other places in this area, Deltona is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Orlando, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Deltona is $155,000. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $13,780 (9%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Orlando is approximately $85,000 (35%) lower.

Rents

Deltona's median rent is $1,174 per month. This is about $127 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $112 (10%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Orlando is approximately $22 (1%) lower.
