Compared to its surrounding areas, Deltona is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Orlando.

Home prices

The median home price in Deltona is $155,000. It is roughly $13,780 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Orlando is approximately $85,000 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Deltona is $1,174. It is roughly $127 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $112 (10%) higher, and it is roughly $22 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Orlando.
