Home prices

The median home in Highland City costs $167,300. This is approximately $13,120 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $50,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $71,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

Highland City's median rent is $1,098 per month. It is about $14 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $36 (3%) higher, and is roughly $33 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.
