Compared to its surrounding areas, Lake Lorraine is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Pensacola, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Lorraine costs $273,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $57,580 (26%) higher.It is approximately $55,500 (25%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pensacola is approximately $90,200 (49%) higher.

Rents

Lake Lorraine's median monthly rent is $1,171. It is roughly $47 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $109 (10%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $217 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Pensacola.
