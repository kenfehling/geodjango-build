Home prices

The median home in Lake Lorraine costs $273,000. It is roughly $57,580 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $55,500 (25%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $90,200 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

Lake Lorraine's median rent is $1,171 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $47 (4%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $109 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Pensacola it is roughly $217 (22%) higher.
