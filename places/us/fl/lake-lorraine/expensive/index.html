In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lake Lorraine is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Pensacola, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Lorraine costs $273,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $57,580 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $55,500 (25%) higher, and it is about $90,200 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

In Lake Lorraine the median rent is $1,171 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $47 (4%) higher. It is about $109 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pensacola it is about $217 (22%) higher.
