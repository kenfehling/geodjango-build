Home prices

The median home in Lake Lorraine costs $273,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $57,580 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $55,500 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola is approximately $90,200 (49%) higher.

Rents

In Lake Lorraine the median rent is $1,171 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $47 (4%) higher. It is approximately $109 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola it is about $217 (22%) higher.
