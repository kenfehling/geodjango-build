Compared to the surrounding areas, Lake Lorraine is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Pensacola however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Lorraine costs $273,000. This is about $57,580 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $55,500 (25%) higher, and it is about $90,200 (49%) higher compared to the city of Pensacola.

Rents

In Lake Lorraine the median rent is $1,171 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $47 (4%) higher. It is roughly $109 (10%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pensacola is roughly $217 (22%) higher.
