Home prices

Edgewood's median home price is $297,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $77,340 (35%) higher.It is roughly $80,400 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tampa is approximately $59,000 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edgewood is $1,025. This is approximately $24 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $37 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $106 (9%) lower.
