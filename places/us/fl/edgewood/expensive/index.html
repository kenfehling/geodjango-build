Home prices

The median home price in Edgewood is $297,900. It is approximately $77,340 (35%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $80,400 (36%) higher, and is about $59,000 (24%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Edgewood the median rent is $1,025 per month. It is about $24 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is approximately $106 (9%) lower.
