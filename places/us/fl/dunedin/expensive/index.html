Compared to nearby areas, Dunedin is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Tampa.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dunedin is $198,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $31,320 (13%) lower. It is roughly $18,800 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $40,200 (16%) lower compared to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Dunedin the median rent is $1,087 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $180 (14%) lower. It is about $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is roughly $44 (3%) lower.
