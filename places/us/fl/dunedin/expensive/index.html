Compared to the surrounding areas, Dunedin is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Dunedin is $198,700. This is roughly $31,320 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $18,800 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $40,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Dunedin the median monthly rent is $1,087. This is approximately $180 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $25 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Tampa it is roughly $44 (3%) lower.
