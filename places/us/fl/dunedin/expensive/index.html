Compared to the surrounding areas, Dunedin is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Tampa, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dunedin costs $198,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $31,320 (13%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $18,800 (8%) lower, and is about $40,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Dunedin's median rent is $1,087 per month. It is about $180 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $25 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa it is approximately $44 (3%) lower.
