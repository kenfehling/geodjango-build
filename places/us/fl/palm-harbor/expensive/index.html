Home prices

The median home in Palm Harbor costs $232,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $6,420 (2%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $15,400 (7%) higher, and it is about $6,000 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

Palm Harbor's median rent is $1,296 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $119 (10%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $234 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is about $165 (14%) higher.
