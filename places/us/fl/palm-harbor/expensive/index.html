Home prices

The value of the median home in Palm Harbor is $232,900. It is roughly $6,420 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $15,400 (7%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tampa is about $6,000 (2%) lower.

Rents

Palm Harbor's median monthly rent is $1,296. This is roughly $119 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $234 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $165 (14%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
