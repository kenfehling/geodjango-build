Rents

In Palm Harbor the median monthly rent is $1,296. This is roughly $119 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $234 (22%) higher, and it is roughly $165 (14%) higher compared to the city of Tampa.
