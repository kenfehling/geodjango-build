Home prices

The value of the median home in Timber Pines is $159,900. This is approximately $41,320 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $57,600 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $79,000 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Tampa.

Rents

In Timber Pines the median rent is $840 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $228 (21%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $222 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tampa is roughly $291 (25%) lower.
