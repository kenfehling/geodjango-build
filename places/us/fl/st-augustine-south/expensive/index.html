Home prices

The median home in St. Augustine South is valued at $208,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $112,460 (35%) lower. It is roughly $9,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $35,200 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Augustine South is $1,304. This is about $67 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $242 (22%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $239 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.
