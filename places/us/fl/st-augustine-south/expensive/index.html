Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Augustine South is a lot less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Jacksonville, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Augustine South is $208,400. This is about $112,460 (35%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $9,100 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $35,200 (20%) higher.

Rents

In St. Augustine South the median monthly rent is $1,304. This is approximately $67 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $242 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville it is about $239 (22%) higher.
