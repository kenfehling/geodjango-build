Home prices

The median home in St. Augustine South costs $208,400. It is about $112,460 (35%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $9,100 (4%) lower, and is roughly $35,200 (20%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

The median rent in St. Augustine South is $1,304 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $67 (5%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $242 (22%) higher, and is approximately $239 (22%) higher compared to the city of Jacksonville.
