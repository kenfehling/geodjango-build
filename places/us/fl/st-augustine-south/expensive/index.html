In comparison to other places in this area, St. Augustine South is significantly less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Jacksonville however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Augustine South costs $208,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $112,460 (35%) lower. It is roughly $9,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Jacksonville it is about $35,200 (20%) higher.

Rents

In St. Augustine South the median monthly rent is $1,304. It is roughly $67 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $242 (22%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jacksonville is roughly $239 (22%) higher.
