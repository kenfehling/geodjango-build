Rents

Rock Hill's median monthly rent is $967. It is approximately $19 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $95 (8%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $168 (14%) lower.
