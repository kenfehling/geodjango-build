Home prices

Rock Hill's median home cost is $155,800. This is roughly $62,600 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $61,700 (28%) lower, and it is about $64,500 (29%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Rock Hill's median rent is $967 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $19 (2%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $95 (8%) lower, and it is roughly $168 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
