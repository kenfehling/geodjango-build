Home prices

Chesterfield's median home cost is $113,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $24,500 (27%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $103,800 (47%) lower, and it is about $106,600 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Chesterfield is $679 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $47 (6%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $383 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $456 (40%) lower.
