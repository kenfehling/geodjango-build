Home prices

The median home in Livingston costs $72,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $5,260 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $145,000 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is approximately $108,600 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Livingston is $850 per month. This is approximately $223 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and is roughly $83 (8%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
