Home prices

The median home in Golden Grove costs $115,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $6,220 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $101,900 (46%) lower, and is about $154,800 (57%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Golden Grove the median rent is $843 a month. It is about $33 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $219 (20%) lower, and it is about $200 (19%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
