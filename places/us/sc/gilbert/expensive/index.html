Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gilbert is $825. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $27 (3%) lower. It is approximately $237 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is about $108 (11%) lower.
