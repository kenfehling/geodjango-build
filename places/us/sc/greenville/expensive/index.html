Home prices

Greenville's median home price is $292,400. It is approximately $188,980 (182%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $74,900 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $22,000 (8%) higher compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Greenville's median monthly rent is $984. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $149 (17%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $78 (7%) lower, and it is about $59 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
