Compared to other places in this area, Greenville is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Greenville is $292,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $188,980 (182%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $74,900 (34%) higher, and it is about $22,000 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Greenville the median rent is $984 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $149 (17%) higher.It is roughly $78 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is approximately $59 (5%) lower.
