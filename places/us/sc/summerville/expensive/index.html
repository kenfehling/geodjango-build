Home prices

The value of the median home in Summerville is $207,800. This is approximately $52,700 (33%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $9,700 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia it is about $26,700 (14%) higher.

Rents

Summerville's median rent is $1,108 a month. It is approximately $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $46 (4%) higher, and is approximately $175 (18%) higher compared to the city of Columbia.
