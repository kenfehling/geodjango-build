Compared to nearby areas, Summerville is substantially more expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbia, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Summerville is valued at $207,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $52,700 (33%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $9,700 (4%) lower, and it is roughly $26,700 (14%) higher compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Summerville's median rent is $1,108 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $16 (1%) lower. It is roughly $46 (4%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $175 (18%) higher compared to the city of Columbia.
