Home prices

Summerville's median home price is $207,800. This is roughly $52,700 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $9,700 (4%) lower, and it is approximately $26,700 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Summerville the median monthly rent is $1,108. It is about $16 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $46 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbia it is roughly $175 (18%) higher.
