Home prices

The price of the median home in Summerville is $207,800. This is roughly $52,700 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $9,700 (4%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $26,700 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Summerville the median monthly rent is $1,108. It is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $46 (4%) higher, and it is roughly $175 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbia.
