Compared to the surrounding areas, Hanahan is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Charleston however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hanahan costs $228,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $73,700 (47%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $11,300 (5%) higher, and it is about $101,800 (30%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In Hanahan the median monthly rent is $1,084. It is roughly $40 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $22 (2%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is about $173 (13%) lower.
