Charleston's median home price is $330,600. It is about $367,083 (52%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $113,100 (52%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation.

Rents

The median rent in Charleston is $1,257 per month. This is about $292 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $195 (18%) higher compared to the entire nation.
