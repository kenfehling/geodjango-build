Home prices

The median home price in Harleyville is $116,200. This is approximately $12,980 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $101,300 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $64,900 (35%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

The median rent in Harleyville is $813 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $71 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia is approximately $120 (12%) lower.
