Home prices

The median home in Atlantic Beach costs $189,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $93,780 (33%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $28,200 (12%) lower, and it is roughly $58,300 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Atlantic Beach the median monthly rent is $822. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $436 (34%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $240 (22%) lower, and it is about $125 (13%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
