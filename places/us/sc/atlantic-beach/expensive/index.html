Home prices

Atlantic Beach's median home value is $189,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $93,780 (33%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $28,200 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $58,300 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Atlantic Beach is $822 a month. It is approximately $436 (34%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $240 (22%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $125 (13%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
