Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Atlantic Beach is $822 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $436 (34%) lower.It is approximately $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $125 (13%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
