Home prices

The median home in Oak Grove is valued at $130,300. It is approximately $22,740 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $87,200 (40%) lower, and is roughly $90,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Oak Grove the median monthly rent is $953. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $27 (2%) higher. It is roughly $109 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $182 (16%) lower.
