The median home cost in Dentsville is $123,600. It is about $51,640 (29%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $93,900 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $96,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Dentsville the median rent is $942 per month. This is about $72 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $120 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $193 (17%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
