Compared to the surrounding areas, Northlake is significantly more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Asheville however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Northlake is $183,000. It is roughly $67,040 (57%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $34,500 (15%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $87,400 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median rent in Northlake is $857 a month. It is approximately $147 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $205 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville it is roughly $186 (17%) lower.
