Home prices

The price of the median home in Chapin is $143,200. It is roughly $32,900 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $74,300 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $77,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chapin is $1,202. This is about $143 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $140 (13%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $67 (5%) higher.
