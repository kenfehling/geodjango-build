Home prices

The value of the median home in Taylors is $165,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $2,940 (1%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $52,300 (24%) lower, and is roughly $105,200 (38%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median rent in Taylors is $917 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $22 (2%) higher.It is about $145 (13%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $126 (12%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
