Home prices

Taylors's median home value is $165,200. It is about $2,940 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $52,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is approximately $105,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

Taylors's median monthly rent is $917. It is roughly $22 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $145 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is approximately $126 (12%) lower.
