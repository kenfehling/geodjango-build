Home prices

The cost of the median home in Walhalla is $104,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $25,080 (19%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $112,900 (51%) lower, and is approximately $165,800 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median rent in Walhalla is $618 a month. This is about $54 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is roughly $425 (40%) lower.
