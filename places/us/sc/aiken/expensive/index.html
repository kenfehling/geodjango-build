Home prices

Aiken's median home price is $188,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $103,980 (122%) higher. It is about $28,900 (13%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $7,500 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Aiken the median rent is $951 per month. It is approximately $191 (25%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $111 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $18 (1%) higher.
