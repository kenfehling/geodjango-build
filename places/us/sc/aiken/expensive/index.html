Home prices

Aiken's median home value is $188,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $103,980 (122%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $28,900 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is roughly $7,500 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Aiken the median rent is $951 a month. This is roughly $191 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $111 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $18 (1%) higher compared to the city of Columbia.
