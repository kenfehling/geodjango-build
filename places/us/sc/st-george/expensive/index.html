Compared to its surrounding areas, St. George is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home in St. George is valued at $130,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $9,820 (8%) higher. It is approximately $87,500 (40%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $26,500 (16%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

St. George's median monthly rent is $760. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $302 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah it is roughly $259 (25%) lower.
