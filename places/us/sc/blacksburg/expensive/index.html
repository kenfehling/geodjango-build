Compared to its surrounding areas, Blacksburg is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Blacksburg is valued at $83,000. It is about $5,140 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $134,500 (61%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $137,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

Blacksburg's median monthly rent is $623. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $49 (7%) lower. It is about $439 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $512 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
