Compared to other places in this area, Blacksburg is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The median home price in Blacksburg is $83,000. Compared to nearby areas it is about $5,140 (5%) lower.It is about $134,500 (61%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $137,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

Blacksburg's median monthly rent is $623. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $49 (7%) lower.It is approximately $439 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $512 (45%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
