Home prices

The median home in Blacksburg is valued at $83,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $5,140 (5%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $134,500 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $137,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Blacksburg the median rent is $623 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $49 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $439 (41%) lower, and is roughly $512 (45%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
