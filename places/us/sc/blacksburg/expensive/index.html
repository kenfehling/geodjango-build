Home prices

Blacksburg's median home price is $83,000. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $5,140 (5%) lower. It is roughly $134,500 (61%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $137,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blacksburg is $623. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $49 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $439 (41%) lower, and is roughly $512 (45%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
