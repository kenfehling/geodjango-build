In comparison to the surrounding areas, Centerville is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

Centerville's median home value is $151,300. It is about $29,000 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is roughly $119,100 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Centerville the median monthly rent is $778. It is about $52 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $284 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $265 (25%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
