In comparison to its nearby areas, Centerville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Centerville is $151,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $29,000 (23%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville is approximately $119,100 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Centerville is $778 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $52 (7%) higher. It is about $284 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $265 (25%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
