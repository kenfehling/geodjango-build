Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laurens is $720. It is approximately $94 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $342 (32%) lower, and it is about $415 (36%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
