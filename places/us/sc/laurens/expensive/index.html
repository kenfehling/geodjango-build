Home prices

The median home cost in Laurens is $93,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $13,380 (16%) higher. It is roughly $123,800 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $126,600 (57%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Laurens is $720 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $94 (14%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $342 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $415 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
