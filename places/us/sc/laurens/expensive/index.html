Home prices

The median home in Laurens costs $93,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13,380 (16%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $123,800 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $126,600 (57%) lower.

Rents

Laurens's median rent is $720 a month. It is roughly $94 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $342 (32%) lower, and is roughly $415 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
