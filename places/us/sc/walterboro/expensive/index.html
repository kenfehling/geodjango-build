Rents

The median rent in Walterboro is $593 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $188 (24%) lower. It is roughly $469 (44%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is roughly $426 (41%) lower.
