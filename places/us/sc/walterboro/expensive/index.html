Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Walterboro is $593 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $188 (24%) lower.It is roughly $469 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is approximately $426 (41%) lower.
