Much like other places in this area, it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home in Walterboro is valued at $107,600. This is approximately $5,320 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $109,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $48,900 (31%) lower.

Rents

Walterboro's median monthly rent is $593. Compared to nearby areas it is about $188 (24%) lower. It is about $469 (44%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $426 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
