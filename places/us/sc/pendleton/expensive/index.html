Home prices

Pendleton's median home value is $126,200. It is about $8,380 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $91,300 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is approximately $144,200 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Pendleton the median monthly rent is $643. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $153 (19%) lower. It is approximately $419 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $400 (38%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
