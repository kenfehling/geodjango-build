Compared to other places in this area, North Augusta is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbia, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in North Augusta is $161,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $67,920 (72%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $56,400 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $20,000 (11%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

North Augusta's median monthly rent is $810. This is approximately $27 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $252 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $123 (13%) lower.
