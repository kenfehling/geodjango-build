Home prices

The median home value in North Augusta is $161,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $67,920 (72%) higher.It is approximately $56,400 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $20,000 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

North Augusta's median rent is $810 per month. This is roughly $27 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $252 (23%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $123 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
