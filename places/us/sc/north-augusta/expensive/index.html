Home prices

The median home cost in North Augusta is $161,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $67,920 (72%) higher.It is about $56,400 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $20,000 (11%) lower.

Rents

North Augusta's median rent is $810 a month. This is about $27 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $252 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbia is approximately $123 (13%) lower.
