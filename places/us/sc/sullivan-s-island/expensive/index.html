Rents

The median monthly rent in Sullivan's Island is $2,167. It is approximately $742 (52%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,105 (104%) higher, and is approximately $910 (72%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
