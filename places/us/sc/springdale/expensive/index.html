Home prices

The median home value in Springdale is $151,100. It is roughly $10,540 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $66,400 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $69,200 (31%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Springdale the median monthly rent is $978. This is roughly $74 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $84 (7%) lower, and is roughly $157 (13%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
