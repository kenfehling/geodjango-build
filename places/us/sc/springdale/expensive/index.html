Home prices

The median home price in Springdale is $151,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $10,540 (7%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $66,400 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $69,200 (31%) lower.

Rents

Springdale's median monthly rent is $978. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $74 (8%) higher.It is about $84 (7%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $157 (13%) lower.
