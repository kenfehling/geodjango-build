Home prices

Springdale's median home price is $151,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $10,540 (7%) higher. It is about $66,400 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $69,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Springdale the median monthly rent is $978. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $74 (8%) higher. It is approximately $84 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $157 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
