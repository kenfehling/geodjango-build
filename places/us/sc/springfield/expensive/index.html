Home prices

The median home in Springfield is valued at $87,900. It is approximately $5,340 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $129,600 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $93,200 (51%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Springfield the median monthly rent is $589. It is approximately $51 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $473 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is about $344 (36%) lower.
