In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lancaster is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $141,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $28,540 (25%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $75,900 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $78,700 (35%) lower.

Rents

Lancaster's median monthly rent is $695. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $117 (14%) lower. It is roughly $367 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $440 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
