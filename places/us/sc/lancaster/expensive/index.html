Home prices

Lancaster's median home price is $141,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $28,540 (25%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $75,900 (34%) lower, and is approximately $78,700 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Lancaster the median rent is $695 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $117 (14%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $367 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $440 (38%) lower.
