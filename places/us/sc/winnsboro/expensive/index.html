Home prices

The median home in Winnsboro is valued at $135,300. It is about $4,200 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $82,200 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $85,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Winnsboro is $724 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $221 (23%) lower. It is about $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $411 (36%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
