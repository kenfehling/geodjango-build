In comparison to the surrounding areas, Utica is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The median home in Utica is valued at $47,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $103,100 (68%) lower. It is about $169,600 (77%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $222,500 (82%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Utica is $869 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $176 (25%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $193 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $174 (16%) lower.
