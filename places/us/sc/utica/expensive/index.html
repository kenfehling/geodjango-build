Home prices

Utica's median home price is $47,900. It is roughly $103,100 (68%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $169,600 (77%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $222,500 (82%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Utica is $869. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $176 (25%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $193 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville it is about $174 (16%) lower.
