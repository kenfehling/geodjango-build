Home prices

The value of the median home in Berea is $114,900. This is approximately $30,320 (35%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $102,600 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is approximately $155,500 (57%) lower.

Rents

Berea's median rent is $774 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $32 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $288 (27%) lower, and is about $269 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
