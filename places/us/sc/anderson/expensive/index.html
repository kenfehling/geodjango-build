Home prices

The median home in Anderson costs $136,800. This is roughly $14,300 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $80,700 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $133,600 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Anderson's median monthly rent is $761. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $14 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $301 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $282 (27%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
