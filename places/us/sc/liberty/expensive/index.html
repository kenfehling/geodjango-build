In comparison to other places in this area, Liberty is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The median home in Liberty costs $96,800. This is about $43,200 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $120,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $173,600 (64%) lower.

Rents

In Liberty the median monthly rent is $810. This is roughly $103 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $252 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Asheville is about $233 (22%) lower.
