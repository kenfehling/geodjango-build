Home prices

The median home in Liberty is valued at $96,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $43,200 (30%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $120,700 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville is approximately $173,600 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Liberty is $810 per month. This is approximately $103 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $252 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $233 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
