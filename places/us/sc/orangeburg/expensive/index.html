Home prices

The median home cost in Orangeburg is $105,600. This is about $19,740 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $111,900 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is roughly $75,500 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Orangeburg is $699 per month. It is about $20 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $363 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $234 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
