Home prices

The median home in Orangeburg is valued at $105,600. This is roughly $19,740 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $111,900 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbia it is roughly $75,500 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Orangeburg is $699 per month. This is roughly $20 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $363 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $234 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
