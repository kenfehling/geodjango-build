Home prices

The median home value in Orangeburg is $105,600. It is roughly $19,740 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $111,900 (51%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $75,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Orangeburg's median monthly rent is $699. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $20 (3%) higher. It is about $363 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $234 (25%) lower.
