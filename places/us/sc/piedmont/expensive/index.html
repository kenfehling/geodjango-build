The value of the median home in Piedmont is $128,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $15,240 (13%) higher. It is approximately $89,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $141,900 (52%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median rent in Piedmont is $825 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $66 (8%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is roughly $218 (20%) lower.
