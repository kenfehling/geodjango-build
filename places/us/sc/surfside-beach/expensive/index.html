Home prices

The median home in Surfside Beach costs $266,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $69,400 (35%) higher. It is about $49,100 (22%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Wilmington is roughly $20,200 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Surfside Beach is $1,040 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $31 (3%) higher. It is roughly $22 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is approximately $42 (4%) higher.
