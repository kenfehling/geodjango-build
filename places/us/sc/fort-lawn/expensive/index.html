Rents

Fort Lawn's median rent is $917 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $172 (23%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $145 (13%) lower, and is roughly $218 (19%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
