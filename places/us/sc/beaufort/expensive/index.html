In comparison to the surrounding areas, Beaufort is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Savannahhowever, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Beaufort is valued at $214,700. It is approximately $44,780 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $2,800 (1%) lower, and is about $58,200 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Beaufort's median rent is $940 per month. This is approximately $100 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $122 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
