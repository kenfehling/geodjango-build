Compared to its nearby areas, Beaufort is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home cost in Beaufort is $214,700. It is approximately $44,780 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $2,800 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is approximately $58,200 (37%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beaufort is $940. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $100 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $122 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $79 (7%) lower.
