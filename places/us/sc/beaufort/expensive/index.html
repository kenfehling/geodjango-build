Home prices

The median home cost in Beaufort is $214,700. It is roughly $44,780 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $2,800 (1%) lower, and is roughly $58,200 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beaufort is $940. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $100 (9%) lower.It is about $122 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is approximately $79 (7%) lower.
