In comparison to other places in this area, Beaufort is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Savannah, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Beaufort's median home price is $214,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $44,780 (26%) higher.It is about $2,800 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $58,200 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Beaufort the median rent is $940 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $100 (9%) lower.It is roughly $122 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Savannah is about $79 (7%) lower.
