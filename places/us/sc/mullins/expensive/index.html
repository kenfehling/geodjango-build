Compared to the surrounding areas, Mullins is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mullins is $73,500. This is about $21,320 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $144,000 (66%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $57,500 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mullins is $475. This is approximately $81 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $587 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $472 (49%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
