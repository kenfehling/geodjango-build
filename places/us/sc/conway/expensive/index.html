Compared to its nearby areas, Conway is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The median home price in Conway is $155,000. This is approximately $27,300 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $24,000 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Conway the median rent is $825 a month. It is approximately $72 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $237 (22%) lower, and is roughly $122 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
