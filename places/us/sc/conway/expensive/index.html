In comparison to the surrounding areas, Conway is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Conway is valued at $155,000. It is roughly $27,300 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower, and is roughly $24,000 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Conway the median monthly rent is $825. It is roughly $72 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $237 (22%) lower, and is approximately $122 (12%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
