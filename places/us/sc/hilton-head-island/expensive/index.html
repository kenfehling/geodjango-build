Compared to the surrounding areas, Hilton Head Island is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Savannah, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hilton Head Island costs $483,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $175,500 (56%) higher.It is about $266,100 (122%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $327,100 (209%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Hilton Head Island the median monthly rent is $1,271. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $209 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Savannah is roughly $252 (24%) higher.
