In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hilton Head Island is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Savannah, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hilton Head Island is $483,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $175,500 (56%) higher. It is approximately $266,100 (122%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $327,100 (209%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Hilton Head Island the median rent is $1,271 a month. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is about $209 (19%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $252 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.
