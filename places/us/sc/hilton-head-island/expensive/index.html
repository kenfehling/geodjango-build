Home prices

The median home price in Hilton Head Island is $483,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $175,500 (56%) higher.It is roughly $266,100 (122%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $327,100 (209%) higher.

Rents

Hilton Head Island's median monthly rent is $1,271. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $209 (19%) higher, and it is roughly $252 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.
