Compared to other places in this area, Hilton Head Island is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home in Hilton Head Island is valued at $483,600. It is roughly $175,500 (56%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $266,100 (122%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is roughly $327,100 (209%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hilton Head Island is $1,271. In comparison to its nearby areas this is is very similar. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $209 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $252 (24%) higher.
