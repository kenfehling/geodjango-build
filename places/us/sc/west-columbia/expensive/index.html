Home prices

Rents

In West Columbia the median monthly rent is $865. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $84 (8%) lower.It is approximately $197 (18%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $270 (23%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
