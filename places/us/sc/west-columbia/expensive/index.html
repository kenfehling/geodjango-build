Home prices

The price of the median home in West Columbia is $142,800. It is approximately $10,420 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $74,700 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $77,500 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in West Columbia is $865 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $84 (8%) lower. It is roughly $197 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $270 (23%) lower.
