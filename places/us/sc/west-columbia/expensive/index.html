Home prices

The median home in West Columbia costs $142,800. This is roughly $10,420 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $74,700 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $77,500 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

West Columbia's median monthly rent is $865. This is approximately $84 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $197 (18%) lower, and is roughly $270 (23%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
