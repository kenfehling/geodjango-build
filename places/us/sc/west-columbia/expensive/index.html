Home prices

The median home in West Columbia is valued at $142,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $10,420 (7%) higher.It is roughly $74,700 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $77,500 (35%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Columbia is $865. It is approximately $84 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $197 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $270 (23%) lower.
