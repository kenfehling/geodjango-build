Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Myrtle Beach is $266,200. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $48,700 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $135,200 (103%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In North Myrtle Beach the median rent is $1,110 a month. This is about $91 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $48 (4%) higher, and it is roughly $163 (17%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.
