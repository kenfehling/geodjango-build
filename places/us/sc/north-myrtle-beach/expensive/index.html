Home prices

The value of the median home in North Myrtle Beach is $266,200. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $48,700 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $135,200 (103%) higher.

Rents

In North Myrtle Beach the median rent is $1,110 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $91 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $48 (4%) higher, and it is about $163 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
