Home prices

Rents

North Myrtle Beach's median monthly rent is $1,110. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $91 (7%) lower.It is approximately $48 (4%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $163 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
