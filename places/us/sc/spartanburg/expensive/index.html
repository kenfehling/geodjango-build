Home prices

The median home in Spartanburg is valued at $125,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $72,120 (134%) higher. It is about $91,900 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $94,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Spartanburg the median rent is $810 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $53 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $252 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $325 (28%) lower.
