Home prices

The cost of the median home in Spartanburg is $125,600. It is approximately $72,120 (134%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $91,900 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $94,700 (42%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Spartanburg's median monthly rent is $810. This is approximately $53 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $252 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte is approximately $325 (28%) lower.
