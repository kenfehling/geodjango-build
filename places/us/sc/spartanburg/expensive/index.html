Home prices

The median home in Spartanburg costs $125,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $72,120 (134%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $91,900 (42%) lower, and is about $94,700 (42%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Spartanburg is $810. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $53 (7%) higher.It is roughly $252 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $325 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
