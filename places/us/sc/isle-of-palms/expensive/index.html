In comparison to its surrounding areas, Isle of Palms is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The median home in Isle of Palms is valued at $852,700. This is about $194,640 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $635,200 (292%) higher, and compared to the city of Charleston is approximately $522,100 (157%) higher.

Rents

In Isle of Palms the median rent is $1,994 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $538 (36%) higher. It is about $932 (87%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $737 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Charleston.
