Home prices

The median home in Hampton is valued at $115,200. It is roughly $48,240 (72%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $102,300 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $41,300 (26%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Hampton's median rent is $646 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $416 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah is roughly $373 (36%) lower.
