Similar to its surrounding areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Charleston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in North Charleston is $174,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $2,880 (1%) lower.It is roughly $42,700 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Charleston is roughly $155,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

In North Charleston the median monthly rent is $1,051. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $47 (4%) lower.It is about $11 (1%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $206 (16%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
