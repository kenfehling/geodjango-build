Much like nearby areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The price of the median home in North Charleston is $174,800. This is roughly $2,880 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $42,700 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is roughly $155,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

North Charleston's median rent is $1,051 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $47 (4%) lower. It is roughly $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $206 (16%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
