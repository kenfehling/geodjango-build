Much like its surrounding areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Charleston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Charleston is valued at $174,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $2,880 (1%) lower. It is about $42,700 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $155,800 (47%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Charleston is $1,051. This is about $47 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $11 (1%) lower, and it is roughly $206 (16%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
