Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chester is $580. This is roughly $101 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $482 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $555 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
