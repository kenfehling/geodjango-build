Home prices

Columbia's median home value is $181,100. This is approximately $11,480 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $36,400 (16%) lower, and is approximately $39,200 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Columbia's median monthly rent is $933. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $36 (4%) higher. It is roughly $129 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $202 (17%) lower.
