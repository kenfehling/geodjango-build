Home prices

Columbia's median home cost is $181,100. This is roughly $11,480 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $36,400 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $39,200 (17%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Columbia the median monthly rent is $933. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $36 (4%) higher.It is approximately $129 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $202 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
