Home prices

Columbia's median home cost is $181,100. This is roughly $11,480 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $36,400 (16%) lower, and is approximately $39,200 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Columbia's median monthly rent is $933. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $36 (4%) higher.It is approximately $129 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $202 (17%) lower.
