Home prices

Columbia's median home cost is $181,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $11,480 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $36,400 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $39,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

Columbia's median monthly rent is $933. This is about $36 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $129 (12%) lower, and it is roughly $202 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
