Home prices

The median home in Cayce is valued at $129,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $14,900 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $87,600 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $90,400 (41%) lower.

Rents

Cayce's median rent is $1,001 a month. It is approximately $101 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $61 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $134 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
