Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lynchburg is $73,600. This is approximately $17,460 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $143,900 (66%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $146,700 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lynchburg is $429. This is about $200 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $633 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $706 (62%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
