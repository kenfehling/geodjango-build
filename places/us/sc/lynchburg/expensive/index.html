In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lynchburg is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg costs $73,600. This is approximately $17,460 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $143,900 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $146,700 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Lynchburg the median rent is $429 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $200 (31%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $633 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $706 (62%) lower.
