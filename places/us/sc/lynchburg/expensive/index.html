In comparison to nearby areas, Lynchburg is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg is valued at $73,600. It is about $17,460 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $143,900 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $146,700 (66%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lynchburg is $429 a month. This is roughly $200 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $633 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $706 (62%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
