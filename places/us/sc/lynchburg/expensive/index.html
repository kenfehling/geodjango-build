Rents

Lynchburg's median monthly rent is $429. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $200 (31%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $633 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $706 (62%) lower.
