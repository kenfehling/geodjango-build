Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg costs $73,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $17,460 (19%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $143,900 (66%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $146,700 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Lynchburg the median monthly rent is $429. This is approximately $200 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $633 (59%) lower, and it is about $706 (62%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
