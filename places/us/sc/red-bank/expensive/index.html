Home prices

The median home in Red Bank is valued at $127,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $26,540 (17%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $89,700 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $92,500 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Red Bank the median rent is $744 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $196 (20%) lower. It is roughly $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $391 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
