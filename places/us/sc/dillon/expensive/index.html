Home prices

Dillon's median home cost is $118,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $34,700 (41%) higher. It is approximately $99,000 (45%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $12,500 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Dillon the median rent is $618 per month. It is roughly $108 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $329 (34%) lower.
