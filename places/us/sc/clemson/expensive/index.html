Compared to the surrounding areas, Clemson is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Asheville however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Clemson's median home value is $236,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $128,980 (119%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $19,000 (8%) higher, and it is about $33,900 (12%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Clemson's median monthly rent is $928. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $222 (31%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $134 (12%) lower, and is about $115 (11%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
