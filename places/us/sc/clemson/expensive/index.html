In comparison to its nearby areas, Clemson is significantly more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The median home value in Clemson is $236,500. It is approximately $128,980 (119%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $19,000 (8%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $33,900 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clemson is $928 per month. It is about $222 (31%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $134 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $115 (11%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
