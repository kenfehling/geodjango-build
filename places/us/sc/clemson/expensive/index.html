Compared to the surrounding areas, Clemson is substantially more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Asheville however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Clemson costs $236,500. This is roughly $128,980 (119%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $19,000 (8%) higher, and it is about $33,900 (12%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Clemson the median rent is $928 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $222 (31%) higher.It is about $134 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is about $115 (11%) lower.
