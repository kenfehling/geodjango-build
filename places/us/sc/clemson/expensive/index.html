Compared to other places in this area, Clemson is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The median home in Clemson costs $236,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $128,980 (119%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $19,000 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Asheville is approximately $33,900 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Clemson the median rent is $928 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $222 (31%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $134 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville is about $115 (11%) lower.
