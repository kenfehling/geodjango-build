Home prices

The median home price in Georgetown is $163,300. This is roughly $186,380 (53%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $54,200 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is about $167,300 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Georgetown is $735. It is about $345 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $327 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $522 (41%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
