Home prices

Rents

Port Royal's median monthly rent is $1,220. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $142 (13%) higher.It is roughly $158 (14%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $201 (19%) higher.
