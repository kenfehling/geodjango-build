Home prices

The median home in Port Royal costs $233,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $2,840 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $15,800 (7%) higher, and is about $76,800 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Port Royal is $1,220 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $142 (13%) higher. It is approximately $158 (14%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $201 (19%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.
