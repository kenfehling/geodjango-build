Home prices

The price of the median home in Port Royal is $233,300. This is roughly $2,840 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $15,800 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $76,800 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Port Royal is $1,220 a month. This is approximately $142 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $158 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $201 (19%) higher.
