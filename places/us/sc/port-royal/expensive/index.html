Home prices

Port Royal's median home cost is $233,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $2,840 (1%) higher. It is approximately $15,800 (7%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $76,800 (49%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Port Royal is $1,220 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $142 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $158 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Savannah it is approximately $201 (19%) higher.
