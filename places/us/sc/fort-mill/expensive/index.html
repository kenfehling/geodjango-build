Rents

The median rent in Fort Mill is $1,073 a month. This is approximately $495 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $62 (5%) lower.
