In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fort Mill is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Charlotte, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Mill is valued at $289,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $22,340 (7%) lower. It is about $71,700 (32%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $68,900 (31%) higher.

Rents

Fort Mill's median rent is $1,073 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $495 (31%) lower. It is roughly $11 (1%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $62 (5%) lower.
