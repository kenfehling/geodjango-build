Home prices

Camden's median home price is $159,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $28,880 (22%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $57,800 (26%) lower, and it is about $60,600 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Camden's median monthly rent is $671. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $90 (11%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $391 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $464 (40%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
