Compared to other places in this area, Trenton is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Columbia, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Trenton is $122,200. This is approximately $24,240 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $95,300 (43%) lower, and is approximately $58,900 (32%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $545 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $155 (22%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $517 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia it is approximately $388 (41%) lower.
