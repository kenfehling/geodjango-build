Compared to other places in this area, Trenton is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Columbia.

Home prices

The median home in Trenton is valued at $122,200. This is approximately $24,240 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $95,300 (43%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $58,900 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Trenton the median rent is $545 a month. It is about $155 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $517 (48%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $388 (41%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
