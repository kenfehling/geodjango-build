Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Trenton is $545 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $155 (22%) lower.It is roughly $517 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $388 (41%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
