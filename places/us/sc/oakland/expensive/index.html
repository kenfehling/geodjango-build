Compared to its surrounding areas, Oakland is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Oakland's median home price is $94,200. It is approximately $21,160 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $123,300 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $126,100 (57%) lower.

Rents

Oakland's median rent is $758 per month. This is roughly $112 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $304 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $377 (33%) lower.
