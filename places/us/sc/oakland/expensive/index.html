In comparison to other places in this area, Oakland is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The median home value in Oakland is $94,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $21,160 (18%) lower.It is about $123,300 (56%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $126,100 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Oakland's median monthly rent is $758. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $112 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $304 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $377 (33%) lower.
