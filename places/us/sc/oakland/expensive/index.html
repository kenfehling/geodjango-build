Compared to nearby areas, Oakland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oakland is $94,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $21,160 (18%) lower. It is about $123,300 (56%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $126,100 (57%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Oakland the median monthly rent is $758. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $112 (12%) lower. It is approximately $304 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $377 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
