Compared to its nearby areas, Florence is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Fayetteville however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Florence is $158,600. This is roughly $72,820 (84%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $58,900 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $27,600 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Florence the median monthly rent is $780. This is approximately $178 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $282 (26%) lower, and is approximately $167 (17%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
