Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $158,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $72,820 (84%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $58,900 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $27,600 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median rent in Florence is $780 per month. This is roughly $178 (29%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $282 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $167 (17%) lower.
