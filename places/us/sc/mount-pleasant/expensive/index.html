Home prices

The median home price in Mount Pleasant is $461,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $207,180 (31%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $243,500 (111%) higher, and it is about $130,400 (39%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median rent is $1,629 a month. It is roughly $93 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $567 (53%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charleston is approximately $372 (29%) higher.
