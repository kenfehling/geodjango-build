Home prices

The median home price in Mount Pleasant is $461,000. It is approximately $207,180 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $243,500 (111%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $130,400 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In Mount Pleasant the median rent is $1,629 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $93 (6%) higher.It is roughly $567 (53%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $372 (29%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
