Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant is valued at $461,000. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $207,180 (31%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $243,500 (111%) higher, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $130,400 (39%) higher.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median rent is $1,629 a month. This is about $93 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $567 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Charleston is approximately $372 (29%) higher.
