Home prices

The median home in Bethune costs $81,500. It is about $25,520 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $136,000 (62%) lower, and is about $138,800 (63%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Bethune the median rent is $775 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $90 (13%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $287 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $360 (31%) lower.
