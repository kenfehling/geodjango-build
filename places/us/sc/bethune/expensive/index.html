Compared to its surrounding areas, Bethune is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bethune costs $81,500. It is roughly $25,520 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $136,000 (62%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $138,800 (63%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Bethune's median monthly rent is $775. This is approximately $90 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and is about $360 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
