Home prices

Hollywood's median home value is $246,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $294,000 (54%) lower.It is roughly $29,000 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is approximately $90,000 (57%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hollywood is $726 a month. It is about $561 (43%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $336 (31%) lower, and is roughly $293 (28%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
