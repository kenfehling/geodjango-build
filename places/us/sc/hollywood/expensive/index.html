Home prices

The median home price in Hollywood is $246,500. This is approximately $294,000 (54%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $29,000 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $90,000 (57%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Hollywood the median monthly rent is $726. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $561 (43%) lower. It is approximately $336 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $293 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
