Compared to its surrounding areas, Seneca is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Asheville, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Seneca is $158,000. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $36,120 (29%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $59,500 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $112,400 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Seneca is $693. Compared to other places in this area it is about $27 (3%) lower. It is roughly $369 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $350 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
