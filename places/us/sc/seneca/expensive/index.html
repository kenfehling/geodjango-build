Compared to its surrounding areas, Seneca is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Asheville, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Seneca costs $158,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $36,120 (29%) higher. It is roughly $59,500 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $112,400 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median rent in Seneca is $693 a month. It is roughly $27 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $369 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $350 (33%) lower.
