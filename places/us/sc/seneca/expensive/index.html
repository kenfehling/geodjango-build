Home prices

The value of the median home in Seneca is $158,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $36,120 (29%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $59,500 (27%) lower, and is roughly $112,400 (41%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Seneca the median rent is $693 per month. It is about $27 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $369 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $350 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
