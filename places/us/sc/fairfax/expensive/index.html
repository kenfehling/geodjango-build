In comparison to other places in this area, Fairfax is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Savannah, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Fairfax's median home cost is $54,000. This is approximately $15,960 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $163,500 (75%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $102,500 (65%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Fairfax the median rent is $621 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $15 (2%) lower.It is about $441 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Savannah is about $398 (39%) lower.
