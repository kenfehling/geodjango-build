Compared to the surrounding areas, Fairfax is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Savannah.

Home prices

The median home price in Fairfax is $54,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,960 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $163,500 (75%) lower, and is approximately $102,500 (65%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Fairfax's median monthly rent is $621. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $15 (2%) lower. It is approximately $441 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $398 (39%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
