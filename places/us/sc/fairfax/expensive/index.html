Home prices

The median home in Fairfax is valued at $54,000. It is approximately $15,960 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $163,500 (75%) lower, and is approximately $102,500 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Fairfax the median monthly rent is $621. It is approximately $15 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $441 (41%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is approximately $398 (39%) lower.
