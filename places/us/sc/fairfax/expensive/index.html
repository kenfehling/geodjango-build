Home prices

The median home value in Fairfax is $54,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $15,960 (22%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $163,500 (75%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah is about $102,500 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Fairfax the median monthly rent is $621. This is roughly $15 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $441 (41%) lower, and it is about $398 (39%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
