Home prices

The median home cost in Cane Savannah is $101,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $15,200 (17%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $115,800 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $118,600 (53%) lower.

Rents

Cane Savannah's median monthly rent is $820. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $50 (6%) higher. It is approximately $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $315 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
