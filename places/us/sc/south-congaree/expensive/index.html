The median home in South Congaree is valued at $130,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $10,940 (7%) lower. It is roughly $86,800 (39%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $89,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Congaree is $786. It is roughly $133 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $276 (25%) lower, and is roughly $349 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
