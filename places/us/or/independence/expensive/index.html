Home prices

The median home cost in Independence is $205,000. It is roughly $13,780 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $12,500 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $207,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Independence the median monthly rent is $1,048. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $87 (9%) higher.It is roughly $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $200 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
