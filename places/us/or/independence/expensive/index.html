Home prices

The median home cost in Independence is $205,000. It is roughly $13,780 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $12,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $207,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Independence the median rent is $1,048 a month. It is approximately $87 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $14 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $200 (16%) lower.
