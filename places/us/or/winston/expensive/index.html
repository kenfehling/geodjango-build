Compared to its nearby areas, Winston is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Eugene, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Winston is $159,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $31,580 (16%) lower. It is about $58,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $129,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.

Rents

The median rent in Winston is $828 a month. It is about $72 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $234 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $203 (19%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
