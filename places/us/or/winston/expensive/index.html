Home prices

The median home in Winston is valued at $159,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $31,580 (16%) lower.It is approximately $58,400 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $129,500 (44%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Winston's median monthly rent is $828. This is approximately $72 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $234 (22%) lower, and is about $203 (19%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
