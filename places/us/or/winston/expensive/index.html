In comparison to the surrounding areas, Winston is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Eugene.

Home prices

Winston's median home price is $159,100. This is roughly $31,580 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $58,400 (26%) lower, and is about $129,500 (44%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

The median rent in Winston is $828 per month. This is approximately $72 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $234 (22%) lower, and it is about $203 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
