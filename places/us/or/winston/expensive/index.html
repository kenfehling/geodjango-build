Compared to the surrounding areas, Winston is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Eugene, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Winston is $159,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $31,580 (16%) lower. It is about $58,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Eugene it is about $129,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

Winston's median monthly rent is $828. It is about $72 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $234 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Eugene it is roughly $203 (19%) lower.
