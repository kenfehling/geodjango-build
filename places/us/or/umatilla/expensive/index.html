In comparison to other places in this area, Umatilla is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Kennewick, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Umatilla costs $137,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $10,920 (8%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $79,900 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Kennewick it is approximately $77,900 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Umatilla is $690 per month. It is approximately $106 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $372 (35%) lower, and is roughly $232 (25%) lower compared to the city of Kennewick.
