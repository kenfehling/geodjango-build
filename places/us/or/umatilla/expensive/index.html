In comparison to the surrounding areas, Umatilla is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kennewick, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Umatilla is $137,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $10,920 (8%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $79,900 (36%) lower, and is roughly $77,900 (36%) lower compared to the city of Kennewick.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Umatilla is $690. It is about $106 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $372 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kennewick is about $232 (25%) lower.
