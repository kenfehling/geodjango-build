Compared to other places in this area, St. Paul is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Paul is $330,600. This is about $80,820 (32%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $113,100 (52%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $81,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Paul is $1,125. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $140 (11%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $123 (9%) lower.
