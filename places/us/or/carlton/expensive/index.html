Compared to other places in this area, Carlton is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Carlton is $276,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $22,540 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $58,700 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $135,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Carlton's median rent is $1,239 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $41 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $177 (16%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Portland.
