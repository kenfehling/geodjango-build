Home prices

The median home in Forest Grove is valued at $319,200. This is about $18,800 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $101,700 (46%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $92,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Forest Grove is $1,081. It is roughly $59 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $19 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $167 (13%) lower.
