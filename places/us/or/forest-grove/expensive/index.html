In comparison to other places in this area, Forest Grove is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Forest Grove's median home value is $319,200. This is approximately $18,800 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $101,700 (46%) higher, and it is about $92,800 (22%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Forest Grove is $1,081. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $59 (5%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $19 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $167 (13%) lower.
