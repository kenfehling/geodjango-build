In comparison to the surrounding areas, Forest Grove is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Forest Grove costs $319,200. This is approximately $18,800 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $101,700 (46%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $92,800 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Forest Grove the median rent is $1,081 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $59 (5%) lower.It is roughly $19 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Portland is about $167 (13%) lower.
