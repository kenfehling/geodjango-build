Home prices

The median home value in Forest Grove is $319,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $18,800 (6%) higher. It is about $101,700 (46%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $92,800 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Forest Grove is $1,081 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $59 (5%) lower. It is roughly $19 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $167 (13%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
