In comparison to other places in this area, Forest Grove is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Forest Grove is valued at $319,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $18,800 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $101,700 (46%) higher, and it is about $92,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Forest Grove's median monthly rent is $1,081. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $59 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $19 (1%) higher, and it is roughly $167 (13%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
