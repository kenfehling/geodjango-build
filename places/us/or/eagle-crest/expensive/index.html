Home prices

The cost of the median home in Eagle Crest is $467,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $128,360 (37%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $249,600 (114%) higher, and is approximately $82,300 (21%) higher compared to the city of Bend.

Rents

Eagle Crest's median rent is $1,742 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $585 (50%) higher. It is approximately $680 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Bend it is roughly $466 (36%) higher.
