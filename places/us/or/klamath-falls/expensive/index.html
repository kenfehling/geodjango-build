In comparison to its surrounding areas, Klamath Falls is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Medford, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Klamath Falls costs $159,500. Compared to nearby areas it is about $34,140 (27%) higher. It is about $58,000 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $105,900 (39%) lower compared to the city of Medford.

Rents

In Klamath Falls the median monthly rent is $765. This is about $44 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $297 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $207 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Medford.
