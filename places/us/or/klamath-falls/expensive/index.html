Compared to other places in this area, Klamath Falls is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Medford.

Home prices

The median home in Klamath Falls is valued at $159,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $34,140 (27%) higher. It is approximately $58,000 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $105,900 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Medford.

Rents

In Klamath Falls the median rent is $765 per month. This is approximately $44 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $297 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Medford it is roughly $207 (21%) lower.
