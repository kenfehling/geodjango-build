Home prices

The median home in McMinnville is valued at $247,400. This is roughly $10,560 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $29,900 (13%) higher, and is approximately $164,600 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In McMinnville the median rent is $987 a month. This is about $198 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $75 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $261 (20%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
