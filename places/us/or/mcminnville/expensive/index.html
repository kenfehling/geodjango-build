Home prices

Rents

In McMinnville the median rent is $987 a month. It is about $198 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $75 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $261 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
