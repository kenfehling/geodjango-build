Home prices

The median home in Coburg is valued at $306,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $84,260 (37%) higher.It is about $88,500 (40%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $106,000 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Coburg the median monthly rent is $856. It is approximately $87 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $206 (19%) lower, and it is about $392 (31%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
