Home prices

The value of the median home in Hillsboro is $342,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $62,980 (15%) lower.It is about $125,000 (57%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $69,500 (16%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $1,453 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $60 (4%) higher.It is about $391 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $205 (16%) higher.
