Home prices

The median home cost in Hillsboro is $342,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $62,980 (15%) lower. It is approximately $125,000 (57%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $69,500 (16%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hillsboro is $1,453. It is approximately $60 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $391 (36%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $205 (16%) higher.
