In comparison to nearby areas, Hillsboro is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hillsboro is $342,500. This is about $62,980 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $125,000 (57%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $69,500 (16%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hillsboro the median monthly rent is $1,453. This is approximately $60 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $391 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $205 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
