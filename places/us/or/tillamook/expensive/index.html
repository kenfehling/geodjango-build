Home prices

The median home cost in Tillamook is $189,700. This is roughly $59,140 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $27,800 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $222,300 (53%) lower.

Rents

Tillamook's median monthly rent is $792. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $125 (13%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $456 (36%) lower.
