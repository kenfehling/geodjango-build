Home prices

The median home in Tillamook costs $189,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $59,140 (23%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $27,800 (12%) lower, and is about $222,300 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Tillamook's median monthly rent is $792. This is about $125 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and is about $456 (36%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
