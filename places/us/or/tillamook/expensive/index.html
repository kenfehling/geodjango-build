Home prices

The median home in Tillamook is valued at $189,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $59,140 (23%) lower. It is approximately $27,800 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $222,300 (53%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Tillamook's median rent is $792 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $125 (13%) lower. It is about $270 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $456 (36%) lower.
