Home prices

The value of the median home in Tillamook is $189,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $59,140 (23%) lower. It is about $27,800 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $222,300 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Tillamook the median rent is $792 per month. This is about $125 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and is roughly $456 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
