In comparison to other places in this area, Happy Valley is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Portland, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Happy Valley costs $508,400. This is approximately $165,560 (48%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $290,900 (133%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $96,400 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Happy Valley is $1,598. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $282 (21%) higher.It is approximately $536 (50%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $350 (28%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
