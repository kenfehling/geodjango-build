Compared to other places in this area, Happy Valley is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Portland, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Happy Valley is $508,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $165,560 (48%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $290,900 (133%) higher, and it is about $96,400 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Happy Valley's median monthly rent is $1,598. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $282 (21%) higher. It is roughly $536 (50%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $350 (28%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
