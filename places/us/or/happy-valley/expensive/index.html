Compared to its surrounding areas, Happy Valley is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Portland, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Happy Valley costs $508,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $165,560 (48%) higher.It is approximately $290,900 (133%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $96,400 (23%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Happy Valley the median rent is $1,598 per month. This is roughly $282 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $536 (50%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $350 (28%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
