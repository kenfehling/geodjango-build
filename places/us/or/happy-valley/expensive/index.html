Home prices

The median home price in Happy Valley is $508,400. This is about $165,560 (48%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $290,900 (133%) higher, and is approximately $96,400 (23%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Happy Valley the median monthly rent is $1,598. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $282 (21%) higher. It is about $536 (50%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $350 (28%) higher.
