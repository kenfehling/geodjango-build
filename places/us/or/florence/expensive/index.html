Home prices

The median home price in Florence is $224,300. It is about $43,300 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $6,800 (3%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $64,300 (22%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Florence's median monthly rent is $885. Compared to nearby areas this is about $33 (3%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $177 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Eugene is approximately $146 (14%) lower.
