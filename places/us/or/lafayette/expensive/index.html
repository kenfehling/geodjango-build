In comparison to other places in this area, Lafayette is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Lafayette is valued at $246,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $24,940 (9%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $28,500 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $166,000 (40%) lower.

Rents

Lafayette's median rent is $1,097 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $119 (9%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $35 (3%) higher, and is roughly $151 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
