Compared to its nearby areas, Lafayette is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Lafayette's median home value is $246,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $24,940 (9%) lower. It is roughly $28,500 (13%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $166,000 (40%) lower.

Rents

Lafayette's median monthly rent is $1,097. It is about $119 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $35 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $151 (12%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
