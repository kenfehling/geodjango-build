Rents

The median rent in Lafayette is $1,097 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $119 (9%) lower. It is roughly $35 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $151 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
