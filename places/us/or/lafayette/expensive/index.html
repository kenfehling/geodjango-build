Home prices

Lafayette's median home cost is $246,000. This is about $24,940 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $28,500 (13%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $166,000 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Lafayette the median monthly rent is $1,097. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $119 (9%) lower. It is roughly $35 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is about $151 (12%) lower.
