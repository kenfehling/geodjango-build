Compared to its surrounding areas, Nyssa is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boise City, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nyssa is valued at $102,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $12,060 (10%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $114,900 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Boise City it is approximately $150,800 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Nyssa is $634 a month. This is approximately $24 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $428 (40%) lower, and is approximately $323 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Boise City.
