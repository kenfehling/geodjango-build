Home prices

The price of the median home in Sheridan is $171,000. This is about $32,280 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $46,500 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $241,000 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Sheridan the median rent is $931 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $49 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $131 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $317 (25%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
