Home prices

The median home value in Mount Hood Village is $318,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $3,200 (1%) higher. It is about $101,400 (46%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $93,100 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Mount Hood Village the median monthly rent is $1,028. It is approximately $185 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $34 (3%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $220 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
