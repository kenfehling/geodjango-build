Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Hood Village is $1,028. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $185 (15%) lower.It is about $34 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $220 (17%) lower.
