Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Amity is $1,194 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $136 (12%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $132 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $54 (4%) lower.
