Home prices

The price of the median home in Arlington is $95,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $27,320 (22%) lower.It is approximately $122,300 (56%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Yakima it is approximately $77,800 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Arlington is $994. This is about $232 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $68 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is roughly $174 (21%) higher.
