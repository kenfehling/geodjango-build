Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $427,400. This is about $169,040 (65%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $209,900 (96%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Medford it is about $162,000 (61%) higher.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $1,147. It is roughly $145 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $85 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Medford is about $175 (18%) higher.
