Compared to the surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Medford, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Jacksonville is $427,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $169,040 (65%) higher.It is approximately $209,900 (96%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $162,000 (61%) higher compared to the city of Medford.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $1,147. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $145 (14%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $85 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Medford is about $175 (18%) higher.
