Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Jacksonville is $1,147 a month. It is approximately $145 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $85 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $175 (18%) higher compared to the city of Medford.
