Compared to its surrounding areas, Jacksonville is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Medford.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $427,400. This is roughly $169,040 (65%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $209,900 (96%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $162,000 (61%) higher compared to the city of Medford.

Rents

Jacksonville's median rent is $1,147 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $145 (14%) higher.It is approximately $85 (8%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $175 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Medford.
