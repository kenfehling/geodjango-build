Compared to its surrounding areas, Lyons is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Lyons's median home price is $208,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $13,620 (6%) lower. It is approximately $9,200 (4%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $203,700 (49%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Lyons is $794 a month. It is approximately $163 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $268 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $454 (36%) lower.
