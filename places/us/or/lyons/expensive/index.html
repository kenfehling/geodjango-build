Compared to its surrounding areas, Lyons is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lyons is valued at $208,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $13,620 (6%) lower. It is about $9,200 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $203,700 (49%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Lyons is $794 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $163 (17%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $268 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $454 (36%) lower.
