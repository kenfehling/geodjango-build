Compared to other places in this area, North Bend is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Eugene, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Bend costs $202,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $45,820 (29%) higher. It is approximately $15,200 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $86,300 (29%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

North Bend's median rent is $904 per month. This is about $114 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $158 (14%) lower, and is about $127 (12%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
