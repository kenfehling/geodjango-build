Home prices

The median home in North Bend is valued at $202,300. It is roughly $45,820 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $15,200 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Eugene is roughly $86,300 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in North Bend is $904 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $114 (14%) higher. It is about $158 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Eugene is about $127 (12%) lower.
