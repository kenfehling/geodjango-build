Home prices

The median home in North Bend costs $202,300. It is approximately $45,820 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $15,200 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $86,300 (29%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

North Bend's median rent is $904 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $114 (14%) higher.It is about $158 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Eugene it is roughly $127 (12%) lower.
