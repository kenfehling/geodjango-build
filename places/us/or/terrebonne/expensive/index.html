In comparison to other places in this area, Terrebonne is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Bend however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Terrebonne's median home price is $231,800. This is about $40,320 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $14,300 (6%) higher, and it is roughly $153,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Bend.

Rents

The median rent in Terrebonne is $917 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $231 (20%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $145 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Bend it is roughly $359 (28%) lower.
