Compared to the surrounding areas, Cornelius is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cornelius is $263,000. It is approximately $48,640 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $45,500 (20%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $149,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cornelius is $1,102 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $33 (2%) lower. It is approximately $40 (3%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $146 (11%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
