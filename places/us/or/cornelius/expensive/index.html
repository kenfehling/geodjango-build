In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cornelius is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cornelius is $263,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $48,640 (15%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $45,500 (20%) higher, and is roughly $149,000 (36%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Cornelius's median monthly rent is $1,102. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $33 (2%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $40 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $146 (11%) lower.
