Home prices

The median home in Cornelius is valued at $263,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $48,640 (15%) lower.It is roughly $45,500 (20%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $149,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cornelius is $1,102. It is approximately $33 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $40 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $146 (11%) lower.
