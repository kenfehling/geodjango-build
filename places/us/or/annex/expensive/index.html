The median home in Annex is valued at $122,700. This is approximately $8,760 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $94,800 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boise City is approximately $130,700 (51%) lower.

Rents
