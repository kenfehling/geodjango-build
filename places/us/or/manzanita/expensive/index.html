Home prices

The median home in Manzanita is valued at $456,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $142,860 (45%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $238,500 (109%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $44,000 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Manzanita is $1,050 a month. This is roughly $185 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $12 (1%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $198 (15%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
