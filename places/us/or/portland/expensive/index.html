Home prices

The median home price in Portland is $412,000. This is about $92,117 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $194,500 (89%) higher.

Rents

In Portland the median monthly rent is $1,248. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $87 (6%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $186 (17%) higher.
