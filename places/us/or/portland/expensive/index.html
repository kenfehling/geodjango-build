Compared to its surrounding areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $412,000. It is about $92,117 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $194,500 (89%) higher compared to the entire nation.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $1,248. This is roughly $87 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $186 (17%) higher.
