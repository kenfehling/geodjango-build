In comparison to the surrounding areas, Portland is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

Portland's median home cost is $412,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $92,117 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $194,500 (89%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Portland is $1,248 a month. This is roughly $87 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $186 (17%) higher.
