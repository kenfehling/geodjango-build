Compared to its surrounding areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

Portland's median home value is $412,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $92,117 (18%) lower.It is roughly $194,500 (89%) higher compared to the US as a whole.

Rents

The median rent in Portland is $1,248 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $87 (6%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $186 (17%) higher.
