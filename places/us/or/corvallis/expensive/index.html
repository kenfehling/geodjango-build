Compared to the surrounding areas, Corvallis is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Portlandhowever, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corvallis costs $330,500. This is roughly $73,200 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $113,000 (51%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $81,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Corvallis's median monthly rent is $1,091. This is about $91 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $29 (2%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $157 (12%) lower.
