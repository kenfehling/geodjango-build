Compared to its nearby areas, Corvallis is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corvallis costs $330,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $73,200 (28%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $113,000 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $81,500 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Corvallis the median rent is $1,091 a month. This is roughly $91 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $29 (2%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $157 (12%) lower.
