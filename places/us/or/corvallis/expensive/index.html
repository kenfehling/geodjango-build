Compared to the surrounding areas, Corvallis is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Corvallis's median home cost is $330,500. It is about $73,200 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $113,000 (51%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $81,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Corvallis is $1,091 a month. This is approximately $91 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $29 (2%) higher, and is about $157 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
