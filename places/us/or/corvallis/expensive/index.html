Compared to nearby areas, Corvallis is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Corvallis is valued at $330,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $73,200 (28%) higher. It is approximately $113,000 (51%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $81,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Corvallis's median rent is $1,091 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $91 (7%) lower. It is approximately $29 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $157 (12%) lower.
