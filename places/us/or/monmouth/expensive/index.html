Home prices

The median home in Monmouth costs $225,100. This is approximately $17,740 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $7,600 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $186,900 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Monmouth the median monthly rent is $1,014. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $62 (5%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $48 (4%) lower, and it is roughly $234 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
