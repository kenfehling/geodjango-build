Home prices

The value of the median home in Monmouth is $225,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17,740 (8%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $7,600 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $186,900 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Monmouth the median rent is $1,014 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $62 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $48 (4%) lower, and it is approximately $234 (18%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
