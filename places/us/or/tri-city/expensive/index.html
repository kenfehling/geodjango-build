In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tri-City is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Eugene, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tri-City is valued at $152,800. It is approximately $11,680 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $64,700 (29%) lower, and is approximately $135,800 (47%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Tri-City's median monthly rent is $830. Compared to other places in this area it is about $37 (4%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $232 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $201 (19%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
