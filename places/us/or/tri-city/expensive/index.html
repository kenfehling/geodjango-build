Compared to the surrounding areas, Tri-City is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Eugene however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Tri-City is $152,800. It is about $11,680 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $64,700 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $135,800 (47%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

The median rent in Tri-City is $830 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $37 (4%) higher. It is roughly $232 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $201 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
