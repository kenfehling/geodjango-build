In comparison to other places in this area, Tri-City is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Eugene however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Tri-City is $152,800. This is about $11,680 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $64,700 (29%) lower, and is roughly $135,800 (47%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tri-City is $830. This is approximately $37 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $232 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Eugene is about $201 (19%) lower.
