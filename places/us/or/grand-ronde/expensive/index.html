Home prices

The median home cost in Grand Ronde is $189,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $24,140 (14%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $27,700 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $222,200 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Grand Ronde the median monthly rent is $446. It is approximately $500 (52%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $616 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $802 (64%) lower.
