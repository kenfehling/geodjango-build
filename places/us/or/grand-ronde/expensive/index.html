Home prices

Grand Ronde's median home cost is $189,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $24,140 (14%) higher. It is approximately $27,700 (12%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $222,200 (53%) lower.

Rents

Grand Ronde's median monthly rent is $446. Compared to nearby areas it is about $500 (52%) lower. It is roughly $616 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $802 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
