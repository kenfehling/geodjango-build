Compared to its surrounding areas, Grand Ronde is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Grand Ronde is $189,800. It is roughly $24,140 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $27,700 (12%) lower, and is approximately $222,200 (53%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Grand Ronde the median monthly rent is $446. It is about $500 (52%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $616 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $802 (64%) lower.
