Home prices

The median home in Grand Ronde costs $189,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $24,140 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $27,700 (12%) lower, and is roughly $222,200 (53%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Grand Ronde the median rent is $446 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $500 (52%) lower.It is roughly $616 (58%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $802 (64%) lower.
