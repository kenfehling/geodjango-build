Home prices

The median home cost in Pacific City is $222,100. It is roughly $18,120 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $4,600 (2%) higher, and is approximately $189,900 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Pacific City is $1,179 per month. It is about $328 (38%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $117 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $69 (5%) lower.
