In comparison to nearby areas, Lake Oswego is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Oswego is valued at $637,200. This is about $67,680 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $419,700 (192%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $225,200 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Oswego is $1,614 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $201 (14%) higher.It is roughly $552 (51%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $366 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
