In comparison to its surrounding areas, Durham is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Durham's median home price is $573,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $97,900 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $356,100 (163%) higher, and is approximately $161,600 (39%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Durham the median rent is $1,205 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $370 (23%) lower. It is approximately $143 (13%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $43 (3%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
