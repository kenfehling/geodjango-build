Compared to its nearby areas, Durham is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Durham's median home cost is $573,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $97,900 (20%) higher.It is approximately $356,100 (163%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $161,600 (39%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Durham the median monthly rent is $1,205. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $370 (23%) lower.It is about $143 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland is about $43 (3%) lower.
