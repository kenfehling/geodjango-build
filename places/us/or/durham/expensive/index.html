Compared to the surrounding areas, Durham is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Durham's median home price is $573,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $97,900 (20%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $356,100 (163%) higher, and it is about $161,600 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Durham's median monthly rent is $1,205. This is approximately $370 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $143 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $43 (3%) lower.
