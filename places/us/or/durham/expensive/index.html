Compared to its surrounding areas, Durham is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $573,600. It is about $97,900 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $356,100 (163%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $161,600 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Durham is $1,205 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $370 (23%) lower. It is about $143 (13%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $43 (3%) lower.
