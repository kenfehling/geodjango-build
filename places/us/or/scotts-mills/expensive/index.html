In comparison to other places in this area, Scotts Mills is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Scotts Mills's median home cost is $273,000. It is roughly $22,960 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $55,500 (25%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $139,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

Scotts Mills's median rent is $844 per month. This is approximately $181 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $218 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $404 (32%) lower.
