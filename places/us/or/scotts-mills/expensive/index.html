In comparison to other places in this area, Scotts Mills is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Scotts Mills is $273,000. It is about $22,960 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $55,500 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $139,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scotts Mills is $844. This is about $181 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $218 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $404 (32%) lower.
