In comparison to its surrounding areas, Scotts Mills is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Scotts Mills is valued at $273,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $22,960 (9%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $55,500 (25%) higher, and it is approximately $139,000 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Scotts Mills's median monthly rent is $844. It is approximately $181 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $218 (20%) lower, and is about $404 (32%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
