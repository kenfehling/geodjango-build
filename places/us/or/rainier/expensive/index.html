Home prices

The median home in Rainier costs $187,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $9,000 (4%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $29,700 (13%) lower, and it is about $224,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rainier is $737. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $146 (16%) lower.It is approximately $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $511 (40%) lower.
