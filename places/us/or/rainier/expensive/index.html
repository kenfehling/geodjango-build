The price of the median home in Rainier is $187,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $9,000 (4%) lower. It is about $29,700 (13%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $224,200 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rainier is $737 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $146 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $325 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $511 (40%) lower.
