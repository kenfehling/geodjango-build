Home prices

The median home in Rainier is valued at $187,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $9,000 (4%) lower.It is roughly $29,700 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $224,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Rainier is $737 per month. This is about $146 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $325 (30%) lower, and is approximately $511 (40%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
