Compared to other places in this area, Gresham is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gresham costs $287,900. It is about $46,020 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $70,400 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $124,100 (30%) lower.

Rents

Gresham's median monthly rent is $1,178. This is roughly $189 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $116 (10%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $70 (5%) lower.
