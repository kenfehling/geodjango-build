Home prices

The median home in Keizer is valued at $260,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $35,860 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $42,700 (19%) higher, and is about $151,800 (36%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Keizer's median rent is $1,056 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $11 (1%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $192 (15%) lower.
