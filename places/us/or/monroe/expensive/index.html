Home prices

The median home in Monroe is valued at $156,700. It is about $51,540 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $60,800 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $255,300 (61%) lower.

Rents

In Monroe the median monthly rent is $748. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $196 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $314 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $500 (40%) lower.
