Home prices

The median home value in Gladstone is $302,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $68,800 (18%) lower. It is approximately $84,700 (38%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $109,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Gladstone the median monthly rent is $1,334. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $272 (25%) higher, and it is about $86 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
