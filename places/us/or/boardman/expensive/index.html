Home prices

Rents

In Boardman the median rent is $722 per month. It is roughly $68 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $340 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $98 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.
