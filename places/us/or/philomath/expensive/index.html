Home prices

Philomath's median home price is $244,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $8,900 (3%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $27,200 (12%) higher, and is about $167,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Philomath's median monthly rent is $899. Compared to nearby areas it is about $212 (19%) lower. It is roughly $163 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $349 (27%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
