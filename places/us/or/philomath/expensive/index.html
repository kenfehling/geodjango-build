Home prices

The median home value in Philomath is $244,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $8,900 (3%) higher. It is about $27,200 (12%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $167,300 (40%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Philomath's median monthly rent is $899. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $212 (19%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $163 (15%) lower, and is approximately $349 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
