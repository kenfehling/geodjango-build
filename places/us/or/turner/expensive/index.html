Home prices

The price of the median home in Turner is $262,500. This is approximately $26,100 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $45,000 (20%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $149,500 (36%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Turner the median monthly rent is $914. This is approximately $117 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $148 (13%) lower, and is approximately $334 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
