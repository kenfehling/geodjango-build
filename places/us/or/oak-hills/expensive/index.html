Home prices

The median home cost in Oak Hills is $452,400. This is about $12,840 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $234,900 (108%) higher, and is approximately $40,400 (9%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Oak Hills the median monthly rent is $1,410. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $348 (32%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $162 (12%) higher.
