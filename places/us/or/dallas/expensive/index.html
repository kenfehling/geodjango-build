In comparison to its nearby areas, Dallas is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dallas is $222,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $25,760 (13%) higher.It is about $5,200 (2%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $189,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Dallas the median rent is $867 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $134 (13%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $195 (18%) lower, and is about $381 (30%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
