The median home price in Dallas is $222,700. It is approximately $25,760 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $5,200 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $189,300 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Dallas is $867 a month. This is roughly $134 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $381 (30%) lower.
