Compared to its surrounding areas, Dallas is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dallas is $222,700. It is roughly $25,760 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $5,200 (2%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $189,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Dallas the median rent is $867 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $134 (13%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $195 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $381 (30%) lower.
