In comparison to its surrounding areas, Salem is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Salem is valued at $242,800. It is about $22,500 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $25,300 (11%) higher, and is approximately $169,200 (41%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Salem is $975. This is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $87 (8%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is about $273 (21%) lower.
