Compared to other places in this area, Salem is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Salem is $242,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $22,500 (10%) higher.It is approximately $25,300 (11%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $169,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

Salem's median rent is $975 a month. This is approximately $16 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $87 (8%) lower, and is roughly $273 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
