Compared to other places in this area, Harrisburg is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Portland, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Harrisburg is $169,300. This is about $48,680 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $48,200 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $242,700 (58%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median rent is $1,118 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $257 (29%) higher. It is roughly $56 (5%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $130 (10%) lower.
