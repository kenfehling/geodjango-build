In comparison to its surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Harrisburg is $169,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $48,680 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $48,200 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $242,700 (58%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Harrisburg is $1,118 a month. This is approximately $257 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $56 (5%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $130 (10%) lower.
