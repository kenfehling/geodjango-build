Home prices

The median home price in Lebanon is $177,900. It is about $40,880 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $39,600 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $234,100 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Lebanon the median rent is $938 per month. This is approximately $110 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $124 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $310 (24%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
