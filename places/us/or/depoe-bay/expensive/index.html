Home prices

The median home in Depoe Bay costs $311,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $87,100 (38%) higher. It is approximately $93,600 (43%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Eugene is roughly $22,500 (7%) higher.

Rents

Depoe Bay's median monthly rent is $1,176. This is about $289 (32%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $114 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Eugene it is roughly $145 (14%) higher.
