The median home price in Brownsville is $208,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $18,540 (9%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $9,500 (4%) lower, and is roughly $204,000 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Brownsville is $959 per month. It is about $43 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $103 (9%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $289 (23%) lower.
