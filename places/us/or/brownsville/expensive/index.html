Home prices

Brownsville's median home price is $208,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $18,540 (9%) higher.It is roughly $9,500 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $204,000 (49%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Brownsville the median rent is $959 per month. It is roughly $43 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $103 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $289 (23%) lower.
