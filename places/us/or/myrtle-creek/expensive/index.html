Home prices

The value of the median home in Myrtle Creek is $166,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $4,760 (2%) higher. It is about $51,000 (23%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Eugene is about $122,100 (42%) lower.

Rents
