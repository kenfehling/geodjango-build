Much like its surrounding areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Eugene.

Home prices

The median home in Myrtle Creek is valued at $166,500. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $4,760 (2%) higher.It is roughly $51,000 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $122,100 (42%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

In Myrtle Creek the median rent is $687 per month. It is approximately $134 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $375 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $344 (33%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
