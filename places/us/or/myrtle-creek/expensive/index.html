Rents

In Myrtle Creek the median rent is $687 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $134 (16%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $375 (35%) lower, and is approximately $344 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
