Compared to its surrounding areas, Mission is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Kennewick.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mission is $196,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $11,980 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $21,300 (9%) lower, and it is roughly $19,300 (8%) lower compared to the city of Kennewick.

Rents

Mission's median rent is $556 per month. This is roughly $202 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $506 (47%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $366 (39%) lower compared to the city of Kennewick.
