Home prices

Rents

Mission's median rent is $556 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $202 (26%) lower.It is roughly $506 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $366 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Kennewick.
