Home prices

The price of the median home in Milwaukie is $323,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $64,760 (16%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $106,000 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $88,500 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Milwaukie the median monthly rent is $1,173. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $183 (13%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $111 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $75 (6%) lower.
