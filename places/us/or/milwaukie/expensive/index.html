Compared to the surrounding areas, Milwaukie is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Portland, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Milwaukie's median home value is $323,500. This is about $64,760 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $106,000 (48%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $88,500 (21%) lower.

Rents

Milwaukie's median monthly rent is $1,173. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $183 (13%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $111 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $75 (6%) lower.
