Compared to other places in this area, Columbia City is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Columbia City is $279,700. This is roughly $15,380 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $62,200 (28%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $132,300 (32%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Columbia City the median monthly rent is $1,162. This is about $26 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $100 (9%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $86 (6%) lower.
