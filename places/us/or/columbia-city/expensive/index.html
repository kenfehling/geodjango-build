In comparison to the surrounding areas, Columbia City is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Columbia City is valued at $279,700. It is roughly $15,380 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $62,200 (28%) higher, and it is roughly $132,300 (32%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Columbia City's median monthly rent is $1,162. It is approximately $26 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $100 (9%) higher, and it is roughly $86 (6%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
