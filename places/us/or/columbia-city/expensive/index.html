In comparison to its surrounding areas, Columbia City is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Columbia City is $279,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $15,380 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $62,200 (28%) higher, and is about $132,300 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbia City is $1,162. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $26 (2%) lower.It is about $100 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $86 (6%) lower.
