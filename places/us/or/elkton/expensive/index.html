Compared to its surrounding areas, Elkton is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Eugene, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Elkton is $232,800. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $52,500 (29%) higher.It is roughly $15,300 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $55,800 (19%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

In Elkton the median monthly rent is $600. This is about $182 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $462 (43%) lower, and is about $431 (41%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
