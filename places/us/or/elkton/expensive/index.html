Home prices

Rents

Elkton's median rent is $600 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $182 (23%) lower.It is about $462 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Eugene is approximately $431 (41%) lower.
