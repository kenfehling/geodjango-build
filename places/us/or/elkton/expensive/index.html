Home prices

The price of the median home in Elkton is $232,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $52,500 (29%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $15,300 (7%) higher, and is approximately $55,800 (19%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

In Elkton the median monthly rent is $600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $182 (23%) lower. It is roughly $462 (43%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $431 (41%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
