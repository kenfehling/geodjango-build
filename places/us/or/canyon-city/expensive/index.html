Home prices

The median home price in Canyon City is $136,500. This is approximately $29,040 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $81,000 (37%) lower, and it is about $35,600 (20%) lower compared to the city of Pendleton.

Rents

Canyon City's median monthly rent is $592. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $68 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $470 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Pendleton it is approximately $192 (24%) lower.
