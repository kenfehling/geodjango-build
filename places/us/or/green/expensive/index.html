Home prices

Green's median home price is $149,300. It is roughly $43,400 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $68,200 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $139,300 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Green's median monthly rent is $1,027. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $298 (40%) higher. It is roughly $35 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Eugene is is very similar.
