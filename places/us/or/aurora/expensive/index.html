Home prices

The median home cost in Aurora is $357,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $80,660 (29%) higher. It is approximately $139,800 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $54,700 (13%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Aurora is $1,557. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $332 (27%) higher. It is roughly $495 (46%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland is about $309 (24%) higher.
