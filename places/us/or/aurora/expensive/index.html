Home prices

The median home in Aurora costs $357,300. This is approximately $80,660 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $139,800 (64%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $54,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

Aurora's median monthly rent is $1,557. This is approximately $332 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $495 (46%) higher, and it is approximately $309 (24%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
