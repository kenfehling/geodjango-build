In comparison to the surrounding areas, Four Corners is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners costs $174,900. It is approximately $58,980 (25%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $42,600 (19%) lower, and is approximately $237,100 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Four Corners's median rent is $1,085 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $116 (11%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $23 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $163 (13%) lower.
