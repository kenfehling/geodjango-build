In comparison to its surrounding areas, Four Corners is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners costs $174,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $58,980 (25%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $42,600 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $237,100 (57%) lower.

Rents

Four Corners's median rent is $1,085 per month. It is roughly $116 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $23 (2%) higher, and is approximately $163 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
