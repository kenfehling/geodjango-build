Compared to its nearby areas, Four Corners is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners is valued at $174,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $58,980 (25%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $42,600 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $237,100 (57%) lower.

Rents

Four Corners's median monthly rent is $1,085. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $116 (11%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $23 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $163 (13%) lower.
