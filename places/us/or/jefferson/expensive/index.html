Home prices

Rents

Jefferson's median monthly rent is $867. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $257 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $195 (18%) lower, and is approximately $381 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
