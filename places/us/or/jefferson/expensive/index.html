Compared to its surrounding areas, Jefferson is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jefferson costs $194,100. It is roughly $59,720 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $23,400 (10%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $217,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Jefferson the median monthly rent is $867. This is about $257 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $195 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $381 (30%) lower.
