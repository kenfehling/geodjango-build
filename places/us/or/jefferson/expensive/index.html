Home prices

The value of the median home in Jefferson is $194,100. This is approximately $59,720 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $23,400 (10%) lower, and is approximately $217,900 (52%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Jefferson's median monthly rent is $867. It is approximately $257 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $195 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $381 (30%) lower.
