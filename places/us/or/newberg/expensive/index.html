Compared to its surrounding areas, Newberg is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home price in Newberg is $320,400. It is about $27,000 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $102,900 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $91,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Newberg the median monthly rent is $1,125. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $193 (14%) lower. It is roughly $63 (5%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $123 (9%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
