Home prices

The median home in Newberg is valued at $320,400. This is about $27,000 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $102,900 (47%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $91,600 (22%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Newberg is $1,125 per month. This is approximately $193 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $63 (5%) higher, and is about $123 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
