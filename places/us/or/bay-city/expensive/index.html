Home prices

Bay City's median home price is $227,500. This is about $13,780 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $10,000 (4%) higher, and it is roughly $184,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Bay City is $992 a month. It is approximately $115 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $70 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $256 (20%) lower.
