Home prices

The median home in Bay City costs $227,500. It is approximately $13,780 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $10,000 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $184,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Bay City the median monthly rent is $992. It is about $115 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $70 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $256 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
