Home prices

Rents

In Lakeside the median rent is $732 per month. It is roughly $120 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $330 (31%) lower, and it is about $299 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
