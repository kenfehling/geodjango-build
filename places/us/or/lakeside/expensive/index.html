Compared to nearby areas, Lakeside is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Eugene, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeside costs $181,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $11,700 (6%) lower.It is approximately $36,500 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Eugene it is roughly $107,600 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Lakeside the median rent is $732 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $120 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $330 (31%) lower, and is approximately $299 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
