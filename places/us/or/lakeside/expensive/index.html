Compared to its surrounding areas, Lakeside is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Eugene.

Home prices

Lakeside's median home price is $181,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $11,700 (6%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $36,500 (16%) lower, and it is about $107,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.

Rents

In Lakeside the median monthly rent is $732. This is roughly $120 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $330 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $299 (29%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
