In comparison to other places in this area, Lincoln City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Eugene.

Home prices

The median home in Lincoln City costs $246,300. It is approximately $35,420 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $28,800 (13%) higher, and is roughly $42,300 (14%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

In Lincoln City the median rent is $933 per month. It is roughly $65 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $129 (12%) lower, and is roughly $98 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
