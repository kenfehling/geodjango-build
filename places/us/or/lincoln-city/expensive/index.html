The price of the median home in Lincoln City is $246,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $35,420 (16%) higher.It is roughly $28,800 (13%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $42,300 (14%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Lincoln City's median rent is $933 a month. This is roughly $65 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $129 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $98 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
