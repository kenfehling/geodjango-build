Home prices

The median home in Gearhart costs $342,200. This is roughly $36,640 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $124,700 (57%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $69,800 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Gearhart the median rent is $1,113 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $189 (20%) higher. It is about $51 (4%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $135 (10%) lower.
