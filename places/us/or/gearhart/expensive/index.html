Home prices

The median home in Gearhart costs $342,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $36,640 (11%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $124,700 (57%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $69,800 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Gearhart is $1,113 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $189 (20%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $51 (4%) higher, and is approximately $135 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
