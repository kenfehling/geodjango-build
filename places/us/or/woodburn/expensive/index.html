Compared to other places in this area, Woodburn is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Woodburn is $205,300. This is approximately $39,400 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $12,200 (5%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $206,700 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Woodburn is $1,029. This is about $144 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $33 (3%) lower, and is about $219 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
