Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cedar Hills is $1,213. This is roughly $112 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $151 (14%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $35 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
