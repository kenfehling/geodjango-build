In comparison to other places in this area, Altamont is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Medford, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Altamont is $157,500. This is roughly $31,740 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $60,000 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Medford it is roughly $107,900 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Altamont is $789. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $73 (10%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $273 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $183 (18%) lower compared to the city of Medford.
