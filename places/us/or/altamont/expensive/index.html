Compared to the surrounding areas, Altamont is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Medford, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Altamont is $157,500. It is about $31,740 (25%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $60,000 (27%) lower, and it is about $107,900 (40%) lower compared to the city of Medford.

Rents

Altamont's median rent is $789 per month. It is roughly $73 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $273 (25%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $183 (18%) lower compared to the city of Medford.
