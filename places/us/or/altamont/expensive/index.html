Compared to other places in this area, Altamont is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Medford however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Altamont is $157,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $31,740 (25%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $60,000 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $107,900 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Medford.

Rents

Altamont's median rent is $789 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $73 (10%) higher.It is roughly $273 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Medford it is approximately $183 (18%) lower.
