Compared to the surrounding areas, Altamont is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Medford however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Altamont costs $157,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $31,740 (25%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $60,000 (27%) lower, and is approximately $107,900 (40%) lower compared to the city of Medford.

Rents

Altamont's median rent is $789 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $73 (10%) higher. It is approximately $273 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Medford it is approximately $183 (18%) lower.
