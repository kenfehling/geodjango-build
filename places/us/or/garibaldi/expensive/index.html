Compared to other places in this area, Garibaldi is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Garibaldi is $261,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27,260 (11%) higher. It is approximately $44,200 (20%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $150,300 (36%) lower.

Rents

Garibaldi's median monthly rent is $1,047. This is approximately $181 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $15 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $201 (16%) lower.
