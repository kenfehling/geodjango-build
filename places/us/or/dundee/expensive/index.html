Home prices

The median home in Dundee is valued at $302,500. This is approximately $28,260 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $85,000 (39%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $109,500 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Dundee the median rent is $1,508 per month. This is approximately $347 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $446 (41%) higher, and is approximately $260 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
