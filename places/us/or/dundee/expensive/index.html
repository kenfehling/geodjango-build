In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dundee is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dundee is valued at $302,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $28,260 (10%) higher. It is roughly $85,000 (39%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $109,500 (26%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dundee is $1,508. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $347 (29%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $446 (41%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $260 (20%) higher.
