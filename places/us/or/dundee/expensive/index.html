Home prices

The value of the median home in Dundee is $302,500. This is roughly $28,260 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $85,000 (39%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $109,500 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Dundee's median monthly rent is $1,508. This is approximately $347 (29%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $446 (41%) higher, and it is roughly $260 (20%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
