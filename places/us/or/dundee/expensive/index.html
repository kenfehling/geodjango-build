Compared to other places in this area, Dundee is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Dundee is valued at $302,500. It is about $28,260 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $85,000 (39%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $109,500 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Dundee is $1,508 per month. It is approximately $347 (29%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $446 (41%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $260 (20%) higher.
