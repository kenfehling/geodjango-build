Compared to its surrounding areas, Troutdale is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Troutdale is valued at $309,600. This is about $15,160 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $92,100 (42%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $102,400 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Troutdale the median monthly rent is $1,195. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $133 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $53 (4%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
