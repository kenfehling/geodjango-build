Home prices

The median home in Troutdale costs $309,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,160 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $92,100 (42%) higher, and is approximately $102,400 (24%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Troutdale the median rent is $1,195 per month. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $133 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $53 (4%) lower.
