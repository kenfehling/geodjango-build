Compared to the surrounding areas, Troutdale is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Troutdale is valued at $309,600. It is approximately $15,160 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $92,100 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $102,400 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Troutdale the median monthly rent is $1,195. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is about $133 (12%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $53 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
