Home prices

The median home cost in Troutdale is $309,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $15,160 (5%) higher.It is about $92,100 (42%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is about $102,400 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Troutdale is $1,195 per month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $133 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $53 (4%) lower.
