Home prices

The median home cost in Oakland is $176,400. It is is very similar compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $41,100 (18%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $112,200 (38%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakland is $759. This is about $52 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $303 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Eugene is about $272 (26%) lower.
