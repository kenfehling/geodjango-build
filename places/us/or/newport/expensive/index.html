Home prices

The median home in Newport is valued at $258,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $29,660 (12%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $40,500 (18%) higher, and is approximately $30,600 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.

Rents

The median rent in Newport is $896 a month. This is roughly $17 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $135 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
