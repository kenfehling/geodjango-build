Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Rockcreek is $1,383 a month. This is roughly $80 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $321 (30%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $135 (10%) higher.
