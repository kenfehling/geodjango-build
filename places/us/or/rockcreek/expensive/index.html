Home prices

The median home in Rockcreek costs $429,100. Compared to nearby areas it is about $26,160 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $211,600 (97%) higher, and is approximately $17,100 (4%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Rockcreek is $1,383 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $80 (5%) lower. It is about $321 (30%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $135 (10%) higher.
