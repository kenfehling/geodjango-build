Compared to its surrounding areas, Rockcreek is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Rockcreek is $429,100. It is roughly $26,160 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $211,600 (97%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $17,100 (4%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Rockcreek the median monthly rent is $1,383. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $80 (5%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $321 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $135 (10%) higher.
