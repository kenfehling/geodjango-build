Home prices

The median home cost in Coos Bay is $181,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $3,360 (1%) higher.It is about $36,300 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Eugene is roughly $107,400 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Coos Bay is $820 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $21 (2%) lower.It is approximately $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $211 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
