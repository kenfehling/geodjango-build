Home prices

Rents

Coos Bay's median rent is $820 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $21 (2%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $242 (22%) lower, and is roughly $211 (20%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
