In comparison to nearby areas, Albany is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home price in Albany is $226,200. This is roughly $41,840 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $8,700 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $185,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

Albany's median monthly rent is $966. It is roughly $235 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $96 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $282 (22%) lower.
