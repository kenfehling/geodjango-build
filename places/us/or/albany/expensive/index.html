Compared to its surrounding areas, Albany is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Albany costs $226,200. It is roughly $41,840 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $8,700 (4%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $185,800 (45%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Albany the median monthly rent is $966. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $235 (19%) lower.It is roughly $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland is about $282 (22%) lower.
