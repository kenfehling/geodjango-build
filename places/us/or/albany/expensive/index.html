Compared to nearby areas, Albany is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Albany costs $226,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $41,840 (15%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $8,700 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $185,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Albany is $966 per month. It is roughly $235 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $282 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
