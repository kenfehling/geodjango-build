Home prices

The median home in Prescott costs $172,900. It is roughly $21,660 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $44,600 (20%) lower, and is roughly $239,100 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Prescott is $1,025. It is about $220 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $223 (17%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
