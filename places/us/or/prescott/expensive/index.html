Home prices

The cost of the median home in Prescott is $172,900. This is approximately $21,660 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $44,600 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $239,100 (58%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Prescott the median rent is $1,025 a month. This is about $220 (27%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $37 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $223 (17%) lower.
