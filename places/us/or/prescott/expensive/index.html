Home prices

The median home in Prescott is valued at $172,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $21,660 (11%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $44,600 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $239,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

Prescott's median rent is $1,025 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $220 (27%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $37 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is about $223 (17%) lower.
