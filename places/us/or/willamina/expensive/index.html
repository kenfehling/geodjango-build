In comparison to its surrounding areas, Willamina is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Willamina is $166,900. This is roughly $15,880 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $50,600 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $245,100 (59%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Willamina the median monthly rent is $918. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $23 (2%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $144 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is about $330 (26%) lower.
