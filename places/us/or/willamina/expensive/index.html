Compared to the surrounding areas, Willamina is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Willamina is valued at $166,900. It is approximately $15,880 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $50,600 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $245,100 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Willamina the median monthly rent is $918. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $23 (2%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $144 (13%) lower, and is roughly $330 (26%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
