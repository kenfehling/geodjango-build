Home prices

The median home in Willamina is valued at $166,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $15,880 (8%) lower.It is about $50,600 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $245,100 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Willamina's median rent is $918 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $23 (2%) higher.It is about $144 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $330 (26%) lower.
