Home prices

Chenoweth's median home cost is $172,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $8,640 (4%) lower. It is roughly $44,700 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $239,200 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Chenoweth is $946 per month. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $116 (10%) lower, and it is approximately $302 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
