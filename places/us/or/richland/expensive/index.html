In comparison to the surrounding areas, Richland is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Nampa however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Richland is $162,500. This is approximately $39,740 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $55,000 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $4,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Nampa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richland is $567. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $84 (12%) lower. It is roughly $495 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Nampa it is approximately $358 (38%) lower.
