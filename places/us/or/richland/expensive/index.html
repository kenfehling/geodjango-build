Home prices

The median home in Richland costs $162,500. This is roughly $39,740 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $55,000 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Nampa is about $4,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Richland is $567 per month. It is roughly $84 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $495 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $358 (38%) lower compared to the city of Nampa.
