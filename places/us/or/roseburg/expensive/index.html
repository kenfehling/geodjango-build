Home prices

The median home cost in Roseburg is $195,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $12,680 (6%) higher.It is roughly $22,100 (10%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Eugene it is approximately $93,200 (32%) lower.

Rents

Roseburg's median rent is $849 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $45 (5%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $213 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eugene it is roughly $182 (17%) lower.
