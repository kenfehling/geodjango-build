In comparison to the surrounding areas, Roseburg is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Eugene however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Roseburg's median home cost is $195,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $12,680 (6%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $22,100 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eugene is roughly $93,200 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roseburg is $849. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $45 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $213 (20%) lower, and it is roughly $182 (17%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
