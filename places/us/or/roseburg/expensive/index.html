Home prices

The median home in Roseburg costs $195,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $12,680 (6%) higher.It is roughly $22,100 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $93,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.

Rents

In Roseburg the median monthly rent is $849. This is roughly $45 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $213 (20%) lower, and is roughly $182 (17%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
