Home prices

The median home in Damascus costs $434,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $116,700 (36%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $216,700 (99%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $22,200 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Damascus is $1,598. This is about $274 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $536 (50%) higher, and it is about $350 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
