Compared to other places in this area, Damascus is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Portland, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Damascus costs $434,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $116,700 (36%) higher. It is about $216,700 (99%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $22,200 (5%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Damascus is $1,598 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $274 (20%) higher. It is approximately $536 (50%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $350 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
