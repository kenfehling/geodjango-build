In comparison to its surrounding areas, Damascus is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Damascus is $434,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $116,700 (36%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $216,700 (99%) higher, and it is roughly $22,200 (5%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Damascus's median monthly rent is $1,598. This is roughly $274 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $536 (50%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $350 (28%) higher.
