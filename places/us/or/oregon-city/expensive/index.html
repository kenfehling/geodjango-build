Home prices

The median home in Oregon City costs $359,200. It is about $121,080 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $141,700 (65%) higher, and is approximately $52,800 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Oregon City's median rent is $1,275 per month. It is roughly $36 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $213 (20%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $27 (2%) higher.
