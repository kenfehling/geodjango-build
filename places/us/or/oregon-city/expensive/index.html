Compared to nearby areas, Oregon City is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Portland, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Oregon City's median home price is $359,200. This is about $121,080 (25%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $141,700 (65%) higher, and is roughly $52,800 (12%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Oregon City the median rent is $1,275 per month. This is about $36 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $213 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $27 (2%) higher.
