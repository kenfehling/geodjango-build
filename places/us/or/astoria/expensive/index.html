In comparison to its surrounding areas, Astoria is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Astoria is $259,600. This is about $28,680 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $42,100 (19%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $152,400 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Astoria is $908. It is roughly $57 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $340 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
