In comparison to its surrounding areas, Astoria is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Astoria costs $259,600. It is approximately $28,680 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $42,100 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $152,400 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Astoria is $908 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $57 (5%) lower. It is about $154 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $340 (27%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
