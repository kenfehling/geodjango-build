Home prices

The median home in Culver is valued at $183,800. This is about $6,760 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $33,700 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Bend is approximately $201,000 (52%) lower.

Rents

Culver's median monthly rent is $882. It is roughly $14 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $180 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Bend it is roughly $394 (30%) lower.
