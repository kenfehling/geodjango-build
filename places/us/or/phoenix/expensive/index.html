Home prices

The median home in Phoenix costs $188,400. It is about $165,120 (46%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $29,100 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Medford is approximately $77,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Phoenix the median monthly rent is $814. This is about $243 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $248 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Medford it is approximately $158 (16%) lower.
