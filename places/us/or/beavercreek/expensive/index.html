In comparison to the surrounding areas, Beavercreek is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Portland, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Beavercreek is $469,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $123,460 (35%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $252,100 (115%) higher, and it is roughly $57,600 (13%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Beavercreek's median rent is $1,088 per month. It is approximately $84 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $26 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $160 (12%) lower.
