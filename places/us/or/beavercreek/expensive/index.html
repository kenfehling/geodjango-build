Home prices

The median home in Beavercreek is valued at $469,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $123,460 (35%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $252,100 (115%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $57,600 (13%) higher.

Rents

In Beavercreek the median monthly rent is $1,088. It is about $84 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $26 (2%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $160 (12%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
