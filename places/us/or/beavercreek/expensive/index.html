Compared to the surrounding areas, Beavercreek is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a little more expensive even compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Beavercreek's median home value is $469,600. It is approximately $123,460 (35%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $252,100 (115%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $57,600 (13%) higher.

Rents

Beavercreek's median rent is $1,088 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $84 (7%) lower.It is about $26 (2%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $160 (12%) lower.
