In comparison to nearby areas, Hood River is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hood River costs $354,700. This is roughly $122,560 (52%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $137,200 (63%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $57,300 (13%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hood River the median monthly rent is $1,142. It is approximately $192 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $80 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $106 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
