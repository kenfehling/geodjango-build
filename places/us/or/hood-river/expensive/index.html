Compared to its surrounding areas, Hood River is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Portlandhowever, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Hood River is $354,700. This is about $122,560 (52%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $137,200 (63%) higher, and it is approximately $57,300 (13%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hood River the median rent is $1,142 per month. It is approximately $192 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $80 (7%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is about $106 (8%) lower.
