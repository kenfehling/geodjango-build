Home prices

The median home in Hood River costs $354,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $122,560 (52%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $137,200 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $57,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

Hood River's median monthly rent is $1,142. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $192 (20%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $80 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $106 (8%) lower.
