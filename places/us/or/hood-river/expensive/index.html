Compared to its surrounding areas, Hood River is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home price in Hood River is $354,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $122,560 (52%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $137,200 (63%) higher, and it is about $57,300 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Hood River's median rent is $1,142 a month. This is about $192 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $80 (7%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $106 (8%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
