Compared to other places in this area, Hood River is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hood River is valued at $354,700. This is approximately $122,560 (52%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $137,200 (63%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $57,300 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hood River the median rent is $1,142 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $192 (20%) higher.It is approximately $80 (7%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $106 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
