In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hood River is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Hood River is valued at $354,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $122,560 (52%) higher.It is about $137,200 (63%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $57,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hood River is $1,142 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $192 (20%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $80 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $106 (8%) lower.
