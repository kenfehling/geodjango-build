In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mill City is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mill City is $174,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $53,940 (23%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $42,800 (19%) lower, and is about $237,300 (57%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Mill City the median rent is $912 a month. This is about $21 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $150 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $336 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
