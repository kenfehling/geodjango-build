Home prices

Mill City's median home price is $174,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $53,940 (23%) lower.It is approximately $42,800 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $237,300 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Mill City the median rent is $912 per month. It is approximately $21 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $150 (14%) lower, and it is about $336 (26%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
