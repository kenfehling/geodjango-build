In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sherwood is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Sherwood costs $405,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $26,880 (6%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $188,400 (86%) higher, and it is roughly $6,100 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Sherwood is $1,485 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $53 (3%) higher. It is roughly $423 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $237 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
