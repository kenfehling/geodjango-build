Compared to other places in this area, Sherwood is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Portland, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Sherwood is $405,900. This is approximately $26,880 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $188,400 (86%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $6,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

Sherwood's median monthly rent is $1,485. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $53 (3%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $423 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $237 (18%) higher.
