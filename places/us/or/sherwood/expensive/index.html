Home prices

The median home in Sherwood costs $405,900. It is about $26,880 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $188,400 (86%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $6,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

Sherwood's median monthly rent is $1,485. This is about $53 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $423 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $237 (18%) higher.
