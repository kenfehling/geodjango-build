Home prices

The median home price in Sherwood is $405,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $26,880 (6%) lower. It is approximately $188,400 (86%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $6,100 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Sherwood's median rent is $1,485 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $53 (3%) higher. It is about $423 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $237 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
