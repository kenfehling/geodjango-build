Home prices

The median home price in Baker City is $146,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $8,600 (6%) higher. It is approximately $71,000 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $57,000 (28%) lower compared to the city of Walla Walla.

Rents

The median rent in Baker City is $661 a month. This is approximately $42 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $401 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Walla Walla is about $301 (31%) lower.
