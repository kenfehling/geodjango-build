Home prices

The cost of the median home in Baker City is $146,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $8,600 (6%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $71,000 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Walla Walla is about $57,000 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Baker City is $661. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $42 (6%) lower.It is about $401 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $301 (31%) lower compared to the city of Walla Walla.
