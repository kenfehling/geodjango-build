Compared to its nearby areas, St. Helens is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in St. Helens is valued at $228,500. This is approximately $86,760 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $11,000 (5%) higher, and is roughly $183,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In St. Helens the median rent is $901 per month. It is roughly $434 (32%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $161 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $347 (27%) lower.
