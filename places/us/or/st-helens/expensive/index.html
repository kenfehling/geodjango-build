Home prices

The median home in St. Helens is valued at $228,500. This is about $86,760 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $11,000 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $183,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in St. Helens is $901 a month. This is about $434 (32%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $161 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $347 (27%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
