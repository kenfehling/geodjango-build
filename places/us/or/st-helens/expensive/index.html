Compared to other places in this area, St. Helens is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Helens is $228,500. It is approximately $86,760 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $11,000 (5%) higher, and it is about $183,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In St. Helens the median rent is $901 per month. This is approximately $434 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $161 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $347 (27%) lower.
