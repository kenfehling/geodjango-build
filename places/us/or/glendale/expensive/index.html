Home prices

The median home in Glendale is valued at $123,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $68,940 (35%) lower. It is roughly $93,900 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $165,000 (57%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

The median rent in Glendale is $770 per month. This is approximately $21 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $292 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $261 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
