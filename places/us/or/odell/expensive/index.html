Home prices

The median home in Odell is valued at $221,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $37,880 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $3,500 (1%) higher, and it is roughly $191,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Odell's median rent is $585 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $476 (44%) lower. It is approximately $477 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $663 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
