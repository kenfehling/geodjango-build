Home prices

The median home in Junction City costs $230,700. This is roughly $3,500 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $13,200 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $181,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Junction City the median monthly rent is $769. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $155 (16%) lower. It is roughly $293 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $479 (38%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
