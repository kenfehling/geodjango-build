Home prices

The median home price in Junction City is $230,700. It is about $3,500 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $13,200 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $181,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

Junction City's median monthly rent is $769. This is roughly $155 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $479 (38%) lower.
