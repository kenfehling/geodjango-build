Home prices

The median home in Lowell is valued at $192,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $17,300 (9%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $24,800 (11%) lower, and it is about $95,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.

Rents

In Lowell the median monthly rent is $944. It is about $78 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $118 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $87 (8%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.
