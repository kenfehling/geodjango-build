Compared to its surrounding areas, Lowell is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Eugene however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lowell is valued at $192,700. It is roughly $17,300 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $24,800 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Eugene is roughly $95,900 (33%) lower.

Rents

Lowell's median monthly rent is $944. This is roughly $78 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $118 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Eugene it is about $87 (8%) lower.
