Compared to the surrounding areas, Lowell is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Eugene, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lowell is $192,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $17,300 (9%) higher. It is approximately $24,800 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $95,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Lowell's median rent is $944 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $78 (9%) higher. It is roughly $118 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $87 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
