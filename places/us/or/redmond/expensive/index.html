In comparison to the surrounding areas, Redmond is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Bend.

Home prices

Redmond's median home price is $258,300. This is roughly $93,740 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $40,800 (18%) higher, and is approximately $126,500 (32%) lower compared to the city of Bend.

Rents

In Redmond the median rent is $1,090 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $134 (10%) lower. It is about $28 (2%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Bend it is about $186 (14%) lower.
