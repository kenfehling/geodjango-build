In comparison to its surrounding areas, Redmond is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Bend.

Home prices

Redmond's median home price is $258,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $93,740 (26%) lower. It is roughly $40,800 (18%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $126,500 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Bend.

Rents

Redmond's median monthly rent is $1,090. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $134 (10%) lower. It is roughly $28 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bend is approximately $186 (14%) lower.
