Compared to nearby areas, Eugene is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Salem.

Home prices

The median home cost in Eugene is $288,600. This is approximately $53,580 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $71,100 (32%) higher, and it is roughly $45,800 (18%) higher compared to the city of Salem.

Rents

In Eugene the median monthly rent is $1,031. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $170 (19%) higher. It is approximately $31 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $56 (5%) higher compared to the city of Salem.
