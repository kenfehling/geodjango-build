In comparison to its surrounding areas, Eugene is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a little more expensive compared to the city of Salem.

Home prices

The median home value in Eugene is $288,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $53,580 (22%) higher.It is about $71,100 (32%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $45,800 (18%) higher compared to the city of Salem.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Eugene is $1,031. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $170 (19%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $31 (2%) lower, and it is about $56 (5%) higher compared to the city of Salem.
