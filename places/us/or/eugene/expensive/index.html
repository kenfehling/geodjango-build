Compared to the surrounding areas, Eugene is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Salem.

Home prices

The median home in Eugene is valued at $288,600. This is approximately $53,580 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $71,100 (32%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Salem is about $45,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Eugene is $1,031 a month. It is roughly $170 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $31 (2%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Salem it is roughly $56 (5%) higher.
