Compared to other places in this area, Eugene is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Salem, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Eugene's median home value is $288,600. This is roughly $53,580 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $71,100 (32%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salem it is roughly $45,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Eugene the median monthly rent is $1,031. It is approximately $170 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $31 (2%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Salem is about $56 (5%) higher.
