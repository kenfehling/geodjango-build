Home prices

The median home in North Plains is valued at $358,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $27,660 (8%) higher. It is about $141,100 (64%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $53,400 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

North Plains's median rent is $1,112 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $140 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $50 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $136 (10%) lower.
