Home prices

The median home value in Silverton is $296,400. It is approximately $72,360 (32%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $78,900 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $115,600 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Silverton is $967 per month. This is roughly $18 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $95 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $281 (22%) lower.
