Compared to its surrounding areas, Silverton is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Silverton is $296,400. It is approximately $72,360 (32%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $78,900 (36%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $115,600 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Silverton is $967. This is approximately $18 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $95 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $281 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
