Home prices

Silverton's median home value is $296,400. It is about $72,360 (32%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $78,900 (36%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $115,600 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Silverton is $967. It is roughly $18 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $95 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $281 (22%) lower.
