Home prices

The median home in Springfield costs $204,700. This is about $27,920 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $12,800 (5%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $83,900 (29%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Springfield's median monthly rent is $929. It is approximately $65 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $133 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eugene is about $102 (9%) lower.
