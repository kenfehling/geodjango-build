In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dayton is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dayton is $198,000. This is about $82,540 (29%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $19,500 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $214,000 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dayton is $1,219. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $28 (2%) higher. It is approximately $157 (14%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $29 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
