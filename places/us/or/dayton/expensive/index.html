Home prices

The median home price in Dayton is $198,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $82,540 (29%) lower. It is roughly $19,500 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $214,000 (51%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Dayton the median rent is $1,219 per month. This is approximately $28 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $157 (14%) higher, and is approximately $29 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
