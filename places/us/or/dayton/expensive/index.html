In comparison to other places in this area, Dayton is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Dayton is $198,000. It is roughly $82,540 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $19,500 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $214,000 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Dayton the median rent is $1,219 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $28 (2%) higher.It is approximately $157 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $29 (2%) lower.
