In comparison to nearby areas, Dayton is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dayton is valued at $198,000. This is about $82,540 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $19,500 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $214,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $1,219. This is about $28 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $157 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $29 (2%) lower.
