Compared to other places in this area, Coquille is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Eugene.

Home prices

The median home in Coquille is valued at $145,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $32,320 (18%) lower. It is roughly $72,300 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $143,400 (49%) lower compared to the city of Eugene.

Rents

Coquille's median monthly rent is $711. This is roughly $115 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $351 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $320 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Eugene.
