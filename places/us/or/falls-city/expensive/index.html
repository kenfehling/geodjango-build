Compared to its surrounding areas, Falls City is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Falls City costs $140,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $57,340 (28%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $76,700 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $271,200 (65%) lower.

Rents

Falls City's median rent is $1,037 per month. This is approximately $81 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $211 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
