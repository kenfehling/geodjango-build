Home prices

The median home price in Tualatin is $419,400. This is roughly $40,880 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $201,900 (92%) higher, and is roughly $7,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tualatin is $1,335. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $189 (12%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $273 (25%) higher, and it is approximately $87 (6%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
