Home prices

The price of the median home in Bethany is $557,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $72,760 (15%) higher. It is approximately $339,800 (156%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $145,300 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Bethany's median monthly rent is $1,682. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $290 (20%) higher. It is approximately $620 (58%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $434 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
