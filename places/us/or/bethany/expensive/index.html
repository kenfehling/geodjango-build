Home prices

The price of the median home in Bethany is $557,300. It is about $72,760 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $339,800 (156%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $145,300 (35%) higher.

Rents

In Bethany the median monthly rent is $1,682. It is roughly $290 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $620 (58%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $434 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
