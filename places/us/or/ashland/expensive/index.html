Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Ashland is $1,085 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $82 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $23 (2%) higher, and is roughly $113 (11%) higher compared to the city of Medford.
