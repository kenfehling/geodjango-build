Compared to the surrounding areas, Ashland is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Medford, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $441,900. It is approximately $139,080 (45%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $224,400 (103%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $176,500 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of Medford.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $1,085. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $82 (8%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $23 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Medford is approximately $113 (11%) higher.
