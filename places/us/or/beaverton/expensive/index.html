Home prices

The median home in Beaverton is valued at $376,500. Compared to nearby areas it is about $103,200 (21%) lower.It is roughly $159,000 (73%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $35,500 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Beaverton the median rent is $1,338 per month. It is roughly $39 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $276 (25%) higher, and it is approximately $90 (7%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
