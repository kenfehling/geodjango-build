Compared to the surrounding areas, Hayesville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Hayesville's median home cost is $201,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $45,660 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $15,800 (7%) lower, and is roughly $210,300 (51%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hayesville the median monthly rent is $941. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $58 (5%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $121 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $307 (24%) lower.
