Compared to nearby areas, Hayesville is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Hayesville's median home price is $201,700. This is about $45,660 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $15,800 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $210,300 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Hayesville the median monthly rent is $941. This is roughly $58 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $121 (11%) lower, and is roughly $307 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
