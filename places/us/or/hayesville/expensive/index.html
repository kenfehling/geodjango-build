Home prices

The median home in Hayesville costs $201,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $45,660 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $15,800 (7%) lower, and is roughly $210,300 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hayesville the median rent is $941 a month. This is about $58 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $307 (24%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
