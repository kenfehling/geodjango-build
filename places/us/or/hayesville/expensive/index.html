In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hayesville is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hayesville is $201,700. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $45,660 (18%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $15,800 (7%) lower, and is approximately $210,300 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Hayesville's median rent is $941 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $58 (5%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $121 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $307 (24%) lower.
