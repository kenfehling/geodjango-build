Home prices

The median home in Hayesville costs $201,700. This is about $45,660 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $15,800 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $210,300 (51%) lower.

Rents

Hayesville's median rent is $941 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $58 (5%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $121 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $307 (24%) lower.
