Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cannon Beach is $587,800. It is approximately $264,180 (81%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $370,300 (170%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $175,800 (42%) higher.

The median rent in Cannon Beach is $839 per month. This is approximately $110 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $223 (20%) lower, and is approximately $409 (32%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
