Home prices

The median home in Vernonia is valued at $187,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $67,700 (26%) lower.It is about $29,600 (13%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is about $224,100 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Vernonia the median rent is $1,344 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $298 (28%) higher.It is about $282 (26%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $96 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
