Home prices

The cost of the median home in Vernonia is $187,900. It is roughly $67,700 (26%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $29,600 (13%) lower, and it is about $224,100 (54%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Vernonia the median rent is $1,344 a month. This is roughly $298 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $282 (26%) higher, and it is about $96 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
