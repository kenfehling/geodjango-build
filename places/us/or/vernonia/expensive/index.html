In comparison to other places in this area, Vernonia is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home value in Vernonia is $187,900. It is about $67,700 (26%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $29,600 (13%) lower, and is approximately $224,100 (54%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Vernonia's median monthly rent is $1,344. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $298 (28%) higher. It is approximately $282 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $96 (7%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
