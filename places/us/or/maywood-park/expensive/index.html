Home prices

The median home in Maywood Park is valued at $370,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $83,660 (29%) higher.It is roughly $152,800 (70%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $41,700 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Maywood Park the median monthly rent is $1,550. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $335 (27%) higher.It is about $488 (45%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $302 (24%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
