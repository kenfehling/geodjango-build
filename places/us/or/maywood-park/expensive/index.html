In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maywood Park is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maywood Park is valued at $370,300. This is about $83,660 (29%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $152,800 (70%) higher, and is about $41,700 (10%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Maywood Park's median rent is $1,550 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $335 (27%) higher. It is about $488 (45%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $302 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
