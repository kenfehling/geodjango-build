Home prices

The median home in Maywood Park costs $370,300. It is about $83,660 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $152,800 (70%) higher, and it is about $41,700 (10%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Maywood Park is $1,550. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $335 (27%) higher. It is about $488 (45%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $302 (24%) higher.
