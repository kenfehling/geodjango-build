Home prices

The median home in The Dalles is valued at $204,400. It is approximately $35,300 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $13,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $207,600 (50%) lower.

Rents

The Dalles's median rent is $831 per month. This is about $192 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $231 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $417 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
