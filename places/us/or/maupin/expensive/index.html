Home prices

Maupin's median home price is $177,200. Compared to nearby areas it is about $28,680 (19%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $40,300 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Bend it is roughly $207,600 (53%) lower.

Rents

Maupin's median rent is $803 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $17 (2%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $259 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Bend is roughly $473 (37%) lower.
