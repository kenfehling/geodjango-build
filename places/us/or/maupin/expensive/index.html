Home prices

The cost of the median home in Maupin is $177,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $28,680 (19%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $40,300 (18%) lower, and is approximately $207,600 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Bend.

Rents

The median rent in Maupin is $803 a month. It is roughly $17 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $259 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Bend it is about $473 (37%) lower.
