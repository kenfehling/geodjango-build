Rents

The median monthly rent in Olive Branch is $1,164. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $92 (8%) higher.It is roughly $102 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $263 (29%) higher.
