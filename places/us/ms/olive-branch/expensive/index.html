Home prices

The median home cost in Olive Branch is $175,600. This is about $32,680 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $41,900 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $73,800 (72%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Olive Branch is $1,164. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $92 (8%) higher.It is about $102 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $263 (29%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
