Compared to the surrounding areas, Olive Branch is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Memphis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Olive Branch is valued at $175,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $32,680 (15%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $41,900 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is approximately $73,800 (72%) higher.

Rents

Olive Branch's median rent is $1,164 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $92 (8%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $102 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $263 (29%) higher.
