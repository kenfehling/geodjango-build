Home prices

The median home in Louisville is valued at $83,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $8,680 (11%) higher. It is about $134,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson is approximately $7,200 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Louisville is $688 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $142 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $374 (35%) lower, and is approximately $159 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
