Compared to its surrounding areas, Louisville is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Jackson, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Louisville is valued at $83,500. It is about $8,680 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $134,000 (61%) lower, and it is about $7,200 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.

Rents

Louisville's median rent is $688 a month. This is about $142 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is approximately $159 (18%) lower.
