In comparison to its surrounding areas, Philadelphia is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Jackson, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Philadelphia is $80,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $4,580 (6%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $136,900 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson is approximately $10,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

Philadelphia's median monthly rent is $755. It is about $219 (40%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Jackson it is about $92 (10%) lower.
