Home prices

The median home in Philadelphia is valued at $80,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $4,580 (6%) higher.It is roughly $136,900 (62%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson it is about $10,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

Philadelphia's median rent is $755 per month. It is roughly $219 (40%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $307 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson it is about $92 (10%) lower.
