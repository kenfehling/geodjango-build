In comparison to the surrounding areas, Philadelphia is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Jackson however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Philadelphia costs $80,600. It is about $13,260 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $136,900 (62%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is about $10,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Philadelphia is $755. This is approximately $221 (41%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $92 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
