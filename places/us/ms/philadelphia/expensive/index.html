Home prices

The median home price in Philadelphia is $80,600. This is approximately $13,260 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $136,900 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is about $10,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

Philadelphia's median rent is $755 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $221 (41%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $307 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson it is about $92 (10%) lower.
