Home prices

The median home in Raleigh is valued at $91,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $8,400 (10%) higher. It is about $125,800 (57%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson is roughly $1,000 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Raleigh the median monthly rent is $421. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $268 (38%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $641 (60%) lower, and is approximately $426 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
