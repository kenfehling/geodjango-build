Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Indianola is $693 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $114 (19%) higher. It is about $369 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $154 (18%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
