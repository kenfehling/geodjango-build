Home prices

The value of the median home in Beaumont is $70,900. It is about $21,400 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $146,600 (67%) lower, and it is roughly $52,700 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.

Rents

In Beaumont the median rent is $510 a month. It is approximately $61 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $552 (51%) lower, and it is roughly $347 (40%) lower compared to the city of Mobile.
