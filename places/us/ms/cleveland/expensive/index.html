Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $806 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $203 (33%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $256 (24%) lower, and is roughly $79 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff.
