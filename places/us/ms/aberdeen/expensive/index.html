Home prices

The median home in Aberdeen is valued at $75,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $31,140 (29%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $142,300 (65%) lower, and it is roughly $103,700 (57%) lower compared to the city of Tuscaloosa.

Rents

Aberdeen's median monthly rent is $585. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $89 (13%) lower.It is approximately $477 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tuscaloosa is about $259 (30%) lower.
