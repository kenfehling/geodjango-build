In comparison to other places in this area, Natchez is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Baton Rouge, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Natchez is valued at $97,500. This is approximately $23,280 (31%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $120,000 (55%) lower, and it is about $76,500 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

In Natchez the median rent is $592 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $109 (15%) lower.It is roughly $470 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $287 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge.
