Compared to its surrounding areas, Natchez is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Baton Rouge, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Natchez's median home cost is $97,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $23,280 (31%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $120,000 (55%) lower, and is approximately $76,500 (43%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

The median rent in Natchez is $592 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $109 (15%) lower.It is roughly $470 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $287 (32%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.
