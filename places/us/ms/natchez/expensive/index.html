Compared to its nearby areas, Natchez is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Baton Rouge, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Natchez is $97,500. It is approximately $23,280 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $120,000 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Baton Rouge is roughly $76,500 (43%) lower.

Rents

Natchez's median rent is $592 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $109 (15%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $470 (44%) lower, and it is about $287 (32%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.
