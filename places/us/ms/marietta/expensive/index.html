Home prices

The median home in Marietta is valued at $78,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $27,420 (25%) lower. It is about $138,600 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is roughly $54,300 (40%) lower.

Rents

Marietta's median monthly rent is $625. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $24 (3%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $437 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is roughly $252 (28%) lower.
