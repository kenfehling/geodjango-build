Home prices

The median home in Long Beach is valued at $151,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $13,180 (8%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $66,100 (30%) lower, and is approximately $80,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

Long Beach's median rent is $965 a month. This is about $76 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $97 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New Orleans is roughly $33 (3%) lower.
