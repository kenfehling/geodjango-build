Home prices

The median home cost in Yazoo City is $70,900. This is approximately $11,760 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $146,600 (67%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $19,800 (21%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

Yazoo City's median rent is $625 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $185 (41%) higher. It is about $437 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $222 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
