Compared to its surrounding areas, Yazoo City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Jackson however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Yazoo City's median home cost is $70,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $11,760 (19%) higher. It is approximately $146,600 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Jackson is approximately $19,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

Yazoo City's median rent is $625 per month. It is approximately $185 (41%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $437 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson is roughly $222 (26%) lower.
