Home prices

The median home price in Rawls Springs is $104,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,140 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $113,100 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is about $13,700 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Rawls Springs the median monthly rent is $873. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $61 (6%) lower. It is about $189 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $26 (3%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.
