Home prices

The median home in Bassfield costs $120,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $18,120 (17%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $97,300 (44%) lower, and it is about $29,500 (32%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

In Bassfield the median rent is $782 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $157 (25%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $280 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson is approximately $65 (7%) lower.
