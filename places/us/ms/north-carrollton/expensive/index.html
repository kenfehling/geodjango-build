Home prices

Rents

The median rent in North Carrollton is $530 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $40 (7%) lower.It is approximately $532 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $317 (37%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
