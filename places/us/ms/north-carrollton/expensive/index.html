Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Carrollton is $82,200. This is roughly $4,020 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $135,300 (62%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $8,500 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.

Rents

North Carrollton's median rent is $530 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $40 (7%) lower.It is roughly $532 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson is about $317 (37%) lower.
