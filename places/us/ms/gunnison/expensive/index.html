Home prices

The median home in Gunnison is valued at $63,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $6,960 (12%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $153,700 (70%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff it is about $11,700 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Gunnison the median monthly rent is $470. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $28 (6%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $592 (55%) lower, and is about $257 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff.
