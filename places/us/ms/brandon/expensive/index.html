Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brandon is $982. It is approximately $98 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $80 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson is approximately $135 (15%) higher.
