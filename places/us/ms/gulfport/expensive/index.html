In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gulfport is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of New Orleans, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Gulfport is $122,300. This is about $29,040 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $95,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is approximately $109,200 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Gulfport is $860 a month. It is approximately $48 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New Orleans is roughly $138 (13%) lower.
