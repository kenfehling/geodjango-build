Compared to other places in this area, Gulfport is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

Gulfport's median home cost is $122,300. This is roughly $29,040 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $95,200 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is approximately $109,200 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Gulfport the median rent is $860 a month. It is about $48 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $138 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.
