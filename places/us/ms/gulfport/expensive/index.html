In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gulfport is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of New Orleans, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Gulfport is $122,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $29,040 (19%) lower. It is roughly $95,200 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is roughly $109,200 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Gulfport the median rent is $860 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $48 (5%) lower. It is about $202 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $138 (13%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.
