In comparison to nearby areas, Gulfport is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

The median home value in Gulfport is $122,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $29,040 (19%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $95,200 (43%) lower, and is roughly $109,200 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

Gulfport's median rent is $860 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $48 (5%) lower. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans is approximately $138 (13%) lower.
