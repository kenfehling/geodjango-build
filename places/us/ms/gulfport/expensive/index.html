Compared to nearby areas, Gulfport is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

Gulfport's median home value is $122,300. This is about $29,040 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $95,200 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is about $109,200 (47%) lower.

Rents

Gulfport's median monthly rent is $860. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $48 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $202 (19%) lower, and is approximately $138 (13%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.
