Home prices

The cost of the median home in Charleston is $67,000. This is about $2,540 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $150,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $34,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

Charleston's median monthly rent is $494. It is approximately $67 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $568 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is about $407 (45%) lower.
