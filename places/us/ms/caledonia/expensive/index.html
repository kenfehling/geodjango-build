Home prices

The value of the median home in Caledonia is $134,500. It is roughly $31,220 (30%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $83,000 (38%) lower, and is approximately $43,400 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Caledonia's median rent is $723 a month. This is roughly $119 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $339 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $114 (13%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
