Compared to the surrounding areas, Caledonia is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Caledonia is valued at $134,500. This is roughly $31,220 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $83,000 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Birmingham is roughly $43,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

Caledonia's median rent is $723 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $119 (19%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $339 (31%) lower, and is roughly $114 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
