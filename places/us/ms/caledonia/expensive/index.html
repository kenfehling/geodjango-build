In comparison to its surrounding areas, Caledonia is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Caledonia is $134,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $31,220 (30%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $83,000 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham it is about $43,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Caledonia is $723. This is approximately $119 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $114 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
