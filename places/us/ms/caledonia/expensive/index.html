Compared to its surrounding areas, Caledonia is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Caledonia's median home value is $134,500. This is roughly $31,220 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $83,000 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham it is approximately $43,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

Caledonia's median monthly rent is $723. This is about $119 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $339 (31%) lower, and it is about $114 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
