Home prices

The median home cost in Southaven is $153,200. It is approximately $3,860 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $64,300 (29%) lower, and is roughly $51,400 (50%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Southaven's median rent is $1,056 per month. This is roughly $57 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is is very similar compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $155 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
