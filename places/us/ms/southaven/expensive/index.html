Home prices

The price of the median home in Southaven is $153,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $3,860 (2%) higher. It is approximately $64,300 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $51,400 (50%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Southaven is $1,056 per month. It is about $57 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and it is approximately $155 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
