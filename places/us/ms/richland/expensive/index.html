Compared to its nearby areas, Richland is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Jackson, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richland is valued at $131,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $26,920 (17%) lower. It is approximately $86,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is roughly $40,400 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richland is $961. This is roughly $61 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $101 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $114 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Jackson.
