In comparison to the surrounding areas, Richland is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Jackson however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Richland's median home value is $131,100. This is about $26,920 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $86,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is about $40,400 (44%) higher.

Rents

Richland's median rent is $961 per month. This is roughly $61 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $101 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is approximately $114 (13%) higher.
