Compared to other places in this area, Richland is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Jackson however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richland is valued at $131,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $26,920 (17%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $86,400 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson it is roughly $40,400 (44%) higher.

Rents

Richland's median monthly rent is $961. This is approximately $61 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $101 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is about $114 (13%) higher.
