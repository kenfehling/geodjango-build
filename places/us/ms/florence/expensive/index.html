In comparison to the surrounding areas, Florence is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Jackson however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Florence is $158,300. It is roughly $38,180 (31%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $59,200 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is roughly $67,600 (74%) higher.

Rents

In Florence the median rent is $971 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $87 (9%) higher. It is approximately $91 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is roughly $124 (14%) higher.
