Compared to other places in this area, Florence is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Jackson, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $158,300. This is about $38,180 (31%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $59,200 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson it is about $67,600 (74%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $971. It is roughly $87 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $91 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson is about $124 (14%) higher.
