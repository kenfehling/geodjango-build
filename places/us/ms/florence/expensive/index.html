In comparison to its surrounding areas, Florence is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Jackson however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Florence's median home cost is $158,300. This is roughly $38,180 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $59,200 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Jackson is roughly $67,600 (74%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $971. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $87 (9%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $91 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is roughly $124 (14%) higher.
