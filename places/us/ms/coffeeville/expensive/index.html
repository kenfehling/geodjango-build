Home prices

The median home in Coffeeville is valued at $75,700. This is approximately $2,140 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $141,800 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is roughly $26,100 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Coffeeville the median rent is $604 a month. This is roughly $36 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $458 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $297 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
