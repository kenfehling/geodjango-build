Home prices

The median home in Decatur costs $106,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $39,900 (60%) higher.It is approximately $111,200 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $15,600 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Jackson.

Rents

The median rent in Decatur is $540 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $68 (11%) lower.It is approximately $522 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $307 (36%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
