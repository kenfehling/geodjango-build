Home prices

The median home in West Hattiesburg costs $144,400. It is approximately $36,860 (34%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $73,100 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $20,800 (16%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

West Hattiesburg's median rent is $843 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $97 (10%) lower. It is roughly $219 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Mobile.
