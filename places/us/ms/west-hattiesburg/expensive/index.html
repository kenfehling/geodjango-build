Home prices

Rents

West Hattiesburg's median monthly rent is $843. This is about $97 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $219 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is about $14 (1%) lower.
