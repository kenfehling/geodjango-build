In comparison to the surrounding areas, Columbus is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus costs $121,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $16,620 (15%) higher.It is about $95,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $30,800 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

Columbus's median monthly rent is $798. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $152 (23%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $264 (24%) lower, and it is about $39 (4%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
