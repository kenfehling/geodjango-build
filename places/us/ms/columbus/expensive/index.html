In comparison to its nearby areas, Columbus is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Birmingham however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Columbus is $121,900. It is about $16,620 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $95,600 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $30,800 (33%) higher compared to the city of Birmingham.

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $798. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $152 (23%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $264 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is approximately $39 (4%) lower.
