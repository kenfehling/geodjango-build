In comparison to its surrounding areas, Columbus is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Birmingham, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Columbus's median home price is $121,900. It is about $16,620 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $95,600 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $30,800 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Columbus the median rent is $798 per month. This is approximately $152 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $264 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $39 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Birmingham.
