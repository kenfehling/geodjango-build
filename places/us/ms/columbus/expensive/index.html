In comparison to its surrounding areas, Columbus is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $121,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,620 (15%) higher. It is roughly $95,600 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is about $30,800 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Columbus the median rent is $798 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $152 (23%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $264 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Birmingham is roughly $39 (4%) lower.
