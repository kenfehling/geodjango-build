Compared to its surrounding areas, Columbus is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Birmingham.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus costs $121,900. It is roughly $16,620 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $95,600 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Birmingham is about $30,800 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Columbus the median rent is $798 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $152 (23%) higher.It is roughly $264 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $39 (4%) lower compared to the city of Birmingham.
