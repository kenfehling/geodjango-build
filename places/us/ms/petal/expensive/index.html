Home prices

The median home in Petal is valued at $141,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $33,500 (30%) higher. It is approximately $75,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $18,000 (14%) higher compared to the city of Mobile.

Rents

Petal's median monthly rent is $884. This is roughly $48 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $178 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Mobile it is roughly $27 (3%) higher.
