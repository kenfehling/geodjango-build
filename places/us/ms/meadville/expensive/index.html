Home prices

The median home in Meadville is valued at $96,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $22,620 (30%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $121,100 (55%) lower, and is approximately $77,600 (44%) lower compared to the city of Baton Rouge.

Rents

Meadville's median rent is $915 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $423 (85%) higher. It is about $147 (13%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Baton Rouge it is approximately $36 (4%) higher.
