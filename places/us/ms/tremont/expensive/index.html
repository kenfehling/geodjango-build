Home prices

Tremont's median home value is $90,600. This is roughly $8,420 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $126,900 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tuscaloosa is roughly $88,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Tremont the median rent is $654 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $138 (26%) higher. It is about $408 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tuscaloosa is approximately $190 (22%) lower.
