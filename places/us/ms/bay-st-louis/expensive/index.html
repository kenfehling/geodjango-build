Home prices

The median home cost in Bay St. Louis is $172,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,100 (8%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $44,900 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is approximately $58,900 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Bay St. Louis the median monthly rent is $835. It is about $77 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $227 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of New Orleans it is roughly $163 (16%) lower.
