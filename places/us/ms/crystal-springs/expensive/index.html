Rents

In Crystal Springs the median monthly rent is $769. It is roughly $42 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $78 (9%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
