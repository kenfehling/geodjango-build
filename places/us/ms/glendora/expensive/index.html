Home prices

The median home in Glendora costs $78,800. It is approximately $3,500 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $138,700 (63%) lower, and it is about $23,000 (22%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Glendora's median monthly rent is $638. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $148 (30%) higher.It is about $424 (39%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $263 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
