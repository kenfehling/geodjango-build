Home prices

The cost of the median home in Benoit is $61,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $8,760 (16%) higher. It is roughly $155,800 (71%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $13,800 (18%) lower compared to the city of Pine Bluff.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Benoit is $591. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $72 (13%) higher. It is approximately $471 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $136 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff.
