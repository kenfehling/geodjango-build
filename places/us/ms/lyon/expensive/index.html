The median home value in Lyon is $105,600. This is roughly $44,100 (71%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $111,900 (51%) lower, and it is about $3,800 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Lyon the median rent is $625 a month. This is roughly $132 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $437 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $276 (30%) lower.
