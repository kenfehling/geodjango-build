Home prices

The median home in Lyon costs $105,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $44,100 (71%) higher.It is approximately $111,900 (51%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $3,800 (3%) higher.

Rents

Lyon's median rent is $625 a month. This is roughly $132 (26%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and it is about $276 (30%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
