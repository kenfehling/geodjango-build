Home prices

The median home value in Lyon is $105,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $44,100 (71%) higher.It is about $111,900 (51%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $3,800 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Lyon the median monthly rent is $625. This is roughly $132 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and it is about $276 (30%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
