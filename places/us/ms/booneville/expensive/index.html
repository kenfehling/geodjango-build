The median home price in Booneville is $106,500. It is approximately $22,140 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $111,000 (51%) lower, and is roughly $4,700 (4%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Booneville's median rent is $555 per month. It is roughly $71 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $507 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $346 (38%) lower.
