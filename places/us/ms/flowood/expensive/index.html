In comparison to the surrounding areas, Flowood is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Jackson, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Flowood costs $187,500. It is about $11,080 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $30,000 (13%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $96,800 (106%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

Flowood's median rent is $1,147 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $64 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $85 (8%) higher, and is roughly $300 (35%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.
