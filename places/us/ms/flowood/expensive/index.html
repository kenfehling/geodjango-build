Home prices

The value of the median home in Flowood is $187,500. It is about $11,080 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $30,000 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is roughly $96,800 (106%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Flowood is $1,147 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $64 (5%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $85 (8%) higher, and is about $300 (35%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.
