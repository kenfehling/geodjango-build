Home prices

The median home in Sandersville costs $95,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $9,380 (10%) higher. It is about $122,500 (56%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $4,300 (4%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

The median rent in Sandersville is $836 per month. This is roughly $96 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $226 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
