Compared to its surrounding areas, Lynchburg is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg is valued at $141,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $11,420 (8%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $76,000 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $39,700 (38%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Lynchburg the median rent is $876 per month. It is roughly $58 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $186 (17%) lower, and is roughly $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
