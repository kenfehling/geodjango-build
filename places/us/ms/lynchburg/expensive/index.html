Compared to nearby areas, Lynchburg is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lynchburg is $141,500. It is about $11,420 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $76,000 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is roughly $39,700 (38%) higher.

Rents

Lynchburg's median rent is $876 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $58 (6%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $186 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is approximately $25 (2%) lower.
