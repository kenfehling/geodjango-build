In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lynchburg is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg is valued at $141,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $11,420 (8%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $76,000 (34%) lower, and is approximately $39,700 (38%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lynchburg is $876. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $58 (6%) lower.It is roughly $186 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is about $25 (2%) lower.
