Home prices

Greenville's median home value is $78,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $14,440 (22%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $139,200 (64%) lower, and is about $2,800 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff.

Rents

The median rent in Greenville is $694 a month. It is roughly $115 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $368 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $33 (4%) lower compared to the city of Pine Bluff.
