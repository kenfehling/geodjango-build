The median home in Greenville is valued at $78,300. This is roughly $14,440 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $139,200 (64%) lower, and it is approximately $2,800 (3%) higher compared to the city of Pine Bluff.

Rents

Greenville's median rent is $694 a month. It is approximately $115 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $368 (34%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $33 (4%) lower compared to the city of Pine Bluff.
