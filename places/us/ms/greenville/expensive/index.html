Home prices

The median home in Greenville costs $78,300. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $14,440 (22%) higher.It is about $139,200 (64%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff it is approximately $2,800 (3%) higher.

Rents

Greenville's median monthly rent is $694. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $115 (19%) higher.It is about $368 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $33 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff.
