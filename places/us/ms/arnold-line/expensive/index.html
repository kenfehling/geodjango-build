Home prices

The median home in Arnold Line costs $105,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $10,300 (8%) lower. It is approximately $112,400 (51%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson it is roughly $14,400 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Arnold Line the median rent is $1,227 a month. This is roughly $364 (42%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $165 (15%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $380 (44%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.
