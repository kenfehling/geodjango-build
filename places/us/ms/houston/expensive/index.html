Home prices

Houston's median home value is $64,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $880 (1%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $152,600 (70%) lower, and it is roughly $80,500 (55%) lower compared to the city of Tupelo.

Rents

In Houston the median rent is $628 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $12 (1%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $434 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Tupelo is roughly $162 (20%) lower.
