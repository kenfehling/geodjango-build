Home prices

The median home in Verona is valued at $75,100. It is approximately $32,340 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $142,400 (65%) lower, and is about $67,300 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Florence.

Rents

Verona's median rent is $667 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $28 (4%) higher. It is about $395 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Florence it is approximately $24 (3%) higher.
