Compared to other places in this area, Shelby is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Shelby costs $64,100. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $19,300 (23%) lower. It is about $153,400 (70%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $37,700 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Shelby's median rent is $481 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $46 (8%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $581 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $420 (46%) lower.
