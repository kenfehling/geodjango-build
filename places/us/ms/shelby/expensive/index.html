Home prices

The median home in Shelby costs $64,100. This is roughly $19,300 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $153,400 (70%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $37,700 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Shelby the median monthly rent is $481. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $46 (8%) lower. It is approximately $581 (54%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $420 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
