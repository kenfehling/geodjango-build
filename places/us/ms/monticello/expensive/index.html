Home prices

The median home in Monticello is valued at $98,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $8,100 (7%) lower. It is approximately $118,600 (54%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is about $8,200 (9%) higher.

Rents

In Monticello the median monthly rent is $589. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $181 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $473 (44%) lower, and is about $258 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
