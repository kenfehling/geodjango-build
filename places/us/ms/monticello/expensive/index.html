In comparison to other places in this area, Monticello is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Jackson, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Monticello is $98,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $8,100 (7%) lower.It is roughly $118,600 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $8,200 (9%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

The median rent in Monticello is $589 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $181 (23%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $473 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $258 (30%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
