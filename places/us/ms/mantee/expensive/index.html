Home prices

The median home in Mantee costs $82,300. It is roughly $14,860 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $135,200 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tupelo it is about $63,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mantee is $717 a month. It is about $88 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $345 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $73 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Tupelo.
