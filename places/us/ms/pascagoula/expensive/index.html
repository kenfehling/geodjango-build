Home prices

The median home value in Pascagoula is $110,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $17,100 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $106,600 (49%) lower, and it is about $120,600 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.

Rents

The median rent in Pascagoula is $752 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $194 (20%) lower. It is approximately $310 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New Orleans is about $246 (24%) lower.
