Compared to nearby areas, Pascagoula is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of New Orleans, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Pascagoula's median home value is $110,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $17,100 (13%) lower. It is roughly $106,600 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is about $120,600 (52%) lower.

Rents

Pascagoula's median rent is $752 per month. It is roughly $194 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $310 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $246 (24%) lower compared to the city of New Orleans.
