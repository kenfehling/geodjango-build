Home prices

The median home in Pascagoula costs $110,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $17,100 (13%) lower.It is approximately $106,600 (49%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New Orleans is about $120,600 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Pascagoula the median monthly rent is $752. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $194 (20%) lower.It is approximately $310 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $246 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of New Orleans.
