In comparison to the surrounding areas, Pascagoula is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New Orleans.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pascagoula is $110,900. It is roughly $17,100 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $106,600 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New Orleans it is about $120,600 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Pascagoula the median rent is $752 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $194 (20%) lower. It is approximately $310 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New Orleans is approximately $246 (24%) lower.
