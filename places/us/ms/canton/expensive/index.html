Home prices

Rents

Canton's median rent is $777 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $199 (20%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $70 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
