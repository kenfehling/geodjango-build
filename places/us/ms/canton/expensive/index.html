In comparison to the surrounding areas, Canton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Jackson.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Canton is $119,200. This is roughly $53,700 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $98,300 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $28,500 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Jackson.

Rents

Canton's median rent is $777 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $199 (20%) lower. It is about $285 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $70 (8%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
