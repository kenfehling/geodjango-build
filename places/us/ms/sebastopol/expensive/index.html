Home prices

The median home in Sebastopol costs $86,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $14,460 (19%) higher.It is roughly $130,700 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $3,900 (4%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

In Sebastopol the median rent is $636 a month. This is roughly $61 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $426 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson it is about $211 (24%) lower.
