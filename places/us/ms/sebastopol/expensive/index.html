Home prices

The median home in Sebastopol is valued at $86,800. This is roughly $14,460 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $130,700 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is approximately $3,900 (4%) lower.

Rents

Sebastopol's median rent is $636 per month. This is about $61 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $426 (40%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $211 (24%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
