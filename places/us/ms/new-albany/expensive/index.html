Home prices

The median home cost in New Albany is $121,900. It is roughly $33,380 (37%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $95,600 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $20,100 (19%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

New Albany's median monthly rent is $754. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $169 (28%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is roughly $147 (16%) lower.
