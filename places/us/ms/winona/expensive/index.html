Home prices

The median home in Winona costs $92,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13,820 (17%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $124,700 (57%) lower, and is roughly $2,100 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Jackson.

Rents

Winona's median rent is $598 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $45 (8%) higher.It is about $464 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson it is roughly $249 (29%) lower.
