In comparison to its nearby areas, Winona is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Jackson, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Winona is $92,800. It is roughly $13,820 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $124,700 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $2,100 (2%) higher compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

Winona's median monthly rent is $598. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $45 (8%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $464 (43%) lower, and it is about $249 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
