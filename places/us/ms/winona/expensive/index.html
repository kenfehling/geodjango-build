Home prices

The median home price in Winona is $92,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $13,820 (17%) higher.It is about $124,700 (57%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is approximately $2,100 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Winona is $598 a month. This is roughly $45 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $464 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson it is about $249 (29%) lower.
