Home prices

The median home in North Vernon is valued at $93,500. This is approximately $17,440 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $124,000 (57%) lower, and it is about $44,500 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Vernon is $689. It is about $128 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $373 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $49 (6%) lower.
