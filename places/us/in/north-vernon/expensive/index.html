Home prices

The price of the median home in North Vernon is $93,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $17,440 (22%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $44,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Vernon is $689. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $128 (15%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $373 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $49 (6%) lower.
