Home prices

The median home in Van Buren costs $63,200. It is roughly $24,860 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $154,300 (70%) lower, and is about $51,900 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Van Buren is $838. This is approximately $122 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $74 (9%) higher.
