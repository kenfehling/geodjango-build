Home prices

The median home in Van Buren costs $63,200. It is roughly $24,860 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $154,300 (70%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $51,900 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Van Buren is $838 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $122 (16%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and is approximately $74 (9%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
