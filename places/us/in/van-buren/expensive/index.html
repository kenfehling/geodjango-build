Compared to other places in this area, Van Buren is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Van Buren is $63,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $24,860 (28%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $154,300 (70%) lower, and it is approximately $51,900 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Van Buren the median monthly rent is $838. This is roughly $122 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $224 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $74 (9%) higher.
