Home prices

Rents

In Dupont the median monthly rent is $913. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $165 (22%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $149 (14%) lower, and is approximately $175 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
