Compared to other places in this area, Dublin is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home value in Dublin is $73,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $5,960 (8%) higher. It is roughly $144,200 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $64,700 (46%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Dublin the median monthly rent is $643. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $45 (6%) lower. It is roughly $419 (39%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $95 (12%) lower.
