Home prices

The median home value in Dublin is $73,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $5,960 (8%) higher.It is about $144,200 (66%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $64,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Dublin the median rent is $643 per month. It is about $45 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $419 (39%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $95 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
