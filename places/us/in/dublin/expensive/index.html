Home prices

Dublin's median home value is $73,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $5,960 (8%) higher.It is roughly $144,200 (66%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $64,700 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Dublin's median monthly rent is $643. This is about $45 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $419 (39%) lower, and it is approximately $95 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
