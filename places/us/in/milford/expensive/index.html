Home prices

The median home cost in Milford is $108,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $11,760 (9%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $109,500 (50%) lower, and is roughly $7,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Milford is $613 a month. It is about $224 (26%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $449 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $151 (19%) lower.
