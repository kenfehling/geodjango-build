Home prices

The median home cost in Portland is $71,100. It is about $2,160 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $146,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $44,000 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Portland is $660 per month. It is approximately $59 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $402 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $104 (13%) lower.
