Compared to its nearby areas, Centerville is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Centerville is valued at $88,300. It is approximately $10,040 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $49,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Centerville is $886 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $165 (22%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $176 (16%) lower, and it is about $148 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
