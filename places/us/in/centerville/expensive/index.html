Compared to other places in this area, Centerville is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Centerville is $88,300. This is approximately $10,040 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $49,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $886. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $165 (22%) higher. It is roughly $176 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $148 (20%) higher.
