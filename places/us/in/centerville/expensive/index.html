Home prices

The price of the median home in Centerville is $88,300. Compared to nearby areas it is about $10,040 (12%) higher. It is roughly $129,200 (59%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $49,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Centerville's median monthly rent is $886. This is about $165 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $176 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $148 (20%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
