Home prices

The cost of the median home in Remington is $94,100. This is approximately $5,600 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $123,400 (56%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $163,900 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Remington the median rent is $721 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $25 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $391 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
