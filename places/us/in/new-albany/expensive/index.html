In comparison to other places in this area, New Albany is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Home prices

The median home in New Albany is valued at $122,800. This is approximately $19,340 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $94,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $67,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Albany is $784. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $43 (5%) lower. It is roughly $278 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $112 (12%) lower.
