Home prices

The median home cost in New Albany is $122,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $19,340 (13%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $94,700 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $67,000 (35%) lower.

Rents

In New Albany the median monthly rent is $784. This is roughly $43 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $278 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is about $112 (12%) lower.
