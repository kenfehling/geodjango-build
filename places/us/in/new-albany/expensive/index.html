Home prices

The median home cost in New Albany is $122,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $19,340 (13%) lower.It is about $94,700 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $67,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

New Albany's median rent is $784 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $43 (5%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $278 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $112 (12%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
