In comparison to its surrounding areas, Richmond is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Richmond is $88,400. It is roughly $6,080 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $129,100 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $49,600 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond is $679 per month. This is roughly $106 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $383 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $59 (7%) lower.
