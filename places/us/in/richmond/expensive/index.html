Compared to its surrounding areas, Richmond is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Richmond is $88,400. This is roughly $6,080 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $129,100 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $49,600 (35%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Richmond's median monthly rent is $679. It is roughly $106 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $383 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $59 (7%) lower.
