Home prices

Richmond's median home price is $88,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $6,080 (7%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $129,100 (59%) lower, and is about $49,600 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Richmond the median monthly rent is $679. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $106 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $383 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $59 (7%) lower.
