Home prices

The median home in Warsaw costs $135,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $22,080 (19%) higher. It is approximately $82,300 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $20,100 (17%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Warsaw the median monthly rent is $827. This is approximately $26 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $235 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $63 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
