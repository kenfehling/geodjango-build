Home prices

The price of the median home in La Crosse is $100,900. This is roughly $15,440 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $116,600 (53%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $157,100 (60%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in La Crosse is $942. This is roughly $145 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $120 (11%) lower, and it is roughly $170 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
