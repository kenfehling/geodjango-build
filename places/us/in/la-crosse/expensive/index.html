Home prices

The median home in La Crosse costs $100,900. It is about $15,440 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $116,600 (53%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $157,100 (60%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

La Crosse's median monthly rent is $942. It is approximately $145 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $120 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $170 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
