Compared to its surrounding areas, La Crosse is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in La Crosse costs $100,900. This is about $15,440 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $116,600 (53%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $157,100 (60%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In La Crosse the median monthly rent is $942. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $145 (18%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $120 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $170 (15%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
