Home prices

The median home in La Crosse costs $100,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $15,440 (13%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $116,600 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $157,100 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in La Crosse is $942. It is approximately $145 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $120 (11%) lower, and is approximately $170 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
