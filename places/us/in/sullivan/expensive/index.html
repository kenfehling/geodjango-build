Home prices

The median home in Sullivan costs $73,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $15,940 (27%) higher. It is roughly $143,800 (66%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $20,500 (21%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sullivan is $616. It is approximately $64 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $446 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $158 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
