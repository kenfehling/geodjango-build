Rents

The median monthly rent in Montpelier is $660. It is approximately $84 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $402 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $104 (13%) lower.
