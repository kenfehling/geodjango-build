Home prices

The median home price in Galveston is $88,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $10,680 (13%) higher. It is roughly $129,200 (59%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $26,800 (23%) lower.

Rents

Galveston's median rent is $732 a month. It is roughly $45 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $330 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $32 (4%) lower.
