Compared to its surrounding areas, Galveston is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Galveston is $88,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $10,680 (13%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $129,200 (59%) lower, and is roughly $26,800 (23%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Galveston's median monthly rent is $732. This is roughly $45 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $330 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is about $32 (4%) lower.
