Rents

Galveston's median rent is $732 per month. It is approximately $45 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $330 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $32 (4%) lower.
