In comparison to its surrounding areas, Galveston is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Galveston costs $88,300. This is about $10,680 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $129,200 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $26,800 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Galveston is $732. It is about $45 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $330 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $32 (4%) lower.
