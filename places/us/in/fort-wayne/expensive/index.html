Home prices

The median home price in Fort Wayne is $115,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $21,017 (15%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $102,400 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Wayne the median rent is $764 a month. This is roughly $97 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $298 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole.
