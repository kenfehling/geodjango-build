The cost of the median home in Fort Wayne is $115,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $21,017 (15%) lower. It is roughly $102,400 (47%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole.

Rents

In Fort Wayne the median monthly rent is $764. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $97 (11%) lower. It is roughly $298 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation.
