Home prices

The median home value in Noblesville is $209,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,180 (6%) lower.It is about $8,400 (3%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $94,000 (81%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Noblesville is $1,039. This is about $35 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $23 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $275 (35%) higher.
