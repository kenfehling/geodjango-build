Home prices

The median home cost in Noblesville is $209,100. It is approximately $14,180 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $8,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $94,000 (81%) higher.

Rents

In Noblesville the median rent is $1,039 per month. It is about $35 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $23 (2%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is about $275 (35%) higher.
