Home prices

The median home price in Monroe City is $75,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $3,960 (5%) higher. It is roughly $142,000 (65%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $18,700 (19%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

In Monroe City the median monthly rent is $846. It is about $204 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $216 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville it is about $72 (9%) higher.
