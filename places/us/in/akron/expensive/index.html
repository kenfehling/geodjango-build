Home prices

The median home in Akron costs $82,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $7,700 (10%) higher.It is roughly $134,700 (61%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $32,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Akron the median rent is $685 per month. This is roughly $21 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $377 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $79 (10%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
