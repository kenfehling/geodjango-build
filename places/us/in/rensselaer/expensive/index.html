Home prices

The median home in Rensselaer is valued at $115,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $33,680 (41%) higher. It is roughly $101,800 (46%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $142,300 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Rensselaer the median rent is $733 a month. This is approximately $56 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $329 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $379 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
