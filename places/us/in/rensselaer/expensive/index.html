Home prices

The median home price in Rensselaer is $115,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $33,680 (41%) higher.It is roughly $101,800 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $142,300 (55%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Rensselaer's median rent is $733 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $56 (8%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $329 (30%) lower, and is roughly $379 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
