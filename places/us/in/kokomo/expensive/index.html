Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kokomo is $89,100. This is about $1,020 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $128,400 (59%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $26,000 (22%) lower.

Rents

Kokomo's median monthly rent is $695. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $367 (34%) lower, and is approximately $69 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
