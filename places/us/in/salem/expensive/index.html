Home prices

The median home in Salem costs $82,600. It is approximately $5,500 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $134,900 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is about $118,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Salem the median monthly rent is $557. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $116 (17%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $505 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington is approximately $360 (39%) lower.
