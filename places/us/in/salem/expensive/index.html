Home prices

The price of the median home in Salem is $82,600. This is roughly $5,500 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $134,900 (62%) lower, and is about $118,100 (58%) lower compared to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Salem is $557. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $116 (17%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $505 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington is roughly $360 (39%) lower.
