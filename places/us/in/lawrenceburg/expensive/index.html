In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lawrenceburg is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrenceburg is valued at $140,900. It is roughly $68,920 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $76,600 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $2,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Lawrenceburg the median rent is $703 a month. This is approximately $221 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $359 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $35 (4%) lower.
