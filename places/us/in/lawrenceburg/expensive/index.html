In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lawrenceburg is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrenceburg costs $140,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $68,920 (32%) lower. It is about $76,600 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $2,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Lawrenceburg's median rent is $703 per month. This is about $221 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $359 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $35 (4%) lower.
