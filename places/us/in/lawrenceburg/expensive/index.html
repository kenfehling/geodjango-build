Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lawrenceburg is $140,900. This is approximately $5,820 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $76,600 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $2,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lawrenceburg is $703. This is about $252 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $359 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $35 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
