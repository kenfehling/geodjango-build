Home prices

The median home price in New Castle is $73,300. It is roughly $12,680 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $144,200 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $64,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

In New Castle the median rent is $707 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $20 (2%) higher. It is roughly $355 (33%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $31 (4%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
