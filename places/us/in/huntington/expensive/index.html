Home prices

The cost of the median home in Huntington is $81,800. It is roughly $2,920 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $135,700 (62%) lower, and it is roughly $33,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Huntington is $691. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $371 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $73 (9%) lower.
