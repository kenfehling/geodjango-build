Home prices

The median home in Greensboro costs $100,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $25,900 (34%) higher.It is about $117,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $38,000 (27%) lower.

Rents

Greensboro's median rent is $633 per month. It is approximately $132 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $429 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $105 (14%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
