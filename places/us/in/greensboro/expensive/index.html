Compared to the surrounding areas, Greensboro is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Greensboro is $100,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $25,900 (34%) higher. It is roughly $117,500 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $38,000 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Greensboro is $633 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $132 (17%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $429 (40%) lower, and is approximately $105 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
