Home prices

The median home in Greensboro is valued at $100,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $25,900 (34%) higher. It is roughly $117,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $38,000 (27%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Greensboro's median monthly rent is $633. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $132 (17%) lower. It is approximately $429 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $105 (14%) lower.
