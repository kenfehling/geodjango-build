Home prices

Mount Vernon's median home cost is $123,200. It is approximately $49,180 (66%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $94,300 (43%) lower, and it is about $29,000 (30%) higher compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Vernon is $698. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $42 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $364 (34%) lower, and is about $76 (9%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.
