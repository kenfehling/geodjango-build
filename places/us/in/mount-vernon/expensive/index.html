Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon costs $123,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $49,180 (66%) higher.It is roughly $94,300 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $29,000 (30%) higher compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

In Mount Vernon the median rent is $698 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $42 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $364 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville it is roughly $76 (9%) lower.
