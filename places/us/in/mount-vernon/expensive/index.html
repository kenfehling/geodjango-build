Home prices

Mount Vernon's median home value is $123,200. This is about $49,180 (66%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $94,300 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville it is about $29,000 (30%) higher.

Rents

Mount Vernon's median monthly rent is $698. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $42 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $364 (34%) lower, and is roughly $76 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
