The cost of the median home in Alexandria is $72,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $5,340 (7%) higher. It is roughly $145,100 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $42,700 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Alexandria the median monthly rent is $780. It is approximately $72 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $16 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
