Home prices

The price of the median home in Columbia City is $125,200. This is roughly $30,680 (32%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $92,300 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $10,100 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbia City is $623. This is approximately $80 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $439 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $141 (18%) lower.
