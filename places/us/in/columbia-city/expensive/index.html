Rents

In Columbia City the median rent is $623 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $80 (11%) lower.It is approximately $439 (41%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $141 (18%) lower.
