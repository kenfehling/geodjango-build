Home prices

The cost of the median home in Columbia City is $125,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $30,680 (32%) higher. It is roughly $92,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $10,100 (8%) higher.

Rents

Columbia City's median rent is $623 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $80 (11%) lower. It is about $439 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $141 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
