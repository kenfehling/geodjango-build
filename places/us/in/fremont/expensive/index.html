Compared to other places in this area, Fremont is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Toledo however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fremont is $100,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $96,120 (48%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $117,200 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Toledo it is roughly $19,500 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fremont is $909. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $139 (18%) higher.It is roughly $153 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $184 (25%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.
