Home prices

Brownsburg's median home price is $195,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $39,140 (25%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $21,800 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington is approximately $5,000 (2%) lower.

Rents

Brownsburg's median monthly rent is $1,135. It is roughly $194 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $73 (6%) higher, and is roughly $218 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Bloomington.
