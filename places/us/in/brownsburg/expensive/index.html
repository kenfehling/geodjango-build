Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownsburg is $1,135. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $194 (20%) higher.It is about $73 (6%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is about $218 (23%) higher.
