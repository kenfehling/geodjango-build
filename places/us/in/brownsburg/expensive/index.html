Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brownsburg is $195,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $39,140 (25%) higher.It is roughly $21,800 (10%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bloomington is roughly $5,000 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownsburg is $1,135. This is about $194 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $73 (6%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is roughly $218 (23%) higher.
