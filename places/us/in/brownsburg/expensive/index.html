Home prices

The median home in Brownsburg costs $195,700. It is about $39,140 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $21,800 (10%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $5,000 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

The median rent in Brownsburg is $1,135 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $194 (20%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $73 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $218 (23%) higher.
