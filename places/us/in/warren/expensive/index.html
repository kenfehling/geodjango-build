Home prices

The cost of the median home in Warren is $78,300. It is about $5,500 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $139,200 (64%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $36,800 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Warren is $910 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $196 (27%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $152 (14%) lower, and it is about $146 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
