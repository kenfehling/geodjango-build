Home prices

The median home in Warren is valued at $78,300. This is roughly $5,500 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $139,200 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $36,800 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Warren the median rent is $910 per month. This is roughly $196 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $152 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $146 (19%) higher.
