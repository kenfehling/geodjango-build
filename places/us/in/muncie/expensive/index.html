Home prices

The median home in Muncie is valued at $73,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $15,560 (17%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $144,000 (66%) lower, and it is about $64,500 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Muncie is $722. This is approximately $15 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $340 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $16 (2%) lower.
