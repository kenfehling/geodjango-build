Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Muncie is $722 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $15 (2%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $340 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $16 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
