In comparison to nearby areas, Muncie is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Muncie costs $73,500. This is approximately $15,560 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $144,000 (66%) lower, and is about $64,500 (46%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Muncie is $722. It is about $15 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $340 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $16 (2%) lower.
