In comparison to other places in this area, Greendale is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Greendale is valued at $131,900. It is approximately $56,140 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $85,600 (39%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $6,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Greendale is $685 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $167 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $377 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $53 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
