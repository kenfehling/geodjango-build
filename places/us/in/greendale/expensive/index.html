In comparison to its nearby areas, Greendale is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Greendale is valued at $131,900. It is roughly $56,140 (29%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $85,600 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $6,100 (4%) lower.

Rents

Greendale's median monthly rent is $685. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $167 (19%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $377 (35%) lower, and is roughly $53 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
