Compared to nearby areas, Greendale is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Greendale is $131,900. It is approximately $25,480 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $85,600 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $6,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Greendale's median rent is $685 per month. It is roughly $160 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $377 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $53 (7%) lower.
