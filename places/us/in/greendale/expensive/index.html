Home prices

The median home cost in Greendale is $131,900. It is roughly $56,140 (29%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $85,600 (39%) lower, and is approximately $6,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Greendale is $685 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $167 (19%) lower. It is roughly $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $53 (7%) lower.
