Compared to its surrounding areas, Greendale is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home value in Greendale is $131,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $25,480 (16%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $85,600 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $6,100 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Greendale is $685 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $160 (18%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $377 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $53 (7%) lower.
