Home prices

The median home in Newburgh costs $153,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $46,920 (44%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $64,000 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville it is about $59,300 (62%) higher.

Rents

In Newburgh the median rent is $833 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $105 (11%) lower. It is about $229 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is approximately $59 (7%) higher.
