Home prices

The price of the median home in West Lafayette is $224,000. It is about $89,240 (66%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $6,500 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Lafayette it is approximately $109,200 (95%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in West Lafayette is $912 a month. This is approximately $78 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $150 (14%) lower, and is approximately $95 (11%) higher compared to the city of Lafayette.
