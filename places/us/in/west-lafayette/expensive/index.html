Rents

The median monthly rent in West Lafayette is $912. This is about $78 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $150 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Lafayette is roughly $95 (11%) higher.
