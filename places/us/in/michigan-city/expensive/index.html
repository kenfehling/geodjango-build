In comparison to other places in this area, Michigan City is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Michigan City is valued at $89,900. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $182,340 (66%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $127,600 (58%) lower, and it is about $168,100 (65%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Michigan City's median monthly rent is $752. Compared to nearby areas this is about $198 (20%) lower.It is about $310 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $360 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
