Home prices

The median home in Greensburg costs $120,800. It is approximately $27,620 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $96,700 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $17,200 (12%) lower.

Rents

Greensburg's median monthly rent is $774. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $61 (8%) higher.It is roughly $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $36 (4%) higher.
