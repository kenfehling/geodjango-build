Home prices

The median home in Greensburg costs $120,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $27,620 (29%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $96,700 (44%) lower, and is approximately $17,200 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Greensburg's median rent is $774 a month. This is approximately $61 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $36 (4%) higher.
