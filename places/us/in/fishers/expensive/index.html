Home prices

The median home in Fishers costs $264,200. It is roughly $98,480 (59%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $46,700 (21%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $149,100 (129%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Fishers the median rent is $1,250 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $243 (24%) higher.It is about $188 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $486 (63%) higher.
