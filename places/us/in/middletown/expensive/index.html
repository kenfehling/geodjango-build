Home prices

The median home in Middletown is valued at $73,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $7,860 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $144,200 (66%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $64,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Middletown is $641 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $107 (14%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $421 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $97 (13%) lower.
