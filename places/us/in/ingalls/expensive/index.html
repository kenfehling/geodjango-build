In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ingalls is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Ingalls is valued at $105,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $73,940 (41%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $111,800 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $9,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Ingalls the median monthly rent is $952. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $59 (5%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $110 (10%) lower, and is about $188 (24%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
