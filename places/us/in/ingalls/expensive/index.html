Compared to the surrounding areas, Ingalls is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Ingalls's median home cost is $105,700. It is about $73,940 (41%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $111,800 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $9,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

Ingalls's median monthly rent is $952. It is approximately $59 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $110 (10%) lower, and is approximately $188 (24%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
