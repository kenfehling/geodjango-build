Home prices

The median home in Spring Grove is valued at $107,400. This is about $25,520 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $110,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $30,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Spring Grove the median monthly rent is $725. This is about $54 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
