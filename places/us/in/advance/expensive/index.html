The median home in Advance is valued at $98,800. This is roughly $19,540 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $101,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

Advance's median monthly rent is $888. It is roughly $51 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $174 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $29 (3%) lower.
