In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Paul is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in St. Paul is $82,700. This is roughly $14,520 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $134,800 (61%) lower, and is about $55,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In St. Paul the median monthly rent is $794. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $84 (11%) higher. It is about $268 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $56 (7%) higher.
