Home prices

St. Paul's median home value is $82,700. It is approximately $14,520 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $134,800 (61%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $55,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In St. Paul the median monthly rent is $794. This is about $84 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $268 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $56 (7%) higher.
