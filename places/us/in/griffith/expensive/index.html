Compared to other places in this area, Griffith is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Griffith costs $141,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $36,380 (20%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $75,800 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $116,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Griffith the median monthly rent is $919. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $146 (13%) lower. It is approximately $143 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $193 (17%) lower.
