Compared to other places in this area, Griffith is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Griffith costs $141,700. This is about $36,380 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $75,800 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $116,300 (45%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Griffith is $919. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $146 (13%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $143 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $193 (17%) lower.
