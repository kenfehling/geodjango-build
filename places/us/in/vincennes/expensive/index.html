Home prices

The value of the median home in Vincennes is $77,700. It is approximately $13,260 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $139,800 (64%) lower, and is about $16,500 (17%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Vincennes is $669. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $15 (2%) lower. It is roughly $393 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Evansville it is approximately $105 (13%) lower.
