Compared to other places in this area, Vincennes is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Evansville.

Home prices

The median home cost in Vincennes is $77,700. This is about $13,260 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $139,800 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is about $16,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

Vincennes's median monthly rent is $669. This is about $15 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $393 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $105 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
