Home prices

The median home price in Vincennes is $77,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $13,260 (20%) higher. It is roughly $139,800 (64%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $16,500 (17%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

Vincennes's median monthly rent is $669. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $15 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $393 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is roughly $105 (13%) lower.
