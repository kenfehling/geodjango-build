Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $165,100. This is approximately $75,000 (83%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $52,400 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $84,300 (104%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In Hamilton the median monthly rent is $755. It is about $28 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $307 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is about $30 (4%) higher.
