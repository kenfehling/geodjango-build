Home prices

The price of the median home in Homecroft is $148,200. It is roughly $22,200 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $69,300 (31%) lower, and is roughly $52,500 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

In Homecroft the median rent is $1,167 a month. This is approximately $235 (25%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $105 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is approximately $250 (27%) higher.
