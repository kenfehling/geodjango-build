Home prices

The median home in Leesburg costs $115,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $16,240 (12%) lower. It is approximately $101,700 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is is very similar.

Rents

Leesburg's median rent is $950 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $160 (20%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $112 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $186 (24%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
