Compared to the surrounding areas, Leesburg is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Leesburg is $115,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $16,240 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $101,700 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is is very similar.

Rents

Leesburg's median monthly rent is $950. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $160 (20%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $112 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $186 (24%) higher.
