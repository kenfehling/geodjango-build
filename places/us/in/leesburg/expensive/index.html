Rents

In Leesburg the median monthly rent is $950. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $160 (20%) higher.It is approximately $112 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $186 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
