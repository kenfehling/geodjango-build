Compared to other places in this area, Leesburg is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

Leesburg's median home price is $115,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $16,240 (12%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $101,700 (46%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Leesburg's median monthly rent is $950. This is about $160 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $112 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $186 (24%) higher.
