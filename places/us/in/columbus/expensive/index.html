Home prices

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $951 per month. It is roughly $107 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $111 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $213 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
