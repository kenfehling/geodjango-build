Home prices

The median home price in Columbus is $166,200. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $82,880 (99%) higher.It is roughly $51,300 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $28,200 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbus is $951. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $107 (12%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $111 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $213 (28%) higher.
