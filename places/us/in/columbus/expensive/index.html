In comparison to its nearby areas, Columbus is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Columbus's median home price is $166,200. It is about $82,880 (99%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $51,300 (23%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $28,200 (20%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $951. It is approximately $107 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $111 (10%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $213 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
