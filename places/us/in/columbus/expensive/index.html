In comparison to its surrounding areas, Columbus is substantially more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Columbus's median home value is $166,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $82,880 (99%) higher. It is roughly $51,300 (23%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $28,200 (20%) higher.

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $951. It is about $107 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $111 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $213 (28%) higher.
