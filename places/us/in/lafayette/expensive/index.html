Compared to the surrounding areas, Lafayette is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Bloomington.

Home prices

Lafayette's median home price is $114,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $48,640 (29%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $102,700 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is approximately $85,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lafayette is $817 per month. This is about $49 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $100 (10%) lower compared to the city of Bloomington.
