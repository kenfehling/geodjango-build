Compared to the surrounding areas, Lafayette is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Bloomington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lafayette costs $114,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $48,640 (29%) lower. It is about $102,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $85,900 (42%) lower compared to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

Lafayette's median rent is $817 a month. It is approximately $49 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $245 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is roughly $100 (10%) lower.
