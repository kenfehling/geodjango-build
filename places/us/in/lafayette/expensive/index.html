Compared to nearby areas, Lafayette is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Bloomington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lafayette costs $114,800. It is approximately $48,640 (29%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $102,700 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is about $85,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lafayette is $817 per month. It is approximately $49 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $100 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.
