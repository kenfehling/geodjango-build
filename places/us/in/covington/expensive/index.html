Home prices

Rents

Covington's median monthly rent is $660. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $69 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $402 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is approximately $292 (30%) lower.
