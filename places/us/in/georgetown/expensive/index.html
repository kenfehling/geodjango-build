Home prices

The median home cost in Georgetown is $193,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $61,120 (46%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $24,300 (11%) lower, and is roughly $7,500 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

Georgetown's median monthly rent is $764. It is approximately $37 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $298 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is about $153 (16%) lower.
