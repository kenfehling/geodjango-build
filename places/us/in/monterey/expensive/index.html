Home prices

The cost of the median home in Monterey is $82,500. This is about $15,800 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $135,000 (62%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $175,500 (68%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Monterey is $792 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $135 (20%) higher.It is roughly $270 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $320 (28%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
