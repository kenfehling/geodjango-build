Home prices

The median home in Monterey costs $82,500. This is about $15,800 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $135,000 (62%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $175,500 (68%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monterey is $792. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $135 (20%) higher. It is about $270 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $320 (28%) lower.
