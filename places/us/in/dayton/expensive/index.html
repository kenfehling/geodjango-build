Home prices

The median home in Dayton is valued at $127,400. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $4,680 (3%) lower. It is about $90,100 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $73,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $865. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $197 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $52 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.
