In comparison to other places in this area, Elkhart is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Elkhart costs $95,900. This is approximately $57,380 (37%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $121,600 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $19,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Elkhart the median monthly rent is $790. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $216 (21%) lower. It is approximately $272 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $26 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
