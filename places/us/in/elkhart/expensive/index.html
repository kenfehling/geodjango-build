Home prices

The median home in Elkhart costs $95,900. This is approximately $57,380 (37%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $121,600 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $19,200 (16%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Elkhart is $790 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $216 (21%) lower.It is approximately $272 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $26 (3%) higher.
