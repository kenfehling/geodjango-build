Rents

The median rent in Union City is $638 per month. This is roughly $24 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $100 (13%) lower.
