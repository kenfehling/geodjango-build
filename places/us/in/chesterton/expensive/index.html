Home prices

The median home in Chesterton costs $190,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $78,280 (29%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $27,100 (12%) lower, and is about $67,600 (26%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Chesterton the median rent is $933 per month. This is about $466 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $129 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $179 (16%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
