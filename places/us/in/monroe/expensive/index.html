Home prices

The median home in Monroe is valued at $110,800. This is approximately $28,140 (34%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $106,700 (49%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $4,300 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Monroe the median rent is $744 per month. This is roughly $125 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $318 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $20 (2%) lower.
