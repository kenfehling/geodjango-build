In comparison to the surrounding areas, Charlestown is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Charlestown costs $136,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $71,500 (34%) lower. It is roughly $81,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $1,500 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Charlestown's median monthly rent is $758. It is roughly $342 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $304 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $20 (2%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
