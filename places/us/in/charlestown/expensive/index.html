Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlestown is $136,500. This is approximately $71,500 (34%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $81,000 (37%) lower, and it is about $1,500 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Charlestown the median monthly rent is $758. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $342 (31%) lower.It is about $304 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $20 (2%) higher.
