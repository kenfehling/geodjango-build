In comparison to the surrounding areas, Evansville is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

The median home in Evansville costs $94,200. This is about $45,633 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $123,300 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Evansville the median rent is $774 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $166 (17%) lower. It is approximately $288 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole.
