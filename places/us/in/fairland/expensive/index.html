Home prices

The median home in Fairland costs $123,000. This is about $12,640 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $94,500 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $15,000 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairland is $846. This is roughly $51 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $216 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $108 (14%) higher.
