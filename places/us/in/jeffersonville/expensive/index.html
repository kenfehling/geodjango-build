In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jeffersonville is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Jeffersonville is valued at $136,900. This is roughly $143,600 (51%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $80,600 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is is very similar.

Rents

In Jeffersonville the median rent is $856 per month. This is roughly $186 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $206 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $118 (15%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
