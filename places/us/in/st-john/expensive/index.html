Home prices

The median home in St. John is valued at $290,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $113,720 (64%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $72,700 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $32,200 (12%) higher.

Rents

In St. John the median monthly rent is $908. This is roughly $173 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $204 (18%) lower.
