In comparison to the surrounding areas, Atlanta is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Atlanta costs $96,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $12,220 (11%) lower. It is approximately $120,800 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $18,400 (15%) lower.

Rents

Atlanta's median rent is $809 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $29 (3%) higher. It is about $253 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $45 (5%) higher.
