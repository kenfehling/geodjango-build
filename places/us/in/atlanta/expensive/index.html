In comparison to the surrounding areas, Atlanta is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home cost in Atlanta is $96,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $12,220 (11%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $120,800 (55%) lower, and is about $18,400 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Atlanta's median monthly rent is $809. This is approximately $29 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $253 (23%) lower, and is approximately $45 (5%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
