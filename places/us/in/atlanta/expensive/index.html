In comparison to other places in this area, Atlanta is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Atlanta is valued at $96,700. It is approximately $12,220 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $120,800 (55%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $18,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Atlanta is $809 per month. This is roughly $29 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $253 (23%) lower, and is about $45 (5%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
