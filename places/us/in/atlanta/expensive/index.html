Home prices

Atlanta's median home cost is $96,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $12,220 (11%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $120,800 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $18,400 (15%) lower.

Rents

Atlanta's median rent is $809 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $29 (3%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $253 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $45 (5%) higher.
