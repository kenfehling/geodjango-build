Compared to its nearby areas, Atlanta is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Atlanta costs $96,700. It is about $12,220 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $120,800 (55%) lower, and is roughly $18,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Atlanta is $809 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $29 (3%) higher. It is roughly $253 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $45 (5%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
