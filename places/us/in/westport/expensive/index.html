Home prices

The median home in Westport is valued at $96,000. It is about $18,880 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $121,500 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $42,000 (30%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Westport the median monthly rent is $804. This is approximately $24 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $258 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $66 (8%) higher.
