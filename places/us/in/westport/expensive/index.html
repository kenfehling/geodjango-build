In comparison to its surrounding areas, Westport is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Westport costs $96,000. This is about $18,880 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $121,500 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $42,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Westport the median rent is $804 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $258 (24%) lower, and it is about $66 (8%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
