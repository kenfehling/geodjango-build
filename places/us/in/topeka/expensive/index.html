In comparison to other places in this area, Topeka is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Topeka costs $99,800. This is about $24,120 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $117,700 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $15,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Topeka is $699. It is approximately $39 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $363 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $65 (8%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
