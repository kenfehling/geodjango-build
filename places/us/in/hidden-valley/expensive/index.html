Home prices

The median home price in Hidden Valley is $195,500. It is about $6,860 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $22,000 (10%) lower, and is about $57,500 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Hidden Valley is $1,380 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $672 (94%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $318 (29%) higher, and is approximately $642 (86%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
