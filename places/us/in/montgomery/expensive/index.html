Compared to the surrounding areas, Montgomery is substantially more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Evansville, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery costs $182,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $107,640 (143%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $34,700 (15%) lower, and is about $88,600 (94%) higher in comparison to the city of Evansville.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $600. This is roughly $24 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $462 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is about $174 (22%) lower.
