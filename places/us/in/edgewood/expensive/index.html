Home prices

The cost of the median home in Edgewood is $107,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $8,860 (8%) higher. It is approximately $110,000 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $7,600 (6%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edgewood is $1,145. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $351 (44%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $83 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $381 (49%) higher.
