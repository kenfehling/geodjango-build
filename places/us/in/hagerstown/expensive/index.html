Home prices

Rents

In Hagerstown the median rent is $746 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $75 (11%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $316 (29%) lower, and is about $8 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
