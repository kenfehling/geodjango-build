Home prices

Butler's median home cost is $81,500. This is about $21,660 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $136,000 (62%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In Butler the median monthly rent is $798. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $168 (26%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $264 (24%) lower, and it is approximately $73 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Toledo.
