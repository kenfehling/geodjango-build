Home prices

The value of the median home in Bremen is $134,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $27,620 (25%) higher. It is about $83,400 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $19,000 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bremen is $719 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $71 (9%) lower. It is about $343 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $45 (5%) lower.
