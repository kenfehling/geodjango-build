Home prices

The median home in Lagrange costs $84,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $40,120 (32%) lower. It is roughly $132,800 (61%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $30,400 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Lagrange the median rent is $605 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $98 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $457 (43%) lower, and is approximately $159 (20%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
