Home prices

The median home cost in Lagrange is $84,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $40,120 (32%) lower. It is roughly $132,800 (61%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $30,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Lagrange the median rent is $605 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $98 (13%) lower. It is about $457 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $159 (20%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
