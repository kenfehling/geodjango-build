Compared to its surrounding areas, Lagrange is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Lagrange is valued at $84,700. It is about $40,120 (32%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $132,800 (61%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $30,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Lagrange's median monthly rent is $605. This is roughly $98 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $457 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $159 (20%) lower.
