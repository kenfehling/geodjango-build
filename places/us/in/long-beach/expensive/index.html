Home prices

The median home value in Long Beach is $437,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $234,780 (115%) higher. It is roughly $220,000 (101%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $179,500 (69%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents
