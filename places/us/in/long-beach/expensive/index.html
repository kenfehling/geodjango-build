Home prices

Rents

In Long Beach the median monthly rent is $1,125. This is roughly $250 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
