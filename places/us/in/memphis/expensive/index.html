Much like its nearby areas, it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Memphis is valued at $159,400. It is approximately $6,840 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $58,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $21,400 (15%) higher.

Rents

Memphis's median monthly rent is $777. This is roughly $217 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $285 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $39 (5%) higher.
