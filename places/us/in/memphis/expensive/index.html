Home prices

The median home price in Memphis is $159,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $6,840 (4%) higher. It is approximately $58,100 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $21,400 (15%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Memphis is $777. This is roughly $217 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $285 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $39 (5%) higher.
