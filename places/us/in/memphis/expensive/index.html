Home prices

The median home in Memphis costs $159,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $6,840 (4%) higher.It is approximately $58,100 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $21,400 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Memphis the median rent is $777 a month. It is roughly $217 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $285 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $39 (5%) higher.
