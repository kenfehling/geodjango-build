Home prices

Memphis's median home price is $159,400. It is roughly $6,840 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $58,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $21,400 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Memphis the median rent is $777 a month. It is approximately $217 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $285 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $39 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
