Home prices

The median home in Lapel is valued at $116,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $25,820 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $101,300 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Lapel is $800 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $182 (18%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $262 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $36 (4%) higher.
