Home prices

Rents

Scottsburg's median rent is $814 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $28 (3%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $248 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $76 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
