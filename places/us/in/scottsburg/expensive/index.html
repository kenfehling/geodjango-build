Compared to its surrounding areas, Scottsburg is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Scottsburg costs $101,200. It is roughly $10,800 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $116,300 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $36,800 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scottsburg is $814. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $28 (3%) higher.It is about $248 (23%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $76 (10%) higher.
