Home prices

The median home value in Sheridan is $128,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $7,540 (5%) lower. It is about $89,000 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $13,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Sheridan the median monthly rent is $719. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $172 (19%) lower. It is about $343 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $45 (5%) lower.
