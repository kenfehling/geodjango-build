Compared to its surrounding areas, Nashville is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Bloomington, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Nashville is $164,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $40,460 (32%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $53,000 (24%) lower, and is about $36,200 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

The median rent in Nashville is $742 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $167 (18%) lower. It is approximately $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is about $175 (19%) lower.
