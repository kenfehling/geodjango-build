Compared to other places in this area, Nashville is substantially more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Bloomington, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nashville is valued at $164,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $40,460 (32%) higher.It is approximately $53,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $36,200 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

Nashville's median monthly rent is $742. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $167 (18%) lower.It is roughly $320 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $175 (19%) lower.
