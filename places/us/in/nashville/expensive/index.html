Compared to its nearby areas, Nashville is substantially more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Bloomington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Nashville is $164,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $40,460 (32%) higher. It is approximately $53,000 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $36,200 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

In Nashville the median rent is $742 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $167 (18%) lower. It is about $320 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $175 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.
