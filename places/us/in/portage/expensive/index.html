Compared to its surrounding areas, Portage is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Portage is $152,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $9,620 (5%) lower.It is about $65,000 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $105,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Portage the median monthly rent is $902. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $212 (19%) lower.It is approximately $160 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $210 (18%) lower.
