In comparison to other places in this area, Portage is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home cost in Portage is $152,500. This is roughly $9,620 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $65,000 (29%) lower, and it is about $105,500 (40%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Portage is $902 per month. This is roughly $212 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $160 (15%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $210 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
