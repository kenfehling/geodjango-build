Home prices

The median home in Portage costs $152,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $9,620 (5%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $65,000 (29%) lower, and it is about $105,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portage is $902. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $212 (19%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $160 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $210 (18%) lower.
