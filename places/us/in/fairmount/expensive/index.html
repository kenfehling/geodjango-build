Compared to the surrounding areas, Fairmount is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fairmount is $84,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $12,880 (18%) higher. It is approximately $133,200 (61%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $30,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Fairmount is $542 a month. It is about $139 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $520 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $222 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
