Home prices

Rents

Lanesville's median monthly rent is $745. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $20 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $317 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington is roughly $172 (18%) lower.
