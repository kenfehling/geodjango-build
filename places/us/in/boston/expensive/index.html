Compared to the surrounding areas, Boston is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Boston costs $80,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17,700 (18%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $137,500 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $58,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

Boston's median monthly rent is $958. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $30 (2%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $104 (9%) lower, and is roughly $220 (29%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
