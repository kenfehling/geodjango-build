Home prices

Utica's median home value is $133,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $162,840 (55%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $84,300 (38%) lower, and it is about $4,800 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Utica's median monthly rent is $900. It is roughly $558 (38%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $162 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $162 (21%) higher.
