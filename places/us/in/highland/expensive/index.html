Home prices

The cost of the median home in Highland is $161,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $25,860 (13%) lower. It is approximately $56,400 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $96,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

Highland's median rent is $1,106 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $35 (3%) higher. It is approximately $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is is very similar.
