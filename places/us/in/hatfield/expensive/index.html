Home prices

The median home value in Hatfield is $96,000. It is about $9,280 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $121,500 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Evansville it is roughly $1,800 (1%) higher.

Rents

Hatfield's median monthly rent is $995. Compared to other places in this area this is about $275 (38%) higher. It is about $67 (6%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Evansville it is about $221 (28%) higher.
