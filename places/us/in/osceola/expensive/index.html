Home prices

The median home in Osceola costs $111,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $30,800 (21%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $105,900 (48%) lower, and is approximately $3,500 (3%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Osceola is $945 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $22 (2%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $117 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is about $181 (23%) higher.
