Home prices

The median home in Munster is valued at $228,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $69,220 (43%) higher.It is approximately $10,900 (5%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $29,600 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Munster the median monthly rent is $1,149. This is approximately $98 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $87 (8%) higher, and is about $37 (3%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
