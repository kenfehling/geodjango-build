Home prices

The median home in Rochester costs $103,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $39,320 (61%) higher.It is approximately $114,400 (52%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $12,000 (10%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Rochester's median rent is $680 per month. This is roughly $23 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $382 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $84 (10%) lower.
