Home prices

The median home in Rochester is valued at $103,100. It is about $39,320 (61%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $114,400 (52%) lower, and it is roughly $12,000 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Rochester's median rent is $680 a month. It is roughly $23 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $84 (10%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
