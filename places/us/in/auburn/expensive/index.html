Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $119,700. It is approximately $43,000 (56%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $97,800 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $4,600 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $707 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $41 (5%) lower. It is about $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $57 (7%) lower.
