Home prices

Decatur's median home cost is $97,700. This is about $8,620 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $119,800 (55%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $17,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Decatur the median monthly rent is $619. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $93 (13%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is about $145 (18%) lower.
