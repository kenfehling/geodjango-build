Home prices

Decatur's median home value is $97,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $8,620 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $119,800 (55%) lower, and is roughly $17,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Decatur is $619 per month. This is about $93 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and is about $145 (18%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
