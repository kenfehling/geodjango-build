Compared to other places in this area, Decatur is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Decatur is valued at $97,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $8,620 (9%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $119,800 (55%) lower, and is approximately $17,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Decatur's median monthly rent is $619. It is roughly $93 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $443 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $145 (18%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
