Home prices

The cost of the median home in Westfield is $254,800. This is roughly $13,900 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is about $37,300 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is approximately $54,100 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Westfield is $1,207 a month. It is about $172 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $145 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is approximately $290 (31%) higher.
