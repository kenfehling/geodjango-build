Compared to its surrounding areas, Westfield is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Bloomington, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Westfield costs $254,800. It is roughly $13,900 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $37,300 (17%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is about $54,100 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westfield is $1,207. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $172 (16%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $145 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is about $290 (31%) higher.
