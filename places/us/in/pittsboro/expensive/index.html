Home prices

Pittsboro's median home price is $162,400. This is about $11,380 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $55,100 (25%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $38,300 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

Pittsboro's median monthly rent is $922. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $17 (1%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $140 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is is very similar.
