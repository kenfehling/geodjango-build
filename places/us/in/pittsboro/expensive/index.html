Home prices

Pittsboro's median home price is $162,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $11,380 (7%) higher. It is approximately $55,100 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is roughly $38,300 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Pittsboro the median rent is $922 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $17 (1%) higher. It is roughly $140 (13%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Bloomington is is very similar.
