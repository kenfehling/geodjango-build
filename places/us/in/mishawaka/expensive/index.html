In comparison to other places in this area, Mishawaka is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

Mishawaka's median home cost is $102,900. This is approximately $24,420 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $114,600 (52%) lower, and is about $12,200 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Mishawaka the median rent is $780 a month. It is approximately $202 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $282 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $16 (2%) higher.
