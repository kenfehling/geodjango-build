Home prices

The median home in Yorktown costs $141,900. It is about $70,780 (99%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $75,600 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $26,800 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Yorktown the median monthly rent is $778. It is roughly $44 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $284 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $14 (1%) higher.
