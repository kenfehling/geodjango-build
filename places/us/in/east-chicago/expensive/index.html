Home prices

Rents

The median rent in East Chicago is $686 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $197 (22%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $376 (35%) lower, and is roughly $426 (38%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
