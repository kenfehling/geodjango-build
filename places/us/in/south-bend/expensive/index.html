Home prices

The median home in South Bend is valued at $85,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $45,660 (34%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $132,300 (60%) lower, and is approximately $172,800 (66%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in South Bend is $789 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $191 (19%) lower. It is about $273 (25%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $323 (29%) lower.
