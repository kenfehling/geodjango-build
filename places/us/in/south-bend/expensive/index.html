The median home in South Bend costs $85,200. This is approximately $45,660 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $132,300 (60%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $172,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

South Bend's median rent is $789 per month. This is approximately $191 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $273 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $323 (29%) lower.
