Home prices

The median home in Walkerton costs $98,800. It is about $6,440 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $118,700 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $159,200 (61%) lower.

Rents

In Walkerton the median rent is $704 per month. This is roughly $18 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $358 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $408 (36%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
