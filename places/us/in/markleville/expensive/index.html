Home prices

The median home in Markleville costs $93,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $14,220 (17%) higher. It is roughly $124,000 (57%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $44,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

Markleville's median rent is $836 per month. This is approximately $75 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $226 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $98 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
