Home prices

Syracuse's median home cost is $127,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $27,840 (27%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $90,100 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $12,300 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Syracuse the median monthly rent is $824. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $107 (14%) higher. It is about $238 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $60 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
