Compared to the surrounding areas, Syracuse is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Syracuse is $127,400. This is about $27,840 (27%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $90,100 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $12,300 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Syracuse is $824. It is about $107 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $238 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $60 (7%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
