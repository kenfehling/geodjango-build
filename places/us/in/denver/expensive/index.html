Compared to the surrounding areas, Denver is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Denver is $76,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $8,580 (12%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $141,500 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $39,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Denver is $760. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $56 (7%) higher.It is approximately $302 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
