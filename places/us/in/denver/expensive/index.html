In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denver is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Denver is valued at $76,000. This is approximately $8,580 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $141,500 (65%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $39,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Denver the median rent is $760 per month. This is roughly $56 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $302 (28%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
