In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denver is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Denver costs $76,000. It is approximately $8,580 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $141,500 (65%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $39,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

Denver's median rent is $760 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $56 (7%) higher.It is roughly $302 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is is very similar.
