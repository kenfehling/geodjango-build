In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denver is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Denver's median home cost is $76,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $8,580 (12%) higher. It is about $141,500 (65%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $39,100 (33%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Denver's median monthly rent is $760. This is approximately $56 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $302 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
