Home prices

The price of the median home in Marion is $67,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $13,960 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $149,700 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $47,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

Marion's median rent is $673 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $120 (15%) lower. It is about $389 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $91 (11%) lower.
