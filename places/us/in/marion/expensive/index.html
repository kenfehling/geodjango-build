In comparison to the surrounding areas, Marion is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Marion costs $67,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $13,960 (17%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $149,700 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $47,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marion is $673. This is approximately $120 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $389 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $91 (11%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
