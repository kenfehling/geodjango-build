Home prices

The median home in Gas City costs $88,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $5,180 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $128,900 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $26,500 (23%) lower.

Rents

Gas City's median monthly rent is $826. Compared to nearby areas it is about $182 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $236 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $62 (8%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
