Home prices

Goshen's median home cost is $121,100. It is roughly $15,460 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $96,400 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $6,000 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Goshen the median monthly rent is $784. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $37 (4%) lower. It is approximately $278 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $20 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
