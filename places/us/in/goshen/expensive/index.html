Compared to the surrounding areas, Goshen is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Goshen is $121,100. This is roughly $15,460 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $96,400 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $6,000 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Goshen is $784. It is roughly $37 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $278 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $20 (2%) higher.
