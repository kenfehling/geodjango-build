Compared to its surrounding areas, Goshen is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Goshen is $121,100. It is about $15,460 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $96,400 (44%) lower, and it is roughly $6,000 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Goshen's median monthly rent is $784. It is about $37 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $278 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $20 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
