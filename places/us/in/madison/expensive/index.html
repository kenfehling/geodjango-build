Rents

In Madison the median rent is $720 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $88 (10%) lower. It is roughly $342 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $18 (2%) lower.
