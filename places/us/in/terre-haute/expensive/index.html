Home prices

The price of the median home in Terre Haute is $78,700. It is about $1,500 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $138,800 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Champaign it is about $83,100 (51%) lower.

Rents

Terre Haute's median rent is $731 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $77 (9%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $331 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is roughly $221 (23%) lower.
