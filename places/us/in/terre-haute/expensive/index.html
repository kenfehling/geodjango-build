Home prices

The cost of the median home in Terre Haute is $78,700. This is approximately $1,500 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $138,800 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Champaign is roughly $83,100 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Terre Haute the median rent is $731 per month. It is approximately $77 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $331 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is roughly $221 (23%) lower.
