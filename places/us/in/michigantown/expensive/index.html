Home prices

The median home in Michigantown costs $87,000. This is about $920 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $130,500 (60%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $28,100 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Michigantown is $796. Compared to other places in this area this is about $116 (17%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $266 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $32 (4%) higher.
