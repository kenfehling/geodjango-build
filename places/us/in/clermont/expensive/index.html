Compared to nearby areas, Clermont is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Bloomington.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Clermont is $125,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $39,400 (23%) lower. It is roughly $91,600 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $74,800 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

In Clermont the median rent is $944 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $77 (7%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $118 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is about $27 (2%) higher.
