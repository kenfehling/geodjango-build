Home prices

The median home price in Modoc is $52,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $19,380 (26%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $165,000 (75%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $85,500 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Modoc is $883 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $208 (30%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $179 (16%) lower, and is about $145 (19%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
