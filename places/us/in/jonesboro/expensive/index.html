Home prices

The median home in Jonesboro costs $69,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $17,500 (20%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $147,800 (67%) lower, and it is roughly $45,400 (39%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Jonesboro the median monthly rent is $754. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $96 (14%) higher.It is approximately $308 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $10 (1%) lower.
