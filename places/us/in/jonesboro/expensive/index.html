Rents

The median monthly rent in Jonesboro is $754. It is about $96 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
