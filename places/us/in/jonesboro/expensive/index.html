Home prices

The median home in Jonesboro is valued at $69,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $17,500 (20%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $147,800 (67%) lower, and it is about $45,400 (39%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Jonesboro the median monthly rent is $754. This is about $96 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $308 (29%) lower, and it is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
