Rents

The median monthly rent in Henryville is $789. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $29 (3%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $273 (25%) lower, and is approximately $51 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
