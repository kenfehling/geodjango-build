Compared to its surrounding areas, Austin is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Austin is $72,100. It is approximately $33,160 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $145,400 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $65,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Austin is $787 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is is very similar. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $275 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $49 (6%) higher.
