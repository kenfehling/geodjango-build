Home prices

The median home price in Hobart is $142,700. It is about $41,700 (41%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $74,800 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $115,300 (44%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hobart is $954. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $38 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $108 (10%) lower, and it is approximately $158 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
