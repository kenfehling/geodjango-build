Compared to the surrounding areas, Whitestown is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Bloomington, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Whitestown is $225,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $15,300 (6%) lower.It is approximately $7,500 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is about $24,300 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Whitestown the median monthly rent is $1,317. This is approximately $246 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $255 (24%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is about $400 (43%) higher.
