Home prices

The value of the median home in Whitestown is $225,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,300 (6%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $7,500 (3%) higher, and it is about $24,300 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

In Whitestown the median monthly rent is $1,317. This is roughly $246 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $255 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $400 (43%) higher.
