In comparison to the surrounding areas, Whitestown is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Bloomington.

Home prices

The median home price in Whitestown is $225,000. It is approximately $15,300 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $7,500 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is about $24,300 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Whitestown the median monthly rent is $1,317. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $246 (22%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $255 (24%) higher, and it is about $400 (43%) higher compared to the city of Bloomington.
