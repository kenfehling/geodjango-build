Home prices

The median home in Cynthiana costs $81,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $59,740 (42%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $136,000 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is about $12,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cynthiana is $577 per month. This is about $143 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $485 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $197 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
