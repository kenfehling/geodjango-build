Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cynthiana is $81,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $59,740 (42%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $136,000 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is about $12,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cynthiana is $577. It is about $143 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $485 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville it is about $197 (25%) lower.
