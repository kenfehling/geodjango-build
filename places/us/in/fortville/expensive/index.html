In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fortville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fortville is $135,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $38,660 (22%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $82,400 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is about $20,000 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fortville is $913. This is roughly $105 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $149 (14%) lower, and it is about $149 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
