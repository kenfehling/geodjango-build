Rents

The median monthly rent in Fortville is $913. It is roughly $105 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $149 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $149 (19%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
