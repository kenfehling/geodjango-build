Home prices

Fountain City's median home cost is $78,100. It is roughly $8,000 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $139,400 (64%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $59,900 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Fountain City is $688 per month. It is roughly $21 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $50 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
