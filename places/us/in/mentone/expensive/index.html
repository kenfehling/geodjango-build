Home prices

The median home value in Mentone is $83,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $3,340 (3%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $134,500 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $32,100 (27%) lower.

Rents

Mentone's median rent is $681 a month. This is roughly $21 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $381 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $83 (10%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
