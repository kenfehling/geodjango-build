Home prices

The price of the median home in Farmersburg is $79,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $14,080 (21%) higher.It is roughly $138,100 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Evansville it is about $14,800 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Farmersburg is $613. This is roughly $134 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $449 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is approximately $161 (20%) lower.
