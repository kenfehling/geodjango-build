Compared to other places in this area, Farmersburg is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Evansville.

Home prices

Farmersburg's median home price is $79,400. This is about $14,080 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $138,100 (63%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is roughly $14,800 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Farmersburg is $613 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $134 (17%) lower. It is approximately $449 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $161 (20%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.
