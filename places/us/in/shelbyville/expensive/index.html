Home prices

The price of the median home in Shelbyville is $102,100. This is roughly $4,180 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $115,400 (53%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $35,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Shelbyville the median rent is $795 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $74 (10%) higher. It is about $267 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $57 (7%) higher.
