Home prices

The price of the median home in Albany is $71,400. This is about $4,160 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $146,100 (67%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $43,700 (37%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Albany the median rent is $653 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $409 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $111 (14%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
