Home prices

Bedford's median home cost is $94,800. This is approximately $15,240 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $122,700 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $105,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Bedford the median monthly rent is $679. This is about $65 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $383 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $238 (25%) lower.
