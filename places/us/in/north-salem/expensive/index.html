In comparison to its nearby areas, North Salem is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Bloomington.

Home prices

The median home in North Salem costs $114,900. It is approximately $8,720 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $102,600 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $85,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Salem is $817. It is approximately $46 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $245 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is about $100 (10%) lower.
