Home prices

The median home cost in North Salem is $114,900. This is about $8,720 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $102,600 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is approximately $85,800 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Salem is $817. This is about $46 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $100 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.
