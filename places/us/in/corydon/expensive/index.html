Home prices

Corydon's median home value is $140,100. It is approximately $18,500 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $77,400 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is about $60,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corydon is $707. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $11 (1%) lower. It is roughly $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $210 (22%) lower compared to the city of Bloomington.
