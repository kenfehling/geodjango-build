Home prices

The median home in Corydon costs $140,100. It is approximately $18,500 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $77,400 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bloomington is approximately $60,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corydon is $707. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $11 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $355 (33%) lower, and it is about $210 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Bloomington.
