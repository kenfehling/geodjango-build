Home prices

Rents

In Upland the median rent is $611 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $139 (18%) lower.It is about $451 (42%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $153 (20%) lower.
