Home prices

The median home in Upland costs $128,300. This is roughly $44,520 (53%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $89,200 (41%) lower, and is about $13,200 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Upland the median rent is $611 per month. It is roughly $139 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $451 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $153 (20%) lower.
