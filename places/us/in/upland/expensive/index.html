Home prices

The cost of the median home in Upland is $128,300. It is roughly $44,520 (53%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $89,200 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $13,200 (11%) higher.

Rents

Upland's median rent is $611 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $139 (18%) lower. It is roughly $451 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $153 (20%) lower.
