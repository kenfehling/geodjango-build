Home prices

The median home in Thermopolis costs $138,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $17,340 (14%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $78,600 (36%) lower, and is approximately $98,000 (41%) lower compared to the city of Cody.

Rents

Thermopolis's median monthly rent is $744. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $92 (14%) higher. It is about $318 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $171 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Cody.
