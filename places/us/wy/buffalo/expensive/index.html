In comparison to its surrounding areas, Buffalo is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Gillette, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Buffalo is $234,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $31,620 (15%) higher.It is about $16,600 (7%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Gillette is roughly $17,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

Buffalo's median rent is $888 a month. This is about $31 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $174 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Gillette.
