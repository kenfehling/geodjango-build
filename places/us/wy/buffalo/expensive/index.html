In comparison to the surrounding areas, Buffalo is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Gillette, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Buffalo is valued at $234,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $31,620 (15%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $16,600 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Gillette it is about $17,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

Buffalo's median rent is $888 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $31 (3%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $174 (16%) lower, and is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to the city of Gillette.
