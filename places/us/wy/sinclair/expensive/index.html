Home prices

The value of the median home in Sinclair is $125,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $8,640 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $92,500 (42%) lower, and is roughly $82,400 (39%) lower compared to the city of Casper.

Rents

In Sinclair the median monthly rent is $763. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $53 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Casper it is roughly $88 (10%) lower.
