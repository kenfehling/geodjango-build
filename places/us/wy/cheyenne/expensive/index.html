The median home value in Cheyenne is $214,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $19,140 (9%) higher.It is about $3,200 (1%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $176,300 (45%) lower compared to the city of Denver.

Rents

Cheyenne's median monthly rent is $915. This is roughly $52 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $147 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Denver is approximately $396 (30%) lower.
