Home prices

The price of the median home in Cheyenne is $214,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $19,140 (9%) higher. It is roughly $3,200 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Denver it is approximately $176,300 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Cheyenne the median rent is $915 a month. It is approximately $52 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $396 (30%) lower compared to the city of Denver.
