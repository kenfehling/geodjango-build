Compared to its surrounding areas, Evanston is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Evanston is valued at $169,400. It is approximately $63,320 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $48,100 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $145,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

In Evanston the median rent is $676 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $182 (21%) lower.It is approximately $386 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is about $309 (31%) lower.
