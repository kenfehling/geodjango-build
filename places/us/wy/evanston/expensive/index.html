Home prices

The median home in Evanston costs $169,400. This is roughly $40,620 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $48,100 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $145,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Evanston is $676. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $141 (17%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $386 (36%) lower, and is roughly $309 (31%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
