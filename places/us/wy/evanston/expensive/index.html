In comparison to the surrounding areas, Evanston is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Salt Lake City however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Evanston costs $169,400. This is roughly $63,320 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $48,100 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $145,100 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Evanston the median rent is $676 a month. It is approximately $182 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $386 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is about $309 (31%) lower.
