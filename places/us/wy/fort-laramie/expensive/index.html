Home prices

The median home value in Fort Laramie is $88,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $66,960 (43%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $129,300 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Cheyenne is roughly $126,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Laramie the median rent is $713 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $79 (10%) lower.It is roughly $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $202 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cheyenne.
