Home prices

The median home in Fort Laramie is valued at $88,200. It is roughly $66,960 (43%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $129,300 (59%) lower, and is roughly $126,100 (58%) lower compared to the city of Cheyenne.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Laramie is $713 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $79 (10%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Cheyenne it is about $202 (22%) lower.
