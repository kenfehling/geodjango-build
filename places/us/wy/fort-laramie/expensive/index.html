Home prices

The median home in Fort Laramie costs $88,200. This is approximately $66,960 (43%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $129,300 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $126,100 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Cheyenne.

Rents

Fort Laramie's median monthly rent is $713. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $79 (10%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cheyenne is roughly $202 (22%) lower.
