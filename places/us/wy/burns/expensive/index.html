Home prices

The median home in Burns is valued at $172,300. It is approximately $41,260 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $45,200 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $195,600 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Collins.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burns is $883. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $129 (17%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $179 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Collins is roughly $463 (34%) lower.
