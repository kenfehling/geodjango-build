Compared to other places in this area, Burns is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Collins, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Burns is $172,300. It is approximately $41,260 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $45,200 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $195,600 (53%) lower compared to the city of Fort Collins.

Rents

In Burns the median monthly rent is $883. It is about $129 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $179 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $463 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Collins.
