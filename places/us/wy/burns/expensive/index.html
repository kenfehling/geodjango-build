Home prices

The price of the median home in Burns is $172,300. This is roughly $41,260 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $45,200 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Fort Collins it is approximately $195,600 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Burns the median rent is $883 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $129 (17%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $179 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Collins it is about $463 (34%) lower.
