Home prices

The price of the median home in Laramie is $216,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $58,360 (36%) higher. It is is very similar in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $151,600 (41%) lower compared to the city of Fort Collins.

Rents

Laramie's median monthly rent is $797. This is roughly $142 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $265 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Collins it is approximately $549 (40%) lower.
