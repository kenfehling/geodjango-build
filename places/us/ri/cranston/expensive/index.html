In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cranston is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cranston is valued at $243,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $18,060 (8%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $26,000 (11%) higher, and is about $289,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Cranston the median rent is $1,070 a month. It is roughly $24 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and is roughly $550 (33%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
