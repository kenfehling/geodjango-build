In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cranston is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cranston is $243,500. This is roughly $18,060 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $26,000 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $289,200 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cranston is $1,070. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $550 (33%) lower.
