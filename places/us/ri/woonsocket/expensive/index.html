Home prices

Woonsocket's median home cost is $173,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $105,180 (37%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $44,200 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $359,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Woonsocket is $912. This is about $138 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $150 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $708 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
