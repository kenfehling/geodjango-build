Home prices

The price of the median home in Bradford is $227,400. This is approximately $142,200 (38%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $9,900 (4%) higher, and it is about $305,300 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Bradford the median rent is $1,017 a month. This is about $19 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $45 (4%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $603 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
