Home prices

The median home price in Westerly is $294,600. This is about $134,960 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $77,100 (35%) higher, and is roughly $238,100 (44%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Westerly is $999 per month. It is approximately $196 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $63 (5%) lower, and is approximately $621 (38%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
