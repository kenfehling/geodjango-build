Home prices

Westerly's median home price is $294,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $134,960 (31%) lower. It is about $77,100 (35%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $238,100 (44%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westerly is $999. It is approximately $196 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $63 (5%) lower, and it is about $621 (38%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
