Home prices

The median home price in Greenville is $285,300. It is roughly $57,940 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $67,800 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $247,400 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greenville is $1,177. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $184 (18%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $115 (10%) higher, and is about $443 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
