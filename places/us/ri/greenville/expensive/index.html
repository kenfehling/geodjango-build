Compared to other places in this area, Greenville is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Greenville is $285,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $57,940 (25%) higher.It is approximately $67,800 (31%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $247,400 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Greenville the median rent is $1,177 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $184 (18%) higher.It is roughly $115 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $443 (27%) lower.
