Home prices

The median home in Newport East is valued at $361,100. It is approximately $27,420 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $143,600 (66%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $171,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newport East is $1,301. It is about $138 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $239 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $319 (19%) lower.
