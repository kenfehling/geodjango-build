In comparison to its surrounding areas, Newport is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newport is $448,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $77,820 (20%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $231,300 (106%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $83,900 (15%) lower.

Rents

Newport's median monthly rent is $1,236. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $60 (5%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $174 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $384 (23%) lower.
