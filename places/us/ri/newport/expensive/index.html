In comparison to nearby areas, Newport is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Newport is $448,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $77,820 (20%) higher. It is approximately $231,300 (106%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $83,900 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Newport's median monthly rent is $1,236. This is about $60 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $174 (16%) higher, and it is roughly $384 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
