Home prices

The median home in Newport is valued at $448,800. It is roughly $77,820 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $231,300 (106%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $83,900 (15%) lower.

Rents

Newport's median monthly rent is $1,236. This is about $60 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $174 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $384 (23%) lower.
