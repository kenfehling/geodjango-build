Compared to nearby areas, Kingston is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Kingston's median home value is $355,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $39,660 (10%) lower. It is about $138,400 (63%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $176,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Kingston's median monthly rent is $1,179. This is approximately $85 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $117 (11%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $441 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
