In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kingston is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Kingston costs $355,900. It is roughly $39,660 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $138,400 (63%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $176,800 (33%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Kingston the median monthly rent is $1,179. It is roughly $85 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $117 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $441 (27%) lower.
