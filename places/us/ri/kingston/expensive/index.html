In comparison to nearby areas, Kingston is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Kingston's median home value is $355,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $39,660 (10%) lower.It is approximately $138,400 (63%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $176,800 (33%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Kingston is $1,179 per month. This is approximately $85 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $117 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $441 (27%) lower.
