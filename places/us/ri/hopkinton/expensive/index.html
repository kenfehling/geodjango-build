Home prices

The price of the median home in Hopkinton is $223,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $30,420 (11%) lower.It is approximately $6,400 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $308,800 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Hopkinton's median monthly rent is $452. This is roughly $589 (56%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $610 (57%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $1,168 (72%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
