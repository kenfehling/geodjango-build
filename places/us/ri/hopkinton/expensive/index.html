Compared to the surrounding areas, Hopkinton is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hopkinton costs $223,900. This is approximately $30,420 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $6,400 (2%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $308,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Hopkinton the median rent is $452 per month. It is roughly $589 (56%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $610 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $1,168 (72%) lower.
