In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hopkinton is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hopkinton is $223,900. It is roughly $30,420 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $6,400 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $308,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Hopkinton the median monthly rent is $452. It is approximately $589 (56%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $610 (57%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $1,168 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
