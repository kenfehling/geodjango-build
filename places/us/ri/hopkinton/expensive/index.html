In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hopkinton is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hopkinton is $223,900. This is roughly $30,420 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $6,400 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $308,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Hopkinton the median rent is $452 per month. This is about $589 (56%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $610 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $1,168 (72%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
