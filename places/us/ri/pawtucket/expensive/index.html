In comparison to other places in this area, Pawtucket is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Pawtucket is valued at $194,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $25,420 (11%) lower. It is approximately $22,900 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $338,100 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pawtucket is $920. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $17 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $142 (13%) lower, and is about $700 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
