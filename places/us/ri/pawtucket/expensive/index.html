Compared to other places in this area, Pawtucket is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pawtucket is valued at $194,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $25,420 (11%) lower.It is about $22,900 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $338,100 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Pawtucket the median monthly rent is $920. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $17 (1%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $142 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $700 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
