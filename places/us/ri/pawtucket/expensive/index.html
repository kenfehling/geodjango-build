In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pawtucket is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Pawtucket is $194,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $25,420 (11%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $22,900 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $338,100 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pawtucket is $920. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $17 (1%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $142 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $700 (43%) lower.
