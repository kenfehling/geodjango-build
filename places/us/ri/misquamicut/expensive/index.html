Home prices

The median home in Misquamicut costs $591,500. It is about $221,320 (59%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $374,000 (171%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $58,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

Misquamicut's median rent is $860 per month. It is approximately $363 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $760 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
