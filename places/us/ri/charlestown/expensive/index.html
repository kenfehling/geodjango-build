Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlestown is $398,500. It is roughly $7,140 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $181,000 (83%) higher, and it is approximately $134,200 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Charlestown's median monthly rent is $1,759. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $482 (37%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $697 (65%) higher, and is about $139 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
