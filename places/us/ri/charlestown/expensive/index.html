Home prices

Charlestown's median home cost is $398,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $7,140 (1%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $181,000 (83%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $134,200 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Charlestown the median monthly rent is $1,759. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $482 (37%) higher.It is roughly $697 (65%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $139 (8%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
