Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlestown is $398,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $7,140 (1%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $181,000 (83%) higher, and it is roughly $134,200 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Charlestown the median rent is $1,759 a month. This is about $482 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $697 (65%) higher, and it is approximately $139 (8%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
