Compared to its surrounding areas, Chepachet is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Chepachet is $243,800. This is approximately $17,380 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $26,300 (12%) higher, and it is roughly $288,900 (54%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Chepachet the median rent is $1,070 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $92 (9%) higher. It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $550 (33%) lower.
