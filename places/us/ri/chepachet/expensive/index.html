Home prices

The median home in Chepachet is valued at $243,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $17,380 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $26,300 (12%) higher, and is roughly $288,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Chepachet's median monthly rent is $1,070. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $92 (9%) higher. It is is very similar compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $550 (33%) lower.
