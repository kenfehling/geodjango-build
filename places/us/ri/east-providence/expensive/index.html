Home prices

The median home in East Providence costs $221,700. It is about $6,200 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $4,200 (1%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $311,000 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in East Providence is $974 per month. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $88 (8%) lower, and is roughly $646 (39%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
