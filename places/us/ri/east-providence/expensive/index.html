Home prices

The median home in East Providence costs $221,700. It is approximately $6,200 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $4,200 (1%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $311,000 (58%) lower.

Rents

In East Providence the median rent is $974 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the entire country it is about $88 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $646 (39%) lower.
