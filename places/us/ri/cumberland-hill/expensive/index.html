Home prices

The price of the median home in Cumberland Hill is $262,100. This is about $51,840 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $44,600 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $270,600 (50%) lower.

Rents

Cumberland Hill's median monthly rent is $921. It is approximately $26 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $141 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $699 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
