Home prices

The median home value in Cumberland Hill is $262,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $51,840 (24%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $44,600 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $270,600 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Cumberland Hill the median monthly rent is $921. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $26 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $141 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $699 (43%) lower.
