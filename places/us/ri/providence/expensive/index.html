In comparison to other places in this area, Providence is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Providence is $200,300. It is about $19,480 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $17,200 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $332,400 (62%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Providence is $994. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $33 (3%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $68 (6%) lower, and it is about $626 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
