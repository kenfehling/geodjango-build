In comparison to nearby areas, Providence is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Providence's median home value is $200,300. This is about $19,480 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $17,200 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $332,400 (62%) lower.

Rents

Providence's median rent is $994 per month. This is roughly $33 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $68 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $626 (38%) lower.
