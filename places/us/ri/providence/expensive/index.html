Compared to other places in this area, Providence is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Providence costs $200,300. This is roughly $19,480 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $17,200 (7%) lower, and is about $332,400 (62%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Providence the median rent is $994 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $33 (3%) higher. It is approximately $68 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $626 (38%) lower.
