In comparison to its nearby areas, Providence is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Providence is $200,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $19,480 (8%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $17,200 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $332,400 (62%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Providence's median monthly rent is $994. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $33 (3%) higher. It is about $68 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $626 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
