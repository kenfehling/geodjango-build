Home prices

The median home value in Warwick is $225,300. It is approximately $6,020 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $7,800 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $307,400 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Warwick's median monthly rent is $1,167. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $112 (10%) higher. It is approximately $105 (9%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $453 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
