Home prices

The median home price in Harmony is $271,500. This is roughly $32,860 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $54,000 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $261,200 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Harmony the median rent is $1,130 a month. It is approximately $162 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $68 (6%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $490 (30%) lower.
