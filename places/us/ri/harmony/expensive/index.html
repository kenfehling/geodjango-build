Compared to the surrounding areas, Harmony is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Harmony is valued at $271,500. It is approximately $32,860 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $54,000 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $261,200 (49%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Harmony's median rent is $1,130 per month. It is approximately $162 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $68 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $490 (30%) lower.
