Compared to its surrounding areas, Harmony is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Harmony costs $271,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $32,860 (13%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $54,000 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $261,200 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harmony is $1,130. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $162 (16%) higher.It is roughly $68 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $490 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
