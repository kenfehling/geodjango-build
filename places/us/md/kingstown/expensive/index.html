Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kingstown is $223,200. This is about $12,200 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $5,700 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $60,200 (36%) higher.

Rents

Kingstown's median monthly rent is $1,125. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $249 (28%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and is approximately $83 (7%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
