Home prices

The value of the median home in Kingstown is $223,200. It is roughly $12,200 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $5,700 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $60,200 (36%) higher.

Rents

In Kingstown the median rent is $1,125 per month. It is roughly $249 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $83 (7%) higher.
