In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kingstown is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kingstown is $223,200. This is roughly $12,200 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $5,700 (2%) higher, and is about $60,200 (36%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Kingstown is $1,125 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $249 (28%) higher. It is roughly $63 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $83 (7%) higher.
