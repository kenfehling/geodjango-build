Home prices

The median home in Kingstown costs $223,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $12,200 (5%) higher. It is about $5,700 (2%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $60,200 (36%) higher.

Rents

Kingstown's median monthly rent is $1,125. It is approximately $249 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $83 (7%) higher.
