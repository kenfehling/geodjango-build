In comparison to other places in this area, Fairmount Heights is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Fairmount Heights is $231,800. It is about $16,920 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $14,300 (6%) higher, and is approximately $369,700 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Fairmount Heights is $1,700 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $365 (27%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $638 (60%) higher, and is about $159 (10%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
