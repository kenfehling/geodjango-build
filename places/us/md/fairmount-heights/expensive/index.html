In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fairmount Heights is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home price in Fairmount Heights is $231,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $16,920 (6%) lower.It is approximately $14,300 (6%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $369,700 (61%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Fairmount Heights is $1,700 per month. This is about $365 (27%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $638 (60%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $159 (10%) higher.
