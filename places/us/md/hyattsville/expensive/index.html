Home prices

The median home in Hyattsville is valued at $336,300. It is about $8,340 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $118,800 (54%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $265,200 (44%) lower.

Rents

Hyattsville's median rent is $1,433 per month. It is approximately $222 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $371 (34%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $108 (7%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
