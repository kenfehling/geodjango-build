Compared to the surrounding areas, Lake Arbor is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Arbor is valued at $315,600. It is about $20,840 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $98,100 (45%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $285,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Arbor the median monthly rent is $1,845. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $96 (5%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $783 (73%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $304 (19%) higher.
