Home prices

The median home in Montgomery Village costs $305,400. This is about $124,200 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $87,900 (40%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $296,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Montgomery Village's median rent is $1,631 a month. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $569 (53%) higher, and is approximately $90 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
