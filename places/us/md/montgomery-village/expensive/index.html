Compared to the surrounding areas, Montgomery Village is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery Village costs $305,400. This is about $124,200 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $87,900 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $296,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomery Village is $1,631 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is approximately $569 (53%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $90 (5%) higher.
