Home prices

The median home in Montgomery Village costs $305,400. It is approximately $124,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $87,900 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $296,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Montgomery Village the median monthly rent is $1,631. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $569 (53%) higher, and is about $90 (5%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
