Home prices

The price of the median home in Linthicum is $305,600. This is approximately $98,080 (47%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $88,100 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $295,900 (49%) lower.

Rents

Linthicum's median monthly rent is $1,529. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $338 (28%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $467 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is is very similar.
