Home prices

The price of the median home in Denton is $193,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $28,080 (16%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $23,700 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $30,800 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Denton the median monthly rent is $906. This is approximately $13 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $156 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $136 (13%) lower.
