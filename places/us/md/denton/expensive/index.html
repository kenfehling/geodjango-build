Rents

In Denton the median rent is $906 per month. This is roughly $13 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $156 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $136 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
