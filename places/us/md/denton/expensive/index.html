Home prices

The median home in Denton costs $193,800. This is approximately $28,080 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $23,700 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $30,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

Denton's median monthly rent is $906. It is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $156 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $136 (13%) lower.
