Compared to other places in this area, North East is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in North East costs $195,200. This is approximately $33,700 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $22,300 (10%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $32,200 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

North East's median monthly rent is $880. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $126 (12%) lower. It is roughly $182 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $162 (15%) lower.
