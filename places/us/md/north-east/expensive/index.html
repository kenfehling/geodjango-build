Home prices

The price of the median home in North East is $195,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $33,700 (14%) lower. It is roughly $22,300 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $32,200 (19%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in North East is $880 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $126 (12%) lower. It is about $182 (17%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $162 (15%) lower.
