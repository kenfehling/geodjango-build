Compared to its surrounding areas, Pasadena is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Pasadena is $314,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $25,940 (7%) lower.It is about $96,900 (44%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $287,100 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Pasadena's median rent is $1,792 per month. It is about $196 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $730 (68%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $251 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
