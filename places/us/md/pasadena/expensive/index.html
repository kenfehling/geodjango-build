Home prices

The median home in Pasadena is valued at $314,400. It is about $25,940 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $96,900 (44%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $287,100 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pasadena is $1,792. It is about $196 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $730 (68%) higher, and it is approximately $251 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
