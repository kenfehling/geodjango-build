Compared to the surrounding areas, Pasadena is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Pasadena's median home price is $314,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $25,940 (7%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $96,900 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $287,100 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pasadena is $1,792 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $196 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $730 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $251 (16%) higher.
