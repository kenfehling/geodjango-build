Compared to other places in this area, Pasadena is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pasadena costs $314,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $25,940 (7%) lower.It is roughly $96,900 (44%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $287,100 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Pasadena the median monthly rent is $1,792. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $196 (12%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $730 (68%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $251 (16%) higher.
