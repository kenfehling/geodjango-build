Home prices

The median home in Mitchellville is valued at $354,300. This is roughly $14,680 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $136,800 (62%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $247,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

Mitchellville's median rent is $2,175 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $126 (6%) higher. It is about $1,113 (104%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $634 (41%) higher.
