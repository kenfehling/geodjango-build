In comparison to nearby areas, Baltimore is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Baltimore is $160,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $37,120 (18%) lower. It is about $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $441,400 (73%) lower.

Rents

In Baltimore the median monthly rent is $1,073. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $75 (6%) lower. It is approximately $11 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $468 (30%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
