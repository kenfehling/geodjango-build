Compared to other places in this area, Baltimore is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Baltimore is valued at $160,100. This is roughly $37,120 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower, and is approximately $441,400 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Baltimore the median rent is $1,073 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $75 (6%) lower.It is about $11 (1%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $468 (30%) lower.
