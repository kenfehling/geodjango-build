In comparison to nearby areas, Baltimore is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Baltimore is $160,100. This is about $37,120 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $57,400 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $441,400 (73%) lower.

Rents

Baltimore's median monthly rent is $1,073. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $75 (6%) lower.It is about $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is about $468 (30%) lower.
