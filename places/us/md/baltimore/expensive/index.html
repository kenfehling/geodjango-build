Home prices

The median home value in Baltimore is $160,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $37,120 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $441,400 (73%) lower.

Rents

In Baltimore the median rent is $1,073 per month. It is approximately $75 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $11 (1%) higher, and is about $468 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
