Home prices

The cost of the median home in Beltsville is $314,200. It is approximately $48,740 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $96,700 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is about $287,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Beltsville the median monthly rent is $1,738. This is about $95 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $676 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $197 (12%) higher.
