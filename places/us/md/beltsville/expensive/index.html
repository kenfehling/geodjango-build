Compared to other places in this area, Beltsville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Beltsville is valued at $314,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $48,740 (13%) lower. It is roughly $96,700 (44%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $287,300 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Beltsville is $1,738 a month. This is roughly $95 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $676 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $197 (12%) higher.
