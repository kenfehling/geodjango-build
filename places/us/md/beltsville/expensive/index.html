Home prices

The value of the median home in Beltsville is $314,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $48,740 (13%) lower. It is approximately $96,700 (44%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $287,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Beltsville the median rent is $1,738 a month. It is about $95 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $676 (63%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $197 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
