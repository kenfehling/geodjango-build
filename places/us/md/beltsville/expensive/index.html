Compared to its surrounding areas, Beltsville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Beltsville is valued at $314,200. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $48,740 (13%) lower.It is about $96,700 (44%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $287,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Beltsville the median monthly rent is $1,738. This is approximately $95 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $676 (63%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $197 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
