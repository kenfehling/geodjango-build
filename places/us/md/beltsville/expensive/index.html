In comparison to other places in this area, Beltsville is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Beltsville is valued at $314,200. This is roughly $48,740 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $96,700 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is about $287,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beltsville is $1,738. This is approximately $95 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $676 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $197 (12%) higher.
