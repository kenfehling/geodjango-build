In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chesapeake City is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home price in Chesapeake City is $270,000. This is approximately $21,540 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $52,500 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $107,000 (65%) higher.

Rents

In Chesapeake City the median rent is $896 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $266 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and is roughly $146 (14%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
