In comparison to its surrounding areas, Chesapeake City is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Chesapeake City is valued at $270,000. This is roughly $21,540 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $52,500 (24%) higher, and it is approximately $107,000 (65%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Chesapeake City's median monthly rent is $896. This is roughly $266 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $166 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $146 (14%) lower.
