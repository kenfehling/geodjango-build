Home prices

The median home in Chesapeake City costs $270,000. This is approximately $21,540 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $52,500 (24%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $107,000 (65%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Chesapeake City's median monthly rent is $896. Compared to other places in this area it is about $266 (22%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and it is about $146 (14%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
