In comparison to its nearby areas, Chesapeake City is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Chesapeake City is $270,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $21,540 (8%) higher. It is about $52,500 (24%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $107,000 (65%) higher.

Rents

Chesapeake City's median rent is $896 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $266 (22%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $146 (14%) lower.
