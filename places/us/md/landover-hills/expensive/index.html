Home prices

The median home in Landover Hills costs $241,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $20,100 (7%) lower. It is roughly $24,100 (11%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $359,900 (59%) lower.

Rents

Landover Hills's median rent is $1,833 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $459 (33%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $771 (72%) higher, and it is about $292 (18%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
