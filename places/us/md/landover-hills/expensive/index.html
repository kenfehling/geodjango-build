Home prices

Landover Hills's median home cost is $241,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $20,100 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $24,100 (11%) higher, and is approximately $359,900 (59%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Landover Hills's median rent is $1,833 per month. It is roughly $459 (33%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $771 (72%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $292 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
