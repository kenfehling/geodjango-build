Home prices

The value of the median home in Riverdale Park is $328,600. It is roughly $8,380 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $111,100 (51%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $272,900 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Riverdale Park is $1,224 a month. It is about $372 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $162 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $317 (20%) lower.
