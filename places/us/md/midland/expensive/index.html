Compared to other places in this area, Midland is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Midland is valued at $80,600. This is approximately $24,960 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $136,900 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $44,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

Midland's median rent is $775 per month. This is roughly $80 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and is approximately $183 (19%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
