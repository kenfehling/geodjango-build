In comparison to other places in this area, Midland is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Midland's median home price is $80,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $24,960 (23%) lower. It is approximately $136,900 (62%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $44,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Midland the median rent is $775 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $80 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and is about $183 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
