Home prices

The median home in Dunkirk costs $434,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $78,800 (22%) higher.It is roughly $216,600 (99%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $167,400 (27%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Dunkirk is $2,361 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $801 (51%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,299 (122%) higher, and is approximately $820 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
