Home prices

The median home in Dunkirk costs $434,100. This is approximately $78,800 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $216,600 (99%) higher, and it is approximately $167,400 (27%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Dunkirk is $2,361 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $801 (51%) higher. It is approximately $1,299 (122%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $820 (53%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
