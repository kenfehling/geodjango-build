Home prices

The value of the median home in Dunkirk is $434,100. This is about $78,800 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $216,600 (99%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $167,400 (27%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Dunkirk the median monthly rent is $2,361. This is about $801 (51%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $1,299 (122%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $820 (53%) higher.
