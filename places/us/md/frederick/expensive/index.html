Compared to its surrounding areas, Frederick is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Frederick is $270,400. This is about $59,340 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $52,900 (24%) higher, and is approximately $331,100 (55%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Frederick the median monthly rent is $1,396. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $132 (8%) lower. It is about $334 (31%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $145 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
