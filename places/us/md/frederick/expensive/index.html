In comparison to its nearby areas, Frederick is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Frederick costs $270,400. This is about $59,340 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $52,900 (24%) higher, and it is approximately $331,100 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Frederick the median monthly rent is $1,396. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $132 (8%) lower.It is roughly $334 (31%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $145 (9%) lower.
