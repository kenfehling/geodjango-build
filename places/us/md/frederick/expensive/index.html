In comparison to other places in this area, Frederick is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home value in Frederick is $270,400. This is about $59,340 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $52,900 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $331,100 (55%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Frederick is $1,396 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $132 (8%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $334 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $145 (9%) lower.
