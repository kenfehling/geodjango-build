Home prices

Rents

Temple Hills's median monthly rent is $1,420. This is roughly $67 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $358 (33%) higher, and is roughly $121 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
