Home prices

The median home in Eldersburg costs $386,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $111,240 (40%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $169,200 (77%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $214,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Eldersburg the median monthly rent is $1,153. It is roughly $238 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $91 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $388 (25%) lower.
