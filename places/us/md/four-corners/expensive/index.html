In comparison to other places in this area, Four Corners is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners is valued at $470,700. This is roughly $26,380 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $253,200 (116%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $130,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Four Corners the median monthly rent is $2,229. It is approximately $530 (31%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,167 (109%) higher, and it is about $688 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
