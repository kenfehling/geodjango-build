In comparison to other places in this area, Four Corners is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners is valued at $470,700. It is roughly $26,380 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $253,200 (116%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $130,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Four Corners is $2,229 a month. It is approximately $530 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,167 (109%) higher, and is about $688 (44%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
