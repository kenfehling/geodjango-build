In comparison to the surrounding areas, Four Corners is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Four Corners is $470,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $26,380 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $253,200 (116%) higher, and it is approximately $130,800 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Four Corners the median monthly rent is $2,229. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $530 (31%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $1,167 (109%) higher, and is approximately $688 (44%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
