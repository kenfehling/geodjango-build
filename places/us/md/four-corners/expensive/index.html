Compared to its surrounding areas, Four Corners is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners is valued at $470,700. This is roughly $26,380 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $253,200 (116%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $130,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

Four Corners's median monthly rent is $2,229. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $530 (31%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,167 (109%) higher, and it is approximately $688 (44%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
