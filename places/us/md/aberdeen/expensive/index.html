In comparison to the surrounding areas, Aberdeen is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Aberdeen is $205,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $34,060 (14%) lower.It is about $11,600 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $42,900 (26%) higher.

Rents

Aberdeen's median monthly rent is $1,027. Compared to nearby areas this is about $50 (4%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $35 (3%) lower, and it is roughly $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
