Compared to its surrounding areas, Taneytown is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Taneytown is $252,700. It is approximately $26,780 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $35,200 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $348,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Taneytown is $1,028. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $34 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $513 (33%) lower.
