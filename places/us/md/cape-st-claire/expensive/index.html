Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Cape St. Claire is $1,564 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $93 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $502 (47%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $23 (1%) higher.
