Home prices

The median home in Cape St. Claire costs $381,800. This is roughly $50,780 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $164,300 (75%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $219,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

Cape St. Claire's median monthly rent is $1,564. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $93 (5%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $502 (47%) higher, and is about $23 (1%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
