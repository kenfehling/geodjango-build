Home prices

The median home in Jessup costs $365,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17,960 (5%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $148,000 (68%) higher, and it is about $236,000 (39%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Jessup is $1,708 a month. This is about $26 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $646 (60%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $167 (10%) higher.
