Home prices

The median home value in Jessup is $365,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $17,960 (5%) higher. It is approximately $148,000 (68%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $236,000 (39%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Jessup's median monthly rent is $1,708. This is roughly $26 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $646 (60%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $167 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
