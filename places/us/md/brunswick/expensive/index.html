Home prices

Brunswick's median home cost is $237,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $107,300 (31%) lower. It is approximately $20,300 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $363,700 (60%) lower.

Rents

Brunswick's median rent is $901 a month. It is approximately $536 (37%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $161 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $640 (41%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
