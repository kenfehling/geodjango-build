Compared to nearby areas, Brunswick is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home value in Brunswick is $237,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $107,300 (31%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $20,300 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $363,700 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Brunswick is $901 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $536 (37%) lower. It is roughly $161 (15%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $640 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
