Home prices

The median home in Berlin is valued at $252,700. It is approximately $68,500 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $35,200 (16%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $96,000 (61%) higher compared to the city of Atlantic City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Berlin is $947. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $321 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $115 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlantic City it is about $43 (4%) higher.
