Home prices

The median home in Severna Park is valued at $546,800. This is about $137,440 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $329,300 (151%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $54,700 (9%) lower.

Rents

Severna Park's median rent is $1,744 a month. This is roughly $145 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $682 (64%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $203 (13%) higher.
