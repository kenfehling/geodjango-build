Home prices

The median home cost in Ilchester is $390,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $99,080 (33%) higher.It is about $173,100 (79%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $210,900 (35%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Ilchester's median monthly rent is $1,799. It is roughly $366 (25%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $737 (69%) higher, and is about $258 (16%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
