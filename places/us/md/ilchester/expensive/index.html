Compared to its nearby areas, Ilchester is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ilchester is $390,600. This is approximately $99,080 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $173,100 (79%) higher, and it is roughly $210,900 (35%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Ilchester is $1,799 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $366 (25%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $737 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $258 (16%) higher.
