In comparison to other places in this area, Ilchester is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Ilchester's median home value is $390,600. This is approximately $99,080 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $173,100 (79%) higher, and it is about $210,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ilchester is $1,799. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $366 (25%) higher. It is roughly $737 (69%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $258 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
