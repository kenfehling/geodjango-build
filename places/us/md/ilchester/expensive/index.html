Home prices

The price of the median home in Ilchester is $390,600. This is about $99,080 (33%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $173,100 (79%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $210,900 (35%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Ilchester's median rent is $1,799 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $366 (25%) higher.It is about $737 (69%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $258 (16%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
