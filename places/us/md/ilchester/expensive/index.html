In comparison to other places in this area, Ilchester is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Ilchester's median home price is $390,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $99,080 (33%) higher. It is roughly $173,100 (79%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington is about $210,900 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Ilchester the median monthly rent is $1,799. This is approximately $366 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $737 (69%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $258 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
