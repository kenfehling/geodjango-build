Home prices

The cost of the median home in Timonium is $380,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $41,320 (12%) higher.It is about $163,200 (75%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $220,800 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Timonium the median rent is $1,366 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $304 (28%) higher, and is about $175 (11%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
