Home prices

The median home in Timonium is valued at $380,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $41,320 (12%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $163,200 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $220,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

Timonium's median rent is $1,366 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is is very similar.It is about $304 (28%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $175 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
