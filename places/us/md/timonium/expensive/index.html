In comparison to nearby areas, Timonium is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home value in Timonium is $380,700. It is about $41,320 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $163,200 (75%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $220,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Timonium the median rent is $1,366 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $304 (28%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $175 (11%) lower.
