Home prices

The median home in Crofton costs $387,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $29,540 (7%) lower. It is roughly $169,900 (78%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $214,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Crofton is $1,928 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $92 (4%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $866 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $387 (25%) higher.
