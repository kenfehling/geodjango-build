Home prices

The median home in Crofton is valued at $387,400. This is approximately $29,540 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $169,900 (78%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $214,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Crofton is $1,928 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $92 (4%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $866 (81%) higher, and is roughly $387 (25%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
