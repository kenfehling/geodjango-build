Compared to nearby areas, Adelphi is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Adelphi is $313,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $103,640 (24%) lower.It is approximately $96,200 (44%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $287,800 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Adelphi the median rent is $1,502 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $102 (6%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $440 (41%) higher, and it is approximately $39 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
