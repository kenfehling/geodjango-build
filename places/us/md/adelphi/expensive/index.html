In comparison to nearby areas, Adelphi is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Adelphi is $313,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $103,640 (24%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $96,200 (44%) higher, and it is about $287,800 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Adelphi the median monthly rent is $1,502. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $102 (6%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $440 (41%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $39 (2%) lower.
