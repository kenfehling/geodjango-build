Compared to other places in this area, Adelphi is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Adelphi is $313,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $103,640 (24%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $96,200 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $287,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Adelphi is $1,502. This is approximately $102 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $440 (41%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $39 (2%) lower.
