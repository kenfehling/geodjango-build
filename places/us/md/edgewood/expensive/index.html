Compared to other places in this area, Edgewood is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Edgewood is valued at $182,300. It is roughly $93,520 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $35,200 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $19,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Edgewood the median monthly rent is $1,230. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $84 (6%) lower. It is about $168 (15%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $188 (18%) higher.
