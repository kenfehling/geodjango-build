Home prices

The median home in Edgewood costs $182,300. This is approximately $93,520 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $35,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $19,300 (11%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Edgewood the median rent is $1,230 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $84 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $168 (15%) higher, and it is approximately $188 (18%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
