Home prices

The value of the median home in Edgewood is $182,300. It is approximately $93,520 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $35,200 (16%) lower, and is approximately $19,300 (11%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Edgewood is $1,230 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $84 (6%) lower.It is approximately $168 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $188 (18%) higher.
