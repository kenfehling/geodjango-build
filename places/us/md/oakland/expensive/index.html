In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oakland is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Oakland is $157,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $63,720 (67%) higher.It is approximately $59,800 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $32,700 (26%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $619 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $35 (5%) lower.It is approximately $443 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $339 (35%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
