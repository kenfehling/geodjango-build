Compared to nearby areas, Oakland is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $157,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $63,720 (67%) higher. It is roughly $59,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $32,700 (26%) higher.

Rents

Oakland's median monthly rent is $619. This is roughly $35 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $443 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $339 (35%) lower.
