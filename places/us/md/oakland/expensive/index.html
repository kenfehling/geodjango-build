In comparison to other places in this area, Oakland is substantially more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $157,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $63,720 (67%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $59,800 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $32,700 (26%) higher.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $619 per month. This is about $35 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $339 (35%) lower.
