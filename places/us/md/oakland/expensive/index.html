In comparison to other places in this area, Oakland is substantially more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $157,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $63,720 (67%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $59,800 (27%) lower, and it is about $32,700 (26%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Oakland is $619 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $35 (5%) lower. It is roughly $443 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $339 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
