Home prices

The median home in Silver Hill costs $182,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $60,960 (25%) lower. It is roughly $35,100 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $419,100 (69%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Silver Hill the median monthly rent is $1,468. It is about $105 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $406 (38%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $73 (4%) lower.
