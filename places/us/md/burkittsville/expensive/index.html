Compared to other places in this area, Burkittsville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Burkittsville is valued at $408,000. This is approximately $81,000 (24%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $190,500 (87%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $193,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Burkittsville is $1,281 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $51 (4%) higher. It is about $219 (20%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $260 (16%) lower.
