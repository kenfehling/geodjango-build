Home prices

The cost of the median home in Burkittsville is $408,000. It is roughly $81,000 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $190,500 (87%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $193,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Burkittsville the median monthly rent is $1,281. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $51 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $219 (20%) higher, and is roughly $260 (16%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
