In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ellicott City is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Ellicott City is $537,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $227,500 (73%) higher.It is about $319,900 (147%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $64,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ellicott City is $1,615 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $104 (6%) higher.It is roughly $553 (52%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $74 (4%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
