Home prices

Ellicott City's median home price is $537,400. This is approximately $227,500 (73%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $319,900 (147%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $64,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ellicott City is $1,615 per month. This is approximately $104 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $553 (52%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $74 (4%) higher.
