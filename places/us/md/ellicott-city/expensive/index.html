Compared to the surrounding areas, Ellicott City is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Washington, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Ellicott City's median home value is $537,400. This is approximately $227,500 (73%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $319,900 (147%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $64,100 (10%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Ellicott City's median monthly rent is $1,615. This is roughly $104 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $553 (52%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $74 (4%) higher.
