Home prices

The median home value in Ellicott City is $537,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $227,500 (73%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $319,900 (147%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $64,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

Ellicott City's median rent is $1,615 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $104 (6%) higher. It is approximately $553 (52%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $74 (4%) higher.
