In comparison to its surrounding areas, Laurel is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Laurel costs $269,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $108,940 (28%) lower. It is approximately $51,500 (23%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $332,500 (55%) lower.

Rents

Laurel's median monthly rent is $1,566. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $419 (21%) lower. It is about $504 (47%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $25 (1%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
