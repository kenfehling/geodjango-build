Compared to other places in this area, Ferndale is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ferndale is $247,800. This is roughly $24,320 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $30,300 (13%) higher, and is approximately $353,700 (58%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Ferndale the median monthly rent is $1,378. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $114 (9%) higher. It is about $316 (29%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $163 (10%) lower.
