Compared to its surrounding areas, Ferndale is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ferndale costs $247,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $24,320 (10%) higher.It is roughly $30,300 (13%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $353,700 (58%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Ferndale's median rent is $1,378 a month. This is approximately $114 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $316 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $163 (10%) lower.
