Compared to the surrounding areas, Ferndale is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ferndale is $247,800. It is roughly $24,320 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $30,300 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $353,700 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Ferndale the median rent is $1,378 a month. It is roughly $114 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $316 (29%) higher, and is about $163 (10%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
