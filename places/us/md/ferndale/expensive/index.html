In comparison to its nearby areas, Ferndale is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Ferndale's median home value is $247,800. This is approximately $24,320 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $30,300 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $353,700 (58%) lower.

Rents

Ferndale's median rent is $1,378 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $114 (9%) higher. It is approximately $316 (29%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $163 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
