Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ferndale is $247,800. It is roughly $24,320 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $30,300 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $353,700 (58%) lower.

Rents

Ferndale's median rent is $1,378 a month. It is approximately $114 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $316 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is about $163 (10%) lower.
