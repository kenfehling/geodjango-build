Home prices

The median home in North Potomac is valued at $672,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $8,580 (1%) higher. It is roughly $455,400 (209%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $71,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

North Potomac's median rent is $2,129 per month. It is roughly $192 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $1,067 (100%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $588 (38%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
