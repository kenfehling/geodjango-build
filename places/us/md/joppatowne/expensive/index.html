Compared to its surrounding areas, Joppatowne is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Joppatowne is $260,900. This is roughly $28,180 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $43,400 (19%) higher, and is about $97,900 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Joppatowne is $1,161. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $308 (20%) lower. It is roughly $99 (9%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $119 (11%) higher.
