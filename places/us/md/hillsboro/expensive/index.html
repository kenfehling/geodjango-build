Rents

The median rent in Hillsboro is $817 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $99 (10%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $245 (23%) lower, and it is about $225 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
