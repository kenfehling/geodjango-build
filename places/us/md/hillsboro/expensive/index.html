Home prices

Hillsboro's median home cost is $182,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $28,820 (13%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $35,400 (16%) lower, and is about $19,100 (11%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $817 a month. It is about $99 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $245 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $225 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
