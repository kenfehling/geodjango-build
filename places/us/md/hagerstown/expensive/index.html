Compared to the surrounding areas, Hagerstown is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hagerstown is $155,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $59,820 (27%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $61,800 (28%) lower, and it is about $445,800 (74%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hagerstown is $832. It is roughly $125 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $230 (21%) lower, and is about $709 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
