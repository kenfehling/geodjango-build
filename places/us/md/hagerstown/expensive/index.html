Compared to its nearby areas, Hagerstown is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hagerstown is $155,700. This is roughly $59,820 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $61,800 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $445,800 (74%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Hagerstown's median rent is $832 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $125 (13%) lower.It is about $230 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $709 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
