Compared to its surrounding areas, Hagerstown is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hagerstown is $155,700. This is about $59,820 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $61,800 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $445,800 (74%) lower.

Rents

In Hagerstown the median monthly rent is $832. It is about $125 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $230 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington is about $709 (46%) lower.
