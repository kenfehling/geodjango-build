Home prices

Hagerstown's median home cost is $155,700. It is about $59,820 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $61,800 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $445,800 (74%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hagerstown is $832 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $125 (13%) lower.It is roughly $230 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $709 (46%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
