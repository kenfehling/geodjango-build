Home prices

Rents

Westminster's median rent is $1,136 per month. This is roughly $127 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $74 (6%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $405 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
