Much like other places in this area, it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Westminster is valued at $242,700. This is about $7,280 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $25,200 (11%) higher, and is approximately $358,800 (59%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westminster is $1,136. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $127 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $74 (6%) higher, and is roughly $405 (26%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
