Compared to the surrounding areas, Columbia is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Columbia is $378,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $69,640 (15%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $160,800 (73%) higher, and it is about $223,200 (37%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Columbia the median rent is $1,722 per month. It is approximately $443 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $660 (62%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $181 (11%) higher.
