Compared to the surrounding areas, Columbia is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Columbia costs $378,300. This is about $69,640 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $160,800 (73%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $223,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

Columbia's median monthly rent is $1,722. This is approximately $443 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $660 (62%) higher, and is about $181 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
