Home prices

Columbia's median home price is $378,300. This is roughly $69,640 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $160,800 (73%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $223,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Columbia is $1,722 a month. This is approximately $443 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $660 (62%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $181 (11%) higher.
