In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cockeysville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Cockeysville is valued at $333,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $30,980 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $115,500 (53%) higher, and it is approximately $268,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Cockeysville's median rent is $1,221 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $180 (12%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $159 (14%) higher, and it is about $320 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
