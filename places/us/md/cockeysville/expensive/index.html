Home prices

The median home in Cockeysville is valued at $333,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $30,980 (8%) lower.It is about $115,500 (53%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $268,500 (44%) lower.

Rents
