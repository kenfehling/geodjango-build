Home prices

The price of the median home in Glen Burnie is $256,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24,180 (8%) lower.It is approximately $39,000 (17%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $345,000 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Glen Burnie is $1,320 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $269 (16%) lower.It is approximately $258 (24%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $221 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
