In comparison to other places in this area, Glen Burnie is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Glen Burnie costs $256,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $24,180 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $39,000 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $345,000 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Glen Burnie the median monthly rent is $1,320. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $269 (16%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $258 (24%) higher, and is approximately $221 (14%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
