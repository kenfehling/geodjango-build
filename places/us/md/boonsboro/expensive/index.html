Home prices

The median home cost in Boonsboro is $271,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $23,560 (9%) higher.It is roughly $54,100 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $329,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

Boonsboro's median monthly rent is $1,359. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $367 (36%) higher.It is roughly $297 (27%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $182 (11%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
