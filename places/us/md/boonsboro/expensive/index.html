In comparison to the surrounding areas, Boonsboro is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Boonsboro's median home cost is $271,600. It is about $23,560 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $54,100 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $329,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

Boonsboro's median monthly rent is $1,359. Compared to other places in this area it is about $367 (36%) higher. It is approximately $297 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $182 (11%) lower.
