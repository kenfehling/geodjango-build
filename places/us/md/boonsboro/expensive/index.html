Compared to other places in this area, Boonsboro is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Boonsboro costs $271,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $23,560 (9%) higher.It is approximately $54,100 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $329,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Boonsboro's median rent is $1,359 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $367 (36%) higher.It is about $297 (27%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $182 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
