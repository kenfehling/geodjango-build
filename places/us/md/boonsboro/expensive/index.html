Home prices

The cost of the median home in Boonsboro is $271,600. It is roughly $23,560 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $54,100 (24%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $329,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

Boonsboro's median monthly rent is $1,359. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $367 (36%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $297 (27%) higher, and is approximately $182 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
