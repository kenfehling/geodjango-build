In comparison to its nearby areas, Germantown is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Germantown is valued at $345,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $114,480 (24%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $127,900 (58%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $256,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Germantown is $1,747. It is about $121 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $685 (64%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $206 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
