In comparison to other places in this area, Germantown is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Germantown's median home cost is $345,400. This is about $114,480 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $127,900 (58%) higher, and is approximately $256,100 (42%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Germantown the median monthly rent is $1,747. This is about $121 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $685 (64%) higher, and it is approximately $206 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
