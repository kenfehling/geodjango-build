Home prices

The price of the median home in Mountain Lake Park is $142,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $45,000 (46%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $75,400 (34%) lower, and is roughly $17,100 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Mountain Lake Park is $554 per month. This is roughly $113 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $508 (47%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $404 (42%) lower.
