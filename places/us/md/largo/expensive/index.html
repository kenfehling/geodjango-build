Home prices

The median home value in Largo is $283,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $12,260 (4%) higher. It is roughly $66,400 (30%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $317,600 (52%) lower.

Rents

Largo's median monthly rent is $1,690. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $66 (4%) higher. It is roughly $628 (59%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $149 (9%) higher.
