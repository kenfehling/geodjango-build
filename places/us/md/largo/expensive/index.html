Home prices

The median home in Largo costs $283,900. This is approximately $12,260 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $66,400 (30%) higher, and is approximately $317,600 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Largo the median rent is $1,690 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $66 (4%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $628 (59%) higher, and it is roughly $149 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
