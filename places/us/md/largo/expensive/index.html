Home prices

The median home in Largo costs $283,900. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $12,260 (4%) higher.It is roughly $66,400 (30%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $317,600 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Largo the median rent is $1,690 per month. This is roughly $66 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $628 (59%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $149 (9%) higher.
