Home prices

The median home in Redland costs $418,500. It is approximately $71,240 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $201,000 (92%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $183,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Redland the median monthly rent is $1,736. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $32 (1%) higher. It is about $674 (63%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $195 (12%) higher.
