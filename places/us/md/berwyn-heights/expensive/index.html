Home prices

The median home cost in Berwyn Heights is $308,000. It is roughly $16,500 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $90,500 (41%) higher, and is roughly $293,500 (48%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Berwyn Heights is $2,070. This is about $444 (27%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $1,008 (94%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $529 (34%) higher.
