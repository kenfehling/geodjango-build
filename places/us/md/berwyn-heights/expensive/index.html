Home prices

The median home in Berwyn Heights is valued at $308,000. It is roughly $16,500 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $90,500 (41%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $293,500 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Berwyn Heights is $2,070 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $444 (27%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $1,008 (94%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $529 (34%) higher.
