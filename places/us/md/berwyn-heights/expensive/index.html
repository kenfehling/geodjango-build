Home prices

The median home in Berwyn Heights costs $308,000. This is approximately $16,500 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $90,500 (41%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $293,500 (48%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Berwyn Heights's median rent is $2,070 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $444 (27%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $1,008 (94%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $529 (34%) higher.
