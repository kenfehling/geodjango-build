Home prices

Berwyn Heights's median home price is $308,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $16,500 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $90,500 (41%) higher, and it is roughly $293,500 (48%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Berwyn Heights the median rent is $2,070 per month. It is approximately $444 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,008 (94%) higher, and it is approximately $529 (34%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
