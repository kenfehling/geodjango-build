Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Laurel is $343,500. It is about $19,880 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $126,000 (57%) higher, and is roughly $258,000 (42%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

South Laurel's median rent is $1,506 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $353 (18%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $444 (41%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $35 (2%) lower.
