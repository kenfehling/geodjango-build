Compared to its surrounding areas, South Laurel is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Laurel costs $343,500. It is about $19,880 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $126,000 (57%) higher, and it is about $258,000 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

South Laurel's median rent is $1,506 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $353 (18%) lower. It is approximately $444 (41%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $35 (2%) lower.
