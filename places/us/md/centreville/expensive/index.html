Home prices

The price of the median home in Centreville is $330,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $29,080 (9%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $112,900 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $167,400 (102%) higher.

Rents

In Centreville the median monthly rent is $1,072. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $401 (27%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is is very similar, and is approximately $30 (2%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
