Home prices

The median home in Brandywine costs $381,800. It is approximately $42,640 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $164,300 (75%) higher, and is approximately $219,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Brandywine is $2,123 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $29 (1%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $1,061 (99%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $582 (37%) higher.
