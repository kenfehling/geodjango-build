Home prices

The value of the median home in Brandywine is $381,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $42,640 (12%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $164,300 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $219,700 (36%) lower.

Rents
