Compared to its surrounding areas, Lutherville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lutherville is $360,200. It is about $25,160 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $142,700 (65%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $241,300 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lutherville is $1,218. This is approximately $143 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $156 (14%) higher, and it is approximately $323 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
