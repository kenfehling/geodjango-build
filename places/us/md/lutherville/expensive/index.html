Home prices

Rents

Lutherville's median rent is $1,218 per month. It is approximately $143 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $156 (14%) higher, and it is approximately $323 (20%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
