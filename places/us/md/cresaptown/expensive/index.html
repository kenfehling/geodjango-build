Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cresaptown is $138,800. This is approximately $26,440 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $78,700 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisonburg it is approximately $64,800 (31%) lower.

Rents

Cresaptown's median monthly rent is $721. This is about $38 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $162 (18%) lower compared to the city of Harrisonburg.
