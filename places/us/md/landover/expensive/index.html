Home prices

Landover's median home cost is $207,400. It is approximately $67,000 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $10,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $394,100 (65%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Landover the median monthly rent is $1,393. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $142 (9%) lower. It is approximately $331 (31%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $148 (9%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
