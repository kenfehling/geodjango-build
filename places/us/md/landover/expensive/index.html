Home prices

The median home price in Landover is $207,400. It is approximately $67,000 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $10,100 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $394,100 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Landover the median rent is $1,393 per month. This is roughly $142 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $331 (31%) higher, and is about $148 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
