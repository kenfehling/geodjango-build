Compared to other places in this area, Robinwood is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Robinwood is $276,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $66,880 (31%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $58,700 (26%) higher, and is about $325,300 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Robinwood is $1,101. This is roughly $188 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $39 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $440 (28%) lower.
