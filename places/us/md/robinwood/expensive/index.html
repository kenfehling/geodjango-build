In comparison to its surrounding areas, Robinwood is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Robinwood's median home cost is $276,200. This is approximately $66,880 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $58,700 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $325,300 (54%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Robinwood's median rent is $1,101 a month. It is about $188 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $39 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $440 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
