Home prices

The median home in Robinwood costs $276,200. This is roughly $66,880 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $58,700 (26%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $325,300 (54%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Robinwood is $1,101 a month. It is about $188 (20%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $39 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $440 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
