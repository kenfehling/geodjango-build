In comparison to its surrounding areas, Woodsboro is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Woodsboro is $325,900. This is roughly $65,440 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $108,400 (49%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $275,600 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Woodsboro is $773. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $339 (30%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $289 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $768 (49%) lower.
