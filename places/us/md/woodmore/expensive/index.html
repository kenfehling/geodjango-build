In comparison to the surrounding areas, Woodmore is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Woodmore is $431,600. This is approximately $66,800 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $214,100 (98%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $169,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Woodmore is $2,627 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $500 (23%) higher. It is roughly $1,565 (147%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $1,086 (70%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
