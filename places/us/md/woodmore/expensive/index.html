Rents

In Woodmore the median rent is $2,627 per month. This is about $500 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $1,565 (147%) higher, and it is approximately $1,086 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
