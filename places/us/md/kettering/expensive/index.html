Home prices

The median home in Kettering is valued at $301,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $27,120 (8%) lower. It is roughly $84,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $300,000 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Kettering is $1,811 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $207 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $749 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $270 (17%) higher.
