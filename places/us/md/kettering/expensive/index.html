Home prices

The median home value in Kettering is $301,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $27,120 (8%) lower.It is about $84,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $300,000 (49%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Kettering the median rent is $1,811 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $207 (10%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $749 (70%) higher, and is roughly $270 (17%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
