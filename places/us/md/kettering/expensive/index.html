Compared to the surrounding areas, Kettering is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kettering is valued at $301,500. This is about $27,120 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $84,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $300,000 (49%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kettering is $1,811. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $207 (10%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $749 (70%) higher, and it is roughly $270 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
