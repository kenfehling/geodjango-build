Home prices

Rents

Essex's median rent is $1,086 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $101 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $24 (2%) higher, and is about $455 (29%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
