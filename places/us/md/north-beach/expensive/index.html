Home prices

The median home cost in North Beach is $300,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $74,460 (19%) lower.It is about $83,100 (38%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $300,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

In North Beach the median rent is $1,670 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $331 (16%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $608 (57%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $129 (8%) higher.
