In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Beach is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

North Beach's median home cost is $300,600. It is roughly $74,460 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $83,100 (38%) higher, and it is about $300,900 (50%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In North Beach the median rent is $1,670 a month. This is roughly $331 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $608 (57%) higher, and is about $129 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
