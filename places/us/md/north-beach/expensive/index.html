Home prices

Rents

The median rent in North Beach is $1,670 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $331 (16%) lower.It is roughly $608 (57%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $129 (8%) higher.
