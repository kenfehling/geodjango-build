Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairland is $1,689. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $229 (11%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $627 (59%) higher, and is roughly $148 (9%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
