Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairland is $349,300. This is about $61,500 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $131,800 (60%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $252,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Fairland the median rent is $1,689 per month. It is approximately $229 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $627 (59%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $148 (9%) higher.
