Compared to other places in this area, Fairland is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fairland is valued at $349,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $61,500 (14%) lower. It is about $131,800 (60%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $252,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

Fairland's median monthly rent is $1,689. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $229 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $627 (59%) higher, and it is approximately $148 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
