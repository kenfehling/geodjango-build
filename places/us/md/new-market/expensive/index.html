Home prices

The price of the median home in New Market is $449,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $70,820 (18%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $231,900 (106%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $152,100 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in New Market is $1,795 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $25 (1%) lower.It is approximately $733 (69%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $254 (16%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
