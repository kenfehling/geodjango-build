In comparison to its surrounding areas, New Market is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in New Market is $449,400. This is approximately $70,820 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $231,900 (106%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $152,100 (25%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

New Market's median rent is $1,795 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $25 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $733 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $254 (16%) higher.
