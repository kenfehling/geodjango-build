Home prices

The median home in New Market is valued at $449,400. This is roughly $70,820 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $231,900 (106%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $152,100 (25%) lower.

Rents

In New Market the median monthly rent is $1,795. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $25 (1%) lower.It is roughly $733 (69%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington is about $254 (16%) higher.
