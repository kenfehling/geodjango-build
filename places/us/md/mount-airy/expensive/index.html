Home prices

Mount Airy's median home price is $371,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $11,520 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $154,000 (70%) higher, and it is about $230,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Mount Airy the median rent is $1,580 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $97 (6%) higher. It is about $518 (48%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $39 (2%) higher.
