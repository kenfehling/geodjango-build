Home prices

The median home in Mount Airy is valued at $371,500. This is about $11,520 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $154,000 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $230,000 (38%) lower.

Rents

Mount Airy's median rent is $1,580 per month. This is approximately $97 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $518 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $39 (2%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
