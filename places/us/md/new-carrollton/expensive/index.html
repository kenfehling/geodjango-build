Home prices

The median home price in New Carrollton is $271,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $4,060 (1%) higher.It is approximately $53,600 (24%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $330,400 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Carrollton is $1,417. It is roughly $151 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $355 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $124 (8%) lower.
