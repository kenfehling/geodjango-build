In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Washington is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Washington costs $358,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $38,060 (9%) lower.It is about $141,000 (64%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $243,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Fort Washington the median monthly rent is $2,084. This is about $154 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $1,022 (96%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $543 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
