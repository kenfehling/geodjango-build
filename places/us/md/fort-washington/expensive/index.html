Home prices

Fort Washington's median home cost is $358,500. It is roughly $38,060 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $141,000 (64%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $243,000 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Washington is $2,084 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $154 (8%) higher.It is approximately $1,022 (96%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $543 (35%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
