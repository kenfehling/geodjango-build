Compared to other places in this area, Fort Washington is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fort Washington is $358,500. This is about $38,060 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $141,000 (64%) higher, and is approximately $243,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Fort Washington the median monthly rent is $2,084. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $154 (8%) higher. It is about $1,022 (96%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $543 (35%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
