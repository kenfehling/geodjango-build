Home prices

The price of the median home in Somerset is $1,475,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $254,540 (20%) higher.It is approximately $1,258,300 (578%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $874,300 (145%) higher.

Rents

Somerset's median rent is $3,501 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $843 (31%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $2,439 (229%) higher, and is approximately $1,960 (127%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
