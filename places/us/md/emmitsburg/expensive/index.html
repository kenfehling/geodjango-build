Compared to its surrounding areas, Emmitsburg is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Emmitsburg's median home cost is $246,800. It is roughly $30,300 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $29,300 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $354,700 (58%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Emmitsburg is $847. This is roughly $82 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $215 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $694 (45%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
