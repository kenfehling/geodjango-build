Home prices

The median home in Edgemere costs $290,200. This is roughly $64,320 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $72,700 (33%) higher, and it is about $311,300 (51%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Edgemere the median monthly rent is $1,333. It is approximately $70 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $271 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $208 (13%) lower.
