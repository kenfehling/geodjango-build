Home prices

The median home in Herald Harbor costs $464,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,460 (2%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $246,800 (113%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $137,200 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Herald Harbor is $1,163 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $569 (32%) lower. It is about $101 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $378 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
