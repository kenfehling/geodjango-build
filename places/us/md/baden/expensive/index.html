Home prices

The median home in Baden is valued at $368,200. This is about $36,620 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $150,700 (69%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $233,300 (38%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Baden is $684 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $1,458 (68%) lower.It is roughly $378 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $857 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
