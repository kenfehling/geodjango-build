Home prices

The median home in Baden costs $368,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $36,620 (9%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $150,700 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $233,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Baden the median rent is $684 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $1,458 (68%) lower. It is about $378 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $857 (55%) lower.
