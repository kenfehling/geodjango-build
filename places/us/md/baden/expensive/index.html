Compared to other places in this area, Baden is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Baden's median home value is $368,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $36,620 (9%) lower. It is roughly $150,700 (69%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $233,300 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Baden's median rent is $684 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $1,458 (68%) lower. It is about $378 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $857 (55%) lower.
