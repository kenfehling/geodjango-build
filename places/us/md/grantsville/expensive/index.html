Home prices

The median home in Grantsville costs $148,300. This is about $26,820 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $69,200 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $23,300 (18%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grantsville is $514. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $168 (24%) lower.It is approximately $548 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $444 (46%) lower.
