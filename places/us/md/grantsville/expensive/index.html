Home prices

The median home in Grantsville costs $148,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $26,820 (22%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $69,200 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $23,300 (18%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Grantsville's median monthly rent is $514. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $168 (24%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $548 (51%) lower, and is roughly $444 (46%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
