Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Grantsville is $514 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $168 (24%) lower. It is approximately $548 (51%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $444 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
