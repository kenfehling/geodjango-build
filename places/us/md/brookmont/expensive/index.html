Home prices

The median home in Brookmont is valued at $1,045,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $103,580 (9%) lower. It is about $828,300 (380%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $444,300 (73%) higher.

Rents

In Brookmont the median rent is $2,306 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $354 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $1,244 (117%) higher, and is about $765 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
