Compared to the surrounding areas, Severn is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Severn is valued at $362,300. This is roughly $26,540 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $144,800 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $239,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Severn is $1,712 a month. This is roughly $199 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $650 (61%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $171 (11%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
