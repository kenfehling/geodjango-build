Home prices

The median home cost in Severn is $362,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $26,540 (7%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $144,800 (66%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $239,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

Severn's median monthly rent is $1,712. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $199 (13%) higher. It is roughly $650 (61%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $171 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
