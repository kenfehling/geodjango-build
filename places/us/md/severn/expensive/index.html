In comparison to the surrounding areas, Severn is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home price in Severn is $362,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $26,540 (7%) higher.It is about $144,800 (66%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $239,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

Severn's median rent is $1,712 per month. This is approximately $199 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $650 (61%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $171 (11%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
