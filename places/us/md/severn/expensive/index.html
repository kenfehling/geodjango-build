In comparison to its surrounding areas, Severn is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Washington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Severn costs $362,300. This is about $26,540 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $144,800 (66%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $239,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

Severn's median monthly rent is $1,712. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $199 (13%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $650 (61%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is about $171 (11%) higher.
