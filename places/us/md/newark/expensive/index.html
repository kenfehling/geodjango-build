Home prices

Rents

In Newark the median monthly rent is $1,595. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $491 (44%) higher.It is about $533 (50%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Salisbury is approximately $538 (50%) higher.
