Home prices

The value of the median home in Spring Ridge is $341,300. This is about $7,980 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $123,800 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $260,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

Spring Ridge's median rent is $1,654 per month. It is approximately $25 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $592 (55%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $113 (7%) higher.
