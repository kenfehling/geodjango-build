Compared to the surrounding areas, Odenton is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Odenton is $323,500. This is about $71,260 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $106,000 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $278,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Odenton is $1,837. This is about $237 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $775 (72%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $296 (19%) higher.
