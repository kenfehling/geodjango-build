Compared to the surrounding areas, Odenton is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Odenton's median home price is $323,500. This is approximately $71,260 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $106,000 (48%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $278,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Odenton is $1,837 per month. It is approximately $237 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $775 (72%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $296 (19%) higher.
