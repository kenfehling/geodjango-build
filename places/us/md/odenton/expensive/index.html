Compared to nearby areas, Odenton is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Odenton is valued at $323,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $71,260 (18%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $106,000 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $278,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

Odenton's median monthly rent is $1,837. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $237 (14%) higher.It is about $775 (72%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $296 (19%) higher.
