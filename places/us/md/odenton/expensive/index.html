In comparison to nearby areas, Odenton is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Odenton is valued at $323,500. This is roughly $71,260 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $106,000 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $278,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Odenton is $1,837 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $237 (14%) higher. It is roughly $775 (72%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $296 (19%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
