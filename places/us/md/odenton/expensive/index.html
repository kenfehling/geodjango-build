Home prices

The median home in Odenton is valued at $323,500. This is approximately $71,260 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $106,000 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $278,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Odenton the median monthly rent is $1,837. This is approximately $237 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $775 (72%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $296 (19%) higher.
