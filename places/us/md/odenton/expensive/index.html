In comparison to nearby areas, Odenton is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Odenton is valued at $323,500. This is about $71,260 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $106,000 (48%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $278,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Odenton the median rent is $1,837 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $237 (14%) higher. It is roughly $775 (72%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $296 (19%) higher.
