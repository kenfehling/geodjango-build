Compared to the surrounding areas, Chillum is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Chillum's median home value is $292,900. It is roughly $113,180 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $75,400 (34%) higher, and it is roughly $308,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Chillum's median monthly rent is $1,450. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $79 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $388 (36%) higher, and is roughly $91 (5%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
