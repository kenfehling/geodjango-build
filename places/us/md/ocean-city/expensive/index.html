In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ocean City is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlantic City, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ocean City costs $298,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $94,020 (23%) lower. It is about $81,000 (37%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlantic City is about $141,800 (90%) higher.

Rents

Ocean City's median rent is $1,040 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $104 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $22 (2%) lower, and is about $136 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlantic City.
