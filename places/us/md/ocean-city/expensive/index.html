In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ocean City is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Atlantic City, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ocean City is valued at $298,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $94,020 (23%) lower. It is about $81,000 (37%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlantic City it is about $141,800 (90%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ocean City is $1,040 per month. This is about $104 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $22 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlantic City is approximately $136 (15%) higher.
