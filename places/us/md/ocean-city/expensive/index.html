Home prices

The median home in Ocean City costs $298,500. This is approximately $94,020 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $81,000 (37%) higher, and is roughly $141,800 (90%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlantic City.

Rents

In Ocean City the median rent is $1,040 per month. It is approximately $104 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $22 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlantic City it is about $136 (15%) higher.
