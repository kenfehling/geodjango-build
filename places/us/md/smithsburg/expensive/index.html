In comparison to other places in this area, Smithsburg is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Smithsburg is $240,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $21,300 (9%) higher.It is approximately $22,500 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $361,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Smithsburg the median rent is $735 a month. It is roughly $352 (32%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $327 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $806 (52%) lower.
