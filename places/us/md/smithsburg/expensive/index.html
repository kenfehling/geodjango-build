In comparison to nearby areas, Smithsburg is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Smithsburg is $240,000. It is roughly $21,300 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $22,500 (10%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $361,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Smithsburg's median rent is $735 a month. It is about $352 (32%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $327 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $806 (52%) lower.
