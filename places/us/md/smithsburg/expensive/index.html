Compared to nearby areas, Smithsburg is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Smithsburg costs $240,000. It is approximately $21,300 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $22,500 (10%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $361,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Smithsburg's median rent is $735 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $352 (32%) lower. It is approximately $327 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $806 (52%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
