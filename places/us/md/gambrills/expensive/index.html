Home prices

The median home in Gambrills costs $444,300. This is roughly $53,620 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $226,800 (104%) higher, and is about $157,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Gambrills the median monthly rent is $1,375. It is roughly $180 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $313 (29%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $166 (10%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
