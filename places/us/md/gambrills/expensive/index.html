Home prices

The price of the median home in Gambrills is $444,300. It is about $53,620 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $226,800 (104%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $157,200 (26%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Gambrills the median rent is $1,375 a month. It is roughly $180 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $313 (29%) higher, and is approximately $166 (10%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
