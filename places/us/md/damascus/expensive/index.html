In comparison to other places in this area, Damascus is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Damascus is valued at $396,700. This is approximately $49,100 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $179,200 (82%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $204,800 (34%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Damascus is $1,605 a month. This is approximately $149 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $543 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $64 (4%) higher.
