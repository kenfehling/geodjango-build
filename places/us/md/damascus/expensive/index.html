Home prices

The median home in Damascus is valued at $396,700. It is roughly $49,100 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $179,200 (82%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $204,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Damascus the median rent is $1,605 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $149 (8%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $543 (51%) higher, and it is about $64 (4%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
