The price of the median home in Edmonston is $273,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $10,560 (3%) lower. It is about $56,000 (25%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $328,000 (54%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents
