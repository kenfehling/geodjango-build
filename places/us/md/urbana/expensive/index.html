Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Urbana is $2,254 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $469 (26%) higher.It is about $1,192 (112%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $713 (46%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
