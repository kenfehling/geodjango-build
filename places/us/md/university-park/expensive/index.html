The median home in University Park costs $486,500. It is roughly $174,180 (55%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $269,000 (123%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $115,000 (19%) lower.

Rents
