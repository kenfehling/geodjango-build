Home prices

The value of the median home in University Park is $486,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $174,180 (55%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $269,000 (123%) higher, and is roughly $115,000 (19%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in University Park is $2,420 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $1,008 (71%) higher.It is approximately $1,358 (127%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $879 (57%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
