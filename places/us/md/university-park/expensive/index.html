Compared to the surrounding areas, University Park is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in University Park costs $486,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $174,180 (55%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $269,000 (123%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $115,000 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in University Park is $2,420 a month. It is about $1,008 (71%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $1,358 (127%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $879 (57%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
