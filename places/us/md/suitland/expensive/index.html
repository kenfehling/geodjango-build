Home prices

The median home price in Suitland is $225,400. This is about $2,460 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $7,900 (3%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $376,100 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Suitland's median monthly rent is $1,397. It is roughly $60 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $335 (31%) higher, and is about $144 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
