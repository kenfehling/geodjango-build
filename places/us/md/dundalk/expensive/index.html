Home prices

The median home in Dundalk is valued at $149,800. This is approximately $65,600 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $67,700 (31%) lower, and is roughly $451,700 (75%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dundalk is $1,141. This is about $73 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $79 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $400 (25%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
