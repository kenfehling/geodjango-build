Compared to its surrounding areas, Bladensburg is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bladensburg costs $255,100. This is about $22,420 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $37,600 (17%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $346,400 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Bladensburg the median monthly rent is $1,357. It is approximately $108 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $295 (27%) higher, and it is approximately $184 (11%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
