Home prices

Melwood's median home value is $316,000. This is about $30,820 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $98,500 (45%) higher, and is approximately $285,500 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Melwood's median rent is $1,688 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $197 (10%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $626 (58%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $147 (9%) higher.
