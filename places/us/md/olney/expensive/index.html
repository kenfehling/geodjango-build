Home prices

The value of the median home in Olney is $506,400. It is approximately $60,860 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $288,900 (132%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $95,100 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Olney the median rent is $1,948 per month. It is approximately $130 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $886 (83%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $407 (26%) higher.
