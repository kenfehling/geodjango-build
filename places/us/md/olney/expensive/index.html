Compared to its surrounding areas, Olney is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Olney is $506,400. This is about $60,860 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $288,900 (132%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $95,100 (15%) lower.

Rents

Olney's median monthly rent is $1,948. This is roughly $130 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $886 (83%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $407 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
