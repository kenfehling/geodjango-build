In comparison to other places in this area, Pocomoke City is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pocomoke City costs $132,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $50,040 (27%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $85,400 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $148,700 (52%) lower.

Rents

Pocomoke City's median rent is $835 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $132 (13%) lower. It is about $227 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $532 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
