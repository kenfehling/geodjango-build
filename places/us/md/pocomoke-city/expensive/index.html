Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pocomoke City is $132,100. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $50,040 (27%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $85,400 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $148,700 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pocomoke City is $835 per month. It is roughly $132 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $227 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $532 (38%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
