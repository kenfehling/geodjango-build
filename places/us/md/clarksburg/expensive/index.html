Home prices

Rents

In Clarksburg the median rent is $2,089 per month. It is roughly $501 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $1,027 (96%) higher, and is about $548 (35%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
