Compared to its surrounding areas, Clarksburg is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Clarksburg is $482,500. This is roughly $85,460 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $265,000 (121%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $119,000 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Clarksburg the median monthly rent is $2,089. It is approximately $501 (31%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $1,027 (96%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $548 (35%) higher.
