In comparison to its nearby areas, California is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in California is valued at $268,700. This is roughly $89,280 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $51,200 (23%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $332,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in California is $1,567. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $54 (3%) higher.It is approximately $505 (47%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $26 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
