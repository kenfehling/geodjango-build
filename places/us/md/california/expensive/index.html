Home prices

The cost of the median home in California is $268,700. This is approximately $89,280 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $51,200 (23%) higher, and is about $332,800 (55%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in California is $1,567 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $54 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $505 (47%) higher, and it is about $26 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
