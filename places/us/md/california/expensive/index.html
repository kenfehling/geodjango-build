In comparison to nearby areas, California is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in California is $268,700. This is roughly $89,280 (24%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $51,200 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $332,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in California is $1,567. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $54 (3%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $505 (47%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $26 (1%) higher.
