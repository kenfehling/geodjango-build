In comparison to nearby areas, California is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in California costs $268,700. This is roughly $89,280 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $51,200 (23%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $332,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in California is $1,567. This is about $54 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $505 (47%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $26 (1%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
