In comparison to the surrounding areas, California is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

California's median home cost is $268,700. It is about $89,280 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $51,200 (23%) higher, and is roughly $332,800 (55%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

California's median rent is $1,567 a month. It is approximately $54 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $505 (47%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $26 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
