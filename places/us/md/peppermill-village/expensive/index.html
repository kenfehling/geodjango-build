In comparison to the surrounding areas, Peppermill Village is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Peppermill Village is valued at $248,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $12,640 (5%) higher. It is approximately $31,000 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $353,000 (58%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Peppermill Village's median monthly rent is $1,651. This is roughly $252 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $589 (55%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $110 (7%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
