Home prices

The median home price in Sharptown is $154,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $1,740 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $63,000 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $447,000 (74%) lower.

Rents

In Sharptown the median rent is $870 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $90 (9%) lower. It is about $192 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $671 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
