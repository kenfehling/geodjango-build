Home prices

The median home cost in Sharptown is $154,500. It is roughly $1,740 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $63,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $447,000 (74%) lower.

Rents

In Sharptown the median rent is $870 a month. It is about $90 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $192 (18%) lower, and is about $671 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
