Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlestown is $244,500. This is approximately $21,960 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $27,000 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $81,500 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Charlestown the median monthly rent is $1,185. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $218 (22%) higher. It is about $123 (11%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $143 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
