In comparison to the surrounding areas, Charlestown is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

Charlestown's median home value is $244,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $21,960 (9%) higher.It is roughly $27,000 (12%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $81,500 (50%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Charlestown is $1,185 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $218 (22%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $123 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $143 (13%) higher.
