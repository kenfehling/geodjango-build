In comparison to its nearby areas, Charlestown is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlestown is $244,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $21,960 (9%) higher.It is about $27,000 (12%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $81,500 (50%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Charlestown is $1,185 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $218 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $123 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $143 (13%) higher.
