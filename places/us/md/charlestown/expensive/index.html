In comparison to its nearby areas, Charlestown is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlestown is $244,500. It is approximately $21,960 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $27,000 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $81,500 (50%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Charlestown is $1,185 per month. It is approximately $218 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $123 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $143 (13%) higher.
