Home prices

The median home in Greenbelt costs $227,400. It is about $63,140 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $9,900 (4%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $374,100 (62%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greenbelt is $1,610. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $548 (51%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $69 (4%) higher.
