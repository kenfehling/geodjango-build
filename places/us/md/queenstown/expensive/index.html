In comparison to nearby areas, Queenstown is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Queenstown is $286,600. This is approximately $76,960 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $69,100 (31%) higher, and it is roughly $123,600 (75%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Queenstown the median monthly rent is $1,629. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $567 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $587 (56%) higher.
