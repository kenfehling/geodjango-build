Home prices

The price of the median home in Queenstown is $286,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $76,960 (21%) lower.It is about $69,100 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $123,600 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Queenstown is $1,629. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas.It is about $567 (53%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $587 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
