Compared to the surrounding areas, Sykesville is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sykesville is $379,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $19,720 (5%) higher. It is about $162,000 (74%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $222,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Sykesville the median monthly rent is $1,356. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $68 (4%) lower. It is approximately $294 (27%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $185 (12%) lower.
