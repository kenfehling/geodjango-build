Home prices

The median home cost in Sykesville is $379,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $19,720 (5%) higher.It is roughly $162,000 (74%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $222,000 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Sykesville the median monthly rent is $1,356. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $68 (4%) lower.It is approximately $294 (27%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $185 (12%) lower.
