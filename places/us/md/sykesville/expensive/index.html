Compared to other places in this area, Sykesville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sykesville costs $379,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $19,720 (5%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $162,000 (74%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $222,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sykesville is $1,356. This is roughly $68 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $294 (27%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $185 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
