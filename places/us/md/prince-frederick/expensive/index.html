Home prices

The median home in Prince Frederick costs $309,400. This is about $23,980 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $91,900 (42%) higher, and is about $292,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Prince Frederick the median rent is $801 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $914 (53%) lower.It is about $261 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $740 (48%) lower.
