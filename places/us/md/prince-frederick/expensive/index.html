Home prices

Prince Frederick's median home price is $309,400. This is approximately $23,980 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $91,900 (42%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $292,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Prince Frederick is $801 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $914 (53%) lower. It is about $261 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $740 (48%) lower.
