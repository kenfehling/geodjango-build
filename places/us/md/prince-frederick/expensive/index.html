Compared to the surrounding areas, Prince Frederick is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Washingtonhowever, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Prince Frederick is $309,400. It is approximately $23,980 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $91,900 (42%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $292,100 (48%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Prince Frederick the median rent is $801 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $914 (53%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $261 (24%) lower, and is approximately $740 (48%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
