Home prices

The median home in Prince Frederick costs $309,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $23,980 (8%) higher.It is roughly $91,900 (42%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $292,100 (48%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Prince Frederick is $801. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $914 (53%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $261 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $740 (48%) lower.
