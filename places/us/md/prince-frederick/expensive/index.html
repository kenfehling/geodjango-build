Home prices

Prince Frederick's median home cost is $309,400. This is about $23,980 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $91,900 (42%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $292,100 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Prince Frederick the median monthly rent is $801. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $914 (53%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $261 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $740 (48%) lower.
