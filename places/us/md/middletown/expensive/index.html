Home prices

Middletown's median home cost is $416,900. This is roughly $75,480 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $199,400 (91%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $184,600 (30%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Middletown's median rent is $1,366 per month. It is roughly $162 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $304 (28%) higher, and is roughly $175 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
