Compared to nearby areas, Middletown is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Middletown is $416,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $75,480 (22%) higher. It is roughly $199,400 (91%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $184,600 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Middletown's median rent is $1,366 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $162 (13%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $304 (28%) higher, and is approximately $175 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
