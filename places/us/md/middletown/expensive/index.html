Home prices

The cost of the median home in Middletown is $416,900. It is approximately $75,480 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $199,400 (91%) higher, and it is approximately $184,600 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middletown is $1,366. It is approximately $162 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $304 (28%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $175 (11%) lower.
