Compared to the surrounding areas, Brookeville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brookeville costs $517,900. This is roughly $50,220 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $300,400 (138%) higher, and it is approximately $83,600 (13%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Brookeville the median monthly rent is $3,083. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $1,585 (105%) higher. It is about $2,021 (190%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $1,542 (100%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
