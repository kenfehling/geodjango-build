Compared to other places in this area, Spencerville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Spencerville costs $564,600. It is about $96,840 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $347,100 (159%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $36,900 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Spencerville the median monthly rent is $2,795. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $1,230 (78%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,733 (163%) higher, and is approximately $1,254 (81%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
