In comparison to its surrounding areas, Spencerville is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Spencerville is valued at $564,600. This is about $96,840 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $347,100 (159%) higher, and is about $36,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Spencerville is $2,795. This is about $1,230 (78%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $1,733 (163%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $1,254 (81%) higher.
