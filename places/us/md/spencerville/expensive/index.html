Home prices

Spencerville's median home cost is $564,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $96,840 (20%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $347,100 (159%) higher, and it is about $36,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Spencerville's median rent is $2,795 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $1,230 (78%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $1,733 (163%) higher, and it is about $1,254 (81%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
