Home prices

Silver Spring's median home value is $516,900. It is approximately $12,240 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $299,400 (137%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $84,600 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Silver Spring's median monthly rent is $1,665. It is about $283 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $603 (56%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $124 (8%) higher.
