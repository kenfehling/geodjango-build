Home prices

The median home value in Adamstown is $443,100. This is roughly $79,580 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $225,600 (103%) higher, and it is about $158,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Adamstown's median rent is $2,045 per month. It is roughly $338 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $983 (92%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $504 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
