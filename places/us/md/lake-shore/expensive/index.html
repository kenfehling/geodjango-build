Much like its surrounding areas, it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Lake Shore is $382,500. This is approximately $7,980 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $165,000 (75%) higher, and is roughly $219,000 (36%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Shore is $1,737. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $29 (1%) lower. It is about $675 (63%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $196 (12%) higher.
