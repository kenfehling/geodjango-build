Home prices

The value of the median home in Lake Shore is $382,500. It is roughly $7,980 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $165,000 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $219,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

Lake Shore's median monthly rent is $1,737. This is about $29 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $675 (63%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $196 (12%) higher.
