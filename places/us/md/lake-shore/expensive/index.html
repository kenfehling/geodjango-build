Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Shore is $382,500. It is approximately $7,980 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $165,000 (75%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $219,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Shore is $1,737. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $29 (1%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $675 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $196 (12%) higher.
