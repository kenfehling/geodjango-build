In comparison to the surrounding areas, Darnestown is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Washington, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Darnestown costs $744,500. It is approximately $98,740 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $527,000 (242%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $143,000 (23%) higher compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Darnestown's median rent is $1,688 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $580 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $626 (58%) higher, and it is approximately $147 (9%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
