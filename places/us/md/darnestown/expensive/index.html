Home prices

The median home value in Darnestown is $744,500. This is roughly $98,740 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $527,000 (242%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $143,000 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Darnestown the median rent is $1,688 a month. This is roughly $580 (25%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $626 (58%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $147 (9%) higher.
