Home prices

Halfway's median home price is $178,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $4,900 (2%) higher. It is roughly $39,300 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $423,300 (70%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Halfway is $890 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $172 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $651 (42%) lower.
