Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rockville is $1,921. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $197 (11%) higher.It is roughly $859 (80%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $380 (24%) higher.
