Home prices

Rockville's median home cost is $540,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $10,820 (2%) higher.It is roughly $322,500 (148%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington is about $61,500 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rockville is $1,921 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $197 (11%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $859 (80%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $380 (24%) higher.
