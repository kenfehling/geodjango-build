Home prices

The median home in Rockville costs $540,000. It is about $10,820 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $322,500 (148%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $61,500 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Rockville's median rent is $1,921 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $197 (11%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $859 (80%) higher, and is about $380 (24%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
