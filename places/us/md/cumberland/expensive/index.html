In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cumberland is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Harrisonburg.

Home prices

The median home value in Cumberland is $94,100. This is approximately $14,080 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $123,400 (56%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Harrisonburg it is roughly $109,500 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Cumberland the median monthly rent is $662. This is approximately $86 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $400 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisonburg it is approximately $221 (25%) lower.
