Home prices

The price of the median home in Brentwood is $298,600. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $81,100 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $302,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Brentwood the median rent is $1,409 per month. This is approximately $37 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $347 (32%) higher, and it is roughly $132 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
