Home prices

Rosemont's median home value is $291,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $43,460 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $73,500 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $310,500 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Rosemont the median monthly rent is $1,714. This is roughly $440 (34%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $652 (61%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $173 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
