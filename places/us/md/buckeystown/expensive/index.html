Home prices

The median home in Buckeystown costs $406,500. This is approximately $21,740 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $189,000 (86%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $195,000 (32%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Buckeystown's median rent is $1,838 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $225 (13%) higher. It is about $776 (73%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington is about $297 (19%) higher.
