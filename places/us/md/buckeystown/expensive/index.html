Home prices

The median home in Buckeystown is valued at $406,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $21,740 (5%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $189,000 (86%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $195,000 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Buckeystown the median monthly rent is $1,838. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $225 (13%) higher.It is approximately $776 (73%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $297 (19%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
