Home prices

The cost of the median home in Long Beach is $277,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $16,840 (5%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $59,700 (27%) higher, and is roughly $324,300 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Long Beach's median rent is $1,531 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $94 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $469 (44%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Washington.
