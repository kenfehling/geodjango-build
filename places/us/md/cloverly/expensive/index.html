Home prices

The median home in Cloverly is valued at $487,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $45,260 (10%) higher.It is approximately $270,300 (124%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $113,700 (18%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Cloverly's median rent is $1,477 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $548 (27%) lower.It is about $415 (39%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $64 (4%) lower.
