In comparison to the surrounding areas, Annapolis is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Annapolis is valued at $420,500. It is about $48,840 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $203,000 (93%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $181,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Annapolis the median monthly rent is $1,583. This is roughly $259 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $521 (49%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $42 (2%) higher.
