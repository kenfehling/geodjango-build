Home prices

The median home in Annapolis is valued at $420,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $48,840 (10%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $203,000 (93%) higher, and it is roughly $181,000 (30%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Annapolis the median monthly rent is $1,583. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $259 (14%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $521 (49%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $42 (2%) higher.
