Compared to its surrounding areas, Annapolis is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Annapolis is $420,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $48,840 (10%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $203,000 (93%) higher, and is roughly $181,000 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Annapolis the median rent is $1,583 a month. This is approximately $259 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $521 (49%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $42 (2%) higher.
