Compared to its surrounding areas, Riverside is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Riverside's median home value is $183,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $43,520 (19%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $34,100 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $20,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Riverside is $1,412 per month. It is approximately $311 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $350 (32%) higher, and it is roughly $370 (35%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
