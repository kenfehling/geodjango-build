Home prices

The median home in Riverside costs $183,400. This is approximately $43,520 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $34,100 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $20,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

Riverside's median monthly rent is $1,412. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $311 (28%) higher.It is about $350 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $370 (35%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
