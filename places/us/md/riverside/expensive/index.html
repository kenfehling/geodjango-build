In comparison to its surrounding areas, Riverside is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside is valued at $183,400. This is roughly $43,520 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $34,100 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $20,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverside is $1,412. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $311 (28%) higher.It is roughly $350 (32%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $370 (35%) higher.
