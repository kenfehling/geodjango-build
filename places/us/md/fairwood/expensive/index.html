Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairwood is $514,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $145,840 (39%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $297,000 (136%) higher, and is roughly $87,000 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Fairwood's median rent is $2,918 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $913 (45%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $1,856 (174%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $1,377 (89%) higher.
