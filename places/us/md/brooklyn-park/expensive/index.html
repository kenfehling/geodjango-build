Home prices

The value of the median home in Brooklyn Park is $210,800. This is roughly $15,680 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $6,700 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $390,700 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brooklyn Park is $1,260. It is approximately $16 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $198 (18%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $281 (18%) lower.
