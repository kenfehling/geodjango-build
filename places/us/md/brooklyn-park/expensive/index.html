Home prices

The median home in Brooklyn Park is valued at $210,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $15,680 (6%) lower. It is approximately $6,700 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $390,700 (64%) lower.

Rents

In Brooklyn Park the median monthly rent is $1,260. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $16 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $198 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $281 (18%) lower.
