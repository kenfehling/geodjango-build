Home prices

Brooklyn Park's median home cost is $210,800. It is roughly $15,680 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $6,700 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $390,700 (64%) lower.

Rents

Brooklyn Park's median rent is $1,260 per month. It is roughly $16 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $198 (18%) higher, and is about $281 (18%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
