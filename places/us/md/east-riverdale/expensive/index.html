Home prices

The median home price in East Riverdale is $249,700. This is roughly $18,760 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $32,200 (14%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $351,800 (58%) lower.

Rents

In East Riverdale the median monthly rent is $1,346. It is approximately $109 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $284 (26%) higher, and it is about $195 (12%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
