Home prices

The median home value in Williamsport is $160,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16,100 (9%) lower.It is about $56,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $440,800 (73%) lower.

Rents

In Williamsport the median monthly rent is $838. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $63 (7%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $224 (21%) lower, and is approximately $703 (45%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
