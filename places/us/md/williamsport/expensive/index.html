Home prices

The median home in Williamsport is valued at $160,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,100 (9%) lower.It is approximately $56,800 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $440,800 (73%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Williamsport is $838. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $63 (7%) lower.It is about $224 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $703 (45%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
