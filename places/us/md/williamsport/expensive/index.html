Home prices

The cost of the median home in Williamsport is $160,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $16,100 (9%) lower. It is about $56,800 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $440,800 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Williamsport the median rent is $838 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $63 (7%) lower. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $703 (45%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
