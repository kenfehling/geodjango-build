In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ocean Pines is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Atlantic City, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ocean Pines is $278,600. This is about $117,900 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $61,100 (28%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlantic City it is approximately $121,900 (77%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ocean Pines is $1,480. This is approximately $424 (40%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $418 (39%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $576 (63%) higher compared to the city of Atlantic City.
