Home prices

The median home in Ocean Pines costs $278,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $117,900 (29%) lower.It is about $61,100 (28%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $121,900 (77%) higher compared to the city of Atlantic City.

Rents

In Ocean Pines the median monthly rent is $1,480. Compared to nearby areas this is about $424 (40%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $418 (39%) higher, and it is approximately $576 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlantic City.
