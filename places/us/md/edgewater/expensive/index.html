Home prices

Rents

In Edgewater the median monthly rent is $1,755. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $52 (2%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $693 (65%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $214 (13%) higher.
