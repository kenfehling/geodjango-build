Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kensington is $1,592. This is roughly $492 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $530 (49%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $51 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
