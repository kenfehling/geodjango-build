In comparison to other places in this area, Kensington is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Washington, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kensington is $712,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $43,480 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $495,000 (227%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $111,000 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kensington is $1,592. It is about $492 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $530 (49%) higher, and is roughly $51 (3%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
