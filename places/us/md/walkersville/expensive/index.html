Compared to other places in this area, Walkersville is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Walkersville is $290,800. This is approximately $34,620 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $73,300 (33%) higher, and is approximately $310,700 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Walkersville the median monthly rent is $1,344. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $47 (3%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $282 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $197 (12%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
