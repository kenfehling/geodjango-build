White Marsh's median home price is $282,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $61,880 (28%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $64,600 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $319,400 (53%) lower.

Rents

White Marsh's median rent is $1,414 a month. It is roughly $101 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $352 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $127 (8%) lower.
