Home prices

The median home in Marlton costs $293,200. It is roughly $30,720 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $75,700 (34%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $308,300 (51%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Marlton is $1,693 per month. This is roughly $311 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $631 (59%) higher, and is about $152 (9%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
