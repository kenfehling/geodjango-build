Compared to its nearby areas, Bel Air is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Bel Air costs $245,400. It is roughly $80,940 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $27,900 (12%) higher, and is roughly $82,400 (50%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Bel Air's median rent is $1,275 per month. It is roughly $194 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $213 (20%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $233 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
