Compared to its surrounding areas, Bel Air is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Bel Air is $245,400. It is approximately $80,940 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $27,900 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $82,400 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Bel Air the median rent is $1,275 per month. This is about $194 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $213 (20%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $233 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
