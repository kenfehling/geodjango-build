Home prices

The median home in Chester is valued at $335,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $28,680 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $117,900 (54%) higher, and it is about $266,100 (44%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Chester is $1,717 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $655 (61%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $176 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
