Home prices

The median home price in Chester is $335,400. It is roughly $28,680 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $117,900 (54%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $266,100 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Chester the median rent is $1,717 a month. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $655 (61%) higher, and it is roughly $176 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
