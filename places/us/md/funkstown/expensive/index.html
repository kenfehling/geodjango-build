Home prices

Funkstown's median home cost is $158,400. It is about $71,300 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $59,100 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $443,100 (73%) lower.

Rents

In Funkstown the median rent is $700 per month. This is about $329 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $362 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $841 (54%) lower.
