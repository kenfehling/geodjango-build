Home prices

The median home in Stevensville is valued at $362,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $4,200 (1%) higher.It is roughly $145,300 (66%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $238,700 (39%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Stevensville the median rent is $1,665 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $63 (3%) lower.It is about $603 (56%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $124 (8%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
