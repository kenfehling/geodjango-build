Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stevensville is $1,665. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $63 (3%) lower.It is approximately $603 (56%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $124 (8%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
