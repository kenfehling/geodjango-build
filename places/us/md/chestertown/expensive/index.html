Compared to other places in this area, Chestertown is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Chestertown's median home value is $242,700. This is roughly $42,960 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $25,200 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $79,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chestertown is $842. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $120 (12%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $220 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $200 (19%) lower.
