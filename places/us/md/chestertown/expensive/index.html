Compared to nearby areas, Chestertown is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Chestertown is $242,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $42,960 (21%) higher.It is roughly $25,200 (11%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $79,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Chestertown is $842 per month. It is approximately $120 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $220 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $200 (19%) lower.
