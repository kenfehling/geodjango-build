Compared to its surrounding areas, Chestertown is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chestertown is valued at $242,700. This is roughly $42,960 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $25,200 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $79,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chestertown is $842. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $120 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $220 (20%) lower, and it is about $200 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
