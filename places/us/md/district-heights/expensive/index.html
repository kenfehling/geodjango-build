In comparison to its surrounding areas, District Heights is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in District Heights is $251,600. This is approximately $21,540 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $34,100 (15%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $349,900 (58%) lower.

Rents

In District Heights the median monthly rent is $1,248. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $114 (8%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $186 (17%) higher, and it is about $293 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
