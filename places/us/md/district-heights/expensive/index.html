In comparison to the surrounding areas, District Heights is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in District Heights is $251,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $21,540 (9%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $34,100 (15%) higher, and is about $349,900 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in District Heights is $1,248 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $114 (8%) lower.It is approximately $186 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $293 (19%) lower.
