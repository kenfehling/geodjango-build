Compared to nearby areas, District Heights is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in District Heights is $251,600. This is about $21,540 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $34,100 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $349,900 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in District Heights is $1,248 per month. It is about $114 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $186 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $293 (19%) lower.
