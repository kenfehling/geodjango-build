Compared to other places in this area, Bowie is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Bowie is $338,100. This is roughly $65,840 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $120,600 (55%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $263,400 (43%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bowie is $1,888. It is roughly $323 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $826 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $347 (22%) higher.
