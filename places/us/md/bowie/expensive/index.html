In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bowie is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bowie costs $338,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $65,840 (16%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $120,600 (55%) higher, and is about $263,400 (43%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Bowie's median rent is $1,888 a month. It is about $323 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $826 (77%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $347 (22%) higher.
