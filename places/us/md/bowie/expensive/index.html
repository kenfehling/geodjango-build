Home prices

The median home price in Bowie is $338,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $65,840 (16%) lower. It is approximately $120,600 (55%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $263,400 (43%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Bowie the median rent is $1,888 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $323 (14%) lower. It is approximately $826 (77%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $347 (22%) higher.
