Home prices

The price of the median home in Waldorf is $279,100. It is about $68,180 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $61,600 (28%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $322,400 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Waldorf's median monthly rent is $1,714. This is roughly $198 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $652 (61%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $173 (11%) higher.
