Home prices

The median home in Waldorf is valued at $279,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $68,180 (19%) lower.It is about $61,600 (28%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $322,400 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waldorf is $1,714. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $198 (10%) lower.It is roughly $652 (61%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $173 (11%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
