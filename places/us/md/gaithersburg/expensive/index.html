In comparison to nearby areas, Gaithersburg is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Gaithersburg is $392,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $89,180 (18%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $174,700 (80%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $209,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Gaithersburg the median rent is $1,740 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $59 (3%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $678 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $199 (12%) higher.
