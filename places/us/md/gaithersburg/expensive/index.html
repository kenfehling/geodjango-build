In comparison to its nearby areas, Gaithersburg is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Gaithersburg is $392,200. It is roughly $89,180 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $174,700 (80%) higher, and is about $209,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Gaithersburg is $1,740 a month. This is roughly $59 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $678 (63%) higher, and is about $199 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
