In comparison to other places in this area, Gaithersburg is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gaithersburg is valued at $392,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $89,180 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $174,700 (80%) higher, and it is about $209,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Gaithersburg the median monthly rent is $1,740. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $59 (3%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $678 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $199 (12%) higher.
