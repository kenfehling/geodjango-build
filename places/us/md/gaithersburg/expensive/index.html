In comparison to its surrounding areas, Gaithersburg is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Gaithersburg is $392,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $89,180 (18%) lower. It is approximately $174,700 (80%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $209,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gaithersburg is $1,740. This is roughly $59 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $678 (63%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $199 (12%) higher.
