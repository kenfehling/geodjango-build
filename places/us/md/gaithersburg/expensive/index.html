Compared to its surrounding areas, Gaithersburg is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gaithersburg is valued at $392,200. This is approximately $89,180 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $174,700 (80%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $209,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Gaithersburg the median rent is $1,740 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $59 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $678 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $199 (12%) higher.
