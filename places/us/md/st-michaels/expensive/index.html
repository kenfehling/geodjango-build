Home prices

The median home in St. Michaels costs $371,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $12,640 (3%) lower. It is about $154,400 (70%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $229,600 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

St. Michaels's median rent is $902 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $560 (38%) lower. It is roughly $160 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $639 (41%) lower.
