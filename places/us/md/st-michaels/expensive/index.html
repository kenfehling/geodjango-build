Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Michaels is $371,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $12,640 (3%) lower. It is about $154,400 (70%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $229,600 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In St. Michaels the median monthly rent is $902. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $560 (38%) lower. It is roughly $160 (15%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $639 (41%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
