Compared to its nearby areas, Towson is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Towson is $363,800. It is roughly $32,100 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $146,300 (67%) higher, and is roughly $237,700 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Towson's median monthly rent is $1,438. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $78 (5%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $376 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $103 (6%) lower.
