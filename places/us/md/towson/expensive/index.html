Home prices

The price of the median home in Towson is $363,800. It is approximately $32,100 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $146,300 (67%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $237,700 (39%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Towson the median monthly rent is $1,438. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $78 (5%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $376 (35%) higher, and it is roughly $103 (6%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
