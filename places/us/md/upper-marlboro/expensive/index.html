In comparison to its surrounding areas, Upper Marlboro is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Upper Marlboro costs $227,300. It is about $74,520 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $9,800 (4%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $374,200 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Upper Marlboro's median rent is $1,893 a month. It is about $112 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $831 (78%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $352 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
