Home prices

The median home in Maryland City is valued at $315,500. It is roughly $19,140 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $98,000 (45%) higher, and it is about $286,000 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Maryland City's median rent is $1,824 per month. This is roughly $250 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $762 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $283 (18%) higher.
