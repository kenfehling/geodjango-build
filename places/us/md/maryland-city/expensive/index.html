Compared to its nearby areas, Maryland City is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maryland City is valued at $315,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $19,140 (5%) lower.It is about $98,000 (45%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $286,000 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Maryland City the median rent is $1,824 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $250 (15%) higher.It is approximately $762 (71%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $283 (18%) higher.
