Home prices

The median home in Maryland City is valued at $315,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $19,140 (5%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $98,000 (45%) higher, and it is approximately $286,000 (47%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Maryland City the median monthly rent is $1,824. It is about $250 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $762 (71%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $283 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
