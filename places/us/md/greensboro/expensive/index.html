Home prices

The cost of the median home in Greensboro is $147,700. It is roughly $27,240 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $69,800 (32%) lower, and is roughly $15,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Greensboro's median monthly rent is $840. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $92 (9%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $222 (20%) lower, and it is roughly $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
