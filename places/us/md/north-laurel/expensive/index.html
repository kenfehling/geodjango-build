Home prices

Rents

The median rent in North Laurel is $1,733 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $403 (18%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $671 (63%) higher, and it is approximately $192 (12%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
