In comparison to the surrounding areas, Takoma Park is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Takoma Park's median home cost is $583,800. It is approximately $237,960 (68%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $366,300 (168%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $17,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Takoma Park the median monthly rent is $1,143. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $348 (23%) lower.It is approximately $81 (7%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $398 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
