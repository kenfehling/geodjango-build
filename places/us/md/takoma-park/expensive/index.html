In comparison to the surrounding areas, Takoma Park is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Washington, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Takoma Park is valued at $583,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $237,960 (68%) higher.It is roughly $366,300 (168%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $17,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

Takoma Park's median rent is $1,143 per month. It is roughly $348 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $81 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $398 (25%) lower.
