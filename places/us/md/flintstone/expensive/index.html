Home prices

The median home in Flintstone costs $173,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $79,980 (85%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $43,900 (20%) lower, and it is roughly $96,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of Frederick.

Rents

In Flintstone the median rent is $1,383 a month. This is approximately $656 (90%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $321 (30%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Frederick.
