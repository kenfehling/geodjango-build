Compared to other places in this area, Mount Rainier is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Rainier costs $354,400. This is roughly $69,160 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $136,900 (62%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $247,100 (41%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Mount Rainier the median monthly rent is $1,245. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $234 (15%) lower. It is about $183 (17%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $296 (19%) lower.
