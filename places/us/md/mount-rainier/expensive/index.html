Home prices

The median home in Mount Rainier costs $354,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $69,160 (24%) higher.It is roughly $136,900 (62%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $247,100 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Rainier is $1,245 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $234 (15%) lower.It is roughly $183 (17%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $296 (19%) lower.
