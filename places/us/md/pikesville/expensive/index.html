Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pikesville is $319,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $33,840 (11%) higher.It is approximately $102,400 (47%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $281,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

Pikesville's median rent is $1,435 per month. This is about $31 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $373 (35%) higher, and is about $106 (6%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
