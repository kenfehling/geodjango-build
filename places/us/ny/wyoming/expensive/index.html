Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wyoming is $93,100. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $9,500 (9%) lower. It is about $124,400 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is about $3,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

Wyoming's median monthly rent is $633. It is roughly $83 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $429 (40%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $143 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
