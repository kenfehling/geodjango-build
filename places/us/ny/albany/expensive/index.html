In comparison to nearby areas, Albany is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Albany costs $179,100. It is roughly $23,020 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $38,400 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $22,900 (14%) higher.

Rents

Albany's median monthly rent is $969. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $156 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $93 (8%) lower, and it is roughly $92 (10%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
