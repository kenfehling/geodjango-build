In comparison to the surrounding areas, Albany is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Springfield however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Albany's median home price is $179,100. This is approximately $23,020 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $38,400 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $22,900 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Albany's median monthly rent is $969. Compared to other places in this area it is about $156 (13%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $93 (8%) lower, and it is approximately $92 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
