In comparison to other places in this area, Albany is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home in Albany costs $179,100. This is about $23,020 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $38,400 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is roughly $22,900 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Albany is $969 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $156 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $93 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is roughly $92 (10%) higher.
