In comparison to nearby areas, Albany is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home cost in Albany is $179,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $23,020 (11%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $38,400 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $22,900 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Albany is $969 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $156 (13%) lower.It is roughly $93 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Springfield is roughly $92 (10%) higher.
