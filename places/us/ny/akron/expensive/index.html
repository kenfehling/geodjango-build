Compared to its surrounding areas, Akron is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Akron costs $144,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $21,700 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $73,300 (33%) lower, and is roughly $54,400 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median rent in Akron is $676 per month. It is roughly $248 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $386 (36%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $100 (12%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
