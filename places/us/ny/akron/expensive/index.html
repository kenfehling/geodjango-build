Home prices

The median home value in Akron is $144,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $21,700 (13%) lower. It is about $73,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $54,400 (60%) higher.

Rents

In Akron the median rent is $676 per month. It is approximately $248 (26%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $386 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is approximately $100 (12%) lower.
