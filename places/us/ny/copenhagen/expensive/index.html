Home prices

The median home in Copenhagen is valued at $92,800. It is approximately $29,460 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $124,700 (57%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $1,600 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Copenhagen's median rent is $742 per month. It is about $309 (29%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $320 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $72 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
