Home prices

Copenhagen's median home value is $92,800. It is about $29,460 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $124,700 (57%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $1,600 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Copenhagen the median monthly rent is $742. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $309 (29%) lower. It is about $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $72 (8%) lower.
