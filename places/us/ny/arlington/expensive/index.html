Home prices

The median home in Arlington costs $201,900. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $24,820 (10%) lower. It is about $15,600 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $404,100 (66%) lower.

Rents

Arlington's median rent is $1,142 a month. It is approximately $533 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $80 (7%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $301 (20%) lower.
