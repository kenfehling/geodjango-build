Home prices

The median home cost in Arlington is $201,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $24,820 (10%) lower. It is roughly $15,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $404,100 (66%) lower.

Rents

Arlington's median rent is $1,142 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $533 (31%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $80 (7%) higher, and is about $301 (20%) lower compared to the city of New York.
