In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Rochester is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Rochester costs $104,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $74,200 (41%) lower. It is about $113,200 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $21,200 (25%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

East Rochester's median monthly rent is $890. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $129 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $172 (16%) lower, and is approximately $42 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
