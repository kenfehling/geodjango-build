Rents

The median monthly rent in East Rochester is $890. It is approximately $129 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $172 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $42 (4%) higher.
