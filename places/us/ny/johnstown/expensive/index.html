Home prices

The median home in Johnstown costs $94,400. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $123,100 (56%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $84,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median rent in Johnstown is $818 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $26 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $244 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany it is roughly $151 (15%) lower.
