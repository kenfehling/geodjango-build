In comparison to its nearby areas, Oneida is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oneida is valued at $114,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $27,600 (19%) lower. It is about $102,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $20,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oneida is $739 per month. It is roughly $145 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $75 (9%) lower.
