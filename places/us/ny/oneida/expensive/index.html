In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oneida is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Oneida is valued at $114,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $27,600 (19%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $102,900 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $20,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

Oneida's median monthly rent is $739. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $145 (16%) lower.It is approximately $323 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $75 (9%) lower.
