Home prices

The median home in Morris is valued at $104,400. It is about $13,000 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $113,100 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $10,000 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morris is $668. It is roughly $9 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $394 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $146 (17%) lower.
