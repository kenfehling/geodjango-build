Home prices

The median home value in Morris is $104,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $13,000 (11%) lower. It is about $113,100 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $10,000 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Morris the median rent is $668 a month. It is approximately $9 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $394 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $146 (17%) lower.
