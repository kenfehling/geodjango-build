Compared to its surrounding areas, Morris is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Morris is $104,400. This is about $13,000 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $113,100 (52%) lower, and it is about $10,000 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morris is $668. This is roughly $9 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $394 (37%) lower, and it is about $146 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
