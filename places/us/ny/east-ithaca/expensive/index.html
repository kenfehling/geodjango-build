Compared to other places in this area, East Ithaca is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Ithaca is valued at $242,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $52,460 (17%) lower.It is about $25,400 (11%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $159,800 (192%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

East Ithaca's median rent is $1,136 a month. It is approximately $125 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $74 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $288 (33%) higher.
