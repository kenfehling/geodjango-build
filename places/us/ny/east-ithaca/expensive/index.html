Compared to its surrounding areas, East Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Ithaca costs $242,900. This is roughly $52,460 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $25,400 (11%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $159,800 (192%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

East Ithaca's median rent is $1,136 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $125 (9%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $74 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $288 (33%) higher.
