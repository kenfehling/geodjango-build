In comparison to the surrounding areas, Utica is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Utica is $95,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $32,560 (25%) lower. It is approximately $121,600 (55%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $1,500 (1%) higher.

Rents

Utica's median rent is $725 a month. This is about $145 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and is about $89 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
