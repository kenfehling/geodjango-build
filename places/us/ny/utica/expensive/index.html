In comparison to the surrounding areas, Utica is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home cost in Utica is $95,900. It is roughly $32,560 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $121,600 (55%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $1,500 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Utica is $725. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $145 (16%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $89 (10%) lower.
