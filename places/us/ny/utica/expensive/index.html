In comparison to other places in this area, Utica is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Utica is $95,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $32,560 (25%) lower.It is roughly $121,600 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $1,500 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Utica is $725. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $145 (16%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $89 (10%) lower.
