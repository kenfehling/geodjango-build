Home prices

The median home in South Glens Falls is valued at $161,300. It is approximately $7,780 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $56,200 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany it is approximately $17,800 (9%) lower.

Rents

South Glens Falls's median rent is $909 per month. It is roughly $41 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $153 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is about $60 (6%) lower.
