Compared to nearby areas, North Tonawanda is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in North Tonawanda is $122,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $18,180 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $94,800 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is about $32,900 (36%) higher.

Rents

North Tonawanda's median monthly rent is $706. This is approximately $153 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $356 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $70 (9%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
