Compared to the surrounding areas, North Tonawanda is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home price in North Tonawanda is $122,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $18,180 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $94,800 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is roughly $32,900 (36%) higher.

Rents

North Tonawanda's median rent is $706 a month. It is roughly $153 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $356 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $70 (9%) lower.
