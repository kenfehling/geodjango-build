In comparison to nearby areas, North Tonawanda is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

North Tonawanda's median home price is $122,700. It is about $18,180 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $94,800 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $32,900 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Tonawanda is $706 per month. This is about $153 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $356 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $70 (9%) lower.
