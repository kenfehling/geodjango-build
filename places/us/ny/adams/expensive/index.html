Home prices

The price of the median home in Adams is $153,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $6,180 (4%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $63,800 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $59,300 (62%) higher.

Rents

Adams's median monthly rent is $744. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $174 (18%) lower. It is approximately $318 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $70 (8%) lower.
