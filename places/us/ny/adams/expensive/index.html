The cost of the median home in Adams is $153,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $6,180 (4%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $63,800 (29%) lower, and it is about $59,300 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Adams the median rent is $744 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $174 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $318 (29%) lower, and is about $70 (8%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
