Home prices

The median home in Croton-on-Hudson is valued at $546,500. This is approximately $213,300 (64%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $329,000 (151%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $59,500 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents
