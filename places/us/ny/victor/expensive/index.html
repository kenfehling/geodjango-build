Home prices

The value of the median home in Victor is $187,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $41,400 (28%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $30,400 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $104,000 (125%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Victor is $791 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $52 (6%) lower. It is roughly $271 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $57 (6%) lower.
