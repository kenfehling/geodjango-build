In comparison to its surrounding areas, Watertown is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Watertown is valued at $133,400. This is approximately $7,880 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $84,100 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $39,000 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Watertown is $806. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $46 (5%) lower. It is roughly $256 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Syracuse.
