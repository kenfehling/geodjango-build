Home prices

The median home in Watertown costs $133,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $7,880 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $84,100 (38%) lower, and is roughly $39,000 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Watertown is $806 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $46 (5%) lower. It is roughly $256 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
