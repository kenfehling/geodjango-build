Home prices

The median home in Canandaigua is valued at $164,400. It is about $34,580 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $81,300 (97%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Canandaigua the median monthly rent is $838. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $76 (9%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $224 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
