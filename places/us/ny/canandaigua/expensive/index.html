Compared to the surrounding areas, Canandaigua is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Canandaigua is $164,400. This is about $34,580 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $53,100 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $81,300 (97%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Canandaigua the median rent is $838 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $76 (9%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is about $10 (1%) lower.
