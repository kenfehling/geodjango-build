Home prices

The median home in Canandaigua is valued at $164,400. It is roughly $34,580 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $81,300 (97%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Canandaigua the median rent is $838 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $76 (9%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and it is about $10 (1%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
