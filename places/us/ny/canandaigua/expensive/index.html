In comparison to the surrounding areas, Canandaigua is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Canandaigua costs $164,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $34,580 (26%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $81,300 (97%) higher.

Rents

In Canandaigua the median monthly rent is $838. It is approximately $76 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $10 (1%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
