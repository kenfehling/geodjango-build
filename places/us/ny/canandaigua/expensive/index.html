In comparison to other places in this area, Canandaigua is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home in Canandaigua is valued at $164,400. It is about $34,580 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $81,300 (97%) higher.

Rents

In Canandaigua the median monthly rent is $838. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $76 (9%) higher. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $10 (1%) lower.
