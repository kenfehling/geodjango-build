Home prices

The median home in Chateaugay is valued at $83,900. This is roughly $4,200 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $133,600 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington it is about $200,600 (70%) lower.

Rents

In Chateaugay the median rent is $672 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $80 (10%) lower.It is about $390 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $541 (44%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
