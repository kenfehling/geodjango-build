Home prices

The median home in East Farmingdale is valued at $439,900. This is roughly $44,540 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $222,400 (102%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $166,100 (27%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In East Farmingdale the median monthly rent is $1,558. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $37 (2%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $496 (46%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $115 (7%) higher.
