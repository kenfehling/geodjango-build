In comparison to the surrounding areas, Jamestown is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Jamestown is $63,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $26,560 (29%) lower.It is roughly $154,300 (70%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $26,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Jamestown the median rent is $616 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $67 (9%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $446 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is roughly $160 (20%) lower.
