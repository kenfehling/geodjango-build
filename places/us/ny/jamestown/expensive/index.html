Compared to other places in this area, Jamestown is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Jamestown is $63,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $26,560 (29%) lower. It is roughly $154,300 (70%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $26,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Jamestown's median rent is $616 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $67 (9%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $446 (41%) lower, and is about $160 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
