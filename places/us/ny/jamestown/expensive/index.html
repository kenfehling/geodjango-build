Compared to the surrounding areas, Jamestown is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

Jamestown's median home value is $63,200. It is approximately $26,560 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $154,300 (70%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $26,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

Jamestown's median monthly rent is $616. This is approximately $67 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $446 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is roughly $160 (20%) lower.
