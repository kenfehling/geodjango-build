Compared to the surrounding areas, Jamestown is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jamestown costs $63,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $26,560 (29%) lower. It is roughly $154,300 (70%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $26,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Jamestown is $616 per month. This is about $67 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $446 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $160 (20%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
