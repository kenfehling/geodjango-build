Compared to other places in this area, Bergen is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Bergen's median home cost is $114,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $7,940 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $103,400 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $24,300 (27%) higher.

Rents

Bergen's median rent is $681 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $172 (20%) lower.It is about $381 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $95 (12%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
