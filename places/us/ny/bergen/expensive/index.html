Compared to nearby areas, Bergen is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Bergen is $114,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $7,940 (6%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $103,400 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is roughly $24,300 (27%) higher.

Rents

Bergen's median monthly rent is $681. It is about $172 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $95 (12%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
