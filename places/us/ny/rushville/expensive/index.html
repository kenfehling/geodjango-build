Home prices

The median home cost in Rushville is $105,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $18,720 (15%) lower. It is roughly $111,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $22,800 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Rushville the median rent is $769 per month. This is about $17 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $293 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $79 (9%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
