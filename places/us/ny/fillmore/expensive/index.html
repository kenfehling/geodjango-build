Home prices

Fillmore's median home price is $88,400. This is roughly $7,800 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $129,100 (59%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $1,400 (1%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Fillmore the median monthly rent is $760. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $160 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $302 (28%) lower, and is approximately $16 (2%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
