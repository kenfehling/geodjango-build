In comparison to other places in this area, Caledonia is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Caledonia is valued at $115,900. This is approximately $26,840 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $101,600 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $26,100 (29%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Caledonia's median rent is $705 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $112 (13%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $357 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $71 (9%) lower.
