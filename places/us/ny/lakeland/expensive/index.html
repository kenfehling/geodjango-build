Home prices

Lakeland's median home price is $135,800. It is approximately $19,680 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $81,700 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $52,700 (63%) higher.

Rents

Lakeland's median rent is $922 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $14 (1%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $140 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is about $74 (8%) higher.
