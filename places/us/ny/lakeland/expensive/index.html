Home prices

The median home in Lakeland costs $135,800. It is approximately $19,680 (16%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $81,700 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $52,700 (63%) higher.

Rents

In Lakeland the median rent is $922 per month. This is about $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $140 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $74 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
