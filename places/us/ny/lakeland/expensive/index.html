In comparison to other places in this area, Lakeland is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lakeland is $135,800. It is approximately $19,680 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $81,700 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $52,700 (63%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakeland is $922. This is about $14 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $140 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $74 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
