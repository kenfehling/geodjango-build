Compared to other places in this area, West Elmira is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Elmira costs $132,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $29,160 (28%) higher. It is approximately $84,900 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $49,500 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in West Elmira is $1,024 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $141 (16%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $38 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $176 (20%) higher.
