Home prices

The median home in West Elmira is valued at $132,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $29,160 (28%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $84,900 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $49,500 (59%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Elmira is $1,024. This is about $141 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $38 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $176 (20%) higher.
