Home prices

The median home price in Liverpool is $136,800. It is about $29,060 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $80,700 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $42,400 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Liverpool is $910. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $17 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $152 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $96 (11%) higher.
