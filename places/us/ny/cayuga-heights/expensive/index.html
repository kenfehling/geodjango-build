Compared to its surrounding areas, Cayuga Heights is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home in Cayuga Heights is valued at $413,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $141,560 (52%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $195,500 (89%) higher, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $329,900 (396%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cayuga Heights is $1,185. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $68 (5%) lower.It is roughly $123 (11%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $337 (39%) higher.
