Rents

In Cayuga Heights the median rent is $1,185 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $68 (5%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $123 (11%) higher, and is approximately $337 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
