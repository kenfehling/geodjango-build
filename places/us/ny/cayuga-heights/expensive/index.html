Home prices

The value of the median home in Cayuga Heights is $413,000. This is about $141,560 (52%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $195,500 (89%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $329,900 (396%) higher.

Rents

Cayuga Heights's median rent is $1,185 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $68 (5%) lower. It is approximately $123 (11%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $337 (39%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.
