Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Island Park is $1,689. This is about $418 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $627 (59%) higher, and it is roughly $246 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.
