Rents

Oriskany Falls's median monthly rent is $736. It is roughly $94 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $326 (30%) lower, and is approximately $78 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
