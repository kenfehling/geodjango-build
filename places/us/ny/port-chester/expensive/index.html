Home prices

The median home in Port Chester is valued at $446,900. This is roughly $348,680 (43%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $229,400 (105%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $159,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Port Chester's median rent is $1,598 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $298 (15%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $536 (50%) higher, and is approximately $155 (10%) higher compared to the city of New York.
