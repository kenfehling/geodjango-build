Home prices

The median home in Cuylerville costs $96,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $45,240 (32%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $121,400 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $6,300 (7%) higher.

Rents

Cuylerville's median rent is $875 a month. This is roughly $12 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $187 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $99 (12%) higher.
