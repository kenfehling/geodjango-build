Home prices

The median home in Cuylerville is valued at $96,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $45,240 (32%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $121,400 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $6,300 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Cuylerville the median monthly rent is $875. It is about $12 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $187 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $99 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
