In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cuylerville is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Cuylerville is valued at $96,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $45,240 (32%) lower.It is about $121,400 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $6,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Cuylerville's median rent is $875 per month. It is approximately $12 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $187 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $99 (12%) higher.
