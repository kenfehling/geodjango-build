Compared to its surrounding areas, Hastings-on-Hudson is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Hastings-on-Hudson's median home price is $664,700. It is about $172,280 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $447,200 (205%) higher, and it is roughly $58,700 (9%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Hastings-on-Hudson is $1,902 a month. This is about $366 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $840 (79%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $459 (31%) higher.
