Home prices

Hamburg's median home cost is $170,100. It is approximately $6,260 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $47,400 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $80,300 (89%) higher.

Rents

Hamburg's median rent is $896 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $86 (10%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $166 (15%) lower, and it is about $120 (15%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
