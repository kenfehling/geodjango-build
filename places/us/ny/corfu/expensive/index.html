Home prices

The median home in Corfu is valued at $118,500. It is roughly $33,640 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $99,000 (45%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $28,700 (31%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Corfu's median monthly rent is $720. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $342 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $56 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
