Home prices

The median home in Ronkonkoma is valued at $345,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $36,780 (9%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $128,400 (59%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $260,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

Ronkonkoma's median monthly rent is $1,681. It is approximately $244 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $619 (58%) higher, and is approximately $238 (16%) higher compared to the city of New York.
