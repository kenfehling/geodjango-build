Compared to its nearby areas, Ronkonkoma is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ronkonkoma is $345,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $36,780 (9%) lower.It is about $128,400 (59%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $260,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Ronkonkoma the median monthly rent is $1,681. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $244 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $619 (58%) higher, and is about $238 (16%) higher compared to the city of New York.
