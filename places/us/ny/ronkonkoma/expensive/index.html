Home prices

The median home in Ronkonkoma is valued at $345,900. This is roughly $36,780 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $128,400 (59%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $260,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

Ronkonkoma's median monthly rent is $1,681. This is approximately $244 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $619 (58%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $238 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
