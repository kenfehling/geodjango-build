Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Ronkonkoma is $1,681 a month. It is roughly $244 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $619 (58%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $238 (16%) higher.
