Compared to its surrounding areas, Pulaski is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pulaski is valued at $124,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $27,240 (27%) higher. It is about $92,600 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $30,500 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Pulaski the median monthly rent is $699. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $119 (14%) lower. It is roughly $363 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $115 (14%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
