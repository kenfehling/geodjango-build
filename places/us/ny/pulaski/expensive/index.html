In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pulaski is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home price in Pulaski is $124,900. This is about $27,240 (27%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $92,600 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $30,500 (32%) higher.

Rents

Pulaski's median rent is $699 per month. This is approximately $119 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $363 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $115 (14%) lower.
