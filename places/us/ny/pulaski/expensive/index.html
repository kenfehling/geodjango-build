Home prices

Pulaski's median home cost is $124,900. This is roughly $27,240 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $92,600 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $30,500 (32%) higher.

Rents

Pulaski's median monthly rent is $699. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $119 (14%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $363 (34%) lower, and is approximately $115 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
