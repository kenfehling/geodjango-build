Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pulaski is $124,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $27,240 (27%) higher. It is roughly $92,600 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $30,500 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Pulaski's median rent is $699 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $119 (14%) lower. It is approximately $363 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $115 (14%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
