Compared to nearby areas, Madison is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Madison costs $100,500. This is approximately $28,360 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $117,000 (53%) lower, and it is about $6,100 (6%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Madison's median monthly rent is $888. This is approximately $99 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $174 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $74 (9%) higher.
