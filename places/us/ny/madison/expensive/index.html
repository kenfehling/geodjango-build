Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $100,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $28,360 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $117,000 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $6,100 (6%) higher.

Rents

In Madison the median monthly rent is $888. It is roughly $99 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $174 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $74 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
