In comparison to its surrounding areas, Madison is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $100,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $28,360 (22%) lower.It is about $117,000 (53%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $6,100 (6%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Madison is $888 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $99 (12%) higher.It is about $174 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $74 (9%) higher.
