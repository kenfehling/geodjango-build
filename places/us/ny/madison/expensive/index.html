Compared to the surrounding areas, Madison is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $100,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $28,360 (22%) lower.It is roughly $117,000 (53%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $6,100 (6%) higher.

Rents

Madison's median rent is $888 per month. This is roughly $99 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $174 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $74 (9%) higher.
