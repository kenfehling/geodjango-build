Home prices

The median home in Elmira costs $74,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $40,560 (35%) lower.It is approximately $143,000 (65%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $8,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

Elmira's median monthly rent is $729. It is roughly $213 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $333 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $119 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
