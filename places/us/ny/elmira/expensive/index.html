In comparison to its surrounding areas, Elmira is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Elmira is valued at $74,500. It is roughly $40,560 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $143,000 (65%) lower, and it is about $8,600 (10%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Elmira is $729 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $213 (22%) lower. It is approximately $333 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $119 (14%) lower.
