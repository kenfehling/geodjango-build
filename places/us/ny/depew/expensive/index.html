Home prices

The median home price in Depew is $120,200. It is roughly $21,800 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $97,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $30,400 (33%) higher.

Rents

Depew's median rent is $847 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $129 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $215 (20%) lower, and is approximately $71 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
