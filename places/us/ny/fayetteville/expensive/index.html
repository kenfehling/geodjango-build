Home prices

Fayetteville's median home cost is $181,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $45,000 (33%) higher. It is roughly $36,500 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $86,600 (91%) higher.

Rents

In Fayetteville the median rent is $1,088 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $296 (37%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $26 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $274 (33%) higher.
