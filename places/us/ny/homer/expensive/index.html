Home prices

Homer's median home cost is $136,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $20,800 (17%) higher.It is roughly $80,600 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $42,500 (45%) higher.

Rents

Homer's median monthly rent is $766. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $29 (3%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $296 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $48 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
