Home prices

The median home value in Homer is $136,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $20,800 (17%) higher. It is approximately $80,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $42,500 (45%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Homer the median monthly rent is $766. It is about $29 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $296 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $48 (5%) lower.
