Home prices

The median home in Baldwinsville is valued at $139,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $25,820 (22%) higher. It is approximately $77,800 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $56,600 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Baldwinsville is $838 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $96 (10%) lower. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
