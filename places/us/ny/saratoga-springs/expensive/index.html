Home prices

The median home in Saratoga Springs costs $365,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $178,020 (94%) higher.It is approximately $148,400 (68%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albany is approximately $186,800 (104%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Saratoga Springs is $1,293 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $279 (27%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $231 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Albany is approximately $324 (33%) higher.
