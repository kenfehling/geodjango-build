Home prices

The price of the median home in Northwest Ithaca is $245,900. It is approximately $58,960 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $28,400 (13%) higher, and is roughly $162,800 (195%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Northwest Ithaca the median rent is $1,056 per month. It is about $223 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and is approximately $208 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
