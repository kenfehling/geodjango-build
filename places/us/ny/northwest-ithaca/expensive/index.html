Home prices

The value of the median home in Northwest Ithaca is $245,900. It is about $58,960 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $28,400 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $162,800 (195%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Northwest Ithaca's median rent is $1,056 per month. It is approximately $223 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is roughly $208 (24%) higher.
