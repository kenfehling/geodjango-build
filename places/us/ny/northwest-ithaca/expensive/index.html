In comparison to its surrounding areas, Northwest Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Northwest Ithaca is valued at $245,900. This is approximately $58,960 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $28,400 (13%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $162,800 (195%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Northwest Ithaca is $1,056 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $223 (17%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and it is approximately $208 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
