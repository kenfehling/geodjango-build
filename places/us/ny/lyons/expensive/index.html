Home prices

The value of the median home in Lyons is $75,900. This is roughly $16,360 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $141,600 (65%) lower, and it is approximately $7,200 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Lyons's median rent is $772 per month. This is about $62 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $76 (8%) lower.
