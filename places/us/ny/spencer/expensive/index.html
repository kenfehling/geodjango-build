Home prices

The median home in Spencer is valued at $114,300. It is about $19,620 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $103,200 (47%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $19,900 (21%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Spencer is $730 a month. This is roughly $171 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $84 (10%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
