Compared to its surrounding areas, Waterloo is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Waterloo costs $87,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $19,420 (18%) lower.It is roughly $130,300 (59%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $4,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

Waterloo's median monthly rent is $767. This is about $52 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $295 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $81 (9%) lower.
