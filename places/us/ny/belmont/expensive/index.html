Home prices

The median home in Belmont costs $67,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $840 (1%) higher. It is about $149,900 (68%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $22,200 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Belmont the median monthly rent is $570. It is approximately $44 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $492 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $206 (26%) lower.
