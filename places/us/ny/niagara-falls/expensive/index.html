Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Niagara Falls is $665 a month. It is approximately $184 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $397 (37%) lower, and it is roughly $111 (14%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
