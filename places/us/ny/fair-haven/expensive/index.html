In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fair Haven is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Fair Haven's median home cost is $110,000. This is about $14,080 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $107,500 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $26,900 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fair Haven is $744. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $83 (12%) higher. It is roughly $318 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $104 (12%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
