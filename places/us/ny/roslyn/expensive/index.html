Home prices

The median home in Roslyn is valued at $450,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $542,540 (54%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $232,500 (106%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $156,000 (25%) lower.

Rents

Roslyn's median monthly rent is $2,521. This is roughly $268 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,459 (137%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $1,078 (74%) higher.
