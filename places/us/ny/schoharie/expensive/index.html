Compared to other places in this area, Schoharie is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Albany however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Schoharie is valued at $142,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $9,240 (6%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $74,700 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is roughly $36,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

Schoharie's median monthly rent is $788. This is approximately $105 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $274 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is about $181 (18%) lower.
