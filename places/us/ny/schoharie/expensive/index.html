Compared to its surrounding areas, Schoharie is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Schoharie is $142,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $9,240 (6%) higher.It is roughly $74,700 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albany is roughly $36,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

Schoharie's median monthly rent is $788. It is about $105 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $274 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $181 (18%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
