Compared to nearby areas, Schoharie is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The median home price in Schoharie is $142,800. This is approximately $9,240 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $74,700 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is approximately $36,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Schoharie the median rent is $788 per month. This is about $105 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany is roughly $181 (18%) lower.
