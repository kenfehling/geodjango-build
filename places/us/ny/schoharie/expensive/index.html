In comparison to other places in this area, Schoharie is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Schoharie is $142,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $9,240 (6%) higher.It is roughly $74,700 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $36,300 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

Schoharie's median rent is $788 per month. This is about $105 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany is roughly $181 (18%) lower.
