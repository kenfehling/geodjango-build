Home prices

Rents

In Cuba the median rent is $604 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $12 (1%) lower.It is about $458 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $172 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
