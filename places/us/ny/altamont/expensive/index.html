Home prices

The price of the median home in Altamont is $249,400. This is approximately $65,340 (35%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $31,900 (14%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $70,300 (39%) higher compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

Altamont's median rent is $815 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $218 (21%) lower. It is roughly $247 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is about $154 (15%) lower.
