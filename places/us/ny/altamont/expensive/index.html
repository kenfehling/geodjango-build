Home prices

The median home in Altamont is valued at $249,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $65,340 (35%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $31,900 (14%) higher, and it is approximately $70,300 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median rent in Altamont is $815 per month. This is roughly $218 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $247 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is approximately $154 (15%) lower.
