Compared to its surrounding areas, North Boston is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Boston costs $157,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9,340 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $60,400 (27%) lower, and is approximately $67,300 (74%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In North Boston the median rent is $845 a month. This is roughly $25 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $217 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $69 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
