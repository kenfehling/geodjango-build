In comparison to nearby areas, North Boston is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Boston costs $157,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $9,340 (5%) lower.It is about $60,400 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $67,300 (74%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Boston is $845. Compared to other places in this area this is about $25 (3%) higher.It is roughly $217 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $69 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
