Home prices

The median home in Lowville is valued at $140,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $33,460 (31%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $76,700 (35%) lower, and is roughly $46,400 (49%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Lowville the median rent is $648 a month. This is about $67 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $414 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $166 (20%) lower.
