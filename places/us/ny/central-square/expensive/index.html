Home prices

The median home in Central Square costs $136,600. It is roughly $18,660 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $80,900 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $42,200 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Central Square is $619. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $138 (18%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $195 (23%) lower.
