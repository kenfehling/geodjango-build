Compared to other places in this area, Central Square is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Central Square costs $136,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $18,660 (15%) higher. It is roughly $80,900 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $42,200 (44%) higher.

Rents

Central Square's median rent is $619 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $138 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $195 (23%) lower.
