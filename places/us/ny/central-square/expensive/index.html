Home prices

Central Square's median home price is $136,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $18,660 (15%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $80,900 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $42,200 (44%) higher.

Rents

In Central Square the median rent is $619 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $138 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $443 (41%) lower, and is about $195 (23%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
