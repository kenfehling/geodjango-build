In comparison to its surrounding areas, Central Square is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Central Square is $136,600. This is about $18,660 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $80,900 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $42,200 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Central Square is $619. Compared to other places in this area this is about $138 (18%) lower. It is roughly $443 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $195 (23%) lower.
