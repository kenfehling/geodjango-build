Home prices

The median home price in Richburg is $58,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9,700 (14%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $158,600 (72%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is roughly $30,900 (34%) lower.

Rents

Richburg's median monthly rent is $743. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $127 (20%) higher. It is about $319 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $33 (4%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
