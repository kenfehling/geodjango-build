Home prices

The median home in Middleburgh costs $138,800. It is approximately $17,800 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $78,700 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany it is about $40,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Middleburgh the median rent is $744 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $84 (10%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $318 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $225 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
