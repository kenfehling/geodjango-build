Home prices

Middleburgh's median home cost is $138,800. This is about $17,800 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $78,700 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $40,300 (22%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

Middleburgh's median rent is $744 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $84 (10%) lower. It is approximately $318 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $225 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
