Home prices

The median home cost in Middleburgh is $138,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $17,800 (14%) higher.It is roughly $78,700 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is about $40,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Middleburgh the median rent is $744 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $84 (10%) lower.It is approximately $318 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is about $225 (23%) lower.
