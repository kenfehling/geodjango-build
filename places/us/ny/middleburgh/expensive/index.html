Home prices

The median home in Middleburgh is valued at $138,800. This is roughly $17,800 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $78,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany is approximately $40,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Middleburgh the median monthly rent is $744. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $84 (10%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $318 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany is roughly $225 (23%) lower.
