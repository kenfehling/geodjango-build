Home prices

The price of the median home in Jefferson Heights is $164,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $32,340 (16%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $52,900 (24%) lower, and is approximately $239,300 (59%) lower compared to the city of Yonkers.

Rents

The median rent in Jefferson Heights is $1,082 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $199 (22%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $20 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Yonkers it is roughly $301 (21%) lower.
