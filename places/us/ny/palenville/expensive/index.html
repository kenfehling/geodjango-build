Home prices

Palenville's median home value is $195,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $8,520 (4%) lower. It is about $22,400 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $410,900 (67%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Palenville is $697 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $269 (27%) lower. It is approximately $365 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $746 (51%) lower.
