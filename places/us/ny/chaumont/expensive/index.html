Home prices

The median home in Chaumont costs $154,200. This is roughly $8,100 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $63,300 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $59,800 (63%) higher.

Rents

Chaumont's median rent is $888 per month. It is roughly $50 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $174 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $74 (9%) higher.
