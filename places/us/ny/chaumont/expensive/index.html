Home prices

Chaumont's median home value is $154,200. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $8,100 (4%) lower. It is approximately $63,300 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $59,800 (63%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Chaumont's median monthly rent is $888. This is roughly $50 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $174 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $74 (9%) higher.
