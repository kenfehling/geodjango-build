Home prices

The median home in Chaumont is valued at $154,200. This is approximately $8,100 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $63,300 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $59,800 (63%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Chaumont the median monthly rent is $888. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $50 (6%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $174 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $74 (9%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
