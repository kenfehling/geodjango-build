Home prices

The median home in Mamaroneck is valued at $623,400. It is roughly $408,560 (39%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $405,900 (186%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $17,400 (2%) higher.

Rents

Mamaroneck's median monthly rent is $1,936. This is approximately $114 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $874 (82%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $493 (34%) higher compared to the city of New York.
