Compared to other places in this area, Mamaroneck is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mamaroneck is $623,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $408,560 (39%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $405,900 (186%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $17,400 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Mamaroneck the median monthly rent is $1,936. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $114 (5%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $874 (82%) higher, and is about $493 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
