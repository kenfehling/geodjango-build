Compared to other places in this area, Mamaroneck is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mamaroneck is $623,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $408,560 (39%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $405,900 (186%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $17,400 (2%) higher.

Rents

Mamaroneck's median rent is $1,936 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $114 (5%) lower. It is approximately $874 (82%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $493 (34%) higher.
