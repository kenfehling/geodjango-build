Home prices

The median home in Endwell is valued at $134,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $32,000 (31%) higher.It is approximately $83,200 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $39,900 (42%) higher.

Rents

Endwell's median rent is $701 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $62 (8%) lower.It is roughly $361 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $113 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
