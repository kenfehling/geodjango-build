In comparison to the surrounding areas, Endwell is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Endwell costs $134,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $32,000 (31%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $83,200 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $39,900 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Endwell is $701. It is approximately $62 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $361 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $113 (13%) lower.
