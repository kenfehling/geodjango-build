Rents

The median monthly rent in Fabius is $683. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $224 (24%) lower. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $131 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
