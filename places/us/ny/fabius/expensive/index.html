Rents

Fabius's median rent is $683 per month. It is approximately $224 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $131 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
