Compared to other places in this area, Youngstown is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Youngstown is valued at $163,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $36,800 (29%) higher. It is about $54,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $73,300 (81%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Youngstown is $738 per month. It is about $68 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $324 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $38 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
