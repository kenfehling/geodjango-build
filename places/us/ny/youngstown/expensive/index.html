Home prices

The median home in Youngstown is valued at $163,100. It is approximately $36,800 (29%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $54,400 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $73,300 (81%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Youngstown's median monthly rent is $738. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $68 (8%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $324 (30%) lower, and is roughly $38 (4%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
