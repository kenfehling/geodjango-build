Home prices

The median home value in Youngstown is $163,100. This is about $36,800 (29%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $54,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is about $73,300 (81%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Youngstown is $738 per month. It is about $68 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $324 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is approximately $38 (4%) lower.
