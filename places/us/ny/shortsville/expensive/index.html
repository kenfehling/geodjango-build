Home prices

The median home in Shortsville is valued at $110,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $6,060 (5%) lower. It is roughly $107,300 (49%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $27,100 (32%) higher.

Rents

Shortsville's median monthly rent is $696. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $85 (10%) lower. It is about $366 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $152 (17%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
