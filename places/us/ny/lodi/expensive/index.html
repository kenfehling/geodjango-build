Compared to the surrounding areas, Lodi is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home in Lodi costs $66,300. This is approximately $52,020 (43%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $151,200 (69%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $16,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Lodi the median rent is $827 a month. This is approximately $82 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $235 (22%) lower, and is about $21 (2%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
