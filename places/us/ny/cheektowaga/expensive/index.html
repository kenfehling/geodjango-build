Compared to other places in this area, Cheektowaga is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cheektowaga costs $114,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $15,740 (12%) lower. It is approximately $103,400 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $24,300 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Cheektowaga's median monthly rent is $845. It is roughly $20 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $217 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $69 (8%) higher.
