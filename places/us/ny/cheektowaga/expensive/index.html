In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cheektowaga is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Cheektowaga is valued at $114,100. This is roughly $15,740 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $103,400 (47%) lower, and it is about $24,300 (27%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Cheektowaga's median rent is $845 per month. It is roughly $20 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $217 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $69 (8%) higher.
