In comparison to other places in this area, Cheektowaga is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cheektowaga is $114,100. This is about $15,740 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $103,400 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $24,300 (27%) higher.

Rents

Cheektowaga's median rent is $845 a month. This is roughly $20 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $217 (20%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is roughly $69 (8%) higher.
