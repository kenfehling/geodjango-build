Cornwall-on-Hudson's median home value is $316,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $20,500 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $99,400 (45%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $289,100 (47%) lower.

Rents
