Home prices

Rents

Cornwall-on-Hudson's median rent is $1,378 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $36 (2%) lower.It is roughly $316 (29%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $65 (4%) lower compared to the city of New York.
