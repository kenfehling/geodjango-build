Rents

The median monthly rent in Cornwall-on-Hudson is $1,378. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $36 (2%) lower.It is roughly $316 (29%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $65 (4%) lower.
