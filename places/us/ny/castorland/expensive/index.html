Home prices

The median home in Castorland is valued at $111,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $7,380 (6%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $105,900 (48%) lower, and is approximately $17,200 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Castorland the median monthly rent is $700. It is about $45 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $114 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
