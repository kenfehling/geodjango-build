Home prices

Saugerties South's median home value is $191,700. This is about $37,140 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $25,800 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $414,300 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Saugerties South the median rent is $1,205 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $104 (9%) higher.It is roughly $143 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $238 (16%) lower.
