Home prices

The median home in Schuylerville costs $157,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32,660 (17%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $59,700 (27%) lower, and it is about $21,300 (11%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

In Schuylerville the median rent is $917 a month. This is roughly $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $145 (13%) lower, and is roughly $52 (5%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
