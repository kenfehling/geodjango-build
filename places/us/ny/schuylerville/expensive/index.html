In comparison to other places in this area, Schuylerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Albany, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Schuylerville is $157,800. This is about $32,660 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $59,700 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Albany is approximately $21,300 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Schuylerville is $917. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $145 (13%) lower, and is roughly $52 (5%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
