Home prices

The cost of the median home in Schuylerville is $157,800. This is about $32,660 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $59,700 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $21,300 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

Schuylerville's median rent is $917 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10 (1%) lower.It is roughly $145 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is about $52 (5%) lower.
