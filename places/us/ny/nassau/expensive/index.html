Home prices

The median home in Nassau costs $162,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $51,520 (24%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $54,800 (25%) lower, and is about $2,600 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Hartford.

Rents

In Nassau the median monthly rent is $776. This is approximately $151 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $286 (26%) lower, and is roughly $209 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Hartford.
