Home prices

The value of the median home in Nassau is $162,700. This is about $51,520 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $54,800 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Hartford it is about $2,600 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nassau is $776. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $151 (16%) lower.It is about $286 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Hartford it is roughly $209 (21%) lower.
