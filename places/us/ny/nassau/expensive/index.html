In comparison to the surrounding areas, Nassau is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Hartford.

Home prices

The median home in Nassau costs $162,700. This is approximately $51,520 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $54,800 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Hartford is about $2,600 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Nassau is $776 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $151 (16%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $286 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Hartford it is approximately $209 (21%) lower.
