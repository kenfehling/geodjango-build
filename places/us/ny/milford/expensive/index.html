In comparison to the surrounding areas, Milford is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Milford's median home price is $114,400. This is about $39,720 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $103,100 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $20,000 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Milford the median monthly rent is $630. This is roughly $154 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $184 (22%) lower.
