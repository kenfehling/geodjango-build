Home prices

The median home in Milford costs $114,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $39,720 (25%) lower.It is approximately $103,100 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $20,000 (21%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Milford the median monthly rent is $630. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $154 (19%) lower.It is about $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $184 (22%) lower.
