Much like other places in this area, it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sandy Creek costs $102,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $2,940 (2%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $114,700 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $8,400 (8%) higher.

Rents

Sandy Creek's median monthly rent is $692. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $169 (19%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $370 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $122 (14%) lower.
