Home prices

The median home in Sandy Creek costs $102,800. This is about $2,940 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $114,700 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $8,400 (8%) higher.

Rents

Sandy Creek's median monthly rent is $692. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $169 (19%) lower. It is roughly $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $122 (14%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
