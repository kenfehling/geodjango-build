Compared to its surrounding areas, New Rochelle is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of New York, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Rochelle costs $575,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $212,380 (26%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $357,800 (164%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $30,700 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Rochelle is $1,534. Compared to nearby areas this is about $412 (21%) lower.It is roughly $472 (44%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $91 (6%) higher.
