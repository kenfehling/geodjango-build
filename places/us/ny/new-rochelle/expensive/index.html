Home prices

The price of the median home in New Rochelle is $575,300. This is approximately $212,380 (26%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $357,800 (164%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $30,700 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in New Rochelle is $1,534 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $412 (21%) lower.It is approximately $472 (44%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York is about $91 (6%) higher.
