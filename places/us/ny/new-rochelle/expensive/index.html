Home prices

New Rochelle's median home price is $575,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $212,380 (26%) lower. It is about $357,800 (164%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $30,700 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

New Rochelle's median rent is $1,534 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $412 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $472 (44%) higher, and is about $91 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
