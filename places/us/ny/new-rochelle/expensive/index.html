In comparison to other places in this area, New Rochelle is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

New Rochelle's median home price is $575,300. This is about $212,380 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $357,800 (164%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $30,700 (5%) lower.

Rents

New Rochelle's median monthly rent is $1,534. It is about $412 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $472 (44%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $91 (6%) higher.
