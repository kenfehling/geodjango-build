Compared to the surrounding areas, New City is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in New City costs $489,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $73,180 (17%) higher. It is about $272,100 (125%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $116,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In New City the median rent is $1,643 a month. It is about $45 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $581 (54%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $200 (13%) higher.
