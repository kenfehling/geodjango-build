Home prices

The median home cost in Lincoln Park is $168,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13,140 (7%) lower. It is approximately $48,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $437,300 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Lincoln Park the median monthly rent is $1,094. This is approximately $62 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $32 (3%) higher, and is about $349 (24%) lower compared to the city of New York.
