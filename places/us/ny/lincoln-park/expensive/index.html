Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Park costs $168,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,140 (7%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $48,800 (22%) lower, and it is about $437,300 (72%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Lincoln Park the median rent is $1,094 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $62 (5%) lower.It is roughly $32 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $349 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
