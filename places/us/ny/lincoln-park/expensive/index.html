Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Park costs $168,700. It is approximately $13,140 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $48,800 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of New York it is about $437,300 (72%) lower.

Rents

Lincoln Park's median rent is $1,094 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $62 (5%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $32 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $349 (24%) lower.
