Home prices

The price of the median home in Seneca Falls is $96,800. This is roughly $6,540 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $120,700 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $13,700 (16%) higher.

Rents

Seneca Falls's median rent is $769 per month. It is about $59 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $79 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
