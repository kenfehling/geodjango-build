Home prices

The median home value in Seneca Falls is $96,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $6,540 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $120,700 (55%) lower, and is roughly $13,700 (16%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Seneca Falls's median monthly rent is $769. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $59 (8%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and it is about $79 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
