Compared to other places in this area, Bloomfield is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home cost in Bloomfield is $143,400. This is about $14,200 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $74,100 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $60,300 (72%) higher.

Rents

In Bloomfield the median rent is $807 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $13 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $255 (24%) lower, and it is about $41 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
