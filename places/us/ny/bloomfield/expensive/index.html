In comparison to nearby areas, Bloomfield is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Bloomfield's median home cost is $143,400. This is about $14,200 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $74,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Rochester is about $60,300 (72%) higher.

Rents

Bloomfield's median rent is $807 a month. This is about $13 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $255 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $41 (4%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
