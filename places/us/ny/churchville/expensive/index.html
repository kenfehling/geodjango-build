Home prices

Churchville's median home value is $147,200. It is about $22,200 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $70,300 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $57,400 (63%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Churchville is $1,058 per month. This is roughly $240 (29%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $282 (36%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
