Home prices

The median home in Westport is valued at $135,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $33,820 (19%) lower.It is about $81,800 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $148,800 (52%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Westport's median rent is $744 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $106 (12%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $318 (29%) lower, and is roughly $469 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
