The median home cost in North New Hyde Park is $621,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $31,840 (4%) lower. It is about $403,500 (185%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $15,000 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North New Hyde Park is $1,948 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $31 (1%) higher. It is about $886 (83%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $505 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
