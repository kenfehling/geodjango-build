Rents

In Calcium the median monthly rent is $1,235. This is approximately $225 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $173 (16%) higher, and it is roughly $421 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
