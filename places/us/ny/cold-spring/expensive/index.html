Compared to other places in this area, Cold Spring is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Cold Spring costs $474,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $161,360 (51%) higher. It is about $257,200 (118%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $131,300 (21%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Cold Spring's median rent is $1,445 a month. It is about $39 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $383 (36%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is is very similar.
