Home prices

The median home in Kingston is valued at $177,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $2,580 (1%) lower. It is approximately $40,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $428,500 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kingston is $1,050. It is approximately $115 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $12 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $393 (27%) lower.
