Home prices

The median home in Kingston is valued at $177,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $2,580 (1%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $40,000 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $428,500 (70%) lower.

Rents

Kingston's median monthly rent is $1,050. Compared to nearby areas it is about $115 (9%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $12 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $393 (27%) lower.
