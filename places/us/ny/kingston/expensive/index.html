Home prices

Kingston's median home value is $177,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $2,580 (1%) lower. It is roughly $40,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $428,500 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kingston is $1,050. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $115 (9%) lower. It is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $393 (27%) lower.
