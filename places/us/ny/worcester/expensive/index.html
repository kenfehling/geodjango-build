Home prices

Worcester's median home value is $107,100. It is approximately $35,340 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $110,400 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $72,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Worcester is $892. This is about $149 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $170 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $77 (7%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
