Home prices

The value of the median home in Cold Brook is $90,000. This is roughly $17,960 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $127,500 (58%) lower, and is approximately $4,400 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cold Brook is $820. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $190 (30%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $242 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is is very similar.
