Home prices

The median home cost in Montauk is $885,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $120,640 (11%) lower. It is approximately $667,500 (306%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $657,900 (289%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents
