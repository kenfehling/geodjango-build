Home prices

The median home in Montauk is valued at $885,000. It is roughly $357,900 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $667,500 (306%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is about $657,900 (289%) higher.

Rents

Montauk's median rent is $2,362 a month. This is about $464 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $1,300 (122%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $1,267 (115%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.
