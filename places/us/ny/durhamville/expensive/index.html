Home prices

The price of the median home in Durhamville is $179,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $48,820 (37%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $38,200 (17%) lower, and is about $84,900 (89%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Durhamville the median monthly rent is $1,258. It is approximately $459 (57%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $196 (18%) higher, and it is roughly $444 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
