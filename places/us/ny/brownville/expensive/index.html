Home prices

Brownville's median home cost is $157,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $5,360 (3%) higher. It is approximately $60,200 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $62,900 (66%) higher.

Rents

Brownville's median rent is $819 a month. This is about $131 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $243 (22%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Syracuse.
