Home prices

The median home price in Brownville is $157,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $5,360 (3%) higher. It is roughly $60,200 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $62,900 (66%) higher.

Rents

Brownville's median rent is $819 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $131 (13%) lower. It is approximately $243 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Syracuse.
