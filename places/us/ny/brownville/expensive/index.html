Home prices

The median home in Brownville costs $157,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $5,360 (3%) higher. It is roughly $60,200 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $62,900 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Brownville is $819 per month. It is approximately $131 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $243 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Syracuse is is very similar.
