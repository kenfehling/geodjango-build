Home prices

The median home in St. Bonaventure costs $97,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $12,280 (14%) higher. It is about $120,400 (55%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $7,300 (8%) higher.

Rents

St. Bonaventure's median rent is $543 a month. This is about $63 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $519 (48%) lower, and is roughly $233 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
