Home prices

The median home in Amityville costs $409,600. This is roughly $25,180 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $192,100 (88%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $196,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Amityville is $1,651. It is roughly $110 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $589 (55%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $208 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
