Home prices

The median home in Islip costs $378,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $6,360 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $161,100 (74%) higher, and is about $227,400 (37%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Islip's median rent is $1,620 per month. It is approximately $384 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $558 (52%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $177 (12%) higher.
