Home prices

The price of the median home in Deer Park is $369,500. It is approximately $48,540 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $152,000 (69%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $236,500 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Deer Park the median rent is $1,573 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $105 (6%) lower. It is roughly $511 (48%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $130 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
