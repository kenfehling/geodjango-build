Home prices

The median home in Manhasset is valued at $1,109,400. It is approximately $111,900 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $891,900 (410%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $503,400 (83%) higher.

Rents

Manhasset's median rent is $1,413 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $440 (23%) lower. It is about $351 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
