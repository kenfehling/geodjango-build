Rents

The median monthly rent in Manhasset is $1,413. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $1,092 (43%) lower.It is approximately $351 (33%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $30 (2%) lower compared to the city of New York.
