Home prices

The median home in Eastchester is valued at $595,200. This is approximately $256,720 (30%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $377,700 (173%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $10,800 (1%) lower.

Rents

Eastchester's median monthly rent is $1,889. It is about $161 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $827 (77%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $446 (30%) higher compared to the city of New York.
