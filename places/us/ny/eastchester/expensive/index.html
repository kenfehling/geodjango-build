Home prices

Eastchester's median home price is $595,200. It is about $256,720 (30%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $377,700 (173%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $10,800 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Eastchester is $1,889 per month. This is approximately $161 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $827 (77%) higher, and is approximately $446 (30%) higher compared to the city of New York.
