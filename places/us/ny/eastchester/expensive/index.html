Compared to its surrounding areas, Eastchester is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home price in Eastchester is $595,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $256,720 (30%) lower. It is about $377,700 (173%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $10,800 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Eastchester is $1,889 per month. It is about $161 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $827 (77%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $446 (30%) higher compared to the city of New York.
