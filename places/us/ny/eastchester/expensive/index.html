In comparison to other places in this area, Eastchester is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Eastchester is $595,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $256,720 (30%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $377,700 (173%) higher, and it is about $10,800 (1%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Eastchester's median rent is $1,889 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $161 (7%) lower. It is approximately $827 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $446 (30%) higher.
