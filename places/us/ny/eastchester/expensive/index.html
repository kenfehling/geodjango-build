Home prices

The median home in Eastchester costs $595,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $256,720 (30%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $377,700 (173%) higher, and it is approximately $10,800 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Eastchester is $1,889 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $161 (7%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $827 (77%) higher, and it is about $446 (30%) higher compared to the city of New York.
