Compared to the surrounding areas, Camillus is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Camillus costs $107,200. It is roughly $32,800 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $110,300 (50%) lower, and it is about $24,100 (29%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Camillus is $742 per month. It is approximately $216 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $320 (30%) lower, and it is about $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
