Compared to nearby areas, Rochester is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Rochester is valued at $83,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $50,600 (37%) lower. It is roughly $134,400 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $6,700 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Rochester the median rent is $848 per month. This is approximately $119 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and it is roughly $72 (9%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
