In comparison to the surrounding areas, Rochester is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Rochester is $83,100. Compared to nearby areas it is about $50,600 (37%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $134,400 (61%) lower, and is roughly $6,700 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Rochester's median monthly rent is $848. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $119 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $72 (9%) higher.
