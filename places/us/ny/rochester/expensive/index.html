Home prices

The price of the median home in Rochester is $83,100. It is about $50,600 (37%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $134,400 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $6,700 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Rochester the median rent is $848 a month. It is approximately $119 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $214 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is approximately $72 (9%) higher.
