Compared to the surrounding areas, Rochester is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rochester costs $83,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $50,600 (37%) lower.It is roughly $134,400 (61%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $6,700 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester is $848. It is approximately $119 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $214 (20%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is roughly $72 (9%) higher.
