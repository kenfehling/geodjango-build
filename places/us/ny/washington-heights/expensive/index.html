Home prices

The median home in Washington Heights is valued at $227,300. This is roughly $17,720 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $9,800 (4%) higher, and it is roughly $378,700 (62%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Washington Heights is $1,611 per month. It is roughly $461 (40%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $549 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $168 (11%) higher.
