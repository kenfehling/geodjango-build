Home prices

The price of the median home in Washington Heights is $227,300. It is about $17,720 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $9,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $378,700 (62%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Washington Heights the median monthly rent is $1,611. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $461 (40%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $549 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $168 (11%) higher.
