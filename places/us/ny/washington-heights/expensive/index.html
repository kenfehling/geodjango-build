In comparison to the surrounding areas, Washington Heights is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of New York however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Washington Heights is valued at $227,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $17,720 (8%) higher.It is approximately $9,800 (4%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $378,700 (62%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Washington Heights's median rent is $1,611 per month. This is approximately $461 (40%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $549 (51%) higher, and is about $168 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
