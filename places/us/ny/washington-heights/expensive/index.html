Compared to its nearby areas, Washington Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Washington Heights costs $227,300. It is approximately $17,720 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $9,800 (4%) higher, and it is approximately $378,700 (62%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Washington Heights the median rent is $1,611 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $461 (40%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $549 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $168 (11%) higher.
