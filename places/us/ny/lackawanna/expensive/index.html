Home prices

Lackawanna's median home value is $94,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $49,300 (34%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $122,800 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $4,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Lackawanna the median rent is $656 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $153 (18%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $406 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $120 (15%) lower.
