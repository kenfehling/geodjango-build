In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lackawanna is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lackawanna costs $94,700. It is about $49,300 (34%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $122,800 (56%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is roughly $4,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

Lackawanna's median monthly rent is $656. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $153 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $406 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is about $120 (15%) lower.
