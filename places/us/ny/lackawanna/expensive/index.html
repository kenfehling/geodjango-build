In comparison to other places in this area, Lackawanna is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lackawanna is $94,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $49,300 (34%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $122,800 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $4,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lackawanna is $656. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $153 (18%) lower.It is approximately $406 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $120 (15%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
