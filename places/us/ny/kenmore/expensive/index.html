Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kenmore is $133,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $13,860 (9%) lower. It is roughly $83,900 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Buffalo is roughly $43,800 (48%) higher.

Rents

Kenmore's median monthly rent is $783. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $103 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $279 (26%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
