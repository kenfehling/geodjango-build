Compared to its nearby areas, Kenmore is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Kenmore is $133,600. It is about $13,860 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $83,900 (38%) lower, and is about $43,800 (48%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Kenmore's median rent is $783 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $103 (11%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $279 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is is very similar.
