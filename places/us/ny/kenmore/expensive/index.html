Home prices

The median home in Kenmore costs $133,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $13,860 (9%) lower.It is roughly $83,900 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $43,800 (48%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Kenmore's median rent is $783 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $103 (11%) lower.It is roughly $279 (26%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Buffalo.
