Home prices

The median home in Kenmore is valued at $133,600. It is about $13,860 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $83,900 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $43,800 (48%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Kenmore the median rent is $783 a month. This is approximately $103 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $279 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is is very similar.
