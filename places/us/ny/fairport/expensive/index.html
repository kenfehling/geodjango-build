Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairport is $183,400. It is roughly $18,360 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $34,100 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $100,300 (120%) higher.

Rents

Fairport's median rent is $998 a month. It is roughly $52 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $64 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is about $150 (17%) higher.
