Home prices

The median home cost in Fairport is $183,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $18,360 (11%) higher. It is about $34,100 (15%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $100,300 (120%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Fairport is $998 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $52 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $64 (6%) lower, and is roughly $150 (17%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.
