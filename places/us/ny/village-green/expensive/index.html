Home prices

The cost of the median home in Village Green is $108,300. It is about $14,500 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $109,200 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $25,200 (30%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Village Green the median monthly rent is $991. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $89 (9%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $71 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $143 (16%) higher.
