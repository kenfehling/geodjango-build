In comparison to other places in this area, Village Green is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Village Green costs $108,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $14,500 (11%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $109,200 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $25,200 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Village Green is $991. It is approximately $89 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $71 (6%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $143 (16%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.
