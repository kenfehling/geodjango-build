Home prices

The median home in Ontario costs $145,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $31,060 (27%) higher.It is about $72,300 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $62,100 (74%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ontario is $751. It is roughly $83 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $311 (29%) lower, and it is about $97 (11%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
