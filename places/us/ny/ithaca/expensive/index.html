Compared to its surrounding areas, Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ithaca is $239,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $54,120 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $21,600 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $156,000 (187%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ithaca is $1,203 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $27 (2%) higher. It is roughly $141 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $355 (41%) higher.
