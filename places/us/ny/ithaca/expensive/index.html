In comparison to other places in this area, Ithaca is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Rochesterhowever, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ithaca is valued at $239,100. It is roughly $54,120 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $21,600 (9%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $156,000 (187%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Ithaca is $1,203 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $27 (2%) higher. It is about $141 (13%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is roughly $355 (41%) higher.
