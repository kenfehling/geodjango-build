Compared to the surrounding areas, Ithaca is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Ithaca's median home value is $239,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $54,120 (18%) lower.It is about $21,600 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $156,000 (187%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ithaca is $1,203. This is roughly $27 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $141 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $355 (41%) higher.
