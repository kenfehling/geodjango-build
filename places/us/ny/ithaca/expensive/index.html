Home prices

The median home in Ithaca costs $239,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $54,120 (18%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $21,600 (9%) higher, and is roughly $156,000 (187%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Ithaca is $1,203 per month. This is about $27 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $141 (13%) higher, and is about $355 (41%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.
