Home prices

The median home in Centerport is valued at $607,700. This is roughly $44,340 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $390,200 (179%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Centerport's median rent is $1,933 a month. It is about $487 (33%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $871 (82%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $490 (33%) higher.
