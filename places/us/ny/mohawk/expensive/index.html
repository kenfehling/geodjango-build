Home prices

Mohawk's median home price is $79,300. This is about $2,700 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $138,200 (63%) lower, and is approximately $15,100 (15%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Mohawk's median rent is $600 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $30 (4%) lower. It is about $462 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $214 (26%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
