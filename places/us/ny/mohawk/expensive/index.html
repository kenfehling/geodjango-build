Home prices

The median home in Mohawk is valued at $79,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $2,700 (3%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $138,200 (63%) lower, and is approximately $15,100 (15%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Mohawk the median rent is $600 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $30 (4%) lower.It is roughly $462 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $214 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
