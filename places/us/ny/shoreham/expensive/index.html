Home prices

The median home in Shoreham costs $527,100. It is about $174,580 (49%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $309,600 (142%) higher, and is about $78,900 (13%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Shoreham's median rent is $2,750 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $1,029 (59%) higher. It is roughly $1,688 (158%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $1,307 (90%) higher compared to the city of New York.
