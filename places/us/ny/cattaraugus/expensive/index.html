Home prices

Rents

Cattaraugus's median rent is $464 a month. It is about $206 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $598 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $312 (40%) lower.
