Home prices

The median home in West Glens Falls is valued at $172,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $20,620 (13%) higher. It is roughly $45,500 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $7,100 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

West Glens Falls's median monthly rent is $1,219. It is roughly $331 (37%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $157 (14%) higher, and is about $250 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Albany.
