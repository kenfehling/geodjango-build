Compared to its surrounding areas, North Massapequa is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

North Massapequa's median home value is $457,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $22,800 (5%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $240,400 (110%) higher, and it is approximately $148,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in North Massapequa is $1,861 a month. This is approximately $109 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $799 (75%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $418 (28%) higher.
