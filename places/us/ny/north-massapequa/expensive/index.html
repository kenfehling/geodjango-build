In comparison to other places in this area, North Massapequa is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home value in North Massapequa is $457,900. It is approximately $22,800 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $240,400 (110%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $148,100 (24%) lower.

Rents

In North Massapequa the median monthly rent is $1,861. It is about $109 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $799 (75%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $418 (28%) higher.
