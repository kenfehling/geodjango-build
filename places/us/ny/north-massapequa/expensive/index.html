The median home in North Massapequa is valued at $457,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $22,800 (5%) higher. It is roughly $240,400 (110%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $148,100 (24%) lower.

Rents
