Compared to the surrounding areas, North Massapequa is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Massapequa is $457,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $22,800 (5%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $240,400 (110%) higher, and is roughly $148,100 (24%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

North Massapequa's median rent is $1,861 a month. It is approximately $109 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $799 (75%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $418 (28%) higher compared to the city of New York.
