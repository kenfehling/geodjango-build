Compared to the surrounding areas, East Massapequa is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in East Massapequa is $442,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $30,420 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $224,700 (103%) higher, and it is roughly $163,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

East Massapequa's median monthly rent is $2,009. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $201 (11%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $947 (89%) higher, and it is about $566 (39%) higher compared to the city of New York.
