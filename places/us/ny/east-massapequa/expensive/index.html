In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Massapequa is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in East Massapequa is $442,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $30,420 (7%) higher. It is about $224,700 (103%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $163,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In East Massapequa the median rent is $2,009 per month. This is about $201 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $947 (89%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $566 (39%) higher.
