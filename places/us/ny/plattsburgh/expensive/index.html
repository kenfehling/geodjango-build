Home prices

Plattsburgh's median home price is $148,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $5,880 (4%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $68,800 (31%) lower, and is roughly $135,800 (47%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Plattsburgh the median monthly rent is $813. It is roughly $31 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $249 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
