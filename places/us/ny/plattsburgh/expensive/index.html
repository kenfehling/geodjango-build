Home prices

Plattsburgh's median home price is $148,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $5,880 (4%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $68,800 (31%) lower, and is roughly $135,800 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Plattsburgh's median rent is $813 per month. This is approximately $31 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington is about $400 (32%) lower.
