Compared to the surrounding areas, Southampton is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of New Haven, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Southampton costs $1,226,400. This is about $375,020 (44%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $1,008,900 (463%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $1,027,400 (516%) higher compared to the city of New Haven.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Southampton is $1,870. It is about $148 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $808 (76%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New Haven it is about $674 (56%) higher.
