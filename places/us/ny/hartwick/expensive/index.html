Home prices

The median home in Hartwick costs $153,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $4,820 (3%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $64,100 (29%) lower, and is roughly $59,000 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Hartwick's median rent is $796 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $88 (12%) higher. It is approximately $266 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $18 (2%) lower.
