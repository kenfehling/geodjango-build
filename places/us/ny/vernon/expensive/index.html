In comparison to the surrounding areas, Vernon is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Vernon costs $121,400. It is roughly $20,040 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $96,100 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $27,000 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Vernon the median monthly rent is $725. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $160 (18%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $337 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $89 (10%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
