Compared to the surrounding areas, Vernon is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Vernon is valued at $121,400. It is approximately $20,040 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $96,100 (44%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $27,000 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Vernon the median rent is $725 per month. It is about $160 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $89 (10%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
