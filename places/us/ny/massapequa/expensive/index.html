Compared to its surrounding areas, Massapequa is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New Yorkhowever, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Massapequa is valued at $536,400. This is about $62,120 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $318,900 (146%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $69,600 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Massapequa the median rent is $2,268 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $298 (15%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,206 (113%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $825 (57%) higher.
