Home prices

The median home value in Massapequa is $536,400. This is roughly $62,120 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $318,900 (146%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $69,600 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Massapequa the median rent is $2,268 per month. This is about $298 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $1,206 (113%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $825 (57%) higher.
