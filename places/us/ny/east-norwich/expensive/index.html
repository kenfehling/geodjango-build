Compared to the surrounding areas, East Norwich is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of New Yorkhowever, it's close to average.

Home prices

East Norwich's median home price is $631,800. This is roughly $546,220 (46%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $414,300 (190%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $25,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In East Norwich the median rent is $2,438 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $128 (5%) higher. It is roughly $1,376 (129%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $995 (68%) higher.
