Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oceanside is $472,100. It is roughly $19,740 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $254,600 (117%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is about $133,900 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Oceanside the median rent is $1,581 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $434 (21%) lower. It is approximately $519 (48%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $138 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
