Home prices

Oceanside's median home price is $472,100. This is approximately $19,740 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $254,600 (117%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $133,900 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Oceanside the median monthly rent is $1,581. It is about $434 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $519 (48%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $138 (9%) higher.
