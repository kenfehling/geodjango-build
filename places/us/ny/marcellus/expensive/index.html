In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marcellus is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Marcellus is $168,300. It is roughly $13,700 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $49,200 (22%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $85,200 (102%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Marcellus's median rent is $830 per month. This is about $120 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $232 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $18 (2%) lower.
