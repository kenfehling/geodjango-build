Home prices

The median home in Marcellus costs $168,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $13,700 (7%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $49,200 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $85,200 (102%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Marcellus the median monthly rent is $830. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $120 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $232 (21%) lower, and is roughly $18 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
