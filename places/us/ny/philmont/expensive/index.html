Home prices

The price of the median home in Philmont is $149,000. This is about $39,340 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $68,500 (31%) lower, and is about $254,900 (63%) lower compared to the city of Yonkers.

Rents

Philmont's median monthly rent is $842. This is about $32 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $220 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $541 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Yonkers.
