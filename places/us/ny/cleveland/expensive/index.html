In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $104,200. This is roughly $7,720 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $113,300 (52%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $9,800 (10%) higher.

Rents

Cleveland's median monthly rent is $850. It is about $192 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $36 (4%) higher.
