In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland costs $104,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $7,720 (6%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $113,300 (52%) lower, and it is roughly $9,800 (10%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $850 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $192 (29%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $36 (4%) higher.
