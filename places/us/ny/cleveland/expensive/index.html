Compared to other places in this area, Cleveland is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland costs $104,200. This is about $7,720 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $113,300 (52%) lower, and it is roughly $9,800 (10%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $850 per month. It is roughly $192 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $36 (4%) higher.
