In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Cleveland is $104,200. It is roughly $7,720 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $113,300 (52%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $9,800 (10%) higher.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $850 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $192 (29%) higher.It is about $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $36 (4%) higher.
