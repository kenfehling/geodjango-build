Home prices

Clifton Springs's median home value is $113,800. It is approximately $12,260 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $103,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $30,700 (36%) higher.

Rents

Clifton Springs's median monthly rent is $630. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $154 (19%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $432 (40%) lower, and is approximately $218 (25%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
