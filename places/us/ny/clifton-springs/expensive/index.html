Home prices

The price of the median home in Clifton Springs is $113,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $12,260 (12%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $103,700 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $30,700 (36%) higher.

Rents

Clifton Springs's median monthly rent is $630. It is roughly $154 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $218 (25%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
