Home prices

The median home in Cobleskill is valued at $132,500. It is roughly $15,580 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $85,000 (39%) lower, and is roughly $46,600 (26%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median rent in Cobleskill is $784 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $48 (6%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $278 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany is approximately $185 (19%) lower.
