Home prices

The median home in Cobleskill is valued at $132,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $15,580 (13%) higher.It is about $85,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $46,600 (26%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

Cobleskill's median rent is $784 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $48 (6%) higher.It is approximately $278 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $185 (19%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
