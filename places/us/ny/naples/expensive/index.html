Home prices

The cost of the median home in Naples is $114,900. It is approximately $31,640 (38%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $102,600 (47%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $31,800 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Naples is $680. This is roughly $45 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $168 (19%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
