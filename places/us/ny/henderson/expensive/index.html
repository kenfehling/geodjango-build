Compared to the surrounding areas, Henderson is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

Henderson's median home price is $132,400. It is about $22,820 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $85,100 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $38,000 (40%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Henderson is $666. This is roughly $174 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $396 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $148 (18%) lower.
