Home prices

The price of the median home in Wilmington is $181,300. This is about $22,080 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington is roughly $103,200 (36%) lower.

Rents

Wilmington's median rent is $907 per month. It is roughly $89 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $155 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $306 (25%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
