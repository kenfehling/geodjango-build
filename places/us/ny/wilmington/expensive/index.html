Home prices

The median home price in Wilmington is $181,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $22,080 (13%) higher. It is about $36,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $103,200 (36%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wilmington is $907. It is roughly $89 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $155 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Burlington is approximately $306 (25%) lower.
