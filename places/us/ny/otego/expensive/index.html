Home prices

The median home in Otego is valued at $140,700. This is approximately $7,700 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $76,800 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $46,300 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Otego is $670. This is approximately $85 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $392 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $144 (17%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
