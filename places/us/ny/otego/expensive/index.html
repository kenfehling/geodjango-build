Home prices

The median home in Otego is valued at $140,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $7,700 (5%) higher.It is about $76,800 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $46,300 (49%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Otego the median rent is $670 per month. This is about $85 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $392 (36%) lower, and is roughly $144 (17%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
