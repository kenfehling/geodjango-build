Similar to other places in this area, it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Philadelphia is $128,600. This is roughly $1,480 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $88,900 (40%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $34,200 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Philadelphia is $679 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $354 (34%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $383 (36%) lower, and is about $135 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
