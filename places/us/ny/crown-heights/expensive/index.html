Rents

The median rent in Crown Heights is $2,007 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $599 (42%) higher. It is approximately $945 (88%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $564 (39%) higher.
