Home prices

The value of the median home in Newburgh is $159,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $78,240 (32%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $58,500 (26%) lower, and is roughly $447,000 (73%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Newburgh's median monthly rent is $1,114. This is approximately $258 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $52 (4%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $329 (22%) lower.
