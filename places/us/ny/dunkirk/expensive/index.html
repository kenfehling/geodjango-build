Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dunkirk is $663. It is about $29 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $399 (37%) lower, and it is roughly $113 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
