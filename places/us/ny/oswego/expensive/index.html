Home prices

The median home in Oswego costs $87,500. It is approximately $16,840 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $130,000 (59%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is approximately $4,400 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oswego is $774 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $58 (8%) higher. It is about $288 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $74 (8%) lower.
