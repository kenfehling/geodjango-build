Compared to the surrounding areas, Oswego is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oswego is valued at $87,500. This is roughly $16,840 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $130,000 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is approximately $4,400 (5%) higher.

Rents

Oswego's median rent is $774 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $58 (8%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $288 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $74 (8%) lower.
