Compared to its surrounding areas, Sleepy Hollow is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sleepy Hollow is $693,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $107,060 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $476,000 (218%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $87,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Sleepy Hollow the median rent is $1,552 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $228 (12%) lower. It is about $490 (46%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $109 (7%) higher.
