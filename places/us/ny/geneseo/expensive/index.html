Compared to its nearby areas, Geneseo is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Geneseo is valued at $159,900. This is roughly $36,640 (29%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $57,600 (26%) lower, and it is about $70,100 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Geneseo the median monthly rent is $861. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $201 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $85 (10%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
