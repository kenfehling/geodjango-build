Home prices

The median home in Geneseo costs $159,900. This is approximately $36,640 (29%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $57,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $70,100 (78%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Geneseo the median monthly rent is $861. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $201 (18%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $85 (10%) higher.
