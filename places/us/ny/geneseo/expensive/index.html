In comparison to its surrounding areas, Geneseo is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Geneseo's median home value is $159,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $36,640 (29%) higher.It is approximately $57,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is roughly $70,100 (78%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Geneseo is $861 per month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $201 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $85 (10%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
