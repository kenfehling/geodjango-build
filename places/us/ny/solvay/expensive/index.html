Compared to other places in this area, Solvay is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home price in Solvay is $113,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $18,160 (13%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $103,700 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $19,400 (20%) higher.

Rents

In Solvay the median rent is $792 per month. This is roughly $187 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $22 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
