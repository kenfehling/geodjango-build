Compared to its surrounding areas, Solvay is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Syracusehowever, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Solvay is $113,800. This is roughly $18,160 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $103,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $19,400 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Solvay's median monthly rent is $792. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $187 (19%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $22 (2%) lower.
