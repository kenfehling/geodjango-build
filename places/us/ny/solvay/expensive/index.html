Home prices

The median home in Solvay is valued at $113,800. It is roughly $18,160 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $103,700 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $19,400 (20%) higher.

Rents

Solvay's median rent is $792 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $187 (19%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $22 (2%) lower.
