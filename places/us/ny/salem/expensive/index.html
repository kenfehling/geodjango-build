Home prices

Salem's median home value is $116,000. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $95,520 (45%) lower.It is roughly $101,500 (46%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is roughly $40,200 (25%) lower.

Rents

Salem's median rent is $860 a month. This is roughly $134 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $202 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield it is approximately $17 (1%) lower.
