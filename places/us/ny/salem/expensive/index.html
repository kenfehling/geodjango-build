Home prices

The median home value in Salem is $116,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $95,520 (45%) lower. It is roughly $101,500 (46%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $40,200 (25%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Salem's median monthly rent is $860. It is roughly $134 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $202 (19%) lower, and it is about $17 (1%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.
