In comparison to its surrounding areas, Salem is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home value in Salem is $116,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $95,520 (45%) lower. It is roughly $101,500 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Springfield it is approximately $40,200 (25%) lower.

Rents

Salem's median monthly rent is $860. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $134 (18%) higher. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is about $17 (1%) lower.
