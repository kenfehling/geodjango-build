Compared to other places in this area, Endicott is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Syracuse, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Endicott costs $88,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $28,180 (24%) lower. It is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $6,100 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Endicott the median monthly rent is $717. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $66 (8%) lower. It is roughly $345 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $97 (11%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
