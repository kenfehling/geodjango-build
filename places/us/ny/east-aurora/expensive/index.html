Home prices

The value of the median home in East Aurora is $221,400. It is about $32,560 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $3,900 (1%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $131,600 (146%) higher.

Rents

In East Aurora the median rent is $837 a month. This is approximately $69 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $225 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $61 (7%) higher.
