Rents

The median rent in East Aurora is $837 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $69 (7%) lower.It is approximately $225 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $61 (7%) higher.
