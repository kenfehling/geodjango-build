Compared to other places in this area, Binghamton is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Binghamton is valued at $91,000. This is about $21,240 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $126,500 (58%) lower, and it is about $3,400 (3%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Binghamton the median rent is $746 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $18 (2%) lower.It is roughly $316 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $68 (8%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
