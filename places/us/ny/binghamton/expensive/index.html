Compared to other places in this area, Binghamton is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Binghamton costs $91,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $21,240 (18%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $126,500 (58%) lower, and is approximately $3,400 (3%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Binghamton's median monthly rent is $746. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $18 (2%) lower.It is about $316 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $68 (8%) lower.
