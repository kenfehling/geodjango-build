In comparison to the surrounding areas, Binghamton is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Binghamton is $91,000. It is roughly $21,240 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $126,500 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $3,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Binghamton the median rent is $746 a month. This is about $18 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $316 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $68 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
