Compared to its surrounding areas, Binghamton is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Binghamton is $91,000. This is approximately $21,240 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $126,500 (58%) lower, and is about $3,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Binghamton's median rent is $746 per month. It is approximately $18 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $316 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $68 (8%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
