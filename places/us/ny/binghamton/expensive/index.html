Compared to nearby areas, Binghamton is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Binghamton is valued at $91,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $21,240 (18%) lower.It is about $126,500 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $3,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Binghamton is $746 per month. It is about $18 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $316 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $68 (8%) lower.
