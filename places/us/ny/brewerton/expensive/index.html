Compared to its surrounding areas, Brewerton is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Brewerton costs $145,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $30,180 (26%) higher. It is roughly $72,500 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $50,600 (53%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brewerton is $877. It is about $106 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $185 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $63 (7%) higher.
