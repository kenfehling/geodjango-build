In comparison to its nearby areas, Brewerton is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brewerton is $145,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $30,180 (26%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $72,500 (33%) lower, and it is about $50,600 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Brewerton the median rent is $877 a month. This is approximately $106 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $185 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $63 (7%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
