Compared to other places in this area, Brewerton is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brewerton is $145,000. It is approximately $30,180 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $72,500 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $50,600 (53%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Brewerton's median monthly rent is $877. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $106 (13%) higher.It is approximately $185 (17%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $63 (7%) higher.
