Compared to other places in this area, Brewerton is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Brewerton is $145,000. This is approximately $30,180 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $72,500 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $50,600 (53%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Brewerton the median monthly rent is $877. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $106 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $185 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $63 (7%) higher.
