Home prices

The price of the median home in Holland is $135,200. This is approximately $6,460 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $82,300 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $45,400 (50%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Holland's median rent is $763 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $52 (7%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $13 (1%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
