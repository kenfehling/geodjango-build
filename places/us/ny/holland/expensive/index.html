In comparison to other places in this area, Holland is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Holland costs $135,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $6,460 (5%) higher. It is roughly $82,300 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $45,400 (50%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median rent in Holland is $763 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $52 (7%) higher. It is approximately $299 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $13 (1%) lower.
