Compared to the surrounding areas, Clayton is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Clayton costs $155,200. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $26,300 (20%) higher. It is roughly $62,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $60,800 (64%) higher.

Rents

Clayton's median rent is $888 per month. This is about $176 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $174 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $74 (9%) higher.
