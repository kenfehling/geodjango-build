Home prices

The median home in Cortland is valued at $109,400. It is about $12,200 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $108,100 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $15,000 (15%) higher.

Rents

Cortland's median monthly rent is $754. This is about $15 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $308 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $60 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
