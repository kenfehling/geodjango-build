Home prices

The median home price in Warrensburg is $141,600. This is approximately $60,240 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $75,900 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany it is approximately $37,500 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Warrensburg the median rent is $858 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $87 (9%) lower.It is about $204 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $111 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
