Compared to its surrounding areas, Hannibal is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home price in Hannibal is $88,900. This is about $8,240 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $128,600 (59%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $5,800 (6%) higher.

Rents

In Hannibal the median monthly rent is $742. It is about $41 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $106 (12%) lower.
