Rents

In Johnson City the median monthly rent is $860. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $119 (16%) higher.It is about $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $46 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
