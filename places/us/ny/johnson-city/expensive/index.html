Home prices

The median home in Johnson City costs $89,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $23,280 (20%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $128,200 (58%) lower, and it is about $5,100 (5%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Johnson City is $860 a month. This is about $119 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $202 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $46 (5%) higher.
