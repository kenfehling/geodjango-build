Home prices

Rents

In Johnson City the median rent is $860 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $119 (16%) higher.It is about $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $46 (5%) higher.
