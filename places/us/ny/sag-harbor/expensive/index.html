Compared to its surrounding areas, Sag Harbor is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Providence.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sag Harbor is $1,254,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $232,520 (22%) higher. It is about $1,036,600 (476%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Providence is about $1,053,800 (526%) higher.

Rents

In Sag Harbor the median monthly rent is $1,930. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $209 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $868 (81%) higher, and is roughly $936 (94%) higher in comparison to the city of Providence.
