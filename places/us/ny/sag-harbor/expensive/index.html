Compared to its nearby areas, Sag Harbor is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Providence, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sag Harbor is $1,254,100. It is roughly $232,520 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $1,036,600 (476%) higher, and is approximately $1,053,800 (526%) higher in comparison to the city of Providence.

Rents

Sag Harbor's median monthly rent is $1,930. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $209 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $868 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of Providence is roughly $936 (94%) higher.
