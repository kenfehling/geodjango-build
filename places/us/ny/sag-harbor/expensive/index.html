Compared to other places in this area, Sag Harbor is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Providence, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sag Harbor is valued at $1,254,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $232,520 (22%) higher.It is approximately $1,036,600 (476%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Providence it is roughly $1,053,800 (526%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sag Harbor is $1,930 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $209 (12%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $868 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of Providence it is roughly $936 (94%) higher.
