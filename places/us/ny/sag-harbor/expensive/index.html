In comparison to other places in this area, Sag Harbor is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Providence.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sag Harbor is $1,254,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $232,520 (22%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $1,036,600 (476%) higher, and compared to the city of Providence it is about $1,053,800 (526%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sag Harbor is $1,930. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $209 (12%) higher.It is about $868 (81%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $936 (94%) higher compared to the city of Providence.
