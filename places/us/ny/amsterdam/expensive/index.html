Compared to other places in this area, Amsterdam is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Albany, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Amsterdam is $86,100. It is approximately $32,520 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $131,400 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is about $93,000 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Amsterdam the median rent is $817 a month. It is about $52 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is approximately $152 (15%) lower.
