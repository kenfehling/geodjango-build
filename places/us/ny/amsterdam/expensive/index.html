Compared to its surrounding areas, Amsterdam is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Albany, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Amsterdam costs $86,100. This is about $32,520 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $131,400 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Albany is approximately $93,000 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Amsterdam the median monthly rent is $817. This is about $52 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $245 (23%) lower, and is approximately $152 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
