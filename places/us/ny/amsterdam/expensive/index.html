In comparison to its surrounding areas, Amsterdam is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Albany, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Amsterdam is $86,100. This is about $32,520 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $131,400 (60%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $93,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median rent in Amsterdam is $817 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $52 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $245 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $152 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
