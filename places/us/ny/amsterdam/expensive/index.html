Compared to other places in this area, Amsterdam is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The median home cost in Amsterdam is $86,100. It is approximately $32,520 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $131,400 (60%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $93,000 (51%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median rent in Amsterdam is $817 a month. It is approximately $52 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $245 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is about $152 (15%) lower.
