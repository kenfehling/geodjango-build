Compared to its surrounding areas, Ellisburg is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Ellisburg is $135,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $15,120 (12%) higher. It is roughly $82,500 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $40,600 (43%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Ellisburg is $900 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $134 (17%) higher. It is approximately $162 (15%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $86 (10%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
