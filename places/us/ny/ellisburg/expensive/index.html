Compared to the surrounding areas, Ellisburg is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ellisburg costs $135,000. It is roughly $15,120 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $82,500 (37%) lower, and is about $40,600 (43%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ellisburg is $900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $134 (17%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $162 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $86 (10%) higher.
