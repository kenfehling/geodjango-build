Home prices

The median home in North Patchogue is valued at $312,700. It is about $55,880 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $95,200 (43%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $293,300 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in North Patchogue is $1,697 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $45 (2%) higher. It is about $635 (59%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $254 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
