Home prices

The median home price in North Patchogue is $312,700. It is about $55,880 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $95,200 (43%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $293,300 (48%) lower.

Rents

In North Patchogue the median monthly rent is $1,697. It is approximately $45 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $635 (59%) higher, and is about $254 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.
