Rents

In Norwich the median rent is $707 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (12%) higher. It is approximately $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $107 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
