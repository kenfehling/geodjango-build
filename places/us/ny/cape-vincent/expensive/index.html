Home prices

The price of the median home in Cape Vincent is $133,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $31,340 (19%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $83,900 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $39,200 (41%) higher.

Rents

In Cape Vincent the median rent is $661 per month. It is approximately $116 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $401 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $153 (18%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
