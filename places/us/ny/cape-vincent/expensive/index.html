In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cape Vincent is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracusehowever, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cape Vincent costs $133,600. It is about $31,340 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $83,900 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $39,200 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Cape Vincent the median monthly rent is $661. It is approximately $116 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $401 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $153 (18%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
