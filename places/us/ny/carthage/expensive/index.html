Compared to other places in this area, Carthage is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home value in Carthage is $110,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $8,220 (6%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $107,000 (49%) lower, and is approximately $16,100 (17%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Carthage is $882 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $141 (13%) lower. It is roughly $180 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $68 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
