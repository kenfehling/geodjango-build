Home prices

The median home price in Carthage is $110,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $8,220 (6%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $107,000 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $16,100 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Carthage the median rent is $882 per month. This is about $141 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $180 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $68 (8%) higher.
