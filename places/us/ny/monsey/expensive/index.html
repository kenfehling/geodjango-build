Home prices

The median home in Monsey costs $676,400. This is about $88,180 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $458,900 (210%) higher, and is about $70,400 (11%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Monsey's median rent is $1,506 a month. This is approximately $66 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $444 (41%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $63 (4%) higher.
