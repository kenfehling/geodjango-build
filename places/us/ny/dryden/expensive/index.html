Compared to its nearby areas, Dryden is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Dryden is $176,300. It is roughly $17,400 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $41,200 (18%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $81,900 (86%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Dryden's median rent is $759 per month. This is roughly $110 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $303 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $55 (6%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
