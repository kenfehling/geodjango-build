Compared to other places in this area, Dryden is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

Dryden's median home price is $176,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $17,400 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $41,200 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $81,900 (86%) higher.

Rents

In Dryden the median monthly rent is $759. It is about $110 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $303 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $55 (6%) lower.
