Home prices

Rents

In Parish the median rent is $681 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $41 (5%) lower.It is roughly $381 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $133 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
