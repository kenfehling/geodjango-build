Home prices

The median home in Parish costs $96,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $18,500 (16%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $120,800 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $2,300 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Parish is $681. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $41 (5%) lower. It is about $381 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $133 (16%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
