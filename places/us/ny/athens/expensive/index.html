In comparison to its surrounding areas, Athens is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Yonkers, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Athens costs $191,600. It is about $21,420 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $25,900 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Yonkers it is about $212,300 (52%) lower.

Rents

Athens's median monthly rent is $869. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $50 (5%) lower. It is approximately $193 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yonkers it is about $514 (37%) lower.
