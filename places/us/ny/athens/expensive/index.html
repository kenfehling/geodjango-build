Home prices

The median home value in Athens is $191,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $21,420 (12%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $25,900 (11%) lower, and is about $212,300 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Yonkers.

Rents

In Athens the median rent is $869 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $50 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $193 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Yonkers it is roughly $514 (37%) lower.
