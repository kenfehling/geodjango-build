Compared to its surrounding areas, Laurens is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

Laurens's median home cost is $113,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $18,980 (14%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $103,600 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $19,500 (20%) higher.

Rents

Laurens's median rent is $594 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $140 (19%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $468 (44%) lower, and it is about $220 (27%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
