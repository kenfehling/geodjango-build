Home prices

Rents

Freeville's median monthly rent is $688. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $311 (31%) lower.It is roughly $374 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $126 (15%) lower.
