Home prices

The median home in Glen Park costs $135,900. This is roughly $4,880 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $81,600 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $41,500 (43%) higher.

Rents

Glen Park's median monthly rent is $975. This is roughly $157 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $87 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $161 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
