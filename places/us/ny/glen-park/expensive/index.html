Home prices

The cost of the median home in Glen Park is $135,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $4,880 (3%) lower.It is about $81,600 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $41,500 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Glen Park the median monthly rent is $975. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $157 (19%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $87 (8%) lower, and is approximately $161 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
