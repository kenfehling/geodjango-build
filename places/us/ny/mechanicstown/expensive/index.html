Home prices

The value of the median home in Mechanicstown is $209,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $19,860 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $7,700 (3%) lower, and is roughly $396,200 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Mechanicstown the median rent is $1,283 per month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $221 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $160 (11%) lower.
