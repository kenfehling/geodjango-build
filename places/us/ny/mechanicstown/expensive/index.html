Home prices

The median home in Mechanicstown is valued at $209,800. This is roughly $19,860 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is about $7,700 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $396,200 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Mechanicstown the median rent is $1,283 per month. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is about $221 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $160 (11%) lower compared to the city of New York.
