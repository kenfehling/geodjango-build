Compared to other places in this area, Mechanicstown is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Mechanicstown costs $209,800. It is approximately $19,860 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $7,700 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $396,200 (65%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mechanicstown is $1,283 per month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $221 (20%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $160 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
