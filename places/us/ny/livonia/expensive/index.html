Home prices

Livonia's median home cost is $140,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $22,640 (13%) lower.It is approximately $77,500 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $50,200 (55%) higher.

Rents

In Livonia the median monthly rent is $721. It is roughly $103 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $55 (7%) lower.
