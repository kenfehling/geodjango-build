In comparison to nearby areas, Livonia is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Livonia is $140,000. This is approximately $22,640 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $77,500 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $50,200 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Livonia's median rent is $721 per month. It is about $103 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $55 (7%) lower.
