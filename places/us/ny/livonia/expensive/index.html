Compared to other places in this area, Livonia is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Livonia's median home cost is $140,000. This is about $22,640 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $77,500 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $50,200 (55%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Livonia the median rent is $721 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $103 (12%) lower.It is approximately $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $55 (7%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
