In comparison to other places in this area, Livonia is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Livonia is valued at $140,000. It is about $22,640 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $77,500 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $50,200 (55%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Livonia the median rent is $721 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $103 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $341 (32%) lower, and it is about $55 (7%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
