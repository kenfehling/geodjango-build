Home prices

The price of the median home in Germantown is $268,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $72,420 (37%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $50,600 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $337,900 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Germantown the median monthly rent is $1,000. This is about $82 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $62 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $443 (30%) lower.
