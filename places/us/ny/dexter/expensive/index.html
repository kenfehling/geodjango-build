In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dexter is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Dexter is $125,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $43,100 (25%) lower. It is about $92,500 (42%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $30,600 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Dexter the median rent is $440 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $516 (53%) lower. It is roughly $622 (58%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $374 (45%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
