Home prices

The median home in Lake Ronkonkoma is valued at $366,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $22,720 (5%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $148,900 (68%) higher, and it is roughly $239,600 (39%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Ronkonkoma is $1,693 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $251 (12%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $631 (59%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $250 (17%) higher.
