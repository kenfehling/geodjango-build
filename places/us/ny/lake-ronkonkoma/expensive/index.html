Home prices

The value of the median home in Lake Ronkonkoma is $366,400. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $22,720 (5%) lower.It is roughly $148,900 (68%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $239,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Ronkonkoma the median monthly rent is $1,693. This is roughly $251 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $631 (59%) higher, and is roughly $250 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.
