Home prices

Lake Ronkonkoma's median home price is $366,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $22,720 (5%) lower.It is approximately $148,900 (68%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $239,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Ronkonkoma the median monthly rent is $1,693. Compared to other places in this area it is about $251 (12%) lower.It is roughly $631 (59%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $250 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.
