Home prices

The median home in Lima costs $146,200. It is approximately $6,860 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $71,300 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $56,400 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lima is $645. This is approximately $147 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $417 (39%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is about $131 (16%) lower.
