In comparison to nearby areas, Scottsville is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Scottsville costs $128,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $24,540 (16%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $89,100 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is roughly $38,600 (42%) higher.

Rents

Scottsville's median rent is $1,036 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $197 (23%) higher. It is approximately $26 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $260 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
