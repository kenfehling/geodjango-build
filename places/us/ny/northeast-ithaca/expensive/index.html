In comparison to the surrounding areas, Northeast Ithaca is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Northeast Ithaca is valued at $260,400. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $40,960 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $42,900 (19%) higher, and it is approximately $177,300 (213%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northeast Ithaca is $1,415. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $192 (15%) higher. It is roughly $353 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is roughly $567 (66%) higher.
