In comparison to nearby areas, Northeast Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Northeast Ithaca's median home price is $260,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $40,960 (13%) lower. It is about $42,900 (19%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $177,300 (213%) higher.

Rents

Northeast Ithaca's median monthly rent is $1,415. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $192 (15%) higher. It is roughly $353 (33%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $567 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
