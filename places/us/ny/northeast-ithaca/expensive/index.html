In comparison to its surrounding areas, Northeast Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Rochesterhowever, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Northeast Ithaca's median home price is $260,400. It is about $40,960 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $42,900 (19%) higher, and is roughly $177,300 (213%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northeast Ithaca is $1,415. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $192 (15%) higher. It is roughly $353 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $567 (66%) higher.
