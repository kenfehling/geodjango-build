Compared to its surrounding areas, Northeast Ithaca is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Northeast Ithaca is $260,400. It is roughly $40,960 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $42,900 (19%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $177,300 (213%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Northeast Ithaca is $1,415 per month. This is about $192 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $353 (33%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $567 (66%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.
