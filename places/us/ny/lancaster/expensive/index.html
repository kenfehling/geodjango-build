In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lancaster is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lancaster is $123,700. This is approximately $56,800 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $93,800 (43%) lower, and it is about $33,900 (37%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Lancaster's median monthly rent is $761. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $321 (29%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $301 (28%) lower, and is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
