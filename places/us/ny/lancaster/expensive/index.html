In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lancaster is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $123,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $56,800 (31%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $93,800 (43%) lower, and it is about $33,900 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Lancaster the median rent is $761 per month. This is about $321 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $301 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
