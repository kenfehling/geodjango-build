Compared to other places in this area, Lancaster is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lancaster is $123,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $56,800 (31%) lower. It is roughly $93,800 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $33,900 (37%) higher.

Rents

Lancaster's median rent is $761 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $321 (29%) lower. It is roughly $301 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
