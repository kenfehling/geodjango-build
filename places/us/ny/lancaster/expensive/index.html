Compared to other places in this area, Lancaster is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Lancaster's median home cost is $123,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $56,800 (31%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $93,800 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is about $33,900 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Lancaster the median rent is $761 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $321 (29%) lower.It is about $301 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
