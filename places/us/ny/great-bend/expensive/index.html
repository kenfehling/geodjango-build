Home prices

The cost of the median home in Great Bend is $128,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $3,500 (2%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $89,100 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $34,000 (36%) higher.

Rents

Great Bend's median monthly rent is $1,770. This is about $808 (83%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $708 (66%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $956 (117%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
