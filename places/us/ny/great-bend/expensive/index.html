Rents

Great Bend's median monthly rent is $1,770. This is approximately $808 (83%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $708 (66%) higher, and it is approximately $956 (117%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
