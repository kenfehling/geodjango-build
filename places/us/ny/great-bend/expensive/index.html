Home prices

The cost of the median home in Great Bend is $128,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $3,500 (2%) lower.It is roughly $89,100 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $34,000 (36%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Great Bend the median monthly rent is $1,770. It is roughly $808 (83%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $708 (66%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $956 (117%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
