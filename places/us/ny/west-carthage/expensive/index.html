Compared to other places in this area, West Carthage is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Carthage costs $133,700. This is about $19,620 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $83,800 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $39,300 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In West Carthage the median monthly rent is $862. It is about $165 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $200 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $48 (5%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
