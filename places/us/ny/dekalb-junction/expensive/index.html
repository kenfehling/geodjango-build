Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in DeKalb Junction is $515. This is approximately $331 (39%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $547 (51%) lower, and it is roughly $291 (36%) lower compared to the city of Watertown.
