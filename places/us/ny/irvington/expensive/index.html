Compared to other places in this area, Irvington is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of New York, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Irvington is valued at $717,100. This is about $127,320 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $499,600 (229%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $111,100 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Irvington's median rent is $1,601 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $244 (13%) lower. It is roughly $539 (50%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $158 (10%) higher.
