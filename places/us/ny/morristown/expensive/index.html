Home prices

The median home value in Morristown is $98,500. This is roughly $21,780 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $119,000 (54%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $34,900 (26%) lower compared to the city of Watertown.

Rents

In Morristown the median rent is $933 a month. This is about $146 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $129 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Watertown is about $127 (15%) higher.
