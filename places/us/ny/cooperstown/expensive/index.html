Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cooperstown is $964. It is approximately $217 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $98 (9%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $150 (18%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
