Home prices

The median home in Cooperstown costs $291,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $163,220 (127%) higher. It is roughly $74,200 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $197,300 (209%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Cooperstown the median monthly rent is $964. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $217 (29%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $98 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $150 (18%) higher.
