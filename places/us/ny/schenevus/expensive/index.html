Home prices

The median home in Schenevus costs $104,500. This is roughly $37,800 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $113,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is roughly $74,600 (41%) lower.

Rents

Schenevus's median monthly rent is $675. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $88 (11%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $387 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is about $294 (30%) lower.
