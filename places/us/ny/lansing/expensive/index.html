Compared to nearby areas, Lansing is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home in Lansing is valued at $277,400. It is approximately $21,160 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $59,900 (27%) higher, and it is approximately $194,300 (233%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Lansing's median rent is $1,421 per month. It is approximately $215 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $359 (33%) higher, and it is roughly $573 (67%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
