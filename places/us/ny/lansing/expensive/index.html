Home prices

The median home in Lansing costs $277,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $21,160 (7%) lower.It is roughly $59,900 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $194,300 (233%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lansing is $1,421. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $215 (17%) higher.It is roughly $359 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $573 (67%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.
