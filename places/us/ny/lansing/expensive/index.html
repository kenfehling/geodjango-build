Compared to its surrounding areas, Lansing is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lansing is $277,400. It is approximately $21,160 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $59,900 (27%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $194,300 (233%) higher.

Rents

In Lansing the median monthly rent is $1,421. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $215 (17%) higher. It is roughly $359 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $573 (67%) higher.
