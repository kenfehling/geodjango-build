Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marlboro is $238,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $11,100 (4%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $20,700 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $367,800 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marlboro is $893. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $743 (45%) lower. It is approximately $169 (15%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $550 (38%) lower.
