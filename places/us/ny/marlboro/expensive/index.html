Home prices

The median home value in Marlboro is $238,200. This is roughly $11,100 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $20,700 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $367,800 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marlboro is $893 per month. It is about $743 (45%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $169 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $550 (38%) lower compared to the city of New York.
