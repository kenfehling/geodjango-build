Home prices

The median home price in New Hartford is $163,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $30,960 (23%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $53,700 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $69,400 (73%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Hartford is $1,059. Compared to nearby areas this is about $252 (31%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and is about $245 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
