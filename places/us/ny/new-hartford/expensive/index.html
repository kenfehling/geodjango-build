Compared to other places in this area, New Hartford is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Hartford costs $163,800. This is about $30,960 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $53,700 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $69,400 (73%) higher.

Rents

In New Hartford the median rent is $1,059 per month. This is approximately $252 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is is very similar compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $245 (30%) higher.
