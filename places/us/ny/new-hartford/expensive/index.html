Compared to other places in this area, New Hartford is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in New Hartford is valued at $163,800. It is about $30,960 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $53,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $69,400 (73%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In New Hartford the median rent is $1,059 per month. This is about $252 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $245 (30%) higher.
