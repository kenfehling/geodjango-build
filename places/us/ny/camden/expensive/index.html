Home prices

Camden's median home value is $99,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $22,580 (18%) lower.It is roughly $117,800 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $5,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

Camden's median monthly rent is $655. This is roughly $217 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $407 (38%) lower, and is roughly $159 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
