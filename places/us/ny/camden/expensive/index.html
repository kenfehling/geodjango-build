Home prices

The median home in Camden costs $99,700. This is roughly $22,580 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $117,800 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $5,300 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Camden the median rent is $655 per month. It is roughly $217 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $407 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $159 (19%) lower.
