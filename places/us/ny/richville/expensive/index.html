Home prices

The median home value in Richville is $80,600. This is about $5,860 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $136,900 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Utica is about $15,300 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Richville the median rent is $1,038 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $358 (52%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $24 (2%) lower, and is roughly $313 (43%) higher compared to the city of Utica.
