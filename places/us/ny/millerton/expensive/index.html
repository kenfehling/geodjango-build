Home prices

The median home cost in Millerton is $242,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $72,540 (23%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $25,000 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $363,500 (59%) lower.

Rents

Millerton's median monthly rent is $955. It is about $130 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $107 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $488 (33%) lower.
