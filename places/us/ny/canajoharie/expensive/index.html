Compared to other places in this area, Canajoharie is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Albany, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Canajoharie is $88,100. This is roughly $9,960 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $129,400 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $91,000 (50%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

In Canajoharie the median monthly rent is $716. This is about $42 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $346 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $253 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
