Compared to the surrounding areas, Canajoharie is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Albany, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Canajoharie is valued at $88,100. It is roughly $9,960 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $129,400 (59%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is roughly $91,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

Canajoharie's median rent is $716 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $42 (6%) higher. It is about $346 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is roughly $253 (26%) lower.
