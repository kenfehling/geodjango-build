In comparison to other places in this area, Canajoharie is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Albany, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Canajoharie is $88,100. This is about $9,960 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $129,400 (59%) lower, and is roughly $91,000 (50%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

In Canajoharie the median rent is $716 per month. This is roughly $42 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $346 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Albany is about $253 (26%) lower.
