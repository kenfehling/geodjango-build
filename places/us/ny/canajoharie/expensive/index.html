Home prices

The value of the median home in Canajoharie is $88,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $9,960 (12%) higher. It is approximately $129,400 (59%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany is roughly $91,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Canajoharie the median monthly rent is $716. It is approximately $42 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $346 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany is roughly $253 (26%) lower.
