In comparison to other places in this area, Canajoharie is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The median home price in Canajoharie is $88,100. It is about $9,960 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $129,400 (59%) lower, and it is about $91,000 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

Canajoharie's median monthly rent is $716. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $42 (6%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $346 (32%) lower, and is about $253 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
