Home prices

The median home in Potsdam costs $113,100. It is approximately $9,940 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $104,400 (48%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $20,300 (15%) lower compared to the city of Watertown.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Potsdam is $758. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $55 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $304 (28%) lower, and is about $48 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Watertown.
