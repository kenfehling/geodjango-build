Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fort Plain is $61,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $21,600 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $155,700 (71%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany it is approximately $117,300 (65%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Plain is $790 a month. It is approximately $131 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $272 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Albany is roughly $179 (18%) lower.
