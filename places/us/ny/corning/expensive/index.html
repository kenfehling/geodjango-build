Compared to the surrounding areas, Corning is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home price in Corning is $112,800. This is approximately $25,700 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $104,700 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $29,700 (35%) higher.

Rents

Corning's median monthly rent is $821. This is roughly $126 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $241 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Rochester it is roughly $27 (3%) lower.
