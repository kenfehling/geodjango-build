In comparison to other places in this area, Corning is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Corning is $112,800. This is approximately $25,700 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $104,700 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is roughly $29,700 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Corning is $821 per month. This is approximately $126 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $27 (3%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
