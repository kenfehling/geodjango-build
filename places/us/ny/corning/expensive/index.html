Home prices

The cost of the median home in Corning is $112,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $25,700 (18%) lower.It is approximately $104,700 (48%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is approximately $29,700 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Corning is $821 a month. It is roughly $126 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $241 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $27 (3%) lower.
