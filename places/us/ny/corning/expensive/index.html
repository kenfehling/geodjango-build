Home prices

The value of the median home in Corning is $112,800. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $25,700 (18%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $104,700 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $29,700 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corning is $821. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $126 (13%) lower.It is about $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $27 (3%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
