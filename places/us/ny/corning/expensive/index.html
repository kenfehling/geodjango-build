Compared to other places in this area, Corning is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Rochesterhowever, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corning is valued at $112,800. It is about $25,700 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $104,700 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $29,700 (35%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Corning's median rent is $821 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $126 (13%) lower. It is about $241 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is roughly $27 (3%) lower.
