Home prices

The value of the median home in Washington Mills is $175,800. This is approximately $24,320 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $41,700 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $81,400 (86%) higher.

Rents

Washington Mills's median rent is $1,056 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $208 (24%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is is very similar, and is about $242 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
