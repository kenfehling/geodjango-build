Home prices

Rents

In Batavia the median monthly rent is $732. It is roughly $16 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $330 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $44 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
