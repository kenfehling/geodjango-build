Compared to other places in this area, Batavia is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Batavia's median home cost is $96,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $10,140 (9%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $120,600 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $7,100 (7%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median rent in Batavia is $732 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $16 (2%) higher. It is about $330 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $44 (5%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
