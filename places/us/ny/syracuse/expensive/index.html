Compared to its surrounding areas, Syracuse is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

The median home value in Syracuse is $94,400. This is approximately $14,867 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $123,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation.

Rents

Syracuse's median rent is $814 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $76 (8%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $248 (23%) lower.
