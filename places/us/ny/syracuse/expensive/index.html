In comparison to its nearby areas, Syracuse is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

The median home in Syracuse is valued at $94,400. It is about $14,867 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $123,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole.

Rents

The median rent in Syracuse is $814 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $76 (8%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $248 (23%) lower.
