In comparison to the surrounding areas, Syracuse is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Syracuse is $94,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $14,867 (13%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $123,100 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Syracuse the median rent is $814 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $76 (8%) lower.It is approximately $248 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole.
