Home prices

The median home price in Florida is $273,800. This is approximately $24,480 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $56,300 (25%) higher, and is approximately $332,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Florida the median rent is $1,640 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $313 (23%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $578 (54%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $197 (13%) higher.
