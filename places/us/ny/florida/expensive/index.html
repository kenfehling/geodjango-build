Home prices

The median home in Florida costs $273,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $24,480 (8%) lower. It is approximately $56,300 (25%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $332,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florida is $1,640. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $313 (23%) higher. It is roughly $578 (54%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $197 (13%) higher compared to the city of New York.
