In comparison to nearby areas, Florida is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Florida is $273,800. This is roughly $24,480 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $56,300 (25%) higher, and it is roughly $332,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Florida's median monthly rent is $1,640. It is approximately $313 (23%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $578 (54%) higher, and is roughly $197 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
