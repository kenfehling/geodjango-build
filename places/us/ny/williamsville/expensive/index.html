Home prices

The median home in Williamsville is valued at $170,800. It is about $32,480 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $46,700 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $81,000 (90%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Williamsville is $986. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $13 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $76 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $210 (27%) higher.
