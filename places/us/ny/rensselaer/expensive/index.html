In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rensselaer is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home price in Rensselaer is $143,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $65,780 (31%) lower. It is approximately $74,200 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Springfield is approximately $12,900 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rensselaer is $965. This is about $55 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $97 (9%) lower, and it is about $88 (10%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
