In comparison to its nearby areas, Rensselaer is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Springfield, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Rensselaer's median home price is $143,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $65,780 (31%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $74,200 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $12,900 (8%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Rensselaer's median rent is $965 per month. This is about $55 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $97 (9%) lower, and is about $88 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
