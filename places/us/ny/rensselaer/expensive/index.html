Compared to other places in this area, Rensselaer is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Springfield, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rensselaer is valued at $143,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $65,780 (31%) lower.It is roughly $74,200 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is approximately $12,900 (8%) lower.

Rents

Rensselaer's median monthly rent is $965. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $55 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $97 (9%) lower, and is roughly $88 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
