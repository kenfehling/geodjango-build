Home prices

The median home value in Rensselaer is $143,300. This is roughly $65,780 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $74,200 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $12,900 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Rensselaer's median rent is $965 a month. It is roughly $55 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $97 (9%) lower, and is roughly $88 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
