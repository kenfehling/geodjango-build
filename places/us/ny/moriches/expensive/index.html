Home prices

The cost of the median home in Moriches is $381,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $67,440 (21%) higher.It is about $163,700 (75%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $224,800 (37%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Moriches is $1,772 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $27 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $710 (66%) higher, and it is approximately $329 (22%) higher compared to the city of New York.
