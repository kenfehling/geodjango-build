Home prices

The median home in Moriches costs $381,200. This is roughly $67,440 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $163,700 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $224,800 (37%) lower.

Rents

Moriches's median rent is $1,772 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $27 (1%) lower. It is about $710 (66%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $329 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
