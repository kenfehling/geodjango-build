Home prices

The median home in Centereach is valued at $346,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $20,420 (5%) lower. It is about $128,800 (59%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $259,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

Centereach's median rent is $1,956 per month. This is roughly $90 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $894 (84%) higher, and is roughly $513 (35%) higher compared to the city of New York.
