Home prices

The cost of the median home in Stamford is $118,100. This is roughly $15,260 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $99,400 (45%) lower, and it is about $61,000 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stamford is $648. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $58 (9%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $414 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany is about $321 (33%) lower.
