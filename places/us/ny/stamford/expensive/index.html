Home prices

Stamford's median home value is $118,100. This is roughly $15,260 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $99,400 (45%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $61,000 (34%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

In Stamford the median rent is $648 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $58 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $414 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany is approximately $321 (33%) lower.
