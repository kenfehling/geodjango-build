Compared to the surrounding areas, Palmyra is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palmyra costs $99,700. It is roughly $8,120 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $117,800 (54%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $16,600 (19%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Palmyra the median monthly rent is $770. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $69 (9%) higher. It is about $292 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $78 (9%) lower.
