In comparison to its surrounding areas, Palmyra is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Palmyra is $99,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $8,120 (7%) lower. It is roughly $117,800 (54%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $16,600 (19%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Palmyra the median monthly rent is $770. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $69 (9%) higher. It is roughly $292 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $78 (9%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
