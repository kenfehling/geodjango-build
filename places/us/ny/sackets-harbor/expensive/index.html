Rents

Sackets Harbor's median rent is $1,293 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $535 (70%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $231 (21%) higher, and it is about $479 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
