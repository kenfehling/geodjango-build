Home prices

The median home in Lake Carmel costs $277,500. This is about $47,220 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $60,000 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $328,500 (54%) lower.

Rents
