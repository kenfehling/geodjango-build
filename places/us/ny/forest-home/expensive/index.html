Home prices

Rents

In Forest Home the median monthly rent is $1,172. It is roughly $100 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $110 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $324 (38%) higher.
