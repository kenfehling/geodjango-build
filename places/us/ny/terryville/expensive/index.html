Home prices

The value of the median home in Terryville is $369,900. It is about $36,500 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $152,400 (70%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $236,100 (38%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Terryville is $2,355. It is about $537 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,293 (121%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $912 (63%) higher.
