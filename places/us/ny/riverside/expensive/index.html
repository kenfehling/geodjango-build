Home prices

The median home in Riverside is valued at $81,400. This is about $45,540 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $136,100 (62%) lower, and it is approximately $1,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Riverside is $692 per month. It is roughly $186 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $370 (34%) lower, and is roughly $156 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
