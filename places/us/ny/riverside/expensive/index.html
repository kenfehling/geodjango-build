Compared to other places in this area, Riverside is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is close to average.

Home prices

Riverside's median home price is $81,400. It is about $45,540 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $136,100 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $1,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverside is $692. This is roughly $186 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $370 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $156 (18%) lower.
