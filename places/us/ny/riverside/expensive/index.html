Home prices

Riverside's median home cost is $81,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $45,540 (35%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $136,100 (62%) lower, and is roughly $1,700 (2%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverside is $692. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $186 (21%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $156 (18%) lower.
