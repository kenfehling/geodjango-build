Home prices

The cost of the median home in Corinth is $143,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $26,700 (15%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $74,300 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany is approximately $35,900 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Corinth the median monthly rent is $867. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $167 (16%) lower. It is roughly $195 (18%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $102 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
