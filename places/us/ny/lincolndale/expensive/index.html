Compared to its surrounding areas, Lincolndale is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lincolndale costs $397,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $21,640 (5%) higher. It is roughly $179,900 (82%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is about $208,600 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Lincolndale the median monthly rent is $1,840. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $67 (3%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $778 (73%) higher, and it is roughly $397 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
