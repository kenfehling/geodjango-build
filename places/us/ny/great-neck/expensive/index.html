In comparison to its surrounding areas, Great Neck is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of New York, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Great Neck costs $843,700. This is about $169,100 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $626,200 (287%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $237,700 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Great Neck is $2,049. Compared to other places in this area this is about $564 (21%) lower.It is about $987 (92%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $606 (41%) higher compared to the city of New York.
