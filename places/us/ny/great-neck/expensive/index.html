Home prices

The price of the median home in Great Neck is $843,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $51,500 (5%) lower. It is roughly $626,200 (287%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $237,700 (39%) higher.

Rents
