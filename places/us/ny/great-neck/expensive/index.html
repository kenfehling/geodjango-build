Home prices

Great Neck's median home value is $843,700. This is about $169,100 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $626,200 (287%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $237,700 (39%) higher.

Rents

Great Neck's median rent is $2,049 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $564 (21%) lower.It is roughly $987 (92%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $606 (41%) higher.
