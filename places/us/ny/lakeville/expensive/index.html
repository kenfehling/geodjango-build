In comparison to other places in this area, Lakeville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeville costs $123,700. This is about $43,200 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $93,800 (43%) lower, and is roughly $33,900 (37%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Lakeville the median rent is $808 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $13 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $254 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is approximately $32 (4%) higher.
