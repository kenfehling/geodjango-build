In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lakeville is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakeville is $123,700. It is approximately $43,200 (25%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $93,800 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $33,900 (37%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Lakeville the median rent is $808 per month. It is approximately $13 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $254 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $32 (4%) higher.
