Compared to nearby areas, Lakeville is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakeville is $123,700. It is about $43,200 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $93,800 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $33,900 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Lakeville the median rent is $808 per month. This is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $254 (23%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is approximately $32 (4%) higher.
