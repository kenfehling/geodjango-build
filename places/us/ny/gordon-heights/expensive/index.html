Home prices

The price of the median home in Gordon Heights is $303,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $12,820 (4%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $85,900 (39%) higher, and is roughly $302,600 (49%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Gordon Heights's median rent is $1,828 a month. This is approximately $123 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $766 (72%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $385 (26%) higher compared to the city of New York.
