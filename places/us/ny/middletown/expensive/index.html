Compared to the surrounding areas, Middletown is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Middletown costs $182,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $53,220 (22%) lower.It is about $35,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $424,000 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Middletown the median monthly rent is $1,230. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $72 (5%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $168 (15%) higher, and it is roughly $213 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
