Home prices

Rents

Middletown's median rent is $1,230 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $72 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $168 (15%) higher, and is approximately $213 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
