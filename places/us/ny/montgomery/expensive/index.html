Home prices

The median home price in Montgomery is $247,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $29,620 (13%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $30,300 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $358,200 (59%) lower.

Rents

In Montgomery the median monthly rent is $1,298. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $71 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $236 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $145 (10%) lower.
