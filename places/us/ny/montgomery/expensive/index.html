Home prices

The median home value in Montgomery is $247,800. It is roughly $29,620 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $30,300 (13%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $358,200 (59%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomery is $1,298 per month. It is about $71 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $236 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $145 (10%) lower.
