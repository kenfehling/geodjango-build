Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $224,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $133,840 (147%) higher. It is about $6,800 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $129,900 (137%) higher.

Rents

Hamilton's median rent is $836 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $35 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $226 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $22 (2%) higher.
