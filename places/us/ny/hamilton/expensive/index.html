Rents

Hamilton's median monthly rent is $836. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $35 (4%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $226 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $22 (2%) higher.
