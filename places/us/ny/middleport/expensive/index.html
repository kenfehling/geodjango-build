Home prices

The median home in Middleport is valued at $87,700. This is roughly $5,660 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $129,800 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is approximately $2,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

Middleport's median rent is $638 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $45 (6%) lower. It is about $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Buffalo is roughly $138 (17%) lower.
