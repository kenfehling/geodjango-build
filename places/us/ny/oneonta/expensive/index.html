Compared to the surrounding areas, Oneonta is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Oneonta costs $153,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $27,160 (21%) higher.It is about $64,300 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $58,800 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Oneonta's median rent is $879 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $177 (25%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $65 (7%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
