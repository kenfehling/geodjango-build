Compared to nearby areas, Oneonta is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oneonta is valued at $153,200. This is roughly $27,160 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $64,300 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $58,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oneonta is $879 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $177 (25%) higher. It is approximately $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $65 (7%) higher.
