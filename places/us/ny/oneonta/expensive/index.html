Compared to its nearby areas, Oneonta is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Oneonta's median home cost is $153,200. This is approximately $27,160 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $64,300 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $58,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

In Oneonta the median monthly rent is $879. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $177 (25%) higher.It is approximately $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $65 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
