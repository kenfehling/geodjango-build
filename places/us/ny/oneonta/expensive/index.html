In comparison to nearby areas, Oneonta is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Oneonta is $153,200. It is roughly $27,160 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $64,300 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $58,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

Oneonta's median rent is $879 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $177 (25%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $65 (7%) higher.
