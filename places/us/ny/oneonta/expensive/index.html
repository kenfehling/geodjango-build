Compared to nearby areas, Oneonta is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Oneonta is valued at $153,200. This is roughly $27,160 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $64,300 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $58,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

In Oneonta the median monthly rent is $879. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $177 (25%) higher.It is roughly $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $65 (7%) higher.
