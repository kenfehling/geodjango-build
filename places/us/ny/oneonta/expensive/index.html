In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oneonta is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Oneonta is valued at $153,200. This is about $27,160 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $64,300 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $58,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

Oneonta's median monthly rent is $879. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $177 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $65 (7%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
