The median home cost in Huntington is $615,000. It is approximately $31,400 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $397,500 (182%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $9,000 (1%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Huntington is $1,990. Compared to other places in this area this is about $437 (28%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $928 (87%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $547 (37%) higher.
