Home prices

The median home in Huntington is valued at $615,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $31,400 (5%) higher. It is approximately $397,500 (182%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $9,000 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Huntington the median rent is $1,990 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $437 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $928 (87%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $547 (37%) higher.
