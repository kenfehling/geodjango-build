Home prices

The median home price in Uniondale is $361,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $167,540 (31%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $143,500 (65%) higher, and it is roughly $245,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Uniondale is $1,933. It is approximately $150 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $871 (82%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $490 (33%) higher.
