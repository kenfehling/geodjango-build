Home prices

Hamlin's median home price is $108,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $22,660 (17%) lower. It is roughly $109,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $18,500 (20%) higher.

Rents

In Hamlin the median monthly rent is $791. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $39 (4%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $271 (25%) lower, and is about $15 (1%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
