Home prices

The cost of the median home in Herkimer is $78,000. This is about $4,260 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $139,500 (64%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $16,400 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Herkimer is $698 per month. It is roughly $87 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $364 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $116 (14%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
