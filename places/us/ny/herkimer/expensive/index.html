Compared to the surrounding areas, Herkimer is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Herkimer is valued at $78,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $4,260 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $139,500 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $16,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Herkimer the median rent is $698 a month. This is roughly $87 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $364 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $116 (14%) lower.
