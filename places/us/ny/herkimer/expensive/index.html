Home prices

The median home cost in Herkimer is $78,000. This is about $4,260 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $139,500 (64%) lower, and it is roughly $16,400 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Herkimer the median rent is $698 per month. This is approximately $87 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $364 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $116 (14%) lower.
