In comparison to its surrounding areas, Brockport is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Buffalo, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Brockport's median home price is $113,800. This is approximately $17,420 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $103,700 (47%) lower, and is about $24,000 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median rent in Brockport is $805 per month. It is about $40 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $257 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is approximately $29 (3%) higher.
