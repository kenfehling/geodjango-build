In comparison to other places in this area, Brockport is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Buffalo however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brockport is $113,800. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $17,420 (13%) lower. It is roughly $103,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $24,000 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

Brockport's median monthly rent is $805. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $40 (4%) lower. It is roughly $257 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $29 (3%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
