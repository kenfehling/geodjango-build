The cost of the median home in Chappaqua is $696,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $34,900 (5%) higher. It is approximately $479,100 (220%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $90,600 (14%) higher.

Rents
