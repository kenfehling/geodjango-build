In comparison to other places in this area, Hobart is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Albany, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hobart costs $126,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $14,320 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $90,700 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is roughly $52,300 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Hobart the median rent is $619 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $443 (41%) lower, and is about $350 (36%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
