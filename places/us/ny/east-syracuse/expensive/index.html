In comparison to its nearby areas, East Syracuse is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

East Syracuse's median home cost is $103,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $18,520 (15%) lower.It is roughly $114,300 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $8,800 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in East Syracuse is $697 per month. This is roughly $225 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $365 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $117 (14%) lower.
