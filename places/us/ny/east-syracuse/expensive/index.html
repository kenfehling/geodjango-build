Home prices

The median home in East Syracuse costs $103,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $18,520 (15%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $114,300 (52%) lower, and it is about $8,800 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in East Syracuse is $697 a month. This is approximately $225 (24%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $365 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $117 (14%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
