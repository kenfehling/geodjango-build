Compared to its nearby areas, East Syracuse is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Syracuse is valued at $103,200. This is approximately $18,520 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $114,300 (52%) lower, and it is about $8,800 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In East Syracuse the median rent is $697 a month. It is approximately $225 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $365 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $117 (14%) lower.
