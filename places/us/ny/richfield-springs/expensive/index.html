Home prices

The median home in Richfield Springs is valued at $108,100. This is about $35,260 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $109,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $13,700 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Richfield Springs the median rent is $670 per month. It is roughly $49 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $392 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $144 (17%) lower.
