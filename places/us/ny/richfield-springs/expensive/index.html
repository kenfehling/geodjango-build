The median home value in Richfield Springs is $108,100. It is roughly $35,260 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $109,400 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $13,700 (14%) higher.

Rents

Richfield Springs's median rent is $670 a month. It is roughly $49 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $392 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $144 (17%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
