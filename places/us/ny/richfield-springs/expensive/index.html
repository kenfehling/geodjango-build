Home prices

The median home in Richfield Springs costs $108,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $35,260 (24%) lower.It is about $109,400 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $13,700 (14%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Richfield Springs's median monthly rent is $670. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $49 (6%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $392 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $144 (17%) lower.
