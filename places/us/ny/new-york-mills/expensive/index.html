Home prices

The median home in New York Mills costs $119,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $4,000 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $97,800 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $25,300 (26%) higher.

Rents

In New York Mills the median rent is $753 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $111 (12%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $309 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $61 (7%) lower.
