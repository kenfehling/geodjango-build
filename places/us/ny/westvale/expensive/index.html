In comparison to the surrounding areas, Westvale is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Westvale is valued at $151,700. This is roughly $33,240 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $65,800 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $57,300 (60%) higher.

Rents

In Westvale the median rent is $1,185 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $318 (36%) higher.It is approximately $123 (11%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $371 (45%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
