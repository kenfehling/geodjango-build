Home prices

The median home value in Westvale is $151,700. This is roughly $33,240 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $65,800 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $57,300 (60%) higher.

Rents

Westvale's median rent is $1,185 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $318 (36%) higher.It is approximately $123 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $371 (45%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
