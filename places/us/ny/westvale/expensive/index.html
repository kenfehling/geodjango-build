In comparison to its nearby areas, Westvale is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Westvale is $151,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $33,240 (28%) higher. It is roughly $65,800 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $57,300 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westvale is $1,185. This is roughly $318 (36%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $123 (11%) higher, and is approximately $371 (45%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
