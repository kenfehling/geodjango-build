Home prices

Patchogue's median home price is $319,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $80,360 (20%) lower.It is about $101,800 (46%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $286,700 (47%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Patchogue is $1,525. This is about $150 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $463 (43%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $82 (5%) higher.
