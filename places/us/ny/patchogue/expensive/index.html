Home prices

The median home price in Patchogue is $319,300. It is approximately $80,360 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $101,800 (46%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $286,700 (47%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Patchogue is $1,525. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $150 (8%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $463 (43%) higher, and it is roughly $82 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
