In comparison to the surrounding areas, New York is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The value of the median home in New York is $606,000. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $181,667 (42%) higher. It is roughly $388,500 (178%) higher compared to the rest of the country.

Rents

In New York the median monthly rent is $1,443. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $118 (7%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $381 (35%) higher.
