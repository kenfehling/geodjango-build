Compared to the surrounding areas, Walton is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Walton is $90,100. This is roughly $28,180 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $127,400 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $4,300 (4%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Walton the median rent is $601 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $128 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $461 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $213 (26%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
