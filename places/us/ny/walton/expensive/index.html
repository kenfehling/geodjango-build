Home prices

The median home price in Walton is $90,100. This is roughly $28,180 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $127,400 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $4,300 (4%) lower.

Rents

Walton's median rent is $601 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $128 (17%) lower. It is roughly $461 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $213 (26%) lower.
