Home prices

The median home in Hauppauge costs $486,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $84,120 (20%) higher. It is about $268,700 (123%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $119,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hauppauge is $2,219. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $566 (34%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $1,157 (108%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $776 (53%) higher.
