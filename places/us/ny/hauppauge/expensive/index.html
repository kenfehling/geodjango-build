Home prices

The value of the median home in Hauppauge is $486,200. This is roughly $84,120 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $268,700 (123%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $119,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hauppauge is $2,219 per month. It is roughly $566 (34%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,157 (108%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $776 (53%) higher.
