Home prices

The median home in Bridgehampton is valued at $1,305,000. It is about $282,200 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $1,087,500 (500%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Providence is about $1,104,700 (551%) higher.

Rents

Bridgehampton's median monthly rent is $1,284. It is approximately $632 (32%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $222 (20%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Providence is about $290 (29%) higher.
