Home prices

The median home cost in Evans Mills is $166,300. It is roughly $31,840 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $51,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $71,900 (76%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Evans Mills the median monthly rent is $708. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $395 (35%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $354 (33%) lower, and it is about $106 (13%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
