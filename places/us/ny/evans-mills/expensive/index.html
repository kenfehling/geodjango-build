Home prices

The median home in Evans Mills is valued at $166,300. This is roughly $31,840 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $51,200 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $71,900 (76%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Evans Mills is $708. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $395 (35%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $354 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $106 (13%) lower.
