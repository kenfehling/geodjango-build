Home prices

The median home in Evans Mills is valued at $166,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $31,840 (23%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $51,200 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $71,900 (76%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Evans Mills is $708 per month. This is roughly $395 (35%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $354 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $106 (13%) lower.
