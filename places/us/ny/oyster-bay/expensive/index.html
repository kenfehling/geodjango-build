Home prices

The median home in Oyster Bay costs $588,300. It is approximately $575,540 (49%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $370,800 (170%) higher, and is roughly $17,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Oyster Bay's median monthly rent is $1,972. This is approximately $235 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $910 (85%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $529 (36%) higher.
