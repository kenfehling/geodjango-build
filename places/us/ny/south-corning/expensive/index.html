In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Corning is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in South Corning is $96,500. This is approximately $45,260 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $121,000 (55%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is roughly $13,400 (16%) higher.

Rents

South Corning's median monthly rent is $1,056. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $156 (17%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and it is approximately $208 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.
