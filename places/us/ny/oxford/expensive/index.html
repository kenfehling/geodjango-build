Home prices

The median home in Oxford costs $102,700. This is about $2,180 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $114,800 (52%) lower, and is roughly $8,300 (8%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oxford is $630. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $24 (3%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $432 (40%) lower, and is about $184 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
