Home prices

The price of the median home in Newark is $94,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $4,160 (4%) lower.It is about $123,100 (56%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $11,300 (13%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newark is $788. It is about $20 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $60 (7%) lower.
