Much like its surrounding areas, it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Newark is $94,400. This is approximately $4,160 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $123,100 (56%) lower, and is approximately $11,300 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Newark's median monthly rent is $788. It is approximately $20 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $60 (7%) lower.
