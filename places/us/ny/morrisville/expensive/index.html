Compared to its nearby areas, Morrisville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Morrisville is $116,500. It is roughly $27,900 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $101,000 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $22,100 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Morrisville is $933 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $109 (13%) higher.It is approximately $129 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $119 (14%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
