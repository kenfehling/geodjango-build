Compared to its surrounding areas, Morrisville is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Morrisville costs $116,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27,900 (19%) lower. It is about $101,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $22,100 (23%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Morrisville's median rent is $933 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $109 (13%) higher. It is roughly $129 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $119 (14%) higher.
