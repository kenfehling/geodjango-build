Compared to other places in this area, Morrisville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Morrisville costs $116,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $27,900 (19%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $101,000 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $22,100 (23%) higher.

Rents

Morrisville's median monthly rent is $933. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $109 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $129 (12%) lower, and it is roughly $119 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
