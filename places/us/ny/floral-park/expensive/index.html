Home prices

The median home in Floral Park costs $593,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $93,200 (18%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $376,400 (173%) higher, and it is roughly $12,100 (1%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Floral Park the median rent is $1,733 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $164 (10%) higher. It is about $671 (63%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $290 (20%) higher.
