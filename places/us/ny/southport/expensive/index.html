Compared to its surrounding areas, Southport is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

Southport's median home price is $81,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16,260 (16%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $136,000 (62%) lower, and is roughly $1,600 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Southport is $793 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $107 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $269 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $55 (6%) lower.
