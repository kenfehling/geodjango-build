Compared to the surrounding areas, Southport is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Rochester however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Southport is $81,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16,260 (16%) lower. It is about $136,000 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $1,600 (1%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Southport the median rent is $793 per month. This is approximately $107 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $269 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is about $55 (6%) lower.
