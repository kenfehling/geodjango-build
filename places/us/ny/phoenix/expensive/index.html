Compared to other places in this area, Phoenix is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Rochester, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Phoenix is $94,000. This is approximately $30,820 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $123,500 (56%) lower, and is approximately $10,900 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Phoenix the median monthly rent is $747. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $138 (15%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $315 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $101 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
