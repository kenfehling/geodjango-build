In comparison to its surrounding areas, Phoenix is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home in Phoenix is valued at $94,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $30,820 (24%) lower.It is about $123,500 (56%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $10,900 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Phoenix is $747 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $138 (15%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $315 (29%) lower, and is about $101 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
