Compared to the surrounding areas, Phoenix is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Rochester, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Phoenix's median home cost is $94,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30,820 (24%) lower.It is about $123,500 (56%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is approximately $10,900 (13%) higher.

Rents

Phoenix's median rent is $747 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $138 (15%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $315 (29%) lower, and is approximately $101 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
