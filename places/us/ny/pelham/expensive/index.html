In comparison to nearby areas, Pelham is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Pelham is $691,800. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $132,180 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $474,300 (218%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $85,800 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pelham is $1,817. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $178 (8%) lower. It is roughly $755 (71%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $374 (25%) higher compared to the city of New York.
