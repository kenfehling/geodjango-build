Home prices

Poughkeepsie's median home price is $196,800. This is roughly $30,740 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $20,700 (9%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $409,200 (67%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Poughkeepsie is $1,113 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $517 (31%) lower.It is approximately $51 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $330 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
