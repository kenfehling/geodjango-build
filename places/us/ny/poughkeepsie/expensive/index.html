In comparison to its surrounding areas, Poughkeepsie is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Poughkeepsie is $196,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $30,940 (13%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $20,700 (9%) lower, and is approximately $409,200 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Poughkeepsie is $1,113. This is about $568 (33%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $51 (4%) higher, and it is about $330 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
