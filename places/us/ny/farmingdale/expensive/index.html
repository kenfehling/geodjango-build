Home prices

The median home in Farmingdale is valued at $402,800. This is roughly $67,280 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $185,300 (85%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $203,200 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Farmingdale the median rent is $1,577 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $198 (11%) lower.It is approximately $515 (48%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $134 (9%) higher.
