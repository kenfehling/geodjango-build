Compared to nearby areas, Farmingdale is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Farmingdale is $402,800. This is about $67,280 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $185,300 (85%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $203,200 (33%) lower.

Rents

Farmingdale's median rent is $1,577 a month. It is approximately $198 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $515 (48%) higher, and it is roughly $134 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
