Home prices

The median home in Farmingdale costs $402,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $67,280 (14%) lower. It is roughly $185,300 (85%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $203,200 (33%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Farmingdale the median monthly rent is $1,577. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $198 (11%) lower. It is about $515 (48%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $134 (9%) higher.
