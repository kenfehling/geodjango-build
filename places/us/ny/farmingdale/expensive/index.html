Compared to other places in this area, Farmingdale is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Farmingdale is $402,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $67,280 (14%) lower.It is about $185,300 (85%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $203,200 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Farmingdale is $1,577 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $198 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $515 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $134 (9%) higher.
