Home prices

The median home price in Apalachin is $133,200. It is about $24,880 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $84,300 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $38,800 (41%) higher.

Rents

Apalachin's median rent is $733 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $39 (5%) lower.It is roughly $329 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $81 (9%) lower.
