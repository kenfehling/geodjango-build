The median home price in Apalachin is $133,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $24,880 (22%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $84,300 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $38,800 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Apalachin is $733 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $39 (5%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $329 (30%) lower, and is about $81 (9%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
