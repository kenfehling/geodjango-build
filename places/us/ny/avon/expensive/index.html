Home prices

The median home price in Avon is $151,200. This is about $8,200 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $66,300 (30%) lower, and it is about $61,400 (68%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Avon the median monthly rent is $720. This is about $46 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $342 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $56 (7%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
