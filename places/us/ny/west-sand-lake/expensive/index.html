The price of the median home in West Sand Lake is $201,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $3,880 (1%) lower. It is roughly $15,700 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $36,500 (22%) higher compared to the city of Hartford.

Rents

The median rent in West Sand Lake is $838 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $100 (10%) lower. It is approximately $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Hartford it is roughly $147 (14%) lower.
