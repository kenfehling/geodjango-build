Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $102,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $62,100 (37%) lower. It is about $115,500 (53%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $18,900 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Auburn is $754. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $21 (2%) higher. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $94 (11%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
