Home prices

The median home price in Auburn is $102,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $62,100 (37%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $115,500 (53%) lower, and it is about $18,900 (22%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $754 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $21 (2%) higher. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $94 (11%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
