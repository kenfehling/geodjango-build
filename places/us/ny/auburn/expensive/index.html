Home prices

The median home in Auburn is valued at $102,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $62,100 (37%) lower.It is approximately $115,500 (53%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $18,900 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Auburn's median monthly rent is $754. It is approximately $21 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Rochester it is roughly $94 (11%) lower.
