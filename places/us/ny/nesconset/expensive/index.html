In comparison to its surrounding areas, Nesconset is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of New York, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Nesconset is $458,500. This is about $32,300 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $241,000 (110%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $147,500 (24%) lower.

Rents

Nesconset's median monthly rent is $2,282. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $483 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $1,220 (114%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $839 (58%) higher.
