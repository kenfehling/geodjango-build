Home prices

Nesconset's median home cost is $458,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $32,300 (7%) higher.It is roughly $241,000 (110%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $147,500 (24%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Nesconset is $2,282 per month. It is about $483 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $1,220 (114%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $839 (58%) higher.
