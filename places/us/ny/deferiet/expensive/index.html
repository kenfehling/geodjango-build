Rents

Deferiet's median monthly rent is $1,042. It is about $39 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $20 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $228 (28%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
