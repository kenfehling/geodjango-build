Home prices

The median home in Deferiet costs $127,100. It is roughly $17,180 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $90,400 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $32,700 (34%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Deferiet is $1,042 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $39 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $20 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $228 (28%) higher.
