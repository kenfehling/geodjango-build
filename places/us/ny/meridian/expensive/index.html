Rents

The median rent in Meridian is $692 per month. It is roughly $52 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $156 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
