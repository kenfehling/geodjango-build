In comparison to the surrounding areas, West End is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in West End costs $139,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $19,680 (16%) higher.It is about $78,500 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $44,600 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in West End is $699 a month. It is approximately $11 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $363 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $115 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
