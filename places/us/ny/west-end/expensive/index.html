In comparison to the surrounding areas, West End is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West End costs $139,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $19,680 (16%) higher.It is about $78,500 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $44,600 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in West End is $699 per month. This is about $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $363 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $115 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
