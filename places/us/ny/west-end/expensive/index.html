In comparison to other places in this area, West End is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The price of the median home in West End is $139,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $19,680 (16%) higher. It is roughly $78,500 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $44,600 (47%) higher.

Rents

In West End the median rent is $699 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $11 (1%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $363 (34%) lower, and is approximately $115 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
