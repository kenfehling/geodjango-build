Compared to other places in this area, West End is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in West End is $139,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $19,680 (16%) higher. It is about $78,500 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $44,600 (47%) higher.

Rents

West End's median rent is $699 a month. It is about $11 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $363 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is approximately $115 (14%) lower.
