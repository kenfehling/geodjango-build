Home prices

The price of the median home in North Syracuse is $114,500. This is about $9,120 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $103,000 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $20,100 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In North Syracuse the median monthly rent is $856. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $42 (5%) higher.
