Home prices

The median home cost in Holbrook is $377,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $21,520 (6%) higher. It is approximately $159,600 (73%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is about $228,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

Holbrook's median monthly rent is $1,915. This is roughly $186 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $853 (80%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $472 (32%) higher compared to the city of New York.
