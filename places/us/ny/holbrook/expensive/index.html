Home prices

The median home in Holbrook is valued at $377,100. It is roughly $21,520 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $159,600 (73%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $228,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Holbrook is $1,915 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $186 (10%) higher. It is about $853 (80%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $472 (32%) higher.
