The value of the median home in Minetto is $125,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $28,640 (29%) higher. It is roughly $92,100 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $42,300 (50%) higher.

Rents
