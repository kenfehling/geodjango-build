In comparison to its surrounding areas, Schenectady is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The median home in Schenectady is valued at $108,700. It is about $82,860 (43%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $108,800 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $70,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Schenectady is $904. Compared to nearby areas this is about $206 (18%) lower.It is roughly $158 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $65 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
