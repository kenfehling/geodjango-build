Compared to the surrounding areas, Schenectady is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Schenectady is $108,700. This is about $82,860 (43%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $108,800 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Albany is approximately $70,400 (39%) lower.

Rents

Schenectady's median monthly rent is $904. It is approximately $206 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $158 (14%) lower, and is roughly $65 (6%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
