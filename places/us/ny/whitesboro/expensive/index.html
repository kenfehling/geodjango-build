Compared to its nearby areas, Whitesboro is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Whitesboro is $93,200. It is about $23,300 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $124,300 (57%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $1,200 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Whitesboro the median rent is $693 a month. This is about $144 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $369 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $121 (14%) lower.
