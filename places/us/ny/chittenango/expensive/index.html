Home prices

The price of the median home in Chittenango is $139,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $15,720 (10%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $77,600 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $45,500 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Chittenango's median rent is $724 a month. This is about $141 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $338 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $90 (11%) lower.
