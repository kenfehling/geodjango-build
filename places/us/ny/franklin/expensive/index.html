Home prices

The median home in Franklin is valued at $152,200. This is about $31,680 (26%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $65,300 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $57,800 (61%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Franklin the median rent is $809 a month. This is roughly $93 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $253 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is is very similar.
