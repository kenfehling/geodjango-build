Compared to the surrounding areas, Franklin is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Franklin is $152,200. Compared to nearby areas it is about $31,680 (26%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $65,300 (30%) lower, and is roughly $57,800 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Franklin's median rent is $809 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $93 (12%) higher. It is about $253 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Syracuse.
