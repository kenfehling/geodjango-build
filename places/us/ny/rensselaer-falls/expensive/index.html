Home prices

The price of the median home in Rensselaer Falls is $84,500. This is roughly $7,400 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $133,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $48,900 (36%) lower compared to the city of Watertown.

Rents

In Rensselaer Falls the median monthly rent is $1,018. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $355 (53%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $44 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Watertown is approximately $212 (26%) higher.
