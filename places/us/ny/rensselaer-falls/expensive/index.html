The price of the median home in Rensselaer Falls is $84,500. This is roughly $7,400 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $133,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Watertown it is approximately $48,900 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Rensselaer Falls the median monthly rent is $1,018. It is roughly $355 (53%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $44 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Watertown is about $212 (26%) higher.
