Home prices

The median home in Rensselaer Falls is valued at $84,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $7,400 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $133,000 (61%) lower, and is approximately $48,900 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Watertown.

Rents

The median rent in Rensselaer Falls is $1,018 a month. This is about $355 (53%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $44 (4%) lower, and is roughly $212 (26%) higher compared to the city of Watertown.
