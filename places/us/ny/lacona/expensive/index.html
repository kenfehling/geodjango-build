Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Lacona is $909 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $92 (11%) higher. It is approximately $153 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $95 (11%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.
