Home prices

Rents

In Lacona the median rent is $909 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $92 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $153 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $95 (11%) higher.
