Home prices

The median home in Lacona costs $107,800. It is approximately $3,060 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $109,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $13,400 (14%) higher.

Rents

Lacona's median rent is $909 per month. It is approximately $92 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $153 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $95 (11%) higher.
