Home prices

Constantia's median home cost is $139,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $22,360 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $78,000 (35%) lower, and it is about $45,100 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Constantia's median monthly rent is $624. It is about $69 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $438 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $190 (23%) lower.
