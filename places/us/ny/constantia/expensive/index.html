Home prices

The median home in Constantia costs $139,500. This is about $22,360 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $78,000 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $45,100 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Constantia is $624 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $69 (9%) lower.It is approximately $438 (41%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $190 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
