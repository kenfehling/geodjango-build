Home prices

Clarkson's median home cost is $158,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $37,820 (31%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $58,800 (27%) lower, and is roughly $68,900 (76%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Clarkson the median monthly rent is $856. This is about $39 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $206 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $80 (10%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
