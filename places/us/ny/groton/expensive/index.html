Home prices

The median home in Groton is valued at $129,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38,900 (23%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $87,800 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $35,300 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Groton's median monthly rent is $677. It is approximately $216 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $385 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $137 (16%) lower.
