Compared to nearby areas, Groton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Groton is $129,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $38,900 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $87,800 (40%) lower, and is about $35,300 (37%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Groton's median rent is $677 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $216 (24%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $385 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $137 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
