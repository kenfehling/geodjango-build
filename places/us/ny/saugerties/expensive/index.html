The median home in Saugerties is valued at $233,300. It is about $6,600 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $15,800 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $372,700 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents
