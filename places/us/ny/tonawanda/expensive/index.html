Home prices

The median home in Tonawanda costs $139,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $7,020 (4%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $78,200 (35%) lower, and it is about $49,500 (55%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

In Tonawanda the median monthly rent is $863. It is is very similar compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $199 (18%) lower, and it is about $87 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
