Home prices

The median home value in Tonawanda is $139,300. This is roughly $7,020 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $78,200 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $49,500 (55%) higher.

Rents

Tonawanda's median monthly rent is $863. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $199 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $87 (11%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.
