Home prices

The median home in Hudson is valued at $220,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $56,460 (34%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $3,300 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Yonkers it is about $183,100 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hudson is $798 per month. It is roughly $136 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $264 (24%) lower, and it is approximately $585 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Yonkers.
