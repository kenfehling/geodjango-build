Home prices

The value of the median home in Hudson is $220,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $56,460 (34%) higher. It is roughly $3,300 (1%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $183,100 (45%) lower compared to the city of Yonkers.

Rents

The median rent in Hudson is $798 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $136 (14%) lower. It is about $264 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $585 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Yonkers.
