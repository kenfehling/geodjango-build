Home prices

The median home value in Canastota is $128,000. It is about $7,860 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $89,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $33,600 (35%) higher.

Rents

Canastota's median rent is $712 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $173 (19%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $350 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $102 (12%) lower.
