Home prices

The median home in Franklin Square is valued at $476,100. It is roughly $25,380 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $258,600 (118%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $129,900 (21%) lower.

Rents

Franklin Square's median rent is $1,761 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $321 (15%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $699 (65%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $318 (22%) higher.
