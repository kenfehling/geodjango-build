Home prices

The median home value in Franklin Square is $476,100. This is about $6,080 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $258,600 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $129,900 (21%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Franklin Square's median monthly rent is $1,761. Compared to nearby areas it is about $23 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $699 (65%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $318 (22%) higher.
