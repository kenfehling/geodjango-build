Compared to the surrounding areas, Madison Heights is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Madison Heights is valued at $124,000. This is about $112,940 (47%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $93,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $74,800 (152%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Madison Heights's median monthly rent is $967. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $323 (25%) lower. It is roughly $95 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $143 (17%) higher.
