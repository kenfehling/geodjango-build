Home prices

The median home cost in Madison Heights is $124,000. It is roughly $112,940 (47%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $93,500 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $74,800 (152%) higher.

Rents

In Madison Heights the median rent is $967 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $323 (25%) lower.It is approximately $95 (8%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $143 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
