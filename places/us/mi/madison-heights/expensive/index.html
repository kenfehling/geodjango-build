In comparison to the surrounding areas, Madison Heights is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Madison Heights is $124,000. This is approximately $112,940 (47%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $93,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $74,800 (152%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Madison Heights is $967. It is roughly $323 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $95 (8%) lower, and it is about $143 (17%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
