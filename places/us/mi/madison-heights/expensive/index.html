In comparison to its nearby areas, Madison Heights is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Madison Heights costs $124,000. It is roughly $112,940 (47%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $93,500 (42%) lower, and is about $74,800 (152%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Madison Heights the median monthly rent is $967. It is about $323 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $95 (8%) lower, and it is about $143 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
