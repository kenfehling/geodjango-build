Home prices

The median home in Madison Heights is valued at $124,000. It is roughly $112,940 (47%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $93,500 (42%) lower, and is approximately $74,800 (152%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Madison Heights is $967 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $323 (25%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $95 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $143 (17%) higher.
