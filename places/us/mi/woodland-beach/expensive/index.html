Home prices

The median home price in Woodland Beach is $123,400. It is roughly $9,080 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $94,100 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $74,200 (150%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Woodland Beach is $986 per month. It is approximately $65 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $76 (7%) lower, and it is roughly $162 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
