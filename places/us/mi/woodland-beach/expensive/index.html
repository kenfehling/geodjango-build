Home prices

The value of the median home in Woodland Beach is $123,400. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $9,080 (7%) higher.It is approximately $94,100 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $74,200 (150%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents
