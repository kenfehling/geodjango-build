Home prices

The median home in Albion costs $56,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $40,340 (41%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $160,700 (73%) lower, and it is roughly $24,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In Albion the median rent is $680 a month. This is roughly $44 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $382 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is about $45 (6%) lower.
