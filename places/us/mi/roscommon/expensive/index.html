Home prices

Rents

In Roscommon the median monthly rent is $490. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $192 (28%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $572 (53%) lower, and is roughly $244 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Saginaw.
