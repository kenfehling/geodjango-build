Home prices

The cost of the median home in Roscommon is $62,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $26,920 (30%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $155,200 (71%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Saginaw is about $22,200 (55%) higher.

Rents

In Roscommon the median rent is $490 a month. It is approximately $192 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $572 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Saginaw is about $244 (33%) lower.
