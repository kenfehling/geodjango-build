In comparison to its surrounding areas, Southfield is substantially less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Southfield is valued at $155,700. It is approximately $190,320 (55%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $61,800 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $106,500 (216%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Southfield is $1,125. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $325 (22%) lower. It is approximately $63 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $301 (36%) higher.
