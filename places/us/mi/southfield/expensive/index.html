Home prices

The median home in Southfield is valued at $155,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $190,320 (55%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $61,800 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $106,500 (216%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Southfield is $1,125. It is approximately $325 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $63 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $301 (36%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
