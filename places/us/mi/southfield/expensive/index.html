In comparison to other places in this area, Southfield is a lot less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Southfield costs $155,700. This is roughly $190,320 (55%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $61,800 (28%) lower, and is roughly $106,500 (216%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Southfield's median rent is $1,125 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $325 (22%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $63 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $301 (36%) higher.
