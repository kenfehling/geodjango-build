Home prices

The value of the median home in Lawrence is $74,300. It is approximately $11,360 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $143,200 (65%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $69,100 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Lawrence the median rent is $560 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $133 (19%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $502 (47%) lower, and is roughly $365 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
