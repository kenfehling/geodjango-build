Home prices

The median home in Lawrence is valued at $74,300. This is approximately $11,360 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $143,200 (65%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $69,100 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lawrence is $560 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $133 (19%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $502 (47%) lower, and it is about $365 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
