Home prices

The median home price in St. Joseph is $196,000. It is roughly $89,060 (83%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $21,500 (9%) lower, and is roughly $62,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In St. Joseph the median rent is $774 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $48 (5%) lower.It is roughly $288 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $338 (30%) lower.
