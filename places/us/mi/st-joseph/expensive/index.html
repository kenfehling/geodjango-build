Home prices

The median home in St. Joseph is valued at $196,000. This is about $89,060 (83%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $21,500 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $62,000 (24%) lower.

Rents

St. Joseph's median rent is $774 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $48 (5%) lower.It is approximately $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $338 (30%) lower.
