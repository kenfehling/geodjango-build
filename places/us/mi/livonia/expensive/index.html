Compared to its nearby areas, Livonia is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Livonia is valued at $191,500. It is approximately $25,060 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $26,000 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $142,300 (289%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Livonia is $1,077. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $86 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $15 (1%) higher, and it is roughly $253 (30%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
