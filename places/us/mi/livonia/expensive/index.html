In comparison to other places in this area, Livonia is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home value in Livonia is $191,500. Compared to nearby areas it is about $25,060 (11%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $26,000 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $142,300 (289%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Livonia is $1,077. It is about $86 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $15 (1%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $253 (30%) higher.
