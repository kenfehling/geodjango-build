In comparison to its nearby areas, Livonia is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Livonia is $191,500. It is about $25,060 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $26,000 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $142,300 (289%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Livonia is $1,077 per month. This is roughly $86 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $15 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $253 (30%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
