Home prices

The median home cost in Otisville is $64,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $24,660 (27%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $153,400 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $14,900 (30%) higher.

Rents

In Otisville the median monthly rent is $683. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $125 (15%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $379 (35%) lower, and it is about $141 (17%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
