Home prices

The median home price in Otisville is $64,100. This is about $24,660 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $153,400 (70%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $14,900 (30%) higher.

Rents

Otisville's median rent is $683 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $125 (15%) lower.It is roughly $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $141 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
