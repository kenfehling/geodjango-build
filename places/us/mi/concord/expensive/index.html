Compared to its surrounding areas, Concord is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Toledo.

Home prices

The median home in Concord costs $113,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $11,440 (11%) higher. It is roughly $104,300 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $32,400 (40%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Concord is $723. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $72 (11%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $339 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Toledo is is very similar.
