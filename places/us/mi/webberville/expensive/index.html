Home prices

The median home in Webberville costs $112,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $40,900 (26%) lower.It is about $105,300 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $63,000 (128%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Webberville is $727. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $145 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $335 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $97 (11%) lower.
