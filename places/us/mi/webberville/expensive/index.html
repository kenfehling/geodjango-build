Home prices

The median home in Webberville costs $112,200. This is roughly $40,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $105,300 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $63,000 (128%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Webberville the median rent is $727 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $145 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $335 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $97 (11%) lower.
