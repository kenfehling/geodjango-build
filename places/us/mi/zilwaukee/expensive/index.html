In comparison to its surrounding areas, Zilwaukee is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Zilwaukee is $72,900. It is approximately $4,360 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $144,600 (66%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $23,700 (48%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Zilwaukee is $920 a month. This is approximately $201 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $142 (13%) lower, and is roughly $96 (11%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
