Compared to its nearby areas, Zilwaukee is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Zilwaukee costs $72,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $4,360 (6%) higher.It is about $144,600 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $23,700 (48%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Zilwaukee is $920 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $201 (27%) higher.It is about $142 (13%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $96 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
