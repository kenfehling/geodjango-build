In comparison to its surrounding areas, Zilwaukee is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Zilwaukee is $72,900. It is roughly $4,360 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $144,600 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $23,700 (48%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Zilwaukee is $920. It is about $201 (27%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $142 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $96 (11%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
