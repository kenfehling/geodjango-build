In comparison to the surrounding areas, Zilwaukee is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Zilwaukee is $72,900. It is roughly $4,360 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $144,600 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $23,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Zilwaukee's median monthly rent is $920. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $201 (27%) higher.It is approximately $142 (13%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $96 (11%) higher.
