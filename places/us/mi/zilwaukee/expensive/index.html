In comparison to the surrounding areas, Zilwaukee is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Zilwaukee's median home cost is $72,900. It is roughly $4,360 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $144,600 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $23,700 (48%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Zilwaukee is $920. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $201 (27%) higher.It is about $142 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $96 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
