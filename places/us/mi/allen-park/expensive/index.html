Home prices

The median home price in Allen Park is $123,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $39,920 (47%) higher. It is approximately $93,700 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $74,600 (151%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Allen Park is $980. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $96 (10%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $82 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $156 (18%) higher.
