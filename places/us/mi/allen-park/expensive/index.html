Home prices

Rents

In Allen Park the median monthly rent is $980. This is approximately $96 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $82 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $156 (18%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
