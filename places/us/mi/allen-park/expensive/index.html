Home prices

The median home in Allen Park costs $123,800. It is approximately $39,920 (47%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $93,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $74,600 (151%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Allen Park is $980 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $96 (10%) higher.It is approximately $82 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $156 (18%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
