Home prices

The median home in Wolverine Lake is valued at $197,000. It is approximately $127,100 (39%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $20,500 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $147,800 (300%) higher.

Rents

Wolverine Lake's median monthly rent is $988. It is about $408 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $74 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $164 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
