Compared to its surrounding areas, Caledonia is significantly more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Caledonia's median home cost is $209,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $57,200 (37%) higher. It is about $8,500 (3%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $65,600 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Caledonia is $842. It is about $64 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $220 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $83 (8%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
