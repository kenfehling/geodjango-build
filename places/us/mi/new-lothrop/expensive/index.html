Home prices

The cost of the median home in New Lothrop is $111,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $7,740 (7%) higher.It is approximately $106,200 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $62,100 (126%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In New Lothrop the median rent is $750 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $9 (1%) lower.It is about $312 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $74 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
