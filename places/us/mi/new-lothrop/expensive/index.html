Home prices

Rents

In New Lothrop the median rent is $750 a month. It is roughly $9 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $74 (8%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
