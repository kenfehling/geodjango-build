Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sylvan Lake is $233,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $143,380 (38%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $16,300 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $184,600 (375%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Sylvan Lake's median rent is $1,712 per month. It is approximately $217 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $650 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $888 (107%) higher.
