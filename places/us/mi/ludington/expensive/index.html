In comparison to other places in this area, Ludington is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Ludington's median home value is $124,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $10,840 (9%) higher. It is about $93,100 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $19,000 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Ludington the median rent is $665 per month. This is about $87 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $397 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $260 (28%) lower.
