Home prices

Rents

In Ludington the median rent is $665 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $87 (11%) lower.It is about $397 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $260 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
