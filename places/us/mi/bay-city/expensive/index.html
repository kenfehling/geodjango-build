In comparison to other places in this area, Bay City is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bay City costs $66,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $30,640 (31%) lower. It is roughly $150,700 (69%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $17,600 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Bay City is $634 a month. This is approximately $252 (28%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $428 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $190 (23%) lower.
