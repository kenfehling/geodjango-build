In comparison to its nearby areas, Bay City is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bay City is valued at $66,800. This is approximately $30,640 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $150,700 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $17,600 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Bay City is $634 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $252 (28%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $428 (40%) lower, and is approximately $190 (23%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
