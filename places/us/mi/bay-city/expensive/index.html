In comparison to nearby areas, Bay City is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Bay City is $66,800. It is approximately $30,640 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $150,700 (69%) lower, and it is roughly $17,600 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Bay City's median monthly rent is $634. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $252 (28%) lower.It is approximately $428 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $190 (23%) lower.
