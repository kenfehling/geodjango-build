Compared to its nearby areas, Bay City is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Bay City is $66,800. It is roughly $30,640 (31%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $150,700 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $17,600 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bay City is $634 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $252 (28%) lower.It is approximately $428 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $190 (23%) lower.
