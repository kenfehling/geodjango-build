Home prices

The median home in Detroit Beach costs $106,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $5,400 (4%) lower.It is about $110,600 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $57,700 (117%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Detroit Beach the median rent is $604 a month. It is roughly $341 (36%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $458 (43%) lower, and it is about $220 (26%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
