Home prices

The median home in Owosso costs $84,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $1,900 (2%) higher.It is approximately $133,200 (61%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $35,100 (71%) higher.

Rents

In Owosso the median rent is $731 per month. This is approximately $33 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $331 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $93 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
