Home prices

The value of the median home in Owosso is $84,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $1,900 (2%) higher. It is about $133,200 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $35,100 (71%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Owosso is $731 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $33 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $331 (31%) lower, and it is about $93 (11%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
