Home prices

The cost of the median home in Greenville is $101,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $4,800 (4%) higher.It is roughly $115,900 (53%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $41,800 (29%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Greenville's median monthly rent is $668. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $16 (2%) higher.It is approximately $394 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $257 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
