Home prices

The value of the median home in Greenville is $101,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $4,800 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $115,900 (53%) lower, and is approximately $41,800 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Greenville is $668 a month. This is roughly $16 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $394 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $257 (27%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
