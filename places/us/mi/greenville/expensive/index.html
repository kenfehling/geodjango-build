Home prices

The median home in Greenville is valued at $101,600. It is about $4,800 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $115,900 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $41,800 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Greenville the median rent is $668 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $16 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $394 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $257 (27%) lower.
