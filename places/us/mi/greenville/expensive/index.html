Home prices

The cost of the median home in Greenville is $101,600. This is roughly $4,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $115,900 (53%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $41,800 (29%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Greenville's median rent is $668 a month. It is roughly $16 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $394 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $257 (27%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
