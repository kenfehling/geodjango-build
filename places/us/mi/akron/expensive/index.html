Home prices

The median home in Akron is valued at $63,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $18,860 (23%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $154,400 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $13,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

Akron's median rent is $638 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $46 (6%) lower.It is roughly $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $186 (22%) lower.
