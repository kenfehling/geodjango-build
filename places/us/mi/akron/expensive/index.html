Compared to other places in this area, Akron is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Akron is valued at $63,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $18,860 (23%) lower. It is roughly $154,400 (70%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $13,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Akron the median monthly rent is $638. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $46 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $424 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $186 (22%) lower.
