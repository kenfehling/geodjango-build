Home prices

The median home price in Akron is $63,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $18,860 (23%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $154,400 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $13,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

Akron's median monthly rent is $638. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $46 (6%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $424 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $186 (22%) lower.
