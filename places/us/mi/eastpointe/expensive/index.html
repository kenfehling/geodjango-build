Compared to the surrounding areas, Eastpointe is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Eastpointe's median home cost is $77,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $50,400 (39%) lower. It is approximately $140,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $28,000 (56%) higher.

Rents

In Eastpointe the median monthly rent is $1,095. It is approximately $61 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $33 (3%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $271 (32%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
