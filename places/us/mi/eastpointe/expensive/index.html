Home prices

The median home cost in Eastpointe is $77,200. This is about $50,400 (39%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $140,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $28,000 (56%) higher.

Rents

In Eastpointe the median rent is $1,095 a month. It is approximately $61 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $33 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $271 (32%) higher.
