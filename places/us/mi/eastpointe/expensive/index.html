Home prices

The median home in Eastpointe is valued at $77,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $50,400 (39%) lower.It is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $28,000 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Eastpointe the median monthly rent is $1,095. It is approximately $61 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $33 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $271 (32%) higher.
