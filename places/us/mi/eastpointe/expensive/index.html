In comparison to other places in this area, Eastpointe is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Eastpointe is $77,200. This is approximately $50,400 (39%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $140,300 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $28,000 (56%) higher.

Rents

Eastpointe's median rent is $1,095 a month. It is about $61 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $271 (32%) higher.
