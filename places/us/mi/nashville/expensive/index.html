In comparison to its surrounding areas, Nashville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Nashville is $77,100. This is roughly $20,940 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $140,400 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is about $66,300 (46%) lower.

Rents

Nashville's median rent is $731 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $10 (1%) lower.It is roughly $331 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $194 (20%) lower.
