Compared to its surrounding areas, Nashville is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nashville is valued at $77,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $20,940 (21%) lower. It is about $140,400 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $66,300 (46%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Nashville's median rent is $731 per month. This is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $331 (31%) lower, and it is about $194 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
