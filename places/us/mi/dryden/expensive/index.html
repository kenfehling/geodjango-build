Home prices

The price of the median home in Dryden is $140,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $6,200 (4%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $77,000 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $91,300 (185%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Dryden the median monthly rent is $960. It is approximately $109 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $102 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $136 (16%) higher.
