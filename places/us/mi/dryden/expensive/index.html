Home prices

Dryden's median home cost is $140,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $6,200 (4%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $77,000 (35%) lower, and is about $91,300 (185%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Dryden's median monthly rent is $960. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $109 (12%) higher. It is about $102 (9%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $136 (16%) higher.
