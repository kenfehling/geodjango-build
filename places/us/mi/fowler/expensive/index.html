Home prices

The median home in Fowler costs $143,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $31,620 (28%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $74,400 (34%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Fowler the median rent is $707 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $56 (8%) higher.It is approximately $355 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $218 (23%) lower.
