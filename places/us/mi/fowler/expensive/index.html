Compared to its surrounding areas, Fowler is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fowler is $143,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $31,620 (28%) higher. It is roughly $74,400 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is is very similar.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fowler is $707. Compared to other places in this area this is about $56 (8%) higher. It is about $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $218 (23%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
