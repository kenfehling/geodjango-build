Home prices

The median home in Lake Orion is valued at $269,900. It is approximately $21,540 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Rents

Lake Orion's median rent is $733 per month. It is about $279 (27%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $329 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $91 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
