Home prices

The median home in Lake Orion is valued at $269,900. This is roughly $21,540 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $52,400 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $220,700 (448%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Lake Orion's median monthly rent is $733. It is approximately $279 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $329 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $91 (11%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
