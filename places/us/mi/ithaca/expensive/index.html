Home prices

Ithaca's median home price is $88,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $11,880 (15%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $128,700 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $54,600 (38%) lower.

Rents

Ithaca's median monthly rent is $699. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $27 (3%) higher. It is roughly $363 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $226 (24%) lower.
