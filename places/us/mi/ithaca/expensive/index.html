Compared to the surrounding areas, Ithaca is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Ithaca's median home cost is $88,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $11,880 (15%) higher. It is roughly $128,700 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $54,600 (38%) lower.

Rents

Ithaca's median rent is $699 per month. It is approximately $27 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $363 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $226 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
