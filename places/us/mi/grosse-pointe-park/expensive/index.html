The median home cost in Grosse Pointe Park is $358,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $137,800 (62%) higher. It is roughly $140,900 (64%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $309,200 (628%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Grosse Pointe Park the median monthly rent is $1,110. This is roughly $57 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $48 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $286 (34%) higher.
