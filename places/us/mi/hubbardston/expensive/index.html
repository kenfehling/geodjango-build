Home prices

Rents

Hubbardston's median monthly rent is $619. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $33 (5%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $306 (33%) lower.
