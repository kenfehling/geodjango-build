Home prices

The median home price in West Monroe is $62,400. This is about $66,440 (51%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $155,100 (71%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $13,200 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Monroe is $1,091. It is roughly $306 (39%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $29 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $267 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
