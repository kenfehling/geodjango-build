Compared to its surrounding areas, Tawas City is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Flint, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tawas City costs $96,300. It is roughly $23,160 (31%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $121,200 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $66,800 (226%) higher compared to the city of Flint.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tawas City is $707. This is roughly $68 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $355 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $9 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Flint.
