Home prices

The median home in Tawas City is valued at $96,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $23,160 (31%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $121,200 (55%) lower, and is approximately $66,800 (226%) higher in comparison to the city of Flint.

Rents

Tawas City's median rent is $707 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $68 (10%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $355 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Flint is approximately $9 (1%) lower.
