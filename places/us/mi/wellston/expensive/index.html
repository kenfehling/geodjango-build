Home prices

The price of the median home in Wellston is $59,300. This is roughly $14,600 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $158,200 (72%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $84,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Wellston is $522 a month. It is roughly $285 (35%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $540 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $403 (43%) lower.
