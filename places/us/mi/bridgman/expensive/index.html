Home prices

The median home in Bridgman is valued at $162,400. It is about $25,420 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $55,100 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $95,600 (37%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Bridgman is $733 a month. It is about $174 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $329 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $379 (34%) lower.
