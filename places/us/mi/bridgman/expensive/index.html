Home prices

The price of the median home in Bridgman is $162,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $25,420 (18%) higher. It is approximately $55,100 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $95,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents
