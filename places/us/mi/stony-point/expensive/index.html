Compared to the surrounding areas, Stony Point is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Stony Point is $121,000. This is about $7,800 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $96,500 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $71,800 (145%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Stony Point is $1,171 per month. It is roughly $358 (43%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $109 (10%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $347 (42%) higher.
