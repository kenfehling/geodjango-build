Compared to its nearby areas, Saline is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Saline costs $254,300. It is approximately $39,840 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $36,800 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $205,100 (416%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Saline is $951. This is about $38 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $111 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $127 (15%) higher.
