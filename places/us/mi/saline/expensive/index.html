Compared to its nearby areas, Saline is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Saline costs $254,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $39,840 (18%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $36,800 (16%) higher, and it is roughly $205,100 (416%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Saline the median monthly rent is $951. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $38 (3%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $111 (10%) lower, and is approximately $127 (15%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
