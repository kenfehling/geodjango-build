Compared to its nearby areas, Saline is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Saline is $254,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $39,840 (18%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $36,800 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $205,100 (416%) higher.

Rents

Saline's median monthly rent is $951. This is roughly $38 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $111 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $127 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
