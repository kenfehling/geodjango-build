Home prices

The value of the median home in Addison is $92,100. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $3,140 (3%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $125,400 (57%) lower, and is approximately $42,900 (87%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Addison the median monthly rent is $658. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $52 (7%) lower. It is about $404 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $166 (20%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
