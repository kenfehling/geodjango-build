In comparison to its nearby areas, Lansing is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lansing is $83,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $75,620 (47%) lower.It is approximately $134,000 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $59,900 (41%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Lansing's median monthly rent is $819. It is about $28 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $106 (11%) lower.
