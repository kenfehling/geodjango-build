In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lansing is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Lansing's median home value is $83,500. This is roughly $75,620 (47%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $134,000 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $59,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

Lansing's median rent is $819 a month. It is about $28 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $243 (22%) lower, and is approximately $106 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
