Compared to nearby areas, Charlevoix is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Traverse City.

Home prices

The median home in Charlevoix costs $167,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $31,740 (23%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $49,700 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Traverse City it is roughly $98,300 (36%) lower.

Rents

Charlevoix's median rent is $567 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $152 (21%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $495 (46%) lower, and is roughly $338 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Traverse City.
