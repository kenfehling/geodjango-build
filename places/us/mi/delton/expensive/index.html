Rents

The median rent in Delton is $325 a month. It is roughly $557 (63%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $737 (69%) lower, and it is roughly $600 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
