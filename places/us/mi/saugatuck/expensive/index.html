Home prices

The median home price in Saugatuck is $420,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $246,420 (141%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $203,200 (93%) higher, and is roughly $277,300 (193%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Saugatuck is $475 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $353 (42%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $587 (55%) lower, and it is about $450 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
