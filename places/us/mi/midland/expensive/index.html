Home prices

The median home value in Midland is $150,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $35,500 (30%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $67,100 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Lansing it is about $66,900 (80%) higher.

Rents

In Midland the median monthly rent is $788. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Lansing it is approximately $31 (3%) lower.
