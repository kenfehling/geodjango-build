Home prices

The median home in Midland costs $150,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $35,500 (30%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $67,100 (30%) lower, and is approximately $66,900 (80%) higher in comparison to the city of Lansing.

Rents

In Midland the median monthly rent is $788. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Lansing it is roughly $31 (3%) lower.
