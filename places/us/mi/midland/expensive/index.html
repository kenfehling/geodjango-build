Home prices

The price of the median home in Midland is $150,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $35,500 (30%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $67,100 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Lansing it is roughly $66,900 (80%) higher.

Rents

Midland's median rent is $788 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is is very similar.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lansing it is approximately $31 (3%) lower.
