Compared to other places in this area, New Haven is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in New Haven is $120,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $51,780 (30%) lower.It is roughly $96,700 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $71,600 (145%) higher.

Rents

In New Haven the median monthly rent is $1,025. Compared to nearby areas this is about $273 (36%) higher.It is approximately $37 (3%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $201 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
