In comparison to the surrounding areas, New Haven is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in New Haven is $120,800. This is approximately $51,780 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $96,700 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $71,600 (145%) higher.

Rents

New Haven's median monthly rent is $1,025. This is roughly $273 (36%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $201 (24%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
