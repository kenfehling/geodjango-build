Compared to its surrounding areas, New Haven is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in New Haven is valued at $120,800. It is approximately $51,780 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $96,700 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $71,600 (145%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In New Haven the median rent is $1,025 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $273 (36%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $37 (3%) lower, and is roughly $201 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
