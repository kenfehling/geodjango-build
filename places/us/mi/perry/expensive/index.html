Home prices

The median home in Perry costs $105,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $31,980 (23%) lower. It is roughly $111,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $56,500 (114%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Perry is $759 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $303 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $65 (7%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
