In comparison to its surrounding areas, Port Huron is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Port Huron costs $86,700. It is about $57,640 (39%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $37,500 (76%) higher.

Rents

Port Huron's median rent is $780 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $59 (8%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $44 (5%) lower.
