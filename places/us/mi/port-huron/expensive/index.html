Compared to the surrounding areas, Port Huron is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Port Huron is valued at $86,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $57,640 (39%) lower. It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $37,500 (76%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Port Huron is $780. Compared to other places in this area it is about $59 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $282 (26%) lower, and is approximately $44 (5%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
