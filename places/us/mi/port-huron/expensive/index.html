Compared to the surrounding areas, Port Huron is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Port Huron is valued at $86,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $57,640 (39%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower, and it is approximately $37,500 (76%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Port Huron is $780 a month. It is roughly $59 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and is roughly $44 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
