In comparison to nearby areas, Port Huron is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Port Huron's median home cost is $86,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $57,640 (39%) lower.It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $37,500 (76%) higher.

Rents

In Port Huron the median monthly rent is $780. This is roughly $59 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $282 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $44 (5%) lower.
