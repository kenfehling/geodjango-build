Home prices

The median home price in River Rouge is $36,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $46,340 (56%) lower.It is roughly $181,200 (83%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $12,900 (26%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

River Rouge's median rent is $585 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $267 (31%) lower.It is roughly $477 (44%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $239 (29%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
