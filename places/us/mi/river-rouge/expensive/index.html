Home prices

The price of the median home in River Rouge is $36,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $46,340 (56%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $181,200 (83%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $12,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

River Rouge's median monthly rent is $585. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $267 (31%) lower. It is about $477 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $239 (29%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
