Compared to its surrounding areas, Roseville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Roseville is $86,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $28,020 (24%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $131,100 (60%) lower, and it is approximately $37,200 (75%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Roseville the median monthly rent is $979. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $84 (9%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $83 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $155 (18%) higher.
