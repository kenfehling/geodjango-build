Compared to its surrounding areas, Roseville is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Roseville costs $86,400. It is roughly $28,020 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $131,100 (60%) lower, and is about $37,200 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Roseville's median rent is $979 per month. This is about $84 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $83 (7%) lower, and it is about $155 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
