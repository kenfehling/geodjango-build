In comparison to nearby areas, Roseville is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Roseville is valued at $86,400. It is roughly $28,020 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $131,100 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $37,200 (75%) higher.

Rents

Roseville's median monthly rent is $979. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $84 (9%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $83 (7%) lower, and is approximately $155 (18%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
