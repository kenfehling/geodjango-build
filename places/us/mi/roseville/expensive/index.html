In comparison to its surrounding areas, Roseville is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Roseville costs $86,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $28,020 (24%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $131,100 (60%) lower, and is about $37,200 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Roseville the median monthly rent is $979. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $84 (9%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $83 (7%) lower, and it is roughly $155 (18%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
