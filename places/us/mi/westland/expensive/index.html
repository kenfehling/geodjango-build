Compared to the surrounding areas, Westland is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Westland costs $121,100. This is roughly $11,080 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $96,400 (44%) lower, and it is roughly $71,900 (146%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westland is $894. This is about $67 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $168 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $70 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
