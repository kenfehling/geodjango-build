Rents

The median rent in Three Rivers is $691 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $31 (4%) lower. It is approximately $371 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $73 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
