In comparison to its nearby areas, Sterling Heights is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sterling Heights is $187,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $16,860 (9%) higher. It is roughly $29,900 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $138,400 (281%) higher.

Rents

In Sterling Heights the median rent is $1,025 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $69 (7%) higher. It is roughly $37 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $201 (24%) higher.
