In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sterling Heights is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Sterling Heights is $187,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $16,860 (9%) higher.It is approximately $29,900 (13%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $138,400 (281%) higher.

Rents

Sterling Heights's median monthly rent is $1,025. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $69 (7%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $37 (3%) lower, and is approximately $201 (24%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
