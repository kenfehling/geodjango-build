Compared to the surrounding areas, Sterling Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Sterling Heights's median home value is $187,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $16,860 (9%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $29,900 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $138,400 (281%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Sterling Heights's median rent is $1,025 per month. This is about $69 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $37 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $201 (24%) higher.
