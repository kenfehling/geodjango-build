Home prices

The median home in Oak Hill is valued at $86,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13,340 (13%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $130,700 (60%) lower, and is approximately $56,600 (39%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Oak Hill is $775 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $44 (6%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $287 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $150 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
