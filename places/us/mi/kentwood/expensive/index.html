Compared to its surrounding areas, Kentwood is a lot less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kentwood costs $158,700. This is roughly $82,260 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $58,800 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $15,300 (10%) higher.

Rents

Kentwood's median monthly rent is $899. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $200 (18%) lower.It is roughly $163 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $26 (2%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
