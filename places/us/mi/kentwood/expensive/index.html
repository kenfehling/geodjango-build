Compared to nearby areas, Kentwood is a lot less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Kentwood's median home value is $158,700. It is about $82,260 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $58,800 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $15,300 (10%) higher.

Rents

Kentwood's median monthly rent is $899. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $200 (18%) lower. It is about $163 (15%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $26 (2%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
