Compared to its surrounding areas, Kentwood is a lot less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Kentwood costs $158,700. This is approximately $82,260 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $58,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $15,300 (10%) higher.

Rents

Kentwood's median monthly rent is $899. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $200 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $163 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $26 (2%) lower.
