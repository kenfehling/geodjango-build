Home prices

The value of the median home in Kentwood is $158,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $82,260 (34%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $58,800 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $15,300 (10%) higher.

Rents
