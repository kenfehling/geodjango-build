Home prices

The median home value in Byron is $109,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $22,720 (17%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $108,300 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $60,000 (121%) higher.

Rents

In Byron the median rent is $625 per month. It is about $103 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $437 (41%) lower, and is about $199 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
