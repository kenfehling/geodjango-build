In comparison to other places in this area, Birmingham is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Birmingham's median home value is $541,500. This is roughly $133,520 (32%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $324,000 (148%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $492,300 (1000%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Birmingham is $1,513. This is roughly $350 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $451 (42%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $689 (83%) higher.
