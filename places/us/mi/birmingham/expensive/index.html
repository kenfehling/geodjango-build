Compared to other places in this area, Birmingham is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Birmingham is valued at $541,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $133,520 (32%) higher.It is roughly $324,000 (148%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $492,300 (1000%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Birmingham the median rent is $1,513 per month. It is roughly $350 (30%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $451 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $689 (83%) higher.
