In comparison to other places in this area, Birmingham is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Birmingham's median home price is $541,500. This is roughly $133,520 (32%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $324,000 (148%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $492,300 (1000%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Birmingham the median monthly rent is $1,513. This is approximately $350 (30%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $451 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $689 (83%) higher.
