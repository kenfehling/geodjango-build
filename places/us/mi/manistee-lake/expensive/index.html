Home prices

Manistee Lake's median home price is $117,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $17,800 (17%) higher.It is roughly $99,900 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Midland it is roughly $32,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Manistee Lake the median rent is $848 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $126 (17%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $214 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $60 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Midland.
