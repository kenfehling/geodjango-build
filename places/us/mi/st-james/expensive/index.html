Home prices

The median home in St. James costs $227,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $55,060 (31%) higher. It is about $10,300 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $135,300 (146%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

St. James's median rent is $817 a month. This is about $143 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $245 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is approximately $119 (17%) higher.
