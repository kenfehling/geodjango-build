Rents

St. James's median monthly rent is $817. This is approximately $143 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $245 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $119 (17%) higher.
