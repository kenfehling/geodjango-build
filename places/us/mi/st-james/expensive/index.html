Home prices

The median home value in St. James is $227,800. It is roughly $55,060 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $10,300 (4%) higher, and it is approximately $135,300 (146%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

St. James's median rent is $817 a month. This is about $143 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $245 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is about $119 (17%) higher.
