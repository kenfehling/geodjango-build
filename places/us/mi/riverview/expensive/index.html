In comparison to other places in this area, Riverview is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverview costs $149,400. It is approximately $28,220 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $68,100 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $100,200 (203%) higher.

Rents

In Riverview the median rent is $795 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $24 (2%) lower.It is approximately $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $29 (3%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
