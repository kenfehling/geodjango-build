In comparison to its surrounding areas, Riverview is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Riverview is $149,400. It is about $28,220 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $68,100 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $100,200 (203%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Riverview is $795 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $24 (2%) lower.It is roughly $267 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $29 (3%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
