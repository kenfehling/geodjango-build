In comparison to its nearby areas, Beulah is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Traverse City however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Beulah is valued at $152,800. It is about $21,460 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $64,700 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Traverse City is approximately $113,300 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Beulah is $356 a month. It is roughly $323 (47%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $706 (66%) lower, and is approximately $549 (60%) lower compared to the city of Traverse City.
