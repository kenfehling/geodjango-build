Home prices

Rents

Beulah's median monthly rent is $356. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $323 (47%) lower.It is roughly $706 (66%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Traverse City it is roughly $549 (60%) lower.
