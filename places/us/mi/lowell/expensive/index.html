Home prices

The median home in Lowell is valued at $148,300. It is about $14,680 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $69,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $4,900 (3%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Lowell the median rent is $829 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $39 (4%) higher.It is roughly $233 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $96 (10%) lower.
