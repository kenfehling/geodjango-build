Home prices

Lowell's median home price is $148,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,680 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $69,200 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $4,900 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Lowell the median rent is $829 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $39 (4%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $233 (21%) lower, and is roughly $96 (10%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
