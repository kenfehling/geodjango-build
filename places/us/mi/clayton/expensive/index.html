In comparison to its surrounding areas, Clayton is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Clayton's median home cost is $69,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $20,900 (23%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $148,000 (68%) lower, and is about $20,300 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clayton is $850. This is roughly $127 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $212 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $26 (3%) higher.
