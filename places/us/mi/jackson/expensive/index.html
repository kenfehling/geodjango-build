Compared to the surrounding areas, Jackson is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Jackson's median home value is $68,400. It is roughly $58,680 (46%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $149,100 (68%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $19,200 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Jackson is $739 a month. This is approximately $8 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $323 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $85 (10%) lower.
