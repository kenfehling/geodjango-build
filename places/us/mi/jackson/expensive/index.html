Home prices

The median home value in Jackson is $68,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $58,680 (46%) lower.It is roughly $149,100 (68%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $19,200 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Jackson's median rent is $739 per month. This is about $8 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $323 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $85 (10%) lower.
