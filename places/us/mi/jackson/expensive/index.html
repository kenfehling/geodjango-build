Compared to its surrounding areas, Jackson is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Jackson is valued at $68,400. This is about $58,680 (46%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $149,100 (68%) lower, and is roughly $19,200 (39%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jackson is $739. This is roughly $8 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $323 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $85 (10%) lower.
