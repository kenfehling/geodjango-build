Home prices

The median home in Jackson costs $68,400. It is roughly $58,680 (46%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $149,100 (68%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $19,200 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Jackson the median rent is $739 a month. It is about $8 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $323 (30%) lower, and is approximately $85 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
