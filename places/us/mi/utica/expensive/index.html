In comparison to its surrounding areas, Utica is a lot less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Utica is $156,800. This is roughly $93,860 (37%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $60,700 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $107,600 (218%) higher.

Rents

Utica's median rent is $884 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $180 (16%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $178 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $60 (7%) higher.
