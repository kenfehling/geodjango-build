In comparison to nearby areas, Utica is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Utica is valued at $156,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $93,860 (37%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $60,700 (27%) lower, and is roughly $107,600 (218%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Utica is $884 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $180 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $178 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $60 (7%) higher.
