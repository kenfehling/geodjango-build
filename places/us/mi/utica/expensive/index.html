Compared to its surrounding areas, Utica is a lot less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home price in Utica is $156,800. This is roughly $93,860 (37%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $60,700 (27%) lower, and is approximately $107,600 (218%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Utica the median monthly rent is $884. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $180 (16%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $178 (16%) lower, and is roughly $60 (7%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
