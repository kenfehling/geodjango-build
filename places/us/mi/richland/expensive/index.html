Compared to its surrounding areas, Richland is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richland is valued at $184,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $12,940 (7%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $33,400 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $69,000 (59%) higher.

Rents

Richland's median monthly rent is $850. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $36 (4%) higher.It is approximately $212 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $86 (11%) higher.
