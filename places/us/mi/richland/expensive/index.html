Home prices

The value of the median home in Richland is $184,100. It is roughly $12,940 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $33,400 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $69,000 (59%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Richland the median rent is $850 a month. It is approximately $36 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $86 (11%) higher.
