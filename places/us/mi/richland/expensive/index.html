The median home price in Richland is $184,100. This is roughly $12,940 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $33,400 (15%) lower, and it is about $69,000 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Richland's median rent is $850 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $36 (4%) higher. It is approximately $212 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $86 (11%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
