In comparison to other places in this area, Richland is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home price in Richland is $184,100. It is about $12,940 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $33,400 (15%) lower, and is approximately $69,000 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richland is $850. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $36 (4%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $86 (11%) higher.
