Home prices

The median home in Richland costs $184,100. It is approximately $12,940 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $33,400 (15%) lower, and is roughly $69,000 (59%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Richland the median monthly rent is $850. This is about $36 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $86 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
