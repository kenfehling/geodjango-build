Compared to its surrounding areas, Richland is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Richland's median home cost is $184,100. This is approximately $12,940 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $33,400 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $69,000 (59%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Richland's median rent is $850 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $36 (4%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $212 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $86 (11%) higher.
