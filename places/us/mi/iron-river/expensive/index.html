Home prices

The median home price in Iron River is $54,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $3,560 (7%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $163,500 (75%) lower, and it is approximately $133,300 (71%) lower compared to the city of Marquette.

Rents

Iron River's median monthly rent is $477. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $111 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $585 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Marquette is about $318 (40%) lower.
