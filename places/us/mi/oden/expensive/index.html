Home prices

Rents

In Oden the median rent is $599 per month. This is about $170 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $463 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $99 (14%) lower.
