In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marine City is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marine City is valued at $122,300. It is about $59,780 (32%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $95,200 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $73,100 (148%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marine City is $803. It is roughly $55 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $259 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $21 (2%) lower.
