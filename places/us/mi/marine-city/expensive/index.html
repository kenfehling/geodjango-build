Compared to nearby areas, Marine City is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Marine City is $122,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $59,780 (32%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $95,200 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $73,100 (148%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Marine City is $803 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $55 (7%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $259 (24%) lower, and is about $21 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
