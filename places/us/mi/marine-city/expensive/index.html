In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marine City is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marine City is valued at $122,300. This is approximately $59,780 (32%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $95,200 (43%) lower, and is roughly $73,100 (148%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Marine City the median rent is $803 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $55 (7%) higher. It is roughly $259 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $21 (2%) lower.
