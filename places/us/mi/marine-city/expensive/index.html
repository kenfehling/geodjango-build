In comparison to other places in this area, Marine City is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Marine City is valued at $122,300. It is about $59,780 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $95,200 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $73,100 (148%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Marine City is $803 a month. This is roughly $55 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $259 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $21 (2%) lower.
