In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marine City is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Marine City is $122,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $59,780 (32%) lower.It is approximately $95,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $73,100 (148%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marine City is $803. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $55 (7%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $259 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $21 (2%) lower.
