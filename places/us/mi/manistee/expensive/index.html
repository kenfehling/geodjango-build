In comparison to the surrounding areas, Manistee is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Muskegon.

Home prices

The median home cost in Manistee is $108,900. It is about $13,180 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $108,600 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Muskegon it is roughly $35,900 (49%) higher.

Rents

Manistee's median rent is $683 a month. This is about $66 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Muskegon is roughly $23 (3%) lower.
