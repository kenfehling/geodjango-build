Home prices

The value of the median home in Manistee is $108,900. This is approximately $13,180 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $108,600 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Muskegon it is roughly $35,900 (49%) higher.

Rents

Manistee's median rent is $683 per month. It is approximately $66 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $379 (35%) lower, and is roughly $23 (3%) lower compared to the city of Muskegon.
