Compared to other places in this area, Manistee is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Muskegonhowever, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manistee costs $108,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $13,180 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $108,600 (49%) lower, and is approximately $35,900 (49%) higher compared to the city of Muskegon.

Rents

Manistee's median rent is $683 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $66 (8%) lower. It is roughly $379 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Muskegon it is roughly $23 (3%) lower.
