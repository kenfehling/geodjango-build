Home prices

The median home value in East Tawas is $95,500. This is about $22,200 (30%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $122,000 (56%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $66,000 (223%) higher in comparison to the city of Flint.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Tawas is $588. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $74 (11%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $474 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $128 (17%) lower compared to the city of Flint.
