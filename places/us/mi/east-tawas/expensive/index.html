In comparison to other places in this area, East Tawas is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Flint however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in East Tawas is $95,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $22,200 (30%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $122,000 (56%) lower, and is roughly $66,000 (223%) higher in comparison to the city of Flint.

Rents

The median rent in East Tawas is $588 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $74 (11%) lower. It is about $474 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $128 (17%) lower compared to the city of Flint.
