Home prices

The median home price in Dexter is $300,900. It is about $67,280 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $83,400 (38%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $251,700 (511%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Dexter the median rent is $1,213 a month. This is approximately $222 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $151 (14%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $389 (47%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
