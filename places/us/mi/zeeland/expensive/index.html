Home prices

Zeeland's median home price is $149,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $17,020 (10%) lower.It is roughly $68,400 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $5,700 (3%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Zeeland the median monthly rent is $847. It is roughly $68 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $215 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $78 (8%) lower.
