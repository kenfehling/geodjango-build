In comparison to other places in this area, Ironwood is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Merrill, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ironwood costs $47,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $12,700 (21%) lower. It is about $170,500 (78%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $38,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Merrill.

Rents

The median rent in Ironwood is $510 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $11 (2%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $552 (51%) lower, and it is roughly $127 (19%) lower compared to the city of Merrill.
