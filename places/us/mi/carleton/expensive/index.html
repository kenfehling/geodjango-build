Compared to nearby areas, Carleton is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Carleton is valued at $136,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $14,360 (9%) lower. It is about $81,100 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $87,200 (177%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carleton is $616. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $317 (33%) lower. It is about $446 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $208 (25%) lower.
