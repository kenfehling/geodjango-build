In comparison to its surrounding areas, Carleton is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Carleton is valued at $136,400. It is about $14,360 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $81,100 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $87,200 (177%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Carleton the median rent is $616 per month. This is roughly $317 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $446 (41%) lower, and is about $208 (25%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
