Home prices

Carleton's median home cost is $136,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14,360 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $81,100 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $87,200 (177%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carleton is $616. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $317 (33%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $446 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $208 (25%) lower.
