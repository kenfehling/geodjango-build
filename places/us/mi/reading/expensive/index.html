Home prices

The median home price in Reading is $76,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $110,320 (59%) lower. It is roughly $141,500 (65%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is about $4,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Reading is $781. This is roughly $43 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $281 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is roughly $56 (7%) higher.
