Home prices

The median home in Reading costs $76,000. This is approximately $110,320 (59%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $141,500 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is roughly $4,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

Reading's median monthly rent is $781. This is about $43 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $281 (26%) lower, and is roughly $56 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Toledo.
