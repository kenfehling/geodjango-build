Home prices

The value of the median home in Carp Lake is $122,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $38,460 (23%) lower.It is roughly $95,000 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $30,000 (32%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carp Lake is $525. This is about $166 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $537 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is roughly $173 (24%) lower.
