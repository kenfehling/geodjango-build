Compared to the surrounding areas, Carp Lake is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Carp Lake is $122,500. This is roughly $38,460 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $95,000 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $30,000 (32%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

In Carp Lake the median rent is $525 a month. This is about $166 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $537 (50%) lower, and is roughly $173 (24%) lower compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
