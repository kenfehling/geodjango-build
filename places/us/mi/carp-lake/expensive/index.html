Home prices

The median home cost in Carp Lake is $122,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $38,460 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $95,000 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $30,000 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

Carp Lake's median rent is $525 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $166 (24%) lower. It is about $537 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $173 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
