Rents

The median rent in Ionia is $813 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $181 (28%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $112 (12%) lower.
