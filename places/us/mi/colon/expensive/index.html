Compared to other places in this area, Colon is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Colon is valued at $90,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $17,200 (23%) higher.It is about $127,200 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $24,800 (21%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Colon's median monthly rent is $555. This is roughly $199 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $507 (47%) lower, and is approximately $209 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
