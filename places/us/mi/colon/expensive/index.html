Compared to other places in this area, Colon is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

Colon's median home value is $90,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $17,200 (23%) higher. It is approximately $127,200 (58%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $24,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

Colon's median rent is $555 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $199 (26%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $507 (47%) lower, and is roughly $209 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
