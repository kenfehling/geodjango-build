Home prices

The price of the median home in Colon is $90,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $17,200 (23%) higher. It is approximately $127,200 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $24,800 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Colon the median rent is $555 a month. This is roughly $199 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $507 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $209 (27%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
