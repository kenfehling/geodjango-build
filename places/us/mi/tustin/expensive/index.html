Home prices

The value of the median home in Tustin is $88,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $6,480 (7%) higher. It is approximately $128,700 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $54,600 (38%) lower.

Rents

Tustin's median rent is $640 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $133 (17%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $422 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $285 (30%) lower.
