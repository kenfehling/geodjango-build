Compared to its surrounding areas, Tustin is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Tustin's median home value is $88,800. This is about $6,480 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $128,700 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $54,600 (38%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Tustin's median monthly rent is $640. It is about $133 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $422 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $285 (30%) lower.
