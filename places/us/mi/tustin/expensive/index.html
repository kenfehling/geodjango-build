Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tustin is $88,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $6,480 (7%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $128,700 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is about $54,600 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tustin is $640 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $133 (17%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $422 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $285 (30%) lower.
