Home prices

The value of the median home in Lakeview is $85,200. This is roughly $9,320 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $132,300 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $58,200 (40%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Lakeview the median monthly rent is $471. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $226 (32%) lower. It is roughly $591 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $454 (49%) lower.
