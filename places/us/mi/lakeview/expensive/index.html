Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lakeview is $85,200. It is approximately $9,320 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $132,300 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $58,200 (40%) lower.

Rents

Lakeview's median rent is $471 a month. This is about $226 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $591 (55%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $454 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
