Compared to other places in this area, Hudsonville is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Hudsonville is $155,300. It is approximately $15,980 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $62,200 (28%) lower, and is approximately $11,900 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hudsonville is $927. This is roughly $68 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $135 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is is very similar.
