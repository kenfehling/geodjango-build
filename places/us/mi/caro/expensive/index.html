Home prices

The value of the median home in Caro is $77,800. This is about $7,580 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $139,700 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $28,600 (58%) higher.

Rents

Caro's median monthly rent is $697. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $60 (9%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and it is about $127 (15%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
