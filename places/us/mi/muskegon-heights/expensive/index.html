Compared to its surrounding areas, Muskegon Heights is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

Muskegon Heights's median home value is $43,900. This is approximately $81,240 (64%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $173,600 (79%) lower, and is roughly $99,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Muskegon Heights is $711 a month. This is about $104 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $351 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $214 (23%) lower.
