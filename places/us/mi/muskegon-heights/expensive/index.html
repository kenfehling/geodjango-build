Home prices

Muskegon Heights's median home price is $43,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $81,240 (64%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $173,600 (79%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $99,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Muskegon Heights the median monthly rent is $711. This is about $104 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $351 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $214 (23%) lower.
