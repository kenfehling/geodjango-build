Home prices

The price of the median home in Minden City is $67,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,300 (17%) lower. It is roughly $150,200 (69%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $18,100 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Minden City's median monthly rent is $600. This is about $26 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $462 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $224 (27%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
