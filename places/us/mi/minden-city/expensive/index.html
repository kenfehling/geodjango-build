Home prices

The median home value in Minden City is $67,300. It is about $14,300 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $150,200 (69%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $18,100 (36%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Minden City the median monthly rent is $600. This is roughly $26 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $462 (43%) lower, and is approximately $224 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
