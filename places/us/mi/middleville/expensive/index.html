Rents

Middleville's median monthly rent is $1,015. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $153 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $47 (4%) lower, and is roughly $90 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
