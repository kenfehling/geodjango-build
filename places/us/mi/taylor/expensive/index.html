Home prices

The median home in Taylor costs $96,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $15,000 (13%) lower. It is roughly $121,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $47,100 (95%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Taylor is $880. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $182 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $56 (6%) higher.
