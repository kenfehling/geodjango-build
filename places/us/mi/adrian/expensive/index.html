Home prices

The value of the median home in Adrian is $76,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $30,840 (28%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $140,700 (64%) lower, and it is roughly $27,600 (56%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Adrian's median rent is $743 per month. This is approximately $79 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $319 (30%) lower, and is approximately $81 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
