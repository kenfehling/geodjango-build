In comparison to nearby areas, Northview is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Northview's median home cost is $167,700. This is about $79,480 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $49,800 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $24,300 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Northview the median rent is $969 per month. This is approximately $108 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $93 (8%) lower, and is about $44 (4%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
