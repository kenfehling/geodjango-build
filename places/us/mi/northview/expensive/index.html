Home prices

The median home in Northview costs $167,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $79,480 (32%) lower.It is approximately $49,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $24,300 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Northview is $969 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $108 (10%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $93 (8%) lower, and it is about $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
