Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Northview is $969 a month. It is approximately $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $93 (8%) lower, and it is roughly $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
