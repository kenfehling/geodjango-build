Similar to other places in this area, it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Williamston costs $161,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $4,760 (3%) higher. It is about $55,600 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $112,700 (229%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Williamston's median rent is $803 a month. This is roughly $79 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $259 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $21 (2%) lower.
