Similar to other places in this area, it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Williamston is valued at $161,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $4,760 (3%) higher. It is about $55,600 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $112,700 (229%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Williamston is $803. It is roughly $79 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $259 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $21 (2%) lower.
