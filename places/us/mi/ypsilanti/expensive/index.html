In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ypsilanti is significantly less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Ypsilanti's median home value is $151,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $72,740 (32%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $102,100 (207%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Ypsilanti is $854 per month. It is roughly $68 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $208 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $30 (3%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
