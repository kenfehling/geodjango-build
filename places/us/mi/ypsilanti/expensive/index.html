Home prices

The median home price in Ypsilanti is $151,300. It is approximately $72,740 (32%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $102,100 (207%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Ypsilanti is $854 per month. It is about $68 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $208 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $30 (3%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
