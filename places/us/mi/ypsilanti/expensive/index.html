In comparison to other places in this area, Ypsilanti is a lot less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Ypsilanti costs $151,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $72,740 (32%) lower.It is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $102,100 (207%) higher.

Rents

Ypsilanti's median rent is $854 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $68 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $208 (19%) lower, and it is about $30 (3%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
