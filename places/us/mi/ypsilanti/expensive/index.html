Compared to its nearby areas, Ypsilanti is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home value in Ypsilanti is $151,300. It is roughly $72,740 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $102,100 (207%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Ypsilanti's median monthly rent is $854. This is approximately $68 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $208 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $30 (3%) higher.
