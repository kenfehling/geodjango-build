Home prices

The median home in Westwood costs $129,100. It is about $4,900 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $88,400 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $14,000 (12%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Westwood's median rent is $903 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $85 (10%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $159 (14%) lower, and is approximately $139 (18%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
