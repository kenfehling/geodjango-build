Home prices

The median home in Westwood is valued at $129,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $4,900 (3%) higher. It is roughly $88,400 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $14,000 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Westwood's median monthly rent is $903. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $85 (10%) higher. It is about $159 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $139 (18%) higher.
