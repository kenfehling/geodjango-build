Rents

In Burlington the median monthly rent is $675. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $45 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $387 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $89 (11%) lower.
