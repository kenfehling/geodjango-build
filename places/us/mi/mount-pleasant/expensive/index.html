Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant is valued at $137,700. It is approximately $28,740 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $79,800 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $5,700 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Pleasant is $755 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $113 (17%) higher.It is roughly $307 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $170 (18%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
