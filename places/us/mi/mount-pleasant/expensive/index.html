Compared to its surrounding areas, Mount Pleasant is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mount Pleasant is $137,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $28,740 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $79,800 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $5,700 (3%) lower.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median monthly rent is $755. It is roughly $113 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $307 (28%) lower, and is about $170 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
