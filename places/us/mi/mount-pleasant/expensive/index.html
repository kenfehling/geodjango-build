In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mount Pleasant is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant is valued at $137,700. It is roughly $28,740 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $79,800 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $5,700 (3%) lower.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median monthly rent is $755. Compared to nearby areas it is about $113 (17%) higher.It is approximately $307 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $170 (18%) lower.
