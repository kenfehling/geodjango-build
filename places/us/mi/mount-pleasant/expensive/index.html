Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant costs $137,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $28,740 (26%) higher.It is approximately $79,800 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $5,700 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Mount Pleasant the median rent is $755 per month. It is roughly $113 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $307 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $170 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
