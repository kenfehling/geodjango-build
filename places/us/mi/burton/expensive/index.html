Compared to nearby areas, Burton is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Burton is $87,600. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $20,060 (18%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $129,900 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $38,400 (78%) higher.

Rents

In Burton the median rent is $791 a month. This is about $19 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $271 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $33 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
