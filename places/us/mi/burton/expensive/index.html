Compared to its surrounding areas, Burton is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Burton's median home value is $87,600. This is roughly $20,060 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $129,900 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $38,400 (78%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burton is $791. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $19 (2%) higher. It is roughly $271 (25%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $33 (4%) lower.
