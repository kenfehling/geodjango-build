Compared to other places in this area, Burton is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Burton costs $87,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $20,060 (18%) lower. It is about $129,900 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $38,400 (78%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Burton the median monthly rent is $791. This is approximately $19 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $271 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $33 (4%) lower.
