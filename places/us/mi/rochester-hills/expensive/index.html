Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rochester Hills is $295,000. This is about $84,980 (40%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $77,500 (35%) higher, and it is about $245,800 (499%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Rochester Hills is $1,287 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $272 (26%) higher.It is about $225 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $463 (56%) higher.
