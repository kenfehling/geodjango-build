In comparison to the surrounding areas, Rochester Hills is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rochester Hills is $295,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $84,980 (40%) higher. It is about $77,500 (35%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $245,800 (499%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester Hills is $1,287. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $272 (26%) higher. It is approximately $225 (21%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $463 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
