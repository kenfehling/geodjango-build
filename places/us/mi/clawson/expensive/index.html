Home prices

The median home in Clawson is valued at $177,500. It is roughly $100,660 (36%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $40,000 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $128,300 (260%) higher.

Rents

Clawson's median rent is $933 a month. It is roughly $245 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $129 (12%) lower, and is about $109 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
