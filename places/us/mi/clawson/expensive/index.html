In comparison to the surrounding areas, Clawson is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home price in Clawson is $177,500. This is about $100,660 (36%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $40,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $128,300 (260%) higher.

Rents

In Clawson the median monthly rent is $933. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $245 (20%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $129 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $109 (13%) higher.
