Home prices

The median home in Beechwood is valued at $156,400. This is approximately $82,220 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $61,100 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $13,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Beechwood is $894 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $113 (14%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $168 (15%) lower, and is roughly $31 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
