Compared to other places in this area, Beechwood is significantly less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Beechwood costs $156,400. This is approximately $82,220 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $61,100 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $13,000 (9%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Beechwood is $894 per month. This is approximately $113 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $168 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $31 (3%) lower.
