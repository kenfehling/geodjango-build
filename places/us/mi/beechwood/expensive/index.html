In comparison to its surrounding areas, Beechwood is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Beechwood is $156,400. It is approximately $82,220 (34%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $61,100 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $13,000 (9%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beechwood is $894. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $113 (14%) higher. It is roughly $168 (15%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is about $31 (3%) lower.
