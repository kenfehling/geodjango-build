Home prices

The price of the median home in Marysville is $133,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $1,560 (1%) lower. It is approximately $83,900 (38%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $84,400 (171%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Marysville is $834 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $89 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and is roughly $10 (1%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
