Home prices

The median home value in Marysville is $133,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $1,560 (1%) lower.It is roughly $83,900 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $84,400 (171%) higher.

Rents

Marysville's median rent is $834 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $89 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $228 (21%) lower, and is approximately $10 (1%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
