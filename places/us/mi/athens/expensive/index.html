Home prices

The median home in Athens is valued at $86,600. It is about $14,040 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $130,900 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $28,500 (24%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Athens's median rent is $800 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $142 (21%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $262 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $36 (4%) higher.
