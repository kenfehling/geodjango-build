Home prices

The median home in Plymouth is valued at $309,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $116,300 (60%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $91,800 (42%) higher, and it is about $260,100 (528%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Plymouth the median monthly rent is $871. This is roughly $161 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $191 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $47 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
