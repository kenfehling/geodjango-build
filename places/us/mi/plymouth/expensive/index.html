The median home in Plymouth is valued at $309,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $116,300 (60%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $91,800 (42%) higher, and is about $260,100 (528%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Plymouth the median rent is $871 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $161 (15%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $191 (17%) lower, and is roughly $47 (5%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
