Rents

Edmore's median rent is $596 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $94 (13%) lower. It is approximately $466 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $329 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
