In comparison to other places in this area, Cheboygan is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cheboygan is valued at $80,600. It is approximately $101,460 (55%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $136,900 (62%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $11,900 (12%) lower.

Rents

Cheboygan's median monthly rent is $719. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $59 (8%) higher.It is approximately $343 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $21 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
