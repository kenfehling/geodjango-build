In comparison to its surrounding areas, Milford is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milford is valued at $256,700. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $35,220 (15%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $39,200 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $207,500 (421%) higher.

Rents

Milford's median rent is $782 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $250 (24%) lower. It is roughly $280 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $42 (5%) lower.
