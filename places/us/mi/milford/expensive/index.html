Home prices

The median home value in Milford is $256,700. This is about $35,220 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $39,200 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $207,500 (421%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milford is $782. This is approximately $250 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and is about $42 (5%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
