Rents

Boyne Falls's median rent is $725 per month. This is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is about $27 (3%) higher.
