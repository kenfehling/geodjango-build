Compared to other places in this area, Boyne Falls is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Boyne Falls's median home price is $105,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24,260 (18%) lower. It is approximately $111,700 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is approximately $13,300 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Boyne Falls the median rent is $725 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $10 (1%) lower. It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is approximately $27 (3%) higher.
