In comparison to its nearby areas, Boyne Falls is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Boyne Falls is $105,800. This is approximately $24,260 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $111,700 (51%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $13,300 (14%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

In Boyne Falls the median rent is $725 a month. It is roughly $10 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $27 (3%) higher.
