Home prices

The cost of the median home in Boyne Falls is $105,800. It is approximately $24,260 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $111,700 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $13,300 (14%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

In Boyne Falls the median rent is $725 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $27 (3%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
