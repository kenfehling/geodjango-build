Home prices

The cost of the median home in Carson City is $84,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $4,020 (4%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $132,900 (61%) lower, and it is about $58,800 (41%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Carson City's median rent is $625 per month. This is about $31 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $437 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $300 (32%) lower.
