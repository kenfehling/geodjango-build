Home prices

The median home in Quinnesec costs $92,100. This is approximately $9,700 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $125,400 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $43,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

Quinnesec's median monthly rent is $783. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $187 (31%) higher. It is roughly $279 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Green Bay it is about $53 (7%) higher.
