Home prices

Byron Center's median home cost is $246,700. This is about $94,200 (61%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $29,200 (13%) higher, and it is roughly $103,300 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Byron Center is $844. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $43 (4%) lower. It is roughly $218 (20%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $81 (8%) lower.
