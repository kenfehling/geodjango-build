Home prices

The median home value in Dearborn is $143,000. This is approximately $65,620 (84%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $74,500 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $93,800 (190%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Dearborn the median monthly rent is $1,022. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $151 (17%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $40 (3%) lower, and it is approximately $198 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
