Home prices

The median home in Dearborn is valued at $143,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $65,620 (84%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $74,500 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $93,800 (190%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dearborn is $1,022. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $151 (17%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $40 (3%) lower, and it is about $198 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
