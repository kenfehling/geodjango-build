Home prices

The median home in Dearborn is valued at $143,000. It is about $65,620 (84%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $74,500 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $93,800 (190%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Dearborn is $1,022 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $151 (17%) higher. It is roughly $40 (3%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $198 (24%) higher.
