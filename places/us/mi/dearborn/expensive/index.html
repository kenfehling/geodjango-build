Home prices

The median home in Dearborn is valued at $143,000. It is approximately $65,620 (84%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $74,500 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $93,800 (190%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Dearborn the median monthly rent is $1,022. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $151 (17%) higher.It is about $40 (3%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $198 (24%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
