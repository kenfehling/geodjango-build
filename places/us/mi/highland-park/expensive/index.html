Compared to nearby areas, Highland Park is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Highland Park is $45,700. This is roughly $108,120 (70%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $171,800 (78%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $3,500 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Highland Park is $596. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $465 (43%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $466 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $228 (27%) lower.
