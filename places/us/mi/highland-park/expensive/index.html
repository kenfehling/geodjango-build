In comparison to nearby areas, Highland Park is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Highland Park costs $45,700. This is about $108,120 (70%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $171,800 (78%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $3,500 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Highland Park the median rent is $596 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $465 (43%) lower. It is roughly $466 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $228 (27%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
