Compared to the surrounding areas, Brooklyn is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brooklyn costs $129,400. It is approximately $35,400 (37%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $88,100 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $80,200 (163%) higher.

Rents

Brooklyn's median monthly rent is $645. It is approximately $56 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $417 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $179 (21%) lower.
