Compared to the surrounding areas, Brooklyn is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Brooklyn costs $129,400. It is about $35,400 (37%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $88,100 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $80,200 (163%) higher.

Rents

In Brooklyn the median monthly rent is $645. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $56 (7%) lower.It is about $417 (39%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $179 (21%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
