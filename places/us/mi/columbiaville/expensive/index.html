In comparison to the surrounding areas, Columbiaville is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Columbiaville is $83,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $12,040 (12%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $133,900 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $34,400 (69%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Columbiaville is $902 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $127 (16%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $160 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $78 (9%) higher.
