Home prices

The median home price in Columbiaville is $83,600. It is approximately $12,040 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $133,900 (61%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $34,400 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Columbiaville the median monthly rent is $902. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $127 (16%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $160 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $78 (9%) higher.
