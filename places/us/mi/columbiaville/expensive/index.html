In comparison to its surrounding areas, Columbiaville is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Columbiaville is $83,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $12,040 (12%) lower. It is about $133,900 (61%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $34,400 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Columbiaville's median monthly rent is $902. It is roughly $127 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $160 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $78 (9%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
