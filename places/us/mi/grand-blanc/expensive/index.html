Compared to its surrounding areas, Grand Blanc is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Grand Blanc costs $171,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $37,660 (28%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $46,400 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $121,900 (247%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Blanc is $691 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $177 (20%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $371 (34%) lower, and is about $133 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
