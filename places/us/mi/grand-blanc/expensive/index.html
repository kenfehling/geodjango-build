Home prices

The value of the median home in Grand Blanc is $171,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $37,660 (28%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $46,400 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $121,900 (247%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Blanc is $691 a month. This is about $177 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $371 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $133 (16%) lower.
