Home prices

Grand Blanc's median home cost is $171,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $37,660 (28%) higher.It is approximately $46,400 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $121,900 (247%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grand Blanc is $691. This is about $177 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $371 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $133 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
