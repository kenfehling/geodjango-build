Home prices

The cost of the median home in Grand Blanc is $171,100. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $37,660 (28%) higher. It is approximately $46,400 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $121,900 (247%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grand Blanc is $691. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $177 (20%) lower. It is approximately $371 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $133 (16%) lower.
