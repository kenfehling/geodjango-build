Compared to its surrounding areas, Grand Blanc is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Grand Blanc is $171,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $37,660 (28%) higher. It is roughly $46,400 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $121,900 (247%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grand Blanc is $691. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $177 (20%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $371 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $133 (16%) lower.
