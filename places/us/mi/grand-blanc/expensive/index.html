Compared to its surrounding areas, Grand Blanc is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Grand Blanc costs $171,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $37,660 (28%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $46,400 (21%) lower, and it is about $121,900 (247%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Blanc is $691 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $177 (20%) lower.It is about $371 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $133 (16%) lower.
