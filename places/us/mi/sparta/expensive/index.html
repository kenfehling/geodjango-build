Compared to other places in this area, Sparta is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

Sparta's median home value is $119,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $18,020 (13%) lower.It is about $97,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $23,700 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sparta is $764. Compared to nearby areas it is about $53 (6%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $298 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $161 (17%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
