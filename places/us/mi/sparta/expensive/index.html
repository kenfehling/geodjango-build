Home prices

The median home in Sparta costs $119,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $18,020 (13%) lower.It is roughly $97,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $23,700 (16%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Sparta the median rent is $764 per month. It is about $53 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $298 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $161 (17%) lower.
