Compared to other places in this area, Sparta is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sparta is $119,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $18,020 (13%) lower. It is approximately $97,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $23,700 (16%) lower.

Rents

Sparta's median rent is $764 a month. It is approximately $53 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $298 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $161 (17%) lower.
