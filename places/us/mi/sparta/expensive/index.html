Rents

Sparta's median rent is $764 per month. It is approximately $53 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $298 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $161 (17%) lower.
