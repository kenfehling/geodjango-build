Home prices

The median home value in Marion is $76,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $9,700 (11%) lower. It is about $140,700 (64%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $66,600 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marion is $527. It is roughly $105 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $535 (50%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $398 (43%) lower.
