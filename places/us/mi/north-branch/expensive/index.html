Home prices

The median home in North Branch is valued at $77,800. This is about $5,820 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $139,700 (64%) lower, and is about $28,600 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

North Branch's median monthly rent is $732. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $53 (7%) higher.It is about $330 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $92 (11%) lower.
