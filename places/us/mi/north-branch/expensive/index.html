In comparison to its surrounding areas, North Branch is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

North Branch's median home cost is $77,800. This is roughly $5,820 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $139,700 (64%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $28,600 (58%) higher.

Rents

North Branch's median monthly rent is $732. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $53 (7%) higher. It is roughly $330 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $92 (11%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
