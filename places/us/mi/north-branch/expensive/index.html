In comparison to its surrounding areas, North Branch is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in North Branch is $77,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $5,820 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $139,700 (64%) lower, and is about $28,600 (58%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

North Branch's median monthly rent is $732. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $53 (7%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $330 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $92 (11%) lower.
