In comparison to its surrounding areas, Harrisville is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Saginaw.

Home prices

The median home in Harrisville is valued at $98,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $13,980 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $119,400 (54%) lower, and it is roughly $58,000 (144%) higher in comparison to the city of Saginaw.

Rents

In Harrisville the median monthly rent is $520. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $114 (18%) lower.It is approximately $542 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Saginaw is approximately $214 (29%) lower.
