Compared to the surrounding areas, Emmett is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home value in Emmett is $121,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $7,280 (5%) lower. It is approximately $96,200 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $72,100 (146%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Emmett is $625. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $112 (15%) lower. It is roughly $437 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $199 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
