Compared to the surrounding areas, Emmett is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Emmett is $121,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $7,280 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $96,200 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $72,100 (146%) higher.

Rents

In Emmett the median rent is $625 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $112 (15%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $437 (41%) lower, and it is about $199 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
