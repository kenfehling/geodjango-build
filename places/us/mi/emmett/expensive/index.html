Compared to the surrounding areas, Emmett is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Emmett costs $121,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $7,280 (5%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $96,200 (44%) lower, and it is roughly $72,100 (146%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Emmett is $625 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $112 (15%) lower.It is approximately $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $199 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
