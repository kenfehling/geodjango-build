Compared to nearby areas, Rapid City is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Mount Pleasant.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rapid City is $138,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $15,260 (12%) higher.It is roughly $79,100 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is is very similar compared to the city of Mount Pleasant.

Rents

In Rapid City the median rent is $729 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $24 (3%) lower.It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Mount Pleasant is approximately $26 (3%) lower.
