Home prices

Rents

In Rapid City the median monthly rent is $729. This is about $24 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $26 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Mount Pleasant.
