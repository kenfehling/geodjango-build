Compared to its nearby areas, Rapid City is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Mount Pleasant.

Home prices

Rapid City's median home cost is $138,400. This is approximately $15,260 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $79,100 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mount Pleasant is is very similar.

Rents

In Rapid City the median monthly rent is $729. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $333 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Mount Pleasant it is approximately $26 (3%) lower.
