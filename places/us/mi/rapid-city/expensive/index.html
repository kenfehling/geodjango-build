Home prices

The median home in Rapid City is valued at $138,400. This is about $15,260 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $79,100 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mount Pleasant it is is very similar.

Rents

Rapid City's median rent is $729 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $333 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mount Pleasant is about $26 (3%) lower.
