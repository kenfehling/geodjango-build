Home prices

The price of the median home in Sunfield is $103,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $2,260 (2%) lower. It is approximately $113,700 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $39,600 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Sunfield the median monthly rent is $620. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $138 (18%) lower. It is roughly $442 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $305 (32%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
