Home prices

The median home in Oxford costs $210,000. This is roughly $13,460 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $7,500 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $160,800 (326%) higher.

Rents

Oxford's median rent is $832 per month. This is roughly $99 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $230 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Detroit.
