Norton Shores's median home cost is $140,400. This is roughly $31,300 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $77,100 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $3,000 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents
