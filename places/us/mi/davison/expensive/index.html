Home prices

The median home in Davison costs $116,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $1,780 (1%) lower.It is roughly $100,900 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $67,400 (136%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Davison the median monthly rent is $736. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $50 (6%) lower.It is about $326 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $88 (10%) lower.
