Compared to the surrounding areas, Inkster is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Inkster costs $48,700. This is about $66,100 (57%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $168,800 (77%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $500 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Inkster is $887 per month. This is about $64 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $175 (16%) lower, and is roughly $63 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
