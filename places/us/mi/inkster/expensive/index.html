Home prices

The median home in Inkster is valued at $48,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $66,100 (57%) lower. It is roughly $168,800 (77%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $500 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Inkster the median rent is $887 a month. It is approximately $64 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $175 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $63 (7%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
