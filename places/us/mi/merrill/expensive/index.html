Home prices

The median home in Merrill is valued at $79,300. This is roughly $11,540 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $138,200 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Ann Arbor is approximately $244,100 (75%) lower.

Rents

In Merrill the median rent is $711 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $35 (5%) higher. It is about $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Ann Arbor it is roughly $526 (42%) lower.
