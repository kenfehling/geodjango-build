Home prices

The value of the median home in Bellevue is $81,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $4,680 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $135,900 (62%) lower, and it is roughly $33,500 (29%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Bellevue the median monthly rent is $742. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is about $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $22 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
