In comparison to nearby areas, Kalamazoo is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kalamazoo costs $106,600. This is approximately $22,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $110,900 (50%) lower, and it is about $8,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Kalamazoo the median monthly rent is $795. It is roughly $44 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $31 (4%) higher.
