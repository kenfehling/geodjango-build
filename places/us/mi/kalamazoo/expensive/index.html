Compared to the surrounding areas, Kalamazoo is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kalamazoo is valued at $106,600. This is approximately $22,100 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $110,900 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $8,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Kalamazoo's median rent is $795 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $44 (5%) lower. It is about $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $31 (4%) higher.
