Home prices

The median home in Kalamazoo is valued at $106,600. It is roughly $22,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $110,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $8,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Kalamazoo is $795 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $44 (5%) lower.It is about $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $31 (4%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
