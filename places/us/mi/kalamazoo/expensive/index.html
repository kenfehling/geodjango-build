In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kalamazoo is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Kalamazoo costs $106,600. This is approximately $22,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $110,900 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $8,500 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kalamazoo is $795. It is approximately $44 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $267 (25%) lower, and is approximately $31 (4%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
