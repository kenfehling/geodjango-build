Compared to other places in this area, Kalamazoo is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Kalamazoo is $106,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $22,100 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $110,900 (50%) lower, and is about $8,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Kalamazoo is $795 per month. This is approximately $44 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $31 (4%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
