Home prices

The median home value in Portage is $164,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $47,920 (41%) higher. It is about $53,200 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $49,200 (42%) higher.

Rents

In Portage the median rent is $839 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $39 (4%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $223 (20%) lower, and is roughly $75 (9%) higher compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
