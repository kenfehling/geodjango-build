Compared to the surrounding areas, Walker is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Walker is valued at $174,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $22,640 (11%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $42,800 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $31,300 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Walker the median rent is $850 per month. It is approximately $138 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $75 (8%) lower.
