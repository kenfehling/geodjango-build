Home prices

The median home value in Walker is $174,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,640 (11%) lower. It is about $42,800 (19%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $31,300 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Walker is $850. This is about $138 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and is approximately $75 (8%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
