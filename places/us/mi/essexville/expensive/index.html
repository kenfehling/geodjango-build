Home prices

The median home in Essexville costs $92,400. This is about $9,900 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $125,100 (57%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $43,200 (87%) higher.

Rents

Essexville's median monthly rent is $936. This is about $203 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $126 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $112 (13%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
