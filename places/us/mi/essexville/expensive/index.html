In comparison to its surrounding areas, Essexville is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Essexville's median home price is $92,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $9,900 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $125,100 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $43,200 (87%) higher.

Rents

In Essexville the median rent is $936 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $203 (27%) higher. It is about $126 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $112 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
