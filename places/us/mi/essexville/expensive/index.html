Compared to nearby areas, Essexville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Essexville is valued at $92,400. This is about $9,900 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $125,100 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $43,200 (87%) higher.

Rents

Essexville's median rent is $936 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $203 (27%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $126 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $112 (13%) higher.
