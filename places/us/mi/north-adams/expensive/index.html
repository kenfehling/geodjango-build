In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Adams is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Toledo.

Home prices

The median home cost in North Adams is $78,200. It is approximately $12,580 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $139,300 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $2,600 (3%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In North Adams the median monthly rent is $775. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $114 (17%) higher. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $50 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Toledo.
