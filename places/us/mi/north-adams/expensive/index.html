Compared to nearby areas, North Adams is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Toledo however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in North Adams costs $78,200. This is roughly $12,580 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $139,300 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is approximately $2,600 (3%) lower.

Rents

In North Adams the median monthly rent is $775. This is roughly $114 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $50 (6%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.
