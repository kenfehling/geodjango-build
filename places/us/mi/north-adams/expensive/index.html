Compared to the surrounding areas, North Adams is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Toledo.

Home prices

The median home cost in North Adams is $78,200. It is approximately $12,580 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $139,300 (64%) lower, and is approximately $2,600 (3%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

The median rent in North Adams is $775 per month. It is approximately $114 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $287 (27%) lower, and is roughly $50 (6%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.
