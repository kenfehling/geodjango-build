Home prices

The median home in Grand Haven costs $161,000. Compared to nearby areas it is about $14,100 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $56,500 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $17,600 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Haven is $776 per month. This is roughly $51 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $286 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $149 (16%) lower.
