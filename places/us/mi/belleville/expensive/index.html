Home prices

The price of the median home in Belleville is $148,100. This is about $47,400 (47%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $69,400 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $98,900 (201%) higher.

Rents

Belleville's median rent is $791 a month. This is about $74 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $271 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $33 (4%) lower.
