Home prices

The median home in Caseville costs $96,900. It is about $13,600 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $120,600 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Flint it is approximately $67,400 (228%) higher.

Rents

Caseville's median rent is $515 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $76 (12%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $547 (51%) lower, and it is about $201 (28%) lower compared to the city of Flint.
