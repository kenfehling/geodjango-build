Compared to the surrounding areas, Vernon is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Vernon is $97,200. This is about $14,660 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $120,300 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $48,000 (97%) higher.

Rents

Vernon's median monthly rent is $725. This is about $24 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $99 (12%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
