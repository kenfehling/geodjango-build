Home prices

The median home price in Vernon is $97,200. This is approximately $14,660 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $120,300 (55%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $48,000 (97%) higher.

Rents

In Vernon the median rent is $725 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $24 (3%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $99 (12%) lower.
