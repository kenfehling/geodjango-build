Compared to the surrounding areas, Vernon is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Vernon is valued at $97,200. It is roughly $14,660 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $120,300 (55%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $48,000 (97%) higher.

Rents

In Vernon the median monthly rent is $725. It is about $24 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $99 (12%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
