Home prices

Vernon's median home cost is $97,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $14,660 (17%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $120,300 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $48,000 (97%) higher.

Rents

Vernon's median rent is $725 a month. It is about $24 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $99 (12%) lower.
