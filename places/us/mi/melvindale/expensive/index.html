In comparison to its surrounding areas, Melvindale is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Melvindale costs $64,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $19,480 (23%) lower. It is approximately $153,200 (70%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $15,100 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Melvindale is $856. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $32 (3%) higher.
