Home prices

The median home in Melvindale costs $64,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $19,480 (23%) lower.It is roughly $153,200 (70%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $15,100 (30%) higher.

Rents

Melvindale's median rent is $856 per month. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $32 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
