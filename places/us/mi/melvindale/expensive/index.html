Home prices

The median home value in Melvindale is $64,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $19,480 (23%) lower. It is approximately $153,200 (70%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $15,100 (30%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Melvindale is $856 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $206 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $32 (3%) higher.
