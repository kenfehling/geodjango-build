Much like the surrounding areas, it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dearborn Heights costs $113,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $4,280 (3%) higher.It is approximately $103,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $64,600 (131%) higher.

Rents

In Dearborn Heights the median monthly rent is $1,047. This is roughly $83 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $15 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $223 (27%) higher.
