Similar to nearby areas, it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dearborn Heights is $113,800. This is approximately $4,280 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $103,700 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $64,600 (131%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Dearborn Heights the median monthly rent is $1,047. Compared to other places in this area this is about $83 (8%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $15 (1%) lower, and it is approximately $223 (27%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
