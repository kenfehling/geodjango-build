Similar to its surrounding areas, it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dearborn Heights is $113,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $4,280 (3%) higher.It is approximately $103,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $64,600 (131%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Dearborn Heights's median monthly rent is $1,047. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $83 (8%) higher.It is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $223 (27%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
