Home prices

The median home value in Atlanta is $67,400. This is approximately $11,760 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $150,100 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Midland it is roughly $83,000 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Atlanta the median rent is $550 per month. It is about $70 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $512 (48%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Midland it is approximately $238 (30%) lower.
