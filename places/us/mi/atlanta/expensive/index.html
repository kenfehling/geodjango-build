Compared to its nearby areas, Atlanta is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Midland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Atlanta is $67,400. It is approximately $11,760 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $150,100 (69%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Midland it is approximately $83,000 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Atlanta the median rent is $550 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $70 (11%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $512 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Midland is about $238 (30%) lower.
