In comparison to its surrounding areas, Atlanta is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Midland.

Home prices

Atlanta's median home price is $67,400. This is approximately $11,760 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $150,100 (69%) lower, and is about $83,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Midland.

Rents

In Atlanta the median monthly rent is $550. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $70 (11%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $512 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Midland it is approximately $238 (30%) lower.
