Compared to its surrounding areas, Atlanta is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Midland.

Home prices

The median home in Atlanta is valued at $67,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,760 (14%) lower.It is approximately $150,100 (69%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Midland is roughly $83,000 (55%) lower.

Rents

Atlanta's median monthly rent is $550. It is roughly $70 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $512 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $238 (30%) lower compared to the city of Midland.
