Home prices

The price of the median home in Twining is $62,100. This is approximately $4,520 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $155,400 (71%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Flint it is approximately $32,600 (110%) higher.

Rents

In Twining the median rent is $850 per month. It is approximately $266 (45%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Flint it is roughly $134 (18%) higher.
