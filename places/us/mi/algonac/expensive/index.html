Home prices

The median home in Algonac costs $113,700. It is approximately $57,800 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $103,800 (47%) lower, and is about $64,500 (131%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Algonac the median monthly rent is $680. This is about $161 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $382 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $144 (17%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
