In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gibraltar is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Gibraltar is $158,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $8,860 (5%) higher. It is roughly $59,400 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $108,900 (221%) higher.

Rents

In Gibraltar the median monthly rent is $942. It is approximately $58 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $120 (11%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $118 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
