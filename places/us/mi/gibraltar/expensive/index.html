Compared to its surrounding areas, Gibraltar is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Gibraltar is $158,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $8,860 (5%) higher. It is roughly $59,400 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $108,900 (221%) higher.

Rents

Gibraltar's median rent is $942 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $58 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $120 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $118 (14%) higher.
