Home prices

The cost of the median home in Gibraltar is $158,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $8,860 (5%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $59,400 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $108,900 (221%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Gibraltar is $942 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $58 (6%) higher.It is about $120 (11%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $118 (14%) higher.
