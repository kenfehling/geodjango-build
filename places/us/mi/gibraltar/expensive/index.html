Home prices

The value of the median home in Gibraltar is $158,100. This is approximately $8,860 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $59,400 (27%) lower, and it is about $108,900 (221%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Gibraltar is $942 a month. It is roughly $58 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $120 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $118 (14%) higher.
