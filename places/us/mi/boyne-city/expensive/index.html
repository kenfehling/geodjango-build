Compared to its surrounding areas, Boyne City is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Boyne City is $147,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $19,780 (15%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $70,000 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $55,000 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

Boyne City's median monthly rent is $737. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $325 (30%) lower, and is about $39 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
