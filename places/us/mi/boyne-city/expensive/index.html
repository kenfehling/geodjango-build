Home prices

The median home in Boyne City costs $147,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $19,780 (15%) higher.It is roughly $70,000 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $55,000 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

In Boyne City the median rent is $737 per month. This is about $21 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $325 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is approximately $39 (5%) higher.
