Compared to its nearby areas, Boyne City is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Home prices

The median home in Boyne City costs $147,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $19,780 (15%) higher.It is about $70,000 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $55,000 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

In Boyne City the median rent is $737 a month. It is approximately $21 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $325 (30%) lower, and is approximately $39 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
