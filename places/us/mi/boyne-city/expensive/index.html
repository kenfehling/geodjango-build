In comparison to its nearby areas, Boyne City is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Boyne City costs $147,500. It is approximately $19,780 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $70,000 (32%) lower, and is approximately $55,000 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

The median rent in Boyne City is $737 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $21 (2%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $325 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is about $39 (5%) higher.
