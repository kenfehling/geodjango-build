Home prices

The median home in Thompsonville costs $80,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $27,400 (25%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $137,500 (63%) lower, and is approximately $7,000 (9%) higher compared to the city of Muskegon.

Rents

In Thompsonville the median rent is $433 a month. This is roughly $282 (39%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $629 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $273 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Muskegon.
