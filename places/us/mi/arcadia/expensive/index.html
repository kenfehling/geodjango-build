Home prices

The median home price in Arcadia is $162,500. This is about $22,120 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $55,000 (25%) lower, and is roughly $89,500 (122%) higher compared to the city of Muskegon.

Rents

Arcadia's median monthly rent is $810. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $224 (38%) higher. It is about $252 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $104 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Muskegon.
