Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hillman is $83,000. This is about $32,560 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $134,500 (61%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $8,400 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Alpena.

Rents

The median rent in Hillman is $541 a month. This is about $50 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $521 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Alpena is about $79 (12%) lower.
