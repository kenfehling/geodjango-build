Home prices

The median home in Fowlerville costs $128,500. This is about $7,620 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $89,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $79,300 (161%) higher.

Rents

Fowlerville's median monthly rent is $746. It is approximately $81 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $316 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $78 (9%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
