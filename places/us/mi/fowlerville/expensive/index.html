In comparison to other places in this area, Fowlerville is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Fowlerville costs $128,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $7,620 (5%) lower. It is roughly $89,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $79,300 (161%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fowlerville is $746. This is roughly $81 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $316 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $78 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
