Home prices

The median home in Fowlerville is valued at $128,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $7,620 (5%) lower. It is about $89,000 (40%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $79,300 (161%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Fowlerville the median rent is $746 per month. This is approximately $81 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $316 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $78 (9%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
