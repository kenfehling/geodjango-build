Compared to other places in this area, St. Clair Shores is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in St. Clair Shores is $135,800. It is roughly $7,640 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $81,700 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $86,600 (176%) higher.

Rents

St. Clair Shores's median monthly rent is $984. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $91 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $78 (7%) lower, and is about $160 (19%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
