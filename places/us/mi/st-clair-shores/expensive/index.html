Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Clair Shores is $135,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $7,640 (5%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $81,700 (37%) lower, and it is about $86,600 (176%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In St. Clair Shores the median rent is $984 per month. This is approximately $91 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $78 (7%) lower, and is approximately $160 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
