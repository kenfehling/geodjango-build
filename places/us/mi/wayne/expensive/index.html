Compared to its surrounding areas, Wayne is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wayne is $85,100. It is approximately $13,280 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $132,400 (60%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $35,900 (72%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wayne is $759 per month. This is roughly $200 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $303 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $65 (7%) lower.
