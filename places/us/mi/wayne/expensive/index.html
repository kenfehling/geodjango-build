In comparison to its nearby areas, Wayne is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Wayne is $85,100. It is about $13,280 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $132,400 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $35,900 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Wayne's median monthly rent is $759. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $200 (20%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $303 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $65 (7%) lower.
