Compared to other places in this area, Wayne is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wayne costs $85,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $13,280 (13%) lower. It is roughly $132,400 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $35,900 (72%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wayne is $759. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $200 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $303 (28%) lower, and is roughly $65 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
