Home prices

Rents

Sheridan's median rent is $696 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $23 (3%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $366 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $229 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
