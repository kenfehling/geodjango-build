Home prices

The median home in Sheridan is valued at $93,100. It is roughly $6,540 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $124,400 (57%) lower, and is approximately $50,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Sheridan the median rent is $696 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $23 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $366 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $229 (24%) lower.
