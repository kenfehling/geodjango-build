Similar to other places in this area, it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Trenton's median home price is $150,800. It is about $4,720 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $66,700 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $101,600 (206%) higher.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $771 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $86 (10%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $291 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $53 (6%) lower.
