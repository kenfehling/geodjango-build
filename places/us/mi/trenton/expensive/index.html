Much like other places in this area, it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Trenton is valued at $150,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $4,720 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $66,700 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $101,600 (206%) higher.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $771 a month. This is roughly $86 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $291 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $53 (6%) lower.
