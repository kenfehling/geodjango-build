Home prices

The median home cost in Trenton is $150,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $4,720 (3%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $66,700 (30%) lower, and is roughly $101,600 (206%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Trenton's median monthly rent is $771. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $86 (10%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $291 (27%) lower, and is about $53 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
