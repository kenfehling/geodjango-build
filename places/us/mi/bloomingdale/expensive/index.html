Home prices

The median home in Bloomingdale is valued at $72,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $20,320 (21%) lower. It is about $145,400 (66%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $71,300 (49%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Bloomingdale the median monthly rent is $803. This is roughly $139 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $259 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $122 (13%) lower.
