In comparison to other places in this area, South Rockwood is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The price of the median home in South Rockwood is $142,500. This is approximately $11,500 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $75,000 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $93,300 (189%) higher.

Rents

South Rockwood's median rent is $1,102 a month. It is roughly $204 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $40 (3%) higher, and it is about $278 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
