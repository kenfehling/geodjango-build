Home prices

The price of the median home in Lewiston is $107,600. This is approximately $30,160 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $109,900 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Midland is about $42,800 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lewiston is $741 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $137 (22%) higher. It is approximately $321 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Midland is roughly $47 (5%) lower.
