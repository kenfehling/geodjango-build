Compared to its nearby areas, Flint is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Flint is $29,500. It is roughly $53,220 (64%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $188,000 (86%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $19,700 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Flint the median rent is $716 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $34 (4%) lower. It is roughly $346 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $108 (13%) lower.
