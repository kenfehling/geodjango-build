In comparison to nearby areas, Flint is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Flint costs $29,500. Compared to nearby areas it is about $53,220 (64%) lower.It is approximately $188,000 (86%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $19,700 (40%) lower.

Rents

Flint's median rent is $716 a month. It is about $34 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $346 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $108 (13%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
