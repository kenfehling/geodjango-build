Home prices

The price of the median home in Lexington is $120,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $32,720 (37%) higher. It is about $96,900 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $71,400 (145%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Lexington is $525 a month. It is about $142 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $537 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $299 (36%) lower.
