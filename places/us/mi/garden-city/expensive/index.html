Much like the surrounding areas, it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Garden City is valued at $111,000. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $106,500 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $61,800 (125%) higher.

Rents

In Garden City the median rent is $1,035 per month. It is about $102 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $27 (2%) lower, and is roughly $211 (25%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
