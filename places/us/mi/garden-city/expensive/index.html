Much like other places in this area, it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Garden City's median home cost is $111,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is is very similar. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $106,500 (48%) lower, and it is about $61,800 (125%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Garden City's median rent is $1,035 per month. This is approximately $102 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $27 (2%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $211 (25%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
