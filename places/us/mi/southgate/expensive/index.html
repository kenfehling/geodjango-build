Home prices

The median home in Southgate costs $112,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $1,800 (1%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $105,200 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $63,100 (128%) higher.

Rents

Southgate's median monthly rent is $876. It is approximately $24 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $186 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $52 (6%) higher.
