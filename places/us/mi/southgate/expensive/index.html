Much like other places in this area, it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Southgate's median home price is $112,300. It is roughly $1,800 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $105,200 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $63,100 (128%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Southgate is $876. This is approximately $24 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $186 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $52 (6%) higher.
