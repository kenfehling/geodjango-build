Home prices

The median home in Southgate is valued at $112,300. This is approximately $1,800 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $105,200 (48%) lower, and is approximately $63,100 (128%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Southgate the median monthly rent is $876. This is approximately $24 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $186 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $52 (6%) higher.
