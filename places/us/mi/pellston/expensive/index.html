Home prices

The price of the median home in Pellston is $86,000. This is roughly $57,760 (40%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $131,500 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $6,500 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pellston is $850. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $169 (24%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is approximately $152 (21%) higher.
