In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rockwood is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rockwood is valued at $142,300. This is roughly $10,100 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $75,200 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $93,100 (189%) higher.

Rents

Rockwood's median monthly rent is $876. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $22 (2%) lower.It is roughly $186 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $52 (6%) higher.
