Compared to other places in this area, Rockwood is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Rockwood is valued at $142,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $10,100 (6%) lower. It is approximately $75,200 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $93,100 (189%) higher.

Rents

In Rockwood the median rent is $876 per month. It is about $22 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $186 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $52 (6%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
