In comparison to other places in this area, Lyons is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lyons is valued at $68,900. It is roughly $42,340 (38%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $148,600 (68%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $74,500 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Lyons's median monthly rent is $753. This is roughly $63 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $309 (29%) lower, and is roughly $172 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
