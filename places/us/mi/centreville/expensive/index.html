Home prices

The median home in Centreville costs $90,800. It is approximately $6,420 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $126,700 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $24,300 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Centreville the median monthly rent is $668. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $394 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $96 (12%) lower.
