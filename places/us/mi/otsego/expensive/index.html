Home prices

The median home in Otsego is valued at $116,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $2,000 (1%) lower. It is roughly $100,800 (46%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $26,700 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Otsego's median rent is $727 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $28 (3%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $335 (31%) lower, and it is about $198 (21%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
