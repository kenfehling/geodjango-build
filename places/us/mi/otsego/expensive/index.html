Home prices

Otsego's median home price is $116,700. This is roughly $2,000 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $100,800 (46%) lower, and is about $26,700 (18%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Otsego is $727 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $28 (3%) lower.It is roughly $335 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $198 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
