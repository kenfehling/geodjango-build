Much like its surrounding areas, it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Otsego's median home price is $116,700. This is approximately $2,000 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $100,800 (46%) lower, and is approximately $26,700 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Otsego is $727 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $28 (3%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $335 (31%) lower, and is roughly $198 (21%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
