Compared to its surrounding areas, Pontiac is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home price in Pontiac is $67,100. It is about $343,420 (83%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $150,400 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $17,900 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Pontiac's median monthly rent is $815. It is about $859 (51%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $247 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $9 (1%) lower.
