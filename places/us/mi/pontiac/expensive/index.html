In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pontiac is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Pontiac's median home price is $67,100. It is approximately $343,420 (83%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $150,400 (69%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $17,900 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Pontiac is $815 a month. It is about $859 (51%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $247 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $9 (1%) lower.
