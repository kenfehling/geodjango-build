In comparison to its nearby areas, Pontiac is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Pontiac costs $67,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $343,420 (83%) lower.It is approximately $150,400 (69%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $17,900 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Pontiac's median monthly rent is $815. This is approximately $859 (51%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $247 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $9 (1%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
