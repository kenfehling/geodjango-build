Home prices

The price of the median home in Pontiac is $67,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $343,420 (83%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $150,400 (69%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $17,900 (36%) higher.

Rents

Pontiac's median rent is $815 per month. It is roughly $859 (51%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $247 (23%) lower, and is about $9 (1%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
