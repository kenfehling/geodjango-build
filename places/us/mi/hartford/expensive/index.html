Home prices

The median home in Hartford is valued at $69,600. This is about $27,680 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $147,900 (68%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $73,800 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Hartford the median monthly rent is $671. This is approximately $17 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $391 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $254 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
