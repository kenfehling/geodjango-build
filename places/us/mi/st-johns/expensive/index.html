Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Johns is $122,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $2,840 (2%) higher. It is about $94,900 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $20,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

In St. Johns the median rent is $772 a month. It is roughly $41 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $290 (27%) lower, and is about $153 (16%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
