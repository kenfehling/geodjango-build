Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Johns is $122,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $2,840 (2%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $94,900 (43%) lower, and is approximately $20,800 (14%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

St. Johns's median rent is $772 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $41 (5%) higher. It is approximately $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $153 (16%) lower.
