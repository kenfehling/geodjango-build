In comparison to the surrounding areas, Carsonville is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Carsonville is $65,800. It is about $29,820 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $151,700 (69%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $16,600 (33%) higher.

Rents

Carsonville's median rent is $725 a month. It is about $120 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $99 (12%) lower.
