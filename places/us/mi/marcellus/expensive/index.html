Compared to the surrounding areas, Marcellus is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

Marcellus's median home price is $73,900. This is roughly $13,440 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $143,600 (66%) lower, and is about $41,200 (35%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Marcellus is $879 a month. It is approximately $165 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $183 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $115 (15%) higher.
