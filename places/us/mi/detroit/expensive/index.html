Compared to its nearby areas, Detroit is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The median home in Detroit is valued at $49,200. This is roughly $86,600 (63%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $168,300 (77%) lower in comparison to the entire country.

Rents

Detroit's median rent is $824 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $159 (16%) lower.It is about $238 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation.
