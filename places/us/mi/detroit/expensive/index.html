In comparison to the surrounding areas, Detroit is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home value in Detroit is $49,200. This is approximately $86,600 (63%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $168,300 (77%) lower.

Rents

Detroit's median rent is $824 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $159 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $238 (22%) lower.
