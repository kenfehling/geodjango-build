Compared to the surrounding areas, Rochester is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rochester costs $366,100. It is about $170,300 (86%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $148,600 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $316,900 (644%) higher.

Rents

In Rochester the median monthly rent is $1,046. It is approximately $18 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $222 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
