Compared to the surrounding areas, Rochester is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rochester costs $366,100. This is about $170,300 (86%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $148,600 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $316,900 (644%) higher.

Rents

Rochester's median monthly rent is $1,046. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $18 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $16 (1%) lower, and is approximately $222 (26%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
