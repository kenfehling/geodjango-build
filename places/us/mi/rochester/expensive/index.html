Compared to the surrounding areas, Rochester is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rochester is $366,100. It is approximately $170,300 (86%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $148,600 (68%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $316,900 (644%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester is $1,046. Compared to nearby areas this is about $18 (1%) lower.It is about $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $222 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
