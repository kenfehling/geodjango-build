Home prices

The median home in Rochester costs $366,100. It is approximately $170,300 (86%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $148,600 (68%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $316,900 (644%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester is $1,046. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $18 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $16 (1%) lower, and is roughly $222 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
