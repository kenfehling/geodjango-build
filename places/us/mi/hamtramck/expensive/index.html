Compared to its nearby areas, Hamtramck is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hamtramck costs $69,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $75,780 (52%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $147,800 (67%) lower, and is about $20,500 (41%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Hamtramck is $729 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $150 (17%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $333 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $95 (11%) lower.
