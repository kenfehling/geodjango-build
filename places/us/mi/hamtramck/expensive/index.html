In comparison to other places in this area, Hamtramck is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hamtramck is valued at $69,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $75,780 (52%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $147,800 (67%) lower, and is approximately $20,500 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Hamtramck the median rent is $729 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $150 (17%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $333 (31%) lower, and it is about $95 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
