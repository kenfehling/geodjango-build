In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hamtramck is significantly less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hamtramck costs $69,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $75,780 (52%) lower. It is approximately $147,800 (67%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $20,500 (41%) higher.

Rents

Hamtramck's median rent is $729 per month. It is approximately $150 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $95 (11%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
