Home prices

The median home in Hanover is valued at $98,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13,920 (12%) lower.It is about $119,400 (54%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $17,300 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In Hanover the median monthly rent is $775. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $84 (12%) higher.It is about $287 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is about $50 (6%) higher.
