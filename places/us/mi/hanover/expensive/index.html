Home prices

The median home price in Hanover is $98,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $13,920 (12%) lower. It is about $119,400 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Toledo is about $17,300 (21%) higher.

Rents

Hanover's median rent is $775 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $84 (12%) higher. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Toledo is approximately $50 (6%) higher.
