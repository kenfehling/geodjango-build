In comparison to other places in this area, Hanover is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Toledo however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hanover costs $98,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13,920 (12%) lower. It is approximately $119,400 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $17,300 (21%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hanover is $775. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $84 (12%) higher. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is about $50 (6%) higher.
