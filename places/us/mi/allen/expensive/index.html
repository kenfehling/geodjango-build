Compared to other places in this area, Allen is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Toledo, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Allen is $76,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $7,680 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $140,600 (64%) lower, and is approximately $3,900 (4%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

Allen's median rent is $605 per month. This is approximately $64 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $457 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is roughly $120 (16%) lower.
