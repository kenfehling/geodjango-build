Home prices

The median home cost in Central Lake is $84,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $36,300 (30%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $133,100 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Traverse City it is about $181,700 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Central Lake the median monthly rent is $581. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $110 (15%) lower.It is about $481 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Traverse City is about $324 (35%) lower.
