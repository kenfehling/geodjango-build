Home prices

The median home cost in Central Lake is $84,400. This is about $36,300 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $133,100 (61%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Traverse City it is approximately $181,700 (68%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Central Lake is $581 per month. It is about $110 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $481 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $324 (35%) lower compared to the city of Traverse City.
