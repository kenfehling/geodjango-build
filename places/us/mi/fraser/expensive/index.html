Home prices

The median home in Fraser costs $149,100. It is approximately $27,240 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $68,400 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $99,900 (203%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fraser is $775. It is roughly $229 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $49 (5%) lower.
