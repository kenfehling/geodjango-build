In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fraser is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fraser is valued at $149,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $27,240 (22%) higher. It is approximately $68,400 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $99,900 (203%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Fraser's median rent is $775 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $229 (22%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and is roughly $49 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
