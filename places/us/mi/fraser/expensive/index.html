Compared to nearby areas, Fraser is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fraser costs $149,100. It is approximately $27,240 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $68,400 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $99,900 (203%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Fraser the median rent is $775 a month. This is roughly $229 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and it is about $49 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
