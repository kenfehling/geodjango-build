Home prices

The median home cost in Hastings is $111,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13,960 (14%) higher. It is about $106,200 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $32,100 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hastings is $921. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $133 (16%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $141 (13%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
