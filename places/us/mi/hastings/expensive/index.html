Home prices

The median home in Hastings costs $111,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $13,960 (14%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $106,200 (48%) lower, and it is about $32,100 (22%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Hastings the median monthly rent is $921. It is about $133 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $141 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is is very similar.
