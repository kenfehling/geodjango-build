Compared to the surrounding areas, North Muskegon is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in North Muskegon is $157,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $65,120 (70%) higher. It is approximately $60,200 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $13,900 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Muskegon is $844 a month. It is about $49 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $218 (20%) lower, and is about $81 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
