Compared to its surrounding areas, North Muskegon is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in North Muskegon costs $157,300. This is about $65,120 (70%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $60,200 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $13,900 (9%) higher.

Rents

In North Muskegon the median rent is $844 a month. This is roughly $49 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $218 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $81 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
