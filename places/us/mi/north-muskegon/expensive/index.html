Compared to other places in this area, North Muskegon is substantially more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in North Muskegon is $157,300. This is approximately $65,120 (70%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $60,200 (27%) lower, and is about $13,900 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In North Muskegon the median rent is $844 a month. It is approximately $49 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $218 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $81 (8%) lower.
