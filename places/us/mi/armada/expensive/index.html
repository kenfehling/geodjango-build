Home prices

The median home in Armada costs $163,100. It is roughly $7,380 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $54,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $113,900 (231%) higher.

Rents

In Armada the median monthly rent is $808. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $20 (2%) higher.It is about $254 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $16 (1%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
