Home prices

The median home value in Armada is $163,100. It is approximately $7,380 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $54,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $113,900 (231%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Armada the median monthly rent is $808. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $20 (2%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
