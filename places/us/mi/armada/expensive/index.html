Home prices

The median home in Armada costs $163,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $7,380 (4%) higher. It is roughly $54,400 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $113,900 (231%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Armada the median monthly rent is $808. It is roughly $20 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and is roughly $16 (1%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
