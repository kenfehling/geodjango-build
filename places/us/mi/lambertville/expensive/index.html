Home prices

The median home value in Lambertville is $182,300. It is roughly $5,840 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $35,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $133,100 (270%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Lambertville is $1,004 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $173 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $58 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $180 (21%) higher.
