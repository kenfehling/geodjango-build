Home prices

The median home price in Howell is $171,100. This is about $4,800 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $46,400 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $121,900 (247%) higher.

Rents

In Howell the median monthly rent is $915. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $116 (14%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $147 (13%) lower, and is approximately $91 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
