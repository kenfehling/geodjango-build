The median home in Howell is valued at $171,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $4,800 (2%) lower.It is about $46,400 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $121,900 (247%) higher.

Rents

In Howell the median rent is $915 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $116 (14%) higher.It is about $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $91 (11%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
