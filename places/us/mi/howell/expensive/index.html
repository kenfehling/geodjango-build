Home prices

The median home in Howell is valued at $171,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $4,800 (2%) lower. It is approximately $46,400 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $121,900 (247%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Howell is $915. This is roughly $116 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $147 (13%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $91 (11%) higher.
