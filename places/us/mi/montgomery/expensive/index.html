Compared to its nearby areas, Montgomery is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Toledo, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home cost is $61,700. It is approximately $129,920 (67%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $155,800 (71%) lower, and it is approximately $19,100 (23%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $811. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $15 (1%) higher. It is about $251 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $86 (11%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.
