Compared to nearby areas, Montgomery is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Toledo, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $61,700. It is about $129,920 (67%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $155,800 (71%) lower, and it is roughly $19,100 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Toledo.

Rents

Montgomery's median rent is $811 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $15 (1%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $251 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is about $86 (11%) higher.
