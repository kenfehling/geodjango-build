In comparison to nearby areas, Montgomery is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Toledo.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery costs $61,700. This is about $129,920 (67%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $155,800 (71%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is about $19,100 (23%) lower.

Rents

In Montgomery the median rent is $811 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $15 (1%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $251 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Toledo it is about $86 (11%) higher.
