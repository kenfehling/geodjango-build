Home prices

The median home in Marshall is valued at $124,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $58,400 (87%) higher. It is approximately $92,600 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is about $9,800 (8%) higher.

Rents

Marshall's median rent is $732 a month. It is about $40 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $330 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $32 (4%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
