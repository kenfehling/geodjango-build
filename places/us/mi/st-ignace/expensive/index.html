Compared to other places in this area, St. Ignace is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

St. Ignace's median home cost is $113,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $49,500 (30%) lower.It is roughly $104,200 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is roughly $20,800 (22%) higher.

Rents

In St. Ignace the median monthly rent is $605. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $70 (10%) lower.It is about $457 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $93 (13%) lower.
