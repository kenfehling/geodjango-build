Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Ignace is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Ignace is $113,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $49,500 (30%) lower.It is roughly $104,200 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $20,800 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Ignace is $605. It is approximately $70 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $457 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $93 (13%) lower compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
