Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Ignace is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Ignace costs $113,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $49,500 (30%) lower. It is roughly $104,200 (47%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $20,800 (22%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

The median rent in St. Ignace is $605 a month. This is approximately $70 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $457 (43%) lower, and is roughly $93 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
