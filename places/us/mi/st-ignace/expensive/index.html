In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Ignace is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in St. Ignace is $113,300. This is roughly $49,500 (30%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $104,200 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $20,800 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

St. Ignace's median rent is $605 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $70 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $457 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is approximately $93 (13%) lower.
