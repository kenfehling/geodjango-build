In comparison to its surrounding areas, Haslett is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Haslett is $198,400. This is about $38,700 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $19,100 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $149,200 (303%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Haslett is $871 a month. This is roughly $28 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $191 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $47 (5%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
