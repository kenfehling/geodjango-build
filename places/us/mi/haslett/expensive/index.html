Compared to the surrounding areas, Haslett is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home price in Haslett is $198,400. This is about $38,700 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $19,100 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $149,200 (303%) higher.

Rents

Haslett's median rent is $871 a month. This is approximately $28 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $191 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $47 (5%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
