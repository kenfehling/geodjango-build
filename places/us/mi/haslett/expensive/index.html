In comparison to other places in this area, Haslett is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home value in Haslett is $198,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $38,700 (24%) higher. It is approximately $19,100 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $149,200 (303%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Haslett's median monthly rent is $871. This is approximately $28 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $191 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $47 (5%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
