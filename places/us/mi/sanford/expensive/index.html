Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sanford is $122,700. This is roughly $7,540 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $94,800 (43%) lower, and is about $39,200 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Lansing.

Rents

In Sanford the median monthly rent is $775. This is approximately $26 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Lansing is roughly $44 (5%) lower.
