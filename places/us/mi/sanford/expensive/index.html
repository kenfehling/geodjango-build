Home prices

The median home cost in Sanford is $122,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $7,540 (6%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $94,800 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $39,200 (46%) higher compared to the city of Lansing.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sanford is $775. It is approximately $26 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $44 (5%) lower compared to the city of Lansing.
