Compared to other places in this area, Mackinac Island is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Home prices

The median home in Mackinac Island costs $373,300. This is approximately $262,500 (236%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $155,800 (71%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $280,800 (303%) higher.

Rents

Mackinac Island's median rent is $595 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $82 (12%) lower. It is roughly $467 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $103 (14%) lower compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
