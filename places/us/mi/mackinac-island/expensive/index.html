Home prices

Rents

Mackinac Island's median monthly rent is $595. This is about $82 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $467 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is about $103 (14%) lower.
