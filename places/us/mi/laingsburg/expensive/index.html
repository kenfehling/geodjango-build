Home prices

The median home in Laingsburg is valued at $142,600. It is roughly $29,960 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $74,900 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $93,400 (189%) higher.

Rents

Laingsburg's median rent is $730 per month. It is about $9 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $94 (11%) lower.
