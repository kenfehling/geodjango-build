Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Petoskey is $714. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $80 (10%) lower.It is roughly $348 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is roughly $16 (2%) higher.
