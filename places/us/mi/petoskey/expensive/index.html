Compared to its nearby areas, Petoskey is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Petoskey is $177,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $30,280 (20%) higher.It is about $40,500 (18%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $84,500 (91%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

In Petoskey the median rent is $714 per month. This is roughly $80 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $348 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $16 (2%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
