Compared to the surrounding areas, Petoskey is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Petoskey is $177,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $30,280 (20%) higher. It is approximately $40,500 (18%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is about $84,500 (91%) higher.

Rents

In Petoskey the median monthly rent is $714. Compared to nearby areas this is about $80 (10%) lower. It is approximately $348 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $16 (2%) higher.
