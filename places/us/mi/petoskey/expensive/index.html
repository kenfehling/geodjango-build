Compared to its nearby areas, Petoskey is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Petoskey is $177,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $30,280 (20%) higher. It is roughly $40,500 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is roughly $84,500 (91%) higher.

Rents

Petoskey's median monthly rent is $714. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $80 (10%) lower. It is about $348 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $16 (2%) higher.
