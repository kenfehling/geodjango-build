Compared to its surrounding areas, Petoskey is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Petoskey is $177,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $30,280 (20%) higher.It is roughly $40,500 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $84,500 (91%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

Petoskey's median rent is $714 per month. This is roughly $80 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $348 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $16 (2%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
