Compared to its nearby areas, Ann Arbor is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ann Arbor is valued at $323,400. This is approximately $117,520 (57%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $105,900 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $274,200 (557%) higher.

Rents

Ann Arbor's median monthly rent is $1,237. This is roughly $278 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $175 (16%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $413 (50%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
