Compared to its surrounding areas, Ann Arbor is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Ann Arbor's median home value is $323,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $117,520 (57%) higher. It is approximately $105,900 (48%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $274,200 (557%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Ann Arbor the median rent is $1,237 per month. This is roughly $278 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $175 (16%) higher, and it is approximately $413 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
