In comparison to nearby areas, Ann Arbor is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Ann Arbor's median home value is $323,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $117,520 (57%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $105,900 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $274,200 (557%) higher.

Rents

In Ann Arbor the median rent is $1,237 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $278 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $175 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $413 (50%) higher.
