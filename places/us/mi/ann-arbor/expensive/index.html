In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ann Arbor is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ann Arbor is $323,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $117,520 (57%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $105,900 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $274,200 (557%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Ann Arbor's median monthly rent is $1,237. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $278 (28%) higher.It is about $175 (16%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $413 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
