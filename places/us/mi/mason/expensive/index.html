In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mason is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mason costs $135,000. It is approximately $24,500 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $82,500 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $85,800 (174%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Mason the median rent is $962 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $37 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $100 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $138 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
