Compared to the surrounding areas, Mason is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Mason is $135,000. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $24,500 (15%) lower. It is about $82,500 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $85,800 (174%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Mason the median monthly rent is $962. It is approximately $37 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $100 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $138 (16%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
