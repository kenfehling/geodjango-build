Compared to its surrounding areas, Mason is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Mason's median home cost is $135,000. It is roughly $24,500 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $82,500 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $85,800 (174%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Mason the median rent is $962 per month. It is approximately $37 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $100 (9%) lower, and it is about $138 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
