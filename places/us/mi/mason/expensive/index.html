Home prices

Mason's median home cost is $135,000. It is roughly $24,500 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $82,500 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $85,800 (174%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Mason's median rent is $962 a month. It is roughly $37 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $100 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $138 (16%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
