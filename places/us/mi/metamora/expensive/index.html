Home prices

Rents

In Metamora the median rent is $940 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $29 (3%) higher.It is roughly $122 (11%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $116 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
