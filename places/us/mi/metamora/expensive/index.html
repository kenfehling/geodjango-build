Home prices

The median home in Metamora costs $162,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $4,760 (3%) higher. It is about $55,000 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $113,300 (230%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Metamora the median monthly rent is $940. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $29 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $122 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $116 (14%) higher.
