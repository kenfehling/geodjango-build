Home prices

The cost of the median home in Metamora is $162,500. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $4,760 (3%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $55,000 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $113,300 (230%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Metamora is $940 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $29 (3%) higher. It is about $122 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $116 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
