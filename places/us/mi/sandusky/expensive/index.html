In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sandusky is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sandusky is valued at $82,400. It is roughly $7,000 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $135,100 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $33,200 (67%) higher.

Rents

In Sandusky the median rent is $566 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $111 (16%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $496 (46%) lower, and is roughly $258 (31%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
