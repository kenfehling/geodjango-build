Compared to its nearby areas, Sandusky is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sandusky costs $82,400. It is about $7,000 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $135,100 (62%) lower, and is roughly $33,200 (67%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Sandusky is $566 a month. This is about $111 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $496 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $258 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
