Home prices

Rents

Dimondale's median rent is $728 per month. This is roughly $110 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $334 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $197 (21%) lower.
