Compared to other places in this area, Coldwater is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Coldwater is $88,200. This is roughly $10,260 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $129,300 (59%) lower, and it is about $26,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Coldwater's median monthly rent is $740. Compared to nearby areas this is about $92 (14%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $322 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $24 (3%) lower.
