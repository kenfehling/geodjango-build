Home prices

The median home in Lennon costs $97,300. It is about $1,840 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $120,200 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $48,100 (97%) higher.

Rents

Lennon's median rent is $877 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $149 (20%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $185 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $53 (6%) higher.
