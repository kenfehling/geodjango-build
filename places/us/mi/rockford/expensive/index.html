Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rockford is $187,600. It is about $54,920 (41%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $29,900 (13%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $44,200 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rockford is $837. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $49 (6%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $225 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $88 (9%) lower.
