Rents

Rockford's median monthly rent is $837. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $49 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $225 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $88 (9%) lower.
