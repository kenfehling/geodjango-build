Compared to the surrounding areas, Lupton is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Saginaw, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lupton is $47,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17,740 (27%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $170,400 (78%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Saginaw it is roughly $7,000 (17%) higher.

Rents

Lupton's median rent is $721 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $143 (24%) higher. It is roughly $341 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $13 (1%) lower compared to the city of Saginaw.
