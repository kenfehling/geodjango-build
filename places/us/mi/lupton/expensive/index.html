Home prices

The median home in Lupton is valued at $47,100. It is about $17,740 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $170,400 (78%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Saginaw is about $7,000 (17%) higher.

Rents

Lupton's median rent is $721 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $143 (24%) higher. It is roughly $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Saginaw is about $13 (1%) lower.
