Home prices

The median home in Whitmore Lake is valued at $174,800. This is approximately $66,420 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $42,700 (19%) lower, and is roughly $125,600 (255%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Whitmore Lake is $986 per month. It is approximately $65 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $76 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $162 (19%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
