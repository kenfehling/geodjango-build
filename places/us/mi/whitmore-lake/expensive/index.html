Rents

The median rent in Whitmore Lake is $986 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $65 (6%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $76 (7%) lower, and is approximately $162 (19%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
