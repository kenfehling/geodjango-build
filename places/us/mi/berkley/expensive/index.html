Compared to its nearby areas, Berkley is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Berkley is $199,200. This is roughly $50,680 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $18,300 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $150,000 (304%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Berkley the median monthly rent is $1,115. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $263 (19%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $53 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $291 (35%) higher.
