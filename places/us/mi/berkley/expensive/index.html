Compared to other places in this area, Berkley is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Berkley is $199,200. It is about $50,680 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $18,300 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $150,000 (304%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Berkley is $1,115 per month. It is roughly $263 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $53 (4%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $291 (35%) higher.
