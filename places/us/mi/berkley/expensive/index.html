Compared to its surrounding areas, Berkley is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home cost in Berkley is $199,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $50,680 (20%) lower.It is roughly $18,300 (8%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $150,000 (304%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Berkley the median monthly rent is $1,115. This is about $263 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $53 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $291 (35%) higher.
