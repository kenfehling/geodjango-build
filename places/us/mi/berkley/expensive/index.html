Compared to the surrounding areas, Berkley is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Berkley's median home cost is $199,200. It is about $50,680 (20%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $18,300 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $150,000 (304%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Berkley is $1,115. This is roughly $263 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $53 (4%) higher, and is roughly $291 (35%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
