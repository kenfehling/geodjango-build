The median home in Croswell is valued at $82,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $19,480 (19%) lower. It is approximately $135,200 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $33,100 (67%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Croswell's median rent is $611 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $54 (8%) lower. It is approximately $451 (42%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $213 (25%) lower.
