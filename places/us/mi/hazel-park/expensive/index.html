In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hazel Park is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Hazel Park costs $73,600. It is roughly $142,940 (66%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $143,900 (66%) lower, and it is about $24,400 (49%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Hazel Park is $978 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $250 (20%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $84 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $154 (18%) higher.
