Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hazel Park is $73,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $142,940 (66%) lower.It is roughly $143,900 (66%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $24,400 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Hazel Park is $978 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $250 (20%) lower.It is about $84 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $154 (18%) higher.
