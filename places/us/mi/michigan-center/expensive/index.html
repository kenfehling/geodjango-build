Compared to other places in this area, Michigan Center is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Michigan Center costs $117,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $16,520 (16%) higher. It is roughly $99,700 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $68,600 (139%) higher.

Rents

In Michigan Center the median monthly rent is $817. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $68 (9%) higher. It is approximately $245 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Detroit.
