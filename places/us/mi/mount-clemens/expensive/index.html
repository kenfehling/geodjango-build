In comparison to its nearby areas, Mount Clemens is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Mount Clemens's median home value is $103,100. This is approximately $40,040 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $114,400 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $53,900 (109%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Clemens the median rent is $746 per month. This is roughly $183 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $316 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $78 (9%) lower.
