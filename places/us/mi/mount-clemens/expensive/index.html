Compared to its nearby areas, Mount Clemens is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Clemens costs $103,100. It is about $40,040 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $114,400 (52%) lower, and is approximately $53,900 (109%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Mount Clemens the median monthly rent is $746. This is roughly $183 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $316 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $78 (9%) lower.
