In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cutlerville is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Cutlerville is valued at $146,600. This is approximately $67,180 (31%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $70,900 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $3,200 (2%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Cutlerville the median monthly rent is $903. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $86 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $159 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $22 (2%) lower.
