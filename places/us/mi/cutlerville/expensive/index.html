In comparison to other places in this area, Cutlerville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cutlerville is $146,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $67,180 (31%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $70,900 (32%) lower, and is roughly $3,200 (2%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Cutlerville's median monthly rent is $903. It is about $86 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $159 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $22 (2%) lower.
