Home prices

The value of the median home in Lincoln is $76,200. It is roughly $40,260 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $141,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $36,100 (90%) higher compared to the city of Saginaw.

Rents

Lincoln's median rent is $521 per month. It is roughly $113 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $541 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Saginaw is about $213 (29%) lower.
