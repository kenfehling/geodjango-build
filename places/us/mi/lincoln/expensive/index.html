Home prices

The median home price in Lincoln is $76,200. This is approximately $40,260 (34%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $141,300 (64%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $36,100 (90%) higher in comparison to the city of Saginaw.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lincoln is $521. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $113 (17%) lower.It is roughly $541 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Saginaw is approximately $213 (29%) lower.
