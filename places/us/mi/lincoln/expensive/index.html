Compared to other places in this area, Lincoln is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Saginaw however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lincoln is $76,200. It is roughly $40,260 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $141,300 (64%) lower, and is about $36,100 (90%) higher compared to the city of Saginaw.

Rents

The median rent in Lincoln is $521 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $113 (17%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $541 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $213 (29%) lower compared to the city of Saginaw.
