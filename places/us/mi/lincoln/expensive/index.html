Home prices

The median home in Lincoln costs $76,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $40,260 (34%) lower.It is approximately $141,300 (64%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $36,100 (90%) higher in comparison to the city of Saginaw.

Rents

In Lincoln the median rent is $521 per month. This is about $113 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $541 (50%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Saginaw it is roughly $213 (29%) lower.
