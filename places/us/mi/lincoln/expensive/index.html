Compared to the surrounding areas, Lincoln is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Saginawhowever, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lincoln costs $76,200. It is approximately $40,260 (34%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $141,300 (64%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Saginaw it is about $36,100 (90%) higher.

Rents

In Lincoln the median monthly rent is $521. It is approximately $113 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $541 (50%) lower, and is about $213 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Saginaw.
