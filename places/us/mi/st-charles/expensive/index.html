Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Charles is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Charles is valued at $81,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $7,260 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $136,500 (62%) lower, and it is about $31,800 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

St. Charles's median monthly rent is $621. It is about $98 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $441 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $203 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
