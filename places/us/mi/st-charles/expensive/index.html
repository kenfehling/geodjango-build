Home prices

The median home in St. Charles is valued at $81,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $7,260 (8%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $136,500 (62%) lower, and is approximately $31,800 (64%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In St. Charles the median rent is $621 per month. This is roughly $98 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $441 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $203 (24%) lower.
