Home prices

The median home in St. Charles is valued at $81,000. It is roughly $7,260 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $136,500 (62%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $31,800 (64%) higher.

Rents

St. Charles's median rent is $621 a month. It is roughly $98 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $441 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $203 (24%) lower.
