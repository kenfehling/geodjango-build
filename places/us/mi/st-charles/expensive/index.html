In comparison to its surrounding areas, St. Charles is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Charles costs $81,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $7,260 (8%) lower. It is approximately $136,500 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $31,800 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Charles is $621. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $98 (13%) lower. It is roughly $441 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $203 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
