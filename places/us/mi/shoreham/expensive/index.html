Home prices

The value of the median home in Shoreham is $199,400. It is about $93,140 (87%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $18,100 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $58,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Shoreham's median monthly rent is $1,138. This is roughly $389 (51%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $76 (7%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $26 (2%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
