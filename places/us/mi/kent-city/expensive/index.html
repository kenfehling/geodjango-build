In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kent City is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Kent City is $104,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16,220 (13%) lower.It is about $112,800 (51%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $38,700 (26%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Kent City the median rent is $585 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $174 (22%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $477 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $340 (36%) lower.
