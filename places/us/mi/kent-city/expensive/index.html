In comparison to other places in this area, Kent City is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Kent City is $104,700. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $16,220 (13%) lower. It is roughly $112,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $38,700 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Kent City the median rent is $585 a month. This is about $174 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $477 (44%) lower, and it is roughly $340 (36%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
