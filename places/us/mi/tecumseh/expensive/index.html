Home prices

The median home in Tecumseh costs $135,600. It is approximately $16,620 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $81,900 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $86,400 (175%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tecumseh is $772. It is roughly $34 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $290 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $52 (6%) lower.
