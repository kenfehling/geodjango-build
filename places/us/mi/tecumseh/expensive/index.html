Home prices

Tecumseh's median home cost is $135,600. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $16,620 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $81,900 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $86,400 (175%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Tecumseh the median monthly rent is $772. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $34 (4%) higher.It is about $290 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $52 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
