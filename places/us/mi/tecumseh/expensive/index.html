In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tecumseh is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tecumseh is valued at $135,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $16,620 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $81,900 (37%) lower, and is approximately $86,400 (175%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Tecumseh is $772 per month. It is roughly $34 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $52 (6%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
