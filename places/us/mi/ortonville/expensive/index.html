Home prices

Rents

Ortonville's median monthly rent is $918. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $30 (3%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $144 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $94 (11%) higher.
