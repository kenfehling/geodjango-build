Compared to the surrounding areas, Mayville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Mayville is $68,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,000 (12%) lower.It is about $148,900 (68%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $19,400 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Mayville the median monthly rent is $623. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $89 (12%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $439 (41%) lower, and is roughly $201 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
