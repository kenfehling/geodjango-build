In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mayville is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Mayville is valued at $68,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $10,000 (12%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $148,900 (68%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $19,400 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mayville is $623. It is about $89 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $439 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $201 (24%) lower.
