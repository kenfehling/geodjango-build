In comparison to other places in this area, Mayville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mayville is $68,600. This is roughly $10,000 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $148,900 (68%) lower, and is about $19,400 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Mayville is $623 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $89 (12%) lower. It is about $439 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $201 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
