Compared to its surrounding areas, Lapeer is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lapeer is $111,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $11,400 (9%) lower.It is approximately $105,600 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $62,700 (127%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Lapeer the median monthly rent is $750. This is approximately $99 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $74 (8%) lower.
