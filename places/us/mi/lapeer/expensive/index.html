Home prices

Lapeer's median home cost is $111,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $11,400 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $105,600 (48%) lower, and it is about $62,700 (127%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lapeer is $750. It is approximately $99 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $312 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $74 (8%) lower.
