Home prices

The median home in Wyoming costs $127,700. It is roughly $49,280 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $89,800 (41%) lower, and is about $15,700 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Wyoming's median monthly rent is $871. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $191 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $54 (5%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
