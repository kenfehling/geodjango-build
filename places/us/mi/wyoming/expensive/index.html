Home prices

The median home in Wyoming is valued at $127,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $49,280 (27%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $89,800 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is about $15,700 (10%) lower.

Rents

Wyoming's median rent is $871 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is roughly $191 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $54 (5%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
