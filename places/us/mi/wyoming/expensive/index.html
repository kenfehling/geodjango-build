Home prices

The median home in Wyoming costs $127,700. This is about $49,280 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $89,800 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $15,700 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Wyoming the median monthly rent is $871. Compared to its surrounding areas it is is very similar. It is about $191 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is about $54 (5%) lower.
