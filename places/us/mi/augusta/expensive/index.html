Home prices

The cost of the median home in Augusta is $95,400. It is about $75,860 (44%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $122,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $19,700 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Augusta the median monthly rent is $785. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $62 (7%) lower.It is approximately $277 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $21 (2%) higher.
