Home prices

Augusta's median home cost is $95,400. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $75,860 (44%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $122,100 (56%) lower, and is roughly $19,700 (17%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Augusta is $785. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $62 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $277 (26%) lower, and is roughly $21 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
