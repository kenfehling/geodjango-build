Compared to the surrounding areas, Big Rapids is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Big Rapids is valued at $93,900. It is roughly $22,480 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $123,600 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $49,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Big Rapids's median monthly rent is $670. This is about $35 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $392 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $255 (27%) lower.
