Compared to nearby areas, Big Rapids is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Big Rapids is $93,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $22,480 (31%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $123,600 (56%) lower, and is approximately $49,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Big Rapids's median rent is $670 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $35 (4%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $392 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $255 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
