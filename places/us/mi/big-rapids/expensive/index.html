Home prices

Rents

Big Rapids's median rent is $670 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $35 (4%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $392 (36%) lower, and is about $255 (27%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
