In comparison to its surrounding areas, Big Rapids is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Big Rapids is valued at $93,900. It is approximately $22,480 (31%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $123,600 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $49,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Big Rapids is $670 per month. It is approximately $35 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $392 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $255 (27%) lower.
