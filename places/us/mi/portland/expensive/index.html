Compared to its surrounding areas, Portland is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

Portland's median home price is $124,700. It is about $14,940 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $92,800 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $18,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Portland the median monthly rent is $925. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $133 (16%) higher. It is about $137 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
