Home prices

Portland's median home value is $124,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $14,940 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $92,800 (42%) lower, and it is about $18,700 (13%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Portland is $925 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $133 (16%) higher.It is approximately $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is is very similar compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
