In comparison to its nearby areas, Portland is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Portland is valued at $124,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $14,940 (13%) higher. It is roughly $92,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $18,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $925. This is roughly $133 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $137 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is is very similar.
