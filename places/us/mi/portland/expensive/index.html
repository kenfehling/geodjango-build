Compared to nearby areas, Portland is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Portland is valued at $124,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $14,940 (13%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $92,800 (42%) lower, and is about $18,700 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Portland the median rent is $925 per month. This is approximately $133 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
