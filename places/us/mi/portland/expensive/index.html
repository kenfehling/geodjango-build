Home prices

The median home price in Portland is $124,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $14,940 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $92,800 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $18,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $925. It is approximately $133 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $137 (12%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
