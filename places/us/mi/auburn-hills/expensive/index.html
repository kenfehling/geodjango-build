In comparison to its nearby areas, Auburn Hills is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Auburn Hills is valued at $158,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $64,580 (28%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $58,900 (27%) lower, and is roughly $109,400 (222%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Auburn Hills the median rent is $1,117 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $45 (3%) lower.It is roughly $55 (5%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $293 (35%) higher.
