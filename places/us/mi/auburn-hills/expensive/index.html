Compared to its surrounding areas, Auburn Hills is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Auburn Hills's median home cost is $158,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $64,580 (28%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $58,900 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $109,400 (222%) higher.

Rents

In Auburn Hills the median monthly rent is $1,117. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $45 (3%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $55 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $293 (35%) higher.
