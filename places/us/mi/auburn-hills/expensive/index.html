Compared to its surrounding areas, Auburn Hills is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Auburn Hills is valued at $158,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $64,580 (28%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $58,900 (27%) lower, and is approximately $109,400 (222%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Auburn Hills's median monthly rent is $1,117. This is about $45 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $55 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $293 (35%) higher.
