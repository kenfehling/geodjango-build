Home prices

The median home in Lake Odessa is valued at $99,100. This is about $4,100 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $118,400 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $44,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Odessa the median rent is $670 a month. It is roughly $78 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $392 (36%) lower, and it is about $255 (27%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
