Compared to the surrounding areas, Brown City is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Brown City is $70,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $15,300 (17%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $146,600 (67%) lower, and it is roughly $21,700 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Brown City the median monthly rent is $655. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $31 (4%) lower. It is roughly $407 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $169 (20%) lower.
