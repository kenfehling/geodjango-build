Compared to its surrounding areas, Brown City is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brown City costs $70,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $15,300 (17%) lower.It is approximately $146,600 (67%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $21,700 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Brown City the median rent is $655 per month. It is roughly $31 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $407 (38%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $169 (20%) lower.
