Compared to other places in this area, Brown City is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brown City is valued at $70,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $15,300 (17%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $146,600 (67%) lower, and is approximately $21,700 (44%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Brown City the median monthly rent is $655. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $31 (4%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $407 (38%) lower, and it is about $169 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
