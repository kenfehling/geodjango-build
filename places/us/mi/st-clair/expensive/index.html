In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Clair is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Clair costs $157,700. It is about $27,360 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $59,800 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $108,500 (220%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in St. Clair is $739 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $25 (3%) lower. It is roughly $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $85 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
