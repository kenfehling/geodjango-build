Home prices

The median home in Beverly Hills is valued at $387,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $5,740 (1%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $169,900 (78%) higher, and it is about $338,200 (687%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Beverly Hills the median rent is $1,238 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $266 (17%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $176 (16%) higher, and is about $414 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
