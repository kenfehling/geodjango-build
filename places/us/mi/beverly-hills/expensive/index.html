Home prices

The median home in Beverly Hills costs $387,400. This is roughly $5,740 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $169,900 (78%) higher, and it is roughly $338,200 (687%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beverly Hills is $1,238. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $266 (17%) lower.It is approximately $176 (16%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $414 (50%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
