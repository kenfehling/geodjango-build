Home prices

The median home price in Beverly Hills is $387,400. This is about $5,740 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $169,900 (78%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $338,200 (687%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beverly Hills is $1,238. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $266 (17%) lower. It is roughly $176 (16%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $414 (50%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
