In comparison to other places in this area, St. Louis is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in St. Louis is $82,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $6,700 (7%) lower.It is roughly $135,400 (62%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $61,300 (42%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in St. Louis is $682 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $21 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $380 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $243 (26%) lower.
