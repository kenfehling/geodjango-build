Compared to nearby areas, St. Louis is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in St. Louis is $82,100. This is roughly $6,700 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $135,400 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $61,300 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Louis is $682. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21 (3%) higher. It is roughly $380 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $243 (26%) lower.
