Home prices

Rents

St. Louis's median rent is $682 a month. It is roughly $21 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $380 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $243 (26%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
