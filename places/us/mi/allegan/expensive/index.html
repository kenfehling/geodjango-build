Compared to other places in this area, Allegan is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Allegan is $122,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $15,460 (14%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $95,400 (43%) lower, and is roughly $21,300 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Allegan the median monthly rent is $835. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $139 (19%) higher.It is about $227 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $90 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
