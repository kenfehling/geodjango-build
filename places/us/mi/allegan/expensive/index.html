Home prices

The median home in Allegan costs $122,100. This is about $15,460 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $95,400 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $21,300 (14%) lower.

Rents

Allegan's median rent is $835 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $139 (19%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $227 (21%) lower, and is about $90 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
