In comparison to the surrounding areas, Allegan is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Allegan is valued at $122,100. This is roughly $15,460 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $95,400 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $21,300 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Allegan's median rent is $835 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $139 (19%) higher. It is about $227 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is about $90 (9%) lower.
