Home prices

The median home in Allegan costs $122,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $15,460 (14%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $95,400 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $21,300 (14%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Allegan's median rent is $835 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $139 (19%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $227 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $90 (9%) lower.
