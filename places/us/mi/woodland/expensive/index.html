Home prices

The median home value in Woodland is $103,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $13,080 (14%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $114,000 (52%) lower, and it is approximately $39,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Woodland the median rent is $656 per month. It is roughly $74 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $406 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $269 (29%) lower.
