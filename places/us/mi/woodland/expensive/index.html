Home prices

The median home price in Woodland is $103,500. This is roughly $13,080 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $114,000 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is about $39,900 (27%) lower.

Rents

Woodland's median monthly rent is $656. It is approximately $74 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $406 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $269 (29%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
