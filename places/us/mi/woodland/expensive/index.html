Home prices

The median home in Woodland costs $103,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $13,080 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $114,000 (52%) lower, and it is approximately $39,900 (27%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Woodland is $656. It is about $74 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $406 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $269 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
