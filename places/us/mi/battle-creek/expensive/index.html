Much like its nearby areas, it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Battle Creek is valued at $86,500. This is about $2,600 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $131,000 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $28,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Battle Creek the median rent is $754 a month. This is roughly $38 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
