Much like other places in this area, it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Battle Creek is $86,500. This is about $2,600 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $131,000 (60%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $28,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Battle Creek the median rent is $754 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $38 (4%) lower.It is about $308 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
