Home prices

The median home in Battle Creek is valued at $86,500. This is approximately $2,600 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $131,000 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $28,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Battle Creek the median rent is $754 per month. It is roughly $38 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $308 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
