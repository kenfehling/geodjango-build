Home prices

The median home in Battle Creek is valued at $86,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $2,600 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $131,000 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $28,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Battle Creek is $754. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $38 (4%) lower.It is about $308 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
