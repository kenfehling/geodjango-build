Home prices

Battle Creek's median home price is $86,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $2,600 (2%) lower. It is about $131,000 (60%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $28,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

Battle Creek's median monthly rent is $754. It is roughly $38 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $308 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
