Home prices

The median home in Battle Creek is valued at $86,500. This is approximately $2,600 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $131,000 (60%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $28,600 (24%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Battle Creek's median monthly rent is $754. It is roughly $38 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
