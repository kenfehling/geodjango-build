Compared to the surrounding areas, Troy is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Troy costs $301,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $96,900 (24%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $84,000 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $252,300 (512%) higher.

Rents

Troy's median monthly rent is $1,215. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $153 (14%) higher, and is about $391 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
