In comparison to other places in this area, Fenton is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fenton costs $144,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $45,320 (23%) lower. It is about $72,900 (33%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $95,400 (193%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fenton is $1,030. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $219 (26%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $32 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $206 (25%) higher.
