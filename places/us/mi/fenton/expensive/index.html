Compared to the surrounding areas, Fenton is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fenton is valued at $144,600. It is about $45,320 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $72,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $95,400 (193%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Fenton is $1,030 per month. It is about $219 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $32 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $206 (25%) higher.
