Home prices

The price of the median home in Fenton is $144,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $45,320 (23%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $72,900 (33%) lower, and is roughly $95,400 (193%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Fenton is $1,030 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $219 (26%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $32 (3%) lower, and is about $206 (25%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
