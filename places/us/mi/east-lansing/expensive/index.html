In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Lansing is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Lansing is valued at $192,800. It is approximately $31,540 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $24,700 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $143,600 (291%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In East Lansing the median monthly rent is $952. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $102 (12%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $110 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $128 (15%) higher.
