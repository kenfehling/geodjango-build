In comparison to other places in this area, East Lansing is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

East Lansing's median home value is $192,800. It is about $31,540 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $24,700 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $143,600 (291%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

East Lansing's median rent is $952 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $102 (12%) higher. It is roughly $110 (10%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $128 (15%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
