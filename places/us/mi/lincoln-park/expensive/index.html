Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lincoln Park is $73,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $16,660 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $143,800 (66%) lower, and is about $24,500 (49%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Lincoln Park the median rent is $842 per month. It is about $17 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $220 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $18 (2%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
