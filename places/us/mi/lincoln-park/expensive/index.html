Compared to the surrounding areas, Lincoln Park is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Lincoln Park's median home price is $73,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $16,660 (18%) lower. It is roughly $143,800 (66%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $24,500 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lincoln Park is $842. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $17 (2%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $220 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $18 (2%) higher.
