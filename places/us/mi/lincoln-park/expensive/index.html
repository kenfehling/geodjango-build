Compared to nearby areas, Lincoln Park is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Lincoln Park is $73,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $16,660 (18%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $143,800 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $24,500 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lincoln Park is $842 a month. This is approximately $17 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $220 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $18 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
