Compared to its nearby areas, Cement City is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home price in Cement City is $73,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $31,960 (30%) lower. It is about $144,300 (66%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $24,000 (48%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Cement City's median monthly rent is $694. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $39 (6%) higher. It is roughly $368 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $130 (15%) lower.
