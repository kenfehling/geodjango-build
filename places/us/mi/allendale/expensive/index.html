Home prices

The price of the median home in Allendale is $196,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $37,220 (23%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $21,000 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $53,100 (37%) higher.

Rents

Allendale's median monthly rent is $962. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $96 (11%) higher. It is about $100 (9%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $37 (4%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
