Home prices

Rents

In Allendale the median rent is $962 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $96 (11%) higher.It is approximately $100 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $37 (4%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
