In comparison to the surrounding areas, Middletown is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Middletown is valued at $67,800. This is roughly $18,020 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $149,700 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $18,600 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Middletown the median monthly rent is $578. It is roughly $171 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $484 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $246 (29%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
