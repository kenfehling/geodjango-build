In comparison to nearby areas, Middletown is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Middletown is valued at $67,800. This is approximately $18,020 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $149,700 (68%) lower, and it is approximately $18,600 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Middletown the median monthly rent is $578. It is roughly $171 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $484 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $246 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
