Home prices

Onsted's median home price is $128,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $30,840 (31%) higher.It is roughly $89,200 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $79,100 (160%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Onsted's median rent is $653 a month. This is about $21 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $409 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $171 (20%) lower.
