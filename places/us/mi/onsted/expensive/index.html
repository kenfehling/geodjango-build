Home prices

The median home in Onsted is valued at $128,300. This is about $30,840 (31%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $89,200 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $79,100 (160%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Onsted is $653. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $21 (3%) lower.It is approximately $409 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $171 (20%) lower.
