Home prices

The cost of the median home in Onsted is $128,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $30,840 (31%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $89,200 (41%) lower, and is about $79,100 (160%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Onsted is $653. It is approximately $21 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $409 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $171 (20%) lower.
