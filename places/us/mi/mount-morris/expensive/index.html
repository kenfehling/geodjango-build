Home prices

The median home in Mount Morris is valued at $58,000. This is about $5,980 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $159,500 (73%) lower, and it is about $8,800 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Mount Morris's median monthly rent is $697. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $365 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $127 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
