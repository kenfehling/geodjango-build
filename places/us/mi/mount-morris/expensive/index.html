Compared to other places in this area, Mount Morris is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Mount Morris's median home cost is $58,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $5,980 (9%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $159,500 (73%) lower, and it is approximately $8,800 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Mount Morris's median monthly rent is $697. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $365 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $127 (15%) lower.
