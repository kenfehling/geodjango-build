In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mount Morris is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mount Morris is $58,000. This is approximately $5,980 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $159,500 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $8,800 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Morris the median rent is $697 a month. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $127 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
