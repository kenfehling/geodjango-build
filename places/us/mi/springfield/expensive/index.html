Home prices

The cost of the median home in Springfield is $73,700. It is roughly $17,960 (19%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $143,800 (66%) lower, and is about $41,400 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

In Springfield the median monthly rent is $658. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $153 (18%) lower.It is approximately $404 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $106 (13%) lower.
