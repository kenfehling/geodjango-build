Compared to its nearby areas, Springfield is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Springfield is $73,700. This is approximately $17,960 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $143,800 (66%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $41,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Springfield is $658. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $153 (18%) lower. It is approximately $404 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $106 (13%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
