In comparison to the surrounding areas, Springfield is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home cost in Springfield is $73,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $17,960 (19%) lower. It is approximately $143,800 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is roughly $41,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

Springfield's median rent is $658 a month. It is about $153 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $404 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $106 (13%) lower.
