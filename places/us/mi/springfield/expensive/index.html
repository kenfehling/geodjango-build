In comparison to its surrounding areas, Springfield is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

Springfield's median home price is $73,700. This is approximately $17,960 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $143,800 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $41,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

Springfield's median monthly rent is $658. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $153 (18%) lower.It is roughly $404 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne it is about $106 (13%) lower.
