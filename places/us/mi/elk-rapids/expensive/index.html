Compared to its nearby areas, Elk Rapids is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Traverse City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Elk Rapids is $229,200. This is approximately $36,820 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $11,700 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $36,900 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Traverse City.

Rents

Elk Rapids's median rent is $827 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $20 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $235 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Traverse City is roughly $78 (8%) lower.
