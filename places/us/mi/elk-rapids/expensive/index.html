Compared to other places in this area, Elk Rapids is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Traverse City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Elk Rapids costs $229,200. This is about $36,820 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $11,700 (5%) higher, and is roughly $36,900 (13%) lower compared to the city of Traverse City.

Rents

In Elk Rapids the median rent is $827 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $20 (2%) lower.It is roughly $235 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $78 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Traverse City.
