In comparison to its surrounding areas, Elk Rapids is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Traverse City however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Elk Rapids is $229,200. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $36,820 (19%) higher. It is about $11,700 (5%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Traverse City is approximately $36,900 (13%) lower.

Rents

Elk Rapids's median monthly rent is $827. This is approximately $20 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $235 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Traverse City it is approximately $78 (8%) lower.
