In comparison to its surrounding areas, Elk Rapids is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Traverse City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Elk Rapids costs $229,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $36,820 (19%) higher. It is roughly $11,700 (5%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Traverse City it is approximately $36,900 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Elk Rapids is $827 per month. This is approximately $20 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $235 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $78 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Traverse City.
