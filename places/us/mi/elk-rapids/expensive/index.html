Compared to the surrounding areas, Elk Rapids is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Traverse City.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Elk Rapids is $229,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $36,820 (19%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $11,700 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Traverse City it is approximately $36,900 (13%) lower.

Rents

Elk Rapids's median rent is $827 per month. It is approximately $20 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $235 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $78 (8%) lower compared to the city of Traverse City.
