Compared to the surrounding areas, Ferndale is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Ferndale's median home cost is $162,000. It is approximately $62,980 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $55,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $112,800 (229%) higher.

Rents

Ferndale's median rent is $1,032 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $295 (22%) lower. It is roughly $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $208 (25%) higher.
