Home prices

Ferndale's median home value is $162,000. It is about $62,980 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $55,500 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $112,800 (229%) higher.

Rents

Ferndale's median monthly rent is $1,032. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $295 (22%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $30 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $208 (25%) higher.
