In comparison to other places in this area, Ferndale is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Ferndale's median home price is $162,000. It is approximately $62,980 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $55,500 (25%) lower, and is roughly $112,800 (229%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Ferndale's median monthly rent is $1,032. This is roughly $295 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $30 (2%) lower, and it is approximately $208 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
