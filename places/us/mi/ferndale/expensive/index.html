In comparison to its nearby areas, Ferndale is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Ferndale is valued at $162,000. This is roughly $62,980 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $55,500 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $112,800 (229%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ferndale is $1,032 per month. It is roughly $295 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $30 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $208 (25%) higher.
