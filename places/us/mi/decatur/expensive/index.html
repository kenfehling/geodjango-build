Home prices

The median home in Decatur is valued at $83,100. It is about $840 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $134,400 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $32,000 (27%) lower.

Rents

Decatur's median rent is $763 a month. This is roughly $100 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $299 (28%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
