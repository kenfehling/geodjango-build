Home prices

Rents

Owendale's median monthly rent is $542. It is approximately $40 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $520 (48%) lower, and is approximately $282 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
