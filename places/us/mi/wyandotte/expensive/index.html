In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wyandotte is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home value in Wyandotte is $109,300. It is roughly $9,040 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $108,200 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $60,100 (122%) higher.

Rents

In Wyandotte the median rent is $763 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $100 (11%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $61 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
