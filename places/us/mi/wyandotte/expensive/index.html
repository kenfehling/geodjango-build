In comparison to other places in this area, Wyandotte is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wyandotte is $109,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $9,040 (9%) higher. It is about $108,200 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $60,100 (122%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Wyandotte the median rent is $763 per month. It is about $100 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $299 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $61 (7%) lower.
