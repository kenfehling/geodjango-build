Compared to other places in this area, Wyandotte is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wyandotte costs $109,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $9,040 (9%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $108,200 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $60,100 (122%) higher.

Rents

In Wyandotte the median rent is $763 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $100 (11%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $299 (28%) lower, and it is about $61 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
