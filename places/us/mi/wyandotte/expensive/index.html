In comparison to nearby areas, Wyandotte is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wyandotte costs $109,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $9,040 (9%) higher.It is approximately $108,200 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $60,100 (122%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wyandotte is $763. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $100 (11%) lower.It is roughly $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $61 (7%) lower.
