The median home in Britton costs $108,700. It is roughly $27,780 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $108,800 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $59,500 (120%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Britton is $850. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $95 (12%) higher. It is roughly $212 (19%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $26 (3%) higher.
