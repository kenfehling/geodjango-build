Compared to nearby areas, Royal Oak is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Royal Oak costs $224,600. It is about $17,340 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $7,100 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $175,400 (356%) higher.

Rents

In Royal Oak the median monthly rent is $1,079. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $216 (16%) lower. It is approximately $17 (1%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $255 (30%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
