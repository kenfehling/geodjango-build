Compared to nearby areas, Royal Oak is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Royal Oak is $224,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $17,340 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $7,100 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $175,400 (356%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Royal Oak is $1,079. This is roughly $216 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $17 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $255 (30%) higher.
