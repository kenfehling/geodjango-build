Home prices

The cost of the median home in Royal Oak is $224,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $17,340 (7%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $7,100 (3%) higher, and it is about $175,400 (356%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Royal Oak the median rent is $1,079 per month. It is about $216 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $17 (1%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $255 (30%) higher.
