Home prices

The median home in Royal Oak is valued at $224,600. This is roughly $17,340 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $7,100 (3%) higher, and is roughly $175,400 (356%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Royal Oak the median rent is $1,079 per month. It is roughly $216 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $17 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $255 (30%) higher.
