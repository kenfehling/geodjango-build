Compared to its surrounding areas, Royal Oak is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Royal Oak is $224,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $17,340 (7%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $7,100 (3%) higher, and is approximately $175,400 (356%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Royal Oak is $1,079. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $216 (16%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $17 (1%) higher, and it is approximately $255 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
