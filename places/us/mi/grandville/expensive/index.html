The median home in Grandville is valued at $161,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $6,720 (4%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $55,700 (25%) lower, and it is about $18,400 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Grandville the median monthly rent is $818. This is roughly $76 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $244 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $107 (11%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
