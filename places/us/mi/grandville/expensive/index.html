Home prices

The cost of the median home in Grandville is $161,800. It is roughly $6,720 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $55,700 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $18,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Grandville is $818 a month. This is about $76 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $244 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $107 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
