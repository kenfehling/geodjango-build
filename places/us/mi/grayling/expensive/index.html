Home prices

The median home in Grayling costs $74,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,120 (16%) lower. It is approximately $143,300 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $76,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Midland.

Rents

Grayling's median monthly rent is $635. It is approximately $71 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $427 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $153 (19%) lower compared to the city of Midland.
