Home prices

The median home in Grayling costs $74,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $15,120 (16%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $143,300 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Midland is roughly $76,200 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Grayling is $635 per month. This is roughly $71 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $427 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $153 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Midland.
