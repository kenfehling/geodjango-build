Compared to its surrounding areas, Maple Rapids is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Rapids is valued at $81,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $19,320 (19%) lower. It is approximately $136,000 (62%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $61,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Maple Rapids the median rent is $572 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $131 (18%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $490 (46%) lower, and it is about $353 (38%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
