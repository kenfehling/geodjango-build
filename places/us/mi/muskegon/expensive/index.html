Home prices

Muskegon's median home cost is $73,000. This is roughly $36,040 (33%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $144,500 (66%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $70,400 (49%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

In Muskegon the median monthly rent is $706. This is roughly $116 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $356 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $219 (23%) lower.
