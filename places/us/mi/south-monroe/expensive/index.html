In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Monroe is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Monroe is valued at $132,400. This is approximately $22,200 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $85,100 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $83,200 (169%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Monroe is $735. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $26 (3%) lower.It is about $327 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $89 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
