Compared to its surrounding areas, South Monroe is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Monroe is valued at $132,400. It is approximately $22,200 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $85,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $83,200 (169%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

South Monroe's median rent is $735 per month. It is approximately $26 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $327 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $89 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
