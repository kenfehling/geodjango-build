Compared to other places in this area, Homer is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Toledo however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Homer is $84,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $5,580 (6%) lower. It is about $132,800 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $3,900 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Toledo.

Rents

Homer's median monthly rent is $635. This is approximately $57 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $427 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is roughly $90 (12%) lower.
