Home prices

The median home in Holland costs $151,300. It is approximately $76,860 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $66,200 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $7,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

Holland's median rent is $873 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $125 (16%) higher.It is roughly $189 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $52 (5%) lower.
