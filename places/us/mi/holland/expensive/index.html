Home prices

The median home cost in Holland is $151,300. This is roughly $76,860 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $7,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Holland is $873 a month. It is roughly $125 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $189 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $52 (5%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.
