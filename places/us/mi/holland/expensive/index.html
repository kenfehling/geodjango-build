Home prices

The median home price in Holland is $151,300. It is roughly $76,860 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is approximately $7,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Holland the median rent is $873 per month. It is roughly $125 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $189 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids is about $52 (5%) lower.
