Compared to the surrounding areas, Holland is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Grand Rapids, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Holland is valued at $151,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $76,860 (33%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $7,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Holland is $873 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $125 (16%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $189 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $52 (5%) lower.
