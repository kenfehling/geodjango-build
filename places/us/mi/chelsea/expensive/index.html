Home prices

The median home cost in Chelsea is $237,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $51,620 (27%) higher. It is roughly $20,000 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $188,300 (382%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chelsea is $1,084. This is approximately $273 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $22 (2%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $260 (31%) higher.
