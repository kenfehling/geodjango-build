Rents

The median rent in Spring Lake is $717 a month. It is roughly $101 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $345 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $208 (22%) lower.
