The median home in Maybee is valued at $159,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $34,500 (27%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $58,300 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $110,000 (223%) higher.

Rents

Maybee's median rent is $858 a month. This is about $55 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $204 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $34 (4%) higher.
