Home prices

Rents

In Durand the median monthly rent is $747. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $30 (4%) higher.It is roughly $315 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $77 (9%) lower.
