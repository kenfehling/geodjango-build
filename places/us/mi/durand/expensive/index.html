Compared to its nearby areas, Durand is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home value in Durand is $74,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $20,620 (21%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $143,400 (65%) lower, and is approximately $24,900 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Durand the median monthly rent is $747. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $30 (4%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $315 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $77 (9%) lower.
