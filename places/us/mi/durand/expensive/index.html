In comparison to other places in this area, Durand is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Durand costs $74,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $20,620 (21%) lower. It is roughly $143,400 (65%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $24,900 (50%) higher.

Rents

Durand's median monthly rent is $747. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $30 (4%) higher. It is about $315 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $77 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
