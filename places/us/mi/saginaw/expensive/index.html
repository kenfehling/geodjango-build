Compared to its surrounding areas, Saginaw is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Saginaw costs $40,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $54,600 (57%) lower. It is roughly $177,400 (81%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $9,100 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Saginaw is $734. This is about $116 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $328 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $90 (10%) lower.
