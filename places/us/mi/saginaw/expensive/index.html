Home prices

The median home value in Saginaw is $40,100. It is approximately $54,600 (57%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $177,400 (81%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $9,100 (18%) lower.

Rents

Saginaw's median monthly rent is $734. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $116 (13%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $328 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $90 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
