In comparison to other places in this area, Saginaw is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Saginaw is $40,100. It is roughly $54,600 (57%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $177,400 (81%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $9,100 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Saginaw is $734 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $116 (13%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $328 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $90 (10%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
