Home prices

The median home in Saginaw is valued at $40,100. This is about $54,600 (57%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $177,400 (81%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $9,100 (18%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Saginaw's median rent is $734 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $116 (13%) lower.It is approximately $328 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $90 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
