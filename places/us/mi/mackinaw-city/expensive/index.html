Home prices

The median home in Mackinaw City is valued at $151,600. This is about $3,540 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $65,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $59,100 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

In Mackinaw City the median rent is $686 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $27 (4%) higher.It is approximately $376 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
