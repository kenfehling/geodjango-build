Similar to its surrounding areas, it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Home prices

The median home in Mackinaw City costs $151,600. This is about $3,540 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $65,900 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is about $59,100 (63%) higher.

Rents

Mackinaw City's median monthly rent is $686. This is approximately $27 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $376 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
