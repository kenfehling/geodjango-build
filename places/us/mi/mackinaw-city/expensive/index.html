Much like other places in this area, it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mackinaw City costs $151,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $3,540 (2%) lower.It is roughly $65,900 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is about $59,100 (63%) higher.

Rents

Mackinaw City's median rent is $686 a month. It is approximately $27 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $376 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is roughly $12 (1%) lower.
