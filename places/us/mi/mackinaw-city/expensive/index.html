Rents

In Mackinaw City the median rent is $686 per month. It is roughly $27 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $376 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $12 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
