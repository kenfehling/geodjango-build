Home prices

The median home in Parkdale costs $93,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $5,540 (5%) lower. It is about $124,200 (57%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Muskegon it is approximately $20,300 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Parkdale the median monthly rent is $539. This is approximately $239 (30%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $523 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Muskegon is approximately $167 (23%) lower.
