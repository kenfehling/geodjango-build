Compared to nearby areas, Deckerville is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Deckerville is valued at $67,300. It is roughly $18,560 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $150,200 (69%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $18,100 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Deckerville is $603 per month. It is roughly $82 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $459 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $221 (26%) lower.
