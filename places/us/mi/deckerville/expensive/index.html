Home prices

The cost of the median home in Deckerville is $67,300. This is about $18,560 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $150,200 (69%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $18,100 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Deckerville is $603. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $82 (11%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $459 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $221 (26%) lower.
