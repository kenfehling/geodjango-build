Compared to other places in this area, Deckerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Deckerville is valued at $67,300. This is approximately $18,560 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $150,200 (69%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $18,100 (36%) higher.

Rents

Deckerville's median rent is $603 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $82 (11%) lower.It is roughly $459 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $221 (26%) lower.
