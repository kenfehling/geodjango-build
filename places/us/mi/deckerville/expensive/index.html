Home prices

The value of the median home in Deckerville is $67,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $18,560 (21%) lower.It is approximately $150,200 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $18,100 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Deckerville is $603. This is approximately $82 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $459 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $221 (26%) lower.
