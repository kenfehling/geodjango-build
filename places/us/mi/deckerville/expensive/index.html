Compared to other places in this area, Deckerville is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Deckerville is $67,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $18,560 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $150,200 (69%) lower, and is roughly $18,100 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Deckerville is $603. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $82 (11%) lower. It is approximately $459 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $221 (26%) lower.
