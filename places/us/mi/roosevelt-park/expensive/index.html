Home prices

The median home in Roosevelt Park costs $114,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $3,480 (3%) higher. It is approximately $103,000 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $28,900 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roosevelt Park is $816. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $22 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $246 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $109 (11%) lower.
