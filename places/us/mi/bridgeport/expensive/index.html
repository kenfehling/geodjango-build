In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bridgeport is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bridgeport costs $71,100. This is approximately $22,580 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $146,400 (67%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $21,900 (44%) higher.

Rents

Bridgeport's median rent is $634 per month. This is approximately $150 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $428 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $190 (23%) lower.
