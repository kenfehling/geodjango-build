Home prices

The median home in Bridgeport is valued at $71,100. It is approximately $22,580 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $146,400 (67%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $21,900 (44%) higher.

Rents

Bridgeport's median rent is $634 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $150 (19%) lower.It is about $428 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $190 (23%) lower.
