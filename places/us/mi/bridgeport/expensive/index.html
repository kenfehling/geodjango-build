Compared to other places in this area, Bridgeport is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bridgeport is valued at $71,100. This is about $22,580 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $146,400 (67%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $21,900 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bridgeport is $634. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $150 (19%) lower. It is approximately $428 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $190 (23%) lower.
