Compared to its surrounding areas, Bridgeport is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Bridgeport's median home price is $71,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $22,580 (24%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $146,400 (67%) lower, and it is about $21,900 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Bridgeport the median rent is $634 per month. It is roughly $150 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $428 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $190 (23%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
