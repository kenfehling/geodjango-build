Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $182,300. This is roughly $17,280 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $35,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $133,100 (270%) higher.

Rents

In Richmond the median monthly rent is $682. It is about $134 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $380 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $142 (17%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
