Home prices

Richmond's median home price is $182,300. It is roughly $17,280 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $35,200 (16%) lower, and is approximately $133,100 (270%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Richmond the median monthly rent is $682. This is about $134 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $380 (35%) lower, and is approximately $142 (17%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
