Home prices

The median home cost in Richmond is $182,300. This is roughly $17,280 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $35,200 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $133,100 (270%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Richmond the median monthly rent is $682. It is roughly $134 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $380 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $142 (17%) lower.
