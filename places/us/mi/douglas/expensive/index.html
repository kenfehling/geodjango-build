Home prices

Rents

Douglas's median rent is $783 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $17 (2%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $279 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $142 (15%) lower.
