Home prices

The median home in Douglas is valued at $316,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $121,620 (62%) higher. It is about $99,200 (45%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $173,300 (120%) higher compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Douglas is $783. It is roughly $17 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $279 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $142 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
