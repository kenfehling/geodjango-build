Rents

The median monthly rent in Douglas is $783. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $17 (2%) higher. It is roughly $279 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $142 (15%) lower.
