Home prices

Rents

In Hudson the median monthly rent is $800. It is approximately $54 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $262 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $24 (2%) lower.
