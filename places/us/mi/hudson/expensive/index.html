Home prices

The price of the median home in Hudson is $80,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $9,500 (13%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $136,800 (62%) lower, and it is about $31,500 (64%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hudson is $800. This is about $54 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $262 (24%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $24 (2%) lower.
