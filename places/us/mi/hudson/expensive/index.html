Home prices

Hudson's median home value is $80,700. It is about $9,500 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $136,800 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $31,500 (64%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hudson is $800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $54 (7%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $262 (24%) lower, and is about $24 (2%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
