Compared to its nearby areas, Memphis is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Memphis's median home value is $126,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $8,200 (6%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $90,700 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $77,600 (157%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Memphis's median rent is $723 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $62 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $339 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $101 (12%) lower.
