In comparison to its surrounding areas, Memphis is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Memphis is $126,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $8,200 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $90,700 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $77,600 (157%) higher.

Rents

In Memphis the median monthly rent is $723. This is approximately $62 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $339 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $101 (12%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
