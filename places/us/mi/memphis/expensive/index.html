In comparison to other places in this area, Memphis is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Memphis costs $126,800. This is roughly $8,200 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $90,700 (41%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $77,600 (157%) higher.

Rents

Memphis's median monthly rent is $723. This is approximately $62 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $101 (12%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
