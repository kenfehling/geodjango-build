Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jasper is $85,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $12,680 (12%) lower.It is approximately $132,500 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $35,800 (72%) higher.

Rents

In Jasper the median rent is $1,029 per month. This is about $259 (33%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $33 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $205 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
