Compared to nearby areas, Jasper is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Jasper is $85,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $12,680 (12%) lower. It is about $132,500 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $35,800 (72%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Jasper the median rent is $1,029 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $259 (33%) higher. It is about $33 (3%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $205 (24%) higher.
