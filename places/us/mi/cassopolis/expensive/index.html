Home prices

The median home in Cassopolis is valued at $64,900. It is roughly $14,620 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $152,600 (70%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $50,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Cassopolis the median monthly rent is $558. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $252 (31%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $504 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne it is approximately $206 (26%) lower.
