Home prices

Eau Claire's median home cost is $112,200. It is about $9,100 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $105,300 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $145,800 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Eau Claire the median rent is $860 a month. It is about $71 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $252 (22%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
