Home prices

The median home cost in Traverse City is $266,100. It is about $86,583 (48%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $48,600 (22%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole.

Rents

In Traverse City the median monthly rent is $905. It is about $24 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $157 (14%) lower.
