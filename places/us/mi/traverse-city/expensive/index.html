Compared to its surrounding areas, Traverse City is a lot more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Traverse City is $266,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $86,583 (48%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $48,600 (22%) higher.

Rents

Traverse City's median monthly rent is $905. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $24 (2%) lower. It is roughly $157 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation.
