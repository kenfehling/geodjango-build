Compared to its nearby areas, Traverse City is substantially more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Traverse City costs $266,100. This is about $86,583 (48%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $48,600 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Traverse City is $905. It is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $157 (14%) lower.
