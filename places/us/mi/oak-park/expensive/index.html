In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oak Park is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park costs $117,700. It is about $135,960 (53%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $99,800 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $68,500 (139%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Oak Park's median rent is $1,120 a month. This is about $248 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $58 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $296 (35%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
