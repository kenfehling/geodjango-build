Compared to its nearby areas, Oak Park is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park is valued at $117,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $135,960 (53%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $99,800 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $68,500 (139%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oak Park is $1,120 a month. It is roughly $248 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $58 (5%) higher, and it is roughly $296 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
