Compared to its surrounding areas, Oak Park is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Oak Park's median home cost is $117,700. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $135,960 (53%) lower.It is about $99,800 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $68,500 (139%) higher.

Rents

In Oak Park the median monthly rent is $1,120. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $248 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $58 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $296 (35%) higher.
