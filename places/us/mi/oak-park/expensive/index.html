In comparison to nearby areas, Oak Park is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park costs $117,700. It is about $135,960 (53%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $99,800 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $68,500 (139%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Oak Park's median monthly rent is $1,120. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $248 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $58 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $296 (35%) higher.
