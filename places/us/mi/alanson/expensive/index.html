Compared to other places in this area, Alanson is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Alanson costs $102,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $48,380 (31%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $114,600 (52%) lower, and is approximately $10,400 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

The median rent in Alanson is $757 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $20 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $305 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is about $59 (8%) higher.
