Compared to the surrounding areas, Alanson is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Home prices

The median home in Alanson is valued at $102,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $48,380 (31%) lower. It is about $114,600 (52%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $10,400 (11%) higher compared to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.

Rents

Alanson's median rent is $757 per month. This is approximately $20 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $305 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is about $59 (8%) higher.
