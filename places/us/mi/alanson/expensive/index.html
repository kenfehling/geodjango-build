Home prices

The median home in Alanson costs $102,900. This is approximately $48,380 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $114,600 (52%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie it is roughly $10,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alanson is $757. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $20 (2%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $305 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie is approximately $59 (8%) higher.
