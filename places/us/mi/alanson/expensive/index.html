Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alanson is $757. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $20 (2%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $305 (28%) lower, and is about $59 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Sault Ste. Marie.
