In comparison to other places in this area, Fremont is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Fremont is valued at $100,700. This is roughly $10,520 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $116,800 (53%) lower, and is roughly $42,700 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Fremont is $683 a month. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $242 (26%) lower.
