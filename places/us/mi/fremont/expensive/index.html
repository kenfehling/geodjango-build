Home prices

The median home in Fremont costs $100,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,520 (11%) higher.It is roughly $116,800 (53%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $42,700 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fremont is $683. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $379 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $242 (26%) lower.
