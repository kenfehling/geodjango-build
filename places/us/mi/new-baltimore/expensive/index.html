Home prices

The value of the median home in New Baltimore is $225,400. This is roughly $75,160 (50%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $7,900 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $176,200 (358%) higher.

Rents

New Baltimore's median rent is $741 a month. This is roughly $65 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $321 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $83 (10%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
