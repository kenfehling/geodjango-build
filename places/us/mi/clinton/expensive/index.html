Home prices

The median home in Clinton costs $126,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $3,680 (2%) lower. It is about $91,000 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $77,300 (157%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clinton is $720. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $342 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $104 (12%) lower.
