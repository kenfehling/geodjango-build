Home prices

Clinton's median home price is $126,500. It is roughly $3,680 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $91,000 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $77,300 (157%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Clinton is $720 per month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $342 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $104 (12%) lower.
