Rents

The median rent in Vicksburg is $701 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $79 (10%) lower. It is roughly $361 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $63 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
