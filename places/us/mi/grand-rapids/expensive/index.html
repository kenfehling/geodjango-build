Compared to the surrounding areas, Grand Rapids is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home cost in Grand Rapids is $143,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $49,083 (25%) lower.It is about $74,100 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Rapids is $925 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $62 (6%) lower.It is approximately $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation.
