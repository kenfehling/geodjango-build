In comparison to other places in this area, Grand Rapids is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The median home in Grand Rapids costs $143,400. This is approximately $49,083 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $74,100 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole.

Rents

In Grand Rapids the median monthly rent is $925. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $62 (6%) lower. It is about $137 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation.
