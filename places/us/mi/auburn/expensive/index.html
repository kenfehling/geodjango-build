Home prices

The median home value in Auburn is $117,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $8,140 (7%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $99,900 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Ann Arbor is approximately $205,800 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Auburn the median rent is $648 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $228 (26%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $414 (38%) lower, and is approximately $589 (47%) lower compared to the city of Ann Arbor.
