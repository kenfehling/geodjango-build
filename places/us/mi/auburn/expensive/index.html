Rents

In Auburn the median rent is $648 a month. This is approximately $228 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $414 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $589 (47%) lower compared to the city of Ann Arbor.
