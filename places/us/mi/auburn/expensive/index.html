Compared to its surrounding areas, Auburn is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Ann Arbor, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $117,600. This is roughly $8,140 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $99,900 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $205,800 (63%) lower compared to the city of Ann Arbor.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $648 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $228 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $414 (38%) lower, and is roughly $589 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Ann Arbor.
