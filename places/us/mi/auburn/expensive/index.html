Compared to its surrounding areas, Auburn is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Ann Arbor.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Auburn is $117,600. It is roughly $8,140 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $99,900 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Ann Arbor is roughly $205,800 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $648 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $228 (26%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $414 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $589 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Ann Arbor.
