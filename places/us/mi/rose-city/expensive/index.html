Home prices

The median home in Rose City is valued at $75,900. This is roughly $15,400 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $141,600 (65%) lower, and is approximately $35,800 (89%) higher in comparison to the city of Saginaw.

Rents

Rose City's median rent is $544 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $106 (16%) lower.It is about $518 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Saginaw is about $190 (25%) lower.
