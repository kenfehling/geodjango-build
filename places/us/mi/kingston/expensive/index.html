Compared to its surrounding areas, Kingston is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kingston is $65,800. This is about $8,500 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $151,700 (69%) lower, and is about $16,600 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Kingston is $563 per month. This is roughly $107 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $499 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $261 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
