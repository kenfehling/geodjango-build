In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kingston is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Kingston costs $65,800. It is approximately $8,500 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $151,700 (69%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $16,600 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Kingston the median monthly rent is $563. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $107 (15%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $499 (46%) lower, and it is about $261 (31%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
