Compared to nearby areas, Kingston is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kingston costs $65,800. It is about $8,500 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $151,700 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $16,600 (33%) higher.

Rents

Kingston's median monthly rent is $563. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $107 (15%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $499 (46%) lower, and it is roughly $261 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
