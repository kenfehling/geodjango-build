The median home in Bloomfield Hills costs $860,800. It is roughly $484,960 (129%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $643,300 (295%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $811,600 (1649%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Bloomfield Hills's median monthly rent is $1,527. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $191 (14%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $465 (43%) higher, and it is about $703 (85%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
