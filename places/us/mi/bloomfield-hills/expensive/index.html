Home prices

The median home price in Bloomfield Hills is $860,800. It is about $484,960 (129%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $643,300 (295%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $811,600 (1649%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomfield Hills is $1,527. This is about $191 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $465 (43%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $703 (85%) higher.
