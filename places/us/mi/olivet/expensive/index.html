Home prices

The median home price in Olivet is $110,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $23,680 (27%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $106,900 (49%) lower, and it is about $4,500 (3%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

Olivet's median rent is $692 a month. This is approximately $78 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and is roughly $72 (9%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
