Home prices

Olivet's median home price is $110,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $23,680 (27%) higher.It is approximately $106,900 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $4,500 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median rent in Olivet is $692 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $78 (10%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $370 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $72 (9%) lower compared to the city of Fort Wayne.
