Home prices

The median home in Olivet is valued at $110,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $23,680 (27%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $106,900 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $4,500 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Olivet the median rent is $692 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $78 (10%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $370 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is about $72 (9%) lower.
