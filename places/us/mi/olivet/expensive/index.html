Compared to nearby areas, Olivet is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Fort Wayne, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Olivet is $110,600. This is about $23,680 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $106,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $4,500 (3%) lower.

Rents

Olivet's median monthly rent is $692. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (10%) lower. It is approximately $370 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $72 (9%) lower.
