Home prices

The median home in Dundee is valued at $170,000. It is approximately $32,320 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $47,500 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $120,800 (245%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dundee is $830. This is roughly $14 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $232 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is is very similar.
