Home prices

The price of the median home in Dundee is $170,000. It is roughly $32,320 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $47,500 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $120,800 (245%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dundee is $830. It is roughly $14 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $232 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is is very similar.
