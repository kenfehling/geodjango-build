In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dundee is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Dundee's median home cost is $170,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32,320 (23%) higher. It is about $47,500 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $120,800 (245%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Dundee the median rent is $830 a month. It is roughly $14 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $232 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is is very similar.
