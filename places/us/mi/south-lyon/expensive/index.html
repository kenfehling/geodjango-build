Home prices

The median home cost in South Lyon is $192,100. This is roughly $65,740 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $25,400 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $142,900 (290%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in South Lyon is $1,042 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $20 (1%) lower, and is about $218 (26%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
