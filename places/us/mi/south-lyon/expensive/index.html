In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Lyon is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in South Lyon is $192,100. This is about $65,740 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $25,400 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $142,900 (290%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

South Lyon's median rent is $1,042 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $20 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $218 (26%) higher.
