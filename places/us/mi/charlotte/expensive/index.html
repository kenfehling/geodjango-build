Home prices

The cost of the median home in Charlotte is $106,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $9,300 (8%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $111,400 (51%) lower, and is roughly $37,300 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Charlotte is $736 a month. This is about $12 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $326 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $189 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.
