Home prices

The median home in Charlotte is valued at $106,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $9,300 (8%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $111,400 (51%) lower, and is roughly $37,300 (26%) lower compared to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Charlotte is $736 per month. It is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $326 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $189 (20%) lower.
