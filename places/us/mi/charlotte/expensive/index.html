Home prices

The median home in Charlotte is valued at $106,100. This is about $9,300 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $111,400 (51%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $37,300 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Charlotte is $736. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $12 (1%) lower. It is approximately $326 (30%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is roughly $189 (20%) lower.
