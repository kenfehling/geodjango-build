Rents

The median rent in Hopkins is $670 a month. This is about $104 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $392 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids it is about $255 (27%) lower.
