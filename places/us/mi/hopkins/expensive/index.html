Home prices

The value of the median home in Hopkins is $123,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $25,160 (16%) lower. It is approximately $94,000 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $19,900 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids.

Rents

Hopkins's median monthly rent is $670. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $104 (13%) lower. It is roughly $392 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Rapids it is approximately $255 (27%) lower.
