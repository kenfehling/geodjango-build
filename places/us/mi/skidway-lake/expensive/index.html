Home prices

The price of the median home in Skidway Lake is $51,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,100 (17%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $165,900 (76%) lower, and compared to the city of Flint is approximately $22,100 (74%) higher.

Rents

In Skidway Lake the median rent is $641 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $14 (2%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $421 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Flint it is approximately $75 (10%) lower.
