Home prices

Skidway Lake's median home value is $51,600. It is roughly $11,100 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $165,900 (76%) lower, and is about $22,100 (74%) higher compared to the city of Flint.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Skidway Lake is $641. This is about $14 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $421 (39%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $75 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Flint.
