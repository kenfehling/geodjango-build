Home prices

The median home in Grosse Pointe Woods costs $244,500. This is about $44,140 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $27,000 (12%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $195,300 (396%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Grosse Pointe Woods the median monthly rent is $1,438. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $288 (25%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $376 (35%) higher, and is about $614 (74%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
