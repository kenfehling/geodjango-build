Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Huntington Woods is $2,014 a month. This is about $856 (73%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $952 (89%) higher, and is approximately $1,190 (144%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
