Home prices

The median home in Corunna is valued at $89,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $7,780 (9%) higher. It is roughly $128,200 (58%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $40,100 (81%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Corunna the median monthly rent is $823. This is roughly $123 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $239 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is is very similar.
