In comparison to other places in this area, Corunna is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Corunna costs $89,300. It is roughly $7,780 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $128,200 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $40,100 (81%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corunna is $823. This is approximately $123 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $239 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is is very similar.
