Home prices

The median home in Keego Harbor costs $153,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $239,260 (60%) lower.It is about $63,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $104,700 (212%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Keego Harbor is $949 per month. It is roughly $699 (42%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $113 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $125 (15%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
