Home prices

The cost of the median home in Keego Harbor is $153,900. It is roughly $239,260 (60%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $63,600 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $104,700 (212%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Keego Harbor's median rent is $949 a month. It is about $699 (42%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $113 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $125 (15%) higher.
