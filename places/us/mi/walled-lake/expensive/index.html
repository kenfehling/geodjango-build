Home prices

The median home cost in Walled Lake is $146,900. It is approximately $187,340 (56%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $70,600 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $97,700 (198%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Walled Lake is $1,037. Compared to nearby areas it is about $427 (29%) lower. It is about $25 (2%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $213 (25%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
