Home prices

The median home in Linden costs $166,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $1,960 (1%) lower. It is approximately $51,000 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $117,300 (238%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Linden is $774 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $78 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $288 (27%) lower, and it is about $50 (6%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
