Home prices

The median home in Linden is valued at $166,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $1,960 (1%) lower. It is about $51,000 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $117,300 (238%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Linden is $774. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $78 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $288 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $50 (6%) lower.
