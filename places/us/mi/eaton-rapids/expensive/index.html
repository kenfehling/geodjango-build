Home prices

The median home in Eaton Rapids costs $119,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is about $97,600 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Toledo it is about $39,100 (48%) higher.

Rents

Eaton Rapids's median rent is $741 per month. This is roughly $83 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $321 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is approximately $16 (2%) higher.
