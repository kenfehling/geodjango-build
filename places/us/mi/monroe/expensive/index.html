In comparison to its surrounding areas, Monroe is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monroe is valued at $122,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $13,080 (11%) higher.It is about $95,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $73,100 (148%) higher.

Rents

In Monroe the median monthly rent is $741. It is about $176 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $321 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $83 (10%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
