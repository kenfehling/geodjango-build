In comparison to the surrounding areas, Monroe is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monroe costs $122,300. It is roughly $13,080 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $95,200 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $73,100 (148%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monroe is $741. This is roughly $176 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $321 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $83 (10%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
