In comparison to its surrounding areas, Monroe is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Monroe is $122,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $13,080 (11%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $95,200 (43%) lower, and it is about $73,100 (148%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monroe is $741. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $176 (19%) lower.It is roughly $321 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $83 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
