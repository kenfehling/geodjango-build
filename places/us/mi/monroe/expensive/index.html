Compared to its surrounding areas, Monroe is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Monroe costs $122,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13,080 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $95,200 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $73,100 (148%) higher.

Rents

Monroe's median rent is $741 per month. It is approximately $176 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $321 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $83 (10%) lower.
