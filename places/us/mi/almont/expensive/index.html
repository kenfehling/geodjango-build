Home prices

The median home in Almont costs $159,700. It is roughly $23,940 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $57,800 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $110,500 (224%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Almont the median monthly rent is $727. This is about $140 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $335 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $97 (11%) lower.
