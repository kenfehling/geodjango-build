Home prices

The median home in Almont is valued at $159,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $23,940 (17%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $57,800 (26%) lower, and it is about $110,500 (224%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Almont is $727. It is about $140 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $335 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $97 (11%) lower.
