Home prices

The median home in Almont is valued at $159,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $23,940 (17%) higher. It is about $57,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $110,500 (224%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Almont's median monthly rent is $727. This is approximately $140 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $335 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $97 (11%) lower.
