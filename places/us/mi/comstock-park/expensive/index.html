Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Comstock Park is $821 per month. This is about $165 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $241 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Rapids is roughly $104 (11%) lower.
