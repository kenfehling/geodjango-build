Compared to its surrounding areas, Eastlake is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Eastlake costs $133,000. It is roughly $106,860 (44%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $84,500 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $63,400 (91%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Eastlake is $869 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $150 (14%) lower.It is about $193 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $150 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
