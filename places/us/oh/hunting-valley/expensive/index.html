In comparison to other places in this area, Hunting Valley is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

Hunting Valley's median home cost is $1,529,200. This is roughly $1,197,920 (361%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,311,700 (603%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $1,459,600 (2097%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Hunting Valley the median monthly rent is $1,167. This is about $86 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $105 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $448 (62%) higher.
