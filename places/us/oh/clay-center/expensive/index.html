Home prices

The median home in Clay Center is valued at $89,400. This is approximately $35,180 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $128,100 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $40,200 (81%) higher.

Rents

In Clay Center the median monthly rent is $940. This is approximately $213 (29%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $122 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $116 (14%) higher.
