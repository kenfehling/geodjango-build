Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clay Center is $89,400. This is roughly $35,180 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $128,100 (58%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $40,200 (81%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clay Center is $940. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $213 (29%) higher.It is about $122 (11%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $116 (14%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
