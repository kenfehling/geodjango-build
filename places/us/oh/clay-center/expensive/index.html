Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clay Center is $89,400. This is roughly $35,180 (28%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $128,100 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $40,200 (81%) higher.

Rents

Clay Center's median monthly rent is $940. Compared to nearby areas this is about $213 (29%) higher. It is about $122 (11%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $116 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
