In comparison to nearby areas, Oak Harbor is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Oak Harbor is $122,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $16,460 (15%) higher.It is roughly $95,300 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $73,000 (148%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oak Harbor is $663. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $23 (3%) lower.It is roughly $399 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $161 (19%) lower.
