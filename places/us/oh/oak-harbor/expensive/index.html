In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oak Harbor is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Oak Harbor's median home price is $122,200. It is about $16,460 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $95,300 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $73,000 (148%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Oak Harbor the median monthly rent is $663. It is roughly $23 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $161 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
