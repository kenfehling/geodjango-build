Rents

Oak Harbor's median rent is $663 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $23 (3%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $399 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $161 (19%) lower.
