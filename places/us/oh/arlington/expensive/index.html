Compared to the surrounding areas, Arlington is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Arlington is $133,500. This is about $52,360 (64%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $84,000 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $18,100 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Arlington's median rent is $596 a month. It is about $130 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $466 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $365 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
