Compared to the surrounding areas, Grand Rapids is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Grand Rapids is valued at $122,200. It is about $6,780 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $95,300 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $73,000 (148%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Rapids is $718 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $91 (11%) lower.It is about $344 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
