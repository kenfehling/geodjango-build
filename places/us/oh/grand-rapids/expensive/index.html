In comparison to the surrounding areas, Grand Rapids is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home price in Grand Rapids is $122,200. This is about $6,780 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $95,300 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $73,000 (148%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Rapids is $718 a month. It is roughly $91 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $344 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
