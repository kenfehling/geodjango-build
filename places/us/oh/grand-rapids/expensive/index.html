In comparison to its nearby areas, Grand Rapids is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Grand Rapids's median home price is $122,200. This is approximately $6,780 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $95,300 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $73,000 (148%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Grand Rapids's median monthly rent is $718. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $91 (11%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $344 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
