Compared to the surrounding areas, Grand Rapids is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Grand Rapids's median home value is $122,200. This is approximately $6,780 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $95,300 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $73,000 (148%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grand Rapids is $718. This is roughly $91 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $344 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $106 (12%) lower.
