Compared to its surrounding areas, Grand Rapids is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Grand Rapids's median home value is $122,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $6,780 (5%) higher. It is approximately $95,300 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $73,000 (148%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Grand Rapids's median monthly rent is $718. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $91 (11%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $344 (32%) lower, and it is about $106 (12%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
