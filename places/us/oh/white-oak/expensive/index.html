Home prices

The median home price in White Oak is $148,800. It is roughly $39,360 (35%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $68,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $10,800 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in White Oak is $815 per month. It is approximately $131 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $247 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $77 (10%) higher.
