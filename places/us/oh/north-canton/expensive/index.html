Home prices

The value of the median home in North Canton is $155,300. This is approximately $2,360 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $85,700 (123%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In North Canton the median rent is $840 a month. It is about $13 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $222 (20%) lower, and is approximately $121 (16%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
