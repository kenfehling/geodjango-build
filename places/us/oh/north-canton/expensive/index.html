Much like its nearby areas, it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in North Canton is $155,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $2,360 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $62,200 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $85,700 (123%) higher.

Rents

In North Canton the median rent is $840 per month. It is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $222 (20%) lower, and is about $121 (16%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
