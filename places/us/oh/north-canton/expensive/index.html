Home prices

The median home in North Canton is valued at $155,300. This is about $2,360 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $85,700 (123%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Canton is $840 a month. It is approximately $13 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $222 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $121 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
