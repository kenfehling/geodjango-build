Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Canton is $155,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $2,360 (1%) higher.It is approximately $62,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $85,700 (123%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in North Canton is $840 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $13 (1%) lower.It is roughly $222 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $121 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
