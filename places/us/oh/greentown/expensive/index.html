Home prices

The value of the median home in Greentown is $205,500. It is approximately $52,580 (34%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $12,000 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $135,900 (195%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Greentown is $922 a month. It is roughly $111 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $140 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $203 (28%) higher.
