Compared to its surrounding areas, Dupont is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Toledo, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dupont is $68,200. It is about $42,680 (38%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $149,300 (68%) lower, and it is about $12,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Toledo.

Rents

Dupont's median monthly rent is $506. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $99 (16%) lower.It is approximately $556 (52%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $219 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Toledo.
