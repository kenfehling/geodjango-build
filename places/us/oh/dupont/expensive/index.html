In comparison to its nearby areas, Dupont is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Toledo.

Home prices

The median home value in Dupont is $68,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $42,680 (38%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $149,300 (68%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is approximately $12,600 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Dupont the median rent is $506 per month. It is approximately $99 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $556 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is roughly $219 (30%) lower.
