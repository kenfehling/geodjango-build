Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Solon is $1,268. This is approximately $206 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $206 (19%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $549 (76%) higher.
