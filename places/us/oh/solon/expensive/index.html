In comparison to its surrounding areas, Solon is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Solon is $286,200. It is about $43,260 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $68,700 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $216,600 (311%) higher.

Rents

Solon's median rent is $1,268 per month. This is roughly $206 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $206 (19%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $549 (76%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
