Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairport Harbor is $104,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $36,300 (25%) lower.It is approximately $113,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $34,900 (50%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Fairport Harbor's median rent is $779 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $80 (9%) lower.It is approximately $283 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $60 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
