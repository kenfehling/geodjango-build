Compared to other places in this area, Fairport Harbor is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Fairport Harbor is $104,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $36,300 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $113,000 (51%) lower, and is roughly $34,900 (50%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Fairport Harbor the median rent is $779 per month. This is approximately $80 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $283 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $60 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
