Home prices

The value of the median home in Harrison is $143,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $35,840 (19%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $73,700 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $5,800 (4%) higher.

Rents

Harrison's median monthly rent is $832. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $29 (3%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $230 (21%) lower, and is approximately $94 (12%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
