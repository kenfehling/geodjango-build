Compared to its nearby areas, Northwood is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Northwood's median home cost is $129,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $7,700 (6%) higher. It is roughly $88,400 (40%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $79,900 (162%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Northwood is $750 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $34 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $312 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $74 (8%) lower.
