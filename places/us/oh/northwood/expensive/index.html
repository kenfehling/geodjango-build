Home prices

Northwood's median home value is $129,100. It is roughly $7,700 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $88,400 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $79,900 (162%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Northwood's median monthly rent is $750. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $34 (4%) higher.It is approximately $312 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $74 (8%) lower.
