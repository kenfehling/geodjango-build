Home prices

The median home in Northwood is valued at $129,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $7,700 (6%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $88,400 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $79,900 (162%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Northwood is $750 per month. This is about $34 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $312 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $74 (8%) lower.
