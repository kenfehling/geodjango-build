In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lebanon is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Lebanon's median home cost is $183,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $73,940 (28%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $34,000 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $31,900 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Lebanon the median monthly rent is $846. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $409 (32%) lower. It is approximately $216 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $115 (11%) lower.
