Compared to nearby areas, Lebanon is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lebanon is valued at $183,500. This is about $73,940 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $34,000 (15%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $31,900 (21%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Lebanon's median rent is $846 per month. This is approximately $409 (32%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $216 (20%) lower, and is roughly $115 (11%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
