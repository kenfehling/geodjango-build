Home prices

The median home in Richmond Heights is valued at $141,100. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $76,400 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $71,500 (102%) higher.

Rents

In Richmond Heights the median monthly rent is $782. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $261 (25%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $63 (8%) higher.
