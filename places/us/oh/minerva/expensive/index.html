Home prices

The median home in Minerva costs $89,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $17,640 (16%) lower.It is about $128,200 (58%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $19,700 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Minerva is $683 per month. This is roughly $21 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $36 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
