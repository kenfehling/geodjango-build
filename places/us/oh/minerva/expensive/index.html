Home prices

The median home in Minerva costs $89,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17,640 (16%) lower. It is about $128,200 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $19,700 (28%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Minerva's median monthly rent is $683. It is roughly $21 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $36 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
