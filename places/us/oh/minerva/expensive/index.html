Home prices

The price of the median home in Minerva is $89,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $17,640 (16%) lower.It is about $128,200 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $19,700 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Minerva is $683 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $21 (2%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $379 (35%) lower, and is about $36 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
