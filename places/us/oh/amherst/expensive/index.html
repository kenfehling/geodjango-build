Compared to its surrounding areas, Amherst is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Amherst is $164,600. It is approximately $29,200 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $52,900 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $115,400 (234%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Amherst is $784 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $95 (10%) lower.It is roughly $278 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $40 (4%) lower.
