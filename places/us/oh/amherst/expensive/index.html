Compared to other places in this area, Amherst is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Amherst is $164,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $29,200 (21%) higher. It is approximately $52,900 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $115,400 (234%) higher.

Rents

In Amherst the median rent is $784 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $95 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $278 (26%) lower, and is about $40 (4%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
