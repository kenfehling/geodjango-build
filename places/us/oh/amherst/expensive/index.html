Compared to nearby areas, Amherst is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Amherst costs $164,600. This is roughly $29,200 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $52,900 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $115,400 (234%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Amherst's median monthly rent is $784. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $95 (10%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $278 (26%) lower, and is approximately $40 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
