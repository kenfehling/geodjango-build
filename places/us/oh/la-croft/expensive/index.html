Compared to other places in this area, La Croft is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is close to average.

Home prices

La Croft's median home cost is $70,400. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $14,240 (16%) lower. It is about $147,100 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $800 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in La Croft is $771. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $145 (23%) higher. It is about $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $52 (7%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
