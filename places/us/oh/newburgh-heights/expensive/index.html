Home prices

Newburgh Heights's median home price is $66,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $45,160 (40%) lower. It is approximately $151,400 (69%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $3,500 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newburgh Heights is $758. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $146 (16%) lower. It is about $304 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $39 (5%) higher.
