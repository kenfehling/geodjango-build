Home prices

The median home in Glenwillow is valued at $229,800. It is roughly $34,980 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $12,300 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $160,200 (230%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Glenwillow is $716 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $256 (26%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $346 (32%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Cleveland.
