Home prices

The cost of the median home in Georgetown is $117,400. This is roughly $31,200 (36%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $100,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $34,200 (22%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Georgetown is $618 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $135 (17%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $444 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $343 (35%) lower.
