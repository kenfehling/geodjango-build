Home prices

The median home cost in Georgetown is $117,400. This is approximately $31,200 (36%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $100,100 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $34,200 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Georgetown the median monthly rent is $618. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $135 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $444 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $343 (35%) lower.
