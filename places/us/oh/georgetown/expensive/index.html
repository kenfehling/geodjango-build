Home prices

Georgetown's median home cost is $117,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $31,200 (36%) higher. It is approximately $100,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $34,200 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Georgetown the median monthly rent is $618. This is approximately $135 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $343 (35%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
