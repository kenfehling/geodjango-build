Compared to the surrounding areas, Tremont City is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Tremont City costs $89,500. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $20,820 (18%) lower. It is roughly $128,000 (58%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $62,100 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tremont City is $725. It is approximately $44 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $236 (24%) lower.
