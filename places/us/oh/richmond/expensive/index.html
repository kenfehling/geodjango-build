Home prices

Rents

Richmond's median rent is $788 per month. This is about $83 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $69 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
