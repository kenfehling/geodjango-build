Home prices

Streetsboro's median home cost is $148,800. It is approximately $61,540 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $68,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $79,200 (113%) higher.

Rents

Streetsboro's median monthly rent is $1,082. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $103 (8%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $20 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $363 (50%) higher.
