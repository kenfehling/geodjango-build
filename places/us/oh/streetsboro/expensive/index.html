Home prices

The cost of the median home in Streetsboro is $148,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $61,540 (29%) lower. It is about $68,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $79,200 (113%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Streetsboro is $1,082 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $103 (8%) lower. It is about $20 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $363 (50%) higher.
