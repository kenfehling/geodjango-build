Compared to other places in this area, Streetsboro is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Streetsboro is valued at $148,800. It is about $61,540 (29%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $68,700 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $79,200 (113%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Streetsboro is $1,082 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $103 (8%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $20 (1%) higher, and it is approximately $363 (50%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
