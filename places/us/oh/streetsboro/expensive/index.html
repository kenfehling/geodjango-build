Compared to other places in this area, Streetsboro is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Streetsboro is $148,800. This is roughly $61,540 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $68,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $79,200 (113%) higher.

Rents

In Streetsboro the median rent is $1,082 per month. It is about $103 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $20 (1%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $363 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
