In comparison to other places in this area, Chesapeake is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Chesapeake is $87,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $25,540 (22%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $129,700 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is approximately $58,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Chesapeake the median rent is $772 per month. This is roughly $42 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $37 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Charleston.
