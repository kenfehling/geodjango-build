In comparison to its surrounding areas, Chesapeake is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Chesapeake is $87,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $25,540 (22%) lower. It is approximately $129,700 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $58,000 (39%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Chesapeake's median monthly rent is $772. It is approximately $42 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $290 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Charleston it is roughly $37 (5%) higher.
