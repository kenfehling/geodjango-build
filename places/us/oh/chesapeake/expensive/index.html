Compared to the surrounding areas, Chesapeake is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Chesapeake's median home cost is $87,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $25,540 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $129,700 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $58,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

Chesapeake's median rent is $772 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $42 (5%) higher. It is about $290 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $37 (5%) higher.
