Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kent is $146,700. It is approximately $1,640 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $70,800 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $77,100 (110%) higher.

Rents

In Kent the median rent is $801 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $82 (9%) lower.It is roughly $261 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $82 (11%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
