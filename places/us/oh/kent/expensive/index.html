Similar to its nearby areas, it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Kent is valued at $146,700. This is about $1,640 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $70,800 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $77,100 (110%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Kent the median rent is $801 per month. This is about $82 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $261 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $82 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
