Home prices

The median home in Kent is valued at $146,700. This is about $1,640 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $70,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $77,100 (110%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Kent's median monthly rent is $801. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $82 (9%) lower. It is about $261 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $82 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
