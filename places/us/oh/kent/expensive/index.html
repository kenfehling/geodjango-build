Similar to the surrounding areas, it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Kent's median home value is $146,700. This is about $1,640 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $70,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $77,100 (110%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Kent the median rent is $801 per month. This is roughly $82 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $261 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $82 (11%) higher.
