Home prices

The median home in Midvale costs $84,800. This is about $6,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $132,700 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $15,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Midvale's median rent is $833 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $122 (17%) higher.It is roughly $229 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $114 (15%) higher.
