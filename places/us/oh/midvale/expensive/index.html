Compared to its surrounding areas, Midvale is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Midvale is $84,800. This is about $6,500 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $132,700 (61%) lower, and is roughly $15,200 (21%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Midvale's median rent is $833 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $122 (17%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $114 (15%) higher.
