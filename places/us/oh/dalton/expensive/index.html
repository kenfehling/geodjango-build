Home prices

Dalton's median home value is $156,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24,240 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $61,000 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $86,900 (124%) higher.

Rents

In Dalton the median monthly rent is $686. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $72 (9%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $376 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $33 (4%) lower.
