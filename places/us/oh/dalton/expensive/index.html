Compared to nearby areas, Dalton is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Dalton costs $156,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,240 (18%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $61,000 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $86,900 (124%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dalton is $686. This is approximately $72 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $376 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $33 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
