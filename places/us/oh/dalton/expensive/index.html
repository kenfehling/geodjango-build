Home prices

Dalton's median home value is $156,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24,240 (18%) higher.It is about $61,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $86,900 (124%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dalton is $686. It is about $72 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $376 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $33 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
