Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dalton is $156,500. It is about $24,240 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $61,000 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $86,900 (124%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dalton is $686. This is about $72 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $376 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $33 (4%) lower.
