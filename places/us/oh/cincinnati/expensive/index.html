In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cincinnati is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cincinnati is valued at $138,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $30,460 (28%) higher. It is approximately $79,500 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is approximately $51,800 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Cincinnati the median rent is $738 per month. This is roughly $38 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $324 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $158 (17%) lower.
