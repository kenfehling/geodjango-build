Compared to the surrounding areas, Cincinnati is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Cincinnati's median home value is $138,000. This is about $30,460 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $79,500 (36%) lower, and is approximately $51,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

Cincinnati's median monthly rent is $738. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $38 (4%) lower.It is about $324 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $158 (17%) lower.
