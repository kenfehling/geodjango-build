Compared to other places in this area, Huron is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Huron is $154,500. It is about $20,940 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $63,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $105,300 (214%) higher.

Rents

In Huron the median monthly rent is $839. It is approximately $107 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $223 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $15 (1%) higher.
