Compared to other places in this area, Huron is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Huron is valued at $154,500. This is approximately $20,940 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $63,000 (28%) lower, and is roughly $105,300 (214%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Huron is $839. It is roughly $107 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $223 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $15 (1%) higher.
