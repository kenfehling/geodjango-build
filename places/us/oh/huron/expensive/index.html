Compared to other places in this area, Huron is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Huron is $154,500. It is approximately $20,940 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $63,000 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $105,300 (214%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Huron is $839. It is roughly $107 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $223 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $15 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
