Compared to its nearby areas, University Heights is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in University Heights is valued at $164,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $16,600 (9%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $53,200 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $94,700 (136%) higher.

Rents

University Heights's median monthly rent is $1,017. It is about $196 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $45 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $298 (41%) higher.
