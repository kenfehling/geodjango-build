In comparison to its nearby areas, University Heights is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in University Heights is $164,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16,600 (9%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $53,200 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $94,700 (136%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in University Heights is $1,017 per month. This is about $196 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $45 (4%) lower, and it is about $298 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
