Home prices

The median home in University Heights is valued at $164,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $16,600 (9%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $53,200 (24%) lower, and is approximately $94,700 (136%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In University Heights the median rent is $1,017 per month. This is roughly $196 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $45 (4%) lower, and is about $298 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
