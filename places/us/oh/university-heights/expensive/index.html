In comparison to nearby areas, University Heights is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in University Heights costs $164,300. It is about $16,600 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $53,200 (24%) lower, and is about $94,700 (136%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

University Heights's median monthly rent is $1,017. It is approximately $196 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $45 (4%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $298 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
