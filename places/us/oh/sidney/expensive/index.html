Rents

In Sidney the median monthly rent is $697. This is roughly $36 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $264 (27%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
