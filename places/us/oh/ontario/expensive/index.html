Home prices

The median home in Ontario is valued at $126,500. This is approximately $36,380 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $91,000 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $25,100 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Ontario the median monthly rent is $791. It is roughly $149 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $271 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $170 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
