Home prices

The median home in Ontario is valued at $126,500. It is about $36,380 (40%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $91,000 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $25,100 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Ontario the median monthly rent is $791. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $149 (23%) higher. It is approximately $271 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $170 (17%) lower.
