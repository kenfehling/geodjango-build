Home prices

Roswell's median home value is $73,800. It is about $9,140 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $143,700 (66%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $4,200 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Roswell is $629 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $127 (16%) lower. It is about $433 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $90 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
