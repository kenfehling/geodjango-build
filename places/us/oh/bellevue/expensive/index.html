Home prices

The median home value in Bellevue is $99,600. It is about $12,800 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $117,900 (54%) lower, and it is approximately $52,000 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bellevue is $701. This is approximately $61 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $361 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $260 (27%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
