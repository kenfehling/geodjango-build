Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sharonville is $157,600. It is roughly $62,060 (28%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $19,600 (14%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Sharonville's median rent is $986 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $128 (11%) lower. It is approximately $76 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $248 (33%) higher.
