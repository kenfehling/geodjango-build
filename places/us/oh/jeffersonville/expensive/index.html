Home prices

The median home in Jeffersonville is valued at $82,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $10,800 (15%) higher. It is about $135,000 (62%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $69,100 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Jeffersonville the median monthly rent is $710. It is roughly $33 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $352 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $251 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
