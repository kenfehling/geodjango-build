Home prices

Rents

Crystal Lakes's median monthly rent is $905. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $185 (25%) higher. It is approximately $157 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $56 (5%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
