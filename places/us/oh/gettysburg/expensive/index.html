Compared to its surrounding areas, Gettysburg is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Gettysburg's median home price is $80,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $27,980 (25%) lower.It is approximately $137,200 (63%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $71,300 (47%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Gettysburg's median monthly rent is $785. This is about $121 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $277 (26%) lower, and is approximately $176 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
