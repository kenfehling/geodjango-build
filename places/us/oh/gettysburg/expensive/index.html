In comparison to its nearby areas, Gettysburg is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Gettysburg is $80,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $27,980 (25%) lower. It is roughly $137,200 (63%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $71,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

Gettysburg's median monthly rent is $785. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $121 (18%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $277 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $176 (18%) lower.
