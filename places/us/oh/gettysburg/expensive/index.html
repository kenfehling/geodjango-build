Compared to the surrounding areas, Gettysburg is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Gettysburg's median home cost is $80,300. This is roughly $27,980 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $137,200 (63%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $71,300 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Gettysburg the median monthly rent is $785. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $121 (18%) higher.It is about $277 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $176 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
