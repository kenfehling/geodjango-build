Home prices

The price of the median home in Rossford is $142,100. It is roughly $7,160 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $75,400 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $92,900 (188%) higher.

Rents

In Rossford the median rent is $710 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $76 (9%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $352 (33%) lower, and it is about $114 (13%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
