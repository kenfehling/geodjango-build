Compared to its surrounding areas, Rossford is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rossford costs $142,100. This is roughly $7,160 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $75,400 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $92,900 (188%) higher.

Rents

In Rossford the median monthly rent is $710. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $76 (9%) lower. It is roughly $352 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $114 (13%) lower.
