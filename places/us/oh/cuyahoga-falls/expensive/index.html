In comparison to other places in this area, Cuyahoga Falls is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Cuyahoga Falls is valued at $127,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $81,540 (38%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $89,900 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $58,000 (83%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Cuyahoga Falls the median monthly rent is $840. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $285 (25%) lower. It is about $222 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $121 (16%) higher.
