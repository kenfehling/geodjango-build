Home prices

Rents

Cuyahoga Falls's median rent is $840 a month. It is approximately $285 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $222 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $121 (16%) higher.
