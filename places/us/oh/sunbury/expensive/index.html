Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sunbury is $227,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $20,040 (8%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $10,300 (4%) higher, and is about $76,200 (50%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Sunbury's median monthly rent is $1,041. This is roughly $97 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $21 (1%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $80 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
