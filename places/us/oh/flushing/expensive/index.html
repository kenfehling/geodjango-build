Home prices

The cost of the median home in Flushing is $68,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $9,560 (12%) lower. It is approximately $149,000 (68%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $56,500 (45%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Flushing's median rent is $770 per month. This is roughly $170 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $292 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $188 (19%) lower.
