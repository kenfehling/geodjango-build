Home prices

The median home cost in North Bend is $325,500. This is about $127,080 (64%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $108,000 (49%) higher, and is roughly $187,500 (135%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In North Bend the median monthly rent is $621. This is roughly $341 (35%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $441 (41%) lower, and is roughly $117 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
