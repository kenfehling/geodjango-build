Rents

The median monthly rent in North Bend is $621. This is roughly $259 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $441 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $117 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
