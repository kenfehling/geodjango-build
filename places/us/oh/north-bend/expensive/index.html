Home prices

The median home price in North Bend is $325,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $127,080 (64%) higher. It is approximately $108,000 (49%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $187,500 (135%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In North Bend the median rent is $621 per month. This is approximately $341 (35%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $441 (41%) lower, and it is about $117 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
