Home prices

The median home value in Bellville is $142,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $47,980 (50%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $75,000 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $9,100 (6%) lower.

Rents

Bellville's median rent is $618 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30 (4%) lower. It is roughly $444 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $343 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
