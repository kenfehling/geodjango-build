In comparison to other places in this area, Antwerp is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Toledo.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Antwerp is $77,400. It is roughly $13,580 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $140,100 (64%) lower, and it is about $3,400 (4%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In Antwerp the median monthly rent is $591. It is roughly $143 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $471 (44%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Toledo is roughly $134 (18%) lower.
