In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lakeline is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lakeline is $126,600. It is roughly $10,860 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $90,900 (41%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $57,000 (81%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Lakeline is $938 per month. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas.It is about $124 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $219 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
