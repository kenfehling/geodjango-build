Home prices

The median home in Grand River costs $122,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,580 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $94,900 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $53,000 (76%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Grand River's median rent is $950 a month. It is approximately $125 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $112 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $231 (32%) higher.
