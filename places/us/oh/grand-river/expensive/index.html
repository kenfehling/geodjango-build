Home prices

The median home value in Grand River is $122,600. It is about $14,580 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $94,900 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $53,000 (76%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Grand River the median rent is $950 a month. This is about $125 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $112 (10%) lower, and is about $231 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
