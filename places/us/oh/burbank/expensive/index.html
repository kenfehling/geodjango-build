In comparison to the surrounding areas, Burbank is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Burbank costs $92,000. This is approximately $30,840 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $125,500 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $59,600 (39%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burbank is $713. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $79 (10%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $349 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $248 (25%) lower.
