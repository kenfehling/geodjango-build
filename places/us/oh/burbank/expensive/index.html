Home prices

The cost of the median home in Burbank is $92,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $30,840 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $125,500 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $59,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burbank is $713. It is roughly $79 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $349 (32%) lower, and is about $248 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
