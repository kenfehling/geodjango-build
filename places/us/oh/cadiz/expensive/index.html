Home prices

The median home value in Cadiz is $83,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $7,420 (9%) higher. It is about $134,400 (61%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $13,500 (19%) higher.

Rents

Cadiz's median rent is $595 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $84 (12%) lower. It is roughly $467 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $124 (17%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
