Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mayfield is $1,166. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $74 (6%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $104 (9%) higher, and is approximately $447 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
