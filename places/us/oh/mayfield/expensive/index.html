Home prices

The median home in Mayfield is valued at $234,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $3,980 (1%) lower. It is roughly $16,500 (7%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $164,400 (236%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mayfield is $1,166. It is roughly $74 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $104 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $447 (62%) higher.
