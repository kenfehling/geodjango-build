Home prices

The value of the median home in Mayfield is $234,000. It is about $3,980 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $16,500 (7%) higher, and it is approximately $164,400 (236%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Mayfield's median rent is $1,166 per month. It is approximately $74 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $104 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $447 (62%) higher.
