Home prices

The median home price in Mayfield is $234,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $3,980 (1%) lower.It is roughly $16,500 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $164,400 (236%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Mayfield's median rent is $1,166 a month. It is about $74 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $104 (9%) higher, and it is about $447 (62%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
