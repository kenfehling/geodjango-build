Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mayfield is $234,000. It is approximately $3,980 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $16,500 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $164,400 (236%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mayfield is $1,166 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $74 (6%) higher.It is about $104 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $447 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
