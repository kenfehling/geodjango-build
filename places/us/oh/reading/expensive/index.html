Compared to its surrounding areas, Reading is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Reading's median home cost is $129,400. This is about $14,440 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $88,100 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $8,600 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Reading the median rent is $726 a month. It is roughly $170 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $336 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $12 (1%) lower.
