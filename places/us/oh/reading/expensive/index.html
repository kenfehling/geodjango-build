Home prices

The median home in Reading is valued at $129,400. This is approximately $14,440 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $88,100 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $8,600 (6%) lower.

Rents

Reading's median rent is $726 per month. This is roughly $170 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $336 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
