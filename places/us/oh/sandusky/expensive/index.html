In comparison to its nearby areas, Sandusky is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Sandusky is valued at $86,600. It is about $91,220 (51%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $130,900 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $37,400 (76%) higher.

Rents

In Sandusky the median monthly rent is $724. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $98 (11%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $338 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $100 (12%) lower.
