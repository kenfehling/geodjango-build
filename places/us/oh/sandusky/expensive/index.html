In comparison to other places in this area, Sandusky is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Sandusky is valued at $86,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $91,220 (51%) lower. It is approximately $130,900 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $37,400 (76%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Sandusky the median monthly rent is $724. It is approximately $98 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $100 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
