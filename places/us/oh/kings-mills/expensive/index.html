Compared to its surrounding areas, Kings Mills is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Kings Mills is valued at $229,900. It is about $16,520 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $12,400 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $78,300 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kings Mills is $1,488 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $344 (30%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $426 (40%) higher, and is about $527 (54%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
