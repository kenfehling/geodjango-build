Home prices

The value of the median home in Kings Mills is $229,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $16,520 (7%) higher.It is approximately $12,400 (5%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $78,300 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kings Mills is $1,488. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $344 (30%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $426 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $527 (54%) higher.
