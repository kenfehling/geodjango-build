In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kings Mills is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Kings Mills is $229,900. This is approximately $16,520 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $12,400 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $78,300 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Kings Mills the median monthly rent is $1,488. This is approximately $344 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $426 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $527 (54%) higher.
