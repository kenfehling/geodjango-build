In comparison to other places in this area, Kings Mills is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kings Mills is valued at $229,900. It is roughly $16,520 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $12,400 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $78,300 (51%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Kings Mills's median monthly rent is $1,488. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $344 (30%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $426 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $527 (54%) higher.
