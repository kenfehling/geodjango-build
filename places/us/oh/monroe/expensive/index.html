Home prices

Monroe's median home price is $180,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $89,020 (32%) lower. It is roughly $36,700 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $29,200 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monroe is $1,072. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $66 (5%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $111 (11%) higher.
