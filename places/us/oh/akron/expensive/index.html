Compared to its nearby areas, Akron is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Akron is valued at $82,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $57,920 (41%) lower. It is roughly $135,100 (62%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $12,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

Akron's median monthly rent is $759. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $57 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $303 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $40 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
