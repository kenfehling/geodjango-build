In comparison to its surrounding areas, Akron is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Akron costs $82,400. It is roughly $57,920 (41%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $135,100 (62%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $12,800 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Akron the median rent is $759 per month. This is approximately $57 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $303 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $40 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
