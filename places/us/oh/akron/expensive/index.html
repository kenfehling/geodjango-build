In comparison to other places in this area, Akron is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Akron costs $82,400. It is roughly $57,920 (41%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $135,100 (62%) lower, and it is about $12,800 (18%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Akron is $759 a month. This is approximately $57 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $303 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $40 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
