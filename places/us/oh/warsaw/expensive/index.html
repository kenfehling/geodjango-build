Home prices

Rents

In Warsaw the median rent is $631 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $34 (5%) lower.It is about $431 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $330 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
