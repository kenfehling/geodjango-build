Rents

In Warsaw the median rent is $631 a month. This is roughly $34 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $431 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $330 (34%) lower.
