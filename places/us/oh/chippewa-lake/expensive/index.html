In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chippewa Lake is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Chippewa Lake is $136,700. This is roughly $18,600 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $80,800 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $67,100 (96%) higher.

Rents

In Chippewa Lake the median rent is $973 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $231 (31%) higher. It is roughly $89 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $254 (35%) higher.
