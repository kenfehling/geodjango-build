Home prices

The median home in Chippewa Lake is valued at $136,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $18,600 (11%) lower.It is approximately $80,800 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $67,100 (96%) higher.

Rents

Chippewa Lake's median rent is $973 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $231 (31%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $89 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $254 (35%) higher.
