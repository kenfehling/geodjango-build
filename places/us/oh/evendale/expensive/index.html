The median home price in Evendale is $295,500. This is approximately $123,540 (71%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $78,000 (35%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $157,500 (114%) higher.

Rents

Evendale's median rent is $1,325 per month. It is roughly $360 (37%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $263 (24%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $587 (79%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
