Home prices

The median home value in Champion Heights is $112,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $16,500 (17%) higher. It is about $105,200 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $42,700 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Champion Heights the median monthly rent is $711. It is approximately $52 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $351 (33%) lower, and is approximately $8 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
