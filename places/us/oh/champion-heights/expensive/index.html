Compared to the surrounding areas, Champion Heights is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Champion Heights is valued at $112,300. This is roughly $16,500 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $105,200 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $42,700 (61%) higher.

Rents

In Champion Heights the median rent is $711 a month. It is roughly $52 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $351 (33%) lower, and is about $8 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
