Home prices

The median home price in Champion Heights is $112,300. This is approximately $16,500 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $105,200 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $42,700 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Champion Heights is $711. This is about $52 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $8 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
