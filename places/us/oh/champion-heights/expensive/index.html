Home prices

Champion Heights's median home price is $112,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $16,500 (17%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $105,200 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $42,700 (61%) higher.

Rents

Champion Heights's median rent is $711 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $52 (6%) lower.It is approximately $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $8 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
