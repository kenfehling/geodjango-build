Home prices

The median home cost in Champion Heights is $112,300. It is approximately $16,500 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $105,200 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $42,700 (61%) higher.

Rents

In Champion Heights the median rent is $711 a month. It is about $52 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $351 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $8 (1%) lower.
