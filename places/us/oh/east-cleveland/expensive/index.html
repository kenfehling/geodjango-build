Compared to its surrounding areas, East Cleveland is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Cleveland is $58,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $106,620 (64%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $159,400 (73%) lower, and is approximately $11,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Cleveland is $638. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $416 (39%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $424 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $81 (11%) lower.
