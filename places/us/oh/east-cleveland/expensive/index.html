Compared to the surrounding areas, East Cleveland is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

East Cleveland's median home price is $58,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $106,620 (64%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $159,400 (73%) lower, and is about $11,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

East Cleveland's median monthly rent is $638. It is roughly $416 (39%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $424 (39%) lower, and is roughly $81 (11%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
