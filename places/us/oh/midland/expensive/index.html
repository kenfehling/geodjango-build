Home prices

The median home in Midland costs $80,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $15,600 (16%) lower. It is roughly $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $71,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Midland the median rent is $800 a month. This is about $73 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $262 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $161 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
