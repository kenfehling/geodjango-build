Compared to other places in this area, Midland is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Midland costs $80,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $15,600 (16%) lower.It is roughly $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $71,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Midland the median monthly rent is $800. This is approximately $73 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $262 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $161 (16%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
