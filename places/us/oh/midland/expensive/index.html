In comparison to its surrounding areas, Midland is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Midland's median home cost is $80,000. This is about $15,600 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $71,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Midland is $800 a month. It is roughly $73 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $262 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $161 (16%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
