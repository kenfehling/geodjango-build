Compared to its nearby areas, Fayette is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fayette costs $69,800. It is roughly $17,360 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $147,700 (67%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $20,600 (41%) higher.

Rents

Fayette's median monthly rent is $624. It is roughly $91 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $438 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $200 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
