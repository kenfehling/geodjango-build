Compared to nearby areas, Fayette is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Fayette costs $69,800. This is about $17,360 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $147,700 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $20,600 (41%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Fayette the median monthly rent is $624. It is approximately $91 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $438 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $200 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
