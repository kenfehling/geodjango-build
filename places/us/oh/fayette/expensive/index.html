Compared to its surrounding areas, Fayette is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Fayette is $69,800. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $17,360 (19%) lower.It is approximately $147,700 (67%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $20,600 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Fayette the median monthly rent is $624. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $91 (12%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $438 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $200 (24%) lower.
