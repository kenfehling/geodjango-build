In comparison to its nearby areas, Fayette is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fayette is $69,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $17,360 (19%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $147,700 (67%) lower, and is about $20,600 (41%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fayette is $624. It is about $91 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $438 (41%) lower, and is approximately $200 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
