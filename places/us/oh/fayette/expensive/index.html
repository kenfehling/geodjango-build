In comparison to its nearby areas, Fayette is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fayette costs $69,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $17,360 (19%) lower.It is about $147,700 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $20,600 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Fayette's median monthly rent is $624. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $91 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $438 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $200 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
