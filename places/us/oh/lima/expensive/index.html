Home prices

The median home in Lima costs $66,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $46,320 (41%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $150,900 (69%) lower, and is about $85,000 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Lima is $667 a month. This is about $160 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $395 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $294 (30%) lower.
