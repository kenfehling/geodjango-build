Compared to its nearby areas, West Carrollton is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Carrollton is valued at $99,000. This is about $31,900 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $118,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $52,600 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Carrollton is $845. This is roughly $12 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $217 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $116 (12%) lower.
