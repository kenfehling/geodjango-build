Compared to the surrounding areas, West Carrollton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in West Carrollton is $99,000. This is about $31,900 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $118,500 (54%) lower, and is about $52,600 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in West Carrollton is $845 per month. It is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $217 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $116 (12%) lower.
