Home prices

Boston Heights's median home price is $397,500. This is approximately $156,560 (64%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $180,000 (82%) higher, and is about $327,900 (471%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Boston Heights the median rent is $934 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $183 (16%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $128 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $215 (29%) higher.
