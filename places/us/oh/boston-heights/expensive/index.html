Home prices

The median home in Boston Heights is valued at $397,500. It is approximately $156,560 (64%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $180,000 (82%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $327,900 (471%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Boston Heights is $934 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $183 (16%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $128 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $215 (29%) higher.
