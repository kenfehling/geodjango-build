The median home cost in Bay Village is $247,900. This is approximately $4,140 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $30,400 (13%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $178,300 (256%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Bay Village the median rent is $1,040 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $11 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $22 (2%) lower, and is roughly $321 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
