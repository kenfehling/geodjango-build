Home prices

The median home in Bay Village costs $247,900. It is about $4,140 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $30,400 (13%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $178,300 (256%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Bay Village is $1,040 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $11 (1%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $22 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $321 (44%) higher.
