Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wayne Lakes is $708. It is roughly $70 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $354 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $30 (4%) lower.
