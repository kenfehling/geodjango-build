Home prices

The median home in Rossmoyne costs $119,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $124,400 (50%) lower.It is roughly $97,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $18,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rossmoyne is $912. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $35 (3%) lower.It is roughly $150 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $174 (23%) higher.
