Home prices

The median home in Rossmoyne costs $119,700. This is roughly $124,400 (50%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $97,800 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $18,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

Rossmoyne's median rent is $912 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $35 (3%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $150 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $174 (23%) higher.
