Home prices

Findlay's median home value is $136,700. This is roughly $26,040 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $80,800 (37%) lower, and is about $14,900 (9%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Findlay is $771 per month. This is approximately $13 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $190 (19%) lower.
