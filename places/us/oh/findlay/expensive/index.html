Home prices

Findlay's median home cost is $136,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $26,040 (23%) higher.It is roughly $80,800 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $14,900 (9%) lower.

Rents

In Findlay the median rent is $771 a month. This is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $291 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $190 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
