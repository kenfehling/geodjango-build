Compared to the surrounding areas, Findlay is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Columbus, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Findlay is $136,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $26,040 (23%) higher. It is approximately $80,800 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $14,900 (9%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Findlay's median rent is $771 a month. This is roughly $13 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $291 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $190 (19%) lower.
