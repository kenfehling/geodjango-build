In comparison to its nearby areas, Newark is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newark is $123,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $60,820 (33%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $94,400 (43%) lower, and is approximately $28,500 (18%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Newark the median monthly rent is $776. This is about $68 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $286 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $185 (19%) lower.
