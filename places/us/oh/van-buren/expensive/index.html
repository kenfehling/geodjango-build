Home prices

The median home in Van Buren is valued at $157,700. This is roughly $47,520 (43%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $59,800 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $6,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Van Buren is $958. This is roughly $179 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $104 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is is very similar.
