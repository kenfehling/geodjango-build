Home prices

The median home in Van Buren is valued at $157,700. Compared to nearby areas this is about $47,520 (43%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $59,800 (27%) lower, and is approximately $6,100 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Van Buren the median monthly rent is $958. This is approximately $179 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $104 (9%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Columbus.
