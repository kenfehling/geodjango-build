Home prices

The median home price in New Burlington is $119,800. It is approximately $19,280 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $97,700 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $18,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in New Burlington is $950 per month. It is about $50 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $112 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $212 (28%) higher.
