Home prices

Rents

New Burlington's median monthly rent is $950. It is about $50 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $112 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $212 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
