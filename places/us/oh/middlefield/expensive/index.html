Home prices

The median home in Middlefield costs $160,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $25,480 (18%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $90,500 (130%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Middlefield's median rent is $692 per month. This is roughly $98 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $370 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $27 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
