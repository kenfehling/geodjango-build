Compared to nearby areas, Middlefield is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Middlefield is $160,100. This is about $25,480 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $90,500 (130%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middlefield is $692. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $98 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and is about $27 (3%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
