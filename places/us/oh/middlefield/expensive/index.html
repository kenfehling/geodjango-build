In comparison to the surrounding areas, Middlefield is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Middlefield is valued at $160,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $25,480 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $57,400 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $90,500 (130%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Middlefield is $692 per month. It is approximately $98 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $370 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $27 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
