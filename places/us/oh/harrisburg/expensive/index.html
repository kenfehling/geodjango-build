Home prices

The median home value in Harrisburg is $118,400. This is roughly $21,480 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $99,100 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $33,200 (21%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Harrisburg's median rent is $1,025 a month. This is roughly $202 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $37 (3%) lower, and it is roughly $64 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
