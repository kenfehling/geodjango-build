In comparison to the surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home price in Harrisburg is $118,400. This is about $21,480 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $99,100 (45%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $33,200 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrisburg is $1,025. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $202 (24%) higher. It is about $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $64 (6%) higher.
