Home prices

The median home in Maineville is valued at $168,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $35,040 (17%) lower.It is approximately $49,100 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $16,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

Maineville's median monthly rent is $1,019. This is approximately $128 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $43 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $58 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
