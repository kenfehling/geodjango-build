Home prices

The price of the median home in Maineville is $168,400. It is about $35,040 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $49,100 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $16,800 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Maineville is $1,019 a month. It is roughly $128 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $43 (4%) lower, and is roughly $58 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
