Home prices

The median home value in Canal Fulton is $154,300. It is about $9,020 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $63,200 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $84,700 (121%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Canal Fulton is $832 per month. This is about $46 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $230 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $113 (15%) higher.
