Home prices

The median home in Marysville is valued at $190,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $50,940 (36%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $26,900 (12%) lower, and is approximately $39,000 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Marysville's median monthly rent is $1,014. It is about $110 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $48 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $53 (5%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
