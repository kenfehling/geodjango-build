Home prices

The median home in Marysville is valued at $190,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $50,940 (36%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $26,900 (12%) lower, and is approximately $39,000 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Marysville the median rent is $1,014 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $110 (12%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $48 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $53 (5%) higher.
