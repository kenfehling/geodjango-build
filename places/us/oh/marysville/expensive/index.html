Home prices

The price of the median home in Marysville is $190,600. This is roughly $50,940 (36%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $26,900 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $39,000 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Marysville's median monthly rent is $1,014. This is approximately $110 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $48 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $53 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
