Home prices

The median home in Fairfield is valued at $159,300. This is roughly $37,660 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $58,200 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $21,300 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Fairfield the median rent is $944 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $126 (11%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $118 (11%) lower, and is approximately $206 (27%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
