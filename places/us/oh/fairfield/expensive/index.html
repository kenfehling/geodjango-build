Home prices

The median home in Fairfield is valued at $159,300. This is approximately $37,660 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $58,200 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $21,300 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Fairfield the median rent is $944 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $126 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $118 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $206 (27%) higher.
