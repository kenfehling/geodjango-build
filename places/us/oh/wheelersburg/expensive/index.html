Home prices

Rents

Wheelersburg's median monthly rent is $621. This is roughly $16 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $441 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $340 (35%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
