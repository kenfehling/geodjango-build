Compared to other places in this area, Franklin is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Franklin's median home cost is $112,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $37,280 (24%) lower. It is approximately $105,400 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $39,500 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Franklin is $788 per month. This is about $105 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $173 (18%) lower.
