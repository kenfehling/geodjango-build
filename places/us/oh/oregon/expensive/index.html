Home prices

The median home cost in Oregon is $144,000. It is about $36,120 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $73,500 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $94,800 (192%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Oregon's median monthly rent is $671. It is roughly $106 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $391 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $153 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
