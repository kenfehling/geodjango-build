Compared to other places in this area, Waterville is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Waterville is $196,400. It is about $17,600 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is about $21,100 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $147,200 (299%) higher.

Rents

Waterville's median monthly rent is $1,001. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $90 (9%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $61 (5%) lower, and is roughly $177 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
