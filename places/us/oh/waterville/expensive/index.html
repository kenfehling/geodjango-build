In comparison to its surrounding areas, Waterville is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Waterville's median home value is $196,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $17,600 (9%) higher. It is about $21,100 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $147,200 (299%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Waterville the median rent is $1,001 per month. This is about $90 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $61 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $177 (21%) higher.
