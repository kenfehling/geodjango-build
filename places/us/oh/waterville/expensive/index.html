In comparison to other places in this area, Waterville is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Waterville is valued at $196,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $17,600 (9%) higher. It is about $21,100 (9%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $147,200 (299%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Waterville is $1,001 a month. This is roughly $90 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $61 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is approximately $177 (21%) higher.
