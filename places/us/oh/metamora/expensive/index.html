Much like its surrounding areas, it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Metamora costs $118,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $2,540 (2%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $98,600 (45%) lower, and it is approximately $69,700 (141%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Metamora the median rent is $832 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $30 (3%) lower. It is approximately $230 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is is very similar.
