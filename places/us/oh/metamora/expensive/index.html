The price of the median home in Metamora is $118,900. It is about $2,540 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $98,600 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $69,700 (141%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Metamora is $832. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $30 (3%) lower. It is approximately $230 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Detroit.
