Home prices

The price of the median home in Christiansburg is $85,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $11,000 (11%) lower. It is approximately $131,600 (60%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $65,700 (43%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Christiansburg the median rent is $672 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $72 (9%) lower. It is roughly $390 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $289 (30%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
