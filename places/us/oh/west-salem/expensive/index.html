Home prices

The median home value in West Salem is $82,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $18,640 (18%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $135,200 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $69,300 (45%) lower.

Rents

In West Salem the median rent is $682 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $183 (21%) lower. It is about $380 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $279 (29%) lower.
