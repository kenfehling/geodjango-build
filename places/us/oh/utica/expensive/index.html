In comparison to other places in this area, Utica is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Utica is valued at $96,600. This is approximately $35,340 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $120,900 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $55,000 (36%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Utica's median rent is $581 a month. It is about $178 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $481 (45%) lower, and is roughly $380 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
