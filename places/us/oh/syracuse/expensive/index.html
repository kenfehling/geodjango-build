Compared to the surrounding areas, Syracuse is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

Syracuse's median home cost is $103,600. This is roughly $33,980 (48%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $113,900 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $48,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Syracuse the median monthly rent is $583. It is approximately $26 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $479 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $378 (39%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
