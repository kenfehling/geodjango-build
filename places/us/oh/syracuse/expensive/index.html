Home prices

The cost of the median home in Syracuse is $103,600. It is approximately $33,980 (48%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $113,900 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $48,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

Syracuse's median monthly rent is $583. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $26 (4%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $479 (45%) lower, and is about $378 (39%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
