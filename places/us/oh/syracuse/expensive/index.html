In comparison to its surrounding areas, Syracuse is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

Syracuse's median home value is $103,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $33,980 (48%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $113,900 (52%) lower, and it is about $48,000 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Syracuse the median rent is $583 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $26 (4%) lower. It is roughly $479 (45%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $378 (39%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
