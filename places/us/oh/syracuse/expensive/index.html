Compared to other places in this area, Syracuse is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home price in Syracuse is $103,600. It is about $33,980 (48%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $113,900 (52%) lower, and is roughly $48,000 (31%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Syracuse the median rent is $583 a month. It is approximately $26 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $479 (45%) lower, and it is approximately $378 (39%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
