Compared to the surrounding areas, Caledonia is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Caledonia is $94,500. It is about $8,460 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $123,000 (56%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $57,100 (37%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Caledonia is $994. Compared to other places in this area it is about $302 (43%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $68 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
