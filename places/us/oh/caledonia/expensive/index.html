Compared to its nearby areas, Caledonia is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Caledonia's median home value is $94,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $8,460 (9%) higher. It is roughly $123,000 (56%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $57,100 (37%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Caledonia the median rent is $994 per month. It is roughly $302 (43%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $68 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $33 (3%) higher.
