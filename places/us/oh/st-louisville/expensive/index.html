Home prices

The median home in St. Louisville is valued at $111,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $55,680 (33%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $105,800 (48%) lower, and is about $39,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In St. Louisville the median rent is $1,000 per month. It is roughly $261 (35%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $62 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $39 (4%) higher.
