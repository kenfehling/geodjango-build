Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Louisville is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Louisville is $111,700. It is approximately $55,680 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $105,800 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $39,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

In St. Louisville the median monthly rent is $1,000. This is about $261 (35%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $62 (5%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $39 (4%) higher.
