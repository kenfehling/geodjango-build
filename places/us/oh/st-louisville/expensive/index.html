Home prices

The median home value in St. Louisville is $111,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $55,680 (33%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $105,800 (48%) lower, and is roughly $39,900 (26%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

St. Louisville's median rent is $1,000 a month. This is approximately $261 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $62 (5%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $39 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
