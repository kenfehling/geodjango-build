Home prices

The median home value in New Knoxville is $123,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $16,740 (11%) lower. It is about $93,900 (43%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $28,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

New Knoxville's median rent is $770 per month. It is about $50 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $292 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $191 (19%) lower.
