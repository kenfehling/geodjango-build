Compared to other places in this area, New Knoxville is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in New Knoxville costs $123,600. This is about $16,740 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $93,900 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $28,000 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in New Knoxville is $770 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $50 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $292 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $191 (19%) lower.
