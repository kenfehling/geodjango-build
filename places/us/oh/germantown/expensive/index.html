Compared to nearby areas, Germantown is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Germantown is $135,000. This is roughly $12,480 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $82,500 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $16,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Germantown the median rent is $837 per month. It is about $9 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $225 (21%) lower, and is approximately $124 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
