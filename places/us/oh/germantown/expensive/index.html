Home prices

The median home price in Germantown is $135,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $12,480 (10%) higher.It is approximately $82,500 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $16,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Germantown is $837 per month. This is approximately $9 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $225 (21%) lower, and is approximately $124 (12%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
