Compared to the surrounding areas, Clayton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Clayton costs $135,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $32,700 (31%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $82,500 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $16,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clayton is $848 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $28 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $113 (11%) lower.
