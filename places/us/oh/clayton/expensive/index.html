Home prices

The median home value in Clayton is $135,000. This is roughly $32,700 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $82,500 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $16,600 (10%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Clayton the median rent is $848 per month. It is roughly $28 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $214 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $113 (11%) lower.
