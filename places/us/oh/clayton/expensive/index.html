In comparison to other places in this area, Clayton is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Clayton is $135,000. It is about $32,700 (31%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $82,500 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $16,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clayton is $848 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $28 (3%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $214 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $113 (11%) lower.
