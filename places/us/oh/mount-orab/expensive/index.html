Home prices

Rents

Mount Orab's median rent is $782 a month. This is approximately $57 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $280 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $179 (18%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
