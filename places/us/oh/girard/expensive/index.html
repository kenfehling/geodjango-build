In comparison to its surrounding areas, Girard is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Girard is valued at $77,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $16,540 (17%) lower. It is roughly $140,400 (64%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $7,500 (10%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Girard's median monthly rent is $634. This is about $35 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $428 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $85 (11%) lower.
