Home prices

The median home in Girard costs $77,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $16,540 (17%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $140,400 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $7,500 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Girard the median monthly rent is $634. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $35 (5%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $428 (40%) lower, and is roughly $85 (11%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
