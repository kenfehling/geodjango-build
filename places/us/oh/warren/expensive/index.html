The median home value in Warren is $62,100. This is approximately $25,000 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $155,400 (71%) lower, and it is approximately $7,500 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Warren's median rent is $660 per month. It is approximately $100 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $402 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $59 (8%) lower.
