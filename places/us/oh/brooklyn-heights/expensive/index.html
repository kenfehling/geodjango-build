In comparison to the surrounding areas, Brooklyn Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Brooklyn Heights is valued at $165,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $26,720 (19%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $51,700 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $96,200 (138%) higher.

Rents

In Brooklyn Heights the median rent is $1,038 a month. It is approximately $17 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $319 (44%) higher.
