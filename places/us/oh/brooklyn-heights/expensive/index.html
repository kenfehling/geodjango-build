Compared to the surrounding areas, Brooklyn Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

Brooklyn Heights's median home cost is $165,800. This is about $26,720 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $51,700 (23%) lower, and is approximately $96,200 (138%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Brooklyn Heights's median rent is $1,038 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $17 (1%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $24 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $319 (44%) higher.
