Compared to its nearby areas, Brooklyn Heights is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Brooklyn Heights's median home cost is $165,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $26,720 (19%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $51,700 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $96,200 (138%) higher.

Rents

Brooklyn Heights's median rent is $1,038 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $17 (1%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $24 (2%) lower, and it is roughly $319 (44%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
