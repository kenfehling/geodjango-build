Compared to other places in this area, Brooklyn Heights is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Brooklyn Heights's median home cost is $165,800. This is roughly $26,720 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $51,700 (23%) lower, and is approximately $96,200 (138%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Brooklyn Heights the median rent is $1,038 per month. This is approximately $17 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $24 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $319 (44%) higher.
