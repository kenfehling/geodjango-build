Home prices

Youngstown's median home cost is $44,800. It is roughly $42,560 (48%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $172,700 (79%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $24,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Youngstown is $662 per month. It is approximately $17 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $400 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $57 (7%) lower.
