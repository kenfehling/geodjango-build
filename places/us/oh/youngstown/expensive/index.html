The median home price in Youngstown is $44,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $42,560 (48%) lower. It is about $172,700 (79%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $24,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents
