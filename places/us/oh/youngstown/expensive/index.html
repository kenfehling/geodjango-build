Compared to nearby areas, Youngstown is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Youngstown's median home value is $44,800. This is approximately $42,560 (48%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $172,700 (79%) lower, and it is approximately $24,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Youngstown the median monthly rent is $662. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $17 (2%) lower. It is roughly $400 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $57 (7%) lower.
