In comparison to the surrounding areas, Elmore is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Elmore is valued at $117,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $12,100 (11%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $100,200 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $68,100 (138%) higher.

Rents

Elmore's median monthly rent is $633. It is about $85 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $429 (40%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $191 (23%) lower.
