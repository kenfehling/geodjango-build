Home prices

The median home in Lake Milton costs $122,700. It is roughly $4,680 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $94,800 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $53,100 (76%) higher.

Rents

In Lake Milton the median monthly rent is $740. This is roughly $77 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $322 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $21 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
