Home prices

The median home value in Lyons is $98,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $5,620 (5%) lower. It is approximately $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $49,600 (100%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Lyons's median rent is $713 a month. This is about $114 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $111 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
