Compared to nearby areas, Lyons is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Lyons's median home value is $98,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $5,620 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $118,700 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $49,600 (100%) higher.

Rents

Lyons's median monthly rent is $713. This is approximately $114 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and is roughly $111 (13%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
