Home prices

South Point's median home price is $119,600. It is roughly $17,240 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $97,900 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is approximately $26,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

South Point's median rent is $686 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $376 (35%) lower, and is about $49 (6%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
