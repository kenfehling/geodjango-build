Home prices

Rents

South Point's median monthly rent is $686. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $22 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $376 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $49 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
