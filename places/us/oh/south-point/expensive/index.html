Home prices

The median home value in South Point is $119,600. This is roughly $17,240 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $97,900 (45%) lower, and is approximately $26,200 (17%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

South Point's median rent is $686 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $22 (3%) higher.It is roughly $376 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is approximately $49 (6%) lower.
