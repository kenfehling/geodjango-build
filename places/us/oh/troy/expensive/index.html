Compared to other places in this area, Troy is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

Troy's median home cost is $139,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $27,160 (24%) higher.It is approximately $78,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $12,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

Troy's median rent is $788 a month. This is approximately $81 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $274 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $173 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
