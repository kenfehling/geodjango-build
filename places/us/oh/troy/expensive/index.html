Home prices

The median home in Troy is valued at $139,200. It is about $27,160 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $78,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $12,400 (8%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Troy's median rent is $788 a month. This is roughly $81 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $173 (18%) lower.
