Home prices

The median home price in Marblehead is $234,000. This is roughly $94,400 (67%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $16,500 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $184,800 (375%) higher.

Rents

Marblehead's median rent is $838 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $82 (10%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and is approximately $14 (1%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
