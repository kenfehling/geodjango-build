Home prices

The price of the median home in Marblehead is $234,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $94,400 (67%) higher. It is approximately $16,500 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $184,800 (375%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Marblehead is $838 a month. It is roughly $82 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $224 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $14 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
