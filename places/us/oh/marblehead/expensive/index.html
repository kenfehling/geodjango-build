Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Marblehead is $838 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $82 (10%) higher.It is about $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $14 (1%) higher.
