Home prices

Rents

The median rent in New Richmond is $702 per month. This is approximately $72 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $360 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $36 (4%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
