Home prices

Somerset's median home cost is $121,400. It is approximately $24,980 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $96,100 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $30,200 (19%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Somerset the median monthly rent is $600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $180 (23%) lower. It is approximately $462 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $361 (37%) lower.
