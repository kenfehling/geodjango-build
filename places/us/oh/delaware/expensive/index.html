Compared to the surrounding areas, Delaware is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Delaware is $186,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $55,260 (22%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $30,600 (14%) lower, and is roughly $35,300 (23%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Delaware the median rent is $931 a month. This is approximately $203 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $131 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $30 (3%) lower.
