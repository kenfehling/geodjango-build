Compared to the surrounding areas, Delaware is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Delaware is valued at $186,900. It is about $55,260 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $30,600 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $35,300 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Delaware's median monthly rent is $931. It is approximately $203 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $131 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $30 (3%) lower.
