In comparison to its surrounding areas, Delaware is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Delaware costs $186,900. It is approximately $55,260 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $30,600 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $35,300 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Delaware is $931 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $203 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $131 (12%) lower, and is about $30 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
