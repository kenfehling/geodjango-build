Home prices

The median home cost in Jefferson is $134,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $32,020 (31%) higher. It is about $83,200 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $64,700 (92%) higher.

Rents

In Jefferson the median monthly rent is $714. It is approximately $85 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
