Home prices

The median home in Jefferson is valued at $134,300. It is about $32,020 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $83,200 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $64,700 (92%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Jefferson is $714 per month. This is about $85 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is is very similar.
