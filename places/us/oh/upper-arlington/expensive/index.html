Compared to the surrounding areas, Upper Arlington is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Upper Arlington costs $397,300. It is roughly $62,020 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $179,800 (82%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $245,700 (162%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Upper Arlington's median monthly rent is $1,274. It is roughly $177 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $212 (19%) higher, and it is approximately $313 (32%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
