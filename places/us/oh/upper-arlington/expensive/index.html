Home prices

The median home in Upper Arlington costs $397,300. This is approximately $62,020 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $179,800 (82%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $245,700 (162%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Upper Arlington is $1,274. It is approximately $177 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $212 (19%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $313 (32%) higher.
