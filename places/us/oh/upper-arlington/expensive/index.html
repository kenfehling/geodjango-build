Rents

Upper Arlington's median rent is $1,274 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $177 (16%) higher.It is about $212 (19%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $313 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
