Compared to its surrounding areas, Richfield is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Richfield is $240,300. This is roughly $70,740 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $22,800 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $170,700 (245%) higher.

Rents

Richfield's median monthly rent is $970. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $253 (20%) lower. It is approximately $92 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $251 (34%) higher.
