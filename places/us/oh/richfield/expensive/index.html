Home prices

The median home in Richfield costs $240,300. This is about $70,740 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $22,800 (10%) higher, and is about $170,700 (245%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Richfield's median rent is $970 per month. It is approximately $253 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $92 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $251 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
