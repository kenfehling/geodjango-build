Compared to nearby areas, Richfield is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Richfield is $240,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $70,740 (22%) lower. It is roughly $22,800 (10%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $170,700 (245%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richfield is $970. This is approximately $253 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $92 (8%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $251 (34%) higher.
