Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wellston is $88,800. It is roughly $6,160 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $128,700 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $62,800 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wellston is $577. It is approximately $123 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $485 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $384 (39%) lower.
