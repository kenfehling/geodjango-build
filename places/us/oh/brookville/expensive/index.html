Home prices

Brookville's median home cost is $131,400. It is about $33,680 (34%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $86,100 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $20,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookville is $682. It is about $110 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $380 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $279 (29%) lower.
