Compared to its surrounding areas, Hillsboro is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hillsboro is $105,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $35,520 (50%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $111,800 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $45,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

Hillsboro's median monthly rent is $639. This is about $72 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $423 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $322 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
