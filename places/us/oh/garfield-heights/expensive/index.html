In comparison to the surrounding areas, Garfield Heights is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's close to average.

Home prices

Garfield Heights's median home cost is $72,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $101,740 (58%) lower.It is about $144,800 (66%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $3,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

Garfield Heights's median rent is $932 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $106 (10%) lower.It is roughly $130 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $213 (29%) higher.
