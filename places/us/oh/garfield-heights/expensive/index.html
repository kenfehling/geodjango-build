Home prices

The cost of the median home in Garfield Heights is $72,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $101,740 (58%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $144,800 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $3,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

Garfield Heights's median monthly rent is $932. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $106 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $130 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $213 (29%) higher.
