Home prices

The median home in Garfield Heights is valued at $72,700. This is approximately $101,740 (58%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $144,800 (66%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $3,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Garfield Heights is $932 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $106 (10%) lower. It is approximately $130 (12%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $213 (29%) higher.
