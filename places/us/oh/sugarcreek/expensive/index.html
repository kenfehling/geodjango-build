The median home in Sugarcreek costs $164,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $5,000 (2%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $53,200 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $12,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

Sugarcreek's median monthly rent is $754. This is approximately $101 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $207 (21%) lower.
