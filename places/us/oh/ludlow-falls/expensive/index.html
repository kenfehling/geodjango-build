Home prices

The median home in Ludlow Falls is valued at $86,800. This is about $25,380 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $130,700 (60%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $64,800 (42%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Ludlow Falls's median monthly rent is $810. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $101 (14%) higher. It is about $252 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $151 (15%) lower.
