Much like the surrounding areas, it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Delta is valued at $116,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $2,800 (2%) lower.It is roughly $101,200 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $67,100 (136%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Delta's median monthly rent is $701. Compared to other places in this area it is about $62 (8%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $361 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $123 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
