Similar to its surrounding areas, it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Delta costs $116,300. This is roughly $2,800 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $101,200 (46%) lower, and it is about $67,100 (136%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Delta is $701. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $62 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $361 (33%) lower, and is approximately $123 (14%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
