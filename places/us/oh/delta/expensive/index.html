Much like the surrounding areas, it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Delta is $116,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $2,800 (2%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $101,200 (46%) lower, and it is roughly $67,100 (136%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Delta's median monthly rent is $701. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $62 (8%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $361 (33%) lower, and is approximately $123 (14%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
