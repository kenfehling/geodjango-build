Home prices

Delta's median home value is $116,300. This is about $2,800 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $101,200 (46%) lower, and is roughly $67,100 (136%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Delta the median monthly rent is $701. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $62 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $361 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $123 (14%) lower.
