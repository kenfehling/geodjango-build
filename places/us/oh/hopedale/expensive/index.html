Home prices

The median home cost in Hopedale is $95,900. This is about $21,540 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $121,600 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $26,300 (37%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hopedale is $827 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $165 (24%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $235 (22%) lower, and is approximately $108 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
