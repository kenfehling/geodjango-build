Home prices

The median home in Northgate is valued at $125,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $1,680 (1%) higher. It is about $92,000 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $12,500 (9%) lower.

Rents

Northgate's median rent is $1,069 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $118 (12%) higher. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $331 (44%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
