Home prices

The median home in Rayland is valued at $71,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $14,520 (16%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $145,800 (67%) lower, and it is about $53,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Rayland the median rent is $586 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $78 (11%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $476 (44%) lower, and it is about $372 (38%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
