Home prices

The median home in Strongsville is valued at $206,300. It is roughly $38,980 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $11,200 (5%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $136,700 (196%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Strongsville is $1,025. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $140 (15%) higher.It is approximately $37 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $306 (42%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
