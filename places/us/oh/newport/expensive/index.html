The price of the median home in Newport is $163,900. It is about $56,300 (52%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $53,600 (24%) lower, and it is about $18,100 (12%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Newport's median rent is $545 a month. It is approximately $42 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $517 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is approximately $190 (25%) lower.
