Home prices

The median home cost in Logan is $108,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $33,480 (44%) higher.It is about $109,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $43,300 (28%) lower.

Rents

Logan's median monthly rent is $617. This is roughly $144 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $445 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $344 (35%) lower.
