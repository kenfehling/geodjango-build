Compared to its surrounding areas, Logan is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home cost in Logan is $108,300. It is approximately $33,480 (44%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $109,200 (50%) lower, and is roughly $43,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Logan is $617 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $144 (18%) lower. It is approximately $445 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $344 (35%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
