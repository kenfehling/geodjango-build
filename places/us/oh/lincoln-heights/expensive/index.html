In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lincoln Heights is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Heights costs $75,600. It is about $110,460 (59%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $141,900 (65%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $62,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lincoln Heights is $771. It is approximately $214 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $291 (27%) lower, and is about $33 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
