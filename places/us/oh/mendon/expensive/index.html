Home prices

Rents

In Mendon the median monthly rent is $820. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $117 (16%) higher.It is roughly $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Toledo is approximately $95 (13%) higher.
