Home prices

The median home in Pleasant Grove costs $138,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $41,220 (42%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $79,200 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $13,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

Pleasant Grove's median rent is $813 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $158 (24%) higher.It is roughly $249 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $148 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
