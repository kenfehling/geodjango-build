Home prices

The price of the median home in Fremont is $85,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $20,400 (19%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $131,600 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $65,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

Fremont's median monthly rent is $679. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $107 (13%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $383 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $282 (29%) lower.
