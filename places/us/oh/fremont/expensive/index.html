Home prices

The price of the median home in Fremont is $85,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $20,400 (19%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $131,600 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $65,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Fremont the median monthly rent is $679. It is about $107 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $383 (36%) lower, and it is about $282 (29%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
