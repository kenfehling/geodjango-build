Compared to other places in this area, Fremont is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fremont is $85,900. This is about $20,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $131,600 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $65,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fremont is $679 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $107 (13%) lower.It is approximately $383 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $282 (29%) lower.
