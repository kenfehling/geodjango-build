The value of the median home in Leavittsburg is $53,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $40,420 (42%) lower. It is about $163,800 (75%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $15,900 (22%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Leavittsburg is $706 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $356 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $13 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
