Home prices

The median home in Lafayette is valued at $86,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $4,580 (5%) higher.It is roughly $130,600 (60%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $64,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

Lafayette's median monthly rent is $875. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $202 (30%) higher.It is approximately $187 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $86 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
