Home prices

The median home price in Lafayette is $86,900. It is approximately $4,580 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $130,600 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $64,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Lafayette the median rent is $875 per month. It is roughly $202 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $187 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $86 (8%) lower.
