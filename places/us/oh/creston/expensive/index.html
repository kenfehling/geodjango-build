The median home in Creston is valued at $118,800. It is about $17,260 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $98,700 (45%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $32,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Creston is $833. It is approximately $90 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $128 (13%) lower.
