The median home in Apple Creek is valued at $111,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $33,860 (23%) lower. It is roughly $105,600 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $39,700 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Apple Creek is $715 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $15 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $347 (32%) lower, and is roughly $246 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
