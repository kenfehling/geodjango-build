Home prices

The cost of the median home in Union City is $56,900. This is approximately $5,860 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $160,600 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $81,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Union City is $530 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $154 (22%) lower. It is approximately $532 (50%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $208 (28%) lower.
