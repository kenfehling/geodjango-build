Westlake's median home value is $258,600. It is approximately $36,980 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $41,100 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $189,000 (271%) higher.

Rents

Westlake's median monthly rent is $1,174. It is approximately $197 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $112 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $455 (63%) higher.
