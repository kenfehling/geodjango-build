Compared to the surrounding areas, Kirtland is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kirtland is valued at $296,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $51,160 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $78,600 (36%) higher, and is about $226,500 (325%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kirtland is $782. It is roughly $383 (32%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $280 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $63 (8%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
