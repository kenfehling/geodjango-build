Rents

The median monthly rent in Kirtland is $782. It is roughly $383 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $280 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $63 (8%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
