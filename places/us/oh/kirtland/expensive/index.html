Home prices

The median home cost in Kirtland is $296,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $51,160 (14%) lower.It is about $78,600 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $226,500 (325%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Kirtland's median rent is $782 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $383 (32%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $63 (8%) higher.
