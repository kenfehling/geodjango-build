Home prices

The median home in Northridge is valued at $133,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32,680 (32%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $84,000 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $18,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Northridge the median rent is $860 a month. This is approximately $81 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $101 (10%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
