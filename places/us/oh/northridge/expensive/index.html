Home prices

The median home value in Northridge is $133,500. It is about $32,680 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $84,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $18,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

Northridge's median rent is $860 per month. This is approximately $81 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $202 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $101 (10%) lower.
