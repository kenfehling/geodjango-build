Home prices

The median home price in Brecksville is $283,000. It is approximately $69,380 (32%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $65,500 (30%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $213,400 (306%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Brecksville's median rent is $1,582 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $526 (49%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $520 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $863 (120%) higher.
