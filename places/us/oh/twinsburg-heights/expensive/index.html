Home prices

The cost of the median home in Twinsburg Heights is $113,400. This is approximately $89,980 (44%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $104,100 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $43,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

In Twinsburg Heights the median rent is $491 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $471 (48%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $571 (53%) lower, and it is about $228 (31%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
