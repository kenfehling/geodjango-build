Home prices

Rents

Rockford's median monthly rent is $675. It is roughly $66 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $387 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $89 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
