Home prices

Frankfort's median home value is $112,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $23,720 (26%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $105,500 (48%) lower, and is approximately $39,600 (26%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Frankfort the median monthly rent is $824. It is about $157 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $238 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $137 (14%) lower.
