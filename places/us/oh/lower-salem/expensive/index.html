Home prices

The median home in Lower Salem is valued at $92,500. This is roughly $13,920 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $125,000 (57%) lower, and it is about $21,400 (30%) higher compared to the city of Canton.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lower Salem is $713. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $68 (8%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $42 (6%) higher compared to the city of Canton.
