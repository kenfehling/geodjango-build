Home prices

The median home cost in Fredericksburg is $106,300. This is approximately $16,540 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $111,200 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $45,300 (29%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Fredericksburg is $717 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $21 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $345 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $244 (25%) lower.
