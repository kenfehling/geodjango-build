Home prices

The price of the median home in Fredericksburg is $106,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $16,540 (13%) lower. It is about $111,200 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $45,300 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Fredericksburg the median rent is $717 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $21 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $345 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $244 (25%) lower.
