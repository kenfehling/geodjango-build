Home prices

The price of the median home in Fredericksburg is $106,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16,540 (13%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $111,200 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $45,300 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fredericksburg is $717 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $21 (3%) higher. It is approximately $345 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $244 (25%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
