Compared to its surrounding areas, Port Clinton is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Port Clinton's median home cost is $110,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $60,560 (35%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $106,700 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $61,600 (125%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Port Clinton the median monthly rent is $621. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $166 (21%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $441 (41%) lower, and is roughly $203 (24%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
