Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Martinsville is $839. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $82 (10%) higher. It is approximately $223 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $122 (12%) lower.
