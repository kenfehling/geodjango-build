Home prices

The median home in Murray City is valued at $63,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $6,940 (9%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $154,200 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $88,300 (58%) lower.

Rents

Murray City's median rent is $531 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $183 (25%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $531 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $430 (44%) lower.
