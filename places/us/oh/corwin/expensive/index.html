Home prices

Corwin's median home price is $194,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $26,960 (16%) higher. It is roughly $22,600 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $43,300 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Corwin is $1,031 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $104 (11%) higher. It is roughly $31 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $70 (7%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
