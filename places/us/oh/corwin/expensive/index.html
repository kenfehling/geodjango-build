Home prices

The median home in Corwin is valued at $194,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $26,960 (16%) higher.It is approximately $22,600 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $43,300 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corwin is $1,031. This is roughly $104 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $31 (2%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $70 (7%) higher.
