Home prices

The median home cost in Corwin is $194,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $26,960 (16%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $22,600 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $43,300 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Corwin's median monthly rent is $1,031. It is about $104 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $31 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $70 (7%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
