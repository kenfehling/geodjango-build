Home prices

The median home cost in Conesville is $81,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $1,440 (1%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $135,600 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $69,700 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Conesville is $591. It is about $97 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $471 (44%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $370 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
