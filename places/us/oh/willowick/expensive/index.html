Home prices

The median home in Willowick costs $119,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $19,860 (14%) lower.It is approximately $98,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $49,500 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Willowick the median rent is $860 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $91 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $202 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $141 (19%) higher.
