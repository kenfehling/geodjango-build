Home prices

The median home price in Willowick is $119,100. This is approximately $19,860 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $98,400 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $49,500 (71%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Willowick is $860 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $91 (9%) lower. It is about $202 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $141 (19%) higher.
