In comparison to other places in this area, Barberton is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Barberton is $86,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $52,180 (37%) lower.It is approximately $130,700 (60%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $17,200 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Barberton is $759 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $82 (9%) lower.It is about $303 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $40 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
