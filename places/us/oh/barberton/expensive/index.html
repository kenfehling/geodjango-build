Home prices

Rents

In Barberton the median rent is $759 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $82 (9%) lower.It is roughly $303 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $40 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
