In comparison to other places in this area, Barberton is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Barberton costs $86,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $52,180 (37%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $130,700 (60%) lower, and is roughly $17,200 (24%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Barberton the median rent is $759 per month. This is about $82 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $303 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $40 (5%) higher.
