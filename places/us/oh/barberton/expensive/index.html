Compared to its surrounding areas, Barberton is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Barberton is valued at $86,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $52,180 (37%) lower. It is approximately $130,700 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $17,200 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Barberton is $759 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $82 (9%) lower. It is approximately $303 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $40 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
