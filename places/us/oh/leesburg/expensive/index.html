Home prices

The median home in Leesburg is valued at $83,000. It is approximately $10,640 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $134,500 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $68,600 (45%) lower.

Rents

Leesburg's median rent is $674 a month. It is about $32 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $388 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $287 (29%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
