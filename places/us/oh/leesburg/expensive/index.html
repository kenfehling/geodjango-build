In comparison to its surrounding areas, Leesburg is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Leesburg costs $83,000. This is approximately $10,640 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $134,500 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $68,600 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Leesburg's median rent is $674 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $32 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $388 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $287 (29%) lower.
