Compared to its nearby areas, Leesburg is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Leesburg is valued at $83,000. It is about $10,640 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $134,500 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $68,600 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Leesburg the median monthly rent is $674. This is about $32 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $388 (36%) lower, and is about $287 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
