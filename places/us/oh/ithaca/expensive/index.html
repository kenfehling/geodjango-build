Compared to other places in this area, Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ithaca costs $83,300. It is about $11,640 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $134,200 (61%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $54,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Ithaca the median monthly rent is $625. This is approximately $89 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $113 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
