Compared to its surrounding areas, Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ithaca is $83,300. It is about $11,640 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $134,200 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $54,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Ithaca the median monthly rent is $625. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $89 (12%) lower. It is about $437 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $113 (15%) lower.
