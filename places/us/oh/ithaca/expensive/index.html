Home prices

The median home in Ithaca is valued at $83,300. This is approximately $11,640 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $134,200 (61%) lower, and it is approximately $54,700 (39%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Ithaca the median monthly rent is $625. This is about $89 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $437 (41%) lower, and is about $113 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
