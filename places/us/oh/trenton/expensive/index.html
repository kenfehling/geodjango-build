In comparison to other places in this area, Trenton is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Trenton costs $136,900. This is about $21,700 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $80,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Trenton the median monthly rent is $957. It is about $34 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $105 (9%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $219 (29%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
