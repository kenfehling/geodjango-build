Compared to its surrounding areas, Trenton is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Trenton's median home cost is $136,900. This is roughly $21,700 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $80,600 (37%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $957 per month. This is approximately $34 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $105 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $219 (29%) higher.
