Compared to the surrounding areas, Trenton is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home cost in Trenton is $136,900. This is about $21,700 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $80,600 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is is very similar.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Trenton is $957. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $34 (3%) higher.It is about $105 (9%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $219 (29%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
