Home prices

The median home cost in Middleport is $64,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $11,780 (15%) lower. It is roughly $153,200 (70%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $87,300 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middleport is $582. This is about $62 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $480 (45%) lower, and is about $379 (39%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
