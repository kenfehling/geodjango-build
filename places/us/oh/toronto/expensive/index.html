In comparison to its surrounding areas, Toronto is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

Toronto's median home price is $84,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $9,340 (9%) lower. It is about $132,700 (61%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $40,200 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Toronto is $582. Compared to other places in this area it is about $84 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $480 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $376 (39%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
