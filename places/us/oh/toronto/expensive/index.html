In comparison to the surrounding areas, Toronto is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Toronto is valued at $84,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $9,340 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $132,700 (61%) lower, and it is about $40,200 (32%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Toronto's median rent is $582 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $84 (12%) lower.It is about $480 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $376 (39%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
