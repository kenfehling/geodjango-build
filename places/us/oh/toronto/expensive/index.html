In comparison to its surrounding areas, Toronto is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Toronto is $84,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $9,340 (9%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $132,700 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $40,200 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Toronto is $582 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $84 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $480 (45%) lower, and is approximately $376 (39%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
