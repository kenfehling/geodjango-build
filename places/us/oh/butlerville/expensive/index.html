Home prices

The median home price in Butlerville is $95,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $55,100 (36%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $121,900 (56%) lower, and is about $56,000 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Butlerville the median rent is $1,107 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $206 (22%) higher. It is approximately $45 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $146 (15%) higher.
