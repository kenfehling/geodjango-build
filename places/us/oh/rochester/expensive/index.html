In comparison to the surrounding areas, Rochester is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rochester is valued at $120,300. It is roughly $17,280 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $97,200 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $31,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester is $1,075. This is about $291 (37%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $13 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $114 (11%) higher.
