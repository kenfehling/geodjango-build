In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rochester is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Rochester is $120,300. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $17,280 (16%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $97,200 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $31,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

Rochester's median monthly rent is $1,075. This is approximately $291 (37%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $13 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $114 (11%) higher.
