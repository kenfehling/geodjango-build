Compared to other places in this area, Bay View is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bay View is valued at $120,200. It is about $42,160 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $97,300 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $71,000 (144%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Bay View is $867 per month. This is approximately $117 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $195 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $43 (5%) higher.
