Compared to the surrounding areas, Bay View is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Bay View is valued at $120,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $42,160 (25%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $97,300 (44%) lower, and is roughly $71,000 (144%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bay View is $867. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $117 (15%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $195 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $43 (5%) higher.
