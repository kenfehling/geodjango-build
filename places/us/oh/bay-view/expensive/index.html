Compared to nearby areas, Bay View is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Bay View's median home cost is $120,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $42,160 (25%) lower.It is about $97,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $71,000 (144%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Bay View's median rent is $867 per month. This is about $117 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $195 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $43 (5%) higher.
