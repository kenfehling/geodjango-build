Home prices

The median home in Canfield is valued at $173,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $68,720 (65%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $43,600 (20%) lower, and is about $104,300 (149%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Canfield the median rent is $586 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $158 (21%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $476 (44%) lower, and is about $133 (18%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
