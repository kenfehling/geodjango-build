In comparison to its nearby areas, North Olmsted is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Clevelandhowever, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in North Olmsted is $156,400. It is roughly $10,580 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $61,100 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $86,800 (124%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Olmsted is $902. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $160 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $183 (25%) higher.
