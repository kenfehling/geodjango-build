In comparison to its nearby areas, North Olmsted is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Olmsted is valued at $156,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $10,580 (6%) lower.It is about $61,100 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $86,800 (124%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Olmsted is $902. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $160 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $183 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
