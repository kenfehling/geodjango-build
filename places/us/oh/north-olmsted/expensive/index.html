Home prices

The median home in North Olmsted is valued at $156,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $10,580 (6%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $61,100 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $86,800 (124%) higher.

Rents

North Olmsted's median monthly rent is $902. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $160 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $183 (25%) higher.
