Home prices

The median home in Malvern costs $96,300. This is about $8,700 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $121,200 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $26,700 (38%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Malvern's median monthly rent is $677. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $9 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $385 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $42 (5%) lower.
