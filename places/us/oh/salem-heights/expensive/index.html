Home prices

The median home in Salem Heights costs $164,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $29,420 (15%) lower. It is about $53,000 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $26,500 (19%) higher.

Rents

Salem Heights's median rent is $1,316 a month. This is approximately $428 (48%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $254 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $578 (78%) higher.
