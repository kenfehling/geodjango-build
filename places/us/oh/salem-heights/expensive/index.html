Compared to the surrounding areas, Salem Heights is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Salem Heights is valued at $164,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $29,420 (15%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $53,000 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $26,500 (19%) higher.

Rents

Salem Heights's median monthly rent is $1,316. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $428 (48%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $254 (23%) higher, and it is roughly $578 (78%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
