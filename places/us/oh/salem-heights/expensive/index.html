Home prices

The median home in Salem Heights is valued at $164,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $29,420 (15%) lower.It is roughly $53,000 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $26,500 (19%) higher.

Rents

Salem Heights's median monthly rent is $1,316. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $428 (48%) higher.It is approximately $254 (23%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $578 (78%) higher.
