Compared to its surrounding areas, Salem Heights is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home price in Salem Heights is $164,500. This is approximately $29,420 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $53,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $26,500 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Salem Heights's median monthly rent is $1,316. This is approximately $428 (48%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $254 (23%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $578 (78%) higher.
