Home prices

The median home value in Salem Heights is $164,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $29,420 (15%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $53,000 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $26,500 (19%) higher.

Rents

Salem Heights's median monthly rent is $1,316. This is approximately $428 (48%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $254 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $578 (78%) higher.
