Home prices

The median home in Broadview Heights costs $228,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $24,580 (12%) higher.It is about $11,000 (5%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $158,900 (228%) higher.

Rents

In Broadview Heights the median rent is $885 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $282 (24%) lower.It is approximately $177 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $166 (23%) higher.
