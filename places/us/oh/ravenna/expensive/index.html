Compared to its surrounding areas, Ravenna is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

Ravenna's median home value is $98,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $35,360 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $118,900 (54%) lower, and it is roughly $29,000 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ravenna is $759. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $161 (17%) lower. It is approximately $303 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $40 (5%) higher.
