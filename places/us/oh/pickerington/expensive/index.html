Rents

Pickerington's median rent is $1,138 a month. It is roughly $103 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $76 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $177 (18%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
