In comparison to other places in this area, Holland is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

Holland's median home value is $162,200. This is about $27,260 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $55,300 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $113,000 (229%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Holland's median rent is $729 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $221 (23%) lower.It is about $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $95 (11%) lower.
