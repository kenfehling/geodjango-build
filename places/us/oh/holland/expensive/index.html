Home prices

The median home in Holland costs $162,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $27,260 (14%) lower. It is about $55,300 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $113,000 (229%) higher.

Rents

In Holland the median monthly rent is $729. It is about $221 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $95 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
