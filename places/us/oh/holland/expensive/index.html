In comparison to its surrounding areas, Holland is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Holland is $162,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $27,260 (14%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $55,300 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $113,000 (229%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Holland is $729 a month. This is about $221 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $95 (11%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
