Compared to its surrounding areas, Washingtonville is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Washingtonville is valued at $78,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $23,320 (22%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $139,200 (64%) lower, and it is roughly $8,700 (12%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Washingtonville the median monthly rent is $675. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $11 (1%) higher.It is about $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $44 (6%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
