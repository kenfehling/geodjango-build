Compared to other places in this area, Washingtonville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Washingtonville is $78,300. This is approximately $23,320 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $139,200 (64%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $8,700 (12%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Washingtonville the median monthly rent is $675. This is roughly $11 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $44 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
