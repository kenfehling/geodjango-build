Home prices

The median home price in Washingtonville is $78,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $23,320 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $139,200 (64%) lower, and is approximately $8,700 (12%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Washingtonville's median rent is $675 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $11 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $387 (36%) lower, and is approximately $44 (6%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
