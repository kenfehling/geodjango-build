In comparison to the surrounding areas, Washingtonville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Washingtonville is valued at $78,300. It is roughly $23,320 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $139,200 (64%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $8,700 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Washingtonville the median monthly rent is $675. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $11 (1%) higher.It is about $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $44 (6%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
