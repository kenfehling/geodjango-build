In comparison to nearby areas, Berea is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Berea is valued at $138,200. This is roughly $22,140 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $79,300 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $68,600 (98%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Berea is $847 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $65 (7%) lower.It is roughly $215 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $128 (17%) higher.
