In comparison to other places in this area, Berea is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home price in Berea is $138,200. It is roughly $22,140 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $79,300 (36%) lower, and is about $68,600 (98%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Berea's median monthly rent is $847. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $215 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $128 (17%) higher.
