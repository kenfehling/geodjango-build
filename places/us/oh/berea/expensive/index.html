Compared to its nearby areas, Berea is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Berea costs $138,200. This is about $22,140 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $79,300 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $68,600 (98%) higher.

Rents

In Berea the median monthly rent is $847. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $65 (7%) lower. It is approximately $215 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $128 (17%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
