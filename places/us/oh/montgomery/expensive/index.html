In comparison to its surrounding areas, Montgomery is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Montgomery is $374,000. It is approximately $85,480 (29%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $156,500 (71%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $236,000 (171%) higher.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $1,183. It is about $114 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $121 (11%) higher, and it is roughly $445 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
