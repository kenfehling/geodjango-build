Home prices

Montgomery's median home price is $374,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $85,480 (29%) higher. It is roughly $156,500 (71%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $236,000 (171%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montgomery is $1,183. This is about $114 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $121 (11%) higher, and it is approximately $445 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
