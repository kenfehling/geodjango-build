Compared to nearby areas, Clinton is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Clinton costs $117,800. This is about $11,900 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $99,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $48,200 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Clinton's median rent is $763 per month. This is about $22 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $44 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
