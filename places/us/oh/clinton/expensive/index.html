Home prices

Clinton's median home value is $117,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $11,900 (9%) lower. It is roughly $99,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $48,200 (69%) higher.

Rents

Clinton's median rent is $763 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $22 (2%) lower. It is approximately $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $44 (6%) higher.
