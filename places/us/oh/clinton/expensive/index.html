Home prices

The median home price in Clinton is $117,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $11,900 (9%) lower. It is roughly $99,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $48,200 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Clinton the median rent is $763 a month. It is approximately $22 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $299 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $44 (6%) higher.
