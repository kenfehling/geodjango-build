In comparison to other places in this area, Lyndhurst is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lyndhurst is $143,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27,100 (15%) lower. It is roughly $73,900 (33%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $74,000 (106%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Lyndhurst's median rent is $1,257 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $149 (13%) higher. It is roughly $195 (18%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $538 (74%) higher.
