In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cherry Grove is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cherry Grove is $182,200. It is roughly $22,260 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $35,300 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $44,200 (32%) higher.

Rents

Cherry Grove's median monthly rent is $1,693. It is approximately $717 (73%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $631 (59%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $955 (129%) higher.
