Home prices

Cherry Grove's median home price is $182,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $22,260 (10%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $35,300 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $44,200 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Cherry Grove the median monthly rent is $1,693. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $717 (73%) higher.It is about $631 (59%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $955 (129%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
