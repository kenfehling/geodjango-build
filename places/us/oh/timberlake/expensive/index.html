Compared to other places in this area, Timberlake is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Timberlake's median home price is $161,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $30,420 (23%) higher. It is roughly $56,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $91,400 (131%) higher.

Rents

In Timberlake the median rent is $981 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $54 (5%) higher. It is roughly $81 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $262 (36%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
