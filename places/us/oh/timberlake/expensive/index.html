Compared to other places in this area, Timberlake is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Timberlake is valued at $161,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $30,420 (23%) higher. It is roughly $56,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $91,400 (131%) higher.

Rents

In Timberlake the median rent is $981 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $54 (5%) higher. It is about $81 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $262 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
