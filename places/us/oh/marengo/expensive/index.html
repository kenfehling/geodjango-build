Home prices

Rents

Marengo's median rent is $675 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $96 (12%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $387 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $286 (29%) lower.
