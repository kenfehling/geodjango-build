Home prices

The price of the median home in Marengo is $120,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $11,460 (10%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $97,200 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $31,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marengo is $675 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $96 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $387 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $286 (29%) lower.
