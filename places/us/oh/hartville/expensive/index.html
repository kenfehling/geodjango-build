Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hartville is $157,100. It is about $5,500 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $60,400 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $87,500 (125%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Hartville's median rent is $724 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $126 (14%) lower. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is is very similar.
