Home prices

The median home cost in Greenwich is $87,300. This is roughly $7,740 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $130,200 (59%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $64,300 (42%) lower.

Rents

Greenwich's median rent is $680 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $55 (7%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $382 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $281 (29%) lower.
