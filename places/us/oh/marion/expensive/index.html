Home prices

The median home in Marion is valued at $74,600. It is roughly $9,960 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $142,900 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $77,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Marion the median monthly rent is $729. It is approximately $133 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $333 (31%) lower, and is approximately $232 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
