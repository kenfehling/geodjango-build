Compared to nearby areas, Marion is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Marion is valued at $74,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $9,960 (11%) lower. It is roughly $142,900 (65%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $77,000 (50%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Marion's median rent is $729 a month. It is approximately $133 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $232 (24%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
