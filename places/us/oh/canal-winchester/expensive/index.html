Rents

The median monthly rent in Canal Winchester is $1,173. It is roughly $193 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $111 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $212 (22%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
