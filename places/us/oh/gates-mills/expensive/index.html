Home prices

The median home in Gates Mills is valued at $505,100. It is approximately $43,720 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $287,600 (132%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $435,500 (625%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gates Mills is $1,571. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $483 (44%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $509 (47%) higher, and it is approximately $852 (118%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
