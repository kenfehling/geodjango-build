Home prices

The cost of the median home in Gates Mills is $505,100. It is approximately $43,720 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $287,600 (132%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $435,500 (625%) higher.

Rents

In Gates Mills the median rent is $1,571 a month. This is about $483 (44%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $509 (47%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $852 (118%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
