Compared to the surrounding areas, Gates Mills is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Gates Mills's median home cost is $505,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $43,720 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $287,600 (132%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $435,500 (625%) higher.

Rents

In Gates Mills the median monthly rent is $1,571. This is roughly $483 (44%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $509 (47%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $852 (118%) higher.
