Rents

In Willoughby Hills the median monthly rent is $925. Compared to nearby areas it is about $117 (11%) lower.It is roughly $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $206 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
