Willoughby Hills's median home cost is $254,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $3,580 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $37,200 (17%) higher, and is roughly $185,100 (265%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Willoughby Hills's median monthly rent is $925. It is approximately $117 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $137 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $206 (28%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
