Home prices

The median home in Willoughby Hills costs $254,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $3,580 (1%) higher. It is about $37,200 (17%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $185,100 (265%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Willoughby Hills is $925 per month. This is about $117 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $137 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $206 (28%) higher.
