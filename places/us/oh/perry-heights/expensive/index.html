Home prices

Perry Heights's median home value is $104,200. This is approximately $12,840 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $113,300 (52%) lower, and is about $34,600 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Perry Heights's median rent is $712 per month. This is about $68 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $350 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is is very similar.
