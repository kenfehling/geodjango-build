Compared to the surrounding areas, Perry Heights is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Perry Heights is valued at $104,200. It is roughly $12,840 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $113,300 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $34,600 (49%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Perry Heights is $712 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $68 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $350 (32%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
