Home prices

The median home in Perry Heights costs $104,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $12,840 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $113,300 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $34,600 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Perry Heights is $712 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $68 (8%) lower. It is approximately $350 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is is very similar.
