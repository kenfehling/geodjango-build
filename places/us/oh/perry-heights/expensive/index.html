Home prices

The median home in Perry Heights is valued at $104,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $12,840 (10%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $113,300 (52%) lower, and it is about $34,600 (49%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Perry Heights the median monthly rent is $712. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $68 (8%) lower.It is roughly $350 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is is very similar.
