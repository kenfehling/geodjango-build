Rents

In North Ridgeville the median rent is $968 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $53 (5%) higher. It is approximately $94 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $249 (34%) higher.
