Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Ridgeville is $968. This is roughly $53 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $94 (8%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $249 (34%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
