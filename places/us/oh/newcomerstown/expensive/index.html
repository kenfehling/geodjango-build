Rents

Newcomerstown's median monthly rent is $713. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $43 (5%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $248 (25%) lower.
