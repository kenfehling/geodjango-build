Home prices

Rents

Newcomerstown's median rent is $713 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $43 (5%) lower. It is approximately $349 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $248 (25%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
