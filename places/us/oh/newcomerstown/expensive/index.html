Home prices

The median home price in Newcomerstown is $78,800. It is about $6,240 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $138,700 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $72,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

Newcomerstown's median monthly rent is $713. Compared to nearby areas this is about $43 (5%) lower. It is about $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $248 (25%) lower.
