Compared to the surrounding areas, Edgewood is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Edgewood costs $82,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $21,260 (20%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $134,700 (61%) lower, and it is approximately $13,200 (18%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Edgewood the median monthly rent is $690. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $372 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $29 (4%) lower.
