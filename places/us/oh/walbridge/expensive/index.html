Home prices

Rents

In Walbridge the median monthly rent is $627. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $132 (17%) lower.It is about $435 (40%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $197 (23%) lower.
