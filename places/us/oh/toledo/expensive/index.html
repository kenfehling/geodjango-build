Compared to nearby areas, Toledo is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home value in Toledo is $80,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $106,160 (56%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $136,700 (62%) lower, and is about $31,600 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Toledo is $725 per month. It is approximately $181 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $337 (31%) lower, and it is about $99 (12%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
