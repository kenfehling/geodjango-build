In comparison to the surrounding areas, Toledo is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Toledo costs $80,800. This is roughly $106,160 (56%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $136,700 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $31,600 (64%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Toledo is $725 per month. It is about $181 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $99 (12%) lower.
