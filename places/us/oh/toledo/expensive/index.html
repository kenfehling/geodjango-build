In comparison to the surrounding areas, Toledo is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Toledo is valued at $80,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $106,160 (56%) lower.It is roughly $136,700 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $31,600 (64%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Toledo's median monthly rent is $725. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $181 (19%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $99 (12%) lower.
