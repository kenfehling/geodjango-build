Compared to nearby areas, Toledo is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Toledo's median home value is $80,800. This is about $106,160 (56%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $136,700 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $31,600 (64%) higher.

Rents

Toledo's median monthly rent is $725. Compared to other places in this area it is about $181 (19%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and is approximately $99 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
