Compared to other places in this area, Waldo is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Waldo is $88,300. This is about $9,160 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $63,300 (41%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Waldo the median rent is $906 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $126 (16%) higher. It is about $156 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $55 (5%) lower.
