Home prices

Brunswick's median home value is $169,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $42,580 (20%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $48,300 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $99,600 (143%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Brunswick the median rent is $912 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is is very similar. It is approximately $150 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $193 (26%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
