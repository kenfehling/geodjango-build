In comparison to nearby areas, Brunswick is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brunswick costs $169,200. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $42,580 (20%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $48,300 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $99,600 (143%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brunswick is $912. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. It is about $150 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $193 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
