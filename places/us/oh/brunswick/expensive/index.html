In comparison to other places in this area, Brunswick is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brunswick costs $169,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $42,580 (20%) lower. It is approximately $48,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $99,600 (143%) higher.

Rents

In Brunswick the median rent is $912 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $150 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $193 (26%) higher.
