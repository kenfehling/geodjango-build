Home prices

The median home in Stratton costs $61,400. This is about $1,640 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $156,100 (71%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $8,200 (11%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Stratton's median monthly rent is $667. It is approximately $114 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $395 (37%) lower, and is about $52 (7%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
