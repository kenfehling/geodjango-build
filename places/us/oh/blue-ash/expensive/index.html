Home prices

Blue Ash's median home price is $284,700. This is roughly $65,900 (30%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $67,200 (30%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $146,700 (106%) higher.

Rents

In Blue Ash the median rent is $1,187 a month. This is roughly $145 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $125 (11%) higher, and it is roughly $449 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
