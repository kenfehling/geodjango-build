Compared to its surrounding areas, Blue Ash is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Blue Ash is $284,700. This is about $65,900 (30%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $67,200 (30%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $146,700 (106%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blue Ash is $1,187. It is about $145 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $125 (11%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $449 (60%) higher.
