In comparison to other places in this area, Blue Ash is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Blue Ash costs $284,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $65,900 (30%) higher. It is approximately $67,200 (30%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $146,700 (106%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blue Ash is $1,187. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $145 (13%) higher. It is about $125 (11%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $449 (60%) higher.
