Home prices

The cost of the median home in Genoa is $109,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $4,200 (3%) lower. It is roughly $107,800 (49%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $60,500 (122%) higher.

Rents

In Genoa the median rent is $740 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $45 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $322 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $84 (10%) lower.
