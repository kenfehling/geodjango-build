Compared to its surrounding areas, Tuscarawas is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Tuscarawas is valued at $98,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $14,940 (17%) higher. It is approximately $118,700 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $29,200 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Tuscarawas's median rent is $630 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $141 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $432 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $89 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
