Compared to the surrounding areas, Tuscarawas is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Tuscarawas is $98,800. It is about $14,940 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $29,200 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Tuscarawas is $630 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $141 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $432 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $89 (12%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
