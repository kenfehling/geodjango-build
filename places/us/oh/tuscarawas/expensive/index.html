Home prices

The median home in Tuscarawas is valued at $98,800. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $14,940 (17%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $118,700 (54%) lower, and is about $29,200 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Tuscarawas's median monthly rent is $630. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $141 (18%) lower.It is about $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $89 (12%) lower.
