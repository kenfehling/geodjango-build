Compared to its surrounding areas, Tuscarawas is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Tuscarawas costs $98,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $14,940 (17%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $118,700 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $29,200 (41%) higher.

Rents

In Tuscarawas the median rent is $630 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $141 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $432 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $89 (12%) lower.
