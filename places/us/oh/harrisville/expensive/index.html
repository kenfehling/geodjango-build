Home prices

The cost of the median home in Harrisville is $79,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $20,460 (20%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $138,100 (63%) lower, and it is roughly $45,600 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Harrisville's median rent is $667 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $19 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $395 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $291 (30%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
