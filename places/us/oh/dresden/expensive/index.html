Home prices

The value of the median home in Dresden is $94,000. This is about $5,520 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $123,500 (56%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $57,600 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Dresden the median monthly rent is $617. This is approximately $52 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $445 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $344 (35%) lower.
