Home prices

The value of the median home in Dresden is $94,000. It is approximately $5,520 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $123,500 (56%) lower, and it is approximately $57,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Dresden the median monthly rent is $617. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $52 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $445 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $344 (35%) lower.
