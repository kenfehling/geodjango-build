Home prices

The median home in North Randall is valued at $94,300. It is approximately $39,080 (29%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $123,200 (56%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $24,700 (35%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

North Randall's median rent is $762 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $117 (13%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $300 (28%) lower, and is approximately $43 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
