Home prices

Rents

In Fort Jennings the median monthly rent is $734. It is approximately $46 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $328 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $9 (1%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.
