Compared to its surrounding areas, Fort Jennings is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Toledo.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Jennings costs $124,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $8,540 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $93,300 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is about $43,400 (53%) higher.

Rents

In Fort Jennings the median monthly rent is $734. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $46 (6%) higher. It is about $328 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is about $9 (1%) higher.
