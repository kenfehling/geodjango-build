In comparison to its surrounding areas, Johnstown is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home value in Johnstown is $165,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $137,040 (45%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $52,200 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $13,700 (9%) higher.

Rents

In Johnstown the median rent is $843 per month. It is roughly $406 (32%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $219 (20%) lower, and it is roughly $118 (12%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
