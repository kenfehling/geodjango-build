Compared to its nearby areas, Johnstown is a lot less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Johnstown is valued at $165,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $137,040 (45%) lower. It is approximately $52,200 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $13,700 (9%) higher.

Rents

Johnstown's median rent is $843 per month. It is approximately $406 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $219 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $118 (12%) lower.
