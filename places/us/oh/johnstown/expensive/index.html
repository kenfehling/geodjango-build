Compared to its surrounding areas, Johnstown is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Johnstown is $165,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $137,040 (45%) lower.It is approximately $52,200 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $13,700 (9%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Johnstown the median monthly rent is $843. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $406 (32%) lower.It is roughly $219 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $118 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
