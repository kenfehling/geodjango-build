In comparison to other places in this area, Johnstown is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Johnstown is valued at $165,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $137,040 (45%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $52,200 (24%) lower, and is approximately $13,700 (9%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Johnstown the median monthly rent is $843. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $406 (32%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $219 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $118 (12%) lower.
