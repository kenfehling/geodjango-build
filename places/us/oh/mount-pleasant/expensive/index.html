Home prices

Mount Pleasant's median home value is $93,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14,920 (19%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $124,300 (57%) lower, and is about $31,800 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Mount Pleasant the median monthly rent is $645. In comparison to its nearby areas this is is very similar.It is about $417 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $313 (32%) lower.
