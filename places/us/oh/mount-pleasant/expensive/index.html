Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant is valued at $93,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $14,920 (19%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $124,300 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $31,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Mount Pleasant the median monthly rent is $645. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $417 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $313 (32%) lower.
