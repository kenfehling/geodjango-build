Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant costs $93,200. This is roughly $14,920 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $124,300 (57%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $31,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Mount Pleasant the median rent is $645 a month. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $417 (39%) lower, and is approximately $313 (32%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
