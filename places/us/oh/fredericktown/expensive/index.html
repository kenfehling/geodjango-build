Compared to the surrounding areas, Fredericktown is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fredericktown is $120,800. This is about $15,760 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $96,700 (44%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $30,800 (20%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Fredericktown's median rent is $685 per month. This is roughly $154 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $276 (28%) lower.
