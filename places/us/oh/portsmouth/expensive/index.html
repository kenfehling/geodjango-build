Home prices

The median home cost in Portsmouth is $78,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $2,940 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $139,100 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $73,200 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Portsmouth the median monthly rent is $606. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $9 (1%) lower.It is about $456 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $355 (36%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
