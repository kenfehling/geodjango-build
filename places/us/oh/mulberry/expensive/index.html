Home prices

The median home in Mulberry costs $161,500. It is roughly $80,720 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $56,000 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $23,500 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mulberry is $891 a month. It is roughly $129 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $171 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $153 (20%) higher.
