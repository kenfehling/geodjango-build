Compared to other places in this area, Fairlawn is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Fairlawn's median home cost is $171,700. This is approximately $14,860 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $45,800 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $102,100 (146%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Fairlawn the median rent is $1,021 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $20 (1%) higher. It is approximately $41 (3%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $302 (42%) higher.
