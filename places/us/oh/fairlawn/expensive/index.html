Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairlawn is $171,700. It is about $14,860 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $45,800 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $102,100 (146%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Fairlawn the median rent is $1,021 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $20 (1%) higher.It is approximately $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $302 (42%) higher.
