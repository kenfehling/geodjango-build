Home prices

The median home cost in Greenville is $98,800. It is approximately $8,460 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $118,700 (54%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $39,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Greenville the median monthly rent is $619. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $87 (12%) lower. It is about $443 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $119 (16%) lower.
