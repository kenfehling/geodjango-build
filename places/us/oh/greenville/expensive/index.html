Compared to its surrounding areas, Greenville is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Greenville is $98,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $8,460 (9%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $118,700 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $39,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Greenville the median rent is $619 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $87 (12%) lower.It is roughly $443 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $119 (16%) lower.
