Home prices

The price of the median home in Greenville is $98,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $8,460 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $118,700 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $39,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

Greenville's median rent is $619 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $87 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $119 (16%) lower.
