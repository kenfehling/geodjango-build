Compared to other places in this area, Parma is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Parma costs $115,500. This is about $30,360 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $102,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $45,900 (65%) higher.

Rents

Parma's median rent is $865 a month. It is roughly $80 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $197 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $146 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
