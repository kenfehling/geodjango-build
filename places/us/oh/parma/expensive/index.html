Compared to nearby areas, Parma is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Parma costs $115,500. It is about $30,360 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $102,000 (46%) lower, and is about $45,900 (65%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Parma's median monthly rent is $865. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $80 (8%) lower. It is roughly $197 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $146 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
