Compared to its surrounding areas, Parma is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Parma's median home price is $115,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30,360 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $102,000 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $45,900 (65%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Parma is $865 a month. It is approximately $80 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $197 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $146 (20%) higher.
