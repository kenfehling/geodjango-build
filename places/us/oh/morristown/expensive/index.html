Compared to other places in this area, Morristown is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Morristown is $93,100. It is roughly $15,700 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $124,400 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $31,900 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Morristown is $629 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $15 (2%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $433 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $329 (34%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
