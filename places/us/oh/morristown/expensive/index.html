Compared to the surrounding areas, Morristown is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Morristown costs $93,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $15,700 (20%) higher.It is approximately $124,400 (57%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $31,900 (25%) lower.

Rents

Morristown's median monthly rent is $629. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $15 (2%) higher.It is approximately $433 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $329 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
