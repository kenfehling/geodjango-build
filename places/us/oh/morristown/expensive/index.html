Compared to other places in this area, Morristown is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Morristown is $93,100. This is about $15,700 (20%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $124,400 (57%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $31,900 (25%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morristown is $629. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $15 (2%) higher. It is about $433 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $329 (34%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
