Home prices

The median home in Mentor is valued at $172,400. It is roughly $20,960 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $45,100 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $102,800 (147%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mentor is $958. This is approximately $40 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $104 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $239 (33%) higher.
