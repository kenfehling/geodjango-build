Home prices

The price of the median home in Mentor is $172,400. It is about $20,960 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $45,100 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $102,800 (147%) higher.

Rents

In Mentor the median rent is $958 a month. It is approximately $40 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $104 (9%) lower, and is about $239 (33%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
