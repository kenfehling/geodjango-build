Home prices

The median home price in Bowersville is $81,800. It is about $5,220 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $135,700 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $69,800 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bowersville is $821. This is approximately $82 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $241 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $140 (14%) lower.
