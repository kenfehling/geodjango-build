Home prices

Rents

Deshler's median monthly rent is $815. This is approximately $77 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $247 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $9 (1%) lower.
