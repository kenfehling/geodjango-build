The cost of the median home in Farmersville is $114,900. This is roughly $26,460 (29%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $102,600 (47%) lower, and is about $36,700 (24%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Farmersville is $870 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $67 (8%) higher. It is about $192 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $91 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
