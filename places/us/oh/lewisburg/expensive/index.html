Compared to other places in this area, Lewisburg is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lewisburg is $109,200. This is about $17,520 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $108,300 (49%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $28,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Lewisburg is $792 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $64 (8%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and is approximately $54 (7%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
