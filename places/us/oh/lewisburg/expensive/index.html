Home prices

The price of the median home in Lewisburg is $109,200. It is approximately $17,520 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $108,300 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $28,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

Lewisburg's median monthly rent is $792. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $64 (8%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $54 (7%) higher.
