Home prices

The price of the median home in Lewisburg is $109,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $17,520 (19%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $108,300 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $28,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lewisburg is $792 a month. This is approximately $64 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $270 (25%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $54 (7%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
