Compared to nearby areas, Lewisburg is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Lewisburg's median home value is $109,200. This is about $17,520 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $108,300 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $28,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Lewisburg is $792 a month. This is approximately $64 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $270 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $54 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
