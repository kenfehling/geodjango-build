Compared to the surrounding areas, Painesville is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Painesville is $103,300. This is roughly $37,740 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $114,200 (52%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $33,700 (48%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Painesville is $780. It is approximately $79 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $282 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $61 (8%) higher.
