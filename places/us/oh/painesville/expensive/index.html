Compared to nearby areas, Painesville is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Painesville is $103,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $37,740 (26%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $114,200 (52%) lower, and is about $33,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Painesville the median monthly rent is $780. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $79 (9%) lower. It is about $282 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $61 (8%) higher.
