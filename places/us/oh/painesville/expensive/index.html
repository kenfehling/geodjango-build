In comparison to its nearby areas, Painesville is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Painesville is valued at $103,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $37,740 (26%) lower. It is roughly $114,200 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $33,700 (48%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Painesville's median monthly rent is $780. It is roughly $79 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $61 (8%) higher.
