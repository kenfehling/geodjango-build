Home prices

The median home cost in Painesville is $103,300. It is about $37,740 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $114,200 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $33,700 (48%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Painesville is $780. This is approximately $79 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $282 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $61 (8%) higher.
