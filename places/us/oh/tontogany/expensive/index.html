Home prices

The median home price in Tontogany is $126,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $25,320 (16%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $91,500 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $76,800 (156%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tontogany is $943. It is roughly $113 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $119 (11%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $119 (14%) higher.
