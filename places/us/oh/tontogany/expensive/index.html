Home prices

The median home in Tontogany is valued at $126,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $25,320 (16%) lower. It is roughly $91,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $76,800 (156%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Tontogany's median rent is $943 per month. This is about $113 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $119 (11%) lower, and is roughly $119 (14%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
