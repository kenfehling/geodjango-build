In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tontogany is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Tontogany's median home cost is $126,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $25,320 (16%) lower.It is roughly $91,500 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is roughly $76,800 (156%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tontogany is $943. It is roughly $113 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $119 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $119 (14%) higher.
