In comparison to its nearby areas, Geneva is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Geneva is $100,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $26,060 (20%) lower. It is approximately $116,600 (53%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $31,300 (44%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Geneva's median rent is $669 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $111 (14%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $393 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $50 (6%) lower.
