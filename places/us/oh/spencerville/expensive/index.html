Compared to other places in this area, Spencerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Toledo, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Spencerville's median home price is $91,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $11,700 (11%) lower. It is roughly $125,700 (57%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is about $11,000 (13%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Spencerville is $656 per month. It is about $205 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $406 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is approximately $69 (9%) lower.
