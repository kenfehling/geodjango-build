In comparison to its surrounding areas, Spencerville is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Toledo however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Spencerville's median home cost is $91,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $11,700 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $125,700 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is roughly $11,000 (13%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Spencerville is $656 a month. It is approximately $205 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $406 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $69 (9%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.
