The median home cost in Montrose-Ghent is $342,000. This is roughly $156,760 (84%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $124,500 (57%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $272,400 (391%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Montrose-Ghent is $1,465 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $497 (51%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $403 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $746 (103%) higher.
