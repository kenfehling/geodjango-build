In comparison to its nearby areas, Dublin is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Dublin is $377,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $76,840 (25%) higher. It is about $160,000 (73%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $225,900 (149%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Dublin's median monthly rent is $1,379. It is approximately $241 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $317 (29%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $418 (43%) higher.
