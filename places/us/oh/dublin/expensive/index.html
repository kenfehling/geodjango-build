Compared to its surrounding areas, Dublin is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Dublin is $377,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $76,840 (25%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $160,000 (73%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $225,900 (149%) higher.

Rents

Dublin's median rent is $1,379 a month. It is approximately $241 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $317 (29%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $418 (43%) higher.
