In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dublin is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Dublin is valued at $377,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $76,840 (25%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $160,000 (73%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $225,900 (149%) higher.

Rents

In Dublin the median monthly rent is $1,379. It is approximately $241 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $317 (29%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $418 (43%) higher.
