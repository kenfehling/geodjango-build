Compared to its surrounding areas, Dublin is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Dublin is $377,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $76,840 (25%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $160,000 (73%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $225,900 (149%) higher.

Rents

Dublin's median monthly rent is $1,379. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $241 (21%) higher. It is roughly $317 (29%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $418 (43%) higher.
