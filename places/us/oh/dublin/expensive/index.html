Compared to the surrounding areas, Dublin is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dublin is valued at $377,500. It is approximately $76,840 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $160,000 (73%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $225,900 (149%) higher.

Rents

In Dublin the median rent is $1,379 per month. It is approximately $241 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $317 (29%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $418 (43%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
