In comparison to the surrounding areas, Loveland Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Loveland Park is $192,200. It is about $24,940 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $25,300 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $40,600 (26%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Loveland Park the median rent is $1,036 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $58 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $26 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $75 (7%) higher.
