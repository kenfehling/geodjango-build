Home prices

The median home in Mayfield Heights is valued at $145,400. It is approximately $425,100 (74%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $72,100 (33%) lower, and it is about $75,800 (108%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Mayfield Heights is $927 a month. It is roughly $453 (32%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $135 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $208 (28%) higher.
