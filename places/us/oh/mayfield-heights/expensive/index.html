Home prices

Mayfield Heights's median home price is $145,400. This is roughly $425,100 (74%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $72,100 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $75,800 (108%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mayfield Heights is $927. This is roughly $453 (32%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $135 (12%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $208 (28%) higher.
