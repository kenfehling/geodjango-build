Compared to its surrounding areas, Mayfield Heights is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mayfield Heights is valued at $145,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $425,100 (74%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $72,100 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $75,800 (108%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Mayfield Heights the median monthly rent is $927. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $453 (32%) lower.It is about $135 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $208 (28%) higher.
