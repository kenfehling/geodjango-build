The median home price in Highpoint is $108,400. This is approximately $176,440 (61%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $109,100 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $29,600 (21%) lower.

Rents

Highpoint's median rent is $984 per month. This is roughly $182 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $78 (7%) lower, and is about $246 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
