Home prices

The median home value in Highpoint is $108,400. This is about $176,440 (61%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $109,100 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $29,600 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Highpoint the median monthly rent is $984. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $182 (15%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $78 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $246 (33%) higher.
