Home prices

The median home price in Rushville is $153,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $53,600 (53%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $63,900 (29%) lower, and is about $2,000 (1%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Rushville the median rent is $943 a month. This is about $245 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $119 (11%) lower, and is approximately $18 (1%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
