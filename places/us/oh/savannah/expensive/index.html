In comparison to the surrounding areas, Savannah is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Savannah is valued at $81,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,280 (14%) lower. It is roughly $136,300 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $70,400 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Savannah is $593. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $157 (20%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $469 (44%) lower, and is roughly $368 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
