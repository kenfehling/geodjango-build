Home prices

The median home in East Canton costs $109,200. This is about $22,100 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $108,300 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $39,600 (56%) higher.

Rents

East Canton's median monthly rent is $795. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $15 (1%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $267 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $76 (10%) higher.
