In comparison to other places in this area, East Canton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home value in East Canton is $109,200. It is about $22,100 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $108,300 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $39,600 (56%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Canton is $795. This is roughly $15 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $267 (25%) lower, and is roughly $76 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
