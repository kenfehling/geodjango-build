In comparison to the surrounding areas, East Canton is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Canton is $109,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $22,100 (16%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $108,300 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $39,600 (56%) higher.

Rents

In East Canton the median rent is $795 per month. This is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $267 (25%) lower, and is roughly $76 (10%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
