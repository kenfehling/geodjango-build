Home prices

The median home price in East Canton is $109,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $22,100 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $108,300 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $39,600 (56%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in East Canton is $795 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $15 (1%) lower. It is about $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $76 (10%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
