Compared to other places in this area, Fruit Hill is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fruit Hill costs $174,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $28,000 (13%) lower.It is about $42,900 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $36,600 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Fruit Hill's median rent is $1,276 per month. It is about $136 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $214 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $538 (72%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
