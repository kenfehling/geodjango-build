Rents

The median rent in Columbus Grove is $747 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $49 (6%) higher. It is about $315 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $214 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
