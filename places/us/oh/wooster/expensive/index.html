Home prices

The price of the median home in Wooster is $143,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $12,560 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $74,400 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $8,500 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Wooster the median monthly rent is $728. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $186 (20%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $334 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $233 (24%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
