Compared to its surrounding areas, Wooster is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Wooster costs $143,100. It is about $12,560 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $74,400 (34%) lower, and is approximately $8,500 (5%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Wooster's median rent is $728 per month. This is approximately $186 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $334 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $233 (24%) lower.
