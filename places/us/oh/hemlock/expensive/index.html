Home prices

The median home in Hemlock costs $65,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $5,160 (8%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $152,500 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $86,600 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hemlock is $736 per month. It is roughly $135 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $326 (30%) lower, and is about $225 (23%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
