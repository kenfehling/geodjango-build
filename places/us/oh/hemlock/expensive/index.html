Home prices

The price of the median home in Hemlock is $65,000. This is about $5,160 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $152,500 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $86,600 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Hemlock the median monthly rent is $736. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $135 (22%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $326 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $225 (23%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
