Home prices

The median home cost in Hamilton is $107,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $21,980 (17%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $110,300 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $30,800 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hamilton is $809 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $110 (11%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $253 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $71 (9%) higher.
