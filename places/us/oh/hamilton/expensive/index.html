Compared to its nearby areas, Hamilton is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hamilton is $107,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $21,980 (17%) lower.It is approximately $110,300 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $30,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Hamilton's median rent is $809 per month. This is approximately $110 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $253 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $71 (9%) higher.
