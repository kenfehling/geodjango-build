Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $107,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $21,980 (17%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $110,300 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $30,800 (22%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Hamilton's median rent is $809 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $110 (11%) lower.It is approximately $253 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $71 (9%) higher.
