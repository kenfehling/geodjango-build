Home prices

The median home in Brooklyn costs $110,600. It is roughly $5,260 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $106,900 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $41,000 (58%) higher.

Rents

Brooklyn's median rent is $733 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $133 (15%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $329 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $14 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
