Much like other places in this area, it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brooklyn costs $110,600. It is approximately $5,260 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $106,900 (49%) lower, and is roughly $41,000 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Brooklyn is $733 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $133 (15%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $329 (30%) lower, and is about $14 (1%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
