Home prices

Sheffield's median home cost is $212,100. This is roughly $41,800 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $5,400 (2%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $162,900 (331%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sheffield is $1,267 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $329 (35%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $205 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $443 (53%) higher.
