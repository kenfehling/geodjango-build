Home prices

The median home value in Columbus is $151,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $167,817 (52%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $65,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $961. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $137 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $101 (9%) lower.
