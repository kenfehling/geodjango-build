Much like its surrounding areas, it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Salem is $96,900. It is approximately $4,080 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $120,600 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $27,300 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Salem's median rent is $677 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $8 (1%) higher. It is approximately $385 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $42 (5%) lower.
