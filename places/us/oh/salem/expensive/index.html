Home prices

The cost of the median home in Salem is $96,900. It is roughly $4,080 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $120,600 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $27,300 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Salem the median rent is $677 a month. This is roughly $8 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $385 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $42 (5%) lower.
