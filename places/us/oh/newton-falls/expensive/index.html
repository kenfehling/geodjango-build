Compared to its surrounding areas, Newton Falls is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Newton Falls is $90,800. This is approximately $14,580 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $126,700 (58%) lower, and is approximately $21,200 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Newton Falls the median monthly rent is $660. It is approximately $110 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $402 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $59 (8%) lower.
