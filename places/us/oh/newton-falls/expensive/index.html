Home prices

Rents

Newton Falls's median rent is $660 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $110 (14%) lower.It is approximately $402 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $59 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
