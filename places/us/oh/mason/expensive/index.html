Home prices

The median home value in Mason is $267,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $35,920 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $49,800 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $115,700 (76%) higher.

Rents

Mason's median monthly rent is $1,348. This is roughly $67 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $286 (26%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $387 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
