Compared to its surrounding areas, Lancaster is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Lancaster's median home value is $125,900. This is roughly $14,500 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $91,600 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $25,700 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $797. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $41 (4%) lower.It is about $265 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $164 (17%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
