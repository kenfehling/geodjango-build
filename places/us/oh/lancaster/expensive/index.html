In comparison to its nearby areas, Lancaster is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lancaster is $125,900. This is roughly $14,500 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $91,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $25,700 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lancaster is $797. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $41 (4%) lower. It is roughly $265 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $164 (17%) lower.
