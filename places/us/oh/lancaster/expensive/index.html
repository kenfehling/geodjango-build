In comparison to its nearby areas, Lancaster is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $125,900. It is roughly $14,500 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $91,600 (42%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $25,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Lancaster is $797 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $41 (4%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $265 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $164 (17%) lower.
