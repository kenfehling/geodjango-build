Home prices

The median home in Nelsonville costs $78,600. This is approximately $10,620 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $138,900 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $73,000 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Nelsonville the median rent is $750 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $86 (12%) higher. It is approximately $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $211 (21%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
