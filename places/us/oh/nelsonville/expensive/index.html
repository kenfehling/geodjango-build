Compared to the surrounding areas, Nelsonville is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Nelsonville's median home price is $78,600. It is about $10,620 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $138,900 (63%) lower, and is approximately $73,000 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Nelsonville's median monthly rent is $750. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $86 (12%) higher. It is roughly $312 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $211 (21%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
