Home prices

The median home in Olmsted Falls costs $158,600. This is roughly $2,340 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $58,900 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $89,000 (127%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Olmsted Falls is $863. This is roughly $46 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $199 (18%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $144 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
