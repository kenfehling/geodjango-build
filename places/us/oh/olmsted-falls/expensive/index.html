Home prices

The median home in Olmsted Falls costs $158,600. It is approximately $2,340 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $58,900 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $89,000 (127%) higher.

Rents

Olmsted Falls's median monthly rent is $863. It is roughly $46 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $199 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $144 (20%) higher.
