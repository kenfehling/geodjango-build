Much like the surrounding areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Olmsted Falls is $158,600. It is approximately $2,340 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $58,900 (27%) lower, and is roughly $89,000 (127%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Olmsted Falls is $863 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $46 (5%) lower. It is about $199 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $144 (20%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
