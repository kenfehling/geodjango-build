Home prices

The median home in New Bremen is valued at $168,500. This is approximately $5,440 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $49,000 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $16,900 (11%) higher.

Rents

New Bremen's median rent is $637 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $73 (10%) lower. It is about $425 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $324 (33%) lower.
