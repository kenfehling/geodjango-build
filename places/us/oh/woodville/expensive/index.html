Home prices

Rents

In Woodville the median rent is $686 per month. This is roughly $38 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $376 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $138 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
