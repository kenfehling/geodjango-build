Compared to other places in this area, Woodville is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Woodville is $129,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $10,940 (9%) higher. It is approximately $88,300 (40%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $80,000 (162%) higher.

Rents

Woodville's median rent is $686 per month. This is approximately $38 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $376 (35%) lower, and is about $138 (16%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
