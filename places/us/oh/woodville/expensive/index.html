In comparison to nearby areas, Woodville is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Woodville is valued at $129,200. It is approximately $10,940 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $88,300 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is about $80,000 (162%) higher.

Rents

In Woodville the median rent is $686 a month. It is about $38 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $376 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $138 (16%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
