Home prices

The median home in Gambier is valued at $234,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $122,580 (109%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $17,400 (8%) higher, and is roughly $83,300 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Gambier's median monthly rent is $648. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $122 (15%) lower. It is about $414 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $313 (32%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
