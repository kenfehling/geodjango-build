Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Athens is $875 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $149 (20%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $187 (17%) lower, and is roughly $86 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
