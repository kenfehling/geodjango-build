Home prices

The cost of the median home in Athens is $191,700. This is approximately $95,780 (99%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $25,800 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $40,100 (26%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Athens the median monthly rent is $875. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $149 (20%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $187 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $86 (8%) lower.
