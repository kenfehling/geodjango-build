In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ashtabula is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ashtabula is $72,200. This is approximately $36,260 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $145,300 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $2,600 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ashtabula is $646 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $50 (7%) lower. It is roughly $416 (39%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $73 (10%) lower.
