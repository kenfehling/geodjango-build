Home prices

The median home in Bedford is valued at $91,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $21,320 (18%) lower. It is approximately $126,100 (57%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $21,800 (31%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Bedford is $850 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $109 (11%) lower. It is approximately $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $131 (18%) higher.
