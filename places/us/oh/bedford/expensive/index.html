Home prices

Bedford's median home price is $91,400. It is approximately $21,320 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $126,100 (57%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $21,800 (31%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Bedford the median monthly rent is $850. It is about $109 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $131 (18%) higher.
