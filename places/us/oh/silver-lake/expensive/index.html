Home prices

Rents

Silver Lake's median rent is $1,575 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $736 (87%) higher. It is roughly $513 (48%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $856 (119%) higher.
