Compared to its surrounding areas, Lowellville is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lowellville is $77,500. This is roughly $19,960 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $140,000 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $7,900 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Lowellville the median rent is $588 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $155 (20%) lower. It is roughly $474 (44%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $131 (18%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
