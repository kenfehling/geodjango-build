Home prices

The value of the median home in Lowellville is $77,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $19,960 (20%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $140,000 (64%) lower, and it is about $7,900 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Lowellville's median rent is $588 a month. This is roughly $155 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $474 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $131 (18%) lower.
