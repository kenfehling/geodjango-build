Home prices

Andover's median home value is $87,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $7,340 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $130,300 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $17,600 (25%) higher.

Rents

In Andover the median rent is $504 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $116 (18%) lower. It is approximately $558 (52%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $215 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
