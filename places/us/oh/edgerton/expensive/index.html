In comparison to the surrounding areas, Edgerton is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Toledo however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Edgerton is $87,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $20,480 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $129,900 (59%) lower, and it is roughly $6,800 (8%) higher compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

The median rent in Edgerton is $676 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $23 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $386 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Toledo it is about $49 (6%) lower.
