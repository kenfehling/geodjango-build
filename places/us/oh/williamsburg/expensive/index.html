Home prices

The cost of the median home in Williamsburg is $123,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $8,440 (6%) lower. It is roughly $94,500 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $28,600 (18%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Williamsburg's median rent is $635 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $109 (14%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $427 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $326 (33%) lower.
