Compared to the surrounding areas, Mansfield is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Mansfield costs $79,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $36,820 (31%) lower. It is about $137,900 (63%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $72,000 (47%) lower.

Rents

Mansfield's median rent is $634 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $50 (7%) lower. It is approximately $428 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $327 (34%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
