Home prices

The value of the median home in West Liberty is $128,300. This is roughly $21,440 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $89,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $23,300 (15%) lower.

Rents

In West Liberty the median rent is $555 per month. This is roughly $234 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $507 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $406 (42%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
