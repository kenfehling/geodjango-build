Compared to its surrounding areas, Lexington is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lexington is $136,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $29,620 (27%) higher. It is approximately $81,300 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $15,400 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lexington is $720 a month. It is about $36 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $342 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $241 (25%) lower.
