Home prices

The median home in Lexington costs $136,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $29,620 (27%) higher. It is approximately $81,300 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $15,400 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Lexington's median monthly rent is $720. It is about $36 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $342 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $241 (25%) lower.
