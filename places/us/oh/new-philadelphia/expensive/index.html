Compared to the surrounding areas, New Philadelphia is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Philadelphia costs $126,100. This is roughly $27,940 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $91,400 (42%) lower, and it is approximately $56,500 (81%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in New Philadelphia is $792 a month. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $270 (25%) lower, and is about $73 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
