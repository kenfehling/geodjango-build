In comparison to its nearby areas, New Philadelphia is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in New Philadelphia is valued at $126,100. It is about $27,940 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $91,400 (42%) lower, and is about $56,500 (81%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In New Philadelphia the median rent is $792 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $270 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $73 (10%) higher.
