Home prices

The median home in Upper Sandusky is valued at $124,800. This is roughly $48,100 (62%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $92,700 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $26,800 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Upper Sandusky is $619 a month. This is about $68 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $443 (41%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $342 (35%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
