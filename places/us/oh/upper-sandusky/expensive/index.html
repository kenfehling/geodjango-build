Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Upper Sandusky is $619 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $68 (9%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $443 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $342 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
