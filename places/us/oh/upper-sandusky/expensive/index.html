Home prices

The median home cost in Upper Sandusky is $124,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $48,100 (62%) higher. It is approximately $92,700 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $26,800 (17%) lower.

Rents

Upper Sandusky's median rent is $619 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $68 (9%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $443 (41%) lower, and is about $342 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
