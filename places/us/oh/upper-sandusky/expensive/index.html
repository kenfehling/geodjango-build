Home prices

The median home in Upper Sandusky is valued at $124,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $48,100 (62%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $92,700 (42%) lower, and it is approximately $26,800 (17%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Upper Sandusky's median monthly rent is $619. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $68 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $443 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $342 (35%) lower.
