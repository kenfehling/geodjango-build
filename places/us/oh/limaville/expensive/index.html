Home prices

The price of the median home in Limaville is $76,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $10,860 (12%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $141,400 (65%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $6,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

In Limaville the median monthly rent is $864. This is roughly $105 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $198 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $145 (20%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
