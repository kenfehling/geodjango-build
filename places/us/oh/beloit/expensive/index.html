Rents

Beloit's median rent is $583 a month. This is roughly $106 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $479 (45%) lower, and is about $136 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
