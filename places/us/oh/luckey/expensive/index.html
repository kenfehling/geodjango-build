Home prices

The median home in Luckey costs $127,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $4,280 (3%) higher. It is roughly $90,500 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $77,800 (158%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

The median rent in Luckey is $719 per month. It is about $59 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $343 (32%) lower, and is about $105 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
