Home prices

The median home cost in Arcadia is $102,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $8,600 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $115,200 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $49,300 (32%) lower.

Rents

Arcadia's median rent is $980 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $212 (27%) higher. It is roughly $82 (7%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $19 (1%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
