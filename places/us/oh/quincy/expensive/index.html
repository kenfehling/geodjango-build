Home prices

The cost of the median home in Quincy is $86,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $12,460 (12%) lower.It is roughly $130,800 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $64,900 (42%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Quincy the median monthly rent is $492. Compared to nearby areas it is about $160 (24%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $570 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $469 (48%) lower.
