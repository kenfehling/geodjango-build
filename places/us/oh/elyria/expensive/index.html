Compared to other places in this area, Elyria is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home cost in Elyria is $99,100. It is approximately $57,700 (36%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $118,400 (54%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is about $49,900 (101%) higher.

Rents

Elyria's median rent is $741 a month. It is roughly $163 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $321 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $83 (10%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
