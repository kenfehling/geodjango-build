In comparison to its surrounding areas, Northfield is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Northfield is valued at $143,700. This is roughly $52,360 (26%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $73,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $74,100 (106%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northfield is $691. This is approximately $327 (32%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $371 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $28 (3%) lower.
