Compared to its surrounding areas, Northfield is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Northfield is valued at $143,700. It is roughly $52,360 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $73,800 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $74,100 (106%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Northfield the median monthly rent is $691. This is about $327 (32%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $371 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $28 (3%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
