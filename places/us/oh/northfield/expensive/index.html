Home prices

The median home in Northfield costs $143,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $52,360 (26%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $73,800 (33%) lower, and is roughly $74,100 (106%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Northfield's median rent is $691 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $327 (32%) lower.It is approximately $371 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $28 (3%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
