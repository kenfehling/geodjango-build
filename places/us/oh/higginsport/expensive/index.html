Home prices

Rents

Higginsport's median rent is $911 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $310 (51%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $151 (14%) lower, and it is roughly $173 (23%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
