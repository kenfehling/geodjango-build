Home prices

The median home in Carrollton costs $115,900. It is about $30,020 (34%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $101,600 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $46,300 (66%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Carrollton is $585 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $95 (14%) lower. It is approximately $477 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $134 (18%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
