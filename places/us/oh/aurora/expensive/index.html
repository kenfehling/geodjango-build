Home prices

The median home value in Aurora is $272,300. This is roughly $106,320 (64%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $54,800 (25%) higher, and it is roughly $202,700 (291%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Aurora is $1,494 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $523 (53%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $432 (40%) higher, and is roughly $775 (107%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
