Home prices

The median home in Aurora costs $272,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $106,320 (64%) higher.It is approximately $54,800 (25%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $202,700 (291%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Aurora the median rent is $1,494 per month. It is about $523 (53%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $432 (40%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $775 (107%) higher.
