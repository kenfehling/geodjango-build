Home prices

The median home in Aurora costs $272,300. This is about $106,320 (64%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $54,800 (25%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $202,700 (291%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Aurora's median rent is $1,494 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $523 (53%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $432 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $775 (107%) higher.
