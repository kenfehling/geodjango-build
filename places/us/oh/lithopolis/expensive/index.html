Home prices

Lithopolis's median home value is $215,600. It is roughly $51,580 (31%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is is very similar compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $64,000 (42%) higher.

Rents

Lithopolis's median monthly rent is $918. It is about $113 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $144 (13%) lower, and it is about $43 (4%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
