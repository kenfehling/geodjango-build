Home prices

The median home in Tallmadge costs $172,900. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $16,460 (10%) higher. It is about $44,600 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $103,300 (148%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Tallmadge the median rent is $775 per month. It is roughly $220 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $56 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
