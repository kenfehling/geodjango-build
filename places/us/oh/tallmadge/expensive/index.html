Home prices

The median home in Tallmadge is valued at $172,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $16,460 (10%) higher.It is roughly $44,600 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $103,300 (148%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Tallmadge the median rent is $775 a month. This is roughly $220 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and is roughly $56 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
