In comparison to the surrounding areas, Tallmadge is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Tallmadge is $172,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $16,460 (10%) higher. It is about $44,600 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $103,300 (148%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Tallmadge's median rent is $775 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $220 (22%) lower. It is about $287 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $56 (7%) higher.
