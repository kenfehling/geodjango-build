Compared to other places in this area, Amsterdam is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Amsterdam costs $47,600. This is roughly $29,480 (38%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $169,900 (78%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $22,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Amsterdam is $608 a month. It is about $103 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $454 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $111 (15%) lower.
