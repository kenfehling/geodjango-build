Home prices

The median home in Greenhills is valued at $121,400. This is approximately $11,520 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $96,100 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $16,600 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Greenhills the median monthly rent is $876. It is about $119 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $186 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $138 (18%) higher.
