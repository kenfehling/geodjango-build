Home prices

The value of the median home in Maple Ridge is $82,400. It is roughly $3,640 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $135,100 (62%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $12,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Maple Ridge the median monthly rent is $753. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $60 (8%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $309 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $34 (4%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
