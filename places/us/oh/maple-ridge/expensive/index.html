Rents

The median monthly rent in Maple Ridge is $753. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $60 (8%) higher.It is about $309 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $34 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
