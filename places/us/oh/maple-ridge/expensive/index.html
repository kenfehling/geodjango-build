Home prices

The value of the median home in Maple Ridge is $82,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $3,640 (4%) lower.It is approximately $135,100 (62%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $12,800 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Maple Ridge the median monthly rent is $753. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $60 (8%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $309 (29%) lower, and is roughly $34 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
