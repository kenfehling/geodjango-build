Home prices

The median home in Conneaut is valued at $89,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $19,920 (18%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $128,000 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $19,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

Conneaut's median rent is $663 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $76 (10%) lower.It is approximately $399 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $56 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
