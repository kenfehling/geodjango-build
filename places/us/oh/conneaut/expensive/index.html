In comparison to other places in this area, Conneaut is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Conneaut is $89,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $19,920 (18%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $128,000 (58%) lower, and is about $19,900 (28%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Conneaut is $663. This is about $76 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $399 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $56 (7%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
