Compared to other places in this area, Conneaut is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Conneaut costs $89,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $19,920 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $128,000 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $19,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

Conneaut's median monthly rent is $663. It is roughly $76 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $56 (7%) lower.
