In comparison to other places in this area, Conneaut is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Conneaut is $89,500. It is about $19,920 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $128,000 (58%) lower, and it is about $19,900 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Conneaut the median rent is $663 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $76 (10%) lower. It is roughly $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $56 (7%) lower.
