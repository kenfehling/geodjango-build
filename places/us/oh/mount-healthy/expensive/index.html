Compared to its surrounding areas, Mount Healthy is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Healthy costs $88,600. It is roughly $11,600 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $128,900 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $49,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Healthy is $765. It is roughly $164 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $297 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $27 (3%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
