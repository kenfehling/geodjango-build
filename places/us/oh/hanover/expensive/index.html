Home prices

The median home in Hanover is valued at $162,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $44,400 (37%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $55,500 (25%) lower, and it is about $10,400 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Hanover is $694 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $170 (19%) lower. It is about $368 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $267 (27%) lower.
