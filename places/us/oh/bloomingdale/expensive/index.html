Rents

The median rent in Bloomingdale is $925 per month. This is roughly $201 (27%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $137 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $33 (3%) lower.
