Home prices

The median home in Donnelsville costs $120,800. This is roughly $14,120 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $96,700 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $30,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Donnelsville the median rent is $680 a month. This is approximately $85 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $281 (29%) lower.
