Home prices

The median home value in New Concord is $146,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $57,100 (63%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $71,000 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $5,100 (3%) lower.

Rents

New Concord's median monthly rent is $694. This is about $39 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $368 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $267 (27%) lower.
