Home prices

The median home price in New Albany is $497,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $310,000 (165%) higher. It is about $280,300 (128%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $346,200 (228%) higher.

Rents

In New Albany the median rent is $1,904 a month. It is approximately $860 (82%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $842 (79%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $943 (98%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
