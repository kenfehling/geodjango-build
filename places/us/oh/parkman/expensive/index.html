In comparison to the surrounding areas, Parkman is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Parkman is $115,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $42,680 (27%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $102,500 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $45,400 (65%) higher.

Rents

Parkman's median rent is $643 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $97 (13%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $419 (39%) lower, and is roughly $76 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
