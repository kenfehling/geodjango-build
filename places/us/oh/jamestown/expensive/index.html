Home prices

Jamestown's median home price is $104,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $7,180 (6%) lower. It is roughly $113,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $47,200 (31%) lower.

Rents

Jamestown's median monthly rent is $698. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $120 (14%) lower. It is approximately $364 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $263 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
