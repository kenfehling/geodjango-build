Home prices

The median home in Jamestown is valued at $104,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $7,180 (6%) lower. It is approximately $113,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $47,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jamestown is $698. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $120 (14%) lower. It is about $364 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $263 (27%) lower.
