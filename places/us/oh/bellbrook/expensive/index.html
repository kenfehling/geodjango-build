Home prices

The median home in Bellbrook costs $180,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $6,720 (3%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $37,200 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $28,700 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bellbrook is $984 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10 (1%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $78 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $23 (2%) higher.
