Home prices

The median home in Bellbrook is valued at $180,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $6,720 (3%) higher. It is roughly $37,200 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $28,700 (18%) higher.

Rents

Bellbrook's median rent is $984 per month. This is roughly $10 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $78 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $23 (2%) higher.
