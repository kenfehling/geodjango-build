Home prices

The median home in Brimfield costs $120,800. It is approximately $21,700 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $96,700 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $51,200 (73%) higher.

Rents

Brimfield's median monthly rent is $1,010. This is about $207 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $52 (4%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $291 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
