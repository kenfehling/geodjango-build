In comparison to the surrounding areas, Uniontown is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Uniontown's median home price is $161,800. This is roughly $19,000 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $55,700 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $92,200 (132%) higher.

Rents

In Uniontown the median monthly rent is $881. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $107 (13%) higher. It is approximately $181 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $162 (22%) higher.
