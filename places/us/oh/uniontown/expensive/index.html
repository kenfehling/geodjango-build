Home prices

The median home in Uniontown is valued at $161,800. It is about $19,000 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $55,700 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $92,200 (132%) higher.

Rents

Uniontown's median monthly rent is $881. It is roughly $107 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $181 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $162 (22%) higher.
