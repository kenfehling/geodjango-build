Home prices

The median home in Coldwater is valued at $141,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $7,160 (4%) lower. It is roughly $75,800 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Wayne is approximately $26,600 (23%) higher.

Rents

Coldwater's median monthly rent is $632. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $430 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne is roughly $132 (17%) lower.
