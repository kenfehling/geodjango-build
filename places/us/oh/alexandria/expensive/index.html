In comparison to other places in this area, Alexandria is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Alexandria is $158,800. It is about $111,960 (41%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $58,700 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $7,200 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Alexandria the median rent is $1,021 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $75 (6%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $41 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $60 (6%) higher.
