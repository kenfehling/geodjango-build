Compared to other places in this area, Alexandria is a lot less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home cost in Alexandria is $158,800. This is about $111,960 (41%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $58,700 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $7,200 (4%) higher.

Rents

Alexandria's median rent is $1,021 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $75 (6%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $41 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $60 (6%) higher.
