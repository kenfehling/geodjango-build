Home prices

The median home in South Amherst is valued at $137,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $2,620 (1%) higher. It is roughly $79,800 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is approximately $88,500 (179%) higher.

Rents

In South Amherst the median monthly rent is $802. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $260 (24%) lower, and is roughly $22 (2%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
