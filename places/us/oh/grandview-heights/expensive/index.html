Home prices

The median home in Grandview Heights is valued at $356,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $41,880 (13%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $139,200 (63%) higher, and it is approximately $205,100 (135%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Grandview Heights the median rent is $1,378 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $340 (32%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $316 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $417 (43%) higher.
