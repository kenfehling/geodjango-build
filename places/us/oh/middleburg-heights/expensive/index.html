In comparison to its surrounding areas, Middleburg Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Middleburg Heights costs $162,000. This is approximately $14,400 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $55,500 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $92,400 (132%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Middleburg Heights is $908 a month. This is roughly $21 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $154 (14%) lower, and is roughly $189 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
