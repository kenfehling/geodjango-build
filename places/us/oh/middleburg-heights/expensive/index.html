Compared to nearby areas, Middleburg Heights is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Middleburg Heights is valued at $162,000. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $14,400 (9%) higher. It is approximately $55,500 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $92,400 (132%) higher.

Rents

Middleburg Heights's median monthly rent is $908. It is about $21 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $189 (26%) higher.
