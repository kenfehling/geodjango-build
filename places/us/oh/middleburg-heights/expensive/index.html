Home prices

Middleburg Heights's median home cost is $162,000. It is roughly $14,400 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $55,500 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $92,400 (132%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middleburg Heights is $908. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $21 (2%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $154 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $189 (26%) higher.
