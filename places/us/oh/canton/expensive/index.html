Compared to its surrounding areas, Canton is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Canton is valued at $71,100. This is approximately $69,400 (49%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $146,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $1,500 (2%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Canton the median monthly rent is $671. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $171 (20%) lower. It is roughly $391 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $48 (6%) lower.
