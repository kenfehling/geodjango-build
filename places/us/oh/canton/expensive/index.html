Home prices

The cost of the median home in Canton is $71,100. It is about $69,400 (49%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $146,400 (67%) lower, and it is about $1,500 (2%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Canton the median monthly rent is $671. This is roughly $171 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $391 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $48 (6%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
