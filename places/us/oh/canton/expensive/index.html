Compared to the surrounding areas, Canton is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Canton is $71,100. This is roughly $69,400 (49%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $146,400 (67%) lower, and is about $1,500 (2%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Canton is $671. This is approximately $171 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $391 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $48 (6%) lower.
