Compared to its nearby areas, Canton is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Canton costs $71,100. This is approximately $69,400 (49%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $146,400 (67%) lower, and is about $1,500 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Canton's median rent is $671 a month. This is about $171 (20%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $391 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $48 (6%) lower.
