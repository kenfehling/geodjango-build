Home prices

The median home price in Piqua is $87,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $17,440 (16%) lower. It is approximately $130,400 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $64,500 (42%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Piqua the median monthly rent is $734. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $9 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $328 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $227 (23%) lower.
