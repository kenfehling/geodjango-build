Rents

In Powell the median rent is $1,677 per month. It is approximately $609 (56%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $615 (57%) higher, and it is roughly $716 (74%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
