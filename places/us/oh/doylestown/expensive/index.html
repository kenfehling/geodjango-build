Compared to other places in this area, Doylestown is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Doylestown costs $152,200. It is about $25,740 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $65,300 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $82,600 (118%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Doylestown's median monthly rent is $689. It is approximately $132 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $30 (4%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
