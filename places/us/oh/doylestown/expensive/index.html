In comparison to its surrounding areas, Doylestown is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Doylestown is valued at $152,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $25,740 (20%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $65,300 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $82,600 (118%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Doylestown is $689. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $132 (16%) lower.It is about $373 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $30 (4%) lower.
