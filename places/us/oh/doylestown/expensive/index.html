In comparison to the surrounding areas, Doylestown is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Doylestown's median home cost is $152,200. This is roughly $25,740 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $65,300 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $82,600 (118%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Doylestown the median rent is $689 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $132 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $373 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $30 (4%) lower.
