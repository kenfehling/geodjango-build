Compared to other places in this area, Florida is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Toledo however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Florida is $80,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $15,140 (15%) lower. It is roughly $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Florida is $950 a month. This is approximately $280 (41%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $112 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo is roughly $225 (31%) higher.
