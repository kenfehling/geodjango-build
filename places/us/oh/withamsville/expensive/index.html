Home prices

The median home price in Withamsville is $147,000. It is roughly $17,420 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $70,500 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $9,000 (6%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Withamsville the median monthly rent is $822. This is about $266 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $84 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
