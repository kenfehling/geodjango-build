In comparison to its surrounding areas, Warrensville Heights is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

Warrensville Heights's median home price is $81,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $85,200 (51%) lower.It is about $136,500 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $11,400 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Warrensville Heights the median rent is $835 per month. It is approximately $11 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $227 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $116 (16%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
