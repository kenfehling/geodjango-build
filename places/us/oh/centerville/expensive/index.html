Compared to its surrounding areas, Centerville is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Centerville costs $186,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $25,760 (15%) higher. It is about $30,600 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $35,300 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $919. In comparison to its nearby areas this is is very similar. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $143 (13%) lower, and it is about $42 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
