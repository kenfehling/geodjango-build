Home prices

The value of the median home in New Waterford is $93,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $4,080 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $123,700 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $24,200 (34%) higher.

Rents

In New Waterford the median rent is $525 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $233 (30%) lower. It is about $537 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $194 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
