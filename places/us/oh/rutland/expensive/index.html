The median home in Rutland is valued at $70,600. This is about $4,220 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $146,900 (67%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $81,000 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Rutland the median rent is $800 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $200 (33%) higher. It is roughly $262 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $161 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
