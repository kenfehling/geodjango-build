Home prices

The median home in Rutland is valued at $70,600. It is approximately $4,220 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $146,900 (67%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $81,000 (53%) lower.

Rents

Rutland's median monthly rent is $800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $200 (33%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $262 (24%) lower, and is about $161 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
