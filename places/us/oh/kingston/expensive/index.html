The median home cost in Kingston is $102,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $2,340 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $114,600 (52%) lower, and is roughly $48,700 (32%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Kingston is $732 per month. This is roughly $76 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $330 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $229 (23%) lower.
