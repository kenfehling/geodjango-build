Compared to other places in this area, Blanchester is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home value in Blanchester is $95,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $8,500 (8%) lower. It is about $121,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $55,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Blanchester the median rent is $713 a month. This is about $182 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $349 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $248 (25%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
