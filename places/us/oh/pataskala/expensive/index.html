Home prices

The median home in Pataskala costs $184,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $47,300 (20%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $33,100 (15%) lower, and it is about $32,800 (21%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Pataskala the median monthly rent is $976. It is about $139 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $86 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $15 (1%) higher.
