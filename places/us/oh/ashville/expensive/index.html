Home prices

The median home value in Ashville is $154,600. It is about $26,280 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $62,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $3,000 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Ashville the median rent is $734 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $115 (13%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $328 (30%) lower, and is roughly $227 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
