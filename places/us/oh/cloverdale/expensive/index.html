Home prices

The value of the median home in Cloverdale is $77,200. This is roughly $31,880 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $3,600 (4%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In Cloverdale the median rent is $439 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $179 (28%) lower.It is about $623 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $286 (39%) lower compared to the city of Toledo.
