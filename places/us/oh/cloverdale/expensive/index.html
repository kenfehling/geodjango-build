Home prices

The median home cost in Cloverdale is $77,200. This is roughly $31,880 (29%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $140,300 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Toledo is roughly $3,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

Cloverdale's median rent is $439 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $179 (28%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $623 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Toledo it is about $286 (39%) lower.
