Home prices

The median home value in Cloverdale is $77,200. It is roughly $31,880 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $3,600 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Toledo.

Rents

In Cloverdale the median rent is $439 a month. This is about $179 (28%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $623 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Toledo it is approximately $286 (39%) lower.
