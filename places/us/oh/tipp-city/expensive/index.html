Home prices

The median home cost in Tipp City is $170,000. This is approximately $51,560 (43%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $47,500 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $18,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

Tipp City's median monthly rent is $780. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $15 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $282 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $181 (18%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
