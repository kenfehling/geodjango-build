Home prices

The median home in Seven Hills costs $174,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $9,500 (5%) lower. It is roughly $43,100 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $104,800 (150%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Seven Hills's median monthly rent is $1,207. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $181 (17%) higher. It is roughly $145 (13%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $488 (67%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
