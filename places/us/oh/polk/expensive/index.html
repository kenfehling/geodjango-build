The value of the median home in Polk is $96,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $7,780 (8%) higher. It is about $121,000 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $55,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

Polk's median rent is $904 per month. It is approximately $89 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $158 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $57 (5%) lower.
