Home prices

The median home in Polk costs $96,500. This is roughly $7,780 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $121,000 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $55,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

Polk's median rent is $904 a month. This is approximately $89 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $158 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $57 (5%) lower.
