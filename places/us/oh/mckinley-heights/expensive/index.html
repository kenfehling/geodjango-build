Home prices

The value of the median home in McKinley Heights is $80,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $5,400 (6%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $137,100 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $10,800 (15%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in McKinley Heights is $641. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $35 (5%) lower.It is roughly $421 (39%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $78 (10%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
