Compared to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland costs $69,600. This is approximately $34,133 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $147,900 (68%) lower compared to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cleveland is $719. It is about $160 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $343 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole.
