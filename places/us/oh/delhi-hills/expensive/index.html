Home prices

The price of the median home in Delhi Hills is $141,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $21,480 (13%) lower. It is about $76,400 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $3,100 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Delhi Hills is $1,377. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $605 (78%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $315 (29%) higher, and is about $639 (86%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
