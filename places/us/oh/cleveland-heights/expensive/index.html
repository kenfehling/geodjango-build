Compared to nearby areas, Cleveland Heights is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland Heights costs $128,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $10,440 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $88,800 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $59,100 (84%) higher.

Rents

Cleveland Heights's median rent is $965 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $39 (3%) lower.It is approximately $97 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $246 (34%) higher.
