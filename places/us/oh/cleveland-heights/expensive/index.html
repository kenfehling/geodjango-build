Compared to its nearby areas, Cleveland Heights is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland Heights costs $128,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $10,440 (7%) lower.It is approximately $88,800 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $59,100 (84%) higher.

Rents

Cleveland Heights's median rent is $965 per month. It is about $39 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $97 (9%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $246 (34%) higher.
