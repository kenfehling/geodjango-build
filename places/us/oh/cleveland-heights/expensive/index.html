In comparison to its nearby areas, Cleveland Heights is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cleveland Heights is $128,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $10,440 (7%) lower. It is roughly $88,800 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $59,100 (84%) higher.

Rents

In Cleveland Heights the median rent is $965 a month. It is about $39 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $97 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $246 (34%) higher.
