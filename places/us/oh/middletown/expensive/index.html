Compared to other places in this area, Middletown is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Middletown costs $97,700. This is approximately $62,180 (38%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $119,800 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $53,900 (35%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Middletown the median rent is $786 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $183 (18%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $276 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $175 (18%) lower.
