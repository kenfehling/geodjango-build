Home prices

The median home cost in Madeira is $294,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $55,800 (23%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $76,900 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $156,400 (113%) higher.

Rents

Madeira's median monthly rent is $1,336. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $368 (38%) higher. It is roughly $274 (25%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $598 (81%) higher.
