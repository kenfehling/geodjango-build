Compared to its surrounding areas, Swanton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Swanton is $128,900. This is about $24,260 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $88,600 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $79,700 (161%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Swanton is $828 a month. It is about $16 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $234 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Detroit.
