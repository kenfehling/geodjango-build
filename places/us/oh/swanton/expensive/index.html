Compared to the surrounding areas, Swanton is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Swanton is valued at $128,900. This is approximately $24,260 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $88,600 (40%) lower, and is about $79,700 (161%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Swanton's median monthly rent is $828. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $16 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $234 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is is very similar.
