Compared to other places in this area, Swanton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Swanton is $128,900. This is roughly $24,260 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $88,600 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $79,700 (161%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Swanton is $828. This is about $16 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $234 (22%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Detroit.
