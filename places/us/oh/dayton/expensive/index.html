Compared to its surrounding areas, Dayton is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Dayton is $66,800. It is approximately $40,960 (38%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $150,700 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $84,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $706 a month. It is roughly $147 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $356 (33%) lower, and is about $255 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
