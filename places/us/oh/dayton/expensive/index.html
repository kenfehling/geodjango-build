Compared to the surrounding areas, Dayton is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dayton costs $66,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $40,960 (38%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $150,700 (69%) lower, and it is approximately $84,800 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Dayton is $706 a month. It is roughly $147 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $356 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $255 (26%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
