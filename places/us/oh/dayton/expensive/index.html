Home prices

Dayton's median home value is $66,800. It is about $40,960 (38%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $150,700 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $84,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Dayton is $706 a month. This is approximately $147 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $356 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $255 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
