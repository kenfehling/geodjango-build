Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richville is $790. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $25 (3%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $272 (25%) lower, and it is about $71 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
