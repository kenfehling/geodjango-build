In comparison to its surrounding areas, Richville is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home value in Richville is $129,200. It is about $17,160 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $88,300 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $59,600 (85%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Richville's median rent is $790 a month. It is about $25 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $272 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $71 (9%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
