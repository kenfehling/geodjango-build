Home prices

Richville's median home price is $129,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $17,160 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $88,300 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $59,600 (85%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Richville's median rent is $790 a month. It is about $25 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $272 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $71 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
