Home prices

The median home in Austintown is valued at $97,300. It is roughly $12,800 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $120,200 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $27,700 (39%) higher.

Rents

Austintown's median rent is $693 a month. This is about $34 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $369 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $26 (3%) lower.
