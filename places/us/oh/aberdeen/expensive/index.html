In comparison to other places in this area, Aberdeen is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Aberdeen is $100,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $9,540 (10%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $117,500 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $38,000 (27%) lower.

Rents

Aberdeen's median rent is $568 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $89 (13%) lower. It is about $494 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $170 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
