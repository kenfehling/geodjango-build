In comparison to other places in this area, Aberdeen is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Aberdeen costs $100,000. This is about $9,540 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $117,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $38,000 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Aberdeen is $568 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $89 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $494 (46%) lower, and is approximately $170 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
