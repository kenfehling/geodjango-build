Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Forest Park is $972. This is about $21 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $90 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $234 (31%) higher.
