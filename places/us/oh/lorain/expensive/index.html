Compared to the surrounding areas, Lorain is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Detroit.

Home prices

The median home in Lorain costs $87,300. This is about $58,380 (40%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $130,200 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit it is about $38,100 (77%) higher.

Rents

In Lorain the median rent is $697 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $216 (23%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Detroit it is approximately $127 (15%) lower.
