Compared to other places in this area, Lorain is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lorain costs $87,300. It is approximately $58,380 (40%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $130,200 (59%) lower, and is about $38,100 (77%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Lorain the median rent is $697 per month. It is about $216 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $365 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $127 (15%) lower compared to the city of Detroit.
