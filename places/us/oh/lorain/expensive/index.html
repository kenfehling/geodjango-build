In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lorain is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lorain costs $87,300. This is about $58,380 (40%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $130,200 (59%) lower, and is about $38,100 (77%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Lorain the median monthly rent is $697. Compared to other places in this area this is about $216 (23%) lower. It is roughly $365 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $127 (15%) lower.
