Home prices

The median home in Sugar Grove costs $105,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $3,500 (3%) lower. It is about $112,300 (51%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $46,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

Sugar Grove's median monthly rent is $1,263. It is roughly $487 (62%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $201 (18%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $302 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
