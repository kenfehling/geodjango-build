Compared to its nearby areas, South Charleston is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

South Charleston's median home value is $99,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $17,360 (14%) lower.It is roughly $118,200 (54%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $52,300 (34%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

South Charleston's median rent is $618 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $138 (18%) lower.It is approximately $444 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $343 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
