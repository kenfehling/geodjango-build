In comparison to its nearby areas, South Charleston is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home cost in South Charleston is $99,300. This is approximately $17,360 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $118,200 (54%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $52,300 (34%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Charleston is $618. This is roughly $138 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $343 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
