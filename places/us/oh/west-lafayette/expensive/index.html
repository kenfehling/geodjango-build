Compared to other places in this area, West Lafayette is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in West Lafayette is valued at $72,500. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $7,920 (9%) lower.It is roughly $145,000 (66%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $79,100 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in West Lafayette is $635 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $80 (11%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $427 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $326 (33%) lower.
