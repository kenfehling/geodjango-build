Home prices

The median home in West Lafayette is valued at $72,500. It is about $7,920 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $145,000 (66%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $79,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

West Lafayette's median monthly rent is $635. This is roughly $80 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $427 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $326 (33%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
