In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Lafayette is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in West Lafayette is $72,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $7,920 (9%) lower. It is roughly $145,000 (66%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $79,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in West Lafayette is $635 a month. This is roughly $80 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $427 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $326 (33%) lower.
