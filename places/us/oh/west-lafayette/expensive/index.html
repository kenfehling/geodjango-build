In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Lafayette is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in West Lafayette is $72,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $7,920 (9%) lower. It is about $145,000 (66%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $79,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In West Lafayette the median monthly rent is $635. It is about $80 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $427 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $326 (33%) lower.
