Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Royalton is $208,600. This is about $42,840 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $8,900 (4%) lower, and it is about $139,000 (199%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in North Royalton is $896 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $177 (24%) higher.
