Home prices

The median home in Kettering is valued at $133,800. It is about $46,680 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $83,700 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $17,800 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kettering is $807. It is approximately $203 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $255 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $154 (16%) lower.
