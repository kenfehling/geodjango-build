Home prices

Rents

Kettering's median rent is $807 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $203 (20%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $255 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $154 (16%) lower.
