Compared to the surrounding areas, Kettering is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Kettering is $133,800. It is roughly $46,680 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $83,700 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $17,800 (11%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Kettering's median monthly rent is $807. It is approximately $203 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $255 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $154 (16%) lower.
