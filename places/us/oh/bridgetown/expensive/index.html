Home prices

The median home value in Bridgetown is $125,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $43,100 (25%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $91,700 (42%) lower, and it is approximately $12,200 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Bridgetown's median rent is $894 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $27 (2%) lower. It is roughly $168 (15%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $156 (21%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
