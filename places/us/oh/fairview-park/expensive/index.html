Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Fairview Park is $785 per month. It is roughly $147 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $277 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $66 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
