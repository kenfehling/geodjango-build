Compared to the surrounding areas, Hanoverton is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Hanoverton's median home value is $106,800. It is about $17,400 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $110,700 (50%) lower, and is approximately $37,200 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Hanoverton's median rent is $667 per month. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $395 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $52 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
