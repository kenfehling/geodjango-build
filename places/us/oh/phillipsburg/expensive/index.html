Rents

In Phillipsburg the median rent is $804 a month. It is roughly $57 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $258 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $157 (16%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
