Home prices

The median home in Glendale is valued at $281,000. It is approximately $131,700 (88%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $63,500 (29%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $143,000 (103%) higher.

Rents

In Glendale the median monthly rent is $1,026. This is approximately $23 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $36 (3%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $288 (39%) higher.
