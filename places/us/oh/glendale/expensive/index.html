Home prices

The median home cost in Glendale is $281,000. This is approximately $131,700 (88%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $63,500 (29%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $143,000 (103%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Glendale is $1,026 a month. This is roughly $23 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $36 (3%) lower, and is roughly $288 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
