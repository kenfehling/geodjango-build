Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Zanesville is $162,600. This is approximately $71,480 (78%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $54,900 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $11,000 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Zanesville is $597 a month. It is about $106 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $465 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $364 (37%) lower.
