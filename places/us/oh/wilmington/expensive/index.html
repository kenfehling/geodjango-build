Home prices

The median home price in Wilmington is $108,500. This is approximately $11,200 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $109,000 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $43,100 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Wilmington the median monthly rent is $745. This is roughly $56 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $317 (29%) lower, and is roughly $216 (22%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
