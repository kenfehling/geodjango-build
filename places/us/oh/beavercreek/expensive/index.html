Home prices

The price of the median home in Beavercreek is $194,400. It is roughly $40,300 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $23,100 (10%) lower, and is about $42,800 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Beavercreek the median monthly rent is $1,208. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $284 (30%) higher.It is roughly $146 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $247 (25%) higher.
