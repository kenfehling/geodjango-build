Home prices

Beavercreek's median home cost is $194,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $40,300 (26%) higher. It is approximately $23,100 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $42,800 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Beavercreek the median rent is $1,208 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $284 (30%) higher. It is about $146 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $247 (25%) higher.
