Compared to nearby areas, Marshallville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marshallville is valued at $106,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $22,440 (17%) lower. It is roughly $111,500 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $36,400 (52%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Marshallville is $768 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $11 (1%) higher. It is approximately $294 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $49 (6%) higher.
