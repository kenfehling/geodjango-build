Home prices

The median home cost in Marshallville is $106,000. It is approximately $22,440 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $111,500 (51%) lower, and is approximately $36,400 (52%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Marshallville the median rent is $768 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $11 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $294 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $49 (6%) higher.
