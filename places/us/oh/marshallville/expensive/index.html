Home prices

Marshallville's median home cost is $106,000. It is roughly $22,440 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $111,500 (51%) lower, and is about $36,400 (52%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marshallville is $768. It is approximately $11 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $294 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $49 (6%) higher.
