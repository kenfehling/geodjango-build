Compared to its surrounding areas, Hudson is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hudson is $345,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $116,440 (50%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $128,000 (58%) higher, and it is approximately $275,900 (396%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Hudson's median monthly rent is $1,769. It is approximately $815 (85%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $707 (66%) higher, and is about $1,050 (146%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
