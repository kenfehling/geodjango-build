In comparison to its nearby areas, Wickliffe is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Wickliffe is $121,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $23,320 (16%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $96,000 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $51,900 (74%) higher.

Rents

In Wickliffe the median monthly rent is $1,029. This is about $172 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $33 (3%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $310 (43%) higher.
