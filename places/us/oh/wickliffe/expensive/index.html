Compared to its surrounding areas, Wickliffe is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Wickliffe's median home cost is $121,500. This is roughly $23,320 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $96,000 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $51,900 (74%) higher.

Rents

In Wickliffe the median monthly rent is $1,029. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $172 (20%) higher.It is approximately $33 (3%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $310 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
