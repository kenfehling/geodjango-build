In comparison to the surrounding areas, East Liverpool is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Liverpool costs $55,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $12,060 (17%) lower. It is roughly $161,900 (74%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $14,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

In East Liverpool the median rent is $649 per month. It is approximately $25 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $413 (38%) lower, and is approximately $70 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
