Home prices

The median home in East Liverpool is valued at $55,600. This is roughly $12,060 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $161,900 (74%) lower, and it is about $14,000 (20%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in East Liverpool is $649 a month. This is approximately $25 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $413 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $70 (9%) lower.
