Home prices

The median home in Osgood costs $163,300. It is roughly $7,620 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $54,200 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $25,300 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Osgood's median rent is $675 per month. It is about $11 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $387 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $63 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
