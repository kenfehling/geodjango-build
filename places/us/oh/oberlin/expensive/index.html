In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oberlin is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Oberlin is $160,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $25,440 (18%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $56,600 (26%) lower, and it is about $111,700 (227%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Oberlin the median monthly rent is $833. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $42 (4%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $229 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $9 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
