Home prices

The median home in Oberlin costs $160,900. This is roughly $25,440 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $56,600 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $111,700 (227%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Oberlin the median rent is $833 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $42 (4%) lower.It is about $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $9 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
