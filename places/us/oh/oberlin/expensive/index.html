In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oberlin is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Oberlin's median home value is $160,900. This is about $25,440 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $56,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $111,700 (227%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

In Oberlin the median rent is $833 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $42 (4%) lower. It is approximately $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $9 (1%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
