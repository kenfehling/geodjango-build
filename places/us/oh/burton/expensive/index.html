Compared to its surrounding areas, Burton is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Burton costs $157,400. It is approximately $24,460 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $60,100 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $87,800 (126%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burton is $856. This is about $154 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $206 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $137 (19%) higher.
