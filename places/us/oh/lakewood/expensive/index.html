Compared to the surrounding areas, Lakewood is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakewood is $158,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $18,300 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $59,400 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $88,500 (127%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakewood is $797. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $9 (1%) lower. It is about $265 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $78 (10%) higher.
