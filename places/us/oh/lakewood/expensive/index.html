Home prices

The median home in Lakewood costs $158,100. This is roughly $18,300 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $59,400 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $88,500 (127%) higher.

Rents

In Lakewood the median monthly rent is $797. This is roughly $9 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $265 (24%) lower, and is roughly $78 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
