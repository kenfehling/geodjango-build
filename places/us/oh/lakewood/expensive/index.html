Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakewood is $158,100. It is approximately $18,300 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $59,400 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $88,500 (127%) higher.

Rents

In Lakewood the median monthly rent is $797. Compared to other places in this area this is about $9 (1%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $265 (24%) lower, and is about $78 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
