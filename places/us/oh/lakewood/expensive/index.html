In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lakewood is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakewood costs $158,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $18,300 (13%) higher.It is about $59,400 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $88,500 (127%) higher.

Rents

In Lakewood the median monthly rent is $797. This is approximately $9 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $265 (24%) lower, and it is about $78 (10%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
