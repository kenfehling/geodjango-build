Home prices

Willoughby's median home value is $152,700. This is roughly $83,220 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $64,800 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $83,100 (119%) higher.

Rents

In Willoughby the median rent is $940 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $65 (6%) lower.It is roughly $122 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $221 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
