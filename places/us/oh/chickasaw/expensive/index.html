Home prices

The price of the median home in Chickasaw is $187,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $19,340 (11%) higher. It is roughly $30,000 (13%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $49,500 (35%) higher.

Rents

Chickasaw's median rent is $564 per month. This is approximately $145 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $498 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $174 (23%) lower.
