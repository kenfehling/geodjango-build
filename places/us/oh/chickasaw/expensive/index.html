Home prices

The median home in Chickasaw costs $187,500. This is about $19,340 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $30,000 (13%) lower, and it is about $49,500 (35%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Chickasaw the median rent is $564 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $145 (20%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $498 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $174 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
