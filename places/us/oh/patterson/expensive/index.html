Home prices

The median home value in Patterson is $63,800. It is about $11,280 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $153,700 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $87,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

Patterson's median monthly rent is $725. It is about $69 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $236 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
