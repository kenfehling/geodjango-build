Home prices

The median home in Norwalk costs $126,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $6,500 (4%) lower.It is about $91,200 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $25,300 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Norwalk's median rent is $675 a month. It is roughly $114 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $387 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $286 (29%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
