Much like the surrounding areas, it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Parma Heights is valued at $116,500. This is approximately $2,580 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $101,000 (46%) lower, and is about $46,900 (67%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Parma Heights the median monthly rent is $838. This is approximately $19 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $119 (16%) higher.
