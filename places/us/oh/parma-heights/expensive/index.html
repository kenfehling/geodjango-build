Similar to other places in this area, it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Parma Heights costs $116,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $2,580 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $101,000 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $46,900 (67%) higher.

Rents

In Parma Heights the median rent is $838 per month. This is about $19 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $119 (16%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
