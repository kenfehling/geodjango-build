Home prices

The value of the median home in Bridgeport is $62,300. This is about $38,860 (38%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $155,200 (71%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $62,700 (50%) lower.

Rents

Bridgeport's median monthly rent is $739. It is about $49 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $219 (22%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
