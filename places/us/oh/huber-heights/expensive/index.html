Compared to its nearby areas, Huber Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Huber Heights is valued at $112,100. This is roughly $8,280 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $105,400 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $39,500 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Huber Heights the median monthly rent is $942. It is about $132 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $120 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $19 (1%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
