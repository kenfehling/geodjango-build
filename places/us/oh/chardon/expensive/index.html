Home prices

The median home in Chardon is valued at $190,200. It is about $141,260 (42%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $27,300 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $120,600 (173%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Chardon is $742 per month. This is approximately $351 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $320 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $23 (3%) higher.
