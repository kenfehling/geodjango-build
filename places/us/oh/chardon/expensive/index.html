Chardon's median home value is $190,200. This is roughly $141,260 (42%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $27,300 (12%) lower, and is approximately $120,600 (173%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Chardon the median monthly rent is $742. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $351 (32%) lower. It is roughly $320 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $23 (3%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
