Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chardon is $742. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $351 (32%) lower.It is approximately $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $23 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
