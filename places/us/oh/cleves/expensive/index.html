Compared to its surrounding areas, Cleves is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cleves is $163,300. It is roughly $67,560 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $54,200 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $25,300 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cleves is $816. This is approximately $107 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $246 (23%) lower, and is approximately $78 (10%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
