Home prices

The median home in Cleves costs $163,300. It is about $13,320 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $54,200 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $25,300 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Cleves the median monthly rent is $816. This is about $25 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $246 (23%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $78 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
