Home prices

The median home in Cleves costs $163,300. It is approximately $13,320 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $54,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $25,300 (18%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Cleves's median monthly rent is $816. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $25 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $246 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $78 (10%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
