Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cleves is $163,300. This is roughly $67,560 (29%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $54,200 (24%) lower, and it is about $25,300 (18%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Cleves the median monthly rent is $816. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $107 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $246 (23%) lower, and is approximately $78 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
