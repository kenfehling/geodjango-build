In comparison to nearby areas, Lynchburg is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg is valued at $108,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $17,700 (19%) higher. It is approximately $109,400 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $43,500 (28%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Lynchburg is $782 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is is very similar. It is about $280 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $179 (18%) lower.
