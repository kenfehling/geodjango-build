In comparison to its nearby areas, Lynchburg is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Columbus, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lynchburg is $108,100. This is roughly $17,700 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $109,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $43,500 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lynchburg is $782 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $179 (18%) lower.
