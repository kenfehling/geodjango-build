In comparison to its nearby areas, Lynchburg is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lynchburg is $108,100. This is about $17,700 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $109,400 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $43,500 (28%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Lynchburg is $782 a month. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is about $280 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $179 (18%) lower.
