Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg is valued at $108,100. It is approximately $17,700 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $109,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $43,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Lynchburg the median monthly rent is $782. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $280 (26%) lower, and it is about $179 (18%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
