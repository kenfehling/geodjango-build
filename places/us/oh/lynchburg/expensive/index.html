Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg is valued at $108,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $17,700 (19%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $109,400 (50%) lower, and it is about $43,500 (28%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Lynchburg the median rent is $782 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is about $280 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $179 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
