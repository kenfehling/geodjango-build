Home prices

The price of the median home in Cedarville is $129,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $28,880 (18%) lower. It is roughly $87,700 (40%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $21,800 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Cedarville is $599 a month. This is approximately $289 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $463 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $362 (37%) lower.
