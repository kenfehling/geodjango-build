Home prices

The median home in St. Marys is valued at $117,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $3,540 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $99,800 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $33,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

St. Marys's median rent is $756 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $10 (1%) lower. It is about $306 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $205 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
