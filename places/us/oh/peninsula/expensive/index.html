The price of the median home in Peninsula is $304,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $25,420 (9%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $86,700 (39%) higher, and is approximately $234,600 (337%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Peninsula's median rent is $1,250 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $31 (2%) higher. It is approximately $188 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $531 (73%) higher.
