Home prices

New Middletown's median home price is $117,100. This is approximately $15,800 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $100,400 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $47,500 (68%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in New Middletown is $582 a month. This is approximately $161 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $480 (45%) lower, and is about $137 (19%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
