Compared to the surrounding areas, New Middletown is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in New Middletown is $117,100. This is about $15,800 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $100,400 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $47,500 (68%) higher.

Rents

New Middletown's median rent is $582 a month. It is approximately $161 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $480 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $137 (19%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
