Home prices

Rents

Chippewa Park's median rent is $657 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $85 (11%) lower. It is approximately $405 (38%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $304 (31%) lower.
