Home prices

The value of the median home in Waynesburg is $103,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $3,500 (3%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $113,700 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $34,200 (49%) higher.

Rents

In Waynesburg the median monthly rent is $736. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $57 (8%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $326 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $17 (2%) higher.
