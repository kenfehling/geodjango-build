Much like its surrounding areas, it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Baltimore's median home value is $131,400. This is about $4,680 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $86,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $20,200 (13%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Baltimore's median monthly rent is $822. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $87 (11%) higher. It is roughly $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $139 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
