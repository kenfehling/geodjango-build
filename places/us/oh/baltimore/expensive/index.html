Home prices

The value of the median home in Baltimore is $131,400. It is approximately $4,680 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $86,100 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $20,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

Baltimore's median rent is $822 a month. It is approximately $87 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $240 (22%) lower, and is approximately $139 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
