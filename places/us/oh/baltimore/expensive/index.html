Similar to other places in this area, it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbus, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Baltimore is $131,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $4,680 (3%) higher. It is roughly $86,100 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $20,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Baltimore's median rent is $822 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $87 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $240 (22%) lower, and is roughly $139 (14%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
