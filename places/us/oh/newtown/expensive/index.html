Compared to the surrounding areas, Newtown is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Newtown is valued at $173,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $55,340 (24%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $43,900 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $35,600 (25%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Newtown is $1,169 a month. It is about $271 (30%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $107 (10%) higher, and is about $431 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
