In comparison to other places in this area, Newtown is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Newtown is $173,600. It is about $55,340 (24%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $43,900 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $35,600 (25%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Newtown is $1,169 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $271 (30%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $107 (10%) higher, and is about $431 (58%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
