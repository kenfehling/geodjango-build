In comparison to its surrounding areas, Newtown is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Newtown is $173,600. This is approximately $55,340 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $43,900 (20%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $35,600 (25%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newtown is $1,169. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $271 (30%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $107 (10%) higher, and is about $431 (58%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
