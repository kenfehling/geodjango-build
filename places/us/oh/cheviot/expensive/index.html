Home prices

The median home in Cheviot costs $95,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $79,340 (45%) lower.It is about $121,900 (56%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $42,400 (30%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cheviot is $744. This is approximately $207 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $318 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is is very similar.
