In comparison to other places in this area, Enon is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Enon costs $155,700. It is approximately $31,200 (25%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $61,800 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $4,100 (2%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Enon is $667. It is about $134 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $395 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $294 (30%) lower.
