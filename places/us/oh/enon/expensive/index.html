Compared to the surrounding areas, Enon is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Enon is valued at $155,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $31,200 (25%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $61,800 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $4,100 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Enon the median rent is $667 per month. This is approximately $134 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $395 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $294 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
