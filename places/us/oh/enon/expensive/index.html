Home prices

The median home price in Enon is $155,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $31,200 (25%) higher.It is about $61,800 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $4,100 (2%) higher.

Rents

Enon's median rent is $667 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $134 (16%) lower.It is approximately $395 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $294 (30%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
