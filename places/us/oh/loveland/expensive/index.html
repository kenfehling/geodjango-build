In comparison to the surrounding areas, Loveland is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Loveland is $201,000. This is about $55,500 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $16,500 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $49,400 (32%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Loveland is $996 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $139 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $66 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $35 (3%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.
