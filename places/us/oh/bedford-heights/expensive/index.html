In comparison to its nearby areas, Bedford Heights is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bedford Heights is $102,600. This is approximately $31,220 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $114,900 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $33,000 (47%) higher.

Rents

Bedford Heights's median rent is $826 a month. It is approximately $98 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $236 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $107 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
