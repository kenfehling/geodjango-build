In comparison to nearby areas, Bedford Heights is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bedford Heights is valued at $102,600. This is roughly $31,220 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $114,900 (52%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $33,000 (47%) higher.

Rents

Bedford Heights's median monthly rent is $826. This is roughly $98 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $236 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $107 (14%) higher.
