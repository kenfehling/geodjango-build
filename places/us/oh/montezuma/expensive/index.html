Compared to other places in this area, Montezuma is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Fort Wayne.

Home prices

The median home in Montezuma costs $111,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $43,860 (28%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $106,100 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $3,700 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montezuma is $764. This is roughly $108 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $298 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Fort Wayne.
