Compared to its nearby areas, Brook Park is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Brook Park is $118,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $9,780 (7%) lower. It is about $99,100 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $48,800 (70%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Brook Park is $863 per month. This is about $42 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $199 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $144 (20%) higher.
