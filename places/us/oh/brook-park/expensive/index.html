Compared to its surrounding areas, Brook Park is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Brook Park is $118,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $9,780 (7%) lower.It is about $99,100 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $48,800 (70%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Brook Park's median monthly rent is $863. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $42 (5%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $199 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $144 (20%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
