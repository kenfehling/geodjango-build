Compared to its nearby areas, Brook Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brook Park is $118,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $9,780 (7%) lower.It is about $99,100 (45%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $48,800 (70%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Brook Park's median rent is $863 per month. It is approximately $42 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $199 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $144 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
