Home prices

The price of the median home in Highland Hills is $80,900. It is roughly $104,360 (56%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $136,600 (62%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $11,300 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Highland Hills's median monthly rent is $561. It is roughly $478 (45%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $501 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $158 (21%) lower.
