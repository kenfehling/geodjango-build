Compared to other places in this area, Highland Hills is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Highland Hills is $80,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $104,360 (56%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $136,600 (62%) lower, and it is roughly $11,300 (16%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Highland Hills's median monthly rent is $561. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $478 (45%) lower. It is about $501 (47%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $158 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
