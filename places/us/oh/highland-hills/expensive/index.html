In comparison to its surrounding areas, Highland Hills is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Highland Hills is $80,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $104,360 (56%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $136,600 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $11,300 (16%) higher.

Rents

Highland Hills's median monthly rent is $561. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $478 (45%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $501 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $158 (21%) lower.
