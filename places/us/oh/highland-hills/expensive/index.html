In comparison to the surrounding areas, Highland Hills is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Highland Hills is $80,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $104,360 (56%) lower. It is about $136,600 (62%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $11,300 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Highland Hills the median monthly rent is $561. This is about $478 (45%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $501 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $158 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
