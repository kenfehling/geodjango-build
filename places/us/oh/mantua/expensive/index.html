Compared to other places in this area, Mantua is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Clevelandhowever, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Mantua is $147,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $30,100 (16%) lower. It is about $69,800 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $78,100 (112%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mantua is $856. This is approximately $107 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $206 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $137 (19%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
