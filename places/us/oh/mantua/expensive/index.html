Compared to its surrounding areas, Mantua is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mantua costs $147,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $30,100 (16%) lower.It is roughly $69,800 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $78,100 (112%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Mantua's median monthly rent is $856. It is about $107 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $206 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $137 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
