Compared to the surrounding areas, Mantua is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mantua costs $147,700. This is about $30,100 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $69,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $78,100 (112%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mantua is $856 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $107 (11%) lower.It is roughly $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $137 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
