In comparison to its nearby areas, Mantua is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mantua is $147,700. This is roughly $30,100 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $69,800 (32%) lower, and is about $78,100 (112%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Mantua is $856 per month. This is approximately $107 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $206 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $137 (19%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
