In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mantua is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Mantua is $147,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $30,100 (16%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $69,800 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $78,100 (112%) higher.

Rents

Mantua's median rent is $856 a month. This is roughly $107 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $137 (19%) higher.
