Compared to other places in this area, Ashland is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ashland is $109,300. This is about $10,860 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $108,200 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $42,300 (27%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Ashland's median monthly rent is $714. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $41 (5%) lower.It is approximately $348 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $247 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
