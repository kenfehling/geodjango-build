Compared to its nearby areas, Ashland is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

Ashland's median home cost is $109,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $10,860 (11%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $108,200 (49%) lower, and is roughly $42,300 (27%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Ashland the median rent is $714 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $41 (5%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $348 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $247 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
