In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ashland is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $109,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $10,860 (11%) higher.It is about $108,200 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $42,300 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $714. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $41 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and is approximately $247 (25%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
