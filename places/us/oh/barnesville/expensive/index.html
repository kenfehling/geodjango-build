The value of the median home in Barnesville is $81,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $5,200 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $136,300 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $43,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Barnesville the median monthly rent is $562. This is about $33 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $500 (47%) lower, and is approximately $396 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
