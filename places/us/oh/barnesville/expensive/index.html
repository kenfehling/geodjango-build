In comparison to its surrounding areas, Barnesville is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Barnesville is $81,200. This is about $5,200 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $136,300 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $43,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Barnesville is $562 a month. It is about $33 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $500 (47%) lower, and is roughly $396 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
