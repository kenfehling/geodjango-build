Home prices

Worthington's median home price is $285,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $22,340 (8%) higher. It is roughly $67,800 (31%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $133,700 (88%) higher.

Rents

In Worthington the median rent is $1,030 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $102 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $32 (3%) lower, and it is about $69 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
