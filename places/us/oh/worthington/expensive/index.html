Home prices

The median home in Worthington costs $285,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,340 (8%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $67,800 (31%) higher, and it is about $133,700 (88%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Worthington is $1,030 a month. This is approximately $102 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is about $32 (3%) lower, and is roughly $69 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
