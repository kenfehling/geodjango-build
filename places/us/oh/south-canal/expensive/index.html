Home prices

The median home in South Canal costs $142,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $33,900 (31%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $74,800 (34%) lower, and is approximately $73,100 (105%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In South Canal the median rent is $850 a month. This is roughly $111 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $131 (18%) higher.
