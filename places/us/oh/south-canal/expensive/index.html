Home prices

The value of the median home in South Canal is $142,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $33,900 (31%) higher. It is about $74,800 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $73,100 (105%) higher.

Rents

South Canal's median monthly rent is $850. It is about $111 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $212 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $131 (18%) higher.
