In comparison to nearby areas, Ottawa is significantly more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Columbus, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Ottawa costs $158,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $45,520 (40%) higher. It is about $58,800 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $7,100 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Ottawa's median monthly rent is $587. This is approximately $110 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $475 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $374 (38%) lower.
