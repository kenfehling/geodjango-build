Compared to nearby areas, Ottawa is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Ottawa is $158,700. It is roughly $45,520 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $58,800 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $7,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Ottawa the median rent is $587 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $110 (15%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $475 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $374 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
