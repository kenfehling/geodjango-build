In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ottawa is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ottawa is $158,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $45,520 (40%) higher. It is roughly $58,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $7,100 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Ottawa's median monthly rent is $587. This is approximately $110 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $475 (44%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $374 (38%) lower.
