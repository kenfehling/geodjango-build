Compared to its surrounding areas, Ottawa is substantially more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Ottawa costs $158,700. It is approximately $45,520 (40%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $58,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $7,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

Ottawa's median monthly rent is $587. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $110 (15%) lower.It is roughly $475 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $374 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
