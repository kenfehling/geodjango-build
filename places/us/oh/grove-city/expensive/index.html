Home prices

The median home in Grove City costs $188,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $61,280 (48%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $29,200 (13%) lower, and it is about $36,700 (24%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grove City is $996. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $151 (17%) higher.It is roughly $66 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $35 (3%) higher.
