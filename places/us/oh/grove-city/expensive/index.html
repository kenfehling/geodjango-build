Home prices

The median home in Grove City is valued at $188,300. This is approximately $61,280 (48%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $29,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $36,700 (24%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Grove City's median monthly rent is $996. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $151 (17%) higher.It is approximately $66 (6%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $35 (3%) higher.
