Home prices

The median home in Grove City costs $188,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $61,280 (48%) higher. It is roughly $29,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $36,700 (24%) higher.

Rents

Grove City's median rent is $996 a month. This is about $151 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $66 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $35 (3%) higher.
