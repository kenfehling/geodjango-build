Home prices

Grove City's median home cost is $188,300. It is approximately $61,280 (48%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $29,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $36,700 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grove City is $996. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $151 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $66 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $35 (3%) higher.
