Home prices

The price of the median home in East Sparta is $98,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,700 (13%) lower.It is roughly $119,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $28,400 (40%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in East Sparta is $663 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $48 (6%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $399 (37%) lower, and it is about $56 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
