Compared to other places in this area, East Sparta is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in East Sparta is $98,000. This is about $14,700 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $119,500 (54%) lower, and it is about $28,400 (40%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Sparta is $663. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $48 (6%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $399 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $56 (7%) lower.
