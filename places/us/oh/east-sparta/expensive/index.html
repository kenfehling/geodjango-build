Compared to the surrounding areas, East Sparta is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in East Sparta is $98,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $14,700 (13%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $119,500 (54%) lower, and it is about $28,400 (40%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

East Sparta's median monthly rent is $663. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $48 (6%) lower.It is about $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $56 (7%) lower.
