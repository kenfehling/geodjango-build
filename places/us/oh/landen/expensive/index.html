In comparison to the surrounding areas, Landen is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Landen's median home price is $201,200. It is about $58,220 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $16,300 (7%) lower, and is about $49,600 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Landen's median monthly rent is $1,191. This is roughly $39 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $129 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $230 (23%) higher.
