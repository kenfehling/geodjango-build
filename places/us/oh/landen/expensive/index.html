Home prices

Rents

Landen's median rent is $1,191 a month. This is approximately $39 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $129 (12%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $230 (23%) higher.
