In comparison to the surrounding areas, Landen is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Landen costs $201,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $58,220 (22%) lower. It is about $16,300 (7%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $49,600 (32%) higher compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Landen is $1,191 a month. It is about $39 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $129 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $230 (23%) higher.
