Home prices

Rents

Englewood's median monthly rent is $802. It is about $35 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $260 (24%) lower, and is approximately $159 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
