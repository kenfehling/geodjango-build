Home prices

The median home price in Englewood is $129,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $23,500 (22%) higher. It is roughly $88,200 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus it is roughly $22,300 (14%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Englewood is $802 per month. It is approximately $35 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $260 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $159 (16%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
