In comparison to its nearby areas, Miami Heights is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Miami Heights's median home value is $224,200. This is roughly $22,120 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $6,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $86,200 (62%) higher.

Rents

In Miami Heights the median rent is $805 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $63 (7%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $257 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $67 (9%) higher.
