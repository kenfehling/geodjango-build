In comparison to its surrounding areas, Miami Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Miami Heights costs $224,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $22,120 (10%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $6,700 (3%) higher, and is approximately $86,200 (62%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Miami Heights's median rent is $805 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $63 (7%) lower.It is about $257 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $67 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
