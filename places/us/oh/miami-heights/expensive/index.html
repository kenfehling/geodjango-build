Compared to other places in this area, Miami Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Miami Heights's median home price is $224,200. This is roughly $22,120 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $6,700 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $86,200 (62%) higher.

Rents

Miami Heights's median monthly rent is $805. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $63 (7%) lower.It is about $257 (24%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $67 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
