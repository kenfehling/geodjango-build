In comparison to other places in this area, Miami Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Miami Heights is valued at $224,200. It is approximately $22,120 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $6,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $86,200 (62%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Miami Heights is $805 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $63 (7%) lower. It is approximately $257 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $67 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
