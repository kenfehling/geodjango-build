The median home in Lakemore is valued at $89,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $47,900 (34%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $128,300 (58%) lower, and is roughly $19,600 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents
