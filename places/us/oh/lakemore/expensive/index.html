Home prices

The median home cost in Lakemore is $89,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $47,900 (34%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $128,300 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $19,600 (28%) higher.

Rents

Lakemore's median rent is $776 a month. This is approximately $34 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $286 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $57 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
