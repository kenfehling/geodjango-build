Home prices

The median home in Medina is valued at $175,200. It is approximately $19,560 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $42,300 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $105,600 (151%) higher.

Rents

In Medina the median rent is $798 a month. This is about $31 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $264 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $79 (10%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
