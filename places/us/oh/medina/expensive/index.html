The median home in Medina is valued at $175,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $19,560 (12%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $42,300 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $105,600 (151%) higher.

Rents

Medina's median monthly rent is $798. It is approximately $31 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $264 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $79 (10%) higher.
