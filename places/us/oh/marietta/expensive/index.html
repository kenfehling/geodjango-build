Home prices

Marietta's median home cost is $108,300. This is roughly $24,140 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $109,200 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $37,500 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marietta is $673 a month. This is approximately $161 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $389 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is about $62 (8%) lower.
