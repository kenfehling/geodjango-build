Home prices

Marietta's median home value is $108,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $24,140 (18%) lower. It is roughly $109,200 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is roughly $37,500 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Marietta the median rent is $673 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $161 (19%) lower. It is about $389 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $62 (8%) lower.
