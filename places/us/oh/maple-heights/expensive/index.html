In comparison to other places in this area, Maple Heights is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Maple Heights is $68,200. This is about $20,200 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $149,300 (68%) lower, and it is approximately $1,400 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Maple Heights's median monthly rent is $926. It is roughly $85 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $136 (12%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $207 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
