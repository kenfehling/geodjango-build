In comparison to its nearby areas, Maple Heights is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Heights costs $68,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $20,200 (22%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $149,300 (68%) lower, and it is roughly $1,400 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Maple Heights's median monthly rent is $926. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $85 (10%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $136 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $207 (28%) higher.
