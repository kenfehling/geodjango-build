Home prices

The value of the median home in Maple Heights is $68,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $20,200 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $149,300 (68%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $1,400 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Maple Heights is $926 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $85 (10%) higher. It is about $136 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $207 (28%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
