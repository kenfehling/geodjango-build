Home prices

The median home in Fairfax is valued at $142,300. This is approximately $125,060 (46%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $75,200 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $4,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

Fairfax's median rent is $968 a month. It is about $90 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $94 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $230 (31%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
