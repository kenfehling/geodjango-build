In comparison to nearby areas, Fairfax is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Fairfax's median home cost is $142,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $125,060 (46%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $75,200 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $4,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

Fairfax's median rent is $968 a month. It is approximately $90 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $94 (8%) lower, and is about $230 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
