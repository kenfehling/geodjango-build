Compared to its surrounding areas, Fairfax is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Fairfax is $142,300. It is approximately $125,060 (46%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $75,200 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $4,300 (3%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Fairfax the median monthly rent is $968. Compared to other places in this area this is about $90 (8%) lower. It is about $94 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $230 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
