Home prices

The value of the median home in Fairfax is $142,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $125,060 (46%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $75,200 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $4,300 (3%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $968. It is roughly $90 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $94 (8%) lower, and is approximately $230 (31%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
