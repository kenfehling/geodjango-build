Home prices

The median home in Centerburg is valued at $154,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $20,720 (11%) lower. It is roughly $62,700 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is approximately $3,200 (2%) higher.

Rents

Centerburg's median rent is $619 a month. This is approximately $365 (37%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $342 (35%) lower.
