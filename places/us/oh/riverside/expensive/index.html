Home prices

The price of the median home in Riverside is $90,300. It is roughly $57,580 (38%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $127,200 (58%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $61,300 (40%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Riverside the median monthly rent is $842. It is about $126 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $220 (20%) lower, and is about $119 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
