Home prices

The median home in Norwood is valued at $135,200. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $31,520 (30%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $82,300 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $2,800 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Norwood is $748 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $36 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $314 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $10 (1%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
