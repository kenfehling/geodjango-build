Home prices

Monfort Heights's median home value is $151,800. This is about $9,720 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $65,700 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $13,800 (10%) higher.

Rents

Monfort Heights's median monthly rent is $908. This is about $49 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $154 (14%) lower, and is approximately $170 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
