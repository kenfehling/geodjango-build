Home prices

The median home in Boardman is valued at $118,900. It is roughly $20,280 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $98,600 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $49,300 (70%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Boardman's median monthly rent is $674. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $66 (8%) lower. It is about $388 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $45 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
