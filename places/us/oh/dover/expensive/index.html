Home prices

The median home in Dover costs $133,500. This is about $20,320 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $84,000 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $63,900 (91%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Dover the median rent is $849 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $121 (16%) higher.It is roughly $213 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $130 (18%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
