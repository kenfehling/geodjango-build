Compared to its nearby areas, Dover is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Dover is $133,500. It is roughly $20,320 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $84,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $63,900 (91%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dover is $849. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $121 (16%) higher. It is approximately $213 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $130 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
