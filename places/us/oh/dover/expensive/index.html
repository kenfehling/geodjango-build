Compared to nearby areas, Dover is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dover is $133,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $20,320 (17%) higher. It is approximately $84,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $63,900 (91%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Dover is $849 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $121 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $213 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $130 (18%) higher.
