Home prices

The median home price in Dover is $133,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $20,320 (17%) higher.It is roughly $84,000 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $63,900 (91%) higher.

Rents

Dover's median rent is $849 per month. It is approximately $121 (16%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $213 (20%) lower, and it is about $130 (18%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
