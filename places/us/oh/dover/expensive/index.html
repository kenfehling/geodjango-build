In comparison to its nearby areas, Dover is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dover is valued at $133,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $20,320 (17%) higher.It is about $84,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $63,900 (91%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dover is $849. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $121 (16%) higher.It is about $213 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $130 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
