Compared to its surrounding areas, Windham is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Windham is $90,800. This is roughly $40,500 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $126,700 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $21,200 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Windham's median rent is $807 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $68 (9%) higher.It is roughly $255 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $88 (12%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
