Compared to the surrounding areas, Windham is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home cost in Windham is $90,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $40,500 (30%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $126,700 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $21,200 (30%) higher.

Rents

Windham's median monthly rent is $807. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $68 (9%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $255 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $88 (12%) higher.
