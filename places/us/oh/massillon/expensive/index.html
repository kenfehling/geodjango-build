Compared to other places in this area, Massillon is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

Massillon's median home value is $102,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $21,260 (17%) lower. It is approximately $115,400 (53%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $32,500 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Massillon's median rent is $689 a month. It is roughly $107 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $30 (4%) lower.
