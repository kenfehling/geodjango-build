In comparison to its surrounding areas, Massillon is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Massillon costs $102,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $21,260 (17%) lower.It is about $115,400 (53%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $32,500 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Massillon the median monthly rent is $689. It is about $107 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $373 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $30 (4%) lower.
