Compared to other places in this area, Massillon is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Massillon's median home price is $102,100. This is about $21,260 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $115,400 (53%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $32,500 (46%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Massillon the median rent is $689 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $107 (13%) lower.It is approximately $373 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $30 (4%) lower.
