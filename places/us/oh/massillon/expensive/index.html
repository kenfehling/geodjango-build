Compared to its nearby areas, Massillon is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Massillon's median home price is $102,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $21,260 (17%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $115,400 (53%) lower, and it is approximately $32,500 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Massillon's median monthly rent is $689. Compared to nearby areas it is about $107 (13%) lower. It is roughly $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $30 (4%) lower.
