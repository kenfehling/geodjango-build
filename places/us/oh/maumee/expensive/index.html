Home prices

Maumee's median home value is $144,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $64,160 (30%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $72,900 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $95,400 (193%) higher.

Rents

Maumee's median rent is $797 per month. It is about $165 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $265 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Detroit it is roughly $27 (3%) lower.
