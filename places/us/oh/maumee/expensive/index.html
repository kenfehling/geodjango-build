In comparison to the surrounding areas, Maumee is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Detroit, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Maumee is $144,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $64,160 (30%) lower. It is about $72,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Detroit is approximately $95,400 (193%) higher.

Rents

Maumee's median monthly rent is $797. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $165 (17%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $265 (24%) lower, and is about $27 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Detroit.
