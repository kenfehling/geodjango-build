Home prices

The median home in Magnolia costs $124,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $23,920 (23%) higher.It is roughly $92,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $55,100 (79%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Magnolia the median rent is $552 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $159 (22%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $510 (48%) lower, and it is about $167 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
