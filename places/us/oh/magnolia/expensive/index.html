In comparison to the surrounding areas, Magnolia is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Magnolia costs $124,700. It is roughly $23,920 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $92,800 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $55,100 (79%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Magnolia is $552 per month. It is roughly $159 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $510 (48%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $167 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
