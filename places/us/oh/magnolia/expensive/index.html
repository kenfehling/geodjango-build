Home prices

The median home in Magnolia is valued at $124,700. This is approximately $23,920 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $92,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $55,100 (79%) higher.

Rents

In Magnolia the median rent is $552 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $159 (22%) lower. It is about $510 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $167 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
