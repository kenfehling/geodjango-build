Home prices

The median home in Coshocton costs $82,200. It is about $4,200 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $135,300 (62%) lower, and is about $69,400 (45%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Coshocton the median monthly rent is $629. This is about $52 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $433 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $332 (34%) lower.
