Home prices

The value of the median home in Galena is $287,500. This is roughly $55,380 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $70,000 (32%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $135,900 (89%) higher.

Rents

In Galena the median rent is $1,361 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $166 (13%) higher. It is approximately $299 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $400 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.
