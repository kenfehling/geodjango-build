The median home in Galena costs $287,500. It is approximately $55,380 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $70,000 (32%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $135,900 (89%) higher in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Galena is $1,361. This is about $166 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $299 (28%) higher, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $400 (41%) higher.
