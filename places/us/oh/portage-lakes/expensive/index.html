Compared to other places in this area, Portage Lakes is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Portage Lakes is $135,200. This is about $17,720 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $82,300 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $65,600 (94%) higher.

Rents

Portage Lakes's median monthly rent is $901. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $129 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $161 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $182 (25%) higher.
