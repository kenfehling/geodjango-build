Home prices

Rents

In Portage Lakes the median monthly rent is $901. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $129 (16%) higher.It is about $161 (15%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $182 (25%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
