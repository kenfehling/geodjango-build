Rents

The median rent in Portage Lakes is $901 per month. This is approximately $129 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $161 (15%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $182 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
