Home prices

The value of the median home in Vermilion is $140,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $3,700 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $76,700 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $91,600 (186%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Vermilion is $890. This is roughly $139 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $172 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $66 (8%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.
