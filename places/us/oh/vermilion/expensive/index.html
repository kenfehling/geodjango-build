Home prices

The median home in Vermilion costs $140,800. This is roughly $3,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $76,700 (35%) lower, and is approximately $91,600 (186%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Vermilion's median monthly rent is $890. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $139 (18%) higher.It is about $172 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $66 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Detroit.
