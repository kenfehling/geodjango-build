Compared to its surrounding areas, Louisville is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Louisville costs $151,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $31,160 (25%) higher. It is roughly $66,400 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $81,500 (117%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Louisville is $736 per month. This is roughly $91 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $326 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $17 (2%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
