In comparison to nearby areas, Louisville is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Louisville is $151,100. This is roughly $31,160 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $66,400 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $81,500 (117%) higher.

Rents

Louisville's median monthly rent is $736. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $91 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $17 (2%) higher.
