Compared to the surrounding areas, Louisville is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Louisville is $151,100. This is about $31,160 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $66,400 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $81,500 (117%) higher.

Rents

In Louisville the median rent is $736 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $91 (11%) lower.It is roughly $326 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $17 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
