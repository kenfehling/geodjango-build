Home prices

Rents

In Atwater the median monthly rent is $1,086. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $329 (43%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $24 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $367 (51%) higher.
