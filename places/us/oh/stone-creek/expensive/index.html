Rents

Stone Creek's median rent is $775 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $39 (5%) higher.It is roughly $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $186 (19%) lower.
