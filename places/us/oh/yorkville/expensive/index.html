Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Yorkville is $606. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $9 (1%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $456 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $352 (36%) lower.
