Rents

The median rent in Orangeville is $756 per month. It is approximately $75 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $306 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $37 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
