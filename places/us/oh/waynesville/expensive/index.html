Home prices

The median home price in Waynesville is $170,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $2,080 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $46,800 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $19,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Waynesville the median monthly rent is $775. This is roughly $204 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $186 (19%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
