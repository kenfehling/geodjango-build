In comparison to other places in this area, Pleasant Hill is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home price in Pleasant Hill is $118,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $12,840 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $99,100 (45%) lower, and is approximately $33,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Pleasant Hill the median rent is $602 a month. It is about $135 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $460 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $359 (37%) lower.
