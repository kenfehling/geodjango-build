Rents

In Pleasant Hill the median rent is $602 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $135 (18%) lower. It is approximately $460 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $359 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
