Home prices

The median home price in Camden is $80,000. It is about $13,060 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $137,500 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $58,000 (42%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Camden's median rent is $645 per month. This is about $404 (38%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $417 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $93 (12%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
