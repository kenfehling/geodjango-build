Compared to its surrounding areas, Rocky Ridge is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Detroit however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rocky Ridge is $86,900. It is roughly $21,620 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $130,600 (60%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $37,700 (76%) higher compared to the city of Detroit.

Rents

Rocky Ridge's median rent is $618 per month. This is approximately $141 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $444 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Detroit is roughly $206 (25%) lower.
