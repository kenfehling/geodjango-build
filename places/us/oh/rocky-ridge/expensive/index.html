Home prices

Rocky Ridge's median home price is $86,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $21,620 (19%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $130,600 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Detroit is roughly $37,700 (76%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rocky Ridge is $618 a month. This is about $141 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Detroit is about $206 (25%) lower.
