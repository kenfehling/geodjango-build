In comparison to nearby areas, New Franklin is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in New Franklin is $149,200. This is approximately $10,140 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $68,300 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $79,600 (114%) higher.

Rents

New Franklin's median rent is $878 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $60 (7%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $184 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $159 (22%) higher.
