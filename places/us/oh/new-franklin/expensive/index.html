Home prices

The value of the median home in New Franklin is $149,200. It is roughly $10,140 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $68,300 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $79,600 (114%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in New Franklin is $878 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $60 (7%) higher.It is about $184 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $159 (22%) higher.
