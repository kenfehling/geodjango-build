In comparison to the surrounding areas, New Franklin is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in New Franklin is $149,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $10,140 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $68,300 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $79,600 (114%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in New Franklin is $878 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $60 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $184 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $159 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
