Compared to the surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home cost in Jacksonville is $62,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $4,240 (6%) lower. It is about $154,700 (71%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is approximately $88,800 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median monthly rent is $696. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $39 (5%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $366 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is about $265 (27%) lower.
