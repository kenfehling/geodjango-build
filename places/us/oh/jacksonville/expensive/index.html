In comparison to other places in this area, Jacksonville is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $62,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $4,240 (6%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $154,700 (71%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $88,800 (58%) lower.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $696. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $39 (5%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $366 (34%) lower, and it is about $265 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
