In comparison to its nearby areas, Jacksonville is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Jacksonville's median home price is $62,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $4,240 (6%) lower. It is roughly $154,700 (71%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $88,800 (58%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median monthly rent is $696. This is about $39 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $366 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $265 (27%) lower.
