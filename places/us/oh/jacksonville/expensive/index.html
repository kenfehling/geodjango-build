Compared to its surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville is valued at $62,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $4,240 (6%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $154,700 (71%) lower, and is about $88,800 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $696. This is roughly $39 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $366 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $265 (27%) lower.
