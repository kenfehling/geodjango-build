Home prices

The value of the median home in North Madison is $122,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $20,440 (14%) lower.It is approximately $95,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cleveland is approximately $52,700 (75%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Madison is $949 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $213 (28%) higher.It is approximately $113 (10%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $230 (31%) higher.
