Compared to other places in this area, North Madison is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Clevelandhowever, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Madison costs $122,300. Compared to nearby areas it is about $20,440 (14%) lower. It is approximately $95,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $52,700 (75%) higher.

Rents

North Madison's median monthly rent is $949. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $213 (28%) higher. It is roughly $113 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $230 (31%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
