The cost of the median home in North Madison is $122,300. This is roughly $20,440 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $95,200 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $52,700 (75%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Madison is $949. It is approximately $213 (28%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $113 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is about $230 (31%) higher.
