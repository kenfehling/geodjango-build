Home prices

The median home price in Springdale is $128,700. It is roughly $23,600 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $88,800 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $9,300 (6%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Springdale the median monthly rent is $1,047. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $52 (5%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $15 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $309 (41%) higher.
