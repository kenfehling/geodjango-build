Home prices

Rents

Springdale's median monthly rent is $1,047. This is roughly $52 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $15 (1%) lower, and is about $309 (41%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
