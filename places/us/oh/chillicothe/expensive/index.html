Compared to the surrounding areas, Chillicothe is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Chillicothe is $116,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $26,420 (29%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $100,700 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $34,800 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chillicothe is $761. This is approximately $36 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $301 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is about $200 (20%) lower.
