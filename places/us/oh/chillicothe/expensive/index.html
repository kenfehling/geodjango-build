In comparison to nearby areas, Chillicothe is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Columbus, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chillicothe costs $116,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $26,420 (29%) higher. It is approximately $100,700 (46%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is about $34,800 (22%) lower.

Rents

Chillicothe's median rent is $761 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $36 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $301 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is about $200 (20%) lower.
