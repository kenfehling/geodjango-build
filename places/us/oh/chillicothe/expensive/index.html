In comparison to its surrounding areas, Chillicothe is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home in Chillicothe is valued at $116,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $26,420 (29%) higher.It is approximately $100,700 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $34,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Chillicothe the median rent is $761 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $36 (4%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $301 (28%) lower, and is about $200 (20%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
