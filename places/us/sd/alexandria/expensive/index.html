Home prices

The median home in Alexandria is valued at $107,300. It is about $5,280 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $110,200 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $82,500 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Alexandria the median monthly rent is $850. This is roughly $226 (36%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and is about $23 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
