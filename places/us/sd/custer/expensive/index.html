Home prices

The price of the median home in Custer is $139,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $2,360 (1%) lower. It is about $78,100 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rapid City it is about $45,300 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Custer is $703 a month. This is about $82 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $359 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $134 (16%) lower compared to the city of Rapid City.
