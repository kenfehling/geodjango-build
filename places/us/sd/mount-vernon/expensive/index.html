Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon is valued at $116,400. It is roughly $37,760 (48%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $101,100 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Mitchell it is approximately $30,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

Mount Vernon's median rent is $680 a month. This is about $61 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mitchell it is about $76 (10%) lower.
