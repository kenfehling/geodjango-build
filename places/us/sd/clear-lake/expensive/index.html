Clear Lake's median home cost is $120,700. This is approximately $43,800 (56%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $96,800 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $69,100 (36%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

The median rent in Clear Lake is $492 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $117 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $570 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $335 (40%) lower.
