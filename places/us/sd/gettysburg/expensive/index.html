In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gettysburg is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Pierre.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Gettysburg is $107,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $23,180 (27%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $110,500 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Pierre it is about $68,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Gettysburg the median rent is $705 a month. This is roughly $64 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $357 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $38 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Pierre.
