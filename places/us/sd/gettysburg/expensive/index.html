Home prices

The median home in Gettysburg costs $107,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $23,180 (27%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $110,500 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pierre it is approximately $68,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

Gettysburg's median rent is $705 a month. It is approximately $64 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $357 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $38 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Pierre.
