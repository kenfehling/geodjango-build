Home prices

Toronto's median home cost is $47,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $41,520 (46%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $170,000 (78%) lower, and is about $142,300 (74%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

Toronto's median monthly rent is $725. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $147 (25%) higher.It is about $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $102 (12%) lower.
