Compared to other places in this area, Toronto is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Sioux Falls, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Toronto costs $47,500. This is about $41,520 (46%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $170,000 (78%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $142,300 (74%) lower.

Rents

Toronto's median rent is $725 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $147 (25%) higher.It is approximately $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $102 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
