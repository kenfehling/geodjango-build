Home prices

The median home in Chamberlain is valued at $157,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $81,880 (108%) higher.It is about $60,400 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $9,900 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Mitchell.

Rents

Chamberlain's median rent is $600 per month. It is roughly $46 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $462 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Mitchell is roughly $156 (20%) lower.
