Home prices

The median home in Fort Pierre costs $158,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $77,160 (95%) higher. It is about $59,500 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $17,600 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Pierre.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Pierre is $761. It is approximately $127 (19%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $301 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Pierre is approximately $94 (14%) higher.
