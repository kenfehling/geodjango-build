Home prices

The median home price in Box Elder is $160,700. This is about $17,940 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $56,800 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City it is approximately $24,000 (12%) lower.

Rents

Box Elder's median monthly rent is $991. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $20 (1%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $71 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $154 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Rapid City.
