In comparison to other places in this area, Box Elder is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Rapid City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Box Elder is valued at $160,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17,940 (10%) lower. It is about $56,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Rapid City is roughly $24,000 (12%) lower.

Rents

Box Elder's median monthly rent is $991. This is about $20 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $71 (6%) lower, and is about $154 (18%) higher compared to the city of Rapid City.
