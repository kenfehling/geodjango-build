Home prices

The median home in Eagle Butte is valued at $51,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $19,040 (27%) lower. It is approximately $166,300 (76%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pierre it is roughly $124,400 (70%) lower.

Rents

Eagle Butte's median monthly rent is $604. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $17 (2%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $458 (43%) lower, and it is about $63 (9%) lower compared to the city of Pierre.
