In comparison to other places in this area, Centerville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Home prices

The median home in Centerville costs $82,000. This is roughly $4,520 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $135,500 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $107,800 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $468. It is approximately $203 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $594 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $359 (43%) lower.
