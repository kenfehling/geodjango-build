In comparison to its surrounding areas, North Sioux City is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in North Sioux City is valued at $148,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $14,020 (8%) lower. It is about $69,400 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $11,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in North Sioux City is $880 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $135 (18%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $182 (17%) lower, and is approximately $43 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
