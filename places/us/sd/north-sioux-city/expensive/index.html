Compared to its surrounding areas, North Sioux City is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home value in North Sioux City is $148,100. This is approximately $14,020 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $69,400 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $11,600 (7%) lower.

Rents

North Sioux City's median monthly rent is $880. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $135 (18%) higher. It is roughly $182 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $43 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
