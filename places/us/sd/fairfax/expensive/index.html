Home prices

The median home in Fairfax costs $46,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $35,880 (43%) lower.It is roughly $170,800 (78%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $100,500 (68%) lower compared to the city of Mitchell.

Rents

Fairfax's median monthly rent is $413. This is approximately $219 (34%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $649 (61%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Mitchell is approximately $343 (45%) lower.
