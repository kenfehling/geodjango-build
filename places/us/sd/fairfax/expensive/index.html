In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fairfax is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Mitchell.

Home prices

The median home in Fairfax costs $46,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $35,880 (43%) lower. It is about $170,800 (78%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Mitchell it is about $100,500 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Fairfax the median rent is $413 per month. It is roughly $219 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $649 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $343 (45%) lower compared to the city of Mitchell.
