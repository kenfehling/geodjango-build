Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairfax is $46,700. It is about $35,880 (43%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $170,800 (78%) lower, and it is approximately $100,500 (68%) lower compared to the city of Mitchell.

Rents

The median rent in Fairfax is $413 per month. This is about $219 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $649 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mitchell it is about $343 (45%) lower.
