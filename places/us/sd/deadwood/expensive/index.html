Compared to its surrounding areas, Deadwood is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Rapid City, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Deadwood is valued at $160,700. This is about $16,980 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $56,800 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City is approximately $24,000 (12%) lower.

Rents

Deadwood's median monthly rent is $593. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $102 (14%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $469 (44%) lower, and it is about $244 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Rapid City.
