Home prices

Deadwood's median home value is $160,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $16,980 (11%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $56,800 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City is approximately $24,000 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Deadwood is $593 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $102 (14%) lower. It is approximately $469 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $244 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Rapid City.
