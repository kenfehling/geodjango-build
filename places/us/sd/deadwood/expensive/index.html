Home prices

Rents

Deadwood's median rent is $593 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $102 (14%) lower.It is approximately $469 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City is roughly $244 (29%) lower.
