Home prices

The median home in Deadwood is valued at $160,700. It is approximately $16,980 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $56,800 (26%) lower, and is about $24,000 (12%) lower compared to the city of Rapid City.

Rents

The median rent in Deadwood is $593 per month. It is approximately $102 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $469 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Rapid City it is roughly $244 (29%) lower.
