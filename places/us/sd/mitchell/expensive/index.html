Home prices

Rents

Mitchell's median monthly rent is $756. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $77 (11%) higher.It is about $306 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $71 (8%) lower.
