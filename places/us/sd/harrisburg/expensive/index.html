Compared to other places in this area, Harrisburg is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Sioux Falls however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Harrisburg is $199,200. It is roughly $48,140 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $18,300 (8%) lower, and it is approximately $9,400 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

The median rent in Harrisburg is $941 per month. This is about $164 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $121 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $114 (13%) higher.
