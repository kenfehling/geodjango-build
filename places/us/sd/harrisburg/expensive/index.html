In comparison to nearby areas, Harrisburg is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Home prices

The median home value in Harrisburg is $199,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $48,140 (31%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $18,300 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $9,400 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median rent is $941 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $164 (21%) higher. It is about $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $114 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
