Home prices

The value of the median home in Harrisburg is $199,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $48,140 (31%) higher.It is approximately $18,300 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $9,400 (4%) higher compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

Harrisburg's median rent is $941 a month. This is roughly $164 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $114 (13%) higher compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
