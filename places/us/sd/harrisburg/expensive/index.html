Compared to nearby areas, Harrisburg is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Sioux Falls, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Harrisburg is valued at $199,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $48,140 (31%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $18,300 (8%) lower, and it is about $9,400 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

Harrisburg's median rent is $941 a month. This is roughly $164 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $121 (11%) lower, and is approximately $114 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
