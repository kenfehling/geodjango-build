Home prices

The median home in Hill City is valued at $167,500. This is roughly $9,520 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $50,000 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City it is about $17,200 (9%) lower.

Rents

Hill City's median rent is $643 a month. It is about $137 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $419 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Rapid City is approximately $194 (23%) lower.
