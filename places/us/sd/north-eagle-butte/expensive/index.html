Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Eagle Butte is $558. This is approximately $39 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $504 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Pierre it is about $109 (16%) lower.
