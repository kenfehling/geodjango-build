Home prices

The median home in Garretson costs $152,000. This is about $4,700 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $65,500 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $37,800 (19%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

In Garretson the median monthly rent is $722. This is roughly $82 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $340 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $105 (12%) lower.
