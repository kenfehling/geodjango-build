Home prices

Blackhawk's median home value is $179,200. It is approximately $14,920 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $38,300 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $5,500 (2%) lower compared to the city of Rapid City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blackhawk is $954. This is roughly $52 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $108 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City is approximately $117 (13%) higher.
