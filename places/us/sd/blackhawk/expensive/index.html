Compared to the surrounding areas, Blackhawk is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Rapid City, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Blackhawk costs $179,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,920 (7%) lower.It is roughly $38,300 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City is approximately $5,500 (2%) lower.

Rents

Blackhawk's median rent is $954 per month. It is approximately $52 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $108 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $117 (13%) higher compared to the city of Rapid City.
