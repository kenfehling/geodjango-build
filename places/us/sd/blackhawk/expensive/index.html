Home prices

The median home price in Blackhawk is $179,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $14,920 (7%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $38,300 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City it is approximately $5,500 (2%) lower.

Rents

In Blackhawk the median rent is $954 a month. It is approximately $52 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City is about $117 (13%) higher.
