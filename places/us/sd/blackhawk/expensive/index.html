Home prices

The median home in Blackhawk costs $179,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $14,920 (7%) lower. It is about $38,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Rapid City is roughly $5,500 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Blackhawk is $954 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $52 (5%) lower. It is roughly $108 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $117 (13%) higher compared to the city of Rapid City.
