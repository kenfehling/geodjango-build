Compared to the surrounding areas, Blackhawk is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Rapid City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Blackhawk is $179,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,920 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $38,300 (17%) lower, and is approximately $5,500 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Rapid City.

Rents

In Blackhawk the median rent is $954 per month. This is roughly $52 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $117 (13%) higher compared to the city of Rapid City.
