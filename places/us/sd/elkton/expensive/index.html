Home prices

The median home value in Elkton is $132,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $13,940 (11%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $84,800 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $57,100 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Elkton is $725 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $164 (29%) higher. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $102 (12%) lower.
