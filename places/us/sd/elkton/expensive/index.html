In comparison to other places in this area, Elkton is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Sioux Falls however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Elkton is $132,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $13,940 (11%) higher. It is roughly $84,800 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $57,100 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elkton is $725. It is roughly $164 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $102 (12%) lower.
