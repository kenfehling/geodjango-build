In comparison to its surrounding areas, Keystone is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Rapid City.

Home prices

The median home price in Keystone is $128,100. It is approximately $35,880 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $89,400 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Rapid City it is about $56,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Keystone the median rent is $581 a month. It is about $190 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $481 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Rapid City it is approximately $256 (30%) lower.
