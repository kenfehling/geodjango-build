Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rapid City is $184,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $8,917 (4%) lower. It is approximately $32,800 (15%) lower compared to the United States as a whole.

Rents

Rapid City's median rent is $837 a month. This is roughly $86 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $225 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole.
