Home prices

Rents

Lead's median monthly rent is $707. It is approximately $23 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $355 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rapid City it is about $130 (15%) lower.
