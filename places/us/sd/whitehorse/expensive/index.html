Home prices

The median home value in Whitehorse is $137,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $78,040 (131%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $80,000 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Pierre is about $38,100 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Whitehorse is $575. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $28 (4%) lower.It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pierre it is roughly $92 (13%) lower.
