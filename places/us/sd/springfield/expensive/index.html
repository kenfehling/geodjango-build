Home prices

Rents

In Springfield the median monthly rent is $496. It is roughly $100 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $566 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $331 (40%) lower.
