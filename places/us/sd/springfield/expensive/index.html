Compared to the surrounding areas, Springfield is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Sioux Falls, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Springfield is $86,500. This is roughly $12,580 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $131,000 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $103,300 (54%) lower.

Rents

Springfield's median monthly rent is $496. It is about $100 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $566 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $331 (40%) lower.
