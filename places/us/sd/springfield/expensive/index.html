In comparison to other places in this area, Springfield is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Sioux Falls, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Springfield costs $86,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $12,580 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $131,000 (60%) lower, and is about $103,300 (54%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Springfield is $496. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $100 (16%) lower. It is about $566 (53%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $331 (40%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
