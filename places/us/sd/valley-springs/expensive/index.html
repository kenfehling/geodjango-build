Home prices

The median home in Valley Springs costs $139,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $18,680 (11%) lower. It is approximately $77,700 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $50,000 (26%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents
