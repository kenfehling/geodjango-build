Home prices

The median home in Lemmon costs $66,700. This is roughly $7,120 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $150,800 (69%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $171,300 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Dickinson.

Rents

Lemmon's median rent is $586 per month. This is about $13 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $476 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $331 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Dickinson.
