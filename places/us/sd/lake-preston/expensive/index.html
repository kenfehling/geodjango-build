Rents

The median rent in Lake Preston is $440 per month. It is roughly $136 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $622 (58%) lower, and it is approximately $387 (46%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
