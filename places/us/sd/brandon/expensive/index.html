In comparison to its surrounding areas, Brandon is a lot more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Sioux Falls, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brandon is $225,200. This is about $82,100 (57%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $7,700 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $35,400 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brandon is $926. It is roughly $154 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $136 (12%) lower, and it is about $99 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
