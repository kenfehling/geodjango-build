Home prices

The median home in Brandon is valued at $225,200. This is roughly $82,100 (57%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $7,700 (3%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $35,400 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

Brandon's median monthly rent is $926. It is about $154 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $136 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $99 (11%) higher.
