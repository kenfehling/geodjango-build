In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kennebec is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Pierre.

Home prices

The median home in Kennebec costs $104,900. This is roughly $19,540 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $112,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $70,700 (40%) lower compared to the city of Pierre.

Rents

In Kennebec the median rent is $750 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $161 (27%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $312 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Pierre it is about $83 (12%) higher.
