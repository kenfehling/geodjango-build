Home prices

The median home in Hudson costs $74,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $29,840 (28%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $143,200 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $115,500 (60%) lower.

Rents

In Hudson the median rent is $506 per month. It is roughly $163 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $556 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $321 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
