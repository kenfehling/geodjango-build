Home prices

Bristol's median home price is $61,900. This is roughly $23,420 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $155,600 (71%) lower, and it is roughly $99,900 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Aberdeen.

Rents

Bristol's median monthly rent is $507. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $168 (24%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $555 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Aberdeen is roughly $178 (25%) lower.
