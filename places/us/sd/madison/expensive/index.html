Home prices

The median home price in Madison is $123,900. This is approximately $16,280 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $93,600 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $65,900 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Madison is $564 per month. This is roughly $155 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $498 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $263 (31%) lower.
