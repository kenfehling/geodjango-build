Home prices

The median home price in Salem is $107,700. This is approximately $14,500 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $109,800 (50%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $82,100 (43%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

In Salem the median rent is $766 per month. This is roughly $204 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $296 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $61 (7%) lower.
