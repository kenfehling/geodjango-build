Home prices

The price of the median home in Salem is $107,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $14,500 (15%) higher. It is about $109,800 (50%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $82,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Salem is $766. This is roughly $204 (36%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $296 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $61 (7%) lower.
