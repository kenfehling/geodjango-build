Home prices

The median home in Humboldt is valued at $123,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $14,860 (10%) lower. It is approximately $93,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $66,000 (34%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

In Humboldt the median monthly rent is $684. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $46 (6%) lower. It is about $378 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $143 (17%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
