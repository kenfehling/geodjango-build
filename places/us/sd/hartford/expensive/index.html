Home prices

The value of the median home in Hartford is $184,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $44,020 (31%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $33,500 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $5,800 (3%) lower.

Rents

Hartford's median rent is $833 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $63 (8%) higher. It is roughly $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
