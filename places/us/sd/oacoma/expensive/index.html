Home prices

The median home value in Oacoma is $140,700. It is roughly $62,200 (79%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $76,800 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mitchell is roughly $6,500 (4%) lower.

Rents

Oacoma's median rent is $648 per month. It is about $11 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $414 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Mitchell it is about $108 (14%) lower.
