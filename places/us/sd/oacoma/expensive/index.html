Home prices

Rents

In Oacoma the median monthly rent is $648. This is roughly $11 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $414 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Mitchell is about $108 (14%) lower.
