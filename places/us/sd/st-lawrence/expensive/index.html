Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Lawrence is $792. It is roughly $181 (29%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $270 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $107 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Aberdeen.
