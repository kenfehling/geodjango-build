Compared to other places in this area, St. Lawrence is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Aberdeen.

Home prices

The median home in St. Lawrence costs $72,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $9,880 (12%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $145,400 (66%) lower, and is approximately $89,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Aberdeen.

Rents

In St. Lawrence the median rent is $792 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $181 (29%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $270 (25%) lower, and is roughly $107 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Aberdeen.
