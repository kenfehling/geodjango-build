Home prices

The median home cost in Grand Prairie is $162,400. It is approximately $51,820 (24%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $55,100 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $25,700 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Grand Prairie the median monthly rent is $1,091. It is about $19 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $29 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $39 (3%) higher.
