Home prices

Grand Prairie's median home price is $162,400. It is about $51,820 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $55,100 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $25,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Grand Prairie the median monthly rent is $1,091. It is roughly $19 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $29 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Dallas is approximately $39 (3%) higher.
