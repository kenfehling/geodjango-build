Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Farmers Branch is $1,378. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $73 (5%) lower.It is approximately $316 (29%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $326 (30%) higher.
