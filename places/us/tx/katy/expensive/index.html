In comparison to other places in this area, Katy is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Houston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Katy's median home value is $197,300. It is approximately $82,820 (29%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $20,200 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is about $25,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Katy the median monthly rent is $1,030. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $481 (31%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $32 (3%) lower, and is about $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
