In comparison to nearby areas, Katy is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Houston, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Katy's median home cost is $197,300. It is about $82,820 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $20,200 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is about $25,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

Katy's median rent is $1,030 a month. It is about $481 (31%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $32 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $11 (1%) lower.
