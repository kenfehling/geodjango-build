In comparison to its nearby areas, Katy is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Houston, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Katy is $197,300. This is approximately $82,820 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $20,200 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Houston it is roughly $25,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Katy is $1,030 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $481 (31%) lower. It is about $32 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $11 (1%) lower.
