In comparison to nearby areas, Katy is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Houston, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Katy is $197,300. This is about $82,820 (29%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $20,200 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $25,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

Katy's median rent is $1,030 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $481 (31%) lower. It is approximately $32 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $11 (1%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
