Home prices

Victoria's median home value is $136,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $11,440 (9%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $81,100 (37%) lower, and is about $4,700 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi.

Rents

In Victoria the median monthly rent is $929. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $255 (37%) higher.It is about $133 (12%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $99 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi.
