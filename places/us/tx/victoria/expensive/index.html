Compared to the surrounding areas, Victoria is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Corpus Christi, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Victoria is $136,400. This is about $11,440 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $81,100 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Corpus Christi is approximately $4,700 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Victoria the median monthly rent is $929. This is approximately $255 (37%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $133 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $99 (9%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.
