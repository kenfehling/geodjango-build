Compared to nearby areas, Victoria is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Corpus Christi.

Home prices

The median home in Victoria is valued at $136,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $11,440 (9%) higher. It is about $81,100 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $4,700 (3%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.

Rents

Victoria's median monthly rent is $929. It is about $255 (37%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $133 (12%) lower, and is roughly $99 (9%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.
