Home prices

The median home cost in Victoria is $136,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $11,440 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $81,100 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $4,700 (3%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.

Rents

In Victoria the median rent is $929 per month. This is roughly $255 (37%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $133 (12%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Corpus Christi it is approximately $99 (9%) lower.
