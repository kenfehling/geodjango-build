Home prices

Mount Pleasant's median home price is $100,600. It is approximately $12,760 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $116,900 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $47,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median rent is $676 per month. This is roughly $29 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $386 (36%) lower, and is roughly $136 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
