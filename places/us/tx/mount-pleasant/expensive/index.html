Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant costs $100,600. It is about $12,760 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $116,900 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $47,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Pleasant is $676 per month. This is about $29 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $386 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is about $136 (16%) lower.
