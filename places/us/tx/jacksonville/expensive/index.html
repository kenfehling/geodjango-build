Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $89,000. It is about $8,320 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $128,500 (59%) lower, and it is about $71,000 (44%) lower compared to the city of Tyler.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $719. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $21 (2%) lower.It is roughly $343 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $204 (22%) lower compared to the city of Tyler.
