Home prices

The median home value in Jacksonville is $89,000. It is approximately $8,320 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $128,500 (59%) lower, and is approximately $71,000 (44%) lower compared to the city of Tyler.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksonville is $719. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $21 (2%) lower. It is roughly $343 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $204 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Tyler.
