Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $89,000. It is about $8,320 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $128,500 (59%) lower, and is about $71,000 (44%) lower compared to the city of Tyler.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksonville is $719. This is about $21 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $343 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $204 (22%) lower compared to the city of Tyler.
