Home prices

The median home in Heath costs $484,600. It is roughly $173,500 (55%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $267,100 (122%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $296,500 (157%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Heath the median rent is $1,993 per month. It is roughly $733 (58%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $931 (87%) higher, and it is approximately $941 (89%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
