In comparison to its surrounding areas, Port Isabel is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Brownsville.

Home prices

The median home in Port Isabel costs $108,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $21,320 (16%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $109,000 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is roughly $18,500 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Port Isabel is $724 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $50 (6%) lower.It is roughly $338 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.
