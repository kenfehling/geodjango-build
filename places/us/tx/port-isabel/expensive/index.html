In comparison to other places in this area, Port Isabel is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Port Isabel is valued at $108,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $29,140 (21%) lower. It is about $109,000 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $18,500 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

In Port Isabel the median rent is $724 per month. It is roughly $88 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is roughly $12 (1%) lower.
