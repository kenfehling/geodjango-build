Home prices

The median home price in Palmview is $89,900. It is about $12,780 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $127,600 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is is very similar.

Rents

In Palmview the median monthly rent is $713. This is roughly $15 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $23 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
