Home prices

The value of the median home in Palmview is $89,900. This is roughly $12,780 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $127,600 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is is very similar.

Rents

In Palmview the median monthly rent is $713. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $15 (2%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $349 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is roughly $23 (3%) lower.
