Home prices

The median home in Selma is valued at $188,300. It is approximately $2,820 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $29,200 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $41,900 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Selma the median monthly rent is $1,442. This is roughly $168 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $380 (35%) higher, and it is roughly $450 (45%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
