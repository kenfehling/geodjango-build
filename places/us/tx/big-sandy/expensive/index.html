Compared to other places in this area, Big Sandy is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The median home in Big Sandy costs $84,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $20,420 (19%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $132,900 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is roughly $63,500 (42%) lower.

Rents

Big Sandy's median rent is $700 a month. It is about $268 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $362 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is about $112 (13%) lower.
