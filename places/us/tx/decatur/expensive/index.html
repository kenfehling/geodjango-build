Home prices

The median home in Decatur is valued at $156,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $42,220 (36%) higher. It is roughly $61,000 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $31,600 (16%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Decatur the median rent is $1,035 per month. This is approximately $68 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $27 (2%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas is approximately $17 (1%) lower.
