Compared to its nearby areas, Rio Grande City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Brownsville.

Home prices

The median home in Rio Grande City costs $72,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $9,440 (15%) higher. It is roughly $145,400 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $17,900 (19%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

In Rio Grande City the median rent is $643 a month. This is roughly $66 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $419 (39%) lower, and is approximately $93 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
