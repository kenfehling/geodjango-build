Compared to other places in this area, Rio Grande City is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Brownsville however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Rio Grande City is $72,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $9,440 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $145,400 (66%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is roughly $17,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

Rio Grande City's median monthly rent is $643. It is about $66 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $419 (39%) lower, and it is about $93 (12%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.
