Compared to other places in this area, Rio Grande City is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rio Grande City is valued at $72,100. This is about $9,440 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $145,400 (66%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $17,900 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rio Grande City is $643. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $66 (11%) higher. It is approximately $419 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $93 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
