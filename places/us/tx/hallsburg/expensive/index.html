Home prices

The median home in Hallsburg costs $146,900. This is approximately $67,260 (84%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $70,600 (32%) lower, and is roughly $41,200 (21%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median rent in Hallsburg is $832 per month. This is approximately $21 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $230 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $220 (20%) lower.
