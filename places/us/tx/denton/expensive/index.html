Compared to other places in this area, Denton is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Dallas, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Denton costs $218,800. This is about $25,820 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $30,700 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Denton the median rent is $1,082 a month. It is roughly $415 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $20 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $30 (2%) higher.
