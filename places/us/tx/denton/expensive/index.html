In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denton is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The median home in Denton is valued at $218,800. This is about $25,820 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $30,700 (16%) higher.

Rents

Denton's median rent is $1,082 a month. It is approximately $415 (27%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $20 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $30 (2%) higher.
