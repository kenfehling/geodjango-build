In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denton is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Denton's median home cost is $218,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $25,820 (10%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is is very similar, and it is approximately $30,700 (16%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Denton is $1,082. It is approximately $415 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $20 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $30 (2%) higher.
