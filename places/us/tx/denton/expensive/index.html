Compared to the surrounding areas, Denton is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The median home in Denton is valued at $218,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $25,820 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is approximately $30,700 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Denton the median monthly rent is $1,082. This is about $415 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $20 (1%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $30 (2%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
