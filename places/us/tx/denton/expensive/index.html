Compared to its surrounding areas, Denton is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Denton is $218,800. This is approximately $25,820 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is is very similar, and it is approximately $30,700 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Denton the median rent is $1,082 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $415 (27%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $20 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $30 (2%) higher.
