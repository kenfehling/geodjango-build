Home prices

Rents

In Sullivan City the median rent is $563 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $60 (9%) lower. It is roughly $499 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is about $173 (23%) lower.
