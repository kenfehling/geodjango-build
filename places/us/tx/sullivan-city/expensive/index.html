Home prices

The price of the median home in Sullivan City is $58,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $13,380 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $159,400 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is about $31,900 (35%) lower.

Rents

Sullivan City's median monthly rent is $563. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $60 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $499 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville is about $173 (23%) lower.
