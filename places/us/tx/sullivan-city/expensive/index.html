Home prices

Sullivan City's median home price is $58,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $13,380 (18%) lower.It is approximately $159,400 (73%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $31,900 (35%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

Sullivan City's median monthly rent is $563. This is approximately $60 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $499 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $173 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
