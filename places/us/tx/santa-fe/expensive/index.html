Compared to its surrounding areas, Santa Fe is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Houston, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Santa Fe is $184,700. It is roughly $16,160 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $32,800 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $12,900 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Santa Fe the median monthly rent is $932. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $150 (13%) lower. It is roughly $130 (12%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $109 (10%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
