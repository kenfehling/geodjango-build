In comparison to other places in this area, Santa Fe is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Houston, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Fe is valued at $184,700. This is approximately $16,160 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $32,800 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $12,900 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Santa Fe the median rent is $932 a month. This is approximately $150 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $130 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is roughly $109 (10%) lower.
