Home prices

The median home in East Mountain is valued at $154,800. It is roughly $30,560 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $62,700 (28%) lower, and it is about $6,700 (4%) higher compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in East Mountain is $953 per month. This is about $23 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $109 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $141 (17%) higher.
