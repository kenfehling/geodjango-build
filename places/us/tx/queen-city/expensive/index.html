In comparison to its nearby areas, Queen City is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The median home in Queen City costs $82,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $11,160 (11%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $135,100 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $65,700 (44%) lower.

Rents

Queen City's median monthly rent is $594. This is roughly $76 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $468 (44%) lower, and it is about $218 (26%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
