Home prices

The price of the median home in Oak Point is $274,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $43,320 (18%) higher.It is roughly $57,400 (26%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $86,800 (46%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Oak Point the median monthly rent is $1,766. It is about $178 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $704 (66%) higher, and compared to the city of Dallas is approximately $714 (67%) higher.
