Home prices

Oak Point's median home price is $274,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $43,320 (18%) higher.It is approximately $57,400 (26%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $86,800 (46%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Oak Point's median monthly rent is $1,766. It is roughly $178 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $704 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $714 (67%) higher.
