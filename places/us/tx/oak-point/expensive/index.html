Home prices

The median home in Oak Point costs $274,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $43,320 (18%) higher. It is roughly $57,400 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $86,800 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Oak Point the median rent is $1,766 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $178 (11%) higher. It is about $704 (66%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $714 (67%) higher.
