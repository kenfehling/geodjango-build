The median home in Nederland costs $132,700. This is roughly $8,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $84,800 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $39,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Nederland the median rent is $873 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $75 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $189 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is about $168 (16%) lower.
