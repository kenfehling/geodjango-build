The median home in McKinney is valued at $309,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $9,440 (2%) lower. It is approximately $91,700 (42%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $121,100 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median rent in McKinney is $1,394 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $22 (1%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $332 (31%) higher, and it is approximately $342 (32%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
