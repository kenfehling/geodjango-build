Home prices

The median home in McKinney is valued at $309,200. It is roughly $9,440 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $91,700 (42%) higher, and is approximately $121,100 (64%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

McKinney's median monthly rent is $1,394. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $22 (1%) higher. It is about $332 (31%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $342 (32%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
