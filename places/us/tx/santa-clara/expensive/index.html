Home prices

The median home value in Santa Clara is $291,500. This is roughly $124,360 (74%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $74,000 (34%) higher, and is about $145,100 (99%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Santa Clara the median monthly rent is $763. This is about $400 (34%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $299 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio is roughly $229 (23%) lower.
