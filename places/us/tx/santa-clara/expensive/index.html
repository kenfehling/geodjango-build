Compared to other places in this area, Santa Clara is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of San Antonio, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Santa Clara is $291,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $124,360 (74%) higher. It is roughly $74,000 (34%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $145,100 (99%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Santa Clara the median rent is $763 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $400 (34%) lower. It is about $299 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $229 (23%) lower.
