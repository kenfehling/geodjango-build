Compared to other places in this area, Santa Clara is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Santa Clara is $291,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $124,360 (74%) higher.It is roughly $74,000 (34%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $145,100 (99%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Santa Clara's median rent is $763 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $400 (34%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $229 (23%) lower.
