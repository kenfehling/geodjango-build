Home prices

Santa Clara's median monthly rent is $763. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $400 (34%) lower.It is about $299 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $229 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
