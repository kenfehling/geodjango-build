Home prices

The price of the median home in Crowley is $157,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $24,380 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $59,600 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is approximately $30,200 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Crowley is $1,249. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $165 (15%) higher. It is approximately $187 (17%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $197 (18%) higher.
