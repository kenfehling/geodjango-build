Compared to its nearby areas, Corinth is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The median home in Corinth is valued at $257,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $77,200 (23%) lower. It is approximately $40,100 (18%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $69,500 (36%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Corinth the median monthly rent is $1,308. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $201 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $246 (23%) higher, and it is about $256 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
