Home prices

The cost of the median home in Corinth is $257,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $77,200 (23%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $40,100 (18%) higher, and is roughly $69,500 (36%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Corinth the median monthly rent is $1,308. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $201 (13%) lower.It is approximately $246 (23%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $256 (24%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
