Compared to its nearby areas, Corinth is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Dallas, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Corinth is $257,600. This is roughly $77,200 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $40,100 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $69,500 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corinth is $1,308. This is roughly $201 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $246 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $256 (24%) higher.
