In comparison to the surrounding areas, Universal City is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Universal City is valued at $168,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,600 (8%) lower.It is about $49,200 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $21,900 (14%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Universal City's median monthly rent is $985. This is approximately $251 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $77 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
