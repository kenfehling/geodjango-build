Home prices

The median home in Universal City costs $168,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $16,600 (8%) lower.It is roughly $49,200 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is roughly $21,900 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Universal City the median rent is $985 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $251 (20%) lower.It is about $77 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio is is very similar.
