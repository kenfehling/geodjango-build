Home prices

The median home in Angleton costs $122,000. This is about $4,060 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $95,500 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $49,800 (28%) lower.

Rents

Angleton's median monthly rent is $923. It is about $53 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $139 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $118 (11%) lower.
