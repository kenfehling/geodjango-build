Home prices

The value of the median home in Angleton is $122,000. It is approximately $4,060 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $95,500 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is about $49,800 (28%) lower.

Rents

Angleton's median monthly rent is $923. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $53 (6%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $139 (13%) lower, and is about $118 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
