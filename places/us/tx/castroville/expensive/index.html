Home prices

The median home in Castroville costs $189,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $96,160 (103%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $28,100 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $43,000 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Castroville the median rent is $978 a month. This is about $236 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $84 (7%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $14 (1%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
