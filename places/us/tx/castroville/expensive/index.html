Home prices

The median home in Castroville costs $189,400. This is about $96,160 (103%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $28,100 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $43,000 (29%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Castroville the median rent is $978 a month. It is approximately $236 (31%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $84 (7%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $14 (1%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
