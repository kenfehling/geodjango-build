Home prices

Castroville's median home cost is $189,400. This is roughly $96,160 (103%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $28,100 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $43,000 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Castroville's median monthly rent is $978. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $236 (31%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $84 (7%) lower, and is approximately $14 (1%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
