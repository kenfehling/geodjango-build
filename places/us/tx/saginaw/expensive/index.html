Home prices

The value of the median home in Saginaw is $168,900. This is approximately $59,540 (54%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $48,600 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $19,200 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Saginaw the median monthly rent is $1,206. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $108 (9%) higher. It is about $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $154 (14%) higher.
