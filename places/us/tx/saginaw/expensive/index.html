Home prices

The value of the median home in Saginaw is $168,900. It is roughly $59,540 (54%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $48,600 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $19,200 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Saginaw the median monthly rent is $1,206. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $108 (9%) higher. It is about $144 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $154 (14%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
