Home prices

The median home in Saginaw costs $168,900. This is about $59,540 (54%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $48,600 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $19,200 (10%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Saginaw's median rent is $1,206 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $108 (9%) higher.It is approximately $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $154 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
