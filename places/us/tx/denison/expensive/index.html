Home prices

The median home in Denison costs $98,300. This is approximately $7,160 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $119,200 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $89,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Denison the median rent is $810 per month. It is approximately $137 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $252 (23%) lower, and is approximately $242 (23%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
