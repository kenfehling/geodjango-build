Home prices

The price of the median home in Cedar Point is $175,000. It is roughly $90,400 (106%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $42,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $3,200 (1%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in Cedar Point is $948 a month. It is roughly $139 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $114 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $93 (8%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
