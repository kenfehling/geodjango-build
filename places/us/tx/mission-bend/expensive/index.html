Home prices

The median home in Mission Bend is valued at $146,800. This is about $86,080 (36%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $70,700 (32%) lower, and is roughly $25,000 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mission Bend is $1,426. It is roughly $41 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $364 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $385 (36%) higher.
