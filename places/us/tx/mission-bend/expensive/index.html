Compared to the surrounding areas, Mission Bend is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Houston, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mission Bend is $146,800. It is approximately $86,080 (36%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $70,700 (32%) lower, and it is about $25,000 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Mission Bend's median monthly rent is $1,426. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $41 (2%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $364 (34%) higher, and it is roughly $385 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
