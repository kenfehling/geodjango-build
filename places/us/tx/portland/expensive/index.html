Compared to nearby areas, Portland is a lot more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Corpus Christi, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Portland is $167,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $47,240 (39%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $50,100 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Corpus Christi is roughly $26,300 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Portland the median monthly rent is $1,250. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $225 (21%) higher. It is about $188 (17%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Corpus Christi is approximately $222 (21%) higher.
