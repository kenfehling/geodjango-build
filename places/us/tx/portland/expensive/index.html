Compared to nearby areas, Portland is significantly more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Corpus Christi however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Portland is $167,400. This is approximately $47,240 (39%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $50,100 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi is approximately $26,300 (18%) higher.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $1,250. This is approximately $225 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $188 (17%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Corpus Christi is roughly $222 (21%) higher.
