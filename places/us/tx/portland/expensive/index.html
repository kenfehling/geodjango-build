Compared to its nearby areas, Portland is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Corpus Christi however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $167,400. It is approximately $47,240 (39%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $50,100 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $26,300 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $1,250. This is roughly $225 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $188 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $222 (21%) higher compared to the city of Corpus Christi.
