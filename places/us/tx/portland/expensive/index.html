In comparison to the surrounding areas, Portland is substantially more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Corpus Christi however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Portland's median home value is $167,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $47,240 (39%) higher.It is roughly $50,100 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi is about $26,300 (18%) higher.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $1,250. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $225 (21%) higher.It is approximately $188 (17%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Corpus Christi is roughly $222 (21%) higher.
