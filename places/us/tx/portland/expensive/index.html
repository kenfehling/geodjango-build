In comparison to its surrounding areas, Portland is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Corpus Christi, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Portland is valued at $167,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $47,240 (39%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $50,100 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $26,300 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi.

Rents

In Portland the median rent is $1,250 a month. It is roughly $225 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $188 (17%) higher, and is approximately $222 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi.
