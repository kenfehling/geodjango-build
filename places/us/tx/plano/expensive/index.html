Home prices

The median home value in Plano is $320,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $21,800 (6%) lower. It is approximately $102,600 (47%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $132,000 (70%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents
