Rents

In Plano the median monthly rent is $1,418. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $193 (12%) lower. It is approximately $356 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $366 (34%) higher.
