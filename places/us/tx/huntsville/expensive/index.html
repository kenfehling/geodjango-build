Home prices

The value of the median home in Huntsville is $165,000. This is roughly $58,100 (54%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $52,500 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $6,800 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Huntsville the median rent is $899 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $85 (10%) higher. It is roughly $163 (15%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $142 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
