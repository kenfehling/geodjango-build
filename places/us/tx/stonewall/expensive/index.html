Home prices

Rents

In Stonewall the median rent is $730 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $215 (22%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $332 (31%) lower, and is approximately $262 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
