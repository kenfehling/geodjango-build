In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hawkins is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hawkins is $83,400. It is about $27,440 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $134,100 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $64,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

Hawkins's median monthly rent is $875. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $56 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $187 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $63 (7%) higher.
