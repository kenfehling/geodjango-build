Home prices

The value of the median home in Weimar is $105,300. It is roughly $34,580 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $112,200 (51%) lower, and is approximately $66,500 (38%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Weimar the median rent is $691 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $165 (19%) lower. It is about $371 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $350 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
