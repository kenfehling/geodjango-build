Home prices

The median home price in Buffalo is $91,700. This is about $10,620 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $125,800 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $33,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Waco.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $646 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $119 (15%) lower.It is about $416 (39%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Waco is approximately $216 (25%) lower.
