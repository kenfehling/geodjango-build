In comparison to its surrounding areas, Buffalo is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Waco however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Buffalo costs $91,700. This is approximately $10,620 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $125,800 (57%) lower, and it is roughly $33,600 (26%) lower compared to the city of Waco.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $646 a month. It is approximately $119 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $416 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Waco it is approximately $216 (25%) lower.
