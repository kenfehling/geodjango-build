Compared to its surrounding areas, Buffalo is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Waco, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Buffalo is $91,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $10,620 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $125,800 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Waco is about $33,600 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Buffalo is $646 per month. It is roughly $119 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $416 (39%) lower, and is roughly $216 (25%) lower compared to the city of Waco.
