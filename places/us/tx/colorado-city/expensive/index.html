Home prices

The median home in Colorado City costs $65,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $4,480 (7%) higher. It is roughly $152,300 (70%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $55,200 (45%) lower compared to the city of Abilene.

Rents

In Colorado City the median rent is $670 a month. This is approximately $44 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $392 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Abilene is roughly $254 (27%) lower.
