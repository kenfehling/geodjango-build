Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Killeen is $923 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $42 (4%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $139 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $357 (27%) lower compared to the city of Austin.
