Compared to its nearby areas, Killeen is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Austin.

Home prices

The median home in Killeen costs $126,200. This is approximately $7,220 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $91,300 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Austin is approximately $211,200 (62%) lower.

Rents

Killeen's median rent is $923 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $42 (4%) lower. It is roughly $139 (13%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $357 (27%) lower compared to the city of Austin.
