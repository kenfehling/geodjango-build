Home prices

The median home in Medina is valued at $48,600. This is roughly $19,140 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $168,900 (77%) lower, and is about $81,300 (62%) lower compared to the city of Laredo.

Rents

Medina's median monthly rent is $487. It is roughly $85 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $575 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Laredo is roughly $364 (42%) lower.
