Home prices

The price of the median home in Lago Vista is $237,200. It is approximately $154,360 (39%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $19,700 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $90,800 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Lago Vista the median monthly rent is $1,420. It is about $120 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $358 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $428 (43%) higher.
