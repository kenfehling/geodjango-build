In comparison to other places in this area, Lake Worth is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Dallas, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lake Worth is $114,600. This is about $7,780 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $102,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $73,500 (39%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Worth is $1,035 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $59 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $27 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $17 (1%) lower.
