Home prices

The median home price in Lake Worth is $114,600. This is about $7,780 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $102,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $73,500 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Lake Worth the median monthly rent is $1,035. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $59 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $27 (2%) lower, and it is roughly $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
