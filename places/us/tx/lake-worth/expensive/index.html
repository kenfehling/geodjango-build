Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake Worth is $114,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $7,780 (6%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $102,900 (47%) lower, and it is roughly $73,500 (39%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Lake Worth's median monthly rent is $1,035. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $59 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $27 (2%) lower, and it is approximately $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
