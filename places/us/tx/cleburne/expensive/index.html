Home prices

The median home value in Cleburne is $112,700. This is about $16,440 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $104,800 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $75,400 (40%) lower.

Rents

Cleburne's median rent is $947 a month. It is roughly $33 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $115 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $105 (9%) lower.
