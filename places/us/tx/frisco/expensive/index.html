Home prices

The median home in Frisco is valued at $395,900. This is about $106,360 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $178,400 (82%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $207,800 (110%) higher.

Rents

Frisco's median monthly rent is $1,505. Compared to other places in this area this is about $80 (5%) lower. It is approximately $443 (41%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $453 (43%) higher.
