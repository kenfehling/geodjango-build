Home prices

The median home in Frisco costs $395,900. This is approximately $106,360 (36%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $178,400 (82%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dallas is approximately $207,800 (110%) higher.

Rents

Frisco's median rent is $1,505 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $80 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $443 (41%) higher, and it is approximately $453 (43%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
