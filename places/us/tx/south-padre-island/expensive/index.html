Compared to other places in this area, South Padre Island is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Brownsville.

Home prices

The median home in South Padre Island costs $306,800. It is approximately $208,820 (213%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $89,300 (41%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Brownsville is approximately $216,800 (240%) higher.

Rents

In South Padre Island the median rent is $1,081 per month. This is roughly $340 (45%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $19 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $345 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
