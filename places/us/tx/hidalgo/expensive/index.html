Home prices

The median home in Hidalgo is valued at $101,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $18,080 (21%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $116,400 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville is approximately $11,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

Hidalgo's median monthly rent is $675. It is about $152 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $387 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $61 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
