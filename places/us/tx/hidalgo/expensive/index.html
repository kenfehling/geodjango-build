Home prices

The median home in Hidalgo is valued at $101,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $18,080 (21%) higher.It is approximately $116,400 (53%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is approximately $11,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

Hidalgo's median rent is $675 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $152 (18%) lower.It is about $387 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Brownsville is approximately $61 (8%) lower.
