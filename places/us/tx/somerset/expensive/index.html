Home prices

Somerset's median home price is $112,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $26,180 (30%) higher.It is about $104,600 (48%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $33,500 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Somerset the median monthly rent is $683. This is roughly $115 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $379 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is roughly $309 (31%) lower.
