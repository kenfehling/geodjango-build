Rents

In Fort Clark Springs the median rent is $842 per month. This is approximately $231 (37%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $220 (20%) lower, and is approximately $160 (23%) higher compared to the city of Del Rio.
