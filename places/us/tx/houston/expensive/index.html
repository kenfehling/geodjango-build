Home prices

The cost of the median home in Houston is $171,800. It is roughly $640,383 (78%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $45,700 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole.

Rents

Houston's median monthly rent is $1,041. It is approximately $351 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $21 (1%) lower.
