In comparison to other places in this area, Houston is a lot less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Houston costs $171,800. This is roughly $640,383 (78%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $45,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Houston the median monthly rent is $1,041. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $351 (25%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $21 (1%) lower.
