Compared to its surrounding areas, Houston is substantially less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The median home in Houston is valued at $171,800. This is approximately $640,383 (78%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $45,700 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Houston is $1,041. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $351 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $21 (1%) lower.
