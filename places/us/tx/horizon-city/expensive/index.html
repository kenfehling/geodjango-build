Home prices

Rents

In Horizon City the median monthly rent is $1,043. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $343 (49%) higher.It is approximately $19 (1%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $206 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of El Paso.
