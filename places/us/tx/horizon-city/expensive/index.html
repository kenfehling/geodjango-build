Home prices

The price of the median home in Horizon City is $121,500. It is roughly $41,460 (51%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $96,000 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of El Paso it is approximately $5,900 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Horizon City is $1,043. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $343 (49%) higher. It is approximately $19 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $206 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of El Paso.
