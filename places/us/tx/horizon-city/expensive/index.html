Home prices

Horizon City's median home value is $121,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $41,460 (51%) higher. It is about $96,000 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $5,900 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.

Rents

In Horizon City the median rent is $1,043 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $343 (49%) higher. It is about $19 (1%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of El Paso is approximately $206 (24%) higher.
