Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Richland Hills is $203,800. This is approximately $17,060 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $13,700 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $15,700 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Richland Hills is $1,165 per month. It is approximately $153 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $103 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $113 (10%) higher.
