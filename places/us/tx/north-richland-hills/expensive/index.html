Home prices

North Richland Hills's median home value is $203,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $17,060 (7%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $13,700 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $15,700 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In North Richland Hills the median rent is $1,165 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $153 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $103 (9%) higher, and is approximately $113 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
