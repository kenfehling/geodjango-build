Home prices

The value of the median home in Live Oak is $151,200. This is approximately $37,120 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $66,300 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $4,800 (3%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Live Oak is $1,133 a month. This is about $74 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $71 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $141 (14%) higher.
