Home prices

The median home in Manor costs $170,300. This is approximately $9,540 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $47,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $23,900 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Manor the median rent is $1,550 per month. This is about $353 (29%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $488 (45%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $558 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
