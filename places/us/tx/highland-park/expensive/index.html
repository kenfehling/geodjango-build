Compared to its surrounding areas, Highland Park is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Highland Park costs $1,508,900. This is roughly $1,088,180 (258%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,291,400 (593%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $1,320,800 (702%) higher.

Rents

In Highland Park the median monthly rent is $2,275. Compared to other places in this area this is about $939 (70%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $1,213 (114%) higher, and is approximately $1,223 (116%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
