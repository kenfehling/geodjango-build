Home prices

The median home price in Hickory Creek is $283,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $46,600 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $65,600 (30%) higher, and is about $95,000 (50%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Hickory Creek the median monthly rent is $1,511. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $42 (2%) higher.It is roughly $449 (42%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $459 (43%) higher.
