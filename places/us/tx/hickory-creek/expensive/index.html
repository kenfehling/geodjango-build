Compared to nearby areas, Hickory Creek is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The median home in Hickory Creek costs $283,100. This is roughly $46,600 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $65,600 (30%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $95,000 (50%) higher.

Rents

Hickory Creek's median monthly rent is $1,511. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $42 (2%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $449 (42%) higher, and it is approximately $459 (43%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
