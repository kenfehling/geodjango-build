Home prices

The median home cost in Breckenridge is $66,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $19,320 (40%) higher. It is about $150,600 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Abilene it is approximately $53,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Breckenridge is $715 a month. It is roughly $65 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $347 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $209 (22%) lower compared to the city of Abilene.
