Home prices

The median home in Lockney is valued at $66,800. This is roughly $7,340 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $150,700 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lubbock is roughly $74,000 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Lockney the median monthly rent is $704. Compared to nearby areas it is about $50 (7%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $358 (33%) lower, and is approximately $251 (26%) lower compared to the city of Lubbock.
