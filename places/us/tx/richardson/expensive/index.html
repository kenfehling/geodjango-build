Home prices

The median home price in Richardson is $274,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $7,680 (2%) lower.It is roughly $56,700 (26%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $86,100 (45%) higher.

Rents

Richardson's median monthly rent is $1,403. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $99 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $341 (32%) higher, and is about $351 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
