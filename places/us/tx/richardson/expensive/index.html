Similar to its surrounding areas, it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Dallas, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richardson costs $274,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $7,680 (2%) lower. It is approximately $56,700 (26%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $86,100 (45%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richardson is $1,403. This is roughly $99 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $341 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $351 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
