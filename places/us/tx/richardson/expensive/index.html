Home prices

The median home price in Richardson is $274,200. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $7,680 (2%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $56,700 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $86,100 (45%) higher.

Rents

In Richardson the median rent is $1,403 per month. It is approximately $99 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $341 (32%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $351 (33%) higher.
