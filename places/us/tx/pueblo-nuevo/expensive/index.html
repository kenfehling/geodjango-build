The median home price in Pueblo Nuevo is $95,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $21,240 (28%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Laredo is about $34,100 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pueblo Nuevo is $613. This is roughly $12 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $449 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $238 (27%) lower compared to the city of Laredo.
