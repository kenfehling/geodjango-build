Compared to its surrounding areas, Hutto is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hutto is $194,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $42,400 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $23,500 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is approximately $47,600 (32%) higher.

Rents

Hutto's median monthly rent is $1,544. This is roughly $279 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $482 (45%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $552 (55%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
