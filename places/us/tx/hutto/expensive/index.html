Home prices

The median home in Hutto is valued at $194,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $42,400 (17%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $23,500 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $47,600 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Hutto is $1,544 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $279 (22%) higher.It is about $482 (45%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $552 (55%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
