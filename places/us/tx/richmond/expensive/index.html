Compared to other places in this area, Richmond is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

Richmond's median home cost is $157,600. It is about $66,080 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $14,200 (8%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond is $840 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $504 (37%) lower.It is approximately $222 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $201 (19%) lower.
