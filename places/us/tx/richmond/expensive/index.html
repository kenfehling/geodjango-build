In comparison to other places in this area, Richmond is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Houston, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Richmond is $157,600. It is about $66,080 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $59,900 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $14,200 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richmond is $840. This is roughly $504 (37%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $222 (20%) lower, and is about $201 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
