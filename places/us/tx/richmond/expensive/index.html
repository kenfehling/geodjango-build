Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $157,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $66,080 (29%) lower. It is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $14,200 (8%) lower.

Rents

Richmond's median monthly rent is $840. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $504 (37%) lower. It is about $222 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $201 (19%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
