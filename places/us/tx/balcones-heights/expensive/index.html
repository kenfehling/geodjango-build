Compared to other places in this area, Balcones Heights is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Balcones Heights is $136,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $352,400 (72%) lower. It is approximately $80,800 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $9,700 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Balcones Heights's median monthly rent is $769. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $460 (37%) lower. It is about $293 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $223 (22%) lower.
