In comparison to the surrounding areas, Balcones Heights is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Balcones Heights is $136,700. This is approximately $352,400 (72%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $80,800 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $9,700 (6%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Balcones Heights the median monthly rent is $769. This is about $460 (37%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $293 (27%) lower, and is about $223 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
