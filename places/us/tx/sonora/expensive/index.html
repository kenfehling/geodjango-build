Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sonora is $96,800. It is roughly $24,520 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $120,700 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $41,200 (29%) lower compared to the city of San Angelo.

Rents

Sonora's median rent is $629 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $99 (18%) higher. It is roughly $433 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Angelo it is about $255 (28%) lower.
