In comparison to the surrounding areas, Coupland is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Austin.

Home prices

The median home cost in Coupland is $159,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $23,760 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $57,800 (26%) lower, and it is about $177,700 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.

Rents

In Coupland the median rent is $850 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $427 (33%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Austin is about $430 (33%) lower.
