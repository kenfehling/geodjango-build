Home prices

Boerne's median home price is $286,300. It is approximately $80,460 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $68,800 (31%) higher, and it is roughly $139,900 (95%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Boerne the median monthly rent is $1,260. This is about $551 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $198 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is roughly $268 (27%) higher.
