Home prices

The median home in Friendswood costs $284,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $68,240 (31%) higher.It is roughly $66,600 (30%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $112,300 (65%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Friendswood the median monthly rent is $1,389. This is roughly $186 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $327 (30%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $348 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
