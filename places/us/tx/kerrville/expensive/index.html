In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kerrville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kerrville is valued at $179,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $23,840 (11%) lower.It is roughly $38,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $32,800 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Kerrville the median rent is $874 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $86 (8%) lower.It is approximately $188 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $118 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
