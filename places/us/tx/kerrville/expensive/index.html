Home prices

The median home cost in Kerrville is $179,200. It is roughly $23,840 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $38,300 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $32,800 (22%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Kerrville is $874 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $86 (8%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $188 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $118 (11%) lower.
