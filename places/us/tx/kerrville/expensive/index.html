Home prices

The median home in Kerrville is valued at $179,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $23,840 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $38,300 (17%) lower, and is roughly $32,800 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Kerrville's median rent is $874 a month. It is about $86 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $188 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $118 (11%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
