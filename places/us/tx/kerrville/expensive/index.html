Compared to the surrounding areas, Kerrville is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Kerrville is $179,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $23,840 (11%) lower.It is roughly $38,300 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio is roughly $32,800 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Kerrville the median rent is $874 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $86 (8%) lower.It is about $188 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $118 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
