Compared to other places in this area, Barton Creek is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

Barton Creek's median home cost is $1,281,300. This is roughly $672,740 (110%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,063,800 (489%) higher, and is approximately $1,134,900 (775%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Barton Creek's median rent is $1,552 per month. This is roughly $530 (25%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $490 (46%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $560 (56%) higher.
