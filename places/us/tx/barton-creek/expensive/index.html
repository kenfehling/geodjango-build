Home prices

The median home value in Barton Creek is $1,281,300. This is about $561,660 (78%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $1,063,800 (489%) higher, and it is roughly $1,134,900 (775%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Barton Creek's median monthly rent is $1,552. This is approximately $634 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $490 (46%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $560 (56%) higher.
