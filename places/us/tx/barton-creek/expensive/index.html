Home prices

Barton Creek's median home cost is $1,281,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $672,740 (110%) higher. It is roughly $1,063,800 (489%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $1,134,900 (775%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Barton Creek's median rent is $1,552 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $530 (25%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $490 (46%) higher, and is about $560 (56%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
