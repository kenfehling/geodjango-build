Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kyle is $196,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $2,860 (1%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $20,800 (9%) lower, and is approximately $50,300 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Kyle's median rent is $1,466 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $135 (10%) higher.It is roughly $404 (38%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $474 (47%) higher.
