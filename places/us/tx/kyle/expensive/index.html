Home prices

The value of the median home in Kyle is $196,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $2,860 (1%) lower. It is approximately $20,800 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $50,300 (34%) higher.

Rents

In Kyle the median monthly rent is $1,466. This is approximately $135 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $404 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is about $474 (47%) higher.
