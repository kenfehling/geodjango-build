Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fritch is $93,800. It is approximately $22,100 (30%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $123,700 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $38,700 (29%) lower compared to the city of Amarillo.

Rents

In Fritch the median monthly rent is $792. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $95 (13%) higher. It is roughly $270 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $58 (6%) lower compared to the city of Amarillo.
