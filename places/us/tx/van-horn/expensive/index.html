Compared to other places in this area, Van Horn is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Marfa, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Van Horn is $62,600. It is roughly $8,250 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $154,900 (71%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $105,500 (62%) lower compared to the city of Marfa.

Rents

Van Horn's median rent is $424 a month. This is about $198 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $638 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Marfa it is approximately $292 (40%) lower.
