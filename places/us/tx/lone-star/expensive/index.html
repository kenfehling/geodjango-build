Home prices

Lone Star's median home price is $108,200. It is approximately $34,580 (46%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $109,300 (50%) lower, and is approximately $39,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in Lone Star is $686 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $39 (5%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $376 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $126 (15%) lower.
