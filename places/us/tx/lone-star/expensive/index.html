Home prices

Rents

Lone Star's median rent is $686 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $39 (5%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $376 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is about $126 (15%) lower.
