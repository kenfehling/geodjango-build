Compared to the surrounding areas, Lone Star is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Shreveport, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lone Star is valued at $108,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $34,580 (46%) higher. It is roughly $109,300 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $39,900 (26%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in Lone Star is $686 per month. This is roughly $39 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $376 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $126 (15%) lower.
