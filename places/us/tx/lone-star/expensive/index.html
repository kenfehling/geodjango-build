Home prices

The value of the median home in Lone Star is $108,200. This is about $34,580 (46%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $109,300 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $39,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

Lone Star's median rent is $686 a month. It is approximately $39 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $376 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $126 (15%) lower.
