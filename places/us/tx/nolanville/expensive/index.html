Home prices

The median home cost in Nolanville is $127,600. This is approximately $18,320 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $89,900 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $209,800 (62%) lower compared to the city of Austin.

Rents

The median rent in Nolanville is $881 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $146 (14%) lower. It is roughly $181 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Austin it is approximately $399 (31%) lower.
