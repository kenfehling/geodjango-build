Home prices

The median home in Carrollton costs $238,300. This is about $76,640 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $20,800 (9%) higher, and is about $50,200 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Carrollton's median rent is $1,236 per month. This is roughly $181 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $174 (16%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $184 (17%) higher.
