Home prices

The median home in Hewitt costs $167,500. This is roughly $20,720 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $50,000 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Austin is roughly $169,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Hewitt the median rent is $1,155 a month. It is approximately $108 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $93 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $125 (9%) lower compared to the city of Austin.
