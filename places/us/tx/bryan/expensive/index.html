Home prices

Bryan's median home value is $147,600. It is about $6,680 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $69,900 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $24,200 (14%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bryan is $915 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $43 (4%) lower. It is about $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $126 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
