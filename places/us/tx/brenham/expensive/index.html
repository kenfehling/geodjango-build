In comparison to the surrounding areas, Brenham is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home price in Brenham is $163,800. It is approximately $26,580 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $53,700 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is roughly $8,000 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Brenham the median monthly rent is $865. It is roughly $86 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $197 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $176 (16%) lower.
