In comparison to other places in this area, Brenham is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Houston, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Brenham costs $163,800. It is roughly $26,580 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $53,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $8,000 (4%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brenham is $865. It is about $86 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $197 (18%) lower, and is roughly $176 (16%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
