Compared to nearby areas, Brenham is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home value in Brenham is $163,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $26,580 (19%) higher. It is roughly $53,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $8,000 (4%) lower.

Rents

Brenham's median rent is $865 a month. This is about $86 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $197 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is approximately $176 (16%) lower.
