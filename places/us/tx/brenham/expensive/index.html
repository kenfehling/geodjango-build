Compared to the surrounding areas, Brenham is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Brenham is $163,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $26,580 (19%) higher. It is about $53,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Houston is about $8,000 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Brenham the median monthly rent is $865. This is roughly $86 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $197 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $176 (16%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
