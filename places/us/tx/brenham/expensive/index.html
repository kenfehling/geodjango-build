Home prices

The value of the median home in Brenham is $163,800. This is roughly $26,580 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $53,700 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $8,000 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Brenham the median rent is $865 per month. This is roughly $86 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $197 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $176 (16%) lower.
