Home prices

The median home in Three Rivers costs $78,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $3,840 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $139,200 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is about $68,100 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Three Rivers the median rent is $635 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $107 (14%) lower. It is roughly $427 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $357 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
