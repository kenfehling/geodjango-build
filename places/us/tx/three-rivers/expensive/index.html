Rents

Three Rivers's median monthly rent is $635. This is approximately $107 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $427 (40%) lower, and is approximately $357 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
