Home prices

The cost of the median home in Warren City is $122,600. It is approximately $8,080 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $94,900 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $25,500 (17%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Warren City's median monthly rent is $938. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $41 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is about $126 (15%) higher.
