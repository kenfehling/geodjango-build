Compared to its surrounding areas, Mesquite is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The median home in Mesquite is valued at $143,000. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $61,660 (30%) lower. It is approximately $74,500 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is approximately $45,100 (23%) lower.

Rents

Mesquite's median monthly rent is $1,104. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $33 (2%) lower. It is approximately $42 (3%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $52 (4%) higher.
