Compared to other places in this area, Mesquite is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Dallas, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Mesquite's median home price is $143,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $61,660 (30%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $74,500 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $45,100 (23%) lower.

Rents

In Mesquite the median rent is $1,104 per month. It is about $33 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $42 (3%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $52 (4%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
