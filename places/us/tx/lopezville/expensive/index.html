Compared to other places in this area, Lopezville is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lopezville is valued at $65,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $39,660 (37%) lower. It is roughly $152,300 (70%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville it is about $24,800 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lopezville is $1,013 a month. It is roughly $302 (42%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $49 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $277 (37%) higher compared to the city of Brownsville.
