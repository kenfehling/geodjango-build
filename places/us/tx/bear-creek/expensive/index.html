Home prices

The price of the median home in Bear Creek is $457,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $140,480 (23%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $239,800 (110%) higher, and is about $310,900 (212%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Bear Creek the median monthly rent is $2,167. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $199 (10%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $1,105 (104%) higher, and it is roughly $1,175 (118%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
