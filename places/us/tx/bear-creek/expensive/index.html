Home prices

The price of the median home in Bear Creek is $457,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $140,480 (23%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $239,800 (110%) higher, and is approximately $310,900 (212%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Bear Creek's median rent is $2,167 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $199 (10%) higher. It is roughly $1,105 (104%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $1,175 (118%) higher.
