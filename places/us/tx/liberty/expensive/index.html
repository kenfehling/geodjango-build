Home prices

The median home cost in Liberty is $93,600. This is roughly $8,360 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $123,900 (56%) lower, and it is roughly $78,200 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Liberty's median rent is $882 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $71 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $180 (16%) lower, and is roughly $159 (15%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
