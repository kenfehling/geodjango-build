Home prices

The median home in Danbury is valued at $138,600. This is roughly $63,400 (84%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $78,900 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is about $33,200 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Danbury is $945 per month. This is roughly $216 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $117 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $96 (9%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
