Home prices

Rents

In Uhland the median rent is $1,161 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $67 (5%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $99 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $169 (17%) higher.
