In comparison to other places in this area, Uhland is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Uhland costs $128,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $64,480 (33%) lower. It is approximately $88,900 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $17,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Uhland is $1,161. This is roughly $67 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $99 (9%) higher, and it is approximately $169 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
