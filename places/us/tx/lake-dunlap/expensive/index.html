Home prices

The median home in Lake Dunlap costs $163,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $11,880 (6%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $54,400 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $16,700 (11%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Lake Dunlap's median rent is $889 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $56 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $173 (16%) lower, and it is about $103 (10%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
