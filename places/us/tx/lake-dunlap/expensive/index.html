Home prices

The median home in Lake Dunlap costs $163,100. It is approximately $11,880 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $54,400 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $16,700 (11%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Lake Dunlap's median monthly rent is $889. This is roughly $56 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $173 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $103 (10%) lower.
