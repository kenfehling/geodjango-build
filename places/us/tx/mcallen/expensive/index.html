Compared to its nearby areas, McAllen is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Brownsville however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in McAllen costs $126,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $6,980 (5%) higher. It is approximately $91,500 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $36,000 (39%) higher compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

In McAllen the median rent is $789 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $75 (8%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $273 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is roughly $53 (7%) higher.
