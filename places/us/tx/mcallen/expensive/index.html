Compared to the surrounding areas, McAllen is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in McAllen is $126,000. This is approximately $6,980 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $91,500 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is about $36,000 (39%) higher.

Rents

McAllen's median rent is $789 a month. It is about $75 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $273 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $53 (7%) higher compared to the city of Brownsville.
