Compared to other places in this area, McAllen is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in McAllen is valued at $126,000. This is roughly $6,980 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $91,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $36,000 (39%) higher compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

In McAllen the median rent is $789 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $75 (8%) lower.It is approximately $273 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $53 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
