Compared to the surrounding areas, McAllen is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Brownsville however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in McAllen is $126,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $6,980 (5%) higher.It is about $91,500 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is about $36,000 (39%) higher.

Rents

McAllen's median monthly rent is $789. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $75 (8%) lower.It is about $273 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $53 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
