Home prices

The median home in New Braunfels costs $219,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $55,440 (33%) higher. It is is very similar in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $72,800 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Braunfels is $1,183. This is about $296 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $121 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $191 (19%) higher.
