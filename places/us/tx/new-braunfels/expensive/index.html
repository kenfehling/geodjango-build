Home prices

The price of the median home in New Braunfels is $219,200. It is roughly $55,440 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $72,800 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Braunfels is $1,183. This is approximately $296 (33%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $121 (11%) higher, and it is about $191 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
