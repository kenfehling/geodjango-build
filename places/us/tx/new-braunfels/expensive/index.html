Home prices

New Braunfels's median home cost is $219,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $55,440 (33%) higher. It is is very similar in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $72,800 (49%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

New Braunfels's median monthly rent is $1,183. This is about $296 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $121 (11%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $191 (19%) higher.
