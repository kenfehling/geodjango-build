Home prices

The price of the median home in Odessa is $151,500. It is about $40,060 (35%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $66,000 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Midland is approximately $68,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Odessa is $1,091. It is roughly $193 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $29 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Midland it is approximately $171 (13%) lower.
