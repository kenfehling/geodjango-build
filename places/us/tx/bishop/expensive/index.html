Rents

Bishop's median monthly rent is $905. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $68 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $157 (14%) lower, and is approximately $123 (11%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.
