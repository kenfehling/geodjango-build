Home prices

Rents

In Bishop the median monthly rent is $905. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $68 (8%) higher. It is approximately $157 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi is about $123 (11%) lower.
