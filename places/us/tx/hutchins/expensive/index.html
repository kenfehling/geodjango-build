Home prices

The value of the median home in Hutchins is $68,800. This is about $32,240 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $148,700 (68%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas is approximately $119,300 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Hutchins the median rent is $1,017 a month. This is about $17 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $45 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $35 (3%) lower.
