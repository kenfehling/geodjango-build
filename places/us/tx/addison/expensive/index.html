Home prices

The median home price in Addison is $335,300. This is roughly $26,720 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $117,800 (54%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $147,200 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Addison's median monthly rent is $1,354. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $292 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $302 (28%) higher.
