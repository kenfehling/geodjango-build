Home prices

Ozona's median home cost is $105,600. This is roughly $10,060 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $111,900 (51%) lower, and is roughly $32,400 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Angelo.

Rents

Ozona's median rent is $554 per month. It is approximately $189 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $508 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of San Angelo is approximately $330 (37%) lower.
