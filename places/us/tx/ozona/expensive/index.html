Home prices

Rents

Ozona's median rent is $554 a month. This is about $189 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $508 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Angelo it is roughly $330 (37%) lower.
