Home prices

Sugar Land's median home cost is $323,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $154,680 (91%) higher. It is about $105,800 (48%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Houston it is roughly $151,500 (88%) higher.

Rents

In Sugar Land the median rent is $1,775 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $371 (26%) higher. It is approximately $713 (67%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $734 (70%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
