Home prices

The median home price in Edinburg is $121,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $46,760 (62%) higher. It is about $95,600 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $31,900 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

In Edinburg the median monthly rent is $794. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $64 (8%) higher. It is about $268 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville it is approximately $58 (7%) higher.
