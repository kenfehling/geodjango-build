Home prices

The median home value in Wake Village is $134,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $7,640 (6%) higher. It is roughly $83,500 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $14,100 (9%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Wake Village's median rent is $745 a month. This is roughly $19 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $317 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $67 (8%) lower.
