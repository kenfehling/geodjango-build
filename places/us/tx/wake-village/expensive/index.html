Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wake Village is $134,000. This is approximately $7,640 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $83,500 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $14,100 (9%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wake Village is $745. It is approximately $19 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $317 (29%) lower, and it is about $67 (8%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
