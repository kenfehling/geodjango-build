Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Springtown is $1,109 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $213 (23%) higher.It is about $47 (4%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $57 (5%) higher.
