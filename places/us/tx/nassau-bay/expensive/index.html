Home prices

The price of the median home in Nassau Bay is $243,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $3,300 (1%) lower. It is roughly $26,200 (12%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $71,900 (41%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nassau Bay is $1,154. It is about $194 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $92 (8%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $113 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
