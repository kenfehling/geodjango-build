Home prices

The cost of the median home in Nassau Bay is $243,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $3,300 (1%) lower. It is about $26,200 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $71,900 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Nassau Bay is $1,154 a month. It is about $194 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $92 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is approximately $113 (10%) higher.
