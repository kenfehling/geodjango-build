Home prices

The median home in Nassau Bay costs $243,700. This is roughly $3,300 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $26,200 (12%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $71,900 (41%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

Nassau Bay's median monthly rent is $1,154. This is approximately $194 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $92 (8%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $113 (10%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
