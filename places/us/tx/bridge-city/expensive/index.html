Compared to its nearby areas, Bridge City is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Bridge City is $134,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $24,640 (22%) higher. It is about $82,600 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $36,900 (21%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

Bridge City's median monthly rent is $1,040. It is roughly $142 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $22 (2%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston is is very similar.
