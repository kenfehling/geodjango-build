In comparison to other places in this area, Bridge City is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Houston however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Bridge City is $134,900. This is about $24,640 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $82,600 (37%) lower, and is approximately $36,900 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Bridge City the median monthly rent is $1,040. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $142 (15%) higher.It is roughly $22 (2%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Houston.
