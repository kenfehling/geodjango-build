Home prices

The median home in Bridge City costs $134,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $24,640 (22%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $82,600 (37%) lower, and is approximately $36,900 (21%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

Bridge City's median rent is $1,040 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $142 (15%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $22 (2%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Houston.
