Home prices

The median home in Texarkana costs $130,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $3,560 (2%) higher. It is roughly $86,900 (39%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $17,500 (11%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Texarkana's median rent is $781 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $63 (8%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $281 (26%) lower, and is approximately $31 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
