Home prices

The median home in Texarkana is valued at $130,600. This is about $3,560 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $86,900 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is about $17,500 (11%) lower.

Rents

Texarkana's median rent is $781 per month. This is approximately $63 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $281 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $31 (3%) lower.
