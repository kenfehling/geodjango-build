Home prices

The median home in Texarkana is valued at $130,600. This is about $3,560 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $86,900 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $17,500 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Texarkana is $781. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $63 (8%) higher.It is approximately $281 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $31 (3%) lower.
