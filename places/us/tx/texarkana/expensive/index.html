Home prices

The cost of the median home in Texarkana is $130,600. It is roughly $3,560 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $86,900 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $17,500 (11%) lower.

Rents

Texarkana's median rent is $781 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $63 (8%) higher. It is roughly $281 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $31 (3%) lower.
