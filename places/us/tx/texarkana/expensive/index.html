Home prices

The median home in Texarkana costs $130,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $3,560 (2%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $86,900 (39%) lower, and is about $17,500 (11%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in Texarkana is $781 per month. This is roughly $63 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $281 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $31 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
