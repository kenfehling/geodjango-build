Home prices

Haltom City's median home price is $111,300. This is approximately $40,380 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $106,200 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $76,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Haltom City the median rent is $1,006 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $139 (12%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $56 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $46 (4%) lower.
