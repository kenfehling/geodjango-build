Home prices

The price of the median home in Haltom City is $111,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $40,380 (26%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $106,200 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $76,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median rent in Haltom City is $1,006 a month. It is roughly $139 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $56 (5%) lower, and it is roughly $46 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
