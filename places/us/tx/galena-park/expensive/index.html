Home prices

The cost of the median home in Galena Park is $80,700. This is approximately $37,000 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $136,800 (62%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $91,100 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Galena Park's median rent is $968 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $24 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $94 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is about $73 (7%) lower.
