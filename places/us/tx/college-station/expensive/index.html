Compared to its surrounding areas, College Station is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home cost in College Station is $241,600. This is roughly $109,580 (83%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $24,100 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $69,800 (40%) higher.

Rents

In College Station the median rent is $983 a month. This is about $11 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $79 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $58 (5%) lower.
