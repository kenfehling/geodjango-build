Home prices

The median home value in College Station is $241,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $109,580 (83%) higher.It is approximately $24,100 (11%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $69,800 (40%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

College Station's median monthly rent is $983. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $11 (1%) higher.It is about $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $58 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
