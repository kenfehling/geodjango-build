Home prices

Dayton's median home price is $168,400. It is about $68,500 (68%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $49,100 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $3,400 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $839. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $78 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $223 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $202 (19%) lower.
