Home prices

Dayton's median home cost is $168,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $68,500 (68%) higher.It is roughly $49,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $3,400 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $839 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (8%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $223 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $202 (19%) lower.
