Port Neches's median home price is $165,900. It is approximately $30,940 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $51,600 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $5,900 (3%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in Port Neches is $996 a month. It is about $73 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $66 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is about $45 (4%) lower.
