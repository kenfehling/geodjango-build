Compared to the surrounding areas, La Porte is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in La Porte is $148,100. This is approximately $60,480 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $69,400 (31%) lower, and it is about $23,700 (13%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in La Porte is $1,075 a month. This is approximately $149 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $13 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $34 (3%) higher.
