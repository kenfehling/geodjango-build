Home prices

The value of the median home in Memphis is $51,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $780 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $165,800 (76%) lower, and it is roughly $80,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Amarillo.

Rents

In Memphis the median monthly rent is $517. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $102 (16%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $545 (51%) lower, and is about $333 (39%) lower compared to the city of Amarillo.
