In comparison to its nearby areas, Kingsland is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kingsland is $121,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $110,340 (47%) lower.It is approximately $95,800 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $24,700 (16%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Kingsland's median rent is $869 per month. This is approximately $70 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $193 (18%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $123 (12%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
