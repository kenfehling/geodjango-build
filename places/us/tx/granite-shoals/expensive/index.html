Home prices

The median home in Granite Shoals costs $132,300. It is approximately $130,760 (49%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $85,200 (39%) lower, and is approximately $14,100 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents
