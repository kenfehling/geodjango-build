Home prices

The median home price in Granite Shoals is $132,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $130,760 (49%) lower.It is about $85,200 (39%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $14,100 (9%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Granite Shoals's median rent is $799 a month. This is approximately $227 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $263 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $193 (19%) lower.
