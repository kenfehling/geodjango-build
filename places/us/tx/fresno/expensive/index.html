In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fresno is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Fresno costs $173,600. It is roughly $20,120 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $43,900 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $1,800 (1%) higher.

Rents

Fresno's median rent is $1,468 per month. This is approximately $187 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $406 (38%) higher, and it is roughly $427 (41%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
