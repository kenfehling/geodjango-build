Compared to its nearby areas, Fresno is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Fresno is valued at $173,600. This is approximately $20,120 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $43,900 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is about $1,800 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Fresno the median monthly rent is $1,468. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $187 (14%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $406 (38%) higher, and it is about $427 (41%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
