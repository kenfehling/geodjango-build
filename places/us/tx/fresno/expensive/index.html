Compared to other places in this area, Fresno is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Houston however, it's close to average.

Home prices

Fresno's median home price is $173,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $20,120 (10%) lower. It is approximately $43,900 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Houston is about $1,800 (1%) higher.

Rents

Fresno's median monthly rent is $1,468. This is about $187 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $406 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $427 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
