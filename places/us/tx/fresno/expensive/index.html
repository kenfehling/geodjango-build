Home prices

Fresno's median home value is $173,600. This is approximately $20,120 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $43,900 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $1,800 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fresno is $1,468. It is approximately $187 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $406 (38%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $427 (41%) higher.
