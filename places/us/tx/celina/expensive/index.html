The median home in Celina costs $318,300. It is about $77,300 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $100,800 (46%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $130,200 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Celina is $1,628. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $117 (7%) higher. It is roughly $566 (53%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $576 (54%) higher.
