Home prices

The median home in Canutillo is valued at $79,800. It is roughly $31,840 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $137,700 (63%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $47,600 (37%) lower compared to the city of El Paso.

Rents

In Canutillo the median rent is $805 a month. This is roughly $58 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $257 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $32 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.
