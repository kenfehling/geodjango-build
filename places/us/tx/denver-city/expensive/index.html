In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denver City is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Lubbock, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Denver City's median home cost is $118,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $22,760 (23%) higher. It is about $99,300 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $22,600 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Lubbock.

Rents

Denver City's median monthly rent is $829. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $133 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $233 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Lubbock is roughly $126 (13%) lower.
