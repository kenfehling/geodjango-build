Home prices

Rents

In Denver City the median rent is $829 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $133 (19%) higher.It is about $233 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lubbock is approximately $126 (13%) lower.
