Compared to the surrounding areas, Denver City is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Lubbock, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Denver City is $118,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $22,760 (23%) higher.It is roughly $99,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $22,600 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Lubbock.

Rents

The median rent in Denver City is $829 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $133 (19%) higher.It is approximately $233 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Lubbock is about $126 (13%) lower.
