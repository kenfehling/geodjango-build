Home prices

The median home in Denver City is valued at $118,200. It is roughly $22,760 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $99,300 (45%) lower, and it is about $22,600 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Lubbock.

Rents

The median rent in Denver City is $829 per month. This is about $133 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $233 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Lubbock is about $126 (13%) lower.
