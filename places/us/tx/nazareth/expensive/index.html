Home prices

The median home in Nazareth is valued at $172,900. It is approximately $104,900 (154%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $44,600 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lubbock is approximately $32,100 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Nazareth the median monthly rent is $560. It is about $133 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $502 (47%) lower, and it is about $395 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Lubbock.
