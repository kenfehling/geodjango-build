Home prices

Rents

Nazareth's median monthly rent is $560. Compared to nearby areas it is about $133 (19%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $502 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lubbock is roughly $395 (41%) lower.
