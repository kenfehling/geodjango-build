Home prices

The median home in Nazareth costs $172,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $104,900 (154%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $44,600 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Lubbock it is roughly $32,100 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Nazareth the median rent is $560 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $133 (19%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $502 (47%) lower, and it is about $395 (41%) lower compared to the city of Lubbock.
