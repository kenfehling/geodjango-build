Home prices

The value of the median home in Rancho Viejo is $212,000. It is roughly $135,880 (178%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $5,500 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is roughly $122,000 (135%) higher.

Rents

Rancho Viejo's median monthly rent is $885. Compared to other places in this area this is about $117 (15%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $177 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $149 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
