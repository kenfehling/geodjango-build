In comparison to other places in this area, Bastrop is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bastrop is valued at $196,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $40,260 (25%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $21,100 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $50,000 (34%) higher.

Rents

Bastrop's median monthly rent is $970. This is approximately $30 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $92 (8%) lower, and it is about $22 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
