In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bastrop is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home cost in Bastrop is $196,400. This is approximately $40,260 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $21,100 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $50,000 (34%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bastrop is $970. This is about $30 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $92 (8%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $22 (2%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
