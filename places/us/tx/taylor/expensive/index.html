In comparison to nearby areas, Taylor is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Austin.

Home prices

The median home in Taylor costs $158,400. It is about $20,820 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $59,100 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Austin is about $179,000 (53%) lower.

Rents

Taylor's median rent is $931 a month. This is roughly $114 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $131 (12%) lower, and it is roughly $349 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.
