Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Taylor is $931 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $114 (10%) lower. It is about $131 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $349 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.
