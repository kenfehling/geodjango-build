Home prices

The value of the median home in Waxahachie is $176,300. It is approximately $10,620 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $41,200 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $11,800 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Waxahachie the median monthly rent is $1,088. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $92 (7%) lower.It is roughly $26 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $36 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
