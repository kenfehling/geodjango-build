Compared to its surrounding areas, Waxahachie is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waxahachie costs $176,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $10,620 (5%) lower. It is roughly $41,200 (18%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $11,800 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waxahachie is $1,088. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $92 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $26 (2%) higher, and it is about $36 (3%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
