Home prices

The median home in Waxahachie is valued at $176,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $10,620 (5%) lower.It is about $41,200 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $11,800 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Waxahachie's median rent is $1,088 per month. This is approximately $92 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $26 (2%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $36 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
