Home prices

Rents

Sudan's median monthly rent is $654. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $408 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lubbock is roughly $301 (31%) lower.
