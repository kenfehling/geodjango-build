Home prices

The median home in Gainesville costs $107,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $50,660 (31%) lower. It is approximately $109,600 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $80,200 (42%) lower.

Rents

Gainesville's median rent is $889 a month. It is roughly $144 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $173 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $163 (15%) lower.
