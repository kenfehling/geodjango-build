Compared to its surrounding areas, Trenton is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Trenton is $79,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $37,980 (32%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $137,700 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $108,300 (57%) lower.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $706 per month. This is roughly $201 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $356 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $346 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
