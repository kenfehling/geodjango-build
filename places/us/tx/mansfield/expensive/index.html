Compared to its surrounding areas, Mansfield is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Dallas, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mansfield is $257,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $32,240 (14%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $39,600 (18%) higher, and is roughly $69,000 (36%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Mansfield the median rent is $1,428 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $305 (27%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $366 (34%) higher, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $376 (35%) higher.
