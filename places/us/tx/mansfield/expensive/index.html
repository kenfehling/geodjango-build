Home prices

The median home in Mansfield is valued at $257,100. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $32,240 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $39,600 (18%) higher, and it is roughly $69,000 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Mansfield the median rent is $1,428 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $305 (27%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $366 (34%) higher, and it is approximately $376 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
