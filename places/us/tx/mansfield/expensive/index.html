Compared to its nearby areas, Mansfield is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Dallas however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mansfield is $257,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $32,240 (14%) higher. It is approximately $39,600 (18%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $69,000 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Mansfield's median monthly rent is $1,428. This is about $305 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $366 (34%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $376 (35%) higher.
