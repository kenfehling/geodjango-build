Compared to the surrounding areas, Mansfield is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Mansfield is $257,100. It is roughly $32,240 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $39,600 (18%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $69,000 (36%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mansfield is $1,428. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $305 (27%) higher.It is approximately $366 (34%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $376 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
