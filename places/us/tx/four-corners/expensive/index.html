Home prices

The median home in Four Corners is valued at $176,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $40,380 (18%) lower.It is approximately $41,000 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $4,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

Four Corners's median rent is $1,679 per month. This is approximately $182 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $617 (58%) higher, and it is approximately $638 (61%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
