In comparison to its surrounding areas, Four Corners is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Houston however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Four Corners is $176,500. This is about $40,380 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $41,000 (18%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $4,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Four Corners the median rent is $1,679 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $182 (12%) higher.It is approximately $617 (58%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $638 (61%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
