Compared to the surrounding areas, Four Corners is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Four Corners costs $176,500. It is roughly $40,380 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $41,000 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $4,700 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Four Corners the median rent is $1,679 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $182 (12%) higher. It is about $617 (58%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is about $638 (61%) higher.
