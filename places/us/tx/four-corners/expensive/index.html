Four Corners's median home price is $176,500. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $40,380 (18%) lower. It is approximately $41,000 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $4,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Four Corners is $1,679. It is about $182 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $617 (58%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston it is roughly $638 (61%) higher.
