Compared to the surrounding areas, Four Corners is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Houston, it's close to average.

Home prices

Four Corners's median home value is $176,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $40,380 (18%) lower.It is approximately $41,000 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $4,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Four Corners is $1,679. It is about $182 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $617 (58%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Houston it is roughly $638 (61%) higher.
