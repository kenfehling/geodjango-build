Compared to the surrounding areas, Athens is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

Athens's median home value is $87,200. It is about $7,520 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $130,300 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $100,900 (53%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Athens's median rent is $854 per month. This is roughly $80 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $208 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $198 (18%) lower.
