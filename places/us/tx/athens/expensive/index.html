Home prices

Athens's median home price is $87,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $7,520 (9%) higher.It is about $130,300 (59%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $100,900 (53%) lower.

Rents

Athens's median monthly rent is $854. It is approximately $80 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $208 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $198 (18%) lower.
