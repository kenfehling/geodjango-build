Home prices

The median home in El Paso costs $127,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $19,850 (18%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $90,100 (41%) lower.

Rents

In El Paso the median rent is $837 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $70 (9%) higher. It is roughly $225 (21%) lower compared to the entire country.
