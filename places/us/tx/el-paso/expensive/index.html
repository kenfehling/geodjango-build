In comparison to other places in this area, El Paso is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The median home value in El Paso is $127,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $19,850 (18%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $90,100 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in El Paso is $837 per month. It is approximately $70 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $225 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation.
