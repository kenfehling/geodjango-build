Home prices

The price of the median home in Weslaco is $83,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $13,480 (19%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $133,600 (61%) lower, and is about $6,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Weslaco is $709. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $141 (24%) higher.It is roughly $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $27 (3%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.
