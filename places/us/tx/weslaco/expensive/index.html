Home prices

The median home in Weslaco is valued at $83,900. It is about $13,480 (19%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $133,600 (61%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $6,100 (6%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

Weslaco's median monthly rent is $709. This is approximately $141 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is roughly $27 (3%) lower.
