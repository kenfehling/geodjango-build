Rents

In Clear Lake Shores the median monthly rent is $1,482. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $193 (15%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $420 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $441 (42%) higher.
