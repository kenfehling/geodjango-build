Home prices

The median home cost in Cameron is $80,000. It is about $6,800 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Austin it is about $257,400 (76%) lower.

Rents

Cameron's median rent is $641 a month. It is roughly $33 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $421 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Austin is approximately $639 (49%) lower.
