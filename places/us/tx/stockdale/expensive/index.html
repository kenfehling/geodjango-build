Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Stockdale is $862 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $20 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $200 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $130 (13%) lower.
