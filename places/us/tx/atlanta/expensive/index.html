Compared to other places in this area, Atlanta is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Atlanta is $87,700. This is approximately $5,160 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $129,800 (59%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $60,400 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Atlanta's median monthly rent is $724. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $78 (12%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $338 (31%) lower, and is about $88 (10%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
