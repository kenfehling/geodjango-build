In comparison to other places in this area, Atlanta is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Atlanta is $87,700. Compared to nearby areas this is about $5,160 (5%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $129,800 (59%) lower, and is roughly $60,400 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atlanta is $724. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $78 (12%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $338 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $88 (10%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
