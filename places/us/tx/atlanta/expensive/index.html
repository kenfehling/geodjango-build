In comparison to the surrounding areas, Atlanta is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Atlanta is $87,700. This is about $5,160 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $129,800 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $60,400 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Atlanta is $724 per month. This is roughly $78 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $338 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is about $88 (10%) lower.
