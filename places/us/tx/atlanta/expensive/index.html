Compared to its nearby areas, Atlanta is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Shreveport, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Atlanta is $87,700. This is roughly $5,160 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $129,800 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $60,400 (40%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Atlanta the median monthly rent is $724. This is roughly $78 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $338 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $88 (10%) lower.
