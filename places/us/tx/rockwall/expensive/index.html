Compared to other places in this area, Rockwall is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rockwall is valued at $261,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $63,740 (19%) lower. It is roughly $43,900 (20%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $73,300 (38%) higher.

Rents

Rockwall's median rent is $1,295 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $352 (21%) lower. It is roughly $233 (21%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Dallas is approximately $243 (23%) higher.
