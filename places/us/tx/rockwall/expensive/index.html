Home prices

The median home value in Rockwall is $261,400. This is about $63,740 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $43,900 (20%) higher, and is roughly $73,300 (38%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median rent in Rockwall is $1,295 per month. It is roughly $352 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $233 (21%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $243 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
