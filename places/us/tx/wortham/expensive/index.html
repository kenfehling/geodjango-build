Home prices

The median home in Wortham is valued at $83,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $16,700 (25%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $134,200 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $104,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

Wortham's median monthly rent is $726. It is about $21 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $336 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $326 (30%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
