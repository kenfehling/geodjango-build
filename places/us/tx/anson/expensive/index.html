Home prices

The median home in Anson is valued at $68,500. It is approximately $11,960 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $149,000 (68%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $51,900 (43%) lower compared to the city of Abilene.

Rents

In Anson the median monthly rent is $723. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $99 (15%) higher. It is roughly $339 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Abilene is about $201 (21%) lower.
