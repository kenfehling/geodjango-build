Home prices

The median home price in Seguin is $134,100. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $52,120 (27%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $83,400 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $12,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Seguin the median monthly rent is $902. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $32 (3%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $160 (15%) lower, and is roughly $90 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
