Home prices

The median home in Longview is valued at $139,600. This is roughly $2,320 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $77,900 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $8,500 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Longview is $840 a month. This is about $157 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $222 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $28 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
