In comparison to its surrounding areas, Shenandoah is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

Shenandoah's median home cost is $261,000. This is approximately $44,720 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $43,500 (19%) higher, and it is about $89,200 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Shenandoah's median rent is $1,071 a month. This is roughly $564 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $30 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
