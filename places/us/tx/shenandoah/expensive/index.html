Home prices

The median home in Shenandoah costs $261,000. It is approximately $44,720 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $43,500 (19%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $89,200 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Shenandoah is $1,071 per month. This is roughly $564 (34%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is is very similar in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $30 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
