Home prices

The median home value in Shenandoah is $261,000. This is roughly $44,720 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $43,500 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $89,200 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Shenandoah the median rent is $1,071 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $564 (34%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $30 (2%) higher.
