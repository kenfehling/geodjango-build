In comparison to the surrounding areas, Austin is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Austin is $337,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $488,000 (59%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $119,900 (55%) higher, and is about $191,000 (130%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Austin's median rent is $1,280 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $1,005 (43%) lower.It is approximately $218 (20%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $288 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
