In comparison to other places in this area, Austin is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's substantially more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home in Austin is valued at $337,400. This is roughly $382,240 (53%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $119,900 (55%) higher, and it is roughly $191,000 (130%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Austin the median rent is $1,280 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $606 (32%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $218 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is approximately $288 (29%) higher.
