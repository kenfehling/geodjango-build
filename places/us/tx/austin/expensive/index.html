Home prices

The median home in Austin costs $337,400. It is approximately $382,240 (53%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $119,900 (55%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $191,000 (130%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Austin is $1,280 a month. It is roughly $606 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $218 (20%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $288 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
