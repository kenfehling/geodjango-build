Compared to the surrounding areas, Austin is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Austin is $337,400. It is roughly $488,000 (59%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $119,900 (55%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $191,000 (130%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Austin's median monthly rent is $1,280. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $1,005 (43%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $218 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is approximately $288 (29%) higher.
