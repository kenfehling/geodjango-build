Markham's median home cost is $77,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $33,040 (29%) lower. It is roughly $139,700 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $94,000 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Markham's median monthly rent is $753. This is roughly $47 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $309 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $288 (27%) lower.
