Compared to the surrounding areas, Winnsboro is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Winnsboro's median home cost is $85,500. It is roughly $18,820 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $132,000 (60%) lower, and is about $102,600 (54%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median rent in Winnsboro is $811 a month. This is about $76 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $251 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $241 (22%) lower.
