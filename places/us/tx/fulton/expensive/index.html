Home prices

The median home cost in Fulton is $127,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $13,460 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $89,600 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $13,200 (9%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.

Rents

In Fulton the median rent is $1,006 per month. This is approximately $122 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $56 (5%) lower, and it is approximately $22 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi.
