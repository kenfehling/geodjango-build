The cost of the median home in Fort Stockton is $96,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $31,040 (47%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $121,400 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Odessa it is approximately $55,400 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Stockton the median rent is $829 per month. It is approximately $55 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $233 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Odessa is approximately $262 (24%) lower.
