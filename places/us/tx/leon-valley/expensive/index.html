Home prices

The median home in Leon Valley is valued at $165,900. This is approximately $264,640 (61%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $51,600 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $19,500 (13%) higher.

Rents

Leon Valley's median rent is $914 per month. This is approximately $458 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $148 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $78 (7%) lower.
