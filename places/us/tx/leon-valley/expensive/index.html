In comparison to its surrounding areas, Leon Valley is significantly less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Leon Valley is $165,900. This is about $264,640 (61%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $51,600 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $19,500 (13%) higher.

Rents

In Leon Valley the median rent is $914 a month. It is approximately $458 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $148 (13%) lower, and is about $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
