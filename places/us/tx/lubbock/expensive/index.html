Compared to its nearby areas, Lubbock is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The median home in Lubbock costs $140,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $12,267 (8%) lower. It is roughly $76,700 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation.

Rents

In Lubbock the median monthly rent is $955. This is approximately $140 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $107 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole.
