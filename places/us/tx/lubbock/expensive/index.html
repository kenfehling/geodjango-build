Home prices

The median home cost in Lubbock is $140,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $12,267 (8%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $76,700 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lubbock is $955 a month. This is roughly $140 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $107 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole.
