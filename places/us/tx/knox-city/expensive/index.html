Home prices

The median home value in Knox City is $38,100. It is about $4,640 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $179,400 (82%) lower, and is about $82,300 (68%) lower compared to the city of Abilene.

Rents

Knox City's median monthly rent is $498. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $62 (11%) lower. It is approximately $564 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Abilene it is approximately $426 (46%) lower.
