Compared to its surrounding areas, South Houston is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home price in South Houston is $94,500. It is approximately $34,840 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $123,000 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is approximately $77,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

In South Houston the median rent is $862 a month. This is about $62 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $200 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $179 (17%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
