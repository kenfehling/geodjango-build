Compared to the surrounding areas, Miami is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Amarillo.

Home prices

The median home in Miami is valued at $93,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $21,200 (29%) higher.It is about $124,400 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $39,400 (29%) lower compared to the city of Amarillo.

Rents

Miami's median monthly rent is $880. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $119 (15%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $182 (17%) lower, and is approximately $30 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Amarillo.
