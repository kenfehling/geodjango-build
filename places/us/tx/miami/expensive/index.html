Compared to nearby areas, Miami is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Amarillo, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Miami's median home price is $93,100. It is about $21,200 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $124,400 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Amarillo it is roughly $39,400 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Miami is $880 a month. It is roughly $119 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $182 (17%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $30 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Amarillo.
