Compared to the surrounding areas, Miami is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Amarillo.

Home prices

The median home in Miami is valued at $93,100. This is about $21,200 (29%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $124,400 (57%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Amarillo is approximately $39,400 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Miami is $880 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $119 (15%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $182 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Amarillo it is approximately $30 (3%) higher.
