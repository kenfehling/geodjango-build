Home prices

The value of the median home in West Orange is $86,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $33,680 (27%) lower. It is about $130,700 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Beaumont is approximately $26,800 (23%) lower.

Rents

West Orange's median monthly rent is $843. It is approximately $65 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $219 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Beaumont it is roughly $25 (2%) lower.
