Home prices

The cost of the median home in West Orange is $86,800. It is about $33,680 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $130,700 (60%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $26,800 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Beaumont.

Rents

West Orange's median rent is $843 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $65 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $219 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Beaumont it is approximately $25 (2%) lower.
