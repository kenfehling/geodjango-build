Home prices

The median home in Hale Center is valued at $69,200. This is about $1,580 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $148,300 (68%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $71,600 (50%) lower compared to the city of Lubbock.

Rents

In Hale Center the median monthly rent is $638. This is about $24 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $424 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $317 (33%) lower compared to the city of Lubbock.
