Compared to its surrounding areas, Beaumont is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Houston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Beaumont's median home cost is $113,600. It is about $34,880 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Houston it is roughly $58,200 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Beaumont the median monthly rent is $868. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $245 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $194 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $173 (16%) lower.
