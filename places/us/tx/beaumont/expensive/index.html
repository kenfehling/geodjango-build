Home prices

The median home cost in Beaumont is $113,600. This is roughly $34,880 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $103,900 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is roughly $58,200 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beaumont is $868. It is approximately $245 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $194 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $173 (16%) lower.
