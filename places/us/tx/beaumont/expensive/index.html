Home prices

The median home in Beaumont costs $113,600. This is about $34,880 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $103,900 (47%) lower, and it is about $58,200 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Beaumont the median rent is $868 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $245 (22%) lower.It is approximately $194 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $173 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
