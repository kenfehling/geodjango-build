Home prices

The median home in Beaumont is valued at $113,600. This is roughly $34,880 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $58,200 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beaumont is $868. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $245 (22%) lower.It is approximately $194 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $173 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
