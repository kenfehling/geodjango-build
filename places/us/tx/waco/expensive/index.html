Home prices

The median home in Waco is valued at $125,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $2,860 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $92,200 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $62,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Waco is $862 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $124 (12%) lower. It is about $200 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $190 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
