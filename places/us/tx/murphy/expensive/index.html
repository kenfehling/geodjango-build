Home prices

Rents

In Murphy the median monthly rent is $2,119. It is roughly $500 (30%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $1,057 (99%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $1,067 (101%) higher.
