Compared to other places in this area, Cleveland is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $91,100. This is about $40,320 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $126,400 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $80,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

Cleveland's median monthly rent is $691. This is about $235 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $371 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston it is about $350 (33%) lower.
