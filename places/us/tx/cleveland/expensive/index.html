Home prices

The median home in Cleveland costs $91,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $40,320 (30%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $126,400 (58%) lower, and it is about $80,700 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $691 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $235 (25%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $371 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is approximately $350 (33%) lower.
