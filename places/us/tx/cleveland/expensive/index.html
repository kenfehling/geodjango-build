Compared to other places in this area, Cleveland is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home value in Cleveland is $91,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $40,320 (30%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $126,400 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $80,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $691 per month. It is roughly $235 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $371 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is roughly $350 (33%) lower.
