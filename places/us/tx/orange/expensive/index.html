Home prices

The median home price in Orange is $100,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $1,320 (1%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $116,600 (53%) lower, and it is approximately $12,700 (11%) lower compared to the city of Beaumont.

Rents

The median rent in Orange is $822 a month. This is about $20 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Beaumont is roughly $46 (5%) lower.
