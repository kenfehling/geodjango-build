Home prices

The cost of the median home in Orange is $100,900. It is about $1,320 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $116,600 (53%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $12,700 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Beaumont.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Orange is $822. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $20 (2%) lower. It is about $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Beaumont is about $46 (5%) lower.
