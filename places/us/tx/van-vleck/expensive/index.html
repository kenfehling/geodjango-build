Home prices

The median home price in Van Vleck is $99,900. This is approximately $7,320 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $117,600 (54%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $71,900 (41%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

Van Vleck's median monthly rent is $855. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $77 (8%) lower. It is about $207 (19%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $186 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
