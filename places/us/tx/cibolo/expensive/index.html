Compared to other places in this area, Cibolo is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cibolo is $230,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $41,180 (21%) higher. It is about $13,300 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $84,400 (57%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Cibolo the median monthly rent is $1,723. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $629 (57%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $661 (62%) higher, and is roughly $731 (73%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
