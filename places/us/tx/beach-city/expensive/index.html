Home prices

The median home in Beach City is valued at $319,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $118,000 (58%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $101,700 (46%) higher, and is about $147,400 (85%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beach City is $1,010. This is about $18 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $52 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $31 (2%) lower.
