Home prices

The value of the median home in Andrews is $147,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $25,080 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $70,100 (32%) lower, and it is about $72,100 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Midland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Andrews is $1,042. It is roughly $92 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $20 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Midland it is roughly $220 (17%) lower.
