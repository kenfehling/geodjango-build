Home prices

The median home in Tioga is valued at $141,100. It is roughly $45,060 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $76,400 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $47,000 (24%) lower.

Rents

Tioga's median rent is $1,011 a month. It is roughly $34 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $51 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $41 (3%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
