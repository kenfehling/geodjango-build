Home prices

Rents

Aransas Pass's median rent is $883 per month. This is approximately $225 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $179 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $145 (14%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.
