Home prices

The median home in Aransas Pass costs $112,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $73,980 (39%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $104,600 (48%) lower, and it is about $28,200 (19%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.

Rents

Aransas Pass's median rent is $883 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $225 (20%) lower. It is about $179 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi is roughly $145 (14%) lower.
