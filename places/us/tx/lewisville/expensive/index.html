Home prices

The median home in Lewisville is valued at $209,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $102,800 (32%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $8,500 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $20,900 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lewisville is $1,210. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $345 (22%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $148 (13%) higher, and is about $158 (15%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
