The median home cost in Lewisville is $209,000. It is approximately $102,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $8,500 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $20,900 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lewisville is $1,210 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $345 (22%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $148 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $158 (15%) higher.
