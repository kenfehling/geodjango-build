Compared to the surrounding areas, Lewisville is significantly less expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lewisville is $209,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $102,800 (32%) lower.It is approximately $8,500 (3%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $20,900 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Lewisville the median rent is $1,210 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $345 (22%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $148 (13%) higher, and is roughly $158 (15%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
