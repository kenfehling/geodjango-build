Home prices

The median home in Laguna Heights is valued at $38,000. It is about $113,740 (74%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $179,500 (82%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Brownsville is roughly $52,000 (57%) lower.

Rents

Laguna Heights's median rent is $540 per month. This is about $309 (36%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $522 (49%) lower, and is approximately $196 (26%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.
