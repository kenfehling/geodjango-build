Home prices

The median home price in Whitesboro is $105,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24,900 (19%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $111,600 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $82,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

Whitesboro's median rent is $919 per month. This is approximately $66 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $143 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $133 (12%) lower.
