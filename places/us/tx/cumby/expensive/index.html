Home prices

Rents

Cumby's median rent is $755 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $63 (7%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $307 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $297 (28%) lower.
