Home prices

The median home in Kilgore costs $122,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $4,500 (3%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $95,300 (43%) lower, and it is about $25,900 (17%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in Kilgore is $833 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $135 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is about $21 (2%) higher.
