Home prices

The price of the median home in Pflugerville is $238,700. It is roughly $8,860 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $21,200 (9%) higher, and it is approximately $92,300 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Pflugerville's median rent is $1,414 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $352 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $422 (42%) higher.
