Much like other places in this area, it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of San Antoniohowever, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pflugerville costs $238,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $8,860 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $21,200 (9%) higher, and is approximately $92,300 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pflugerville is $1,414. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $352 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $422 (42%) higher.
