Similar to the surrounding areas, it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pflugerville is valued at $238,700. This is about $8,860 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $21,200 (9%) higher, and is approximately $92,300 (63%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Pflugerville's median rent is $1,414 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $352 (33%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $422 (42%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
