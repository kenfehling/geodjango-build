Home prices

The median home in Westminster is valued at $193,800. It is about $16,020 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $23,700 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $5,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westminster is $967. This is about $366 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $95 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $85 (8%) lower.
