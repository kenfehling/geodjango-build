Compared to nearby areas, Texas City is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Houston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Texas City is $124,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $37,400 (23%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $93,300 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $47,600 (27%) lower.

Rents

Texas City's median rent is $970 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $128 (11%) lower. It is roughly $92 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $71 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
