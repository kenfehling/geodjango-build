Compared to the surrounding areas, Texas City is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Houston, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Texas City costs $124,200. It is about $37,400 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $93,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $47,600 (27%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Texas City is $970. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $128 (11%) lower.It is roughly $92 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $71 (6%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
