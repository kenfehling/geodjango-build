Home prices

The median home in Lancaster is valued at $136,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $3,580 (2%) higher. It is roughly $80,800 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $51,400 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Lancaster the median rent is $1,074 per month. This is roughly $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $12 (1%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $22 (2%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
