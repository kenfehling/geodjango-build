Home prices

The median home in Lancaster is valued at $136,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $3,580 (2%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $80,800 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $51,400 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lancaster is $1,074 a month. It is roughly $49 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $22 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
