Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $136,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $3,580 (2%) higher.It is about $80,800 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $51,400 (27%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Lancaster's median rent is $1,074 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $49 (4%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $12 (1%) higher, and is about $22 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
