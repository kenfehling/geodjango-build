Home prices

The median home in Pittsburg costs $82,900. This is about $12,900 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $134,600 (61%) lower, and is approximately $65,200 (44%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

The median rent in Pittsburg is $723 per month. This is roughly $94 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $339 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $89 (10%) lower.
