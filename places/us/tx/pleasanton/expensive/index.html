Home prices

The median home in Pleasanton costs $158,900. This is about $82,600 (108%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $58,600 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $12,500 (8%) higher.

Rents

Pleasanton's median rent is $1,049 per month. This is about $278 (35%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $13 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $57 (5%) higher.
