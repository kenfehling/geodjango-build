Home prices

The median home in Bulverde costs $308,300. This is about $47,580 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $90,800 (41%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $161,900 (110%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Bulverde is $1,104 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $633 (36%) lower. It is approximately $42 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is approximately $112 (11%) higher.
