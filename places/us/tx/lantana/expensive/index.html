Home prices

The median home in Lantana costs $396,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30,300 (7%) lower. It is roughly $179,100 (82%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $208,500 (110%) higher.

Rents

In Lantana the median rent is $2,779 a month. This is about $1,232 (79%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $1,717 (161%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $1,727 (164%) higher.
