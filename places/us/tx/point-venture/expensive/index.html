The cost of the median home in Point Venture is $285,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $138,300 (32%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $67,700 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $138,800 (94%) higher.

Rents
