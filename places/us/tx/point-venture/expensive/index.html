Home prices

Point Venture's median home value is $285,200. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $138,300 (32%) lower. It is about $67,700 (31%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $138,800 (94%) higher.

Rents

Point Venture's median rent is $1,621 a month. It is approximately $338 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $559 (52%) higher, and is about $629 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
