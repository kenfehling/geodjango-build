Home prices

The median home in Naples is valued at $79,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $21,340 (21%) lower.It is roughly $137,800 (63%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $68,400 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Naples is $544. This is roughly $95 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $518 (48%) lower, and is approximately $268 (33%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
