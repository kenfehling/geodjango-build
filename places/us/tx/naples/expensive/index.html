Compared to other places in this area, Naples is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Shreveport, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Naples costs $79,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $21,340 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $137,800 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is about $68,400 (46%) lower.

Rents

Naples's median rent is $544 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $95 (14%) lower. It is roughly $518 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $268 (33%) lower.
