Home prices

The median home in Greenville costs $104,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $18,380 (14%) lower. It is about $113,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $83,700 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greenville is $900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $24 (2%) lower. It is about $162 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $152 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
