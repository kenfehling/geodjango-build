Home prices

The median home in Greenville costs $104,400. This is approximately $18,380 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $113,100 (52%) lower, and is roughly $83,700 (44%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Greenville's median rent is $900 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $24 (2%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $162 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $152 (14%) lower.
