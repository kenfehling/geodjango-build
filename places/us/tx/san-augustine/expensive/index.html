In comparison to nearby areas, San Augustine is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Shreveport, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in San Augustine is $89,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,400 (19%) higher. It is roughly $128,400 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $59,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

San Augustine's median rent is $542 a month. This is about $17 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $520 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $270 (33%) lower.
