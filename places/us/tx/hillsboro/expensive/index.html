Home prices

The price of the median home in Hillsboro is $82,500. This is about $9,460 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $135,000 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $105,600 (56%) lower.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $790 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $19 (2%) lower.It is about $272 (25%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $262 (24%) lower.
