In comparison to other places in this area, Hillsboro is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro costs $82,500. This is roughly $9,460 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $135,000 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $105,600 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Hillsboro the median rent is $790 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $19 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $272 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $262 (24%) lower.
