Home prices

The median home in Monte Alto is valued at $62,100. It is approximately $1,400 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $155,400 (71%) lower, and it is approximately $27,900 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

Monte Alto's median monthly rent is $569. It is approximately $16 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $493 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is roughly $167 (22%) lower.
