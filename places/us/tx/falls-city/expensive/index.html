Home prices

The median home in Falls City costs $109,800. It is approximately $9,160 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $107,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $36,600 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Falls City's median monthly rent is $989. This is about $202 (25%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $73 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is is very similar compared to the city of San Antonio.
