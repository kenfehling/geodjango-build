Compared to the surrounding areas, Laguna Park is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Dallas, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Laguna Park costs $141,200. This is approximately $51,820 (57%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $76,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $46,900 (24%) lower.

Rents

Laguna Park's median rent is $917 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $142 (18%) higher.It is about $145 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $135 (12%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
