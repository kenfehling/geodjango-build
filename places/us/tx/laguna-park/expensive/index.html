Home prices

The value of the median home in Laguna Park is $141,200. This is about $51,820 (57%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $76,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $46,900 (24%) lower.

Rents

Laguna Park's median rent is $917 per month. It is approximately $142 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $145 (13%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $135 (12%) lower.
