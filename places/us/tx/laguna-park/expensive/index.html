Home prices

The median home value in Laguna Park is $141,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $51,820 (57%) higher.It is approximately $76,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $46,900 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Laguna Park the median monthly rent is $917. Compared to other places in this area it is about $142 (18%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $145 (13%) lower, and is about $135 (12%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
