Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Laguna Park is $917 a month. This is about $142 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $145 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $135 (12%) lower.
