In comparison to its nearby areas, Manvel is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Manvel is $252,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $15,280 (6%) higher. It is about $35,300 (16%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $81,000 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Manvel is $1,128. This is about $129 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $66 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $87 (8%) higher.
