Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Manvel is $1,128. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $129 (10%) lower.It is approximately $66 (6%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $87 (8%) higher.
