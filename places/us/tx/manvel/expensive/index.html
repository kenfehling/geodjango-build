Compared to the surrounding areas, Manvel is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Houston, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manvel is valued at $252,800. It is about $15,280 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $35,300 (16%) higher, and is roughly $81,000 (47%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Manvel is $1,128. This is approximately $129 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $66 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston is about $87 (8%) higher.
