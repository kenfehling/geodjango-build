Home prices

The median home in Manvel is valued at $252,800. It is approximately $15,280 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $35,300 (16%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $81,000 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Manvel the median rent is $1,128 per month. This is approximately $129 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $66 (6%) higher, and it is approximately $87 (8%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
