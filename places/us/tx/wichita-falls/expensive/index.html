Home prices

The median home price in Wichita Falls is $102,400. This is roughly $15,800 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $115,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation.

Rents

Wichita Falls's median rent is $794 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $71 (8%) lower. It is approximately $268 (25%) lower compared to the entire country.
