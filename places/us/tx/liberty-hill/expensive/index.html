Compared to other places in this area, Liberty Hill is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Liberty Hill is valued at $179,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $55,100 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $38,100 (17%) lower, and is about $33,000 (22%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Liberty Hill's median monthly rent is $876. It is about $352 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $186 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $116 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
