Much like other places in this area, it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Round Rock costs $244,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $10,680 (4%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $26,800 (12%) higher, and it is approximately $97,900 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Round Rock the median rent is $1,316 per month. It is roughly $90 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $254 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $324 (32%) higher.
