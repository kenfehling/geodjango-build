Home prices

The value of the median home in Round Rock is $244,300. This is roughly $10,680 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $26,800 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $97,900 (66%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Round Rock is $1,316 a month. This is about $90 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $254 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $324 (32%) higher.
