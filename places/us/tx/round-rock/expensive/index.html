Home prices

The median home in Round Rock is valued at $244,300. This is roughly $10,680 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $26,800 (12%) higher, and is about $97,900 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Round Rock is $1,316 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $90 (6%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $254 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is approximately $324 (32%) higher.
