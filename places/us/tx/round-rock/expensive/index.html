Home prices

The cost of the median home in Round Rock is $244,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $10,680 (4%) lower.It is approximately $26,800 (12%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $97,900 (66%) higher.

Rents

Round Rock's median monthly rent is $1,316. This is about $90 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $254 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is approximately $324 (32%) higher.
