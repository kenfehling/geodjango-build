Similar to other places in this area, it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home in Round Rock costs $244,300. This is roughly $10,680 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $26,800 (12%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $97,900 (66%) higher.

Rents

In Round Rock the median rent is $1,316 a month. This is approximately $90 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $254 (23%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is approximately $324 (32%) higher.
