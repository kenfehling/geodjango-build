Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Woodcreek is $1,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $204 (15%) higher.It is about $438 (41%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $508 (51%) higher.
