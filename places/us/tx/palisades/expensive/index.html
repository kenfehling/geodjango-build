In comparison to other places in this area, Palisades is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Amarillo however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Palisades is $146,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $54,780 (27%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $71,400 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $13,600 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Amarillo.

Rents

Palisades's median rent is $1,125 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $96 (7%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Amarillo is approximately $275 (32%) higher.
