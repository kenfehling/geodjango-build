Home prices

The median home price in Palisades is $146,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $54,780 (27%) lower.It is about $71,400 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Amarillo is approximately $13,600 (10%) higher.

Rents

Palisades's median monthly rent is $1,125. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $96 (7%) lower.It is about $63 (5%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $275 (32%) higher compared to the city of Amarillo.
