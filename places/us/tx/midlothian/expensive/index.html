Home prices

The median home in Midlothian is valued at $228,000. This is roughly $47,780 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $10,500 (4%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $39,900 (21%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Midlothian the median monthly rent is $1,192. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar.It is approximately $130 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $140 (13%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
