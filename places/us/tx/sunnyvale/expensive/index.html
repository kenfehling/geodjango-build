Compared to the surrounding areas, Sunnyvale is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Dallas, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sunnyvale is valued at $380,400. This is roughly $149,140 (64%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $162,900 (74%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $192,300 (102%) higher.

Rents

Sunnyvale's median monthly rent is $843. This is roughly $552 (39%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $219 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $209 (19%) lower.
