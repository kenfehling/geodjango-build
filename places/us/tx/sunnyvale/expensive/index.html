Home prices

The median home in Sunnyvale costs $380,400. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $149,140 (64%) higher. It is approximately $162,900 (74%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $192,300 (102%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Sunnyvale's median rent is $843 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $552 (39%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $219 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $209 (19%) lower.
