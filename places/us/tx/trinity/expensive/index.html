Home prices

The cost of the median home in Trinity is $54,300. It is roughly $52,880 (49%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $163,200 (75%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $117,500 (68%) lower.

Rents

Trinity's median rent is $634 per month. This is about $190 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $428 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $407 (39%) lower.
