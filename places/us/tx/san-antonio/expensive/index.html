In comparison to its surrounding areas, San Antonio is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home price in San Antonio is $146,400. This is approximately $283,967 (65%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $71,100 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole.

Rents

In San Antonio the median rent is $992 per month. This is about $160 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $70 (6%) lower compared to the entire country.
