Home prices

The median home in Dickinson costs $159,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $17,920 (10%) lower.It is about $58,200 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $12,500 (7%) lower.

Rents

Dickinson's median rent is $938 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $208 (18%) lower.It is approximately $124 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $103 (9%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
