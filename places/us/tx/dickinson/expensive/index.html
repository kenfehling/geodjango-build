Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dickinson is $159,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $17,920 (10%) lower. It is about $58,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $12,500 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Dickinson the median monthly rent is $938. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $208 (18%) lower. It is roughly $124 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $103 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
