Home prices

The median home in Dickinson costs $159,300. This is about $17,920 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $58,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $12,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dickinson is $938. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $208 (18%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $124 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $103 (9%) lower.
