Home prices

The median home value in Fayetteville is $158,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $33,200 (26%) higher. It is roughly $58,700 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $13,000 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Fayetteville the median rent is $875 a month. It is about $118 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $187 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $166 (15%) lower.
