Home prices

The median home in Galveston costs $182,800. It is roughly $58,940 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $34,700 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $11,000 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Galveston is $991. It is roughly $274 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $71 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is about $50 (4%) lower.
