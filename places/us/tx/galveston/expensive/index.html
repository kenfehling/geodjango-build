In comparison to nearby areas, Galveston is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Houston, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Galveston costs $182,800. This is roughly $58,940 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $34,700 (15%) lower, and is about $11,000 (6%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

Galveston's median rent is $991 a month. This is approximately $274 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $71 (6%) lower, and is approximately $50 (4%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
