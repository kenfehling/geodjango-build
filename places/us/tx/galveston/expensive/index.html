In comparison to its surrounding areas, Galveston is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Houstonhowever, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Galveston is $182,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $58,940 (24%) lower. It is about $34,700 (15%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Houston it is about $11,000 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Galveston is $991 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $274 (21%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $71 (6%) lower, and is roughly $50 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
