Home prices

The median home price in Galveston is $182,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $58,940 (24%) lower. It is approximately $34,700 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $11,000 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Galveston is $991 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $274 (21%) lower. It is approximately $71 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $50 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
