Home prices

The cost of the median home in Carthage is $107,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $18,100 (20%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $110,200 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport is roughly $40,800 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Carthage the median rent is $751 per month. This is roughly $178 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $311 (29%) lower, and it is about $61 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.
