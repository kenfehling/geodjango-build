Compared to the surrounding areas, League City is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Houston, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in League City is $245,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $29,600 (13%) higher.It is roughly $27,600 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $73,300 (42%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In League City the median monthly rent is $1,377. It is about $150 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $315 (29%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $336 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
