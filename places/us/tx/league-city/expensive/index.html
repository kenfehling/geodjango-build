In comparison to its surrounding areas, League City is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in League City is valued at $245,100. This is approximately $29,600 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $27,600 (12%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is about $73,300 (42%) higher.

Rents

In League City the median rent is $1,377 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $150 (12%) higher. It is roughly $315 (29%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $336 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
