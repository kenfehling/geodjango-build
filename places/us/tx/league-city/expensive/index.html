Home prices

The median home cost in League City is $245,100. It is approximately $29,600 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $27,600 (12%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $73,300 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in League City is $1,377. Compared to other places in this area this is about $150 (12%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $315 (29%) higher, and it is approximately $336 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
