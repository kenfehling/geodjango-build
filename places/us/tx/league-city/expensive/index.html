Compared to the surrounding areas, League City is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Houston however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in League City is $245,100. It is approximately $29,600 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $27,600 (12%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $73,300 (42%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In League City the median rent is $1,377 a month. This is approximately $150 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $315 (29%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $336 (32%) higher.
