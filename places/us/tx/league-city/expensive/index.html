In comparison to the surrounding areas, League City is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Houston, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in League City costs $245,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $29,600 (13%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $27,600 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $73,300 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in League City is $1,377. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $150 (12%) higher. It is about $315 (29%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $336 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
