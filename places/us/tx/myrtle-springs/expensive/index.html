Home prices

The cost of the median home in Myrtle Springs is $212,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $112,700 (112%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $5,000 (2%) lower, and is roughly $24,400 (12%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Myrtle Springs the median rent is $711 per month. It is about $113 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $351 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is approximately $341 (32%) lower.
