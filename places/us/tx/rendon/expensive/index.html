Home prices

The median home in Rendon costs $231,400. This is about $70,280 (43%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $13,900 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $43,300 (23%) higher.

Rents

Rendon's median rent is $951 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $219 (18%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $111 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $101 (9%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
