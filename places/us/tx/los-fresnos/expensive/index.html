Home prices

The median home in Los Fresnos costs $105,400. This is about $10,320 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $112,100 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $15,400 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

In Los Fresnos the median monthly rent is $863. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $98 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $199 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is roughly $127 (17%) higher.
