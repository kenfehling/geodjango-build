Compared to the surrounding areas, Pearland is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Pearland costs $244,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $39,440 (19%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $27,300 (12%) higher, and it is approximately $73,000 (42%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Pearland the median rent is $1,413 per month. This is about $281 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $351 (33%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $372 (35%) higher.
