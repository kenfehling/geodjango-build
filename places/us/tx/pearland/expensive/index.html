Compared to its nearby areas, Pearland is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Houston however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pearland is valued at $244,800. This is approximately $39,440 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $27,300 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston it is about $73,000 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pearland is $1,413. Compared to other places in this area this is about $281 (24%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $351 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $372 (35%) higher.
