In comparison to nearby areas, Pearland is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Pearland costs $244,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $39,440 (19%) higher. It is roughly $27,300 (12%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $73,000 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pearland is $1,413. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $281 (24%) higher. It is roughly $351 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $372 (35%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
