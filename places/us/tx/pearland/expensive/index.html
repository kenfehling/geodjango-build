In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pearland is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Houstonhowever, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pearland costs $244,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $39,440 (19%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $27,300 (12%) higher, and it is approximately $73,000 (42%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Pearland the median rent is $1,413 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $281 (24%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $351 (33%) higher, and it is about $372 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
