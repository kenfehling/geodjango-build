Home prices

The median home in Stafford is valued at $180,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $1,900 (1%) lower. It is approximately $37,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Houston is roughly $8,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Stafford is $1,222 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $366 (23%) lower. It is approximately $160 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $181 (17%) higher.
