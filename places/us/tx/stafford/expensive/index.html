The median home in Stafford costs $180,400. This is roughly $1,900 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $37,100 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Houston is roughly $8,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Stafford the median monthly rent is $1,222. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $366 (23%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $160 (15%) higher, and it is approximately $181 (17%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
