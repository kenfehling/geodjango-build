Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Stafford is $1,222 per month. This is about $366 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $160 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $181 (17%) higher.
