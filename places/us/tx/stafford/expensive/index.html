Home prices

The value of the median home in Stafford is $180,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $1,900 (1%) lower.It is approximately $37,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $8,600 (5%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Stafford the median rent is $1,222 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $366 (23%) lower.It is roughly $160 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $181 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
