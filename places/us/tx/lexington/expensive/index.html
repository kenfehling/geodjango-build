Home prices

The value of the median home in Lexington is $133,500. It is approximately $21,540 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $84,000 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $38,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in Lexington is $913 a month. This is approximately $166 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $149 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Houston is about $128 (12%) lower.
