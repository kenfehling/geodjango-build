Home prices

The median home in Lexington is valued at $133,500. It is about $21,540 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $84,000 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $38,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lexington is $913 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $166 (22%) higher.It is roughly $149 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $128 (12%) lower.
