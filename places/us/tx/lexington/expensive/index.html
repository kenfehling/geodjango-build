Home prices

Lexington's median home cost is $133,500. This is roughly $21,540 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $84,000 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $38,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in Lexington is $913 a month. It is roughly $166 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $149 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $128 (12%) lower.
