Compared to other places in this area, Cottonwood Shores is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home in Cottonwood Shores is valued at $134,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $128,360 (48%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $83,200 (38%) lower, and it is about $12,100 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Cottonwood Shores's median monthly rent is $1,113. It is approximately $149 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $51 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $121 (12%) higher.
