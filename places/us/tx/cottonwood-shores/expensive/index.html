In comparison to other places in this area, Cottonwood Shores is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Cottonwood Shores's median home price is $134,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $128,360 (48%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $83,200 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $12,100 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Cottonwood Shores the median monthly rent is $1,113. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $149 (15%) higher. It is roughly $51 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $121 (12%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
