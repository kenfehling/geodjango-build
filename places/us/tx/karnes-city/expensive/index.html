Home prices

Rents

Karnes City's median rent is $829 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $93 (12%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $233 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $163 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
