Home prices

The median home cost in Karnes City is $76,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $18,420 (19%) lower. It is roughly $141,000 (64%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $69,900 (47%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Karnes City's median rent is $829 a month. This is approximately $93 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $233 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $163 (16%) lower.
