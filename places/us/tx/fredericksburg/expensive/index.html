In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fredericksburg is substantially more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Fredericksburg's median home price is $275,500. This is approximately $108,900 (65%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $58,000 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $129,100 (88%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Fredericksburg is $964 per month. It is about $117 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $98 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $28 (2%) lower.
