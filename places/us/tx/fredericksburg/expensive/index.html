Home prices

The median home price in Fredericksburg is $275,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $108,900 (65%) higher.It is roughly $58,000 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $129,100 (88%) higher.

Rents

In Fredericksburg the median rent is $964 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $117 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $98 (9%) lower, and is roughly $28 (2%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
