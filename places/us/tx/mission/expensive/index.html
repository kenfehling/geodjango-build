In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mission is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Brownsville.

Home prices

Mission's median home price is $112,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,960 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $105,100 (48%) lower, and it is about $22,400 (24%) higher compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

Mission's median monthly rent is $819. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $98 (13%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is approximately $83 (11%) higher.
