In comparison to other places in this area, Mission is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mission costs $112,400. This is approximately $10,960 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $105,100 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is approximately $22,400 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mission is $819. It is about $98 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville it is approximately $83 (11%) higher.
