Home prices

The median home cost in Mission is $112,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $10,960 (8%) lower.It is approximately $105,100 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $22,400 (24%) higher compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

The median rent in Mission is $819 per month. This is roughly $98 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is approximately $83 (11%) higher.
