Home prices

The price of the median home in Mission is $112,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $10,960 (8%) lower.It is roughly $105,100 (48%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is about $22,400 (24%) higher.

Rents

In Mission the median monthly rent is $819. It is about $98 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $243 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $83 (11%) higher compared to the city of Brownsville.
