Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownwood is $785. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $69 (9%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $277 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $139 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Abilene.
