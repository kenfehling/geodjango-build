Compared to the surrounding areas, Brownwood is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Abilene.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brownwood is $95,400. This is roughly $18,480 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $122,100 (56%) lower, and it is about $25,000 (20%) lower compared to the city of Abilene.

Rents

In Brownwood the median rent is $785 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $69 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $277 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Abilene it is about $139 (15%) lower.
