Rents

In Eagle Pass the median monthly rent is $624. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $53 (7%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $438 (41%) lower, and is roughly $58 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Del Rio.
