Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Eagle Pass is $624 per month. It is approximately $53 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $438 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Del Rio is approximately $58 (8%) lower.
