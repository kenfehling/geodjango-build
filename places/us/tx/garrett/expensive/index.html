Home prices

Rents

Garrett's median monthly rent is $867. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $179 (17%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $195 (18%) lower, and is roughly $185 (17%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
