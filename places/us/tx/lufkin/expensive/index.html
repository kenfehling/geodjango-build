Home prices

The median home in Lufkin costs $116,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $16,080 (15%) higher. It is approximately $100,700 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tyler it is about $43,200 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Lufkin the median monthly rent is $856. It is roughly $202 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $206 (19%) lower, and is approximately $67 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Tyler.
