Home prices

The median home price in Lufkin is $116,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,080 (15%) higher. It is about $100,700 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tyler it is approximately $43,200 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Lufkin the median monthly rent is $856. This is roughly $202 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $206 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tyler is approximately $67 (7%) lower.
