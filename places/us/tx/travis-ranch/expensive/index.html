Home prices

The median home cost in Travis Ranch is $218,100. It is about $117,780 (35%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $30,000 (15%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Travis Ranch the median rent is $1,511 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $257 (20%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $449 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $459 (43%) higher.
