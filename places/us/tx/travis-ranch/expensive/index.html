Home prices

The median home in Travis Ranch costs $218,100. This is roughly $141,460 (39%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and it is roughly $30,000 (15%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Travis Ranch is $1,511. It is about $219 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $449 (42%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $459 (43%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
