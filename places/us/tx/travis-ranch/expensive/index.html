Home prices

The median home in Travis Ranch is valued at $218,100. This is roughly $141,460 (39%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $30,000 (15%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Travis Ranch is $1,511 a month. It is about $219 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $449 (42%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is approximately $459 (43%) higher.
