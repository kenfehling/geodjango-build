Home prices

The median home in Uvalde costs $77,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $24,440 (45%) higher. It is approximately $139,800 (64%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $68,700 (46%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Uvalde is $633 a month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $429 (40%) lower, and is approximately $359 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
