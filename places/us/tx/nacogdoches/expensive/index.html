In comparison to its surrounding areas, Nacogdoches is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Nacogdoches is $149,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $57,380 (62%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $67,600 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is about $1,800 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Nacogdoches is $771 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $44 (5%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $291 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $41 (5%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
