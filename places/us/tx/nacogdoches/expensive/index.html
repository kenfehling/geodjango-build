Home prices

The price of the median home in Nacogdoches is $149,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $57,380 (62%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $67,600 (31%) lower, and is roughly $1,800 (1%) higher compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Nacogdoches the median monthly rent is $771. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $44 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $291 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is about $41 (5%) lower.
