Home prices

The price of the median home in Georgetown is $266,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $62,080 (30%) higher.It is roughly $49,400 (22%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Austin is about $70,500 (20%) lower.

Rents

Georgetown's median rent is $1,231 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $169 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Austin is about $49 (3%) lower.
