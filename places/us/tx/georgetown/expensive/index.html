In comparison to the surrounding areas, Georgetown is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Austin.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Georgetown is $266,900. This is roughly $62,080 (30%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $49,400 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Austin is roughly $70,500 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Georgetown is $1,231 a month. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $169 (15%) higher, and it is approximately $49 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.
