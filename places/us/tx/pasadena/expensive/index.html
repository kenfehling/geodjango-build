Home prices

The price of the median home in Pasadena is $124,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $14,280 (10%) lower.It is roughly $93,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $47,600 (27%) lower.

Rents

Pasadena's median rent is $963 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $133 (12%) lower.It is about $99 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $78 (7%) lower.
