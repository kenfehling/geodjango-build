Home prices

The median home in Pasadena costs $124,200. This is approximately $14,280 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $93,300 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $47,600 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pasadena is $963. This is approximately $133 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $99 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $78 (7%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
