In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pasadena is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Houston, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Pasadena's median home cost is $124,200. This is roughly $14,280 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $93,300 (42%) lower, and it is about $47,600 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Pasadena the median monthly rent is $963. It is about $133 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $99 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
