Compared to nearby areas, Fort Hood is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Austin, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Fort Hood is $92,000. It is roughly $48,260 (34%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $125,500 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Austin is about $245,400 (72%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Hood is $1,252 per month. It is roughly $353 (39%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $190 (17%) higher, and it is roughly $28 (2%) lower compared to the city of Austin.
