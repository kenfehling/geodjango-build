Home prices

The median home in Bay City is valued at $99,200. This is roughly $14,040 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $118,300 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $72,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Bay City the median monthly rent is $861. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $201 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Houston is about $180 (17%) lower.
