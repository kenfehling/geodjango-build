Compared to its surrounding areas, Bay City is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Bay City costs $99,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $14,040 (12%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $118,300 (54%) lower, and is roughly $72,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Bay City the median monthly rent is $861. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $201 (18%) lower, and is approximately $180 (17%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
