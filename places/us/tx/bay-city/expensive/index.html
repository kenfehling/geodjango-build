Home prices

The value of the median home in Bay City is $99,200. It is about $14,040 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $118,300 (54%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is roughly $72,600 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Bay City the median rent is $861 a month. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is about $201 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $180 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
