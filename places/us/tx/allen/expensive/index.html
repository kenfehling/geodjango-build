Home prices

The price of the median home in Allen is $303,300. It is roughly $87,000 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $85,800 (39%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $115,200 (61%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Allen is $1,487 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $176 (10%) lower. It is roughly $425 (40%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $435 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
