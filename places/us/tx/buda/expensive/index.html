Home prices

The value of the median home in Buda is $257,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $34,180 (11%) lower.It is approximately $40,300 (18%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $111,400 (76%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Buda the median rent is $1,394 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $280 (16%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $332 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $402 (40%) higher.
