Home prices

The median home in Robinson is valued at $165,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $14,880 (9%) higher. It is roughly $52,200 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Austin it is roughly $172,100 (51%) lower.

Rents

Robinson's median rent is $1,244 per month. This is about $162 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $182 (17%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $36 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.
