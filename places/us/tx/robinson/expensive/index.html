Home prices

The cost of the median home in Robinson is $165,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $14,880 (9%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $52,200 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Austin it is approximately $172,100 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Robinson is $1,244 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $162 (14%) higher.It is approximately $182 (17%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Austin is about $36 (2%) lower.
