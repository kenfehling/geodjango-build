Compared to its nearby areas, Webster is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Houston, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Webster is $186,400. It is roughly $91,240 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $31,100 (14%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $14,600 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Webster's median rent is $1,131 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $191 (14%) lower. It is about $69 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $90 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
