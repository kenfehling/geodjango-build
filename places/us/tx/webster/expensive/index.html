Compared to the surrounding areas, Webster is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Houston, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Webster is $186,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $91,240 (32%) lower.It is roughly $31,100 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $14,600 (8%) higher.

Rents

Webster's median monthly rent is $1,131. It is approximately $191 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $69 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston is about $90 (8%) higher.
