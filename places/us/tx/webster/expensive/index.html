Compared to other places in this area, Webster is significantly less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Houstonhowever, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Webster is $186,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $91,240 (32%) lower. It is approximately $31,100 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $14,600 (8%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

Webster's median rent is $1,131 a month. This is approximately $191 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $69 (6%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston is about $90 (8%) higher.
