Home prices

Rents

Webster's median monthly rent is $1,131. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $191 (14%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $69 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $90 (8%) higher.
