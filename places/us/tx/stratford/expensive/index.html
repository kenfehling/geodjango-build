Home prices

Stratford's median home price is $105,500. This is approximately $25,300 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $112,000 (51%) lower, and it is roughly $27,000 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Amarillo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stratford is $753. It is about $59 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $309 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Amarillo it is roughly $97 (11%) lower.
