Home prices

The median home in Northlake costs $428,700. It is approximately $58,460 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $211,200 (97%) higher, and it is roughly $240,600 (127%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Northlake the median monthly rent is $1,188. This is about $240 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $126 (11%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $136 (12%) higher.
