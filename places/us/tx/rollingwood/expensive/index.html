Home prices

The median home in Rollingwood costs $1,103,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $382,200 (53%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $885,600 (407%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is roughly $956,700 (653%) higher.

Rents

In Rollingwood the median rent is $3,501 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $1,548 (79%) higher.It is about $2,439 (229%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $2,509 (252%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
