Home prices

The price of the median home in Fort Hancock is $52,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $22,740 (30%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $165,500 (76%) lower, and is roughly $75,400 (59%) lower compared to the city of El Paso.

Rents

Fort Hancock's median rent is $653 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $58 (9%) higher. It is roughly $409 (38%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $184 (21%) lower compared to the city of El Paso.
