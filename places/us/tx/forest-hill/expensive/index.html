The median home in Forest Hill costs $94,400. This is about $127,980 (57%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $123,100 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $93,700 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Forest Hill is $1,107 a month. This is roughly $24 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $45 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $55 (5%) higher.
