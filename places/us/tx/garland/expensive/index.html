Rents

Garland's median monthly rent is $1,133. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $363 (24%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $71 (6%) higher, and it is approximately $81 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Dallas.
