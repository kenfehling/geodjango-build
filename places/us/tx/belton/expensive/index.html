Home prices

Belton's median home value is $158,400. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $4,280 (2%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $59,100 (27%) lower, and is roughly $179,000 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.

Rents

Belton's median monthly rent is $924. It is about $38 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $138 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Austin it is approximately $356 (27%) lower.
