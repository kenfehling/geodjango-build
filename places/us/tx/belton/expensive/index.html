Home prices

The price of the median home in Belton is $158,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $4,280 (2%) lower.It is roughly $59,100 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Austin is approximately $179,000 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Belton is $924 per month. This is roughly $38 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $138 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $356 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.
