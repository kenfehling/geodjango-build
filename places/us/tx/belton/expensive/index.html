Home prices

The cost of the median home in Belton is $158,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $4,280 (2%) lower. It is approximately $59,100 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Austin it is roughly $179,000 (53%) lower.

Rents

Belton's median monthly rent is $924. It is approximately $38 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $138 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Austin it is approximately $356 (27%) lower.
