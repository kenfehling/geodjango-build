Home prices

The median home in Belton costs $158,400. It is about $4,280 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $59,100 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $179,000 (53%) lower compared to the city of Austin.

Rents

The median rent in Belton is $924 a month. This is roughly $38 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $138 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Austin it is about $356 (27%) lower.
