Compared to the surrounding areas, Socorro is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of El Paso.

Home prices

The median home in Socorro is valued at $93,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9,380 (11%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $124,300 (57%) lower, and is roughly $34,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.

Rents

Socorro's median rent is $726 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $13 (1%) lower. It is roughly $336 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of El Paso it is roughly $111 (13%) lower.
