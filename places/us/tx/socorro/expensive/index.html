Compared to other places in this area, Socorro is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of El Paso.

Home prices

The median home in Socorro is valued at $93,200. It is approximately $9,380 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $124,300 (57%) lower, and it is roughly $34,200 (26%) lower compared to the city of El Paso.

Rents

In Socorro the median monthly rent is $726. Compared to nearby areas this is about $13 (1%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $336 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of El Paso it is approximately $111 (13%) lower.
