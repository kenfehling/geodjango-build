Home prices

The price of the median home in Socorro is $93,200. This is approximately $9,380 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $124,300 (57%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $34,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of El Paso.

Rents

Socorro's median monthly rent is $726. It is about $13 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $336 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of El Paso is roughly $111 (13%) lower.
