In comparison to other places in this area, Socorro is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of El Paso.

Home prices

Socorro's median home price is $93,200. This is about $9,380 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $124,300 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of El Paso it is roughly $34,200 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Socorro the median monthly rent is $726. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13 (1%) lower. It is roughly $336 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of El Paso is roughly $111 (13%) lower.
