Compared to the surrounding areas, Plains is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Lubbock.

Home prices

The median home cost in Plains is $60,700. This is about $22,920 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $156,800 (72%) lower, and is approximately $80,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Lubbock.

Rents

The median rent in Plains is $645 per month. It is roughly $46 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $417 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Lubbock it is approximately $310 (32%) lower.
