Home prices

The median home in Frankston costs $115,900. This is roughly $15,020 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $101,600 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is roughly $72,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Frankston the median monthly rent is $773. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $253 (24%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $289 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is approximately $279 (26%) lower.
