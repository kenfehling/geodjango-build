Home prices

The median home in Crosbyton costs $54,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $8,140 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $162,800 (74%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lubbock it is roughly $86,100 (61%) lower.

Rents

Crosbyton's median rent is $600 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $91 (17%) higher. It is roughly $462 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Lubbock it is about $355 (37%) lower.
