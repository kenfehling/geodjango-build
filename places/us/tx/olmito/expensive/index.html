The median home in Olmito costs $89,800. It is roughly $10,760 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $127,700 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is is very similar.

Rents

Olmito's median rent is $833 per month. This is approximately $55 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $229 (21%) lower, and is about $97 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
