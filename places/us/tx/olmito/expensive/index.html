Home prices

Olmito's median home value is $89,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10,760 (10%) lower. It is about $127,700 (58%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Brownsville is is very similar.

Rents

Olmito's median rent is $833 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $55 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $229 (21%) lower, and it is about $97 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
