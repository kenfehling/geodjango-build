In comparison to its nearby areas, Harker Heights is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Austin.

Home prices

The median home in Harker Heights costs $201,400. It is approximately $44,820 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $16,100 (7%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Austin is roughly $136,000 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harker Heights is $936. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $70 (6%) lower. It is about $126 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $344 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.
