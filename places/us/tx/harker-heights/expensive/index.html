Compared to the surrounding areas, Harker Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Austin however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Harker Heights is $201,400. This is roughly $44,820 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $16,100 (7%) lower, and is approximately $136,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Austin.

Rents

Harker Heights's median rent is $936 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $70 (6%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $126 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $344 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.
