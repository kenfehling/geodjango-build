Compared to its nearby areas, Harker Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Austin.

Home prices

Harker Heights's median home cost is $201,400. It is about $44,820 (28%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $16,100 (7%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $136,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Austin.

Rents

The median rent in Harker Heights is $936 per month. This is about $70 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $126 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Austin it is roughly $344 (26%) lower.
