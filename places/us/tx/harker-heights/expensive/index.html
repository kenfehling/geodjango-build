Home prices

The median home price in Harker Heights is $201,400. This is about $44,820 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $16,100 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Austin is roughly $136,000 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harker Heights is $936. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $70 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $126 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $344 (26%) lower compared to the city of Austin.
