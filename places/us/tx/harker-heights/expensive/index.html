In comparison to the surrounding areas, Harker Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Austinhowever, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Harker Heights costs $201,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $44,820 (28%) higher. It is about $16,100 (7%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $136,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Austin.

Rents

The median rent in Harker Heights is $936 per month. It is roughly $70 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $126 (11%) lower, and it is about $344 (26%) lower compared to the city of Austin.
