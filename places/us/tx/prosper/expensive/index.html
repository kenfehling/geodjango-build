Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Prosper is $1,497. It is approximately $206 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $435 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $445 (42%) higher.
