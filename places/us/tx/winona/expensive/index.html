Home prices

The median home in Winona is valued at $81,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $25,700 (24%) lower. It is about $136,400 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $67,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Winona the median rent is $656 a month. This is about $230 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $406 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $156 (19%) lower.
