In comparison to its nearby areas, Columbus is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Houston, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus costs $143,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $25,760 (21%) higher. It is approximately $74,200 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $28,500 (16%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Columbus the median rent is $593 per month. This is about $227 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $469 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston is roughly $448 (43%) lower.
