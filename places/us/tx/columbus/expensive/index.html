Home prices

The value of the median home in Columbus is $143,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $25,760 (21%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $74,200 (34%) lower, and it is about $28,500 (16%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in Columbus is $593 a month. This is approximately $227 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $469 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is about $448 (43%) lower.
