In comparison to its surrounding areas, Columbus is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus costs $143,300. This is about $25,760 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $74,200 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is approximately $28,500 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Columbus is $593 per month. This is roughly $227 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $469 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $448 (43%) lower.
