Home prices

The median home in Surfside Beach is valued at $205,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $95,040 (85%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $11,700 (5%) lower, and is approximately $34,000 (19%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents
