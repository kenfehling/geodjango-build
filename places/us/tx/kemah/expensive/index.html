Home prices

The median home cost in Kemah is $204,000. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $36,060 (15%) lower. It is roughly $13,500 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $32,200 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Kemah's median rent is $1,525 per month. It is roughly $301 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $463 (43%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $484 (46%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
