Compared to nearby areas, Kemah is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

Kemah's median home value is $204,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $36,060 (15%) lower. It is roughly $13,500 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $32,200 (18%) higher.

Rents

Kemah's median monthly rent is $1,525. This is roughly $301 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $463 (43%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $484 (46%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
