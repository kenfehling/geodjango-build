Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kemah is $204,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $36,060 (15%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $13,500 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is about $32,200 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kemah is $1,525. This is roughly $301 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $463 (43%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $484 (46%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
