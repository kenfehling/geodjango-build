In comparison to other places in this area, Kemah is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Houston.

Home prices

The median home in Kemah is valued at $204,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $36,060 (15%) lower. It is approximately $13,500 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $32,200 (18%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median rent in Kemah is $1,525 a month. It is roughly $301 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $463 (43%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston it is about $484 (46%) higher.
