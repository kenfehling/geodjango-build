Compared to its surrounding areas, Kemah is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Houston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Kemah's median home value is $204,000. This is approximately $36,060 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $13,500 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is about $32,200 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kemah is $1,525 a month. This is approximately $301 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $463 (43%) higher, and compared to the city of Houston it is approximately $484 (46%) higher.
