Home prices

The median home in San Diego is valued at $36,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $34,280 (48%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $180,900 (83%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Corpus Christi it is roughly $104,500 (74%) lower.

Rents

San Diego's median rent is $669 per month. It is approximately $70 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $393 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $359 (34%) lower compared to the city of Corpus Christi.
