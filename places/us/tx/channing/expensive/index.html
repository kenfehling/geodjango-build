Home prices

Rents

In Channing the median rent is $640 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $231 (26%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $422 (39%) lower, and it is about $210 (24%) lower compared to the city of Amarillo.
