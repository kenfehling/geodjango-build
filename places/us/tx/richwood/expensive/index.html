Home prices

The median home in Richwood costs $175,700. This is approximately $54,800 (45%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $41,800 (19%) lower, and it is about $3,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richwood is $1,034. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $58 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $28 (2%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Houston.
