Rents

In Lamar the median monthly rent is $1,099. This is roughly $231 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $37 (3%) higher, and it is roughly $71 (6%) higher compared to the city of Corpus Christi.
