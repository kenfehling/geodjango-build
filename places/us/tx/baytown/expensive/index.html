In comparison to the surrounding areas, Baytown is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Houston however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Baytown is $119,900. It is approximately $77,980 (39%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $97,600 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $51,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Baytown the median rent is $945 a month. It is about $149 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $117 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is approximately $96 (9%) lower.
