Home prices

Baytown's median home price is $119,900. This is approximately $77,980 (39%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $97,600 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is approximately $51,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Baytown the median rent is $945 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $149 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $117 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is about $96 (9%) lower.
