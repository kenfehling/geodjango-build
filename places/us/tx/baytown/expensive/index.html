Home prices

The median home price in Baytown is $119,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $77,980 (39%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $97,600 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston is about $51,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Baytown the median rent is $945 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $149 (13%) lower. It is about $117 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $96 (9%) lower.
