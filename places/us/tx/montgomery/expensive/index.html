Rents

The median monthly rent in Montgomery is $1,123. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $58 (5%) higher.It is approximately $61 (5%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $82 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.
