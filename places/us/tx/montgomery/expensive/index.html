Home prices

The median home cost in Montgomery is $309,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $157,980 (103%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $92,400 (42%) higher, and it is roughly $138,100 (80%) higher in comparison to the city of Houston.

Rents

Montgomery's median rent is $1,123 per month. This is about $58 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $61 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $82 (7%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
