In comparison to its surrounding areas, Leander is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Leander is $263,400. This is approximately $54,140 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $45,900 (21%) higher, and is approximately $117,000 (79%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Leander the median monthly rent is $1,511. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $243 (19%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $449 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is approximately $519 (52%) higher.
