Compared to nearby areas, Leander is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home in Leander is valued at $263,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $54,140 (17%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $45,900 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $117,000 (79%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Leander is $1,511. This is about $243 (19%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $449 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $519 (52%) higher.
