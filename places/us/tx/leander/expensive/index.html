In comparison to its surrounding areas, Leander is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

Leander's median home value is $263,400. It is approximately $54,140 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $45,900 (21%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $117,000 (79%) higher.

Rents

In Leander the median monthly rent is $1,511. This is approximately $243 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $449 (42%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is about $519 (52%) higher.
