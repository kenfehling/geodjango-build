Home prices

The median home in Leander is valued at $263,400. It is about $54,140 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $45,900 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is approximately $117,000 (79%) higher.

Rents

In Leander the median rent is $1,511 a month. This is about $243 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $449 (42%) higher, and is approximately $519 (52%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
