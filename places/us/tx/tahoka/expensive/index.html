Home prices

Rents

Tahoka's median monthly rent is $621. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $97 (13%) lower.It is approximately $441 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lubbock is roughly $334 (34%) lower.
