Compared to the surrounding areas, Olivarez is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Olivarez is valued at $69,300. This is about $8,000 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $148,200 (68%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $20,700 (23%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

The median rent in Olivarez is $597 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $19 (3%) higher. It is roughly $465 (43%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Brownsville it is approximately $139 (18%) lower.
