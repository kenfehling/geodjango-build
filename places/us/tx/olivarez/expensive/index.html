Compared to its surrounding areas, Olivarez is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Brownsville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Olivarez is $69,300. It is about $8,000 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $148,200 (68%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $20,700 (23%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.

Rents

In Olivarez the median monthly rent is $597. This is about $19 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $465 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $139 (18%) lower compared to the city of Brownsville.
