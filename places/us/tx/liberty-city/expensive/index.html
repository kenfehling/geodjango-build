Home prices

The price of the median home in Liberty City is $130,300. It is about $5,260 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $87,200 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $17,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Liberty City the median monthly rent is $946. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $18 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $116 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $134 (16%) higher.
