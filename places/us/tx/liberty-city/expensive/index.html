Home prices

The price of the median home in Liberty City is $130,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $5,260 (4%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $87,200 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Shreveport it is roughly $17,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Liberty City the median rent is $946 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $18 (1%) higher. It is roughly $116 (10%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is approximately $134 (16%) higher.
