Home prices

The median home value in Conroe is $187,300. This is approximately $29,480 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $30,200 (13%) lower, and is roughly $15,500 (9%) higher compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

Conroe's median monthly rent is $1,085. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $144 (11%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $23 (2%) higher, and it is about $44 (4%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
