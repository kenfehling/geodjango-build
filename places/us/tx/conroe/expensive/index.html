Compared to other places in this area, Conroe is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Houston, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Conroe is $187,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $29,480 (18%) higher. It is about $30,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Houston is approximately $15,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

In Conroe the median monthly rent is $1,085. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $144 (11%) lower. It is roughly $23 (2%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $44 (4%) higher compared to the city of Houston.
