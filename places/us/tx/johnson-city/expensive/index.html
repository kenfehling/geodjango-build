Home prices

The median home in Johnson City is valued at $178,800. It is roughly $102,440 (36%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $38,700 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is about $32,400 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Johnson City the median rent is $904 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $45 (4%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $158 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $88 (8%) lower.
