In comparison to other places in this area, Johnson City is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of San Antonio however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Johnson City is $178,800. It is roughly $102,440 (36%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $38,700 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is about $32,400 (22%) higher.

Rents

Johnson City's median rent is $904 a month. This is about $45 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $158 (14%) lower, and it is about $88 (8%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
