Compared to its surrounding areas, Johnson City is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Antonio, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Johnson City is valued at $178,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $102,440 (36%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $38,700 (17%) lower, and is roughly $32,400 (22%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Johnson City the median rent is $904 per month. It is about $45 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $158 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is about $88 (8%) lower.
