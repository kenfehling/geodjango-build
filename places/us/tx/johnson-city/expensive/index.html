Compared to the surrounding areas, Johnson City is substantially less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home price in Johnson City is $178,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $102,440 (36%) lower.It is about $38,700 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $32,400 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Johnson City's median rent is $904 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $45 (4%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $158 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $88 (8%) lower.
