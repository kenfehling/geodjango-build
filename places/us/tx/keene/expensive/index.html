Home prices

The median home price in Keene is $142,200. This is approximately $18,980 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $75,300 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $45,900 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Keene is $834. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $139 (14%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $218 (20%) lower.
