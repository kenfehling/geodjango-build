Home prices

The median home in Keene costs $142,200. It is roughly $18,980 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $75,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $45,900 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Keene the median monthly rent is $834. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $139 (14%) lower.It is approximately $228 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $218 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
