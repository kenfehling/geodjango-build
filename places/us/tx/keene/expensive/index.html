Home prices

The median home in Keene costs $142,200. It is roughly $18,980 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $75,300 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $45,900 (24%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Keene's median rent is $834 per month. This is approximately $139 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and it is about $218 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
