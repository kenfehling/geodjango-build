Home prices

The median home price in Richland Hills is $149,400. This is approximately $22,500 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $68,100 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $38,700 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richland Hills is $955. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $200 (17%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $107 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $97 (9%) lower.
