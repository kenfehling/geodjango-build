Compared to its surrounding areas, Brownsboro is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

Brownsboro's median home cost is $95,200. This is approximately $40,940 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $122,300 (56%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $92,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Brownsboro's median rent is $850 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $23 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $202 (19%) lower.
