Home prices

The price of the median home in Brownsboro is $95,200. This is approximately $40,940 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $122,300 (56%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $92,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownsboro is $850. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $23 (2%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and is roughly $202 (19%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
