Rents

The median rent in Crosby is $852 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $197 (18%) lower. It is roughly $210 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is about $189 (18%) lower.
