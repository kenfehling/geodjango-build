Home prices

The median home in Early costs $110,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $36,360 (49%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $107,200 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Abilene it is approximately $10,100 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Early is $795 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $81 (11%) higher. It is about $267 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Abilene is about $129 (13%) lower.
