Home prices

The price of the median home in Ladonia is $71,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $7,560 (9%) lower. It is roughly $146,200 (67%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $116,800 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Ladonia's median rent is $916 per month. This is about $170 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $146 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $136 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
