Home prices

Rents

Laguna Vista's median monthly rent is $916. It is approximately $160 (21%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $146 (13%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $180 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Brownsville.
