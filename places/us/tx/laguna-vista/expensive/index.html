Home prices

The median home value in Laguna Vista is $180,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $57,500 (46%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $36,800 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brownsville is about $90,700 (100%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Vista is $916. This is roughly $142 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $146 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Brownsville is about $180 (24%) higher.
