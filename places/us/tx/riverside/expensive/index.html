Home prices

The price of the median home in Riverside is $156,300. It is about $38,720 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $61,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Houston is roughly $15,500 (9%) lower.

Rents

Riverside's median rent is $868 per month. This is about $39 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $194 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $173 (16%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
