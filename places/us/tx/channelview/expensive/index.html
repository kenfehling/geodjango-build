Home prices

The median home in Channelview costs $124,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $35,200 (39%) higher.It is roughly $92,600 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston it is about $46,900 (27%) lower.

Rents

Channelview's median monthly rent is $977. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $54 (5%) higher.It is roughly $85 (8%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Houston it is roughly $64 (6%) lower.
