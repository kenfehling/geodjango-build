Home prices

The cost of the median home in Omaha is $96,300. This is approximately $12,840 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $121,200 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport is about $51,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Omaha is $525 a month. It is approximately $121 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $537 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $287 (35%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
