Home prices

Cedar Park's median home cost is $301,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $21,280 (6%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $84,100 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $155,200 (106%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cedar Park is $1,408. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32 (2%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $346 (32%) higher, and it is approximately $416 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
