Compared to its nearby areas, Cedar Park is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of San Antonio.

Home prices

The median home in Cedar Park is valued at $301,600. It is roughly $21,280 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $84,100 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $155,200 (106%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Cedar Park's median rent is $1,408 per month. This is about $32 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $346 (32%) higher, and is approximately $416 (41%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
