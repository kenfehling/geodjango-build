Home prices

The median home in Cedar Park is valued at $301,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $21,280 (6%) lower. It is about $84,100 (38%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is roughly $155,200 (106%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cedar Park is $1,408 a month. This is roughly $32 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $346 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is roughly $416 (41%) higher.
