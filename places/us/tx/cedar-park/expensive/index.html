In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cedar Park is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cedar Park costs $301,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $21,280 (6%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $84,100 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio it is about $155,200 (106%) higher.

Rents

In Cedar Park the median monthly rent is $1,408. This is about $32 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $346 (32%) higher, and is approximately $416 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
