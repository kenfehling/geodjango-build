Compared to its surrounding areas, Cedar Park is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cedar Park is valued at $301,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $21,280 (6%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $84,100 (38%) higher, and is roughly $155,200 (106%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

Cedar Park's median rent is $1,408 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $32 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $346 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is approximately $416 (41%) higher.
