Compared to the surrounding areas, Jonestown is a lot less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Antonio however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Jonestown is $197,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $205,160 (50%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $19,900 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is approximately $51,200 (34%) higher.

Rents

Jonestown's median rent is $979 a month. This is roughly $606 (38%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $83 (7%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $13 (1%) lower compared to the city of San Antonio.
