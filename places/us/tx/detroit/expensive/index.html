Home prices

The median home cost in Detroit is $64,200. This is roughly $16,520 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $153,300 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Longview is roughly $75,400 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Detroit the median monthly rent is $467. This is roughly $311 (39%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $595 (56%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $373 (44%) lower compared to the city of Longview.
