In comparison to its surrounding areas, Detroit is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Longview.

Home prices

Detroit's median home price is $64,200. This is approximately $16,520 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $153,300 (70%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $75,400 (54%) lower compared to the city of Longview.

Rents

In Detroit the median monthly rent is $467. It is approximately $311 (39%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $595 (56%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Longview it is roughly $373 (44%) lower.
