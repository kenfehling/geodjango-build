Home prices

The price of the median home in Wells Branch is $246,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $7,920 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $29,100 (13%) higher, and is roughly $100,200 (68%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in Wells Branch is $1,149 a month. It is approximately $291 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $87 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of San Antonio is about $157 (15%) higher.
