Home prices

The median home in Wells Branch costs $246,600. It is roughly $7,920 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $29,100 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $100,200 (68%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In Wells Branch the median monthly rent is $1,149. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $291 (20%) lower. It is about $87 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $157 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of San Antonio.
