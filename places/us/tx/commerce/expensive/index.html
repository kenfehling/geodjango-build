Home prices

The price of the median home in Commerce is $70,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $4,220 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $146,700 (67%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $117,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Commerce the median monthly rent is $821. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $79 (10%) higher. It is about $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $231 (21%) lower.
