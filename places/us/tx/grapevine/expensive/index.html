Rents

Grapevine's median monthly rent is $1,380. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $65 (4%) lower.It is about $318 (29%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is approximately $328 (31%) higher.
