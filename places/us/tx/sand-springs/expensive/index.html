Home prices

The value of the median home in Sand Springs is $78,900. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $11,740 (12%) lower. It is roughly $138,600 (63%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Midland is roughly $140,600 (64%) lower.

Rents

In Sand Springs the median monthly rent is $825. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $172 (26%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Midland is about $437 (34%) lower.
