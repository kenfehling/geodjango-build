Home prices

The median home in Wharton is valued at $92,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $36,360 (28%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $124,800 (57%) lower, and is about $79,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Houston.

Rents

In Wharton the median rent is $777 per month. This is about $110 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $285 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $264 (25%) lower compared to the city of Houston.
