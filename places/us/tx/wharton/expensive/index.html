Home prices

Wharton's median home cost is $92,700. This is roughly $36,360 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $124,800 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Houston it is roughly $79,100 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Wharton is $777 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $110 (12%) lower. It is roughly $285 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $264 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Houston.
