Home prices

Rents

In Nevada the median monthly rent is $1,088. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $502 (31%) lower. It is roughly $26 (2%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $36 (3%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
