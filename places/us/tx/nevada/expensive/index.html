Home prices

Nevada's median home cost is $249,000. This is about $29,240 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $31,500 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $60,900 (32%) higher.

Rents

Nevada's median rent is $1,088 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $502 (31%) lower.It is roughly $26 (2%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $36 (3%) higher.
