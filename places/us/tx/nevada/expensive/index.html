Home prices

The median home cost in Nevada is $249,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $29,240 (13%) higher. It is approximately $31,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is roughly $60,900 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Nevada the median monthly rent is $1,088. This is about $502 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $26 (2%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $36 (3%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.
