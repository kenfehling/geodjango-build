Home prices

The median home in Nevada is valued at $249,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $29,240 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $31,500 (14%) higher, and it is about $60,900 (32%) higher compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Nevada's median rent is $1,088 a month. It is roughly $502 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $26 (2%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Dallas it is roughly $36 (3%) higher.
