Home prices

The median home in San Marcos costs $171,500. It is about $16,960 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $46,000 (21%) lower, and is approximately $25,100 (17%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

In San Marcos the median rent is $1,064 per month. This is approximately $21 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is is very similar compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $72 (7%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.
