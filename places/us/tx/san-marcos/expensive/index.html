Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Marcos is $171,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $16,960 (10%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $46,000 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $25,100 (17%) higher compared to the city of San Antonio.

Rents

The median rent in San Marcos is $1,064 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $21 (1%) lower. It is is very similar in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Antonio is about $72 (7%) higher.
