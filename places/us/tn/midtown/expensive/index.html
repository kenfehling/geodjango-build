Home prices

Midtown's median home value is $69,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $39,960 (36%) lower. It is approximately $147,600 (67%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $66,400 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Midtown the median monthly rent is $788. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $151 (23%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $57 (6%) lower.
