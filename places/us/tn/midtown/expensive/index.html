Home prices

The median home cost in Midtown is $69,900. This is roughly $39,960 (36%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $147,600 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $66,400 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Midtown is $788 per month. This is approximately $151 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $57 (6%) lower.
