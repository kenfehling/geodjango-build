Home prices

The median home value in Midtown is $69,900. This is roughly $39,960 (36%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $147,600 (67%) lower, and is roughly $66,400 (48%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Midtown the median rent is $788 per month. It is about $151 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $57 (6%) lower.
