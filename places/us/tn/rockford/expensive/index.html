Home prices

Rockford's median home price is $188,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $9,400 (5%) higher.It is approximately $29,400 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $51,800 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Rockford the median rent is $663 per month. This is about $224 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $182 (21%) lower.
