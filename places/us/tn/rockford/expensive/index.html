Home prices

The median home in Rockford is valued at $188,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $9,400 (5%) higher.It is about $29,400 (13%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $51,800 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Rockford is $663 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $224 (25%) lower.It is about $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $182 (21%) lower.
