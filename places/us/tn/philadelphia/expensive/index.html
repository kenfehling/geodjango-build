Home prices

The median home in Philadelphia is valued at $117,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $12,880 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $99,600 (45%) lower, and is about $18,400 (13%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Philadelphia the median rent is $737 a month. It is about $26 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $325 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $108 (12%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
