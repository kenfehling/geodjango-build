Home prices

Philadelphia's median home cost is $117,900. This is about $12,880 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $99,600 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $18,400 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Philadelphia the median rent is $737 per month. It is about $26 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $108 (12%) lower.
