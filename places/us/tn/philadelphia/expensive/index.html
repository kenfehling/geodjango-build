In comparison to other places in this area, Philadelphia is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Philadelphia costs $117,900. It is approximately $12,880 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $99,600 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $18,400 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Philadelphia's median rent is $737 per month. This is approximately $26 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $325 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $108 (12%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
