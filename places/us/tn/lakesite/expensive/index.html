Home prices

The median home price in Lakesite is $215,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $31,160 (16%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is is very similar, and it is approximately $79,400 (58%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Lakesite's median monthly rent is $898. This is approximately $117 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $164 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $53 (6%) higher.
