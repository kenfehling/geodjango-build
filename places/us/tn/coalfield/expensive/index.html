Home prices

Coalfield's median home cost is $127,100. It is approximately $17,200 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $90,400 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $9,200 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Coalfield's median rent is $808 a month. It is about $176 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $254 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $37 (4%) lower.
