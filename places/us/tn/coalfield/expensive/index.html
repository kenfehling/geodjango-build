Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Coalfield is $808 a month. It is roughly $176 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $254 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $37 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
