Home prices

Cookeville's median home price is $166,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $49,700 (42%) higher. It is about $50,800 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Chattanooga.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cookeville is $730. It is about $107 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $332 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chattanooga it is approximately $129 (15%) lower.
