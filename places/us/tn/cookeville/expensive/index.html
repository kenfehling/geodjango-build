Rents

In Cookeville the median rent is $730 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $107 (17%) higher.It is about $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chattanooga it is roughly $129 (15%) lower.
