Compared to other places in this area, Bartlett is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Memphis however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Bartlett is $185,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $28,720 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $32,400 (14%) lower, and is roughly $83,300 (81%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Bartlett's median monthly rent is $1,383. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $218 (18%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $321 (30%) higher, and it is about $482 (53%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
