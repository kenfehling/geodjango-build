In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bartlett is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Memphis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bartlett costs $185,100. It is approximately $28,720 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $32,400 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is approximately $83,300 (81%) higher.

Rents

In Bartlett the median rent is $1,383 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $218 (18%) higher.It is roughly $321 (30%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $482 (53%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
