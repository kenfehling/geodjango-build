Home prices

The median home in Bartlett is valued at $185,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $28,720 (13%) lower. It is roughly $32,400 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is roughly $83,300 (81%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bartlett is $1,383. It is roughly $218 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $321 (30%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Memphis it is roughly $482 (53%) higher.
