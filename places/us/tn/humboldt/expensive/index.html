Compared to its surrounding areas, Humboldt is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home price in Humboldt is $81,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $47,080 (36%) lower. It is approximately $135,600 (62%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $19,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Humboldt is $684 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $195 (22%) lower. It is about $378 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is about $217 (24%) lower.
