In comparison to its surrounding areas, Humboldt is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home cost in Humboldt is $81,900. This is roughly $47,080 (36%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $135,600 (62%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is roughly $19,900 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Humboldt the median monthly rent is $684. This is about $195 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $378 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $217 (24%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
