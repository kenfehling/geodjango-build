Home prices

The median home in Trenton costs $93,400. It is approximately $3,500 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $124,100 (57%) lower, and it is roughly $8,400 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Trenton the median monthly rent is $660. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $66 (9%) lower. It is roughly $402 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is roughly $241 (26%) lower.
