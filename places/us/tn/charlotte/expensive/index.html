Home prices

The median home value in Charlotte is $119,700. This is roughly $45,380 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $97,800 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $34,600 (22%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

The median rent in Charlotte is $703 a month. It is about $119 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $359 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $258 (26%) lower.
