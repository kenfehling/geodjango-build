Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Charlotte is $703. It is approximately $119 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $359 (33%) lower, and is about $258 (26%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
