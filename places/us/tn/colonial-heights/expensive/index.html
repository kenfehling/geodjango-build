Home prices

The median home in Colonial Heights costs $145,400. It is roughly $5,280 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $72,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $9,100 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Colonial Heights is $945. This is about $217 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $117 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $100 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
