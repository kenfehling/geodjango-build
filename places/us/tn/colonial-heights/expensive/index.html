Home prices

The cost of the median home in Colonial Heights is $145,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $5,280 (3%) lower. It is about $72,100 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $9,100 (6%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Colonial Heights's median monthly rent is $945. This is approximately $217 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $117 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $100 (11%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
