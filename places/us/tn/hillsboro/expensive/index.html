Home prices

The median home price in Hillsboro is $136,800. It is roughly $13,380 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $80,700 (37%) lower, and is roughly $46,100 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median rent in Hillsboro is $477 per month. It is roughly $257 (35%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $585 (55%) lower, and it is about $350 (42%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
