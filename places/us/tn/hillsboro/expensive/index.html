Home prices

Rents

Hillsboro's median monthly rent is $477. It is approximately $257 (35%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $585 (55%) lower, and is approximately $350 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
