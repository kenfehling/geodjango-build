Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hillsboro is $136,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $13,380 (10%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $80,700 (37%) lower, and it is about $46,100 (25%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

In Hillsboro the median monthly rent is $477. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $257 (35%) lower.It is approximately $585 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $350 (42%) lower.
