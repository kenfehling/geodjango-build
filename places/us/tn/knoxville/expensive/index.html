In comparison to its surrounding areas, Knoxville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

The median home cost in Knoxville is $136,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $35,000 (20%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $81,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Knoxville is $845. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $72 (9%) higher. It is roughly $217 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation.
