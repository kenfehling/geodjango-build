Home prices

The value of the median home in Ooltewah is $142,400. This is about $39,560 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $75,100 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $6,100 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Ooltewah is $912 a month. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $150 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $67 (7%) higher.
