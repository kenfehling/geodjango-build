Compared to its surrounding areas, Gray is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Asheville however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gray is valued at $171,900. This is approximately $14,880 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $45,600 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville is approximately $98,500 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gray is $905. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $155 (20%) higher. It is roughly $157 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $138 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
