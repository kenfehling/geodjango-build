Compared to its surrounding areas, South Cleveland is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in South Cleveland is valued at $132,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $18,840 (12%) lower.It is roughly $84,600 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $3,400 (2%) lower.

Rents

In South Cleveland the median rent is $811 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $251 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $34 (4%) lower.
