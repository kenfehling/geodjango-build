Home prices

The value of the median home in South Cleveland is $132,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $18,840 (12%) lower. It is roughly $84,600 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $3,400 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In South Cleveland the median monthly rent is $811. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $251 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $34 (4%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
