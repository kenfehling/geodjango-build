The median home price in Elkton is $120,800. This is about $10,480 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $96,700 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is roughly $62,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

Elkton's median rent is $638 a month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is about $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is approximately $189 (22%) lower.
