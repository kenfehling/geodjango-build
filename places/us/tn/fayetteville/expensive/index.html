Home prices

The value of the median home in Fayetteville is $105,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $8,780 (7%) lower. It is approximately $112,100 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $77,500 (42%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

In Fayetteville the median monthly rent is $676. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $49 (6%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $386 (36%) lower, and it is about $151 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
