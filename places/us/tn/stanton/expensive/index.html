Home prices

Rents

Stanton's median monthly rent is $479. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $197 (29%) lower. It is approximately $583 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is roughly $422 (46%) lower.
