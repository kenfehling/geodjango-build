The median home in Gatlinburg is valued at $210,600. This is approximately $7,420 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $6,900 (3%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $74,300 (54%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Gatlinburg the median monthly rent is $792. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $270 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $53 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
