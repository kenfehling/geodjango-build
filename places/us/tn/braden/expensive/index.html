Home prices

Braden's median home price is $164,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,300 (17%) higher. It is about $53,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $62,500 (61%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Braden is $775 per month. This is about $234 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Memphis is approximately $126 (13%) lower.
