Home prices

Braden's median home price is $164,300. It is roughly $24,300 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $53,200 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $62,500 (61%) higher.

Rents

In Braden the median rent is $775 a month. This is approximately $234 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is roughly $126 (13%) lower.
