In comparison to its surrounding areas, Braden is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Braden is valued at $164,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $24,300 (17%) higher. It is about $53,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $62,500 (61%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Braden is $775. This is about $234 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $126 (13%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
