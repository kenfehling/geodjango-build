Home prices

Rents

Riceville's median rent is $799 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $114 (16%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $263 (24%) lower, and is approximately $46 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
