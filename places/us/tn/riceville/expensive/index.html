Home prices

The median home in Riceville is valued at $127,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $17,440 (15%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $89,800 (41%) lower, and is approximately $8,600 (6%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Riceville's median rent is $799 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $114 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $263 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $46 (5%) lower.
