Home prices

The median home cost in Flat Top Mountain is $190,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $22,640 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $27,200 (12%) lower, and is roughly $54,000 (39%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Flat Top Mountain the median monthly rent is $833. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $88 (11%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $229 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $12 (1%) lower.
