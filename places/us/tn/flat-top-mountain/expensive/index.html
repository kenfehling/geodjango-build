In comparison to its surrounding areas, Flat Top Mountain is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Flat Top Mountain is $190,300. It is roughly $22,640 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $27,200 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $54,000 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Flat Top Mountain is $833 per month. This is about $88 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $229 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $12 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
