Rents

Auburntown's median monthly rent is $775. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $141 (22%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and is about $52 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
