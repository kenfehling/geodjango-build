Home prices

The price of the median home in Pegram is $231,300. It is about $367,860 (61%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $13,800 (6%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $77,000 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pegram is $1,115. This is approximately $656 (37%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $53 (4%) higher, and is about $154 (16%) higher compared to the city of Clarksville.
