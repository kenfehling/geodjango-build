Home prices

Pegram's median home price is $231,300. This is roughly $367,860 (61%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $13,800 (6%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $77,000 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pegram is $1,115. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $656 (37%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $53 (4%) higher, and is approximately $154 (16%) higher compared to the city of Clarksville.
