Home prices

The median home in Woodbury costs $145,000. This is about $25,680 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $72,500 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $37,900 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

In Woodbury the median monthly rent is $617. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $38 (5%) lower. It is about $445 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is about $210 (25%) lower.
