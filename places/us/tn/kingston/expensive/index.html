Home prices

The median home cost in Kingston is $167,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $77,280 (85%) higher.It is approximately $49,900 (22%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $31,300 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Kingston the median monthly rent is $728. It is approximately $79 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $334 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $117 (13%) lower.
