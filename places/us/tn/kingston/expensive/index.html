In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kingston is substantially more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kingston is valued at $167,600. It is approximately $77,280 (85%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $49,900 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $31,300 (22%) higher.

Rents

Kingston's median rent is $728 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $79 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $334 (31%) lower, and is about $117 (13%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
