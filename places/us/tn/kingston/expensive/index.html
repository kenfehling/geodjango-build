Home prices

The median home in Kingston costs $167,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $77,280 (85%) higher.It is approximately $49,900 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $31,300 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kingston is $728. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $79 (12%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $334 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $117 (13%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
