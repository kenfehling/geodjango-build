Home prices

Kingston's median home price is $167,600. It is roughly $77,280 (85%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $49,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $31,300 (22%) higher.

Rents

Kingston's median monthly rent is $728. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $79 (12%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $334 (31%) lower, and is roughly $117 (13%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
