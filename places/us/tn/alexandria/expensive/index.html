Home prices

Rents

In Alexandria the median monthly rent is $439. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $274 (38%) lower. It is approximately $623 (58%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chattanooga is approximately $420 (48%) lower.
