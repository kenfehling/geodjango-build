Home prices

The median home in Alexandria is valued at $115,500. This is approximately $15,940 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $102,000 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Chattanooga is roughly $52,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alexandria is $439. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $274 (38%) lower. It is approximately $623 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chattanooga it is about $420 (48%) lower.
