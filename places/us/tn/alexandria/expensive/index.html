In comparison to its surrounding areas, Alexandria is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Chattanooga.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Alexandria is $115,500. It is approximately $15,940 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $102,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $52,000 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Chattanooga.

Rents

Alexandria's median monthly rent is $439. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $274 (38%) lower. It is roughly $623 (58%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chattanooga is roughly $420 (48%) lower.
