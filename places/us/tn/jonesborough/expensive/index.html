Home prices

The median home price in Jonesborough is $169,000. It is about $16,080 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $48,500 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $32,700 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Jonesborough is $952 per month. This is roughly $212 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $110 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $107 (12%) higher.
