Home prices

Rents

Jonesborough's median rent is $952 a month. It is approximately $212 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $110 (10%) lower, and it is approximately $107 (12%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
