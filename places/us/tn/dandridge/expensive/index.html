Home prices

Rents

In Dandridge the median rent is $832 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $133 (18%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $230 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $13 (1%) lower.
