Home prices

The median home cost in Murfreesboro is $238,000. This is approximately $10,220 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $20,500 (9%) higher, and is approximately $55,100 (30%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median rent in Murfreesboro is $1,052 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $323 (23%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $225 (27%) higher.
