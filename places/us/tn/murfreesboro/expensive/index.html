Home prices

The median home in Murfreesboro costs $238,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $10,220 (4%) lower. It is approximately $20,500 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $55,100 (30%) higher.

Rents

In Murfreesboro the median rent is $1,052 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $323 (23%) lower. It is is very similar in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $225 (27%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.
