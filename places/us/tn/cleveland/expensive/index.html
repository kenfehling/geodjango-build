Compared to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Cleveland is $171,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $43,660 (34%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $46,000 (21%) lower, and is approximately $35,200 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $768 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $69 (9%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $294 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $77 (9%) lower.
