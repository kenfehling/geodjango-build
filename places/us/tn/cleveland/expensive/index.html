In comparison to its nearby areas, Cleveland is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $171,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $43,660 (34%) higher.It is roughly $46,000 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $35,200 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $768 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $69 (9%) higher.It is about $294 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $77 (9%) lower.
