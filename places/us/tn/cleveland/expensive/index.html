Compared to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxvillehowever, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Cleveland's median home value is $171,500. This is approximately $43,660 (34%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $46,000 (21%) lower, and it is about $35,200 (25%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Cleveland's median monthly rent is $768. This is roughly $69 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $294 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $77 (9%) lower.
