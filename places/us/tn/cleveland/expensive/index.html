Home prices

Cleveland's median home value is $171,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $43,660 (34%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $46,000 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $35,200 (25%) higher.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $768. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $69 (9%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $294 (27%) lower, and is roughly $77 (9%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
