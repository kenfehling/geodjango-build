Home prices

The value of the median home in Smithville is $125,300. It is about $3,120 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $92,200 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Chattanooga it is approximately $42,200 (25%) lower.

Rents

Smithville's median rent is $632 per month. It is approximately $13 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $430 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $227 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Chattanooga.
