Home prices

Chattanooga's median home price is $167,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $2,740 (1%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $50,000 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $15,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

Chattanooga's median monthly rent is $859. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $40 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $203 (19%) lower, and is roughly $32 (3%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.
