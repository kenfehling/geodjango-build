Home prices

The median home in Plainview costs $120,700. This is approximately $1,820 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $96,800 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $15,600 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Plainview the median monthly rent is $758. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $60 (8%) higher. It is roughly $304 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $87 (10%) lower.
