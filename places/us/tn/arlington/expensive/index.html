In comparison to its surrounding areas, Arlington is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Arlington costs $238,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $57,480 (31%) higher.It is about $21,000 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $136,700 (134%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Arlington the median rent is $1,801 a month. It is about $729 (68%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $739 (69%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is roughly $900 (99%) higher.
