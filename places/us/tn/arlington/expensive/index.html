Home prices

The median home in Arlington costs $238,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $57,480 (31%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $21,000 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $136,700 (134%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Arlington is $1,801. It is about $729 (68%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $739 (69%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $900 (99%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
