Home prices

The median home in Springfield is valued at $154,800. This is roughly $33,340 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $62,700 (28%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Springfield is $835. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $122 (12%) lower. It is approximately $227 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is roughly $126 (13%) lower.
