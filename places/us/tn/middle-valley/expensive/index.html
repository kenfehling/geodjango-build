In comparison to other places in this area, Middle Valley is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Middle Valley is $171,500. This is about $61,460 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $46,000 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $35,200 (25%) higher.

Rents

Middle Valley's median monthly rent is $877. It is approximately $27 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $185 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $32 (3%) higher.
