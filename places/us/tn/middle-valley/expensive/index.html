Home prices

The median home in Middle Valley costs $171,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $61,460 (26%) lower.It is roughly $46,000 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $35,200 (25%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Middle Valley the median rent is $877 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $27 (3%) lower.It is approximately $185 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $32 (3%) higher.
