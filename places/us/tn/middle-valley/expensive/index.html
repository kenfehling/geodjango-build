Home prices

The median home value in Middle Valley is $171,500. This is roughly $61,460 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $46,000 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $35,200 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Middle Valley's median monthly rent is $877. This is about $27 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $185 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $32 (3%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
