Much like nearby areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home cost in Sevierville is $188,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $4,680 (2%) lower. It is about $29,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $52,000 (38%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sevierville is $772 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $31 (3%) lower. It is roughly $290 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $73 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
