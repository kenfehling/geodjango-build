Similar to its nearby areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sevierville costs $188,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $4,680 (2%) lower.It is about $29,200 (13%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $52,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Sevierville is $772 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $31 (3%) lower.It is roughly $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $73 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
