Similar to its nearby areas, it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sevierville is $188,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $4,680 (2%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $29,200 (13%) lower, and it is about $52,000 (38%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Sevierville is $772 a month. It is roughly $31 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $73 (8%) lower.
