Much like its nearby areas, it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Sevierville is $188,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $4,680 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $29,200 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $52,000 (38%) higher.

Rents

In Sevierville the median monthly rent is $772. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $31 (3%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $290 (27%) lower, and is roughly $73 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
