Home prices

The median home in Altamont costs $73,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $27,780 (27%) lower.It is about $143,700 (66%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $109,100 (59%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

In Altamont the median monthly rent is $618. It is roughly $75 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $444 (41%) lower, and is approximately $209 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
