In comparison to other places in this area, Harrogate is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Harrogate is valued at $142,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $30,720 (27%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $74,600 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $6,600 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrogate is $724. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $117 (19%) higher.It is about $338 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $121 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
