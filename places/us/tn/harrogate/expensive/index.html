Compared to its surrounding areas, Harrogate is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxvillehowever, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Harrogate is $142,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $30,720 (27%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $74,600 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $6,600 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Harrogate is $724 per month. It is roughly $117 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $338 (31%) lower, and it is about $121 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
