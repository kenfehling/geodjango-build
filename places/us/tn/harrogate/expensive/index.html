In comparison to nearby areas, Harrogate is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Harrogate is valued at $142,900. It is approximately $30,720 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $74,600 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $6,600 (4%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Harrogate the median rent is $724 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $117 (19%) higher. It is about $338 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $121 (14%) lower.
