Home prices

The median home in Somerville costs $164,200. It is about $50,080 (43%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $53,300 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $62,400 (61%) higher.

Rents

In Somerville the median rent is $548 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $349 (38%) lower. It is about $514 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is approximately $353 (39%) lower.
