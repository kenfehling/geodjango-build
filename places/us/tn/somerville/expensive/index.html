Home prices

Somerville's median home cost is $164,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $50,080 (43%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $53,300 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $62,400 (61%) higher.

Rents

In Somerville the median rent is $548 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $349 (38%) lower. It is roughly $514 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis is approximately $353 (39%) lower.
