Compared to other places in this area, Waynesboro is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Waynesboro is $86,900. It is about $7,580 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $130,600 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $96,000 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waynesboro is $575. This is roughly $43 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $252 (30%) lower.
