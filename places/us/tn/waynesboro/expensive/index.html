Compared to the surrounding areas, Waynesboro is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Waynesboro is valued at $86,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $7,580 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $130,600 (60%) lower, and it is approximately $96,000 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waynesboro is $575. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $43 (8%) higher. It is about $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $252 (30%) lower.
