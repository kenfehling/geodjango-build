Home prices

The median home value in Mount Carmel is $151,600. This is about $31,540 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $65,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $15,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Carmel the median rent is $682 a month. It is approximately $100 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $380 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $163 (19%) lower.
