Home prices

Mount Carmel's median home cost is $151,600. This is roughly $31,540 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $65,900 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $15,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Carmel is $682 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $100 (17%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $380 (35%) lower, and is roughly $163 (19%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
