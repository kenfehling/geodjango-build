Compared to other places in this area, Mount Carmel is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mount Carmel is $151,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $31,540 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $65,900 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $15,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Carmel the median monthly rent is $682. This is roughly $100 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $380 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $163 (19%) lower.
