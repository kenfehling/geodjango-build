In comparison to its surrounding areas, Alcoa is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Alcoa is $137,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $50,960 (26%) lower. It is about $79,700 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $1,500 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alcoa is $796. This is approximately $64 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $266 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $49 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
