Home prices

The price of the median home in Alcoa is $137,800. It is approximately $50,960 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $79,700 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $1,500 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Alcoa the median rent is $796 per month. It is roughly $64 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $266 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $49 (5%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
