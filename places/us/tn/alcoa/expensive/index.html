Home prices

Alcoa's median home value is $137,800. This is about $50,960 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $79,700 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $1,500 (1%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alcoa is $796. This is roughly $64 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $266 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $49 (5%) lower.
