Home prices

The median home in Savannah is valued at $115,000. It is roughly $30,800 (36%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $102,500 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $18,200 (13%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

Savannah's median monthly rent is $589. It is roughly $16 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $473 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is approximately $288 (32%) lower.
