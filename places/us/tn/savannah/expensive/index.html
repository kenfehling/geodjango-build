Home prices

The median home in Savannah costs $115,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30,800 (36%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $102,500 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $18,200 (13%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.

Rents

The median rent in Savannah is $589 per month. This is approximately $16 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $473 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $288 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
