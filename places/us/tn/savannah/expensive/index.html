Home prices

The cost of the median home in Savannah is $115,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $30,800 (36%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $102,500 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Jackson is roughly $18,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

Savannah's median rent is $589 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $16 (2%) higher. It is about $473 (44%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is about $288 (32%) lower.
