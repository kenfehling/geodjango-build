Home prices

The median home in Henderson is valued at $109,300. This is about $23,080 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $108,200 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $7,500 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Henderson is $630 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $58 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $432 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $271 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
