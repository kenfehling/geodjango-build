Home prices

Henderson's median home cost is $109,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $23,080 (26%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $108,200 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $7,500 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Henderson's median rent is $630 a month. This is roughly $58 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $432 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $271 (30%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
