Home prices

Rents

Livingston's median monthly rent is $495. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $26 (5%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $567 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $350 (41%) lower.
