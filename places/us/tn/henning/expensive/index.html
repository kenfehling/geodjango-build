Home prices

The price of the median home in Henning is $67,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $37,340 (35%) lower. It is about $149,700 (68%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $34,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Henning the median monthly rent is $543. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $131 (19%) lower. It is roughly $519 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $358 (39%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
