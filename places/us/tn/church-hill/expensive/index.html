In comparison to other places in this area, Church Hill is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Church Hill is valued at $142,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $7,320 (5%) higher. It is about $74,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $6,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

Church Hill's median rent is $593 per month. This is about $53 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $469 (44%) lower, and is about $252 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
