In comparison to other places in this area, Church Hill is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's close to average.

Home prices

Church Hill's median home value is $142,600. This is about $7,320 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $74,900 (34%) lower, and is about $6,300 (4%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Church Hill's median rent is $593 per month. It is roughly $53 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $469 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $252 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
