In comparison to the surrounding areas, Church Hill is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Church Hill is $142,600. This is about $7,320 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $74,900 (34%) lower, and is about $6,300 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Church Hill the median monthly rent is $593. This is roughly $53 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $469 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $252 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
