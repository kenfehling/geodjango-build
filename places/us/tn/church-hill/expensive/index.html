In comparison to the surrounding areas, Church Hill is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

Church Hill's median home value is $142,600. Compared to nearby areas this is about $7,320 (5%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $74,900 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $6,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

Church Hill's median rent is $593 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $53 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $469 (44%) lower, and it is about $252 (29%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
