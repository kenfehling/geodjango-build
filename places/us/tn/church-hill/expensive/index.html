In comparison to its surrounding areas, Church Hill is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it is close to average.

Home prices

Church Hill's median home price is $142,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $7,320 (5%) higher.It is approximately $74,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $6,300 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Church Hill the median rent is $593 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $53 (8%) lower.It is about $469 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $252 (29%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
