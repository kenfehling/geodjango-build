Compared to other places in this area, Church Hill is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Church Hill is $142,600. It is about $7,320 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $74,900 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $6,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Church Hill is $593. It is about $53 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $469 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $252 (29%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
