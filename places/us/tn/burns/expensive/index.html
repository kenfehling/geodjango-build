Home prices

The median home value in Burns is $172,100. This is about $6,820 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $45,400 (20%) lower, and is approximately $17,800 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

In Burns the median monthly rent is $906. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $58 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $156 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $55 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.
