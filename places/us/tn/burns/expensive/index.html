Home prices

The median home value in Burns is $172,100. This is roughly $6,820 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $45,400 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $17,800 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

In Burns the median rent is $906 a month. This is approximately $58 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $156 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $55 (5%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
