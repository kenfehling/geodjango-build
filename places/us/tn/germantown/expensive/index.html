In comparison to the surrounding areas, Germantown is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Germantown is $331,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $62,300 (23%) higher.It is about $114,200 (52%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Memphis it is roughly $229,900 (225%) higher.

Rents

Germantown's median monthly rent is $1,387. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $364 (35%) higher.It is roughly $325 (30%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $486 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
