Home prices

Germantown's median home cost is $331,700. It is roughly $62,300 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $114,200 (52%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $229,900 (225%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Germantown the median rent is $1,387 a month. This is roughly $364 (35%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $325 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of Memphis is approximately $486 (53%) higher.
