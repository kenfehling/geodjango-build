Home prices

Middleton's median home cost is $93,100. This is about $18,060 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $124,400 (57%) lower, and is roughly $8,700 (8%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Middleton's median rent is $726 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $62 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $336 (31%) lower, and is about $175 (19%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
