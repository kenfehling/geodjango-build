Home prices

The cost of the median home in Middleton is $93,100. This is approximately $18,060 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $124,400 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $8,700 (8%) lower.

Rents

Middleton's median rent is $726 a month. This is roughly $62 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $336 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Memphis is roughly $175 (19%) lower.
