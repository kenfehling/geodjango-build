In comparison to other places in this area, Wildwood Lake is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Wildwood Lake's median home cost is $146,300. This is approximately $24,520 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $71,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $10,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Wildwood Lake the median monthly rent is $709. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $94 (11%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $353 (33%) lower, and is approximately $136 (16%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
