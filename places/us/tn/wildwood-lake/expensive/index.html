Home prices

Wildwood Lake's median home cost is $146,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $24,520 (14%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $71,200 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $10,000 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Wildwood Lake the median monthly rent is $709. This is approximately $94 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $353 (33%) lower, and it is about $136 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
