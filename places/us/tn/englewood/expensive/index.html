Home prices

The median home cost in Englewood is $86,500. This is roughly $25,780 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $131,000 (60%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $49,800 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Englewood the median rent is $611 per month. It is about $24 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $451 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $234 (27%) lower.
