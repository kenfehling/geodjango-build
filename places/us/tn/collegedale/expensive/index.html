Home prices

The median home in Collegedale is valued at $229,000. This is roughly $67,280 (41%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $11,500 (5%) higher, and it is roughly $92,700 (68%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Collegedale's median monthly rent is $986. This is about $226 (29%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $76 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $141 (16%) higher.
