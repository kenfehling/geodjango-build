In comparison to the surrounding areas, Maryville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Knoxvillehowever, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maryville is valued at $204,500. This is about $29,080 (16%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $13,000 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $68,200 (50%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Maryville is $881 per month. This is approximately $38 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $181 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $36 (4%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
