In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maryville is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maryville is valued at $204,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $29,080 (16%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $13,000 (5%) lower, and it is about $68,200 (50%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Maryville's median monthly rent is $881. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $38 (4%) higher.It is roughly $181 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $36 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
