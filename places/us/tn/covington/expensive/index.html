Home prices

The median home value in Covington is $111,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $7,720 (6%) lower.It is roughly $105,700 (48%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $10,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Covington's median monthly rent is $703. Compared to other places in this area it is about $11 (1%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $359 (33%) lower, and is about $198 (21%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
