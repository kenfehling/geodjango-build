In comparison to its nearby areas, Covington is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Memphis, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Covington is $111,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $7,720 (6%) lower. It is about $105,700 (48%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $10,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Covington's median monthly rent is $703. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $11 (1%) lower. It is about $359 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $198 (21%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
