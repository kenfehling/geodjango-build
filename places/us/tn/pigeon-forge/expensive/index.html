Compared to the surrounding areas, Pigeon Forge is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pigeon Forge is $171,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $39,380 (18%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $45,900 (21%) lower, and is approximately $35,300 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pigeon Forge is $755. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $40 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $307 (28%) lower, and it is about $90 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
