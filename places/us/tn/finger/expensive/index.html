Home prices

The median home in Finger costs $89,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $5,820 (6%) higher. It is approximately $128,300 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $12,600 (12%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Finger is $728 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $132 (22%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $334 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $173 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
