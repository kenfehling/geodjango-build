Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dyersburg is $664. Compared to nearby areas it is about $32 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $398 (37%) lower, and is about $237 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
