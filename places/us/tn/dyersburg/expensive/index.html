Home prices

The median home in Dyersburg is valued at $105,800. It is about $38,140 (56%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $111,700 (51%) lower, and it is about $4,000 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Dyersburg's median rent is $664 a month. It is roughly $32 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is about $398 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $237 (26%) lower.
