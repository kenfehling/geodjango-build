Rents

Dyersburg's median rent is $664 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $32 (5%) higher. It is approximately $398 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $237 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
