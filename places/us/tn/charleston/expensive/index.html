In comparison to other places in this area, Charleston is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Charleston is $122,400. This is approximately $13,120 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $95,100 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $13,900 (10%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Charleston is $688 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $27 (3%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $374 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $157 (18%) lower.
