Home prices

The median home in Charleston costs $122,400. It is approximately $13,120 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $95,100 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $13,900 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Charleston is $688. It is approximately $27 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $374 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $157 (18%) lower.
