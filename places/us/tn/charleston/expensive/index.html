Compared to its surrounding areas, Charleston is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Charleston is valued at $122,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $13,120 (9%) lower. It is about $95,100 (43%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $13,900 (10%) lower.

Rents

Charleston's median rent is $688 a month. This is roughly $27 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $374 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $157 (18%) lower.
