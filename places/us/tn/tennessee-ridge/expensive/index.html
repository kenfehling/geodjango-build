Home prices

Tennessee Ridge's median home value is $101,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $5,700 (5%) lower. It is roughly $116,000 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $52,800 (34%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

The median rent in Tennessee Ridge is $696 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $83 (13%) higher. It is about $366 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is roughly $265 (27%) lower.
