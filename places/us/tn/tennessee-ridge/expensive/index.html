Home prices

The median home in Tennessee Ridge is valued at $101,500. It is approximately $5,700 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $116,000 (53%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is roughly $52,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tennessee Ridge is $696. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $83 (13%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $366 (34%) lower, and it is about $265 (27%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
