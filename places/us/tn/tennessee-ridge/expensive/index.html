Home prices

Tennessee Ridge's median home cost is $101,500. This is approximately $5,700 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $116,000 (53%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is roughly $52,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tennessee Ridge is $696 per month. This is about $83 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $366 (34%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $265 (27%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
