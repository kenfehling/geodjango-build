Home prices

The median home value in Yorkville is $70,500. This is approximately $1,420 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $147,000 (67%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $31,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

Yorkville's median monthly rent is $700. This is roughly $52 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $362 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $201 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
