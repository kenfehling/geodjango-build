In comparison to nearby areas, Brighton is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Memphis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Brighton is $135,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $15,580 (10%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $81,600 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $34,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

Brighton's median monthly rent is $986. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $162 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $76 (7%) lower, and is roughly $85 (9%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
