Home prices

The median home price in Brighton is $135,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $15,580 (10%) lower.It is about $81,600 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $34,100 (33%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Brighton's median rent is $986 per month. This is about $162 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $76 (7%) lower, and is about $85 (9%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
