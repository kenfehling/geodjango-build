In comparison to its surrounding areas, Brighton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Memphis however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Brighton is $135,900. This is roughly $15,580 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $81,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $34,100 (33%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Brighton's median rent is $986 per month. This is approximately $162 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $76 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $85 (9%) higher.
