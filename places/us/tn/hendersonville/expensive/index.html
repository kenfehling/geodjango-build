In comparison to its nearby areas, Hendersonville is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Clarksville, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hendersonville is $266,100. It is roughly $37,140 (16%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $48,600 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is about $111,800 (72%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hendersonville is $1,173 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $54 (4%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $111 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is roughly $212 (22%) higher.
