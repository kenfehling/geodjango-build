In comparison to its nearby areas, Hendersonville is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Clarksville.

Home prices

The median home value in Hendersonville is $266,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $37,140 (16%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $48,600 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is about $111,800 (72%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hendersonville is $1,173 a month. It is about $54 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $111 (10%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $212 (22%) higher.
