Home prices

The median home in Spring City is valued at $93,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $9,560 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $123,900 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $42,700 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Spring City the median monthly rent is $524. It is about $127 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $538 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $321 (37%) lower.
