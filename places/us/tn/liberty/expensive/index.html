Home prices

The value of the median home in Liberty is $135,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $14,440 (11%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $81,800 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chattanooga it is roughly $31,800 (18%) lower.

Rents

Liberty's median rent is $688 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $34 (5%) higher. It is roughly $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chattanooga it is about $171 (19%) lower.
