In comparison to other places in this area, Liberty is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chattanooga, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Liberty costs $135,700. This is approximately $14,440 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $81,800 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Chattanooga it is roughly $31,800 (18%) lower.

Rents

Liberty's median rent is $688 per month. This is approximately $34 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $374 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chattanooga it is about $171 (19%) lower.
