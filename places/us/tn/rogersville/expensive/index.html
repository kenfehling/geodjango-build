Home prices

Rogersville's median home value is $140,500. It is approximately $29,620 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $77,000 (35%) lower, and is approximately $4,200 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rogersville is $595. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $38 (6%) higher.It is about $467 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $250 (29%) lower.
