In comparison to other places in this area, Rogersville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Rogersville is $140,500. This is roughly $29,620 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $77,000 (35%) lower, and is roughly $4,200 (3%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rogersville is $595. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38 (6%) higher. It is approximately $467 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $250 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
