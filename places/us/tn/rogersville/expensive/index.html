Home prices

The median home in Rogersville costs $140,500. This is roughly $29,620 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $77,000 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $4,200 (3%) higher.

Rents

Rogersville's median rent is $595 per month. This is roughly $38 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $467 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $250 (29%) lower.
