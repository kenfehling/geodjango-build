In comparison to other places in this area, Manchester is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Manchester is valued at $129,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $15,440 (13%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $87,800 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $53,200 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Manchester is $709 per month. It is about $9 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is about $118 (14%) lower.
