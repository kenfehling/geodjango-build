Compared to its surrounding areas, Manchester is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Huntsville, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Manchester is $129,700. It is roughly $15,440 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $87,800 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $53,200 (29%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median rent in Manchester is $709 a month. This is approximately $9 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is about $118 (14%) lower.
