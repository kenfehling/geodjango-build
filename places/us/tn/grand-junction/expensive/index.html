Home prices

The price of the median home in Grand Junction is $83,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $4,540 (5%) lower. It is approximately $134,000 (61%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $18,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Grand Junction's median rent is $933 per month. It is about $160 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $129 (12%) lower, and is roughly $32 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
