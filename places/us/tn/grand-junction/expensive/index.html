Home prices

The value of the median home in Grand Junction is $83,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $4,540 (5%) lower. It is roughly $134,000 (61%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $18,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Grand Junction the median monthly rent is $933. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $160 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $129 (12%) lower, and is about $32 (3%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
