Home prices

The median home in Grand Junction costs $83,500. This is about $4,540 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $134,000 (61%) lower, and it is about $18,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Grand Junction the median monthly rent is $933. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $160 (20%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $129 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $32 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
