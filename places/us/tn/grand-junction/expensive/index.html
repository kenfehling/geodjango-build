Compared to the surrounding areas, Grand Junction is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Memphis, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Grand Junction's median home value is $83,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $4,540 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $134,000 (61%) lower, and is approximately $18,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Grand Junction's median monthly rent is $933. It is roughly $160 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $129 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $32 (3%) higher.
