Compared to nearby areas, Oneida is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home price in Oneida is $110,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $25,040 (29%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $107,300 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $26,100 (19%) lower.

Rents

Oneida's median monthly rent is $470. This is about $49 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $592 (55%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $375 (44%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
