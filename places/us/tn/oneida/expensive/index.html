Home prices

The median home price in Oneida is $110,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $25,040 (29%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $107,300 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $26,100 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oneida is $470. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $49 (9%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $592 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $375 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
