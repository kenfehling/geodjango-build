Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oneida is $110,200. This is roughly $25,040 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $107,300 (49%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $26,100 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Oneida the median rent is $470 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $49 (9%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $592 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $375 (44%) lower.
