Home prices

The median home in Kenton is valued at $83,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $13,680 (19%) higher. It is approximately $134,500 (61%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $18,800 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Kenton the median rent is $625 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $7 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $276 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
