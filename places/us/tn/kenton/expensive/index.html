Home prices

Kenton's median home price is $83,000. It is about $13,680 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $134,500 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is about $18,800 (18%) lower.

Rents

Kenton's median monthly rent is $625. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $7 (1%) lower. It is approximately $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $276 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
