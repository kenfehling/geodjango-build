Home prices

The median home in Greeneville costs $129,000. This is roughly $8,800 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $88,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $7,300 (5%) lower.

Rents

Greeneville's median rent is $587 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $53 (8%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $475 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $258 (30%) lower.
