Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jasper is $137,800. It is roughly $38,720 (39%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $79,700 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $45,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jasper is $746. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $160 (27%) higher. It is about $316 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $81 (9%) lower.
