In comparison to its surrounding areas, Central is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The median home in Central is valued at $126,200. It is approximately $9,560 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $91,300 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $144,200 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Central's median rent is $588 a month. This is roughly $134 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $474 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is about $455 (43%) lower.
