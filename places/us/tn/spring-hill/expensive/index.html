In comparison to its surrounding areas, Spring Hill is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Spring Hill is $286,800. This is approximately $20,120 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $69,300 (31%) higher, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $103,900 (56%) higher.

Rents

In Spring Hill the median rent is $1,401 a month. This is about $132 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $339 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is about $574 (69%) higher.
