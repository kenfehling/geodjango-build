Home prices

Rents

In Spring Hill the median rent is $1,401 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $132 (10%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $339 (31%) higher, and is about $574 (69%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.
