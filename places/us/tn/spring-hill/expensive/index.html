Home prices

Rents

In Spring Hill the median monthly rent is $1,401. It is about $132 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $339 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $574 (69%) higher.
