Home prices

The median home in Spring Hill is valued at $286,800. This is approximately $20,120 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $69,300 (31%) higher, and it is roughly $103,900 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Spring Hill's median rent is $1,401 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $132 (10%) higher. It is roughly $339 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $574 (69%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.
