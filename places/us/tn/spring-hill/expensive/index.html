Home prices

Rents

Spring Hill's median rent is $1,401 a month. It is approximately $132 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $339 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $574 (69%) higher.
