Home prices

The median home cost in Jackson is $133,200. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $10,600 (8%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $84,300 (38%) lower, and is about $31,400 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Jackson the median rent is $877 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $14 (1%) higher. It is approximately $185 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $24 (2%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
