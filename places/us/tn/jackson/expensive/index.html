Home prices

The median home in Jackson is valued at $133,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $10,600 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $84,300 (38%) lower, and is approximately $31,400 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Jackson's median rent is $877 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $14 (1%) higher. It is about $185 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $24 (2%) lower.
