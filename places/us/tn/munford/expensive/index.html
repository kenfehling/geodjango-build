In comparison to the surrounding areas, Munford is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Munford is valued at $168,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $24,720 (17%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $49,200 (22%) lower, and is approximately $66,500 (65%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Munford is $893 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $10 (1%) lower. It is about $169 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Memphis.
