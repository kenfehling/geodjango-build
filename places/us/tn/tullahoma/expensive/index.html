In comparison to other places in this area, Tullahoma is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tullahoma is $146,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $21,220 (16%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $71,000 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $36,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Tullahoma the median monthly rent is $758. This is about $77 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $304 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is roughly $69 (8%) lower.
