Home prices

Tullahoma's median home price is $146,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $21,220 (16%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $71,000 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is approximately $36,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Tullahoma the median rent is $758 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $77 (9%) lower.It is approximately $304 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $69 (8%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
