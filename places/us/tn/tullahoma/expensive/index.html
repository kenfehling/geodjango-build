Compared to the surrounding areas, Tullahoma is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Huntsville however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tullahoma is $146,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $21,220 (16%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $71,000 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $36,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Tullahoma the median rent is $758 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $77 (9%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $304 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $69 (8%) lower.
