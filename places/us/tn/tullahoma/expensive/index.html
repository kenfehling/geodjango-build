Compared to its nearby areas, Tullahoma is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home value in Tullahoma is $146,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $21,220 (16%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $71,000 (32%) lower, and is roughly $36,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tullahoma is $758. This is about $77 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $304 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $69 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
