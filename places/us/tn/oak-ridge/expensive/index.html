Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oak Ridge is $155,800. It is roughly $26,180 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $61,700 (28%) lower, and is approximately $19,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Oak Ridge's median rent is $806 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $34 (4%) lower.It is approximately $256 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $39 (4%) lower.
