Compared to the surrounding areas, Oak Ridge is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home price in Oak Ridge is $155,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $26,180 (14%) lower. It is roughly $61,700 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $19,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oak Ridge is $806. This is about $34 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $256 (24%) lower, and is about $39 (4%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
