Home prices

The median home in Shelbyville is valued at $124,500. This is approximately $5,060 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $93,000 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $58,400 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Shelbyville is $768. It is approximately $64 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $294 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $59 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
