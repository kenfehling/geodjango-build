Home prices

Rents

Shelbyville's median monthly rent is $768. It is approximately $64 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $294 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $59 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
