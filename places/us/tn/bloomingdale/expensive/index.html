In comparison to other places in this area, Bloomingdale is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

Bloomingdale's median home price is $110,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $18,940 (14%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $107,500 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $160,400 (59%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Bloomingdale the median monthly rent is $585. This is about $86 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $477 (44%) lower, and it is about $458 (43%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
