Home prices

The median home value in Bloomingdale is $110,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $18,940 (14%) lower. It is roughly $107,500 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $160,400 (59%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomingdale is $585. This is approximately $86 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $477 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville it is about $458 (43%) lower.
