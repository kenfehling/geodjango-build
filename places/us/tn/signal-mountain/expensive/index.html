Home prices

Rents

Signal Mountain's median rent is $1,938 per month. This is roughly $1,007 (108%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $876 (82%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $1,111 (134%) higher.
