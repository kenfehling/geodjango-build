Home prices

The median home in Signal Mountain costs $345,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $77,420 (28%) higher. It is approximately $128,400 (59%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Huntsville is roughly $163,000 (89%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Signal Mountain is $1,938. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $1,007 (108%) higher. It is roughly $876 (82%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $1,111 (134%) higher in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
