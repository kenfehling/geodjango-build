Compared to the surrounding areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Clarksville.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $144,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $22,740 (13%) lower. It is roughly $73,000 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $9,800 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Portland the median rent is $850 per month. This is approximately $16 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $212 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $111 (11%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
