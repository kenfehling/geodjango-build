In comparison to the surrounding areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Clarksville.

Home prices

The median home in Portland is valued at $144,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $22,740 (13%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $73,000 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $9,800 (6%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $850. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $16 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $111 (11%) lower.
