Compared to the surrounding areas, Portland is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Clarksville.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $144,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,740 (13%) lower.It is approximately $73,000 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is about $9,800 (6%) lower.

Rents

Portland's median rent is $850 per month. It is roughly $16 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $212 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $111 (11%) lower.
