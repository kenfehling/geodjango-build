Home prices

The median home in Mosheim is valued at $107,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $17,360 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $110,300 (50%) lower, and is roughly $29,100 (21%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mosheim is $663. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38 (6%) higher. It is approximately $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $182 (21%) lower.
