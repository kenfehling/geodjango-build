Home prices

Mosheim's median home cost is $107,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $17,360 (13%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $110,300 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $29,100 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Mosheim the median rent is $663 per month. This is approximately $38 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $399 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $182 (21%) lower.
