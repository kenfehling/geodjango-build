In comparison to other places in this area, Etowah is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Etowah's median home cost is $98,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $11,760 (10%) lower. It is about $118,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $37,700 (27%) lower.

Rents

Etowah's median monthly rent is $572. Compared to nearby areas it is about $98 (14%) lower. It is about $490 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $273 (32%) lower.
