Compared to nearby areas, Etowah is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Etowah's median home price is $98,600. This is roughly $11,760 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $118,900 (54%) lower, and is roughly $37,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Etowah the median monthly rent is $572. It is about $98 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $490 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $273 (32%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
