Madisonville's median home value is $136,000. This is about $24,460 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $81,500 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Madisonville is $618 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $56 (8%) lower. It is roughly $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $227 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
