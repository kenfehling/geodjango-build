Home prices

The cost of the median home in Madisonville is $136,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $24,460 (21%) higher. It is roughly $81,500 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is is very similar.

Rents

In Madisonville the median rent is $618 a month. This is roughly $56 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $444 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $227 (26%) lower.
