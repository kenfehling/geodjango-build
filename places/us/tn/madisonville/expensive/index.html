Home prices

The median home in Madisonville is valued at $136,000. This is about $24,460 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $81,500 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is is very similar.

Rents

In Madisonville the median monthly rent is $618. This is roughly $56 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $444 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $227 (26%) lower.
