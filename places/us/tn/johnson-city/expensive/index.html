Home prices

The median home value in Johnson City is $167,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $29,200 (21%) higher. It is about $50,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $102,900 (38%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Johnson City the median monthly rent is $755. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $307 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
