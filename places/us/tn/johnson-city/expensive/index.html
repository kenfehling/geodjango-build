Compared to its surrounding areas, Johnson City is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Johnson City is $167,500. This is roughly $29,200 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $50,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $102,900 (38%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Johnson City's median rent is $755 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
