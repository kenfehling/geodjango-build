Home prices

The median home in Millersville costs $186,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $14,440 (7%) lower.It is approximately $31,200 (14%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $32,000 (20%) higher compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

Millersville's median rent is $941 a month. This is approximately $84 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Clarksville is roughly $20 (2%) lower.
