Home prices

The median home cost in Millersville is $186,300. This is roughly $14,440 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $31,200 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $32,000 (20%) higher.

Rents

Millersville's median rent is $941 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $84 (8%) lower. It is about $121 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $20 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.
