Home prices

The median home value in Harrison is $152,000. It is roughly $33,220 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $65,500 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $15,700 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Harrison the median monthly rent is $785. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $121 (13%) lower. It is approximately $277 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $60 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
