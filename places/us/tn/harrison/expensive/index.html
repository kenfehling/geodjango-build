Home prices

Harrison's median home cost is $152,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $33,220 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $65,500 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $15,700 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Harrison is $785 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $121 (13%) lower. It is roughly $277 (26%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $60 (7%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
