Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sparta is $93,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $23,440 (19%) lower.It is approximately $123,600 (56%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $73,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Chattanooga.

Rents

Sparta's median rent is $640 per month. It is roughly $27 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $422 (39%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chattanooga is roughly $219 (25%) lower.
