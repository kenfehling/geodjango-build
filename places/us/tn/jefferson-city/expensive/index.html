Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City costs $127,400. It is about $6,280 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $90,100 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $8,900 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Jefferson City the median monthly rent is $657. It is approximately $77 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $405 (38%) lower, and it is about $188 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
