Home prices

The median home price in Jefferson City is $127,400. This is roughly $6,280 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $90,100 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $8,900 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Jefferson City is $657 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $77 (10%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $405 (38%) lower, and is about $188 (22%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
