Much like its nearby areas, it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City costs $127,400. It is about $6,280 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $90,100 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $8,900 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Jefferson City the median monthly rent is $657. This is approximately $77 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $405 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $188 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
