Much like the surrounding areas, it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City costs $127,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $6,280 (4%) lower.It is about $90,100 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $8,900 (6%) lower.

Rents

Jefferson City's median monthly rent is $657. It is about $77 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $405 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $188 (22%) lower.
