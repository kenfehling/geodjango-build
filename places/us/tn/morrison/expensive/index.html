In comparison to other places in this area, Morrison is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Morrison costs $98,300. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $7,380 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $119,200 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is about $84,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

Morrison's median rent is $388 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $221 (36%) lower. It is roughly $674 (63%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is approximately $439 (53%) lower.
