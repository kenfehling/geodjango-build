Home prices

The cost of the median home in New Market is $121,600. This is about $10,660 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $95,900 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $14,700 (10%) lower.

Rents

New Market's median rent is $771 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $80 (11%) higher. It is about $291 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $74 (8%) lower.
