Home prices

The median home in New Market costs $121,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $10,660 (8%) lower. It is approximately $95,900 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $14,700 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in New Market is $771 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $80 (11%) higher. It is about $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $74 (8%) lower.
