Compared to other places in this area, Williston is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Williston is valued at $99,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $24,360 (19%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $118,100 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $2,400 (2%) lower.

Rents

In Williston the median rent is $950 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $38 (4%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $112 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $49 (5%) higher.
