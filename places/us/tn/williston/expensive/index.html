Home prices

The price of the median home in Williston is $99,400. It is approximately $24,360 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $118,100 (54%) lower, and it is approximately $2,400 (2%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Williston the median monthly rent is $950. This is roughly $38 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $112 (10%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $49 (5%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
