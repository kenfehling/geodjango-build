Home prices

The median home in Lewisburg costs $96,800. It is roughly $12,060 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $120,700 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $86,100 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Lewisburg the median rent is $724 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $38 (4%) lower.It is about $338 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $103 (12%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
