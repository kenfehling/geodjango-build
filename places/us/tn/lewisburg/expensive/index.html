Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lewisburg is $96,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $12,060 (11%) lower. It is about $120,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $86,100 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Lewisburg's median rent is $724 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $38 (4%) lower. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $103 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
