In comparison to nearby areas, Memphis is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

Memphis's median home cost is $101,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $65,650 (39%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $115,700 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Memphis is $901. It is about $173 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $161 (15%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole.
