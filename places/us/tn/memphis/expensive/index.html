Compared to other places in this area, Memphis is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Memphis is valued at $101,800. It is roughly $65,650 (39%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $115,700 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Memphis the median monthly rent is $901. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $173 (16%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $161 (15%) lower.
