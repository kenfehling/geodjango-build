Memphis's median home price is $101,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $65,650 (39%) lower. It is roughly $115,700 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Memphis is $901. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $173 (16%) lower. It is roughly $161 (15%) lower compared to the entire country.
