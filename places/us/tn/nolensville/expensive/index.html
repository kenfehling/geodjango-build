Compared to other places in this area, Nolensville is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Huntsville, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Nolensville's median home cost is $455,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $33,180 (7%) higher. It is approximately $238,200 (109%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $272,800 (149%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nolensville is $2,263. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $691 (43%) higher. It is about $1,201 (113%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $1,436 (173%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.
