Home prices

The value of the median home in Huntsville is $120,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $31,840 (35%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $96,800 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $15,600 (11%) lower.

Rents

Huntsville's median rent is $427 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $126 (22%) lower. It is roughly $635 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $418 (49%) lower.
