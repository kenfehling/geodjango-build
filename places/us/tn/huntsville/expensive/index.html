Home prices

The median home in Huntsville is valued at $120,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $31,840 (35%) higher. It is roughly $96,800 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $15,600 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Huntsville is $427 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $126 (22%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $635 (59%) lower, and is about $418 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
