Home prices

The median home price in South Carthage is $130,500. It is about $7,440 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $87,000 (40%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Clarksville is roughly $23,800 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in South Carthage is $622 per month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $440 (41%) lower, and is roughly $339 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.
