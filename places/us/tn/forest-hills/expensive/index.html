Home prices

Forest Hills's median home cost is $853,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $146,300 (20%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $635,500 (292%) higher, and is about $698,700 (452%) higher compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

The median rent in Forest Hills is $3,234 per month. This is approximately $1,222 (60%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $2,172 (204%) higher, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $2,273 (236%) higher.
