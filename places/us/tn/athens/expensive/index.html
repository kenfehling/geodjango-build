Compared to its surrounding areas, Athens is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Athens is valued at $148,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $44,240 (42%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $68,700 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $12,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Athens is $708 per month. This is roughly $58 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $354 (33%) lower, and is approximately $137 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
