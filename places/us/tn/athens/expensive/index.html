Home prices

Athens's median home cost is $148,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $44,240 (42%) higher. It is roughly $68,700 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $12,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Athens is $708 per month. It is roughly $58 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $354 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $137 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
