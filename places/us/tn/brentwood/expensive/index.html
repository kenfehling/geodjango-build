In comparison to the surrounding areas, Brentwood is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Huntsville, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brentwood is valued at $655,400. It is about $132,520 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $437,900 (201%) higher, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $472,500 (258%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brentwood is $2,085. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $288 (12%) lower. It is roughly $1,023 (96%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $1,258 (152%) higher in comparison to the city of Huntsville.
