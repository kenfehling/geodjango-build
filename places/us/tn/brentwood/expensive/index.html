Home prices

The median home in Brentwood costs $655,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $132,520 (16%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $437,900 (201%) higher, and it is about $472,500 (258%) higher in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Brentwood's median rent is $2,085 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $288 (12%) lower. It is approximately $1,023 (96%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $1,258 (152%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.
