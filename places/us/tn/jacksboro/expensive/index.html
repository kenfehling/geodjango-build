Home prices

The median home in Jacksboro costs $148,300. This is roughly $37,380 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $69,200 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $12,000 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Jacksboro the median rent is $602 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $49 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $460 (43%) lower, and is roughly $243 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
