Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jacksboro is $148,300. It is approximately $37,380 (33%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $69,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $12,000 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksboro is $602. This is roughly $49 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $460 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $243 (28%) lower.
