In comparison to other places in this area, Dayton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Dayton's median home value is $99,000. This is about $32,120 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $118,500 (54%) lower, and is roughly $37,300 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Dayton is $678 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $31 (4%) lower. It is approximately $384 (36%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $167 (19%) lower.
