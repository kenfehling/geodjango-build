Home prices

Rents

In Dayton the median rent is $678 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $31 (4%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $384 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $167 (19%) lower.
