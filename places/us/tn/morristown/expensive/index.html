Home prices

Morristown's median home price is $115,900. This is approximately $29,140 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $101,600 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $20,400 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morristown is $731. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $99 (15%) higher.It is roughly $331 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $114 (13%) lower.
