In comparison to other places in this area, Morristown is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Morristown is $115,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $29,140 (20%) lower. It is approximately $101,600 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $20,400 (14%) lower.

Rents

Morristown's median monthly rent is $731. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $99 (15%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $331 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $114 (13%) lower.
