Home prices

The median home cost in Hopewell is $168,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $49,840 (42%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $49,400 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $31,800 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hopewell is $573 a month. This is roughly $192 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $489 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $272 (32%) lower.
