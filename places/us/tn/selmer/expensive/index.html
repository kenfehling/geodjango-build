Home prices

The cost of the median home in Selmer is $98,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $15,580 (18%) higher. It is approximately $118,800 (54%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $3,100 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Selmer is $630. This is about $30 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $432 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $271 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
