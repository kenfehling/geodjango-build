Home prices

The median home cost in Selmer is $98,700. It is about $15,580 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $118,800 (54%) lower, and is about $3,100 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Selmer the median monthly rent is $630. This is about $30 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $432 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is about $271 (30%) lower.
