Home prices

Crossville's median home value is $147,000. This is approximately $16,820 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $70,500 (32%) lower, and is roughly $10,700 (7%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Crossville is $670 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $392 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $175 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
