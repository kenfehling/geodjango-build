The cost of the median home in Medon is $100,800. It is approximately $5,460 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $116,700 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is is very similar.

Rents

In Medon the median monthly rent is $438. This is approximately $250 (36%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $624 (58%) lower, and is roughly $463 (51%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
