Home prices

The cost of the median home in Medon is $100,800. It is roughly $5,460 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $116,700 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is is very similar.

Rents

In Medon the median rent is $438 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $250 (36%) lower. It is about $624 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $463 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
