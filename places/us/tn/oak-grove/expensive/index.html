Home prices

The median home in Oak Grove is valued at $175,300. It is approximately $30,600 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $42,200 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Asheville is approximately $95,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

Oak Grove's median rent is $682 a month. This is approximately $154 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $380 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville is approximately $361 (34%) lower.
