Home prices

Eagleville's median home price is $206,500. It is approximately $37,220 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $11,000 (5%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $23,600 (12%) higher compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Eagleville is $1,170. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $23 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $108 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is roughly $343 (41%) higher.
