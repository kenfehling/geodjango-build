Home prices

The cost of the median home in Caryville is $124,300. It is roughly $8,580 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $93,200 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $12,000 (8%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Caryville the median rent is $653 per month. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $409 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $192 (22%) lower.
