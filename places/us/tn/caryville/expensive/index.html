Home prices

The cost of the median home in Caryville is $124,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $8,580 (7%) higher.It is about $93,200 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $12,000 (8%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Caryville's median rent is $653 a month. It is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $409 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $192 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
