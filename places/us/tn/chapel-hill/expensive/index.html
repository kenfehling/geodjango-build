Home prices

The median home in Chapel Hill costs $143,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $27,840 (16%) lower. It is approximately $74,400 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is about $39,800 (21%) lower.

Rents

Chapel Hill's median rent is $1,002 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $83 (8%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $60 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $175 (21%) higher.
