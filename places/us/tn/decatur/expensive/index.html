Compared to its surrounding areas, Decatur is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Decatur is valued at $125,800. This is about $15,140 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $91,700 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $10,500 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Decatur is $620. This is roughly $52 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $442 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $225 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
