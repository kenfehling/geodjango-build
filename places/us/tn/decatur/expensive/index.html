Compared to other places in this area, Decatur is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Decatur's median home cost is $125,800. It is about $15,140 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $91,700 (42%) lower, and is roughly $10,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Decatur's median rent is $620 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $52 (7%) lower. It is about $442 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $225 (26%) lower.
