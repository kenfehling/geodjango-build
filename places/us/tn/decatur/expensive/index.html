Home prices

The median home cost in Decatur is $125,800. This is approximately $15,140 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $91,700 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $10,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Decatur's median monthly rent is $620. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $52 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $442 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $225 (26%) lower.
