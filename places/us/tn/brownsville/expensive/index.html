Home prices

The median home in Brownsville is valued at $88,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $3,720 (4%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $129,400 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $13,700 (13%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Brownsville is $640 per month. It is about $7 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $422 (39%) lower, and is approximately $261 (28%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
