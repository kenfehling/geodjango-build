Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brownsville is $88,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $3,720 (4%) higher. It is roughly $129,400 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is approximately $13,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Brownsville the median monthly rent is $640. It is approximately $7 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $422 (39%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $261 (28%) lower.
