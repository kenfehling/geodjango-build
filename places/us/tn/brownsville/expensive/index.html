Home prices

The median home in Brownsville costs $88,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $3,720 (4%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $129,400 (59%) lower, and it is about $13,700 (13%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownsville is $640. This is approximately $7 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $422 (39%) lower, and it is approximately $261 (28%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
