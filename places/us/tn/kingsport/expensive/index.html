In comparison to other places in this area, Kingsport is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Kingsport is $145,800. This is about $13,220 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $71,700 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $9,500 (6%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Kingsport the median rent is $655 per month. This is about $68 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $407 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $190 (22%) lower.
