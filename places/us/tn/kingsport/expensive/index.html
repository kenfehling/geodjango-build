Compared to nearby areas, Kingsport is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kingsport is $145,800. This is roughly $13,220 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $71,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $9,500 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kingsport is $655. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $68 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $407 (38%) lower, and is roughly $190 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
