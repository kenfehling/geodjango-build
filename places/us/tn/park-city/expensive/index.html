Compared to the surrounding areas, Park City is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Park City is $114,800. This is about $14,160 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $102,700 (47%) lower, and it is roughly $68,100 (37%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Park City is $758. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $78 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $304 (28%) lower, and is about $69 (8%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
