Compared to other places in this area, Park City is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

Park City's median home cost is $114,800. This is roughly $14,160 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $102,700 (47%) lower, and it is roughly $68,100 (37%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Park City's median rent is $758 per month. It is roughly $78 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $304 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $69 (8%) lower.
