Home prices

Rents

In Alamo the median rent is $634 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $67 (9%) lower.It is approximately $428 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $267 (29%) lower.
