Home prices

Wildwood's median home cost is $181,500. It is roughly $13,540 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $36,000 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $45,200 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wildwood is $1,034 per month. This is about $210 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $28 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $189 (22%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
