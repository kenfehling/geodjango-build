Compared to its surrounding areas, Wildwood is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wildwood is $181,500. This is about $13,540 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $36,000 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $45,200 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wildwood is $1,034 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $210 (25%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $28 (2%) lower, and is about $189 (22%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
