In comparison to other places in this area, Wildwood is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Wildwood costs $181,500. It is about $13,540 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $36,000 (16%) lower, and is roughly $45,200 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Wildwood is $1,034 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $210 (25%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $28 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $189 (22%) higher.
