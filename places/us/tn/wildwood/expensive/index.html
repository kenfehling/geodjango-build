Compared to other places in this area, Wildwood is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wildwood costs $181,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $13,540 (8%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $36,000 (16%) lower, and is roughly $45,200 (33%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Wildwood the median monthly rent is $1,034. It is about $210 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $28 (2%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $189 (22%) higher.
