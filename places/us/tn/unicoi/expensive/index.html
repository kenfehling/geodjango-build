Home prices

The median home value in Unicoi is $166,300. This is approximately $25,060 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $51,200 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Asheville is roughly $104,100 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Unicoi the median monthly rent is $638. This is roughly $116 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $424 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $405 (38%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
