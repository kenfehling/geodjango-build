Home prices

The median home cost in Lafayette is $95,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $3,540 (3%) higher. It is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $58,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

Lafayette's median rent is $709 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $108 (17%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $353 (33%) lower, and it is about $252 (26%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
