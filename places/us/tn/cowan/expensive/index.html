Home prices

The median home in Cowan costs $78,600. It is roughly $76,380 (49%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $138,900 (63%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $104,300 (57%) lower.

Rents

Cowan's median monthly rent is $567. This is roughly $210 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $495 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $260 (31%) lower.
