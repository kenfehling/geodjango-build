Home prices

The median home in Townsend costs $232,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $51,420 (28%) higher. It is approximately $15,100 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $96,300 (70%) higher.

Rents

In Townsend the median monthly rent is $775. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $91 (10%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and is approximately $70 (8%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
