Home prices

The median home in Townsend is valued at $232,600. It is approximately $51,420 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $15,100 (6%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $96,300 (70%) higher.

Rents

Townsend's median rent is $775 a month. It is roughly $91 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $70 (8%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
