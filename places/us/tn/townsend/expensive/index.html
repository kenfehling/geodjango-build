Compared to other places in this area, Townsend is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Townsend costs $232,600. It is about $51,420 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $15,100 (6%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $96,300 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Townsend is $775. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $91 (10%) lower.It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $70 (8%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
