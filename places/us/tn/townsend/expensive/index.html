Home prices

The median home in Townsend costs $232,600. It is roughly $51,420 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $15,100 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $96,300 (70%) higher.

Rents

In Townsend the median rent is $775 per month. It is roughly $91 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $287 (27%) lower, and is roughly $70 (8%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
