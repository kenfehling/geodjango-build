Compared to the surrounding areas, Collierville is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Collierville costs $319,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $58,280 (22%) higher.It is about $101,800 (46%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $217,500 (213%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Collierville the median monthly rent is $1,331. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $463 (53%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $269 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $430 (47%) higher.
