In comparison to its surrounding areas, Collierville is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Collierville costs $319,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $58,280 (22%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $101,800 (46%) higher, and is about $217,500 (213%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Collierville is $1,331 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $463 (53%) higher. It is roughly $269 (25%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $430 (47%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
