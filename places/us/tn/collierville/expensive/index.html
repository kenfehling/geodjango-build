In comparison to its nearby areas, Collierville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Collierville costs $319,300. It is approximately $58,280 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $101,800 (46%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $217,500 (213%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Collierville is $1,331 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $463 (53%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $269 (25%) higher, and it is about $430 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
