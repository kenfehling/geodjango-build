In comparison to nearby areas, Collierville is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Memphis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Collierville costs $319,300. It is approximately $58,280 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $101,800 (46%) higher, and compared to the city of Memphis it is roughly $217,500 (213%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Collierville is $1,331 per month. This is about $463 (53%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $269 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $430 (47%) higher.
