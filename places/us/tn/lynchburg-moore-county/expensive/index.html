Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg, Moore County is valued at $187,100. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $84,520 (82%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $30,400 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $4,200 (2%) higher.

Rents

Lynchburg, Moore County's median rent is $672 a month. It is roughly $145 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $390 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $155 (18%) lower.
