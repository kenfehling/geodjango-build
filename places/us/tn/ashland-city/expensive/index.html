In comparison to nearby areas, Ashland City is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Clarksville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ashland City is $141,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $70,460 (33%) lower. It is roughly $75,800 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $12,600 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Ashland City the median rent is $985 per month. This is approximately $25 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $77 (7%) lower, and is approximately $24 (2%) higher compared to the city of Clarksville.
