Home prices

The median home in Bristol is valued at $133,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $17,920 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $83,900 (38%) lower, and is roughly $136,800 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Bristol the median monthly rent is $666. This is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $396 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville it is approximately $377 (36%) lower.
