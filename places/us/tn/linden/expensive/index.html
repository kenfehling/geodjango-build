Home prices

The median home cost in Linden is $76,800. It is about $15,340 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $140,700 (64%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $77,500 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Linden the median rent is $556 per month. It is approximately $24 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $506 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $405 (42%) lower.
