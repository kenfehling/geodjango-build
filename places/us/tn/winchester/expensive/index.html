Home prices

The price of the median home in Winchester is $146,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $18,120 (14%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $70,700 (32%) lower, and is roughly $36,100 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Winchester's median monthly rent is $636. It is roughly $142 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $426 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is approximately $191 (23%) lower.
