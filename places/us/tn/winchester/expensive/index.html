Home prices

The median home in Winchester is valued at $146,800. It is roughly $18,120 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $70,700 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is approximately $36,100 (19%) lower.

Rents

Winchester's median monthly rent is $636. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $142 (18%) lower.It is roughly $426 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $191 (23%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
