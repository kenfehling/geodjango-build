Home prices

The median home in Winchester is valued at $146,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $18,120 (14%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $70,700 (32%) lower, and it is about $36,100 (19%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Winchester's median monthly rent is $636. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $142 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $426 (40%) lower, and is about $191 (23%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
