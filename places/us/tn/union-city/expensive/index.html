Compared to other places in this area, Union City is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Jackson.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Union City is $96,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $18,980 (24%) higher. It is approximately $120,800 (55%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Jackson is about $36,500 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Union City is $584 a month. This is roughly $44 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $478 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $293 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
