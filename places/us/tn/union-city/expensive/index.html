Home prices

Rents

Union City's median monthly rent is $584. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $44 (7%) lower.It is about $478 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $293 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
