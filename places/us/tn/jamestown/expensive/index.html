In comparison to other places in this area, Jamestown is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Jamestown is $67,600. This is approximately $26,680 (28%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $149,900 (68%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $68,700 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Jamestown the median rent is $411 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $120 (22%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $651 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $434 (51%) lower.
