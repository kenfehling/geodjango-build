Home prices

The median home in Jamestown is valued at $67,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $26,680 (28%) lower.It is roughly $149,900 (68%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $68,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jamestown is $411. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $120 (22%) lower.It is approximately $651 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $434 (51%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
