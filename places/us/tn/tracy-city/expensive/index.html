Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tracy City is $103,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $28,340 (21%) lower.It is about $113,900 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $79,300 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tracy City is $604 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $86 (12%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $458 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $223 (26%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
