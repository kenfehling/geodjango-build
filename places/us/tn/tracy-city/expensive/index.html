In comparison to other places in this area, Tracy City is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Huntsville, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tracy City costs $103,600. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $28,340 (21%) lower. It is about $113,900 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is about $79,300 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tracy City is $604 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $86 (12%) lower. It is roughly $458 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Huntsville is approximately $223 (26%) lower.
