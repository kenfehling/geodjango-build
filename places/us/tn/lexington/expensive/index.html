Home prices

Rents

In Lexington the median monthly rent is $636. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $12 (1%) higher.It is roughly $426 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $241 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Jackson.
