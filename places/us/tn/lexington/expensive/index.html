Home prices

The median home in Lexington is valued at $114,900. This is roughly $13,440 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $102,600 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is approximately $18,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

Lexington's median monthly rent is $636. It is roughly $12 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $426 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $241 (27%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
