In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lexington is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Jackson.

Home prices

The median home in Lexington is valued at $114,900. It is about $13,440 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $102,600 (47%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jackson is approximately $18,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

Lexington's median rent is $636 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $12 (1%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $426 (40%) lower, and is about $241 (27%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
