Home prices

The median home in Louisville costs $232,000. It is about $70,660 (43%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $14,500 (6%) higher, and it is about $95,700 (70%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Louisville the median monthly rent is $853. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10 (1%) higher.It is approximately $209 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
