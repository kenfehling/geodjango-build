Home prices

The median home in Louisville costs $232,000. This is approximately $70,660 (43%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $14,500 (6%) higher, and is about $95,700 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Louisville is $853. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $10 (1%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $209 (19%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
