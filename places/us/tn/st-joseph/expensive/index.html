Home prices

The median home in St. Joseph costs $74,200. It is about $53,840 (42%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $143,300 (65%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $108,700 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in St. Joseph is $558 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $122 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $504 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $269 (32%) lower.
