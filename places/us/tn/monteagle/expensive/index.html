Home prices

The median home price in Monteagle is $147,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $9,200 (6%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $70,300 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $35,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monteagle is $667. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27 (3%) lower. It is about $395 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is about $160 (19%) lower.
