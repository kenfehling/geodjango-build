Home prices

Monteagle's median home cost is $147,200. This is approximately $9,200 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $70,300 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Huntsville is roughly $35,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

Monteagle's median rent is $667 per month. It is roughly $27 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $395 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville is roughly $160 (19%) lower.
