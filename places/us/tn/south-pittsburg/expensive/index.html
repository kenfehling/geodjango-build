Compared to the surrounding areas, South Pittsburg is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Huntsville, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Pittsburg costs $92,600. This is roughly $15,440 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $124,900 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $90,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

In South Pittsburg the median rent is $521 per month. This is roughly $41 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $541 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $306 (37%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
