Home prices

The value of the median home in Lawrenceburg is $101,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $12,180 (13%) higher.It is approximately $116,000 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $81,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

Lawrenceburg's median monthly rent is $613. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $95 (13%) lower.It is roughly $449 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Huntsville is about $214 (25%) lower.
