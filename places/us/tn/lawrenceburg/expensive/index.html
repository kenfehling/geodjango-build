Compared to other places in this area, Lawrenceburg is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrenceburg costs $101,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $12,180 (13%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $116,000 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Huntsville is roughly $81,400 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Lawrenceburg the median rent is $613 a month. This is about $95 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $449 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $214 (25%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
