In comparison to nearby areas, Lawrenceburg is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrenceburg is valued at $101,500. It is approximately $12,180 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $116,000 (53%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $81,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

In Lawrenceburg the median monthly rent is $613. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $95 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $449 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is approximately $214 (25%) lower.
