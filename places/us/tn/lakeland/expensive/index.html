Home prices

The median home price in Lakeland is $257,400. It is approximately $87,220 (51%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $39,900 (18%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $155,600 (152%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Lakeland the median rent is $1,200 a month. This is about $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $138 (12%) higher, and it is approximately $299 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
