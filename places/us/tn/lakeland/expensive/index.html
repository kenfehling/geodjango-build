Home prices

The median home price in Lakeland is $257,400. It is roughly $87,220 (51%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $39,900 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $155,600 (152%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Lakeland the median rent is $1,200 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $25 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $138 (12%) higher, and is about $299 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.
