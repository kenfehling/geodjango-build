Home prices

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median rent is $538 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $211 (28%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $524 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $289 (34%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
