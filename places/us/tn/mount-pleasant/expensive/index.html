Compared to its surrounding areas, Mount Pleasant is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Huntsville.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Pleasant is valued at $107,800. This is roughly $8,340 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $109,700 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $75,100 (41%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Pleasant is $538 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $211 (28%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $524 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $289 (34%) lower compared to the city of Huntsville.
