Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mount Pleasant is $107,800. This is about $8,340 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $109,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville it is roughly $75,100 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Pleasant is $538 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $211 (28%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $524 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Huntsville is about $289 (34%) lower.
