Compared to its nearby areas, Oakland is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $185,800. This is approximately $11,400 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $31,700 (14%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis is approximately $84,000 (82%) higher.

Rents

In Oakland the median monthly rent is $1,502. It is about $503 (50%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $440 (41%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is roughly $601 (66%) higher.
