Home prices

Rents

In Oakland the median monthly rent is $1,502. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $503 (50%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $440 (41%) higher, and is roughly $601 (66%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
