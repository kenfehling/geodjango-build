Compared to other places in this area, Oakland is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $185,800. This is roughly $11,400 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $31,700 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is about $84,000 (82%) higher.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $1,502 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $503 (50%) higher. It is approximately $440 (41%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $601 (66%) higher.
