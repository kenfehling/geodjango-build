Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakland is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home value in Oakland is $185,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $11,400 (6%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $31,700 (14%) lower, and is roughly $84,000 (82%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Oakland is $1,502 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $503 (50%) higher. It is approximately $440 (41%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $601 (66%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.
