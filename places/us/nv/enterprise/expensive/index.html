Home prices

Enterprise's median home value is $315,300. It is approximately $10,780 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $97,800 (44%) higher, and it is approximately $56,700 (21%) higher compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

The median rent in Enterprise is $1,434 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $184 (14%) higher.It is about $372 (35%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $332 (30%) higher compared to the city of Las Vegas.
