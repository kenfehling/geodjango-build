Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Mesquite is $879 a month. This is approximately $33 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $183 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $223 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.
