Compared to other places in this area, Laughlin is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Las Vegas, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Laughlin's median home cost is $157,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $31,600 (25%) higher. It is roughly $60,300 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $101,400 (39%) lower compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

Laughlin's median rent is $751 a month. This is roughly $34 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $311 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $351 (31%) lower compared to the city of Las Vegas.
