Home prices

The value of the median home in Laughlin is $157,200. It is approximately $31,600 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $60,300 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is roughly $101,400 (39%) lower.

Rents

Laughlin's median monthly rent is $751. This is approximately $34 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $311 (29%) lower, and it is about $351 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.
