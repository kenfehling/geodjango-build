Compared to nearby areas, Laughlin is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Las Vegas, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Laughlin costs $157,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $31,600 (25%) higher. It is approximately $60,300 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is about $101,400 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laughlin is $751. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $34 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $311 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is about $351 (31%) lower.
