Home prices

The median home in Fernley costs $221,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $94,720 (74%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $3,700 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Reno is about $113,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Fernley the median rent is $1,147 a month. This is approximately $377 (48%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $85 (8%) higher, and is approximately $118 (11%) higher compared to the city of Reno.
