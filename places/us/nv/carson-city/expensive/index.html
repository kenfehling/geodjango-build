Compared to the surrounding areas, Carson City is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Reno, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Carson City is $273,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $157,840 (36%) lower.It is about $56,300 (25%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is roughly $61,200 (18%) lower.

Rents

Carson City's median monthly rent is $940. It is approximately $125 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $122 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $89 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Reno.
