Compared to its nearby areas, Carson City is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Reno.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Carson City is $273,800. This is approximately $157,840 (36%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $56,300 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Reno is approximately $61,200 (18%) lower.

Rents

In Carson City the median monthly rent is $940. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $125 (11%) lower. It is about $122 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $89 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Reno.
