In comparison to its surrounding areas, Carson City is significantly less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Reno however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Carson City's median home cost is $273,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $157,840 (36%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $56,300 (25%) higher, and is about $61,200 (18%) lower compared to the city of Reno.

Rents

Carson City's median rent is $940 per month. This is approximately $125 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $122 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Reno it is about $89 (8%) lower.
