Compared to its surrounding areas, Stagecoach is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Reno, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Stagecoach costs $160,600. It is roughly $43,380 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $56,900 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is about $174,400 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Stagecoach is $985 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $37 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $77 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $44 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Reno.
