Rents

Stagecoach's median monthly rent is $985. It is roughly $37 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $77 (7%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $44 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Reno.
