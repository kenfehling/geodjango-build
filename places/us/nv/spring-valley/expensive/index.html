Home prices

The median home in Spring Valley is valued at $262,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $43,460 (14%) lower.It is about $44,500 (20%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas is approximately $3,400 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Spring Valley the median rent is $1,191 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $35 (2%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $129 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is approximately $89 (8%) higher.
