Home prices

The cost of the median home in Spring Valley is $262,000. It is about $43,460 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $44,500 (20%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $3,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

Spring Valley's median monthly rent is $1,191. It is roughly $35 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $129 (12%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is roughly $89 (8%) higher.
