In comparison to its surrounding areas, Reno is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

Reno's median home cost is $335,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $20,567 (6%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $117,500 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Reno is $1,029 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $306 (22%) lower. It is approximately $33 (3%) lower compared to the entire country.
