In comparison to other places in this area, Boulder City is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Home prices

Boulder City's median home cost is $287,000. This is approximately $43,120 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $69,500 (31%) higher, and it is about $28,400 (10%) higher compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

The median rent in Boulder City is $1,002 per month. This is approximately $149 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $60 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is about $100 (9%) lower.
