Home prices

The price of the median home in Boulder City is $287,000. It is about $43,120 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $69,500 (31%) higher, and it is about $28,400 (10%) higher compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

Boulder City's median rent is $1,002 a month. It is approximately $149 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $60 (5%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is approximately $100 (9%) lower.
