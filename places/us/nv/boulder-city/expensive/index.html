In comparison to other places in this area, Boulder City is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Home prices

The median home value in Boulder City is $287,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $43,120 (17%) higher.It is roughly $69,500 (31%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas it is roughly $28,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

Boulder City's median rent is $1,002 per month. This is roughly $149 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $60 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is approximately $100 (9%) lower.
