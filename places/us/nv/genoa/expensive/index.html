Home prices

Rents

In Genoa the median monthly rent is $732. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $880 (54%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $330 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $297 (28%) lower compared to the city of Reno.
