Compared to other places in this area, Henderson is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Las Vegas however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Henderson's median home value is $318,800. It is roughly $104,540 (48%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $101,300 (46%) higher, and is about $60,200 (23%) higher compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

In Henderson the median rent is $1,292 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $208 (19%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $230 (21%) higher, and is about $190 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.
