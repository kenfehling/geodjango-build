Compared to other places in this area, Henderson is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Las Vegas, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Henderson costs $318,800. It is roughly $104,540 (48%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $101,300 (46%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas it is about $60,200 (23%) higher.

Rents

Henderson's median rent is $1,292 per month. This is roughly $208 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $230 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $190 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.
