Compared to the surrounding areas, Mount Charleston is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Las Vegas, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mount Charleston is $446,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $178,260 (66%) higher.It is approximately $229,400 (105%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $188,300 (72%) higher compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Charleston is $1,075. It is approximately $62 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $13 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is approximately $27 (2%) lower.
