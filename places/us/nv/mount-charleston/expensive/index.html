Home prices

The value of the median home in Mount Charleston is $446,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $178,260 (66%) higher. It is approximately $229,400 (105%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is approximately $188,300 (72%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Charleston the median rent is $1,075 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $62 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $13 (1%) higher, and it is about $27 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.
