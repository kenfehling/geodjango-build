In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mount Charleston is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Las Vegas, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Mount Charleston's median home price is $446,900. It is approximately $178,260 (66%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $229,400 (105%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $188,300 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Charleston is $1,075 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $62 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $13 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas it is roughly $27 (2%) lower.
