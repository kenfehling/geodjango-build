Compared to the surrounding areas, Mount Charleston is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Home prices

The median home price in Mount Charleston is $446,900. It is roughly $178,260 (66%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $229,400 (105%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is about $188,300 (72%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Charleston the median monthly rent is $1,075. It is about $62 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is roughly $27 (2%) lower.
