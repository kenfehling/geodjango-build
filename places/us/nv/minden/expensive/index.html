Home prices

Minden's median home cost is $344,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $176,700 (33%) lower. It is roughly $127,200 (58%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $9,700 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Reno.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Minden is $1,281. This is about $233 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $219 (20%) higher, and it is about $252 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Reno.
