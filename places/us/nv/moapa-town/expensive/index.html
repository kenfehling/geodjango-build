In comparison to other places in this area, Moapa Town is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Home prices

The median home in Moapa Town is valued at $152,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $46,300 (23%) lower. It is approximately $64,600 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is roughly $105,700 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Moapa Town is $678 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $339 (33%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $384 (36%) lower, and is roughly $424 (38%) lower compared to the city of Las Vegas.
