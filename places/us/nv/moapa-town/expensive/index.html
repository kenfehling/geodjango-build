Home prices

The median home in Moapa Town is valued at $152,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $46,300 (23%) lower.It is roughly $64,600 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $105,700 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

Moapa Town's median monthly rent is $678. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $339 (33%) lower.It is approximately $384 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is approximately $424 (38%) lower.
