Home prices

The median home in Pahrump costs $173,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $149,340 (46%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $43,600 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas is about $84,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

Pahrump's median rent is $894 a month. This is about $280 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $168 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas is approximately $208 (18%) lower.
