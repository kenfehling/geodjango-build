Home prices

The median home in Winchester costs $162,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $26,620 (14%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $55,100 (25%) lower, and is about $96,200 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

Winchester's median rent is $933 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $227 (19%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $129 (12%) lower, and it is roughly $169 (15%) lower compared to the city of Las Vegas.
