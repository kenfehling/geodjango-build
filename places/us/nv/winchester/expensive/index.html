Compared to the surrounding areas, Winchester is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Winchester is $162,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $26,620 (14%) lower.It is about $55,100 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $96,200 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.

Rents

Winchester's median rent is $933 a month. It is roughly $227 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $129 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas is approximately $169 (15%) lower.
