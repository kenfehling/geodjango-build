Compared to nearby areas, Winchester is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Home prices

Winchester's median home price is $162,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $26,620 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $55,100 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is roughly $96,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Winchester the median rent is $933 a month. It is about $227 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $129 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $169 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.
