Home prices

The median home price in Fallon is $168,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $31,120 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $49,300 (22%) lower, and is roughly $166,800 (49%) lower compared to the city of Reno.

Rents

Fallon's median rent is $887 per month. This is approximately $65 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $175 (16%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Reno is approximately $142 (13%) lower.
