Home prices

The median home in Fallon is valued at $168,200. It is approximately $31,120 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $49,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $166,800 (49%) lower compared to the city of Reno.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fallon is $887. This is about $65 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $175 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is about $142 (13%) lower.
