Home prices

The median home value in Fallon is $168,200. This is approximately $31,120 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $49,300 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Reno it is approximately $166,800 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Fallon the median rent is $887 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $65 (7%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $175 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is about $142 (13%) lower.
