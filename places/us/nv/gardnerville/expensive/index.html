In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gardnerville is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Reno, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Gardnerville is $304,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $155,080 (33%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $86,500 (39%) higher, and is approximately $31,000 (9%) lower compared to the city of Reno.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gardnerville is $972. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $306 (23%) lower. It is about $90 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Reno is about $57 (5%) lower.
