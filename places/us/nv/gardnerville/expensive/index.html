Compared to its nearby areas, Gardnerville is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Reno however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Gardnerville's median home price is $304,000. This is about $155,080 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $86,500 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $31,000 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Reno.

Rents

In Gardnerville the median monthly rent is $972. It is roughly $306 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $90 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $57 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Reno.
