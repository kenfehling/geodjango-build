In comparison to nearby areas, Gardnerville is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Reno however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Gardnerville is $304,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $155,080 (33%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $86,500 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Reno it is roughly $31,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

Gardnerville's median monthly rent is $972. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $306 (23%) lower.It is approximately $90 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is approximately $57 (5%) lower.
