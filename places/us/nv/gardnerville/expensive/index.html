In comparison to other places in this area, Gardnerville is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Reno, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Gardnerville is $304,000. This is about $155,080 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $86,500 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is roughly $31,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gardnerville is $972. It is roughly $306 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $90 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Reno is roughly $57 (5%) lower.
