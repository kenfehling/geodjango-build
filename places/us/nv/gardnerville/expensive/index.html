In comparison to other places in this area, Gardnerville is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Reno.

Home prices

The median home in Gardnerville is valued at $304,000. This is roughly $155,080 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $86,500 (39%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is about $31,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

Gardnerville's median monthly rent is $972. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $306 (23%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $90 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Reno it is approximately $57 (5%) lower.
