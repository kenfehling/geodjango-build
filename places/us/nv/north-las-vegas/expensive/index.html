In comparison to nearby areas, North Las Vegas is a lot more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Las Vegas.

Home prices

North Las Vegas's median home cost is $233,600. It is roughly $64,500 (38%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $16,100 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Las Vegas it is roughly $25,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

North Las Vegas's median rent is $1,206 a month. It is about $97 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $104 (9%) higher compared to the city of Las Vegas.
