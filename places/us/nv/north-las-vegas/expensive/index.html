In comparison to other places in this area, North Las Vegas is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Las Vegas, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in North Las Vegas is $233,600. This is approximately $64,500 (38%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $16,100 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas it is roughly $25,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

In North Las Vegas the median monthly rent is $1,206. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $97 (8%) higher.It is about $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas is about $104 (9%) higher.
