In comparison to its nearby areas, North Las Vegas is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Las Vegas, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in North Las Vegas is $233,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $64,500 (38%) higher. It is about $16,100 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Las Vegas is approximately $25,000 (9%) lower.

Rents

In North Las Vegas the median rent is $1,206 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $97 (8%) higher. It is roughly $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Las Vegas it is approximately $104 (9%) higher.
