Rents

The median rent in Bunkerville is $567 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $407 (41%) lower. It is roughly $495 (46%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $535 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Las Vegas.
