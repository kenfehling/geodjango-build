Compared to the surrounding areas, Las Vegas is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

Las Vegas's median home price is $258,600. This is about $34,500 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $41,100 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Las Vegas is $1,102 a month. It is about $172 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $40 (3%) higher.
