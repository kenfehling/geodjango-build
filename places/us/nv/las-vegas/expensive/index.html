Home prices

The median home in Las Vegas is valued at $258,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $34,500 (15%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $41,100 (18%) higher.

Rents

Las Vegas's median monthly rent is $1,102. It is about $172 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $40 (3%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole.
