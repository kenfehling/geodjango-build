The cost of the median home in Las Vegas is $258,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $34,500 (15%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $41,100 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Las Vegas is $1,102. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $172 (13%) lower.It is approximately $40 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country.
