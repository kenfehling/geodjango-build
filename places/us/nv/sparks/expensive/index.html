Home prices

The median home in Sparks costs $301,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $10,300 (3%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $84,000 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Reno is about $33,500 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sparks is $1,138. This is about $255 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $76 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Reno is about $109 (10%) higher.
