Compared to the surrounding areas, Dayton is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Reno.

Home prices

The median home cost in Dayton is $248,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $57,620 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $30,500 (14%) higher, and is about $87,000 (25%) lower compared to the city of Reno.

Rents

In Dayton the median rent is $936 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $180 (16%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $126 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Reno is approximately $93 (9%) lower.
