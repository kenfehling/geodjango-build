Home prices

The median home in Dayton costs $248,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $57,620 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $30,500 (14%) higher, and is about $87,000 (25%) lower compared to the city of Reno.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $936 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $180 (16%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $126 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Reno it is approximately $93 (9%) lower.
