Home prices

The median home in Fritz Creek costs $299,500. It is about $44,920 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $82,000 (37%) higher, and is roughly $70,400 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.

Rents

In Fritz Creek the median monthly rent is $891. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $81 (8%) lower.It is about $171 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Kenai it is approximately $59 (6%) lower.
