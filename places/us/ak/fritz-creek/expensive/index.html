Home prices

Fritz Creek's median home value is $299,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $44,920 (17%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $82,000 (37%) higher, and compared to the city of Kenai it is approximately $70,400 (30%) higher.

Rents

Fritz Creek's median monthly rent is $891. It is roughly $81 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $171 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kenai is about $59 (6%) lower.
