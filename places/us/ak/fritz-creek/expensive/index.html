In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fritz Creek is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Kenai.

Home prices

The median home in Fritz Creek costs $299,500. This is roughly $44,920 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $82,000 (37%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $70,400 (30%) higher compared to the city of Kenai.

Rents

Fritz Creek's median monthly rent is $891. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $81 (8%) lower.It is approximately $171 (16%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kenai it is approximately $59 (6%) lower.
