Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Diamond Ridge is $1,174 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $237 (25%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $112 (10%) higher, and is about $224 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.
