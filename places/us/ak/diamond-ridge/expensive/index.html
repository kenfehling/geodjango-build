In comparison to other places in this area, Diamond Ridge is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Kenai.

Home prices

The median home in Diamond Ridge is valued at $291,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $47,320 (19%) higher.It is approximately $74,300 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $62,700 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Diamond Ridge is $1,174. It is about $237 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $112 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kenai it is approximately $224 (23%) higher.
