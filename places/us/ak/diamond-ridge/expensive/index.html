Home prices

The price of the median home in Diamond Ridge is $291,800. It is about $47,320 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $74,300 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Kenai is approximately $62,700 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Diamond Ridge the median rent is $1,174 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $237 (25%) higher. It is roughly $112 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $224 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.
