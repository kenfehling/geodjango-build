Compared to its surrounding areas, Badger is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Fairbanks, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Badger costs $224,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $18,900 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $7,400 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fairbanks is approximately $13,400 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Badger is $1,309 a month. It is roughly $239 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $247 (23%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fairbanks it is about $79 (5%) lower.
