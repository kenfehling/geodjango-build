Home prices

The median home cost in Fort Yukon is $106,400. It is about $84,500 (385%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $111,100 (51%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $84,500 (385%) higher compared to the city of Venetie.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Yukon is $900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $412 (84%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $162 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Venetie it is roughly $412 (84%) higher.
