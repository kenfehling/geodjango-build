Home prices

The median home in Fort Yukon is valued at $106,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $84,500 (385%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $111,100 (51%) lower, and is about $84,500 (385%) higher compared to the city of Venetie.

Rents

Fort Yukon's median rent is $900 a month. It is roughly $412 (84%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $162 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Venetie it is about $412 (84%) higher.
