Compared to its nearby areas, Houston is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Anchorage however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Houston costs $163,900. It is about $59,980 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $53,600 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is approximately $150,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Houston the median rent is $971 a month. It is approximately $142 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $91 (8%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $349 (26%) lower.
