In comparison to other places in this area, Houston is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Anchorage, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Houston's median home price is $163,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $59,980 (26%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $53,600 (24%) lower, and is about $150,900 (47%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median rent in Houston is $971 a month. It is about $142 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $91 (8%) lower, and it is about $349 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
