Compared to its surrounding areas, Houston is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Anchorage however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Houston is $163,900. This is roughly $59,980 (26%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $53,600 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $150,900 (47%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median rent in Houston is $971 per month. It is about $142 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $91 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is about $349 (26%) lower.
