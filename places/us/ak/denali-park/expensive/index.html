Home prices

The median home in Denali Park is valued at $360,700. It is roughly $177,680 (97%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $143,200 (65%) higher, and is roughly $149,200 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Fairbanks.

Rents

Denali Park's median rent is $729 a month. This is approximately $71 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fairbanks it is about $659 (47%) lower.
