The median home in Denali Park costs $360,700. This is roughly $177,680 (97%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $143,200 (65%) higher, and is about $149,200 (70%) higher compared to the city of Fairbanks.

Rents

Denali Park's median rent is $729 a month. It is about $71 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $659 (47%) lower compared to the city of Fairbanks.
