Much like its surrounding areas, it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Anchorage, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Butte costs $254,400. It is approximately $4,980 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $36,900 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $60,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median rent in Butte is $1,219 per month. This is approximately $230 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $157 (14%) higher, and it is about $101 (7%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.
