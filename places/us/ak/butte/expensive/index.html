Similar to other places in this area, it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

Butte's median home cost is $254,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $4,980 (1%) higher.It is approximately $36,900 (16%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $60,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

In Butte the median rent is $1,219 a month. It is approximately $230 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $157 (14%) higher, and it is about $101 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
