Home prices

The median home price in Butte is $254,400. It is approximately $4,980 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $36,900 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $60,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Butte is $1,219 per month. This is about $230 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $157 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Anchorage it is approximately $101 (7%) lower.
