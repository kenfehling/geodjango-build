Home prices

The median home in Butte is valued at $254,400. This is about $4,980 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $36,900 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Anchorage is approximately $60,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Butte is $1,219 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $230 (23%) higher. It is about $157 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Anchorage is approximately $101 (7%) lower.
