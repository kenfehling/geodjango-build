Home prices

The median home in Butte is valued at $254,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $4,980 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $36,900 (16%) higher, and is approximately $60,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

In Butte the median monthly rent is $1,219. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $230 (23%) higher. It is approximately $157 (14%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $101 (7%) lower.
