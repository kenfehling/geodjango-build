Home prices

The median home value in Sutton-Alpine is $164,500. This is roughly $78,220 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $53,000 (24%) lower, and is about $150,300 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sutton-Alpine is $1,086. It is approximately $69 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $24 (2%) higher, and is about $234 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
