Home prices

The price of the median home in Fox River is $247,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,560 (4%) higher.It is roughly $29,700 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $67,600 (21%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fox River is $825. It is approximately $60 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $495 (37%) lower.
