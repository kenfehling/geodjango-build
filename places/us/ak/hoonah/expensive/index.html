Compared to other places in this area, Hoonah is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Juneau.

Home prices

The median home value in Hoonah is $205,400. This is about $64,320 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $12,100 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Juneau it is roughly $140,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hoonah is $1,038. It is approximately $111 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $24 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Juneau it is roughly $272 (20%) lower.
