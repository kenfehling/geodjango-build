Home prices

The median home in Hoonah costs $205,400. This is roughly $64,320 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $12,100 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Juneau it is about $140,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Hoonah the median rent is $1,038 a month. It is approximately $111 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $24 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $272 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Juneau.
