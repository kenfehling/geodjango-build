Compared to its surrounding areas, Hoonah is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Juneau, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Hoonah's median home value is $205,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $64,320 (23%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $12,100 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Juneau is roughly $140,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hoonah is $1,038 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $111 (11%) higher. It is approximately $24 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Juneau it is about $272 (20%) lower.
