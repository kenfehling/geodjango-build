In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hoonah is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Juneau.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hoonah is $205,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $64,320 (23%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $12,100 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Juneau it is roughly $140,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hoonah is $1,038 per month. It is about $111 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Juneau is about $272 (20%) lower.
