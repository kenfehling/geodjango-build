Similar to the surrounding areas, it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Kenai is $229,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $3,880 (1%) lower. It is about $11,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $85,700 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

Kenai's median monthly rent is $950. It is about $219 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $112 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $370 (28%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.
