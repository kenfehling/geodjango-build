Much like the surrounding areas, it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Anchorage, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kenai costs $229,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $3,880 (1%) lower.It is about $11,600 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Anchorage it is approximately $85,700 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kenai is $950. It is roughly $219 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $112 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage is about $370 (28%) lower.
