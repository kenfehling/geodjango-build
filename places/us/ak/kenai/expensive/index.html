Much like its surrounding areas, it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

Kenai's median home cost is $229,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $3,880 (1%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $11,600 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $85,700 (27%) lower.

Rents

Kenai's median rent is $950 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $219 (18%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $112 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Anchorage is roughly $370 (28%) lower.
