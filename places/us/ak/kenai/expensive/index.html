Home prices

The median home in Kenai costs $229,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $3,880 (1%) lower.It is roughly $11,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $85,700 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

In Kenai the median monthly rent is $950. It is roughly $219 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $112 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $370 (28%) lower.
