Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Saxman is $788. This is approximately $56 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $274 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Ketchikan it is approximately $318 (28%) lower.
