Home prices

Rents

In North Pole the median monthly rent is $1,122. It is approximately $15 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $60 (5%) higher, and is approximately $266 (19%) lower compared to the city of Fairbanks.
