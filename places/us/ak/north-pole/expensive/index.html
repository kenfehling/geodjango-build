Home prices

North Pole's median home value is $226,000. It is approximately $17,580 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $8,500 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fairbanks is approximately $14,500 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Pole is $1,122 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $15 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $60 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fairbanks it is approximately $266 (19%) lower.
