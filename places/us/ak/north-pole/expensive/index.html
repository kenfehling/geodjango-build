North Pole's median home cost is $226,000. This is about $17,580 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $8,500 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Fairbanks it is about $14,500 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in North Pole is $1,122 per month. It is about $15 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $60 (5%) higher, and it is about $266 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Fairbanks.
