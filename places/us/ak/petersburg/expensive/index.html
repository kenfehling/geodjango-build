Home prices

The median home in Petersburg costs $228,900. This is approximately $50,460 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $11,400 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $117,000 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Juneau.

Rents

Petersburg's median monthly rent is $956. It is roughly $114 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $106 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $354 (27%) lower compared to the city of Juneau.
