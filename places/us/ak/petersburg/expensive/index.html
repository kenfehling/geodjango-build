Home prices

The median home in Petersburg costs $228,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $50,460 (28%) higher.It is about $11,400 (5%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Juneau it is roughly $117,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Petersburg the median monthly rent is $956. It is roughly $114 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $106 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Juneau it is approximately $354 (27%) lower.
