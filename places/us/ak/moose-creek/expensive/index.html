Home prices

The median home value in Moose Creek is $205,400. This is about $27,460 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $12,100 (5%) lower, and it is about $6,100 (2%) lower compared to the city of Fairbanks.

Rents

Moose Creek's median rent is $1,164 per month. It is approximately $59 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $102 (9%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fairbanks it is roughly $224 (16%) lower.
