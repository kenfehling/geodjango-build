Home prices

The median home value in Cohoe is $209,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $24,300 (10%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $7,800 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage is approximately $105,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

Cohoe's median rent is $708 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $368 (34%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $354 (33%) lower, and is about $612 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
