Home prices

The price of the median home in Cohoe is $209,700. It is roughly $24,300 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $7,800 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $105,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cohoe is $708. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $368 (34%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $354 (33%) lower, and is roughly $612 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
