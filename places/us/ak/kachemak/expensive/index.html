Compared to its surrounding areas, Kachemak is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Kenai.

Home prices

The median home in Kachemak costs $298,400. This is approximately $55,240 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $80,900 (37%) higher, and is about $69,300 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.

Rents

Kachemak's median rent is $1,317 per month. This is about $409 (45%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $255 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kenai it is roughly $367 (38%) higher.
