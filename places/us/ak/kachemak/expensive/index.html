Compared to its surrounding areas, Kachemak is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kenai, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kachemak costs $298,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $55,240 (22%) higher.It is about $80,900 (37%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $69,300 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.

Rents

The median rent in Kachemak is $1,317 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $409 (45%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $255 (24%) higher, and is roughly $367 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.
