Home prices

The median home in Kachemak is valued at $298,400. This is roughly $55,240 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $80,900 (37%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $69,300 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.

Rents

The median rent in Kachemak is $1,317 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $409 (45%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $255 (24%) higher, and is approximately $367 (38%) higher compared to the city of Kenai.
