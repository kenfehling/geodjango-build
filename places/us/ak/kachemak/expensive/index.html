In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kachemak is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Kenai, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Kachemak is $298,400. It is about $55,240 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $80,900 (37%) higher, and it is approximately $69,300 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Kenai.

Rents

Kachemak's median monthly rent is $1,317. This is roughly $409 (45%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $255 (24%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Kenai is roughly $367 (38%) higher.
