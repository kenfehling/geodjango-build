Home prices

The median home price in Soldotna is $211,400. This is roughly $41,320 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $6,100 (2%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $103,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

Soldotna's median monthly rent is $1,019. This is about $115 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $43 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $301 (22%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.
