Home prices

The median home in Homer costs $282,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $36,040 (14%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $64,900 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Kenai is about $53,300 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Homer is $1,020 per month. It is about $53 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $42 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Kenai it is approximately $70 (7%) higher.
