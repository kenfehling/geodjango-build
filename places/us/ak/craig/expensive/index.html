In comparison to its surrounding areas, Craig is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Petersburg, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Craig is $208,200. This is approximately $43,675 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $9,300 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $20,700 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Petersburg.

Rents

In Craig the median monthly rent is $896. This is about $146 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $166 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Petersburg is about $60 (6%) lower.
