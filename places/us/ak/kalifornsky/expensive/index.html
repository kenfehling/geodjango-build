Compared to other places in this area, Kalifornsky is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Anchorage however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kalifornsky is valued at $251,400. It is roughly $23,920 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $33,900 (15%) higher, and it is about $63,400 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median rent in Kalifornsky is $1,254 per month. It is roughly $231 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $192 (18%) higher, and is approximately $66 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
