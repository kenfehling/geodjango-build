Home prices

The price of the median home in Susitna North is $186,600. Compared to nearby areas this is about $27,200 (12%) lower. It is roughly $30,900 (14%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Anchorage it is about $128,200 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Susitna North is $991. This is about $111 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $71 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $329 (24%) lower.
