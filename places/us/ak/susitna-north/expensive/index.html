Home prices

The median home in Susitna North costs $186,600. It is approximately $27,200 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $30,900 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $128,200 (40%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

In Susitna North the median monthly rent is $991. This is about $111 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $71 (6%) lower, and is approximately $329 (24%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.
