Home prices

The median home cost in Susitna North is $186,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $27,200 (12%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $30,900 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage is about $128,200 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Susitna North the median rent is $991 a month. It is about $111 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $71 (6%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage is approximately $329 (24%) lower.
