In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kobuk is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Galena, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Kobuk's median home price is $133,300. It is about $58,050 (77%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $84,200 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Galena it is approximately $13,500 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Kobuk the median rent is $775 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $21 (2%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Galena is about $475 (38%) lower.
