Home prices

The median home in Kobuk costs $133,300. It is roughly $58,050 (77%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $84,200 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Galena it is approximately $13,500 (11%) higher.

Rents

Kobuk's median monthly rent is $775. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $21 (2%) lower. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Galena it is about $475 (38%) lower.
