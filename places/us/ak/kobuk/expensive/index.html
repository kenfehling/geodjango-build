Home prices

The median home in Kobuk is valued at $133,300. This is roughly $58,050 (77%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $84,200 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Galena is approximately $13,500 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kobuk is $775. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $21 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $475 (38%) lower compared to the city of Galena.
