In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cantwell is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

The median home in Cantwell costs $158,000. This is about $46,680 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $59,500 (27%) lower, and it is about $156,800 (49%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

In Cantwell the median monthly rent is $700. It is roughly $147 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is about $620 (46%) lower.
