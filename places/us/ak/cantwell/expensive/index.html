Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Cantwell is $700 per month. It is approximately $147 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $620 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
