Compared to nearby areas, Sitka is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Juneau, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Sitka is $358,600. It is approximately $158,067 (78%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $141,100 (64%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $12,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Juneau.

Rents

Sitka's median rent is $1,131 a month. It is approximately $268 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $69 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Juneau is roughly $179 (13%) lower.
