Compared to its surrounding areas, Sitka is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Juneau.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sitka is $358,600. It is about $158,067 (78%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $141,100 (64%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $12,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Juneau.

Rents

Sitka's median rent is $1,131 a month. It is roughly $268 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $69 (6%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $179 (13%) lower compared to the city of Juneau.
