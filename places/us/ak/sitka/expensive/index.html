Home prices

The median home value in Sitka is $358,600. This is about $160,860 (81%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $141,100 (64%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Juneau is roughly $12,700 (3%) higher.

Rents

Sitka's median rent is $1,131 per month. It is roughly $239 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $69 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Juneau is roughly $179 (13%) lower.
