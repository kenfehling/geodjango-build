Home prices

Sitka's median home cost is $358,600. It is roughly $160,860 (81%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $141,100 (64%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Juneau is about $12,700 (3%) higher.

Rents

Sitka's median monthly rent is $1,131. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $239 (26%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $69 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Juneau it is approximately $179 (13%) lower.
