Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sitka is $358,600. It is about $160,860 (81%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $141,100 (64%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $12,700 (3%) higher compared to the city of Juneau.

Rents

In Sitka the median monthly rent is $1,131. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $239 (26%) higher.It is about $69 (6%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $179 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Juneau.
