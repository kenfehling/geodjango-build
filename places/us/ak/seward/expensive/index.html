Home prices

The median home cost in Seward is $243,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $33,180 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $26,000 (11%) higher, and it is roughly $71,300 (22%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Seward is $983. It is about $179 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $337 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
