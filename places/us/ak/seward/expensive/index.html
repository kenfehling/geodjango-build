Compared to its nearby areas, Seward is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Anchorage, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Seward is $243,500. It is approximately $33,180 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $26,000 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage is about $71,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Seward the median monthly rent is $983. This is approximately $179 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $79 (7%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $337 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
