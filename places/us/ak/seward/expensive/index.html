Compared to nearby areas, Seward is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

Seward's median home cost is $243,500. It is approximately $33,180 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $26,000 (11%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $71,300 (22%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

In Seward the median rent is $983 per month. This is about $179 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $337 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
