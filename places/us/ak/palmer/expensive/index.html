Compared to other places in this area, Palmer is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

Palmer's median home cost is $207,200. It is roughly $61,840 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $10,300 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $107,600 (34%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palmer is $976. Compared to other places in this area it is about $110 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $86 (8%) lower, and it is roughly $344 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
