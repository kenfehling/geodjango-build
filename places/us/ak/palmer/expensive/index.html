In comparison to the surrounding areas, Palmer is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Anchorage however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Palmer is $207,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $61,840 (22%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $10,300 (4%) lower, and is roughly $107,600 (34%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median rent in Palmer is $976 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $110 (10%) lower.It is approximately $86 (8%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $344 (26%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.
