Home prices

The median home cost in Talkeetna is $222,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $25,540 (12%) higher.It is approximately $4,700 (2%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $92,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

Talkeetna's median monthly rent is $1,220. This is about $208 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $158 (14%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Anchorage is about $100 (7%) lower.
