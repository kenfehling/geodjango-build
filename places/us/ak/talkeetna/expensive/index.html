Compared to the surrounding areas, Talkeetna is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

The median home price in Talkeetna is $222,200. It is roughly $25,540 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $4,700 (2%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $92,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Talkeetna is $1,220. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $208 (20%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $158 (14%) higher, and it is roughly $100 (7%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.
