Compared to the surrounding areas, Talkeetna is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Anchorage.

Home prices

The median home in Talkeetna is valued at $222,200. This is about $25,540 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $4,700 (2%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $92,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Talkeetna is $1,220. It is roughly $208 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $158 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $100 (7%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.
