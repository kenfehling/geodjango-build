Home prices

The median home in Talkeetna costs $222,200. This is about $25,540 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $4,700 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Anchorage it is roughly $92,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Talkeetna is $1,220 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $208 (20%) higher.It is roughly $158 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Anchorage is approximately $100 (7%) lower.
