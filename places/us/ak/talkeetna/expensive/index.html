Home prices

The median home in Talkeetna costs $222,200. It is approximately $25,540 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $4,700 (2%) higher, and it is about $92,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Anchorage.

Rents
