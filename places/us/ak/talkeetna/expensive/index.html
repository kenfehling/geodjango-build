Home prices

The median home value in Talkeetna is $222,200. This is approximately $25,540 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $4,700 (2%) higher, and it is about $92,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

In Talkeetna the median rent is $1,220 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $208 (20%) higher.It is approximately $158 (14%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage is roughly $100 (7%) lower.
