Home prices

Rents

In Chena Ridge the median monthly rent is $789. Compared to nearby areas this is about $185 (19%) lower. It is roughly $273 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $599 (43%) lower compared to the city of Fairbanks.
