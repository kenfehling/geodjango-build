Home prices

The median home price in Chena Ridge is $281,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $31,880 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $63,500 (29%) higher, and is approximately $69,500 (32%) higher compared to the city of Fairbanks.

Rents

Chena Ridge's median monthly rent is $789. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $185 (19%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $273 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fairbanks is roughly $599 (43%) lower.
