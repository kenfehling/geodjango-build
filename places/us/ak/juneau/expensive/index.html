Compared to the surrounding areas, Juneau is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The median home value in Juneau is $345,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $140,300 (68%) higher. It is roughly $128,400 (59%) higher compared to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median rent in Juneau is $1,310 per month. It is approximately $440 (50%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $248 (23%) higher.
