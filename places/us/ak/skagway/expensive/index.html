Home prices

The median home cost in Skagway is $343,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $74,750 (27%) higher.It is approximately $125,600 (57%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Juneau.

Rents

Skagway's median rent is $1,008 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $32 (3%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $54 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Juneau is roughly $302 (23%) lower.
