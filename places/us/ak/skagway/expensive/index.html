Compared to its surrounding areas, Skagway is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Juneau.

Home prices

The median home cost in Skagway is $343,100. This is approximately $74,750 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $125,600 (57%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Juneau.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Skagway is $1,008. Compared to other places in this area this is about $32 (3%) lower. It is roughly $54 (5%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $302 (23%) lower compared to the city of Juneau.
