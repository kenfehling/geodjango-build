Rents

In Steele Creek the median monthly rent is $861. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $299 (25%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $201 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $527 (37%) lower compared to the city of Fairbanks.
