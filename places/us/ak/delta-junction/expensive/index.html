Home prices

The median home value in Delta Junction is $222,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $51,560 (18%) lower. It is approximately $4,700 (2%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $24,300 (12%) higher compared to the city of Deltana.

Rents
