Home prices

The median home cost in Ridgeway is $257,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $13,880 (5%) higher. It is approximately $39,900 (18%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $57,400 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

Ridgeway's median rent is $1,564 a month. It is about $539 (52%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $502 (47%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Anchorage it is approximately $244 (18%) higher.
