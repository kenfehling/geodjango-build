In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ridgeway is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Anchorage however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ridgeway costs $257,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $13,880 (5%) higher. It is roughly $39,900 (18%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $57,400 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Anchorage.

Rents

Ridgeway's median rent is $1,564 per month. It is roughly $539 (52%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $502 (47%) higher, and it is about $244 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Anchorage.
