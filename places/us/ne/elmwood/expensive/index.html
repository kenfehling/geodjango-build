Home prices

The cost of the median home in Elmwood is $125,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $1,340 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $91,700 (42%) lower, and it is about $33,900 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elmwood is $758. This is roughly $30 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $304 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $165 (17%) lower.
