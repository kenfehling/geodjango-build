Home prices

The price of the median home in Tecumseh is $74,800. This is approximately $13,960 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $142,700 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Omaha is approximately $84,900 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tecumseh is $691. This is about $116 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $371 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is roughly $232 (25%) lower.
