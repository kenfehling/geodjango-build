Compared to the surrounding areas, Ogallala is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of North Platte, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Ogallala's median home value is $107,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $24,540 (29%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $110,300 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of North Platte it is about $11,700 (9%) lower.

Rents

In Ogallala the median rent is $635 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16 (2%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $427 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of North Platte is about $122 (16%) lower.
