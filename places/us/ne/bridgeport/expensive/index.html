Rents

The median monthly rent in Bridgeport is $740. This is approximately $55 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $322 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $38 (4%) lower compared to the city of Scottsbluff.
