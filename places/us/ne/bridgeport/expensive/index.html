Home prices

The median home cost in Bridgeport is $88,300. This is about $21,060 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower, and is approximately $28,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Scottsbluff.

Rents

Bridgeport's median rent is $740 per month. This is approximately $55 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $322 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Scottsbluff it is about $38 (4%) lower.
