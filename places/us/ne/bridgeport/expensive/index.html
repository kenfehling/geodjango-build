Home prices

The median home cost in Bridgeport is $88,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $21,060 (31%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower, and it is about $28,300 (24%) lower compared to the city of Scottsbluff.

Rents

The median rent in Bridgeport is $740 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $55 (8%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $322 (30%) lower, and it is about $38 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Scottsbluff.
