Home prices

The value of the median home in Juniata is $106,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $14,920 (16%) higher.It is about $110,700 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Island it is about $34,000 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Juniata is $758 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $55 (7%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $304 (28%) lower, and is about $9 (1%) lower compared to the city of Grand Island.
