Home prices

Valparaiso's median home price is $132,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $36,020 (37%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $84,600 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $26,800 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Valparaiso is $804 per month. This is approximately $99 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $258 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $119 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
