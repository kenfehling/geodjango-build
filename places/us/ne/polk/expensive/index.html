Home prices

The median home in Polk is valued at $53,500. This is roughly $24,080 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $164,000 (75%) lower, and it is about $116,300 (68%) lower compared to the city of Lincoln.

Rents

In Polk the median monthly rent is $738. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $106 (16%) higher. It is approximately $324 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Lincoln it is about $114 (13%) lower.
