Home prices

Rents

In Polk the median rent is $738 a month. This is approximately $106 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $324 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Lincoln is roughly $114 (13%) lower.
