Compared to its surrounding areas, Denton is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in Denton is valued at $131,300. It is roughly $10,260 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $86,200 (39%) lower, and is about $28,400 (17%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Denton is $700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $89 (11%) lower.It is roughly $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $223 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
