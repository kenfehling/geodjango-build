Home prices

The median home in Denton is valued at $131,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $10,260 (7%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $86,200 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is about $28,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Denton is $700 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $89 (11%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $362 (34%) lower, and is about $223 (24%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
