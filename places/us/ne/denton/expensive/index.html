In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denton is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in Denton is valued at $131,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $10,260 (7%) lower. It is approximately $86,200 (39%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $28,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Denton the median rent is $700 a month. This is roughly $89 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is about $223 (24%) lower.
