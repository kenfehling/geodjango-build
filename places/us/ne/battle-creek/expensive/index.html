Home prices

The cost of the median home in Battle Creek is $146,400. This is about $44,880 (44%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $71,100 (32%) lower, and it is about $29,800 (25%) higher compared to the city of Sioux City.

Rents

Battle Creek's median rent is $651 per month. It is roughly $173 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $411 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux City it is about $132 (16%) lower.
