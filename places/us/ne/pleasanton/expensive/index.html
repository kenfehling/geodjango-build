Home prices

The median home in Pleasanton costs $115,600. It is roughly $9,260 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $101,900 (46%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Island it is about $25,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Pleasanton the median rent is $936 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $215 (29%) higher. It is roughly $126 (11%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $169 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Grand Island.
