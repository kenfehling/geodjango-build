Rents

Unadilla's median rent is $692 a month. It is roughly $62 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $231 (25%) lower.
