Home prices

The price of the median home in Unadilla is $126,000. It is roughly $32,980 (35%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $91,500 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $33,700 (21%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Unadilla's median monthly rent is $692. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $62 (8%) lower. It is approximately $370 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $231 (25%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
