In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kenesaw is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Grand Island.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kenesaw is $105,700. It is approximately $22,660 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $111,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $35,100 (24%) lower compared to the city of Grand Island.

Rents

In Kenesaw the median monthly rent is $703. This is roughly $20 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $359 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Grand Island is about $64 (8%) lower.
