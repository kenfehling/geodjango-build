Home prices

The median home in Kenesaw is valued at $105,700. This is approximately $22,660 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $111,800 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $35,100 (24%) lower compared to the city of Grand Island.

Rents

In Kenesaw the median monthly rent is $703. It is about $20 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $359 (33%) lower, and is approximately $64 (8%) lower compared to the city of Grand Island.
