Home prices

The median home in Kenesaw is valued at $105,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $22,660 (27%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $111,800 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Island it is roughly $35,100 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kenesaw is $703 per month. This is about $20 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $359 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Island it is roughly $64 (8%) lower.
