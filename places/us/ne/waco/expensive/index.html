The cost of the median home in Waco is $108,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $13,860 (14%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $109,300 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is approximately $51,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waco is $656. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $43 (6%) lower. It is approximately $406 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is about $267 (28%) lower.
