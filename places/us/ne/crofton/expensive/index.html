Home prices

The price of the median home in Crofton is $102,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $5,360 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $115,200 (52%) lower, and it is approximately $87,500 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents
