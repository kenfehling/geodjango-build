Home prices

The median home in South Sioux City costs $116,200. This is approximately $70,880 (37%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $101,300 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is roughly $43,500 (27%) lower.

Rents

South Sioux City's median rent is $815 per month. This is about $85 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $247 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $108 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
