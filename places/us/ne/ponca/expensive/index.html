Home prices

The median home in Ponca costs $90,300. This is roughly $36,800 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $127,200 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $99,500 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ponca is $575 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $81 (12%) lower. It is approximately $487 (45%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is about $252 (30%) lower.
