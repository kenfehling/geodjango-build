Home prices

Ponca's median home price is $90,300. This is about $36,800 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $127,200 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $99,500 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Ponca the median rent is $575 per month. This is approximately $81 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $487 (45%) lower, and it is about $252 (30%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
