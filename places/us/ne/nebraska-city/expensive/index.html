Home prices

The value of the median home in Nebraska City is $117,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $48,640 (71%) higher. It is roughly $100,500 (46%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is roughly $42,700 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Nebraska City the median monthly rent is $701. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $361 (33%) lower, and it is about $222 (24%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
