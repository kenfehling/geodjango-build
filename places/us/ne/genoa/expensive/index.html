Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Genoa is $772 a month. This is about $57 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $290 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $80 (9%) lower compared to the city of Lincoln.
