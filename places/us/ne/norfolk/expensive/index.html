Home prices

The cost of the median home in Norfolk is $145,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $25,540 (21%) higher. It is about $72,100 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Sioux City it is about $28,800 (24%) higher.

Rents

Norfolk's median rent is $696 per month. It is approximately $42 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $366 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux City it is approximately $87 (11%) lower.
