Home prices

The median home value in Norfolk is $145,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $25,540 (21%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $72,100 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $28,800 (24%) higher compared to the city of Sioux City.

Rents

The median rent in Norfolk is $696 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $42 (6%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $366 (34%) lower, and is roughly $87 (11%) lower compared to the city of Sioux City.
