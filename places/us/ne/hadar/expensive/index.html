Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Hadar is $867 a month. This is approximately $88 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $195 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Sioux City is approximately $84 (10%) higher.
