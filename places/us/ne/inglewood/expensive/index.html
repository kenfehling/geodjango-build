Home prices

The value of the median home in Inglewood is $101,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $10,880 (9%) lower. It is about $116,400 (53%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $58,600 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Inglewood the median rent is $928 a month. It is approximately $41 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $134 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is is very similar.
