Home prices

The median home value in Osceola is $89,700. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $9,000 (11%) higher. It is approximately $127,800 (58%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $70,000 (43%) lower.

Rents

Osceola's median rent is $563 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $84 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $499 (46%) lower, and is roughly $360 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
