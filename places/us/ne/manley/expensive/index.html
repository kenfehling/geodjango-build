Home prices

Manley's median home price is $128,300. This is approximately $1,600 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $89,200 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is roughly $31,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Manley is $694 a month. This is about $23 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $368 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $229 (24%) lower.
