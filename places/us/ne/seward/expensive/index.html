Seward's median home cost is $158,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $40,920 (34%) higher. It is approximately $58,800 (27%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is is very similar.

Rents

Seward's median rent is $714 per month. This is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $348 (32%) lower, and is about $209 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
