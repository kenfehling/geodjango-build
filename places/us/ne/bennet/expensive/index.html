Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bennet is $909. This is roughly $57 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $153 (14%) lower, and is roughly $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
