Home prices

The cost of the median home in Springfield is $149,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $33,960 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $67,600 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $9,800 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Springfield is $907 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $23 (2%) higher. It is roughly $155 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
