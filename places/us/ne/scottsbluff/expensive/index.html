The median home in Scottsbluff is valued at $116,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $24,240 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $100,900 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cheyenne it is about $97,700 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scottsbluff is $778. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $55 (7%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $284 (26%) lower, and is approximately $137 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Cheyenne.
