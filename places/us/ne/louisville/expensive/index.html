Compared to its nearby areas, Louisville is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

Louisville's median home cost is $143,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $8,360 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $74,500 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $16,700 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Louisville is $525. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $316 (37%) lower. It is about $537 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $398 (43%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
