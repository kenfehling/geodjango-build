Home prices

Louisville's median home cost is $143,000. This is approximately $8,360 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $74,500 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $16,700 (10%) lower.

Rents

Louisville's median rent is $525 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $316 (37%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $537 (50%) lower, and is roughly $398 (43%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
