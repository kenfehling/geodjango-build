Rents

Ithaca's median monthly rent is $850. It is roughly $67 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $73 (7%) lower.
