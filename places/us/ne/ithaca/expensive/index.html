Compared to nearby areas, Ithaca is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in Ithaca costs $97,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $27,280 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $120,000 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $62,200 (38%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Ithaca is $850 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $67 (8%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $73 (7%) lower.
