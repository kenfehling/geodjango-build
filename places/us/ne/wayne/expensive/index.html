Home prices

The median home value in Wayne is $133,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $63,780 (92%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $84,500 (38%) lower, and is roughly $26,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Wayne is $645 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $9 (1%) higher.It is about $417 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is approximately $278 (30%) lower.
