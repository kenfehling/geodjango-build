The cost of the median home in Dunbar is $62,500. This is approximately $23,580 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $155,000 (71%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $97,200 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Dunbar's median rent is $670 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $44 (6%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $392 (36%) lower, and is about $253 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
