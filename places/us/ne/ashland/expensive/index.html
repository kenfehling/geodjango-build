Compared to the surrounding areas, Ashland is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Omaha, it is close to average.

Home prices

Ashland's median home cost is $156,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $21,060 (15%) higher.It is approximately $61,100 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $3,300 (2%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Ashland is $769 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $40 (4%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and is roughly $154 (16%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
