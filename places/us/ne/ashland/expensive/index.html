Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $156,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $21,060 (15%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $61,100 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $3,300 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $769. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $40 (4%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $293 (27%) lower, and is roughly $154 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
