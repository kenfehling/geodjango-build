Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $145,500. This is roughly $61,060 (72%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $72,000 (33%) lower, and is approximately $14,200 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $763. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $90 (10%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $299 (28%) lower, and it is about $160 (17%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
