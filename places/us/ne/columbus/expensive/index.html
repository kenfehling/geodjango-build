Home prices

Columbus's median home value is $145,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $61,060 (72%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $72,000 (33%) lower, and is about $14,200 (8%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Columbus the median rent is $763 a month. It is roughly $90 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $299 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $160 (17%) lower.
