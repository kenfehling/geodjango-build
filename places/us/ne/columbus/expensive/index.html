Home prices

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $763. This is roughly $90 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $160 (17%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
