Compared to other places in this area, Columbus is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $145,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $61,060 (72%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $72,000 (33%) lower, and is about $14,200 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Columbus is $763 a month. This is approximately $90 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $299 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $160 (17%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
