Home prices

The median home in David City is valued at $116,400. This is approximately $33,340 (40%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $101,100 (46%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $43,300 (27%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

David City's median rent is $772 a month. It is roughly $42 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $290 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $151 (16%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
