Home prices

The median home cost in Hastings is $119,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $26,780 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $97,700 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Island it is roughly $21,000 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Hastings the median monthly rent is $717. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $13 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $345 (32%) lower, and is roughly $50 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Island.
