Home prices

Wakefield's median home price is $94,400. This is about $13,400 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $123,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is approximately $65,300 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wakefield is $733. It is roughly $155 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $329 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is approximately $190 (20%) lower.
