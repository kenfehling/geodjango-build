Home prices

The median home value in Dakota City is $114,400. This is approximately $63,060 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $103,100 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $45,300 (28%) lower.

Rents

Dakota City's median monthly rent is $721. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $192 (21%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $341 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $202 (21%) lower.
