In comparison to its nearby areas, Dakota City is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dakota City is $114,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $63,060 (35%) lower. It is roughly $103,100 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Omaha it is approximately $45,300 (28%) lower.

Rents

Dakota City's median rent is $721 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $192 (21%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $202 (21%) lower.
