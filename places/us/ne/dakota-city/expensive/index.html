In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dakota City is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

Dakota City's median home price is $114,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $63,060 (35%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $103,100 (47%) lower, and it is about $45,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Dakota City is $721 a month. This is roughly $192 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $341 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $202 (21%) lower.
