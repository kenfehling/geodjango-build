In comparison to other places in this area, Dakota City is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Omaha, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Dakota City is $114,400. This is roughly $63,060 (35%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $103,100 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $45,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Dakota City is $721 per month. This is about $192 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $341 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha is about $202 (21%) lower.
