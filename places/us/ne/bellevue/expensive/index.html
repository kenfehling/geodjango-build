Home prices

The median home price in Bellevue is $148,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $3,860 (2%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $68,600 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $10,800 (6%) lower.

Rents

In Bellevue the median monthly rent is $944. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $18 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $118 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $21 (2%) higher compared to the city of Omaha.
