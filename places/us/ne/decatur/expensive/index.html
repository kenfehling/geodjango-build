Home prices

The median home in Decatur is valued at $78,500. It is approximately $6,620 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $139,000 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is about $81,200 (50%) lower.

Rents

Decatur's median monthly rent is $494. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $135 (21%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $568 (53%) lower, and is approximately $429 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
