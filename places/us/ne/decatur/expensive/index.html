Home prices

Decatur's median home cost is $78,500. It is approximately $6,620 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $139,000 (63%) lower, and it is approximately $81,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Decatur's median monthly rent is $494. This is approximately $135 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $568 (53%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is about $429 (46%) lower.
