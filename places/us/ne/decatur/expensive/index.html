Compared to its surrounding areas, Decatur is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in Decatur costs $78,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $6,620 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $139,000 (63%) lower, and is roughly $81,200 (50%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Decatur the median rent is $494 per month. It is approximately $135 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $568 (53%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Omaha is roughly $429 (46%) lower.
