Home prices

The median home in Bartlett costs $49,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $9,920 (16%) lower. It is roughly $168,300 (77%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $91,600 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Grand Island.

Rents

In Bartlett the median rent is $475 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $139 (22%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $587 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Island is approximately $292 (38%) lower.
