Compared to its surrounding areas, West Point is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Omaha, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Point is valued at $116,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $39,280 (50%) higher. It is approximately $100,600 (46%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $42,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in West Point is $716 a month. It is about $27 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $346 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $207 (22%) lower.
