In comparison to the surrounding areas, Syracuse is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Omaha, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Syracuse costs $133,700. It is about $46,720 (53%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $83,800 (38%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $26,000 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Syracuse the median rent is $611 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $142 (18%) lower. It is roughly $451 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $312 (33%) lower.
