Compared to nearby areas, Syracuse is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Syracuse is $133,700. It is roughly $46,720 (53%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $83,800 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $26,000 (16%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Syracuse is $611 a month. It is roughly $142 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $451 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha is about $312 (33%) lower.
