Compared to the surrounding areas, Syracuse is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home price in Syracuse is $133,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $46,720 (53%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $83,800 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is approximately $26,000 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Syracuse the median rent is $611 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $142 (18%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $451 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $312 (33%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
