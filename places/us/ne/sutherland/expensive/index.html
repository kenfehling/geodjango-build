Home prices

The median home in Sutherland is valued at $111,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $22,420 (25%) higher. It is about $106,000 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of North Platte it is approximately $7,400 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sutherland is $810. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $131 (19%) higher. It is about $252 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $53 (7%) higher compared to the city of North Platte.
