Home prices

The value of the median home in Sutherland is $111,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $22,420 (25%) higher. It is approximately $106,000 (48%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $7,400 (6%) lower compared to the city of North Platte.

Rents

In Sutherland the median monthly rent is $810. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $131 (19%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $252 (23%) lower, and is about $53 (7%) higher compared to the city of North Platte.
