Home prices

The cost of the median home in Murray is $126,000. It is roughly $17,980 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $91,500 (42%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha is approximately $33,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Murray is $794 per month. This is approximately $51 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $268 (25%) lower, and it is about $129 (13%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
