Compared to its surrounding areas, Sutton is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Lincoln, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sutton costs $105,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $22,760 (27%) higher. It is roughly $112,100 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $64,400 (37%) lower compared to the city of Lincoln.

Rents

Sutton's median rent is $701 a month. This is about $112 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $361 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $151 (17%) lower compared to the city of Lincoln.
