Humboldt's median home value is $46,800. It is about $7,760 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $170,700 (78%) lower, and it is roughly $112,900 (70%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Humboldt's median monthly rent is $429. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $129 (23%) lower. It is about $633 (59%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $494 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
