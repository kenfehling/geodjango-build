Compared to the surrounding areas, Falls City is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Falls City is valued at $69,800. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $15,120 (27%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $147,700 (67%) lower, and it is roughly $84,800 (54%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Falls City's median rent is $515 a month. This is approximately $16 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $547 (51%) lower, and is about $426 (45%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
