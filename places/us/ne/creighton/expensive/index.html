Home prices

The median home in Creighton is valued at $88,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $11,480 (14%) higher. It is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Norfolk is about $57,100 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Creighton is $592 per month. It is about $47 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $470 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Norfolk is roughly $104 (14%) lower.
