In comparison to other places in this area, Creighton is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Norfolk.

Home prices

The median home in Creighton costs $88,300. It is about $11,480 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $129,200 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Norfolk is approximately $57,100 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Creighton the median monthly rent is $592. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $47 (7%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $470 (44%) lower, and is about $104 (14%) lower compared to the city of Norfolk.
