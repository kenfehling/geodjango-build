Home prices

The median home value in Creighton is $88,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,480 (14%) higher. It is approximately $129,200 (59%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Norfolk it is approximately $57,100 (39%) lower.

Rents

Creighton's median monthly rent is $592. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $47 (7%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $470 (44%) lower, and is about $104 (14%) lower compared to the city of Norfolk.
