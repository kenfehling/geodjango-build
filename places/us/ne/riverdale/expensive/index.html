Home prices

Riverdale's median home price is $122,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $1,420 (1%) lower. It is approximately $95,000 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Grand Island is about $18,300 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Riverdale is $850 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $25 (3%) higher. It is about $212 (19%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $83 (10%) higher compared to the city of Grand Island.
