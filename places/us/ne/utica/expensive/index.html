Compared to its surrounding areas, Utica is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Omaha however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Utica is $131,400. It is approximately $41,120 (45%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $86,100 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha is approximately $28,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Utica the median rent is $750 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $89 (13%) higher. It is approximately $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $173 (18%) lower.
