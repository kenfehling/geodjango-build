Home prices

The value of the median home in Utica is $131,400. This is roughly $41,120 (45%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $86,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Omaha is roughly $28,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

Utica's median monthly rent is $750. It is about $89 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $312 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $173 (18%) lower.
