Rents

In St. Paul the median monthly rent is $684. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $100 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $378 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Grand Island is approximately $83 (10%) lower.
