Home prices

The median home in Stroud costs $83,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $14,820 (21%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $133,800 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $74,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Stroud is $688 per month. This is approximately $57 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $374 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is about $183 (21%) lower.
