Home prices

Marlow's median home cost is $101,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $8,620 (7%) lower.It is approximately $116,400 (53%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $57,400 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

In Marlow the median monthly rent is $682. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $27 (3%) lower.It is roughly $380 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $189 (21%) lower.
