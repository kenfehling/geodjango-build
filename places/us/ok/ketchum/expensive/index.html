Home prices

The value of the median home in Ketchum is $85,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,340 (14%) lower. It is roughly $132,500 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is approximately $54,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ketchum is $631 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $113 (15%) lower. It is approximately $431 (40%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $198 (23%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
