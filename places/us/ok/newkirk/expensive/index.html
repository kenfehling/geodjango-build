Rents

The median rent in Newkirk is $607 a month. This is about $124 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $455 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is roughly $222 (26%) lower.
