Home prices

The price of the median home in Panama is $60,000. This is roughly $11,440 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $157,500 (72%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $147,600 (71%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Panama the median monthly rent is $729. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $72 (10%) higher. It is roughly $333 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $70 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
