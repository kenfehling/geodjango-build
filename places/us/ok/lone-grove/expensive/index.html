Rents

The median monthly rent in Lone Grove is $866. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $122 (16%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $196 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Worth is roughly $194 (18%) lower.
