Home prices

Miami's median home price is $80,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $13,380 (20%) higher.It is approximately $137,300 (63%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $59,700 (42%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Miami the median monthly rent is $709. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $97 (15%) higher.It is roughly $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $120 (14%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
