Compared to nearby areas, Miami is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The median home in Miami costs $80,200. It is roughly $13,380 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $137,300 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $59,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miami is $709. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $97 (15%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $353 (33%) lower, and is about $120 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
