Home prices

The median home value in Miami is $80,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $13,380 (20%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $137,300 (63%) lower, and it is about $59,700 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miami is $709. It is roughly $97 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $353 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $120 (14%) lower.
