Compared to other places in this area, Miami is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Tulsa, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Miami is valued at $80,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,380 (20%) higher. It is approximately $137,300 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $59,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Miami is $709 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $97 (15%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $353 (33%) lower, and is about $120 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
