Compared to its surrounding areas, Moore is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

Moore's median home cost is $139,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14,560 (11%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $78,200 (35%) lower, and is about $19,200 (12%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Moore is $1,021. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $197 (23%) higher. It is roughly $41 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $150 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
