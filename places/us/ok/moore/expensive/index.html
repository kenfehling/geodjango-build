Home prices

The median home value in Moore is $139,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,560 (11%) higher. It is approximately $78,200 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $19,200 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Moore is $1,021. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $197 (23%) higher. It is roughly $41 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $150 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
