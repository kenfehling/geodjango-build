Compared to the surrounding areas, Moore is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Moore's median home cost is $139,300. It is about $14,560 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $78,200 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $19,200 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Moore the median rent is $1,021 a month. This is about $197 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $41 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $150 (17%) higher.
