Home prices

Rents

In Moore the median monthly rent is $1,021. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $197 (23%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $41 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $150 (17%) higher.
