Home prices

Rents

In Meridian the median rent is $830 per month. It is approximately $132 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $232 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $41 (4%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
