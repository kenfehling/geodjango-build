Rents

In Fort Cobb the median monthly rent is $525. It is approximately $31 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $537 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $346 (39%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
