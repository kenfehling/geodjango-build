Home prices

The price of the median home in Morrison is $132,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $46,720 (54%) higher. It is roughly $85,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $26,400 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morrison is $768. This is roughly $43 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $294 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $103 (11%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
