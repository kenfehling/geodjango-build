Compared to other places in this area, Kansas is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The median home in Kansas is valued at $84,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $9,900 (13%) higher. It is roughly $132,700 (61%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $55,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Kansas's median monthly rent is $666. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $134 (16%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $396 (37%) lower, and is approximately $163 (19%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
