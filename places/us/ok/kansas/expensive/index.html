In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kansas is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The median home in Kansas costs $84,800. This is about $9,900 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $132,700 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $55,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Kansas's median rent is $666 per month. It is about $134 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $396 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is about $163 (19%) lower.
