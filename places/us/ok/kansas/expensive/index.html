Compared to the surrounding areas, Kansas is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

Kansas's median home cost is $84,800. This is roughly $9,900 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $132,700 (61%) lower, and it is roughly $55,100 (39%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Kansas's median monthly rent is $666. This is about $134 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $396 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is approximately $163 (19%) lower.
