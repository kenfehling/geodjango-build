Home prices

Prague's median home price is $107,300. This is approximately $26,260 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $110,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $51,200 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Prague the median rent is $633 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $21 (3%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $429 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is about $238 (27%) lower.
