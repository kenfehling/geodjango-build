Home prices

Chattanooga's median home price is $78,100. This is roughly $16,880 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $139,400 (64%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $80,400 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Chattanooga the median rent is $1,054 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $402 (61%) higher.It is is very similar compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is roughly $183 (21%) higher.
