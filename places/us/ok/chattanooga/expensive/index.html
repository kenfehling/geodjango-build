Compared to other places in this area, Chattanooga is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Chattanooga is $78,100. This is roughly $16,880 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $139,400 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is roughly $80,400 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chattanooga is $1,054. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $402 (61%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $183 (21%) higher.
