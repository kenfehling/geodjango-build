Home prices

Chattanooga's median home price is $78,100. It is approximately $16,880 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $139,400 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $80,400 (50%) lower.

Rents

Chattanooga's median rent is $1,054 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $402 (61%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $183 (21%) higher.
