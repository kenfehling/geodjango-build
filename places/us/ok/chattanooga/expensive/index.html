Home prices

The median home cost in Chattanooga is $78,100. This is about $16,880 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $139,400 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $80,400 (50%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

In Chattanooga the median monthly rent is $1,054. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $402 (61%) higher.It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is about $183 (21%) higher.
