Home prices

The value of the median home in Fort Towson is $87,100. It is approximately $6,460 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $130,400 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Greenville it is approximately $17,300 (16%) lower.

Rents

Fort Towson's median rent is $521 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $122 (18%) lower. It is about $541 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Greenville is about $379 (42%) lower.
