Home prices

The median home in Crescent is valued at $103,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $11,280 (12%) higher. It is roughly $114,000 (52%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $55,000 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Crescent is $695 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $73 (9%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $367 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $176 (20%) lower.
