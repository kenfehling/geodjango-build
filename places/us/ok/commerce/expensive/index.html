In comparison to nearby areas, Commerce is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The median home in Commerce is valued at $58,700. This is about $10,060 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $158,800 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $81,200 (58%) lower.

Rents

Commerce's median monthly rent is $594. This is about $44 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $468 (44%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $235 (28%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
