Home prices

Rents

In Commerce the median monthly rent is $594. It is approximately $44 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $468 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $235 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
