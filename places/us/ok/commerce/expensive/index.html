Home prices

Rents

In Commerce the median monthly rent is $594. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $44 (6%) lower.It is approximately $468 (44%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa is approximately $235 (28%) lower.
