Compared to its surrounding areas, Union City is substantially more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Union City costs $199,200. This is about $58,320 (41%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $18,300 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is about $40,700 (25%) higher.

Rents

In Union City the median rent is $708 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $127 (15%) lower. It is about $354 (33%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is about $163 (18%) lower.
