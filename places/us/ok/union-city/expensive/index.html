Home prices

The value of the median home in Union City is $199,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $58,320 (41%) higher.It is approximately $18,300 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $40,700 (25%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Union City is $708 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $127 (15%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $354 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $163 (18%) lower.
