Home prices

The median home in Ponca City costs $96,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $18,100 (23%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $120,900 (55%) lower, and is about $61,900 (39%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

Ponca City's median rent is $678 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $54 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $384 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is about $193 (22%) lower.
