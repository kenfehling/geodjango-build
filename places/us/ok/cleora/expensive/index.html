Home prices

The median home price in Cleora is $172,500. This is about $66,560 (62%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $45,000 (20%) lower, and it is about $32,600 (23%) higher compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median rent in Cleora is $850 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $139 (19%) higher.It is approximately $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $21 (2%) higher compared to the city of Tulsa.
