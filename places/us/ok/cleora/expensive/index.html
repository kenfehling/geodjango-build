Home prices

The median home in Cleora is valued at $172,500. This is approximately $66,560 (62%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $45,000 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $32,600 (23%) higher compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median rent in Cleora is $850 per month. It is about $139 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $21 (2%) higher compared to the city of Tulsa.
