Home prices

The median home in Guthrie costs $125,400. This is approximately $13,760 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $92,100 (42%) lower, and is roughly $33,100 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Guthrie is $787. This is roughly $52 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $275 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is about $84 (9%) lower.
