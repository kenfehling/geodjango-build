Home prices

The cost of the median home in Guthrie is $125,400. This is about $13,760 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $92,100 (42%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $33,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Guthrie is $787. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $52 (6%) lower. It is approximately $275 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $84 (9%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
