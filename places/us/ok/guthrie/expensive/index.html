Compared to the surrounding areas, Guthrie is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

The median home in Guthrie is valued at $125,400. This is about $13,760 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $92,100 (42%) lower, and is approximately $33,100 (20%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

In Guthrie the median monthly rent is $787. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $52 (6%) lower. It is about $275 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $84 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
