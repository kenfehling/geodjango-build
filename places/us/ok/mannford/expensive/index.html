Home prices

The value of the median home in Mannford is $123,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $13,700 (12%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $94,200 (43%) lower, and is approximately $35,200 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

Mannford's median monthly rent is $815. Compared to nearby areas it is about $101 (14%) higher.It is approximately $247 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $56 (6%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
