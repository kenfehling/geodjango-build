Home prices

The median home in Mannford is valued at $123,300. This is roughly $13,700 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $94,200 (43%) lower, and is roughly $35,200 (22%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mannford is $815. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $101 (14%) higher. It is roughly $247 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $56 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
