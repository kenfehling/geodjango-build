Compared to other places in this area, Mannford is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mannford is $123,300. This is roughly $13,700 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $94,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $35,200 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Mannford the median rent is $815 per month. It is roughly $101 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $247 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $56 (6%) lower.
