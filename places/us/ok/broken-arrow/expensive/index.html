Home prices

The median home price in Broken Arrow is $166,900. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $50,600 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $27,000 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Broken Arrow's median rent is $1,037 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $45 (4%) higher. It is roughly $25 (2%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is about $208 (25%) higher.
