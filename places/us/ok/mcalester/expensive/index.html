Compared to the surrounding areas, McAlester is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The median home in McAlester is valued at $101,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $12,740 (14%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $115,900 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is about $38,300 (27%) lower.

Rents

McAlester's median rent is $730 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $17 (2%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $332 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa is about $99 (11%) lower.
