Home prices

The cost of the median home in McAlester is $101,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $12,740 (14%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $115,900 (53%) lower, and is approximately $38,300 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents
