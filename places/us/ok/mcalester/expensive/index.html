Compared to other places in this area, McAlester is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The median home price in McAlester is $101,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $12,740 (14%) higher.It is about $115,900 (53%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is about $38,300 (27%) lower.

Rents

In McAlester the median rent is $730 a month. This is roughly $17 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $332 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $99 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
