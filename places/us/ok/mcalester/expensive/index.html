Home prices

The value of the median home in McAlester is $101,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $12,740 (14%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $115,900 (53%) lower, and is roughly $38,300 (27%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In McAlester the median rent is $730 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $17 (2%) higher.It is roughly $332 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is roughly $99 (11%) lower.
