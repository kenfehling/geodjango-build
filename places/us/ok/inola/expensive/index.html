Compared to other places in this area, Inola is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The median home in Inola is valued at $117,200. This is about $27,320 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $100,300 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $22,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Inola the median rent is $775 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $250 (24%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $54 (6%) lower.
