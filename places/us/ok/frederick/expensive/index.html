Home prices

The value of the median home in Frederick is $51,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $5,140 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $165,800 (76%) lower, and it is roughly $61,100 (54%) lower compared to the city of Lawton.

Rents

Frederick's median monthly rent is $631. In comparison to nearby areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $431 (40%) lower, and is approximately $201 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Lawton.
