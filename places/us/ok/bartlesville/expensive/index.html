Home prices

The value of the median home in Bartlesville is $123,200. This is approximately $48,720 (65%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $94,300 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is roughly $16,700 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bartlesville is $744 per month. It is about $49 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $318 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa is approximately $85 (10%) lower.
