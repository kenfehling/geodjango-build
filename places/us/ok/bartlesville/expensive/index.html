Home prices

The median home price in Bartlesville is $123,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $48,720 (65%) higher. It is about $94,300 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $16,700 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Bartlesville's median rent is $744 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $49 (6%) lower. It is roughly $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa is approximately $85 (10%) lower.
