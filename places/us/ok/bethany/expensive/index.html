In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bethany is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bethany is $119,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $162,660 (57%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $97,600 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $38,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bethany is $855. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $237 (21%) lower. It is about $207 (19%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $16 (1%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
