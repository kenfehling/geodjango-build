Home prices

The median home in Bethany costs $119,900. This is approximately $162,660 (57%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $97,600 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is about $38,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bethany is $855 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $237 (21%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $207 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
