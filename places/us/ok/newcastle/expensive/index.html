Home prices

The median home value in Newcastle is $197,900. It is roughly $9,740 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $19,600 (9%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $39,400 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newcastle is $769. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $61 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $102 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
