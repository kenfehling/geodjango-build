Home prices

The median home in Salina is valued at $79,400. It is about $21,620 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $138,100 (63%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $60,500 (43%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Salina the median monthly rent is $673. It is roughly $75 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $389 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa is roughly $156 (18%) lower.
