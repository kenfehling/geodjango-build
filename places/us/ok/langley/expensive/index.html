Home prices

The median home price in Langley is $86,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $7,500 (7%) lower. It is approximately $131,200 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $53,600 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median rent in Langley is $653 a month. It is about $68 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $409 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is approximately $176 (21%) lower.
