Home prices

Wyandotte's median home price is $64,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $23,180 (26%) lower. It is about $153,300 (70%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $75,700 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Wyandotte's median monthly rent is $575. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $85 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $487 (45%) lower, and it is about $254 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
