Home prices

The price of the median home in Wyandotte is $64,200. It is roughly $23,180 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $153,300 (70%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $75,700 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wyandotte is $575. This is approximately $85 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $254 (30%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
