In comparison to other places in this area, Wyandotte is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Tulsa, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Wyandotte is $64,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $23,180 (26%) lower. It is roughly $153,300 (70%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $75,700 (54%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wyandotte is $575. It is approximately $85 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $487 (45%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa is approximately $254 (30%) lower.
