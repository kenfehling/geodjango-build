Home prices

The median home cost in Cleveland is $86,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $10,320 (13%) higher. It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $71,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $720 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $88 (13%) higher. It is about $342 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $151 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
