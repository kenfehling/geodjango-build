Home prices

The median home in Cleveland costs $86,700. This is roughly $10,320 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $130,800 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $71,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $720 per month. It is about $88 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $342 (32%) lower, and it is about $151 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
