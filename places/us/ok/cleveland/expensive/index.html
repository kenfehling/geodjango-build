Home prices

The median home value in Cleveland is $86,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $10,320 (13%) higher.It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $71,800 (45%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $720 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $88 (13%) higher.It is approximately $342 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $151 (17%) lower.
