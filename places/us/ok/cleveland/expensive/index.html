Home prices

The value of the median home in Cleveland is $86,700. It is roughly $10,320 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $71,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $720. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $88 (13%) higher.It is approximately $342 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $151 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
