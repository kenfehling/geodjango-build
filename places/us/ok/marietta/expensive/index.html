The price of the median home in Marietta is $83,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $5,920 (6%) lower.It is roughly $133,900 (61%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $104,500 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.

Rents

Marietta's median rent is $705 a month. This is about $54 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $357 (33%) lower, and is about $347 (32%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
