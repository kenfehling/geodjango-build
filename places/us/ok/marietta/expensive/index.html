Home prices

Marietta's median home value is $83,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $5,920 (6%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $133,900 (61%) lower, and it is about $104,500 (55%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.

Rents

In Marietta the median monthly rent is $705. It is approximately $54 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $357 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $347 (32%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
