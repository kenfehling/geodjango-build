Compared to its surrounding areas, Marietta is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

Marietta's median home price is $83,600. This is about $5,920 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $133,900 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas is about $104,500 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marietta is $705 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $54 (7%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $357 (33%) lower, and is approximately $347 (32%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
