Rents

Stillwater's median rent is $832 a month. It is approximately $141 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $230 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $39 (4%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
