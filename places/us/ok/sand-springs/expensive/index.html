Compared to the surrounding areas, Sand Springs is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Sand Springs's median home cost is $127,800. This is about $18,260 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $89,700 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $30,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

Sand Springs's median monthly rent is $868. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $77 (9%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $194 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is is very similar.
