Compared to its surrounding areas, Sand Springs is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Oklahoma City however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sand Springs is valued at $127,800. It is about $18,260 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $89,700 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is roughly $30,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Sand Springs is $868 per month. It is roughly $77 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $194 (18%) lower compared to the entire country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
