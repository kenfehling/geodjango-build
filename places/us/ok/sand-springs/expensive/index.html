Compared to the surrounding areas, Sand Springs is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

The median home in Sand Springs is valued at $127,800. This is roughly $18,260 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $89,700 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $30,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sand Springs is $868. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $77 (9%) higher.It is approximately $194 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is is very similar.
