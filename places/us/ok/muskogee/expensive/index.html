Home prices

The median home in Muskogee costs $92,300. This is approximately $4,060 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $125,200 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $47,600 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Muskogee's median rent is $685 a month. It is approximately $42 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $377 (35%) lower, and is roughly $144 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
