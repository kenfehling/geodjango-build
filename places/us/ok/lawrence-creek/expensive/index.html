Compared to the surrounding areas, Lawrence Creek is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrence Creek is valued at $137,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $47,280 (52%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $80,000 (36%) lower, and is approximately $21,000 (13%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

In Lawrence Creek the median monthly rent is $575. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $122 (17%) lower.It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $296 (33%) lower.
