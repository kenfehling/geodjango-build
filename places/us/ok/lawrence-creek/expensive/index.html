Home prices

The median home price in Lawrence Creek is $137,500. It is about $47,280 (52%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $80,000 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is roughly $21,000 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Lawrence Creek the median rent is $575 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $122 (17%) lower.It is roughly $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $296 (33%) lower.
