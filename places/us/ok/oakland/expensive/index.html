Home prices

The median home value in Oakland is $77,200. This is roughly $4,720 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $140,300 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is roughly $110,900 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakland is $683. It is approximately $21 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $379 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $369 (35%) lower.
