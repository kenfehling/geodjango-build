Home prices

Oakland's median home cost is $77,200. This is roughly $4,720 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Dallas is about $110,900 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Oakland the median monthly rent is $683. This is approximately $21 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $369 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Dallas.
