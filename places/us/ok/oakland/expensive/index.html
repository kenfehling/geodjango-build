In comparison to other places in this area, Oakland is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Dallas, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Oakland is $77,200. It is about $4,720 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is about $110,900 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Oakland is $683 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $21 (3%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $379 (35%) lower, and is approximately $369 (35%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
