Home prices

The price of the median home in Lexington is $86,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $44,520 (34%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $131,100 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $72,100 (45%) lower.

Rents

Lexington's median monthly rent is $784. Compared to nearby areas this is about $35 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $278 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $87 (9%) lower.
