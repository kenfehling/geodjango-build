Home prices

The value of the median home in Norman is $183,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $41,160 (28%) higher.It is roughly $34,300 (15%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $24,700 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Norman the median rent is $876 a month. This is about $15 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $186 (17%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
