In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chickasha is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chickasha is $85,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $17,640 (25%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $131,600 (60%) lower, and it is approximately $72,600 (45%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

In Chickasha the median rent is $713 per month. This is roughly $53 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $158 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
