Compared to its surrounding areas, Chickasha is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chickasha is $85,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $17,640 (25%) higher.It is approximately $131,600 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $72,600 (45%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median rent in Chickasha is $713 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $53 (8%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is roughly $158 (18%) lower.
