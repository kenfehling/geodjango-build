Home prices

The median home cost in Flint Creek is $63,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $15,180 (19%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $153,600 (70%) lower, and it is roughly $76,000 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Flint Creek's median rent is $928 a month. This is about $180 (24%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $134 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $99 (11%) higher compared to the city of Tulsa.
