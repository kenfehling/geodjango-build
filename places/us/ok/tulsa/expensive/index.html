Compared to other places in this area, Tulsa is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home in Tulsa costs $139,900. This is approximately $11,417 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $77,600 (35%) lower compared to the entire country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tulsa is $829. This is approximately $98 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $233 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country.
