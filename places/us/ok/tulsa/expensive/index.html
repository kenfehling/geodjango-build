Compared to other places in this area, Tulsa is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Tulsa is $139,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,417 (8%) higher. It is approximately $77,600 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole.

Rents

The median rent in Tulsa is $829 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $98 (10%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $233 (21%) lower.
