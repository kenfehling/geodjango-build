Home prices

The median home in Oklahoma City costs $158,500. It is roughly $73,067 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $59,000 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation.

Rents

In Oklahoma City the median rent is $871 per month. It is approximately $171 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $191 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole.
