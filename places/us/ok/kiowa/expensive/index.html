Home prices

The value of the median home in Kiowa is $59,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $23,580 (28%) lower. It is roughly $157,700 (72%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Muskogee it is roughly $32,500 (35%) lower.

Rents

Kiowa's median monthly rent is $674. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $81 (10%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $388 (36%) lower, and is approximately $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Muskogee.
