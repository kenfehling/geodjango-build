Home prices

The median home price in Del City is $84,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $30,120 (26%) lower. It is approximately $133,300 (61%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $74,300 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Del City is $854 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is is very similar. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $208 (19%) lower, and it is about $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
