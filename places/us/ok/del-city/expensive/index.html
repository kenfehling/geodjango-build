Home prices

The median home in Del City costs $84,200. It is approximately $30,120 (26%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $133,300 (61%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $74,300 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Del City the median rent is $854 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $208 (19%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $17 (1%) lower.
