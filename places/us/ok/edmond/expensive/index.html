Home prices

The median home in Edmond costs $240,600. It is approximately $20,560 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $23,100 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is about $82,100 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Edmond the median rent is $1,047 a month. It is approximately $31 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $176 (20%) higher.
