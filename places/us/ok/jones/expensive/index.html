Home prices

The median home in Jones is valued at $132,300. It is about $12,400 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $85,200 (39%) lower, and it is roughly $26,200 (16%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median rent in Jones is $745 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $72 (8%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $317 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is roughly $126 (14%) lower.
