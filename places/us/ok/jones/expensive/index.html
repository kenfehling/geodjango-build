Home prices

The median home in Jones is valued at $132,300. It is approximately $12,400 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $85,200 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is roughly $26,200 (16%) lower.

Rents

Jones's median rent is $745 a month. This is roughly $72 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $317 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is about $126 (14%) lower.
