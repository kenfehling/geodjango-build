Home prices

The median home in Lenapah is valued at $51,300. This is roughly $8,300 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $166,200 (76%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa is roughly $88,600 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Lenapah the median monthly rent is $647. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $61 (8%) lower. It is approximately $415 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is about $182 (21%) lower.
