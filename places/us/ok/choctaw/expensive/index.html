Compared to the surrounding areas, Choctaw is substantially more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Choctaw costs $178,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $60,940 (51%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $38,900 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is about $20,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Choctaw is $938 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $116 (14%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $124 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is about $67 (7%) higher.
