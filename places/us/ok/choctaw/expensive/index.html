Compared to its surrounding areas, Choctaw is a lot more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Choctaw is $178,600. It is about $60,940 (51%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $38,900 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $20,100 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

In Choctaw the median monthly rent is $938. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $116 (14%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $67 (7%) higher.
