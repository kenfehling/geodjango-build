Home prices

Choctaw's median home cost is $178,600. It is about $60,940 (51%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $38,900 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $20,100 (12%) higher compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

Choctaw's median rent is $938 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $116 (14%) higher.It is roughly $124 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $67 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
