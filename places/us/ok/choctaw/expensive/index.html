Home prices

The median home value in Choctaw is $178,600. This is roughly $60,940 (51%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $38,900 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $20,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

Choctaw's median rent is $938 per month. This is about $116 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $67 (7%) higher.
