Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pittsburg is $57,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $18,320 (24%) lower. It is about $159,700 (73%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $34,500 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Muskogee.

Rents

In Pittsburg the median rent is $663 a month. This is approximately $21 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $399 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Muskogee is roughly $22 (3%) lower.
