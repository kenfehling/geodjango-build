Home prices

The median home in Rock Island is valued at $98,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $12,640 (14%) higher. It is roughly $119,000 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $109,100 (52%) lower.

Rents

Rock Island's median rent is $669 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $72 (9%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $393 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $130 (16%) lower.
