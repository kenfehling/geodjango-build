Home prices

Owasso's median home value is $172,700. This is roughly $64,220 (59%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $44,800 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $32,800 (23%) higher compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Owasso the median rent is $996 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $91 (10%) higher. It is roughly $66 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa is roughly $167 (20%) higher.
