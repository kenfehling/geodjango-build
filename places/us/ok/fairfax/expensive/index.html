Compared to its surrounding areas, Fairfax is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Home prices

The median home in Fairfax is valued at $37,600. This is approximately $17,600 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $179,900 (82%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $120,900 (76%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $463. It is roughly $90 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $599 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City is approximately $408 (46%) lower.
