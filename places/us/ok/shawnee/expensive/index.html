Home prices

Shawnee's median home cost is $109,600. This is roughly $10,440 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $107,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $48,900 (30%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Shawnee is $697. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $70 (9%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $365 (34%) lower, and is about $174 (19%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
