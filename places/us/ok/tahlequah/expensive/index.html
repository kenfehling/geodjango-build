Home prices

Tahlequah's median home price is $124,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $32,260 (34%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $92,900 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $15,300 (10%) lower.

Rents

Tahlequah's median rent is $681 a month. It is roughly $65 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $381 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $148 (17%) lower.
