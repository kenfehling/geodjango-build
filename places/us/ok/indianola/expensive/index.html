Home prices

The median home in Indianola costs $66,700. This is approximately $14,120 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $150,800 (69%) lower, and it is about $73,200 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

Indianola's median rent is $725 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $140 (23%) higher. It is about $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $104 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
