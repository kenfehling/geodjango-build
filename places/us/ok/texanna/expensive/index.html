Home prices

The median home in Texanna costs $131,000. It is approximately $50,220 (62%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $86,500 (39%) lower, and is roughly $8,900 (6%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Texanna the median monthly rent is $613. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $53 (9%) higher. It is about $449 (42%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is about $216 (26%) lower.
