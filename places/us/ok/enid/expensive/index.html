Home prices

The median home in Enid costs $108,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $9,720 (9%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $109,200 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $50,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

Enid's median monthly rent is $860. It is about $11 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $202 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is about $11 (1%) lower.
