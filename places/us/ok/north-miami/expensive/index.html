Compared to its surrounding areas, North Miami is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tulsa.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Miami is $57,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $11,980 (17%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $160,400 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is approximately $82,800 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Miami is $675. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $53 (8%) higher.It is roughly $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $154 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
