Home prices

The price of the median home in Durant is $123,300. It is roughly $41,740 (51%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $94,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $64,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Durant is $748 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $61 (7%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $314 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Dallas it is approximately $304 (28%) lower.
