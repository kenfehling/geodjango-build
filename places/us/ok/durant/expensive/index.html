Compared to the surrounding areas, Durant is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Dallas.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Durant is $123,300. This is roughly $41,740 (51%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $94,200 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Dallas it is about $64,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Durant is $748 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $61 (7%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $314 (29%) lower, and is about $304 (28%) lower compared to the city of Dallas.
