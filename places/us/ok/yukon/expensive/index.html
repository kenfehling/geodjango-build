Home prices

Yukon's median home price is $143,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $1,520 (1%) lower. It is roughly $74,400 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $15,400 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

In Yukon the median rent is $948 a month. This is about $132 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $114 (10%) lower, and is approximately $77 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.
