Home prices

The value of the median home in Yukon is $143,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $1,520 (1%) lower. It is about $74,400 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $15,400 (9%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

Yukon's median rent is $948 a month. It is about $132 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $114 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is about $77 (8%) higher.
