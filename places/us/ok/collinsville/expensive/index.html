Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Collinsville is $1,029 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $147 (16%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $33 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $200 (24%) higher.
