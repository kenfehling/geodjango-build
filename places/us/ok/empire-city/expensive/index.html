Compared to the surrounding areas, Empire City is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Oklahoma City, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Empire City costs $115,100. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $8,420 (7%) higher.It is approximately $102,400 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Oklahoma City it is about $43,400 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Empire City the median monthly rent is $792. It is roughly $91 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $79 (9%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
