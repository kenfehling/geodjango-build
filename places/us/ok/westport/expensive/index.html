Home prices

The median home in Westport is valued at $165,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $75,060 (83%) higher. It is about $52,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $6,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westport is $917. This is about $273 (42%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $145 (13%) lower, and is roughly $46 (5%) higher compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
