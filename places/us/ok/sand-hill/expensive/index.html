Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sand Hill is $106,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,480 (15%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $111,200 (51%) lower, and is about $33,600 (24%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Sand Hill the median monthly rent is $775. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $192 (32%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $54 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
