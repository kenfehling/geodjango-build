Home prices

The median home value in Winchester is $155,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $13,360 (9%) higher. It is approximately $61,900 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $2,900 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Winchester is $757. This is approximately $109 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $305 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $114 (13%) lower compared to the city of Oklahoma City.
