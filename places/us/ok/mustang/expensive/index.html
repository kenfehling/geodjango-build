The median home in Mustang costs $160,200. This is about $14,880 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $57,300 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City it is approximately $1,700 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mustang is $1,056. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $283 (36%) higher. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Oklahoma City is roughly $185 (21%) higher.
