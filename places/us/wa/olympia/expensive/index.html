In comparison to the surrounding areas, Olympia is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home value in Olympia is $297,200. It is approximately $39,760 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $79,700 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $365,900 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Olympia's median monthly rent is $1,145. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $171 (12%) lower.It is about $83 (7%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $469 (29%) lower.
