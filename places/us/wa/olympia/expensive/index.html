In comparison to other places in this area, Olympia is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Olympia costs $297,200. It is approximately $39,760 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $79,700 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $365,900 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Olympia is $1,145. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $171 (12%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $83 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $469 (29%) lower.
