Compared to its nearby areas, Olympia is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home value in Olympia is $297,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $39,760 (15%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $79,700 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $365,900 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Olympia the median rent is $1,145 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $171 (12%) lower. It is approximately $83 (7%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $469 (29%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
