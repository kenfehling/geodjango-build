Compared to its nearby areas, Olympia is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Olympia is valued at $297,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $39,760 (15%) higher. It is approximately $79,700 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $365,900 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Olympia is $1,145 per month. This is roughly $171 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $83 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $469 (29%) lower.
