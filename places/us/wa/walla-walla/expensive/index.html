Home prices

Walla Walla's median home value is $203,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $3,060 (1%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $14,000 (6%) lower, and it is about $12,000 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Kennewick.

Rents

Walla Walla's median rent is $962 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $111 (13%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $100 (9%) lower, and is roughly $40 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Kennewick.
