In comparison to other places in this area, Mount Vernon is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mount Vernon is $269,100. This is approximately $32,320 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $51,600 (23%) higher, and it is about $394,000 (59%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Vernon is $1,019. It is approximately $30 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $43 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $595 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
