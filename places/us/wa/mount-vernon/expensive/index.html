In comparison to its nearby areas, Mount Vernon is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon costs $269,100. This is approximately $32,320 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $51,600 (23%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $394,000 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Vernon is $1,019 per month. This is about $30 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $43 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $595 (36%) lower.
