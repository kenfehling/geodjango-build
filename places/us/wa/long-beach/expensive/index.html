Home prices

Long Beach's median home cost is $200,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $25,020 (11%) lower. It is about $16,700 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $141,700 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Hillsboro.

Rents

Long Beach's median monthly rent is $655. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $256 (28%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $407 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $798 (54%) lower compared to the city of Hillsboro.
