Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairfield is $155,300. This is roughly $24,400 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $32,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Spokane.

Rents

The median rent in Fairfield is $1,010 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $258 (34%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $52 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Spokane it is approximately $144 (16%) higher.
