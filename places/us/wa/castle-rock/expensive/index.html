Home prices

The cost of the median home in Castle Rock is $165,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $17,280 (9%) lower. It is approximately $52,100 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $497,700 (75%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Castle Rock the median monthly rent is $795. It is roughly $97 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $819 (50%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
