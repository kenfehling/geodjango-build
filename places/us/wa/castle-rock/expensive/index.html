Compared to other places in this area, Castle Rock is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Castle Rock is $165,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $17,280 (9%) lower.It is about $52,100 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $497,700 (75%) lower.

Rents

Castle Rock's median monthly rent is $795. This is about $97 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $819 (50%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
