Home prices

The median home in Hockinson is valued at $461,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $103,820 (29%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $243,900 (112%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $49,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

Hockinson's median rent is $1,800 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $326 (22%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $738 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $552 (44%) higher.
