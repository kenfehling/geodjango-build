Home prices

The median home in Hockinson costs $461,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $103,820 (29%) higher. It is approximately $243,900 (112%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $49,400 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hockinson the median rent is $1,800 a month. It is about $326 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $738 (69%) higher, and it is about $552 (44%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
