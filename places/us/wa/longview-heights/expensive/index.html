In comparison to the surrounding areas, Longview Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Longview Heights costs $224,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $46,400 (26%) higher. It is approximately $6,900 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $187,600 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Longview Heights is $924 per month. This is about $115 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $138 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $324 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
