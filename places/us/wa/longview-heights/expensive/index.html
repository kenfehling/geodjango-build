Home prices

The median home in Longview Heights is valued at $224,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $46,400 (26%) higher.It is roughly $6,900 (3%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $187,600 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Longview Heights is $924. It is approximately $115 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $138 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $324 (25%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
