Home prices

Battle Ground's median home cost is $282,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $164,520 (36%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $65,400 (30%) higher, and is roughly $129,100 (31%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Battle Ground is $1,245 per month. It is roughly $327 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $183 (17%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is is very similar.
