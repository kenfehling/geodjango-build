In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bremerton is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bremerton costs $238,600. This is about $14,680 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $21,100 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $424,500 (64%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Bremerton the median monthly rent is $1,033. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $131 (11%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $29 (2%) lower, and is about $581 (35%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
