Compared to other places in this area, Bremerton is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bremerton is valued at $238,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $14,680 (5%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $21,100 (9%) higher, and is about $424,500 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Bremerton the median rent is $1,033 per month. It is approximately $131 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $29 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $581 (35%) lower.
