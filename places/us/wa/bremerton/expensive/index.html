Home prices

The median home cost in Bremerton is $238,600. This is about $14,680 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $21,100 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $424,500 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bremerton is $1,033. This is about $131 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $29 (2%) lower, and is about $581 (35%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
