In comparison to its nearby areas, Bremerton is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bremerton is valued at $238,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $14,680 (5%) lower. It is approximately $21,100 (9%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $424,500 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Bremerton is $1,033 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $131 (11%) lower. It is approximately $29 (2%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $581 (35%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
