Compared to other places in this area, White Center is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in White Center is $383,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $36,480 (10%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $166,400 (76%) higher, and is approximately $279,200 (42%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in White Center is $1,192 a month. It is roughly $134 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $130 (12%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $422 (26%) lower.
