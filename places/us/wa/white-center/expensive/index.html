Home prices

The median home cost in White Center is $383,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $36,480 (10%) higher.It is roughly $166,400 (76%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $279,200 (42%) lower.

Rents

In White Center the median rent is $1,192 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $134 (10%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $130 (12%) higher, and is roughly $422 (26%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
