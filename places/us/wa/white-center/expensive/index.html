Home prices

The median home in White Center costs $383,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $36,480 (10%) higher. It is approximately $166,400 (76%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $279,200 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in White Center is $1,192 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $134 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $130 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $422 (26%) lower.
