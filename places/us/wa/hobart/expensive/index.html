Home prices

The median home in Hobart is valued at $551,200. It is about $99,680 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $333,700 (153%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $111,900 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Hobart is $1,601 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $82 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $539 (50%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Seattle.
