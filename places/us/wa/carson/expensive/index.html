Compared to other places in this area, Carson is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Carson is $228,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $44,220 (16%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $10,800 (4%) higher, and it is about $183,700 (44%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carson is $1,006. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $133 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $56 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $242 (19%) lower.
