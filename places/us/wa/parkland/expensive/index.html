Compared to other places in this area, Parkland is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Parkland's median home cost is $223,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $37,540 (14%) lower.It is approximately $5,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $439,700 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Parkland's median rent is $1,106 a month. It is about $103 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $44 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $508 (31%) lower.
