In comparison to other places in this area, Parkland is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Parkland is valued at $223,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $37,540 (14%) lower. It is approximately $5,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $439,700 (66%) lower.

Rents

Parkland's median rent is $1,106 per month. It is approximately $103 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $44 (4%) higher, and is about $508 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
