In comparison to nearby areas, Parkland is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Parkland's median home price is $223,400. This is roughly $37,540 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $5,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $439,700 (66%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Parkland's median rent is $1,106 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $103 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $44 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $508 (31%) lower.
