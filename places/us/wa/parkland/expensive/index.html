In comparison to nearby areas, Parkland is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Parkland's median home cost is $223,400. This is about $37,540 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $5,900 (2%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $439,700 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Parkland the median rent is $1,106 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $103 (8%) lower.It is roughly $44 (4%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $508 (31%) lower.
