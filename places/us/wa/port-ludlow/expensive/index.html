Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Port Ludlow is $1,848 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $720 (63%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $786 (74%) higher, and it is approximately $234 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
