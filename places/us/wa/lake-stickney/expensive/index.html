Home prices

The median home value in Lake Stickney is $432,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $29,280 (6%) lower. It is about $215,200 (98%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $230,400 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Lake Stickney's median monthly rent is $1,492. Compared to nearby areas this is about $217 (12%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $430 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $122 (7%) lower.
