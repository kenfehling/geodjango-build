Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Stickney is $432,700. It is approximately $29,280 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $215,200 (98%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $230,400 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Stickney is $1,492 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $217 (12%) lower.It is approximately $430 (40%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $122 (7%) lower.
