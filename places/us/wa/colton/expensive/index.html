Home prices

The median home in Colton is valued at $202,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $2,760 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $14,900 (6%) lower, and is roughly $15,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Spokane.

Rents

Colton's median monthly rent is $725. This is about $44 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Spokane it is roughly $141 (16%) lower.
