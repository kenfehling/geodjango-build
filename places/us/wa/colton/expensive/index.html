Home prices

The median home value in Colton is $202,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $2,760 (1%) lower. It is approximately $14,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Spokane it is approximately $15,000 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Colton the median rent is $725 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $44 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and is approximately $141 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Spokane.
