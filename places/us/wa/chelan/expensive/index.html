Compared to other places in this area, Chelan is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Yakima, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chelan costs $334,300. This is about $141,300 (73%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $116,800 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $161,300 (93%) higher.

Rents

Chelan's median rent is $867 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $71 (8%) higher. It is about $195 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $47 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
