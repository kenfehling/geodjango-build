Home prices

The median home in Chelan costs $334,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $141,300 (73%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $116,800 (53%) higher, and is approximately $161,300 (93%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Chelan the median rent is $867 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $71 (8%) higher. It is about $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $47 (5%) higher.
