Home prices

Rents

Chelan's median monthly rent is $867. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $71 (8%) higher.It is about $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is about $47 (5%) higher.
