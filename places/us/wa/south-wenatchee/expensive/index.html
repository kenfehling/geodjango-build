Compared to its nearby areas, South Wenatchee is a lot less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Yakima, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Wenatchee is valued at $160,100. This is roughly $114,600 (41%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $12,900 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Wenatchee is $1,023. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $146 (16%) higher. It is roughly $39 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $203 (24%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.
