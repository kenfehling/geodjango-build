Compared to the surrounding areas, South Wenatchee is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The median home in South Wenatchee costs $160,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $114,600 (41%) lower.It is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $12,900 (7%) lower.

Rents

In South Wenatchee the median monthly rent is $1,023. It is roughly $146 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $39 (3%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $203 (24%) higher.
