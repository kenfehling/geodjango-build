In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Wenatchee is a lot less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The median home cost in South Wenatchee is $160,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $114,600 (41%) lower.It is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $12,900 (7%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In South Wenatchee the median monthly rent is $1,023. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $146 (16%) higher.It is roughly $39 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $203 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
