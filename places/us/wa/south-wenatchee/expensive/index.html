Compared to its nearby areas, South Wenatchee is a lot less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The price of the median home in South Wenatchee is $160,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $114,600 (41%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower, and is roughly $12,900 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In South Wenatchee the median rent is $1,023 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $146 (16%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $39 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Yakima it is approximately $203 (24%) higher.
