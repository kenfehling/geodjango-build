In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Wenatchee is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Yakima, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in South Wenatchee is $160,100. This is about $114,600 (41%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $12,900 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Wenatchee is $1,023. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $146 (16%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $39 (3%) lower, and it is roughly $203 (24%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.
