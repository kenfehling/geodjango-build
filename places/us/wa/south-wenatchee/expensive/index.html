Compared to other places in this area, South Wenatchee is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

South Wenatchee's median home price is $160,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $114,600 (41%) lower.It is about $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $12,900 (7%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In South Wenatchee the median monthly rent is $1,023. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $146 (16%) higher.It is approximately $39 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Yakima is about $203 (24%) higher.
