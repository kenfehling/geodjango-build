In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Wenatchee is a lot less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Yakima, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Wenatchee costs $160,100. This is about $114,600 (41%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $12,900 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In South Wenatchee the median monthly rent is $1,023. Compared to nearby areas this is about $146 (16%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $39 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $203 (24%) higher.
