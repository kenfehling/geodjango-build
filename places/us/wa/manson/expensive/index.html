Compared to its surrounding areas, Manson is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Yakima, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Manson is $265,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $61,260 (29%) higher. It is approximately $48,400 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $92,900 (53%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Manson the median monthly rent is $673. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $181 (21%) lower. It is approximately $389 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $147 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.
