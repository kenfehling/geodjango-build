Home prices

The median home price in Manson is $265,900. It is roughly $61,260 (29%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $48,400 (22%) higher, and it is about $92,900 (53%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Manson the median monthly rent is $673. This is about $181 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $389 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $147 (17%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
