Home prices

The median home in Manson is valued at $265,900. It is roughly $61,260 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $48,400 (22%) higher, and it is roughly $92,900 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

The median rent in Manson is $673 per month. This is approximately $181 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $389 (36%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $147 (17%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
