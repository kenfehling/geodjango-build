Compared to its surrounding areas, Woodland is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Woodland costs $252,100. It is approximately $48,500 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $34,600 (15%) higher, and it is roughly $159,900 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Woodland the median monthly rent is $862. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $386 (30%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $200 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $386 (30%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
