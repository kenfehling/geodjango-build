Compared to the surrounding areas, Parkwood is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Parkwood is valued at $266,900. It is approximately $15,500 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $49,400 (22%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $396,200 (59%) lower.

Rents

Parkwood's median rent is $1,154 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $208 (15%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $92 (8%) higher, and is approximately $460 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
