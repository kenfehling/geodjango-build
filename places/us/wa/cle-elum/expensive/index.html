Home prices

Rents

Cle Elum's median rent is $979 a month. It is approximately $76 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $83 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bellevue is approximately $968 (49%) lower.
