Home prices

The median home in Federal Way costs $328,800. This is about $53,780 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $111,300 (51%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $334,300 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Federal Way is $1,334. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $237 (15%) lower. It is approximately $272 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $280 (17%) lower.
