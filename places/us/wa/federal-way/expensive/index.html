Home prices

The cost of the median home in Federal Way is $328,800. It is roughly $53,780 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $111,300 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $334,300 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Federal Way is $1,334 a month. It is roughly $237 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $272 (25%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $280 (17%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
