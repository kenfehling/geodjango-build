Home prices

Bellevue's median home value is $809,200. Compared to nearby areas it is about $197,940 (19%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $591,700 (272%) higher, and it is approximately $146,100 (22%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Bellevue is $1,947 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $885 (83%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $333 (20%) higher.
