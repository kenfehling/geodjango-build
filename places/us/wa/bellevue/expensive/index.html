Home prices

Bellevue's median home cost is $809,200. It is approximately $691,340 (46%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $591,700 (272%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $146,100 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Bellevue the median rent is $1,947 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $237 (10%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $885 (83%) higher, and it is about $333 (20%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
