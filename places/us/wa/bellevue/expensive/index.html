Home prices

The median home in Bellevue is valued at $809,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $197,940 (19%) lower. It is about $591,700 (272%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $146,100 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Bellevue the median monthly rent is $1,947. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is is very similar. It is about $885 (83%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $333 (20%) higher.
