Home prices

Coupeville's median home price is $342,800. This is about $13,760 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $125,300 (57%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $320,300 (48%) lower.

Rents

Coupeville's median rent is $779 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $299 (27%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $283 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $835 (51%) lower.
