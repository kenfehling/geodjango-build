Home prices

The median home in Wenatchee is valued at $253,600. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $36,100 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $80,600 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wenatchee is $934. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $39 (4%) higher. It is about $128 (12%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is roughly $114 (13%) higher.
