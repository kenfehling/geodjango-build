Much like its surrounding areas, it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The median home in Wenatchee is valued at $253,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $36,100 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Yakima it is about $80,600 (46%) higher.

Rents

Wenatchee's median rent is $934 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $39 (4%) higher. It is about $128 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $114 (13%) higher.
