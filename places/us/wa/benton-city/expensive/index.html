Compared to its surrounding areas, Benton City is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Benton City is $148,400. This is about $77,960 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $69,100 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Yakima is approximately $24,600 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Benton City the median rent is $863 per month. This is about $146 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $199 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $43 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
