Compared to its surrounding areas, East Wenatchee is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The median home in East Wenatchee costs $274,800. It is roughly $23,040 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $57,300 (26%) higher, and it is approximately $101,800 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

The median rent in East Wenatchee is $938 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $44 (4%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is about $118 (14%) higher.
