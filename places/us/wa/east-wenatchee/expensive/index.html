In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Wenatchee is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Yakima however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Wenatchee costs $274,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $23,040 (9%) higher.It is about $57,300 (26%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $101,800 (58%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

East Wenatchee's median rent is $938 per month. This is roughly $44 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $118 (14%) higher.
