Compared to its nearby areas, East Wenatchee is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Yakima, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in East Wenatchee is $274,800. This is roughly $23,040 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $57,300 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is about $101,800 (58%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Wenatchee is $938. It is about $44 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $124 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $118 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
