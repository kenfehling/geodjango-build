Home prices

The median home price in East Wenatchee is $274,800. This is approximately $23,040 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $57,300 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is roughly $101,800 (58%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Wenatchee is $938. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $44 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $124 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $118 (14%) higher.
