Home prices

The median home in Rockford is valued at $153,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $21,760 (16%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $64,400 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Spokane it is approximately $34,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rockford is $783. It is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $279 (26%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Spokane is about $83 (9%) lower.
