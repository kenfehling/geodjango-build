In comparison to the surrounding areas, Tukwila is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tukwila is $315,000. This is roughly $49,520 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $97,500 (44%) higher, and it is approximately $348,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tukwila is $1,282. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $96 (6%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $220 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $332 (20%) lower.
