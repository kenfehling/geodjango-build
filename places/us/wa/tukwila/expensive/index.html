In comparison to its nearby areas, Tukwila is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tukwila is valued at $315,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $49,520 (13%) lower. It is roughly $97,500 (44%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $348,100 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tukwila is $1,282 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $96 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $220 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $332 (20%) lower.
