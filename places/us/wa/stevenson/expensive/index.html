Home prices

The median home in Stevenson costs $284,600. This is approximately $23,340 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $67,100 (30%) higher, and is roughly $127,400 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Stevenson's median rent is $793 per month. It is approximately $123 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $269 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $455 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
