In comparison to other places in this area, Kelso is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Kelso's median home value is $148,100. It is approximately $46,660 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $69,400 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $263,900 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Kelso's median rent is $833 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $40 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $415 (33%) lower.
