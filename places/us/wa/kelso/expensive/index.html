Compared to its surrounding areas, Kelso is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Kelso is valued at $148,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $46,660 (23%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $69,400 (31%) lower, and it is about $263,900 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kelso is $833. It is roughly $40 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $415 (33%) lower.
