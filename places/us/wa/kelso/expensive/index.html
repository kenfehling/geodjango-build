In comparison to other places in this area, Kelso is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Kelso is valued at $148,100. It is about $46,660 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $69,400 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $263,900 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Kelso the median rent is $833 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $40 (4%) lower.It is about $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $415 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
