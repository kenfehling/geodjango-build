Compared to its surrounding areas, Kelso is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kelso is $148,100. This is roughly $46,660 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $69,400 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $263,900 (64%) lower.

Rents

In Kelso the median monthly rent is $833. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $40 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $229 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $415 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
