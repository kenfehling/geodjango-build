In comparison to other places in this area, Kelso is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Kelso is $148,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $46,660 (23%) lower. It is roughly $69,400 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $263,900 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Kelso the median monthly rent is $833. It is about $40 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $415 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
