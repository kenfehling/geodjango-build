Home prices

The median home in University Place costs $367,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $11,600 (3%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $149,700 (68%) higher, and it is approximately $295,900 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in University Place is $1,198. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $43 (3%) lower. It is roughly $136 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $416 (25%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
