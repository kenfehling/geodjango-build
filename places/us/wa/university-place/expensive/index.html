Home prices

University Place's median home price is $367,200. It is roughly $11,600 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $149,700 (68%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $295,900 (44%) lower.

Rents

In University Place the median rent is $1,198 a month. This is about $43 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $136 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $416 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
