Home prices

The median home in University Place is valued at $367,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $11,600 (3%) lower. It is about $149,700 (68%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $295,900 (44%) lower.

Rents

In University Place the median monthly rent is $1,198. Compared to nearby areas it is about $43 (3%) lower. It is about $136 (12%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $416 (25%) lower.
