Home prices

Rents

Mattawa's median rent is $847 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $133 (13%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $215 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $27 (3%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.
