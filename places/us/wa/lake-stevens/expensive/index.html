Compared to nearby areas, Lake Stevens is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Lake Stevens's median home cost is $356,900. This is roughly $87,120 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $139,400 (64%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $306,200 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Stevens is $1,671. It is approximately $131 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $609 (57%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $57 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
