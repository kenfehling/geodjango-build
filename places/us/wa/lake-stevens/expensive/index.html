Home prices

The median home in Lake Stevens costs $356,900. It is roughly $87,120 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $139,400 (64%) higher, and it is about $306,200 (46%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Lake Stevens the median rent is $1,671 per month. This is roughly $131 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $609 (57%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $57 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
