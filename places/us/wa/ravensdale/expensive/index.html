Home prices

Rents

Ravensdale's median monthly rent is $1,380. It is about $147 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $318 (29%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $234 (14%) lower.
