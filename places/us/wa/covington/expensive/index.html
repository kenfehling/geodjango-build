Home prices

The median home in Covington is valued at $357,300. This is about $69,660 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $139,800 (64%) higher, and is roughly $305,800 (46%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Covington is $1,764 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $305 (20%) higher.It is roughly $702 (66%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $150 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
