In comparison to other places in this area, Covington is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Covington costs $357,300. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $69,660 (16%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $139,800 (64%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $305,800 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Covington the median monthly rent is $1,764. It is roughly $305 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $702 (66%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $150 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
