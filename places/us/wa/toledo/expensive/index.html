Compared to its nearby areas, Toledo is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home cost in Toledo is $157,700. This is roughly $9,440 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $59,800 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $505,400 (76%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Toledo is $838 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $92 (9%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and is roughly $776 (48%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
