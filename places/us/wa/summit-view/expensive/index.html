Home prices

The median home in Summit View costs $281,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $10,400 (3%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $64,400 (29%) higher, and is approximately $381,200 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Summit View is $1,418 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $101 (7%) higher. It is approximately $356 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $196 (12%) lower.
