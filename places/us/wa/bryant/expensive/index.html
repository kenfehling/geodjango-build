Home prices

The median home in Bryant is valued at $409,300. This is about $32,440 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $191,800 (88%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $253,800 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Bryant the median rent is $998 per month. This is approximately $290 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $64 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $616 (38%) lower.
