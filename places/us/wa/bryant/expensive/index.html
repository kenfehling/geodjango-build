Home prices

The price of the median home in Bryant is $409,300. This is approximately $32,440 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $191,800 (88%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $253,800 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Bryant the median rent is $998 per month. This is about $290 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $64 (6%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $616 (38%) lower.
