Home prices

The median home in Bryant costs $409,300. This is approximately $32,440 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $191,800 (88%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $253,800 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bryant is $998. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $290 (22%) lower. It is approximately $64 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $616 (38%) lower.
