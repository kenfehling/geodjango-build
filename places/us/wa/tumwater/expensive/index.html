Compared to the surrounding areas, Tumwater is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tumwater costs $286,700. This is roughly $51,740 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $69,200 (31%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $376,400 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tumwater is $1,196 per month. This is approximately $20 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $134 (12%) higher, and is about $418 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
