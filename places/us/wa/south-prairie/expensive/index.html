Rents

The median monthly rent in South Prairie is $1,250. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $78 (5%) lower. It is approximately $188 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $364 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
