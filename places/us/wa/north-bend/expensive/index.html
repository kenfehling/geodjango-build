In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Bend is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattlehowever, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Bend is valued at $548,900. It is roughly $33,720 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $331,400 (152%) higher, and it is roughly $114,200 (17%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In North Bend the median rent is $1,286 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $283 (18%) lower. It is roughly $224 (21%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $328 (20%) lower.
