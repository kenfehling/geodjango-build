Compared to its nearby areas, North Bend is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Bend is $548,900. It is roughly $33,720 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $331,400 (152%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $114,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Bend is $1,286. It is roughly $283 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $224 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $328 (20%) lower.
