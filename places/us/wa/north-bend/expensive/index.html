Compared to its surrounding areas, North Bend is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in North Bend is $548,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $33,720 (6%) higher. It is roughly $331,400 (152%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $114,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

North Bend's median monthly rent is $1,286. This is roughly $283 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $224 (21%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $328 (20%) lower.
