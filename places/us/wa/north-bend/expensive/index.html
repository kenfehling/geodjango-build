In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Bend is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in North Bend is $548,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $33,720 (6%) higher.It is roughly $331,400 (152%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $114,200 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

North Bend's median rent is $1,286 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $283 (18%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $224 (21%) higher, and it is approximately $328 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
