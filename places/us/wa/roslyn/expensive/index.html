The median home in Roslyn is valued at $302,900. This is roughly $58,740 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $85,400 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $360,200 (54%) lower.

Rents

Roslyn's median rent is $930 per month. This is approximately $17 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $132 (12%) lower, and it is about $684 (42%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
