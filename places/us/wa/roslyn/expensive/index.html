Home prices

The value of the median home in Roslyn is $302,900. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $58,740 (24%) higher.It is roughly $85,400 (39%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $360,200 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Roslyn's median rent is $930 a month. It is about $17 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $684 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
