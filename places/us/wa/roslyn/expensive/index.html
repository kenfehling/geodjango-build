Home prices

The median home cost in Roslyn is $302,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $58,740 (24%) higher. It is approximately $85,400 (39%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $360,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Roslyn the median monthly rent is $930. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $17 (1%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $132 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $684 (42%) lower.
