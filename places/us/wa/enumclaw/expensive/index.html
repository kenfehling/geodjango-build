Home prices

The price of the median home in Enumclaw is $299,000. This is roughly $5,400 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $81,500 (37%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $364,100 (54%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Enumclaw the median monthly rent is $1,150. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $130 (10%) lower.It is approximately $88 (8%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $464 (28%) lower.
