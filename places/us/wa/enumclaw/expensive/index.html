Home prices

The median home in Enumclaw costs $299,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $5,400 (1%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $81,500 (37%) higher, and is roughly $364,100 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Enumclaw's median rent is $1,150 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $130 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $88 (8%) higher, and is about $464 (28%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
