Compared to other places in this area, Eatonville is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Eatonville's median home cost is $235,800. Compared to nearby areas it is about $14,320 (5%) lower. It is about $18,300 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $427,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Eatonville the median monthly rent is $1,152. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $304 (20%) lower. It is approximately $90 (8%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $462 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
