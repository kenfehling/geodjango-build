Compared to other places in this area, Graham is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Graham costs $288,000. This is about $27,120 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $70,500 (32%) higher, and is about $375,100 (56%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Graham is $1,641 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $77 (4%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $579 (54%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $27 (1%) higher.
