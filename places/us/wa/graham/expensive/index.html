Compared to the surrounding areas, Graham is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Graham is $288,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $27,120 (10%) higher. It is roughly $70,500 (32%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $375,100 (56%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Graham's median rent is $1,641 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $77 (4%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $579 (54%) higher, and it is about $27 (1%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
