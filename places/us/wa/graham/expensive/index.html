Home prices

The median home in Graham costs $288,000. It is about $27,120 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $70,500 (32%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $375,100 (56%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Graham is $1,641 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $77 (4%) higher.It is about $579 (54%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $27 (1%) higher.
