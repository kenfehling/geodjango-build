Much like its nearby areas, it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Shoreline is valued at $519,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $15,720 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $302,400 (139%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $143,200 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Shoreline is $1,574. It is roughly $81 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $512 (48%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $40 (2%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
