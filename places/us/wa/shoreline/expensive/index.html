Home prices

Rents

In Shoreline the median monthly rent is $1,574. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $81 (4%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $512 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $40 (2%) lower.
