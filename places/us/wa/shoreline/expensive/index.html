Home prices

The median home in Shoreline costs $519,900. This is about $15,720 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $302,400 (139%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $143,200 (21%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Shoreline the median monthly rent is $1,574. This is roughly $81 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $512 (48%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $40 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
