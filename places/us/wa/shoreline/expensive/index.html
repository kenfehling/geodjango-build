Home prices

The value of the median home in Shoreline is $519,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,720 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $302,400 (139%) higher, and is approximately $143,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Shoreline's median monthly rent is $1,574. This is roughly $81 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $512 (48%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $40 (2%) lower.
