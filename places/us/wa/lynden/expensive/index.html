Home prices

The median home in Lynden is valued at $348,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $66,300 (23%) higher.It is about $130,600 (60%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $315,000 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Lynden's median rent is $1,209 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $183 (17%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $147 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $405 (25%) lower.
