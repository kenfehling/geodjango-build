Home prices

The median home in Lynden costs $348,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $66,300 (23%) higher.It is approximately $130,600 (60%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $315,000 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Lynden the median rent is $1,209 per month. This is about $183 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $147 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $405 (25%) lower.
