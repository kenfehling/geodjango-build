In comparison to other places in this area, Burley is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home price in Burley is $316,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $34,280 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $98,600 (45%) higher, and is about $347,000 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Burley the median rent is $1,324 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $255 (16%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $262 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $290 (17%) lower.
