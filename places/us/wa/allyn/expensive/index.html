Home prices

The median home in Allyn is valued at $337,200. It is approximately $49,420 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $119,700 (55%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $325,900 (49%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Allyn the median rent is $622 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $517 (45%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $440 (41%) lower, and is roughly $992 (61%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
