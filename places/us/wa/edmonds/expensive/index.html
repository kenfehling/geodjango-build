In comparison to its surrounding areas, Edmonds is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Edmonds costs $536,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $95,280 (21%) higher.It is about $319,400 (146%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $126,200 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edmonds is $1,466. It is approximately $65 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $404 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $148 (9%) lower.
