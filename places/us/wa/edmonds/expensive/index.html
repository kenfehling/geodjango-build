Compared to other places in this area, Edmonds is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Edmonds is $536,900. It is about $95,280 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $319,400 (146%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $126,200 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Edmonds is $1,466 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $65 (4%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $404 (38%) higher, and it is roughly $148 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
