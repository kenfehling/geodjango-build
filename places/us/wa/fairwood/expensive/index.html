Home prices

The median home in Fairwood is valued at $424,800. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $207,300 (95%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $238,300 (35%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Fairwood the median rent is $1,719 a month. It is approximately $200 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $657 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $105 (6%) higher.
