Home prices

Rents

Sedro-Woolley's median monthly rent is $1,253. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $246 (24%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $191 (17%) higher, and it is about $361 (22%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
