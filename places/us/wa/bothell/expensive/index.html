In comparison to nearby areas, Bothell is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bothell is $530,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $35,260 (6%) lower. It is approximately $312,800 (143%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $132,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bothell is $1,719. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $68 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $657 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $105 (6%) higher.
