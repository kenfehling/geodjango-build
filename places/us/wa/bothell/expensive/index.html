Compared to its surrounding areas, Bothell is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bothell is $530,300. This is roughly $35,260 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $312,800 (143%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $132,800 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bothell is $1,719. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $68 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $657 (61%) higher, and is about $105 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
