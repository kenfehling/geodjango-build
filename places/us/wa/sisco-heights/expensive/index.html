Compared to its surrounding areas, Sisco Heights is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Sisco Heights is $418,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $91,840 (28%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $201,000 (92%) higher, and is roughly $244,600 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Sisco Heights's median rent is $1,330 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $45 (3%) lower.It is approximately $268 (25%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $284 (17%) lower.
