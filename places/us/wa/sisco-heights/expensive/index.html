Compared to its surrounding areas, Sisco Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Sisco Heights's median home cost is $418,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $91,840 (28%) higher. It is about $201,000 (92%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $244,600 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Sisco Heights the median monthly rent is $1,330. This is roughly $45 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $268 (25%) higher, and it is approximately $284 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
