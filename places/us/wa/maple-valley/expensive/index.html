In comparison to nearby areas, Maple Valley is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Valley costs $414,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $45,780 (9%) lower. It is roughly $196,900 (90%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $248,700 (37%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Maple Valley's median rent is $2,003 a month. This is roughly $601 (42%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $941 (88%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $389 (24%) higher.
