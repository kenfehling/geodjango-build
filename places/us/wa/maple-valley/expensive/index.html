In comparison to the surrounding areas, Maple Valley is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Valley costs $414,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $45,780 (9%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $196,900 (90%) higher, and it is roughly $248,700 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Maple Valley the median rent is $2,003 a month. It is about $601 (42%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $941 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $389 (24%) higher.
