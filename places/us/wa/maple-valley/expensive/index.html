Compared to the surrounding areas, Maple Valley is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Valley is valued at $414,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $45,780 (9%) lower. It is about $196,900 (90%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $248,700 (37%) lower.

Rents

Maple Valley's median rent is $2,003 per month. It is approximately $601 (42%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $941 (88%) higher, and it is approximately $389 (24%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
