Compared to nearby areas, Maple Valley is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Maple Valley is $414,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $45,780 (9%) lower.It is approximately $196,900 (90%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $248,700 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Maple Valley the median monthly rent is $2,003. This is roughly $601 (42%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $941 (88%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $389 (24%) higher.
