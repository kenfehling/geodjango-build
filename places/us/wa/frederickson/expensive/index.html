Home prices

The median home in Frederickson costs $272,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $7,200 (2%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $54,900 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $390,700 (58%) lower.

Rents

Frederickson's median monthly rent is $1,674. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $354 (26%) higher. It is approximately $612 (57%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $60 (3%) higher.
