Home prices

The median home value in Goldendale is $156,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $6,660 (4%) higher. It is approximately $60,600 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is approximately $16,100 (9%) lower.

Rents

In Goldendale the median rent is $747 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $98 (11%) lower. It is about $315 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $73 (8%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
