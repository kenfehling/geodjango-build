In comparison to its nearby areas, Redmond is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Seattle, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Redmond is $703,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $37,460 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $485,500 (223%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $39,900 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Redmond is $1,929 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar. Compared to the entire country it is about $867 (81%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $315 (19%) higher.
