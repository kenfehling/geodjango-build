In comparison to its surrounding areas, Redmond is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home cost in Redmond is $703,000. This is about $37,460 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $485,500 (223%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $39,900 (6%) higher.

Rents

In Redmond the median rent is $1,929 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $867 (81%) higher, and it is about $315 (19%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
