Home prices

Redmond's median home value is $703,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $550,900 (43%) lower.It is about $485,500 (223%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $39,900 (6%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Redmond is $1,929 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $440 (18%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $867 (81%) higher, and it is approximately $315 (19%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
