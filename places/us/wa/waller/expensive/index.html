Home prices

The cost of the median home in Waller is $314,000. This is roughly $6,780 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $96,500 (44%) higher, and it is approximately $349,100 (52%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Waller's median monthly rent is $1,144. This is approximately $171 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $82 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $470 (29%) lower.
