Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ellensburg is $234,000. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $50,960 (27%) higher.It is approximately $16,500 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $61,000 (35%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Ellensburg the median rent is $1,004 a month. This is approximately $55 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $58 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Yakima is about $184 (22%) higher.
