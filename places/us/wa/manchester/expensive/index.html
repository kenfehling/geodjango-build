Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Manchester is $1,387 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $137 (10%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $325 (30%) higher, and it is approximately $227 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
