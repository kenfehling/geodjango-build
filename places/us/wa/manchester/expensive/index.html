In comparison to its surrounding areas, Manchester is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home price in Manchester is $290,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $49,720 (20%) higher. It is approximately $73,000 (33%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $372,600 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Manchester's median rent is $1,387 per month. This is approximately $137 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $325 (30%) higher, and it is approximately $227 (14%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
