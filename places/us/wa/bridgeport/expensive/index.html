Home prices

Rents

In Bridgeport the median rent is $692 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $39 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee is approximately $242 (25%) lower.
