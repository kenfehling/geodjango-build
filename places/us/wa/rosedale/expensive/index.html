Compared to its nearby areas, Rosedale is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Rosedale is valued at $482,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $72,880 (17%) higher. It is roughly $265,400 (122%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $180,200 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Rosedale is $865 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $680 (44%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $197 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $749 (46%) lower.
