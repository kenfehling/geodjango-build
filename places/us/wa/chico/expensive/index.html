In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chico is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chico is $427,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $172,100 (67%) higher. It is approximately $209,500 (96%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $236,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Chico the median monthly rent is $1,833. It is roughly $657 (55%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $771 (72%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $219 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
