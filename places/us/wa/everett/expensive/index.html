Home prices

Everett's median home price is $322,700. This is about $102,460 (24%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $105,200 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $340,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

Everett's median rent is $1,241 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $185 (12%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $179 (16%) higher, and it is approximately $373 (23%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
