In comparison to other places in this area, Everett is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Everett's median home cost is $322,700. It is about $102,460 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $105,200 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $340,400 (51%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Everett's median monthly rent is $1,241. It is approximately $185 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $179 (16%) higher, and is roughly $373 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
