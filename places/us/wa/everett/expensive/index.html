Compared to other places in this area, Everett is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Everett's median home value is $322,700. This is approximately $102,460 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $105,200 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $340,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Everett is $1,241. This is approximately $185 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $179 (16%) higher, and it is roughly $373 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
