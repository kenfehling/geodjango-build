Home prices

The median home in Ferndale costs $300,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $36,120 (10%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $83,100 (38%) higher, and is approximately $362,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ferndale is $996. This is roughly $110 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $66 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $618 (38%) lower.
