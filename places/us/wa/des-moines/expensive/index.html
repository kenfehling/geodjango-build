In comparison to nearby areas, Des Moines is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Des Moines is $359,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $31,280 (8%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $141,600 (65%) higher, and it is roughly $304,000 (45%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Des Moines the median rent is $1,337 per month. This is approximately $92 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $275 (25%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $277 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
