In comparison to other places in this area, Des Moines is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Des Moines is $359,100. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $31,280 (8%) lower.It is about $141,600 (65%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $304,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Des Moines is $1,337 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $92 (6%) lower.It is about $275 (25%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $277 (17%) lower.
