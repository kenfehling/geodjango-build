Compared to its nearby areas, Des Moines is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Des Moines is $359,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $31,280 (8%) lower. It is about $141,600 (65%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $304,000 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Des Moines is $1,337. Compared to nearby areas it is about $92 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $275 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $277 (17%) lower.
