Compared to other places in this area, Des Moines is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Des Moines's median home price is $359,100. It is approximately $31,280 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $141,600 (65%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $304,000 (45%) lower.

Rents

Des Moines's median monthly rent is $1,337. This is about $92 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $275 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $277 (17%) lower.
