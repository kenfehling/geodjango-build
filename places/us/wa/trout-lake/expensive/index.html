Home prices

Rents

Trout Lake's median monthly rent is $1,080. This is roughly $124 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $18 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $168 (13%) lower.
