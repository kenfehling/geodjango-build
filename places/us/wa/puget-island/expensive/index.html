In comparison to other places in this area, Puget Island is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Puget Island is valued at $223,300. It is approximately $20,120 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $5,800 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $188,700 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Puget Island the median monthly rent is $1,125. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $325 (40%) higher. It is roughly $63 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $123 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
