Compared to the surrounding areas, Puget Island is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Puget Island is $223,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $20,120 (9%) higher.It is roughly $5,800 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $188,700 (45%) lower.

Rents

Puget Island's median rent is $1,125 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $325 (40%) higher.It is approximately $63 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $123 (9%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
