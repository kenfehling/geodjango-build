Compared to its surrounding areas, Puget Island is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Puget Island is valued at $223,300. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $20,120 (9%) higher.It is about $5,800 (2%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $188,700 (45%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Puget Island is $1,125 per month. This is roughly $325 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $63 (5%) higher, and it is about $123 (9%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
