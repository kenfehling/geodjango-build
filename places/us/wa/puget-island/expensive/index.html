In comparison to its nearby areas, Puget Island is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Puget Island costs $223,300. It is about $20,120 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $5,800 (2%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $188,700 (45%) lower.

Rents

Puget Island's median rent is $1,125 per month. It is about $325 (40%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and it is roughly $123 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
