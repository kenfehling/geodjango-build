In comparison to the surrounding areas, Puget Island is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Puget Island costs $223,300. This is roughly $20,120 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $5,800 (2%) higher, and is roughly $188,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Puget Island is $1,125 per month. It is roughly $325 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and it is about $123 (9%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
