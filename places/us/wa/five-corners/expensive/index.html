In comparison to the surrounding areas, Five Corners is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Five Corners costs $277,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $31,780 (10%) lower. It is about $59,800 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $134,700 (32%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Five Corners's median monthly rent is $1,399. It is about $58 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $337 (31%) higher, and is approximately $151 (12%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
