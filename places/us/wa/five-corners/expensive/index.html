Compared to other places in this area, Five Corners is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Five Corners is valued at $277,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $31,780 (10%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $59,800 (27%) higher, and it is about $134,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Five Corners's median monthly rent is $1,399. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $58 (4%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $337 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $151 (12%) higher.
