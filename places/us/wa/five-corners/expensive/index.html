In comparison to the surrounding areas, Five Corners is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Five Corners's median home price is $277,300. This is roughly $31,780 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $59,800 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $134,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Five Corners the median monthly rent is $1,399. It is about $58 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $337 (31%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $151 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
