Home prices

Rents

Five Corners's median monthly rent is $1,399. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $58 (4%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $337 (31%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $151 (12%) higher.
