Compared to the surrounding areas, Five Corners is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Five Corners is $277,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $31,780 (10%) lower.It is about $59,800 (27%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $134,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Five Corners is $1,399 a month. It is roughly $58 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $337 (31%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $151 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
