Compared to the surrounding areas, Marysville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marysville is valued at $325,300. It is roughly $68,560 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $107,800 (49%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $337,800 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Marysville the median monthly rent is $1,387. It is about $126 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $325 (30%) higher, and it is about $227 (14%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
