In comparison to other places in this area, Marysville is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home value in Marysville is $325,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $68,560 (17%) lower. It is about $107,800 (49%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $337,800 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marysville is $1,387. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $126 (8%) lower. It is about $325 (30%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $227 (14%) lower.
