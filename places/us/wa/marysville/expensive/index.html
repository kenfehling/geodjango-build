Home prices

The median home in Marysville costs $325,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $68,560 (17%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $107,800 (49%) higher, and it is about $337,800 (50%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Marysville the median rent is $1,387 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $126 (8%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $325 (30%) higher, and is about $227 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
