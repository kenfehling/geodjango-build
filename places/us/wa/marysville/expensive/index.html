Home prices

The median home cost in Marysville is $325,300. This is approximately $68,560 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $107,800 (49%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $337,800 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marysville is $1,387. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $126 (8%) lower. It is about $325 (30%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $227 (14%) lower.
