Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marysville is $325,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $68,560 (17%) lower.It is approximately $107,800 (49%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $337,800 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Marysville the median monthly rent is $1,387. This is roughly $126 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $325 (30%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $227 (14%) lower.
