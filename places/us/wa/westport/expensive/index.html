Home prices

The median home value in Westport is $188,900. This is approximately $29,080 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $28,600 (13%) lower, and is roughly $108,300 (36%) lower compared to the city of Olympia.

Rents

Westport's median rent is $772 per month. This is about $116 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $290 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Olympia is approximately $373 (32%) lower.
