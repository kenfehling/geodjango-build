Home prices

The median home in Grandview costs $145,400. This is approximately $10,220 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $72,100 (33%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is about $27,600 (15%) lower.

Rents

Grandview's median rent is $782 per month. This is about $10 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $38 (4%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
