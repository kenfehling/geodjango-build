Home prices

The median home in Grandview costs $145,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $10,220 (7%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $72,100 (33%) lower, and is roughly $27,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

Grandview's median monthly rent is $782. It is roughly $10 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and is roughly $38 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.
