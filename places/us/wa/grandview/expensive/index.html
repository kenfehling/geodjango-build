Home prices

The price of the median home in Grandview is $145,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $10,220 (7%) higher.It is approximately $72,100 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Yakima is about $27,600 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grandview is $782. This is about $10 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $280 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $38 (4%) lower.
