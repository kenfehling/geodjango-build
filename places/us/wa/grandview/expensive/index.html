Home prices

Grandview's median home cost is $145,400. It is about $10,220 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $72,100 (33%) lower, and is approximately $27,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

The median rent in Grandview is $782 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $10 (1%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and is approximately $38 (4%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
