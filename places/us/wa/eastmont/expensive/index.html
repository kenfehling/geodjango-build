Home prices

The value of the median home in Eastmont is $433,800. This is approximately $20,700 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $216,300 (99%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $229,300 (34%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Eastmont the median rent is $1,702 a month. This is approximately $167 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $640 (60%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $88 (5%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
