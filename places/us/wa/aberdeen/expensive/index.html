Home prices

The price of the median home in Aberdeen is $124,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $62,280 (33%) lower. It is roughly $93,200 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tacoma it is approximately $153,600 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Aberdeen is $754. This is roughly $119 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tacoma is about $426 (36%) lower.
