In comparison to its surrounding areas, Aberdeen is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Tacoma.

Home prices

Aberdeen's median home value is $124,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $62,280 (33%) lower. It is roughly $93,200 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Tacoma it is about $153,600 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Aberdeen the median rent is $754 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $119 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tacoma is about $426 (36%) lower.
