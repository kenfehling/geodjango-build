Compared to its nearby areas, Pacific is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Pacific costs $281,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $42,880 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $64,300 (29%) higher, and is roughly $381,300 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Pacific is $1,231 a month. This is roughly $105 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $169 (15%) higher, and is approximately $383 (23%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
