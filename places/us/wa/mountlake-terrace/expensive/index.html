Home prices

The median home value in Mountlake Terrace is $392,600. This is approximately $71,500 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $175,100 (80%) higher, and it is about $270,500 (40%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Mountlake Terrace's median rent is $1,464 per month. This is about $171 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $402 (37%) higher, and it is about $150 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
