Home prices

The median home in Mountlake Terrace costs $392,600. It is about $71,500 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $175,100 (80%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $270,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Mountlake Terrace the median rent is $1,464 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $171 (10%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $402 (37%) higher, and it is about $150 (9%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
