Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Kingston is $1,277 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $143 (10%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $215 (20%) higher, and it is about $337 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
