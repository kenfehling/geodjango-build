In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kingston is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kingston is valued at $351,900. This is roughly $29,900 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $134,400 (61%) higher, and is roughly $311,200 (46%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Kingston's median rent is $1,277 a month. This is approximately $143 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $215 (20%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $337 (20%) lower.
