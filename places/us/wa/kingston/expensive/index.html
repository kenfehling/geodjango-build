Compared to the surrounding areas, Kingston is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Kingston's median home cost is $351,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $29,900 (7%) lower.It is roughly $134,400 (61%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $311,200 (46%) lower.

Rents

Kingston's median rent is $1,277 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $143 (10%) lower.It is approximately $215 (20%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $337 (20%) lower.
