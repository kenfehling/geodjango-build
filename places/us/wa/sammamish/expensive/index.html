Home prices

The median home value in Sammamish is $830,000. This is approximately $141,820 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $612,500 (281%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $166,900 (25%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sammamish is $2,395. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $580 (31%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $1,333 (125%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $781 (48%) higher.
