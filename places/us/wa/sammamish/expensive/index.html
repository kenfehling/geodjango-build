Compared to other places in this area, Sammamish is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a little more expensive even compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Sammamish is valued at $830,000. This is about $141,820 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $612,500 (281%) higher, and it is about $166,900 (25%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Sammamish the median rent is $2,395 per month. This is approximately $580 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $1,333 (125%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $781 (48%) higher.
