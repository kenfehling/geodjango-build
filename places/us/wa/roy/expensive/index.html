Home prices

The cost of the median home in Roy is $233,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $14,620 (6%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $15,600 (7%) higher, and is about $430,000 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roy is $998. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $380 (27%) lower. It is approximately $64 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $616 (38%) lower.
