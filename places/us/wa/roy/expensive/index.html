Rents

In Roy the median rent is $998 a month. This is approximately $380 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $64 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $616 (38%) lower.
