In comparison to the surrounding areas, Arlington is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Arlington is $323,200. It is about $81,560 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $105,700 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $339,900 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Arlington the median rent is $1,341 per month. It is roughly $46 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $279 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $273 (16%) lower.
