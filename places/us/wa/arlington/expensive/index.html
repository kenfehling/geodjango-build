Compared to other places in this area, Arlington is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Arlington's median home value is $323,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $81,560 (20%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $105,700 (48%) higher, and is roughly $339,900 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Arlington is $1,341. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $46 (3%) lower.It is about $279 (26%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $273 (16%) lower.
