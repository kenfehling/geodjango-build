Home prices

The value of the median home in Arlington is $323,200. It is about $81,560 (20%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $105,700 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $339,900 (51%) lower.

Rents

Arlington's median monthly rent is $1,341. It is about $46 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $279 (26%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $273 (16%) lower.
