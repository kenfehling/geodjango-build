In comparison to nearby areas, Arlington is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Arlington is $323,200. This is roughly $81,560 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $105,700 (48%) higher, and is about $339,900 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Arlington the median rent is $1,341 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $46 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $279 (26%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $273 (16%) lower.
