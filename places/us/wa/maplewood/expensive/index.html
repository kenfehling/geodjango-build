Compared to other places in this area, Maplewood is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Maplewood costs $433,700. It is roughly $24,740 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $216,200 (99%) higher, and is approximately $229,400 (34%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Maplewood the median rent is $1,676 per month. This is roughly $260 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $614 (57%) higher, and it is roughly $62 (3%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
