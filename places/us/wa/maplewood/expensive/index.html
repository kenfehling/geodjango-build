Compared to its surrounding areas, Maplewood is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Maplewood is $433,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24,740 (6%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $216,200 (99%) higher, and it is about $229,400 (34%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Maplewood is $1,676 per month. It is about $260 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $614 (57%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $62 (3%) higher.
