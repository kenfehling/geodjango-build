In comparison to other places in this area, Maplewood is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maplewood costs $433,700. It is roughly $24,740 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $216,200 (99%) higher, and it is roughly $229,400 (34%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Maplewood's median rent is $1,676 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $260 (18%) higher.It is about $614 (57%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $62 (3%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
