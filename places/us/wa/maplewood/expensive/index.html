Compared to nearby areas, Maplewood is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maplewood costs $433,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $24,740 (6%) higher. It is approximately $216,200 (99%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $229,400 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Maplewood the median monthly rent is $1,676. It is approximately $260 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $614 (57%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $62 (3%) higher.
