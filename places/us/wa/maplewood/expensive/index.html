Compared to its nearby areas, Maplewood is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maplewood costs $433,700. This is approximately $24,740 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $216,200 (99%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $229,400 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Maplewood is $1,676 per month. It is approximately $260 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $614 (57%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $62 (3%) higher.
