In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maplewood is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Maplewood is $433,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $24,740 (6%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $216,200 (99%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $229,400 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Maplewood is $1,676 per month. This is roughly $260 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $614 (57%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $62 (3%) higher.
