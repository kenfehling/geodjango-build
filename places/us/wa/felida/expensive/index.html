Home prices

The cost of the median home in Felida is $450,100. It is about $105,900 (30%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $232,600 (106%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $38,100 (9%) higher.

Rents

Felida's median rent is $1,941 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $531 (37%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $879 (82%) higher, and it is roughly $693 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.
