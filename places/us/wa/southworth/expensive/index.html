In comparison to the surrounding areas, Southworth is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Southworth costs $298,600. This is roughly $22,540 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $81,100 (37%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $364,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Southworth's median rent is $1,669 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $410 (32%) higher. It is approximately $607 (57%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $55 (3%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
