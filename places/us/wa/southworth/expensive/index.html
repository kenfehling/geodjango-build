Home prices

The median home in Southworth costs $298,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $22,540 (8%) higher. It is about $81,100 (37%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $364,500 (54%) lower.

Rents

Southworth's median monthly rent is $1,669. This is about $410 (32%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $607 (57%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $55 (3%) higher.
