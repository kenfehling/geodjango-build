Home prices

Cashmere's median home price is $243,400. This is approximately $54,460 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $25,900 (11%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $70,400 (40%) higher.

Rents

In Cashmere the median rent is $826 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $142 (14%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $236 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is is very similar.
