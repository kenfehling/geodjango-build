In comparison to other places in this area, Cashmere is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Yakima however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cashmere is $243,400. This is roughly $54,460 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $25,900 (11%) higher, and it is about $70,400 (40%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Cashmere the median rent is $826 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $142 (14%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $236 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is is very similar.
