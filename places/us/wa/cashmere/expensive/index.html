Home prices

The median home in Cashmere is valued at $243,400. It is approximately $54,460 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $25,900 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $70,400 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

Cashmere's median rent is $826 a month. This is about $142 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $236 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Yakima it is is very similar.
