Home prices

The price of the median home in Chain Lake is $512,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $88,500 (20%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $294,700 (135%) higher, and is roughly $150,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Chain Lake's median rent is $1,625 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $151 (10%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $563 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is is very similar.
