Home prices

Rents

In Riverside the median rent is $697 per month. This is about $66 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee it is approximately $237 (25%) lower.
