Compared to its nearby areas, Riverside is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Wenatchee.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside costs $125,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,140 (10%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower, and is about $128,600 (50%) lower compared to the city of Wenatchee.

Rents

In Riverside the median monthly rent is $697. This is approximately $66 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and is approximately $237 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Wenatchee.
