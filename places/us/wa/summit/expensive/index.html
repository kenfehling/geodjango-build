Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Summit is $1,243. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $47 (3%) higher.It is roughly $181 (17%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $371 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
