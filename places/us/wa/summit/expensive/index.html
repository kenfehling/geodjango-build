In comparison to its surrounding areas, Summit is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Summit costs $299,300. It is about $17,780 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $81,800 (37%) higher, and it is about $363,800 (54%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Summit is $1,243 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $47 (3%) higher. It is roughly $181 (17%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $371 (22%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
