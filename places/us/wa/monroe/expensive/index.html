In comparison to other places in this area, Monroe is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monroe costs $357,000. It is approximately $149,980 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $139,500 (64%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $306,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Monroe the median rent is $1,391 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $297 (17%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $329 (30%) higher, and it is approximately $223 (13%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
