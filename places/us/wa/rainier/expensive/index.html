Home prices

The median home in Rainier costs $206,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $17,260 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $11,500 (5%) lower, and is roughly $457,100 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Rainier's median monthly rent is $1,338. It is about $214 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $276 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $276 (17%) lower.
