Home prices

The median home in Snohomish is valued at $366,500. This is about $53,920 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $149,000 (68%) higher, and it is about $296,600 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Snohomish the median monthly rent is $1,067. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $440 (29%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and it is about $547 (33%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
