Home prices

The median home cost in Snohomish is $366,500. It is about $53,920 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $149,000 (68%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $296,600 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Snohomish is $1,067. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $440 (29%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $547 (33%) lower.
