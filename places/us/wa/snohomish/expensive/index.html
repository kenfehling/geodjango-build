Home prices

The value of the median home in Snohomish is $366,500. It is roughly $53,920 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $149,000 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $296,600 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Snohomish the median rent is $1,067 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $440 (29%) lower.It is is very similar compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $547 (33%) lower.
