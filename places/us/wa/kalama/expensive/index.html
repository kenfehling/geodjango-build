The median home in Kalama is valued at $248,200. This is roughly $40,080 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $30,700 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $163,800 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kalama is $775. This is roughly $149 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $473 (37%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
