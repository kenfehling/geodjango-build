Compared to its nearby areas, Renton is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Renton is $400,500. It is approximately $79,440 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $183,000 (84%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $262,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Renton is $1,543 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $114 (6%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $481 (45%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $71 (4%) lower.
