In comparison to the surrounding areas, Renton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Renton is $400,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $79,440 (16%) lower.It is roughly $183,000 (84%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $262,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

Renton's median monthly rent is $1,543. It is approximately $114 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $481 (45%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $71 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
