Compared to other places in this area, Renton is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Renton is $400,500. This is roughly $79,440 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $183,000 (84%) higher, and is about $262,600 (39%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Renton is $1,543. This is approximately $114 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $481 (45%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $71 (4%) lower.
