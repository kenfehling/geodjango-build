Compared to the surrounding areas, Renton is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Renton costs $400,500. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $79,440 (16%) lower.It is approximately $183,000 (84%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $262,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Renton is $1,543. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $114 (6%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $481 (45%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $71 (4%) lower.
