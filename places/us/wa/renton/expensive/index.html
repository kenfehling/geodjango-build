Compared to its nearby areas, Renton is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Renton is $400,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $79,440 (16%) lower. It is approximately $183,000 (84%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $262,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Renton the median rent is $1,543 a month. It is approximately $114 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $481 (45%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $71 (4%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
