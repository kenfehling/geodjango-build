Home prices

The median home in Fern Prairie is valued at $371,800. This is about $41,000 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $154,300 (70%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $40,200 (9%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Fern Prairie's median monthly rent is $1,317. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $75 (6%) higher. It is about $255 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $69 (5%) higher.
