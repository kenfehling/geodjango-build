Home prices

Rents

In Fern Prairie the median rent is $1,317 per month. This is approximately $75 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $255 (24%) higher, and is roughly $69 (5%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
