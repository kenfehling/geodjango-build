Compared to its surrounding areas, Omak is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Wenatchee, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Omak is $112,600. This is about $30,020 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $104,900 (48%) lower, and it is about $141,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Wenatchee.

Rents

Omak's median rent is $702 a month. It is roughly $72 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $360 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee is about $232 (24%) lower.
