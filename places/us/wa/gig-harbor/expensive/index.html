Home prices

Gig Harbor's median home value is $475,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $11,880 (2%) lower. It is approximately $257,900 (118%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $187,700 (28%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Gig Harbor is $1,419 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $133 (8%) lower. It is approximately $357 (33%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $195 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
