Home prices

The median home value in Gig Harbor is $475,400. It is approximately $11,880 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $257,900 (118%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $187,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

Gig Harbor's median rent is $1,419 a month. This is about $133 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $357 (33%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $195 (12%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
