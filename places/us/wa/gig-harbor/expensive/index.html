Home prices

The cost of the median home in Gig Harbor is $475,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $11,880 (2%) lower. It is about $257,900 (118%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $187,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Gig Harbor the median rent is $1,419 per month. This is roughly $133 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $357 (33%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $195 (12%) lower.
