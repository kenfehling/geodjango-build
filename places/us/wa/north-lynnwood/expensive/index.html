Home prices

North Lynnwood's median home cost is $363,700. This is about $96,380 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $146,200 (67%) higher, and is roughly $299,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In North Lynnwood the median rent is $1,683 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $58 (3%) higher.It is roughly $621 (58%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $69 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
