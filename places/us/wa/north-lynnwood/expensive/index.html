In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Lynnwood is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Lynnwood costs $363,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $96,380 (20%) lower. It is roughly $146,200 (67%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $299,400 (45%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

North Lynnwood's median rent is $1,683 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $58 (3%) higher. It is about $621 (58%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $69 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
