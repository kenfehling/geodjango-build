In comparison to the surrounding areas, Alderton is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Alderton costs $325,700. This is approximately $25,260 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $108,200 (49%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $337,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Alderton the median monthly rent is $1,477. This is approximately $170 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $415 (39%) higher, and is approximately $137 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
