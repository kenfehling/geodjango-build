In comparison to nearby areas, Alderton is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Alderton is $325,700. This is approximately $25,260 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $108,200 (49%) higher, and is approximately $337,400 (50%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Alderton is $1,477 a month. It is about $170 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $415 (39%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $137 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
