Home prices

The median home in Alderton is valued at $325,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $25,260 (8%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $108,200 (49%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $337,400 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Alderton is $1,477 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $170 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $415 (39%) higher, and it is about $137 (8%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
