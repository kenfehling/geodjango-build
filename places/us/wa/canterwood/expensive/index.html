Home prices

Rents

Canterwood's median monthly rent is $2,101. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $777 (58%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,039 (97%) higher, and is roughly $487 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
