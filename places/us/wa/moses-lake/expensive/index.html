Home prices

The median home in Moses Lake costs $164,700. It is roughly $3,580 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $52,800 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Yakima it is about $8,300 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Moses Lake is $898 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $196 (27%) higher. It is approximately $164 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Yakima is approximately $78 (9%) higher.
