Home prices

The median home in Moses Lake is valued at $164,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $3,580 (2%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $52,800 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $8,300 (4%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Moses Lake the median monthly rent is $898. This is roughly $196 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $164 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $78 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
