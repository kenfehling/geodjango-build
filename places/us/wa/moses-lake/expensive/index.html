Home prices

The value of the median home in Moses Lake is $164,700. This is approximately $3,580 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $52,800 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $8,300 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

Moses Lake's median rent is $898 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $196 (27%) higher. It is about $164 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $78 (9%) higher.
