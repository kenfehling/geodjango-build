Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cascade Valley is $631. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $125 (16%) lower.It is roughly $431 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $189 (23%) lower.
