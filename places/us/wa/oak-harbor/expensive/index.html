Compared to other places in this area, Oak Harbor is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home value in Oak Harbor is $277,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $82,320 (22%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $60,000 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $385,600 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Oak Harbor the median monthly rent is $1,234. This is roughly $192 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $172 (16%) higher, and is about $380 (23%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
