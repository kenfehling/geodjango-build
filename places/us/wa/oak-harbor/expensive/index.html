In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oak Harbor is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Harbor costs $277,500. It is about $82,320 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $60,000 (27%) higher, and is about $385,600 (58%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Oak Harbor is $1,234 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $192 (18%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $172 (16%) higher, and it is approximately $380 (23%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
