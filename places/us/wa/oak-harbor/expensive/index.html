Home prices

The median home in Oak Harbor is valued at $277,500. This is about $82,320 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $60,000 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $385,600 (58%) lower.

Rents

Oak Harbor's median monthly rent is $1,234. Compared to nearby areas this is about $192 (18%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $172 (16%) higher, and it is about $380 (23%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
