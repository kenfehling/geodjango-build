Home prices

The median home in Oak Harbor is valued at $277,500. It is approximately $82,320 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $60,000 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $385,600 (58%) lower.

Rents

Oak Harbor's median rent is $1,234 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $192 (18%) higher.It is approximately $172 (16%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $380 (23%) lower.
