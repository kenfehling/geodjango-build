Home prices

The median home in Almira costs $163,600. This is about $17,920 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $53,900 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $99,900 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Richland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Almira is $967. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $363 (60%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $95 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Richland is roughly $120 (11%) lower.
