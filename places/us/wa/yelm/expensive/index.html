Home prices

The median home in Yelm is valued at $255,200. This is roughly $38,280 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $37,700 (17%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $407,900 (61%) lower.

Rents

In Yelm the median rent is $1,373 per month. It is roughly $143 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $311 (29%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $241 (14%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
