In comparison to the surrounding areas, Clear Lake is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clear Lake is $325,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $51,520 (18%) higher. It is approximately $107,900 (49%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $337,700 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Clear Lake the median rent is $698 a month. It is roughly $420 (37%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $364 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $916 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
