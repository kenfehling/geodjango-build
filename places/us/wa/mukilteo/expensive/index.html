Home prices

The median home in Mukilteo is valued at $582,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $130,720 (28%) higher.It is approximately $365,200 (167%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $80,400 (12%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Mukilteo is $1,796 per month. This is approximately $320 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $734 (69%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $182 (11%) higher.
