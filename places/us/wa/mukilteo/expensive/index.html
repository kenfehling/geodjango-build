In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mukilteo is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattlehowever, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mukilteo is valued at $582,700. This is approximately $130,720 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $365,200 (167%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $80,400 (12%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Mukilteo's median monthly rent is $1,796. It is about $320 (21%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $734 (69%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $182 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
