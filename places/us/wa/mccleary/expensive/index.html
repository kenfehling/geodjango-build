Home prices

The median home cost in McCleary is $164,700. It is about $23,900 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $52,800 (24%) lower, and it is approximately $498,400 (75%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in McCleary is $955 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $120 (14%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $107 (10%) lower, and is approximately $659 (40%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
