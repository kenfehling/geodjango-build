Home prices

The median home in Selah costs $224,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $40,920 (22%) higher. It is approximately $7,300 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Yakima it is approximately $51,800 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Selah the median monthly rent is $1,027. This is roughly $78 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $35 (3%) lower, and is about $207 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
