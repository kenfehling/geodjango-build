In comparison to other places in this area, Boulevard Park is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Boulevard Park is $326,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $32,160 (8%) lower. It is about $109,200 (50%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $336,400 (50%) lower.

Rents

Boulevard Park's median rent is $1,392 a month. This is about $106 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $330 (31%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $222 (13%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
