Home prices

The median home in Boulevard Park costs $326,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $32,160 (8%) lower. It is about $109,200 (50%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $336,400 (50%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Boulevard Park is $1,392 per month. It is approximately $106 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $330 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $222 (13%) lower.
