In comparison to nearby areas, Snoqualmie is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Snoqualmie is $610,700. It is approximately $84,060 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $393,200 (180%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $52,400 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Snoqualmie the median rent is $1,960 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $459 (30%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $898 (84%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $346 (21%) higher.
