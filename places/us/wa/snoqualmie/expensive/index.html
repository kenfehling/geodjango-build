Compared to its surrounding areas, Snoqualmie is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Snoqualmie's median home value is $610,700. This is approximately $84,060 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $393,200 (180%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $52,400 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Snoqualmie is $1,960. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $459 (30%) higher. It is about $898 (84%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $346 (21%) higher.
