Compared to its nearby areas, Snoqualmie is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Snoqualmie's median home price is $610,700. This is roughly $84,060 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $393,200 (180%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $52,400 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Snoqualmie is $1,960 a month. It is approximately $459 (30%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $898 (84%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $346 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
