Compared to its nearby areas, Snoqualmie is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Snoqualmie is valued at $610,700. It is roughly $84,060 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $393,200 (180%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $52,400 (7%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Snoqualmie the median rent is $1,960 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $459 (30%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $898 (84%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $346 (21%) higher.
