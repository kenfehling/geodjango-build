In comparison to its nearby areas, Snoqualmie is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattlehowever, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Snoqualmie costs $610,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $84,060 (15%) higher. It is about $393,200 (180%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $52,400 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Snoqualmie the median rent is $1,960 a month. It is about $459 (30%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $898 (84%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $346 (21%) higher.
