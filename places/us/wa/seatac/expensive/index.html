Compared to the surrounding areas, SeaTac is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

SeaTac's median home value is $321,300. This is about $95,460 (22%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $103,800 (47%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $341,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In SeaTac the median monthly rent is $1,286. This is roughly $60 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $224 (21%) higher, and is about $328 (20%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
