Home prices

The median home value in SeaTac is $321,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $95,460 (22%) lower. It is roughly $103,800 (47%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $341,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In SeaTac the median monthly rent is $1,286. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $60 (4%) lower. It is roughly $224 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $328 (20%) lower.
