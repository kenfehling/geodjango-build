In comparison to the surrounding areas, SeaTac is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in SeaTac costs $321,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $95,460 (22%) lower.It is roughly $103,800 (47%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $341,800 (51%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

SeaTac's median rent is $1,286 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $60 (4%) lower.It is about $224 (21%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $328 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
