Compared to other places in this area, Edgewood is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Edgewood is $409,100. This is roughly $137,120 (50%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $191,600 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $254,000 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Edgewood the median rent is $1,362 per month. It is about $141 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $300 (28%) higher, and is roughly $252 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
