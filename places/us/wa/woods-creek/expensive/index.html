Home prices

The median home in Woods Creek costs $445,000. This is roughly $69,420 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $227,500 (104%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $218,100 (32%) lower.

Rents

Woods Creek's median rent is $1,456 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $43 (3%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $394 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $158 (9%) lower.
