Home prices

The median home in Stanwood costs $340,900. This is approximately $64,600 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $123,400 (56%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $322,200 (48%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stanwood is $1,072. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $281 (20%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $542 (33%) lower.
