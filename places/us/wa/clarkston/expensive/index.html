In comparison to its surrounding areas, Clarkston is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Spokane.

Home prices

The median home in Clarkston costs $143,000. It is about $57,480 (28%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $74,500 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Spokane is approximately $44,600 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clarkston is $745 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $317 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Spokane is roughly $121 (13%) lower.
