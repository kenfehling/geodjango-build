In comparison to its surrounding areas, Clarkston is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Spokane.

Home prices

The median home cost in Clarkston is $143,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $57,480 (28%) lower. It is about $74,500 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $44,600 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Spokane.

Rents

Clarkston's median rent is $745 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is is very similar. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $317 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Spokane it is approximately $121 (13%) lower.
