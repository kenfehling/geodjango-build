Home prices

Rents

Kittitas's median monthly rent is $1,016. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $89 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $46 (4%) lower, and is roughly $196 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
