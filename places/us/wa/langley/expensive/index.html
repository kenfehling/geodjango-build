Rents

The median rent in Langley is $1,139 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $188 (14%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $77 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $475 (29%) lower.
