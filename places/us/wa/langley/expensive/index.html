Home prices

Langley's median home value is $475,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $51,840 (12%) higher. It is roughly $257,500 (118%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $188,100 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Langley is $1,139. It is roughly $188 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $77 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $475 (29%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
