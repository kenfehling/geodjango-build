Home prices

The median home value in Lake Shore is $334,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $17,180 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $117,000 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $77,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Shore the median monthly rent is $1,691. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $239 (16%) higher. It is roughly $629 (59%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $443 (35%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
