Home prices

Lake Shore's median home cost is $334,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $17,180 (4%) lower. It is roughly $117,000 (53%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $77,500 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Lake Shore's median rent is $1,691 a month. It is roughly $239 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $629 (59%) higher, and it is about $443 (35%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
