In comparison to other places in this area, Waterville is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Yakima, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Waterville is $174,000. It is about $132,480 (43%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $43,500 (19%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Yakima it is is very similar.

Rents

In Waterville the median rent is $743 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $211 (22%) lower. It is roughly $319 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $77 (9%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
