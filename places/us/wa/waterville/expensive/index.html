Home prices

The median home in Waterville costs $174,000. This is about $132,480 (43%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $43,500 (19%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Waterville the median rent is $743 per month. This is roughly $211 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $319 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $77 (9%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
