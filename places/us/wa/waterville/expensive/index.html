Compared to its surrounding areas, Waterville is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The median home in Waterville costs $174,000. This is roughly $132,480 (43%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $43,500 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Yakima is is very similar.

Rents

Waterville's median rent is $743 per month. It is roughly $211 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $319 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $77 (9%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
