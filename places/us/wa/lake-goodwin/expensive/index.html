Home prices

The median home price in Lake Goodwin is $401,500. It is roughly $39,920 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $184,000 (84%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $261,600 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Lake Goodwin's median rent is $1,500 a month. It is about $134 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $438 (41%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $114 (7%) lower.
