Home prices

South Bend's median home value is $122,700. This is about $34,440 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $94,800 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Olympia is approximately $174,500 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in South Bend is $548 a month. This is approximately $295 (34%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $514 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Olympia it is roughly $597 (52%) lower.
