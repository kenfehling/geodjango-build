Compared to its nearby areas, South Bend is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Olympia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in South Bend is $122,700. It is about $34,440 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $94,800 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Olympia is about $174,500 (58%) lower.

Rents

South Bend's median rent is $548 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $295 (34%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $514 (48%) lower, and it is about $597 (52%) lower compared to the city of Olympia.
