Home prices

The median home in Ruston costs $464,600. This is about $13,840 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $247,100 (113%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $198,500 (29%) lower.

Rents

Ruston's median rent is $1,257 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $173 (12%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $195 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $357 (22%) lower.
