Home prices

The median home price in Ruston is $464,600. This is approximately $13,840 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $247,100 (113%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $198,500 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Ruston the median rent is $1,257 a month. It is about $173 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $195 (18%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $357 (22%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
