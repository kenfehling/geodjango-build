Home prices

The median home in Ruston is valued at $464,600. It is approximately $13,840 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $247,100 (113%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $198,500 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Ruston the median rent is $1,257 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $173 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $195 (18%) higher, and it is approximately $357 (22%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
