Compared to other places in this area, Elk Plain is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Elk Plain is valued at $228,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $33,600 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $11,200 (5%) higher, and is approximately $434,400 (65%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Elk Plain is $1,360 a month. It is approximately $158 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $298 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $254 (15%) lower.
