In comparison to other places in this area, Richland is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Yakima, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Richland's median home cost is $263,500. This is roughly $57,920 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $46,000 (21%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $90,500 (52%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.

Rents

The median rent in Richland is $1,087 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $77 (7%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $25 (2%) higher, and it is about $267 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.
