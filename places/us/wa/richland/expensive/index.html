In comparison to its surrounding areas, Richland is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Yakima, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richland costs $263,500. This is about $57,920 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $46,000 (21%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $90,500 (52%) higher.

Rents

In Richland the median monthly rent is $1,087. This is roughly $77 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $267 (32%) higher.
