Home prices

The median home price in Richland is $263,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $57,920 (28%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $46,000 (21%) higher, and it is about $90,500 (52%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

Richland's median monthly rent is $1,087. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $77 (7%) higher.It is roughly $25 (2%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $267 (32%) higher compared to the city of Yakima.
