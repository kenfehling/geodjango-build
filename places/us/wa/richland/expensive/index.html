Compared to its surrounding areas, Richland is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Yakima however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richland costs $263,500. It is roughly $57,920 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $46,000 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is roughly $90,500 (52%) higher.

Rents

Richland's median rent is $1,087 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $77 (7%) higher.It is about $25 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is approximately $267 (32%) higher.
