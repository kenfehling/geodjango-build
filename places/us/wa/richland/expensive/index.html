Compared to other places in this area, Richland is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The median home in Richland costs $263,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $57,920 (28%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $46,000 (21%) higher, and it is approximately $90,500 (52%) higher in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

Richland's median monthly rent is $1,087. It is about $77 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $25 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Yakima is approximately $267 (32%) higher.
