Home prices

The value of the median home in Spokane Valley is $204,700. This is roughly $18,540 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $12,800 (5%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Spokane is approximately $17,100 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Spokane Valley is $946. This is about $128 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $116 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Spokane is about $80 (9%) higher.
