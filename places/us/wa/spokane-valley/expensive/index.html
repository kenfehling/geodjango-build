Rents

Spokane Valley's median rent is $946 per month. It is approximately $128 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $116 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Spokane it is approximately $80 (9%) higher.
