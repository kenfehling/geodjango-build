Home prices

Kent's median home cost is $346,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $10,060 (2%) lower.It is approximately $128,900 (59%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $316,700 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Kent's median monthly rent is $1,385. This is approximately $176 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $323 (30%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $229 (14%) lower.
