Home prices

The price of the median home in Kent is $346,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $10,060 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $128,900 (59%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $316,700 (47%) lower.

Rents

Kent's median rent is $1,385 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $176 (11%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $323 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $229 (14%) lower.
