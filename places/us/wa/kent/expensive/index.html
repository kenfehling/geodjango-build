Much like the surrounding areas, it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kent is $346,400. This is approximately $10,060 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $128,900 (59%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $316,700 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kent is $1,385 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $176 (11%) lower. It is roughly $323 (30%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $229 (14%) lower.
