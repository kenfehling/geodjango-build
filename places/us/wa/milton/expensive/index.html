Compared to the surrounding areas, Milton is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Milton is $303,900. This is about $38,480 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $86,400 (39%) higher, and it is about $359,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Milton is $1,320 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $107 (7%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $258 (24%) higher, and is about $294 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
