Home prices

The price of the median home in Milton is $303,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38,480 (11%) lower.It is roughly $86,400 (39%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $359,200 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Milton the median monthly rent is $1,320. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $107 (7%) lower.It is approximately $258 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $294 (18%) lower.
