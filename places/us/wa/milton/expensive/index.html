Home prices

The value of the median home in Milton is $303,900. It is approximately $38,480 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $86,400 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $359,200 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Milton the median monthly rent is $1,320. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $107 (7%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $258 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $294 (18%) lower.
