Compared to other places in this area, Midland is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Midland is valued at $211,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $66,820 (24%) lower.It is roughly $6,300 (2%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $451,900 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Midland the median rent is $1,096 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $93 (7%) lower.It is approximately $34 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $518 (32%) lower.
