Compared to the surrounding areas, Midland is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Midland's median home price is $211,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $66,820 (24%) lower.It is about $6,300 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $451,900 (68%) lower.

Rents

Midland's median rent is $1,096 a month. It is approximately $93 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $34 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $518 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
