Compared to its surrounding areas, Midland is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Midland is $211,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $66,820 (24%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $6,300 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $451,900 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Midland the median rent is $1,096 per month. This is approximately $93 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $34 (3%) higher, and it is roughly $518 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
