Home prices

The median home in Midland is valued at $211,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $66,820 (24%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $6,300 (2%) lower, and is about $451,900 (68%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Midland's median rent is $1,096 a month. It is roughly $93 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $34 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $518 (32%) lower.
