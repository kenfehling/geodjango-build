In comparison to other places in this area, Puyallup is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Puyallup is valued at $329,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $25,040 (8%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $111,500 (51%) higher, and is approximately $334,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Puyallup's median monthly rent is $1,288. This is approximately $31 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $226 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $326 (20%) lower.
