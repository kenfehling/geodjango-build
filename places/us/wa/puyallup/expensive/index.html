In comparison to its nearby areas, Puyallup is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Puyallup is $329,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $25,040 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $111,500 (51%) higher, and is roughly $334,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Puyallup the median rent is $1,288 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $31 (2%) higher. It is roughly $226 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $326 (20%) lower.
