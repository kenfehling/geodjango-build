Home prices

Puyallup's median home cost is $329,000. This is roughly $25,040 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $111,500 (51%) higher, and it is roughly $334,100 (50%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Puyallup's median monthly rent is $1,288. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $31 (2%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $226 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $326 (20%) lower.
