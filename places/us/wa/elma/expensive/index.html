Compared to nearby areas, Elma is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Elma is valued at $167,900. It is roughly $31,920 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $49,600 (22%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $495,200 (74%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Elma is $608 per month. It is approximately $273 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $454 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $1,006 (62%) lower.
