Home prices

The median home in Onalaska costs $208,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $61,160 (41%) higher.It is about $9,200 (4%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $454,800 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Onalaska the median monthly rent is $753. This is about $137 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $309 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $861 (53%) lower.
