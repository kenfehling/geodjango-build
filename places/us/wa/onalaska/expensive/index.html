Home prices

Onalaska's median home cost is $208,300. This is approximately $61,160 (41%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $9,200 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $454,800 (68%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Onalaska is $753. This is approximately $137 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $309 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $861 (53%) lower.
