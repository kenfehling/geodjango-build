Home prices

The median home in Onalaska is valued at $208,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $61,160 (41%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $9,200 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $454,800 (68%) lower.

Rents

Onalaska's median monthly rent is $753. It is roughly $137 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $309 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $861 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
