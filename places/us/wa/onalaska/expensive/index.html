In comparison to other places in this area, Onalaska is significantly more expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Onalaska costs $208,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $61,160 (41%) higher. It is about $9,200 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $454,800 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Onalaska the median rent is $753 per month. This is about $137 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $309 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $861 (53%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
