Home prices

The median home value in Granger is $115,200. It is approximately $14,860 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $102,300 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Yakima is approximately $57,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Granger is $809 per month. This is approximately $56 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $253 (23%) lower, and is about $11 (1%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
