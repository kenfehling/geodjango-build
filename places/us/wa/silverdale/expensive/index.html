Home prices

The median home in Silverdale is valued at $332,700. It is about $24,100 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $115,200 (52%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $330,400 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Silverdale is $1,419. It is approximately $124 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $357 (33%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $195 (12%) lower.
