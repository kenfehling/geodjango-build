Compared to other places in this area, Silverdale is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Silverdale's median home price is $332,700. This is about $24,100 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $115,200 (52%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $330,400 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Silverdale is $1,419 per month. It is about $124 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $357 (33%) higher, and is about $195 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
