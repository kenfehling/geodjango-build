Home prices

The price of the median home in Kirkland is $662,300. This is roughly $792,881 (54%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $444,800 (204%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is is very similar.

Rents

Kirkland's median monthly rent is $1,861. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $554 (22%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $799 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $247 (15%) higher.
