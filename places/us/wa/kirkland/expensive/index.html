In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kirkland is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Kirkland costs $662,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $51,640 (8%) higher. It is approximately $444,800 (204%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Kirkland the median rent is $1,861 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $146 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $799 (75%) higher, and is about $247 (15%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
