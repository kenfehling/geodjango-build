Compared to other places in this area, Lake Forest Park is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Forest Park is valued at $623,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $132,840 (27%) higher.It is about $406,000 (186%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $39,600 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Forest Park is $1,617. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $23 (1%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $555 (52%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is is very similar.
