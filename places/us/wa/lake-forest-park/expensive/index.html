In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lake Forest Park is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home value in Lake Forest Park is $623,500. It is approximately $132,840 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $406,000 (186%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $39,600 (5%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Forest Park is $1,617. It is about $23 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $555 (52%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is is very similar.
