Compared to other places in this area, Lake Forest Park is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake Forest Park is $623,500. This is approximately $132,840 (27%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $406,000 (186%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $39,600 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Forest Park is $1,617 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $23 (1%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $555 (52%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Seattle.
