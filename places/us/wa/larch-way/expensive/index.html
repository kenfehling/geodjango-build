The median home in Larch Way costs $464,400. This is approximately $50,120 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $246,900 (113%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $198,700 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Larch Way the median rent is $1,651 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $85 (5%) higher. It is roughly $589 (55%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $37 (2%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
