The median home in Longview costs $190,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $4,100 (2%) higher. It is roughly $27,100 (12%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $221,600 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Longview is $859. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $203 (19%) lower, and is about $389 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
