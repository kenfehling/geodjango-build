Much like its surrounding areas, it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Longview costs $190,400. It is about $4,100 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $27,100 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $221,600 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Longview the median rent is $859 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $9 (1%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $203 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $389 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
