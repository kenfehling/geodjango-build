Home prices

The price of the median home in Longview is $190,400. It is approximately $4,100 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $27,100 (12%) lower, and is approximately $221,600 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Longview the median rent is $859 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $9 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $203 (19%) lower, and is approximately $389 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
