Compared to nearby areas, Okanogan is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Wenatchee.

Home prices

The median home value in Okanogan is $148,700. Compared to nearby areas this is about $21,520 (16%) higher.It is about $68,800 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $104,900 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Wenatchee.

Rents

In Okanogan the median monthly rent is $674. It is approximately $25 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $388 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee is roughly $260 (27%) lower.
