In comparison to nearby areas, Okanogan is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Wenatchee.

Home prices

Okanogan's median home value is $148,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $21,520 (16%) higher. It is approximately $68,800 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee is approximately $104,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Okanogan is $674 per month. It is approximately $25 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $388 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Wenatchee it is roughly $260 (27%) lower.
