In comparison to other places in this area, Okanogan is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Wenatchee.

Home prices

The median home value in Okanogan is $148,700. This is about $21,520 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $68,800 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Wenatchee it is approximately $104,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

Okanogan's median rent is $674 a month. This is about $25 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $388 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Wenatchee is about $260 (27%) lower.
