In comparison to the surrounding areas, Okanogan is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Wenatchee, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Okanogan is valued at $148,700. This is roughly $21,520 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $68,800 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee is about $104,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Okanogan the median monthly rent is $674. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $25 (3%) higher. It is roughly $388 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $260 (27%) lower compared to the city of Wenatchee.
