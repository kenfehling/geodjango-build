Compared to nearby areas, Okanogan is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Wenatchee, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Okanogan is valued at $148,700. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $21,520 (16%) higher.It is approximately $68,800 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee is approximately $104,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Okanogan the median monthly rent is $674. This is about $25 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $388 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Wenatchee is roughly $260 (27%) lower.
