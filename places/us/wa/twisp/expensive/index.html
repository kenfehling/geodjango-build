Home prices

The cost of the median home in Twisp is $165,900. This is approximately $9,060 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $51,600 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee is roughly $87,700 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Twisp the median monthly rent is $710. This is approximately $48 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $352 (33%) lower, and is approximately $224 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Wenatchee.
