Home prices

The median home in Twisp costs $165,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $9,060 (5%) lower. It is approximately $51,600 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Wenatchee it is approximately $87,700 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Twisp is $710. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $48 (7%) higher. It is about $352 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $224 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Wenatchee.
