Home prices

The median home in Morton is valued at $156,000. It is about $15,580 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $61,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $507,100 (76%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Morton's median rent is $729 per month. It is roughly $165 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $333 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $885 (54%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
