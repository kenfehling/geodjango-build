Home prices

The median home in Indianola costs $310,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $48,540 (13%) lower. It is approximately $92,600 (42%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $353,000 (53%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Indianola is $1,336. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $98 (7%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $274 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $278 (17%) lower.
