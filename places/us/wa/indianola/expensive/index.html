Home prices

The median home in Indianola costs $310,100. This is roughly $48,540 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $92,600 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $353,000 (53%) lower.

Rents

Indianola's median rent is $1,336 per month. It is about $98 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $274 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $278 (17%) lower.
