Compared to other places in this area, Washougal is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Washougal is valued at $325,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $17,460 (5%) higher.It is about $108,000 (49%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $86,500 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Washougal the median rent is $1,171 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $67 (5%) lower.It is roughly $109 (10%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $77 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
