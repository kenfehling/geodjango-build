Compared to the surrounding areas, Washougal is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Portland, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Washougal is $325,500. This is approximately $17,460 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $108,000 (49%) higher, and it is approximately $86,500 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Washougal's median monthly rent is $1,171. It is roughly $67 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $109 (10%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $77 (6%) lower.
