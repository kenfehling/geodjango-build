In comparison to nearby areas, Washougal is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Washougal is $325,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $17,460 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $108,000 (49%) higher, and it is approximately $86,500 (20%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Washougal's median monthly rent is $1,171. This is about $67 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $109 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is about $77 (6%) lower.
