Home prices

The price of the median home in Three Lakes is $474,600. It is about $23,340 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $257,100 (118%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $188,500 (28%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Three Lakes the median rent is $1,840 per month. This is roughly $236 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $778 (73%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $226 (14%) higher.
