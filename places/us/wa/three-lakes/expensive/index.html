Home prices

The median home in Three Lakes costs $474,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $23,340 (4%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $257,100 (118%) higher, and is about $188,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Three Lakes the median monthly rent is $1,840. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $236 (14%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $778 (73%) higher, and it is roughly $226 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
