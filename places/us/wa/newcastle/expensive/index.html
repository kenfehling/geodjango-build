In comparison to other places in this area, Newcastle is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Seattle, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Newcastle is $759,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $109,840 (12%) lower.It is roughly $542,000 (249%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $96,400 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Newcastle the median rent is $1,814 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $128 (7%) higher.It is roughly $752 (70%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $200 (12%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
