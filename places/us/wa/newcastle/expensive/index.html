Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Newcastle is $1,814 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $128 (7%) higher. It is about $752 (70%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $200 (12%) higher.
