Compared to its nearby areas, Centralia is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Centralia's median home value is $157,300. It is approximately $38,680 (19%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $60,200 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $505,800 (76%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centralia is $876. This is approximately $121 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $186 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $738 (45%) lower.
