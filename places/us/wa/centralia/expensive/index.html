Compared to its surrounding areas, Centralia is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Centralia costs $157,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $38,680 (19%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $60,200 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $505,800 (76%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Centralia is $876 a month. This is roughly $121 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $186 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $738 (45%) lower.
