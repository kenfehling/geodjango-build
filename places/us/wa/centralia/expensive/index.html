Home prices

The price of the median home in Centralia is $157,300. This is roughly $38,680 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $60,200 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $505,800 (76%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Centralia's median monthly rent is $876. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $121 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $186 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $738 (45%) lower.
