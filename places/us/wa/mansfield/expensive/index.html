Compared to other places in this area, Mansfield is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Wenatchee, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Mansfield is $130,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $40,980 (23%) lower. It is about $86,700 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Wenatchee it is about $122,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mansfield is $675. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $13 (1%) lower. It is about $387 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $259 (27%) lower compared to the city of Wenatchee.
