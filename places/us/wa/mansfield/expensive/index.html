Home prices

The median home in Mansfield costs $130,800. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $40,980 (23%) lower.It is roughly $86,700 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Wenatchee it is roughly $122,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mansfield is $675 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13 (1%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $387 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Wenatchee is roughly $259 (27%) lower.
