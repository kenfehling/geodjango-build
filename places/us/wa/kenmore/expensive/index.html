In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kenmore is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Kenmore costs $572,900. It is approximately $65,500 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $355,400 (163%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $90,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kenmore is $1,433. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $311 (17%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $371 (34%) higher, and it is about $181 (11%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
