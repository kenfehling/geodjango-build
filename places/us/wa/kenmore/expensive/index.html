In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kenmore is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kenmore is valued at $572,900. It is approximately $65,500 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $355,400 (163%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $90,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

Kenmore's median rent is $1,433 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $311 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $371 (34%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $181 (11%) lower.
