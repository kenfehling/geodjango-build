Rents

Centerville's median monthly rent is $1,000. It is roughly $206 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $62 (5%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is approximately $180 (21%) higher.
