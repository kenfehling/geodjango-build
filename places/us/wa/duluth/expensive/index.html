Compared to other places in this area, Duluth is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a little more expensive even compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Duluth's median home cost is $470,500. This is roughly $82,420 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $253,000 (116%) higher, and is roughly $58,500 (14%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Duluth is $1,170 per month. This is approximately $338 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $108 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $78 (6%) lower.
