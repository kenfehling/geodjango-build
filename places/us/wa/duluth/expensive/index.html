In comparison to the surrounding areas, Duluth is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home cost in Duluth is $470,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $82,420 (21%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $253,000 (116%) higher, and it is roughly $58,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Duluth the median monthly rent is $1,170. It is approximately $338 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $108 (10%) higher, and it is approximately $78 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
