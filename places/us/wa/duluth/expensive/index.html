Compared to nearby areas, Duluth is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Portland, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Duluth's median home cost is $470,500. This is approximately $82,420 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $253,000 (116%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $58,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

Duluth's median monthly rent is $1,170. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $338 (22%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $108 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $78 (6%) lower.
