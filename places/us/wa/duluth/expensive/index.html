Compared to nearby areas, Duluth is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Portland, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Duluth is $470,500. It is roughly $82,420 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $253,000 (116%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $58,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Duluth the median monthly rent is $1,170. It is roughly $338 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $108 (10%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $78 (6%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
