Compared to other places in this area, Duluth is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Duluth is valued at $470,500. It is roughly $82,420 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $253,000 (116%) higher, and is roughly $58,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Duluth is $1,170. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $338 (22%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $108 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $78 (6%) lower.
