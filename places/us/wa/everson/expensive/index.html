Home prices

The median home in Everson costs $264,800. This is roughly $4,680 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $47,300 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $398,300 (60%) lower.

Rents

In Everson the median rent is $979 a month. This is approximately $66 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $83 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $635 (39%) lower.
