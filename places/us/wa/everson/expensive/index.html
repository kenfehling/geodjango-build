Home prices

The median home in Everson is valued at $264,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $4,680 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $47,300 (21%) higher, and it is about $398,300 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Everson's median rent is $979 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $66 (6%) lower. It is roughly $83 (7%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $635 (39%) lower.
