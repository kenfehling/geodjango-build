Home prices

The median home in Cosmopolis is valued at $165,300. It is approximately $13,080 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $52,200 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $112,600 (40%) lower compared to the city of Tacoma.

Rents

In Cosmopolis the median rent is $864 per month. This is about $13 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $198 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tacoma it is about $316 (26%) lower.
