Home prices

The median home cost in Cosmopolis is $165,300. This is about $13,080 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $52,200 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Tacoma is about $112,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

Cosmopolis's median monthly rent is $864. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $198 (18%) lower, and is about $316 (26%) lower compared to the city of Tacoma.
