In comparison to its nearby areas, Lakewood is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakewood is $269,200. This is approximately $32,140 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $51,700 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $393,900 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lakewood is $1,034 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $127 (10%) lower. It is approximately $28 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $580 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
