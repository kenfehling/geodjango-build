In comparison to its nearby areas, Lakewood is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Lakewood's median home cost is $269,200. This is about $32,140 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $51,700 (23%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $393,900 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Lakewood the median rent is $1,034 per month. This is roughly $127 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $28 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $580 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
