Home prices

The median home in Lakewood costs $269,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $32,140 (10%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $51,700 (23%) higher, and is roughly $393,900 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakewood is $1,034. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $127 (10%) lower.It is approximately $28 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $580 (35%) lower.
