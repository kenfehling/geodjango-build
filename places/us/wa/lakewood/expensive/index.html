In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lakewood is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Lakewood's median home price is $269,200. It is approximately $32,140 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $51,700 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $393,900 (59%) lower.

Rents

In Lakewood the median monthly rent is $1,034. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $127 (10%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $28 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $580 (35%) lower.
