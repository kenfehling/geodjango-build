Home prices

The median home in Lakewood costs $269,200. It is approximately $32,140 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $51,700 (23%) higher, and is about $393,900 (59%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Lakewood the median monthly rent is $1,034. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $127 (10%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $28 (2%) lower, and is about $580 (35%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
