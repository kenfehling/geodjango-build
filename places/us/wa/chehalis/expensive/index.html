Home prices

The median home in Chehalis is valued at $182,000. It is about $6,040 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $35,500 (16%) lower, and is approximately $481,100 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Chehalis is $867 a month. This is roughly $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $195 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $747 (46%) lower.
