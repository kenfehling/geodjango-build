Home prices

The median home in Chehalis costs $182,000. It is approximately $6,040 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $35,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $481,100 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Chehalis's median monthly rent is $867. It is about $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $195 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $747 (46%) lower.
