Home prices

The median home in Chehalis is valued at $182,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $6,040 (3%) higher.It is about $35,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is roughly $481,100 (72%) lower.

Rents

Chehalis's median rent is $867 per month. This is approximately $96 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $195 (18%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $747 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
