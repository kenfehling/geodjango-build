In comparison to other places in this area, Issaquah is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Issaquah costs $642,300. This is about $40,780 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $424,800 (195%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $20,800 (3%) lower.

Rents

Issaquah's median rent is $1,982 a month. It is roughly $92 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $920 (86%) higher, and is approximately $368 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
