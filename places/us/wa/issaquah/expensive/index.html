In comparison to the surrounding areas, Issaquah is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Issaquah is valued at $642,300. It is roughly $40,780 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $424,800 (195%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $20,800 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Issaquah the median monthly rent is $1,982. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $92 (4%) higher.It is about $920 (86%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $368 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
