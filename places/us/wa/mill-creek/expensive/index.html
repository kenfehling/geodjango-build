Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mill Creek is $536,800. It is approximately $69,560 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $319,300 (146%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $126,300 (19%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Mill Creek the median rent is $1,812 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is is very similar.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $750 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $198 (12%) higher.
