Home prices

The median home in Mill Creek costs $536,800. It is roughly $69,560 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $319,300 (146%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $126,300 (19%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Mill Creek is $1,812 a month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $750 (70%) higher, and it is about $198 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Seattle.
