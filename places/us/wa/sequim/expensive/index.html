Compared to other places in this area, Sequim is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Sequim is valued at $238,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $15,900 (6%) lower. It is about $21,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $424,600 (64%) lower.

Rents

Sequim's median monthly rent is $970. This is roughly $73 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $92 (8%) lower, and it is approximately $644 (39%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
