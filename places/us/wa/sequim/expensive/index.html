Home prices

The price of the median home in Sequim is $238,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $15,900 (6%) lower.It is approximately $21,000 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $424,600 (64%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Sequim the median rent is $970 a month. This is approximately $73 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $92 (8%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $644 (39%) lower.
