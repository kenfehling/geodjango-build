Home prices

Sequim's median home cost is $238,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $15,900 (6%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $21,000 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $424,600 (64%) lower.

Rents

Sequim's median rent is $970 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $73 (8%) higher. It is approximately $92 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $644 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
