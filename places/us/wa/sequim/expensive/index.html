Home prices

The median home cost in Sequim is $238,500. This is approximately $15,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $21,000 (9%) higher, and it is approximately $424,600 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Sequim the median monthly rent is $970. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $73 (8%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $92 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $644 (39%) lower.
