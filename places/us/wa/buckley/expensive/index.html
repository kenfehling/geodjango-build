Compared to other places in this area, Buckley is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Buckley is valued at $334,000. This is about $64,620 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $116,500 (53%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $329,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

Buckley's median monthly rent is $1,352. This is approximately $54 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $290 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $262 (16%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
