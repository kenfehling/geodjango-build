Compared to the surrounding areas, Buckley is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Buckley costs $334,000. This is roughly $64,620 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $116,500 (53%) higher, and it is about $329,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Buckley's median rent is $1,352 per month. It is about $54 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $290 (27%) higher, and it is roughly $262 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
