In comparison to its nearby areas, Vancouver is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Vancouver costs $286,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $20,740 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $69,000 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $125,500 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Vancouver is $1,219 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $158 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $157 (14%) higher, and is roughly $29 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
