Compared to nearby areas, Vancouver is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Vancouver's median home cost is $286,500. It is roughly $20,740 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $69,000 (31%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $125,500 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Vancouver the median monthly rent is $1,219. It is approximately $158 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $157 (14%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $29 (2%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
