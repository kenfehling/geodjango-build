Compared to nearby areas, Vancouver is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Portland, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Vancouver is valued at $286,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $20,740 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $69,000 (31%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $125,500 (30%) lower.

Rents

Vancouver's median rent is $1,219 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $158 (11%) lower.It is roughly $157 (14%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $29 (2%) lower.
