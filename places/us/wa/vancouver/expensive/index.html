Compared to its nearby areas, Vancouver is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Vancouver is valued at $286,500. It is about $20,740 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $69,000 (31%) higher, and is approximately $125,500 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Vancouver is $1,219. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $158 (11%) lower. It is about $157 (14%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $29 (2%) lower.
