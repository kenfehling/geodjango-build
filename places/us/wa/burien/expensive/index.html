Home prices

The median home in Burien is valued at $380,300. It is about $16,200 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $162,800 (74%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $282,800 (42%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Burien's median monthly rent is $1,268. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $50 (3%) lower. It is about $206 (19%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $346 (21%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
