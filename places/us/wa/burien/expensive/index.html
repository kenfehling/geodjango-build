Home prices

The median home in Burien is valued at $380,300. It is roughly $16,200 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $162,800 (74%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $282,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Burien's median monthly rent is $1,268. This is approximately $50 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $206 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $346 (21%) lower.
