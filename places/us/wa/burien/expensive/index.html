Home prices

Burien's median home cost is $380,300. This is about $16,200 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $162,800 (74%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $282,800 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Burien is $1,268 a month. It is roughly $50 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $206 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $346 (21%) lower.
