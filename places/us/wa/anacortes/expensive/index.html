Home prices

The median home cost in Anacortes is $406,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $137,960 (51%) higher.It is about $188,900 (86%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $256,700 (38%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Anacortes the median monthly rent is $1,193. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $88 (7%) higher.It is about $131 (12%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $421 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
