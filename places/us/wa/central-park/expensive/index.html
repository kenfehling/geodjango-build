Home prices

Rents

In Central Park the median rent is $814 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $47 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $248 (23%) lower, and is about $366 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Tacoma.
