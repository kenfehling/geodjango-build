Home prices

The median home in Clover Creek is valued at $271,500. It is about $20,180 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $54,000 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $391,600 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clover Creek is $1,036. This is approximately $187 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $26 (2%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $578 (35%) lower.
