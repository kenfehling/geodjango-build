Home prices

The median home in Leavenworth is valued at $355,600. This is about $69,760 (24%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $138,100 (63%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $32,900 (10%) higher compared to the city of Everett.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Leavenworth is $876. It is roughly $122 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $186 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Everett it is approximately $365 (29%) lower.
