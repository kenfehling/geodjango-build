Home prices

The value of the median home in Leavenworth is $355,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $69,760 (24%) higher. It is about $138,100 (63%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $32,900 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Everett.

Rents

Leavenworth's median monthly rent is $876. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $122 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $186 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Everett it is approximately $365 (29%) lower.
