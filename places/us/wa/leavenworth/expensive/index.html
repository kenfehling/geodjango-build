Compared to other places in this area, Leavenworth is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Everett.

Home prices

Leavenworth's median home price is $355,600. This is roughly $69,760 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $138,100 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Everett is approximately $32,900 (10%) higher.

Rents

Leavenworth's median rent is $876 per month. It is roughly $122 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $186 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $365 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Everett.
