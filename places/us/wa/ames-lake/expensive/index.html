Home prices

The median home in Ames Lake is valued at $670,300. This is approximately $50,720 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $452,800 (208%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $7,200 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ames Lake is $1,932. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $118 (6%) higher. It is about $870 (81%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $318 (19%) higher compared to the city of Seattle.
