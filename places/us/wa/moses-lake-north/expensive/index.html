Home prices

The median home in Moses Lake North is valued at $118,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $52,380 (30%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $99,100 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $54,600 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Moses Lake North the median rent is $739 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $49 (7%) higher. It is about $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is about $81 (9%) lower.
