Compared to its surrounding areas, Tacoma is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

Tacoma's median home cost is $277,900. This is approximately $132,340 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $60,400 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $385,200 (58%) lower.

Rents

Tacoma's median rent is $1,180 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $98 (7%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $118 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $434 (26%) lower.
