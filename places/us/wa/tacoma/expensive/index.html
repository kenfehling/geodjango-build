In comparison to other places in this area, Tacoma is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home cost in Tacoma is $277,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $132,340 (32%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $60,400 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $385,200 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tacoma is $1,180. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $98 (7%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $118 (11%) higher, and is about $434 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
