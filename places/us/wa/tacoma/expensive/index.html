In comparison to nearby areas, Tacoma is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Tacoma costs $277,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $132,340 (32%) lower.It is about $60,400 (27%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $385,200 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Tacoma the median rent is $1,180 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $98 (7%) lower.It is about $118 (11%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle it is about $434 (26%) lower.
