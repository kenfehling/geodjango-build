Home prices

The median home in North Bonneville is valued at $248,200. This is approximately $14,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $30,700 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $163,800 (39%) lower.

Rents

North Bonneville's median rent is $828 a month. This is roughly $87 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $234 (22%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $420 (33%) lower.
