Compared to its surrounding areas, Lynnwood is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lynnwood is $395,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $24,580 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $177,800 (81%) higher, and it is about $267,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lynnwood is $1,303. This is roughly $309 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $241 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $311 (19%) lower.
