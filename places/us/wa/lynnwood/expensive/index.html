In comparison to nearby areas, Lynnwood is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lynnwood is valued at $395,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24,580 (5%) lower.It is roughly $177,800 (81%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $267,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Lynnwood the median monthly rent is $1,303. It is roughly $309 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $241 (22%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $311 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
