Compared to the surrounding areas, Lynnwood is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lynnwood is valued at $395,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24,580 (5%) lower. It is approximately $177,800 (81%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $267,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Lynnwood the median rent is $1,303 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $309 (19%) lower. It is about $241 (22%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $311 (19%) lower.
