In comparison to its nearby areas, Lynnwood is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home in Lynnwood costs $395,300. It is approximately $24,580 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $177,800 (81%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $267,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Lynnwood's median rent is $1,303 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $309 (19%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $241 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $311 (19%) lower.
