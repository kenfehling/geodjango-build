Compared to the surrounding areas, Yakima is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The median home in Yakima is valued at $173,000. It is about $17,700 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $44,500 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Yakima is $820. This is roughly $142 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $242 (22%) lower.
