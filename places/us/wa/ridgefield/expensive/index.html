Compared to nearby areas, Ridgefield is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Ridgefield costs $398,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $27,140 (7%) higher. It is about $181,100 (83%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $13,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

Ridgefield's median rent is $1,746 per month. It is about $305 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $684 (64%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $498 (39%) higher.
