Compared to other places in this area, Ridgefield is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Ridgefield's median home value is $398,600. This is approximately $27,140 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $181,100 (83%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $13,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Ridgefield the median monthly rent is $1,746. It is approximately $305 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $684 (64%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $498 (39%) higher.
