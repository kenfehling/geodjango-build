Home prices

The median home in Burlington costs $240,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $47,080 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $23,300 (10%) higher, and it is about $422,300 (63%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Burlington the median rent is $1,092 per month. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $30 (2%) higher, and is roughly $522 (32%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
