Home prices

The median home in Burlington is valued at $240,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $66,280 (21%) lower. It is approximately $23,300 (10%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $422,300 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Burlington's median rent is $1,092 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $117 (12%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $30 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $522 (32%) lower.
