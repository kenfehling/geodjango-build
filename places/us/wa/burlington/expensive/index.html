Compared to its surrounding areas, Burlington is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Burlington is $240,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $66,280 (21%) lower. It is approximately $23,300 (10%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $422,300 (63%) lower.

Rents

Burlington's median rent is $1,092 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $117 (12%) higher. It is about $30 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $522 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
