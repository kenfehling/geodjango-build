Home prices

The median home cost in Ocean Shores is $233,300. It is approximately $82,360 (54%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $15,800 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $63,900 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Olympia.

Rents

In Ocean Shores the median rent is $987 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $142 (16%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $75 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $158 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Olympia.
