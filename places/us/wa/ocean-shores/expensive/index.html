Compared to the surrounding areas, Ocean Shores is significantly more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Olympia, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ocean Shores is $233,300. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $82,360 (54%) higher. It is about $15,800 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Olympia it is about $63,900 (21%) lower.

Rents

Ocean Shores's median rent is $987 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $142 (16%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $75 (7%) lower, and it is about $158 (13%) lower compared to the city of Olympia.
