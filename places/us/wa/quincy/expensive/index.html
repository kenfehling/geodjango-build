Home prices

Rents

In Quincy the median monthly rent is $775. It is approximately $115 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $45 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.
