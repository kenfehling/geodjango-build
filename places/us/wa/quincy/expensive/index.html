Home prices

The median home in Quincy costs $153,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $12,080 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $64,000 (29%) lower, and is roughly $19,500 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Yakima.

Rents

In Quincy the median rent is $775 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $115 (17%) higher. It is about $287 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yakima it is approximately $45 (5%) lower.
