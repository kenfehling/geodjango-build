In comparison to other places in this area, Quincy is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Yakima.

Home prices

The median home value in Quincy is $153,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $12,080 (8%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $64,000 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Yakima it is roughly $19,500 (11%) lower.

Rents

Quincy's median rent is $775 a month. This is roughly $115 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $45 (5%) lower compared to the city of Yakima.
