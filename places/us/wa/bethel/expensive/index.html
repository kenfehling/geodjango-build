Home prices

The median home cost in Bethel is $264,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $6,100 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $46,900 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $398,700 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bethel is $1,233. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $62 (4%) lower. It is roughly $171 (16%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $381 (23%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
