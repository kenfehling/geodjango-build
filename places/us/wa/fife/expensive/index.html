In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fife is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fife is valued at $288,500. It is approximately $29,060 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $71,000 (32%) higher, and it is roughly $374,600 (56%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fife is $1,351. It is approximately $65 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $289 (27%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is approximately $263 (16%) lower.
