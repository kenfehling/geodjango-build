Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fife is $288,500. It is roughly $29,060 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $71,000 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is roughly $374,600 (56%) lower.

Rents

Fife's median monthly rent is $1,351. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $65 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $289 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle it is approximately $263 (16%) lower.
