Home prices

The value of the median home in Riverbend is $441,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $101,060 (18%) lower. It is approximately $224,100 (103%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $221,500 (33%) lower.

Rents

Riverbend's median rent is $1,786 a month. It is roughly $295 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $724 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is about $172 (10%) higher.
