Home prices

The cost of the median home in Riverbend is $441,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $101,060 (18%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $224,100 (103%) higher, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $221,500 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Riverbend is $1,786 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $295 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $724 (68%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $172 (10%) higher.
