Home prices

The median home in Spanaway costs $240,800. This is approximately $14,780 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $23,300 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $422,300 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Spanaway the median rent is $1,251 a month. It is about $68 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $189 (17%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $363 (22%) lower compared to the city of Seattle.
