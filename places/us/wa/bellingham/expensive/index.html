Compared to nearby areas, Bellingham is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home value in Bellingham is $374,700. It is approximately $58,640 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $157,200 (72%) higher, and it is about $288,400 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

In Bellingham the median monthly rent is $1,056. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $158 (13%) lower. It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is roughly $558 (34%) lower.
