In comparison to nearby areas, Bellingham is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Seattle however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bellingham is $374,700. This is roughly $58,640 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $157,200 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is about $288,400 (43%) lower.

Rents

Bellingham's median monthly rent is $1,056. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $158 (13%) lower.It is is very similar compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $558 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
