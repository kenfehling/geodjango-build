In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bellingham is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Seattle.

Home prices

The median home price in Bellingham is $374,700. This is about $58,640 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $157,200 (72%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $288,400 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

Bellingham's median rent is $1,056 per month. It is roughly $158 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $558 (34%) lower.
