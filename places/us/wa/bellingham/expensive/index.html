Home prices

The median home in Bellingham is valued at $374,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $58,640 (18%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $157,200 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Seattle is approximately $288,400 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bellingham is $1,056 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $158 (13%) lower. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Seattle it is roughly $558 (34%) lower.
