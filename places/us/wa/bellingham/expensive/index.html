Compared to its nearby areas, Bellingham is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Seattle, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bellingham is $374,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $58,640 (18%) higher. It is about $157,200 (72%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $288,400 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bellingham is $1,056. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $158 (13%) lower. It is is very similar compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Seattle is approximately $558 (34%) lower.
