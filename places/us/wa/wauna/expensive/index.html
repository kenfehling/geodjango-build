Rents

In Wauna the median monthly rent is $1,292. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $131 (9%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $230 (21%) higher, and it is roughly $322 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.
