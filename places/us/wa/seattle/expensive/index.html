Compared to nearby areas, Seattle is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Seattle costs $663,100. This is approximately $157,783 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $445,600 (204%) higher compared to the rest of the nation.

Rents

Seattle's median rent is $1,614 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $41 (2%) lower. It is about $552 (51%) higher compared to the rest of the nation.
