In comparison to nearby areas, Seattle is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The median home in Seattle costs $663,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $157,783 (19%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $445,600 (204%) higher.

Rents

Seattle's median monthly rent is $1,614. This is about $41 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $552 (51%) higher compared to the rest of the country.
