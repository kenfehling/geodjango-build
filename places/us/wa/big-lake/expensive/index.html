Home prices

The median home in Big Lake costs $386,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $85,280 (28%) higher.It is about $168,600 (77%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $277,000 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Seattle.

Rents

The median rent in Big Lake is $977 a month. It is roughly $110 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $85 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Seattle it is about $637 (39%) lower.
