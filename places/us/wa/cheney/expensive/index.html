Home prices

Rents

Cheney's median rent is $893 per month. This is approximately $82 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $169 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $27 (3%) higher compared to the city of Spokane.
