Home prices

The cost of the median home in Peterstown is $116,800. This is about $27,840 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $100,700 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke is about $18,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Peterstown is $760. This is about $84 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $302 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $54 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
