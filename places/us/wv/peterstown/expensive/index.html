Home prices

The median home cost in Peterstown is $116,800. This is about $27,840 (31%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $100,700 (46%) lower, and it is about $18,300 (13%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

In Peterstown the median rent is $760 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $84 (12%) higher.It is approximately $302 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is roughly $54 (6%) lower.
