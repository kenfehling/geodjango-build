Compared to its surrounding areas, Milton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milton costs $119,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $32,940 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $98,300 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is about $26,600 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milton is $687. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $90 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $375 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $48 (6%) lower.
