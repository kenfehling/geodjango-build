Home prices

The median home in Milton is valued at $119,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $32,940 (21%) lower. It is about $98,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is roughly $26,600 (18%) lower.

Rents

In Milton the median rent is $687 per month. It is approximately $90 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $375 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is roughly $48 (6%) lower.
