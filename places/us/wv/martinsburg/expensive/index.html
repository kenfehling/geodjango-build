In comparison to its surrounding areas, Martinsburg is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Martinsburg is valued at $157,200. This is about $35,160 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $60,300 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $444,300 (73%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Martinsburg is $948. It is about $155 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $114 (10%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $593 (38%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
