Home prices

The cost of the median home in Martinsburg is $157,200. It is approximately $35,160 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $60,300 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $444,300 (73%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Martinsburg is $948 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $155 (14%) lower.It is approximately $114 (10%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $593 (38%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
