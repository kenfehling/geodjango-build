Home prices

The median home in Martinsburg is valued at $157,200. This is about $35,160 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $60,300 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $444,300 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Martinsburg's median rent is $948 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $155 (14%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $114 (10%) lower, and it is about $593 (38%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
