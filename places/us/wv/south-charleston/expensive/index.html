Compared to its surrounding areas, South Charleston is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Charleston however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Charleston costs $130,700. It is approximately $14,720 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $86,800 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $15,100 (10%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In South Charleston the median monthly rent is $702. It is about $85 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $360 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is roughly $33 (4%) lower.
