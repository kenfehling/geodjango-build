In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Charleston is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The median home in South Charleston costs $130,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $14,720 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $86,800 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is roughly $15,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

South Charleston's median rent is $702 per month. It is roughly $85 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $360 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $33 (4%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
