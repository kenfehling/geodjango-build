In comparison to nearby areas, South Charleston is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Charleston, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Charleston is valued at $130,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,720 (12%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $86,800 (39%) lower, and is approximately $15,100 (10%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In South Charleston the median rent is $702 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $85 (10%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $360 (33%) lower, and it is about $33 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
