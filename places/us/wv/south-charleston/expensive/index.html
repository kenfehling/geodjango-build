In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Charleston is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in South Charleston is $130,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14,720 (12%) higher.It is approximately $86,800 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $15,100 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In South Charleston the median monthly rent is $702. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $85 (10%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $360 (33%) lower, and is about $33 (4%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
