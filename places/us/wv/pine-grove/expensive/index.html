Home prices

The median home value in Pine Grove is $89,600. This is roughly $5,420 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $127,900 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $35,400 (28%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Pine Grove's median monthly rent is $265. It is about $319 (54%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $797 (75%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $693 (72%) lower.
