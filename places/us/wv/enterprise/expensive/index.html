Home prices

The median home value in Enterprise is $87,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $32,660 (27%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $129,900 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $37,400 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Enterprise is $680. It is roughly $70 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $278 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
