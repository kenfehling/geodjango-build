Home prices

The median home in Pleasant Valley costs $153,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $30,300 (24%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $64,100 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $28,400 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Pleasant Valley the median rent is $570 per month. It is approximately $195 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $492 (46%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $388 (40%) lower.
