Compared to the surrounding areas, Pleasant Valley is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pleasant Valley costs $153,400. It is roughly $30,300 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $64,100 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $28,400 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Pleasant Valley the median monthly rent is $570. This is about $195 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $492 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $388 (40%) lower.
