Home prices

The median home cost in Pleasant Valley is $153,400. It is about $30,300 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $64,100 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $28,400 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Pleasant Valley the median monthly rent is $570. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $195 (25%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $492 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $388 (40%) lower.
