Home prices

The median home in Shepherdstown is valued at $303,600. This is approximately $66,740 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $86,100 (39%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $297,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Shepherdstown the median monthly rent is $916. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $120 (11%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $146 (13%) lower, and is approximately $625 (40%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
