Home prices

The median home in West Liberty costs $107,900. This is about $16,940 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $109,600 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $17,100 (13%) lower.

Rents

West Liberty's median rent is $604 a month. It is approximately $49 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $458 (43%) lower, and is about $354 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
