Home prices

The median home price in West Liberty is $107,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16,940 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $109,600 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $17,100 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in West Liberty is $604 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $49 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $458 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $354 (36%) lower.
