Compared to other places in this area, West Liberty is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Liberty costs $107,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $16,940 (18%) higher. It is roughly $109,600 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $17,100 (13%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in West Liberty is $604 a month. It is about $49 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $458 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $354 (36%) lower.
