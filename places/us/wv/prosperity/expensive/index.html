Home prices

The median home in Prosperity is valued at $115,700. This is about $17,080 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $101,800 (46%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is roughly $30,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

Prosperity's median rent is $878 per month. This is approximately $324 (58%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $184 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $143 (19%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
