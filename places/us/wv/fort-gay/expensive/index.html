Home prices

The median home in Fort Gay costs $80,000. This is about $36,840 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $137,500 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is roughly $65,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Gay is $564 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $156 (21%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $498 (46%) lower, and is about $171 (23%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
