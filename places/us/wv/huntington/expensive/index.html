In comparison to other places in this area, Huntington is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Huntington is $98,200. It is roughly $20,100 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $119,300 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is roughly $47,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

Huntington's median monthly rent is $712. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $58 (7%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $350 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is about $23 (3%) lower.
