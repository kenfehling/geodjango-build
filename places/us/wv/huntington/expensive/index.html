Compared to its nearby areas, Huntington is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Huntington's median home cost is $98,200. This is roughly $20,100 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $119,300 (54%) lower, and it is about $47,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In Huntington the median rent is $712 per month. It is roughly $58 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $350 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $23 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
