In comparison to its surrounding areas, Huntington is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Huntington is valued at $98,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $20,100 (16%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $119,300 (54%) lower, and it is approximately $47,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Huntington's median monthly rent is $712. It is approximately $58 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $350 (32%) lower, and is roughly $23 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
