Home prices

Rents

Mitchell Heights's median rent is $979 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $304 (45%) higher. It is approximately $83 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $244 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Charleston.
