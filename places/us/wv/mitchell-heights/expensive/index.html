Home prices

Rents

Mitchell Heights's median rent is $979 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $304 (45%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $83 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $244 (33%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
