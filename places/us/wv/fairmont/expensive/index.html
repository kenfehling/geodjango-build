Compared to other places in this area, Fairmont is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Fairmont costs $110,900. It is approximately $20,700 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $106,600 (49%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $14,100 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairmont is $754. It is about $26 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $204 (21%) lower.
