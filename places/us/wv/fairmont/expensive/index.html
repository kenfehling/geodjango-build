Home prices

The median home value in Fairmont is $110,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $20,700 (15%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $106,600 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $14,100 (11%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Fairmont's median rent is $754 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $26 (3%) higher.It is approximately $308 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $204 (21%) lower.
