Compared to other places in this area, Fairmont is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fairmont is valued at $110,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $20,700 (15%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $106,600 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $14,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

Fairmont's median rent is $754 per month. It is about $26 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $204 (21%) lower.
