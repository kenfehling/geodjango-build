Compared to its surrounding areas, Fairmont is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairmont is $110,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $20,700 (15%) lower. It is roughly $106,600 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $14,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

Fairmont's median monthly rent is $754. It is about $26 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $204 (21%) lower.
