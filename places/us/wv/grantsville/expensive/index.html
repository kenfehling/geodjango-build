Compared to the surrounding areas, Grantsville is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Charleston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Grantsville costs $66,200. It is approximately $4,800 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $151,300 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is approximately $79,600 (54%) lower.

Rents

Grantsville's median rent is $529 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $37 (7%) higher.It is roughly $533 (50%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is roughly $206 (28%) lower.
