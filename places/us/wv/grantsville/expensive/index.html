In comparison to other places in this area, Grantsville is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The median home in Grantsville costs $66,200. Compared to nearby areas it is about $4,800 (7%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $151,300 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is about $79,600 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Grantsville is $529 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $37 (7%) higher. It is approximately $533 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is approximately $206 (28%) lower.
