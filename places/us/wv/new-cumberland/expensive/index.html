Home prices

The median home in New Cumberland costs $81,000. This is roughly $12,220 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $136,500 (62%) lower, and is roughly $44,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in New Cumberland is $446 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $167 (27%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $616 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $512 (53%) lower.
