Home prices

The median home in Wardensville is valued at $154,200. It is roughly $40,220 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $63,300 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $116,200 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Frederick.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wardensville is $710. This is roughly $185 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $352 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Frederick is roughly $686 (49%) lower.
