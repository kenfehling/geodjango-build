Compared to nearby areas, Bradley is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Charleston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bradley is valued at $115,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $16,720 (16%) higher. It is roughly $102,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $30,400 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

The median rent in Bradley is $689 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $97 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $373 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is about $46 (6%) lower.
