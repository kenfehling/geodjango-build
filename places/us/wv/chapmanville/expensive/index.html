Home prices

The median home in Chapmanville is valued at $110,300. This is roughly $16,960 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $107,200 (49%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $35,500 (24%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Chapmanville's median monthly rent is $613. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $135 (18%) lower.It is about $449 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is roughly $122 (16%) lower.
