Home prices

The median home in Pennsboro is valued at $56,500. This is about $11,360 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $161,000 (74%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is approximately $89,300 (61%) lower.

Rents

Pennsboro's median rent is $667 per month. It is roughly $160 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $395 (37%) lower, and it is about $68 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
