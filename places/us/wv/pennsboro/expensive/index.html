Compared to the surrounding areas, Pennsboro is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Charleston, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pennsboro is valued at $56,500. It is about $11,360 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $161,000 (74%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $89,300 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Pennsboro's median rent is $667 per month. It is roughly $160 (31%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $395 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $68 (9%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
