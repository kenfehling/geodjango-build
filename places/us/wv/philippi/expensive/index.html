Home prices

Rents

Philippi's median monthly rent is $552. This is approximately $63 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $510 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $406 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
