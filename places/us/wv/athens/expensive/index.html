Home prices

The price of the median home in Athens is $114,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $32,300 (39%) higher. It is about $103,400 (47%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke is roughly $21,000 (15%) lower.

Rents

Athens's median monthly rent is $663. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. It is about $399 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $151 (18%) lower.
