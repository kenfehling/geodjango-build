Home prices

The price of the median home in Athens is $114,100. This is about $32,300 (39%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $103,400 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $21,000 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

The median rent in Athens is $663 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar.It is roughly $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $151 (18%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.
