Home prices

West Hamlin's median home cost is $113,600. It is roughly $25,800 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $103,900 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $32,200 (22%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

West Hamlin's median monthly rent is $409. It is roughly $362 (46%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $653 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is about $326 (44%) lower.
