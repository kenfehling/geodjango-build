Home prices

The median home in Mallory is valued at $100,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $29,060 (40%) higher. It is about $117,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is roughly $45,800 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mallory is $585. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $69 (10%) lower. It is about $477 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $150 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
