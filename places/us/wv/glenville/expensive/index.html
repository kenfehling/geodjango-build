Home prices

The median home price in Glenville is $75,600. It is about $8,860 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $141,900 (65%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $70,200 (48%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Glenville's median rent is $613 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $69 (12%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $449 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $122 (16%) lower.
