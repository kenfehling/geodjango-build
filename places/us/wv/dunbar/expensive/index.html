Home prices

The median home cost in Dunbar is $98,400. It is roughly $24,040 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $119,100 (54%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $47,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

Dunbar's median monthly rent is $856. This is approximately $100 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $206 (19%) lower, and is approximately $121 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Charleston.
