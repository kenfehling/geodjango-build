Home prices

The median home in Cassville is valued at $184,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $35,840 (24%) higher. It is approximately $33,300 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $59,200 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cassville is $624. Compared to other places in this area this is about $173 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $438 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $334 (34%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
