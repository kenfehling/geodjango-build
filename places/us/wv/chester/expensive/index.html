Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chester is $91,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $29,700 (48%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $126,300 (58%) lower, and is approximately $21,600 (31%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chester is $605. It is roughly $153 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $457 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $114 (15%) lower.
