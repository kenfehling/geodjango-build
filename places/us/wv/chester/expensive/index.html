Home prices

The median home in Chester costs $91,200. This is roughly $29,700 (48%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $126,300 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $21,600 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Chester the median rent is $605 a month. This is roughly $153 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $457 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $114 (15%) lower.
