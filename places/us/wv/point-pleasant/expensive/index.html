Compared to nearby areas, Point Pleasant is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbus.

Home prices

The median home price in Point Pleasant is $79,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $12,340 (13%) lower.It is approximately $137,800 (63%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $71,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

Point Pleasant's median monthly rent is $557. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $91 (14%) lower.It is about $505 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $404 (42%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.
