Home prices

The median home cost in Point Pleasant is $79,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $12,340 (13%) lower.It is about $137,800 (63%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $71,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median rent in Point Pleasant is $557 a month. It is roughly $91 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $505 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is roughly $404 (42%) lower.
