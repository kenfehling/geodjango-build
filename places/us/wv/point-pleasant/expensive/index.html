Home prices

The median home value in Point Pleasant is $79,700. It is roughly $12,340 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $137,800 (63%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $71,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Point Pleasant is $557. This is roughly $91 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $505 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $404 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
