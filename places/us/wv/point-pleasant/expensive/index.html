Compared to other places in this area, Point Pleasant is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbus, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Point Pleasant is $79,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $12,340 (13%) lower. It is about $137,800 (63%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $71,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

Point Pleasant's median monthly rent is $557. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $91 (14%) lower. It is roughly $505 (47%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $404 (42%) lower.
