Home prices

Point Pleasant's median home price is $79,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $12,340 (13%) lower. It is about $137,800 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $71,900 (47%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

In Point Pleasant the median monthly rent is $557. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $91 (14%) lower. It is approximately $505 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbus is roughly $404 (42%) lower.
