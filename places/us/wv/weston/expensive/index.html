Home prices

The median home price in Weston is $77,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $23,020 (22%) lower.It is approximately $139,900 (64%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $132,700 (63%) lower compared to the city of Morgantown.

Rents

Weston's median rent is $664 a month. This is roughly $18 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $398 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Morgantown it is approximately $187 (21%) lower.
