Home prices

St. Marys's median home cost is $113,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,880 (17%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $104,400 (48%) lower, and is about $32,700 (22%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In St. Marys the median rent is $488 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $24 (5%) higher. It is about $574 (54%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $247 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
