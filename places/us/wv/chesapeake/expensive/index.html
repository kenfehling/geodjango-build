Compared to the surrounding areas, Chesapeake is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The median home in Chesapeake costs $62,500. It is about $20,400 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $155,000 (71%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $83,300 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Chesapeake's median monthly rent is $711. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $49 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $351 (33%) lower, and is roughly $24 (3%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
