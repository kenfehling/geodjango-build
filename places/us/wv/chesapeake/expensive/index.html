In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chesapeake is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Chesapeake is $62,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $20,400 (24%) lower. It is roughly $155,000 (71%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is approximately $83,300 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chesapeake is $711. This is about $49 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $351 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is approximately $24 (3%) lower.
