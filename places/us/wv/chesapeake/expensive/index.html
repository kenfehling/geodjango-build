In comparison to its nearby areas, Chesapeake is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chesapeake is $62,500. This is about $20,400 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $155,000 (71%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $83,300 (57%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Chesapeake's median monthly rent is $711. This is about $49 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $351 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is roughly $24 (3%) lower.
