Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chesapeake is $62,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $20,400 (24%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $155,000 (71%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is approximately $83,300 (57%) lower.

Rents

Chesapeake's median monthly rent is $711. It is roughly $49 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is about $24 (3%) lower.
