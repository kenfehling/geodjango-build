Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wheeling is $110,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $22,800 (26%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $107,400 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $14,900 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wheeling is $658. It is approximately $145 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $404 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $300 (31%) lower.
