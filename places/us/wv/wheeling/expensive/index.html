Home prices

The median home in Wheeling is valued at $110,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $22,800 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $107,400 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $14,900 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Wheeling the median monthly rent is $658. It is about $145 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $404 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $300 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
