Compared to the surrounding areas, Wheeling is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Wheeling costs $110,100. It is roughly $22,800 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $107,400 (49%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $14,900 (11%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Wheeling is $658 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $145 (18%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $404 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $300 (31%) lower.
