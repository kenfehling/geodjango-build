Home prices

The median home cost in Wheeling is $110,100. This is roughly $22,800 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $107,400 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $14,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

Wheeling's median rent is $658 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $145 (18%) lower. It is approximately $404 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $300 (31%) lower.
