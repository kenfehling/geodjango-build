Compared to its surrounding areas, Newburg is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Newburg is $61,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $27,500 (30%) lower. It is approximately $155,800 (71%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $63,300 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newburg is $625. This is about $88 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $333 (34%) lower.
