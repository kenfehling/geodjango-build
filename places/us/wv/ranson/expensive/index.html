Home prices

Ranson's median home value is $183,500. This is roughly $69,580 (27%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $34,000 (15%) lower, and is roughly $418,000 (69%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Ranson the median rent is $1,203 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $154 (14%) higher. It is about $141 (13%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $338 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
