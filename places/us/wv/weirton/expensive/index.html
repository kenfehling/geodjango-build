Rents

The median monthly rent in Weirton is $704. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $34 (5%) higher. It is approximately $358 (33%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $254 (26%) lower.
