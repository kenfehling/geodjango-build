Home prices

The median home in Glen Dale costs $151,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $77,980 (105%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $65,800 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $26,700 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Glen Dale is $681 per month. It is about $62 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $381 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $277 (28%) lower.
