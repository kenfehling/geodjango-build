Rents

The median monthly rent in Glen Dale is $681. It is approximately $62 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $381 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $277 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
