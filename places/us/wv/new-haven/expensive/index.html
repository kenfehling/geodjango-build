Home prices

The median home in New Haven costs $77,600. This is roughly $2,780 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $139,900 (64%) lower, and is about $74,000 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.

Rents

New Haven's median monthly rent is $625. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $25 (4%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus is approximately $336 (34%) lower.
