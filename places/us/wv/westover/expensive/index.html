Compared to nearby areas, Westover is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

Westover's median home cost is $145,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $22,080 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $72,100 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $20,400 (16%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Westover's median rent is $750 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $312 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $208 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
