Compared to the surrounding areas, Westover is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Westover costs $145,400. This is roughly $22,080 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $72,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $20,400 (16%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Westover's median monthly rent is $750. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is about $312 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $208 (21%) lower.
