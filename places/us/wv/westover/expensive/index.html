In comparison to the surrounding areas, Westover is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

Westover's median home value is $145,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $22,080 (13%) lower. It is approximately $72,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $20,400 (16%) higher.

Rents

Westover's median rent is $750 per month. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is about $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $208 (21%) lower.
