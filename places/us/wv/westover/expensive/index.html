Compared to the surrounding areas, Westover is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Westover is $145,400. This is roughly $22,080 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $72,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $20,400 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Westover is $750 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar.It is roughly $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $208 (21%) lower.
