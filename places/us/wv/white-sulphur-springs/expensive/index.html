Rents

The median rent in White Sulphur Springs is $637 per month. This is approximately $68 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $425 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $177 (21%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.
