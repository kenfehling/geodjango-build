Home prices

The median home in White Sulphur Springs is valued at $125,700. It is approximately $7,620 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $91,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is roughly $9,400 (6%) lower.

Rents

In White Sulphur Springs the median monthly rent is $637. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $68 (11%) higher. It is approximately $425 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $177 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
