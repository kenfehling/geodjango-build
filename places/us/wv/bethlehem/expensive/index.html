Home prices

The median home in Bethlehem costs $158,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $87,420 (123%) higher. It is approximately $59,400 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $33,100 (26%) higher.

Rents

Bethlehem's median monthly rent is $1,083. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $384 (54%) higher. It is approximately $21 (1%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $125 (13%) higher.
