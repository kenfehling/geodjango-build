Home prices

Rents

West Milford's median monthly rent is $806. This is approximately $9 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $256 (24%) lower, and is approximately $152 (15%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
