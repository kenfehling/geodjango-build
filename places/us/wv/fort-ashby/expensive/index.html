Home prices

The value of the median home in Fort Ashby is $151,200. It is roughly $41,620 (37%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $66,300 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $119,200 (44%) lower compared to the city of Frederick.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Ashby is $728. This is approximately $31 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $334 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $668 (47%) lower compared to the city of Frederick.
