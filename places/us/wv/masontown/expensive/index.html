Home prices

Rents

In Masontown the median rent is $675 per month. This is roughly $70 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $283 (29%) lower.
