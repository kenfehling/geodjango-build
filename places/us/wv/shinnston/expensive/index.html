Home prices

The median home in Shinnston costs $109,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $2,460 (2%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $108,300 (49%) lower, and is about $15,800 (12%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Shinnston the median rent is $810 a month. This is roughly $54 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $252 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $148 (15%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
