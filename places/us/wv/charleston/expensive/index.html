Home prices

The cost of the median home in Charleston is $145,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $46,333 (46%) higher. It is roughly $71,700 (32%) lower compared to the entire country.

Rents

The median rent in Charleston is $735 per month. This is roughly $106 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $327 (30%) lower.
