Home prices

The median home in Vienna is valued at $134,900. It is roughly $4,560 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $82,600 (37%) lower, and is about $10,900 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Vienna's median rent is $657 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $79 (10%) lower. It is roughly $405 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $78 (10%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
