Home prices

The median home cost in Smithers is $113,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $26,040 (29%) higher. It is approximately $104,100 (47%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $32,400 (22%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

The median rent in Smithers is $577 a month. This is approximately $100 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $485 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is approximately $158 (21%) lower.
