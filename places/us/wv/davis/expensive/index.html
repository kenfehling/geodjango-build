Compared to the surrounding areas, Davis is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Harrisonburg.

Home prices

Davis's median home cost is $113,800. This is about $32,700 (40%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $103,700 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Harrisonburg is approximately $89,800 (44%) lower.

Rents

Davis's median rent is $675 per month. It is about $100 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $387 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $208 (23%) lower compared to the city of Harrisonburg.
