Home prices

The median home price in Davis is $113,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $32,700 (40%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $103,700 (47%) lower, and is approximately $89,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisonburg.

Rents

Davis's median rent is $675 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $100 (17%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $387 (36%) lower, and is roughly $208 (23%) lower compared to the city of Harrisonburg.
