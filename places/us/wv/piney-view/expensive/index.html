Home prices

The median home in Piney View is valued at $112,500. It is about $13,240 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $105,000 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $22,600 (16%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

The median rent in Piney View is $441 per month. This is about $201 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $621 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $373 (45%) lower.
