Home prices

The median home in Inwood costs $168,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $58,640 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $49,100 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $433,100 (72%) lower.

Rents

In Inwood the median monthly rent is $1,438. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $368 (34%) higher. It is approximately $376 (35%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $103 (6%) lower.
