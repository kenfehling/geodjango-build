Home prices

The median home in Hartford City is valued at $74,300. This is roughly $1,180 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $143,200 (65%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbus it is roughly $77,300 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Hartford City the median monthly rent is $648. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $52 (8%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $414 (38%) lower, and is roughly $313 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbus.
