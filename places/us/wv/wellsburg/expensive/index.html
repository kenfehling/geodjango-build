Home prices

Wellsburg's median home cost is $87,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $7,620 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $130,300 (59%) lower, and is approximately $37,800 (30%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Wellsburg the median monthly rent is $570. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $127 (18%) lower. It is about $492 (46%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $388 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
