Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wellsburg is $87,200. This is roughly $7,620 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $130,300 (59%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $37,800 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Wellsburg the median rent is $570 per month. It is approximately $127 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $492 (46%) lower, and is approximately $388 (40%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
