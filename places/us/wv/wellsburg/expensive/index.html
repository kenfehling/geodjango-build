Home prices

The median home price in Wellsburg is $87,200. It is approximately $7,620 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $130,300 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $37,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

Wellsburg's median rent is $570 a month. This is about $127 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $492 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $388 (40%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
