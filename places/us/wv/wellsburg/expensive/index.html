Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wellsburg is $87,200. It is approximately $7,620 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $130,300 (59%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $37,800 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Wellsburg the median rent is $570 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $127 (18%) lower.It is approximately $492 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $388 (40%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
