Compared to its nearby areas, Morgantown is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Morgantown is valued at $210,300. It is roughly $32,860 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $7,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $85,300 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Morgantown's median rent is $851 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $68 (8%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $211 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $107 (11%) lower.
