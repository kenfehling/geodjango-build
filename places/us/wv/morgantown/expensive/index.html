Compared to other places in this area, Morgantown is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Morgantown is $210,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $32,860 (18%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $7,200 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $85,300 (68%) higher.

Rents

Morgantown's median monthly rent is $851. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $68 (8%) higher.It is approximately $211 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $107 (11%) lower.
