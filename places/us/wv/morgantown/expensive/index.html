In comparison to nearby areas, Morgantown is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Morgantown is $210,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $32,860 (18%) higher. It is roughly $7,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $85,300 (68%) higher.

Rents

In Morgantown the median rent is $851 per month. This is about $68 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $211 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $107 (11%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
