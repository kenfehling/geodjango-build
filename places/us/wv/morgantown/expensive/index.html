Compared to its surrounding areas, Morgantown is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Morgantown's median home value is $210,300. It is approximately $32,860 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $7,200 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $85,300 (68%) higher.

Rents

In Morgantown the median monthly rent is $851. It is about $68 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $211 (19%) lower, and is approximately $107 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
