In comparison to its nearby areas, Morgantown is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Morgantown is valued at $210,300. It is approximately $32,860 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $7,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $85,300 (68%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Morgantown is $851 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $68 (8%) higher. It is about $211 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $107 (11%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
