Rents

The median rent in Mabscott is $688 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $110 (13%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $374 (35%) lower, and is roughly $126 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
