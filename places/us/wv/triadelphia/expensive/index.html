Home prices

The median home in Triadelphia costs $59,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $35,640 (37%) lower. It is approximately $158,300 (72%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $65,800 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Triadelphia the median monthly rent is $941. This is about $198 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $121 (11%) lower, and is approximately $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
