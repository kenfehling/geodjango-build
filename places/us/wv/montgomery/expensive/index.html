Home prices

Montgomery's median home cost is $77,200. It is approximately $17,400 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $68,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montgomery is $474. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $223 (32%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $588 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $261 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
