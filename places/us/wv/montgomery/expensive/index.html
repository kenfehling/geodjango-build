Compared to nearby areas, Montgomery is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home cost is $77,200. This is approximately $17,400 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $140,300 (64%) lower, and it is about $68,600 (47%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $474. It is roughly $223 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $588 (55%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $261 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
