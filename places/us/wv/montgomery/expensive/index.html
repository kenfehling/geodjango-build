In comparison to other places in this area, Montgomery is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

Montgomery's median home value is $77,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $17,400 (18%) lower. It is about $140,300 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is about $68,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

Montgomery's median rent is $474 a month. It is about $223 (32%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $588 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $261 (35%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
