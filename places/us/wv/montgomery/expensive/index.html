In comparison to its surrounding areas, Montgomery is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery costs $77,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $17,400 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $68,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montgomery is $474. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $223 (32%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $588 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $261 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
