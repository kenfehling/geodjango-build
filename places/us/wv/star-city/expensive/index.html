Compared to its nearby areas, Star City is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Star City costs $164,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $17,520 (9%) lower. It is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $39,400 (31%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Star City is $809. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $36 (4%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $253 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $149 (15%) lower.
