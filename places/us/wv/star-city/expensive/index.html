Home prices

The median home in Star City is valued at $164,400. This is about $17,520 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $53,100 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $39,400 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Star City the median rent is $809 per month. This is about $36 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $253 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $149 (15%) lower.
