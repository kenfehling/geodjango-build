Compared to the surrounding areas, Star City is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

Star City's median home price is $164,400. This is approximately $17,520 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $53,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $39,400 (31%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Star City's median rent is $809 per month. This is roughly $36 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $253 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $149 (15%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
