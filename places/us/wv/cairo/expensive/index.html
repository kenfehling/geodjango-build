Home prices

The median home in Cairo is valued at $56,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $29,380 (34%) lower. It is about $161,400 (74%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $89,700 (61%) lower.

Rents

Cairo's median rent is $614 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $83 (15%) higher. It is roughly $448 (42%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is roughly $121 (16%) lower.
