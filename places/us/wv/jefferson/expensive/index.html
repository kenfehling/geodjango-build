Compared to its surrounding areas, Jefferson is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The median home in Jefferson is valued at $131,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $15,800 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $85,900 (39%) lower, and is about $14,200 (9%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Jefferson's median rent is $771 per month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $36 (4%) higher.
