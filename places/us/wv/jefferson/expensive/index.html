Compared to the surrounding areas, Jefferson is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Charleston, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Jefferson is $131,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $15,800 (13%) higher. It is approximately $85,900 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is approximately $14,200 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jefferson is $771. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is roughly $291 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is roughly $36 (4%) higher.
