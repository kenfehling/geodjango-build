Home prices

Fayetteville's median home price is $130,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $43,400 (49%) higher.It is about $87,100 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is approximately $15,400 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fayetteville is $810 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $147 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $252 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $75 (10%) higher.
