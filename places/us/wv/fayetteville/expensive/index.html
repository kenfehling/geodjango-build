Home prices

Rents

Fayetteville's median monthly rent is $810. This is roughly $147 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $252 (23%) lower, and is roughly $75 (10%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
