In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fayetteville is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Charleston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fayetteville is valued at $130,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $43,400 (49%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $87,100 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is roughly $15,400 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fayetteville is $810 a month. It is roughly $147 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $252 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is approximately $75 (10%) higher.
