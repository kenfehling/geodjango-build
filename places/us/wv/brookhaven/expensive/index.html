Rents

Brookhaven's median rent is $717 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $66 (8%) lower.It is roughly $345 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $241 (25%) lower.
