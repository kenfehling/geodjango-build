In comparison to nearby areas, Brookhaven is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brookhaven is $207,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $40,960 (24%) higher. It is about $9,800 (4%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $82,700 (66%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookhaven is $717. This is roughly $66 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $345 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $241 (25%) lower.
