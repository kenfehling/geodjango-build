Compared to its surrounding areas, Brookhaven is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brookhaven costs $207,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $40,960 (24%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $9,800 (4%) lower, and it is roughly $82,700 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Brookhaven is $717 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $66 (8%) lower. It is approximately $345 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $241 (25%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
