Compared to its surrounding areas, Washington is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The median home in Washington is valued at $167,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $46,840 (38%) higher. It is approximately $50,400 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is about $21,300 (14%) higher.

Rents

Washington's median rent is $987 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $269 (37%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $75 (7%) lower, and it is roughly $252 (34%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
