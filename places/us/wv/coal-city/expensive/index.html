Compared to other places in this area, Coal City is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Roanoke, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Coal City is valued at $78,400. This is about $13,460 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $139,100 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is about $56,700 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Coal City the median monthly rent is $383. It is roughly $397 (50%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $679 (63%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is about $431 (52%) lower.
