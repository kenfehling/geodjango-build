Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Richwood is $549 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $79 (16%) higher. It is roughly $513 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $265 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
