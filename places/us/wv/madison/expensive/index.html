Compared to its surrounding areas, Madison is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $120,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $32,680 (37%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $97,200 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston is about $25,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Madison is $741. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $22 (3%) higher. It is approximately $321 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is is very similar.
