In comparison to the surrounding areas, MacArthur is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Roanoke however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in MacArthur costs $117,800. This is roughly $24,660 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $99,700 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $17,300 (12%) lower.

Rents

MacArthur's median rent is $851 a month. This is about $138 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $211 (19%) lower, and is approximately $37 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
