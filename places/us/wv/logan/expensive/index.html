Home prices

The median home in Logan is valued at $89,600. It is about $9,080 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $127,900 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is roughly $56,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

Logan's median monthly rent is $652. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $115 (15%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $410 (38%) lower, and it is about $83 (11%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
