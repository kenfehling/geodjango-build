Home prices

The median home in Logan costs $89,600. It is about $9,080 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $127,900 (58%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $56,200 (38%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Logan's median rent is $652 a month. This is roughly $115 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $410 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $83 (11%) lower.
