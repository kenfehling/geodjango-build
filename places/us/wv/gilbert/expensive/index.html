Compared to nearby areas, Gilbert is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gilbert is valued at $83,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $9,480 (12%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $133,700 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is roughly $62,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

Gilbert's median rent is $533 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $163 (23%) lower. It is about $529 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $202 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
