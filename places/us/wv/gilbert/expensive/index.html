In comparison to other places in this area, Gilbert is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Gilbert's median home value is $83,800. It is about $9,480 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $133,700 (61%) lower, and it is approximately $62,000 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

In Gilbert the median monthly rent is $533. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $163 (23%) lower. It is roughly $529 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is about $202 (27%) lower.
