In comparison to its surrounding areas, Princeton is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Roanoke.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Princeton is $93,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21,700 (30%) higher. It is roughly $123,800 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is about $41,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Princeton is $711. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $56 (8%) higher. It is approximately $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $103 (12%) lower.
