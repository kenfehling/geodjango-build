Compared to other places in this area, Princeton is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Roanoke.

Home prices

The median home in Princeton is valued at $93,700. It is approximately $21,700 (30%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $123,800 (56%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $41,400 (30%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Princeton is $711. It is roughly $56 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $103 (12%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.
