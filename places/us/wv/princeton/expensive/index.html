Compared to the surrounding areas, Princeton is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Roanoke.

Home prices

The median home in Princeton is valued at $93,700. It is about $21,700 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $123,800 (56%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $41,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Princeton the median monthly rent is $711. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $56 (8%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $351 (33%) lower, and it is about $103 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
