Home prices

The median home in Williamstown is valued at $141,000. This is about $3,840 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $76,500 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is about $4,800 (3%) lower.

Rents

Williamstown's median monthly rent is $747. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $48 (6%) lower. It is approximately $315 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $12 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Charleston.
