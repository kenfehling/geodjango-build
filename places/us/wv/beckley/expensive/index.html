Compared to its surrounding areas, Beckley is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Roanoke.

Home prices

The median home in Beckley costs $110,000. It is about $8,300 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $107,500 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke is approximately $25,100 (18%) lower.

Rents

Beckley's median monthly rent is $685. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $68 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $377 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke is roughly $129 (15%) lower.
