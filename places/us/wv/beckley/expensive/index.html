Home prices

The price of the median home in Beckley is $110,000. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $8,300 (8%) higher. It is approximately $107,500 (49%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke is about $25,100 (18%) lower.

Rents

In Beckley the median rent is $685 a month. This is roughly $68 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Roanoke is approximately $129 (15%) lower.
