Home prices

The median home in McMechen is valued at $69,400. This is roughly $20,780 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $148,100 (68%) lower, and is roughly $55,600 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

McMechen's median monthly rent is $670. This is roughly $49 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $392 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $288 (30%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
