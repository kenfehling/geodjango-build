In comparison to its nearby areas, McMechen is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in McMechen is $69,400. This is roughly $20,780 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $148,100 (68%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $55,600 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In McMechen the median rent is $670 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $49 (7%) higher. It is about $392 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $288 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
