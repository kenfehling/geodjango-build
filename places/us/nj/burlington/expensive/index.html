Home prices

The price of the median home in Burlington is $161,400. It is roughly $13,120 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $56,100 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $444,600 (73%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Burlington's median monthly rent is $1,035. Compared to other places in this area this is about $35 (3%) lower.It is approximately $27 (2%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $408 (28%) lower.
