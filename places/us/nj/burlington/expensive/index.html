Compared to other places in this area, Burlington is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Burlington is $161,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $13,120 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $56,100 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $444,600 (73%) lower.

Rents

In Burlington the median rent is $1,035 a month. It is approximately $35 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $27 (2%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $408 (28%) lower compared to the city of New York.
