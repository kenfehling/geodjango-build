Home prices

The median home in Dayton costs $372,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $15,180 (4%) higher. It is about $154,600 (71%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $233,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dayton is $1,725. This is about $54 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $663 (62%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $282 (19%) higher compared to the city of New York.
