Similar to other places in this area, it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Dayton costs $372,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $15,180 (4%) higher. It is roughly $154,600 (71%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $233,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $1,725 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $54 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $663 (62%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $282 (19%) higher.
