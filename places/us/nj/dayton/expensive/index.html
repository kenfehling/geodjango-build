Home prices

The median home price in Dayton is $372,100. This is about $15,180 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $154,600 (71%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $233,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $1,725. This is approximately $54 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $663 (62%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $282 (19%) higher.
