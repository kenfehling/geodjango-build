Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brielle is $640,000. This is approximately $40,660 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $422,500 (194%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $34,000 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Brielle the median rent is $2,205 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $651 (41%) higher. It is approximately $1,143 (107%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $762 (52%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
