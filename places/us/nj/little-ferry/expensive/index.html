Home prices

The median home in Little Ferry is valued at $329,300. It is approximately $94,440 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $111,800 (51%) higher, and is approximately $276,700 (45%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Little Ferry's median rent is $1,349 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $43 (3%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $287 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $94 (6%) lower.
