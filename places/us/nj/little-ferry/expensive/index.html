Rents

The median monthly rent in Little Ferry is $1,349. It is roughly $43 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $287 (27%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $94 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
