Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pine Lake Park is $250,200. It is roughly $87,380 (53%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $32,700 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $355,800 (58%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents
