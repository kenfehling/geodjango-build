Compared to other places in this area, Rahway is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Rahway is valued at $272,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24,780 (8%) lower. It is roughly $55,200 (25%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $333,300 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rahway is $1,310. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $229 (14%) lower. It is about $248 (23%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $133 (9%) lower compared to the city of New York.
