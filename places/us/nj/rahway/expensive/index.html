Home prices

The median home in Rahway costs $272,700. It is roughly $24,780 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $55,200 (25%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $333,300 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rahway is $1,310 per month. This is roughly $229 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $248 (23%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $133 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
