In comparison to its surrounding areas, Chatham is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Chatham is $746,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $104,560 (12%) lower.It is about $528,800 (243%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $140,300 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chatham is $1,845. This is approximately $287 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $783 (73%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $402 (27%) higher.
