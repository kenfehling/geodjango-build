Home prices

Glassboro's median home value is $202,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $28,760 (12%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $14,700 (6%) lower, and is roughly $403,200 (66%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Glassboro the median monthly rent is $1,242. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $76 (6%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $180 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $201 (13%) lower.
