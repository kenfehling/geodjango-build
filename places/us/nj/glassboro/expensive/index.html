In comparison to other places in this area, Glassboro is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Glassboro is valued at $202,800. This is roughly $28,760 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $14,700 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is about $403,200 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Glassboro the median rent is $1,242 a month. This is roughly $76 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $180 (16%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $201 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
