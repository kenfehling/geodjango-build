Compared to its surrounding areas, Hightstown is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hightstown is $282,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $59,820 (17%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $65,200 (29%) higher, and it is roughly $323,300 (53%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Hightstown is $1,458 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $202 (12%) lower.It is about $396 (37%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $15 (1%) higher compared to the city of New York.
