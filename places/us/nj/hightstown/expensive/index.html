Compared to its surrounding areas, Hightstown is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Hightstown is $282,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $59,820 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $65,200 (29%) higher, and is approximately $323,300 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Hightstown's median monthly rent is $1,458. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $202 (12%) lower. It is about $396 (37%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $15 (1%) higher.
