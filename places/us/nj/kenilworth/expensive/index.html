Home prices

The price of the median home in Kenilworth is $345,500. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $106,380 (23%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $128,000 (58%) higher, and it is about $260,500 (42%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Kenilworth is $1,521 per month. It is approximately $50 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $459 (43%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $78 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
