Compared to its nearby areas, Madison is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of New York, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Madison's median home value is $724,700. This is approximately $52,440 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $507,200 (233%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $118,700 (19%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Madison's median rent is $1,807 a month. It is roughly $119 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $745 (70%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $364 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
