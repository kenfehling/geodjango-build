In comparison to its surrounding areas, Madison is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Madison is $724,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $52,440 (7%) higher. It is approximately $507,200 (233%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $118,700 (19%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Madison is $1,807. It is about $119 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $745 (70%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $364 (25%) higher compared to the city of New York.
