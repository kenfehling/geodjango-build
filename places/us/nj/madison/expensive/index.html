Compared to nearby areas, Madison is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of New York, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Madison costs $724,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $52,440 (7%) higher. It is approximately $507,200 (233%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $118,700 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Madison the median monthly rent is $1,807. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $119 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $745 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $364 (25%) higher.
