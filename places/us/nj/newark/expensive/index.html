Compared to nearby areas, Newark is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Newark is $245,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $54,480 (18%) lower. It is roughly $27,700 (12%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $360,800 (59%) lower.

Rents

Newark's median rent is $1,085 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $208 (16%) lower. It is approximately $23 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $358 (24%) lower.
