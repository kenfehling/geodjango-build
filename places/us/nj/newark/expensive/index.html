Home prices

The median home in Newark costs $245,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $54,480 (18%) lower.It is about $27,700 (12%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $360,800 (59%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Newark the median monthly rent is $1,085. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $208 (16%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $23 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $358 (24%) lower.
