Compared to nearby areas, Newark is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newark is $245,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $54,480 (18%) lower. It is approximately $27,700 (12%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $360,800 (59%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Newark the median monthly rent is $1,085. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $208 (16%) lower. It is roughly $23 (2%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $358 (24%) lower.
