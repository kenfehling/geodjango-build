Home prices

The median home in Hamilton Square is valued at $302,900. It is about $4,900 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $85,400 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $303,100 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Hamilton Square the median monthly rent is $1,153. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $107 (8%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $91 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $290 (20%) lower.
