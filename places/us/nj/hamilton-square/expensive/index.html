Home prices

The median home in Hamilton Square is valued at $302,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $4,900 (1%) higher.It is about $85,400 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $303,100 (50%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hamilton Square the median monthly rent is $1,153. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $107 (8%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $91 (8%) higher, and is about $290 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
