Home prices

The median home price in Hamilton Square is $302,900. This is roughly $4,900 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $85,400 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $303,100 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hamilton Square is $1,153 per month. This is approximately $107 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $91 (8%) higher, and is approximately $290 (20%) lower compared to the city of New York.
