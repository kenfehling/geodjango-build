Compared to other places in this area, Frenchtown is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Frenchtown is $302,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $16,880 (5%) higher. It is approximately $84,600 (38%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $303,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Frenchtown the median monthly rent is $1,335. It is about $52 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $273 (25%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $108 (7%) lower compared to the city of New York.
