Compared to the surrounding areas, Frenchtown is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Frenchtown's median home price is $302,100. It is about $16,880 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $84,600 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $303,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Frenchtown the median rent is $1,335 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $52 (4%) higher. It is roughly $273 (25%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $108 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
