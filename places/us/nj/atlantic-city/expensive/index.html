In comparison to its surrounding areas, Atlantic City is a lot less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Atlantic City is valued at $156,700. It is roughly $130,200 (45%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $60,800 (27%) lower, and is about $449,300 (74%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Atlantic City the median monthly rent is $904. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $373 (29%) lower.It is approximately $158 (14%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $539 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
