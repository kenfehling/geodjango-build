In comparison to the surrounding areas, Atlantic City is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Atlantic City is valued at $156,700. This is approximately $130,200 (45%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $60,800 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $449,300 (74%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Atlantic City the median rent is $904 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $373 (29%) lower.It is approximately $158 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $539 (37%) lower.
