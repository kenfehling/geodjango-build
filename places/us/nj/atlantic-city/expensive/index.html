In comparison to nearby areas, Atlantic City is significantly less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Atlantic City is valued at $156,700. It is about $130,200 (45%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $60,800 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $449,300 (74%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Atlantic City is $904 per month. This is about $373 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $158 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $539 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
