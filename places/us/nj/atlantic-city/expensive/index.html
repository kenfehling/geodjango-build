In comparison to other places in this area, Atlantic City is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Atlantic City's median home price is $156,700. It is roughly $130,200 (45%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $60,800 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $449,300 (74%) lower.

Rents

In Atlantic City the median rent is $904 per month. This is approximately $373 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $158 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $539 (37%) lower.
