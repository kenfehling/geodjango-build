Home prices

The median home in Andover is valued at $282,100. It is approximately $18,340 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $64,600 (29%) higher, and is about $323,900 (53%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Andover's median rent is $1,115 per month. This is approximately $303 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $53 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $328 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
