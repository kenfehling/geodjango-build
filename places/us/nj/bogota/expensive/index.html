In comparison to other places in this area, Bogota is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Bogota is valued at $336,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $45,980 (12%) lower.It is approximately $118,500 (54%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $270,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

Bogota's median rent is $1,394 a month. It is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $332 (31%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $49 (3%) lower.
