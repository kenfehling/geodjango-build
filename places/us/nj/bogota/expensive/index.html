Home prices

The median home value in Bogota is $336,000. It is roughly $45,980 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $118,500 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $270,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bogota is $1,394 a month. It is approximately $15 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $332 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $49 (3%) lower.
