Home prices

Park Ridge's median home price is $588,900. It is roughly $257,820 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $371,400 (170%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $17,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Park Ridge is $1,474 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $237 (13%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $412 (38%) higher, and is roughly $31 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
