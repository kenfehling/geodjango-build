Home prices

The value of the median home in Cresskill is $677,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $93,620 (16%) higher. It is approximately $460,000 (211%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $71,500 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cresskill is $2,686 per month. It is roughly $694 (34%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $1,624 (152%) higher, and is roughly $1,243 (86%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
