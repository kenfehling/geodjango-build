Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wildwood is $237,300. It is about $94,360 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $19,800 (9%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $74,300 (45%) higher.

Rents

Wildwood's median rent is $1,096 per month. It is roughly $27 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $34 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $54 (5%) higher.
