Compared to other places in this area, Wildwood is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

Wildwood's median home value is $237,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $94,360 (28%) lower. It is approximately $19,800 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $74,300 (45%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Wildwood the median rent is $1,096 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $27 (2%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $34 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $54 (5%) higher.
