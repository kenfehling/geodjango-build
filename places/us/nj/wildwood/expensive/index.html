Home prices

Rents

Wildwood's median monthly rent is $1,096. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $27 (2%) higher.It is approximately $34 (3%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $54 (5%) higher.
