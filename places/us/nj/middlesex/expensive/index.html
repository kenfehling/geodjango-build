Home prices

The median home price in Middlesex is $307,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $18,740 (5%) lower.It is roughly $89,500 (41%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $299,000 (49%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middlesex is $1,416. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $172 (10%) lower.It is approximately $354 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $27 (1%) lower compared to the city of New York.
