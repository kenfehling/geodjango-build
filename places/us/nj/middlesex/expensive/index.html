Home prices

The cost of the median home in Middlesex is $307,000. This is about $18,740 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $89,500 (41%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $299,000 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middlesex is $1,416. Compared to nearby areas it is about $172 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $354 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $27 (1%) lower.
