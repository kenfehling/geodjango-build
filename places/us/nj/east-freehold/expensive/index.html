Home prices

The median home cost in East Freehold is $476,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $83,320 (21%) higher. It is approximately $259,200 (119%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $129,300 (21%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

East Freehold's median rent is $1,681 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $74 (4%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $619 (58%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $238 (16%) higher.
