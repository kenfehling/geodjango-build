Compared to the surrounding areas, Ashland is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of New Yorkhowever, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $233,000. This is about $46,480 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $15,500 (7%) higher, and is approximately $373,000 (61%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $1,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $585 (52%) higher. It is roughly $638 (60%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $257 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.
