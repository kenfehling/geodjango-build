Compared to nearby areas, Ashland is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ashland is $233,000. This is about $46,480 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $15,500 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $373,000 (61%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $1,700. This is about $585 (52%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $638 (60%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $257 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
