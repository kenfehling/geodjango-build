Compared to the surrounding areas, Ashland is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of New Yorkhowever, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $233,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $46,480 (16%) lower. It is approximately $15,500 (7%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $373,000 (61%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Ashland's median rent is $1,700 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $585 (52%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $638 (60%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $257 (17%) higher.
