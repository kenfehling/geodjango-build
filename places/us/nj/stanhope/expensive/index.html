Home prices

The cost of the median home in Stanhope is $247,400. It is approximately $48,280 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $29,900 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $358,600 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stanhope is $1,657. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $212 (14%) higher.It is roughly $595 (56%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $214 (14%) higher compared to the city of New York.
