Compared to other places in this area, Linden is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Linden is valued at $281,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $18,440 (7%) higher.It is approximately $64,300 (29%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $324,200 (53%) lower.

Rents

Linden's median monthly rent is $1,313. This is about $36 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $251 (23%) higher, and is roughly $130 (9%) lower compared to the city of New York.
