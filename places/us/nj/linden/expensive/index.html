In comparison to other places in this area, Linden is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Linden costs $281,800. This is about $18,440 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $64,300 (29%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $324,200 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Linden the median rent is $1,313 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $36 (2%) lower.It is about $251 (23%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $130 (9%) lower.
