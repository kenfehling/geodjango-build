In comparison to its surrounding areas, Linden is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Linden is $281,800. This is about $18,440 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $64,300 (29%) higher, and it is roughly $324,200 (53%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Linden the median monthly rent is $1,313. It is about $36 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $251 (23%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $130 (9%) lower.
