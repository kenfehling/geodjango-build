Compared to nearby areas, Linden is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Linden is $281,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $18,440 (7%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $64,300 (29%) higher, and is roughly $324,200 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Linden's median rent is $1,313 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $36 (2%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $251 (23%) higher, and it is roughly $130 (9%) lower compared to the city of New York.
