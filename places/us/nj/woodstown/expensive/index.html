Home prices

The median home value in Woodstown is $251,400. It is approximately $32,960 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $33,900 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $88,400 (54%) higher.

Rents

In Woodstown the median rent is $984 per month. It is approximately $200 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $58 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
