Compared to other places in this area, Woodstown is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Woodstown costs $251,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $32,960 (15%) higher. It is approximately $33,900 (15%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $88,400 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Woodstown the median rent is $984 a month. It is roughly $200 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $78 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $58 (5%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
