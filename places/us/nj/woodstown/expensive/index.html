In comparison to its surrounding areas, Woodstown is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Woodstown costs $251,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $32,960 (15%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $33,900 (15%) higher, and it is about $88,400 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Woodstown's median rent is $984 a month. It is about $200 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $78 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $58 (5%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
