Home prices

The price of the median home in Groveville is $315,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $23,680 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $98,100 (45%) higher, and it is roughly $290,400 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Groveville is $925 per month. It is about $365 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $137 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $518 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
