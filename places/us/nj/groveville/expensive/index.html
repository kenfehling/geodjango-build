In comparison to the surrounding areas, Groveville is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Groveville is $315,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $23,680 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $98,100 (45%) higher, and is roughly $290,400 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Groveville's median monthly rent is $925. This is about $365 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $137 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $518 (35%) lower.
