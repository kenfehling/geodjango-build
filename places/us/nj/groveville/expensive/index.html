Home prices

The value of the median home in Groveville is $315,600. It is approximately $23,680 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $98,100 (45%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $290,400 (47%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Groveville the median rent is $925 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $365 (28%) lower. It is roughly $137 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $518 (35%) lower.
