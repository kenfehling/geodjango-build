Home prices

The value of the median home in Hampton is $260,500. It is about $15,680 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $43,000 (19%) higher, and it is about $345,500 (57%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hampton is $525. It is roughly $974 (64%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $537 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $918 (63%) lower.
