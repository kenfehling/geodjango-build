Home prices

The median home price in Hampton is $260,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $15,680 (6%) higher. It is approximately $43,000 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $345,500 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hampton the median rent is $525 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $974 (64%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $537 (50%) lower, and is roughly $918 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
