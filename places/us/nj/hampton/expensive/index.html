Compared to other places in this area, Hampton is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hampton is $260,500. This is approximately $15,680 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $43,000 (19%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $345,500 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hampton is $525 per month. This is about $974 (64%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $537 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $918 (63%) lower compared to the city of New York.
