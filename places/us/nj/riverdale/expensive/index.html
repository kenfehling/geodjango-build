Compared to the surrounding areas, Riverdale is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Riverdale's median home value is $347,400. It is about $22,600 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $129,900 (59%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $258,600 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Riverdale is $2,098 a month. This is roughly $471 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,036 (97%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $655 (45%) higher.
