In comparison to its surrounding areas, Riverdale is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverdale costs $347,400. This is roughly $22,600 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $129,900 (59%) higher, and is roughly $258,600 (42%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Riverdale the median rent is $2,098 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $471 (28%) higher.It is about $1,036 (97%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $655 (45%) higher.
