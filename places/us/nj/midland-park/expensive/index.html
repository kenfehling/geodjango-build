Home prices

Midland Park's median home price is $513,400. This is about $161,120 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $295,900 (136%) higher, and it is roughly $92,600 (15%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Midland Park is $1,781 per month. This is roughly $268 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $719 (67%) higher, and is about $338 (23%) higher compared to the city of New York.
