Home prices

Midland Park's median home value is $513,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $161,120 (23%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $295,900 (136%) higher, and is about $92,600 (15%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Midland Park is $1,781 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $268 (13%) lower.It is approximately $719 (67%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $338 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
