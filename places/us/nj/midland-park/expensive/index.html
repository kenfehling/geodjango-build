Home prices

The median home in Midland Park is valued at $513,400. This is roughly $161,120 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $295,900 (136%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $92,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Midland Park the median rent is $1,781 per month. This is roughly $268 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $719 (67%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $338 (23%) higher.
