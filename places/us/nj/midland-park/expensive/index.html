In comparison to nearby areas, Midland Park is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Midland Park is $513,400. This is roughly $161,120 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $295,900 (136%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $92,600 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Midland Park is $1,781 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $268 (13%) lower. It is about $719 (67%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $338 (23%) higher.
