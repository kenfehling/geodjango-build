Compared to other places in this area, Midland Park is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Midland Park's median home cost is $513,400. It is roughly $161,120 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $295,900 (136%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $92,600 (15%) lower.

Rents

Midland Park's median monthly rent is $1,781. It is approximately $268 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $719 (67%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $338 (23%) higher compared to the city of New York.
