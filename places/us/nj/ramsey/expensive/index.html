Home prices

The median home in Ramsey is valued at $598,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $277,120 (31%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $381,400 (175%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $7,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ramsey is $1,646. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $386 (18%) lower.It is about $584 (54%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $203 (14%) higher.
