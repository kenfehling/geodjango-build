In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ramsey is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Ramsey costs $598,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $277,120 (31%) lower. It is roughly $381,400 (175%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $7,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Ramsey the median rent is $1,646 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $386 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $584 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $203 (14%) higher.
