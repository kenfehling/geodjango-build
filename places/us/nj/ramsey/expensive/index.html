Home prices

The median home in Ramsey is valued at $598,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $277,120 (31%) lower.It is roughly $381,400 (175%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $7,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ramsey is $1,646 a month. It is approximately $386 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $584 (54%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $203 (14%) higher.
