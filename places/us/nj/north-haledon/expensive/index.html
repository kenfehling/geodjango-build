Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Haledon is $435,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $19,900 (4%) higher. It is roughly $218,200 (100%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $170,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In North Haledon the median rent is $2,188 per month. This is approximately $597 (37%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $1,126 (106%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $745 (51%) higher.
