Compared to the surrounding areas, Interlaken is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of New York, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Interlaken is valued at $655,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $213,920 (24%) lower. It is roughly $438,000 (201%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $49,500 (8%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Interlaken's median monthly rent is $3,050. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $1,808 (145%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,988 (187%) higher, and it is about $1,607 (111%) higher compared to the city of New York.
