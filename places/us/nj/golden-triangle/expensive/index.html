Home prices

The price of the median home in Golden Triangle is $222,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $73,820 (24%) lower. It is roughly $4,600 (2%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $383,900 (63%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Golden Triangle the median rent is $1,533 per month. It is roughly $280 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $471 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $90 (6%) higher.
