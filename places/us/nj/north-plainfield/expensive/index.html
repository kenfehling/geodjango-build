In comparison to other places in this area, North Plainfield is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Plainfield costs $272,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $131,580 (32%) lower. It is about $55,400 (25%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is about $333,100 (54%) lower.

Rents

In North Plainfield the median rent is $1,382 per month. This is roughly $389 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $320 (30%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $61 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
