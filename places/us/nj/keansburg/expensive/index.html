Home prices

Rents

Keansburg's median monthly rent is $1,325. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $155 (10%) lower. It is roughly $263 (24%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $118 (8%) lower.
