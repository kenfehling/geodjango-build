Home prices

The median home in Gibbsboro costs $215,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $47,340 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is is very similar, and it is about $390,600 (64%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Gibbsboro's median rent is $2,167 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $1,133 (109%) higher. It is about $1,105 (104%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is about $724 (50%) higher.
