Home prices

The cost of the median home in Magnolia is $159,600. It is about $10,880 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $57,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $446,400 (73%) lower.

Rents

In Magnolia the median rent is $1,034 per month. This is about $64 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $28 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $409 (28%) lower.
