Compared to nearby areas, Highland Park is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home price in Highland Park is $373,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $55,480 (17%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $156,200 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $232,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

Highland Park's median rent is $1,467 a month. It is roughly $55 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $405 (38%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $24 (1%) higher.
