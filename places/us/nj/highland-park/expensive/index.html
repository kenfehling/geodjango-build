Home prices

Highland Park's median home price is $373,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $55,480 (17%) higher.It is roughly $156,200 (71%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $232,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Highland Park the median monthly rent is $1,467. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $55 (3%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $405 (38%) higher, and is approximately $24 (1%) higher compared to the city of New York.
