In comparison to its surrounding areas, Highland Park is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Highland Park costs $373,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $55,480 (17%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $156,200 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $232,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

Highland Park's median rent is $1,467 a month. This is approximately $55 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $405 (38%) higher, and is roughly $24 (1%) higher compared to the city of New York.
