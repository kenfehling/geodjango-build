Compared to its surrounding areas, Mendham is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of New York, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mendham is $662,300. It is roughly $42,760 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $444,800 (204%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $56,300 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Mendham the median monthly rent is $1,581. This is approximately $161 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $519 (48%) higher, and is roughly $138 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
