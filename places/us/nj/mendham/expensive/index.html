Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mendham is $662,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $42,760 (6%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $444,800 (204%) higher, and it is roughly $56,300 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Mendham the median rent is $1,581 a month. It is about $161 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $519 (48%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $138 (9%) higher compared to the city of New York.
