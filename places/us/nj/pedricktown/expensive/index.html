Home prices

The value of the median home in Pedricktown is $172,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $3,320 (1%) lower. It is approximately $45,300 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $9,200 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pedricktown is $1,202. This is approximately $124 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $140 (13%) higher, and is roughly $160 (15%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
