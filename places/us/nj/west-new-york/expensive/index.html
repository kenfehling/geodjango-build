In comparison to the surrounding areas, West New York is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The price of the median home in West New York is $326,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $131,760 (28%) lower. It is roughly $109,400 (50%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $279,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In West New York the median monthly rent is $1,296. This is about $331 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $234 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $147 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
