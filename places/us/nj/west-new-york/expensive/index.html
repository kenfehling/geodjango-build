Home prices

The median home in West New York is valued at $326,900. It is roughly $131,760 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $109,400 (50%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $279,100 (46%) lower.

Rents

In West New York the median monthly rent is $1,296. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $331 (20%) lower.It is approximately $234 (22%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $147 (10%) lower compared to the city of New York.
