Compared to other places in this area, West New York is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in West New York costs $326,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $131,760 (28%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $109,400 (50%) higher, and it is roughly $279,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

West New York's median monthly rent is $1,296. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $331 (20%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $234 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $147 (10%) lower.
