Home prices

The cost of the median home in Monmouth Junction is $433,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $41,080 (10%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $216,300 (99%) higher, and it is about $172,200 (28%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Monmouth Junction is $2,356 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $657 (38%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $1,294 (121%) higher, and it is about $913 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
