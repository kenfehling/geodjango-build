Compared to other places in this area, Glen Ridge is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Glen Ridge is valued at $653,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $245,400 (60%) higher. It is roughly $435,900 (200%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $47,400 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Glen Ridge's median rent is $1,723 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $284 (19%) higher. It is about $661 (62%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $280 (19%) higher.
