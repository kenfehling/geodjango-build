Home prices

The median home in Clementon is valued at $134,800. This is about $32,040 (19%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $82,700 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $471,200 (77%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Clementon's median monthly rent is $963. It is roughly $242 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $99 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $480 (33%) lower.
