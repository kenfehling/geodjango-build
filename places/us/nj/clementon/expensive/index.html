Home prices

The median home cost in Clementon is $134,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $32,040 (19%) lower. It is about $82,700 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $471,200 (77%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clementon is $963. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $242 (20%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $99 (9%) lower, and is approximately $480 (33%) lower compared to the city of New York.
