Home prices

The median home in Metuchen costs $437,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $116,780 (36%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $219,900 (101%) higher, and it is roughly $168,600 (27%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Metuchen's median rent is $1,541 a month. This is about $125 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $479 (45%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $98 (6%) higher.
