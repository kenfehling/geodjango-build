Compared to other places in this area, Metuchen is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Metuchen is $437,400. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $116,780 (36%) higher. It is roughly $219,900 (101%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $168,600 (27%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Metuchen's median monthly rent is $1,541. Compared to nearby areas it is about $125 (7%) lower. It is approximately $479 (45%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $98 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
