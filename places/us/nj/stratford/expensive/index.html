Home prices

The value of the median home in Stratford is $179,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $8,280 (4%) higher.It is approximately $38,400 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $426,900 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Stratford is $991 per month. It is about $50 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $71 (6%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $452 (31%) lower compared to the city of New York.
