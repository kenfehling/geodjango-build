Home prices

The cost of the median home in Stratford is $179,100. It is roughly $8,280 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $38,400 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $426,900 (70%) lower.

Rents

Stratford's median monthly rent is $991. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $50 (4%) lower. It is roughly $71 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $452 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
