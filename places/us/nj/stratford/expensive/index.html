Home prices

The median home in Stratford costs $179,100. This is roughly $8,280 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $38,400 (17%) lower, and is approximately $426,900 (70%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Stratford the median monthly rent is $991. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $50 (4%) lower. It is about $71 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $452 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
