Home prices

The value of the median home in Pompton Lakes is $302,700. This is roughly $55,820 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $85,200 (39%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $303,300 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Pompton Lakes is $1,536 per month. It is approximately $124 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $474 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $93 (6%) higher.
