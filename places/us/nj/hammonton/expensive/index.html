Compared to its nearby areas, Hammonton is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hammonton costs $235,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $63,540 (36%) higher. It is approximately $18,000 (8%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $370,500 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hammonton is $1,204. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $172 (16%) higher. It is about $142 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $239 (16%) lower compared to the city of New York.
