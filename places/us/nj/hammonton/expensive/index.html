Compared to other places in this area, Hammonton is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Hammonton is valued at $235,500. This is roughly $63,540 (36%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $18,000 (8%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $370,500 (61%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Hammonton's median rent is $1,204 per month. This is about $172 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $142 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $239 (16%) lower.
