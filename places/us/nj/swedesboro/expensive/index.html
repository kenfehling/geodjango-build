Home prices

The value of the median home in Swedesboro is $202,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $25,300 (11%) lower.It is about $14,900 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $39,600 (24%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Swedesboro's median monthly rent is $1,338. It is roughly $134 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $276 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $296 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
