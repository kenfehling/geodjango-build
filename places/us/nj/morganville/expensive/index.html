Home prices

The median home value in Morganville is $553,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $181,320 (48%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $336,300 (154%) higher, and it is approximately $52,200 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morganville is $2,292. It is about $636 (38%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $1,230 (115%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $849 (58%) higher.
