Home prices

Springdale's median home value is $364,900. This is approximately $125,440 (52%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $147,400 (67%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $241,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Springdale the median rent is $1,281 a month. It is about $164 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $219 (20%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $162 (11%) lower.
