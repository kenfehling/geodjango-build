Home prices

The median home cost in Guttenberg is $323,100. It is approximately $108,640 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $105,600 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $282,900 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Guttenberg the median rent is $1,439 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $177 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $377 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is is very similar.
