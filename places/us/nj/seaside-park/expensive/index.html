In comparison to its surrounding areas, Seaside Park is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of New York, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Seaside Park costs $709,700. It is roughly $273,140 (62%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $492,200 (226%) higher, and is roughly $103,700 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Seaside Park's median monthly rent is $1,322. This is about $146 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $260 (24%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $121 (8%) lower compared to the city of New York.
