In comparison to other places in this area, Seaside Park is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Seaside Park costs $709,700. It is about $273,140 (62%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $492,200 (226%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $103,700 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Seaside Park is $1,322 a month. It is about $146 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $260 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $121 (8%) lower.
