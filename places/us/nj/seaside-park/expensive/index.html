Home prices

The median home in Seaside Park is valued at $709,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $273,140 (62%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $492,200 (226%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $103,700 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Seaside Park the median rent is $1,322 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $146 (9%) lower.It is about $260 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $121 (8%) lower.
