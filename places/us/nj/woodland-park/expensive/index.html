Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Woodland Park is $1,480 a month. This is approximately $91 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $418 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $37 (2%) higher.
