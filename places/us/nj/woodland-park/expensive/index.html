Compared to other places in this area, Woodland Park is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Woodland Park is valued at $365,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $57,640 (18%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $148,400 (68%) higher, and it is approximately $240,100 (39%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Woodland Park's median monthly rent is $1,480. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $91 (6%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $418 (39%) higher, and it is approximately $37 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
