In comparison to its nearby areas, Woodland Park is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Woodland Park is valued at $365,900. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $57,640 (18%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $148,400 (68%) higher, and it is roughly $240,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Woodland Park the median rent is $1,480 a month. This is approximately $91 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $418 (39%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $37 (2%) higher compared to the city of New York.
