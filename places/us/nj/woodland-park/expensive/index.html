Compared to other places in this area, Woodland Park is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Woodland Park is $365,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $57,640 (18%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $148,400 (68%) higher, and it is approximately $240,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Woodland Park's median rent is $1,480 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $91 (6%) higher. It is approximately $418 (39%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $37 (2%) higher.
