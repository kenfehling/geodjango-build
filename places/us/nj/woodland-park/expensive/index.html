Compared to other places in this area, Woodland Park is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of New York however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Woodland Park is valued at $365,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $57,640 (18%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $148,400 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $240,100 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Woodland Park the median monthly rent is $1,480. It is approximately $91 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $418 (39%) higher, and is roughly $37 (2%) higher compared to the city of New York.
