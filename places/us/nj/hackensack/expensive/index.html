Home prices

The median home in Hackensack costs $296,300. This is approximately $113,340 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $78,800 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $309,700 (51%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hackensack the median rent is $1,440 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $53 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $378 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is is very similar.
