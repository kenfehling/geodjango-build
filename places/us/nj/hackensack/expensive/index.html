Home prices

Hackensack's median home cost is $296,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $113,340 (27%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $78,800 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $309,700 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hackensack is $1,440 per month. This is approximately $53 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $378 (35%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of New York.
