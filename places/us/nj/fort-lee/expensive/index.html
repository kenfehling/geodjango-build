Home prices

The median home in Fort Lee costs $324,600. It is roughly $167,820 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $107,100 (49%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $281,400 (46%) lower.

Rents

Fort Lee's median monthly rent is $1,923. It is approximately $262 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $861 (81%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $480 (33%) higher.
