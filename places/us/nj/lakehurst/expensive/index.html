Home prices

The median home in Lakehurst costs $190,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $57,020 (42%) higher. It is approximately $27,200 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $415,700 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Lakehurst the median monthly rent is $1,689. This is about $436 (34%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $627 (59%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $246 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
