Home prices

Oakland's median home value is $468,700. This is approximately $25,240 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $251,200 (115%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $137,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $1,642 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $261 (13%) lower. It is about $580 (54%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $199 (13%) higher.
