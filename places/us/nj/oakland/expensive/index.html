Compared to other places in this area, Oakland is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Oakland is $468,700. This is approximately $25,240 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $251,200 (115%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $137,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $1,642 per month. It is roughly $261 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $580 (54%) higher, and it is about $199 (13%) higher compared to the city of New York.
