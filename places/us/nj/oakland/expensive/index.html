Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakland is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Oakland is $468,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $25,240 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $251,200 (115%) higher, and it is about $137,300 (22%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Oakland is $1,642 a month. It is approximately $261 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $580 (54%) higher, and it is approximately $199 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
