In comparison to the surrounding areas, Pitman is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Pitman is $185,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $57,400 (23%) lower. It is roughly $31,900 (14%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $420,400 (69%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Pitman's median rent is $1,204 a month. It is approximately $82 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $142 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $239 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
