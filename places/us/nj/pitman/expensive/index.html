Home prices

Rents

Pitman's median rent is $1,204 per month. It is about $82 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $142 (13%) higher, and it is approximately $239 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
