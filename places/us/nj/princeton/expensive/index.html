Home prices

The median home in Princeton costs $866,200. It is about $432,400 (99%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $648,700 (298%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $260,200 (42%) higher.

Rents

Princeton's median rent is $1,532 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $90 (5%) lower. It is roughly $470 (44%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $89 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
