Home prices

The median home price in Palisades Park is $558,400. This is approximately $144,360 (34%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $340,900 (156%) higher, and is roughly $47,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palisades Park is $1,520. This is approximately $37 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $458 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $77 (5%) higher.
