Home prices

Rents

The median rent in North Middletown is $1,662 a month. It is roughly $282 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $600 (56%) higher, and it is about $219 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
