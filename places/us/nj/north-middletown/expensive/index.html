Home prices

The median home in North Middletown is valued at $235,800. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $51,780 (18%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $18,300 (8%) higher, and it is roughly $370,200 (61%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Middletown is $1,662. It is about $282 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $600 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $219 (15%) higher.
