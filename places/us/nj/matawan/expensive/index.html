Home prices

The median home in Matawan costs $350,400. This is roughly $6,260 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $132,900 (61%) higher, and it is roughly $255,600 (42%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Matawan is $1,506 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the entire country it is about $444 (41%) higher, and is about $63 (4%) higher compared to the city of New York.
