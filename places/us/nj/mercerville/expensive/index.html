Home prices

The price of the median home in Mercerville is $259,300. It is approximately $3,460 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $41,800 (19%) higher, and is approximately $346,700 (57%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Mercerville the median rent is $1,403 a month. This is roughly $173 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $341 (32%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $40 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
