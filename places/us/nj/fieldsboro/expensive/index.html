Home prices

The median home in Fieldsboro is valued at $194,100. This is about $15,700 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $23,400 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $411,900 (67%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Fieldsboro the median monthly rent is $1,639. It is about $463 (39%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $577 (54%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $196 (13%) higher.
