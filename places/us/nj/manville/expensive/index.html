In comparison to other places in this area, Manville is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of New York however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manville is valued at $262,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $76,240 (22%) lower.It is approximately $44,500 (20%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $344,000 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Manville's median rent is $1,355 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $439 (24%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $293 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $88 (6%) lower.
