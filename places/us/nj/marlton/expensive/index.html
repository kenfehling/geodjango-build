In comparison to other places in this area, Marlton is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marlton is valued at $249,200. This is about $39,600 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $31,700 (14%) higher, and it is about $356,800 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Marlton the median monthly rent is $1,298. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $175 (11%) lower. It is approximately $236 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $145 (10%) lower.
