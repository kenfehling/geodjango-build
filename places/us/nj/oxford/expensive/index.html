Home prices

The price of the median home in Oxford is $173,400. It is roughly $69,040 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $44,100 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $432,600 (71%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oxford is $674. This is approximately $722 (51%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $388 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $769 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
