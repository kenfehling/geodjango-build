Home prices

The median home in Williamstown costs $205,900. It is approximately $15,980 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $11,600 (5%) lower, and it is approximately $400,100 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Williamstown is $1,003 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $156 (13%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $59 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $440 (30%) lower.
