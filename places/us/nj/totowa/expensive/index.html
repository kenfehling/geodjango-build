Compared to its surrounding areas, Totowa is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Totowa is $371,200. It is about $79,820 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $153,700 (70%) higher, and it is about $234,800 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Totowa's median monthly rent is $1,569. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $192 (13%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $507 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $126 (8%) higher.
