Compared to the surrounding areas, Cedarville is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

Cedarville's median home cost is $149,100. This is roughly $12,920 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $68,400 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $13,900 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Cedarville the median rent is $1,243 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $206 (19%) higher. It is about $181 (17%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $201 (19%) higher.
