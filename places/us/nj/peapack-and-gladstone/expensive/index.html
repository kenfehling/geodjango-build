Compared to the surrounding areas, Peapack and Gladstone is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Peapack and Gladstone is valued at $680,900. It is about $80,640 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $463,400 (213%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $74,900 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Peapack and Gladstone is $1,526. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $277 (15%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $464 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $83 (5%) higher.
