Compared to the surrounding areas, Peapack and Gladstone is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of New York, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Peapack and Gladstone is $680,900. It is about $80,640 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $463,400 (213%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $74,900 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Peapack and Gladstone the median rent is $1,526 a month. This is roughly $277 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $464 (43%) higher, and is approximately $83 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
