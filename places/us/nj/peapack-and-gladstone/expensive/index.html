Home prices

The median home in Peapack and Gladstone costs $680,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $80,640 (13%) higher.It is about $463,400 (213%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $74,900 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Peapack and Gladstone the median rent is $1,526 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $277 (15%) lower.It is roughly $464 (43%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $83 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
