The median home in New Providence costs $607,100. This is about $83,900 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $389,600 (179%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is is very similar.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Providence is $1,733. It is approximately $263 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $671 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $290 (20%) higher.
