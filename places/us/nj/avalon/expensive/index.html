Home prices

The price of the median home in Avalon is $1,192,600. It is approximately $627,820 (111%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $975,100 (448%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $1,029,600 (631%) higher.

Rents

Avalon's median monthly rent is $1,333. Compared to nearby areas this is about $54 (4%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $271 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $291 (27%) higher.
