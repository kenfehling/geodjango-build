Compared to other places in this area, Avalon is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Avalon costs $1,192,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $627,820 (111%) higher. It is approximately $975,100 (448%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $1,029,600 (631%) higher.

Rents

In Avalon the median rent is $1,333 a month. This is about $54 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $271 (25%) higher, and is approximately $291 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
