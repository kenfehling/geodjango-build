Home prices

The median home cost in Morris Plains is $517,500. It is roughly $92,880 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $300,000 (137%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $88,500 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Morris Plains the median rent is $2,524 per month. It is about $1,076 (74%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,462 (137%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $1,081 (74%) higher.
