Home prices

The median home price in Lake Mohawk is $349,200. It is roughly $97,240 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $131,700 (60%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $256,800 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Mohawk the median monthly rent is $1,638. It is roughly $236 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $576 (54%) higher, and it is approximately $195 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
