Home prices

The price of the median home in Spring Lake Heights is $492,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $285,420 (36%) lower. It is roughly $274,800 (126%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $113,700 (18%) lower.

Rents
