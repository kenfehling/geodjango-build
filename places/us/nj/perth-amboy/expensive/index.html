Home prices

The median home value in Perth Amboy is $261,800. This is roughly $31,380 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $44,300 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $344,200 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Perth Amboy is $1,340. Compared to nearby areas this is about $115 (7%) lower. It is roughly $278 (26%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $103 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
