Compared to other places in this area, Perth Amboy is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Perth Amboy is $261,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $31,380 (10%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $44,300 (20%) higher, and is about $344,200 (56%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Perth Amboy is $1,340 per month. This is roughly $115 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $278 (26%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $103 (7%) lower.
