In comparison to the surrounding areas, Perth Amboy is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Perth Amboy is $261,800. It is roughly $31,380 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $44,300 (20%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $344,200 (56%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Perth Amboy's median monthly rent is $1,340. This is about $115 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $278 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $103 (7%) lower.
