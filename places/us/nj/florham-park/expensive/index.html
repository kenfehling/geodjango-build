Compared to nearby areas, Florham Park is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Florham Park is $645,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $198,140 (23%) lower.It is about $428,300 (196%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $39,800 (6%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Florham Park the median monthly rent is $2,441. It is approximately $429 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,379 (129%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $998 (69%) higher.
