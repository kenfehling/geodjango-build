In comparison to nearby areas, Florham Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of New York, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florham Park costs $645,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $198,140 (23%) lower. It is approximately $428,300 (196%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $39,800 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Florham Park's median rent is $2,441 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $429 (21%) higher. It is approximately $1,379 (129%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $998 (69%) higher.
