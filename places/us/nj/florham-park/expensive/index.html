Home prices

Florham Park's median home value is $645,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $198,140 (23%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $428,300 (196%) higher, and is roughly $39,800 (6%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Florham Park the median monthly rent is $2,441. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $429 (21%) higher.It is approximately $1,379 (129%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $998 (69%) higher compared to the city of New York.
