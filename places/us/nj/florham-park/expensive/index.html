In comparison to other places in this area, Florham Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of New York, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Florham Park's median home cost is $645,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $198,140 (23%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $428,300 (196%) higher, and it is roughly $39,800 (6%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Florham Park's median rent is $2,441 a month. This is roughly $429 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $1,379 (129%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $998 (69%) higher.
