In comparison to its surrounding areas, Florham Park is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Florham Park's median home value is $645,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $198,140 (23%) lower. It is about $428,300 (196%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $39,800 (6%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Florham Park is $2,441 per month. This is approximately $429 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,379 (129%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $998 (69%) higher.
