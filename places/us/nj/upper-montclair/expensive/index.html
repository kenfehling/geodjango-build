Home prices

The median home in Upper Montclair costs $766,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $355,680 (86%) higher.It is approximately $548,600 (252%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $160,100 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Upper Montclair is $2,008 a month. This is approximately $519 (34%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $946 (89%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $565 (39%) higher.
