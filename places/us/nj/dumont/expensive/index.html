Compared to its surrounding areas, Dumont is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Dumont's median home value is $376,100. This is roughly $175,800 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $158,600 (72%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $229,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

Dumont's median rent is $1,306 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $688 (34%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $244 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $137 (9%) lower.
