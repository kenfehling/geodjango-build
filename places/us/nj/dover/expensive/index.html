Home prices

The median home in Dover is valued at $268,000. It is about $30,420 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $50,500 (23%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $338,000 (55%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Dover is $1,461 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $56 (3%) lower. It is about $399 (37%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $18 (1%) higher.
