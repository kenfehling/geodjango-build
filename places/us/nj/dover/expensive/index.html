Compared to other places in this area, Dover is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Dover costs $268,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $30,420 (10%) lower. It is about $50,500 (23%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $338,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Dover is $1,461 per month. It is about $56 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $399 (37%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $18 (1%) higher compared to the city of New York.
