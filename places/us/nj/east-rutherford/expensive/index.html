Compared to the surrounding areas, East Rutherford is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in East Rutherford costs $381,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,860 (6%) lower. It is approximately $163,600 (75%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $224,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In East Rutherford the median monthly rent is $1,586. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $197 (14%) higher. It is about $524 (49%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $143 (9%) higher.
