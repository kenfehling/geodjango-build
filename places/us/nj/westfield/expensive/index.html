In comparison to its surrounding areas, Westfield is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Westfield is $743,400. This is approximately $322,560 (76%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $525,900 (241%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $137,400 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Westfield the median rent is $1,608 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $151 (8%) lower. It is approximately $546 (51%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $165 (11%) higher compared to the city of New York.
