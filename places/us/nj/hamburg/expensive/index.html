Compared to its surrounding areas, Hamburg is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hamburg costs $182,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $16,000 (8%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $35,000 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $423,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Hamburg the median monthly rent is $1,376. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $87 (6%) higher.It is approximately $314 (29%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $67 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
