In comparison to other places in this area, Hamburg is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hamburg is valued at $182,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $16,000 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $35,000 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $423,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hamburg the median monthly rent is $1,376. It is about $87 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $314 (29%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $67 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
