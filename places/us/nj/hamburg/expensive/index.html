In comparison to other places in this area, Hamburg is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Hamburg is $182,500. This is approximately $16,000 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $35,000 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $423,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Hamburg the median rent is $1,376 per month. It is roughly $87 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $314 (29%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $67 (4%) lower.
