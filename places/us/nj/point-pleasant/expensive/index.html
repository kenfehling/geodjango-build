Home prices

The cost of the median home in Point Pleasant is $386,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $300,100 (43%) lower.It is roughly $168,700 (77%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $219,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

Point Pleasant's median rent is $1,634 per month. This is about $106 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $572 (53%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $191 (13%) higher compared to the city of New York.
