Compared to its nearby areas, Jersey City is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of New York, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jersey City is $373,700. It is roughly $38,700 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $156,200 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $232,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

Jersey City's median monthly rent is $1,401. Compared to nearby areas this is about $120 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $339 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $42 (2%) lower.
