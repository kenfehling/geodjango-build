Home prices

The median home value in Jersey City is $373,700. This is about $38,700 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $156,200 (71%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is about $232,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Jersey City the median monthly rent is $1,401. This is about $120 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $339 (31%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $42 (2%) lower.
