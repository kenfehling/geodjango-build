In comparison to nearby areas, Jersey City is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home value in Jersey City is $373,700. This is roughly $38,700 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $156,200 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $232,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Jersey City is $1,401 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $120 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $339 (31%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $42 (2%) lower.
