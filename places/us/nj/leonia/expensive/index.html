Home prices

Leonia's median home cost is $506,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $114,820 (29%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $288,600 (132%) higher, and is roughly $99,900 (16%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Leonia the median monthly rent is $1,444. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $91 (5%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $382 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is is very similar.
