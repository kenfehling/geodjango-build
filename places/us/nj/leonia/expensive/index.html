Home prices

The median home in Leonia is valued at $506,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $114,820 (29%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $288,600 (132%) higher, and it is about $99,900 (16%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Leonia's median monthly rent is $1,444. This is roughly $91 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $382 (35%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of New York.
