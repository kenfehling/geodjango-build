Home prices

The price of the median home in Leonia is $506,100. This is approximately $114,820 (29%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $288,600 (132%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $99,900 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Leonia the median monthly rent is $1,444. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $91 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $382 (35%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of New York.
