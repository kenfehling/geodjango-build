In comparison to other places in this area, New Brunswick is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Brunswick costs $251,100. This is about $83,080 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $33,600 (15%) higher, and it is about $354,900 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

New Brunswick's median monthly rent is $1,470. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $15 (1%) lower. It is approximately $408 (38%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $27 (1%) higher.
