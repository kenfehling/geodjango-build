Compared to its surrounding areas, New Brunswick is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in New Brunswick is $251,100. This is roughly $83,080 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $33,600 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $354,900 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in New Brunswick is $1,470 a month. This is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $408 (38%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $27 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
