In comparison to its surrounding areas, Colonia is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Colonia is $363,900. This is about $77,000 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $146,400 (67%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $242,100 (39%) lower.

Rents

Colonia's median monthly rent is $1,637. It is roughly $80 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $575 (54%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $194 (13%) higher compared to the city of New York.
