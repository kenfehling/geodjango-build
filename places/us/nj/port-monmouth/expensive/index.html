Home prices

Port Monmouth's median home value is $284,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $6,540 (2%) higher. It is about $66,900 (30%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $321,600 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Port Monmouth is $958. This is roughly $563 (37%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $104 (9%) lower, and it is about $485 (33%) lower compared to the city of New York.
