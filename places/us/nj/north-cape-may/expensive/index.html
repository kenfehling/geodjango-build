Rents

North Cape May's median rent is $1,269 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $171 (15%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $207 (19%) higher, and is approximately $227 (21%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
