In comparison to the surrounding areas, Paramus is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Paramus's median home cost is $600,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $73,460 (13%) higher. It is about $382,700 (175%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is is very similar.

Rents

In Paramus the median monthly rent is $2,011. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $378 (23%) higher. It is roughly $949 (89%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $568 (39%) higher.
