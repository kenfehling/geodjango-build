Compared to nearby areas, East Franklin is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Franklin costs $268,300. It is roughly $71,000 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $50,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $337,700 (55%) lower.

Rents

East Franklin's median monthly rent is $1,360. This is about $183 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $298 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $83 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
