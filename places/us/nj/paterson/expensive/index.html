In comparison to other places in this area, Paterson is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Paterson is $244,200. This is approximately $93,920 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $26,700 (12%) higher, and it is about $361,800 (59%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Paterson's median monthly rent is $1,193. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $316 (20%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $131 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $250 (17%) lower.
