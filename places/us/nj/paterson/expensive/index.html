Home prices

The median home cost in Paterson is $244,200. It is roughly $93,920 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $26,700 (12%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $361,800 (59%) lower.

Rents

Paterson's median monthly rent is $1,193. It is roughly $316 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $131 (12%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $250 (17%) lower.
