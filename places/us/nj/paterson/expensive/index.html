Home prices

The cost of the median home in Paterson is $244,200. It is about $93,920 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $26,700 (12%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $361,800 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Paterson is $1,193 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $316 (20%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $131 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $250 (17%) lower.
