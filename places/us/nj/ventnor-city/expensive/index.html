Compared to its surrounding areas, Ventnor City is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Ventnor City is $273,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $103,740 (27%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $56,000 (25%) higher, and is roughly $332,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Ventnor City's median monthly rent is $1,163. This is about $211 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $101 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $280 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
