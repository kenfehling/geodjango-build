Compared to its surrounding areas, Berlin is significantly more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Berlin is $239,200. It is about $81,220 (51%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $21,700 (9%) higher, and it is roughly $366,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Berlin's median rent is $1,167 per month. It is approximately $32 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $105 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $276 (19%) lower.
