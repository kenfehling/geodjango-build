Home prices

Berlin's median home price is $239,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $81,220 (51%) higher. It is approximately $21,700 (9%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $366,800 (60%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Berlin is $1,167 per month. It is about $32 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $105 (9%) higher, and is roughly $276 (19%) lower compared to the city of New York.
