Home prices

Belmar's median home value is $599,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $135,520 (29%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $382,400 (175%) higher, and it is approximately $6,100 (1%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Belmar's median monthly rent is $1,568. It is about $103 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $506 (47%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $125 (8%) higher.
