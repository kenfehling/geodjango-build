Home prices

The median home in Belmar is valued at $599,900. It is roughly $135,520 (29%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $382,400 (175%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $6,100 (1%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Belmar's median rent is $1,568 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $103 (7%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $506 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $125 (8%) higher.
