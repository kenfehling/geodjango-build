Home prices

The median home value in Browns Mills is $157,600. This is roughly $47,420 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower, and is roughly $448,400 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Browns Mills's median monthly rent is $1,162. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $100 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $281 (19%) lower.
