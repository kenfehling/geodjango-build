Home prices

Eatontown's median home cost is $336,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $143,280 (29%) lower.It is approximately $118,700 (54%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is about $269,800 (44%) lower.

Rents

Eatontown's median rent is $1,232 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $811 (39%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $170 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $211 (14%) lower.
