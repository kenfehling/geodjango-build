Compared to nearby areas, Eatontown is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home value in Eatontown is $336,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $143,280 (29%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $118,700 (54%) higher, and is approximately $269,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Eatontown is $1,232 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $811 (39%) lower. It is about $170 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $211 (14%) lower.
