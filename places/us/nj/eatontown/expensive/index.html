Home prices

The median home in Eatontown costs $336,200. It is about $143,280 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $118,700 (54%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $269,800 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Eatontown is $1,232. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $811 (39%) lower.It is roughly $170 (16%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $211 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
