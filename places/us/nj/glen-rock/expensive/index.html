Home prices

Rents

Glen Rock's median monthly rent is $1,840. This is approximately $67 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $778 (73%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $397 (27%) higher.
