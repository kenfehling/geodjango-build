Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hasbrouck Heights is $429,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $59,100 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $211,600 (97%) higher, and it is roughly $176,900 (29%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hasbrouck Heights the median rent is $1,445 per month. This is approximately $63 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $383 (36%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of New York.
