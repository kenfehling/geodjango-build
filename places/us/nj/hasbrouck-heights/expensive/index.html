Home prices

The median home in Hasbrouck Heights is valued at $429,100. This is roughly $59,100 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $211,600 (97%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $176,900 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hasbrouck Heights is $1,445. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $63 (4%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $383 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is is very similar.
