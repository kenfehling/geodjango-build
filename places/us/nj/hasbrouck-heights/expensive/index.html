Home prices

Hasbrouck Heights's median home price is $429,100. This is roughly $59,100 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $211,600 (97%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $176,900 (29%) lower.

Rents

Hasbrouck Heights's median rent is $1,445 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $63 (4%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $383 (36%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of New York.
