Home prices

The median home in Sussex costs $212,400. It is about $20,220 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $5,100 (2%) lower, and is about $393,600 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Sussex is $1,216 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $77 (5%) lower. It is roughly $154 (14%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $227 (15%) lower.
