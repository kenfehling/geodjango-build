Compared to the surrounding areas, Fair Haven is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fair Haven is valued at $731,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $84,600 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $514,400 (236%) higher, and it is about $125,900 (20%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fair Haven is $2,288. It is about $24 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $1,226 (115%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $845 (58%) higher.
