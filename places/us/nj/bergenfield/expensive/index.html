Home prices

The median home in Bergenfield costs $358,200. It is roughly $207,960 (36%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $140,700 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $247,800 (40%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Bergenfield the median monthly rent is $1,420. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $455 (24%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $358 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $23 (1%) lower.
