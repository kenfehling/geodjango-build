In comparison to other places in this area, Bellmawr is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bellmawr is $167,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $11,940 (6%) lower.It is about $49,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $438,300 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Bellmawr's median rent is $898 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $150 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $164 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $545 (37%) lower.
