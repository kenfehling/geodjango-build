Home prices

The median home in Clayton costs $197,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,420 (5%) lower. It is roughly $19,900 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $408,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Clayton's median rent is $1,033 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $209 (16%) lower. It is roughly $29 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $410 (28%) lower.
