Home prices

The median home in Island Heights costs $389,600. It is about $132,300 (51%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $172,100 (79%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $216,400 (35%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Island Heights's median monthly rent is $1,651. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $120 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $589 (55%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $208 (14%) higher.
