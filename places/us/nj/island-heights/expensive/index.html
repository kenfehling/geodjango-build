Home prices

The price of the median home in Island Heights is $389,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $132,300 (51%) higher. It is about $172,100 (79%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $216,400 (35%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Island Heights the median rent is $1,651 a month. This is roughly $120 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $589 (55%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $208 (14%) higher.
