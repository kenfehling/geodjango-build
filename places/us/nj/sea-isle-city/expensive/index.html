Home prices

The price of the median home in Sea Isle City is $671,700. It is roughly $41,460 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $454,200 (208%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $508,700 (312%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Sea Isle City is $1,444 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $212 (17%) higher.It is about $382 (35%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $402 (38%) higher.
