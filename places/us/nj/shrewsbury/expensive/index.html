Compared to its nearby areas, Shrewsbury is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Shrewsbury is $589,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $80,740 (15%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $371,900 (170%) higher, and it is about $16,600 (2%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Shrewsbury's median rent is $2,735 per month. This is roughly $755 (38%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $1,673 (157%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $1,292 (89%) higher.
