Compared to its surrounding areas, Seaside Heights is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Seaside Heights is $296,600. This is about $222,580 (42%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $79,100 (36%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $309,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Seaside Heights is $1,152. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $350 (23%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $90 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $291 (20%) lower.
