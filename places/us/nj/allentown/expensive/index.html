Much like the surrounding areas, it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Allentown is $320,200. It is about $13,480 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $102,700 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $285,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Allentown is $1,229. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $19 (1%) higher. It is about $167 (15%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $214 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
