Home prices

The median home in Allentown costs $320,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $13,480 (4%) higher. It is about $102,700 (47%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $285,800 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Allentown the median monthly rent is $1,229. This is approximately $19 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $167 (15%) higher, and it is roughly $214 (14%) lower compared to the city of New York.
