Home prices

The cost of the median home in Allentown is $320,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $13,480 (4%) higher.It is approximately $102,700 (47%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $285,800 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Allentown's median rent is $1,229 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $19 (1%) higher.It is approximately $167 (15%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $214 (14%) lower compared to the city of New York.
