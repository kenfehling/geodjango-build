Home prices

The median home value in Brookdale is $378,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $135,620 (26%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $161,100 (74%) higher, and is roughly $227,400 (37%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Brookdale is $1,469 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $84 (5%) lower. It is approximately $407 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $26 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
