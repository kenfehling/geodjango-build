Compared to its surrounding areas, Brookdale is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Brookdale costs $378,600. It is roughly $135,620 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $161,100 (74%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $227,400 (37%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookdale is $1,469. It is approximately $84 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $407 (38%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $26 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
