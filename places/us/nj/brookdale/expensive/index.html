Home prices

The median home in Brookdale is valued at $378,600. This is approximately $135,620 (26%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $161,100 (74%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $227,400 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookdale is $1,469. This is approximately $84 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $407 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $26 (1%) higher.
