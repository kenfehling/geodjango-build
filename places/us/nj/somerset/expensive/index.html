In comparison to other places in this area, Somerset is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Somerset is $315,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $76,760 (19%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $98,400 (45%) higher, and is roughly $290,100 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Somerset the median monthly rent is $1,629. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $40 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $567 (53%) higher, and is approximately $186 (12%) higher compared to the city of New York.
