In comparison to its surrounding areas, Harrison is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Harrison is valued at $333,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $51,720 (18%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $116,200 (53%) higher, and is approximately $272,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Harrison is $1,504 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $295 (24%) higher. It is approximately $442 (41%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $61 (4%) higher.
