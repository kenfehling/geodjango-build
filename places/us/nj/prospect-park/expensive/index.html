In comparison to other places in this area, Prospect Park is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Prospect Park's median home cost is $266,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $85,020 (24%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $48,800 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $339,700 (56%) lower.

Rents

Prospect Park's median rent is $1,439 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $128 (8%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $377 (35%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of New York.
