Home prices

The median home in Prospect Park costs $266,300. It is approximately $85,020 (24%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is about $48,800 (22%) higher, and it is about $339,700 (56%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Prospect Park the median monthly rent is $1,439. This is roughly $128 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $377 (35%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is is very similar.
