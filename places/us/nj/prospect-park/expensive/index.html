Home prices

The median home price in Prospect Park is $266,300. It is roughly $85,020 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $48,800 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $339,700 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Prospect Park is $1,439. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $128 (8%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $377 (35%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of New York.
