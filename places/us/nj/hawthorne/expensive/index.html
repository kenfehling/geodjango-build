Compared to its surrounding areas, Hawthorne is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of New York however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hawthorne is $381,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $27,860 (6%) lower.It is approximately $163,500 (75%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $225,000 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Hawthorne the median rent is $1,485 per month. It is about $202 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $423 (39%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $42 (2%) higher.
