In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hawthorne is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Hawthorne's median home cost is $381,000. It is approximately $27,860 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $163,500 (75%) higher, and is roughly $225,000 (37%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hawthorne the median monthly rent is $1,485. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $202 (11%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $423 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $42 (2%) higher.
