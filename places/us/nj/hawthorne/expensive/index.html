Home prices

The median home in Hawthorne costs $381,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $27,860 (6%) lower.It is approximately $163,500 (75%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $225,000 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Hawthorne the median rent is $1,485 per month. It is about $202 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $423 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $42 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
