Home prices

The median home in Butler costs $355,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $13,240 (3%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $137,700 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $250,800 (41%) lower.

Rents

Butler's median monthly rent is $1,321. It is about $462 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $259 (24%) higher, and is approximately $122 (8%) lower compared to the city of New York.
