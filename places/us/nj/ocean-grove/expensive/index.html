Home prices

Rents

Ocean Grove's median rent is $1,281 per month. This is roughly $58 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $219 (20%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $162 (11%) lower compared to the city of New York.
