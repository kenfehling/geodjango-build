Home prices

The price of the median home in Ogdensburg is $223,400. This is about $15,500 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $5,900 (2%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $382,600 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Ogdensburg the median monthly rent is $1,440. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $111 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $378 (35%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of New York.
