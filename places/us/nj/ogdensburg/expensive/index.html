Home prices

The price of the median home in Ogdensburg is $223,400. This is about $15,500 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $5,900 (2%) higher, and it is approximately $382,600 (63%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Ogdensburg's median monthly rent is $1,440. It is about $111 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $378 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is is very similar.
