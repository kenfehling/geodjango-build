Home prices

The value of the median home in Strathmore is $381,300. It is approximately $43,340 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $163,800 (75%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $224,700 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Strathmore the median monthly rent is $1,494. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $432 (40%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $51 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
