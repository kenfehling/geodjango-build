Home prices

The median home in North Arlington is valued at $363,400. This is approximately $28,820 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $145,900 (67%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $242,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in North Arlington is $1,375 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $75 (5%) lower.It is about $313 (29%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $68 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
