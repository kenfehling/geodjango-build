Home prices

Rents

Hoboken's median rent is $2,315 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $905 (64%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,253 (117%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $872 (60%) higher.
