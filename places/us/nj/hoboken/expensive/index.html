In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hoboken is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hoboken is valued at $720,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $355,640 (97%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $503,200 (231%) higher, and it is roughly $114,700 (18%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hoboken the median monthly rent is $2,315. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $905 (64%) higher. It is about $1,253 (117%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $872 (60%) higher compared to the city of New York.
