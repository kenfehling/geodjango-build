Home prices

The median home in Hoboken is valued at $720,700. This is roughly $355,640 (97%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $503,200 (231%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $114,700 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Hoboken the median monthly rent is $2,315. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $905 (64%) higher. It is approximately $1,253 (117%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $872 (60%) higher compared to the city of New York.
