Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hoboken is $720,700. It is about $355,640 (97%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $503,200 (231%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $114,700 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Hoboken's median rent is $2,315 per month. It is roughly $905 (64%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $1,253 (117%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $872 (60%) higher.
