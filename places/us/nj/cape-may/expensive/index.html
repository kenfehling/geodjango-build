In comparison to other places in this area, Cape May is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cape May costs $652,900. This is approximately $305,240 (87%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $435,400 (200%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $489,900 (300%) higher.

Rents

Cape May's median monthly rent is $1,009. It is approximately $100 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $53 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $33 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
