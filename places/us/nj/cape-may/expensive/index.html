Home prices

The median home in Cape May costs $652,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $305,240 (87%) higher. It is approximately $435,400 (200%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $489,900 (300%) higher.

Rents

In Cape May the median rent is $1,009 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $100 (9%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $53 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $33 (3%) lower.
