Compared to other places in this area, Cape May is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cape May is $652,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $305,240 (87%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $435,400 (200%) higher, and is about $489,900 (300%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cape May is $1,009. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $100 (9%) lower.It is approximately $53 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $33 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
