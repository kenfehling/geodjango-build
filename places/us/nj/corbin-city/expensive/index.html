Home prices

The value of the median home in Corbin City is $257,100. It is approximately $43,700 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $39,600 (18%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $94,100 (57%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Corbin City's median rent is $1,286 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $16 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $224 (21%) higher, and it is approximately $244 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
