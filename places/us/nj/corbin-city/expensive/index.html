Compared to other places in this area, Corbin City is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Corbin City's median home value is $257,100. It is about $43,700 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $39,600 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $94,100 (57%) higher.

Rents

Corbin City's median rent is $1,286 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $16 (1%) lower. It is about $224 (21%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $244 (23%) higher.
