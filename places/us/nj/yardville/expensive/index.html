Home prices

Yardville's median home cost is $251,500. It is approximately $49,780 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $34,000 (15%) higher, and is about $354,500 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Yardville is $1,199. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $21 (1%) lower. It is about $137 (12%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $244 (16%) lower.
