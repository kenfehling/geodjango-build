Home prices

The median home in Keyport is valued at $264,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $40,120 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $46,700 (21%) higher, and it is approximately $341,800 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Keyport the median rent is $1,160 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $291 (20%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $98 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $283 (19%) lower.
