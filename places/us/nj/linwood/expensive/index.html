Home prices

The median home cost in Linwood is $290,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $128,540 (30%) lower. It is roughly $72,500 (33%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $316,000 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Linwood is $1,583 a month. This is roughly $158 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $521 (49%) higher, and is approximately $140 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
