Compared to other places in this area, Somerville is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Somerville costs $322,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $47,180 (12%) lower. It is about $104,500 (48%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $284,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Somerville is $1,388 a month. It is about $185 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $326 (30%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $55 (3%) lower compared to the city of New York.
