Compared to its surrounding areas, Somerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Somerville is valued at $322,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $47,180 (12%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $104,500 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $284,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Somerville the median monthly rent is $1,388. Compared to nearby areas it is about $185 (11%) lower.It is approximately $326 (30%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $55 (3%) lower.
