Home prices

The median home value in Somerville is $322,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $47,180 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $104,500 (48%) higher, and is roughly $284,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Somerville's median monthly rent is $1,388. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $185 (11%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $326 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $55 (3%) lower.
