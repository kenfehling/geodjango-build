Compared to its surrounding areas, Milford is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milford costs $259,000. It is approximately $21,520 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $41,500 (19%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $347,000 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Milford is $1,301 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $145 (12%) higher. It is approximately $239 (22%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $142 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
