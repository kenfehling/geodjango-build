Home prices

Rents

Lincoln Park's median rent is $1,643 per month. It is about $92 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $581 (54%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $200 (13%) higher.
