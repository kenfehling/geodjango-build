Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Park is valued at $330,900. This is approximately $6,140 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $113,400 (52%) higher, and is roughly $275,100 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Lincoln Park is $1,643 per month. This is roughly $92 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $581 (54%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $200 (13%) higher.
