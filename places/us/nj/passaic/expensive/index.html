In comparison to nearby areas, Passaic is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of New York however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Passaic is valued at $332,700. It is about $47,400 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $115,200 (52%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $273,300 (45%) lower.

Rents

Passaic's median monthly rent is $1,183. This is about $256 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $121 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $260 (18%) lower.
