Home prices

The median home in Passaic is valued at $332,700. It is roughly $47,400 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $115,200 (52%) higher, and is about $273,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Passaic's median rent is $1,183 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $256 (17%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $121 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $260 (18%) lower.
