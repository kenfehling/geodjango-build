Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Passaic is $1,183 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $256 (17%) lower.It is approximately $121 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $260 (18%) lower.
