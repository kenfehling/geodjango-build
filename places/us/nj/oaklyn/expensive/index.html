Compared to the surrounding areas, Oaklyn is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Oaklyn is valued at $186,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $29,060 (18%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $31,200 (14%) lower, and is roughly $419,700 (69%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Oaklyn's median rent is $917 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $108 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $145 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $526 (36%) lower.
