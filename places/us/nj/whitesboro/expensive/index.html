Rents

The median monthly rent in Whitesboro is $1,389. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $209 (17%) higher.It is roughly $327 (30%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $347 (33%) higher.
