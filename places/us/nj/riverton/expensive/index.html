Home prices

The median home in Riverton costs $300,500. It is approximately $38,360 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $83,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $305,500 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Riverton is $1,379 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $180 (15%) higher.It is approximately $317 (29%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $64 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
