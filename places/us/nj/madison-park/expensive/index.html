Home prices

Madison Park's median home price is $289,100. This is about $7,660 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $71,600 (32%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $316,900 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Madison Park's median monthly rent is $1,285. This is about $229 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $223 (20%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $158 (10%) lower.
