In comparison to other places in this area, Clifton is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Clifton is $345,400. This is roughly $92,920 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $127,900 (58%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $260,600 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clifton is $1,409 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $36 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $347 (32%) higher, and is roughly $34 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
