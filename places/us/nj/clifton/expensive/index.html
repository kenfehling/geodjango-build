Home prices

The median home in Clifton costs $345,400. This is approximately $92,920 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $127,900 (58%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $260,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Clifton the median rent is $1,409 a month. It is about $36 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $347 (32%) higher, and is approximately $34 (2%) lower compared to the city of New York.
