Home prices

The median home in Ridgewood costs $763,300. It is roughly $213,960 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $545,800 (250%) higher, and it is approximately $157,300 (25%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Ridgewood the median rent is $2,026 a month. It is about $113 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $964 (90%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $583 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
