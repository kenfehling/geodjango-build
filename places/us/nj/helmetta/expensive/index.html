Home prices

The median home in Helmetta costs $253,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $32,500 (11%) lower. It is about $35,800 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $352,700 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Helmetta is $1,770 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $508 (40%) higher. It is roughly $708 (66%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $327 (22%) higher compared to the city of New York.
