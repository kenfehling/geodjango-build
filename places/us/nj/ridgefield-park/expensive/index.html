Compared to other places in this area, Ridgefield Park is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Ridgefield Park is $349,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $85,360 (19%) lower. It is about $131,600 (60%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $256,900 (42%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Ridgefield Park the median rent is $1,368 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $90 (6%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $306 (28%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $75 (5%) lower.
