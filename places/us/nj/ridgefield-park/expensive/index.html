Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Ridgefield Park is $1,368 per month. It is approximately $90 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $306 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York is about $75 (5%) lower.
