Home prices

The median home value in Garfield is $338,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $36,260 (9%) lower. It is approximately $120,900 (55%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is about $267,600 (44%) lower.

Rents

Garfield's median rent is $1,326 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $49 (3%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $264 (24%) higher, and is roughly $117 (8%) lower compared to the city of New York.
