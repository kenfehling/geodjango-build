In comparison to other places in this area, Hackettstown is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hackettstown is $274,300. It is about $34,420 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $56,800 (26%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $331,700 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Hackettstown is $1,182 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $252 (17%) lower. It is roughly $120 (11%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $261 (18%) lower.
