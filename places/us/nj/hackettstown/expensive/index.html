Compared to its surrounding areas, Hackettstown is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Hackettstown's median home price is $274,300. This is roughly $34,420 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $56,800 (26%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $331,700 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Hackettstown the median monthly rent is $1,182. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $252 (17%) lower. It is approximately $120 (11%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $261 (18%) lower compared to the city of New York.
