Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hackettstown is $1,182. It is approximately $252 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $120 (11%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $261 (18%) lower compared to the city of New York.
