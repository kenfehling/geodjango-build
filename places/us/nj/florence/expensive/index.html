In comparison to nearby areas, Florence is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $181,900. This is approximately $12,480 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $35,600 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $424,100 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $1,044. This is roughly $82 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $18 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $399 (27%) lower.
