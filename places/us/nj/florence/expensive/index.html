In comparison to the surrounding areas, Florence is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Florence costs $181,900. It is roughly $12,480 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $35,600 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $424,100 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Florence is $1,044. It is roughly $82 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $18 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $399 (27%) lower.
