In comparison to its surrounding areas, Florence is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Florence is $181,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $12,480 (6%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $35,600 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $424,100 (69%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Florence the median monthly rent is $1,044. Compared to other places in this area it is about $82 (7%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $18 (1%) lower, and it is roughly $399 (27%) lower compared to the city of New York.
