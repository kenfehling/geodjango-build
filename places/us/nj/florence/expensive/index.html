In comparison to the surrounding areas, Florence is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $181,900. It is about $12,480 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $35,600 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $424,100 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Florence is $1,044 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $82 (7%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $18 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $399 (27%) lower.
