Compared to nearby areas, Martinsville is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home value in Martinsville is $559,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $215,980 (62%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $342,300 (157%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $46,200 (7%) lower.

Rents

Martinsville's median monthly rent is $1,657. This is about $209 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $595 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $214 (14%) higher.
