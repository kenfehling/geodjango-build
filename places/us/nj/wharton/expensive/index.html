Home prices

The median home in Wharton is valued at $299,600. It is about $17,200 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $82,100 (37%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $306,400 (50%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Wharton the median rent is $1,681 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $192 (12%) higher.It is approximately $619 (58%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $238 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
