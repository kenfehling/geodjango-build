Home prices

The median home cost in Wharton is $299,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $17,200 (6%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $82,100 (37%) higher, and it is about $306,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Wharton's median rent is $1,681 a month. This is roughly $192 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $619 (58%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $238 (16%) higher.
