Home prices

Rents

In Mount Ephraim the median monthly rent is $926. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $93 (9%) lower.It is about $136 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $517 (35%) lower.
