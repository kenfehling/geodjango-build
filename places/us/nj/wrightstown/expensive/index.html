Home prices

Rents

In Wrightstown the median rent is $946 per month. This is about $263 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $116 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $497 (34%) lower.
