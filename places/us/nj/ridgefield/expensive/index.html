Home prices

The price of the median home in Ridgefield is $442,500. This is approximately $14,560 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $225,000 (103%) higher, and it is approximately $163,500 (26%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Ridgefield is $1,584 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $58 (3%) lower. It is roughly $522 (49%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $141 (9%) higher.
