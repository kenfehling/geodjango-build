Compared to its surrounding areas, Beach Haven is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of New York, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Beach Haven is valued at $760,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $294,320 (63%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $542,600 (249%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $154,100 (25%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Beach Haven is $1,313 a month. It is roughly $71 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $251 (23%) higher, and it is approximately $130 (9%) lower compared to the city of New York.
