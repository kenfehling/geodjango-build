In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bloomingdale is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Bloomingdale is $303,300. This is approximately $75,520 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $85,800 (39%) higher, and it is roughly $302,700 (49%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Bloomingdale's median rent is $1,645 per month. It is about $73 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $583 (54%) higher, and it is roughly $202 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
