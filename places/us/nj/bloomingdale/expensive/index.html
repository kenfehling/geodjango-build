Home prices

The value of the median home in Bloomingdale is $303,300. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $75,520 (19%) lower.It is about $85,800 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $302,700 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Bloomingdale the median monthly rent is $1,645. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $73 (4%) lower.It is approximately $583 (54%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $202 (13%) higher.
