Home prices

The median home in Bloomingdale is valued at $303,300. This is approximately $75,520 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $85,800 (39%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $302,700 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomingdale is $1,645. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $73 (4%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $583 (54%) higher, and is roughly $202 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
