Home prices

Maywood's median home cost is $389,300. This is about $11,540 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $171,800 (78%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $216,700 (35%) lower.

Rents

Maywood's median rent is $1,601 per month. This is roughly $97 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $539 (50%) higher, and it is about $158 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
