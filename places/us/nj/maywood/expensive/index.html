Home prices

The median home in Maywood is valued at $389,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $11,540 (3%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $171,800 (78%) higher, and is about $216,700 (35%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Maywood the median monthly rent is $1,601. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $97 (6%) higher. It is about $539 (50%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $158 (10%) higher.
