Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Maywood is $1,601. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $97 (6%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $539 (50%) higher, and is approximately $158 (10%) higher compared to the city of New York.
