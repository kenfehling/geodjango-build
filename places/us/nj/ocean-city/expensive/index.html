Compared to its nearby areas, Ocean City is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Ocean City is $574,100. This is approximately $145,460 (33%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $356,600 (163%) higher, and is about $411,100 (252%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Ocean City is $1,264 a month. It is about $159 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $202 (19%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $222 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
