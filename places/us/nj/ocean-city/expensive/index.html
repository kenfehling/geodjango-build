In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ocean City is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Ocean City is $574,100. This is about $145,460 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $356,600 (163%) higher, and it is roughly $411,100 (252%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Ocean City is $1,264 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $159 (11%) lower. It is about $202 (19%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $222 (21%) higher.
