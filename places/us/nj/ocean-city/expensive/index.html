Home prices

The price of the median home in Ocean City is $574,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $145,460 (33%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $356,600 (163%) higher, and is about $411,100 (252%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Ocean City the median monthly rent is $1,264. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $159 (11%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $202 (19%) higher, and it is about $222 (21%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
