Home prices

The price of the median home in Rio Grande is $266,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $30,000 (12%) higher.It is roughly $48,900 (22%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $103,400 (63%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rio Grande is $1,111 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $73 (6%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $49 (4%) higher, and is approximately $69 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
