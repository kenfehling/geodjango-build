In comparison to other places in this area, Secaucus is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Secaucus's median home value is $460,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $113,740 (32%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $243,300 (111%) higher, and is roughly $145,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Secaucus the median monthly rent is $1,689. It is roughly $305 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $627 (59%) higher, and it is roughly $246 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.
