Home prices

The median home price in Secaucus is $460,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $113,740 (32%) higher.It is about $243,300 (111%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $145,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Secaucus the median rent is $1,689 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $305 (22%) higher.It is approximately $627 (59%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $246 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
