Home prices

The median home in Secaucus is valued at $460,800. It is roughly $113,740 (32%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $243,300 (111%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $145,200 (23%) lower.

Rents

In Secaucus the median monthly rent is $1,689. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $305 (22%) higher.It is roughly $627 (59%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $246 (17%) higher.
