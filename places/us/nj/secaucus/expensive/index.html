In comparison to its surrounding areas, Secaucus is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Secaucus is valued at $460,800. It is about $113,740 (32%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $243,300 (111%) higher, and is roughly $145,200 (23%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Secaucus the median monthly rent is $1,689. This is roughly $305 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $627 (59%) higher, and is roughly $246 (17%) higher compared to the city of New York.
