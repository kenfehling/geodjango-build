Compared to its surrounding areas, Union City is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Union City's median home cost is $340,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $100,240 (22%) lower.It is about $123,300 (56%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $265,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Union City the median rent is $1,225 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $403 (24%) lower.It is approximately $163 (15%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $218 (15%) lower.
