Home prices

Hopatcong's median home price is $237,000. It is approximately $54,360 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $19,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $369,000 (60%) lower.

Rents

In Hopatcong the median rent is $1,456 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $24 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $394 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is is very similar.
