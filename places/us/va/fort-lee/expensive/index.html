Compared to the surrounding areas, Fort Lee is substantially more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Fort Lee is $225,000. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $78,120 (53%) higher. It is about $7,500 (3%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $55,800 (19%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

Fort Lee's median rent is $1,697 per month. It is about $722 (74%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $635 (59%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $330 (24%) higher.
