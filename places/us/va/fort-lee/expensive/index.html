Home prices

The median home in Fort Lee costs $225,000. It is roughly $78,120 (53%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $7,500 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $55,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

Fort Lee's median monthly rent is $1,697. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $722 (74%) higher. It is approximately $635 (59%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $330 (24%) higher compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
