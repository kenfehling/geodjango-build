Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Lansdowne is $1,873 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $232 (11%) lower.It is approximately $811 (76%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $332 (21%) higher.
