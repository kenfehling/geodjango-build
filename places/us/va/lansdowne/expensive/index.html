Home prices

Lansdowne's median home value is $527,100. It is about $49,540 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $309,600 (142%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $74,400 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Lansdowne the median rent is $1,873 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $232 (11%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $811 (76%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $332 (21%) higher.
