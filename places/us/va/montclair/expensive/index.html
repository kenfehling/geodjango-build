Compared to its surrounding areas, Montclair is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Montclair is $414,200. This is roughly $98,900 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $196,700 (90%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $187,300 (31%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Montclair the median monthly rent is $2,089. This is approximately $363 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $1,027 (96%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $548 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
