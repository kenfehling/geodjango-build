Home prices

The median home in Kings Park is valued at $527,200. This is roughly $9,980 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $309,700 (142%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $74,300 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kings Park is $2,624 per month. It is approximately $578 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $1,562 (147%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $1,083 (70%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
