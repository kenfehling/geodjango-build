Home prices

The median home in Kings Park is valued at $527,200. It is approximately $9,980 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $309,700 (142%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $74,300 (12%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Kings Park's median rent is $2,624 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $578 (28%) higher. It is roughly $1,562 (147%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $1,083 (70%) higher.
