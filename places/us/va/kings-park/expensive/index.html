Home prices

The median home in Kings Park costs $527,200. This is roughly $9,980 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $309,700 (142%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $74,300 (12%) lower.

Rents

Kings Park's median rent is $2,624 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $578 (28%) higher. It is approximately $1,562 (147%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $1,083 (70%) higher.
