Home prices

The median home in Mount Sidney costs $216,000. It is approximately $24,560 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and is about $55,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

In Mount Sidney the median monthly rent is $528. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $363 (40%) lower. It is approximately $534 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $329 (38%) lower compared to the city of Lynchburg.
