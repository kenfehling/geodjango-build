Home prices

The median home in Mount Sidney is valued at $216,000. This is approximately $24,560 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and is approximately $55,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Sidney is $528. It is about $363 (40%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $534 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg is roughly $329 (38%) lower.
