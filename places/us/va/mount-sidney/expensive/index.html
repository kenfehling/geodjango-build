In comparison to other places in this area, Mount Sidney is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Sidney is valued at $216,000. It is approximately $24,560 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $55,900 (34%) higher compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

In Mount Sidney the median monthly rent is $528. This is roughly $363 (40%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $534 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is about $329 (38%) lower.
