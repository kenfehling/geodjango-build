Home prices

The median home in Mount Sidney is valued at $216,000. It is roughly $24,560 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $55,900 (34%) higher compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Sidney is $528. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $363 (40%) lower. It is about $534 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is roughly $329 (38%) lower.
