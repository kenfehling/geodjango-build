Compared to its surrounding areas, Belmont is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Washington, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Belmont is $711,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $232,680 (48%) higher. It is about $493,900 (227%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $109,900 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Belmont is $3,229. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $1,378 (74%) higher. It is about $2,167 (204%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $1,688 (109%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
