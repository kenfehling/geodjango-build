Home prices

The value of the median home in Wyndham is $493,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $187,560 (61%) higher. It is about $275,700 (126%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $108,300 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Wyndham is $1,696 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $435 (34%) higher. It is about $634 (59%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $155 (10%) higher.
