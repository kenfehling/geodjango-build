Home prices

The price of the median home in Wyndham is $493,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $187,560 (61%) higher.It is roughly $275,700 (126%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $108,300 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Wyndham's median rent is $1,696 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $435 (34%) higher.It is about $634 (59%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $155 (10%) higher.
