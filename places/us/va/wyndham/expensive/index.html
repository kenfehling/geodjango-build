Home prices

The median home in Wyndham is valued at $493,200. It is approximately $187,560 (61%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $275,700 (126%) higher, and is roughly $108,300 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Wyndham's median rent is $1,696 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $435 (34%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $634 (59%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $155 (10%) higher.
