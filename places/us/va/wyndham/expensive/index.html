In comparison to other places in this area, Wyndham is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wyndham is $493,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $187,560 (61%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $275,700 (126%) higher, and it is approximately $108,300 (18%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Wyndham's median monthly rent is $1,696. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $435 (34%) higher. It is approximately $634 (59%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $155 (10%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
