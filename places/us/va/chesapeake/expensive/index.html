Compared to other places in this area, Chesapeake is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Chesapeake is $273,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $33,660 (14%) higher. It is roughly $56,200 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

Chesapeake's median rent is $1,279 per month. It is approximately $40 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $217 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $88 (6%) lower.
