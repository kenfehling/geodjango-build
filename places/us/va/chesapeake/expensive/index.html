Compared to its surrounding areas, Chesapeake is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

Chesapeake's median home price is $273,700. This is approximately $33,660 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $56,200 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chesapeake is $1,279. This is approximately $40 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $217 (20%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $88 (6%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
