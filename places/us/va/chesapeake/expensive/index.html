Home prices

Chesapeake's median home value is $273,700. It is approximately $33,660 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $56,200 (25%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chesapeake is $1,279. It is approximately $40 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $217 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $88 (6%) lower.
