In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chesapeake is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

The median home in Chesapeake is valued at $273,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $33,660 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $56,200 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

In Chesapeake the median monthly rent is $1,279. This is roughly $40 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $217 (20%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $88 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
