In comparison to other places in this area, Chesapeake is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Chesapeake is $273,700. It is approximately $33,660 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $56,200 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $7,100 (2%) lower.

Rents

In Chesapeake the median rent is $1,279 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $40 (3%) higher.It is approximately $217 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is about $88 (6%) lower.
