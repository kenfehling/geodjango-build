In comparison to nearby areas, Hampton is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hampton costs $186,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $42,580 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $30,800 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $94,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

Hampton's median monthly rent is $1,118. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $151 (11%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $56 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $249 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
