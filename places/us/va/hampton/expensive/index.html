Compared to the surrounding areas, Hampton is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beachhowever, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Hampton's median home price is $186,700. It is approximately $42,580 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $30,800 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $94,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hampton is $1,118 per month. It is approximately $151 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $56 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $249 (18%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
