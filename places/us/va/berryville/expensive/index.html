Compared to the surrounding areas, Berryville is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Berryville is $304,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $24,940 (8%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $87,400 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $296,600 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Berryville is $1,099. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $121 (9%) lower. It is approximately $37 (3%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $442 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
