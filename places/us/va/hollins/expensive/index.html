Home prices

The price of the median home in Hollins is $173,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $34,480 (16%) lower.It is roughly $44,300 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro it is roughly $16,900 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Hollins the median monthly rent is $939. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $85 (9%) higher.It is about $123 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $62 (7%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.
