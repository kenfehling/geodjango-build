In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hollins is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Greensboro.

Home prices

The median home value in Hollins is $173,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $34,480 (16%) lower.It is approximately $44,300 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $16,900 (10%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

The median rent in Hollins is $939 a month. It is approximately $85 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $123 (11%) lower, and is approximately $62 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
