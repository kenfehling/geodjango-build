Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Paul is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Johnson City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in St. Paul is $141,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $59,880 (73%) higher. It is approximately $75,700 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Johnson City it is about $25,700 (15%) lower.

Rents

St. Paul's median rent is $415 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $96 (18%) lower. It is approximately $647 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $340 (45%) lower compared to the city of Johnson City.
