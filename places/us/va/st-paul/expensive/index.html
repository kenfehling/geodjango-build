Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Paul is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Johnson City.

Home prices

St. Paul's median home cost is $141,800. It is approximately $59,880 (73%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $75,700 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Johnson City it is about $25,700 (15%) lower.

Rents

In St. Paul the median rent is $415 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $96 (18%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $647 (60%) lower, and is approximately $340 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Johnson City.
