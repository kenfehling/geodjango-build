Home prices

The median home in St. Paul costs $141,800. This is about $59,880 (73%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $75,700 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Johnson City it is approximately $25,700 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in St. Paul is $415 per month. It is about $96 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $647 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Johnson City it is about $340 (45%) lower.
