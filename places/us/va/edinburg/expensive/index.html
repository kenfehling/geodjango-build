In comparison to the surrounding areas, Edinburg is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Frederick.

Home prices

The median home in Edinburg costs $149,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $31,460 (17%) lower. It is roughly $67,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Frederick it is about $120,600 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Edinburg is $797 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $141 (15%) lower. It is roughly $265 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Frederick is about $599 (42%) lower.
