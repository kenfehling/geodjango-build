Home prices

The median home cost in Edinburg is $149,800. It is about $31,460 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $67,700 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Frederick is roughly $120,600 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Edinburg is $797 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $141 (15%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $265 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Frederick is roughly $599 (42%) lower.
