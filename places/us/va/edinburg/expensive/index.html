Home prices

The median home in Edinburg costs $149,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $31,460 (17%) lower.It is about $67,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $120,600 (44%) lower compared to the city of Frederick.

Rents

Edinburg's median monthly rent is $797. It is approximately $141 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $265 (24%) lower, and is approximately $599 (42%) lower compared to the city of Frederick.
