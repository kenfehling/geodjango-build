Compared to its nearby areas, New Market is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Charlottesville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Market costs $233,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $71,680 (44%) higher. It is roughly $15,800 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $66,300 (22%) lower compared to the city of Charlottesville.

Rents

New Market's median monthly rent is $725. This is roughly $73 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlottesville is roughly $417 (36%) lower.
