Compared to the surrounding areas, New Market is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Charlottesville, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Market is valued at $233,300. This is roughly $71,680 (44%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $15,800 (7%) higher, and is about $66,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlottesville.

Rents

New Market's median rent is $725 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $73 (9%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlottesville it is roughly $417 (36%) lower.
