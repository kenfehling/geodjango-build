Home prices

The median home in New Market is valued at $233,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $71,680 (44%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $15,800 (7%) higher, and it is about $66,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlottesville.

Rents

New Market's median monthly rent is $725. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $73 (9%) lower.It is approximately $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $417 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlottesville.
