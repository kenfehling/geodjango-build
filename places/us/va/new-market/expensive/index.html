Home prices

The median home in New Market costs $233,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $71,680 (44%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $15,800 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Charlottesville it is roughly $66,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Market is $725. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $73 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $337 (31%) lower, and it is about $417 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlottesville.
