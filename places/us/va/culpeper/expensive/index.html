The price of the median home in Culpeper is $263,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $2,880 (1%) lower. It is roughly $46,100 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $337,900 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Culpeper is $1,153. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $55 (4%) lower. It is about $91 (8%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $388 (25%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
