Home prices

The median home cost in Culpeper is $263,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $2,880 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $46,100 (21%) higher, and it is about $337,900 (56%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Culpeper's median rent is $1,153 per month. It is roughly $55 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $91 (8%) higher, and it is approximately $388 (25%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
