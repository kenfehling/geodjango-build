Home prices

McLean's median home price is $1,031,200. It is approximately $283,860 (37%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $813,700 (374%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $429,700 (71%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in McLean is $3,065. This is roughly $853 (38%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $2,003 (188%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $1,524 (98%) higher.
