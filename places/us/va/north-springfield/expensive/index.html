Home prices

The price of the median home in North Springfield is $474,300. This is about $11,920 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $256,800 (118%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $127,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In North Springfield the median monthly rent is $2,008. This is approximately $66 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $946 (89%) higher, and is about $467 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
