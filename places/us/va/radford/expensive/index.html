Home prices

Radford's median home price is $170,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $6,580 (4%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $46,700 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $14,500 (9%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Radford's median monthly rent is $728. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $50 (6%) lower. It is roughly $334 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro it is roughly $149 (16%) lower.
