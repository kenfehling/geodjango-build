Home prices

The value of the median home in Chester is $221,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $59,700 (36%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $4,400 (2%) higher, and it is about $8,600 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Richmond.

Rents

In Chester the median rent is $1,197 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $103 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $135 (12%) higher, and is roughly $172 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.
