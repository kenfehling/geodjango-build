In comparison to other places in this area, Franklin is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Franklin's median home cost is $164,100. This is approximately $40,080 (32%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $53,400 (24%) lower, and it is about $116,700 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

In Franklin the median monthly rent is $860. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $61 (7%) higher.It is approximately $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $507 (37%) lower.
