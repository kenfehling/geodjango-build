Home prices

The median home price in Franklin is $164,100. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $40,080 (32%) higher.It is roughly $53,400 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $116,700 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Franklin the median rent is $860 per month. It is roughly $61 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $202 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $507 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
