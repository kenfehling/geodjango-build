Compared to its surrounding areas, Franklin is a lot more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

The median home in Franklin costs $164,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $40,080 (32%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $53,400 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $116,700 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Franklin is $860 per month. This is approximately $61 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $202 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $507 (37%) lower.
