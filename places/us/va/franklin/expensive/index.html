Compared to other places in this area, Franklin is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Franklin is $164,100. This is roughly $40,080 (32%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $53,400 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $116,700 (41%) lower.

Rents

Franklin's median rent is $860 per month. This is approximately $61 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $202 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $507 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
