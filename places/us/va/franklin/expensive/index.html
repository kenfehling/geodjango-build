Home prices

The median home in Franklin is valued at $164,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $40,080 (32%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $53,400 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $116,700 (41%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

Franklin's median rent is $860 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $61 (7%) higher. It is about $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $507 (37%) lower.
