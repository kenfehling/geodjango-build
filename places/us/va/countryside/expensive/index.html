Home prices

The median home value in Countryside is $428,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $19,640 (4%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $210,800 (96%) higher, and it is about $173,200 (28%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Countryside the median rent is $1,889 a month. It is approximately $138 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $827 (77%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $348 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
