Home prices

The median home cost in Elkton is $183,400. It is approximately $56,920 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $34,100 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $23,300 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

The median rent in Elkton is $811 per month. It is about $217 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $251 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $46 (5%) lower compared to the city of Lynchburg.
