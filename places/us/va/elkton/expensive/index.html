Rents

In Elkton the median rent is $811 a month. This is approximately $217 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $251 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $46 (5%) lower compared to the city of Lynchburg.
