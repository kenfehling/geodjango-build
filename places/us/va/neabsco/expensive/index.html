Home prices

The median home cost in Neabsco is $381,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $67,780 (21%) higher.It is approximately $163,500 (75%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $220,500 (36%) lower.

Rents

Neabsco's median monthly rent is $1,884. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $125 (7%) higher.It is about $822 (77%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $343 (22%) higher.
