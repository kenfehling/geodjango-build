Compared to the surrounding areas, Neabsco is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Neabsco is $381,000. This is roughly $67,780 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $163,500 (75%) higher, and is roughly $220,500 (36%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Neabsco's median rent is $1,884 per month. This is roughly $125 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $822 (77%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $343 (22%) higher.
