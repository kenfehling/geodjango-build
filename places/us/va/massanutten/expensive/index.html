Home prices

Massanutten's median home value is $260,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $29,640 (12%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $43,200 (19%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg is roughly $100,600 (62%) higher.

Rents

Massanutten's median rent is $1,142 per month. This is roughly $210 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $80 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $285 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.
