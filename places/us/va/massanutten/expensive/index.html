Home prices

The median home cost in Massanutten is $260,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $29,640 (12%) higher.It is roughly $43,200 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Lynchburg it is approximately $100,600 (62%) higher.

Rents

Massanutten's median monthly rent is $1,142. It is roughly $210 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $80 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Lynchburg is approximately $285 (33%) higher.
