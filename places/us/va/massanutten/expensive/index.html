Rents

Massanutten's median rent is $1,142 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $210 (22%) higher. It is roughly $80 (7%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is about $285 (33%) higher.
