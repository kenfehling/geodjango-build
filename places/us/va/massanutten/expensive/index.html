Rents

Massanutten's median rent is $1,142 per month. This is roughly $210 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $80 (7%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is roughly $285 (33%) higher.
