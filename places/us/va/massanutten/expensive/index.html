Compared to its surrounding areas, Massanutten is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Home prices

The median home price in Massanutten is $260,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $29,640 (12%) higher. It is approximately $43,200 (19%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg is approximately $100,600 (62%) higher.

Rents

In Massanutten the median rent is $1,142 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $210 (22%) higher. It is approximately $80 (7%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lynchburg is approximately $285 (33%) higher.
