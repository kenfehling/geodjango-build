Compared to its surrounding areas, Halifax is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Raleigh, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Halifax's median home cost is $157,600. It is roughly $50,620 (47%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is about $90,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

Halifax's median monthly rent is $675. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $32 (4%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $387 (36%) lower, and it is about $446 (39%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
