Home prices

Rents

Remington's median rent is $1,101 per month. It is roughly $225 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $39 (3%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $440 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
