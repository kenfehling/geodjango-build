Home prices

The median home value in Harrisonburg is $203,600. It is roughly $65,100 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $13,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $43,500 (27%) higher compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

In Harrisonburg the median rent is $883 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $128 (12%) lower.It is about $179 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is approximately $26 (3%) higher.
