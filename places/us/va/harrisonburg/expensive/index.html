Home prices

The median home in Harrisonburg costs $203,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $65,100 (24%) lower. It is roughly $13,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lynchburg it is about $43,500 (27%) higher.

Rents

Harrisonburg's median monthly rent is $883. This is approximately $128 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $179 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is approximately $26 (3%) higher.
