Home prices

The median home value in Harrisonburg is $203,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $65,100 (24%) lower. It is about $13,900 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $43,500 (27%) higher compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

The median rent in Harrisonburg is $883 per month. It is roughly $128 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $179 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $26 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.
