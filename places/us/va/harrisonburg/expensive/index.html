In comparison to its surrounding areas, Harrisonburg is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Harrisonburg is $203,600. It is approximately $65,100 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $13,900 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $43,500 (27%) higher compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

Harrisonburg's median monthly rent is $883. This is approximately $128 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $179 (16%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lynchburg is roughly $26 (3%) higher.
