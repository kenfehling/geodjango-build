Compared to other places in this area, Harrisonburg is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Lynchburg however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Harrisonburg is $203,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $65,100 (24%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $13,900 (6%) lower, and is roughly $43,500 (27%) higher compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

In Harrisonburg the median rent is $883 per month. This is approximately $128 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $179 (16%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Lynchburg is approximately $26 (3%) higher.
