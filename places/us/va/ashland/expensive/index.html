Compared to nearby areas, Ashland is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Ashland is $191,900. This is about $112,020 (36%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $25,600 (11%) lower, and is about $409,600 (68%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Ashland's median monthly rent is $992. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $320 (24%) lower. It is approximately $70 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $549 (35%) lower.
