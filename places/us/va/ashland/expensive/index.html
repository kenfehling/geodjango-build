Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Ashland is $992 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $320 (24%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $70 (6%) lower, and is roughly $549 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
