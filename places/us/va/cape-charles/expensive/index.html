Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cape Charles is $345,500. This is approximately $165,340 (91%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $128,000 (58%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $64,700 (23%) higher.

Rents

Cape Charles's median rent is $618 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $170 (21%) lower. It is roughly $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $749 (54%) lower.
