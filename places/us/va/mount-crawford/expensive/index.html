Home prices

Mount Crawford's median home price is $224,100. This is about $35,620 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $6,600 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $64,000 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Crawford is $925. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $50 (5%) lower. It is approximately $137 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg is approximately $68 (7%) higher.
