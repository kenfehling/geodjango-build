Home prices

The median home cost in Floris is $700,900. It is roughly $206,260 (41%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $483,400 (222%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $99,400 (16%) higher compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Floris's median rent is $2,714 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $660 (32%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $1,652 (155%) higher, and it is about $1,173 (76%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
