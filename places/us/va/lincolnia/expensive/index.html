Home prices

The median home in Lincolnia costs $474,700. This is approximately $17,460 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $257,200 (118%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $126,800 (21%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Lincolnia's median rent is $1,820 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $758 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $279 (18%) higher.
