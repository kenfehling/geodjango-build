Compared to other places in this area, Madison Heights is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Roanoke.

Home prices

The median home in Madison Heights is valued at $138,400. It is roughly $35,180 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $79,100 (36%) lower, and is about $3,300 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

In Madison Heights the median rent is $690 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $185 (21%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $372 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is about $124 (15%) lower.
