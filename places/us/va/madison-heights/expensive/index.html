Rents

The median rent in Madison Heights is $690 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $185 (21%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $372 (35%) lower, and is about $124 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
