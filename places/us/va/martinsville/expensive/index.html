Home prices

The median home in Martinsville is valued at $87,700. It is roughly $26,660 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $129,800 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $68,600 (43%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

The median rent in Martinsville is $656 per month. It is roughly $42 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $406 (38%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $221 (25%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.
