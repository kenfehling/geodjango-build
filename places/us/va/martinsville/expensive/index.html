Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Martinsville is $656. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $42 (5%) lower. It is roughly $406 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $221 (25%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.
