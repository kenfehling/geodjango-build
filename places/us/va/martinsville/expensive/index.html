Home prices

The median home price in Martinsville is $87,700. Compared to nearby areas this is about $26,660 (23%) lower.It is roughly $129,800 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $68,600 (43%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Martinsville's median rent is $656 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $42 (5%) lower.It is roughly $406 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $221 (25%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.
