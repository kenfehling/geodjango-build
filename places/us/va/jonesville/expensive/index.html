Compared to the surrounding areas, Jonesville is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Jonesville is $103,100. It is about $22,620 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $114,400 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $33,200 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Jonesville the median rent is $389 per month. This is about $93 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $673 (63%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $456 (53%) lower.
