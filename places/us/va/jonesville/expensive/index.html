Home prices

Jonesville's median home cost is $103,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $22,620 (28%) higher.It is approximately $114,400 (52%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $33,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Jonesville's median rent is $389 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $93 (19%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $673 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $456 (53%) lower.
