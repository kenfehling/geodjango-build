Home prices

The median home in Jonesville is valued at $103,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $22,620 (28%) higher. It is about $114,400 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $33,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Jonesville the median monthly rent is $389. It is about $93 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $673 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $456 (53%) lower.
