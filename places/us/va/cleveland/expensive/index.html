Compared to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Johnson City, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Cleveland is $63,300. It is about $35,700 (36%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $154,200 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $104,200 (62%) lower compared to the city of Johnson City.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $330 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $228 (40%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $732 (68%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Johnson City it is approximately $425 (56%) lower.
