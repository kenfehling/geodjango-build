Home prices

The median home value in Bassett is $96,700. This is roughly $5,580 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $120,800 (55%) lower, and is about $59,600 (38%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

In Bassett the median monthly rent is $579. This is about $143 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $483 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $298 (33%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.
