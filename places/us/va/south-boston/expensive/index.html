Home prices

The median home cost in South Boston is $132,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $20,260 (18%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $85,200 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is about $116,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

South Boston's median monthly rent is $671. This is about $37 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $391 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $450 (40%) lower.
