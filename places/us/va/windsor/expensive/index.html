Home prices

The median home in Windsor is valued at $172,600. This is about $11,280 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $108,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Windsor the median rent is $903 per month. It is approximately $96 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $159 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $464 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
