Home prices

The median home in Windsor costs $172,600. This is roughly $11,280 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $44,900 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $108,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

Windsor's median monthly rent is $903. This is about $96 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $159 (14%) lower, and is approximately $464 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
