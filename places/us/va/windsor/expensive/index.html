Compared to nearby areas, Windsor is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

The median home cost in Windsor is $172,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,280 (6%) lower. It is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $108,200 (38%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

Windsor's median rent is $903 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $96 (9%) lower. It is approximately $159 (14%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $464 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
