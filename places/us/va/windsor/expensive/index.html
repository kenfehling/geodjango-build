Compared to other places in this area, Windsor is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Windsor costs $172,600. This is approximately $11,280 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $44,900 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $108,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Windsor the median rent is $903 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $96 (9%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $159 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $464 (33%) lower.
