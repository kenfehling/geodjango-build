Compared to its surrounding areas, Collinsville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Greensboro, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Collinsville is $102,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $11,940 (13%) higher.It is approximately $115,500 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is approximately $54,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Collinsville is $637. This is about $74 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $425 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro it is approximately $240 (27%) lower.
