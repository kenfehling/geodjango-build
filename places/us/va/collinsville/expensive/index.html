Home prices

The median home in Collinsville is valued at $102,000. It is approximately $11,940 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $115,500 (53%) lower, and it is about $54,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Collinsville's median rent is $637 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $74 (10%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $425 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is approximately $240 (27%) lower.
