Compared to the surrounding areas, Collinsville is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Greensboro.

Home prices

The median home in Collinsville is valued at $102,000. It is about $11,940 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $115,500 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is about $54,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

Collinsville's median rent is $637 a month. This is roughly $74 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $425 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Greensboro is approximately $240 (27%) lower.
