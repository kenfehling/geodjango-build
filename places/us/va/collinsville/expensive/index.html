Home prices

Rents

In Collinsville the median monthly rent is $637. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $74 (10%) lower. It is approximately $425 (40%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is approximately $240 (27%) lower.
