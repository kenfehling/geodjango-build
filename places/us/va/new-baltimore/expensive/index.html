Home prices

The median home in New Baltimore costs $474,400. It is approximately $51,000 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $256,900 (118%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is about $127,100 (21%) lower.

Rents

In New Baltimore the median monthly rent is $2,155. This is approximately $364 (20%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $1,093 (102%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $614 (39%) higher.
