Compared to the surrounding areas, New Baltimore is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Baltimore costs $474,400. This is approximately $51,000 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $256,900 (118%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $127,100 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Baltimore is $2,155. It is about $364 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $1,093 (102%) higher, and is about $614 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
