Home prices

The price of the median home in Bridgewater is $234,300. This is about $23,380 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $16,800 (7%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lynchburg it is roughly $74,200 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Bridgewater the median rent is $896 per month. It is about $85 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $166 (15%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg is approximately $39 (4%) higher.
