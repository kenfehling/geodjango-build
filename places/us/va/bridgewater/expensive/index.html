Compared to other places in this area, Bridgewater is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Lynchburg, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bridgewater is valued at $234,300. It is about $23,380 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $16,800 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Lynchburg it is approximately $74,200 (46%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bridgewater is $896 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $85 (8%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $166 (15%) lower, and is roughly $39 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.
