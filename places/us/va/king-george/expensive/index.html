Home prices

The median home cost in King George is $276,100. This is approximately $5,460 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $58,600 (26%) higher, and it is approximately $325,400 (54%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In King George the median monthly rent is $1,315. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $211 (19%) higher. It is approximately $253 (23%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $226 (14%) lower.
