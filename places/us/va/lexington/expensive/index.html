Home prices

The median home in Lexington costs $235,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $119,240 (102%) higher. It is approximately $18,200 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Roanoke is approximately $100,600 (74%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lexington is $788. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $13 (1%) higher. It is roughly $274 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $26 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
