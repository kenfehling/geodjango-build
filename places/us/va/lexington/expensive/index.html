Home prices

The value of the median home in Lexington is $235,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $119,240 (102%) higher. It is roughly $18,200 (8%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $100,600 (74%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lexington is $788. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is about $26 (3%) lower.
