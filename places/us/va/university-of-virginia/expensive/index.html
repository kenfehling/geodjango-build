Home prices

University of Virginia's median home cost is $397,200. This is about $99,660 (33%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $179,700 (82%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $166,700 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.

Rents

University of Virginia's median monthly rent is $1,126. This is about $176 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $64 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Richmond is about $101 (9%) higher.
