Compared to other places in this area, University of Virginia is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Richmond.

Home prices

The median home in University of Virginia costs $397,200. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $99,660 (33%) higher. It is roughly $179,700 (82%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Richmond is roughly $166,700 (72%) higher.

Rents

University of Virginia's median monthly rent is $1,126. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $176 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $64 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Richmond is approximately $101 (9%) higher.
