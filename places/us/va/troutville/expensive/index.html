Home prices

The median home in Troutville is valued at $170,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $61,380 (26%) lower. It is approximately $47,000 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $14,200 (9%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Troutville's median rent is $859 per month. It is approximately $48 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $203 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Greensboro is approximately $18 (2%) lower.
