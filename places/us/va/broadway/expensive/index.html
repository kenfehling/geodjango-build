Home prices

The median home in Broadway costs $196,300. This is approximately $23,880 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $21,200 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $36,200 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

Broadway's median rent is $743 a month. It is about $88 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $319 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $114 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.
