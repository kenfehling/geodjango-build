Home prices

The value of the median home in Blue Ridge is $189,900. It is approximately $25,400 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $27,600 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro it is approximately $33,600 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Blue Ridge the median rent is $725 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $154 (17%) lower.It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is approximately $152 (17%) lower.
