Home prices

Franconia's median home value is $421,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $56,160 (11%) lower.It is about $203,600 (93%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $180,400 (29%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Franconia's median rent is $2,066 per month. It is about $104 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $1,004 (94%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $525 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
