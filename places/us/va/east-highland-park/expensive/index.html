Home prices

The value of the median home in East Highland Park is $150,700. This is about $32,240 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $66,800 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Richmond is roughly $79,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in East Highland Park is $1,053 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $38 (3%) lower. It is is very similar in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $28 (2%) higher compared to the city of Richmond.
