Home prices

The median home in Crozet costs $378,800. This is about $151,840 (66%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $161,300 (74%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $148,300 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.

Rents

The median rent in Crozet is $1,437 per month. This is approximately $524 (57%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $375 (35%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Richmond it is approximately $412 (40%) higher.
