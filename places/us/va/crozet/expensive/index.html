Crozet's median home price is $378,800. It is about $151,840 (66%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $161,300 (74%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $148,300 (64%) higher compared to the city of Richmond.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Crozet is $1,437. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $524 (57%) higher. It is about $375 (35%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Richmond it is roughly $412 (40%) higher.
