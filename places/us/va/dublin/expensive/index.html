Home prices

The median home in Dublin costs $134,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $7,020 (4%) lower. It is approximately $82,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $21,400 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Dublin's median monthly rent is $691. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $35 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $371 (34%) lower, and is roughly $186 (21%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.
