Home prices

The price of the median home in Dublin is $134,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $7,020 (4%) lower. It is about $82,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $21,400 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Dublin's median rent is $691 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $35 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $371 (34%) lower, and it is about $186 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
