Home prices

The median home in Dublin costs $134,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $7,020 (4%) lower.It is roughly $82,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $21,400 (13%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

The median rent in Dublin is $691 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $35 (4%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $371 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is about $186 (21%) lower.
