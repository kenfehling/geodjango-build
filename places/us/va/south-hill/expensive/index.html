Compared to other places in this area, South Hill is substantially more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Raleigh, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Hill is $153,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $51,340 (50%) higher. It is roughly $63,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $94,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In South Hill the median rent is $731 a month. It is approximately $83 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $331 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $390 (34%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
