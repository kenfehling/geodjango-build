Home prices

The median home in South Hill costs $153,900. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $51,340 (50%) higher. It is roughly $63,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $94,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In South Hill the median rent is $731 a month. This is roughly $83 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $331 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $390 (34%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
