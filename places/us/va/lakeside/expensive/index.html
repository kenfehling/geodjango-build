Home prices

The median home price in Lakeside is $186,100. It is approximately $13,880 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $31,400 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Richmond is approximately $44,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Lakeside the median monthly rent is $1,012. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $163 (13%) lower. It is approximately $50 (4%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $13 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Richmond.
