In comparison to other places in this area, Tuckahoe is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Richmond, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tuckahoe is $313,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $33,140 (11%) higher.It is roughly $95,800 (44%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $82,800 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.

Rents

The median rent in Tuckahoe is $1,160 per month. This is about $106 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $98 (9%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $135 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.
