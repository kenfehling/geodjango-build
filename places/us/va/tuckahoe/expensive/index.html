Compared to its surrounding areas, Tuckahoe is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Richmond, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Tuckahoe is $313,300. This is about $33,140 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $95,800 (44%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $82,800 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.

Rents

Tuckahoe's median monthly rent is $1,160. It is roughly $106 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $98 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $135 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.
