In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tuckahoe is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Richmond, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tuckahoe costs $313,300. This is about $33,140 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $95,800 (44%) higher, and it is approximately $82,800 (35%) higher compared to the city of Richmond.

Rents

In Tuckahoe the median rent is $1,160 a month. It is about $106 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $98 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Richmond is roughly $135 (13%) higher.
