Home prices

The median home price in Loch Lomond is $326,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $34,140 (11%) higher. It is roughly $108,800 (50%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $275,200 (45%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Loch Lomond is $1,787 per month. This is approximately $178 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $725 (68%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $246 (15%) higher.
