In comparison to its surrounding areas, Blacksburg is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Greensboro.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Blacksburg is $298,400. It is about $115,500 (63%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $80,900 (37%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Greensboro is about $142,100 (90%) higher.

Rents

In Blacksburg the median monthly rent is $1,107. This is about $299 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $45 (4%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is approximately $230 (26%) higher.
