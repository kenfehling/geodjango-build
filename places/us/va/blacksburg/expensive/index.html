In comparison to other places in this area, Blacksburg is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Greensboro.

Home prices

The median home in Blacksburg costs $298,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $115,500 (63%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $80,900 (37%) higher, and compared to the city of Greensboro is about $142,100 (90%) higher.

Rents

In Blacksburg the median monthly rent is $1,107. This is roughly $299 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $45 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Greensboro is approximately $230 (26%) higher.
