Home prices

Mechanicsville's median home cost is $241,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $51,500 (27%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $23,900 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $360,100 (59%) lower.

Rents

Mechanicsville's median monthly rent is $1,202. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $78 (6%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $140 (13%) higher, and is roughly $339 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
