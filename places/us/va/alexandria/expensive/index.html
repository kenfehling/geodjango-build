In comparison to its surrounding areas, Alexandria is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Alexandria costs $572,900. This is approximately $94,920 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $355,400 (163%) higher, and is about $28,600 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alexandria is $1,747. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $20 (1%) higher. It is approximately $685 (64%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $206 (13%) higher.
