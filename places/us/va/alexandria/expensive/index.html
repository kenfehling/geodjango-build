In comparison to its surrounding areas, Alexandria is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Alexandria is $572,900. This is roughly $94,920 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $355,400 (163%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $28,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

Alexandria's median rent is $1,747 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $20 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $685 (64%) higher, and is about $206 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
