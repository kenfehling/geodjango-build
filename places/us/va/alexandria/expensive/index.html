Home prices

The median home cost in Alexandria is $572,900. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $102,720 (21%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $355,400 (163%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $28,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Alexandria the median monthly rent is $1,747. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $58 (3%) higher.It is roughly $685 (64%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $206 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
