Home prices

Alexandria's median home price is $572,900. It is approximately $102,720 (21%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $355,400 (163%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $28,600 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Alexandria's median monthly rent is $1,747. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $58 (3%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $685 (64%) higher, and it is about $206 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
