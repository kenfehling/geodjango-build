In comparison to the surrounding areas, Alexandria is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Alexandria's median home price is $572,900. This is roughly $102,720 (21%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $355,400 (163%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $28,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Alexandria is $1,747 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $58 (3%) higher.It is about $685 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $206 (13%) higher.
