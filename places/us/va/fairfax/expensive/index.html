In comparison to nearby areas, Fairfax is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home price in Fairfax is $560,400. It is about $76,760 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $342,900 (157%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $41,100 (6%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $1,834. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $114 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $772 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $293 (19%) higher.
