In comparison to its nearby areas, Fairfax is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Fairfax is $560,400. It is approximately $76,760 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $342,900 (157%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $41,100 (6%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Fairfax the median rent is $1,834 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $114 (5%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $772 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $293 (19%) higher.
