In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fairfax is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Fairfax is valued at $560,400. It is roughly $76,760 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $342,900 (157%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $41,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Fairfax's median monthly rent is $1,834. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $114 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $772 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $293 (19%) higher.
