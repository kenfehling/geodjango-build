Home prices

The median home price in Smithfield is $282,800. It is about $78,020 (38%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $65,300 (30%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

The median rent in Smithfield is $925 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $287 (23%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $137 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $442 (32%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
