Home prices

The median home in Luray costs $169,600. This is roughly $61,740 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $47,900 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $222,600 (56%) lower compared to the city of Gaithersburg.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Luray is $844. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $38 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $218 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Gaithersburg it is about $896 (51%) lower.
