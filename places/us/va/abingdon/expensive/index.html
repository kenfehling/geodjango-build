Compared to its surrounding areas, Abingdon is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The median home in Abingdon costs $169,500. It is approximately $29,240 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $48,000 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Asheville it is about $100,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Abingdon is $692. It is about $11 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $370 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $351 (33%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
