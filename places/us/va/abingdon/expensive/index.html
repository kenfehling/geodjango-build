Compared to its surrounding areas, Abingdon is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

Abingdon's median home price is $169,500. This is about $29,240 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $48,000 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is roughly $100,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Abingdon is $692. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $11 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $370 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville is about $351 (33%) lower.
