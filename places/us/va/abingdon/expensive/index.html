Home prices

The price of the median home in Abingdon is $169,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $29,240 (20%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $48,000 (22%) lower, and is approximately $100,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Abingdon the median rent is $692 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $11 (1%) higher.It is roughly $370 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $351 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
