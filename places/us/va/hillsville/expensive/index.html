Home prices

The median home price in Hillsville is $112,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $5,640 (4%) lower. It is approximately $105,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is roughly $43,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Hillsville the median monthly rent is $551. This is about $61 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $511 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Greensboro is roughly $326 (37%) lower.
