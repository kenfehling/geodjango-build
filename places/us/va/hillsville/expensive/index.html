Similar to other places in this area, it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Greensboro, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsville costs $112,400. This is approximately $5,640 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $105,100 (48%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is roughly $43,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

Hillsville's median rent is $551 a month. This is approximately $61 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $511 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $326 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
