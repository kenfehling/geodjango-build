Home prices

The value of the median home in Hillsville is $112,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $5,640 (4%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $105,100 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is roughly $43,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hillsville is $551 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $61 (9%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $511 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro it is about $326 (37%) lower.
