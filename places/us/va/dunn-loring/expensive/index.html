Home prices

The median home price in Dunn Loring is $741,600. It is about $139,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $524,100 (240%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $140,100 (23%) higher compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Dunn Loring the median monthly rent is $2,870. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $848 (41%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,808 (170%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $1,329 (86%) higher.
