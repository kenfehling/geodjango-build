Home prices

The median home in La Crosse is valued at $85,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $30,980 (26%) lower. It is approximately $132,200 (60%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $163,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

La Crosse's median rent is $680 a month. This is roughly $22 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $382 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $441 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
