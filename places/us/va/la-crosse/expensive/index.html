In comparison to other places in this area, La Crosse is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in La Crosse costs $85,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $30,980 (26%) lower. It is roughly $132,200 (60%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $163,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

In La Crosse the median monthly rent is $680. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $22 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $382 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $441 (39%) lower.
