Compared to other places in this area, La Crosse is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in La Crosse costs $85,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $30,980 (26%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $132,200 (60%) lower, and is about $163,000 (65%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in La Crosse is $680 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $22 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $382 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $441 (39%) lower.
