In comparison to other places in this area, Mount Vernon is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Mount Vernon is $627,900. This is roughly $123,560 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $410,400 (188%) higher, and is roughly $26,400 (4%) higher compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Mount Vernon's median rent is $1,472 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $743 (33%) lower. It is roughly $410 (38%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $69 (4%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
