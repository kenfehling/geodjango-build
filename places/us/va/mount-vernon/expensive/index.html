Compared to its surrounding areas, Mount Vernon is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Mount Vernon's median home price is $627,900. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $123,560 (24%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $410,400 (188%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $26,400 (4%) higher.

Rents

Mount Vernon's median rent is $1,472 per month. This is about $743 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $410 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $69 (4%) lower.
