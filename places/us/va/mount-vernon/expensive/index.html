Compared to the surrounding areas, Mount Vernon is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Mount Vernon is $627,900. It is about $123,560 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $410,400 (188%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $26,400 (4%) higher.

Rents

Mount Vernon's median rent is $1,472 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $743 (33%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $410 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is about $69 (4%) lower.
