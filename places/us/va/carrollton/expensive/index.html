Home prices

The median home in Carrollton is valued at $290,200. This is roughly $96,840 (50%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $72,700 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $9,400 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Carrollton is $1,306 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $137 (11%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $244 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $61 (4%) lower.
