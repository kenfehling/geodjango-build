Home prices

The cost of the median home in Enon is $168,200. This is approximately $8,280 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $49,300 (22%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $112,600 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

In Enon the median rent is $1,345 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $170 (14%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $283 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $22 (1%) lower.
