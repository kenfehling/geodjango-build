Home prices

The median home in Tazewell is valued at $119,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $26,920 (29%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $98,100 (45%) lower, and it is about $48,100 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Johnson City.

Rents

Tazewell's median rent is $647 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $25 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $415 (39%) lower, and is approximately $108 (14%) lower compared to the city of Johnson City.
