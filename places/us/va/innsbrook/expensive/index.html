Home prices

The median home in Innsbrook costs $363,600. This is about $57,420 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $146,100 (67%) higher, and is about $133,100 (57%) higher compared to the city of Richmond.

Rents

Innsbrook's median rent is $1,185 a month. It is approximately $175 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $123 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Richmond it is roughly $160 (15%) higher.
