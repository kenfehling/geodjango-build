Home prices

Rents

Innsbrook's median monthly rent is $1,185. This is approximately $175 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $123 (11%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $160 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.
