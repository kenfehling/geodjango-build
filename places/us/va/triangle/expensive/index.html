Home prices

The value of the median home in Triangle is $452,200. This is about $141,900 (45%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $234,700 (107%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $149,300 (24%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Triangle the median rent is $1,165 a month. This is approximately $464 (28%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $103 (9%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $376 (24%) lower.
