Compared to the surrounding areas, Triangle is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Triangle is valued at $452,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $141,900 (45%) higher. It is about $234,700 (107%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $149,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Triangle's median rent is $1,165 per month. This is about $464 (28%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $103 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $376 (24%) lower.
