Home prices

The median home in Triangle costs $452,200. This is roughly $141,900 (45%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $234,700 (107%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $149,300 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Triangle is $1,165 per month. This is roughly $464 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $103 (9%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $376 (24%) lower.
