Home prices

Rents

Suffolk's median monthly rent is $1,201. It is about $209 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $139 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $166 (12%) lower.
