Compared to its nearby areas, Suffolk is a lot more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Suffolk is valued at $254,400. This is approximately $87,140 (52%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $36,900 (16%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $26,400 (9%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

Suffolk's median rent is $1,201 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $209 (21%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $139 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $166 (12%) lower.
