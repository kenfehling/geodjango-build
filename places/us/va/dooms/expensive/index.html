Home prices

The median home price in Dooms is $171,800. This is roughly $45,480 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $45,700 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg is about $11,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

Dooms's median monthly rent is $774. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $255 (24%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $288 (27%) lower, and it is about $83 (9%) lower compared to the city of Lynchburg.
