Home prices

The median home in Dooms is valued at $171,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $45,480 (20%) lower. It is about $45,700 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $11,700 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

In Dooms the median monthly rent is $774. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $255 (24%) lower. It is roughly $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is roughly $83 (9%) lower.
