Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Caroline is $1,352. It is approximately $143 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $290 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $189 (12%) lower.
