Compared to its surrounding areas, Newport News is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Newport News is valued at $194,000. This is roughly $77,220 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $23,500 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $86,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Newport News the median monthly rent is $1,057. It is approximately $169 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $310 (22%) lower.
