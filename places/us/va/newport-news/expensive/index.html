Home prices

The value of the median home in Newport News is $194,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $77,220 (28%) lower. It is approximately $23,500 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $86,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

Newport News's median rent is $1,057 per month. This is about $169 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $310 (22%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
