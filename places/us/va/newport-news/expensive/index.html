Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newport News is $1,057. It is approximately $169 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is is very similar compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $310 (22%) lower.
