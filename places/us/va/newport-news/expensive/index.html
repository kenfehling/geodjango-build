Home prices

The price of the median home in Newport News is $194,000. This is about $77,220 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $23,500 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $86,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Newport News is $1,057 a month. It is roughly $169 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $310 (22%) lower.
