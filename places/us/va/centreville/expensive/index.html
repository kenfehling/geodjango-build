Home prices

The median home in Centreville is valued at $437,500. It is about $21,400 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $220,000 (101%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $164,000 (27%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Centreville is $1,870 a month. This is roughly $27 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $808 (76%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $329 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
