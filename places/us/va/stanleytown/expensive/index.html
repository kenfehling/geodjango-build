Home prices

The value of the median home in Stanleytown is $102,300. This is about $12,300 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $115,200 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $54,000 (34%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stanleytown is $580. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $142 (19%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $482 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is approximately $297 (33%) lower.
