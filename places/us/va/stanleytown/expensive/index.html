Home prices

The price of the median home in Stanleytown is $102,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $12,300 (13%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $115,200 (52%) lower, and is about $54,000 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

In Stanleytown the median monthly rent is $580. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $142 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $482 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $297 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
