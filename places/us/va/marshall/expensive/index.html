Home prices

The median home value in Marshall is $332,500. This is about $87,140 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $115,000 (52%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $269,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Marshall the median rent is $1,450 per month. This is roughly $23 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $388 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $91 (5%) lower.
