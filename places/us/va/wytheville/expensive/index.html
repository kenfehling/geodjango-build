Compared to the surrounding areas, Wytheville is significantly more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Winston-Salem.

Home prices

The median home value in Wytheville is $159,700. It is approximately $41,780 (35%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $57,800 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Winston-Salem it is about $11,800 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Wytheville the median rent is $640 a month. This is roughly $24 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $422 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $166 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Winston-Salem.
