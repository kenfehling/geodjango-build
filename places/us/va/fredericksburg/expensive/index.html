In comparison to other places in this area, Fredericksburg is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Fredericksburg's median home value is $367,200. It is approximately $99,560 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $149,700 (68%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Washington is about $234,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

Fredericksburg's median rent is $1,258 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $190 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $196 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $283 (18%) lower.
