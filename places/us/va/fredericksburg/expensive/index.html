Compared to other places in this area, Fredericksburg is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home value in Fredericksburg is $367,200. This is roughly $99,560 (37%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $149,700 (68%) higher, and it is about $234,300 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Fredericksburg is $1,258 per month. It is about $190 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $196 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $283 (18%) lower.
