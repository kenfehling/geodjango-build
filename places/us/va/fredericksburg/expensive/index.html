Compared to the surrounding areas, Fredericksburg is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home value in Fredericksburg is $367,200. It is about $99,560 (37%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $149,700 (68%) higher, and it is roughly $234,300 (38%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fredericksburg is $1,258. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $190 (13%) lower.It is roughly $196 (18%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $283 (18%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
