Compared to its surrounding areas, Fredericksburg is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Fredericksburg's median home price is $367,200. It is approximately $99,560 (37%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $149,700 (68%) higher, and is roughly $234,300 (38%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Fredericksburg is $1,258 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $190 (13%) lower.It is roughly $196 (18%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $283 (18%) lower.
