Home prices

Rents

Shenandoah's median monthly rent is $876. This is roughly $46 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $186 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is about $19 (2%) higher.
