Home prices

The median home in Reston is valued at $499,200. This is approximately $113,800 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $281,700 (129%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $102,300 (17%) lower.

Rents
