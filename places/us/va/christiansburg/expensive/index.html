Home prices

The cost of the median home in Christiansburg is $190,000. This is about $14,580 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $27,500 (12%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $33,700 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Christiansburg's median monthly rent is $910. It is approximately $62 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $152 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $33 (3%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.
