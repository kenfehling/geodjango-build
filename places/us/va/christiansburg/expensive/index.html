Compared to the surrounding areas, Christiansburg is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Greensboro.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Christiansburg is $190,000. It is approximately $14,580 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $27,500 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is roughly $33,700 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Christiansburg is $910 per month. This is roughly $62 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $152 (14%) lower, and it is about $33 (3%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.
