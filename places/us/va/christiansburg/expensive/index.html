Rents

In Christiansburg the median monthly rent is $910. This is roughly $62 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $152 (14%) lower, and is approximately $33 (3%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.
