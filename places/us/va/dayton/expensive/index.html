Compared to the surrounding areas, Dayton is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Lynchburg.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dayton is $230,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $27,940 (10%) lower.It is roughly $13,000 (5%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is about $70,400 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Dayton is $769 per month. This is about $238 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and is approximately $88 (10%) lower compared to the city of Lynchburg.
