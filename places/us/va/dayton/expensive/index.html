Compared to other places in this area, Dayton is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Lynchburg however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Dayton is $230,500. It is approximately $27,940 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $13,000 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $70,400 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Lynchburg.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $769 a month. It is roughly $238 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $88 (10%) lower compared to the city of Lynchburg.
