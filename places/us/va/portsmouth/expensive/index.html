Compared to other places in this area, Portsmouth is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Portsmouth is $170,900. It is roughly $77,120 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $46,600 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $109,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

Portsmouth's median rent is $1,048 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $89 (7%) lower.It is approximately $14 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $319 (23%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
