In comparison to other places in this area, Portsmouth is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Portsmouth's median home cost is $170,900. It is roughly $77,120 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $46,600 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $109,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Portsmouth the median rent is $1,048 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $89 (7%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $14 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $319 (23%) lower.
