In comparison to the surrounding areas, New Castle is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Roanoke, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in New Castle is valued at $195,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $32,200 (14%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $21,700 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke is roughly $60,700 (44%) higher.

Rents

In New Castle the median rent is $794 a month. It is approximately $140 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $268 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $20 (2%) lower.
