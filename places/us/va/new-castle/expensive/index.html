Home prices

The median home in New Castle is valued at $195,800. It is roughly $32,200 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $21,700 (9%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is about $60,700 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in New Castle is $794 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $140 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $268 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $20 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
