Home prices

The median home in Lyndhurst is valued at $185,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $54,020 (22%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $31,900 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke is about $50,500 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Lyndhurst the median rent is $1,138 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $235 (26%) higher.It is roughly $76 (7%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is roughly $324 (39%) higher.
