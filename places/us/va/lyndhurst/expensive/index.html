In comparison to other places in this area, Lyndhurst is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Roanoke.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lyndhurst is $185,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $54,020 (22%) lower. It is about $31,900 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke is roughly $50,500 (37%) higher.

Rents

Lyndhurst's median rent is $1,138 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $235 (26%) higher. It is roughly $76 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke is roughly $324 (39%) higher.
