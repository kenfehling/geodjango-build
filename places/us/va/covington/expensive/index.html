Home prices

The cost of the median home in Covington is $72,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $63,540 (46%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $144,600 (66%) lower, and is about $62,200 (46%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Covington is $619. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $165 (21%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $195 (23%) lower compared to the city of Roanoke.
