Compared to its nearby areas, Arlington is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Arlington is $705,400. It is about $161,560 (29%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $487,900 (224%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $103,900 (17%) higher.

Rents

Arlington's median monthly rent is $1,970. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $303 (18%) higher.It is approximately $908 (85%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $429 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
