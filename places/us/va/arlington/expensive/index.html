Compared to the surrounding areas, Arlington is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Washington, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Arlington is $705,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $161,560 (29%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $487,900 (224%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is about $103,900 (17%) higher.

Rents

Arlington's median monthly rent is $1,970. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $303 (18%) higher. It is roughly $908 (85%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $429 (27%) higher.
