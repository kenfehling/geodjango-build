In comparison to its nearby areas, Arlington is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Washington, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Arlington is $705,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $161,560 (29%) higher.It is about $487,900 (224%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $103,900 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Arlington the median rent is $1,970 per month. It is roughly $303 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $908 (85%) higher, and it is about $429 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
