In comparison to its surrounding areas, Vienna is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Vienna costs $743,500. This is about $142,920 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $526,000 (241%) higher, and is about $142,000 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Vienna is $2,081. This is approximately $42 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $1,019 (95%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $540 (35%) higher.
