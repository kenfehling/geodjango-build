Home prices

The median home in Bland costs $92,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $13,760 (12%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $124,900 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Winston-Salem it is approximately $55,300 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Bland the median monthly rent is $676. Compared to nearby areas it is about $18 (2%) higher.It is about $386 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $130 (16%) lower compared to the city of Winston-Salem.
