Compared to its surrounding areas, Bland is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Winston-Salem, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Bland is $92,600. This is about $13,760 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $124,900 (57%) lower, and it is roughly $55,300 (37%) lower compared to the city of Winston-Salem.

Rents

Bland's median rent is $676 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $18 (2%) higher. It is about $386 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Winston-Salem is roughly $130 (16%) lower.
