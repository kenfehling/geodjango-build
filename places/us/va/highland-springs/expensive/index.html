Home prices

The median home value in Highland Springs is $145,800. It is approximately $28,180 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $71,700 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Richmond is roughly $84,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Highland Springs is $1,063. This is approximately $28 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is is very similar compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Richmond is approximately $38 (3%) higher.
