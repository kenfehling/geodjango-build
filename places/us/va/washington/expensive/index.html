Home prices

Washington's median home price is $477,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $252,500 (112%) higher.It is about $260,300 (119%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $123,700 (20%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Washington is $939 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $138 (12%) lower.It is roughly $123 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $602 (39%) lower.
