Home prices

The median home in Washington is valued at $477,800. It is roughly $252,500 (112%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $260,300 (119%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $123,700 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Washington the median monthly rent is $939. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $138 (12%) lower. It is roughly $123 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington is about $602 (39%) lower.
