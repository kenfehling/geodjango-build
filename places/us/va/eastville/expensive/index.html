Rents

The median monthly rent in Eastville is $622. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $97 (13%) lower. It is roughly $440 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $745 (54%) lower.
