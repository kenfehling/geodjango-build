Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg costs $160,100. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $25,000 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

The median rent in Lynchburg is $857 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $48 (5%) higher. It is about $205 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Roanoke is about $43 (5%) higher.
