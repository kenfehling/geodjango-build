Home prices

Rents

Lynchburg's median monthly rent is $857. It is roughly $48 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $205 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $43 (5%) higher compared to the city of Roanoke.
