Home prices

The median home in Lynchburg costs $160,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $57,400 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $25,000 (18%) higher.

Rents

Lynchburg's median monthly rent is $857. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $48 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $205 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $43 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
