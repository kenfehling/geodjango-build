Home prices

The price of the median home in Lynchburg is $160,100. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Roanoke is roughly $25,000 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lynchburg is $857 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $48 (5%) higher. It is about $205 (19%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is about $43 (5%) higher.
