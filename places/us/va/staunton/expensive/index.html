In comparison to its surrounding areas, Staunton is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Roanoke, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Staunton is $169,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $32,600 (16%) lower.It is about $48,500 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $33,900 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Staunton is $861. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $18 (2%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $201 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $47 (5%) higher.
