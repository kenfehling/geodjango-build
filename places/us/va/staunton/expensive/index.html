Compared to other places in this area, Staunton is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Roanoke however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Staunton is $169,000. It is roughly $32,600 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $48,500 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $33,900 (25%) higher compared to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

Staunton's median monthly rent is $861. It is about $18 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $201 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke is roughly $47 (5%) higher.
