In comparison to the surrounding areas, Colonial Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Colonial Heights is valued at $171,700. It is about $23,200 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $45,800 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $109,100 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Colonial Heights the median rent is $1,038 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $102 (8%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $24 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is about $329 (24%) lower.
