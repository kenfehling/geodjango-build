In comparison to the surrounding areas, Colonial Heights is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Colonial Heights is valued at $171,700. This is about $23,200 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $45,800 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $109,100 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

In Colonial Heights the median rent is $1,038 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $102 (8%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $24 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $329 (24%) lower.
