In comparison to other places in this area, Colonial Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Colonial Heights is $171,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $23,200 (15%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $45,800 (21%) lower, and is about $109,100 (38%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

Colonial Heights's median monthly rent is $1,038. This is about $102 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $24 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $329 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
