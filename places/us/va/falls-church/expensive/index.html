In comparison to other places in this area, Falls Church is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Washington, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Falls Church costs $789,300. This is approximately $233,340 (41%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $571,800 (262%) higher, and is roughly $187,800 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Falls Church's median rent is $1,867 per month. It is approximately $103 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $805 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $326 (21%) higher.
