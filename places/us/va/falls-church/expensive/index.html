Home prices

Falls Church's median home value is $789,300. It is about $233,340 (41%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $571,800 (262%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $187,800 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Falls Church the median rent is $1,867 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $103 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $805 (75%) higher, and it is approximately $326 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
