Compared to other places in this area, Falls Church is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Washington, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Falls Church is valued at $789,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $233,340 (41%) higher. It is roughly $571,800 (262%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $187,800 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Falls Church's median rent is $1,867 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $103 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $805 (75%) higher, and is about $326 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
