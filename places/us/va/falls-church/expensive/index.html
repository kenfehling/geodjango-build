The cost of the median home in Falls Church is $789,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $233,340 (41%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $571,800 (262%) higher, and is roughly $187,800 (31%) higher compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Falls Church is $1,867 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $103 (5%) higher. It is roughly $805 (75%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $326 (21%) higher.
