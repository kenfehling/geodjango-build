In comparison to its surrounding areas, Salem is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Greensboro, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Salem is valued at $181,900. It is roughly $11,540 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $35,600 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is about $25,600 (16%) higher.

Rents

Salem's median rent is $913 a month. This is approximately $10 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $149 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $36 (4%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.
