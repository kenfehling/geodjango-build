In comparison to the surrounding areas, Salem is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Greensboro.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Salem is $181,900. This is about $11,540 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $35,600 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $25,600 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Salem's median rent is $913 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $10 (1%) higher.It is roughly $149 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $36 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
