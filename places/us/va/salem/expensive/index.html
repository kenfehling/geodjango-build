In comparison to the surrounding areas, Salem is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Greensboro, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Salem costs $181,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $11,540 (6%) higher. It is about $35,600 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $25,600 (16%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

The median rent in Salem is $913 a month. It is about $10 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $149 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $36 (4%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.
