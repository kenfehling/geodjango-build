Home prices

The median home in Appalachia costs $58,700. It is approximately $34,080 (36%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $158,800 (73%) lower, and is roughly $108,800 (64%) lower compared to the city of Johnson City.

Rents

The median rent in Appalachia is $370 per month. It is roughly $278 (42%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $692 (65%) lower, and it is about $385 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Johnson City.
