Home prices

Appalachia's median home value is $58,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $34,080 (36%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $158,800 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Johnson City it is approximately $108,800 (64%) lower.

Rents

Appalachia's median monthly rent is $370. This is approximately $278 (42%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $692 (65%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $385 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Johnson City.
