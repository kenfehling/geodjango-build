Home prices

Rents

In Appalachia the median rent is $370 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $278 (42%) lower.It is about $692 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $385 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Johnson City.
