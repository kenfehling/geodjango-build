Home prices

The median home in Hayfield costs $532,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $94,360 (21%) higher. It is approximately $315,000 (144%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $69,000 (11%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Hayfield is $2,826 a month. This is roughly $1,022 (56%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $1,764 (166%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $1,285 (83%) higher.
