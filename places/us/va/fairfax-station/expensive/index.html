Home prices

The median home in Fairfax Station costs $662,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $106,720 (19%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $444,900 (204%) higher, and it is about $60,900 (10%) higher compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Fairfax Station's median rent is $2,565 per month. It is roughly $515 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $1,503 (141%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $1,024 (66%) higher.
