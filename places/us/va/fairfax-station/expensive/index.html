Home prices

The value of the median home in Fairfax Station is $662,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $106,720 (19%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $444,900 (204%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $60,900 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fairfax Station is $2,565 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $515 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,503 (141%) higher, and it is approximately $1,024 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
