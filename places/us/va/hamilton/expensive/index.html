In comparison to other places in this area, Hamilton is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $362,800. This is roughly $42,180 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $145,300 (66%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $238,700 (39%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Hamilton's median monthly rent is $1,281. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $300 (18%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $219 (20%) higher, and it is approximately $260 (16%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
