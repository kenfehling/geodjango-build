Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hamilton is $362,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $42,180 (10%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $145,300 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $238,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Hamilton the median monthly rent is $1,281. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $300 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $219 (20%) higher, and is roughly $260 (16%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
