Home prices

Hamilton's median home cost is $362,800. This is approximately $42,180 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $145,300 (66%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $238,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

Hamilton's median monthly rent is $1,281. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $300 (18%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $219 (20%) higher, and it is about $260 (16%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
