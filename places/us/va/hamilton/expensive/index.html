Compared to the surrounding areas, Hamilton is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hamilton is $362,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $42,180 (10%) lower.It is about $145,300 (66%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is approximately $238,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hamilton is $1,281. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $300 (18%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $219 (20%) higher, and it is about $260 (16%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
