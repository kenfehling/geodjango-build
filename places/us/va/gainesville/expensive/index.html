Home prices

The value of the median home in Gainesville is $491,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $112,320 (29%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $274,000 (125%) higher, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $110,000 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Gainesville is $2,059 a month. It is about $156 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $997 (93%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $518 (33%) higher.
