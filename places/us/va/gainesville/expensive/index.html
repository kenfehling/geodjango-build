Home prices

The price of the median home in Gainesville is $491,500. It is approximately $112,320 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $274,000 (125%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $110,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Gainesville's median rent is $2,059 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $156 (8%) higher.It is about $997 (93%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $518 (33%) higher.
