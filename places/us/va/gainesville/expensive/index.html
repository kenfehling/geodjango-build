In comparison to other places in this area, Gainesville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gainesville costs $491,500. This is about $112,320 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $274,000 (125%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $110,000 (18%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Gainesville is $2,059 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $156 (8%) higher.It is roughly $997 (93%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $518 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
