Compared to other places in this area, Gainesville is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Gainesville is valued at $491,500. It is about $112,320 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $274,000 (125%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $110,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Gainesville is $2,059 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $156 (8%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $997 (93%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $518 (33%) higher.
