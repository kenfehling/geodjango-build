In comparison to other places in this area, Merrimac is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Greensboro, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Merrimac is $211,400. This is about $11,100 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $6,100 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is approximately $55,100 (35%) higher.

Rents

Merrimac's median rent is $662 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $235 (26%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $400 (37%) lower, and is roughly $215 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
