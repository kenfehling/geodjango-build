Compared to nearby areas, Merrimac is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Greensboro, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Merrimac costs $211,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $11,100 (5%) higher.It is about $6,100 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $55,100 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Merrimac's median rent is $662 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $235 (26%) lower.It is approximately $400 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Greensboro is about $215 (24%) lower.
