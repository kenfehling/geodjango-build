Home prices

The median home cost in Merrimac is $211,400. It is approximately $11,100 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $6,100 (2%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $55,100 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

The median rent in Merrimac is $662 a month. This is roughly $235 (26%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $400 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is roughly $215 (24%) lower.
