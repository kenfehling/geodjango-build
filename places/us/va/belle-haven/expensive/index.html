Home prices

The cost of the median home in Belle Haven is $148,400. It is approximately $34,740 (30%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $69,100 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $132,400 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

The median rent in Belle Haven is $881 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $101 (13%) higher.It is approximately $181 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $486 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
