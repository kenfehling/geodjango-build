Home prices

The median home in Warrenton costs $342,600. It is about $58,240 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $125,100 (57%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $258,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Warrenton the median monthly rent is $1,314. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $470 (26%) lower. It is approximately $252 (23%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $227 (14%) lower.
