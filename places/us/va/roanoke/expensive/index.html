Compared to its surrounding areas, Roanoke is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Greensboro, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Roanoke costs $135,100. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $64,680 (32%) lower. It is approximately $82,400 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is about $21,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Roanoke is $814 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $57 (6%) lower. It is approximately $248 (23%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is approximately $63 (7%) lower.
