Compared to its nearby areas, Roanoke is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Greensboro, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Roanoke is $135,100. This is roughly $64,680 (32%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $82,400 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro is approximately $21,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roanoke is $814. This is roughly $57 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $248 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $63 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
