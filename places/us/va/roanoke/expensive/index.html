Home prices

The median home in Roanoke is valued at $135,100. It is about $64,680 (32%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $82,400 (37%) lower, and is approximately $21,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

In Roanoke the median rent is $814 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $57 (6%) lower.It is about $248 (23%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $63 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
