Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Roanoke is $814 a month. It is approximately $57 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $248 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Greensboro it is about $63 (7%) lower.
