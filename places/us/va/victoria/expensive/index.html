Home prices

The median home in Victoria is valued at $88,000. This is approximately $27,560 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $129,500 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $160,300 (64%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Victoria the median monthly rent is $702. This is approximately $33 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $360 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is about $419 (37%) lower.
