Home prices

The median home in Potomac Mills is valued at $342,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $5,780 (1%) higher.It is about $125,000 (57%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $259,000 (43%) lower.

Rents

Potomac Mills's median rent is $1,801 a month. This is about $125 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $739 (69%) higher, and is roughly $260 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
