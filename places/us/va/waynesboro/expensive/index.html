Home prices

The value of the median home in Waynesboro is $174,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $6,900 (3%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $43,400 (19%) lower, and it is about $39,000 (28%) higher compared to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

Waynesboro's median rent is $821 a month. This is approximately $105 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $241 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is is very similar.
