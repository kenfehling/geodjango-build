Home prices

The median home value in Waynesboro is $174,100. This is approximately $6,900 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $43,400 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is about $39,000 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Waynesboro the median monthly rent is $821. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $105 (11%) lower. It is approximately $241 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Roanoke.
