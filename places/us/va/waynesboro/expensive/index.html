Home prices

The median home price in Waynesboro is $174,100. This is about $6,900 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $43,400 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is approximately $39,000 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Waynesboro is $821 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $105 (11%) lower.It is roughly $241 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Roanoke.
