In comparison to the surrounding areas, Burke is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Washington, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Burke's median home price is $541,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $43,920 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $324,400 (149%) higher, and is roughly $59,600 (9%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Burke's median monthly rent is $2,424. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $166 (7%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,362 (128%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $883 (57%) higher.
