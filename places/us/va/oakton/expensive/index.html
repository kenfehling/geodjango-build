Home prices

The value of the median home in Oakton is $631,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $25,280 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $414,200 (190%) higher, and it is roughly $30,200 (5%) higher compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Oakton's median monthly rent is $1,923. This is roughly $49 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $861 (81%) higher, and it is about $382 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
