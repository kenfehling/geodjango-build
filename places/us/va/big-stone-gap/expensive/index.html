Home prices

The median home in Big Stone Gap is valued at $126,100. It is roughly $45,420 (56%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $91,400 (42%) lower, and it is approximately $41,400 (24%) lower compared to the city of Johnson City.

Rents

Big Stone Gap's median rent is $690 a month. This is about $109 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $372 (35%) lower, and is approximately $65 (8%) lower compared to the city of Johnson City.
