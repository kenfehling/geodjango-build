Home prices

The median home in Concord costs $120,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $26,420 (17%) lower. It is about $97,000 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Durham is about $103,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Concord the median rent is $813 per month. This is roughly $50 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $245 (23%) lower compared to the city of Durham.
