Home prices

The median home in Concord costs $120,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $26,420 (17%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $97,000 (44%) lower, and is roughly $103,600 (46%) lower compared to the city of Durham.

Rents

In Concord the median rent is $813 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $50 (6%) higher. It is approximately $249 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Durham it is approximately $245 (23%) lower.
