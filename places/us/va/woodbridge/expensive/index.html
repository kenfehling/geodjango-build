In comparison to its surrounding areas, Woodbridge is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Woodbridge is $357,400. This is approximately $74,040 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $139,900 (64%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is about $244,100 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Woodbridge is $1,918 per month. It is roughly $67 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $856 (80%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $377 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
