Home prices

The median home in Woodbridge is valued at $357,400. This is roughly $74,040 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $139,900 (64%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $244,100 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Woodbridge the median monthly rent is $1,918. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $67 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $856 (80%) higher, and is about $377 (24%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
