Compared to other places in this area, Williamsburg is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Williamsburg costs $306,000. This is about $42,180 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $88,500 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $25,200 (8%) higher.

Rents

Williamsburg's median monthly rent is $1,193. It is about $200 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $131 (12%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is about $174 (12%) lower.
