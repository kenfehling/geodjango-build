Rents

Williamsburg's median rent is $1,193 a month. It is roughly $200 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $131 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is approximately $174 (12%) lower.
