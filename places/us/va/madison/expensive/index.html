Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $235,000. It is approximately $22,540 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $17,500 (8%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $366,500 (60%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Madison the median rent is $777 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $197 (20%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is approximately $764 (49%) lower.
