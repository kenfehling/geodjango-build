Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $235,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $22,540 (10%) higher. It is approximately $17,500 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $366,500 (60%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Madison the median rent is $777 per month. It is about $197 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and is approximately $764 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
