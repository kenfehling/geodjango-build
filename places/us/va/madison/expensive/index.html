Home prices

The median home value in Madison is $235,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $22,540 (10%) higher. It is roughly $17,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $366,500 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Madison is $777. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $197 (20%) lower. It is roughly $285 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $764 (49%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
