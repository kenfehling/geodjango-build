Rocky Mount's median home cost is $143,000. This is roughly $205,120 (58%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $74,500 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Greensboro is roughly $13,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Rocky Mount the median rent is $754 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $142 (15%) lower. It is approximately $308 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $123 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Greensboro.
