Home prices

The median home in Fishersville costs $255,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $75,760 (42%) higher. It is about $38,300 (17%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $120,700 (89%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

Fishersville's median monthly rent is $1,044. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $131 (14%) higher. It is roughly $18 (1%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is roughly $230 (28%) higher.
