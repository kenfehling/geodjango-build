The median home cost in Fishersville is $255,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $75,760 (42%) higher. It is about $38,300 (17%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Roanoke is about $120,700 (89%) higher.

Rents

Fishersville's median monthly rent is $1,044. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $131 (14%) higher. It is about $18 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $230 (28%) higher compared to the city of Roanoke.
