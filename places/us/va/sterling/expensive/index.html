Home prices

The median home cost in Sterling is $359,000. It is approximately $105,180 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $141,500 (65%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $242,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Sterling the median rent is $1,812 a month. This is approximately $331 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $750 (70%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is about $271 (17%) higher.
