In comparison to other places in this area, Charlottesville is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Richmond, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Charlottesville is $299,600. This is roughly $17,460 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $82,100 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Richmond is roughly $69,100 (29%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Charlottesville is $1,142 per month. It is about $156 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $80 (7%) higher, and is roughly $117 (11%) higher compared to the city of Richmond.
