In comparison to its surrounding areas, Charlottesville is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Richmond, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlottesville is $299,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $17,460 (5%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $82,100 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Richmond it is about $69,100 (29%) higher.

Rents

Charlottesville's median monthly rent is $1,142. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $156 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $80 (7%) higher, and it is about $117 (11%) higher compared to the city of Richmond.
