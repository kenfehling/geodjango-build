Compared to its nearby areas, Charlottesville is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Richmond.

Home prices

The median home cost in Charlottesville is $299,600. This is approximately $17,460 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $82,100 (37%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $69,100 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.

Rents

The median rent in Charlottesville is $1,142 per month. This is approximately $156 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $80 (7%) higher, and is roughly $117 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.
