Compared to nearby areas, Charlottesville is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Richmond however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Charlottesville costs $299,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $17,460 (5%) lower.It is about $82,100 (37%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $69,100 (29%) higher compared to the city of Richmond.

Rents

In Charlottesville the median monthly rent is $1,142. This is about $156 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $80 (7%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $117 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Richmond.
