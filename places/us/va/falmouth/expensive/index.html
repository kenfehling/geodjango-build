Compared to its surrounding areas, Falmouth is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Washington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Falmouth is valued at $319,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $24,100 (8%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $102,200 (46%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $281,800 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Falmouth the median monthly rent is $996. It is approximately $615 (38%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $66 (6%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $545 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
