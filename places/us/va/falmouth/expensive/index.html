In comparison to its surrounding areas, Falmouth is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Falmouth costs $319,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $24,100 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $102,200 (46%) higher, and is roughly $281,800 (46%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Falmouth's median monthly rent is $996. It is roughly $615 (38%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $66 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $545 (35%) lower.
