Home prices

The value of the median home in Weyers Cave is $236,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $35,860 (17%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $18,800 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg it is approximately $76,200 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Weyers Cave the median monthly rent is $1,008. This is roughly $206 (25%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $54 (5%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Lynchburg is roughly $151 (17%) higher.
