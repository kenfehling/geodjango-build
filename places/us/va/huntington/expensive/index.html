In comparison to its nearby areas, Huntington is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Huntington is $403,300. Compared to nearby areas it is about $39,540 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $185,800 (85%) higher, and is approximately $198,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Huntington's median rent is $1,744 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $151 (9%) higher. It is roughly $682 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $203 (13%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
