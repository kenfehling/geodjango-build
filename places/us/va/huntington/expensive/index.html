Much like its surrounding areas, it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home in Huntington is valued at $403,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $13,040 (3%) higher.It is approximately $185,800 (85%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $198,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Huntington the median rent is $1,744 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $84 (5%) higher.It is about $682 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $203 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.
