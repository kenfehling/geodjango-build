Home prices

The median home value in Huntington is $403,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $13,040 (3%) higher.It is about $185,800 (85%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $198,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Huntington the median rent is $1,744 per month. This is about $84 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $682 (64%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $203 (13%) higher.
