In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sandston is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

The median home in Sandston costs $136,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $19,940 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $81,100 (37%) lower, and it is about $144,400 (51%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

The median rent in Sandston is $1,023 per month. This is about $13 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $39 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $344 (25%) lower.
