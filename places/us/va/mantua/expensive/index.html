Home prices

The median home in Mantua is valued at $780,000. This is about $208,960 (36%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $562,500 (258%) higher, and it is approximately $178,500 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Mantua the median monthly rent is $1,959. This is approximately $139 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $897 (84%) higher, and is about $418 (27%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
