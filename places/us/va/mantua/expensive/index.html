Home prices

The median home in Mantua is valued at $780,000. This is about $208,960 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $562,500 (258%) higher, and it is approximately $178,500 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Mantua's median monthly rent is $1,959. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $139 (7%) higher. It is approximately $897 (84%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $418 (27%) higher.
