Home prices

Rents

County Center's median monthly rent is $1,872. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $19 (1%) lower.It is approximately $810 (76%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $331 (21%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
