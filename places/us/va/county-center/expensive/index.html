Home prices

The value of the median home in County Center is $480,500. This is about $100,320 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $263,000 (120%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $121,000 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

In County Center the median rent is $1,872 a month. This is approximately $19 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $810 (76%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $331 (21%) higher.
