Home prices

The cost of the median home in County Center is $480,500. It is about $100,320 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $263,000 (120%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $121,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

In County Center the median rent is $1,872 a month. It is about $19 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $810 (76%) higher, and is roughly $331 (21%) higher compared to the city of Washington.
