Home prices

The value of the median home in Cloverdale is $280,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $83,080 (42%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $62,800 (28%) higher, and it is approximately $124,000 (79%) higher in comparison to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

Cloverdale's median rent is $785 a month. This is roughly $82 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $277 (26%) lower, and is about $92 (10%) lower compared to the city of Greensboro.
