Home prices

The median home in Cloverdale is valued at $280,300. This is approximately $83,080 (42%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $62,800 (28%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $124,000 (79%) higher compared to the city of Greensboro.

Rents

In Cloverdale the median monthly rent is $785. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $82 (9%) lower.It is about $277 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Greensboro it is about $92 (10%) lower.
