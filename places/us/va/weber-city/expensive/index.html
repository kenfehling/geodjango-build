Home prices

The median home in Weber City costs $105,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $24,340 (18%) lower.It is approximately $112,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Asheville it is roughly $164,900 (60%) lower.

Rents

Weber City's median rent is $587 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $84 (12%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $475 (44%) lower, and is about $456 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
