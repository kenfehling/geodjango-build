Compared to the surrounding areas, Weber City is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Asheville, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Weber City is $105,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $24,340 (18%) lower. It is about $112,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $164,900 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Weber City the median rent is $587 a month. It is roughly $84 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $475 (44%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $456 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
