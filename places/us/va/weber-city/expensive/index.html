Home prices

The median home in Weber City costs $105,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24,340 (18%) lower.It is approximately $112,000 (51%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $164,900 (60%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Weber City's median rent is $587 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $84 (12%) lower.It is about $475 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $456 (43%) lower.
