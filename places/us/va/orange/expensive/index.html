In comparison to the surrounding areas, Orange is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Orange is $220,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $20,120 (10%) higher. It is about $3,300 (1%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $380,700 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Orange's median monthly rent is $946. This is about $89 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $116 (10%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $595 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
