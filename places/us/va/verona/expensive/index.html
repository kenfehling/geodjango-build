Home prices

The median home in Verona costs $162,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $31,440 (16%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $55,100 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Roanoke it is roughly $27,300 (20%) higher.

Rents

In Verona the median monthly rent is $849. It is approximately $20 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $213 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Roanoke is about $35 (4%) higher.
