Home prices

The median home in Verona is valued at $162,400. This is roughly $31,440 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $55,100 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $27,300 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

In Verona the median monthly rent is $849. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $20 (2%) higher.It is approximately $213 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Roanoke it is about $35 (4%) higher.
