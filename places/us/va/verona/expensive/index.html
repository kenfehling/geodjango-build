Compared to its surrounding areas, Verona is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Roanoke, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Verona costs $162,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $31,440 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $55,100 (25%) lower, and is roughly $27,300 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.

Rents

The median rent in Verona is $849 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $20 (2%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $213 (20%) lower, and it is about $35 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Roanoke.
