Home prices

The price of the median home in Scottsburg is $75,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $47,540 (38%) lower. It is about $141,700 (65%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $172,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Scottsburg the median rent is $776 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $89 (12%) higher. It is about $286 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $345 (30%) lower.
