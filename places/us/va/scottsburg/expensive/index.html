Home prices

Scottsburg's median home price is $75,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $47,540 (38%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $141,700 (65%) lower, and is about $172,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Scottsburg's median rent is $776 per month. This is roughly $89 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $286 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $345 (30%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
