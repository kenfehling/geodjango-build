Home prices

Advance's median home cost is $90,800. This is about $13,540 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $126,700 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale is approximately $26,700 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Advance the median rent is $575 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $13 (2%) lower. It is about $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $139 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Carbondale.
