Home prices

The median home cost in Parkway is $128,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $10,020 (8%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $89,100 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $10,300 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Parkway the median rent is $833 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $27 (3%) higher. It is roughly $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is is very similar compared to the city of St. Louis.
