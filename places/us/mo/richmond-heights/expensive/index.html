Home prices

The median home in Richmond Heights is valued at $253,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $49,900 (16%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $36,200 (16%) higher, and it is roughly $115,000 (82%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond Heights is $1,035 per month. This is roughly $25 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $27 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $207 (25%) higher.
