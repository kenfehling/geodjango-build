Compared to other places in this area, Richmond Heights is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Richmond Heights is $253,700. This is approximately $49,900 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $36,200 (16%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $115,000 (82%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond Heights is $1,035 per month. It is approximately $25 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $27 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $207 (25%) higher.
