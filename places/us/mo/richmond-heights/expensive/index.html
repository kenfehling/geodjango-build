Compared to other places in this area, Richmond Heights is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richmond Heights is valued at $253,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $49,900 (16%) lower. It is roughly $36,200 (16%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $115,000 (82%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richmond Heights is $1,035. This is roughly $25 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $27 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $207 (25%) higher.
