Home prices

The value of the median home in Richmond Heights is $253,700. It is roughly $49,900 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $36,200 (16%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $115,000 (82%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond Heights is $1,035 a month. It is about $25 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $27 (2%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $207 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
