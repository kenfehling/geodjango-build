Home prices

The value of the median home in Dearborn is $118,800. This is roughly $20,800 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $98,700 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $35,800 (23%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Dearborn is $575 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $323 (35%) lower.It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $366 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
