Compared to the surrounding areas, Dearborn is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Dearborn costs $118,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $20,800 (14%) lower. It is roughly $98,700 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $35,800 (23%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Dearborn is $575 a month. It is approximately $323 (35%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $487 (45%) lower, and it is about $366 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
