Home prices

Rents

In Grandview the median monthly rent is $830. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $387 (31%) lower.It is roughly $232 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $111 (11%) lower.
