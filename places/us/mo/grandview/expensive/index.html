Compared to its surrounding areas, Grandview is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Grandview is $108,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $151,580 (58%) lower. It is about $108,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $46,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

Grandview's median monthly rent is $830. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $387 (31%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $232 (21%) lower, and it is about $111 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
