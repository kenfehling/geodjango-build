Home prices

The median home cost in Grandview is $108,600. It is roughly $151,580 (58%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $108,900 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $46,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

Grandview's median monthly rent is $830. It is about $387 (31%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $232 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $111 (11%) lower.
