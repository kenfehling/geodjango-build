Home prices

Grandview's median home cost is $108,600. This is approximately $151,580 (58%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $108,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $46,000 (29%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Grandview's median monthly rent is $830. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $387 (31%) lower.It is roughly $232 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $111 (11%) lower.
