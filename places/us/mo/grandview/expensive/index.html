Compared to the surrounding areas, Grandview is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Grandview is valued at $108,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $151,580 (58%) lower. It is about $108,900 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $46,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grandview is $830. This is approximately $387 (31%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $232 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $111 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
