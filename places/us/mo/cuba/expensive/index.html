Home prices

Cuba's median home price is $109,500. This is about $25,100 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $108,000 (49%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $29,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Cuba the median rent is $547 per month. This is approximately $106 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $515 (48%) lower, and is roughly $281 (33%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
