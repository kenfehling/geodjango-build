Home prices

The median home in Cuba costs $109,500. It is approximately $25,100 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $108,000 (49%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $29,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Cuba is $547 per month. This is roughly $106 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $515 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $281 (33%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
