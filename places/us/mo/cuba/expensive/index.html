Home prices

The median home in Cuba costs $109,500. It is approximately $25,100 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $108,000 (49%) lower, and is about $29,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cuba is $547. This is about $106 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $515 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $281 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
