Compared to its surrounding areas, Crystal Lake Park is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Crystal Lake Park is valued at $421,300. This is about $47,900 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $203,800 (93%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $282,600 (203%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Crystal Lake Park is $1,750 per month. It is about $383 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $688 (64%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $922 (111%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
