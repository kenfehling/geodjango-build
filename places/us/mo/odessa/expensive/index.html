Home prices

Odessa's median home price is $137,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $44,880 (48%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $79,600 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $16,700 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Odessa the median rent is $721 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $67 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $341 (32%) lower, and is about $220 (23%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
