Home prices

The median home in Fremont Hills is valued at $370,500. It is roughly $230,640 (164%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $153,000 (70%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $252,400 (213%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fremont Hills is $750. It is about $57 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $312 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is is very similar.
