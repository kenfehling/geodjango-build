Home prices

The median home in Fremont Hills costs $370,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $230,640 (164%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $153,000 (70%) higher, and is approximately $252,400 (213%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Fremont Hills's median rent is $750 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $57 (7%) lower.It is about $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Springfield it is is very similar.
