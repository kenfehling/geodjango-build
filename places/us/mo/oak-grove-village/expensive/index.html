Home prices

The median home price in Oak Grove Village is $98,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $2,920 (3%) higher. It is about $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $39,900 (28%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Oak Grove Village's median rent is $663 a month. This is approximately $74 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $399 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $165 (19%) lower.
