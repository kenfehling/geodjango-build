Home prices

The value of the median home in Jamestown is $105,600. This is roughly $11,700 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $111,900 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is approximately $86,200 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jamestown is $406. This is about $202 (33%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $656 (61%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbia is about $481 (54%) lower.
