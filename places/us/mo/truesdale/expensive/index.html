Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Truesdale is $817 a month. This is roughly $11 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $11 (1%) lower.
