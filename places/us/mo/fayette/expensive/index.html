Home prices

Rents

Fayette's median monthly rent is $758. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $93 (13%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $304 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is about $129 (14%) lower.
