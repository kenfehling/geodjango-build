In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fayette is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbia, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Fayette's median home price is $94,700. It is roughly $13,120 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $122,800 (56%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $97,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

The median rent in Fayette is $758 a month. It is about $93 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $304 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia it is roughly $129 (14%) lower.
