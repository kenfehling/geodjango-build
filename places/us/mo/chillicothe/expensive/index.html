Home prices

The median home in Chillicothe is valued at $91,200. It is roughly $26,460 (40%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $126,300 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $63,400 (41%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Chillicothe the median rent is $678 per month. It is about $152 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $384 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $263 (27%) lower.
