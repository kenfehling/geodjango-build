Home prices

The median home cost in Brentwood is $228,800. This is about $113,900 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $11,300 (5%) higher, and it is roughly $90,100 (64%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Brentwood the median rent is $1,239 a month. This is about $143 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $177 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $411 (49%) higher.
