Compared to other places in this area, Centerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Rolla, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Centerville is $58,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,820 (19%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $158,900 (73%) lower, and is about $72,500 (55%) lower compared to the city of Rolla.

Rents

Centerville's median monthly rent is $638. This is about $149 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $424 (39%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rolla is about $99 (13%) lower.
