Home prices

The median home cost in Crystal City is $134,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $12,540 (8%) lower.It is roughly $83,500 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $4,700 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Crystal City is $851 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $182 (17%) lower.It is roughly $211 (19%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $23 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
