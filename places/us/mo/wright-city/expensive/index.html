Compared to other places in this area, Wright City is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Wright City is valued at $147,500. It is about $66,160 (30%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $70,000 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $8,800 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wright City is $784. It is roughly $106 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $278 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $44 (5%) lower.
