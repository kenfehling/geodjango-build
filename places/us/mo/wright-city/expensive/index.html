Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wright City is $147,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $66,160 (30%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $70,000 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $8,800 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wright City is $784 per month. This is about $106 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $278 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $44 (5%) lower.
