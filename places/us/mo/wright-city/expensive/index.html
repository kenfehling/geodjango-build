Compared to its nearby areas, Wright City is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wright City is valued at $147,500. It is roughly $66,160 (30%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $70,000 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $8,800 (6%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Wright City is $784 a month. This is approximately $106 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $278 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $44 (5%) lower.
