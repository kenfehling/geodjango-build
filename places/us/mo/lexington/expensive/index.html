Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lexington is $122,500. This is about $46,480 (61%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $95,000 (43%) lower, and is approximately $32,100 (20%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Lexington is $636 a month. It is roughly $57 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $426 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $305 (32%) lower.
