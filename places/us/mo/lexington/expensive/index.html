Compared to the surrounding areas, Lexington is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lexington is $122,500. This is roughly $46,480 (61%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $95,000 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $32,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Lexington the median rent is $636 a month. This is about $57 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $426 (40%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $305 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
