Home prices

Lexington's median home value is $122,500. This is approximately $46,480 (61%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $95,000 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $32,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

Lexington's median rent is $636 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $57 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $426 (40%) lower, and is approximately $305 (32%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
