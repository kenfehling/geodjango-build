Home prices

The median home in Oakwood Park costs $142,300. It is roughly $9,160 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $75,200 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $12,300 (7%) lower.

Rents

Oakwood Park's median monthly rent is $1,125. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $105 (10%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and is approximately $184 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
