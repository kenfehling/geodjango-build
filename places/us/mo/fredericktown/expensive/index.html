Compared to its surrounding areas, Fredericktown is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fredericktown is valued at $85,900. This is approximately $14,360 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $131,600 (60%) lower, and is approximately $52,800 (38%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Fredericktown the median monthly rent is $721. It is roughly $182 (33%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $341 (32%) lower, and is about $107 (12%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
