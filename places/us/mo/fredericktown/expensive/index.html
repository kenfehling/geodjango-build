Home prices

The median home in Fredericktown costs $85,900. This is roughly $14,360 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $131,600 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $52,800 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fredericktown is $721 per month. It is roughly $182 (33%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $107 (12%) lower.
