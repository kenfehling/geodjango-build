Home prices

The value of the median home in Fredericktown is $85,900. It is approximately $14,360 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $131,600 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $52,800 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Fredericktown the median rent is $721 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $182 (33%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $341 (32%) lower, and is about $107 (12%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
