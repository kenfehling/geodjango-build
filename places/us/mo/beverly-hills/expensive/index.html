Compared to the surrounding areas, Beverly Hills is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Beverly Hills's median home price is $64,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $4,920 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $152,900 (70%) lower, and it is approximately $74,100 (53%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Beverly Hills the median rent is $904 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $16 (1%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $158 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $76 (9%) higher.
