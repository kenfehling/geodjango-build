Compared to its surrounding areas, Beverly Hills is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Beverly Hills is valued at $64,600. It is roughly $4,920 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $152,900 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $74,100 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Beverly Hills the median rent is $904 per month. This is roughly $16 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $158 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $76 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
