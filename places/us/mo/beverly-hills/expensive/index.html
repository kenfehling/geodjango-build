Home prices

The median home price in Beverly Hills is $64,600. It is approximately $4,920 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $152,900 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $74,100 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beverly Hills is $904. This is roughly $16 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $158 (14%) lower, and is roughly $76 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
