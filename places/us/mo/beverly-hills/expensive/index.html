Home prices

The median home price in Beverly Hills is $64,600. It is about $4,920 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $152,900 (70%) lower, and it is roughly $74,100 (53%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Beverly Hills's median rent is $904 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $16 (1%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $158 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $76 (9%) higher.
