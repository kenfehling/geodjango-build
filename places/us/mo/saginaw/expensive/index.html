Home prices

The value of the median home in Saginaw is $147,400. It is approximately $22,300 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $70,100 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $29,300 (24%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Saginaw's median rent is $792 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $38 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $270 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $48 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
