Home prices

The median home in Saginaw costs $147,400. It is about $22,300 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $70,100 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $29,300 (24%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Saginaw's median monthly rent is $792. This is approximately $38 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is approximately $48 (6%) higher.
