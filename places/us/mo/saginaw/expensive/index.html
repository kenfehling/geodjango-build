Home prices

The median home cost in Saginaw is $147,400. This is approximately $22,300 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $70,100 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $29,300 (24%) higher.

Rents

Saginaw's median rent is $792 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $38 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is roughly $48 (6%) higher.
