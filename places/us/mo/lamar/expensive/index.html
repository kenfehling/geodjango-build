Home prices

The median home in Lamar costs $89,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $17,860 (24%) higher. It is about $127,900 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $28,500 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Lamar's median monthly rent is $561. It is roughly $17 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $501 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $183 (24%) lower.
