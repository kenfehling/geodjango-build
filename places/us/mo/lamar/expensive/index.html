Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lamar is $89,600. This is roughly $17,860 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $127,900 (58%) lower, and it is approximately $28,500 (24%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lamar is $561. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $17 (2%) lower.It is roughly $501 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield is approximately $183 (24%) lower.
