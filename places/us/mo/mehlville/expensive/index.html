Home prices

The median home in Mehlville is valued at $158,000. This is approximately $5,380 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $59,500 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $19,300 (13%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Mehlville the median rent is $804 per month. This is roughly $57 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $258 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
