Home prices

The median home value in Mehlville is $158,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $5,380 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $59,500 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $19,300 (13%) higher.

Rents

Mehlville's median rent is $804 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $57 (6%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $258 (24%) lower, and it is approximately $24 (2%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
