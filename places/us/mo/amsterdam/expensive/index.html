Home prices

Amsterdam's median home cost is $97,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $7,620 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $120,500 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $57,600 (37%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Amsterdam is $818 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $55 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $244 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $123 (13%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
