Compared to other places in this area, Amsterdam is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Amsterdam costs $97,000. This is about $7,620 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $120,500 (55%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $57,600 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Amsterdam the median rent is $818 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $55 (6%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $244 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $123 (13%) lower.
