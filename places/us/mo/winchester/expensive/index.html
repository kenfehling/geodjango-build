Home prices

Rents

In Winchester the median rent is $1,210 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $180 (17%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $148 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $382 (46%) higher.
