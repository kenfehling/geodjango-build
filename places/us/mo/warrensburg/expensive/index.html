Home prices

The cost of the median home in Warrensburg is $160,400. This is approximately $84,660 (111%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $57,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $5,800 (3%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Warrensburg is $773 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $87 (12%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $289 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $168 (17%) lower.
