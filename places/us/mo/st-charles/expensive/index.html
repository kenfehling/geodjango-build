Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Charles is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Charles is valued at $200,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,080 (6%) higher. It is roughly $17,100 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $61,700 (44%) higher.

Rents

St. Charles's median rent is $985 per month. It is about $45 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $77 (7%) lower, and is roughly $157 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
