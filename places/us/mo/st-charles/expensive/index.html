Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Charles is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in St. Charles is $200,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $13,080 (6%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $17,100 (7%) lower, and is approximately $61,700 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Charles is $985. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $45 (4%) lower.It is roughly $77 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $157 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
