In comparison to other places in this area, St. Charles is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Charles is $200,400. It is roughly $13,080 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $17,100 (7%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $61,700 (44%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In St. Charles the median monthly rent is $985. It is roughly $45 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $77 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $157 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
