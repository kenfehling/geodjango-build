Compared to its nearby areas, St. Charles is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Charles is valued at $200,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $13,080 (6%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $17,100 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $61,700 (44%) higher.

Rents

In St. Charles the median rent is $985 a month. This is about $45 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $77 (7%) lower, and is approximately $157 (18%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
