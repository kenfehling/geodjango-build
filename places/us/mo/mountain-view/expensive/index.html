Home prices

The median home in Mountain View is valued at $91,200. This is approximately $17,040 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $126,300 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Rolla it is roughly $39,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

Mountain View's median rent is $479 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $56 (10%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $583 (54%) lower, and is roughly $258 (35%) lower compared to the city of Rolla.
