Home prices

Lancaster's median home cost is $67,000. It is roughly $9,460 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $150,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $49,300 (42%) lower compared to the city of Quincy.

Rents

Lancaster's median rent is $453 per month. This is about $22 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $609 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $257 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Quincy.
