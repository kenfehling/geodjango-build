Compared to other places in this area, Lancaster is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Quincy, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lancaster is $67,000. It is about $9,460 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $150,500 (69%) lower, and it is roughly $49,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Quincy.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lancaster is $453. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $22 (5%) higher. It is approximately $609 (57%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Quincy it is approximately $257 (36%) lower.
