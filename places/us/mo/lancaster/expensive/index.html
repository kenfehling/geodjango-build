Home prices

The median home cost in Lancaster is $67,000. It is about $9,460 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $150,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Quincy is about $49,300 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lancaster is $453 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22 (5%) higher.It is roughly $609 (57%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Quincy is about $257 (36%) lower.
