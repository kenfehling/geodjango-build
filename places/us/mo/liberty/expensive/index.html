Home prices

The median home in Liberty costs $182,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $63,120 (53%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $35,500 (16%) lower, and is about $27,400 (17%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Liberty is $922 per month. This is about $16 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $140 (13%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $19 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
