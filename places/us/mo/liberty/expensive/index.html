Home prices

The price of the median home in Liberty is $182,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $63,120 (53%) higher. It is roughly $35,500 (16%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is about $27,400 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Liberty is $922. Compared to nearby areas this is about $16 (1%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $140 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $19 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
