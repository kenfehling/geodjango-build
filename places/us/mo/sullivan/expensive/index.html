Home prices

The median home in Sullivan costs $104,600. It is approximately $17,300 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $112,900 (51%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $34,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Sullivan the median rent is $684 per month. This is about $10 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $378 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $144 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
