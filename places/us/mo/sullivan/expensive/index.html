Compared to other places in this area, Sullivan is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sullivan is valued at $104,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $17,300 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $112,900 (51%) lower, and is roughly $34,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Sullivan the median rent is $684 a month. This is approximately $10 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $378 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $144 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
