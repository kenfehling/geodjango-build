Compared to its surrounding areas, Crestwood is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Crestwood is $208,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $54,540 (20%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $8,900 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $69,900 (50%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Crestwood is $952. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $143 (13%) lower. It is about $110 (10%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $124 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
