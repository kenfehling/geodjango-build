Home prices

The median home in Crestwood is valued at $208,600. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $54,540 (20%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $8,900 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $69,900 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Crestwood the median rent is $952 per month. This is approximately $143 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $110 (10%) lower, and is about $124 (14%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
