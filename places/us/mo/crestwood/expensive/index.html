Home prices

The cost of the median home in Crestwood is $208,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $54,540 (20%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $8,900 (4%) lower, and is approximately $69,900 (50%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Crestwood is $952 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $143 (13%) lower.It is about $110 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $124 (14%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
