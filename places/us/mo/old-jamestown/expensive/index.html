Home prices

The median home in Old Jamestown costs $176,800. It is roughly $74,200 (72%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $40,700 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $38,100 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Old Jamestown is $1,432 a month. This is approximately $569 (66%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $370 (34%) higher, and is roughly $604 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
