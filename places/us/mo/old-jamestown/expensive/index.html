Home prices

The median home in Old Jamestown costs $176,800. This is roughly $74,200 (72%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $40,700 (18%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $38,100 (27%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Old Jamestown is $1,432. This is approximately $569 (66%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $370 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $604 (72%) higher.
