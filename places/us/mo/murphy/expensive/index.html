Compared to its surrounding areas, Murphy is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is close to average.

Home prices

Murphy's median home value is $131,900. It is roughly $98,100 (42%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $85,600 (39%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $6,800 (4%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Murphy is $914. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $252 (21%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $148 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $86 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
