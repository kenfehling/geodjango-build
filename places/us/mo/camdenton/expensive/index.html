In comparison to the surrounding areas, Camdenton is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Springfield however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Camdenton is valued at $116,400. It is about $28,560 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $101,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $1,700 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Camdenton is $756 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $55 (7%) higher. It is about $306 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield it is approximately $12 (1%) higher.
