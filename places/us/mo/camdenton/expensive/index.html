Home prices

The value of the median home in Camdenton is $116,400. It is roughly $28,560 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $101,100 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is about $1,700 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Camdenton is $756 a month. It is about $55 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $306 (28%) lower, and is roughly $12 (1%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
