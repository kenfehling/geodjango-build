Home prices

The median home in Syracuse costs $55,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $21,980 (28%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $162,500 (74%) lower, and it is roughly $136,800 (71%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Syracuse's median rent is $775 per month. It is about $160 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $112 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
