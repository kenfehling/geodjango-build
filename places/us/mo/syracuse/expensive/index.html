In comparison to other places in this area, Syracuse is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Columbia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Syracuse is $55,000. This is roughly $21,980 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $162,500 (74%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $136,800 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Syracuse's median monthly rent is $775. It is approximately $160 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is about $112 (12%) lower.
