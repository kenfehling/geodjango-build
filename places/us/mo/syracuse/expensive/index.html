In comparison to other places in this area, Syracuse is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Columbia, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Syracuse costs $55,000. This is approximately $21,980 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $162,500 (74%) lower, and is roughly $136,800 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Syracuse the median monthly rent is $775. This is approximately $160 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and is roughly $112 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
