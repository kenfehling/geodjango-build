Home prices

The median home in Pleasant Hill is valued at $178,300. It is roughly $6,900 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $39,200 (18%) lower, and is roughly $23,700 (15%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Pleasant Hill's median monthly rent is $976. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $168 (14%) lower. It is roughly $86 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $35 (3%) higher.
