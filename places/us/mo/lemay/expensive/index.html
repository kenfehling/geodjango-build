Home prices

The median home in Lemay costs $105,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $11,500 (9%) lower. It is about $112,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $33,200 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lemay is $850 per month. This is about $41 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $212 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $22 (2%) higher.
