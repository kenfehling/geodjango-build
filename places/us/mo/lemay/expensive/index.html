Home prices

The value of the median home in Lemay is $105,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $11,500 (9%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $112,000 (51%) lower, and is approximately $33,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents
