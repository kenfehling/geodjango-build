Home prices

The median home in West Plains costs $99,700. This is roughly $22,880 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $117,800 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rolla is roughly $31,400 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Plains is $624. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $84 (15%) higher. It is roughly $438 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $113 (15%) lower compared to the city of Rolla.
