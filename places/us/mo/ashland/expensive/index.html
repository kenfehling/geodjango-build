Home prices

The median home cost in Ashland is $187,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $74,680 (65%) higher.It is approximately $29,600 (13%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $3,900 (2%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $808. It is roughly $223 (38%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $254 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is roughly $79 (8%) lower.
