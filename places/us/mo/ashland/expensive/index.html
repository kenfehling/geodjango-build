Home prices

The median home in Ashland is valued at $187,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $74,680 (65%) higher. It is about $29,600 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $3,900 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Ashland's median rent is $808 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $223 (38%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and is about $79 (8%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
