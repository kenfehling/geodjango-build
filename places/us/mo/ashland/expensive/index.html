In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ashland is significantly more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbia however, it's close to average.

Home prices

Ashland's median home cost is $187,900. It is about $74,680 (65%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $29,600 (13%) lower, and it is about $3,900 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Ashland's median monthly rent is $808. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $223 (38%) higher. It is approximately $254 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is about $79 (8%) lower.
