Home prices

Eolia's median home price is $112,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $35,800 (46%) higher. It is about $105,000 (48%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $26,200 (18%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Eolia is $788. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $173 (28%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $274 (25%) lower, and is roughly $40 (4%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
