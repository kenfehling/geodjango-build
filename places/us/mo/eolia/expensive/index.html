Home prices

The value of the median home in Eolia is $112,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $35,800 (46%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $105,000 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $26,200 (18%) lower.

Rents

Eolia's median monthly rent is $788. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $173 (28%) higher. It is about $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $40 (4%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
