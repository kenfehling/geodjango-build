Home prices

The median home in Essex costs $76,800. This is approximately $3,820 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $140,700 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jonesboro is roughly $76,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Essex the median rent is $638 per month. It is approximately $57 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $424 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $126 (16%) lower compared to the city of Jonesboro.
