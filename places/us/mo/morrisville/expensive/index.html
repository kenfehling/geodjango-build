In comparison to the surrounding areas, Morrisville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home in Morrisville is valued at $112,000. This is about $7,900 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $105,500 (48%) lower, and is approximately $6,100 (5%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Morrisville the median monthly rent is $618. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $71 (10%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $444 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is about $126 (16%) lower.
