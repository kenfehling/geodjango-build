Home prices

The median home price in Smithton is $74,700. This is roughly $7,240 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $142,800 (65%) lower, and is roughly $117,100 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Smithton's median monthly rent is $717. This is about $7 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $345 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia it is roughly $170 (19%) lower.
