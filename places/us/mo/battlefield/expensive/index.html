Home prices

The median home in Battlefield is valued at $157,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $27,740 (14%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is roughly $39,500 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Battlefield the median monthly rent is $773. It is roughly $66 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $289 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $29 (3%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
