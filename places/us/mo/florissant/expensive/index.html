Home prices

The median home value in Florissant is $97,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $18,280 (15%) lower. It is about $119,900 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $41,100 (29%) lower.

Rents
