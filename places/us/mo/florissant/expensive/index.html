In comparison to its surrounding areas, Florissant is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Florissant's median home cost is $97,600. This is approximately $18,280 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $119,900 (55%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $41,100 (29%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Florissant's median rent is $1,034 per month. It is approximately $52 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $28 (2%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $206 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
