Home prices

Rents

In Marshfield the median monthly rent is $630. Compared to nearby areas it is about $29 (4%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $432 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is approximately $114 (15%) lower.
