Compared to other places in this area, Rock Hill is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rock Hill is $192,700. It is roughly $122,120 (38%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $24,800 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $54,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Rock Hill's median monthly rent is $983. Compared to other places in this area it is about $259 (20%) lower.It is approximately $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $155 (18%) higher.
