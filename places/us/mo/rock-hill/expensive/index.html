Home prices

The median home value in Rock Hill is $192,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $122,120 (38%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $24,800 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $54,000 (38%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rock Hill is $983 a month. It is about $259 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $155 (18%) higher.
