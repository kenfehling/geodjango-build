The median home price in Holt is $117,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $12,160 (11%) higher. It is roughly $100,000 (45%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $37,100 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Holt's median rent is $838 per month. It is roughly $23 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $103 (10%) lower.
