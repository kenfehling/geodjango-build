In comparison to other places in this area, Arnold is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Arnold costs $157,600. It is about $23,160 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $18,900 (13%) higher.

Rents

Arnold's median monthly rent is $955. This is approximately $41 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $107 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $127 (15%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
