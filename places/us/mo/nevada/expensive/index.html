In comparison to its surrounding areas, Nevada is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Nevada costs $71,100. It is approximately $16,880 (31%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $146,400 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $83,500 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Nevada is $645 per month. It is roughly $15 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $417 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $296 (31%) lower.
