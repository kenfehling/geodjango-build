Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Nevada is $645 per month. It is approximately $15 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $417 (39%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $296 (31%) lower.
