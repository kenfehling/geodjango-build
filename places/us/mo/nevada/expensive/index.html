Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nevada is $645. It is about $15 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $417 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $296 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
