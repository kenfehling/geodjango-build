Home prices

The median home in Bridgeton is valued at $168,700. This is about $66,900 (65%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $48,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $30,000 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bridgeton is $991. This is about $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $71 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $163 (19%) higher.
