Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bridgeton is $168,700. It is roughly $66,900 (65%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $48,800 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $30,000 (21%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Bridgeton the median rent is $991 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $55 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $71 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $163 (19%) higher.
