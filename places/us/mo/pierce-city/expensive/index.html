Home prices

The price of the median home in Pierce City is $87,800. It is approximately $12,080 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $129,700 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is about $30,300 (25%) lower.

Rents

Pierce City's median rent is $642 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24 (3%) higher. It is about $420 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $102 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
