Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Joseph is $114,100. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $103,400 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $40,500 (26%) lower.

Rents

In St. Joseph the median rent is $782 per month. It is about $84 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $280 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $159 (16%) lower.
