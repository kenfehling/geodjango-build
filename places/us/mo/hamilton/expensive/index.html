Compared to other places in this area, Hamilton is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $87,400. This is roughly $19,100 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $130,100 (59%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $67,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Hamilton the median monthly rent is $656. It is approximately $23 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $406 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $285 (30%) lower.
