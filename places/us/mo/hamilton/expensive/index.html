Home prices

The value of the median home in Hamilton is $87,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $19,100 (27%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $130,100 (59%) lower, and is about $67,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Hamilton's median monthly rent is $656. This is approximately $23 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $406 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $285 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
