Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $87,400. This is approximately $19,100 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $130,100 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $67,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Hamilton is $656 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $23 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $406 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is approximately $285 (30%) lower.
