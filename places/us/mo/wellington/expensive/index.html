Home prices

The median home cost in Wellington is $110,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $13,440 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $107,400 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is roughly $44,500 (28%) lower.

Rents

Wellington's median rent is $277 per month. It is approximately $484 (63%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $785 (73%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $664 (70%) lower.
