Home prices

The median home in Marceline costs $63,700. This is about $7,140 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $153,800 (70%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $128,100 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Marceline the median rent is $535 per month. This is roughly $15 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $527 (49%) lower, and is about $352 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
