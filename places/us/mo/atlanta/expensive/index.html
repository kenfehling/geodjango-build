Home prices

The median home in Atlanta is valued at $58,400. It is about $6,420 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $159,100 (73%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Columbia it is approximately $133,400 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Atlanta the median rent is $554 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $39 (7%) higher.It is about $508 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $333 (37%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
