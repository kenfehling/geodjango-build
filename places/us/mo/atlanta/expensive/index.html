Compared to its surrounding areas, Atlanta is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbia, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Atlanta is $58,400. This is approximately $6,420 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $159,100 (73%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is roughly $133,400 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Atlanta the median rent is $554 a month. It is about $39 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $508 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $333 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
