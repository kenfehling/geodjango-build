Home prices

The median home in Atlanta is valued at $58,400. This is roughly $6,420 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $159,100 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $133,400 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Atlanta the median rent is $554 a month. It is roughly $39 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $508 (47%) lower, and is about $333 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
