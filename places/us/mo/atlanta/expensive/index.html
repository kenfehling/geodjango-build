In comparison to nearby areas, Atlanta is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Columbia, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Atlanta's median home cost is $58,400. This is approximately $6,420 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $159,100 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia is approximately $133,400 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Atlanta the median monthly rent is $554. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $39 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $508 (47%) lower, and is approximately $333 (37%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
