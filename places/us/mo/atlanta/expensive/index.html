In comparison to other places in this area, Atlanta is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbia, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Atlanta is valued at $58,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $6,420 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $159,100 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia is roughly $133,400 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Atlanta is $554 a month. It is about $39 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $508 (47%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Columbia is roughly $333 (37%) lower.
