In comparison to other places in this area, Maplewood is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maplewood costs $189,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $106,980 (36%) lower.It is about $27,900 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $50,900 (36%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Maplewood's median rent is $763 per month. It is approximately $321 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $65 (7%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
