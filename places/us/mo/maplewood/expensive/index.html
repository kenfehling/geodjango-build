Compared to its surrounding areas, Maplewood is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home price in Maplewood is $189,600. It is roughly $106,980 (36%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $27,900 (12%) lower, and is roughly $50,900 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Maplewood is $763 a month. It is roughly $321 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $65 (7%) lower.
