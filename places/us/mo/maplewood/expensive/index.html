Compared to its surrounding areas, Maplewood is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Maplewood is $189,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $106,980 (36%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $27,900 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $50,900 (36%) higher.

Rents

In Maplewood the median rent is $763 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $321 (29%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $65 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
