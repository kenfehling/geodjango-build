Home prices

The median home in North Kansas City costs $136,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $22,260 (14%) lower. It is roughly $80,800 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $17,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

In North Kansas City the median rent is $882 a month. It is roughly $149 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $180 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $59 (6%) lower.
