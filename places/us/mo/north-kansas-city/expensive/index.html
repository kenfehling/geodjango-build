Home prices

The median home in North Kansas City is valued at $136,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $22,260 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $80,800 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $17,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

North Kansas City's median monthly rent is $882. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $149 (14%) lower. It is roughly $180 (16%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $59 (6%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
