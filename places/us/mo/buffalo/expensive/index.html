In comparison to its surrounding areas, Buffalo is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Buffalo is $96,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $7,340 (8%) higher.It is approximately $121,000 (55%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $21,600 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Buffalo is $757 per month. This is approximately $135 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
