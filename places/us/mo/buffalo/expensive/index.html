In comparison to nearby areas, Buffalo is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home in Buffalo is valued at $96,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $7,340 (8%) higher. It is roughly $121,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is roughly $21,600 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Buffalo is $757 per month. This is approximately $135 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $305 (28%) lower, and is about $13 (1%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
