In comparison to its surrounding areas, Buffalo is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Buffalo costs $96,500. It is approximately $7,340 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $121,000 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $21,600 (18%) lower.

Rents

Buffalo's median monthly rent is $757. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $135 (21%) higher.It is approximately $305 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
