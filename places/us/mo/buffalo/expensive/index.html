Home prices

Buffalo's median home price is $96,500. It is about $7,340 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $121,000 (55%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $21,600 (18%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $757 per month. This is approximately $135 (21%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is approximately $13 (1%) higher.
