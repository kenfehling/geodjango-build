Home prices

Rents

Buffalo's median rent is $757 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $135 (21%) higher.It is about $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is roughly $13 (1%) higher.
