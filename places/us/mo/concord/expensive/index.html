Home prices

The median home in Concord costs $204,100. It is roughly $26,280 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $13,400 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $65,400 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Concord is $1,080 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $48 (4%) higher. It is approximately $18 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $252 (30%) higher.
