In comparison to the surrounding areas, Concord is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home value in Concord is $204,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $26,280 (11%) lower. It is roughly $13,400 (6%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $65,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Concord the median rent is $1,080 per month. It is about $48 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $18 (1%) higher, and is roughly $252 (30%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
