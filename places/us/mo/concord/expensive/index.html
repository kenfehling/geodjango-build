Home prices

Rents

In Concord the median monthly rent is $1,080. It is about $48 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $18 (1%) higher, and is about $252 (30%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
