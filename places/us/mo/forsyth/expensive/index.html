Home prices

The median home price in Forsyth is $118,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $44,360 (59%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $98,900 (45%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Forsyth's median monthly rent is $659. It is about $21 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $403 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $85 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
