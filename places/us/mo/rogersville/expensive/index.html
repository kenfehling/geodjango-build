Home prices

The median home value in Rogersville is $132,800. This is roughly $35,880 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $84,700 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $14,700 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Rogersville the median rent is $696 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $57 (7%) lower. It is approximately $366 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $48 (6%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.
