Home prices

The median home in Wildwood costs $378,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $115,520 (44%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $160,500 (73%) higher, and it is approximately $239,300 (172%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Wildwood the median rent is $1,210 per month. This is roughly $53 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $148 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $382 (46%) higher.
