Home prices

Rents

In Lebanon the median monthly rent is $666. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $43 (6%) higher. It is about $396 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $78 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
