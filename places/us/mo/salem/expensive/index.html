In comparison to its surrounding areas, Salem is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Jefferson City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Salem is $77,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $5,620 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $140,200 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Jefferson City is approximately $70,500 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Salem is $508. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $124 (19%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $554 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Jefferson City is roughly $126 (19%) lower.
