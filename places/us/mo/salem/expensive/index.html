Compared to the surrounding areas, Salem is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Jefferson City.

Home prices

The median home in Salem costs $77,300. It is roughly $5,620 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $140,200 (64%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Jefferson City is approximately $70,500 (47%) lower.

Rents

Salem's median rent is $508 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $124 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $554 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Jefferson City it is roughly $126 (19%) lower.
