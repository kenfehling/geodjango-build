In comparison to other places in this area, Salem is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Jefferson City.

Home prices

Salem's median home value is $77,300. It is approximately $5,620 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $140,200 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Jefferson City it is about $70,500 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Salem the median rent is $508 per month. It is roughly $124 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $554 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Jefferson City is approximately $126 (19%) lower.
