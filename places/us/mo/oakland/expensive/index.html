Compared to nearby areas, Oakland is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Oakland's median home cost is $318,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $17,360 (5%) lower.It is about $101,000 (46%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $179,800 (129%) higher.

Rents

In Oakland the median monthly rent is $1,176. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $70 (5%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $114 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $348 (42%) higher.
