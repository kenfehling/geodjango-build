Compared to its surrounding areas, Oakland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Oakland's median home price is $318,500. It is about $17,360 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $101,000 (46%) higher, and it is approximately $179,800 (129%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Oakland's median rent is $1,176 a month. This is about $70 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $114 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $348 (42%) higher.
