In comparison to other places in this area, Oakland is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $318,500. It is approximately $17,360 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $101,000 (46%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $179,800 (129%) higher.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $1,176 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $70 (5%) lower. It is roughly $114 (10%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $348 (42%) higher.
