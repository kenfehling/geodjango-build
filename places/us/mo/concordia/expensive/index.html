Home prices

The median home cost in Concordia is $106,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $27,120 (34%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $111,200 (51%) lower, and is approximately $48,300 (31%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Concordia's median rent is $618 per month. This is roughly $103 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $444 (41%) lower, and is approximately $323 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
