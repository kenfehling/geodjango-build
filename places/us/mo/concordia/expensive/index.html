Home prices

The median home in Concordia costs $106,300. It is about $27,120 (34%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $111,200 (51%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $48,300 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Concordia is $618. It is about $103 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $444 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $323 (34%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
