Home prices

The median home in Maryland Heights is valued at $158,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $6,900 (4%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $58,700 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $20,100 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Maryland Heights the median monthly rent is $1,043. This is roughly $61 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $19 (1%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $215 (25%) higher.
