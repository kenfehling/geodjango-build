Home prices

Rents

In Maryland Heights the median rent is $1,043 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $61 (6%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $19 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $215 (25%) higher.
