Rents

Brooklyn Heights's median monthly rent is $875. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $76 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $187 (17%) lower, and is approximately $131 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
