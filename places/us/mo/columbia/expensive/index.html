The cost of the median home in Columbia is $191,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $54,383 (39%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $25,700 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Columbia the median monthly rent is $887. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $188 (26%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $175 (16%) lower.
