Home prices

Manchester's median home value is $229,700. This is approximately $12,500 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $12,200 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $91,000 (65%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Manchester is $1,061. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $147 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is is very similar, and it is about $233 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
