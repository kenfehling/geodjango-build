Home prices

The median home price in Manchester is $229,700. This is approximately $12,500 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $12,200 (5%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $91,000 (65%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Manchester the median monthly rent is $1,061. It is about $147 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $233 (28%) higher.
