Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pasadena Hills is $197,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $118,500 (150%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $20,000 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $58,800 (42%) higher.

Rents

Pasadena Hills's median rent is $888 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $28 (3%) lower. It is approximately $174 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $60 (7%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
