Home prices

The median home in Hillsdale costs $60,500. This is about $5,940 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $157,000 (72%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $78,200 (56%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Hillsdale's median rent is $930 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13 (1%) higher.It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $102 (12%) higher.
