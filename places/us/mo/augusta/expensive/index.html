Home prices

The value of the median home in Augusta is $204,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $59,940 (41%) higher. It is approximately $12,700 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $66,100 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Augusta the median rent is $842 per month. This is roughly $41 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $220 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $14 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
