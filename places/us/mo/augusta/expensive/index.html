Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Augusta is $842 a month. It is roughly $41 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $220 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $14 (1%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
