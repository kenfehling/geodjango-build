Home prices

The median home value in Joplin is $125,900. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $91,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $7,800 (6%) higher.

Rents

Joplin's median monthly rent is $789. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $41 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $273 (25%) lower, and it is about $45 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
