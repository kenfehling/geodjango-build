Home prices

The value of the median home in Blue Springs is $166,000. This is approximately $40,480 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $51,500 (23%) lower, and is roughly $11,400 (7%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Blue Springs the median rent is $971 per month. This is approximately $56 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $91 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $30 (3%) higher.
