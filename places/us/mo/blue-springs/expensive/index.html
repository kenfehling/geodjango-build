In comparison to its surrounding areas, Blue Springs is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The median home in Blue Springs costs $166,000. This is about $40,480 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $51,500 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $11,400 (7%) higher.

Rents

Blue Springs's median rent is $971 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $56 (5%) lower.It is roughly $91 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $30 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
