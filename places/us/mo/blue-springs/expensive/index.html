In comparison to other places in this area, Blue Springs is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Kansas City however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Blue Springs is $166,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $40,480 (19%) lower. It is roughly $51,500 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $11,400 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Blue Springs's median monthly rent is $971. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $56 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $91 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $30 (3%) higher.
