Home prices

The cost of the median home in Anderson is $86,800. It is approximately $3,240 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $130,700 (60%) lower, and is about $31,300 (26%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Anderson is $698 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $91 (14%) higher. It is approximately $364 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $46 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
