Compared to other places in this area, Moscow Mills is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Moscow Mills is valued at $95,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $87,420 (47%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $121,600 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $42,800 (30%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Moscow Mills's median monthly rent is $975. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $48 (5%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $87 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $147 (17%) higher.
