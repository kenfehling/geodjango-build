In comparison to the surrounding areas, Moscow Mills is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

Moscow Mills's median home price is $95,900. This is roughly $87,420 (47%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $121,600 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $42,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Moscow Mills the median rent is $975 a month. This is approximately $48 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $87 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $147 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
