In comparison to other places in this area, Moscow Mills is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Moscow Mills is valued at $95,900. It is about $87,420 (47%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $121,600 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $42,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Moscow Mills the median monthly rent is $975. This is roughly $48 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $87 (8%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $147 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
