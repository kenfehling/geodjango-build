Home prices

The median home value in Moscow Mills is $95,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $87,420 (47%) lower.It is roughly $121,600 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $42,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

Moscow Mills's median rent is $975 a month. It is roughly $48 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $87 (8%) lower, and is approximately $147 (17%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
