Compared to the surrounding areas, Gainesville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Springfield, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Gainesville's median home cost is $81,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $28,780 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $136,200 (62%) lower, and is approximately $36,800 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Gainesville the median monthly rent is $568. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $37 (6%) lower. It is about $494 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $176 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
