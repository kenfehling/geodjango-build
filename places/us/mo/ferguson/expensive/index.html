Home prices

Rents

In Ferguson the median rent is $929 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $124 (11%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $133 (12%) lower, and is approximately $101 (12%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
