Home prices

Rents

In Ferguson the median monthly rent is $929. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $124 (11%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $133 (12%) lower, and is approximately $101 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
