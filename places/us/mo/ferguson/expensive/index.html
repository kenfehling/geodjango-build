Compared to its nearby areas, Ferguson is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ferguson costs $83,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $15,840 (23%) higher. It is about $134,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $55,200 (39%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Ferguson the median rent is $929 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $124 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $133 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $101 (12%) higher.
