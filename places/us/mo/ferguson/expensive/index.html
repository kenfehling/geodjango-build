Compared to the surrounding areas, Ferguson is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Ferguson costs $83,500. It is roughly $15,840 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $134,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $55,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Ferguson the median monthly rent is $929. This is approximately $124 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $133 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $101 (12%) higher.
