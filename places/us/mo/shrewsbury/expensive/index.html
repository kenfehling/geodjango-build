Home prices

The value of the median home in Shrewsbury is $174,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $40,860 (18%) lower. It is about $43,200 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $35,600 (25%) higher.

Rents

Shrewsbury's median monthly rent is $915. This is approximately $108 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $147 (13%) lower, and is approximately $87 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
