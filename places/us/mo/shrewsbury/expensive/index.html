Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Shrewsbury is $915 per month. It is roughly $108 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $87 (10%) higher.
