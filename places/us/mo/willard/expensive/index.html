Home prices

The median home in Willard costs $139,400. This is about $28,600 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $78,100 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $21,300 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Willard is $834. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $95 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is about $90 (12%) higher.
