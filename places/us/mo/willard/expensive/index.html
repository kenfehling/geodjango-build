Home prices

The cost of the median home in Willard is $139,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $28,600 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $78,100 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $21,300 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Willard the median rent is $834 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $95 (12%) higher. It is approximately $228 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $90 (12%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
