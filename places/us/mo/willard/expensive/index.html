Home prices

The median home in Willard is valued at $139,400. It is roughly $28,600 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $78,100 (35%) lower, and is about $21,300 (18%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Willard is $834 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $95 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $228 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is roughly $90 (12%) higher.
