Home prices

Affton's median home price is $149,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $13,240 (8%) lower. It is roughly $68,200 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $10,600 (7%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Affton is $885. It is roughly $32 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $177 (16%) lower, and is roughly $57 (6%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
