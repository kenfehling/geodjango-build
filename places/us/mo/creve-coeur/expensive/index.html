Compared to the surrounding areas, Creve Coeur is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Creve Coeur is $402,300. It is approximately $53,900 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $184,800 (84%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $263,600 (190%) higher.

Rents

Creve Coeur's median monthly rent is $1,244. This is approximately $27 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $182 (17%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $416 (50%) higher.
