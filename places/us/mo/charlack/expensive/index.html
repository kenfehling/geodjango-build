Home prices

The price of the median home in Charlack is $78,600. It is about $14,440 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $138,900 (63%) lower, and is roughly $60,100 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Charlack's median rent is $913 a month. This is about $30 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $149 (14%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $85 (10%) higher.
