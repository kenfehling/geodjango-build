Home prices

The median home in Charlack costs $78,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $14,440 (15%) lower. It is about $138,900 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $60,100 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Charlack's median rent is $913 a month. This is approximately $30 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $149 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $85 (10%) higher.
