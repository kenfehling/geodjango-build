The value of the median home in Olivette is $338,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $219,980 (186%) higher. It is approximately $120,600 (55%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $199,400 (143%) higher.

Rents
