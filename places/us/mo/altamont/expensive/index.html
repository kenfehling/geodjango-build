Home prices

The value of the median home in Altamont is $46,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $14,320 (23%) lower. It is roughly $171,500 (78%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $108,600 (70%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Altamont is $692. This is approximately $60 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $370 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $249 (26%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
