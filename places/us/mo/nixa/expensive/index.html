Compared to nearby areas, Nixa is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Nixa is $160,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $22,760 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $56,900 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is roughly $42,500 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Nixa is $834 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $90 (12%) higher.
