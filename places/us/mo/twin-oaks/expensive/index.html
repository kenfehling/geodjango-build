Home prices

The median home in Twin Oaks is valued at $177,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $75,260 (29%) lower.It is roughly $40,100 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $38,700 (27%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Twin Oaks's median rent is $825 per month. This is about $430 (34%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $237 (22%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
