Compared to its surrounding areas, Jonesburg is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Jonesburg is valued at $103,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $13,820 (15%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $114,000 (52%) lower, and it is approximately $35,200 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Jonesburg's median monthly rent is $746. It is roughly $41 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $316 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $82 (9%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
