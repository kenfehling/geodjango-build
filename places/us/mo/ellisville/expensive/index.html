In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ellisville is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Ellisville costs $250,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $12,860 (5%) higher. It is about $33,200 (15%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $112,000 (80%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ellisville is $1,130. It is about $41 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $68 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $302 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
