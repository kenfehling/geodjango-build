Ellisville's median home price is $250,700. This is about $12,860 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $33,200 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $112,000 (80%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Ellisville's median rent is $1,130 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $41 (3%) higher. It is about $68 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $302 (36%) higher.
