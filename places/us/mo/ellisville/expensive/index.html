Home prices

Rents

Ellisville's median rent is $1,130 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $41 (3%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $68 (6%) higher, and it is approximately $302 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
