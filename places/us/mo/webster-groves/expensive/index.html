Compared to other places in this area, Webster Groves is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Webster Groves costs $296,800. This is approximately $43,160 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $79,300 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $158,100 (113%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Webster Groves is $1,349 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $296 (28%) higher. It is about $287 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $521 (62%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
