Home prices

Rents

St. Martins's median rent is $587 a month. It is roughly $44 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $475 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is roughly $300 (33%) lower.
