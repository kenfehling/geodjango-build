Home prices

The median home cost in Lockwood is $65,600. This is roughly $4,460 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $151,900 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is about $52,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

Lockwood's median rent is $686 a month. It is about $140 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $376 (35%) lower, and it is about $58 (7%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.
