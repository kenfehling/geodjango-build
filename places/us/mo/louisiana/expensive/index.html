Home prices

The price of the median home in Louisiana is $84,400. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $14,540 (20%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $133,100 (61%) lower, and is approximately $54,300 (39%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Louisiana is $694. This is about $187 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $368 (34%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $134 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
