In comparison to the surrounding areas, O'Fallon is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

O'Fallon's median home cost is $226,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $71,180 (23%) lower.It is about $8,800 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $87,600 (63%) higher.

Rents

O'Fallon's median rent is $1,103 a month. This is about $64 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $41 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $275 (33%) higher.
