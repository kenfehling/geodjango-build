Home prices

O'Fallon's median home value is $226,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $71,180 (23%) lower.It is about $8,800 (4%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $87,600 (63%) higher.

Rents

In O'Fallon the median monthly rent is $1,103. It is roughly $64 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $275 (33%) higher.
