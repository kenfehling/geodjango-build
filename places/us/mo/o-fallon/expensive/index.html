Home prices

The median home in O'Fallon costs $226,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $71,180 (23%) lower. It is approximately $8,800 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $87,600 (63%) higher.

Rents

In O'Fallon the median monthly rent is $1,103. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $64 (6%) higher. It is about $41 (3%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $275 (33%) higher.
