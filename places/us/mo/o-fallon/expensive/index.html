Home prices

The cost of the median home in O'Fallon is $226,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $71,180 (23%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $8,800 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $87,600 (63%) higher.

Rents

In O'Fallon the median monthly rent is $1,103. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $64 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $41 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $275 (33%) higher.
