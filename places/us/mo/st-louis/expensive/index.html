Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Louis is $138,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $62,717 (31%) lower.It is roughly $78,800 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole.

Rents

In St. Louis the median monthly rent is $828. It is about $53 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $234 (22%) lower.
