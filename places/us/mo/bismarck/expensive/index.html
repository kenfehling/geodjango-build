In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bismarck is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Bismarck is valued at $78,900. It is approximately $15,540 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $138,600 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $59,800 (43%) lower.

Rents

Bismarck's median rent is $752 a month. It is approximately $42 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $310 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $76 (9%) lower.
