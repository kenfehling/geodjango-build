In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bismarck is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

Bismarck's median home cost is $78,900. This is about $15,540 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $138,600 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $59,800 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Bismarck the median monthly rent is $752. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $42 (5%) higher. It is roughly $310 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $76 (9%) lower.
