Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Bismarck is $752 a month. It is roughly $42 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $310 (29%) lower, and is approximately $76 (9%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
