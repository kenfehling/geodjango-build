Home prices

The median home price in Bismarck is $78,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $15,540 (24%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $138,600 (63%) lower, and is roughly $59,800 (43%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Bismarck the median monthly rent is $752. It is about $42 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $310 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $76 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
