Home prices

The median home in Josephville costs $273,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $6,360 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $56,000 (25%) higher, and it is approximately $134,800 (97%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Josephville the median rent is $688 a month. This is roughly $348 (33%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $374 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $140 (16%) lower.
