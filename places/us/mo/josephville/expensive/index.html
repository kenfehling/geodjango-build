Home prices

The price of the median home in Josephville is $273,500. It is roughly $6,360 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $56,000 (25%) higher, and is about $134,800 (97%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Josephville is $688. This is approximately $348 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $374 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $140 (16%) lower.
