Home prices

Josephville's median home cost is $273,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $6,360 (2%) higher.It is approximately $56,000 (25%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $134,800 (97%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Josephville the median rent is $688 per month. This is roughly $348 (33%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $374 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $140 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
