Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jackson is $164,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $59,620 (56%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $52,700 (24%) lower, and it is about $47,300 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Carbondale.

Rents

Jackson's median monthly rent is $880. It is roughly $105 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $182 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale it is approximately $166 (23%) higher.
