Home prices

The price of the median home in Windsor is $75,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $5,120 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $141,600 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $78,700 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Windsor is $619 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $60 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and it is about $322 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
