Home prices

The value of the median home in Windsor is $75,900. This is roughly $5,120 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $141,600 (65%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $78,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Windsor is $619 per month. It is about $60 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $443 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City is about $322 (34%) lower.
