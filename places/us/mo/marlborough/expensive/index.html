In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marlborough is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home price in Marlborough is $167,900. This is approximately $28,740 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $49,600 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $29,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Marlborough is $782 per month. It is roughly $233 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $280 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $46 (5%) lower.
