Home prices

The median home in Marlborough costs $167,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $28,740 (14%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $49,600 (22%) lower, and it is about $29,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Marlborough the median monthly rent is $782. This is roughly $233 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $280 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $46 (5%) lower.
