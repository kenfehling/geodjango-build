In comparison to the surrounding areas, Marlborough is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marlborough costs $167,900. It is roughly $28,740 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $49,600 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $29,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marlborough is $782. It is about $233 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $46 (5%) lower.
