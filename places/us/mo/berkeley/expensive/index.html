In comparison to its surrounding areas, Berkeley is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Berkeley's median home value is $63,700. It is approximately $11,820 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $153,800 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $75,000 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Berkeley the median monthly rent is $993. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $22 (2%) lower. It is about $69 (6%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $165 (19%) higher.
