Compared to the surrounding areas, Berkeley is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Berkeley is valued at $63,700. It is about $11,820 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $153,800 (70%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $75,000 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Berkeley the median rent is $993 a month. It is roughly $22 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $69 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $165 (19%) higher.
