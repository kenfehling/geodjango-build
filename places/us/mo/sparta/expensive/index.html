Home prices

Sparta's median home cost is $103,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $85,400 (45%) lower.It is roughly $114,500 (52%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is approximately $15,100 (12%) lower.

Rents

Sparta's median rent is $722 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $76 (9%) lower.It is approximately $340 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $22 (2%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.
