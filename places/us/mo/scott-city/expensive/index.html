Compared to its surrounding areas, Scott City is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Carbondale, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Scott City is $93,300. This is about $15,060 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $124,200 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale is about $24,200 (20%) lower.

Rents

Scott City's median monthly rent is $751. This is approximately $73 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $311 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale it is roughly $37 (5%) higher.
