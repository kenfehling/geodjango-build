In comparison to other places in this area, Scott City is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Carbondale, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Scott City costs $93,300. This is roughly $15,060 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $124,200 (57%) lower, and is about $24,200 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Carbondale.

Rents

In Scott City the median monthly rent is $751. It is approximately $73 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $311 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $37 (5%) higher compared to the city of Carbondale.
