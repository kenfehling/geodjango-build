In comparison to other places in this area, Scott City is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Carbondale.

Home prices

Scott City's median home value is $93,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,060 (13%) lower.It is approximately $124,200 (57%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale it is about $24,200 (20%) lower.

Rents

Scott City's median rent is $751 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $73 (10%) higher.It is roughly $311 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale is approximately $37 (5%) higher.
