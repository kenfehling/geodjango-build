Home prices

The median home in St. George costs $117,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $29,580 (20%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $100,100 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $21,300 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. George is $717. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $221 (23%) lower. It is approximately $345 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $111 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
