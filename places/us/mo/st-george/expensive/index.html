Home prices

St. George's median home price is $117,400. It is approximately $29,580 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $100,100 (46%) lower, and is roughly $21,300 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In St. George the median monthly rent is $717. It is approximately $221 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $345 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $111 (13%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
