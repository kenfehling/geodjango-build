Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. George is $117,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $29,580 (20%) lower.It is roughly $100,100 (46%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $21,300 (15%) lower.

Rents

St. George's median rent is $717 per month. It is about $221 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $345 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $111 (13%) lower.
