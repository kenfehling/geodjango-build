Home prices

The median home price in Butler is $81,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $7,620 (8%) lower. It is roughly $136,400 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $73,500 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Butler is $642 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $56 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $420 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is about $299 (31%) lower.
