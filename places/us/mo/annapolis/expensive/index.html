Home prices

The median home value in Annapolis is $80,500. This is roughly $6,160 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $137,000 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cape Girardeau it is about $71,300 (46%) lower.

Rents

Annapolis's median monthly rent is $625. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $64 (11%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and is approximately $169 (21%) lower compared to the city of Cape Girardeau.
