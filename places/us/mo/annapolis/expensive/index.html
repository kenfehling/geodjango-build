Compared to the surrounding areas, Annapolis is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Cape Girardeau.

Home prices

The median home in Annapolis is valued at $80,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $6,160 (8%) higher.It is roughly $137,000 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $71,300 (46%) lower compared to the city of Cape Girardeau.

Rents

Annapolis's median rent is $625 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $64 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $437 (41%) lower, and it is about $169 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Cape Girardeau.
