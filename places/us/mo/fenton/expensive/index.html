Compared to other places in this area, Fenton is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fenton is valued at $269,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $50,400 (23%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $51,500 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $130,300 (93%) higher.

Rents

In Fenton the median rent is $1,218 a month. This is about $131 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $156 (14%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $390 (47%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
