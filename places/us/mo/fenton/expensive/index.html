Compared to nearby areas, Fenton is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

Fenton's median home cost is $269,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $50,400 (23%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $51,500 (23%) higher, and it is approximately $130,300 (93%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Fenton's median monthly rent is $1,218. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $131 (12%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $156 (14%) higher, and it is about $390 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
