Home prices

Fenton's median home value is $269,000. It is approximately $50,400 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $51,500 (23%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $130,300 (93%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Fenton the median rent is $1,218 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $131 (12%) higher. It is roughly $156 (14%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $390 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
