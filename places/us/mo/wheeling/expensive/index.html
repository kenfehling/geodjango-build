Home prices

The median home price in Wheeling is $65,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $4,620 (6%) lower. It is roughly $152,500 (70%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $89,600 (57%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Wheeling the median rent is $678 a month. This is roughly $170 (33%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $384 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $263 (27%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
