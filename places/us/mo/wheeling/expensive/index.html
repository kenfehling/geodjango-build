Compared to the surrounding areas, Wheeling is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Wheeling is $65,000. This is approximately $4,620 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $152,500 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $89,600 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Wheeling is $678 a month. It is about $170 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $384 (36%) lower, and is roughly $263 (27%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
