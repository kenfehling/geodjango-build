Home prices

Wheeling's median home price is $65,000. It is roughly $4,620 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $152,500 (70%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $89,600 (57%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Wheeling's median rent is $678 a month. It is roughly $170 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $384 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $263 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
