Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City costs $147,800. It is about $26,920 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $69,700 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia is approximately $44,000 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Jefferson City is $634 per month. It is roughly $23 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $428 (40%) lower, and is about $253 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
