Compared to the surrounding areas, Jefferson City is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Columbia.

Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City is valued at $147,800. It is approximately $26,920 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $69,700 (32%) lower, and is approximately $44,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Jefferson City's median monthly rent is $634. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $23 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $428 (40%) lower, and is about $253 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
