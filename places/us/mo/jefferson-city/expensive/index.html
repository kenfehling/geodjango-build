In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jefferson City is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Columbia, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jefferson City is valued at $147,800. This is roughly $26,920 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $69,700 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $44,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

Jefferson City's median rent is $634 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $23 (3%) lower. It is approximately $428 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Columbia it is approximately $253 (28%) lower.
