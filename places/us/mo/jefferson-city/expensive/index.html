Home prices

The value of the median home in Jefferson City is $147,800. This is roughly $26,920 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $69,700 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $44,000 (22%) lower.

Rents

Jefferson City's median rent is $634 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $23 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $428 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $253 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
