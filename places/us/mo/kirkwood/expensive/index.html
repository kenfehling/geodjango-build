Home prices

The median home in Kirkwood costs $315,000. This is roughly $52,160 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $97,500 (44%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $176,300 (127%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Kirkwood's median rent is $1,074 per month. This is roughly $297 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $12 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $246 (29%) higher.
