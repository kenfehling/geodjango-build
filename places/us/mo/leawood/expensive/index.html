Home prices

The median home in Leawood is valued at $188,400. It is approximately $76,520 (68%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $29,100 (13%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield is roughly $70,300 (59%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Leawood is $570. It is approximately $293 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $492 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $174 (23%) lower.
