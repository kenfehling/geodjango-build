Home prices

The median home value in Leawood is $188,400. This is about $76,520 (68%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $29,100 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $70,300 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Leawood the median rent is $570 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $293 (33%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $492 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $174 (23%) lower.
