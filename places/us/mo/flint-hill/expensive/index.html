Home prices

The median home in Flint Hill costs $276,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $22,640 (8%) higher. It is about $59,100 (27%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $137,900 (99%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Flint Hill is $975 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $79 (8%) higher. It is roughly $87 (8%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $147 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
