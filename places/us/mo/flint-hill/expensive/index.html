In comparison to other places in this area, Flint Hill is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Flint Hill is valued at $276,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $22,640 (8%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $59,100 (27%) higher, and is roughly $137,900 (99%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Flint Hill's median monthly rent is $975. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $79 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $87 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $147 (17%) higher.
