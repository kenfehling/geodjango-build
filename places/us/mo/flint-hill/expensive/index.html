Home prices

Rents

Flint Hill's median monthly rent is $975. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $79 (8%) higher.It is about $87 (8%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $147 (17%) higher.
