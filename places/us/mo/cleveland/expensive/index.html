Compared to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is significantly more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Kansas City, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $209,900. It is approximately $75,780 (56%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $7,600 (3%) lower, and is approximately $55,300 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $856. This is roughly $36 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $85 (9%) lower.
