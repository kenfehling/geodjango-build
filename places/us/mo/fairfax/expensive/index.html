Compared to nearby areas, Fairfax is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Kansas City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairfax is $66,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $8,540 (14%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $151,300 (69%) lower, and it is roughly $88,400 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Fairfax the median rent is $482 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $99 (17%) lower.It is roughly $580 (54%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $459 (48%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
