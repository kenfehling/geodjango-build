Home prices

The median home in Fairfax costs $66,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $8,540 (14%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $151,300 (69%) lower, and it is about $88,400 (57%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Fairfax the median monthly rent is $482. This is approximately $99 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $580 (54%) lower, and is roughly $459 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
