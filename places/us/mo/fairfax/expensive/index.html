Home prices

Rents

Fairfax's median monthly rent is $482. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $99 (17%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $580 (54%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is approximately $459 (48%) lower.
