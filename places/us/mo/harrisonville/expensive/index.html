Home prices

Rents

In Harrisonville the median monthly rent is $845. Compared to nearby areas it is about $21 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $217 (20%) lower, and it is roughly $96 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
