Home prices

Pleasant Valley's median home value is $148,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $9,040 (6%) higher. It is approximately $69,000 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City it is approximately $6,100 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pleasant Valley is $924 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $14 (1%) lower. It is roughly $138 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
