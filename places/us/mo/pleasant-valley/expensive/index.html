The median home cost in Pleasant Valley is $148,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $9,040 (6%) higher.It is roughly $69,000 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $6,100 (3%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Pleasant Valley the median rent is $924 per month. This is roughly $14 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $138 (12%) lower, and is about $17 (1%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
