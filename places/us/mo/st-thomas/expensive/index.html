Rents

In St. Thomas the median monthly rent is $820. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $272 (49%) higher. It is roughly $242 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $67 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
