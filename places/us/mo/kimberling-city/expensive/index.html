Home prices

The price of the median home in Kimberling City is $158,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $25,760 (14%) lower.It is roughly $59,300 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $40,100 (33%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Kimberling City the median rent is $542 a month. This is about $248 (31%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $520 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $202 (27%) lower.
