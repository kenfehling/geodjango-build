In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kimberling City is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home in Kimberling City is valued at $158,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $25,760 (14%) lower. It is about $59,300 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $40,100 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kimberling City is $542. This is about $248 (31%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $520 (48%) lower, and is approximately $202 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
