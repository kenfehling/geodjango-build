Home prices

The median home in Warrenton costs $127,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $29,800 (18%) lower. It is about $89,800 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $11,000 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Warrenton the median monthly rent is $803. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $259 (24%) lower, and is approximately $25 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
