In comparison to its surrounding areas, St. Peters is significantly less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home price in St. Peters is $182,200. This is approximately $104,500 (36%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $35,300 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $43,500 (31%) higher.

Rents

In St. Peters the median rent is $990 per month. It is about $277 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $72 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $162 (19%) higher.
