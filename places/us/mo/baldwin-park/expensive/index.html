Rents

The median rent in Baldwin Park is $850 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $319 (27%) lower. It is roughly $212 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $91 (9%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
