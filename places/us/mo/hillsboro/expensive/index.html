Home prices

Hillsboro's median home cost is $153,600. It is roughly $33,820 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $63,900 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $14,900 (10%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Hillsboro the median rent is $835 per month. This is roughly $129 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $227 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of St. Louis.
