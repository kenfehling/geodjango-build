Compared to its nearby areas, Wentzville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wentzville is $228,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $70,900 (23%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $10,900 (5%) higher, and is about $89,700 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Wentzville is $1,009 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $89 (9%) higher. It is approximately $53 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $181 (21%) higher.
