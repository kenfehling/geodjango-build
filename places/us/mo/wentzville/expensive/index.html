The median home in Wentzville costs $228,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $70,900 (23%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $10,900 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $89,700 (64%) higher.

Rents

Wentzville's median monthly rent is $1,009. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $89 (9%) higher. It is approximately $53 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $181 (21%) higher.
