Home prices

The median home in Wentzville is valued at $228,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $70,900 (23%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $10,900 (5%) higher, and is approximately $89,700 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Wentzville is $1,009 a month. This is about $89 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $53 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $181 (21%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
