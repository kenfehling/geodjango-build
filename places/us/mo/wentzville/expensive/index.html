Home prices

The median home in Wentzville costs $228,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $70,900 (23%) lower.It is approximately $10,900 (5%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $89,700 (64%) higher.

Rents

In Wentzville the median monthly rent is $1,009. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $89 (9%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $53 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $181 (21%) higher.
