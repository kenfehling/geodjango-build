Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakville is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Oakville is $234,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $54,480 (30%) higher. It is roughly $17,300 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $96,100 (69%) higher.

Rents

Oakville's median rent is $907 per month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is about $155 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $79 (9%) higher.
