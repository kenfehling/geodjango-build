The value of the median home in Oakville is $234,800. It is approximately $54,480 (30%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $17,300 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $96,100 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Oakville's median rent is $907 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is is very similar. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $155 (14%) lower, and it is about $79 (9%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
