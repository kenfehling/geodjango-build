Home prices

The median home value in Valley Park is $179,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $73,920 (29%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower, and is approximately $41,000 (29%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Valley Park's median rent is $998 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $239 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $64 (6%) lower, and is about $170 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
