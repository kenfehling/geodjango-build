In comparison to the surrounding areas, Valley Park is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Valley Park is $179,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $73,920 (29%) lower. It is roughly $37,800 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $41,000 (29%) higher.

Rents

Valley Park's median rent is $998 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $239 (19%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $64 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $170 (20%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
