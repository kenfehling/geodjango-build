Home prices

Osceola's median home value is $73,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $17,560 (31%) higher. It is roughly $144,400 (66%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $81,500 (52%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Osceola is $423. This is approximately $176 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $639 (60%) lower, and is roughly $518 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
