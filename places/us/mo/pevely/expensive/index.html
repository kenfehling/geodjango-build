In comparison to its nearby areas, Pevely is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Pevely costs $132,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $14,900 (10%) lower. It is approximately $85,000 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $6,200 (4%) lower.

Rents

Pevely's median rent is $702 a month. It is roughly $379 (35%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $360 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $126 (15%) lower.
