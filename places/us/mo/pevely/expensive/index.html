Compared to the surrounding areas, Pevely is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Pevely is $132,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $14,900 (10%) lower. It is roughly $85,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $6,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Pevely is $702 a month. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $360 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $126 (15%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
