Home prices

The median home in Weldon Spring is valued at $397,300. This is approximately $117,740 (42%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $179,800 (82%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $258,600 (186%) higher.

Rents
