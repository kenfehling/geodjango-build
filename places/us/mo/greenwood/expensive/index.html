Home prices

The median home in Greenwood is valued at $170,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $38,420 (18%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $47,000 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $15,900 (10%) higher.

Rents

Greenwood's median rent is $1,454 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $384 (35%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $392 (36%) higher, and it is about $513 (54%) higher compared to the city of Kansas City.
