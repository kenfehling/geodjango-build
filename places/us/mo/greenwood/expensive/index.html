In comparison to its surrounding areas, Greenwood is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Greenwood's median home price is $170,500. It is about $38,420 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $47,000 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $15,900 (10%) higher.

Rents

Greenwood's median monthly rent is $1,454. It is roughly $384 (35%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $392 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $513 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
