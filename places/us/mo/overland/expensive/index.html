Home prices

The median home in Overland costs $89,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $7,780 (9%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $128,500 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $49,700 (35%) lower.

Rents

Overland's median monthly rent is $966. This is roughly $45 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $96 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $138 (16%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
