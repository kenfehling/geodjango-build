Compared to other places in this area, Perryville is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Perryville costs $129,300. It is approximately $23,060 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $88,200 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $9,400 (6%) lower.

Rents

Perryville's median monthly rent is $714. This is about $174 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $348 (32%) lower, and is about $114 (13%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
