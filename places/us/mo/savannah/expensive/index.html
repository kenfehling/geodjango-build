The median home price in Savannah is $113,500. This is approximately $29,880 (35%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $104,000 (47%) lower, and it is about $41,100 (26%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

In Savannah the median monthly rent is $753. This is about $113 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $309 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is about $188 (19%) lower.
