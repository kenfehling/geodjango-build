In comparison to its surrounding areas, Savannah is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Kansas City, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Savannah is valued at $113,500. This is approximately $29,880 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $104,000 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kansas City it is roughly $41,100 (26%) lower.

Rents

Savannah's median rent is $753 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $113 (17%) higher. It is about $309 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Kansas City is roughly $188 (19%) lower.
