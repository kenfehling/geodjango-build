Home prices

The median home in St. Paul is valued at $330,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $97,340 (41%) higher.It is about $112,500 (51%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $191,300 (137%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in St. Paul is $963 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $35 (3%) lower.It is approximately $99 (9%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $135 (16%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
