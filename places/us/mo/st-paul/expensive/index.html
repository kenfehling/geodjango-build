Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Paul is $963. It is about $35 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $99 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $135 (16%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
