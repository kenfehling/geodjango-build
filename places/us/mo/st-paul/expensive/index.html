In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Paul is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Paul is valued at $330,000. It is about $97,340 (41%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $112,500 (51%) higher, and it is about $191,300 (137%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

St. Paul's median monthly rent is $963. It is approximately $35 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $99 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $135 (16%) higher.
