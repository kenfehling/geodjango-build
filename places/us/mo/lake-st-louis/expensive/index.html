Home prices

Lake St. Louis's median home price is $274,400. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $56,900 (26%) higher, and it is about $135,700 (97%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Lake St. Louis's median monthly rent is $1,130. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $143 (14%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $68 (6%) higher, and is approximately $302 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
