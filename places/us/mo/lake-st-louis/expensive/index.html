Home prices

The value of the median home in Lake St. Louis is $274,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $56,900 (26%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $135,700 (97%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Lake St. Louis's median rent is $1,130 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $143 (14%) higher.It is roughly $68 (6%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $302 (36%) higher.
