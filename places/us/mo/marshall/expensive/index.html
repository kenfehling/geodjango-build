Rents

The median monthly rent in Marshall is $647. This is approximately $53 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $415 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $294 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
