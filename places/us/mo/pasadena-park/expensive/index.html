Home prices

The median home cost in Pasadena Park is $104,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $7,020 (7%) higher. It is roughly $112,900 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $34,100 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pasadena Park is $1,097 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $223 (25%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $35 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $269 (32%) higher.
