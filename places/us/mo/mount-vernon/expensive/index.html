Home prices

The median home value in Mount Vernon is $106,400. This is approximately $54,040 (103%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $111,100 (51%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $11,700 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Mount Vernon's median rent is $584 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $92 (18%) higher.It is approximately $478 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield it is about $160 (21%) lower.
