In comparison to its surrounding areas, Branson is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Springfieldhowever, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Branson costs $157,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $36,160 (29%) higher. It is roughly $60,000 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $39,400 (33%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Branson's median rent is $857 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $190 (28%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $205 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is approximately $113 (15%) higher.
