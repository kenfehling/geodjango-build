In comparison to nearby areas, Branson is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Branson's median home price is $157,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $36,160 (29%) higher.It is approximately $60,000 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $39,400 (33%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Branson is $857 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $190 (28%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $205 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is approximately $113 (15%) higher.
