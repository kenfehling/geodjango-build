Compared to the surrounding areas, Branson is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Springfield however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Branson costs $157,500. This is roughly $36,160 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $60,000 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is roughly $39,400 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Branson the median rent is $857 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $190 (28%) higher.It is about $205 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is approximately $113 (15%) higher.
