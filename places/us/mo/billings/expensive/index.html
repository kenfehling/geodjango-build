Home prices

Rents

Billings's median rent is $756 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $57 (7%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $306 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
