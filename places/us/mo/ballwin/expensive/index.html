The price of the median home in Ballwin is $261,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $65,960 (33%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $44,400 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $123,200 (88%) higher.

Rents
