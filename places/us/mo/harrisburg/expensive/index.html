Home prices

The median home value in Harrisburg is $133,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $42,020 (46%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $84,500 (38%) lower, and is roughly $58,800 (30%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrisburg is $775. This is about $35 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $112 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
