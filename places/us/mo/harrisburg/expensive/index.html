Compared to other places in this area, Harrisburg is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Columbia, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Harrisburg costs $133,000. This is approximately $42,020 (46%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $84,500 (38%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Columbia it is about $58,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Harrisburg is $775 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $35 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia is approximately $112 (12%) lower.
