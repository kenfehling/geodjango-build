Compared to other places in this area, Harrisburg is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Columbia, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Harrisburg's median home cost is $133,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $42,020 (46%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $84,500 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbia it is about $58,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrisburg is $775. It is about $35 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is approximately $112 (12%) lower.
