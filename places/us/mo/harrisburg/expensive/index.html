Compared to other places in this area, Harrisburg is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Columbia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Harrisburg is $133,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $42,020 (46%) higher. It is approximately $84,500 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is approximately $58,800 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Harrisburg is $775 per month. It is about $35 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $112 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.
