Home prices

The median home price in Harrisburg is $133,000. It is approximately $42,020 (46%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $84,500 (38%) lower, and is approximately $58,800 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median rent is $775 a month. It is about $35 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $287 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $112 (12%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
