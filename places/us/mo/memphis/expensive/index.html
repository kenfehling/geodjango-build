Home prices

Memphis's median home cost is $67,300. This is approximately $15,300 (29%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $150,200 (69%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Quincy is about $49,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Memphis is $518 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $29 (5%) lower.It is roughly $544 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Quincy is approximately $192 (27%) lower.
