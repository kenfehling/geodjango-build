Compared to its surrounding areas, Memphis is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Quincy.

Home prices

The median home value in Memphis is $67,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $15,300 (29%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $150,200 (69%) lower, and it is about $49,000 (42%) lower compared to the city of Quincy.

Rents

Memphis's median monthly rent is $518. It is about $29 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $544 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Quincy is roughly $192 (27%) lower.
