Home prices

The median home cost in Memphis is $67,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $15,300 (29%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $150,200 (69%) lower, and it is approximately $49,000 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Quincy.

Rents

The median rent in Memphis is $518 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $29 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $544 (51%) lower, and is about $192 (27%) lower compared to the city of Quincy.
