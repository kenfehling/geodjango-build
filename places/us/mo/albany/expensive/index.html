Home prices

The median home in Albany costs $73,500. This is approximately $2,740 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $144,000 (66%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $81,100 (52%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Albany's median monthly rent is $532. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $10 (1%) lower. It is about $530 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $409 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.
