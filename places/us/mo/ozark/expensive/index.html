Compared to the surrounding areas, Ozark is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ozark is $154,000. It is roughly $24,200 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $63,500 (29%) lower, and is roughly $35,900 (30%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Ozark the median rent is $836 per month. It is roughly $61 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $226 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $92 (12%) higher.
