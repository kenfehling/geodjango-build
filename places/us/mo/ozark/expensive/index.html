Home prices

The median home cost in Ozark is $154,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $24,200 (13%) lower.It is approximately $63,500 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Springfield it is approximately $35,900 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ozark is $836 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $61 (7%) higher.It is roughly $226 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $92 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
