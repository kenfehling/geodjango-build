Home prices

The median home cost in Desloge is $95,000. It is roughly $4,400 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $122,500 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $43,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Desloge is $587 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $148 (20%) lower. It is about $475 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $241 (29%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
