Home prices

The cost of the median home in Castle Point is $47,600. This is about $20,580 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $169,900 (78%) lower, and is roughly $91,100 (65%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Castle Point is $884 a month. It is roughly $99 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $178 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $56 (6%) higher.
