Home prices

The median home cost in Castle Point is $47,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $20,580 (30%) lower.It is approximately $169,900 (78%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $91,100 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Castle Point the median monthly rent is $884. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $99 (10%) lower.It is roughly $178 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $56 (6%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
