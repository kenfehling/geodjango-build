Home prices

Birmingham's median home cost is $106,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $9,280 (8%) lower. It is approximately $110,900 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $48,000 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

The median rent in Birmingham is $896 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $63 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $166 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $45 (4%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
