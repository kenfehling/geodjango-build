In comparison to other places in this area, Hazelwood is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Hazelwood's median home value is $118,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $22,400 (23%) higher. It is roughly $98,600 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $19,800 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Hazelwood's median rent is $884 per month. This is approximately $113 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $178 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $56 (6%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
