Home prices

The value of the median home in Hazelwood is $118,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $22,400 (23%) higher. It is approximately $98,600 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $19,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hazelwood is $884. This is roughly $113 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $178 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $56 (6%) higher.
