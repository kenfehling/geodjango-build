Home prices

The median home value in California is $111,300. This is approximately $18,500 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $106,200 (48%) lower, and is about $80,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Columbia.

Rents

California's median monthly rent is $605. It is about $30 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $457 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is roughly $282 (31%) lower.
