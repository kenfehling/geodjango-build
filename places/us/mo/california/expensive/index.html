Home prices

The cost of the median home in California is $111,300. It is about $18,500 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $106,200 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Columbia is about $80,500 (41%) lower.

Rents

In California the median monthly rent is $605. This is roughly $30 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $457 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $282 (31%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.
