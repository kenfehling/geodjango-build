Home prices

The median home in Houston costs $101,600. This is about $25,100 (32%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $115,900 (53%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $16,500 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Houston is $605 per month. It is about $67 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $457 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $139 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
