Home prices

Houston's median home cost is $101,600. This is approximately $25,100 (32%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $115,900 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $16,500 (13%) lower.

Rents

Houston's median monthly rent is $605. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $67 (12%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $457 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $139 (18%) lower.
