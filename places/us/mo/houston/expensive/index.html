Home prices

The median home in Houston costs $101,600. It is approximately $25,100 (32%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $115,900 (53%) lower, and it is about $16,500 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Houston's median monthly rent is $605. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $67 (12%) higher. It is roughly $457 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is roughly $139 (18%) lower.
