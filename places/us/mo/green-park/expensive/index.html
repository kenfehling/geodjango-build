In comparison to the surrounding areas, Green Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Green Park is valued at $164,600. It is roughly $16,980 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $52,900 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $25,900 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Green Park the median monthly rent is $838. This is roughly $35 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $10 (1%) higher.
