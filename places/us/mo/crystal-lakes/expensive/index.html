Home prices

The median home cost in Crystal Lakes is $116,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,420 (16%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $101,500 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $38,600 (24%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.

Rents

Crystal Lakes's median rent is $820 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $41 (4%) lower. It is approximately $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $121 (12%) lower compared to the city of Kansas City.
