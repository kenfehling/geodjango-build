Home prices

The median home cost in New Castle is $194,300. It is about $18,300 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $23,200 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $31,300 (19%) higher.

Rents

New Castle's median monthly rent is $1,320. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $292 (28%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $258 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $278 (26%) higher.
