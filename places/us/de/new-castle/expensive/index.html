Compared to the surrounding areas, New Castle is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in New Castle is $194,300. This is about $18,300 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $23,200 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $31,300 (19%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Castle is $1,320. This is about $292 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $258 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $278 (26%) higher.
