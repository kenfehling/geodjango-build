Home prices

The price of the median home in Woodside is $168,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $57,820 (25%) lower. It is roughly $48,700 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $5,800 (3%) higher.

Rents

Woodside's median monthly rent is $965. It is roughly $198 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $97 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $77 (7%) lower.
