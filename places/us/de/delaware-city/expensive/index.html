Home prices

The median home in Delaware City is valued at $189,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $6,860 (3%) higher.It is approximately $27,800 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $26,700 (16%) higher.

Rents

Delaware City's median rent is $1,068 a month. It is roughly $126 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and it is about $26 (2%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
