Home prices

The median home in Glasgow costs $274,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $44,100 (19%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $56,500 (25%) higher, and is about $111,000 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Glasgow the median monthly rent is $1,377. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $193 (16%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $315 (29%) higher, and it is about $335 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
