Compared to its surrounding areas, Glasgow is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Glasgow is $274,000. It is roughly $44,100 (19%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $56,500 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $111,000 (68%) higher.

Rents

Glasgow's median rent is $1,377 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $193 (16%) higher.It is approximately $315 (29%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $335 (32%) higher.
