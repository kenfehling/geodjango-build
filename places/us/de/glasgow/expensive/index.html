Home prices

The median home in Glasgow costs $274,000. It is roughly $44,100 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $56,500 (25%) higher, and it is roughly $111,000 (68%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Glasgow the median rent is $1,377 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $193 (16%) higher.It is about $315 (29%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $335 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
