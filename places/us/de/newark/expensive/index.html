Home prices

The median home in Newark is valued at $277,800. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar.It is about $60,300 (27%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $114,800 (70%) higher.

Rents

Newark's median monthly rent is $1,308. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $120 (8%) lower.It is about $246 (23%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $266 (25%) higher.
