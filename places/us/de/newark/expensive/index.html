Much like other places in this area, it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Newark is $277,800. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. It is about $60,300 (27%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $114,800 (70%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Newark is $1,308 per month. It is about $120 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $246 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $266 (25%) higher.
