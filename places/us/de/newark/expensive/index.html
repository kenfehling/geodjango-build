Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Newark is $1,308 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $120 (8%) lower.It is approximately $246 (23%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $266 (25%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
