Home prices

The median home in Newark is valued at $277,800. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $60,300 (27%) higher, and it is about $114,800 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Newark is $1,308 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $120 (8%) lower. It is about $246 (23%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $266 (25%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
