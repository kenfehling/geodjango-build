Home prices

The median home cost in Middletown is $301,700. It is roughly $46,540 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $84,200 (38%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $138,700 (85%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Middletown the median rent is $1,254 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $161 (14%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $192 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $212 (20%) higher.
