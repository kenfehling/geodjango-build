Compared to other places in this area, Middletown is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Middletown is $301,700. It is about $46,540 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $84,200 (38%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $138,700 (85%) higher.

Rents

In Middletown the median rent is $1,254 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $161 (14%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $192 (18%) higher, and it is about $212 (20%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
