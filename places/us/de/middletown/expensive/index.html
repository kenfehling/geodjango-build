Home prices

The cost of the median home in Middletown is $301,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $46,540 (18%) higher.It is roughly $84,200 (38%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $138,700 (85%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Middletown's median monthly rent is $1,254. This is approximately $161 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $192 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $212 (20%) higher.
