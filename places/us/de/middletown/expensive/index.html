Rents

Middletown's median monthly rent is $1,254. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $161 (14%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $192 (18%) higher, and is about $212 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
