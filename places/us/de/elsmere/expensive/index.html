Home prices

The median home in Elsmere is valued at $174,700. It is roughly $110,960 (38%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $42,800 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $11,700 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents
