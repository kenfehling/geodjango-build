Home prices

Rents

Frankford's median monthly rent is $1,206. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $36 (2%) lower.It is approximately $144 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $205 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
