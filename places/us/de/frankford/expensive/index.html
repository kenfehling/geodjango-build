Home prices

The median home cost in Frankford is $169,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $109,920 (39%) lower. It is about $48,400 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is is very similar.

Rents

Frankford's median monthly rent is $1,206. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $36 (2%) lower. It is about $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Wilmington is about $205 (20%) higher.
