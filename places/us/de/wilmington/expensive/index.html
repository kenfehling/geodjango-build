Compared to nearby areas, Wilmington is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Wilmington costs $168,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $16,700 (9%) lower. It is approximately $49,500 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $5,000 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington is $1,001 per month. This is approximately $53 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $61 (5%) lower, and is roughly $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
