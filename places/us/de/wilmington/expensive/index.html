Home prices

The median home cost in Wilmington is $168,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $16,700 (9%) lower.It is roughly $49,500 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $5,000 (3%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Wilmington's median rent is $1,001 per month. It is about $53 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $61 (5%) lower, and is roughly $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
