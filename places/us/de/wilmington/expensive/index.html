The median home in Wilmington costs $168,000. This is about $16,700 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $49,500 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $5,000 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington is $1,001 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $53 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $61 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $41 (3%) lower.
