Home prices

The median home in Wilmington is valued at $168,000. This is approximately $16,700 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $49,500 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $5,000 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Wilmington the median rent is $1,001 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $53 (4%) lower.It is roughly $61 (5%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
