Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wilmington is $168,000. It is approximately $16,700 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $49,500 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $5,000 (3%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Wilmington's median monthly rent is $1,001. It is approximately $53 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $61 (5%) lower, and it is approximately $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
