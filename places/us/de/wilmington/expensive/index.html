Compared to other places in this area, Wilmington is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Wilmington is valued at $168,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $16,700 (9%) lower. It is roughly $49,500 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $5,000 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Wilmington the median rent is $1,001 a month. It is approximately $53 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $61 (5%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
