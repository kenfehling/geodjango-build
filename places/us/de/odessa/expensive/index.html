Home prices

The median home price in Odessa is $276,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $23,260 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $59,100 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $113,600 (69%) higher.

Rents

In Odessa the median rent is $1,232 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $122 (10%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $170 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $190 (18%) higher.
