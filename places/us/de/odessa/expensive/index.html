Home prices

The median home in Odessa is valued at $276,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $23,260 (9%) higher. It is roughly $59,100 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $113,600 (69%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Odessa's median monthly rent is $1,232. It is roughly $122 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $170 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $190 (18%) higher.
