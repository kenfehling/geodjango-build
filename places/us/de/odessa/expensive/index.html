In comparison to the surrounding areas, Odessa is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Odessa costs $276,600. It is roughly $23,260 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $59,100 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $113,600 (69%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Odessa is $1,232 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $122 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $170 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $190 (18%) higher.
