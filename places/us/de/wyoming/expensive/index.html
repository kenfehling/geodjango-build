Compared to the surrounding areas, Wyoming is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home price in Wyoming is $186,200. This is roughly $34,540 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $31,300 (14%) lower, and it is about $23,200 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Wyoming the median rent is $1,015 per month. It is roughly $206 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $47 (4%) lower, and is approximately $27 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
