Home prices

The value of the median home in Wyoming is $186,200. It is roughly $34,540 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $31,300 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $23,200 (14%) higher.

Rents

Wyoming's median monthly rent is $1,015. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $206 (16%) lower. It is about $47 (4%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $27 (2%) lower.
