Compared to nearby areas, Wyoming is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wyoming is $186,200. This is roughly $34,540 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $31,300 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $23,200 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Wyoming the median rent is $1,015 a month. This is approximately $206 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $47 (4%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $27 (2%) lower.
