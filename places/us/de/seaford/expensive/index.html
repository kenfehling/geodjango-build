Home prices

Rents

Seaford's median monthly rent is $859. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $53 (6%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $203 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore is approximately $214 (19%) lower.
