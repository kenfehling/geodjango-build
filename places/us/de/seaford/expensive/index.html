Home prices

The cost of the median home in Seaford is $170,300. It is about $2,220 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $47,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $10,200 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

In Seaford the median monthly rent is $859. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $53 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $203 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Baltimore it is about $214 (19%) lower.
