Home prices

The median home value in Bellefonte is $226,300. This is roughly $2,980 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $8,800 (4%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $63,300 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Bellefonte's median monthly rent is $1,078. This is approximately $128 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $16 (1%) higher, and it is roughly $36 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
