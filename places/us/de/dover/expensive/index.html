Home prices

The median home in Dover is valued at $175,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $40,920 (18%) lower.It is approximately $42,200 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $12,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dover is $1,038. It is approximately $62 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $24 (2%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is is very similar.
