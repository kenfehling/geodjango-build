Home prices

The price of the median home in Dover is $175,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $40,920 (18%) lower. It is approximately $42,200 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $12,300 (7%) higher.

Rents

Dover's median rent is $1,038 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $62 (5%) lower. It is about $24 (2%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
