Home prices

The value of the median home in Dover is $175,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $40,920 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $42,200 (19%) lower, and is about $12,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Dover the median monthly rent is $1,038. This is approximately $62 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $24 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is is very similar.
