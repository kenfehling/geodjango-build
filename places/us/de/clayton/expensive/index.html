Home prices

The price of the median home in Clayton is $242,900. It is roughly $62,600 (34%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $25,400 (11%) higher, and is approximately $79,900 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Clayton the median monthly rent is $1,317. This is about $165 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $255 (24%) higher, and it is roughly $275 (26%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
