Home prices

The median home in Clayton costs $242,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $62,600 (34%) higher.It is roughly $25,400 (11%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $79,900 (49%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Clayton the median rent is $1,317 per month. This is roughly $165 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $255 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $275 (26%) higher.
