Home prices

The price of the median home in Conway is $171,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $58,860 (52%) higher. It is about $45,900 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is about $4,000 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Conway the median rent is $825 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $110 (15%) higher. It is about $237 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is roughly $47 (5%) lower.
