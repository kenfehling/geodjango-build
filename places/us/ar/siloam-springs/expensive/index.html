Home prices

The median home price in Siloam Springs is $135,500. This is approximately $50,560 (59%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $82,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $4,400 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

In Siloam Springs the median rent is $743 per month. This is roughly $12 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $319 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $86 (10%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
