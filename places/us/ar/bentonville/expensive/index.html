Compared to its surrounding areas, Bentonville is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bentonville costs $232,600. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $38,460 (19%) higher.It is about $15,100 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $114,500 (96%) higher.

Rents

Bentonville's median monthly rent is $955. It is about $110 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $107 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $211 (28%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
