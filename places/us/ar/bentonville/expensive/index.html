Compared to its nearby areas, Bentonville is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home value in Bentonville is $232,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $38,460 (19%) higher. It is roughly $15,100 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $114,500 (96%) higher.

Rents

Bentonville's median rent is $955 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $110 (12%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $107 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $211 (28%) higher.
