In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bentonville is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bentonville is $232,600. It is about $38,460 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $15,100 (6%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $114,500 (96%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Bentonville is $955 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $110 (12%) higher. It is about $107 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $211 (28%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
