Home prices

The median home in Lamar costs $97,400. This is roughly $10,500 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $120,100 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is about $70,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lamar is $515 a month. It is approximately $112 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $547 (51%) lower, and it is about $357 (40%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
