Home prices

The price of the median home in Lamar is $97,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10,500 (12%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $120,100 (55%) lower, and is about $70,200 (41%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents
