Home prices

The median home price in Newport is $72,500. This is roughly $11,600 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $145,000 (66%) lower, and is about $29,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Newport the median monthly rent is $600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $60 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $462 (43%) lower, and is approximately $301 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
