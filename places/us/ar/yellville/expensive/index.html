Home prices

The median home in Yellville is valued at $110,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $27,020 (32%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $106,600 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $7,200 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Yellville the median monthly rent is $635. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $9 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $427 (40%) lower, and is about $109 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
