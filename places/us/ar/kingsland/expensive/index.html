Compared to the surrounding areas, Kingsland is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Little Rock.

Home prices

The median home in Kingsland costs $52,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $16,600 (23%) lower. It is approximately $164,800 (75%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Little Rock is about $114,900 (68%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kingsland is $594. Compared to other places in this area this is about $31 (5%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $468 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $278 (31%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
