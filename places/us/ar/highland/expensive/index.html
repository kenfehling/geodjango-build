Home prices

The median home cost in Highland is $94,300. This is about $22,120 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $123,200 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $58,600 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Jonesboro.

Rents

The median rent in Highland is $613 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $70 (12%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $449 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jonesboro it is about $151 (19%) lower.
