Home prices

Texarkana's median home value is $119,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $9,520 (7%) lower.It is roughly $97,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $28,400 (19%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

Texarkana's median monthly rent is $739. Compared to nearby areas this is about $12 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $323 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Shreveport it is about $73 (8%) lower.
