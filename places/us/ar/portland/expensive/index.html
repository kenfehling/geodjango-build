In comparison to its surrounding areas, Portland is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Monroe.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $76,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $22,580 (41%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $140,800 (64%) lower, and it is about $62,900 (45%) lower compared to the city of Monroe.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $452. It is roughly $131 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $610 (57%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $250 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Monroe.
