In comparison to its nearby areas, Portland is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Monroe.

Home prices

The median home in Portland is valued at $76,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $22,580 (41%) higher. It is roughly $140,800 (64%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Monroe it is roughly $62,900 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Portland is $452 a month. This is about $131 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $610 (57%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $250 (35%) lower compared to the city of Monroe.
