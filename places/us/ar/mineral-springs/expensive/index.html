Home prices

The median home in Mineral Springs costs $87,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $20,440 (30%) higher. It is about $129,600 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $35,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Hot Springs.

Rents

The median rent in Mineral Springs is $650 a month. This is roughly $42 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $412 (38%) lower, and it is about $77 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Hot Springs.
