Rents

Greenwood's median monthly rent is $744. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $38 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $318 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $55 (6%) lower.
