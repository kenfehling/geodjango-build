Home prices

Marion's median home cost is $153,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $78,540 (104%) higher. It is about $63,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $52,100 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Marion the median rent is $859 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $264 (44%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $203 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $42 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
