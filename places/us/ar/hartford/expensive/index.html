Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hartford is $60,000. It is roughly $3,960 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $157,500 (72%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $147,600 (71%) lower.

Rents

Hartford's median monthly rent is $755. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $81 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $307 (28%) lower, and is roughly $44 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
