In comparison to nearby areas, Hartford is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Hartford is $60,000. It is roughly $3,960 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $157,500 (72%) lower, and it is roughly $147,600 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hartford is $755. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $81 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $307 (28%) lower, and is about $44 (5%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
