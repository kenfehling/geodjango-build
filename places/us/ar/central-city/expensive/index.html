Home prices

Rents

In Central City the median rent is $641 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $111 (14%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $421 (39%) lower, and is about $158 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
