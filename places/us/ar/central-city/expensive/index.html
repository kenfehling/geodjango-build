In comparison to other places in this area, Central City is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Fayetteville however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Central City costs $144,600. It is roughly $28,820 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $72,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is about $63,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Central City is $641 per month. This is approximately $111 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $421 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $158 (19%) lower.
