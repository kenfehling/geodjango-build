In comparison to its surrounding areas, Central City is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Central City is $144,600. It is roughly $28,820 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $72,900 (33%) lower, and is about $63,000 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Central City is $641. It is about $111 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $421 (39%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is about $158 (19%) lower.
