Home prices

The value of the median home in Arkadelphia is $121,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $44,980 (59%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $96,300 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $46,400 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Arkadelphia the median rent is $589 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $71 (10%) lower.It is about $473 (44%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $283 (32%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
