Home prices

The median home value in Arkadelphia is $121,200. It is about $44,980 (59%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $96,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is roughly $46,400 (27%) lower.

Rents

Arkadelphia's median rent is $589 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $71 (10%) lower.It is roughly $473 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $283 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
