Home prices

Rents

In Arkadelphia the median rent is $589 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $71 (10%) lower.It is about $473 (44%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is approximately $283 (32%) lower.
