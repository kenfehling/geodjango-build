Home prices

The median home in Arkadelphia costs $121,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $44,980 (59%) higher. It is approximately $96,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is approximately $46,400 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Arkadelphia the median monthly rent is $589. It is roughly $71 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $473 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $283 (32%) lower.
