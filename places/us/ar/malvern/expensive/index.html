Home prices

The median home in Malvern is valued at $93,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,080 (20%) higher. It is about $124,300 (57%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $74,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

Malvern's median monthly rent is $585. This is approximately $68 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $477 (44%) lower, and is roughly $287 (32%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
