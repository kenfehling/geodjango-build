Home prices

The price of the median home in Harrisburg is $74,700. This is approximately $3,300 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $142,800 (65%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $27,100 (26%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

Harrisburg's median rent is $661 per month. It is about $37 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $401 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $240 (26%) lower.
