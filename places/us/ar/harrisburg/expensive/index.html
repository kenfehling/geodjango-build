Home prices

The value of the median home in Harrisburg is $74,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $3,300 (4%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $142,800 (65%) lower, and is approximately $27,100 (26%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrisburg is $661. This is roughly $37 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $401 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is approximately $240 (26%) lower.
