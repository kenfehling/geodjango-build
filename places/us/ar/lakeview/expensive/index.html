Home prices

The median home in Lakeview costs $143,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $37,340 (35%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $74,500 (34%) lower, and it is about $24,900 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Lakeview's median rent is $750 a month. This is roughly $147 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $312 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is is very similar.
