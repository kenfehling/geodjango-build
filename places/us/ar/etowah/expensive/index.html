Home prices

The cost of the median home in Etowah is $71,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $11,900 (19%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $145,600 (66%) lower, and it is about $29,900 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Etowah the median monthly rent is $762. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $188 (32%) higher.It is roughly $300 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $139 (15%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
