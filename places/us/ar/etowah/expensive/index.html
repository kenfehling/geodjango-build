Compared to its surrounding areas, Etowah is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Etowah's median home cost is $71,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $11,900 (19%) higher. It is approximately $145,600 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $29,900 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Etowah the median monthly rent is $762. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $188 (32%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $300 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Memphis it is roughly $139 (15%) lower.
