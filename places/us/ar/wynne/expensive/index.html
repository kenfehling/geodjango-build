Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wynne is $89,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $35,900 (66%) higher. It is roughly $127,600 (58%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $11,900 (11%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Wynne the median monthly rent is $728. This is roughly $52 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $334 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $173 (19%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
