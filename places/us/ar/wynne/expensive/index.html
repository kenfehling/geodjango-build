Wynne's median home cost is $89,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $35,900 (66%) higher. It is approximately $127,600 (58%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Memphis it is about $11,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Wynne the median rent is $728 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $52 (7%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $334 (31%) lower, and it is about $173 (19%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
