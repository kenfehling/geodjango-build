In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lewisville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Shreveport.

Home prices

The median home in Lewisville costs $46,500. This is roughly $18,480 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $171,000 (78%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $101,600 (68%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.

Rents

In Lewisville the median rent is $481 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $131 (21%) lower. It is approximately $581 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $331 (40%) lower compared to the city of Shreveport.
