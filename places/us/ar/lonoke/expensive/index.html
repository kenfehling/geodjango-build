Home prices

The median home in Lonoke costs $127,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $14,520 (12%) higher.It is about $90,100 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $40,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lonoke is $611. It is about $194 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $451 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $261 (29%) lower.
