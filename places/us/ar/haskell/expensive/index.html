Home prices

The median home cost in Haskell is $128,900. It is approximately $7,880 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $88,600 (40%) lower, and it is about $38,700 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Haskell the median rent is $1,101 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $340 (44%) higher. It is about $39 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $229 (26%) higher.
