In comparison to its nearby areas, Rockport is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Little Rock.

Home prices

The median home in Rockport costs $118,900. It is about $30,140 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $98,600 (45%) lower, and is roughly $48,700 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Rockport the median monthly rent is $669. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $89 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $393 (37%) lower, and is approximately $203 (23%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
