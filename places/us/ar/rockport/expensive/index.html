Home prices

The median home price in Rockport is $118,900. This is about $30,140 (33%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $98,600 (45%) lower, and it is about $48,700 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Rockport the median monthly rent is $669. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $89 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $393 (37%) lower, and is about $203 (23%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
