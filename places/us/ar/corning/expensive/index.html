Home prices

The price of the median home in Corning is $73,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $31,740 (76%) higher. It is approximately $144,100 (66%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Jonesboro it is approximately $79,500 (51%) lower.

Rents

Corning's median rent is $580 per month. This is approximately $16 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $482 (45%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $184 (24%) lower compared to the city of Jonesboro.
