Home prices

The median home in Cherokee Village is valued at $74,100. It is about $2,120 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $143,400 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Jonesboro is roughly $78,800 (51%) lower.

Rents

Cherokee Village's median rent is $705 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $180 (34%) higher. It is approximately $357 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Jonesboro is approximately $59 (7%) lower.
