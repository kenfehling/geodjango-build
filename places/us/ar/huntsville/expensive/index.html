Home prices

The median home in Huntsville costs $104,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $108,900 (51%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $113,100 (52%) lower, and is roughly $13,700 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Huntsville is $602 a month. This is approximately $228 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $460 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $142 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
