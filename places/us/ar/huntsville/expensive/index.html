In comparison to its surrounding areas, Huntsville is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Huntsville is valued at $104,400. This is about $108,900 (51%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $113,100 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is approximately $13,700 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Huntsville is $602 per month. It is roughly $228 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $460 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $142 (19%) lower.
