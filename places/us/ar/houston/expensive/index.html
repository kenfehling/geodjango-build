In comparison to its nearby areas, Houston is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Little Rock.

Home prices

Houston's median home cost is $72,500. This is roughly $12,840 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $145,000 (66%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $95,100 (56%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Houston the median rent is $683 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is approximately $189 (21%) lower.
