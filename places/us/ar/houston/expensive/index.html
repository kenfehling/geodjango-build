Home prices

The price of the median home in Houston is $72,500. This is about $12,840 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $145,000 (66%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $95,100 (56%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Houston is $683. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $379 (35%) lower, and is about $189 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
