Home prices

The median home in Houston costs $72,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $12,840 (15%) lower.It is about $145,000 (66%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $95,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median rent in Houston is $683 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $379 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $189 (21%) lower.
