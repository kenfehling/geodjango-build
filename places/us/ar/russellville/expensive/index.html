Home prices

The median home in Russellville is valued at $128,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $27,840 (27%) higher. It is approximately $89,200 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $39,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Russellville is $675 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $31 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $387 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $197 (22%) lower.
