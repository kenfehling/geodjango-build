Home prices

The median home in Russellville costs $128,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $27,840 (27%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $89,200 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is about $39,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

In Russellville the median rent is $675 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $31 (4%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $387 (36%) lower, and is about $197 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
