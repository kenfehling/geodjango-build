Home prices

The median home in Russellville is valued at $128,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $27,840 (27%) higher. It is approximately $89,200 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $39,300 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Russellville is $675. This is roughly $31 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $387 (36%) lower, and is approximately $197 (22%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
