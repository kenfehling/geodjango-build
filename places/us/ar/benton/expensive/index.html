Home prices

The median home in Benton costs $149,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $33,740 (29%) higher. It is about $68,100 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is approximately $18,200 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Benton is $796 a month. It is approximately $148 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $266 (25%) lower, and is roughly $76 (8%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
