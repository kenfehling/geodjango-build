Home prices

The median home in Benton costs $149,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $33,740 (29%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $68,100 (31%) lower, and is roughly $18,200 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Benton is $796. It is roughly $148 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $266 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $76 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
