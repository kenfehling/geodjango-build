Home prices

Rents

Greenbrier's median rent is $847 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $100 (13%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $215 (20%) lower, and is about $25 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
