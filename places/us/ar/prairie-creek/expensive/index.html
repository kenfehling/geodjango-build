Home prices

The median home in Prairie Creek is valued at $222,800. This is approximately $53,160 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $5,300 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is roughly $104,700 (88%) higher.

Rents

Prairie Creek's median rent is $867 a month. This is about $14 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $195 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $123 (16%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
