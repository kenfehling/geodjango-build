Home prices

The median home in Prairie Creek costs $222,800. This is approximately $53,160 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $5,300 (2%) higher, and it is roughly $104,700 (88%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Prairie Creek is $867. This is roughly $14 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $123 (16%) higher.
