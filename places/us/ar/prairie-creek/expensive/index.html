Rents

The median monthly rent in Prairie Creek is $867. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14 (1%) higher.It is roughly $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $123 (16%) higher.
