Home prices

The median home in North Little Rock costs $135,200. This is roughly $11,000 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $82,300 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $32,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

In North Little Rock the median rent is $854 per month. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $208 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $18 (2%) lower.
