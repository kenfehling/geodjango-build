In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Little Rock is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Little Rock.

Home prices

The median home in North Little Rock costs $135,200. This is roughly $11,000 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $82,300 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Little Rock is approximately $32,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

North Little Rock's median rent is $854 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $208 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $18 (2%) lower.
