In comparison to nearby areas, Lowell is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Springfield however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lowell is valued at $157,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $48,820 (23%) lower. It is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Springfield is roughly $39,500 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Lowell the median monthly rent is $836. This is about $34 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $226 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is roughly $92 (12%) higher.
