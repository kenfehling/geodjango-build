In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lowell is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home in Lowell costs $157,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $48,820 (23%) lower. It is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Springfield is roughly $39,500 (33%) higher.

Rents

Lowell's median monthly rent is $836. This is about $34 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $226 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield is approximately $92 (12%) higher.
