In comparison to its nearby areas, Lowell is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lowell is $157,600. This is about $48,820 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $39,500 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lowell is $836. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $34 (3%) lower. It is approximately $226 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $92 (12%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
