In comparison to nearby areas, Lowell is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home value in Lowell is $157,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $48,820 (23%) lower. It is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $39,500 (33%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Lowell's median rent is $836 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $34 (3%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $226 (21%) lower, and it is about $92 (12%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
