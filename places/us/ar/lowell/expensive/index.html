In comparison to other places in this area, Lowell is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lowell is $157,600. This is about $48,820 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $39,500 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Lowell is $836 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $34 (3%) lower.It is about $226 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $92 (12%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
