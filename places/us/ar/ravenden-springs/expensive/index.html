Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ravenden Springs is $480. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $28 (5%) lower. It is roughly $582 (54%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Jonesboro it is approximately $284 (37%) lower.
