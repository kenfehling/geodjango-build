Rents

In Mansfield the median rent is $591 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $117 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $471 (44%) lower, and is roughly $208 (26%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
