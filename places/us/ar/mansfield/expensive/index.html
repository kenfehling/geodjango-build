Rents

The median monthly rent in Mansfield is $591. It is roughly $117 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $471 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $208 (26%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
