Home prices

The median home in Van Buren costs $116,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $14,220 (13%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $101,300 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $91,400 (44%) lower.

Rents

Van Buren's median rent is $724 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $110 (17%) higher.It is roughly $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $75 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
