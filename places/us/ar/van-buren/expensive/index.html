In comparison to nearby areas, Van Buren is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Van Buren is valued at $116,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $14,220 (13%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $101,300 (46%) lower, and it is roughly $91,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Van Buren the median monthly rent is $724. It is approximately $110 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $338 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is about $75 (9%) lower.
