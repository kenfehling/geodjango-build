Home prices

The median home value in Van Buren is $116,200. This is about $14,220 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $101,300 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $91,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Van Buren the median rent is $724 a month. This is roughly $110 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $338 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $75 (9%) lower.
