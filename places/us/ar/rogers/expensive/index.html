Compared to its nearby areas, Rogers is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rogers is $178,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $31,760 (15%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $39,500 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is about $59,900 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Rogers the median rent is $898 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $24 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $164 (15%) lower, and is about $154 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
