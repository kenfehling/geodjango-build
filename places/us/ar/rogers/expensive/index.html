In comparison to nearby areas, Rogers is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rogers is $178,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $31,760 (15%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $39,500 (18%) lower, and is roughly $59,900 (50%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rogers is $898. It is approximately $24 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $164 (15%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is approximately $154 (20%) higher.
