Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rogers is $178,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $31,760 (15%) lower.It is approximately $39,500 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $59,900 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Rogers's median rent is $898 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $24 (2%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $164 (15%) lower, and is roughly $154 (20%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
