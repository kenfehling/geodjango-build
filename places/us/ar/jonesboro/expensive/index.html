Home prices

The median home in Jonesboro costs $152,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $64,600 (73%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $64,600 (29%) lower, and is roughly $51,100 (50%) higher compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

The median rent in Jonesboro is $764 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $33 (4%) higher.It is roughly $298 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $137 (15%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
