Compared to other places in this area, Jonesboro is significantly more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Jonesboro is $152,900. It is roughly $64,600 (73%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $64,600 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is approximately $51,100 (50%) higher.

Rents

Jonesboro's median monthly rent is $764. This is roughly $33 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $298 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $137 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.
