Compared to its surrounding areas, Jonesboro is a lot more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

The median home value in Jonesboro is $152,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $64,600 (73%) higher.It is approximately $64,600 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $51,100 (50%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Jonesboro is $764 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $33 (4%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $298 (28%) lower, and is about $137 (15%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
