Home prices

The median home cost in Jonesboro is $152,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $64,600 (73%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $64,600 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $51,100 (50%) higher.

Rents

Jonesboro's median rent is $764 per month. This is approximately $33 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $298 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $137 (15%) lower.
