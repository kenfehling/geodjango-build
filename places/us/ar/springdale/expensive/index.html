In comparison to the surrounding areas, Springdale is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Springdale is $150,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $63,500 (29%) lower.It is about $66,600 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $32,800 (27%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

Springdale's median rent is $777 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $151 (16%) lower.It is approximately $285 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $33 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
