Compared to the surrounding areas, Springdale is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Springfield however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Springdale costs $150,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $63,500 (29%) lower. It is roughly $66,600 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is approximately $32,800 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Springdale the median monthly rent is $777. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $151 (16%) lower. It is roughly $285 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield it is approximately $33 (4%) higher.
