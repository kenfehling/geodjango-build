In comparison to other places in this area, Springdale is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Springfield however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Springdale costs $150,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $63,500 (29%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $66,600 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is about $32,800 (27%) higher.

Rents

Springdale's median monthly rent is $777. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $151 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $285 (26%) lower, and is approximately $33 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
