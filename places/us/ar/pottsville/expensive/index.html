Home prices

The median home in Pottsville costs $131,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $31,200 (31%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $86,400 (39%) lower, and it is roughly $36,500 (21%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

Pottsville's median monthly rent is $697. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $57 (8%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $175 (20%) lower.
