Compared to its surrounding areas, Pottsville is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Little Rock, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pottsville is $131,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $31,200 (31%) higher.It is about $86,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Little Rock is approximately $36,500 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Pottsville the median rent is $697 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $57 (8%) higher.It is approximately $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $175 (20%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
