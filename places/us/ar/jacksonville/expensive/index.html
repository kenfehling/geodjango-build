In comparison to the surrounding areas, Jacksonville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Little Rock, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Jacksonville's median home value is $125,800. This is roughly $9,960 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $91,700 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Little Rock is about $41,800 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median monthly rent is $819. This is roughly $30 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $243 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $53 (6%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
