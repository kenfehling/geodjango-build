Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $125,800. It is about $9,960 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $91,700 (42%) lower, and is about $41,800 (24%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $819. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $30 (3%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $243 (22%) lower, and is approximately $53 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
