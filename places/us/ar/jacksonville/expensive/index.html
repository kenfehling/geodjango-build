Compared to its surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Little Rock.

Home prices

The median home cost in Jacksonville is $125,800. It is roughly $9,960 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $91,700 (42%) lower, and is approximately $41,800 (24%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median rent is $819 a month. It is roughly $30 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $243 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $53 (6%) lower.
