Home prices

Rents

In Mena the median rent is $660 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $78 (13%) higher. It is about $402 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $25 (3%) lower compared to the city of Fort Smith.
