Home prices

Lake City's median home price is $93,500. It is about $13,360 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $8,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lake City is $838 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $259 (44%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis it is approximately $63 (6%) lower.
