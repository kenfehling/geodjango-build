Home prices

Lake City's median home value is $93,500. This is roughly $13,360 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $8,300 (8%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.

Rents

In Lake City the median monthly rent is $838. This is roughly $259 (44%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis is roughly $63 (6%) lower.
