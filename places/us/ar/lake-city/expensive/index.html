Compared to nearby areas, Lake City is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Memphis.

Home prices

Lake City's median home value is $93,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $13,360 (16%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $124,000 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis it is roughly $8,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Lake City the median monthly rent is $838. This is about $259 (44%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $63 (6%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
