Home prices

The median home in Midland is valued at $64,100. This is roughly $12,040 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $153,400 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $143,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Midland is $542. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $160 (22%) lower. It is approximately $520 (48%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $257 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
