Compared to nearby areas, Midland is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The median home in Midland is valued at $64,100. This is roughly $12,040 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $153,400 (70%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $143,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Midland the median rent is $542 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $160 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $520 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $257 (32%) lower.
