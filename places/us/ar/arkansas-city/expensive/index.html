Rents

The median monthly rent in Arkansas City is $595. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $73 (14%) higher.It is about $467 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $132 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff.
