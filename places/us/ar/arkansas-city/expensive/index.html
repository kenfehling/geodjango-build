Home prices

The value of the median home in Arkansas City is $60,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $1,440 (2%) higher. It is roughly $157,500 (72%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $15,500 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff.

Rents

In Arkansas City the median monthly rent is $595. It is roughly $73 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $467 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pine Bluff it is roughly $132 (18%) lower.
