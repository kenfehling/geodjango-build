Home prices

Rents

In Western Grove the median rent is $490 a month. This is approximately $72 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $572 (53%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $254 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
