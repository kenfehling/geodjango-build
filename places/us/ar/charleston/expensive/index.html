Home prices

The median home in Charleston is valued at $92,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $3,300 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $125,500 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is roughly $115,600 (55%) lower.

Rents

Charleston's median rent is $691 per month. This is roughly $38 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $371 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $108 (13%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
