In comparison to the surrounding areas, Austin is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Little Rock, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Austin costs $143,400. It is about $26,300 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $74,100 (34%) lower, and is approximately $24,200 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median rent in Austin is $960 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $177 (22%) higher. It is about $102 (9%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $88 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
