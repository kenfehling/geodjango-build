In comparison to its nearby areas, Austin is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Little Rock, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Austin is $143,400. It is approximately $26,300 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $74,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is about $24,200 (14%) lower.

Rents

Austin's median rent is $960 per month. This is roughly $177 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $102 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $88 (10%) higher.
