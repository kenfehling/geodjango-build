In comparison to nearby areas, Austin is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Little Rock however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Austin is $143,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $26,300 (22%) higher.It is about $74,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is about $24,200 (14%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Austin is $960. It is about $177 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $102 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $88 (10%) higher.
