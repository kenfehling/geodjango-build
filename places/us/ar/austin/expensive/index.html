Compared to its nearby areas, Austin is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Little Rock.

Home prices

The median home in Austin costs $143,400. It is approximately $26,300 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $74,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $24,200 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Austin is $960. It is roughly $177 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $102 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is about $88 (10%) higher.
