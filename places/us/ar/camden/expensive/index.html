Home prices

The median home in Camden costs $77,000. This is about $22,320 (40%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $140,500 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is about $90,600 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Camden the median rent is $511 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $71 (12%) lower. It is about $551 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $361 (41%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
