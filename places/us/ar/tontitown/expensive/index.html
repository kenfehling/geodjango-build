In comparison to other places in this area, Tontitown is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The median home in Tontitown is valued at $265,300. It is about $73,780 (38%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $47,800 (21%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is roughly $57,700 (27%) higher.

Rents

Tontitown's median monthly rent is $1,092. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $227 (26%) higher.It is roughly $30 (2%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $293 (36%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.
