Compared to other places in this area, Tontitown is significantly more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Tontitown's median home price is $265,300. This is roughly $73,780 (38%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $47,800 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $57,700 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Tontitown the median rent is $1,092 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $227 (26%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $30 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $293 (36%) higher.
