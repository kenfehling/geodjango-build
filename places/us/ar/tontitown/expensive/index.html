Compared to other places in this area, Tontitown is a lot more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a little more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Tontitown is $265,300. This is about $73,780 (38%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $47,800 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $57,700 (27%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Tontitown is $1,092 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $227 (26%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $30 (2%) higher, and it is approximately $293 (36%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.
