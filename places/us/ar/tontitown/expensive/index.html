In comparison to nearby areas, Tontitown is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Fayetteville, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tontitown costs $265,300. This is roughly $73,780 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $47,800 (21%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $57,700 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median rent in Tontitown is $1,092 a month. This is approximately $227 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $30 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $293 (36%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.
