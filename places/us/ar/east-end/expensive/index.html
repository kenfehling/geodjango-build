Home prices

The median home in East End is valued at $140,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $46,880 (50%) higher. It is about $77,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $27,400 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

East End's median rent is $750 per month. This is about $97 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $312 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Little Rock is about $122 (13%) lower.
