Home prices

The value of the median home in East End is $140,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $46,880 (50%) higher. It is roughly $77,300 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $27,400 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in East End is $750 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $97 (11%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $312 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is roughly $122 (13%) lower.
