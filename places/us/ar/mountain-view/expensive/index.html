The median home cost in Mountain View is $160,200. This is approximately $87,480 (120%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $57,300 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is about $7,400 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Mountain View the median rent is $553 a month. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $509 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $319 (36%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
