Home prices

The value of the median home in Johnson is $168,700. It is roughly $45,940 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $48,800 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $38,900 (18%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

Johnson's median rent is $903 a month. It is about $20 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $159 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $104 (13%) higher.
