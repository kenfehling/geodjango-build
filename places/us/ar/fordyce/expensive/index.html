Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fordyce is $74,100. This is approximately $9,080 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $143,400 (65%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is about $93,500 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Fordyce the median rent is $699 a month. This is about $157 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $363 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is approximately $173 (19%) lower.
