Compared to its surrounding areas, West Memphis is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

West Memphis's median home value is $86,800. It is about $18,480 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $130,700 (60%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $15,000 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Memphis.

Rents

West Memphis's median rent is $707 a month. This is about $15 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $355 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $194 (21%) lower.
