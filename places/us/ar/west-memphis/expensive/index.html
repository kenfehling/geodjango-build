In comparison to its surrounding areas, West Memphis is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Memphis, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Memphis costs $86,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $18,480 (17%) lower.It is roughly $130,700 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Memphis is approximately $15,000 (14%) lower.

Rents

West Memphis's median monthly rent is $707. This is approximately $15 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $355 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Memphis is about $194 (21%) lower.
