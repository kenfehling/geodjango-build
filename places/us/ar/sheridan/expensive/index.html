Home prices

Rents

In Sheridan the median monthly rent is $719. Compared to nearby areas it is about $65 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $343 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $153 (17%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
