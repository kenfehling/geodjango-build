Home prices

Sheridan's median home value is $132,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $41,120 (45%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $85,200 (39%) lower, and is about $35,300 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median rent in Sheridan is $719 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $65 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $343 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $153 (17%) lower.
