Home prices

The median home cost in Damascus is $112,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $19,160 (14%) lower.It is approximately $105,000 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $55,100 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Damascus is $769 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $22 (3%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $293 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $103 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
