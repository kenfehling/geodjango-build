Home prices

The median home in Damascus is valued at $112,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $19,160 (14%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $105,000 (48%) lower, and it is roughly $55,100 (32%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

Damascus's median monthly rent is $769. It is about $22 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $293 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $103 (11%) lower.
