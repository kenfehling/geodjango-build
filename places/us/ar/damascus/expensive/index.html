Home prices

Rents

Damascus's median rent is $769 per month. This is approximately $22 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $103 (11%) lower.
