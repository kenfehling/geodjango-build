Home prices

Norfork's median home price is $143,300. This is roughly $52,600 (57%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $74,200 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $28,300 (16%) lower compared to the city of Conway.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Norfork is $611. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $23 (3%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $451 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Conway it is roughly $214 (25%) lower.
