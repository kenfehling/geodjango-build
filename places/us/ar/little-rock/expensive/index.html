In comparison to other places in this area, Little Rock is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Little Rock is $167,600. It is roughly $56,433 (50%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $49,900 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole.

Rents

In Little Rock the median rent is $872 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $20 (2%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $190 (17%) lower.
