Compared to its surrounding areas, Pea Ridge is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home in Pea Ridge costs $133,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $58,940 (30%) lower. It is roughly $84,100 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $15,300 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pea Ridge is $1,029. This is approximately $209 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $33 (3%) lower, and it is roughly $285 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
