Rents

The median rent in Pea Ridge is $1,029 a month. This is approximately $209 (25%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $33 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is roughly $285 (38%) higher.
