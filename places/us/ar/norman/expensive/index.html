Home prices

The price of the median home in Norman is $65,300. This is roughly $7,020 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $152,200 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is about $102,300 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Norman is $573 per month. This is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $489 (46%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is about $299 (34%) lower.
