In comparison to other places in this area, Fayetteville is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Fort Smith however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fayetteville is $207,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $26,860 (14%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $9,900 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Smith is approximately $85,800 (70%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fayetteville is $799 a month. It is approximately $168 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $263 (24%) lower, and it is roughly $114 (16%) higher compared to the city of Fort Smith.
