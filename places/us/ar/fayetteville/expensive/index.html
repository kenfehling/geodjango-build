Compared to nearby areas, Fayetteville is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Fort Smith, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Fayetteville's median home price is $207,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $26,860 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $9,900 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Smith is about $85,800 (70%) higher.

Rents

In Fayetteville the median rent is $799 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $168 (17%) lower.It is roughly $263 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $114 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Smith.
