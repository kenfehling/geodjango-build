Compared to the surrounding areas, Fayetteville is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Fort Smith however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fayetteville is valued at $207,600. This is about $26,860 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $9,900 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $85,800 (70%) higher compared to the city of Fort Smith.

Rents

Fayetteville's median monthly rent is $799. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $168 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $263 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fort Smith it is about $114 (16%) higher.
