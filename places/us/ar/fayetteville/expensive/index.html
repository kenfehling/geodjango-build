In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fayetteville is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Smithhowever, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fayetteville is $207,600. It is approximately $26,860 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $9,900 (4%) lower, and is about $85,800 (70%) higher compared to the city of Fort Smith.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fayetteville is $799. It is approximately $168 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $263 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $114 (16%) higher compared to the city of Fort Smith.
