Compared to other places in this area, Bella Vista is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Springfield.

Home prices

The median home price in Bella Vista is $168,000. This is approximately $8,100 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $49,500 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $49,900 (42%) higher.

Rents

Bella Vista's median rent is $942 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $120 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $198 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Springfield.
