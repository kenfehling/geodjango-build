Home prices

The median home value in Bella Vista is $168,000. It is approximately $8,100 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $49,500 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $49,900 (42%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Bella Vista the median monthly rent is $942. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $120 (11%) lower, and is roughly $198 (26%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
