Compared to its surrounding areas, Centerton is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Centerton is $185,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $23,380 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $32,100 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield is roughly $67,300 (56%) higher.

Rents

In Centerton the median monthly rent is $1,091. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $286 (35%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $29 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Springfield is approximately $347 (46%) higher.
