In comparison to other places in this area, Centerton is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Springfield, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Centerton's median home value is $185,400. This is roughly $23,380 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $32,100 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Springfield it is about $67,300 (56%) higher.

Rents

Centerton's median monthly rent is $1,091. Compared to other places in this area this is about $286 (35%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $29 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Springfield it is about $347 (46%) higher.
