Home prices

The value of the median home in Centerton is $185,400. This is approximately $23,380 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $32,100 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $67,300 (56%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Centerton is $1,091 a month. It is roughly $286 (35%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $29 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $347 (46%) higher.
