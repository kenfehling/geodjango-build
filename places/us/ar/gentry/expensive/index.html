In comparison to its surrounding areas, Gentry is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Tulsa however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gentry is valued at $124,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $9,760 (8%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $93,100 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $15,500 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median rent in Gentry is $798 a month. This is about $106 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $264 (24%) lower, and is about $31 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.
