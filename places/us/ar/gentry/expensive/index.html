Home prices

The median home in Gentry costs $124,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $9,760 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $93,100 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Tulsa it is roughly $15,500 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Gentry the median monthly rent is $798. This is about $106 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $264 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $31 (3%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
