In comparison to the surrounding areas, Nashville is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Hot Springs however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Nashville is $89,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $19,800 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $128,000 (58%) lower, and is approximately $33,400 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Hot Springs.

Rents

The median rent in Nashville is $539 a month. It is approximately $86 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $523 (49%) lower, and it is about $188 (25%) lower compared to the city of Hot Springs.
