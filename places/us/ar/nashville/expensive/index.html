In comparison to its surrounding areas, Nashville is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Hot Springs.

Home prices

The median home value in Nashville is $89,500. This is about $19,800 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $128,000 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Hot Springs is about $33,400 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Nashville the median monthly rent is $539. This is approximately $86 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $523 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Hot Springs it is roughly $188 (25%) lower.
