In comparison to its nearby areas, Nashville is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Hot Springs however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nashville costs $89,500. It is roughly $19,800 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $128,000 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $33,400 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Hot Springs.

Rents

Nashville's median monthly rent is $539. This is approximately $86 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $523 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Hot Springs is about $188 (25%) lower.
