In comparison to other places in this area, Nashville is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Hot Springs, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Nashville is $89,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $19,800 (28%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $128,000 (58%) lower, and is roughly $33,400 (27%) lower compared to the city of Hot Springs.

Rents

Nashville's median rent is $539 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $86 (13%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $523 (49%) lower, and is approximately $188 (25%) lower compared to the city of Hot Springs.
