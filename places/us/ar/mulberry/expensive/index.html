Rents

Mulberry's median monthly rent is $596. This is approximately $19 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $466 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $203 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
