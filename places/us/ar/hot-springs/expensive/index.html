Home prices

Hot Springs's median home price is $122,900. It is approximately $65,360 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $94,600 (43%) lower, and it is about $44,700 (26%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median rent in Hot Springs is $727 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $69 (8%) lower. It is roughly $335 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $145 (16%) lower.
