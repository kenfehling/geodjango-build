Home prices

The median home cost in Gassville is $93,900. This is about $21,540 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $123,600 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Springfield is about $24,200 (20%) lower.

Rents

Gassville's median monthly rent is $768. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $136 (21%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $294 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $24 (3%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.
