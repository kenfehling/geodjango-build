In comparison to other places in this area, Corinth is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Little Rock, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corinth is valued at $92,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $6,640 (7%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $125,000 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $75,100 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Corinth the median monthly rent is $575. It is about $57 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $487 (45%) lower, and is approximately $297 (34%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
