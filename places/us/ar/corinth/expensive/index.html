Home prices

The median home cost in Corinth is $92,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $6,640 (7%) higher. It is roughly $125,000 (57%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Little Rock it is roughly $75,100 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Corinth the median rent is $575 per month. It is approximately $57 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $297 (34%) lower.
