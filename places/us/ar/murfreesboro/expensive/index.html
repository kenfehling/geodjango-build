Compared to its surrounding areas, Murfreesboro is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Hot Springs.

Home prices

The median home price in Murfreesboro is $78,100. It is approximately $18,240 (30%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $139,400 (64%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Hot Springs it is approximately $44,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Murfreesboro the median rent is $549 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $11 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $513 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Hot Springs it is roughly $178 (24%) lower.
