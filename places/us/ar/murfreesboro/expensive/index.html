In comparison to the surrounding areas, Murfreesboro is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Hot Springs, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Murfreesboro costs $78,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $18,240 (30%) higher.It is about $139,400 (64%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $44,800 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Hot Springs.

Rents

In Murfreesboro the median monthly rent is $549. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $11 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $513 (48%) lower, and it is about $178 (24%) lower compared to the city of Hot Springs.
