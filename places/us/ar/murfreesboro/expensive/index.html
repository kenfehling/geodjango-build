Compared to its nearby areas, Murfreesboro is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Hot Springs, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Murfreesboro costs $78,100. This is approximately $18,240 (30%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $139,400 (64%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Hot Springs it is roughly $44,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Murfreesboro is $549 a month. This is roughly $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $513 (48%) lower, and is approximately $178 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Hot Springs.
