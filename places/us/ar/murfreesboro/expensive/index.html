Compared to the surrounding areas, Murfreesboro is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Hot Springs.

Home prices

The median home price in Murfreesboro is $78,100. This is approximately $18,240 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $139,400 (64%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $44,800 (36%) lower compared to the city of Hot Springs.

Rents

In Murfreesboro the median monthly rent is $549. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $11 (1%) lower.It is roughly $513 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Hot Springs it is roughly $178 (24%) lower.
