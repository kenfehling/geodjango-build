Home prices

The median home in Shannon Hills is valued at $141,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $31,080 (28%) higher. It is approximately $76,000 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is about $26,100 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Shannon Hills the median rent is $1,086 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $265 (32%) higher. It is about $24 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $214 (24%) higher compared to the city of Little Rock.
