Home prices

The median home in Hazen is valued at $77,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $4,140 (5%) higher. It is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Memphis is roughly $24,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

Hazen's median rent is $699 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $141 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $363 (34%) lower, and is about $202 (22%) lower compared to the city of Memphis.
