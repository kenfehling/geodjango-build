Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lincoln is $103,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $14,740 (12%) lower. It is roughly $113,900 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $36,300 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Tulsa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lincoln is $781. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $45 (6%) higher. It is roughly $281 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $48 (5%) lower compared to the city of Tulsa.
