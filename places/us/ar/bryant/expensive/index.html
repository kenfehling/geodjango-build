Home prices

Bryant's median home price is $165,400. This is roughly $57,920 (53%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $52,100 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $2,200 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Bryant the median rent is $882 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $11 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $180 (16%) lower, and is about $10 (1%) higher compared to the city of Little Rock.
