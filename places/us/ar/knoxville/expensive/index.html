Home prices

The median home value in Knoxville is $97,300. This is approximately $3,120 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $120,200 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is about $70,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Knoxville the median monthly rent is $725. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $122 (20%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $147 (16%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
