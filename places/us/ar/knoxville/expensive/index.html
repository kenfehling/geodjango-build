Home prices

The median home in Knoxville is valued at $97,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $3,120 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $120,200 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $70,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Knoxville the median rent is $725 a month. This is roughly $122 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is approximately $147 (16%) lower.
