Home prices

Knoxville's median home value is $97,300. This is about $3,120 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $120,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is about $70,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Knoxville is $725 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $122 (20%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $147 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
