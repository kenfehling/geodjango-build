Home prices

The median home in Hot Springs Village is valued at $207,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $95,200 (84%) higher.It is approximately $9,700 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $40,200 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Hot Springs Village the median rent is $1,076 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $200 (15%) lower.It is roughly $14 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $204 (23%) higher compared to the city of Little Rock.
