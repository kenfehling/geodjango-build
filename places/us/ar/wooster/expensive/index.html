Home prices

The median home value in Wooster is $205,800. It is approximately $94,120 (84%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $11,700 (5%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $38,200 (22%) higher compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median rent in Wooster is $725 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $36 (4%) lower. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $147 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
