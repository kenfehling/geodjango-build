In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wooster is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Little Rock however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wooster costs $205,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $94,120 (84%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $11,700 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock is approximately $38,200 (22%) higher.

Rents

Wooster's median rent is $725 per month. This is roughly $36 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $147 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
