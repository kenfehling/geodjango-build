Home prices

The median home in Wooster is valued at $205,800. This is roughly $94,120 (84%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $11,700 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is about $38,200 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Wooster the median monthly rent is $725. This is roughly $36 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $147 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.
