Home prices

The median home in Wooster is valued at $205,800. It is roughly $94,120 (84%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $11,700 (5%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $38,200 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Wooster the median rent is $725 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $36 (4%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and is about $147 (16%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
