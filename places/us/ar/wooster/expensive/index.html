Home prices

The value of the median home in Wooster is $205,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $94,120 (84%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $11,700 (5%) lower, and is roughly $38,200 (22%) higher compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

The median rent in Wooster is $725 per month. It is about $36 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $147 (16%) lower compared to the city of Little Rock.
