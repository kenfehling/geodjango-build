Home prices

The median home in Wooster is valued at $205,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $94,120 (84%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $11,700 (5%) lower, and is roughly $38,200 (22%) higher compared to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Wooster the median rent is $725 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $36 (4%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Little Rock it is approximately $147 (16%) lower.
