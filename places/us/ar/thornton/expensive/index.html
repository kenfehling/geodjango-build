Home prices

The median home in Thornton costs $71,700. This is about $7,600 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $145,800 (67%) lower, and is about $95,900 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Little Rock.

Rents

In Thornton the median monthly rent is $576. This is about $37 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $486 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Little Rock is roughly $296 (33%) lower.
