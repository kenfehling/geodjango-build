Rents

Mount Pleasant's median monthly rent is $532. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $37 (7%) higher. It is roughly $530 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $322 (37%) lower compared to the city of North Little Rock.
