Rents

The median rent in Pocahontas is $620 per month. It is approximately $90 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $442 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $144 (18%) lower compared to the city of Jonesboro.
