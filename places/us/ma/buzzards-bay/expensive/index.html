Home prices

The value of the median home in Buzzards Bay is $294,700. This is approximately $42,360 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $77,200 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $238,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Buzzards Bay is $1,303. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $117 (9%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $241 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $317 (19%) lower.
