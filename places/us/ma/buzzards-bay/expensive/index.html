In comparison to its surrounding areas, Buzzards Bay is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Buzzards Bay is $294,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $42,360 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $77,200 (35%) higher, and it is approximately $238,000 (44%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Buzzards Bay the median rent is $1,303 per month. It is approximately $117 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $241 (22%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $317 (19%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
