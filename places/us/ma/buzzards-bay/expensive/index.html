In comparison to other places in this area, Buzzards Bay is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Buzzards Bay is valued at $294,700. This is roughly $42,360 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $77,200 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $238,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

Buzzards Bay's median rent is $1,303 per month. It is approximately $117 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $241 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $317 (19%) lower.
