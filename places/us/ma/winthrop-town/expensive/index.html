The cost of the median home in Winthrop Town is $439,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $8,260 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $222,300 (102%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $92,900 (17%) lower.

Rents

Winthrop Town's median monthly rent is $1,415. It is roughly $53 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $353 (33%) higher, and is about $205 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
