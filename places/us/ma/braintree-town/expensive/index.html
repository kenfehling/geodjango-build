Compared to the surrounding areas, Braintree Town is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Braintree Town is valued at $456,500. This is about $40,720 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $239,000 (109%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $76,200 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Braintree Town the median rent is $1,522 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $164 (12%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $460 (43%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $98 (6%) lower.
