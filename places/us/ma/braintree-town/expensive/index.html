Home prices

The median home in Braintree Town is valued at $456,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $40,720 (9%) higher.It is approximately $239,000 (109%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $76,200 (14%) lower.

Rents

Braintree Town's median rent is $1,522 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $164 (12%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $460 (43%) higher, and it is about $98 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
