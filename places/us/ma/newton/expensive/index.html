Compared to other places in this area, Newton is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Newton costs $914,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $107,940 (13%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $697,200 (320%) higher, and it is approximately $382,000 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Newton is $1,940 per month. It is roughly $86 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $878 (82%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $320 (19%) higher.
