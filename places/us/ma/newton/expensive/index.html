In comparison to the surrounding areas, Newton is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Newton is $914,700. It is roughly $107,940 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $697,200 (320%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $382,000 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Newton the median monthly rent is $1,940. This is about $86 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $878 (82%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $320 (19%) higher.
