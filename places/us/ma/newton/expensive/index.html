Compared to its surrounding areas, Newton is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Newton is valued at $914,700. It is approximately $107,940 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $697,200 (320%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $382,000 (71%) higher.

Rents

Newton's median monthly rent is $1,940. It is approximately $86 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $878 (82%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $320 (19%) higher.
