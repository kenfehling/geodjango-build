Home prices

The median home value in Newton is $914,700. This is about $107,940 (13%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $697,200 (320%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $382,000 (71%) higher.

Rents

Newton's median monthly rent is $1,940. It is approximately $86 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $878 (82%) higher, and it is roughly $320 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
