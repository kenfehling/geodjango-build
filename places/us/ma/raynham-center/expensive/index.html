Home prices

The median home in Raynham Center is valued at $348,900. This is approximately $27,400 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $131,400 (60%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $183,800 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents
