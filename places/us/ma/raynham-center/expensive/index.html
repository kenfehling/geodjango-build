Compared to the surrounding areas, Raynham Center is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Bostonhowever, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Raynham Center's median home price is $348,900. It is roughly $27,400 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $131,400 (60%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $183,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

Raynham Center's median rent is $1,750 per month. It is approximately $399 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $688 (64%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $130 (8%) higher.
