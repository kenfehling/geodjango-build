Home prices

The median home in Raynham Center is valued at $348,900. This is roughly $27,400 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $131,400 (60%) higher, and it is roughly $183,800 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Raynham Center's median rent is $1,750 per month. It is about $399 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $688 (64%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $130 (8%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
