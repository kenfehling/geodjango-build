Compared to its surrounding areas, Attleboro is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Attleboro is valued at $292,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $50,040 (20%) higher. It is about $75,400 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $239,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

Attleboro's median monthly rent is $1,081. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $96 (9%) higher. It is roughly $19 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $539 (33%) lower.
