Compared to nearby areas, Attleboro is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Attleboro costs $292,900. This is about $50,040 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $75,400 (34%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $239,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Attleboro is $1,081. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $96 (9%) higher.It is roughly $19 (1%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $539 (33%) lower.
