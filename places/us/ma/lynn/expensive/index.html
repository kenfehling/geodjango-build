In comparison to nearby areas, Lynn is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lynn is $323,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $126,980 (28%) lower.It is approximately $105,600 (48%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $209,600 (39%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lynn is $1,189. This is roughly $204 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $127 (11%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $431 (26%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
