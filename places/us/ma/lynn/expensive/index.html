In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lynn is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Lynn's median home price is $323,100. It is roughly $126,980 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $105,600 (48%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $209,600 (39%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Lynn is $1,189 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $204 (14%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $127 (11%) higher, and it is about $431 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
