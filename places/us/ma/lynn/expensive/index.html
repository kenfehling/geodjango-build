Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lynn is $323,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $126,980 (28%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $105,600 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $209,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lynn is $1,189. It is roughly $204 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $127 (11%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $431 (26%) lower.
