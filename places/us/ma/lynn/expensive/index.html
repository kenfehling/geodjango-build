In comparison to its nearby areas, Lynn is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Lynn costs $323,100. This is approximately $126,980 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $105,600 (48%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $209,600 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Lynn the median rent is $1,189 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $204 (14%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $127 (11%) higher, and is roughly $431 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
