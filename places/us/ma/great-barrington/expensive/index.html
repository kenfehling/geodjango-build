Compared to its nearby areas, Great Barrington is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Yonkers, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Great Barrington is valued at $384,600. This is roughly $164,420 (74%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $167,100 (76%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $19,300 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Yonkers.

Rents

In Great Barrington the median monthly rent is $1,016. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $46 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yonkers is roughly $367 (26%) lower.
