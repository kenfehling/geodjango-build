Home prices

The median home price in Great Barrington is $384,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $164,420 (74%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $167,100 (76%) higher, and is approximately $19,300 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Yonkers.

Rents

In Great Barrington the median rent is $1,016 per month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $46 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $367 (26%) lower compared to the city of Yonkers.
