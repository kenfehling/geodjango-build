Home prices

The value of the median home in Great Barrington is $384,600. It is roughly $164,420 (74%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $167,100 (76%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $19,300 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Yonkers.

Rents

The median rent in Great Barrington is $1,016 per month. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $46 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $367 (26%) lower compared to the city of Yonkers.
