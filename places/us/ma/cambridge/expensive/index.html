Compared to its nearby areas, Cambridge is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Cambridge's median home cost is $768,300. It is about $170,180 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $550,800 (253%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $235,600 (44%) higher.

Rents

Cambridge's median monthly rent is $2,221. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $421 (23%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $1,159 (109%) higher, and it is about $601 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
