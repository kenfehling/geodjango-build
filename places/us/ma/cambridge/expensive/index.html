Compared to its surrounding areas, Cambridge is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cambridge is $768,300. This is roughly $170,180 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $550,800 (253%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $235,600 (44%) higher.

Rents

In Cambridge the median monthly rent is $2,221. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $421 (23%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $1,159 (109%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $601 (37%) higher.
