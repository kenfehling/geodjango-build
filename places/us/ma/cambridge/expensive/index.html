In comparison to its nearby areas, Cambridge is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cambridge is $768,300. It is approximately $170,180 (28%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $550,800 (253%) higher, and it is approximately $235,600 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Cambridge the median monthly rent is $2,221. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $421 (23%) higher.It is roughly $1,159 (109%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $601 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
