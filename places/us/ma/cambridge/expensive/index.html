Compared to the surrounding areas, Cambridge is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cambridge is $768,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $170,180 (28%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $550,800 (253%) higher, and is approximately $235,600 (44%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Cambridge is $2,221 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $421 (23%) higher. It is approximately $1,159 (109%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $601 (37%) higher.
