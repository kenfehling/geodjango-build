Home prices

The cost of the median home in Warren is $168,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $44,160 (20%) lower. It is about $49,200 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $364,400 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Warren the median monthly rent is $647. This is approximately $357 (35%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $415 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $973 (60%) lower.
