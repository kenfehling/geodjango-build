Home prices

The median home cost in Haverhill is $300,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $110,020 (26%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $83,100 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $232,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Haverhill is $1,208. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $25 (2%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $146 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $412 (25%) lower.
