Compared to other places in this area, Monument Beach is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Monument Beach costs $397,700. This is roughly $75,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $180,200 (82%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $135,000 (25%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Monument Beach is $1,474 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $243 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $412 (38%) higher, and it is about $146 (9%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
