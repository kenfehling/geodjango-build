Compared to other places in this area, Bridgewater Town is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bridgewater Town costs $358,900. It is roughly $54,760 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $141,400 (65%) higher, and is about $173,800 (32%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Bridgewater Town's median monthly rent is $1,597. It is about $251 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $535 (50%) higher, and it is approximately $23 (1%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
