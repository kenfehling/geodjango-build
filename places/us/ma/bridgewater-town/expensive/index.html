Bridgewater Town's median home cost is $358,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $54,760 (18%) higher. It is approximately $141,400 (65%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $173,800 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bridgewater Town is $1,597. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $251 (18%) higher. It is approximately $535 (50%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $23 (1%) lower.
