Compared to the surrounding areas, Bridgewater Town is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Bridgewater Town's median home price is $358,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $54,760 (18%) higher.It is roughly $141,400 (65%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $173,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Bridgewater Town the median rent is $1,597 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $251 (18%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $535 (50%) higher, and is approximately $23 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
