Home prices

The median home in Mansfield Center costs $347,400. This is roughly $28,840 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $129,900 (59%) higher, and it is approximately $185,300 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Mansfield Center the median monthly rent is $1,257. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is about $195 (18%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $363 (22%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
