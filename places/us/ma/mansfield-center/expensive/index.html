Home prices

The price of the median home in Mansfield Center is $347,400. This is about $28,840 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $129,900 (59%) higher, and it is approximately $185,300 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mansfield Center is $1,257. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $195 (18%) higher, and is roughly $363 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
