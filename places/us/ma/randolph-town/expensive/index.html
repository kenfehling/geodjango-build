In comparison to the surrounding areas, Randolph Town is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Randolph Town is $331,300. It is about $109,520 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $113,800 (52%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $201,400 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Randolph Town's median rent is $1,507 per month. It is approximately $146 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $445 (41%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $113 (6%) lower.
