Home prices

The price of the median home in Randolph Town is $331,300. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $109,520 (24%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $113,800 (52%) higher, and is roughly $201,400 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Randolph Town the median monthly rent is $1,507. It is roughly $146 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $445 (41%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $113 (6%) lower.
