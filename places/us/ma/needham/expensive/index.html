Home prices

The median home in Needham costs $855,300. It is approximately $26,700 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $637,800 (293%) higher, and is about $322,600 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Needham's median rent is $1,483 a month. It is approximately $705 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $421 (39%) higher, and it is roughly $137 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
