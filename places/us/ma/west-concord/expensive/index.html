Home prices

The median home cost in West Concord is $669,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $175,760 (35%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $451,700 (207%) higher, and it is approximately $136,500 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

West Concord's median rent is $2,065 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $545 (35%) higher. It is approximately $1,003 (94%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $445 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
