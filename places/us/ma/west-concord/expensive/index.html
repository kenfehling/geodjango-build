In comparison to other places in this area, West Concord is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Concord is valued at $669,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $175,760 (35%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $451,700 (207%) higher, and it is roughly $136,500 (25%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

West Concord's median rent is $2,065 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $545 (35%) higher. It is about $1,003 (94%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $445 (27%) higher.
