Compared to other places in this area, Revere is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Revere is $373,800. This is about $24,840 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $156,300 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $158,900 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Revere is $1,419 per month. This is about $62 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $357 (33%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $201 (12%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
