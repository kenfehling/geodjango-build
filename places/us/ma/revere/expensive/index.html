Compared to nearby areas, Revere is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Revere is valued at $373,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $24,840 (6%) lower.It is roughly $156,300 (71%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $158,900 (29%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Revere is $1,419 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $62 (4%) higher.It is approximately $357 (33%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $201 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
