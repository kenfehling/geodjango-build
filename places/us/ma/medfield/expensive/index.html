Home prices

The median home value in Medfield is $615,200. This is approximately $91,420 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $397,700 (182%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $82,500 (15%) higher.

Rents

Medfield's median monthly rent is $1,347. It is approximately $530 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $285 (26%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $273 (16%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
