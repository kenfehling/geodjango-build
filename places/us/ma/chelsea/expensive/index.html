Home prices

The median home price in Chelsea is $350,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $106,400 (23%) lower. It is about $133,300 (61%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $181,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chelsea is $1,419. This is about $129 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $357 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $201 (12%) lower.
