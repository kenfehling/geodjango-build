Home prices

Rents

Chelsea's median monthly rent is $1,419. It is roughly $129 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $357 (33%) higher, and it is roughly $201 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
