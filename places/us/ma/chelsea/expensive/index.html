In comparison to its surrounding areas, Chelsea is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chelsea costs $350,800. This is approximately $106,400 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $133,300 (61%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $181,900 (34%) lower.

Rents

Chelsea's median rent is $1,419 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $129 (8%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $357 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $201 (12%) lower.
