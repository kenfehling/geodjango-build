Compared to its nearby areas, Chelsea is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chelsea is valued at $350,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $106,400 (23%) lower. It is roughly $133,300 (61%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $181,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Chelsea's median monthly rent is $1,419. It is about $129 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $357 (33%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $201 (12%) lower.
