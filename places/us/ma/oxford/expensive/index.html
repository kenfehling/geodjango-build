Home prices

Oxford's median home cost is $236,800. It is about $14,420 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $19,300 (8%) higher, and is roughly $295,900 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Oxford the median rent is $820 a month. It is roughly $173 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $242 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $800 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
