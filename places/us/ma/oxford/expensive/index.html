Compared to its surrounding areas, Oxford is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home price in Oxford is $236,800. It is roughly $14,420 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $19,300 (8%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $295,900 (55%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oxford is $820. This is about $173 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $800 (49%) lower.
