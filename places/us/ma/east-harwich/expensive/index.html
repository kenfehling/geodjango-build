Home prices

Rents

The median rent in East Harwich is $1,603 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $449 (38%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $541 (50%) higher, and is roughly $17 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
