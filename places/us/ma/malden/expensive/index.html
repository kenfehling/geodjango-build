Home prices

The price of the median home in Malden is $401,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $38,400 (8%) lower. It is roughly $184,400 (84%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $130,800 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Malden is $1,577 a month. It is about $52 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $515 (48%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $43 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
