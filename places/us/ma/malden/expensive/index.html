Compared to the surrounding areas, Malden is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Malden is $401,900. It is about $38,400 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $184,400 (84%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is about $130,800 (24%) lower.

Rents

Malden's median monthly rent is $1,577. It is approximately $52 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $515 (48%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $43 (2%) lower.
