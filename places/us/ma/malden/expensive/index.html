Home prices

The median home price in Malden is $401,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $38,400 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $184,400 (84%) higher, and is approximately $130,800 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Malden is $1,577. This is approximately $52 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $515 (48%) higher, and is approximately $43 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
