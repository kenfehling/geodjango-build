Compared to its surrounding areas, Taunton is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Taunton costs $271,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $65,120 (19%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $54,300 (24%) higher, and it is approximately $260,900 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Taunton is $1,012. This is about $486 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $50 (4%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $608 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
