Compared to the surrounding areas, Taunton is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Taunton is valued at $271,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $65,120 (19%) lower.It is roughly $54,300 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $260,900 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Taunton's median rent is $1,012 per month. It is roughly $486 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $50 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $608 (37%) lower.
