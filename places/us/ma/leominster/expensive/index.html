Home prices

The median home cost in Leominster is $241,800. Compared to other places in this area it is is very similar. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $24,300 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $290,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Leominster is $973. This is approximately $28 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $89 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $647 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
