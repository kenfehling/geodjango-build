Home prices

The median home in Leominster is valued at $241,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is about $24,300 (11%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $290,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

Leominster's median monthly rent is $973. Compared to other places in this area it is about $28 (2%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $89 (8%) lower, and is approximately $647 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
