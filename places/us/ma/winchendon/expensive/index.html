The median home price in Winchendon is $184,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $26,100 (16%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $33,400 (15%) lower, and is about $348,600 (65%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Winchendon is $695. It is approximately $93 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $367 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $925 (57%) lower.
