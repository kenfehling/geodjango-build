Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Watertown Town is $1,853. This is about $71 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $791 (74%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $233 (14%) higher.
