Compared to other places in this area, Watertown Town is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Watertown Town is $559,700. It is about $184,560 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $342,200 (157%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $27,000 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Watertown Town the median rent is $1,853 a month. It is approximately $71 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $791 (74%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $233 (14%) higher.
