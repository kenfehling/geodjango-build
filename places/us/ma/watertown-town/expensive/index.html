Compared to other places in this area, Watertown Town is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Boston, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Watertown Town is $559,700. It is about $184,560 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $342,200 (157%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $27,000 (5%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Watertown Town's median rent is $1,853 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $71 (3%) lower. It is roughly $791 (74%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $233 (14%) higher.
