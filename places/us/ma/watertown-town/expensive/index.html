In comparison to the surrounding areas, Watertown Town is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Watertown Town is $559,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $184,560 (24%) lower. It is about $342,200 (157%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $27,000 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Watertown Town the median rent is $1,853 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $71 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $791 (74%) higher, and is roughly $233 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
