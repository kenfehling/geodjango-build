Home prices

The median home in Baldwinville is valued at $183,300. It is approximately $8,840 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $34,200 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $349,400 (65%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Baldwinville is $519. It is roughly $410 (44%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $543 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $1,101 (67%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
