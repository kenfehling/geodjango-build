Home prices

The price of the median home in Walpole is $418,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $28,360 (6%) lower. It is roughly $200,500 (92%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $114,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Walpole the median monthly rent is $1,201. This is approximately $299 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $139 (13%) higher, and it is roughly $419 (25%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
