Home prices

Rents

In Everett the median rent is $1,454 a month. This is roughly $167 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $392 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $166 (10%) lower.
