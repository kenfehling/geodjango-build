Home prices

Everett's median home value is $405,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $52,600 (11%) lower. It is roughly $188,300 (86%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $126,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Everett the median rent is $1,454 per month. It is about $167 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $392 (36%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $166 (10%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
