Compared to its surrounding areas, Everett is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Everett is valued at $405,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $52,600 (11%) lower. It is about $188,300 (86%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $126,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Everett is $1,454. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $167 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $392 (36%) higher, and is roughly $166 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
