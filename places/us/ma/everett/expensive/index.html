Home prices

The median home in Everett costs $405,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $52,600 (11%) lower.It is roughly $188,300 (86%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $126,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Everett the median rent is $1,454 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $167 (10%) lower.It is roughly $392 (36%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $166 (10%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
