In comparison to its surrounding areas, Plymouth is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Plymouth is valued at $348,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $294,880 (45%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $131,300 (60%) higher, and is approximately $183,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Plymouth's median monthly rent is $1,319. This is approximately $435 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $257 (24%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $301 (18%) lower.
