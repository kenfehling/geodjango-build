Home prices

The median home in Plymouth is valued at $348,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $294,880 (45%) lower. It is approximately $131,300 (60%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $183,900 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Plymouth is $1,319. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $435 (24%) lower. It is about $257 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $301 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
