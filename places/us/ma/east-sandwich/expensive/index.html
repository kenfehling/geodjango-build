Home prices

East Sandwich's median home cost is $484,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $101,860 (26%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $266,900 (122%) higher, and is about $48,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In East Sandwich the median monthly rent is $1,289. This is approximately $24 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $227 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $331 (20%) lower.
