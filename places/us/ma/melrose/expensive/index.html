Compared to its nearby areas, Melrose is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Boston, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Melrose's median home cost is $570,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $110,700 (24%) higher. It is roughly $352,800 (162%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $37,600 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Melrose is $1,516. It is approximately $84 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $454 (42%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $104 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
