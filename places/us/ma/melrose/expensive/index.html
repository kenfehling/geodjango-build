In comparison to the surrounding areas, Melrose is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Melrose is valued at $570,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $110,700 (24%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $352,800 (162%) higher, and it is about $37,600 (7%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Melrose's median monthly rent is $1,516. It is about $84 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $454 (42%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $104 (6%) lower.
