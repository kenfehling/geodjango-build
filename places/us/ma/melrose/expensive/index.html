Compared to nearby areas, Melrose is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a little more expensive even compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Melrose is valued at $570,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $110,700 (24%) higher.It is approximately $352,800 (162%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $37,600 (7%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Melrose the median rent is $1,516 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $84 (5%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $454 (42%) higher, and it is approximately $104 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
