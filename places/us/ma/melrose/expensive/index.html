Home prices

The median home in Melrose is valued at $570,300. This is roughly $110,700 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $352,800 (162%) higher, and is approximately $37,600 (7%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Melrose the median monthly rent is $1,516. This is roughly $84 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $454 (42%) higher, and it is approximately $104 (6%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
