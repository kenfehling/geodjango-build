In comparison to its nearby areas, Melrose is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Boston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Melrose costs $570,300. This is roughly $110,700 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $352,800 (162%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $37,600 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Melrose the median monthly rent is $1,516. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $84 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $454 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $104 (6%) lower.
