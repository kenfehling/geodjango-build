In comparison to other places in this area, Westborough is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Westborough costs $380,800. It is about $25,780 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $163,300 (75%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $151,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Westborough the median rent is $1,226 a month. This is about $112 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $164 (15%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $394 (24%) lower.
