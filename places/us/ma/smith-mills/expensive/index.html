Home prices

The median home cost in Smith Mills is $305,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $38,900 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $88,200 (40%) higher, and is about $227,000 (42%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Smith Mills's median monthly rent is $560. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $307 (35%) lower.It is roughly $502 (47%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $1,060 (65%) lower.
