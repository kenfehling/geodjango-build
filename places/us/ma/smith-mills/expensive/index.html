Home prices

The median home cost in Smith Mills is $305,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $38,900 (14%) higher. It is about $88,200 (40%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $227,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

Smith Mills's median rent is $560 a month. It is roughly $307 (35%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $502 (47%) lower, and it is about $1,060 (65%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
