Home prices

The median home price in Smith Mills is $305,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $38,900 (14%) higher.It is approximately $88,200 (40%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $227,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Smith Mills the median rent is $560 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $307 (35%) lower.It is roughly $502 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $1,060 (65%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
