Home prices

The median home price in Marblehead is $675,400. This is about $277,300 (69%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $457,900 (210%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $142,700 (26%) higher.

Rents

Marblehead's median rent is $1,578 a month. This is approximately $170 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $516 (48%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $42 (2%) lower.
