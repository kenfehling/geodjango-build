Home prices

The price of the median home in Palmer Town is $197,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $32,620 (14%) lower. It is about $20,500 (9%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $335,700 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Palmer Town the median rent is $962 a month. This is approximately $162 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $100 (9%) lower, and is about $658 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
