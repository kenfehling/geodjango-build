Home prices

The value of the median home in Palmer Town is $197,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $32,620 (14%) lower. It is approximately $20,500 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $335,700 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Palmer Town the median rent is $962 a month. This is about $162 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $100 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $658 (40%) lower.
