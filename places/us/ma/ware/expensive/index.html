Home prices

The median home in Ware costs $179,400. It is approximately $37,240 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $38,100 (17%) lower, and it is about $353,300 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Ware is $943 per month. This is roughly $25 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $119 (11%) lower, and is roughly $677 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
