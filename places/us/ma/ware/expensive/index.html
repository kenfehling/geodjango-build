Home prices

The median home price in Ware is $179,400. This is approximately $37,240 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $38,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $353,300 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Ware the median monthly rent is $943. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $25 (2%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $119 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $677 (41%) lower.
