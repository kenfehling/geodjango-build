Home prices

The median home in Ware costs $179,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $37,240 (17%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $38,100 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $353,300 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Ware is $943 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $25 (2%) higher. It is approximately $119 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $677 (41%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
