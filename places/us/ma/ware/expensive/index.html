Home prices

The median home in Ware costs $179,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $37,240 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $38,100 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $353,300 (66%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Ware the median monthly rent is $943. Compared to other places in this area this is about $25 (2%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $119 (11%) lower, and is roughly $677 (41%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
