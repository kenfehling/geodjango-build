Home prices

The median home in Medford costs $500,800. It is approximately $110,880 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $283,300 (130%) higher, and is approximately $31,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Medford is $1,817. This is approximately $110 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $755 (71%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $197 (12%) higher.
