Home prices

Medford's median home cost is $500,800. This is about $110,880 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $283,300 (130%) higher, and is about $31,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Medford the median monthly rent is $1,817. It is roughly $110 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $755 (71%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $197 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
