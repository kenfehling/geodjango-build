Home prices

The median home in Medford is valued at $500,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $110,880 (18%) lower.It is about $283,300 (130%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $31,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Medford is $1,817. This is roughly $110 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $755 (71%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $197 (12%) higher.
