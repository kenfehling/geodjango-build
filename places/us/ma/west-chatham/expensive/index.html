Home prices

The median home in West Chatham costs $628,000. This is approximately $219,480 (53%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $410,500 (188%) higher, and is roughly $95,300 (17%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In West Chatham the median rent is $1,353 per month. It is approximately $149 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $291 (27%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $267 (16%) lower.
