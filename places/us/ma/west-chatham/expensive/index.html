Compared to the surrounding areas, West Chatham is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in West Chatham is $628,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $219,480 (53%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $410,500 (188%) higher, and is roughly $95,300 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

West Chatham's median monthly rent is $1,353. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $149 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $291 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $267 (16%) lower.
