Compared to its nearby areas, Arlington is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Boston, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Arlington is $642,200. It is roughly $63,520 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $424,700 (195%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $109,500 (20%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Arlington's median rent is $1,730 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $159 (8%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $668 (62%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $110 (6%) higher.
