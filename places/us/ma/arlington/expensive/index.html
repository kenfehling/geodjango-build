Compared to its surrounding areas, Arlington is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Arlington costs $642,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $63,520 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $424,700 (195%) higher, and it is roughly $109,500 (20%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Arlington is $1,730 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $159 (8%) lower. It is roughly $668 (62%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $110 (6%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
