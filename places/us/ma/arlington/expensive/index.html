Home prices

Arlington's median home price is $642,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $63,520 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $424,700 (195%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $109,500 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Arlington is $1,730. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $159 (8%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $668 (62%) higher, and it is approximately $110 (6%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
