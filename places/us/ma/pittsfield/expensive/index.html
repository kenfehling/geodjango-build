Compared to other places in this area, Pittsfield is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of New Haven.

Home prices

Pittsfield's median home price is $174,300. This is roughly $51,180 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $43,200 (19%) lower, and it is about $24,700 (12%) lower compared to the city of New Haven.

Rents

Pittsfield's median rent is $893 per month. It is about $16 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $169 (15%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New Haven it is approximately $303 (25%) lower.
