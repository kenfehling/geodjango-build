Compared to other places in this area, North Adams is a lot less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home in North Adams is valued at $140,900. It is about $72,000 (33%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $76,600 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $86,200 (37%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In North Adams the median monthly rent is $757. It is roughly $141 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is about $338 (30%) lower.
