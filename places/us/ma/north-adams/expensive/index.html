Home prices

The price of the median home in North Adams is $140,900. This is approximately $72,000 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $76,600 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $86,200 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In North Adams the median rent is $757 per month. It is about $141 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $305 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $338 (30%) lower.
