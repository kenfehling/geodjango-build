Rents

The median monthly rent in North Adams is $757. It is approximately $141 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $305 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is approximately $338 (30%) lower.
