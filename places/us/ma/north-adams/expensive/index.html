In comparison to other places in this area, North Adams is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home value in North Adams is $140,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $72,000 (33%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $76,600 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is roughly $86,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

North Adams's median rent is $757 a month. This is approximately $141 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $338 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
