In comparison to the surrounding areas, East Dennis is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Dennis is $454,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $70,140 (18%) higher. It is about $237,400 (109%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $77,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

In East Dennis the median monthly rent is $1,278. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $113 (8%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $216 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $342 (21%) lower.
