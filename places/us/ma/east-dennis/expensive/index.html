Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Dennis is $1,278. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $113 (8%) lower. It is approximately $216 (20%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $342 (21%) lower.
