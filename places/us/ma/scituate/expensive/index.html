Home prices

The median home in Scituate is valued at $540,100. This is about $30,500 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $322,600 (148%) higher, and it is about $7,400 (1%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Scituate the median rent is $1,093 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $397 (26%) lower.It is about $31 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $527 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
