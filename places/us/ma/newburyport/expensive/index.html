Compared to other places in this area, Newburyport is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Newburyport is $548,400. It is approximately $170,640 (45%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $330,900 (152%) higher, and is about $15,700 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Newburyport the median rent is $1,338 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $123 (10%) higher. It is approximately $276 (25%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $282 (17%) lower.
