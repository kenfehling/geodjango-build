Home prices

The price of the median home in Newburyport is $548,400. It is about $170,640 (45%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $330,900 (152%) higher, and it is about $15,700 (2%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newburyport is $1,338. This is roughly $123 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $276 (25%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $282 (17%) lower.
