Home prices

The median home in Newburyport costs $548,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $170,640 (45%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $330,900 (152%) higher, and it is approximately $15,700 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Newburyport the median monthly rent is $1,338. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $123 (10%) higher.It is approximately $276 (25%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $282 (17%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
