Home prices

The median home in Newburyport is valued at $548,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $170,640 (45%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $330,900 (152%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $15,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Newburyport the median monthly rent is $1,338. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $123 (10%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $276 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $282 (17%) lower.
