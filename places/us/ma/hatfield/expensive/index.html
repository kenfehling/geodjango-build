Home prices

The value of the median home in Hatfield is $311,500. It is about $27,980 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $94,000 (43%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is roughly $84,400 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Hatfield the median monthly rent is $859. This is approximately $407 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $203 (19%) lower, and is approximately $236 (21%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.
