In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hatfield is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hatfield is $311,500. It is about $27,980 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $94,000 (43%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $84,400 (37%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Hatfield the median monthly rent is $859. It is roughly $407 (32%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $203 (19%) lower, and is roughly $236 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
