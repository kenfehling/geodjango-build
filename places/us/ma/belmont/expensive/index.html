Compared to the surrounding areas, Belmont is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Belmont is $859,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $225,320 (35%) higher. It is roughly $642,100 (295%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $326,900 (61%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Belmont the median monthly rent is $2,002. This is approximately $121 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $940 (88%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $382 (23%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
