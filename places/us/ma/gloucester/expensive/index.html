Compared to other places in this area, Gloucester is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home cost in Gloucester is $425,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $79,020 (15%) lower.It is about $207,900 (95%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is about $107,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

Gloucester's median rent is $1,185 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $155 (11%) lower.It is roughly $123 (11%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $435 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
