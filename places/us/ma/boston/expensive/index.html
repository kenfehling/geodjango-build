Compared to other places in this area, Boston is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home cost in Boston is $532,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $129,517 (19%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $315,200 (144%) higher.

Rents

In Boston the median monthly rent is $1,620. This is roughly $221 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $558 (52%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole.
