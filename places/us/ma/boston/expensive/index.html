Compared to the surrounding areas, Boston is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home value in Boston is $532,700. It is approximately $129,517 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $315,200 (144%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation.

Rents

In Boston the median monthly rent is $1,620. It is roughly $221 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $558 (52%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole.
