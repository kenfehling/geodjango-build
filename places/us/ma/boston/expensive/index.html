Compared to nearby areas, Boston is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home cost in Boston is $532,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $129,517 (19%) lower. It is roughly $315,200 (144%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole.

Rents

Boston's median monthly rent is $1,620. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $221 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $558 (52%) higher.
