In comparison to nearby areas, Boston is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Boston is $532,700. It is roughly $129,517 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $315,200 (144%) higher.

Rents

In Boston the median monthly rent is $1,620. It is approximately $221 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $558 (52%) higher.
