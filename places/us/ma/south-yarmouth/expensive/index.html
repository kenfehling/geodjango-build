Compared to its surrounding areas, South Yarmouth is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Yarmouth is valued at $329,600. It is approximately $33,220 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $112,100 (51%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $203,100 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In South Yarmouth the median rent is $1,350 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $90 (6%) lower. It is roughly $288 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $270 (16%) lower.
