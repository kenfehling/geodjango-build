Compared to its surrounding areas, Provincetown is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a little more expensive even compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Provincetown is valued at $626,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $185,280 (42%) higher. It is about $408,800 (187%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $93,600 (17%) higher.

Rents

Provincetown's median monthly rent is $1,202. It is approximately $228 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $140 (13%) higher, and is approximately $418 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
