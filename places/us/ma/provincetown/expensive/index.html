Home prices

The median home in Provincetown costs $626,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $185,280 (42%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $408,800 (187%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $93,600 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Provincetown is $1,202 per month. This is roughly $228 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $140 (13%) higher, and is about $418 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
