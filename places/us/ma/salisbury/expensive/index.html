Compared to other places in this area, Salisbury is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Salisbury costs $359,900. This is about $69,840 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $142,400 (65%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $172,800 (32%) lower.

Rents

Salisbury's median monthly rent is $1,440. It is approximately $251 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $378 (35%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $180 (11%) lower.
