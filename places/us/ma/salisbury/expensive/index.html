Compared to the surrounding areas, Salisbury is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home price in Salisbury is $359,900. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $69,840 (16%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $142,400 (65%) higher, and it is about $172,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Salisbury is $1,440 a month. It is roughly $251 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $378 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $180 (11%) lower.
