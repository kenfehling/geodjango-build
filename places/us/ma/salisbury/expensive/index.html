In comparison to its surrounding areas, Salisbury is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Salisbury's median home cost is $359,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $69,840 (16%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $142,400 (65%) higher, and it is roughly $172,800 (32%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Salisbury's median rent is $1,440 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $251 (21%) higher.It is roughly $378 (35%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $180 (11%) lower.
