Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fiskdale is $309,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $95,540 (44%) higher. It is approximately $92,400 (42%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $222,800 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Fiskdale the median rent is $1,097 a month. This is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $35 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $523 (32%) lower.
