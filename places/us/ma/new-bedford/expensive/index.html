In comparison to the surrounding areas, New Bedford is substantially less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in New Bedford is $226,900. This is about $107,220 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $9,400 (4%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $305,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

In New Bedford the median monthly rent is $847. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $46 (5%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $215 (20%) lower, and is approximately $773 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
