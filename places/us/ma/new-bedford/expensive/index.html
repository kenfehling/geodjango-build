Compared to the surrounding areas, New Bedford is substantially less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in New Bedford costs $226,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $107,220 (32%) lower. It is approximately $9,400 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $305,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

In New Bedford the median monthly rent is $847. This is about $46 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $215 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $773 (47%) lower.
