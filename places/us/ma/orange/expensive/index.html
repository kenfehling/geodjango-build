Home prices

The median home in Orange costs $155,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $50,240 (24%) lower. It is approximately $62,300 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $377,500 (70%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Orange the median rent is $953 a month. This is approximately $130 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $109 (10%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $667 (41%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
