Compared to the surrounding areas, Orange is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Orange's median home price is $155,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $50,240 (24%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $62,300 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is about $377,500 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Orange is $953 a month. This is approximately $130 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $109 (10%) lower, and it is approximately $667 (41%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
