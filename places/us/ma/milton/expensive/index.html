Compared to other places in this area, Milton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Boston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Milton is $618,800. It is roughly $96,640 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $401,300 (184%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $86,100 (16%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Milton the median rent is $1,330 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $363 (21%) lower.It is roughly $268 (25%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $290 (17%) lower.
