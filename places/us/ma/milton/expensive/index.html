Home prices

The median home cost in Milton is $618,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $96,640 (18%) higher. It is about $401,300 (184%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $86,100 (16%) higher.

Rents

Milton's median rent is $1,330 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $363 (21%) lower. It is roughly $268 (25%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $290 (17%) lower.
