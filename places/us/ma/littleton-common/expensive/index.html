Home prices

The value of the median home in Littleton Common is $358,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $36,360 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $141,000 (64%) higher, and is approximately $174,200 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Littleton Common's median rent is $1,007 per month. This is approximately $178 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $55 (5%) lower, and is about $613 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
