In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bellingham is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Bellingham costs $278,300. This is roughly $32,960 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $60,800 (27%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $254,400 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Bellingham the median rent is $1,311 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $27 (2%) lower.It is about $249 (23%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $309 (19%) lower.
