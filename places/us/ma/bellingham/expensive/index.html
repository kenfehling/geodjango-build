In comparison to other places in this area, Bellingham is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bellingham is valued at $278,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $32,960 (10%) lower. It is about $60,800 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $254,400 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bellingham is $1,311 per month. This is about $27 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $249 (23%) higher, and is about $309 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
