Home prices

The cost of the median home in Waltham is $536,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $236,680 (30%) lower.It is about $319,000 (146%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston it is is very similar.

Rents

In Waltham the median monthly rent is $1,726. It is about $274 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $664 (62%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $106 (6%) higher.
