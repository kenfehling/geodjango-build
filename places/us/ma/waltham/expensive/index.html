Compared to other places in this area, Waltham is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Waltham is $536,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $236,680 (30%) lower. It is about $319,000 (146%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Waltham the median rent is $1,726 per month. It is roughly $274 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $664 (62%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $106 (6%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
