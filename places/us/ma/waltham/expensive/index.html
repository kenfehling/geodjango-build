Compared to its surrounding areas, Waltham is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Waltham is valued at $536,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $236,680 (30%) lower.It is roughly $319,000 (146%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Waltham is $1,726 a month. This is approximately $274 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $664 (62%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $106 (6%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
