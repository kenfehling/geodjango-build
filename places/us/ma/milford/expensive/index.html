Compared to its surrounding areas, Milford is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milford costs $303,300. It is approximately $70,760 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $85,800 (39%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $229,400 (43%) lower.

Rents

Milford's median monthly rent is $1,233. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $93 (6%) lower.It is approximately $171 (16%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $387 (23%) lower.
