In comparison to its surrounding areas, Milford is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milford is valued at $303,300. This is about $70,760 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $85,800 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $229,400 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Milford the median monthly rent is $1,233. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $93 (6%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $171 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $387 (23%) lower.
