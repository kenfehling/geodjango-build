In comparison to nearby areas, Milford is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milford is valued at $303,300. It is approximately $70,760 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $85,800 (39%) higher, and it is approximately $229,400 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Milford the median monthly rent is $1,233. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $93 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $171 (16%) higher, and it is about $387 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
