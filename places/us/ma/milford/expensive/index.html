In comparison to its nearby areas, Milford is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Milford costs $303,300. It is about $70,760 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $85,800 (39%) higher, and is about $229,400 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Milford's median monthly rent is $1,233. This is approximately $93 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $171 (16%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $387 (23%) lower.
