Home prices

The median home in Wilmington is valued at $473,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $20,100 (4%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $256,300 (117%) higher, and it is about $58,900 (11%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Wilmington the median rent is $1,981 per month. It is approximately $373 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $919 (86%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $361 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
