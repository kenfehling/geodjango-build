Home prices

Gardner's median home value is $185,700. It is about $4,740 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $31,800 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $347,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Gardner the median monthly rent is $838. It is about $26 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $224 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $782 (48%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
