Much like its surrounding areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home cost in Gardner is $185,700. This is roughly $4,740 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $31,800 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $347,000 (65%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Gardner's median rent is $838 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $26 (3%) higher. It is about $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $782 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
