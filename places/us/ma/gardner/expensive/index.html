Home prices

The median home in Gardner costs $185,700. This is approximately $4,740 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $31,800 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $347,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Gardner the median monthly rent is $838. This is roughly $26 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $782 (48%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
