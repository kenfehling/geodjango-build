Home prices

The cost of the median home in Gardner is $185,700. It is approximately $4,740 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $31,800 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $347,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Gardner is $838 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $26 (3%) higher. It is about $224 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $782 (48%) lower.
