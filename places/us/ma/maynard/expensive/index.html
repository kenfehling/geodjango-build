In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maynard is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maynard costs $350,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $121,220 (25%) lower.It is approximately $132,700 (61%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $182,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Maynard's median rent is $1,176 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $135 (10%) lower.It is roughly $114 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $444 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
