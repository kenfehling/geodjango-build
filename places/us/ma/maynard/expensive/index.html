In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maynard is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Maynard's median home cost is $350,200. It is roughly $121,220 (25%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $132,700 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $182,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

Maynard's median monthly rent is $1,176. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $135 (10%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $114 (10%) higher, and is approximately $444 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
