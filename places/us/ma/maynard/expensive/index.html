Compared to the surrounding areas, Maynard is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Maynard's median home value is $350,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $121,220 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $132,700 (61%) higher, and is approximately $182,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Maynard's median monthly rent is $1,176. It is roughly $135 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $114 (10%) higher, and is about $444 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
