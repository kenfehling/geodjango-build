Compared to its surrounding areas, Maynard is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home price in Maynard is $350,200. This is roughly $121,220 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $132,700 (61%) higher, and is about $182,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Maynard's median monthly rent is $1,176. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $135 (10%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $114 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $444 (27%) lower.
