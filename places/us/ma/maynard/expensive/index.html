Compared to its surrounding areas, Maynard is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maynard costs $350,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $121,220 (25%) lower.It is approximately $132,700 (61%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $182,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Maynard is $1,176. This is approximately $135 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $114 (10%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $444 (27%) lower.
