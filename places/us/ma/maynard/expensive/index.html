Compared to the surrounding areas, Maynard is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Maynard's median home cost is $350,200. It is approximately $121,220 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $132,700 (61%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $182,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Maynard is $1,176. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $135 (10%) lower. It is about $114 (10%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $444 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
