Home prices

The value of the median home in Sturbridge is $273,700. This is roughly $38,400 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $56,200 (25%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $259,000 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Sturbridge the median rent is $1,480 per month. It is roughly $438 (42%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $418 (39%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $140 (8%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
