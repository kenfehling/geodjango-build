Home prices

Sturbridge's median home value is $273,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $38,400 (16%) higher. It is about $56,200 (25%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $259,000 (48%) lower.

Rents

Sturbridge's median monthly rent is $1,480. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $438 (42%) higher. It is roughly $418 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $140 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
