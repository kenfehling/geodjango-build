Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sturbridge is $1,480. It is approximately $438 (42%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $418 (39%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $140 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
