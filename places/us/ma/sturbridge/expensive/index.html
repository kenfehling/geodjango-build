Home prices

The median home in Sturbridge costs $273,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $38,400 (16%) higher. It is roughly $56,200 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $259,000 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Sturbridge the median rent is $1,480 a month. This is approximately $438 (42%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $418 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $140 (8%) lower.
