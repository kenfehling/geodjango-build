Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Sturbridge is $1,480 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $438 (42%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $418 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $140 (8%) lower.
