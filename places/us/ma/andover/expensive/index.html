Home prices

The median home value in Andover is $564,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $182,060 (47%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $346,700 (159%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $31,500 (5%) higher.

Rents

Andover's median monthly rent is $1,006. It is approximately $368 (26%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $56 (5%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $614 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
