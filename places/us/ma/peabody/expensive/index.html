Compared to other places in this area, Peabody is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Peabody is $387,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $71,440 (15%) lower.It is roughly $169,900 (78%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $145,300 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Peabody is $1,366. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $16 (1%) lower.It is approximately $304 (28%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $254 (15%) lower.
