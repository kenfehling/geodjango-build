Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pocasset is $362,000. This is about $7,880 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $144,500 (66%) higher, and it is roughly $170,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Pocasset's median rent is $1,401 per month. This is roughly $121 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $339 (31%) higher, and it is roughly $219 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
