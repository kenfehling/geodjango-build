Home prices

Pocasset's median home price is $362,000. This is approximately $7,880 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $144,500 (66%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $170,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

Pocasset's median rent is $1,401 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $121 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $339 (31%) higher, and is approximately $219 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
