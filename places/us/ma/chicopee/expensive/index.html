Compared to other places in this area, Chicopee is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home cost in Chicopee is $182,100. It is about $45,520 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $35,400 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $45,000 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Chicopee the median rent is $923 a month. It is about $103 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $139 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is approximately $172 (15%) lower.
