Compared to other places in this area, Chicopee is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home in Chicopee is valued at $182,100. Compared to nearby areas it is about $45,520 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $35,400 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $45,000 (19%) lower.

Rents

Chicopee's median rent is $923 a month. This is roughly $103 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $139 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $172 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
