In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Westport is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in North Westport costs $293,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $30,440 (11%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $75,500 (34%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $239,700 (44%) lower.

Rents

North Westport's median monthly rent is $1,057. This is approximately $201 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is is very similar in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $563 (34%) lower.
