Home prices

The median home in North Westport is valued at $293,000. This is roughly $30,440 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $75,500 (34%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $239,700 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In North Westport the median monthly rent is $1,057. It is roughly $201 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Boston is about $563 (34%) lower.
