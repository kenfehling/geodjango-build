In comparison to other places in this area, North Westport is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in North Westport is $293,000. This is roughly $30,440 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $75,500 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $239,700 (44%) lower.

Rents

In North Westport the median rent is $1,057 per month. This is roughly $201 (23%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $563 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
