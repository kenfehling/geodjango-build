In comparison to its surrounding areas, North Westport is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

North Westport's median home value is $293,000. It is about $30,440 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $75,500 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $239,700 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Westport is $1,057. This is about $201 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and is roughly $563 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
