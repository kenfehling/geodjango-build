Compared to other places in this area, East Douglas is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in East Douglas is valued at $287,100. This is about $26,560 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $69,600 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $245,600 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in East Douglas is $948 per month. It is about $55 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $114 (10%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $672 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
