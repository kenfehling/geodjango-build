Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Saugus is $919. This is approximately $494 (34%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $143 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $701 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
