Compared to the surrounding areas, Saugus is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Saugus is valued at $394,900. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $40,080 (9%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $177,400 (81%) higher, and is approximately $137,800 (25%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Saugus is $919 per month. This is about $494 (34%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $143 (13%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $701 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
