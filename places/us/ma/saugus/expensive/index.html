Home prices

The value of the median home in Saugus is $394,900. It is about $40,080 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $177,400 (81%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $137,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Saugus is $919 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $494 (34%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $143 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $701 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
