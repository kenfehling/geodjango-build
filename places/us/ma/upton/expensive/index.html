Home prices

The median home in Upton is valued at $363,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $3,680 (1%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $146,000 (67%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $169,200 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Upton is $944 per month. This is about $353 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $118 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $676 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
