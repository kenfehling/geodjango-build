Home prices

Kingston's median home cost is $332,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $279,080 (45%) lower. It is approximately $114,800 (52%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $200,400 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Kingston the median rent is $1,185 a month. It is approximately $511 (30%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $123 (11%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $435 (26%) lower.
