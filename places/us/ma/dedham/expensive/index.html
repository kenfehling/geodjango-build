Compared to its surrounding areas, Dedham is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Bostonhowever, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dedham is $461,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $298,480 (39%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $243,700 (112%) higher, and it is roughly $71,500 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dedham is $1,669. It is roughly $285 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $607 (57%) higher, and it is roughly $49 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
