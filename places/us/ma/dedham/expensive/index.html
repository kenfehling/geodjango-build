In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dedham is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Dedham is $461,200. It is approximately $298,480 (39%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $243,700 (112%) higher, and is roughly $71,500 (13%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Dedham the median monthly rent is $1,669. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $285 (14%) lower.It is roughly $607 (57%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $49 (3%) higher.
