In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dedham is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dedham is valued at $461,200. This is approximately $298,480 (39%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $243,700 (112%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston is about $71,500 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Dedham is $1,669 a month. This is roughly $285 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $607 (57%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $49 (3%) higher.
