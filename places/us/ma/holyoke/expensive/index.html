Home prices

The median home in Holyoke costs $195,800. It is approximately $31,700 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $21,700 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester is roughly $31,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

Holyoke's median rent is $838 a month. This is roughly $61 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $224 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $257 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
