Home prices

The median home in Lenox costs $269,100. This is about $9,580 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $51,600 (23%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New Haven is about $70,100 (35%) higher.

Rents

Lenox's median rent is $837 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $139 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $225 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $359 (30%) lower compared to the city of New Haven.
