In comparison to the surrounding areas, Reading is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Reading costs $562,800. This is approximately $43,040 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $345,300 (158%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $30,100 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Reading the median monthly rent is $1,354. This is roughly $272 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $292 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $266 (16%) lower.
