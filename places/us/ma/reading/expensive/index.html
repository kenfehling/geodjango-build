Compared to other places in this area, Reading is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Reading is valued at $562,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $43,040 (8%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $345,300 (158%) higher, and is roughly $30,100 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Reading the median rent is $1,354 per month. This is roughly $272 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $292 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $266 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
