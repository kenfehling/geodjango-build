Home prices

The median home in Reading costs $562,800. This is about $43,040 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $345,300 (158%) higher, and is about $30,100 (5%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Reading the median monthly rent is $1,354. It is about $272 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $292 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $266 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
