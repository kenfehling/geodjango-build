Compared to other places in this area, Reading is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Boston, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Reading is $562,800. It is roughly $43,040 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $345,300 (158%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $30,100 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Reading the median monthly rent is $1,354. This is approximately $272 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $292 (27%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $266 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
