In comparison to the surrounding areas, Reading is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Reading costs $562,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $43,040 (8%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $345,300 (158%) higher, and it is about $30,100 (5%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Reading's median rent is $1,354 per month. It is roughly $272 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $292 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $266 (16%) lower.
