Home prices

The median home price in Abington is $357,300. This is approximately $6,740 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $139,800 (64%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $175,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

Abington's median rent is $1,219 per month. It is roughly $99 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $157 (14%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $401 (24%) lower.
