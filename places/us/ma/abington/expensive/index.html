Home prices

Abington's median home price is $357,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $6,740 (1%) higher.It is approximately $139,800 (64%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $175,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Abington the median monthly rent is $1,219. It is about $99 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $157 (14%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $401 (24%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
