Home prices

Rents

East Pepperell's median rent is $922 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $67 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $140 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $698 (43%) lower.
