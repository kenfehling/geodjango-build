Home prices

The median home price in East Pepperell is $313,200. This is roughly $15,640 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $95,700 (43%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $219,500 (41%) lower.

Rents

East Pepperell's median monthly rent is $922. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $67 (6%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $140 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $698 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
