Home prices

The price of the median home in East Pepperell is $313,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $15,640 (5%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $95,700 (43%) higher, and is about $219,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in East Pepperell is $922 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $67 (6%) lower. It is about $140 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $698 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
