Compared to its surrounding areas, Burlington is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Burlington costs $533,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $101,080 (15%) lower.It is roughly $316,300 (145%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Burlington is $1,960 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is roughly $898 (84%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $340 (20%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
