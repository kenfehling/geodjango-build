Compared to other places in this area, Burlington is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it's close to average.

Home prices

Burlington's median home value is $533,800. This is approximately $101,080 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $316,300 (145%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is is very similar.

Rents

In Burlington the median rent is $1,960 per month. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $898 (84%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $340 (20%) higher.
