Compared to its surrounding areas, Westfield is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester however, it's close to average.

Home prices

Westfield's median home value is $234,400. It is about $32,660 (16%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $16,900 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is about $7,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westfield is $969. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $45 (4%) higher. It is roughly $93 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $126 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
