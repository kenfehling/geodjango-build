Compared to other places in this area, Westfield is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Worcester, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Westfield is $234,400. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $32,660 (16%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $16,900 (7%) higher, and is approximately $7,300 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Westfield's median monthly rent is $969. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $45 (4%) higher. It is about $93 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $126 (11%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.
