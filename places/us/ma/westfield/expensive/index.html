In comparison to the surrounding areas, Westfield is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Worcesterhowever, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Westfield is valued at $234,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $32,660 (16%) higher. It is roughly $16,900 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Worcester it is approximately $7,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

Westfield's median rent is $969 a month. It is about $45 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $93 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is about $126 (11%) lower.
