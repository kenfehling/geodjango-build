Home prices

The median home value in Amesbury Town is $336,200. This is roughly $84,000 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $118,700 (54%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $196,500 (36%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Amesbury Town the median monthly rent is $1,195. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $48 (3%) lower. It is roughly $133 (12%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $425 (26%) lower.
