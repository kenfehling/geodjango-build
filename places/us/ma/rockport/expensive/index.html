In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rockport is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Rockport's median home cost is $563,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $99,220 (21%) higher. It is about $346,400 (159%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $31,200 (5%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Rockport is $1,352 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $67 (5%) higher. It is approximately $290 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $268 (16%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
