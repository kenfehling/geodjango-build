Home prices

Rents

In Clinton the median monthly rent is $1,089. It is roughly $36 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $27 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $531 (32%) lower.
