Home prices

The median home in Clinton costs $243,200. It is about $75,860 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $25,700 (11%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $289,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Clinton's median monthly rent is $1,089. This is roughly $36 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $27 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $531 (32%) lower.
