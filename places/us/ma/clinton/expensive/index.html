Home prices

The median home cost in Clinton is $243,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $75,860 (23%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $25,700 (11%) higher, and is approximately $289,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Clinton the median rent is $1,089 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $36 (3%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $27 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $531 (32%) lower.
