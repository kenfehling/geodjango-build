In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Yarmouth is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Yarmouth is valued at $292,800. This is roughly $52,280 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $75,300 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $239,900 (45%) lower.

Rents

West Yarmouth's median rent is $1,420 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $25 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $358 (33%) higher, and it is about $200 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
