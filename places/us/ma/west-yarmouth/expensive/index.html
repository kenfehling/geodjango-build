Home prices

The value of the median home in West Yarmouth is $292,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $52,280 (15%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $75,300 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $239,900 (45%) lower.

Rents
