In comparison to other places in this area, West Yarmouth is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in West Yarmouth is $292,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $52,280 (15%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $75,300 (34%) higher, and it is roughly $239,900 (45%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

West Yarmouth's median rent is $1,420 a month. It is about $25 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $358 (33%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $200 (12%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
