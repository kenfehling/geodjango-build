Compared to its surrounding areas, Brookline is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Brookline costs $933,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $179,800 (23%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $715,700 (329%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $400,500 (75%) higher.

Rents

In Brookline the median rent is $2,268 per month. It is approximately $290 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,206 (113%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $648 (39%) higher.
