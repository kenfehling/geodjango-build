Home prices

Brookline's median home value is $933,200. It is roughly $179,800 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $715,700 (329%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $400,500 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Brookline the median rent is $2,268 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $290 (14%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $1,206 (113%) higher, and is approximately $648 (39%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
