In comparison to the surrounding areas, Brookline is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Brookline is $933,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $179,800 (23%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $715,700 (329%) higher, and is roughly $400,500 (75%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Brookline is $2,268 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $290 (14%) higher.It is roughly $1,206 (113%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $648 (39%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
