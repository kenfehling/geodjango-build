Compared to its surrounding areas, Brookline is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brookline is valued at $933,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $179,800 (23%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $715,700 (329%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $400,500 (75%) higher.

Rents

In Brookline the median monthly rent is $2,268. This is about $290 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $1,206 (113%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $648 (39%) higher.
