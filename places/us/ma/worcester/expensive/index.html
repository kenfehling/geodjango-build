Compared to other places in this area, Worcester is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Bostonhowever, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Worcester is $227,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $94,260 (29%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $9,600 (4%) higher, and it is about $305,600 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Worcester is $1,095. It is roughly $19 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $525 (32%) lower.
