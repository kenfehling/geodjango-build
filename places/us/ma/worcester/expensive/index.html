In comparison to the surrounding areas, Worcester is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Worcester is valued at $227,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $94,260 (29%) lower.It is roughly $9,600 (4%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $305,600 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Worcester is $1,095 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $19 (1%) higher.It is about $33 (3%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $525 (32%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
