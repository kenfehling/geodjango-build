In comparison to other places in this area, Worcester is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Worcester is valued at $227,100. It is roughly $94,260 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $9,600 (4%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $305,600 (57%) lower.

Rents

Worcester's median rent is $1,095 per month. This is approximately $19 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $33 (3%) higher, and is roughly $525 (32%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
