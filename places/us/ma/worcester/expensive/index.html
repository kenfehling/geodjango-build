Compared to other places in this area, Worcester is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Worcester is valued at $227,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $94,260 (29%) lower.It is approximately $9,600 (4%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $305,600 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Worcester's median monthly rent is $1,095. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $19 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $33 (3%) higher, and is about $525 (32%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
