Home prices

The median home in Springfield is valued at $156,200. This is roughly $104,140 (40%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $61,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $70,900 (31%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Springfield's median monthly rent is $877. This is about $129 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $185 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $218 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
