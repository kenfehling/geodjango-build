Home prices

The median home price in Lexington is $889,700. It is roughly $186,520 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $672,200 (309%) higher, and it is roughly $357,000 (67%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Lexington is $2,475 a month. It is approximately $611 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $1,413 (133%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $855 (52%) higher.
