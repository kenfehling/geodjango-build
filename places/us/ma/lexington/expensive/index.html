In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lexington is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lexington costs $889,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $186,520 (26%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $672,200 (309%) higher, and it is approximately $357,000 (67%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Lexington's median rent is $2,475 a month. It is approximately $611 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $1,413 (133%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston is about $855 (52%) higher.
