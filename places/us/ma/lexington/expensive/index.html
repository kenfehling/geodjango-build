Home prices

The median home in Lexington costs $889,700. This is about $186,520 (26%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $672,200 (309%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $357,000 (67%) higher.

Rents

In Lexington the median rent is $2,475 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $611 (32%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $1,413 (133%) higher, and is approximately $855 (52%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
