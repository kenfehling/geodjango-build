Compared to the surrounding areas, Northampton is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home in Northampton is valued at $332,100. This is about $50,200 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $114,600 (52%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $105,000 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

The median rent in Northampton is $1,158 per month. It is roughly $58 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $96 (9%) higher, and it is about $63 (5%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
