Home prices

The median home cost in Northampton is $332,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $50,200 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $114,600 (52%) higher, and is roughly $105,000 (46%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Northampton's median rent is $1,158 per month. This is about $58 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $96 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is approximately $63 (5%) higher.
