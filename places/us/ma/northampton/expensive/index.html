Home prices

Northampton's median home price is $332,100. This is approximately $50,200 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $114,600 (52%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is about $105,000 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Northampton the median monthly rent is $1,158. It is roughly $58 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $96 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is about $63 (5%) higher.
