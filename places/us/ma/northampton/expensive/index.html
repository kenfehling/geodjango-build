Home prices

Northampton's median home cost is $332,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $50,200 (17%) higher.It is approximately $114,600 (52%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is roughly $105,000 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Northampton the median rent is $1,158 per month. This is approximately $58 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $96 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester is roughly $63 (5%) higher.
