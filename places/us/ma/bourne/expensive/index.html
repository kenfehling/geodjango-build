In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bourne is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bourne is $374,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $63,480 (20%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $157,000 (72%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $158,200 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bourne is $873 per month. It is about $411 (31%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $189 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $747 (46%) lower.
