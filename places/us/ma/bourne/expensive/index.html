Compared to the surrounding areas, Bourne is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Bourne costs $374,500. This is roughly $63,480 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $157,000 (72%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $158,200 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bourne is $873. This is approximately $411 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $189 (17%) lower, and is approximately $747 (46%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
