Compared to other places in this area, Bourne is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Bourne's median home price is $374,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $63,480 (20%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $157,000 (72%) higher, and it is approximately $158,200 (29%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Bourne is $873 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $411 (31%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $189 (17%) lower, and is roughly $747 (46%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
