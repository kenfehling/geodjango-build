In comparison to other places in this area, Bourne is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bourne is valued at $374,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $63,480 (20%) higher. It is roughly $157,000 (72%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $158,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Bourne's median rent is $873 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $411 (31%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $189 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $747 (46%) lower.
