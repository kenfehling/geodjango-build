Home prices

The median home in Bourne is valued at $374,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $63,480 (20%) higher.It is roughly $157,000 (72%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $158,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Bourne's median monthly rent is $873. This is roughly $411 (31%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $189 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $747 (46%) lower.
