Home prices

The median home in Hopkinton costs $497,400. This is roughly $146,320 (41%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $279,900 (128%) higher, and it is about $35,300 (6%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Hopkinton the median monthly rent is $1,653. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $417 (33%) higher. It is about $591 (55%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $33 (2%) higher.
