Rents

In North Falmouth the median rent is $1,406 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $80 (6%) higher. It is roughly $344 (32%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $214 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
