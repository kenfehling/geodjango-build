Home prices

The median home in Foxborough is valued at $356,800. This is approximately $45,520 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $139,300 (64%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $175,900 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Foxborough the median monthly rent is $1,321. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $259 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $299 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
