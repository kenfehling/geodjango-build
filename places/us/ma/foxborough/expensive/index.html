Compared to its surrounding areas, Foxborough is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home price in Foxborough is $356,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $45,520 (11%) lower.It is approximately $139,300 (64%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $175,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Foxborough the median monthly rent is $1,321. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar.It is roughly $259 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $299 (18%) lower.
