Compared to the surrounding areas, Foxborough is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Foxborough costs $356,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $45,520 (11%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $139,300 (64%) higher, and it is roughly $175,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Foxborough the median rent is $1,321 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar. It is about $259 (24%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $299 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
