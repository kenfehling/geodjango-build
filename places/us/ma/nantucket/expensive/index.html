In comparison to the surrounding areas, Nantucket is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Fall River, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nantucket is valued at $1,001,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $588,760 (142%) higher.It is about $784,200 (360%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $759,500 (313%) higher in comparison to the city of Fall River.

Rents

Nantucket's median rent is $1,769 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $466 (35%) higher.It is approximately $707 (66%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $965 (120%) higher compared to the city of Fall River.
