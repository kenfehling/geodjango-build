Home prices

The median home in Nantucket is valued at $1,001,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $588,760 (142%) higher.It is roughly $784,200 (360%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $759,500 (313%) higher in comparison to the city of Fall River.

Rents

The median rent in Nantucket is $1,769 per month. It is roughly $466 (35%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $707 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fall River it is about $965 (120%) higher.
