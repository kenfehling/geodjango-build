Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Nantucket is $1,769 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $466 (35%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $707 (66%) higher, and it is approximately $965 (120%) higher in comparison to the city of Fall River.
