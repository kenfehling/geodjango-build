Home prices

The median home in Groton is valued at $379,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $72,400 (23%) higher. It is about $162,200 (74%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $153,000 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Groton the median monthly rent is $900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $40 (4%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $162 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $720 (44%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
