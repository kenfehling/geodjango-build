Home prices

Rents

In South Dennis the median monthly rent is $1,374. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $64 (4%) higher.It is about $312 (29%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $246 (15%) lower.
