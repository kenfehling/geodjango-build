In comparison to its surrounding areas, North Lakeville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in North Lakeville is $340,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $47,840 (16%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $122,900 (56%) higher, and is about $192,300 (36%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In North Lakeville the median rent is $1,580 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $188 (13%) higher. It is about $518 (48%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $40 (2%) lower.
