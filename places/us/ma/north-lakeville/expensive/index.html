Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Lakeville is $1,580. This is approximately $188 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $518 (48%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $40 (2%) lower.
