Home prices

The value of the median home in North Lakeville is $340,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $47,840 (16%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $122,900 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $192,300 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in North Lakeville is $1,580 per month. It is approximately $188 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $518 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $40 (2%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
