Compared to nearby areas, Fitchburg is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fitchburg costs $196,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $50,740 (20%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $20,900 (9%) lower, and is roughly $336,100 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fitchburg is $1,016. It is roughly $143 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $46 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $604 (37%) lower.
