In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fitchburg is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fitchburg costs $196,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $50,740 (20%) lower. It is about $20,900 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $336,100 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fitchburg is $1,016. It is approximately $143 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $46 (4%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $604 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
