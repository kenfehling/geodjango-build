In comparison to other places in this area, Fitchburg is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fitchburg is $196,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $50,740 (20%) lower.It is roughly $20,900 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $336,100 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Fitchburg's median monthly rent is $1,016. This is roughly $143 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $46 (4%) lower, and is roughly $604 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
