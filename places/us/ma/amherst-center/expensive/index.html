Home prices

The cost of the median home in Amherst Center is $350,600. This is about $36,300 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $133,100 (61%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester is about $123,500 (54%) higher.

Rents

In Amherst Center the median monthly rent is $1,297. It is approximately $171 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $235 (22%) higher, and is approximately $202 (18%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
