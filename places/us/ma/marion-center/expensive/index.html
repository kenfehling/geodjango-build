Home prices

The median home value in Marion Center is $608,800. This is roughly $302,600 (98%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $391,300 (179%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $76,100 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Marion Center the median rent is $1,500 a month. It is roughly $282 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $438 (41%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $120 (7%) lower.
