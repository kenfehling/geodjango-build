Compared to the surrounding areas, Northborough is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Northborough is $414,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $107,720 (35%) higher.It is roughly $196,600 (90%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $118,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Northborough's median rent is $1,176 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $46 (3%) lower.It is about $114 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $444 (27%) lower.
