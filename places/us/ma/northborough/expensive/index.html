Compared to nearby areas, Northborough is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Northborough costs $414,100. It is roughly $107,720 (35%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $196,600 (90%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $118,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Northborough the median monthly rent is $1,176. It is roughly $46 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $114 (10%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $444 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
