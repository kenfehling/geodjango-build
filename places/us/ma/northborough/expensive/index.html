Rents

The median monthly rent in Northborough is $1,176. This is approximately $46 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $114 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $444 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
