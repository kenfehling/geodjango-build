In comparison to nearby areas, Northborough is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Northborough is valued at $414,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $107,720 (35%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $196,600 (90%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $118,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Northborough is $1,176 a month. This is approximately $46 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $114 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $444 (27%) lower.
