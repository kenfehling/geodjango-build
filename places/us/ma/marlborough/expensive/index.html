Home prices

The median home cost in Marlborough is $348,500. It is about $28,860 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $131,000 (60%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $184,200 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Marlborough the median rent is $1,482 a month. This is approximately $236 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $420 (39%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $138 (8%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
