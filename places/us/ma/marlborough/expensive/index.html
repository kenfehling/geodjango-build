In comparison to the surrounding areas, Marlborough is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Marlborough is valued at $348,500. This is about $28,860 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $131,000 (60%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $184,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marlborough is $1,482 a month. This is approximately $236 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $420 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $138 (8%) lower.
