Compared to the surrounding areas, Marlborough is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marlborough costs $348,500. This is approximately $28,860 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $131,000 (60%) higher, and it is roughly $184,200 (34%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marlborough is $1,482. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $236 (18%) higher.It is approximately $420 (39%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $138 (8%) lower.
