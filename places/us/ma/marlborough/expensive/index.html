In comparison to its nearby areas, Marlborough is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Marlborough is $348,500. This is about $28,860 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $131,000 (60%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $184,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Marlborough the median monthly rent is $1,482. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $236 (18%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $420 (39%) higher, and it is approximately $138 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
