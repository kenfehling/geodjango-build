Home prices

The median home in Webster costs $202,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $6,940 (3%) lower. It is about $14,600 (6%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $329,800 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Webster is $909 a month. This is approximately $56 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $153 (14%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $711 (43%) lower.
