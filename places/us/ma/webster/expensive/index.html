Home prices

The median home in Webster costs $202,900. It is about $6,940 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $14,600 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $329,800 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Webster's median monthly rent is $909. It is roughly $56 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $153 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $711 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
