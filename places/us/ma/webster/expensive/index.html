Home prices

The median home in Webster is valued at $202,900. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $6,940 (3%) lower. It is about $14,600 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $329,800 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Webster is $909 per month. It is approximately $56 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $153 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $711 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
