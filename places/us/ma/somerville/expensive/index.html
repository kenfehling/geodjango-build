Compared to its nearby areas, Somerville is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Boston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Somerville's median home cost is $664,700. It is about $179,180 (36%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $447,200 (205%) higher, and is approximately $132,000 (24%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Somerville the median monthly rent is $1,874. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $176 (10%) higher.It is roughly $812 (76%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $254 (15%) higher.
