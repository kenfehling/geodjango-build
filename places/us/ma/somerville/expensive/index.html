In comparison to its nearby areas, Somerville is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Somerville costs $664,700. It is approximately $179,180 (36%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $447,200 (205%) higher, and is about $132,000 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Somerville's median rent is $1,874 per month. This is about $176 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $812 (76%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $254 (15%) higher.
