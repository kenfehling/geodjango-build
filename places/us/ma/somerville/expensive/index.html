Compared to other places in this area, Somerville is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Boston, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Somerville costs $664,700. It is approximately $179,180 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $447,200 (205%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $132,000 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Somerville's median rent is $1,874 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $176 (10%) higher.It is approximately $812 (76%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $254 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
