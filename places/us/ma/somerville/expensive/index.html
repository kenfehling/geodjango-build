Compared to the surrounding areas, Somerville is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Boston, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Somerville's median home cost is $664,700. It is roughly $179,180 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $447,200 (205%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $132,000 (24%) higher.

Rents

In Somerville the median rent is $1,874 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $176 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $812 (76%) higher, and is roughly $254 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.
