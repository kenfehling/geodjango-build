Compared to other places in this area, Somerville is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Somerville is $664,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $179,180 (36%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $447,200 (205%) higher, and is roughly $132,000 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Somerville the median monthly rent is $1,874. It is roughly $176 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $812 (76%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $254 (15%) higher compared to the city of Boston.
