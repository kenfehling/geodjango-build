Home prices

The median home cost in Housatonic is $275,000. This is about $21,720 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $57,500 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New Haven is roughly $76,000 (38%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Housatonic is $1,269 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $390 (44%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $207 (19%) higher, and it is about $73 (6%) higher compared to the city of New Haven.
