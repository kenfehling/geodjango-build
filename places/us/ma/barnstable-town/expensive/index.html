Rents

The median monthly rent in Barnstable Town is $1,336. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $115 (7%) lower. It is approximately $274 (25%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $284 (17%) lower.
