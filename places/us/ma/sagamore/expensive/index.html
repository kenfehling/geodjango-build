Home prices

Sagamore's median home cost is $412,400. It is roughly $70,100 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $194,900 (89%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $120,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Sagamore the median monthly rent is $1,341. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $196 (17%) higher. It is about $279 (26%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $279 (17%) lower.
