Home prices

The median home in Sagamore is valued at $412,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $70,100 (20%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $194,900 (89%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $120,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sagamore is $1,341. This is approximately $196 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $279 (26%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $279 (17%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
