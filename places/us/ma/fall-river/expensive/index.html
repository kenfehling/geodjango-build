Home prices

Fall River's median home value is $242,200. This is approximately $31,600 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $24,700 (11%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $290,500 (54%) lower.

Rents

Fall River's median rent is $804 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $194 (19%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $258 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $816 (50%) lower.
