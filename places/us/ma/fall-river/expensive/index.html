Compared to nearby areas, Fall River is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Fall River's median home cost is $242,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $31,600 (11%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $24,700 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $290,500 (54%) lower.

Rents

Fall River's median rent is $804 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $194 (19%) lower. It is approximately $258 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $816 (50%) lower.
