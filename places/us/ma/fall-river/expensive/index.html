Home prices

The median home in Fall River costs $242,200. It is approximately $31,600 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $24,700 (11%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $290,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Fall River is $804 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $194 (19%) lower. It is roughly $258 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $816 (50%) lower.
