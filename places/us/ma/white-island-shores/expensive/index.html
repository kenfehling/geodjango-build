Home prices

Rents

White Island Shores's median monthly rent is $895. It is approximately $342 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $167 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $725 (44%) lower.
