In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lynnfield is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lynnfield costs $670,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $215,040 (47%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $453,200 (208%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $138,000 (25%) higher.

Rents

Lynnfield's median monthly rent is $1,669. This is roughly $389 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $607 (57%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $49 (3%) higher.
