In comparison to other places in this area, Lynnfield is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lynnfield is $670,700. It is approximately $215,040 (47%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $453,200 (208%) higher, and it is about $138,000 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Lynnfield the median monthly rent is $1,669. It is approximately $389 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $607 (57%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $49 (3%) higher.
