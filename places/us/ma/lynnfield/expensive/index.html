Compared to other places in this area, Lynnfield is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Boston, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lynnfield costs $670,700. This is about $215,040 (47%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $453,200 (208%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $138,000 (25%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lynnfield is $1,669 per month. This is roughly $389 (30%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $607 (57%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $49 (3%) higher.
