In comparison to its surrounding areas, Vineyard Haven is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Vineyard Haven costs $577,500. It is about $81,540 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $360,000 (165%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $44,800 (8%) higher compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Vineyard Haven's median monthly rent is $1,387. It is roughly $37 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $325 (30%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $233 (14%) lower.
