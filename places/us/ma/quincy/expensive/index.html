In comparison to the surrounding areas, Quincy is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Quincy is $428,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $105,740 (19%) lower. It is approximately $211,100 (97%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $104,100 (19%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Quincy the median monthly rent is $1,501. Compared to other places in this area this is about $85 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $439 (41%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $119 (7%) lower.
