Home prices

The median home in North Plymouth is valued at $324,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $324,280 (49%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $106,800 (49%) higher, and it is roughly $208,400 (39%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

North Plymouth's median monthly rent is $1,385. This is approximately $356 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $323 (30%) higher, and is about $235 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
