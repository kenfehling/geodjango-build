Compared to its nearby areas, Brockton is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Brockton is $264,800. This is roughly $104,040 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $47,300 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $267,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Brockton the median monthly rent is $1,111. This is roughly $239 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $49 (4%) higher, and is about $509 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
