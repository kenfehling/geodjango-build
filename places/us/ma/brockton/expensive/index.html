In comparison to other places in this area, Brockton is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Brockton's median home price is $264,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $104,040 (28%) lower.It is about $47,300 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $267,900 (50%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Brockton is $1,111 per month. This is roughly $239 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $49 (4%) higher, and it is approximately $509 (31%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
