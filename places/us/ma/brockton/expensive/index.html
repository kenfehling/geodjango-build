Compared to other places in this area, Brockton is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Brockton is valued at $264,800. It is about $104,040 (28%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $47,300 (21%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $267,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Brockton the median rent is $1,111 a month. This is roughly $239 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $49 (4%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $509 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
