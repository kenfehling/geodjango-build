In comparison to nearby areas, Brockton is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Brockton costs $264,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $104,040 (28%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $47,300 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $267,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Brockton the median monthly rent is $1,111. It is roughly $239 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $49 (4%) higher, and it is roughly $509 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
