Home prices

Brockton's median home value is $264,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $104,040 (28%) lower.It is about $47,300 (21%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $267,900 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brockton is $1,111. Compared to nearby areas this is about $239 (17%) lower.It is about $49 (4%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $509 (31%) lower.
