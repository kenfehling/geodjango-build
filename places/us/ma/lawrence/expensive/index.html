In comparison to other places in this area, Lawrence is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lawrence is $271,100. It is roughly $117,220 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $53,600 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $261,600 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Lawrence the median rent is $1,145 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $172 (13%) lower. It is roughly $83 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $475 (29%) lower.
