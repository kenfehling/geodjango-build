In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lawrence is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lawrence is $271,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $117,220 (30%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $53,600 (24%) higher, and is approximately $261,600 (49%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lawrence is $1,145. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $172 (13%) lower. It is approximately $83 (7%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $475 (29%) lower.
