In comparison to nearby areas, Lawrence is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home value in Lawrence is $271,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $117,220 (30%) lower.It is about $53,600 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $261,600 (49%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lawrence is $1,145. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $172 (13%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $83 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $475 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
