Compared to other places in this area, Lawrence is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Lawrence is $271,100. This is about $117,220 (30%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $53,600 (24%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $261,600 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lawrence is $1,145. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $172 (13%) lower. It is roughly $83 (7%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $475 (29%) lower.
