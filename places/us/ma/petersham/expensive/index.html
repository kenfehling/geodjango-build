The median home in Petersham costs $289,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $110,000 (61%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $71,800 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $243,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

Petersham's median monthly rent is $1,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $434 (50%) higher. It is roughly $238 (22%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $320 (19%) lower.
