Home prices

The median home cost in North Amherst is $390,400. This is about $66,700 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $172,900 (79%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Worcester is approximately $163,300 (71%) higher.

Rents

North Amherst's median monthly rent is $1,367. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $202 (17%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $305 (28%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester it is approximately $272 (24%) higher.
