In comparison to other places in this area, Salem is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Boston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Salem is valued at $367,200. It is roughly $92,540 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $149,700 (68%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $165,500 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Salem is $1,289. This is about $177 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $227 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $331 (20%) lower.
