Home prices

The median home price in Salem is $367,200. It is approximately $92,540 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $149,700 (68%) higher, and is about $165,500 (31%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Salem the median monthly rent is $1,289. It is approximately $177 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $227 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $331 (20%) lower.
