Home prices

The median home in Salem is valued at $367,200. It is roughly $92,540 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $149,700 (68%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $165,500 (31%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Salem is $1,289 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $177 (12%) lower. It is about $227 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $331 (20%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
