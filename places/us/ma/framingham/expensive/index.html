Home prices

Framingham's median home cost is $409,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $189,360 (31%) lower. It is roughly $191,900 (88%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $123,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

Framingham's median monthly rent is $1,434. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $27 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $372 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $186 (11%) lower.
