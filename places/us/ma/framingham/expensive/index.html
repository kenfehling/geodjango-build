Home prices

The median home cost in Framingham is $409,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $189,360 (31%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $191,900 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $123,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

Framingham's median rent is $1,434 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $372 (35%) higher, and is roughly $186 (11%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
