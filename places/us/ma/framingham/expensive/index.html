In comparison to its surrounding areas, Framingham is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Framingham's median home cost is $409,400. It is approximately $189,360 (31%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $191,900 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $123,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

Framingham's median monthly rent is $1,434. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $27 (1%) higher. It is roughly $372 (35%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $186 (11%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
