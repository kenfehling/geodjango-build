Compared to the surrounding areas, Framingham is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home cost in Framingham is $409,400. It is roughly $189,360 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $191,900 (88%) higher, and it is roughly $123,300 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Framingham's median monthly rent is $1,434. This is approximately $27 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $372 (35%) higher, and it is approximately $186 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
