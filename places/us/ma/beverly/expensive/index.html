Home prices

The median home in Beverly is valued at $437,300. Compared to nearby areas it is about $29,280 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $219,800 (101%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $95,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

Beverly's median monthly rent is $1,282. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $227 (15%) lower. It is approximately $220 (20%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $338 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
