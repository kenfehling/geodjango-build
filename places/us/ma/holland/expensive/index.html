In comparison to its surrounding areas, Holland is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Holland is $220,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $12,260 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $2,900 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $312,300 (58%) lower.

Rents

Holland's median rent is $1,280 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $213 (19%) higher.It is about $218 (20%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $340 (20%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
