Home prices

Rents

In Middleborough Center the median rent is $1,277 a month. It is about $176 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $215 (20%) higher, and it is approximately $343 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
