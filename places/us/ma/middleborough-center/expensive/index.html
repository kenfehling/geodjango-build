Home prices

The value of the median home in Middleborough Center is $294,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $6,880 (2%) lower. It is about $77,300 (35%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $237,900 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Middleborough Center the median monthly rent is $1,277. This is approximately $176 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $215 (20%) higher, and is approximately $343 (21%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
