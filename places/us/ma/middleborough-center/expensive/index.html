Home prices

Rents

In Middleborough Center the median monthly rent is $1,277. This is approximately $176 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $215 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $343 (21%) lower.
