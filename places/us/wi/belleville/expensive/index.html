The median home in Belleville is valued at $190,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $18,360 (8%) lower. It is roughly $26,800 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $55,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents
