Home prices

Lancaster's median home price is $134,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,640 (22%) higher. It is roughly $82,600 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Madison is approximately $111,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lancaster is $627. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $47 (8%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $435 (40%) lower, and is about $491 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
