In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lancaster is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $134,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $24,640 (22%) higher.It is about $82,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $111,400 (45%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

Lancaster's median monthly rent is $627. It is roughly $47 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $435 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is roughly $491 (43%) lower.
