Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $627. This is approximately $47 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $435 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is approximately $491 (43%) lower.
