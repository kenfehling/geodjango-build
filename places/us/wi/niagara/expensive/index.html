Home prices

The median home in Niagara costs $74,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $11,180 (13%) lower. It is approximately $142,800 (65%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $61,200 (45%) lower compared to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

Niagara's median rent is $684 per month. This is about $68 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $378 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay is about $46 (6%) lower.
