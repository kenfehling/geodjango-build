Home prices

The value of the median home in Nashotah is $367,200. This is about $399,100 (52%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $149,700 (68%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $109,200 (42%) higher.

Rents

Nashotah's median monthly rent is $1,193. It is approximately $70 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $131 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $81 (7%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
