In comparison to its surrounding areas, Menasha is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Menasha is $131,100. It is roughly $32,440 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $86,400 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $9,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Menasha the median monthly rent is $759. It is roughly $87 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $303 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $99 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
