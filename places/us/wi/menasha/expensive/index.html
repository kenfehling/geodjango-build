Home prices

Menasha's median home cost is $131,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $32,440 (19%) lower.It is approximately $86,400 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $9,000 (7%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Menasha the median rent is $759 per month. This is about $87 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $303 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $99 (11%) lower.
