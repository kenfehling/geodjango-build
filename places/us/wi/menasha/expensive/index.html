In comparison to nearby areas, Menasha is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Menasha is valued at $131,100. It is roughly $32,440 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $86,400 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $9,000 (7%) higher.

Rents

Menasha's median monthly rent is $759. It is approximately $87 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $303 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $99 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
