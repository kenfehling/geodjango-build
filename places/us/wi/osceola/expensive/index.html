Home prices

The value of the median home in Osceola is $149,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $22,900 (13%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $68,000 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $102,100 (40%) lower.

Rents

Osceola's median monthly rent is $822. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $32 (3%) lower.It is about $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $205 (19%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
