Compared to other places in this area, Osceola is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Osceola is $149,500. It is roughly $22,900 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $68,000 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $102,100 (40%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Osceola the median rent is $822 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $32 (3%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $240 (22%) lower, and is roughly $205 (19%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
