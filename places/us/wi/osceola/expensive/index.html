Home prices

The median home in Osceola costs $149,500. This is about $22,900 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $68,000 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $102,100 (40%) lower.

Rents

Osceola's median monthly rent is $822. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $32 (3%) lower.It is approximately $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $205 (19%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
