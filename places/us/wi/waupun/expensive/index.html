Home prices

The median home value in Waupun is $120,600. This is about $14,800 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $96,900 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $1,500 (1%) lower.

Rents

Waupun's median rent is $701 per month. This is approximately $11 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $361 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $157 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
