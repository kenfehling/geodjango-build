Home prices

The median home cost in Rosendale is $157,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $39,820 (33%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $35,500 (29%) higher.

Rents

Rosendale's median monthly rent is $587. It is about $141 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $475 (44%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $271 (31%) lower.
