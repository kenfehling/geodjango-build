In comparison to other places in this area, Rosendale is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rosendale is valued at $157,600. This is roughly $39,820 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $35,500 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Rosendale the median rent is $587 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $141 (19%) lower. It is roughly $475 (44%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $271 (31%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
