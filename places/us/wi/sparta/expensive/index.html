Compared to nearby areas, Sparta is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Eau Claire, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sparta is $123,300. It is approximately $9,860 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $94,200 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $31,700 (20%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

Sparta's median monthly rent is $827. This is about $83 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $235 (22%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Eau Claire.
