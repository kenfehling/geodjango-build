Compared to the surrounding areas, Sparta is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Home prices

The median home in Sparta is valued at $123,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $9,860 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $94,200 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire it is approximately $31,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

Sparta's median rent is $827 per month. It is roughly $83 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $235 (22%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Eau Claire.
