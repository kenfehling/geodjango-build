Home prices

The median home in Sparta costs $123,300. It is approximately $9,860 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $94,200 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $31,700 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Sparta the median monthly rent is $827. This is roughly $83 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $235 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is is very similar.
