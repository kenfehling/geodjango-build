Home prices

The value of the median home in Ferryville is $168,100. This is roughly $45,960 (37%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $49,400 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of La Crosse is approximately $25,600 (17%) higher.

Rents

Ferryville's median monthly rent is $542. It is about $141 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $520 (48%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of La Crosse it is roughly $251 (31%) lower.
