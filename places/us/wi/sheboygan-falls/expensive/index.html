In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sheboygan Falls is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Sheboygan Falls is valued at $146,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $17,440 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $70,600 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $24,800 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sheboygan Falls is $764 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $122 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $298 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $94 (10%) lower.
