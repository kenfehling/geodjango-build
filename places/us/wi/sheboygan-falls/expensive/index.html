Compared to other places in this area, Sheboygan Falls is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sheboygan Falls is $146,900. This is approximately $17,440 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $70,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $24,800 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Sheboygan Falls the median monthly rent is $764. This is about $122 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $298 (28%) lower, and is roughly $94 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
