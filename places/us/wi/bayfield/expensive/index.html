Home prices

The median home in Bayfield is valued at $172,700. This is roughly $69,080 (66%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $44,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $9,400 (5%) higher compared to the city of Duluth.

Rents

In Bayfield the median monthly rent is $668. This is approximately $80 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $394 (37%) lower, and is roughly $152 (18%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
