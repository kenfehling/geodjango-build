Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bayfield is $172,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $69,080 (66%) higher.It is approximately $44,800 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Duluth is roughly $9,400 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bayfield is $668. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $80 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $394 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Duluth is approximately $152 (18%) lower.
