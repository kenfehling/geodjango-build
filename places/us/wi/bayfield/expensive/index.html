Home prices

Rents

In Bayfield the median rent is $668 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $80 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $394 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $152 (18%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
