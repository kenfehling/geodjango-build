Home prices

The median home in West Baraboo is valued at $158,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $52,940 (50%) higher. It is approximately $59,200 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $88,000 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Baraboo is $880. It is approximately $140 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $182 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is approximately $238 (21%) lower.
