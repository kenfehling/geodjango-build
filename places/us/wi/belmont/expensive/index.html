Home prices

The cost of the median home in Belmont is $152,100. This is roughly $23,120 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $65,400 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is approximately $94,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

Belmont's median rent is $732 a month. It is roughly $42 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $330 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is approximately $386 (34%) lower.
