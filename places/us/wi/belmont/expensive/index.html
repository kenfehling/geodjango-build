The cost of the median home in Belmont is $152,100. This is roughly $23,120 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $65,400 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is approximately $94,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

Belmont's median monthly rent is $732. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $42 (6%) higher. It is about $330 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $386 (34%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
