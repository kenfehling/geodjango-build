Home prices

The median home in Belmont costs $152,100. This is roughly $23,120 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $65,400 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Madison it is roughly $94,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Belmont is $732 a month. It is about $42 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $330 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Madison is roughly $386 (34%) lower.
