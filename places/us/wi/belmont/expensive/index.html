Home prices

The median home in Belmont costs $152,100. It is roughly $23,120 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $65,400 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $94,200 (38%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Belmont is $732 a month. This is about $42 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $330 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $386 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
