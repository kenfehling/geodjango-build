Rents

The median monthly rent in Deerfield is $846. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $162 (16%) lower. It is roughly $216 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $12 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
