Home prices

The value of the median home in Athens is $121,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $6,320 (5%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $95,900 (44%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

Athens's median monthly rent is $530. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $138 (20%) lower. It is about $532 (50%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $178 (25%) lower compared to the city of Wausau.
