Home prices

The median home in Hixton is valued at $109,800. This is approximately $4,020 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $107,700 (49%) lower, and is about $45,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

Hixton's median monthly rent is $496. This is approximately $151 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $566 (53%) lower, and it is about $339 (40%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.
