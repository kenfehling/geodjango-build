In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wisconsin Dells is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home in Wisconsin Dells costs $161,400. This is approximately $27,980 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $56,100 (25%) lower, and is about $84,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Wisconsin Dells the median rent is $833 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $44 (5%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $229 (21%) lower, and it is about $285 (25%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
