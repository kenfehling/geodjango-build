Compared to its nearby areas, Wisconsin Dells is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home in Wisconsin Dells is valued at $161,400. It is about $27,980 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $56,100 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $84,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Wisconsin Dells is $833 per month. This is approximately $44 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $285 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
