In comparison to other places in this area, Wisconsin Dells is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Madison, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Wisconsin Dells is $161,400. This is roughly $27,980 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $56,100 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $84,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Wisconsin Dells the median monthly rent is $833. This is approximately $44 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is about $285 (25%) lower.
