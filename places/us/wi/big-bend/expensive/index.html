The cost of the median home in Big Bend is $223,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $47,820 (17%) lower.It is about $5,600 (2%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $34,900 (13%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Big Bend's median rent is $1,110 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16 (1%) higher.It is roughly $48 (4%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is is very similar.
