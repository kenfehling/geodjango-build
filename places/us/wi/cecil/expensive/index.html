Home prices

The median home price in Cecil is $138,000. It is about $46,200 (50%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $79,500 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $2,100 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

In Cecil the median monthly rent is $782. This is about $209 (36%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $280 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay is approximately $52 (7%) higher.
