Home prices

The median home in Colfax costs $115,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $21,180 (15%) lower. It is approximately $101,800 (46%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Paul is approximately $92,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

Colfax's median monthly rent is $639. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $127 (16%) lower. It is about $423 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Paul it is approximately $329 (33%) lower.
