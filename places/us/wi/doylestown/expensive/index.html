Home prices

The median home price in Doylestown is $97,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $52,360 (34%) lower. It is roughly $119,700 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $24,300 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Doylestown is $925. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $54 (6%) higher. It is roughly $137 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $67 (7%) higher.
