Compared to the surrounding areas, Doylestown is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Doylestown's median home cost is $97,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $52,360 (34%) lower. It is roughly $119,700 (55%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $24,300 (19%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Doylestown is $925. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $54 (6%) higher. It is about $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $67 (7%) higher.
