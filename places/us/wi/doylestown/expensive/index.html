Home prices

The median home in Doylestown is valued at $97,800. It is roughly $52,360 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $119,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $24,300 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Doylestown is $925 a month. This is roughly $54 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $137 (12%) lower, and is about $67 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
