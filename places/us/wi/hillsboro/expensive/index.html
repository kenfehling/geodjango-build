Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro costs $107,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $16,220 (17%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $110,400 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $139,200 (56%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $605 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $48 (7%) lower. It is approximately $457 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $513 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
