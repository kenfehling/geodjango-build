Home prices

The median home in St. Croix Falls is valued at $156,100. Compared to nearby areas it is about $11,060 (7%) higher. It is about $61,400 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $95,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

In St. Croix Falls the median monthly rent is $738. This is roughly $64 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $324 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $289 (28%) lower.
