Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Croix Falls is $738. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $64 (8%) lower.It is approximately $324 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $289 (28%) lower.
