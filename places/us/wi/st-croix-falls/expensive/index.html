Home prices

The median home in St. Croix Falls is valued at $156,100. It is roughly $11,060 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $61,400 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $95,500 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In St. Croix Falls the median monthly rent is $738. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $64 (8%) lower. It is roughly $324 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $289 (28%) lower.
