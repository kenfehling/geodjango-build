In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cumberland is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Cumberland is valued at $123,500. This is roughly $24,700 (25%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $94,000 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $128,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Cumberland is $628 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $39 (5%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $434 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $399 (38%) lower.
