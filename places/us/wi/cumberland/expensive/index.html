Home prices

The median home in Cumberland costs $123,500. It is approximately $24,700 (25%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $94,000 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $128,100 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cumberland is $628 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $39 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $434 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $399 (38%) lower.
