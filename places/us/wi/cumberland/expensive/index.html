Home prices

The median home value in Cumberland is $123,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24,700 (25%) higher.It is approximately $94,000 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $128,100 (50%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cumberland is $628. It is about $39 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $434 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $399 (38%) lower.
