Home prices

The median home in Baraboo costs $152,600. It is about $28,420 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $64,900 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Madison is about $93,700 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Baraboo the median monthly rent is $801. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $16 (1%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $261 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is approximately $317 (28%) lower.
