In comparison to the surrounding areas, Baraboo is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home in Baraboo costs $152,600. This is roughly $28,420 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $64,900 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is roughly $93,700 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Baraboo is $801 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $16 (1%) lower. It is roughly $261 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is roughly $317 (28%) lower.
