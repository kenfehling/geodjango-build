Home prices

The median home price in Baraboo is $152,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $28,420 (22%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $64,900 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is approximately $93,700 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Baraboo the median rent is $801 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16 (1%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $261 (24%) lower, and is approximately $317 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
