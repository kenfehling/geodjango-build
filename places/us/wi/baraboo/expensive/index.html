Compared to the surrounding areas, Baraboo is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Baraboo is $152,600. It is roughly $28,420 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $64,900 (29%) lower, and is approximately $93,700 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Baraboo the median rent is $801 per month. This is approximately $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $261 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $317 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
