Home prices

Baraboo's median home price is $152,600. It is about $28,420 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $64,900 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is about $93,700 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Baraboo is $801. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $16 (1%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $261 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is approximately $317 (28%) lower.
