Compared to its surrounding areas, Humbird is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Humbird is $78,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $12,140 (13%) lower. It is approximately $138,700 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is roughly $76,200 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Humbird the median rent is $677 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $85 (14%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $385 (36%) lower, and it is about $158 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.
