Home prices

The value of the median home in Humbird is $78,800. It is roughly $12,140 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $138,700 (63%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Eau Claire is approximately $76,200 (49%) lower.

Rents
