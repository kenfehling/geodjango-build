Home prices

The median home in Elmwood costs $122,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $7,380 (6%) higher. It is roughly $95,000 (43%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $129,100 (51%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Elmwood is $660 per month. This is approximately $67 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $402 (37%) lower, and is approximately $367 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
