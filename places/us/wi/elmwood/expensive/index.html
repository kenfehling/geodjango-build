Home prices

The median home in Elmwood is valued at $122,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $7,380 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $95,000 (43%) lower, and it is about $129,100 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Elmwood is $660 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $67 (9%) lower. It is about $402 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $367 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
