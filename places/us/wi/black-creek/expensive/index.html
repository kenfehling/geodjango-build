Home prices

The cost of the median home in Black Creek is $135,700. This is roughly $3,160 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $81,800 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $13,600 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Black Creek is $606. It is approximately $54 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $456 (42%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $252 (29%) lower.
