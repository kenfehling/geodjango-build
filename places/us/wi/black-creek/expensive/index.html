Home prices

Rents

Black Creek's median rent is $606 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $54 (8%) lower.It is approximately $456 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $252 (29%) lower.
