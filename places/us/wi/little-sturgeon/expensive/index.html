Home prices

The median home in Little Sturgeon costs $191,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $42,740 (28%) higher. It is roughly $25,800 (11%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $55,800 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

The median rent in Little Sturgeon is $529 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $225 (29%) lower. It is roughly $533 (50%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Green Bay is roughly $201 (27%) lower.
