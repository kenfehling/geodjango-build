Home prices

The cost of the median home in Little Sturgeon is $191,700. It is about $42,740 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $25,800 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay is approximately $55,800 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Little Sturgeon is $529 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $225 (29%) lower. It is approximately $533 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $201 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Green Bay.
