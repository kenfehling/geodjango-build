Home prices

The median home cost in Mishicot is $119,400. This is about $5,760 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $98,100 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $2,700 (2%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Mishicot's median rent is $681 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $73 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and is roughly $177 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
