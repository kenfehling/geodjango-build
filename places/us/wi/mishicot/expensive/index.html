Compared to the surrounding areas, Mishicot is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mishicot is $119,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $5,760 (5%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $98,100 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $2,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

In Mishicot the median rent is $681 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $73 (12%) higher.It is roughly $381 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $177 (20%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
