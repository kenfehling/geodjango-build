In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mishicot is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mishicot is $119,400. It is roughly $5,760 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $98,100 (45%) lower, and it is approximately $2,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Mishicot is $681 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $73 (12%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $177 (20%) lower.
