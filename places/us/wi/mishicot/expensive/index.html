Home prices

Rents

Mishicot's median monthly rent is $681. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $73 (12%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $381 (35%) lower, and is roughly $177 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
