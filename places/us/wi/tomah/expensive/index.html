Home prices

The median home cost in Tomah is $120,600. This is approximately $13,460 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $96,900 (44%) lower, and is about $125,700 (51%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Tomah the median rent is $827 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $72 (9%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $235 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $291 (26%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
