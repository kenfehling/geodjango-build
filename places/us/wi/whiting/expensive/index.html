Home prices

The median home cost in Whiting is $156,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $3,020 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $60,600 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $89,400 (36%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Whiting is $911. This is approximately $128 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $151 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $207 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
