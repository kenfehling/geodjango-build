Home prices

The median home in Whiting is valued at $156,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $3,020 (1%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $60,600 (27%) lower, and it is about $89,400 (36%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

Whiting's median monthly rent is $911. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $128 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $151 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is about $207 (18%) lower.
