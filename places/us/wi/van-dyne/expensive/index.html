Home prices

The median home in Van Dyne costs $130,800. This is approximately $2,860 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $86,700 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $8,700 (7%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Van Dyne's median rent is $571 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $153 (21%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $491 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $287 (33%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
