Home prices

The median home in Van Dyne costs $130,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $2,860 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $86,700 (39%) lower, and is approximately $8,700 (7%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Van Dyne the median rent is $571 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $153 (21%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $491 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $287 (33%) lower.
