Home prices

Rents

Van Dyne's median rent is $571 a month. This is roughly $153 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $491 (46%) lower, and is about $287 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
