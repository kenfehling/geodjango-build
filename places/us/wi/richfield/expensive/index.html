Rents

The median rent in Richfield is $1,038 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $83 (8%) higher. It is about $24 (2%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $74 (6%) lower.
