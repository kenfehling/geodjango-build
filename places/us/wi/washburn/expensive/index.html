Home prices

The median home in Washburn is valued at $142,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32,960 (30%) higher.It is roughly $74,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Duluth it is approximately $20,700 (12%) lower.

Rents

Washburn's median monthly rent is $658. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $68 (11%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $404 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth is roughly $162 (19%) lower.
