Home prices

The median home in Okauchee Lake costs $367,200. It is about $481,940 (56%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $149,700 (68%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $109,200 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Okauchee Lake is $890 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $572 (39%) lower. It is about $172 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $222 (19%) lower.
