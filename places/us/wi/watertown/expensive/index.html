Home prices

Watertown's median home price is $153,100. This is roughly $144,320 (48%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $64,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $31,000 (25%) higher.

Rents

In Watertown the median rent is $860 a month. This is approximately $247 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is is very similar.
