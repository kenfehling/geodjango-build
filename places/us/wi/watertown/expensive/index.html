Home prices

The price of the median home in Watertown is $153,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $144,320 (48%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $64,400 (29%) lower, and it is about $31,000 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Watertown the median rent is $860 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $247 (22%) lower.It is approximately $202 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is is very similar.
