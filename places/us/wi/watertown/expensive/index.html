Home prices

The median home in Watertown is valued at $153,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $144,320 (48%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $64,400 (29%) lower, and it is about $31,000 (25%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Watertown is $860. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $247 (22%) lower. It is roughly $202 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Milwaukee.
