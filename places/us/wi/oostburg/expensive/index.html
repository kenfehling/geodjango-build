Home prices

The value of the median home in Oostburg is $153,300. This is approximately $18,880 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $64,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $31,200 (25%) higher.

Rents

Oostburg's median rent is $745 a month. It is approximately $133 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $317 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $113 (13%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
