Rents

The median monthly rent in Oostburg is $745. It is roughly $133 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $317 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $113 (13%) lower.
