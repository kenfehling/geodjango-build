In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oostburg is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oostburg is $153,300. It is approximately $18,880 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $64,200 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $31,200 (25%) higher.

Rents

Oostburg's median monthly rent is $745. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $133 (15%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $317 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $113 (13%) lower.
