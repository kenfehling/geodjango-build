Home prices

Rents

In Boyceville the median monthly rent is $719. This is about $171 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $343 (32%) lower, and is approximately $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
