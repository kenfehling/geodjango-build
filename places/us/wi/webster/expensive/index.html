Home prices

The median home value in Webster is $94,800. This is roughly $5,640 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $122,700 (56%) lower, and it is approximately $156,800 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Webster's median monthly rent is $548. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $237 (30%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $514 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $479 (46%) lower.
