Compared to other places in this area, Oconomowoc Lake is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Oconomowoc Lake is $891,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $534,940 (149%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $674,200 (309%) higher, and is about $633,700 (245%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oconomowoc Lake is $1,688. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $601 (55%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $626 (58%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $576 (51%) higher.
