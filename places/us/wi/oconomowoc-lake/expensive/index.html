Home prices

The median home in Oconomowoc Lake costs $891,700. This is approximately $534,940 (149%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $674,200 (309%) higher, and is approximately $633,700 (245%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Oconomowoc Lake is $1,688 per month. It is approximately $601 (55%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $626 (58%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $576 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
