Home prices

The median home in Oconomowoc Lake is valued at $891,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $534,940 (149%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $674,200 (309%) higher, and it is roughly $633,700 (245%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oconomowoc Lake is $1,688. This is roughly $601 (55%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $626 (58%) higher, and it is about $576 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
