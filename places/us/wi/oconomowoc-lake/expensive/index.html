In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oconomowoc Lake is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Oconomowoc Lake is $891,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $534,940 (149%) higher. It is approximately $674,200 (309%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $633,700 (245%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Oconomowoc Lake the median monthly rent is $1,688. This is about $601 (55%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $626 (58%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $576 (51%) higher.
