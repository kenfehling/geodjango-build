In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sturgeon Bay is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Green Bay.

Home prices

The median home in Sturgeon Bay costs $147,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $56,420 (27%) lower. It is approximately $70,500 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $11,100 (8%) higher compared to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

The median rent in Sturgeon Bay is $790 per month. This is approximately $83 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $272 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $60 (8%) higher compared to the city of Green Bay.
