Compared to nearby areas, Sturgeon Bay is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Green Bay.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sturgeon Bay is $147,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $56,420 (27%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $70,500 (32%) lower, and it is about $11,100 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

In Sturgeon Bay the median rent is $790 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $83 (11%) higher. It is approximately $272 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Green Bay is approximately $60 (8%) higher.
