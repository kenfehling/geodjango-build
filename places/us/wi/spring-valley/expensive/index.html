Home prices

Rents

Spring Valley's median rent is $905 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $89 (10%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $157 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $122 (11%) lower.
