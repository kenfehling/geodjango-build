In comparison to the surrounding areas, Monona is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Monona is $272,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $78,640 (22%) lower. It is about $54,700 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $150,100 (122%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Monona the median monthly rent is $935. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $300 (24%) lower. It is roughly $127 (11%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $77 (8%) higher.
