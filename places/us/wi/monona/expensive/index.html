Compared to other places in this area, Monona is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Monona is $272,200. This is roughly $78,640 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $54,700 (25%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $150,100 (122%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monona is $935. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $300 (24%) lower.It is approximately $127 (11%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $77 (8%) higher.
