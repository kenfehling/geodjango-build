Home prices

The median home in Monona costs $272,200. It is about $78,640 (22%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $54,700 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $150,100 (122%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Monona is $935 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $300 (24%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $127 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $77 (8%) higher.
