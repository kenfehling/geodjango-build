Home prices

The price of the median home in Grantsburg is $95,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $33,240 (25%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower, and is roughly $155,800 (61%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Grantsburg's median rent is $727 per month. It is roughly $120 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $335 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $300 (29%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
