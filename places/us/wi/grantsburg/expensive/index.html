In comparison to its surrounding areas, Grantsburg is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Grantsburg is valued at $95,800. This is about $33,240 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $121,700 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $155,800 (61%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grantsburg is $727. This is approximately $120 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $335 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $300 (29%) lower.
