In comparison to its surrounding areas, Winchester is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Winchester is valued at $164,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $17,100 (9%) lower. It is roughly $52,700 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $42,700 (34%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Winchester is $825 a month. This is about $59 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $33 (3%) lower.
