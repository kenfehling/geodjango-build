Home prices

Fremont's median home price is $157,000. This is approximately $33,400 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $60,500 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is about $89,300 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Fremont the median monthly rent is $650. It is roughly $34 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $412 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is about $468 (41%) lower.
