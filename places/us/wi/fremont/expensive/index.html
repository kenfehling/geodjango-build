Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fremont is $157,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $33,400 (27%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $60,500 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is approximately $89,300 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Fremont the median monthly rent is $650. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $34 (4%) lower. It is approximately $412 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Madison it is about $468 (41%) lower.
