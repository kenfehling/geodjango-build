Home prices

The median home in Fitchburg is valued at $315,900. This is roughly $34,880 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $98,400 (45%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $69,600 (28%) higher compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Fitchburg is $1,054 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $96 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is is very similar, and it is about $64 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
