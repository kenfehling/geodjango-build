Home prices

The cost of the median home in Polonia is $181,000. It is approximately $28,780 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $36,500 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay it is roughly $45,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Polonia the median monthly rent is $810. This is approximately $116 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $252 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $80 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Green Bay.
