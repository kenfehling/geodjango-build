Home prices

The cost of the median home in Somerset is $172,600. This is about $82,900 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $79,000 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Somerset is $960. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $151 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $102 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $67 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
