Home prices

Berlin's median home cost is $109,000. It is about $9,320 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $108,500 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $13,100 (10%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Berlin is $742. This is about $142 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $320 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $116 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
