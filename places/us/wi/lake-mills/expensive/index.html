Compared to the surrounding areas, Lake Mills is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Mills costs $198,100. This is approximately $13,080 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $19,400 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $76,000 (62%) higher.

Rents

Lake Mills's median rent is $978 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $60 (6%) higher.It is roughly $84 (7%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $120 (13%) higher.
