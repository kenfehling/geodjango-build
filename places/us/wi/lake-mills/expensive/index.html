Compared to its surrounding areas, Lake Mills is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake Mills is $198,100. It is roughly $13,080 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $19,400 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $76,000 (62%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Mills is $978. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $60 (6%) higher.It is roughly $84 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $120 (13%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
