Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Mills is $198,100. This is about $13,080 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $19,400 (8%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $76,000 (62%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Mills is $978. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $60 (6%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $84 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $120 (13%) higher.
