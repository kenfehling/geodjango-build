Home prices

Rents

Campbellsport's median rent is $724 a month. This is roughly $61 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $134 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
