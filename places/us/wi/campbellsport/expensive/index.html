Home prices

The cost of the median home in Campbellsport is $151,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $1,720 (1%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $66,200 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $29,200 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Campbellsport is $724. This is roughly $61 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $134 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
