Home prices

The median home price in Campbellsport is $151,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $1,720 (1%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower, and is about $29,200 (23%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Campbellsport's median rent is $724 a month. This is about $61 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $134 (15%) lower.
