Home prices

West Milwaukee's median home price is $137,500. This is roughly $30,660 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $80,000 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $120,500 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Milwaukee is $738. This is approximately $170 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $324 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $374 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
