Home prices

The median home in West Milwaukee is valued at $137,500. It is roughly $30,660 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $80,000 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $120,500 (46%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In West Milwaukee the median rent is $738 a month. This is about $170 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $324 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $374 (33%) lower.
