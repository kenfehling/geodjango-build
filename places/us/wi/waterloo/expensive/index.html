Much like other places in this area, it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home cost in Waterloo is $161,500. It is approximately $8,320 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $56,000 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $39,400 (32%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Waterloo is $753 a month. This is roughly $115 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $309 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $105 (12%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
