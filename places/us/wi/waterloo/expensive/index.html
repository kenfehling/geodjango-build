Home prices

The median home in Waterloo is valued at $161,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $8,320 (4%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $56,000 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $39,400 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Waterloo the median rent is $753 a month. This is approximately $115 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $309 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $105 (12%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
