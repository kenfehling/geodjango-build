In comparison to the surrounding areas, Marion is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Green Bay.

Home prices

The median home price in Marion is $90,000. This is about $9,540 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $127,500 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay is about $45,900 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marion is $615 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $314 (33%) lower. It is roughly $447 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $115 (15%) lower compared to the city of Green Bay.
