In comparison to nearby areas, Sheboygan is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Sheboygan's median home cost is $114,500. This is about $65,100 (36%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $103,000 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $7,600 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Sheboygan is $699 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $193 (21%) lower. It is approximately $363 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $159 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
