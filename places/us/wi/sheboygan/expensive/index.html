Compared to the surrounding areas, Sheboygan is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sheboygan is $114,500. It is about $65,100 (36%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $103,000 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $7,600 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Sheboygan the median monthly rent is $699. This is about $193 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $363 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $159 (18%) lower.
