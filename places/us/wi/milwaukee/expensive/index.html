Compared to the surrounding areas, Milwaukee is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milwaukee is valued at $122,100. It is roughly $143,220 (53%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $95,400 (43%) lower, and is approximately $135,900 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Milwaukee the median rent is $858 per month. It is approximately $177 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $204 (19%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $254 (22%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
