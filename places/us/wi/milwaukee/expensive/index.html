Compared to its nearby areas, Milwaukee is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Milwaukee is $122,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $143,220 (53%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $95,400 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $135,900 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Milwaukee is $858 per month. This is approximately $177 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $204 (19%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $254 (22%) lower.
