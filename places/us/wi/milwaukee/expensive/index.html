Home prices

The median home in Milwaukee costs $122,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $143,220 (53%) lower.It is roughly $95,400 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $135,900 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milwaukee is $858. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $177 (17%) lower.It is about $204 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $254 (22%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
