Home prices

The median home in Milwaukee costs $122,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $143,220 (53%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $95,400 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $135,900 (52%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milwaukee is $858. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $177 (17%) lower. It is roughly $204 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $254 (22%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
