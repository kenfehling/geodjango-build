In comparison to nearby areas, Hartford is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Hartford is valued at $188,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $20,720 (9%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $29,500 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $65,900 (53%) higher.

Rents

In Hartford the median rent is $818 per month. This is about $47 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $244 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $40 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
