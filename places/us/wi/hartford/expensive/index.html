In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hartford is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Hartford's median home price is $188,000. This is about $20,720 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $29,500 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $65,900 (53%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hartford is $818. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $47 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $244 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $40 (4%) lower.
