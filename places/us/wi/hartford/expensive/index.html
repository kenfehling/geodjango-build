Home prices

The median home value in Hartford is $188,000. It is about $20,720 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $29,500 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $65,900 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Hartford the median rent is $818 per month. It is about $47 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $244 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $40 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
