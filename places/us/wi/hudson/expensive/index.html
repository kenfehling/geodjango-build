In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hudson is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Hudson is $243,400. This is approximately $19,320 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $25,900 (11%) higher, and is about $8,200 (3%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Hudson the median rent is $1,167 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $24 (2%) higher. It is approximately $105 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $140 (13%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
