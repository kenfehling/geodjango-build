Home prices

The median home in Hudson costs $243,400. This is about $19,320 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $25,900 (11%) higher, and it is roughly $8,200 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hudson is $1,167. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24 (2%) higher.It is roughly $105 (9%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $140 (13%) higher.
