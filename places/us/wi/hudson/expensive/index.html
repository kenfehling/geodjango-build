In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hudson is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Hudson is valued at $243,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $19,320 (7%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $25,900 (11%) higher, and is approximately $8,200 (3%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Hudson's median rent is $1,167 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24 (2%) higher. It is approximately $105 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $140 (13%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
