Home prices

The median home in Hudson is valued at $243,400. It is roughly $19,320 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $25,900 (11%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $8,200 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hudson is $1,167 a month. This is about $24 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $105 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $140 (13%) higher.
