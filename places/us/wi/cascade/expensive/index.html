Compared to the surrounding areas, Cascade is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cascade is $163,400. This is about $10,760 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $54,100 (24%) lower, and is about $41,300 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Cascade is $702 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $126 (15%) lower. It is approximately $360 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $156 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
