Home prices

Cascade's median home price is $163,400. It is approximately $10,760 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $54,100 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $41,300 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Cascade the median rent is $702 per month. This is roughly $126 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $360 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $156 (18%) lower.
