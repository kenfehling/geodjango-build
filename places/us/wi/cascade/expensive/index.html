Compared to its surrounding areas, Cascade is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Cascade costs $163,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $10,760 (7%) higher. It is about $54,100 (24%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $41,300 (33%) higher.

Rents

Cascade's median rent is $702 a month. It is approximately $126 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $360 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $156 (18%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
