In comparison to other places in this area, Windsor is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Windsor is $285,300. This is roughly $37,720 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $67,800 (31%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $163,200 (133%) higher.

Rents

Windsor's median rent is $1,100 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $31 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $38 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $242 (28%) higher.
