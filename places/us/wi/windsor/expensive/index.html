Compared to the surrounding areas, Windsor is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Windsor's median home value is $285,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $37,720 (15%) higher.It is roughly $67,800 (31%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $163,200 (133%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Windsor is $1,100 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $31 (2%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $38 (3%) higher, and is approximately $242 (28%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
