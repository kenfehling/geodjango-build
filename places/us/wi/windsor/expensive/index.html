Compared to the surrounding areas, Windsor is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Windsor is $285,300. It is approximately $37,720 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $67,800 (31%) higher, and it is approximately $163,200 (133%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Windsor is $1,100 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $31 (2%) higher.It is approximately $38 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $242 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
