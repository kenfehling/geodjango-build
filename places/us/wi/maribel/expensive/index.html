Rents

In Maribel the median monthly rent is $533. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $109 (16%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $529 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $325 (37%) lower.
