Home prices

The median home cost in Maribel is $129,000. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $3,120 (2%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $88,500 (40%) lower, and is approximately $6,900 (5%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Maribel the median rent is $533 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $109 (16%) lower. It is about $529 (49%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $325 (37%) lower.
