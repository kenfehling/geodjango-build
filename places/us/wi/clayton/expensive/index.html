Compared to its surrounding areas, Clayton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Clayton is $113,000. This is roughly $7,280 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $104,500 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $138,600 (55%) lower.

Rents

Clayton's median rent is $806 per month. This is roughly $155 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $256 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $221 (21%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
