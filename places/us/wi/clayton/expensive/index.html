Rents

In Clayton the median rent is $806 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $155 (23%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $256 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $221 (21%) lower.
