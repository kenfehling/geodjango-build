Home prices

Slinger's median home value is $257,100. It is about $46,240 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $39,600 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $135,000 (110%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Slinger is $920 per month. This is approximately $9 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $142 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $62 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
