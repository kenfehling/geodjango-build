In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bellevue is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Bellevue is valued at $188,400. This is roughly $31,920 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $29,100 (13%) lower, and is about $66,300 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Bellevue's median monthly rent is $890. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $115 (14%) higher. It is roughly $172 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $32 (3%) higher.
