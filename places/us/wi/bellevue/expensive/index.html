Compared to other places in this area, Bellevue is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bellevue costs $188,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $31,920 (20%) higher. It is approximately $29,100 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $66,300 (54%) higher.

Rents

Bellevue's median monthly rent is $890. It is about $115 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $172 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $32 (3%) higher.
