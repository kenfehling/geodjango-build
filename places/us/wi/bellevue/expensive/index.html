Compared to its nearby areas, Bellevue is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Bellevue is $188,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $31,920 (20%) higher.It is about $29,100 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $66,300 (54%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Bellevue the median monthly rent is $890. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $115 (14%) higher.It is roughly $172 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $32 (3%) higher.
