In comparison to nearby areas, Bellevue is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Bellevue is $188,400. It is about $31,920 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $29,100 (13%) lower, and it is about $66,300 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Bellevue's median rent is $890 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $115 (14%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $172 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $32 (3%) higher.
