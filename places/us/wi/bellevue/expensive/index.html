In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bellevue is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukeehowever, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bellevue costs $188,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $31,920 (20%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $29,100 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $66,300 (54%) higher.

Rents

Bellevue's median monthly rent is $890. It is approximately $115 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $172 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $32 (3%) higher.
