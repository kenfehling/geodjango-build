Home prices

The median home cost in Bristol is $266,500. This is approximately $65,080 (32%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $49,000 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $8,500 (3%) higher.

Rents

Bristol's median monthly rent is $987. This is about $100 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $75 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $125 (11%) lower.
