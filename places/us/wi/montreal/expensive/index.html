In comparison to other places in this area, Montreal is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Merrill, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Montreal is $77,000. This is about $23,300 (43%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $140,500 (64%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $8,300 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Merrill.

Rents

In Montreal the median rent is $555 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $65 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $507 (47%) lower, and is about $82 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Merrill.
