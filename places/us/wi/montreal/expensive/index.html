Compared to the surrounding areas, Montreal is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Merrill.

Home prices

The median home in Montreal costs $77,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $23,300 (43%) higher.It is approximately $140,500 (64%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Merrill it is approximately $8,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

Montreal's median rent is $555 per month. It is about $65 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $507 (47%) lower, and it is about $82 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Merrill.
