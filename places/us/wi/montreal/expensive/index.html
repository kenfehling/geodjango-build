In comparison to the surrounding areas, Montreal is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Merrill, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montreal costs $77,000. It is roughly $23,300 (43%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $140,500 (64%) lower, and it is roughly $8,300 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Merrill.

Rents

Montreal's median monthly rent is $555. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $65 (13%) higher. It is approximately $507 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $82 (12%) lower compared to the city of Merrill.
