Home prices

The value of the median home in Montreal is $77,000. This is roughly $23,300 (43%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $140,500 (64%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $8,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Merrill.

Rents

Montreal's median rent is $555 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $65 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $507 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Merrill it is about $82 (12%) lower.
