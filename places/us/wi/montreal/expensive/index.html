Compared to nearby areas, Montreal is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Merrill, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Montreal is $77,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $23,300 (43%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $140,500 (64%) lower, and it is about $8,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Merrill.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montreal is $555. It is roughly $65 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $507 (47%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $82 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Merrill.
