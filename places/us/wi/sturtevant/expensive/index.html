In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sturtevant is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sturtevant costs $160,200. It is roughly $29,680 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $57,300 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $97,800 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Sturtevant the median rent is $864 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $96 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $198 (18%) lower, and is approximately $248 (22%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
