Compared to other places in this area, Wisconsin Rapids is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Madison, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Wisconsin Rapids is $91,100. This is approximately $21,860 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $126,400 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $155,200 (63%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Wisconsin Rapids is $758 per month. This is roughly $10 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $304 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is roughly $360 (32%) lower.
