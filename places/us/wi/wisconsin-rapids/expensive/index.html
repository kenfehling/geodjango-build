In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wisconsin Rapids is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Madison, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wisconsin Rapids is $91,100. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $21,860 (19%) lower.It is roughly $126,400 (58%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $155,200 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

Wisconsin Rapids's median monthly rent is $758. This is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $304 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $360 (32%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
