In comparison to nearby areas, Wisconsin Rapids is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

Wisconsin Rapids's median home price is $91,100. It is approximately $21,860 (19%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $126,400 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is about $155,200 (63%) lower.

Rents

Wisconsin Rapids's median rent is $758 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $10 (1%) lower. It is roughly $304 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $360 (32%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
