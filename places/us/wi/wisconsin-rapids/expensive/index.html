In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wisconsin Rapids is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home in Wisconsin Rapids costs $91,100. It is approximately $21,860 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $126,400 (58%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $155,200 (63%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wisconsin Rapids is $758. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $10 (1%) lower. It is roughly $304 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $360 (32%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
