Compared to nearby areas, Wisconsin Rapids is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home price in Wisconsin Rapids is $91,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $21,860 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $126,400 (58%) lower, and is roughly $155,200 (63%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Wisconsin Rapids the median rent is $758 a month. It is roughly $10 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $304 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $360 (32%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
