Home prices

The median home cost in Hobart is $270,800. It is approximately $97,140 (55%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $53,300 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $148,700 (121%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hobart is $824 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $24 (2%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $238 (22%) lower, and is about $34 (3%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
