Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hobart is $270,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $97,140 (55%) higher.It is roughly $53,300 (24%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $148,700 (121%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Hobart the median rent is $824 per month. This is approximately $24 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $238 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $34 (3%) lower.
