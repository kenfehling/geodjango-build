Home prices

Portage's median home cost is $125,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $25,960 (17%) lower. It is approximately $92,300 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Madison it is approximately $121,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Portage the median monthly rent is $770. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $58 (8%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $292 (27%) lower, and it is about $348 (31%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
