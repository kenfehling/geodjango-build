In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marshfield is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Eau Claire, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marshfield costs $141,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $8,200 (6%) higher. It is about $76,200 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is approximately $13,700 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marshfield is $685. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $117 (20%) higher. It is about $377 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire it is roughly $150 (17%) lower.
