Home prices

The median home value in Marshfield is $141,300. It is roughly $8,200 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $76,200 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $13,700 (8%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Marshfield the median monthly rent is $685. This is about $117 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Eau Claire is approximately $150 (17%) lower.
