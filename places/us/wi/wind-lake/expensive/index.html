Home prices

The median home in Wind Lake is valued at $270,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $17,720 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $52,800 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $12,300 (4%) higher.

Rents

Wind Lake's median rent is $974 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $97 (9%) lower. It is approximately $88 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $138 (12%) lower.
