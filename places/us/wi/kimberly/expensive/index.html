In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kimberly is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Kimberly's median home price is $146,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $23,300 (13%) lower. It is about $70,600 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $24,800 (20%) higher.

Rents

In Kimberly the median monthly rent is $737. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $147 (16%) lower. It is about $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $121 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
