In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kimberly is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Kimberly's median home value is $146,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $23,300 (13%) lower.It is approximately $70,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $24,800 (20%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kimberly is $737. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $147 (16%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $325 (30%) lower, and it is about $121 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
