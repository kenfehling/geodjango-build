Compared to nearby areas, Kimberly is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Kimberly's median home value is $146,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $23,300 (13%) lower.It is approximately $70,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $24,800 (20%) higher.

Rents

Kimberly's median rent is $737 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $147 (16%) lower.It is about $325 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $121 (14%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
