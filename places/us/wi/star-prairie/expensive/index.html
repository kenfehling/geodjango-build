Home prices

Star Prairie's median home cost is $163,500. This is roughly $10,280 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $54,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $88,100 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Star Prairie the median monthly rent is $781. This is roughly $11 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $281 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $246 (23%) lower.
