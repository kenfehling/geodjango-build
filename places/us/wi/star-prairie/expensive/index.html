Home prices

The price of the median home in Star Prairie is $163,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $10,280 (6%) higher.It is about $54,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $88,100 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Star Prairie the median rent is $781 per month. This is roughly $11 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $281 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $246 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
