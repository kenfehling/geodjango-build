Compared to nearby areas, North Hudson is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in North Hudson is $263,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $18,280 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $45,700 (21%) higher, and it is about $11,600 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

North Hudson's median monthly rent is $1,115. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $110 (8%) lower. It is approximately $53 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $88 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
