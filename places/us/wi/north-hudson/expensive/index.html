Home prices

The median home in North Hudson costs $263,200. It is approximately $18,280 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $45,700 (21%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $11,600 (4%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In North Hudson the median rent is $1,115 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $110 (8%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $53 (4%) higher, and it is approximately $88 (8%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
