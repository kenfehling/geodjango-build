Compared to its surrounding areas, North Hudson is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in North Hudson costs $263,200. It is roughly $18,280 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $45,700 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $11,600 (4%) higher.

Rents

In North Hudson the median monthly rent is $1,115. It is roughly $110 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $53 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $88 (8%) higher.
