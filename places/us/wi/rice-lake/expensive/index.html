Home prices

The price of the median home in Rice Lake is $130,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $20,960 (19%) higher. It is about $87,300 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Duluth it is approximately $33,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

Rice Lake's median monthly rent is $712. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $24 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $350 (32%) lower, and is roughly $108 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Duluth.
