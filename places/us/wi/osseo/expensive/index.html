Home prices

The median home in Osseo costs $124,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $10,340 (9%) higher.It is roughly $93,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $30,800 (19%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Osseo the median rent is $680 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24 (3%) higher.It is roughly $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $155 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.
