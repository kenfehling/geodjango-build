In comparison to other places in this area, Osseo is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Home prices

The median home price in Osseo is $124,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $10,340 (9%) higher.It is roughly $93,300 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $30,800 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Osseo the median monthly rent is $680. This is roughly $24 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $382 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Eau Claire is approximately $155 (18%) lower.
