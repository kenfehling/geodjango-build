Home prices

The median home cost in Waubeka is $218,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $29,520 (15%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $96,200 (78%) higher.

Rents

In Waubeka the median monthly rent is $821. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $20 (2%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $241 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $37 (4%) lower.
