Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Waubeka is $821 per month. This is roughly $20 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $37 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
