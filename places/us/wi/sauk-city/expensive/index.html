In comparison to nearby areas, Sauk City is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Madison, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Sauk City is $207,400. This is about $34,460 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $10,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $38,900 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sauk City is $724. This is approximately $139 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $394 (35%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
