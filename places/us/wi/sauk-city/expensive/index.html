Home prices

The median home in Sauk City is valued at $207,400. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $34,460 (19%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $10,100 (4%) lower, and it is approximately $38,900 (15%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Sauk City is $724 per month. This is approximately $139 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $338 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $394 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
