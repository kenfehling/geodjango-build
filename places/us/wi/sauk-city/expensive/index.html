In comparison to nearby areas, Sauk City is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home in Sauk City is valued at $207,400. It is roughly $34,460 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $10,100 (4%) lower, and it is roughly $38,900 (15%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Sauk City the median monthly rent is $724. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $139 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $338 (31%) lower, and is about $394 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
