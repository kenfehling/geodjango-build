Compared to its surrounding areas, Sauk City is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Madison however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sauk City is $207,400. It is approximately $34,460 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $10,100 (4%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is about $38,900 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Sauk City the median rent is $724 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $139 (16%) lower.It is about $338 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is roughly $394 (35%) lower.
