Home prices

The price of the median home in Medford is $111,100. This is approximately $20,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $106,400 (48%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is roughly $43,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Medford is $661. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $59 (8%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $401 (37%) lower, and is about $174 (20%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.
