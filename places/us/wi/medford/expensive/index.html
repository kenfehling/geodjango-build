Home prices

The median home in Medford costs $111,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $20,800 (23%) higher. It is about $106,400 (48%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $43,900 (28%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

The median rent in Medford is $661 a month. It is roughly $59 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $401 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is roughly $174 (20%) lower.
