Home prices

The median home cost in Medford is $111,100. This is approximately $20,800 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $106,400 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is about $43,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Medford the median monthly rent is $661. This is roughly $59 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $401 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $174 (20%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.
