Home prices

Rents

Brillion's median monthly rent is $705. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $51 (7%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $357 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $153 (17%) lower.
