Home prices

Rents

In Brillion the median monthly rent is $705. This is approximately $51 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $357 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $153 (17%) lower.
