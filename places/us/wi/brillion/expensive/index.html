Compared to its surrounding areas, Brillion is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Brillion is $129,100. This is roughly $20,100 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $88,400 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $7,000 (5%) higher.

Rents

Brillion's median monthly rent is $705. It is about $51 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $357 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $153 (17%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
