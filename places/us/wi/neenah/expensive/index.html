In comparison to other places in this area, Neenah is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Neenah is $141,100. Compared to nearby areas it is about $24,020 (14%) lower.It is about $76,400 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $19,000 (15%) higher.

Rents

Neenah's median monthly rent is $750. This is approximately $115 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $108 (12%) lower.
