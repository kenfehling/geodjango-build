Compared to its nearby areas, Neenah is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Neenah is $141,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24,020 (14%) lower. It is approximately $76,400 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $19,000 (15%) higher.

Rents

Neenah's median rent is $750 a month. It is approximately $115 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $312 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $108 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
