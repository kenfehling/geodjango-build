Home prices

The price of the median home in Neenah is $141,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $24,020 (14%) lower.It is approximately $76,400 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $19,000 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Neenah the median monthly rent is $750. It is approximately $115 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $312 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $108 (12%) lower.
