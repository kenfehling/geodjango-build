Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomfield is $1,120. It is roughly $120 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $58 (5%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Chicago.
