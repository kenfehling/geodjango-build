In comparison to other places in this area, Bloomfield is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bloomfield is valued at $168,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $18,300 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $48,700 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $89,200 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Bloomfield's median rent is $1,120 a month. This is approximately $120 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $58 (5%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is is very similar.
