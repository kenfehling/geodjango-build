In comparison to nearby areas, Bloomfield is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bloomfield costs $168,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $18,300 (9%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $48,700 (22%) lower, and is about $89,200 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Bloomfield the median rent is $1,120 per month. This is about $120 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $58 (5%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Chicago.
