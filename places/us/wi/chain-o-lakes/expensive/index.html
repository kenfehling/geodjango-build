Home prices

The median home cost in Chain O' Lakes is $454,600. This is approximately $343,360 (308%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $237,100 (109%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $208,300 (84%) higher in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

Chain O' Lakes's median monthly rent is $812. It is roughly $87 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is about $250 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is about $306 (27%) lower.
