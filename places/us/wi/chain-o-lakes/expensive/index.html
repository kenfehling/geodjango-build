Home prices

The median home in Chain O' Lakes costs $454,600. It is about $343,360 (308%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $237,100 (109%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $208,300 (84%) higher compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Chain O' Lakes the median monthly rent is $812. This is approximately $87 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $250 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is roughly $306 (27%) lower.
