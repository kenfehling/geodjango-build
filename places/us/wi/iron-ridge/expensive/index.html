Compared to other places in this area, Iron Ridge is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Iron Ridge costs $134,600. It is approximately $26,880 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $82,900 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $12,500 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Iron Ridge is $792. This is about $26 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $66 (7%) lower.
