Rents

In Iron Ridge the median rent is $792 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $26 (3%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $66 (7%) lower.
