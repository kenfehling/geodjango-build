Rents

The median rent in Iron Ridge is $792 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $26 (3%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $270 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $66 (7%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
