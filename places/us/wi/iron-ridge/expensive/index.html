In comparison to its surrounding areas, Iron Ridge is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Iron Ridge is valued at $134,600. This is approximately $26,880 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $82,900 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $12,500 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Iron Ridge's median monthly rent is $792. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $26 (3%) lower. It is about $270 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $66 (7%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
