Home prices

The value of the median home in Poplar is $170,400. This is roughly $14,540 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $47,100 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $7,100 (4%) higher compared to the city of Duluth.

Rents

In Poplar the median rent is $1,179 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $392 (49%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $117 (11%) higher, and is about $359 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Duluth.
