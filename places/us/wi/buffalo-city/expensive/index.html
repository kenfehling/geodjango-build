Home prices

The value of the median home in Buffalo City is $152,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $20,820 (15%) higher.It is approximately $64,900 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $47,500 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Buffalo City is $1,011. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $264 (35%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $51 (4%) lower, and it is about $37 (3%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.
