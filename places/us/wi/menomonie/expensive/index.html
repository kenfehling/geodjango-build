Home prices

The median home in Menomonie costs $145,200. This is about $14,900 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $72,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $106,400 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Menomonie is $762. This is about $26 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $300 (28%) lower, and it is about $265 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
