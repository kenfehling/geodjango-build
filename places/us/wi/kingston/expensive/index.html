Home prices

The median home in Kingston costs $108,100. This is about $1,560 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $109,400 (50%) lower, and is about $14,000 (11%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Kingston's median monthly rent is $715. This is about $106 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $347 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $143 (16%) lower.
