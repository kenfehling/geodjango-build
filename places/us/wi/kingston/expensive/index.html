The median home cost in Kingston is $108,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $1,560 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $109,400 (50%) lower, and is approximately $14,000 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Kingston is $715 a month. This is approximately $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $347 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $143 (16%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
