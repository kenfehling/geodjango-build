Home prices

Rents

In Kohler the median rent is $958 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $118 (14%) higher. It is about $104 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $100 (11%) higher.
