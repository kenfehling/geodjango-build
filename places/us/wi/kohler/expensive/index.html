Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kohler is $252,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $100,500 (66%) higher.It is about $35,000 (16%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $130,400 (106%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Kohler's median monthly rent is $958. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $118 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $104 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $100 (11%) higher.
