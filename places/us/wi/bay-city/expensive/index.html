In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bay City is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bay City is valued at $125,400. This is approximately $29,860 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $92,100 (42%) lower, and is roughly $126,200 (50%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Bay City the median monthly rent is $729. It is approximately $47 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $298 (29%) lower.
