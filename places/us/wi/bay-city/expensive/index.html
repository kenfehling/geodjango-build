Home prices

The median home in Bay City is valued at $125,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $29,860 (19%) lower. It is about $92,100 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $126,200 (50%) lower.

Rents

Bay City's median monthly rent is $729. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $47 (6%) lower. It is about $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $298 (29%) lower.
