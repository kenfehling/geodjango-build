Compared to other places in this area, Bay City is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bay City is valued at $125,400. It is approximately $29,860 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $92,100 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $126,200 (50%) lower.

Rents

Bay City's median rent is $729 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $47 (6%) lower. It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $298 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
