Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ixonia is $243,300. It is about $127,040 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $25,800 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $14,700 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Ixonia the median rent is $966 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $197 (16%) lower. It is roughly $96 (9%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $146 (13%) lower.
