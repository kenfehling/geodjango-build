Home prices

The price of the median home in Shorewood is $338,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is approximately $120,900 (55%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $80,400 (31%) higher.

Rents

Shorewood's median monthly rent is $1,004. This is approximately $148 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $58 (5%) lower, and is roughly $108 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
