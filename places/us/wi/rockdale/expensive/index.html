The median home price in Rockdale is $173,900. This is roughly $48,700 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $43,600 (20%) lower, and is about $51,800 (42%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rockdale is $890. This is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $172 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $32 (3%) higher.
