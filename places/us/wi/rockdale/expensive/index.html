Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rockdale is $173,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $48,700 (21%) lower. It is roughly $43,600 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $51,800 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rockdale is $890 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $13 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $172 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $32 (3%) higher.
