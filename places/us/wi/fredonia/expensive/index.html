Home prices

The median home in Fredonia is valued at $198,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $6,120 (3%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $18,700 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $76,700 (62%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Fredonia is $908 per month. This is approximately $84 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $50 (5%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
