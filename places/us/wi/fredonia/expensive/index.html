Home prices

The median home cost in Fredonia is $198,800. It is approximately $6,120 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $18,700 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $76,700 (62%) higher.

Rents

Fredonia's median rent is $908 a month. This is about $84 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $154 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $50 (5%) higher.
