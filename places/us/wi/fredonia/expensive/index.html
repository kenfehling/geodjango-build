Home prices

The value of the median home in Fredonia is $198,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $6,120 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $18,700 (8%) lower, and it is about $76,700 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fredonia is $908. It is roughly $84 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $154 (14%) lower, and is approximately $50 (5%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
