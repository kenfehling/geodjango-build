Similar to other places in this area, it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Fredonia costs $198,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $6,120 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $18,700 (8%) lower, and is approximately $76,700 (62%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fredonia is $908. This is roughly $84 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $154 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $50 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
