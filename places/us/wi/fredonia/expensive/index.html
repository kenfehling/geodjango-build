Home prices

The median home in Fredonia costs $198,800. It is approximately $6,120 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $18,700 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $76,700 (62%) higher.

Rents

Fredonia's median rent is $908 a month. It is approximately $84 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $154 (14%) lower, and it is roughly $50 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
