Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Dallas is $753 per month. It is approximately $110 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $309 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $215 (22%) lower compared to the city of St. Paul.
