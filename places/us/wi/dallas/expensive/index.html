Dallas's median home cost is $86,000. This is roughly $11,040 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $131,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $122,000 (58%) lower compared to the city of St. Paul.

Rents

In Dallas the median rent is $753 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $110 (17%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $309 (29%) lower, and it is about $215 (22%) lower compared to the city of St. Paul.
