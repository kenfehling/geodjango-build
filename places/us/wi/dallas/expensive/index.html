Home prices

Dallas's median home value is $86,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $11,040 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $131,500 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Paul is approximately $122,000 (58%) lower.

Rents

Dallas's median rent is $753 a month. It is approximately $110 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $309 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Paul is approximately $215 (22%) lower.
