Home prices

The median home in Jackson is valued at $209,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $54,780 (20%) lower. It is approximately $7,700 (3%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $87,700 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Jackson the median rent is $1,000 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $52 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $62 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $142 (16%) higher.
