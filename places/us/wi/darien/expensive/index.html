Home prices

The median home price in Darien is $152,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $58,580 (27%) lower. It is roughly $65,400 (30%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $105,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Darien the median monthly rent is $1,033. It is about $143 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $29 (2%) lower, and is about $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
