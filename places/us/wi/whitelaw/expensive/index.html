Home prices

The value of the median home in Whitelaw is $133,000. This is approximately $16,880 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $84,500 (38%) lower, and is roughly $10,900 (8%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Whitelaw the median monthly rent is $717. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $98 (15%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $345 (32%) lower, and it is about $141 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
