Home prices

The median home in Maine costs $192,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $59,020 (44%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $25,100 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Wausau it is roughly $70,500 (57%) higher.

Rents

In Maine the median rent is $777 per month. This is approximately $27 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $285 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wausau is about $69 (9%) higher.
