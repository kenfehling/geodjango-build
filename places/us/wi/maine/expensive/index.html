In comparison to other places in this area, Maine is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Wausau, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Maine's median home price is $192,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $59,020 (44%) higher. It is roughly $25,100 (11%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $70,500 (57%) higher compared to the city of Wausau.

Rents

The median rent in Maine is $777 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $27 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Wausau is about $69 (9%) higher.
