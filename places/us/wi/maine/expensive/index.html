Home prices

The median home cost in Maine is $192,400. It is about $59,020 (44%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $25,100 (11%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $70,500 (57%) higher compared to the city of Wausau.

Rents

Maine's median rent is $777 a month. It is roughly $27 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $285 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Wausau is roughly $69 (9%) higher.
