Home prices

The median home price in New Glarus is $165,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $14,840 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $52,400 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is roughly $81,200 (32%) lower.

Rents

In New Glarus the median rent is $793 a month. This is approximately $29 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $269 (25%) lower, and it is about $325 (29%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
