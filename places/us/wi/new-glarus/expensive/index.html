New Glarus's median home price is $165,100. This is roughly $14,840 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $52,400 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is approximately $81,200 (32%) lower.

Rents

In New Glarus the median rent is $793 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $29 (3%) lower. It is about $269 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $325 (29%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
