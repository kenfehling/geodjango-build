Home prices

Pewaukee's median home value is $315,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $3,520 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $97,800 (44%) higher, and it is approximately $57,300 (22%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Pewaukee the median rent is $1,338 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $241 (21%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $276 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $226 (20%) higher.
