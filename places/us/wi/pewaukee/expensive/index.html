Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pewaukee is $315,300. This is about $3,520 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $97,800 (44%) higher, and is about $57,300 (22%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Pewaukee the median monthly rent is $1,338. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $241 (21%) higher.It is roughly $276 (25%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $226 (20%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
