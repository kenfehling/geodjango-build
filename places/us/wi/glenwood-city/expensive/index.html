Home prices

The median home in Glenwood City costs $120,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $7,940 (6%) lower.It is roughly $97,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $131,600 (52%) lower.

Rents

Glenwood City's median rent is $891 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $20 (2%) higher.It is roughly $171 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $136 (13%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
