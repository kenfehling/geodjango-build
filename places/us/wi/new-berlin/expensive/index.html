Home prices

The median home in New Berlin is valued at $259,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $33,180 (14%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $42,300 (19%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in New Berlin is $1,211 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $241 (24%) higher. It is roughly $149 (14%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $99 (8%) higher.
