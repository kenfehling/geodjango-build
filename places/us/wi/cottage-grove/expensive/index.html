Home prices

The median home price in Cottage Grove is $277,500. It is approximately $48,780 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $60,000 (27%) higher, and is about $155,400 (127%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Cottage Grove the median monthly rent is $1,232. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $267 (27%) higher.It is about $170 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $374 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
