Home prices

Cottage Grove's median home cost is $277,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $48,780 (21%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $60,000 (27%) higher, and it is about $155,400 (127%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Cottage Grove the median monthly rent is $1,232. It is about $267 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $170 (16%) higher, and it is approximately $374 (43%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
