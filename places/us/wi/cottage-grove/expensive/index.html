Home prices

The value of the median home in Cottage Grove is $277,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $48,780 (21%) higher. It is about $60,000 (27%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $155,400 (127%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cottage Grove is $1,232. It is about $267 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $170 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $374 (43%) higher.
