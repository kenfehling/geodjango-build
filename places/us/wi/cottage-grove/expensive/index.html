Home prices

Rents

In Cottage Grove the median rent is $1,232 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $267 (27%) higher.It is about $170 (16%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $374 (43%) higher.
