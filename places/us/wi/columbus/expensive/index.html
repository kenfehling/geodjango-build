In comparison to its surrounding areas, Columbus is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus costs $165,400. It is about $35,400 (27%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $52,100 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $43,300 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbus is $810. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $65 (7%) lower.It is about $252 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $48 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
