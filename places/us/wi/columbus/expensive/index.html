Compared to nearby areas, Columbus is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Columbus is $165,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $35,400 (27%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $52,100 (23%) lower, and is approximately $43,300 (35%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $810 per month. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $252 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $48 (5%) lower.
