Compared to its nearby areas, Columbus is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Columbus is $165,400. This is approximately $35,400 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $52,100 (23%) lower, and is about $43,300 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $810 a month. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $252 (23%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $48 (5%) lower.
