Compared to its surrounding areas, Columbus is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Columbus's median home cost is $165,400. This is about $35,400 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $52,100 (23%) lower, and it is about $43,300 (35%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $810. This is roughly $65 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $252 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $48 (5%) lower.
