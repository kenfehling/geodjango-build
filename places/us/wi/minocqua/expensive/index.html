Home prices

The price of the median home in Minocqua is $142,500. It is roughly $10,880 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $75,000 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $20,600 (16%) higher compared to the city of Wausau.

Rents

The median rent in Minocqua is $650 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $24 (3%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $412 (38%) lower, and is approximately $58 (8%) lower compared to the city of Wausau.
