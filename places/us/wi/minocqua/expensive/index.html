In comparison to the surrounding areas, Minocqua is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Wausau, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Minocqua is $142,500. It is about $10,880 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $75,000 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $20,600 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

In Minocqua the median rent is $650 a month. It is roughly $24 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $412 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wausau is about $58 (8%) lower.
