Home prices

The median home in Minocqua costs $142,500. This is roughly $10,880 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $75,000 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $20,600 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

In Minocqua the median monthly rent is $650. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $24 (3%) higher.It is roughly $412 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wausau is roughly $58 (8%) lower.
