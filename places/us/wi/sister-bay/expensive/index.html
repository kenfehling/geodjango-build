Home prices

The price of the median home in Sister Bay is $264,200. This is approximately $33,580 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $46,700 (21%) higher, and it is about $128,300 (94%) higher in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

Sister Bay's median monthly rent is $899. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $130 (16%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $163 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Green Bay is roughly $169 (23%) higher.
