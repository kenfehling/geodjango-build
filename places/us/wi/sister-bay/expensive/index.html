Home prices

Rents

In Sister Bay the median rent is $899 per month. This is about $130 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $163 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay is roughly $169 (23%) higher.
