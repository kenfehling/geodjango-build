In comparison to its nearby areas, Cleveland is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Cleveland's median home price is $133,300. This is approximately $22,720 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $84,200 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $11,200 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $748 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is is very similar. It is roughly $314 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $110 (12%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
