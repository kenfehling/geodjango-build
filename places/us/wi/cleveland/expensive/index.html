In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cleveland is $133,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $22,720 (14%) lower.It is about $84,200 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $11,200 (9%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $748. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar.It is about $314 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $110 (12%) lower.
