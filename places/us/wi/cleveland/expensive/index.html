Compared to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cleveland is $133,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $22,720 (14%) lower.It is approximately $84,200 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $11,200 (9%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $748 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $314 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $110 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
