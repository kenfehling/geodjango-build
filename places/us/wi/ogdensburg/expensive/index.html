The median home in Ogdensburg is valued at $85,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $21,040 (19%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $132,100 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is approximately $160,900 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Ogdensburg the median rent is $592 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $126 (17%) lower. It is roughly $470 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $526 (47%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
