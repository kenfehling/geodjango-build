Home prices

The median home in Holmen is valued at $197,600. It is about $34,160 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $19,900 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $2,500 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Holmen is $902. This is roughly $154 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $160 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $72 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
