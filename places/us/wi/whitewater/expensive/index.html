Home prices

The median home in Whitewater costs $168,700. This is about $52,060 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $48,800 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $89,300 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Whitewater the median rent is $774 per month. It is about $209 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $288 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $338 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
