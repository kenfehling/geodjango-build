In comparison to its surrounding areas, Waukesha is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Waukesha is $212,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $69,260 (24%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $4,800 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $45,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Waukesha the median rent is $944 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $296 (23%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $118 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $168 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
