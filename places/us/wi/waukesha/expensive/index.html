Compared to other places in this area, Waukesha is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Waukesha's median home cost is $212,700. This is approximately $69,260 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $4,800 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $45,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Waukesha the median monthly rent is $944. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $296 (23%) lower. It is roughly $118 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $168 (15%) lower.
