Home prices

The median home value in Waukesha is $212,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $69,260 (24%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $4,800 (2%) lower, and is about $45,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waukesha is $944. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $296 (23%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $118 (11%) lower, and is roughly $168 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
