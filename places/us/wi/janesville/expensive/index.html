In comparison to other places in this area, Janesville is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Janesville is $142,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $16,140 (12%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $75,000 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $115,500 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Janesville the median rent is $858 a month. It is roughly $83 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $204 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $254 (22%) lower.
