Rents

In Verona the median monthly rent is $1,168. It is approximately $35 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $106 (9%) higher, and is roughly $50 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Madison.
