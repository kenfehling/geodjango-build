Home prices

The median home value in Verona is $310,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $42,360 (11%) lower. It is about $93,200 (42%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $64,400 (26%) higher compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Verona the median rent is $1,168 per month. It is roughly $35 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $106 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is roughly $50 (4%) higher.
