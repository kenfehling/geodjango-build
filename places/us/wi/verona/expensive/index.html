Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Verona is $1,168 per month. This is approximately $35 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $106 (9%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Madison is about $50 (4%) higher.
