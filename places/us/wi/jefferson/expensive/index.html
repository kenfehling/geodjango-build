Home prices

Rents

In Jefferson the median rent is $811 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $152 (15%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $251 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $47 (5%) lower.
