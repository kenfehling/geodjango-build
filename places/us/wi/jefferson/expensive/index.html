Rents

In Jefferson the median monthly rent is $811. This is approximately $152 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $251 (23%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $47 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
