Home prices

Jefferson's median home price is $162,500. This is approximately $43,580 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $55,000 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $40,400 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jefferson is $811. This is roughly $152 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $251 (23%) lower, and is about $47 (5%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
