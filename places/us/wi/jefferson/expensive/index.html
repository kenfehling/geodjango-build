Home prices

The value of the median home in Jefferson is $162,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $43,580 (21%) lower. It is approximately $55,000 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $40,400 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Jefferson's median monthly rent is $811. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $152 (15%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $251 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $47 (5%) lower.
