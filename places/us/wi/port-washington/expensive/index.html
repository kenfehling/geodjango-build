Home prices

The median home in Port Washington costs $197,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $30,840 (13%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $19,600 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $75,800 (62%) higher.

Rents

Port Washington's median rent is $890 a month. It is approximately $35 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $172 (16%) lower, and is approximately $32 (3%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
