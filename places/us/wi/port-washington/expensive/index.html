The cost of the median home in Port Washington is $197,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $30,840 (13%) lower. It is roughly $19,600 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $75,800 (62%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Port Washington is $890 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $35 (4%) higher. It is approximately $172 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $32 (3%) higher.
