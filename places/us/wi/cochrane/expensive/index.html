Home prices

The median home in Cochrane is valued at $109,600. It is about $30,780 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $107,900 (49%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $90,500 (45%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cochrane is $394. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $476 (54%) lower. It is approximately $668 (62%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $580 (59%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
