Home prices

The value of the median home in Cochrane is $109,600. This is roughly $30,780 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $107,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $90,500 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Cochrane's median monthly rent is $394. This is roughly $476 (54%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $668 (62%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $580 (59%) lower.
