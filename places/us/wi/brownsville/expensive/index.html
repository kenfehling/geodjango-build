Home prices

The median home price in Brownsville is $173,700. It is about $34,760 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $43,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $51,600 (42%) higher.

Rents

In Brownsville the median monthly rent is $825. This is approximately $110 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $237 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $33 (3%) lower.
