Compared to nearby areas, Brownsville is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brownsville is $173,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $34,760 (25%) higher. It is about $43,800 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $51,600 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownsville is $825. This is about $110 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $33 (3%) lower.
