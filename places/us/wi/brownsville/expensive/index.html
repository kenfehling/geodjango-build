Home prices

The median home in Brownsville is valued at $173,700. This is roughly $34,760 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $43,800 (20%) lower, and is approximately $51,600 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Brownsville is $825 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $110 (15%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $237 (22%) lower, and it is about $33 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
