Home prices

The median home price in Hager City is $161,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $2,560 (1%) lower. It is about $56,200 (25%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $90,300 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Hager City the median monthly rent is $738. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $133 (15%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $324 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $289 (28%) lower.
