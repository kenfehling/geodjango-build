In comparison to other places in this area, Downing is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Downing is valued at $122,500. It is about $10,440 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $95,000 (43%) lower, and is about $129,100 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Downing is $1,125. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $345 (44%) higher. It is about $63 (5%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $98 (9%) higher.
