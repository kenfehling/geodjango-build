Compared to nearby areas, Oshkosh is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukeehowever, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Oshkosh is valued at $125,000. It is approximately $48,600 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $2,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

Oshkosh's median monthly rent is $752. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $23 (3%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $310 (29%) lower, and it is about $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
