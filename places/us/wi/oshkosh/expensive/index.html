Compared to nearby areas, Oshkosh is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Milwaukeehowever, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Oshkosh is valued at $125,000. This is roughly $48,600 (27%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $92,500 (42%) lower, and is about $2,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Oshkosh the median rent is $752 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $23 (3%) higher. It is approximately $310 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
