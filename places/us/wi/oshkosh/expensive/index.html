In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oshkosh is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Oshkosh is $125,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $48,600 (27%) lower.It is about $92,500 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $2,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Oshkosh the median monthly rent is $752. It is about $23 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $310 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $106 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
