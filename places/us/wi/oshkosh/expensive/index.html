In comparison to its nearby areas, Oshkosh is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Oshkosh is $125,000. It is roughly $48,600 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $2,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oshkosh is $752 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $23 (3%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $310 (29%) lower, and is roughly $106 (12%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
