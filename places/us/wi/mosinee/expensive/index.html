Home prices

The price of the median home in Mosinee is $122,100. This is approximately $31,180 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $95,400 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $2,900 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.

Rents

In Mosinee the median rent is $707 a month. It is about $144 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Oshkosh it is about $45 (5%) lower.
