Compared to its surrounding areas, Cedarburg is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Cedarburg is $303,500. This is approximately $33,940 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $86,000 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $45,500 (17%) higher.

Rents

Cedarburg's median rent is $860 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $213 (19%) lower. It is roughly $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $252 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
