Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cedarburg is $303,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $33,940 (12%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $86,000 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $45,500 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Cedarburg the median rent is $860 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $213 (19%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $202 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $252 (22%) lower.
