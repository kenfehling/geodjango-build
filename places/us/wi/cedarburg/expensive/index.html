Home prices

The median home in Cedarburg is valued at $303,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $33,940 (12%) higher. It is about $86,000 (39%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $45,500 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cedarburg is $860 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $213 (19%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $202 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $252 (22%) lower.
