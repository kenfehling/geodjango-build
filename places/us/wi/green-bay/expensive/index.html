Compared to other places in this area, Green Bay is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Green Bay is $135,900. It is roughly $45,740 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $81,600 (37%) lower, and is about $13,800 (11%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Green Bay is $730. This is approximately $115 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $332 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $128 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
