Compared to its surrounding areas, Green Bay is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Green Bay is $135,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $45,740 (25%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $81,600 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $13,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Green Bay the median monthly rent is $730. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $115 (13%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $332 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $128 (14%) lower.
