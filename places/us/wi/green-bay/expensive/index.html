Compared to the surrounding areas, Green Bay is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home cost in Green Bay is $135,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $45,740 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $81,600 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $13,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Green Bay is $730 per month. It is approximately $115 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $128 (14%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
