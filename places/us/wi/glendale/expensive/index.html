Home prices

The median home in Glendale costs $220,900. This is approximately $151,320 (40%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $3,400 (1%) higher, and it is about $37,100 (14%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Glendale the median rent is $1,075 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $86 (7%) lower. It is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $37 (3%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
