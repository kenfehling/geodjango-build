Home prices

The median home price in Glendale is $220,900. This is approximately $151,320 (40%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $3,400 (1%) higher, and it is roughly $37,100 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Glendale the median rent is $1,075 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $86 (7%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $13 (1%) higher, and is approximately $37 (3%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
