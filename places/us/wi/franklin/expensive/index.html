Home prices

Franklin's median home price is $251,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $22,700 (9%) higher. It is roughly $33,900 (15%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $6,600 (2%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Franklin is $1,066. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $21 (2%) higher. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $46 (4%) lower.
