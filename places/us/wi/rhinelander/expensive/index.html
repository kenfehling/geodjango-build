Compared to other places in this area, Rhinelander is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Wausau.

Home prices

The median home price in Rhinelander is $90,600. This is approximately $47,060 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $126,900 (58%) lower, and it is roughly $31,300 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

Rhinelander's median rent is $799 per month. This is roughly $93 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $263 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $91 (12%) higher compared to the city of Wausau.
