Home prices

The median home in Horicon is valued at $136,600. This is about $18,180 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $80,900 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $14,500 (11%) higher.

Rents

Horicon's median rent is $861 a month. This is about $82 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $201 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is is very similar.
