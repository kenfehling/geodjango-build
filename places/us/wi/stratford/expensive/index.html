Home prices

The median home in Stratford is valued at $137,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $8,140 (6%) higher. It is roughly $80,400 (36%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Wausau is about $15,200 (12%) higher.

Rents

Stratford's median monthly rent is $545. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $79 (12%) lower. It is about $517 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $163 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Wausau.
