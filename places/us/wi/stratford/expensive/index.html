Home prices

The median home in Stratford costs $137,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $8,140 (6%) higher.It is approximately $80,400 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $15,200 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

Stratford's median rent is $545 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $79 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $517 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wausau it is roughly $163 (23%) lower.
