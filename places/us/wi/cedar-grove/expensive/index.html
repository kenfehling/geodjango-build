Home prices

The price of the median home in Cedar Grove is $162,000. It is approximately $1,940 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $55,500 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $39,900 (32%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cedar Grove is $757 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $106 (12%) lower. It is roughly $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $101 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
