Compared to the surrounding areas, Caledonia is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Caledonia is $208,600. It is roughly $38,020 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $8,900 (4%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $49,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

Caledonia's median rent is $917 per month. This is roughly $26 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $145 (13%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $195 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
