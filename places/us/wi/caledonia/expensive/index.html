Compared to its surrounding areas, Caledonia is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Caledonia is valued at $208,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $38,020 (22%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $8,900 (4%) lower, and is about $49,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Caledonia is $917 a month. This is approximately $26 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $145 (13%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $195 (17%) lower.
