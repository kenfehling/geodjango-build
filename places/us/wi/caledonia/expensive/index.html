In comparison to its surrounding areas, Caledonia is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Caledonia is $208,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $38,020 (22%) higher.It is approximately $8,900 (4%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $49,400 (19%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Caledonia is $917. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $26 (2%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $145 (13%) lower, and it is roughly $195 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
