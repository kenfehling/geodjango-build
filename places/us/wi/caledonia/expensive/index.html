Home prices

The value of the median home in Caledonia is $208,600. It is about $38,020 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $8,900 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $49,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Caledonia is $917. It is approximately $26 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $145 (13%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $195 (17%) lower.
