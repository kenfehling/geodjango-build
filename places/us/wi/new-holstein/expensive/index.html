In comparison to other places in this area, New Holstein is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in New Holstein is $118,600. This is approximately $20,080 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $98,900 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $3,500 (2%) lower.

Rents

New Holstein's median monthly rent is $723. This is roughly $49 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $135 (15%) lower.
