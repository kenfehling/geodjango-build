Home prices

The median home price in New Holstein is $118,600. It is about $20,080 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $98,900 (45%) lower, and is roughly $3,500 (2%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In New Holstein the median monthly rent is $723. Compared to other places in this area it is about $49 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $339 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $135 (15%) lower.
