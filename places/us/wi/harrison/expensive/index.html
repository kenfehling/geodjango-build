Home prices

The median home value in Harrison is $223,300. It is about $54,120 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $5,800 (2%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $101,200 (82%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Harrison is $1,125 per month. It is roughly $316 (39%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $63 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $267 (31%) higher.
