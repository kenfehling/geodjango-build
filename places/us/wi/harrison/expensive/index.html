Home prices

The median home in Harrison is valued at $223,300. It is approximately $54,120 (31%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $5,800 (2%) higher, and it is roughly $101,200 (82%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Harrison is $1,125 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $316 (39%) higher. It is about $63 (5%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $267 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
