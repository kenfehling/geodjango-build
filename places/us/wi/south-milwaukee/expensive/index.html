Home prices

South Milwaukee's median home cost is $162,000. It is about $38,860 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $55,500 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $96,000 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Milwaukee is $838. This is roughly $92 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $224 (21%) lower, and it is about $274 (24%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
