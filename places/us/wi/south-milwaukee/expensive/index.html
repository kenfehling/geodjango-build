Compared to the surrounding areas, South Milwaukee is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in South Milwaukee is $162,000. This is roughly $38,860 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $55,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $96,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In South Milwaukee the median monthly rent is $838. Compared to other places in this area it is about $92 (9%) lower. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $274 (24%) lower.
