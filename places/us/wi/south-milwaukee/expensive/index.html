Home prices

The median home price in South Milwaukee is $162,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $38,860 (19%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $55,500 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $96,000 (37%) lower.

Rents

South Milwaukee's median monthly rent is $838. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $92 (9%) lower.It is roughly $224 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $274 (24%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
