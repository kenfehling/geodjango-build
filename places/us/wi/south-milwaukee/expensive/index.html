In comparison to other places in this area, South Milwaukee is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in South Milwaukee costs $162,000. This is about $38,860 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $55,500 (25%) lower, and is about $96,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In South Milwaukee the median rent is $838 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $92 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $224 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $274 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
