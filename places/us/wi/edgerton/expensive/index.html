Home prices

The cost of the median home in Edgerton is $158,200. This is approximately $31,620 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $59,300 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $36,100 (29%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Edgerton is $777 a month. It is roughly $97 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and it is about $81 (9%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
