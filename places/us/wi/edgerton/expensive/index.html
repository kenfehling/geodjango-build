Home prices

The median home in Edgerton is valued at $158,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $31,620 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $59,300 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $36,100 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Edgerton the median monthly rent is $777. It is approximately $97 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and is roughly $81 (9%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
