Home prices

The median home value in Burlington is $185,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $14,900 (7%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $32,200 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $72,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

Burlington's median monthly rent is $846. This is roughly $177 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $216 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $266 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
