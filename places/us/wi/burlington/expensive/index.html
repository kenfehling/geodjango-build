Home prices

The median home in Burlington costs $185,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,900 (7%) lower. It is roughly $32,200 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $72,700 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Burlington is $846 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $177 (17%) lower. It is roughly $216 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $266 (23%) lower.
