Home prices

The price of the median home in Burlington is $185,300. It is approximately $14,900 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $32,200 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $72,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Burlington is $846 a month. It is about $177 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $216 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $266 (23%) lower.
