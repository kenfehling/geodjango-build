In comparison to its surrounding areas, Roberts is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home price in Roberts is $170,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $31,580 (15%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $46,600 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $80,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Roberts's median rent is $965 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $42 (4%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $97 (9%) lower, and is about $62 (6%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
