Home prices

The median home in Oak Creek is valued at $231,200. This is approximately $32,860 (16%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $13,700 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $26,800 (10%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oak Creek is $1,063. It is about $145 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
