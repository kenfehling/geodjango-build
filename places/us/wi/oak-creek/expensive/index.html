Home prices

The median home value in Oak Creek is $231,200. This is about $32,860 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $13,700 (6%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $26,800 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Oak Creek's median rent is $1,063 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $145 (15%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is is very similar, and is roughly $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
