Home prices

Allouez's median home value is $159,200. It is approximately $29,440 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $58,300 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $37,100 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Allouez is $819. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is about $243 (22%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $39 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
