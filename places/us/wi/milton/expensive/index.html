In comparison to its surrounding areas, Milton is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Milton is $149,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $29,000 (16%) lower. It is about $67,700 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $108,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Milton's median monthly rent is $787. It is about $49 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $275 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $325 (29%) lower.
