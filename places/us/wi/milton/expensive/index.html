Home prices

Milton's median home price is $149,800. This is roughly $29,000 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $67,700 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $108,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milton is $787. This is roughly $49 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $275 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $325 (29%) lower.
