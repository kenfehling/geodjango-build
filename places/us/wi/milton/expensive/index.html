Home prices

The median home in Milton is valued at $149,800. This is approximately $29,000 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $67,700 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $108,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

Milton's median rent is $787 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $49 (5%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $275 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $325 (29%) lower.
