Home prices

The median home in Amherst is valued at $136,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $60,720 (30%) lower.It is roughly $81,200 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $110,000 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

Amherst's median rent is $720 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $26 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $342 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is approximately $398 (35%) lower.
