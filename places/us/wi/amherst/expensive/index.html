Home prices

The median home cost in Amherst is $136,300. It is about $60,720 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $81,200 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $110,000 (44%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

Amherst's median rent is $720 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $26 (3%) higher. It is about $342 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $398 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
