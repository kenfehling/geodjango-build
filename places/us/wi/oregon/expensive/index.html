Compared to nearby areas, Oregon is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Madison however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Oregon is $256,500. This is roughly $26,480 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $39,000 (17%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $10,200 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

Oregon's median monthly rent is $879. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $163 (15%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $183 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is approximately $239 (21%) lower.
