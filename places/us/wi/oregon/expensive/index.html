Compared to its nearby areas, Oregon is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home price in Oregon is $256,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $26,480 (11%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $39,000 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Madison it is approximately $10,200 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oregon is $879 a month. This is roughly $163 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $239 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
