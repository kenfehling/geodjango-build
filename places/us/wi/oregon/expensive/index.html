In comparison to its nearby areas, Oregon is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home in Oregon is valued at $256,500. This is about $26,480 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $39,000 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Madison it is roughly $10,200 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oregon is $879 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $163 (15%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and it is about $239 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
