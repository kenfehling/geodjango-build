In comparison to its nearby areas, Oregon is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Madison, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Oregon is valued at $256,500. It is approximately $26,480 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $39,000 (17%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $10,200 (4%) higher compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Oregon the median rent is $879 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $163 (15%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is about $239 (21%) lower.
