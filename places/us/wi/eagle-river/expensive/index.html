Home prices

The median home in Eagle River costs $116,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $47,220 (28%) lower. It is about $101,400 (46%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $5,800 (4%) lower compared to the city of Wausau.

Rents

Eagle River's median rent is $684 a month. This is roughly $28 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $378 (35%) lower, and is approximately $24 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Wausau.
