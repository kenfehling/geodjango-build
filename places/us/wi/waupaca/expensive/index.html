The median home value in Waupaca is $104,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $70,400 (40%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $112,600 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is roughly $141,400 (57%) lower.

Rents

Waupaca's median rent is $647 per month. It is about $109 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $415 (39%) lower, and is approximately $471 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
