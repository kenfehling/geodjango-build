Home prices

The median home in Friesland is valued at $116,300. This is about $6,060 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $101,200 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $5,800 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Friesland's median monthly rent is $850. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $39 (4%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $212 (19%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Milwaukee.
