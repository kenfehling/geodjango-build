Home prices

The median home cost in Warrens is $117,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $6,300 (5%) higher.It is about $99,600 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire it is roughly $37,100 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Warrens is $1,013. Compared to other places in this area it is about $296 (41%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $49 (4%) lower, and it is approximately $178 (21%) higher compared to the city of Eau Claire.
