Home prices

The median home in Warrens costs $117,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $6,300 (5%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $99,600 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is approximately $37,100 (23%) lower.

Rents

In Warrens the median rent is $1,013 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $296 (41%) higher. It is about $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $178 (21%) higher compared to the city of Eau Claire.
