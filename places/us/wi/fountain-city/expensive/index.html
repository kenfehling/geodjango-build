Home prices

Fountain City's median home price is $155,100. This is about $8,980 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $62,400 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $45,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Fountain City the median monthly rent is $947. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $197 (26%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $115 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $27 (2%) lower.
