Home prices

Cuba City's median home cost is $150,300. It is about $23,740 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $67,200 (30%) lower, and is about $96,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cuba City is $559. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $135 (19%) lower. It is about $503 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Madison is roughly $559 (50%) lower.
