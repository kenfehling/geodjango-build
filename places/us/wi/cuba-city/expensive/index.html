Compared to nearby areas, Cuba City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Madison, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cuba City is $150,300. This is about $23,740 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $67,200 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $96,000 (38%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Cuba City the median rent is $559 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $135 (19%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $503 (47%) lower, and it is roughly $559 (50%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
