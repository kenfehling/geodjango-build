Home prices

The value of the median home in Cuba City is $150,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $23,740 (18%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $67,200 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is roughly $96,000 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Cuba City the median monthly rent is $559. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $135 (19%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $503 (47%) lower, and is roughly $559 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
