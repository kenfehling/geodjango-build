Home prices

The median home in Tennyson costs $128,000. This is about $45,780 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $89,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is roughly $118,300 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tennyson is $713 per month. It is roughly $61 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $349 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is about $405 (36%) lower.
