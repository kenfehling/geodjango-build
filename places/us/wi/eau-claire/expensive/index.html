Compared to other places in this area, Eau Claire is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Rochester, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Eau Claire is valued at $155,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $8,660 (5%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $45,100 (22%) lower.

Rents

Eau Claire's median rent is $835 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $10 (1%) higher. It is about $227 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $139 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
