Compared to the surrounding areas, Eau Claire is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home price in Eau Claire is $155,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $8,660 (5%) higher. It is about $62,500 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $45,100 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Eau Claire the median monthly rent is $835. This is roughly $10 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $227 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $139 (14%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
