Home prices

The median home in Eau Claire is valued at $155,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $8,660 (5%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower, and is about $45,100 (22%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Eau Claire's median rent is $835 a month. This is approximately $10 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $227 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $139 (14%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
