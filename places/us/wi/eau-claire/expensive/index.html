Home prices

Eau Claire's median home cost is $155,000. This is approximately $8,660 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $62,500 (28%) lower, and is approximately $45,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Eau Claire is $835. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $227 (21%) lower, and is about $139 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
