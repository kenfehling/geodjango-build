In comparison to other places in this area, Cambridge is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home price in Cambridge is $208,300. It is approximately $15,640 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $9,200 (4%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $86,200 (70%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Cambridge the median rent is $886 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $45 (4%) lower.It is about $176 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $28 (3%) higher.
