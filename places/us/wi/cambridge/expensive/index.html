Home prices

The median home price in Cambridge is $208,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $15,640 (6%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $9,200 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $86,200 (70%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cambridge is $886. This is roughly $45 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $176 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $28 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
