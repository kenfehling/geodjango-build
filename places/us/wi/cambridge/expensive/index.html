In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cambridge is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cambridge is $208,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,640 (6%) lower. It is roughly $9,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $86,200 (70%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Cambridge the median monthly rent is $886. It is roughly $45 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $176 (16%) lower, and is about $28 (3%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
