Compared to other places in this area, Cambridge is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cambridge is valued at $208,300. This is about $15,640 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $9,200 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $86,200 (70%) higher.

Rents

In Cambridge the median rent is $886 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $45 (4%) lower. It is approximately $176 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $28 (3%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
