Compared to nearby areas, Cambridge is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cambridge is $208,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $15,640 (6%) lower. It is approximately $9,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $86,200 (70%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Cambridge's median rent is $886 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $45 (4%) lower. It is roughly $176 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $28 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
