Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brookfield is $318,800. This is approximately $71,620 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $101,300 (46%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $60,800 (23%) higher.

Rents

Brookfield's median monthly rent is $1,526. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $519 (51%) higher. It is approximately $464 (43%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $414 (37%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
