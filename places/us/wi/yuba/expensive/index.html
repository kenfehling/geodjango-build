Home prices

The median home in Yuba costs $108,300. This is roughly $20,560 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $109,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $138,000 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Yuba is $613 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $43 (6%) lower. It is about $449 (42%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is roughly $505 (45%) lower.
