Home prices

The price of the median home in Wittenberg is $95,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $10,180 (12%) higher. It is roughly $122,500 (56%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $40,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

Wittenberg's median rent is $613 per month. This is approximately $44 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $449 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay it is approximately $117 (16%) lower.
