Rents

Winneconne's median rent is $659 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $86 (11%) lower.It is roughly $403 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $199 (23%) lower.
