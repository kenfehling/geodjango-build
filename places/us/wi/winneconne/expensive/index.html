In comparison to its surrounding areas, Winneconne is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Winneconne is $155,900. It is roughly $16,520 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $61,600 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $33,800 (27%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Winneconne is $659 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $86 (11%) lower. It is approximately $403 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $199 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
