Home prices

The median home price in Eastman is $112,500. It is approximately $6,380 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $105,000 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $29,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

Eastman's median rent is $558 per month. It is approximately $113 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $504 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $209 (27%) lower.
