Downsville's median home value is $130,400. It is about $12,360 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $87,100 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $121,200 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Downsville the median rent is $659 per month. This is about $43 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $403 (37%) lower, and is about $368 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
