Home prices

The median home in Downsville is valued at $130,400. It is approximately $12,360 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $87,100 (40%) lower, and is approximately $121,200 (48%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Downsville the median monthly rent is $659. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $43 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $403 (37%) lower, and is roughly $368 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
