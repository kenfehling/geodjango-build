Home prices

The median home in Neosho is valued at $169,900. It is about $7,600 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $47,600 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $47,800 (39%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Neosho is $642 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $209 (24%) lower. It is about $420 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $216 (25%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
