The median home in Spencer costs $112,300. This is approximately $3,420 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $105,200 (48%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $42,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Spencer the median monthly rent is $665. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $40 (6%) higher. It is roughly $397 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $170 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.
