Home prices

The median home in Spencer costs $112,300. It is approximately $3,420 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $105,200 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $42,700 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Spencer the median rent is $665 per month. This is approximately $40 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $397 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire it is about $170 (20%) lower.
