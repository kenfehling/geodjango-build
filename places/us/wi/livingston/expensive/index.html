Home prices

The price of the median home in Livingston is $112,000. It is roughly $6,160 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $105,500 (48%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is approximately $134,300 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Livingston the median monthly rent is $772. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $65 (9%) higher. It is roughly $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $346 (30%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
