In comparison to the surrounding areas, Livingston is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Madison, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Livingston is $112,000. It is about $6,160 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $105,500 (48%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is about $134,300 (54%) lower.

Rents

Livingston's median rent is $772 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $65 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $290 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is roughly $346 (30%) lower.
