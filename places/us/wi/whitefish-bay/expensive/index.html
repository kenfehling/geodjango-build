Home prices

The value of the median home in Whitefish Bay is $387,000. It is roughly $57,000 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $169,500 (77%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $129,000 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Whitefish Bay is $1,243 per month. This is roughly $139 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $181 (17%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $131 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
