Compared to other places in this area, Whitefish Bay is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Whitefish Bay is valued at $387,000. This is roughly $57,000 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $169,500 (77%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $129,000 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Whitefish Bay the median monthly rent is $1,243. This is approximately $139 (12%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $181 (17%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $131 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
