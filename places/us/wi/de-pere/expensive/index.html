Much like other places in this area, it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

De Pere's median home cost is $177,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $7,480 (4%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $40,000 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $55,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

In De Pere the median monthly rent is $895. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $84 (10%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $167 (15%) lower, and is approximately $37 (4%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
