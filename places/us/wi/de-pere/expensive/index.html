Home prices

The median home in De Pere costs $177,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $7,480 (4%) lower. It is approximately $40,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $55,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

De Pere's median monthly rent is $895. This is roughly $84 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $167 (15%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $37 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
