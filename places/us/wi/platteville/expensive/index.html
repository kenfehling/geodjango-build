Home prices

The median home in Platteville costs $152,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $23,060 (17%) higher. It is about $65,100 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is roughly $93,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

Platteville's median monthly rent is $829. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $180 (27%) higher. It is roughly $233 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $289 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
