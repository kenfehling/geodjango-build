The cost of the median home in Platteville is $152,400. It is approximately $23,060 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $65,100 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is approximately $93,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

Platteville's median monthly rent is $829. It is approximately $180 (27%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $233 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $289 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
