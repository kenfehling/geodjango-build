Home prices

The median home in Platteville costs $152,400. This is about $23,060 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $65,100 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $93,900 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Platteville is $829 a month. It is approximately $180 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $233 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is approximately $289 (25%) lower.
