Home prices

The value of the median home in Newburg is $189,900. This is approximately $5,660 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $27,600 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $67,800 (55%) higher.

Rents

Newburg's median rent is $910 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $69 (8%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $152 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $52 (6%) higher.
