Home prices

Newburg's median home value is $189,900. It is about $5,660 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $27,600 (12%) lower, and is roughly $67,800 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newburg is $910. This is roughly $69 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $152 (14%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $52 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
