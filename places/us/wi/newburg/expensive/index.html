Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newburg is $189,900. It is approximately $5,660 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $27,600 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $67,800 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newburg is $910. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $69 (8%) higher. It is roughly $152 (14%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $52 (6%) higher.
