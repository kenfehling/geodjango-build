Home prices

The median home in Chetek costs $116,100. This is approximately $8,140 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $101,400 (46%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $38,900 (25%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

The median rent in Chetek is $713 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $10 (1%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is approximately $122 (14%) lower.
