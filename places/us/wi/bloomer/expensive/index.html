Home prices

The median home in Bloomer costs $136,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $3,760 (2%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $80,600 (37%) lower, and is approximately $18,100 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomer is $531. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $224 (29%) lower. It is about $531 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is approximately $304 (36%) lower.
