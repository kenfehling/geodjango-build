Home prices

Rents

In Bloomer the median rent is $531 a month. This is about $224 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $531 (50%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is approximately $304 (36%) lower.
