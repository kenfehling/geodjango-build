Home prices

The median home in Bloomer is valued at $136,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $3,760 (2%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $80,600 (37%) lower, and it is roughly $18,100 (11%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Bloomer the median monthly rent is $531. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $224 (29%) lower. It is approximately $531 (50%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is about $304 (36%) lower.
