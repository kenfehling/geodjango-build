The median home in Beloit is valued at $89,900. This is approximately $52,320 (36%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $127,600 (58%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $168,100 (65%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents
