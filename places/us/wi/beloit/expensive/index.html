Home prices

The median home in Beloit is valued at $89,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $52,320 (36%) lower. It is roughly $127,600 (58%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $168,100 (65%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Beloit is $800 a month. This is roughly $61 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $262 (24%) lower, and is approximately $312 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
