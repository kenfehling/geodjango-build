Home prices

The median home in Kiel is valued at $121,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $19,600 (13%) lower. It is approximately $95,700 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Kiel the median monthly rent is $724. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $51 (7%) higher. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $134 (15%) lower.
