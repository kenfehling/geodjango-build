Home prices

The median home value in Kiel is $121,800. This is about $19,600 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $95,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Kiel's median rent is $724 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $51 (7%) higher. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $134 (15%) lower.
