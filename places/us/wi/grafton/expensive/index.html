The median home in Grafton is valued at $234,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $35,260 (13%) lower. It is roughly $17,300 (7%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $23,200 (8%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents
