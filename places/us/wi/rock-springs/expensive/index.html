The median home price in Rock Springs is $121,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $15,860 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $96,200 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is roughly $125,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

Rock Springs's median rent is $835 a month. It is roughly $63 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $227 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $283 (25%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
