Home prices

Park Ridge's median home cost is $186,400. It is approximately $31,480 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $31,100 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oshkosh is about $61,400 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Park Ridge is $800. It is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $262 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $48 (6%) higher compared to the city of Oshkosh.
