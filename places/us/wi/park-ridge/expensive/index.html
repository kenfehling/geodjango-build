Home prices

The median home price in Park Ridge is $186,400. This is about $31,480 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $31,100 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $61,400 (49%) higher compared to the city of Oshkosh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Park Ridge is $800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $262 (24%) lower, and it is about $48 (6%) higher compared to the city of Oshkosh.
