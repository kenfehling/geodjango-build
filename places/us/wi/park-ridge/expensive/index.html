Home prices

The median home in Park Ridge is valued at $186,400. It is approximately $31,480 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $31,100 (14%) lower, and is roughly $61,400 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.

Rents

Park Ridge's median rent is $800 per month. It is is very similar compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $262 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Oshkosh it is roughly $48 (6%) higher.
