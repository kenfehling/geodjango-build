Home prices

The median home value in Park Ridge is $186,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $31,480 (20%) higher. It is approximately $31,100 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $61,400 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.

Rents

Park Ridge's median rent is $800 per month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is about $262 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $48 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.
