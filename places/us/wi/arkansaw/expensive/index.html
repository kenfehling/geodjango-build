Home prices

The median home in Arkansaw is valued at $88,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $34,420 (28%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $129,200 (59%) lower, and it is roughly $163,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Arkansaw's median rent is $674 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $25 (3%) higher. It is approximately $388 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $353 (34%) lower.
