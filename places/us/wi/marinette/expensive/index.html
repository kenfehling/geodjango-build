Compared to other places in this area, Marinette is significantly less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Green Bay, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Marinette's median home price is $86,400. This is roughly $76,100 (46%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $131,100 (60%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $49,500 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marinette is $699. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $64 (10%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $363 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Green Bay it is about $31 (4%) lower.
