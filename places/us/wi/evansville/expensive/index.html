Compared to other places in this area, Evansville is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Evansville's median home value is $187,000. It is roughly $32,380 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $30,500 (14%) lower, and is about $64,900 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Evansville the median monthly rent is $713. This is about $205 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $349 (32%) lower, and it is about $145 (16%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
