In comparison to the surrounding areas, Evansville is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Evansville is valued at $187,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $32,380 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $30,500 (14%) lower, and it is roughly $64,900 (53%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Evansville's median monthly rent is $713. It is approximately $205 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and it is about $145 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
