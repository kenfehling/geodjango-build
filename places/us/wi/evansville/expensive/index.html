In comparison to its surrounding areas, Evansville is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Evansville is valued at $187,000. This is roughly $32,380 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $30,500 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $64,900 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Evansville the median monthly rent is $713. This is roughly $205 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $349 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $145 (16%) lower.
