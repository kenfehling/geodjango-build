Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Francis is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Francis is $152,000. It is roughly $17,400 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $65,500 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $106,000 (41%) lower.

Rents

In St. Francis the median rent is $751 a month. It is about $112 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $311 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $361 (32%) lower.
