Home prices

Rents

The median rent in St. Francis is $751 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $112 (13%) lower.It is about $311 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $361 (32%) lower.
