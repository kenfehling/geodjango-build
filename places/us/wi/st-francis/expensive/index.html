Home prices

St. Francis's median home price is $152,000. This is roughly $17,400 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $65,500 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $106,000 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In St. Francis the median monthly rent is $751. This is roughly $112 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $311 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $361 (32%) lower.
