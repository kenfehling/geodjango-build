Home prices

The median home in New Auburn costs $114,800. It is roughly $9,360 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $102,700 (47%) lower, and is approximately $40,200 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Auburn is $825. It is about $181 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is about $10 (1%) lower.
