Home prices

The value of the median home in New Auburn is $114,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $9,360 (8%) higher.It is approximately $102,700 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $40,200 (25%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

The median rent in New Auburn is $825 a month. It is about $181 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
