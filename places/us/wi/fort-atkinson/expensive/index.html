In comparison to nearby areas, Fort Atkinson is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Fort Atkinson is $157,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $52,500 (25%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $60,500 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $101,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Atkinson the median monthly rent is $838. This is about $46 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and is approximately $274 (24%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
