In comparison to nearby areas, Fort Atkinson is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fort Atkinson is $157,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $52,500 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $60,500 (27%) lower, and is roughly $101,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Fort Atkinson the median rent is $838 a month. It is approximately $46 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $224 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $274 (24%) lower.
