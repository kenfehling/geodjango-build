In comparison to other places in this area, Kenosha is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Kenosha is $152,500. It is approximately $59,360 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $65,000 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $105,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kenosha is $908. It is roughly $83 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $204 (18%) lower.
