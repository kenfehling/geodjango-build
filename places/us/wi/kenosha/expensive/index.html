Compared to its surrounding areas, Kenosha is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kenosha is valued at $152,500. It is about $59,360 (28%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $65,000 (29%) lower, and it is about $105,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Kenosha's median monthly rent is $908. It is approximately $83 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $154 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $204 (18%) lower.
