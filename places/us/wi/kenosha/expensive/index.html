Compared to its surrounding areas, Kenosha is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Kenosha is valued at $152,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $59,360 (28%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $65,000 (29%) lower, and it is about $105,500 (40%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Kenosha's median rent is $908 a month. This is about $83 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $154 (14%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $204 (18%) lower.
