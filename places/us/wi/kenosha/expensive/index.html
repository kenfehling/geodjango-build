In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kenosha is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Kenosha is $152,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $59,360 (28%) lower. It is approximately $65,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $105,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

Kenosha's median monthly rent is $908. It is approximately $83 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $154 (14%) lower, and is roughly $204 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
