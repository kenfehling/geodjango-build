In comparison to other places in this area, Fond du Lac is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fond du Lac is $126,200. It is roughly $8,180 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $91,300 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $4,100 (3%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Fond du Lac's median monthly rent is $730. It is approximately $37 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $332 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $128 (14%) lower.
