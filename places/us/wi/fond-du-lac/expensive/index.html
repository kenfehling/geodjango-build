Compared to its nearby areas, Fond du Lac is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fond du Lac is $126,200. This is about $8,180 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $91,300 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $4,100 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Fond du Lac the median rent is $730 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $37 (5%) higher.It is about $332 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $128 (14%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
