In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fond du Lac is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Fond du Lac is $126,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $8,180 (6%) lower.It is approximately $91,300 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $4,100 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fond du Lac is $730. This is about $37 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $332 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $128 (14%) lower.
