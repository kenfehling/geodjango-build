In comparison to its surrounding areas, Abbotsford is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Eau Claire, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Abbotsford is valued at $103,200. This is approximately $8,820 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $114,300 (52%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $51,800 (33%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Abbotsford is $725. This is about $61 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and is roughly $110 (13%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.
