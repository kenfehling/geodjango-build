In comparison to its surrounding areas, Abbotsford is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Eau Claire, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Abbotsford's median home cost is $103,200. This is about $8,820 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $114,300 (52%) lower, and it is approximately $51,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

Abbotsford's median rent is $725 per month. This is roughly $61 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is approximately $110 (13%) lower.
