Home prices

The median home value in Abbotsford is $103,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $8,820 (9%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $114,300 (52%) lower, and is roughly $51,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

In Abbotsford the median rent is $725 per month. This is approximately $61 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $110 (13%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.
