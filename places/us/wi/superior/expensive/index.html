Home prices

The median home cost in Superior is $160,300. This is roughly $21,860 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $57,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $3,000 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Duluth.

Rents

The median rent in Superior is $1,128 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $157 (16%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $66 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Duluth is about $308 (37%) higher.
