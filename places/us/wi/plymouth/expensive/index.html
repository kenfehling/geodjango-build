Home prices

The price of the median home in Plymouth is $144,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $8,000 (5%) lower. It is about $72,800 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $22,600 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Plymouth the median rent is $707 a month. It is about $48 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $151 (17%) lower.
