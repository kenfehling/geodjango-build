Compared to other places in this area, Plymouth is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Plymouth is valued at $144,700. This is roughly $8,000 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $72,800 (33%) lower, and is approximately $22,600 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Plymouth the median monthly rent is $707. It is roughly $48 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $355 (33%) lower, and is roughly $151 (17%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
