Home prices

The cost of the median home in Plymouth is $144,700. This is roughly $8,000 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $72,800 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $22,600 (18%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Plymouth is $707 a month. This is roughly $48 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $355 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $151 (17%) lower.
