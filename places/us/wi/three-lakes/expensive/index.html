Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Three Lakes is $960. This is roughly $298 (45%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $102 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $252 (35%) higher compared to the city of Wausau.
