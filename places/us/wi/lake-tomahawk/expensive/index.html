Home prices

The median home price in Lake Tomahawk is $148,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $4,220 (2%) higher.It is roughly $68,600 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Wausau it is approximately $27,000 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Lake Tomahawk the median monthly rent is $761. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $96 (14%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $301 (28%) lower, and it is about $53 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Wausau.
