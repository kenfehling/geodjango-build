In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hammond is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hammond costs $148,100. This is about $20,240 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $69,400 (31%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $103,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hammond is $909. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $18 (1%) higher. It is approximately $153 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $118 (11%) lower.
