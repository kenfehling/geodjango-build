Home prices

The median home value in Forest Junction is $135,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $33,820 (19%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $82,200 (37%) lower, and it is about $13,200 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Forest Junction the median monthly rent is $584. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $247 (29%) lower.It is roughly $478 (45%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $274 (31%) lower.
