Home prices

The median home in Junction City is valued at $98,500. It is approximately $34,500 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $119,000 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oshkosh is approximately $26,500 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Junction City the median rent is $663 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $48 (6%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $399 (37%) lower, and is approximately $89 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.
