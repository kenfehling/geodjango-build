Home prices

The median home value in Elm Grove is $370,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $144,760 (64%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $153,000 (70%) higher, and it is approximately $112,500 (43%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Elm Grove's median monthly rent is $950. It is roughly $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $112 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $162 (14%) lower.
