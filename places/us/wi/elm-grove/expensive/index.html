Home prices

The median home in Elm Grove is valued at $370,500. It is about $144,760 (64%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $153,000 (70%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $112,500 (43%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Elm Grove's median rent is $950 a month. This is approximately $147 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $112 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $162 (14%) lower.
