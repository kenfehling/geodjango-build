Home prices

The median home in Elm Grove is valued at $370,500. It is about $144,760 (64%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $153,000 (70%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $112,500 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Elm Grove the median rent is $950 a month. This is approximately $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $112 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $162 (14%) lower.
