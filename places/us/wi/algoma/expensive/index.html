Home prices

The value of the median home in Algoma is $106,300. It is approximately $52,880 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $111,200 (51%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $29,600 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

Algoma's median rent is $609 a month. It is approximately $102 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $453 (42%) lower, and is approximately $121 (16%) lower compared to the city of Green Bay.
