Home prices

Auburndale's median home cost is $155,200. This is approximately $34,680 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $62,300 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $33,300 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

Auburndale's median rent is $568 a month. This is roughly $46 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $494 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wausau it is roughly $140 (19%) lower.
