The median home in Muskego costs $291,700. It is approximately $45,580 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $74,200 (34%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $33,700 (13%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Muskego the median rent is $1,096 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $34 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $16 (1%) lower.
