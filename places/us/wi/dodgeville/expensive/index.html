Home prices

The median home in Dodgeville costs $164,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $34,500 (26%) higher. It is approximately $53,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $81,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Dodgeville the median rent is $795 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $19 (2%) higher. It is about $267 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $323 (28%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
