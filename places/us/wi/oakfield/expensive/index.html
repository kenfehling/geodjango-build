The value of the median home in Oakfield is $163,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $23,620 (16%) higher. It is about $54,400 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $41,000 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oakfield is $720 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $75 (9%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $342 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $138 (16%) lower.
