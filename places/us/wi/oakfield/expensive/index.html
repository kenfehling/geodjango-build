Compared to its surrounding areas, Oakfield is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Oakfield is $163,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $23,620 (16%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $54,400 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $41,000 (33%) higher.

Rents

Oakfield's median monthly rent is $720. This is about $75 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $342 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $138 (16%) lower.
