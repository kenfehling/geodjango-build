Home prices

Rents

Oakfield's median rent is $720 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $75 (9%) lower.It is roughly $342 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $138 (16%) lower.
