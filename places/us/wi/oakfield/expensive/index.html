Home prices

The median home price in Oakfield is $163,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $23,620 (16%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $54,400 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $41,000 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Oakfield is $720 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $75 (9%) lower.It is about $342 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $138 (16%) lower.
