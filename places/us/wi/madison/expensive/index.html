Compared to nearby areas, Madison is substantially less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home value in Madison is $246,300. This is about $121,600 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $28,800 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $124,200 (101%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Madison the median monthly rent is $1,118. This is approximately $50 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $56 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $260 (30%) higher.
