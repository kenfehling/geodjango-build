In comparison to other places in this area, Madison is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home price in Madison is $246,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $118,000 (32%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $28,800 (13%) higher.

Rents

In Madison the median rent is $1,118 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $24 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $56 (5%) higher.
