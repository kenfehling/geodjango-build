Home prices

The median home price in Madison is $246,300. It is approximately $121,600 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $28,800 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $124,200 (101%) higher.

Rents

In Madison the median monthly rent is $1,118. It is about $50 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $56 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $260 (30%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
