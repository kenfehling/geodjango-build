In comparison to other places in this area, Madison is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Madison's median home price is $246,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $121,600 (33%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $28,800 (13%) higher, and is approximately $124,200 (101%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Madison is $1,118. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $50 (4%) lower.It is about $56 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $260 (30%) higher.
