Rents

The median monthly rent in Wrightstown is $988. It is approximately $215 (27%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $74 (6%) lower, and is roughly $130 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
