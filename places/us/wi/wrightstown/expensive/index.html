Home prices

Rents

Wrightstown's median rent is $988 a month. This is approximately $215 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $74 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $130 (15%) higher.
