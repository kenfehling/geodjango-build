Home prices

The median home in Wrightstown is valued at $183,200. It is approximately $27,460 (17%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $34,300 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $61,100 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Wrightstown's median monthly rent is $988. It is roughly $215 (27%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $74 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $130 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
