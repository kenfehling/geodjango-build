Home prices

The median home cost in Adams is $84,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $10,300 (10%) lower. It is approximately $133,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Madison it is approximately $161,800 (65%) lower.

Rents

Adams's median rent is $518 per month. This is approximately $187 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $544 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is about $600 (53%) lower.
