Home prices

The median home in Boulder Junction is valued at $145,800. This is approximately $13,040 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $71,700 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wausau is roughly $23,900 (19%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Boulder Junction is $620. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $63 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $442 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wausau it is roughly $88 (12%) lower.
