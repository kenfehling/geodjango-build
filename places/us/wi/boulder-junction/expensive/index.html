In comparison to other places in this area, Boulder Junction is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Wausau, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Boulder Junction costs $145,800. This is about $13,040 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $71,700 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $23,900 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

Boulder Junction's median rent is $620 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $63 (11%) higher. It is about $442 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $88 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Wausau.
