Home prices

The median home cost in Two Rivers is $94,300. This is about $25,160 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $123,200 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $27,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Two Rivers the median rent is $673 per month. This is approximately $27 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $389 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $185 (21%) lower.
