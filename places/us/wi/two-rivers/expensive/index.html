Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Two Rivers is $673. This is approximately $27 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $389 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $185 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
