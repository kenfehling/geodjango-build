Home prices

The median home in Johnson Creek costs $202,200. It is about $11,700 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $15,300 (7%) lower, and is about $80,100 (65%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Johnson Creek the median monthly rent is $994. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $81 (8%) higher. It is roughly $68 (6%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $136 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
