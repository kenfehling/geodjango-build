Home prices

The median home in Butternut is valued at $65,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $26,420 (28%) lower.It is roughly $151,800 (69%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $56,200 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Wausau.

Rents

In Butternut the median rent is $575 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $36 (6%) higher.It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wausau is roughly $133 (18%) lower.
