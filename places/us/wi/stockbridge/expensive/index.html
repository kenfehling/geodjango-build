Home prices

The value of the median home in Stockbridge is $160,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $8,920 (5%) lower. It is about $57,400 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $38,000 (31%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Stockbridge the median rent is $764 per month. This is roughly $39 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $298 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $94 (10%) lower.
