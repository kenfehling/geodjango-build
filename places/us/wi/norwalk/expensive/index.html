The median home in Norwalk is valued at $87,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $26,240 (23%) lower. It is about $130,500 (60%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $159,300 (64%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

Norwalk's median rent is $650 per month. It is roughly $78 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $412 (38%) lower, and is approximately $468 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
