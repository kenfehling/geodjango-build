In comparison to the surrounding areas, Manitowoc is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manitowoc is valued at $107,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $10,780 (9%) lower.It is approximately $110,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $15,000 (12%) lower.

Rents

Manitowoc's median rent is $665 a month. It is roughly $38 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $397 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $193 (22%) lower.
