Compared to its nearby areas, Manitowoc is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home cost in Manitowoc is $107,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $10,780 (9%) lower.It is about $110,400 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $15,000 (12%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Manitowoc the median rent is $665 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $38 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $397 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $193 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
