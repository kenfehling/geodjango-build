Compared to other places in this area, Manitowoc is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manitowoc costs $107,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $10,780 (9%) lower. It is approximately $110,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $15,000 (12%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Manitowoc's median rent is $665 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $38 (5%) lower. It is approximately $397 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $193 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
