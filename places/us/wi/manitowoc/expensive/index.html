In comparison to its surrounding areas, Manitowoc is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Manitowoc costs $107,100. This is about $10,780 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $110,400 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is about $15,000 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Manitowoc the median rent is $665 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $38 (5%) lower.It is about $397 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $193 (22%) lower.
