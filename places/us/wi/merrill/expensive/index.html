Home prices

The median home cost in Merrill is $85,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $65,020 (43%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $132,200 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Wausau it is about $36,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

Merrill's median rent is $637 per month. This is roughly $100 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $425 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Wausau it is roughly $71 (10%) lower.
