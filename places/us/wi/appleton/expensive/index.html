Home prices

The median home price in Appleton is $147,800. It is about $7,000 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $69,700 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $25,700 (21%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Appleton the median monthly rent is $808. This is roughly $21 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $50 (5%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
