Home prices

The median home in Appleton is valued at $147,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $7,000 (4%) lower.It is approximately $69,700 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $25,700 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Appleton the median monthly rent is $808. Compared to nearby areas this is about $21 (2%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $254 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $50 (5%) lower.
