Much like its surrounding areas, it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Appleton costs $147,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $7,000 (4%) lower.It is about $69,700 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $25,700 (21%) higher.

Rents

Appleton's median monthly rent is $808. It is about $21 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and is roughly $50 (5%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
