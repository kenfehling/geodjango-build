Rents

Lake Geneva's median monthly rent is $1,002. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $18 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $60 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $110 (9%) lower.
