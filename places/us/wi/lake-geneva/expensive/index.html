In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lake Geneva is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake Geneva is $197,300. This is approximately $45,300 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $20,200 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $60,700 (23%) lower.

Rents

Lake Geneva's median rent is $1,002 per month. This is about $18 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $60 (5%) lower, and is approximately $110 (9%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
