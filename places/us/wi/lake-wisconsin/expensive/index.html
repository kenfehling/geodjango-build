Home prices

The median home value in Lake Wisconsin is $309,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $136,300 (78%) higher. It is roughly $92,400 (42%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $63,600 (25%) higher compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Wisconsin is $1,008. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $197 (24%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $54 (5%) lower, and it is roughly $110 (9%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
