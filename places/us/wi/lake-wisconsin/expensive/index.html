Compared to the surrounding areas, Lake Wisconsin is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Madison.

Home prices

The median home value in Lake Wisconsin is $309,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $136,300 (78%) higher. It is roughly $92,400 (42%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $63,600 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Lake Wisconsin the median rent is $1,008 per month. It is roughly $197 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $54 (5%) lower, and is roughly $110 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.
