Home prices

The median home in Lake Wisconsin is valued at $309,900. It is roughly $136,300 (78%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $92,400 (42%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $63,600 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Wisconsin is $1,008 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $197 (24%) higher.It is roughly $54 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Madison it is about $110 (9%) lower.
