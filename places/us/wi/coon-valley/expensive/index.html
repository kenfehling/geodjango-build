Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Coon Valley is $671 per month. This is approximately $100 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $391 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire it is approximately $164 (19%) lower.
