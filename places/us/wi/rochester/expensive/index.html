Compared to nearby areas, Rochester is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Rochester's median home cost is $243,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $12,760 (5%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $25,700 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $14,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rochester is $879 a month. It is about $141 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and is about $233 (20%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
