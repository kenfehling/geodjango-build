In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rochester is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Rochester is $243,200. It is about $12,760 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $25,700 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $14,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Rochester the median rent is $879 per month. This is about $141 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $183 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $233 (20%) lower.
