Home prices

The median home in Rochester is valued at $243,200. It is approximately $12,760 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $25,700 (11%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $14,800 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester is $879. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $141 (13%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $183 (17%) lower, and is approximately $233 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
