Compared to its nearby areas, Rochester is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rochester is $243,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $12,760 (5%) higher. It is about $25,700 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $14,800 (5%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Rochester's median rent is $879 per month. It is roughly $141 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $183 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $233 (20%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
