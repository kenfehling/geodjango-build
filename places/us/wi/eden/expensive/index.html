Home prices

Eden's median home price is $118,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $37,440 (24%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $99,000 (45%) lower, and it is approximately $3,600 (2%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Eden the median rent is $623 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $148 (19%) lower. It is roughly $439 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $235 (27%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
