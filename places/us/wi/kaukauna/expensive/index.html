In comparison to other places in this area, Kaukauna is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home value in Kaukauna is $142,500. This is roughly $20,560 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $75,000 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $20,400 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Kaukauna the median monthly rent is $855. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $22 (2%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $207 (19%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Milwaukee.
