Compared to other places in this area, Kaukauna is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kaukauna is $142,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $20,560 (12%) lower. It is approximately $75,000 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $20,400 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Kaukauna's median monthly rent is $855. It is approximately $22 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $207 (19%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Milwaukee.
