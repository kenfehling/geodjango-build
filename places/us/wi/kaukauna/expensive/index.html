In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kaukauna is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kaukauna is $142,500. It is approximately $20,560 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $75,000 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $20,400 (16%) higher.

Rents

Kaukauna's median monthly rent is $855. It is approximately $22 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $207 (19%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Milwaukee.
