Compared to nearby areas, Kaukauna is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Kaukauna is $142,500. This is about $20,560 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $75,000 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $20,400 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kaukauna is $855. This is about $22 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $207 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
