Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hollandale is $123,800. This is roughly $40,980 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $93,700 (43%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Madison it is about $122,500 (49%) lower.

Rents

Hollandale's median monthly rent is $702. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $146 (17%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $360 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is about $416 (37%) lower.
