Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fox Point is $325,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $26,140 (7%) lower.It is approximately $108,200 (49%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $67,700 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Fox Point is $1,252 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $61 (5%) higher.It is about $190 (17%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $140 (12%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
