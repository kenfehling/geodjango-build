Home prices

Rents

In Hayward the median rent is $666 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $238 (55%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $396 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is approximately $154 (18%) lower.
