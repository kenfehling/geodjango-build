Home prices

Rents

Howard's median rent is $829 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $31 (3%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $233 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $99 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Green Bay.
