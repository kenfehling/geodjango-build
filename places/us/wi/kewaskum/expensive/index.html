Home prices

The median home in Kewaskum is valued at $173,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $15,340 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $44,000 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $51,400 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kewaskum is $935. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $113 (13%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $127 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $77 (8%) higher.
