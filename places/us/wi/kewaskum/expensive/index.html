Compared to the surrounding areas, Kewaskum is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Kewaskum is $173,500. This is approximately $15,340 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $44,000 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $51,400 (42%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Kewaskum's median rent is $935 per month. It is roughly $113 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $127 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $77 (8%) higher.
