Home prices

Kewaskum's median home value is $173,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $15,340 (9%) higher.It is approximately $44,000 (20%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $51,400 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kewaskum is $935 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $113 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $127 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $77 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
