In comparison to the surrounding areas, La Crosse is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Rochester, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

La Crosse's median home price is $142,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $29,140 (16%) lower. It is roughly $75,000 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $57,600 (28%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

La Crosse's median rent is $793 per month. This is approximately $41 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $269 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $181 (18%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
