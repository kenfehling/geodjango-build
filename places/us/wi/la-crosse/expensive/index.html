Compared to the surrounding areas, La Crosse is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

La Crosse's median home cost is $142,500. This is roughly $29,140 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $75,000 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is roughly $57,600 (28%) lower.

Rents

La Crosse's median rent is $793 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $41 (4%) lower.It is roughly $269 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $181 (18%) lower.
