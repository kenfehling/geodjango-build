Home prices

The median home price in La Crosse is $142,500. This is about $29,140 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $75,000 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $57,600 (28%) lower.

Rents

In La Crosse the median rent is $793 per month. This is roughly $41 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $269 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $181 (18%) lower.
