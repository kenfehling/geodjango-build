Home prices

The median home price in Chaseburg is $136,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $2,340 (1%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $81,300 (37%) lower, and it is about $18,800 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

The median rent in Chaseburg is $685 a month. This is approximately $46 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $377 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is approximately $150 (17%) lower.
