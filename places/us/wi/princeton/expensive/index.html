Home prices

The cost of the median home in Princeton is $88,200. This is approximately $38,220 (30%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $129,300 (59%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $33,900 (27%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Princeton the median rent is $640 per month. This is roughly $36 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $422 (39%) lower, and it is approximately $218 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
