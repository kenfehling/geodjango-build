Home prices

Pulaski's median home value is $152,700. This is approximately $27,200 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $64,800 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $16,800 (12%) higher compared to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pulaski is $672. This is about $155 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $390 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay is roughly $58 (7%) lower.
