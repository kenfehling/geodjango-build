Elkhart Lake's median home cost is $182,500. It is about $47,040 (34%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $35,000 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $60,400 (49%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Elkhart Lake is $756 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $40 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $306 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $102 (11%) lower.
