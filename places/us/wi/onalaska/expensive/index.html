In comparison to nearby areas, Onalaska is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home cost in Onalaska is $196,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $27,960 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $20,900 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $3,500 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Onalaska the median monthly rent is $890. Compared to nearby areas this is about $105 (13%) higher. It is approximately $172 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $84 (8%) lower.
