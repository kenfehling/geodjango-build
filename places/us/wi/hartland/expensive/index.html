Compared to other places in this area, Hartland is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Hartland's median home value is $292,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $400,120 (57%) lower.It is about $75,000 (34%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $34,500 (13%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Hartland is $1,044 per month. This is approximately $108 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $18 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $68 (6%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
