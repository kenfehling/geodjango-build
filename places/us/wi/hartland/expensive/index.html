Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hartland is $292,500. It is roughly $400,120 (57%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $75,000 (34%) higher, and is about $34,500 (13%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Hartland's median monthly rent is $1,044. It is approximately $108 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $18 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $68 (6%) lower.
