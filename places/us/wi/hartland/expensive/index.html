In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hartland is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Hartland's median home value is $292,500. This is approximately $400,120 (57%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $75,000 (34%) higher, and it is roughly $34,500 (13%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Hartland's median rent is $1,044 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $108 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $18 (1%) lower, and is about $68 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
