Home prices

The median home in Hartland costs $292,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $400,120 (57%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $75,000 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $34,500 (13%) higher.

Rents

Hartland's median rent is $1,044 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $108 (9%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $18 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $68 (6%) lower.
