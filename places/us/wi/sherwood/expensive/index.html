Home prices

Sherwood's median home price is $238,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $78,140 (48%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $21,400 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $116,800 (95%) higher.

Rents

In Sherwood the median monthly rent is $842. This is roughly $57 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $220 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
