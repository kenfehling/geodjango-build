In comparison to nearby areas, Marquette is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Marquette costs $124,200. This is approximately $20,140 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $93,300 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $2,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marquette is $583. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $189 (24%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $479 (45%) lower, and is about $275 (32%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
