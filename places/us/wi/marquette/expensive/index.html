In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marquette is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Marquette is $124,200. It is roughly $20,140 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $93,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $2,100 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Marquette is $583 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $189 (24%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $479 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $275 (32%) lower.
