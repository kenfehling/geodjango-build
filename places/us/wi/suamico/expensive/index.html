Home prices

The cost of the median home in Suamico is $249,400. This is approximately $55,800 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $31,900 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Green Bay is roughly $113,500 (83%) higher.

Rents

In Suamico the median rent is $1,193 a month. This is approximately $396 (49%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $131 (12%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $463 (63%) higher compared to the city of Green Bay.
