Home prices

The median home in North Fond du Lac costs $113,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $25,640 (18%) lower. It is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $8,500 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

North Fond du Lac's median rent is $942 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $296 (45%) higher. It is roughly $120 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $84 (9%) higher.
