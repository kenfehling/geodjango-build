Home prices

The value of the median home in North Fond du Lac is $113,600. This is about $25,640 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $103,900 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $8,500 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Fond du Lac is $942. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $296 (45%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $120 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $84 (9%) higher.
