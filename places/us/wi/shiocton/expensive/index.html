Home prices

The price of the median home in Shiocton is $130,800. This is approximately $12,680 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $86,700 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $5,100 (3%) lower compared to the city of Green Bay.

Rents

The median rent in Shiocton is $682 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $19 (2%) lower. It is roughly $380 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Green Bay it is about $48 (6%) lower.
