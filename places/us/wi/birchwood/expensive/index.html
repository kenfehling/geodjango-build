Home prices

The median home price in Birchwood is $88,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $15,140 (14%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $128,700 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is roughly $74,500 (45%) lower.

Rents

Birchwood's median monthly rent is $594. This is roughly $33 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $468 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $226 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Duluth.
