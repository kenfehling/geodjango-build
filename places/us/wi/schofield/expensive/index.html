Home prices

The median home price in Schofield is $105,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $50,820 (32%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $111,800 (51%) lower, and is about $19,300 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.

Rents

The median rent in Schofield is $805 a month. This is approximately $26 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $257 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Oshkosh is approximately $53 (7%) higher.
