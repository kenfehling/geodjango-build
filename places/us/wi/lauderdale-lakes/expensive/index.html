Home prices

The value of the median home in Lauderdale Lakes is $366,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $195,620 (114%) higher. It is about $148,700 (68%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $108,200 (41%) higher.

Rents

Lauderdale Lakes's median rent is $1,577 a month. This is about $731 (86%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $515 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $465 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
