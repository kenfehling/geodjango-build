In comparison to the surrounding areas, Genoa City is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Genoa City is $160,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $22,340 (12%) lower. It is approximately $56,800 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $97,300 (37%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Genoa City is $1,063 a month. It is about $77 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is is very similar, and is about $49 (4%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
