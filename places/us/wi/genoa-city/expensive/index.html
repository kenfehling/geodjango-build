Compared to other places in this area, Genoa City is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Genoa City is valued at $160,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $22,340 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $56,800 (26%) lower, and it is about $97,300 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Genoa City's median rent is $1,063 a month. This is approximately $77 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and it is about $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
