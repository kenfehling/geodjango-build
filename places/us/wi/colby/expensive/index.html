Colby's median home value is $115,000. This is about $21,920 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $102,500 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $40,000 (25%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

Colby's median monthly rent is $643. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $36 (5%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $419 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire is about $192 (22%) lower.
