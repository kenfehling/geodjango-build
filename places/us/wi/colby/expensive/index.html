Home prices

Colby's median home price is $115,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $21,920 (23%) higher.It is roughly $102,500 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $40,000 (25%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.

Rents

Colby's median rent is $643 per month. This is about $36 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $419 (39%) lower, and it is about $192 (22%) lower compared to the city of Eau Claire.
