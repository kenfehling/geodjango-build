In comparison to nearby areas, Altoona is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home in Altoona is valued at $157,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $9,160 (5%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower, and is roughly $42,500 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Altoona's median rent is $778 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $93 (10%) lower. It is roughly $284 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $196 (20%) lower.
