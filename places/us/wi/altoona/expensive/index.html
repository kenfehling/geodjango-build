Home prices

The median home price in Altoona is $157,600. It is roughly $9,160 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $42,500 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Altoona the median rent is $778 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $93 (10%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $284 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $196 (20%) lower.
