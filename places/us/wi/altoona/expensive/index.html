Home prices

Altoona's median home price is $157,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $9,160 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $59,900 (27%) lower, and is about $42,500 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Altoona's median rent is $778 per month. It is approximately $93 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $284 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $196 (20%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
