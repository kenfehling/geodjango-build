Home prices

The price of the median home in Altoona is $157,600. This is roughly $9,160 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $59,900 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is about $42,500 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Altoona is $778 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $93 (10%) lower. It is roughly $284 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $196 (20%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
