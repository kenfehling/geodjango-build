In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ashwaubenon is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Ashwaubenon's median home price is $170,600. It is about $15,760 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $46,900 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $48,500 (39%) higher.

Rents

Ashwaubenon's median monthly rent is $792. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $40 (4%) lower. It is roughly $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $66 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
