In comparison to its surrounding areas, Ashwaubenon is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Ashwaubenon is valued at $170,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,760 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $46,900 (21%) lower, and is approximately $48,500 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Ashwaubenon's median rent is $792 a month. This is approximately $40 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $66 (7%) lower.
