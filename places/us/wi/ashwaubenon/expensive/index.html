Compared to its surrounding areas, Ashwaubenon is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ashwaubenon is $170,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,760 (8%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $46,900 (21%) lower, and it is about $48,500 (39%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Ashwaubenon's median monthly rent is $792. It is about $40 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $66 (7%) lower.
