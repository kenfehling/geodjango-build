Compared to the surrounding areas, Ashwaubenon is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Ashwaubenon's median home value is $170,600. This is about $15,760 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $46,900 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is approximately $48,500 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ashwaubenon is $792 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $40 (4%) lower.It is about $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $66 (7%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
