Rents

The median rent in Yorkville is $1,257 a month. It is approximately $358 (39%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $195 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $145 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
