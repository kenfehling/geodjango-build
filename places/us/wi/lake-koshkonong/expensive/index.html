Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake Koshkonong is $264,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $81,500 (44%) higher. It is roughly $47,300 (21%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $142,700 (116%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Lake Koshkonong's median monthly rent is $890. It is approximately $21 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $172 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $32 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
