Compared to its surrounding areas, Merton is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home cost in Merton is $448,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $182,240 (28%) lower. It is approximately $231,100 (106%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $190,600 (73%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Merton's median rent is $983 a month. It is about $221 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $79 (7%) lower, and it is about $129 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
