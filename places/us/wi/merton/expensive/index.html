Home prices

The median home in Merton is valued at $448,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $182,240 (28%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $231,100 (106%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $190,600 (73%) higher.

Rents

Merton's median rent is $983 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $221 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $79 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $129 (11%) lower.
