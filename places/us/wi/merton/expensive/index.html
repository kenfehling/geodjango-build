Home prices

The median home in Merton is valued at $448,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $182,240 (28%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $231,100 (106%) higher, and it is approximately $190,600 (73%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Merton is $983 per month. It is approximately $221 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $129 (11%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
