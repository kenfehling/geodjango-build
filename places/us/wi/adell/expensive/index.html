Home prices

The median home in Adell costs $139,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $21,380 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $78,000 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $17,400 (14%) higher.

Rents

Adell's median rent is $630 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $222 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $432 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $228 (26%) lower.
