In comparison to its surrounding areas, Walworth is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Walworth is $172,000. It is roughly $59,520 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $45,500 (20%) lower, and is approximately $86,000 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Walworth's median monthly rent is $849. It is approximately $111 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $213 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $263 (23%) lower.
