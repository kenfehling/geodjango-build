Home prices

The median home in Walworth costs $172,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $59,520 (25%) lower.It is about $45,500 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $86,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

Walworth's median monthly rent is $849. It is approximately $111 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $213 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $263 (23%) lower.
