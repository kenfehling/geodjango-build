Home prices

The median home in Oconomowoc costs $259,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $313,560 (54%) lower.It is approximately $41,700 (19%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Oconomowoc the median rent is $972 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $411 (29%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $90 (8%) lower, and is approximately $140 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
