Home prices

Oconomowoc's median home cost is $259,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $313,560 (54%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $41,700 (19%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Oconomowoc the median rent is $972 a month. This is roughly $411 (29%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $90 (8%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $140 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
