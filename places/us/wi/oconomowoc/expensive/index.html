Home prices

The median home in Oconomowoc is valued at $259,200. This is roughly $313,560 (54%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $41,700 (19%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is is very similar.

Rents

Oconomowoc's median monthly rent is $972. It is about $411 (29%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $90 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $140 (12%) lower.
