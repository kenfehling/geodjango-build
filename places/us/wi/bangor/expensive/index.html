Compared to other places in this area, Bangor is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Eau Claire however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Bangor is $148,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $14,840 (9%) lower.It is approximately $69,100 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Eau Claire it is approximately $6,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Bangor the median monthly rent is $757. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $101 (11%) lower.It is roughly $305 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Eau Claire is about $78 (9%) lower.
