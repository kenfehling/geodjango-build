Compared to the surrounding areas, Bangor is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Eau Claire, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Bangor is $148,400. It is approximately $14,840 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $69,100 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire it is roughly $6,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bangor is $757. It is approximately $101 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $305 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Eau Claire it is roughly $78 (9%) lower.
