Home prices

The median home price in Potosi is $143,800. This is roughly $26,820 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $73,700 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $102,500 (41%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Potosi is $518 a month. This is approximately $173 (25%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $544 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is about $600 (53%) lower.
