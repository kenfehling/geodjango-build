Rents

In Mayville the median rent is $689 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $116 (14%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $373 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee is approximately $169 (19%) lower.
