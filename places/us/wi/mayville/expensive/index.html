Compared to other places in this area, Mayville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mayville costs $130,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $22,840 (14%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $86,600 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is about $8,800 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Mayville the median rent is $689 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $116 (14%) lower.It is about $373 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee it is about $169 (19%) lower.
