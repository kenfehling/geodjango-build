In comparison to other places in this area, Mayville is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Mayville costs $130,900. This is about $22,840 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $86,600 (39%) lower, and is approximately $8,800 (7%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mayville is $689. It is roughly $116 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $373 (35%) lower, and it is about $169 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
