In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mayville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Mayville is $130,900. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $22,840 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $86,600 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $8,800 (7%) higher.

Rents

Mayville's median rent is $689 per month. This is about $116 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $169 (19%) lower.
