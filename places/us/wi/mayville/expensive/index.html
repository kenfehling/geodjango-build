Compared to nearby areas, Mayville is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Mayville's median home price is $130,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $22,840 (14%) lower. It is approximately $86,600 (39%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $8,800 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median rent in Mayville is $689 per month. It is roughly $116 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $373 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $169 (19%) lower.
