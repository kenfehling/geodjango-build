Home prices

The median home in Saukville is valued at $188,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $36,680 (16%) lower.It is roughly $29,200 (13%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $66,200 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Saukville is $730. It is approximately $174 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $332 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $128 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
