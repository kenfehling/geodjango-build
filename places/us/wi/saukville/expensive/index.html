Compared to nearby areas, Saukville is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Milwaukee, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Saukville is valued at $188,300. This is about $36,680 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $29,200 (13%) lower, and is about $66,200 (54%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Saukville's median rent is $730 per month. It is about $174 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $128 (14%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
