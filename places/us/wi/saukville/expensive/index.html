Compared to its surrounding areas, Saukville is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Saukville is $188,300. This is approximately $36,680 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $29,200 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is about $66,200 (54%) higher.

Rents

Saukville's median monthly rent is $730. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $174 (19%) lower. It is roughly $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $128 (14%) lower compared to the city of Milwaukee.
