Compared to other places in this area, Saukville is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Saukville is valued at $188,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $36,680 (16%) lower.It is about $29,200 (13%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $66,200 (54%) higher.

Rents

Saukville's median monthly rent is $730. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $174 (19%) lower.It is about $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Milwaukee it is roughly $128 (14%) lower.
