In comparison to nearby areas, Sun Prairie is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Sun Prairie is $239,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $25,120 (9%) lower.It is about $21,700 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $117,100 (95%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

Sun Prairie's median monthly rent is $1,087. This is approximately $38 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $25 (2%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $229 (26%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
