In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sun Prairie is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

The median home in Sun Prairie is valued at $239,200. It is approximately $25,120 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $21,700 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $117,100 (95%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Sun Prairie the median monthly rent is $1,087. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $38 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $25 (2%) higher, and it is about $229 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Milwaukee.
