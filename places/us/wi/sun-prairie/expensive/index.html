In comparison to other places in this area, Sun Prairie is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Home prices

Sun Prairie's median home price is $239,200. It is roughly $25,120 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $21,700 (9%) higher, and it is roughly $117,100 (95%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.

Rents

In Sun Prairie the median monthly rent is $1,087. This is about $38 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $25 (2%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $229 (26%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
