In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sun Prairie is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Milwaukee however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Sun Prairie's median home cost is $239,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $25,120 (9%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $21,700 (9%) higher, and compared to the city of Milwaukee is roughly $117,100 (95%) higher.

Rents

In Sun Prairie the median monthly rent is $1,087. It is roughly $38 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $25 (2%) higher, and it is approximately $229 (26%) higher compared to the city of Milwaukee.
