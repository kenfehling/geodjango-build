Home prices

Rents

In Prairie du Sac the median rent is $1,041 per month. This is approximately $241 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $21 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is approximately $77 (6%) lower.
