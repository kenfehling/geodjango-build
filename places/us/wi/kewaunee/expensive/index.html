Home prices

The median home in Kewaunee is valued at $101,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $30,920 (23%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $115,700 (53%) lower, and is roughly $34,100 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Green Bay.

Rents
