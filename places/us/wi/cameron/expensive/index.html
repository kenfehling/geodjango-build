Home prices

The median home in Cameron is valued at $118,800. This is roughly $17,000 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $98,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is approximately $44,500 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cameron is $808 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $132 (19%) higher. It is about $254 (23%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
