In comparison to other places in this area, Stevens Point is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Oshkosh.

Home prices

The median home in Stevens Point is valued at $140,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $19,520 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $77,400 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oshkosh it is about $15,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

Stevens Point's median rent is $737 a month. This is roughly $73 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Oshkosh it is about $15 (1%) lower.
