In comparison to its nearby areas, Stevens Point is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Oshkoshhowever, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Stevens Point is $140,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $19,520 (12%) lower. It is roughly $77,400 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oshkosh is roughly $15,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stevens Point is $737. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $73 (9%) lower. It is approximately $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.
