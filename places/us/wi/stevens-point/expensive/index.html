In comparison to its nearby areas, Stevens Point is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Oshkosh however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Stevens Point costs $140,100. This is about $19,520 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $77,400 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Oshkosh is about $15,100 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Stevens Point is $737 per month. This is roughly $73 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $325 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Oshkosh it is approximately $15 (1%) lower.
