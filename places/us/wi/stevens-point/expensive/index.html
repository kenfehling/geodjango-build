Compared to other places in this area, Stevens Point is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Oshkosh.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Stevens Point is $140,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $19,520 (12%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $77,400 (35%) lower, and it is about $15,100 (12%) higher compared to the city of Oshkosh.

Rents

In Stevens Point the median rent is $737 a month. It is about $73 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.
