Compared to its surrounding areas, Stevens Point is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Oshkosh, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Stevens Point is $140,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $19,520 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $77,400 (35%) lower, and is roughly $15,100 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Oshkosh.

Rents

The median rent in Stevens Point is $737 per month. It is approximately $73 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $325 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Oshkosh.
