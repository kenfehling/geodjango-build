Home prices

The median home in Corning costs $72,900. It is about $20,700 (39%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $144,600 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $86,800 (54%) lower.

Rents

Corning's median monthly rent is $554. This is approximately $84 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $508 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha is approximately $369 (39%) lower.
