Home prices

The median home in Earlham costs $169,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $41,460 (32%) higher. It is about $48,400 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $35,900 (26%) higher.

Rents

Earlham's median monthly rent is $681. It is roughly $30 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $381 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $174 (20%) lower.
