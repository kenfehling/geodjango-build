Home prices

The median home in Earlham is valued at $169,100. It is roughly $41,460 (32%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $48,400 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $35,900 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Earlham is $681 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30 (4%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $174 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
