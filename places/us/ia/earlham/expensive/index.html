In comparison to its surrounding areas, Earlham is substantially more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Earlham is $169,100. It is about $41,460 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $48,400 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $35,900 (26%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Earlham is $681 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $30 (4%) lower. It is approximately $381 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $174 (20%) lower.
