Rents

In Wyoming the median monthly rent is $575. It is roughly $108 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $192 (25%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
