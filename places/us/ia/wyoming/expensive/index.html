Home prices

The median home in Wyoming costs $68,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $6,560 (10%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $149,400 (68%) lower, and it is about $73,700 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Wyoming's median monthly rent is $575. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $108 (15%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $487 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $192 (25%) lower.
