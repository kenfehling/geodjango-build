Home prices

The median home cost in Wyoming is $68,100. It is about $6,560 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $149,400 (68%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $73,700 (51%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wyoming is $575. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $108 (15%) lower.It is about $487 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $192 (25%) lower.
