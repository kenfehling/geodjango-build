Home prices

The cost of the median home in Washington is $116,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $32,320 (38%) higher. It is roughly $100,600 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $24,900 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Washington is $798. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $119 (17%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $264 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $31 (4%) higher.
