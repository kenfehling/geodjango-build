In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lakeside is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Sioux City, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeside costs $138,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $56,960 (69%) higher. It is roughly $78,700 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Sioux City it is about $22,200 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Lakeside the median monthly rent is $735. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $100 (15%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $327 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux City is about $48 (6%) lower.
