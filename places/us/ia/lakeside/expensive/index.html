Home prices

The median home in Lakeside is valued at $138,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $56,960 (69%) higher.It is roughly $78,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Sioux City it is about $22,200 (19%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakeside is $735. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $100 (15%) higher.It is roughly $327 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Sioux City is roughly $48 (6%) lower.
