Home prices

The median home in Lakeside is valued at $138,800. It is about $56,960 (69%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $78,700 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux City is about $22,200 (19%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakeside is $735. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $100 (15%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $327 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux City is about $48 (6%) lower.
