Home prices

The median home value in Ogden is $114,400. It is roughly $44,260 (63%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $103,100 (47%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $18,800 (14%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Ogden's median monthly rent is $752. This is approximately $45 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $310 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $103 (12%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
