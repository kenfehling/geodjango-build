Compared to the surrounding areas, Ogden is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ogden is $114,400. It is roughly $44,260 (63%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $103,100 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $18,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

Ogden's median rent is $752 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $45 (6%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $310 (29%) lower, and is approximately $103 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
