Home prices

The value of the median home in Dixon is $111,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $4,160 (3%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $105,700 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $30,000 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Dixon is $833 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $125 (17%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $66 (8%) higher.
