Home prices

Dixon's median home cost is $111,800. This is approximately $4,160 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $105,700 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $30,000 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Dixon's median monthly rent is $833. Compared to nearby areas it is about $125 (17%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $229 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $66 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
