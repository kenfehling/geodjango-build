Home prices

The median home price in Mediapolis is $118,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $39,360 (49%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $98,700 (45%) lower, and it is about $23,000 (16%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Mediapolis is $602 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $118 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $460 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $165 (21%) lower.
