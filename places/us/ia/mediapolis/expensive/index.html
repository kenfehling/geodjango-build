Home prices

Rents

Mediapolis's median rent is $602 per month. It is roughly $118 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $460 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $165 (21%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
