Home prices

The median home in Center Point is valued at $164,200. It is roughly $4,900 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $53,300 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $22,400 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Center Point the median monthly rent is $729. It is approximately $8 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $333 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $38 (4%) lower.
