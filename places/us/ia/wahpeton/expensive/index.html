In comparison to other places in this area, Wahpeton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Dodge, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wahpeton is valued at $350,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $72,860 (26%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $132,500 (60%) higher, and it is about $256,400 (273%) higher compared to the city of Fort Dodge.

Rents

The median rent in Wahpeton is $1,458 per month. This is approximately $465 (46%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $396 (37%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Dodge it is roughly $806 (123%) higher.
