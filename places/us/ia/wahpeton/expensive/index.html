Home prices

The median home cost in Wahpeton is $350,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $72,860 (26%) higher.It is approximately $132,500 (60%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge is about $256,400 (273%) higher.

Rents

Wahpeton's median monthly rent is $1,458. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $465 (46%) higher.It is approximately $396 (37%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $806 (123%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge.
