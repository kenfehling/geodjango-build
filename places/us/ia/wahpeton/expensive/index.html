Compared to other places in this area, Wahpeton is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Fort Dodge.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wahpeton is $350,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $72,860 (26%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $132,500 (60%) higher, and compared to the city of Fort Dodge is approximately $256,400 (273%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wahpeton is $1,458 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $465 (46%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $396 (37%) higher, and is approximately $806 (123%) higher compared to the city of Fort Dodge.
