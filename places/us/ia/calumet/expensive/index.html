Home prices

The median home in Calumet costs $67,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $7,060 (9%) lower. It is about $150,000 (68%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $122,300 (64%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Calumet is $446. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $191 (29%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $616 (58%) lower, and is roughly $381 (46%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
