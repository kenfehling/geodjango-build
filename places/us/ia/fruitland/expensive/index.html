Home prices

The value of the median home in Fruitland is $170,800. This is roughly $85,580 (100%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $46,700 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $29,000 (20%) higher.

Rents

Fruitland's median monthly rent is $683. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $107 (13%) lower.It is roughly $379 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $84 (10%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
