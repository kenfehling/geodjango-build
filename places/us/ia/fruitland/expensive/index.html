Home prices

The median home price in Fruitland is $170,800. It is roughly $85,580 (100%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $46,700 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $29,000 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fruitland is $683. This is approximately $107 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $379 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $84 (10%) lower.
