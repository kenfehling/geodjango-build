Home prices

The median home value in Neola is $120,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $6,020 (4%) lower. It is approximately $96,800 (44%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $39,000 (24%) lower.

Rents

Neola's median monthly rent is $870. It is approximately $109 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $192 (18%) lower, and is roughly $53 (5%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
