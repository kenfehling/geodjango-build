Home prices

The cost of the median home in Thurman is $80,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $28,660 (26%) lower. It is roughly $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is approximately $79,700 (49%) lower.

Rents

Thurman's median monthly rent is $733. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $53 (7%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $329 (30%) lower, and is roughly $190 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
