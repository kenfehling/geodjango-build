Rents

The median monthly rent in Springville is $704. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $17 (2%) higher. It is about $358 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $63 (8%) lower.
