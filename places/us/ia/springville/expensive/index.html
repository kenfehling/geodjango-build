Home prices

The median home in Springville costs $128,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $19,380 (13%) lower.It is about $89,100 (40%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $13,400 (9%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Springville is $704. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $17 (2%) higher.It is roughly $358 (33%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $63 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
