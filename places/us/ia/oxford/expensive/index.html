Rents

The median rent in Oxford is $950 a month. This is roughly $90 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $112 (10%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $183 (23%) higher.
