Home prices

The median home in Lawton is valued at $171,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $12,960 (8%) higher. It is approximately $45,900 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha is about $11,900 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lawton is $677 a month. It is roughly $138 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $385 (36%) lower, and it is about $246 (26%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
