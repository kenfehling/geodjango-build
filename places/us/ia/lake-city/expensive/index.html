Home prices

The price of the median home in Lake City is $81,200. This is about $21,080 (35%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $136,300 (62%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $52,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Lake City the median rent is $595 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $100 (20%) higher. It is approximately $467 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $260 (30%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
