Compared to its nearby areas, Melbourne is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home price in Melbourne is $109,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $9,120 (9%) higher. It is about $107,800 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $23,500 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Melbourne's median monthly rent is $737. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $74 (11%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $325 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $118 (13%) lower.
