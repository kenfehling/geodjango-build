In comparison to its surrounding areas, Melbourne is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Melbourne costs $109,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $9,120 (9%) higher.It is roughly $107,800 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $23,500 (17%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Melbourne's median monthly rent is $737. Compared to nearby areas this is about $74 (11%) higher.It is approximately $325 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $118 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
