Home prices

The median home in Alta costs $111,000. It is about $14,260 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $106,500 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Sioux City it is approximately $5,600 (4%) lower.

Rents

Alta's median rent is $744 a month. This is roughly $118 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $39 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux City.
