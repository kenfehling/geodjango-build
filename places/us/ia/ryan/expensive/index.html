Home prices

The median home value in Ryan is $108,500. It is roughly $7,980 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $109,000 (50%) lower, and is approximately $33,300 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Ryan the median rent is $556 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (12%) lower. It is roughly $506 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $211 (27%) lower.
