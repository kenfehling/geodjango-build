Home prices

The median home cost in Williamsburg is $159,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $46,160 (40%) higher. It is approximately $57,800 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $17,900 (12%) higher.

Rents

Williamsburg's median monthly rent is $537. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $112 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $525 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $230 (29%) lower.
