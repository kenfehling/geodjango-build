Home prices

The median home value in Williamsburg is $159,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $46,160 (40%) higher.It is approximately $57,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $17,900 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Williamsburg is $537 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $112 (17%) lower.It is roughly $525 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $230 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
