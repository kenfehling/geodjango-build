Compared to its nearby areas, Denver is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Denver's median home value is $162,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $19,840 (13%) higher.It is roughly $54,700 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $21,000 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Denver the median rent is $588 per month. This is approximately $17 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $474 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $179 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
