In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denver is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Denver is $162,800. It is about $19,840 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $54,700 (25%) lower, and is approximately $21,000 (14%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Denver is $588 per month. It is roughly $17 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $474 (44%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $179 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
