Compared to its nearby areas, Denver is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Denver costs $162,800. It is roughly $19,840 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $54,700 (25%) lower, and is about $21,000 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Denver the median rent is $588 per month. This is approximately $17 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $474 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $179 (23%) lower.
