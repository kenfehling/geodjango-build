In comparison to its nearby areas, Denver is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Denver is $162,800. This is roughly $19,840 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $54,700 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $21,000 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Denver is $588 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $17 (2%) lower. It is roughly $474 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $179 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
