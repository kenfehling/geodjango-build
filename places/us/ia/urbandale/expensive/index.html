The median home cost in Urbandale is $237,500. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $20,000 (9%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $104,300 (78%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Urbandale's median monthly rent is $935. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $121 (11%) lower. It is about $127 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $80 (9%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
