Compared to its surrounding areas, Independence is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Independence costs $133,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $25,120 (23%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $84,400 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $8,700 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Independence is $746 per month. It is about $140 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $316 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $21 (2%) lower.
