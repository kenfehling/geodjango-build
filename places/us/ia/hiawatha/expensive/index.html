Home prices

Rents

Hiawatha's median rent is $773 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $150 (16%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $289 (27%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
