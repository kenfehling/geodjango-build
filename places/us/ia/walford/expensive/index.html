Home prices

The median home price in Walford is $215,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $22,920 (11%) higher.It is about $2,500 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $73,200 (51%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Walford is $639. This is roughly $117 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $423 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $128 (16%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
