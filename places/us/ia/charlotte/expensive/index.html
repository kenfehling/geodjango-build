Home prices

The median home in Charlotte costs $103,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $17,160 (14%) lower. It is about $113,900 (52%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $38,200 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Charlotte is $575 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $79 (12%) lower. It is about $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $192 (25%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
