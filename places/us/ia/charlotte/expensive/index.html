Compared to its nearby areas, Charlotte is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Charlotte costs $103,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $17,160 (14%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $113,900 (52%) lower, and it is about $38,200 (26%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Charlotte the median rent is $575 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $79 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $487 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $192 (25%) lower.
