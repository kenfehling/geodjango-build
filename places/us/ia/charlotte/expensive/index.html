Home prices

The median home in Charlotte costs $103,600. This is about $17,160 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $113,900 (52%) lower, and it is approximately $38,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Charlotte the median monthly rent is $575. It is approximately $79 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $487 (45%) lower, and is roughly $192 (25%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
