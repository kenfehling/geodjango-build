Home prices

The value of the median home in Charlotte is $103,600. This is about $17,160 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $113,900 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $38,200 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Charlotte the median monthly rent is $575. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $79 (12%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $487 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $192 (25%) lower.
