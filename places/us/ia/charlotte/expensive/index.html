Compared to nearby areas, Charlotte is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

Charlotte's median home value is $103,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $17,160 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $113,900 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $38,200 (26%) lower.

Rents

Charlotte's median rent is $575 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $79 (12%) lower. It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $192 (25%) lower.
