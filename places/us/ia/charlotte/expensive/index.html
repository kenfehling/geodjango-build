In comparison to the surrounding areas, Charlotte is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Charlotte is valued at $103,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $17,160 (14%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $113,900 (52%) lower, and is roughly $38,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Charlotte's median rent is $575 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $79 (12%) lower. It is roughly $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $192 (25%) lower.
