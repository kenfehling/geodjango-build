Home prices

The cost of the median home in Shelby is $102,900. This is about $8,960 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $114,600 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $56,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Shelby's median monthly rent is $756. This is about $31 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $306 (28%) lower, and it is about $167 (18%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
