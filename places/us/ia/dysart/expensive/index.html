Home prices

The median home in Dysart is valued at $124,900. It is about $44,780 (55%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $92,600 (42%) lower, and it is approximately $16,900 (11%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Dysart's median monthly rent is $769. This is roughly $103 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
