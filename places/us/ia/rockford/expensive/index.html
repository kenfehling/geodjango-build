Home prices

The median home in Rockford costs $100,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $12,840 (14%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $116,600 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Ames it is approximately $105,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rockford is $467 per month. This is roughly $152 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $595 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Ames it is about $463 (49%) lower.
