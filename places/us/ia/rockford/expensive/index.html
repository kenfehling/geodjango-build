Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rockford is $100,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $12,840 (14%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $116,600 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Ames it is about $105,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rockford is $467. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $152 (24%) lower. It is about $595 (56%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Ames it is roughly $463 (49%) lower.
