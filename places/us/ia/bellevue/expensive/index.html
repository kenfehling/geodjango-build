Home prices

The median home in Bellevue costs $156,500. It is about $24,120 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $61,000 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Madison is roughly $89,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bellevue is $655 per month. This is about $206 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $407 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $463 (41%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
