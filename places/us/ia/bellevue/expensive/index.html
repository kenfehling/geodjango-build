Home prices

The median home in Bellevue is valued at $156,500. This is about $24,120 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $61,000 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Madison it is approximately $89,800 (36%) lower.

Rents

Bellevue's median monthly rent is $655. This is roughly $206 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $407 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is about $463 (41%) lower.
