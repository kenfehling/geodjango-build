Home prices

The median home in Durant is valued at $161,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $41,380 (34%) higher.It is approximately $55,900 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $19,800 (13%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Durant the median monthly rent is $867. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $177 (25%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $195 (18%) lower, and it is about $100 (13%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
