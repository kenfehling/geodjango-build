Home prices

The price of the median home in Center Junction is $58,800. This is roughly $29,860 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $158,700 (72%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $83,000 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Center Junction's median rent is $830 a month. This is roughly $199 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $232 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $63 (8%) higher.
