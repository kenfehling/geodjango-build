Compared to its surrounding areas, Newton is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newton is $114,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $8,980 (8%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $103,300 (47%) lower, and is roughly $19,000 (14%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Newton the median rent is $736 per month. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $326 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $119 (13%) lower.
