Home prices

The median home value in Newton is $114,200. This is approximately $8,980 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $103,300 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $19,000 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Newton the median monthly rent is $736. It is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $119 (13%) lower.
