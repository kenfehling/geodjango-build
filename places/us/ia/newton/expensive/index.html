Home prices

The price of the median home in Newton is $114,200. It is roughly $8,980 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $103,300 (47%) lower, and is roughly $19,000 (14%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Newton is $736 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $13 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $119 (13%) lower.
