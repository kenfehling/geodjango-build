In comparison to other places in this area, Newton is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newton is $114,200. This is approximately $8,980 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $103,300 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $19,000 (14%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newton is $736. It is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $326 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $119 (13%) lower.
