Home prices

The median home cost in Monona is $98,600. This is approximately $13,520 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $118,900 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $43,200 (30%) lower.

Rents

Monona's median monthly rent is $830. It is about $194 (30%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $232 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $63 (8%) higher.
