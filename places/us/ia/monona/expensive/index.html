Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Monona is $830 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $194 (30%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $232 (21%) lower, and is roughly $63 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
