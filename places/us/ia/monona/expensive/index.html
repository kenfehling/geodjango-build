Compared to its surrounding areas, Monona is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monona is valued at $98,600. It is roughly $13,520 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $118,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $43,200 (30%) lower.

Rents

Monona's median rent is $830 per month. This is approximately $194 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $232 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $63 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
