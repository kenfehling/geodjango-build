Rents

In Jesup the median rent is $595 per month. This is roughly $66 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $467 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $172 (22%) lower.
