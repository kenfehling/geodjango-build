Home prices

The value of the median home in Jesup is $156,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $4,640 (3%) higher. It is roughly $60,900 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $14,800 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Jesup is $595 a month. This is about $66 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $467 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $172 (22%) lower.
