Compared to the surrounding areas, Des Moines is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The median home in Des Moines is valued at $133,200. This is roughly $61,550 (31%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $84,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Des Moines is $855 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $200 (18%) lower. It is approximately $207 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation.
