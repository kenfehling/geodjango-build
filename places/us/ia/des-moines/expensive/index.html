In comparison to its nearby areas, Des Moines is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Des Moines is valued at $133,200. It is roughly $61,550 (31%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $84,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Des Moines is $855 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $200 (18%) lower. It is approximately $207 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation.
