In comparison to its surrounding areas, Des Moines is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Des Moines costs $133,200. This is roughly $61,550 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $84,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Des Moines the median monthly rent is $855. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $200 (18%) lower. It is approximately $207 (19%) lower compared to the entire country.
