Home prices

The median home cost in Oakland is $110,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $3,180 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $106,700 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $48,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $655 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $93 (12%) lower. It is roughly $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is approximately $268 (29%) lower.
