Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oakland is $110,800. This is approximately $3,180 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $106,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $48,900 (30%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $655 a month. This is about $93 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $407 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is roughly $268 (29%) lower.
