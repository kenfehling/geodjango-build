Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $110,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $3,180 (2%) lower.It is about $106,700 (49%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $48,900 (30%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Oakland's median rent is $655 a month. This is about $93 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $268 (29%) lower.
