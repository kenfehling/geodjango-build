Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $110,800. It is roughly $3,180 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $106,700 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $48,900 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $655 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $93 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $407 (38%) lower, and is roughly $268 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
