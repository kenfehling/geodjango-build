Similar to the surrounding areas, it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Omaha, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $110,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $3,180 (2%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $106,700 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $48,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $655 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $93 (12%) lower. It is about $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $268 (29%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
