Home prices

The median home cost in Clearfield is $60,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $9,960 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $156,800 (72%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $72,500 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clearfield is $840 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $157 (22%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $222 (20%) lower, and is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
