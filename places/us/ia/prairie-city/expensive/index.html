Home prices

The median home in Prairie City costs $137,100. This is roughly $20,820 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $80,400 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $3,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Prairie City's median monthly rent is $878. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $213 (32%) higher. It is approximately $184 (17%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $23 (2%) higher.
