In comparison to the surrounding areas, Prairie City is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Prairie City is valued at $137,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $20,820 (17%) higher. It is about $80,400 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $3,900 (2%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Prairie City the median monthly rent is $878. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $213 (32%) higher. It is approximately $184 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $23 (2%) higher.
