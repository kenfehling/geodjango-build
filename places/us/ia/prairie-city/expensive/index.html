Compared to its surrounding areas, Prairie City is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Prairie City is $137,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $20,820 (17%) higher.It is about $80,400 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $3,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Prairie City's median monthly rent is $878. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $213 (32%) higher.It is roughly $184 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $23 (2%) higher.
