Similar to other places in this area, it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of La Crosse.

Home prices

Lansing's median home cost is $115,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $1,820 (1%) lower. It is about $101,600 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $26,600 (18%) lower compared to the city of La Crosse.

Rents

In Lansing the median rent is $694 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $105 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $368 (34%) lower, and is roughly $99 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of La Crosse.
