Much like the surrounding areas, it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of La Crosse, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lansing is valued at $115,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $1,820 (1%) lower. It is about $101,600 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of La Crosse it is roughly $26,600 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lansing is $694 a month. It is roughly $105 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $368 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $99 (12%) lower compared to the city of La Crosse.
