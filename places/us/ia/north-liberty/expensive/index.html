Compared to its surrounding areas, North Liberty is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in North Liberty is $220,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24,260 (9%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $3,300 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $79,000 (55%) higher.

Rents

North Liberty's median rent is $1,110 per month. It is about $86 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $48 (4%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $343 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
