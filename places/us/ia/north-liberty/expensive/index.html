In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Liberty is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Liberty is valued at $220,800. It is approximately $24,260 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $3,300 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $79,000 (55%) higher.

Rents

North Liberty's median monthly rent is $1,110. This is about $86 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $48 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $343 (44%) higher.
