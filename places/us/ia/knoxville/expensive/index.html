Home prices

The median home in Knoxville costs $95,100. It is roughly $6,280 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $122,400 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $38,100 (28%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Knoxville is $672 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $8 (1%) higher.It is approximately $390 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $183 (21%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
