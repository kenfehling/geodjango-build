McClelland's median home cost is $158,600. This is about $10,460 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $58,900 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha is is very similar.

Rents

In McClelland the median rent is $917 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $60 (6%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $145 (13%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Omaha.
