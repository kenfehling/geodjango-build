Home prices

The median home in Waterloo costs $117,400. This is roughly $47,200 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $100,100 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $24,400 (17%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Waterloo's median monthly rent is $765. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $297 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is is very similar.
