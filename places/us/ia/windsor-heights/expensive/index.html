In comparison to its surrounding areas, Windsor Heights is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Windsor Heights is $186,700. This is about $56,180 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $30,800 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $53,500 (40%) higher.

Rents

In Windsor Heights the median monthly rent is $1,019. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $43 (4%) lower, and it is about $164 (19%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
