Compared to its surrounding areas, Roland is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Roland is $146,300. This is roughly $10,140 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $71,200 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $13,100 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Roland is $615 a month. This is approximately $118 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $447 (42%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $240 (28%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
