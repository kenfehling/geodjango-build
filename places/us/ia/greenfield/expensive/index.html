Rents

Greenfield's median rent is $523 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $83 (13%) lower. It is roughly $539 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $332 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
