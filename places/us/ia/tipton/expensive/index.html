Home prices

The median home in Tipton is valued at $126,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $26,920 (27%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $90,900 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $15,200 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Tipton's median rent is $606 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $67 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $456 (42%) lower, and it is about $161 (20%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
