Home prices

Rents

New Sharon's median rent is $488 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $164 (25%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $574 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $367 (42%) lower.
