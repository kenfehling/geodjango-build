Home prices

The median home in New Sharon costs $111,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $19,760 (21%) higher. It is roughly $105,900 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $21,600 (16%) lower.

Rents

In New Sharon the median monthly rent is $488. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $164 (25%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $574 (54%) lower, and is roughly $367 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
