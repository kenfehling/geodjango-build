Home prices

The median home in Beacon is valued at $67,500. This is about $18,420 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $150,000 (68%) lower, and it is about $65,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beacon is $793. It is approximately $113 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $269 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $62 (7%) lower.
