Home prices

The median home in Marcus is valued at $101,500. This is roughly $7,680 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $116,000 (53%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $88,300 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

In Marcus the median monthly rent is $673. This is about $10 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $389 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $154 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
