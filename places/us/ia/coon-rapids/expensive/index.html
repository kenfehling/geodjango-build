Home prices

The median home cost in Coon Rapids is $88,600. It is roughly $13,740 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $128,900 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $44,600 (33%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Coon Rapids is $619. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $14 (2%) higher. It is approximately $443 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $236 (27%) lower.
