In comparison to its surrounding areas, Coon Rapids is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

Coon Rapids's median home value is $88,600. This is approximately $13,740 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $128,900 (59%) lower, and is about $44,600 (33%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Coon Rapids is $619 a month. This is approximately $14 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $443 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $236 (27%) lower.
