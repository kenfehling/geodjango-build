Home prices

The median home cost in Clermont is $108,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $21,560 (24%) higher. It is about $109,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $33,400 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Clermont the median monthly rent is $515. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $130 (20%) lower. It is about $547 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $252 (32%) lower.
