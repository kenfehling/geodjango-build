Home prices

Clermont's median home cost is $108,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $21,560 (24%) higher.It is roughly $109,100 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $33,400 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Clermont is $515. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $130 (20%) lower.It is roughly $547 (51%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $252 (32%) lower.
