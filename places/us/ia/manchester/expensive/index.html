Home prices

The median home cost in Manchester is $110,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $10,060 (10%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $107,100 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $31,400 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Manchester is $576 per month. It is roughly $37 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $486 (45%) lower, and it is about $191 (24%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
