In comparison to other places in this area, Manchester is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manchester is valued at $110,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $10,060 (10%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $107,100 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $31,400 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Manchester the median rent is $576 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $37 (6%) lower.It is roughly $486 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $191 (24%) lower.
