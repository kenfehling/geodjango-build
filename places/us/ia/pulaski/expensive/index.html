Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pulaski is $56,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $16,160 (22%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $160,900 (73%) lower, and it is roughly $158,500 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of Iowa City.

Rents

Pulaski's median monthly rent is $770. It is roughly $141 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $292 (27%) lower, and is approximately $197 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Iowa City.
