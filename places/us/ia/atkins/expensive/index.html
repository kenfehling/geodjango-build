Compared to the surrounding areas, Atkins is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Atkins is $223,500. This is roughly $39,640 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $6,000 (2%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $81,700 (57%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atkins is $585. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $276 (32%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $477 (44%) lower, and it is about $182 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
