Home prices

The median home in Hull costs $146,900. This is about $11,400 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $70,600 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is about $42,900 (22%) lower.

Rents

Hull's median rent is $732 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $8 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $330 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $95 (11%) lower.
