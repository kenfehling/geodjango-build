Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hull is $732. This is roughly $8 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $330 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $95 (11%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
