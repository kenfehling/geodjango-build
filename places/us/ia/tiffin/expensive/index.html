Compared to the surrounding areas, Tiffin is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tiffin costs $233,000. This is roughly $12,920 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $15,500 (7%) higher, and is approximately $91,200 (64%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Tiffin's median rent is $994 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $91 (8%) lower.It is roughly $68 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $227 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
