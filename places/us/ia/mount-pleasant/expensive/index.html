Home prices

The median home value in Mount Pleasant is $116,500. This is approximately $56,200 (93%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $101,000 (46%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $25,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median rent is $691 a month. This is roughly $63 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $371 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $76 (9%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
