Compared to its surrounding areas, Eldridge is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Eldridge is $254,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $68,540 (36%) higher. It is roughly $36,800 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Peoria is roughly $128,600 (102%) higher.

Rents

Eldridge's median rent is $736 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $275 (27%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is about $70 (8%) lower.
