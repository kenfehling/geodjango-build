In comparison to other places in this area, Marshalltown is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marshalltown costs $94,300. It is about $13,940 (17%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $123,200 (56%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $38,900 (29%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Marshalltown the median monthly rent is $685. This is approximately $189 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $377 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $170 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
