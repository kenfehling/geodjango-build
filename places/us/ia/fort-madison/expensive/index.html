Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Fort Madison is $709 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $89 (14%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $353 (33%) lower, and is approximately $58 (7%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
