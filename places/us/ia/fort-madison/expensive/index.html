Home prices

The median home in Fort Madison costs $81,600. This is approximately $15,340 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $135,900 (62%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $60,200 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Madison the median monthly rent is $709. It is approximately $89 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $353 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $58 (7%) lower.
