In comparison to its nearby areas, Fort Madison is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Madison costs $81,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $15,340 (15%) lower. It is about $135,900 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $60,200 (42%) lower.

Rents

Fort Madison's median rent is $709 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $89 (14%) higher. It is roughly $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $58 (7%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
