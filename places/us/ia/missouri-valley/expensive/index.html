Home prices

The median home value in Missouri Valley is $92,000. It is about $25,080 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $125,500 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $67,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

Missouri Valley's median rent is $783 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $32 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $279 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $140 (15%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
