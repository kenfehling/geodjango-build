In comparison to other places in this area, Dakota City is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dakota City is valued at $83,400. It is approximately $4,360 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $134,100 (61%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $49,800 (37%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Dakota City is $725 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $155 (27%) higher.It is about $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $130 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
