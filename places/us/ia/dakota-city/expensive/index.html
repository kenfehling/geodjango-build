Compared to other places in this area, Dakota City is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Dakota City costs $83,400. This is about $4,360 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $134,100 (61%) lower, and it is roughly $49,800 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Dakota City is $725 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $155 (27%) higher. It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $130 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
