Home prices

Rents

In Kelley the median monthly rent is $1,563. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $690 (79%) higher. It is approximately $501 (47%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $708 (82%) higher.
