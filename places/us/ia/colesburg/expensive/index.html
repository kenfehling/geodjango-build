Compared to nearby areas, Colesburg is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Colesburg is $91,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $9,760 (9%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $126,000 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $50,300 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Colesburg the median monthly rent is $521. This is roughly $13 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $541 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $246 (32%) lower.
