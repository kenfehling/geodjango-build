Home prices

The median home price in Hudson is $194,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $40,640 (26%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $23,500 (10%) lower, and is about $52,200 (36%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Hudson the median monthly rent is $790. Compared to nearby areas this is is very similar. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $272 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $23 (2%) higher.
