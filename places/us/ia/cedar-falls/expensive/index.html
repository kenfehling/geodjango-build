In comparison to nearby areas, Cedar Falls is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cedar Falls costs $189,100. It is approximately $36,400 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $28,400 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $47,300 (33%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Cedar Falls the median rent is $875 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $170 (24%) higher. It is roughly $187 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $108 (14%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
