Compared to its surrounding areas, Cedar Falls is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cedar Falls is valued at $189,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $36,400 (23%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $28,400 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $47,300 (33%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Cedar Falls the median monthly rent is $875. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $170 (24%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $187 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $108 (14%) higher.
