In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cedar Falls is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cedar Falls costs $189,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $36,400 (23%) higher.It is about $28,400 (13%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $47,300 (33%) higher.

Rents

Cedar Falls's median monthly rent is $875. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $170 (24%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $187 (17%) lower, and is roughly $108 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
