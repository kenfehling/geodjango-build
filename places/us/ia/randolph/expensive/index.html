Home prices

The median home cost in Randolph is $82,800. This is about $10,380 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $134,700 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha is roughly $76,900 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Randolph the median rent is $625 a month. It is about $129 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha is about $298 (32%) lower.
