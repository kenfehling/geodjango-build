Home prices

The median home price in Dexter is $132,800. This is roughly $28,520 (27%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $84,700 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Dexter is $596 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.It is about $466 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $259 (30%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
