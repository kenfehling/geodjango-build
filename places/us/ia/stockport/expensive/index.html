Home prices

The median home in Stockport is valued at $57,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $6,500 (10%) lower. It is approximately $160,400 (73%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $84,700 (59%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Stockport the median monthly rent is $495. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $136 (21%) lower. It is approximately $567 (53%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $272 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
