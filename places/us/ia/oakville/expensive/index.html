Home prices

The median home cost in Oakville is $81,300. It is about $5,220 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $136,200 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $60,500 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Oakville is $680 per month. This is roughly $36 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $382 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $87 (11%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
