Home prices

Rents

In Oakville the median rent is $680 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $36 (5%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $382 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $87 (11%) lower.
