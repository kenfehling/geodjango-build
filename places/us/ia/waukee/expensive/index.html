Home prices

The median home value in Waukee is $238,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $24,920 (11%) higher. It is roughly $21,400 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $105,700 (79%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Waukee's median rent is $1,130 a month. This is approximately $246 (27%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $68 (6%) higher, and is roughly $275 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
