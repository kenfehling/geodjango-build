Home prices

The median home in Waukee is valued at $238,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $24,920 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $21,400 (9%) higher, and it is approximately $105,700 (79%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Waukee's median monthly rent is $1,130. It is about $246 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $68 (6%) higher, and is roughly $275 (32%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
