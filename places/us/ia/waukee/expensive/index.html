Home prices

The value of the median home in Waukee is $238,900. This is about $24,920 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $21,400 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $105,700 (79%) higher.

Rents

In Waukee the median rent is $1,130 a month. This is about $246 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $68 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $275 (32%) higher.
