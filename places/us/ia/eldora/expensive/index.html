Home prices

Rents

In Eldora the median monthly rent is $632. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $79 (14%) higher. It is roughly $430 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $223 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
