Similar to other places in this area, it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Cincinnati is valued at $52,800. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $164,700 (75%) lower, and is about $80,400 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Cincinnati's median rent is $608 per month. This is about $64 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $454 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $247 (28%) lower.
