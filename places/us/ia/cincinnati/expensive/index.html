Much like other places in this area, it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Des Moines, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cincinnati costs $52,800. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $164,700 (75%) lower, and it is roughly $80,400 (60%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Cincinnati's median monthly rent is $608. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $64 (11%) higher. It is about $454 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $247 (28%) lower.
