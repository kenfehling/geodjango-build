Home prices

The median home in Columbus Junction is valued at $94,600. It is roughly $11,960 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $122,900 (56%) lower, and is roughly $47,200 (33%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Columbus Junction the median rent is $608 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $136 (18%) lower.It is about $454 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $159 (20%) lower.
