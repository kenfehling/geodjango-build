Home prices

The cost of the median home in Columbus Junction is $94,600. It is roughly $11,960 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $122,900 (56%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $47,200 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Columbus Junction the median monthly rent is $608. This is roughly $136 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $454 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $159 (20%) lower.
