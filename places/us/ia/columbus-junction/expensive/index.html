Home prices

The median home in Columbus Junction costs $94,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $11,960 (14%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $122,900 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $47,200 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Columbus Junction the median rent is $608 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $136 (18%) lower. It is about $454 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $159 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
