Home prices

The median home in Polk City costs $249,000. It is roughly $52,220 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $31,500 (14%) higher, and it is roughly $115,800 (86%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Polk City's median monthly rent is $1,152. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $179 (18%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $90 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $297 (34%) higher.
