Compared to other places in this area, Polk City is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Polk City costs $249,000. This is approximately $52,220 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $31,500 (14%) higher, and it is approximately $115,800 (86%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Polk City the median rent is $1,152 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $179 (18%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $90 (8%) higher, and it is about $297 (34%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
