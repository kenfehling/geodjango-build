Home prices

Polk City's median home price is $249,000. This is approximately $52,220 (26%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $31,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $115,800 (86%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Polk City is $1,152 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $179 (18%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $90 (8%) higher, and is approximately $297 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
