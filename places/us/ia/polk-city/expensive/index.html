Compared to its surrounding areas, Polk City is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home price in Polk City is $249,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $52,220 (26%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $31,500 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $115,800 (86%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Polk City is $1,152. It is about $179 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $90 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $297 (34%) higher.
