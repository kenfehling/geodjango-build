Home prices

The median home price in University Park is $85,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $5,060 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $131,800 (60%) lower, and is roughly $47,500 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in University Park is $704 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is about $358 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $151 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
