The median home in University Park costs $85,700. This is roughly $5,060 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $131,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $47,500 (35%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In University Park the median rent is $704 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is approximately $358 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $151 (17%) lower.
