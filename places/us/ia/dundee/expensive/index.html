Compared to nearby areas, Dundee is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dundee costs $109,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $16,480 (17%) higher. It is approximately $107,900 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $32,200 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Dundee the median rent is $575 per month. This is approximately $9 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $487 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $192 (25%) lower.
