Home prices

The price of the median home in Dundee is $109,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16,480 (17%) higher.It is about $107,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $32,200 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dundee is $575. It is approximately $9 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $487 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $192 (25%) lower.
