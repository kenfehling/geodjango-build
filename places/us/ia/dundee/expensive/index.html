Compared to its surrounding areas, Dundee is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Dundee costs $109,600. This is about $16,480 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $107,900 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $32,200 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dundee is $575. It is approximately $9 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $487 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $192 (25%) lower.
