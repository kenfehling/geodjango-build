Home prices

The median home value in Conrad is $106,100. This is about $33,020 (45%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $111,400 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $27,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Conrad is $643 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $20 (3%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $419 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $212 (24%) lower.
