In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marion is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Marion costs $168,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $15,360 (8%) lower. It is about $48,800 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $26,900 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marion is $725. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $195 (21%) lower. It is approximately $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $42 (5%) lower.
