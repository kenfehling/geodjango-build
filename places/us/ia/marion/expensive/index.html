Compared to the surrounding areas, Marion is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marion costs $168,700. This is roughly $15,360 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $48,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $26,900 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Marion's median monthly rent is $725. This is roughly $195 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $42 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
