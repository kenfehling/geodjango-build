Home prices

Marion's median home price is $168,700. It is approximately $15,360 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $48,800 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $26,900 (18%) higher.

Rents

Marion's median monthly rent is $725. This is about $195 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and is approximately $42 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
