Compared to other places in this area, Orleans is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Mankato, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Orleans is valued at $387,500. This is approximately $69,500 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $170,000 (78%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Mankato is roughly $208,100 (115%) higher.

Rents

Orleans's median rent is $933 per month. It is roughly $217 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $129 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mankato it is approximately $34 (3%) higher.
