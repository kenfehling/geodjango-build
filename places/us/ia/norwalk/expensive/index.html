Home prices

The median home in Norwalk is valued at $202,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $5,620 (2%) lower. It is about $14,600 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $69,700 (52%) higher.

Rents

In Norwalk the median rent is $834 per month. It is roughly $51 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $228 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $21 (2%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
