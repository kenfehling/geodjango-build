Home prices

Norwalk's median home value is $202,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $5,620 (2%) lower.It is about $14,600 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $69,700 (52%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Norwalk is $834 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $51 (5%) lower.It is roughly $228 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $21 (2%) lower.
