Much like its surrounding areas, it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home cost in Norwalk is $202,900. It is roughly $5,620 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $14,600 (6%) lower, and is about $69,700 (52%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Norwalk's median monthly rent is $834. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $51 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $21 (2%) lower.
