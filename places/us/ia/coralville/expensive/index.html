Home prices

Coralville's median home value is $227,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $15,980 (6%) lower.It is roughly $10,200 (4%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $85,900 (60%) higher.

Rents

Coralville's median rent is $896 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $171 (16%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $129 (16%) higher.
