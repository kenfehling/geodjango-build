Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Charles is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Charles is $153,000. This is roughly $38,620 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $64,500 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $19,800 (14%) higher.

Rents

In St. Charles the median rent is $684 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $117 (14%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $378 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $171 (19%) lower.
