In comparison to other places in this area, St. Charles is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in St. Charles is valued at $153,000. It is approximately $38,620 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $64,500 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $19,800 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

St. Charles's median rent is $684 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $117 (14%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $378 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $171 (19%) lower.
