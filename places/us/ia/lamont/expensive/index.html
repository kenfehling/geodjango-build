Home prices

The median home cost in Lamont is $63,700. This is approximately $31,460 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $153,800 (70%) lower, and it is about $78,100 (55%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Lamont's median monthly rent is $646. Compared to nearby areas this is about $39 (6%) higher. It is about $416 (39%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $121 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
