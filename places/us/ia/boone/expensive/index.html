Home prices

Boone's median home price is $112,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $36,240 (24%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $105,300 (48%) lower, and is about $21,000 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Boone the median rent is $738 a month. This is about $142 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $324 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $117 (13%) lower.
