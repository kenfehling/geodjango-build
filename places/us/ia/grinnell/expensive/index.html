The cost of the median home in Grinnell is $137,500. This is approximately $51,320 (59%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $80,000 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $4,300 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Grinnell's median rent is $739 a month. It is roughly $135 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $116 (13%) lower.
