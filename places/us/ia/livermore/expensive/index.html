In comparison to the surrounding areas, Livermore is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Livermore is $55,000. It is approximately $14,280 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $162,500 (74%) lower, and it is about $78,200 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Livermore the median rent is $692 a month. This is roughly $89 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $370 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $163 (19%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
