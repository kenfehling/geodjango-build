Home prices

Rents

In Livermore the median rent is $692 a month. This is roughly $89 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $163 (19%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
