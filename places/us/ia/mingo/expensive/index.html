Compared to other places in this area, Mingo is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Mingo is $90,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $45,660 (33%) lower.It is roughly $126,700 (58%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $42,400 (31%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Mingo the median rent is $675 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $88 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $387 (36%) lower, and is roughly $180 (21%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
