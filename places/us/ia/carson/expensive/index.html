Home prices

Rents

Carson's median monthly rent is $688. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $42 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $374 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $235 (25%) lower.
