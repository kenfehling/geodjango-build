In comparison to nearby areas, Fort Atkinson is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Atkinson costs $125,000. This is approximately $21,680 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $16,800 (11%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Atkinson is $625. It is roughly $10 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $437 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $142 (18%) lower.
