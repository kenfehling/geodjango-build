Compared to its surrounding areas, Fort Atkinson is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fort Atkinson is $125,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $21,680 (20%) higher.It is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $16,800 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Atkinson the median rent is $625 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $10 (1%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $142 (18%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
