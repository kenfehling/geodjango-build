Compared to its nearby areas, Fort Atkinson is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Atkinson is valued at $125,000. It is about $21,680 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $16,800 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Atkinson is $625 per month. It is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $437 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $142 (18%) lower.
