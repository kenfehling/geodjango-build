In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Atkinson is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Atkinson costs $125,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $21,680 (20%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $92,500 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $16,800 (11%) lower.

Rents

Fort Atkinson's median rent is $625 a month. This is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $437 (41%) lower, and is about $142 (18%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
