Home prices

The median home cost in Story City is $145,500. It is approximately $15,120 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $72,000 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $12,300 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Story City is $736. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $326 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $119 (13%) lower.
