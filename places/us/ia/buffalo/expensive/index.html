Compared to its nearby areas, Buffalo is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Peoria however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Buffalo is valued at $121,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $9,000 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $96,400 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $4,600 (3%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Buffalo's median monthly rent is $478. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $302 (38%) lower. It is about $584 (54%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $328 (40%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
