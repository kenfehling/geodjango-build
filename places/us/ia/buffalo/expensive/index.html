Home prices

The median home in Buffalo is valued at $121,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $15,340 (14%) higher.It is about $96,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is roughly $4,600 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $478 per month. It is roughly $303 (38%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $584 (54%) lower, and is approximately $328 (40%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
