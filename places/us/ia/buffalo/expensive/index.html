Compared to other places in this area, Buffalo is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Peoria, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Buffalo is valued at $121,100. This is approximately $15,340 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $96,400 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $4,600 (3%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Buffalo is $478 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $303 (38%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $584 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is approximately $328 (40%) lower.
