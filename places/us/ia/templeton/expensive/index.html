Home prices

Templeton's median home price is $112,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $6,100 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $105,000 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $47,200 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Templeton is $539. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $61 (10%) lower. It is roughly $523 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is approximately $384 (41%) lower.
