In comparison to the surrounding areas, Templeton is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Omaha, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Templeton's median home price is $112,500. It is roughly $6,100 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $105,000 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is about $47,200 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Templeton the median rent is $539 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $61 (10%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $523 (49%) lower, and is approximately $384 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
