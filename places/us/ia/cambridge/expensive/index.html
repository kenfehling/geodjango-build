Compared to its surrounding areas, Cambridge is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cambridge is valued at $143,300. Compared to nearby areas it is about $13,380 (8%) lower. It is roughly $74,200 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $10,100 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cambridge is $900. This is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $162 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $45 (5%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
