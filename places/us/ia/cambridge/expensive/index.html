Home prices

The median home in Cambridge is valued at $143,300. It is approximately $13,380 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $74,200 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $10,100 (7%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Cambridge the median rent is $900 a month. This is approximately $15 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $162 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $45 (5%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
