In comparison to other places in this area, Cambridge is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Cambridge's median home value is $143,300. This is about $13,380 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $74,200 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $10,100 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Cambridge's median monthly rent is $900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15 (1%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $162 (15%) lower, and it is about $45 (5%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
