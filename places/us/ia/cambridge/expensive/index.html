Home prices

The median home in Cambridge costs $143,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $13,380 (8%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $74,200 (34%) lower, and it is about $10,100 (7%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Cambridge the median rent is $900 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $15 (1%) lower.It is approximately $162 (15%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $45 (5%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
