Compared to nearby areas, Cambridge is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moineshowever, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cambridge is valued at $143,300. This is roughly $13,380 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $74,200 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $10,100 (7%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Cambridge's median monthly rent is $900. This is approximately $15 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $162 (15%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $45 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
