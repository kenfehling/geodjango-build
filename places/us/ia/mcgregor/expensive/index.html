Home prices

The median home in McGregor costs $116,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $17,620 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $101,200 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $25,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

In McGregor the median monthly rent is $517. It is roughly $157 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $545 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $250 (32%) lower.
