Compared to other places in this area, McGregor is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in McGregor is valued at $116,300. This is approximately $17,620 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $101,200 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $25,500 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

McGregor's median monthly rent is $517. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $157 (23%) lower.It is approximately $545 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $250 (32%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
