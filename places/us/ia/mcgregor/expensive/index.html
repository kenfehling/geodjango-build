Compared to other places in this area, McGregor is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in McGregor is $116,300. This is approximately $17,620 (17%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $101,200 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $25,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

In McGregor the median monthly rent is $517. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $157 (23%) lower. It is approximately $545 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $250 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
