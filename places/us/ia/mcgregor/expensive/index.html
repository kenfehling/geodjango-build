Home prices

The median home price in McGregor is $116,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $17,620 (17%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $101,200 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $25,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

McGregor's median monthly rent is $517. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $157 (23%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $545 (51%) lower, and it is roughly $250 (32%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
