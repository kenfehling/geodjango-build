In comparison to its surrounding areas, McGregor is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in McGregor costs $116,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $17,620 (17%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $101,200 (46%) lower, and is approximately $25,500 (17%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In McGregor the median monthly rent is $517. It is approximately $157 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $545 (51%) lower, and is about $250 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
