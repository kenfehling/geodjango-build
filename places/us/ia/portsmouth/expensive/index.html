Home prices

The median home in Portsmouth is valued at $107,800. It is approximately $8,840 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $109,700 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $51,900 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Portsmouth the median monthly rent is $583. This is approximately $104 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $479 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is about $340 (36%) lower.
