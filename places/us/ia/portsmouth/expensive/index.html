Rents

Portsmouth's median monthly rent is $583. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $104 (15%) lower. It is roughly $479 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is approximately $340 (36%) lower.
