Home prices

The median home value in Mason City is $114,900. It is roughly $3,820 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $102,600 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Ames is about $91,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mason City is $723 per month. This is about $81 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $339 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $207 (22%) lower compared to the city of Ames.
