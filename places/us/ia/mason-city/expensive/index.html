Home prices

The median home in Mason City costs $114,900. This is about $3,820 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $102,600 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Ames is about $91,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

Mason City's median monthly rent is $723. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $81 (12%) higher. It is roughly $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $207 (22%) lower compared to the city of Ames.
