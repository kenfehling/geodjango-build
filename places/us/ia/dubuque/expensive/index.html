Home prices

Dubuque's median home cost is $143,500. This is approximately $45,960 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $74,000 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $102,800 (41%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

Dubuque's median rent is $791 a month. This is approximately $124 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $271 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $327 (29%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
