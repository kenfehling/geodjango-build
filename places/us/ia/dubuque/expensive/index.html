Home prices

The median home price in Dubuque is $143,500. This is roughly $45,960 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $74,000 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is roughly $102,800 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Dubuque the median rent is $791 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $124 (18%) higher.It is about $271 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $327 (29%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
