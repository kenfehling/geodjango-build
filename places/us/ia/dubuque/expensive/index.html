Compared to the surrounding areas, Dubuque is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Madison, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dubuque costs $143,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $45,960 (24%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $74,000 (34%) lower, and is about $102,800 (41%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Dubuque the median monthly rent is $791. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $124 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $271 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison is approximately $327 (29%) lower.
