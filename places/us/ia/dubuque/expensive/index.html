Compared to its surrounding areas, Dubuque is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Madison, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dubuque is valued at $143,500. It is roughly $45,960 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $74,000 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $102,800 (41%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

In Dubuque the median monthly rent is $791. This is approximately $124 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $271 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Madison it is approximately $327 (29%) lower.
