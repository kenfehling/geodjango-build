In comparison to other places in this area, Mystic is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

Mystic's median home value is $44,700. It is about $12,780 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $172,800 (79%) lower, and is about $88,500 (66%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Mystic's median monthly rent is $343. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $281 (44%) lower. It is approximately $719 (67%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $512 (59%) lower.
