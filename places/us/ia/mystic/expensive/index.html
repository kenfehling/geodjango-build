Home prices

Mystic's median home price is $44,700. This is roughly $12,780 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $172,800 (79%) lower, and is about $88,500 (66%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Mystic is $343 a month. This is about $281 (44%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $719 (67%) lower, and it is about $512 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
