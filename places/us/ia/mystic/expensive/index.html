Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mystic is $44,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $12,780 (22%) lower. It is approximately $172,800 (79%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $88,500 (66%) lower.

Rents

Mystic's median rent is $343 a month. It is approximately $281 (44%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $719 (67%) lower, and it is about $512 (59%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
