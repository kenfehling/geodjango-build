Rents

The median rent in Newhall is $517 a month. This is approximately $194 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $545 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $250 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
