Home prices

The median home in Stanwood costs $97,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $3,240 (3%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $119,600 (54%) lower, and is roughly $43,900 (30%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Stanwood the median monthly rent is $754. It is approximately $72 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $13 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
