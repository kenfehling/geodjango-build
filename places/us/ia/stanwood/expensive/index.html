Home prices

The median home value in Stanwood is $97,900. This is approximately $3,240 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $119,600 (54%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $43,900 (30%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Stanwood is $754. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $72 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
