Home prices

The median home value in Stanwood is $97,900. This is roughly $3,240 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $119,600 (54%) lower, and it is about $43,900 (30%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Stanwood's median rent is $754 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $72 (10%) higher. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
