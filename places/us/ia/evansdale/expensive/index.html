Home prices

The cost of the median home in Evansdale is $118,800. It is about $38,360 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $98,700 (45%) lower, and it is approximately $23,000 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Evansdale is $813 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $111 (15%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $249 (23%) lower, and is roughly $46 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
