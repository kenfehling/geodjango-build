In comparison to other places in this area, Evansdale is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Evansdale's median home value is $118,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $38,360 (24%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $98,700 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $23,000 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Evansdale is $813 per month. It is about $111 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $249 (23%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $46 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
