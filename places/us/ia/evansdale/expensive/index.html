In comparison to other places in this area, Evansdale is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Evansdale is $118,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $38,360 (24%) lower. It is roughly $98,700 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $23,000 (16%) lower.

Rents

Evansdale's median monthly rent is $813. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $111 (15%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and is roughly $46 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
