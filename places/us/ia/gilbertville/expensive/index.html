Home prices

The median home in Gilbertville is valued at $153,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $9,980 (6%) higher. It is approximately $64,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $11,500 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Gilbertville the median rent is $579 per month. It is about $108 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $483 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $188 (24%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
