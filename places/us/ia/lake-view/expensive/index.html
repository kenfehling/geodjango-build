Home prices

The median home in Lake View is valued at $119,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $34,420 (40%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $98,400 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $40,600 (25%) lower.

Rents

Lake View's median rent is $514 a month. It is roughly $64 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $548 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is roughly $409 (44%) lower.
