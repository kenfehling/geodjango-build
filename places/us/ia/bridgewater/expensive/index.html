The price of the median home in Bridgewater is $32,700. This is about $41,320 (55%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $184,800 (84%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is approximately $127,000 (79%) lower.

Rents

In Bridgewater the median rent is $444 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $80 (15%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $618 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha is approximately $479 (51%) lower.
