Home prices

The median home in Delaware is valued at $96,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $1,020 (1%) lower. It is about $121,000 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $45,300 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Delaware the median rent is $700 per month. It is approximately $115 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $67 (8%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
