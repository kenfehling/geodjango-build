Home prices

The median home in Delaware costs $96,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $1,020 (1%) lower.It is about $121,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $45,300 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Delaware is $700 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $115 (19%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $67 (8%) lower.
