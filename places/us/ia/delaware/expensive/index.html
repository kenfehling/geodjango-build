Home prices

The median home in Delaware costs $96,500. This is approximately $1,020 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $121,000 (55%) lower, and is about $45,300 (31%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Delaware is $700 a month. This is roughly $115 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and is roughly $67 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
