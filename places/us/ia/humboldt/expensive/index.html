Home prices

The median home in Humboldt costs $117,300. This is roughly $46,660 (66%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $100,200 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $15,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Humboldt the median monthly rent is $562. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $57 (9%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $500 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $293 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
