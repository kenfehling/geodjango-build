Compared to its surrounding areas, Humboldt is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Humboldt is $117,300. It is approximately $46,660 (66%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $100,200 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $15,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Humboldt is $562 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $57 (9%) lower. It is approximately $500 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $293 (34%) lower.
