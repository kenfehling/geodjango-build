In comparison to other places in this area, Humboldt is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Humboldt is valued at $117,300. This is about $46,660 (66%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $100,200 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $15,900 (11%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Humboldt the median monthly rent is $562. This is approximately $57 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $500 (47%) lower, and it is roughly $293 (34%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
