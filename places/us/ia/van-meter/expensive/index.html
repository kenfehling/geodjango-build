Home prices

The median home in Van Meter costs $169,300. This is approximately $20,660 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $48,200 (22%) lower, and is about $36,100 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Van Meter the median monthly rent is $778. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $67 (7%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $284 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $77 (9%) lower.
