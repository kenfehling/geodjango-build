Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clinton is $99,500. This is roughly $13,820 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $118,000 (54%) lower, and it is approximately $7,900 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Rockford.

Rents

In Clinton the median rent is $687 per month. It is about $37 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $375 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford is roughly $85 (11%) lower.
