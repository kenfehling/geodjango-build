Home prices

The median home in Traer is valued at $118,400. This is roughly $26,080 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $99,100 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $14,800 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Traer is $719 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $20 (2%) higher. It is approximately $343 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $136 (15%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
