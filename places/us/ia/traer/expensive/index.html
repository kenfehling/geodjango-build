Home prices

Traer's median home cost is $118,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $26,080 (28%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $99,100 (45%) lower, and is approximately $14,800 (11%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Traer's median rent is $719 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $20 (2%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $343 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $136 (15%) lower.
