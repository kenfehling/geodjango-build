Home prices

Cherokee's median home cost is $90,400. This is approximately $7,200 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $127,100 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $99,400 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cherokee is $551. This is roughly $55 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $511 (48%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $276 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
