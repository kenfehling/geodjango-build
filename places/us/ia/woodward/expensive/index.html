Home prices

The median home in Woodward is valued at $137,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $15,600 (12%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $80,000 (36%) lower, and is approximately $4,300 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Woodward is $763 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $64 (7%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $92 (10%) lower.
