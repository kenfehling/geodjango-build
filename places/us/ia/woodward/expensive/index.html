Compared to other places in this area, Woodward is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Woodward costs $137,500. This is about $15,600 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $80,000 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $4,300 (3%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Woodward the median rent is $763 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $64 (7%) lower.It is approximately $299 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $92 (10%) lower.
