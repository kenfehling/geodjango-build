Home prices

The value of the median home in Lisbon is $154,600. This is roughly $23,060 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $62,900 (28%) lower, and is roughly $12,800 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Lisbon the median rent is $707 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $25 (3%) lower.It is approximately $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $60 (7%) lower.
