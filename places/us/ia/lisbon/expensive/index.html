Compared to the surrounding areas, Lisbon is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home price in Lisbon is $154,600. It is approximately $23,060 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $62,900 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $12,800 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lisbon is $707 per month. It is about $25 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $60 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
