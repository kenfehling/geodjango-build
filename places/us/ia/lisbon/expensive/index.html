Compared to its surrounding areas, Lisbon is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home price in Lisbon is $154,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $23,060 (12%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $62,900 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $12,800 (9%) higher.

Rents

In Lisbon the median rent is $707 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $25 (3%) lower.It is roughly $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $60 (7%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
