Home prices

The median home in Lisbon is valued at $154,600. It is approximately $23,060 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $62,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $12,800 (9%) higher.

Rents

In Lisbon the median monthly rent is $707. This is approximately $25 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $355 (33%) lower, and it is about $60 (7%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
