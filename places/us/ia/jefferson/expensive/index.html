Home prices

The median home in Jefferson costs $94,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $41,160 (77%) higher.It is approximately $123,400 (56%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $39,100 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Jefferson the median rent is $648 per month. It is approximately $80 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $414 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $207 (24%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
