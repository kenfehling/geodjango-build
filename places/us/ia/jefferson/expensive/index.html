Home prices

Rents

Jefferson's median rent is $648 a month. This is roughly $80 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $414 (38%) lower, and is about $207 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
