Home prices

The price of the median home in Jefferson is $94,100. It is about $41,160 (77%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $123,400 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $39,100 (29%) lower.

Rents

Jefferson's median rent is $648 a month. This is approximately $80 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $414 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $207 (24%) lower.
