Home prices

The median home value in Grandview is $101,300. It is roughly $4,780 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $116,200 (53%) lower, and is roughly $40,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Grandview's median rent is $679 per month. It is roughly $30 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $383 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $88 (11%) lower.
