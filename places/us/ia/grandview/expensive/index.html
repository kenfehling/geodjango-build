Home prices

The cost of the median home in Grandview is $101,300. It is roughly $4,780 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $116,200 (53%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $40,500 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grandview is $679. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $30 (4%) lower.It is approximately $383 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $88 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
