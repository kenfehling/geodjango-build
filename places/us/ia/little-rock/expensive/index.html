Home prices

The value of the median home in Little Rock is $58,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $9,500 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $159,300 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $131,600 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Little Rock is $570. It is about $130 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $492 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $257 (31%) lower.
