In comparison to other places in this area, Little Rock is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Sioux Falls, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Little Rock is $58,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $9,500 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $159,300 (73%) lower, and is about $131,600 (69%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

Little Rock's median monthly rent is $570. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $130 (18%) lower. It is about $492 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $257 (31%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
