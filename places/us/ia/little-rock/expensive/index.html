Home prices

The median home in Little Rock costs $58,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $9,500 (14%) lower.It is roughly $159,300 (73%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $131,600 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Little Rock is $570. It is about $130 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $492 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $257 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
