Home prices

The cost of the median home in Little Rock is $58,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $9,500 (14%) lower.It is about $159,300 (73%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $131,600 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Little Rock the median rent is $570 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $130 (18%) lower.It is roughly $492 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $257 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
