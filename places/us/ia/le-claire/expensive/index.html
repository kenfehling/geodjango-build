Home prices

The median home value in Le Claire is $224,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $50,700 (29%) higher. It is about $7,300 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is roughly $99,100 (78%) higher.

Rents

In Le Claire the median monthly rent is $858. It is approximately $51 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $204 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $52 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.
