Home prices

The median home in Le Claire is valued at $224,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $50,700 (29%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $7,300 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $99,100 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Le Claire is $858 per month. It is about $51 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $204 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $52 (6%) higher compared to the city of Peoria.
