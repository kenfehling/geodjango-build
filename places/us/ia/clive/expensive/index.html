Home prices

Rents

Clive's median monthly rent is $984. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $37 (3%) lower.It is approximately $78 (7%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $129 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
