Home prices

Clive's median home value is $264,300. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $42,820 (19%) higher.It is about $46,800 (21%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $131,100 (98%) higher.

Rents

Clive's median rent is $984 a month. This is roughly $37 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $129 (15%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
