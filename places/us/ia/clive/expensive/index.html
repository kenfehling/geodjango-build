Home prices

The median home in Clive is valued at $264,300. It is about $42,820 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $46,800 (21%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $131,100 (98%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Clive's median rent is $984 per month. This is roughly $37 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $78 (7%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $129 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
