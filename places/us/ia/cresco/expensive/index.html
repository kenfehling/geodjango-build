Home prices

The median home in Cresco is valued at $85,600. This is about $23,560 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $131,900 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $114,500 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Cresco the median rent is $637 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $68 (12%) higher. It is approximately $425 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $337 (34%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
