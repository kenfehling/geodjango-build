In comparison to the surrounding areas, Centralia is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

Centralia's median home cost is $173,400. It is roughly $17,460 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $44,100 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $31,600 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Centralia is $742 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $58 (8%) higher. It is roughly $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $25 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
