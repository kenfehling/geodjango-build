Home prices

The median home in Centralia is valued at $173,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $17,460 (9%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $44,100 (20%) lower, and is roughly $31,600 (22%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Centralia the median rent is $742 per month. This is roughly $58 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $25 (3%) lower.
