Compared to other places in this area, Centralia is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Centralia is $173,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $17,460 (9%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $44,100 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $31,600 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Centralia the median rent is $742 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $58 (8%) higher.It is roughly $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $25 (3%) lower.
