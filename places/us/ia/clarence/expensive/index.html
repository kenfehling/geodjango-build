Home prices

The median home in Clarence is valued at $97,400. This is about $6,840 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $120,100 (55%) lower, and is roughly $44,400 (31%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Clarence's median rent is $532 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $172 (24%) lower. It is about $530 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $235 (30%) lower.
