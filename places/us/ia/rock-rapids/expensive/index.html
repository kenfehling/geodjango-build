Compared to its surrounding areas, Rock Rapids is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Sioux Falls, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rock Rapids is valued at $140,900. This is approximately $54,580 (63%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $76,600 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $48,900 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

In Rock Rapids the median monthly rent is $561. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $169 (23%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $501 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $266 (32%) lower.
