Home prices

The median home in Rock Rapids is valued at $140,900. This is approximately $54,580 (63%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $76,600 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $48,900 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Rock Rapids the median rent is $561 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $169 (23%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $501 (47%) lower, and it is roughly $266 (32%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
