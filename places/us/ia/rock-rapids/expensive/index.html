Home prices

The price of the median home in Rock Rapids is $140,900. This is roughly $54,580 (63%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $76,600 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $48,900 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

In Rock Rapids the median monthly rent is $561. It is about $169 (23%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $501 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $266 (32%) lower.
