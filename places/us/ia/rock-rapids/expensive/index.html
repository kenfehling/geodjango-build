Home prices

Rock Rapids's median home cost is $140,900. It is about $54,580 (63%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $76,600 (35%) lower, and is roughly $48,900 (25%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

Rock Rapids's median monthly rent is $561. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $169 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $501 (47%) lower, and it is about $266 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
