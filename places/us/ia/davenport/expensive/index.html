Home prices

The median home value in Davenport is $131,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $52,100 (28%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $85,800 (39%) lower, and is about $6,000 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In Davenport the median rent is $771 a month. It is approximately $71 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $291 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria is about $35 (4%) lower.
