Home prices

The median home in Davenport is valued at $131,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $52,100 (28%) lower. It is roughly $85,800 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $6,000 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Davenport's median rent is $771 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $71 (8%) lower. It is approximately $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Peoria it is roughly $35 (4%) lower.
