In comparison to its nearby areas, Davenport is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Peoria, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Davenport is $131,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $52,100 (28%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $85,800 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria is approximately $6,000 (4%) higher.

Rents

Davenport's median monthly rent is $771. This is approximately $71 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $291 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $35 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
