Compared to the surrounding areas, West Burlington is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in West Burlington is $107,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $12,380 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $110,100 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $34,400 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in West Burlington is $748 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $38 (5%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $314 (29%) lower, and is about $19 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
