In comparison to other places in this area, West Burlington is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Burlington costs $107,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $12,380 (13%) higher.It is roughly $110,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $34,400 (24%) lower.

Rents

West Burlington's median rent is $748 per month. It is about $38 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $314 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $19 (2%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
