Home prices

The median home cost in West Burlington is $107,400. It is about $12,380 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $110,100 (50%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $34,400 (24%) lower.

Rents

In West Burlington the median monthly rent is $748. It is approximately $38 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $314 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $19 (2%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
