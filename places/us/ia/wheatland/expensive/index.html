Home prices

The median home price in Wheatland is $96,500. This is about $3,980 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $121,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $45,300 (31%) lower.

Rents

Wheatland's median rent is $739 a month. It is roughly $76 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $323 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $28 (3%) lower.
