Rents

The median monthly rent in Wheatland is $739. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $76 (11%) higher. It is approximately $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $28 (3%) lower.
