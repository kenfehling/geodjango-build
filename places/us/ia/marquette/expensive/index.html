Home prices

The median home in Marquette costs $107,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $2,000 (1%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $110,000 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $34,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Marquette the median rent is $575 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $55 (8%) lower.It is approximately $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $192 (25%) lower.
