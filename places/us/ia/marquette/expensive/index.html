Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marquette is $107,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $2,000 (1%) higher.It is approximately $110,000 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $34,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Marquette is $575 a month. This is approximately $55 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $487 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $192 (25%) lower.
