Much like its surrounding areas, it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marquette is $107,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $2,000 (1%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $110,000 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $34,300 (24%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marquette is $575. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $55 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $487 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $192 (25%) lower.
