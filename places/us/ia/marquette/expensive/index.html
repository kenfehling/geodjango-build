Home prices

Marquette's median home value is $107,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $2,000 (1%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $110,000 (50%) lower, and is roughly $34,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marquette is $575. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $55 (8%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $487 (45%) lower, and it is approximately $192 (25%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
