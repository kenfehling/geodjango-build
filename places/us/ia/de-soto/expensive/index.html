Compared to other places in this area, De Soto is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in De Soto is valued at $143,000. It is approximately $27,940 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $74,500 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $9,800 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

De Soto's median monthly rent is $690. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $144 (17%) lower. It is approximately $372 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $165 (19%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
