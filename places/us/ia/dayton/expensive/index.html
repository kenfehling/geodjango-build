In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dayton is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dayton is $86,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $14,820 (20%) higher.It is about $130,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $46,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $509. This is roughly $34 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $553 (52%) lower, and it is about $346 (40%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
