Dayton's median home value is $86,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $14,820 (20%) higher. It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $46,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $509. It is roughly $34 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $553 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $346 (40%) lower.
