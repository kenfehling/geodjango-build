Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dayton is $86,700. It is about $14,820 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $130,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $46,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Dayton is $509 per month. It is approximately $34 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $553 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $346 (40%) lower.
