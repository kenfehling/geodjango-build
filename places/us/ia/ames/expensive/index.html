Home prices

The median home in Ames is valued at $205,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $38,120 (22%) higher.It is roughly $11,600 (5%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $72,700 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ames is $930. This is about $11 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $132 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $75 (8%) higher.
