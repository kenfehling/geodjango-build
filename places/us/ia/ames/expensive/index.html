Compared to other places in this area, Ames is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Ames costs $205,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $38,120 (22%) higher. It is approximately $11,600 (5%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $72,700 (54%) higher.

Rents

In Ames the median rent is $930 per month. This is approximately $11 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $75 (8%) higher.
