Home prices

The median home in Madrid is valued at $135,700. This is roughly $40,700 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $81,800 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $2,500 (1%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Madrid's median rent is $756 a month. It is roughly $157 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $306 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $99 (11%) lower.
