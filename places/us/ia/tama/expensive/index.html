Home prices

The price of the median home in Tama is $86,400. This is roughly $7,180 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $131,100 (60%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $46,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Tama the median rent is $717 a month. This is roughly $43 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $345 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $138 (16%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
