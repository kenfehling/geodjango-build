Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Davis City is $608. It is approximately $26 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $454 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $247 (28%) lower.
