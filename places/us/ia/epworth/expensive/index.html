Home prices

The median home in Epworth costs $167,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $28,640 (14%) lower. It is about $50,100 (23%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $25,600 (18%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Epworth is $704. This is about $17 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $358 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $63 (8%) lower.
