Home prices

The cost of the median home in Epworth is $167,400. This is roughly $28,640 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $50,100 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $25,600 (18%) higher.

Rents

Epworth's median rent is $704 per month. This is roughly $17 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $358 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $63 (8%) lower.
