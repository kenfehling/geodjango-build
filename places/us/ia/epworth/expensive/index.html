Home prices

Rents

In Epworth the median monthly rent is $704. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $17 (2%) lower. It is roughly $358 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $63 (8%) lower.
