Home prices

The median home in Toledo costs $87,700. It is roughly $8,740 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $129,800 (59%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $45,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

Toledo's median monthly rent is $755. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $307 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $100 (11%) lower.
