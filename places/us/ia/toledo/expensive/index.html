Home prices

The median home in Toledo is valued at $87,700. It is about $8,740 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $129,800 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $45,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Toledo is $755 a month. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $100 (11%) lower.
