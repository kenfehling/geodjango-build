In comparison to its surrounding areas, Toledo is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Des Moines, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Toledo's median home cost is $87,700. This is roughly $8,740 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $129,800 (59%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $45,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Toledo's median rent is $755 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $307 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $100 (11%) lower.
