In comparison to its surrounding areas, Toledo is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Toledo's median home value is $87,700. This is approximately $8,740 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $129,800 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $45,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Toledo is $755 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is about $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $100 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
