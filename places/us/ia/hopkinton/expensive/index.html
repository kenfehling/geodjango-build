Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hopkinton is $417. This is approximately $216 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $645 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $350 (45%) lower.
