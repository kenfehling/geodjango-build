Home prices

Rents

In Hopkinton the median rent is $417 a month. This is roughly $216 (34%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $645 (60%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $350 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
