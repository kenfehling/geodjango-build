Rents

Hopkinton's median rent is $417 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $216 (34%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $645 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $350 (45%) lower.
