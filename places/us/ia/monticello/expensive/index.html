In comparison to other places in this area, Monticello is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monticello costs $120,400. This is roughly $24,220 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $97,100 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $21,400 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Monticello the median rent is $613 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $35 (5%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $449 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $154 (20%) lower.
