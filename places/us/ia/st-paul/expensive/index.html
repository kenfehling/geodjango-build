Home prices

St. Paul's median home cost is $143,800. It is approximately $52,700 (57%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $73,700 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $2,000 (1%) higher.

Rents

In St. Paul the median rent is $688 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $80 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $374 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $79 (10%) lower.
