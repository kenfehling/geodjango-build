Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Paul is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Paul is $143,800. This is about $52,700 (57%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $73,700 (33%) lower, and is approximately $2,000 (1%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Paul is $688. It is approximately $80 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $374 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $79 (10%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
