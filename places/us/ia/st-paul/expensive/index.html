Home prices

The value of the median home in St. Paul is $143,800. It is roughly $52,700 (57%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $73,700 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $2,000 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in St. Paul is $688 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $80 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $374 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $79 (10%) lower.
