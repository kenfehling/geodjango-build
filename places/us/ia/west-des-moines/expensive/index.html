Home prices

The median home in West Des Moines costs $218,500. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $31,000 (12%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $85,300 (64%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Des Moines is $1,017. It is approximately $34 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $45 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $162 (18%) higher.
