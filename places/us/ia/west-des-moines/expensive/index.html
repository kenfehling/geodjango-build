Home prices

The median home in West Des Moines is valued at $218,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $31,000 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and it is about $85,300 (64%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Des Moines is $1,017. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $34 (3%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $45 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $162 (18%) higher.
