Home prices

Rents

West Des Moines's median monthly rent is $1,017. This is roughly $34 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $45 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $162 (18%) higher.
