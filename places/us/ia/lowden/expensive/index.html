Compared to other places in this area, Lowden is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lowden is $98,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $7,660 (8%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $118,700 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $43,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Lowden the median rent is $745 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $131 (21%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $317 (29%) lower, and is roughly $22 (2%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
