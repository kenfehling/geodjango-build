Compared to the surrounding areas, Lowden is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Lowden costs $98,800. This is about $7,660 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $43,000 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lowden is $745 a month. This is about $131 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $317 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $22 (2%) lower.
