Compared to the surrounding areas, Lowden is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

Lowden's median home value is $98,800. It is about $7,660 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $43,000 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Lowden the median monthly rent is $745. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $131 (21%) higher. It is about $317 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $22 (2%) lower.
