Compared to its surrounding areas, Sheffield is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sheffield is $97,400. This is about $20,900 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $120,100 (55%) lower, and is roughly $35,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sheffield is $671. It is about $51 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $391 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $184 (21%) lower.
