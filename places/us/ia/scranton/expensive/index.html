Home prices

The median home in Scranton costs $44,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $31,380 (41%) lower. It is approximately $172,800 (79%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $88,500 (66%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Scranton the median monthly rent is $623. This is about $11 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $439 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $232 (27%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
