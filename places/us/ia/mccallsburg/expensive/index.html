Home prices

The median home cost in McCallsburg is $125,400. It is approximately $20,820 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $92,100 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $7,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

In McCallsburg the median rent is $833 a month. This is approximately $160 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $229 (21%) lower, and it is about $22 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
