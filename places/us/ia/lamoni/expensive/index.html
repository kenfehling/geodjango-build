Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lamoni is $614. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $48 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $448 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $241 (28%) lower.
