Compared to other places in this area, Beaverdale is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

Beaverdale's median home cost is $95,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $9,900 (9%) lower. It is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $46,000 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Beaverdale the median rent is $861 per month. It is roughly $121 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $201 (18%) lower, and is approximately $94 (12%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
