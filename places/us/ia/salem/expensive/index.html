In comparison to the surrounding areas, Salem is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Salem is valued at $60,000. This is roughly $30,700 (33%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $157,500 (72%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $81,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

Salem's median monthly rent is $645. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $51 (7%) lower. It is roughly $417 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $122 (15%) lower.
