Home prices

The cost of the median home in Salem is $60,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $30,700 (33%) lower.It is about $157,500 (72%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $81,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Salem the median rent is $645 a month. It is about $51 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $417 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $122 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
