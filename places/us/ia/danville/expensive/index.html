Home prices

The median home in Danville costs $110,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $7,620 (7%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $107,100 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $31,400 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Danville the median rent is $703 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $69 (8%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $359 (33%) lower, and is roughly $64 (8%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
