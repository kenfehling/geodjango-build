Compared to its surrounding areas, Danville is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Danville is $110,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $7,620 (7%) higher. It is roughly $107,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $31,400 (22%) lower.

Rents

Danville's median monthly rent is $703. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $69 (8%) lower. It is roughly $359 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $64 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
