In comparison to nearby areas, Danville is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Danville costs $110,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $7,620 (7%) higher. It is about $107,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $31,400 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Danville the median monthly rent is $703. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $69 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $359 (33%) lower, and is about $64 (8%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
