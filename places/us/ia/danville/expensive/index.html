Compared to other places in this area, Danville is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Danville costs $110,400. This is roughly $7,620 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $107,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $31,400 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Danville's median monthly rent is $703. It is approximately $69 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $359 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $64 (8%) lower.
