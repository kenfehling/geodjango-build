Home prices

The median home price in Mitchellville is $137,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $11,620 (7%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $80,200 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $4,100 (3%) higher.

Rents

Mitchellville's median monthly rent is $698. It is about $113 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $364 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $157 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
