Home prices

The median home in Keota is valued at $88,300. This is about $6,720 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $129,200 (59%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $53,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Keota is $700 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $10 (1%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $362 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $67 (8%) lower.
