The median home in Keota is valued at $88,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $6,720 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $129,200 (59%) lower, and is about $53,500 (37%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Keota the median monthly rent is $700. It is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $67 (8%) lower.
