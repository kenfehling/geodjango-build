Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Okoboji is $974 per month. This is roughly $116 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $88 (8%) lower, and it is about $322 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge.
