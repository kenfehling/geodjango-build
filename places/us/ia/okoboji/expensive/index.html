Compared to its surrounding areas, Okoboji is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Fort Dodge.

Home prices

The median home in Okoboji costs $333,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $53,300 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $116,200 (53%) higher, and is approximately $240,100 (256%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge.

Rents

Okoboji's median rent is $974 per month. It is approximately $116 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $88 (8%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Dodge it is roughly $322 (49%) higher.
