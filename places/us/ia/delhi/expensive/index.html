Rents

The median rent in Delhi is $638 per month. It is roughly $70 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $424 (39%) lower, and it is roughly $129 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
