Home prices

Rents

Cascade's median rent is $755 per month. It is approximately $84 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
