Compared to its surrounding areas, Cascade is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Cascade is valued at $147,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $11,740 (8%) higher. It is about $70,500 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $5,200 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cascade is $755. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $84 (12%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $307 (28%) lower, and is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
