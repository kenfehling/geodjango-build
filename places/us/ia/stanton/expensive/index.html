Home prices

Stanton's median home cost is $78,200. This is roughly $6,480 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $139,300 (64%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $81,500 (51%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Stanton's median rent is $809 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $144 (21%) higher.It is roughly $253 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Omaha is roughly $114 (12%) lower.
