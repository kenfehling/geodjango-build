Compared to the surrounding areas, Villisca is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in Villisca is valued at $63,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $15,180 (19%) lower.It is approximately $154,200 (70%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is approximately $96,400 (60%) lower.

Rents

Villisca's median rent is $611 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $24 (3%) lower.It is approximately $451 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $312 (33%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
