Compared to the surrounding areas, Villisca is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Villisca is $63,300. It is roughly $15,180 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $154,200 (70%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $96,400 (60%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Villisca the median rent is $611 per month. This is roughly $24 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $451 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $312 (33%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
