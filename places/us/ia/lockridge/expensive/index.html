Home prices

The median home in Lockridge is valued at $60,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $8,700 (12%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $157,500 (72%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $81,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lockridge is $672. It is approximately $11 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $390 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $95 (12%) lower.
