Compared to other places in this area, Nashua is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Nashua is valued at $91,800. It is about $6,140 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $125,700 (57%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $50,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nashua is $531. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $59 (9%) lower. It is roughly $531 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $236 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
