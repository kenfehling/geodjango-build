Home prices

Rents

Nashua's median rent is $531 a month. It is approximately $59 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $531 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $236 (30%) lower.
