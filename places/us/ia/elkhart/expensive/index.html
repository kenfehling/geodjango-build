In comparison to the surrounding areas, Elkhart is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Elkhart is valued at $150,500. This is about $55,020 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $67,000 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $17,300 (12%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elkhart is $920. It is roughly $31 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $142 (13%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $65 (7%) higher.
