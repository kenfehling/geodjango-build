Rents

In Grand Junction the median rent is $615 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $15 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $447 (42%) lower, and is roughly $240 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
