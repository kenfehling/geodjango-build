Home prices

The median home value in Grand Junction is $66,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $11,200 (14%) lower.It is about $151,100 (69%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $66,800 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Grand Junction is $615 a month. It is about $15 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $447 (42%) lower, and is approximately $240 (28%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
