Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grand Junction is $615. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $15 (2%) higher. It is approximately $447 (42%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $240 (28%) lower.
