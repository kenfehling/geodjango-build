Home prices

Grand Junction's median home cost is $66,400. It is about $11,200 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $151,100 (69%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $66,800 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Grand Junction's median rent is $615 per month. This is approximately $15 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $447 (42%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $240 (28%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
