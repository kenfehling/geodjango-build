Home prices

The median home in Keosauqua is valued at $100,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $47,020 (88%) higher. It is about $117,500 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $41,800 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Keosauqua the median monthly rent is $591. It is approximately $7 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $471 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $176 (22%) lower.
