Home prices

The median home in Burlington is valued at $93,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $4,120 (4%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $124,300 (57%) lower, and it is roughly $48,600 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Burlington is $799 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $125 (18%) higher. It is approximately $263 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $32 (4%) higher.
