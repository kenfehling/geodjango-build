Rents

The median monthly rent in Burlington is $799. This is approximately $125 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $263 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $32 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
