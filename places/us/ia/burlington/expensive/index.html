Much like other places in this area, it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

Burlington's median home price is $93,200. It is about $4,120 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $124,300 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $48,600 (34%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Burlington the median monthly rent is $799. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $125 (18%) higher. It is about $263 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $32 (4%) higher.
