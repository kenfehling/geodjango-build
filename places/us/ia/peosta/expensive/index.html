Compared to its surrounding areas, Peosta is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Peosta is $231,600. This is approximately $51,540 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $14,100 (6%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $89,800 (63%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Peosta's median rent is $560 a month. It is approximately $202 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $502 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $207 (26%) lower.
