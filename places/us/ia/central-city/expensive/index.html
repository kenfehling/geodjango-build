In comparison to the surrounding areas, Central City is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Central City is $135,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $17,320 (14%) higher. It is about $81,800 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $6,100 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Central City the median rent is $505 per month. It is about $139 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $557 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $262 (34%) lower.
