Home prices

Rents

Central City's median monthly rent is $505. This is approximately $139 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $557 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $262 (34%) lower.
