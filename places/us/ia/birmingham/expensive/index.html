Compared to nearby areas, Birmingham is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Birmingham costs $64,200. This is roughly $13,320 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $153,300 (70%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $77,600 (54%) lower.

Rents

Birmingham's median rent is $596 per month. This is approximately $47 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $466 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $171 (22%) lower.
