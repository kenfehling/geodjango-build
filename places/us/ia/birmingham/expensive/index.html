Home prices

The median home in Birmingham is valued at $64,200. This is roughly $13,320 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $153,300 (70%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $77,600 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Birmingham's median rent is $596 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $47 (7%) lower. It is approximately $466 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $171 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
