Compared to other places in this area, Birmingham is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Birmingham is valued at $64,200. It is approximately $13,320 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $153,300 (70%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $77,600 (54%) lower.

Rents

Birmingham's median monthly rent is $596. It is roughly $47 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $466 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $171 (22%) lower.
