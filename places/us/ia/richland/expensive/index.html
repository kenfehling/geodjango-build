Home prices

The median home in Richland costs $86,400. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $26,840 (23%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $131,100 (60%) lower, and it is approximately $55,400 (39%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Richland's median monthly rent is $671. This is about $22 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $391 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $96 (12%) lower.
