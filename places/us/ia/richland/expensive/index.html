Home prices

The median home in Richland is valued at $86,400. This is approximately $26,840 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $131,100 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $55,400 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Richland the median rent is $671 a month. It is roughly $22 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $391 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $96 (12%) lower.
