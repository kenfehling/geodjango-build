Home prices

The median home in Urbana is valued at $151,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $19,280 (14%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $65,900 (30%) lower, and is approximately $9,800 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Urbana is $637 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $76 (10%) lower.It is approximately $425 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $130 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
