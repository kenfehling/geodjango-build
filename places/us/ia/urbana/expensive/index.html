Home prices

The median home in Urbana costs $151,600. This is about $19,280 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $65,900 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $9,800 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Urbana is $637 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $76 (10%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $425 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $130 (16%) lower.
