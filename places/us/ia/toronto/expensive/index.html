In comparison to its surrounding areas, Toronto is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

Toronto's median home price is $72,000. This is approximately $9,020 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $145,500 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $69,800 (49%) lower.

Rents

Toronto's median monthly rent is $596. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $21 (3%) lower. It is roughly $466 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $171 (22%) lower.
