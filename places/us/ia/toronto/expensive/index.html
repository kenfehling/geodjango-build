Compared to the surrounding areas, Toronto is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Toronto is $72,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $9,020 (11%) lower. It is about $145,500 (66%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $69,800 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Toronto the median monthly rent is $596. Compared to other places in this area it is about $21 (3%) lower. It is about $466 (43%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $171 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
