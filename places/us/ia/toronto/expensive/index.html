Home prices

The value of the median home in Toronto is $72,000. This is roughly $9,020 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $145,500 (66%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $69,800 (49%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Toronto is $596. It is about $21 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $466 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $171 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
