Home prices

The median home price in Toronto is $72,000. It is about $9,020 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $145,500 (66%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $69,800 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Toronto is $596. This is roughly $21 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $466 (43%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $171 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
