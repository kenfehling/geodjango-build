Home prices

The value of the median home in New Hampton is $107,100. It is about $19,800 (22%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $110,400 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $34,700 (24%) lower.

Rents

New Hampton's median monthly rent is $707. This is about $148 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $355 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $60 (7%) lower.
