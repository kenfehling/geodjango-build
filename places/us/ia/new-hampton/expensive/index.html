Home prices

Rents

In New Hampton the median rent is $707 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $148 (26%) higher. It is roughly $355 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $60 (7%) lower.
