Home prices

Hills's median home cost is $158,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $37,840 (19%) lower. It is roughly $58,900 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $16,800 (11%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Hills the median rent is $990 per month. It is roughly $66 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $72 (6%) lower, and is approximately $223 (29%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
