Home prices

Kanawha's median home cost is $51,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $11,280 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $165,900 (76%) lower, and is approximately $81,600 (61%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Kanawha's median monthly rent is $569. It is roughly $98 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $493 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $286 (33%) lower.
