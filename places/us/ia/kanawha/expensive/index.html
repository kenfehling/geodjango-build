Rents

The median rent in Kanawha is $569 a month. This is roughly $98 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $493 (46%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $286 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
