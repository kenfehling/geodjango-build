Compared to its nearby areas, Fairfield is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fairfield is $114,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $11,700 (11%) higher. It is roughly $103,300 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $27,600 (19%) lower.

Rents

Fairfield's median monthly rent is $681. This is about $41 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $381 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $86 (11%) lower.
