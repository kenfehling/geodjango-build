Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairfield is $114,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $11,700 (11%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $103,300 (47%) lower, and is about $27,600 (19%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfield is $681. It is about $41 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $381 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $86 (11%) lower.
