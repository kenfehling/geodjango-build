Compared to other places in this area, Somers is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Somers costs $45,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $11,740 (20%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $172,000 (79%) lower, and is approximately $87,700 (65%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Somers the median rent is $731 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $223 (44%) higher. It is about $331 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $124 (14%) lower.
