Home prices

The median home in Nevada costs $138,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $18,060 (11%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $78,800 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $5,500 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Nevada the median monthly rent is $650. It is about $158 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $412 (38%) lower, and is approximately $205 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
