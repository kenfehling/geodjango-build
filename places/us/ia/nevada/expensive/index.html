In comparison to nearby areas, Nevada is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Nevada is valued at $138,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $18,060 (11%) lower. It is approximately $78,800 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $5,500 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Nevada is $650 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $158 (19%) lower. It is roughly $412 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $205 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
