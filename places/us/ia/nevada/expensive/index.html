In comparison to its surrounding areas, Nevada is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Nevada is valued at $138,700. It is roughly $18,060 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $78,800 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $5,500 (4%) higher.

Rents

Nevada's median monthly rent is $650. It is about $158 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $412 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $205 (23%) lower.
