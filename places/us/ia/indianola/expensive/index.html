Home prices

The price of the median home in Indianola is $163,300. This is about $10,720 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $54,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $30,100 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Indianola the median rent is $784 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $76 (8%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $278 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $71 (8%) lower.
