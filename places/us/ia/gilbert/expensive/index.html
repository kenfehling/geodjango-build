Home prices

The price of the median home in Gilbert is $206,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $58,540 (39%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $11,100 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $73,200 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Gilbert is $710 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $21 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $352 (33%) lower, and it is about $145 (16%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
