Home prices

The value of the median home in Gilbert is $206,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $58,540 (39%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $11,100 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $73,200 (54%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gilbert is $710. This is approximately $21 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $352 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $145 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
