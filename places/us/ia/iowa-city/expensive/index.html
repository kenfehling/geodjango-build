Compared to the surrounding areas, Iowa City is a little less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Iowa City is $215,100. It is approximately $12,060 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $2,400 (1%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $73,300 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Iowa City is $967. This is about $131 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $95 (8%) lower, and it is roughly $200 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
