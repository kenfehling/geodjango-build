In comparison to nearby areas, Iowa City is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Iowa City costs $215,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $12,060 (5%) lower.It is roughly $2,400 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $73,300 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Iowa City the median rent is $967 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $131 (11%) lower.It is approximately $95 (8%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $200 (26%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
