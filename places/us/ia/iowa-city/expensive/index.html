Compared to nearby areas, Iowa City is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home value in Iowa City is $215,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $12,060 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $2,400 (1%) lower, and is approximately $73,300 (51%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Iowa City the median monthly rent is $967. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $131 (11%) lower. It is approximately $95 (8%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $200 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
