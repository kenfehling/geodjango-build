Compared to other places in this area, Iowa City is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Iowa City is $215,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $12,060 (5%) lower. It is about $2,400 (1%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $73,300 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Iowa City the median rent is $967 a month. It is about $131 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $95 (8%) lower, and it is about $200 (26%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
