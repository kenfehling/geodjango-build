Home prices

The median home in Hartford costs $103,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $78,740 (43%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $113,800 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $29,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Hartford the median monthly rent is $1,117. This is roughly $321 (40%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $55 (5%) higher, and it is roughly $262 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
