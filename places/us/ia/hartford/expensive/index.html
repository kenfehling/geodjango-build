Home prices

The median home cost in Hartford is $103,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $78,740 (43%) lower.It is about $113,800 (52%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $29,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hartford is $1,117 a month. It is approximately $321 (40%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $262 (30%) higher.
