Compared to its surrounding areas, Hartford is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Hartford is $103,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $78,740 (43%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $113,800 (52%) lower, and is approximately $29,500 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Hartford the median monthly rent is $1,117. This is about $321 (40%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $55 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $262 (30%) higher.
