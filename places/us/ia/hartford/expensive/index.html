In comparison to its nearby areas, Hartford is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hartford is $103,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $78,740 (43%) lower. It is approximately $113,800 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $29,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hartford is $1,117 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $321 (40%) higher. It is approximately $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $262 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
