In comparison to nearby areas, Hartford is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hartford is $103,700. This is approximately $78,740 (43%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $113,800 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $29,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

Hartford's median monthly rent is $1,117. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $321 (40%) higher. It is roughly $55 (5%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $262 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
