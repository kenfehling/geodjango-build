Home prices

The median home in Long Grove costs $255,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $85,180 (49%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $38,300 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is roughly $130,100 (103%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Long Grove is $1,536 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $620 (67%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $474 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Peoria is approximately $730 (90%) higher.
