Home prices

The cost of the median home in Thornton is $69,600. This is approximately $5,260 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $147,900 (68%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $63,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

Thornton's median rent is $541 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $53 (8%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $521 (49%) lower, and is about $314 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
