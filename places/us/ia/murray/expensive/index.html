Compared to other places in this area, Murray is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Des Moines.

Home prices

The median home in Murray is valued at $55,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $20,740 (27%) lower. It is approximately $161,800 (74%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $77,500 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Murray's median rent is $843 a month. It is about $53 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $219 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $12 (1%) lower.
