Home prices

The price of the median home in Pleasant Hill is $201,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $33,340 (19%) higher.It is about $16,000 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $68,300 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pleasant Hill is $1,002. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $210 (26%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $60 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $147 (17%) higher.
