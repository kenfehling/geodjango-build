Compared to its surrounding areas, Pleasant Hill is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Pleasant Hill is $201,500. It is roughly $33,340 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $16,000 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $68,300 (51%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Pleasant Hill is $1,002 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $210 (26%) higher. It is about $60 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $147 (17%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
