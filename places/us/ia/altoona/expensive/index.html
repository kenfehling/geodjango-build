Home prices

The median home in Altoona is valued at $196,600. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $20,900 (9%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $63,400 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Altoona the median rent is $931 a month. It is roughly $131 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $131 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $76 (8%) higher.
