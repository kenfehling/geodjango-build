Home prices

The median home in Altoona is valued at $196,600. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $20,900 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $63,400 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Altoona is $931. It is about $131 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $131 (12%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $76 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
