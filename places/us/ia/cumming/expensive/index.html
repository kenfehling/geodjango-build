Home prices

The median home cost in Cumming is $354,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $172,460 (94%) higher. It is about $137,200 (63%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $221,500 (166%) higher.

Rents

In Cumming the median monthly rent is $950. It is approximately $89 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $112 (10%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $95 (11%) higher.
