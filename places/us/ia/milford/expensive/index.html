Home prices

Milford's median home price is $145,700. It is approximately $172,300 (54%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $71,800 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Dodge is roughly $52,100 (55%) higher.

Rents

In Milford the median rent is $675 a month. It is about $475 (41%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $387 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $23 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge.
