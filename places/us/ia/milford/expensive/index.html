Compared to its surrounding areas, Milford is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Fort Dodge however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milford is valued at $145,700. This is about $172,300 (54%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $71,800 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Dodge it is about $52,100 (55%) higher.

Rents

Milford's median monthly rent is $675. It is approximately $475 (41%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge it is roughly $23 (3%) higher.
