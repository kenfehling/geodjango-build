Home prices

The cost of the median home in Milford is $145,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $172,300 (54%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $71,800 (33%) lower, and it is about $52,100 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge.

Rents

In Milford the median monthly rent is $675. It is roughly $475 (41%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $387 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Fort Dodge is roughly $23 (3%) higher.
