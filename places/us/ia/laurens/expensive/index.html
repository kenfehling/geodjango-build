Home prices

The median home value in Laurens is $65,900. It is about $17,880 (37%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $151,600 (69%) lower, and it is roughly $27,700 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Fort Dodge.

Rents

The median rent in Laurens is $620 a month. It is about $24 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $442 (41%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Fort Dodge it is roughly $32 (4%) lower.
