Home prices

The median home in Dyersville costs $159,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24,820 (18%) higher. It is approximately $58,300 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $17,400 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Dyersville's median rent is $733 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $63 (9%) higher. It is roughly $329 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $34 (4%) lower.
