Home prices

The median home cost in Spillville is $122,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $10,080 (8%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $94,600 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $18,900 (13%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Spillville the median rent is $741 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $115 (18%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $321 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $26 (3%) lower.
