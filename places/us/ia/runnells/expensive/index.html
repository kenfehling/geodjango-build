Rents

In Runnells the median monthly rent is $695. It is approximately $190 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $367 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $160 (18%) lower.
