Home prices

The median home in Runnells is valued at $140,500. It is about $8,120 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $77,000 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $7,300 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Runnells's median monthly rent is $695. This is about $190 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $367 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $160 (18%) lower.
