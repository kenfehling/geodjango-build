Home prices

The median home in Webster City costs $97,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $29,840 (44%) higher.It is roughly $120,500 (55%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $36,200 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Webster City the median monthly rent is $700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $73 (11%) higher.It is approximately $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $155 (18%) lower.
