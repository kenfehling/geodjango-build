Home prices

The median home in Webster City costs $97,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $29,840 (44%) higher. It is approximately $120,500 (55%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $36,200 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Webster City is $700 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $73 (11%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $155 (18%) lower.
