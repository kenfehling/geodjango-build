Compared to the surrounding areas, Centerville is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Des Moines, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Centerville costs $68,400. This is roughly $12,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $149,100 (68%) lower, and it is approximately $64,800 (48%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Centerville's median rent is $618 per month. This is approximately $51 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $444 (41%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $237 (27%) lower.
