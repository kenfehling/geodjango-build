Compared to other places in this area, Centerville is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Des Moines, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Centerville is $68,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $12,800 (23%) higher. It is approximately $149,100 (68%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $64,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

Centerville's median rent is $618 per month. It is roughly $51 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $444 (41%) lower, and is roughly $237 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
