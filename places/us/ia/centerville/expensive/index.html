Home prices

The value of the median home in Centerville is $68,400. This is roughly $12,800 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $149,100 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $64,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

Centerville's median rent is $618 a month. It is about $51 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $237 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
