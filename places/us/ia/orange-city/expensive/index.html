Home prices

Rents

In Orange City the median rent is $679 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $103 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $383 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $148 (17%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
