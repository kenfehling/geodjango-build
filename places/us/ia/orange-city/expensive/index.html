Home prices

The cost of the median home in Orange City is $171,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $34,140 (24%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $46,200 (21%) lower, and is approximately $18,500 (9%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

The median rent in Orange City is $679 per month. It is about $103 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $383 (36%) lower, and is roughly $148 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
