Home prices

The median home in Orange City costs $171,300. This is approximately $34,140 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $46,200 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $18,500 (9%) lower.

Rents

Orange City's median monthly rent is $679. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $103 (13%) lower.It is approximately $383 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is roughly $148 (17%) lower.
