Home prices

The median home in Orange City is valued at $171,300. It is about $34,140 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $46,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $18,500 (9%) lower.

Rents

In Orange City the median monthly rent is $679. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $103 (13%) lower. It is roughly $383 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $148 (17%) lower.
