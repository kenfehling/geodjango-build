Compared to its surrounding areas, Montezuma is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montezuma costs $106,800. This is about $27,900 (35%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $110,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $26,400 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Montezuma the median rent is $697 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $60 (9%) higher. It is about $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $158 (18%) lower.
