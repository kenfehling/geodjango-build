Home prices

The median home value in Montezuma is $106,800. This is about $27,900 (35%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $110,700 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $26,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Montezuma is $697 a month. It is roughly $60 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and is about $158 (18%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
