Compared to the surrounding areas, Plainfield is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Plainfield is valued at $82,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $30,240 (26%) lower. It is about $134,800 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $59,100 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Plainfield the median rent is $625 a month. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $437 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $142 (18%) lower.
