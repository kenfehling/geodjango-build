Rents

The median rent in Lake Park is $775 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $94 (10%) lower. It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $52 (6%) lower.
