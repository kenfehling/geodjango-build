Home prices

The median home price in Muscatine is $110,800. This is roughly $1,200 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $106,700 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $31,000 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Muscatine's median monthly rent is $856. It is roughly $68 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $206 (19%) lower, and is about $89 (11%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
