Home prices

The median home in Muscatine costs $110,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $1,200 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $106,700 (49%) lower, and is about $31,000 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Muscatine the median monthly rent is $856. This is approximately $68 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $206 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $89 (11%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
