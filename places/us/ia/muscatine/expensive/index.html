Home prices

The median home price in Muscatine is $110,800. It is approximately $1,200 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $106,700 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $31,000 (21%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Muscatine is $856 a month. It is about $68 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $206 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $89 (11%) higher.
