Home prices

The cost of the median home in Arcadia is $113,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $11,660 (11%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $104,000 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha is about $46,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Arcadia is $533. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $70 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $529 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $390 (42%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
