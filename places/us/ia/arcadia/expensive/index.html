Rents

The median monthly rent in Arcadia is $533. It is approximately $70 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $529 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $390 (42%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
