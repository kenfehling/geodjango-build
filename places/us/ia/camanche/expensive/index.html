Home prices

Rents

In Camanche the median monthly rent is $630. Compared to nearby areas this is about $101 (13%) lower. It is about $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $142 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.
