Home prices

The median home in Decorah costs $189,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $62,560 (49%) higher. It is about $27,800 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $47,900 (33%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Decorah the median rent is $634 a month. It is approximately $36 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $428 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $133 (17%) lower.
