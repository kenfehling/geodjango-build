Home prices

Rents

Decorah's median monthly rent is $634. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $36 (5%) lower.It is about $428 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $133 (17%) lower.
