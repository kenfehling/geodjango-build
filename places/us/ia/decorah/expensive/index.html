In comparison to other places in this area, Decorah is a lot more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Decorah is $189,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $62,560 (49%) higher. It is about $27,800 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $47,900 (33%) higher.

Rents

Decorah's median rent is $634 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $36 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $428 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $133 (17%) lower.
