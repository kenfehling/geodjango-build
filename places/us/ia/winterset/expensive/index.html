Home prices

The price of the median home in Winterset is $158,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $43,260 (37%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $59,200 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $25,100 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Winterset the median monthly rent is $740. It is roughly $21 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $322 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $115 (13%) lower.
