In comparison to the surrounding areas, Keystone is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Keystone is $98,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $14,740 (17%) higher. It is roughly $118,900 (54%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $43,200 (30%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Keystone is $820. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $212 (34%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $242 (22%) lower, and is approximately $53 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
