Compared to its surrounding areas, Keystone is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Keystone costs $98,600. This is approximately $14,740 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $118,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $43,200 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Keystone the median monthly rent is $820. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $212 (34%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $242 (22%) lower, and is about $53 (6%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
