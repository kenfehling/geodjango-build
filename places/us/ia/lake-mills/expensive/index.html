In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lake Mills is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home value in Lake Mills is $101,600. This is roughly $27,300 (36%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $115,900 (53%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $98,500 (49%) lower.

Rents

Lake Mills's median monthly rent is $616. It is roughly $14 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $446 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $358 (36%) lower.
