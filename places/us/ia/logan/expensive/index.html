Home prices

The price of the median home in Logan is $118,300. This is about $31,320 (36%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $99,200 (45%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $41,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Logan the median rent is $751 per month. It is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $311 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is about $172 (18%) lower.
