Home prices

The median home in Logan costs $118,300. It is roughly $31,320 (36%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $99,200 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $41,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

Logan's median monthly rent is $751. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $311 (29%) lower, and is approximately $172 (18%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
