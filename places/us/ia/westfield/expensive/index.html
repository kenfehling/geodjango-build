Home prices

The median home in Westfield is valued at $78,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $28,380 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $139,200 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $111,500 (58%) lower.

Rents

Westfield's median rent is $536 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $77 (12%) lower. It is roughly $526 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $291 (35%) lower.
