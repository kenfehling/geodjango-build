Home prices

The median home price in Sioux Center is $208,900. It is approximately $70,440 (50%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $8,600 (3%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $19,100 (10%) higher compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

In Sioux Center the median monthly rent is $815. This is roughly $10 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $247 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
