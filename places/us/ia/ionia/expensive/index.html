Home prices

The median home in Ionia is valued at $80,200. This is roughly $15,880 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $137,300 (63%) lower, and is about $61,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Ionia's median monthly rent is $444. This is roughly $160 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $618 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $323 (42%) lower.
