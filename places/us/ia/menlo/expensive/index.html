Home prices

The cost of the median home in Menlo is $74,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $20,480 (21%) lower. It is about $143,300 (65%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $59,000 (44%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Menlo's median rent is $318 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $355 (52%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $744 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $537 (62%) lower.
