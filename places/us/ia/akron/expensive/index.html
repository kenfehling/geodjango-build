Home prices

The median home value in Akron is $107,400. This is approximately $8,560 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $110,100 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $82,400 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Akron is $621. It is approximately $87 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $441 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $206 (24%) lower.
