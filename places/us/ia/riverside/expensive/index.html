Compared to other places in this area, Riverside is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside costs $152,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $21,520 (12%) lower.It is approximately $65,100 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $10,600 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Riverside the median rent is $705 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $253 (26%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $357 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $62 (8%) lower.
