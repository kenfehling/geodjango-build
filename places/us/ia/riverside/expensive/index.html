Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverside is $705. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $253 (26%) lower.It is approximately $357 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $62 (8%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
