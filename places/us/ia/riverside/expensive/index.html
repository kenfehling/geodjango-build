Compared to its nearby areas, Riverside is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside is valued at $152,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $21,520 (12%) lower. It is roughly $65,100 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $10,600 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Riverside the median rent is $705 per month. This is roughly $253 (26%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $357 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $62 (8%) lower.
