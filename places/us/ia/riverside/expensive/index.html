Compared to other places in this area, Riverside is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Riverside is $152,400. It is roughly $21,520 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $65,100 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $10,600 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median rent in Riverside is $705 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $253 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $357 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is approximately $62 (8%) lower.
