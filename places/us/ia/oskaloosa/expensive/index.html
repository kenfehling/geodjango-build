In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oskaloosa is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oskaloosa costs $96,300. It is approximately $7,660 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $121,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $36,900 (27%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Oskaloosa the median rent is $653 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $67 (9%) lower. It is roughly $409 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $202 (23%) lower.
