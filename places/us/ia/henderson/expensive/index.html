Home prices

The value of the median home in Henderson is $71,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $31,320 (30%) lower. It is about $145,900 (67%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $88,100 (55%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Henderson the median rent is $769 a month. It is roughly $19 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $293 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Omaha it is approximately $154 (16%) lower.
