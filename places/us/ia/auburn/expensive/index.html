Home prices

The median home in Auburn is valued at $66,500. This is roughly $31,660 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $151,000 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Ames it is roughly $139,400 (67%) lower.

Rents

In Auburn the median monthly rent is $586. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $80 (15%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $476 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Ames it is roughly $344 (36%) lower.
