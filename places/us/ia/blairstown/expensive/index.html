Compared to its surrounding areas, Blairstown is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Blairstown costs $118,300. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $8,940 (8%) higher. It is roughly $99,200 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $23,500 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Blairstown is $563 per month. It is about $55 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $499 (46%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $204 (26%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
