Home prices

Rents

In Carlisle the median rent is $779 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $137 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $283 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $76 (8%) lower.
