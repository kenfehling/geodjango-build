Home prices

Rents

In Rock Valley the median rent is $669 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $12 (1%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $393 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $158 (19%) lower.
