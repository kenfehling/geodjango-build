Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairfax is $221,400. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $28,080 (14%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $3,900 (1%) higher, and is approximately $79,600 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $1,375. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $619 (81%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $313 (29%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $608 (79%) higher.
