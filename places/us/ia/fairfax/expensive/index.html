In comparison to nearby areas, Fairfax is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fairfax is $221,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $28,080 (14%) higher. It is about $3,900 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $79,600 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $1,375. This is roughly $619 (81%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $313 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $608 (79%) higher.
