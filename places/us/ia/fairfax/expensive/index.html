Home prices

The median home in Fairfax is valued at $221,400. It is roughly $28,080 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $3,900 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $79,600 (56%) higher.

Rents

In Fairfax the median rent is $1,375 per month. It is about $619 (81%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $313 (29%) higher, and it is roughly $608 (79%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
