In comparison to its nearby areas, Fairfax is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairfax is $221,400. It is approximately $28,080 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $3,900 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $79,600 (56%) higher.

Rents

Fairfax's median monthly rent is $1,375. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $619 (81%) higher.It is about $313 (29%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $608 (79%) higher.
