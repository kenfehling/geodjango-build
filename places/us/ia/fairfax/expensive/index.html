Compared to nearby areas, Fairfax is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Fairfax's median home value is $221,400. This is about $28,080 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $3,900 (1%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $79,600 (56%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $1,375. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $619 (81%) higher. It is about $313 (29%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $608 (79%) higher.
