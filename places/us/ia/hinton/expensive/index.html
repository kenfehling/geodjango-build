Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hinton is $185,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $53,080 (39%) higher. It is about $31,700 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $26,100 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Hinton the median rent is $730 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $51 (6%) lower. It is roughly $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $193 (20%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
