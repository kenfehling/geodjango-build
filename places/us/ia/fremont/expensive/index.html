Home prices

Rents

Fremont's median rent is $578 per month. This is about $166 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $484 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is approximately $277 (32%) lower.
