Compared to other places in this area, Fremont is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fremont is $90,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $10,340 (12%) higher. It is approximately $126,600 (58%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $42,300 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fremont is $578. It is about $166 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $484 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $277 (32%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.
