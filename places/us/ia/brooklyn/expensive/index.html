In comparison to its nearby areas, Brooklyn is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brooklyn is valued at $104,500. It is approximately $22,340 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $113,000 (51%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $28,700 (21%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Brooklyn the median monthly rent is $727. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $102 (16%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $335 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $128 (14%) lower.
