Home prices

The price of the median home in Brooklyn is $104,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,340 (27%) higher.It is roughly $113,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $28,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Brooklyn the median monthly rent is $727. This is roughly $102 (16%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $335 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $128 (14%) lower.
