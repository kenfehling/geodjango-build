Home prices

Iowa Falls's median home value is $88,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $19,480 (28%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $129,300 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $45,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Iowa Falls is $750. It is about $175 (30%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $312 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $105 (12%) lower.
