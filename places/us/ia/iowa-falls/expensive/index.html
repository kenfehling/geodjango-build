Home prices

Iowa Falls's median home value is $88,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $19,480 (28%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $129,300 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $45,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Iowa Falls is $750. This is roughly $175 (30%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is roughly $105 (12%) lower.
