Home prices

The median home in Garwin is valued at $72,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $20,700 (22%) lower. It is approximately $145,300 (66%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $61,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Garwin the median rent is $667 a month. It is approximately $85 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $395 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $188 (21%) lower.
