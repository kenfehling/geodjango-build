Home prices

The median home in West Liberty costs $125,300. It is about $3,140 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $92,200 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $16,500 (11%) lower.

Rents

In West Liberty the median rent is $889 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $126 (16%) higher. It is about $173 (16%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $122 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
