Home prices

The median home price in Johnston is $268,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $52,280 (24%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $50,800 (23%) higher, and is approximately $135,100 (101%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Johnston is $1,146 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $84 (7%) higher, and is approximately $291 (34%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
