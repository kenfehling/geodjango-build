Home prices

The median home in Johnston is valued at $268,300. It is roughly $52,280 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $50,800 (23%) higher, and it is roughly $135,100 (101%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Johnston the median rent is $1,146 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $84 (7%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $291 (34%) higher compared to the city of Des Moines.
