Home prices

The median home in Johnston costs $268,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $52,280 (24%) higher.It is roughly $50,800 (23%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $135,100 (101%) higher in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

In Johnston the median monthly rent is $1,146. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $84 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is about $291 (34%) higher.
