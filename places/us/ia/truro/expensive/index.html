Home prices

The median home in Truro costs $111,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $6,920 (6%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $106,100 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $21,800 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Truro the median rent is $795 a month. This is about $9 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $267 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $60 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
