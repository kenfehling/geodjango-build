Home prices

The median home price in Truro is $111,400. This is about $6,920 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $106,100 (48%) lower, and it is roughly $21,800 (16%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Truro is $795 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $9 (1%) lower. It is approximately $267 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Des Moines is about $60 (7%) lower.
