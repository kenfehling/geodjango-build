Home prices

The median home in Fort Dodge costs $93,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $20,700 (28%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $123,900 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is about $39,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Dodge the median monthly rent is $652. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $87 (15%) higher. It is roughly $410 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines is approximately $203 (23%) lower.
