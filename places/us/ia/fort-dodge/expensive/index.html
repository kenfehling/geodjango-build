Home prices

The median home in Fort Dodge is valued at $93,600. This is about $20,700 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $123,900 (56%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $39,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Dodge the median monthly rent is $652. It is approximately $87 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $410 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is roughly $203 (23%) lower.
