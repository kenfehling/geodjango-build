Home prices

Rents

In Fort Dodge the median monthly rent is $652. It is approximately $87 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $410 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $203 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
