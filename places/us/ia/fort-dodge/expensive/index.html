In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fort Dodge is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Des Moines however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Fort Dodge's median home price is $93,600. This is roughly $20,700 (28%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $123,900 (56%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $39,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Fort Dodge's median rent is $652 per month. It is approximately $87 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $410 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $203 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
