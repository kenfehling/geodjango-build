Home prices

The median home value in Fort Dodge is $93,600. It is about $20,700 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $123,900 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines is roughly $39,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Dodge is $652 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $87 (15%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $410 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Des Moines it is about $203 (23%) lower.
