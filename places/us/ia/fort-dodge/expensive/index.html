Compared to its surrounding areas, Fort Dodge is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Des Moines, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Fort Dodge is $93,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $20,700 (28%) higher.It is about $123,900 (56%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $39,600 (29%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

Fort Dodge's median rent is $652 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $87 (15%) higher.It is about $410 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $203 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
