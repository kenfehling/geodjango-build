Home prices

The median home in Decatur City costs $47,300. This is about $11,740 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $170,200 (78%) lower, and is roughly $85,900 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Decatur City is $705. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $42 (6%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $357 (33%) lower, and is roughly $150 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Des Moines.
