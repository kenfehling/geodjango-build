The cost of the median home in Decatur City is $47,300. This is approximately $11,740 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $170,200 (78%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $85,900 (64%) lower compared to the city of Des Moines.

Rents

The median rent in Decatur City is $705 per month. It is approximately $42 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $357 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $150 (17%) lower.
