Rents

In Lynnville the median monthly rent is $660. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $34 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $402 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Des Moines it is approximately $195 (22%) lower.
