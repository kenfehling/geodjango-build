Home prices

The median home in Princeton is valued at $179,800. This is about $2,500 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $37,700 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $54,100 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Princeton's median monthly rent is $750. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $121 (13%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $312 (29%) lower, and is approximately $56 (6%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
