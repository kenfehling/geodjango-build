Home prices

The median home in Blencoe is valued at $64,300. It is roughly $4,500 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $153,200 (70%) lower, and it is approximately $95,400 (59%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Blencoe is $630 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $44 (7%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $432 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha it is about $293 (31%) lower.
