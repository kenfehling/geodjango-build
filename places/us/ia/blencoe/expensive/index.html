Compared to its surrounding areas, Blencoe is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Omaha, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Blencoe is $64,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $4,500 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $153,200 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is approximately $95,400 (59%) lower.

Rents

Blencoe's median monthly rent is $630. This is roughly $44 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $293 (31%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
