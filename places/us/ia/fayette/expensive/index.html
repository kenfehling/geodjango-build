Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fayette is $651. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $144 (28%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $411 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $116 (15%) lower.
