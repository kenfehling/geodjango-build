In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fayette is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fayette costs $86,100. This is about $4,460 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $131,400 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $55,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

Fayette's median rent is $651 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $144 (28%) higher. It is roughly $411 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $116 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
