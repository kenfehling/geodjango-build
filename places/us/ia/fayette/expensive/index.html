Home prices

The median home value in Fayette is $86,100. This is approximately $4,460 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $131,400 (60%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $55,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Fayette the median rent is $651 a month. This is roughly $144 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $411 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $116 (15%) lower.
