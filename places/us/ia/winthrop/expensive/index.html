Home prices

The median home in Winthrop costs $103,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $8,820 (9%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $113,800 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $38,100 (26%) lower.

Rents

Winthrop's median monthly rent is $700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $75 (12%) higher.It is roughly $362 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $67 (8%) lower compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.
