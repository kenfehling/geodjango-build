Home prices

The price of the median home in Anita is $67,000. This is about $10,160 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $150,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $92,700 (58%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Anita the median rent is $752 a month. This is roughly $103 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $310 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $171 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
