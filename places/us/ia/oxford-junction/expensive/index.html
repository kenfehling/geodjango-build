Home prices

The median home price in Oxford Junction is $52,500. It is approximately $17,180 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $165,000 (75%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $89,300 (62%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oxford Junction is $639. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $114 (21%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $423 (39%) lower, and is about $128 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
