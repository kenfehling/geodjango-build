Home prices

The median home in Raymond is valued at $182,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $45,940 (33%) higher. It is about $35,400 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $40,300 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Raymond's median rent is $486 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $245 (33%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $576 (54%) lower, and is about $281 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
