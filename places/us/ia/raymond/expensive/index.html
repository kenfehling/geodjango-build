Rents

The median monthly rent in Raymond is $486. It is about $245 (33%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $576 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is approximately $281 (36%) lower.
