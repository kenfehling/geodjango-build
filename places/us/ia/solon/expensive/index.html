Home prices

The median home value in Solon is $253,800. This is approximately $49,220 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $36,300 (16%) higher, and is about $112,000 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Solon the median monthly rent is $765. It is about $76 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $297 (27%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
