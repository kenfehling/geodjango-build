Compared to the surrounding areas, Solon is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The median home in Solon costs $253,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $49,220 (24%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $36,300 (16%) higher, and it is about $112,000 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

Solon's median monthly rent is $765. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $76 (9%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $297 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is is very similar.
