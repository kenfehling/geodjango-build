Home prices

The median home value in Ventura is $191,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $88,320 (85%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $26,100 (12%) lower, and is about $14,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Ames.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ventura is $1,018. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $411 (67%) higher. It is roughly $44 (4%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Ames is roughly $88 (9%) higher.
