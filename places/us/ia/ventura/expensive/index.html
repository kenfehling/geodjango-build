Home prices

The price of the median home in Ventura is $191,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $88,320 (85%) higher. It is approximately $26,100 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $14,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Ames.

Rents

The median rent in Ventura is $1,018 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $411 (67%) higher. It is about $44 (4%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Ames is approximately $88 (9%) higher.
