Compared to the surrounding areas, Alburnett is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Alburnett is $161,300. This is roughly $20,160 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $56,200 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $19,500 (13%) higher.

Rents

In Alburnett the median monthly rent is $570. This is roughly $285 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $492 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $197 (25%) lower.
