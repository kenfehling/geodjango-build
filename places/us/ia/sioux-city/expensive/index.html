Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sioux City is $116,600. It is about $70,400 (37%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $100,900 (46%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $43,100 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sioux City is $783. This is about $123 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $279 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is approximately $140 (15%) lower.
