In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sioux City is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Omaha.

Home prices

The median home in Sioux City is valued at $116,600. This is about $70,400 (37%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $100,900 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Omaha it is about $43,100 (26%) lower.

Rents

In Sioux City the median rent is $783 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $123 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $279 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is about $140 (15%) lower.
