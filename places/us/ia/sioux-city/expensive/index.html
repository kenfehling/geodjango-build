Compared to other places in this area, Sioux City is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Omaha, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sioux City is valued at $116,600. It is approximately $70,400 (37%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $100,900 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $43,100 (26%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.

Rents

The median rent in Sioux City is $783 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $123 (13%) lower. It is approximately $279 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $140 (15%) lower compared to the city of Omaha.
