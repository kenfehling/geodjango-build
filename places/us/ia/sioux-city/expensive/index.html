Home prices

The median home in Sioux City is valued at $116,600. This is approximately $70,400 (37%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $100,900 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $43,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.

Rents

In Sioux City the median monthly rent is $783. This is about $123 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $279 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $140 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
