Home prices

The median home price in Kingsley is $125,000. This is approximately $26,060 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $92,500 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Omaha is roughly $34,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kingsley is $591 a month. It is roughly $11 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $471 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Omaha it is roughly $332 (35%) lower.
