Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Kingsley is $591 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $11 (1%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $471 (44%) lower, and it is about $332 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Omaha.
