Compared to its nearby areas, Mount Vernon is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon costs $203,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $35,500 (21%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $14,100 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $61,600 (43%) higher compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Mount Vernon the median monthly rent is $757. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $35 (4%) higher.It is about $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
