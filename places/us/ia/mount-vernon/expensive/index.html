Home prices

The price of the median home in Mount Vernon is $203,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $35,500 (21%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $14,100 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $61,600 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Rents

In Mount Vernon the median monthly rent is $757. This is about $35 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
