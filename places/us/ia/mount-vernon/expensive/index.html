Compared to other places in this area, Mount Vernon is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cedar Rapids, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon costs $203,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $35,500 (21%) higher. It is roughly $14,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids is about $61,600 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Vernon the median monthly rent is $757. This is approximately $35 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $305 (28%) lower, and is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids.
