Compared to its surrounding areas, Mount Vernon is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cedar Rapids.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mount Vernon is $203,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $35,500 (21%) higher. It is roughly $14,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $61,600 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Vernon is $757. This is approximately $35 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
