In comparison to nearby areas, Mount Vernon is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cedar Rapids however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Vernon costs $203,400. This is about $35,500 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $14,100 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Cedar Rapids it is about $61,600 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mount Vernon is $757 per month. This is roughly $35 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $305 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cedar Rapids is roughly $10 (1%) lower.
