Home prices

The price of the median home in Northwood is $97,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $20,640 (26%) higher. It is about $119,900 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $102,500 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northwood is $680. It is roughly $121 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $382 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $294 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
