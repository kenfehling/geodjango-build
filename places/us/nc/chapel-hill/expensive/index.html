Compared to nearby areas, Chapel Hill is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home price in Chapel Hill is $399,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $135,740 (51%) higher.It is roughly $182,200 (83%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $151,400 (60%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in Chapel Hill is $1,191 a month. It is about $107 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $129 (12%) higher, and is roughly $70 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
