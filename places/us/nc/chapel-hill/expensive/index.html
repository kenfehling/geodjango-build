In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chapel Hill is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

Chapel Hill's median home cost is $399,700. It is about $135,740 (51%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $182,200 (83%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $151,400 (60%) higher.

Rents

In Chapel Hill the median rent is $1,191 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $107 (9%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $129 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $70 (6%) higher.
