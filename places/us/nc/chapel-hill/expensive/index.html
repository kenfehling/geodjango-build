Compared to the surrounding areas, Chapel Hill is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Raleigh, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chapel Hill costs $399,700. This is approximately $135,740 (51%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $182,200 (83%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh is about $151,400 (60%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Chapel Hill is $1,191 per month. This is roughly $107 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $129 (12%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is about $70 (6%) higher.
