Rents

The median rent in Goldston is $840 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $47 (5%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $222 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is roughly $281 (25%) lower.
