Home prices

Goldston's median home cost is $158,100. This is about $9,360 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $59,400 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $90,200 (36%) lower.

Rents

Goldston's median rent is $840 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $47 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $222 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $281 (25%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
