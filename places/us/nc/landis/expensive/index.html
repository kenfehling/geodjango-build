Home prices

The price of the median home in Landis is $140,800. This is approximately $5,220 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $76,700 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $79,500 (36%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Landis is $1,068 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $367 (52%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is is very similar, and is about $67 (5%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
