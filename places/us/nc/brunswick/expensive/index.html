Home prices

The price of the median home in Brunswick is $110,800. This is about $11,240 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $106,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $20,200 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brunswick is $716. This is approximately $64 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $346 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $231 (24%) lower.
