Home prices

The median home in Brunswick costs $110,800. This is approximately $11,240 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $106,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $20,200 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Brunswick the median rent is $716 per month. This is approximately $64 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $346 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $231 (24%) lower.
