Home prices

The median home price in Huntersville is $301,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $32,940 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $84,000 (38%) higher, and it is approximately $81,200 (36%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Huntersville the median monthly rent is $1,305. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $66 (5%) higher. It is approximately $243 (22%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $170 (14%) higher.
