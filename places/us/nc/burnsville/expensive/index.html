Home prices

Rents

Burnsville's median rent is $542 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $132 (19%) lower.It is about $520 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $501 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
