Home prices

Rents

Burnsville's median rent is $542 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $132 (19%) lower.It is roughly $520 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $501 (48%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
