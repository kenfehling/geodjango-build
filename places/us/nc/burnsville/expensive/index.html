Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burnsville is $542. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $132 (19%) lower. It is about $520 (48%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is roughly $501 (48%) lower.
