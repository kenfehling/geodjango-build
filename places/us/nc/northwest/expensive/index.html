Home prices

The median home in Northwest is valued at $100,700. This is about $55,480 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $116,800 (53%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $30,300 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northwest is $750. This is approximately $86 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $312 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $197 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
