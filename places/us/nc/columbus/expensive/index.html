Home prices

The cost of the median home in Columbus is $157,600. It is about $14,020 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $59,900 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is approximately $62,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Columbus the median monthly rent is $692. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $199 (22%) lower. It is approximately $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $443 (39%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
