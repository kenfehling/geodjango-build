Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbus is $692. This is about $199 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $370 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte is approximately $443 (39%) lower.
