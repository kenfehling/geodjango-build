Home prices

The value of the median home in Columbus is $157,600. This is roughly $14,020 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $62,700 (28%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Columbus the median rent is $692 a month. This is roughly $199 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $443 (39%) lower.
