In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Canton is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in West Canton is $139,100. This is about $42,220 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $78,400 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $2,800 (2%) higher.

Rents

In West Canton the median rent is $708 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $118 (14%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $354 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $137 (16%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
