Home prices

The price of the median home in West Canton is $139,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $42,220 (23%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $78,400 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $2,800 (2%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Canton is $708. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $118 (14%) lower. It is roughly $354 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $137 (16%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
