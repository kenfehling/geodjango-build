Home prices

The median home cost in Princeville is $71,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $9,860 (12%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $145,800 (67%) lower, and is roughly $176,600 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Princeville's median rent is $713 per month. It is about $43 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $349 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $408 (36%) lower.
