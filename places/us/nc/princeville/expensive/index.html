In comparison to nearby areas, Princeville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Princeville is $71,700. This is approximately $9,860 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $145,800 (67%) lower, and is roughly $176,600 (71%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Princeville the median monthly rent is $713. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $43 (6%) higher. It is roughly $349 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $408 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
