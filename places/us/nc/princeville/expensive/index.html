In comparison to its surrounding areas, Princeville is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Princeville is $71,700. It is about $9,860 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $145,800 (67%) lower, and it is about $176,600 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Princeville's median monthly rent is $713. It is about $43 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $408 (36%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
