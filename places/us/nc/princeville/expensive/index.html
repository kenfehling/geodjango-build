Home prices

The price of the median home in Princeville is $71,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $9,860 (12%) lower.It is approximately $145,800 (67%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $176,600 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in Princeville is $713 per month. This is roughly $43 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $349 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is roughly $408 (36%) lower.
