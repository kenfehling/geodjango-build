Home prices

Rents

Beaufort's median rent is $715 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $288 (28%) lower.It is about $347 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $283 (28%) lower compared to the city of Wilmington.
