Home prices

The median home value in Pine Knoll Shores is $387,600. It is approximately $159,240 (69%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $170,100 (78%) higher, and is roughly $141,200 (57%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

Pine Knoll Shores's median rent is $1,214 a month. It is roughly $301 (32%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $152 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Wilmington it is roughly $216 (21%) higher.
