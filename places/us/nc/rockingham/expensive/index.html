Home prices

The median home in Rockingham is valued at $97,100. It is about $22,180 (29%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $120,400 (55%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $123,200 (55%) lower.

Rents

Rockingham's median rent is $619 per month. This is approximately $40 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $516 (45%) lower.
