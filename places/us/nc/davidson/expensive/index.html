Compared to its surrounding areas, Davidson is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Davidson is valued at $448,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $159,660 (55%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $230,800 (106%) higher, and it is approximately $228,000 (103%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Davidson the median rent is $1,264 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $79 (6%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $202 (19%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $129 (11%) higher.
