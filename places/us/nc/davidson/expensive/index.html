In comparison to the surrounding areas, Davidson is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Davidson is $448,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $159,660 (55%) higher. It is about $230,800 (106%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $228,000 (103%) higher.

Rents

In Davidson the median monthly rent is $1,264. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $79 (6%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $202 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $129 (11%) higher.
