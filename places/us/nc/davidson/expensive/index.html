Home prices

Davidson's median home price is $448,300. This is roughly $159,660 (55%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $230,800 (106%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $228,000 (103%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Davidson's median monthly rent is $1,264. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $79 (6%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $202 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $129 (11%) higher.
