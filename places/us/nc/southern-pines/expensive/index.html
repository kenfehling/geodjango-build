In comparison to the surrounding areas, Southern Pines is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Southern Pines is valued at $289,700. It is roughly $65,780 (29%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $72,200 (33%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $69,400 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Southern Pines the median monthly rent is $912. It is about $134 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $150 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $223 (19%) lower.
