Compared to other places in this area, Grantsboro is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Greenville.

Home prices

The median home in Grantsboro is valued at $91,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $38,640 (29%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $126,300 (58%) lower, and it is about $70,000 (43%) lower compared to the city of Greenville.

Rents

In Grantsboro the median rent is $731 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $85 (10%) lower. It is roughly $331 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Greenville it is approximately $88 (10%) lower.
