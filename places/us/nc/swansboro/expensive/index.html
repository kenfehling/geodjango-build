Home prices

Swansboro's median home value is $202,000. This is roughly $53,860 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $15,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $44,400 (18%) lower compared to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

The median rent in Swansboro is $1,108 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $116 (9%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $46 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington is approximately $110 (11%) higher.
