Home prices

The median home in Swansboro costs $202,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $53,860 (21%) lower. It is approximately $15,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $44,400 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

Swansboro's median monthly rent is $1,108. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $116 (9%) lower. It is roughly $46 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Wilmington is approximately $110 (11%) higher.
