Home prices

The median home in Swansboro costs $202,000. It is roughly $53,860 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $15,500 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $44,400 (18%) lower compared to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

The median rent in Swansboro is $1,108 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $116 (9%) lower.It is approximately $46 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $110 (11%) higher compared to the city of Wilmington.
