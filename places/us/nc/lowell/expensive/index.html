Home prices

The value of the median home in Lowell is $117,700. This is about $82,600 (41%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $99,800 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $102,600 (46%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lowell is $840. This is about $152 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $222 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $295 (25%) lower.
