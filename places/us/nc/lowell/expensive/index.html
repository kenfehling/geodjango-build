Compared to other places in this area, Lowell is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lowell is $117,700. This is approximately $82,600 (41%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $99,800 (45%) lower, and is approximately $102,600 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Lowell is $840 a month. This is approximately $152 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $222 (20%) lower, and is approximately $295 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
