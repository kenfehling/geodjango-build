Rents

The median rent in Atkinson is $886 a month. It is roughly $200 (29%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $176 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $61 (6%) lower.
