Home prices

The median home in Balfour costs $162,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $42,980 (20%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $54,700 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $107,600 (39%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Balfour the median rent is $627 a month. It is about $169 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $435 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $416 (39%) lower.
