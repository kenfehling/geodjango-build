Home prices

The median home in Balfour costs $162,800. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $42,980 (20%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $54,700 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Asheville it is about $107,600 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Balfour the median rent is $627 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $169 (21%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $435 (40%) lower, and it is about $416 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
