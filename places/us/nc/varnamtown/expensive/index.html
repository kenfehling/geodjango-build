Home prices

The median home in Varnamtown is valued at $167,600. This is approximately $128,400 (43%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $49,900 (22%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $36,600 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Varnamtown the median monthly rent is $836. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $252 (23%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $226 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is roughly $111 (11%) lower.
