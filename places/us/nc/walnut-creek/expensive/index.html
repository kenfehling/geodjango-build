Home prices

The median home in Walnut Creek is valued at $373,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $278,460 (292%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $156,100 (71%) higher, and is approximately $125,300 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Walnut Creek's median rent is $1,700 a month. This is about $932 (121%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $638 (60%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is about $579 (51%) higher.
