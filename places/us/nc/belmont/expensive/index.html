Home prices

The median home in Belmont costs $210,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $25,460 (13%) higher. It is about $6,900 (3%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $9,700 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Belmont the median monthly rent is $1,013. This is about $59 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $49 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $122 (10%) lower.
