Home prices

The median home value in St. James is $456,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $262,680 (135%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $239,100 (109%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is roughly $210,200 (85%) higher.

Rents

St. James's median monthly rent is $1,862. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $962 (106%) higher. It is about $800 (75%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $864 (86%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
