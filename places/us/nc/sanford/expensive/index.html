Home prices

The median home price in Sanford is $142,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $3,960 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $75,300 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $106,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Sanford the median monthly rent is $753. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $60 (7%) lower. It is about $309 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $368 (32%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
