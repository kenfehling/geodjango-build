Home prices

The median home in McAdenville costs $289,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $120,140 (70%) higher. It is about $72,000 (33%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $69,200 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In McAdenville the median rent is $958 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $104 (9%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $177 (15%) lower.
