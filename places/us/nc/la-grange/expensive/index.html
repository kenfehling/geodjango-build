Home prices

The value of the median home in La Grange is $106,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $46,120 (30%) lower. It is approximately $111,200 (51%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $142,000 (57%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

La Grange's median monthly rent is $875. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $60 (6%) lower. It is about $187 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $246 (21%) lower.
