Home prices

The price of the median home in Laurel Park is $270,800. It is approximately $67,280 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $53,300 (24%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Laurel Park's median rent is $826 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $45 (5%) higher. It is about $236 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $217 (20%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
