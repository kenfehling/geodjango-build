Rents

Laurel Park's median monthly rent is $826. It is roughly $45 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $236 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $217 (20%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
