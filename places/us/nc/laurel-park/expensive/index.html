Laurel Park's median home value is $270,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $67,280 (33%) higher. It is about $53,300 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is is very similar.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laurel Park is $826. This is approximately $45 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $236 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Asheville is about $217 (20%) lower.
