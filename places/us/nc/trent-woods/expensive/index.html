Home prices

Rents

Trent Woods's median rent is $1,292 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $271 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $230 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is roughly $294 (29%) higher.
