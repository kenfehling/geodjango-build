Rents

Spring Lake's median rent is $1,032 a month. This is about $246 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $30 (2%) lower, and is roughly $89 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
