Compared to other places in this area, Sunset Beach is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Sunset Beach's median home price is $267,900. It is roughly $18,400 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $50,400 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $136,900 (104%) higher.

Rents

In Sunset Beach the median rent is $1,232 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $189 (18%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $170 (16%) higher, and it is roughly $285 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
