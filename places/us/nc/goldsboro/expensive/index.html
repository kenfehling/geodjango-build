Home prices

The median home in Goldsboro is valued at $127,300. It is roughly $33,340 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $90,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $121,000 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Goldsboro is $791 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $145 (15%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $271 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $330 (29%) lower.
