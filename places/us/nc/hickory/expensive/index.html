Home prices

The price of the median home in Hickory is $168,600. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $59,140 (54%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $48,900 (22%) lower, and is about $51,700 (23%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hickory is $734. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $12 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $328 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $401 (35%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
