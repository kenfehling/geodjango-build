Home prices

The median home in Hickory costs $168,600. This is roughly $59,140 (54%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $48,900 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $51,700 (23%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Hickory's median rent is $734 per month. This is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $328 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $401 (35%) lower.
