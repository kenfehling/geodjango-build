Compared to its surrounding areas, Jonesville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

Jonesville's median home cost is $98,600. It is approximately $21,580 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $118,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Jonesville the median rent is $563 a month. This is roughly $118 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $499 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $572 (50%) lower.
