Compared to the surrounding areas, Kings Mountain is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kings Mountain costs $123,600. It is roughly $21,940 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $93,900 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $96,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kings Mountain is $741. This is about $17 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $321 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $394 (34%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
