Home prices

The median home in Kings Mountain costs $123,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $21,940 (21%) higher.It is approximately $93,900 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $96,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Kings Mountain the median rent is $741 per month. It is roughly $17 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $321 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $394 (34%) lower.
