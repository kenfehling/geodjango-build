Home prices

The median home in Kings Mountain is valued at $123,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $21,940 (21%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $93,900 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $96,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

Kings Mountain's median monthly rent is $741. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $17 (2%) lower. It is roughly $321 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is approximately $394 (34%) lower.
