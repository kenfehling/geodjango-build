Home prices

The median home price in Kings Mountain is $123,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $21,940 (21%) higher. It is roughly $93,900 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $96,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Kings Mountain's median monthly rent is $741. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $17 (2%) lower. It is approximately $321 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $394 (34%) lower.
