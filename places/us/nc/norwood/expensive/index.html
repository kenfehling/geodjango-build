Home prices

The cost of the median home in Norwood is $96,700. This is about $27,460 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $120,800 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $123,600 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Norwood is $739 a month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $396 (34%) lower.
