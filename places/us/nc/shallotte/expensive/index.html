The median home in Shallotte costs $218,900. It is about $83,680 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is roughly $87,900 (67%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Shallotte is $784 a month. This is about $223 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $278 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $163 (17%) lower.
