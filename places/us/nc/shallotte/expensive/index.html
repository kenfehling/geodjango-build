Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Shallotte is $784 per month. It is about $223 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $278 (26%) lower, and is approximately $163 (17%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
