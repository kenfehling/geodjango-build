Home prices

The median home in Shallotte is valued at $218,900. This is approximately $83,680 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $87,900 (67%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Shallotte the median monthly rent is $784. It is about $223 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $278 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $163 (17%) lower.
