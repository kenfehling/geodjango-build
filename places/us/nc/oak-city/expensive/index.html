Home prices

Rents

In Oak City the median rent is $615 a month. It is roughly $96 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $447 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $506 (45%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
