In comparison to the surrounding areas, Valley Hill is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Asheville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Valley Hill is $261,700. This is approximately $24,480 (10%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $44,200 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Asheville is approximately $8,700 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Valley Hill is $907. It is about $66 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $155 (14%) lower, and is roughly $136 (13%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.
