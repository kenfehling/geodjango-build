Home prices

The median home value in Valley Hill is $261,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24,480 (10%) higher.It is about $44,200 (20%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Asheville is about $8,700 (3%) lower.

Rents

Valley Hill's median monthly rent is $907. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $66 (7%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $155 (14%) lower, and it is roughly $136 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
