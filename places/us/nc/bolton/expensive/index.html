Home prices

Bolton's median home value is $87,800. It is about $63,940 (42%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $129,700 (59%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $43,200 (32%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median rent in Bolton is $884 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $151 (20%) higher. It is approximately $178 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $63 (6%) lower.
