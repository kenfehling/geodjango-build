Home prices

The value of the median home in Roseboro is $88,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $27,140 (23%) lower. It is roughly $128,600 (59%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $159,400 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Roseboro's median monthly rent is $549. This is approximately $139 (20%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $513 (48%) lower, and it is approximately $572 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
