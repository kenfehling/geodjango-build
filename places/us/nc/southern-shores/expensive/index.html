In comparison to the surrounding areas, Southern Shores is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Virginia Beach, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Southern Shores is $465,200. This is approximately $103,340 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $247,700 (113%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $184,400 (65%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Southern Shores is $1,914 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $719 (60%) higher. It is roughly $852 (80%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $547 (40%) higher compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
