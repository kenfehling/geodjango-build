Home prices

Four Oaks's median home cost is $151,900. This is approximately $28,500 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $65,600 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $96,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Four Oaks the median rent is $822 a month. It is roughly $25 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $299 (26%) lower.
