Home prices

The median home in Lake Waccamaw costs $263,500. It is about $172,520 (189%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $46,000 (21%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $132,500 (101%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Lake Waccamaw the median monthly rent is $904. This is roughly $243 (36%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $158 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is about $43 (4%) lower.
