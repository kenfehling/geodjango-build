Rents

The median rent in Foscoe is $791 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $98 (10%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $271 (25%) lower, and is roughly $344 (30%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
