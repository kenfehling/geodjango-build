Compared to its surrounding areas, North Wilkesboro is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

North Wilkesboro's median home cost is $116,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $13,940 (10%) lower. It is roughly $101,100 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in North Wilkesboro is $607 per month. This is about $96 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $455 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $528 (46%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
