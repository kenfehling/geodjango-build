Home prices

Rents

North Wilkesboro's median monthly rent is $607. This is roughly $96 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $455 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $528 (46%) lower.
