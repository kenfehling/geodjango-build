Home prices

The median home price in North Wilkesboro is $116,400. This is about $13,940 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $101,100 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte is approximately $103,900 (47%) lower.

Rents

In North Wilkesboro the median rent is $607 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $96 (13%) lower.It is about $455 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $528 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
