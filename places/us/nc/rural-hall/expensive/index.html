In comparison to the surrounding areas, Rural Hall is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rural Hall is $124,300. It is about $27,460 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $93,200 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $96,000 (43%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Rural Hall is $800 per month. This is approximately $11 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $262 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $335 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
