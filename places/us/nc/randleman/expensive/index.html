Home prices

Randleman's median home price is $125,200. This is roughly $7,040 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $92,300 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $95,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Randleman is $586. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $139 (19%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $476 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $549 (48%) lower.
