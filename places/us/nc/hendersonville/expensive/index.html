Home prices

The median home in Hendersonville is valued at $188,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $11,480 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $29,400 (13%) lower, and it is about $82,300 (30%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

In Hendersonville the median rent is $864 a month. This is about $133 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $198 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Asheville is about $179 (17%) lower.
