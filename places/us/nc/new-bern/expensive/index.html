Compared to other places in this area, New Bern is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Wilmington.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in New Bern is $155,900. This is roughly $37,500 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $61,600 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Wilmington it is approximately $90,500 (36%) lower.

Rents

New Bern's median rent is $859 per month. This is about $249 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $203 (19%) lower, and is approximately $139 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
