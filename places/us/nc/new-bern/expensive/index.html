Compared to its nearby areas, New Bern is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Wilmington.

Home prices

The median home in New Bern costs $155,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $37,500 (19%) lower. It is about $61,600 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $90,500 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

In New Bern the median monthly rent is $859. It is roughly $249 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $203 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $139 (13%) lower compared to the city of Wilmington.
