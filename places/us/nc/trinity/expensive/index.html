Home prices

The median home in Trinity costs $142,400. It is approximately $7,900 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $75,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $77,900 (35%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Trinity the median rent is $748 a month. It is roughly $8 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $314 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $387 (34%) lower.
