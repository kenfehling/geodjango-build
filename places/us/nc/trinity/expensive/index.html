Compared to the surrounding areas, Trinity is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

Trinity's median home value is $142,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $7,900 (5%) lower. It is approximately $75,100 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $77,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Trinity's median monthly rent is $748. This is approximately $8 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $314 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $387 (34%) lower.
