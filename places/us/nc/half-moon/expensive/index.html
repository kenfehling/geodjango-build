Rents

The median monthly rent in Half Moon is $908. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $54 (5%) lower. It is approximately $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $90 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
