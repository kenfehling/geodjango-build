Home prices

The median home in Half Moon is valued at $150,000. It is approximately $4,580 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $67,500 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $96,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

Half Moon's median rent is $908 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $54 (5%) lower.It is roughly $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is roughly $90 (9%) lower.
