Home prices

The median home in Erwin costs $91,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $39,960 (30%) lower. It is roughly $126,500 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $157,300 (63%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Erwin the median monthly rent is $824. It is approximately $69 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $238 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $297 (26%) lower.
