In comparison to the surrounding areas, Silver City is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in Silver City is valued at $87,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $48,420 (35%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $130,500 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $161,300 (64%) lower.

Rents

Silver City's median rent is $810 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $42 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $252 (23%) lower, and it is about $311 (27%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
