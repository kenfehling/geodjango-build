Home prices

The median home in Midland costs $210,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $3,920 (1%) lower. It is roughly $7,000 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $9,800 (4%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Midland the median rent is $857 per month. This is about $59 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $205 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $278 (24%) lower.
