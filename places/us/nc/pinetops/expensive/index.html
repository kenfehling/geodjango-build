Compared to other places in this area, Pinetops is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pinetops costs $66,800. This is about $13,720 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $150,700 (69%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $181,500 (73%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pinetops is $583. This is approximately $77 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $479 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $538 (47%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
