Home prices

The median home in Centerville is valued at $138,800. It is about $17,860 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $78,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $109,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Centerville's median rent is $695 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $25 (3%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $367 (34%) lower, and it is about $426 (38%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
