Home prices

The median home in Statesville is valued at $151,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $19,580 (14%) higher. It is about $65,700 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $68,500 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Statesville is $739. It is roughly $34 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $396 (34%) lower.
