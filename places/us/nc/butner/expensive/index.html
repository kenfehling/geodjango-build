Home prices

The median home in Butner costs $136,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $42,500 (23%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $80,800 (37%) lower, and is roughly $111,600 (44%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Butner the median monthly rent is $923. It is about $24 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $139 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $198 (17%) lower.
