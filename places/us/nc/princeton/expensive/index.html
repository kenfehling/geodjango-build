Home prices

The median home value in Princeton is $95,700. It is roughly $9,820 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $121,800 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $152,600 (61%) lower.

Rents

In Princeton the median monthly rent is $734. This is approximately $38 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $328 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $387 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
