Home prices

Rents

Kelford's median rent is $833 a month. This is about $239 (40%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is about $534 (39%) lower.
