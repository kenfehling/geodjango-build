Home prices

The value of the median home in Ansonville is $80,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $13,620 (14%) lower. It is approximately $136,700 (62%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $139,500 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ansonville is $822. It is approximately $102 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $240 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $313 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
