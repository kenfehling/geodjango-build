Home prices

Spencer's median home price is $98,500. It is approximately $38,020 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $119,000 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $121,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Spencer is $926 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $259 (38%) higher. It is roughly $136 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $209 (18%) lower.
