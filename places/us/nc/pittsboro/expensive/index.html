Compared to its nearby areas, Pittsboro is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Pittsboro is $255,100. It is approximately $43,840 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $37,600 (17%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $6,800 (2%) higher.

Rents

Pittsboro's median rent is $842 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $146 (14%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $220 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $279 (24%) lower.
