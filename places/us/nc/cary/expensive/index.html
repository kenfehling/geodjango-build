Compared to its surrounding areas, Cary is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Raleigh, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cary is $356,400. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $71,120 (24%) higher.It is roughly $138,900 (63%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $108,100 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cary is $1,246. It is roughly $49 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $184 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh is about $125 (11%) higher.
