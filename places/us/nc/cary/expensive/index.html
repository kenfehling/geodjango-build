In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cary is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in Cary is valued at $356,400. It is about $71,120 (24%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $138,900 (63%) higher, and it is about $108,100 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cary is $1,246. This is roughly $49 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $184 (17%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is about $125 (11%) higher.
