In comparison to other places in this area, Cary is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Raleigh, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cary is $356,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $71,120 (24%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $138,900 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $108,100 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Cary the median rent is $1,246 per month. It is roughly $49 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $184 (17%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $125 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
