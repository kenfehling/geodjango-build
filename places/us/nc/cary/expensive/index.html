Compared to other places in this area, Cary is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Raleigh, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cary is $356,400. This is roughly $71,120 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $138,900 (63%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $108,100 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Cary's median monthly rent is $1,246. It is approximately $49 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $184 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $125 (11%) higher.
