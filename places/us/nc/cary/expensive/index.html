Compared to nearby areas, Cary is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Raleigh, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Cary's median home value is $356,400. It is roughly $71,120 (24%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $138,900 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh is about $108,100 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cary is $1,246 per month. This is roughly $49 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $184 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $125 (11%) higher.
