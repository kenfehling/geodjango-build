Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morganton is $685. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $53 (8%) higher.It is approximately $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $450 (39%) lower.
