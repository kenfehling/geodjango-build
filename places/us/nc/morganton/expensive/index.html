Home prices

Morganton's median home cost is $160,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $30,760 (23%) higher.It is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $60,200 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Morganton is $685 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $53 (8%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $377 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $450 (39%) lower.
