In comparison to its surrounding areas, Morganton is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Morganton is $160,100. It is roughly $30,760 (23%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $57,400 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $60,200 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Morganton is $685 per month. It is about $53 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $450 (39%) lower.
