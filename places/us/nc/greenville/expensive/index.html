Home prices

Greenville's median home cost is $161,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $35,340 (28%) higher. It is approximately $56,300 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $87,100 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greenville is $819. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $28 (3%) higher. It is roughly $243 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $302 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
