Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Greenville is $819 a month. It is roughly $28 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $243 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $302 (26%) lower.
