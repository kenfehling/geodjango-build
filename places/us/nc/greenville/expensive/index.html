Home prices

The median home price in Greenville is $161,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $35,340 (28%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $56,300 (25%) lower, and is about $87,100 (35%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Greenville's median rent is $819 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $28 (3%) higher.It is approximately $243 (22%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $302 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
