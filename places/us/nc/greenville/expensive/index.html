Home prices

The median home cost in Greenville is $161,200. This is about $35,340 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $56,300 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is roughly $87,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Greenville is $819 a month. This is roughly $28 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $243 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $302 (26%) lower.
