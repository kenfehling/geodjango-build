In comparison to the surrounding areas, Greenville is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

Greenville's median home cost is $161,200. It is about $35,340 (28%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $56,300 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is roughly $87,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Greenville is $819 a month. This is roughly $28 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $302 (26%) lower.
