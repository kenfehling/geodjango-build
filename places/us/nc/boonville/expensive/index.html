The median home cost in Boonville is $137,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $15,260 (12%) higher. It is approximately $80,500 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $83,300 (37%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Boonville is $588 per month. It is approximately $124 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $474 (44%) lower, and is approximately $547 (48%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
