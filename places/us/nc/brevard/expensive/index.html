Compared to its nearby areas, Brevard is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brevard is valued at $232,500. This is about $15,460 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $15,000 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $96,200 (70%) higher.

Rents

Brevard's median monthly rent is $723. This is about $65 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $122 (14%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
