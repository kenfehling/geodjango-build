In comparison to its nearby areas, Brevard is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Brevard costs $232,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $15,460 (7%) higher. It is about $15,000 (6%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $96,200 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Brevard the median monthly rent is $723. This is approximately $65 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $339 (31%) lower, and is about $122 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
