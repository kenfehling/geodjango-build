Compared to nearby areas, Brevard is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Brevard is valued at $232,500. This is about $15,460 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $15,000 (6%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $96,200 (70%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Brevard the median rent is $723 a month. This is roughly $65 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $339 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $122 (14%) lower.
