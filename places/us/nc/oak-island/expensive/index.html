Home prices

The median home price in Oak Island is $255,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $21,840 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $38,400 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is approximately $9,500 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Oak Island the median rent is $1,072 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14 (1%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is roughly $74 (7%) higher.
