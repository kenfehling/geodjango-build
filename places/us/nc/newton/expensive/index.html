Home prices

The median home cost in Newton is $110,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $12,580 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $107,300 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $110,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

Newton's median monthly rent is $754. This is roughly $58 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $381 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
