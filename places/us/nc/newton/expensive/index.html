Home prices

The median home in Newton costs $110,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $12,580 (10%) lower. It is approximately $107,300 (49%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $110,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Newton is $754 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $58 (7%) lower. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $381 (33%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
