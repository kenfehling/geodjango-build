Compared to its surrounding areas, Newton is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Newton is $110,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $12,580 (10%) lower. It is about $107,300 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $110,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Newton's median rent is $754 a month. This is roughly $58 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $381 (33%) lower.
