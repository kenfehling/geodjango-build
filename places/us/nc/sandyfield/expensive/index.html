Home prices

The median home value in Sandyfield is $77,200. This is approximately $44,100 (36%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $140,300 (64%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $53,800 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Sandyfield the median monthly rent is $425. It is roughly $369 (46%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $637 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $522 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
