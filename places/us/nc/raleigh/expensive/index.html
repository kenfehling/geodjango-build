In comparison to other places in this area, Raleigh is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

Raleigh's median home cost is $248,300. This is about $33,083 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $30,800 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the nation.

Rents

Raleigh's median rent is $1,121 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $18 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $59 (5%) higher.
