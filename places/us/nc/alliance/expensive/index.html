Home prices

Rents

Alliance's median monthly rent is $329. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $525 (61%) lower.It is about $733 (69%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $490 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Greenville.
