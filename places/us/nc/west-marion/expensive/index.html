Home prices

The median home in West Marion costs $96,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $33,160 (25%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $120,900 (55%) lower, and it is approximately $123,700 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In West Marion the median rent is $735 per month. It is about $85 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $327 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $400 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
