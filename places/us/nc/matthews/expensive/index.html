Home prices

The median home in Matthews is valued at $265,300. This is roughly $50,040 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $47,800 (21%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $45,000 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Matthews is $1,279. It is roughly $65 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $217 (20%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $144 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
