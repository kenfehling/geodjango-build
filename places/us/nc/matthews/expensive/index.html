Matthews's median home cost is $265,300. It is approximately $50,040 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $47,800 (21%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $45,000 (20%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Matthews is $1,279. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $65 (4%) lower. It is approximately $217 (20%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $144 (12%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
