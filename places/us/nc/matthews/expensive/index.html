Home prices

The cost of the median home in Matthews is $265,300. It is approximately $50,040 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $47,800 (21%) higher, and it is roughly $45,000 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Matthews the median rent is $1,279 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $65 (4%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $217 (20%) higher, and it is approximately $144 (12%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
