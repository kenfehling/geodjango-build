Home prices

The median home cost in Tabor City is $96,800. It is roughly $3,440 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $120,700 (55%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $34,200 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tabor City is $637. It is approximately $87 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $425 (40%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $310 (32%) lower compared to the city of Fayetteville.
