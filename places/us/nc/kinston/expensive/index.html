Compared to the surrounding areas, Kinston is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in Kinston is valued at $101,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $20,280 (24%) higher. It is roughly $115,700 (53%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $146,500 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Kinston's median monthly rent is $703. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $31 (4%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $359 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $418 (37%) lower.
