Home prices

The median home in Kinston costs $101,800. This is approximately $20,280 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $115,700 (53%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $146,500 (59%) lower.

Rents

In Kinston the median monthly rent is $703. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $31 (4%) higher. It is about $359 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $418 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
