Home prices

The price of the median home in Laurinburg is $103,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $39,360 (61%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $114,300 (52%) lower, and is roughly $117,100 (53%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Laurinburg is $693 per month. This is approximately $42 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $369 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $442 (38%) lower.
