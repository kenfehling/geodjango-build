In comparison to the surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Charlotte, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Harrisburg's median home cost is $288,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $78,720 (37%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $71,400 (32%) higher, and it is approximately $68,600 (31%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median rent is $1,634 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $685 (72%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $572 (53%) higher, and is roughly $499 (43%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
