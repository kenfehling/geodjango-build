Compared to other places in this area, Harrisburg is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Charlotte, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Harrisburg is valued at $288,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $78,720 (37%) higher.It is about $71,400 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $68,600 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median rent is $1,634 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $685 (72%) higher.It is roughly $572 (53%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $499 (43%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
