Compared to its nearby areas, Harrisburg is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Harrisburg is $288,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $78,720 (37%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $71,400 (32%) higher, and is roughly $68,600 (31%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Harrisburg's median monthly rent is $1,634. Compared to other places in this area this is about $685 (72%) higher. It is roughly $572 (53%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $499 (43%) higher.
