Compared to its surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

Harrisburg's median home price is $288,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $78,720 (37%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $71,400 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $68,600 (31%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Harrisburg is $1,634 a month. This is roughly $685 (72%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $572 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $499 (43%) higher.
