Home prices

The median home in Harrisburg is valued at $288,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $78,720 (37%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $71,400 (32%) higher, and is roughly $68,600 (31%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrisburg is $1,634. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $685 (72%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $572 (53%) higher, and it is approximately $499 (43%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
