Home prices

The median home in Fremont is valued at $83,100. This is about $7,920 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $134,400 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $165,200 (66%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fremont is $697 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $67 (8%) lower.It is approximately $365 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $424 (37%) lower.
