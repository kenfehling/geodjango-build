Compared to other places in this area, Forest Hills is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Forest Hills costs $246,600. This is approximately $47,340 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $29,100 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $110,300 (80%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Forest Hills the median rent is $770 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $68 (9%) higher.It is approximately $292 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $75 (8%) lower.
