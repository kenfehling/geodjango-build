Compared to nearby areas, Forest Hills is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Forest Hills is $246,600. It is roughly $47,340 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $29,100 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $110,300 (80%) higher.

Rents

Forest Hills's median rent is $770 per month. It is approximately $68 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $292 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $75 (8%) lower.
