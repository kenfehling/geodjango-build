Compared to the surrounding areas, Forest Hills is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Forest Hills is $246,600. It is roughly $47,340 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $29,100 (13%) higher, and it is about $110,300 (80%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Forest Hills the median rent is $770 per month. This is approximately $68 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $292 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $75 (8%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
