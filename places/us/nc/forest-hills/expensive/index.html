In comparison to other places in this area, Forest Hills is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Forest Hills is $246,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $47,340 (23%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $29,100 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $110,300 (80%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Forest Hills is $770. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $68 (9%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $292 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $75 (8%) lower.
