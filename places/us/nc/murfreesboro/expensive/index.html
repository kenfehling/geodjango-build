Home prices

The median home in Murfreesboro is valued at $126,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $39,860 (46%) higher.It is about $91,200 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $154,500 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

Murfreesboro's median rent is $717 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $73 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $345 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $650 (47%) lower.
