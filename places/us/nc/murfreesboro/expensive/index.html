Home prices

The median home in Murfreesboro is valued at $126,300. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $39,860 (46%) higher. It is roughly $91,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $154,500 (55%) lower.

Rents

Murfreesboro's median monthly rent is $717. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $73 (11%) higher. It is roughly $345 (32%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $650 (47%) lower.
