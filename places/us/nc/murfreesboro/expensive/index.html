Compared to its surrounding areas, Murfreesboro is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

The median home value in Murfreesboro is $126,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $39,860 (46%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $91,200 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $154,500 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

In Murfreesboro the median rent is $717 per month. It is about $73 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $345 (32%) lower, and is about $650 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
