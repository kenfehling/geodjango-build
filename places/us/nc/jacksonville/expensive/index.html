Much like its surrounding areas, it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Wilmington, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville is valued at $154,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $4,140 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $62,600 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is about $91,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $992. This is roughly $58 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $70 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Wilmington it is is very similar.
