Home prices

The median home price in Jacksonville is $154,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $4,140 (2%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $62,600 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is roughly $91,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

Jacksonville's median rent is $992 per month. This is about $58 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $70 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Wilmington it is is very similar.
