Home prices

Jacksonville's median home price is $154,900. It is approximately $4,140 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $62,600 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington is approximately $91,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median rent is $992 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $58 (5%) lower. It is approximately $70 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Wilmington.
