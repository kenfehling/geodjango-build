In comparison to other places in this area, Wilmington is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Fayetteville however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wilmington is $246,400. It is about $51,280 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $28,900 (13%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is about $115,400 (88%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wilmington is $998. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $252 (20%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $64 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $51 (5%) higher.
