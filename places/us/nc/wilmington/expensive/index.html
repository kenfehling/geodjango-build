Compared to the surrounding areas, Wilmington is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Fayetteville however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wilmington is valued at $246,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $51,280 (17%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $28,900 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $115,400 (88%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington is $998 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $252 (20%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $64 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $51 (5%) higher.
