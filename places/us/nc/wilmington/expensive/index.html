Home prices

Wilmington's median home value is $246,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $51,280 (17%) lower.It is about $28,900 (13%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $115,400 (88%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington is $998 per month. This is about $252 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $64 (6%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is about $51 (5%) higher.
