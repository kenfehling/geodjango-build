Compared to other places in this area, Wilmington is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The median home in Wilmington costs $246,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $51,280 (17%) lower. It is roughly $28,900 (13%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $115,400 (88%) higher.

Rents

In Wilmington the median monthly rent is $998. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $252 (20%) lower. It is approximately $64 (6%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $51 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
