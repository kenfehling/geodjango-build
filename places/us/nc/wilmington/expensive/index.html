In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wilmington is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wilmington is $246,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $51,280 (17%) lower. It is approximately $28,900 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $115,400 (88%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wilmington is $998. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $252 (20%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $64 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is about $51 (5%) higher.
