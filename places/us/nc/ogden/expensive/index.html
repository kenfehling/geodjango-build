Home prices

The value of the median home in Ogden is $331,300. This is roughly $34,960 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $113,800 (52%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $200,300 (152%) higher.

Rents
