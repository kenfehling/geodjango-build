Compared to its surrounding areas, Ogden is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The median home in Ogden costs $331,300. This is about $34,960 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $113,800 (52%) higher, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $200,300 (152%) higher.

Rents

Ogden's median monthly rent is $1,140. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $100 (8%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $78 (7%) higher, and is approximately $193 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
