Home prices

The median home cost in Ogden is $331,300. It is about $34,960 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $113,800 (52%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $200,300 (152%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ogden is $1,140 per month. This is about $100 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $78 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $193 (20%) higher.
