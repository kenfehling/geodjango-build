Home prices

The cost of the median home in Saluda is $259,500. This is approximately $46,300 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $42,000 (19%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $10,900 (4%) lower compared to the city of Asheville.

Rents

Saluda's median rent is $994 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $108 (12%) higher. It is about $68 (6%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Asheville it is about $49 (4%) lower.
