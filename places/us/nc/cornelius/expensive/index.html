Compared to the surrounding areas, Cornelius is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cornelius is $322,400. It is about $19,940 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $104,900 (48%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $102,100 (46%) higher.

Rents

Cornelius's median rent is $1,219 per month. It is about $86 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $157 (14%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $84 (7%) higher.
