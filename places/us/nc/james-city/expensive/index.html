Home prices

James City's median home value is $153,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $48,200 (23%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $64,100 (29%) lower, and is about $93,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

The median rent in James City is $886 a month. This is roughly $294 (24%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $176 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $112 (11%) lower compared to the city of Wilmington.
