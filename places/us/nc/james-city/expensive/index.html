Home prices

The cost of the median home in James City is $153,400. It is approximately $48,200 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $64,100 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $93,000 (37%) lower compared to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

James City's median rent is $886 a month. This is about $294 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $176 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $112 (11%) lower compared to the city of Wilmington.
