Home prices

The median home in Gastonia is valued at $155,200. It is roughly $3,040 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $62,300 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $65,100 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gastonia is $879. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $256 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
