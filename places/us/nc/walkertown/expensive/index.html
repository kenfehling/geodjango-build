Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Walkertown is $756. It is roughly $357 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $306 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $379 (33%) lower.
