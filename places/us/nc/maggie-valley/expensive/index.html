Home prices

The median home in Maggie Valley is valued at $202,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $33,260 (19%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $15,500 (7%) lower, and it is about $65,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents
