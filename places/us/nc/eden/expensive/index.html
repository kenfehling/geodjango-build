Home prices

The median home in Eden is valued at $87,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $24,760 (22%) lower. It is approximately $130,000 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $160,800 (64%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Eden the median monthly rent is $666. This is roughly $36 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $396 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $455 (40%) lower.
