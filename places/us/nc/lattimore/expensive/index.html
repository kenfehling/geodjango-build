Home prices

Lattimore's median home cost is $99,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $16,340 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $118,100 (54%) lower, and it is about $120,900 (54%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lattimore is $625. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $35 (5%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and it is about $510 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
