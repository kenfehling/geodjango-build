Home prices

The value of the median home in Murraysville is $192,300. This is about $80,100 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $25,200 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $61,300 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Murraysville the median monthly rent is $1,206. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $122 (11%) higher. It is roughly $144 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $259 (27%) higher.
