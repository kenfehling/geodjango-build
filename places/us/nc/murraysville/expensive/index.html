The median home in Murraysville is valued at $192,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $80,100 (29%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $25,200 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $61,300 (46%) higher.

Rents
