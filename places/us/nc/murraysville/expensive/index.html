Home prices

Rents

Murraysville's median monthly rent is $1,206. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $122 (11%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $144 (13%) higher, and it is about $259 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
