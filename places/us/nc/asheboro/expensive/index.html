Home prices

Asheboro's median home value is $120,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,060 (14%) higher. It is approximately $96,600 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $99,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

Asheboro's median monthly rent is $670. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $18 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $392 (36%) lower, and is roughly $465 (40%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
