Home prices

The median home in Asheboro is valued at $120,900. It is roughly $15,060 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $96,600 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $99,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

Asheboro's median monthly rent is $670. This is approximately $18 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $392 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $465 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
