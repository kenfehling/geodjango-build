Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Asheboro is $670 a month. This is about $18 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $392 (36%) lower, and it is about $465 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
