In comparison to its surrounding areas, Asheboro is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Asheboro is $120,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $15,060 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $96,600 (44%) lower, and is roughly $99,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Asheboro is $670. It is about $18 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $392 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $465 (40%) lower.
