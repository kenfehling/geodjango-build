In comparison to the surrounding areas, Carrboro is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Carrboro is $368,800. This is about $98,660 (36%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $151,300 (69%) higher, and is about $120,500 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in Carrboro is $1,078 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $29 (2%) lower. It is approximately $16 (1%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $43 (3%) lower.
