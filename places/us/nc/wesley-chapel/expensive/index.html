Compared to the surrounding areas, Wesley Chapel is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wesley Chapel is valued at $348,700. This is approximately $22,740 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $131,200 (60%) higher, and it is about $128,400 (58%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Wesley Chapel is $1,551 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $92 (5%) lower. It is approximately $489 (46%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $416 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
