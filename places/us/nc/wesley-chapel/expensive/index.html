Home prices

Rents

Wesley Chapel's median monthly rent is $1,551. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $92 (5%) lower. It is roughly $489 (46%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $416 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
