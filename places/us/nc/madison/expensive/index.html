Compared to other places in this area, Madison is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

Madison's median home value is $114,200. This is about $20,120 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $103,300 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $134,100 (54%) lower.

Rents

Madison's median rent is $643 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $31 (4%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $419 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $478 (42%) lower.
