Home prices

The value of the median home in Madison is $114,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $20,120 (14%) lower. It is approximately $103,300 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $134,100 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Madison's median rent is $643 a month. It is about $31 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $419 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $478 (42%) lower.
