Compared to other places in this area, Madison is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Madison is $114,200. It is approximately $20,120 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $103,300 (47%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $134,100 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Madison the median monthly rent is $643. This is roughly $31 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $419 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $478 (42%) lower.
