Rents

The median monthly rent in Lewisville is $863. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $27 (3%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $199 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $272 (23%) lower.
