Compared to nearby areas, Lewisville is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lewisville is valued at $195,400. This is approximately $33,420 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $22,100 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $24,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

Lewisville's median rent is $863 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $27 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $199 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $272 (23%) lower.
