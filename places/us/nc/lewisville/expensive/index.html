Home prices

The median home in Lewisville is valued at $195,400. This is roughly $33,420 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $22,100 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $24,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

Lewisville's median monthly rent is $863. This is roughly $27 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $199 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $272 (23%) lower.
