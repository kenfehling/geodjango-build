Home prices

The median home in Calabash costs $139,300. It is about $145,380 (51%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $78,200 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $8,300 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Calabash is $979 per month. It is roughly $180 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $83 (7%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $32 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
