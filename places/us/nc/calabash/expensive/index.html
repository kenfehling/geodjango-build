In comparison to its surrounding areas, Calabash is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Calabash is valued at $139,300. It is about $145,380 (51%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $78,200 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $8,300 (6%) higher.

Rents

In Calabash the median monthly rent is $979. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $180 (15%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $83 (7%) lower, and it is about $32 (3%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.
