Home prices

The median home price in Kings Grant is $174,000. It is approximately $29,860 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $43,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $43,000 (32%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kings Grant is $1,164. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $106 (10%) higher. It is roughly $102 (9%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is about $217 (22%) higher.
