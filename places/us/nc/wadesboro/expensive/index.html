Home prices

The median home in Wadesboro costs $81,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $3,880 (4%) lower. It is approximately $136,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $139,200 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wadesboro is $778. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $69 (9%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $284 (26%) lower, and is approximately $357 (31%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
