Home prices

The value of the median home in Wadesboro is $81,100. It is about $3,880 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $136,400 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $139,200 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Wadesboro is $778 per month. It is approximately $69 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $284 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $357 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
