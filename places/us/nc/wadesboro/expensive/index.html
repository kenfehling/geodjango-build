Home prices

The value of the median home in Wadesboro is $81,100. This is about $3,880 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $136,400 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $139,200 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Wadesboro the median monthly rent is $778. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $69 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $284 (26%) lower, and is roughly $357 (31%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
