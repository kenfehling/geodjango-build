In comparison to nearby areas, Denver is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. It is close to average compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The median home in Denver costs $215,600. This is roughly $96,700 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $4,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

Denver's median monthly rent is $808. This is about $338 (29%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $327 (28%) lower.
