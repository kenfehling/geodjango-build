Home prices

Denver's median home cost is $215,600. It is approximately $96,700 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $4,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Denver is $808 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $338 (29%) lower.It is roughly $254 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $327 (28%) lower.
