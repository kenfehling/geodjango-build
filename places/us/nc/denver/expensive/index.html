Compared to nearby areas, Denver is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Denver costs $215,600. This is roughly $96,700 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is is very similar compared to the entire nation, and is about $4,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Denver is $808 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $338 (29%) lower.It is roughly $254 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $327 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
