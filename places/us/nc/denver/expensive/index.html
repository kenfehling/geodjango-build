Home prices

The median home in Denver costs $215,600. This is roughly $96,700 (30%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is is very similar in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $4,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Denver is $808 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $338 (29%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $254 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $327 (28%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
