Home prices

The median home cost in Rocky Point is $178,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $23,300 (11%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $39,200 (18%) lower, and it is about $47,300 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Rocky Point the median rent is $638 per month. This is approximately $180 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $424 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $309 (32%) lower.
