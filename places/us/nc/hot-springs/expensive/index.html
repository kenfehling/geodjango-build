Rents

Hot Springs's median monthly rent is $420. This is approximately $214 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $642 (60%) lower, and is roughly $425 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
