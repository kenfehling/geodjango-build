Home prices

The median home in Hot Springs is valued at $162,100. It is about $3,940 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $55,400 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $25,800 (18%) higher.

Rents

Hot Springs's median monthly rent is $420. It is roughly $214 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $642 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $425 (50%) lower.
