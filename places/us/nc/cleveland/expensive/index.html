In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cleveland costs $120,100. This is about $16,240 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $97,400 (44%) lower, and it is approximately $100,200 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cleveland is $663. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $187 (21%) lower.It is about $399 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $472 (41%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
