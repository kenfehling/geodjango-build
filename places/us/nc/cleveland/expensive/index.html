In comparison to its nearby areas, Cleveland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Cleveland is $120,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $16,240 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $97,400 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $100,200 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $663. This is approximately $187 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $399 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $472 (41%) lower.
