In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cleveland is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cleveland is $120,100. This is about $16,240 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $97,400 (44%) lower, and is approximately $100,200 (45%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $663 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $187 (21%) lower.It is roughly $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $472 (41%) lower.
