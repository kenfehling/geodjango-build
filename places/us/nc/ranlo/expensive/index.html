Rents

In Ranlo the median monthly rent is $1,075. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $188 (21%) higher. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $60 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
