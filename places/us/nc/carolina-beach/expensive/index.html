In comparison to the surrounding areas, Carolina Beach is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Wilmington, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Carolina Beach is $353,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $79,360 (28%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $135,600 (62%) higher, and it is about $106,700 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

Carolina Beach's median rent is $1,153 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $119 (11%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $91 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington is roughly $155 (15%) higher.
