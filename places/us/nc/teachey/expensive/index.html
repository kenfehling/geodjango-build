Home prices

The median home in Teachey costs $141,700. It is about $48,500 (52%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $75,800 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $106,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Teachey the median rent is $754 per month. This is about $89 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $308 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $367 (32%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
