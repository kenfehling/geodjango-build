Home prices

The median home in Wrightsville Beach is valued at $798,700. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $525,920 (192%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $581,200 (267%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is approximately $552,300 (224%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wrightsville Beach is $1,655 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $518 (45%) higher. It is approximately $593 (55%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is about $657 (65%) higher.
