Home prices

The median home in Edenton is valued at $167,500. It is about $59,500 (55%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $50,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $113,300 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Edenton is $757 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $73 (10%) higher. It is roughly $305 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $610 (44%) lower.
