Home prices

Rents

In Edenton the median rent is $757 a month. This is about $73 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $305 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $610 (44%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
