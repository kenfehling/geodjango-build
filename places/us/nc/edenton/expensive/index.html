Home prices

The median home in Edenton costs $167,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $59,500 (55%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $50,000 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $113,300 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edenton is $757. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $73 (10%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $305 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $610 (44%) lower compared to the city of Virginia Beach.
