Home prices

The value of the median home in Polkton is $76,300. It is roughly $19,060 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $141,200 (64%) lower, and it is about $144,000 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Polkton's median rent is $680 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $89 (11%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $382 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $455 (40%) lower.
