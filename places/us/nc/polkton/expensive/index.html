Compared to the surrounding areas, Polkton is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Polkton is valued at $76,300. It is roughly $19,060 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $141,200 (64%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $144,000 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Polkton the median monthly rent is $680. This is approximately $89 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $455 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
