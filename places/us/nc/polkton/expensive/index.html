Home prices

Polkton's median home value is $76,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $19,060 (19%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $141,200 (64%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $144,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

Polkton's median monthly rent is $680. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $89 (11%) lower. It is about $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $455 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
