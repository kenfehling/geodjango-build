Home prices

The median home in St. Pauls costs $81,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $7,420 (8%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $136,400 (62%) lower, and it is about $167,200 (67%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

St. Pauls's median rent is $630 per month. This is approximately $40 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $432 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $491 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
