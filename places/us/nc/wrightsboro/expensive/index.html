In comparison to its nearby areas, Wrightsboro is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wrightsboro is $141,800. It is approximately $42,860 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $75,700 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $10,800 (8%) higher.

Rents

Wrightsboro's median rent is $980 per month. It is roughly $32 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $82 (7%) lower, and is about $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
