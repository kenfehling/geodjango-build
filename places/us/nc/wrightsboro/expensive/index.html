Home prices

Wrightsboro's median home value is $141,800. It is approximately $42,860 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $75,700 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is about $10,800 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Wrightsboro the median rent is $980 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $32 (3%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $82 (7%) lower, and it is about $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
