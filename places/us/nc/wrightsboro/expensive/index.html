In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wrightsboro is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wrightsboro is $141,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $42,860 (23%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $75,700 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $10,800 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wrightsboro is $980 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $32 (3%) lower.It is roughly $82 (7%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
