Compared to its surrounding areas, Wrightsboro is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Fayetteville however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Wrightsboro is $141,800. This is approximately $42,860 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $75,700 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $10,800 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Wrightsboro the median monthly rent is $980. It is roughly $32 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $82 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is approximately $33 (3%) higher.
