In comparison to nearby areas, Asheville is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Asheville is valued at $270,400. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $84,420 (23%) lower. It is roughly $52,900 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $134,100 (98%) higher.

Rents

Asheville's median monthly rent is $1,043. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $26 (2%) lower. It is about $19 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $198 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
