Compared to nearby areas, Asheville is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Asheville costs $270,400. It is about $84,420 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $52,900 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $134,100 (98%) higher.

Rents

Asheville's median monthly rent is $1,043. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $26 (2%) lower.It is approximately $19 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $198 (23%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
