In comparison to the surrounding areas, Asheville is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Asheville is $270,400. It is roughly $84,420 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $52,900 (24%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $134,100 (98%) higher.

Rents

Asheville's median monthly rent is $1,043. This is about $26 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $19 (1%) lower, and is roughly $198 (23%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
