In comparison to its surrounding areas, Asheville is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Asheville is $270,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $84,420 (23%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $52,900 (24%) higher, and is about $134,100 (98%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Asheville is $1,043. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $26 (2%) lower. It is about $19 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $198 (23%) higher.
