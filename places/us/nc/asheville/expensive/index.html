Compared to nearby areas, Asheville is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Asheville's median home price is $270,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $84,420 (23%) lower.It is about $52,900 (24%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $134,100 (98%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Asheville is $1,043. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $26 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $19 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $198 (23%) higher.
