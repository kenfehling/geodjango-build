In comparison to other places in this area, Asheville is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Asheville is $270,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $84,420 (23%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $52,900 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $134,100 (98%) higher.

Rents

In Asheville the median monthly rent is $1,043. It is roughly $26 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $19 (1%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $198 (23%) higher.
