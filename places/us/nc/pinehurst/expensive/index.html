Home prices

The median home value in Pinehurst is $293,100. It is approximately $84,560 (40%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $75,600 (34%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $72,800 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Pinehurst the median rent is $1,420 a month. This is about $606 (74%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $358 (33%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $285 (25%) higher.
