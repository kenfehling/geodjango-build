Home prices

Rents

Mount Pleasant's median rent is $868 a month. It is approximately $33 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $194 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $267 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
