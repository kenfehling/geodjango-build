Home prices

Burlington's median home value is $134,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $70,200 (34%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $83,400 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $114,200 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burlington is $816. It is about $100 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $246 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $305 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
