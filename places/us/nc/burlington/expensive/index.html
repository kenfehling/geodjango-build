Compared to its surrounding areas, Burlington is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in Burlington costs $134,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $70,200 (34%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $83,400 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $114,200 (45%) lower.

Rents

Burlington's median rent is $816 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $100 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $246 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $305 (27%) lower.
