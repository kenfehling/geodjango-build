Compared to its surrounding areas, Burlington is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Burlington's median home value is $134,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $70,200 (34%) lower. It is approximately $83,400 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $114,200 (45%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Burlington the median rent is $816 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $100 (10%) lower. It is roughly $246 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $305 (27%) lower.
