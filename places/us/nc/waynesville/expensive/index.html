Home prices

Rents

In Waynesville the median rent is $814 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $9 (1%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $248 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $31 (3%) lower.
