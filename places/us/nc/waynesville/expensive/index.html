In comparison to nearby areas, Waynesville is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Waynesville is valued at $161,800. This is approximately $14,980 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $55,700 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $25,500 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Waynesville the median monthly rent is $814. Compared to nearby areas it is about $9 (1%) higher. It is approximately $248 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $31 (3%) lower.
