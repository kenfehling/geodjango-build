In comparison to nearby areas, Waynesville is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waynesville costs $161,800. It is about $14,980 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $55,700 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $25,500 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Waynesville is $814 a month. This is roughly $9 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $248 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $31 (3%) lower.
