Home prices

Cherryville's median home cost is $125,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $27,340 (27%) higher.It is about $91,900 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $94,700 (42%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Cherryville is $754 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $52 (7%) higher.It is roughly $308 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $381 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
