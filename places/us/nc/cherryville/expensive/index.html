Home prices

The median home value in Cherryville is $125,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $27,340 (27%) higher. It is approximately $91,900 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $94,700 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Cherryville the median rent is $754 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $52 (7%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $381 (33%) lower.
