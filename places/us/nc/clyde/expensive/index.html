The cost of the median home in Clyde is $161,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $15,580 (8%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $56,200 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $25,000 (18%) higher.

Rents

Clyde's median rent is $808 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $254 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $37 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
