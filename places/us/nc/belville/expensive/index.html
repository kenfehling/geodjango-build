Home prices

The median home cost in Belville is $177,600. This is approximately $4,640 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $39,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $46,600 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

Belville's median rent is $1,351 per month. This is roughly $336 (33%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $289 (27%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $404 (42%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.
