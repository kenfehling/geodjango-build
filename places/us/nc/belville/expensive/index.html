Much like its surrounding areas, it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Belville is $177,600. It is roughly $4,640 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $39,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $46,600 (35%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

Belville's median rent is $1,351 per month. It is approximately $336 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $289 (27%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $404 (42%) higher.
