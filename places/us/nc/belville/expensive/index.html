Home prices

The median home in Belville costs $177,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $4,640 (2%) lower.It is roughly $39,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $46,600 (35%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Belville the median monthly rent is $1,351. This is roughly $336 (33%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $289 (27%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $404 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
