Home prices

The value of the median home in Belville is $177,600. This is roughly $4,640 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $39,900 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is about $46,600 (35%) higher.

Rents

Belville's median rent is $1,351 per month. This is roughly $336 (33%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $289 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is about $404 (42%) higher.
