Much like other places in this area, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Belville is $177,600. This is approximately $4,640 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $39,900 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $46,600 (35%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

In Belville the median rent is $1,351 per month. It is roughly $336 (33%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $289 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $404 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
