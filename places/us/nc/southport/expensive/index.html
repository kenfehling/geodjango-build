Home prices

The median home in Southport is valued at $252,600. It is approximately $83,660 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $35,100 (16%) higher, and is roughly $6,200 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

Southport's median monthly rent is $980. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $295 (23%) lower. It is roughly $82 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $18 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
