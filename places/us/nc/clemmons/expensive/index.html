Home prices

The median home in Clemmons costs $226,600. This is roughly $64,600 (39%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $9,100 (4%) higher, and it is approximately $6,300 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Clemmons's median rent is $866 a month. This is approximately $21 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $196 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $269 (23%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
