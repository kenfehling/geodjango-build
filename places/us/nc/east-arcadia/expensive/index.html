Home prices

The median home in East Arcadia costs $73,800. It is about $74,140 (50%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $143,700 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is about $57,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

In East Arcadia the median rent is $658 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $104 (13%) lower. It is approximately $404 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is approximately $289 (30%) lower.
