Home prices

The median home in East Arcadia is valued at $73,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $74,140 (50%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $143,700 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $57,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

East Arcadia's median monthly rent is $658. This is about $104 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $404 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $289 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
