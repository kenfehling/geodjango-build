Home prices

The median home in Concord is valued at $206,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $29,060 (16%) higher. It is roughly $10,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $13,700 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Concord the median monthly rent is $963. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $54 (5%) lower. It is roughly $99 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $172 (15%) lower.
