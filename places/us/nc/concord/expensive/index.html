Home prices

The median home price in Concord is $206,600. It is about $29,060 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $10,900 (5%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $13,700 (6%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Concord's median rent is $963 a month. It is approximately $54 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $99 (9%) lower, and is roughly $172 (15%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
