Compared to other places in this area, Canton is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

Canton's median home value is $143,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $36,460 (20%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $73,600 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $7,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Canton the median monthly rent is $683. It is approximately $148 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $379 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $162 (19%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
