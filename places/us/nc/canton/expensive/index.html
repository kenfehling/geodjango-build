Home prices

The cost of the median home in Canton is $143,900. It is about $36,460 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $73,600 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $7,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Canton is $683 per month. It is approximately $148 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $379 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $162 (19%) lower.
