Home prices

The value of the median home in Canton is $143,900. It is roughly $36,460 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $73,600 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $7,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Canton is $683. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $148 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $379 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $162 (19%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
