Home prices

Rents

Kannapolis's median monthly rent is $856. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $74 (7%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $206 (19%) lower, and is about $279 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
