Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Kannapolis is $856 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $74 (7%) lower. It is approximately $206 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $279 (24%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
