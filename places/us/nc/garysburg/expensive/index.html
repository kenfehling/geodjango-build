Compared to its surrounding areas, Garysburg is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home value in Garysburg is $74,000. It is roughly $6,260 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $143,500 (65%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $174,300 (70%) lower.

Rents

In Garysburg the median rent is $844 a month. It is approximately $135 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $218 (20%) lower, and is roughly $277 (24%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
