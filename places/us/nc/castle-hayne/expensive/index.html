Home prices

The median home in Castle Hayne is valued at $175,900. This is roughly $5,280 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $41,600 (19%) lower, and is roughly $44,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Castle Hayne is $776. It is roughly $122 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $286 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is roughly $171 (18%) lower.
