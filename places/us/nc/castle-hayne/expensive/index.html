Home prices

The price of the median home in Castle Hayne is $175,900. It is about $5,280 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $41,600 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $44,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

Castle Hayne's median rent is $776 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $122 (13%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $286 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is roughly $171 (18%) lower.
