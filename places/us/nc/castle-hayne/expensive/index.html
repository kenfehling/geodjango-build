Home prices

The median home cost in Castle Hayne is $175,900. This is roughly $5,280 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $41,600 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is roughly $44,900 (34%) higher.

Rents

In Castle Hayne the median rent is $776 a month. This is roughly $122 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $286 (26%) lower, and is approximately $171 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
