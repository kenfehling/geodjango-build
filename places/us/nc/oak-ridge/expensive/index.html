Home prices

Oak Ridge's median home price is $383,300. This is approximately $174,940 (83%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $165,800 (76%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $163,000 (73%) higher in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Oak Ridge is $2,059 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $1,202 (140%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $997 (93%) higher, and is about $924 (81%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
