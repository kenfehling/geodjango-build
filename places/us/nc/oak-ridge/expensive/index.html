Home prices

The median home cost in Oak Ridge is $383,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $174,940 (83%) higher. It is approximately $165,800 (76%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $163,000 (73%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Oak Ridge's median rent is $2,059 a month. It is about $1,202 (140%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $997 (93%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $924 (81%) higher.
