Home prices

Rents

Oak Ridge's median rent is $2,059 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $1,202 (140%) higher.It is about $997 (93%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $924 (81%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
