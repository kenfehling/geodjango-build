In comparison to the surrounding areas, Atlantic Beach is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Wilmington.

Home prices

Atlantic Beach's median home value is $383,600. This is approximately $139,060 (56%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $166,100 (76%) higher, and is roughly $137,200 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

In Atlantic Beach the median rent is $958 per month. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $104 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is about $40 (4%) lower.
