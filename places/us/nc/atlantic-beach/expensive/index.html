The median home in Atlantic Beach costs $383,600. It is about $139,060 (56%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $166,100 (76%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $137,200 (55%) higher compared to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

In Atlantic Beach the median rent is $958 per month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $104 (9%) lower, and is approximately $40 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
