Home prices

Rents

In Greensboro the median monthly rent is $877. It is about $295 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $185 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $258 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
