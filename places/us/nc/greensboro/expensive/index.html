In comparison to its surrounding areas, Greensboro is significantly less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Greensboro is $156,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $117,220 (42%) lower. It is approximately $61,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $64,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Greensboro the median rent is $877 a month. This is approximately $295 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $185 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $258 (22%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
