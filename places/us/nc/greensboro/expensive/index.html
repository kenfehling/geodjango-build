Home prices

The median home in Greensboro is valued at $156,300. It is roughly $117,220 (42%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $61,200 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is approximately $64,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

Greensboro's median rent is $877 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $295 (25%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $185 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is approximately $258 (22%) lower.
