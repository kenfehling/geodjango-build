Compared to the surrounding areas, Greensboro is significantly less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Charlotte, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Greensboro's median home value is $156,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $117,220 (42%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $61,200 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $64,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greensboro is $877. Compared to other places in this area this is about $295 (25%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $185 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $258 (22%) lower.
