Compared to its surrounding areas, Forest Oaks is significantly more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Forest Oaks is $257,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $97,000 (60%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $40,400 (18%) higher, and it is approximately $37,600 (17%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Forest Oaks's median monthly rent is $1,202. It is roughly $389 (47%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $140 (13%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $67 (5%) higher.
