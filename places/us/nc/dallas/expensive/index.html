Home prices

The median home value in Dallas is $135,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $2,400 (1%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $82,500 (37%) lower, and it is about $85,300 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Dallas's median rent is $723 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $154 (17%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $339 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $412 (36%) lower.
