Home prices

The median home cost in Dallas is $135,000. It is about $2,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $82,500 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $85,300 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Dallas's median rent is $723 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $154 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $339 (31%) lower, and is approximately $412 (36%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
