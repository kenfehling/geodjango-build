Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Dallas is $723 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $154 (17%) lower.It is about $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $412 (36%) lower.
