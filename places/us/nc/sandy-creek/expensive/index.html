Home prices

The price of the median home in Sandy Creek is $133,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $35,960 (21%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $83,800 (38%) lower, and it is about $2,700 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

Sandy Creek's median monthly rent is $833. It is roughly $171 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $114 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
