Home prices

The median home in Marshall costs $190,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $38,900 (16%) lower. It is roughly $27,500 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $53,700 (39%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marshall is $397. This is about $448 (53%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $665 (62%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $448 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
