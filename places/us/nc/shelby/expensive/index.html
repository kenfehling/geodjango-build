Compared to its nearby areas, Shelby is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Shelby is $119,300. It is about $15,940 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $98,200 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $101,000 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Shelby is $707 per month. It is roughly $23 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $355 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $428 (37%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
