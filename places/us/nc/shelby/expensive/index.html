Home prices

The value of the median home in Shelby is $119,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $15,940 (15%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $98,200 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $101,000 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Shelby is $707 per month. It is roughly $23 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $355 (33%) lower, and is approximately $428 (37%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
