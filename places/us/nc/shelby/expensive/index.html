Home prices

The value of the median home in Shelby is $119,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $15,940 (15%) higher. It is roughly $98,200 (45%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $101,000 (45%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Shelby the median rent is $707 a month. It is approximately $23 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $355 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $428 (37%) lower.
