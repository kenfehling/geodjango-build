Home prices

The median home cost in Shelby is $119,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $15,940 (15%) higher.It is roughly $98,200 (45%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $101,000 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Shelby is $707 per month. This is approximately $23 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $355 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $428 (37%) lower.
