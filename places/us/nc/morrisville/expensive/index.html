Home prices

The price of the median home in Morrisville is $331,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $6,020 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $114,300 (52%) higher, and is about $83,500 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in Morrisville is $1,398 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $140 (11%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $336 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is about $277 (24%) higher.
