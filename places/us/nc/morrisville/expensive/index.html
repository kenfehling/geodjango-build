Much like other places in this area, it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home price in Morrisville is $331,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $6,020 (1%) higher. It is about $114,300 (52%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $83,500 (33%) higher compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morrisville is $1,398. It is about $140 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $336 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $277 (24%) higher.
