Home prices

Trenton's median home price is $93,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $18,540 (16%) lower. It is approximately $124,500 (57%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Wilmington it is about $153,400 (62%) lower.

Rents

Trenton's median monthly rent is $700. This is approximately $87 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $362 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Wilmington it is roughly $298 (29%) lower.
