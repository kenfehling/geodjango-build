The median home in Taylorsville costs $118,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $22,080 (15%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $99,200 (45%) lower, and is approximately $102,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Taylorsville the median rent is $658 per month. This is roughly $25 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $404 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $477 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
