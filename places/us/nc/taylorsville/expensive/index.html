Home prices

The median home in Taylorsville is valued at $118,300. This is approximately $22,080 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $99,200 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is roughly $102,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

Taylorsville's median monthly rent is $658. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $25 (3%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $404 (38%) lower, and is roughly $477 (42%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
