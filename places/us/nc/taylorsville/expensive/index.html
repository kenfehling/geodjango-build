Home prices

The price of the median home in Taylorsville is $118,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $22,080 (15%) lower. It is about $99,200 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $102,000 (46%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Taylorsville the median monthly rent is $658. It is about $25 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $404 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $477 (42%) lower.
