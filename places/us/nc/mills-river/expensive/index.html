Compared to nearby areas, Mills River is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home price in Mills River is $257,800. It is roughly $54,560 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $40,300 (18%) higher, and is about $121,500 (89%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Mills River's median rent is $956 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $148 (18%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $106 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $111 (13%) higher.
