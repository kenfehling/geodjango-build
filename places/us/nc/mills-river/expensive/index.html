In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mills River is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mills River is valued at $257,800. This is approximately $54,560 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $40,300 (18%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $121,500 (89%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Mills River's median rent is $956 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $148 (18%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $106 (9%) lower, and is approximately $111 (13%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.
