Compared to its surrounding areas, Mills River is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mills River is valued at $257,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $54,560 (26%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $40,300 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $121,500 (89%) higher.

Rents

Mills River's median monthly rent is $956. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $148 (18%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $106 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $111 (13%) higher.
