Home prices

The value of the median home in Denton is $95,600. It is roughly $24,780 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $121,900 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is about $124,700 (56%) lower.

Rents

Denton's median rent is $626 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $146 (18%) lower. It is about $436 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $509 (44%) lower.
