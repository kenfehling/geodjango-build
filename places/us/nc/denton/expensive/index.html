In comparison to the surrounding areas, Denton is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The median home value in Denton is $95,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $24,780 (20%) lower. It is roughly $121,900 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $124,700 (56%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Denton the median monthly rent is $626. It is approximately $146 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $436 (41%) lower, and is roughly $509 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
