Home prices

The median home in Faison costs $86,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $5,940 (6%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower, and is roughly $161,600 (65%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Faison is $533. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $159 (23%) lower. It is approximately $529 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is about $588 (52%) lower.
