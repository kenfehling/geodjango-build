In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cullowhee is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Cullowhee costs $230,300. It is roughly $27,780 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $12,800 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $94,000 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cullowhee is $658. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $66 (9%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $404 (38%) lower, and is approximately $187 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
