Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Cullowhee is $658 per month. It is roughly $66 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $404 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $187 (22%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
