The median home in Cullowhee costs $230,300. It is roughly $27,780 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $12,800 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $94,000 (68%) higher.

Rents

Cullowhee's median monthly rent is $658. This is roughly $66 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $404 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is about $187 (22%) lower.
