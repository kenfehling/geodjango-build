Compared to its nearby areas, Cullowhee is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

Cullowhee's median home cost is $230,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $27,780 (13%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $12,800 (5%) higher, and is approximately $94,000 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Cullowhee the median rent is $658 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $66 (9%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $404 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $187 (22%) lower.
