Compared to nearby areas, Weldon is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in Weldon costs $103,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $23,860 (30%) higher. It is roughly $114,500 (52%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is about $145,300 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Weldon is $649 per month. It is roughly $88 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $413 (38%) lower, and is approximately $472 (42%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
