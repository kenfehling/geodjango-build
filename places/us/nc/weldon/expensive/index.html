Home prices

The median home in Weldon is valued at $103,000. It is approximately $23,860 (30%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $114,500 (52%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $145,300 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Weldon is $649. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $88 (11%) lower.It is roughly $413 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $472 (42%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
