Home prices

The price of the median home in Clinton is $122,700. This is approximately $28,260 (29%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $94,800 (43%) lower, and it is about $125,600 (50%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Clinton the median rent is $664 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $60 (9%) higher. It is approximately $398 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is about $457 (40%) lower.
