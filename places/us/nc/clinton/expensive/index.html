Home prices

The median home in Clinton costs $122,700. This is about $28,260 (29%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $94,800 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is about $125,600 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Clinton the median monthly rent is $664. It is about $60 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $398 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $457 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
