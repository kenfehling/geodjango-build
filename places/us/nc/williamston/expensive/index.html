In comparison to its surrounding areas, Williamston is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Home prices

The median home price in Williamston is $96,000. This is roughly $13,460 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $121,500 (55%) lower, and it is about $184,800 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

In Williamston the median rent is $653 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $15 (2%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $409 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $714 (52%) lower.
