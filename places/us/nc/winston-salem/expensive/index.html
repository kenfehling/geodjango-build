In comparison to its nearby areas, Winston-Salem is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Charlotte however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Winston-Salem costs $147,900. It is about $32,800 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $69,600 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $72,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

Winston-Salem's median rent is $806 a month. This is approximately $71 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $256 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $329 (28%) lower.
