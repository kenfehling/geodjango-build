Compared to other places in this area, Winston-Salem is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Charlotte, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Winston-Salem is $147,900. It is approximately $32,800 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $69,600 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $72,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Winston-Salem is $806. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $71 (8%) lower. It is approximately $256 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $329 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
