Compared to the surrounding areas, Winston-Salem is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Charlotte, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Winston-Salem is $147,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $32,800 (18%) lower. It is about $69,600 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $72,400 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Winston-Salem the median monthly rent is $806. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $71 (8%) lower. It is roughly $256 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $329 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
