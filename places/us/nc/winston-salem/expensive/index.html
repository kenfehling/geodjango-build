Compared to its nearby areas, Winston-Salem is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

Winston-Salem's median home price is $147,900. It is approximately $32,800 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $69,600 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $72,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Winston-Salem the median monthly rent is $806. This is roughly $71 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $256 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $329 (28%) lower.
