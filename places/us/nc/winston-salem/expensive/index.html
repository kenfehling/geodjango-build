In comparison to the surrounding areas, Winston-Salem is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Charlotte.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Winston-Salem is $147,900. This is roughly $32,800 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $69,600 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $72,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Winston-Salem the median monthly rent is $806. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $71 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $256 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $329 (28%) lower.
