Home prices

The median home in Dillsboro costs $182,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $27,640 (13%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $35,100 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $46,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Dillsboro the median monthly rent is $730. Compared to nearby areas this is about $67 (10%) higher. It is approximately $332 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $115 (13%) lower.
