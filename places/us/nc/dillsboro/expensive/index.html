In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dillsboro is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Dillsboro's median home value is $182,400. It is about $27,640 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $35,100 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $46,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Dillsboro the median rent is $730 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $67 (10%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $332 (31%) lower, and is roughly $115 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
