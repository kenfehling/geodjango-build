Home prices

Dillsboro's median home price is $182,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $27,640 (13%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $35,100 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is roughly $46,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Dillsboro the median rent is $730 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $67 (10%) higher. It is approximately $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $115 (13%) lower.
