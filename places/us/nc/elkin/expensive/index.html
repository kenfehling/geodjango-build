Home prices

The price of the median home in Elkin is $137,800. This is about $24,200 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $79,700 (36%) lower, and is roughly $82,500 (37%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents
