In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kenly is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in Kenly costs $83,900. This is about $11,800 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $133,600 (61%) lower, and it is roughly $164,400 (66%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in Kenly is $687 a month. This is approximately $74 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $375 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $434 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
