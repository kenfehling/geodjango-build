Home prices

The median home price in Apex is $327,800. This is roughly $55,680 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $110,300 (50%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $79,500 (32%) higher compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Apex's median rent is $1,380 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $134 (10%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $318 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $259 (23%) higher.
