Home prices

The median home in Apex is valued at $327,800. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $55,680 (20%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $110,300 (50%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh is about $79,500 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Apex the median monthly rent is $1,380. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $134 (10%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $318 (29%) higher, and is about $259 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
