In comparison to the surrounding areas, Apex is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Raleigh, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Apex is valued at $327,800. This is about $55,680 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $110,300 (50%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $79,500 (32%) higher.

Rents

Apex's median monthly rent is $1,380. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $134 (10%) higher. It is approximately $318 (29%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is roughly $259 (23%) higher.
