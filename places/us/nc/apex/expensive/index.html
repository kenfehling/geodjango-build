Home prices

The cost of the median home in Apex is $327,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $55,680 (20%) higher.It is approximately $110,300 (50%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $79,500 (32%) higher compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Apex is $1,380. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $134 (10%) higher.It is approximately $318 (29%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $259 (23%) higher.
