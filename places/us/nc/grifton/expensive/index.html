Home prices

The median home in Grifton costs $83,800. This is about $21,640 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $133,700 (61%) lower, and it is roughly $164,500 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Grifton the median monthly rent is $607. It is roughly $111 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $455 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $514 (45%) lower.
