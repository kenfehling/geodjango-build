Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clayton is $192,900. It is roughly $14,820 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $24,600 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $55,400 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Clayton's median rent is $1,083 per month. This is about $73 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $21 (1%) higher, and it is approximately $38 (3%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
