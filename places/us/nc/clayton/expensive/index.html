Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clayton is $192,900. This is about $14,820 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $24,600 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $55,400 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Clayton is $1,083 per month. It is about $73 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $21 (1%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $38 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
