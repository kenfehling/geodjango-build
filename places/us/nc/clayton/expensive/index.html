Home prices

The median home in Clayton is valued at $192,900. This is roughly $14,820 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $24,600 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $55,400 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Clayton the median rent is $1,083 a month. This is approximately $73 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $21 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $38 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
