Home prices

The median home in Lansing costs $75,000. This is approximately $55,280 (42%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $142,500 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $72,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Winston-Salem.

Rents

Lansing's median monthly rent is $631. It is about $61 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $431 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $175 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Winston-Salem.
