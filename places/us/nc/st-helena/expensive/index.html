Home prices

The median home in St. Helena is valued at $178,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,900 (9%) higher.It is about $39,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville it is about $47,400 (36%) higher.

Rents

In St. Helena the median rent is $725 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $36 (4%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $337 (31%) lower, and is roughly $222 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Fayetteville.
