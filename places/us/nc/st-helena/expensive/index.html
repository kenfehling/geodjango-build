Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Helena is $178,400. It is about $15,900 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $39,100 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is about $47,400 (36%) higher.

Rents

In St. Helena the median rent is $725 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $36 (4%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Fayetteville it is about $222 (23%) lower.
