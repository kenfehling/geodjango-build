Compared to other places in this area, St. Helena is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

St. Helena's median home price is $178,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $15,900 (9%) higher. It is roughly $39,100 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $47,400 (36%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

St. Helena's median rent is $725 a month. It is about $36 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fayetteville is roughly $222 (23%) lower.
