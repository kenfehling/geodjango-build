Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Helena is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Fayetteville, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

St. Helena's median home cost is $178,400. It is approximately $15,900 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $39,100 (17%) lower, and is about $47,400 (36%) higher compared to the city of Fayetteville.

Rents

St. Helena's median rent is $725 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $36 (4%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fayetteville is about $222 (23%) lower.
