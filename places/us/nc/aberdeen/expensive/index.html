Home prices

The median home in Aberdeen is valued at $202,100. This is about $5,920 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $15,400 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $18,200 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Aberdeen the median rent is $815 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $74 (8%) lower.It is roughly $247 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $320 (28%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
