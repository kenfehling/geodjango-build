Compared to its surrounding areas, Sylva is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Knoxville, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sylva is $144,400. This is about $73,240 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $73,100 (33%) lower, and is approximately $8,100 (5%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Sylva's median rent is $697 a month. It is approximately $28 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is about $148 (17%) lower.
