In comparison to other places in this area, Hope Mills is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Hope Mills is $134,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $13,980 (11%) higher. It is approximately $82,600 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $113,400 (45%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Hope Mills the median rent is $928 a month. This is approximately $64 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $134 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is about $193 (17%) lower.
