Compared to its nearby areas, Hope Mills is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Hope Mills's median home value is $134,900. It is about $13,980 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $82,600 (37%) lower, and it is about $113,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hope Mills is $928. This is approximately $64 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $134 (12%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $193 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
