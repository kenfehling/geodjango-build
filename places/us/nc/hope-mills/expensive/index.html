Compared to nearby areas, Hope Mills is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hope Mills is $134,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $13,980 (11%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $82,600 (37%) lower, and is roughly $113,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Hope Mills the median rent is $928 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $64 (7%) higher. It is approximately $134 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $193 (17%) lower.
