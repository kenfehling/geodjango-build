In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hope Mills is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hope Mills is $134,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $13,980 (11%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $82,600 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $113,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Hope Mills the median monthly rent is $928. This is about $64 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $134 (12%) lower, and is approximately $193 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
