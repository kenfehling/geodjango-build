Home prices

The median home in Taylortown is valued at $144,600. This is about $142,820 (49%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $72,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $75,700 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Taylortown is $499. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $666 (57%) lower. It is approximately $563 (53%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $636 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.
