Home prices

The median home in Lowesville is valued at $262,300. This is roughly $18,540 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $44,800 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $42,000 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Lowesville the median monthly rent is $1,213. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $45 (3%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $151 (14%) higher, and is about $78 (6%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.
