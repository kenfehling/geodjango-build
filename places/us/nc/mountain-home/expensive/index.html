Home prices

Rents

Mountain Home's median monthly rent is $781. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $46 (5%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $281 (26%) lower, and is about $262 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Asheville.
