Home prices

The price of the median home in Thomasville is $116,800. This is roughly $30,500 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $100,700 (46%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $103,500 (46%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

The median rent in Thomasville is $693 per month. It is approximately $66 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $369 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is approximately $442 (38%) lower.
