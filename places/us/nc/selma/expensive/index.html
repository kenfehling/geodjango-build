In comparison to other places in this area, Selma is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The median home in Selma is valued at $105,200. This is about $8,740 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $112,300 (51%) lower, and is approximately $143,100 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Selma's median rent is $820 a month. This is roughly $43 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $301 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
