Home prices

The median home in Elizabeth City is valued at $137,800. This is about $68,660 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $79,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is approximately $143,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elizabeth City is $881. It is approximately $95 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $181 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach is roughly $486 (35%) lower.
