Home prices

Rents

Enfield's median monthly rent is $724. It is approximately $83 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $338 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $397 (35%) lower.
