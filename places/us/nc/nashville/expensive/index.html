In comparison to the surrounding areas, Nashville is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Nashville is $136,900. It is approximately $29,840 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $80,600 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $111,400 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Nashville the median rent is $865 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $115 (15%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $197 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $256 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
