Compared to nearby areas, Nashville is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Nashville is $136,900. It is approximately $29,840 (27%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $80,600 (37%) lower, and it is about $111,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in Nashville is $865 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $115 (15%) higher.It is roughly $197 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $256 (22%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
