Rents

In Roxboro the median rent is $653 per month. This is roughly $88 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $409 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $468 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
