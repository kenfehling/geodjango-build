Home prices

The cost of the median home in Louisburg is $161,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $16,080 (11%) higher. It is approximately $56,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $87,200 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Louisburg is $586 a month. This is about $285 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $476 (44%) lower, and is about $535 (47%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
