Compared to the surrounding areas, Manteo is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Virginia Beach however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Manteo costs $293,900. This is approximately $35,440 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $76,400 (35%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach is about $13,100 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Manteo the median rent is $1,093 per month. This is roughly $58 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $31 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $274 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.
