Compared to other places in this area, Bryson City is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home in Bryson City is valued at $167,500. It is approximately $29,700 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $50,000 (22%) lower, and is about $31,200 (22%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

In Bryson City the median rent is $702 a month. It is about $47 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $360 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $143 (16%) lower.
