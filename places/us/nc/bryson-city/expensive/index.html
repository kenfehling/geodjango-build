In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bryson City is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Knoxville however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bryson City costs $167,500. It is approximately $29,700 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $50,000 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $31,200 (22%) higher compared to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

Bryson City's median rent is $702 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $47 (7%) higher. It is roughly $360 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $143 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.
