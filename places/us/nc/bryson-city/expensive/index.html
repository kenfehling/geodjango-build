In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bryson City is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The median home price in Bryson City is $167,500. This is about $29,700 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $50,000 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Knoxville is about $31,200 (22%) higher.

Rents

Bryson City's median monthly rent is $702. It is roughly $47 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $360 (33%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville it is about $143 (16%) lower.
