In comparison to nearby areas, Durham is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Durham is $224,100. This is roughly $75,540 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $6,600 (3%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $24,200 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median rent in Durham is $1,058 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $65 (5%) lower.It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $63 (5%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
