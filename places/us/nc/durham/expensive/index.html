Home prices

The value of the median home in Durham is $224,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $75,540 (25%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $6,600 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $24,200 (9%) lower.

Rents

Durham's median rent is $1,058 per month. It is roughly $65 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is is very similar compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is approximately $63 (5%) lower.
