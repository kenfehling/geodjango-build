Compared to its nearby areas, Durham is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Durham costs $224,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $75,540 (25%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $6,600 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $24,200 (9%) lower.

Rents

Durham's median rent is $1,058 a month. It is roughly $65 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh is roughly $63 (5%) lower.
