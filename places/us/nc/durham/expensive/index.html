In comparison to the surrounding areas, Durham is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $224,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $75,540 (25%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $6,600 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is about $24,200 (9%) lower.

Rents

Durham's median monthly rent is $1,058. It is roughly $65 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Raleigh is approximately $63 (5%) lower.
