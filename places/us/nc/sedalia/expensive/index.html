In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sedalia is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sedalia is valued at $146,900. This is roughly $67,460 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $70,600 (32%) lower, and is roughly $101,400 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sedalia is $870. It is approximately $32 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $192 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $251 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
