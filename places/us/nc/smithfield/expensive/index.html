Compared to the surrounding areas, Smithfield is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Smithfield is valued at $142,200. It is roughly $22,060 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $75,300 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $106,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Smithfield is $688 per month. It is approximately $143 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $433 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
