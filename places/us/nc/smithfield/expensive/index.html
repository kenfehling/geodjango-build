In comparison to the surrounding areas, Smithfield is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Smithfield costs $142,200. It is roughly $22,060 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $75,300 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $106,100 (42%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Smithfield's median rent is $688 a month. It is about $143 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $374 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $433 (38%) lower.
