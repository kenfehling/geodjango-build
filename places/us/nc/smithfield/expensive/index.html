Home prices

The median home cost in Smithfield is $142,200. It is roughly $22,060 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $75,300 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $106,100 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Smithfield the median rent is $688 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $143 (17%) lower. It is roughly $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $433 (38%) lower.
