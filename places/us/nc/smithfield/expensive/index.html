Home prices

The median home in Smithfield costs $142,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $22,060 (18%) higher.It is roughly $75,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh it is about $106,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Smithfield the median rent is $688 a month. This is approximately $143 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $374 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $433 (38%) lower.
