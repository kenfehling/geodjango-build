Rents

In Fuquay-Varina the median monthly rent is $1,160. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $42 (3%) higher.It is approximately $98 (9%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $39 (3%) higher.
