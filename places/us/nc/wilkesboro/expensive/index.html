Home prices

The median home cost in Wilkesboro is $176,900. It is approximately $57,400 (48%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $40,600 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $43,400 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wilkesboro is $622. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $69 (10%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $440 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is about $513 (45%) lower.
