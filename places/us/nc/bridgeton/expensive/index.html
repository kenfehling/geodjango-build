The median home in Bridgeton is valued at $141,300. It is roughly $62,720 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $76,200 (35%) lower, and is roughly $105,100 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bridgeton is $1,042. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $107 (9%) lower. It is roughly $20 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
