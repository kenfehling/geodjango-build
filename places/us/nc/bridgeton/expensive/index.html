Home prices

Bridgeton's median home value is $141,300. This is about $62,720 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $76,200 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Wilmington is roughly $105,100 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Bridgeton the median monthly rent is $1,042. It is roughly $107 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $20 (1%) lower, and it is about $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Wilmington.
