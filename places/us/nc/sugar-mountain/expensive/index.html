Home prices

The median home cost in Sugar Mountain is $232,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $17,520 (7%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $14,800 (6%) higher, and it is roughly $12,000 (5%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Sugar Mountain's median monthly rent is $963. It is approximately $121 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $99 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is roughly $172 (15%) lower.
