Home prices

The median home in Fallston is valued at $110,600. This is roughly $7,160 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $106,900 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $109,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Fallston's median rent is $494 per month. It is approximately $245 (33%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $568 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $641 (56%) lower.
