Home prices

The value of the median home in Boone is $268,100. It is roughly $30,300 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $50,600 (23%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $47,800 (21%) higher compared to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

Boone's median monthly rent is $900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $69 (8%) higher. It is approximately $162 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Charlotte it is approximately $235 (20%) lower.
