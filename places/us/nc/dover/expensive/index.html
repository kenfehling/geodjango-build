Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dover is $76,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $14,640 (16%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $141,000 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is about $171,800 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Dover the median rent is $713 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $64 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $349 (32%) lower, and is about $408 (36%) lower compared to the city of Raleigh.
