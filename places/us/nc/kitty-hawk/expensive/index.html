Home prices

The median home in Kitty Hawk costs $348,900. It is roughly $36,220 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $131,400 (60%) higher, and it is approximately $68,100 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kitty Hawk is $1,282. This is about $40 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $220 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is about $85 (6%) lower.
