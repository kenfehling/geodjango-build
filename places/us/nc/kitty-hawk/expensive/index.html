Home prices

The value of the median home in Kitty Hawk is $348,900. It is approximately $36,220 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $131,400 (60%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $68,100 (24%) higher compared to the city of Virginia Beach.

Rents

Kitty Hawk's median monthly rent is $1,282. It is approximately $40 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $220 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Virginia Beach it is roughly $85 (6%) lower.
