Home prices

The cost of the median home in Troy is $103,800. Compared to nearby areas it is about $14,360 (16%) higher. It is approximately $113,700 (52%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte is about $116,500 (52%) lower.

Rents

Troy's median rent is $475 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $166 (25%) lower. It is approximately $587 (55%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Charlotte is about $660 (58%) lower.
