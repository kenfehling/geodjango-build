Home prices

The median home in Cedar Point is valued at $322,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $90,860 (39%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $105,100 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington is approximately $76,200 (30%) higher.

Rents

Cedar Point's median rent is $1,219 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17 (1%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $157 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Wilmington is about $221 (22%) higher.
