Home prices

The median home cost in Cedar Point is $322,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $90,860 (39%) higher.It is about $105,100 (48%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is about $76,200 (30%) higher.

Rents

Cedar Point's median rent is $1,219 per month. This is about $17 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $157 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $221 (22%) higher compared to the city of Wilmington.
