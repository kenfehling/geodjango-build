In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cedar Point is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Wilmington, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cedar Point is $322,600. It is approximately $90,860 (39%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $105,100 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington is about $76,200 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cedar Point is $1,219 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $17 (1%) higher.It is about $157 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington is roughly $221 (22%) higher.
