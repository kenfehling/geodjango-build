In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cedar Point is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Wilmington however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Cedar Point's median home cost is $322,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $90,860 (39%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $105,100 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington is about $76,200 (30%) higher.

Rents

Cedar Point's median monthly rent is $1,219. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $17 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $157 (14%) higher, and it is about $221 (22%) higher compared to the city of Wilmington.
