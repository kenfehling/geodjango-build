In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wake Forest is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wake Forest is $297,300. This is about $109,240 (58%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $79,800 (36%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $49,000 (19%) higher.

Rents

Wake Forest's median monthly rent is $1,117. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $250 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $55 (5%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
