In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wake Forest is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Raleigh, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wake Forest is $297,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $109,240 (58%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $79,800 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Raleigh is approximately $49,000 (19%) higher.

Rents

Wake Forest's median rent is $1,117 per month. It is about $250 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $55 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Raleigh.
