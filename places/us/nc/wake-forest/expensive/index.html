Compared to nearby areas, Wake Forest is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Raleigh.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wake Forest is $297,300. It is about $109,240 (58%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $79,800 (36%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $49,000 (19%) higher compared to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Wake Forest's median monthly rent is $1,117. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $250 (28%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $55 (5%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
