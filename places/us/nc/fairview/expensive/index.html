Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Fairview is $593 per month. This is about $479 (44%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $469 (44%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charlotte it is roughly $542 (47%) lower.
