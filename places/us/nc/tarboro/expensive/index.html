Home prices

The median home in Tarboro is valued at $112,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $39,940 (55%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $105,400 (48%) lower, and is roughly $136,200 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

In Tarboro the median monthly rent is $651. This is roughly $18 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $411 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Raleigh is about $470 (41%) lower.
