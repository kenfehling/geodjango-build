Home prices

The price of the median home in Liberty is $110,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $17,980 (13%) lower. It is roughly $107,000 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $109,800 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Charlotte.

Rents

In Liberty the median rent is $669 a month. This is about $136 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $393 (37%) lower, and it is about $466 (41%) lower compared to the city of Charlotte.
