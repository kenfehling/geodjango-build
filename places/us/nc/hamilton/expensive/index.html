Home prices

The median home in Hamilton is valued at $57,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $25,380 (30%) lower. It is roughly $159,800 (73%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh it is roughly $190,600 (76%) lower.

Rents

Hamilton's median monthly rent is $477. It is about $243 (33%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $585 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $644 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Raleigh.
