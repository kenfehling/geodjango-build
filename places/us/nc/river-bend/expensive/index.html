Home prices

The price of the median home in River Bend is $169,200. This is approximately $18,740 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $48,300 (22%) lower, and is roughly $77,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Wilmington.

Rents

In River Bend the median monthly rent is $905. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $93 (9%) lower. It is about $157 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Wilmington it is about $93 (9%) lower.
