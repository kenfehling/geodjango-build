In comparison to other places in this area, Hillsborough is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Raleigh, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsborough is valued at $261,200. It is roughly $18,680 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $43,700 (20%) higher, and it is roughly $12,900 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Raleigh.

Rents

Hillsborough's median rent is $1,025 a month. This is roughly $11 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $37 (3%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Raleigh is roughly $96 (8%) lower.
