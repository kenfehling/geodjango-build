Home prices

The median home cost in Bethlehem is $147,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $15,960 (12%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $70,100 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Manchester it is about $80,200 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Bethlehem the median monthly rent is $819. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21 (2%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is about $316 (27%) lower.
