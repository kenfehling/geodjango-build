Compared to its surrounding areas, Bethlehem is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Manchester, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bethlehem is valued at $147,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,960 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $70,100 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is approximately $80,200 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Bethlehem the median rent is $819 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $21 (2%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $243 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $316 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Manchester.
