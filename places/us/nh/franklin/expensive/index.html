Home prices

The median home cost in Franklin is $164,800. It is roughly $17,060 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $52,700 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $367,900 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Franklin's median rent is $847 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $68 (7%) lower.It is about $215 (20%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $773 (47%) lower.
