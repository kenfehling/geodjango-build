Home prices

The median home in Franklin costs $164,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $17,060 (9%) lower.It is about $52,700 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $367,900 (69%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Franklin the median rent is $847 per month. It is roughly $68 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $215 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $773 (47%) lower.
