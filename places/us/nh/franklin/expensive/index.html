Home prices

The value of the median home in Franklin is $164,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $17,060 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $52,700 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $367,900 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Franklin is $847 per month. This is about $68 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $215 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $773 (47%) lower.
