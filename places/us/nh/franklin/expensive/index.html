Compared to nearby areas, Franklin is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Franklin's median home value is $164,800. It is about $17,060 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $52,700 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $367,900 (69%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Franklin is $847 a month. This is roughly $68 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $215 (20%) lower, and is roughly $773 (47%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
