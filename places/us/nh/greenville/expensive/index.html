Home prices

Greenville's median home price is $175,000. This is roughly $34,500 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $42,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $357,700 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Greenville's median rent is $992 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $63 (6%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $70 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $628 (38%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
