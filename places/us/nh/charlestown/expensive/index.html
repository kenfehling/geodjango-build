Home prices

The median home price in Charlestown is $130,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $22,420 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $87,400 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is about $97,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Charlestown is $969 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $123 (14%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $93 (8%) lower, and it is about $126 (11%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.
