Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ashland is $164,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $27,320 (14%) lower.It is roughly $52,600 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $62,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Manchester.

Rents

In Ashland the median monthly rent is $780. Compared to other places in this area this is about $201 (20%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Manchester it is approximately $355 (31%) lower.
