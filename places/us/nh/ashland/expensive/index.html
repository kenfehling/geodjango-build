Compared to its nearby areas, Ashland is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Manchester, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ashland is $164,900. This is about $27,320 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $52,600 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $62,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Manchester.

Rents

Ashland's median rent is $780 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $201 (20%) lower. It is about $282 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Manchester it is about $355 (31%) lower.
