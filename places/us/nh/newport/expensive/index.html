Compared to other places in this area, Newport is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home in Newport costs $138,600. This is roughly $57,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $78,900 (36%) lower, and is roughly $88,500 (38%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Newport's median monthly rent is $882. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $168 (16%) lower. It is approximately $180 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $213 (19%) lower.
