Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hampton is $372,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $10,740 (2%) lower. It is about $154,500 (71%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $160,700 (30%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Hampton's median rent is $1,179 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $61 (4%) lower. It is about $117 (11%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston is about $441 (27%) lower.
