Home prices

Hampton's median home value is $372,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $10,740 (2%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $154,500 (71%) higher, and is about $160,700 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Hampton's median rent is $1,179 a month. It is about $61 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $117 (11%) higher, and it is approximately $441 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
