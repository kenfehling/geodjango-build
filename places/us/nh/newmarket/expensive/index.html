Home prices

The median home in Newmarket costs $255,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $59,820 (18%) lower. It is roughly $37,900 (17%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $277,300 (52%) lower.

Rents

In Newmarket the median monthly rent is $1,278. This is approximately $127 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $216 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $342 (21%) lower.
