Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newmarket is $255,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $59,820 (18%) lower.It is approximately $37,900 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $277,300 (52%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Newmarket the median rent is $1,278 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $127 (11%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $216 (20%) higher, and is roughly $342 (21%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
