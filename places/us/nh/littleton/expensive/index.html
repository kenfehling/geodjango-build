In comparison to other places in this area, Littleton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Manchester, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Littleton is valued at $161,700. This is about $30,520 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $55,800 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is about $65,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Littleton is $702 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $114 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $360 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is approximately $433 (38%) lower.
