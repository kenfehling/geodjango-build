Home prices

The price of the median home in Goffstown is $237,400. This is about $15,100 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $19,900 (9%) higher, and it is approximately $295,300 (55%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Goffstown is $891. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $267 (23%) lower.It is about $171 (16%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $729 (44%) lower.
