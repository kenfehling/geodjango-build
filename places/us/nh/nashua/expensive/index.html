Home prices

The median home in Nashua costs $267,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $3,260 (1%) higher.It is roughly $50,400 (23%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $264,800 (49%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nashua is $1,281. It is approximately $110 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $219 (20%) higher, and is roughly $339 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
