Home prices

The median home cost in Nashua is $267,900. It is about $3,260 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $50,400 (23%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $264,800 (49%) lower.

Rents

Nashua's median monthly rent is $1,281. It is roughly $110 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $219 (20%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $339 (20%) lower.
