Home prices

The median home in Nashua costs $267,900. This is approximately $3,260 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $50,400 (23%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $264,800 (49%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Nashua's median monthly rent is $1,281. It is roughly $110 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $219 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $339 (20%) lower.
