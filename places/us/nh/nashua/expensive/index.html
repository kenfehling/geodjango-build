Home prices

Nashua's median home value is $267,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $3,260 (1%) higher.It is about $50,400 (23%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $264,800 (49%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Nashua's median rent is $1,281 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $110 (9%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $219 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $339 (20%) lower.
