Home prices

The median home cost in Keene is $190,500. This is roughly $9,600 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $27,000 (12%) lower, and is about $342,200 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Keene's median rent is $983 a month. It is about $38 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $637 (39%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
