Home prices

The median home in Keene costs $190,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $9,600 (4%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $27,000 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $342,200 (64%) lower.

Rents

Keene's median rent is $983 a month. This is approximately $38 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $79 (7%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $637 (39%) lower.
