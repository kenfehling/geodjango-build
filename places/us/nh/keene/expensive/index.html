Home prices

The median home in Keene costs $190,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $9,600 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $27,000 (12%) lower, and is approximately $342,200 (64%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Keene's median monthly rent is $983. It is approximately $38 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $79 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $637 (39%) lower.
