Home prices

The price of the median home in Winchester is $112,800. It is roughly $56,840 (33%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $104,700 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $419,900 (78%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Winchester is $1,025 a month. This is approximately $163 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $37 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $595 (36%) lower.
