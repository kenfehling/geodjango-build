Home prices

The median home cost in Bristol is $163,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $23,660 (12%) lower. It is about $54,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is roughly $63,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bristol is $830. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $118 (12%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $232 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is approximately $265 (24%) lower.
