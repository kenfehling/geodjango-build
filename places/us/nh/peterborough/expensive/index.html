Home prices

The median home cost in Peterborough is $224,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $19,600 (9%) higher.It is approximately $7,200 (3%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $308,000 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Peterborough the median monthly rent is $870. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $88 (9%) lower.It is roughly $192 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $750 (46%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
