Home prices

The median home cost in Peterborough is $224,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $19,600 (9%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $7,200 (3%) higher, and it is roughly $308,000 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Peterborough's median rent is $870 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $88 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $192 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $750 (46%) lower.
