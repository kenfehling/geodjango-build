Home prices

The median home value in Hampton Beach is $342,900. It is roughly $27,780 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $125,400 (57%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $189,800 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Hampton Beach the median rent is $1,077 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $161 (13%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $15 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $543 (33%) lower.
