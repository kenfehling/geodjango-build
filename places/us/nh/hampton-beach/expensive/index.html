Home prices

Hampton Beach's median home value is $342,900. This is about $27,780 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $125,400 (57%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $189,800 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Hampton Beach the median rent is $1,077 per month. This is approximately $161 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $15 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $543 (33%) lower.
