In comparison to other places in this area, Marlborough is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home value in Marlborough is $182,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $14,340 (7%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $35,400 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $350,600 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Marlborough the median rent is $831 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $174 (17%) lower.It is roughly $231 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $789 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
