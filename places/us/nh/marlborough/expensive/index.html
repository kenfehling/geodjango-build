Compared to other places in this area, Marlborough is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Marlborough's median home cost is $182,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $14,340 (7%) lower. It is about $35,400 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $350,600 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Marlborough is $831 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $174 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $231 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $789 (48%) lower.
