In comparison to other places in this area, Rochester is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Rochester is $172,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $23,620 (12%) lower. It is approximately $45,200 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $360,400 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester is $1,041. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $65 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $21 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $579 (35%) lower.
