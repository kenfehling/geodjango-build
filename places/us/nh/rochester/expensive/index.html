In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rochester is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rochester is valued at $172,300. This is roughly $23,620 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $45,200 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $360,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Rochester the median rent is $1,041 a month. This is about $65 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $21 (1%) lower, and it is about $579 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
