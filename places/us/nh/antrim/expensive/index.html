Home prices

The median home price in Antrim is $181,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $41,800 (18%) lower. It is approximately $35,600 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $350,800 (65%) lower.

Rents

Antrim's median rent is $957 per month. This is roughly $27 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $105 (9%) lower, and it is about $663 (40%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
