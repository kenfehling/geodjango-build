Home prices

The median home in Newfields is valued at $366,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $73,140 (24%) higher.It is approximately $148,700 (68%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $166,500 (31%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newfields is $1,042. It is roughly $156 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $20 (1%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $578 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
