Rents

Meredith's median monthly rent is $1,011. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $123 (10%) lower. It is about $51 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $1,210 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Cambridge.
