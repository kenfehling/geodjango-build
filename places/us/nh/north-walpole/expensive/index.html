Home prices

Rents

The median rent in North Walpole is $856 a month. This is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $206 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is roughly $239 (21%) lower.
