In comparison to other places in this area, Hinsdale is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home in Hinsdale costs $152,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $20,700 (11%) lower. It is about $65,100 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $74,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Hinsdale the median monthly rent is $722. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $182 (20%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $340 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $373 (34%) lower.
