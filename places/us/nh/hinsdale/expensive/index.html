Compared to its surrounding areas, Hinsdale is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Worcester, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Hinsdale's median home cost is $152,400. This is about $20,700 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $65,100 (29%) lower, and is approximately $74,700 (32%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Hinsdale's median monthly rent is $722. It is approximately $182 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $340 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $373 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
