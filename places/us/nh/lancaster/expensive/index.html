Home prices

The price of the median home in Lancaster is $126,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $16,460 (14%) higher. It is approximately $90,600 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Concord it is approximately $103,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Lancaster the median rent is $782 a month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is about $280 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $301 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Concord.
