Home prices

The median home cost in Lancaster is $126,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $16,460 (14%) higher. It is approximately $90,600 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $103,300 (44%) lower compared to the city of Concord.

Rents

The median rent in Lancaster is $782 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $280 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $301 (27%) lower compared to the city of Concord.
