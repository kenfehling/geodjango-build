Compared to other places in this area, Portsmouth is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home price in Portsmouth is $425,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $101,700 (31%) higher.It is roughly $208,100 (95%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $107,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Portsmouth is $1,305 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $169 (14%) higher.It is roughly $243 (22%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston is about $315 (19%) lower.
