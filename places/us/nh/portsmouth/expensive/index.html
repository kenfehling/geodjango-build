Compared to its nearby areas, Portsmouth is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Portsmouth costs $425,600. This is about $101,700 (31%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $208,100 (95%) higher, and it is approximately $107,100 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Portsmouth the median rent is $1,305 per month. It is about $169 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $243 (22%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $315 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
