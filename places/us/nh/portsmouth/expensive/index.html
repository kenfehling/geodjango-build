Compared to other places in this area, Portsmouth is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Bostonhowever, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Portsmouth is $425,600. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $101,700 (31%) higher. It is roughly $208,100 (95%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $107,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Portsmouth the median rent is $1,305 a month. This is roughly $169 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $243 (22%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $315 (19%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
