Home prices

The median home in Portsmouth costs $425,600. It is approximately $101,700 (31%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $208,100 (95%) higher, and is approximately $107,100 (20%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Portsmouth the median monthly rent is $1,305. It is roughly $169 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $243 (22%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is about $315 (19%) lower.
