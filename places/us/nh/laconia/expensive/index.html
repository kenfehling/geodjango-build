Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laconia is $962. It is roughly $47 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $100 (9%) lower, and is roughly $658 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
