Home prices

The median home in Laconia costs $193,300. This is about $13,300 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $24,200 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $339,400 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Laconia's median rent is $962 a month. It is roughly $47 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $100 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $658 (40%) lower.
