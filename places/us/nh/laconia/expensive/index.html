Home prices

Rents

Laconia's median rent is $962 per month. It is approximately $47 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $100 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $658 (40%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
