The median home in Manchester is valued at $227,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $5,320 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $10,100 (4%) higher, and is roughly $305,100 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Manchester the median rent is $1,135 per month. It is roughly $78 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $73 (6%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $485 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
