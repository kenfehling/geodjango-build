Manchester's median home cost is $227,600. This is about $5,320 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $10,100 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $305,100 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Manchester is $1,135. It is roughly $78 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $73 (6%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $485 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
