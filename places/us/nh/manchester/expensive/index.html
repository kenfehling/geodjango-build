Home prices

The median home in Manchester is valued at $227,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $5,320 (2%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $10,100 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $305,100 (57%) lower.

Rents

Manchester's median rent is $1,135 per month. It is approximately $78 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $73 (6%) higher, and it is roughly $485 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
