Compared to nearby areas, Woodsville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Manchester.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Woodsville is $107,100. It is about $40,080 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $110,400 (50%) lower, and it is about $120,500 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Manchester.

Rents

Woodsville's median monthly rent is $945. It is about $181 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $117 (11%) lower, and it is about $190 (16%) lower compared to the city of Manchester.
