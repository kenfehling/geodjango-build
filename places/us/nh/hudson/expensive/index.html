Home prices

The median home in Hudson is valued at $237,000. It is about $33,820 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $19,500 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $295,700 (55%) lower.

Rents

Hudson's median rent is $1,177 a month. This is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $115 (10%) higher, and is roughly $443 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
