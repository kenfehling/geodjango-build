Home prices

The median home value in Hudson is $237,000. This is about $33,820 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $19,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $295,700 (55%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hudson is $1,177. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $15 (1%) lower.It is roughly $115 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $443 (27%) lower.
