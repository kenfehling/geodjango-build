Home prices

The median home in Hudson is valued at $237,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $33,820 (12%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $19,500 (8%) higher, and it is roughly $295,700 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Hudson the median rent is $1,177 per month. This is about $15 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $115 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $443 (27%) lower.
