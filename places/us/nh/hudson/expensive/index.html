Home prices

The median home in Hudson is valued at $237,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $33,820 (12%) lower.It is roughly $19,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $295,700 (55%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hudson is $1,177. This is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $115 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $443 (27%) lower.
