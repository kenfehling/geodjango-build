Home prices

The value of the median home in Hudson is $237,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $33,820 (12%) lower. It is approximately $19,500 (8%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $295,700 (55%) lower.

Rents

Hudson's median rent is $1,177 per month. This is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $115 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston is about $443 (27%) lower.
