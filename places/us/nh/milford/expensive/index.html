In comparison to the surrounding areas, Milford is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Milford's median home cost is $233,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $25,600 (12%) higher.It is roughly $16,300 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $298,900 (56%) lower.

Rents

Milford's median rent is $1,198 per month. This is about $134 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $136 (12%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $422 (26%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
