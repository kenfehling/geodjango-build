Home prices

The cost of the median home in Milford is $233,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $25,600 (12%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $16,300 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $298,900 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milford is $1,198. This is approximately $134 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $136 (12%) higher, and is approximately $422 (26%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
