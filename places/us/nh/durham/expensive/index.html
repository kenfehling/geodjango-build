In comparison to other places in this area, Durham is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Bostonhowever, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $348,500. This is approximately $68,580 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $131,000 (60%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $184,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Durham the median rent is $942 per month. It is approximately $154 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $120 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $678 (41%) lower.
