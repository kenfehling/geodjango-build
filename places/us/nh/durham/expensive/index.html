Compared to the surrounding areas, Durham is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $348,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $68,580 (24%) higher.It is approximately $131,000 (60%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $184,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Durham the median rent is $942 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $154 (14%) lower.It is approximately $120 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $678 (41%) lower.
