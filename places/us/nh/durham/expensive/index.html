In comparison to its surrounding areas, Durham is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $348,500. This is about $68,580 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $131,000 (60%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $184,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Durham the median monthly rent is $942. This is roughly $154 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $120 (11%) lower, and it is roughly $678 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
