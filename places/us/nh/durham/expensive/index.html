Compared to its nearby areas, Durham is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $348,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $68,580 (24%) higher. It is about $131,000 (60%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $184,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

Durham's median monthly rent is $942. It is approximately $154 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $120 (11%) lower, and is roughly $678 (41%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
