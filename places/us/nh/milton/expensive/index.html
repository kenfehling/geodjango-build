Home prices

The median home in Milton costs $159,700. It is about $14,380 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $57,800 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $373,000 (70%) lower.

Rents

In Milton the median rent is $686 per month. This is about $248 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $376 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is approximately $934 (57%) lower.
