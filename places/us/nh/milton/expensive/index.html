Compared to its surrounding areas, Milton is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Milton's median home cost is $159,700. It is about $14,380 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $57,800 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $373,000 (70%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Milton is $686 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $248 (26%) lower. It is approximately $376 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $934 (57%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
