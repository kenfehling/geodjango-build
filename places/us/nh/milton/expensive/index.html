Compared to the surrounding areas, Milton is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Milton's median home price is $159,700. This is roughly $14,380 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $57,800 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $373,000 (70%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Milton's median monthly rent is $686. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $248 (26%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $376 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $934 (57%) lower.
