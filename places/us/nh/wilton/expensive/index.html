Compared to the surrounding areas, Wilton is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wilton is $190,900. This is about $17,240 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $26,600 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $341,800 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wilton is $963. It is about $66 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $99 (9%) lower, and is about $657 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
