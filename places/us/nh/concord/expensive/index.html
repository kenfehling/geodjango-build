Compared to its surrounding areas, Concord is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Concord is valued at $230,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $20,360 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $12,700 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $302,500 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Concord the median monthly rent is $1,083. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $181 (20%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $21 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $537 (33%) lower.
