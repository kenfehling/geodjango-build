Home prices

The median home in Concord is valued at $230,200. This is approximately $20,360 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $12,700 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $302,500 (56%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Concord is $1,083. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $181 (20%) higher.It is roughly $21 (1%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $537 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
