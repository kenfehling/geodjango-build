Home prices

Concord's median home value is $230,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $20,360 (9%) higher.It is about $12,700 (5%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $302,500 (56%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Concord is $1,083 a month. This is roughly $181 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $21 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $537 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
