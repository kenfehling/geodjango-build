In comparison to its surrounding areas, Concord is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Concord is $230,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $20,360 (9%) higher. It is about $12,700 (5%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $302,500 (56%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Concord's median monthly rent is $1,083. It is roughly $181 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $21 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $537 (33%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
