Compared to its surrounding areas, Loudon is a lot more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Loudon's median home cost is $240,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $60,800 (33%) higher. It is about $23,300 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $291,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Loudon is $518 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $397 (43%) lower. It is about $544 (51%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $1,102 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
