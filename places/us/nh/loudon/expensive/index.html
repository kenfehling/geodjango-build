Home prices

The price of the median home in Loudon is $240,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $60,800 (33%) higher.It is approximately $23,300 (10%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $291,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Loudon is $518 a month. It is roughly $397 (43%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $544 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $1,102 (68%) lower.
