Home prices

The median home in Hanover costs $603,700. Compared to nearby areas this is about $341,480 (130%) higher. It is approximately $386,200 (177%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Manchester it is approximately $376,100 (165%) higher.

Rents

Hanover's median rent is $1,415 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $272 (23%) higher. It is approximately $353 (33%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $280 (24%) higher compared to the city of Manchester.
