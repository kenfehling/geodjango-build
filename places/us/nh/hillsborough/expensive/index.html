Compared to nearby areas, Hillsborough is significantly less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home price in Hillsborough is $153,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $72,540 (32%) lower. It is about $64,400 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $379,600 (71%) lower.

Rents

Hillsborough's median rent is $1,015 a month. It is approximately $79 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $47 (4%) lower, and it is approximately $605 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
