Home prices

Hillsborough's median home price is $153,100. It is about $72,540 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $64,400 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $379,600 (71%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hillsborough is $1,015. This is roughly $79 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $47 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $605 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
