The median home in Hillsborough is valued at $153,100. It is approximately $72,540 (32%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $64,400 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $379,600 (71%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Hillsborough the median monthly rent is $1,015. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $79 (8%) higher. It is roughly $47 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $605 (37%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
