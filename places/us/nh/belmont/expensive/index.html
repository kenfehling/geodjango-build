Home prices

The median home price in Belmont is $157,900. This is approximately $27,640 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $59,600 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $374,800 (70%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Belmont's median monthly rent is $994. It is approximately $187 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $68 (6%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $626 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
