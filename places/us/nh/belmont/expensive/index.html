Home prices

The median home price in Belmont is $157,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $27,640 (14%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $59,600 (27%) lower, and is about $374,800 (70%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Belmont is $994 per month. It is about $187 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $68 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $626 (38%) lower.
