Compared to other places in this area, Pittsfield is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Pittsfield's median home price is $158,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $36,580 (18%) lower. It is about $58,600 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $373,800 (70%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Pittsfield's median monthly rent is $866. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $9 (1%) lower. It is roughly $196 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston is about $754 (46%) lower.
