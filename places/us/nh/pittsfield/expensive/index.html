Compared to its surrounding areas, Pittsfield is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pittsfield is $158,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $36,580 (18%) lower. It is approximately $58,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $373,800 (70%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Pittsfield's median rent is $866 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $9 (1%) lower. It is approximately $196 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $754 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
