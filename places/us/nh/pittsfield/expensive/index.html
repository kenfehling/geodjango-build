Compared to the surrounding areas, Pittsfield is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home cost in Pittsfield is $158,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $36,580 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $58,600 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $373,800 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pittsfield is $866. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $9 (1%) lower.It is about $196 (18%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $754 (46%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
