Compared to nearby areas, Waldoboro is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waldoboro costs $180,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $51,580 (22%) lower. It is roughly $37,200 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $108,700 (37%) lower.

Rents

Waldoboro's median rent is $342 a month. This is approximately $435 (56%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $720 (67%) lower, and it is approximately $782 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
