Home prices

The median home in Rockland is valued at $172,800. This is approximately $89,180 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $44,700 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $116,200 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rockland is $782 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $51 (7%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $280 (26%) lower, and is approximately $342 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
