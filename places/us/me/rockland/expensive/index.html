Compared to other places in this area, Rockland is a lot less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rockland is $172,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $89,180 (34%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $44,700 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $116,200 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rockland is $782 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $51 (7%) higher. It is about $280 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $342 (30%) lower.
