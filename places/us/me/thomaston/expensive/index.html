Home prices

The median home cost in Thomaston is $181,400. This is about $78,860 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $36,100 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $107,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Thomaston is $744 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $318 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $380 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
