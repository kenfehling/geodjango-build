Home prices

Farmingdale's median home cost is $158,000. This is approximately $2,260 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $59,500 (27%) lower, and is about $131,000 (45%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Farmingdale the median monthly rent is $759. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16 (2%) higher. It is roughly $303 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $365 (32%) lower.
