Home prices

The value of the median home in Farmingdale is $158,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $2,260 (1%) lower.It is approximately $59,500 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $131,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Farmingdale's median monthly rent is $759. It is about $16 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $303 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $365 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
