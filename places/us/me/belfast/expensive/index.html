In comparison to its surrounding areas, Belfast is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Belfast costs $190,500. This is about $82,980 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $27,000 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $98,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Belfast the median monthly rent is $888. This is about $165 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $174 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $236 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
