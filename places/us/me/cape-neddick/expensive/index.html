Compared to its surrounding areas, Cape Neddick is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Cape Neddick's median home price is $421,600. It is approximately $85,440 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $204,100 (93%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $111,100 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cape Neddick is $1,268 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $237 (22%) higher. It is about $206 (19%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $352 (21%) lower.
