Home prices

The median home value in Bath is $175,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $27,800 (13%) lower. It is about $41,800 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $113,300 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Bath the median rent is $862 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $70 (8%) higher. It is roughly $200 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $262 (23%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
