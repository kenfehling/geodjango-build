In comparison to other places in this area, Bath is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Bath's median home cost is $175,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $27,800 (13%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $41,800 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $113,300 (39%) lower.

Rents

Bath's median rent is $862 a month. It is about $70 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $200 (18%) lower, and is roughly $262 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
