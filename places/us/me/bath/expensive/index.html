In comparison to other places in this area, Bath is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bath costs $175,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $27,800 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $41,800 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $113,300 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bath is $862. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $70 (8%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $200 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $262 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
