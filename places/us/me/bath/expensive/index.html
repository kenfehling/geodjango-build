Compared to its nearby areas, Bath is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Bath costs $175,700. It is about $27,800 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $41,800 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $113,300 (39%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Bath is $862 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $70 (8%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $200 (18%) lower, and is approximately $262 (23%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
