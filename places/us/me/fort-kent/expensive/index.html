Home prices

The median home in Fort Kent costs $107,100. It is about $21,580 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $110,400 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Presque Isle it is roughly $8,300 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Kent is $661. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $172 (35%) higher. It is approximately $401 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Presque Isle is roughly $69 (11%) higher.
