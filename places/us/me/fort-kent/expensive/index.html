Compared to other places in this area, Fort Kent is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Presque Isle however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Fort Kent's median home cost is $107,100. This is about $21,580 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $110,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $8,300 (7%) lower compared to the city of Presque Isle.

Rents

In Fort Kent the median rent is $661 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $172 (35%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $401 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $69 (11%) higher compared to the city of Presque Isle.
