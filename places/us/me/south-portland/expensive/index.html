In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Portland is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Manchester.

Home prices

The median home in South Portland is valued at $257,200. It is roughly $44,500 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $39,700 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is about $29,600 (13%) higher.

Rents

In South Portland the median rent is $1,305 a month. This is about $176 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $243 (22%) higher, and it is approximately $170 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Manchester.
