In comparison to its surrounding areas, South Portland is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Manchester, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Portland is $257,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $44,500 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $39,700 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is roughly $29,600 (13%) higher.

Rents

In South Portland the median monthly rent is $1,305. It is about $176 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $243 (22%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is about $170 (14%) higher.
