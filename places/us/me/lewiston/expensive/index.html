In comparison to nearby areas, Lewiston is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lewiston is $140,600. This is approximately $30,800 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $76,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $148,400 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Lewiston's median monthly rent is $748. Compared to other places in this area this is about $126 (14%) lower. It is approximately $314 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $376 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
