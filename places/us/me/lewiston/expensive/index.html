Compared to other places in this area, Lewiston is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lewiston is $140,600. It is roughly $30,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $76,900 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $148,400 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Lewiston is $748 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $126 (14%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $314 (29%) lower, and it is about $376 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
