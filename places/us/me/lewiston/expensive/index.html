Home prices

The median home price in Lewiston is $140,600. It is roughly $30,800 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $76,900 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $148,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Lewiston the median rent is $748 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $126 (14%) lower.It is roughly $314 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $376 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
