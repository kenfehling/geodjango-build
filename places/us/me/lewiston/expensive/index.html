In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lewiston is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lewiston is $140,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $30,800 (17%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $76,900 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is about $148,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

Lewiston's median monthly rent is $748. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $126 (14%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $314 (29%) lower, and is about $376 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
