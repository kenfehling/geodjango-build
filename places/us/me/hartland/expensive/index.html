Compared to nearby areas, Hartland is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Hartland costs $78,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $33,800 (30%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $139,400 (64%) lower, and is roughly $210,900 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Hartland's median monthly rent is $709. This is roughly $17 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $353 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $415 (36%) lower.
