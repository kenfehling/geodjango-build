Compared to its nearby areas, Hallowell is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Portland, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hallowell costs $192,000. This is approximately $38,540 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $25,500 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $97,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hallowell is $863 per month. This is roughly $140 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $199 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is roughly $261 (23%) lower.
