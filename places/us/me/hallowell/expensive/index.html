In comparison to its nearby areas, Hallowell is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Hallowell is $192,000. It is approximately $38,540 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $25,500 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $97,000 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Hallowell is $863 per month. This is roughly $140 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $199 (18%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $261 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
