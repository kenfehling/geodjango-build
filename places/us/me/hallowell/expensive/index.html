Home prices

Hallowell's median home cost is $192,000. It is about $38,540 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $25,500 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $97,000 (33%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Hallowell the median monthly rent is $863. This is roughly $140 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $199 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $261 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
