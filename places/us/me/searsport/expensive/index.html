Home prices

The price of the median home in Searsport is $179,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $96,180 (34%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $38,000 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lewiston it is roughly $38,900 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Searsport the median rent is $507 a month. This is approximately $292 (36%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $555 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $241 (32%) lower compared to the city of Lewiston.
