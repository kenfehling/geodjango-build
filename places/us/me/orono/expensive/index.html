Home prices

The median home in Orono is valued at $172,800. This is roughly $14,480 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $44,700 (20%) lower, and is about $15,800 (10%) higher compared to the city of Bangor.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Orono is $907. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $86 (10%) higher. It is approximately $155 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Bangor it is approximately $91 (11%) higher.
