Home prices

The median home in Orono is valued at $172,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14,480 (9%) higher. It is roughly $44,700 (20%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bangor it is approximately $15,800 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Orono is $907 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $86 (10%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $155 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bangor is about $91 (11%) higher.
