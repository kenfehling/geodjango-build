Compared to its nearby areas, Camden is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Camden costs $350,500. This is approximately $100,740 (40%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $133,000 (61%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $61,500 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Camden is $941 per month. This is about $165 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $121 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $183 (16%) lower.
