Home prices

The median home in Yarmouth is valued at $329,600. This is roughly $3,600 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $112,100 (51%) higher, and it is about $40,600 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Yarmouth the median rent is $1,201 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $47 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $139 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $77 (6%) higher.
