Home prices

The median home in Yarmouth costs $329,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $3,600 (1%) higher. It is about $112,100 (51%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $40,600 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Yarmouth's median monthly rent is $1,201. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $47 (3%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $139 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $77 (6%) higher.
