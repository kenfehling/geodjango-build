Home prices

The median home value in Yarmouth is $329,600. This is about $3,600 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $112,100 (51%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $40,600 (14%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Yarmouth is $1,201. This is about $47 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $139 (13%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $77 (6%) higher.
