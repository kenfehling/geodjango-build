Home prices

The price of the median home in Mapleton is $135,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $46,800 (52%) higher. It is approximately $82,300 (37%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Presque Isle is approximately $19,800 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mapleton is $565 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $92 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $497 (46%) lower, and is roughly $27 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Presque Isle.
