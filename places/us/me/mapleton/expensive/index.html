Home prices

Mapleton's median home cost is $135,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $46,800 (52%) higher.It is approximately $82,300 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Presque Isle is approximately $19,800 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mapleton is $565. This is approximately $92 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $497 (46%) lower, and it is approximately $27 (4%) lower compared to the city of Presque Isle.
