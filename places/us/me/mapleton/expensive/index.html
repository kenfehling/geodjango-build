Home prices

The median home value in Mapleton is $135,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $46,800 (52%) higher. It is approximately $82,300 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Presque Isle it is roughly $19,800 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mapleton is $565 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $92 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $497 (46%) lower, and is about $27 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Presque Isle.
