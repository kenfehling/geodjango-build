Compared to the surrounding areas, Madison is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $76,700. This is about $32,860 (29%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $140,800 (64%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $212,300 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Madison is $709. Compared to its nearby areas it is is very similar. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $353 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $415 (36%) lower.
