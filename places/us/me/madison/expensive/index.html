Compared to the surrounding areas, Madison is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Madison is $76,700. This is about $32,860 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $140,800 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $212,300 (73%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Madison is $709 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $353 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $415 (36%) lower.
