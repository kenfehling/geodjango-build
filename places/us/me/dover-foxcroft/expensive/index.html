Home prices

The median home in Dover-Foxcroft is valued at $109,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $11,080 (11%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $107,900 (49%) lower, and is approximately $47,400 (30%) lower compared to the city of Bangor.

Rents
