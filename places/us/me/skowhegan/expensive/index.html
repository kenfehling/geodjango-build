Compared to its surrounding areas, Skowhegan is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home price in Skowhegan is $101,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $8,180 (8%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $116,300 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $187,800 (64%) lower.

Rents

Skowhegan's median rent is $803 a month. It is about $62 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $259 (24%) lower, and is approximately $321 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
