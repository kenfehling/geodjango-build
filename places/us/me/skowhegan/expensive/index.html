Home prices

The median home cost in Skowhegan is $101,200. This is roughly $8,180 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $116,300 (53%) lower, and it is approximately $187,800 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Skowhegan's median monthly rent is $803. It is about $62 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $259 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $321 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
