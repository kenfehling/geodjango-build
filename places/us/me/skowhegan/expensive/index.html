Home prices

The median home price in Skowhegan is $101,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $8,180 (8%) higher.It is approximately $116,300 (53%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $187,800 (64%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Skowhegan's median monthly rent is $803. This is approximately $62 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $259 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $321 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
