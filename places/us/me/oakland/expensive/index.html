In comparison to other places in this area, Oakland is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Portland, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $166,000. This is roughly $49,760 (42%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $51,500 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $123,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Oakland is $817 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $53 (6%) higher. It is about $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $307 (27%) lower.
