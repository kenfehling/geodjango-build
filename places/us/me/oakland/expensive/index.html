Compared to other places in this area, Oakland is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $166,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $49,760 (42%) higher.It is roughly $51,500 (23%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $123,000 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Oakland the median monthly rent is $817. This is approximately $53 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $245 (23%) lower, and it is about $307 (27%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
