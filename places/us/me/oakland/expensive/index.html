Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakland is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Portland, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Oakland is $166,000. It is about $49,760 (42%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $51,500 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $123,000 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $817 per month. This is about $53 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $307 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
