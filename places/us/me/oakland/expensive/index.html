Compared to its surrounding areas, Oakland is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $166,000. This is approximately $49,760 (42%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $51,500 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $123,000 (42%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Oakland's median monthly rent is $817. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $53 (6%) higher. It is roughly $245 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $307 (27%) lower.
