Compared to the surrounding areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Manchester however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Portland is $289,000. It is approximately $50,700 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $71,500 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is about $61,400 (26%) higher.

Rents

Portland's median rent is $1,124 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $189 (14%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $62 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Manchester is is very similar.
