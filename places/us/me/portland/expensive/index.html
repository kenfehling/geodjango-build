In comparison to other places in this area, Portland is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Manchester, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Portland's median home cost is $289,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $50,700 (14%) lower.It is approximately $71,500 (32%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is roughly $61,400 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $1,124. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $189 (14%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $62 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Manchester is is very similar.
