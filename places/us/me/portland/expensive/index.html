Compared to its surrounding areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Manchester, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Portland is $289,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $50,700 (14%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $71,500 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Manchester is about $61,400 (26%) higher.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $1,124. Compared to other places in this area it is about $189 (14%) lower. It is roughly $62 (5%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Manchester is is very similar.
