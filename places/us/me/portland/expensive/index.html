Compared to nearby areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Manchester.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Portland is $289,000. This is approximately $50,700 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $71,500 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Manchester is approximately $61,400 (26%) higher.

Rents

In Portland the median rent is $1,124 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $189 (14%) lower. It is roughly $62 (5%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Manchester.
