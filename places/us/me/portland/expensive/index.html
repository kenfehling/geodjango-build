In comparison to other places in this area, Portland is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Manchester, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Portland is $289,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $50,700 (14%) lower. It is about $71,500 (32%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is roughly $61,400 (26%) higher.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $1,124. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $189 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $62 (5%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Manchester.
