Compared to other places in this area, Portland is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Manchester however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $289,000. This is approximately $50,700 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $71,500 (32%) higher, and it is approximately $61,400 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Manchester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $1,124. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $189 (14%) lower.It is about $62 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is is very similar.
