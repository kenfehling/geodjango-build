Compared to other places in this area, Rumford is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Rumford's median home value is $78,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $13,760 (14%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $139,500 (64%) lower, and is roughly $211,000 (73%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Rumford is $570 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $11 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $492 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $554 (49%) lower.
