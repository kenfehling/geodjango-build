Home prices

Augusta's median home price is $144,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $18,100 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $72,700 (33%) lower, and is approximately $144,200 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Augusta's median rent is $721 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $30 (3%) lower. It is roughly $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $403 (35%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
