Compared to its nearby areas, Augusta is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Augusta costs $144,800. This is roughly $18,100 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $72,700 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $144,200 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Augusta is $721. This is roughly $30 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $403 (35%) lower.
