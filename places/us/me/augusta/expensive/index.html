Home prices

The median home in Augusta is valued at $144,800. It is about $18,100 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $72,700 (33%) lower, and is approximately $144,200 (49%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Augusta is $721. This is approximately $30 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $341 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is approximately $403 (35%) lower.
