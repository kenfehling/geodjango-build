In comparison to other places in this area, Augusta is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home value in Augusta is $144,800. This is roughly $18,100 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $72,700 (33%) lower, and it is about $144,200 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Augusta's median monthly rent is $721. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $30 (3%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $341 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $403 (35%) lower.
