Compared to the surrounding areas, Augusta is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Augusta is $144,800. This is approximately $18,100 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $72,700 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $144,200 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Augusta is $721. It is approximately $30 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $403 (35%) lower.
