The cost of the median home in Winthrop is $196,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $43,460 (28%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $21,400 (9%) lower, and it is roughly $92,900 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Winthrop's median monthly rent is $752. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is approximately $310 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $372 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
