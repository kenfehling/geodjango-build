In comparison to its surrounding areas, Newcastle is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Portlandhowever, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newcastle is $309,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $96,700 (45%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $91,900 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $20,400 (7%) higher.

Rents

Newcastle's median rent is $775 per month. This is about $108 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $349 (31%) lower.
