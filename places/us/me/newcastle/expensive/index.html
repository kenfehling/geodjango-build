Home prices

The median home in Newcastle costs $309,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $96,700 (45%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $91,900 (42%) higher, and it is roughly $20,400 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Newcastle's median monthly rent is $775. This is roughly $108 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is about $349 (31%) lower.
