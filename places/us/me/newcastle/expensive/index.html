Home prices

The median home in Newcastle is valued at $309,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $96,700 (45%) higher.It is approximately $91,900 (42%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is about $20,400 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Newcastle the median rent is $775 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $108 (16%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $287 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $349 (31%) lower.
