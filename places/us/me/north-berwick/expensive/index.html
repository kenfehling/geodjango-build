Home prices

North Berwick's median home price is $238,000. It is roughly $9,140 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $20,500 (9%) higher, and is about $294,700 (55%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In North Berwick the median monthly rent is $763. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $260 (25%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $299 (28%) lower, and it is about $857 (52%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
