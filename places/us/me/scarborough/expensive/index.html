Home prices

Scarborough's median home cost is $295,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $44,200 (17%) higher. It is roughly $78,300 (36%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $68,200 (29%) higher compared to the city of Manchester.

Rents

The median rent in Scarborough is $1,125 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $16 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $63 (5%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Manchester.
