Compared to its surrounding areas, Scarborough is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Manchester.

Home prices

Scarborough's median home cost is $295,800. This is approximately $44,200 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $78,300 (36%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $68,200 (29%) higher compared to the city of Manchester.

Rents

The median rent in Scarborough is $1,125 per month. It is roughly $16 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $63 (5%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Manchester.
