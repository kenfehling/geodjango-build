Home prices

The cost of the median home in Farmington is $173,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $85,240 (96%) higher. It is approximately $44,300 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $115,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Farmington the median monthly rent is $630. This is about $17 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $494 (43%) lower.
