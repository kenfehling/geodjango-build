In comparison to other places in this area, Ellsworth is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Bangor however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ellsworth costs $178,400. This is about $24,320 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $39,100 (17%) lower, and is approximately $21,400 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Bangor.

Rents

The median rent in Ellsworth is $787 per month. This is approximately $66 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $275 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Bangor it is roughly $29 (3%) lower.
