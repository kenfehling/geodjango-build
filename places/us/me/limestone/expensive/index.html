Home prices

The median home in Limestone costs $73,100. It is roughly $20,180 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $144,400 (66%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $42,300 (36%) lower compared to the city of Presque Isle.

Rents

Limestone's median rent is $816 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $321 (64%) higher. It is roughly $246 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $224 (37%) higher compared to the city of Presque Isle.
