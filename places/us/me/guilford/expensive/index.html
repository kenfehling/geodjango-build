Compared to other places in this area, Guilford is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Lewiston.

Home prices

Guilford's median home cost is $105,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,720 (18%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $111,600 (51%) lower, and it is about $34,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Lewiston.

Rents

In Guilford the median monthly rent is $528. It is approximately $132 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $534 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Lewiston it is approximately $220 (29%) lower.
