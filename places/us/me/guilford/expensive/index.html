Home prices

The median home in Guilford costs $105,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $16,720 (18%) higher. It is about $111,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $34,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Lewiston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Guilford is $528. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $132 (20%) lower. It is roughly $534 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $220 (29%) lower compared to the city of Lewiston.
