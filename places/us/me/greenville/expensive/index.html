Home prices

The median home in Greenville costs $97,100. It is roughly $6,920 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $120,400 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Bangor it is roughly $59,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

Greenville's median monthly rent is $481. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $169 (26%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $581 (54%) lower, and is roughly $335 (41%) lower compared to the city of Bangor.
