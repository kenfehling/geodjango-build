Home prices

The median home in Fort Fairfield costs $92,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $1,560 (1%) lower. It is approximately $125,400 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Presque Isle is about $23,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

Fort Fairfield's median monthly rent is $334. This is about $265 (44%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $728 (68%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Presque Isle it is roughly $258 (43%) lower.
