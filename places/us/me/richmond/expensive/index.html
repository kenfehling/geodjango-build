Home prices

Richmond's median home price is $164,100. This is roughly $2,180 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $53,400 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $124,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richmond is $717. It is roughly $30 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $345 (32%) lower, and it is about $407 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
