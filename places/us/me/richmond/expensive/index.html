Much like its surrounding areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Richmond is $164,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $2,180 (1%) higher.It is about $53,400 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $124,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

Richmond's median rent is $717 a month. It is approximately $30 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $345 (32%) lower, and it is about $407 (36%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
