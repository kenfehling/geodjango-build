Much like its nearby areas, it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Portland however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $164,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $2,180 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $53,400 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $124,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond is $717 a month. This is roughly $30 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $345 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is roughly $407 (36%) lower.
