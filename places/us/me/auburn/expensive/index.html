Similar to other places in this area, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $165,200. This is approximately $4,200 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $52,300 (24%) lower, and is roughly $123,800 (42%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Auburn is $789. It is roughly $90 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $273 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $335 (29%) lower.
