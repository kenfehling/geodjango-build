Home prices

The median home in Auburn is valued at $165,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $4,200 (2%) higher.It is approximately $52,300 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $123,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $789 per month. This is approximately $90 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $273 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $335 (29%) lower.
