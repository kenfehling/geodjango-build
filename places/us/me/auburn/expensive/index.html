Home prices

The median home in Auburn is valued at $165,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $4,200 (2%) higher. It is roughly $52,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $123,800 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Auburn's median monthly rent is $789. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $90 (10%) lower. It is roughly $273 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $335 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
