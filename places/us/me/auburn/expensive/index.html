Home prices

The median home cost in Auburn is $165,200. It is about $4,200 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $52,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $123,800 (42%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Auburn is $789. This is roughly $90 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $273 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $335 (29%) lower.
