Home prices

The value of the median home in Topsham is $230,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $55,700 (31%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $12,900 (5%) higher, and it is about $58,600 (20%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Topsham's median rent is $924 per month. This is about $88 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $138 (12%) lower, and is roughly $200 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.
