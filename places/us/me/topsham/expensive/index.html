Home prices

The median home in Topsham costs $230,400. It is roughly $55,700 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $12,900 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $58,600 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Topsham's median monthly rent is $924. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $88 (10%) higher. It is about $138 (12%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $200 (17%) lower.
