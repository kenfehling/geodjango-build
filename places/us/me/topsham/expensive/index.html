Compared to nearby areas, Topsham is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Portland however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Topsham is $230,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $55,700 (31%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $12,900 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $58,600 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Topsham the median monthly rent is $924. This is about $88 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $138 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is about $200 (17%) lower.
