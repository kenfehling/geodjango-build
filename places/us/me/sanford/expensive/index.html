Home prices

Sanford's median home value is $183,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $71,460 (27%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $33,700 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $348,900 (65%) lower.

Rents

Sanford's median rent is $901 per month. It is roughly $29 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $161 (15%) lower, and is approximately $719 (44%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
