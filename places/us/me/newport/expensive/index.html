Home prices

The value of the median home in Newport is $147,500. It is roughly $35,960 (32%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $70,000 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Lewiston is roughly $6,900 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Newport the median rent is $723 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $89 (14%) higher. It is approximately $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $25 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Lewiston.
