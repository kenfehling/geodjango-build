Compared to the surrounding areas, Falmouth is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Portland, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Falmouth costs $428,700. This is roughly $101,580 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $211,200 (97%) higher, and is about $139,700 (48%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

Falmouth's median monthly rent is $1,356. It is roughly $66 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $294 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $232 (20%) higher.
