In comparison to its nearby areas, Falmouth is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Falmouth is valued at $428,700. This is roughly $101,580 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $211,200 (97%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $139,700 (48%) higher.

Rents

Falmouth's median rent is $1,356 per month. This is roughly $66 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $294 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Portland it is about $232 (20%) higher.
