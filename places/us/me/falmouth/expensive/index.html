Compared to other places in this area, Falmouth is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Falmouth costs $428,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $101,580 (31%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $211,200 (97%) higher, and is approximately $139,700 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

Falmouth's median rent is $1,356 a month. It is approximately $66 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $294 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Portland is roughly $232 (20%) higher.
