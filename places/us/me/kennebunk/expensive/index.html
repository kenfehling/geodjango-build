Home prices

The value of the median home in Kennebunk is $301,700. It is roughly $18,620 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $84,200 (38%) higher, and is approximately $231,000 (43%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Kennebunk's median rent is $935 per month. It is about $34 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $127 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $685 (42%) lower.
