In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kennebunk is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kennebunk is $301,700. This is roughly $18,620 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $84,200 (38%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $231,000 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Kennebunk is $935 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $34 (3%) lower. It is approximately $127 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $685 (42%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
