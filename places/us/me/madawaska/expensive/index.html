Home prices

The median home in Madawaska is valued at $109,000. It is roughly $24,040 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $108,500 (49%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $6,400 (5%) lower compared to the city of Presque Isle.

Rents

In Madawaska the median rent is $497 per month. It is approximately $102 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $565 (53%) lower, and is approximately $95 (16%) lower compared to the city of Presque Isle.
