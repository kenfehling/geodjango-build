Home prices

The median home in Madawaska is valued at $109,000. This is roughly $24,040 (28%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $108,500 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $6,400 (5%) lower compared to the city of Presque Isle.

Rents

The median rent in Madawaska is $497 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $102 (17%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $565 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Presque Isle is about $95 (16%) lower.
