Compared to other places in this area, Madawaska is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Presque Isle, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Madawaska is $109,000. It is roughly $24,040 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $108,500 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Presque Isle is roughly $6,400 (5%) lower.

Rents

Madawaska's median rent is $497 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $102 (17%) lower. It is approximately $565 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Presque Isle is about $95 (16%) lower.
