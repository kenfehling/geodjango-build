Home prices

The median home in Cumberland Center is valued at $332,000. It is about $6,480 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $114,500 (52%) higher, and is about $43,000 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Cumberland Center is $1,164 a month. This is about $92 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $102 (9%) higher, and is approximately $40 (3%) higher compared to the city of Portland.
