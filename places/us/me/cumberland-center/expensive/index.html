Home prices

The median home in Cumberland Center is valued at $332,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $6,480 (1%) higher.It is about $114,500 (52%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $43,000 (14%) higher compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Cumberland Center is $1,164 a month. This is approximately $92 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $102 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Portland it is roughly $40 (3%) higher.
