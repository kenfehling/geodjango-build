Home prices

The value of the median home in Bar Harbor is $327,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $56,360 (20%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $109,900 (50%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bangor is roughly $170,400 (108%) higher.

Rents

Bar Harbor's median rent is $829 a month. It is approximately $152 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $233 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to the city of Bangor.
