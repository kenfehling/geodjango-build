Home prices

The median home value in Winterport is $172,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $4,240 (2%) lower. It is approximately $45,000 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Bangor is approximately $15,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

Winterport's median rent is $706 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $13 (1%) higher. It is roughly $356 (33%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bangor is roughly $110 (13%) lower.
