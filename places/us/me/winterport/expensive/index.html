Home prices

The median home in Winterport is valued at $172,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $4,240 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $45,000 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Bangor it is approximately $15,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Winterport is $706. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $13 (1%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $356 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $110 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Bangor.
