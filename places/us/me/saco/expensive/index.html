Home prices

Saco's median home value is $258,900. It is roughly $2,700 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $41,400 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $273,800 (51%) lower.

Rents

Saco's median monthly rent is $1,173. This is approximately $116 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $111 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $447 (27%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
