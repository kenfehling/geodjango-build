Home prices

The median home in Saco costs $258,900. It is approximately $2,700 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $41,400 (19%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $273,800 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Saco's median rent is $1,173 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $116 (11%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $111 (10%) higher, and it is approximately $447 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
