Compared to other places in this area, Randolph is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

The median home in Randolph is valued at $127,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32,220 (20%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $89,800 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Portland is approximately $161,300 (55%) lower.

Rents

Randolph's median rent is $648 a month. It is approximately $111 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $414 (38%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $476 (42%) lower compared to the city of Portland.
