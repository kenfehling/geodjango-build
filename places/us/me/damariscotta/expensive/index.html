The median home value in Damariscotta is $288,300. This is roughly $71,380 (32%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $70,800 (32%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

The median rent in Damariscotta is $852 per month. It is about $200 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $210 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Portland it is approximately $272 (24%) lower.
