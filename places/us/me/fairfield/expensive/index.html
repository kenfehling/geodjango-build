Compared to the surrounding areas, Fairfield is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Portland.

Home prices

Fairfield's median home value is $101,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $27,400 (21%) lower. It is approximately $115,800 (53%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $187,300 (64%) lower compared to the city of Portland.

Rents

In Fairfield the median monthly rent is $675. It is approximately $117 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $387 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Portland is approximately $449 (39%) lower.
