Home prices

The median home in Biddeford costs $245,900. It is roughly $64,060 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $28,400 (13%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $286,800 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Biddeford the median monthly rent is $929. It is approximately $130 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $133 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $691 (42%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
