In comparison to the surrounding areas, Biddeford is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Biddeford costs $245,900. It is approximately $64,060 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $28,400 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $286,800 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Biddeford is $929 a month. This is about $130 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $133 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $691 (42%) lower.
