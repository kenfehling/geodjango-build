Compared to its surrounding areas, Biddeford is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

Biddeford's median home value is $245,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $64,060 (20%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $28,400 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $286,800 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Biddeford the median rent is $929 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $130 (12%) lower.It is roughly $133 (12%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $691 (42%) lower.
