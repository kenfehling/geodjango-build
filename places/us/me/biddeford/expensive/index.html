Home prices

The price of the median home in Biddeford is $245,900. This is approximately $64,060 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $28,400 (13%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $286,800 (53%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Biddeford is $929. It is about $130 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $133 (12%) lower, and it is about $691 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
