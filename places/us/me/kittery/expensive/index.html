In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kittery is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Kittery is $297,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $85,820 (22%) lower. It is roughly $79,800 (36%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $235,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Kittery is $1,247 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $176 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $185 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $373 (23%) lower.
