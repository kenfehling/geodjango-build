In comparison to nearby areas, Kittery is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kittery costs $297,300. It is about $85,820 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $79,800 (36%) higher, and it is roughly $235,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Kittery is $1,247 per month. This is roughly $176 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $185 (17%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $373 (23%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
