In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kittery is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Boston however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Kittery is $297,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $85,820 (22%) lower.It is approximately $79,800 (36%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $235,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Kittery's median monthly rent is $1,247. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $176 (16%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $185 (17%) higher, and it is roughly $373 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
