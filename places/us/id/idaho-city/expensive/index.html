Home prices

Rents

In Idaho City the median rent is $482 per month. It is about $633 (56%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $580 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boise City it is about $475 (49%) lower.
