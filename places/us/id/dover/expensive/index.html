Home prices

Rents

Dover's median monthly rent is $1,556. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $731 (88%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $494 (46%) higher, and is roughly $690 (79%) higher in comparison to the city of Spokane.
