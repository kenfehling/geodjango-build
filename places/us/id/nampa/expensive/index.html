Compared to other places in this area, Nampa is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boise City.

Home prices

The median home cost in Nampa is $167,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $54,500 (24%) lower. It is approximately $50,300 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boise City it is about $86,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

Nampa's median rent is $925 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $100 (9%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $137 (12%) lower, and it is about $32 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Boise City.
