Home prices

The median home value in Nampa is $167,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $54,500 (24%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $50,300 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Boise City is approximately $86,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nampa is $925. This is roughly $100 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $137 (12%) lower, and is about $32 (3%) lower compared to the city of Boise City.
