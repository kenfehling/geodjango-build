Compared to its surrounding areas, Hayden is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Spokane however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Hayden is $256,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $64,920 (20%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $38,600 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Spokane is approximately $68,500 (36%) higher.

Rents

In Hayden the median rent is $1,073 a month. It is roughly $14 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $11 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Spokane is roughly $207 (23%) higher.
