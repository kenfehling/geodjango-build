Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Hayden is $1,073 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14 (1%) higher.It is about $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Spokane is approximately $207 (23%) higher.
