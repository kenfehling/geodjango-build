Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Hayden is $1,073 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $14 (1%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $11 (1%) higher, and it is about $207 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Spokane.
