Home prices

Kootenai's median home value is $172,400. This is about $83,480 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is about $45,100 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Spokane is approximately $15,200 (8%) lower.

Rents

Kootenai's median monthly rent is $988. Compared to other places in this area it is about $79 (7%) lower.It is roughly $74 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Spokane is about $122 (14%) higher.
