Home prices

The median home in Rexburg is valued at $211,700. It is roughly $64,420 (43%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $5,800 (2%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $47,200 (28%) higher compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

In Rexburg the median monthly rent is $717. It is roughly $273 (27%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $345 (32%) lower, and is about $59 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Idaho Falls.
