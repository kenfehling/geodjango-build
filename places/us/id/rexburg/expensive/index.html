Home prices

The median home in Rexburg costs $211,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $64,420 (43%) higher. It is approximately $5,800 (2%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $47,200 (28%) higher compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rexburg is $717. This is roughly $273 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $345 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Idaho Falls is approximately $59 (7%) lower.
