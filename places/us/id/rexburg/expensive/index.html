Home prices

The value of the median home in Rexburg is $211,700. It is about $64,420 (43%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $5,800 (2%) lower, and it is about $47,200 (28%) higher compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

In Rexburg the median rent is $717 a month. It is approximately $273 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $345 (32%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Idaho Falls it is approximately $59 (7%) lower.
