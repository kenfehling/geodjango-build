Home prices

Rents

Rexburg's median monthly rent is $717. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $273 (27%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $345 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Idaho Falls is about $59 (7%) lower.
