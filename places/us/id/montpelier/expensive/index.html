In comparison to the surrounding areas, Montpelier is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Ogden.

Home prices

The median home cost in Montpelier is $116,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $23,380 (16%) lower. It is about $101,000 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $53,600 (31%) lower compared to the city of Ogden.

Rents

The median rent in Montpelier is $552 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $169 (23%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $510 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Ogden it is roughly $266 (32%) lower.
