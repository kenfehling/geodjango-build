Home prices

The median home in Montpelier costs $116,500. It is roughly $23,380 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $101,000 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $53,600 (31%) lower compared to the city of Ogden.

Rents

Montpelier's median monthly rent is $552. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $169 (23%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $510 (48%) lower, and it is roughly $266 (32%) lower compared to the city of Ogden.
