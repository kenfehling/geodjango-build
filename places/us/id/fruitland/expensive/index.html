Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fruitland is $738. It is approximately $33 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $324 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boise City it is approximately $219 (22%) lower.
