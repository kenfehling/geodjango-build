Home prices

Fruitland's median home value is $154,400. This is roughly $45,000 (41%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $63,100 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Boise City it is about $99,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fruitland is $738. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $33 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $324 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Boise City it is roughly $219 (22%) lower.
