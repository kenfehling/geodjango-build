Home prices

The median home in Midvale costs $150,600. This is about $48,300 (47%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $66,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $102,800 (40%) lower compared to the city of Boise City.

Rents

In Midvale the median monthly rent is $722. It is roughly $63 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $340 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $235 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Boise City.
