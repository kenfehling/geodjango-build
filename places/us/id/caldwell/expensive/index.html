In comparison to its surrounding areas, Caldwell is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Boise City however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Caldwell's median home price is $152,400. It is about $26,540 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $65,100 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $101,000 (39%) lower compared to the city of Boise City.

Rents

Caldwell's median rent is $878 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $63 (6%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $184 (17%) lower, and it is approximately $79 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Boise City.
