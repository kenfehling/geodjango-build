Home prices

The cost of the median home in Caldwell is $152,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $26,540 (14%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $65,100 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boise City it is roughly $101,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Caldwell the median rent is $878 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $63 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $184 (17%) lower, and is approximately $79 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Boise City.
