Home prices

The median home in Caldwell costs $152,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $26,540 (14%) lower. It is about $65,100 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boise City is approximately $101,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Caldwell the median monthly rent is $878. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $63 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $184 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boise City it is about $79 (8%) lower.
