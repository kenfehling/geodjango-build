Compared to other places in this area, Caldwell is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boise City.

Home prices

The median home in Caldwell costs $152,400. This is roughly $26,540 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $65,100 (29%) lower, and is approximately $101,000 (39%) lower compared to the city of Boise City.

Rents

In Caldwell the median rent is $878 per month. It is approximately $63 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $184 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Boise City is roughly $79 (8%) lower.
