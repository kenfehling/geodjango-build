Home prices

Caldwell's median home cost is $152,400. This is approximately $26,540 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $65,100 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $101,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Boise City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Caldwell is $878. This is approximately $63 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $184 (17%) lower, and is about $79 (8%) lower compared to the city of Boise City.
