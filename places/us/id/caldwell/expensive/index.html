In comparison to the surrounding areas, Caldwell is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Boise City, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Caldwell is $152,400. This is about $26,540 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $65,100 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boise City is roughly $101,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

Caldwell's median rent is $878 a month. It is approximately $63 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $184 (17%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $79 (8%) lower compared to the city of Boise City.
