Home prices

The median home in Rigby costs $136,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $5,120 (3%) lower. It is approximately $81,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $28,000 (17%) lower compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rigby is $763. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $11 (1%) higher. It is approximately $299 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $13 (1%) lower compared to the city of Idaho Falls.
