Home prices

The median home cost in Victor is $268,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $135,540 (33%) lower. It is roughly $51,300 (23%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $104,300 (63%) higher compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

The median rent in Victor is $950 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $139 (17%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $112 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $174 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Idaho Falls.
