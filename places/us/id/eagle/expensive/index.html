Home prices

The median home price in Eagle is $426,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $143,220 (50%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $208,500 (95%) higher, and is roughly $172,600 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Boise City.

Rents

Eagle's median monthly rent is $1,138. It is roughly $43 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $76 (7%) higher, and is roughly $181 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Boise City.
