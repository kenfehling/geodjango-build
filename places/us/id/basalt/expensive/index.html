Home prices

The median home in Basalt is valued at $136,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $19,440 (12%) lower. It is roughly $81,000 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Idaho Falls is roughly $28,000 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Basalt the median monthly rent is $806. It is about $130 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $256 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Idaho Falls is approximately $30 (3%) higher.
