Home prices

The cost of the median home in Groveland is $229,200. This is roughly $95,920 (71%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $11,700 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $64,700 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Groveland is $715. This is roughly $71 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $347 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Idaho Falls it is approximately $61 (7%) lower.
