Compared to its surrounding areas, Groveland is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Home prices

The median home in Groveland costs $229,200. This is approximately $95,920 (71%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $11,700 (5%) higher, and is approximately $64,700 (39%) higher compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

In Groveland the median rent is $715 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $71 (11%) higher. It is about $347 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Idaho Falls it is approximately $61 (7%) lower.
