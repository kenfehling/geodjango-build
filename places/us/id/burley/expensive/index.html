The median home in Burley is valued at $128,100. It is about $16,720 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $89,400 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pocatello is about $20,100 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Burley the median monthly rent is $669. It is about $24 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $393 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $13 (1%) higher compared to the city of Pocatello.
