In comparison to its nearby areas, Lewiston is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Spokane however, it is close to average.

Home prices

Lewiston's median home value is $189,400. This is about $39,280 (26%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $28,100 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Spokane.

Rents

The median rent in Lewiston is $722 per month. This is roughly $79 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $340 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Spokane it is approximately $144 (16%) lower.
