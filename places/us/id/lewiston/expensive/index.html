In comparison to other places in this area, Lewiston is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Spokane.

Home prices

The median home in Lewiston costs $189,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $39,280 (26%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $28,100 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Spokane it is is very similar.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lewiston is $722. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $79 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $340 (32%) lower, and is about $144 (16%) lower compared to the city of Spokane.
