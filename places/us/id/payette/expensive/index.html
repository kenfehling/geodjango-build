Home prices

Payette's median home cost is $118,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $7,100 (5%) lower. It is roughly $99,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $135,200 (53%) lower compared to the city of Boise City.

Rents

Payette's median monthly rent is $760. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $27 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $302 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boise City is about $197 (20%) lower.
