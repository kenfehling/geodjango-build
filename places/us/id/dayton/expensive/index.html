Home prices

The value of the median home in Dayton is $167,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $16,640 (9%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $49,700 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Salt Lake City is roughly $146,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $875. This is about $29 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $187 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $110 (11%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.
