Home prices

The median home in Menan costs $157,600. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $19,920 (14%) higher. It is about $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $6,900 (4%) lower compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

Menan's median monthly rent is $803. This is about $144 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $259 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Idaho Falls is about $27 (3%) higher.
