Home prices

Rents

In Preston the median monthly rent is $657. This is about $232 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $405 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City it is approximately $328 (33%) lower.
