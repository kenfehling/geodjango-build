Compared to the surrounding areas, Preston is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Home prices

The median home price in Preston is $168,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16,280 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $49,400 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $146,400 (46%) lower compared to the city of Salt Lake City.

Rents

Preston's median rent is $657 a month. It is roughly $232 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $405 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Salt Lake City is approximately $328 (33%) lower.
