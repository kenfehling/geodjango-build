In comparison to the surrounding areas, Moreland is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Home prices

Moreland's median home cost is $157,000. This is about $9,280 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $60,500 (27%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $7,500 (4%) lower compared to the city of Idaho Falls.

Rents

In Moreland the median monthly rent is $638. It is about $21 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $424 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Idaho Falls is roughly $138 (17%) lower.
