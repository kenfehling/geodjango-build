Home prices

The median home in Coeur d'Alene is valued at $234,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $89,800 (27%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $17,400 (8%) higher, and it is approximately $47,300 (25%) higher compared to the city of Spokane.

Rents

In Coeur d'Alene the median rent is $982 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $85 (7%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $80 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $116 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Spokane.
