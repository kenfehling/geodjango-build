Home prices

The median home in Sandpoint costs $249,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $8,920 (3%) higher.It is roughly $31,900 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $61,800 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Spokane.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sandpoint is $965. This is approximately $107 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $97 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Spokane it is approximately $99 (11%) higher.
