Home prices

The median home value in Sandpoint is $249,400. It is approximately $8,920 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $31,900 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Spokane it is about $61,800 (32%) higher.

Rents

Sandpoint's median rent is $965 per month. It is approximately $107 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $97 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $99 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Spokane.
