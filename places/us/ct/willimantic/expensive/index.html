Home prices

The median home in Willimantic is valued at $148,900. It is approximately $59,740 (28%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $68,600 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $383,800 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Willimantic the median monthly rent is $877. It is about $379 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $185 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $743 (45%) lower.
