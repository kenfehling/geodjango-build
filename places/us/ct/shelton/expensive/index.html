Home prices

The price of the median home in Shelton is $349,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $55,700 (18%) higher. It is about $131,800 (60%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is about $256,700 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Shelton is $1,466 per month. It is about $35 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $404 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $23 (1%) higher.
