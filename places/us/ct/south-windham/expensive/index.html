Home prices

The median home in South Windham costs $149,300. This is approximately $56,420 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $68,200 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $383,400 (71%) lower.

Rents

South Windham's median rent is $1,026 a month. This is approximately $103 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $36 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $594 (36%) lower.
