Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Windham is $149,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $56,420 (27%) lower.It is approximately $68,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $383,400 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

In South Windham the median rent is $1,026 a month. It is roughly $103 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $36 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is about $594 (36%) lower.
