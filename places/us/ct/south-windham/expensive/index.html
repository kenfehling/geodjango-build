Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Windham is $149,300. It is roughly $56,420 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $68,200 (31%) lower, and is roughly $383,400 (71%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In South Windham the median monthly rent is $1,026. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $103 (9%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $36 (3%) lower, and is approximately $594 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
