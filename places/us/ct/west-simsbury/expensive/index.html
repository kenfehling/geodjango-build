Home prices

The price of the median home in West Simsbury is $363,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $110,680 (43%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $145,700 (66%) higher, and is about $136,100 (59%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents
