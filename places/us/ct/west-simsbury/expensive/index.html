Compared to the surrounding areas, West Simsbury is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Simsbury costs $363,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $110,680 (43%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $145,700 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is about $136,100 (59%) higher.

Rents

West Simsbury's median monthly rent is $2,107. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $983 (87%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $1,045 (98%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is approximately $1,012 (92%) higher.
