Home prices

The median home in West Simsbury costs $363,200. Compared to nearby areas it is about $110,680 (43%) higher. It is approximately $145,700 (66%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $136,100 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In West Simsbury the median monthly rent is $2,107. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $983 (87%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $1,045 (98%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $1,012 (92%) higher.
