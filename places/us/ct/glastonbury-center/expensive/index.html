Home prices

The median home in Glastonbury Center costs $299,100. This is about $102,860 (52%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $81,600 (37%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $72,000 (31%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Glastonbury Center the median rent is $1,250 per month. This is approximately $154 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $188 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester is about $155 (14%) higher.
