Compared to other places in this area, Glastonbury Center is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Glastonbury Center is $299,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $102,860 (52%) higher.It is roughly $81,600 (37%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $72,000 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Glastonbury Center the median rent is $1,250 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $154 (14%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $188 (17%) higher, and it is about $155 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.
