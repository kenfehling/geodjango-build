In comparison to its surrounding areas, Glastonbury Center is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home in Glastonbury Center is valued at $299,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $102,860 (52%) higher.It is approximately $81,600 (37%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is about $72,000 (31%) higher.

Rents

Glastonbury Center's median rent is $1,250 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $154 (14%) higher.It is about $188 (17%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $155 (14%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
