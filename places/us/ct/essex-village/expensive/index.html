In comparison to its surrounding areas, Essex Village is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Essex Village is $447,200. It is roughly $89,100 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $229,700 (105%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $220,100 (96%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Essex Village's median rent is $1,545 per month. This is about $109 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $483 (45%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $450 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.
