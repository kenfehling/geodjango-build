Home prices

The median home in Stamford is valued at $532,700. This is roughly $787,480 (59%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $315,200 (144%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $73,300 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Stamford the median rent is $1,812 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $297 (14%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $750 (70%) higher, and is approximately $369 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
