Home prices

The median home price in Naugatuck is $183,400. It is about $60,680 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $34,100 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $422,600 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Naugatuck is $1,069 per month. It is roughly $45 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is is very similar in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $374 (25%) lower.
