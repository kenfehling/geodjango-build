Home prices

The median home in Naugatuck costs $183,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $60,680 (24%) lower. It is approximately $34,100 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $422,600 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Naugatuck is $1,069. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $45 (4%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and is approximately $374 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
