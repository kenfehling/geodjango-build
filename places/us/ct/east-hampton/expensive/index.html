Home prices

The median home price in East Hampton is $241,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $15,980 (6%) lower.It is about $23,700 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Worcester it is approximately $14,100 (6%) higher.

Rents

In East Hampton the median rent is $1,153 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $73 (6%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $91 (8%) higher, and it is approximately $58 (5%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
