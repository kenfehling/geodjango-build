Compared to the surrounding areas, East Hampton is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in East Hampton is $241,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $15,980 (6%) lower.It is roughly $23,700 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is roughly $14,100 (6%) higher.

Rents

East Hampton's median monthly rent is $1,153. It is approximately $73 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $91 (8%) higher, and is roughly $58 (5%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
