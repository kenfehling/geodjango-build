In comparison to other places in this area, Norwalk is significantly less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Norwalk is $435,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $401,980 (47%) lower. It is about $218,300 (100%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is about $170,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Norwalk is $1,685 a month. It is roughly $632 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $623 (58%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $242 (16%) higher.
