Compared to its nearby areas, New Haven is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

New Haven's median home value is $199,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $71,400 (26%) lower. It is roughly $18,500 (8%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $407,000 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Haven is $1,196. It is roughly $130 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $134 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $247 (17%) lower.
