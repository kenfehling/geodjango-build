Compared to its nearby areas, New Haven is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in New Haven is $199,000. This is approximately $71,400 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $18,500 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $407,000 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Haven is $1,196. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $130 (9%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $134 (12%) higher, and is about $247 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
