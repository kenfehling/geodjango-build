In comparison to the surrounding areas, New Haven is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

New Haven's median home cost is $199,000. It is roughly $71,400 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $18,500 (8%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $407,000 (67%) lower.

Rents

New Haven's median rent is $1,196 per month. It is approximately $130 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $134 (12%) higher, and is roughly $247 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
