Compared to other places in this area, East Hartford is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in East Hartford is $164,200. This is about $44,080 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $53,300 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is about $62,900 (27%) lower.

Rents

East Hartford's median rent is $1,032 a month. This is roughly $61 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $63 (5%) lower.
