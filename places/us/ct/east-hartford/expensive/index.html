Compared to its nearby areas, East Hartford is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in East Hartford is $164,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $44,080 (21%) lower. It is about $53,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $62,900 (27%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In East Hartford the median rent is $1,032 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $61 (5%) lower. It is roughly $30 (2%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $63 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
