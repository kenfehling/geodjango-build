In comparison to other places in this area, East Hartford is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Worcester, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Hartford is $164,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $44,080 (21%) lower.It is roughly $53,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $62,900 (27%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In East Hartford the median rent is $1,032 a month. It is approximately $61 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Worcester it is about $63 (5%) lower.
