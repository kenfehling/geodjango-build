In comparison to other places in this area, East Hartford is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Hartford is $164,200. It is about $44,080 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $53,300 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $62,900 (27%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In East Hartford the median rent is $1,032 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $61 (5%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $30 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester is roughly $63 (5%) lower.
