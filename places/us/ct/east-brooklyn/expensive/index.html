In comparison to the surrounding areas, East Brooklyn is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Boston, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Brooklyn costs $205,700. It is roughly $39,220 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $11,800 (5%) lower, and it is approximately $327,000 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

East Brooklyn's median rent is $821 per month. This is approximately $162 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $799 (49%) lower.
