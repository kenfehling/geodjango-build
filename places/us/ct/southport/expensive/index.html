Home prices

The median home in Southport costs $703,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $233,160 (49%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $485,700 (223%) higher, and is approximately $97,200 (16%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Southport the median monthly rent is $2,208. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $362 (19%) higher. It is about $1,146 (107%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $765 (53%) higher.
