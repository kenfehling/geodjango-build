Compared to the surrounding areas, Bristol is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Bristol's median home value is $197,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $16,400 (7%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $19,700 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is about $408,200 (67%) lower.

Rents

Bristol's median rent is $1,027 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $35 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $416 (28%) lower.
