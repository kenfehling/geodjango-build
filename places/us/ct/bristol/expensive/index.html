Compared to the surrounding areas, Bristol is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bristol is valued at $197,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $16,400 (7%) lower. It is about $19,700 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $408,200 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Bristol's median rent is $1,027 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $35 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $416 (28%) lower.
