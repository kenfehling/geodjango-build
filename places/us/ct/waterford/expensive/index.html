Home prices

The price of the median home in Waterford is $197,600. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $118,780 (37%) lower. It is approximately $19,900 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is approximately $29,500 (12%) lower.

Rents

Waterford's median rent is $1,187 a month. This is roughly $160 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $125 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $92 (8%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
