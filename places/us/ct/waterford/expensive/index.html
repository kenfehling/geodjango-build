Compared to its surrounding areas, Waterford is significantly less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waterford costs $197,600. This is roughly $118,780 (37%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $19,900 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $29,500 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Waterford's median rent is $1,187 per month. This is roughly $160 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $125 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester it is approximately $92 (8%) higher.
