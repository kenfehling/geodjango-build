Home prices

The cost of the median home in East Haven is $212,800. This is approximately $54,840 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $4,700 (2%) lower, and is approximately $393,200 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

East Haven's median rent is $1,213 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $110 (8%) lower. It is roughly $151 (14%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $230 (15%) lower compared to the city of New York.
