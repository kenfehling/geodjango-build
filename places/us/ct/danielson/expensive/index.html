Home prices

The median home in Danielson costs $156,500. This is roughly $32,180 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $61,000 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is about $376,200 (70%) lower.

Rents

In Danielson the median rent is $1,048 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $59 (5%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $14 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $572 (35%) lower.
