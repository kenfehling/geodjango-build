Home prices

Danielson's median home value is $156,500. It is about $32,180 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $61,000 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $376,200 (70%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Danielson is $1,048. It is roughly $59 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $14 (1%) lower, and is roughly $572 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
