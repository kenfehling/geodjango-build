Home prices

The median home price in Danielson is $156,500. Compared to nearby areas it is about $32,180 (17%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $61,000 (28%) lower, and it is roughly $376,200 (70%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Danielson's median rent is $1,048 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $59 (5%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $14 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $572 (35%) lower.
