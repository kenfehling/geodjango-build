Compared to nearby areas, Norwich is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Norwich is $164,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $22,520 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $53,300 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is approximately $368,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Norwich is $1,061 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $146 (12%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $559 (34%) lower.
