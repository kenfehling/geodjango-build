In comparison to its nearby areas, Norwich is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Norwich is valued at $164,200. It is roughly $22,520 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $53,300 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is about $368,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Norwich the median rent is $1,061 a month. This is about $146 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston is approximately $559 (34%) lower.
