Home prices

The median home in South Coventry costs $245,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $56,540 (29%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $28,300 (13%) higher, and it is roughly $286,900 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in South Coventry is $778 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $498 (39%) lower. It is roughly $284 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Boston is about $842 (51%) lower.
