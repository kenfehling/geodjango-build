Home prices

Rents

South Coventry's median monthly rent is $778. This is about $498 (39%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $284 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $842 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
