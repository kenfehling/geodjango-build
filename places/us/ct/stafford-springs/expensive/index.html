Home prices

The cost of the median home in Stafford Springs is $161,300. It is about $50,080 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $56,200 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $371,400 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

Stafford Springs's median monthly rent is $959. It is roughly $84 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $103 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Boston is roughly $661 (40%) lower.
