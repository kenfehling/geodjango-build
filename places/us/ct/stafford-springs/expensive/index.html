Home prices

The value of the median home in Stafford Springs is $161,300. It is approximately $50,080 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $56,200 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $371,400 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Stafford Springs the median monthly rent is $959. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $84 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $103 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $661 (40%) lower.
