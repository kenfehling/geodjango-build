Bethlehem Village's median home cost is $321,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $93,140 (40%) higher. It is roughly $103,500 (47%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $285,000 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Bethlehem Village the median monthly rent is $1,440. This is roughly $509 (54%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $378 (35%) higher compared to the entire country, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of New York.
