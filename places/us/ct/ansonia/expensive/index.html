Home prices

The median home price in Ansonia is $214,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $53,460 (19%) lower.It is about $3,300 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $391,800 (64%) lower.

Rents

Ansonia's median rent is $1,101 per month. It is about $211 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $39 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $342 (23%) lower compared to the city of New York.
