Home prices

The median home in Ansonia costs $214,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $53,460 (19%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $3,300 (1%) lower, and is about $391,800 (64%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Ansonia is $1,101 a month. It is approximately $211 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $39 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $342 (23%) lower.
