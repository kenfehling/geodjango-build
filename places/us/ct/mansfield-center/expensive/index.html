Compared to the surrounding areas, Mansfield Center is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The median home in Mansfield Center is valued at $225,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $31,580 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $7,500 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $307,700 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.

Rents

The median rent in Mansfield Center is $1,375 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $219 (18%) higher. It is approximately $313 (29%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Boston it is roughly $245 (15%) lower.
