Compared to the surrounding areas, Collinsville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Collinsville's median home price is $313,600. This is approximately $24,520 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $96,100 (44%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $292,400 (48%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Collinsville the median rent is $1,134 a month. This is about $239 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $72 (6%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $309 (21%) lower compared to the city of New York.
