Compared to the surrounding areas, Durham is a bit more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Durham's median home cost is $275,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $39,560 (16%) higher.It is roughly $58,300 (26%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $330,200 (54%) lower.

Rents

Durham's median rent is $1,492 a month. This is about $465 (45%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $430 (40%) higher, and it is approximately $49 (3%) higher compared to the city of New York.
