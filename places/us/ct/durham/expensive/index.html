In comparison to its surrounding areas, Durham is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Durham costs $275,800. This is approximately $39,560 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $58,300 (26%) higher, and it is approximately $330,200 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Durham is $1,492. This is approximately $465 (45%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $430 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $49 (3%) higher.
