Compared to other places in this area, Durham is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Durham costs $275,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $39,560 (16%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $58,300 (26%) higher, and it is approximately $330,200 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Durham the median rent is $1,492 a month. This is about $465 (45%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $430 (40%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $49 (3%) higher.
