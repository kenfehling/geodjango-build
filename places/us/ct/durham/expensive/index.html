Compared to the surrounding areas, Durham is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of New Yorkhowever, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Durham is valued at $275,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $39,560 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $58,300 (26%) higher, and is about $330,200 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Durham the median monthly rent is $1,492. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $465 (45%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $430 (40%) higher, and it is about $49 (3%) higher compared to the city of New York.
