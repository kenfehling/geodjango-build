Home prices

The median home value in Westport is $1,150,400. This is approximately $527,140 (84%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $932,900 (428%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $544,400 (89%) higher.

Rents

In Westport the median rent is $1,956 per month. This is approximately $177 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $894 (84%) higher, and is approximately $513 (35%) higher compared to the city of New York.
