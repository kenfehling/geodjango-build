Home prices

The median home value in Wallingford Center is $238,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $28,920 (10%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $20,700 (9%) higher, and it is about $367,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Wallingford Center's median monthly rent is $1,049. This is approximately $126 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $13 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is about $394 (27%) lower.
