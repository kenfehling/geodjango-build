Home prices

Rents

Wauregan's median rent is $1,129 a month. This is approximately $183 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $67 (6%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $491 (30%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
