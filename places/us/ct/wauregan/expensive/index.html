Rents

In Wauregan the median rent is $1,129 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $183 (19%) higher. It is about $67 (6%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $491 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
