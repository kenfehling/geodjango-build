Home prices

The median home in Kensington costs $260,300. This is roughly $42,580 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $42,800 (19%) higher, and it is approximately $345,700 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Kensington's median monthly rent is $1,244. This is roughly $156 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $182 (17%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $199 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
