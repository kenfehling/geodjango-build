Home prices

Greenwich's median home price is $1,128,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $337,920 (42%) higher. It is roughly $911,300 (418%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $522,800 (86%) higher.

Rents

In Greenwich the median monthly rent is $1,803. It is roughly $132 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $741 (69%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $360 (24%) higher compared to the city of New York.
