Compared to its surrounding areas, Greenwich is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of New York, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Greenwich's median home value is $1,128,800. This is about $337,920 (42%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $911,300 (418%) higher, and it is approximately $522,800 (86%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greenwich is $1,803. It is about $132 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $741 (69%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is about $360 (24%) higher.
