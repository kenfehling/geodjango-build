Home prices

The price of the median home in Thomaston is $195,300. This is about $29,280 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $22,200 (10%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $410,700 (67%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Thomaston the median monthly rent is $836. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $265 (24%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $226 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $607 (42%) lower.
