Home prices

The price of the median home in Old Saybrook Center is $398,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $47,460 (13%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $180,700 (83%) higher, and it is approximately $171,100 (75%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Old Saybrook Center the median rent is $1,381 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $35 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $319 (30%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is about $286 (26%) higher.
