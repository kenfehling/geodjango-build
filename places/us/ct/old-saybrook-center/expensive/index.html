Compared to other places in this area, Old Saybrook Center is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Old Saybrook Center costs $398,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $47,460 (13%) higher. It is approximately $180,700 (83%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Worcester it is about $171,100 (75%) higher.

Rents

Old Saybrook Center's median monthly rent is $1,381. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $35 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $319 (30%) higher, and is roughly $286 (26%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
