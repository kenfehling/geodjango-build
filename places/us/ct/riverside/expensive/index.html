Home prices

The price of the median home in Riverside is $1,383,800. This is about $345,600 (33%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,166,300 (536%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $777,800 (128%) higher compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Riverside is $2,168 a month. This is roughly $185 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $1,106 (104%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $725 (50%) higher.
