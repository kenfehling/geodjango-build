Compared to its surrounding areas, North Haven is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of New York, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in North Haven is $298,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $52,960 (21%) higher.It is roughly $80,800 (37%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $307,700 (50%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In North Haven the median monthly rent is $1,325. This is approximately $185 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $263 (24%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $118 (8%) lower.
