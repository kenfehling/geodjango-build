In comparison to nearby areas, North Haven is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Haven is valued at $298,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $52,960 (21%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $80,800 (37%) higher, and it is about $307,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In North Haven the median monthly rent is $1,325. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $185 (16%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $263 (24%) higher, and it is about $118 (8%) lower compared to the city of New York.
