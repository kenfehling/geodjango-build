Compared to its surrounding areas, Georgetown is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Georgetown is valued at $633,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $58,540 (10%) higher. It is about $416,300 (191%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $27,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Georgetown's median monthly rent is $2,744. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $803 (41%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $1,682 (158%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is about $1,301 (90%) higher.
