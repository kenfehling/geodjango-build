Compared to nearby areas, West Haven is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

West Haven's median home cost is $193,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $76,820 (28%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $23,700 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $412,200 (68%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in West Haven is $1,164 per month. It is approximately $143 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $102 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is about $279 (19%) lower.
