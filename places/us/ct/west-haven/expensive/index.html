In comparison to nearby areas, West Haven is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The price of the median home in West Haven is $193,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $76,820 (28%) lower. It is roughly $23,700 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $412,200 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Haven is $1,164. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $143 (10%) lower. It is about $102 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $279 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
