Home prices

The price of the median home in Putnam is $177,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $12,900 (6%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $40,400 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $355,600 (66%) lower.

Rents

Putnam's median rent is $873 per month. This is roughly $53 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $189 (17%) lower, and it is about $747 (46%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
