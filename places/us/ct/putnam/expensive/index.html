In comparison to its surrounding areas, Putnam is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Boston.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Putnam is $177,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $12,900 (6%) lower. It is approximately $40,400 (18%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is roughly $355,600 (66%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Putnam is $873. Compared to other places in this area it is about $53 (5%) lower. It is roughly $189 (17%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $747 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
