Compared to other places in this area, Putnam is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Boston, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Putnam costs $177,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $12,900 (6%) lower.It is about $40,400 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is roughly $355,600 (66%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Putnam is $873 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $53 (5%) lower.It is approximately $189 (17%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $747 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Boston.
