Home prices

Putnam's median home cost is $177,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $12,900 (6%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $40,400 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $355,600 (66%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

Putnam's median monthly rent is $873. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $53 (5%) lower. It is roughly $189 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Boston it is approximately $747 (46%) lower.
