The median home in Stonington costs $590,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $108,640 (22%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $373,400 (171%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Boston it is about $58,200 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Stonington the median rent is $1,418 per month. It is about $116 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $356 (33%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $202 (12%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
