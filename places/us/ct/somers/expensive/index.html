Home prices

The value of the median home in Somers is $269,800. It is about $88,060 (48%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $52,300 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $262,900 (49%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Somers the median rent is $938 a month. This is roughly $292 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $124 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $682 (42%) lower compared to the city of Boston.
