Compared to its nearby areas, Portland is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Worcester, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Portland is $229,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $32,960 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $11,900 (5%) higher, and is about $2,300 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Portland's median monthly rent is $1,094. This is approximately $26 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $32 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester is is very similar.
