Compared to the surrounding areas, Portland is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Worcester, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $229,400. It is approximately $32,960 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $11,900 (5%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $2,300 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

The median rent in Portland is $1,094 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $26 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $32 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is is very similar.
