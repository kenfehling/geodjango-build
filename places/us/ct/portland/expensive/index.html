In comparison to its nearby areas, Portland is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Worcester, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Portland is $229,400. It is approximately $32,960 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $11,900 (5%) higher, and is approximately $2,300 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Portland the median rent is $1,094 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $26 (2%) lower. It is approximately $32 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is is very similar.
