Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Coventry Lake is $1,638. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $555 (51%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $576 (54%) higher, and compared to the city of Boston is about $18 (1%) higher.
