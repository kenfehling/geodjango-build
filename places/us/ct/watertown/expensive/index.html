In comparison to its surrounding areas, Watertown is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Watertown is valued at $220,400. This is approximately $18,960 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $2,900 (1%) higher, and it is roughly $385,600 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Watertown's median rent is $952 per month. It is roughly $145 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $110 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $491 (34%) lower.
