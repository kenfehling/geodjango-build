In comparison to the surrounding areas, Simsbury Center is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Worcester, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Simsbury Center is $301,900. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $27,440 (9%) higher. It is roughly $84,400 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $74,800 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Simsbury Center the median monthly rent is $1,496. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $212 (16%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $434 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is about $401 (36%) higher.
