Rents

In Simsbury Center the median rent is $1,496 a month. It is about $212 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $434 (40%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $401 (36%) higher.
