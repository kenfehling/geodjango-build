In comparison to its surrounding areas, West Hartford is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Hartford costs $334,300. It is roughly $140,080 (72%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $116,800 (53%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Worcester is about $107,200 (47%) higher.

Rents

West Hartford's median rent is $1,325 a month. This is about $253 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $263 (24%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $230 (21%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.
