Compared to the surrounding areas, West Hartford is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Hartford is valued at $334,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $140,080 (72%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $116,800 (53%) higher, and it is approximately $107,200 (47%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In West Hartford the median monthly rent is $1,325. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $253 (23%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $263 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester it is about $230 (21%) higher.
