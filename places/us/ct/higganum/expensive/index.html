Home prices

The cost of the median home in Higganum is $310,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $62,080 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $92,900 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $83,300 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Higganum is $831 per month. This is roughly $348 (29%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $231 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester is roughly $264 (24%) lower.
