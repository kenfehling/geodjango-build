The median home in Plainfield Village costs $173,300. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. It is roughly $44,200 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $359,400 (67%) lower compared to the city of Boston.

Rents

In Plainfield Village the median monthly rent is $862. This is approximately $159 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $200 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Boston is about $758 (46%) lower.
