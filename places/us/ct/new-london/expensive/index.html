Home prices

The median home in New London costs $181,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $74,840 (29%) lower. It is about $35,600 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $45,200 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

New London's median monthly rent is $987. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $128 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $75 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester is roughly $108 (9%) lower.
