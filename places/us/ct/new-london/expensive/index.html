Home prices

The value of the median home in New London is $181,900. It is about $74,840 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $35,600 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $45,200 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

The median rent in New London is $987 per month. It is approximately $128 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $75 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester is approximately $108 (9%) lower.
