Home prices

The price of the median home in New London is $181,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $74,840 (29%) lower. It is roughly $35,600 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Worcester is about $45,200 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in New London is $987 a month. It is approximately $128 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $75 (7%) lower, and is approximately $108 (9%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.
