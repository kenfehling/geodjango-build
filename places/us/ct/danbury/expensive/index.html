Compared to other places in this area, Danbury is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Danbury costs $299,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $18,700 (5%) lower. It is about $82,100 (37%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $306,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Danbury's median rent is $1,466 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $131 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $404 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $23 (1%) higher.
