Compared to other places in this area, Danbury is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Danbury is valued at $299,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $18,700 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $82,100 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $306,400 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Danbury is $1,466 per month. This is about $131 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $404 (38%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $23 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
