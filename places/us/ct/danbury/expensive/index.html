Home prices

Rents

Danbury's median rent is $1,466 a month. It is approximately $131 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $404 (38%) higher, and it is approximately $23 (1%) higher compared to the city of New York.
