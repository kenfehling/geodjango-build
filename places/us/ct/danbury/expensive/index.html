Home prices

Danbury's median home cost is $299,600. It is roughly $18,700 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $82,100 (37%) higher, and it is about $306,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Danbury's median rent is $1,466 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $131 (8%) lower.It is roughly $404 (38%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $23 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
