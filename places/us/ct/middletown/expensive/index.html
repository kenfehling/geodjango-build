Home prices

Middletown's median home price is $231,300. It is about $19,920 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $13,800 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Worcester is about $4,200 (1%) higher.

Rents

Middletown's median monthly rent is $1,133. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar. It is roughly $71 (6%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $38 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.
