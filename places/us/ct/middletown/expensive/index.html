Home prices

The median home value in Middletown is $231,300. This is about $19,920 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $13,800 (6%) higher, and is about $4,200 (1%) higher compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Middletown's median rent is $1,133 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is is very similar.It is about $71 (6%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $38 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Worcester.
