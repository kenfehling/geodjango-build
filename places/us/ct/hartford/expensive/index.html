Compared to other places in this area, Hartford is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Hartford's median home price is $165,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $76,440 (31%) lower. It is about $52,200 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Worcester is roughly $61,800 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hartford is $985. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $152 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $77 (7%) lower, and is approximately $110 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
