In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hartford is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Worcester, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Hartford is $165,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $76,440 (31%) lower.It is approximately $52,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $61,800 (27%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

In Hartford the median monthly rent is $985. It is about $152 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $77 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Worcester is approximately $110 (10%) lower.
