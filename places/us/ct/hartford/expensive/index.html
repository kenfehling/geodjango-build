Compared to nearby areas, Hartford is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Worcester, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Hartford is $165,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $76,440 (31%) lower. It is about $52,200 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $61,800 (27%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

Hartford's median monthly rent is $985. This is roughly $152 (13%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $77 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Worcester it is roughly $110 (10%) lower.
