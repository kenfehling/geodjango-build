In comparison to its surrounding areas, Makawao is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Kihei, it is close to average.

Home prices

Makawao's median home price is $603,800. It is about $128,940 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $386,300 (177%) higher, and it is approximately $13,600 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.

Rents

In Makawao the median monthly rent is $1,336. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $558 (29%) lower. It is approximately $274 (25%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $306 (18%) lower compared to the city of Kihei.
