Home prices

The value of the median home in Makawao is $603,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $128,940 (17%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $386,300 (177%) higher, and compared to the city of Kihei it is roughly $13,600 (2%) higher.

Rents

Makawao's median rent is $1,336 a month. This is about $558 (29%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $274 (25%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $306 (18%) lower compared to the city of Kihei.
