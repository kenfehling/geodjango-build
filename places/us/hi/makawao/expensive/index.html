Home prices

The median home value in Makawao is $603,800. This is about $128,940 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $386,300 (177%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kihei is roughly $13,600 (2%) higher.

Rents

Makawao's median rent is $1,336 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $558 (29%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $274 (25%) higher, and it is roughly $306 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Kihei.
