Compared to its surrounding areas, Makawao is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Kihei however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Makawao is $603,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $128,940 (17%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $386,300 (177%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kihei is about $13,600 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Makawao is $1,336. This is approximately $558 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $274 (25%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $306 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Kihei.
