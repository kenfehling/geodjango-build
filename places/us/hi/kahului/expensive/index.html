In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kahului is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home in Kahului costs $606,600. This is approximately $53,017 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $389,100 (178%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median rent in Kahului is $1,346 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $384 (22%) lower. It is approximately $284 (26%) higher in comparison to the entire country.
