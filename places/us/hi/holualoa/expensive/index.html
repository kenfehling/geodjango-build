Home prices

The median home in Holualoa costs $632,900. It is roughly $111,420 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $415,400 (190%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $303,700 (92%) higher compared to the city of Hilo.

Rents

In Holualoa the median rent is $1,775 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $483 (37%) higher.It is approximately $713 (67%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Hilo it is about $730 (69%) higher.
