Much like its surrounding areas, it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in Haleiwa is valued at $761,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $19,600 (2%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $544,100 (250%) higher, and it is approximately $78,600 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Haleiwa is $1,577. In comparison to other places in this area it is is very similar. Compared to the entire nation it is about $515 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $86 (5%) higher.
