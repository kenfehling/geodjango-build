Home prices

The median home price in Haleiwa is $761,600. This is approximately $19,600 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $544,100 (250%) higher, and it is about $78,600 (11%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Haleiwa is $1,577. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar.It is approximately $515 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is about $86 (5%) higher.
