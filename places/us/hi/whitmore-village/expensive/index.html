Home prices

The median home in Whitmore Village is valued at $567,500. This is roughly $34,220 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $350,000 (160%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $115,500 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Whitmore Village the median monthly rent is $1,295. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $408 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $233 (21%) higher, and is about $196 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
