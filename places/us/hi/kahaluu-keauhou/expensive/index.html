Home prices

The median home price in Kahaluu-Keauhou is $657,300. This is approximately $162,300 (32%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $439,800 (202%) higher, and compared to the city of Hilo it is approximately $328,100 (99%) higher.

Rents

Kahaluu-Keauhou's median rent is $1,719 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $429 (33%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $657 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Hilo it is roughly $674 (64%) higher.
