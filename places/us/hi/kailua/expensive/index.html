Compared to the surrounding areas, Kailua is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kailua is valued at $959,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $138,920 (16%) higher. It is approximately $741,700 (341%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $276,200 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median rent in Kailua is $2,500 a month. It is approximately $411 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $1,438 (135%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $1,009 (67%) higher.
