Compared to its surrounding areas, Kailua is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in Kailua is valued at $959,200. This is roughly $138,920 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $741,700 (341%) higher, and it is approximately $276,200 (40%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Kailua the median rent is $2,500 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $411 (19%) higher. It is about $1,438 (135%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $1,009 (67%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
