Home prices

Rents

Honomu's median monthly rent is $1,458. It is approximately $369 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $396 (37%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $413 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Hilo.
