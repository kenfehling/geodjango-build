Home prices

The value of the median home in Wahiawa is $590,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $24,100 (4%) higher.It is approximately $373,400 (171%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $92,100 (13%) lower compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wahiawa is $1,288. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $515 (28%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $226 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is about $203 (13%) lower.
