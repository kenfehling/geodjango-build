Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wahiawa is $590,900. It is approximately $24,100 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $373,400 (171%) higher, and is roughly $92,100 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wahiawa is $1,288. Compared to other places in this area it is about $515 (28%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $226 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is approximately $203 (13%) lower.
