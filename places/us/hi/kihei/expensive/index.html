Compared to its nearby areas, Kihei is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Kahului, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Kihei is $590,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $162,020 (21%) lower. It is about $372,700 (171%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $16,400 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Kahului.

Rents

Kihei's median monthly rent is $1,642. This is about $96 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $580 (54%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $296 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Kahului.
