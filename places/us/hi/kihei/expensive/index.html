Home prices

Kihei's median home price is $590,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $162,020 (21%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $372,700 (171%) higher, and is roughly $16,400 (2%) lower compared to the city of Kahului.

Rents

Kihei's median rent is $1,642 per month. This is roughly $96 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $580 (54%) higher, and it is roughly $296 (21%) higher compared to the city of Kahului.
