In comparison to other places in this area, Kihei is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Kahului, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Kihei costs $590,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $162,020 (21%) lower.It is about $372,700 (171%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $16,400 (2%) lower compared to the city of Kahului.

Rents

The median rent in Kihei is $1,642 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $96 (5%) lower.It is roughly $580 (54%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Kahului is roughly $296 (21%) higher.
