In comparison to its surrounding areas, Kihei is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Kahului.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kihei is $590,200. It is about $162,020 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $372,700 (171%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $16,400 (2%) lower compared to the city of Kahului.

Rents

In Kihei the median rent is $1,642 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $96 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $580 (54%) higher, and it is roughly $296 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Kahului.
