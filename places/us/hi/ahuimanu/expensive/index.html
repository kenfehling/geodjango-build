Home prices

The median home in Ahuimanu is valued at $708,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $169,920 (19%) lower. It is roughly $490,800 (225%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $25,300 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median rent in Ahuimanu is $2,032 per month. This is roughly $225 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $970 (91%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $541 (36%) higher.
