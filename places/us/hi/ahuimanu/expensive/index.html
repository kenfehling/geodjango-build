The median home in Ahuimanu costs $708,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $169,920 (19%) lower. It is roughly $490,800 (225%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $25,300 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents
