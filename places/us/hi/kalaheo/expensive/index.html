Home prices

The median home in Kalaheo is valued at $698,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $147,180 (26%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $481,100 (221%) higher, and compared to the city of Kapaa it is roughly $154,700 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kalaheo is $1,385. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $103 (6%) lower. It is roughly $323 (30%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Kapaa is is very similar.
