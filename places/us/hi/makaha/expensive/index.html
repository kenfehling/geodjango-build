Home prices

The median home in Makaha costs $404,900. It is approximately $18,480 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $187,400 (86%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is approximately $278,100 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Makaha is $1,467 per month. This is about $161 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $405 (38%) higher, and it is approximately $24 (1%) lower compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
