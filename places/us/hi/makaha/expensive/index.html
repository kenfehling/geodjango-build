Home prices

The median home in Makaha is valued at $404,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $18,480 (4%) lower. It is roughly $187,400 (86%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is about $278,100 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Makaha the median monthly rent is $1,467. This is approximately $161 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $405 (38%) higher, and is about $24 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
