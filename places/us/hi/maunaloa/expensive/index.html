Home prices

The median home in Maunaloa costs $248,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $102,220 (29%) lower. It is approximately $30,800 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $434,700 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Maunaloa is $650 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $441 (40%) lower. It is roughly $412 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $841 (56%) lower.
