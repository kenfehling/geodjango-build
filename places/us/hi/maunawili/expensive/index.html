Home prices

The median home price in Maunawili is $1,080,000. This is about $305,800 (39%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $862,500 (396%) higher, and is about $397,000 (58%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Maunawili the median rent is $3,250 a month. It is roughly $1,401 (75%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $2,188 (206%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is about $1,759 (117%) higher.
