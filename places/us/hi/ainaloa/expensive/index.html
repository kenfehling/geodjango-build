In comparison to other places in this area, Ainaloa is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Hilo however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ainaloa is valued at $167,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $37,940 (18%) lower. It is about $50,000 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $161,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Hilo.

Rents

Ainaloa's median monthly rent is $1,147. It is about $74 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $85 (8%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $102 (9%) higher compared to the city of Hilo.
