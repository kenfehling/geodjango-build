Home prices

Ainaloa's median home value is $167,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $37,940 (18%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $50,000 (22%) lower, and it is about $161,700 (49%) lower compared to the city of Hilo.

Rents

The median rent in Ainaloa is $1,147 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $74 (6%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $85 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Hilo it is about $102 (9%) higher.
