In comparison to nearby areas, East Honolulu is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Honolulu is valued at $959,800. This is about $161,560 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $742,300 (341%) higher, and is approximately $276,800 (40%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Honolulu is $2,666. It is approximately $700 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,604 (151%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $1,175 (78%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
