Compared to nearby areas, East Honolulu is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in East Honolulu costs $959,800. This is approximately $161,560 (20%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $742,300 (341%) higher, and it is approximately $276,800 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In East Honolulu the median monthly rent is $2,666. It is roughly $700 (35%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,604 (151%) higher, and it is about $1,175 (78%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
