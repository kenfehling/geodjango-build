Home prices

The value of the median home in Kalihiwai is $962,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $264,440 (37%) higher. It is about $745,000 (342%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $418,600 (76%) higher compared to the city of Kapaa.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kalihiwai is $1,366. This is approximately $88 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $304 (28%) higher, and compared to the city of Kapaa it is approximately $23 (1%) lower.
