Compared to the surrounding areas, Kaneohe Station is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kaneohe Station costs $1,069,400. This is roughly $187,280 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $851,900 (391%) higher, and is approximately $386,400 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Kaneohe Station the median monthly rent is $3,118. It is approximately $753 (31%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $2,056 (193%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $1,627 (109%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
