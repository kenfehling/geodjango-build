Home prices

Kahaluu's median home value is $892,400. This is roughly $51,000 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $674,900 (310%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $209,400 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median rent in Kahaluu is $2,086 a month. It is about $160 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $1,024 (96%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $595 (39%) higher.
