Home prices

The median home cost in Royal Kunia is $624,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $40,160 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $407,400 (187%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu is approximately $58,100 (8%) lower.

Rents
