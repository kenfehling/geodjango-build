Home prices

The median home cost in Hauula is $669,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $118,500 (15%) lower. It is roughly $452,200 (207%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is about $13,300 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hauula is $1,896 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $294 (18%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $834 (78%) higher, and it is approximately $405 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
