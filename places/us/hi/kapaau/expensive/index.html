Compared to the surrounding areas, Kapaau is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Hilo, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Kapaau is $368,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $115,320 (23%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $151,300 (69%) higher, and it is roughly $39,600 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Hilo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kapaau is $1,102. This is roughly $269 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $40 (3%) higher, and is about $57 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Hilo.
