Compared to the surrounding areas, Waialua is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in Waialua costs $687,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $122,240 (21%) higher. It is roughly $470,000 (216%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Waialua the median rent is $1,333 per month. This is roughly $191 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $271 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $158 (10%) lower.
