Compared to other places in this area, Waialua is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Urban Honolulu however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Waialua is $687,500. It is approximately $122,240 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $470,000 (216%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is is very similar.

Rents

Waialua's median rent is $1,333 a month. This is about $191 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $271 (25%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $158 (10%) lower.
