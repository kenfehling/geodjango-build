Home prices

The median home in Kawela Bay costs $650,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $142,120 (17%) lower. It is approximately $433,200 (199%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $32,300 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents
