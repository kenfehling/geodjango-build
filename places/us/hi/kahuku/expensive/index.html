Home prices

Kahuku's median home cost is $598,300. This is approximately $182,360 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $380,800 (175%) higher, and is roughly $84,700 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Kahuku the median monthly rent is $1,044. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $759 (42%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $18 (1%) lower, and it is approximately $447 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
