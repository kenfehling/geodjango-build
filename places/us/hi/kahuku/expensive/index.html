Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kahuku is $1,044. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $759 (42%) lower. It is roughly $18 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $447 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
