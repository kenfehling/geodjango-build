Home prices

The median home value in Kilauea is $725,100. It is roughly $20,440 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $507,600 (233%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa it is roughly $181,200 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Kilauea the median rent is $1,958 a month. It is approximately $623 (46%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $896 (84%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa is roughly $569 (40%) higher.
