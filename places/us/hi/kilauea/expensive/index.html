Home prices

The median home in Kilauea is valued at $725,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $230,440 (24%) lower.It is approximately $507,600 (233%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Kapaa it is roughly $181,200 (33%) higher.

Rents

Kilauea's median rent is $1,958 a month. It is roughly $576 (41%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $896 (84%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $569 (40%) higher compared to the city of Kapaa.
