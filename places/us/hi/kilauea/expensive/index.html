Compared to the surrounding areas, Kilauea is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Kapaa, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kilauea is $725,100. This is about $230,440 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $507,600 (233%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa is approximately $181,200 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kilauea is $1,958 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $576 (41%) higher.It is roughly $896 (84%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa it is approximately $569 (40%) higher.
