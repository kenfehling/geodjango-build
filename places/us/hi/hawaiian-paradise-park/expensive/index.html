Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hawaiian Paradise Park is $303,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $70,940 (30%) higher. It is approximately $85,800 (39%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Hilo it is approximately $25,900 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Hawaiian Paradise Park the median monthly rent is $1,306. This is about $158 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $244 (22%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $261 (24%) higher compared to the city of Hilo.
