Compared to its surrounding areas, Mililani Mauka is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Urban Honolulu however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Mililani Mauka is $691,200. This is roughly $144,460 (26%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $473,700 (217%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is approximately $8,200 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mililani Mauka is $2,226 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $610 (37%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $1,164 (109%) higher, and is roughly $735 (49%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
