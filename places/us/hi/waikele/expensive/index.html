Compared to the surrounding areas, Waikele is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waikele is valued at $508,000. This is approximately $105,560 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $290,500 (133%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $175,000 (25%) lower compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median rent in Waikele is $2,317 per month. It is roughly $579 (33%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,255 (118%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is about $826 (55%) higher.
