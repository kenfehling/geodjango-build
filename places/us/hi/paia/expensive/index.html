In comparison to the surrounding areas, Paia is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Kihei, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Paia is valued at $695,400. This is roughly $50,040 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $477,900 (219%) higher, and is about $105,200 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Paia is $2,210. This is about $593 (36%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,148 (108%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $568 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.
