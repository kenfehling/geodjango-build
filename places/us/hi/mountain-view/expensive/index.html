Home prices

The median home in Mountain View is valued at $197,200. It is about $23,780 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $20,300 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $132,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Hilo.

Rents

The median rent in Mountain View is $976 per month. It is roughly $22 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $86 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Hilo is roughly $69 (6%) lower.
