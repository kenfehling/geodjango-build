Compared to its surrounding areas, Mountain View is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Hilo however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mountain View is $197,200. This is roughly $23,780 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $20,300 (9%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $132,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Hilo.

Rents

The median rent in Mountain View is $976 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $22 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $86 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Hilo it is about $69 (6%) lower.
