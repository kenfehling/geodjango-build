Home prices

The price of the median home in Mountain View is $197,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $23,780 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $20,300 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Hilo it is about $132,000 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Mountain View the median rent is $976 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $22 (2%) lower.It is roughly $86 (8%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $69 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Hilo.
