Home prices

The median home cost in Princeville is $851,200. It is about $130,880 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $633,700 (291%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $307,300 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Kapaa.

Rents

Princeville's median monthly rent is $1,594. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $186 (13%) higher. It is approximately $532 (50%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Kapaa is approximately $205 (14%) higher.
