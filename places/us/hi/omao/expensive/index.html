Home prices

Omao's median home cost is $700,900. It is about $48,180 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $483,400 (222%) higher, and is approximately $157,000 (28%) higher compared to the city of Kapaa.

Rents

In Omao the median monthly rent is $1,850. It is roughly $476 (34%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $788 (74%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa it is about $461 (33%) higher.
