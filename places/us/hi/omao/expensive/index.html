Home prices

The median home price in Omao is $700,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $48,180 (7%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $483,400 (222%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa is approximately $157,000 (28%) higher.

Rents

Omao's median rent is $1,850 a month. It is approximately $476 (34%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $788 (74%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Kapaa it is about $461 (33%) higher.
