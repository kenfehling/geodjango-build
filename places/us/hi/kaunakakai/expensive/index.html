The median home cost in Kaunakakai is $307,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $47,120 (13%) lower. It is approximately $89,800 (41%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is about $375,700 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kaunakakai is $836 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $398 (32%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $226 (21%) lower, and is roughly $655 (43%) lower compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
