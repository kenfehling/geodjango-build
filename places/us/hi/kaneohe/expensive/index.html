In comparison to its nearby areas, Kaneohe is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Urban Honolulu, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Kaneohe is $770,700. It is approximately $171,160 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $553,200 (254%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is approximately $87,700 (12%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kaneohe is $1,957 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $640 (24%) lower. It is roughly $895 (84%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $466 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
