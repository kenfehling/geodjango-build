The median home value in Fern Forest is $127,500. It is approximately $59,860 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $90,000 (41%) lower, and it is about $201,700 (61%) lower compared to the city of Hilo.

Rents

Fern Forest's median monthly rent is $838. It is roughly $187 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Hilo is roughly $207 (19%) lower.
