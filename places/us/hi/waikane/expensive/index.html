Home prices

The median home price in Waikane is $833,300. This is about $93,280 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $615,800 (283%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $150,300 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

Waikane's median monthly rent is $1,768. It is approximately $163 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $706 (66%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $277 (18%) higher.
