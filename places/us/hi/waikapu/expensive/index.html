In comparison to nearby areas, Waikapu is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Kihei, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waikapu is valued at $625,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $114,380 (15%) lower.It is about $408,400 (187%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Kihei it is about $35,700 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waikapu is $2,250. This is roughly $590 (35%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,188 (111%) higher, and it is approximately $608 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.
