Compared to its surrounding areas, Waikapu is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Kihei, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waikapu costs $625,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $114,380 (15%) lower.It is roughly $408,400 (187%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $35,700 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.

Rents

The median rent in Waikapu is $2,250 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $590 (35%) higher.It is roughly $1,188 (111%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Kihei it is roughly $608 (37%) higher.
