In comparison to other places in this area, Waikapu is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Kihei, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Waikapu's median home cost is $625,900. This is roughly $114,380 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $408,400 (187%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Kihei is about $35,700 (6%) higher.

Rents

Waikapu's median rent is $2,250 per month. This is about $590 (35%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,188 (111%) higher, and it is roughly $608 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.
