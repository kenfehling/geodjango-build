Compared to nearby areas, Waikapu is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Kihei, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Waikapu is $625,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $114,380 (15%) lower. It is about $408,400 (187%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $35,700 (6%) higher compared to the city of Kihei.

Rents

In Waikapu the median monthly rent is $2,250. It is approximately $590 (35%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $1,188 (111%) higher, and compared to the city of Kihei it is approximately $608 (37%) higher.
