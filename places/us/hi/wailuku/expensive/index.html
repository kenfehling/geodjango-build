Home prices

The median home in Wailuku is valued at $544,900. It is approximately $22,080 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $327,400 (150%) higher, and is approximately $45,300 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Kihei.

Rents

The median rent in Wailuku is $1,285 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $466 (26%) lower. It is about $223 (20%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Kihei it is about $357 (21%) lower.
