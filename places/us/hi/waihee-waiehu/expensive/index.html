Home prices

The cost of the median home in Waihee-Waiehu is $516,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $56,280 (9%) lower. It is approximately $298,900 (137%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Kihei is approximately $73,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Waihee-Waiehu the median rent is $1,347 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $392 (22%) lower. It is approximately $285 (26%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $295 (17%) lower compared to the city of Kihei.
