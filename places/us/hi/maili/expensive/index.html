In comparison to the surrounding areas, Maili is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in Maili is valued at $464,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $59,120 (14%) higher. It is roughly $247,400 (113%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $218,100 (31%) lower.

Rents

Maili's median rent is $2,119 a month. This is roughly $480 (29%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $1,057 (99%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is approximately $628 (42%) higher.
