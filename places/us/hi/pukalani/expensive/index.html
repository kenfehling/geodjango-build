Home prices

Pukalani's median home cost is $674,300. It is approximately $95,000 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $456,800 (210%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Kihei it is approximately $84,100 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Pukalani is $1,802 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $97 (5%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $740 (69%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kihei it is approximately $160 (9%) higher.
