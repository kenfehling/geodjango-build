Home prices

The median home in Lawai is valued at $589,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $85,860 (12%) lower.It is roughly $371,700 (170%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $45,300 (8%) higher compared to the city of Kapaa.

Rents

Lawai's median monthly rent is $1,714. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $313 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $652 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa it is approximately $325 (23%) higher.
