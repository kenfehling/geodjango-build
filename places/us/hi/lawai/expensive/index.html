Home prices

The median home price in Lawai is $589,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $85,860 (12%) lower. It is roughly $371,700 (170%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $45,300 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Kapaa.

Rents

The median rent in Lawai is $1,714 a month. This is approximately $313 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $652 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Kapaa is about $325 (23%) higher.
