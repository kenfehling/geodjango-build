Home prices

Lawai's median home value is $589,200. This is approximately $85,860 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $371,700 (170%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Kapaa is about $45,300 (8%) higher.

Rents
