Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lawai is $589,200. It is roughly $85,860 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $371,700 (170%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Kapaa is approximately $45,300 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Lawai the median monthly rent is $1,714. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $313 (22%) higher. It is approximately $652 (61%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $325 (23%) higher compared to the city of Kapaa.
