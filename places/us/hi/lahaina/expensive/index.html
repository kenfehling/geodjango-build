In comparison to its nearby areas, Lahaina is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lahaina is $686,800. It is approximately $285,860 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $469,300 (215%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is is very similar.

Rents

Lahaina's median rent is $1,333 a month. This is about $966 (42%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $271 (25%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is about $158 (10%) lower.
