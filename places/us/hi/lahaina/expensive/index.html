Compared to other places in this area, Lahaina is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lahaina is $686,800. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $285,860 (29%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $469,300 (215%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is is very similar.

Rents

In Lahaina the median rent is $1,333 per month. This is about $966 (42%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $271 (25%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu is about $158 (10%) lower.
