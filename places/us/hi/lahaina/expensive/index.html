In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lahaina is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lahaina is $686,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $285,860 (29%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $469,300 (215%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is is very similar.

Rents

In Lahaina the median rent is $1,333 a month. This is about $966 (42%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $271 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu is about $158 (10%) lower.
