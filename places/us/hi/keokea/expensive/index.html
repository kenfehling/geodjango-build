Compared to its surrounding areas, Keokea is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Kihei, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Keokea is $655,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $165,800 (20%) lower.It is about $437,900 (201%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Kihei is approximately $65,200 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Keokea is $1,512 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $205 (11%) lower.It is about $450 (42%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $130 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Kihei.
