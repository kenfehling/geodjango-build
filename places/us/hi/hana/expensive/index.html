Home prices

The median home in Hana is valued at $428,200. This is approximately $385,980 (47%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $210,700 (96%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $162,000 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Kihei.

Rents

Hana's median rent is $1,050 a month. This is roughly $618 (37%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $12 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Kihei it is approximately $592 (36%) lower.
