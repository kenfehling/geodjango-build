Home prices

The median home price in Hawaiian Beaches is $181,900. This is roughly $38,480 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $35,600 (16%) lower, and is approximately $147,300 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Hilo.

Rents

Hawaiian Beaches's median rent is $1,250 a month. It is about $142 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $188 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Hilo it is roughly $205 (19%) higher.
