Home prices

The median home in Hawaiian Beaches costs $181,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $38,480 (17%) lower.It is roughly $35,600 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Hilo is about $147,300 (44%) lower.

Rents
