Home prices

The price of the median home in Hawi is $362,400. This is roughly $139,500 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $144,900 (66%) higher, and compared to the city of Hilo it is about $33,200 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Hawi the median rent is $1,144 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $311 (21%) lower. It is roughly $82 (7%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $99 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Hilo.
