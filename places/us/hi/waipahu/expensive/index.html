In comparison to the surrounding areas, Waipahu is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Waipahu is $626,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $36,760 (6%) higher.It is approximately $409,100 (188%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is approximately $56,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Waipahu is $1,343. It is roughly $590 (30%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $281 (26%) higher, and is roughly $148 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
