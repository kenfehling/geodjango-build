Compared to other places in this area, Waipahu is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Urban Honoluluhowever, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Waipahu's median home value is $626,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $36,760 (6%) higher. It is about $409,100 (188%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $56,400 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

Waipahu's median rent is $1,343 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $590 (30%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $281 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $148 (9%) lower.
