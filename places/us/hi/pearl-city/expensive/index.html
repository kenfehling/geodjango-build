In comparison to the surrounding areas, Pearl City is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in Pearl City costs $667,000. It is roughly $46,680 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $449,500 (206%) higher, and it is about $16,000 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

Pearl City's median monthly rent is $1,908. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $97 (4%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $846 (79%) higher, and is approximately $417 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.
