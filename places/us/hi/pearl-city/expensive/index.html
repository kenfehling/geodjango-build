In comparison to other places in this area, Pearl City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in Pearl City is valued at $667,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $46,680 (7%) higher. It is roughly $449,500 (206%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $16,000 (2%) lower compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Pearl City the median monthly rent is $1,908. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $97 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $846 (79%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $417 (27%) higher.
