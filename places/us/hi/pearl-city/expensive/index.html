Home prices

The price of the median home in Pearl City is $667,000. It is approximately $46,680 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $449,500 (206%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is about $16,000 (2%) lower.

Rents

In Pearl City the median rent is $1,908 a month. This is about $97 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $846 (79%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu is about $417 (27%) higher.
