Compared to nearby areas, Pearl City is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Urban Honolulu, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Pearl City costs $667,000. It is approximately $46,680 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $449,500 (206%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $16,000 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Pearl City the median monthly rent is $1,908. This is approximately $97 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $846 (79%) higher, and is about $417 (27%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
