Home prices

The median home in Olinda is valued at $951,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $218,440 (29%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $734,400 (337%) higher, and is approximately $361,700 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.

Rents

In Olinda the median monthly rent is $1,859. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $225 (13%) higher. It is about $797 (75%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $217 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Kihei.
