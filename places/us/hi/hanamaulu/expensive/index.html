Home prices

The median home in Hanamaulu costs $469,500. It is roughly $76,560 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $252,000 (115%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Makaha is about $64,600 (15%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hanamaulu is $1,146. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $439 (27%) lower. It is about $84 (7%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $321 (21%) lower compared to the city of Makaha.
