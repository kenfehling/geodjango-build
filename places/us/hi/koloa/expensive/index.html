Home prices

Koloa's median home cost is $591,500. This is approximately $87,360 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $374,000 (171%) higher, and it is approximately $47,600 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Kapaa.

Rents
