Compared to its surrounding areas, Halawa is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Urban Honolulu, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Halawa is $785,200. This is approximately $164,800 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $567,700 (261%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $102,200 (14%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Halawa the median rent is $1,761 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $78 (4%) lower. It is about $699 (65%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $270 (18%) higher.
