In comparison to its surrounding areas, Halawa is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Urban Honolulu, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Halawa costs $785,200. It is about $164,800 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $567,700 (261%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $102,200 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

In Halawa the median rent is $1,761 per month. This is approximately $78 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $699 (65%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $270 (18%) higher.
