Compared to the surrounding areas, Halawa is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Home prices

The median home in Halawa is valued at $785,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $164,800 (26%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $567,700 (261%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $102,200 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Halawa is $1,761. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $78 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $699 (65%) higher, and is approximately $270 (18%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
