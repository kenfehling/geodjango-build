Home prices

The median home in Halawa costs $785,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $164,800 (26%) higher.It is roughly $567,700 (261%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $102,200 (14%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.

Rents

The median rent in Halawa is $1,761 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $78 (4%) lower.It is about $699 (65%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu is about $270 (18%) higher.
