Home prices

The median home price in Kaanapali is $1,144,800. It is approximately $285,640 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $927,300 (426%) higher, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu is roughly $461,800 (67%) higher.

Rents

Kaanapali's median rent is $2,681 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $832 (45%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,619 (152%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Urban Honolulu it is roughly $1,190 (79%) higher.
