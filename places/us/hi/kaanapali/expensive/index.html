The median home cost in Kaanapali is $1,144,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $285,640 (33%) higher. It is approximately $927,300 (426%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Urban Honolulu it is approximately $461,800 (67%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Kaanapali is $2,681 per month. This is roughly $832 (45%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,619 (152%) higher, and it is about $1,190 (79%) higher compared to the city of Urban Honolulu.
