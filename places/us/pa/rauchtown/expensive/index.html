Home prices

The median home in Rauchtown costs $152,500. It is approximately $31,420 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $65,000 (29%) lower, and is about $71,700 (88%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Rauchtown the median monthly rent is $700. It is about $116 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $362 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $156 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
