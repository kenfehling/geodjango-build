Home prices

The cost of the median home in Rauchtown is $152,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $31,420 (25%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $65,000 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $71,700 (88%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rauchtown is $700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $116 (14%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and is approximately $156 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
