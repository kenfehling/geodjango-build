Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pennside is $153,000. This is roughly $16,600 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $64,500 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $10,000 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pennside is $960 per month. It is about $162 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $102 (9%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $82 (7%) lower.
