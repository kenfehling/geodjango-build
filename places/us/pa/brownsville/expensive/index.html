Home prices

Brownsville's median home cost is $57,900. This is approximately $23,440 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $159,600 (73%) lower, and it is about $67,100 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownsville is $634. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $97 (13%) lower. It is about $428 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $324 (33%) lower.
