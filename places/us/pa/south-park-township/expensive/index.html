The median home in South Park Township costs $167,000. It is about $3,800 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $50,500 (23%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $42,000 (33%) higher.

Rents

South Park Township's median rent is $1,000 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $120 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $62 (5%) lower, and is approximately $42 (4%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
