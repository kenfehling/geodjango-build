Home prices

The median home in Bellefonte is valued at $191,600. It is about $10,020 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $25,900 (11%) lower, and is roughly $110,800 (137%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Bellefonte is $807 a month. It is approximately $120 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $255 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $49 (5%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
