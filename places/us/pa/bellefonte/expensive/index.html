Home prices

The median home in Bellefonte is valued at $191,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $10,020 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $25,900 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $110,800 (137%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Bellefonte the median monthly rent is $807. It is roughly $120 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $255 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $49 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
