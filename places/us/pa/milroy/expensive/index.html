Compared to the surrounding areas, Milroy is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

Milroy's median home value is $94,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $20,440 (17%) lower. It is about $122,600 (56%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $14,100 (17%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Milroy is $646 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $63 (8%) lower. It is about $416 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is about $210 (24%) lower.
