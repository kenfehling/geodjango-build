Home prices

The median home in Milroy is valued at $94,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $20,440 (17%) lower. It is about $122,600 (56%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $14,100 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Milroy's median monthly rent is $646. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $63 (8%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $416 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $210 (24%) lower.
