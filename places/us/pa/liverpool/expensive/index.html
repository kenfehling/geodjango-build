Home prices

The median home in Liverpool costs $127,700. This is approximately $10,840 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $89,800 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is about $32,400 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Liverpool is $689 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $19 (2%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $373 (35%) lower, and it is about $384 (35%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
