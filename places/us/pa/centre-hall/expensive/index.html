Rents

Centre Hall's median monthly rent is $867. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $48 (5%) lower. It is roughly $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $11 (1%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
