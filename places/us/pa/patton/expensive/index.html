Rents

In Patton the median monthly rent is $388. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $207 (34%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $674 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $570 (59%) lower.
