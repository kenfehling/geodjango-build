Compared to its nearby areas, Houserville is a bit less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The median home price in Houserville is $225,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $60,240 (21%) lower. It is roughly $8,200 (3%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $144,900 (179%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Houserville the median monthly rent is $1,257. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $166 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $195 (18%) higher, and is about $401 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
