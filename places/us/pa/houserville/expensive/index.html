Compared to nearby areas, Houserville is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Houserville is valued at $225,700. This is approximately $60,240 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $8,200 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $144,900 (179%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Houserville the median rent is $1,257 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $166 (15%) higher. It is approximately $195 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is about $401 (46%) higher.
