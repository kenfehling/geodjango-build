Home prices

The median home in Stroudsburg costs $161,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $11,340 (6%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $56,000 (25%) lower, and is roughly $444,500 (73%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Stroudsburg's median monthly rent is $1,006. It is about $93 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $56 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $437 (30%) lower.
