Home prices

Doylestown's median home price is $408,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $71,420 (21%) higher. It is approximately $190,600 (87%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $197,900 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Doylestown the median monthly rent is $1,418. This is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $356 (33%) higher, and is roughly $25 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
