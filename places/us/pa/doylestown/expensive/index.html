Compared to other places in this area, Doylestown is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Doylestown's median home price is $408,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $71,420 (21%) higher. It is about $190,600 (87%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $197,900 (32%) lower.

Rents

Doylestown's median monthly rent is $1,418. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the entire country it is about $356 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $25 (1%) lower.
