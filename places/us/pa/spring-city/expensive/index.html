Home prices

The median home in Spring City is valued at $181,300. This is roughly $100,580 (35%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $18,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Spring City is $891. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $460 (34%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $171 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $151 (14%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
