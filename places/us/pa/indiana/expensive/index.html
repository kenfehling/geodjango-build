Home prices

The median home in Indiana is valued at $113,900. It is roughly $17,780 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $103,600 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $11,100 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Indiana is $741. It is about $44 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $321 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $217 (22%) lower.
