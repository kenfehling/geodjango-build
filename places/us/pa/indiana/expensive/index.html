In comparison to its nearby areas, Indiana is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Indiana is $113,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $17,780 (18%) higher. It is approximately $103,600 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $11,100 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Indiana the median rent is $741 a month. This is roughly $44 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $321 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $217 (22%) lower.
