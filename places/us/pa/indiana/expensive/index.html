Compared to its surrounding areas, Indiana is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

Indiana's median home cost is $113,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $17,780 (18%) higher.It is roughly $103,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $11,100 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Indiana the median monthly rent is $741. It is about $44 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $321 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $217 (22%) lower.
