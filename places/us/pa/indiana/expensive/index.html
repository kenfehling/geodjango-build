Home prices

The median home in Indiana is valued at $113,900. It is roughly $17,780 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $103,600 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $11,100 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Indiana is $741 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $44 (6%) higher. It is about $321 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $217 (22%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
