Home prices

The median home price in Fayette City is $69,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $19,680 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $147,800 (67%) lower, and is about $55,300 (44%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fayette City is $763. It is about $79 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $299 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $195 (20%) lower.
