Compared to the surrounding areas, Houston is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Pittsburghhowever, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Houston is $140,500. It is about $78,360 (35%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $77,000 (35%) lower, and is roughly $15,500 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Houston the median rent is $709 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $426 (37%) lower. It is roughly $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $249 (25%) lower.
