Compared to its surrounding areas, Houston is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Houston is valued at $140,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $78,360 (35%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $77,000 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $15,500 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Houston the median rent is $709 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $426 (37%) lower.It is roughly $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $249 (25%) lower.
