Compared to nearby areas, Houston is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home cost in Houston is $140,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $78,360 (35%) lower.It is about $77,000 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $15,500 (12%) higher.

Rents

Houston's median rent is $709 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $426 (37%) lower.It is about $353 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $249 (25%) lower.
