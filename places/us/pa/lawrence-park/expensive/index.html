In comparison to other places in this area, Lawrence Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Erie however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Lawrence Park's median home cost is $104,900. It is approximately $31,280 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $112,600 (51%) lower, and is about $15,800 (17%) higher compared to the city of Erie.

Rents

The median rent in Lawrence Park is $896 a month. This is about $69 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $166 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Erie is about $186 (26%) higher.
