Home prices

The median home in Lawrence Park costs $104,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $31,280 (22%) lower.It is approximately $112,600 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $15,800 (17%) higher compared to the city of Erie.

Rents

In Lawrence Park the median rent is $896 a month. It is approximately $69 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $166 (15%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Erie it is roughly $186 (26%) higher.
