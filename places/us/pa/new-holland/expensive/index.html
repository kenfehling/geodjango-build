The median home in New Holland is valued at $203,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $48,140 (19%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $14,400 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $40,100 (24%) higher.

Rents

New Holland's median monthly rent is $957. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $31 (3%) lower. It is about $105 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $85 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
