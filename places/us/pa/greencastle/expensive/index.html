Home prices

The median home price in Greencastle is $172,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $22,280 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $44,800 (20%) lower, and it is about $428,800 (71%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greencastle is $866. This is approximately $189 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $196 (18%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $675 (43%) lower.
