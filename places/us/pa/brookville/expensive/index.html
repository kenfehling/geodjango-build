In comparison to its surrounding areas, Brookville is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home cost in Brookville is $98,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $18,520 (23%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $119,300 (54%) lower, and it is about $26,800 (21%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Brookville's median monthly rent is $667. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $28 (4%) higher. It is about $395 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $291 (30%) lower.
