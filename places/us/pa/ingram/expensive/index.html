Compared to its surrounding areas, Ingram is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Ingram costs $105,300. This is about $61,160 (36%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $112,200 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $19,700 (15%) lower.

Rents

Ingram's median monthly rent is $823. It is approximately $346 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $239 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $135 (14%) lower.
