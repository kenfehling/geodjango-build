Home prices

The price of the median home in Halfway House is $223,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $44,700 (25%) higher. It is about $5,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $60,400 (37%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Halfway House is $1,159 a month. It is approximately $112 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $97 (9%) higher, and is approximately $117 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
