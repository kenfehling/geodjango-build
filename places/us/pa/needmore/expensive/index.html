Home prices

The median home in Needmore is valued at $150,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $17,340 (12%) higher. It is approximately $66,700 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Gaithersburg is about $241,400 (61%) lower.

Rents

Needmore's median rent is $717 per month. This is approximately $14 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $345 (32%) lower, and it is about $1,023 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Gaithersburg.
