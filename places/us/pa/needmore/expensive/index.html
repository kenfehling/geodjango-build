In comparison to the surrounding areas, Needmore is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Gaithersburg, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Needmore is $150,800. This is about $17,340 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $66,700 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Gaithersburg is about $241,400 (61%) lower.

Rents

In Needmore the median monthly rent is $717. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $14 (2%) higher. It is roughly $345 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $1,023 (58%) lower compared to the city of Gaithersburg.
