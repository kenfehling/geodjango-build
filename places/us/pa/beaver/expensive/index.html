Home prices

The median home cost in Beaver is $190,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $88,980 (87%) higher. It is about $26,700 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $65,800 (52%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Beaver's median rent is $762 per month. It is approximately $89 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $300 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $196 (20%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
