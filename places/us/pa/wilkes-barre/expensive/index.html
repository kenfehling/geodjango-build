Compared to nearby areas, Wilkes-Barre is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wilkes-Barre is $75,100. Compared to nearby areas it is about $25,080 (25%) lower.It is roughly $142,400 (65%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Allentown it is approximately $56,200 (42%) lower.

Rents

Wilkes-Barre's median monthly rent is $766. It is roughly $20 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $296 (27%) lower, and is about $238 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
