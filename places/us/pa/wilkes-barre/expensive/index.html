Home prices

Rents

In Wilkes-Barre the median rent is $766 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $20 (2%) lower.It is approximately $296 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $238 (23%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
