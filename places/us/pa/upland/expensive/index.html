Compared to its surrounding areas, Upland is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home value in Upland is $114,400. It is roughly $25,560 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $103,100 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $48,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Upland the median monthly rent is $769. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $301 (28%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $273 (26%) lower.
