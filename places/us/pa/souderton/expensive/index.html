Home prices

The value of the median home in Souderton is $227,900. This is about $33,840 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $10,400 (4%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $378,100 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Souderton the median rent is $993 per month. It is roughly $173 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $69 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $450 (31%) lower.
