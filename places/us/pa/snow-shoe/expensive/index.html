Home prices

The median home in Snow Shoe is valued at $122,500. This is about $15,920 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $95,000 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $41,700 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Snow Shoe the median rent is $683 per month. This is about $86 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $379 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is about $173 (20%) lower.
