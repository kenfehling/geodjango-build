Home prices

The median home in New Milford is valued at $129,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $28,940 (28%) higher.It is about $88,200 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $34,900 (36%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In New Milford the median rent is $775 a month. This is roughly $68 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $39 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
