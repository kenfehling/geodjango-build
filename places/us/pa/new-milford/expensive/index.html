Compared to its surrounding areas, New Milford is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in New Milford is $129,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $28,940 (28%) higher. It is about $88,200 (40%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $34,900 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Milford is $775. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $68 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $287 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $39 (4%) lower.
