Compared to its surrounding areas, Morgantown is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Morgantown costs $206,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $13,240 (6%) lower.It is roughly $10,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $43,600 (26%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Morgantown's median rent is $791 per month. This is about $101 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $271 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $251 (24%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
