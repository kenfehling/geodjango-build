Compared to its surrounding areas, Morgantown is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Morgantown's median home cost is $206,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $13,240 (6%) lower. It is about $10,900 (5%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $43,600 (26%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Morgantown's median rent is $791 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $101 (11%) lower. It is approximately $271 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $251 (24%) lower.
