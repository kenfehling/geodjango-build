Home prices

Morgantown's median home cost is $206,600. This is approximately $13,240 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $10,900 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $43,600 (26%) higher.

Rents

In Morgantown the median rent is $791 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $101 (11%) lower. It is roughly $271 (25%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $251 (24%) lower.
