Home prices

The median home in Mount Morris costs $150,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $41,900 (38%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $66,600 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $25,900 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Morris is $842. It is approximately $80 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $220 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $116 (12%) lower.
