Home prices

The median home value in Mount Morris is $150,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $41,900 (38%) higher. It is about $66,600 (30%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $25,900 (20%) higher.

Rents

Mount Morris's median monthly rent is $842. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $80 (10%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $220 (20%) lower, and it is roughly $116 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
