Home prices

The median home cost in Turbotville is $148,400. It is about $15,280 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $69,100 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Reading it is roughly $75,200 (102%) higher.

Rents

Turbotville's median rent is $600 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $157 (20%) lower. It is about $462 (43%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Reading it is about $211 (26%) lower.
