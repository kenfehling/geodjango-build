Home prices

The median home in Lewisburg is valued at $200,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $58,020 (40%) higher.It is about $17,200 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Reading is about $127,100 (173%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lewisburg is $724. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $124 (14%) lower.It is about $338 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $87 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Reading.
