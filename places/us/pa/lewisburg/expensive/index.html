Home prices

Lewisburg's median home cost is $200,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $58,020 (40%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $17,200 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Reading is roughly $127,100 (173%) higher.

Rents

In Lewisburg the median monthly rent is $724. It is roughly $124 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $338 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reading it is about $87 (10%) lower.
