Home prices

The median home in Lewisburg is valued at $200,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $58,020 (40%) higher. It is approximately $17,200 (7%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Reading it is approximately $127,100 (173%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lewisburg is $724. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $124 (14%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $338 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Reading it is roughly $87 (10%) lower.
