Home prices

The median home in New Cumberland costs $169,700. This is about $35,020 (26%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $47,800 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $431,800 (71%) lower.

Rents

In New Cumberland the median monthly rent is $826. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $105 (11%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $236 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $715 (46%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
