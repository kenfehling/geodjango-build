Home prices

The median home price in Highland Park is $158,700. This is about $82,340 (107%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $58,800 (27%) lower, and it is about $77,900 (96%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Highland Park the median rent is $735 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $26 (3%) higher.It is roughly $327 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $121 (14%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
