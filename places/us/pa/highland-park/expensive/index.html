Home prices

Rents

In Highland Park the median monthly rent is $735. It is about $26 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $327 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $121 (14%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
