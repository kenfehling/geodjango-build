Home prices

Rents

In Beaver Falls the median rent is $631 per month. This is approximately $179 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $431 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $327 (34%) lower.
