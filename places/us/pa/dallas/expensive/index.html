Home prices

Dallas's median home cost is $174,000. This is about $13,440 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $43,500 (19%) lower, and is approximately $42,700 (32%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

In Dallas the median rent is $819 a month. It is approximately $36 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $243 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $185 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
