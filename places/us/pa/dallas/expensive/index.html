Compared to other places in this area, Dallas is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Allentownhowever, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dallas is valued at $174,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $13,440 (8%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $43,500 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown it is approximately $42,700 (32%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dallas is $819. This is roughly $36 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is about $185 (18%) lower.
