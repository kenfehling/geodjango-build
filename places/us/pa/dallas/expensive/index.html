In comparison to its nearby areas, Dallas is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Allentown, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Dallas is $174,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $13,440 (8%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $43,500 (19%) lower, and is roughly $42,700 (32%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Dallas's median rent is $819 a month. It is about $36 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $243 (22%) lower, and is approximately $185 (18%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
