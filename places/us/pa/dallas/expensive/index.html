In comparison to its nearby areas, Dallas is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Allentown however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Dallas's median home cost is $174,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $13,440 (8%) higher. It is roughly $43,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Allentown it is about $42,700 (32%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Dallas is $819 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $36 (4%) lower. It is approximately $243 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $185 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
