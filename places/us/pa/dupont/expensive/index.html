In comparison to other places in this area, Dupont is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Allentown, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dupont costs $106,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $20,620 (16%) lower.It is about $111,300 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $25,100 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dupont is $739. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $142 (16%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $323 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $265 (26%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
