Home prices

The median home in Dupont costs $106,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $20,620 (16%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $111,300 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is approximately $25,100 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Dupont the median rent is $739 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $142 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $323 (30%) lower, and is about $265 (26%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
