The price of the median home in Norwood is $167,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $6,100 (3%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $49,600 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $438,100 (72%) lower.

Rents

Norwood's median monthly rent is $982. This is about $138 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $80 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $461 (31%) lower.
