Home prices

The value of the median home in Bentleyville is $114,200. This is approximately $45,220 (65%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $103,300 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $10,800 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Bentleyville the median monthly rent is $498. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $279 (35%) lower. It is roughly $564 (53%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $460 (48%) lower.
