In comparison to its surrounding areas, Franklin is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Franklin is $38,400. It is roughly $11,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $179,100 (82%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $86,600 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Franklin is $500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $55 (9%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $562 (52%) lower, and is roughly $458 (47%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
