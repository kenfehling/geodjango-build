Home prices

The median home in Franklin is valued at $38,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $11,000 (22%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $179,100 (82%) lower, and it is approximately $86,600 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Franklin is $500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $55 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $562 (52%) lower, and is roughly $458 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
