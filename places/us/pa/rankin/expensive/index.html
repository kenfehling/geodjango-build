In comparison to nearby areas, Rankin is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Rankin is $49,100. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $21,600 (30%) lower. It is approximately $168,400 (77%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $75,900 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rankin is $613. It is about $75 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $449 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $345 (36%) lower.
