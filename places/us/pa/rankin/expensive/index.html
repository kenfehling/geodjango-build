Rents

Rankin's median rent is $613 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $75 (10%) lower.It is roughly $449 (42%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $345 (36%) lower.
