Home prices

The median home in Whitehall costs $162,600. It is about $27,760 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $54,900 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $37,600 (30%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Whitehall's median rent is $779 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $75 (8%) lower. It is roughly $283 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $179 (18%) lower.
