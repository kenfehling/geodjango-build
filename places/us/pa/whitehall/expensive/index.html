Home prices

The median home cost in Whitehall is $162,600. This is roughly $27,760 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $54,900 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $37,600 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Whitehall is $779. This is about $75 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $283 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $179 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
