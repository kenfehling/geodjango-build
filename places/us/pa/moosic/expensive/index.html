Compared to its surrounding areas, Moosic is significantly more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Allentown, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Moosic costs $166,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $43,220 (35%) higher. It is roughly $51,500 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Allentown is about $34,700 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Moosic is $746. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $30 (3%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $316 (29%) lower, and it is about $258 (25%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
