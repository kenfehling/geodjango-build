Compared to its surrounding areas, Towanda is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Towanda costs $130,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $19,760 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $87,500 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $35,600 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Towanda the median monthly rent is $800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $36 (4%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $262 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $14 (1%) lower.
