Home prices

Towanda's median home value is $130,000. This is about $19,760 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $87,500 (40%) lower, and it is about $35,600 (37%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Towanda's median rent is $800 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $36 (4%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $262 (24%) lower, and is roughly $14 (1%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
