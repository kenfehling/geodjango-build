Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hershey is $272,100. It is approximately $82,480 (43%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $54,600 (25%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is roughly $112,000 (69%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hershey is $1,058. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $96 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
