Compared to other places in this area, Hershey is a lot more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Baltimore, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hershey costs $272,100. This is approximately $82,480 (43%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $54,600 (25%) higher, and is about $112,000 (69%) higher compared to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

Hershey's median monthly rent is $1,058. It is approximately $96 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and is roughly $15 (1%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
