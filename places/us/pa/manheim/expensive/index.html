Home prices

Manheim's median home price is $155,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $46,480 (23%) lower.It is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $7,700 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Manheim the median monthly rent is $811. This is roughly $148 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $251 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $231 (22%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
