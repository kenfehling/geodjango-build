Home prices

The median home in Manheim is valued at $155,300. It is approximately $46,480 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $7,700 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Manheim the median rent is $811 a month. It is about $148 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $251 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $231 (22%) lower.
