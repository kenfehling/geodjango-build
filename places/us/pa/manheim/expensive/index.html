Home prices

The median home in Manheim costs $155,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $46,480 (23%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $62,200 (28%) lower, and it is about $7,700 (4%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Manheim's median rent is $811 per month. This is roughly $148 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $251 (23%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $231 (22%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
