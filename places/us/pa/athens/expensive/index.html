Home prices

The median home in Athens costs $125,400. It is roughly $14,000 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $92,100 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $31,000 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Athens the median monthly rent is $450. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $287 (38%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $612 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $364 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
