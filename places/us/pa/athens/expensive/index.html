Compared to the surrounding areas, Athens is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home in Athens costs $125,400. This is roughly $14,000 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $92,100 (42%) lower, and is roughly $31,000 (32%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Athens is $450. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $287 (38%) lower. It is approximately $612 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $364 (44%) lower.
