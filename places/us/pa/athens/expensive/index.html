Compared to other places in this area, Athens is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Athens is $125,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $14,000 (12%) higher.It is roughly $92,100 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $31,000 (32%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Athens is $450 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $287 (38%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $612 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $364 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
