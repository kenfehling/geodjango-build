In comparison to nearby areas, Athens is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Athens is $125,400. This is approximately $14,000 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $92,100 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is roughly $31,000 (32%) higher.

Rents

Athens's median monthly rent is $450. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $287 (38%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $612 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $364 (44%) lower.
