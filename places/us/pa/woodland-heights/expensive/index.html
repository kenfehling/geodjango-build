Home prices

The price of the median home in Woodland Heights is $107,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $31,940 (42%) higher. It is approximately $110,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $17,700 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Woodland Heights is $670 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is is very similar. It is about $392 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $288 (30%) lower.
