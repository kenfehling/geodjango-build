Home prices

Rents

In Georgetown the median rent is $853 per month. It is approximately $109 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $209 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $151 (15%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
