Compared to the surrounding areas, Georgetown is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in Georgetown is valued at $84,700. This is about $5,460 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $132,800 (61%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $46,600 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Georgetown's median monthly rent is $853. It is roughly $109 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $209 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $151 (15%) lower.
