Home prices

Rents

Fairdale's median monthly rent is $392. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $281 (41%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $670 (63%) lower, and is about $566 (59%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
