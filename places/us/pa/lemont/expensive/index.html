Home prices

The median home in Lemont is valued at $257,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $22,680 (8%) lower. It is approximately $39,500 (18%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is approximately $176,200 (218%) higher.

Rents

In Lemont the median monthly rent is $891. It is roughly $274 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $171 (16%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $35 (4%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
