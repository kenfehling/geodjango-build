Home prices

The median home in Lemont costs $257,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $22,680 (8%) lower. It is approximately $39,500 (18%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $176,200 (218%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Lemont the median rent is $891 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $274 (23%) lower. It is roughly $171 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is about $35 (4%) higher.
