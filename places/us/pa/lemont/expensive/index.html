In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lemont is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The median home in Lemont costs $257,000. It is approximately $22,680 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $39,500 (18%) higher, and it is about $176,200 (218%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Lemont the median rent is $891 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $274 (23%) lower.It is approximately $171 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $35 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
