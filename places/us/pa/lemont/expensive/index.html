Compared to nearby areas, Lemont is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lemont is valued at $257,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $22,680 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $39,500 (18%) higher, and is approximately $176,200 (218%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Lemont's median rent is $891 per month. It is about $274 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $171 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $35 (4%) higher.
