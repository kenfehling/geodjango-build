In comparison to nearby areas, Lemont is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lemont is $257,000. It is about $22,680 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $39,500 (18%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $176,200 (218%) higher.

Rents

In Lemont the median rent is $891 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $274 (23%) lower. It is approximately $171 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $35 (4%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
