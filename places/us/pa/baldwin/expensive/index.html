Much like other places in this area, it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Baldwin is $135,800. It is roughly $5,300 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $81,700 (37%) lower, and it is about $10,800 (8%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Baldwin's median rent is $793 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $14 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $269 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $165 (17%) lower.
