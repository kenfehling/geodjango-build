In Baldwin the median rent is $793 a month. This is roughly $14 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $269 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $165 (17%) lower.
