Home prices

The median home in Sand Hill is valued at $147,800. This is approximately $23,020 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $69,700 (32%) lower, and is approximately $15,200 (9%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Sand Hill the median monthly rent is $984. This is approximately $126 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $78 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $58 (5%) lower.
