Rents

The median monthly rent in Sand Hill is $984. This is approximately $126 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $78 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $58 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
