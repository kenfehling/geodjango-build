Home prices

The median home in Valley View costs $111,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $32,500 (41%) higher. It is about $106,300 (48%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown is roughly $20,100 (15%) lower.

Rents

Valley View's median monthly rent is $802. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $107 (15%) higher. It is roughly $260 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $202 (20%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
