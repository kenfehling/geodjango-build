Home prices

The median home in Inkerman costs $124,400. This is approximately $34,600 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $93,100 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $6,900 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Inkerman the median rent is $966 per month. This is roughly $83 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $38 (3%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
