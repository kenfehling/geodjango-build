Home prices

Flemington's median home price is $115,600. This is roughly $2,020 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $101,900 (46%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $34,800 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Flemington is $880 a month. This is about $46 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $182 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $24 (2%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
