Home prices

The median home in Flemington costs $115,600. This is approximately $2,020 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $101,900 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is about $34,800 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Flemington is $880. It is about $46 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $182 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $24 (2%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
