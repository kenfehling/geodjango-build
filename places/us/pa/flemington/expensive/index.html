Home prices

The value of the median home in Flemington is $115,600. It is approximately $2,020 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $101,900 (46%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $34,800 (43%) higher.

Rents

Flemington's median monthly rent is $880. This is approximately $46 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $182 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $24 (2%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
