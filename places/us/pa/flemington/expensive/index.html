Home prices

The median home in Flemington costs $115,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $2,020 (1%) lower. It is roughly $101,900 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is approximately $34,800 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Flemington is $880. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $46 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $182 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is about $24 (2%) higher.
