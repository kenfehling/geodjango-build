Compared to its surrounding areas, Portland is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Portland costs $180,700. It is about $19,480 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $36,800 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $425,300 (70%) lower.

Rents

In Portland the median monthly rent is $1,085. This is approximately $131 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $23 (2%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $358 (24%) lower.
