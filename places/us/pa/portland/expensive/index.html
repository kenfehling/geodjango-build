In comparison to the surrounding areas, Portland is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Portland's median home cost is $180,700. This is about $19,480 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $36,800 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $425,300 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $1,085. This is approximately $131 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $23 (2%) higher, and is approximately $358 (24%) lower compared to the city of New York.
