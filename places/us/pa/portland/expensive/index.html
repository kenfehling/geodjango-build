In comparison to the surrounding areas, Portland is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Portland is valued at $180,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $19,480 (9%) lower. It is roughly $36,800 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $425,300 (70%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Portland is $1,085. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $131 (10%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $23 (2%) higher, and is roughly $358 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
