Compared to other places in this area, Portland is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Portland's median home cost is $180,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $19,480 (9%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $36,800 (16%) lower, and it is about $425,300 (70%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Portland is $1,085 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $131 (10%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $23 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $358 (24%) lower.
