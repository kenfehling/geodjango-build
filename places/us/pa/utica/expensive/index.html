Compared to its surrounding areas, Utica is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Utica costs $66,900. It is approximately $17,700 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $150,600 (69%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $58,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Utica is $583 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $63 (9%) lower.It is about $479 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $375 (39%) lower.
