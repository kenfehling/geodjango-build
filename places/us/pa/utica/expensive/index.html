Home prices

The cost of the median home in Utica is $66,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $17,700 (20%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $150,600 (69%) lower, and it is approximately $58,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Utica is $583. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $63 (9%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $479 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $375 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
