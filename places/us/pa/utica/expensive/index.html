Home prices

The median home in Utica costs $66,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $17,700 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $150,600 (69%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $58,100 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Utica is $583 a month. This is approximately $63 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $479 (45%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $375 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
