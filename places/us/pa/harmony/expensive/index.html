Compared to its surrounding areas, Harmony is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Harmony is $203,300. It is roughly $42,600 (26%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $14,200 (6%) lower, and is roughly $78,300 (62%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Harmony the median rent is $1,101 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $202 (22%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $39 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $143 (14%) higher.
