Home prices

The value of the median home in Hughestown is $123,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $8,480 (7%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $93,700 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $7,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Hughestown's median monthly rent is $948. This is roughly $151 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $114 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $56 (5%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
