Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marion is $172,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $8,580 (5%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $45,500 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $429,500 (71%) lower.

Rents

In Marion the median monthly rent is $1,146. This is approximately $187 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $84 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $395 (25%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
