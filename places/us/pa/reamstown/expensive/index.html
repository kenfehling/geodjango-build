Home prices

The median home in Reamstown costs $182,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $3,880 (2%) higher.It is about $35,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $19,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Reamstown the median rent is $859 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $112 (11%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $203 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
