Home prices

The median home cost in Reamstown is $182,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $3,880 (2%) higher.It is approximately $35,200 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $19,300 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Reamstown's median monthly rent is $859. It is about $112 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $203 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $183 (17%) lower.
