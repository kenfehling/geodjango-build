Home prices

The median home price in Reamstown is $182,300. This is roughly $3,880 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $35,200 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $19,300 (11%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Reamstown the median monthly rent is $859. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $112 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $203 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $183 (17%) lower.
