Home prices

The median home in Garden View is valued at $115,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $32,380 (21%) lower. It is approximately $101,900 (46%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $34,800 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Garden View the median rent is $934 a month. It is about $146 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $128 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $78 (9%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
