Home prices

The median home in Garden View costs $115,600. This is approximately $32,380 (21%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $101,900 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is about $34,800 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Garden View the median rent is $934 per month. This is roughly $146 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $128 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $78 (9%) higher.
