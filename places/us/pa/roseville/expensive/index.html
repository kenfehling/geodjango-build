Home prices

The median home in Roseville costs $132,700. This is roughly $11,220 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $84,800 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Binghamton is approximately $41,700 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Roseville is $600 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $78 (11%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $462 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Binghamton it is roughly $146 (19%) lower.
