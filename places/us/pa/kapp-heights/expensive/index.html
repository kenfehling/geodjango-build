The median home value in Kapp Heights is $125,500. This is about $28,960 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $92,000 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Reading is roughly $52,300 (71%) higher.

Rents

In Kapp Heights the median rent is $950 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $251 (35%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $112 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $139 (17%) higher compared to the city of Reading.
