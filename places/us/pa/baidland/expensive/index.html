Home prices

The price of the median home in Baidland is $176,800. It is roughly $85,060 (92%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $40,700 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $51,800 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Baidland the median rent is $645 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $30 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $417 (39%) lower, and is about $313 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
