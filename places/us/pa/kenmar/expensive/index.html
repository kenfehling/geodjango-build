Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kenmar is $165,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $20,180 (13%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $52,100 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Scranton it is roughly $62,400 (60%) higher.

Rents

Kenmar's median monthly rent is $704. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $141 (16%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $358 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Scranton is roughly $78 (9%) lower.
