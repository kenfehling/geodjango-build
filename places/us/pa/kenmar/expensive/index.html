Compared to other places in this area, Kenmar is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Scranton.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Kenmar is $165,400. It is about $20,180 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $52,100 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $62,400 (60%) higher compared to the city of Scranton.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kenmar is $704. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $141 (16%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $358 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Scranton it is about $78 (9%) lower.
