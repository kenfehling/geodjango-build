Home prices

The price of the median home in South New Castle is $71,300. This is approximately $8,540 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $146,200 (67%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $1,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

South New Castle's median monthly rent is $692. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $31 (4%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $370 (34%) lower, and is approximately $27 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
