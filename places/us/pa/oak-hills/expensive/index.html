Home prices

The median home in Oak Hills costs $193,200. It is about $39,780 (25%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $24,300 (11%) lower, and it is about $68,200 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Oak Hills the median rent is $976 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $148 (17%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $86 (8%) lower, and is roughly $18 (1%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
