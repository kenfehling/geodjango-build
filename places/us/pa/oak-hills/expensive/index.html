Home prices

The median home in Oak Hills costs $193,200. This is approximately $39,780 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $24,300 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $68,200 (54%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Oak Hills the median monthly rent is $976. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $148 (17%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $86 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $18 (1%) higher.
