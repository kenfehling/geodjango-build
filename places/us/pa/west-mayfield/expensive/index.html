Home prices

The cost of the median home in West Mayfield is $101,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $4,060 (3%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $116,100 (53%) lower, and it is approximately $23,600 (18%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Mayfield is $855. Compared to other places in this area this is about $118 (16%) higher. It is approximately $207 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $103 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
