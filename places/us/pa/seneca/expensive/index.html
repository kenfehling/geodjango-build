Home prices

The price of the median home in Seneca is $114,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $40,220 (54%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $103,300 (47%) lower, and is about $10,800 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Seneca's median rent is $877 a month. This is roughly $245 (38%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $185 (17%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $81 (8%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
