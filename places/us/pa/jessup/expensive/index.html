Home prices

The median home in Jessup is valued at $144,800. This is about $1,760 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $72,700 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $13,500 (10%) higher.

Rents

In Jessup the median monthly rent is $841. It is approximately $28 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $221 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $163 (16%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
