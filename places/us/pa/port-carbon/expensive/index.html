Home prices

Port Carbon's median home cost is $79,600. This is about $5,440 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $137,900 (63%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $83,400 (51%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Port Carbon's median rent is $744 per month. This is roughly $63 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $318 (29%) lower, and is roughly $298 (28%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
