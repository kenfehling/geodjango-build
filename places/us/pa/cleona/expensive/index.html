Compared to the surrounding areas, Cleona is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Cleona is valued at $145,200. This is roughly $15,040 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $72,300 (33%) lower, and it is about $17,800 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Cleona is $841 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $85 (9%) lower. It is about $221 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $201 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
