Compared to its surrounding areas, Cleona is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Cleona is $145,200. It is roughly $15,040 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $72,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $17,800 (10%) lower.

Rents

Cleona's median monthly rent is $841. This is about $85 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $221 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $201 (19%) lower.
