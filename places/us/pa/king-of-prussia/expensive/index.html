Compared to other places in this area, King of Prussia is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in King of Prussia is valued at $320,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $46,640 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $103,300 (47%) higher, and it is roughly $285,200 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

King of Prussia's median rent is $1,456 a month. It is about $136 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $394 (37%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of New York.
