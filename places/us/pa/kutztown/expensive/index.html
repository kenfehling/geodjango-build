Home prices

The median home cost in Kutztown is $166,700. This is roughly $20,280 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $50,800 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $3,700 (2%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Kutztown is $931 per month. It is approximately $17 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $131 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $111 (10%) lower.
