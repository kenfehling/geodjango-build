Compared to its surrounding areas, Kutztown is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Kutztown is valued at $166,700. This is approximately $20,280 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $50,800 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $3,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kutztown is $931. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $17 (1%) lower. It is approximately $131 (12%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $111 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
