Home prices

Kutztown's median home cost is $166,700. It is about $20,280 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $50,800 (23%) lower, and is about $3,700 (2%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Kutztown the median monthly rent is $931. It is approximately $17 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $131 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $111 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
