Home prices

The median home in Rogersville costs $108,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $3,240 (2%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $108,700 (49%) lower, and is about $16,200 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Rogersville the median monthly rent is $840. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $110 (15%) higher. It is about $222 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $118 (12%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
