Home prices

The median home cost in Edinboro is $165,800. This is roughly $57,020 (52%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $51,700 (23%) lower, and it is about $76,700 (86%) higher compared to the city of Erie.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edinboro is $847. This is about $77 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $215 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Erie is approximately $137 (19%) higher.
