Home prices

The median home cost in Delta is $160,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $117,820 (42%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $56,900 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $2,400 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Delta is $1,005 per month. This is approximately $90 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $57 (5%) lower, and it is about $37 (3%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
