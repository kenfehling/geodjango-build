Home prices

Sheatown's median home value is $108,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $5,000 (4%) higher. It is roughly $109,000 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $22,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Sheatown's median rent is $743 per month. This is about $65 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $319 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is approximately $261 (25%) lower.
