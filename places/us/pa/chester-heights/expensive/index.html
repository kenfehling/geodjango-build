Home prices

Rents

In Chester Heights the median monthly rent is $1,920. This is about $502 (35%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $858 (80%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $878 (84%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
