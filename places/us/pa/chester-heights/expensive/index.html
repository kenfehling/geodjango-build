Home prices

The median home in Chester Heights costs $249,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $13,240 (5%) higher. It is about $31,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $86,000 (52%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Chester Heights's median monthly rent is $1,920. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $502 (35%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $858 (80%) higher, and is roughly $878 (84%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
