In comparison to the surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Baltimore, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Harrisburg costs $80,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $59,820 (42%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $136,700 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore is approximately $79,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

Harrisburg's median monthly rent is $856. It is about $66 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $206 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $217 (20%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
