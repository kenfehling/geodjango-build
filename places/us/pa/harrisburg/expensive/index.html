Home prices

The cost of the median home in Harrisburg is $80,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $59,820 (42%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $136,700 (62%) lower, and is approximately $79,300 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrisburg is $856. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $66 (7%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $206 (19%) lower, and is roughly $217 (20%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
