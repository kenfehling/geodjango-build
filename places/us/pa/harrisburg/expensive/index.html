Compared to its nearby areas, Harrisburg is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Baltimore, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Harrisburg is $80,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $59,820 (42%) lower. It is approximately $136,700 (62%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $79,300 (49%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median rent is $856 per month. This is about $66 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $206 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $217 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.
