In comparison to its surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Baltimore, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Harrisburg's median home value is $80,800. It is about $59,820 (42%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $136,700 (62%) lower, and it is about $79,300 (49%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

The median rent in Harrisburg is $856 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $66 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $206 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Baltimore it is approximately $217 (20%) lower.
