Home prices

The median home in Pennsburg costs $205,600. This is approximately $9,900 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $11,900 (5%) lower, and is approximately $400,400 (66%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Pennsburg's median rent is $1,073 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $75 (6%) lower. It is roughly $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $370 (25%) lower.
