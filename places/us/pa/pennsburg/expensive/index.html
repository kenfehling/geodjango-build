In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pennsburg is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of New York, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Pennsburg's median home price is $205,600. It is about $9,900 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $11,900 (5%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $400,400 (66%) lower.

Rents

Pennsburg's median rent is $1,073 a month. This is approximately $75 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $370 (25%) lower compared to the city of New York.
