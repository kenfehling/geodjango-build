Compared to nearby areas, Collegeville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of New York, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Collegeville costs $335,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $44,020 (15%) higher. It is approximately $117,900 (54%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $270,600 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Collegeville is $1,187 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $241 (16%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $125 (11%) higher, and is roughly $256 (17%) lower compared to the city of New York.
