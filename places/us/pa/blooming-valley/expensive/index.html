The median home cost in Blooming Valley is $119,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $25,060 (26%) higher. It is roughly $98,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $5,900 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Blooming Valley's median rent is $613 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $33 (5%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $449 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $345 (36%) lower.
