Home prices

The median home in Bloomsburg costs $136,000. This is about $15,820 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $81,500 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is roughly $4,700 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Bloomsburg the median monthly rent is $762. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $56 (6%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $300 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is about $242 (24%) lower.
