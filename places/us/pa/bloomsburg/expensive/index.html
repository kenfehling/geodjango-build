Home prices

The median home price in Bloomsburg is $136,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $15,820 (10%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $81,500 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $4,700 (3%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bloomsburg is $762. This is roughly $56 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $300 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $242 (24%) lower.
