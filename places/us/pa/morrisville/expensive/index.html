Home prices

The median home in Morrisville costs $154,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $57,900 (60%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $63,500 (29%) lower, and is approximately $29,000 (23%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Morrisville the median rent is $706 per month. This is approximately $44 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $356 (33%) lower, and is about $252 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
