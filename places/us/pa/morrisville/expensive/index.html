In comparison to its surrounding areas, Morrisville is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Morrisville is valued at $154,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $57,900 (60%) higher. It is approximately $63,500 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $29,000 (23%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Morrisville's median monthly rent is $706. This is about $44 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $356 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $252 (26%) lower.
