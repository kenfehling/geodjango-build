Home prices

The median home in Morrisville costs $154,000. It is roughly $57,900 (60%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $63,500 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $29,000 (23%) higher.

Rents

Morrisville's median monthly rent is $706. It is roughly $44 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $356 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $252 (26%) lower.
