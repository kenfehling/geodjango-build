Home prices

The median home in Pikes Creek is valued at $206,400. It is roughly $45,320 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $11,100 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown it is approximately $75,100 (57%) higher.

Rents

Pikes Creek's median monthly rent is $1,236. It is approximately $422 (51%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $174 (16%) higher, and it is roughly $232 (23%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.
