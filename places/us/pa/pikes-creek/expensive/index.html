Home prices

The median home price in Pikes Creek is $206,400. It is approximately $45,320 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $11,100 (5%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $75,100 (57%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median rent in Pikes Creek is $1,236 a month. This is roughly $422 (51%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $174 (16%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $232 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Allentown.
