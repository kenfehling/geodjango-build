Home prices

The price of the median home in Spring Mills is $97,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $62,740 (39%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $120,000 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $16,700 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Spring Mills is $820 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is is very similar.In comparison to the entire country it is about $242 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $36 (4%) lower.
