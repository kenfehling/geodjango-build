Home prices

The median home in Spring Mills costs $97,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $62,740 (39%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $120,000 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $16,700 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Spring Mills is $820. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $36 (4%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
