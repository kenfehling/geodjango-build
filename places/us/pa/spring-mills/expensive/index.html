Compared to other places in this area, Spring Mills is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Spring Mills costs $97,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $62,740 (39%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $120,000 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $16,700 (20%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Spring Mills the median rent is $820 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $242 (22%) lower, and is about $36 (4%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
