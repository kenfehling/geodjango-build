Home prices

Downingtown's median home value is $242,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $75,900 (23%) lower. It is about $25,200 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $79,700 (48%) higher.

Rents

In Downingtown the median rent is $1,194 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $362 (23%) lower. It is about $132 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $152 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
