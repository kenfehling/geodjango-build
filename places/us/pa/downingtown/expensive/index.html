Compared to its surrounding areas, Downingtown is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home cost in Downingtown is $242,700. It is about $75,900 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $25,200 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $79,700 (48%) higher.

Rents

In Downingtown the median monthly rent is $1,194. It is roughly $362 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $132 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $152 (14%) higher.
