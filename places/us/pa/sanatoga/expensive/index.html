Home prices

The median home in Sanatoga costs $191,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $16,780 (8%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $25,600 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $28,900 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Sanatoga the median rent is $1,011 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $263 (20%) lower. It is approximately $51 (4%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $31 (2%) lower.
