Rents

The median rent in Sanatoga is $1,011 per month. This is roughly $263 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $51 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $31 (2%) lower.
