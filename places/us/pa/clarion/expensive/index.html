Home prices

Clarion's median home price is $124,200. This is roughly $21,160 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $93,300 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is is very similar.

Rents

Clarion's median rent is $649 a month. It is about $47 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $413 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $309 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
