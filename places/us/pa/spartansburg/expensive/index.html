Rents

In Spartansburg the median monthly rent is $713. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $8 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and is approximately $63 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
