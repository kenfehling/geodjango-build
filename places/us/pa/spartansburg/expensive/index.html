Home prices

The median home in Spartansburg costs $78,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $3,180 (3%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $139,300 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $11,600 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Spartansburg the median monthly rent is $713. It is approximately $8 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $349 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is about $63 (8%) lower.
