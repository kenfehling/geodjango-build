Home prices

The median home in Jacobus is valued at $192,800. It is approximately $24,180 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $24,700 (11%) lower, and it is about $408,700 (67%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Jacobus is $985 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $18 (1%) higher. It is about $77 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $556 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
