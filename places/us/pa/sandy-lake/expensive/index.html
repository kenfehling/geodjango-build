Rents

Sandy Lake's median rent is $616 a month. It is about $178 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $446 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $342 (35%) lower.
