Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairhope is $88,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $1,920 (2%) higher.It is approximately $129,500 (59%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $37,000 (29%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Fairhope the median rent is $780 a month. It is roughly $67 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $282 (26%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $178 (18%) lower.
