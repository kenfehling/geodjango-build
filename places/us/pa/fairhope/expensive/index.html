Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairhope is $88,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $1,920 (2%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $129,500 (59%) lower, and is roughly $37,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Fairhope is $780 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $67 (9%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $178 (18%) lower.
