Rents

The median rent in Fairhope is $780 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $67 (9%) higher.It is approximately $282 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $178 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
