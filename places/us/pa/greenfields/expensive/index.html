Home prices

The cost of the median home in Greenfields is $163,200. This is approximately $14,580 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $54,300 (24%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Greenfields the median rent is $1,198 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $137 (12%) higher. It is approximately $136 (12%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $156 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
