In comparison to the surrounding areas, Greenfields is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Greenfields is $163,200. This is about $14,580 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $54,300 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is is very similar.

Rents

In Greenfields the median monthly rent is $1,198. Compared to other places in this area this is about $137 (12%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $136 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $156 (14%) higher.
