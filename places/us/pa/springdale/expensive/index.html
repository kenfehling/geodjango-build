Compared to the surrounding areas, Springdale is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

Springdale's median home value is $103,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $15,000 (12%) lower.It is roughly $113,800 (52%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $21,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Springdale the median monthly rent is $661. Compared to other places in this area this is about $148 (18%) lower.It is roughly $401 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $297 (31%) lower.
