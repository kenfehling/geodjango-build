Compared to nearby areas, Springdale is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Springdale costs $103,700. This is roughly $15,000 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $113,800 (52%) lower, and it is about $21,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Springdale the median monthly rent is $661. It is approximately $148 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $401 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $297 (31%) lower.
