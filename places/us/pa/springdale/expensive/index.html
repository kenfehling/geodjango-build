Home prices

The median home in Springdale costs $103,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $15,000 (12%) lower. It is approximately $113,800 (52%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $21,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Springdale is $661. Compared to nearby areas this is about $148 (18%) lower. It is approximately $401 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $297 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
