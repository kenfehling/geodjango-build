Home prices

Huntingdon's median home price is $117,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $15,100 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $100,500 (46%) lower, and is roughly $36,200 (44%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Huntingdon the median rent is $646 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $127 (16%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $416 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is about $210 (24%) lower.
