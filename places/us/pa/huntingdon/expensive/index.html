Compared to the surrounding areas, Huntingdon is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Huntingdon is $117,000. It is roughly $15,100 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $100,500 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $36,200 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Huntingdon is $646 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $127 (16%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $416 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is about $210 (24%) lower.
