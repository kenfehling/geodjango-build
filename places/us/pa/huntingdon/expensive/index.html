Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Huntingdon is $646 per month. It is approximately $127 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $416 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $210 (24%) lower.
