Home prices

Rents

Huntingdon's median rent is $646 per month. This is approximately $127 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $416 (39%) lower, and it is about $210 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
