Home prices

The median home in Armagh costs $102,500. It is approximately $37,440 (57%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $115,000 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $22,500 (18%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Armagh the median rent is $863 per month. It is roughly $228 (35%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $199 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $95 (9%) lower.
