Compared to nearby areas, Nanticoke is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Allentown, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Nanticoke is $80,300. It is approximately $32,440 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $137,200 (63%) lower, and is approximately $51,000 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nanticoke is $667. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $174 (20%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $395 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $337 (33%) lower.
