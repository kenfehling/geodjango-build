Compared to the surrounding areas, Nanticoke is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in Nanticoke costs $80,300. It is about $32,440 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $137,200 (63%) lower, and it is roughly $51,000 (38%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

In Nanticoke the median monthly rent is $667. It is about $174 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $395 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown is roughly $337 (33%) lower.
