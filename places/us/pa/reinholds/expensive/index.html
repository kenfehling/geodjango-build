Home prices

Rents

Reinholds's median monthly rent is $1,083. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $174 (19%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $21 (1%) higher, and is about $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
