Home prices

The median home cost in Reinholds is $179,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $18,740 (9%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $38,400 (17%) lower, and it is about $16,100 (9%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Reinholds is $1,083. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $174 (19%) higher. It is about $21 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $41 (3%) higher.
