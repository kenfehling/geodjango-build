Compared to the surrounding areas, East Berwick is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in East Berwick is valued at $135,400. This is approximately $32,360 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $82,100 (37%) lower, and is roughly $4,100 (3%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median rent in East Berwick is $772 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $14 (1%) higher.It is about $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Allentown is approximately $232 (23%) lower.
