Home prices

The median home in East Berwick costs $135,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $32,360 (31%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $82,100 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $4,100 (3%) higher.

Rents

In East Berwick the median monthly rent is $772. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $14 (1%) higher.It is approximately $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown is about $232 (23%) lower.
