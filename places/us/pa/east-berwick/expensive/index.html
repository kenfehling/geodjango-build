Home prices

The median home in East Berwick costs $135,400. It is about $32,360 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $82,100 (37%) lower, and is approximately $4,100 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Berwick is $772. This is approximately $14 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $290 (27%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Allentown is roughly $232 (23%) lower.
