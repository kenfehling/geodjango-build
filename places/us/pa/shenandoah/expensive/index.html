Home prices

Shenandoah's median home value is $28,200. This is approximately $37,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $189,300 (87%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $134,800 (82%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Shenandoah is $557 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $164 (22%) lower.It is roughly $505 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $485 (46%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
