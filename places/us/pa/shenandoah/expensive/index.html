Home prices

The cost of the median home in Shenandoah is $28,200. It is approximately $37,800 (57%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $189,300 (87%) lower, and it is roughly $134,800 (82%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Shenandoah the median monthly rent is $557. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $164 (22%) lower. It is approximately $505 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $485 (46%) lower.
