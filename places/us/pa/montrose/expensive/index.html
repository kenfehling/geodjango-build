In comparison to the surrounding areas, Montrose is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Montrose is $140,600. This is roughly $26,780 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $76,900 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $46,200 (48%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median rent in Montrose is $625 per month. It is approximately $167 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $437 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $189 (23%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
