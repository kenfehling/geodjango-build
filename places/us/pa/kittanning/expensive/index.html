Compared to nearby areas, Kittanning is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kittanning costs $73,800. This is about $31,900 (30%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $143,700 (66%) lower, and it is about $51,200 (40%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Kittanning's median rent is $611 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $79 (11%) lower. It is roughly $451 (42%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $347 (36%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
