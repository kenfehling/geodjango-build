Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kittanning is $73,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $31,900 (30%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $143,700 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $51,200 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kittanning is $611 per month. This is roughly $79 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $451 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $347 (36%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
