Home prices

The median home in State College costs $312,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $43,800 (16%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $94,900 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $231,600 (286%) higher.

Rents

In State College the median rent is $1,054 a month. This is roughly $78 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and it is roughly $198 (23%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
