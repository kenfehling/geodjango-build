In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fullerton is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fullerton is $177,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $9,800 (5%) higher.It is about $39,600 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $428,100 (70%) lower.

Rents

In Fullerton the median monthly rent is $1,122. This is approximately $142 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $60 (5%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $321 (22%) lower compared to the city of New York.
