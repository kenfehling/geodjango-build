Compared to the surrounding areas, Fullerton is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Fullerton is $177,900. It is roughly $9,800 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $39,600 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $428,100 (70%) lower.

Rents

In Fullerton the median monthly rent is $1,122. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $142 (14%) higher. It is approximately $60 (5%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $321 (22%) lower.
