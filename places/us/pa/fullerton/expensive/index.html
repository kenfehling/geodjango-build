In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fullerton is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fullerton is valued at $177,900. It is about $9,800 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $39,600 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $428,100 (70%) lower.

Rents

Fullerton's median monthly rent is $1,122. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $142 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $60 (5%) higher, and it is approximately $321 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
