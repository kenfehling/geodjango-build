Home prices

The median home value in Berwyn is $475,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $144,360 (43%) higher.It is roughly $257,700 (118%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $130,800 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Berwyn is $1,351 per month. This is roughly $249 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $289 (27%) higher, and is about $92 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
