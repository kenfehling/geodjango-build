The median home cost in Chester is $72,400. This is about $59,740 (45%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $145,100 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $90,600 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chester is $860. This is approximately $227 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $202 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $182 (17%) lower.
