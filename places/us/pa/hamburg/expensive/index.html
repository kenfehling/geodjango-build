In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hamburg is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home value in Hamburg is $150,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $13,340 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $67,400 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $12,900 (7%) lower.

Rents

Hamburg's median monthly rent is $906. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $10 (1%) lower. It is about $156 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $136 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
