Home prices

Hamburg's median home cost is $150,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $13,340 (9%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $67,400 (30%) lower, and is about $12,900 (7%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Hamburg is $906 a month. This is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $156 (14%) lower, and is roughly $136 (13%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
