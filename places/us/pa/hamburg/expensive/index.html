Home prices

The median home in Hamburg is valued at $150,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $13,340 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $67,400 (30%) lower, and it is about $12,900 (7%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Hamburg's median monthly rent is $906. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $10 (1%) lower. It is approximately $156 (14%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $136 (13%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
