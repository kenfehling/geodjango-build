Home prices

The median home in Hamburg is valued at $150,100. It is about $13,340 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $67,400 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $12,900 (7%) lower.

Rents

Hamburg's median monthly rent is $906. This is approximately $10 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $156 (14%) lower, and it is roughly $136 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
