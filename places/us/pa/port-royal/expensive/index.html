Compared to its surrounding areas, Port Royal is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Port Royal is $124,000. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $20,960 (20%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $93,500 (42%) lower, and is about $43,200 (53%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Port Royal's median monthly rent is $630. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $51 (7%) lower. It is roughly $432 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $226 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
