In comparison to its surrounding areas, Port Royal is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The median home cost in Port Royal is $124,000. It is approximately $20,960 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $93,500 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $43,200 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Port Royal is $630. It is roughly $51 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $432 (40%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $226 (26%) lower.
