Home prices

The price of the median home in Edgeworth is $603,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $211,420 (53%) higher. It is approximately $385,600 (177%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $478,100 (382%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Edgeworth the median rent is $1,241 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $248 (24%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $179 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $283 (29%) higher.
