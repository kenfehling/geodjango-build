Home prices

The median home in Lawrence costs $106,800. Compared to nearby areas it is about $124,760 (53%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $110,700 (50%) lower, and is roughly $18,200 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Lawrence's median monthly rent is $861. It is approximately $397 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $201 (18%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $97 (10%) lower.
