Rents

In Oakdale the median rent is $728 a month. It is approximately $124 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $334 (31%) lower, and is roughly $230 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
