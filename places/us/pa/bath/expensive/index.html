Home prices

The median home value in Bath is $153,400. It is approximately $40,600 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $64,100 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $452,600 (74%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Bath the median rent is $1,211 per month. This is about $192 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $149 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $232 (16%) lower compared to the city of New York.
