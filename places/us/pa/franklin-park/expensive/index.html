Home prices

The median home in Franklin Park is valued at $350,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $172,440 (32%) lower.It is approximately $132,700 (61%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $225,200 (180%) higher.

Rents

Franklin Park's median rent is $1,673 per month. This is roughly $684 (69%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $611 (57%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $715 (74%) higher.
