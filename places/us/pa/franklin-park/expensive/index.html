Compared to the surrounding areas, Franklin Park is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home price in Franklin Park is $350,200. This is about $172,440 (32%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $132,700 (61%) higher, and is approximately $225,200 (180%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Franklin Park is $1,673 per month. It is approximately $684 (69%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $611 (57%) higher, and it is approximately $715 (74%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
