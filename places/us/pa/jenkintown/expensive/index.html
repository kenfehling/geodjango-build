The median home in Jenkintown costs $308,400. This is approximately $9,180 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $90,900 (41%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $297,600 (49%) lower.

Rents

Jenkintown's median monthly rent is $1,379. It is roughly $213 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $317 (29%) higher, and it is roughly $64 (4%) lower compared to the city of New York.
