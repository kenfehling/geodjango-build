In comparison to its surrounding areas, Waynesboro is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home price in Waynesboro is $142,600. It is about $26,880 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $74,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $458,900 (76%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Waynesboro's median rent is $794 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $128 (13%) lower. It is approximately $268 (25%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $747 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
