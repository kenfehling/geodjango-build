Home prices

The median home price in Waynesboro is $142,600. Compared to nearby areas this is about $26,880 (15%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $74,900 (34%) lower, and it is roughly $458,900 (76%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

Waynesboro's median rent is $794 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $128 (13%) lower. It is roughly $268 (25%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $747 (48%) lower.
