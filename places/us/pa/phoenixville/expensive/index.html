Home prices

The median home in Phoenixville is valued at $237,200. It is approximately $25,800 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $19,700 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $74,200 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Phoenixville is $1,200 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $18 (1%) lower. It is roughly $138 (12%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $158 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
