In comparison to its surrounding areas, Johnstown is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Johnstown costs $38,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $51,000 (57%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $179,400 (82%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $86,900 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Johnstown the median monthly rent is $523. This is approximately $151 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $539 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $435 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
