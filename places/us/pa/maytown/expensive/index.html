Home prices

The median home in Maytown is valued at $182,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $40,580 (28%) higher.It is approximately $35,000 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $419,000 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Maytown the median monthly rent is $1,176. It is about $204 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $114 (10%) higher, and is roughly $365 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
