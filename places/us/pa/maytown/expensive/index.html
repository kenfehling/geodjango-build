Home prices

The median home in Maytown is valued at $182,500. This is approximately $40,580 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $35,000 (16%) lower, and is roughly $419,000 (69%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Maytown's median rent is $1,176 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $204 (21%) higher. It is approximately $114 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington it is about $365 (23%) lower.
