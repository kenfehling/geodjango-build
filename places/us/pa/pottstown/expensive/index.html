Home prices

The median home value in Pottstown is $124,400. This is roughly $88,560 (41%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $93,100 (42%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $38,600 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Pottstown the median monthly rent is $979. This is roughly $307 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $83 (7%) lower, and is roughly $63 (6%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
