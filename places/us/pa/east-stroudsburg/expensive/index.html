Home prices

The median home in East Stroudsburg costs $146,800. This is roughly $28,980 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $70,700 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is about $459,200 (75%) lower.

Rents

In East Stroudsburg the median monthly rent is $1,124. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $49 (4%) higher. It is approximately $62 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $319 (22%) lower.
