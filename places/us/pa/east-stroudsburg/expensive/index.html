Home prices

East Stroudsburg's median home value is $146,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $28,980 (16%) lower.It is roughly $70,700 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $459,200 (75%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in East Stroudsburg is $1,124 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $49 (4%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $62 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $319 (22%) lower.
