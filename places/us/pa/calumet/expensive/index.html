In comparison to the surrounding areas, Calumet is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Calumet is $134,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $7,860 (6%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $82,900 (38%) lower, and is about $9,600 (7%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Calumet is $630. This is roughly $134 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $432 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $328 (34%) lower.
