Compared to other places in this area, Greensburg is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home cost in Greensburg is $134,000. It is roughly $43,620 (48%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $83,500 (38%) lower, and is about $9,000 (7%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Greensburg the median rent is $656 per month. This is approximately $75 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $406 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $302 (31%) lower.
