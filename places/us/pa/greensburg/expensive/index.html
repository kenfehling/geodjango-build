Home prices

The median home in Greensburg is valued at $134,000. It is roughly $43,620 (48%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $83,500 (38%) lower, and is about $9,000 (7%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Greensburg the median monthly rent is $656. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $75 (10%) lower.It is roughly $406 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $302 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
