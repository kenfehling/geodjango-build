Home prices

The median home in Levittown costs $226,500. It is approximately $52,040 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $9,000 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $379,500 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Levittown the median rent is $1,129 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $16 (1%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $67 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $314 (21%) lower.
