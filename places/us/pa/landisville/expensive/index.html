Compared to other places in this area, Landisville is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Landisville is valued at $230,800. This is about $43,120 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $13,300 (6%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $67,800 (41%) higher.

Rents

In Landisville the median monthly rent is $953. This is roughly $39 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $109 (10%) lower, and is approximately $89 (8%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
