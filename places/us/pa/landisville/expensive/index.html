Home prices

The median home value in Landisville is $230,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $43,120 (22%) higher.It is about $13,300 (6%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $67,800 (41%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Landisville is $953. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $39 (4%) higher.It is about $109 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $89 (8%) lower.
