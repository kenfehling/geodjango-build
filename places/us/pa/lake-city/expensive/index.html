Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lake City is $120,200. This is approximately $4,740 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $97,300 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $50,600 (72%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Lake City's median monthly rent is $823. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $43 (5%) higher.It is roughly $239 (22%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $104 (14%) higher.
