Similar to its surrounding areas, it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cleveland however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lake City is $120,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $4,740 (3%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $97,300 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is roughly $50,600 (72%) higher.

Rents

Lake City's median monthly rent is $823. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $43 (5%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $239 (22%) lower, and it is about $104 (14%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
