Compared to other places in this area, Cooperstown is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cooperstown costs $84,400. Compared to nearby areas this is about $13,520 (19%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $133,100 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $40,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

Cooperstown's median monthly rent is $633. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is about $429 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $325 (33%) lower.
