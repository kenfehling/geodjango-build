Compared to the surrounding areas, Cooperstown is a little more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cooperstown is valued at $84,400. It is roughly $13,520 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $133,100 (61%) lower, and it is approximately $40,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Cooperstown is $633 a month. It is is very similar compared to nearby areas. It is about $429 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $325 (33%) lower.
