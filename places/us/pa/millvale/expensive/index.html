Rents

The median rent in Millvale is $717 a month. It is roughly $120 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $345 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $241 (25%) lower.
