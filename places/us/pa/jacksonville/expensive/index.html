Home prices

The median home value in Jacksonville is $69,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $27,180 (28%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $148,500 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $56,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksonville is $837. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $175 (26%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $225 (21%) lower, and is about $121 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
