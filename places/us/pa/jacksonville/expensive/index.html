Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville costs $69,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $27,180 (28%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $148,500 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $56,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median rent is $837 per month. This is roughly $175 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $225 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $121 (12%) lower.
