Home prices

The value of the median home in Masontown is $89,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $1,460 (1%) higher. It is roughly $128,500 (59%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $36,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Masontown the median rent is $708 a month. It is approximately $70 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $354 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $250 (26%) lower.
