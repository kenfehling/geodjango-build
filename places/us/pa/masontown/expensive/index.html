Home prices

Rents

In Masontown the median monthly rent is $708. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $70 (11%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $354 (33%) lower, and is about $250 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
