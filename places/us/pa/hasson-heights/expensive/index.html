Home prices

Hasson Heights's median home price is $87,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $8,660 (10%) higher.It is roughly $129,600 (59%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $37,100 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hasson Heights is $615 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $69 (10%) lower.It is about $447 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $343 (35%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
