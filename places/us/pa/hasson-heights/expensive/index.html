Home prices

The median home in Hasson Heights costs $87,900. It is about $8,660 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $129,600 (59%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $37,100 (29%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Hasson Heights the median rent is $615 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $69 (10%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $447 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $343 (35%) lower.
