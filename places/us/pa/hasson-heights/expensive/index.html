Home prices

Rents

Hasson Heights's median rent is $615 a month. It is roughly $69 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $447 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $343 (35%) lower.
