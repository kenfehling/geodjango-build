In comparison to the surrounding areas, Tremont is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Tremont is valued at $70,400. This is roughly $35,220 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $147,100 (67%) lower, and it is roughly $92,600 (56%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Tremont's median monthly rent is $655. It is about $24 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $387 (37%) lower.
