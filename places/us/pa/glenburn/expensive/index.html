Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Glenburn is $1,100 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $260 (30%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $38 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Allentown it is roughly $96 (9%) higher.
