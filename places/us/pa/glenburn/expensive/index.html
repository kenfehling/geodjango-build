Home prices

The median home in Glenburn is valued at $247,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $51,480 (26%) higher. It is about $29,800 (13%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is about $116,000 (88%) higher.

Rents

Glenburn's median monthly rent is $1,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $260 (30%) higher. It is roughly $38 (3%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $96 (9%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.
