Home prices

The median home in Glenburn costs $247,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $51,480 (26%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $29,800 (13%) higher, and it is roughly $116,000 (88%) higher in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Glenburn is $1,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $260 (30%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $38 (3%) higher, and is approximately $96 (9%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.
