Compared to its surrounding areas, Marshallton is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in Marshallton is valued at $64,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $11,000 (20%) higher. It is about $152,700 (70%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is roughly $66,500 (50%) lower.

Rents

Marshallton's median monthly rent is $509. It is roughly $144 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $553 (52%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is about $495 (49%) lower.
