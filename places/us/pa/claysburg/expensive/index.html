Compared to other places in this area, Claysburg is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Hagerstown.

Home prices

The median home in Claysburg costs $109,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $12,120 (9%) lower. It is roughly $108,200 (49%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Hagerstown it is approximately $46,400 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Claysburg is $739 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $17 (2%) higher. It is approximately $323 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Hagerstown is approximately $93 (11%) lower.
