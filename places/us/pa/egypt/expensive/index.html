Home prices

The cost of the median home in Egypt is $229,200. This is about $48,460 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $11,700 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $376,800 (62%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Egypt is $1,767. It is roughly $759 (75%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $705 (66%) higher, and it is about $324 (22%) higher compared to the city of New York.
