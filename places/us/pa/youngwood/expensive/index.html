Home prices

The cost of the median home in Youngwood is $113,000. It is about $4,400 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $104,500 (48%) lower, and is approximately $12,000 (9%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Youngwood's median rent is $663 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $88 (11%) lower. It is about $399 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $295 (30%) lower.
