Home prices

The price of the median home in Ellport is $98,900. This is approximately $13,560 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $118,600 (54%) lower, and is about $26,100 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ellport is $667. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $24 (3%) higher. It is approximately $395 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $291 (30%) lower.
