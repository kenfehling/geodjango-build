Home prices

Rents

Rebersburg's median monthly rent is $736. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $41 (5%) lower. It is roughly $326 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is about $120 (14%) lower.
