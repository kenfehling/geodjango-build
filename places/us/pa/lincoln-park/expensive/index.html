Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lincoln Park is $159,600. It is approximately $3,800 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $57,900 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $3,400 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lincoln Park is $1,275 per month. It is about $21 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $213 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $233 (22%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
