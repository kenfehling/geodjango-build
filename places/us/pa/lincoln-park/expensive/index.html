Home prices

The median home in Lincoln Park costs $159,600. This is roughly $3,800 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $57,900 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $3,400 (2%) lower.

Rents

Lincoln Park's median monthly rent is $1,275. Compared to other places in this area this is about $21 (1%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $213 (20%) higher, and is approximately $233 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
