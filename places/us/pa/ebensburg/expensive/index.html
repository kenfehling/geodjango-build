Home prices

The median home in Ebensburg is valued at $145,900. This is approximately $47,540 (48%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $71,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $20,900 (16%) higher.

Rents

Ebensburg's median monthly rent is $728. It is approximately $20 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $334 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $230 (24%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
