Home prices

The median home in Chalfont costs $322,900. It is approximately $15,540 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $105,400 (48%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $283,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Chalfont is $1,302 a month. It is approximately $80 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $240 (22%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $141 (9%) lower.
