Home prices

The median home in Etna is valued at $88,900. This is about $62,280 (41%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $128,600 (59%) lower, and it is roughly $36,100 (28%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Etna's median rent is $731 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $131 (15%) lower.It is about $331 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $227 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
