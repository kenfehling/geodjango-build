Compared to its surrounding areas, Allentown is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Allentown is $131,300. This is approximately $58,400 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $86,200 (39%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $474,700 (78%) lower.

Rents

In Allentown the median rent is $1,004 a month. This is roughly $76 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $58 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $439 (30%) lower.
