In comparison to other places in this area, Allentown is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of New York, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Allentown's median home price is $131,300. This is approximately $58,400 (30%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $86,200 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $474,700 (78%) lower.

Rents

Allentown's median rent is $1,004 per month. It is about $76 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $58 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $439 (30%) lower.
