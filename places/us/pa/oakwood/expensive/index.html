In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oakwood is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Oakwood is $85,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9,660 (10%) lower. It is approximately $131,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $16,100 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakwood is $778. It is approximately $99 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $284 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is approximately $59 (8%) higher.
