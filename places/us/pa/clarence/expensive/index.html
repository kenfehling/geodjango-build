Home prices

Rents

Clarence's median rent is $669 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $102 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $393 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $187 (21%) lower.
