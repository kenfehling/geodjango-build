Home prices

Rents

Elderton's median monthly rent is $788. This is roughly $50 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $170 (17%) lower.
