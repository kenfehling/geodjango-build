Rents

Elderton's median rent is $788 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $50 (6%) higher. It is roughly $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $170 (17%) lower.
