Home prices

The median home in Ellwood City is valued at $97,700. This is roughly $12,120 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $119,800 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $27,300 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ellwood City is $675. It is about $34 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $387 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $283 (29%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
