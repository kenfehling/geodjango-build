Home prices

The median home in Ellwood City costs $97,700. This is about $12,120 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $119,800 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $27,300 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Ellwood City's median monthly rent is $675. This is approximately $34 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $283 (29%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
