In comparison to other places in this area, Fort Washington is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Washington costs $432,400. It is about $100,240 (30%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $214,900 (98%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $173,600 (28%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

Fort Washington's median monthly rent is $1,663. This is roughly $305 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $601 (56%) higher, and is roughly $220 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
