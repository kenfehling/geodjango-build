Rents

In Conneaut Lake the median monthly rent is $664. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $11 (1%) lower.It is approximately $398 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $55 (7%) lower.
