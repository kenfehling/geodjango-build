Home prices

Rents

In Conneaut Lake the median rent is $664 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $11 (1%) lower.It is roughly $398 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $55 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
