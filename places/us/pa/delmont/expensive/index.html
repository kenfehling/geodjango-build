Compared to its surrounding areas, Delmont is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Delmont costs $181,800. This is approximately $43,620 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $35,700 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $56,800 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Delmont is $717 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $165 (18%) lower.It is approximately $345 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $241 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
