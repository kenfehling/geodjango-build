In comparison to its surrounding areas, Delmont is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Delmont is $181,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $43,620 (31%) higher. It is approximately $35,700 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $56,800 (45%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Delmont's median rent is $717 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $165 (18%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $345 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $241 (25%) lower.
