Home prices

The median home cost in Delmont is $181,800. This is about $43,620 (31%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $35,700 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $56,800 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Delmont is $717 per month. It is about $165 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $345 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $241 (25%) lower.
