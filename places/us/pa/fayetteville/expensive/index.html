Home prices

The value of the median home in Fayetteville is $151,600. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $13,880 (8%) lower. It is about $65,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $449,900 (74%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In Fayetteville the median monthly rent is $884. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $54 (5%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $178 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is about $657 (42%) lower.
