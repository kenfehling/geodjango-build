Compared to its surrounding areas, Fayetteville is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Washington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fayetteville is valued at $151,600. It is roughly $13,880 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $65,900 (30%) lower, and it is about $449,900 (74%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Fayetteville's median monthly rent is $884. It is approximately $54 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $178 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $657 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
