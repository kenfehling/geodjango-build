Home prices

Rents

Bryn Mawr's median rent is $1,510 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $28 (1%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $448 (42%) higher, and it is approximately $67 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
