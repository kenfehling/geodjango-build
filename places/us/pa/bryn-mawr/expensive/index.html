Home prices

The median home in Bryn Mawr costs $296,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $81,580 (21%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $78,900 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $309,600 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Bryn Mawr the median monthly rent is $1,510. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $28 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $448 (42%) higher, and it is roughly $67 (4%) higher compared to the city of New York.
