Compared to its surrounding areas, Polk is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Polk is valued at $62,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $27,220 (30%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $155,300 (71%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $62,800 (50%) lower.

Rents

Polk's median monthly rent is $607. This is roughly $46 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $455 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $351 (36%) lower.
