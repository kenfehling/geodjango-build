Home prices

Rents

The median rent in North Irwin is $673 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $122 (15%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $389 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $285 (29%) lower.
