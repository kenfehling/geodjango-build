Compared to the surrounding areas, Dauphin is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Baltimore.

Home prices

The median home in Dauphin costs $158,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $31,340 (24%) higher. It is about $59,300 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Baltimore it is roughly $1,900 (1%) lower.

Rents

Dauphin's median monthly rent is $725. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $72 (9%) lower. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore is roughly $348 (32%) lower.
