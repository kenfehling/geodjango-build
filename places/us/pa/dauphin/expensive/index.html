Home prices

The median home in Dauphin costs $158,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $31,340 (24%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $59,300 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Baltimore it is about $1,900 (1%) lower.

Rents

Dauphin's median rent is $725 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $72 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and it is about $348 (32%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
