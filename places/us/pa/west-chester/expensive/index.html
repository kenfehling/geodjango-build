Compared to nearby areas, West Chester is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

West Chester's median home cost is $378,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $69,860 (22%) higher. It is approximately $160,900 (73%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $215,400 (132%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in West Chester is $1,293 a month. This is roughly $215 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $231 (21%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $251 (24%) higher.
