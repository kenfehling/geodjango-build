Compared to its nearby areas, West Chester is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Chester is valued at $378,400. This is about $69,860 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $160,900 (73%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $215,400 (132%) higher.

Rents

In West Chester the median rent is $1,293 per month. It is approximately $215 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $231 (21%) higher, and is approximately $251 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
