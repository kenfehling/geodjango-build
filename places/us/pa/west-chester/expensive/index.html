Home prices

West Chester's median home value is $378,400. It is about $69,860 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $160,900 (73%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $215,400 (132%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

West Chester's median rent is $1,293 per month. This is about $215 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $231 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $251 (24%) higher.
