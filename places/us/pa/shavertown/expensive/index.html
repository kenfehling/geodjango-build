Home prices

Shavertown's median home price is $141,000. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $7,440 (5%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $76,500 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $9,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

Shavertown's median monthly rent is $640. This is about $130 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $422 (39%) lower, and is roughly $364 (36%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
