In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mount Union is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Union costs $77,300. It is roughly $5,900 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $140,200 (64%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $3,500 (4%) lower.

Rents

Mount Union's median monthly rent is $539. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $184 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $523 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $317 (37%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
