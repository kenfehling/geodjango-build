Compared to other places in this area, Lewistown is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The median home in Lewistown costs $74,600. This is approximately $18,580 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $142,900 (65%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $6,200 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Lewistown the median rent is $649 a month. This is roughly $77 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $413 (38%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $207 (24%) lower.
