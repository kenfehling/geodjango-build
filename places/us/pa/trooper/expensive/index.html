Home prices

The value of the median home in Trooper is $324,200. This is about $27,740 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $106,700 (49%) higher, and is approximately $281,800 (46%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Trooper is $1,136 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $357 (23%) lower. It is about $74 (6%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $307 (21%) lower.
