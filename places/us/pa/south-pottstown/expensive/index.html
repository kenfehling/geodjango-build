In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Pottstown is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The value of the median home in South Pottstown is $184,500. This is roughly $16,440 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $33,000 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $21,500 (13%) higher.

Rents

South Pottstown's median monthly rent is $1,047. It is roughly $226 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is is very similar.
