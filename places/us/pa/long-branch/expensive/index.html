Compared to other places in this area, Long Branch is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Long Branch is $113,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $27,080 (31%) higher. It is roughly $104,000 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $11,500 (9%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Long Branch the median monthly rent is $744. It is approximately $14 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $214 (22%) lower.
