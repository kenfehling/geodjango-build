Home prices

The median home price in Long Branch is $113,500. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $27,080 (31%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $104,000 (47%) lower, and is about $11,500 (9%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Long Branch is $744. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $14 (1%) higher.It is roughly $318 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $214 (22%) lower.
