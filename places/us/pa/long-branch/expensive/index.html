Compared to nearby areas, Long Branch is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Long Branch is valued at $113,500. This is approximately $27,080 (31%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $104,000 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $11,500 (9%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Long Branch the median rent is $744 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $14 (1%) higher. It is about $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $214 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
