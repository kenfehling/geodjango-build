Compared to the surrounding areas, Long Branch is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Long Branch is valued at $113,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $27,080 (31%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $104,000 (47%) lower, and it is about $11,500 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Long Branch is $744 per month. This is approximately $14 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $318 (29%) lower, and is roughly $214 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
