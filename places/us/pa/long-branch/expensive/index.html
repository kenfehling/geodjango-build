Compared to other places in this area, Long Branch is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Long Branch is $113,500. It is roughly $27,080 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $104,000 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $11,500 (9%) lower.

Rents

Long Branch's median rent is $744 a month. This is roughly $14 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $318 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $214 (22%) lower.
