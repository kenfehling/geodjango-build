Home prices

The value of the median home in Smithfield is $102,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $17,340 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $114,700 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $22,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Smithfield is $710 per month. It is about $101 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $352 (33%) lower, and is about $248 (25%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
