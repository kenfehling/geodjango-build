Home prices

Smithfield's median home price is $102,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $17,340 (20%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $114,700 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $22,200 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Smithfield the median monthly rent is $710. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $101 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $352 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $248 (25%) lower.
