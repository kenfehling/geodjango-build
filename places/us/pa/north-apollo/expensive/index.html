Home prices

The median home in North Apollo is valued at $102,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $33,220 (48%) higher. It is approximately $115,300 (53%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $22,800 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Apollo is $619. It is roughly $30 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $443 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $339 (35%) lower.
