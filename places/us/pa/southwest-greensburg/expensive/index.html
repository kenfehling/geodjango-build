Compared to its nearby areas, Southwest Greensburg is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Southwest Greensburg is valued at $118,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $19,220 (19%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $99,300 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $6,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

Southwest Greensburg's median rent is $697 per month. It is approximately $14 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $261 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
