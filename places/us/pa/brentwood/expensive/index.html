Brentwood's median home cost is $98,500. It is approximately $30,740 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $119,000 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $26,500 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Brentwood the median rent is $764 per month. It is about $90 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $298 (28%) lower, and is about $194 (20%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
