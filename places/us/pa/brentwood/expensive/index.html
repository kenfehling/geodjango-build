The median home price in Brentwood is $98,500. It is about $30,740 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $119,000 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $26,500 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Brentwood the median monthly rent is $764. It is approximately $90 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $298 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $194 (20%) lower.
