Home prices

The value of the median home in Deemston is $157,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $84,220 (115%) higher. It is roughly $60,300 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $32,200 (25%) higher.

Rents

In Deemston the median rent is $480 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $424 (46%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $582 (54%) lower, and is approximately $478 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
