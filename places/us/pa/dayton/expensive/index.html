Home prices

Dayton's median home price is $78,100. This is approximately $4,940 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $139,400 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $46,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $556. This is roughly $120 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $506 (47%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $402 (41%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
