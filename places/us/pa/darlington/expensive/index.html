In comparison to its surrounding areas, Darlington is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home cost in Darlington is $95,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $6,720 (6%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $122,500 (56%) lower, and it is about $25,400 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Darlington the median monthly rent is $645. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $28 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $417 (39%) lower, and is about $74 (10%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
