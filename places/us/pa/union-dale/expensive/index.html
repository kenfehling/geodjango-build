Home prices

The median home in Union Dale is valued at $172,100. This is approximately $63,000 (57%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $45,400 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $40,800 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Union Dale's median rent is $638 per month. It is about $79 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $366 (36%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
