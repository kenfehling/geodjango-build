In comparison to nearby areas, Montgomery is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Reading, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $107,400. It is approximately $37,040 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $110,100 (50%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $34,200 (46%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montgomery is $773. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $32 (3%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $289 (27%) lower, and it is about $38 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Reading.
