In comparison to other places in this area, Montgomery is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Reading.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $107,400. It is approximately $37,040 (25%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $110,100 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $34,200 (46%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

In Montgomery the median monthly rent is $773. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $32 (3%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $289 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Reading is roughly $38 (4%) lower.
