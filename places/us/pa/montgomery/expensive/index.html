Compared to other places in this area, Montgomery is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Reading however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomery costs $107,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $37,040 (25%) lower.It is about $110,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reading it is about $34,200 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Montgomery the median rent is $773 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $32 (3%) lower.It is approximately $289 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Reading is approximately $38 (4%) lower.
