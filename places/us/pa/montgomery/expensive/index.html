In comparison to its nearby areas, Montgomery is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Reading however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Montgomery is $107,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $37,040 (25%) lower. It is roughly $110,100 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $34,200 (46%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

Montgomery's median monthly rent is $773. This is approximately $32 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $289 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Reading it is roughly $38 (4%) lower.
