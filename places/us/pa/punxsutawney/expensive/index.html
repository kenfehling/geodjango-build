Home prices

The price of the median home in Punxsutawney is $83,700. This is about $2,740 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $133,800 (61%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $41,300 (33%) lower.

Rents

Punxsutawney's median rent is $629 per month. It is about $46 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $433 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $329 (34%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
