Home prices

The cost of the median home in Jerseytown is $97,500. It is roughly $56,020 (36%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $120,000 (55%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown is roughly $33,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

Jerseytown's median rent is $775 per month. This is approximately $19 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $229 (22%) lower.
