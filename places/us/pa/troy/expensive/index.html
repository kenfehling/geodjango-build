In comparison to its surrounding areas, Troy is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Scranton, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Troy is valued at $139,500. This is about $12,960 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $78,000 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Scranton is about $36,500 (35%) higher.

Rents

Troy's median monthly rent is $625. It is roughly $30 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $157 (20%) lower compared to the city of Scranton.
