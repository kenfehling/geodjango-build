Home prices

Rents

In Troy the median monthly rent is $625. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $30 (4%) lower.It is approximately $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $157 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Scranton.
