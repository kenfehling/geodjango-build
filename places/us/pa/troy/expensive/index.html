In comparison to the surrounding areas, Troy is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Scranton, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Troy costs $139,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $12,960 (10%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $78,000 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Scranton it is roughly $36,500 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Troy is $625. It is approximately $30 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $157 (20%) lower compared to the city of Scranton.
