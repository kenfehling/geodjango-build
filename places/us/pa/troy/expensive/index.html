Compared to nearby areas, Troy is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Scranton, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Troy is $139,500. It is approximately $12,960 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $78,000 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $36,500 (35%) higher compared to the city of Scranton.

Rents

Troy's median rent is $625 per month. It is about $30 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Scranton it is approximately $157 (20%) lower.
