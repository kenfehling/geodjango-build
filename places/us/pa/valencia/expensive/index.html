Rents

Valencia's median rent is $2,446 per month. It is roughly $1,189 (94%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,384 (130%) higher, and is approximately $1,488 (155%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
