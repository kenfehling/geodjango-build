Home prices

Valencia's median home price is $155,000. This is about $54,620 (26%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $62,500 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $30,000 (24%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Valencia is $2,446 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $1,189 (94%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,384 (130%) higher, and it is approximately $1,488 (155%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
