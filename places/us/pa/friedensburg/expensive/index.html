Home prices

The median home price in Friedensburg is $143,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $52,200 (57%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $74,100 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $19,600 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Friedensburg is $771 per month. This is approximately $36 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $291 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $271 (26%) lower.
