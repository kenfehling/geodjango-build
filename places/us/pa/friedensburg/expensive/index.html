In comparison to other places in this area, Friedensburg is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Friedensburg costs $143,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $52,200 (57%) higher.It is approximately $74,100 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $19,600 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Friedensburg is $771. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $36 (4%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $291 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $271 (26%) lower.
