Home prices

The price of the median home in Bowmanstown is $141,100. It is about $21,660 (18%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $76,400 (35%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $21,900 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Bowmanstown is $736 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $35 (4%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $306 (29%) lower.
