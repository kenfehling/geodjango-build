Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cheswick is $125,500. It is roughly $11,160 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $92,000 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Cheswick's median monthly rent is $940. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $187 (24%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $122 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $18 (1%) lower.
