Home prices

The median home in Hyde Park costs $115,800. This is approximately $34,220 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $101,700 (46%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $47,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Hyde Park the median monthly rent is $858. This is about $283 (24%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $204 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $184 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
