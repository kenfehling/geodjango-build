Home prices

The median home in Star Junction is valued at $81,500. This is roughly $15,080 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $136,000 (62%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $43,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Star Junction is $654. This is approximately $81 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $408 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $304 (31%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
