Home prices

The median home in Allenwood is valued at $155,700. It is approximately $29,500 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $61,800 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $82,500 (112%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

Allenwood's median monthly rent is $938. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $247 (35%) higher. It is about $124 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $127 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.
