In comparison to its surrounding areas, Philadelphia is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of New York, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Philadelphia costs $163,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $41,880 (20%) lower.It is approximately $54,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $443,000 (73%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Philadelphia is $1,042 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $95 (8%) lower.It is roughly $20 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $401 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
