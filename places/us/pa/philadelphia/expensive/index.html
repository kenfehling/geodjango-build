In comparison to the surrounding areas, Philadelphia is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of New Yorkhowever, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Philadelphia's median home value is $163,000. This is about $41,880 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $54,500 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $443,000 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Philadelphia is $1,042. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $95 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $20 (1%) lower, and is roughly $401 (27%) lower compared to the city of New York.
