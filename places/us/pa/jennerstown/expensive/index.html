Rents

In Jennerstown the median monthly rent is $876. It is approximately $347 (65%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $186 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $82 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
