Home prices

Rents

Greens Landing's median rent is $903 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $256 (39%) higher. It is approximately $159 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $89 (10%) higher.
