In comparison to the surrounding areas, Greens Landing is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Greens Landing is valued at $126,700. This is approximately $15,560 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $90,800 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $32,300 (34%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Greens Landing the median monthly rent is $903. This is roughly $256 (39%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $159 (14%) lower, and is about $89 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
