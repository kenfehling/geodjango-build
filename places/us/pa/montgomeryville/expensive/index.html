Compared to other places in this area, Montgomeryville is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomeryville is valued at $350,200. It is approximately $79,140 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $132,700 (61%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $255,800 (42%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomeryville is $1,663 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $425 (34%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $601 (56%) higher, and it is approximately $220 (15%) higher compared to the city of New York.
