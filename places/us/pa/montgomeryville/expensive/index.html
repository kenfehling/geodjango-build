Compared to its surrounding areas, Montgomeryville is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Montgomeryville costs $350,200. This is roughly $79,140 (29%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $132,700 (61%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $255,800 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Montgomeryville the median rent is $1,663 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $425 (34%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $601 (56%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $220 (15%) higher.
