Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mifflinville is $133,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $22,140 (19%) higher. It is roughly $84,000 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $2,200 (1%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median rent in Mifflinville is $894 a month. This is approximately $124 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $168 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $110 (10%) lower.
