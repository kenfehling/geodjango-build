Compared to the surrounding areas, Industry is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Industry is $139,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $46,900 (50%) higher. It is roughly $78,000 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $14,500 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Industry's median monthly rent is $820. This is approximately $70 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $138 (14%) lower.
