Home prices

Rents

In Industry the median rent is $820 a month. It is about $70 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $242 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $138 (14%) lower.
