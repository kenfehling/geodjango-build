Home prices

The median home in Liberty is valued at $92,900. This is about $32,220 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $124,600 (57%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $12,100 (14%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Liberty's median rent is $769 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $96 (14%) higher.It is roughly $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $87 (10%) lower.
