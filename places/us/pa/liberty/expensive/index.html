Compared to other places in this area, Liberty is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Liberty costs $92,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $32,220 (25%) lower. It is approximately $124,600 (57%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $12,100 (14%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Liberty's median rent is $769 a month. This is approximately $96 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $293 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is about $87 (10%) lower.
