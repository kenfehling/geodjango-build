Home prices

The median home in Versailles costs $68,200. It is approximately $18,120 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $149,300 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $56,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Versailles is $763 per month. This is roughly $25 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $299 (28%) lower, and it is about $195 (20%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
