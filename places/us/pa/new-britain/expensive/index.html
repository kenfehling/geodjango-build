Home prices

The value of the median home in New Britain is $294,500. This is roughly $44,140 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $77,000 (35%) higher, and it is about $311,500 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in New Britain is $1,643 a month. This is approximately $283 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $581 (54%) higher, and it is roughly $200 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
