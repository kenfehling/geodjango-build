Home prices

The value of the median home in New Britain is $294,500. This is roughly $44,140 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $77,000 (35%) higher, and compared to the city of New York is about $311,500 (51%) lower.

Rents

In New Britain the median rent is $1,643 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $283 (20%) higher.It is approximately $581 (54%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $200 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
