In comparison to other places in this area, Oklahoma is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oklahoma is $107,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $33,100 (44%) higher. It is approximately $110,200 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $17,700 (14%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Oklahoma's median rent is $697 a month. This is roughly $53 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $365 (34%) lower, and is roughly $261 (27%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
