In comparison to its nearby areas, East Berlin is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Berlin is $184,800. It is approximately $30,660 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $32,700 (15%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $416,700 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Berlin is $896. This is about $51 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $166 (15%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Washington is about $645 (41%) lower.
