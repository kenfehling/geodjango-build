Compared to its surrounding areas, East Berlin is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Berlin costs $184,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $30,660 (19%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $32,700 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $416,700 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Berlin is $896. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $51 (5%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $645 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
