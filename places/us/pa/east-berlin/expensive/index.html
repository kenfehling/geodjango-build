In comparison to the surrounding areas, East Berlin is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in East Berlin is $184,800. It is roughly $30,660 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $32,700 (15%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $416,700 (69%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

In East Berlin the median monthly rent is $896. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $51 (5%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $166 (15%) lower, and is roughly $645 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
