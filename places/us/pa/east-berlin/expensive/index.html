Compared to its nearby areas, East Berlin is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Washington, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

East Berlin's median home cost is $184,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $30,660 (19%) higher. It is about $32,700 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is approximately $416,700 (69%) lower.

Rents

East Berlin's median monthly rent is $896. This is approximately $51 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $166 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $645 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
