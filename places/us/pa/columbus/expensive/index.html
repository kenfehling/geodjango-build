Home prices

Columbus's median home price is $93,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $10,060 (12%) higher.It is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is roughly $3,700 (4%) higher.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $708 per month. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $354 (33%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo is approximately $68 (8%) lower.
