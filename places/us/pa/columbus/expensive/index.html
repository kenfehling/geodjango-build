Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $93,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $10,060 (12%) higher.It is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo it is about $3,700 (4%) higher.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $708 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $354 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $68 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Buffalo.
