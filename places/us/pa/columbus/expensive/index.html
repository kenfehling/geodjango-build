Compared to its surrounding areas, Columbus is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus costs $93,500. This is about $10,060 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $124,000 (57%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Buffalo it is about $3,700 (4%) higher.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $708 a month. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $354 (33%) lower, and is roughly $68 (8%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
