Compared to other places in this area, Columbus is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Columbus is $93,500. This is roughly $10,060 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $124,000 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $3,700 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Columbus is $708 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $354 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $68 (8%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
