Home prices

Rents

In Bessemer the median monthly rent is $679. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $41 (6%) higher.It is approximately $383 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $40 (5%) lower.
