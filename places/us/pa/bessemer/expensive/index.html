Home prices

The median home value in Bessemer is $95,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $10,680 (12%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower, and it is roughly $26,200 (37%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Bessemer's median monthly rent is $679. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $41 (6%) higher. It is approximately $383 (36%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $40 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
