In comparison to other places in this area, Bessemer is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Bessemer is $95,800. This is roughly $10,680 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $121,700 (55%) lower, and is roughly $26,200 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

Bessemer's median monthly rent is $679. It is roughly $41 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $383 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $40 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
