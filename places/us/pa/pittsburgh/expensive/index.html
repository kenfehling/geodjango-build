In comparison to other places in this area, Pittsburgh is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Pittsburgh is $125,000. This is approximately $31,433 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $92,500 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation.

Rents

In Pittsburgh the median monthly rent is $958. This is approximately $76 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $104 (9%) lower.
