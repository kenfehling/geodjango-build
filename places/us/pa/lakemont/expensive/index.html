Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakemont is $124,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $5,600 (4%) higher.It is approximately $93,100 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Hagerstown is approximately $31,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Lakemont the median rent is $808 per month. It is approximately $38 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and it is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to the city of Hagerstown.
