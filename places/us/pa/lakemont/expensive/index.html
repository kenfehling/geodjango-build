Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakemont is $124,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $5,600 (4%) higher. It is roughly $93,100 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Hagerstown is roughly $31,300 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakemont is $808. This is approximately $38 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and is about $24 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Hagerstown.
