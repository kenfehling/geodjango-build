Home prices

The median home in Allenport costs $74,400. It is roughly $2,420 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $143,100 (65%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $6,400 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Allenport the median monthly rent is $616. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $91 (12%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $446 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is about $240 (28%) lower.
