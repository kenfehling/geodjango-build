Compared to the surrounding areas, Martinsburg is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Frederick, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Martinsburg costs $137,500. It is roughly $25,340 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $80,000 (36%) lower, and it is roughly $132,900 (49%) lower compared to the city of Frederick.

Rents

The median rent in Martinsburg is $652 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $81 (11%) lower. It is approximately $410 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Frederick is about $744 (53%) lower.
