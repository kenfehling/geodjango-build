Home prices

Rents

Martinsburg's median rent is $652 per month. It is about $81 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $410 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $744 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of Frederick.
