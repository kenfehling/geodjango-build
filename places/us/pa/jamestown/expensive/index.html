In comparison to its nearby areas, Jamestown is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Jamestown is $73,500. This is roughly $16,740 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $144,000 (66%) lower, and is roughly $3,900 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Jamestown is $752 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $131 (21%) higher.It is about $310 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is about $33 (4%) higher.
