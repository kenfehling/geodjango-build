Compared to the surrounding areas, Jamestown is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Clevelandhowever, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Jamestown is $73,500. This is roughly $16,740 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $144,000 (66%) lower, and it is roughly $3,900 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Jamestown is $752 a month. This is roughly $131 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $310 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $33 (4%) higher.
