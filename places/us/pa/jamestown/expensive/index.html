In comparison to nearby areas, Jamestown is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jamestown is valued at $73,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $16,740 (18%) lower. It is about $144,000 (66%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cleveland is about $3,900 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Jamestown is $752 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $131 (21%) higher. It is approximately $310 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $33 (4%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
