In comparison to other places in this area, Jamestown is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Jamestown costs $73,500. It is about $16,740 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $144,000 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $3,900 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Jamestown the median monthly rent is $752. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $131 (21%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $310 (29%) lower, and it is about $33 (4%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
