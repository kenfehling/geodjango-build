Home prices

The median home price in Lamar is $138,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $4,800 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $79,100 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is about $57,600 (71%) higher.

Rents

Lamar's median monthly rent is $653. Compared to other places in this area this is about $79 (10%) lower. It is roughly $409 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $203 (23%) lower.
