Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lamar is $138,400. This is approximately $4,800 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $79,100 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $57,600 (71%) higher.

Rents

Lamar's median rent is $653 a month. This is approximately $79 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $409 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $203 (23%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
