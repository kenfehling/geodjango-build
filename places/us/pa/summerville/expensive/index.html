In comparison to other places in this area, Summerville is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Summerville costs $71,400. This is roughly $16,620 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $146,100 (67%) lower, and is roughly $53,600 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Summerville is $498 a month. It is roughly $143 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $564 (53%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $460 (48%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
