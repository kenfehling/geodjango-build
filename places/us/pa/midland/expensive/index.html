Compared to other places in this area, Midland is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Midland is valued at $64,800. This is about $42,740 (39%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $152,700 (70%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $60,200 (48%) lower.

Rents

Midland's median monthly rent is $539. This is roughly $268 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $523 (49%) lower, and is roughly $419 (43%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
