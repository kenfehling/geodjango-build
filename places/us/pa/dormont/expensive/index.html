Home prices

The median home value in Dormont is $147,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $26,640 (22%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $70,000 (32%) lower, and it is about $22,500 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Dormont the median monthly rent is $860. It is about $79 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $202 (19%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $98 (10%) lower.
