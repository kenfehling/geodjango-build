In comparison to the surrounding areas, Riverside is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Allentown however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside is valued at $173,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $32,800 (23%) higher. It is roughly $43,600 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $42,600 (32%) higher compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Riverside's median monthly rent is $821. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $115 (16%) higher. It is approximately $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $183 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
