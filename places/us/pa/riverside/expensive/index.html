Compared to its surrounding areas, Riverside is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside costs $173,900. This is roughly $32,800 (23%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $43,600 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $42,600 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Riverside the median rent is $821 a month. This is about $115 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $241 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $183 (18%) lower.
