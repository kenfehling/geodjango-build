The cost of the median home in Central City is $65,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $24,180 (27%) lower. It is about $152,300 (70%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $59,800 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Central City is $680. It is about $38 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $382 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $278 (29%) lower.
