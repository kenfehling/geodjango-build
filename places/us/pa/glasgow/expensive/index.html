Home prices

Glasgow's median home value is $48,300. It is approximately $41,500 (46%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $169,200 (77%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $76,700 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Glasgow is $821. It is about $140 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $241 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $137 (14%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
