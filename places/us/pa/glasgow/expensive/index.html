Rents

Glasgow's median monthly rent is $821. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $140 (20%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $241 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $137 (14%) lower.
