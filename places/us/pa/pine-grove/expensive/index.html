Home prices

Rents

Pine Grove's median rent is $688 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $39 (6%) higher. It is about $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $354 (33%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
