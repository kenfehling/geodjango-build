Home prices

The median home in Pine Grove is valued at $126,200. It is approximately $19,680 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $91,300 (41%) lower, and is approximately $36,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Pine Grove's median rent is $688 a month. This is roughly $39 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $374 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $354 (33%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
