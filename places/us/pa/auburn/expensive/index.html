In comparison to the surrounding areas, Auburn is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Auburn's median home cost is $96,700. This is approximately $31,400 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $120,800 (55%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $66,300 (40%) lower.

Rents

Auburn's median rent is $825 a month. It is roughly $33 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $237 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $217 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
