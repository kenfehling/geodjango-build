Home prices

Auburn's median home value is $96,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $31,400 (24%) lower. It is about $120,800 (55%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $66,300 (40%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $825 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $33 (4%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $237 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $217 (20%) lower.
