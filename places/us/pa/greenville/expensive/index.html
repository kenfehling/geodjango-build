In comparison to its surrounding areas, Greenville is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

Greenville's median home value is $73,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $15,160 (17%) lower. It is about $143,800 (66%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $4,100 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greenville is $605. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $32 (5%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $457 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $114 (15%) lower.
