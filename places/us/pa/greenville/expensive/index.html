Compared to other places in this area, Greenville is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Greenville is $73,700. This is roughly $15,160 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $143,800 (66%) lower, and is about $4,100 (5%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median rent in Greenville is $605 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $32 (5%) lower. It is roughly $457 (43%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $114 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
