In comparison to the surrounding areas, Greenville is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cleveland.

Home prices

The median home in Greenville costs $73,700. This is about $15,160 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $143,800 (66%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $4,100 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Greenville the median monthly rent is $605. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $32 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $457 (43%) lower, and is approximately $114 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cleveland.
