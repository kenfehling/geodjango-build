Compared to the surrounding areas, Northumberland is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in Northumberland is valued at $127,500. It is about $14,080 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $90,000 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $3,800 (2%) lower.

Rents

Northumberland's median rent is $761 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is about $301 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $243 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
