Home prices

The median home in Northumberland is valued at $127,500. It is approximately $14,080 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $90,000 (41%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $3,800 (2%) lower.

Rents

Northumberland's median monthly rent is $761. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $301 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $243 (24%) lower.
