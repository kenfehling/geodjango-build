Home prices

The price of the median home in Northumberland is $127,500. It is about $14,080 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $90,000 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is about $3,800 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northumberland is $761. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas.It is about $301 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown is about $243 (24%) lower.
