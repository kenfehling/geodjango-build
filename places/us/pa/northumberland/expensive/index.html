In comparison to other places in this area, Northumberland is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home value in Northumberland is $127,500. This is about $14,080 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $90,000 (41%) lower, and is about $3,800 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Northumberland's median monthly rent is $761. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $301 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $243 (24%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
