In comparison to nearby areas, Lenape Heights is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lenape Heights is $114,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $25,760 (29%) higher. It is approximately $103,500 (47%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $11,000 (8%) lower.

Rents

Lenape Heights's median monthly rent is $833. It is roughly $145 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $229 (21%) lower, and is approximately $125 (13%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
