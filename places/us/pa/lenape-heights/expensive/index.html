Home prices

The median home in Lenape Heights is valued at $114,000. It is roughly $25,760 (29%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $103,500 (47%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $11,000 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Lenape Heights the median monthly rent is $833. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $145 (21%) higher. It is approximately $229 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $125 (13%) lower.
