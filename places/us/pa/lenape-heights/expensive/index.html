Home prices

Rents

Lenape Heights's median rent is $833 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $145 (21%) higher.It is approximately $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $125 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
