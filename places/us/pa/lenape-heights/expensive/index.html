Rents

Lenape Heights's median monthly rent is $833. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $145 (21%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $229 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $125 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
