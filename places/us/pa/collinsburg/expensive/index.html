In comparison to the surrounding areas, Collinsburg is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Collinsburg is $103,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $13,860 (15%) higher. It is about $113,800 (52%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $21,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Collinsburg the median rent is $906 a month. This is about $201 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $156 (14%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $52 (5%) lower.
