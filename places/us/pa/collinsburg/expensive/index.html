Home prices

The median home cost in Collinsburg is $103,700. This is roughly $13,860 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $113,800 (52%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $21,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Collinsburg the median rent is $906 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $201 (28%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $156 (14%) lower, and is roughly $52 (5%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
