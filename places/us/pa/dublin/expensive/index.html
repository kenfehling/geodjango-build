Rents

The median monthly rent in Dublin is $1,128. This is approximately $162 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $66 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $315 (21%) lower.
