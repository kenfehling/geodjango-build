Compared to the surrounding areas, Dublin is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of New York however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dublin is $325,000. This is about $32,940 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $107,500 (49%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is about $281,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Dublin the median rent is $1,128 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $162 (12%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $66 (6%) higher, and is roughly $315 (21%) lower compared to the city of New York.
