Home prices

The median home cost in Lincoln is $95,100. It is approximately $22,160 (30%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $122,400 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $29,900 (23%) lower.

Rents

Lincoln's median monthly rent is $667. Compared to other places in this area it is about $94 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $395 (37%) lower, and is roughly $291 (30%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
