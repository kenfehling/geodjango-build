Compared to the surrounding areas, Lincoln is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lincoln costs $95,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $22,160 (30%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $122,400 (56%) lower, and is approximately $29,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Lincoln's median monthly rent is $667. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $94 (12%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $395 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $291 (30%) lower.
