Home prices

The median home in Lincoln costs $95,100. It is approximately $22,160 (30%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $122,400 (56%) lower, and is about $29,900 (23%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Lincoln is $667 per month. This is approximately $94 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $395 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $291 (30%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
