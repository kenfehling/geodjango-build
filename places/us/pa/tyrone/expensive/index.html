In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tyrone is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Hagerstown, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tyrone is valued at $95,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $16,260 (14%) lower. It is roughly $121,600 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $59,800 (38%) lower compared to the city of Hagerstown.

Rents

Tyrone's median rent is $631 a month. This is about $62 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $431 (40%) lower, and is about $201 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Hagerstown.
