Home prices

The median home in Freedom is valued at $75,800. This is about $45,860 (37%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $141,700 (65%) lower, and is approximately $49,200 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Freedom's median monthly rent is $610. This is roughly $22 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $452 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $348 (36%) lower.
