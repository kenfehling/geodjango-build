Home prices

Rents

In South Philipsburg the median monthly rent is $874. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $241 (38%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $188 (17%) lower, and it is about $180 (17%) lower compared to the city of State College.
