Home prices

The median home in Brookhaven is valued at $200,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $78,120 (63%) higher. It is about $16,700 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $37,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Brookhaven's median monthly rent is $1,282. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $315 (32%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $220 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $240 (23%) higher.
