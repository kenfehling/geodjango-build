Home prices

The median home in Lavelle is valued at $100,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $38,040 (61%) higher. It is about $117,200 (53%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $31,000 (23%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

In Lavelle the median rent is $565 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $188 (24%) lower. It is roughly $497 (46%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown it is approximately $439 (43%) lower.
