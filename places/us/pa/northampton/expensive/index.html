Home prices

The median home value in Northampton is $174,600. It is roughly $6,720 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $42,900 (19%) lower, and is approximately $431,400 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Northampton's median rent is $1,011 per month. This is about $27 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $51 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $432 (29%) lower.
