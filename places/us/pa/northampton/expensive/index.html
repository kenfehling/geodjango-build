Home prices

The value of the median home in Northampton is $174,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $6,720 (3%) lower. It is approximately $42,900 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $431,400 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Northampton the median monthly rent is $1,011. It is approximately $27 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $51 (4%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of New York is about $432 (29%) lower.
