Home prices

The median home in Northampton is valued at $174,600. This is approximately $6,720 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $42,900 (19%) lower, and is roughly $431,400 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northampton is $1,011. It is about $27 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $51 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $432 (29%) lower compared to the city of New York.
