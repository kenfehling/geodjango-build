Home prices

The median home in Pleasant Hills costs $166,900. This is roughly $8,400 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $50,600 (23%) lower, and is approximately $41,900 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Pleasant Hills the median monthly rent is $995. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $104 (11%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $67 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $37 (3%) higher.
