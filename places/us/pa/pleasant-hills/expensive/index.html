Home prices

Pleasant Hills's median home value is $166,900. It is approximately $8,400 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $50,600 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $41,900 (33%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Pleasant Hills's median rent is $995 a month. This is about $104 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $67 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $37 (3%) higher.
