Home prices

The median home value in Pleasant Hills is $166,900. This is about $8,400 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $50,600 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $41,900 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pleasant Hills is $995. This is about $104 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $67 (6%) lower, and it is about $37 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
