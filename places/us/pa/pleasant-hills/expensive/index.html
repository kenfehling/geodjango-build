Rents

Pleasant Hills's median monthly rent is $995. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $104 (11%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $67 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $37 (3%) higher.
