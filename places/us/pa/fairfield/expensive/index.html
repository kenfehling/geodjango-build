Home prices

The median home price in Fairfield is $181,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $11,400 (5%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $36,000 (16%) lower, and it is about $420,000 (69%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

Fairfield's median rent is $941 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $128 (15%) higher. It is approximately $121 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $600 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
