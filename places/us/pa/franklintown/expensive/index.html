Home prices

The value of the median home in Franklintown is $126,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $45,260 (26%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $91,000 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $475,000 (78%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Franklintown is $963 a month. This is roughly $152 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $99 (9%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $578 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
