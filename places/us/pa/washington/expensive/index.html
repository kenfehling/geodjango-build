In comparison to its surrounding areas, Washington is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Washington's median home price is $100,700. This is roughly $90,580 (47%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $116,800 (53%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $24,300 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Washington's median monthly rent is $697. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $224 (24%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $365 (34%) lower, and is about $261 (27%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
