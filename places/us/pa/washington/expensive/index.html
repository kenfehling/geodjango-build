Compared to other places in this area, Washington is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home cost in Washington is $100,700. It is roughly $90,580 (47%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $116,800 (53%) lower, and is about $24,300 (19%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Washington's median rent is $697 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $224 (24%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $365 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $261 (27%) lower.
