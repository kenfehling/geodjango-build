In comparison to other places in this area, Denver is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphiahowever, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Denver is valued at $172,500. It is about $21,000 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $45,000 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $9,500 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Denver the median monthly rent is $911. This is roughly $59 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $151 (14%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $131 (12%) lower.
