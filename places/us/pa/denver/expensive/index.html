In comparison to nearby areas, Denver is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Denver's median home value is $172,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $21,000 (10%) lower. It is about $45,000 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $9,500 (5%) higher.

Rents

Denver's median monthly rent is $911. It is roughly $59 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $151 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $131 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
