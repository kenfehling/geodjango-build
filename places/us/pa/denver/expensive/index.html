Compared to its nearby areas, Denver is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Denver is $172,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $21,000 (10%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $45,000 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $9,500 (5%) higher.

Rents

Denver's median monthly rent is $911. This is approximately $59 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $151 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $131 (12%) lower.
