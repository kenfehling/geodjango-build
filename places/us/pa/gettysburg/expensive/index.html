Compared to other places in this area, Gettysburg is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Gettysburg is $227,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $47,760 (26%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $9,800 (4%) higher, and is approximately $374,200 (62%) lower compared to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Gettysburg is $891. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $125 (12%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $171 (16%) lower, and it is about $650 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
