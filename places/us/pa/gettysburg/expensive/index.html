In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gettysburg is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Gettysburg is $227,300. This is approximately $47,760 (26%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $9,800 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $374,200 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Gettysburg the median monthly rent is $891. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $125 (12%) lower. It is about $171 (16%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $650 (42%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
