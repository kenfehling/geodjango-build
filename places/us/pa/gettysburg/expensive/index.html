Home prices

The value of the median home in Gettysburg is $227,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $47,760 (26%) higher. It is roughly $9,800 (4%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $374,200 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Gettysburg the median rent is $891 a month. It is about $125 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $171 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $650 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
