Compared to its surrounding areas, Gettysburg is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Gettysburg's median home cost is $227,300. This is approximately $47,760 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $9,800 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington is roughly $374,200 (62%) lower.

Rents

In Gettysburg the median monthly rent is $891. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $125 (12%) lower.It is approximately $171 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $650 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
