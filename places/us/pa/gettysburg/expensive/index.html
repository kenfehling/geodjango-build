In comparison to other places in this area, Gettysburg is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Washington.

Home prices

Gettysburg's median home price is $227,300. It is roughly $47,760 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $9,800 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Washington it is roughly $374,200 (62%) lower.

Rents

Gettysburg's median monthly rent is $891. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $125 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $171 (16%) lower, and is roughly $650 (42%) lower compared to the city of Washington.
