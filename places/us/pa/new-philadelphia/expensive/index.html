The median home in New Philadelphia costs $58,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $14,740 (20%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $159,100 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $104,600 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Philadelphia is $600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $146 (19%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $462 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $442 (42%) lower.
