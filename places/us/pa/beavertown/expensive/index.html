Compared to other places in this area, Beavertown is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Reading.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Beavertown is $125,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $6,580 (5%) higher. It is about $91,800 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $52,500 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beavertown is $822. This is about $130 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $240 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reading is about $11 (1%) higher.
