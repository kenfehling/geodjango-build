Compared to its nearby areas, Beavertown is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Reading.

Home prices

The median home in Beavertown is valued at $125,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $6,580 (5%) higher. It is approximately $91,800 (42%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $52,500 (71%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

Beavertown's median rent is $822 per month. It is roughly $130 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $240 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.
