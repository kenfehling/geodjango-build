Compared to other places in this area, Beavertown is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Reading however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Beavertown's median home cost is $125,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $6,580 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $91,800 (42%) lower, and is roughly $52,500 (71%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

In Beavertown the median monthly rent is $822. This is about $130 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $240 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reading is roughly $11 (1%) higher.
