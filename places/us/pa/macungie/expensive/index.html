Home prices

The value of the median home in Macungie is $183,500. This is about $49,700 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $34,000 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $20,500 (12%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Macungie's median monthly rent is $1,203. It is roughly $199 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $141 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $161 (15%) higher.
