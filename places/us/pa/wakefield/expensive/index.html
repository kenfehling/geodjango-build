In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wakefield is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wakefield costs $236,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $34,860 (17%) higher. It is about $18,700 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $73,200 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Wakefield's median rent is $1,075 a month. It is roughly $98 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $33 (3%) higher.
