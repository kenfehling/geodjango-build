Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Wakefield is $1,075 a month. It is roughly $98 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $33 (3%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
