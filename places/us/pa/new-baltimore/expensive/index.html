Home prices

The median home value in New Baltimore is $78,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $16,740 (17%) lower. It is approximately $138,800 (63%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $46,300 (37%) lower.

Rents

New Baltimore's median monthly rent is $688. This is about $10 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $374 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $270 (28%) lower.
