Rents

The median monthly rent in Cambridge Springs is $583. It is approximately $182 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $479 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Erie it is roughly $127 (17%) lower.
