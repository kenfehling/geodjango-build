Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Dunnstown is $697 per month. It is approximately $174 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $159 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
