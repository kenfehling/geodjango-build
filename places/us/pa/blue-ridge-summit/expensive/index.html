Home prices

The median home in Blue Ridge Summit is valued at $173,400. This is roughly $2,820 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $44,100 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $428,100 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Blue Ridge Summit is $648 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $182 (21%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $414 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $893 (57%) lower.
