Home prices

The cost of the median home in West Falls is $145,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $39,800 (21%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $72,200 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is roughly $14,000 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in West Falls is $629 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $221 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $433 (40%) lower, and is approximately $375 (37%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
