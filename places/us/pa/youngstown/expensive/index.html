Home prices

The median home in Youngstown costs $89,300. This is approximately $31,180 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $128,200 (58%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $35,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Youngstown is $681 a month. It is approximately $57 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $381 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $277 (28%) lower.
