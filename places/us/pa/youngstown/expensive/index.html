In comparison to its surrounding areas, Youngstown is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home value in Youngstown is $89,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $31,180 (25%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $128,200 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $35,700 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Youngstown the median rent is $681 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $57 (7%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and is roughly $277 (28%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
