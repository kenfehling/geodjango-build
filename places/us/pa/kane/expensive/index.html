Home prices

The median home cost in Kane is $55,600. This is approximately $16,500 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $161,900 (74%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $34,200 (38%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median rent in Kane is $595 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9 (1%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $467 (43%) lower, and it is about $181 (23%) lower compared to the city of Buffalo.
