Home prices

The median home in St. Lawrence costs $151,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $5,740 (3%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $65,600 (30%) lower, and is roughly $11,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in St. Lawrence is $910 per month. It is approximately $361 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $152 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $132 (12%) lower.
