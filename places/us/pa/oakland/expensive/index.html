Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $74,300. It is approximately $20,460 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $143,200 (65%) lower, and is roughly $20,100 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

Oakland's median monthly rent is $666. This is approximately $30 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $396 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is about $148 (18%) lower.
