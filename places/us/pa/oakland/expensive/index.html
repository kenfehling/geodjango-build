In comparison to its surrounding areas, Oakland is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Oakland's median home value is $74,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $20,460 (21%) lower.It is roughly $143,200 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $20,100 (21%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakland is $666. This is roughly $30 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $396 (37%) lower, and it is about $148 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
