In comparison to the surrounding areas, McClure is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in McClure is valued at $97,600. It is roughly $16,320 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $119,900 (55%) lower, and is about $16,800 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in McClure is $718 per month. This is approximately $46 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $344 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $138 (16%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
