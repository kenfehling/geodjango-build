Compared to the surrounding areas, Milton is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Reading, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Milton is $131,500. Compared to nearby areas it is about $14,120 (9%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $86,000 (39%) lower, and is approximately $58,300 (79%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.

Rents

In Milton the median rent is $680 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $181 (21%) lower. It is roughly $382 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $131 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Reading.
