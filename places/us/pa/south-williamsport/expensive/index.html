Home prices

The median home cost in South Williamsport is $142,200. This is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $75,300 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $39,200 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Scranton.

Rents

South Williamsport's median monthly rent is $875. It is approximately $75 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $187 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Scranton is about $93 (11%) higher.
