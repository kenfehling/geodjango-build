The value of the median home in Oxford is $215,200. This is approximately $13,240 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $2,300 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $52,200 (32%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oxford is $998. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $158 (13%) lower. It is roughly $64 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $44 (4%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
