Home prices

The price of the median home in Petersburg is $83,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $30,040 (26%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $134,300 (61%) lower, and it is approximately $2,400 (2%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Petersburg's median monthly rent is $873. This is approximately $177 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $189 (17%) lower, and is about $17 (1%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.
