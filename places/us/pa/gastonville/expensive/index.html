Home prices

The median home in Gastonville costs $140,500. It is approximately $35,600 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $77,000 (35%) lower, and it is about $15,500 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Gastonville's median rent is $536 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $437 (44%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $526 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $422 (44%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
