Compared to its surrounding areas, Gastonville is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Gastonville is valued at $140,500. It is approximately $35,600 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $77,000 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $15,500 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Gastonville the median monthly rent is $536. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $437 (44%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $526 (49%) lower, and it is about $422 (44%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
