Home prices

The median home in Hickory is valued at $143,400. This is roughly $55,960 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $74,100 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $18,400 (14%) higher.

Rents

Hickory's median rent is $769 a month. It is roughly $57 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $189 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
