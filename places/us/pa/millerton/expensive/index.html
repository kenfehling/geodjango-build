Home prices

Millerton's median home price is $126,100. It is about $22,740 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $91,400 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $43,000 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In Millerton the median monthly rent is $550. It is about $226 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $512 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $298 (35%) lower.
