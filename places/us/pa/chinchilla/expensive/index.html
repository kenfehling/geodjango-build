Home prices

The median home cost in Chinchilla is $175,400. It is roughly $23,340 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $42,100 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown it is about $44,100 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Chinchilla the median rent is $771 a month. This is roughly $181 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $233 (23%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
