Compared to the surrounding areas, Westfield is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home in Westfield is valued at $85,700. It is approximately $11,740 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $131,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $2,600 (3%) higher compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Westfield is $510. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $119 (18%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $552 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $338 (39%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
