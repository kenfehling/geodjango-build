Compared to the surrounding areas, Royersford is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Royersford is $201,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $76,340 (27%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $16,000 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $38,500 (23%) higher.

Rents

Royersford's median rent is $1,130 per month. It is approximately $173 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $68 (6%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $88 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
