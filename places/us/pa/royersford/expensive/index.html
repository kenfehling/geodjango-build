The median home in Royersford costs $201,500. This is roughly $76,340 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $16,000 (7%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $38,500 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Royersford the median rent is $1,130 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $173 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $68 (6%) higher, and it is about $88 (8%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
