Home prices

The price of the median home in Latrobe is $117,800. It is roughly $3,020 (2%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $99,700 (45%) lower, and is roughly $7,200 (5%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Latrobe's median rent is $612 a month. This is roughly $140 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $450 (42%) lower, and is approximately $346 (36%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
