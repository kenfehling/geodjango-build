Much like the surrounding areas, it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Latrobe costs $117,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $3,020 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $99,700 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $7,200 (5%) lower.

Rents

Latrobe's median monthly rent is $612. This is roughly $140 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $450 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $346 (36%) lower.
