Home prices

Middleburg's median home cost is $134,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $1,480 (1%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $82,800 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $61,500 (84%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

The median rent in Middleburg is $627 a month. This is approximately $78 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $435 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $184 (22%) lower compared to the city of Reading.
