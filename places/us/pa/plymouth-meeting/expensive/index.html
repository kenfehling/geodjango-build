Home prices

The median home in Plymouth Meeting costs $364,700. This is roughly $66,480 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $147,200 (67%) higher, and is about $241,300 (39%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Plymouth Meeting the median monthly rent is $1,592. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $172 (12%) higher. It is approximately $530 (49%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $149 (10%) higher.
