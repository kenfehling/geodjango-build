In comparison to nearby areas, Marion Heights is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in Marion Heights is valued at $73,600. It is approximately $25,820 (54%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $143,900 (66%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $57,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

Marion Heights's median rent is $507 a month. It is about $127 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $555 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown it is roughly $497 (49%) lower.
