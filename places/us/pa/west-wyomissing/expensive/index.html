Home prices

West Wyomissing's median home price is $142,100. This is approximately $27,540 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $75,400 (34%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $20,900 (12%) lower.

Rents

West Wyomissing's median rent is $1,205 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $98 (7%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $143 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $163 (15%) higher.
