Home prices

The cost of the median home in New Albany is $104,700. It is approximately $22,600 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $112,800 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $1,700 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Scranton.

Rents

The median rent in New Albany is $663 per month. It is approximately $19 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $399 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Scranton is about $119 (15%) lower.
