Compared to its nearby areas, Burnham is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Burnham costs $82,700. This is approximately $8,860 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $134,800 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $1,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Burnham the median monthly rent is $741. This is approximately $34 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $321 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $115 (13%) lower.
