Home prices

The median home in Burnham costs $82,700. It is approximately $8,860 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $134,800 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is about $1,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Burnham is $741 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $34 (4%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $321 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $115 (13%) lower.
