Home prices

The value of the median home in Burnham is $82,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $8,860 (9%) lower. It is about $134,800 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $1,900 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Burnham is $741 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $34 (4%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $321 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $115 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
