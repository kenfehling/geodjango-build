Home prices

The median home in Trexlertown is valued at $197,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $58,780 (22%) lower. It is roughly $19,700 (9%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $34,800 (21%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Trexlertown the median rent is $1,652 per month. It is approximately $278 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $590 (55%) higher, and is approximately $610 (58%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
