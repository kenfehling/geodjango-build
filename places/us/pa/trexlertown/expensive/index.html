The median home in Trexlertown is valued at $197,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $58,780 (22%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $19,700 (9%) lower, and is approximately $34,800 (21%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents
