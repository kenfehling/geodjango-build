Compared to the surrounding areas, Altoona is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Altoona costs $88,500. It is about $31,800 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $129,000 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole.

Rents

In Altoona the median monthly rent is $697. It is about $84 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation.
