Compared to the surrounding areas, Palmdale is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Baltimore.

Home prices

The median home in Palmdale costs $176,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $32,240 (15%) lower.It is about $41,000 (18%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $16,400 (10%) higher compared to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

The median rent in Palmdale is $958 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $24 (2%) lower.It is about $104 (9%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $115 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.
