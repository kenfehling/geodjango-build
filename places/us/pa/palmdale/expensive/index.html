In comparison to its surrounding areas, Palmdale is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Baltimore.

Home prices

The median home in Palmdale is valued at $176,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $32,240 (15%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $41,000 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Baltimore is approximately $16,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palmdale is $958. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $24 (2%) lower. It is roughly $104 (9%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is approximately $115 (10%) lower.
