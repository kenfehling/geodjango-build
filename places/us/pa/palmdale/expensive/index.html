Home prices

The median home in Palmdale costs $176,500. It is roughly $32,240 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $41,000 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is approximately $16,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

Palmdale's median rent is $958 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $24 (2%) lower.It is roughly $104 (9%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Baltimore is approximately $115 (10%) lower.
