Home prices

The value of the median home in Crafton is $147,900. Compared to other places in this area this is about $24,420 (14%) lower.It is roughly $69,600 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $22,900 (18%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Crafton is $806 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $379 (31%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $256 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $152 (15%) lower.
