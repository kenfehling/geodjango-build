In comparison to its surrounding areas, Crafton is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Crafton costs $147,900. It is approximately $24,420 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $69,600 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $22,900 (18%) higher.

Rents

Crafton's median rent is $806 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $379 (31%) lower. It is approximately $256 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $152 (15%) lower.
