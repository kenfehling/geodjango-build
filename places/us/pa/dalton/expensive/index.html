The price of the median home in Dalton is $204,200. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $13,300 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $72,900 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median rent in Dalton is $799 per month. This is approximately $101 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $263 (24%) lower, and it is about $205 (20%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
