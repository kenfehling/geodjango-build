Home prices

The median home in Dalton is valued at $204,200. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $13,300 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is roughly $72,900 (55%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dalton is $799. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $101 (11%) lower. It is approximately $263 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $205 (20%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
