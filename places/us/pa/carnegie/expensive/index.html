Home prices

The median home value in Carnegie is $123,700. It is roughly $55,820 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $93,800 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $1,300 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carnegie is $758. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $437 (36%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $304 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $200 (20%) lower.
