Home prices

Rents

In Carnegie the median monthly rent is $758. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $437 (36%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $304 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $200 (20%) lower.
