Home prices

The median home cost in Boyertown is $155,900. This is roughly $68,140 (30%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $61,600 (28%) lower, and is roughly $7,100 (4%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Boyertown's median rent is $851 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $405 (32%) lower.It is roughly $211 (19%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $191 (18%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
