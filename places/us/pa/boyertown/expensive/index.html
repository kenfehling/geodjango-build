The median home cost in Boyertown is $155,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $68,140 (30%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $61,600 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $7,100 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Boyertown is $851 a month. It is about $405 (32%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $211 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $191 (18%) lower.
