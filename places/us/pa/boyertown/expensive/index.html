Home prices

The median home in Boyertown is valued at $155,900. It is roughly $68,140 (30%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $61,600 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $7,100 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Boyertown the median monthly rent is $851. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $405 (32%) lower.It is approximately $211 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $191 (18%) lower.
