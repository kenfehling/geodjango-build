Home prices

The median home in Muncy costs $154,500. It is about $12,000 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $63,000 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $81,300 (111%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.

Rents

Muncy's median monthly rent is $812. This is approximately $69 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $250 (23%) lower, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Reading.
