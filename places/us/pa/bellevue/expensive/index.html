Compared to other places in this area, Bellevue is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bellevue is $122,000. It is roughly $10,320 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $95,500 (43%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $3,000 (2%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Bellevue the median monthly rent is $779. This is roughly $45 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $283 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $179 (18%) lower.
