Home prices

The median home in Bellevue costs $122,000. This is approximately $10,320 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $95,500 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $3,000 (2%) lower.

Rents

Bellevue's median rent is $779 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $45 (5%) lower.It is roughly $283 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $179 (18%) lower.
