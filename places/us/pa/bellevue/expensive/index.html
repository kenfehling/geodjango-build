Bellevue's median home cost is $122,000. It is about $10,320 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $95,500 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $3,000 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bellevue is $779. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $45 (5%) lower. It is about $283 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $179 (18%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
