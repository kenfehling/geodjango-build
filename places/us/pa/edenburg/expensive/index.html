Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edenburg is $1,140. This is about $271 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $78 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $98 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
