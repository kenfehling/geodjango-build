Home prices

Sankertown's median home cost is $93,500. It is approximately $2,960 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $124,000 (57%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $31,500 (25%) lower.

Rents

Sankertown's median rent is $713 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $44 (5%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $349 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $245 (25%) lower.
