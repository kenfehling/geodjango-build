Compared to its surrounding areas, Girard is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Girard's median home value is $129,800. It is about $6,780 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $87,700 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $60,200 (86%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Girard the median rent is $587 a month. It is roughly $240 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $475 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $132 (18%) lower.
