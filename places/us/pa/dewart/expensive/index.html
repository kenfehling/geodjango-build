Home prices

The median home price in Dewart is $108,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $27,140 (20%) lower. It is about $109,000 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $35,300 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.

Rents

In Dewart the median rent is $637 a month. It is roughly $115 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $425 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $174 (21%) lower compared to the city of Reading.
