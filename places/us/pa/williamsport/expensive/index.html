In comparison to nearby areas, Williamsport is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Williamsport costs $110,700. This is roughly $38,260 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $106,800 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is about $29,900 (37%) higher.

Rents

Williamsport's median monthly rent is $763. It is roughly $60 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $299 (28%) lower, and is about $93 (10%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
