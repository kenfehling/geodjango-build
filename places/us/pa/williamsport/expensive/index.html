In comparison to its surrounding areas, Williamsport is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The median home in Williamsport costs $110,700. It is about $38,260 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $106,800 (49%) lower, and is roughly $29,900 (37%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Williamsport's median rent is $763 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $60 (7%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $299 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $93 (10%) lower.
