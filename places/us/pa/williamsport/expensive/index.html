In comparison to nearby areas, Williamsport is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Williamsport costs $110,700. This is roughly $38,260 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $106,800 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is about $29,900 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Williamsport the median monthly rent is $763. Compared to other places in this area this is about $60 (7%) lower. It is about $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $93 (10%) lower.
