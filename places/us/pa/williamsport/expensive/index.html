Compared to its nearby areas, Williamsport is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Williamsport costs $110,700. It is about $38,260 (25%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $106,800 (49%) lower, and is about $29,900 (37%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Williamsport's median monthly rent is $763. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $60 (7%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and is approximately $93 (10%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
