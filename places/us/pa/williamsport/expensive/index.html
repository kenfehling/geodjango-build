Compared to its nearby areas, Williamsport is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Williamsport is $110,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $38,260 (25%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $106,800 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $29,900 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Williamsport the median rent is $763 a month. This is about $60 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $299 (28%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $93 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
