Compared to the surrounding areas, DuBois is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in DuBois is $93,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $9,980 (11%) higher. It is roughly $124,100 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $31,600 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In DuBois the median rent is $673 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $73 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $389 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $285 (29%) lower.
