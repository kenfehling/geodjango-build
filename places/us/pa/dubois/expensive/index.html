Home prices

The median home price in DuBois is $93,400. It is approximately $9,980 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $124,100 (57%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $31,600 (25%) lower.

Rents

DuBois's median rent is $673 a month. This is approximately $73 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $389 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $285 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
