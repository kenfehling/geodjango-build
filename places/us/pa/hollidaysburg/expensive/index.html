The value of the median home in Hollidaysburg is $159,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $48,060 (43%) higher.It is approximately $58,300 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $3,500 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Hagerstown.

Rents

Hollidaysburg's median rent is $750 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $312 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Hagerstown it is about $82 (9%) lower.
