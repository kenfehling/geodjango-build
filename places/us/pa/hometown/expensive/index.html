Home prices

Hometown's median home value is $172,500. It is roughly $104,420 (153%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $45,000 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $9,500 (5%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hometown is $882. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $167 (23%) higher. It is about $180 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $160 (15%) lower.
