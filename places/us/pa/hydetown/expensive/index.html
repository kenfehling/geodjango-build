Rents

The median monthly rent in Hydetown is $677. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $54 (8%) higher. It is about $385 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $281 (29%) lower.
