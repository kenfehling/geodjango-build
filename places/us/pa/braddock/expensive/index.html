Home prices

The price of the median home in Braddock is $33,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $25,300 (43%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $184,200 (84%) lower, and it is about $91,700 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Braddock's median rent is $763 a month. This is roughly $179 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $299 (28%) lower, and is approximately $195 (20%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
