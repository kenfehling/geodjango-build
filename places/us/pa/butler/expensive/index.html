Home prices

Rents

Butler's median monthly rent is $665. It is roughly $197 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $397 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $293 (30%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
