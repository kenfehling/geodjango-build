In comparison to its surrounding areas, Butler is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

Butler's median home value is $100,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $57,340 (36%) lower. It is roughly $117,300 (53%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $24,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Butler is $665. This is roughly $197 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $397 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $293 (30%) lower.
