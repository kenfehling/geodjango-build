Home prices

Easton's median home price is $124,800. This is roughly $31,140 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $92,700 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $481,200 (79%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Easton is $986 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $105 (9%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $76 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $457 (31%) lower compared to the city of New York.
