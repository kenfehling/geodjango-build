Home prices

Easton's median home cost is $124,800. This is approximately $31,140 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $92,700 (42%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $481,200 (79%) lower.

Rents

In Easton the median monthly rent is $986. It is approximately $105 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $76 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $457 (31%) lower compared to the city of New York.
