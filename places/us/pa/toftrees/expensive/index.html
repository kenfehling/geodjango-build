Home prices

The median home in Toftrees is valued at $245,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $6,120 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $28,100 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $164,800 (203%) higher.

Rents

Toftrees's median monthly rent is $1,270. This is approximately $243 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $208 (19%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is about $414 (48%) higher.
