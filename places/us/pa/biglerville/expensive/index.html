In comparison to the surrounding areas, Biglerville is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Biglerville costs $169,200. It is about $15,480 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $48,300 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $432,300 (71%) lower.

Rents

Biglerville's median rent is $879 a month. This is approximately $50 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $662 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
