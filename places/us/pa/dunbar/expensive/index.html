Compared to the surrounding areas, Dunbar is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Dunbar is valued at $69,700. It is about $5,920 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $147,800 (67%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $55,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

Dunbar's median rent is $713 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $46 (6%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $349 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $245 (25%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
