Home prices

The cost of the median home in Dunbar is $69,700. It is roughly $5,920 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $147,800 (67%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $55,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dunbar is $713. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $46 (6%) higher. It is roughly $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $245 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
