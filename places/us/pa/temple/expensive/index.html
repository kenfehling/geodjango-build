Home prices

The price of the median home in Temple is $130,400. It is about $22,860 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $87,100 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $32,600 (19%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Temple the median rent is $959 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $165 (14%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $103 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $83 (7%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
