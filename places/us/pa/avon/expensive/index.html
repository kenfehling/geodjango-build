Home prices

The value of the median home in Avon is $160,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $38,740 (31%) higher.It is about $56,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $2,100 (1%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Avon the median monthly rent is $730. It is roughly $179 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $332 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $312 (29%) lower.
