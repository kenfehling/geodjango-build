Rents

The median monthly rent in Kenilworth is $907. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $364 (28%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $155 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $135 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
