Home prices

The median home in Lansdale is valued at $232,800. This is about $49,980 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $15,300 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $373,200 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lansdale is $1,156 per month. It is approximately $148 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $94 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $287 (19%) lower.
