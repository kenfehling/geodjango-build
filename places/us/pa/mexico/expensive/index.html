Compared to the surrounding areas, Mexico is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Mexico is $88,300. This is about $21,880 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $129,200 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $7,500 (9%) higher.

Rents

Mexico's median rent is $658 per month. This is roughly $18 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $404 (38%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is about $198 (23%) lower.
