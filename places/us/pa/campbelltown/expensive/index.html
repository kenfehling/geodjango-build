Home prices

The value of the median home in Campbelltown is $226,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $43,160 (23%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $9,300 (4%) higher, and is roughly $66,700 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

Campbelltown's median monthly rent is $848. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $180 (17%) lower. It is roughly $214 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $225 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.
