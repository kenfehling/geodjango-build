Compared to other places in this area, Campbelltown is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Baltimore however, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Campbelltown costs $226,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $43,160 (23%) higher.It is approximately $9,300 (4%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $66,700 (41%) higher compared to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Campbelltown is $848. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $180 (17%) lower.It is roughly $214 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $225 (20%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
