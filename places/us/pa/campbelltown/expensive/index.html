Compared to nearby areas, Campbelltown is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Baltimore.

Home prices

The median home value in Campbelltown is $226,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $43,160 (23%) higher. It is about $9,300 (4%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $66,700 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

In Campbelltown the median monthly rent is $848. It is roughly $180 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Baltimore it is roughly $225 (20%) lower.
