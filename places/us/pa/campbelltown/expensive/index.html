Home prices

The median home cost in Campbelltown is $226,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $43,160 (23%) higher. It is roughly $9,300 (4%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is about $66,700 (41%) higher.

Rents

Campbelltown's median rent is $848 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $180 (17%) lower. It is about $214 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $225 (20%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
