Home prices

The value of the median home in Williamstown is $72,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $16,200 (18%) lower. It is about $144,900 (66%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $87,500 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

Williamstown's median monthly rent is $658. This is roughly $43 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $404 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is approximately $415 (38%) lower.
