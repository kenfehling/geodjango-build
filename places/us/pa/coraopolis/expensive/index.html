Home prices

The median home in Coraopolis is valued at $94,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $157,500 (62%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $122,800 (56%) lower, and is about $30,300 (24%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Coraopolis the median rent is $700 a month. It is about $429 (38%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $258 (26%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
