Compared to other places in this area, Coraopolis is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Coraopolis's median home price is $94,700. It is approximately $157,500 (62%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $122,800 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $30,300 (24%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Coraopolis's median monthly rent is $700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $429 (38%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $362 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $258 (26%) lower.
