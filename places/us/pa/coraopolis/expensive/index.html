Home prices

The cost of the median home in Coraopolis is $94,700. It is roughly $157,500 (62%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $122,800 (56%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $30,300 (24%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Coraopolis is $700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $429 (38%) lower.It is about $362 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $258 (26%) lower.
