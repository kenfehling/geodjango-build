Rents

Coraopolis's median rent is $700 a month. This is roughly $429 (38%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and is roughly $258 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
