Home prices

The median home in Blandon is valued at $194,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $49,940 (34%) higher. It is approximately $23,500 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is about $31,000 (19%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Blandon is $1,215 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $227 (23%) higher. It is about $153 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $173 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
