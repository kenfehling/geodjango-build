Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Blandon is $1,215 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $227 (23%) higher.It is approximately $153 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $173 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
