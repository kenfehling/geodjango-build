Home prices

The price of the median home in Croydon is $196,400. It is approximately $11,000 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $21,100 (9%) lower, and is roughly $409,600 (67%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Croydon the median rent is $1,064 a month. This is approximately $68 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is is very similar compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $379 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
