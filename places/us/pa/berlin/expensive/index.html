Home prices

The median home in Berlin costs $108,600. This is approximately $14,980 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $108,900 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $16,400 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Berlin is $517 per month. It is roughly $136 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $545 (51%) lower, and is roughly $441 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
