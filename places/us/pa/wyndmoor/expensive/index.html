The median home in Wyndmoor is valued at $318,700. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $101,200 (46%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $287,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Wyndmoor the median rent is $1,174 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $157 (11%) lower. It is approximately $112 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York it is about $269 (18%) lower.
