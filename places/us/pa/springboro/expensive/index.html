Home prices

The median home in Springboro costs $81,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $12,360 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $136,400 (62%) lower, and it is roughly $11,500 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Springboro the median monthly rent is $736. It is about $116 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $326 (30%) lower, and is approximately $17 (2%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.
