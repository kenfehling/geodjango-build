Compared to other places in this area, Hookstown is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hookstown is $86,700. It is approximately $12,220 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $130,800 (60%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $38,300 (30%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Hookstown the median rent is $925 a month. It is roughly $247 (36%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $137 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $33 (3%) lower.
