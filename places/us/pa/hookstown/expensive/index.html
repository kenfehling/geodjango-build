Home prices

The median home cost in Hookstown is $86,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $12,220 (16%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $130,800 (60%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $38,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hookstown is $925 a month. This is about $247 (36%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $137 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $33 (3%) lower.
