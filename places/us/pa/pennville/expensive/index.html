Home prices

The median home in Pennville costs $158,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $4,500 (2%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $59,000 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington it is roughly $443,000 (73%) lower.

Rents

In Pennville the median monthly rent is $1,017. This is about $91 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $45 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $524 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
