Compared to the surrounding areas, Richland is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home value in Richland is $161,100. It is roughly $11,360 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $56,400 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $1,900 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Richland's median monthly rent is $938. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $81 (9%) higher. It is roughly $124 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $104 (9%) lower.
