Home prices

The median home in Richland costs $161,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $11,360 (7%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $56,400 (25%) lower, and is about $1,900 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Richland the median rent is $938 a month. This is about $81 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $124 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $104 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
