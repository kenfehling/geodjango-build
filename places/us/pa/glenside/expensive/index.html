Home prices

The median home cost in Glenside is $283,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $44,040 (13%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $65,500 (30%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $323,000 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Glenside is $1,126 a month. This is approximately $215 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $64 (6%) higher, and is approximately $317 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
