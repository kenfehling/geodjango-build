Home prices

The value of the median home in Glenside is $283,000. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $44,040 (13%) lower. It is roughly $65,500 (30%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $323,000 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Glenside is $1,126 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $215 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $64 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is about $317 (21%) lower.
