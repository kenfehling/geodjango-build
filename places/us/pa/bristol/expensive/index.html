Home prices

The median home price in Bristol is $174,700. This is about $26,440 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $42,800 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $431,300 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Bristol's median rent is $1,028 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $45 (4%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $34 (3%) lower, and it is about $415 (28%) lower compared to the city of New York.
