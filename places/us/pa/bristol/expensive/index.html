Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bristol is $174,700. It is about $26,440 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $42,800 (19%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $431,300 (71%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Bristol is $1,028 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $45 (4%) lower.It is roughly $34 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $415 (28%) lower.
