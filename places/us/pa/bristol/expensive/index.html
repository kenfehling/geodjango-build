In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bristol is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Bristol is valued at $174,700. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $26,440 (13%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $42,800 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York is roughly $431,300 (71%) lower.

Rents

In Bristol the median rent is $1,028 per month. It is about $45 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $34 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $415 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
