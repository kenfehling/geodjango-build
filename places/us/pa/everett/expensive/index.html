Home prices

The median home in Everett is valued at $92,400. This is about $8,760 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $125,100 (57%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Frederick is approximately $178,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

Everett's median rent is $578 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $149 (20%) lower. It is approximately $484 (45%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $818 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Frederick.
