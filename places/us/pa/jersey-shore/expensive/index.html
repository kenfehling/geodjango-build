Home prices

The median home cost in Jersey Shore is $109,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $24,380 (18%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $108,400 (49%) lower, and it is roughly $28,300 (35%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Jersey Shore the median monthly rent is $721. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $81 (10%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $135 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
