In comparison to other places in this area, Jersey Shore is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Jersey Shore is $109,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $24,380 (18%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $108,400 (49%) lower, and is about $28,300 (35%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Jersey Shore's median rent is $721 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $81 (10%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is about $135 (15%) lower.
