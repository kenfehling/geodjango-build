In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jersey Shore is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Jersey Shore is $109,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $24,380 (18%) lower.It is about $108,400 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $28,300 (35%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Jersey Shore is $721 per month. It is approximately $81 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $341 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is about $135 (15%) lower.
