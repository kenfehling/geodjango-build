Home prices

The median home in Williamsburg is valued at $91,200. It is roughly $45,960 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $126,300 (58%) lower, and it is roughly $10,400 (12%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Williamsburg is $570 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $290 (33%) lower. It is roughly $492 (46%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $286 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
