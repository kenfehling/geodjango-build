The median home value in Williamsburg is $91,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $45,960 (33%) lower.It is roughly $126,300 (58%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $10,400 (12%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Williamsburg the median monthly rent is $570. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $290 (33%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $492 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $286 (33%) lower.
