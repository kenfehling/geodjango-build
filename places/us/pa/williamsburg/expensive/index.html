Home prices

The median home price in Williamsburg is $91,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $45,960 (33%) lower. It is approximately $126,300 (58%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $10,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

Williamsburg's median rent is $570 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $290 (33%) lower. It is roughly $492 (46%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $286 (33%) lower.
