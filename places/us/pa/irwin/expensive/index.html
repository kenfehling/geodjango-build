Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Irwin is $704 per month. It is about $92 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $358 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $254 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
