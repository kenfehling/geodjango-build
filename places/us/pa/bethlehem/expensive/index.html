Compared to its surrounding areas, Bethlehem is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

Bethlehem's median home price is $173,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $10,840 (6%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $44,000 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $432,500 (71%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bethlehem is $1,038 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $24 (2%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $405 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
