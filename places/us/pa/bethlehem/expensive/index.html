Compared to the surrounding areas, Bethlehem is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Bethlehem is $173,500. It is roughly $10,840 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $44,000 (20%) lower, and it is approximately $432,500 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Bethlehem is $1,038 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is is very similar. It is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $405 (28%) lower compared to the city of New York.
