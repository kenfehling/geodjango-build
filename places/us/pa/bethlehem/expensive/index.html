Compared to other places in this area, Bethlehem is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Bethlehem is valued at $173,500. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $10,840 (6%) higher.It is about $44,000 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $432,500 (71%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Bethlehem the median monthly rent is $1,038. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $24 (2%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is approximately $405 (28%) lower.
