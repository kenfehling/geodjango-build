Home prices

Rents

Evans City's median rent is $815 per month. This is roughly $144 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $247 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $143 (14%) lower.
