Rents

The median rent in Evans City is $815 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $144 (15%) lower. It is roughly $247 (23%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $143 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
