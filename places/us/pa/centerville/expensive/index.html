In comparison to its surrounding areas, Centerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Centerville is $75,000. It is roughly $10,360 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $142,500 (65%) lower, and is about $50,000 (40%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Centerville the median rent is $763 a month. It is about $129 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $299 (28%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $195 (20%) lower.
