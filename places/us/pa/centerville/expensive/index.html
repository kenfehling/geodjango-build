Home prices

The median home in Centerville costs $75,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $10,360 (12%) lower. It is roughly $142,500 (65%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $50,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $763. This is approximately $129 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $299 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $195 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
