Compared to its surrounding areas, Canonsburg is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Canonsburg costs $133,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $102,180 (43%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $83,600 (38%) lower, and is approximately $8,900 (7%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Canonsburg the median rent is $793 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $272 (25%) lower.It is roughly $269 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $165 (17%) lower.
