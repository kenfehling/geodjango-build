Rents

New Kensington's median monthly rent is $606. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $98 (13%) lower.It is roughly $456 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $352 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
