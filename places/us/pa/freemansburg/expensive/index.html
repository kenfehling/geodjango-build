Home prices

The median home in Freemansburg costs $161,200. It is approximately $18,280 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $56,300 (25%) lower, and it is about $444,800 (73%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

Freemansburg's median rent is $1,333 per month. It is roughly $154 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $271 (25%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of New York is about $110 (7%) lower.
