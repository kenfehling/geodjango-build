The median home in Hughesville costs $140,100. This is about $5,280 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $77,400 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $66,900 (91%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

In Hughesville the median monthly rent is $650. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $125 (16%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $412 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Reading is roughly $161 (19%) lower.
