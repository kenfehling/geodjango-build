Home prices

Rents

In Bally the median rent is $1,081 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $86 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $19 (1%) higher, and is roughly $39 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
