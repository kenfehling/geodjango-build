Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bally is $172,100. It is roughly $11,020 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $45,400 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $9,100 (5%) higher.

Rents

Bally's median monthly rent is $1,081. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $86 (8%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $19 (1%) higher, and is about $39 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
