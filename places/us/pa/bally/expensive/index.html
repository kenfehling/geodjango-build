Compared to other places in this area, Bally is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Bally is valued at $172,100. This is approximately $11,020 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $45,400 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $9,100 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bally is $1,081. This is roughly $86 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $19 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $39 (3%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
