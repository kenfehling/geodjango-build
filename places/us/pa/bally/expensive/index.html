Home prices

The median home in Bally costs $172,100. It is approximately $11,020 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $45,400 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $9,100 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Bally's median rent is $1,081 per month. This is roughly $86 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $19 (1%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $39 (3%) higher.
