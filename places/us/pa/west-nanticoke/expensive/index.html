Home prices

The median home in West Nanticoke is valued at $102,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $14,060 (15%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $115,100 (52%) lower, and is roughly $28,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

The median rent in West Nanticoke is $658 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $71 (9%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $404 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is approximately $346 (34%) lower.
