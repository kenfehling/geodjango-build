Home prices

The median home in West Nanticoke is valued at $102,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $14,060 (15%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $115,100 (52%) lower, and it is about $28,900 (22%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

In West Nanticoke the median monthly rent is $658. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $71 (9%) lower.It is about $404 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is about $346 (34%) lower.
