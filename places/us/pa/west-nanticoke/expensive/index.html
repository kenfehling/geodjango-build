Home prices

The median home in West Nanticoke is valued at $102,400. This is about $14,060 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $115,100 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $28,900 (22%) lower.

Rents

West Nanticoke's median rent is $658 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $71 (9%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $404 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $346 (34%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
