Compared to other places in this area, North East is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Buffalo.

Home prices

The median home value in North East is $127,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $30,860 (31%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $89,900 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $37,800 (42%) higher compared to the city of Buffalo.

Rents

The median rent in North East is $667 a month. It is about $38 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $395 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Buffalo is about $109 (14%) lower.
