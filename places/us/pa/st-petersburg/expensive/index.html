Home prices

The median home cost in St. Petersburg is $81,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $29,820 (26%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $136,200 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $43,700 (34%) lower.

Rents

In St. Petersburg the median rent is $725 per month. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $233 (24%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
