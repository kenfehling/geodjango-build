Compared to its surrounding areas, Belleville is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Belleville is $117,200. This is roughly $24,360 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $100,300 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $36,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Belleville is $549. It is roughly $159 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $513 (48%) lower, and it is about $307 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
