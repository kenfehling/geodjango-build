Home prices

The median home in Belleville costs $117,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $24,360 (26%) higher. It is roughly $100,300 (46%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $36,400 (45%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

In Belleville the median monthly rent is $549. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $159 (22%) lower. It is about $513 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $307 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
