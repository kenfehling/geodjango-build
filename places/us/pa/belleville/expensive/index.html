Home prices

Rents

In Belleville the median rent is $549 per month. It is about $159 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $513 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $307 (35%) lower.
