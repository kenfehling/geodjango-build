Home prices

Tarentum's median home cost is $59,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $41,620 (41%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $158,200 (72%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $65,700 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tarentum is $585. It is about $144 (19%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $477 (44%) lower, and is about $373 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
