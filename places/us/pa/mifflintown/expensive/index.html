Home prices

The median home in Mifflintown costs $84,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $26,200 (23%) lower. It is roughly $132,800 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $3,900 (4%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

Mifflintown's median rent is $609 per month. It is about $76 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $453 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $247 (28%) lower.
