Home prices

The median home in Mifflintown is valued at $84,700. This is roughly $26,200 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $132,800 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $3,900 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Mifflintown the median rent is $609 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $76 (11%) lower. It is approximately $453 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $247 (28%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
