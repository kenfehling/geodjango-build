Home prices

The median home in Swoyersville is valued at $125,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $14,740 (13%) higher. It is approximately $92,000 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $5,800 (4%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.

Rents

In Swoyersville the median rent is $692 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $83 (10%) lower. It is about $370 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Allentown it is about $312 (31%) lower.
