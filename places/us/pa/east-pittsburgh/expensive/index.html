In comparison to other places in this area, East Pittsburgh is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Pittsburgh costs $43,100. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $9,460 (17%) lower. It is about $174,400 (80%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $81,900 (65%) lower.

Rents

East Pittsburgh's median monthly rent is $618. It is about $21 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $444 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $340 (35%) lower.
