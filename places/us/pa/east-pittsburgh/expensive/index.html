Compared to its surrounding areas, East Pittsburgh is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in East Pittsburgh is $43,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $9,460 (17%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $174,400 (80%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $81,900 (65%) lower.

Rents

East Pittsburgh's median rent is $618 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $21 (3%) lower.It is approximately $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $340 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
