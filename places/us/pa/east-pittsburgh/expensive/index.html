Compared to the surrounding areas, East Pittsburgh is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in East Pittsburgh is $43,100. This is about $9,460 (17%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $174,400 (80%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $81,900 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

East Pittsburgh's median monthly rent is $618. Compared to other places in this area it is about $21 (3%) lower.It is about $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $340 (35%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
