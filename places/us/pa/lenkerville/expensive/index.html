Home prices

The median home in Lenkerville is valued at $112,500. It is roughly $5,740 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $105,000 (48%) lower, and it is roughly $47,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

In Lenkerville the median monthly rent is $792. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $143 (22%) higher. It is about $270 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Baltimore it is about $281 (26%) lower.
