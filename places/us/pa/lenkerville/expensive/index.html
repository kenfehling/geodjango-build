Much like the surrounding areas, it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Baltimore, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lenkerville is $112,500. This is roughly $5,740 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $105,000 (48%) lower, and is about $47,600 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

In Lenkerville the median rent is $792 a month. This is approximately $143 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and is about $281 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.
