Home prices

Rents

Lenkerville's median monthly rent is $792. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $143 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and is approximately $281 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.
