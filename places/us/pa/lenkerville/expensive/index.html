Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lenkerville is $112,500. This is approximately $5,740 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $105,000 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is roughly $47,600 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Lenkerville the median monthly rent is $792. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $143 (22%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $270 (25%) lower, and is roughly $281 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.
