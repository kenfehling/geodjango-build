Home prices

Conway's median home price is $134,700. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $43,120 (47%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $82,800 (38%) lower, and is roughly $9,700 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Conway the median monthly rent is $547. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $60 (9%) lower. It is approximately $515 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $411 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
