Home prices

Conway's median home price is $134,700. It is about $43,120 (47%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $82,800 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $9,700 (7%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Conway's median rent is $547 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $60 (9%) lower.It is approximately $515 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $411 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
