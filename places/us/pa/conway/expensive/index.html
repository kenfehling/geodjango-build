Home prices

The price of the median home in Conway is $134,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $43,120 (47%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $82,800 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $9,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Conway is $547. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $60 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $515 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $411 (42%) lower.
