Home prices

The median home value in Conway is $134,700. It is approximately $43,120 (47%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $82,800 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $9,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

Conway's median monthly rent is $547. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $60 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $515 (48%) lower, and is approximately $411 (42%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
