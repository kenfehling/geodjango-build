Home prices

The median home cost in Conway is $134,700. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $43,120 (47%) higher.It is roughly $82,800 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $9,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

Conway's median monthly rent is $547. This is about $60 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $515 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $411 (42%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
