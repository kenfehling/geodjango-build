In comparison to its surrounding areas, Montoursville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Scranton, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Montoursville is $166,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $23,260 (16%) higher. It is roughly $50,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Scranton it is approximately $63,600 (61%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Montoursville is $908 per month. It is roughly $136 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $154 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Scranton it is roughly $126 (16%) higher.
