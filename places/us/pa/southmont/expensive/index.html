In comparison to the surrounding areas, Southmont is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Southmont is valued at $112,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $33,320 (41%) higher. It is roughly $104,800 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $12,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Southmont is $744 per month. It is about $110 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $318 (29%) lower, and is approximately $214 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
