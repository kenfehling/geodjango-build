Home prices

The median home price in Southmont is $112,700. It is about $33,320 (41%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $104,800 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $12,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

Southmont's median monthly rent is $744. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $110 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $318 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $214 (22%) lower.
