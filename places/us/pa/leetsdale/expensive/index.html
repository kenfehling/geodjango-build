In comparison to the surrounding areas, Leetsdale is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Leetsdale's median home cost is $85,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $334,700 (79%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $132,000 (60%) lower, and is roughly $39,500 (31%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Leetsdale's median monthly rent is $832. It is approximately $108 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $230 (21%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $126 (13%) lower.
