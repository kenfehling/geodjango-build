The price of the median home in Leetsdale is $85,500. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $334,700 (79%) lower. It is about $132,000 (60%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $39,500 (31%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Leetsdale is $832. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $108 (11%) lower. It is approximately $230 (21%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $126 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
