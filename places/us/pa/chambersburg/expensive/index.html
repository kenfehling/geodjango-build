Home prices

Rents

In Chambersburg the median rent is $820 a month. This is about $178 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $721 (46%) lower.
