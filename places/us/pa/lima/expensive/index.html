Home prices

The median home in Lima is valued at $283,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $15,460 (5%) higher. It is roughly $65,800 (30%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $120,300 (73%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Lima's median rent is $2,428 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $1,137 (88%) higher. It is roughly $1,366 (128%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $1,386 (133%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
