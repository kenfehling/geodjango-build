Compared to other places in this area, Thompsontown is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Reading, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Thompsontown costs $128,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $10,940 (9%) higher. It is approximately $89,400 (41%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $54,900 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.

Rents

The median rent in Thompsontown is $596 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $119 (16%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $466 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Reading is roughly $215 (26%) lower.
