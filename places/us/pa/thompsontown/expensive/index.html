In comparison to other places in this area, Thompsontown is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Reading however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Thompsontown is $128,100. This is about $10,940 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $89,400 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $54,900 (75%) higher compared to the city of Reading.

Rents

In Thompsontown the median rent is $596 per month. This is approximately $119 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $466 (43%) lower, and it is roughly $215 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Reading.
