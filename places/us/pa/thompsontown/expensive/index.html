Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Thompsontown is $596. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $119 (16%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $466 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Reading is approximately $215 (26%) lower.
