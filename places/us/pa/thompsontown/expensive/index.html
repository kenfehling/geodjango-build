Rents

The median monthly rent in Thompsontown is $596. It is roughly $119 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $466 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Reading it is approximately $215 (26%) lower.
