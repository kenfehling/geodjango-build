Compared to its surrounding areas, Arlington Heights is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home cost in Arlington Heights is $186,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $18,660 (11%) higher. It is roughly $31,000 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $419,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In Arlington Heights the median rent is $917 a month. This is roughly $199 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $145 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $526 (36%) lower.
