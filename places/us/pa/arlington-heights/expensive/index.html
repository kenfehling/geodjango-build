Compared to its nearby areas, Arlington Heights is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Arlington Heights is valued at $186,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $18,660 (11%) higher.It is about $31,000 (14%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $419,500 (69%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Arlington Heights the median rent is $917 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $199 (17%) lower.It is about $145 (13%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $526 (36%) lower.
