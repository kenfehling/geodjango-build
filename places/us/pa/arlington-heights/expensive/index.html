Compared to its surrounding areas, Arlington Heights is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home value in Arlington Heights is $186,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $18,660 (11%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $31,000 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of New York it is approximately $419,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Arlington Heights the median rent is $917 a month. This is about $199 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $145 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is about $526 (36%) lower.
