Home prices

The median home in Arlington Heights costs $186,500. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $18,660 (11%) higher. It is roughly $31,000 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $419,500 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

The median rent in Arlington Heights is $917 a month. It is approximately $199 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $145 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $526 (36%) lower compared to the city of New York.
