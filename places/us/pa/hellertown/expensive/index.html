Home prices

The price of the median home in Hellertown is $185,400. This is approximately $2,640 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $32,100 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of New York it is roughly $420,600 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Hellertown the median monthly rent is $995. This is about $160 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $67 (6%) lower, and it is about $448 (31%) lower compared to the city of New York.
