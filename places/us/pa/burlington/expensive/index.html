Home prices

The price of the median home in Burlington is $115,000. This is roughly $14,740 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $102,500 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $20,600 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burlington is $682. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $90 (11%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $380 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $132 (16%) lower.
