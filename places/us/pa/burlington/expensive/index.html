Compared to its surrounding areas, Burlington is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Burlington costs $115,000. It is roughly $14,740 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $102,500 (47%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $20,600 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burlington is $682. This is roughly $90 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $380 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Syracuse is about $132 (16%) lower.
