Home prices

The cost of the median home in Herndon is $85,800. This is roughly $32,320 (27%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $131,700 (60%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reading is roughly $12,600 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Herndon is $669. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $74 (9%) lower. It is roughly $393 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Reading is about $142 (17%) lower.
