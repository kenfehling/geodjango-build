Home prices

The cost of the median home in White Oak is $107,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $38,300 (55%) higher. It is roughly $110,500 (50%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $18,000 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in White Oak is $808 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $115 (16%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $254 (23%) lower, and is approximately $150 (15%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
