In comparison to other places in this area, Mayfield is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home price in Mayfield is $134,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $6,960 (5%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $83,500 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is approximately $2,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mayfield is $830 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $57 (7%) higher.It is roughly $232 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Allentown it is roughly $174 (17%) lower.
