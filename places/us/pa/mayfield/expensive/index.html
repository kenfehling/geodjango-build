Compared to nearby areas, Mayfield is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Allentown however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Mayfield costs $134,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $6,960 (5%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $83,500 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown it is approximately $2,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mayfield is $830 a month. This is approximately $57 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $232 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $174 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
