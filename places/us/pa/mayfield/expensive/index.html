In comparison to other places in this area, Mayfield is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home in Mayfield is valued at $134,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $6,960 (5%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $83,500 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is approximately $2,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mayfield is $830 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $57 (7%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $232 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is approximately $174 (17%) lower.
