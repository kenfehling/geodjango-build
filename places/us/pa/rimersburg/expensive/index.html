Home prices

The median home value in Rimersburg is $91,000. This is approximately $7,380 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $126,500 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $34,000 (27%) lower.

Rents

Rimersburg's median monthly rent is $553. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $509 (47%) lower, and is about $405 (42%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
