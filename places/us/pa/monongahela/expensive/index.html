In comparison to its nearby areas, Monongahela is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Monongahela is $81,800. This is roughly $24,820 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $135,700 (62%) lower, and it is about $43,200 (34%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Monongahela is $676 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $27 (4%) higher. It is about $386 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $282 (29%) lower.
