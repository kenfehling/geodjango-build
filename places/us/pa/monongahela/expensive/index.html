Compared to its surrounding areas, Monongahela is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Monongahela is $81,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $24,820 (23%) lower. It is about $135,700 (62%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $43,200 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Monongahela's median monthly rent is $676. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $27 (4%) higher. It is approximately $386 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $282 (29%) lower.
