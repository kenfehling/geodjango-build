Compared to nearby areas, Monongahela is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monongahela costs $81,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24,820 (23%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $135,700 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $43,200 (34%) lower.

Rents

Monongahela's median rent is $676 a month. This is about $27 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $386 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $282 (29%) lower.
