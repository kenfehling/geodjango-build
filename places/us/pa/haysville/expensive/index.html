Home prices

The median home in Haysville is valued at $104,400. It is approximately $303,800 (74%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $113,100 (52%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $20,600 (16%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Haysville the median rent is $1,607 per month. It is approximately $596 (58%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $545 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $649 (67%) higher.
