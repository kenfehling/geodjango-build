Home prices

The median home cost in Haysville is $104,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $303,800 (74%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $113,100 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $20,600 (16%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Haysville is $1,607. This is about $596 (58%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $545 (51%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $649 (67%) higher.
