Rents

Haysville's median monthly rent is $1,607. It is approximately $596 (58%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $545 (51%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $649 (67%) higher.
