The median home cost in Albion is $95,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $16,000 (14%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $121,600 (55%) lower, and is roughly $26,300 (37%) higher compared to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Albion is $540. It is about $229 (29%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $522 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $179 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
