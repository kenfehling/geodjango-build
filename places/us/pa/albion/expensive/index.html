Home prices

The median home in Albion is valued at $95,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,000 (14%) lower. It is about $121,600 (55%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $26,300 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Cleveland.

Rents

In Albion the median monthly rent is $540. It is about $229 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $522 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is roughly $179 (24%) lower.
