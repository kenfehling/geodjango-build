Rents

In Dillsburg the median monthly rent is $780. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $66 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $761 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
