Home prices

The median home in Courtdale costs $101,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $2,580 (2%) lower. It is roughly $115,700 (53%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Allentown it is approximately $29,500 (22%) lower.

Rents

Courtdale's median monthly rent is $814. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $50 (6%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $248 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $190 (18%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
