The median home in Littlestown costs $183,100. This is roughly $3,200 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $34,400 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $418,400 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Littlestown is $930. This is approximately $165 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $132 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $611 (39%) lower.
