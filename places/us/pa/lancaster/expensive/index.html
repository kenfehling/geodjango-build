Compared to the surrounding areas, Lancaster is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $114,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $133,120 (53%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $102,900 (47%) lower, and is roughly $48,400 (29%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $866. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $451 (34%) lower. It is approximately $196 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $176 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
