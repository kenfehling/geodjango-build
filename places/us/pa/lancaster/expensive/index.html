Home prices

The median home in Lancaster is valued at $114,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $133,120 (53%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $102,900 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $48,400 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lancaster is $866 a month. It is roughly $451 (34%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $196 (18%) lower, and it is about $176 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
