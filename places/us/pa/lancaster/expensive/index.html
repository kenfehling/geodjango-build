In comparison to its nearby areas, Lancaster is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lancaster is $114,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $133,120 (53%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $102,900 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $48,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lancaster is $866. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $451 (34%) lower.It is approximately $196 (18%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $176 (16%) lower.
