In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lancaster is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster is valued at $114,600. This is roughly $133,120 (53%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $102,900 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $48,400 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lancaster is $866. This is about $451 (34%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $196 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $176 (16%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
