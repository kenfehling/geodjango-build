Home prices

The median home in Lancaster is valued at $114,600. This is approximately $133,120 (53%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $102,900 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $48,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $866. It is approximately $451 (34%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $196 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $176 (16%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
