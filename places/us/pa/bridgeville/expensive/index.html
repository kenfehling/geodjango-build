Home prices

The median home in Bridgeville is valued at $139,600. It is roughly $26,760 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $77,900 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $14,600 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

Bridgeville's median monthly rent is $775. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $272 (26%) lower.It is roughly $287 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $183 (19%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
