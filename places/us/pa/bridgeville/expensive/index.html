Compared to its surrounding areas, Bridgeville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bridgeville costs $139,600. It is approximately $26,760 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $77,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $14,600 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Bridgeville the median rent is $775 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $272 (26%) lower. It is about $287 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $183 (19%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
