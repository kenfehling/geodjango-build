In comparison to other places in this area, Fredonia is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cleveland, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Fredonia's median home price is $79,700. This is roughly $63,960 (44%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $137,800 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of Cleveland is roughly $10,100 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fredonia is $500. This is approximately $335 (40%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $562 (52%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland it is approximately $219 (30%) lower.
