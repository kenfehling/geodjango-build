The price of the median home in Bellwood is $121,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $11,900 (10%) higher. It is about $96,200 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $34,400 (22%) lower compared to the city of Hagerstown.

Rents

Bellwood's median rent is $760 per month. This is roughly $85 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $302 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Hagerstown is roughly $72 (8%) lower.
