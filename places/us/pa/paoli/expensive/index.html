Home prices

The median home in Paoli costs $344,600. This is approximately $26,240 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $127,100 (58%) higher, and it is roughly $181,600 (111%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median rent in Paoli is $1,516 a month. This is roughly $78 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $454 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $474 (45%) higher.
