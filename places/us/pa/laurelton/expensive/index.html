Compared to other places in this area, Laurelton is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Laurelton is $158,300. It is about $24,760 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $59,200 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $77,500 (95%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laurelton is $843. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $105 (14%) higher. It is approximately $219 (20%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
