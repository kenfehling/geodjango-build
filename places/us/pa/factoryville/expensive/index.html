Home prices

The cost of the median home in Factoryville is $155,400. It is approximately $47,320 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $62,100 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $24,100 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Factoryville the median monthly rent is $588. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $320 (35%) lower. It is about $474 (44%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $416 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
