In comparison to other places in this area, Yeagertown is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Yeagertown is $91,600. This is approximately $20,340 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $125,900 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $10,800 (13%) higher.

Rents

Yeagertown's median rent is $771 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $87 (12%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $291 (27%) lower, and is about $85 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.
