Home prices

Rents

Yeagertown's median monthly rent is $771. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $87 (12%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $291 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $85 (9%) lower.
