Compared to the surrounding areas, Freeburg is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Reading, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Freeburg is $131,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $7,180 (5%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $86,200 (39%) lower, and it is about $58,100 (79%) higher in comparison to the city of Reading.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Freeburg is $715. This is roughly $12 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $347 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Reading is approximately $96 (11%) lower.
