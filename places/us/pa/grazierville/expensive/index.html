In comparison to other places in this area, Grazierville is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Hagerstown, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Grazierville's median home value is $94,400. This is about $20,380 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $123,100 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $61,300 (39%) lower compared to the city of Hagerstown.

Rents

The median rent in Grazierville is $540 a month. It is approximately $179 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $522 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Hagerstown it is approximately $292 (35%) lower.
