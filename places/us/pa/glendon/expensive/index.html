Home prices

The median home price in Glendon is $151,600. This is roughly $20,920 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $65,900 (30%) lower, and is approximately $454,400 (74%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Glendon is $1,287. This is roughly $96 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $225 (21%) higher, and it is roughly $156 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
