Home prices

The value of the median home in Akron is $195,300. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $26,720 (12%) lower.It is roughly $22,200 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $32,300 (19%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Akron is $977. This is roughly $33 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $85 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $65 (6%) lower.
