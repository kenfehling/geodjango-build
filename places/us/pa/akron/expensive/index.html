Compared to the surrounding areas, Akron is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Akron is $195,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $26,720 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $22,200 (10%) lower, and is about $32,300 (19%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Akron is $977. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $33 (3%) higher. It is roughly $85 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $65 (6%) lower.
