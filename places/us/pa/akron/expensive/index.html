Home prices

The price of the median home in Akron is $195,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $26,720 (12%) lower.It is about $22,200 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $32,300 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Akron the median rent is $977 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $33 (3%) higher.It is about $85 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $65 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
