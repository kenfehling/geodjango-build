In comparison to other places in this area, Millheim is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

Millheim's median home value is $154,900. This is approximately $12,580 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $62,600 (28%) lower, and it is about $74,100 (91%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Millheim is $750 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $35 (4%) lower. It is roughly $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $106 (12%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
