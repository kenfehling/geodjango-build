Home prices

The cost of the median home in Millheim is $154,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $12,580 (8%) higher.It is roughly $62,600 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $74,100 (91%) higher compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Millheim is $750. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $35 (4%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $312 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Harrisburg is approximately $106 (12%) lower.
