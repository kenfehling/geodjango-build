Home prices

The value of the median home in Millheim is $154,900. It is about $12,580 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $62,600 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is approximately $74,100 (91%) higher.

Rents

Millheim's median monthly rent is $750. It is roughly $35 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $312 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $106 (12%) lower.
