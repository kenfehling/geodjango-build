Home prices

The median home in Fairview costs $107,800. This is about $18,060 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $109,700 (50%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is approximately $17,200 (13%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fairview is $975 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $276 (39%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $87 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $17 (1%) higher.
