Home prices

Rents

In Clay the median rent is $1,075 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $106 (10%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $13 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $33 (3%) higher.
