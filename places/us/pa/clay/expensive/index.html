Compared to other places in this area, Clay is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Clay costs $225,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $30,220 (15%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $7,800 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is about $62,300 (38%) higher.

Rents

In Clay the median rent is $1,075 per month. This is roughly $106 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $13 (1%) higher, and it is approximately $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
