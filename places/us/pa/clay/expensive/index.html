In comparison to the surrounding areas, Clay is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Philadelphia, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Clay is $225,300. It is about $30,220 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $7,800 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $62,300 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Clay the median rent is $1,075 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $106 (10%) higher. It is roughly $13 (1%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $33 (3%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.
