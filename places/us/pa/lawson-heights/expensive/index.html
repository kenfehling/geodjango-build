Rents

In Lawson Heights the median rent is $855 per month. It is approximately $152 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $207 (19%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $103 (10%) lower.
