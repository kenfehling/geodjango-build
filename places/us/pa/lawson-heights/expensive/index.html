Home prices

The median home in Lawson Heights costs $161,800. It is approximately $55,820 (52%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $55,700 (25%) lower, and it is about $36,800 (29%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In Lawson Heights the median rent is $855 per month. It is about $152 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $207 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $103 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
