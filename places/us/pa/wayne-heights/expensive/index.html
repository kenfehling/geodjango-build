Home prices

The median home value in Wayne Heights is $206,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30,440 (17%) higher.It is roughly $10,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $394,900 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.

Rents

The median rent in Wayne Heights is $936 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $93 (11%) higher.It is roughly $126 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Washington is approximately $605 (39%) lower.
