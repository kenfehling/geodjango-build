In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wayne Heights is a bit more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Washington however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wayne Heights is $206,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $30,440 (17%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $10,900 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is about $394,900 (65%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wayne Heights is $936. This is roughly $93 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $126 (11%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $605 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Washington.
