The median home in Wayne Heights is valued at $206,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $30,440 (17%) higher. It is roughly $10,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $394,900 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Wayne Heights the median monthly rent is $936. This is roughly $93 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $126 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Washington is roughly $605 (39%) lower.
