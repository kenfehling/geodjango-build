Compared to other places in this area, Somerset is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Somerset is $112,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $16,040 (16%) higher. It is roughly $104,800 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $12,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Somerset is $535. It is roughly $48 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $527 (49%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $423 (44%) lower.
