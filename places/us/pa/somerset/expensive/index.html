Home prices

The median home cost in Somerset is $112,700. It is about $16,040 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $104,800 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $12,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

The median rent in Somerset is $535 per month. It is approximately $48 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $527 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $423 (44%) lower.
