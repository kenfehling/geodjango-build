Home prices

The median home in Somerset costs $112,700. It is roughly $16,040 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $104,800 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $12,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

Somerset's median rent is $535 per month. It is approximately $48 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $527 (49%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $423 (44%) lower.
