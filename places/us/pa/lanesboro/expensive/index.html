Home prices

The median home in Lanesboro is valued at $86,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $6,420 (6%) lower. It is about $131,500 (60%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is approximately $8,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lanesboro is $504 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $225 (30%) lower. It is about $558 (52%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $310 (38%) lower compared to the city of Syracuse.
