Compared to its surrounding areas, Lock Haven is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Harrisburg however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Lock Haven is $104,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $15,340 (12%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $113,000 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg it is roughly $23,700 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Lock Haven the median rent is $747 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $114 (13%) lower. It is roughly $315 (29%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Harrisburg it is approximately $109 (12%) lower.
