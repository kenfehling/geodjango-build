In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lock Haven is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Harrisburg.

Home prices

The median home in Lock Haven is valued at $104,500. This is approximately $15,340 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $113,000 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $23,700 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Harrisburg.

Rents

The median rent in Lock Haven is $747 a month. It is roughly $114 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $315 (29%) lower, and it is about $109 (12%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
