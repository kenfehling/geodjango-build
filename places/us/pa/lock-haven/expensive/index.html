Compared to the surrounding areas, Lock Haven is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Harrisburg, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lock Haven is $104,500. This is about $15,340 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $113,000 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Harrisburg is roughly $23,700 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Lock Haven the median monthly rent is $747. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $114 (13%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $315 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $109 (12%) lower compared to the city of Harrisburg.
