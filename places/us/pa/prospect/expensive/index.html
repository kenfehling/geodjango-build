Home prices

Rents

In Prospect the median monthly rent is $855. This is about $57 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $207 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $103 (10%) lower.
