Home prices

The median home in Prospect costs $137,500. It is approximately $7,420 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $80,000 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is about $12,500 (10%) higher.

Rents

Prospect's median rent is $855 a month. This is approximately $57 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $207 (19%) lower, and is about $103 (10%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
