Compared to its nearby areas, California is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in California is valued at $114,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $30,080 (35%) higher.It is approximately $103,300 (47%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $10,800 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In California the median rent is $597 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $177 (22%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $465 (43%) lower, and is roughly $361 (37%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
