In comparison to the surrounding areas, California is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in California is valued at $114,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $30,080 (35%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $103,300 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is about $10,800 (8%) lower.

Rents

In California the median rent is $597 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $177 (22%) lower.It is approximately $465 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $361 (37%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
