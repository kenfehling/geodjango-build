In comparison to the surrounding areas, Kennett Square is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Kennett Square is $263,300. This is roughly $71,500 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $45,800 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $100,300 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Kennett Square's median rent is $1,103 a month. This is roughly $510 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $41 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia it is approximately $61 (5%) higher.
