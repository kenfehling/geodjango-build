Compared to its surrounding areas, Kennett Square is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Philadelphia however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Kennett Square's median home value is $263,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $71,500 (21%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $45,800 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia it is roughly $100,300 (61%) higher.

Rents

Kennett Square's median monthly rent is $1,103. This is roughly $510 (31%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $61 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
