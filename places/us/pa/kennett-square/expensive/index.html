Home prices

Kennett Square's median home cost is $263,300. It is about $71,500 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $45,800 (21%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $100,300 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Kennett Square's median rent is $1,103 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $510 (31%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $41 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $61 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
