In comparison to other places in this area, Kennett Square is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kennett Square is $263,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $71,500 (21%) lower.It is approximately $45,800 (21%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $100,300 (61%) higher compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kennett Square is $1,103. It is approximately $510 (31%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $41 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is roughly $61 (5%) higher.
