In comparison to its surrounding areas, Nazareth is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of New York, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nazareth costs $194,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $16,220 (7%) lower. It is roughly $22,900 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of New York it is roughly $411,400 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Nazareth is $957 per month. It is approximately $166 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $105 (9%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $486 (33%) lower compared to the city of New York.
