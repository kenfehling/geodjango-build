Home prices

The median home in Nazareth costs $194,600. This is roughly $16,220 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $22,900 (10%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $411,400 (67%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Nazareth the median monthly rent is $957. This is approximately $166 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $105 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of New York is approximately $486 (33%) lower.
