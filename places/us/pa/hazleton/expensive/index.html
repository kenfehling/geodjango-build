Home prices

The median home in Hazleton is valued at $97,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $15,780 (19%) higher. It is about $119,600 (54%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $65,100 (39%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Hazleton the median monthly rent is $812. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $133 (14%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $250 (23%) lower, and is approximately $230 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.
