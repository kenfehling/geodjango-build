Home prices

The median home value in Hazleton is $97,900. This is about $15,780 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $119,600 (54%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $65,100 (39%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Hazleton the median rent is $812 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $133 (14%) lower.It is roughly $250 (23%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Philadelphia is about $230 (22%) lower.
