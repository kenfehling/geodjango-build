Home prices

The median home price in Mount Penn is $110,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $34,160 (23%) lower. It is about $106,800 (49%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $52,300 (32%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Mount Penn's median rent is $997 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $118 (10%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $65 (6%) lower, and is roughly $45 (4%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
