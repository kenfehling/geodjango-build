Home prices

The median home in New Castle is valued at $61,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $19,820 (24%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $155,600 (71%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cleveland is about $7,700 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in New Castle is $676 a month. This is about $12 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $386 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $43 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Cleveland.
