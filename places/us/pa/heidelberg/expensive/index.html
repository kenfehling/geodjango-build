In comparison to other places in this area, Heidelberg is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Pittsburgh however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Heidelberg is valued at $117,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $68,700 (36%) lower.It is about $100,400 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $7,900 (6%) lower.

Rents

Heidelberg's median monthly rent is $823. This is about $304 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $239 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $135 (14%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
