Rents

Cresson's median monthly rent is $622. It is about $153 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $440 (41%) lower, and it is roughly $336 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
