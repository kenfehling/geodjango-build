Compared to other places in this area, Cresson is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Home prices

The median home in Cresson costs $107,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $13,480 (14%) higher.It is about $110,300 (50%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh is roughly $17,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

Cresson's median monthly rent is $622. It is approximately $153 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $440 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $336 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
