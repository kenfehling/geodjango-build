The value of the median home in Lehighton is $114,200. This is approximately $17,420 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $103,300 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is about $48,800 (29%) lower.

Rents

Lehighton's median monthly rent is $740. This is is very similar in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $322 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $302 (28%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
