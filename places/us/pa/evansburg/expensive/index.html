Home prices

The median home in Evansburg costs $376,800. This is approximately $60,200 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $159,300 (73%) higher, and compared to the city of New York it is approximately $229,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Evansburg the median rent is $2,024 per month. It is about $676 (50%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $962 (90%) higher, and is roughly $581 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of New York.
