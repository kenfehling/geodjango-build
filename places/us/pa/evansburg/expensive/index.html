Home prices

Evansburg's median home cost is $376,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $69,160 (22%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $159,300 (73%) higher, and in comparison to the city of New York is about $229,200 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Evansburg is $2,024. It is approximately $763 (60%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $962 (90%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $581 (40%) higher compared to the city of New York.
