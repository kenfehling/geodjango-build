Home prices

Fernville's median home cost is $231,300. This is approximately $98,540 (74%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $13,800 (6%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is about $100,000 (76%) higher.

Rents

Fernville's median rent is $1,083 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $329 (43%) higher. It is approximately $21 (1%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $79 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Allentown.
