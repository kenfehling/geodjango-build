Home prices

The median home value in Sayre is $131,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $21,200 (19%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $86,100 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is about $37,000 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Sayre the median rent is $736 a month. This is about $56 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $326 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $78 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
