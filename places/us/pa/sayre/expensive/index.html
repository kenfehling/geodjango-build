Compared to other places in this area, Sayre is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Syracuse however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Sayre's median home price is $131,400. This is about $21,200 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $86,100 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $37,000 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sayre is $736 a month. It is about $56 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $78 (9%) lower.
