Compared to nearby areas, Scranton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Allentown, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Scranton is valued at $103,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $39,360 (27%) lower. It is roughly $114,500 (52%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown is about $28,300 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Scranton the median rent is $782 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9 (1%) lower. It is approximately $280 (26%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $222 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Allentown.
