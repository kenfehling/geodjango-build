Compared to nearby areas, Scranton is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Allentown.

Home prices

The median home cost in Scranton is $103,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $39,360 (27%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $114,500 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown it is roughly $28,300 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scranton is $782. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $9 (1%) lower.It is about $280 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $222 (22%) lower.
