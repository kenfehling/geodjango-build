Rents

The median rent in Green Tree is $1,411 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $395 (38%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $349 (32%) higher, and is roughly $453 (47%) higher compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
