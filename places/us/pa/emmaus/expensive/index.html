Compared to other places in this area, Emmaus is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of New York.

Home prices

The median home in Emmaus is valued at $184,900. This is approximately $67,540 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $32,600 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $421,100 (69%) lower compared to the city of New York.

Rents

In Emmaus the median rent is $1,091 per month. This is approximately $236 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $29 (2%) higher, and is approximately $352 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.
