Home prices

The median home in South Bethlehem costs $85,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $5,680 (7%) higher. It is roughly $131,900 (60%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $39,400 (31%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.

Rents

In South Bethlehem the median rent is $525 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $68 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $537 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is roughly $433 (45%) lower.
