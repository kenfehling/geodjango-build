In comparison to other places in this area, South Bethlehem is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Pittsburgh, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South Bethlehem costs $85,600. This is about $5,680 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $131,900 (60%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh is about $39,400 (31%) lower.

Rents

In South Bethlehem the median monthly rent is $525. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $68 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $537 (50%) lower, and is roughly $433 (45%) lower compared to the city of Pittsburgh.
