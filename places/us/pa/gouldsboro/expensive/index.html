Home prices

Gouldsboro's median home price is $160,200. This is about $25,680 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $57,300 (26%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $2,800 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

Gouldsboro's median rent is $788 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $171 (17%) lower. It is approximately $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $254 (24%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
