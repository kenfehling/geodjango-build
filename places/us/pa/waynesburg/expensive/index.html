The median home in Waynesburg is valued at $106,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is is very similar. It is about $111,500 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $19,000 (15%) lower.

Rents

Waynesburg's median rent is $672 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $84 (11%) lower. It is approximately $390 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Pittsburgh it is approximately $286 (29%) lower.
