Home prices

The median home in Ephrata costs $155,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $44,860 (22%) lower.It is roughly $62,100 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $7,600 (4%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.

Rents

In Ephrata the median monthly rent is $860. It is approximately $122 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $202 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Philadelphia is approximately $182 (17%) lower.
