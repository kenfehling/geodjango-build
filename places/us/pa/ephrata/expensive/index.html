Rents

The median monthly rent in Ephrata is $860. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $122 (12%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $202 (19%) lower, and it is about $182 (17%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
