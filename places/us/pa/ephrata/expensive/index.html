Rents

The median monthly rent in Ephrata is $860. This is roughly $122 (12%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $182 (17%) lower compared to the city of Philadelphia.
