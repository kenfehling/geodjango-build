Rents

The median rent in Penn is $863 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $105 (13%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $199 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $95 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Pittsburgh.
