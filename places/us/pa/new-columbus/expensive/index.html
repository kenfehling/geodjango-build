Home prices

The median home in New Columbus costs $140,000. This is about $37,000 (35%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $77,500 (35%) lower, and is roughly $8,700 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Allentown.

Rents

New Columbus's median rent is $975 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $198 (25%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $87 (8%) lower, and it is approximately $29 (2%) lower compared to the city of Allentown.
