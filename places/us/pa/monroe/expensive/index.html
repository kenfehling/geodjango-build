In comparison to its surrounding areas, Monroe is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Syracuse, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monroe costs $128,500. This is about $17,960 (16%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $89,000 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $34,100 (36%) higher compared to the city of Syracuse.

Rents

In Monroe the median monthly rent is $883. It is about $135 (18%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $179 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse it is approximately $69 (8%) higher.
