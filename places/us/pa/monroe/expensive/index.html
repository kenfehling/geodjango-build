In comparison to other places in this area, Monroe is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Syracuse.

Home prices

The median home cost in Monroe is $128,500. This is approximately $17,960 (16%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $89,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Syracuse is roughly $34,100 (36%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monroe is $883. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $135 (18%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $179 (16%) lower, and is roughly $69 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Syracuse.
