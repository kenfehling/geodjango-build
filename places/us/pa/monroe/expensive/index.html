Home prices

The median home cost in Monroe is $128,500. It is about $17,960 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $89,000 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Syracuse it is about $34,100 (36%) higher.

Rents

In Monroe the median monthly rent is $883. This is about $135 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $179 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Syracuse is roughly $69 (8%) higher.
