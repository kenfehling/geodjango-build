Home prices

The median home in Rutherford is valued at $147,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $29,540 (16%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $70,400 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $13,000 (8%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.

Rents

The median rent in Rutherford is $1,137 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $160 (16%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $75 (7%) higher, and is approximately $64 (5%) higher compared to the city of Baltimore.
