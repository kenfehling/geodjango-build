Home prices

West Easton's median home cost is $154,600. This is about $17,320 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $62,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $451,400 (74%) lower in comparison to the city of New York.

Rents

In West Easton the median rent is $1,007 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $240 (19%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $55 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of New York is roughly $436 (30%) lower.
