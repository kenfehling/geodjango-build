Home prices

Trucksville's median home cost is $172,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $46,000 (36%) higher. It is roughly $45,500 (20%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is approximately $40,700 (30%) higher.

Rents

In Trucksville the median monthly rent is $868. It is approximately $161 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $194 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Allentown it is roughly $136 (13%) lower.
