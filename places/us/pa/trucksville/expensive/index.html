Home prices

The median home in Trucksville costs $172,000. This is about $46,000 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $45,500 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown it is roughly $40,700 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Trucksville is $868 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $161 (22%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $194 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of Allentown is roughly $136 (13%) lower.
