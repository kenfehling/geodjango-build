Home prices

The median home in Berrysburg is valued at $112,800. It is roughly $12,740 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $104,700 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is approximately $47,300 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Berrysburg is $771. Compared to other places in this area this is about $125 (19%) higher. It is roughly $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $302 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Baltimore.
