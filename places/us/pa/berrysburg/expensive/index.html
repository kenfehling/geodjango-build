Compared to other places in this area, Berrysburg is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Baltimore however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Berrysburg is valued at $112,800. It is about $12,740 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $104,700 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Baltimore it is approximately $47,300 (29%) lower.

Rents

In Berrysburg the median monthly rent is $771. It is approximately $125 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $291 (27%) lower, and it is approximately $302 (28%) lower compared to the city of Baltimore.
