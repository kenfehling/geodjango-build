Home prices

The median home cost in Austin is $59,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $24,180 (28%) lower.It is about $158,300 (72%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of State College it is roughly $253,200 (81%) lower.

Rents

In Austin the median rent is $663 per month. This is roughly $78 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $399 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of State College is roughly $391 (37%) lower.
