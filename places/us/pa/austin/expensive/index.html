Home prices

The price of the median home in Austin is $59,200. It is about $24,180 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $158,300 (72%) lower, and it is about $253,200 (81%) lower compared to the city of State College.

Rents

In Austin the median rent is $663 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $78 (10%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $399 (37%) lower, and it is about $391 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of State College.
