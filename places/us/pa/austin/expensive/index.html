Home prices

Austin's median home cost is $59,200. This is roughly $24,180 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $158,300 (72%) lower, and compared to the city of State College it is approximately $253,200 (81%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Austin is $663 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $78 (10%) lower. It is roughly $399 (37%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $391 (37%) lower compared to the city of State College.
