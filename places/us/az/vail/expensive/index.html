Home prices

The value of the median home in Vail is $237,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $33,660 (16%) higher.It is approximately $20,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $82,200 (52%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.

Rents

In Vail the median rent is $1,445 a month. It is approximately $245 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $383 (36%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $599 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Tucson.
