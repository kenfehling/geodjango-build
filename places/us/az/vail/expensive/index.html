Home prices

The median home in Vail is valued at $237,500. It is roughly $33,660 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $20,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tucson it is roughly $82,200 (52%) higher.

Rents

Vail's median monthly rent is $1,445. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $245 (20%) higher.It is about $383 (36%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $599 (70%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.
