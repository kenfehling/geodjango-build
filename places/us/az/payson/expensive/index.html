Home prices

The median home cost in Payson is $236,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $52,740 (28%) higher. It is approximately $18,800 (8%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is is very similar.

Rents

Payson's median rent is $966 per month. It is about $189 (24%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $96 (9%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $87 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
