Compared to the surrounding areas, Payson is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The median home in Payson costs $236,300. This is about $52,740 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $18,800 (8%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Payson the median monthly rent is $966. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $189 (24%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $96 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Phoenix is about $87 (8%) lower.
