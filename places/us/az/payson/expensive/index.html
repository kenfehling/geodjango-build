Home prices

The cost of the median home in Payson is $236,300. This is roughly $52,740 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $18,800 (8%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Payson's median rent is $966 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $189 (24%) higher.It is about $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Phoenix is roughly $87 (8%) lower.
