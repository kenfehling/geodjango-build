Home prices

Sahuarita's median home cost is $214,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $44,720 (26%) higher.It is roughly $3,400 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Tucson it is about $58,800 (37%) higher.

Rents

Sahuarita's median monthly rent is $1,392. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $286 (25%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $330 (31%) higher, and it is approximately $546 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Tucson.
