Home prices

The median home in Sahuarita costs $214,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $44,720 (26%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $3,400 (1%) lower, and it is roughly $58,800 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Tucson.

Rents

In Sahuarita the median rent is $1,392 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $286 (25%) higher. It is roughly $330 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $546 (64%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.
