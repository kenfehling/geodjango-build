Compared to its nearby areas, Sahuarita is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Tucson, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Sahuarita's median home cost is $214,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $44,720 (26%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $3,400 (1%) lower, and it is approximately $58,800 (37%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.

Rents

The median rent in Sahuarita is $1,392 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $286 (25%) higher.It is approximately $330 (31%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tucson it is approximately $546 (64%) higher.
