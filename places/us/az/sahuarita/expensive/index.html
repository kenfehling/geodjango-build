Compared to its surrounding areas, Sahuarita is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Tucson, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Sahuarita's median home cost is $214,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $44,720 (26%) higher. It is about $3,400 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $58,800 (37%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.

Rents

In Sahuarita the median monthly rent is $1,392. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $286 (25%) higher. It is about $330 (31%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $546 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Tucson.
