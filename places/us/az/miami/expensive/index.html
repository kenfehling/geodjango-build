Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Miami is $689. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $35 (4%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $373 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $364 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
