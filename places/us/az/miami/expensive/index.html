Home prices

Miami's median home value is $66,600. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $26,640 (28%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $150,900 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $168,800 (71%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Miami is $689 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $35 (4%) lower. It is roughly $373 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $364 (34%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.
