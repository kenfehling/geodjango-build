Home prices

The median home in Thatcher costs $173,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $48,680 (38%) higher. It is approximately $43,800 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tucson is about $18,400 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Thatcher the median monthly rent is $905. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $86 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $157 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $59 (6%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.
