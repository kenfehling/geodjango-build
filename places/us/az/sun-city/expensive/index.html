Home prices

The median home in Sun City costs $159,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $54,180 (25%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $57,600 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $75,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Sun City the median monthly rent is $1,088. This is about $164 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $26 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $35 (3%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
