Compared to the surrounding areas, Sun City is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Phoenixhowever, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Sun City's median home value is $159,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $54,180 (25%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $57,600 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $75,500 (32%) lower.

Rents

Sun City's median rent is $1,088 a month. It is about $164 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $26 (2%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $35 (3%) higher.
