Compared to nearby areas, Sun City is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Phoenix, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sun City is $159,900. This is roughly $54,180 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $57,600 (26%) lower, and it is roughly $75,500 (32%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Sun City the median monthly rent is $1,088. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $164 (13%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $26 (2%) higher, and it is about $35 (3%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
