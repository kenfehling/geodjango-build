In comparison to its surrounding areas, Drexel Heights is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Tucson, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Drexel Heights is $137,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $29,660 (27%) higher. It is approximately $80,300 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Tucson it is about $18,100 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Drexel Heights is $945. It is approximately $61 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $117 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Tucson it is roughly $99 (11%) higher.
