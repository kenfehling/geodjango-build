Compared to the surrounding areas, Sedona is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The median home in Sedona is valued at $507,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $297,740 (142%) higher.It is about $289,900 (133%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Phoenix is about $272,000 (115%) higher.

Rents

In Sedona the median rent is $1,300 a month. It is about $315 (31%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $238 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix is approximately $247 (23%) higher.
