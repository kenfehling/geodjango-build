Home prices

The median home in Sedona is valued at $507,400. It is about $297,740 (142%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $289,900 (133%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $272,000 (115%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sedona is $1,300. This is roughly $315 (31%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $238 (22%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $247 (23%) higher.
