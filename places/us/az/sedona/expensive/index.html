In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sedona is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The median home price in Sedona is $507,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $297,740 (142%) higher. It is roughly $289,900 (133%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is about $272,000 (115%) higher.

Rents

In Sedona the median rent is $1,300 per month. It is approximately $315 (31%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $238 (22%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is approximately $247 (23%) higher.
