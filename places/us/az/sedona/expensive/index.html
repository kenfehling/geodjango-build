Compared to nearby areas, Sedona is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sedona is valued at $507,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $297,740 (142%) higher. It is about $289,900 (133%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $272,000 (115%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sedona is $1,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $315 (31%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $238 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is approximately $247 (23%) higher.
