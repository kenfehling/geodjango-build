Home prices

The median home in Jerome costs $287,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $64,480 (28%) higher. It is approximately $69,500 (31%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is about $51,600 (21%) higher.

Rents

Jerome's median rent is $844 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $161 (16%) lower. It is roughly $218 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $209 (19%) lower.
