Compared to the surrounding areas, Fountain Hills is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fountain Hills is $402,100. It is approximately $212,020 (34%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $184,600 (84%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is about $166,700 (70%) higher.

Rents

In Fountain Hills the median monthly rent is $1,259. This is approximately $184 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $197 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $206 (19%) higher.
