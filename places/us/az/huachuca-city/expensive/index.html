Home prices

The median home cost in Huachuca City is $93,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $56,380 (37%) lower. It is roughly $123,700 (56%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Tucson is roughly $61,500 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Huachuca City the median monthly rent is $648. This is approximately $72 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $414 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tucson it is about $198 (23%) lower.
