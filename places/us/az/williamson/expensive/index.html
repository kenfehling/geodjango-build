Home prices

The median home in Williamson costs $435,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $190,320 (77%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $218,400 (100%) higher, and it is about $200,500 (85%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Williamson is $840. This is about $79 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $222 (20%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is about $213 (20%) lower.
