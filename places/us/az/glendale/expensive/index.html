Home prices

The median home price in Glendale is $214,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $11,580 (5%) higher.It is approximately $2,800 (1%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is roughly $20,700 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Glendale is $997 per month. It is about $273 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $65 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is approximately $56 (5%) lower.
