In comparison to the surrounding areas, Glendale is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The median home in Glendale is valued at $214,700. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $11,580 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $2,800 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is roughly $20,700 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Glendale is $997. This is about $273 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $65 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $56 (5%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.
