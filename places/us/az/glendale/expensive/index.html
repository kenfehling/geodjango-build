Home prices

The median home in Glendale costs $214,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $11,580 (5%) higher.It is approximately $2,800 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $20,700 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Glendale the median rent is $997 per month. It is approximately $273 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $65 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $56 (5%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.
