In comparison to its surrounding areas, Glendale is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Phoenix, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Glendale is $214,700. This is about $11,580 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $2,800 (1%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $20,700 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median rent in Glendale is $997 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $273 (21%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $65 (6%) lower, and is roughly $56 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
