Home prices

The value of the median home in Glendale is $214,700. It is about $11,580 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $2,800 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $20,700 (8%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Glendale the median rent is $997 a month. This is approximately $273 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $65 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $56 (5%) lower.
