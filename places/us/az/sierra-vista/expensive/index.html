Home prices

Sierra Vista's median home cost is $172,600. It is approximately $45,320 (35%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $44,900 (20%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $17,300 (11%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.

Rents

Sierra Vista's median monthly rent is $938. This is approximately $166 (21%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $124 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $92 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Tucson.
