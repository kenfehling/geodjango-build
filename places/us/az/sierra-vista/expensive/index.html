In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sierra Vista is significantly more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Tucson, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Sierra Vista's median home cost is $172,600. This is about $45,320 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Tucson is about $17,300 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sierra Vista is $938 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $166 (21%) higher.It is approximately $124 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Tucson it is about $92 (10%) higher.
