Home prices

The median home in Sierra Vista costs $172,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $45,320 (35%) higher. It is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $17,300 (11%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.

Rents

Sierra Vista's median monthly rent is $938. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $166 (21%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $124 (11%) lower, and is about $92 (10%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.
