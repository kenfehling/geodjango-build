Home prices

Rents

San Luis's median rent is $671 per month. It is roughly $125 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $391 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Yuma is roughly $220 (24%) lower.
