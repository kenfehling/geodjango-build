Home prices

The median home in Mesquite Creek costs $122,500. This is roughly $8,140 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $95,000 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Paradise it is approximately $116,900 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mesquite Creek is $1,011 per month. This is about $377 (59%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $51 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $33 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Paradise.
