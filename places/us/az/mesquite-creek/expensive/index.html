Home prices

The median home price in Mesquite Creek is $122,500. This is about $8,140 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $95,000 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Paradise it is about $116,900 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mesquite Creek is $1,011 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $377 (59%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $51 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Paradise is about $33 (3%) higher.
