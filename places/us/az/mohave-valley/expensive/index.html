Home prices

The median home cost in Mohave Valley is $165,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $43,700 (35%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $51,800 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $153,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Henderson.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mohave Valley is $544. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $183 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $518 (48%) lower, and is roughly $748 (57%) lower compared to the city of Henderson.
