Home prices

The median home in Mohave Valley is valued at $165,700. It is roughly $43,700 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $51,800 (23%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Henderson is roughly $153,100 (48%) lower.

Rents

Mohave Valley's median rent is $544 a month. This is roughly $183 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $518 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Henderson it is about $748 (57%) lower.
