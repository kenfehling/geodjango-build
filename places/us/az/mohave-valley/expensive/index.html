Compared to the surrounding areas, Mohave Valley is substantially more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Henderson however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mohave Valley is valued at $165,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $43,700 (35%) higher.It is about $51,800 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $153,100 (48%) lower compared to the city of Henderson.

Rents

The median rent in Mohave Valley is $544 per month. It is approximately $183 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $518 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Henderson it is about $748 (57%) lower.
