Home prices

The median home in Fort Apache is valued at $70,800. This is about $11,940 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $146,700 (67%) lower, and it is approximately $100,000 (58%) lower compared to the city of Show Low.

Rents

The median rent in Fort Apache is $427 per month. It is approximately $45 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $635 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Show Low it is about $503 (54%) lower.
