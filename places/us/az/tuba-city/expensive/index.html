Rents

Tuba City's median monthly rent is $632. It is roughly $29 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $430 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $633 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Flagstaff.
