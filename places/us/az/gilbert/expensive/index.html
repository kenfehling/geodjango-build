In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gilbert is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Gilbert is $328,700. It is approximately $44,460 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $111,200 (51%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Phoenix is roughly $93,300 (39%) higher.

Rents

Gilbert's median monthly rent is $1,518. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $206 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $456 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is about $465 (44%) higher.
