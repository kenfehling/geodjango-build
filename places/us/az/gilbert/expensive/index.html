In comparison to its surrounding areas, Gilbert is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Phoenix, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Gilbert costs $328,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $44,460 (15%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $111,200 (51%) higher, and is approximately $93,300 (39%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Gilbert's median monthly rent is $1,518. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $206 (15%) higher. It is about $456 (42%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $465 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
