In comparison to nearby areas, Gilbert is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The median home in Gilbert is valued at $328,700. It is approximately $44,460 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $111,200 (51%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Phoenix is roughly $93,300 (39%) higher.

Rents

Gilbert's median monthly rent is $1,518. It is approximately $206 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $456 (42%) higher, and is roughly $465 (44%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
