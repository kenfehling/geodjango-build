In comparison to the surrounding areas, Gilbert is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Gilbert is $328,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $44,460 (15%) higher.It is about $111,200 (51%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is about $93,300 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Gilbert the median rent is $1,518 a month. It is about $206 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $456 (42%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $465 (44%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
