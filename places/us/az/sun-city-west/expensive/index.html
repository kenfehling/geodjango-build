Compared to other places in this area, Sun City West is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Phoenix however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sun City West is valued at $221,500. This is about $25,480 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $4,000 (1%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $13,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Sun City West's median rent is $1,250 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $75 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $188 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is approximately $197 (18%) higher.
