Home prices

Sun City West's median home price is $221,500. This is roughly $25,480 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $4,000 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $13,900 (5%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Sun City West the median rent is $1,250 a month. This is approximately $75 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $188 (17%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $197 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
