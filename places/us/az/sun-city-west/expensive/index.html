Home prices

The value of the median home in Sun City West is $221,500. It is roughly $25,480 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $4,000 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is roughly $13,900 (5%) lower.

Rents

Sun City West's median rent is $1,250 a month. It is about $75 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $188 (17%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $197 (18%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
