Home prices

The median home in Sun City West is valued at $221,500. This is approximately $25,480 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $4,000 (1%) higher, and it is approximately $13,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Sun City West the median monthly rent is $1,250. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $75 (6%) higher. It is roughly $188 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $197 (18%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
