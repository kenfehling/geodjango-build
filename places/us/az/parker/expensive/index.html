Home prices

Parker's median home price is $158,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $67,520 (74%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $59,400 (27%) lower, and is approximately $90,100 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City.

Rents

The median rent in Parker is $858 a month. It is about $216 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $204 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $62 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City.
