Compared to its nearby areas, Parker is a lot more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Lake Havasu City.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Parker is $158,100. This is about $67,520 (74%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $59,400 (27%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Lake Havasu City it is roughly $90,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

Parker's median monthly rent is $858. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $216 (33%) higher. It is about $204 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $62 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City.
