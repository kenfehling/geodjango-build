Compared to its surrounding areas, Parker is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Lake Havasu City, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Parker is $158,100. It is roughly $67,520 (74%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $59,400 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City is roughly $90,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Parker is $858. Compared to nearby areas it is about $216 (33%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $204 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Lake Havasu City is roughly $62 (6%) lower.
