Compared to other places in this area, Parker is substantially more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Lake Havasu City however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Parker is $158,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $67,520 (74%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $59,400 (27%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City it is approximately $90,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Parker is $858. It is about $216 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $204 (19%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $62 (6%) lower compared to the city of Lake Havasu City.
