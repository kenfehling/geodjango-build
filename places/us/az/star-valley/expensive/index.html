Home prices

Rents

In Star Valley the median rent is $323 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $582 (64%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $739 (69%) lower, and it is about $730 (69%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.
