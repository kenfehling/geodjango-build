In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tucson is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home price in Tucson is $155,300. This is roughly $62,117 (28%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Tucson the median monthly rent is $846. This is roughly $198 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $216 (20%) lower compared to the US as a whole.
