Home prices

The median home in Tucson is valued at $155,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $62,117 (28%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Tucson is $846 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $198 (18%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $216 (20%) lower.
