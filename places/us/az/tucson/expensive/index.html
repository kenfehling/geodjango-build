Compared to other places in this area, Tucson is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country.

Home prices

The median home in Tucson is valued at $155,300. This is about $62,117 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $62,200 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tucson is $846. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $198 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $216 (20%) lower.
