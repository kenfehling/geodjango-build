Home prices

The median home in Tucson costs $155,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $62,117 (28%) lower. It is approximately $62,200 (28%) lower compared to the entire country.

Rents

Tucson's median rent is $846 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $198 (18%) lower. It is approximately $216 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country.
