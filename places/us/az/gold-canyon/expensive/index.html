Home prices

The median home in Gold Canyon costs $298,100. This is roughly $53,640 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $80,600 (37%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $62,700 (26%) higher.

Rents

Gold Canyon's median rent is $1,013 a month. This is roughly $251 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $49 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $40 (3%) lower.
