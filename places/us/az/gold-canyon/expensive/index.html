Home prices

Rents

In Gold Canyon the median rent is $1,013 a month. This is about $251 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $49 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $40 (3%) lower.
