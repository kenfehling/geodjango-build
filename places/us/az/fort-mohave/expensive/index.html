Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fort Mohave is $163,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $30,340 (22%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $54,300 (24%) lower, and is approximately $76,200 (31%) lower compared to the city of Paradise.

Rents

Fort Mohave's median rent is $916 a month. This is about $236 (34%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $146 (13%) lower, and it is about $62 (6%) lower compared to the city of Paradise.
