Compared to the surrounding areas, Fort Mohave is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Paradise.

Home prices

The median home in Fort Mohave costs $163,200. Compared to nearby areas it is about $30,340 (22%) higher. It is roughly $54,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $76,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Paradise.

Rents

Fort Mohave's median monthly rent is $916. It is roughly $236 (34%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $146 (13%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Paradise is roughly $62 (6%) lower.
