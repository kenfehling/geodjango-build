Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Fort Mohave is $916 a month. This is about $236 (34%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $146 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Paradise it is about $62 (6%) lower.
