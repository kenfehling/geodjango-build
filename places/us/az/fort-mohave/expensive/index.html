Home prices

The median home in Fort Mohave costs $163,200. It is roughly $30,340 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $54,300 (24%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $76,200 (31%) lower compared to the city of Paradise.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Mohave is $916. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $236 (34%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $146 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Paradise it is roughly $62 (6%) lower.
