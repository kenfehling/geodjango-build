Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fort Mohave is $163,200. This is approximately $30,340 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $54,300 (24%) lower, and is about $76,200 (31%) lower compared to the city of Paradise.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Mohave is $916. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $236 (34%) higher. It is about $146 (13%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Paradise is roughly $62 (6%) lower.
