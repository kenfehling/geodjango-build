Home prices

The median home in Sierra Vista Southeast is valued at $188,900. It is roughly $70,640 (59%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $28,600 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $33,600 (21%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sierra Vista Southeast is $797. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $30 (3%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $265 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tucson is roughly $49 (5%) lower.
