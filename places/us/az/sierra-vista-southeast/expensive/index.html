Home prices

The median home in Sierra Vista Southeast is valued at $188,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $70,640 (59%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $28,600 (13%) lower, and it is about $33,600 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Tucson.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sierra Vista Southeast is $797. It is about $30 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $265 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $49 (5%) lower compared to the city of Tucson.
