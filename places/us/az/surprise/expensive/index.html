Compared to the surrounding areas, Surprise is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The median home value in Surprise is $239,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $47,080 (24%) higher. It is about $22,000 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is roughly $4,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

Surprise's median monthly rent is $1,349. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $193 (16%) higher. It is approximately $287 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $296 (28%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
