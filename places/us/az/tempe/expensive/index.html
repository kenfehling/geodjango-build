Home prices

The price of the median home in Tempe is $272,900. It is roughly $221,560 (44%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $55,400 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is about $37,500 (15%) higher.

Rents
