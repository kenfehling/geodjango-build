In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tempe is significantly less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Tempe is $272,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $221,560 (44%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $55,400 (25%) higher, and it is about $37,500 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tempe is $1,164. This is approximately $248 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $102 (9%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $111 (10%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
