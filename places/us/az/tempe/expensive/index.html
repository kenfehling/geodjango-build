Home prices

Tempe's median home price is $272,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $221,560 (44%) lower. It is roughly $55,400 (25%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $37,500 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tempe is $1,164. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $248 (17%) lower. It is roughly $102 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $111 (10%) higher.
