In comparison to other places in this area, Youngtown is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Phoenix however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Youngtown is valued at $155,400. This is about $59,580 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $62,100 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $80,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Youngtown the median rent is $957 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $321 (25%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $105 (9%) lower, and is roughly $96 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
