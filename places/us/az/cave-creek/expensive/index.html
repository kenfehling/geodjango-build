In comparison to its nearby areas, Cave Creek is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cave Creek is valued at $482,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $40,540 (9%) higher. It is about $265,100 (121%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is about $247,200 (105%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cave Creek is $1,335. This is about $196 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $273 (25%) higher, and it is roughly $282 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
