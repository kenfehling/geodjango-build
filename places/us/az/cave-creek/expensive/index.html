In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cave Creek is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cave Creek is $482,600. It is approximately $40,540 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $265,100 (121%) higher, and is roughly $247,200 (105%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Cave Creek's median rent is $1,335 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $196 (12%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $273 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix is about $282 (26%) higher.
