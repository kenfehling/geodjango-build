In comparison to its nearby areas, Cave Creek is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Phoenix, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Cave Creek is $482,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $40,540 (9%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $265,100 (121%) higher, and is about $247,200 (105%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median rent in Cave Creek is $1,335 a month. It is about $196 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $273 (25%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is about $282 (26%) higher.
