In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cave Creek is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cave Creek is $482,600. It is roughly $40,540 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $265,100 (121%) higher, and is roughly $247,200 (105%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Cave Creek the median rent is $1,335 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $196 (12%) lower.It is approximately $273 (25%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is about $282 (26%) higher.
