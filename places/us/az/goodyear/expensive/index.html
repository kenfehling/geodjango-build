Home prices

The value of the median home in Goodyear is $290,800. It is roughly $77,020 (36%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $73,300 (33%) higher, and it is about $55,400 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Goodyear's median monthly rent is $1,375. This is approximately $281 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $313 (29%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $322 (30%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
