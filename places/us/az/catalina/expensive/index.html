Home prices

The value of the median home in Catalina is $167,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $137,140 (44%) lower.It is approximately $49,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $12,600 (8%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.

Rents

In Catalina the median rent is $902 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $280 (23%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $160 (15%) lower, and it is about $56 (6%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.
