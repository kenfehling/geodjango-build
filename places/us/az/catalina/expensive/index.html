In comparison to its surrounding areas, Catalina is a lot less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Tucson however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Catalina is $167,900. It is about $137,140 (44%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $49,600 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tucson it is approximately $12,600 (8%) higher.

Rents

Catalina's median rent is $902 a month. It is about $280 (23%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $160 (15%) lower, and is roughly $56 (6%) higher compared to the city of Tucson.
