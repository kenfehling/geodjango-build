Compared to its surrounding areas, St. Michaels is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Gallup.

Home prices

The median home in St. Michaels costs $77,700. This is approximately $30,900 (66%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $139,800 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of Gallup it is roughly $54,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

In St. Michaels the median monthly rent is $458. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $24 (4%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $604 (56%) lower, and compared to the city of Gallup it is roughly $277 (37%) lower.
