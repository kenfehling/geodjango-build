Home prices

The median home in Chandler is valued at $300,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $60,680 (25%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $83,300 (38%) higher, and it is about $65,400 (27%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Chandler's median rent is $1,312 a month. This is about $149 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $250 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is about $259 (24%) higher.
