In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chandler is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Phoenix, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chandler is valued at $300,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $60,680 (25%) higher. It is about $83,300 (38%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is approximately $65,400 (27%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chandler is $1,312. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $149 (12%) higher. It is roughly $250 (23%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $259 (24%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
