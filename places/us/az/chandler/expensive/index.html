In comparison to other places in this area, Chandler is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Phoenix, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chandler costs $300,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $60,680 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $83,300 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is about $65,400 (27%) higher.

Rents

Chandler's median rent is $1,312 per month. This is approximately $149 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $250 (23%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $259 (24%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
