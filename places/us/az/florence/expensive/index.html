In comparison to its surrounding areas, Florence is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Phoenix however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florence is valued at $153,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $29,600 (16%) lower.It is about $64,000 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $81,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median rent in Florence is $824 a month. This is about $288 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $238 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $229 (21%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.
