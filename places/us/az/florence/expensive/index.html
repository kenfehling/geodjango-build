Compared to its surrounding areas, Florence is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Phoenix, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florence costs $153,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $29,600 (16%) lower. It is approximately $64,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $81,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Florence's median rent is $824 a month. It is about $288 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $238 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
