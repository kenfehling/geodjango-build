Rents

The median monthly rent in Bisbee is $704. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $47 (6%) lower. It is roughly $358 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Tucson it is roughly $142 (16%) lower.
