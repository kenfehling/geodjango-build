Compared to other places in this area, Lake Havasu City is substantially more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

Lake Havasu City's median home cost is $248,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $138,467 (126%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $30,700 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Havasu City is $920. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $240 (35%) higher. It is roughly $142 (13%) lower compared to the entire nation.
