Compared to other places in this area, Phoenix is significantly less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Phoenix is $235,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $161,567 (40%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $17,900 (8%) higher.

Rents

In Phoenix the median monthly rent is $1,053. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $232 (18%) lower. It is is very similar compared to the entire country.
