Home prices

The median home in Phoenix is valued at $235,400. It is roughly $161,567 (40%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $17,900 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the country.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Phoenix is $1,053. This is roughly $232 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar.
