Home prices

The median home in Camp Verde is valued at $193,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $16,560 (7%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $24,400 (11%) lower, and it is about $42,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median rent in Camp Verde is $710 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $275 (27%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $352 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $343 (32%) lower.
