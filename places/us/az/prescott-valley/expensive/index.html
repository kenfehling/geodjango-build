Home prices

Prescott Valley's median home cost is $228,700. It is about $72,280 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $11,200 (5%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is approximately $6,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

Prescott Valley's median rent is $992 per month. It is about $109 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $70 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $61 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
