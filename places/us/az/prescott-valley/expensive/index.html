In comparison to the surrounding areas, Prescott Valley is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Phoenix, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Prescott Valley costs $228,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $72,280 (24%) lower. It is about $11,200 (5%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $6,700 (2%) lower.

Rents

Prescott Valley's median rent is $992 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $109 (12%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $70 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is about $61 (5%) lower.
