Home prices

The median home price in Chino Valley is $229,100. It is approximately $74,200 (24%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $11,600 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $6,300 (2%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Chino Valley the median rent is $875 a month. It is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $187 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Phoenix is about $178 (16%) lower.
