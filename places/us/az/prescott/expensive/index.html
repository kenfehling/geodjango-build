In comparison to other places in this area, Prescott is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Prescott is $347,700. It is roughly $105,500 (43%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $130,200 (59%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $112,300 (47%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Prescott is $893. It is roughly $22 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $169 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $160 (15%) lower compared to the city of Phoenix.
