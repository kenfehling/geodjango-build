In comparison to its surrounding areas, Prescott is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Phoenix, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Prescott's median home price is $347,700. It is about $105,500 (43%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $130,200 (59%) higher, and is about $112,300 (47%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Prescott's median monthly rent is $893. It is roughly $22 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $169 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $160 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
