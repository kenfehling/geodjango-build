Home prices

The cost of the median home in Nogales is $119,700. It is approximately $65,300 (35%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $97,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $35,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Tucson.

Rents

In Nogales the median rent is $590 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $519 (46%) lower.It is roughly $472 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tucson it is roughly $256 (30%) lower.
