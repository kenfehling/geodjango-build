In comparison to the surrounding areas, Queen Creek is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The median home price in Queen Creek is $348,600. This is roughly $80,340 (29%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $131,100 (60%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $113,200 (48%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

Queen Creek's median monthly rent is $1,590. This is approximately $254 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $528 (49%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $537 (50%) higher.
