Compared to other places in this area, Queen Creek is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Queen Creek's median home price is $348,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $80,340 (29%) higher.It is roughly $131,100 (60%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $113,200 (48%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Queen Creek the median monthly rent is $1,590. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $254 (19%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $528 (49%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $537 (50%) higher.
