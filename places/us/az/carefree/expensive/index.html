Home prices

The median home cost in Carefree is $747,000. It is roughly $330,300 (79%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $529,500 (243%) higher, and is roughly $511,600 (217%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Carefree the median monthly rent is $1,446. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $64 (4%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $384 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is about $393 (37%) higher.
