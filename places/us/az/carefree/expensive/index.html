Compared to nearby areas, Carefree is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Carefree is $747,000. It is roughly $330,300 (79%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $529,500 (243%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is roughly $511,600 (217%) higher.

Rents

Carefree's median monthly rent is $1,446. This is roughly $64 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $384 (36%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $393 (37%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
