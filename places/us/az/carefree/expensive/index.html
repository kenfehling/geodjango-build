Home prices

Rents

Carefree's median monthly rent is $1,446. This is approximately $64 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $384 (36%) higher, and is approximately $393 (37%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
