Compared to nearby areas, Scottsdale is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Scottsdale is $475,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $223,640 (31%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $257,800 (118%) higher, and compared to the city of Phoenix it is approximately $239,900 (101%) higher.

Rents

In Scottsdale the median rent is $1,365 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $277 (16%) lower. It is about $303 (28%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is approximately $312 (29%) higher.
