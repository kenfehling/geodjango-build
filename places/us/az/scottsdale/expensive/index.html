In comparison to nearby areas, Scottsdale is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Phoenix, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Scottsdale's median home price is $475,300. It is about $223,640 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $257,800 (118%) higher, and it is about $239,900 (101%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scottsdale is $1,365. It is roughly $277 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $303 (28%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $312 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.
