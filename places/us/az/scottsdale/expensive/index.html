Compared to its nearby areas, Scottsdale is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Phoenix, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Scottsdale is valued at $475,300. It is about $223,640 (31%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $257,800 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $239,900 (101%) higher in comparison to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

In Scottsdale the median rent is $1,365 per month. It is about $277 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $303 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $312 (29%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
