In comparison to other places in this area, Scottsdale is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

Scottsdale's median home price is $475,300. This is approximately $223,640 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $257,800 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix is roughly $239,900 (101%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Scottsdale is $1,365 per month. It is about $277 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $303 (28%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $312 (29%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.
