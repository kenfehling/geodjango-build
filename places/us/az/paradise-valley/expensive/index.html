Compared to its nearby areas, Paradise Valley is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Phoenix.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Paradise Valley is $1,516,200. It is about $1,223,180 (417%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,298,700 (597%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $1,280,800 (544%) higher compared to the city of Phoenix.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Paradise Valley is $2,547. It is about $1,462 (134%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $1,485 (139%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Phoenix it is about $1,494 (141%) higher.
