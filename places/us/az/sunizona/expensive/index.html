Home prices

The median home cost in Sunizona is $75,700. This is approximately $23,540 (23%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $141,800 (65%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $79,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of Tucson.

Rents

The median rent in Sunizona is $638 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $97 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $424 (39%) lower, and is about $208 (24%) lower compared to the city of Tucson.
