Home prices

Rents

In Tombstone the median monthly rent is $563. It is about $160 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $499 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tucson it is about $283 (33%) lower.
