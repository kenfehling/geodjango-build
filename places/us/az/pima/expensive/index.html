Home prices

The median home in Pima costs $138,300. It is roughly $9,820 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $79,200 (36%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Tucson it is roughly $17,000 (10%) lower.

Rents

Pima's median monthly rent is $963. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $175 (22%) higher. It is roughly $99 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Tucson it is approximately $117 (13%) higher.
