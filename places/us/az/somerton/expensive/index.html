In comparison to other places in this area, Somerton is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Yuma.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Somerton is $127,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38,460 (43%) higher. It is approximately $90,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $16,400 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Yuma.

Rents

Somerton's median rent is $750 per month. It is about $31 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $312 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yuma it is about $141 (15%) lower.
