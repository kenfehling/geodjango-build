Compared to the surrounding areas, Somerton is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Yuma, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Somerton costs $127,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $38,460 (43%) higher.It is roughly $90,500 (41%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Yuma it is roughly $16,400 (11%) lower.

Rents

Somerton's median rent is $750 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $31 (3%) lower.It is approximately $312 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $141 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Yuma.
