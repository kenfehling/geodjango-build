Home prices

The median home in Somerton costs $127,000. This is about $38,460 (43%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $90,500 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Yuma is approximately $16,400 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Somerton is $750. It is approximately $31 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $141 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Yuma.
