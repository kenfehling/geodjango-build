Home prices

The median home value in Tucson Estates is $132,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $18,800 (16%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $85,300 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Tucson it is approximately $23,100 (14%) lower.

Rents

Tucson Estates's median rent is $995 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $126 (14%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $67 (6%) lower, and compared to the city of Tucson is roughly $149 (17%) higher.
