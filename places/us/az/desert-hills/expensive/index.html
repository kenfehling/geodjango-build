Home prices

The median home in Desert Hills is valued at $167,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $44,180 (35%) higher. It is about $49,700 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City it is roughly $80,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Desert Hills the median monthly rent is $736. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $128 (21%) higher. It is about $326 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Lake Havasu City is approximately $184 (19%) lower.
