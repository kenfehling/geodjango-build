Home prices

The median home in Desert Hills is valued at $167,800. This is roughly $44,180 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $49,700 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $80,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Lake Havasu City.

Rents

In Desert Hills the median rent is $736 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $128 (21%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $326 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City is about $184 (19%) lower.
