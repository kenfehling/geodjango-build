Home prices

The median home in Desert Hills is valued at $167,800. It is roughly $44,180 (35%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $49,700 (22%) lower, and is approximately $80,400 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Desert Hills is $736. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $128 (21%) higher.It is roughly $326 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Lake Havasu City is about $184 (19%) lower.
