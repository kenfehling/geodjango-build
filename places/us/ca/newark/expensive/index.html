In comparison to nearby areas, Newark is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Newark is valued at $742,100. It is about $117,200 (13%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $524,600 (241%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $355,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Newark is $2,277 a month. It is roughly $328 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,215 (114%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $382 (20%) higher.
