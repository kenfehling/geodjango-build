Compared to other places in this area, Newark is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Newark costs $742,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $277,800 (27%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $524,600 (241%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $355,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Newark the median rent is $2,277 a month. It is about $181 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $1,215 (114%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $382 (20%) higher.
