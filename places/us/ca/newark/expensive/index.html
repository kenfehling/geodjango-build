In comparison to nearby areas, Newark is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Newark's median home value is $742,100. It is roughly $277,800 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $524,600 (241%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $355,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

Newark's median rent is $2,277 a month. It is approximately $181 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $1,215 (114%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $382 (20%) higher.
