Compared to its surrounding areas, Newark is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Newark is $742,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $117,200 (13%) lower. It is roughly $524,600 (241%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $355,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Newark's median monthly rent is $2,277. This is approximately $328 (16%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $1,215 (114%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $382 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
