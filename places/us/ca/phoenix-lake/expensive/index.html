Home prices

Phoenix Lake's median home cost is $346,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $71,480 (26%) higher.It is roughly $128,600 (59%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento is roughly $9,200 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Phoenix Lake is $1,445 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $263 (22%) higher.It is roughly $383 (36%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $182 (14%) higher.
