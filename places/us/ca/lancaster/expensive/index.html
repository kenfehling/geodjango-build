In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lancaster is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Lancaster's median home value is $261,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $60,700 (18%) lower.It is roughly $43,900 (20%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $375,500 (58%) lower.

Rents

Lancaster's median monthly rent is $1,215. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $17 (1%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $153 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $235 (16%) lower.
