In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lancaster is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $261,400. It is approximately $60,700 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $43,900 (20%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $375,500 (58%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $1,215. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $17 (1%) higher. It is about $153 (14%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $235 (16%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
