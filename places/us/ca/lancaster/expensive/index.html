Compared to nearby areas, Lancaster is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeleshowever, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lancaster costs $261,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $60,700 (18%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $43,900 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $375,500 (58%) lower.

Rents

Lancaster's median rent is $1,215 per month. It is roughly $17 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $153 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $235 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
