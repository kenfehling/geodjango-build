Home prices

The value of the median home in Santa Margarita is $525,300. This is roughly $39,420 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $307,800 (141%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara it is roughly $526,200 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Margarita is $978. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $484 (33%) lower.It is approximately $84 (7%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Santa Barbara it is roughly $808 (45%) lower.
