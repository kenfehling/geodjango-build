Home prices

Rents

Santa Margarita's median monthly rent is $978. It is about $484 (33%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $84 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara it is about $808 (45%) lower.
