Home prices

The median home in Woodland costs $365,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $17,580 (5%) higher.It is approximately $147,500 (67%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $732,800 (66%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Woodland's median monthly rent is $1,200. This is roughly $16 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $138 (12%) higher, and it is roughly $695 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
