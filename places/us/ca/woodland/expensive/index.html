In comparison to other places in this area, Woodland is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Woodland is $365,000. This is approximately $17,580 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $147,500 (67%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $732,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

Woodland's median rent is $1,200 per month. It is about $16 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $138 (12%) higher, and is about $695 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
