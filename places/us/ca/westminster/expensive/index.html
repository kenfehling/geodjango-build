In comparison to its surrounding areas, Westminster is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Westminster costs $603,700. This is approximately $79,580 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $386,200 (177%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $33,200 (5%) lower.

Rents

Westminster's median monthly rent is $1,585. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $108 (6%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $523 (49%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $135 (9%) higher.
