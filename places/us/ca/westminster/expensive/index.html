Compared to its surrounding areas, Westminster is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in Westminster is $603,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $79,580 (15%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $386,200 (177%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $33,200 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Westminster is $1,585 a month. It is roughly $108 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $523 (49%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $135 (9%) higher.
