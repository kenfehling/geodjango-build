Home prices

The median home value in Westminster is $603,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $79,580 (15%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $386,200 (177%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $33,200 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Westminster the median monthly rent is $1,585. Compared to other places in this area it is about $108 (6%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $523 (49%) higher, and is roughly $135 (9%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
