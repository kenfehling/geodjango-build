Compared to its nearby areas, Pittsburg is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pittsburg costs $386,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $105,400 (21%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $168,700 (77%) higher, and is roughly $711,600 (64%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pittsburg is $1,734. This is roughly $143 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $672 (63%) higher, and is roughly $161 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
