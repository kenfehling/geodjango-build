Home prices

The median home cost in Pittsburg is $386,200. This is approximately $105,400 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $168,700 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $711,600 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pittsburg is $1,734. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $143 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $672 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $161 (8%) lower.
