In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pittsburg is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pittsburg is valued at $386,200. This is about $105,400 (21%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $168,700 (77%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $711,600 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Pittsburg is $1,734 a month. It is about $143 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $672 (63%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $161 (8%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
