Compared to other places in this area, Pittsburg is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Pittsburg's median home value is $386,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $105,400 (21%) lower.It is approximately $168,700 (77%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $711,600 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Pittsburg's median monthly rent is $1,734. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $143 (7%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $672 (63%) higher, and it is roughly $161 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
