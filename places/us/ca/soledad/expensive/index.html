Home prices

Soledad's median home price is $370,800. This is approximately $62,660 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $153,300 (70%) higher, and compared to the city of San Jose is roughly $493,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

Soledad's median monthly rent is $1,274. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $88 (6%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $212 (19%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Jose is about $833 (39%) lower.
