In comparison to its surrounding areas, Soledad is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Jose.

Home prices

The median home in Soledad costs $370,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $62,660 (14%) lower.It is about $153,300 (70%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $493,800 (57%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

Soledad's median monthly rent is $1,274. It is approximately $88 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $212 (19%) higher, and is roughly $833 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
