Home prices

The median home cost in Carlsbad is $814,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $202,280 (33%) higher. It is about $597,200 (274%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $177,800 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carlsbad is $2,025. It is approximately $206 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $963 (90%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $575 (39%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
