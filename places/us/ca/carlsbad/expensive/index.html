Home prices

Carlsbad's median home cost is $814,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $202,280 (33%) higher.It is roughly $597,200 (274%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $177,800 (27%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carlsbad is $2,025. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $206 (11%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $963 (90%) higher, and it is about $575 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
