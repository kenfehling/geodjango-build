Home prices

The median home in Carlsbad costs $814,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $202,280 (33%) higher. It is approximately $597,200 (274%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $177,800 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Carlsbad's median monthly rent is $2,025. It is approximately $206 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $963 (90%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $575 (39%) higher.
