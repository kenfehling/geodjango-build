Compared to the surrounding areas, Carlsbad is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Carlsbad costs $814,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $202,280 (33%) higher.It is about $597,200 (274%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $177,800 (27%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Carlsbad the median monthly rent is $2,025. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $206 (11%) higher.It is roughly $963 (90%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $575 (39%) higher.
