Home prices

The median home in Sun Village costs $233,500. It is about $69,560 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $16,000 (7%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $403,400 (63%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Sun Village is $1,246 a month. It is about $84 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $184 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $204 (14%) lower.
