In comparison to its surrounding areas, Santa Fe Springs is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Fe Springs is valued at $447,400. This is approximately $51,560 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $229,900 (105%) higher, and it is roughly $189,500 (29%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Fe Springs is $1,382 a month. It is approximately $131 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $320 (30%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $68 (4%) lower.
