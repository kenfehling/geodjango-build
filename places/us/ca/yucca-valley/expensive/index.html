Compared to the surrounding areas, Yucca Valley is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Diego, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Yucca Valley is valued at $172,600. It is about $32,960 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower, and it is about $430,000 (71%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

Yucca Valley's median rent is $919 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $25 (2%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $143 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is about $776 (45%) lower.
