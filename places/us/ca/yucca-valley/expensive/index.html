Compared to nearby areas, Yucca Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Diego, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Yucca Valley costs $172,600. This is roughly $32,960 (23%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $44,900 (20%) lower, and is approximately $430,000 (71%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

In Yucca Valley the median rent is $919 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $25 (2%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $143 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of San Diego is about $776 (45%) lower.
