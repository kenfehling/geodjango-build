Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Los Alamos is $1,669. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $40 (2%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $607 (57%) higher, and it is about $565 (51%) higher in comparison to the city of Bakersfield.
