Home prices

The median home value in Blacklake is $788,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $190,460 (31%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $571,200 (262%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara is about $262,800 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Blacklake is $2,388. It is about $608 (34%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $1,326 (124%) higher, and it is about $602 (33%) higher compared to the city of Santa Barbara.
