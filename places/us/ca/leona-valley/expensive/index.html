Rents

Leona Valley's median monthly rent is $834. It is approximately $491 (37%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $228 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $616 (42%) lower.
