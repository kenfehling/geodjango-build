Home prices

The median home value in Sand City is $513,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $309,040 (37%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $296,400 (136%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $583,900 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Sand City the median monthly rent is $1,692. It is approximately $425 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $630 (59%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $203 (10%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
