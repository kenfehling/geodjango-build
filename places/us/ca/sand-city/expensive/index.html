Home prices

The median home in Sand City is valued at $513,900. It is approximately $309,040 (37%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $296,400 (136%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $583,900 (53%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Sand City's median monthly rent is $1,692. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $425 (20%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $630 (59%) higher, and it is roughly $203 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
