Compared to other places in this area, Sonoma is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sonoma is valued at $785,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $140,920 (21%) higher.It is roughly $568,400 (261%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $311,900 (28%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Sonoma the median rent is $1,836 a month. It is roughly $43 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $774 (72%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $59 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
