In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sonoma is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sonoma is valued at $785,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $140,920 (21%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $568,400 (261%) higher, and it is approximately $311,900 (28%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Sonoma's median rent is $1,836 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $43 (2%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $774 (72%) higher, and is about $59 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
