Compared to nearby areas, Sonoma is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sonoma costs $785,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $140,920 (21%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $568,400 (261%) higher, and it is about $311,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sonoma is $1,836. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $43 (2%) higher. It is roughly $774 (72%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $59 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
