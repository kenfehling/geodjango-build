In comparison to its nearby areas, Sonoma is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Sonoma costs $785,900. This is roughly $140,920 (21%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $568,400 (261%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $311,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Sonoma the median monthly rent is $1,836. This is about $43 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $774 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $59 (3%) lower.
