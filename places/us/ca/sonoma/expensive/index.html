In comparison to other places in this area, Sonoma is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Sonoma is valued at $785,900. It is about $140,920 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $568,400 (261%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $311,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Sonoma's median monthly rent is $1,836. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $43 (2%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $774 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $59 (3%) lower.
