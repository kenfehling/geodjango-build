Compared to other places in this area, Napa is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Napa's median home cost is $616,700. It is roughly $87,700 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $399,200 (183%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $481,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Napa is $1,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $314 (15%) lower.It is about $638 (60%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $195 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
