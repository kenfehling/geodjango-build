Compared to other places in this area, Napa is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Napa costs $616,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $87,700 (12%) lower. It is about $399,200 (183%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $481,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Napa the median rent is $1,700 a month. This is roughly $314 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $638 (60%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $195 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
