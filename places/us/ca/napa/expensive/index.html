Compared to its surrounding areas, Napa is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Napa is valued at $616,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $87,700 (12%) lower. It is approximately $399,200 (183%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $481,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

Napa's median monthly rent is $1,700. It is approximately $314 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $638 (60%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $195 (10%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
