In comparison to other places in this area, Napa is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Napa is $616,700. This is roughly $87,700 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $399,200 (183%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $481,100 (43%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Napa's median rent is $1,700 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $314 (15%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $638 (60%) higher, and it is about $195 (10%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
