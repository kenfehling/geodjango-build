Rents

The median monthly rent in Redwood Valley is $1,348. This is approximately $172 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $286 (26%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Santa Rosa is roughly $261 (16%) lower.
