Compared to nearby areas, San Fernando is substantially less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Fernando is $431,100. It is roughly $259,040 (37%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $213,600 (98%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $205,800 (32%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in San Fernando is $1,342. It is approximately $424 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $280 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $108 (7%) lower.
