Home prices

The median home value in Delhi is $239,700. This is roughly $17,240 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $22,200 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $624,900 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.

Rents

The median rent in Delhi is $1,180 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $83 (7%) higher. It is about $118 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $927 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
