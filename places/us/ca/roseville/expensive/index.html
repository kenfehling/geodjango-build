Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roseville is $1,604. This is about $138 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $542 (51%) higher, and is approximately $159 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Oakland.
