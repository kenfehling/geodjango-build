Compared to its nearby areas, Roseville is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Oakland however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Roseville is $444,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $81,960 (22%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $226,900 (104%) higher, and it is roughly $243,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Oakland.

Rents

In Roseville the median rent is $1,604 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $138 (9%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $542 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Oakland is approximately $159 (11%) higher.
