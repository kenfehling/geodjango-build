In comparison to nearby areas, Roseville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Sacramento, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Roseville costs $444,400. It is roughly $81,960 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $226,900 (104%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $107,500 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

In Roseville the median rent is $1,604 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $138 (9%) higher. It is about $542 (51%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $341 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
