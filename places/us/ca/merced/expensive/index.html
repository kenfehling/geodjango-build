Home prices

The median home in Merced is valued at $237,500. It is roughly $2,660 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $20,000 (9%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Jose it is about $627,100 (72%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Merced is $1,005. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $79 (8%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $57 (5%) lower, and is approximately $1,102 (52%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.
