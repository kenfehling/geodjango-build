Compared to other places in this area, Vineyard is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Vineyard is $384,100. It is roughly $75,600 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $166,600 (76%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $713,700 (65%) lower.

Rents

Vineyard's median monthly rent is $1,580. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $203 (14%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $518 (48%) higher, and it is about $315 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
