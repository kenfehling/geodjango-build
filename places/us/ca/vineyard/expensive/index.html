In comparison to its surrounding areas, Vineyard is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Vineyard is valued at $384,100. It is roughly $75,600 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $166,600 (76%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $713,700 (65%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Vineyard is $1,580 per month. It is about $203 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $518 (48%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $315 (16%) lower.
