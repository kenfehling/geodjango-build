In comparison to other places in this area, Vineyard is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Vineyard costs $384,100. It is approximately $75,600 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $166,600 (76%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $713,700 (65%) lower.

Rents

Vineyard's median monthly rent is $1,580. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $203 (14%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $518 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $315 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
