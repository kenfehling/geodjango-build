In comparison to its surrounding areas, Vineyard is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Vineyard's median home value is $384,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $75,600 (24%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $166,600 (76%) higher, and it is approximately $713,700 (65%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Vineyard is $1,580. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $203 (14%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $518 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $315 (16%) lower.
