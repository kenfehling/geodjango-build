Compared to the surrounding areas, Vineyard is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Vineyard is $384,100. This is approximately $75,600 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $166,600 (76%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $713,700 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Vineyard the median rent is $1,580 a month. This is roughly $203 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $518 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $315 (16%) lower.
