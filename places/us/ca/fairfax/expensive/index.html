In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fairfax is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairfax is $849,800. This is about $329,680 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $632,300 (290%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $248,000 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Fairfax the median monthly rent is $1,887. Compared to other places in this area it is about $117 (5%) lower. It is approximately $825 (77%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
