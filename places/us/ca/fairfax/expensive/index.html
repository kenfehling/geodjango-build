In comparison to other places in this area, Fairfax is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Fairfax costs $849,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $329,680 (27%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $632,300 (290%) higher, and is approximately $248,000 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Fairfax is $1,887 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $117 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $825 (77%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is is very similar.
