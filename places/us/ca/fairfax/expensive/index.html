Compared to nearby areas, Fairfax is a lot less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Fairfax costs $849,800. This is roughly $534,300 (38%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $632,300 (290%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $248,000 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $1,887. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $128 (6%) lower.It is approximately $825 (77%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
