Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairfax is $849,800. It is about $329,680 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $632,300 (290%) higher, and it is roughly $248,000 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Fairfax's median monthly rent is $1,887. It is approximately $117 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $825 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is is very similar.
