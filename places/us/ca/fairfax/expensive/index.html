Compared to its surrounding areas, Fairfax is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairfax is $849,800. This is roughly $329,680 (27%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $632,300 (290%) higher, and is about $248,000 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Fairfax is $1,887 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $117 (5%) lower. It is roughly $825 (77%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is is very similar.
