In comparison to its nearby areas, Foster City is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Foster City costs $1,245,800. It is about $207,480 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $1,028,300 (472%) higher, and is about $148,000 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Foster City is $3,209 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $814 (34%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $2,147 (202%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $1,314 (69%) higher.
