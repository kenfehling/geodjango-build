In comparison to its surrounding areas, Foster City is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of San Francisco, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Foster City is valued at $1,245,800. This is roughly $207,480 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,028,300 (472%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $148,000 (13%) higher.

Rents

Foster City's median monthly rent is $3,209. This is roughly $814 (34%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $2,147 (202%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $1,314 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
