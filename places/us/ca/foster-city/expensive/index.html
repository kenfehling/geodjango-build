Compared to its surrounding areas, Foster City is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Foster City is valued at $1,245,800. It is roughly $207,480 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $1,028,300 (472%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $148,000 (13%) higher.

Rents

Foster City's median monthly rent is $3,209. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $814 (34%) higher. It is approximately $2,147 (202%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $1,314 (69%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
