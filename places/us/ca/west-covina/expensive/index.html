Compared to its surrounding areas, West Covina is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Covina costs $540,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $88,620 (19%) higher.It is roughly $323,000 (148%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $96,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in West Covina is $1,674 per month. This is roughly $279 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $612 (57%) higher, and it is about $224 (15%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
