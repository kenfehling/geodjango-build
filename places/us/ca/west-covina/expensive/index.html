Compared to nearby areas, West Covina is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in West Covina is valued at $540,500. This is about $88,620 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $323,000 (148%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $96,400 (15%) lower.

Rents

West Covina's median monthly rent is $1,674. This is about $279 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $612 (57%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $224 (15%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
