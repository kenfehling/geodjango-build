Compared to the surrounding areas, Three Rivers is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Fresno, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Three Rivers costs $295,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $125,080 (73%) higher. It is about $77,900 (35%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $53,400 (22%) higher compared to the city of Fresno.

Rents

Three Rivers's median monthly rent is $1,305. It is roughly $463 (54%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $243 (22%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fresno it is roughly $300 (29%) higher.
