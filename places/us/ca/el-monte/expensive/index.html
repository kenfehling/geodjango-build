Home prices

The median home in El Monte costs $454,900. This is roughly $111,020 (19%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $237,400 (109%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $182,000 (28%) lower.

Rents

El Monte's median monthly rent is $1,282. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $242 (15%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $220 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $168 (11%) lower.
