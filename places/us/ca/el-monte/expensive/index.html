Home prices

The price of the median home in El Monte is $454,900. It is approximately $111,020 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $237,400 (109%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $182,000 (28%) lower.

Rents

In El Monte the median monthly rent is $1,282. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $242 (15%) lower. It is about $220 (20%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $168 (11%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
