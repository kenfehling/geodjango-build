Compared to its nearby areas, El Monte is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The price of the median home in El Monte is $454,900. This is approximately $111,020 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $237,400 (109%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $182,000 (28%) lower.

Rents

In El Monte the median monthly rent is $1,282. This is approximately $242 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $220 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $168 (11%) lower.
