Home prices

The median home price in El Monte is $454,900. It is approximately $111,020 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $237,400 (109%) higher, and it is approximately $182,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

El Monte's median rent is $1,282 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $242 (15%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $220 (20%) higher, and is about $168 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
