Home prices

The median home cost in El Monte is $454,900. It is roughly $111,020 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $237,400 (109%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $182,000 (28%) lower.

Rents

In El Monte the median rent is $1,282 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $242 (15%) lower. It is about $220 (20%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $168 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
