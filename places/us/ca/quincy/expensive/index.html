Compared to nearby areas, Quincy is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Reno, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Quincy is valued at $274,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $46,160 (20%) higher. It is roughly $57,000 (26%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reno is about $60,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

Quincy's median rent is $941 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $42 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $121 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Reno it is roughly $88 (8%) lower.
