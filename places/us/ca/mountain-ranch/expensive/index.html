Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mountain Ranch is $286,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $9,640 (3%) lower. It is approximately $68,700 (31%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is about $50,700 (15%) lower.

Rents

Mountain Ranch's median monthly rent is $791. It is roughly $759 (48%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $271 (25%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $472 (37%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
