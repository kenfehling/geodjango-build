Home prices

Rents

Morgan Hill's median rent is $1,763 a month. This is about $156 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $701 (66%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $132 (6%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
