Home prices

The median home cost in Morgan Hill is $855,100. It is roughly $45,360 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $637,600 (293%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $242,700 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morgan Hill is $1,763. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $156 (8%) lower.It is roughly $701 (66%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $132 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
