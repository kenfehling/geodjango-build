Home prices

The median home in Seaside costs $488,400. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $170,940 (25%) lower.It is roughly $270,900 (124%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $609,400 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Seaside is $1,781 per month. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $719 (67%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $114 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
