Compared to the surrounding areas, Anaheim is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Anaheim costs $575,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $220,200 (27%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $358,100 (164%) higher, and it is approximately $61,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Anaheim the median monthly rent is $1,651. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $518 (23%) lower.It is roughly $589 (55%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $201 (13%) higher.
