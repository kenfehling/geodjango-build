In comparison to other places in this area, Anaheim is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Anaheim is $575,600. This is approximately $168,600 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $358,100 (164%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $61,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

Anaheim's median rent is $1,651 per month. This is approximately $225 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $589 (55%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $201 (13%) higher.
