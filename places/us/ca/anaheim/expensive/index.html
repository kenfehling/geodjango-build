In comparison to its surrounding areas, Anaheim is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

Anaheim's median home price is $575,600. This is roughly $220,200 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $358,100 (164%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $61,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

Anaheim's median monthly rent is $1,651. It is roughly $518 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $589 (55%) higher, and is roughly $201 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
