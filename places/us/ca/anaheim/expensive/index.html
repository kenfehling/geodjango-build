Compared to other places in this area, Anaheim is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Anaheim is $575,600. This is about $168,600 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $358,100 (164%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $61,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Anaheim is $1,651. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $225 (11%) lower. It is roughly $589 (55%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $201 (13%) higher.
