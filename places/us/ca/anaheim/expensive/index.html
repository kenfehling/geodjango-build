Home prices

The value of the median home in Anaheim is $575,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $168,600 (22%) lower. It is roughly $358,100 (164%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $61,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

In Anaheim the median rent is $1,651 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $225 (11%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $589 (55%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $201 (13%) higher.
