Compared to other places in this area, Yucaipa is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Yucaipa is valued at $327,400. This is approximately $22,440 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $109,900 (50%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $309,500 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Yucaipa is $1,180 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $64 (5%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $118 (11%) higher, and is approximately $270 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
