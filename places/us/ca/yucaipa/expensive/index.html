Home prices

Yucaipa's median home value is $327,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $22,440 (7%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $109,900 (50%) higher, and it is about $309,500 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Yucaipa's median rent is $1,180 per month. It is about $64 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $118 (11%) higher, and is about $270 (18%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
