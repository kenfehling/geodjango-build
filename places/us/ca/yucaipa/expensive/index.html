Home prices

The cost of the median home in Yucaipa is $327,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $22,440 (7%) higher. It is roughly $109,900 (50%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $309,500 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Yucaipa is $1,180. This is approximately $64 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $118 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $270 (18%) lower.
