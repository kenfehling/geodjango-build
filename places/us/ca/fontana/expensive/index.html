Home prices

The median home in Fontana is valued at $371,700. It is approximately $32,980 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $154,200 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $265,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fontana is $1,345 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $42 (3%) lower.It is roughly $283 (26%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $105 (7%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
