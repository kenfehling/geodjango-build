Compared to other places in this area, Fontana is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Fontana is $371,700. This is about $32,980 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $154,200 (70%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $265,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Fontana the median monthly rent is $1,345. This is approximately $42 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $283 (26%) higher, and is about $105 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
