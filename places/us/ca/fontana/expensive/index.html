Home prices

The median home in Fontana costs $371,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $32,980 (9%) higher. It is about $154,200 (70%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $265,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fontana is $1,345. This is about $42 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $283 (26%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $105 (7%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
