Compared to its surrounding areas, San Clemente is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in San Clemente is valued at $906,100. This is roughly $102,600 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $688,600 (316%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $269,200 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

San Clemente's median monthly rent is $1,880. It is approximately $289 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $818 (77%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $430 (29%) higher.
