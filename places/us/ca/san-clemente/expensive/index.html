Compared to its nearby areas, San Clemente is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Clemente costs $906,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $97,360 (9%) lower.It is approximately $688,600 (316%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $269,200 (42%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in San Clemente is $1,880 per month. This is about $313 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $818 (77%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $430 (29%) higher.
