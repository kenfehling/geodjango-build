Home prices

Rents

Marysville's median monthly rent is $933. This is roughly $71 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $129 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is approximately $330 (26%) lower.
