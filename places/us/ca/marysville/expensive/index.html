Home prices

The median home in Marysville is valued at $207,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $16,780 (7%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $10,300 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $129,700 (38%) lower.

Rents

Marysville's median monthly rent is $933. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $71 (7%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $129 (12%) lower, and it is approximately $330 (26%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
