Home prices

Rents

In Davenport the median rent is $1,563 a month. It is roughly $171 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $501 (47%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $332 (17%) lower.
