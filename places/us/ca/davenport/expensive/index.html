Home prices

The cost of the median home in Davenport is $852,700. It is approximately $143,340 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $635,200 (292%) higher, and it is about $245,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Davenport the median rent is $1,563 a month. This is approximately $171 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $501 (47%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $332 (17%) lower.
