Home prices

The median home in Davenport is valued at $852,700. This is approximately $143,340 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $635,200 (292%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $245,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Davenport is $1,563. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $171 (12%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $501 (47%) higher, and it is about $332 (17%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
