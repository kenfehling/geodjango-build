Compared to other places in this area, Fountain Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fountain Valley is $734,800. It is roughly $97,020 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $517,300 (237%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $97,900 (15%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Fountain Valley's median rent is $2,034 a month. This is roughly $380 (23%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $972 (91%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $584 (40%) higher.
