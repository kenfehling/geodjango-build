Compared to the surrounding areas, Fullerton is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Fullerton is valued at $650,000. It is approximately $69,460 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $432,500 (198%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $13,100 (2%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Fullerton's median rent is $1,669 per month. This is about $31 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $607 (57%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $219 (15%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
