Home prices

The median home cost in Fullerton is $650,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $69,460 (11%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $432,500 (198%) higher, and it is roughly $13,100 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Fullerton the median monthly rent is $1,669. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $31 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $607 (57%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $219 (15%) higher.
