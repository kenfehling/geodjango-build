In comparison to its surrounding areas, Fullerton is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeleshowever, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fullerton is $650,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $69,460 (11%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $432,500 (198%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $13,100 (2%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fullerton is $1,669. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $31 (1%) higher. It is roughly $607 (57%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $219 (15%) higher.
