In comparison to nearby areas, Fullerton is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Fullerton is valued at $650,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $69,460 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $432,500 (198%) higher, and is about $13,100 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Fullerton's median rent is $1,669 per month. This is about $31 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $607 (57%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $219 (15%) higher.
