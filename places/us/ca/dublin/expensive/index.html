Home prices

Dublin's median home cost is $882,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $190,320 (17%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $664,700 (305%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $215,600 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dublin is $2,681. It is about $236 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,619 (152%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $786 (41%) higher.
