In comparison to other places in this area, Dublin is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Dublin is $882,200. It is roughly $190,320 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $664,700 (305%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $215,600 (19%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Dublin's median rent is $2,681 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $236 (9%) higher.It is roughly $1,619 (152%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $786 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
