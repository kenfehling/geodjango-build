In comparison to its nearby areas, Dublin is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Dublin's median home value is $882,200. This is about $138,580 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $664,700 (305%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $215,600 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dublin is $2,681. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $438 (19%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $1,619 (152%) higher, and is roughly $786 (41%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
