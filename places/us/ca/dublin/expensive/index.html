In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dublin is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Dublin is $882,200. It is roughly $190,320 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $664,700 (305%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $215,600 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dublin is $2,681. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $236 (9%) higher.It is approximately $1,619 (152%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $786 (41%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
