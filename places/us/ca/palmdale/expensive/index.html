Compared to the surrounding areas, Palmdale is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palmdale is valued at $280,000. It is about $14,820 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $62,500 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $356,900 (56%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palmdale is $1,319. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $60 (4%) higher.It is approximately $257 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $131 (9%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
