Home prices

Palmdale's median home cost is $280,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $14,820 (5%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $62,500 (28%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $356,900 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palmdale is $1,319. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $60 (4%) higher.It is roughly $257 (24%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $131 (9%) lower.
