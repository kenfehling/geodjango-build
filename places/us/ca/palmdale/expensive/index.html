In comparison to other places in this area, Palmdale is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeleshowever, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palmdale is valued at $280,000. This is approximately $14,820 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $62,500 (28%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $356,900 (56%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palmdale is $1,319. It is about $60 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $257 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $131 (9%) lower.
