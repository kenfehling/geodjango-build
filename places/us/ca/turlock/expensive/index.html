Home prices

The value of the median home in Turlock is $310,100. It is roughly $36,000 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $92,600 (42%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $554,500 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Turlock is $1,123. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $147 (11%) lower.It is about $61 (5%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Jose it is approximately $984 (46%) lower.
