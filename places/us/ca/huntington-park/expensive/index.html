Home prices

Huntington Park's median home cost is $412,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $14,920 (3%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $195,100 (89%) higher, and is about $224,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Huntington Park is $1,081 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $104 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $19 (1%) higher, and it is about $369 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
