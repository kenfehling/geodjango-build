Home prices

The median home in Huntington Park is valued at $412,600. It is about $14,920 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $195,100 (89%) higher, and it is approximately $224,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Huntington Park is $1,081 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $104 (8%) lower.It is roughly $19 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $369 (25%) lower.
