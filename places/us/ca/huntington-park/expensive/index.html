Home prices

The price of the median home in Huntington Park is $412,600. It is roughly $14,920 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $195,100 (89%) higher, and it is approximately $224,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Huntington Park is $1,081 a month. It is approximately $104 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $19 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $369 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
