Compared to the surrounding areas, East Palo Alto is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Palo Alto is $821,200. This is about $488,720 (37%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $603,700 (277%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $276,600 (25%) lower.

Rents

East Palo Alto's median monthly rent is $1,766. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $637 (26%) lower. It is approximately $704 (66%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $129 (6%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
