Similar to other places in this area, it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in Gardena is $474,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $21,340 (4%) lower. It is roughly $257,200 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $162,200 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Gardena the median monthly rent is $1,309. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $33 (2%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $247 (23%) higher, and is approximately $141 (9%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
