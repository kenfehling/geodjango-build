Home prices

The price of the median home in Gardena is $474,700. It is about $21,340 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $257,200 (118%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $162,200 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Gardena is $1,309 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $33 (2%) higher.It is approximately $247 (23%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $141 (9%) lower.
