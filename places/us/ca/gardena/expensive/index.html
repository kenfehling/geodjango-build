Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Gardena is $1,309 per month. It is approximately $33 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $247 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $141 (9%) lower.
