Compared to the surrounding areas, Hollister is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Jose however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hollister is valued at $476,800. It is approximately $161,020 (25%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $259,300 (119%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $387,800 (44%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

Hollister's median rent is $1,517 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $250 (14%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $455 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of San Jose is roughly $590 (28%) lower.
