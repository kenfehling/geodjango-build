Home prices

The cost of the median home in Corning is $167,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $3,840 (2%) lower.It is about $49,800 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chico it is about $160,000 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corning is $832. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $75 (8%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $230 (21%) lower, and is roughly $268 (24%) lower compared to the city of Chico.
