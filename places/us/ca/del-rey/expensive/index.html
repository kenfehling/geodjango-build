Home prices

The median home in Del Rey is valued at $181,900. It is approximately $19,800 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $35,600 (16%) lower, and is roughly $60,100 (24%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.

Rents

In Del Rey the median monthly rent is $659. It is approximately $272 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $403 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is about $346 (34%) lower.
