Home prices

The median home cost in Janesville is $286,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $115,100 (67%) higher. It is roughly $69,000 (31%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $48,500 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Reno.

Rents

Janesville's median monthly rent is $1,254. This is about $358 (39%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $192 (18%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Reno it is about $225 (21%) higher.
