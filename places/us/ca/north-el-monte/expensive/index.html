Home prices

The median home in North El Monte costs $633,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $35,040 (5%) lower. It is roughly $415,900 (191%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in North El Monte is $1,828 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $337 (22%) higher. It is about $766 (72%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $378 (26%) higher.
