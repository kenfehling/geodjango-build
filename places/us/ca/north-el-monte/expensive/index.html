Home prices

The median home in North El Monte costs $633,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $35,040 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $415,900 (191%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North El Monte is $1,828. This is roughly $337 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $766 (72%) higher, and it is approximately $378 (26%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
