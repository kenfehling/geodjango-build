Home prices

North El Monte's median home cost is $633,400. This is about $35,040 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $415,900 (191%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in North El Monte is $1,828 per month. It is about $337 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $766 (72%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $378 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
