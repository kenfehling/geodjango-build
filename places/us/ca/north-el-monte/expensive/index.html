Compared to other places in this area, North El Monte is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in North El Monte costs $633,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $35,040 (5%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $415,900 (191%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in North El Monte is $1,828 per month. It is approximately $337 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $766 (72%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $378 (26%) higher.
