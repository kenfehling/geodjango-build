Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Antonio Heights is $750,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $223,140 (42%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $533,000 (245%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $113,600 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in San Antonio Heights is $1,438 a month. It is about $103 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $376 (35%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is is very similar.
