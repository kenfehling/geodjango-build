Compared to its surrounding areas, Oroville is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Sacramento however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Oroville's median home cost is $183,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $12,400 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $33,900 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $153,300 (45%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median rent in Oroville is $848 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $176 (17%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Sacramento is approximately $415 (32%) lower.
