Home prices

The median home in Oroville costs $183,600. It is about $12,400 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $33,900 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is about $153,300 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Oroville the median monthly rent is $848. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $176 (17%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $214 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $415 (32%) lower.
