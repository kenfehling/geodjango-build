Home prices

The median home in Pacifica costs $882,800. This is about $105,380 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $665,300 (305%) higher, and it is about $215,000 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Pacifica's median monthly rent is $2,414. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $230 (10%) higher.It is approximately $1,352 (127%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $519 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
