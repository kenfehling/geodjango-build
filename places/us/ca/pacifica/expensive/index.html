Home prices

The median home cost in Pacifica is $882,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $105,380 (10%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $665,300 (305%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $215,000 (19%) lower.

Rents
