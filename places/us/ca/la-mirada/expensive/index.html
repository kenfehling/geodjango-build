Home prices

The value of the median home in La Mirada is $569,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $44,520 (8%) higher. It is roughly $352,000 (161%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $67,400 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in La Mirada is $1,675. It is roughly $147 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $613 (57%) higher, and it is about $225 (15%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
