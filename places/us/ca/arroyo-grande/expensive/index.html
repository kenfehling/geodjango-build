In comparison to other places in this area, Arroyo Grande is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Santa Barbara.

Home prices

The median home value in Arroyo Grande is $591,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $33,400 (5%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $374,300 (172%) higher, and it is about $459,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara.

Rents

Arroyo Grande's median monthly rent is $1,459. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $211 (12%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $397 (37%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara it is roughly $327 (18%) lower.
