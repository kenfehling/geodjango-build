Compared to the surrounding areas, Arroyo Grande is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Santa Barbara.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Arroyo Grande is $591,800. It is roughly $33,400 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $374,300 (172%) higher, and it is about $459,700 (43%) lower compared to the city of Santa Barbara.

Rents

Arroyo Grande's median monthly rent is $1,459. This is approximately $211 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $397 (37%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara is about $327 (18%) lower.
