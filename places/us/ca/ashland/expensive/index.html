Home prices

Ashland's median home value is $498,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $111,600 (18%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $281,000 (129%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $599,300 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ashland is $1,598. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $195 (10%) lower.It is roughly $536 (50%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $297 (15%) lower.
