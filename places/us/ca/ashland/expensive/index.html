In comparison to its nearby areas, Ashland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $498,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $111,600 (18%) lower. It is roughly $281,000 (129%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $599,300 (54%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Ashland's median monthly rent is $1,598. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $195 (10%) lower. It is roughly $536 (50%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $297 (15%) lower.
