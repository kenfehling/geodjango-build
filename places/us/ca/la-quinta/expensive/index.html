Home prices

The median home in La Quinta is valued at $398,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $14,140 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $180,700 (83%) higher, and is approximately $204,400 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

La Quinta's median rent is $1,449 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $370 (34%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $387 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of San Diego is about $246 (14%) lower.
