Home prices

Temescal Valley's median home value is $453,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $65,920 (17%) higher.It is about $236,100 (108%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $183,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Temescal Valley is $2,361. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $921 (63%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,299 (122%) higher, and is approximately $911 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
