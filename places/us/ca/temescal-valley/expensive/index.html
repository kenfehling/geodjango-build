Compared to the surrounding areas, Temescal Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Temescal Valley is $453,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $65,920 (17%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $236,100 (108%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $183,300 (28%) lower.

Rents

Temescal Valley's median monthly rent is $2,361. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $921 (63%) higher. It is roughly $1,299 (122%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $911 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
