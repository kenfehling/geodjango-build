Home prices

The median home in Temescal Valley costs $453,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $65,920 (17%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $236,100 (108%) higher, and is approximately $183,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Temescal Valley is $2,361 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $921 (63%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,299 (122%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $911 (62%) higher.
