Compared to the surrounding areas, Bonny Doon is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Bonny Doon is $868,600. This is about $162,420 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $651,100 (299%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $229,200 (20%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bonny Doon is $1,170. It is about $301 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $108 (10%) higher, and is about $725 (38%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
