Compared to other places in this area, Bonny Doon is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Bonny Doon is $868,600. This is roughly $162,420 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $651,100 (299%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $229,200 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bonny Doon is $1,170 per month. This is about $301 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $108 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $725 (38%) lower.
