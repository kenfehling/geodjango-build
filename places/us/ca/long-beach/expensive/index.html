Home prices

The median home in Long Beach is valued at $556,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $13,760 (2%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $338,600 (155%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $80,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Long Beach the median monthly rent is $1,324. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $455 (25%) lower.It is approximately $262 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $126 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
