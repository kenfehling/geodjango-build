Home prices

The price of the median home in Long Beach is $556,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $13,760 (2%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $338,600 (155%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $80,800 (12%) lower.

Rents

Long Beach's median monthly rent is $1,324. This is about $455 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $262 (24%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $126 (8%) lower.
