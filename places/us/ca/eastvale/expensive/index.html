Compared to the surrounding areas, Eastvale is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Eastvale's median home cost is $574,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $138,480 (31%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $357,000 (164%) higher, and it is about $62,400 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Eastvale is $2,623. This is about $1,082 (70%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $1,561 (146%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $1,173 (80%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
