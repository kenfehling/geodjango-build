Compared to nearby areas, Glendale is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Glendale is $769,900. This is about $218,280 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $552,400 (253%) higher, and is roughly $133,000 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Glendale the median monthly rent is $1,622. It is roughly $354 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $560 (52%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $172 (11%) higher.
