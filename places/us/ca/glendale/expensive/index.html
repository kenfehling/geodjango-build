Compared to the surrounding areas, Glendale is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Glendale is $769,900. This is approximately $218,280 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $552,400 (253%) higher, and it is roughly $133,000 (20%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Glendale the median monthly rent is $1,622. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $354 (17%) lower.It is approximately $560 (52%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $172 (11%) higher.
