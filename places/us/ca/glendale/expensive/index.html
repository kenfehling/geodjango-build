In comparison to other places in this area, Glendale is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home cost in Glendale is $769,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $218,280 (22%) lower.It is approximately $552,400 (253%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $133,000 (20%) higher.

Rents

Glendale's median rent is $1,622 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $354 (17%) lower.It is roughly $560 (52%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $172 (11%) higher.
