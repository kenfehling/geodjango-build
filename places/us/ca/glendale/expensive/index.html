Compared to its surrounding areas, Glendale is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Glendale is $769,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $218,280 (22%) lower. It is approximately $552,400 (253%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $133,000 (20%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Glendale is $1,622 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $354 (17%) lower. It is roughly $560 (52%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $172 (11%) higher.
