Home prices

The median home cost in Modesto is $283,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $124,960 (78%) higher.It is about $66,300 (30%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Jose is about $580,800 (67%) lower.

Rents

Modesto's median rent is $1,176 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $188 (19%) higher.It is about $114 (10%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Jose it is approximately $931 (44%) lower.
