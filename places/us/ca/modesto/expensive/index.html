Home prices

The median home in Modesto costs $283,800. This is about $124,960 (78%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $66,300 (30%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $580,800 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.

Rents

In Modesto the median monthly rent is $1,176. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $188 (19%) higher.It is about $114 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of San Jose is roughly $931 (44%) lower.
