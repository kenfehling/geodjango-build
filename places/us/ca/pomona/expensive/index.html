Home prices

The median home in Pomona costs $394,500. This is about $197,180 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $177,000 (81%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $242,400 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Pomona is $1,362 per month. This is approximately $469 (25%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $300 (28%) higher, and it is roughly $88 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
