Home prices

The median home price in Pomona is $394,500. This is roughly $197,180 (33%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $177,000 (81%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $242,400 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pomona is $1,362. This is approximately $469 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $300 (28%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $88 (6%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
