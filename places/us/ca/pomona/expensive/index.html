Home prices

The median home price in Pomona is $394,500. It is approximately $197,180 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $177,000 (81%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $242,400 (38%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pomona is $1,362. It is approximately $469 (25%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $300 (28%) higher, and is about $88 (6%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
