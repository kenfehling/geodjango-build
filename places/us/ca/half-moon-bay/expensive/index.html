Home prices

The median home price in Half Moon Bay is $999,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $274,660 (21%) lower. It is roughly $782,000 (359%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $98,300 (8%) lower.

Rents

In Half Moon Bay the median monthly rent is $1,869. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $242 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $807 (75%) higher, and it is about $26 (1%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
