In comparison to other places in this area, Half Moon Bay is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Half Moon Bay costs $999,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $369,160 (26%) lower.It is roughly $782,000 (359%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $98,300 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Half Moon Bay is $1,869 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $471 (20%) lower.It is roughly $807 (75%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $26 (1%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
