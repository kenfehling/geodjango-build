Compared to its nearby areas, Half Moon Bay is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Half Moon Bay is valued at $999,500. This is roughly $274,660 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $782,000 (359%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $98,300 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Half Moon Bay the median monthly rent is $1,869. It is about $242 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $807 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $26 (1%) lower.
