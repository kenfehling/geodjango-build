Compared to its surrounding areas, Santa Paula is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Paula costs $383,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $128,780 (25%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $166,200 (76%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $253,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

Santa Paula's median rent is $1,277 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $502 (28%) lower.It is approximately $215 (20%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $173 (11%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
