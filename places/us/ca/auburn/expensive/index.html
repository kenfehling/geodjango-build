Compared to its surrounding areas, Auburn is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Sacramento, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Auburn is $447,600. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $23,640 (5%) higher.It is about $230,100 (105%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $110,700 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Auburn's median monthly rent is $1,147. It is approximately $374 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $85 (8%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento is about $116 (9%) lower.
