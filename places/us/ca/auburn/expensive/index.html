Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $447,600. It is about $23,640 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $230,100 (105%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $110,700 (32%) higher.

Rents

In Auburn the median monthly rent is $1,147. This is approximately $374 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $85 (8%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $116 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
