Compared to other places in this area, Auburn is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Sacramento, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $447,600. This is roughly $23,640 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $230,100 (105%) higher, and is approximately $110,700 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $1,147 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $374 (24%) lower. It is approximately $85 (8%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $116 (9%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
