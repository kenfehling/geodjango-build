Compared to its surrounding areas, Auburn is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Sacramento, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $447,600. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $23,640 (5%) higher.It is approximately $230,100 (105%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is roughly $110,700 (32%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $1,147 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $374 (24%) lower.It is about $85 (8%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $116 (9%) lower.
