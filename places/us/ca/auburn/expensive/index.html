Compared to nearby areas, Auburn is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Sacramento, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Auburn is $447,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $23,640 (5%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $230,100 (105%) higher, and it is approximately $110,700 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median rent in Auburn is $1,147 a month. This is roughly $374 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $85 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is approximately $116 (9%) lower.
