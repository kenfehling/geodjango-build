Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairview is $2,005. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $258 (14%) higher.It is approximately $943 (88%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $110 (5%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
