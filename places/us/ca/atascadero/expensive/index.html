Home prices

Atascadero's median home value is $493,200. It is approximately $103,320 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $275,700 (126%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $149,100 (43%) higher compared to the city of Santa Maria.

Rents

In Atascadero the median rent is $1,415 a month. This is about $104 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $353 (33%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Santa Maria.
