Home prices

The median home in Atascadero costs $493,200. This is roughly $103,320 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $275,700 (126%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Santa Maria it is about $149,100 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Atascadero is $1,415 a month. It is roughly $104 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $353 (33%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Santa Maria is is very similar.
