In comparison to other places in this area, Redwood City is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Redwood City is $1,251,000. This is about $214,960 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $1,033,500 (475%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $153,200 (13%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Redwood City is $2,355. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $232 (8%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,293 (121%) higher, and is approximately $460 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
