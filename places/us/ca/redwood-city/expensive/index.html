Compared to other places in this area, Redwood City is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of San Francisco, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Redwood City's median home value is $1,251,000. It is approximately $365,800 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $1,033,500 (475%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $153,200 (13%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Redwood City is $2,355 per month. This is roughly $290 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,293 (121%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $460 (24%) higher.
