Compared to its nearby areas, Redwood City is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Redwood City costs $1,251,000. This is about $214,960 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $1,033,500 (475%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $153,200 (13%) higher.

Rents

In Redwood City the median rent is $2,355 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $232 (8%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,293 (121%) higher, and is about $460 (24%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
