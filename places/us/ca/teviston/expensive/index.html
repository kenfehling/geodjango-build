Home prices

The median home cost in Teviston is $158,900. This is roughly $43,000 (37%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $58,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is about $83,100 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Teviston is $891 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $66 (7%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $171 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Fresno is about $114 (11%) lower.
