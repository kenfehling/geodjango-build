Home prices

The median home in Mendota costs $158,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $22,380 (12%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $58,800 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Fresno is about $83,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

Mendota's median rent is $784 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $51 (6%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $278 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is about $221 (21%) lower.
