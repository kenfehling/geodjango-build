Home prices

The median home in Mendota is valued at $158,700. It is about $22,380 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $58,800 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Fresno it is roughly $83,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Mendota the median rent is $784 a month. This is approximately $51 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $278 (26%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $221 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.
