Home prices

The median home price in Tulare is $207,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21,820 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $10,500 (4%) lower, and it is about $35,000 (14%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tulare is $1,055. Compared to nearby areas this is about $82 (8%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and is roughly $50 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Fresno.
