Compared to its surrounding areas, Moraga is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home value in Moraga is $1,140,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $192,860 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $923,200 (424%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $42,900 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Moraga the median monthly rent is $2,128. It is roughly $341 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $1,066 (100%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $233 (12%) higher.
