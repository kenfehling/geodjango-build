Home prices

The median home in Moraga costs $1,140,700. This is approximately $192,860 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $923,200 (424%) higher, and it is about $42,900 (3%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Moraga is $2,128. It is approximately $341 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $1,066 (100%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $233 (12%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
