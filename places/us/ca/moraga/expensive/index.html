Compared to other places in this area, Moraga is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Moraga costs $1,140,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $192,860 (14%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $923,200 (424%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $42,900 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Moraga is $2,128 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $341 (13%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,066 (100%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $233 (12%) higher.
