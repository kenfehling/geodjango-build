Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Parksdale is $882 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $189 (17%) lower. It is approximately $180 (16%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $123 (12%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.
