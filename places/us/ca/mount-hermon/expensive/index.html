Home prices

Rents

In Mount Hermon the median rent is $1,200 a month. This is roughly $432 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $138 (12%) higher, and it is roughly $695 (36%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
