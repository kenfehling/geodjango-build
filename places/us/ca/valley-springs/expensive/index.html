Compared to the surrounding areas, Valley Springs is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Jose, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Valley Springs is $296,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $69,280 (30%) higher. It is about $79,100 (36%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $568,000 (65%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

The median rent in Valley Springs is $1,632 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $286 (21%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $570 (53%) higher, and is roughly $475 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.
