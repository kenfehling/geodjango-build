In comparison to the surrounding areas, Valley Springs is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Jose.

Home prices

The median home price in Valley Springs is $296,600. It is about $69,280 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $79,100 (36%) higher, and it is roughly $568,000 (65%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Valley Springs is $1,632. This is about $286 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $570 (53%) higher, and is roughly $475 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
