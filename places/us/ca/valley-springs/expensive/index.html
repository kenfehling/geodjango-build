Home prices

The median home in Valley Springs is valued at $296,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $69,280 (30%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $79,100 (36%) higher, and is about $568,000 (65%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

In Valley Springs the median rent is $1,632 per month. It is approximately $286 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $570 (53%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Jose is about $475 (22%) lower.
