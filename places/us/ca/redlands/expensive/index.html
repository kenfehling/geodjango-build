Compared to nearby areas, Redlands is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Redlands is valued at $390,700. This is roughly $58,760 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $173,200 (79%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $246,200 (38%) lower.

Rents

Redlands's median rent is $1,326 per month. This is about $111 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $264 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $124 (8%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
