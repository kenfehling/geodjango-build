Compared to the surrounding areas, Redlands is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Redlands is valued at $390,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $58,760 (17%) higher.It is roughly $173,200 (79%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $246,200 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Redlands the median monthly rent is $1,326. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $111 (9%) higher.It is about $264 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $124 (8%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
