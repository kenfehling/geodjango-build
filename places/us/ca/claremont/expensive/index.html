Compared to nearby areas, Claremont is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Claremont costs $672,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $113,340 (20%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $455,000 (209%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $35,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Claremont the median rent is $1,561 a month. It is about $38 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $499 (46%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $111 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
