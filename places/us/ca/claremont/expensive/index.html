Compared to the surrounding areas, Claremont is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Claremont is valued at $672,500. This is roughly $113,340 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $455,000 (209%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $35,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Claremont is $1,561 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $38 (2%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $499 (46%) higher, and is approximately $111 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
