Home prices

The median home value in Claremont is $672,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $113,340 (20%) higher.It is approximately $455,000 (209%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $35,600 (5%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Claremont is $1,561 per month. This is about $38 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $499 (46%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $111 (7%) higher.
