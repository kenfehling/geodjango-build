Compared to other places in this area, Claremont is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

Claremont's median home price is $672,500. This is roughly $113,340 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $455,000 (209%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $35,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Claremont the median rent is $1,561 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $38 (2%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $499 (46%) higher, and is about $111 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
