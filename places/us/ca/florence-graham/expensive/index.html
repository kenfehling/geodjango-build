Compared to other places in this area, Florence-Graham is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Florence-Graham is $357,000. This is about $50,760 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $139,500 (64%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $279,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

Florence-Graham's median monthly rent is $1,173. It is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $111 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $277 (19%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
