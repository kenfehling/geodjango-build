Rents

The median rent in Bostonia is $1,493 per month. This is roughly $155 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $431 (40%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is roughly $202 (11%) lower.
