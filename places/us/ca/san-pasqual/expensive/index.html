Home prices

The median home in San Pasqual costs $915,300. It is about $103,280 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $697,800 (320%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $278,400 (43%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Pasqual the median monthly rent is $1,482. This is roughly $464 (23%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $420 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $32 (2%) higher.
