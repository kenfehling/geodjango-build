Compared to other places in this area, San Pasqual is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in San Pasqual is valued at $915,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $77,200 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $697,800 (320%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $278,400 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in San Pasqual is $1,482. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $138 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $420 (39%) higher, and is roughly $32 (2%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
