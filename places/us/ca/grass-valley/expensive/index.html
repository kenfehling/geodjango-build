Home prices

Rents

In Grass Valley the median monthly rent is $1,076. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $383 (26%) lower.It is about $14 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is roughly $187 (14%) lower.
