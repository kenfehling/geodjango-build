Compared to its surrounding areas, Encinitas is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home cost in Encinitas is $947,700. It is about $374,300 (28%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $730,200 (335%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $310,800 (48%) higher.

Rents

Encinitas's median rent is $1,980 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $338 (14%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $918 (86%) higher, and is about $530 (36%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
