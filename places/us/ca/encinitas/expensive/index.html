Home prices

The median home in Encinitas costs $947,700. It is roughly $221,880 (30%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $730,200 (335%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $310,800 (48%) higher.

Rents

Encinitas's median monthly rent is $1,980. It is roughly $56 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $918 (86%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $530 (36%) higher.
