In comparison to its surrounding areas, Encinitas is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home cost in Encinitas is $947,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $221,880 (30%) higher. It is approximately $730,200 (335%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $310,800 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Encinitas is $1,980 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $56 (2%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $918 (86%) higher, and is approximately $530 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
