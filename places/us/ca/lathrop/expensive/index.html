Home prices

The median home in Lathrop costs $358,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $67,360 (23%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $141,400 (65%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $738,900 (67%) lower.

Rents

Lathrop's median rent is $1,715 per month. This is about $443 (34%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $653 (61%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $180 (9%) lower.
