Home prices

The median home in Lathrop is valued at $358,900. This is about $67,360 (23%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $141,400 (65%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $738,900 (67%) lower.

Rents

In Lathrop the median monthly rent is $1,715. It is roughly $443 (34%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $653 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $180 (9%) lower.
