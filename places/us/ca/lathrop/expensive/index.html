Home prices

Rents

Lathrop's median monthly rent is $1,715. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $443 (34%) higher.It is about $653 (61%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $180 (9%) lower.
