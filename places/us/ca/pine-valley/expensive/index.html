Home prices

The price of the median home in Pine Valley is $459,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $18,120 (4%) higher. It is approximately $242,200 (111%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $142,900 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Pine Valley is $1,732 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $310 (21%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $670 (63%) higher, and is roughly $37 (2%) higher compared to the city of San Diego.
