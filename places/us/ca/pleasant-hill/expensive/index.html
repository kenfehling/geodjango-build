Compared to the surrounding areas, Pleasant Hill is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Pleasant Hill is valued at $716,300. This is approximately $45,840 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $498,800 (229%) higher, and is about $381,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Pleasant Hill the median rent is $2,014 a month. It is about $145 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $952 (89%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $119 (6%) higher.
