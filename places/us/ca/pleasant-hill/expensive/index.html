Home prices

The value of the median home in Pleasant Hill is $716,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $45,840 (6%) higher. It is about $498,800 (229%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $381,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Pleasant Hill the median monthly rent is $2,014. This is roughly $145 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $952 (89%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $119 (6%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
