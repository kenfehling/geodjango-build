Home prices

The median home price in Vista is $480,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $132,660 (21%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $263,400 (121%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $156,000 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Vista the median monthly rent is $1,669. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $306 (15%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $607 (57%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $219 (15%) higher.
