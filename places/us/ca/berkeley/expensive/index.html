Similar to its nearby areas, it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Berkeley costs $1,004,900. It is about $19,220 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is about $787,400 (362%) higher, and is roughly $92,900 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Berkeley is $1,722 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $715 (29%) lower.It is approximately $660 (62%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $173 (9%) lower.
