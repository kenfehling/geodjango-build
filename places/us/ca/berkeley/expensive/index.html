Compared to its nearby areas, Berkeley is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Berkeley is $1,004,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $87,900 (9%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $787,400 (362%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $92,900 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Berkeley is $1,722 a month. It is approximately $632 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $660 (62%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $173 (9%) lower.
