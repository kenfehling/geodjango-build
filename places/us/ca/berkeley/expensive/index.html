In comparison to nearby areas, Berkeley is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Franciscohowever, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Berkeley is $1,004,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $87,900 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $787,400 (362%) higher, and it is roughly $92,900 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Berkeley's median rent is $1,722 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $632 (26%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $660 (62%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $173 (9%) lower.
