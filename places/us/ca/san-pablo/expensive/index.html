In comparison to its surrounding areas, San Pablo is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

San Pablo's median home price is $355,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $112,680 (24%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $137,500 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $742,800 (67%) lower.

Rents

In San Pablo the median rent is $1,416 a month. It is approximately $299 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $354 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $479 (25%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
