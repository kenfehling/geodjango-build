Compared to the surrounding areas, San Pablo is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Pablo costs $355,000. It is about $112,680 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $137,500 (63%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $742,800 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In San Pablo the median rent is $1,416 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $299 (17%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $354 (33%) higher, and it is approximately $479 (25%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
