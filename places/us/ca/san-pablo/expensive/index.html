In comparison to its surrounding areas, San Pablo is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

San Pablo's median home price is $355,000. It is roughly $112,680 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $137,500 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $742,800 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in San Pablo is $1,416. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $299 (17%) lower. It is roughly $354 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $479 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
