Compared to other places in this area, San Pablo is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in San Pablo costs $355,000. This is roughly $112,680 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $137,500 (63%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $742,800 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in San Pablo is $1,416 per month. This is approximately $299 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $354 (33%) higher, and is approximately $479 (25%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
