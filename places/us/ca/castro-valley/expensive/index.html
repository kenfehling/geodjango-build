Compared to other places in this area, Castro Valley is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Castro Valley is $741,500. It is approximately $180,000 (32%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $524,000 (240%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $356,300 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Castro Valley the median rent is $1,791 a month. This is approximately $36 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $729 (68%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $104 (5%) lower.
