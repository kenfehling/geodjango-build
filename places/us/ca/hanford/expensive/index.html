Home prices

The median home in Hanford costs $221,300. This is approximately $66,120 (42%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $3,800 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $20,700 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

Hanford's median rent is $992 per month. This is roughly $37 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $70 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is roughly $13 (1%) lower.
