Home prices

The median home in Hanford is valued at $221,300. This is about $66,120 (42%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $3,800 (1%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $20,700 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

Hanford's median rent is $992 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $37 (3%) higher. It is approximately $70 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $13 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.
