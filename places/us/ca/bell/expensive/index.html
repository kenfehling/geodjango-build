Home prices

The median home price in Bell is $413,400. This is approximately $9,480 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $195,900 (90%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $223,500 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bell is $1,192 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $16 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $130 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $258 (17%) lower.
