Compared to the surrounding areas, Monrovia is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Monrovia is $663,400. It is roughly $61,380 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $445,900 (205%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $26,500 (4%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Monrovia's median monthly rent is $1,590. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $55 (3%) higher. It is about $528 (49%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $140 (9%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
