In comparison to its surrounding areas, Monrovia is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Monrovia is $663,400. This is about $61,380 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $445,900 (205%) higher, and it is about $26,500 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monrovia is $1,590. It is about $55 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $528 (49%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $140 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
