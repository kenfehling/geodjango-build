The median home in Citrus is valued at $425,800. It is about $52,100 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $208,300 (95%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $211,100 (33%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Citrus is $1,863. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $246 (15%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $801 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $413 (28%) higher.
