Compared to its nearby areas, Ojai is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ojai costs $713,100. It is about $197,480 (38%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $495,600 (227%) higher, and it is about $76,200 (11%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Ojai the median monthly rent is $1,533. It is is very similar compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $471 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $83 (5%) higher.
