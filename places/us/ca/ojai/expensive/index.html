Home prices

The median home in Ojai is valued at $713,100. It is roughly $197,480 (38%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $495,600 (227%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $76,200 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Ojai is $1,533 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is is very similar.It is approximately $471 (44%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $83 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
