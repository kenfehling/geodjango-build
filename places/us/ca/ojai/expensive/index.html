Home prices

The median home cost in Ojai is $713,100. It is about $197,480 (38%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $495,600 (227%) higher, and is approximately $76,200 (11%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Ojai's median monthly rent is $1,533. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $471 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $83 (5%) higher.
