Home prices

The median home cost in Shasta is $327,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $128,660 (64%) higher.It is roughly $109,500 (50%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chico is is very similar.

Rents

In Shasta the median rent is $1,217 per month. It is roughly $125 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $155 (14%) higher, and it is roughly $117 (10%) higher compared to the city of Chico.
