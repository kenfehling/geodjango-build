Home prices

The median home in Lake Arrowhead costs $373,700. It is approximately $104,680 (38%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $156,200 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $263,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Arrowhead is $1,123. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $17 (1%) lower. It is about $61 (5%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $327 (22%) lower.
