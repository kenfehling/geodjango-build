Home prices

Jamestown's median home price is $190,200. This is about $101,040 (34%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $27,300 (12%) lower, and is approximately $146,700 (43%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Jamestown's median rent is $875 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $131 (13%) lower.It is roughly $187 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $388 (30%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
