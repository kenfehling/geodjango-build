In comparison to its nearby areas, Brisbane is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home price in Brisbane is $889,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $66,520 (8%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $671,700 (308%) higher, and is roughly $208,600 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brisbane is $1,919. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $263 (12%) lower.It is approximately $857 (80%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $24 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
