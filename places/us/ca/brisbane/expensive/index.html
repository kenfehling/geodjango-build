In comparison to its surrounding areas, Brisbane is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home price in Brisbane is $889,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $66,520 (8%) higher.It is about $671,700 (308%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $208,600 (19%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Brisbane the median monthly rent is $1,919. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $263 (12%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $857 (80%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $24 (1%) higher.
