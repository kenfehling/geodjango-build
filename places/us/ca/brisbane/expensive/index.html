Compared to its surrounding areas, Brisbane is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Brisbane is $889,200. This is approximately $66,520 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $671,700 (308%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $208,600 (19%) lower.

Rents

Brisbane's median monthly rent is $1,919. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $263 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $857 (80%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $24 (1%) higher.
