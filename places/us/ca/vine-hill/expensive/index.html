Home prices

The median home value in Vine Hill is $483,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $27,640 (5%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $265,800 (122%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $614,500 (55%) lower.

Rents

Vine Hill's median rent is $1,811 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $91 (4%) lower. It is approximately $749 (70%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $84 (4%) lower.
