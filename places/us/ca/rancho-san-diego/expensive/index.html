Home prices

The median home in Rancho San Diego is valued at $611,000. It is roughly $76,980 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $393,500 (180%) higher, and is approximately $8,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

Rancho San Diego's median rent is $1,900 a month. This is roughly $228 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $838 (78%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is about $205 (12%) higher.
