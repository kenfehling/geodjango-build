Compared to other places in this area, Rancho San Diego is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of San Diego however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho San Diego is valued at $611,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $76,980 (14%) higher.It is approximately $393,500 (180%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Diego it is roughly $8,400 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rancho San Diego is $1,900 per month. It is about $228 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $838 (78%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is about $205 (12%) higher.
