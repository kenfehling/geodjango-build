Home prices

The median home in Los Olivos costs $882,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $188,360 (27%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $665,100 (305%) higher, and is roughly $635,600 (257%) higher in comparison to the city of Bakersfield.

Rents

Los Olivos's median monthly rent is $2,015. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $421 (26%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $953 (89%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is roughly $911 (82%) higher.
