Home prices

The price of the median home in Los Olivos is $882,600. It is about $188,360 (27%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $665,100 (305%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Bakersfield it is about $635,600 (257%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Los Olivos is $2,015. Compared to other places in this area it is about $421 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $953 (89%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is approximately $911 (82%) higher.
