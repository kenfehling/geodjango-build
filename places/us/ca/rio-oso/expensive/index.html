Compared to its surrounding areas, Rio Oso is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Sacramento, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rio Oso costs $426,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $88,240 (26%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $209,200 (96%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento is roughly $89,800 (26%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rio Oso is $1,333 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $27 (2%) higher. It is approximately $271 (25%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $70 (5%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
