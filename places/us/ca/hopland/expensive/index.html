In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hopland is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Santa Rosa however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hopland costs $408,300. This is approximately $33,080 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $190,800 (87%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Santa Rosa is approximately $132,300 (24%) lower.

Rents

Hopland's median rent is $1,231 per month. It is about $143 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $169 (15%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $378 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Rosa.
