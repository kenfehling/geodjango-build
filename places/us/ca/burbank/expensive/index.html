Home prices

The median home in Burbank costs $778,100. This is approximately $271,660 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $560,600 (257%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $319,700 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Burbank the median rent is $1,566 per month. It is approximately $671 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $504 (47%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $329 (17%) lower.
