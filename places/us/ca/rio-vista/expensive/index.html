Home prices

The median home in Rio Vista costs $343,800. This is approximately $21,120 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $126,300 (58%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $754,000 (68%) lower.

Rents

In Rio Vista the median rent is $1,368 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $341 (33%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $306 (28%) higher, and it is about $527 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
