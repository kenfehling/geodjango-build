The price of the median home in Oak Hills is $347,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $107,940 (44%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $130,400 (59%) higher, and is about $289,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents
