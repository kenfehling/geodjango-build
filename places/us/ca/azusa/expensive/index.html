Compared to the surrounding areas, Azusa is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Azusa is valued at $418,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $56,460 (11%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $201,300 (92%) higher, and is approximately $218,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Azusa's median rent is $1,468 per month. It is approximately $141 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $406 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $18 (1%) higher.
