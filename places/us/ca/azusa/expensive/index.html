Home prices

Azusa's median home cost is $418,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $56,460 (11%) lower.It is approximately $201,300 (92%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $218,100 (34%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Azusa the median rent is $1,468 a month. It is approximately $141 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $406 (38%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $18 (1%) higher.
