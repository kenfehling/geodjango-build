Compared to the surrounding areas, Azusa is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Azusa is valued at $418,800. This is roughly $56,460 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $201,300 (92%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $218,100 (34%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Azusa is $1,468 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $141 (8%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $406 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $18 (1%) higher.
