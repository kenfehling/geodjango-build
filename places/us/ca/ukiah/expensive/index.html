Home prices

Rents

In Ukiah the median rent is $1,205 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $82 (7%) higher.It is about $143 (13%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $404 (25%) lower compared to the city of Santa Rosa.
