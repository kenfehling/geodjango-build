Compared to other places in this area, Ukiah is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Santa Rosa however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Ukiah is $349,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $70,640 (16%) lower. It is about $132,100 (60%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $191,000 (35%) lower compared to the city of Santa Rosa.

Rents

In Ukiah the median monthly rent is $1,205. Compared to nearby areas it is about $82 (7%) higher. It is approximately $143 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $404 (25%) lower compared to the city of Santa Rosa.
