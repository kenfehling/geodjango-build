In comparison to its surrounding areas, Contra Costa Centre is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Contra Costa Centre is valued at $666,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $254,180 (27%) lower. It is approximately $448,600 (206%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $431,700 (39%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Contra Costa Centre is $2,223 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $26 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,161 (109%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $328 (17%) higher.
