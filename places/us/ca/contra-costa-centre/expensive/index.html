In comparison to other places in this area, Contra Costa Centre is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Contra Costa Centre's median home price is $666,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $254,180 (27%) lower.It is roughly $448,600 (206%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $431,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Contra Costa Centre is $2,223. This is roughly $26 (1%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,161 (109%) higher, and is approximately $328 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
