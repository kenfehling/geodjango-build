In comparison to nearby areas, Contra Costa Centre is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home price in Contra Costa Centre is $666,100. This is roughly $254,180 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $448,600 (206%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $431,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Contra Costa Centre is $2,223 a month. This is approximately $26 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $1,161 (109%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $328 (17%) higher.
