In comparison to other places in this area, Contra Costa Centre is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Contra Costa Centre costs $666,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $254,180 (27%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $448,600 (206%) higher, and it is about $431,700 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Contra Costa Centre is $2,223. It is roughly $26 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $1,161 (109%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $328 (17%) higher.
