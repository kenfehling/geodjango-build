Home prices

The median home value in San Juan Bautista is $587,500. This is about $28,660 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $370,000 (170%) higher, and is about $510,300 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In San Juan Bautista the median monthly rent is $1,574. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $190 (10%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $512 (48%) higher, and it is about $321 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
