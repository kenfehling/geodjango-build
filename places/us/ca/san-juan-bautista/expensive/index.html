Home prices

San Juan Bautista's median home value is $587,500. This is about $28,660 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $370,000 (170%) higher, and it is about $510,300 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In San Juan Bautista the median rent is $1,574 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $190 (10%) lower. It is roughly $512 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $321 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
