Home prices

The price of the median home in San Juan Bautista is $587,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $28,660 (5%) higher. It is approximately $370,000 (170%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $510,300 (46%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

San Juan Bautista's median rent is $1,574 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $190 (10%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $512 (48%) higher, and it is approximately $321 (16%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
