Home prices

Rents

The median rent in San Mateo is $2,475 per month. It is roughly $271 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $1,413 (133%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $580 (30%) higher.
