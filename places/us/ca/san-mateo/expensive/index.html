Home prices

The median home value in San Mateo is $1,098,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $589,400 (34%) lower.It is approximately $880,900 (405%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

San Mateo's median rent is $2,475 a month. It is approximately $271 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,413 (133%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $580 (30%) higher.
