Compared to its surrounding areas, San Mateo is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home price in San Mateo is $1,098,400. It is roughly $514,540 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $880,900 (405%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is is very similar.

Rents

In San Mateo the median rent is $2,475 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $37 (1%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,413 (133%) higher, and it is approximately $580 (30%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
