In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oxnard is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oxnard is $453,900. It is roughly $111,100 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $236,400 (108%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $183,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Oxnard's median monthly rent is $1,593. This is roughly $268 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $531 (50%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $143 (9%) higher.
