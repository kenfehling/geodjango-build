Home prices

Clayton's median home value is $771,400. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $56,260 (7%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $553,900 (254%) higher, and is approximately $326,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Clayton is $2,627 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $603 (29%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $1,565 (147%) higher, and it is roughly $732 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
