Compared to other places in this area, Clayton is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Clayton costs $771,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $56,260 (7%) higher. It is roughly $553,900 (254%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $326,400 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Clayton's median rent is $2,627 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $603 (29%) higher. It is about $1,565 (147%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $732 (38%) higher.
