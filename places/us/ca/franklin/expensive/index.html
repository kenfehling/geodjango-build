Compared to the surrounding areas, Franklin is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Jose.

Home prices

The median home cost in Franklin is $195,900. This is approximately $68,220 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $21,600 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Jose is about $668,700 (77%) lower.

Rents

Franklin's median monthly rent is $1,105. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $139 (14%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $43 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of San Jose is about $1,002 (47%) lower.
