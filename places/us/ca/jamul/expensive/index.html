Home prices

Jamul's median home value is $669,700. This is about $102,600 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $452,200 (207%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Diego it is roughly $67,100 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Jamul the median monthly rent is $1,592. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $192 (10%) lower. It is approximately $530 (49%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is about $103 (6%) lower.
