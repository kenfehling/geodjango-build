Home prices

The median home price in Lennox is $454,800. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $183,780 (28%) lower. It is approximately $237,300 (109%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $182,100 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Lennox's median rent is $1,175 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $424 (26%) lower. It is about $113 (10%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $275 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
