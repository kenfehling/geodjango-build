Home prices

The median home value in Biola is $156,200. This is approximately $67,880 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $61,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is roughly $85,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Biola the median rent is $939 per month. This is approximately $72 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $123 (11%) lower, and it is roughly $66 (6%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.
