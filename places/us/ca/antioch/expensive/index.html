Compared to other places in this area, Antioch is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Antioch costs $397,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $179,680 (31%) lower. It is about $179,900 (82%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $700,400 (63%) lower.

Rents

Antioch's median rent is $1,790 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $192 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $728 (68%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $105 (5%) lower.
