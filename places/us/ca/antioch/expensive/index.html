In comparison to other places in this area, Antioch is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Antioch costs $397,400. It is roughly $179,680 (31%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $179,900 (82%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $700,400 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Antioch the median monthly rent is $1,790. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $192 (9%) lower.It is about $728 (68%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $105 (5%) lower.
