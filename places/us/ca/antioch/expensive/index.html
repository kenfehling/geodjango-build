In comparison to its surrounding areas, Antioch is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Antioch is valued at $397,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $179,680 (31%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $179,900 (82%) higher, and is about $700,400 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Antioch's median rent is $1,790 a month. It is roughly $192 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $728 (68%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $105 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
