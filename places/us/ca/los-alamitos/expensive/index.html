Home prices

Los Alamitos's median home value is $761,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $220,580 (40%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $543,700 (249%) higher, and it is approximately $124,300 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Los Alamitos's median rent is $1,823 a month. This is approximately $67 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $761 (71%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $373 (25%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
