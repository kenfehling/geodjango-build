Home prices

The median home in Los Alamitos costs $761,200. This is about $220,580 (40%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $543,700 (249%) higher, and is about $124,300 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Los Alamitos is $1,823 per month. It is about $67 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $761 (71%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $373 (25%) higher.
