Home prices

The median home price in Los Alamitos is $761,200. It is approximately $220,580 (40%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $543,700 (249%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $124,300 (19%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Los Alamitos the median monthly rent is $1,823. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $67 (3%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $761 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $373 (25%) higher.
