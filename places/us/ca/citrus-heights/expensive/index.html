Compared to other places in this area, Citrus Heights is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Oakland.

Home prices

The median home in Citrus Heights is valued at $297,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $31,160 (9%) lower. It is about $80,200 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $389,700 (56%) lower in comparison to the city of Oakland.

Rents

Citrus Heights's median rent is $1,260 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $90 (6%) lower. It is roughly $198 (18%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Oakland it is about $185 (12%) lower.
