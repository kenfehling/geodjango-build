Moorpark's median home cost is $632,900. This is roughly $46,280 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $415,400 (190%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is is very similar.

Rents

In Moorpark the median rent is $2,052 a month. This is about $53 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $990 (93%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $602 (41%) higher.
