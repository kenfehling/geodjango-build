Compared to other places in this area, Moorpark is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

Moorpark's median home value is $632,900. It is approximately $46,280 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $415,400 (190%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is is very similar.

Rents

In Moorpark the median rent is $2,052 per month. This is approximately $53 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $990 (93%) higher, and it is about $602 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
