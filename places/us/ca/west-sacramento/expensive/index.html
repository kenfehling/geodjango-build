In comparison to other places in this area, West Sacramento is a lot more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home price in West Sacramento is $349,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $114,000 (48%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $132,300 (60%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $748,000 (68%) lower.

Rents

In West Sacramento the median monthly rent is $1,026. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $79 (7%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $36 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $869 (45%) lower.
