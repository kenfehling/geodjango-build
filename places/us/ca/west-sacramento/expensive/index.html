In comparison to its nearby areas, West Sacramento is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

West Sacramento's median home price is $349,800. This is approximately $114,000 (48%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $132,300 (60%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $748,000 (68%) lower.

Rents

West Sacramento's median rent is $1,026 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $79 (7%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $36 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $869 (45%) lower.
