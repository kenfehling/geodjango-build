In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Sacramento is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in West Sacramento is valued at $349,800. This is approximately $114,000 (48%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $132,300 (60%) higher, and it is approximately $748,000 (68%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

West Sacramento's median monthly rent is $1,026. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $79 (7%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $36 (3%) lower, and is about $869 (45%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
