Compared to its surrounding areas, West Sacramento is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West Sacramento is valued at $349,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $114,000 (48%) higher.It is approximately $132,300 (60%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $748,000 (68%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

West Sacramento's median rent is $1,026 a month. It is approximately $79 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $36 (3%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $869 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
