Home prices

The median home in Berry Creek costs $173,000. This is roughly $22,100 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $44,500 (20%) lower, and it is about $163,900 (48%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median rent in Berry Creek is $798 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $146 (15%) lower.It is roughly $264 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $465 (36%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
