The median home in Walnut Park is valued at $410,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $13,200 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $192,800 (88%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $226,600 (35%) lower.

Rents

Walnut Park's median monthly rent is $1,152. It is approximately $13 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $90 (8%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $298 (20%) lower.
