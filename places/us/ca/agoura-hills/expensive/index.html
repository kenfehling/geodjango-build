Compared to other places in this area, Agoura Hills is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Agoura Hills costs $815,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $405,200 (33%) lower.It is approximately $597,500 (274%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $178,100 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Agoura Hills the median monthly rent is $2,529. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $333 (11%) lower.It is about $1,467 (138%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $1,079 (74%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
