In comparison to other places in this area, Agoura Hills is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Agoura Hills is $815,000. It is about $405,200 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $597,500 (274%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $178,100 (27%) higher.

Rents

Agoura Hills's median monthly rent is $2,529. It is approximately $333 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,467 (138%) higher, and it is about $1,079 (74%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
