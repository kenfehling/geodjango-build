Compared to the surrounding areas, Agoura Hills is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Agoura Hills is valued at $815,000. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $184,420 (18%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $597,500 (274%) higher, and is roughly $178,100 (27%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Agoura Hills is $2,529 a month. This is about $53 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $1,467 (138%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $1,079 (74%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
