Compared to other places in this area, Agoura Hills is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Agoura Hills costs $815,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $184,420 (18%) lower. It is about $597,500 (274%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $178,100 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Agoura Hills's median rent is $2,529 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $53 (2%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $1,467 (138%) higher, and it is about $1,079 (74%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
