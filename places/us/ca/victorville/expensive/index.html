Home prices

The median home in Victorville costs $221,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $17,120 (7%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $3,700 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $415,700 (65%) lower.

Rents

Victorville's median monthly rent is $1,259. This is approximately $38 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $197 (18%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $191 (13%) lower.
