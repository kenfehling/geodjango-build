Home prices

Sky Valley's median home cost is $150,300. This is roughly $60,620 (28%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $67,200 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $452,300 (75%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

In Sky Valley the median rent is $897 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $437 (32%) lower.It is about $165 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $798 (47%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
