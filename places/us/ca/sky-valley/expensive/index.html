Home prices

The price of the median home in Sky Valley is $150,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $60,620 (28%) lower.It is roughly $67,200 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is approximately $452,300 (75%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sky Valley is $897. This is approximately $437 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $165 (15%) lower, and it is roughly $798 (47%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
