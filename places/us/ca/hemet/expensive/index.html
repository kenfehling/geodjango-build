Home prices

The value of the median home in Hemet is $196,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $38,800 (16%) lower. It is roughly $20,800 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $440,200 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Hemet the median rent is $1,096 a month. It is about $214 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $34 (3%) higher, and is roughly $354 (24%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
