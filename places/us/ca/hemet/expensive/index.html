Home prices

Rents

In Hemet the median monthly rent is $1,096. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $214 (16%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $34 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $354 (24%) lower.
