Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hemet is $196,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $38,800 (16%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $20,800 (9%) lower, and it is roughly $440,200 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Hemet's median rent is $1,096 per month. This is about $214 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $34 (3%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $354 (24%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
