In comparison to its surrounding areas, Hemet is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Hemet costs $196,700. It is about $38,800 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $20,800 (9%) lower, and is approximately $440,200 (69%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Hemet's median rent is $1,096 per month. It is roughly $214 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $34 (3%) higher, and is roughly $354 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
