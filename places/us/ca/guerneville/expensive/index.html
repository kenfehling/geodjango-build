Home prices

Rents

In Guerneville the median rent is $1,261 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $34 (2%) lower.It is about $199 (18%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $634 (33%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
