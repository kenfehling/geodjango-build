In comparison to other places in this area, Santa Ana is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Santa Ana is $491,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $195,940 (28%) lower.It is approximately $273,800 (125%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $145,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

Santa Ana's median rent is $1,563 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $212 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $501 (47%) higher, and is roughly $113 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
