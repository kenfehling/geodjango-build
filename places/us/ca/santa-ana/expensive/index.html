Compared to its nearby areas, Santa Ana is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Ana costs $491,300. This is approximately $195,940 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $273,800 (125%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $145,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

In Santa Ana the median monthly rent is $1,563. It is approximately $212 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $501 (47%) higher, and it is roughly $113 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
