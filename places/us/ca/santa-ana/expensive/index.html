In comparison to nearby areas, Santa Ana is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

Santa Ana's median home cost is $491,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $195,940 (28%) lower. It is approximately $273,800 (125%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $145,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Ana is $1,563 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $212 (11%) lower. It is about $501 (47%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $113 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
