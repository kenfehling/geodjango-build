Home prices

The median home in Santa Ana costs $491,300. It is roughly $195,940 (28%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $273,800 (125%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $145,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

Santa Ana's median rent is $1,563 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $212 (11%) lower. It is approximately $501 (47%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $113 (7%) higher.
