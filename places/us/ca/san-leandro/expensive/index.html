Home prices

The median home price in San Leandro is $590,800. It is is very similar in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $373,300 (171%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $507,000 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in San Leandro is $1,652 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $131 (7%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $590 (55%) higher, and it is approximately $243 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
