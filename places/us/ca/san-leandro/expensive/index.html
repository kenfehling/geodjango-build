Much like other places in this area, it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Leandro costs $590,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $373,300 (171%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $507,000 (46%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in San Leandro is $1,652 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $131 (7%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $590 (55%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $243 (12%) lower.
