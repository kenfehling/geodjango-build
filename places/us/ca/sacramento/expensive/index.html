In comparison to its nearby areas, Sacramento is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sacramento is $336,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $74,760 (28%) higher. It is about $119,400 (54%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $760,900 (69%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Sacramento's median rent is $1,263 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $219 (20%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $201 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $632 (33%) lower.
