In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sacramento is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sacramento costs $336,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $74,760 (28%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $119,400 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $760,900 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Sacramento the median rent is $1,263 a month. This is roughly $219 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $201 (18%) higher, and is approximately $632 (33%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
