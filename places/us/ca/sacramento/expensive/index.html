Compared to its nearby areas, Sacramento is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sacramento is $336,900. This is about $74,760 (28%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $119,400 (54%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $760,900 (69%) lower.

Rents

Sacramento's median monthly rent is $1,263. This is approximately $219 (20%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $201 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $632 (33%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
