Compared to other places in this area, Sacramento is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Sacramento is valued at $336,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $74,760 (28%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $119,400 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $760,900 (69%) lower.

Rents

Sacramento's median monthly rent is $1,263. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $219 (20%) higher.It is roughly $201 (18%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $632 (33%) lower.
