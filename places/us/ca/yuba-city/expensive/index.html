Compared to the surrounding areas, Yuba City is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Sacramento however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Yuba City is $277,400. This is about $67,460 (32%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $59,900 (27%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is about $59,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

In Yuba City the median rent is $1,047 a month. This is about $66 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $15 (1%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $216 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
