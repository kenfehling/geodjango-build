In comparison to the surrounding areas, Yuba City is substantially more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Sacramento, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Yuba City is valued at $277,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $67,460 (32%) higher. It is approximately $59,900 (27%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Sacramento is about $59,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

Yuba City's median monthly rent is $1,047. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $66 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $15 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $216 (17%) lower.
