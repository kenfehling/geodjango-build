Compared to the surrounding areas, Yuba City is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Sacramento, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Yuba City is $277,400. It is approximately $67,460 (32%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $59,900 (27%) higher, and is roughly $59,500 (17%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Yuba City's median monthly rent is $1,047. It is roughly $66 (6%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $15 (1%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $216 (17%) lower.
