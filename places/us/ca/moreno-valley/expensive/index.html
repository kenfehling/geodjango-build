Home prices

Moreno Valley's median home cost is $312,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $5,100 (1%) higher. It is approximately $94,500 (43%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $324,900 (51%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Moreno Valley's median rent is $1,494 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $252 (20%) higher. It is roughly $432 (40%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $44 (3%) higher.
