Rents

The median monthly rent in Georgetown is $1,396. This is approximately $28 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $334 (31%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $133 (10%) higher.
