Home prices

The median home in Orange Cove costs $152,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $29,580 (16%) lower. It is approximately $65,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $89,700 (37%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.

Rents

In Orange Cove the median rent is $742 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $211 (22%) lower. It is approximately $320 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is about $263 (26%) lower.
