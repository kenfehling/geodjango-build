Home prices

The median home in Petaluma is valued at $633,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $62,680 (10%) higher.It is about $416,400 (191%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $463,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Petaluma the median monthly rent is $1,830. It is about $75 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $768 (72%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $65 (3%) lower.
