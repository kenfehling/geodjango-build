Home prices

The price of the median home in Petaluma is $633,900. This is about $62,680 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $416,400 (191%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $463,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

Petaluma's median monthly rent is $1,830. This is roughly $75 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $768 (72%) higher, and it is approximately $65 (3%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
