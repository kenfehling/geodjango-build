The median home in Petaluma is valued at $633,900. It is approximately $62,680 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $416,400 (191%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $463,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Petaluma is $1,830. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $75 (4%) higher. It is approximately $768 (72%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $65 (3%) lower.
