In comparison to its surrounding areas, Petaluma is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Petaluma costs $633,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $62,680 (10%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $416,400 (191%) higher, and is about $463,900 (42%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Petaluma's median monthly rent is $1,830. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $75 (4%) higher. It is roughly $768 (72%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $65 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
