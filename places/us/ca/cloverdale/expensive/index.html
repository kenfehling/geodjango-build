Home prices

The median home in Cloverdale costs $476,100. This is roughly $28,580 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $258,600 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $621,700 (56%) lower.

Rents

Cloverdale's median monthly rent is $1,341. Compared to its surrounding areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $279 (26%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $554 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
