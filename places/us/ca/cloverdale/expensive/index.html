Compared to its surrounding areas, Cloverdale is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Cloverdale is $476,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $28,580 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $258,600 (118%) higher, and it is roughly $621,700 (56%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Cloverdale is $1,341 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is is very similar. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $279 (26%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $554 (29%) lower.
