Compared to the surrounding areas, Glendora is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Glendora is valued at $601,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $115,180 (23%) higher.It is about $384,300 (176%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $35,100 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Glendora is $1,716 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $25 (1%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $654 (61%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $266 (18%) higher.
