Compared to its nearby areas, Glendora is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Glendora's median home cost is $601,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $115,180 (23%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $384,300 (176%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $35,100 (5%) lower.

Rents

Glendora's median monthly rent is $1,716. It is about $25 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $654 (61%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $266 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
