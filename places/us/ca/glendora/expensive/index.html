In comparison to the surrounding areas, Glendora is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in Glendora is $601,800. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $115,180 (23%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $384,300 (176%) higher, and is approximately $35,100 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Glendora is $1,716 a month. This is about $25 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $654 (61%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $266 (18%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
