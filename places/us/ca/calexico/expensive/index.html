Home prices

The median home cost in Calexico is $207,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $16,360 (8%) higher. It is roughly $9,900 (4%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Yuma it is roughly $64,200 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Calexico is $904 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $158 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $13 (1%) higher compared to the city of Yuma.
