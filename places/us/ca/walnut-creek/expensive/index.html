Home prices

The median home in Walnut Creek costs $793,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $380,800 (32%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $575,600 (264%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $304,700 (27%) lower.

Rents
