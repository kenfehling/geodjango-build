In comparison to the surrounding areas, Walnut Creek is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Walnut Creek costs $793,100. This is approximately $380,800 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $575,600 (264%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $304,700 (27%) lower.

Rents

Walnut Creek's median rent is $2,095 a month. It is about $256 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $1,033 (97%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $200 (10%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
