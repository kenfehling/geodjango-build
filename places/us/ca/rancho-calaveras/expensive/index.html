Home prices

The median home price in Rancho Calaveras is $289,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $34,540 (13%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $72,400 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of San Jose is approximately $574,700 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Rancho Calaveras the median rent is $1,764 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $394 (28%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $702 (66%) higher, and it is roughly $343 (16%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.
