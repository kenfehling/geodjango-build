Compared to the surrounding areas, Rancho Calaveras is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Jose.

Home prices

Rancho Calaveras's median home cost is $289,900. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $34,540 (13%) higher. It is roughly $72,400 (33%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Jose it is approximately $574,700 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Rancho Calaveras the median monthly rent is $1,764. This is roughly $394 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $702 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Jose is roughly $343 (16%) lower.
