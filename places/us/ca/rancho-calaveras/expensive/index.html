Compared to its surrounding areas, Rancho Calaveras is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Jose.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho Calaveras costs $289,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $34,540 (13%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $72,400 (33%) higher, and is roughly $574,700 (66%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

The median rent in Rancho Calaveras is $1,764 a month. It is roughly $394 (28%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $702 (66%) higher, and compared to the city of San Jose is about $343 (16%) lower.
