In comparison to the surrounding areas, Novato is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Novato is $782,500. It is roughly $142,160 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $565,000 (259%) higher, and is approximately $315,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Novato the median monthly rent is $2,045. It is about $134 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $983 (92%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $150 (7%) higher.
