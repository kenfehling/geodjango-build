Compared to its nearby areas, Novato is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Novato is $782,500. This is roughly $142,160 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $565,000 (259%) higher, and is approximately $315,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Novato's median monthly rent is $2,045. This is roughly $134 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $983 (92%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $150 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
