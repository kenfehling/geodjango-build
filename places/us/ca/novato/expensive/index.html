Home prices

The median home in Novato is valued at $782,500. It is approximately $142,160 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $565,000 (259%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $315,300 (28%) lower.

Rents

Novato's median rent is $2,045 per month. This is roughly $134 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $983 (92%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $150 (7%) higher.
