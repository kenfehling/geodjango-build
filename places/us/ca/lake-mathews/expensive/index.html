Home prices

The median home in Lake Mathews costs $526,600. This is approximately $177,960 (51%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $309,100 (142%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $110,300 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Mathews is $1,477 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $152 (9%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $415 (39%) higher, and is approximately $27 (1%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
