Compared to its surrounding areas, Thousand Oaks is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Thousand Oaks is $726,100. It is roughly $264,380 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $508,600 (233%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $89,200 (14%) higher.

Rents

Thousand Oaks's median rent is $2,101 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $672 (24%) lower.It is approximately $1,039 (97%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $651 (44%) higher.
