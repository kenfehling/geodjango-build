Home prices

The median home in Clyde costs $435,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $50,140 (13%) higher. It is roughly $217,500 (100%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $662,800 (60%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Clyde's median rent is $1,768 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $118 (7%) higher. It is roughly $706 (66%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $127 (6%) lower.
