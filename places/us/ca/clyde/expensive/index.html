Compared to the surrounding areas, Clyde is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clyde is $435,000. This is about $50,140 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $217,500 (100%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $662,800 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Clyde the median rent is $1,768 a month. This is approximately $118 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $706 (66%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $127 (6%) lower.
