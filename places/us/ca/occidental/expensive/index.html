Home prices

The value of the median home in Occidental is $633,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $18,880 (3%) higher. It is roughly $415,800 (191%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $464,500 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Occidental the median monthly rent is $2,265. This is about $1,088 (92%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,203 (113%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $370 (19%) higher.
