Home prices

The median home in Occidental is valued at $633,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $18,880 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $415,800 (191%) higher, and it is about $464,500 (42%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Occidental's median monthly rent is $2,265. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $1,088 (92%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,203 (113%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $370 (19%) higher.
