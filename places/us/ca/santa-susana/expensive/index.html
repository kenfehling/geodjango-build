Home prices

The median home in Santa Susana is valued at $588,000. This is about $355,440 (37%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $370,500 (170%) higher, and it is approximately $48,900 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Santa Susana the median monthly rent is $1,470. This is roughly $1,023 (41%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $408 (38%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $20 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
