Dos Palos's median home price is $194,500. This is approximately $21,320 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $23,000 (10%) lower, and it is about $670,100 (77%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents
