Compared to nearby areas, Vallejo is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Vallejo is $370,000. This is about $153,360 (29%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $152,500 (70%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $727,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

In Vallejo the median rent is $1,532 per month. It is approximately $194 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $470 (44%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $363 (19%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
