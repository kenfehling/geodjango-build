In comparison to its nearby areas, Vallejo is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Vallejo costs $370,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $153,360 (29%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $152,500 (70%) higher, and is about $727,800 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Vallejo is $1,532. It is roughly $194 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $470 (44%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $363 (19%) lower.
