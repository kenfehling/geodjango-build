Compared to other places in this area, Vallejo is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Vallejo costs $370,000. This is about $153,360 (29%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $152,500 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $727,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

Vallejo's median monthly rent is $1,532. It is about $194 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $470 (44%) higher, and is approximately $363 (19%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
