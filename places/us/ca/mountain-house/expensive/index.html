Home prices

The median home in Mountain House is valued at $595,700. This is approximately $44,040 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $378,200 (173%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $502,100 (45%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Mountain House the median rent is $2,547 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $643 (33%) higher. It is about $1,485 (139%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $652 (34%) higher.
