Compared to the surrounding areas, Quartz Hill is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Quartz Hill is $335,500. This is roughly $28,220 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $118,000 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $301,400 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Quartz Hill is $1,141 a month. It is roughly $71 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $79 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $309 (21%) lower.
