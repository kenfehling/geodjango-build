Home prices

The median home in Quartz Hill costs $335,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $28,220 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $118,000 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $301,400 (47%) lower.

Rents
