In comparison to other places in this area, San Marcos is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

San Marcos's median home cost is $538,200. It is approximately $55,420 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $320,700 (147%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $98,700 (15%) lower.

Rents

San Marcos's median rent is $1,704 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $203 (10%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $642 (60%) higher, and it is about $254 (17%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
