Compared to other places in this area, San Marcos is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in San Marcos is $538,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $55,420 (9%) lower.It is roughly $320,700 (147%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $98,700 (15%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

San Marcos's median rent is $1,704 per month. This is roughly $203 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $642 (60%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $254 (17%) higher.
