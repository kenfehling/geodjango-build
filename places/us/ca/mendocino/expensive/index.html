In comparison to other places in this area, Mendocino is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Ukiah.

Home prices

The median home in Mendocino is valued at $474,600. This is roughly $160,580 (51%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $257,100 (118%) higher, and compared to the city of Ukiah it is approximately $125,000 (35%) higher.

Rents

Mendocino's median monthly rent is $1,150. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $200 (14%) lower. It is roughly $88 (8%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Ukiah it is about $55 (4%) lower.
