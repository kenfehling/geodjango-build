Home prices

Mendocino's median home cost is $474,600. This is approximately $160,580 (51%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $257,100 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $125,000 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Ukiah.

Rents

The median rent in Mendocino is $1,150 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $200 (14%) lower. It is roughly $88 (8%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $55 (4%) lower compared to the city of Ukiah.
