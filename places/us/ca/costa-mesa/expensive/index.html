Compared to nearby areas, Costa Mesa is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Costa Mesa is $749,400. This is roughly $144,500 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $531,900 (244%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $112,500 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Costa Mesa the median rent is $1,857 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $35 (1%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $795 (74%) higher, and is about $407 (28%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
