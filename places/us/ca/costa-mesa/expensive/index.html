Compared to its nearby areas, Costa Mesa is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home cost in Costa Mesa is $749,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $144,500 (16%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $531,900 (244%) higher, and is about $112,500 (17%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Costa Mesa the median rent is $1,857 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $35 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $795 (74%) higher, and it is about $407 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
