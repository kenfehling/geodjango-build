In comparison to other places in this area, Rollingwood is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Rollingwood is valued at $398,800. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $60,120 (13%) lower. It is about $181,300 (83%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $699,000 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Rollingwood's median monthly rent is $1,918. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $304 (18%) higher. It is about $856 (80%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $23 (1%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
