In comparison to other places in this area, Rollingwood is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Rollingwood is $398,800. It is about $60,120 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $181,300 (83%) higher, and it is approximately $699,000 (63%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Rollingwood's median rent is $1,918 a month. This is about $304 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $856 (80%) higher, and it is roughly $23 (1%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
