Home prices

The price of the median home in Rollingwood is $398,800. It is approximately $60,120 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $181,300 (83%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $699,000 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Rollingwood the median rent is $1,918 per month. This is approximately $304 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $856 (80%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $23 (1%) higher.
