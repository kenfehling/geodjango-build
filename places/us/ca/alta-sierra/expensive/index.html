Home prices

The median home in Alta Sierra costs $395,700. This is about $25,900 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $178,200 (81%) higher, and is approximately $58,800 (17%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

In Alta Sierra the median monthly rent is $1,847. This is about $415 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $785 (73%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is roughly $584 (46%) higher.
