Rents

In Alta Sierra the median monthly rent is $1,847. This is about $415 (29%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $785 (73%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $584 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
