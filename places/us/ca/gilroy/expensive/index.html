Compared to its surrounding areas, Gilroy is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Gilroy is $663,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $138,180 (17%) lower. It is approximately $445,600 (204%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $434,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

Gilroy's median monthly rent is $1,881. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $35 (1%) higher. It is approximately $819 (77%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is is very similar compared to the city of San Francisco.
