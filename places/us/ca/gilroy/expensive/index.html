Home prices

The median home in Gilroy is valued at $663,100. This is roughly $138,180 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $445,600 (204%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $434,700 (39%) lower.

Rents

Gilroy's median monthly rent is $1,881. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $35 (1%) higher. It is roughly $819 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
