Home prices

The median home price in Rose Hills is $599,300. This is approximately $124,340 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $381,800 (175%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $37,600 (5%) lower.

Rents

Rose Hills's median monthly rent is $1,619. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $183 (12%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $557 (52%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $169 (11%) higher.
