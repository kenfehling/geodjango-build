In comparison to other places in this area, Nuevo is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Nuevo's median home price is $324,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $66,420 (25%) higher.It is roughly $107,100 (49%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $312,300 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Nuevo's median rent is $1,346 a month. This is about $45 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $284 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $104 (7%) lower.
