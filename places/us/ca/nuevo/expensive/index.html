Home prices

The median home in Nuevo is valued at $324,600. It is approximately $66,420 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $107,100 (49%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $312,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Nuevo the median monthly rent is $1,346. This is roughly $45 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $284 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $104 (7%) lower.
