Compared to its surrounding areas, San Miguel is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of San Francisco, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

San Miguel's median home cost is $1,179,000. It is approximately $82,280 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $961,500 (442%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $81,200 (7%) higher.

Rents

In San Miguel the median monthly rent is $2,350. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $50 (2%) higher. It is about $1,288 (121%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $455 (24%) higher.
