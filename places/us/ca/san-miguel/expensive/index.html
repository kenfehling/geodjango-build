Home prices

The median home in San Miguel is valued at $1,179,000. This is approximately $82,280 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $961,500 (442%) higher, and is roughly $81,200 (7%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in San Miguel is $2,350 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $50 (2%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $1,288 (121%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $455 (24%) higher.
