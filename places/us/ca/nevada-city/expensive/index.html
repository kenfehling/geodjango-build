Rents

In Nevada City the median monthly rent is $1,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $311 (22%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $38 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is roughly $163 (12%) lower.
