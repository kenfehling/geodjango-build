Home prices

Nevada City's median home cost is $417,600. It is about $87,460 (26%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $200,100 (92%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is roughly $80,700 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Nevada City the median monthly rent is $1,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $311 (22%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $38 (3%) higher, and is roughly $163 (12%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
