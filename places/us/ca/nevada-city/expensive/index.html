Home prices

The median home in Nevada City costs $417,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $87,460 (26%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $200,100 (92%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $80,700 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nevada City is $1,100. It is approximately $311 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $38 (3%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $163 (12%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
