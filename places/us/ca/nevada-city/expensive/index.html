Home prices

The median home in Nevada City is valued at $417,600. It is about $87,460 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $200,100 (92%) higher, and is roughly $80,700 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nevada City is $1,100. This is about $311 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $38 (3%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $163 (12%) lower.
