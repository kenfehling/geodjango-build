In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marin City is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Marin City is valued at $733,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $712,040 (49%) lower. It is about $516,200 (237%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $364,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marin City is $1,662. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $606 (26%) lower. It is approximately $600 (56%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $233 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
