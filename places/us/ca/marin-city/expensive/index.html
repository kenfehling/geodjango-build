Home prices

Marin City's median home value is $733,700. This is about $1,062,241 (59%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $516,200 (237%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $364,100 (33%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Marin City the median monthly rent is $1,662. It is roughly $792 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $600 (56%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $233 (12%) lower.
