In comparison to its nearby areas, Marin City is substantially less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Marin City is $733,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $712,040 (49%) lower. It is about $516,200 (237%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $364,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marin City is $1,662 a month. It is roughly $606 (26%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $600 (56%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $233 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
