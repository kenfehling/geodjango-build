Home prices

Marin City's median home price is $733,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $1,062,241 (59%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $516,200 (237%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $364,100 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Marin City the median rent is $1,662 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $792 (32%) lower.It is about $600 (56%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $233 (12%) lower.
