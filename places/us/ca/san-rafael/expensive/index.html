Home prices

San Rafael's median home cost is $923,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $419,340 (31%) lower.It is about $705,600 (324%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $174,700 (15%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in San Rafael is $1,937. It is about $82 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $875 (82%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $42 (2%) higher.
