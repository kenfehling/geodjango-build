Compared to other places in this area, San Rafael is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Rafael is valued at $923,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $281,180 (23%) lower. It is roughly $705,600 (324%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $174,700 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

San Rafael's median monthly rent is $1,937. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $37 (1%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $875 (82%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $42 (2%) higher.
