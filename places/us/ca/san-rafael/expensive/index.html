Home prices

The median home in San Rafael costs $923,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $419,340 (31%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $705,600 (324%) higher, and is about $174,700 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

San Rafael's median rent is $1,937 per month. It is roughly $82 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $875 (82%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $42 (2%) higher.
