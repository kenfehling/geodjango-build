Home prices

The median home in El Cerrito costs $804,700. This is about $143,940 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $587,200 (269%) higher, and it is about $293,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in El Cerrito is $1,956. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $55 (2%) lower. It is about $894 (84%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $61 (3%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
