Compared to the surrounding areas, El Cerrito is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

El Cerrito's median home value is $804,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $143,940 (21%) higher. It is about $587,200 (269%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $293,100 (26%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

El Cerrito's median rent is $1,956 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $55 (2%) lower. It is roughly $894 (84%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $61 (3%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
