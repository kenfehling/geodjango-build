Home prices

El Cerrito's median home cost is $804,700. This is about $143,940 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $587,200 (269%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $293,100 (26%) lower.

Rents

El Cerrito's median rent is $1,956 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $55 (2%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $894 (84%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $61 (3%) higher.
