Home prices

The price of the median home in El Cerrito is $804,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $143,940 (21%) higher.It is roughly $587,200 (269%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $293,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In El Cerrito the median monthly rent is $1,956. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $55 (2%) lower.It is roughly $894 (84%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $61 (3%) higher.
