Home prices

The price of the median home in Cold Springs is $341,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $59,480 (14%) lower.It is approximately $123,900 (56%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $4,500 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cold Springs is $1,324. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $87 (6%) higher.It is roughly $262 (24%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is roughly $61 (4%) higher.
