Home prices

Rents

Cold Springs's median monthly rent is $1,324. It is approximately $87 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $262 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento is approximately $61 (4%) higher.
