In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sunnyvale is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Sunnyvale costs $1,223,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $525,461 (30%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,006,100 (462%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $125,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Sunnyvale the median monthly rent is $2,508. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $476 (15%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,446 (136%) higher, and is approximately $613 (32%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
