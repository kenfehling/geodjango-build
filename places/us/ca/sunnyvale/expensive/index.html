Compared to other places in this area, Sunnyvale is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Sunnyvale is $1,223,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $172,820 (16%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $1,006,100 (462%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $125,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

In Sunnyvale the median monthly rent is $2,508. This is about $287 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $1,446 (136%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $613 (32%) higher.
