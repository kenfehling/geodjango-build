In comparison to its nearby areas, Sunnyvale is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of San Francisco, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Sunnyvale is $1,223,600. This is approximately $172,820 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $1,006,100 (462%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $125,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Sunnyvale is $2,508 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $287 (12%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $1,446 (136%) higher, and is roughly $613 (32%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
