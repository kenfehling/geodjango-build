Home prices

The cost of the median home in Healdsburg is $724,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $156,300 (27%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $507,200 (233%) higher, and it is roughly $373,100 (33%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Healdsburg is $1,736. This is about $276 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $674 (63%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $159 (8%) lower.
