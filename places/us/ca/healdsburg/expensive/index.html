In comparison to other places in this area, Healdsburg is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Healdsburg is $724,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $156,300 (27%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $507,200 (233%) higher, and it is about $373,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Healdsburg's median monthly rent is $1,736. It is approximately $276 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $674 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $159 (8%) lower.
