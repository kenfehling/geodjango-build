Home prices

The median home in Healdsburg costs $724,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $156,300 (27%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $507,200 (233%) higher, and is roughly $373,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Healdsburg is $1,736 per month. It is roughly $276 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $674 (63%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $159 (8%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
