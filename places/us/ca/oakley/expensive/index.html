Rents

Oakley's median monthly rent is $1,688. This is approximately $96 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $626 (58%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $207 (10%) lower.
