In comparison to other places in this area, Manteca is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home value in Manteca is $386,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $72,180 (22%) higher. It is approximately $168,900 (77%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $711,400 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Manteca's median rent is $1,451 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $101 (7%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $389 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $444 (23%) lower.
