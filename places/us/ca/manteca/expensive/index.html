Home prices

The median home in Manteca costs $386,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $72,180 (22%) higher. It is about $168,900 (77%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $711,400 (64%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Manteca's median monthly rent is $1,451. This is approximately $101 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $389 (36%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $444 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
