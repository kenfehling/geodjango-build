Home prices

Rents

Manteca's median monthly rent is $1,451. This is approximately $101 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $389 (36%) higher, and it is about $444 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
