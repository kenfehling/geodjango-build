Home prices

The median home value in Manteca is $386,400. It is approximately $72,180 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $168,900 (77%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $711,400 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Manteca is $1,451 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $101 (7%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $389 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $444 (23%) lower.
