Compared to its surrounding areas, Los Osos is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Santa Maria.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Los Osos is $551,500. It is approximately $164,580 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $334,000 (153%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $207,400 (60%) higher compared to the city of Santa Maria.

Rents

The median rent in Los Osos is $1,520 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $145 (10%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $458 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Maria is roughly $106 (7%) higher.
