In comparison to other places in this area, Santa Monica is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Monica costs $1,382,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $538,680 (63%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,165,200 (535%) higher, and is approximately $745,800 (117%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Santa Monica's median rent is $1,802 per month. This is approximately $201 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $740 (69%) higher, and is approximately $352 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
