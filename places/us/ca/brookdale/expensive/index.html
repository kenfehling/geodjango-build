Home prices

The median home cost in Brookdale is $648,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $27,440 (4%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $431,400 (198%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $448,900 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brookdale is $909. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $646 (41%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $153 (14%) lower, and it is roughly $986 (52%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
