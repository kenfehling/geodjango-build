Home prices

The median home in Brookdale is valued at $648,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $27,440 (4%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $431,400 (198%) higher, and is approximately $448,900 (40%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Brookdale the median monthly rent is $909. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $646 (41%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $153 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $986 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
