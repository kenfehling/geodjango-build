Compared to other places in this area, Indio is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Diego.

Home prices

The median home in Indio is valued at $281,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $130,240 (31%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $63,900 (29%) higher, and is approximately $321,200 (53%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

Indio's median rent is $1,084 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $269 (19%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $22 (2%) higher, and is about $611 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
