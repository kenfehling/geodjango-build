Home prices

The median home in Orange is valued at $656,100. It is about $108,820 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $438,600 (201%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $19,200 (3%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Orange the median rent is $1,806 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $315 (14%) lower.It is about $744 (70%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $356 (24%) higher.
