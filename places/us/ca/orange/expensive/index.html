In comparison to the surrounding areas, Orange is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Orange costs $656,100. This is about $108,820 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $438,600 (201%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $19,200 (3%) higher.

Rents

Orange's median monthly rent is $1,806. It is approximately $315 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $744 (70%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $356 (24%) higher.
