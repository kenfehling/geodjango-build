Rents

The median rent in Concord is $1,716 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $352 (17%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $654 (61%) higher, and is approximately $179 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
