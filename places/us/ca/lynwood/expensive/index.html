Compared to its surrounding areas, Lynwood is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Lynwood's median home cost is $399,100. This is approximately $38,500 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $181,600 (83%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $237,800 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lynwood is $1,211. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $29 (2%) lower. It is approximately $149 (14%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $239 (16%) lower.
