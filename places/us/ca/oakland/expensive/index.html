Compared to other places in this area, Oakland is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of San Franciscohowever, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Oakland's median home price is $687,400. This is approximately $129,740 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $469,900 (216%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $410,400 (37%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Oakland's median monthly rent is $1,445. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $499 (25%) lower. It is about $383 (36%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $450 (23%) lower.
