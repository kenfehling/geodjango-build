In comparison to nearby areas, Oakland is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Oakland's median home price is $687,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $129,740 (15%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $469,900 (216%) higher, and is about $410,400 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakland is $1,445. It is approximately $499 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $383 (36%) higher, and is approximately $450 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
