Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakland is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $687,400. This is approximately $411,580 (37%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $469,900 (216%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $410,400 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakland is $1,445. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $814 (36%) lower.It is about $383 (36%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $450 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
