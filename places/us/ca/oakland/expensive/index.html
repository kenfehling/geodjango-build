Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakland is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $687,400. This is about $411,580 (37%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $469,900 (216%) higher, and is approximately $410,400 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Oakland's median rent is $1,445 per month. This is roughly $814 (36%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $383 (36%) higher, and it is approximately $450 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
