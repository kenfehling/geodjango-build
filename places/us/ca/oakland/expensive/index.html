In comparison to other places in this area, Oakland is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Oakland is $687,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $129,740 (15%) lower. It is about $469,900 (216%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $410,400 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Oakland the median monthly rent is $1,445. It is approximately $499 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $383 (36%) higher, and it is approximately $450 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
