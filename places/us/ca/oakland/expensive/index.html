In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oakland is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Oakland's median home cost is $687,400. It is about $129,740 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $469,900 (216%) higher, and it is approximately $410,400 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Oakland is $1,445 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $499 (25%) lower. It is about $383 (36%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $450 (23%) lower.
