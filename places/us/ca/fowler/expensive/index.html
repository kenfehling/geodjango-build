Home prices

The price of the median home in Fowler is $253,300. It is about $72,720 (40%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $35,800 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $11,300 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

The median rent in Fowler is $1,010 a month. This is about $118 (13%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $52 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fresno it is is very similar.
