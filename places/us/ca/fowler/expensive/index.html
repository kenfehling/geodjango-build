Home prices

The median home value in Fowler is $253,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $72,720 (40%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $35,800 (16%) higher, and is about $11,300 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

Fowler's median rent is $1,010 a month. It is about $118 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $52 (4%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fresno is is very similar.
