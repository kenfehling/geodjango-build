Home prices

The median home in Mountain View Acres is valued at $224,000. It is about $13,760 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $6,500 (2%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $412,900 (64%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Mountain View Acres the median monthly rent is $1,346. This is roughly $66 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $284 (26%) higher, and it is about $104 (7%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
