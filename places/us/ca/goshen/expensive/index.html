Home prices

The median home in Goshen is valued at $157,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $31,520 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $59,600 (27%) lower, and is approximately $84,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

The median rent in Goshen is $798 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $9 (1%) lower. It is approximately $264 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $207 (20%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.
