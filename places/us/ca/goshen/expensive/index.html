Home prices

The cost of the median home in Goshen is $157,900. It is about $31,520 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $59,600 (27%) lower, and it is roughly $84,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

Goshen's median rent is $798 a month. It is roughly $9 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $264 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is roughly $207 (20%) lower.
