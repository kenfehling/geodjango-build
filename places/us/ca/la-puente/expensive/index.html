In comparison to the surrounding areas, La Puente is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in La Puente costs $419,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $50,140 (10%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $202,300 (93%) higher, and it is approximately $217,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

La Puente's median rent is $1,410 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $35 (2%) lower. It is about $348 (32%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $40 (2%) lower.
