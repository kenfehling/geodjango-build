Compared to the surrounding areas, La Puente is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in La Puente is $419,800. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $50,140 (10%) lower.It is about $202,300 (93%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $217,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

La Puente's median monthly rent is $1,410. This is about $35 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $348 (32%) higher, and it is roughly $40 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
