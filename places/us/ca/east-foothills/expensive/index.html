In comparison to the surrounding areas, East Foothills is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Foothills costs $887,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $53,880 (6%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $670,200 (308%) higher, and is approximately $210,100 (19%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in East Foothills is $2,460 per month. This is about $394 (19%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,398 (131%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $565 (29%) higher.
