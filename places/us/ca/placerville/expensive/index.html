In comparison to the surrounding areas, Placerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Sacramento, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Placerville is $355,500. It is approximately $42,560 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $138,000 (63%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $18,600 (5%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Placerville is $1,099. It is about $183 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $37 (3%) higher, and it is roughly $164 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
