In comparison to other places in this area, Placerville is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Sacramento, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Placerville is $355,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $42,560 (10%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $138,000 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is roughly $18,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Placerville the median monthly rent is $1,099. It is roughly $183 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $37 (3%) higher, and is about $164 (12%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
