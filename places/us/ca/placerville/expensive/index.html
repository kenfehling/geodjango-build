Compared to the surrounding areas, Placerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Sacramento.

Home prices

Placerville's median home value is $355,500. It is roughly $42,560 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $138,000 (63%) higher, and is about $18,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

In Placerville the median rent is $1,099 per month. It is approximately $183 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $37 (3%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $164 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
