Home prices

The median home in South Pasadena is valued at $1,071,000. It is about $312,800 (41%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $853,500 (392%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $434,100 (68%) higher.

Rents

In South Pasadena the median rent is $1,802 per month. This is about $279 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $740 (69%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $352 (24%) higher.
