In comparison to nearby areas, Watsonville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Watsonville is $464,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $100,080 (17%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $246,700 (113%) higher, and it is about $633,600 (57%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Watsonville's median monthly rent is $1,409. This is about $274 (16%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $347 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $486 (25%) lower.
