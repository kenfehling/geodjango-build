In comparison to other places in this area, Watsonville is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home price in Watsonville is $464,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $100,080 (17%) lower. It is about $246,700 (113%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $633,600 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Watsonville the median monthly rent is $1,409. It is about $274 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $347 (32%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $486 (25%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
