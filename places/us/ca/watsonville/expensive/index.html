Home prices

The median home in Watsonville costs $464,200. It is approximately $100,080 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $246,700 (113%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $633,600 (57%) lower.

Rents

Watsonville's median monthly rent is $1,409. This is about $274 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $347 (32%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $486 (25%) lower.
