Home prices

The median home in Watsonville costs $464,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $100,080 (17%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $246,700 (113%) higher, and is roughly $633,600 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Watsonville the median rent is $1,409 a month. This is approximately $274 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $347 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $486 (25%) lower.
