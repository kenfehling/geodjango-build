In comparison to other places in this area, Milpitas is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milpitas is valued at $858,600. It is roughly $83,540 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $641,100 (294%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $239,200 (21%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milpitas is $2,428. This is roughly $120 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $1,366 (128%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $533 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
