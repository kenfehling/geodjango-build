Home prices

The median home in Milpitas is valued at $858,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $83,540 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $641,100 (294%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $239,200 (21%) lower.

Rents
