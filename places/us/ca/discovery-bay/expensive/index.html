Home prices

Discovery Bay's median home price is $615,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $117,940 (23%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $398,400 (183%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $481,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Discovery Bay the median monthly rent is $2,541. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $1,000 (64%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,479 (139%) higher, and is about $646 (34%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
