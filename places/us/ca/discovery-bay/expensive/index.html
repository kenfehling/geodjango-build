In comparison to the surrounding areas, Discovery Bay is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Discovery Bay is valued at $615,900. This is about $117,940 (23%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $398,400 (183%) higher, and it is about $481,900 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Discovery Bay is $2,541 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $1,000 (64%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $1,479 (139%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $646 (34%) higher.
