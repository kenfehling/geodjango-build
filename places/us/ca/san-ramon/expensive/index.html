Home prices

The median home in San Ramon is valued at $958,800. This is approximately $46,120 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $741,300 (340%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $139,000 (12%) lower.

Rents

In San Ramon the median monthly rent is $2,391. It is roughly $145 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $1,329 (125%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $496 (26%) higher.
