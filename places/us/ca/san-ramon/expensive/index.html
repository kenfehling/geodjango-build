Home prices

The median home price in San Ramon is $958,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $118,880 (11%) lower.It is roughly $741,300 (340%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $139,000 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In San Ramon the median rent is $2,391 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $76 (3%) lower.It is roughly $1,329 (125%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $496 (26%) higher.
