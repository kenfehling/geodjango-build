Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Ramon is $958,800. It is roughly $118,880 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $741,300 (340%) higher, and is approximately $139,000 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in San Ramon is $2,391 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $76 (3%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $1,329 (125%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $496 (26%) higher.
