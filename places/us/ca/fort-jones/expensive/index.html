Home prices

The median home in Fort Jones is valued at $169,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $21,320 (14%) higher.It is about $47,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $95,300 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Redding.

Rents

In Fort Jones the median rent is $886 a month. This is about $29 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $176 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Redding it is roughly $189 (17%) lower.
