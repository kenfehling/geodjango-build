Home prices

The median home in Fort Jones is valued at $169,600. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $21,320 (14%) higher. It is roughly $47,900 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Redding it is about $95,300 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Fort Jones the median rent is $886 a month. It is approximately $29 (3%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $176 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $189 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Redding.
