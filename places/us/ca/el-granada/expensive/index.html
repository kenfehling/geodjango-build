Compared to the surrounding areas, El Granada is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in El Granada is $1,122,200. This is approximately $102,540 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $904,700 (415%) higher, and is approximately $24,400 (2%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In El Granada the median rent is $2,202 a month. This is approximately $125 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,140 (107%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $307 (16%) higher.
