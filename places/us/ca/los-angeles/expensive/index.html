In comparison to the surrounding areas, Los Angeles is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

The median home price in Los Angeles is $636,900. This is about $293,267 (31%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $419,400 (192%) higher.

Rents

In Los Angeles the median rent is $1,450 a month. This is approximately $288 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $388 (36%) higher.
