Compared to its surrounding areas, Los Angeles is substantially less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Los Angeles is $636,900. This is roughly $498,284 (43%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $419,400 (192%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Los Angeles is $1,450. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $392 (21%) lower.It is about $388 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire nation.
