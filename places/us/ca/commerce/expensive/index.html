Home prices

The median home in Commerce is valued at $411,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $8,080 (1%) lower.It is about $193,700 (89%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $225,700 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Commerce the median rent is $1,139 a month. This is approximately $64 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $77 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $311 (21%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
