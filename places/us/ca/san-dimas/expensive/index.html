Compared to other places in this area, San Dimas is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Dimas is valued at $576,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $67,100 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $359,300 (165%) higher, and is roughly $60,100 (9%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Dimas the median rent is $1,808 a month. This is about $133 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $746 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $358 (24%) higher.
