In comparison to its surrounding areas, San Dimas is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Dimas is $576,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $67,100 (13%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $359,300 (165%) higher, and is about $60,100 (9%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Dimas the median rent is $1,808 a month. It is about $133 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $746 (70%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $358 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
