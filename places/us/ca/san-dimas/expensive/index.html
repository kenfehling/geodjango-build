Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Dimas is $576,800. It is approximately $67,100 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $359,300 (165%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $60,100 (9%) lower.

Rents

In San Dimas the median rent is $1,808 a month. This is about $133 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $746 (70%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $358 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
