In comparison to other places in this area, Marina del Rey is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Marina del Rey is valued at $791,700. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $238,300 (23%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $574,200 (264%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $154,800 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Marina del Rey is $3,011 per month. It is approximately $1,197 (66%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,949 (183%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $1,561 (107%) higher.
