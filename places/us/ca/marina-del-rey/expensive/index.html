In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marina del Rey is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Marina del Rey is valued at $791,700. This is roughly $238,300 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $574,200 (264%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $154,800 (24%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Marina del Rey the median rent is $3,011 a month. This is approximately $1,197 (66%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $1,949 (183%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $1,561 (107%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
