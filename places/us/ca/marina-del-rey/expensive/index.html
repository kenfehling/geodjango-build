Compared to the surrounding areas, Marina del Rey is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Marina del Rey is $791,700. This is about $238,300 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $574,200 (264%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $154,800 (24%) higher.

Rents

In Marina del Rey the median monthly rent is $3,011. This is roughly $1,197 (66%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,949 (183%) higher, and it is roughly $1,561 (107%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
