Home prices

The median home in Carmichael is valued at $380,200. It is roughly $17,500 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $162,700 (74%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $307,200 (44%) lower compared to the city of Oakland.

Rents

In Carmichael the median monthly rent is $1,178. This is about $148 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $116 (10%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oakland is roughly $267 (18%) lower.
