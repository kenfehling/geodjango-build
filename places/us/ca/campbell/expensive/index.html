In comparison to nearby areas, Campbell is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Campbell is $1,079,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $211,920 (16%) lower. It is roughly $861,500 (396%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $18,800 (1%) lower.

Rents

Campbell's median monthly rent is $2,233. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $70 (3%) lower. It is about $1,171 (110%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $338 (17%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
