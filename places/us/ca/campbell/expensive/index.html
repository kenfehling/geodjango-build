Home prices

Campbell's median home price is $1,079,000. It is about $211,920 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $861,500 (396%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $18,800 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Campbell is $2,233. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $70 (3%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,171 (110%) higher, and is about $338 (17%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
