Compared to its nearby areas, Woodlands is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Santa Barbara however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Woodlands is $786,700. It is about $255,000 (47%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $569,200 (261%) higher, and compared to the city of Santa Barbara it is about $264,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

Woodlands's median rent is $3,246 per month. It is approximately $1,687 (108%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $2,184 (205%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara is approximately $1,460 (81%) higher.
