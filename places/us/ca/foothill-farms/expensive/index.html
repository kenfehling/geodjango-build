Home prices

The cost of the median home in Foothill Farms is $252,900. Compared to other places in this area it is about $46,080 (15%) lower.It is roughly $35,400 (16%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $434,500 (63%) lower compared to the city of Oakland.

Rents

Foothill Farms's median rent is $1,218 per month. This is approximately $78 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $156 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $227 (15%) lower compared to the city of Oakland.
