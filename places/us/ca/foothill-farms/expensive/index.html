Home prices

The price of the median home in Foothill Farms is $252,900. It is approximately $46,080 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $35,400 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $434,500 (63%) lower compared to the city of Oakland.

Rents

In Foothill Farms the median monthly rent is $1,218. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $78 (5%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $156 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Oakland it is approximately $227 (15%) lower.
