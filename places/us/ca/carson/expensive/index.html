Home prices

Carson's median home price is $462,600. This is approximately $71,560 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $245,100 (112%) higher, and is approximately $174,300 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Carson the median rent is $1,524 a month. This is about $117 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $462 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $74 (5%) higher.
