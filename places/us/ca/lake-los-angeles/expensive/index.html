Home prices

The median home in Lake Los Angeles costs $180,200. It is approximately $70,880 (28%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $37,300 (17%) lower, and is approximately $456,700 (71%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Lake Los Angeles's median rent is $1,104 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $160 (12%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $42 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $346 (23%) lower.
