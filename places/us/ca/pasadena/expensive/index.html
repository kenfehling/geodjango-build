Compared to other places in this area, Pasadena is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Pasadena is $785,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $85,420 (9%) lower. It is approximately $568,200 (261%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $148,800 (23%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Pasadena the median rent is $1,710 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $67 (4%) higher. It is about $648 (61%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $260 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
