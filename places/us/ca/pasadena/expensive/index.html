In comparison to nearby areas, Pasadena is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pasadena is valued at $785,700. This is roughly $85,420 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $568,200 (261%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $148,800 (23%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Pasadena is $1,710 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $67 (4%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $648 (61%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $260 (17%) higher.
