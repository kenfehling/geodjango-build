Home prices

The median home in Santee costs $445,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $5,060 (1%) lower.It is roughly $228,000 (104%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $157,100 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

Santee's median rent is $1,744 per month. This is about $264 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $682 (64%) higher, and it is about $49 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of San Diego.
