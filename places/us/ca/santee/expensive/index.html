Much like its surrounding areas, it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Diego.

Home prices

Santee's median home price is $445,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $5,060 (1%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $228,000 (104%) higher, and is approximately $157,100 (26%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Santee is $1,744 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $264 (17%) higher. It is approximately $682 (64%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is approximately $49 (2%) higher.
