Home prices

The median home in Santee is valued at $445,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $5,060 (1%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $228,000 (104%) higher, and is roughly $157,100 (26%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Santee is $1,744 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $264 (17%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $682 (64%) higher, and is roughly $49 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of San Diego.
