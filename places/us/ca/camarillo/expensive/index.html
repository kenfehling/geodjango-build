Home prices

The median home in Camarillo is valued at $602,000. It is about $67,120 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $384,500 (176%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $34,900 (5%) lower.

Rents
