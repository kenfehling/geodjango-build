In comparison to its surrounding areas, Camarillo is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Camarillo is $602,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $67,120 (12%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $384,500 (176%) higher, and it is roughly $34,900 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Camarillo is $2,006 a month. It is approximately $95 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $944 (88%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $556 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
