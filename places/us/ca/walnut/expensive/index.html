Home prices

The median home in Walnut costs $774,400. This is approximately $281,800 (57%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $556,900 (256%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $137,500 (21%) higher.

Rents

Walnut's median rent is $2,359 a month. It is approximately $802 (51%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $1,297 (122%) higher, and is approximately $909 (62%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
