Home prices

The value of the median home in Mono Vista is $254,500. This is about $40,600 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $37,000 (17%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento is approximately $82,400 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Mono Vista the median monthly rent is $1,444. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $303 (26%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $382 (35%) higher, and it is approximately $181 (14%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
