In comparison to other places in this area, San Bruno is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in San Bruno is valued at $908,300. This is approximately $252,680 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $690,800 (317%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $189,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

In San Bruno the median rent is $2,372 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $79 (3%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,310 (123%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $477 (25%) higher.
