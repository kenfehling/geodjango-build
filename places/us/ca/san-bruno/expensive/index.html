Home prices

The median home cost in San Bruno is $908,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $252,680 (21%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $690,800 (317%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $189,500 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in San Bruno is $2,372 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $79 (3%) higher. It is about $1,310 (123%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $477 (25%) higher.
