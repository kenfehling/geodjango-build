Home prices

The median home in Forest Meadows costs $370,400. This is about $74,640 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $152,900 (70%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $33,500 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

In Forest Meadows the median rent is $1,461 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $352 (31%) higher. It is roughly $399 (37%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is about $198 (15%) higher.
