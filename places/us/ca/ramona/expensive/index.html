Home prices

Rents

Ramona's median monthly rent is $1,480. It is approximately $127 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $418 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is approximately $215 (12%) lower.
