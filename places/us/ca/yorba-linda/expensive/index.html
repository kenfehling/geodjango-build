Rents

The median rent in Yorba Linda is $2,079 per month. This is roughly $232 (12%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $1,017 (95%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $629 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
