In comparison to nearby areas, Palm Springs is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Diego.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Springs is valued at $389,800. It is approximately $91,580 (30%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $172,300 (79%) higher, and is roughly $212,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

Palm Springs's median rent is $1,114 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $118 (9%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $52 (4%) higher, and it is about $581 (34%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
