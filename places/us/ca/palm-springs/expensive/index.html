In comparison to its nearby areas, Palm Springs is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Diego, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Palm Springs is $389,800. This is approximately $91,580 (30%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $172,300 (79%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is roughly $212,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palm Springs is $1,114. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $118 (9%) lower.It is roughly $52 (4%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Diego it is roughly $581 (34%) lower.
