Compared to nearby areas, Palm Springs is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Diego however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Palm Springs is $389,800. It is approximately $91,580 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $172,300 (79%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Diego it is approximately $212,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palm Springs is $1,114. It is approximately $118 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $52 (4%) higher, and is roughly $581 (34%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
