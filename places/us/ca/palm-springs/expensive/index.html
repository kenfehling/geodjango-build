Compared to its surrounding areas, Palm Springs is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Diego, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Palm Springs is $389,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $91,580 (30%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $172,300 (79%) higher, and compared to the city of San Diego it is about $212,800 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Palm Springs is $1,114 a month. This is approximately $118 (9%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $52 (4%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Diego it is approximately $581 (34%) lower.
