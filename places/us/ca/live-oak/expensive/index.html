Home prices

The price of the median home in Live Oak is $203,400. This is approximately $23,540 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $14,100 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $133,500 (39%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median rent in Live Oak is $927 a month. This is approximately $57 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $135 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $336 (26%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
