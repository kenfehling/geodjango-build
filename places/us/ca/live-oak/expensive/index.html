Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Live Oak is $927 per month. This is roughly $57 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $135 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $336 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
