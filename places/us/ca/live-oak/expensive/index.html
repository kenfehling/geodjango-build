Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Live Oak is $927. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $57 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $135 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is about $336 (26%) lower.
