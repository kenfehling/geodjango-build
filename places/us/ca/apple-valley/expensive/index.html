Compared to its nearby areas, Apple Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home value in Apple Valley is $255,000. This is about $23,440 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $37,500 (17%) higher, and it is approximately $381,900 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Apple Valley the median rent is $1,060 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $277 (20%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $390 (26%) lower.
