Compared to other places in this area, Apple Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Apple Valley costs $255,000. This is about $23,440 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $37,500 (17%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $381,900 (59%) lower.

Rents

Apple Valley's median rent is $1,060 a month. This is roughly $277 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is is very similar, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $390 (26%) lower.
