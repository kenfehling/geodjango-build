Home prices

The value of the median home in Apple Valley is $255,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $23,440 (10%) higher.It is approximately $37,500 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $381,900 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Apple Valley is $1,060. This is roughly $277 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is is very similar compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $390 (26%) lower.
