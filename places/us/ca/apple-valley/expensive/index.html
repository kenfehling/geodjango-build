Home prices

The median home value in Apple Valley is $255,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $23,440 (10%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $37,500 (17%) higher, and it is roughly $381,900 (59%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Apple Valley the median rent is $1,060 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $277 (20%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is is very similar, and it is roughly $390 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
