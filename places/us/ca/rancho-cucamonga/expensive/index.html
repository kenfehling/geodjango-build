Compared to other places in this area, Rancho Cucamonga is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rancho Cucamonga is $495,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $43,280 (8%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $278,300 (127%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $141,100 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rancho Cucamonga is $1,777 per month. This is approximately $318 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $715 (67%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $327 (22%) higher.
