Home prices

The value of the median home in Rancho Cucamonga is $495,800. It is approximately $43,280 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $278,300 (127%) higher, and it is roughly $141,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Rancho Cucamonga's median rent is $1,777 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $318 (21%) higher.It is about $715 (67%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $327 (22%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
