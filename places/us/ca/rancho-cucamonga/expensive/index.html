Compared to its surrounding areas, Rancho Cucamonga is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Rancho Cucamonga's median home value is $495,800. It is approximately $43,280 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $278,300 (127%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $141,100 (22%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Rancho Cucamonga the median monthly rent is $1,777. This is about $318 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $715 (67%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $327 (22%) higher.
