Compared to its nearby areas, Rancho Cucamonga is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Rancho Cucamonga's median home value is $495,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $43,280 (8%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $278,300 (127%) higher, and it is about $141,100 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Rancho Cucamonga's median rent is $1,777 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $318 (21%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $715 (67%) higher, and it is roughly $327 (22%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
