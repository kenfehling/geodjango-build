Compared to its surrounding areas, Rancho Cucamonga is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho Cucamonga costs $495,800. It is about $43,280 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $278,300 (127%) higher, and it is about $141,100 (22%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Rancho Cucamonga the median rent is $1,777 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $318 (21%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $715 (67%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $327 (22%) higher.
