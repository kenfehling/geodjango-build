Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Easton is $1,113. This is about $175 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $51 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is about $108 (10%) higher.
