Home prices

The median home in Jurupa Valley is valued at $363,000. It is roughly $62,280 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $145,500 (66%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $273,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Jurupa Valley the median rent is $1,324 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $372 (21%) lower. It is roughly $262 (24%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $126 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
