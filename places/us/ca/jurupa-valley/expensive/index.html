Home prices

Jurupa Valley's median home value is $363,000. This is approximately $62,280 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $145,500 (66%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $273,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jurupa Valley is $1,324. This is about $372 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $262 (24%) higher, and it is about $126 (8%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
