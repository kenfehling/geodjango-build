Compared to its nearby areas, East Pasadena is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in East Pasadena is $829,900. It is about $69,000 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $612,400 (281%) higher, and is roughly $193,000 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In East Pasadena the median rent is $1,741 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $162 (10%) higher. It is about $679 (63%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $291 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
