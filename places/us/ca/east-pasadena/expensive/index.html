Home prices

The median home cost in East Pasadena is $829,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $325,940 (28%) lower.It is roughly $612,400 (281%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $193,000 (30%) higher.

Rents

East Pasadena's median rent is $1,741 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $169 (8%) lower.It is approximately $679 (63%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $291 (20%) higher.
