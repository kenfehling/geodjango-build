Compared to the surrounding areas, East Pasadena is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

East Pasadena's median home cost is $829,900. It is roughly $69,000 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $612,400 (281%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $193,000 (30%) higher.

Rents

In East Pasadena the median monthly rent is $1,741. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $162 (10%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $679 (63%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $291 (20%) higher.
