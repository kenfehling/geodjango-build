Compared to the surrounding areas, Oak Park is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park is valued at $760,200. This is roughly $151,840 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $542,700 (249%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $123,300 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Oak Park the median monthly rent is $2,489. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $1,427 (134%) higher, and it is approximately $1,039 (71%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
