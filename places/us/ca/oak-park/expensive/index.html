In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oak Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park is valued at $760,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $151,840 (16%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $542,700 (249%) higher, and is roughly $123,300 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Oak Park the median rent is $2,489 per month. This is is very similar compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $1,427 (134%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $1,039 (71%) higher.
