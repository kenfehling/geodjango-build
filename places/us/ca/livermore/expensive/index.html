Compared to the surrounding areas, Livermore is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Livermore costs $744,200. This is roughly $325,200 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $526,700 (242%) higher, and it is about $353,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Livermore is $2,063 per month. This is roughly $127 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,001 (94%) higher, and it is roughly $168 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
