In comparison to nearby areas, Livermore is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Livermore is $744,200. This is about $325,200 (30%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $526,700 (242%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $353,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

Livermore's median monthly rent is $2,063. It is roughly $127 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $1,001 (94%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $168 (8%) higher.
