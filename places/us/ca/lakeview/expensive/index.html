Compared to its nearby areas, Lakeview is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeview is valued at $346,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $82,680 (31%) higher. It is roughly $128,600 (59%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $290,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakeview is $1,048. It is about $243 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $14 (1%) lower, and is roughly $402 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
