Home prices

The median home in Lakeview is valued at $346,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $82,680 (31%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $128,600 (59%) higher, and it is approximately $290,800 (45%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Lakeview the median rent is $1,048 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $243 (18%) lower.It is approximately $14 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $402 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
