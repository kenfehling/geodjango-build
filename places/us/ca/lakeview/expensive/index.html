Home prices

Rents

Lakeview's median monthly rent is $1,048. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $243 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $14 (1%) lower, and it is about $402 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
