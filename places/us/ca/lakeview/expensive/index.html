In comparison to other places in this area, Lakeview is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeview costs $346,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $82,680 (31%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $128,600 (59%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $290,800 (45%) lower.

Rents

Lakeview's median monthly rent is $1,048. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $243 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $14 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $402 (27%) lower.
