Home prices

Pleasanton's median home cost is $986,800. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $77,260 (8%) higher.It is about $769,300 (353%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $111,000 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Pleasanton is $2,396 per month. This is roughly $207 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $1,334 (125%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $501 (26%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
