Home prices

The price of the median home in Pleasanton is $986,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $77,260 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $769,300 (353%) higher, and it is approximately $111,000 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Pleasanton's median monthly rent is $2,396. This is approximately $207 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,334 (125%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $501 (26%) higher.
