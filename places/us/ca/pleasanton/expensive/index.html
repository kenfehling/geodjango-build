Compared to other places in this area, Pleasanton is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Pleasanton is valued at $986,800. It is approximately $77,260 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $769,300 (353%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $111,000 (10%) lower.

Rents

Pleasanton's median rent is $2,396 a month. This is about $207 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $1,334 (125%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $501 (26%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
