Home prices

The median home in Garden Grove is valued at $554,400. This is roughly $16,860 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $336,900 (154%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $82,500 (12%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Garden Grove is $1,590. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $528 (49%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $140 (9%) higher.
