Home prices

The price of the median home in Garden Grove is $554,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,860 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $336,900 (154%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $82,500 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Garden Grove the median monthly rent is $1,590. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $528 (49%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $140 (9%) higher.
