Home prices

The median home price in Arnold is $293,000. This is roughly $18,240 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $75,500 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $43,900 (13%) lower.

Rents

Arnold's median rent is $1,311 a month. This is about $172 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $249 (23%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $48 (3%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
