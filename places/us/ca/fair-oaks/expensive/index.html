Home prices

The median home in Fair Oaks costs $476,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $61,340 (14%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $258,600 (118%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Oakland it is roughly $211,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Fair Oaks the median rent is $1,339 a month. This is approximately $157 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $277 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Oakland is about $106 (7%) lower.
