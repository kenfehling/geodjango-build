Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fair Oaks is $1,339. It is approximately $157 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $277 (26%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $106 (7%) lower compared to the city of Oakland.
