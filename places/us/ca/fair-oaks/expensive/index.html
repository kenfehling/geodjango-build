Home prices

Fair Oaks's median home value is $476,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $61,340 (14%) higher. It is about $258,600 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Oakland is about $211,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fair Oaks is $1,339 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $157 (10%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $277 (26%) higher, and compared to the city of Oakland is roughly $106 (7%) lower.
