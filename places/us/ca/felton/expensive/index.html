Home prices

The median home value in Felton is $632,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $208,040 (24%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $415,400 (190%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $464,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Felton the median rent is $1,818 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $310 (20%) higher.It is roughly $756 (71%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $77 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
