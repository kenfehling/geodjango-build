Compared to other places in this area, Felton is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Felton is valued at $632,900. It is roughly $208,040 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $415,400 (190%) higher, and it is roughly $464,900 (42%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Felton is $1,818. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $310 (20%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $756 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $77 (4%) lower.
