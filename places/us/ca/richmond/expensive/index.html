In comparison to the surrounding areas, Richmond is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $462,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $45,020 (8%) lower.It is roughly $245,100 (112%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $635,200 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richmond is $1,509. It is about $254 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $447 (42%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $386 (20%) lower.
