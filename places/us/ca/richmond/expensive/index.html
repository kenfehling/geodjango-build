In comparison to nearby areas, Richmond is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $462,600. It is about $45,020 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $245,100 (112%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $635,200 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond is $1,509 a month. This is approximately $254 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $447 (42%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $386 (20%) lower.
