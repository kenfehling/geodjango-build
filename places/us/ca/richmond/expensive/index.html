Compared to nearby areas, Richmond is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Richmond is $462,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $45,020 (8%) lower.It is roughly $245,100 (112%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $635,200 (57%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Richmond is $1,509. It is about $254 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $447 (42%) higher, and is about $386 (20%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
