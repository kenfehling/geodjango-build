In comparison to the surrounding areas, Richmond is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home value in Richmond is $462,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $45,020 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $245,100 (112%) higher, and is about $635,200 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Richmond the median rent is $1,509 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $254 (14%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $447 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $386 (20%) lower.
