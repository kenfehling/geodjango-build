Compared to its nearby areas, Richmond is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Richmond is valued at $462,600. It is about $45,020 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $245,100 (112%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $635,200 (57%) lower.

Rents

Richmond's median monthly rent is $1,509. This is roughly $254 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $447 (42%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $386 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
