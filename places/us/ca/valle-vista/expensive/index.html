Compared to nearby areas, Valle Vista is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Diego.

Home prices

The median home in Valle Vista costs $184,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $57,240 (23%) lower. It is approximately $32,900 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Diego it is approximately $418,000 (69%) lower.

Rents

Valle Vista's median rent is $1,173 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $95 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $111 (10%) higher, and is roughly $522 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
