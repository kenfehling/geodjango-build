Home prices

The median home value in Valle Vista is $184,600. It is roughly $57,240 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $32,900 (15%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Diego is approximately $418,000 (69%) lower.

Rents

Valle Vista's median monthly rent is $1,173. This is roughly $95 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $111 (10%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $522 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
