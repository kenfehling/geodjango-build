Home prices

The median home value in Indian Wells is $722,500. It is approximately $372,880 (106%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $505,000 (232%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Diego it is approximately $119,900 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Indian Wells the median rent is $788 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $712 (47%) lower.It is about $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is about $907 (53%) lower.
