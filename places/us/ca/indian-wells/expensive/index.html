In comparison to other places in this area, Indian Wells is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of San Diego, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Indian Wells is valued at $722,500. This is about $372,880 (106%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $505,000 (232%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is approximately $119,900 (19%) higher.

Rents

Indian Wells's median rent is $788 per month. It is about $712 (47%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $274 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of San Diego is approximately $907 (53%) lower.
