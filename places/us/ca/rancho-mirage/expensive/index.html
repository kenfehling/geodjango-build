Home prices

The median home in Rancho Mirage costs $498,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $113,940 (29%) higher. It is approximately $281,200 (129%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $103,900 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

Rancho Mirage's median rent is $1,191 a month. It is about $80 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $129 (12%) higher, and is approximately $504 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
