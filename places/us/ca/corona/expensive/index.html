Home prices

The median home in Corona costs $467,000. This is approximately $5,620 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $249,500 (114%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $169,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Corona is $1,641 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $303 (15%) lower.It is roughly $579 (54%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $191 (13%) higher.
