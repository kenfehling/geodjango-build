Home prices

The median home price in Corona is $467,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $5,620 (1%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $249,500 (114%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $169,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Corona is $1,641 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $303 (15%) lower.It is about $579 (54%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $191 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
