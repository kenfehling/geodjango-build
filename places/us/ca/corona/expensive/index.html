Home prices

The cost of the median home in Corona is $467,000. This is roughly $5,620 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $249,500 (114%) higher, and it is about $169,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corona is $1,641. This is about $303 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $579 (54%) higher, and it is about $191 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
