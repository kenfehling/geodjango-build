In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mountain View is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Mountain View's median home value is $421,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $158,060 (27%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $204,000 (93%) higher, and it is about $676,300 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Mountain View the median rent is $1,529 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $478 (23%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $467 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $366 (19%) lower.
