Compared to its surrounding areas, Mountain View is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Mountain View costs $421,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $158,060 (27%) lower. It is approximately $204,000 (93%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $676,300 (61%) lower.

Rents

Mountain View's median monthly rent is $1,529. It is roughly $478 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $467 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $366 (19%) lower.
