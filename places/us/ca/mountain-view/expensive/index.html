In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mountain View is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home value in Mountain View is $421,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $158,060 (27%) lower. It is roughly $204,000 (93%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $676,300 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mountain View is $1,529. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $478 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $467 (43%) higher, and is roughly $366 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
