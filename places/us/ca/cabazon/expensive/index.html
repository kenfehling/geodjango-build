Compared to its surrounding areas, Cabazon is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Diego, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Cabazon's median home value is $190,600. This is approximately $53,080 (21%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $26,900 (12%) lower, and it is roughly $412,000 (68%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Cabazon is $877 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $221 (20%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $185 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $818 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
