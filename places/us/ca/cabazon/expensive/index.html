Home prices

Rents

Cabazon's median rent is $877 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $221 (20%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $185 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is roughly $818 (48%) lower.
