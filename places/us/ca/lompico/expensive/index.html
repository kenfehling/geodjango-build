In comparison to other places in this area, Lompico is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lompico costs $538,700. This is about $149,080 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $321,200 (147%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $559,100 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lompico is $2,153. This is roughly $686 (46%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $1,091 (102%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $258 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
