In comparison to the surrounding areas, Redondo Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Redondo Beach is $926,500. It is about $118,700 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $709,000 (325%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $289,600 (45%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Redondo Beach's median rent is $2,072 a month. This is about $299 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $1,010 (95%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $622 (42%) higher.
