Home prices

The value of the median home in Redondo Beach is $926,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $155,020 (14%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $709,000 (325%) higher, and is approximately $289,600 (45%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Redondo Beach is $2,072. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $225 (12%) higher.It is roughly $1,010 (95%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $622 (42%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
