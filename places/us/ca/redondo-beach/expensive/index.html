In comparison to other places in this area, Redondo Beach is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Redondo Beach is $926,500. This is about $118,700 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $709,000 (325%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $289,600 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Redondo Beach is $2,072. It is approximately $299 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $1,010 (95%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $622 (42%) higher.
