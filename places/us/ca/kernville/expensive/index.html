Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Kernville is $672 per month. It is approximately $164 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $390 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield is about $432 (39%) lower.
