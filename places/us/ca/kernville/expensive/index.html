Home prices

Rents

Kernville's median monthly rent is $672. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $164 (19%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $390 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield is roughly $432 (39%) lower.
