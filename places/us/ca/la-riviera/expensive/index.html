Rents

La Riviera's median monthly rent is $1,357. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $74 (5%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $295 (27%) higher, and is approximately $538 (28%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
