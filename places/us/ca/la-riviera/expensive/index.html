Home prices

The median home in La Riviera is valued at $290,800. It is about $49,420 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $73,300 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $807,000 (73%) lower.

Rents

La Riviera's median monthly rent is $1,357. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $74 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $295 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $538 (28%) lower.
