In comparison to other places in this area, Beaumont is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in Beaumont is $320,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $57,380 (21%) higher. It is about $103,000 (47%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $316,400 (49%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Beaumont is $1,320 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $243 (22%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $258 (24%) higher, and is roughly $130 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
