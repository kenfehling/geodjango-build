Home prices

The median home in Beaumont is valued at $320,500. This is approximately $57,380 (21%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $103,000 (47%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $316,400 (49%) lower.

Rents

Beaumont's median monthly rent is $1,320. This is roughly $243 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $258 (24%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $130 (8%) lower.
