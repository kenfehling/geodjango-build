In comparison to its nearby areas, Beaumont is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in Beaumont is $320,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $57,380 (21%) higher. It is roughly $103,000 (47%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $316,400 (49%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Beaumont's median monthly rent is $1,320. This is about $243 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $258 (24%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $130 (8%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
