In comparison to the surrounding areas, Beaumont is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Beaumont costs $320,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $57,380 (21%) higher.It is roughly $103,000 (47%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $316,400 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Beaumont is $1,320. It is about $243 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $258 (24%) higher, and it is roughly $130 (8%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
