Home prices

The median home value in Irvine is $838,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $43,680 (4%) lower. It is roughly $620,500 (285%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $201,100 (31%) higher.

Rents

Irvine's median rent is $2,361 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $377 (19%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $1,299 (122%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $911 (62%) higher.
