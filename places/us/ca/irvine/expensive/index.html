Home prices

The median home in Irvine is valued at $838,000. It is about $43,680 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $620,500 (285%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $201,100 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Irvine the median rent is $2,361 a month. This is roughly $377 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $1,299 (122%) higher, and it is roughly $911 (62%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
