Home prices

The median home cost in Irvine is $838,000. It is about $43,680 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $620,500 (285%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $201,100 (31%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Irvine is $2,361 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $377 (19%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $1,299 (122%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $911 (62%) higher.
