Rents

In Lake of the Woods the median rent is $722 per month. It is about $323 (30%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $340 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $728 (50%) lower.
