Compared to other places in this area, Rio Linda is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Rio Linda's median home cost is $274,200. This is approximately $22,860 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $56,700 (26%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $823,600 (75%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Rio Linda's median rent is $1,305 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $243 (22%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $590 (31%) lower.
