Home prices

The median home in Rio Linda is valued at $274,200. This is approximately $22,860 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $56,700 (26%) higher, and is roughly $823,600 (75%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Rio Linda's median rent is $1,305 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $243 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $590 (31%) lower.
