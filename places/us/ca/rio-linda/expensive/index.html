Compared to the surrounding areas, Rio Linda is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Rio Linda is $274,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $22,860 (7%) lower.It is about $56,700 (26%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $823,600 (75%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Rio Linda is $1,305 a month. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $243 (22%) higher, and is roughly $590 (31%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
