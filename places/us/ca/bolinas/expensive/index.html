Home prices

The median home cost in Bolinas is $1,094,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $62,420 (5%) lower. It is roughly $876,900 (403%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Bolinas the median monthly rent is $1,694. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $167 (8%) lower. It is roughly $632 (59%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $201 (10%) lower.
