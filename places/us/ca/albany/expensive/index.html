In comparison to its nearby areas, Albany is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Albany's median home price is $882,500. It is approximately $110,720 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $665,000 (305%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $215,300 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Albany's median monthly rent is $1,856. It is about $310 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $794 (74%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $39 (2%) lower.
