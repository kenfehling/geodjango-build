In comparison to its surrounding areas, Albany is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Albany's median home price is $882,500. This is approximately $110,720 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $665,000 (305%) higher, and it is roughly $215,300 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Albany the median monthly rent is $1,856. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $310 (14%) lower. It is about $794 (74%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $39 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
