In comparison to its surrounding areas, Albany is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Albany is $882,500. This is roughly $110,720 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $665,000 (305%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $215,300 (19%) lower.

Rents

Albany's median rent is $1,856 per month. This is roughly $310 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $794 (74%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $39 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
