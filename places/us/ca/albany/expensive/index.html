In comparison to nearby areas, Albany is a little more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Albany is $882,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $110,720 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $665,000 (305%) higher, and it is about $215,300 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Albany is $1,856 per month. This is roughly $310 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $794 (74%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $39 (2%) lower.
