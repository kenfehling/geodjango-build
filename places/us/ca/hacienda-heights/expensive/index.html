Home prices

Hacienda Heights's median home value is $595,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $27,120 (4%) higher.It is roughly $378,200 (173%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $41,200 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hacienda Heights is $1,882 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $517 (37%) higher.It is approximately $820 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $432 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
