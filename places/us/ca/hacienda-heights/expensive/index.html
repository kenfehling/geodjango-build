The cost of the median home in Hacienda Heights is $595,700. It is roughly $27,120 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $378,200 (173%) higher, and is roughly $41,200 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Hacienda Heights the median rent is $1,882 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $517 (37%) higher. It is approximately $820 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $432 (29%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
