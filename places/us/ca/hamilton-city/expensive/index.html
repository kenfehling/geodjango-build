Home prices

The median home in Hamilton City costs $166,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $108,760 (39%) lower. It is approximately $51,000 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is about $170,400 (50%) lower.

Rents

Hamilton City's median monthly rent is $715. It is about $337 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $347 (32%) lower, and is about $548 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
