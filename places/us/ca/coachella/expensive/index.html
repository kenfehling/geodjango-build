Home prices

The median home price in Coachella is $219,400. This is roughly $91,840 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is is very similar compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is approximately $383,200 (63%) lower.

Rents

Coachella's median monthly rent is $879. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $475 (35%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $183 (17%) lower, and it is roughly $816 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
