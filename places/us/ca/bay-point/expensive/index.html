Home prices

The median home in Bay Point costs $302,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $101,320 (25%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $85,400 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $794,900 (72%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bay Point is $1,486 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $262 (14%) lower.It is roughly $424 (39%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $409 (21%) lower.
