In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bay Point is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Bay Point costs $302,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $101,320 (25%) lower.It is approximately $85,400 (39%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $794,900 (72%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Bay Point the median monthly rent is $1,486. It is about $262 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $424 (39%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $409 (21%) lower.
