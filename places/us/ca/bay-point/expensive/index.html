Compared to its nearby areas, Bay Point is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Bay Point is valued at $302,900. This is approximately $101,320 (25%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $85,400 (39%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $794,900 (72%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bay Point is $1,486 a month. It is roughly $262 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $424 (39%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $409 (21%) lower.
