Home prices

The median home price in Del Monte Forest is $1,356,500. This is about $489,640 (56%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $1,139,000 (523%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $258,700 (23%) higher.

Rents

Del Monte Forest's median monthly rent is $2,914. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $956 (48%) higher.It is approximately $1,852 (174%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $1,019 (53%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
