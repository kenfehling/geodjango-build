Compared to the surrounding areas, Del Monte Forest is a lot more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Del Monte Forest is $1,356,500. This is about $489,640 (56%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,139,000 (523%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $258,700 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Del Monte Forest the median rent is $2,914 per month. This is roughly $956 (48%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $1,852 (174%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $1,019 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
