Compared to its surrounding areas, Del Monte Forest is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of San Francisco, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Del Monte Forest costs $1,356,500. It is about $489,640 (56%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $1,139,000 (523%) higher, and it is about $258,700 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Del Monte Forest the median rent is $2,914 per month. This is approximately $956 (48%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,852 (174%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $1,019 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
