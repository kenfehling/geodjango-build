Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ripon is $452,000. Compared to other places in this area it is about $129,120 (39%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $234,500 (107%) higher, and it is roughly $645,800 (58%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Ripon the median monthly rent is $1,466. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $61 (4%) higher. It is roughly $404 (38%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $429 (22%) lower.
