The price of the median home in Ripon is $452,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $129,120 (39%) higher. It is approximately $234,500 (107%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $645,800 (58%) lower.

Rents
