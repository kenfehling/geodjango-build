Home prices

The median home in Borrego Springs costs $186,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $10,400 (5%) lower. It is approximately $31,100 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Diego is about $416,200 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Borrego Springs is $733 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $135 (15%) lower. It is roughly $329 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $962 (56%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
