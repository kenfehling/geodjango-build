Home prices

Rents

In Big Bear City the median monthly rent is $1,113. This is approximately $114 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $51 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is approximately $582 (34%) lower.
