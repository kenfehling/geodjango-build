In comparison to other places in this area, South Lake Tahoe is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Reno.

Home prices

The median home in South Lake Tahoe is valued at $403,600. This is about $363,200 (47%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $186,100 (85%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is roughly $68,600 (20%) higher.

Rents

South Lake Tahoe's median rent is $1,020 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $561 (35%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $42 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Reno it is is very similar.
