In comparison to its nearby areas, South Lake Tahoe is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Reno, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in South Lake Tahoe is $403,600. It is about $363,200 (47%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $186,100 (85%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Reno it is roughly $68,600 (20%) higher.

Rents

South Lake Tahoe's median monthly rent is $1,020. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $561 (35%) lower. It is approximately $42 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Reno.
