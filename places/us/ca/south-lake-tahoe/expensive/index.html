Home prices

The median home cost in South Lake Tahoe is $403,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $363,200 (47%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $186,100 (85%) higher, and it is approximately $68,600 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Reno.

Rents

In South Lake Tahoe the median monthly rent is $1,020. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $561 (35%) lower. It is about $42 (3%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Reno.
