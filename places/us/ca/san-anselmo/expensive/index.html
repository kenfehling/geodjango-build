Compared to its surrounding areas, San Anselmo is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in San Anselmo is $1,066,800. This is approximately $349,040 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $849,300 (390%) higher, and is roughly $31,000 (2%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in San Anselmo is $1,930 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $223 (10%) lower.It is about $868 (81%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $35 (1%) higher.
