In comparison to the surrounding areas, San Anselmo is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home value in San Anselmo is $1,066,800. It is about $108,740 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $849,300 (390%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $31,000 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In San Anselmo the median monthly rent is $1,930. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $28 (1%) higher. It is roughly $868 (81%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $35 (1%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
