Home prices

San Anselmo's median home price is $1,066,800. This is about $349,040 (24%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $849,300 (390%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $31,000 (2%) lower.

Rents

San Anselmo's median monthly rent is $1,930. It is approximately $223 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $868 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $35 (1%) higher.
