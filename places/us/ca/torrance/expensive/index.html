Compared to its surrounding areas, Torrance is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

Torrance's median home value is $762,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $63,620 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $545,200 (250%) higher, and it is roughly $125,800 (19%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Torrance the median monthly rent is $1,736. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $89 (5%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $674 (63%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $286 (19%) higher.
