Compared to other places in this area, Torrance is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Torrance's median home price is $762,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $63,620 (7%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $545,200 (250%) higher, and it is approximately $125,800 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Torrance the median rent is $1,736 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $89 (5%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $674 (63%) higher, and it is about $286 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
