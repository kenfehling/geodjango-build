Compared to nearby areas, Torrance is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Torrance's median home cost is $762,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $63,620 (7%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $545,200 (250%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $125,800 (19%) higher.

Rents

In Torrance the median rent is $1,736 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $89 (5%) higher. It is roughly $674 (63%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $286 (19%) higher.
