Home prices

The median home value in West Modesto is $149,700. This is roughly $45,080 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $67,800 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $714,900 (82%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

The median rent in West Modesto is $1,029 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $30 (2%) lower. It is approximately $33 (3%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $1,078 (51%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
