In comparison to its nearby areas, West Modesto is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Jose.

Home prices

The median home cost in West Modesto is $149,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $45,080 (23%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $67,800 (31%) lower, and is about $714,900 (82%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

In West Modesto the median rent is $1,029 per month. This is about $30 (2%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $33 (3%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Jose it is about $1,078 (51%) lower.
