Compared to other places in this area, Belmont is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's substantially more expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Belmont costs $1,527,500. This is roughly $130,560 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $1,310,000 (602%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $429,700 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Belmont the median rent is $2,342 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $226 (8%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $1,280 (120%) higher, and is roughly $447 (23%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
