Compared to its surrounding areas, Belmont is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Belmont costs $1,527,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $130,560 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,310,000 (602%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $429,700 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Belmont the median monthly rent is $2,342. It is roughly $226 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,280 (120%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $447 (23%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
