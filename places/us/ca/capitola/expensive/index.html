Compared to other places in this area, Capitola is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Capitola's median home cost is $721,900. It is approximately $79,540 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $504,400 (231%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $375,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Capitola the median rent is $1,780 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $51 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $718 (67%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $115 (6%) lower.
