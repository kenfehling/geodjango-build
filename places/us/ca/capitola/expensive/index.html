Home prices

The median home in Capitola costs $721,900. This is approximately $79,540 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $504,400 (231%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $375,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Capitola the median monthly rent is $1,780. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $51 (2%) higher.It is roughly $718 (67%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $115 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
