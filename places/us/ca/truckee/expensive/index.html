Home prices

Truckee's median home price is $593,400. It is approximately $65,020 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $375,900 (172%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $258,400 (77%) higher in comparison to the city of Reno.

Rents

Truckee's median monthly rent is $1,759. This is about $383 (27%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $697 (65%) higher, and compared to the city of Reno it is approximately $730 (70%) higher.
