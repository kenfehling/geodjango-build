Home prices

The median home value in Truckee is $593,400. It is roughly $65,020 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $375,900 (172%) higher, and is about $258,400 (77%) higher in comparison to the city of Reno.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Truckee is $1,759. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $383 (27%) higher. It is roughly $697 (65%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $730 (70%) higher compared to the city of Reno.
