In comparison to other places in this area, Alta is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of Sacramento.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Alta is $384,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $57,980 (17%) higher. It is about $166,600 (76%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is about $47,200 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Alta is $1,453 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $226 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $391 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is about $190 (15%) higher.
