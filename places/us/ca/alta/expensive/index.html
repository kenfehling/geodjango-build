Compared to its nearby areas, Alta is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Sacramento.

Home prices

Alta's median home price is $384,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $57,980 (17%) higher. It is about $166,600 (76%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sacramento is roughly $47,200 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Alta the median monthly rent is $1,453. This is about $226 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $391 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is approximately $190 (15%) higher.
