Home prices

The median home in Alta costs $384,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $57,980 (17%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $166,600 (76%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento is roughly $47,200 (14%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Alta is $1,453 per month. It is about $226 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $391 (36%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $190 (15%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
