Home prices

The price of the median home in Granite Hills is $650,700. It is approximately $190,640 (41%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $433,200 (199%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is approximately $48,100 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Granite Hills is $2,380. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $849 (55%) higher. It is roughly $1,318 (124%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is roughly $685 (40%) higher.
