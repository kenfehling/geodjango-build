Home prices

The median home cost in Westmont is $419,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $43,300 (9%) lower.It is approximately $202,400 (93%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $217,000 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Westmont the median rent is $1,185 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $60 (4%) lower.It is roughly $123 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $265 (18%) lower.
