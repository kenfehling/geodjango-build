Compared to other places in this area, Westlake Village is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Westlake Village is $953,700. This is about $159,420 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $736,200 (338%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $316,800 (49%) higher.

Rents

In Westlake Village the median rent is $2,900 per month. This is approximately $486 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $1,838 (173%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $1,450 (100%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
