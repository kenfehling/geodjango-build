Home prices

The median home cost in Westlake Village is $953,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $159,420 (20%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $736,200 (338%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $316,800 (49%) higher.

Rents

In Westlake Village the median monthly rent is $2,900. It is approximately $486 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $1,838 (173%) higher, and it is about $1,450 (100%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
