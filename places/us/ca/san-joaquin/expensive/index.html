Home prices

The median home in San Joaquin costs $164,900. This is approximately $14,260 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $52,600 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Fresno is roughly $77,100 (31%) lower.

Rents

In San Joaquin the median monthly rent is $675. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $221 (24%) lower. It is approximately $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $330 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.
