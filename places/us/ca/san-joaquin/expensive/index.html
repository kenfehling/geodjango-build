Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Joaquin is $164,900. It is approximately $14,260 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $52,600 (24%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $77,100 (31%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.

Rents

San Joaquin's median rent is $675 per month. This is about $221 (24%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $387 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $330 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.
