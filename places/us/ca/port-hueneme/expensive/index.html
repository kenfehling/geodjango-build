Compared to its surrounding areas, Port Hueneme is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Port Hueneme is $412,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $161,140 (28%) lower. It is about $194,700 (89%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $224,700 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Port Hueneme is $1,640 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $212 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $578 (54%) higher, and it is approximately $190 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
