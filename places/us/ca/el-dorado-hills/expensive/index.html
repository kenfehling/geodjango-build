Home prices

The median home price in El Dorado Hills is $630,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $106,240 (20%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $412,500 (189%) higher, and it is approximately $293,100 (86%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

El Dorado Hills's median rent is $1,964 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $451 (29%) higher. It is approximately $902 (84%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $701 (55%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
