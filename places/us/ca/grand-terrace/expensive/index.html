Home prices

The median home cost in Grand Terrace is $324,400. This is about $13,120 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $106,900 (49%) higher, and it is about $312,500 (49%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Grand Terrace the median rent is $1,405 per month. This is roughly $167 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $343 (32%) higher, and is about $45 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
