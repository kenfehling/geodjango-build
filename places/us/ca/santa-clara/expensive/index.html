Compared to other places in this area, Santa Clara is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Santa Clara's median home cost is $1,034,000. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $98,780 (8%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $816,500 (375%) higher, and is roughly $63,800 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Clara is $2,396 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $192 (8%) higher. It is about $1,334 (125%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $501 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
