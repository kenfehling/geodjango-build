In comparison to its nearby areas, Santa Clara is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home value in Santa Clara is $1,034,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $98,780 (8%) lower.It is roughly $816,500 (375%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $63,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

Santa Clara's median monthly rent is $2,396. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $192 (8%) higher.It is roughly $1,334 (125%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $501 (26%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
