Compared to the surrounding areas, Cameron Park is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Sacramento, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Cameron Park's median home price is $438,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $23,580 (5%) lower. It is approximately $221,200 (101%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $101,800 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Cameron Park is $1,393 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $65 (4%) lower. It is approximately $331 (31%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $130 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
