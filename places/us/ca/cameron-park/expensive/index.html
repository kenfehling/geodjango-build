Home prices

Rents

In Cameron Park the median monthly rent is $1,393. It is approximately $65 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $331 (31%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento is about $130 (10%) higher.
