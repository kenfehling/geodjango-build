Rents

Cameron Park's median monthly rent is $1,393. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $65 (4%) lower.It is approximately $331 (31%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $130 (10%) higher.
