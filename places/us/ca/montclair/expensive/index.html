In comparison to its surrounding areas, Montclair is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Montclair is $354,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $156,700 (30%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $137,000 (62%) higher, and is approximately $282,400 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Montclair's median rent is $1,405 a month. It is approximately $72 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $343 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $45 (3%) lower.
