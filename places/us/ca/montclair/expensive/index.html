Compared to its surrounding areas, Montclair is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Montclair is valued at $354,500. It is roughly $156,700 (30%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $137,000 (62%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $282,400 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Montclair the median rent is $1,405 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $72 (4%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $343 (32%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $45 (3%) lower.
