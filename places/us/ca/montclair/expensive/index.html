Compared to its surrounding areas, Montclair is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Montclair is $354,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $156,700 (30%) lower.It is about $137,000 (62%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $282,400 (44%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Montclair is $1,405. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $72 (4%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $343 (32%) higher, and it is about $45 (3%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
