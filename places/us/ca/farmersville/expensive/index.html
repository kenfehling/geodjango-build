Home prices

The cost of the median home in Farmersville is $153,800. This is roughly $70,740 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $63,700 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fresno it is about $88,200 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Farmersville is $718. This is about $163 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $344 (32%) lower, and is about $287 (28%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.
