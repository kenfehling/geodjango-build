Home prices

The median home in Farmersville is valued at $153,800. This is roughly $70,740 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $63,700 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Fresno it is roughly $88,200 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Farmersville the median rent is $718 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $163 (18%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $344 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is approximately $287 (28%) lower.
