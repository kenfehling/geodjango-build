Home prices

The cost of the median home in West Park is $155,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $52,840 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $61,900 (28%) lower, and it is approximately $86,400 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

In West Park the median rent is $1,020 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $11 (1%) higher. It is roughly $42 (3%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is about $15 (1%) higher.
