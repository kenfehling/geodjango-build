Home prices

The median home in West Park costs $155,600. This is about $52,840 (25%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $61,900 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of Fresno it is about $86,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

In West Park the median rent is $1,020 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $11 (1%) higher. It is approximately $42 (3%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is roughly $15 (1%) higher.
