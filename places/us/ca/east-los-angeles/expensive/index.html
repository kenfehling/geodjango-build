In comparison to its nearby areas, East Los Angeles is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in East Los Angeles is $415,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $70,020 (14%) lower. It is approximately $198,100 (91%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $221,300 (34%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in East Los Angeles is $1,134 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $184 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $72 (6%) higher, and is approximately $316 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
