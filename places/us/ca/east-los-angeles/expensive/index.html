In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Los Angeles is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Los Angeles is valued at $415,600. This is roughly $70,020 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $198,100 (91%) higher, and is approximately $221,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In East Los Angeles the median monthly rent is $1,134. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $184 (13%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $72 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $316 (21%) lower.
