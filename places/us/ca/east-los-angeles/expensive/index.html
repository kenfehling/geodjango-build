In comparison to nearby areas, East Los Angeles is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeleshowever, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in East Los Angeles is $415,600. This is roughly $70,020 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $198,100 (91%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $221,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Los Angeles is $1,134. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $184 (13%) lower. It is about $72 (6%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $316 (21%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
