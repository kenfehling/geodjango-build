In comparison to other places in this area, East Los Angeles is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in East Los Angeles costs $415,600. This is roughly $70,020 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $198,100 (91%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $221,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

East Los Angeles's median monthly rent is $1,134. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $184 (13%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $72 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $316 (21%) lower.
