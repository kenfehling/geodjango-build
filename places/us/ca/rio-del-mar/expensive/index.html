Home prices

The median home in Rio del Mar costs $927,400. It is approximately $129,400 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $709,900 (326%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $170,400 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Rio del Mar the median monthly rent is $2,235. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $275 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,173 (110%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $340 (17%) higher.
