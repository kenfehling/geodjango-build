Compared to other places in this area, Rio del Mar is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home cost in Rio del Mar is $927,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $129,400 (16%) higher. It is roughly $709,900 (326%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $170,400 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rio del Mar is $2,235. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $275 (14%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $1,173 (110%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $340 (17%) higher.
