Home prices

Rio del Mar's median home value is $927,400. This is about $129,400 (16%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $709,900 (326%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $170,400 (15%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Rio del Mar the median monthly rent is $2,235. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $275 (14%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $1,173 (110%) higher, and is roughly $340 (17%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
