Home prices

The value of the median home in Desert Hot Springs is $194,500. It is about $22,540 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $23,000 (10%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Diego it is roughly $408,100 (67%) lower.

Rents

In Desert Hot Springs the median monthly rent is $963. It is about $104 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $99 (9%) lower, and is approximately $732 (43%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
