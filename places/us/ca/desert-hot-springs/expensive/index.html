Home prices

The median home value in Desert Hot Springs is $194,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $22,540 (13%) higher.It is approximately $23,000 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $408,100 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

In Desert Hot Springs the median rent is $963 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $104 (9%) lower.It is about $99 (9%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $732 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
