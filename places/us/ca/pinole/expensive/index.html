Home prices

The median home in Pinole costs $500,900. This is about $51,760 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $283,400 (130%) higher, and is about $596,900 (54%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Pinole is $1,835 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $67 (3%) lower. It is about $773 (72%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $60 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
