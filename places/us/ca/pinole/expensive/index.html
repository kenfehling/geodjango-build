Compared to its surrounding areas, Pinole is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pinole costs $500,900. This is roughly $51,760 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $283,400 (130%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $596,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Pinole the median rent is $1,835 a month. It is roughly $67 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $773 (72%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $60 (3%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
