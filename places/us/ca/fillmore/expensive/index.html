Home prices

The median home in Fillmore costs $426,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $31,740 (6%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $209,100 (96%) higher, and is approximately $210,300 (33%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Fillmore is $1,422 per month. It is approximately $184 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $360 (33%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $28 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
