In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fillmore is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Fillmore is valued at $426,600. This is approximately $31,740 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $209,100 (96%) higher, and is roughly $210,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Fillmore the median monthly rent is $1,422. It is approximately $184 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $360 (33%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $28 (1%) lower.
