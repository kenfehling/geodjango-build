Rents

In Oakdale the median rent is $1,099 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $134 (10%) lower. It is about $37 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $1,008 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
