Home prices

Oakdale's median home price is $310,100. This is roughly $25,560 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $92,600 (42%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $554,500 (64%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

In Oakdale the median rent is $1,099 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $134 (10%) lower.It is about $37 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Jose is roughly $1,008 (47%) lower.
