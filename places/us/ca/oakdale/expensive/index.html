Home prices

The median home in Oakdale is valued at $310,100. It is approximately $25,560 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $92,600 (42%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Jose it is roughly $554,500 (64%) lower.

Rents

In Oakdale the median rent is $1,099 per month. This is about $134 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $37 (3%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $1,008 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
