Compared to other places in this area, Lake Wildwood is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Sacramento, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lake Wildwood is valued at $395,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $37,480 (10%) higher.It is approximately $178,400 (82%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $59,000 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lake Wildwood is $1,905 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $548 (40%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $843 (79%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is roughly $642 (50%) higher.
