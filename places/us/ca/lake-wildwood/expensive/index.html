Home prices

The median home in Lake Wildwood costs $395,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $37,480 (10%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $178,400 (82%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is about $59,000 (17%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Wildwood is $1,905. This is approximately $548 (40%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $843 (79%) higher, and it is about $642 (50%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
