Compared to its surrounding areas, Orangevale is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Sacramento, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Orangevale's median home cost is $372,500. It is approximately $145,740 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $155,000 (71%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is about $35,600 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Orangevale is $1,460. It is about $179 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $398 (37%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento is roughly $197 (15%) higher.
