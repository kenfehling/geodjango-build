Compared to other places in this area, Orangevale is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Sacramento, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Orangevale is $372,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $145,740 (28%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $155,000 (71%) higher, and is approximately $35,600 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median rent in Orangevale is $1,460 a month. This is approximately $179 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $398 (37%) higher, and is about $197 (15%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
