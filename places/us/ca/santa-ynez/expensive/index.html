Compared to other places in this area, Santa Ynez is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Bakersfield, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Ynez is valued at $854,300. This is approximately $154,400 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $636,800 (292%) higher, and is roughly $607,300 (245%) higher in comparison to the city of Bakersfield.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Ynez is $1,425 a month. This is roughly $287 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $363 (34%) higher, and is roughly $321 (29%) higher compared to the city of Bakersfield.
