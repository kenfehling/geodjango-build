In comparison to other places in this area, Santa Ynez is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Bakersfield, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Ynez costs $854,300. This is approximately $154,400 (22%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $636,800 (292%) higher, and compared to the city of Bakersfield is about $607,300 (245%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Ynez is $1,425 per month. This is about $287 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $363 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Bakersfield it is approximately $321 (29%) higher.
