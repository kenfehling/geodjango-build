In comparison to other places in this area, Santa Ynez is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Bakersfield.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Ynez costs $854,300. It is roughly $154,400 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $636,800 (292%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is roughly $607,300 (245%) higher.

Rents

In Santa Ynez the median rent is $1,425 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $287 (16%) lower.It is roughly $363 (34%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bakersfield it is roughly $321 (29%) higher.
