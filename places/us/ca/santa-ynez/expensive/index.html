Compared to other places in this area, Santa Ynez is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Bakersfield, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Santa Ynez's median home cost is $854,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $154,400 (22%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $636,800 (292%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield is roughly $607,300 (245%) higher.

Rents

In Santa Ynez the median rent is $1,425 per month. It is roughly $287 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $363 (34%) higher, and is approximately $321 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Bakersfield.
