Home prices

The price of the median home in Callender is $683,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $63,620 (10%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $465,500 (214%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara is roughly $368,500 (35%) lower.

Rents

Callender's median rent is $1,459 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $507 (25%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $397 (37%) higher, and is approximately $327 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara.
