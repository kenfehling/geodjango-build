Home prices

The median home in Callender is valued at $683,000. This is about $63,620 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $465,500 (214%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Santa Barbara is about $368,500 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Callender is $1,459. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $507 (25%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $397 (37%) higher, and it is about $327 (18%) lower compared to the city of Santa Barbara.
