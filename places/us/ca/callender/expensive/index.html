Home prices

The median home in Callender is valued at $683,000. It is about $63,620 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $465,500 (214%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara it is about $368,500 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Callender the median rent is $1,459 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $507 (25%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $397 (37%) higher, and it is roughly $327 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara.
