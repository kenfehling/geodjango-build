Home prices

The median home cost in Paramount is $343,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $83,760 (19%) lower. It is about $126,300 (58%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $293,100 (46%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Paramount the median monthly rent is $1,388. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $97 (7%) higher. It is approximately $326 (30%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $62 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
