The median home in Sunnyside costs $295,000. This is about $122,160 (70%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $77,500 (35%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $53,000 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Fresno.

Rents

In Sunnyside the median rent is $943 a month. It is roughly $26 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $119 (11%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $62 (6%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.
