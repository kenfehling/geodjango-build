Home prices

The median home in Sunnyside costs $295,000. This is about $122,160 (70%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $77,500 (35%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is approximately $53,000 (21%) higher.

Rents

Sunnyside's median monthly rent is $943. It is about $26 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $119 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Fresno is roughly $62 (6%) lower.
