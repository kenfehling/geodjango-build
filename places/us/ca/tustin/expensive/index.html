In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tustin is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Tustin costs $647,500. It is approximately $171,620 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $430,000 (197%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $10,600 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Tustin is $1,856 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $397 (17%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $794 (74%) higher, and it is about $406 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
