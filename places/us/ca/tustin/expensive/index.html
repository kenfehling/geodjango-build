Compared to the surrounding areas, Tustin is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tustin is $647,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $84,680 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $430,000 (197%) higher, and is roughly $10,600 (1%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Tustin's median monthly rent is $1,856. It is roughly $68 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $794 (74%) higher, and is roughly $406 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
