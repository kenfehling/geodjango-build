In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tustin is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Tustin is valued at $647,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $171,620 (20%) lower.It is roughly $430,000 (197%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $10,600 (1%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Tustin is $1,856 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $397 (17%) lower.It is about $794 (74%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $406 (28%) higher.
