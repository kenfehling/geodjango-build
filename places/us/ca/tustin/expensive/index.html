Compared to other places in this area, Tustin is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Tustin costs $647,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $84,680 (11%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $430,000 (197%) higher, and it is about $10,600 (1%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Tustin the median monthly rent is $1,856. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $68 (3%) lower. It is roughly $794 (74%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $406 (28%) higher.
