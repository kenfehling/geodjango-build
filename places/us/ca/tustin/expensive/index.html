Compared to the surrounding areas, Tustin is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Tustin costs $647,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $171,620 (20%) lower.It is roughly $430,000 (197%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $10,600 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tustin is $1,856. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $397 (17%) lower.It is approximately $794 (74%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $406 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
