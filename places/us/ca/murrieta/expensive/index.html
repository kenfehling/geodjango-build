Home prices

The cost of the median home in Murrieta is $414,100. It is roughly $12,220 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $196,600 (90%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $222,800 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Murrieta the median monthly rent is $1,761. It is about $66 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $699 (65%) higher, and it is about $311 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
