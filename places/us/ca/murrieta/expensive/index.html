Home prices

The median home cost in Murrieta is $414,100. It is approximately $12,220 (3%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $196,600 (90%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $222,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Murrieta is $1,761 a month. This is approximately $66 (3%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $699 (65%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $311 (21%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
