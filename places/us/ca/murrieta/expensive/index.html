Home prices

Murrieta's median home price is $414,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $12,220 (3%) higher. It is about $196,600 (90%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $222,800 (34%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Murrieta the median rent is $1,761 a month. This is roughly $66 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $699 (65%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $311 (21%) higher.
