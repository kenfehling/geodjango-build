Home prices

Rents

In Murrieta the median rent is $1,761 per month. It is approximately $66 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $699 (65%) higher, and it is about $311 (21%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
