Home prices

Hayward's median home value is $581,200. This is about $15,240 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $363,700 (167%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $516,600 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Hayward the median rent is $1,825 per month. It is approximately $40 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $763 (71%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $70 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
