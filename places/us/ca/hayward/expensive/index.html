Similar to the surrounding areas, it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Hayward is $581,200. This is approximately $15,240 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $363,700 (167%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $516,600 (47%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hayward is $1,825. This is roughly $40 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $763 (71%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $70 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
