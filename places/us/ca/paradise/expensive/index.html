Compared to its surrounding areas, Paradise is a bit less expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Sacramento, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Paradise's median home cost is $223,400. This is approximately $58,300 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $5,900 (2%) higher, and is approximately $113,500 (33%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Paradise's median monthly rent is $1,079. This is approximately $91 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $17 (1%) higher, and is roughly $184 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
