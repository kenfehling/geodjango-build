Compared to its surrounding areas, Paradise is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Sacramento, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Paradise is $223,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $58,300 (20%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $5,900 (2%) higher, and it is about $113,500 (33%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Paradise's median rent is $1,079 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $91 (9%) higher. It is roughly $17 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $184 (14%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
