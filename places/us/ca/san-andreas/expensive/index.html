Home prices

The price of the median home in San Andreas is $209,800. It is approximately $98,200 (31%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $7,700 (3%) lower, and compared to the city of Sacramento is roughly $127,100 (37%) lower.

Rents

In San Andreas the median monthly rent is $1,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $93 (6%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $238 (22%) higher, and it is roughly $37 (2%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
