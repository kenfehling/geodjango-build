Compared to its nearby areas, San Andreas is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Sacramento however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Andreas is valued at $209,800. This is roughly $98,200 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $7,700 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $127,100 (37%) lower.

Rents

San Andreas's median monthly rent is $1,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $93 (6%) lower. It is approximately $238 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $37 (2%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
