In comparison to its nearby areas, San Andreas is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Sacramento however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in San Andreas is $209,800. It is roughly $98,200 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $7,700 (3%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is approximately $127,100 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in San Andreas is $1,300 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $93 (6%) lower.It is roughly $238 (22%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $37 (2%) higher.
