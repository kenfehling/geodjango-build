In comparison to the surrounding areas, Alpine is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of San Diego.

Home prices

The median home in Alpine costs $606,200. This is about $123,860 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $388,700 (178%) higher, and compared to the city of San Diego is is very similar.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alpine is $1,652. It is approximately $35 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $590 (55%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is approximately $43 (2%) lower.
