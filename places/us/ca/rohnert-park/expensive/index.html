Rents

Rohnert Park's median monthly rent is $1,688. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $77 (4%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $626 (58%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $207 (10%) lower.
