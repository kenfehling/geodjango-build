Compared to the surrounding areas, Santa Maria is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Bakersfield, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Maria costs $344,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $115,480 (25%) lower. It is roughly $126,600 (58%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bakersfield is approximately $97,100 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Maria is $1,414. It is approximately $384 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $352 (33%) higher, and it is roughly $310 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Bakersfield.
