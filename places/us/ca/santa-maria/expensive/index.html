Compared to the surrounding areas, Santa Maria is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Bakersfield, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Santa Maria is $344,100. This is roughly $115,480 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $126,600 (58%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $97,100 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Bakersfield.

Rents

In Santa Maria the median rent is $1,414 per month. This is approximately $384 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $352 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Bakersfield is approximately $310 (28%) higher.
