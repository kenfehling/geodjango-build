Home prices

The value of the median home in Santa Maria is $344,100. This is approximately $115,480 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $126,600 (58%) higher, and it is approximately $97,100 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Bakersfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Maria is $1,414. It is roughly $384 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $352 (33%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $310 (28%) higher compared to the city of Bakersfield.
