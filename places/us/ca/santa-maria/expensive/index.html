Compared to the surrounding areas, Santa Maria is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Bakersfield.

Home prices

Santa Maria's median home value is $344,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $115,480 (25%) lower.It is approximately $126,600 (58%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Bakersfield is approximately $97,100 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Maria is $1,414 a month. This is about $384 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $352 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Bakersfield it is approximately $310 (28%) higher.
