In comparison to its surrounding areas, Santa Maria is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Bakersfield.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Maria costs $344,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $115,480 (25%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $126,600 (58%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is roughly $97,100 (39%) higher.

Rents

In Santa Maria the median rent is $1,414 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $384 (21%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $352 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is about $310 (28%) higher.
