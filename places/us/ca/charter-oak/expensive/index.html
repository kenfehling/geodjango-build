Home prices

The median home in Charter Oak costs $423,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $86,580 (16%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $205,900 (94%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $213,500 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Charter Oak the median monthly rent is $1,804. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $108 (6%) higher. It is approximately $742 (69%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $354 (24%) higher.
