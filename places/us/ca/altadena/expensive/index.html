Compared to its surrounding areas, Altadena is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Altadena's median home value is $742,800. It is roughly $260,920 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $525,300 (241%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $105,900 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Altadena is $1,692 per month. This is roughly $189 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $630 (59%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $242 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
