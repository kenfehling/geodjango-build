Compared to other places in this area, Altadena is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Altadena is $742,800. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $260,920 (25%) lower. It is approximately $525,300 (241%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $105,900 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Altadena is $1,692. This is approximately $189 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $630 (59%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $242 (16%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
