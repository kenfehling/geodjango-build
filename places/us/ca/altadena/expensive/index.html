In comparison to other places in this area, Altadena is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Altadena is valued at $742,800. This is approximately $260,920 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $525,300 (241%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $105,900 (16%) higher.

Rents

In Altadena the median rent is $1,692 per month. This is approximately $189 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $630 (59%) higher, and is roughly $242 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
