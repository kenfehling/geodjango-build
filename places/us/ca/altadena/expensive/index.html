Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Altadena is $1,692. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $189 (10%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $630 (59%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $242 (16%) higher.
