Home prices

The median home in Altadena is valued at $742,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $260,920 (25%) lower.It is about $525,300 (241%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $105,900 (16%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Altadena is $1,692 a month. This is roughly $189 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $630 (59%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $242 (16%) higher.
