Home prices

The median home price in Hartley is $541,700. This is roughly $147,080 (37%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $324,200 (149%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $556,100 (50%) lower.

Rents

In Hartley the median rent is $1,303 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $271 (17%) lower. It is approximately $241 (22%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $592 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
