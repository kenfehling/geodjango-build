Home prices

The price of the median home in Hartley is $541,700. This is approximately $147,080 (37%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $324,200 (149%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $556,100 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Hartley the median monthly rent is $1,303. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $271 (17%) lower. It is roughly $241 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $592 (31%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
