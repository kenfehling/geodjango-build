In comparison to other places in this area, Simi Valley is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Simi Valley is $584,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $252,640 (30%) lower.It is approximately $367,400 (168%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $52,000 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Simi Valley the median rent is $2,023 a month. It is about $183 (8%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $961 (90%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $573 (39%) higher.
