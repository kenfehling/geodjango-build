Home prices

The median home cost in Inverness is $946,000. It is roughly $160,340 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $728,500 (334%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $151,800 (13%) lower.

Rents

Inverness's median monthly rent is $2,303. This is roughly $133 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,241 (116%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $408 (21%) higher.
