Home prices

The median home in Inverness costs $946,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $160,340 (14%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $728,500 (334%) higher, and it is approximately $151,800 (13%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Inverness the median rent is $2,303 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $133 (6%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $1,241 (116%) higher, and it is about $408 (21%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
