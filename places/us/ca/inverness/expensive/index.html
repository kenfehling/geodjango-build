Home prices

The value of the median home in Inverness is $946,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $160,340 (14%) lower. It is about $728,500 (334%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $151,800 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Inverness the median rent is $2,303 per month. It is about $133 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,241 (116%) higher, and is about $408 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
