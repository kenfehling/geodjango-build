Home prices

The median home cost in Inverness is $946,000. This is roughly $160,340 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $728,500 (334%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $151,800 (13%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Inverness the median monthly rent is $2,303. This is approximately $133 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,241 (116%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $408 (21%) higher.
