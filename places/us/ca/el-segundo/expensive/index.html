Compared to other places in this area, El Segundo is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in El Segundo is valued at $1,094,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $352,020 (47%) higher. It is roughly $876,800 (403%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $457,400 (71%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in El Segundo is $1,928 per month. This is about $257 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $866 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $478 (32%) higher.
