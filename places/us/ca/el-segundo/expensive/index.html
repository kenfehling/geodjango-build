Home prices

Rents

El Segundo's median monthly rent is $1,928. This is approximately $168 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $866 (81%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $478 (32%) higher.
