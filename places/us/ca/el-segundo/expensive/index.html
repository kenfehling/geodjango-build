In comparison to its nearby areas, El Segundo is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in El Segundo is $1,094,300. It is about $352,020 (47%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $876,800 (403%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $457,400 (71%) higher.

Rents

El Segundo's median monthly rent is $1,928. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $257 (15%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $866 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $478 (32%) higher.
