In comparison to other places in this area, El Segundo is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in El Segundo is valued at $1,094,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $352,020 (47%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $876,800 (403%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $457,400 (71%) higher.

Rents

El Segundo's median monthly rent is $1,928. It is about $257 (15%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $866 (81%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $478 (32%) higher.
