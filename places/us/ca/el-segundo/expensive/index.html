Compared to its surrounding areas, El Segundo is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in El Segundo is $1,094,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $260,600 (31%) higher.It is roughly $876,800 (403%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $457,400 (71%) higher.

Rents

El Segundo's median rent is $1,928 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $168 (9%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $866 (81%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $478 (32%) higher.
