Home prices

The median home in Cherry Valley costs $276,100. This is roughly $6,020 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $58,600 (26%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $360,800 (56%) lower.

Rents

Cherry Valley's median rent is $969 per month. It is roughly $173 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $93 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $481 (33%) lower.
