Home prices

The value of the median home in Wrightwood is $342,600. Compared to other places in this area this is about $34,960 (9%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $125,100 (57%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $294,300 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Wrightwood the median monthly rent is $1,030. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $228 (18%) lower. It is approximately $32 (3%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $420 (28%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
