Home prices

The median home in Pacific Grove is valued at $857,900. It is approximately $108,680 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $640,400 (294%) higher, and it is roughly $239,900 (21%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Pacific Grove the median rent is $1,758 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $431 (19%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $696 (65%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $137 (7%) lower.
