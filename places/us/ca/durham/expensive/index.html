In comparison to its nearby areas, Durham is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Sacramento.

Home prices

The median home in Durham costs $500,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $287,320 (134%) higher. It is about $282,800 (130%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $163,400 (48%) higher.

Rents

In Durham the median rent is $1,433 per month. It is roughly $460 (47%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $371 (34%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $170 (13%) higher.
