In comparison to nearby areas, San Gabriel is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in San Gabriel is $680,700. It is about $69,740 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $463,200 (212%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $43,800 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Gabriel the median rent is $1,486 a month. This is approximately $59 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $424 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $36 (2%) higher.
