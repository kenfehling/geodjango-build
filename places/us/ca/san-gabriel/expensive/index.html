Home prices

The median home price in San Gabriel is $680,700. This is roughly $69,740 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $463,200 (212%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $43,800 (6%) higher.

Rents

In San Gabriel the median rent is $1,486 a month. This is roughly $59 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $424 (39%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $36 (2%) higher.
