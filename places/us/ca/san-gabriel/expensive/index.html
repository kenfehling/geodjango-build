Home prices

The median home in San Gabriel costs $680,700. This is about $255,540 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $463,200 (212%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $43,800 (6%) higher.

Rents

In San Gabriel the median monthly rent is $1,486. This is approximately $358 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $424 (39%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $36 (2%) higher.
