Compared to its surrounding areas, La Crescenta-Montrose is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home cost in La Crescenta-Montrose is $823,400. This is roughly $122,940 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $605,900 (278%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $186,500 (29%) higher.

Rents

In La Crescenta-Montrose the median monthly rent is $1,819. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $54 (2%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $757 (71%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $369 (25%) higher.
