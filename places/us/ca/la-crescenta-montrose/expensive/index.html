Rents

The median rent in La Crescenta-Montrose is $1,819 a month. It is about $54 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $757 (71%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $369 (25%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
