Compared to the surrounding areas, East Nicolaus is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Oakland, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Nicolaus costs $310,400. It is roughly $45,960 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $92,900 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Oakland is approximately $377,000 (54%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Nicolaus is $1,313. It is roughly $90 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $251 (23%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Oakland is about $132 (9%) lower.
