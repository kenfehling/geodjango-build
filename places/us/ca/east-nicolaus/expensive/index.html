In comparison to nearby areas, East Nicolaus is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Oakland however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in East Nicolaus is $310,400. This is roughly $45,960 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $92,900 (42%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Oakland is approximately $377,000 (54%) lower.

Rents

In East Nicolaus the median monthly rent is $1,313. This is about $90 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $251 (23%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Oakland it is roughly $132 (9%) lower.
