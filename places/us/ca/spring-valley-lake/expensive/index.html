Home prices

The median home price in Spring Valley Lake is $281,100. This is roughly $54,760 (24%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $63,600 (29%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $355,800 (55%) lower.

Rents

Spring Valley Lake's median rent is $1,713 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $506 (41%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $651 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $263 (18%) higher.
