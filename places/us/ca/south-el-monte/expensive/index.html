Home prices

South El Monte's median home cost is $445,500. This is approximately $76,740 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $228,000 (104%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $191,400 (30%) lower.

Rents

South El Monte's median monthly rent is $1,333. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $134 (9%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $271 (25%) higher, and is approximately $117 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
