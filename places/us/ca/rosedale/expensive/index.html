Home prices

The median home in Rosedale is valued at $412,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $263,900 (177%) higher. It is about $195,000 (89%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield is roughly $165,500 (67%) higher.

Rents

Rosedale's median monthly rent is $903. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $76 (7%) lower. It is about $159 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $201 (18%) lower compared to the city of Bakersfield.
