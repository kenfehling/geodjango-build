Home prices

The median home in Rosedale is valued at $412,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $263,900 (177%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $195,000 (89%) higher, and is roughly $165,500 (67%) higher compared to the city of Bakersfield.

Rents

Rosedale's median rent is $903 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $76 (7%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $159 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is approximately $201 (18%) lower.
