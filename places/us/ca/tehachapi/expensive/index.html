In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tehachapi is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Tehachapi costs $211,200. It is approximately $17,140 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $6,300 (2%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $425,700 (66%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tehachapi is $1,015. It is is very similar compared to nearby areas.It is about $47 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $435 (30%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
