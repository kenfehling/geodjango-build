In comparison to other places in this area, Tehachapi is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tehachapi is $211,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $17,140 (8%) higher. It is roughly $6,300 (2%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $425,700 (66%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Tehachapi is $1,015 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $47 (4%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $435 (30%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
