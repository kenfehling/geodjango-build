Home prices

The median home value in Bakersfield is $247,000. This is roughly $33,140 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $29,500 (13%) higher, and is approximately $389,900 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Bakersfield is $1,104 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $172 (18%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $42 (3%) higher, and is roughly $346 (23%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
