Home prices

Bakersfield's median home price is $247,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $33,140 (15%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $29,500 (13%) higher, and is approximately $389,900 (61%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Bakersfield's median rent is $1,104 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $172 (18%) higher.It is approximately $42 (3%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $346 (23%) lower.
