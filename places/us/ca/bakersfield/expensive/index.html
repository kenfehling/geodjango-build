Compared to its surrounding areas, Bakersfield is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Bakersfield costs $247,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $33,140 (15%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $29,500 (13%) higher, and is roughly $389,900 (61%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Bakersfield the median monthly rent is $1,104. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $172 (18%) higher. It is about $42 (3%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $346 (23%) lower.
