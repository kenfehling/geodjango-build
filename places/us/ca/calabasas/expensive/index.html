Compared to its surrounding areas, Calabasas is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Calabasas is valued at $1,076,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $61,840 (6%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $859,100 (394%) higher, and is approximately $439,700 (69%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Calabasas the median rent is $2,505 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $280 (10%) lower. It is roughly $1,443 (135%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $1,055 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
