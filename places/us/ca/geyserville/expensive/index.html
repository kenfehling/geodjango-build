In comparison to the surrounding areas, Geyserville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Geyserville costs $625,000. This is roughly $229,040 (26%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $407,500 (187%) higher, and is approximately $472,800 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Geyserville is $1,292 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $231 (15%) lower.It is approximately $230 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $603 (31%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
