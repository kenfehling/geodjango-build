Compared to its nearby areas, Geyserville is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Geyserville costs $625,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $229,040 (26%) lower.It is about $407,500 (187%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $472,800 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Geyserville the median monthly rent is $1,292. It is roughly $231 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $230 (21%) higher, and is about $603 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
