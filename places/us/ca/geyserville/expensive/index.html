In comparison to other places in this area, Geyserville is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Geyserville is valued at $625,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $54,180 (9%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $407,500 (187%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $472,800 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Geyserville the median rent is $1,292 per month. It is approximately $291 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $230 (21%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $603 (31%) lower.
