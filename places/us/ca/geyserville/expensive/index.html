Compared to its surrounding areas, Geyserville is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Geyserville is valued at $625,000. This is roughly $54,180 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $407,500 (187%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $472,800 (43%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Geyserville's median monthly rent is $1,292. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $291 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $230 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $603 (31%) lower.
