The median home in Sunnyside-Tahoe City is valued at $613,300. This is about $29,140 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $395,800 (181%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $278,300 (83%) higher in comparison to the city of Reno.

Rents

In Sunnyside-Tahoe City the median monthly rent is $1,095. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $233 (17%) lower. It is approximately $33 (3%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Reno it is about $66 (6%) higher.
