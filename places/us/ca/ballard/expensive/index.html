Home prices

Ballard's median home cost is $914,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $227,000 (33%) higher. It is about $697,300 (320%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is approximately $667,800 (270%) higher.

Rents

Ballard's median rent is $1,583 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $97 (5%) lower. It is approximately $521 (49%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $479 (43%) higher compared to the city of Bakersfield.
