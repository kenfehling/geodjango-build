Home prices

The median home in Ballard is valued at $914,800. This is roughly $227,000 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $697,300 (320%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $667,800 (270%) higher compared to the city of Bakersfield.

Rents

Ballard's median monthly rent is $1,583. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $97 (5%) lower.It is roughly $521 (49%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $479 (43%) higher compared to the city of Bakersfield.
