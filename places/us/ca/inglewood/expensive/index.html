Compared to its nearby areas, Inglewood is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

Inglewood's median home cost is $483,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $83,080 (14%) lower. It is approximately $265,500 (122%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $153,900 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Inglewood is $1,358 a month. This is roughly $105 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $296 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $92 (6%) lower.
