Compared to its nearby areas, Sausalito is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Sausalito's median home cost is $1,346,000. This is roughly $327,481 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $1,128,500 (518%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $248,200 (22%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sausalito is $2,583. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $313 (13%) higher.It is about $1,521 (143%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $688 (36%) higher.
