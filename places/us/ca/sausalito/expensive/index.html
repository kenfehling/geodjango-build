Much like the surrounding areas, it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Sausalito costs $1,346,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $22,720 (1%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $1,128,500 (518%) higher, and is about $248,200 (22%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Sausalito the median rent is $2,583 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $499 (23%) higher. It is approximately $1,521 (143%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $688 (36%) higher.
