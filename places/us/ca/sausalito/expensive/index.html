In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sausalito is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sausalito is $1,346,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $327,481 (19%) lower.It is roughly $1,128,500 (518%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $248,200 (22%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Sausalito's median rent is $2,583 per month. It is roughly $313 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $1,521 (143%) higher, and is approximately $688 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
