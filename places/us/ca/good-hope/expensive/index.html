Home prices

The median home in Good Hope costs $233,600. This is approximately $55,980 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $16,100 (7%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $403,300 (63%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Good Hope the median monthly rent is $1,121. This is about $294 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $59 (5%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $329 (22%) lower.
