Home prices

The value of the median home in Ferndale is $410,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $110,980 (37%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $193,300 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Eureka is approximately $129,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ferndale is $1,130. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $181 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $68 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Eureka it is about $214 (23%) higher.
