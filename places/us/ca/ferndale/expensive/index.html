Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ferndale is $410,800. It is roughly $110,980 (37%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $193,300 (88%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Eureka it is about $129,400 (45%) higher.

Rents

Ferndale's median rent is $1,130 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $181 (19%) higher. It is roughly $68 (6%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $214 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Eureka.
