Home prices

The cost of the median home in Menifee is $334,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $46,080 (15%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $117,100 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $302,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Menifee is $1,556. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $162 (11%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $494 (46%) higher, and is approximately $106 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
