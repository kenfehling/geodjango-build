Home prices

The median home in Menifee costs $334,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $46,080 (15%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $117,100 (53%) higher, and is roughly $302,300 (47%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Menifee is $1,556. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $162 (11%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $494 (46%) higher, and it is about $106 (7%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
