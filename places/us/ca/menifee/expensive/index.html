In comparison to the surrounding areas, Menifee is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in Menifee is $334,600. It is about $46,080 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $117,100 (53%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $302,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

In Menifee the median monthly rent is $1,556. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $162 (11%) higher. It is about $494 (46%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $106 (7%) higher.
