Home prices

Rents

Menifee's median monthly rent is $1,556. This is roughly $162 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $494 (46%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $106 (7%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
