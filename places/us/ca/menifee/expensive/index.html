Compared to its surrounding areas, Menifee is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Menifee is $334,600. This is approximately $46,080 (15%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $117,100 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $302,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

Menifee's median monthly rent is $1,556. This is about $162 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $494 (46%) higher, and it is about $106 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
