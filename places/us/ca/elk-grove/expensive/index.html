Home prices

The price of the median home in Elk Grove is $406,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $107,520 (35%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $188,800 (86%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $691,500 (62%) lower.

Rents

Elk Grove's median rent is $1,695 per month. It is about $423 (33%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $633 (59%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $200 (10%) lower.
