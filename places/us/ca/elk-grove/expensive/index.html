Home prices

Elk Grove's median home cost is $406,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $107,520 (35%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $188,800 (86%) higher, and it is roughly $691,500 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Elk Grove's median monthly rent is $1,695. It is about $423 (33%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $633 (59%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $200 (10%) lower.
