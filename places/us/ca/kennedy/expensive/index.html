Home prices

The median home in Kennedy costs $166,600. This is approximately $46,280 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $50,900 (23%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $931,200 (84%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Kennedy's median monthly rent is $1,127. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $96 (9%) higher. It is roughly $65 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $768 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
