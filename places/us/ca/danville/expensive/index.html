Home prices

The median home in Danville costs $1,091,900. It is about $92,820 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $874,400 (402%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Danville is $2,489 a month. This is approximately $188 (8%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $1,427 (134%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $594 (31%) higher.
