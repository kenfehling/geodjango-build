In comparison to its surrounding areas, San Diego is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in San Diego costs $602,600. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $122,050 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $385,100 (177%) higher.

Rents

San Diego's median monthly rent is $1,695. This is roughly $220 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $633 (59%) higher compared to the United States as a whole.
