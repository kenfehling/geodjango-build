In comparison to the surrounding areas, San Diego is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in San Diego costs $602,600. Compared to nearby areas this is about $122,050 (25%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $385,100 (177%) higher.

Rents

San Diego's median monthly rent is $1,695. It is approximately $220 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $633 (59%) higher.
