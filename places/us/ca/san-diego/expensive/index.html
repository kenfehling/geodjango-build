Home prices

San Diego's median home cost is $602,600. It is about $122,050 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $385,100 (177%) higher.

Rents

San Diego's median monthly rent is $1,695. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $220 (14%) higher.It is roughly $633 (59%) higher compared to the US as a whole.
