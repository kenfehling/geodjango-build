Compared to the surrounding areas, San Diego is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole.

Home prices

The value of the median home in San Diego is $602,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $122,050 (25%) higher.It is about $385,100 (177%) higher compared to the country as a whole.

Rents

In San Diego the median rent is $1,695 per month. It is approximately $220 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $633 (59%) higher.
