In comparison to the surrounding areas, San Diego is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country.

Home prices

The median home in San Diego is valued at $602,600. It is approximately $122,050 (25%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $385,100 (177%) higher compared to the entire country.

Rents

San Diego's median monthly rent is $1,695. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $220 (14%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $633 (59%) higher.
