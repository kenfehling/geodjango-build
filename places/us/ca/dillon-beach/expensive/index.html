In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dillon Beach is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dillon Beach costs $964,300. This is approximately $264,140 (37%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $746,800 (343%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $133,500 (12%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dillon Beach is $2,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $941 (53%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,638 (154%) higher, and is about $805 (42%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
