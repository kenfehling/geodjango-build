In comparison to its surrounding areas, Union City is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Union City costs $765,500. It is approximately $53,220 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $548,000 (251%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $332,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

Union City's median monthly rent is $2,202. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $190 (9%) higher. It is about $1,140 (107%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $307 (16%) higher.
