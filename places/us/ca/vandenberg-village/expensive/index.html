Compared to the surrounding areas, Vandenberg Village is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Santa Barbara however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Vandenberg Village is $391,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $19,260 (5%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $173,500 (79%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara it is about $660,500 (62%) lower.

Rents

Vandenberg Village's median monthly rent is $1,798. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $289 (19%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $736 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Santa Barbara is is very similar.
