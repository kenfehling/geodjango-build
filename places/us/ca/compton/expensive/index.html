Home prices

Compton's median home value is $355,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $6,020 (1%) lower.It is roughly $137,700 (63%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $281,700 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Compton the median monthly rent is $1,219. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $38 (3%) lower.It is roughly $157 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $231 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
