Much like its surrounding areas, it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Compton costs $355,200. This is about $6,020 (1%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $137,700 (63%) higher, and is approximately $281,700 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Compton is $1,219 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $38 (3%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $157 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $231 (15%) lower.
