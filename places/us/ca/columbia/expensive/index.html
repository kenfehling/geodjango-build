Home prices

The median home in Columbia is valued at $318,100. It is approximately $52,440 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $100,600 (46%) higher, and it is about $18,800 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

In Columbia the median monthly rent is $706. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $334 (32%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $356 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is roughly $557 (44%) lower.
