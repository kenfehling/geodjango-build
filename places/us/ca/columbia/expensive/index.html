In comparison to the surrounding areas, Columbia is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Sacramento.

Home prices

The median home value in Columbia is $318,100. This is roughly $52,440 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $100,600 (46%) higher, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $18,800 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Columbia the median rent is $706 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $334 (32%) lower. It is approximately $356 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is roughly $557 (44%) lower.
