Home prices

Visalia's median home value is $236,400. This is about $50,960 (27%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $18,900 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Fresno is roughly $5,600 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Visalia is $1,050. It is roughly $224 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $12 (1%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $45 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Fresno.
