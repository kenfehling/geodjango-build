The median home in Visalia costs $236,400. This is approximately $50,960 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $18,900 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is approximately $5,600 (2%) lower.

Rents

Visalia's median rent is $1,050 a month. It is about $224 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is about $45 (4%) higher.
