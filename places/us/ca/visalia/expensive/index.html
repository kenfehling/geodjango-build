Home prices

The median home in Visalia costs $236,400. It is roughly $50,960 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $18,900 (8%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is approximately $5,600 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Visalia is $1,050 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $224 (27%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $12 (1%) lower, and is roughly $45 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Fresno.
