Home prices

The median home value in Visalia is $236,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $50,960 (27%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $18,900 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is roughly $5,600 (2%) lower.

Rents

Visalia's median rent is $1,050 per month. This is about $224 (27%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Fresno is approximately $45 (4%) higher.
