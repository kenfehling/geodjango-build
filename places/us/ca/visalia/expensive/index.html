Home prices

Visalia's median home value is $236,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $50,960 (27%) higher.It is roughly $18,900 (8%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $5,600 (2%) lower compared to the city of Fresno.

Rents

In Visalia the median monthly rent is $1,050. This is roughly $224 (27%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $12 (1%) lower, and compared to the city of Fresno it is approximately $45 (4%) higher.
