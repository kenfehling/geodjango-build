Home prices

The median home in Topanga is valued at $1,064,400. It is about $286,560 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $846,900 (389%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $427,500 (67%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Topanga is $3,088 per month. It is roughly $657 (27%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $2,026 (190%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $1,638 (112%) higher.
