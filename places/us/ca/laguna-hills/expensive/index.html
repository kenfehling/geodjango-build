In comparison to other places in this area, Laguna Hills is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Laguna Hills is valued at $707,700. This is approximately $66,460 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $490,200 (225%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $70,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Hills is $2,111. It is about $49 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $1,049 (98%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $661 (45%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
