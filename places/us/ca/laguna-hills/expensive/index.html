Compared to its surrounding areas, Laguna Hills is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Laguna Hills is $707,700. It is roughly $66,460 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $490,200 (225%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $70,800 (11%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Hills is $2,111. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $49 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $1,049 (98%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $661 (45%) higher.
