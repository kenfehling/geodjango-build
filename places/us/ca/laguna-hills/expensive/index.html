In comparison to nearby areas, Laguna Hills is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Laguna Hills is $707,700. This is approximately $133,500 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $490,200 (225%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $70,800 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Hills is $2,111. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $73 (3%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,049 (98%) higher, and is about $661 (45%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
