Compared to other places in this area, Laguna Hills is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Laguna Hills is valued at $707,700. It is approximately $66,460 (10%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $490,200 (225%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $70,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

Laguna Hills's median rent is $2,111 a month. It is approximately $49 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $1,049 (98%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $661 (45%) higher.
