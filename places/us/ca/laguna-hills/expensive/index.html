Compared to the surrounding areas, Laguna Hills is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

Laguna Hills's median home cost is $707,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $133,500 (15%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $490,200 (225%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $70,800 (11%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Hills is $2,111. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $73 (3%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $1,049 (98%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $661 (45%) higher.
