Home prices

The median home value in Big Bend is $120,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $47,600 (28%) lower. It is about $96,700 (44%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chico it is approximately $206,900 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Big Bend the median monthly rent is $638. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $304 (32%) lower. It is about $424 (39%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $462 (42%) lower compared to the city of Chico.
