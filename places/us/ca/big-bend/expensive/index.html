Compared to its surrounding areas, Big Bend is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Chico, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Big Bend costs $120,800. It is approximately $47,600 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $96,700 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $206,900 (63%) lower compared to the city of Chico.

Rents

In Big Bend the median monthly rent is $638. It is roughly $304 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $424 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chico it is about $462 (42%) lower.
