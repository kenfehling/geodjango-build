Home prices

The cost of the median home in La Mesa is $530,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $48,140 (9%) higher. It is approximately $312,900 (143%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $72,200 (11%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

In La Mesa the median monthly rent is $1,547. It is approximately $111 (7%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $485 (45%) higher, and it is approximately $148 (8%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
