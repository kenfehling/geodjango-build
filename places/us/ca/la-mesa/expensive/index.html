In comparison to its surrounding areas, La Mesa is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Diego, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in La Mesa costs $530,400. This is approximately $48,140 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $312,900 (143%) higher, and it is roughly $72,200 (11%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

In La Mesa the median monthly rent is $1,547. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $111 (7%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $485 (45%) higher, and it is roughly $148 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
