Compared to the surrounding areas, La Mesa is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Diego however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in La Mesa is $530,400. This is approximately $48,140 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $312,900 (143%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is about $72,200 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in La Mesa is $1,547 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $111 (7%) higher.It is roughly $485 (45%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is about $148 (8%) lower.
