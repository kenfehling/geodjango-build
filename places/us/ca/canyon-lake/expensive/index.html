Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Canyon Lake is $2,060 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $678 (49%) higher. It is about $998 (93%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $610 (42%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
