Home prices

The median home cost in Santa Rosa is $540,600. It is roughly $104,220 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $323,100 (148%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $557,200 (50%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Santa Rosa the median rent is $1,609 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $182 (12%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $547 (51%) higher, and it is approximately $286 (15%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
