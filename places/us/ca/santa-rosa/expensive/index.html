Compared to its surrounding areas, Santa Rosa is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Santa Rosa is $540,600. This is approximately $104,220 (16%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $323,100 (148%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $557,200 (50%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Rosa is $1,609. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $182 (12%) higher. It is approximately $547 (51%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $286 (15%) lower.
