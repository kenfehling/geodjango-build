In comparison to the surrounding areas, Martinez is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Martinez costs $596,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $51,820 (9%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $378,900 (174%) higher, and is about $501,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Martinez is $1,728. This is about $240 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $666 (62%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $167 (8%) lower.
