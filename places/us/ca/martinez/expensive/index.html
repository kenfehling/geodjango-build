In comparison to nearby areas, Martinez is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Martinez costs $596,400. This is about $51,820 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $378,900 (174%) higher, and it is roughly $501,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Martinez is $1,728. Compared to nearby areas it is about $240 (12%) lower.It is roughly $666 (62%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $167 (8%) lower.
