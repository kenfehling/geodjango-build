In comparison to other places in this area, Martinez is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Martinez is valued at $596,400. This is approximately $51,820 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $378,900 (174%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $501,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Martinez the median rent is $1,728 per month. This is about $240 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $666 (62%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $167 (8%) lower.
