Compared to its nearby areas, Martinez is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Martinez's median home value is $596,400. It is about $51,820 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $378,900 (174%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $501,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Martinez is $1,728 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $240 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $666 (62%) higher, and it is roughly $167 (8%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
