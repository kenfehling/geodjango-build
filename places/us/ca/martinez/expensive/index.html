In comparison to its nearby areas, Martinez is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Martinez is valued at $596,400. This is roughly $51,820 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $378,900 (174%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $501,400 (45%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Martinez the median monthly rent is $1,728. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $240 (12%) lower. It is roughly $666 (62%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $167 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
