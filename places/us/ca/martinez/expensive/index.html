Compared to its nearby areas, Martinez is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Martinez costs $596,400. This is roughly $51,820 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $378,900 (174%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $501,400 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Martinez the median rent is $1,728 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $240 (12%) lower. It is roughly $666 (62%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $167 (8%) lower.
