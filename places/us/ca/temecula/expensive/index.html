Much like its surrounding areas, it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Temecula's median home value is $444,800. Compared to other places in this area it is about $17,040 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $227,300 (104%) higher, and is about $192,100 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Temecula is $1,856 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $405 (27%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $794 (74%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $406 (28%) higher.
