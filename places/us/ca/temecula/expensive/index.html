Rents

Temecula's median rent is $1,856 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $405 (27%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $794 (74%) higher, and it is approximately $406 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
