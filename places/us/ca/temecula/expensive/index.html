Home prices

The cost of the median home in Temecula is $444,800. It is roughly $17,040 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $227,300 (104%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $192,100 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Temecula is $1,856. This is approximately $405 (27%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $794 (74%) higher, and it is roughly $406 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
