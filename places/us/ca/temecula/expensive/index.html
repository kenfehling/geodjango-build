Home prices

Temecula's median home price is $444,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $17,040 (3%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $227,300 (104%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $192,100 (30%) lower.

Rents

Temecula's median monthly rent is $1,856. It is roughly $405 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $794 (74%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $406 (28%) higher.
