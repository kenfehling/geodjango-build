The cost of the median home in Temecula is $444,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $17,040 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $227,300 (104%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $192,100 (30%) lower.

Rents
