Home prices

The median home cost in Temecula is $444,800. It is approximately $17,040 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $227,300 (104%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $192,100 (30%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Temecula is $1,856. This is about $405 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $794 (74%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $406 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
