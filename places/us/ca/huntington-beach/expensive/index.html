In comparison to nearby areas, Huntington Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Huntington Beach's median home cost is $771,100. This is roughly $164,520 (27%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $553,600 (254%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $134,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Huntington Beach the median rent is $1,903 a month. It is about $154 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $841 (79%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $453 (31%) higher.
