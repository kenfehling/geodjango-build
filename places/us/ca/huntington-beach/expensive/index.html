Compared to its surrounding areas, Huntington Beach is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Huntington Beach costs $771,100. Compared to nearby areas it is about $164,520 (27%) higher.It is about $553,600 (254%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $134,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

Huntington Beach's median monthly rent is $1,903. It is roughly $154 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $841 (79%) higher, and it is approximately $453 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
