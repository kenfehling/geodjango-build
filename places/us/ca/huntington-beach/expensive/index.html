Compared to nearby areas, Huntington Beach is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Huntington Beach is $771,100. It is about $164,520 (27%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $553,600 (254%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $134,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Huntington Beach's median monthly rent is $1,903. This is approximately $154 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $841 (79%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $453 (31%) higher.
