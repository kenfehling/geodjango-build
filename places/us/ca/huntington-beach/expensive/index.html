In comparison to other places in this area, Huntington Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home price in Huntington Beach is $771,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $164,520 (27%) higher. It is approximately $553,600 (254%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $134,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

Huntington Beach's median monthly rent is $1,903. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $154 (8%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $841 (79%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $453 (31%) higher.
