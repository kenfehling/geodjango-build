In comparison to the surrounding areas, Huntington Beach is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Huntington Beach is valued at $771,100. It is roughly $164,520 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $553,600 (254%) higher, and is about $134,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Huntington Beach is $1,903. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $154 (8%) higher. It is approximately $841 (79%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $453 (31%) higher.
