Compared to the surrounding areas, Huntington Beach is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Huntington Beach's median home value is $771,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $164,520 (27%) higher.It is approximately $553,600 (254%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $134,200 (21%) higher.

Rents

Huntington Beach's median rent is $1,903 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $154 (8%) higher.It is about $841 (79%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $453 (31%) higher.
