Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Boulder Creek is $1,389. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $70 (4%) lower.It is about $327 (30%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $506 (26%) lower.
