Compared to its surrounding areas, San Bernardino is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Bernardino costs $249,400. This is roughly $54,580 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $31,900 (14%) higher, and is about $387,500 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Bernardino the median monthly rent is $1,059. It is about $182 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is is very similar, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $391 (26%) lower.
