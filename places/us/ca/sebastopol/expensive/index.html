In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sebastopol is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Sebastopol is valued at $638,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $69,780 (12%) higher.It is approximately $421,100 (193%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $459,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

In Sebastopol the median rent is $1,410 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $163 (10%) lower.It is approximately $348 (32%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $485 (25%) lower.
