Compared to its nearby areas, Sebastopol is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sebastopol is valued at $638,600. This is roughly $69,780 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $421,100 (193%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $459,200 (41%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Sebastopol the median rent is $1,410 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $163 (10%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $348 (32%) higher, and is about $485 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
