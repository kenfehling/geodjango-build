In comparison to nearby areas, Sebastopol is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Sebastopol's median home price is $638,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $69,780 (12%) higher. It is approximately $421,100 (193%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $459,200 (41%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Sebastopol is $1,410 per month. It is approximately $163 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $348 (32%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $485 (25%) lower.
