Compared to the surrounding areas, Sebastopol is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sebastopol costs $638,600. This is roughly $69,780 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $421,100 (193%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $459,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Sebastopol is $1,410 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $163 (10%) lower.It is about $348 (32%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $485 (25%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
