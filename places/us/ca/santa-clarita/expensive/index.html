Much like its surrounding areas, it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Santa Clarita is $537,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $6,220 (1%) higher.It is about $319,500 (146%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $99,900 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Santa Clarita the median rent is $1,895 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $285 (17%) higher.It is approximately $833 (78%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $445 (30%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
