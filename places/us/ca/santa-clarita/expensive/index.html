The median home in Santa Clarita is valued at $537,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $6,220 (1%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $319,500 (146%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $99,900 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Clarita is $1,895. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $285 (17%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $833 (78%) higher, and it is about $445 (30%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
