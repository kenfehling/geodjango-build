Similar to other places in this area, it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Santa Clarita is $537,000. It is approximately $6,220 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $319,500 (146%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $99,900 (15%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Santa Clarita the median monthly rent is $1,895. It is roughly $285 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $833 (78%) higher, and it is roughly $445 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
