In comparison to its nearby areas, Cherryland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Cherryland is valued at $490,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $121,680 (19%) lower. It is roughly $272,600 (125%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $607,700 (55%) lower.

Rents

In Cherryland the median monthly rent is $1,671. It is roughly $108 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $609 (57%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $224 (11%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
