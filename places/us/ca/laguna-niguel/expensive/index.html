Compared to nearby areas, Laguna Niguel is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home value in Laguna Niguel is $827,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $137,520 (14%) lower. It is about $609,600 (280%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $190,200 (29%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Laguna Niguel is $2,263. Compared to other places in this area this is about $71 (3%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $1,201 (113%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $813 (56%) higher.
