In comparison to other places in this area, Laguna Niguel is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Laguna Niguel is valued at $827,100. This is roughly $137,520 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $609,600 (280%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $190,200 (29%) higher.

Rents

Laguna Niguel's median monthly rent is $2,263. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $71 (3%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $1,201 (113%) higher, and is roughly $813 (56%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
