Compared to its surrounding areas, Laguna Niguel is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Laguna Niguel's median home value is $827,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $137,520 (14%) lower. It is about $609,600 (280%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $190,200 (29%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Laguna Niguel the median rent is $2,263 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $71 (3%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,201 (113%) higher, and it is approximately $813 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
