In comparison to its nearby areas, Laguna Niguel is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Laguna Niguel is valued at $827,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $137,520 (14%) lower.It is roughly $609,600 (280%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $190,200 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Laguna Niguel the median monthly rent is $2,263. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $71 (3%) higher.It is roughly $1,201 (113%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $813 (56%) higher.
