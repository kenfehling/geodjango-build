Compared to its surrounding areas, Dana Point is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dana Point costs $878,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $130,720 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $660,800 (303%) higher, and is about $241,400 (37%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Dana Point the median monthly rent is $2,061. This is about $96 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $999 (94%) higher, and is roughly $611 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
