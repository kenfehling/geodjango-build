Home prices

The value of the median home in Dana Point is $878,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $130,720 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $660,800 (303%) higher, and it is roughly $241,400 (37%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Dana Point the median rent is $2,061 per month. This is about $96 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $999 (94%) higher, and is approximately $611 (42%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
