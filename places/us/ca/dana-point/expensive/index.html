In comparison to other places in this area, Dana Point is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dana Point costs $878,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $130,720 (12%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $660,800 (303%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $241,400 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Dana Point the median monthly rent is $2,061. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $96 (4%) lower.It is roughly $999 (94%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $611 (42%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
