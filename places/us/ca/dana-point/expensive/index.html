In comparison to the surrounding areas, Dana Point is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dana Point costs $878,300. This is approximately $130,720 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $660,800 (303%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $241,400 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Dana Point's median rent is $2,061 a month. It is roughly $96 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $999 (94%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $611 (42%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
