Compared to other places in this area, Dana Point is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dana Point is valued at $878,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $130,720 (12%) lower. It is approximately $660,800 (303%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $241,400 (37%) higher.

Rents

In Dana Point the median monthly rent is $2,061. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $96 (4%) lower. It is about $999 (94%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $611 (42%) higher.
