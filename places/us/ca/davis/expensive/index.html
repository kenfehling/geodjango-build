Compared to the surrounding areas, Davis is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Davis is valued at $652,300. This is roughly $285,120 (77%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $434,800 (199%) higher, and it is roughly $445,500 (40%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Davis the median rent is $1,567 a month. This is about $289 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $505 (47%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $328 (17%) lower.
