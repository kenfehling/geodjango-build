In comparison to its nearby areas, Davis is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Davis costs $652,300. It is roughly $285,120 (77%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $434,800 (199%) higher, and it is approximately $445,500 (40%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Davis the median monthly rent is $1,567. It is about $289 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $505 (47%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $328 (17%) lower.
