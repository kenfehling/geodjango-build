Compared to its nearby areas, Davis is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Davis's median home price is $652,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $285,120 (77%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $434,800 (199%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $445,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

Davis's median monthly rent is $1,567. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $289 (22%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $505 (47%) higher, and is about $328 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
