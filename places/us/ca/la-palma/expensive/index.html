Compared to other places in this area, La Palma is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's close to average.

Home prices

La Palma's median home cost is $659,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $98,500 (17%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $441,600 (203%) higher, and it is approximately $22,200 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

La Palma's median monthly rent is $1,775. This is approximately $30 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $713 (67%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $325 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
