Home prices

The value of the median home in La Palma is $659,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $98,500 (17%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $441,600 (203%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $22,200 (3%) higher.

Rents

In La Palma the median rent is $1,775 per month. This is roughly $30 (1%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $713 (67%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $325 (22%) higher.
