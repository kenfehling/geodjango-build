Home prices

The median home cost in Lewiston is $273,000. This is roughly $17,740 (6%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $55,500 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Redding it is roughly $8,100 (3%) higher.

Rents

Lewiston's median rent is $825 per month. This is about $31 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $237 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Redding is approximately $250 (23%) lower.
