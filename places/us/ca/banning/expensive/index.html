Home prices

The median home cost in Banning is $234,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $31,520 (11%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $17,400 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $402,000 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Banning is $1,010. This is roughly $86 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $52 (4%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $440 (30%) lower.
