In comparison to its surrounding areas, Banning is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Banning is $234,900. This is approximately $31,520 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $17,400 (8%) higher, and it is about $402,000 (63%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Banning the median monthly rent is $1,010. This is approximately $86 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $52 (4%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $440 (30%) lower.
