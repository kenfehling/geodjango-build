Home prices

The median home in Banning is valued at $234,900. This is about $31,520 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $17,400 (8%) higher, and is about $402,000 (63%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Banning the median rent is $1,010 a month. This is roughly $86 (7%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $52 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $440 (30%) lower.
