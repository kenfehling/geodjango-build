In comparison to its surrounding areas, Banning is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Banning costs $234,900. This is roughly $31,520 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $17,400 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $402,000 (63%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Banning is $1,010 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $86 (7%) lower.It is about $52 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $440 (30%) lower.
