Home prices

The median home in East Hemet costs $243,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $16,880 (7%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $25,600 (11%) higher, and is about $393,800 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In East Hemet the median rent is $1,311 per month. This is approximately $44 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $249 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $139 (9%) lower.
