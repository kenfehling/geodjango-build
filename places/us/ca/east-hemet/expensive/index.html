Home prices

The median home cost in East Hemet is $243,100. This is approximately $16,880 (7%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $25,600 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $393,800 (61%) lower.

Rents

East Hemet's median monthly rent is $1,311. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $44 (3%) higher. It is approximately $249 (23%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $139 (9%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
