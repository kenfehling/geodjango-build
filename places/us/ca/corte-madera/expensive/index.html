In comparison to its surrounding areas, Corte Madera is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Corte Madera's median home value is $1,200,300. It is approximately $254,180 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $982,800 (451%) higher, and it is roughly $102,500 (9%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Corte Madera's median rent is $2,642 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $564 (27%) higher. It is approximately $1,580 (148%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $747 (39%) higher.
