Home prices

The median home in Corte Madera costs $1,200,300. It is approximately $254,180 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $982,800 (451%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $102,500 (9%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Corte Madera's median monthly rent is $2,642. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $564 (27%) higher. It is approximately $1,580 (148%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $747 (39%) higher.
