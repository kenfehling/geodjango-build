Compared to its surrounding areas, Dixon is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dixon is valued at $385,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $87,600 (18%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $167,900 (77%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $712,400 (64%) lower.

Rents

Dixon's median rent is $1,426 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $24 (1%) higher. It is about $364 (34%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $469 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
