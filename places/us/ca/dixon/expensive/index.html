Compared to other places in this area, Dixon is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dixon is $385,400. It is approximately $87,600 (18%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $167,900 (77%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $712,400 (64%) lower.

Rents

Dixon's median monthly rent is $1,426. This is approximately $24 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $364 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $469 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
