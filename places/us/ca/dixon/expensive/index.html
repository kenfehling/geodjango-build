Home prices

The median home in Dixon is valued at $385,400. It is about $87,600 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $167,900 (77%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $712,400 (64%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Dixon the median rent is $1,426 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $24 (1%) higher.It is approximately $364 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $469 (24%) lower.
