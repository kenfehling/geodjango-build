Home prices

The cost of the median home in Twentynine Palms is $146,400. It is about $14,260 (10%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $71,100 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Riverside it is about $218,900 (59%) lower.

Rents

Twentynine Palms's median rent is $957 a month. It is about $72 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $105 (9%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Riverside is about $421 (30%) lower.
