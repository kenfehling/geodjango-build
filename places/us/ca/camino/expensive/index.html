Home prices

Camino's median home cost is $394,300. It is approximately $33,780 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $176,800 (81%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is roughly $57,400 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Camino the median rent is $1,405 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $242 (20%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $343 (32%) higher, and is roughly $142 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
