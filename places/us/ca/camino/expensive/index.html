Home prices

Rents

Camino's median monthly rent is $1,405. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $242 (20%) higher.It is roughly $343 (32%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $142 (11%) higher.
