In comparison to nearby areas, West Hollywood is significantly less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in West Hollywood is valued at $755,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $455,740 (37%) lower.It is roughly $537,900 (247%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $118,500 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Hollywood is $1,664. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $213 (11%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $602 (56%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $214 (14%) higher.
