Compared to the surrounding areas, West Hollywood is substantially less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in West Hollywood is $755,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $455,740 (37%) lower.It is about $537,900 (247%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $118,500 (18%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In West Hollywood the median rent is $1,664 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $213 (11%) lower.It is approximately $602 (56%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $214 (14%) higher.
