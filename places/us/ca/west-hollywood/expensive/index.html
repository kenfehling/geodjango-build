In comparison to the surrounding areas, West Hollywood is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in West Hollywood is $755,400. It is roughly $214,080 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $537,900 (247%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $118,500 (18%) higher.

Rents

West Hollywood's median monthly rent is $1,664. This is approximately $366 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $602 (56%) higher, and is about $214 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
