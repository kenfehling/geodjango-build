Home prices

West Hollywood's median home price is $755,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $214,080 (22%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $537,900 (247%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $118,500 (18%) higher.

Rents

West Hollywood's median monthly rent is $1,664. This is approximately $366 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $602 (56%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $214 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
