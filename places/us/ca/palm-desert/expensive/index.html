Compared to other places in this area, Palm Desert is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Diego.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Palm Desert is $350,400. This is roughly $73,780 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $132,900 (61%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Diego it is roughly $252,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

Palm Desert's median monthly rent is $1,319. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $35 (2%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $257 (24%) higher, and is roughly $376 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
