Compared to nearby areas, Palm Desert is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Diego however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Desert is valued at $350,400. This is about $73,780 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $132,900 (61%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $252,200 (41%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

In Palm Desert the median monthly rent is $1,319. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $35 (2%) lower. It is about $257 (24%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is approximately $376 (22%) lower.
