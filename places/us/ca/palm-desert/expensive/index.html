In comparison to nearby areas, Palm Desert is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of San Diego, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palm Desert costs $350,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $73,780 (17%) lower.It is about $132,900 (61%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $252,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

Palm Desert's median monthly rent is $1,319. This is about $35 (2%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $257 (24%) higher, and is roughly $376 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
