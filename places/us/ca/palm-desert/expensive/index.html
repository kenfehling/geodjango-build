In comparison to other places in this area, Palm Desert is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Diego.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Palm Desert is $350,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $73,780 (17%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $132,900 (61%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is roughly $252,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Palm Desert is $1,319 per month. This is about $35 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $257 (24%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego it is about $376 (22%) lower.
