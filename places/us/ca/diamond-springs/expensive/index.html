In comparison to other places in this area, Diamond Springs is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Sacramento however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Diamond Springs is $326,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $77,480 (19%) lower.It is about $108,900 (50%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento it is approximately $10,500 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Diamond Springs the median monthly rent is $1,304. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $63 (5%) higher.It is about $242 (22%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $41 (3%) higher.
