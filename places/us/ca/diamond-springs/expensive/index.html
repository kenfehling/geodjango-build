Home prices

The median home price in Diamond Springs is $326,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $77,480 (19%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $108,900 (50%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is approximately $10,500 (3%) lower.

Rents

Diamond Springs's median rent is $1,304 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $63 (5%) higher.It is approximately $242 (22%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $41 (3%) higher compared to the city of Sacramento.
