Compared to the surrounding areas, Diamond Springs is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Sacramento however, it is close to average.

Home prices

Diamond Springs's median home cost is $326,400. This is about $77,480 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $108,900 (50%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $10,500 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Diamond Springs's median monthly rent is $1,304. This is approximately $63 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $242 (22%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Sacramento is approximately $41 (3%) higher.
