Home prices

The cost of the median home in Benicia is $585,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $113,960 (24%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $368,100 (169%) higher, and is roughly $512,200 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Benicia's median monthly rent is $1,858. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $352 (23%) higher.It is roughly $796 (74%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $37 (1%) lower.
