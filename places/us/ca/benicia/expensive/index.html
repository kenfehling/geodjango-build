In comparison to its surrounding areas, Benicia is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Benicia costs $585,600. It is roughly $113,960 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $368,100 (169%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $512,200 (46%) lower.

Rents

Benicia's median rent is $1,858 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $352 (23%) higher. It is approximately $796 (74%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $37 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
