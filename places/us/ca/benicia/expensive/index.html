Compared to the surrounding areas, Benicia is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Benicia costs $585,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $113,960 (24%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $368,100 (169%) higher, and it is approximately $512,200 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Benicia's median monthly rent is $1,858. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $352 (23%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $796 (74%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $37 (1%) lower.
