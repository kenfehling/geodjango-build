Home prices

The median home in Cypress is valued at $632,900. It is about $9,120 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $415,400 (190%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Cypress the median monthly rent is $1,890. This is approximately $40 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $828 (77%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $440 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
