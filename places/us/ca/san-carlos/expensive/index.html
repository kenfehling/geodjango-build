In comparison to nearby areas, San Carlos is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot more expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

San Carlos's median home value is $1,625,700. This is roughly $234,680 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $1,408,200 (647%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $527,900 (48%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In San Carlos the median monthly rent is $2,321. It is approximately $273 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $1,259 (118%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $426 (22%) higher.
