Home prices

The value of the median home in Sierra Madre is $969,700. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $109,140 (12%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $752,200 (345%) higher, and is roughly $332,800 (52%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Sierra Madre the median rent is $1,615 per month. It is roughly $20 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $553 (52%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $165 (11%) higher.
