Home prices

The cost of the median home in Santa Cruz is $854,200. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $636,700 (292%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $243,600 (22%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Cruz is $1,889 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $281 (17%) higher.It is about $827 (77%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is is very similar.
