Home prices

The cost of the median home in Santa Cruz is $854,200. Compared to other places in this area it is is very similar. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $636,700 (292%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $243,600 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Cruz is $1,889 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $281 (17%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $827 (77%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is is very similar.
