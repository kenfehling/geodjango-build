Home prices

The median home in Santa Cruz is valued at $854,200. It is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $636,700 (292%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $243,600 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Santa Cruz is $1,889. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $281 (17%) higher. It is about $827 (77%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of San Francisco.
