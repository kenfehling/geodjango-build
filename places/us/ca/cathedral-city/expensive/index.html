In comparison to other places in this area, Cathedral City is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Diego however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cathedral City costs $279,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $21,080 (8%) higher.It is roughly $62,000 (28%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is approximately $323,100 (53%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cathedral City is $1,193. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $100 (9%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $131 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of San Diego it is about $502 (29%) lower.
