In comparison to the surrounding areas, Cathedral City is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of San Diego however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cathedral City costs $279,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $21,080 (8%) higher.It is approximately $62,000 (28%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $323,100 (53%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Cathedral City is $1,193 per month. This is approximately $100 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $131 (12%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Diego is roughly $502 (29%) lower.
