Home prices

The value of the median home in Cathedral City is $279,500. This is approximately $21,080 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $62,000 (28%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $323,100 (53%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Cathedral City is $1,193 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $100 (9%) higher. It is roughly $131 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $502 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
