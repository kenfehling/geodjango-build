Compared to its surrounding areas, Collierville is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Collierville costs $404,300. This is approximately $26,160 (6%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $186,800 (85%) higher, and is approximately $693,500 (63%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Collierville's median rent is $1,180 a month. It is about $43 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $118 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $715 (37%) lower.
