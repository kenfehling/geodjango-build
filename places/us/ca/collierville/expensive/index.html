In comparison to the surrounding areas, Collierville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Collierville is $404,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $26,160 (6%) lower.It is about $186,800 (85%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $693,500 (63%) lower.

Rents

Collierville's median monthly rent is $1,180. This is about $43 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $118 (11%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $715 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
