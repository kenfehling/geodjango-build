Compared to the surrounding areas, Collierville is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Collierville is $404,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $26,160 (6%) lower. It is approximately $186,800 (85%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $693,500 (63%) lower.

Rents

In Collierville the median rent is $1,180 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $43 (3%) lower. It is approximately $118 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $715 (37%) lower.
