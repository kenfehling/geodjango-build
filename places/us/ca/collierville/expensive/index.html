In comparison to its surrounding areas, Collierville is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Collierville's median home price is $404,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $26,160 (6%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $186,800 (85%) higher, and it is about $693,500 (63%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Collierville is $1,180 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $43 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $118 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $715 (37%) lower.
