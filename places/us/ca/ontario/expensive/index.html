Compared to the surrounding areas, Ontario is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ontario is $375,000. It is roughly $106,920 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $157,500 (72%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $261,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

Ontario's median rent is $1,499 a month. It is about $272 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $437 (41%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $49 (3%) higher.
