Compared to other places in this area, Ontario is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Ontario costs $375,000. It is approximately $106,920 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $157,500 (72%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $261,900 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ontario is $1,499 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $272 (15%) lower. It is about $437 (41%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $49 (3%) higher.
