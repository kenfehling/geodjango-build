In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ontario is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Ontario is $375,000. This is roughly $106,920 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $157,500 (72%) higher, and it is about $261,900 (41%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Ontario the median monthly rent is $1,499. This is roughly $272 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $437 (41%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $49 (3%) higher.
