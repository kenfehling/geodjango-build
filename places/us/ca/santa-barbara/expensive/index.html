Compared to the surrounding areas, Santa Barbara is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Barbara is valued at $1,051,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $66,040 (6%) higher. It is approximately $834,000 (383%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $414,600 (65%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Santa Barbara is $1,786 per month. It is about $372 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $724 (68%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $336 (23%) higher.
