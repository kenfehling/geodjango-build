In comparison to its surrounding areas, Santa Barbara is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Santa Barbara costs $1,051,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $66,040 (6%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $834,000 (383%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $414,600 (65%) higher.

Rents

Santa Barbara's median monthly rent is $1,786. This is roughly $372 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $724 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $336 (23%) higher.
