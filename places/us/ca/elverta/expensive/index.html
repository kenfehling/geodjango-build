Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elverta is $1,509. This is approximately $137 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $447 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Oakland is approximately $64 (4%) higher.
