Compared to other places in this area, Newport Beach is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Newport Beach costs $1,898,900. It is about $1,042,840 (121%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,681,400 (773%) higher, and it is about $1,262,000 (198%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Newport Beach is $2,251. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $145 (6%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $1,189 (111%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $801 (55%) higher.
