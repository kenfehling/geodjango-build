Home prices

Newport Beach's median home cost is $1,898,900. It is roughly $1,042,840 (121%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $1,681,400 (773%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $1,262,000 (198%) higher.

Rents

Newport Beach's median rent is $2,251 per month. It is roughly $145 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $1,189 (111%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $801 (55%) higher.
