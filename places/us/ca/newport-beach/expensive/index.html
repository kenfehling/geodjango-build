Home prices

The median home in Newport Beach is valued at $1,898,900. This is about $1,042,840 (121%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $1,681,400 (773%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $1,262,000 (198%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Newport Beach is $2,251 a month. This is about $145 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $1,189 (111%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $801 (55%) higher.
