Home prices

The median home price in Elizabeth Lake is $281,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $35,720 (11%) lower.It is approximately $64,400 (29%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $355,000 (55%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elizabeth Lake is $1,363. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $144 (11%) higher.It is approximately $301 (28%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $87 (6%) lower.
