The median monthly rent in Elizabeth Lake is $1,363. This is roughly $144 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $301 (28%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $87 (6%) lower.
