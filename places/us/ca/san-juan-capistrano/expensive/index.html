Compared to its nearby areas, San Juan Capistrano is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Los Angeles, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Juan Capistrano costs $720,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $320,440 (30%) lower.It is about $502,700 (231%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $83,300 (13%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Juan Capistrano the median rent is $2,002 a month. It is approximately $167 (7%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $940 (88%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $552 (38%) higher.
