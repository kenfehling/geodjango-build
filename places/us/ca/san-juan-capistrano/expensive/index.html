Home prices

The median home in San Juan Capistrano is valued at $720,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $120,480 (14%) lower. It is roughly $502,700 (231%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $83,300 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Juan Capistrano the median rent is $2,002 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $143 (6%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $940 (88%) higher, and is approximately $552 (38%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
