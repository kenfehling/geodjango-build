Home prices

The median home value in San Juan Capistrano is $720,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $320,440 (30%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $502,700 (231%) higher, and it is about $83,300 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In San Juan Capistrano the median rent is $2,002 a month. It is approximately $167 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $940 (88%) higher, and is roughly $552 (38%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
