Home prices

The median home in Pismo Beach is valued at $768,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $129,600 (20%) higher.It is roughly $551,100 (253%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $282,900 (26%) lower compared to the city of Santa Barbara.

Rents

Pismo Beach's median rent is $1,774 per month. It is about $343 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $712 (67%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Santa Barbara.
