In comparison to nearby areas, Pismo Beach is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Santa Barbara, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Pismo Beach costs $768,600. It is approximately $129,600 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $551,100 (253%) higher, and compared to the city of Santa Barbara is roughly $282,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

Pismo Beach's median rent is $1,774 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $343 (23%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $712 (67%) higher, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara.
