Home prices

The median home cost in Winchester is $315,500. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $76,600 (32%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $98,000 (45%) higher, and it is about $321,400 (50%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Winchester is $1,272 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $65 (4%) lower. It is about $210 (19%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $178 (12%) lower.
