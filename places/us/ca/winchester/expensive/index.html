Home prices

The value of the median home in Winchester is $315,500. It is about $76,600 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $98,000 (45%) higher, and is about $321,400 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Winchester is $1,272. This is about $65 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $210 (19%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $178 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
