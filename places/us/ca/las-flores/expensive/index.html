Home prices

The value of the median home in Las Flores is $771,800. This is about $13,540 (1%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $554,300 (254%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $134,900 (21%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Las Flores is $2,180. This is roughly $231 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $1,118 (105%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $730 (50%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
