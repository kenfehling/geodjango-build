Home prices

The cost of the median home in Salinas is $415,000. It is about $50,940 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $197,500 (90%) higher, and compared to the city of San Jose is approximately $449,600 (52%) lower.

Rents

Salinas's median monthly rent is $1,411. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $127 (8%) lower. It is about $349 (32%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Jose it is approximately $696 (33%) lower.
