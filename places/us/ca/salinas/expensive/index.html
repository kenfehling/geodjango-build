Compared to the surrounding areas, Salinas is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Jose, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Salinas is $415,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $50,940 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $197,500 (90%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Jose is about $449,600 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Salinas is $1,411 per month. It is roughly $127 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $349 (32%) higher, and is approximately $696 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
