Compared to its nearby areas, Rancho Santa Margarita is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Rancho Santa Margarita is $662,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $120,200 (15%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $444,700 (204%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $25,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

Rancho Santa Margarita's median monthly rent is $2,084. This is about $315 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $1,022 (96%) higher, and is roughly $634 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
