Compared to its surrounding areas, Rancho Santa Margarita is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Rancho Santa Margarita is $662,200. It is about $120,200 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $444,700 (204%) higher, and it is approximately $25,300 (3%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Rancho Santa Margarita is $2,084 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $315 (13%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,022 (96%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $634 (43%) higher.
