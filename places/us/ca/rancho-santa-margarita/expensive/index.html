Compared to the surrounding areas, Rancho Santa Margarita is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is close to average.

Home prices

Rancho Santa Margarita's median home cost is $662,200. It is roughly $110,540 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $444,700 (204%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $25,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Rancho Santa Margarita the median monthly rent is $2,084. This is roughly $283 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $1,022 (96%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $634 (43%) higher.
