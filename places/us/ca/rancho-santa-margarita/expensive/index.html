Compared to other places in this area, Rancho Santa Margarita is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho Santa Margarita is valued at $662,200. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $110,540 (14%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $444,700 (204%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $25,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Rancho Santa Margarita the median rent is $2,084 per month. It is about $283 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $1,022 (96%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $634 (43%) higher.
