In comparison to the surrounding areas, Rancho Santa Margarita is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho Santa Margarita costs $662,200. This is approximately $110,540 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $444,700 (204%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $25,300 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rancho Santa Margarita is $2,084 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $283 (11%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $1,022 (96%) higher, and it is about $634 (43%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
