Rents

The median rent in Del Mar is $2,320 per month. It is approximately $70 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $1,258 (118%) higher, and is roughly $870 (60%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
