Compared to other places in this area, Larkspur is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of San Francisco, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Larkspur costs $1,309,200. This is roughly $190,120 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $1,091,700 (501%) higher, and is approximately $211,400 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Larkspur's median rent is $2,080 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $164 (7%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,018 (95%) higher, and it is approximately $185 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
