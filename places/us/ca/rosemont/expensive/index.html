Home prices

Rosemont's median home value is $293,400. This is roughly $3,460 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $75,900 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $804,400 (73%) lower.

Rents

Rosemont's median rent is $1,365 a month. This is approximately $122 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $303 (28%) higher, and it is approximately $530 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
