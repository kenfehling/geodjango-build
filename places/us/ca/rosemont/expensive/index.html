Home prices

The median home in Rosemont is valued at $293,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $3,460 (1%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $75,900 (34%) higher, and is approximately $804,400 (73%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Rosemont's median rent is $1,365 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $122 (9%) higher. It is approximately $303 (28%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $530 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
