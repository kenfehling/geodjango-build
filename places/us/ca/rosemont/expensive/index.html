Home prices

The median home price in Rosemont is $293,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $3,460 (1%) higher.It is roughly $75,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $804,400 (73%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rosemont is $1,365 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $122 (9%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $303 (28%) higher, and it is approximately $530 (27%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
