Home prices

The median home in Oceanside costs $492,600. It is approximately $131,900 (21%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $275,100 (126%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $144,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Oceanside's median rent is $1,753 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $74 (4%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $691 (65%) higher, and is roughly $303 (20%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
