Home prices

The median home in Oceanside costs $492,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $131,900 (21%) lower. It is roughly $275,100 (126%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $144,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in Oceanside is $1,753 a month. This is roughly $74 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $691 (65%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $303 (20%) higher.
