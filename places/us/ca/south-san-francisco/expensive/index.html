In comparison to its surrounding areas, South San Francisco is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

South San Francisco's median home cost is $844,000. This is approximately $107,420 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $626,500 (288%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $253,800 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

South San Francisco's median rent is $2,105 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $145 (6%) lower. It is approximately $1,043 (98%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $210 (11%) higher.
