Compared to the surrounding areas, San Francisco is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is a bit more expensive even compared to the city of San Jose.

Home prices

The median home price in San Francisco is $1,097,800. This is about $278,940 (34%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $880,300 (404%) higher, and is about $233,200 (26%) higher compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

San Francisco's median rent is $1,895 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $197 (9%) lower.It is approximately $833 (78%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $212 (10%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.
