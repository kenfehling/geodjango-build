In comparison to other places in this area, San Francisco is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of San Jose, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Francisco is valued at $1,097,800. This is about $278,940 (34%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $880,300 (404%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $233,200 (26%) higher compared to the city of San Jose.

Rents

The median rent in San Francisco is $1,895 a month. It is roughly $197 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $833 (78%) higher, and it is roughly $212 (10%) lower compared to the city of San Jose.
