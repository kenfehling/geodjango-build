Compared to nearby areas, Alameda is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Alameda is valued at $859,900. This is about $127,560 (17%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $642,400 (295%) higher, and it is roughly $237,900 (21%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Alameda the median rent is $1,836 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $774 (72%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $59 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
