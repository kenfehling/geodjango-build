Home prices

The median home in Alameda costs $859,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $94,600 (9%) lower.It is approximately $642,400 (295%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $237,900 (21%) lower.

Rents

Alameda's median monthly rent is $1,836. This is about $250 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $774 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $59 (3%) lower.
