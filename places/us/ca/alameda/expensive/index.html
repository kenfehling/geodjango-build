In comparison to its nearby areas, Alameda is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Alameda's median home value is $859,900. This is approximately $127,560 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $642,400 (295%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $237,900 (21%) lower.

Rents

Alameda's median monthly rent is $1,836. Compared to nearby areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $774 (72%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $59 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
