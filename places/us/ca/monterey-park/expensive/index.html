Compared to its surrounding areas, Monterey Park is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Monterey Park is valued at $616,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $96,240 (18%) higher. It is roughly $398,500 (183%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $20,900 (3%) lower.

Rents

Monterey Park's median monthly rent is $1,400. This is approximately $26 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $338 (31%) higher, and it is about $50 (3%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
