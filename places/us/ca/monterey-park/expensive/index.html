Home prices

The value of the median home in Monterey Park is $616,000. It is roughly $96,240 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $398,500 (183%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $20,900 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monterey Park is $1,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $26 (1%) higher.It is about $338 (31%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $50 (3%) lower.
