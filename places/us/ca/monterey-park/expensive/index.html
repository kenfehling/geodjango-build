Compared to the surrounding areas, Monterey Park is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home value in Monterey Park is $616,000. It is roughly $96,240 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $398,500 (183%) higher, and it is approximately $20,900 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Monterey Park the median rent is $1,400 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $26 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $338 (31%) higher, and it is roughly $50 (3%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
