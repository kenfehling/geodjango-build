Home prices

The median home cost in Monterey Park is $616,000. This is approximately $96,240 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $398,500 (183%) higher, and it is roughly $20,900 (3%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Monterey Park is $1,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $26 (1%) higher.It is about $338 (31%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $50 (3%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
