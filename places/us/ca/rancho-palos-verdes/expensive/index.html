Home prices

The price of the median home in Rancho Palos Verdes is $1,156,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $123,200 (11%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $939,200 (431%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $519,800 (81%) higher.

Rents

In Rancho Palos Verdes the median rent is $2,643 a month. This is about $535 (25%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,581 (148%) higher, and it is about $1,193 (82%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
