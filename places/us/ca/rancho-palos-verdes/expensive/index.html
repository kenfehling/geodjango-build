Compared to the surrounding areas, Rancho Palos Verdes is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho Palos Verdes costs $1,156,700. It is approximately $123,200 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $939,200 (431%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $519,800 (81%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rancho Palos Verdes is $2,643 per month. This is about $535 (25%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $1,581 (148%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is about $1,193 (82%) higher.
