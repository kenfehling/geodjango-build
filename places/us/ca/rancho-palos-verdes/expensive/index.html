Compared to its nearby areas, Rancho Palos Verdes is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho Palos Verdes is valued at $1,156,700. It is approximately $123,200 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $939,200 (431%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $519,800 (81%) higher.

Rents

Rancho Palos Verdes's median monthly rent is $2,643. It is roughly $535 (25%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $1,581 (148%) higher, and is roughly $1,193 (82%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
