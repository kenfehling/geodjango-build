Compared to its surrounding areas, Rancho Palos Verdes is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home value in Rancho Palos Verdes is $1,156,700. It is approximately $123,200 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $939,200 (431%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $519,800 (81%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rancho Palos Verdes is $2,643. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $535 (25%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $1,581 (148%) higher, and is about $1,193 (82%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
