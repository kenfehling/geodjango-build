Compared to other places in this area, Rancho Palos Verdes is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Rancho Palos Verdes is valued at $1,156,700. It is about $123,200 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $939,200 (431%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $519,800 (81%) higher.

Rents

Rancho Palos Verdes's median monthly rent is $2,643. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $535 (25%) higher.It is about $1,581 (148%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $1,193 (82%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
