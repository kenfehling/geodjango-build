In comparison to its surrounding areas, Monterey is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Monterey is $782,500. This is about $199,160 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $565,000 (259%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $315,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Monterey the median rent is $1,793 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $389 (17%) lower.It is about $731 (68%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $102 (5%) lower.
