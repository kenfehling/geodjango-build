Compared to nearby areas, Monterey is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Monterey is valued at $782,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $199,160 (20%) lower. It is about $565,000 (259%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $315,300 (28%) lower.

Rents

Monterey's median rent is $1,793 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $389 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $731 (68%) higher, and it is approximately $102 (5%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
