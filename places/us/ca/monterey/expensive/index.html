In comparison to other places in this area, Monterey is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Monterey is valued at $782,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $199,160 (20%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $565,000 (259%) higher, and it is roughly $315,300 (28%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Monterey the median rent is $1,793 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $389 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is about $731 (68%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $102 (5%) lower.
