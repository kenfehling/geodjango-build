Home prices

The median home in Loma Linda is valued at $367,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $26,440 (7%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $149,900 (68%) higher, and it is roughly $269,500 (42%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Loma Linda is $1,338. This is about $118 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $276 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $112 (7%) lower.
