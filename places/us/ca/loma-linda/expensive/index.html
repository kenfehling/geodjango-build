Compared to other places in this area, Loma Linda is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Loma Linda is $367,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $26,440 (7%) higher.It is about $149,900 (68%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $269,500 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Loma Linda is $1,338. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $118 (9%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $276 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $112 (7%) lower.
