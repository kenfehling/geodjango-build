Home prices

The median home value in Loma Linda is $367,400. This is about $26,440 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $149,900 (68%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $269,500 (42%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Loma Linda's median monthly rent is $1,338. This is roughly $118 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $276 (25%) higher, and it is roughly $112 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
