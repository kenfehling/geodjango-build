Compared to other places in this area, Lakewood is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakewood is $568,600. It is about $57,500 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $351,100 (161%) higher, and it is approximately $68,300 (10%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Lakewood's median rent is $1,839 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $181 (10%) higher. It is roughly $777 (73%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $389 (26%) higher.
