Compared to nearby areas, Lakewood is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in Lakewood is valued at $568,600. It is about $57,500 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $351,100 (161%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $68,300 (10%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Lakewood's median rent is $1,839 a month. This is roughly $181 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $777 (73%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $389 (26%) higher.
