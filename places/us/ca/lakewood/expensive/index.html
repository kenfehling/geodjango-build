Compared to other places in this area, Lakewood is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakewood is $568,600. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $57,500 (11%) higher.It is about $351,100 (161%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $68,300 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Lakewood the median rent is $1,839 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $181 (10%) higher.It is approximately $777 (73%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $389 (26%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
