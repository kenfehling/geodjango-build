Compared to other places in this area, Cupertino is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cupertino costs $1,711,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $713,980 (71%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,493,800 (686%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $613,500 (55%) higher.

Rents

Cupertino's median monthly rent is $3,163. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $1,112 (54%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $2,101 (197%) higher, and is roughly $1,268 (66%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
