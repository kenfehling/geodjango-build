Home prices

The median home value in Bloomington is $270,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $56,000 (17%) lower. It is about $52,500 (24%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $366,900 (57%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Bloomington's median rent is $1,240 a month. This is about $65 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $178 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $210 (14%) lower.
