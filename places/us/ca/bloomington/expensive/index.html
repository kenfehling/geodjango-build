Home prices

The median home value in Bloomington is $270,000. This is roughly $56,000 (17%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $52,500 (24%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $366,900 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Bloomington's median monthly rent is $1,240. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $65 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $178 (16%) higher, and it is about $210 (14%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
