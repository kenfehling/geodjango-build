Compared to the surrounding areas, Irwindale is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Irwindale costs $430,200. It is roughly $80,340 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $212,700 (97%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $206,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Irwindale is $1,333 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $224 (14%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $271 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $117 (8%) lower.
