In comparison to its surrounding areas, Irwindale is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Irwindale is $430,200. This is about $80,340 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $212,700 (97%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $206,700 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Irwindale is $1,333 per month. It is roughly $224 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $271 (25%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $117 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
