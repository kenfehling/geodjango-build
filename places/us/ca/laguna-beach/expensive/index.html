Home prices

The median home in Laguna Beach is valued at $1,771,600. This is roughly $1,116,360 (170%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $1,554,100 (714%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $1,134,700 (178%) higher.

Rents
