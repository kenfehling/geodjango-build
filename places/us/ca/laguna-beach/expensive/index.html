Home prices

The price of the median home in Laguna Beach is $1,771,600. It is roughly $1,116,360 (170%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $1,554,100 (714%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $1,134,700 (178%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Laguna Beach the median rent is $2,299 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $153 (7%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $1,237 (116%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $849 (58%) higher.
