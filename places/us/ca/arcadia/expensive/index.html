Home prices

The median home in Arcadia is valued at $1,097,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $430,740 (64%) higher. It is about $880,100 (404%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $460,700 (72%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Arcadia the median rent is $1,662 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $73 (4%) higher. It is approximately $600 (56%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $212 (14%) higher.
