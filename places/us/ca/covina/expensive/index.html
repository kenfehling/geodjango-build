Home prices

The price of the median home in Covina is $509,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $60,240 (13%) higher. It is roughly $291,700 (134%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $127,700 (20%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Covina the median rent is $1,484 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $200 (11%) lower. It is approximately $422 (39%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $34 (2%) higher.
