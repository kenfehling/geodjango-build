Home prices

The median home price in Covina is $509,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $60,240 (13%) higher.It is roughly $291,700 (134%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $127,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Covina the median rent is $1,484 per month. This is about $200 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $422 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $34 (2%) higher.
