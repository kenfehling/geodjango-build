In comparison to its nearby areas, Covina is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Covina costs $509,200. This is roughly $60,240 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $291,700 (134%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $127,700 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Covina is $1,484. This is approximately $200 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $422 (39%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $34 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
