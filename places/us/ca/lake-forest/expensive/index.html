The cost of the median home in Lake Forest is $647,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $9,640 (1%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $430,000 (197%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $10,600 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Lake Forest the median monthly rent is $2,025. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $91 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $963 (90%) higher, and it is about $575 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
