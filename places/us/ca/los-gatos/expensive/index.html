In comparison to its surrounding areas, Los Gatos is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly more expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Los Gatos is valued at $1,694,000. It is roughly $682,040 (67%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $1,476,500 (678%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $596,200 (54%) higher.

Rents

Los Gatos's median monthly rent is $2,341. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $239 (11%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $1,279 (120%) higher, and it is roughly $446 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
