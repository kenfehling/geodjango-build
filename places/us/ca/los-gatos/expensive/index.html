Home prices

The median home value in Los Gatos is $1,694,000. This is approximately $682,040 (67%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,476,500 (678%) higher, and is about $596,200 (54%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Los Gatos the median monthly rent is $2,341. This is roughly $239 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $1,279 (120%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $446 (23%) higher.
