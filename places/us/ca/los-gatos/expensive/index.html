In comparison to nearby areas, Los Gatos is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Los Gatos costs $1,694,000. This is roughly $212,040 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $1,476,500 (678%) higher, and is about $596,200 (54%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Los Gatos is $2,341. This is roughly $301 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $1,279 (120%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $446 (23%) higher.
