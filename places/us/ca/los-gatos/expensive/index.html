In comparison to its surrounding areas, Los Gatos is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially more expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Los Gatos costs $1,694,000. It is approximately $212,040 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $1,476,500 (678%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $596,200 (54%) higher.

Rents

In Los Gatos the median monthly rent is $2,341. It is roughly $301 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $1,279 (120%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $446 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
