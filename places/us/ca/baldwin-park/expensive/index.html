Compared to other places in this area, Baldwin Park is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Baldwin Park is $408,600. This is approximately $70,680 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $191,100 (87%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $228,300 (35%) lower.

Rents

Baldwin Park's median rent is $1,471 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $63 (4%) lower. It is about $409 (38%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $21 (1%) higher.
