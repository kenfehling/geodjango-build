Compared to other places in this area, Baldwin Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Baldwin Park is $408,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $70,680 (14%) lower.It is roughly $191,100 (87%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $228,300 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Baldwin Park the median rent is $1,471 a month. It is approximately $63 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $409 (38%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $21 (1%) higher.
