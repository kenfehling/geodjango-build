In comparison to other places in this area, Baldwin Park is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Baldwin Park's median home value is $408,600. It is about $70,680 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $191,100 (87%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $228,300 (35%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In Baldwin Park the median rent is $1,471 a month. This is roughly $63 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $409 (38%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $21 (1%) higher.
