Home prices

The value of the median home in San Jacinto is $241,000. It is about $8,240 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $23,500 (10%) higher, and it is approximately $395,900 (62%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median rent in San Jacinto is $1,224 per month. This is roughly $16 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $162 (15%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $226 (15%) lower.
