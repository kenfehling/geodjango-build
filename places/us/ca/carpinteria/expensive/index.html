In comparison to its nearby areas, Carpinteria is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home value in Carpinteria is $705,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $322,260 (31%) lower. It is roughly $487,800 (224%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $68,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Carpinteria is $1,940 per month. This is roughly $85 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $878 (82%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $490 (33%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
