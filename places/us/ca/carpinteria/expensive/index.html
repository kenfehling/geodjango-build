Compared to other places in this area, Carpinteria is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. It is somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

Carpinteria's median home price is $705,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $322,260 (31%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $487,800 (224%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is approximately $68,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carpinteria is $1,940. This is about $85 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $878 (82%) higher, and it is roughly $490 (33%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.
