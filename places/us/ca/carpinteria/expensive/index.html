Home prices

Carpinteria's median home price is $705,300. It is roughly $322,260 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $487,800 (224%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $68,400 (10%) higher compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carpinteria is $1,940. This is roughly $85 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $878 (82%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $490 (33%) higher.
