Home prices

Rents

Carpinteria's median monthly rent is $1,940. It is approximately $238 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $878 (82%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $490 (33%) higher.
