Home prices

The median home in Carpinteria is valued at $705,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $604,420 (46%) lower.It is approximately $487,800 (224%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $68,400 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Carpinteria's median rent is $1,940 per month. It is approximately $238 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $878 (82%) higher, and is about $490 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
