Much like other places in this area, it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Santa Barbara, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in San Luis Obispo costs $643,200. This is approximately $31,520 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $425,700 (195%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara it is approximately $408,300 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in San Luis Obispo is $1,575. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $123 (8%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $513 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara is roughly $211 (11%) lower.
