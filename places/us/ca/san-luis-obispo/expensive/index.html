Home prices

The median home value in San Luis Obispo is $643,200. This is approximately $31,520 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $425,700 (195%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $408,300 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Santa Barbara.

Rents

San Luis Obispo's median rent is $1,575 a month. It is approximately $123 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $513 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Santa Barbara is roughly $211 (11%) lower.
