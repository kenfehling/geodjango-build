Compared to its surrounding areas, Lafayette is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's somewhat more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Lafayette costs $1,428,900. It is approximately $366,660 (34%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $1,211,400 (556%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $331,100 (30%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Lafayette is $2,222 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $90 (3%) lower.It is approximately $1,160 (109%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $327 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
