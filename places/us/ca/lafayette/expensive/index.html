Home prices

The median home in Lafayette is valued at $1,428,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $366,660 (34%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $1,211,400 (556%) higher, and it is approximately $331,100 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Lafayette's median monthly rent is $2,222. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $90 (3%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,160 (109%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $327 (17%) higher.
