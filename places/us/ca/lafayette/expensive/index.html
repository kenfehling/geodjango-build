In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lafayette is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a bit more expensive even compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Lafayette costs $1,428,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $366,660 (34%) higher.It is roughly $1,211,400 (556%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $331,100 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Lafayette's median rent is $2,222 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $90 (3%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $1,160 (109%) higher, and is about $327 (17%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
