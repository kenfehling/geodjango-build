Compared to its surrounding areas, Chico is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Sacramento, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Chico costs $327,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $78,240 (31%) higher.It is roughly $110,200 (50%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sacramento is about $9,200 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Chico is $1,100 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $189 (20%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $38 (3%) higher, and it is roughly $163 (12%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
