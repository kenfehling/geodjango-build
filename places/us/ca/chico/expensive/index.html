Compared to nearby areas, Chico is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Sacramento, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Chico costs $327,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $78,240 (31%) higher.It is approximately $110,200 (50%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $9,200 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

The median rent in Chico is $1,100 per month. It is roughly $189 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $38 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is approximately $163 (12%) lower.
