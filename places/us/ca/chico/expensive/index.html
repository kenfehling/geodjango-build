Home prices

The median home in Chico is valued at $327,700. It is approximately $76,900 (30%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $110,200 (50%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is about $9,200 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Chico is $1,100 a month. It is approximately $91 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $38 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $163 (12%) lower compared to the city of Sacramento.
