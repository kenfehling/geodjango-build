Home prices

The median home in Gonzales costs $356,000. This is about $112,920 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $138,500 (63%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Jose it is about $508,600 (58%) lower.

Rents

Gonzales's median monthly rent is $1,190. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $243 (16%) lower. It is about $128 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $917 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.
