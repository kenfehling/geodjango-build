Home prices

The median home in Tiburon costs $2,000,001. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $457,321 (29%) higher.It is about $1,782,501 (819%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $902,201 (82%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tiburon is $2,440. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $141 (6%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $1,378 (129%) higher, and is about $545 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
