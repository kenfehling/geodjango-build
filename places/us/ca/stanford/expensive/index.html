In comparison to other places in this area, Stanford is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Stanford is $2,000,001. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $235,760 (13%) higher.It is about $1,782,501 (819%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $902,201 (82%) higher.

Rents

Stanford's median rent is $1,617 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $1,014 (38%) lower.It is about $555 (52%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $278 (14%) lower.
