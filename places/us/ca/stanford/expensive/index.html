Home prices

The median home in Stanford is valued at $2,000,001. This is approximately $235,760 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $1,782,501 (819%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $902,201 (82%) higher.

Rents

In Stanford the median monthly rent is $1,617. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $1,014 (38%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $555 (52%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $278 (14%) lower.
