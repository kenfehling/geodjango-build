Home prices

The median home cost in Isla Vista is $466,700. This is about $635,720 (57%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $249,200 (114%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $170,200 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Isla Vista's median rent is $1,503 per month. This is roughly $712 (32%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $441 (41%) higher, and it is roughly $53 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
