Home prices

The median home price in Biggs is $211,400. This is roughly $29,860 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $6,100 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento it is about $125,500 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Biggs the median monthly rent is $992. This is about $65 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $70 (6%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $271 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
