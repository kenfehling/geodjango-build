Home prices

The median home in Palermo is valued at $215,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $25,640 (13%) higher. It is approximately $2,200 (1%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $121,600 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Palermo's median monthly rent is $885. It is about $132 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $177 (16%) lower, and it is roughly $378 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Sacramento.
