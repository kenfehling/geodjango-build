Compared to nearby areas, La Verne is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Los Angeles however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in La Verne is $588,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $54,500 (10%) higher. It is roughly $370,800 (170%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $48,600 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in La Verne is $1,510 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $140 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $448 (42%) higher, and is about $60 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
