In comparison to other places in this area, La Verne is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in La Verne costs $588,300. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $54,500 (10%) higher. It is approximately $370,800 (170%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $48,600 (7%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

In La Verne the median monthly rent is $1,510. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $140 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $448 (42%) higher, and is roughly $60 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
