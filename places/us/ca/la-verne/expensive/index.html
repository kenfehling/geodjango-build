In comparison to its surrounding areas, La Verne is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Home prices

The median home in La Verne costs $588,300. This is about $54,500 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $370,800 (170%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $48,600 (7%) lower.

Rents

In La Verne the median rent is $1,510 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $140 (8%) lower.It is roughly $448 (42%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $60 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
