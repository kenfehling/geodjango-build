Home prices

La Verne's median home cost is $588,300. It is approximately $54,500 (10%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $370,800 (170%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $48,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

La Verne's median monthly rent is $1,510. This is about $140 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $448 (42%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $60 (4%) higher.
