Compared to other places in this area, La Verne is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in La Verne is $588,300. This is about $54,500 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $370,800 (170%) higher, and it is roughly $48,600 (7%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

The median monthly rent in La Verne is $1,510. This is roughly $140 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $448 (42%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is approximately $60 (4%) higher.
