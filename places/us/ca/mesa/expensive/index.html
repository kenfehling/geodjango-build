In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mesa is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Fresno.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Mesa is $414,000. It is about $115,000 (38%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $196,500 (90%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Fresno is approximately $172,000 (71%) higher.

Rents

Mesa's median monthly rent is $1,413. It is roughly $302 (27%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $351 (33%) higher, and it is roughly $408 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Fresno.
