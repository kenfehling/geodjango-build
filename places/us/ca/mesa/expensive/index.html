Home prices

The median home in Mesa costs $414,000. It is about $115,000 (38%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $196,500 (90%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Fresno it is approximately $172,000 (71%) higher.

Rents

Mesa's median rent is $1,413 a month. This is approximately $302 (27%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $351 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Fresno it is roughly $408 (40%) higher.
