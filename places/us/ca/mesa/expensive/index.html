Compared to its surrounding areas, Mesa is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Fresno.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mesa is $414,000. This is about $115,000 (38%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $196,500 (90%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Fresno it is roughly $172,000 (71%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mesa is $1,413. It is roughly $302 (27%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $351 (33%) higher, and is about $408 (40%) higher compared to the city of Fresno.
