Home prices

The median home in Colton is valued at $257,500. This is roughly $77,940 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $40,000 (18%) higher, and it is approximately $379,400 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Colton's median rent is $1,189 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $79 (6%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $127 (11%) higher, and is approximately $261 (18%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
