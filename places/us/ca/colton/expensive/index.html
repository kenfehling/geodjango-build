Home prices

Rents

Colton's median rent is $1,189 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $79 (6%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $127 (11%) higher, and it is roughly $261 (18%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
