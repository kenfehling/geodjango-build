Compared to its surrounding areas, Joshua Tree is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of San Diego, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Joshua Tree's median home price is $150,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $15,580 (11%) higher. It is approximately $67,400 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $452,500 (75%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Joshua Tree is $896 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $166 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $799 (47%) lower in comparison to the city of San Diego.
