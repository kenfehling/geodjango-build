Home prices

Joshua Tree's median home cost is $150,100. This is about $15,580 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $67,400 (30%) lower, and is roughly $452,500 (75%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.

Rents

The median rent in Joshua Tree is $896 per month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $166 (15%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $799 (47%) lower compared to the city of San Diego.
