Compared to other places in this area, Marina is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Marina's median home value is $513,000. This is roughly $34,080 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $295,500 (135%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $584,800 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Marina the median rent is $1,402 a month. This is about $348 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $340 (32%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is about $493 (26%) lower.
