Home prices

Rents

In Hillsborough the median monthly rent is $3,490. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $1,087 (45%) higher.It is about $2,428 (228%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $1,595 (84%) higher.
