Home prices

The median home price in Windsor is $597,500. It is approximately $15,080 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $380,000 (174%) higher, and it is approximately $500,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Windsor the median monthly rent is $1,770. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $278 (18%) higher. It is about $708 (66%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $125 (6%) lower.
