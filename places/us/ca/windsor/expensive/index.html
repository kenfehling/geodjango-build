Home prices

The median home in Windsor costs $597,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $15,080 (2%) lower. It is approximately $380,000 (174%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $500,300 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Windsor's median rent is $1,770 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $278 (18%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $708 (66%) higher, and it is approximately $125 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
