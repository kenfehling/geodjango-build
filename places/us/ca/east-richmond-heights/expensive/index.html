Compared to nearby areas, East Richmond Heights is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in East Richmond Heights is valued at $615,900. It is approximately $112,200 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $398,400 (183%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $481,900 (43%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in East Richmond Heights is $2,007 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $323 (19%) higher. It is approximately $945 (88%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $112 (5%) higher.
