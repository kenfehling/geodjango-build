Home prices

The median home in East Richmond Heights is valued at $615,900. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $112,200 (22%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $398,400 (183%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is roughly $481,900 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in East Richmond Heights is $2,007 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $323 (19%) higher.It is approximately $945 (88%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $112 (5%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
