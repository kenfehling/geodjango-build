In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Richmond Heights is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in East Richmond Heights is $615,900. This is about $112,200 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $398,400 (183%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $481,900 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In East Richmond Heights the median monthly rent is $2,007. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $323 (19%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $945 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is about $112 (5%) higher.
