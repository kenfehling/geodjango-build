Home prices

The median home in Los Banos costs $293,400. It is approximately $87,480 (42%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $75,900 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $571,200 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of San Jose.

Rents

In Los Banos the median rent is $1,260 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $396 (45%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $198 (18%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Jose is approximately $847 (40%) lower.
