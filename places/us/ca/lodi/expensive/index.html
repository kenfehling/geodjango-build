Compared to its surrounding areas, Lodi is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of San Francisco, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Lodi's median home cost is $323,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $101,240 (23%) lower. It is approximately $105,900 (48%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $774,400 (70%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lodi is $1,181. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $30 (2%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $119 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $714 (37%) lower.
