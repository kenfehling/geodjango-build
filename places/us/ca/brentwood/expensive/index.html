In comparison to nearby areas, Brentwood is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brentwood is valued at $580,500. It is approximately $29,760 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $363,000 (166%) higher, and is about $517,300 (47%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

In Brentwood the median rent is $2,156 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $326 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $1,094 (103%) higher, and it is approximately $261 (13%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
