Compared to other places in this area, Brentwood is a little more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Brentwood is valued at $580,500. This is roughly $29,760 (5%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $363,000 (166%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $517,300 (47%) lower.

Rents

Brentwood's median monthly rent is $2,156. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $326 (17%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $1,094 (103%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $261 (13%) higher.
