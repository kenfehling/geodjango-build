Home prices

The median home value in Pleasure Point is $913,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $145,700 (18%) higher. It is approximately $696,400 (320%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is about $183,900 (16%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

The median rent in Pleasure Point is $1,458 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $336 (18%) lower. It is roughly $396 (37%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $437 (23%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
