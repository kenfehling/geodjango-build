Home prices

The value of the median home in Wofford Heights is $107,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $37,980 (26%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $110,000 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Bakersfield is about $139,500 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Wofford Heights the median rent is $960 per month. It is approximately $181 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $102 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bakersfield it is approximately $144 (13%) lower.
