Home prices

The median home in Maywood is valued at $387,000. It is roughly $26,640 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $169,500 (77%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles it is roughly $249,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

Maywood's median rent is $1,104 per month. This is roughly $70 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $42 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is roughly $346 (23%) lower.
