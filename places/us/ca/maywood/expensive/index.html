In comparison to its nearby areas, Maywood is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Maywood is $387,000. This is about $26,640 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $169,500 (77%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $249,900 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.

Rents

Maywood's median rent is $1,104 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $70 (5%) lower.It is approximately $42 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $346 (23%) lower compared to the city of Los Angeles.
