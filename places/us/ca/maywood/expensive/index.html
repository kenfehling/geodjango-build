Compared to the surrounding areas, Maywood is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Los Angeles, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maywood is valued at $387,000. This is roughly $26,640 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $169,500 (77%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles is about $249,900 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Maywood the median rent is $1,104 a month. It is about $70 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $42 (3%) higher, and it is roughly $346 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Los Angeles.
