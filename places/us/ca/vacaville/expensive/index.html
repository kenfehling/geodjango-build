Home prices

The median home in Vacaville costs $422,400. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $204,900 (94%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $675,400 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Vacaville is $1,641 per month. This is approximately $134 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $579 (54%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $254 (13%) lower.
