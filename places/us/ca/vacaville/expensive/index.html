Home prices

The median home in Vacaville costs $422,400. This is is very similar compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $204,900 (94%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $675,400 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.

Rents

Vacaville's median rent is $1,641 a month. It is about $134 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $579 (54%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $254 (13%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
