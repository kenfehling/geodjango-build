Home prices

The median home price in Hercules is $561,600. It is about $112,540 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $344,100 (158%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is about $536,200 (48%) lower.

Rents

Hercules's median monthly rent is $2,201. This is about $475 (27%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $1,139 (107%) higher, and is approximately $306 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
