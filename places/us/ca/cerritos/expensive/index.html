Home prices

The median home cost in Cerritos is $685,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $170,460 (33%) higher. It is about $467,800 (215%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Los Angeles is about $48,400 (7%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cerritos is $2,410. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $756 (45%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $1,348 (126%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Los Angeles it is about $960 (66%) higher.
