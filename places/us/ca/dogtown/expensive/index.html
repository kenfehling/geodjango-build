The median home in Dogtown costs $552,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $143,160 (34%) higher. It is about $335,000 (154%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is approximately $545,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dogtown is $1,344. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $138 (11%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $551 (29%) lower.
