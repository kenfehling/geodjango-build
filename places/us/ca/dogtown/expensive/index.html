Home prices

The median home in Dogtown costs $552,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $143,160 (34%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $335,000 (154%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $545,300 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dogtown is $1,344. This is about $138 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $282 (26%) higher, and is roughly $551 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
