Home prices

The median home in Rancho Murieta is valued at $474,000. It is about $65,760 (16%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $256,500 (117%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $137,100 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Sacramento.

Rents

Rancho Murieta's median monthly rent is $2,086. It is approximately $642 (44%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $1,024 (96%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Sacramento is roughly $823 (65%) higher.
