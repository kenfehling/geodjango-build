Compared to nearby areas, Scotts Valley is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home cost in Scotts Valley is $815,600. It is about $77,180 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $598,100 (274%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of San Francisco is roughly $282,200 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Scotts Valley is $1,975. This is about $302 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $913 (85%) higher, and is about $80 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
