In comparison to nearby areas, Fremont is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

The median home in Fremont is valued at $931,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $54,720 (6%) higher. It is roughly $714,100 (328%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco is approximately $166,200 (15%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fremont is $2,345. This is approximately $289 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $1,283 (120%) higher, and it is approximately $450 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of San Francisco.
