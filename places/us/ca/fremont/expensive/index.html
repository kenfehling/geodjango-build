Compared to the surrounding areas, Fremont is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of San Francisco.

Home prices

Fremont's median home cost is $931,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $54,720 (6%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $714,100 (328%) higher, and compared to the city of San Francisco it is roughly $166,200 (15%) lower.

Rents

Fremont's median rent is $2,345 per month. It is approximately $289 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $1,283 (120%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is about $450 (23%) higher.
