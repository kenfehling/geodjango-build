Compared to its nearby areas, Fremont is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of San Francisco however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Fremont's median home value is $931,600. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $54,720 (6%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $714,100 (328%) higher, and in comparison to the city of San Francisco it is approximately $166,200 (15%) lower.

Rents

Fremont's median rent is $2,345 a month. This is roughly $289 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $1,283 (120%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $450 (23%) higher compared to the city of San Francisco.
