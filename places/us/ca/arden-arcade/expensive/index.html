Rents

The median monthly rent in Arden-Arcade is $1,080. This is approximately $177 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $18 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $815 (43%) lower compared to the city of San Francisco.
