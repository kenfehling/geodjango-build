Home prices

Stowe's median home value is $325,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $117,720 (56%) higher. It is about $107,500 (49%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $40,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Stowe the median rent is $778 per month. This is roughly $193 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $284 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $435 (35%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
