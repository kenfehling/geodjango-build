Home prices

The median home cost in Hartland is $195,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $65,920 (25%) lower. It is about $22,300 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $32,400 (14%) lower compared to the city of Manchester.

Rents

In Hartland the median monthly rent is $885. This is approximately $154 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $177 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Manchester it is roughly $250 (22%) lower.
