Home prices

The value of the median home in Hartland is $195,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $65,920 (25%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $22,300 (10%) lower, and is approximately $32,400 (14%) lower compared to the city of Manchester.

Rents

Hartland's median monthly rent is $885. It is approximately $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $177 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $250 (22%) lower compared to the city of Manchester.
