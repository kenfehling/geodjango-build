Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Poultney is $769 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $67 (8%) lower. It is roughly $293 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $200 (20%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
