In comparison to other places in this area, West Rutland is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The median home value in West Rutland is $145,700. It is roughly $25,260 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $71,800 (33%) lower, and it is about $33,400 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median rent in West Rutland is $813 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $92 (10%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $249 (23%) lower, and it is about $156 (16%) lower compared to the city of Albany.
