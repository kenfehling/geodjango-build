Home prices

The median home cost in Wallingford is $152,700. This is roughly $15,180 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $64,800 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $26,400 (14%) lower compared to the city of Albany.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wallingford is $940. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $90 (10%) higher. It is about $122 (11%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $29 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
