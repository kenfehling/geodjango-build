Home prices

The value of the median home in Hinesburg is $296,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $23,040 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $79,100 (36%) higher, and it is about $12,100 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Hinesburg's median monthly rent is $1,141. Compared to other places in this area it is about $94 (7%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $79 (7%) higher, and it is approximately $72 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
