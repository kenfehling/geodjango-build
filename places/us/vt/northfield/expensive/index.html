In comparison to nearby areas, Northfield is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Burlington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Northfield costs $150,900. It is about $35,820 (19%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $66,600 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $133,600 (46%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Northfield the median rent is $860 a month. This is about $112 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $202 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $353 (29%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
