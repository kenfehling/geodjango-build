Home prices

The median home in Northfield costs $150,900. This is roughly $35,820 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $66,600 (30%) lower, and is about $133,600 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northfield is $860. It is roughly $112 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $202 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is roughly $353 (29%) lower.
