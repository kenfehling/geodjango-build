Home prices

The median home in Greensboro costs $297,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $169,180 (132%) higher. It is roughly $79,700 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $12,700 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Greensboro the median rent is $429 a month. This is about $336 (43%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $633 (59%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $784 (64%) lower.
