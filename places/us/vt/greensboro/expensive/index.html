Compared to the surrounding areas, Greensboro is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's close to average compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home in Greensboro costs $297,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $169,180 (132%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $79,700 (36%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is roughly $12,700 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Greensboro the median monthly rent is $429. It is about $336 (43%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $633 (59%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $784 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
