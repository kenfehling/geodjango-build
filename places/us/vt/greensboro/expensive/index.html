Home prices

The price of the median home in Greensboro is $297,200. This is approximately $169,180 (132%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $79,700 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Burlington it is roughly $12,700 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Greensboro is $429. This is about $336 (43%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $633 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $784 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
