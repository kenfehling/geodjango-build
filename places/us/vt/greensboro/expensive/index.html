In comparison to other places in this area, Greensboro is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Burlington, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Greensboro is valued at $297,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $169,180 (132%) higher. It is approximately $79,700 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $12,700 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Greensboro the median monthly rent is $429. This is about $336 (43%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $633 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is approximately $784 (64%) lower.
