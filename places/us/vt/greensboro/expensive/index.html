Home prices

Rents

In Greensboro the median monthly rent is $429. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $336 (43%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $633 (59%) lower, and is about $784 (64%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
