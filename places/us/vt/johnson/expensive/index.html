The median home in Johnson costs $157,600. It is approximately $68,020 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $59,900 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $126,900 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Johnson the median monthly rent is $843. This is approximately $120 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $219 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $370 (30%) lower.
