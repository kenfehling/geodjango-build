Compared to nearby areas, Chelsea is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home in Chelsea is valued at $116,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $32,480 (21%) lower. It is approximately $100,800 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $167,800 (58%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Chelsea the median rent is $680 a month. This is approximately $239 (26%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $382 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is about $533 (43%) lower.
