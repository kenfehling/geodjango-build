Home prices

Arlington's median home price is $202,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $31,880 (13%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $15,000 (6%) lower, and is about $46,300 (29%) higher compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Arlington the median rent is $885 a month. It is roughly $114 (14%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $177 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Springfield.
