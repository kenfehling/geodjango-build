Home prices

The median home in Newbury costs $170,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $34,520 (25%) higher.It is about $47,400 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $57,500 (25%) lower compared to the city of Manchester.

Rents

In Newbury the median monthly rent is $720. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $109 (13%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $342 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is approximately $415 (36%) lower.
