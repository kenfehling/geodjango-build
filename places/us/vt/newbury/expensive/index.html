Home prices

The cost of the median home in Newbury is $170,100. It is roughly $34,520 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $47,400 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Manchester is approximately $57,500 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Newbury the median rent is $720 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $109 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $342 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $415 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Manchester.
