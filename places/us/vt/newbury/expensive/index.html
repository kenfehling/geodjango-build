Home prices

The median home value in Newbury is $170,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $34,520 (25%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $47,400 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is about $57,500 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Newbury is $720 per month. It is roughly $109 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $342 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $415 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Manchester.
