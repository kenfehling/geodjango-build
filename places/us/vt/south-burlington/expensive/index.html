Compared to its surrounding areas, South Burlington is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Burlington, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

South Burlington's median home cost is $307,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $63,580 (26%) higher.It is about $90,000 (41%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $23,000 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in South Burlington is $1,401. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $247 (21%) higher.It is approximately $339 (31%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $188 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.
