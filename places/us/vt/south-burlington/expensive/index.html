Home prices

The median home in South Burlington costs $307,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $63,580 (26%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $90,000 (41%) higher, and it is about $23,000 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In South Burlington the median rent is $1,401 per month. It is roughly $247 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $339 (31%) higher, and is roughly $188 (15%) higher compared to the city of Burlington.
