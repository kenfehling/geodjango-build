Home prices

Rents

In South Burlington the median rent is $1,401 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $247 (21%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $339 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $188 (15%) higher.
