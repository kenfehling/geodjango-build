Compared to other places in this area, St. Albans is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Albans is $180,800. This is roughly $22,440 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $36,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $103,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

St. Albans's median monthly rent is $1,005. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $122 (13%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $57 (5%) lower, and it is approximately $208 (17%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
