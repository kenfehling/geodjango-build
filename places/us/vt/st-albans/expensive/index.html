In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Albans is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Burlington, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

St. Albans's median home price is $180,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $22,440 (11%) lower. It is approximately $36,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is approximately $103,700 (36%) lower.

Rents

In St. Albans the median rent is $1,005 per month. It is approximately $122 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $57 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $208 (17%) lower.
