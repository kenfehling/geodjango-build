In comparison to its surrounding areas, St. Albans is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

St. Albans's median home value is $180,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $22,440 (11%) lower. It is approximately $36,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $103,700 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in St. Albans is $1,005 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $122 (13%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $57 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is approximately $208 (17%) lower.
