Home prices

East Barre's median home cost is $181,300. This is approximately $29,260 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $103,200 (36%) lower.

Rents

In East Barre the median rent is $1,000 per month. It is roughly $57 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $62 (5%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $213 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
