Home prices

The median home price in White River Junction is $213,600. It is roughly $147,880 (40%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $3,900 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Manchester it is roughly $14,000 (6%) lower.

Rents

White River Junction's median rent is $922 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $243 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $140 (13%) lower, and it is approximately $213 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Manchester.
