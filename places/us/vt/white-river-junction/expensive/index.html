Home prices

The median home value in White River Junction is $213,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $147,880 (40%) lower.It is about $3,900 (1%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is about $14,000 (6%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in White River Junction is $922 per month. It is approximately $243 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $140 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Manchester it is approximately $213 (18%) lower.
