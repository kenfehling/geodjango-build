Home prices

The median home in Graniteville costs $116,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $43,720 (27%) lower. It is approximately $101,300 (46%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $168,300 (59%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Graniteville the median rent is $906 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $39 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $156 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is about $307 (25%) lower.
