Home prices

The value of the median home in Graniteville is $116,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $43,720 (27%) lower.It is about $101,300 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $168,300 (59%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Graniteville the median monthly rent is $906. This is approximately $39 (4%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $156 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington is approximately $307 (25%) lower.
