Home prices

The median home in Cambridge costs $181,300. It is about $46,800 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $103,200 (36%) lower.

Rents

Cambridge's median monthly rent is $1,008. This is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $54 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington is roughly $205 (16%) lower.
