Compared to its surrounding areas, Cambridge is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Burlington however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cambridge costs $181,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $46,800 (20%) lower.It is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $103,200 (36%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Cambridge's median monthly rent is $1,008. In comparison to its nearby areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $54 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $205 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
