In comparison to other places in this area, Cambridge is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home in Cambridge costs $181,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $46,800 (20%) lower. It is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $103,200 (36%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Cambridge the median monthly rent is $1,008. Compared to its nearby areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $54 (5%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $205 (16%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
