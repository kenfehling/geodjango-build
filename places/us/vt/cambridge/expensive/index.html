In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cambridge is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Burlington however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Cambridge is $181,300. This is about $46,800 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower, and it is about $103,200 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cambridge is $1,008. In comparison to nearby areas it is is very similar. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $54 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $205 (16%) lower.
