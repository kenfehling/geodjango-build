Home prices

The median home cost in Cambridge is $181,300. It is approximately $46,800 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $36,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $103,200 (36%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Cambridge is $1,008 a month. It is is very similar in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $54 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington it is roughly $205 (16%) lower.
