The median home in Waterbury costs $257,000. This is about $10,360 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $39,500 (18%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Burlington is about $27,500 (9%) lower.

Rents

In Waterbury the median rent is $986 a month. It is about $43 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $76 (7%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is approximately $227 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
