Compared to nearby areas, Rochester is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home price in Rochester is $192,100. It is approximately $40,260 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $25,400 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $92,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

Rochester's median monthly rent is $692. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $182 (20%) lower. It is approximately $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is about $521 (42%) lower.
