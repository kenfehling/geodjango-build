Home prices

The price of the median home in Rochester is $192,100. It is about $40,260 (26%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $25,400 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $92,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Rochester the median monthly rent is $692. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $182 (20%) lower.It is roughly $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $521 (42%) lower.
