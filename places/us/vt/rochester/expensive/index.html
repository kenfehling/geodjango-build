Home prices

The price of the median home in Rochester is $192,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $40,260 (26%) higher.It is about $25,400 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Burlington it is about $92,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Rochester the median monthly rent is $692. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $182 (20%) lower.It is approximately $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $521 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
