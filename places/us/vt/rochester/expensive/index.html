Compared to nearby areas, Rochester is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

Rochester's median home cost is $192,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $40,260 (26%) higher. It is roughly $25,400 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $92,400 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Rochester is $692 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $182 (20%) lower. It is approximately $370 (34%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $521 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
