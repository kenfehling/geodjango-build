Compared to other places in this area, Rochester is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Burlington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rochester costs $192,100. This is about $40,260 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $25,400 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $92,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Rochester's median rent is $692 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $182 (20%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $370 (34%) lower, and is approximately $521 (42%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
