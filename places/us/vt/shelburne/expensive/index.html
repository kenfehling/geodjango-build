Home prices

The median home in Shelburne is valued at $278,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $28,900 (11%) higher.It is roughly $61,100 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $5,900 (2%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Shelburne's median monthly rent is $1,349. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $185 (15%) higher.It is about $287 (27%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $136 (11%) higher.
