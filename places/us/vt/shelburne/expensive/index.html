Home prices

The median home in Shelburne is valued at $278,600. This is about $28,900 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $61,100 (28%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $5,900 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Shelburne is $1,349. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $185 (15%) higher.It is about $287 (27%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $136 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.
