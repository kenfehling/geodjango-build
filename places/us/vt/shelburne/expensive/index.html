In comparison to its surrounding areas, Shelburne is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

Shelburne's median home value is $278,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $28,900 (11%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $61,100 (28%) higher, and is approximately $5,900 (2%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Shelburne's median rent is $1,349 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $185 (15%) higher. It is about $287 (27%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Burlington is roughly $136 (11%) higher.
