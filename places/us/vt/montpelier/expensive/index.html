Home prices

The median home in Montpelier is valued at $252,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $97,740 (63%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $35,100 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Burlington is roughly $31,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Montpelier the median rent is $1,021 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $73 (7%) higher.It is about $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is approximately $192 (15%) lower.
