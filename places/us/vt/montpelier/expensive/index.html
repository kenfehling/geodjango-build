In comparison to its surrounding areas, Montpelier is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Burlington, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Montpelier costs $252,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $97,740 (63%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $35,100 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Burlington is approximately $31,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

Montpelier's median rent is $1,021 a month. This is roughly $73 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $192 (15%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
