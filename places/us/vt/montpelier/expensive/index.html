Compared to other places in this area, Montpelier is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Burlington, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Montpelier's median home cost is $252,600. This is about $97,740 (63%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $35,100 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Burlington is roughly $31,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

In Montpelier the median rent is $1,021 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $73 (7%) higher. It is approximately $41 (3%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is about $192 (15%) lower.
