Compared to other places in this area, Rutland is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Albany.

Home prices

The median home in Rutland costs $153,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $16,140 (9%) lower. It is approximately $64,200 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albany is approximately $25,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

In Rutland the median monthly rent is $780. This is approximately $131 (14%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $282 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is approximately $189 (19%) lower.
