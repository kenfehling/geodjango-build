Home prices

The price of the median home in Rutland is $153,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $16,140 (9%) lower.It is roughly $64,200 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is approximately $25,800 (14%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rutland is $780 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $131 (14%) lower.It is roughly $282 (26%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is approximately $189 (19%) lower.
