In comparison to the surrounding areas, Milton is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home price in Milton is $255,400. Compared to other places in this area it is about $21,220 (9%) higher.It is approximately $37,900 (17%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Burlington it is roughly $29,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Milton is $1,169 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $95 (8%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $107 (10%) higher, and is roughly $44 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
