Compared to the surrounding areas, Milton is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Burlington however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Milton costs $255,400. It is roughly $21,220 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $37,900 (17%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $29,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

Milton's median rent is $1,169 a month. It is about $95 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $107 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $44 (3%) lower.
