Home prices

The median home cost in Milton is $255,400. This is approximately $21,220 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $37,900 (17%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is about $29,100 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Milton the median monthly rent is $1,169. This is about $95 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $107 (10%) higher, and it is roughly $44 (3%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
