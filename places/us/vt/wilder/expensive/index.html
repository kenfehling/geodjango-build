Home prices

The median home in Wilder is valued at $233,500. Compared to other places in this area it is about $124,000 (34%) lower. It is approximately $16,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is about $5,900 (2%) higher.

Rents

In Wilder the median monthly rent is $1,355. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $276 (25%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $293 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Manchester is approximately $220 (19%) higher.
