Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Troy is $706. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $94 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $356 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is roughly $507 (41%) lower.
