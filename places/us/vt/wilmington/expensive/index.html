In comparison to other places in this area, Wilmington is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Worcester.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wilmington is $190,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,260 (7%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $27,500 (12%) lower, and is roughly $37,100 (16%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington is $1,005 per month. It is about $152 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $57 (5%) lower, and it is roughly $90 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
