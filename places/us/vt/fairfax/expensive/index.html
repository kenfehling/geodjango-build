Compared to nearby areas, Fairfax is a little more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Fairfax is $285,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $57,900 (25%) higher. It is about $67,900 (31%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is is very similar.

Rents

In Fairfax the median rent is $900 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $200 (18%) lower. It is approximately $162 (15%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $313 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
