Home prices

The median home in Fairfax is valued at $285,400. It is about $57,900 (25%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $67,900 (31%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Fairfax's median rent is $900 a month. It is approximately $200 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $162 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington it is roughly $313 (25%) lower.
