Home prices

The price of the median home in Fairfax is $285,400. This is approximately $57,900 (25%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $67,900 (31%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Fairfax's median rent is $900 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $200 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $162 (15%) lower, and it is about $313 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
