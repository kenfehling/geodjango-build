Home prices

The median home in South Barre costs $172,300. It is approximately $23,600 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $45,200 (20%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $112,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

South Barre's median monthly rent is $1,289. This is roughly $421 (48%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $227 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Burlington is about $76 (6%) higher.
