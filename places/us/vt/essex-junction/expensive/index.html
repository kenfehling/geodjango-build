Home prices

The cost of the median home in Essex Junction is $298,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $33,200 (12%) higher.It is roughly $80,700 (37%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $13,700 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Essex Junction's median rent is $1,313 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $107 (8%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $251 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Burlington is about $100 (8%) higher.
