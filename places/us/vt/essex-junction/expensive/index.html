In comparison to its nearby areas, Essex Junction is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home in Essex Junction costs $298,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $33,200 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $80,700 (37%) higher, and is about $13,700 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Essex Junction is $1,313 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $107 (8%) higher. It is about $251 (23%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $100 (8%) higher.
