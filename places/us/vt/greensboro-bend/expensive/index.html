Home prices

The median home in Greensboro Bend costs $165,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $1,660 (1%) higher. It is approximately $51,900 (23%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $118,900 (41%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Greensboro Bend is $971 a month. It is roughly $309 (46%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $91 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $242 (19%) lower.
