Home prices

The median home in Greensboro Bend costs $165,600. This is about $1,660 (1%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $51,900 (23%) lower, and it is approximately $118,900 (41%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Greensboro Bend is $971 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $309 (46%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $91 (8%) lower, and is roughly $242 (19%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
