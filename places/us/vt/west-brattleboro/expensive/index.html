Rents

West Brattleboro's median rent is $794 per month. This is about $55 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $268 (25%) lower, and is approximately $301 (27%) lower compared to the city of Worcester.
