Home prices

St. Johnsbury's median home cost is $139,900. It is roughly $9,660 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $77,600 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $144,600 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Johnsbury is $753. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $119 (13%) lower. It is about $309 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is roughly $460 (37%) lower.
