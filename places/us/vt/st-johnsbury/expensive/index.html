Compared to its nearby areas, St. Johnsbury is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home price in St. Johnsbury is $139,900. It is about $9,660 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $77,600 (35%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $144,600 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In St. Johnsbury the median monthly rent is $753. It is about $119 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $309 (29%) lower, and is approximately $460 (37%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
