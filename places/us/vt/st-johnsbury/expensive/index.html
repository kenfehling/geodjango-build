Home prices

St. Johnsbury's median home cost is $139,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $9,660 (6%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $77,600 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $144,600 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In St. Johnsbury the median monthly rent is $753. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $119 (13%) lower.It is approximately $309 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $460 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
