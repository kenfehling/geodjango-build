Compared to the surrounding areas, Canaan is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Barre, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Canaan is $95,000. It is about $17,640 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $122,500 (56%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Barre is approximately $63,300 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Canaan is $736. It is roughly $14 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $326 (30%) lower, and is is very similar compared to the city of Barre.
