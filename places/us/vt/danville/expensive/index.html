The median home price in Danville is $175,800. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $42,620 (32%) higher. It is approximately $41,700 (19%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Burlington is roughly $108,700 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Danville the median rent is $833 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $38 (4%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $229 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington is approximately $380 (31%) lower.
