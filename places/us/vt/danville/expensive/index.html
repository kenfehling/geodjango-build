Home prices

The median home in Danville is valued at $175,800. This is about $42,620 (32%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $41,700 (19%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $108,700 (38%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Danville's median monthly rent is $833. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $38 (4%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is roughly $380 (31%) lower.
