Home prices

Danville's median home cost is $175,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $42,620 (32%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $41,700 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $108,700 (38%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Danville is $833 per month. It is approximately $38 (4%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $380 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
