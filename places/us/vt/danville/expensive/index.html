Compared to the surrounding areas, Danville is a lot more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home cost in Danville is $175,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $42,620 (32%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $41,700 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $108,700 (38%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Danville the median rent is $833 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $38 (4%) lower. It is about $229 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is approximately $380 (31%) lower.
