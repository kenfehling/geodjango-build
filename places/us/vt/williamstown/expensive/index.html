Home prices

The median home cost in Williamstown is $136,800. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $19,000 (12%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $80,700 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is roughly $147,700 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Williamstown is $835 per month. It is approximately $124 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $227 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is about $378 (31%) lower.
