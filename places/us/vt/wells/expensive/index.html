Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wells is $148,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $10,580 (6%) lower. It is about $68,600 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $30,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.

Rents

In Wells the median monthly rent is $867. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $23 (2%) higher. It is about $195 (18%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Albany it is about $102 (10%) lower.
