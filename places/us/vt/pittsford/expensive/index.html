Home prices

Pittsford's median home cost is $145,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $30,440 (17%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $72,400 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Albany it is roughly $34,000 (18%) lower.

Rents

Pittsford's median rent is $903 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $125 (16%) higher. It is approximately $159 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $66 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Albany.
