Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $258,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $22,920 (8%) lower.It is about $40,800 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $26,200 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Richmond's median rent is $967 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $303 (23%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $95 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Burlington it is about $246 (20%) lower.
