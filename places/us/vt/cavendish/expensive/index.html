Home prices

The cost of the median home in Cavendish is $152,500. It is approximately $12,900 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $65,000 (29%) lower, and is approximately $3,700 (2%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median rent in Cavendish is $1,336 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $525 (64%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $274 (25%) higher, and compared to the city of Springfield it is roughly $459 (52%) higher.
