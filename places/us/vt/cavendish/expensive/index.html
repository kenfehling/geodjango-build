Home prices

The median home price in Cavendish is $152,500. It is about $12,900 (7%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $65,000 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $3,700 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.

Rents

In Cavendish the median monthly rent is $1,336. It is roughly $525 (64%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $274 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Springfield it is approximately $459 (52%) higher.
