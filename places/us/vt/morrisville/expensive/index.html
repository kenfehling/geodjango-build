Home prices

The value of the median home in Morrisville is $179,700. It is about $21,800 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $37,800 (17%) lower, and is roughly $104,800 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

Morrisville's median monthly rent is $928. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $25 (2%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $134 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Burlington is about $285 (23%) lower.
