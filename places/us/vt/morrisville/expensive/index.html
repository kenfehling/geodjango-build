Compared to nearby areas, Morrisville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Burlington.

Home prices

The median home in Morrisville is valued at $179,700. It is approximately $21,800 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $37,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $104,800 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morrisville is $928. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $25 (2%) higher.It is about $134 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $285 (23%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.
