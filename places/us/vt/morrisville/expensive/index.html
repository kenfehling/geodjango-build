In comparison to the surrounding areas, Morrisville is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Burlington, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

Morrisville's median home cost is $179,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $21,800 (10%) lower.It is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $104,800 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Morrisville is $928 a month. This is approximately $25 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $134 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $285 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
