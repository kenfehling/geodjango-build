In comparison to other places in this area, Morrisville is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Burlington however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Morrisville is $179,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $21,800 (10%) lower. It is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $104,800 (36%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Morrisville the median rent is $928 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $25 (2%) higher. It is approximately $134 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Burlington is approximately $285 (23%) lower.
