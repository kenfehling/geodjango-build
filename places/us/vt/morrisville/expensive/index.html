Home prices

The median home in Morrisville costs $179,700. It is roughly $21,800 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $37,800 (17%) lower, and is roughly $104,800 (36%) lower compared to the city of Burlington.

Rents

In Morrisville the median monthly rent is $928. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $25 (2%) higher. It is about $134 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $285 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
