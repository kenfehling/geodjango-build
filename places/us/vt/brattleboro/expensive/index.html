Home prices

The median home in Brattleboro is valued at $203,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $8,380 (4%) higher. It is approximately $14,200 (6%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $23,800 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brattleboro is $880. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $29 (3%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $182 (17%) lower, and is approximately $215 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Worcester.
