Compared to its nearby areas, Concord is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Burlington, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Concord's median home price is $112,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $33,040 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $105,400 (48%) lower, and it is roughly $172,400 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.

Rents

The median rent in Concord is $1,058 per month. This is approximately $298 (39%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is is very similar in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $155 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Burlington.
