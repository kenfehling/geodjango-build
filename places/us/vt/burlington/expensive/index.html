Compared to other places in this area, Burlington is somewhat more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home cost in Burlington is $284,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $63,067 (28%) higher. It is about $67,000 (30%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation.

Rents

In Burlington the median monthly rent is $1,213. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $60 (5%) higher. It is approximately $151 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the nation.
