Home prices

Rents

In Manchester Center the median rent is $829 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $98 (13%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $233 (21%) lower, and is roughly $48 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Springfield.
