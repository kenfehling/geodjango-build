Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hillcrest is $124,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $6,100 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $92,900 (42%) lower, and is approximately $133,400 (51%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Hillcrest the median monthly rent is $906. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $62 (7%) higher. It is approximately $156 (14%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $206 (18%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
