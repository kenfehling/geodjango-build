Home prices

The median home in Hillcrest is valued at $124,600. It is roughly $6,100 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $92,900 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $133,400 (51%) lower.

Rents

In Hillcrest the median rent is $906 per month. It is approximately $62 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $156 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $206 (18%) lower.
