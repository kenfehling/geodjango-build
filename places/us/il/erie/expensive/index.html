Home prices

The price of the median home in Erie is $130,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $27,560 (26%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $87,500 (40%) lower, and it is approximately $38,400 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Rockford.

Rents

In Erie the median rent is $605 a month. It is about $144 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $457 (43%) lower, and it is approximately $167 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.
