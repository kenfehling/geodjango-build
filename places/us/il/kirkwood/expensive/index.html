Home prices

The median home in Kirkwood is valued at $78,300. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $8,180 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $139,200 (64%) lower, and is approximately $47,400 (37%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Kirkwood is $481 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $127 (20%) lower. It is roughly $581 (54%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Peoria is roughly $325 (40%) lower.
