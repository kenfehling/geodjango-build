Home prices

The median home price in Alsip is $171,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $18,760 (12%) higher. It is approximately $46,100 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $86,600 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Alsip the median rent is $961 per month. It is approximately $37 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $101 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $151 (13%) lower.
