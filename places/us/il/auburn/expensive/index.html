Home prices

The cost of the median home in Auburn is $113,400. It is roughly $2,280 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $104,100 (47%) lower, and is roughly $25,300 (18%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Auburn's median monthly rent is $846. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $39 (4%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $216 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $18 (2%) higher.
