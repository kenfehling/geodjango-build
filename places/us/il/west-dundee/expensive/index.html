Compared to the surrounding areas, West Dundee is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The value of the median home in West Dundee is $264,800. This is about $40,360 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $47,300 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $6,800 (2%) higher.

Rents

In West Dundee the median rent is $1,281 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $54 (4%) lower. It is approximately $219 (20%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $169 (15%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
