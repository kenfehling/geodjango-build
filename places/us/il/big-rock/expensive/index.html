Home prices

The price of the median home in Big Rock is $237,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $12,840 (5%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $19,900 (9%) higher, and is about $20,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Big Rock the median rent is $1,100 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $28 (2%) higher. It is approximately $38 (3%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $12 (1%) lower.
