In comparison to its surrounding areas, Grandview is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Grandview is $72,700. It is roughly $55,840 (43%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $144,800 (66%) lower, and is roughly $66,000 (47%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Grandview the median rent is $916 per month. This is roughly $17 (1%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $146 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $88 (10%) higher.
