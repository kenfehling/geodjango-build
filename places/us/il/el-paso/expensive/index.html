Home prices

El Paso's median home cost is $122,700. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $11,020 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $94,800 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Aurora is roughly $61,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in El Paso is $763. This is roughly $65 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Aurora it is roughly $465 (37%) lower.
