Compared to its surrounding areas, El Paso is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Aurora.

Home prices

The median home in El Paso costs $122,700. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $11,020 (9%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $94,800 (43%) lower, and is roughly $61,700 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Aurora.

Rents

El Paso's median rent is $763 per month. It is roughly $65 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $465 (37%) lower compared to the city of Aurora.
