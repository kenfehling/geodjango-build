Home prices

The median home in El Paso costs $122,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $11,020 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $94,800 (43%) lower, and it is about $61,700 (33%) lower compared to the city of Aurora.

Rents

The median monthly rent in El Paso is $763. This is approximately $65 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $465 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Aurora.
