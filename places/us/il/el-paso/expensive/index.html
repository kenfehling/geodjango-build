Compared to other places in this area, El Paso is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Aurora.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in El Paso is $122,700. This is about $11,020 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $94,800 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Aurora is about $61,700 (33%) lower.

Rents

In El Paso the median monthly rent is $763. This is approximately $65 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $299 (28%) lower, and is about $465 (37%) lower compared to the city of Aurora.
