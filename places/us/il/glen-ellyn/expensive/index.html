Home prices

The median home in Glen Ellyn is valued at $453,900. This is roughly $180,980 (66%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $236,400 (108%) higher, and is about $195,900 (75%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Glen Ellyn's median rent is $1,106 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $380 (25%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $44 (4%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Chicago.
