Home prices

Rents

In Glen Ellyn the median monthly rent is $1,106. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $380 (25%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $44 (4%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Chicago.
