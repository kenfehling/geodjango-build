Home prices

The median home in Glen Ellyn is valued at $453,900. It is about $180,980 (66%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $236,400 (108%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $195,900 (75%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Glen Ellyn's median monthly rent is $1,106. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $380 (25%) lower. It is approximately $44 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is is very similar.
