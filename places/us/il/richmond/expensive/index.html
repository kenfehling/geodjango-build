Compared to its surrounding areas, Richmond is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Richmond is $188,000. This is approximately $12,660 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $29,500 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $70,000 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Richmond the median monthly rent is $938. Compared to nearby areas it is about $88 (8%) lower. It is about $124 (11%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $174 (15%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
