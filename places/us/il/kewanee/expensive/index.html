Home prices

The price of the median home in Kewanee is $60,000. This is approximately $7,500 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $157,500 (72%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford is about $31,600 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Kewanee the median rent is $621 a month. This is about $21 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $441 (41%) lower, and is about $151 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.
