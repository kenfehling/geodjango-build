In comparison to its nearby areas, Westchester is a little more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Westchester costs $241,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $23,360 (10%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $23,600 (10%) higher, and it is roughly $16,900 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Westchester's median rent is $1,431 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $327 (29%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $369 (34%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $319 (28%) higher.
