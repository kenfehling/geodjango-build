Compared to other places in this area, Westchester is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Westchester is valued at $241,100. It is about $23,360 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $23,600 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $16,900 (6%) lower.

Rents

Westchester's median rent is $1,431 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $327 (29%) higher.It is about $369 (34%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $319 (28%) higher.
