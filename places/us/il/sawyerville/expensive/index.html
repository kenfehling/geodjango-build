Home prices

The median home in Sawyerville is valued at $79,400. This is roughly $11,140 (16%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $138,100 (63%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $59,300 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sawyerville is $841. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $137 (19%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $221 (20%) lower, and is roughly $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
