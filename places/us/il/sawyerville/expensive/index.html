Compared to the surrounding areas, Sawyerville is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Sawyerville is $79,400. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $11,140 (16%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $138,100 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $59,300 (42%) lower.

Rents

Sawyerville's median monthly rent is $841. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $137 (19%) higher.It is roughly $221 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $13 (1%) higher.
