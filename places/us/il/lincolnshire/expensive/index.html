Compared to its surrounding areas, Lincolnshire is a little less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Lincolnshire's median home value is $508,700. It is about $44,120 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $291,200 (133%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $250,700 (97%) higher.

Rents

In Lincolnshire the median monthly rent is $2,791. This is approximately $970 (53%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $1,729 (162%) higher, and it is about $1,679 (150%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
