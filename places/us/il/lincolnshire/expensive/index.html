Home prices

The median home in Lincolnshire is valued at $508,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $44,120 (7%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $291,200 (133%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $250,700 (97%) higher.

Rents

In Lincolnshire the median monthly rent is $2,791. This is approximately $970 (53%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $1,729 (162%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $1,679 (150%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
