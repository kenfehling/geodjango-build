Home prices

The median home price in Urbana is $155,000. This is roughly $13,340 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $62,500 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is about $6,800 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Urbana the median monthly rent is $838. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $25 (3%) higher. It is roughly $224 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $114 (11%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
