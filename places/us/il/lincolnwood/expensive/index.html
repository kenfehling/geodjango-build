Rents

In Lincolnwood the median rent is $1,894 per month. It is roughly $575 (43%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $832 (78%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $782 (70%) higher.
