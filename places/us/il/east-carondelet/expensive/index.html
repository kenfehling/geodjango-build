Home prices

The median home value in East Carondelet is $63,500. It is approximately $47,980 (43%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $154,000 (70%) lower, and is approximately $75,200 (54%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Carondelet is $718. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $195 (21%) lower. It is approximately $344 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $110 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
