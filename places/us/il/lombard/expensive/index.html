Home prices

The median home value in Lombard is $261,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $76,660 (22%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $43,700 (20%) higher, and is about $3,200 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Lombard is $1,438 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $157 (12%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $376 (35%) higher, and it is approximately $326 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
