Home prices

The median home in Lombard is valued at $261,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $76,660 (22%) lower.It is roughly $43,700 (20%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $3,200 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lombard is $1,438. This is approximately $157 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $376 (35%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $326 (29%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
