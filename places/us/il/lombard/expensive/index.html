Home prices

Lombard's median home cost is $261,200. It is approximately $76,660 (22%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $43,700 (20%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $3,200 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lombard is $1,438. Compared to other places in this area this is about $157 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $376 (35%) higher, and it is about $326 (29%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
