Home prices

The median home in Lombard is valued at $261,200. This is approximately $76,660 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $43,700 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $3,200 (1%) higher.

Rents

Lombard's median rent is $1,438 a month. This is about $157 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $376 (35%) higher, and it is approximately $326 (29%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
