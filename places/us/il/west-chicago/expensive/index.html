Compared to other places in this area, West Chicago is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in West Chicago is valued at $249,500. It is about $93,400 (27%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $32,000 (14%) higher, and it is about $8,500 (3%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in West Chicago is $1,083 a month. This is about $843 (43%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $21 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $29 (2%) lower.
