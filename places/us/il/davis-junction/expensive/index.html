Home prices

The median home in Davis Junction costs $145,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $7,440 (5%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $71,700 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $112,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Davis Junction the median rent is $996 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $179 (21%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $66 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $116 (10%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
