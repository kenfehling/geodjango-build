Compared to its surrounding areas, Roxana is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Roxana costs $80,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $36,580 (31%) lower. It is about $137,200 (63%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $58,400 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roxana is $785. This is roughly $29 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $277 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $43 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
