Home prices

The price of the median home in Camargo is $121,400. It is approximately $33,160 (37%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $96,100 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $40,400 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median rent in Camargo is $783 per month. This is roughly $59 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $279 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is about $169 (17%) lower.
