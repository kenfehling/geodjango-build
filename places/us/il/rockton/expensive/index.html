Compared to other places in this area, Rockton is a lot more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Rockton's median home cost is $172,100. It is roughly $49,840 (40%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $45,400 (20%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $85,900 (33%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Rockton the median rent is $750 a month. It is about $148 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $312 (29%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $362 (32%) lower.
