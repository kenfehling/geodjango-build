Compared to its nearby areas, Peru is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Rockford.

Home prices

The median home in Peru is valued at $127,400. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $20,060 (18%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $90,100 (41%) lower, and it is approximately $35,800 (39%) higher compared to the city of Rockford.

Rents

Peru's median rent is $744 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $33 (4%) higher. It is roughly $318 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $28 (3%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
