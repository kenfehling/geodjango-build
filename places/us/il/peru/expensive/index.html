Home prices

Rents

Peru's median monthly rent is $744. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $33 (4%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $318 (29%) lower, and is approximately $28 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.
