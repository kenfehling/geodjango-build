In comparison to other places in this area, Oak Park is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park costs $387,300. It is about $96,900 (33%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $169,800 (78%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $129,300 (50%) higher.

Rents

Oak Park's median monthly rent is $1,175. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $120 (11%) higher. It is about $113 (10%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $63 (5%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
