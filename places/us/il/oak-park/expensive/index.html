Compared to nearby areas, Oak Park is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park is valued at $387,300. This is roughly $96,900 (33%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $169,800 (78%) higher, and is roughly $129,300 (50%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oak Park is $1,175. It is about $120 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $113 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $63 (5%) higher.
