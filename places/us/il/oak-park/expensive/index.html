Compared to the surrounding areas, Oak Park is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Oak Park is $387,300. Compared to other places in this area it is about $96,900 (33%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $169,800 (78%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $129,300 (50%) higher.

Rents

Oak Park's median monthly rent is $1,175. It is roughly $120 (11%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $113 (10%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $63 (5%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
