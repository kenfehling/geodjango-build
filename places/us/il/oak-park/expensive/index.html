In comparison to other places in this area, Oak Park is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Oak Park is $387,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $96,900 (33%) higher.It is about $169,800 (78%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $129,300 (50%) higher.

Rents

In Oak Park the median rent is $1,175 a month. It is roughly $120 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $113 (10%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $63 (5%) higher.
