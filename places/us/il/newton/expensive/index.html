Compared to the surrounding areas, Newton is substantially more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Evansville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Newton is $73,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $25,620 (53%) higher. It is roughly $144,200 (66%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is about $20,900 (22%) lower.

Rents

Newton's median rent is $515 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $101 (16%) lower. It is about $547 (51%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $259 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
