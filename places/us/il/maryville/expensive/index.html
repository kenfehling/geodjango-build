Home prices

Maryville's median home cost is $209,500. This is approximately $38,280 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $8,000 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $70,800 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Maryville the median rent is $934 per month. It is roughly $28 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $128 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $106 (12%) higher.
