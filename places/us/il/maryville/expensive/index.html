Home prices

The median home in Maryville is valued at $209,500. It is about $38,280 (22%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $8,000 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $70,800 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Maryville the median rent is $934 a month. This is roughly $28 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $128 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $106 (12%) higher.
