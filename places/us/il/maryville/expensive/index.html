In comparison to its surrounding areas, Maryville is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the entire country. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Maryville is valued at $209,500. It is about $38,280 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $8,000 (3%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $70,800 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Maryville is $934 a month. This is approximately $28 (3%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $128 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $106 (12%) higher.
