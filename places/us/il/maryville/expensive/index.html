Home prices

Maryville's median home price is $209,500. This is roughly $38,280 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $8,000 (3%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $70,800 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Maryville the median rent is $934 a month. This is about $28 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $128 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $106 (12%) higher.
