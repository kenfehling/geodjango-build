Home prices

The median home cost in McHenry is $181,300. It is roughly $26,360 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $76,700 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in McHenry is $1,221. This is about $69 (5%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $159 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $109 (9%) higher.
