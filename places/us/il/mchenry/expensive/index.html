Home prices

McHenry's median home price is $181,300. It is about $26,360 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower, and it is approximately $76,700 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in McHenry is $1,221. This is about $69 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $159 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $109 (9%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
