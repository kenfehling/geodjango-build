Home prices

The median home in McHenry costs $181,300. It is approximately $26,360 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $36,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $76,700 (29%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

McHenry's median monthly rent is $1,221. This is approximately $69 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $159 (14%) higher, and is approximately $109 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
