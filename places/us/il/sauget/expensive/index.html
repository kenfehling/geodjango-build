Home prices

The median home value in Sauget is $45,000. This is roughly $3,700 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $172,500 (79%) lower, and it is about $93,700 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Sauget's median rent is $725 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $41 (5%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $103 (12%) lower.
