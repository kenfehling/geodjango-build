Home prices

The median home cost in Hennepin is $152,000. It is about $70,560 (86%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $65,500 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford is roughly $60,400 (65%) higher.

Rents

In Hennepin the median rent is $713 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $63 (9%) higher.It is approximately $349 (32%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $59 (7%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
