Compared to other places in this area, Oak Forest is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oak Forest is $194,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $73,620 (61%) higher.It is approximately $23,300 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $63,800 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Oak Forest's median rent is $1,080 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $12 (1%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $18 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $32 (2%) lower.
