Home prices

The median home in Oak Forest is valued at $194,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $73,620 (61%) higher. It is roughly $23,300 (10%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $63,800 (24%) lower.

Rents

Oak Forest's median rent is $1,080 a month. It is roughly $12 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $18 (1%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $32 (2%) lower.
