Home prices

Rents

In Oak Forest the median monthly rent is $1,080. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $12 (1%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $18 (1%) higher, and is about $32 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
