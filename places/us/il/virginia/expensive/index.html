Home prices

The median home in Virginia is valued at $71,600. This is approximately $8,880 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $145,900 (67%) lower, and it is about $67,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Virginia's median monthly rent is $556. Compared to nearby areas it is about $113 (16%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $506 (47%) lower, and is about $272 (32%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
