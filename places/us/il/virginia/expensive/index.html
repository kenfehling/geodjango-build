Home prices

The median home price in Virginia is $71,600. This is about $8,880 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $145,900 (67%) lower, and is approximately $67,100 (48%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Virginia is $556 a month. This is approximately $113 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $506 (47%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $272 (32%) lower.
