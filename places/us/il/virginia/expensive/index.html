Compared to the surrounding areas, Virginia is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Virginia costs $71,600. This is roughly $8,880 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $145,900 (67%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $67,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Virginia the median rent is $556 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $113 (16%) lower. It is about $506 (47%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $272 (32%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
