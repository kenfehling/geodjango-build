The median home in Crest Hill is valued at $169,200. It is about $7,140 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $48,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $88,800 (34%) lower.

Rents
