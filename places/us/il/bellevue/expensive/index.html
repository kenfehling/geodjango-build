Compared to the surrounding areas, Bellevue is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Peoria, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bellevue is valued at $82,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $14,620 (15%) lower. It is approximately $135,500 (62%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $43,700 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Bellevue's median rent is $647 a month. This is approximately $220 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $415 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Peoria it is approximately $159 (19%) lower.
