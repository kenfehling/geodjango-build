Home prices

Rents

In Bellevue the median monthly rent is $647. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $220 (25%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $415 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria is about $159 (19%) lower.
