Home prices

The median home in Bellevue is valued at $82,000. It is about $14,620 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $135,500 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria is about $43,700 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bellevue is $647 per month. It is approximately $220 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $415 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $159 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
