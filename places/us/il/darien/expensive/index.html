In comparison to its surrounding areas, Darien is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Darien costs $330,000. It is about $93,960 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $112,500 (51%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $72,000 (27%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Darien's median rent is $1,201 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $206 (14%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $139 (13%) higher, and it is approximately $89 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
