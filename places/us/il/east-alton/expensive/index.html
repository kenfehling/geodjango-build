Home prices

The median home in East Alton costs $81,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $18,260 (18%) lower. It is approximately $136,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $57,600 (41%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents
