Home prices

The median home in Oswego is valued at $254,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $28,500 (12%) higher.It is roughly $37,400 (17%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $3,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Oswego the median rent is $1,584 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $190 (13%) higher.It is roughly $522 (49%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $472 (42%) higher.
