In comparison to other places in this area, Oswego is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Oswego is $254,900. This is about $28,500 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $37,400 (17%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $3,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

Oswego's median rent is $1,584 a month. This is about $190 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $522 (49%) higher, and is approximately $472 (42%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
