Compared to the surrounding areas, Oswego is somewhat more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Oswego is valued at $254,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $28,500 (12%) higher. It is about $37,400 (17%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $3,100 (1%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Oswego's median rent is $1,584 per month. It is approximately $190 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $522 (49%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $472 (42%) higher.
