Compared to its surrounding areas, Hillsboro is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsboro is valued at $90,200. This is about $25,540 (39%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $127,300 (58%) lower, and it is approximately $48,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Hillsboro's median rent is $661 a month. This is roughly $75 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $401 (37%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $167 (20%) lower.
