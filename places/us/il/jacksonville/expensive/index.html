In comparison to its surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville is valued at $103,900. It is about $12,460 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $113,600 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $34,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $716. It is approximately $35 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $346 (32%) lower, and is roughly $112 (13%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
