In comparison to its nearby areas, Jacksonville is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville is valued at $103,900. It is about $12,460 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $113,600 (52%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $34,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

Jacksonville's median monthly rent is $716. This is roughly $35 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $346 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $112 (13%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
