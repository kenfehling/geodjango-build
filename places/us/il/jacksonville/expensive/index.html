Compared to the surrounding areas, Jacksonville is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Jacksonville is valued at $103,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $12,460 (13%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $113,600 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $34,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

In Jacksonville the median rent is $716 per month. It is roughly $35 (5%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $346 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $112 (13%) lower.
