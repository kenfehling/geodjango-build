Compared to nearby areas, Jacksonville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

Jacksonville's median home price is $103,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $12,460 (13%) higher.It is about $113,600 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $34,800 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jacksonville is $716. It is roughly $35 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $346 (32%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $112 (13%) lower.
