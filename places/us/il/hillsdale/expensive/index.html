Compared to nearby areas, Hillsdale is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Rockford, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hillsdale is valued at $75,300. This is roughly $100,640 (57%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $142,200 (65%) lower, and it is about $16,300 (17%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.

Rents

Hillsdale's median monthly rent is $675. This is roughly $77 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $387 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $97 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.
