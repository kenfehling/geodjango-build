Home prices

The value of the median home in McLeansboro is $60,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $4,520 (8%) higher. It is roughly $156,600 (72%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $33,300 (35%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in McLeansboro is $544. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $25 (4%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $518 (48%) lower, and it is roughly $230 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
