Home prices

The median home in Crystal Lake is valued at $221,300. It is roughly $65,280 (22%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $3,800 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $36,700 (14%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Crystal Lake is $1,274 a month. This is about $288 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $212 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $162 (14%) higher.
