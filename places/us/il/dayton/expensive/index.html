In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dayton is significantly more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Dayton is valued at $202,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $77,020 (61%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $15,200 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $55,700 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Dayton the median rent is $930 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $20 (2%) higher. It is about $132 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $182 (16%) lower.
