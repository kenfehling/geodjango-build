Home prices

The median home value in Dayton is $202,300. This is roughly $77,020 (61%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $15,200 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $55,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Dayton the median rent is $930 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $20 (2%) higher. It is roughly $132 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $182 (16%) lower.
