Home prices

Dayton's median home price is $202,300. It is approximately $77,020 (61%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $15,200 (6%) lower, and is roughly $55,700 (21%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $930 a month. It is roughly $20 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $132 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $182 (16%) lower.
