Compared to other places in this area, Dayton is significantly more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Dayton is $202,300. This is approximately $77,020 (61%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $15,200 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $55,700 (21%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $930 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $20 (2%) higher.It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $182 (16%) lower.
