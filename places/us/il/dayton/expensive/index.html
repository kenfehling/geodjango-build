Home prices

Dayton's median home cost is $202,300. Compared to nearby areas this is about $77,020 (61%) higher.It is approximately $15,200 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $55,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

Dayton's median monthly rent is $930. It is roughly $20 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $132 (12%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $182 (16%) lower.
