Home prices

The median home in Monee is valued at $163,400. It is about $16,560 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $54,100 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $94,600 (36%) lower.

Rents

Monee's median rent is $855 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $264 (23%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $207 (19%) lower, and is about $257 (23%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
