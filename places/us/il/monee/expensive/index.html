In comparison to the surrounding areas, Monee is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Monee is $163,400. This is roughly $16,560 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $54,100 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $94,600 (36%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Monee is $855 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $264 (23%) lower. It is about $207 (19%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $257 (23%) lower.
