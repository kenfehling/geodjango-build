Compared to its surrounding areas, Monee is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Monee is $163,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $16,560 (9%) lower. It is roughly $54,100 (24%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $94,600 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Monee the median monthly rent is $855. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $264 (23%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $207 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $257 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
