In comparison to other places in this area, Athens is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Peoria however, it's close to average.

Home prices

Athens's median home value is $123,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $11,000 (8%) lower. It is about $94,400 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $2,600 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In Athens the median monthly rent is $788. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $44 (5%) higher. It is about $274 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is approximately $18 (2%) lower.
