Home prices

The median home in Athens is valued at $123,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $11,000 (8%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $94,400 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria it is about $2,600 (2%) lower.

Rents

In Athens the median monthly rent is $788. It is roughly $44 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $274 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $18 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
