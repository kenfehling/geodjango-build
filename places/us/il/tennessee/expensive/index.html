Home prices

The cost of the median home in Tennessee is $70,800. It is about $2,660 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $146,700 (67%) lower, and is approximately $54,900 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Tennessee's median monthly rent is $608. This is roughly $132 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $454 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is about $198 (24%) lower.
