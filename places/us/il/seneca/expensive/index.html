Home prices

The median home value in Seneca is $161,000. It is about $7,380 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $56,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $97,000 (37%) lower.

Rents

Seneca's median monthly rent is $741. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $248 (25%) lower. It is approximately $321 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $371 (33%) lower.
