In comparison to the surrounding areas, Sheffield is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Rockford.

Home prices

The median home in Sheffield costs $79,200. This is roughly $3,920 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $138,300 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is roughly $12,400 (13%) lower.

Rents

Sheffield's median rent is $706 per month. This is roughly $38 (5%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $356 (33%) lower, and it is about $66 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.
