Home prices

The value of the median home in Sheffield is $79,200. This is roughly $3,920 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $138,300 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of Rockford is roughly $12,400 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Sheffield the median rent is $706 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $38 (5%) lower.It is about $356 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is roughly $66 (8%) lower.
