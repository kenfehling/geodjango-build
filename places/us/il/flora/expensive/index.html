Home prices

The median home in Flora is valued at $78,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $16,500 (26%) higher. It is roughly $139,000 (63%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is approximately $15,700 (16%) lower.

Rents

In Flora the median rent is $606 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $114 (23%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $456 (42%) lower, and is roughly $168 (21%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.
