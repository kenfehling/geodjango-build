Home prices

The value of the median home in South Jacksonville is $111,400. It is approximately $18,140 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $106,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $27,300 (19%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in South Jacksonville is $725 a month. It is approximately $38 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $337 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $103 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
