In comparison to the surrounding areas, South Jacksonville is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home value in South Jacksonville is $111,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $18,140 (19%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $106,100 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $27,300 (19%) lower.

Rents

South Jacksonville's median rent is $725 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $38 (5%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $103 (12%) lower.
