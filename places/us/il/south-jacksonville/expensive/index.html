Compared to its surrounding areas, South Jacksonville is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

South Jacksonville's median home value is $111,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $18,140 (19%) higher.It is about $106,100 (48%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $27,300 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in South Jacksonville is $725 per month. This is approximately $38 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $337 (31%) lower, and is approximately $103 (12%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
