Compared to its nearby areas, Morton Grove is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Morton Grove is valued at $317,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $70,040 (18%) lower. It is about $99,700 (45%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $59,200 (22%) higher.

Rents

Morton Grove's median rent is $1,823 a month. It is about $321 (21%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $761 (71%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $711 (63%) higher.
