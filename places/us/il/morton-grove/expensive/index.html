Compared to other places in this area, Morton Grove is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Morton Grove is valued at $317,200. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $70,040 (18%) lower. It is about $99,700 (45%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $59,200 (22%) higher.

Rents

Morton Grove's median monthly rent is $1,823. It is approximately $321 (21%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $761 (71%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $711 (63%) higher.
