Home prices

The price of the median home in Morton Grove is $317,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $70,040 (18%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $99,700 (45%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $59,200 (22%) higher.

Rents

Morton Grove's median rent is $1,823 per month. It is approximately $321 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $761 (71%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $711 (63%) higher.
