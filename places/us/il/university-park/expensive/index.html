Home prices

The median home cost in University Park is $124,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $7,380 (5%) lower.It is roughly $93,400 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $133,900 (51%) lower.

Rents

In University Park the median rent is $978 per month. This is roughly $78 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $84 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $134 (12%) lower.
