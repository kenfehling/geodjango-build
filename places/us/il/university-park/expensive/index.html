Compared to the surrounding areas, University Park is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in University Park is $124,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $7,380 (5%) lower. It is about $93,400 (42%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $133,900 (51%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

University Park's median rent is $978 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $78 (7%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $84 (7%) lower, and it is approximately $134 (12%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
