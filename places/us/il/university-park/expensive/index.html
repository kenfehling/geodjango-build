Compared to other places in this area, University Park is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in University Park is $124,100. This is about $7,380 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $93,400 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $133,900 (51%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in University Park is $978. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $78 (7%) lower. It is approximately $84 (7%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $134 (12%) lower.
