Home prices

Lansing's median home cost is $129,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $13,560 (9%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $87,600 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $128,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

In Lansing the median rent is $1,004 per month. This is about $112 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $58 (5%) lower, and is about $108 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
