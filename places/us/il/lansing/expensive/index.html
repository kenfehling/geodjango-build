In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lansing is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Lansing's median home value is $129,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13,560 (9%) lower. It is approximately $87,600 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $128,100 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lansing is $1,004. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $112 (10%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $58 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $108 (9%) lower.
