In comparison to the surrounding areas, Livingston is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

Livingston's median home cost is $79,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $17,900 (18%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $138,200 (63%) lower, and it is about $59,400 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Livingston is $726. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $42 (5%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $336 (31%) lower, and is about $102 (12%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
