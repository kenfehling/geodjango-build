Villa Park's median home price is $245,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $40,100 (14%) lower. It is about $28,300 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $12,200 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Villa Park is $1,161. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $143 (10%) lower. It is about $99 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $49 (4%) higher.
