Home prices

Villa Park's median home value is $245,800. It is about $40,100 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $28,300 (13%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $12,200 (4%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Villa Park is $1,161. It is about $143 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $99 (9%) higher, and is approximately $49 (4%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
