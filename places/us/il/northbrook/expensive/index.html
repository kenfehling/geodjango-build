Home prices

The cost of the median home in Northbrook is $534,800. It is roughly $104,200 (16%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $317,300 (145%) higher, and it is roughly $276,800 (107%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Northbrook is $2,056 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $245 (13%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $994 (93%) higher, and is roughly $944 (84%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
