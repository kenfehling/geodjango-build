Home prices

The median home cost in Northbrook is $534,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $104,200 (16%) lower. It is about $317,300 (145%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $276,800 (107%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Northbrook is $2,056 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $245 (13%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $994 (93%) higher, and it is about $944 (84%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
