Home prices

The median home in Libertyville is valued at $447,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $69,720 (18%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $230,400 (105%) higher, and it is roughly $189,900 (73%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Libertyville the median rent is $1,313 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $291 (18%) lower. It is roughly $251 (23%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $201 (18%) higher.
