Compared to its nearby areas, Libertyville is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Libertyville costs $447,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $69,720 (18%) higher. It is about $230,400 (105%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $189,900 (73%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Libertyville is $1,313 per month. It is approximately $291 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $251 (23%) higher, and it is approximately $201 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
