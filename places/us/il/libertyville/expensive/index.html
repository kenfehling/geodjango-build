Compared to its surrounding areas, Libertyville is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Libertyville is $447,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $69,720 (18%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $230,400 (105%) higher, and it is approximately $189,900 (73%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Libertyville is $1,313. It is about $291 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $251 (23%) higher, and it is roughly $201 (18%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
