Home prices

Dallas City's median home price is $71,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $11,460 (13%) lower. It is roughly $146,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $144,000 (66%) lower in comparison to the city of Iowa City.

Rents

The median rent in Dallas City is $583 a month. This is about $105 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $479 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Iowa City is roughly $384 (39%) lower.
