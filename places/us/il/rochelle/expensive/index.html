The median home in Rochelle is valued at $114,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $1,700 (1%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $103,000 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $143,500 (55%) lower.

Rents

Rochelle's median rent is $782 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $58 (6%) lower. It is approximately $280 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $330 (29%) lower.
