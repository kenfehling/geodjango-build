Home prices

Rents

Loraine's median rent is $618 per month. This is approximately $26 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $92 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Quincy.
