Home prices

The median home in New Milford is valued at $116,700. It is approximately $17,140 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $100,800 (46%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $141,300 (54%) lower.

Rents

New Milford's median monthly rent is $689. This is about $191 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $423 (38%) lower.
