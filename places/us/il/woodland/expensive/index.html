In comparison to other places in this area, Woodland is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Woodland costs $66,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $9,180 (12%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $150,800 (69%) lower, and is roughly $191,300 (74%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Woodland's median rent is $755 per month. It is about $16 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $357 (32%) lower.
