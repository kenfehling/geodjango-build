Home prices

The price of the median home in Collinsville is $127,300. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $28,280 (28%) higher.It is about $90,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $11,400 (8%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Collinsville is $902 a month. This is roughly $22 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $160 (15%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $74 (8%) higher.
