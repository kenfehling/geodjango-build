Home prices

Rents

In Collinsville the median rent is $902 per month. It is roughly $22 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $160 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $74 (8%) higher.
