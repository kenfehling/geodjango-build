Home prices

Rents

In Collinsville the median rent is $902 a month. This is approximately $22 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $160 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $74 (8%) higher.
