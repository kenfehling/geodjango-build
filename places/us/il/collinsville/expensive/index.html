The cost of the median home in Collinsville is $127,300. This is roughly $28,280 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $90,200 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $11,400 (8%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Collinsville is $902. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $22 (2%) higher. It is roughly $160 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $74 (8%) higher.
