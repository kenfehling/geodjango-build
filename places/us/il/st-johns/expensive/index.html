Compared to other places in this area, St. Johns is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in St. Johns costs $66,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $6,920 (11%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $151,500 (69%) lower, and it is roughly $72,700 (52%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

St. Johns's median monthly rent is $930. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $319 (52%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $132 (12%) lower, and is about $102 (12%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
