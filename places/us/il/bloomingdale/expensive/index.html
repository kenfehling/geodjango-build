In comparison to other places in this area, Bloomingdale is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bloomingdale is $302,400. This is approximately $59,680 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $84,900 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $44,400 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Bloomingdale the median rent is $1,399 per month. It is roughly $114 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $337 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $287 (25%) higher.
