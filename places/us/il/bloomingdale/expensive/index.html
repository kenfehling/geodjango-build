Home prices

The median home in Bloomingdale is valued at $302,400. It is about $59,680 (24%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $84,900 (39%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $44,400 (17%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Bloomingdale is $1,399 a month. This is approximately $114 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $337 (31%) higher, and is approximately $287 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
