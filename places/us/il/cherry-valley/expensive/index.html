Home prices

The price of the median home in Cherry Valley is $170,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $60,120 (54%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $47,400 (21%) lower, and is approximately $87,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Cherry Valley is $739 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $61 (7%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $323 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $373 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
