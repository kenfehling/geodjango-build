Compared to its surrounding areas, Cherry Valley is a lot more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cherry Valley is valued at $170,100. This is approximately $60,120 (54%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $47,400 (21%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $87,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cherry Valley is $739. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $61 (7%) lower.It is approximately $323 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $373 (33%) lower.
