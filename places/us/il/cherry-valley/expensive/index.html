Home prices

Cherry Valley's median home cost is $170,100. It is about $60,120 (54%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $47,400 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $87,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Cherry Valley the median monthly rent is $739. Compared to nearby areas it is about $61 (7%) lower.It is about $323 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $373 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
