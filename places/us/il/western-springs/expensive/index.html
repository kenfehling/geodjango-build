In comparison to nearby areas, Western Springs is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Western Springs is valued at $609,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $165,460 (37%) higher. It is about $392,400 (180%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is about $351,900 (136%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Western Springs's median rent is $2,009 a month. This is about $753 (60%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $947 (89%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $897 (80%) higher.
