Home prices

The cost of the median home in Western Springs is $609,900. This is approximately $165,460 (37%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $392,400 (180%) higher, and is about $351,900 (136%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Western Springs the median rent is $2,009 per month. This is approximately $753 (60%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $947 (89%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $897 (80%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
