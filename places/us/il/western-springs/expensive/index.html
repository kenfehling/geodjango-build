Home prices

The median home cost in Western Springs is $609,900. It is roughly $165,460 (37%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $392,400 (180%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $351,900 (136%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Western Springs's median rent is $2,009 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $753 (60%) higher.It is about $947 (89%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $897 (80%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
