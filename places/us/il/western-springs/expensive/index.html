Compared to the surrounding areas, Western Springs is substantially more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Western Springs's median home price is $609,900. It is approximately $165,460 (37%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $392,400 (180%) higher, and is approximately $351,900 (136%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Western Springs the median rent is $2,009 a month. This is about $753 (60%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $947 (89%) higher, and it is roughly $897 (80%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
