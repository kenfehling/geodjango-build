Home prices

The cost of the median home in Kankakee is $88,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $51,040 (36%) lower. It is roughly $129,000 (59%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $169,500 (65%) lower.

Rents

Kankakee's median rent is $820 per month. It is about $123 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $242 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $292 (26%) lower.
