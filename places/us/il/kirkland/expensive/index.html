Home prices

The median home in Kirkland is valued at $142,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $17,020 (10%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $75,400 (34%) lower, and is roughly $115,900 (44%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Kirkland's median rent is $981 per month. This is approximately $134 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $81 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $131 (11%) lower.
