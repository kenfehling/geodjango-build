Home prices

The median home in Fillmore costs $57,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $8,460 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $159,600 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $80,800 (58%) lower.

Rents

Fillmore's median rent is $700 a month. This is approximately $122 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $128 (15%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
