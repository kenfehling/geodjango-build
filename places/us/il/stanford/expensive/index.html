Home prices

The median home in Stanford costs $87,800. It is roughly $21,940 (19%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $129,700 (59%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Champaign it is about $74,000 (45%) lower.

Rents

Stanford's median monthly rent is $813. It is about $78 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $249 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is roughly $139 (14%) lower.
