Home prices

The median home in Hampton is valued at $152,600. It is about $38,660 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $64,900 (29%) lower, and it is approximately $26,900 (21%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In Hampton the median monthly rent is $926. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $46 (5%) higher.It is approximately $136 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $120 (14%) higher.
