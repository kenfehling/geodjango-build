In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hampton is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Peoria, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hampton costs $152,600. This is about $38,660 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $64,900 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is approximately $26,900 (21%) higher.

Rents

In Hampton the median rent is $926 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $46 (5%) higher.It is approximately $136 (12%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $120 (14%) higher compared to the city of Peoria.
