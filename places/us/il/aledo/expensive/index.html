Home prices

The median home in Aledo costs $97,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $11,840 (13%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $119,600 (54%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is roughly $27,800 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Aledo is $621 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $59 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $441 (41%) lower, and is approximately $185 (22%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
