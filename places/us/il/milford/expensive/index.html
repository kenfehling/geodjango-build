Compared to the surrounding areas, Milford is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Milford is $60,600. It is about $14,600 (19%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $156,900 (72%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $197,400 (76%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Milford the median rent is $601 per month. This is approximately $71 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $461 (43%) lower, and is about $511 (45%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
