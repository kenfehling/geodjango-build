Compared to other places in this area, Raleigh is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Evansville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Raleigh is $66,300. It is roughly $8,560 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $151,200 (69%) lower, and it is about $27,900 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.

Rents

The median rent in Raleigh is $575 per month. It is about $35 (5%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $487 (45%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $199 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
