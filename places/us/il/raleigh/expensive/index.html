Home prices

The median home cost in Raleigh is $66,300. This is approximately $8,560 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $151,200 (69%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is approximately $27,900 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Raleigh is $575 per month. It is about $35 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $487 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $199 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
