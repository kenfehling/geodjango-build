In comparison to its nearby areas, Lemont is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lemont costs $363,000. This is approximately $48,080 (15%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $145,500 (66%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $105,000 (40%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lemont is $1,069. It is roughly $364 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is is very similar, and is about $43 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
