Home prices

The median home value in Wenona is $96,400. This is about $15,960 (19%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $121,100 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is roughly $4,800 (5%) higher.

Rents

Wenona's median monthly rent is $610. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $98 (13%) lower. It is roughly $452 (42%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $162 (20%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
