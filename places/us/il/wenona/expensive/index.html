Home prices

The median home in Wenona costs $96,400. This is approximately $15,960 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $121,100 (55%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Rockford it is approximately $4,800 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wenona is $610 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $98 (13%) lower. It is about $452 (42%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $162 (20%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
