Compared to other places in this area, Marseilles is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marseilles is $108,400. It is approximately $29,800 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $109,100 (50%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $149,600 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Marseilles the median monthly rent is $949. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $72 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $113 (10%) lower, and it is about $163 (14%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
