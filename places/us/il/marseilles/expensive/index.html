Compared to its surrounding areas, Marseilles is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Marseilles is $108,400. This is roughly $29,800 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $109,100 (50%) lower, and is roughly $149,600 (57%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Marseilles the median rent is $949 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $72 (8%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $113 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $163 (14%) lower.
