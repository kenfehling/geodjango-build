Home prices

The median home in Braidwood costs $162,400. This is about $4,240 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $55,100 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $95,600 (37%) lower.

Rents

Braidwood's median monthly rent is $979. This is about $103 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $83 (7%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $133 (11%) lower.
