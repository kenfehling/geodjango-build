Home prices

The median home in Braidwood costs $162,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $4,240 (2%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $55,100 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $95,600 (37%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Braidwood's median rent is $979 a month. This is about $103 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $83 (7%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $133 (11%) lower.
