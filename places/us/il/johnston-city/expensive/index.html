Home prices

Rents

Johnston City's median monthly rent is $532. This is approximately $216 (28%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $530 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $182 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Carbondale.
