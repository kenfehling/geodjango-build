Home prices

The cost of the median home in Johnston City is $65,800. This is roughly $10,860 (14%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $151,700 (69%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Carbondale it is about $51,700 (43%) lower.

Rents

Johnston City's median rent is $532 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $216 (28%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $530 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale is roughly $182 (25%) lower.
