Home prices

Wilsonville's median home price is $56,300. This is roughly $24,480 (30%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $161,200 (74%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $82,400 (59%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Wilsonville the median rent is $763 a month. It is approximately $61 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and it is about $65 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
