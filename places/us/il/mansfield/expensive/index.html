Home prices

The median home in Mansfield is valued at $125,600. It is roughly $1,940 (1%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $91,900 (42%) lower, and is about $36,200 (22%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Mansfield's median rent is $863 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $27 (2%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $199 (18%) lower, and is about $89 (9%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
