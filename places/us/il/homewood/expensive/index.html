Home prices

The median home in Homewood is valued at $163,100. It is about $23,460 (16%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $54,400 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $94,900 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Homewood is $1,156. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $295 (20%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $94 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $44 (3%) higher.
