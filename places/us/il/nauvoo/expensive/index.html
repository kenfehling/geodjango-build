Home prices

The median home in Nauvoo is valued at $103,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $16,460 (18%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $114,200 (52%) lower, and is roughly $111,800 (51%) lower compared to the city of Iowa City.

Rents

The median rent in Nauvoo is $696 per month. It is about $38 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $366 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Iowa City it is roughly $271 (28%) lower.
