Home prices

The median home in Nauvoo costs $103,300. This is approximately $16,460 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $114,200 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Iowa City it is roughly $111,800 (51%) lower.

Rents

Nauvoo's median monthly rent is $696. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $38 (5%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $366 (34%) lower, and is approximately $271 (28%) lower compared to the city of Iowa City.
