Home prices

The median home price in Nauvoo is $103,300. It is about $16,460 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $114,200 (52%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Iowa City is approximately $111,800 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Nauvoo is $696 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $38 (5%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $366 (34%) lower, and is about $271 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Iowa City.
