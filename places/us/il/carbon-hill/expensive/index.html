Home prices

The median home in Carbon Hill is valued at $156,000. It is approximately $3,440 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $61,500 (28%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $102,000 (39%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Carbon Hill the median monthly rent is $1,188. This is about $148 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $126 (11%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $76 (6%) higher.
