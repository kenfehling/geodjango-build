Home prices

The median home in Butler costs $65,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $4,100 (5%) lower. It is roughly $152,000 (69%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $73,200 (52%) lower.

Rents

Butler's median monthly rent is $783. It is about $221 (39%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $279 (26%) lower, and it is about $45 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
