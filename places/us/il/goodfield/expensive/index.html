Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Goodfield is $613. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $160 (20%) lower. It is about $449 (42%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $339 (35%) lower.
