Home prices

The median home in Huntley costs $251,400. This is roughly $5,360 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $33,900 (15%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $6,600 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Huntley is $1,231. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $359 (22%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $169 (15%) higher, and it is approximately $119 (10%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
