Home prices

The median home value in Huntley is $251,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $5,360 (2%) higher. It is roughly $33,900 (15%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $6,600 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Huntley is $1,231 per month. This is roughly $359 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $169 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $119 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
