Home prices

The price of the median home in Waukegan is $132,200. It is roughly $20,800 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $85,300 (39%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $125,800 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Waukegan the median rent is $960 a month. This is roughly $291 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $102 (9%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $152 (13%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
