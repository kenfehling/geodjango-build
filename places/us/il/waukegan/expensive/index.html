In comparison to other places in this area, Waukegan is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Waukegan is $132,200. This is about $20,800 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $85,300 (39%) lower, and it is roughly $125,800 (48%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Waukegan is $960 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $291 (23%) lower.It is roughly $102 (9%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $152 (13%) lower.
