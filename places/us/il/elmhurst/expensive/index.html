Home prices

The price of the median home in Elmhurst is $432,600. This is roughly $253,280 (141%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $215,100 (98%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $174,600 (67%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elmhurst is $1,497. This is approximately $429 (40%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $435 (40%) higher, and is roughly $385 (34%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
