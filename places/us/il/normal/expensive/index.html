Home prices

The median home value in Normal is $170,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $22,680 (15%) higher. It is about $47,300 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $8,400 (5%) higher compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Normal's median rent is $875 per month. This is roughly $44 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $187 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is approximately $77 (8%) lower.
