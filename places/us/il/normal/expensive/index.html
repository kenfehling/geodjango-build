Home prices

The price of the median home in Normal is $170,200. This is approximately $22,680 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $47,300 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is approximately $8,400 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Normal the median monthly rent is $875. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $44 (5%) higher.It is approximately $187 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Champaign is roughly $77 (8%) lower.
