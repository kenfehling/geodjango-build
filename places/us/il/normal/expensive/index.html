Home prices

The median home price in Normal is $170,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,680 (15%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $47,300 (21%) lower, and is approximately $8,400 (5%) higher compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Normal's median rent is $875 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $44 (5%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $187 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is roughly $77 (8%) lower.
