Home prices

The median home in Westmont is valued at $301,900. This is roughly $219,860 (42%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $84,400 (38%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $43,900 (17%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Westmont's median monthly rent is $1,139. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $414 (26%) lower. It is approximately $77 (7%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $27 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
