Home prices

The median home in Altamont is valued at $98,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $26,580 (36%) higher. It is about $118,900 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $63,200 (39%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

In Altamont the median rent is $748 a month. It is roughly $211 (39%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $314 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $204 (21%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
