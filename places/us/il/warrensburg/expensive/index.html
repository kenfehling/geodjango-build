Home prices

The median home in Warrensburg is valued at $98,800. This is about $15,820 (13%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $63,000 (38%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Warrensburg is $619. It is about $162 (20%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $443 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign is approximately $333 (34%) lower.
