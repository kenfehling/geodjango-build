Home prices

The median home in Liberty costs $95,900. It is about $6,960 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $121,600 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Charles it is about $104,500 (52%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Liberty is $734 per month. It is approximately $26 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $328 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Charles it is approximately $251 (25%) lower.
