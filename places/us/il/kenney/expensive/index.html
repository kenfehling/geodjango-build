Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Kenney is $638 a month. It is about $13 (2%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $424 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $314 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
