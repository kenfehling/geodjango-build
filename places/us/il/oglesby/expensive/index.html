Home prices

Rents

Oglesby's median monthly rent is $604. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $153 (20%) lower.It is about $458 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $168 (21%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
