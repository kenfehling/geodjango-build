Home prices

The median home in Ringwood costs $289,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $78,200 (37%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $71,900 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $31,400 (12%) higher.

Rents

In Ringwood the median monthly rent is $1,188. It is roughly $33 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $126 (11%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $76 (6%) higher.
