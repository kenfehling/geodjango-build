Rents

The median monthly rent in Hebron is $995. It is approximately $87 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $67 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $117 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
