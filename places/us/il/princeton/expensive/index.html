In comparison to the surrounding areas, Princeton is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Rockford, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Princeton is $120,800. It is about $49,980 (70%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $96,700 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Rockford is about $29,200 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Princeton the median monthly rent is $650. It is about $150 (18%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $412 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Rockford it is roughly $122 (15%) lower.
