Compared to other places in this area, Princeton is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Rockford.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Princeton is $120,800. It is approximately $49,980 (70%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $96,700 (44%) lower, and is roughly $29,200 (31%) higher compared to the city of Rockford.

Rents

Princeton's median rent is $650 per month. This is approximately $150 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $412 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is about $122 (15%) lower.
