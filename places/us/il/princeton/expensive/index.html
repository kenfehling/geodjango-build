Home prices

The median home in Princeton is valued at $120,800. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $49,980 (70%) higher.It is about $96,700 (44%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Rockford it is approximately $29,200 (31%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Princeton is $650. Compared to nearby areas it is about $150 (18%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $412 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Rockford it is roughly $122 (15%) lower.
