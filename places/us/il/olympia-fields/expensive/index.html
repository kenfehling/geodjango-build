Home prices

Olympia Fields's median home price is $226,500. This is approximately $80,480 (55%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $9,000 (4%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $31,500 (12%) lower.

Rents

In Olympia Fields the median rent is $1,866 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $572 (44%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $804 (75%) higher, and it is about $754 (67%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
