Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Lisle is $1,279 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $131 (9%) lower. It is approximately $217 (20%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $167 (15%) higher.
