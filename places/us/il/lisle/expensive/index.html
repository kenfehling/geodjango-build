Home prices

The median home price in Lisle is $356,900. It is roughly $32,640 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $139,400 (64%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $98,900 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Lisle the median monthly rent is $1,279. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $131 (9%) lower.It is approximately $217 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $167 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
