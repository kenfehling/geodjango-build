Home prices

The cost of the median home in Albany is $118,100. This is roughly $3,040 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $99,400 (45%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is roughly $26,500 (28%) higher.

Rents

Albany's median rent is $755 a month. This is about $49 (7%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $307 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Rockford is roughly $17 (2%) lower.
