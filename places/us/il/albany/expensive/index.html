Home prices

The price of the median home in Albany is $118,100. It is approximately $3,040 (2%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $99,400 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is about $26,500 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Albany the median rent is $755 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $49 (7%) higher.It is roughly $307 (28%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $17 (2%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
