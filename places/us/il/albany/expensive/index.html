Much like its nearby areas, it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Rockford, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Albany is $118,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $3,040 (2%) higher. It is roughly $99,400 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $26,500 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Rockford.

Rents

In Albany the median monthly rent is $755. This is roughly $49 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $307 (28%) lower, and is roughly $17 (2%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
