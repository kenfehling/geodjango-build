Home prices

The price of the median home in Pontiac is $97,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $6,680 (7%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $120,500 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $161,000 (62%) lower.

Rents

Pontiac's median monthly rent is $715. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $111 (13%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $347 (32%) lower, and is about $397 (35%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
