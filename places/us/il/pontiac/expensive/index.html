Home prices

The median home cost in Pontiac is $97,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $6,680 (7%) higher. It is about $120,500 (55%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $161,000 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Pontiac's median monthly rent is $715. It is about $111 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $347 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $397 (35%) lower.
