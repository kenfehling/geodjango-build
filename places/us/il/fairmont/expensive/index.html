Home prices

The median home in Fairmont is valued at $105,300. This is roughly $44,500 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $112,200 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $152,700 (59%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairmont is $1,127. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $71 (6%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $65 (6%) higher, and is approximately $15 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
