Compared to the surrounding areas, Arlington Heights is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Arlington Heights is valued at $358,300. This is about $90,540 (33%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $140,800 (64%) higher, and it is approximately $100,300 (38%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Arlington Heights the median rent is $1,368 per month. It is approximately $153 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $306 (28%) higher, and is approximately $256 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
