Compared to other places in this area, Arlington Heights is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Arlington Heights's median home cost is $358,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $90,540 (33%) higher.It is roughly $140,800 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $100,300 (38%) higher.

Rents

In Arlington Heights the median rent is $1,368 a month. This is approximately $153 (12%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $306 (28%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $256 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
