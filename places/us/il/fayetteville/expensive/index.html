Rents

The median monthly rent in Fayetteville is $719. It is about $32 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $343 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $109 (13%) lower.
