Home prices

The price of the median home in West Peoria is $88,700. This is approximately $2,480 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $128,800 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $37,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in West Peoria is $756. It is approximately $13 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $306 (28%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $50 (6%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
