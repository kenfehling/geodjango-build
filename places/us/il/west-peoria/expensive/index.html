Home prices

The price of the median home in West Peoria is $88,700. This is about $2,480 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $128,800 (59%) lower, and is approximately $37,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

West Peoria's median monthly rent is $756. It is approximately $13 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $306 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $50 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
