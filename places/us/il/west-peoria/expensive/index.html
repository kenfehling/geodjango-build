Home prices

The median home in West Peoria is valued at $88,700. It is roughly $2,480 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $128,800 (59%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $37,000 (29%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In West Peoria the median rent is $756 a month. This is roughly $13 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $306 (28%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is approximately $50 (6%) lower.
