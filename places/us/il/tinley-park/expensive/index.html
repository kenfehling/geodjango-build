The median home price in Tinley Park is $231,400. This is approximately $19,180 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $13,900 (6%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $26,600 (10%) lower.

Rents
