Home prices

The median home in Tinley Park costs $231,400. It is about $19,180 (9%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $13,900 (6%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $26,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

Tinley Park's median rent is $1,198 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is is very similar.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $136 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $86 (7%) higher.
