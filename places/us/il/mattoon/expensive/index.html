Compared to the surrounding areas, Mattoon is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Champaign.

Home prices

The median home in Mattoon costs $85,400. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $5,960 (7%) higher. It is approximately $132,100 (60%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $76,400 (47%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mattoon is $663. It is roughly $37 (5%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $399 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $289 (30%) lower.
