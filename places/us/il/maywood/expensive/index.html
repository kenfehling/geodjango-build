Compared to other places in this area, Maywood is a lot less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Maywood is valued at $151,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $115,320 (43%) lower. It is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $106,700 (41%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Maywood is $965 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $106 (9%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $97 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $147 (13%) lower.
