Home prices

The median home in Jonesboro costs $83,600. It is approximately $20,760 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $133,900 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Carbondale is about $33,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Jonesboro the median monthly rent is $567. It is approximately $69 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $495 (46%) lower, and is approximately $147 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Carbondale.
