Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Salem is $671. This is approximately $124 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $391 (36%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $157 (18%) lower.
