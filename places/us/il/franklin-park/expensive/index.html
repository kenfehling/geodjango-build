Home prices

The median home in Franklin Park costs $203,800. It is approximately $22,480 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $13,700 (6%) lower, and it is approximately $54,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Franklin Park the median rent is $881 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $130 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $181 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $231 (20%) lower.
