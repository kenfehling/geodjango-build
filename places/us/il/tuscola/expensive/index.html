In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tuscola is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Champaign.

Home prices

Tuscola's median home value is $115,500. This is roughly $21,660 (23%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $102,000 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is approximately $46,300 (28%) lower.

Rents

Tuscola's median monthly rent is $733. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $19 (2%) higher. It is about $329 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $219 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
