Home prices

The median home price in Hinsdale is $884,700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $451,480 (104%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $667,200 (306%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $626,700 (242%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hinsdale is $1,879 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $620 (49%) higher. It is about $817 (76%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $767 (68%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
