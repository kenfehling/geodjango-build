In comparison to the surrounding areas, Long Grove is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Long Grove is $648,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $263,820 (68%) higher. It is about $431,200 (198%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $390,700 (151%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Long Grove is $3,250 a month. This is approximately $1,279 (64%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $2,188 (206%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $2,138 (192%) higher.
