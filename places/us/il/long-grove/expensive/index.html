Home prices

The median home in Long Grove costs $648,700. This is about $263,820 (68%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $431,200 (198%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $390,700 (151%) higher.

Rents

Long Grove's median monthly rent is $3,250. It is about $1,279 (64%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $2,188 (206%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $2,138 (192%) higher.
