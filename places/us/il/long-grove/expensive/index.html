Compared to the surrounding areas, Long Grove is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Long Grove is $648,700. It is roughly $263,820 (68%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $431,200 (198%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $390,700 (151%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Long Grove is $3,250 per month. This is approximately $1,279 (64%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $2,188 (206%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $2,138 (192%) higher.
