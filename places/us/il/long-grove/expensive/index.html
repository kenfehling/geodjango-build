Home prices

The median home value in Long Grove is $648,700. This is approximately $263,820 (68%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $431,200 (198%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $390,700 (151%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Long Grove is $3,250. It is approximately $1,279 (64%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is about $2,188 (206%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $2,138 (192%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
