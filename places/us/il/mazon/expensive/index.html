The median home in Mazon costs $151,100. This is roughly $6,060 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $66,400 (30%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $106,900 (41%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Mazon the median rent is $969 a month. This is about $69 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $93 (8%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $143 (12%) lower.
