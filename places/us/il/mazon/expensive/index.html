Home prices

The median home in Mazon is valued at $151,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $6,060 (4%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $66,400 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $106,900 (41%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Mazon is $969 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $69 (6%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $93 (8%) lower, and is approximately $143 (12%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
