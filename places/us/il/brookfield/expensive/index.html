Home prices

Brookfield's median home price is $245,200. It is approximately $92,540 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $27,700 (12%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $12,800 (4%) lower.

Rents

In Brookfield the median rent is $1,029 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $86 (7%) lower. It is about $33 (3%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $83 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
