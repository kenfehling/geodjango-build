In comparison to its surrounding areas, Warsaw is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Iowa City, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Warsaw costs $82,700. It is roughly $10,800 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $134,800 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Iowa City it is approximately $132,400 (61%) lower.

Rents

Warsaw's median monthly rent is $631. It is roughly $14 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $431 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $336 (34%) lower compared to the city of Iowa City.
