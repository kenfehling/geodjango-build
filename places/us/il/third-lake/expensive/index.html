Home prices

The median home in Third Lake is valued at $267,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $64,980 (32%) higher.It is about $49,900 (22%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $9,400 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Third Lake's median rent is $1,469 a month. It is about $159 (12%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $407 (38%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $357 (32%) higher.
