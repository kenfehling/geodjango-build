Home prices

The median home cost in Bloomington is $168,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $8,980 (5%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $49,200 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign is about $6,500 (4%) higher.

Rents

In Bloomington the median rent is $836 per month. This is about $26 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $226 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $116 (12%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
