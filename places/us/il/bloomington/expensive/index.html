Home prices

The median home cost in Bloomington is $168,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $8,980 (5%) higher. It is about $49,200 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $6,500 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

In Bloomington the median rent is $836 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $26 (3%) lower. It is about $226 (21%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $116 (12%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
