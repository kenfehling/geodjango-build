Home prices

The price of the median home in Bloomington is $168,300. This is about $8,980 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $49,200 (22%) lower, and is about $6,500 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

In Bloomington the median rent is $836 a month. This is roughly $26 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $226 (21%) lower, and is approximately $116 (12%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
