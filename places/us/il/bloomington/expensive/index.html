Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bloomington is $168,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $8,980 (5%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $49,200 (22%) lower, and it is about $6,500 (4%) higher compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Bloomington's median rent is $836 per month. This is approximately $26 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $226 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $116 (12%) lower.
