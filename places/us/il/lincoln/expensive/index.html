Home prices

The median home in Lincoln is valued at $88,300. This is roughly $6,480 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $129,200 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is approximately $73,500 (45%) lower.

Rents

Lincoln's median monthly rent is $692. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $47 (7%) higher. It is approximately $370 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Champaign is roughly $260 (27%) lower.
