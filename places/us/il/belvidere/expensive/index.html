Home prices

Rents

Belvidere's median monthly rent is $776. This is approximately $358 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $286 (26%) lower, and is roughly $336 (30%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
