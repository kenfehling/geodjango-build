Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Charles is a little less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in St. Charles is $293,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $38,260 (11%) lower. It is approximately $75,800 (34%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $35,300 (13%) higher.

Rents

St. Charles's median monthly rent is $1,288. It is approximately $451 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $226 (21%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $176 (15%) higher.
