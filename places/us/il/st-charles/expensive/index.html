In comparison to nearby areas, St. Charles is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Charles costs $293,300. It is approximately $38,260 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $75,800 (34%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $35,300 (13%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Charles is $1,288. It is roughly $451 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $226 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $176 (15%) higher.
