Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Charles is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Charles is valued at $293,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $38,260 (11%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $75,800 (34%) higher, and is approximately $35,300 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

St. Charles's median monthly rent is $1,288. This is about $451 (25%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $226 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $176 (15%) higher.
