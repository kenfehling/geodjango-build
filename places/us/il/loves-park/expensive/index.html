Home prices

The median home in Loves Park is valued at $111,900. This is about $15,980 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $105,600 (48%) lower, and it is about $146,100 (56%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Loves Park is $848 a month. This is roughly $209 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $214 (20%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $264 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
