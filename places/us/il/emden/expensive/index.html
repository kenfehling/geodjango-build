Home prices

The cost of the median home in Emden is $96,500. This is roughly $8,160 (9%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $121,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $65,300 (40%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Emden's median rent is $700 a month. It is about $75 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $252 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
