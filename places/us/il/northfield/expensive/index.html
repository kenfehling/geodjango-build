Compared to nearby areas, Northfield is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Northfield is $638,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $236,440 (27%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $420,900 (193%) higher, and it is approximately $380,400 (147%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Northfield the median rent is $2,083 per month. It is about $210 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $1,021 (96%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $971 (87%) higher.
