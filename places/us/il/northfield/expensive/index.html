Home prices

The median home in Northfield is valued at $638,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $236,440 (27%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $420,900 (193%) higher, and is approximately $380,400 (147%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Northfield's median rent is $2,083 a month. It is about $210 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $1,021 (96%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $971 (87%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
