In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Moline is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Peoria, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in East Moline is $110,800. This is about $26,580 (19%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $106,700 (49%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Peoria it is about $14,900 (11%) lower.

Rents

In East Moline the median rent is $658 a month. It is about $253 (27%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $404 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $148 (18%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
