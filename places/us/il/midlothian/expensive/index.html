Compared to its surrounding areas, Midlothian is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Midlothian is valued at $150,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $23,200 (18%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $67,100 (30%) lower, and is roughly $107,600 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Midlothian's median monthly rent is $1,034. It is about $20 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $28 (2%) lower, and is roughly $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
