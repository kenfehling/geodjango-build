Home prices

The median home in Midlothian costs $150,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $23,200 (18%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $67,100 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $107,600 (41%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Midlothian is $1,034. This is approximately $20 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $28 (2%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $78 (7%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
