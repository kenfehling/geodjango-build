Home prices

The value of the median home in Midlothian is $150,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $23,200 (18%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $67,100 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $107,600 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Midlothian's median rent is $1,034 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $20 (1%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $28 (2%) lower, and is about $78 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
