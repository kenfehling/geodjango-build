Compared to its nearby areas, Wheaton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wheaton is valued at $358,000. This is approximately $60,080 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $140,500 (64%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $100,000 (38%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wheaton is $1,419. This is roughly $47 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $357 (33%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $307 (27%) higher.
