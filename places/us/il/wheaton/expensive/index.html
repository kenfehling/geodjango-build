In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wheaton is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wheaton is $358,000. It is roughly $60,080 (20%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $140,500 (64%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $100,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Wheaton is $1,419 a month. It is roughly $47 (3%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $357 (33%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $307 (27%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
