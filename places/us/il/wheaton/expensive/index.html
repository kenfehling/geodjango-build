Compared to nearby areas, Wheaton is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home cost in Wheaton is $358,000. It is roughly $60,080 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $140,500 (64%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $100,000 (38%) higher.

Rents

Wheaton's median monthly rent is $1,419. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $47 (3%) lower.It is about $357 (33%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $307 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
