In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wheaton is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Wheaton's median home cost is $358,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $60,080 (20%) higher. It is roughly $140,500 (64%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $100,000 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Wheaton's median rent is $1,419 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $47 (3%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $357 (33%) higher, and it is about $307 (27%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
