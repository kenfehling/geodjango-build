Home prices

Salem's median home cost is $78,500. This is about $27,960 (55%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $139,000 (63%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $60,200 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Salem is $620 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $20 (3%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $442 (41%) lower, and it is about $208 (25%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
