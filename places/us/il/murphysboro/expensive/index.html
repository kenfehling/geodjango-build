Home prices

The median home in Murphysboro is valued at $70,500. This is roughly $4,000 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $147,000 (67%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $68,200 (49%) lower.

Rents

Murphysboro's median rent is $594 per month. This is about $37 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $468 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $234 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
