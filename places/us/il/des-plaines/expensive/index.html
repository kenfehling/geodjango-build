Compared to its surrounding areas, Des Plaines is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Des Plaines is $256,700. This is roughly $82,300 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $39,200 (18%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is is very similar.

Rents

Des Plaines's median rent is $1,144 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $34 (2%) lower.It is roughly $82 (7%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $32 (2%) higher.
