Home prices

The median home in Reynolds is valued at $120,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $6,620 (5%) higher. It is approximately $97,500 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Peoria is roughly $5,700 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Reynolds is $844. It is about $188 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $218 (20%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $38 (4%) higher.
