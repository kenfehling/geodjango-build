Home prices

The median home price in Bethalto is $133,300. This is about $44,380 (49%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $84,200 (38%) lower, and is roughly $5,400 (3%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Bethalto is $803. This is roughly $33 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $259 (24%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $25 (3%) lower.
