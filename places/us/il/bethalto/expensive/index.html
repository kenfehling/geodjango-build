Compared to other places in this area, Bethalto is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is close to average.

Home prices

Bethalto's median home value is $133,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $44,380 (49%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $84,200 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $5,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Bethalto the median rent is $803 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $33 (4%) higher. It is about $259 (24%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $25 (3%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
