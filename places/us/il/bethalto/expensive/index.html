Compared to the surrounding areas, Bethalto is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Bethalto is valued at $133,300. This is roughly $44,380 (49%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $84,200 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $5,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

In Bethalto the median rent is $803 per month. This is roughly $33 (4%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $259 (24%) lower, and is about $25 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
