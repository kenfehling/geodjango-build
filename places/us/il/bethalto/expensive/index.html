Home prices

Bethalto's median home cost is $133,300. It is roughly $44,380 (49%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $84,200 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $5,400 (3%) lower.

Rents

Bethalto's median monthly rent is $803. This is roughly $33 (4%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $259 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $25 (3%) lower.
