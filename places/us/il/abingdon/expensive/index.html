Home prices

The price of the median home in Abingdon is $61,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,640 (19%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $155,800 (71%) lower, and it is approximately $64,000 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Abingdon's median rent is $547 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $114 (17%) lower. It is roughly $515 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $259 (32%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
