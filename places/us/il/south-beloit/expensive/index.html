Home prices

Rents

In South Beloit the median monthly rent is $784. Compared to nearby areas this is about $80 (9%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $278 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $328 (29%) lower.
