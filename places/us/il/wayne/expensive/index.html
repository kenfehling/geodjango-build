Home prices

Rents

In Wayne the median monthly rent is $3,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $2,127 (154%) higher. It is roughly $2,438 (229%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $2,388 (214%) higher.
