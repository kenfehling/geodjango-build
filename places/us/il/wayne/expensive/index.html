Home prices

The median home price in Wayne is $530,100. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $250,940 (89%) higher. It is roughly $312,600 (143%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $272,100 (105%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Wayne the median rent is $3,500 a month. This is approximately $2,127 (154%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $2,438 (229%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $2,388 (214%) higher.
