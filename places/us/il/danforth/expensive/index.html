Home prices

Rents

Danforth's median rent is $879 per month. It is about $141 (19%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $233 (20%) lower.
