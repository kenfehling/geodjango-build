Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Ohio is $625 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $261 (29%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $437 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is about $147 (19%) lower.
