Home prices

The price of the median home in Marquette Heights is $103,300. This is roughly $2,300 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $114,200 (52%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $22,400 (17%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Marquette Heights's median rent is $1,112 per month. It is about $360 (47%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $50 (4%) higher, and is approximately $306 (37%) higher compared to the city of Peoria.
