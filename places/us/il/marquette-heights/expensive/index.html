Home prices

Marquette Heights's median home cost is $103,300. It is about $2,300 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $114,200 (52%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is approximately $22,400 (17%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marquette Heights is $1,112. This is approximately $360 (47%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $50 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $306 (37%) higher compared to the city of Peoria.
