Home prices

The median home cost in Colchester is $67,200. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $14,580 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $150,300 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $58,500 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Colchester is $663. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $71 (9%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $399 (37%) lower, and is approximately $143 (17%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
