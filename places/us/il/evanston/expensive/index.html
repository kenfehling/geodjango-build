Compared to its surrounding areas, Evanston is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Evanston is valued at $391,400. This is approximately $180,800 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $173,900 (79%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $133,400 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Evanston the median monthly rent is $1,376. It is about $500 (26%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $314 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $264 (23%) higher.
