In comparison to the surrounding areas, Evanston is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Evanston's median home value is $391,400. It is roughly $180,800 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $173,900 (79%) higher, and is about $133,400 (51%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Evanston's median monthly rent is $1,376. This is about $500 (26%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $314 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $264 (23%) higher.
