Compared to its surrounding areas, Evanston is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Evanston is $391,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $180,800 (31%) lower. It is roughly $173,900 (79%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $133,400 (51%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Evanston is $1,376. Compared to other places in this area this is about $500 (26%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $314 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $264 (23%) higher.
