Home prices

The median home in Chicago Heights is valued at $105,900. Compared to nearby areas it is about $15,620 (12%) lower.It is about $111,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $152,100 (58%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Chicago Heights is $940 a month. It is roughly $291 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $122 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $172 (15%) lower.
