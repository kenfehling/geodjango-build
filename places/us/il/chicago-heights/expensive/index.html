In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chicago Heights is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Chicago Heights is valued at $105,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $15,620 (12%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $111,600 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $152,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chicago Heights is $940. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $291 (23%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $122 (11%) lower, and it is about $172 (15%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
