Compared to other places in this area, Chicago Heights is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Chicago Heights is $105,900. It is approximately $15,620 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $111,600 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $152,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chicago Heights is $940. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $291 (23%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $122 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $172 (15%) lower.
