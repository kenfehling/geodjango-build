Home prices

Rents

In Glendale Heights the median monthly rent is $1,272. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $35 (2%) higher.It is roughly $210 (19%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $160 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
