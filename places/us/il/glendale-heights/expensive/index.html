Home prices

The value of the median home in Glendale Heights is $194,400. It is approximately $107,760 (35%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $23,100 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $63,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Glendale Heights the median monthly rent is $1,272. It is about $35 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $210 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $160 (14%) higher.
