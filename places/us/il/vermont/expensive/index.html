Compared to its surrounding areas, Vermont is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The median home price in Vermont is $51,600. This is roughly $9,780 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $165,900 (76%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria it is approximately $74,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Vermont is $717. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $68 (10%) higher. It is approximately $345 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $89 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
