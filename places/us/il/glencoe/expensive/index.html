In comparison to its surrounding areas, Glencoe is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Glencoe is $953,700. It is about $159,600 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $736,200 (338%) higher, and is roughly $695,700 (269%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Glencoe's median rent is $1,574 a month. It is roughly $511 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $512 (48%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $462 (41%) higher.
