Compared to its nearby areas, Glencoe is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Glencoe is $953,700. It is roughly $159,600 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $736,200 (338%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $695,700 (269%) higher.

Rents

Glencoe's median monthly rent is $1,574. It is about $511 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $512 (48%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $462 (41%) higher.
