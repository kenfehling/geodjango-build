In comparison to nearby areas, Glencoe is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Glencoe's median home cost is $953,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $159,600 (20%) higher.It is roughly $736,200 (338%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $695,700 (269%) higher.

Rents

Glencoe's median monthly rent is $1,574. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $511 (24%) lower.It is roughly $512 (48%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $462 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
