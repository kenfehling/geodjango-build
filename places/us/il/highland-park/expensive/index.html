Home prices

The median home price in Highland Park is $584,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $82,100 (12%) lower. It is approximately $367,000 (168%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $326,500 (126%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Highland Park the median rent is $1,754 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $183 (11%) higher. It is approximately $692 (65%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $642 (57%) higher.
