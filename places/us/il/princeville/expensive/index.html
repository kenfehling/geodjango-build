In comparison to its surrounding areas, Princeville is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The median home value in Princeville is $128,000. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $11,880 (8%) lower. It is approximately $89,500 (41%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $2,300 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Princeville is $818. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $68 (9%) higher. It is approximately $244 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is about $12 (1%) higher.
