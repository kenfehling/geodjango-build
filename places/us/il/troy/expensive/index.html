Compared to other places in this area, Troy is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Troy is $178,800. It is roughly $11,260 (5%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $38,700 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $40,100 (28%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Troy is $818. This is approximately $169 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $244 (22%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $10 (1%) lower.
