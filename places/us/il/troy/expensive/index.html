Compared to nearby areas, Troy is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Troy is valued at $178,800. It is approximately $11,260 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $38,700 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $40,100 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Troy the median monthly rent is $818. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $169 (17%) lower. It is about $244 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $10 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
