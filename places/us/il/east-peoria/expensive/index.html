Compared to nearby areas, East Peoria is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The median home price in East Peoria is $139,800. This is approximately $28,480 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $77,700 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $14,100 (11%) higher compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In East Peoria the median monthly rent is $687. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $133 (16%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $375 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria is approximately $119 (14%) lower.
