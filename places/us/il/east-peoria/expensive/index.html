Rents

The median monthly rent in East Peoria is $687. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $133 (16%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $375 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is approximately $119 (14%) lower.
