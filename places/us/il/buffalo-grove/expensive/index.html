Home prices

The median home in Buffalo Grove costs $332,300. This is about $161,260 (32%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $114,800 (52%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $74,300 (28%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Buffalo Grove is $1,643. It is roughly $776 (32%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $581 (54%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $531 (47%) higher.
