Home prices

The price of the median home in Washington Park is $45,500. This is about $13,500 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $172,000 (79%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $93,200 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Washington Park the median rent is $835 per month. It is about $139 (20%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $227 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
