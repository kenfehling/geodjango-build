Home prices

The median home in North Pekin is valued at $89,800. This is about $18,500 (17%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $127,700 (58%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $35,900 (28%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In North Pekin the median rent is $928 a month. This is roughly $139 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $134 (12%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $122 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.
