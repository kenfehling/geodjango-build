In comparison to other places in this area, North Pekin is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Peoria, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in North Pekin is $89,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $18,500 (17%) lower. It is roughly $127,700 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Peoria it is about $35,900 (28%) lower.

Rents

In North Pekin the median rent is $928 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $139 (17%) higher. It is approximately $134 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $122 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.
