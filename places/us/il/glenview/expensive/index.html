Home prices

The price of the median home in Glenview is $499,900. It is roughly $73,400 (12%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $282,400 (129%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $241,900 (93%) higher.

Rents

Glenview's median monthly rent is $1,771. It is about $25 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is about $709 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $659 (59%) higher.
