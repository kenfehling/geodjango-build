Compared to other places in this area, Glenview is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Glenview is $499,900. It is about $73,400 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $282,400 (129%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $241,900 (93%) higher.

Rents

Glenview's median monthly rent is $1,771. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $25 (1%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $709 (66%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $659 (59%) higher.
