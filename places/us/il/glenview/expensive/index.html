Compared to nearby areas, Glenview is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Glenview is $499,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $73,400 (12%) lower. It is roughly $282,400 (129%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $241,900 (93%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Glenview's median monthly rent is $1,771. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $25 (1%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $709 (66%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $659 (59%) higher.
