Home prices

Rents

Lockport's median rent is $1,241 a month. It is approximately $24 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $179 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $129 (11%) higher.
