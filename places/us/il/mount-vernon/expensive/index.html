In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mount Vernon is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Carbondale.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mount Vernon is $78,600. This is about $7,380 (10%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $138,900 (63%) lower, and it is approximately $38,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Carbondale.

Rents

In Mount Vernon the median rent is $714 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $110 (18%) higher.It is roughly $348 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale is is very similar.
