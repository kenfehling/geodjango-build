In comparison to other places in this area, Hartford is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hartford is $76,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $11,820 (13%) lower. It is about $141,100 (64%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $62,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Hartford the median monthly rent is $901. It is roughly $151 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $161 (15%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $73 (8%) higher.
