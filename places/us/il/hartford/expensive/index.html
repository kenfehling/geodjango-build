Hartford's median home value is $76,400. This is roughly $11,820 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $141,100 (64%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $62,300 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hartford is $901. It is about $151 (20%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $161 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $73 (8%) higher.
