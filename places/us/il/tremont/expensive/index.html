In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tremont is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Champaign however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Tremont is $164,200. This is roughly $37,180 (29%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $53,300 (24%) lower, and is approximately $2,400 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Tremont's median rent is $638 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $139 (17%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $424 (39%) lower, and it is about $314 (32%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
