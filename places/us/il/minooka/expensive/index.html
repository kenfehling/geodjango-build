Home prices

The median home price in Minooka is $212,500. This is about $18,580 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $5,000 (2%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $45,500 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Minooka the median rent is $1,233 per month. This is roughly $46 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $171 (16%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $121 (10%) higher.
