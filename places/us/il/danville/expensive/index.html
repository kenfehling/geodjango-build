Home prices

The median home in Danville costs $66,700. It is about $11,180 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $150,800 (69%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $95,100 (58%) lower.

Rents

In Danville the median monthly rent is $695. This is is very similar compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $367 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $257 (26%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
