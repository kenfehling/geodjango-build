Home prices

The median home in Victoria costs $50,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $21,340 (29%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $167,500 (77%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $75,700 (60%) lower.

Rents

Victoria's median rent is $632 a month. This is about $27 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $430 (40%) lower, and is roughly $174 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
