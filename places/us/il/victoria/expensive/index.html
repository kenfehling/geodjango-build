Home prices

Victoria's median home cost is $50,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $21,340 (29%) lower. It is about $167,500 (77%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Peoria it is approximately $75,700 (60%) lower.

Rents

In Victoria the median rent is $632 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27 (4%) higher. It is about $430 (40%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Peoria it is about $174 (21%) lower.
