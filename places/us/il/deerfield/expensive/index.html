Compared to nearby areas, Deerfield is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Deerfield is $518,500. It is about $113,460 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $301,000 (138%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $260,500 (100%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Deerfield is $1,875 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $135 (7%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $813 (76%) higher, and is about $763 (68%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
