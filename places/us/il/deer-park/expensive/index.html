Home prices

The median home cost in Deer Park is $552,800. This is approximately $76,300 (16%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $335,300 (154%) higher, and is approximately $294,800 (114%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Deer Park is $1,302 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $847 (39%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $240 (22%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $190 (17%) higher.
