Rents

In Carol Stream the median monthly rent is $1,192. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $304 (20%) lower. It is roughly $130 (12%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $80 (7%) higher.
