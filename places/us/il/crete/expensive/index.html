Home prices

The median home cost in Crete is $171,200. It is about $75,900 (79%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $46,300 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $86,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

Crete's median monthly rent is $1,094. It is about $26 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $32 (3%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $18 (1%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
