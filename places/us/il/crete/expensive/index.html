Home prices

The median home in Crete costs $171,200. This is roughly $75,900 (79%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $46,300 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $86,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

Crete's median rent is $1,094 per month. It is roughly $26 (2%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $32 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $18 (1%) lower.
