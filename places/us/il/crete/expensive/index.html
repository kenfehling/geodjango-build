Compared to nearby areas, Crete is a lot more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Crete is $171,200. It is about $75,900 (79%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $46,300 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $86,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

In Crete the median monthly rent is $1,094. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $26 (2%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $32 (3%) higher, and is about $18 (1%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
