Home prices

The price of the median home in Glen Carbon is $226,500. It is approximately $68,860 (43%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $9,000 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $87,800 (63%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Glen Carbon is $990 per month. This is approximately $141 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $72 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $162 (19%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.
