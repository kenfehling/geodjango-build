In comparison to other places in this area, Omaha is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Evansville, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Omaha costs $45,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $7,960 (14%) lower. It is roughly $171,900 (79%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $48,600 (51%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

Omaha's median monthly rent is $346. It is roughly $227 (39%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $716 (67%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is roughly $428 (55%) lower.
