Home prices

The median home in Middletown costs $61,800. This is approximately $18,720 (23%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $155,700 (71%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign is approximately $100,000 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Middletown is $655 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $15 (2%) higher. It is roughly $407 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Champaign it is about $297 (31%) lower.
