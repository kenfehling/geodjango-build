Home prices

The median home in Bridgeport costs $55,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $9,540 (14%) lower. It is approximately $161,800 (74%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Evansville is roughly $38,500 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Bridgeport the median rent is $560 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $58 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is about $502 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville is about $214 (27%) lower.
