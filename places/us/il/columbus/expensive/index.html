Compared to its surrounding areas, Columbus is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Quincy however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Columbus is $105,000. It is approximately $17,300 (19%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $112,500 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Quincy it is about $11,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Columbus is $830 a month. It is roughly $149 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $232 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $120 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Quincy.
