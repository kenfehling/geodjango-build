In comparison to other places in this area, Columbus is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Quincy however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus costs $105,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $17,300 (19%) higher.It is roughly $112,500 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Quincy it is about $11,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

Columbus's median monthly rent is $830. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $149 (21%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is about $232 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Quincy it is approximately $120 (16%) higher.
