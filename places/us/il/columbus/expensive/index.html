Compared to the surrounding areas, Columbus is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Quincy however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $105,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $17,300 (19%) higher.It is approximately $112,500 (51%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $11,300 (9%) lower compared to the city of Quincy.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbus is $830. It is about $149 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $232 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $120 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Quincy.
