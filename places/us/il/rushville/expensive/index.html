Compared to other places in this area, Rushville is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The median home in Rushville costs $71,100. It is about $7,420 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $146,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $54,600 (43%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In Rushville the median monthly rent is $673. It is roughly $19 (2%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $389 (36%) lower, and is about $133 (16%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
