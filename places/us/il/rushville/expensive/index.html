Home prices

The median home in Rushville is valued at $71,100. This is roughly $7,420 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $146,400 (67%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $54,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Rushville's median monthly rent is $673. It is about $19 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $389 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $133 (16%) lower.
