Home prices

The median home in Naperville costs $416,700. It is approximately $165,020 (65%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $199,200 (91%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $158,700 (61%) higher.

Rents

In Naperville the median monthly rent is $1,516. It is about $178 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $454 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $404 (36%) higher.
