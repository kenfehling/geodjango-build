Much like other places in this area, it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kingston costs $162,600. This is about $2,720 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $54,900 (25%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $95,400 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Kingston the median rent is $748 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $254 (25%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $314 (29%) lower, and it is about $364 (32%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
