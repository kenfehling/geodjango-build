Compared to the surrounding areas, Skokie is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Skokie is valued at $318,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $100,580 (23%) lower. It is roughly $101,300 (46%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $60,800 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Skokie's median rent is $1,271 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $273 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $209 (19%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $159 (14%) higher.
