Latham's median home cost is $91,300. This is about $9,460 (11%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $126,200 (58%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $70,500 (43%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median rent in Latham is $488 a month. This is roughly $125 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $574 (54%) lower, and is roughly $464 (48%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
