Home prices

The median home price in Kansas is $57,700. This is roughly $18,040 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $159,800 (73%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is roughly $104,100 (64%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kansas is $322. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $334 (50%) lower. It is roughly $740 (69%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is roughly $630 (66%) lower.
