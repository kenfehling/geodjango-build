Compared to other places in this area, Kansas is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Champaign, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kansas is valued at $57,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $18,040 (23%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $159,800 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign it is roughly $104,100 (64%) lower.

Rents

Kansas's median monthly rent is $322. Compared to other places in this area this is about $334 (50%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $740 (69%) lower, and it is approximately $630 (66%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
