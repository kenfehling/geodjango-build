In comparison to other places in this area, Ottawa is a little less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Ottawa's median home value is $128,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $12,140 (8%) lower. It is about $89,500 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $130,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

Ottawa's median monthly rent is $819. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $113 (12%) lower. It is about $243 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $293 (26%) lower.
