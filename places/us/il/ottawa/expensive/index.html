In comparison to other places in this area, Ottawa is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home cost in Ottawa is $128,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $12,140 (8%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $89,500 (41%) lower, and is roughly $130,000 (50%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ottawa is $819. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $113 (12%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $243 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $293 (26%) lower.
