Compared to nearby areas, Ottawa is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Ottawa is valued at $128,000. This is about $12,140 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $89,500 (41%) lower, and it is about $130,000 (50%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Ottawa the median monthly rent is $819. It is approximately $113 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $243 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $293 (26%) lower.
