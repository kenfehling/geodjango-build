In comparison to other places in this area, Ottawa is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Ottawa costs $128,000. This is approximately $12,140 (8%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $89,500 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $130,000 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ottawa is $819. This is approximately $113 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $243 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $293 (26%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
