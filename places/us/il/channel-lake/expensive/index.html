Compared to its nearby areas, Channel Lake is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Channel Lake is $173,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $15,200 (8%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $43,600 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $84,100 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Channel Lake is $1,106 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $39 (3%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $44 (4%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Chicago.
