Home prices

The median home in Park City costs $14,700. This is roughly $248,260 (94%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $202,800 (93%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $243,300 (94%) lower.

Rents

Park City's median monthly rent is $923. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $279 (23%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $139 (13%) lower, and is roughly $189 (16%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
