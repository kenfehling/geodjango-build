Much like its surrounding areas, it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Wilmington is $168,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $6,360 (3%) lower. It is about $49,000 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $89,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wilmington is $877. This is about $171 (16%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $185 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $235 (21%) lower.
