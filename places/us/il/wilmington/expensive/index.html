Similar to other places in this area, it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Wilmington's median home value is $168,500. This is about $6,360 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $49,000 (22%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $89,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Wilmington's median monthly rent is $877. It is approximately $171 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $185 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $235 (21%) lower.
