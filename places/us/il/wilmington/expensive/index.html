Home prices

The median home in Wilmington is valued at $168,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $6,360 (3%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $49,000 (22%) lower, and it is approximately $89,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington is $877 a month. This is roughly $171 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $185 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $235 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
