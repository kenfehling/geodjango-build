In comparison to nearby areas, Harrisburg is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Evansville.

Home prices

The median home in Harrisburg is valued at $69,800. This is roughly $8,460 (13%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $147,700 (67%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville it is approximately $24,400 (25%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Harrisburg is $570. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $22 (3%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $492 (46%) lower, and is approximately $204 (26%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.
