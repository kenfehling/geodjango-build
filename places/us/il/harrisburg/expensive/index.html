Compared to the surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Evansville, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Harrisburg is $69,800. This is roughly $8,460 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $147,700 (67%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $24,400 (25%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median monthly rent is $570. This is approximately $22 (3%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $492 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $204 (26%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.
