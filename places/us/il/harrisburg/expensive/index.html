Compared to its surrounding areas, Harrisburg is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Evansville however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Harrisburg is $69,800. This is roughly $8,460 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $147,700 (67%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $24,400 (25%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

In Harrisburg the median rent is $570 per month. This is roughly $22 (3%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $492 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville is roughly $204 (26%) lower.
