In comparison to the surrounding areas, Harrisburg is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Evansville.

Home prices

The median home in Harrisburg is valued at $69,800. It is approximately $8,460 (13%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $147,700 (67%) lower, and it is approximately $24,400 (25%) lower compared to the city of Evansville.

Rents

Harrisburg's median monthly rent is $570. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $22 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $492 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is roughly $204 (26%) lower.
