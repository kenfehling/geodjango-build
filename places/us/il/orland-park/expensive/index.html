Home prices

The price of the median home in Orland Park is $287,700. It is approximately $9,600 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $70,200 (32%) higher, and is approximately $29,700 (11%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Orland Park is $1,233. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $140 (10%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $171 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $121 (10%) higher.
