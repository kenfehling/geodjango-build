Home prices

Orland Park's median home cost is $287,700. It is approximately $9,600 (3%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $70,200 (32%) higher, and it is about $29,700 (11%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Orland Park's median monthly rent is $1,233. Compared to other places in this area this is about $140 (10%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $171 (16%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $121 (10%) higher.
