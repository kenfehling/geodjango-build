Home prices

The median home in Orland Park costs $287,700. It is roughly $9,600 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $70,200 (32%) higher, and it is approximately $29,700 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Orland Park the median rent is $1,233 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $140 (10%) lower. It is approximately $171 (16%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $121 (10%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
