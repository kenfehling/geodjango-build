Home prices

The price of the median home in Greenwood is $275,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $84,940 (44%) higher. It is about $57,500 (26%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $17,000 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Greenwood the median rent is $1,281 a month. It is approximately $161 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $219 (20%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $169 (15%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
