Compared to its surrounding areas, Manhattan is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Manhattan is valued at $241,400. This is about $30,780 (14%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $23,900 (10%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $16,600 (6%) lower.

Rents

Manhattan's median monthly rent is $1,321. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $280 (26%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $259 (24%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $209 (18%) higher.
