In comparison to its surrounding areas, Manhattan is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Manhattan is $241,400. This is about $30,780 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $23,900 (10%) higher, and is roughly $16,600 (6%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Manhattan the median monthly rent is $1,321. This is roughly $280 (26%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $259 (24%) higher, and is about $209 (18%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
