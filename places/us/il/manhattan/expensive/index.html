Home prices

Rents

In Manhattan the median rent is $1,321 per month. It is about $280 (26%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $259 (24%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $209 (18%) higher.
