Home prices

The median home price in Markham is $90,000. This is approximately $24,360 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $127,500 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $168,000 (65%) lower.

Rents

Markham's median rent is $1,261 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $106 (9%) higher.It is approximately $199 (18%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $149 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
