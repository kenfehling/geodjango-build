Compared to other places in this area, Allendale is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Evansville however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Allendale is $71,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $16,860 (30%) higher. It is about $145,900 (67%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Evansville is roughly $22,600 (23%) lower.

Rents

Allendale's median rent is $538 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $104 (16%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $524 (49%) lower, and it is about $236 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.
