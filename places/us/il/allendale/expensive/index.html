Home prices

The median home in Allendale is valued at $71,600. This is approximately $16,860 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $145,900 (67%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is about $22,600 (23%) lower.

Rents

In Allendale the median monthly rent is $538. This is approximately $104 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $524 (49%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville it is roughly $236 (30%) lower.
