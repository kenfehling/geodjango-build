Home prices

Lakemoor's median home cost is $182,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $15,260 (7%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $35,500 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $76,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lakemoor is $1,368 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $87 (6%) lower.It is about $306 (28%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $256 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
