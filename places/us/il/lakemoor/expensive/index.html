Home prices

Rents

Lakemoor's median rent is $1,368 a month. It is about $87 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $306 (28%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $256 (23%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
