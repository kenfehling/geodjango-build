In comparison to its surrounding areas, Sandwich is a little less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sandwich is valued at $171,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $20,780 (10%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $46,500 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $87,000 (33%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Sandwich's median rent is $1,051 per month. This is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $11 (1%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $61 (5%) lower.
