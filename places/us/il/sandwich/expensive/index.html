In comparison to other places in this area, Sandwich is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Sandwich's median home cost is $171,000. This is roughly $20,780 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $46,500 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $87,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

Sandwich's median monthly rent is $1,051. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $12 (1%) higher. It is about $11 (1%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $61 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
