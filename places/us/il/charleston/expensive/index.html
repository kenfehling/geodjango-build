Home prices

Charleston's median home value is $104,900. It is approximately $32,440 (44%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $112,600 (51%) lower, and it is approximately $56,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

In Charleston the median monthly rent is $723. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $75 (11%) higher.It is approximately $339 (31%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Champaign is approximately $229 (24%) lower.
