Home prices

The median home in Charleston costs $104,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $32,440 (44%) higher. It is approximately $112,600 (51%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is roughly $56,900 (35%) lower.

Rents

Charleston's median monthly rent is $723. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $75 (11%) higher. It is roughly $339 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is about $229 (24%) lower.
