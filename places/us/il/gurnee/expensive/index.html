Gurnee's median home value is $259,500. This is approximately $84,580 (48%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $42,000 (19%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Gurnee's median rent is $1,210 per month. This is approximately $51 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $148 (13%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $98 (8%) higher.
