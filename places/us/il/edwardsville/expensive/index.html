In comparison to the surrounding areas, Edwardsville is significantly more expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. It is a lot more expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Edwardsville costs $228,300. This is roughly $94,480 (70%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $10,800 (4%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $89,600 (64%) higher.

Rents

In Edwardsville the median rent is $971 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $115 (13%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $91 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $143 (17%) higher.
