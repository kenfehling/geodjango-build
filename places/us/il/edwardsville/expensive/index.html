Home prices

The price of the median home in Edwardsville is $228,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $94,480 (70%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $10,800 (4%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $89,600 (64%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edwardsville is $971. This is approximately $115 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $91 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $143 (17%) higher.
