Compared to other places in this area, Matteson is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Matteson's median home value is $165,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16,740 (9%) lower. It is roughly $52,400 (24%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $92,900 (36%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Matteson is $1,227 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $251 (16%) lower. It is roughly $165 (15%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $115 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
