Rents

In Matteson the median rent is $1,227 a month. It is roughly $251 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $165 (15%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $115 (10%) higher.
