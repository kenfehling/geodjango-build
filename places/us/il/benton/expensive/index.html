Compared to the surrounding areas, Benton is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Evansville.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Benton is $72,300. It is approximately $11,300 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $145,200 (66%) lower, and it is approximately $21,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.

Rents

Benton's median monthly rent is $667. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $395 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Evansville is roughly $107 (13%) lower.
