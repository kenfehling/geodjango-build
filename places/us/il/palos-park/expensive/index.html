Compared to other places in this area, Palos Park is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Palos Park costs $418,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $126,560 (43%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $201,100 (92%) higher, and is about $160,600 (62%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Palos Park is $1,668. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $508 (43%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $606 (57%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $556 (50%) higher.
