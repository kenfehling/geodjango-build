Home prices

The median home price in South Chicago Heights is $103,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $2,060 (1%) lower. It is about $114,300 (52%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $154,800 (60%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

South Chicago Heights's median monthly rent is $1,046. It is about $24 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $16 (1%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $66 (5%) lower.
