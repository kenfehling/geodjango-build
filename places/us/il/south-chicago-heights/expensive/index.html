Home prices

Rents

In South Chicago Heights the median monthly rent is $1,046. It is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $16 (1%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $66 (5%) lower.
