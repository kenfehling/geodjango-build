Compared to the surrounding areas, Nashville is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Nashville is $128,700. It is approximately $44,060 (52%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $88,800 (40%) lower, and is approximately $10,000 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nashville is $729. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $37 (4%) lower.It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $99 (11%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
