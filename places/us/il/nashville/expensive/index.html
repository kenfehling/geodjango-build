Compared to its surrounding areas, Nashville is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nashville costs $128,700. This is approximately $44,060 (52%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $88,800 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $10,000 (7%) lower.

Rents

Nashville's median monthly rent is $729. This is roughly $37 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $333 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $99 (11%) lower.
