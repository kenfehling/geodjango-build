Compared to its nearby areas, Nashville is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home value in Nashville is $128,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $44,060 (52%) higher. It is approximately $88,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $10,000 (7%) lower.

Rents

Nashville's median monthly rent is $729. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $37 (4%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $333 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $99 (11%) lower.
