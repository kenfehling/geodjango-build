In comparison to the surrounding areas, Nashville is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Nashville's median home cost is $128,700. This is about $44,060 (52%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $88,800 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $10,000 (7%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Nashville is $729. This is roughly $37 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $333 (31%) lower, and it is approximately $99 (11%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
