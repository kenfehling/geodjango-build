In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lake Zurich is significantly less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Lake Zurich is $337,300. This is about $171,820 (33%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $119,800 (55%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $79,300 (30%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Lake Zurich the median rent is $1,562 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $354 (18%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $500 (47%) higher, and it is about $450 (40%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
