In comparison to nearby areas, Lake Zurich is a lot less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a little more expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lake Zurich is $337,300. This is approximately $171,820 (33%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $119,800 (55%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $79,300 (30%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Lake Zurich's median rent is $1,562 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $354 (18%) lower.It is about $500 (47%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $450 (40%) higher.
