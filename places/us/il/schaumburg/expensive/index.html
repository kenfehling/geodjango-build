Home prices

The value of the median home in Schaumburg is $251,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $17,220 (7%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $33,600 (15%) higher, and is roughly $6,900 (2%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Schaumburg is $1,440. Compared to nearby areas this is is very similar.It is approximately $378 (35%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $328 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
