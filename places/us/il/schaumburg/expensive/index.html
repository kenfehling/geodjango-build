Home prices

The median home in Schaumburg costs $251,100. This is approximately $17,220 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $33,600 (15%) higher, and it is approximately $6,900 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Schaumburg the median rent is $1,440 per month. It is is very similar compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $378 (35%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $328 (29%) higher.
