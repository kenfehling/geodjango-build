In comparison to other places in this area, Schaumburg is a little more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Schaumburg is $251,100. It is approximately $17,220 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $33,600 (15%) higher, and is roughly $6,900 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Schaumburg's median rent is $1,440 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is roughly $378 (35%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $328 (29%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
