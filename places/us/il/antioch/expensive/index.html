Home prices

The cost of the median home in Antioch is $221,100. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $18,160 (8%) higher. It is roughly $3,600 (1%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $36,900 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Antioch the median rent is $1,003 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $302 (23%) lower. It is about $59 (5%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $109 (9%) lower.
