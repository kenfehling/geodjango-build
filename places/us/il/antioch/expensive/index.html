Compared to other places in this area, Antioch is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Antioch costs $221,100. This is about $18,160 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $3,600 (1%) higher, and is roughly $36,900 (14%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Antioch the median rent is $1,003 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $302 (23%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $59 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $109 (9%) lower.
