In comparison to its surrounding areas, Phoenix is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Phoenix is $75,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $18,320 (19%) lower. It is about $142,400 (65%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $182,900 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Phoenix is $907 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $236 (20%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $155 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $205 (18%) lower.
