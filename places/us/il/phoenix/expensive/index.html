Compared to its surrounding areas, Phoenix is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Phoenix is valued at $75,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $18,320 (19%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $142,400 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $182,900 (70%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Phoenix is $907 per month. It is about $236 (20%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $155 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $205 (18%) lower.
