In comparison to its nearby areas, Phoenix is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Phoenix costs $75,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $18,320 (19%) lower. It is approximately $142,400 (65%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $182,900 (70%) lower.

Rents

Phoenix's median monthly rent is $907. It is roughly $236 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $155 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $205 (18%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
