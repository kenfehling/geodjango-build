Home prices

The median home value in Arlington is $86,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $2,240 (2%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $130,800 (60%) lower, and compared to the city of Rockford is approximately $4,900 (5%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Arlington is $1,188 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $355 (42%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $126 (11%) higher, and is approximately $416 (53%) higher in comparison to the city of Rockford.
