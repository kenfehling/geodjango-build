Compared to the surrounding areas, Park Ridge is substantially more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home cost in Park Ridge is $437,000. This is about $133,760 (44%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $219,500 (100%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $179,000 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Park Ridge the median rent is $1,433 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $182 (14%) higher. It is roughly $371 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $321 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
