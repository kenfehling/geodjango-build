The median home in Park Ridge costs $437,000. This is roughly $133,760 (44%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $219,500 (100%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $179,000 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Park Ridge's median monthly rent is $1,433. This is approximately $182 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $371 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $321 (28%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
