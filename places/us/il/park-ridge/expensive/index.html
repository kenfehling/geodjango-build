Compared to the surrounding areas, Park Ridge is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Park Ridge costs $437,000. This is approximately $133,760 (44%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $219,500 (100%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $179,000 (69%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Park Ridge is $1,433 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $182 (14%) higher. It is about $371 (34%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $321 (28%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
