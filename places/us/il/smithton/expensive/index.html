Home prices

The median home in Smithton is valued at $170,900. It is roughly $35,360 (26%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $46,600 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $32,200 (23%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Smithton is $748 a month. This is roughly $26 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $314 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $80 (9%) lower.
