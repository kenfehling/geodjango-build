Home prices

The median home value in Morton is $199,400. This is about $75,440 (60%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $18,100 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $37,600 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Morton is $878. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $85 (10%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $184 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is approximately $74 (7%) lower.
