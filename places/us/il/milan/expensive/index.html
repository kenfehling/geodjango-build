Home prices

The median home in Milan is valued at $108,300. This is approximately $20,760 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $109,200 (50%) lower, and it is about $17,400 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Milan is $758. It is about $26 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $304 (28%) lower, and is approximately $48 (5%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
