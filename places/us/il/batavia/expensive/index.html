In comparison to the surrounding areas, Batavia is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Batavia is valued at $304,400. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $41,940 (15%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $86,900 (39%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $46,400 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Batavia the median rent is $1,187 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $69 (5%) lower. It is roughly $125 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $75 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
