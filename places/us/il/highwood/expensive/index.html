Home prices

The median home in Highwood costs $354,800. This is roughly $371,520 (51%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $137,300 (63%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $96,800 (37%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Highwood's median monthly rent is $1,287. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $526 (29%) lower. It is approximately $225 (21%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $175 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
