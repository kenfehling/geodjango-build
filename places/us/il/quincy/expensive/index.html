Home prices

The median home value in Quincy is $116,300. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $14,800 (14%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $101,200 (46%) lower, and is about $75,500 (39%) lower compared to the city of Columbia.

Rents

In Quincy the median rent is $710 a month. This is roughly $79 (12%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $352 (33%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Columbia it is roughly $177 (19%) lower.
