Home prices

The median home cost in Jerome is $105,900. Compared to nearby areas this is about $10,100 (8%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $111,600 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $32,800 (23%) lower.

Rents

Jerome's median monthly rent is $915. It is about $63 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $87 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
