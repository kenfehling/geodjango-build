Compared to its surrounding areas, Waterloo is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Waterloo's median home price is $192,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $28,020 (17%) higher.It is approximately $25,400 (11%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $53,400 (38%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Waterloo's median monthly rent is $830. Compared to other places in this area it is about $18 (2%) lower.It is approximately $232 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
