In comparison to the surrounding areas, Waterloo is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Waterloo is $192,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $28,020 (17%) higher. It is about $25,400 (11%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $53,400 (38%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Waterloo is $830 a month. It is approximately $18 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $232 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
