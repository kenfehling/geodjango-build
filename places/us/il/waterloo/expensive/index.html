Compared to other places in this area, Waterloo is a bit more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Waterloo costs $192,100. This is about $28,020 (17%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $25,400 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $53,400 (38%) higher.

Rents

Waterloo's median rent is $830 per month. This is approximately $18 (2%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $232 (21%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
