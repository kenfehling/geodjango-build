Home prices

The price of the median home in Waterloo is $192,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $28,020 (17%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $25,400 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $53,400 (38%) higher.

Rents

In Waterloo the median rent is $830 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $18 (2%) lower.It is approximately $232 (21%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
