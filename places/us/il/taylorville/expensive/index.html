Home prices

The median home in Taylorville costs $88,900. It is roughly $13,060 (17%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $128,600 (59%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $49,800 (35%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Taylorville is $672 per month. It is roughly $35 (4%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $390 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $156 (18%) lower.
