Compared to its surrounding areas, Louisville is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Evansville however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Louisville costs $71,300. It is approximately $6,080 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $146,200 (67%) lower, and is about $22,900 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Evansville.

Rents

Louisville's median rent is $536 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is is very similar.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $526 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is about $238 (30%) lower.
