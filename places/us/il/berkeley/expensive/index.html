In comparison to other places in this area, Berkeley is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Berkeley costs $178,700. This is roughly $32,480 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $38,800 (17%) lower, and is about $79,300 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Berkeley's median rent is $943 per month. It is roughly $218 (18%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $119 (11%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $169 (15%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
