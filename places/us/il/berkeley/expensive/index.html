Compared to nearby areas, Berkeley is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Berkeley costs $178,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $32,480 (15%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $38,800 (17%) lower, and is about $79,300 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Berkeley the median monthly rent is $943. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $218 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $119 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $169 (15%) lower.
