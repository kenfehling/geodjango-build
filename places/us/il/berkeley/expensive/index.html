In comparison to other places in this area, Berkeley is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Berkeley is valued at $178,700. Compared to other places in this area it is about $32,480 (15%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $38,800 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $79,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

Berkeley's median monthly rent is $943. It is approximately $218 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $119 (11%) lower, and it is approximately $169 (15%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
