In comparison to the surrounding areas, Berkeley is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Berkeley is $178,700. It is roughly $32,480 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $38,800 (17%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $79,300 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Berkeley is $943 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $218 (18%) lower.It is about $119 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $169 (15%) lower.
