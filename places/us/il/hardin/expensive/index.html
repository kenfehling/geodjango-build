Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hardin is $75,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $10,080 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $141,800 (65%) lower, and it is about $63,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hardin is $386. It is approximately $271 (41%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $676 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $442 (53%) lower.
