Compared to nearby areas, Ashland is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ashland costs $98,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $11,920 (13%) higher.It is about $118,700 (54%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $39,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Ashland's median monthly rent is $789. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $133 (20%) higher.It is approximately $273 (25%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $39 (4%) lower.
