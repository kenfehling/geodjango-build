Home prices

The median home cost in Ashland is $98,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $11,920 (13%) higher.It is roughly $118,700 (54%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $39,900 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Ashland the median rent is $789 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $133 (20%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $273 (25%) lower, and it is roughly $39 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
