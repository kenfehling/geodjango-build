In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dalzell is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's substantially more expensive compared to the city of Rockford.

Home prices

Dalzell's median home price is $128,400. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $23,740 (22%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $89,100 (40%) lower, and is approximately $36,800 (40%) higher in comparison to the city of Rockford.

Rents

The median rent in Dalzell is $808 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $78 (10%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $254 (23%) lower, and compared to the city of Rockford it is roughly $36 (4%) higher.
