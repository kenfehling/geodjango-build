Home prices

Springfield's median home cost is $133,400. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $19,220 (16%) higher.It is roughly $84,100 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $5,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Springfield is $805. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $165 (16%) lower.It is roughly $257 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $23 (2%) lower.
