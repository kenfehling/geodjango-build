In comparison to other places in this area, Springfield is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it is close to average.

Home prices

Springfield's median home price is $133,400. This is roughly $19,220 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $84,100 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $5,300 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Springfield's median monthly rent is $805. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $165 (16%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $257 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $23 (2%) lower.
