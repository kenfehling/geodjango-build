Compared to its nearby areas, Springfield is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Springfield is $133,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $19,220 (16%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $84,100 (38%) lower, and is about $5,300 (3%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Springfield is $805 a month. This is about $165 (16%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $257 (24%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $23 (2%) lower.
