In comparison to other places in this area, Springfield is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The median home in Springfield is valued at $133,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $19,220 (16%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $84,100 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $5,300 (3%) lower.

Rents

Springfield's median monthly rent is $805. It is roughly $165 (16%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $257 (24%) lower, and it is approximately $23 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
