Home prices

The median home cost in Montgomery is $221,500. This is approximately $6,120 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $4,000 (1%) higher, and is roughly $36,500 (14%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomery is $1,177 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $164 (12%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $115 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $65 (5%) higher.
