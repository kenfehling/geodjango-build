Home prices

The median home in Montgomery is valued at $221,500. It is approximately $6,120 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $4,000 (1%) higher, and it is roughly $36,500 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Montgomery the median monthly rent is $1,177. It is about $164 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is about $115 (10%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $65 (5%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
