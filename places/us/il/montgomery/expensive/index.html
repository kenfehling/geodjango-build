Home prices

The median home price in Montgomery is $221,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $6,120 (2%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $4,000 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $36,500 (14%) lower.

Rents

Montgomery's median rent is $1,177 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $164 (12%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $115 (10%) higher, and it is approximately $65 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
