Home prices

The price of the median home in Decatur is $83,000. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $58,840 (41%) lower. It is roughly $134,500 (61%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is roughly $78,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

Decatur's median rent is $683 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $190 (21%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $379 (35%) lower, and is roughly $269 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
