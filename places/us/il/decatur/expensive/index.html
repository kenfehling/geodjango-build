Home prices

The median home cost in Decatur is $83,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $58,840 (41%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $134,500 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is about $78,800 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Decatur is $683. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $190 (21%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $379 (35%) lower, and it is approximately $269 (28%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
