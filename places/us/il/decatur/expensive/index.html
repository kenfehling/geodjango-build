Home prices

Decatur's median home price is $83,000. This is roughly $58,840 (41%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $134,500 (61%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $78,800 (48%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median rent in Decatur is $683 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $190 (21%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $379 (35%) lower, and is about $269 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
