Home prices

The median home in Marengo costs $154,200. This is about $38,740 (20%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $63,300 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $103,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Marengo is $912 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $145 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $150 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $200 (17%) lower.
