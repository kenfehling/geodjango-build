In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marengo is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Marengo is $154,200. This is roughly $38,740 (20%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $63,300 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $103,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Marengo the median monthly rent is $912. It is approximately $145 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $150 (14%) lower, and is approximately $200 (17%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
