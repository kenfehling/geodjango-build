Compared to other places in this area, Marengo is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Marengo costs $154,200. This is roughly $38,740 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $63,300 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $103,800 (40%) lower.

Rents

In Marengo the median rent is $912 per month. It is about $145 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $150 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $200 (17%) lower.
