In comparison to its surrounding areas, Marengo is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Marengo is $154,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $38,740 (20%) lower. It is approximately $63,300 (29%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $103,800 (40%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Marengo the median rent is $912 per month. It is about $145 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $150 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $200 (17%) lower.
