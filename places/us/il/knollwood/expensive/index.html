Home prices

Knollwood's median home price is $324,100. It is approximately $200,860 (38%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $106,600 (49%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $66,100 (25%) higher.

Rents

In Knollwood the median rent is $1,545 a month. It is is very similar compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $483 (45%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $433 (38%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
