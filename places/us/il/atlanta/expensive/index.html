Home prices

The value of the median home in Atlanta is $95,300. This is roughly $9,700 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $122,200 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $66,500 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atlanta is $745. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $64 (7%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $317 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign it is about $207 (21%) lower.
