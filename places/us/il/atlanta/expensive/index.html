In comparison to other places in this area, Atlanta is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Champaign.

Home prices

Atlanta's median home cost is $95,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $9,700 (11%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $122,200 (56%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $66,500 (41%) lower.

Rents

Atlanta's median monthly rent is $745. This is about $64 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $317 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign is roughly $207 (21%) lower.
