Home prices

The median home in Atlanta costs $95,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $9,700 (11%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $122,200 (56%) lower, and is approximately $66,500 (41%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Atlanta is $745. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $64 (7%) lower. It is about $317 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Champaign it is roughly $207 (21%) lower.
