Home prices

Rents

In Wood River the median monthly rent is $779. This is about $22 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $283 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $49 (5%) lower.
