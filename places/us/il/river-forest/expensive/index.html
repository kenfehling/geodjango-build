Home prices

River Forest's median home price is $596,900. This is roughly $352,360 (144%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $379,400 (174%) higher, and is about $338,900 (131%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in River Forest is $1,143. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $76 (7%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $81 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $31 (2%) higher.
