Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Hillside is $1,181 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $59 (5%) higher.It is roughly $119 (11%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $69 (6%) higher.
