Home prices

The price of the median home in Hillside is $171,900. This is roughly $2,220 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $45,600 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $86,100 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Hillside's median rent is $1,181 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $59 (5%) higher. It is approximately $119 (11%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $69 (6%) higher.
