Home prices

The value of the median home in Hillside is $171,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $2,220 (1%) higher. It is roughly $45,600 (20%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $86,100 (33%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Hillside the median rent is $1,181 a month. This is about $59 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $119 (11%) higher, and it is approximately $69 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
