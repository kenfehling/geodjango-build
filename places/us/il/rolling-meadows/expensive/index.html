In comparison to other places in this area, Rolling Meadows is a lot less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Rolling Meadows is $241,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $129,340 (34%) lower. It is about $23,700 (10%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $16,800 (6%) lower.

Rents

Rolling Meadows's median monthly rent is $1,378. It is about $300 (17%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $316 (29%) higher, and it is roughly $266 (23%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
