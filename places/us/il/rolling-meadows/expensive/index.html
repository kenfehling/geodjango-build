Home prices

The median home in Rolling Meadows is valued at $241,200. It is roughly $129,340 (34%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $23,700 (10%) higher, and it is roughly $16,800 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Rolling Meadows the median rent is $1,378 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $300 (17%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $316 (29%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $266 (23%) higher.
