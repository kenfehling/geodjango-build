Compared to its nearby areas, Rolling Meadows is substantially less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rolling Meadows costs $241,200. It is about $129,340 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $23,700 (10%) higher, and is roughly $16,800 (6%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Rolling Meadows is $1,378 per month. This is roughly $300 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $316 (29%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $266 (23%) higher.
