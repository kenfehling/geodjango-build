Home prices

The median home in Madison is valued at $44,700. This is about $18,840 (29%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $172,800 (79%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $94,000 (67%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Madison is $738 a month. It is roughly $46 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $324 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $90 (10%) lower.
