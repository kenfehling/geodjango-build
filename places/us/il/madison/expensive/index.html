Home prices

Madison's median home price is $44,700. This is roughly $18,840 (29%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $172,800 (79%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $94,000 (67%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Madison's median monthly rent is $738. This is roughly $46 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $324 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $90 (10%) lower.
