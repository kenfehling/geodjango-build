Home prices

The median home in Andover costs $113,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $10,820 (10%) higher. It is roughly $104,400 (48%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $12,600 (10%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Andover is $732 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $60 (8%) higher. It is roughly $330 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $74 (9%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
