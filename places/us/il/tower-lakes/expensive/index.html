Home prices

Tower Lakes's median home value is $481,000. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $215,560 (81%) higher.It is roughly $263,500 (121%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $223,000 (86%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Tower Lakes is $2,156 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $811 (60%) higher.It is roughly $1,094 (103%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $1,044 (93%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
