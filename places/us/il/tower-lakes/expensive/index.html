Home prices

The median home in Tower Lakes is valued at $481,000. This is about $215,560 (81%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $263,500 (121%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $223,000 (86%) higher.

Rents

In Tower Lakes the median monthly rent is $2,156. It is about $811 (60%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $1,094 (103%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $1,044 (93%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
