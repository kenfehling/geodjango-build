In comparison to its surrounding areas, Tower Lakes is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Tower Lakes is $481,000. This is about $215,560 (81%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $263,500 (121%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $223,000 (86%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Tower Lakes is $2,156. This is approximately $811 (60%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $1,094 (103%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $1,044 (93%) higher.
