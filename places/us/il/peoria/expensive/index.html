Home prices

Peoria's median home price is $125,700. It is approximately $34,740 (38%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $91,800 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Springfield it is approximately $7,700 (5%) lower.

Rents

Peoria's median monthly rent is $806. This is about $38 (4%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $256 (24%) lower, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Springfield.
