Home prices

The median home price in Peoria is $125,700. This is approximately $34,740 (38%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $91,800 (42%) lower, and is about $7,700 (5%) lower compared to the city of Springfield.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Peoria is $806. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $38 (4%) higher. It is approximately $256 (24%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Springfield.
