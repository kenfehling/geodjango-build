Home prices

The median home in Indian Creek costs $397,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $41,140 (9%) lower. It is roughly $179,700 (82%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $139,200 (53%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Indian Creek is $2,250 per month. It is approximately $174 (8%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,188 (111%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $1,138 (102%) higher.
