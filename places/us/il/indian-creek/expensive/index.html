Rents

The median rent in Indian Creek is $2,250 a month. It is about $174 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $1,188 (111%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $1,138 (102%) higher.
