Home prices

Rents

In Davis the median monthly rent is $850. It is roughly $76 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and it is roughly $268 (23%) lower compared to the city of Madison.
