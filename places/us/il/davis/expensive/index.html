Compared to the surrounding areas, Davis is a bit less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Madison, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Davis is $105,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $11,360 (9%) lower. It is roughly $112,000 (51%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $140,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

Davis's median monthly rent is $850. This is roughly $76 (9%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison is approximately $268 (23%) lower.
