Home prices

The median home in Davis is valued at $105,500. It is about $11,360 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $112,000 (51%) lower, and it is about $140,800 (57%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Davis is $850 a month. This is roughly $76 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $212 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is roughly $268 (23%) lower.
