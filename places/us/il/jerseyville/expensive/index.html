Home prices

The median home in Jerseyville is valued at $114,000. This is about $13,820 (13%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $103,500 (47%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $24,700 (17%) lower.

Rents

Jerseyville's median rent is $626 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $110 (14%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $436 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $202 (24%) lower.
