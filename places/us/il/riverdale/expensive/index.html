Compared to nearby areas, Riverdale is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home value in Riverdale is $79,000. It is approximately $14,640 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $138,500 (63%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $179,000 (69%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Riverdale's median rent is $980 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $19 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $82 (7%) lower, and is about $132 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
