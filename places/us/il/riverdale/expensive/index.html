Home prices

Riverdale's median home value is $79,000. This is about $14,640 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $138,500 (63%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $179,000 (69%) lower.

Rents

Riverdale's median monthly rent is $980. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $19 (1%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $82 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $132 (11%) lower.
