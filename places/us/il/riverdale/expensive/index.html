Home prices

The median home price in Riverdale is $79,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $14,640 (15%) lower. It is roughly $138,500 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $179,000 (69%) lower.

Rents

In Riverdale the median rent is $980 a month. This is about $19 (1%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $82 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $132 (11%) lower.
