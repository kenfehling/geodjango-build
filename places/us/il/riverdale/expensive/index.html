Home prices

The cost of the median home in Riverdale is $79,000. This is roughly $14,640 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $138,500 (63%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $179,000 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Riverdale is $980 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $19 (1%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $82 (7%) lower, and is about $132 (11%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
