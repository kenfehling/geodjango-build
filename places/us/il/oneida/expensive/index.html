Home prices

The median home in Oneida is valued at $81,500. It is roughly $7,540 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $136,000 (62%) lower, and is approximately $44,200 (35%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Oneida's median monthly rent is $833. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $216 (34%) higher. It is roughly $229 (21%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $27 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.
