Home prices

The median home value in Lewistown is $81,600. It is about $17,060 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $135,900 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria it is approximately $44,100 (35%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lewistown is $570 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $62 (9%) lower. It is approximately $492 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $236 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
