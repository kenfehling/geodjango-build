In comparison to its nearby areas, DeKalb is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

DeKalb's median home value is $157,900. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $22,100 (12%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $59,600 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $100,100 (38%) lower.

Rents

DeKalb's median rent is $903 a month. It is about $360 (28%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $159 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $209 (18%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
