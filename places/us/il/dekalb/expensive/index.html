In comparison to its surrounding areas, DeKalb is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in DeKalb costs $157,900. It is approximately $22,100 (12%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $59,600 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $100,100 (38%) lower.

Rents

DeKalb's median rent is $903 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $360 (28%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $159 (14%) lower, and is approximately $209 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
