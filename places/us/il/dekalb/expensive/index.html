In comparison to the surrounding areas, DeKalb is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in DeKalb is valued at $157,900. This is approximately $22,100 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $59,600 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $100,100 (38%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In DeKalb the median rent is $903 per month. This is roughly $360 (28%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $159 (14%) lower, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $209 (18%) lower.
