Compared to its surrounding areas, DeKalb is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

DeKalb's median home price is $157,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $22,100 (12%) lower.It is approximately $59,600 (27%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $100,100 (38%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in DeKalb is $903 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $360 (28%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $159 (14%) lower, and it is approximately $209 (18%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
