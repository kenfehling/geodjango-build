In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mundelein is substantially less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Mundelein is $237,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $247,680 (51%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $20,000 (9%) higher, and it is about $20,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Mundelein is $1,418 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $506 (26%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $356 (33%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $306 (27%) higher.
