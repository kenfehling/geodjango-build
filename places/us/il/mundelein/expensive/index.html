In comparison to its surrounding areas, Mundelein is a lot less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Mundelein is valued at $237,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $247,680 (51%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $20,000 (9%) higher, and it is approximately $20,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Mundelein the median monthly rent is $1,418. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $506 (26%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $356 (33%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $306 (27%) higher.
