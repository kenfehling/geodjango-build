Compared to other places in this area, Mundelein is a lot less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Mundelein is valued at $237,500. This is about $247,680 (51%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $20,000 (9%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $20,500 (7%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Mundelein the median monthly rent is $1,418. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $506 (26%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $356 (33%) higher, and it is about $306 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
