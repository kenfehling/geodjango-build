Home prices

The median home in Malden is valued at $78,500. It is roughly $8,620 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $139,000 (63%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $13,100 (14%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.

Rents

Malden's median rent is $845 a month. This is about $37 (4%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $217 (20%) lower, and is approximately $73 (9%) higher compared to the city of Rockford.
