Compared to other places in this area, Sheridan is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sheridan is valued at $148,600. This is about $26,900 (15%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $68,900 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $109,400 (42%) lower.

Rents

In Sheridan the median monthly rent is $1,008. Compared to nearby areas it is about $60 (6%) higher. It is approximately $54 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $104 (9%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
