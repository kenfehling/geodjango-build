Home prices

The median home in Sheridan is valued at $148,600. This is about $26,900 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $68,900 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $109,400 (42%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Sheridan the median monthly rent is $1,008. This is approximately $60 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $54 (5%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $104 (9%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
