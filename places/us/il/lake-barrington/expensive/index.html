Compared to nearby areas, Lake Barrington is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Barrington is $379,700. This is roughly $59,580 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $162,200 (74%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $121,700 (47%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Barrington is $1,389. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $150 (9%) lower. It is roughly $327 (30%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $277 (24%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
