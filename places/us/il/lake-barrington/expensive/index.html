Home prices

The median home in Lake Barrington is valued at $379,700. It is roughly $59,580 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $162,200 (74%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is about $121,700 (47%) higher.

Rents

In Lake Barrington the median rent is $1,389 per month. It is approximately $150 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $327 (30%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $277 (24%) higher.
