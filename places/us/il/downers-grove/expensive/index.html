Compared to its surrounding areas, Downers Grove is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Downers Grove is $353,100. It is roughly $122,880 (25%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $135,600 (62%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $95,100 (36%) higher.

Rents

In Downers Grove the median rent is $1,315 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $20 (1%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $253 (23%) higher, and it is about $203 (18%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
