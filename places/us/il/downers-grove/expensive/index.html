Compared to nearby areas, Downers Grove is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Downers Grove's median home cost is $353,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $122,880 (25%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $135,600 (62%) higher, and is roughly $95,100 (36%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Downers Grove's median monthly rent is $1,315. This is approximately $20 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $253 (23%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $203 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
