In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chillicothe is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Rockford, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chillicothe costs $121,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $15,000 (14%) higher.It is roughly $95,600 (43%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Rockford it is roughly $30,300 (33%) higher.

Rents

In Chillicothe the median rent is $724 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $70 (8%) lower.It is approximately $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rockford is about $48 (6%) lower.
