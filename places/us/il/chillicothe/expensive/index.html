Compared to other places in this area, Chillicothe is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Rockford however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Chillicothe's median home price is $121,900. This is approximately $15,000 (14%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $95,600 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Rockford is approximately $30,300 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chillicothe is $724. This is approximately $70 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $338 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Rockford is approximately $48 (6%) lower.
