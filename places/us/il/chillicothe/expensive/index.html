In comparison to the surrounding areas, Chillicothe is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Rockford, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Chillicothe is $121,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $15,000 (14%) higher. It is about $95,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $30,300 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Rockford.

Rents

Chillicothe's median monthly rent is $724. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $70 (8%) lower. It is approximately $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $48 (6%) lower compared to the city of Rockford.
