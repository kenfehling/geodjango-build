Compared to the surrounding areas, Chillicothe is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Rockford, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chillicothe costs $121,900. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $15,000 (14%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $95,600 (43%) lower, and it is about $30,300 (33%) higher compared to the city of Rockford.

Rents

The median rent in Chillicothe is $724 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $70 (8%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $338 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rockford it is roughly $48 (6%) lower.
