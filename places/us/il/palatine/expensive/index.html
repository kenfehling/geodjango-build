Home prices

The median home cost in Palatine is $284,400. This is approximately $168,480 (37%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $66,900 (30%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $26,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Palatine is $1,200 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $557 (31%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $138 (12%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $88 (7%) higher.
