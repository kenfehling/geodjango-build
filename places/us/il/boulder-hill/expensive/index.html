Home prices

The median home in Boulder Hill is valued at $161,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $69,160 (29%) lower.It is about $56,100 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $96,600 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Boulder Hill is $1,588. Compared to other places in this area it is about $320 (25%) higher.It is roughly $526 (49%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is approximately $476 (42%) higher.
