In comparison to its surrounding areas, Knoxville is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Peoria, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Knoxville is $106,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $24,660 (30%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $111,400 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is approximately $19,600 (15%) lower.

Rents

Knoxville's median monthly rent is $649. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $33 (5%) higher. It is about $413 (38%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $157 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
