In comparison to nearby areas, Knoxville is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Peoria however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Knoxville is $106,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $24,660 (30%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $111,400 (51%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is approximately $19,600 (15%) lower.

Rents

Knoxville's median monthly rent is $649. This is roughly $33 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $413 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is about $157 (19%) lower.
