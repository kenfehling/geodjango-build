In comparison to the surrounding areas, Knoxville is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Peoria, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Knoxville is $106,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $24,660 (30%) higher.It is approximately $111,400 (51%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $19,600 (15%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Knoxville is $649. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $33 (5%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $413 (38%) lower, and is approximately $157 (19%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
