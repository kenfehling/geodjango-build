Compared to the surrounding areas, Knoxville is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Peoria, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Knoxville's median home cost is $106,100. This is roughly $24,660 (30%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $111,400 (51%) lower, and it is roughly $19,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Knoxville is $649. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $33 (5%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $413 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is approximately $157 (19%) lower.
