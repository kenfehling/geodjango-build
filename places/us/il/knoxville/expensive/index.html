Compared to its surrounding areas, Knoxville is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Peoria however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Knoxville is $106,100. It is about $24,660 (30%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $111,400 (51%) lower, and is roughly $19,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Knoxville's median monthly rent is $649. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $33 (5%) higher.It is roughly $413 (38%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Peoria is roughly $157 (19%) lower.
