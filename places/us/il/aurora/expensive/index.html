Home prices

The median home in Aurora costs $184,400. This is about $85,760 (31%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $33,100 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $73,600 (28%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Aurora is $1,228 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $100 (7%) lower. It is approximately $166 (15%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $116 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
