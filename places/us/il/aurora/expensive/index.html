Home prices

The value of the median home in Aurora is $184,400. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $85,760 (31%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $33,100 (15%) lower, and it is approximately $73,600 (28%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Aurora is $1,228 a month. This is roughly $100 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $166 (15%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $116 (10%) higher.
