Compared to its surrounding areas, Hamilton is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Iowa City.

Home prices

Hamilton's median home value is $91,700. It is about $21,600 (30%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $125,800 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $123,400 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Iowa City.

Rents

The median rent in Hamilton is $655 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $15 (2%) higher.It is roughly $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Iowa City is roughly $312 (32%) lower.
