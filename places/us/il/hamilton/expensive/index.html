Compared to nearby areas, Hamilton is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Iowa City.

Home prices

The median home in Hamilton costs $91,700. It is approximately $21,600 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $125,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Iowa City it is roughly $123,400 (57%) lower.

Rents

Hamilton's median monthly rent is $655. It is approximately $15 (2%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $407 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Iowa City is approximately $312 (32%) lower.
