Home prices

The price of the median home in Delavan is $110,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $20,900 (23%) higher. It is roughly $107,200 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $51,500 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Delavan is $743. Compared to other places in this area it is about $12 (1%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $319 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign is roughly $209 (21%) lower.
