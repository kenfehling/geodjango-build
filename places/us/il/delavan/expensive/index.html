Home prices

Delavan's median home cost is $110,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $20,900 (23%) higher. It is about $107,200 (49%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $51,500 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Delavan's median rent is $743 a month. This is about $12 (1%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $319 (30%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is roughly $209 (21%) lower.
