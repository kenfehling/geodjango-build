Rents

The median monthly rent in Mahomet is $939. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $148 (18%) higher.It is roughly $123 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $13 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
