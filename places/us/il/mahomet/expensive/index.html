Home prices

The median home in Mahomet is valued at $240,300. This is about $125,180 (108%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $22,800 (10%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $78,500 (48%) higher compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mahomet is $939. This is approximately $148 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $123 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
