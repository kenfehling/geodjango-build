Home prices

The median home in Cambridge costs $102,600. This is roughly $12,100 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $114,900 (52%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $23,100 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Cambridge is $647 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $73 (10%) lower. It is about $415 (39%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $159 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
