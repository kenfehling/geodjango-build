Home prices

The median home in Marion is valued at $115,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $15,840 (15%) higher.It is about $102,000 (46%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $2,000 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Carbondale.

Rents

Marion's median monthly rent is $740. It is about $69 (8%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $322 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $26 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Carbondale.
