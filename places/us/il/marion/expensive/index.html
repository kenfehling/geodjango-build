In comparison to nearby areas, Marion is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Carbondale, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Marion is $115,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $15,840 (15%) higher. It is roughly $102,000 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Carbondale is approximately $2,000 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Marion the median rent is $740 per month. This is approximately $69 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $322 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $26 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Carbondale.
