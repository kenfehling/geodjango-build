Home prices

The median home cost in Marion is $115,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $15,840 (15%) higher.It is about $102,000 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $2,000 (1%) lower compared to the city of Carbondale.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Marion is $740. It is approximately $69 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $322 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Carbondale it is about $26 (3%) higher.
