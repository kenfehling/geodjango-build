Home prices

The median home in Marion costs $115,500. It is approximately $15,840 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $102,000 (46%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $2,000 (1%) lower compared to the city of Carbondale.

Rents

In Marion the median rent is $740 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $69 (8%) lower. It is roughly $322 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Carbondale it is about $26 (3%) higher.
