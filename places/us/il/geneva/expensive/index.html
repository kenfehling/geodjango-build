Home prices

The price of the median home in Geneva is $338,300. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $13,480 (4%) higher. It is roughly $120,800 (55%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $80,300 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Geneva the median monthly rent is $1,510. It is about $136 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $448 (42%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $398 (35%) higher.
