Home prices

The median home in Indian Head Park is valued at $246,200. This is approximately $116,620 (32%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $28,700 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $11,800 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Indian Head Park is $986 per month. This is about $585 (37%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $76 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $126 (11%) lower.
