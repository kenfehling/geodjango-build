Home prices

The price of the median home in Round Lake Heights is $168,500. This is about $12,540 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $49,000 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $89,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

Round Lake Heights's median monthly rent is $1,470. This is approximately $233 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $408 (38%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $358 (32%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
