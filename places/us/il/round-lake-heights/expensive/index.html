Home prices

Round Lake Heights's median home price is $168,500. This is about $12,540 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $49,000 (22%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $89,500 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Round Lake Heights's median rent is $1,470 per month. It is roughly $233 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $408 (38%) higher compared to the entire country, and is about $358 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
