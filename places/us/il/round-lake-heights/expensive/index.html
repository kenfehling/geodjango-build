Home prices

The median home in Round Lake Heights is valued at $168,500. It is roughly $12,540 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $49,000 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $89,500 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Round Lake Heights is $1,470. It is about $233 (18%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $408 (38%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $358 (32%) higher.
