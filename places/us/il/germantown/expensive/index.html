Compared to nearby areas, Germantown is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Germantown is $143,600. It is approximately $16,800 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $73,900 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $4,900 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Germantown's median monthly rent is $777. It is about $102 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $285 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $51 (6%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
