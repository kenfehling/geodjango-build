Compared to its surrounding areas, Germantown is a little less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Germantown is $143,600. It is approximately $16,800 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $73,900 (33%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $4,900 (3%) higher compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Germantown's median rent is $777 per month. This is roughly $102 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $285 (26%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $51 (6%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
