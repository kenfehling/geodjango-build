Compared to nearby areas, Germantown is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Germantown costs $143,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,800 (10%) lower. It is roughly $73,900 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $4,900 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Germantown is $777 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $102 (11%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and is roughly $51 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
