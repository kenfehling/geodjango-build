Compared to the surrounding areas, Germantown is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

Germantown's median home price is $143,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $16,800 (10%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $73,900 (33%) lower, and it is approximately $4,900 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Germantown is $777 per month. It is approximately $102 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $285 (26%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $51 (6%) lower.
