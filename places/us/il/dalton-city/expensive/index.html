Home prices

The median home price in Dalton City is $93,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $16,200 (14%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is approximately $68,300 (42%) lower.

Rents

Dalton City's median rent is $529 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $106 (16%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $533 (50%) lower, and is about $423 (44%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
