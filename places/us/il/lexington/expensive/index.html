Home prices

The median home in Lexington costs $146,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $36,320 (32%) higher.It is approximately $71,000 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Aurora it is approximately $37,900 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Lexington the median monthly rent is $702. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $15 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $360 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Aurora is roughly $526 (42%) lower.
