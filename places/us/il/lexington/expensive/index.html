Home prices

The median home value in Lexington is $146,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $36,320 (32%) higher. It is approximately $71,000 (32%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $37,900 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Aurora.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lexington is $702. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $15 (2%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $360 (33%) lower, and is roughly $526 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Aurora.
