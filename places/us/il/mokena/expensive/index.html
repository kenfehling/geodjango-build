Home prices

The median home cost in Mokena is $319,200. This is roughly $47,500 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $101,700 (46%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $61,200 (23%) higher.

Rents

Mokena's median rent is $1,085 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $97 (8%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $23 (2%) higher, and it is approximately $27 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
