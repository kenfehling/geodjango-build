Home prices

The median home in Mokena is valued at $319,200. This is about $47,500 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $101,700 (46%) higher, and it is about $61,200 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Mokena is $1,085 per month. This is about $97 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $23 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $27 (2%) lower.
