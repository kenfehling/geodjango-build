Home prices

Winnebago's median home cost is $140,300. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $37,200 (36%) higher. It is approximately $77,200 (35%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $106,000 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Winnebago is $821 per month. This is about $58 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $241 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is about $297 (26%) lower.
