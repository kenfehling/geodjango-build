Home prices

The median home in Winnebago is valued at $140,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $37,200 (36%) higher. It is roughly $77,200 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $106,000 (43%) lower compared to the city of Madison.

Rents

The median rent in Winnebago is $821 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $58 (7%) higher. It is about $241 (22%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Madison it is roughly $297 (26%) lower.
