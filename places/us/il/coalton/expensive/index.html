Home prices

Coalton's median home cost is $71,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $10,380 (16%) higher. It is approximately $145,700 (66%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $66,900 (48%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Coalton is $683 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $122 (21%) higher. It is roughly $379 (35%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $145 (17%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
