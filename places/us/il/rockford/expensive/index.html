In comparison to nearby areas, Rockford is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rockford is valued at $91,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $40,340 (30%) lower. It is about $125,900 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $166,400 (64%) lower.

Rents

In Rockford the median rent is $772 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $39 (4%) lower. It is approximately $290 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $340 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
