In comparison to the surrounding areas, Riverside is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Riverside is $406,300. This is approximately $192,880 (90%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $188,800 (86%) higher, and is about $148,300 (57%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Riverside the median rent is $1,209 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $196 (19%) higher. It is roughly $147 (13%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $97 (8%) higher.
