Home prices

The price of the median home in Riverside is $406,300. It is roughly $192,880 (90%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $188,800 (86%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $148,300 (57%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Riverside's median rent is $1,209 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $196 (19%) higher. It is roughly $147 (13%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $97 (8%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
