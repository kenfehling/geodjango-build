In comparison to the surrounding areas, Riverside is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Riverside costs $406,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $192,880 (90%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $188,800 (86%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $148,300 (57%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Riverside is $1,209 per month. This is roughly $196 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $147 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $97 (8%) higher.
