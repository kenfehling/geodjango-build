Rents

The median monthly rent in Riverside is $1,209. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $196 (19%) higher.It is about $147 (13%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $97 (8%) higher.
