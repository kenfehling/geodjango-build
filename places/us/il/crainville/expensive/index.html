Compared to other places in this area, Crainville is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Carbondale, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Crainville is $149,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $40,160 (36%) higher. It is about $68,100 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $31,900 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Carbondale.

Rents

In Crainville the median monthly rent is $980. This is approximately $245 (33%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $82 (7%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $266 (37%) higher in comparison to the city of Carbondale.
