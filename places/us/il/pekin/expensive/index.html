Home prices

The median home in Pekin costs $106,200. It is roughly $13,140 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $111,300 (51%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $19,500 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Pekin's median monthly rent is $659. It is roughly $194 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $403 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is roughly $147 (18%) lower.
