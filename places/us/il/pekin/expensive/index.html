Home prices

The median home value in Pekin is $106,200. It is about $13,140 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $111,300 (51%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $19,500 (15%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Pekin's median rent is $659 a month. This is roughly $194 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $403 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Peoria it is about $147 (18%) lower.
