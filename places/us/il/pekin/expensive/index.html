In comparison to the surrounding areas, Pekin is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Pekin is $106,200. It is about $13,140 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $111,300 (51%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria it is approximately $19,500 (15%) lower.

Rents

Pekin's median monthly rent is $659. This is roughly $194 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $403 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is approximately $147 (18%) lower.
