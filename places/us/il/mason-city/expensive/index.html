Compared to other places in this area, Mason City is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The median home in Mason City is valued at $76,600. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $7,460 (10%) higher.It is approximately $140,900 (64%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $49,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Mason City's median rent is $596 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $63 (9%) lower.It is roughly $466 (43%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $210 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
