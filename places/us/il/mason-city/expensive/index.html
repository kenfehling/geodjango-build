Home prices

The median home in Mason City is valued at $76,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $7,460 (10%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $140,900 (64%) lower, and is about $49,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In Mason City the median monthly rent is $596. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $63 (9%) lower. It is approximately $466 (43%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $210 (26%) lower.
