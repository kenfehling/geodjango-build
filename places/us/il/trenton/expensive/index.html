Compared to nearby areas, Trenton is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Trenton is $143,400. This is about $8,540 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $74,100 (34%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $4,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $724 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $124 (14%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $338 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $104 (12%) lower.
