Home prices

Trenton's median home value is $143,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $8,540 (6%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $74,100 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $4,700 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Trenton is $724 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $124 (14%) lower.It is roughly $338 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $104 (12%) lower.
