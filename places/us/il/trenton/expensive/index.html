In comparison to other places in this area, Trenton is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Louis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Trenton is $143,400. It is approximately $8,540 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $74,100 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Louis is roughly $4,700 (3%) higher.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $724 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $124 (14%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $338 (31%) lower, and is roughly $104 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
