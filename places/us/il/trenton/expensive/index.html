Home prices

Trenton's median home value is $143,400. This is about $8,540 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $74,100 (34%) lower, and it is about $4,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Trenton the median rent is $724 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $124 (14%) lower. It is roughly $338 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is approximately $104 (12%) lower.
