Home prices

The median home price in Staunton is $99,700. It is about $30,440 (43%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $117,800 (54%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $39,000 (28%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

The median rent in Staunton is $692 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $98 (12%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $370 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is about $136 (16%) lower.
