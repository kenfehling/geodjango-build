Home prices

The median home in Staunton is valued at $99,700. This is about $30,440 (43%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $117,800 (54%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Louis is approximately $39,000 (28%) lower.

Rents

Staunton's median rent is $692 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $98 (12%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $370 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis is about $136 (16%) lower.
