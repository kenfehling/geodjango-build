Home prices

The median home cost in Staunton is $99,700. It is about $30,440 (43%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $117,800 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $39,000 (28%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

Staunton's median monthly rent is $692. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $98 (12%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $370 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $136 (16%) lower.
