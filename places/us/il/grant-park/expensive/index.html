In comparison to other places in this area, Grant Park is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home price in Grant Park is $156,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $19,460 (14%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $61,000 (28%) lower, and is roughly $101,500 (39%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grant Park is $841. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $61 (6%) lower.It is roughly $221 (20%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $271 (24%) lower.
