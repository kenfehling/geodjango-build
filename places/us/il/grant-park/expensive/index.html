In comparison to its surrounding areas, Grant Park is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Grant Park's median home price is $156,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $19,460 (14%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $61,000 (28%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $101,500 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Grant Park is $841 a month. It is roughly $61 (6%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $221 (20%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $271 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
