In comparison to its surrounding areas, Grant Park is a bit more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Grant Park costs $156,500. This is about $19,460 (14%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $61,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $101,500 (39%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Grant Park is $841 a month. It is roughly $61 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $221 (20%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $271 (24%) lower.
