Home prices

Ogden's median home cost is $125,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $7,860 (6%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $91,900 (42%) lower, and is approximately $36,200 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

Ogden's median rent is $969 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $145 (17%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $93 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign it is approximately $17 (1%) higher.
