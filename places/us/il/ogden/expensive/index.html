Home prices

The median home in Ogden is valued at $125,600. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $7,860 (6%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $91,900 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign it is roughly $36,200 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Ogden is $969. It is approximately $145 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $93 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign is approximately $17 (1%) higher.
