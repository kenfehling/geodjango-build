Home prices

The cost of the median home in Coal City is $197,100. This is about $45,880 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $20,400 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $60,900 (23%) lower.

Rents

Coal City's median monthly rent is $1,006. It is approximately $71 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $56 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $106 (9%) lower.
