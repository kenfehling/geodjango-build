Home prices

The median home cost in Moline is $119,500. It is roughly $22,540 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $98,000 (45%) lower, and is approximately $6,200 (4%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In Moline the median monthly rent is $770. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $127 (14%) lower. It is approximately $292 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is approximately $36 (4%) lower.
