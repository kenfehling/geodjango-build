Compared to its surrounding areas, Moline is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Peoria however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Moline is valued at $119,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $22,540 (15%) lower.It is roughly $98,000 (45%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is roughly $6,200 (4%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Moline is $770. It is approximately $127 (14%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $292 (27%) lower, and is roughly $36 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
