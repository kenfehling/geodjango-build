Compared to other places in this area, Moline is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The median home in Moline is valued at $119,500. It is about $22,540 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $98,000 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria is roughly $6,200 (4%) lower.

Rents

Moline's median rent is $770 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $127 (14%) lower.It is about $292 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $36 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.
