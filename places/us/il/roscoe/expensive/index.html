Home prices

The median home in Roscoe is valued at $163,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $30,240 (22%) higher.It is roughly $54,500 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $95,000 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Roscoe's median monthly rent is $1,102. This is roughly $222 (25%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $40 (3%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is is very similar.
