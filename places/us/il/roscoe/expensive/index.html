Home prices

The median home in Roscoe costs $163,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $30,240 (22%) higher.It is approximately $54,500 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $95,000 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Roscoe is $1,102 per month. It is about $222 (25%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $40 (3%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Chicago.
