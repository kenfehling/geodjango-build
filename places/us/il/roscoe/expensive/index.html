Home prices

The median home in Roscoe is valued at $163,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $30,240 (22%) higher. It is roughly $54,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $95,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Roscoe the median monthly rent is $1,102. It is roughly $222 (25%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $40 (3%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Chicago.
