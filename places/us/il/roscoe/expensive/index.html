Home prices

The median home in Roscoe costs $163,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $30,240 (22%) higher. It is about $54,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $95,000 (36%) lower.

Rents

Roscoe's median rent is $1,102 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $222 (25%) higher. It is approximately $40 (3%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Chicago.
