Home prices

Rents

In Oakland the median rent is $669 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $111 (19%) higher.It is approximately $393 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $283 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
