Compared to other places in this area, Oakland is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Champaign, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland is valued at $73,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $10,620 (16%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $144,000 (66%) lower, and is roughly $88,300 (54%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median rent in Oakland is $669 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $111 (19%) higher. It is approximately $393 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is about $283 (29%) lower.
