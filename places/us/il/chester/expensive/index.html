Home prices

The median home cost in Chester is $101,700. This is roughly $13,540 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $115,800 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is approximately $37,000 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Chester is $535 a month. This is approximately $174 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $527 (49%) lower, and is roughly $293 (35%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
