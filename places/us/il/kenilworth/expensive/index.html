Home prices

The median home in Kenilworth costs $1,121,100. This is approximately $491,480 (78%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $903,600 (415%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $863,100 (334%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Kenilworth's median rent is $2,906 per month. This is about $1,338 (85%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,844 (173%) higher, and it is roughly $1,794 (161%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
