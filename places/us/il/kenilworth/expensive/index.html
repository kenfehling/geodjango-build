Compared to its surrounding areas, Kenilworth is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Kenilworth's median home value is $1,121,100. Compared to other places in this area it is about $491,480 (78%) higher. It is roughly $903,600 (415%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $863,100 (334%) higher.

Rents

In Kenilworth the median monthly rent is $2,906. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $1,338 (85%) higher. It is about $1,844 (173%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $1,794 (161%) higher.
