Compared to nearby areas, Kenilworth is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Kenilworth's median home price is $1,121,100. This is about $491,480 (78%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $903,600 (415%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $863,100 (334%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kenilworth is $2,906. This is roughly $1,338 (85%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $1,844 (173%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $1,794 (161%) higher.
