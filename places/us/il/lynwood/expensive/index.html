Compared to the surrounding areas, Lynwood is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Lynwood costs $150,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $13,240 (9%) higher.It is roughly $66,700 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $107,200 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Lynwood's median rent is $913 per month. This is about $149 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $149 (14%) lower, and is approximately $199 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.
