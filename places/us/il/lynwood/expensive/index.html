Home prices

The value of the median home in Lynwood is $150,800. It is about $13,240 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $66,700 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $107,200 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lynwood is $913 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $149 (14%) lower. It is roughly $149 (14%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $199 (17%) lower.
