In comparison to its nearby areas, Winnetka is a lot more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

Winnetka's median home cost is $1,091,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $307,520 (39%) higher. It is approximately $874,200 (401%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $833,700 (323%) higher.

Rents

In Winnetka the median monthly rent is $1,628. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $336 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $566 (53%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $516 (46%) higher.
