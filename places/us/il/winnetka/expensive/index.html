Compared to nearby areas, Winnetka is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Winnetka is $1,091,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $307,520 (39%) higher.It is roughly $874,200 (401%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $833,700 (323%) higher.

Rents

In Winnetka the median rent is $1,628 per month. This is about $336 (17%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $566 (53%) higher, and it is roughly $516 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
