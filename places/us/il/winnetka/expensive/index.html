Compared to nearby areas, Winnetka is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Winnetka is $1,091,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $307,520 (39%) higher. It is about $874,200 (401%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $833,700 (323%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Winnetka the median rent is $1,628 per month. It is about $336 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $566 (53%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $516 (46%) higher.
