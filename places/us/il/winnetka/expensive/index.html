In comparison to the surrounding areas, Winnetka is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Chicago, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Winnetka costs $1,091,700. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $307,520 (39%) higher.It is approximately $874,200 (401%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $833,700 (323%) higher.

Rents

In Winnetka the median monthly rent is $1,628. This is approximately $336 (17%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $566 (53%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $516 (46%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
