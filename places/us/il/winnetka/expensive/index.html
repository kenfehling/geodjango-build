In comparison to the surrounding areas, Winnetka is significantly more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Winnetka costs $1,091,700. This is approximately $307,520 (39%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $874,200 (401%) higher, and it is approximately $833,700 (323%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Winnetka is $1,628. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $336 (17%) lower. It is about $566 (53%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago is roughly $516 (46%) higher.
