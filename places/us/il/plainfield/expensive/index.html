Home prices

The median home in Plainfield is valued at $319,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $111,620 (53%) higher. It is roughly $102,000 (46%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $61,500 (23%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Plainfield's median monthly rent is $1,795. This is roughly $332 (22%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $733 (69%) higher, and it is about $683 (61%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
