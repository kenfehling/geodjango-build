Home prices

The median home in Plainfield costs $319,500. It is roughly $111,620 (53%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $102,000 (46%) higher, and it is approximately $61,500 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Plainfield's median monthly rent is $1,795. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $332 (22%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $733 (69%) higher, and is roughly $683 (61%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
