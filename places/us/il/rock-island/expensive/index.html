Home prices

Rock Island's median home value is $104,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $18,660 (15%) lower. It is approximately $112,900 (51%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $21,100 (16%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Rock Island's median monthly rent is $729. This is approximately $12 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $333 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is about $77 (9%) lower.
