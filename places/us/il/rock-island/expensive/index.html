Home prices

The median home in Rock Island costs $104,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $18,660 (15%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $112,900 (51%) lower, and is approximately $21,100 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Rock Island is $729 per month. This is about $12 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $77 (9%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.
