Home prices

The median home in Hanna City costs $133,900. It is about $39,340 (41%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $83,600 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $8,200 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Hanna City is $678 a month. This is roughly $90 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $384 (36%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Peoria is approximately $128 (15%) lower.
