Home prices

The median home in Wyoming costs $76,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $920 (1%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $140,600 (64%) lower, and is about $14,700 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.

Rents

The median rent in Wyoming is $612 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $63 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $450 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Rockford is roughly $160 (20%) lower.
