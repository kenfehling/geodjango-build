In comparison to its nearby areas, Rosemont is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Rosemont is $371,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $81,380 (28%) higher. It is about $154,000 (70%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $113,500 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Rosemont the median rent is $948 per month. It is approximately $211 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $114 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $164 (14%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
