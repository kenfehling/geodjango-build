Compared to other places in this area, Rosemont is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Rosemont is valued at $371,500. It is about $81,380 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $154,000 (70%) higher, and it is about $113,500 (43%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Rosemont the median rent is $948 a month. It is about $211 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $114 (10%) lower, and is about $164 (14%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.
