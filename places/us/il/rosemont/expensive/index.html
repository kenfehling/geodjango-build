Compared to its surrounding areas, Rosemont is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire nation. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Rosemont's median home price is $371,500. It is roughly $81,380 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $154,000 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $113,500 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rosemont is $948. It is about $211 (18%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $114 (10%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $164 (14%) lower.
