In comparison to other places in this area, Long Lake is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home cost in Long Lake is $155,800. It is approximately $32,640 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $61,700 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is roughly $102,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Long Lake the median rent is $1,210 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $113 (8%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $148 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is approximately $98 (8%) higher.
