Home prices

The median home in Long Lake costs $155,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $32,640 (17%) lower.It is approximately $61,700 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $102,200 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Long Lake's median monthly rent is $1,210. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $113 (8%) lower.It is about $148 (13%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $98 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
