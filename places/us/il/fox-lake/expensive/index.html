Home prices

The median home in Fox Lake costs $161,100. This is roughly $25,480 (13%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $56,400 (25%) lower, and it is about $96,900 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Fox Lake the median rent is $1,058 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $207 (16%) lower. It is is very similar compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $54 (4%) lower.
