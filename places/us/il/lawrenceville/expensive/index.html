Home prices

The median home price in Lawrenceville is $73,500. It is roughly $15,880 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $144,000 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is about $20,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

In Lawrenceville the median monthly rent is $691. It is about $87 (14%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $371 (34%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Evansville it is roughly $83 (10%) lower.
