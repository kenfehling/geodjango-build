Compared to other places in this area, Lawrenceville is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Evansville.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrenceville is valued at $73,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $15,880 (27%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $144,000 (66%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is approximately $20,700 (21%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lawrenceville is $691. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $87 (14%) higher.It is about $371 (34%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Evansville is approximately $83 (10%) lower.
