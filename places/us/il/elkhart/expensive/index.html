Home prices

The median home in Elkhart costs $110,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $16,000 (16%) higher. It is roughly $107,300 (49%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $51,600 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Elkhart is $700. It is roughly $22 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $252 (26%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
