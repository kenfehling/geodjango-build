Home prices

The median home in Lakewood Shores costs $151,400. It is roughly $2,320 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $66,100 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $106,600 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Lakewood Shores the median rent is $1,286 per month. This is approximately $272 (26%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $224 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $174 (15%) higher.
