Home prices

The median home cost in Lakewood Shores is $151,400. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $8,040 (5%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $66,100 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $106,600 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Lakewood Shores the median rent is $1,286 a month. This is about $246 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $224 (21%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $174 (15%) higher.
