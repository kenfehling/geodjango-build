Compared to its surrounding areas, Prospect Heights is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Chicago, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Prospect Heights is valued at $284,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $66,560 (18%) lower.It is approximately $66,800 (30%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $26,300 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Prospect Heights's median rent is $1,099 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $394 (26%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $37 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $13 (1%) lower.
