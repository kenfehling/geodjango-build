Home prices

The median home in Prospect Heights is valued at $284,300. This is approximately $66,560 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $66,800 (30%) higher, and it is approximately $26,300 (10%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Prospect Heights the median monthly rent is $1,099. It is roughly $394 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $37 (3%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
