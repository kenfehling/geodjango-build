Home prices

Prospect Heights's median home value is $284,300. This is roughly $66,560 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $66,800 (30%) higher, and is about $26,300 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Prospect Heights's median rent is $1,099 a month. It is roughly $394 (26%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $37 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $13 (1%) lower.
