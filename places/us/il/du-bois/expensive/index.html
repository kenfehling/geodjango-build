Home prices

The median home value in Du Bois is $63,500. This is approximately $3,160 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $154,000 (70%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is about $75,200 (54%) lower.

Rents

In Du Bois the median rent is $525 a month. It is about $117 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $537 (50%) lower, and it is approximately $303 (36%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
