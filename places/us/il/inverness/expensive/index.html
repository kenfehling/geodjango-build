Home prices

Rents

Inverness's median monthly rent is $3,250. It is about $1,897 (140%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $2,188 (206%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $2,138 (192%) higher.
