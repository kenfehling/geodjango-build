Home prices

The median home value in Inverness is $628,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $259,680 (70%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $411,200 (189%) higher, and it is about $370,700 (143%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Inverness's median monthly rent is $3,250. It is about $1,897 (140%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $2,188 (206%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is approximately $2,138 (192%) higher.
