In comparison to nearby areas, Inverness is significantly more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Chicago, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Inverness is valued at $628,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $259,680 (70%) higher. It is roughly $411,200 (189%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is roughly $370,700 (143%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Inverness the median rent is $3,250 per month. It is approximately $1,897 (140%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $2,188 (206%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Chicago is about $2,138 (192%) higher.
