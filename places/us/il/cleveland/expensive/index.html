Home prices

Cleveland's median home cost is $97,500. It is approximately $29,720 (23%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $120,000 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $28,200 (22%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

Cleveland's median rent is $933 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $130 (16%) higher.It is approximately $129 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Peoria it is approximately $127 (15%) higher.
