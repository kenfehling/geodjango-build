Home prices

The price of the median home in Cleveland is $97,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $29,720 (23%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $120,000 (55%) lower, and is about $28,200 (22%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $933. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $130 (16%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $129 (12%) lower, and is roughly $127 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.
