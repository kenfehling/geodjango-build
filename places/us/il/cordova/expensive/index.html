Cordova's median home cost is $143,100. This is about $41,540 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $74,400 (34%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $51,500 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Rockford.

Rents

In Cordova the median monthly rent is $760. It is roughly $109 (12%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $302 (28%) lower, and is roughly $12 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Rockford.
