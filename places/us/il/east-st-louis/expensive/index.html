Home prices

The median home in East St. Louis costs $54,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $4,520 (9%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $163,000 (74%) lower, and it is roughly $84,200 (60%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

East St. Louis's median monthly rent is $484. It is roughly $234 (32%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $578 (54%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $344 (41%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
