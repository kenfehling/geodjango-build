Compared to the surrounding areas, East St. Louis is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the entire country. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of St. Louis.

Home prices

East St. Louis's median home cost is $54,500. This is approximately $4,520 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $163,000 (74%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Louis it is roughly $84,200 (60%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East St. Louis is $484. It is approximately $234 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $578 (54%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis is roughly $344 (41%) lower.
