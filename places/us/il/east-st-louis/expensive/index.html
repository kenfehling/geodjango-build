Compared to its surrounding areas, East St. Louis is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Louis, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

East St. Louis's median home value is $54,500. This is roughly $4,520 (9%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $163,000 (74%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Louis it is about $84,200 (60%) lower.

Rents

In East St. Louis the median rent is $484 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $234 (32%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $578 (54%) lower, and it is roughly $344 (41%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.
