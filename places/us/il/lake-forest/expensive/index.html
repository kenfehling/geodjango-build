Home prices

The median home in Lake Forest is valued at $842,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $282,600 (50%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $625,400 (287%) higher, and it is approximately $584,900 (226%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Lake Forest's median rent is $1,831 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $64 (3%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $769 (72%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $719 (64%) higher.
