Home prices

Lake Forest's median home price is $842,900. It is roughly $282,600 (50%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $625,400 (287%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $584,900 (226%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Lake Forest the median monthly rent is $1,831. Compared to nearby areas it is about $64 (3%) higher.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $769 (72%) higher, and it is about $719 (64%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
