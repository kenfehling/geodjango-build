Compared to the surrounding areas, Lake Forest is significantly more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home cost in Lake Forest is $842,900. This is roughly $282,600 (50%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $625,400 (287%) higher, and is approximately $584,900 (226%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Forest is $1,831. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $64 (3%) higher. It is roughly $769 (72%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is about $719 (64%) higher.
