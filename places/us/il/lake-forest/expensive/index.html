Home prices

The median home in Lake Forest is valued at $842,900. It is about $282,600 (50%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $625,400 (287%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $584,900 (226%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

Lake Forest's median rent is $1,831 a month. This is approximately $64 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $769 (72%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is about $719 (64%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
