Compared to nearby areas, Buffalo is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Champaign, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Buffalo costs $89,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $30,340 (25%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $127,700 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign it is about $72,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median monthly rent is $725. It is about $68 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $227 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
