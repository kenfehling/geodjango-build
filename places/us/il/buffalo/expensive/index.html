Home prices

The median home cost in Buffalo is $89,800. It is roughly $30,340 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $127,700 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign is roughly $72,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $725 a month. This is roughly $68 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $337 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is about $227 (23%) lower.
