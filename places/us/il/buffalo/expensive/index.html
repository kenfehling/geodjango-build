Home prices

The median home in Buffalo costs $89,800. It is approximately $30,340 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $127,700 (58%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Champaign it is roughly $72,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median monthly rent is $725. It is roughly $68 (8%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is approximately $227 (23%) lower.
