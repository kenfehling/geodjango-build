Home prices

The median home in Buffalo costs $89,800. This is roughly $30,340 (25%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $127,700 (58%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Champaign it is approximately $72,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Buffalo is $725. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $68 (8%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $337 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $227 (23%) lower compared to the city of Champaign.
