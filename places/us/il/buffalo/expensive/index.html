Compared to its surrounding areas, Buffalo is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Champaign.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Buffalo is $89,800. This is about $30,340 (25%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $127,700 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Champaign it is roughly $72,000 (44%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $725 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $68 (8%) lower. It is about $337 (31%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $227 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Champaign.
