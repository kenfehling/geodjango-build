Compared to the surrounding areas, Hainesville is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Chicago, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Hainesville is $169,200. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $23,880 (12%) lower. It is roughly $48,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $88,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hainesville is $1,454 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $167 (12%) higher. It is approximately $392 (36%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $342 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
