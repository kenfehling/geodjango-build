Compared to its nearby areas, Hainesville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Chicago, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hainesville costs $169,200. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $23,880 (12%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $48,300 (22%) lower, and is roughly $88,800 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

In Hainesville the median monthly rent is $1,454. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $167 (12%) higher.It is about $392 (36%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is about $342 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
