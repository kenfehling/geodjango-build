In comparison to other places in this area, Hainesville is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Hainesville costs $169,200. This is roughly $23,880 (12%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is about $48,300 (22%) lower, and is approximately $88,800 (34%) lower compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median rent in Hainesville is $1,454 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $167 (12%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $392 (36%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $342 (30%) higher.
