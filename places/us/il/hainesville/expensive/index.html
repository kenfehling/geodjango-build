In comparison to its nearby areas, Hainesville is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Hainesville costs $169,200. It is about $23,880 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $48,300 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Chicago it is roughly $88,800 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Hainesville the median monthly rent is $1,454. It is roughly $167 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $392 (36%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $342 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
