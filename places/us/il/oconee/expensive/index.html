Home prices

The value of the median home in Oconee is $69,000. It is approximately $3,940 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $148,500 (68%) lower, and it is roughly $69,700 (50%) lower compared to the city of St. Louis.

Rents

In Oconee the median monthly rent is $665. It is roughly $30 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $397 (37%) lower, and it is roughly $163 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Louis.
