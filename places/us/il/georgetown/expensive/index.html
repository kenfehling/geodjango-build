In comparison to its surrounding areas, Georgetown is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Peoria.

Home prices

The median home in Georgetown is valued at $91,600. This is about $14,700 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $125,900 (57%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $34,100 (27%) lower compared to the city of Peoria.

Rents

The median rent in Georgetown is $1,142 a month. It is roughly $504 (79%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $80 (7%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $336 (41%) higher in comparison to the city of Peoria.
