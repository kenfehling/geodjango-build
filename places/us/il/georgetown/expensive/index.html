Home prices

The cost of the median home in Georgetown is $91,600. It is about $14,700 (19%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $125,900 (57%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is approximately $34,100 (27%) lower.

Rents

In Georgetown the median monthly rent is $1,142. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $504 (79%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $80 (7%) higher, and it is roughly $336 (41%) higher compared to the city of Peoria.
