Home prices

The median home in Mount Prospect costs $330,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $62,360 (23%) higher. It is roughly $112,700 (51%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Chicago it is roughly $72,200 (27%) higher.

Rents

In Mount Prospect the median rent is $1,132 per month. It is about $119 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $70 (6%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $20 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
