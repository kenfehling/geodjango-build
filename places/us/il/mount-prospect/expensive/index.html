In comparison to its nearby areas, Mount Prospect is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a little more expensive compared to the city of Chicago.

Home prices

The median home in Mount Prospect is valued at $330,200. It is about $62,360 (23%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $112,700 (51%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $72,200 (27%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Prospect is $1,132. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $119 (9%) lower. It is roughly $70 (6%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $20 (1%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.
