Home prices

Mount Prospect's median home price is $330,200. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $62,360 (23%) higher.It is about $112,700 (51%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $72,200 (27%) higher compared to the city of Chicago.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Prospect is $1,132. This is approximately $119 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $70 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Chicago is about $20 (1%) higher.
