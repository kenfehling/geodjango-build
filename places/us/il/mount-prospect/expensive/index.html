In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mount Prospect is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Chicago, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Mount Prospect's median home cost is $330,200. This is about $62,360 (23%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $112,700 (51%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Chicago it is approximately $72,200 (27%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Mount Prospect is $1,132. Compared to nearby areas this is about $119 (9%) lower.It is roughly $70 (6%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $20 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Chicago.
