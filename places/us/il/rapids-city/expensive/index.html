Home prices

The median home in Rapids City costs $180,100. This is roughly $22,620 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $37,400 (17%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is approximately $54,400 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rapids City is $908. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $108 (13%) higher.It is roughly $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Peoria is about $102 (12%) higher.
