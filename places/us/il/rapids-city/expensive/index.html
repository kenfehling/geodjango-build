In comparison to the surrounding areas, Rapids City is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Peoria however, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Rapids City is $180,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $22,620 (14%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $37,400 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria it is about $54,400 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Rapids City the median rent is $908 per month. It is roughly $108 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $154 (14%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $102 (12%) higher compared to the city of Peoria.
