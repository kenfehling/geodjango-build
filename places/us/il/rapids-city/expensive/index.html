Home prices

The median home cost in Rapids City is $180,100. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $22,620 (14%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $37,400 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Peoria it is roughly $54,400 (43%) higher.

Rents

In Rapids City the median monthly rent is $908. This is approximately $108 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $154 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Peoria it is about $102 (12%) higher.
