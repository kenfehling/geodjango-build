The price of the median home in Bowling Green is $161,000. This is approximately $49,780 (44%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Rents

The median rent in Bowling Green is $778 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $19 (2%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $284 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $183 (19%) lower.
