Home prices

The median home cost in Camargo is $83,800. This is roughly $13,940 (14%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $133,700 (61%) lower, and is roughly $54,200 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Camargo the median rent is $812 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $141 (21%) higher. It is roughly $250 (23%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $74 (10%) higher.
