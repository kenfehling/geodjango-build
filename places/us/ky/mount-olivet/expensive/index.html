Home prices

The median home in Mount Olivet costs $75,000. This is roughly $15,240 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $142,500 (65%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $63,000 (45%) lower.

Rents

Mount Olivet's median rent is $425 per month. This is approximately $232 (35%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $637 (59%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $313 (42%) lower.
