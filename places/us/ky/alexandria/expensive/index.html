Home prices

The median home in Alexandria costs $179,700. This is approximately $19,420 (12%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $41,700 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Alexandria is $867 a month. It is roughly $46 (5%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $195 (18%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $129 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
