Home prices

The median home price in Alexandria is $179,700. This is about $19,420 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $37,800 (17%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $41,700 (30%) higher.

Rents

Alexandria's median monthly rent is $867. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $46 (5%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $195 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $129 (17%) higher.
