In comparison to other places in this area, Alexandria is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Alexandria costs $179,700. This is approximately $19,420 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $37,800 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $41,700 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Alexandria the median rent is $867 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $46 (5%) lower. It is about $195 (18%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $129 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
