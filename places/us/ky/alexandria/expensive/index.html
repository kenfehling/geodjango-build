Home prices

The median home cost in Alexandria is $179,700. Compared to other places in this area this is about $19,420 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $37,800 (17%) lower, and is approximately $41,700 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alexandria is $867. This is approximately $46 (5%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $195 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $129 (17%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
