In comparison to the surrounding areas, Columbus is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Jackson.

Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $56,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $7,720 (11%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $160,600 (73%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Jackson it is approximately $76,300 (57%) lower.

Rents

Columbus's median monthly rent is $582. It is about $36 (6%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $480 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $295 (33%) lower compared to the city of Jackson.
