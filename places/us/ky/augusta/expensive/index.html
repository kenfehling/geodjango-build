Compared to its nearby areas, Augusta is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Augusta's median home cost is $110,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $18,560 (20%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $107,500 (49%) lower, and it is about $28,000 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Augusta's median monthly rent is $568. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $86 (13%) lower.It is roughly $494 (46%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $170 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
