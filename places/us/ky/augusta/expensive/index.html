Compared to the surrounding areas, Augusta is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Augusta's median home cost is $110,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $18,560 (20%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $107,500 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $28,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Augusta the median monthly rent is $568. It is approximately $86 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $494 (46%) lower, and it is roughly $170 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
