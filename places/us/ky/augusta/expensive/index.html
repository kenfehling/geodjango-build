In comparison to nearby areas, Augusta is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Augusta costs $110,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $18,560 (20%) higher.It is about $107,500 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $28,000 (20%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Augusta the median rent is $568 a month. It is roughly $86 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $494 (46%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $170 (23%) lower.
