Home prices

The median home in Augusta is valued at $110,000. Compared to other places in this area this is about $18,560 (20%) higher. It is roughly $107,500 (49%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $28,000 (20%) lower.

Rents

In Augusta the median monthly rent is $568. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $86 (13%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $494 (46%) lower, and is approximately $170 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
