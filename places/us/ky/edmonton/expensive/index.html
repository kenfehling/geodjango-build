Compared to other places in this area, Edmonton is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Bowling Green.

Home prices

The median home value in Edmonton is $82,100. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $11,000 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $135,400 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bowling Green it is roughly $78,900 (49%) lower.

Rents

Edmonton's median monthly rent is $446. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $55 (11%) lower. It is roughly $616 (58%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Bowling Green is approximately $332 (42%) lower.
