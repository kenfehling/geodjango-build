Home prices

The cost of the median home in Edmonton is $82,100. This is approximately $11,000 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $135,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $78,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Bowling Green.

Rents

In Edmonton the median monthly rent is $446. This is about $55 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $616 (58%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $332 (42%) lower compared to the city of Bowling Green.
