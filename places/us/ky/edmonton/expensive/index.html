Compared to the surrounding areas, Edmonton is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Bowling Green, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Edmonton is $82,100. It is roughly $11,000 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $135,400 (62%) lower, and is about $78,900 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of Bowling Green.

Rents

Edmonton's median monthly rent is $446. It is roughly $55 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $616 (58%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bowling Green is roughly $332 (42%) lower.
