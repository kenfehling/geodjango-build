Compared to other places in this area, Edmonton is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Bowling Green, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Edmonton is $82,100. It is about $11,000 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $135,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $78,900 (49%) lower compared to the city of Bowling Green.

Rents

Edmonton's median monthly rent is $446. It is approximately $55 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $616 (58%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $332 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Bowling Green.
