In comparison to other places in this area, Edmonton is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Bowling Green, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Edmonton costs $82,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $11,000 (11%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $135,400 (62%) lower, and is roughly $78,900 (49%) lower compared to the city of Bowling Green.

Rents

The median rent in Edmonton is $446 a month. It is about $55 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $616 (58%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bowling Green is approximately $332 (42%) lower.
