Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cadiz is $551. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $76 (12%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $511 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is about $410 (42%) lower.
