Home prices

Williamstown's median home value is $130,500. This is approximately $25,620 (24%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $87,000 (40%) lower, and is roughly $7,500 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Williamstown the median rent is $719 a month. It is approximately $40 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $343 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $19 (2%) lower.
