Home prices

The median home in Williamstown is valued at $130,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $25,620 (24%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $87,000 (40%) lower, and is roughly $7,500 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Williamstown is $719 a month. It is approximately $40 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $343 (32%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $19 (2%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
