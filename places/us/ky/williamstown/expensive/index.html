Compared to other places in this area, Williamstown is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Williamstown costs $130,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $25,620 (24%) higher.It is about $87,000 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $7,500 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Williamstown the median monthly rent is $719. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $40 (5%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $343 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $19 (2%) lower.
