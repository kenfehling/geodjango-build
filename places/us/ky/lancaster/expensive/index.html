Home prices

The value of the median home in Lancaster is $91,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $15,740 (14%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $125,800 (57%) lower, and it is approximately $98,100 (51%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median rent in Lancaster is $685 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $55 (8%) higher. It is roughly $377 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is about $211 (23%) lower.
