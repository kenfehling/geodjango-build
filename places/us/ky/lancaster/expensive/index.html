Home prices

The price of the median home in Lancaster is $91,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $15,740 (14%) lower.It is roughly $125,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $98,100 (51%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $685. It is roughly $55 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $377 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $211 (23%) lower.
