Home prices

Rents

In St. Matthews the median monthly rent is $1,031. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $476 (31%) lower.It is roughly $31 (2%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $135 (15%) higher.
