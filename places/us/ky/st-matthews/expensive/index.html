Home prices

Rents

St. Matthews's median rent is $1,031 per month. It is about $476 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $31 (2%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is roughly $135 (15%) higher.
