Compared to its surrounding areas, Worthington Hills is substantially less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Worthington Hills's median home price is $156,200. It is about $82,600 (34%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $61,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $18,200 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Worthington Hills's median monthly rent is $1,103. It is about $138 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $41 (3%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $365 (49%) higher.
