Compared to other places in this area, Brownsboro Village is a little less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially more expensive compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brownsboro Village is $263,500. This is about $48,760 (15%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $46,000 (21%) higher, and is roughly $73,700 (38%) higher compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median rent in Brownsboro Village is $1,297 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $168 (11%) lower. It is about $235 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $401 (44%) higher.
