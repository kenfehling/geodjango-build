Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brownsboro Village is $263,500. It is about $48,760 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $46,000 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is about $73,700 (38%) higher.

Rents

In Brownsboro Village the median rent is $1,297 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $168 (11%) lower.It is roughly $235 (22%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $401 (44%) higher compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
