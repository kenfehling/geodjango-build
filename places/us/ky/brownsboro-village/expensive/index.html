In comparison to its surrounding areas, Brownsboro Village is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Brownsboro Village is valued at $263,500. This is about $48,760 (15%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $46,000 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $73,700 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brownsboro Village is $1,297. It is about $168 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $235 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is about $401 (44%) higher.
