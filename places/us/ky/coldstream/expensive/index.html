Compared to other places in this area, Coldstream is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Coldstream is $165,200. It is approximately $71,800 (30%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $52,300 (24%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $27,200 (19%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Coldstream is $1,614 per month. This is roughly $475 (41%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $552 (51%) higher, and it is approximately $876 (118%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
