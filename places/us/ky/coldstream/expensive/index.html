Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Coldstream is $1,614 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $475 (41%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $552 (51%) higher, and it is roughly $876 (118%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
