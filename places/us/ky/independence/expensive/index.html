Home prices

The median home in Independence costs $173,300. This is roughly $19,180 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $44,200 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $35,300 (25%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Independence the median rent is $960 a month. This is roughly $22 (2%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $102 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $222 (30%) higher.
