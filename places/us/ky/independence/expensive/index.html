Home prices

The median home value in Independence is $173,300. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $19,180 (12%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $44,200 (20%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $35,300 (25%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Independence is $960 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $22 (2%) lower. It is about $102 (9%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $222 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
