Home prices

The price of the median home in Independence is $173,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $19,180 (12%) higher. It is approximately $44,200 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $35,300 (25%) higher.

Rents

Independence's median rent is $960 per month. This is approximately $22 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $102 (9%) lower, and is approximately $222 (30%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
