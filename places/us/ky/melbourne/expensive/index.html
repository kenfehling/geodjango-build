Home prices

Melbourne's median home value is $128,700. It is about $31,100 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $88,800 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $9,300 (6%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Melbourne is $475 per month. It is approximately $650 (57%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $587 (55%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $263 (35%) lower.
