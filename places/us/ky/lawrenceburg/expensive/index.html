Rents

In Lawrenceburg the median monthly rent is $697. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $70 (9%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $365 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $41 (5%) lower.
