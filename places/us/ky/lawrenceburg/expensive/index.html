Compared to its surrounding areas, Lawrenceburg is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrenceburg costs $131,100. This is approximately $21,760 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $86,400 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $6,900 (5%) lower.

Rents

In Lawrenceburg the median rent is $697 per month. It is roughly $70 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $41 (5%) lower.
