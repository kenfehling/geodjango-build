Rents

Lawrenceburg's median rent is $697 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $70 (9%) lower. It is approximately $365 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $41 (5%) lower.
