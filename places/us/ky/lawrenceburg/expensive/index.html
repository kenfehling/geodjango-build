In comparison to other places in this area, Lawrenceburg is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Lawrenceburg costs $131,100. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $21,760 (14%) lower.It is roughly $86,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $6,900 (5%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Lawrenceburg the median monthly rent is $697. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $70 (9%) lower.It is roughly $365 (34%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $41 (5%) lower.
