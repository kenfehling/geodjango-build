Home prices

The median home in Sacramento costs $71,000. It is roughly $14,380 (16%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $146,500 (67%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $83,300 (53%) lower.

Rents

In Sacramento the median rent is $589 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is is very similar.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $473 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is roughly $372 (38%) lower.
