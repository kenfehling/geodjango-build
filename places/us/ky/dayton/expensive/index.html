Compared to nearby areas, Dayton is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Dayton is $107,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $49,300 (31%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $110,500 (50%) lower, and is roughly $31,000 (22%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Dayton is $855 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $11 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $207 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $117 (15%) higher.
