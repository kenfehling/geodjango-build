Compared to its surrounding areas, Dayton is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Dayton is valued at $107,000. This is roughly $49,300 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $110,500 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $31,000 (22%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $855 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11 (1%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is about $207 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $117 (15%) higher.
