Home prices

Dayton's median home price is $107,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $49,300 (31%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $110,500 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $31,000 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Dayton is $855. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $11 (1%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $207 (19%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $117 (15%) higher.
