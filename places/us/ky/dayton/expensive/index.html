In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dayton is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Dayton is $107,000. This is roughly $49,300 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $110,500 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $31,000 (22%) lower.

Rents

Dayton's median monthly rent is $855. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $11 (1%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $207 (19%) lower, and it is approximately $117 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
