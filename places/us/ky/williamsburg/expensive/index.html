Home prices

Rents

In Williamsburg the median rent is $582 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $49 (9%) higher.It is roughly $480 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette is roughly $314 (35%) lower.
