Home prices

The median home in Williamsburg costs $95,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $10,480 (12%) higher. It is about $122,300 (56%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $94,600 (49%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Williamsburg is $582. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $49 (9%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $480 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $314 (35%) lower.
