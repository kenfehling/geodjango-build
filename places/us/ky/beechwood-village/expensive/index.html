In comparison to its nearby areas, Beechwood Village is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Beechwood Village costs $262,200. This is roughly $33,700 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $44,700 (20%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $124,200 (89%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Beechwood Village is $994 per month. It is about $521 (34%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $68 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $256 (34%) higher.
