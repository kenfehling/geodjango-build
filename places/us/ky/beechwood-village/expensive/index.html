Home prices

The median home in Beechwood Village costs $262,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $33,700 (11%) lower.It is about $44,700 (20%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $124,200 (89%) higher.

Rents

In Beechwood Village the median monthly rent is $994. It is approximately $521 (34%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $68 (6%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $256 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
