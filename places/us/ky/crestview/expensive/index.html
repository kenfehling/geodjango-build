Home prices

The median home value in Crestview is $132,100. It is approximately $8,480 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $85,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $5,900 (4%) lower.

Rents

Crestview's median monthly rent is $1,300. It is about $421 (47%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $238 (22%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $562 (76%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
