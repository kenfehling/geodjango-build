Home prices

The median home in Highland Heights costs $108,900. This is approximately $42,860 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $108,600 (49%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $29,100 (21%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Highland Heights's median rent is $921 per month. This is about $105 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $141 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $183 (24%) higher.
