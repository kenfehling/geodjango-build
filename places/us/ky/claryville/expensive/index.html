Home prices

The median home price in Claryville is $210,000. It is approximately $40,900 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $7,500 (3%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $72,000 (52%) higher.

Rents

In Claryville the median rent is $838 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $58 (6%) lower.It is roughly $224 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $100 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
