Home prices

The median home in Greenville is valued at $126,000. This is roughly $35,680 (39%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $91,500 (42%) lower, and is approximately $28,300 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

Greenville's median rent is $514 a month. It is roughly $131 (20%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $548 (51%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $447 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.
