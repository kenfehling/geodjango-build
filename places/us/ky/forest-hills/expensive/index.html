Home prices

The median home cost in Forest Hills is $198,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $32,260 (13%) lower. It is approximately $18,900 (8%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $60,600 (43%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Forest Hills's median rent is $1,438 per month. It is about $243 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $376 (35%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $700 (94%) higher.
