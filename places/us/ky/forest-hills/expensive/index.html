Home prices

The median home price in Forest Hills is $198,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $32,260 (13%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $18,900 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $60,600 (43%) higher.

Rents
