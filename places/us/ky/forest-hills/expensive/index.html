In comparison to the surrounding areas, Forest Hills is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Forest Hills is valued at $198,600. It is approximately $32,260 (13%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $18,900 (8%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $60,600 (43%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Forest Hills is $1,438 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $243 (20%) higher. It is about $376 (35%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $700 (94%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
