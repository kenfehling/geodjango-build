Home prices

The median home in Murray costs $157,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $63,620 (67%) higher. It is approximately $60,000 (27%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $3,200 (2%) higher.

Rents

Murray's median rent is $704 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $71 (11%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $358 (33%) lower, and it is about $257 (26%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
