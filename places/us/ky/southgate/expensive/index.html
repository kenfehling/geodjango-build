Rents

In Southgate the median rent is $880 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $76 (7%) lower. It is approximately $182 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $142 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
