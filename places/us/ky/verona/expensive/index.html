Home prices

The value of the median home in Verona is $200,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $46,000 (29%) higher. It is about $17,500 (8%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $62,000 (44%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Verona is $846 a month. It is approximately $154 (15%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $216 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $108 (14%) higher.
