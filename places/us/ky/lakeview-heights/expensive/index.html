Home prices

Rents

Lakeview Heights's median monthly rent is $990. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $476 (92%) higher.It is roughly $72 (6%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $252 (34%) higher.
