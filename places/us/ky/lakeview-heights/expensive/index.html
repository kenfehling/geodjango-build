Compared to the surrounding areas, Lakeview Heights is substantially more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeview Heights is valued at $159,600. This is roughly $56,460 (54%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $57,900 (26%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $21,600 (15%) higher.

Rents

In Lakeview Heights the median monthly rent is $990. Compared to nearby areas it is about $476 (92%) higher. It is about $72 (6%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $252 (34%) higher.
