Home prices

The median home in Cannonsburg is valued at $126,200. It is roughly $22,600 (21%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $91,300 (41%) lower, and is approximately $19,600 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cannonsburg is $956. This is roughly $309 (47%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $106 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $221 (30%) higher.
