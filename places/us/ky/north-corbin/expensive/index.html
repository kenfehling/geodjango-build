Home prices

The value of the median home in North Corbin is $84,600. It is about $27,800 (24%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $132,900 (61%) lower, and is roughly $105,200 (55%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median rent in North Corbin is $642 per month. This is about $8 (1%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $420 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $254 (28%) lower.
