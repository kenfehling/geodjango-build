Compared to its nearby areas, Trenton is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Clarksville, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Trenton is $125,600. This is about $17,920 (16%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $91,900 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $28,700 (18%) lower.

Rents

Trenton's median rent is $775 a month. This is roughly $93 (13%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $287 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is approximately $186 (19%) lower.
