Compared to its surrounding areas, Sonora is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the entire country. However it's a little less expensive compared to the city of Bowling Green.

Home prices

Sonora's median home cost is $120,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $10,500 (9%) higher. It is approximately $97,000 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $40,500 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Bowling Green.

Rents

Sonora's median monthly rent is $928. It is roughly $264 (39%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $134 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Bowling Green it is roughly $150 (19%) higher.
