Home prices

The price of the median home in Buffalo is $123,900. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $20,380 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $93,600 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $65,900 (34%) lower.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $618 a month. It is about $77 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $444 (41%) lower, and it is about $278 (31%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
