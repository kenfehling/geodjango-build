Home prices

The median home price in Buffalo is $123,900. This is about $20,380 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $93,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $65,900 (34%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median rent in Buffalo is $618 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $77 (11%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $444 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is about $278 (31%) lower.
