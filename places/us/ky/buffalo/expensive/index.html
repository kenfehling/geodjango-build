Home prices

The cost of the median home in Buffalo is $123,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $20,380 (19%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $93,600 (43%) lower, and is roughly $65,900 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

Buffalo's median monthly rent is $618. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $77 (11%) lower. It is approximately $444 (41%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $278 (31%) lower.
