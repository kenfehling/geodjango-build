Home prices

The median home in Lincolnshire is valued at $208,000. It is roughly $26,280 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $9,500 (4%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $18,200 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

In Lincolnshire the median monthly rent is $1,359. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $73 (5%) higher. It is about $297 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $463 (51%) higher compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
