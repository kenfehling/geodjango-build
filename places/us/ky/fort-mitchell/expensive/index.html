Rents

Fort Mitchell's median rent is $789 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $89 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $273 (25%) lower, and is roughly $51 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
