Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fort Mitchell is $272,200. It is approximately $75,420 (38%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $54,700 (25%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $134,200 (97%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Fort Mitchell the median monthly rent is $789. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $89 (10%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $273 (25%) lower, and it is about $51 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
