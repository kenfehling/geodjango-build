Home prices

The median home cost in Goshen is $204,800. It is approximately $32,300 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $12,700 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $66,800 (48%) higher.

Rents

In Goshen the median rent is $1,682 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $610 (56%) higher. It is about $620 (58%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $944 (127%) higher.
