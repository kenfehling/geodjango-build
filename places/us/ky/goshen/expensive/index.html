In comparison to its nearby areas, Goshen is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Goshen is $204,800. This is about $32,300 (13%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $12,700 (5%) lower, and it is roughly $66,800 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Goshen's median rent is $1,682 per month. This is approximately $610 (56%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $620 (58%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $944 (127%) higher.
