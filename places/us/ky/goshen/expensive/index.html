Compared to the surrounding areas, Goshen is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Goshen is $204,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $32,300 (13%) lower.It is about $12,700 (5%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $66,800 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Goshen's median rent is $1,682 a month. This is about $610 (56%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $620 (58%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $944 (127%) higher.
