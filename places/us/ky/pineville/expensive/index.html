Compared to other places in this area, Pineville is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Pineville is $89,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $39,540 (30%) lower. It is about $128,200 (58%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $47,000 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pineville is $337 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $369 (52%) lower. It is approximately $725 (68%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $508 (60%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
