Home prices

The median home value in Worthville is $46,400. This is approximately $47,020 (50%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $171,100 (78%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $91,600 (66%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Worthville is $792 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $159 (25%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $270 (25%) lower, and is about $54 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
