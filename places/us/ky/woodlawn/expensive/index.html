Home prices

The median home in Woodlawn costs $138,300. It is approximately $10,180 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $79,200 (36%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Woodlawn is $1,143 a month. It is approximately $308 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $81 (7%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $405 (54%) higher.
