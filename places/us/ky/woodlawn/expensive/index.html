Home prices

The median home in Woodlawn costs $138,300. It is about $10,180 (6%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $79,200 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is is very similar.

Rents

Woodlawn's median monthly rent is $1,143. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $308 (36%) higher. It is roughly $81 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $405 (54%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
