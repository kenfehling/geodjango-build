Home prices

The value of the median home in Bancroft is $275,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $27,080 (10%) higher. It is about $57,800 (26%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $137,300 (99%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Bancroft the median rent is $1,938 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $643 (49%) higher. It is about $876 (82%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $1,200 (162%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
