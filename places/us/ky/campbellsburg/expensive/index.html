Home prices

Campbellsburg's median home price is $111,200. It is approximately $22,580 (25%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $106,300 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $26,800 (19%) lower.

Rents

Campbellsburg's median monthly rent is $631. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $65 (9%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $431 (40%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $107 (14%) lower.
