Compared to other places in this area, Crescent Springs is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the United States as a whole. However it is significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home cost in Crescent Springs is $220,500. It is about $13,160 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $3,000 (1%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $82,500 (59%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Crescent Springs is $729 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $94 (11%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $333 (31%) lower, and is about $9 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
