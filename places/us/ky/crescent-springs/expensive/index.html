Compared to other places in this area, Crescent Springs is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Crescent Springs costs $220,500. It is roughly $13,160 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $3,000 (1%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $82,500 (59%) higher.

Rents

Crescent Springs's median rent is $729 per month. This is approximately $94 (11%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $333 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $9 (1%) lower.
