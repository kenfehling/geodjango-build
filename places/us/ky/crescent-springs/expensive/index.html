In comparison to its surrounding areas, Crescent Springs is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However it's significantly more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Crescent Springs's median home cost is $220,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $13,160 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $3,000 (1%) higher, and is roughly $82,500 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Crescent Springs's median monthly rent is $729. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $94 (11%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $333 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $9 (1%) lower.
