Home prices

The median home value in Crescent Springs is $220,500. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $13,160 (6%) higher.It is roughly $3,000 (1%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $82,500 (59%) higher.

Rents

In Crescent Springs the median rent is $729 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $94 (11%) lower.It is approximately $333 (31%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $9 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
