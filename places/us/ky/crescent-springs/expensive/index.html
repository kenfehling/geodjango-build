Compared to the surrounding areas, Crescent Springs is somewhat more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Crescent Springs costs $220,500. This is approximately $13,160 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $3,000 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $82,500 (59%) higher.

Rents

In Crescent Springs the median monthly rent is $729. This is about $94 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $333 (31%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $9 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
