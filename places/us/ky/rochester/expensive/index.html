Home prices

Rochester's median home price is $109,200. It is approximately $38,880 (55%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $108,300 (49%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is approximately $45,100 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rochester is $930 per month. This is approximately $310 (50%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $132 (12%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $31 (3%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
