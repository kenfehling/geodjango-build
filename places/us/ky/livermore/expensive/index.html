Compared to its surrounding areas, Livermore is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Clarksville, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Livermore's median home price is $78,600. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $4,680 (6%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $138,900 (63%) lower, and it is roughly $75,700 (49%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

In Livermore the median rent is $407 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $192 (32%) lower.It is approximately $655 (61%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is roughly $554 (57%) lower.
