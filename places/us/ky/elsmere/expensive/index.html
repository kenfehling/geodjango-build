Home prices

The median home in Elsmere costs $111,400. This is approximately $85,400 (43%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $106,100 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $26,600 (19%) lower.

Rents

Elsmere's median rent is $1,082 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $81 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $20 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $344 (46%) higher.
