Home prices

The cost of the median home in Midway is $186,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $28,960 (18%) higher. It is about $30,900 (14%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $48,600 (35%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Midway is $826. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $34 (4%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $236 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $88 (11%) higher.
