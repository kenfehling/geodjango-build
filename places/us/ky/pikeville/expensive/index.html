Home prices

The value of the median home in Pikeville is $182,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $126,580 (226%) higher. It is approximately $35,000 (16%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Johnson City is about $15,000 (8%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Pikeville is $666 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $101 (17%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $396 (37%) lower, and is approximately $89 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Johnson City.
