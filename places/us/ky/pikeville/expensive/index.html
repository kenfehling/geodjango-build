Home prices

The median home in Pikeville is valued at $182,500. This is about $126,580 (226%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $35,000 (16%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $15,000 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Johnson City.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pikeville is $666. This is approximately $101 (17%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $396 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Johnson City is about $89 (11%) lower.
