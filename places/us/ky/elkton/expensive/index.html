Home prices

The median home in Elkton costs $95,400. It is roughly $14,280 (13%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $122,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $58,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Elkton the median monthly rent is $678. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $43 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $384 (36%) lower, and is about $283 (29%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
