Home prices

The value of the median home in Elkton is $95,400. Compared to nearby areas it is about $14,280 (13%) lower. It is approximately $122,100 (56%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $58,900 (38%) lower.

Rents

In Elkton the median monthly rent is $678. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $43 (6%) higher. It is approximately $384 (36%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $283 (29%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
