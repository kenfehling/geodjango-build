The median home value in Tompkinsville is $73,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $8,780 (10%) lower. It is approximately $144,100 (66%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $164,600 (69%) lower compared to the city of Murfreesboro.

Rents

The median rent in Tompkinsville is $455 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $12 (2%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $607 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Murfreesboro it is about $597 (56%) lower.
