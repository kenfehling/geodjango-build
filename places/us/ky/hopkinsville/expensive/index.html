Home prices

Hopkinsville's median home price is $116,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $11,180 (10%) higher.It is approximately $100,900 (46%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $37,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

In Hopkinsville the median monthly rent is $725. It is approximately $35 (5%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $236 (24%) lower.
