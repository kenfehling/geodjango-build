Home prices

The median home in Fort Wright is valued at $201,900. It is roughly $16,160 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $15,600 (7%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $63,900 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fort Wright is $879. This is about $99 (12%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is about $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $141 (19%) higher.
