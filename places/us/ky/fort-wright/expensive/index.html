Home prices

The median home value in Fort Wright is $201,900. This is about $16,160 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $15,600 (7%) lower, and it is roughly $63,900 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Fort Wright the median monthly rent is $879. It is roughly $99 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $141 (19%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
