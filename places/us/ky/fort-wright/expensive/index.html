Home prices

The median home value in Fort Wright is $201,900. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $16,160 (8%) higher.It is approximately $15,600 (7%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $63,900 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Fort Wright the median monthly rent is $879. Compared to other places in this area it is about $99 (12%) higher.It is roughly $183 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $141 (19%) higher.
