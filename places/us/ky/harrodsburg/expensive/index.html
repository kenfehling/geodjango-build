Home prices

Harrodsburg's median home cost is $100,700. This is about $24,720 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $116,800 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $89,100 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Harrodsburg the median monthly rent is $608. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $131 (17%) lower.It is roughly $454 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $288 (32%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
