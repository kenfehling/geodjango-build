Home prices

The median home in Madisonville costs $125,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $40,880 (48%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $92,500 (42%) lower, and it is roughly $29,300 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

The median rent in Madisonville is $746 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $39 (5%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $316 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is about $215 (22%) lower.
