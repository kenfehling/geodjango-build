Home prices

The median home in Somerset costs $118,800. This is roughly $22,080 (22%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $98,700 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $71,000 (37%) lower.

Rents

Somerset's median monthly rent is $657. This is about $9 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $405 (38%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is about $239 (26%) lower.
