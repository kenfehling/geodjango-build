Home prices

The median home in Somerset costs $118,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,080 (22%) higher. It is roughly $98,700 (45%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $71,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

Somerset's median monthly rent is $657. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $9 (1%) higher. It is approximately $405 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $239 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
