Compared to other places in this area, Somerset is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Home prices

The median home value in Somerset is $118,800. This is about $22,080 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $98,700 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $71,000 (37%) lower.

Rents

Somerset's median monthly rent is $657. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $9 (1%) higher.It is about $405 (38%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $239 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
