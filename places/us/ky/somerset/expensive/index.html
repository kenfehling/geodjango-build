Compared to other places in this area, Somerset is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

Somerset's median home cost is $118,800. It is about $22,080 (22%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $98,700 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $71,000 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

In Somerset the median monthly rent is $657. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $9 (1%) higher. It is about $405 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $239 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
