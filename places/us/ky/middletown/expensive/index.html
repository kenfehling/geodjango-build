Compared to the surrounding areas, Middletown is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Middletown costs $215,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $76,760 (26%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $2,400 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $77,100 (55%) higher.

Rents

Middletown's median monthly rent is $1,103. This is roughly $214 (16%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $365 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
