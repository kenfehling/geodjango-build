Compared to its nearby areas, Middletown is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Middletown is $215,100. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $76,760 (26%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $2,400 (1%) lower, and it is approximately $77,100 (55%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middletown is $1,103. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $214 (16%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $41 (3%) higher, and is about $365 (49%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
