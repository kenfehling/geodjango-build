Compared to its surrounding areas, Middletown is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Middletown is $215,100. It is about $76,760 (26%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $2,400 (1%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $77,100 (55%) higher.

Rents

Middletown's median rent is $1,103 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $214 (16%) lower.It is roughly $41 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $365 (49%) higher.
