Home prices

The median home in Middletown costs $215,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $76,760 (26%) lower.It is approximately $2,400 (1%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $77,100 (55%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Middletown is $1,103. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $214 (16%) lower.It is roughly $41 (3%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $365 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
