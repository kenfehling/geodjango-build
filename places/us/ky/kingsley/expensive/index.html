Compared to its nearby areas, Kingsley is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette however, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kingsley is $269,300. This is roughly $30,340 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $51,800 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is roughly $79,500 (41%) higher.

Rents

In Kingsley the median rent is $1,125 a month. It is about $313 (21%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $63 (5%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $229 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
