Home prices

Rents

In Cynthiana the median monthly rent is $568. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $98 (14%) lower.It is about $494 (46%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $170 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
