Home prices

The median home in Cynthiana costs $116,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $13,500 (13%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $101,500 (46%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $22,000 (15%) lower.

Rents

In Cynthiana the median monthly rent is $568. This is roughly $98 (14%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $494 (46%) lower, and it is about $170 (23%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
