Home prices

The median home in Millersburg is valued at $100,700. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $6,880 (7%) higher. It is approximately $116,800 (53%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $37,300 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Millersburg is $610 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $74 (10%) lower. It is approximately $452 (42%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $128 (17%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
