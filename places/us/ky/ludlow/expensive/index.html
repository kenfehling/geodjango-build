Home prices

The median home in Ludlow costs $112,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $56,960 (33%) lower.It is approximately $105,500 (48%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $26,000 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ludlow is $931 per month. It is about $160 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $131 (12%) lower, and it is about $193 (26%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
