Compared to the surrounding areas, Ludlow is a lot less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Ludlow is valued at $112,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $56,960 (33%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $105,500 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $26,000 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ludlow is $931 per month. This is approximately $160 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $131 (12%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $193 (26%) higher.
