Home prices

The median home price in Ludlow is $112,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $56,960 (33%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $105,500 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $26,000 (18%) lower.

Rents

In Ludlow the median rent is $931 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $160 (20%) higher. It is approximately $131 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $193 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
