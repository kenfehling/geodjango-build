In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ludlow is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Ludlow costs $112,000. This is about $56,960 (33%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $105,500 (48%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $26,000 (18%) lower.

Rents

In Ludlow the median monthly rent is $931. It is approximately $160 (20%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $131 (12%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $193 (26%) higher.
