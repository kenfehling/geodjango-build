Home prices

The median home in Shelbyville costs $178,600. It is approximately $37,900 (26%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $38,900 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $40,600 (29%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Shelbyville's median rent is $835 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $86 (11%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $227 (21%) lower, and is about $97 (13%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
