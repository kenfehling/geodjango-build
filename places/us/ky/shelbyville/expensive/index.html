Home prices

Rents

Shelbyville's median monthly rent is $835. It is roughly $86 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $227 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $97 (13%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
