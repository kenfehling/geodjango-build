Compared to the surrounding areas, Shelbyville is a little more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a little more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Shelbyville costs $178,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $37,900 (26%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $38,900 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $40,600 (29%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Shelbyville is $835 per month. It is roughly $86 (11%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $227 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $97 (13%) higher.
