Home prices

The median home in Norwood is valued at $288,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $56,060 (24%) higher. It is roughly $70,700 (32%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $150,200 (108%) higher.

Rents

Norwood's median monthly rent is $1,444. This is approximately $191 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $382 (35%) higher, and is about $706 (95%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
