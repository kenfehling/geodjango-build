Home prices

The median home in Norwood costs $288,200. It is about $56,060 (24%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $70,700 (32%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $150,200 (108%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Norwood is $1,444. It is approximately $191 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $382 (35%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $706 (95%) higher.
