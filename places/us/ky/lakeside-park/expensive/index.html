In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lakeside Park is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Lakeside Park is $253,200. It is approximately $26,080 (11%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $35,700 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $115,200 (83%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Lakeside Park is $833 per month. This is approximately $110 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $95 (12%) higher.
