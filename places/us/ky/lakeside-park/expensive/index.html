Compared to other places in this area, Lakeside Park is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeside Park costs $253,200. This is about $26,080 (11%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $35,700 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $115,200 (83%) higher.

Rents

In Lakeside Park the median rent is $833 per month. This is roughly $110 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $229 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $95 (12%) higher.
