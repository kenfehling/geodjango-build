Home prices

The median home in Lakeside Park is valued at $253,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $26,080 (11%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $35,700 (16%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $115,200 (83%) higher.

Rents

In Lakeside Park the median monthly rent is $833. This is about $110 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $229 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $95 (12%) higher.
