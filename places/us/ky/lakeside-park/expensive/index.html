In comparison to its surrounding areas, Lakeside Park is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeside Park is valued at $253,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $26,080 (11%) higher.It is about $35,700 (16%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $115,200 (83%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakeside Park is $833. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $110 (11%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $229 (21%) lower, and it is about $95 (12%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
