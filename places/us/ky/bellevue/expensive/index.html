In comparison to the surrounding areas, Bellevue is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Cincinnatihowever, it's close to average.

Home prices

Bellevue's median home cost is $136,400. It is approximately $12,460 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $81,100 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $1,600 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Bellevue's median rent is $794 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $110 (12%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $268 (25%) lower, and is approximately $56 (7%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
