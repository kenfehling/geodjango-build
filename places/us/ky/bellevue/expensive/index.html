In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bellevue is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Bellevue is valued at $136,400. It is about $12,460 (8%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $81,100 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $1,600 (1%) lower.

Rents

Bellevue's median monthly rent is $794. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $110 (12%) lower.It is roughly $268 (25%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $56 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
