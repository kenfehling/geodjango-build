Home prices

The price of the median home in Wildwood is $236,900. It is roughly $3,300 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $19,400 (8%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $98,900 (71%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Wildwood the median rent is $1,667 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $249 (17%) higher.It is roughly $605 (56%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $929 (125%) higher.
