Home prices

The median home in Henderson costs $120,700. This is about $5,040 (4%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $96,800 (44%) lower, and it is roughly $33,600 (21%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

Henderson's median monthly rent is $685. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $224 (24%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $377 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is about $276 (28%) lower.
