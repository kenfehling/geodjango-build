Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakland is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Clarksville.

Home prices

The median home in Oakland costs $122,400. It is approximately $16,280 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $95,100 (43%) lower, and is roughly $31,900 (20%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakland is $636. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $112 (14%) lower. It is about $426 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is approximately $325 (33%) lower.
