Home prices

The value of the median home in Oakland is $122,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $16,280 (15%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $95,100 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is about $31,900 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Oakland is $636. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $112 (14%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $426 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is roughly $325 (33%) lower.
