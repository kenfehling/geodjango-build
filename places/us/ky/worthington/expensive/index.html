Home prices

The median home in Worthington is valued at $99,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $3,980 (4%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $117,900 (54%) lower, and it is about $46,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Worthington's median rent is $795 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $155 (24%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $267 (25%) lower, and is roughly $60 (8%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
