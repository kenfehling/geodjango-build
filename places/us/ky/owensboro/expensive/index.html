Compared to nearby areas, Owensboro is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of Clarksville.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Owensboro is $113,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $12,320 (12%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $104,100 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $40,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

In Owensboro the median monthly rent is $769. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $30 (4%) higher. It is roughly $293 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is about $192 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.
