In comparison to other places in this area, Owensboro is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Clarksville however, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Owensboro is $113,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $12,320 (12%) higher.It is roughly $104,100 (47%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $40,900 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Owensboro is $769. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $30 (4%) higher.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $293 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $192 (19%) lower.
