In comparison to its nearby areas, Owensboro is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Clarksville, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Owensboro is valued at $113,400. It is approximately $12,320 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $104,100 (47%) lower, and is about $40,900 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

Owensboro's median rent is $769 per month. It is roughly $30 (4%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $293 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Clarksville it is about $192 (19%) lower.
