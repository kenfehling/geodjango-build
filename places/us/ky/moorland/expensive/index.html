Home prices

The median home in Moorland is valued at $142,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $35,680 (20%) lower. It is approximately $75,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $4,200 (3%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Moorland is $668 a month. This is approximately $487 (42%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $394 (37%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $70 (9%) lower.
