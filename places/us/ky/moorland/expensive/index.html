Compared to the surrounding areas, Moorland is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Moorland is $142,200. This is about $35,680 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $75,300 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $4,200 (3%) higher.

Rents

Moorland's median rent is $668 a month. It is approximately $487 (42%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $394 (37%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $70 (9%) lower.
