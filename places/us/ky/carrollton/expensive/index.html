Home prices

The median home in Carrollton costs $136,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $55,740 (69%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $81,100 (37%) lower, and is about $1,600 (1%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Carrollton the median rent is $622 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $144 (18%) lower. It is about $440 (41%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $116 (15%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
