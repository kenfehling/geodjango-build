Compared to other places in this area, Jeffersontown is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Jeffersontown is valued at $181,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $70,540 (27%) lower. It is approximately $36,000 (16%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $43,500 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Jeffersontown is $981 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $253 (20%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $81 (7%) lower, and it is about $243 (32%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
