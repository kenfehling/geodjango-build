Home prices

The median home in Jeffersontown is valued at $181,500. This is approximately $70,540 (27%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $36,000 (16%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $43,500 (31%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Jeffersontown is $981 a month. This is roughly $253 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $81 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $243 (32%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
