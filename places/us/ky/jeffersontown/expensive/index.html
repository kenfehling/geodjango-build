Home prices

The median home cost in Jeffersontown is $181,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $70,540 (27%) lower.It is approximately $36,000 (16%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $43,500 (31%) higher.

Rents

Jeffersontown's median rent is $981 per month. It is about $253 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $81 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $243 (32%) higher.
