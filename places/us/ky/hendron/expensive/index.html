Home prices

The median home in Hendron is valued at $164,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $48,060 (41%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $52,600 (24%) lower, and is about $10,600 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

In Hendron the median monthly rent is $754. It is approximately $68 (9%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $308 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is roughly $207 (21%) lower.
