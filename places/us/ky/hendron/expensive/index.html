Home prices

The median home in Hendron is valued at $164,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $48,060 (41%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $52,600 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is roughly $10,600 (6%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hendron is $754. It is roughly $68 (9%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $308 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $207 (21%) lower.
