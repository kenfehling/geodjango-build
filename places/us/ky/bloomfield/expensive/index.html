Rents

Bloomfield's median monthly rent is $744. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $14 (1%) higher. It is roughly $318 (29%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $152 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
