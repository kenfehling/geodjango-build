Compared to other places in this area, Briarwood is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Briarwood is $148,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $49,320 (24%) lower. It is approximately $69,500 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $10,000 (7%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Briarwood is $1,316 per month. This is roughly $161 (13%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $254 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $578 (78%) higher.
