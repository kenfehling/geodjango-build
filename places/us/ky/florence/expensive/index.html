Compared to nearby areas, Florence is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Florence is $153,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $17,580 (10%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $63,800 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $15,700 (11%) higher.

Rents

Florence's median rent is $962 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $189 (16%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $100 (9%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $224 (30%) higher.
