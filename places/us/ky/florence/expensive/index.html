Compared to nearby areas, Florence is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Florence is $153,700. This is approximately $17,580 (10%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $63,800 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $15,700 (11%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Florence's median monthly rent is $962. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $189 (16%) lower. It is roughly $100 (9%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $224 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
