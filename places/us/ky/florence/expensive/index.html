Compared to nearby areas, Florence is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Florence costs $153,700. Compared to nearby areas it is about $17,580 (10%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $63,800 (29%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $15,700 (11%) higher.

Rents

Florence's median monthly rent is $962. Compared to nearby areas this is about $189 (16%) lower.It is about $100 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $224 (30%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
