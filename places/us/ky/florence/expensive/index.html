In comparison to its nearby areas, Florence is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Florence is $153,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $17,580 (10%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $63,800 (29%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $15,700 (11%) higher.

Rents

Florence's median monthly rent is $962. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $189 (16%) lower. It is approximately $100 (9%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $224 (30%) higher.
