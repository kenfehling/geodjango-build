Home prices

The price of the median home in Glencoe is $80,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $24,120 (23%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $137,500 (63%) lower, and it is roughly $58,000 (42%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Glencoe the median rent is $654 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $87 (11%) lower. It is about $408 (38%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $84 (11%) lower.
