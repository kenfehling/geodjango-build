Compared to its surrounding areas, Hebron is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

Hebron's median home price is $159,200. This is roughly $12,700 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $58,300 (26%) lower, and is roughly $21,200 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Hebron is $1,114 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $64 (6%) higher. It is about $52 (4%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $376 (50%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
