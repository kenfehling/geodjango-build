Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $152,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $48,580 (46%) higher.It is approximately $64,900 (29%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is approximately $37,200 (19%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Richmond is $687 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $42 (6%) higher.It is about $375 (35%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is about $209 (23%) lower.
