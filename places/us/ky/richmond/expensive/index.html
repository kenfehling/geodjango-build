Home prices

The median home in Richmond costs $152,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $48,580 (46%) higher.It is about $64,900 (29%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $37,200 (19%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

In Richmond the median monthly rent is $687. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $42 (6%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $375 (35%) lower, and is about $209 (23%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
