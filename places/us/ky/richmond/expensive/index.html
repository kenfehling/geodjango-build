Home prices

The median home price in Richmond is $152,600. It is about $48,580 (46%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $64,900 (29%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette is roughly $37,200 (19%) lower.

Rents

Richmond's median rent is $687 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $42 (6%) higher. It is roughly $375 (35%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $209 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
