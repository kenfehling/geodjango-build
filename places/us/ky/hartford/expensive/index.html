Compared to nearby areas, Hartford is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Clarksville.

Home prices

The median home cost in Hartford is $87,200. This is about $13,260 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $130,300 (59%) lower, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $67,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Hartford the median rent is $527 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $66 (11%) lower. It is about $535 (50%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $434 (45%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
