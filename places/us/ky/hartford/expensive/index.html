Compared to its surrounding areas, Hartford is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Clarksville, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hartford costs $87,200. This is about $13,260 (17%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $130,300 (59%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is roughly $67,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Hartford is $527. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $66 (11%) lower.It is approximately $535 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $434 (45%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
