In comparison to nearby areas, Raceland is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Raceland is $126,100. It is about $20,260 (19%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $91,400 (42%) lower, and is roughly $19,700 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Raceland's median rent is $657 per month. It is about $59 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $405 (38%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $78 (10%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
