Home prices

The price of the median home in Raceland is $126,100. This is roughly $20,260 (19%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $91,400 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $19,700 (13%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Raceland's median rent is $657 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $59 (8%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $405 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Charleston is approximately $78 (10%) lower.
