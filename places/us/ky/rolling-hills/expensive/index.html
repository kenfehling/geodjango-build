In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rolling Hills is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rolling Hills is valued at $180,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,260 (11%) lower. It is about $37,500 (17%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $42,000 (30%) higher.

Rents

Rolling Hills's median rent is $883 a month. This is roughly $438 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $179 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $145 (19%) higher.
