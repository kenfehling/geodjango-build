Home prices

The median home in Rolling Hills costs $180,000. It is roughly $22,260 (11%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $37,500 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $42,000 (30%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Rolling Hills is $883 per month. This is about $438 (33%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $179 (16%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $145 (19%) higher.
