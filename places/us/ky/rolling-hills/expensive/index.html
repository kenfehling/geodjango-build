Home prices

The value of the median home in Rolling Hills is $180,000. It is approximately $22,260 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $37,500 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $42,000 (30%) higher.

Rents

Rolling Hills's median monthly rent is $883. It is roughly $438 (33%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $179 (16%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $145 (19%) higher.
