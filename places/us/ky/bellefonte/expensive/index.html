Rents

The median monthly rent in Bellefonte is $1,313. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $515 (64%) higher.It is roughly $251 (23%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $578 (78%) higher compared to the city of Charleston.
