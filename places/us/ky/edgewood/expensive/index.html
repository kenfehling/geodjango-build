Rents

In Edgewood the median rent is $1,166 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $216 (22%) higher.It is roughly $104 (9%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $428 (57%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
