Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Edgewood is $1,166. This is roughly $216 (22%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $104 (9%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $428 (57%) higher.
