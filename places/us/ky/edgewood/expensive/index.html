Compared to its nearby areas, Edgewood is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Edgewood costs $225,600. It is roughly $27,940 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $8,100 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $87,600 (63%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Edgewood is $1,166 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $216 (22%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $104 (9%) higher, and it is about $428 (57%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
