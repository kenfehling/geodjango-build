Home prices

The median home cost in Francisville is $281,200. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $86,920 (44%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $63,700 (29%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $143,200 (103%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Francisville is $1,190. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $280 (30%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $128 (12%) higher, and it is about $452 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
