Rents

The median monthly rent in Sycamore is $1,625. This is roughly $171 (11%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $563 (53%) higher, and is approximately $887 (120%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
