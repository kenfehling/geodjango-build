Home prices

The median home price in Benton is $133,600. Compared to other places in this area it is about $19,660 (17%) higher. It is roughly $83,900 (38%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is roughly $20,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

Benton's median rent is $722 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $15 (2%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $340 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $239 (24%) lower.
