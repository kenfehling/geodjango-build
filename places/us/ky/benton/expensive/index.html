Home prices

The median home in Benton costs $133,600. Compared to nearby areas it is about $19,660 (17%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $83,900 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is approximately $20,700 (13%) lower.

Rents

Benton's median rent is $722 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $15 (2%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $340 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is about $239 (24%) lower.
