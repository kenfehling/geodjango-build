Home prices

The cost of the median home in Springfield is $120,700. It is roughly $21,480 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $96,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $69,100 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

In Springfield the median monthly rent is $588. This is roughly $127 (17%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $474 (44%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is approximately $308 (34%) lower.
