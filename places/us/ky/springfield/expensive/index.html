Compared to its surrounding areas, Springfield is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Home prices

The median home value in Springfield is $120,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $21,480 (21%) higher.It is approximately $96,800 (44%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is approximately $69,100 (36%) lower.

Rents

In Springfield the median monthly rent is $588. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $127 (17%) lower.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $474 (44%) lower, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $308 (34%) lower.
