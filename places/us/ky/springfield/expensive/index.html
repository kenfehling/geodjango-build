In comparison to the surrounding areas, Springfield is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Springfield costs $120,700. This is roughly $21,480 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $96,800 (44%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $69,100 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median rent in Springfield is $588 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $127 (17%) lower. It is approximately $474 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $308 (34%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
