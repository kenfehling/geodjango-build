Home prices

The median home in Wellington costs $253,400. It is about $13,700 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $35,900 (16%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette is approximately $63,600 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wellington is $1,450 a month. This is about $432 (42%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $388 (36%) higher, and it is approximately $554 (61%) higher in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
