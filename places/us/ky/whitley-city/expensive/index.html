Home prices

Whitley City's median home cost is $63,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $24,660 (27%) lower. It is about $153,700 (70%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $126,000 (66%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Whitley City is $559. It is approximately $15 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $503 (47%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is about $337 (37%) lower.
