Home prices

The median home value in Scottsville is $109,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $7,420 (7%) higher. It is roughly $107,700 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is approximately $44,500 (28%) lower.

Rents

Scottsville's median rent is $588 per month. This is approximately $141 (19%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $474 (44%) lower, and it is about $373 (38%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
