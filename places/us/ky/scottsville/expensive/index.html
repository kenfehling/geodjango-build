Rents

The median rent in Scottsville is $588 per month. It is about $141 (19%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $474 (44%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $373 (38%) lower.
