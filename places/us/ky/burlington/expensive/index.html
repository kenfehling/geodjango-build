Home prices

Burlington's median home cost is $168,900. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $37,920 (18%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $48,600 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $30,900 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Burlington the median rent is $988 per month. It is about $223 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $74 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $250 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
