In comparison to other places in this area, Burlington is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Burlington costs $168,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $37,920 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $48,600 (22%) lower, and it is about $30,900 (22%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Burlington is $988 per month. This is about $223 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $74 (6%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $250 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
