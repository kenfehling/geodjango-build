Home prices

Burlington's median home value is $168,900. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $37,920 (18%) lower. It is approximately $48,600 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $30,900 (22%) higher.

Rents

In Burlington the median rent is $988 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $223 (18%) lower. It is approximately $74 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $250 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
