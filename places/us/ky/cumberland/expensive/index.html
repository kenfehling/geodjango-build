Home prices

Cumberland's median home value is $47,100. This is approximately $22,040 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $170,400 (78%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is roughly $89,200 (65%) lower.

Rents

In Cumberland the median rent is $413 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $137 (24%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $649 (61%) lower, and compared to the city of Knoxville is roughly $432 (51%) lower.
