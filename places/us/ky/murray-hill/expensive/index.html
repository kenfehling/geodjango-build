Home prices

The median home in Murray Hill costs $266,700. This is approximately $52,620 (24%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $49,200 (22%) higher, and is approximately $128,700 (93%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Murray Hill the median monthly rent is $2,382. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $1,054 (79%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $1,320 (124%) higher, and is about $1,644 (222%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
