Compared to the surrounding areas, Murray Hill is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Murray Hill is valued at $266,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $52,620 (24%) higher. It is about $49,200 (22%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $128,700 (93%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Murray Hill is $2,382. Compared to other places in this area it is about $1,054 (79%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $1,320 (124%) higher, and it is roughly $1,644 (222%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
