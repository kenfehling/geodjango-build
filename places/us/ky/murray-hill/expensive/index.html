Rents

Murray Hill's median monthly rent is $2,382. It is about $1,054 (79%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is about $1,320 (124%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $1,644 (222%) higher.
