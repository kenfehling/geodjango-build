Home prices

Rents

In Benham the median monthly rent is $579. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $29 (5%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $483 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville it is approximately $266 (31%) lower.
