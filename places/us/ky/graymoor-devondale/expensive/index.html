Rents

The median monthly rent in Graymoor-Devondale is $919. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $418 (31%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $143 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is about $181 (24%) higher.
