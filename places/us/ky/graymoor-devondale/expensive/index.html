Home prices

The median home cost in Graymoor-Devondale is $248,000. It is about $6,700 (2%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $30,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $110,000 (79%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Graymoor-Devondale is $919. It is about $418 (31%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $143 (13%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $181 (24%) higher.
