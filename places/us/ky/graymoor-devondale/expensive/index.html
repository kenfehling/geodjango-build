Home prices

The price of the median home in Graymoor-Devondale is $248,000. This is approximately $6,700 (2%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $30,500 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $110,000 (79%) higher.

Rents

In Graymoor-Devondale the median rent is $919 per month. It is about $418 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $143 (13%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $181 (24%) higher.
