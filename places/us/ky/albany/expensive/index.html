In comparison to other places in this area, Albany is somewhat less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Knoxville.

Home prices

Albany's median home cost is $64,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $23,240 (26%) lower. It is about $153,200 (70%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $72,000 (52%) lower in comparison to the city of Knoxville.

Rents

The median rent in Albany is $390 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $12 (3%) lower. It is approximately $672 (63%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $455 (53%) lower compared to the city of Knoxville.
