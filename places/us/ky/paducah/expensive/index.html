Compared to its surrounding areas, Paducah is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Clarksville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Paducah is $118,400. It is about $8,840 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $99,100 (45%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is about $35,900 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Paducah is $657. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $40 (5%) lower.It is approximately $405 (38%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is approximately $304 (31%) lower.
