Compared to its surrounding areas, Paducah is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Clarksville, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Paducah is valued at $118,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $8,840 (8%) higher. It is approximately $99,100 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville it is roughly $35,900 (23%) lower.

Rents

Paducah's median rent is $657 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $40 (5%) lower. It is approximately $405 (38%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is approximately $304 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.
