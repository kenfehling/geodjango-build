Compared to other places in this area, Paducah is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Clarksville, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Paducah is $118,400. This is roughly $8,840 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $99,100 (45%) lower, and it is roughly $35,900 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Clarksville.

Rents

Paducah's median rent is $657 a month. It is approximately $40 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $405 (38%) lower, and it is approximately $304 (31%) lower compared to the city of Clarksville.
