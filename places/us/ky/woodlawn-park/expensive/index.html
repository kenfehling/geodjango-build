Home prices

The median home in Woodlawn Park is valued at $226,100. It is about $37,300 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $8,600 (3%) higher, and is approximately $88,100 (63%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

Woodlawn Park's median monthly rent is $1,391. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $222 (18%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $329 (30%) higher, and is roughly $653 (88%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
