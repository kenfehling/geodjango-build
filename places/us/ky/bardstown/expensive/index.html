The median home value in Bardstown is $123,500. It is roughly $18,220 (17%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $94,000 (43%) lower, and it is about $66,300 (34%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

Bardstown's median monthly rent is $676. This is about $10 (1%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $386 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is approximately $220 (24%) lower.
