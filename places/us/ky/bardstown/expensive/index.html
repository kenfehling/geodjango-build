Home prices

The median home price in Bardstown is $123,500. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $18,220 (17%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $94,000 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette it is about $66,300 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bardstown is $676 per month. It is roughly $10 (1%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $386 (36%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $220 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
