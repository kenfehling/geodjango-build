Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Taylorsville is $609 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $198 (24%) lower. It is about $453 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $129 (17%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.
