The median home in Northfield costs $391,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $110,220 (39%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $174,200 (80%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $253,700 (183%) higher.

Rents

In Northfield the median monthly rent is $1,154. It is approximately $375 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $92 (8%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $416 (56%) higher.
