Home prices

The cost of the median home in Brownsville is $77,500. It is approximately $22,560 (22%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $140,000 (64%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Clarksville is approximately $76,800 (49%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Brownsville is $553 per month. This is approximately $171 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is about $509 (47%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Clarksville is approximately $408 (42%) lower.
