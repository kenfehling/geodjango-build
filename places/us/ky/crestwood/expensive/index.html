In comparison to the surrounding areas, Crestwood is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a lot more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Crestwood costs $277,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $62,840 (29%) higher. It is roughly $59,900 (27%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $139,400 (101%) higher.

Rents

In Crestwood the median rent is $872 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $415 (32%) lower. It is approximately $190 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $134 (18%) higher.
