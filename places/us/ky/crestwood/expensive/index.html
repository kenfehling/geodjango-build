Home prices

Rents

In Crestwood the median rent is $872 a month. It is about $415 (32%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $190 (17%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $134 (18%) higher.
