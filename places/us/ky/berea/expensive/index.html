Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Berea is $715 per month. This is roughly $133 (22%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $347 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette is about $181 (20%) lower.
