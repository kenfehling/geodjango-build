Rents

Walton's median monthly rent is $894. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $182 (16%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $168 (15%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $156 (21%) higher.
