Home prices

The median home in Walton costs $159,900. This is roughly $23,620 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $57,600 (26%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $21,900 (15%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Walton the median monthly rent is $894. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $182 (16%) lower. It is about $168 (15%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is approximately $156 (21%) higher.
