In comparison to other places in this area, Covington is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Covington is valued at $111,500. It is roughly $62,520 (35%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $106,000 (48%) lower, and is roughly $26,500 (19%) lower compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Covington the median rent is $714 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $162 (18%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $24 (3%) lower.
