Compared to other places in this area, Covington is a lot less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Cincinnati however, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

Covington's median home value is $111,500. It is roughly $62,520 (35%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is about $106,000 (48%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $26,500 (19%) lower.

Rents

In Covington the median monthly rent is $714. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $162 (18%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $24 (3%) lower.
