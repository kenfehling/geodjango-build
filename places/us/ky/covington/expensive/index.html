Compared to its surrounding areas, Covington is significantly less expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Covington is $111,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $62,520 (35%) lower. It is approximately $106,000 (48%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $26,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Covington is $714 a month. This is about $162 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $348 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $24 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
