Home prices

Covington's median home value is $111,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $62,520 (35%) lower.It is roughly $106,000 (48%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is about $26,500 (19%) lower.

Rents

Covington's median rent is $714 a month. It is about $162 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $24 (3%) lower.
