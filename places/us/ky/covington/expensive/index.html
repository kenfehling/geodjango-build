Compared to other places in this area, Covington is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Covington's median home price is $111,500. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $62,520 (35%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $106,000 (48%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $26,500 (19%) lower.

Rents

Covington's median monthly rent is $714. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $162 (18%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $348 (32%) lower, and it is approximately $24 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
