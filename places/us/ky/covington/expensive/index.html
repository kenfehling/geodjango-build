Compared to the surrounding areas, Covington is a lot less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Covington is valued at $111,500. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $62,520 (35%) lower. It is approximately $106,000 (48%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $26,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

The median rent in Covington is $714 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $162 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $348 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $24 (3%) lower.
