Compared to other places in this area, Woodland Hills is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home in Woodland Hills is valued at $178,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $120,800 (40%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $39,100 (17%) lower, and is approximately $40,400 (29%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Woodland Hills the median rent is $1,357 per month. This is about $91 (7%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $295 (27%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $619 (83%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
