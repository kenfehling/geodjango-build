Compared to other places in this area, Woodland Hills is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Cincinnati.

Home prices

The median home price in Woodland Hills is $178,400. This is about $120,800 (40%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $39,100 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $40,400 (29%) higher.

Rents

Woodland Hills's median monthly rent is $1,357. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $91 (7%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $295 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $619 (83%) higher.
