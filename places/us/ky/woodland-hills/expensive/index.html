Home prices

The median home in Woodland Hills is valued at $178,400. This is roughly $120,800 (40%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $39,100 (17%) lower, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $40,400 (29%) higher.

Rents

In Woodland Hills the median monthly rent is $1,357. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $91 (7%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $295 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is about $619 (83%) higher.
