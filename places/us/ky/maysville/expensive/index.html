Home prices

The median home price in Maysville is $132,200. It is roughly $52,320 (65%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $85,300 (39%) lower, and it is about $5,800 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.

Rents

In Maysville the median rent is $628 a month. This is about $27 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $434 (40%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $110 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
