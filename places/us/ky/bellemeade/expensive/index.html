In comparison to other places in this area, Bellemeade is a bit less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Bellemeade costs $246,100. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $14,260 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $28,600 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $108,100 (78%) higher.

Rents

Bellemeade's median monthly rent is $1,875. This is approximately $535 (39%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $813 (76%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is about $1,137 (154%) higher.
