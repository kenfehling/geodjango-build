Home prices

The median home value in Villa Hills is $220,200. It is approximately $52,400 (31%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $2,700 (1%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati it is approximately $82,200 (59%) higher.

Rents

Villa Hills's median rent is $933 a month. It is roughly $69 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $129 (12%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $195 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
