In comparison to the surrounding areas, Villa Hills is a bit more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Cincinnati, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Villa Hills is valued at $220,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $52,400 (31%) higher. It is approximately $2,700 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Cincinnati it is roughly $82,200 (59%) higher.

Rents

In Villa Hills the median monthly rent is $933. It is approximately $69 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $129 (12%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is approximately $195 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
