Home prices

The median home in Villa Hills costs $220,200. This is roughly $52,400 (31%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $2,700 (1%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $82,200 (59%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Villa Hills is $933. This is about $69 (7%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $129 (12%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $195 (26%) higher compared to the city of Cincinnati.
