Compared to the surrounding areas, Louisa is a bit more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Charleston, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Louisa is $137,700. It is roughly $32,400 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $79,800 (36%) lower, and is approximately $8,100 (5%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

The median rent in Louisa is $553 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $169 (23%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $509 (47%) lower, and it is approximately $182 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.
