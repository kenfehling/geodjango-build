In comparison to the surrounding areas, Louisa is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Charleston however, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Louisa costs $137,700. It is roughly $32,400 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $79,800 (36%) lower, and it is about $8,100 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Charleston.

Rents

The median rent in Louisa is $553 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $169 (23%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $509 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is roughly $182 (24%) lower.
