Home prices

The price of the median home in Louisa is $137,700. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $32,400 (30%) higher. It is about $79,800 (36%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Charleston it is about $8,100 (5%) lower.

Rents

Louisa's median monthly rent is $553. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $169 (23%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $509 (47%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is about $182 (24%) lower.
