In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ashland is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Charleston.

Home prices

Ashland's median home cost is $102,800. This is approximately $27,520 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $114,700 (52%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Charleston is roughly $43,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ashland is $684 a month. It is roughly $149 (17%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $378 (35%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $51 (6%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.
