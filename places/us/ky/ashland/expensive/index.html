Compared to other places in this area, Ashland is a little less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Ashland's median home price is $102,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is approximately $32,020 (23%) lower.It is about $114,700 (52%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $43,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Ashland is $684 a month. It is about $150 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $378 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is roughly $51 (6%) lower.
