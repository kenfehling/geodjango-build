Compared to its surrounding areas, Ashland is somewhat less expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Charleston, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Ashland is $102,800. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $32,020 (23%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $114,700 (52%) lower, and it is about $43,000 (29%) lower compared to the city of Charleston.

Rents

Ashland's median rent is $684 per month. It is roughly $150 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $378 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of Charleston it is approximately $51 (6%) lower.
