Home prices

The price of the median home in Jamestown is $101,600. This is approximately $4,400 (4%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $115,900 (53%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $88,200 (46%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

Jamestown's median rent is $524 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $12 (2%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $538 (50%) lower, and it is roughly $372 (41%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
