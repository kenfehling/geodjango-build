Home prices

The median home in Jamestown costs $101,600. This is approximately $4,400 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $115,900 (53%) lower, and is roughly $88,200 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Lexington-Fayette.

Rents

In Jamestown the median rent is $524 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $12 (2%) higher. It is roughly $538 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $372 (41%) lower compared to the city of Lexington-Fayette.
