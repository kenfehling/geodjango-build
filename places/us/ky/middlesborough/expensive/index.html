Home prices

The median home value in Middlesborough is $91,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $30,960 (25%) lower.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $126,000 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Knoxville is approximately $44,800 (32%) lower.

Rents

Middlesborough's median rent is $538 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $106 (16%) lower.It is roughly $524 (49%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Knoxville is approximately $307 (36%) lower.
