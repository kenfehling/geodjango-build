The price of the median home in Winchester is $128,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $5,140 (4%) higher. It is roughly $89,300 (41%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Cincinnati is roughly $9,800 (7%) lower.

Rents

In Winchester the median rent is $698 a month. It is roughly $94 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $364 (34%) lower, and is about $40 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Cincinnati.
