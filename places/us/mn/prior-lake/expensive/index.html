Compared to other places in this area, Prior Lake is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It's a little more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Prior Lake is valued at $322,400. It is roughly $17,180 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $104,900 (48%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $70,800 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Prior Lake the median monthly rent is $1,143. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $368 (24%) lower.It is about $81 (7%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $116 (11%) higher.
