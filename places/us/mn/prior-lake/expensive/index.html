Home prices

The median home in Prior Lake costs $322,400. This is approximately $17,180 (5%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $104,900 (48%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $70,800 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Prior Lake the median rent is $1,143 per month. It is about $368 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is about $81 (7%) higher, and is roughly $116 (11%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
