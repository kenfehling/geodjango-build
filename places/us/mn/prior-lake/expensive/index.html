In comparison to other places in this area, Prior Lake is somewhat more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Prior Lake costs $322,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $17,180 (5%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $104,900 (48%) higher, and is approximately $70,800 (28%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Prior Lake the median monthly rent is $1,143. This is approximately $368 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $81 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $116 (11%) higher.
