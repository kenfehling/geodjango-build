Home prices

The value of the median home in New Germany is $150,000. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $37,520 (20%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $67,500 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $101,600 (40%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Germany is $783. Compared to other places in this area this is about $13 (1%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $279 (26%) lower, and is approximately $244 (23%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
