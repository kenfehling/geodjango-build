In comparison to the surrounding areas, North Mankato is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in North Mankato is $193,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $33,260 (20%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $24,100 (11%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $58,200 (23%) lower.

Rents

North Mankato's median monthly rent is $818. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $37 (4%) lower. It is roughly $244 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $209 (20%) lower.
