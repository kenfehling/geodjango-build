Compared to its surrounding areas, North Mankato is a bit more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Mankato costs $193,400. This is approximately $33,260 (20%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $24,100 (11%) lower, and it is about $58,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In North Mankato the median rent is $818 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $37 (4%) lower. It is roughly $244 (22%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $209 (20%) lower.
