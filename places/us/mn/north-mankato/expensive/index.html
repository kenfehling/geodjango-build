In comparison to its surrounding areas, North Mankato is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home price in North Mankato is $193,400. It is roughly $33,260 (20%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $24,100 (11%) lower, and it is about $58,200 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

North Mankato's median monthly rent is $818. It is approximately $37 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $244 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $209 (20%) lower.
