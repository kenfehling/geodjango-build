Home prices

Rents

In Utica the median monthly rent is $632. It is about $78 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $430 (40%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $342 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
