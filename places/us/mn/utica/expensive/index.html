Home prices

The median home in Utica costs $136,600. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $25,860 (15%) lower.It is about $80,900 (37%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $63,500 (31%) lower.

Rents

In Utica the median rent is $632 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $78 (10%) lower.It is approximately $430 (40%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is approximately $342 (35%) lower.
