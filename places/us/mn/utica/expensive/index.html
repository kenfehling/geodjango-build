Home prices

The median home price in Utica is $136,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $25,860 (15%) lower. It is roughly $80,900 (37%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $63,500 (31%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Utica is $632. This is approximately $78 (10%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $430 (40%) lower, and is about $342 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
