The median home value in Utica is $136,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $25,860 (15%) lower. It is approximately $80,900 (37%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester it is roughly $63,500 (31%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Utica is $632. It is roughly $78 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $430 (40%) lower, and is roughly $342 (35%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
