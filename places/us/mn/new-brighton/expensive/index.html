In comparison to its surrounding areas, New Brighton is a lot more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in New Brighton is $256,600. This is approximately $65,300 (34%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $39,100 (17%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $5,000 (1%) higher.

Rents

In New Brighton the median rent is $1,038 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $41 (3%) lower. It is approximately $24 (2%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
