Home prices

The median home in Jenkins is valued at $162,500. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $64,960 (28%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $55,000 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is about $7,000 (4%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Jenkins is $763. It is roughly $114 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $299 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is roughly $39 (4%) lower.
