The value of the median home in Jenkins is $162,500. This is about $64,960 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $55,000 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is roughly $7,000 (4%) higher.

Rents

Jenkins's median monthly rent is $763. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $114 (12%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is about $39 (4%) lower.
