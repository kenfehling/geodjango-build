In comparison to its surrounding areas, South St. Paul is a little less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

South St. Paul's median home price is $192,100. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $54,440 (22%) lower.It is roughly $25,400 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $59,500 (23%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in South St. Paul is $937 per month. This is roughly $300 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $125 (11%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $90 (8%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
