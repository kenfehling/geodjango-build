Home prices

The median home in South St. Paul is valued at $192,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $54,440 (22%) lower.It is roughly $25,400 (11%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $59,500 (23%) lower.

Rents

South St. Paul's median monthly rent is $937. This is about $300 (24%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $125 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $90 (8%) lower.
