In comparison to its nearby areas, South St. Paul is somewhat less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in South St. Paul costs $192,100. It is approximately $54,440 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $25,400 (11%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $59,500 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in South St. Paul is $937 a month. It is about $300 (24%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $125 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $90 (8%) lower.
