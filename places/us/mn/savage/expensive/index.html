Home prices

The median home in Savage costs $307,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $27,940 (9%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $90,000 (41%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $55,900 (22%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Savage is $1,286. This is roughly $62 (5%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is about $224 (21%) higher, and is approximately $259 (25%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
