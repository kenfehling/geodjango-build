In comparison to its surrounding areas, Savage is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Savage is valued at $307,500. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $27,940 (9%) higher. It is about $90,000 (41%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $55,900 (22%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Savage is $1,286. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $62 (5%) higher. It is about $224 (21%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $259 (25%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
