In comparison to other places in this area, Glenwood is somewhat more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of St. Cloud however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Glenwood costs $148,800. This is approximately $28,380 (23%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $68,700 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $6,700 (4%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Glenwood's median monthly rent is $544. It is approximately $140 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $518 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $258 (32%) lower.
