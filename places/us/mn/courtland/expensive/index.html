Home prices

Rents

In Courtland the median monthly rent is $915. This is about $220 (31%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $112 (10%) lower.
