Home prices

The median home cost in Breezy Point is $250,800. Compared to other places in this area this is about $5,340 (2%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $33,300 (15%) higher, and it is roughly $95,300 (61%) higher compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median rent in Breezy Point is $1,288 a month. It is approximately $476 (58%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $226 (21%) higher, and compared to the city of St. Cloud is approximately $486 (60%) higher.
