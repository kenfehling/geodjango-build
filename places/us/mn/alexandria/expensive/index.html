In comparison to other places in this area, Alexandria is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Alexandria costs $177,100. Compared to other places in this area this is about $44,740 (33%) higher.It is roughly $40,400 (18%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $21,600 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Alexandria's median monthly rent is $712. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13 (1%) lower.It is approximately $350 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $90 (11%) lower.
