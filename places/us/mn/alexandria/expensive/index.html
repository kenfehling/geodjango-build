Home prices

The median home cost in Alexandria is $177,100. This is approximately $44,740 (33%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $40,400 (18%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $21,600 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Alexandria's median rent is $712 per month. It is roughly $13 (1%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $350 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is about $90 (11%) lower.
