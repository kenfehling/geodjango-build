Home prices

The median home in Alexandria is valued at $177,100. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $44,740 (33%) higher. It is about $40,400 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $21,600 (13%) higher compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Alexandria is $712. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $13 (1%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $350 (32%) lower, and it is about $90 (11%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
