Home prices

Alexandria's median home price is $177,100. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $44,740 (33%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $40,400 (18%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud is roughly $21,600 (13%) higher.

Rents

In Alexandria the median rent is $712 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $13 (1%) lower.It is roughly $350 (32%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $90 (11%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
