Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Winnebago is $635. It is roughly $51 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $427 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $543 (46%) lower.
