Home prices

Winnebago's median home price is $63,300. It is roughly $6,100 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $154,200 (70%) lower, and compared to the city of Bloomington it is roughly $188,000 (74%) lower.

Rents

In Winnebago the median monthly rent is $635. It is approximately $51 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $427 (40%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington it is approximately $543 (46%) lower.
