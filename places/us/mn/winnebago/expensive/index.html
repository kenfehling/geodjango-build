Home prices

The median home in Winnebago is valued at $63,300. This is approximately $6,100 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $154,200 (70%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Bloomington is roughly $188,000 (74%) lower.

Rents

In Winnebago the median rent is $635 per month. This is about $51 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $427 (40%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Bloomington is about $543 (46%) lower.
