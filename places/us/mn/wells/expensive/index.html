Compared to its surrounding areas, Wells is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Wells is $83,800. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $5,220 (6%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $133,700 (61%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $167,800 (66%) lower.

Rents

Wells's median rent is $604 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $27 (4%) lower. It is roughly $458 (43%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $423 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
