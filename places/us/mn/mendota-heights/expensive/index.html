Home prices

Rents

Mendota Heights's median monthly rent is $1,127. This is approximately $78 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $65 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $100 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
