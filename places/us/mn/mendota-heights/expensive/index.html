In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mendota Heights is substantially more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Mendota Heights is $392,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $136,260 (53%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $175,100 (80%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $141,000 (56%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Mendota Heights is $1,127 a month. This is about $78 (6%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $65 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $100 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
