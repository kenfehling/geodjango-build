Home prices

The median home in Mendota Heights costs $392,600. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $136,260 (53%) higher. It is approximately $175,100 (80%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $141,000 (56%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Mendota Heights's median rent is $1,127 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $78 (6%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $65 (6%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $100 (9%) higher.
