Rents

The median rent in Arlington is $714 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $13 (1%) lower. It is roughly $348 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $313 (30%) lower.
