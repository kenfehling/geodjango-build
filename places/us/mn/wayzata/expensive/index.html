Home prices

The median home cost in Wayzata is $523,600. This is about $40,580 (7%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is about $306,100 (140%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $272,000 (108%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Wayzata is $1,163. It is roughly $331 (22%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $101 (9%) higher, and is roughly $136 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
