Home prices

The median home in Wayzata is valued at $523,600. It is roughly $40,580 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $306,100 (140%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $272,000 (108%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Wayzata's median monthly rent is $1,163. It is approximately $331 (22%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $101 (9%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $136 (13%) higher.
