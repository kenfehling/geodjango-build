In comparison to the surrounding areas, Wayzata is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. It's significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Wayzata is valued at $523,600. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $40,580 (7%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $306,100 (140%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $272,000 (108%) higher.

Rents

Wayzata's median monthly rent is $1,163. It is roughly $331 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $101 (9%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $136 (13%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
