Home prices

The median home in Ramsey costs $251,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $22,800 (8%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $34,400 (15%) higher, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Ramsey the median rent is $1,399 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $82 (6%) higher.It is about $337 (31%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $372 (36%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
