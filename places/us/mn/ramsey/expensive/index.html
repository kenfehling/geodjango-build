In comparison to the surrounding areas, Ramsey is a little less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Ramsey's median home cost is $251,900. This is about $22,800 (8%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $34,400 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is is very similar.

Rents

In Ramsey the median monthly rent is $1,399. It is about $82 (6%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $337 (31%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $372 (36%) higher.
