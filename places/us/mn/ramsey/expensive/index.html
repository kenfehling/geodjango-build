Rents

Ramsey's median monthly rent is $1,399. This is roughly $82 (6%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $337 (31%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $372 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
