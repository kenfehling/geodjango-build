Home prices

The median home price in Ramsey is $251,900. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $22,800 (8%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $34,400 (15%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Ramsey is $1,399 a month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $82 (6%) higher. It is approximately $337 (31%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $372 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
