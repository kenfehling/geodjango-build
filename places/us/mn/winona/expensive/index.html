Compared to its surrounding areas, Winona is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Rochester, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Winona is valued at $148,800. This is roughly $8,360 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $68,700 (31%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $51,300 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Winona's median rent is $672 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $187 (21%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $390 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester it is roughly $302 (31%) lower.
