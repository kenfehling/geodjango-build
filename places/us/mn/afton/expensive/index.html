Compared to other places in this area, Afton is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Afton is $475,000. This is approximately $201,420 (73%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $257,500 (118%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $223,400 (88%) higher.

Rents

In Afton the median rent is $1,292 per month. It is about $102 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $230 (21%) higher, and it is about $265 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
