Compared to nearby areas, Lake City is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Lake City costs $169,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $36,400 (27%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $47,900 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $82,000 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Lake City is $845 a month. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $111 (15%) higher.It is approximately $217 (20%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $182 (17%) lower.
