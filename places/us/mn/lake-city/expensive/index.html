In comparison to nearby areas, Lake City is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lake City is $169,600. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $36,400 (27%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $47,900 (22%) lower, and it is about $82,000 (32%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Lake City the median rent is $845 a month. It is about $111 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $217 (20%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $182 (17%) lower.
