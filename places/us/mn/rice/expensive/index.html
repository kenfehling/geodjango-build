Home prices

The median home price in Rice is $167,800. This is about $16,520 (10%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $49,700 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $83,800 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rice is $671 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $76 (10%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $391 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $356 (34%) lower.
