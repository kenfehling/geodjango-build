Compared to other places in this area, La Prairie is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Duluth.

Home prices

The value of the median home in La Prairie is $146,000. This is approximately $7,420 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $71,500 (32%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $17,300 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Duluth.

Rents

The median rent in La Prairie is $636 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $107 (14%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $426 (40%) lower, and it is about $184 (22%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
