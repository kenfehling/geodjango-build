Compared to the surrounding areas, La Prairie is a little more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Duluth however, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in La Prairie is valued at $146,000. It is about $7,420 (5%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $71,500 (32%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Duluth is roughly $17,300 (10%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in La Prairie is $636 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $107 (14%) lower. It is approximately $426 (40%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Duluth is about $184 (22%) lower.
