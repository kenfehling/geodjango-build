Home prices

La Prairie's median home price is $146,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $7,420 (5%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $71,500 (32%) lower, and it is roughly $17,300 (10%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.

Rents

The median rent in La Prairie is $636 per month. This is about $107 (14%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $426 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of Duluth is roughly $184 (22%) lower.
