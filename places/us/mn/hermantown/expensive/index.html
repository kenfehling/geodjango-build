Home prices

The median home in Hermantown is valued at $238,800. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $62,140 (35%) higher.It is roughly $21,300 (9%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is about $75,500 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Hermantown the median rent is $1,095 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $52 (4%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $33 (3%) higher, and is roughly $275 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Duluth.
