Compared to other places in this area, Hermantown is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Duluth, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

Hermantown's median home cost is $238,800. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $62,140 (35%) higher. It is about $21,300 (9%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $75,500 (46%) higher compared to the city of Duluth.

Rents

The median rent in Hermantown is $1,095 a month. It is about $52 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $33 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $275 (33%) higher compared to the city of Duluth.
