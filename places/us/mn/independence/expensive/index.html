Home prices

Independence's median home value is $502,900. This is roughly $227,260 (82%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $285,400 (131%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $251,300 (99%) higher.

Rents

Independence's median monthly rent is $1,637. It is about $697 (74%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $575 (54%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $610 (59%) higher.
