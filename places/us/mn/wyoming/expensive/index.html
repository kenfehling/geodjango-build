Home prices

The median home in Wyoming costs $242,600. It is about $5,300 (2%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $25,100 (11%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $9,000 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Wyoming is $1,096 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $108 (10%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $34 (3%) higher, and is roughly $69 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
