Home prices

The median home in Watertown is valued at $208,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $136,560 (39%) lower. It is about $9,200 (4%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $43,300 (17%) lower.

Rents

Watertown's median monthly rent is $694. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $923 (57%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $368 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $333 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
