Compared to nearby areas, Chaska is significantly less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Chaska's median home value is $266,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $183,520 (40%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $48,700 (22%) higher, and is roughly $14,600 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Chaska's median rent is $1,178 per month. It is approximately $358 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $116 (10%) higher, and is about $151 (14%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
