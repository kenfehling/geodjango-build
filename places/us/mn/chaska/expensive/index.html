Home prices

The median home price in Chaska is $266,200. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $183,520 (40%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $48,700 (22%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $14,600 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Chaska the median rent is $1,178 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $358 (23%) lower.It is approximately $116 (10%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $151 (14%) higher.
