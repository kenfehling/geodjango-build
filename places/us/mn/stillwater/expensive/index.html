Compared to other places in this area, Stillwater is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a little more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Stillwater's median home price is $291,400. This is about $17,820 (6%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $73,900 (33%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $39,800 (15%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Stillwater's median monthly rent is $1,014. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $307 (23%) lower. It is about $48 (4%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $13 (1%) lower.
