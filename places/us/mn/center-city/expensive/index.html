Compared to nearby areas, Center City is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. However it is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Center City costs $225,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $30,980 (15%) higher.It is approximately $7,500 (3%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $26,600 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Center City the median rent is $987 a month. This is about $108 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $75 (7%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $40 (3%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
