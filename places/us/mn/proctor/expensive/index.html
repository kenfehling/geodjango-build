Home prices

The median home in Proctor is valued at $147,000. This is approximately $38,180 (20%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $70,500 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Duluth is roughly $16,300 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Proctor is $653 per month. It is approximately $479 (42%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $409 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $167 (20%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
