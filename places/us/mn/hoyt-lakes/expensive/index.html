Home prices

Hoyt Lakes's median home value is $69,600. This is about $9,540 (12%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $147,900 (68%) lower, and compared to the city of Duluth it is roughly $93,700 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Hoyt Lakes is $615 per month. It is roughly $14 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $447 (42%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is roughly $205 (25%) lower.
