Compared to nearby areas, St. Cloud is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Cloud is $155,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $28,860 (15%) lower. It is approximately $62,000 (28%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $96,100 (38%) lower.

Rents

In St. Cloud the median monthly rent is $802. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $54 (6%) lower. It is approximately $260 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $225 (21%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
