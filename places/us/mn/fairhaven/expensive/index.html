Home prices

The median home in Fairhaven costs $157,700. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $9,240 (5%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $59,800 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $93,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Fairhaven the median rent is $927 a month. This is roughly $135 (17%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $135 (12%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $100 (9%) lower.
