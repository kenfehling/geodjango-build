Rents

The median rent in Sacred Heart is $652 a month. This is approximately $89 (15%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $410 (38%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is approximately $150 (18%) lower.
