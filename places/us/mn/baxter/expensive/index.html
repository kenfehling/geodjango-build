Home prices

The median home price in Baxter is $208,500. Compared to other places in this area this is about $34,240 (14%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $9,000 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $53,000 (34%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Baxter is $998. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $121 (13%) higher.It is about $64 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is about $196 (24%) higher compared to the city of St. Cloud.
