Home prices

The median home in Baxter is valued at $208,500. This is approximately $34,240 (14%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $9,000 (4%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $53,000 (34%) higher compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Baxter's median monthly rent is $998. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $121 (13%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $64 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is about $196 (24%) higher.
