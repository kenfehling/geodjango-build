In comparison to its surrounding areas, Baxter is a little less expensive, and it is similar compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Baxter is $208,500. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $34,240 (14%) lower. It is approximately $9,000 (4%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is about $53,000 (34%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Baxter is $998 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $121 (13%) higher. It is roughly $64 (6%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is roughly $196 (24%) higher.
