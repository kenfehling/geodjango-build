Home prices

The median home in Mendota costs $231,300. Compared to nearby areas it is about $60,480 (20%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $13,800 (6%) higher, and it is roughly $20,300 (8%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Mendota's median rent is $875 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $386 (30%) lower. It is approximately $187 (17%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $152 (14%) lower.
