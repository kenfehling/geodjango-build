The median home in Maplewood costs $217,300. This is approximately $25,900 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is is very similar compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $34,300 (13%) lower.

Rents

In Maplewood the median rent is $1,133 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $20 (1%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $71 (6%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $106 (10%) higher.
