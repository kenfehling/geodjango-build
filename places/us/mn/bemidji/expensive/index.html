Home prices

Bemidji's median home price is $139,900. It is roughly $16,650 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $77,600 (35%) lower compared to the entire country.

Rents

The median rent in Bemidji is $787 per month. It is about $69 (9%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $275 (25%) lower.
