Home prices

The value of the median home in Upsala is $137,500. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $32,740 (31%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $80,000 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is about $18,000 (11%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Upsala is $675. This is roughly $52 (8%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $387 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud is about $127 (15%) lower.
