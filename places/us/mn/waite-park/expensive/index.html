Home prices

The median home cost in Waite Park is $151,700. This is roughly $50,360 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $65,800 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $99,900 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Waite Park the median rent is $844 per month. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas.It is about $218 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $183 (17%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
