Compared to other places in this area, Albertville is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Albertville costs $250,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $21,820 (8%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $32,900 (15%) higher, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Albertville the median rent is $970 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $73 (6%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $92 (8%) lower, and it is approximately $57 (5%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
