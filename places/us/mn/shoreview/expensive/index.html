Home prices

Rents

Shoreview's median monthly rent is $1,214. This is approximately $234 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $152 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $187 (18%) higher.
