Home prices

The cost of the median home in Shoreview is $275,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $50,600 (15%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $58,300 (26%) higher, and is approximately $24,200 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Shoreview's median rent is $1,214 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $234 (16%) lower. It is approximately $152 (14%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $187 (18%) higher.
