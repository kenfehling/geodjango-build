Home prices

The price of the median home in Faribault is $153,500. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $17,540 (10%) lower. It is roughly $64,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $98,100 (38%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Faribault's median rent is $822 per month. This is about $28 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $240 (22%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $205 (19%) lower.
