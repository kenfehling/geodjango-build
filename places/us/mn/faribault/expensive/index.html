Compared to other places in this area, Faribault is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home cost in Faribault is $153,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $17,540 (10%) lower.It is roughly $64,000 (29%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $98,100 (38%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Faribault the median monthly rent is $822. This is roughly $28 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $240 (22%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $205 (19%) lower.
