Compared to other places in this area, Maple Plain is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Plain costs $248,900. This is roughly $218,820 (46%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $31,400 (14%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $2,700 (1%) lower.

Rents

In Maple Plain the median rent is $915 a month. It is approximately $293 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is about $147 (13%) lower, and is roughly $112 (10%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
