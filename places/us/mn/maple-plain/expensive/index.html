Home prices

The median home cost in Maple Plain is $248,900. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $218,820 (46%) lower.It is approximately $31,400 (14%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $2,700 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Maple Plain is $915 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $293 (24%) lower.It is about $147 (13%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $112 (10%) lower.
