In comparison to the surrounding areas, Maple Plain is significantly less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Maple Plain is $248,900. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $218,820 (46%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $31,400 (14%) higher, and it is about $2,700 (1%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Maple Plain's median monthly rent is $915. This is roughly $293 (24%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $147 (13%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $112 (10%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
