Home prices

The median home in Fairfax costs $78,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $13,000 (19%) higher. It is about $139,100 (63%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $77,100 (49%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $470. It is approximately $130 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $592 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is about $332 (41%) lower.
