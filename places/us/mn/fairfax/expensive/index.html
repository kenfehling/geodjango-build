Compared to the surrounding areas, Fairfax is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Fairfax is $78,400. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $13,000 (19%) higher. It is approximately $139,100 (63%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and it is about $77,100 (49%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fairfax is $470. It is about $130 (21%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $592 (55%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $332 (41%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
