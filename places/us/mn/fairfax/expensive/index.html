In comparison to the surrounding areas, Fairfax is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Home prices

The median home in Fairfax costs $78,400. It is about $13,000 (19%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $139,100 (63%) lower, and is approximately $77,100 (49%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median rent in Fairfax is $470 a month. It is approximately $130 (21%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $592 (55%) lower, and is roughly $332 (41%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
