Home prices

The median home price in St. Charles is $168,300. It is about $9,140 (5%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $49,200 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Paul is about $39,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

In St. Charles the median rent is $611 a month. This is roughly $70 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $451 (42%) lower, and it is approximately $357 (36%) lower compared to the city of St. Paul.
