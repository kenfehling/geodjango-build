Compared to other places in this area, St. Charles is somewhat more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of St. Paul.

Home prices

St. Charles's median home cost is $168,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $9,140 (5%) higher. It is roughly $49,200 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Paul it is approximately $39,700 (19%) lower.

Rents

St. Charles's median rent is $611 a month. This is about $70 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $451 (42%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Paul it is roughly $357 (36%) lower.
