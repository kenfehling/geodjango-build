In comparison to the surrounding areas, Austin is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Austin costs $107,800. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $24,360 (29%) higher.It is about $109,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $143,800 (57%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Austin's median rent is $738 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is about $91 (14%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $324 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $289 (28%) lower.
