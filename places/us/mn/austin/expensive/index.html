Compared to other places in this area, Austin is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Austin is $107,800. It is roughly $24,360 (29%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $109,700 (50%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $143,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

Austin's median monthly rent is $738. This is roughly $91 (14%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $324 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $289 (28%) lower.
