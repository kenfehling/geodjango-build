In comparison to the surrounding areas, Austin is a little more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Austin is valued at $107,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $24,360 (29%) higher.It is roughly $109,700 (50%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $143,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Austin is $738 a month. It is roughly $91 (14%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $324 (30%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $289 (28%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
