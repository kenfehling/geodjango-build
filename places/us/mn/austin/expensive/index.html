Compared to other places in this area, Austin is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Austin is $107,800. Compared to nearby areas this is about $24,360 (29%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $109,700 (50%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $143,800 (57%) lower.

Rents

In Austin the median rent is $738 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $91 (14%) higher.It is approximately $324 (30%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $289 (28%) lower.
