In comparison to nearby areas, Austin is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Austin is $107,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $24,360 (29%) higher. It is roughly $109,700 (50%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is approximately $143,800 (57%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Austin the median monthly rent is $738. It is roughly $91 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $324 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is about $289 (28%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
