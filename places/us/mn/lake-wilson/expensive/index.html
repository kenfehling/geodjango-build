Home prices

The median home value in Lake Wilson is $57,300. It is roughly $14,540 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $160,200 (73%) lower, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $132,500 (69%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Wilson is $631. This is roughly $17 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $431 (40%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $196 (23%) lower.
