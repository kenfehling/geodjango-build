Home prices

The price of the median home in Paynesville is $129,400. This is roughly $6,300 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $88,100 (40%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Cloud is roughly $26,100 (16%) lower.

Rents

Paynesville's median rent is $642 a month. This is roughly $60 (8%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $420 (39%) lower, and it is approximately $160 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.
