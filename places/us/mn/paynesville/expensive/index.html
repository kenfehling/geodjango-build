Home prices

Paynesville's median home price is $129,400. This is about $6,300 (4%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $88,100 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is roughly $26,100 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

In Paynesville the median rent is $642 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $60 (8%) lower. It is roughly $420 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is about $160 (19%) lower.
