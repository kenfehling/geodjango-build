Compared to other places in this area, Nashwauk is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Duluth.

Home prices

Nashwauk's median home cost is $87,600. It is about $5,780 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $129,900 (59%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is about $75,700 (46%) lower.

Rents

In Nashwauk the median rent is $777 per month. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $129 (19%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $285 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Duluth it is approximately $43 (5%) lower.
