Compared to its surrounding areas, Hutchinson is significantly more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Hutchinson's median home price is $149,200. It is about $41,780 (38%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is about $68,300 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $102,400 (40%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Hutchinson the median monthly rent is $835. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $133 (18%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $227 (21%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $192 (18%) lower.
