Rents

In Hutchinson the median monthly rent is $835. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $133 (18%) higher.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $227 (21%) lower, and is approximately $192 (18%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
