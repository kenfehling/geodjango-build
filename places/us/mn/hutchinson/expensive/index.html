Home prices

The median home cost in Hutchinson is $149,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $41,780 (38%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $68,300 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $102,400 (40%) lower.

Rents

Hutchinson's median monthly rent is $835. It is approximately $133 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $227 (21%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $192 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
