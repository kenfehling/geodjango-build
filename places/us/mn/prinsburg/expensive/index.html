Home prices

The median home cost in Prinsburg is $132,700. It is approximately $49,180 (58%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $84,800 (38%) lower, and it is about $22,800 (14%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Prinsburg's median monthly rent is $733. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $82 (12%) higher. It is approximately $329 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $69 (8%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.
