Home prices

Rush City's median home price is $152,200. This is roughly $20,080 (11%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $65,300 (30%) lower, and is about $99,400 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Rush City is $827 per month. It is roughly $80 (10%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $235 (22%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $200 (19%) lower.
