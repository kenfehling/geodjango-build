Home prices

The median home in Moose Lake is valued at $122,900. This is about $7,120 (6%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $94,600 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $40,400 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Duluth.

Rents

The median rent in Moose Lake is $699 a month. It is approximately $102 (12%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $363 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is roughly $121 (14%) lower.
