Home prices

The median home cost in Cleveland is $151,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $4,860 (3%) lower. It is approximately $66,100 (30%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $100,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $625 per month. It is about $92 (12%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $402 (39%) lower.
