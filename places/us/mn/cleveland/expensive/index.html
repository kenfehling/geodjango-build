Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $151,400. It is roughly $4,860 (3%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $66,100 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $100,200 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Cleveland the median monthly rent is $625. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $92 (12%) lower.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $402 (39%) lower.
