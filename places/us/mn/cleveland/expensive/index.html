Home prices

The median home in Cleveland is valued at $151,400. It is approximately $4,860 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $66,100 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $100,200 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Cleveland the median rent is $625 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $92 (12%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $437 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $402 (39%) lower.
