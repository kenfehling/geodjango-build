Home prices

Cleveland's median home price is $151,400. This is about $4,860 (3%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $66,100 (30%) lower, and is about $100,200 (39%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Cleveland is $625 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $92 (12%) lower. It is about $437 (41%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $402 (39%) lower.
