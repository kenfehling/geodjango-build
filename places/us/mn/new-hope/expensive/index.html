The median home in New Hope costs $225,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $128,360 (36%) lower. It is roughly $7,600 (3%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $26,500 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

New Hope's median rent is $1,034 per month. This is about $112 (9%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $28 (2%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is is very similar.
