In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dennison is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Dennison costs $165,600. It is about $25,760 (13%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $51,900 (23%) lower, and is about $86,000 (34%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Dennison the median monthly rent is $1,188. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $298 (33%) higher. It is approximately $126 (11%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $161 (15%) higher.
