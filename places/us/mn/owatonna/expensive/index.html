Home prices

The median home in Owatonna costs $166,600. This is approximately $37,000 (28%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is about $50,900 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $85,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Owatonna is $773 a month. This is is very similar in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $289 (27%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $254 (24%) lower.
