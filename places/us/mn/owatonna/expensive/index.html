In comparison to its surrounding areas, Owatonna is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Owatonna is $166,600. It is about $37,000 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $50,900 (23%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $85,000 (33%) lower.

Rents

Owatonna's median monthly rent is $773. This is is very similar compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $289 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $254 (24%) lower.
