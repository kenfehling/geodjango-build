Home prices

The median home in Vineland is valued at $137,500. It is about $16,660 (13%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $80,000 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $114,100 (45%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Vineland is $608 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $177 (22%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $454 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $419 (40%) lower.
