Home prices

Rents

In Vineland the median rent is $608 a month. It is about $177 (22%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $454 (42%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $419 (40%) lower.
