Home prices

The median home in St. Bonifacius costs $252,500. Compared to nearby areas this is about $126,540 (33%) lower. It is about $35,000 (16%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is is very similar compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

St. Bonifacius's median monthly rent is $1,595. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $119 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $533 (50%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $568 (55%) higher.
