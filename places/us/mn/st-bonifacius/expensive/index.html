Compared to other places in this area, St. Bonifacius is substantially less expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is close to average.

Home prices

St. Bonifacius's median home value is $252,500. This is approximately $126,540 (33%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $35,000 (16%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in St. Bonifacius is $1,595 a month. It is roughly $119 (8%) higher compared to nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $533 (50%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $568 (55%) higher.
