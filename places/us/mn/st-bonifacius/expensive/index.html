In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Bonifacius is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Bonifacius is $252,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $126,540 (33%) lower.It is roughly $35,000 (16%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is is very similar.

Rents

In St. Bonifacius the median monthly rent is $1,595. It is approximately $119 (8%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $533 (50%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $568 (55%) higher.
