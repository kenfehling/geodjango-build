Rents

The median rent in St. Bonifacius is $1,595 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $119 (8%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $533 (50%) higher, and is roughly $568 (55%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
