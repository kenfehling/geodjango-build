Rents

The median rent in Rollingstone is $675 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $197 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $387 (36%) lower, and is approximately $299 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
