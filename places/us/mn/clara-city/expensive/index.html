Compared to the surrounding areas, Clara City is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Clara City is $117,500. It is roughly $27,020 (29%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $100,000 (45%) lower, and is roughly $38,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Clara City's median rent is $685 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $43 (6%) higher. It is roughly $377 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is about $117 (14%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
