Home prices

The cost of the median home in Clara City is $117,500. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $27,020 (29%) higher.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $100,000 (45%) lower, and it is about $38,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Clara City's median rent is $685 a month. It is approximately $43 (6%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $377 (35%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is about $117 (14%) lower.
