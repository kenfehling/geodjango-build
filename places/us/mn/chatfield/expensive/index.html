Rents

In Chatfield the median monthly rent is $722. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $55 (8%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $340 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Paul is approximately $246 (25%) lower.
