In comparison to its nearby areas, Houston is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's substantially less expensive compared to the city of Rochester.

Home prices

The median home cost in Houston is $103,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $39,080 (27%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $113,900 (52%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $96,500 (48%) lower.

Rents

In Houston the median rent is $639 a month. This is about $52 (7%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $423 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is roughly $335 (34%) lower.
