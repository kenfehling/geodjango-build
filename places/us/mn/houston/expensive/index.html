Home prices

Houston's median home cost is $103,600. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $39,080 (27%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $113,900 (52%) lower, and compared to the city of Rochester is approximately $96,500 (48%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Houston is $639. This is about $52 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $423 (39%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $335 (34%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
