Home prices

Houston's median home price is $103,600. It is roughly $39,080 (27%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $113,900 (52%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is about $96,500 (48%) lower.

Rents

Houston's median rent is $639 per month. This is about $52 (7%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $423 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $335 (34%) lower.
