In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hanover is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hanover is valued at $297,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $25,360 (7%) lower.It is roughly $80,300 (36%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $46,200 (18%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Hanover is $589 per month. It is about $652 (52%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $473 (44%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $438 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
