Home prices

The median home in Hanover costs $297,800. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $25,360 (7%) lower.It is about $80,300 (36%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $46,200 (18%) higher.

Rents

In Hanover the median rent is $589 a month. Compared to other places in this area this is about $652 (52%) lower.It is about $473 (44%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $438 (42%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
