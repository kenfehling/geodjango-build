Home prices

The cost of the median home in Hanover is $297,800. This is roughly $25,360 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $80,300 (36%) higher, and it is roughly $46,200 (18%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Hanover the median monthly rent is $589. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $652 (52%) lower.It is approximately $473 (44%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $438 (42%) lower.
