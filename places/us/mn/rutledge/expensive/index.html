Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rutledge is $792. This is about $124 (18%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $270 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $235 (22%) lower.
