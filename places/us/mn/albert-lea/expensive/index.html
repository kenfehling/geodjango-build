Compared to other places in this area, Albert Lea is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Albert Lea costs $97,100. This is approximately $20,780 (27%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $120,400 (55%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $154,500 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Albert Lea the median rent is $687 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $120 (21%) higher. It is roughly $375 (35%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $340 (33%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
