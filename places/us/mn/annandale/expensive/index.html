Home prices

Rents

In Annandale the median monthly rent is $572. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $221 (27%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $490 (46%) lower, and it is about $455 (44%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
