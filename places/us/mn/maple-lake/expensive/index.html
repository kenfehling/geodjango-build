Home prices

The median home price in Maple Lake is $161,700. It is about $17,320 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $55,800 (25%) lower, and is roughly $89,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Maple Lake is $721. It is approximately $136 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is about $341 (32%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $306 (29%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
