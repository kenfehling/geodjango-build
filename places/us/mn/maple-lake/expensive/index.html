Home prices

Rents

In Maple Lake the median monthly rent is $721. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $136 (15%) lower.It is about $341 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $306 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
