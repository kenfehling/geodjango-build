Compared to its nearby areas, Maple Lake is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Lake costs $161,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $17,320 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $55,800 (25%) lower, and it is approximately $89,900 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Maple Lake is $721. It is about $136 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $306 (29%) lower.
