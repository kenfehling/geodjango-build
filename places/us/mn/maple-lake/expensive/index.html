In comparison to other places in this area, Maple Lake is a little less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Maple Lake costs $161,700. This is about $17,320 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $55,800 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $89,900 (35%) lower.

Rents

In Maple Lake the median monthly rent is $721. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $136 (15%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $306 (29%) lower.
