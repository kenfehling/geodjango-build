Home prices

The median home in St. Anthony is valued at $269,300. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $86,700 (47%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $51,800 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $17,700 (7%) higher.

Rents

In St. Anthony the median monthly rent is $1,092. This is roughly $125 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $30 (2%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $65 (6%) higher.
