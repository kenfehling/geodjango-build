In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Anthony is significantly more expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the entire country. It is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

St. Anthony's median home price is $269,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $86,700 (47%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $51,800 (23%) higher, and is approximately $17,700 (7%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

St. Anthony's median rent is $1,092 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $125 (12%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $30 (2%) higher, and is approximately $65 (6%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
