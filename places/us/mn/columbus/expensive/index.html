Home prices

The cost of the median home in Columbus is $275,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $57,800 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $23,700 (9%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Columbus is $888. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $278 (23%) lower.It is about $174 (16%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $139 (13%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
