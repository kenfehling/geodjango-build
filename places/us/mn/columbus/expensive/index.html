Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $275,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is is very similar. Compared to the entire nation it is about $57,800 (26%) higher, and it is roughly $23,700 (9%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $888 a month. It is about $278 (23%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $174 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $139 (13%) lower.
