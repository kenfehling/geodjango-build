Home prices

The median home in Columbus is valued at $275,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is is very similar.It is roughly $57,800 (26%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $23,700 (9%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Columbus's median rent is $888 per month. It is approximately $278 (23%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $174 (16%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $139 (13%) lower.
