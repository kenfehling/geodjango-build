Compared to the surrounding areas, Medicine Lake is significantly more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Medicine Lake's median home cost is $712,500. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $442,420 (163%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $495,000 (227%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $460,900 (183%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Medicine Lake is $944. It is about $270 (22%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $118 (11%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $83 (8%) lower.
