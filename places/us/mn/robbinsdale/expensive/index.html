In comparison to the surrounding areas, Robbinsdale is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is a little less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home value in Robbinsdale is $201,100. This is about $14,340 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $16,400 (7%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $50,500 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Robbinsdale is $982 per month. This is about $129 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $80 (7%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $45 (4%) lower.
