Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Robbinsdale is $982. It is about $129 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $80 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $45 (4%) lower.
