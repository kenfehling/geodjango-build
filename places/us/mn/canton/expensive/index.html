Home prices

The median home in Canton costs $71,700. This is approximately $51,300 (41%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is about $145,800 (67%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Rochester is roughly $128,400 (64%) lower.

Rents

Canton's median monthly rent is $561. This is approximately $71 (11%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $501 (47%) lower, and is approximately $413 (42%) lower compared to the city of Rochester.
