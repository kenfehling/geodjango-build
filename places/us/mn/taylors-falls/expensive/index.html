Home prices

The cost of the median home in Taylors Falls is $181,900. This is about $42,020 (30%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is roughly $35,600 (16%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $69,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Taylors Falls's median monthly rent is $667. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $149 (18%) lower.It is about $395 (37%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $360 (35%) lower.
