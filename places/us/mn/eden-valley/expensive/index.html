Compared to its nearby areas, Eden Valley is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home cost in Eden Valley is $112,200. Compared to other places in this area this is about $22,660 (16%) lower. It is roughly $105,300 (48%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $139,400 (55%) lower.

Rents

Eden Valley's median rent is $641 per month. This is roughly $122 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $421 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $386 (37%) lower.
