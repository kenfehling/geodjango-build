In comparison to nearby areas, St. Paul is somewhat less expensive, and it's similar compared to the rest of the country. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

St. Paul's median home price is $208,000. This is approximately $86,680 (29%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $9,500 (4%) lower, and is roughly $43,600 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in St. Paul is $968 a month. It is about $299 (23%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $94 (8%) lower, and is about $59 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
