Compared to other places in this area, St. Paul is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Paul is $208,000. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $86,680 (29%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $9,500 (4%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $43,600 (17%) lower.

Rents

In St. Paul the median monthly rent is $968. It is approximately $299 (23%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $94 (8%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $59 (5%) lower.
