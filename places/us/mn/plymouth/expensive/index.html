In comparison to its surrounding areas, Plymouth is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Plymouth costs $351,700. It is roughly $40,940 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $134,200 (61%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $100,100 (39%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Plymouth is $1,355. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $237 (21%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $293 (27%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $328 (31%) higher.
