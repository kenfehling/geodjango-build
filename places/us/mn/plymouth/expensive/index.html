Compared to the surrounding areas, Plymouth is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Plymouth's median home value is $351,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $40,940 (10%) lower.It is roughly $134,200 (61%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $100,100 (39%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Plymouth is $1,355 a month. This is about $237 (21%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $293 (27%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $328 (31%) higher.
