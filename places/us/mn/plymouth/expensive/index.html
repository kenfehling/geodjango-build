Compared to other places in this area, Plymouth is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Plymouth costs $351,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $40,940 (10%) lower.It is about $134,200 (61%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $100,100 (39%) higher.

Rents

Plymouth's median rent is $1,355 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $237 (21%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $293 (27%) higher, and it is approximately $328 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
