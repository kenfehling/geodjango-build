Home prices

The price of the median home in Eagan is $288,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $11,420 (4%) higher.It is approximately $70,700 (32%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $36,600 (14%) higher.

Rents

In Eagan the median monthly rent is $1,201. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $56 (4%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $139 (13%) higher, and it is about $174 (16%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
