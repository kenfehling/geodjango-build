Home prices

The price of the median home in Eagan is $288,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $11,420 (4%) higher.It is approximately $70,700 (32%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $36,600 (14%) higher.

Rents

Eagan's median monthly rent is $1,201. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $56 (4%) higher.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $139 (13%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $174 (16%) higher.
