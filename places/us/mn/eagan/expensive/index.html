Home prices

The price of the median home in Eagan is $288,200. This is approximately $11,420 (4%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $70,700 (32%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $36,600 (14%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Eagan is $1,201 a month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $56 (4%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $139 (13%) higher, and it is about $174 (16%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
