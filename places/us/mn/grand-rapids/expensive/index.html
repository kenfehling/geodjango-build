In comparison to nearby areas, Grand Rapids is a little more expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Bemidji, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Grand Rapids is $151,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $13,780 (10%) higher. It is about $66,200 (30%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $11,400 (8%) higher compared to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Grand Rapids is $670. This is about $67 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $392 (36%) lower, and compared to the city of Bemidji is about $117 (14%) lower.
