In comparison to other places in this area, Grand Rapids is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Bemidji, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Grand Rapids is $151,300. It is roughly $13,780 (10%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is roughly $11,400 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

In Grand Rapids the median rent is $670 a month. This is about $67 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $392 (36%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bemidji is approximately $117 (14%) lower.
