In comparison to nearby areas, Grand Rapids is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Bemidji, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

Grand Rapids's median home value is $151,300. It is approximately $13,780 (10%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $66,200 (30%) lower, and compared to the city of Bemidji is about $11,400 (8%) higher.

Rents

Grand Rapids's median monthly rent is $670. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $67 (9%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $392 (36%) lower, and it is approximately $117 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Bemidji.
