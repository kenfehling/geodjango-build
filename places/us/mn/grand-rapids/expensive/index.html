In comparison to nearby areas, Grand Rapids is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Bemidji, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Grand Rapids is $151,300. It is roughly $13,780 (10%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $11,400 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

In Grand Rapids the median rent is $670 per month. This is approximately $67 (9%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $392 (36%) lower, and it is about $117 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Bemidji.
