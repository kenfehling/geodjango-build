Compared to its surrounding areas, Woodbury is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the entire country. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Woodbury is $323,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $62,420 (23%) higher. It is roughly $105,500 (48%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $71,400 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Woodbury the median rent is $1,525 per month. This is about $458 (42%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $463 (43%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $498 (48%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
