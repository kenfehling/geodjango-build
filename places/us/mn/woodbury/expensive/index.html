Home prices

The median home price in Woodbury is $323,000. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $62,420 (23%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $105,500 (48%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $71,400 (28%) higher.

Rents

In Woodbury the median monthly rent is $1,525. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $458 (42%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $463 (43%) higher, and is about $498 (48%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
