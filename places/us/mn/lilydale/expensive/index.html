Compared to the surrounding areas, Lilydale is a lot more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Lilydale is $370,700. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $126,020 (51%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $153,200 (70%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $119,100 (47%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lilydale is $1,998. This is roughly $1,014 (103%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $936 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $971 (94%) higher.
