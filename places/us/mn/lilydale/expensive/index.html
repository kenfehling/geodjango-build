Home prices

The value of the median home in Lilydale is $370,700. It is roughly $126,020 (51%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $153,200 (70%) higher, and it is approximately $119,100 (47%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Lilydale the median monthly rent is $1,998. It is roughly $1,014 (103%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $936 (88%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $971 (94%) higher.
