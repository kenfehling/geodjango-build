Home prices

The median home in Lilydale is valued at $370,700. This is approximately $126,020 (51%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $153,200 (70%) higher, and it is roughly $119,100 (47%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Lilydale's median monthly rent is $1,998. This is roughly $1,014 (103%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $936 (88%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $971 (94%) higher.
