Home prices

Rents

Lafayette's median monthly rent is $763. It is approximately $65 (9%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $299 (28%) lower, and is roughly $264 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
