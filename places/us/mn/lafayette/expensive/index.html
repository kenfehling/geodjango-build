Home prices

The median home cost in Lafayette is $93,900. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $33,880 (26%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $123,600 (56%) lower, and it is approximately $157,700 (62%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lafayette is $763. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $65 (9%) higher.It is about $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $264 (25%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
