Home prices

The price of the median home in Cologne is $242,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $44,060 (15%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $24,500 (11%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $9,600 (3%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Cologne is $681. It is roughly $579 (45%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $381 (35%) lower, and it is roughly $346 (33%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
