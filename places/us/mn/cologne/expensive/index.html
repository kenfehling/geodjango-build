Compared to other places in this area, Cologne is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Cologne costs $242,000. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $44,060 (15%) lower. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $24,500 (11%) higher, and is roughly $9,600 (3%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Cologne the median monthly rent is $681. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $579 (45%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $381 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $346 (33%) lower.
