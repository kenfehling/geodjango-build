Home prices

The median home in Park Rapids costs $144,900. It is approximately $35,340 (32%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $72,600 (33%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $5,000 (3%) higher compared to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Park Rapids is $572. This is approximately $149 (20%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $490 (46%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is about $215 (27%) lower compared to the city of Bemidji.
