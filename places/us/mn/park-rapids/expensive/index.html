Home prices

The median home price in Park Rapids is $144,900. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $35,340 (32%) higher. It is approximately $72,600 (33%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Bemidji is about $5,000 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Park Rapids the median monthly rent is $572. It is about $149 (20%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $490 (46%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $215 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Bemidji.
