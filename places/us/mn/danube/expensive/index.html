Home prices

The median home in Danube costs $81,300. It is roughly $9,500 (10%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $136,200 (62%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is about $74,200 (47%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Danube is $631. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $10 (1%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $431 (40%) lower, and is roughly $171 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.
