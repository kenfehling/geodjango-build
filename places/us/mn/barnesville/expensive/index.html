Compared to its surrounding areas, Barnesville is a little more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Fargo, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Barnesville is valued at $161,100. This is about $29,400 (22%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $56,400 (25%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo it is about $51,000 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Barnesville the median rent is $728 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $47 (6%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $334 (31%) lower, and is about $95 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.
