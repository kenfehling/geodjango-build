In comparison to other places in this area, Barnesville is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Fargo, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Barnesville is $161,100. This is roughly $29,400 (22%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $56,400 (25%) lower, and is roughly $51,000 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.

Rents

Barnesville's median monthly rent is $728. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $47 (6%) higher. It is roughly $334 (31%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Fargo it is about $95 (11%) lower.
