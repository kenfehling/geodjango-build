Home prices

The median home in Minneapolis costs $251,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $6,100 (2%) lower. It is roughly $34,100 (15%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation.

Rents

In Minneapolis the median rent is $1,027 a month. It is roughly $90 (8%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $35 (3%) lower.
