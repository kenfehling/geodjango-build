Home prices

The price of the median home in Minneapolis is $251,600. This is roughly $6,100 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $34,100 (15%) higher.

Rents

Minneapolis's median rent is $1,027 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $90 (8%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $35 (3%) lower.
