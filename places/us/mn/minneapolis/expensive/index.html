Home prices

The price of the median home in Minneapolis is $251,600. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $6,100 (2%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $34,100 (15%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Minneapolis is $1,027. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $90 (8%) lower.It is approximately $35 (3%) lower compared to the nation as a whole.
