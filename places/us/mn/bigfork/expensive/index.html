Home prices

The median home in Bigfork costs $102,300. It is roughly $6,760 (7%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $115,200 (52%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $37,600 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

Bigfork's median rent is $906 a month. It is approximately $285 (45%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $156 (14%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Bemidji it is approximately $119 (15%) higher.
