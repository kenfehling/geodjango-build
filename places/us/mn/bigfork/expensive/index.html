Home prices

The cost of the median home in Bigfork is $102,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $6,760 (7%) higher.It is about $115,200 (52%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Bemidji is roughly $37,600 (26%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bigfork is $906 per month. This is approximately $285 (45%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $156 (14%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $119 (15%) higher in comparison to the city of Bemidji.
