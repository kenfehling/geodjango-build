In comparison to its surrounding areas, East Grand Forks is a little more expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Fargo, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in East Grand Forks is valued at $196,500. This is roughly $39,200 (24%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $21,000 (9%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $15,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Grand Forks is $721. It is about $27 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $341 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fargo it is approximately $102 (12%) lower.
