Home prices

The median home in East Grand Forks costs $196,500. It is roughly $39,200 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $21,000 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $15,600 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.

Rents

The median monthly rent in East Grand Forks is $721. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $27 (3%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $341 (32%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo it is roughly $102 (12%) lower.
