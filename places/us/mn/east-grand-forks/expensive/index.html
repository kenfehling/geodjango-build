Compared to its surrounding areas, East Grand Forks is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Fargo, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

East Grand Forks's median home value is $196,500. It is approximately $39,200 (24%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $21,000 (9%) lower, and it is roughly $15,600 (7%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.

Rents

East Grand Forks's median monthly rent is $721. It is roughly $27 (3%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $341 (32%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Fargo it is roughly $102 (12%) lower.
