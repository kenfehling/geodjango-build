Home prices

The median home in Evansville is valued at $118,500. This is about $4,320 (3%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $99,000 (45%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $76,100 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Moorhead.

Rents

In Evansville the median rent is $642 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $13 (2%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $420 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Moorhead is approximately $243 (27%) lower.
