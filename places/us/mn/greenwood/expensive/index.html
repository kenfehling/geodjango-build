Home prices

The median home in Greenwood is valued at $933,100. It is about $381,200 (69%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $715,600 (329%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $681,500 (270%) higher.

Rents

Greenwood's median monthly rent is $1,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $110 (7%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $338 (31%) higher, and is roughly $373 (36%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
