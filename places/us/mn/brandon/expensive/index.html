Home prices

The median home value in Brandon is $134,500. It is approximately $23,520 (21%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $83,000 (38%) lower, and it is roughly $42,600 (24%) lower compared to the city of Alexandria.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Brandon is $558. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $114 (16%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $504 (47%) lower, and is about $154 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Alexandria.
