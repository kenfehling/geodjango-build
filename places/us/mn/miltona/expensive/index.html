Home prices

The price of the median home in Miltona is $136,800. It is roughly $18,060 (15%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $80,700 (37%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $18,700 (12%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

In Miltona the median monthly rent is $713. This is approximately $15 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $349 (32%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is about $89 (11%) lower.
