Compared to its surrounding areas, North Oaks is significantly more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in North Oaks is $633,300. This is about $365,300 (136%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the entire country it is about $415,800 (191%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $381,700 (151%) higher.

Rents

In North Oaks the median rent is $2,614 a month. This is approximately $1,270 (94%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $1,552 (146%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $1,587 (154%) higher.
