Home prices

North Oaks's median home value is $633,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $365,300 (136%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $415,800 (191%) higher, and it is roughly $381,700 (151%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Oaks is $2,614. Compared to other places in this area it is about $1,270 (94%) higher.It is approximately $1,552 (146%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is roughly $1,587 (154%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
