Compared to its surrounding areas, North Oaks is a lot more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

North Oaks's median home value is $633,300. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $365,300 (136%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is about $415,800 (191%) higher, and is roughly $381,700 (151%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In North Oaks the median rent is $2,614 per month. It is roughly $1,270 (94%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $1,552 (146%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $1,587 (154%) higher.
