Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in North Oaks is $2,614. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $1,270 (94%) higher.It is approximately $1,552 (146%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $1,587 (154%) higher.
