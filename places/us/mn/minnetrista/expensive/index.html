Compared to its nearby areas, Minnetrista is significantly more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Minnetrista costs $477,800. This is approximately $154,780 (47%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $260,300 (119%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $226,200 (89%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Minnetrista is $3,016 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $1,834 (155%) higher.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $1,954 (183%) higher, and it is about $1,989 (193%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
