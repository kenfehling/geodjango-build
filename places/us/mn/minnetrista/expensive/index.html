Compared to its nearby areas, Minnetrista is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Minnetrista is $477,800. It is approximately $154,780 (47%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $260,300 (119%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $226,200 (89%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Minnetrista is $3,016 per month. This is about $1,834 (155%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $1,954 (183%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $1,989 (193%) higher.
