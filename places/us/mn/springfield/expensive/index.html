Home prices

The median home cost in Springfield is $87,300. It is roughly $17,480 (25%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $130,200 (59%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mankato it is about $92,100 (51%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Springfield is $600 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $22 (3%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $462 (43%) lower, and compared to the city of Mankato is approximately $299 (33%) lower.
