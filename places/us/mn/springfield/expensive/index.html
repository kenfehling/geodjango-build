In comparison to other places in this area, Springfield is a little more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Mankato, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Springfield costs $87,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $17,480 (25%) higher. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $130,200 (59%) lower, and it is approximately $92,100 (51%) lower compared to the city of Mankato.

Rents

Springfield's median rent is $600 a month. This is about $22 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $462 (43%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Mankato it is roughly $299 (33%) lower.
