Home prices

The median home in Karlstad is valued at $81,300. It is approximately $9,700 (13%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $136,200 (62%) lower, and compared to the city of Grand Forks is about $129,000 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Karlstad is $558 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $8 (1%) lower. It is roughly $504 (47%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Grand Forks it is roughly $253 (31%) lower.
