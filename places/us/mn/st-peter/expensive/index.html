In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Peter is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Peter is valued at $170,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $15,980 (10%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $47,300 (21%) lower, and it is roughly $81,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In St. Peter the median rent is $861 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $166 (23%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $201 (18%) lower, and it is approximately $166 (16%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
