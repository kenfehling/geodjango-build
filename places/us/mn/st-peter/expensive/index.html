Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Peter is somewhat more expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

St. Peter's median home cost is $170,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $15,980 (10%) higher. It is about $47,300 (21%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $81,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

St. Peter's median rent is $861 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $166 (23%) higher. It is roughly $201 (18%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $166 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
