Home prices

St. Peter's median home value is $170,200. Compared to other places in this area it is about $15,980 (10%) higher.It is about $47,300 (21%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $81,400 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

St. Peter's median rent is $861 per month. It is approximately $166 (23%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $201 (18%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $166 (16%) lower.
