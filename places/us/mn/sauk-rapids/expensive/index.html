Home prices

The median home cost in Sauk Rapids is $170,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is is very similar. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $47,500 (21%) lower, and it is approximately $81,600 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Sauk Rapids's median monthly rent is $746. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $67 (8%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $316 (29%) lower, and it is roughly $281 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
