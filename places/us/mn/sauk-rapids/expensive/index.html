Home prices

The median home in Sauk Rapids costs $170,000. Compared to nearby areas it is is very similar.It is approximately $47,500 (21%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $81,600 (32%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Sauk Rapids the median rent is $746 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $67 (8%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $316 (29%) lower, and is roughly $281 (27%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
