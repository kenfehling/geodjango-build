Home prices

The median home in Coon Rapids costs $204,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $12,600 (5%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $12,800 (5%) lower, and it is approximately $46,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Coon Rapids's median rent is $1,137 per month. It is approximately $22 (1%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $75 (7%) higher, and it is about $110 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
