Home prices

The median home in Coon Rapids is valued at $204,700. This is roughly $12,600 (5%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is about $12,800 (5%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $46,900 (18%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Coon Rapids is $1,137. This is about $22 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $75 (7%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $110 (10%) higher.
