Home prices

The cost of the median home in Coon Rapids is $204,700. This is approximately $12,600 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $12,800 (5%) lower compared to the entire country, and is about $46,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Coon Rapids is $1,137. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $22 (1%) higher. It is approximately $75 (7%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $110 (10%) higher.
