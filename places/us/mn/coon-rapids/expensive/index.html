Home prices

The median home cost in Coon Rapids is $204,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $12,600 (5%) lower.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $12,800 (5%) lower, and it is about $46,900 (18%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Coon Rapids's median rent is $1,137 a month. It is roughly $22 (1%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $75 (7%) higher, and is roughly $110 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
