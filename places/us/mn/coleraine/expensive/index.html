Home prices

The value of the median home in Coleraine is $138,600. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $27,480 (24%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $78,900 (36%) lower, and is about $24,700 (15%) lower in comparison to the city of Duluth.

Rents

Coleraine's median rent is $715 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $27 (3%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $347 (32%) lower, and is roughly $105 (12%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
