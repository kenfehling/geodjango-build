Home prices

The median home in Baudette is valued at $94,200. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $7,920 (9%) higher. It is roughly $123,300 (56%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $45,700 (32%) lower in comparison to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

Baudette's median monthly rent is $617. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $48 (8%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $445 (41%) lower, and compared to the city of Bemidji it is roughly $170 (21%) lower.
