Compared to its surrounding areas, Corcoran is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Corcoran is $335,300. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $49,460 (12%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is about $117,800 (54%) higher, and is approximately $83,700 (33%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Corcoran the median monthly rent is $1,681. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $672 (66%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $619 (58%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $654 (63%) higher.
