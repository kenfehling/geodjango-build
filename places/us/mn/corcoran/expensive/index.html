In comparison to nearby areas, Corcoran is a bit less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Corcoran's median home cost is $335,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $49,460 (12%) lower. It is about $117,800 (54%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $83,700 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Corcoran is $1,681. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $672 (66%) higher. It is roughly $619 (58%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $654 (63%) higher.
