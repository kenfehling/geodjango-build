Compared to the surrounding areas, Corcoran is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Corcoran is valued at $335,300. It is roughly $49,460 (12%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is about $117,800 (54%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $83,700 (33%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Corcoran's median rent is $1,681 per month. This is about $672 (66%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $619 (58%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $654 (63%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
