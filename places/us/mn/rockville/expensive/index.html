Home prices

The median home cost in Rockville is $254,700. It is approximately $79,480 (45%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $37,200 (17%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $3,100 (1%) higher.

Rents

In Rockville the median monthly rent is $705. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $157 (18%) lower.It is about $357 (33%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $322 (31%) lower.
