Compared to the surrounding areas, Rockville is significantly more expensive, and it is somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

Rockville's median home price is $254,700. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $79,480 (45%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $37,200 (17%) higher, and is approximately $3,100 (1%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Rockville's median rent is $705 a month. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $157 (18%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $357 (33%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $322 (31%) lower.
