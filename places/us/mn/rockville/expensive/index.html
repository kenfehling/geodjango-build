In comparison to its surrounding areas, Rockville is substantially more expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Rockville costs $254,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $79,480 (45%) higher.It is about $37,200 (17%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $3,100 (1%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Rockville the median rent is $705 a month. This is about $157 (18%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $357 (33%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $322 (31%) lower.
