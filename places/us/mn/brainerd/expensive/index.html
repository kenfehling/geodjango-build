Compared to its surrounding areas, Brainerd is substantially less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Brainerd is $117,900. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $80,920 (40%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $99,600 (45%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is roughly $37,600 (24%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Brainerd is $733 a month. This is about $181 (19%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $329 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $69 (8%) lower.
