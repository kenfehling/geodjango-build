Home prices

The price of the median home in Tyler is $79,200. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $13,760 (21%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $138,300 (63%) lower, and it is roughly $110,600 (58%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.

Rents

The median rent in Tyler is $644 a month. This is approximately $13 (2%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $418 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $183 (22%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
