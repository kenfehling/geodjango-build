Home prices

Rents

Tyler's median rent is $644 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $13 (2%) higher. It is approximately $418 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $183 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
