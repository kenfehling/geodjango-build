Home prices

The median home in Loretto costs $260,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $163,900 (38%) lower. It is approximately $42,900 (19%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $8,800 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Loretto's median monthly rent is $645. This is approximately $732 (53%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $417 (39%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $382 (37%) lower.
