In comparison to the surrounding areas, Loretto is substantially less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home value in Loretto is $260,400. It is about $163,900 (38%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $42,900 (19%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $8,800 (3%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Loretto is $645 per month. This is approximately $732 (53%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $417 (39%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $382 (37%) lower.
