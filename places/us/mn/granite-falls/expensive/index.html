Home prices

Granite Falls's median home price is $125,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $64,120 (104%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $92,200 (42%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Brookings it is about $55,700 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Granite Falls the median monthly rent is $583. It is roughly $43 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $479 (45%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $170 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Brookings.
