Compared to other places in this area, Dayton is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

Dayton's median home value is $313,500. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $50,020 (18%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $96,000 (44%) higher, and it is approximately $61,900 (24%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Dayton the median monthly rent is $1,135. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $118 (9%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $73 (6%) higher, and is about $108 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
