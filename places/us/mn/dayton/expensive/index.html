Compared to its nearby areas, Dayton is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dayton is valued at $313,500. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $50,020 (18%) higher. It is roughly $96,000 (44%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $61,900 (24%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Dayton is $1,135 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $118 (9%) lower. It is about $73 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $108 (10%) higher.
