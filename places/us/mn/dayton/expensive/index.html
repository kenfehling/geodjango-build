Home prices

The median home in Dayton is valued at $313,500. It is approximately $50,020 (18%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $96,000 (44%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $61,900 (24%) higher.

Rents

Dayton's median rent is $1,135 per month. It is about $118 (9%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $73 (6%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $108 (10%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
