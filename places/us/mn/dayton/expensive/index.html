In comparison to its surrounding areas, Dayton is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Dayton costs $313,500. This is approximately $50,020 (18%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $96,000 (44%) higher, and it is roughly $61,900 (24%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Dayton the median rent is $1,135 per month. This is approximately $118 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $73 (6%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $108 (10%) higher.
