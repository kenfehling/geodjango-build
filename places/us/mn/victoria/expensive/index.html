Home prices

The median home in Victoria costs $427,400. This is roughly $16,300 (3%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $209,900 (96%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $175,800 (69%) higher.

Rents

In Victoria the median monthly rent is $1,285. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $95 (6%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $223 (20%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $258 (25%) higher.
