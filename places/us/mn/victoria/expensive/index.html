Home prices

The cost of the median home in Victoria is $427,400. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $16,300 (3%) higher.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $209,900 (96%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $175,800 (69%) higher.

Rents

Victoria's median monthly rent is $1,285. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $95 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $223 (20%) higher, and it is roughly $258 (25%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
