Home prices

The value of the median home in Victoria is $427,400. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $16,300 (3%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $209,900 (96%) higher, and is approximately $175,800 (69%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Victoria is $1,285. This is approximately $95 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $223 (20%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $258 (25%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
