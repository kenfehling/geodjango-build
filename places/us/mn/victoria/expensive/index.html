Home prices

Victoria's median home price is $427,400. It is approximately $16,300 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $209,900 (96%) higher, and it is roughly $175,800 (69%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Victoria the median rent is $1,285 per month. This is roughly $95 (6%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $223 (20%) higher, and it is roughly $258 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
