Home prices

The median home in Victoria is valued at $427,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $16,300 (3%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $209,900 (96%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $175,800 (69%) higher.

Rents

Victoria's median rent is $1,285 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is about $95 (6%) lower. It is roughly $223 (20%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $258 (25%) higher.
