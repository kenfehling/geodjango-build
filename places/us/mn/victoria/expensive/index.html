Home prices

The median home in Victoria is valued at $427,400. It is about $16,300 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $209,900 (96%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $175,800 (69%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Victoria is $1,285. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $95 (6%) lower.It is roughly $223 (20%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $258 (25%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
