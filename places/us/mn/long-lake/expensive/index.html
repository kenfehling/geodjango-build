In comparison to its surrounding areas, Long Lake is significantly less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's a bit more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Long Lake is valued at $289,100. This is roughly $272,000 (48%) lower compared to nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $71,600 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $37,500 (14%) higher.

Rents

Long Lake's median monthly rent is $961. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $490 (33%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is approximately $101 (9%) lower, and it is approximately $66 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
