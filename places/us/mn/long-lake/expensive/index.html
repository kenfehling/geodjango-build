Compared to the surrounding areas, Long Lake is significantly less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a bit more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Long Lake is valued at $289,100. This is about $272,000 (48%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $71,600 (32%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and is about $37,500 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Long Lake the median monthly rent is $961. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $490 (33%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $101 (9%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $66 (6%) lower.
