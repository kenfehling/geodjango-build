In comparison to the surrounding areas, St. Joseph is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Joseph is $170,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $16,480 (8%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $47,300 (21%) lower, and it is about $81,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In St. Joseph the median rent is $777 per month. It is roughly $36 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $285 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $250 (24%) lower.
