Compared to the surrounding areas, St. Joseph is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in St. Joseph is $170,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $16,480 (8%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $47,300 (21%) lower, and is approximately $81,400 (32%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

St. Joseph's median rent is $777 a month. This is approximately $36 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $250 (24%) lower.
