Compared to nearby areas, St. Joseph is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Joseph is valued at $170,200. This is roughly $16,480 (8%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is roughly $47,300 (21%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $81,400 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in St. Joseph is $777 per month. This is about $36 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $285 (26%) lower, and it is approximately $250 (24%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
