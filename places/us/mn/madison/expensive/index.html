Home prices

The value of the median home in Madison is $67,600. This is about $14,940 (28%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $149,900 (68%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $113,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Brookings.

Rents

In Madison the median rent is $522 per month. It is about $17 (3%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $540 (50%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is approximately $231 (30%) lower in comparison to the city of Brookings.
