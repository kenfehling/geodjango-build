Home prices

The median home cost in Madison is $67,600. This is about $14,940 (28%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $149,900 (68%) lower, and is approximately $113,400 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Brookings.

Rents

In Madison the median rent is $522 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $17 (3%) higher.It is roughly $540 (50%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $231 (30%) lower compared to the city of Brookings.
