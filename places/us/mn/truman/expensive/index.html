Home prices

The cost of the median home in Truman is $78,000. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $7,840 (11%) higher. It is about $139,500 (64%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $101,400 (56%) lower compared to the city of Mankato.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Truman is $614. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $7 (1%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $448 (42%) lower, and is roughly $285 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Mankato.
