Compared to its nearby areas, Lakeland is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Lakeland is $258,300. It is roughly $46,660 (15%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $40,800 (18%) higher, and is about $6,700 (2%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Lakeland the median rent is $1,112 a month. It is roughly $32 (2%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $50 (4%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $85 (8%) higher.
