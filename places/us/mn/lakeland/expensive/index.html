Compared to the surrounding areas, Lakeland is a little less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeland costs $258,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $46,660 (15%) lower.It is roughly $40,800 (18%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is roughly $6,700 (2%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Lakeland's median monthly rent is $1,112. This is roughly $32 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $50 (4%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is roughly $85 (8%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
