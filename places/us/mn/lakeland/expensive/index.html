In comparison to the surrounding areas, Lakeland is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeland costs $258,300. It is approximately $46,660 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $40,800 (18%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $6,700 (2%) higher.

Rents

Lakeland's median rent is $1,112 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $32 (2%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $50 (4%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $85 (8%) higher.
