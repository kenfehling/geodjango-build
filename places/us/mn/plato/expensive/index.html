Home prices

The median home in Plato costs $162,500. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $2,760 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $55,000 (25%) lower, and it is about $89,100 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Plato's median rent is $767 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $20 (2%) higher. It is roughly $295 (27%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $260 (25%) lower.
