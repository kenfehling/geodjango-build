Compared to its surrounding areas, Chanhassen is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Chanhassen costs $381,300. It is approximately $164,520 (30%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $163,800 (75%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $129,700 (51%) higher.

Rents

In Chanhassen the median monthly rent is $1,378. This is approximately $88 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $316 (29%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $351 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
