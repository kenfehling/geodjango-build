Compared to its nearby areas, Chanhassen is a little less expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Chanhassen is $381,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $164,520 (30%) lower.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $163,800 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $129,700 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Chanhassen is $1,378 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $88 (6%) higher.It is approximately $316 (29%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is about $351 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
