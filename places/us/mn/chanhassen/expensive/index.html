In comparison to its nearby areas, Chanhassen is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

Chanhassen's median home price is $381,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $164,520 (30%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $163,800 (75%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $129,700 (51%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Chanhassen is $1,378. It is approximately $88 (6%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $316 (29%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $351 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
