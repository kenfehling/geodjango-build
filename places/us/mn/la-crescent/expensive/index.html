Home prices

The median home in La Crescent costs $184,100. This is roughly $23,100 (14%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $33,400 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $16,000 (7%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

In La Crescent the median rent is $851 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $86 (11%) higher. It is approximately $211 (19%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Rochester it is approximately $123 (12%) lower.
