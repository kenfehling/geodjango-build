Compared to other places in this area, Wabasha is a little more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's significantly less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Wabasha's median home value is $165,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $24,740 (17%) higher. It is roughly $52,500 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $86,600 (34%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Wabasha is $696 a month. This is about $56 (7%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $366 (34%) lower, and it is approximately $331 (32%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
