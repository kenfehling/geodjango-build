Home prices

Pennock's median home cost is $118,800. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $15,080 (14%) higher. It is about $98,700 (45%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $36,700 (23%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Pennock's median monthly rent is $632. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $96 (13%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is about $430 (40%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud is approximately $170 (21%) lower.
