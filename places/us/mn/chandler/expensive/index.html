Home prices

The median home price in Chandler is $61,900. This is approximately $13,040 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area.Compared to the entire country it is about $155,600 (71%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is about $127,900 (67%) lower.

Rents

Chandler's median rent is $803 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $135 (20%) higher.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $259 (24%) lower, and is approximately $24 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls.
