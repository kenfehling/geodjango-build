Home prices

Rents

Golden Valley's median rent is $1,349 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $291 (27%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $287 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $322 (31%) higher.
