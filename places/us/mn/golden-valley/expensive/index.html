Home prices

The median home in Golden Valley costs $304,700. This is roughly $16,820 (5%) lower compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $87,200 (40%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $53,100 (21%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Golden Valley is $1,349 a month. This is roughly $291 (27%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $287 (27%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $322 (31%) higher.
