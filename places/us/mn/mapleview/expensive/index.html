Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Mapleview is $788 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $136 (20%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $239 (23%) lower.
