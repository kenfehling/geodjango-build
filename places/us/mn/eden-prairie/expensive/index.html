In comparison to its nearby areas, Eden Prairie is a bit more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Eden Prairie is $368,100. It is approximately $28,400 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $150,600 (69%) higher, and is approximately $116,500 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Eden Prairie the median rent is $1,402 a month. It is about $111 (8%) higher compared to other places in this area.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $340 (32%) higher, and is approximately $375 (36%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
