In comparison to other places in this area, Eden Prairie is a little more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Eden Prairie costs $368,100. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $28,400 (8%) higher. It is approximately $150,600 (69%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $116,500 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Eden Prairie the median rent is $1,402 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $111 (8%) higher. It is about $340 (32%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $375 (36%) higher.
