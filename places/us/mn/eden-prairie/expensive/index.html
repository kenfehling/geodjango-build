Compared to its nearby areas, Eden Prairie is somewhat more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Eden Prairie's median home price is $368,100. It is roughly $28,400 (8%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $150,600 (69%) higher, and it is approximately $116,500 (46%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Eden Prairie is $1,402 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $111 (8%) higher. It is about $340 (32%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $375 (36%) higher.
