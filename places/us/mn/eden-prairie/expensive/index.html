Compared to its surrounding areas, Eden Prairie is a little more expensive, and it is a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Eden Prairie costs $368,100. Compared to nearby areas this is roughly $28,400 (8%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $150,600 (69%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $116,500 (46%) higher.

Rents

In Eden Prairie the median monthly rent is $1,402. This is roughly $111 (8%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $340 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $375 (36%) higher.
