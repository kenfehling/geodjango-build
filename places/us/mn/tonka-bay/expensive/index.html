Compared to its surrounding areas, Tonka Bay is a bit more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly more expensive even compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Tonka Bay's median home value is $661,400. This is roughly $55,160 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $443,900 (204%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $409,800 (162%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Tonka Bay's median monthly rent is $1,667. It is approximately $211 (14%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $605 (56%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $640 (62%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
