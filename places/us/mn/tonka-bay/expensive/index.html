Compared to other places in this area, Tonka Bay is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Tonka Bay is $661,400. This is roughly $55,160 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $443,900 (204%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $409,800 (162%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Tonka Bay's median rent is $1,667 a month. It is roughly $211 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $605 (56%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $640 (62%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
