Compared to the surrounding areas, Tonka Bay is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the entire country. It's a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Tonka Bay costs $661,400. It is approximately $55,160 (9%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $443,900 (204%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $409,800 (162%) higher.

Rents

Tonka Bay's median monthly rent is $1,667. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $211 (14%) higher. It is about $605 (56%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $640 (62%) higher.
