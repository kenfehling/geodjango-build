Compared to its surrounding areas, Tonka Bay is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Tonka Bay is $661,400. This is roughly $55,160 (9%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $443,900 (204%) higher, and is roughly $409,800 (162%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Tonka Bay's median rent is $1,667 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $211 (14%) higher. It is roughly $605 (56%) higher compared to the entire country, and is roughly $640 (62%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
