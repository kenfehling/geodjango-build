In comparison to the surrounding areas, Tonka Bay is somewhat more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It is substantially more expensive even compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Tonka Bay is valued at $661,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $55,160 (9%) higher.It is about $443,900 (204%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $409,800 (162%) higher.

Rents

Tonka Bay's median monthly rent is $1,667. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $211 (14%) higher.It is approximately $605 (56%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $640 (62%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
