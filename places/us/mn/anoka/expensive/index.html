Compared to the surrounding areas, Anoka is a little less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a bit less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Anoka's median home value is $203,100. This is roughly $56,380 (21%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $14,400 (6%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $48,500 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Anoka is $962. This is about $349 (26%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $100 (9%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $65 (6%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
