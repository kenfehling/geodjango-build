Home prices

The median home price in Lake Crystal is $153,200. This is approximately $25,780 (20%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $64,300 (29%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $98,400 (39%) lower.

Rents

In Lake Crystal the median monthly rent is $719. This is approximately $24 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $343 (32%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $308 (29%) lower.
