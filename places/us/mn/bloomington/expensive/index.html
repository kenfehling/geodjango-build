In comparison to its surrounding areas, Bloomington is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Bloomington is $251,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $73,620 (22%) lower.It is approximately $33,800 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Bloomington the median rent is $1,178 a month. This is roughly $102 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $116 (10%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is roughly $151 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
