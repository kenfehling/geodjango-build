In comparison to nearby areas, Bloomington is a bit less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Bloomington is valued at $251,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $73,620 (22%) lower. It is roughly $33,800 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Bloomington is $1,178 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $102 (7%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $116 (10%) higher, and it is about $151 (14%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
