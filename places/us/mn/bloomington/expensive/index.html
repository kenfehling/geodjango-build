Compared to nearby areas, Bloomington is somewhat less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Bloomington is valued at $251,300. This is about $73,620 (22%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $33,800 (15%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is is very similar.

Rents

The median rent in Bloomington is $1,178 a month. This is approximately $102 (7%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $116 (10%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is about $151 (14%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
