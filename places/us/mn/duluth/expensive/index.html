Compared to the surrounding areas, Duluth is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the entire nation.

Home prices

The median home in Duluth is valued at $163,300. This is about $25,550 (13%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $54,200 (24%) lower compared to the entire country.

Rents

Duluth's median rent is $820 a month. It is about $216 (20%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is about $242 (22%) lower.
