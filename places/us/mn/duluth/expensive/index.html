Compared to other places in this area, Duluth is a bit less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the US as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Duluth is valued at $163,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $25,550 (13%) lower. It is approximately $54,200 (24%) lower in comparison to the entire nation.

Rents

In Duluth the median monthly rent is $820. It is roughly $216 (20%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $242 (22%) lower.
