Compared to the surrounding areas, Duluth is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole.

Home prices

The median home in Duluth is valued at $163,300. This is about $25,550 (13%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $54,200 (24%) lower.

Rents

In Duluth the median rent is $820 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $216 (20%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $242 (22%) lower.
