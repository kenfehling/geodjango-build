Home prices

The median home price in Appleton is $49,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $10,900 (17%) lower. It is approximately $167,600 (77%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Brookings is about $131,100 (72%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Appleton is $528 per month. It is approximately $116 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $534 (50%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Brookings it is about $225 (29%) lower.
