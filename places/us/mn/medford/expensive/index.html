Home prices

The median home in Medford is valued at $162,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $21,220 (15%) higher. It is about $55,300 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $89,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

Medford's median rent is $700 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $83 (10%) lower. It is roughly $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $327 (31%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
