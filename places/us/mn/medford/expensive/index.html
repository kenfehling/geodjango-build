Compared to other places in this area, Medford is a bit more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home cost in Medford is $162,200. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $21,220 (15%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $55,300 (25%) lower, and is about $89,400 (35%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Medford is $700 a month. It is roughly $83 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $327 (31%) lower.
