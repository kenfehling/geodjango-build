In comparison to its surrounding areas, Medford is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Medford is $162,200. It is approximately $21,220 (15%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $55,300 (25%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $89,400 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Medford is $700. Compared to nearby areas this is approximately $83 (10%) lower. It is roughly $362 (34%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $327 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
