Home prices

The median home in Medford is valued at $162,200. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $21,220 (15%) higher.It is about $55,300 (25%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is about $89,400 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Medford's median rent is $700 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $83 (10%) lower.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $362 (34%) lower, and is roughly $327 (31%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
