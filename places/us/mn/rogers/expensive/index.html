Compared to its surrounding areas, Rogers is somewhat more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Rogers is valued at $331,200. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $37,680 (12%) higher. It is about $113,700 (52%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $79,600 (31%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Rogers the median rent is $1,162 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $71 (6%) higher. It is roughly $100 (9%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $135 (13%) higher.
