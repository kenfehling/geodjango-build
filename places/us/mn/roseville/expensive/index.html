Home prices

The median home price in Roseville is $254,200. It is roughly $6,640 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is about $36,700 (16%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $2,600 (1%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roseville is $1,050. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $90 (7%) lower.It is approximately $12 (1%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $23 (2%) higher.
