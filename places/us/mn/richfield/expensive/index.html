Home prices

Richfield's median home price is $225,200. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $99,420 (30%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $7,700 (3%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $26,400 (10%) lower.

Rents

In Richfield the median rent is $1,079 per month. This is about $84 (7%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $17 (1%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $52 (5%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
