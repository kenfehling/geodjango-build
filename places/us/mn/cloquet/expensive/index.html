Home prices

The median home in Cloquet is valued at $142,700. This is approximately $30,200 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $74,800 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth is roughly $20,600 (12%) lower.

Rents

Cloquet's median rent is $761 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $93 (10%) lower. It is roughly $301 (28%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Duluth it is approximately $59 (7%) lower.
