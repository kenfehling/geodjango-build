Home prices

The median home in Rosemount is valued at $279,000. This is approximately $34,640 (14%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $61,500 (28%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $27,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rosemount is $1,218. This is roughly $130 (11%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $156 (14%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $191 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
