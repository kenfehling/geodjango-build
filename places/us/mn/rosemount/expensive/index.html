Home prices

The median home in Rosemount costs $279,000. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $34,640 (14%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $61,500 (28%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $27,400 (10%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rosemount is $1,218. This is roughly $130 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $156 (14%) higher, and is about $191 (18%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
