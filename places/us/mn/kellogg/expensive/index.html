Compared to its surrounding areas, Kellogg is somewhat more expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is a little less expensive compared to the city of St. Paul.

Home prices

The median home in Kellogg costs $145,400. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $9,340 (6%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $72,100 (33%) lower, and is roughly $62,600 (30%) lower compared to the city of St. Paul.

Rents

The median rent in Kellogg is $863 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $175 (25%) higher. It is roughly $199 (18%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Paul it is roughly $105 (10%) lower.
