Home prices

The median home in Kellogg costs $145,400. This is roughly $9,340 (6%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $72,100 (33%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Paul is roughly $62,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Kellogg is $863. This is approximately $175 (25%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire country it is about $199 (18%) lower, and it is roughly $105 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Paul.
