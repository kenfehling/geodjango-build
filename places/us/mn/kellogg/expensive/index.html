Compared to its surrounding areas, Kellogg is a little more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Paul, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

Kellogg's median home price is $145,400. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $9,340 (6%) higher. It is roughly $72,100 (33%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Paul it is about $62,600 (30%) lower.

Rents

In Kellogg the median rent is $863 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $175 (25%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $199 (18%) lower, and is roughly $105 (10%) lower compared to the city of St. Paul.
