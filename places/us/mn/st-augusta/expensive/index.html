Compared to nearby areas, St. Augusta is a bit more expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in St. Augusta costs $228,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $50,080 (28%) higher. It is about $10,900 (5%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $23,200 (9%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in St. Augusta is $947. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $124 (15%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $115 (10%) lower, and it is roughly $80 (7%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
