Compared to its nearby areas, St. Augusta is a little more expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

St. Augusta's median home price is $228,400. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $50,080 (28%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $10,900 (5%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $23,200 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in St. Augusta is $947 a month. This is about $124 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $115 (10%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $80 (7%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
