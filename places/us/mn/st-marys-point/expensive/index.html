Home prices

The median home in St. Marys Point costs $314,600. It is roughly $20,900 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $97,100 (44%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $63,000 (25%) higher.

Rents

In St. Marys Point the median rent is $1,281 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $171 (15%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $219 (20%) higher, and is roughly $254 (24%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
