Home prices

The cost of the median home in Medina is $606,100. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $243,340 (67%) higher.In comparison to the entire nation it is about $388,600 (178%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $354,500 (140%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Medina is $1,338 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $206 (18%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is roughly $276 (25%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $311 (30%) higher.
