Compared to its surrounding areas, Otsego is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Otsego is $261,600. It is about $16,680 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $44,100 (20%) higher, and it is about $10,000 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Otsego's median rent is $1,362 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $395 (40%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $300 (28%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $335 (32%) higher.
