Compared to nearby areas, Otsego is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Otsego is $261,600. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $16,680 (5%) lower. It is roughly $44,100 (20%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $10,000 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Otsego the median rent is $1,362 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $395 (40%) higher. It is roughly $300 (28%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $335 (32%) higher.
