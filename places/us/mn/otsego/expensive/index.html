Home prices

The median home in Otsego is valued at $261,600. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $16,680 (5%) lower. It is roughly $44,100 (20%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $10,000 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Otsego is $1,362. It is roughly $395 (40%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $300 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $335 (32%) higher.
