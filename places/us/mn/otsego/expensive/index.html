In comparison to nearby areas, Otsego is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Otsego's median home price is $261,600. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $16,680 (5%) lower.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $44,100 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $10,000 (3%) higher.

Rents

Otsego's median monthly rent is $1,362. It is about $395 (40%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $300 (28%) higher compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $335 (32%) higher.
