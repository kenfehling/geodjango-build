Home prices

The median home price in Montgomery is $151,600. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $16,640 (9%) lower.It is approximately $65,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $100,000 (39%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Montgomery the median rent is $909 a month. It is roughly $24 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is about $153 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $118 (11%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
