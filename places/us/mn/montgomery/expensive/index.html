Home prices

The median home price in Montgomery is $151,600. It is approximately $16,640 (9%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $65,900 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $100,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomery is $909 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $24 (2%) lower.It is roughly $153 (14%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $118 (11%) lower.
