In comparison to the surrounding areas, Montgomery is a bit less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Montgomery is $151,600. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $16,640 (9%) lower. It is approximately $65,900 (30%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $100,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

Montgomery's median rent is $909 per month. This is about $24 (2%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $153 (14%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $118 (11%) lower.
