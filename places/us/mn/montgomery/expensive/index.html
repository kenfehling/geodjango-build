Compared to its nearby areas, Montgomery is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Montgomery is $151,600. This is roughly $16,640 (9%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $65,900 (30%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $100,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

Montgomery's median rent is $909 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $24 (2%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $153 (14%) lower, and it is about $118 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
