Home prices

The price of the median home in Montgomery is $151,600. This is about $16,640 (9%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $65,900 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $100,000 (39%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Montgomery is $909 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $24 (2%) lower.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $153 (14%) lower, and is roughly $118 (11%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
