In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pequot Lakes is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However it is a little more expensive compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Home prices

The median home in Pequot Lakes costs $164,000. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $63,160 (27%) lower. It is roughly $53,500 (24%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is roughly $8,500 (5%) higher.

Rents

In Pequot Lakes the median rent is $672 per month. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $223 (24%) lower. It is roughly $390 (36%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $130 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.
