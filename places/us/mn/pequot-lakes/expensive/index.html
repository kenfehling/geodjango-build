Compared to other places in this area, Pequot Lakes is a bit less expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Cloud however, it's a bit more expensive.

Home prices

Pequot Lakes's median home cost is $164,000. This is about $63,160 (27%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $53,500 (24%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is approximately $8,500 (5%) higher compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

Pequot Lakes's median rent is $672 a month. This is roughly $223 (24%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.It is approximately $390 (36%) lower in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $130 (16%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
