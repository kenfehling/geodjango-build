Compared to other places in this area, Pequot Lakes is somewhat less expensive, and it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Pequot Lakes is $164,000. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $63,160 (27%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $53,500 (24%) lower, and compared to the city of St. Cloud is roughly $8,500 (5%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Pequot Lakes is $672. This is approximately $223 (24%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $390 (36%) lower, and is roughly $130 (16%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
