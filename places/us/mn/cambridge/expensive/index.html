Home prices

The median home cost in Cambridge is $157,700. It is about $17,540 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $59,800 (27%) lower, and is about $93,900 (37%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Cambridge the median rent is $952 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $158 (19%) higher.It is about $110 (10%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $75 (7%) lower.
