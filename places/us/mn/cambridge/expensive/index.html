In comparison to its surrounding areas, Cambridge is a bit less expensive, and it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Cambridge costs $157,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $17,540 (10%) lower. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $59,800 (27%) lower, and is approximately $93,900 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Cambridge is $952 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $158 (19%) higher. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $110 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $75 (7%) lower.
