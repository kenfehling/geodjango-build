Compared to the surrounding areas, Cambridge is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Cambridge's median home value is $157,700. This is approximately $17,540 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas.It is approximately $59,800 (27%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $93,900 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Cambridge is $952 per month. It is roughly $158 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $110 (10%) lower, and is roughly $75 (7%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
