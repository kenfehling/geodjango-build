Home prices

The median home cost in Champlin is $237,800. This is approximately $8,960 (3%) higher compared to its nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $20,300 (9%) higher, and is roughly $13,800 (5%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Champlin is $1,227. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $162 (15%) higher.It is about $165 (15%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $200 (19%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
