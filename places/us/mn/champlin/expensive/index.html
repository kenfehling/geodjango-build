Rents

The median rent in Champlin is $1,227 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $162 (15%) higher.It is approximately $165 (15%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $200 (19%) higher.
