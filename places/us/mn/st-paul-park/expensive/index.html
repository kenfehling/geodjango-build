Home prices

The price of the median home in St. Paul Park is $191,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $55,340 (22%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $25,700 (11%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $59,800 (23%) lower.

Rents

St. Paul Park's median rent is $1,130 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $39 (3%) lower. It is roughly $68 (6%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $103 (10%) higher.
