Home prices

The cost of the median home in St. Clair is $149,100. This is approximately $17,380 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $68,400 (31%) lower compared to the entire country, and is approximately $102,500 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

St. Clair's median rent is $633 a month. It is roughly $280 (30%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $429 (40%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $394 (38%) lower.
