Home prices

The median home in Gem Lake is valued at $331,700. This is about $52,400 (18%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $114,200 (52%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $80,100 (31%) higher.

Rents

In Gem Lake the median rent is $1,536 a month. It is about $425 (38%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $474 (44%) higher, and is about $509 (49%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
