Compared to other places in this area, New Ulm is a little more expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in New Ulm is valued at $141,300. Compared to other places in this area this is about $12,200 (9%) higher. It is approximately $76,200 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $110,300 (43%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

New Ulm's median monthly rent is $643. This is about $79 (10%) lower compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $419 (39%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $384 (37%) lower.
