Home prices

Oronoco's median home cost is $308,100. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $125,220 (68%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $90,600 (41%) higher, and is about $56,500 (22%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Oronoco's median rent is $938 a month. This is roughly $74 (8%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $124 (11%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $89 (8%) lower.
