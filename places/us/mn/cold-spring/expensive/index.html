Home prices

The median home in Cold Spring costs $170,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is is very similar. It is approximately $47,200 (21%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $81,300 (32%) lower.

Rents

In Cold Spring the median monthly rent is $942. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $129 (15%) higher. It is approximately $120 (11%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $85 (8%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
