Home prices

Henderson's median home value is $137,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $17,080 (11%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $80,200 (36%) lower, and is roughly $114,300 (45%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Henderson's median monthly rent is $788. This is approximately $31 (4%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $274 (25%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $239 (23%) lower.
