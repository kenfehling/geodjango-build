Compared to its surrounding areas, Little Rock is a bit less expensive, and it's substantially less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Bemidji, it is a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Little Rock is $63,000. Compared to nearby areas it is about $6,760 (9%) lower. It is roughly $154,500 (71%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $76,900 (54%) lower compared to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

Little Rock's median monthly rent is $493. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $24 (4%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $569 (53%) lower, and compared to the city of Bemidji it is approximately $294 (37%) lower.
