Compared to other places in this area, Little Rock is somewhat less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Bemidji, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home cost in Little Rock is $63,000. This is approximately $6,760 (9%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $154,500 (71%) lower, and compared to the city of Bemidji it is approximately $76,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

Little Rock's median rent is $493 per month. This is roughly $24 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $569 (53%) lower, and is roughly $294 (37%) lower in comparison to the city of Bemidji.
