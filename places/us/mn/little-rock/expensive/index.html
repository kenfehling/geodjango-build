Home prices

The median home cost in Little Rock is $63,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $6,760 (9%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $154,500 (71%) lower, and compared to the city of Bemidji it is approximately $76,900 (54%) lower.

Rents

Little Rock's median monthly rent is $493. It is roughly $24 (4%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas.It is approximately $569 (53%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bemidji is approximately $294 (37%) lower.
