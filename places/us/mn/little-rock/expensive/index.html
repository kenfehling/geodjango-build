Home prices

The median home in Little Rock costs $63,000. It is about $6,760 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $154,500 (71%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $76,900 (54%) lower in comparison to the city of Bemidji.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Little Rock is $493. It is about $24 (4%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $569 (53%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Bemidji it is roughly $294 (37%) lower.
