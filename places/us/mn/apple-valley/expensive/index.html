Home prices

The price of the median home in Apple Valley is $258,000. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $18,000 (6%) lower. It is about $40,500 (18%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and is approximately $6,400 (2%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Apple Valley the median rent is $1,353 per month. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $141 (11%) higher. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $291 (27%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $326 (31%) higher.
