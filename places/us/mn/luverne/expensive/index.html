Compared to its surrounding areas, Luverne is a lot more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Compared to the city of Sioux Falls however, it's a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Luverne is valued at $141,600. This is about $47,580 (50%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $75,900 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls it is about $48,200 (25%) lower.

Rents

Luverne's median rent is $669 per month. This is about $33 (4%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $393 (37%) lower, and it is roughly $158 (19%) lower compared to the city of Sioux Falls.
