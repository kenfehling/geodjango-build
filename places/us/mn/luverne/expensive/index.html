Home prices

Rents

In Luverne the median monthly rent is $669. It is roughly $33 (4%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $393 (37%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls it is approximately $158 (19%) lower.
