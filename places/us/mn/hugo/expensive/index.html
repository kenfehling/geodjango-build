Compared to its surrounding areas, Hugo is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire country. However it's close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Hugo costs $259,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $97,440 (27%) lower. It is approximately $41,800 (19%) higher compared to the entire country, and it is about $7,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Hugo the median monthly rent is $1,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $303 (25%) higher. It is approximately $438 (41%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $473 (46%) higher.
