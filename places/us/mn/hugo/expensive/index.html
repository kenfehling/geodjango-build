Home prices

Hugo's median home price is $259,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $97,440 (27%) lower.It is approximately $41,800 (19%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $7,700 (3%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Hugo's median rent is $1,500 a month. It is about $303 (25%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $438 (41%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $473 (46%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
