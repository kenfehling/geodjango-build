Compared to the surrounding areas, Hugo is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Hugo costs $259,300. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $97,440 (27%) lower. It is roughly $41,800 (19%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $7,700 (3%) higher.

Rents

Hugo's median rent is $1,500 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is about $303 (25%) higher. It is roughly $438 (41%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $473 (46%) higher.
