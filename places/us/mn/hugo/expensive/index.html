Home prices

The median home in Hugo costs $259,300. It is about $97,440 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $41,800 (19%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $7,700 (3%) higher.

Rents

In Hugo the median monthly rent is $1,500. It is roughly $303 (25%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $438 (41%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is about $473 (46%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
