Home prices

The value of the median home in Barnum is $115,100. This is approximately $20,980 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $102,400 (47%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Duluth is roughly $48,200 (29%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Barnum is $771. This is roughly $122 (18%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $291 (27%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is roughly $49 (5%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
