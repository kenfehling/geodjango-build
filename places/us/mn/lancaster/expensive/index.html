Home prices

The median home cost in Lancaster is $86,100. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,840 (20%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $131,400 (60%) lower, and is about $124,200 (59%) lower compared to the city of Grand Forks.

Rents

Lancaster's median monthly rent is $532. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $54 (9%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $530 (49%) lower, and it is approximately $279 (34%) lower compared to the city of Grand Forks.
