Rents

In Lancaster the median monthly rent is $532. This is approximately $54 (9%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $530 (49%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Grand Forks is roughly $279 (34%) lower.
