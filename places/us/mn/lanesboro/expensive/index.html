Home prices

The median home in Lanesboro costs $140,100. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $4,220 (3%) higher. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $77,400 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Rochester is about $60,000 (29%) lower.

Rents

Lanesboro's median rent is $525 a month. It is approximately $117 (18%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $537 (50%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $449 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
