Home prices

The median home cost in Lanesboro is $140,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $4,220 (3%) higher.It is roughly $77,400 (35%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is approximately $60,000 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.

Rents

Lanesboro's median monthly rent is $525. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $117 (18%) lower.Compared to the entire country it is approximately $537 (50%) lower, and it is about $449 (46%) lower in comparison to the city of Rochester.
