Home prices

The median home in Keewatin is valued at $62,200. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $19,480 (23%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is about $155,300 (71%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is about $101,100 (61%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Keewatin is $698 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $54 (8%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $364 (34%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth is roughly $122 (14%) lower.
