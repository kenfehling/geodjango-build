Home prices

Rents

Nicollet's median monthly rent is $742. This is about $48 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $320 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $285 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
