Home prices

The median home in Nicollet costs $162,200. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $4,420 (2%) lower.It is roughly $55,300 (25%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $89,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

Nicollet's median rent is $742 a month. This is approximately $48 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $320 (30%) lower, and it is approximately $285 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
