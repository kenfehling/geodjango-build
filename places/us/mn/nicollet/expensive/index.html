Much like nearby areas, it's a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Nicollet is valued at $162,200. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $4,420 (2%) lower. It is roughly $55,300 (25%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $89,400 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Nicollet is $742 per month. This is roughly $48 (6%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $285 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
