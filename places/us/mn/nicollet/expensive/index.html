The value of the median home in Nicollet is $162,200. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $4,420 (2%) lower. It is approximately $55,300 (25%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $89,400 (35%) lower.

Rents

Nicollet's median rent is $742 per month. It is roughly $48 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $320 (30%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $285 (27%) lower.
