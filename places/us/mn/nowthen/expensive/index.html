Home prices

The value of the median home in Nowthen is $329,600. This is about $72,980 (28%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $112,100 (51%) higher, and is about $78,000 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Nowthen's median rent is $1,607 per month. This is about $305 (23%) higher compared to other places in this area. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $545 (51%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $580 (56%) higher.
