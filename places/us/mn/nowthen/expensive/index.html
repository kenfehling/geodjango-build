Home prices

The median home in Nowthen is valued at $329,600. This is approximately $72,980 (28%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $112,100 (51%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is about $78,000 (31%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Nowthen's median rent is $1,607 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $305 (23%) higher. Compared to the rest of the nation it is roughly $545 (51%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $580 (56%) higher.
