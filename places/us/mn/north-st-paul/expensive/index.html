Home prices

The price of the median home in North St. Paul is $206,100. Compared to nearby areas this is about $55,380 (21%) lower.In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $11,400 (5%) lower, and it is about $45,500 (18%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In North St. Paul the median rent is $988 a month. This is roughly $226 (18%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $74 (6%) lower compared to the entire nation, and it is about $39 (3%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
