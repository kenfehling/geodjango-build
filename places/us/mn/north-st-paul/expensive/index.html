Home prices

The median home price in North St. Paul is $206,100. It is roughly $55,380 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $11,400 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $45,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

In North St. Paul the median rent is $988 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $226 (18%) lower. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $74 (6%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $39 (3%) lower.
