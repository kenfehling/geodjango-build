Home prices

The median home in North St. Paul costs $206,100. This is approximately $55,380 (21%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $11,400 (5%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $45,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

In North St. Paul the median monthly rent is $988. It is approximately $226 (18%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $74 (6%) lower, and it is roughly $39 (3%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
