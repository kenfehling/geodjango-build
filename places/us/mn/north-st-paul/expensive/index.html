Compared to the surrounding areas, North St. Paul is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a bit less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in North St. Paul is $206,100. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $55,380 (21%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $11,400 (5%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $45,500 (18%) lower.

Rents

In North St. Paul the median monthly rent is $988. This is approximately $226 (18%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $74 (6%) lower, and it is about $39 (3%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
