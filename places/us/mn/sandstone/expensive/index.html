Compared to other places in this area, Sandstone is substantially less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot less expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Sandstone is $77,500. This is approximately $36,800 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $140,000 (64%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is approximately $174,100 (69%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Sandstone's median monthly rent is $824. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $183 (28%) higher. It is roughly $238 (22%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is roughly $203 (19%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
