Home prices

Rents

Sandstone's median rent is $824 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $183 (28%) higher.Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $238 (22%) lower, and it is roughly $203 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
