Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sandstone is $824. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $183 (28%) higher. It is approximately $238 (22%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $203 (19%) lower.
