Home prices

Sauk Centre's median home cost is $144,200. This is roughly $28,740 (24%) higher compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $73,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $11,300 (7%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median rent in Sauk Centre is $642 per month. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is roughly $75 (10%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $420 (39%) lower, and is approximately $160 (19%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
