Compared to the surrounding areas, Sauk Centre is a bit more expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Sauk Centre is valued at $144,200. It is roughly $28,740 (24%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $73,300 (33%) lower, and it is roughly $11,300 (7%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

In Sauk Centre the median monthly rent is $642. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $75 (10%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $420 (39%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is approximately $160 (19%) lower.
