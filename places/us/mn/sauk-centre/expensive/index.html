Home prices

The median home price in Sauk Centre is $144,200. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $28,740 (24%) higher.It is approximately $73,300 (33%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $11,300 (7%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Sauk Centre is $642. This is about $75 (10%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $420 (39%) lower, and is about $160 (19%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
