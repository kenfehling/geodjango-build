In comparison to the surrounding areas, Centerville is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home value in Centerville is $263,500. This is roughly $48,280 (15%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $46,000 (21%) higher, and is approximately $11,900 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $1,163. It is about $394 (25%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $101 (9%) higher, and is approximately $136 (13%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
