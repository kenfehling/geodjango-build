In comparison to other places in this area, Centerville is somewhat less expensive, and it's a bit more expensive compared to the country as a whole. However it's close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Centerville is $263,500. It is about $48,280 (15%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $46,000 (21%) higher, and it is roughly $11,900 (4%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Centerville the median rent is $1,163 per month. This is roughly $394 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $101 (9%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $136 (13%) higher.
