In comparison to its surrounding areas, Centerville is somewhat less expensive, and it is a bit more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

Centerville's median home value is $263,500. This is about $48,280 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is about $46,000 (21%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $11,900 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Centerville's median rent is $1,163 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $394 (25%) lower.It is roughly $101 (9%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is roughly $136 (13%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
