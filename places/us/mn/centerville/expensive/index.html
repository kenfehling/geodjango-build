In comparison to other places in this area, Centerville is a little less expensive, and it is a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home price in Centerville is $263,500. This is roughly $48,280 (15%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is approximately $46,000 (21%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $11,900 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Centerville is $1,163. It is roughly $394 (25%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $101 (9%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $136 (13%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
