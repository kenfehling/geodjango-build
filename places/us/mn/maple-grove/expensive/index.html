Home prices

The cost of the median home in Maple Grove is $293,400. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is roughly $31,500 (12%) higher.It is approximately $75,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and is approximately $41,800 (16%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Maple Grove's median rent is $1,513 a month. This is about $286 (23%) higher compared to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire country it is about $451 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $486 (47%) higher.
