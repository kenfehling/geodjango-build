Home prices

The median home in Maple Grove costs $293,400. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $31,500 (12%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $75,900 (34%) higher, and it is roughly $41,800 (16%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Maple Grove is $1,513 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $286 (23%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $451 (42%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $486 (47%) higher.
