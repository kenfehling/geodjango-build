Compared to the surrounding areas, Spring Park is somewhat less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is a lot more expensive even compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

Spring Park's median home cost is $425,000. This is approximately $88,180 (17%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $207,500 (95%) higher, and is roughly $173,400 (68%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Spring Park's median monthly rent is $1,075. In comparison to other places in this area it is roughly $689 (39%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $13 (1%) higher, and is approximately $48 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
