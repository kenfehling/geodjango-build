In comparison to other places in this area, Spring Park is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

Spring Park's median home cost is $425,000. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $88,180 (17%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $207,500 (95%) higher, and it is about $173,400 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Spring Park the median rent is $1,075 a month. This is approximately $689 (39%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $48 (4%) higher.
