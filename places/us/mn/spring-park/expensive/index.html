In comparison to its surrounding areas, Spring Park is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Spring Park is $425,000. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $88,180 (17%) lower. It is about $207,500 (95%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is about $173,400 (68%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Spring Park's median monthly rent is $1,075. It is roughly $689 (39%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $13 (1%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $48 (4%) higher.
