Home prices

The median home in Lake Shore costs $376,000. It is about $141,700 (60%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is about $158,500 (72%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is roughly $220,500 (141%) higher.

Rents

Lake Shore's median rent is $780 a month. It is about $177 (18%) lower compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $282 (26%) lower, and it is about $22 (2%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
