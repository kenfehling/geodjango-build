Home prices

The median home value in Lake Shore is $376,000. Compared to nearby areas this is about $141,700 (60%) higher. It is approximately $158,500 (72%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $220,500 (141%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lake Shore is $780. Compared to nearby areas it is about $177 (18%) lower. It is about $282 (26%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is roughly $22 (2%) lower.
