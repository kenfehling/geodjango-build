Home prices

The median home in Delano is valued at $258,700. Compared to other places in this area this is approximately $4,940 (1%) lower. It is approximately $41,200 (18%) higher in comparison to the rest of the country, and is roughly $7,100 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Delano's median rent is $851 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $193 (18%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $211 (19%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $176 (17%) lower.
