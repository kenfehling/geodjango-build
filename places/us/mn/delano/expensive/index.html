Home prices

The median home in Delano is valued at $258,700. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $4,940 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $41,200 (18%) higher, and it is approximately $7,100 (2%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Delano is $851. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $193 (18%) lower. It is about $211 (19%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is roughly $176 (17%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
