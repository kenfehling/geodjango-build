Compared to the surrounding areas, West St. Paul is a little less expensive, and it's a little less expensive compared to the entire nation. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in West St. Paul is valued at $199,400. Compared to other places in this area this is about $79,540 (28%) lower.It is approximately $18,100 (8%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $52,200 (20%) lower.

Rents

In West St. Paul the median monthly rent is $1,013. This is about $168 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is approximately $49 (4%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and is roughly $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
