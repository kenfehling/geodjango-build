In comparison to nearby areas, West St. Paul is somewhat less expensive, and it's somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

West St. Paul's median home value is $199,400. This is approximately $79,540 (28%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $18,100 (8%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is approximately $52,200 (20%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

West St. Paul's median rent is $1,013 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $168 (14%) lower. It is roughly $49 (4%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
