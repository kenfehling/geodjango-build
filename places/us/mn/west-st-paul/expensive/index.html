Home prices

West St. Paul's median home value is $199,400. It is approximately $79,540 (28%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is roughly $18,100 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $52,200 (20%) lower.

Rents

West St. Paul's median monthly rent is $1,013. It is approximately $168 (14%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $49 (4%) lower, and is roughly $14 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
