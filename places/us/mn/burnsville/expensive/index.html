In comparison to its surrounding areas, Burnsville is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is close to average.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in Burnsville is $247,500. This is approximately $35,720 (12%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $30,000 (13%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $4,100 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Burnsville is $1,228 per month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $48 (3%) lower.It is about $166 (15%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $201 (19%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
