Home prices

The value of the median home in Burnsville is $247,500. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $35,720 (12%) lower.Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $30,000 (13%) higher, and it is approximately $4,100 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Burnsville is $1,228. It is about $48 (3%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $166 (15%) higher, and is about $201 (19%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
