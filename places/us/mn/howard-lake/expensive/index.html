Home prices

The median home in Howard Lake costs $153,800. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $10,400 (6%) lower. Compared to the entire country it is about $63,700 (29%) lower, and is about $97,800 (38%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Howard Lake's median monthly rent is $774. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $91 (10%) lower. It is approximately $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $253 (24%) lower.
