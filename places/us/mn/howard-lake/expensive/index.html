Home prices

The median home in Howard Lake is valued at $153,800. This is roughly $10,400 (6%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $63,700 (29%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $97,800 (38%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Howard Lake is $774. It is roughly $91 (10%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $253 (24%) lower.
