Home prices

Rents

The median rent in Howard Lake is $774 per month. It is roughly $91 (10%) lower in comparison to other places in this area.It is about $288 (27%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $253 (24%) lower.
