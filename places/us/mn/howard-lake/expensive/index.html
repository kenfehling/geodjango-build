Home prices

Rents

The median monthly rent in Howard Lake is $774. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $91 (10%) lower.It is roughly $288 (27%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is approximately $253 (24%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
