In comparison to the surrounding areas, Hartland is a little less expensive, and it's significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It's a lot less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Hartland is valued at $80,000. It is roughly $19,360 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $171,600 (68%) lower.

Rents

Hartland's median rent is $447 a month. It is about $207 (31%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $615 (57%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $580 (56%) lower.
