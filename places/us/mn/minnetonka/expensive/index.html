Compared to the surrounding areas, Minnetonka is somewhat less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Minnetonka is valued at $347,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $157,400 (31%) lower.It is roughly $130,400 (59%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is approximately $96,300 (38%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Minnetonka the median monthly rent is $1,428. This is approximately $19 (1%) higher compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $366 (34%) higher, and is roughly $401 (39%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
