Compared to its surrounding areas, Minnetonka is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Minnetonka is valued at $347,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $157,400 (31%) lower. It is about $130,400 (59%) higher compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $96,300 (38%) higher.

Rents

Minnetonka's median rent is $1,428 a month. It is approximately $19 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $366 (34%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $401 (39%) higher.
