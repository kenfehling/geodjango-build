In comparison to its nearby areas, Minnetonka is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Minnetonka costs $347,900. This is about $157,400 (31%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $130,400 (59%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $96,300 (38%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Minnetonka's median monthly rent is $1,428. This is approximately $19 (1%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $366 (34%) higher, and it is roughly $401 (39%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
