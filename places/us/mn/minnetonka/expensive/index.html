Compared to other places in this area, Minnetonka is somewhat less expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the rest of the country. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Minnetonka is $347,900. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $157,400 (31%) lower. It is roughly $130,400 (59%) higher compared to the entire nation, and it is approximately $96,300 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Minnetonka is $1,428 per month. It is about $19 (1%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is approximately $366 (34%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $401 (39%) higher.
