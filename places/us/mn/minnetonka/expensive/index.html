In comparison to its surrounding areas, Minnetonka is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Minnetonka costs $347,900. This is approximately $157,400 (31%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is roughly $130,400 (59%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and it is roughly $96,300 (38%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Minnetonka is $1,428. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $19 (1%) higher. It is roughly $366 (34%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $401 (39%) higher.
