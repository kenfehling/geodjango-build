Compared to its surrounding areas, Virginia is a bit more expensive, and it's a lot less expensive compared to the country as a whole. Compared to the city of Duluth, it's significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Virginia costs $92,200. This is approximately $4,680 (5%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is roughly $125,300 (57%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is about $71,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

In Virginia the median rent is $626 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $55 (8%) lower. It is approximately $436 (41%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $194 (23%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
