Home prices

The median home in Virginia is valued at $92,200. It is about $4,680 (5%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $125,300 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is approximately $71,100 (43%) lower.

Rents

Virginia's median rent is $626 a month. It is about $55 (8%) lower compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $436 (41%) lower compared to the US as a whole, and is about $194 (23%) lower compared to the city of Duluth.
