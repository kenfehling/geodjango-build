Rents

Twin Valley's median monthly rent is $630. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $68 (9%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $432 (40%) lower, and it is roughly $193 (23%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.
