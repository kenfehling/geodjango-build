Home prices

The median home cost in Nisswa is $249,800. This is approximately $9,740 (3%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $32,300 (14%) higher, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is roughly $94,300 (60%) higher.

Rents

In Nisswa the median rent is $990 per month. This is roughly $75 (8%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $72 (6%) lower, and it is about $188 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.
