Home prices

Rents

Ivanhoe's median monthly rent is $750. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $191 (34%) higher. It is approximately $312 (29%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and compared to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $77 (9%) lower.
