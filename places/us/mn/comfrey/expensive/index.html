Home prices

The cost of the median home in Comfrey is $82,000. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $17,700 (27%) higher. It is approximately $135,500 (62%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $97,400 (54%) lower compared to the city of Mankato.

Rents

Comfrey's median rent is $563 per month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $54 (8%) lower. It is roughly $499 (46%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Mankato is about $336 (37%) lower.
