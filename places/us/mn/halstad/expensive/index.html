Home prices

The median home in Halstad is valued at $86,900. It is about $6,340 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $130,600 (60%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Fargo is roughly $125,200 (59%) lower.

Rents

In Halstad the median monthly rent is $689. It is roughly $22 (3%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $373 (35%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $134 (16%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.
