Home prices

New Prague's median home value is $247,100. This is about $57,580 (30%) higher compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $29,600 (13%) higher, and is roughly $4,500 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

New Prague's median monthly rent is $880. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $140 (13%) lower. It is about $182 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $147 (14%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
