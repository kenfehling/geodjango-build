Home prices

The median home in New Prague is valued at $247,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $57,580 (30%) higher. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is roughly $29,600 (13%) higher, and is about $4,500 (1%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in New Prague is $880. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is approximately $140 (13%) lower. It is approximately $182 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and it is about $147 (14%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
