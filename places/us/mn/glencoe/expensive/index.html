Home prices

The median home cost in Glencoe is $147,300. It is about $14,780 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $70,200 (32%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and it is about $104,300 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Glencoe is $689 per month. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $123 (15%) lower.It is approximately $373 (35%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $338 (32%) lower.
