Home prices

Rents

In Shakopee the median monthly rent is $1,211. It is approximately $66 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is roughly $149 (14%) higher, and is about $184 (17%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
