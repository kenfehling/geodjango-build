Rents

The median rent in Buckman is $775 per month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $19 (2%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $287 (27%) lower, and is roughly $252 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
