Compared to the surrounding areas, Hopkins is significantly less expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's a little less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Hopkins costs $227,500. It is roughly $195,100 (46%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $10,000 (4%) higher, and it is approximately $24,100 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Hopkins the median rent is $1,032 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $239 (18%) lower. It is roughly $30 (2%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is is very similar.
