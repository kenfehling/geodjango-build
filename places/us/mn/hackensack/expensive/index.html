Rents

The median monthly rent in Hackensack is $656. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $173 (20%) lower. In comparison to the entire nation it is approximately $406 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $146 (18%) lower.
