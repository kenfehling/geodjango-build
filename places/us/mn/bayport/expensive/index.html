The median home value in Bayport is $248,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is about $11,440 (4%) lower. It is roughly $31,400 (14%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $2,700 (1%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bayport is $1,343 per month. It is about $133 (11%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $281 (26%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $316 (30%) higher.
