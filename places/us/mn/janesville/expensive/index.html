Rents

In Janesville the median monthly rent is $763. It is roughly $89 (10%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $299 (28%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $264 (25%) lower.
