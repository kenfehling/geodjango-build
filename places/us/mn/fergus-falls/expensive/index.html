Home prices

The median home in Fergus Falls costs $132,300. It is approximately $35,580 (36%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $85,200 (39%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Fargo it is roughly $79,800 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fergus Falls is $653. Compared to its nearby areas this is approximately $48 (6%) lower. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is about $409 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fargo is about $170 (20%) lower.
