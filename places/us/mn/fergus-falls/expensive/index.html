Home prices

Fergus Falls's median home value is $132,300. Compared to nearby areas it is roughly $35,580 (36%) higher. It is about $85,200 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Fargo is about $79,800 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Fergus Falls the median monthly rent is $653. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $48 (6%) lower. Compared to the country as a whole it is about $409 (38%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo it is about $170 (20%) lower.
