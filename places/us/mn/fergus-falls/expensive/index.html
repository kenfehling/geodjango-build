Home prices

The price of the median home in Fergus Falls is $132,300. It is approximately $35,580 (36%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $85,200 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo it is approximately $79,800 (37%) lower.

Rents

In Fergus Falls the median rent is $653 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $48 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $409 (38%) lower, and it is about $170 (20%) lower in comparison to the city of Fargo.
