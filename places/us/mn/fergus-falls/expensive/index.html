Home prices

The median home in Fergus Falls is valued at $132,300. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $35,580 (36%) higher.Compared to the entire country it is about $85,200 (39%) lower, and compared to the city of Fargo is roughly $79,800 (37%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Fergus Falls is $653. Compared to other places in this area this is about $48 (6%) lower.It is about $409 (38%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $170 (20%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.
