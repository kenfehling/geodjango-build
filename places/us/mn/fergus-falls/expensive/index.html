Home prices

The median home in Fergus Falls costs $132,300. It is about $35,580 (36%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $85,200 (39%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $79,800 (37%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.

Rents

Fergus Falls's median monthly rent is $653. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $48 (6%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is about $409 (38%) lower, and is about $170 (20%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.
