Home prices

Fergus Falls's median home value is $132,300. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $35,580 (36%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is roughly $85,200 (39%) lower, and is approximately $79,800 (37%) lower compared to the city of Fargo.

Rents

In Fergus Falls the median monthly rent is $653. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $48 (6%) lower. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $409 (38%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Fargo it is about $170 (20%) lower.
