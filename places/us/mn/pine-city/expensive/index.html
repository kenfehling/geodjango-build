In comparison to its surrounding areas, Pine City is somewhat more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis however, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

Pine City's median home price is $148,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is approximately $24,220 (19%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $69,200 (31%) lower, and it is roughly $103,300 (41%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Pine City is $725 per month. It is roughly $243 (25%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $302 (29%) lower.
