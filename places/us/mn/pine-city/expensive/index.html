Home prices

Pine City's median home price is $148,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $24,220 (19%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $69,200 (31%) lower, and is roughly $103,300 (41%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Pine City the median monthly rent is $725. It is approximately $243 (25%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $337 (31%) lower, and is approximately $302 (29%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
