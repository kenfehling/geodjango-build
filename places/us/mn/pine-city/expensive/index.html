Home prices

The median home in Pine City costs $148,300. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $24,220 (19%) higher.It is approximately $69,200 (31%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $103,300 (41%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Pine City is $725 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $243 (25%) lower.It is about $337 (31%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $302 (29%) lower.
