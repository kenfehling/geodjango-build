Home prices

The median home price in Buffalo is $203,700. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $5,640 (2%) higher. It is roughly $13,800 (6%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and it is roughly $47,900 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Buffalo the median rent is $977 per month. It is approximately $17 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $85 (8%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $50 (4%) lower.
