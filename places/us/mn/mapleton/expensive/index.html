Home prices

The median home value in Mapleton is $124,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $14,860 (13%) higher.It is about $93,200 (42%) lower in comparison to the rest of the country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $127,300 (50%) lower.

Rents

Mapleton's median monthly rent is $664. Compared to other places in this area it is about $50 (7%) lower.It is about $398 (37%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and is about $363 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
