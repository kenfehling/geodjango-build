In comparison to the surrounding areas, Mapleton is somewhat more expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the entire country. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is significantly less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Mapleton is $124,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is roughly $14,860 (13%) higher.It is approximately $93,200 (42%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $127,300 (50%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Mapleton is $664 a month. It is about $50 (7%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is roughly $398 (37%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is about $363 (35%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.
