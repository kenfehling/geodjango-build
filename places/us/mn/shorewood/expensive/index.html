Home prices

The median home in Shorewood costs $485,300. It is about $156,160 (24%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is approximately $267,800 (123%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $233,700 (92%) higher.

Rents

In Shorewood the median rent is $1,639 a month. This is approximately $177 (12%) higher compared to nearby areas.It is roughly $577 (54%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is approximately $612 (59%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
