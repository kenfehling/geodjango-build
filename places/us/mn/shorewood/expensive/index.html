Compared to nearby areas, Shorewood is a bit less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Shorewood is $485,300. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $156,160 (24%) lower. It is about $267,800 (123%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $233,700 (92%) higher.

Rents

Shorewood's median rent is $1,639 per month. This is approximately $177 (12%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $577 (54%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $612 (59%) higher.
