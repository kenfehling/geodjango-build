Home prices

The median home in Flensburg is valued at $151,300. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $37,620 (33%) higher.Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is about $4,200 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Flensburg is $579. In comparison to its nearby areas this is about $94 (14%) lower.It is about $483 (45%) lower compared to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud is roughly $223 (27%) lower.
