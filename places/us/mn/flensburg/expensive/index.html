Home prices

The median home price in Flensburg is $151,300. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $37,620 (33%) higher. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower, and it is roughly $4,200 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Flensburg is $579. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $94 (14%) lower. It is about $483 (45%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $223 (27%) lower.
