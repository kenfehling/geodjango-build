In comparison to its surrounding areas, Flensburg is significantly more expensive, and it is a bit less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Flensburg costs $151,300. This is about $37,620 (33%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $66,200 (30%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is roughly $4,200 (2%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Flensburg is $579. This is about $94 (14%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $483 (45%) lower, and is roughly $223 (27%) lower in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.
