Home prices

Circle Pines's median home price is $197,100. This is roughly $39,420 (16%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is about $20,400 (9%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $54,500 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Circle Pines is $1,308. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $159 (13%) higher. It is about $246 (23%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and is roughly $281 (27%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
