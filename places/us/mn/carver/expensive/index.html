Home prices

The median home in Carver costs $337,500. This is about $22,240 (7%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $120,000 (55%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $85,900 (34%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Carver is $2,429. Compared to its nearby areas it is roughly $1,197 (97%) higher. It is about $1,367 (128%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $1,402 (136%) higher.
