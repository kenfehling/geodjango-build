Home prices

Carver's median home cost is $337,500. It is about $22,240 (7%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $120,000 (55%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $85,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Carver is $2,429 per month. It is approximately $1,197 (97%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $1,367 (128%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and it is approximately $1,402 (136%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
