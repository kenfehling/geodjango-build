In comparison to its surrounding areas, Carver is a little more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it's substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Carver is valued at $337,500. It is approximately $22,240 (7%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $120,000 (55%) higher, and it is about $85,900 (34%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Carver the median monthly rent is $2,429. Compared to its surrounding areas this is approximately $1,197 (97%) higher. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $1,367 (128%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $1,402 (136%) higher.
