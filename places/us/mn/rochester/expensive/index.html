Home prices

The median home cost in Rochester is $200,100. This is about $12,640 (5%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $17,400 (7%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $51,500 (20%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Rochester is $974. This is approximately $160 (19%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $88 (8%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $53 (5%) lower.
