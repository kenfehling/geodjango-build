Compared to its nearby areas, Lakeville is a bit more expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the US as a whole. It's somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Lakeville costs $311,100. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $33,180 (11%) higher. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $93,600 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is about $59,500 (23%) higher.

Rents

In Lakeville the median rent is $1,360 a month. This is roughly $148 (12%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. It is about $298 (28%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $333 (32%) higher.
