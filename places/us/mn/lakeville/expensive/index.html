Compared to nearby areas, Lakeville is a little more expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It is a bit more expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The price of the median home in Lakeville is $311,100. It is roughly $33,180 (11%) higher compared to other places in this area. It is approximately $93,600 (43%) higher compared to the US as a whole, and it is about $59,500 (23%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Lakeville is $1,360. Compared to its surrounding areas it is about $148 (12%) higher. It is about $298 (28%) higher compared to the entire country, and is approximately $333 (32%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.
