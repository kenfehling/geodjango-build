Home prices

The median home in Soudan is valued at $70,800. This is approximately $9,140 (11%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $146,700 (67%) lower, and compared to the city of Duluth it is roughly $92,500 (56%) lower.

Rents

In Soudan the median rent is $475 a month. In comparison to other places in this area this is about $126 (20%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $587 (55%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Duluth it is approximately $345 (42%) lower.
