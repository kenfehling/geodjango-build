Home prices

The median home in Elkton costs $80,000. In comparison to nearby areas this is about $22,900 (22%) lower. It is about $137,500 (63%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is approximately $171,600 (68%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Elkton the median monthly rent is $725. This is roughly $146 (25%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $337 (31%) lower, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $302 (29%) lower.
