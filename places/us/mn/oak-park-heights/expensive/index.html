In comparison to the surrounding areas, Oak Park Heights is a little less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home cost in Oak Park Heights is $245,400. It is roughly $49,300 (16%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $27,900 (12%) higher, and it is about $6,200 (2%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Oak Park Heights's median monthly rent is $1,641. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $457 (38%) higher. It is approximately $579 (54%) higher in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $614 (59%) higher.
