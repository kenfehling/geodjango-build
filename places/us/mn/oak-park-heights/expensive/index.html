Compared to other places in this area, Oak Park Heights is a bit less expensive, and it's a little more expensive compared to the entire country. However it is close to average compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Oak Park Heights costs $245,400. In comparison to its nearby areas it is about $49,300 (16%) lower. It is about $27,900 (12%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $6,200 (2%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Oak Park Heights the median rent is $1,641 a month. It is roughly $457 (38%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is approximately $579 (54%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is about $614 (59%) higher.
