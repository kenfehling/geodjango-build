Rents

In Oakdale the median rent is $1,073 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas it is approximately $73 (6%) lower.It is about $11 (1%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $46 (4%) higher.
