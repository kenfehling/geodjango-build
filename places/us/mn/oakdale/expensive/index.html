Compared to the surrounding areas, Oakdale is somewhat less expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. Compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Oakdale costs $227,700. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $48,440 (17%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $10,200 (4%) higher, and is approximately $23,900 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Oakdale's median rent is $1,073 a month. It is roughly $73 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $11 (1%) higher in comparison to the entire nation, and it is about $46 (4%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
