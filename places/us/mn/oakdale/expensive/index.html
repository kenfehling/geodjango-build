Home prices

The median home in Oakdale costs $227,700. This is about $48,440 (17%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the nation as a whole it is about $10,200 (4%) higher, and is about $23,900 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Oakdale's median monthly rent is $1,073. This is roughly $73 (6%) lower in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the entire nation it is about $11 (1%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $46 (4%) higher.
