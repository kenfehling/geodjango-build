Home prices

The median home in Northfield is valued at $226,000. It is roughly $36,140 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is about $8,500 (3%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $25,600 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Northfield is $825. In comparison to its nearby areas it is roughly $294 (26%) lower. It is roughly $237 (22%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is about $202 (19%) lower.
