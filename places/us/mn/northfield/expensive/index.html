In comparison to the surrounding areas, Northfield is a bit more expensive, and it is similar compared to the entire country. However it's somewhat less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home value in Northfield is $226,000. This is about $36,140 (19%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas.It is roughly $8,500 (3%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $25,600 (10%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Northfield's median rent is $825 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas it is approximately $294 (26%) lower.It is about $237 (22%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $202 (19%) lower.
