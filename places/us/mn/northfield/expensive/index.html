Home prices

Northfield's median home value is $226,000. Compared to other places in this area this is roughly $36,140 (19%) higher.In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $8,500 (3%) higher, and is roughly $25,600 (10%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Northfield's median rent is $825 per month. It is about $294 (26%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.It is about $237 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $202 (19%) lower.
