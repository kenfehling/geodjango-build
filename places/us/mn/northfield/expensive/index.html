In comparison to the surrounding areas, Northfield is a little more expensive, and it is similar compared to the US as a whole. However compared to the city of Minneapolis, it is somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Northfield is $226,000. In comparison to nearby areas it is about $36,140 (19%) higher. It is approximately $8,500 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is roughly $25,600 (10%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Northfield the median rent is $825 a month. It is approximately $294 (26%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is about $237 (22%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $202 (19%) lower.
