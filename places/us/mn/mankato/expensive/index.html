Much like other places in this area, it's a bit less expensive compared to the nation as a whole. It's a bit less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home in Mankato is valued at $179,400. This is about $8,400 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. It is about $38,100 (17%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $72,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

In Mankato the median monthly rent is $899. Compared to the surrounding areas it is roughly $79 (9%) higher. It is approximately $163 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $128 (12%) lower.
