Much like nearby areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. It's a little less expensive compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Home prices

The median home value in Mankato is $179,400. It is approximately $8,400 (4%) higher compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $38,100 (17%) lower, and is about $72,200 (28%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Mankato is $899 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $79 (9%) higher. It is roughly $163 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $128 (12%) lower.
