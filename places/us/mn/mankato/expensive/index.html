Home prices

The median home in Mankato is valued at $179,400. In comparison to other places in this area it is about $8,400 (4%) higher. It is about $38,100 (17%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $72,200 (28%) lower.

Rents

Mankato's median rent is $899 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $79 (9%) higher. In comparison to the entire country it is about $163 (15%) lower, and is roughly $128 (12%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
