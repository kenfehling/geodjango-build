Home prices

The value of the median home in Greenfield is $428,300. It is roughly $112,640 (35%) higher compared to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $210,800 (96%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $176,700 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Greenfield's median monthly rent is $1,313. This is about $208 (18%) higher compared to other places in this area. Compared to the United States as a whole it is approximately $251 (23%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is approximately $286 (27%) higher.
