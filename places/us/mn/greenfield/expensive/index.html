Compared to the surrounding areas, Greenfield is substantially more expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the US as a whole. Compared to even the city of Minneapolis, it is a lot more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Greenfield costs $428,300. This is roughly $112,640 (35%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is approximately $210,800 (96%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $176,700 (70%) higher in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Greenfield's median rent is $1,313 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $208 (18%) higher.In comparison to the entire country it is about $251 (23%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $286 (27%) higher.
