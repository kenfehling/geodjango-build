The median home cost in Darwin is $117,900. This is about $7,580 (6%) lower compared to nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $99,600 (45%) lower, and is roughly $133,700 (53%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

In Darwin the median monthly rent is $650. It is about $86 (11%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is approximately $412 (38%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is approximately $377 (36%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
