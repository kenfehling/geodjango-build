Home prices

The median home in New Auburn is valued at $93,300. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $32,620 (25%) lower. It is about $124,200 (57%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and is about $158,300 (62%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

New Auburn's median monthly rent is $620. This is approximately $78 (11%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is roughly $442 (41%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is roughly $407 (39%) lower.
