Home prices

The price of the median home in Rockford is $181,900. This is about $167,720 (47%) lower compared to nearby areas.Compared to the country as a whole it is about $35,600 (16%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Minneapolis is approximately $69,700 (27%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Rockford is $974 per month. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $33 (3%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is about $88 (8%) lower, and is about $53 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
