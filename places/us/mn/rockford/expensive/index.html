Rents

In Rockford the median rent is $974 per month. This is approximately $33 (3%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $88 (8%) lower, and is approximately $53 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
