Home prices

The median home price in Rockford is $181,900. It is about $167,720 (47%) lower compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $35,600 (16%) lower, and is approximately $69,700 (27%) lower compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

Rockford's median monthly rent is $974. This is approximately $33 (3%) lower compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is approximately $88 (8%) lower, and is about $53 (5%) lower in comparison to the city of Minneapolis.
