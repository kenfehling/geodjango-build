Home prices

The median home in Minneota costs $108,900. It is about $18,660 (20%) higher compared to its nearby areas. Compared to the entire country it is roughly $108,600 (49%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is roughly $80,900 (42%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Minneota is $685 per month. It is approximately $197 (40%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $377 (35%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Sioux Falls is approximately $142 (17%) lower.
