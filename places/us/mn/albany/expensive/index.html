In comparison to its surrounding areas, Albany is a little more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of St. Cloud however, it's close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Albany is valued at $155,900. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $11,900 (8%) higher.It is about $61,600 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

The median rent in Albany is $493 per month. This is roughly $145 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $569 (53%) lower, and in comparison to the city of St. Cloud it is approximately $309 (38%) lower.
