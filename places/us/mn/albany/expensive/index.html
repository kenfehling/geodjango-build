Compared to nearby areas, Albany is a bit more expensive, and it is somewhat less expensive compared to the country as a whole. However compared to the city of St. Cloud, it is close to average.

Home prices

The median home in Albany costs $155,900. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $11,900 (8%) higher. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $61,600 (28%) lower, and is is very similar in comparison to the city of St. Cloud.

Rents

In Albany the median rent is $493 per month. This is about $145 (22%) lower compared to its surrounding areas. It is about $569 (53%) lower in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $309 (38%) lower compared to the city of St. Cloud.
