Home prices

The median home in Edina is valued at $476,300. In comparison to other places in this area this is approximately $211,600 (79%) higher. It is approximately $258,800 (118%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $224,700 (89%) higher.

Rents

In Edina the median rent is $1,404 per month. It is about $214 (18%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the country as a whole it is approximately $342 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is roughly $377 (36%) higher.
