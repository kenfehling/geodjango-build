Compared to other places in this area, Edina is substantially more expensive, and it's a lot more expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Even when compared to the city of Minneapolis, it's significantly more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Edina costs $476,300. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $211,600 (79%) higher. It is about $258,800 (118%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and it is roughly $224,700 (89%) higher compared to the city of Minneapolis.

Rents

The median rent in Edina is $1,404 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is roughly $214 (18%) higher. In comparison to the US as a whole it is approximately $342 (32%) higher, and compared to the city of Minneapolis it is about $377 (36%) higher.
