Compared to its surrounding areas, Roswell is a bit less expensive, and it's significantly more expensive compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's a little more expensive.

Home prices

The median home value in Roswell is $374,700. This is approximately $41,660 (10%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $157,200 (72%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $84,300 (29%) higher.

Rents

Roswell's median rent is $1,281 per month. It is approximately $160 (11%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $219 (20%) higher, and it is roughly $128 (11%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
