Rents

In Roswell the median monthly rent is $1,281. Compared to the surrounding areas this is roughly $160 (11%) lower. It is roughly $219 (20%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $128 (11%) higher.
