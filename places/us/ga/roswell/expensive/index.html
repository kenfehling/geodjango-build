In comparison to the surrounding areas, Roswell is a little less expensive, and it's substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is somewhat more expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Roswell costs $374,700. It is approximately $41,660 (10%) lower compared to other places in this area. Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $157,200 (72%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $84,300 (29%) higher.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Roswell is $1,281. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is about $160 (11%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is about $219 (20%) higher, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $128 (11%) higher.
