Compared to its surrounding areas, Atlanta is a little less expensive, and it is significantly more expensive compared to the country as a whole.

Home prices

The median home cost in Atlanta is $290,400. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $29,017 (9%) lower. In comparison to the US as a whole it is roughly $72,900 (33%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Atlanta is $1,153 a month. This is about $48 (4%) lower in comparison to other places in this area. It is approximately $91 (8%) higher compared to the United States as a whole.
