In comparison to its nearby areas, Atlanta is a bit less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the rest of the nation.

Home prices

The value of the median home in Atlanta is $290,400. It is roughly $29,017 (9%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $72,900 (33%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole.

Rents

Atlanta's median rent is $1,153 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is roughly $48 (4%) lower. In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $91 (8%) higher.
