Home prices

The median home in Hamilton is valued at $219,700. This is roughly $98,720 (81%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire nation it is roughly $2,200 (1%) higher, and it is about $70,700 (24%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Hamilton is $1,076 a month. In comparison to its surrounding areas this is roughly $370 (52%) higher. It is roughly $14 (1%) higher compared to the entire nation, and is about $77 (6%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
