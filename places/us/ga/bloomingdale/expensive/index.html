Home prices

Bloomingdale's median home cost is $141,900. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $29,440 (17%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $75,600 (34%) lower, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $14,600 (9%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Bloomingdale is $835 a month. Compared to the surrounding areas this is about $296 (26%) lower. Compared to the rest of the nation it is about $227 (21%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is roughly $184 (18%) lower.
