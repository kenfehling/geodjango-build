Home prices

The median home value in Locust Grove is $155,200. Compared to nearby areas this is about $10,680 (7%) higher.It is about $62,300 (28%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $135,200 (46%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Locust Grove is $1,173 a month. Compared to nearby areas it is about $117 (11%) higher.It is about $111 (10%) higher compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $20 (1%) higher.
