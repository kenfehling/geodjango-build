Home prices

The median home price in Locust Grove is $155,200. In comparison to nearby areas it is approximately $10,680 (7%) higher. It is roughly $62,300 (28%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $135,200 (46%) lower.

Rents

Locust Grove's median rent is $1,173 per month. In comparison to other places in this area it is approximately $117 (11%) higher. Compared to the rest of the country it is about $111 (10%) higher, and it is about $20 (1%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
