Home prices

Locust Grove's median home price is $155,200. This is approximately $10,680 (7%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.It is approximately $62,300 (28%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and it is approximately $135,200 (46%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median monthly rent in Locust Grove is $1,173. It is about $117 (11%) higher compared to the surrounding areas.In comparison to the US as a whole it is about $111 (10%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $20 (1%) higher.
