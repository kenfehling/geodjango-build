Home prices

Wilmington Island's median home value is $263,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is approximately $102,080 (27%) lower. It is approximately $46,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and it is roughly $107,200 (68%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

In Wilmington Island the median rent is $1,260 a month. It is about $32 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $198 (18%) higher, and is about $241 (23%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.
