Home prices

The median home in Wilmington Island costs $263,700. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $102,080 (27%) lower.It is about $46,200 (21%) higher compared to the United States as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is roughly $107,200 (68%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington Island is $1,260 per month. Compared to its nearby areas it is approximately $32 (2%) lower.Compared to the rest of the country it is approximately $198 (18%) higher, and compared to the city of Savannah is roughly $241 (23%) higher.
