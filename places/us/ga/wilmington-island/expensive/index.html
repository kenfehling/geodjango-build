Home prices

The cost of the median home in Wilmington Island is $263,700. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $102,080 (27%) lower.It is roughly $46,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Savannah it is about $107,200 (68%) higher.

Rents

The median rent in Wilmington Island is $1,260 a month. In comparison to its nearby areas this is roughly $32 (2%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $198 (18%) higher, and is approximately $241 (23%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.
