Home prices

The median home cost in Wilmington Island is $263,700. This is roughly $102,080 (27%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. Compared to the nation as a whole it is approximately $46,200 (21%) higher, and is approximately $107,200 (68%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Wilmington Island's median rent is $1,260 per month. This is approximately $32 (2%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $198 (18%) higher, and is roughly $241 (23%) higher in comparison to the city of Savannah.
