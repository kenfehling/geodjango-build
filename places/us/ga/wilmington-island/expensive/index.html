In comparison to its surrounding areas, Wilmington Island is a bit less expensive, and it's somewhat more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Savannah, it is substantially more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Wilmington Island is valued at $263,700. This is approximately $102,080 (27%) lower in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $46,200 (21%) higher in comparison to the US as a whole, and it is about $107,200 (68%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

Wilmington Island's median rent is $1,260 per month. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $32 (2%) lower. It is roughly $198 (18%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Savannah it is about $241 (23%) higher.
