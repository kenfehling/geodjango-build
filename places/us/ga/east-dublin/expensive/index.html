Home prices

The value of the median home in East Dublin is $93,500. This is approximately $11,300 (13%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is about $124,000 (57%) lower compared to the entire country, and in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County it is approximately $26,700 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in East Dublin is $639 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $39 (5%) lower. Compared to the entire nation it is approximately $423 (39%) lower, and it is about $179 (21%) lower in comparison to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
