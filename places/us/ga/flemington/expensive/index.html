Home prices

Flemington's median home value is $183,900. This is approximately $62,080 (50%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $33,600 (15%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is approximately $27,400 (17%) higher compared to the city of Savannah.

Rents

The median rent in Flemington is $825 per month. This is about $48 (5%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. In comparison to the entire country it is roughly $237 (22%) lower, and is about $194 (19%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
