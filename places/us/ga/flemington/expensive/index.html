Home prices

The value of the median home in Flemington is $183,900. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $62,080 (50%) higher.It is about $33,600 (15%) lower compared to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Savannah is roughly $27,400 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Flemington the median monthly rent is $825. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is approximately $48 (5%) lower.It is roughly $237 (22%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and is roughly $194 (19%) lower in comparison to the city of Savannah.
