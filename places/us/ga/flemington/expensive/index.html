Home prices

The median home in Flemington is valued at $183,900. This is approximately $62,080 (50%) higher in comparison to the surrounding areas. It is approximately $33,600 (15%) lower compared to the entire country, and compared to the city of Savannah is approximately $27,400 (17%) higher.

Rents

In Flemington the median monthly rent is $825. Compared to its surrounding areas it is roughly $48 (5%) lower. Compared to the nation as a whole it is about $237 (22%) lower, and is about $194 (19%) lower compared to the city of Savannah.
