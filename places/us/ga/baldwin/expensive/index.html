The median home value in Baldwin is $111,800. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $1,480 (1%) higher. Compared to the United States as a whole it is about $105,700 (48%) lower, and is roughly $178,600 (61%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Baldwin is $607 a month. This is about $171 (22%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is approximately $455 (42%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $546 (47%) lower.
