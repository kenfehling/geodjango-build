Home prices

The median home in Baldwin is valued at $111,800. This is about $1,480 (1%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the nation as a whole it is roughly $105,700 (48%) lower, and it is about $178,600 (61%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

The median rent in Baldwin is $607 a month. In comparison to nearby areas this is approximately $171 (22%) lower. It is about $455 (42%) lower compared to the entire country, and is roughly $546 (47%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
