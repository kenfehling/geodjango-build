Home prices

The median home in Danville is valued at $65,300. It is about $17,640 (21%) lower compared to its nearby areas. It is approximately $152,200 (69%) lower compared to the country as a whole, and compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County is about $54,900 (45%) lower.

Rents

In Danville the median monthly rent is $582. Compared to the surrounding areas this is approximately $103 (15%) lower. Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $480 (45%) lower, and is approximately $236 (28%) lower compared to the city of Macon-Bibb County.
