Home prices

The median home in Kingsland is valued at $156,700. In comparison to the surrounding areas this is about $44,980 (40%) higher.It is about $60,800 (27%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is roughly $16,500 (9%) lower in comparison to the city of Jacksonville.

Rents

Kingsland's median rent is $907 a month. This is roughly $154 (20%) higher compared to other places in this area.It is roughly $155 (14%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Jacksonville is about $158 (14%) lower.
