Home prices

The median home in LaFayette is valued at $93,500. This is approximately $17,440 (15%) lower compared to other places in this area. It is roughly $124,000 (57%) lower compared to the entire country, and it is about $196,900 (67%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In LaFayette the median monthly rent is $659. It is about $89 (11%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $403 (37%) lower, and it is approximately $494 (42%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
