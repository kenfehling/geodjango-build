In comparison to the surrounding areas, LaFayette is a little less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the United States as a whole. Compared to the city of Atlanta, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The cost of the median home in LaFayette is $93,500. It is about $17,440 (15%) lower in comparison to nearby areas. It is roughly $124,000 (57%) lower compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $196,900 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in LaFayette is $659 per month. In comparison to other places in this area this is roughly $89 (11%) lower. It is about $403 (37%) lower in comparison to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $494 (42%) lower.
