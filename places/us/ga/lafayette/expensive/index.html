Home prices

The value of the median home in LaFayette is $93,500. In comparison to nearby areas this is roughly $17,440 (15%) lower.In comparison to the rest of the country it is approximately $124,000 (57%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $196,900 (67%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in LaFayette is $659 a month. It is about $89 (11%) lower compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the entire country it is approximately $403 (37%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is approximately $494 (42%) lower.
