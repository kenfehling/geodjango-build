Compared to other places in this area, Valdosta is a little less expensive, and it is substantially less expensive compared to the rest of the country. It is significantly less expensive compared to the city of Tallahassee.

Home prices

Valdosta's median home cost is $121,800. It is roughly $8,420 (6%) lower in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $95,700 (43%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and it is roughly $82,000 (40%) lower in comparison to the city of Tallahassee.

Rents

Valdosta's median rent is $780 a month. In comparison to nearby areas it is is very similar. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $282 (26%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Tallahassee is roughly $243 (23%) lower.
