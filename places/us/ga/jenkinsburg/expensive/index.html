Home prices

Rents

In Jenkinsburg the median rent is $1,097 a month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $52 (4%) higher. Compared to the entire country it is about $35 (3%) higher, and it is approximately $56 (4%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.
