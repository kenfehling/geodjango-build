Rents

The median monthly rent in Jenkinsburg is $1,097. This is about $52 (4%) higher compared to other places in this area.In comparison to the United States as a whole it is roughly $35 (3%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $56 (4%) lower.
