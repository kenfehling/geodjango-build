Home prices

The median home in Jenkinsburg is valued at $112,500. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $36,840 (24%) lower. It is about $105,000 (48%) lower compared to the United States as a whole, and is approximately $177,900 (61%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

Jenkinsburg's median rent is $1,097 a month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $52 (4%) higher. It is approximately $35 (3%) higher in comparison to the United States as a whole, and is about $56 (4%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
