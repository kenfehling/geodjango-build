Much like other places in this area, it is a little less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. However it is a lot less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home cost in Kennesaw is $194,800. It is roughly $4,820 (2%) lower compared to its nearby areas.In comparison to the country as a whole it is about $22,700 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is roughly $95,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

Kennesaw's median rent is $1,270 a month. It is approximately $169 (15%) higher in comparison to nearby areas.Compared to the US as a whole it is approximately $208 (19%) higher, and is about $117 (10%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
