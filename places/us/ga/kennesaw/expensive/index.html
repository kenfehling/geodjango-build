Much like the surrounding areas, it is somewhat less expensive compared to the rest of the country. However it is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home price in Kennesaw is $194,800. This is approximately $4,820 (2%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $22,700 (10%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is about $95,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

Kennesaw's median monthly rent is $1,270. It is approximately $169 (15%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is roughly $208 (19%) higher in comparison to the country as a whole, and is about $117 (10%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
