Much like the surrounding areas, it is a little less expensive compared to the entire country. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is substantially less expensive.

Home prices

The median home in Kennesaw is valued at $194,800. Compared to its nearby areas this is roughly $4,820 (2%) lower.Compared to the US as a whole it is about $22,700 (10%) lower, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $95,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kennesaw is $1,270 per month. Compared to its surrounding areas this is about $169 (15%) higher.It is approximately $208 (19%) higher compared to the nation as a whole, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is about $117 (10%) higher.
