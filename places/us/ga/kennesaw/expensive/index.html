Home prices

Kennesaw's median home value is $194,800. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $4,820 (2%) lower. It is roughly $22,700 (10%) lower compared to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $95,600 (32%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Kennesaw is $1,270 per month. It is about $169 (15%) higher compared to nearby areas. It is roughly $208 (19%) higher in comparison to the nation as a whole, and compared to the city of Atlanta is about $117 (10%) higher.
