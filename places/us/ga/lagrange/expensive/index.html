Home prices

The median home in LaGrange is valued at $127,100. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $13,000 (11%) higher. It is roughly $90,400 (41%) lower in comparison to the entire nation, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $163,300 (56%) lower.

Rents

The median monthly rent in LaGrange is $814. This is about $108 (15%) higher in comparison to other places in this area. Compared to the rest of the country it is roughly $248 (23%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $339 (29%) lower.
