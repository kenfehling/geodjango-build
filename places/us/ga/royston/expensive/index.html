Home prices

Royston's median home cost is $99,700. Compared to the surrounding areas it is about $12,020 (13%) higher. It is approximately $117,800 (54%) lower compared to the entire nation, and is about $372,100 (78%) lower compared to the city of Sandy Springs.

Rents

Royston's median monthly rent is $582. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is roughly $65 (10%) lower. It is about $480 (45%) lower in comparison to the nation as a whole, and is about $745 (56%) lower compared to the city of Sandy Springs.
