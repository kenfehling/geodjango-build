Home prices

The median home cost in North Druid Hills is $366,800. This is approximately $62,140 (14%) lower compared to its nearby areas.It is roughly $149,300 (68%) higher compared to the rest of the nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $76,400 (26%) higher.

Rents

In North Druid Hills the median rent is $1,456 per month. Compared to other places in this area it is approximately $209 (16%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $394 (37%) higher, and is about $303 (26%) higher in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
