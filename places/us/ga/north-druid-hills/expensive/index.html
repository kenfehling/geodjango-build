Compared to the surrounding areas, North Druid Hills is a little less expensive, and it is substantially more expensive compared to the entire nation. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's somewhat more expensive.

Home prices

The median home in North Druid Hills costs $366,800. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $62,140 (14%) lower. It is roughly $149,300 (68%) higher compared to the country as a whole, and is approximately $76,400 (26%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

North Druid Hills's median monthly rent is $1,456. It is about $209 (16%) higher in comparison to its surrounding areas. It is roughly $394 (37%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and is about $303 (26%) higher compared to the city of Atlanta.
