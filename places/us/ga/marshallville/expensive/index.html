Home prices

The price of the median home in Marshallville is $67,900. This is approximately $16,420 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. In comparison to the rest of the nation it is approximately $149,600 (68%) lower, and it is about $73,400 (51%) lower compared to the city of Columbus.

Rents

Marshallville's median rent is $442 a month. It is roughly $153 (25%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. Compared to the rest of the nation it is approximately $620 (58%) lower, and compared to the city of Columbus it is approximately $464 (51%) lower.
