Home prices

The median home in Turin is valued at $311,700. This is about $113,160 (56%) higher compared to its surrounding areas.Compared to the nation as a whole it is roughly $94,200 (43%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta it is roughly $21,300 (7%) higher.

Rents

In Turin the median monthly rent is $844. Compared to nearby areas it is approximately $229 (21%) lower.It is about $218 (20%) lower compared to the rest of the country, and is about $309 (26%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.
