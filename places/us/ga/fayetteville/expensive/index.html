Compared to its nearby areas, Fayetteville is a little more expensive, and it's similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it's somewhat less expensive.

Home prices

The median home price in Fayetteville is $224,100. This is roughly $39,400 (21%) higher in comparison to its nearby areas. It is about $6,600 (3%) higher in comparison to the rest of the nation, and it is about $66,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Fayetteville the median rent is $1,157 a month. It is roughly $13 (1%) lower in comparison to its nearby areas. It is approximately $95 (8%) higher compared to the rest of the country, and is is very similar compared to the city of Atlanta.
