Compared to the surrounding areas, Fayetteville is somewhat more expensive, and it is similar compared to the nation as a whole. However compared to the city of Atlanta, it is a little less expensive.

Home prices

Fayetteville's median home cost is $224,100. It is roughly $39,400 (21%) higher compared to the surrounding areas. It is roughly $6,600 (3%) higher compared to the entire nation, and compared to the city of Atlanta it is about $66,300 (22%) lower.

Rents

The median rent in Fayetteville is $1,157 a month. Compared to its nearby areas this is about $13 (1%) lower. In comparison to the rest of the country it is roughly $95 (8%) higher, and is is very similar compared to the city of Atlanta.
