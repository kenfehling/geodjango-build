Home prices

The median home in Fayetteville costs $224,100. In comparison to its surrounding areas it is about $39,400 (21%) higher.Compared to the entire nation it is roughly $6,600 (3%) higher, and is approximately $66,300 (22%) lower in comparison to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Fayetteville the median monthly rent is $1,157. In comparison to the surrounding areas it is approximately $13 (1%) lower.In comparison to the country as a whole it is approximately $95 (8%) higher, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is is very similar.
