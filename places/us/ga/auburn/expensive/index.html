Compared to its nearby areas, Auburn is significantly less expensive, and it is significantly less expensive compared to the rest of the nation. It is substantially less expensive compared to the city of Atlanta.

Home prices

The median home in Auburn costs $142,000. It is about $68,140 (32%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. It is about $75,500 (34%) lower in comparison to the entire country, and it is about $148,400 (51%) lower compared to the city of Atlanta.

Rents

In Auburn the median rent is $864 a month. It is roughly $213 (19%) lower compared to the surrounding areas. Compared to the US as a whole it is roughly $198 (18%) lower, and in comparison to the city of Atlanta is approximately $289 (25%) lower.
